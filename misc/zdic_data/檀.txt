                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">檀</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>檀</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tán.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>落叶乔木，木质坚硬，用于制家具、乐器（亦称“青檀”）。</li><li>浅绛色：～口（形容红艳的嘴唇）。</li><li>〔～香〕常绿乔木，产在热带及亚热带，木质坚硬，有香气，可制器物及香料，又可入药。</li><li>〔紫～〕常绿乔木，木材坚硬，带红色，可制贵重家具或工艺品。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sandalwood, hardwood; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Tan  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> santal</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>檀</strong> <span class="dicpy">tán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，亶( <span class="dicpy">dǎn</span>)声。本义:木名。古书中称檀的木很多，时无定指。常指豆科的黄檀，紫檀(即红木);榆科的青檀)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sandalwood]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">檀，檀木也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坎坎伐檀兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·魏风·伐檀》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:檀车(古代车子多用檀木为之，故称);檀板(乐器名。檀木制的拍板);檀柘(檀树与柘树);檀桐(檀木与桐木);檀槽(檀木制成的琵琶、琴等弦乐器上架弦的槽格)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 香木檀的省称，即檀香 <span class="encs">[sandalwood]</span>。如:檀印(檀香);檀林(旃檀之林。佛寺的尊称);檀香扇(用檀香木做骨架的扇)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 情郎 <span class="encs">[fancy man]</span>。如:檀奴(檀郎，女子对丈夫或情郎的昵称);檀郎谢女(喻指多才而钟情的青年男女)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>檀</strong> <span class="dicpy">tán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>浅红色，浅绛色 <span class="encs">[light red]</span>。如:檀口香腮(比喻女子的嘴和脸红而香);檀口(女子粉红色的嘴唇);檀子(浅红色颜料)</p><p>◎ <strong>檀</strong> <span class="dicpy">tán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>布施 <span class="encs">[alms]</span>。表示与佛教有关的事物。如:檀那国(佛语。指没有邪恶凶杀，人们都好善乐施的理想国度);檀越(檀主，檀那。施主)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/檀板">檀板</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
