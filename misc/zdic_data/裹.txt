                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>裹</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">guǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǒ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄛˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>包；缠绕：～脚。把这包糖～好。～足不前。</li>
	<li>夹带；夹杂：不该把次货～进去卖。好人坏人～在一起一时分不清。</li>
	<li>方言，吸（奶）：小孩生下来就会～奶。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wrap, bind; encircle, confine</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Verband  (S)​,verbinden  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> envelopper</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">裹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>裹</strong> <span class="dicpy">guǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，果声。本义:包，缠)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wrap up;bind together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裹，缠也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朱三百，裹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">富之以国裹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;君臣》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓财货所苞裹而藏也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无不裹也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;本生》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹囊也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绿叶紫裹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋玉</span><span class="smcs">《高唐赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">濯颖散裹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《江赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃取蒙冲头舰十艘，载燥荻枯柴，灌油其中，裹以帷幕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伤者手为裹创，死者厚棺殓，酹醊而哭之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裹束(包裹束缚);裹足(包裹足部。亦作&ldquo;果足&rdquo;:盘缠，盘费);裹革(马革裹尸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 携带 <span class="encs">[bring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾闻</span><span class="diczx2">开元</span>中，下天富蕃，号为理平，踵千里者不裹粮。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孙樵</span><span class="smcs">《书褒城驿壁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裹粮策马(携带粮食，鞭赶马匹。形容忠义之士踊跃参军的情景);裹持(包裹挟持，犹携带)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 停止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却宾客以业诸侯，使天下之士退而不敢西向，裹足不入秦，此所谓藉寇兵而赍盗粮者也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李斯</span><span class="smcs">《谏逐客书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裹足(形容有所顾虑而止步);裹足不前(缠住脚不前进。形容有所顾虑而止步不敢向前)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 包罗;笼罩 <span class="encs">[surround]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:裹合(纠合;聚集);裹掖(包围);裹抹(遮掩)</span></p>
