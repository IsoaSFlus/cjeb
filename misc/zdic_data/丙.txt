                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丙</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>丙</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐng.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄥˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>天干的第三位，用作顺序第三的代称。</li>	<li>火的代称（五行中&ldquo;丙&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">丁</span>&rdquo;属火）：付～（把信件等烧掉）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丙</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。本义:鱼尾。<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释鱼》</span>曰:&lsquo;鱼枕谓之丁，鱼肠谓之乙，鱼尾谓之丙&rsquo;。乙之象鱼肠，丙之象鱼尾，可无庸说。&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 鱼尾 <span class="encs">[fishtail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鱼尾谓之丙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为天干的第三位，与地支相配，用以纪年、月、日 <span class="encs">[the third of the ten &ldquo;Celestial Stems&rdquo;]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其日丙丁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丙刚丁柔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释天》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丙子、丙戍、丙寅以纪年;丙科(汉代考试的第三等科目);丙夜(即三更。半夜，夜中子时)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代以十干配五方，丙为南方之位，因以指南方 <span class="encs">[south]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丙位南方，万物成炳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丙向(南向，即朝南)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 五行中丙丁属火，因以为火的代称。丙为阳火，丁为阴火 <span class="encs">[fire]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丙丁神(火神);付丙(烧掉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 在可数序列中的第三个 <span class="encs">[the third]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丙等</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>丙</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄅㄧㄥˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>bǐng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0267.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">天干的第三位。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用來排列次序等第，在甲、乙之後，表示第三。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>丙</b>班」、「<b>丙</b>等」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">火的別稱。如火災稱為「付丙」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子&middot;天文》：「其日丙丁。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;高誘&middot;注：「丙丁，皆火也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如漢代有丙吉。</span></p></li>
