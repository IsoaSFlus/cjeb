                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">气</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>气</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">氣</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">q&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>没有一定的形状、体积，能自由散布的物体：～体。</li>	<li>呼吸：没～了。～厥。～促。～息。一～呵成。</li>	<li>自然界寒、暧、阴、晴等现象：～候。～温。～象。</li>	<li>鼻子闻到的味：～味。臭～。</li>	<li>人的精神状态：～概。～节。～魄。～派。～馁。</li>	<li>怒，或使人发怒：不要～我了。～恼。～盛（<span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng </span>）。忍～吞声。</li>	<li>欺压：受～。</li>	<li>中医指能使人体器官发挥机能的动力：～功。～血。～虚。</li>	<li>中医指某种症象：痰～。湿～。</li>	<li>景象：和～。～氛。～韵（文章或书法绘画的意境或韵味）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">气</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>气</strong></p>
<p>氣 <span class="dicpy">q&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文、小篆字形。象云气蒸腾上升的样子。&ldquo;气&rdquo;是汉字部首之一。本义:云气)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cloud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气，云气也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，云者，地面之气，湿热之气升而为雨，其色白，干热之气，散而为风，其色黑。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天气下降，地气上腾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地有气。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;总目》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">望其气。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此天子气也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窗以达气。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:气朔(指显示吉凶的云气和每月的朔日);气祲(古代泛指据以占卜吉凶的云气);气纬(指云气星象);气晕(日、月四周所生的晕气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 气体的通称 <span class="encs">[gas]</span>。既没有独立的形态又没有固定的体积而趋向无限膨胀的流体。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气蚀。又指天然气。如:气田</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 空气 <span class="encs">[air]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气谓嘘吸出入者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭义》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:气球(一种供游戏用的足球，用皮革制成，内充空气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 气象 <span class="encs">[meteorology]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故在天为气。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;天元纪大论》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓风热温燥寒。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天有六气&hellip;六气曰阴、阳、风、雨、晦、明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山气日夕佳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《饮酒》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春气动。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 节气;节候 <span class="encs">[solar term]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气序(节气;季气;气候);气和(气候调和);气劲(气候寒冽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 气味 <span class="encs">[odor;smell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">香气环旋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恶气袭衣裙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与饮食之气相薄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:气子(气味);气韵</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 呼吸;气息 <span class="encs">[breath]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武</span>气绝半日复息。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缢即气绝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">启时气绝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:气命(性命);气歇(缓一口气);气疾(呼吸系统疾病);气凑(呼吸急促)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 声气，语气 <span class="encs">[mood]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气长(理直气壮);气拍(醒木;惊堂木);气竭声嘶(气力竭尽，声音嘶哑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 景象;气氛 <span class="encs">[atmosphere]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以达秋气。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 指社会风气和习俗 <span class="encs">[general mood;common practice]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气土(犹风土);气尚(风尚，气节);气俗(风气习俗);气脉(风气，习俗);气习(风气习俗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 指人、物的属性或一地的天然特点 <span class="encs">[character]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气秀(气质优美);气昏(气昏混浊);气品(指万物);气习(气质，习性);气孱(气质虚弱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 气运 <span class="encs">[luck]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气脉(犹气运)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 指人的元气 <span class="encs">[qi]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气者，体之充也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气母(元气的本原)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 指精神状态，情绪 <span class="encs">[morale;spirit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恢弘志气。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敢以矜气。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其气浩然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气叹(泄气);气不忿(不甘心;不服气);气不愤(气不忿);气合(意气相投);气志(指精神、意志)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 气派;气概 <span class="encs">[manner;style]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气色(气势;气派);气性(气势;威风);气傲(气高;意气高傲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 义气 <span class="encs">[personal loyalty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气谊(情谊;交情);气侠(义气豪侠);气义(义气;情谊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(18)</span> 气焰;权势 <span class="encs">[arrogance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一鼓作气。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以厉其气。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气吞万里如虎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《京口北固亭怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豪民气尽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气沮(气势衰弱);气魄(气势)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(19)</span> 指作家的气质或作品的风格，气势 <span class="encs">[style]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气格(指诗文的气韵和风格);气习(诗文的风格)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(20)</span> 作风;习气 <span class="encs">[style]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气重(气性大);气信(意气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(21)</span> 中医学术语。指脉气和营卫 <span class="encs">[energy of life;vital energy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五气、五声、五色。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;疾医》</span>。注:&ldquo;肺气热，心气次之，肝气凉，脾气温，肾气寒。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气疫(谓染疫气而得病);气脉(血气与脉息)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(22)</span> 指效力，作用 <span class="encs">[effect]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气业(功业)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(23)</span> 通&ldquo;器&rdquo;。用具，工具 <span class="encs">[utensil;apparatus]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三者本于心，然后乐气从之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>气</strong></p>
<p>氣 <span class="dicpy">q&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 使生气;使气恼 <span class="encs">[enrage]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气人;他故意气她一下</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 愤怒;气恼;生气 <span class="encs">[be angry]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:气闹(气恼;恼怒);气蛊(气恼);气寒(气恼至极点);气不舍(生气不过);气虎虎(气愤);气狠狠(形容生气发狠的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;乞&rdquo;。向人求讨 <span class="encs">[go begging;beg]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是若人气。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;非儒下》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>气</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 氣 <span class="dicpy">q&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后缀</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用在形容词后，相当于&ldquo;样子&rdquo; 。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:秀气，俊气，美气</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">气</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下" target="_blank">辰集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下&amp;bs=气" target="_blank">气</a>】
                              气·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=4" target="_blank">4</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6C14.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】去旣切【集韻】丘旣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音炁。【說文】雲气也。象形。一曰息也。或作氣、炁。</p><p>又與人物也。今作乞。</p><p>又【玉篇】去乙切【廣韻】去訖切【集韻】【類篇】欺訖切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音乞。【博雅】求也。一曰取也。或省文作乞。【徐官&middot;古今印史】氣，小篆本作气。氣爲火所化，其出必炎上，故象炎上之形。凡求乞者必於上，因借爲求乞字。气、乞本同一字也。後世隷楷以二字易混，乃省一筆以別之。</p><p>又【六書正譌】气，俗用氣，乃稟氣之氣。雲气必用气。○按天地人物之氣雖別，而气、氣字義實同，分屬則泥矣，《正譌》之說非是。</p><p>（氣）〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23C5B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣱛</span>【唐韻】去旣切【集韻】【韻會】【類篇】丘旣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音气。【玉篇】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>也，息也。【文子&middot;守弱篇】形者，生之舍也。氣者，生之元也。【易&middot;乾卦】同氣相求。【繫辭】精氣爲物。【禮&middot;月令】孟春之月，天氣下降，地氣上騰。</p><p>又【祭義】氣也者，神之盛也。【註】氣謂噓吸出入者也。</p><p>又天氣曰元氣。【後漢&middot;明帝紀】升靈臺，望元氣。【註】元氣，天氣也。</p><p>又陰陽曰二氣。【太極圖說】二氣交感，化生萬物。</p><p>又五氣。【史記&middot;五帝紀】炎帝修德振兵，治五氣。【註】王肅曰：五氣，五方之氣。</p><p>又【書&middot;洪範&middot;曰雨曰暘曰燠曰寒曰風註】雨木氣，暘金氣，燠火氣，寒水氣，風土氣爲五氣。</p><p>又【素問】寒熱風燥濕，五氣之聚也。寒生水，熱生火，風生木，燥生金，濕生土。</p><p>又六氣。【左傳&middot;昭元年】六氣：隂陽風雨晦明也。</p><p>又【莊子&middot;逍遙遊】乗天地之正，而御六氣之辨。【註】平旦爲朝霞，日中爲正陽，日入爲飛泉，夜半爲沆瀣，與天<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>地黃爲六氣。【王逸&middot;楚辭註】《陵陽子明經》春食朝霞，日欲出時黃氣也。秋食淪隂，日沒已後赤黃氣也。冬食沆瀣，北方夜半氣也。夏食正陽，南方日中氣也。幷天<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>地黃之氣爲六氣。</p><p>又年有二十四氣。【內經】五日謂之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>，三<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>謂之氣。【書&middot;正義】二十八宿，布於四方，隨天轉運，所以敘氣節也。氣節者，一歲三百六十五日有餘，分爲十二月，有二十四氣，一爲節氣，謂月初也，一爲中氣，謂月半也，以彼迭見之星，敘此月之節氣。</p><p>又曆家有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>氣法。【司馬彪&middot;續漢書】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>氣之法，爲室三重，塗亹周密，布緹幔室中，以木爲按，每律各一，內庳外高，從其方位，加律上，以葭灰抑其內端，案歷而<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>之。氣所動者，其灰斯散，人及風所動者，其灰聚。</p><p>又有望雲氣法。【周禮&middot;春官】保章氏以五雲之物，辨吉凶水旱之祲象。【註】視日旁雲氣之色，靑爲蟲，白爲喪，赤爲兵荒，黑爲水，黃爲豐。【史記&middot;天官書】雲氣有獸居上者勝。【又】日旁雲氣，人主象。【又】金寶之上皆有氣，不可不察。海旁蜃氣象樓臺，廣野氣成宮闕。然雲氣各象其山川人民所聚積，精華實息者吉，虛耗者凶。【邵諤&middot;望氣經】鬱鬱蔥蔥，隱隱隆隆，佳氣也。綿綿絞絞，條條片片，兵氣也。澤澤燄燄，女子氣也。如藤蔓挂樹者，寶氣也。紫氛如樓者，玉氣也。</p><p>又道家有食氣法。【參同契】食氣鳴腸胃，吐正吸外邪。</p><p>又以鼻觸物亦曰氣。【禮&middot;少儀】執食飮者勿氣。【疏】執尊長飮食，不鼻臭之也。</p><p>又【唐韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>許旣切。與餼、䊠<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>通。【說文】饋客芻米也。引《春秋傳》齊人來氣諸侯。</p><p>又叶去訖切，音乞。【成公綏&middot;嘯賦】聲不假器，用不借物。近取諸身，役心御氣。</p><p>又叶丘謁切，音朅。【嵆康&middot;寒食散賦】當吐利之困患兮，守危殆而假氣。喜乳哺之遂安兮，信衆疾之日歇。</p>
