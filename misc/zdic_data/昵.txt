						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>昵</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄧˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 亲近：～爱。亲～。～比（亲近勾结）。～称（表示亲近的称呼）。</p>
<ol>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> intimate, close; approach</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ähnlich sein, anfliegen ,geläufig, allgemein bekannt</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>昵</strong></p>
<p>暱 <span class="dicpy">n&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，尼声。字又作&ldquo;暱&rdquo;。从日，匿( <span class="dicpy">n&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:亲近，亲昵) 同本义 <span class="encs">[close;intimate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昵之以观其不狎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《逸周书&bull;官人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">初，澹从弟混与刘毅昵，澹常以为忧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南史&bull;谢澹传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昵就(亲近;亲昵);昵比(亲近勾结);昵好(亲善);昵近(亲近);昵狎(亲近;亲狎);昵依(偎依);</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span>指亲宠；亲戚。<span class="encs">[relative]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">官不及私昵，惟其能。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&bull;说命中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昵交(亲近的朋友);昵侍(近侍，亲侍);昵友(亲密的朋友);</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span>近，距离近。<span class="encs">[be close to;draw near]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昵道(近路;捷径)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昵</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上" target="_blank">辰集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上&amp;bs=日" target="_blank">日</a>】
                              昵·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6635.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>尼質切，音匿。【說文】暱或从尼。【書&middot;說命】官不及私昵。【傳】昵，近也。</p>
<p>又【泰誓】昵比罪人。【疏】昵，親近也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】入質切，音日。義同。</p>
<p>又乃禮切，音禰。【書&middot;高宗肜日】典祀無豐于昵。【釋文】昵，考也，謂禰廟也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>質力切，音職。黏也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;弓人】凡昵之類不能方。【音義】昵，又音職。【註】鄭司農云：謂膠善戾，故書昵。或作樴。杜子春云：樴讀爲不義不昵之昵。或爲䵑。䵑，黏也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶尼結切。【殷臣&middot;奇布賦】燎無爍而不焦，在兹林而獨昵。火焚木而不枯，木吐火而無竭。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">暱</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“昵”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/暱">暱</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=日">日</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/66B1.svg">                          <p>日近也。从日匿聲。《春秋傳》曰：&ldquo;私降暱燕。&rdquo;昵，暱或从尼。尼質切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/66B1_2584.svg" /><span class="swbf">(暱)</span><span class="ywyy">日近也。</span><span class="swdz">日謂日日也，皆日之引伸之義也。釋詁、小雅傳皆云：暱，近也。左傳：不義不暱。非其私暱，誰敢任之。</span><span class="ywyy">从日匿聲。</span><span class="swdz">舉形聲包會意。尼質切。古音在一部。魚力切。</span><span class="ywyy">春秋傳曰：私降暱燕。</span><span class="swdz">昭卄五年左傳文。今本作昵。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/66B1_2585.svg" /><span class="swbf">(昵)</span><span class="ywyy">或从尼作。</span><span class="swdz">攷工記：凡昵之類不能方。故書昵或作樴，或爲䵑。杜子春讀爲不義不昵之昵。按古文叚尼爲昵。古文尙書：典祀無豐於尼。釋詁云：卽尼也。孫炎曰：卽猶今也。尼，近也。郭樸引尸子：悅尼而來遠。釋文引尸子：不避遠尼。自衛包改尙書作昵，宋開寶閒又改釋文，而古文之讀不應爾雅矣。</span></p>                          </div>
