                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">檐</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>檐</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yán.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>房顶伸出墙壁的部分：房～儿。廊～。飞～。前～。～沟。～头。～牙。</li><li>覆盖物的边沿或伸出的部分：帽～儿。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> eaves of house; brim, rim</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dachgesims, Dachvorsprung  (S)</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>檐</strong></p><p>簷 <span class="dicpy">yán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，詹( <span class="dicpy">zhān</span>)声。本义:屋檐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[eaves]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">复庙重檐。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·明堂位》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“重檐，重承壁材也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">榆柳荫后檐，桃李罗堂前。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">檐牙高啄(檐牙:屋顶伸出的边沿。高啄:像鸟向高处啄食一样)。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:檐石(屋檐下的石板);檐宇(屋檐);檐花(靠近屋檐下边开的花);檐板(屋檐板);檐阴(屋檐下阳光不到处);檐梁(檐下的屋梁);檐帷(檐下的帘幕);檐楣(屋檐);檐马(也称风铃，铁马);檐铁(挂在屋檐下的风铃)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 某些器物上形状像屋檐的部分 <span class="encs">[brim]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时逆帽檐风刮顶，旋呵鞭手冻粘须。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜荀鹤</span><span class="smcs">《早发》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:帽檐儿</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/檐沟">檐沟</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/檐口">檐口</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/檐溜">檐溜</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/檐马">檐马</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/檐头">檐头</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/檐瓦">檐瓦</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
