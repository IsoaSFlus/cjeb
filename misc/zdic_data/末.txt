                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">末</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>末</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mò.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>尖端，梢：～梢。～端。秋毫之～（毫毛尖端）。</li><li>最后，终了：～了（<span class="dicpy">liǎo </span>）。～尾。～日。～代。穷途～路。</li><li>非根本的，次要的，差一等的：～业。～技。舍本逐～。</li><li>碎屑：～子。碎～。</li><li>传统戏剧角色名，一般扮演中年以上男子：正～。副～。外～。～本（以男角主唱的杂剧）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> final, last, end; insignificant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ende, Finale, abschliessendes Stadium, letzter Teil  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> extrémité,fin,poudre,dernier,final</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">末</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>末</strong> <span class="dicpy">mò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。金文字形。<span class="yhcs">“木”</span>上加一点，指明树木末梢所在处。本义:树梢)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tip of tree]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">末，木上曰末。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不揣其本，而齐其末。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">末有十日，其华照下地。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·地形》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">末大必折。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:末大(树木枝端粗大。喻部属势力强大);末大必折(树木枝端粗大，必折其干。喻下属权重，危及上级);末杪(末尾);末梢(末尾;最后)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指物的末端、末尾 <span class="encs">[last stage;the end]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元首末要。——<span class="smcs">《周书·武顺》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风淫末疾。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公元年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“四肢也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明足以察秋毫之末。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">譬若锥之处囊中，其末立见。——<span class="smcs">《史记·平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非特其末见而已。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">强弩之末。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左手执卷末。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:末大不掉(犹尾大不掉。比喻部属势力强大，难以驾驭);末岁(岁末);末冬(冬末);末秋(秋末);末春(春末)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代称农为本，反本为末，即工商业 <span class="encs">[industry and commerce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">末技游食之民。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span>。注:(末伎。古指工商业不值得重视的技能，游食，不劳而食)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:末民(古称从事工商业的人);末产(手工业、商业);末作(古代指工商业);末利(古代指工商业);末生(末业。指工商业)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 传统戏曲角色名。主要扮演中年男子 <span class="encs">[actor for a mid-aged man as in classic opera]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杂剧有正末、副末之名。——</span><span class="diczx2">焦循</span><span class="smcs">《剧说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 末期;晚年 <span class="encs">[end;last stage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">武王</span>末受命。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">申末酉初。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">崇祯</span>末。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:末尾三梢(收场，结局);物有本末，事有终始;他三月末回来;末限(最后期限);末垂(最后的时日)</p><p><span
