                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粒</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>粒</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>成颗的东西，细小的固体：米～。盐～。颗～。～子（ａ．成果的、细小的东西，“<span class="diczx3">子</span>”读轻声；ｂ．指“基本粒子”，物理学上指构成物体的最简单的物质）。</li><li>量词，多指颗粒状的东西：一～米。一～珍珠。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> grain; small particle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Korn; Körnchen  (S)​,ZEW für kleine runde Dinge  (Zähl)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grain,granule,(classificateur pour les petits objets sphériques)​</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>粒</strong> <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从米，立声。本义:米粒，谷粒)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grain]</span>。如:粒食(以米谷为食);粒米束薪(极言粮草之少);粒米狼戾(谷粒撒得满地都是。形容粮食充盈)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 粒状物 <span class="encs">[granule;bead]</span>。如:盐粒;豆粒;砂粒儿;细粒</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>粒</strong> <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 进食;吃饭 <span class="encs">[take food]</span>。如:粒食(以谷物为食)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 养活 <span class="encs">[support;feed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生我百谷，粒我烝民。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">傅咸</span><span class="smcs">《喜雨赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“立”</span>。成 <span class="encs">[accomplish;succeed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烝民乃粒，万邦作乂。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋陶谟》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>粒</strong> <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p>用于呈圆形的器物，相当于<span class="yhcs">“颗”</span>。如:三粒子弹</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/粒度">粒度</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/粒间">粒间</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/粒雪">粒雪</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/粒子">粒子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
