                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">臭</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>臭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ch&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>不好闻的气味，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">香</span>&rdquo;相对：～味儿。～氧。恶～。</li>	<li>香气：&ldquo;左佩刀，右备容～，烨然若神人&rdquo;。</li>	<li>惹人厌恶的：～钱。～美。～架子。～名远扬。</li>	<li>狠狠地：～骂一通。</li>	<li>指子弹、炮弹坏，失效：～火。</li></ol><p>● <strong>臭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xi&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>气味的总称：无声无～。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/嗅">嗅</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> smell, stink, emit foul odor</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schlecht riechend, stinkend (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> odeur,puant,dégoûtant,détestable</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">臭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>臭</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从犬，从自(鼻的本字)。狗鼻特别灵敏，故从二字会意。(<span class="dicpy">xi&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> )本义:闻气味)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 香气 <span class="encs">[odor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">同心之言，其臭如兰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 难闻的气味 <span class="encs">[bad smell;stink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">惠公</span>改葬 <span class="diczx2">申生</span>，臭彻于外。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:臭不 可当(极言其臭);臭短臊长(各种污言秽语);臭皮囊(肉体;躯壳)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>臭</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 惹人厌恶 <span class="encs">[abhorrent]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:臭幺(臭架子);臭老鼠(惹厌的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 腐败 <span class="encs">[rotten]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若乘舟，汝弗济，臭厥载。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚中》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>臭</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>〈方〉∶失效 <span class="encs">[lose efficacy]</span>。</p>
