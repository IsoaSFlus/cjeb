                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">册</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>册</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">冊</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">c&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄜˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古代称编串好的竹简，现在指装潢好的纸本子：纪念～。花名～。画～。</li>	<li>量词，指书籍：一～书。</li>	<li>古代帝王祭祀天地神仙的文书或封爵的诏书：～文（文体名，为册命、册书等诰命文字的一种。简称&ldquo;册&rdquo;）。～命。～封。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> book, volume, register, list</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Band, Exemplar (Buch)​</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tome,volume,exemplaire</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">册</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>册</strong></p>
<p>冊 <span class="dicpy">c&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文、小篆字形与甲骨文相似，象简册形。本义:书简。古代文书用竹简。编简名为册，后凡簿籍均可称&ldquo;册&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[book]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">册，符命也。诸侯进受于王者。象其札，一长一短，中有二编之形。古文又从竹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 经传多以策为之，字俗作筴</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">史乃册祝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;金滕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公归，乃纳册于金滕之匮中。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王命作册。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洛诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有册有典。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;多士》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:名册;画册;纪念册;册书(史籍，史书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古时称编串好的许多竹简，现在指装订好的纸本子 <span class="encs">[volume]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:册籍(名册);册历(亦作&ldquo;历册&rdquo;。账本)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 特指皇帝的诏书 <span class="encs">[imperial edict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祝册自京师至。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《南海神庙碑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:册文(古代帝王祭祀时告天地神袛的文书);册书(古时君王施于臣下的文书。有祝册、玉册、立册、封册、哀册、赠册、谥册、赠谥册、祭册、赐册、免册等十一种)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>册</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 册封，封爵 <span class="encs">[grant in a imperial edict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">册太子则授玺绶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书&middot;百官至》</span></span></p>
