                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>脸</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">臉</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˇ</span></p><ol><li>面孔，头的前部从额到下巴：～颊。～孔。～形（亦作“脸型”）。～色。～谱。</li><li>物体的前部：鞋～儿。门～儿。</li><li>体面，面子，颜面：～面。～皮。～软。丢～。赏～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> face; cheek; reputation</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ansehen, Prestige  (S)​,Gesicht  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> visage,face,réputation</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">脸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>脸</strong></p><p>臉 <span class="dicpy">liǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，佥( <span class="dicpy">qiān</span>)声。古代表示人的整个面部用<span class="yhcs">“面”</span>。<span class="yhcs">“脸”</span>字在魏晋时期才出现，只表示两颊的上部，到了唐宋时期，口语中才用<span class="yhcs">“脸”</span>表示整个面部。本义:两颊的上部)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cheeks]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蛾眉与曼脸，见此空愁人。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《小垂手》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">满面黄沙满鬓风，眉销残黛脸销红。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《昭君怨》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">头圆筋骨紧，两脸明且光。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《冬至日寄小侄阿宜》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:脸蛋(脸的两旁部分);脸巴子(面颊);脸上(面颊之上)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 脸面，人头的前部，包括下巴、嘴、鼻、颊、眼，且通常包括前额 <span class="encs">[face]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">脸波春傍窈娘堤。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《天津桥》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你看你的脸…尽是血，我都不认识你了。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹禺</span><span class="smcs">《雷雨》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:脸脑(面孔);脸帕(洗脸巾);脸道(脸面，面容);脸庞(脸面形状轮廓);脸谱;脸相;脸模子;脸貌。又指情面;面子</p><p><span class="diczx2">婆惜</span>也不曾睡着，听得 <span class="diczx2">宋江</span>骂时，扭过身回道:<span class="yhcs">“你不羞这脸!”</span>——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:没脸见人;赏脸;丢尽了脸</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指某些物体的前部 <span class="encs">[front]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东西房全是敞脸的，是存车的所在。——</span><span class="diczx2">老舍</span><span class="smcs">《骆驼祥子》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:鞋脸;城门脸;脸戏儿(鞋面子);脸壳子(方言。喻指虚伪的表面);门脸儿(城门附近的地方;商店的门面)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 脸色;脸上的表情 <span class="encs">[facial expression]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谁知你转背儿著他人也，又另是一样脸。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">吴骞</span><span class="smcs">《扶风传信录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:急赤白脸(心里着急，脸色难看);哭丧着脸;涎着脸;绷脸</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脸薄">脸薄</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
