                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>姻</strong></p><p>婣 <span class="dicpy">yīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从女，从因，因亦声。本义:女婿家)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[family of son-in-law]</span>。结亲的男家，指夫或夫之父</p><p><span class="diczx1">姻，婿家也，女之所因，故曰姻。从女，从因，因亦声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">婿之父为姻。又，婿之党为姻兄弟。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不思旧姻。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·我行其野》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">琐琐姻亚。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·节南山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">某以得外昏姻。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏媾姻娅。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“壻父曰姻，两壻相谓曰亚。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆弦姻也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:姻母(对兄弟妻之母、姐妹夫之母以及疏亲前辈之妻的称呼)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 妻或妻之父 <span class="encs">[wife or wife's father]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏姻之礼。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“婿曰昏，妻曰姻。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 如:姻翁(亲家公。儿子的丈人或女儿的公公);姻臣(帝王的外戚);姻母(姻伯母。对兄弟妻之母、姐妹夫之母的称呼)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姻亲 <span class="encs">[relations by marriage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">泾阳</span>之妻，则 <span class="diczx2">洞庭君</span>之爱女也。淑性茂质，为九姻所重。——<span class="smcs">《太平广记》</span>引<span class="smcs">《异闻集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:姻绱(姻亲;亲戚);姻妾(姻亲的婢妾);姻里(帝王的姻戚);姻昵(近的姻亲);姻通(姻亲之谊);姻族(有姻亲关系的各家族或其成员);姻丧(外亲之丧);姻连(姻戚，姻眷，姻媛，姻援，姻私，姻亚，姻好，姻媾，姻懿。都指姻亲);姻家(联姻的家族或其成员);姻兄(姻亲中同辈弟兄的互称)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 婚姻 <span class="encs">[marriage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">源</span>频叨诸府戎禁，预班通彻而托姻结，唯利是求。——<span class="smcs">《文选·沈约·奏弹王源》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:姻缘辐辏(形容男女结成夫妻从各方面来说都很合适)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/姻亲">姻亲</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
