                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>南</strong> <span class="dicpy">nā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>另见 <span class="dicpy">nán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/南无">南无</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>南</strong> <span class="dicpy">nán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形，甲骨文字形，是钟镈之类的乐器。①本义:乐器。<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·鼓钟》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“以雅以南。”</span>②方位词)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 方位名。和<span class="yhcs">“北”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[south]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南，草木至南方有枝任也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出户南望。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南抚</span><span class="diczx2">夷</span><span class="diczx2">越</span>。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旌麾南指。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南极</span><span class="diczx2">潇</span><span class="diczx2">湘</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:南薰(借指从南面刮来的风);南离(南方)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 南方;南面 <span class="encs">[South]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">阮仲容</span>步兵居道南，诸 <span class="diczx2">阮</span>居道北。——<span class="smcs">《新说新语·言语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
