                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">函</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>函</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>匣，盒子：石～。剑～。</li>
	<li>套子，引申为量词：书～。信一～（信一封）。</li>
	<li>信件：～件。公～。来～。</li>
	<li>包含，容纳：～夏（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">夏</span>&rdquo;，指中国；&ldquo;函夏&rdquo;即包括整个中国）。巨蚌～珠。</li>
	<li>铠甲：～人（制甲的人）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> correspondence; a case; a box</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Briefumschlag, Umschlag, Kuvert, Briefkuvert  (S)​,Schreiben, Brief  (S)​,Han  (Eig, Fam)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">函</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>函</strong></p>
<p>圅 <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。今隶误作函。本义:舌)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tongue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圅，舌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若合而函吾中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span>。注:&ldquo;入也。&rdquo;按，如舌之在口中也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">口上曰臄，口下曰圅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">噱，圅舌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释亲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 盛物的匣子、套子 <span class="encs">[case]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">函封之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">函封。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">买五人之脰而函之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剑函;镜函</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 传达消息或指示的信件(古代寄信用木函) <span class="encs">[letter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:函仪(信件礼物);函章(信件公文);函片(信件);函札(书信)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 邮政 <span class="encs">[mail]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:函购</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 信封 <span class="encs">[envelope]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">发函伸纸，&hellip;。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;吴质&middot;答东阿王书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 铠甲 <span class="encs">[armor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">函，铠也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:函人(制造铠甲的工人)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>函</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 包含;容纳 <span class="encs">[contain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">席间函丈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;容也。&rdquo;</span></p>
