<p><span class="dicpy">n&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄢˊ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">男</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>男</strong> <span class="dicpy">n&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从田，从力。表示用力(一说指耒)在田间耕作。本义:男人，与&ldquo;女&rdquo;相对)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[man]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男，丈夫也。从男从力，言男用力于田也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃生男子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;斯干》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则百斯男。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;思齐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男女奔窜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男女衣著，悉如外人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:男事(古指成年男子应尽的职责);男夫(成年男子);男圻(犹男服);男教(对男子的教化);男德(有德行的男子);男权(男子在家庭、社会中的支配性特权)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为儿子 <span class="encs">[boy;son]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三男邺成戍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一男附书至。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信知生男恶，反是生女好。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生子是男。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两女一男。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:男花女花(一男半女;引伸为孩子);长男;次男;生男育女。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又指儿子对父母的自称 <span class="encs">[your son]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男病已愈，胃口亦渐开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《书信》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 封建制度五等爵位的第五等 <span class="encs">[baron]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王者之制禄爵，公、侯、伯、子、男，凡五等。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
