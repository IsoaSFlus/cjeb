                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">私</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>私</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙˉ</span></p><ol><li>个人的，自己的，与“<span class="diczx3">公</span>”相对：～人。～有。～见。～仇。～情。～营。～欲。</li><li>不公开的，秘密而又不合法的：～自。～刑。～货。走～。～生子。</li><li>暗地里：～议。～奔。隐～。窃窃～语。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">私</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>私</strong> <span class="dicpy">sī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾，厶( <span class="dicpy">sī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:禾;庄稼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[standing grain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">私，禾也。北道名禾主人曰私主人。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“盖禾有名私者也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 中国古时女子称姊妹之夫为私 <span class="encs">[sister’s husband]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谭公维私。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·硕人》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏引 <span class="diczx2">孙炎</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“私，无正亲之言。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 男女阴部 <span class="encs">[pudenda of men and women;private part of the body]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早有私病，不近妇人。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">伶玄</span><span class="smcs">《赵飞燕外传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:私病;私处(生殖器)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 日常衣服 <span class="encs">[everyday clothes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薄污我私。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·葛覃》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:私衣(官吏日常的便衣)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 非法的货物 <span class="encs">[illegal goods]</span>。如:走私;贩私;缉私</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 古指私田 <span class="encs">[private field]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雨我公田，遂及我私。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·大田》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骏发尔私，终三十里。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·噫嘻》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古代卿大夫的家臣 <span class="encs">[servant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">某也，夫子之贱私。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又指诸侯国君的嬖臣妾媵</p><p><span class="diczx1">君多私。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:蓄私</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>私</strong> <span class="dicpy">sī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“厶”</span>。私人的;自己的。与<span class="yhcs">“公”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[individual;private;personal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>之为 <span class="diczx2">汉</span>，几四十年矣，公私之积，犹可哀痛。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">丹</span>不忍以乇之私，而伤长者之意，愿足下更虑之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼覆无遗谓之公，反公为私。——<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">私者，下之所以侵法乱主也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·任法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皇天无私阿兮。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡君君之私也。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·燕礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓独受恩厚也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大夫有私丧。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·杂记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“妻女子之丧也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不以私邑累公邑也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·昭公五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>                     <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">私</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>私</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄙ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>sī <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5554.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">属于个人事物。与「公」相对。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大公无<b>私</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《书经·周官》：「以公灭<b>私</b>，民其允怀。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">财产。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史·第三回》：「你不看见城里张府上那些老爷，都有万贯家<b>私</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">私下的言行举止。</span><span class="gc_yy">《论语·为政》：「退而省其<b>私</b>，亦足以发。回也不愚。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代指姊妹的丈夫。</span><span class="gc_yy">《诗经·卫风·硕人》：「邢侯之姨，谭公为<b>私</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">非法的货物。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「走<b>私</b>」、「缉<b>私</b>」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">生殖器官。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「男<b>私</b>」、「女<b>私</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">清·袁枚《子不语·卷一一·医妒》：「然裙裂损，几露其<b>私</b>焉。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">个人的、非官方的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>私</b>宅」、「私怨」、「私塾」、「<b>私</b>生活」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">非法、不公开的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「私盐」、「私娼」。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">暗中的、秘密的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>私</b>议」、「私相授受」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷七九·范雎蔡泽传》：「与<b>私</b>约而去。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·白居易〈长恨歌〉：「七月七日长生殿，夜半无人私语时。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">偏重一方。</span><span class="gc_yy">《礼记·孔子闲居》：「天无<b>私</b>覆，地无<b>私</b>载，日月无<b>私</b>照。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                              私·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=7" target="_blank">7</a>　·部外筆画：2</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/79C1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】息夷切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】相咨切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音司。【說文】禾也。北道名禾主人曰私主人。</p><p>又對公而言謂之私。【書·周官】以公滅私。【詩·小雅】雨我公田，遂及我私。【禮·孔子閒居】天無私覆，地無私載，日月無私照。</p><p>又家臣稱私。【儀禮·士相見禮】見於大夫曰：某也，夫子之賤私。【禮·玉藻】士自稱於大夫曰外私。【註】此大夫非己所臣，則稱外私。</p><p>又恩私也。【儀禮·燕禮】寡君，君之私也。【註】謂獨受恩厚。【禮·郊特牲】婦餕餘，私之也。【釋名】私，恤也，所恤念也。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋親】女子謂姊妹之夫曰私。【詩·衞風】譚公維私。</p><p>又天子以下皆曰私。【前漢·張放傳】大官私官。【服虔註】私官，皇后之官。【後漢·百官志】中宮私府令一人。</p><p>又姓。漢私匡。</p><p>又便也。【左傳·襄十五年】師慧過宋，朝將私焉。【註】謂小便。○按《說文》專以禾訓私，而厶自爲部，訓姦衺也。引韓非、倉頡作字，自營爲厶，則私不兼公私義。今經傳公厶字皆作私，不復作厶矣。</p>
