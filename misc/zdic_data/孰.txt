                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">孰</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>孰</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>谁，哪个：～是～非。</li>
	<li>什么：是可忍，～不可忍？</li>
	<li>用在表示抉择的反问语句中，有比较的意思：～与。～若。～何。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/熟">熟</a>&rdquo;，程度深。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> who? which? what? which one?</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> das, der ,das, was ,welcher, welche, welches</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> qui,que</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">孰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>孰</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。左上是&ldquo;享&rdquo;，左下是&ldquo;羊&rdquo;，表示食物是羊肉;右边是&ldquo;丮&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span>)，表示手持。合起来表示手持熟食来吃。本义:熟，煮熟)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。&ldquo;熟&rdquo;的古字<span class="encs">[cooked]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孰，食饪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作熟。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宰夫胹熊蹯不孰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孰烂</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;熟&rdquo;。成熟 <span class="encs">[ripe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寒暑和节，而五谷以时孰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;富国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腥其俎，孰其俎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五谷时孰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群生而万民殖，五谷孰而草木茂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;董仲舒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 庄稼丰收;五谷有收成 <span class="encs">[harvest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫妇节而天地和，风雨节而五谷孰，衣服节而肌肤和。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>孰</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p> 通&ldquo;熟&rdquo;。缜密，仔细，周详 <span class="encs">[careful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣恐天下之议君，愿孰察之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;更法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兼权之，孰计之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;不苟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宁孰谏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡虑事欲孰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;议兵》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓精审。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">思之未孰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周髀算经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唯大王与群臣孰计议之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明日，</span><span class="diczx2">徐</span>公来，孰视之，自以为不如。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孰复(反复思虑);孰虑(计虑周祥);孰视(细看);孰计(考虑缜密周到);孰论(仔细辨析);孰察(仔细观察)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>孰</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 谁，哪个人或哪些人 <span class="encs">[who]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孰不可忍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人非生而行之者，孰能无惑?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>

<p>又【正韻】何也。【論語】是可忍也，孰不可忍也。</p>
<p>又【正字通】審也。【漢武策賢良制】其孰之復之。○按《說文》生孰字本但作孰，後人加火，以別生熟之熟，而孰但爲誰孰字矣。</p>
