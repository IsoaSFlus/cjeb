						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>隘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄞˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>险要的地方：～口。要～。关～。险～。</li>
	<li>狭窄：～巷。～路。狭～。</li>
	<li>穷：～穷。～窘。贫～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> narrow, confined; a strategic pass</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> étroit,défilé,passage difficile,confiné,en détresse</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">隘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>隘</strong> <span class="dicpy">&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。籀文从阜，益声。阜，土山，从&ldquo;阜&rdquo;的字多与地势有关。本义:狭窄;狭小)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[narrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隘，陋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子以为隘矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span>。注:&ldquo;狭陋也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相逢狭路间，道隘不容车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《古诗十九首》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诞置之隘巷，牛间腓字之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;生民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为心胸狭窄 <span class="encs">[narrow-minded;intolerent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">伯夷</span>隘， <span class="diczx2">柳下惠</span>不恭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或言其太隘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此为宰相听事诚隘。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:狭隘(宽度小; <span class="encs">[心胸、气量、见识]</span> 局限在一个小范围内;不宏大)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>隘</strong> <span class="dicpy">&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 险要的通道，通常处在陡峭山谷的两个山峰之间 <span class="encs">[defile;bottleneck;pass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一人守隘，万夫莫向。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《蜀都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筑石寨土堡于要隘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span>。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:要隘(险要的关口)</p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中" target="_blank">戌集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中&amp;bs=阜" target="_blank">阜</a>】
                              隘·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9698.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【正韻】烏懈切【韻會】幺解切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音䅬。【左傳&middot;僖二十二年】古之爲軍也，不以阻隘也。【戰國策】塞黽隘。</p>
<p>又【說文】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E5F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨹟</span>也。【玉篇】急也，陝也。【詩&middot;大雅】誕置之隘巷。【禮&middot;禮器】君子以爲隘矣。【戰國策】人之急也，若自在隘窘之中。【荀子&middot;王霸篇】生民則致貧隘。</p>
<p>又【正韻】乙革切。與阨阸<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。【戰國策】太子辭於齊王而歸，齊王隘之。【又】寡人之所以閉關不通使者，爲中山之獨與燕趙爲王，而寡人不與聞焉，是以隘之。【註】隘，亦不通也。讀作阨。</p>
<p>又叶於旣切，衣去聲。【謝莊&middot;宋孝武帝文】芝蓋迫軨，上驤眷轡。萬㝢肅其北<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28339.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨌹</span>，靈阿具其深隘。</p>
<p>又叶伊昔切，音益。【屈原&middot;離騷】惟黨人之偸樂兮，路幽昧以險隘。豈余身之憚殃兮，恐皇輿之敗績。　【說文】籀作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28F74.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨽴</span>。【玉篇】本作阸。【集韻】或作阨。【篇海】一作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28F48.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨽈</span>。</p>
