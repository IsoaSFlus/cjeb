                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">导</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>导</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">導</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>指引，带领：领～。引～。向～（引路的人）。倡～。推～。～引。～游。～向。～师。～言。</li>
	<li>传引，传向：传～。～热。～致（引起）。</li>
	<li>启发：开～。教～。因势利～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> direct, guide, lead, conduct</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> leiten, führen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> conduire (à)​,diriger (vers)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">导</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>导</strong></p>
<p>導 <span class="dicpy">dǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从寸，道声。&ldquo;寸&rdquo;与&ldquo;手&rdquo;意思相近。本义:以手牵引，引导)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lead;guide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">导，引也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其治宜导引按轎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓摇筋骨动支节。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">导官令本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《续汉书&middot;百官志》</span>。注:&ldquo;择也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">导官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;邓后纪》</span>。注:&ldquo;主导择米以供祭祀。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">导一茎六穗于庖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑氏</span>曰:&ldquo;导，择也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虞人导前，鹰犬罗后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot;马中锡<span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:导子(官员出行时在前面开路的仪仗队);导从(古代官员出行时，担任前导和后从的人);导仗(前导的仪仗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 教导 <span class="encs">[instruct;teach]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而不可以导人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;缪称》</span>。注:&ldquo;教也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">手持杯珓导我掷，云此最吉余难同。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《谒衡岳庙遂宿岳寺题门楼》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:导播(电视台中指导表演、监督整个节目播出的专业人员);导训(引导而使和顺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 疏通 <span class="encs">[dredge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为川者决之使导。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:导行费(汉灵帝时，郡国、地方每在进贡朝廷之外，另有物品给中署，做为所献之物的导行费);导款(疏通);导泄(疏导排泄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 启发，开导 <span class="encs">[inspire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忠信以导之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;适威》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹先也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以导于民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;训也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后导以取保，出启于外。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:导谕(劝导晓谕);导示(指导启示)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 传导 <span class="encs">[transmit;conduct]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:导电;导热</span></p>
