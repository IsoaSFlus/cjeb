                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>油</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄡˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>动植物体内所含的脂肪或矿产的碳氢化合物的混和液体，一般不溶于水，容易燃烧：～饼。～布。～彩。～画。～亮。～水（亦指不正常的收益或额外好处）。～轮。～垢。奶～。石～。食～。汽～。添～加醋。</li>	<li>用油或漆涂抹：～刷（①涂抹油、漆；②涂抹油、漆的刷子）。</li>	<li>被油弄脏：衣服～了一大片。</li>	<li>狡猾：～滑。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">油</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>油</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，由声。本义:油水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[You River]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">油，油水出武陵孱陵西，东南入</span><span class="diczx2">江</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，在今湖北荆州府公安县北。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古水名。也作&ldquo;繇&rdquo;。发源于湖北五峰县界，东流经松滋县界，至公安县西南油口注入长江。今其上游汇入松滋河，南流注入澧水，下游为荆江分洪区，故址已不复存在</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 动物的脂肪和由植物或矿物中提炼出来的脂质物 <span class="encs">[oil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积油满万石，则自然生火。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张华</span><span class="smcs">《博物志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:油尽灯枯(形容人被消耗得心力衰竭，生命垂危);油盏(油灯);油海(指特大的油田);油火虫虫(方言。即萤火虫)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>油</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 涂油 <span class="encs">[apply tung oil or paint]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茶色贵白，而饼茶多以珍膏油其面。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡襄</span><span class="smcs">《茶录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:油窗户;油抹(上漆上油);油壁香车(一种油漆彩绘的车，多为妇女乘用);油搭扇(一种竹骨黑油纸的折扇);油衣(雨衣);油鬏髻(油鬏髻。女人头上梳的发髻。亦代指女人)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>油</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 比喻浮滑，不诚实 <span class="encs">[oily;glib]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若不多读书，多贯穿，而遽言性情，则开后学油腔滑调、信口成章之恶习矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王士祯</span><span class="smcs">《师友传灯录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:油嘴贫舌(爱多说话，耍嘴皮子);油花和尚(油头滑脑不守清规的和尚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 色泽光润的样子 <span class="encs">[glossy;shiny]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禾黍油油。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;宋微子世家》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;光悦貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
