                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>荚</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">莢</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiá.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄚˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 豆科植物的长形果实，亦指狭长无隔膜的其他草木的果实：豆～。～果。皂～。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pods of leguminous plants; pods</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Behälter, Fuß (zur Befestigung von Instrumenten)​  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> graine,gousse</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>荚</strong></p><p>莢 <span class="dicpy">jiá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，夹声。本义:<span class="yhcs">“荚果”</span>的简称)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。豆科植物的长角果或蓇葖，为单片或有两个以上果片(如蒴果) <span class="encs">[pods of leguminous plants]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荚，草实也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豆角谓之荚。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得时之菽，其荚二七，以为族。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·审时》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其植物宜荚物。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司徒》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“荠荚王棘之属。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:豆荚;皂荚;结荚;荚物(植物果实的一种名称。凡草木结实时，有狭长的果皮包裹着，至成熟时，皮自破裂而籽出者，统称荚物);荚钱(榆荚钱的简称，又名五分钱)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 某些树木的翅果 <span class="encs">[samara]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">榆荚还飞买笑钱。——</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《和人题真娘墓》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/荚果">荚果</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/83A2.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】古協切【韻會】吉協切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音夾。【說文】草實。【博雅】豆角謂之莢。【周禮·地官·大司徒】其植物宜莢物。【註】莢物，薺莢、王棘之屬。【疏】卽今人謂之皁莢是也。</p><p>又蓂莢，堯時瑞草。</p><p>又莢蒾。【本草】葉似木槿及楡、柞小樹。</p><p>又錢名。【前漢·食貨志】漢興，以爲秦錢重難用，更令民鑄莢錢。【註】如楡莢也。</p><p>又陵名。【水經注】李夫人塚，塚形三成，世謂之莢陵。</p><p>又姓。【通志·氏族略】莢氏。【風俗通】莢成僖子，晉大夫。【王僧孺·百家志】荀永之娶平陽莢氏。</p><p>
