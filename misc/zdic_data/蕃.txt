<p>● <strong>蕃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄢˊ</span></p>

	<li>茂盛：～茂。～昌。～芜。</li>
	<li>繁多：～衍（同&ldquo;繁衍&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> foreign things</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> luxuriant,abondant,divers et varié</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蕃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蕃</strong> <span class="dicpy">bō</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &mdash;&mdash;吐蕃( <span class="dicpy">Tǔbō</span>) <span class="encs">[Tibetan regime in ancient China]</span> 我国古代少数民族</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">fān;f&aacute;n</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蕃</strong> <span class="dicpy">fān</span></p>
<p>〈名，动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;藩&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 篱落;屏障 <span class="encs">[fence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四国于蕃，四方于宣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;崧高》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫蕃篱之鶠，岂能与之料天地之高哉?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋玉</span><span class="smcs">《对楚王问》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蕃蔽(屏障);蕃篱(篱笆。引申为屏障)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 藩屏;捍卫 <span class="encs">[shield; guard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故封建亲戚以蕃屏周。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:蕃杆(藩屏;护卫);蕃辅(捍卫辅助);蕃宣(本指藩篱与垣墙。引申为藩屏护卫);蕃卫(捍卫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 封建王朝分封的侯国 <span class="encs">[state]</span>。如:蕃王(封建王朝分封的侯王;蕃岳古代对诸侯王的代称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 颊侧</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蕃者，颊侧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《灵枢经》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">张志聪</span>注:&ldquo;蕃蔽在外。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;番&rdquo; <span class="encs">[foreign]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 周代谓九州之外的夷服、镇服、蕃服。后用以泛指域外或外族。如:蕃人(我国古代对外族或异国人的泛称);蕃舶(海外入境的船舶);蕃国(周代指夷服、镇服、蕃服。建于九州以外的国家)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 轮流更替。如:蕃匠(即番匠。唐代轮班到官府工场服役的工匠);蕃变(变迁;变化)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蕃</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[luxurious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蕃，草茂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蕃衍盈升。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;椒聊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庶草蕃庑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span>。传:&ldquo;滋也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">繁启蕃长于春夏，畜积收藏于秋冬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客</span><span class="diczx2">莆田</span><span class="diczx2">徐</span>生为予三致其种，种之，生且蕃。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">徐光启</span><span class="smcs">《甘薯疏序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蕃茂(草木繁盛);蕃茏(草木繁盛);蕃华(盛开的花。比喻青春年华)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 众多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水陆草木之花，可爱者甚蕃。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:蕃多(繁多);蕃庶(众多);蕃祉(多福，福泽深厚)</p>
