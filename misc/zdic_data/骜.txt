						<p>● <strong>骜</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">驁</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>骏马。</li>
	<li>马不驯良，喻傲慢，不驯顺：～放。～忽。桀～不驯。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>骜</strong></p>
<p>驁 <span class="dicpy">&agrave;o</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从马，敖声。本义:好马，良马) 同本义 <span class="encs">[noble steed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">良马期乎千里，不期乎骥骜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span>。注:&ldquo;千里马也。王者乘之游敖，因曰骥骜也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骜放(任性傲物)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>骜</strong></p>
<p>驁 <span class="dicpy">&agrave;o</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>放纵奔驰 <span class="encs">[gallop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">服偃蹇以低昂兮，骖连蜷以骄骜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;远游》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>骜</strong></p>
<p>驁 <span class="dicpy">&agrave;o</span></p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;傲&rdquo;。骄傲 <span class="encs">[arrogant;proud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士骜爵禄者，固轻其主。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;下贤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫智伯之为人也，好利而骜愎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;十过》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">始皇刚暴而骜狠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《抱朴子&middot;极言》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
