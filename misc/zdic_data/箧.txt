						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>箧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">篋</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>箱子一类的东西：藤～。行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）～。书～。～笥（收藏东西的竹器）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rattan box, suitcase, or case</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Aktenmappe, Aktentasche  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> malle</p></div>

<p>◎ <strong>箧</strong></p>
<p>篋 <span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>小箱子，藏物之具。大曰箱，小曰箧 <span class="encs">[small suitcase]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">负箧曳屣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家书一箧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:箧衍(竹制的方形箱子;书箧;藤箧);箧笥(收藏文书或衣物的竹箱);箧扇(弃置在箧笥中的扇子。比喻失宠的妇人)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7BCB.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】苦協切【集韻】【韻會】詰叶切【正韻】乞協切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蛺。【廣韻】箱篋。【禮&middot;學記】入學鼓篋，孫其業也。【註】鼓篋，擊鼓警衆，乃發篋出所治經書也。【儀禮註】隋方曰篋。【疏】隋，謂狹而長也。【左傳&middot;昭十三年】衞人使屠伯，饋叔向羹與一篋錦。</p>
<p>又山名。【水經注】高城東北五十里有篋山。</p>
<p>又去急切。【張翰&middot;豆羹賦】是刈是穫，充簞盈篋。香鑠和調，周疾赴急。</p>
