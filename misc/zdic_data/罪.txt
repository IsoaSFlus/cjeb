                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>罪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄟˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>犯法的行为：犯～。～人。认～。有～。～恶。～名。～魁。～行。～证。～责。～孽（迷信的人认为应受到报应的罪恶）。～不容诛。</li>	<li>过失，把过失归到某人身上：功～。～愆。～尤（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">尤</span>&rdquo;，过失，罪过）。归～于人。</li>	<li>刑罚：判～。畏～。待～。请～。</li>	<li>苦难，痛苦：受～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>罪</strong></p>
<p>辠 <span class="dicpy">zu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从辛，从自，言罪人蹙鼻苦辛之忧，自亦声。本义:作恶或犯法的行为)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[crime;guilt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辠，犯法也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>以辠似皇字，改用罪。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辜辟戾辠也。经传皆以罪为之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畏此罪罟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子以赦过宥罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;解卦象下传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无功不赏，无罪不罚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">离</span><span class="diczx2">桓</span>之罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或闻无罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恶不忍闻，罪不容诛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">治臣之罪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">某知罪矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罪首(罪魁祸首);罪失(罪愆和过失);罪眚(罪愆，罪过);罪瑕(罪愆);罪祸(罪愆);罪累(罪过);罪悔(罪过);罪殃(罪过);罪辜(罪咎);罪衅(罪行;过恶);罪例(犯罪的案例)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 罪人 <span class="encs">[offender;sinner]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃惟四方之多罪，逋逃是崇是长。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;牧誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾见强弩长戟，诛罪安民，以取公侯者有矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">北齐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">颜之推</span><span class="smcs">《颜氏家训》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罪囚(罪犯);罪孥(罪犯的家属);罪徒(服刑的囚犯);罪疾(邪恶的罪人);罪脑(罪首);罪隶(古时罪人家属之男性没入官府为奴者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 过失;错误 <span class="encs">[fault;blame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王曰:&ldquo;此则寡人之罪也。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此天之亡我，非战之罪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不替</span><span class="diczx2">孟明</span>，孤之过也。大夫问罪?且吾不以一眚掩大德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罪诟(过错;诟病);罪失(罪愆和过失);罪负(过失)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 苦难;祸殃 <span class="encs">[hardship;pain;misfortune;sin;disaster]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:受罪;罪殃(祸殃);罪逆(罪孽);罪疾(灾祸);罪恼(罪孽烦恼);罪障(佛教罪孽能障碍诸善，故称&ldquo;罪障&rdquo;);罪苦(佛教语。苦难。因为罪孽而蒙受苦难)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 刑罚 <span class="encs">[penalty; punishment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">墨罪五百，劓罪五百&hellip;杀罪五百。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 捕鱼竹网 <span class="encs">[bamboo fishnet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罪，捕鱼器&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字汇补》</span></span></p>
