                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>蜡</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">蠟</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄚˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>动物、植物或矿物所产生的油质，具有可塑性，易熔化，不溶于水，可溶于二硫化碳和苯：石～。蜂～。～版。～笔。～疗。～染。～人。～纸。～烛。～黄（形容颜色黄得像蜡）。～丸。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>蜡</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄚˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>古时的祭礼，于年终大祭万物，周朝称为&ldquo;蜡&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>

<p>● <strong>蜡</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>蝇蛆。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蜡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蜡</strong></p>
<p>蠟 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，( <span class="dicpy">li&egrave;</span>)声。本义:动物、植物或矿物所产生的某些油质。如:蜂蜡)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 动物、矿物或植物所产生的油质，具有可塑性，能燃能熔，不溶于水，是一羟基醇或固醇的脂肪酸酯 <span class="encs">[wax]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蜡烛有心还惜别，替人垂泪到天明。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《赠别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蜡圆(蜡丸);蜡弹(蜡丸，用蜡封裹的密件);蜡查(蜡埚，蜡滓。白蜡(虫蜡)或黄蜡(蜂蜡)的渣滓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 蜡烛的简称 <span class="encs">[candle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蜡炬成灰泪始干。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《无题》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蜡火(蜡烛火);蜡烟(蜡烛的烟);蜡红(烛花);蜡香(蜡烛燃点时产生的气味);蜡光(烛光)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蜡</strong></p>
<p>蠟 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用蜡涂抹 <span class="encs">[wax;polish]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蜡引(涂蜡于纸张或布帛上，以防水湿);蜡本(用蜡涂绢上，所临摹的画本);蜡纸(表面涂蜡的纸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;</span></p>
