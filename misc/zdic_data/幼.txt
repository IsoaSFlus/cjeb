<span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> class="encs">[child]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">携幼入室。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扶老携幼。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幼吾幼以及人之幼，老吾老以及人之老。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:幼壮(青少年时期);幼科(中医指小儿科，也叫<span class="yhcs">“小方脉科”</span>);幼妇(少女。借指<span class="yhcs">“妙”</span>字);幼孺(孩童)</p><p>◎ <strong>幼</strong> <span class="dicpy">yòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
