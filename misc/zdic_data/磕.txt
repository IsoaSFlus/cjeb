                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">磕</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>磕</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kē.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄜˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>碰在硬东西上：头～破了。～～碰碰（喻人和人之间发生的小冲突）。～头碰脑。～头（亦作&ldquo;叩头&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>把东西向较硬处碰使附着物掉下来：～打。～烟袋锅儿。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hit; collide, knock into; sound</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anschließen, abgreifen ,schlagen, stoßen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> frapper,heurter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">磕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>磕</strong> <span class="dicpy">kē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈象〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span>声。本义:石声或鼓声) 同本义 <span class="encs">[bump]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 石头撞击声</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">磕，石声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 物相碰声</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">榜舫兮下流，东注兮磕磕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《九怀尊嘉》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:磕擦擦(形容物体磨擦碰撞声或马蹄声);磕磕(物相碰声);磕扑扑(急促的脚步声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 眯稳莸犊痴渡鄙H磕叉(磕叉叉;磕擦。砍砸斩杀声)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>磕</strong> <span class="dicpy">kē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 碰击，碰撞 <span class="encs">[knock against sth.hard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云缠风束乱敲磕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《大雨行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:磕打(捶打;比喻折磨);磕开(击开;撞开);磕撞(碰撞，撞击);磕竹(用两块瓢形的竹、木片占卜吉凶);磕牙料嘴(斗嘴，耍嘴皮子);磕绊(由说话引起的争端冲突)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;嗑&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">k&egrave;</span>)。用上下牙咬有壳的或硬的东西 <span class="encs">[crack sth. between the teeth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">黛玉</span>磕着瓜子儿，只管抿着嘴儿笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 叩头，旧时的跪拜礼 <span class="encs">[kowtow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爹!就这样跪着磕吧，别换衣服啦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹禺</span><span class="smcs">《北京人》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:磕头礼拜(叩头行礼);磕扑(双膝同时着地的声音)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 倾侧倒下 <span class="encs">[fall]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:磕伏(俯卧;趴);磕爬四五六(方言。向前跌倒，即跌个狗吃尿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;瞌&rdquo; <span class="encs">[nap]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:磕睡(睡眠，亦指倦极小睡);磕睡虫(神话中一种促人睡眠的虫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 套;戴;扣上 <span class="encs">[put on]</span>。</p>
