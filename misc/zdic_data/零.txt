                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>零</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>液体降落：感激涕～。</li>
	<li>植物凋谢：～落。凋～。～散（<span class="dicpy">s&agrave;n </span>）。</li>
	<li>整数以外的尾数：～数儿。</li>
	<li>部分的，细碎的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">整</span>&rdquo;相对：～碎。～卖。～钱。～售。～乱。～工。～打碎敲。</li>
	<li>整数系统中一个重要的数，小于一切自然数，是介于正数和负数之间唯一的数，记作&ldquo;０&rdquo;。有时用来表示某种量的基准，如摄氏温度计上的冰点，记作&ldquo;０℃&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> zero; fragment, fraction</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Null, Bruchteil, Rest, Nieselregen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> zéro,0,nul,quantité fractionnaire,se flétrir,se faner,nombre en sus d'un chiffre rond,néant,zéro degré,rien</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">零</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>零</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从雨，令声。本义:下雨。指落细雨)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">零，余雨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">零雨其濛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;东山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:零雨(徐徐飘落的雨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为如雨一般地落下;降落 <span class="encs">[fall;drop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今当远离，临表涕零。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵雨既零，命彼倌人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鄘风&middot;定之方中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阿母得闻之，零泪应声落。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔雀东南飞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">零露溥兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;野有蔓草》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柯叶汇而而零茂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;班固&middot;幽通赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涕零如雨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:零泪(落泪);零露(自天空陨落露珠);感激涕零</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 凋零，凋落 <span class="encs">[wither]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悼芳草之先零。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;远游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟草木之零落兮，恐差人之迟暮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岁暮百草零，疾风高冈裂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《自京赴奉先县咏怀五百字》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:零替(散落，衰败);零茂(凋落和茂盛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 比喻人死亡或到暮年 <span class="encs">[die;end]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">海内知识，零落殆尽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孔融</span><span class="smcs">《论盛孝章书》</span></p>
