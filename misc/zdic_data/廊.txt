						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>廊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">láng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/láng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>房屋前檐伸出的部分，可避风雨，遮太阳：～子。前～后厦。</li><li>庑下，殿下外屋：～庑（堂前廊屋）。</li><li>有顶的过道：长～。走～。画～。游～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>廊</strong> <span class="dicpy">láng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/láng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，郎声。从<span class="yhcs">“广”</span>，表示与房屋有关。本义:厅堂周围的屋)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rooms around hall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平公恐惧，优于廊室之间。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:廊房(廊室。殿堂周围的房舍);廊屋(廊室);廊庑(堂前的廊屋;屋檐下的过道或独立有顶的通道)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 走廊 <span class="encs">[veranda]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高柳早莺啼，长廊春雨响。——</span><span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《谒瑀上人》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入门穿廊，过前后厅。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:回廊(曲折环绕的走廊);游廊(连接两个或几个独立建筑物的走廊);廊牙(廊檐下像牙齿般的装饰);廊厩(围廊旁的房舍)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 朝廷、国家 <span class="encs">[court]</span>。如:廊肆(即廊庙。指朝廷);廊庙志(担负朝廷重任的志向。指参政的心愿);廊庙才(比喻能担负国家重任者)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/廊道">廊道</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/廊庙">廊庙</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/廊檐">廊檐</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/廊腰缦回">廊腰缦回</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
