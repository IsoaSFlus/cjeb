                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>衍</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>延长，开展：～绎。～生。推～。展～。敷～。～生物。</li>
	<li>多余的（指文字）：～文（书籍中因缮写、刻板、排版错误而多出来的字句）。</li>
	<li>低而平坦之地：～沃（土地平坦肥美。亦作&ldquo;沃衍&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>衍</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从水，从行。本义:水流入海)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 水循河道流汇于海 <span class="encs">[flow into sea]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衍，水朝宗于海貌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至今衍于四海。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书大传》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹溢也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水直衍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;法》</span>。注:&ldquo;达也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 溢出，水满而出 <span class="encs">[overflow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东注太湖，衍溢陂池。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在中也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;需》</span>。虞注:&ldquo;流也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:衍溢(水满溢);衍涝(谓雨水满溢)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 散开 <span class="encs">[disperse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衍溢漂疾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 分布 <span class="encs">[be distributed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千乘方毂，万骑骈罗，衍陈于岐、梁，东横乎大河。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;演&rdquo;。推演;演述 <span class="encs">[deduce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大衍之数五十。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:衍说(讲说);衍声(谓文字从声音上演变);衍形(谓文字从形体上演变);衍算(推算数字);衍忒(推算过失)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 扩展，扩充 <span class="encs">[expand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">丘明</span>衍传， <span class="diczx2">迁</span>、 <span class="diczx2">固</span>承流，史书始粲然大备。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">章炳麟</span>语</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:衍文义书(借题发挥的文章);衍功(谓广建功业);衍食(扩大食邑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 动植物孳生繁茂 <span class="encs">[lush]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荐草多衍，则六畜易繁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 通&ldquo;延&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n</span>)绵延 <span class="encs">[be continuous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二曰衍祭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;春官&middot;大祝》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;衍，当为延。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无迁无衍，命乃长久。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;白心》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:衍曼(绵延不绝貌);衍嗣(绵延，继续);衍庆(绵延吉庆)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>衍</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 以盛、多、过多、多余为特点的 <span class="encs">[redundant;superfluous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国富人衍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜笃</span><span class="smcs">《论都赋》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/884D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>以淺切，音演。水溢也。【說文】水朝宗于海也。</p>
<p>又【小爾雅】澤之廣者謂之衍。</p>
<p>又美也。【詩&middot;小雅】釃酒有衍。</p>
<p>又布也。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】離靡廣衍。</p>
<p>又游衍。自恣之意。【詩&middot;大雅】昊天曰旦，及爾游衍。</p>
<p>又衍沃，平美之地。【左傳&middot;襄二十五年】井衍沃。</p>
<p>又曼衍，無極也。【莊子&middot;齊物論】和之以天倪，因之以曼衍。</p>
<p>又沙衍，水中有沙者。【穆天子傳】天子乃遂東征，南絕沙衍。</p>
<p>又篋衍，笥也。【莊子&middot;天運篇】芻狗之未陳也，盛以篋衍，巾以文繡。</p>
<p>又【博雅】衍衍，行也。【謝朓詩】衍衍淸風爛。</p>
<p>又水名。【史記&middot;荆軻傳】丹匿衍水中。【索隱曰】在遼東。</p>
<p>又朐衍，地名。【前漢&middot;地理志】屬北地郡。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】衍，子姓，宋微仲衍之後。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】于線切，延去聲。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】夷然切，音延。進也。【周禮&middot;春官】望祀望衍。【鄭註】讀爲延。【疏】衍，衍祭也。【劉歆&middot;甘泉賦】高巒峻阻，臨眺曠衍。叶泉字韻。　【直音】或作衍。衍字从彡，不从氵作。</p>
