<p>● <strong>要</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>索取：～账。～价。</li>
	<li>希望，想：～强。～好。</li>
	<li>请求：她～我给她读报。</li>
	<li>重大，值得重视的：重～。～人。～领。纲～。～言不烦。</li>
	<li>应该，必须：须～。</li>
	<li>将（<span class="dicpy">jiāng</span>）：将～。快～。</li>
	<li>如果，倘若：～是。</li>
	<li>表选择：～么。～不。～不然。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">要</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>要</strong> <span class="dicpy">yāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形。中间象人形，两旁为两手形。表示两手叉腰。本义:人腰)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &ldquo;腰&rdquo;的古字 <span class="encs">[waist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者，</span><span class="diczx2">楚灵王</span>好细要。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">户服艾以盈要兮，谓幽兰其不可佩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故量食而食之，量要而带之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:要膂(腰和脊骨。比喻重要部位);要章(腰间所佩的印);要支(腰肢)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 五服之一。古代指离都城一千五百里至二千里的地区 <span class="encs">[suburb]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:要服(古代王畿以外按距离分为五服。相传一千五百里至二千里为要服);要荒(要服和荒服。古称王畿外极远之地为荒服，泛指远方之国);要蛮(极偏远的蛮荒之地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>要</strong> <span class="dicpy">yāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 约请;邀请 <span class="encs">[invite]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">便要还家，设酒杀鸡作食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">张良</span>出，要 <span class="diczx2">项伯</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">石崇</span>每要客燕集。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;汰侈门》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:要引(邀迎荐举);要约(邀约;邀请)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 约言。以明誓的方式就某事作出庄严的承诺或表示某种决心。亦指所订立的誓约、盟约 <span class="encs">[ally;promise;pledge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂要黄河誓，须勒燕然石。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔湜</span><span class="smcs">《寒垣行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:要约(立盟;立约;约定);要契(契约;盟约)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;徼&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span>)。探求;求取 <span class="encs">[seek;pursue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不庶几，不要幸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋&middot;内篇问下》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孙星衍</span>云:&ldquo;要与徼通。&rdquo;</p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;邀&rdquo;。拦阻;截击 <span class="encs">[intercept]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 迎候;迎接 <span class="encs">[meet]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:要途(迎于中途);要候(中途等候;迎候);要道(遮道;迎候于路上)</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>要</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 要点，纲要 <span class="encs">[important point]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事在四方，要在中央。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故其治国也，察要而已矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纪要(记录要点的文字);扼要(抓住要点);要谛(要义);要窍(要诀;关键的办法);要归(重点所在;要旨);要本(要旨;根本);要端(要点);要令(主旨;要旨);要义(要旨);要机(犹要旨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 计数的簿书 <span class="encs">[book]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大役与虑，事属其植。受其要，以待考而赏诛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;要者，簿书也。考，谓考较其功。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:要会(会计;簿书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 权柄 <span class="encs">[power]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事在四方，要在中央，圣人执要，四方来效。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:要贵(犹显贵，权贵);要势(谓权贵的势力。常指居要位有权势者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指重要的地位或职务 <span class="encs">[important position]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以吾二人久居内要，常有不善之言，恐祸及人，深宜虑之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:要道(比喻职位要且有权有势的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 要子 <span class="encs">[straw rope;cord]</span>。用麦秆、稻草等临时拧成的绳状物，用来捆麦子、稻子等;捆货物用的或打包用的条状物。如:打要子</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 总要 <span class="encs">[scheme]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">要以不能免。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">要离</span>之刺 <span class="diczx2">庆忌</span>也，仓鹰击于殿上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">重大的、重要的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「要港」、「要人」、「要職」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">切當的。參見「<a href='/hans/要道' target=_blank>要道</a> 」條。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">概括、總括。用於文言文。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「要言之，為人應以誠字居心。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷一八&middot;高祖功臣侯者年表》：「帝王者各殊禮而異務，要以成功為統紀，豈可緄乎？」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">應該。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「你要知道，這是不容易的事。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">即將。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「我要出門了。」、「天要下雨了。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示提醒或命令人做某事。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「不論環境如何困厄，我們都要腳踏實地，好好做人。」、「別忘了要告訴他，要仔細檢查，別再出錯。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示想、希望做某事。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈竹逕〉詩：「若要添風月，應除數百竿。」</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;無名氏《白兔記&middot;第一二齣》：「要知心腹事，但聽口中言。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">連</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">如果。表示假設的關係。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「明天要是下雨，我就不去了。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">要麼的簡稱。表示選擇的關係。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「這件事要就是不做，要就是一口氣將它完成。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8981.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21897.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡢗</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2697C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥼</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20F85.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠾅</span>【唐韻】於霄切【集韻】【韻會】伊消切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音邀。【博雅】約也。【論語】久要不忘平生之言。【註】久要，舊約也。【左傳&middot;哀十四年】使季路要我，吾無盟矣。</p>
<p>又求也。【孟子】脩其天爵，以要人爵。</p>
<p>又䙅也。【詩&middot;魏風】要之襋之，好人服之。</p>
<p>又會也。【禮&middot;樂記】要其節奏。【註】要，猶會也。【釋文】要，一遙反。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】勒也。</p>
<p>又劫也。【前漢&middot;文帝紀】上自欲征匈奴，羣臣諫不聽，皇太后固要，上乃止。</p>
<p>又劾也，察也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;鄕士】異其死<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>之罪而要之。【疏】要，劾實也。【書&middot;康誥要囚傳】要察囚情，得其辭以斷獄。</p>
<p>又衞圻之外，謂之要服。【書&middot;禹貢】五百里要服。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】吳人要離之後，漢有河南令要兢，唐建中朔方大將軍要珍。</p>
<p>又水名。【水經注】濡水，又東南流與要水合。</p>
<p>又靑要，山名。【山海經】靑要之山，寔惟帝之密都。</p>
<p>又高要，縣名。【一統志】屬廣州府。</p>
<p>又與腰通。【說文】身中也，象人要自<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26951.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥑</span>之形。今作腰。別詳肉部。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】於笑切。讀去聲。【篇海】凡要也，要會也。【孝經】先王有至德要道。【晉書&middot;宣帝紀】軍事大要有五。</p>
<p>又《論語》久要，亦讀去聲。【王安石&middot;老人行】古來人事已如此，今日何須論久要。叶入誚韻。</p>
<p>又【集韻】伊鳥切，音杳。與騕同。騕褭，良馬名。或作要。</p>
<p>又以紹切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24577.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤕷</span>。與偠同。偠紹或作要紹。舒緩貌也。</p>
