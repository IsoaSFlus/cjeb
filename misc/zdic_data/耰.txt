                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>耰</strong> <span class="dicpy">yōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代的一种农具，弄碎土块，平整田地用 <span class="encs">[rake]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民以耰鉏櫄梃相挞击，犯法滋蟓，盗贼不胜。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“耰，摩田之器也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:耰钼(用来平田松土的农具)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 锄把 <span class="encs">[hoe's handle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鉏耰棘矜，不敌于钩戟长鎩。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注引 <span class="diczx2">服虔</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“耰，鉏柄也。”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>耰</strong> <span class="dicpy">yōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用耰松土并使土块细碎。亦指覆种 <span class="encs">[plough with rake]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">播种而耰之。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:耰锄(犹耡耰)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指耕种 <span class="encs">[tiller]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使农不辍耰，工不下机。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《长杨赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:耰耦(泛指从事耕种的人)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8030.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】於求切【正韻】於尤切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音憂。覆種也。【說文徐註】摩田器，布種後以此器摩之，使土開發處復合覆種也。【論語】耰而不輟。【註】耰，覆種也。【莊子·則陽篇】深其耕而熟耰之，其禾繁以滋。【註】耰，鋤也。【淮南子·氾論訓】民勞而利薄，後世爲之耒耜耰鋤。【註】耰，椓塊椎也。三輔謂之儓，所以覆種也。</p><p>又【韻補】叶於救切，音又【史記·龜筴傳】耕之耰之，鉏之耨之。</p><p>又叶爾皎切，音擾。【梁武帝·籍田詩】公卿秉耒耜，庶甿荷鉏耰。一人慙百王，三推先億兆。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6ACC.svg">                          <p>摩田器。从木憂聲。《論語》曰：“櫌而不輟。”於求切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6ACC_3027.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(櫌)</span><span class="ywyy">摩田器也。</span><span class="swdz">漢石經論語。櫌不輟。五經文字曰。經典及釋文皆作耰。鄭曰。耰、覆種也。與許合。許以物言。鄭以人用物言。齊語。深耕而疾耰之。以待時雨。韋曰。耰、摩平也。齊民要術曰。耕荒畢。以鐵齒<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28AD2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨫒</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28A2F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨨯</span>再徧杷之。漫擲黍穄。勞亦再徧。按先云再徧杷之。卽國語所謂疾耰待時雨也。後云勞亦再徧。卽鄭所謂覆種也。許云摩田。當兼此二者。賈又曰。春耕尋手勞。秋耕待白背勞。古曰耰。今曰勞。勞郎到切。集韵作橯。若高誘云。櫌、椎也。如淳云。椎、塊椎也。服䖍、孟康云。櫌、鉏柄也。椎塊尚近之。鉏柄之說未可信矣。</span><span class="ywyy">從木。憂聲。</span><span class="swdz">於求切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">論語曰。櫌而不輟。</span><span class="swdz">微子篇。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
