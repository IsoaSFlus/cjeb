                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>出</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">⓬齣</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>从里面到外面：～访。初～茅庐。～笼。</li>	<li>往外拿，支付：～力。～钱。～谋画策。入不敷～。</li>	<li>离开：～发。～轨。～嫁。</li>	<li>产生，生长：～产。～品。～人才。</li>	<li>发生：～事。</li>	<li>显露：～现。～名。</li>	<li>超过：～色。～类拔萃（超出同类之上）。</li>	<li>来到：～席。～勤。</li>	<li>引文、典故来源于某处：～处（<span class="dicpy">ch&ugrave;</span>）。语～《孟子》。</li>	<li>显得量多：这米～饭。</li>	<li>放在动词后，表示趋向或效果：提～问题。</li>	<li>传（<span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;n</span>）奇中的一回，戏曲的一个独立剧目。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">出</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>出</strong> <span class="dicpy">chū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。象草木益滋上出达之形。本义:长出)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grow up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出，进也。象草木益滋，上出达也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>讲的&ldquo;出&rdquo;，其实是茁。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">句者毕出，萌者尽达。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令&middot;季春》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万物出乎震。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为出入的&ldquo;出&rdquo;。出去，出来，与&ldquo;入&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[go out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸侯出庙门俟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;顾命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出自北门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;北门》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我出我车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;出车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出车彭彭。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群众既皆承命，相揖趋出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;康王之诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我以日始出时去人近。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">月出惊山鸟，时鸣春涧中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《鸟鸣涧》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:出关;出塞;出城;出东门</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 出现;显露 <span class="encs">[appear;show]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出自东方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;日月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">月出之光。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;鸡鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风霜高洁，水落而石出。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:出化(显现);出落(透露，显示;弄得，只落得);出日(日出之处);出出(连续出现的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 生产;产生 <span class="encs">[produce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殚其地之出。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:出产(生产)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 高出;超出 <span class="encs">[exceed;go beyond;surpass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古之圣人，其出人也远矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:出人(超越别人);出服(出了五服。指出了规定的服丧范围);出尖(拔尖;强出头);出景(突出);出月(出了本月;下个月)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 使出;拿出;取出 <span class="encs">[bring out;take out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余人各复延至其家，皆出酒食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贫者出力</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:出费(拿出费用);出分子(拿出若干钱来送礼);出捐(拿出)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 发出，发布 <span class="encs">[issue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出言有章。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;都人士》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:出案(张榜);出布告</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 出版;张贴出 <span class="encs">[publish;put up]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出书;出期刊</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 出仕 <span class="encs">[become an official]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出充(出任);出佐(出任地方辅佐之官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 逃亡 <span class="encs">[escape]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能改而出圜土者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司寇》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓逃亡也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:出彀(逃出樊笼);出甲(指逃兵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 出嫁 <span class="encs">[marry]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出入(女子嫁人为出，未嫁为入);出门子(出嫁);出室(出嫁);出女(已嫁之女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 发泄;发散 <span class="encs">[give vent to;diffuse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如: 出火(发泄欲火);出宝(赌博的一种)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 出生;生育 <span class="encs">[bear]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出胎(降生);出腹(出生);出幼(发育长大成人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 驱逐 <span class="encs">[drive out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出夫(被逐出的赘婿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 遗弃;休弃 <span class="encs">[divorce]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出母(被父休弃的生母);出妻(休弃妻子);出妇(被丈夫休弃的妇女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 清除;清扫 <span class="encs">[clear away]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出粪(清除粪秽以肥田)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(18)</span> 舍弃;除去;去掉 <span class="encs">[throw away;abandon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出命(献出生命);出洁(清除)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(19)</span> 脱离;释放;开脱 <span class="encs">[separate oneself from;release]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出山(指经 过深山修行后重返人世;比喻出来干事);出籍(脱离所属的名籍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(20)</span> 卖出 <span class="encs">[sell]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出笏(卖掉，出手);出著(出着。出手)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(21)</span> 指军队出动 <span class="encs">[set out;turn out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出卒(出兵，派出军队);出陈(出阵。出兵列阵应战);出旅(出兵，出军)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(22)</span> 杀，处决 <span class="encs">[kill]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出人(杀人;处决犯人);出公事(处决犯人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(23)</span> 开脱罪名 <span class="encs">[absolve sb.from guilt or blame]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出脱(从轻发落或免予处理);出活(开脱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(24)</span> 经过，穿过 <span class="encs">[pass]</span>。 如:出边(渡越边境)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(25)</span> 发生 <span class="encs">[happen]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:出事(发生事故);出岔(发生意外，出差错)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(26)</span> 用在动词后表示向外，显露或完成。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:想出;看出;打出;跑出;走出;冒出</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>出</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄔㄨ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>chū <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4801.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">自內至外。與「入」相對。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「足不出戶」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;鄭風&middot;出其東門》：「出其東門，有女如雲。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">產生、發生。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「出汗」、「出水痘」、「<b>出</b>紕漏」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;說卦》：「萬物<b>出</b>乎震，震東方也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">脫離、離開。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「出家」、「出軌」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;小雅&middot;賓之初筵》：「既醉而<b>出</b>，並受其福；醉而不<b>出</b>，是謂伐德。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">給予、支付。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「出錢」、「出納」、「支<b>出</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">做官、出任其事。</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;繫辭上》：「君子之道，或<b>出</b>或處。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《宋書&middot;卷六七&middot;謝靈運傳》：「<b>出</b>為永嘉太守。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表現、顯露。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「水落石<b>出</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《莊子&middot;天地》：「至言不<b>出</b>，俗言勝也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">發洩。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>出</b>怨氣」、「<b>出</b>悶氣」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">超越。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「出眾」、「出人頭地」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈師說〉：「古之聖人，其出人也遠矣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=凵" target="_blank">凵</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/51FA.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】赤律切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】尺律切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>春入聲。【說文】進也。【廣韻】見也，遠也。【增韻】出入也，吐也，寫也。</p><p>又生也。【爾雅&middot;釋訓】男子謂姊妹之子爲出。【左傳&middot;成十三年】康公，我之自出。【註】秦康公，晉之甥也。</p><p>又【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;大司<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨥</span>】其不能改而出圜土者殺。【註】出謂越獄逃亡也。</p><p>又【增韻】斥也。【正韻】亦作黜絀。</p><p>又【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】尺類切【正韻】蚩瑞切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>推去聲。自中而外也。</p><p>又【正韻】凡物自出，則入聲。非自出而出之，則去聲。然亦有互用者。</p><p>又叶尺僞切，吹去聲。【詩&middot;小雅】匪舌是出，維躬是瘁。</p><p>又叶敕律切，音黜。【馬融&middot;圍棋賦】熒惑窘乏兮無令詐出，深念遠慮兮勝乃可必。</p><p>又【韻補】叶側劣切，音茁。【曹植&middot;卞后誄】詳惟聖善，岐嶷秀出。德配姜嫄，不忝先哲。</p><p>又叶赤知切，音侈。【穆天子傳&middot;西王母謠】白雲在天，丘陵自出。道里悠遠，山川閒之。</p><p>又叶赤至切，音熾。【楚辭&middot;九章】竊快在其中心兮，揚厥憑而不竢。芳與澤其雜糅兮，羌芳華自中出。【靈樞經】男內女外，堅拒勿出。謹守勿內，是謂得氣。</p>
