                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纷</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>纷</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3"><img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">紛</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>众多，杂乱：～～。～繁。～杂。～飞。～纭。五彩缤～。～乱。～扰。～争。</li>
	<li>古代旗子上的飘带。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> in disorder, scattered, tangled</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> multiple,nombreux</p></div>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纷</strong></p>
<p>紛 <span class="dicpy">fēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，分声。从&ldquo;糸&rdquo;，表示与线丝等有关。本义:马尾韬，扎束马尾的丝麻织物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[horsetail bag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纷，马尾韬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;韬，剑衣也。引申凡为衣之称。<span class="smcs">《释名》</span>曰:&lsquo;纷，放也，防其放弛以拘之也。&rsquo;&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 旗上的飘带 <span class="encs">[ribbon on a flag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">青云为纷，虹霓为缳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《羽猎赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:佩纷(佩饰丝带)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 花边 <span class="encs">[lace]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">次席纷纯，左右玉几。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 祸乱;灾难 <span class="encs">[disaster]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唯天轨之不辟兮，何纯絜而离纷!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 争执;纠纷 <span class="encs">[dispute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谈言微中，亦可以解纷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纷纠(纠纷;纷扰;祸乱);纷哓(争辩不休)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 抹布;拭物的佩巾 <span class="encs">[rag]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:纷帨(用来拭物的佩巾)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>纷</strong></p>
<p>紛 <span class="dicpy">fēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 盛多，各种各样 <span class="encs">[many and various]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且矫诏纷出，钩党之捕遍于天下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是众枪齐发，敌人纷堕如落叶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 艚又如:纷委(盛多);纷若(盛多的样子);纷营(众多，杂乱);纷郁(盛多的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 杂乱 <span class="encs">[confused;disorderly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">霰雪纷其无垠兮，云霏霏其承宇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">班其相纷些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涫涫纷纷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;解蔽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狱之放纷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顾自民国肇造，变乱纷乘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《&lt;黄花冈七十二烈士事略&gt; 序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纷腾(杂乱喧腾);纷嚣(纷乱喧嚣);纷错(杂乱的样子);纷纶(乱而多。忙乱);纷罗(排列混乱无序的样子。或指众多并列)</span></p>
