                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">炮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>炮</strong> <span class="dicpy">bāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一种烹调方法 <span class="encs">[quick-fry]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用锅等在旺火上急炒 <span class="encs">[牛肉片、羊肉片等]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炮羊肉</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 中药制法之一。把药物放在高温铁锅里急炒，使其焦黄爆裂。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炮姜</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 把物品放在器物上烘烤或焙 <span class="encs">[dry by heat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:湿衣服搁在热炕上，一会儿就炮干了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">p&aacute;o;p&agrave;o</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>炮</strong></p>
