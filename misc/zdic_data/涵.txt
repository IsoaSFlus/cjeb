                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>涵</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄢˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>包容，包含：～蓄。～容。～养（ａ．蓄积并保持，如&ldquo;～～水源&rdquo;；ｂ．指修养，如&ldquo;他很有～～&rdquo;）。包～。蕴～。海～。</li>
	<li>沉，潜：～泳（水中潜行，喻深入体会）。～淹。～濡（浸渍，滋润）。</li>
	<li>公路或铁路下面通沟渠的管道：～洞。桥～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涵</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，圅声。本义:水泽众多) 同本义 <span class="encs">[abundance of lakes and ponds]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涵，水泽多也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涵泳乎其中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涵，沉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>十</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:涵碧(形容水色碧绿深沉)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>涵</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 浸润;滋润 <span class="encs">[soak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">僭始既涵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;巧言》</span>。按，谓浸润渐渍也。传训容，谓借为含</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:涵煦(滋润覆照);涵濡(滋润，浸渍);涵浸(浸渍，滋润);涵畅(滋润化育，使之发扬);涵润(滋润)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 包含，包容 <span class="encs">[contain]</span>。如:涵容(包容;包涵);涵沈(含藏);涵映(包含映照)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 包涵，宽容 <span class="encs">[forgive]</span>。如:涵忍(包涵容忍);涵谅(包涵原谅);涵容(包涵;宽容)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 沉浸 <span class="encs">[immerse]</span>。如:涵泳(在水中潜行。即游泳，沉浸，薰陶);涵浸(浸渍，滋润)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>涵</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>涵洞 <span class="encs">[culvert]</span>。如:桥涵</p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">水泽多。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「涵泽」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字&middot;水部》：「涵，水泽多也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">浸润、湿润。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字&middot;水部》「涵」字&middot;清&middot;段玉裁&middot;注：「涵，所受润泽多也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;王安石〈送江宁彭给事赴阙〉诗：「威加诸部风霜肃，惠浸连营雨露涵。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">包容、容纳。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「包涵」、「海涵」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;孟浩然〈望洞庭湖赠张丞相〉诗：「八月湖水平，涵虚混太清。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜牧〈九日齐安登高〉诗：「江涵秋影雁初飞，与客携壶上翠微。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">沉潜、潜水。参见「涵淹」、「涵泳」等条。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">涵洞的简称。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「桥涵」。</span></p></li>
