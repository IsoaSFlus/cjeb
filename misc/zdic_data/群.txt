                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">群</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>群</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&uacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>相聚成伙的，聚集在一起的：～岛。～山。～书。～芳。～居。～落（<span class="dicpy">lu&ograve;</span>）。～集。～雕。～蚁附膻（许多蚂蚁附着在有膻味的东西上；喻臭味相投的人趋炎附势，追逐私利）。</li>
	<li>众人：～众。～情。～雄。～策～力。～威～胆。</li>
	<li>量词，用于成群的人或物：一～孩子。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> (same as U+7FA3 羣) group, crowd, multitude, mob</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gruppe, Herde, Menge, Schar, Schwarm (S)​,gruppiert (Adj)​,ZEW für Gruppen (Mensch, Tier)​ (Zähl)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> groupe,troupeau,essaim</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">群</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>群</strong></p>
<p>羣 <span class="dicpy">q&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从羊，君声。本义:羊群;兽群;人群)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 三个以上的禽兽相聚而成的集体 <span class="encs">[crowd;group]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群，辈也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兽三为群。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或群或友。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;吉日》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三百维群。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;无羊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大夫不掩群。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群疑满服，众难塞胸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《后出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故近者聚而为群。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《封建论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:群才(有才能的人们);群生(一切生物);群立(站立在人群中);群有(佛教指众生或万物);群儿(一群小儿。多用作轻蔑之辞);群品(万事万物;佛教指众生)。也指其他动物相聚而成的集体。又如:猪群;马群;大鱼群</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 朋辈 <span class="encs">[friends]</span>。如:群好(互相亲善的人们);群季(诸弟);群朋(互相依附，结为党与);群流(同辈);群萃(同类;侪辈)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 百姓 <span class="encs">[common people]</span>。如:群元(指人民、百姓);群口铄金(众口铄金。形容舆论影响的强大或比喻人多口杂，足以混淆是非)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 集团，社会集体 <span class="encs">[group]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又有大者，众群之长又就而听命焉，以安其属。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《封建论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:群法(社会法则);群治(对各种社会问题的治理和处置);群俗(社会风尚)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 泛指多数 <span class="encs">[majority]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 指人</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王为群姓立社，曰大社。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 指事物</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猎者张罗，百兽群拢，或得或失。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>群</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用于聚集在一起的人或物 <span class="encs">[herd;group;flock]</span>。如:一群天鹅;一群野火鸡;一群白蚁</p>
<p>◎ <strong>群</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 聚集;会合，联合 <span class="encs">[assemble;gather together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而群天下之英杰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;非十二子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子矜而不争，群而不党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以避群害。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲清偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群聚而笑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群怪聚骂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:群而不党(聚集在一起，却不结党);群曲(合唱的曲子。大多为一人引端，众人和声);群行(结队而行);群萃(事业相同的人集聚在一起);群处(成群地相处)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 随俗 <span class="encs">[comply with covention]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仆进不能参名于二立，退又不能群彼数子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>

<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 众多 <span class="encs">[in crowds;in flocks;in groups]</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">相聚一起的人或物。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「鳥<b>群</b>」、「三五成<b>群</b>」、「鶴立雞<b>群</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;檀弓上》：「吾離<b>群</b>而索居，亦已久矣。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈與元九書〉：「<b>群</b>分而氣同，形異而情一。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞。計算群聚的人或物的單位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「一<b>群</b>牛」、「一<b>群</b>小孩」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第二七回》：「只見寶釵出來了，寶玉、襲人一<b>群</b>人都送了出來。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">聚集、聚合。</span><span class="gc_yy">《國語&middot;齊語》：「令夫士，<b>群</b>萃而州處。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;勸學》：「草木疇生，禽獸<b>群</b>焉，物各從其類也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">眾多。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「群英」、「群經諸子」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;中庸》：「體<b>群</b>臣也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;王勃〈滕王閣序〉：「登高作賦，是所望於<b>群</b>公。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">成群的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「群島」、「<b>群</b>峰」、「群居」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
