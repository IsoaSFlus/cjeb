                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">穷</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>穷</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">窮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄩㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>缺乏财物：贫～。～苦。～则思变。</li>
	<li>处境恶劣：～困。～蹙。～窘。～当益坚（处境越穷困，意志应当越坚定）。～而后工（旧时指文人处境穷困，诗就写得好）。</li>
	<li>达到极点：～目。～形尽相。～兵黩武。</li>
	<li>完了：～尽。山～水尽。日暮途～。</li>
	<li>推究到极点：～物之理。～追（➊极力追寻；➋尽力紧追）。～究。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> poor, destitute, impoverished</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> arm, unvermögend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pauvre,fin,limite,extrêmement</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">穷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>穷</strong></p>
<p>窮 <span class="dicpy">qi&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，躬声。躬，身体，身在穴下，很窘困。简化字为会意，力在穴下，有劲使不出。本义:穷尽，完结)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[end;limit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷，极也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷，竟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">横四海兮焉穷?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九歌&middot;云中君》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤水穷焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;大荒南经》</span>。注:&ldquo;流极于此山也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与物变化而无所终穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;下贤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷高极远，而测深厚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儒有博学而不穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">图穷而匕首见。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲穷其林。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲穷千里目，更上一层楼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王之涣</span><span class="smcs">《登鹳雀楼》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">接天莲叶无穷碧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杨万里</span><span class="smcs">《晓出净慈寺送林子方》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乐亦无穷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">理屈词穷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:穷形尽致(比喻说话不留余地，毫无保留);穷师远讨(穷竭兵力进行远征);穷兵屯戍(尽全部兵力驻守边疆);无穷(没有穷尽;没有限度);穷了(穷尽，终止);穷生(尽其生年，终生)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 贫穷，缺乏衣食钱财 <span class="encs">[poor;poverty-stricken]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷，贫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分贫，振穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至无有者穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷饿无聊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:穷酸饿醋(穷酸);穷腮乞脸(穷酸相);穷拉拉(形容很穷);穷滴滴(很穷);穷薄(贫穷;穷困);穷暴(贫穷之极);贫穷(生产资料和生活资料缺乏);穷通显晦(困窘、亨通、显赫、背晦);穷迫(穷困窘迫);穷厮(对贫穷男子的贱称);穷丁(穷汉。贫穷男子);穷鬼;穷日子;穷达(困穷与显达)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 边远的 <span class="encs">[out-of-the-way]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:穷城(历尽边城);穷边(荒僻的边远地区);穷泽(僻远的水乡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 特指不得志 <span class="encs">[not have a successful career]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">固将愁苦而终穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人穷则反本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:穷人</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 小;浅 <span class="encs">[small;shallow]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:穷径(小路);穷流(干涸的河流);穷枝(小枝;末枝);穷波(浅水);穷井(枯井)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[great;big]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:穷怒(盛怒);穷溟(传说中的大海);穷观(大观)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 高 <span class="encs">[high]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:穷龄(高龄);穷岫(高山);穷高(到达高处);穷陸(高地);穷崖绝谷(高山深谷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 破旧;破烂 <span class="encs">[worn-out]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:穷破(陈旧残破)</span></p>
