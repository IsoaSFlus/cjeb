						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蛟</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>古代传说中一种能发洪水的龙：～龙得水。</li>    <li>指鼍、鳄之类的动物。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> scaly dragon with four legs</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Haifisch  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> crocodile</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蛟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蛟</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，交声。本义:古代传说中能发水的一种龙)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mythical flood dragon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蛟，龙之属也。池鱼，满三千六百，蛟来为之长，能率鱼飞置笱水中，即蛟去。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其中有虎蛟。注:<span class="yhcs">“蛟似蛇四足，龙属。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">母龙曰蛟。——<span class="smcs">《抱朴子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">义兴</span>水中有蛟。(蛟，这里指鼍，鳄一类的动物。)——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·自新》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蛟虬(蛟与虬。虬:古代传说中一种有角的小龙。亦泛指水族);蛟螭(蛟龙。螭:传说为蛟龙之属的一种动物);蛟兕(蛟龙与兕牛)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又有古书说是无角龙 <span class="encs">[hornless dragon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蛟，龙属。无角曰蛟。——<span class="smcs">《韵会》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘六蛟兮蜿蝉。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·守志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“龙无角曰蛟。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又指小龙或母龙 <span class="encs">[young or male dragon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">麾蛟龙使梁津兮，诏西皇使涉予。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王逸</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“小曰蛟，大曰龙。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“鲛”</span>。鲨鱼 <span class="encs">[shark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胁蛟犀兕，坚若金石。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·弱民》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自寻阳浮江，亲射蛟江中。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·武帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:蛟革(鲨鱼皮);蛟胎(用鲨鱼皮制成的剑鞘);蛟鱼(传说中的人鱼)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“鲛”</span>。蛟人。传说居于海底的人 <span class="encs">[person resem bling tlood dragon]</span>。如:蛟珠(传说蛟人所泣之珠);蛟绡(传说蛟人所织的丝织品。泛指绢帛)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                     <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>蛟</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧㄠ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jiāo <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3191.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">传说中能发洪水，像龙一类的动物。</span><span class="gc_yy">汉·王充《论衡·龙虚》：「<b>蛟</b>则龙之类也，蛟龙见而云雨至，云雨至则雷电击。」</span><span class="gc_yx">也称为「蛟龙」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/86DF.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古肴切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居肴切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音交。【說文】龍之屬也，池魚三千六百，蛟來爲之長，能率魚飛，置笱水中卽去。【埤雅】蛟，其狀似蛇而四足，細頸，頸有白嬰，大者數圍，卵生，眉交，故謂之蛟。【山海經】蛟大者十數圍，卵如一二石甕，能吞人。【述異記】虎魚老者爲蛟。【酉陽雜俎】魚二千斤爲蛟。【禮·月令】季夏，命漁師伐蛟。【前漢·武帝紀】自尋陽浮江，親射蛟江中，獲之。</p><p>又蛟羊。【述異記】蛟羊似羊而無角。</p>
