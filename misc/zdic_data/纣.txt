                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纣</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>纣</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">紂</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄡˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>商代最后一个君主的谥号。一作&ldquo;受&rdquo;，亦称&ldquo;帝辛&rdquo;。《史記&bull;殷本紀》：&ldquo;帝乙崩，子辛立，是為帝辛，天下謂之紂。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>马緧，即驾车马后部的革带。《方言》卷九：&ldquo;車紂，自關而東，周、洛、韓、汝、潁而東謂之䋺，或謂之曲綯，或謂之曲綸；自關而西謂之紂。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/牖">牖</a>（yŏu）&rdquo;。窗。清朱珔《説文叚借義證&bull;糸部》：&ldquo;紂，可為牖之假借，紂、牖叠韻字。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> name of an emperor; saddle part</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Di Xin,sangle de selle de cheval</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纣</strong></p>
<p>紂 <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 驾车马后部的革带 <span class="encs">[back leather belt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纣，马緧也。从糸，肘省声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">车纣，自</span><span class="diczx2">关</span>而东， <span class="diczx2">周</span>、 <span class="diczx2">洛</span>、 <span class="diczx2">韩</span>、 <span class="diczx2">汝</span>、 <span class="diczx2">颍</span>而东谓之鞧，或谓之曲绹，或谓之曲纶;自 <span class="diczx2">关</span>而西谓之纣。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《方言》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 商代最末的君主名，史称纣王<span class="encs">[name of tyrant ，last emperor of Shang Dyn.]</span>。历史上有名的暴君</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">桀纣</span>贵为天子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;功名》</span>。注:&ldquo;贱仁多累曰纣。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">残义损善曰纣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以同言</span><span class="diczx2">楚威王</span>于侧纣之间。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
