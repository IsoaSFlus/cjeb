                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">靖</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>靖</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>平安，安静：～冥（幽深闲静）。～默。</li><li>平定，使秩序安定：绥～。～难（<span class="dicpy">nàn </span>）（平定叛乱）。</li><li>图谋，谋议：“实～夷我邦”。</li><li>恭敬：“士处～，敬老与贵，交不失礼。”</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">静</span>”，静止。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pacify; appease; calm, peaceful</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ruhig, still</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> calmer,pacifier,calme,paisible</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">靖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>靖</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从立，青声。本义:立容安静)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[still;quiet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靖，立竫也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自作不靖。——<span class="smcs">《书·盘庚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靖共尔位。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小明》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虎靖衡门。——<span class="smcs">《樊阳令杨君碑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靖以待命犹可，动必忧。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">清靖而无私虑。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·精神》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">清靖寡欲，与物无竞。——<span class="smcs">《北史·袁韦修传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既防溢而靖志兮，迨我暇以翱翔。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:靖密(安静致密);靖冥(宁静幽深);靖默(沉静缄默);靖重(安祥凝重)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 安定;和平 <span class="encs">[stable;peaceful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靖，安也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:靖晏(平静安逸，太平无事);靖遏(安定)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 善 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东门之栗，有靖家室。——<span class="smcs">《太平御览》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 细小 <span class="encs">[small]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靖，一曰细貌。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有小人国，名曰靖人。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·大荒东经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:靖人(短小的人。也作净人、诤人);靖言(小人巧言)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 谦卑恭敬的样子 <span class="encs">[respectful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士处靖。——<span class="smcs">《管子·大匡》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“卑敬貌。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:靖恭(恭敬谨慎);靖共(敬慎谦恭。共，通恭)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>靖</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 使安定 <span class="encs">[pacify;quiet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以靖乡土。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:靖边(安定边境);靖匡(安定、挽救、扶助)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 止息 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸侯靖兵，好以为事。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:靖兵(息兵，休战)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 平定 <span class="encs">[pacify]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日靖四方。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·我将》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:靖难(靖乱。平定乱事);靖青绥(安抚)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 治理 <span class="encs">[administer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靖，治也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span
