                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">繇</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>繇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>随从。《説文&bull;系部》&ldquo;䌛，隨從也&rdquo;</li>
	<li>草木茂盛貌。也作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/蘨">蘨</a>&rdquo;。《書&bull;禹貢》：&ldquo;厥草惟繇，厥木惟條。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/徭">徭</a>&rdquo;。徭役。《商君書&bull;徕民》：&ldquo;民無一日之繇，官無數錢之費。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/摇">摇</a>&rdquo;。摇动。《史記&bull;蘇秦列傳》：&ldquo;我起乎宜陽而觸平陽，二日而莫不盡繇。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/謡">謡</a>&rdquo;。歌谣。《漢書.李尋傳》：&ldquo;揆山川變動，參人民繇俗。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/遥">遥</a>&rdquo;。远。《荀子&bull;禮論》：&ldquo;先王恐其不文也，是以繇其期足之日也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>人名用字。&ldquo;咎繇&rdquo;即&ldquo;皐陶&rdquo;。尧、舜的臣子。《正字通&bull;糸部》：&ldquo;繇，皐陶，《漢百官表》咎繇，與陶同。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>介词。相当于&ldquo;<a href="/hans/於">於</a>&rdquo;。《爾雅&bull;釋詁上》：&ldquo;繇，於也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。《後漢書&bull;郅惲傳》：&ldquo;西部督郵繇延，天資忠良。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>繇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/由">由</a>&rdquo;。介词。自；从。《爾雅&bull;釋水》：&ldquo;以衣涉水為厲，繇膝以下為揭。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/由">由</a>&rdquo;。介词。原由。《漢書&bull;元帝紀》：&ldquo;懼於天地之戒，不知所繇。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/由">由</a>&rdquo;。经过；经历。《漢書&bull;胡建傳》引黄帝《李法》：&ldquo;壁壘已定，穿窬不繇路，是謂姦人。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/由">由</a>&rdquo;。办法。明夏完淳《南都大略》：&ldquo;自以江南天塹，飛渡無繇。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/由">由</a>&rdquo;。用。《吕氏春秋&bull;貴當》：&ldquo;名號大顯，不可彊求，必繇其道。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/由">由</a>&rdquo;。随；听从。《文選&bull;韋孟〈諷諫〉》：&ldquo;賞罰之行，非繇王室。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/猷">猷</a>&rdquo;。道理，道术。《爾雅&bull;釋詁下》：&ldquo;繇，道也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/猷">猷</a>&rdquo;。尚且；仍然。《廣韻&bull;尤韻》：&ldquo;繇，猶也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/𨙂"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28642.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨙂</span></a>&rdquo;。疾行。《廣韻&bull;宵韻》：&ldquo;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28642.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨙂</span>，疾行。又音由。或作繇。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>闲适貌。《漢書&bull;叙傳上》：&ldquo;近者陸子優繇，《新語》以興。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>忧愁。《爾雅&bull;釋詁下》：&ldquo;繇，憂也。&rdquo;</li>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/431B_8725.svg" /><span class="swbf">(䌛)</span><span class="ywyy">隨從也。</span><span class="swdz">辵部曰：從，隨行也。隨，從也。䌛與隨，從三篆爲轉注。从系者，謂引之而往也。爾雅釋故曰：䌛，道也。詩書䌛作猷。叚借字。小雅。匪大猶是經。大雅。遠猶辰吿。傳皆曰：猶，道也。書大誥。猷爾多邦。猷亦道也。道路及導引，古同作道。皆隨從之義也。䌛之譌體作䌛。亦用爲傜役字。傜役者，隨從而爲之者也。</span><span class="ywyy">从系，</span><span class="swdz">有所系而隨從之也。</span><span class="ywyy">䚻聲。</span><span class="swdz">余招切。按此音非也。當以周切。三部。</span></p>
