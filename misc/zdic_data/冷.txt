                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>冷</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lěng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lěng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>温度低，与“<span class="diczx3">热</span>”相对：～天。～藏。～冻。～涩。～飕飕。</li><li>寂静，不热闹：～落。～寂。～静。～清。</li><li>生僻，少见的：～僻。～字。</li><li>不热情，不温和：～遇。～淡。～峻。～漠。～嘲热讽。～若冰霜。～酷无情。</li><li>不受欢迎的，没有人过问的：～货。～门儿。</li><li>突然，意料以外的：～然。～不防。～枪。～战。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">冷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>冷</strong> <span class="dicpy">lěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lěng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从仌( <span class="dicpy">bīng</span>)，冰，令声。本义:凉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cool]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冷，寒也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">画堂鹦鹉鸟，冷暖不相知。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《乌夜啼》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:冷香(清雅的香气);冷泉(清凉的泉水);冷翠(给人以清凉感的翠绿色)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 寒冷 <span class="encs">[cold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">布衾多年冷似铁。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:冰冷(很冷);干冷(干燥而寒冷);冷丁丁(亦作<span class="yhcs">“冷化化”</span>。形容天气寒冷。也形容身体冰凉;表情冷淡、严肃的样子);冷噤(冷禁。冷战;寒颤);冷铺(乞丐居住。也指驿亭);冷节遗芳(寒冷时节开的花)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 冷清;冷落 <span class="encs">[cold and cheerless;desolate;lonely]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">门前冷落鞍马稀，老大嫁作商人妇。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸公衮衮登台省，广</span><span class="diczx2">文</span>先生官独冷。—— <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《醉时歌赠广文馆学士郑虔》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:冷巷(僻静的小巷);冷闷(冷落;冷冰冰);冷曹(清冷的官署);冷局(冷落的衙门);冷寂(冷落寂寞)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 冷遇;对人冷淡 <span class="encs">[cool;be cold in manner]</span>。如:冷鼻凹(冷淡无情的面孔);冷脸(冷淡无表情的面孔);冷二郎(柳湘莲的绰号);冷肠(不热心于世事);冷心冷面(态度冷淡，对人没有感情)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 生僻;冷僻;少见 <span class="encs">[rare;strange]</span>。如:冷字(生僻 不常见的字);冷着(冷招，意想不到的招数、法子);冷业(冷僻的行当);冷眼(别人看不见的时机)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 突然，乘人不备 <span class="encs">[sudden]</span>。如:冷箭</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 不畅销 <span class="encs">[not readily marketable]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">这<span class="smcs">《千家诗》</span>还算一半是冷货，一年不过百把部。——<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:冷书(不热门的书)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>冷</strong> <span class="dicpy">lěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lěng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 冷却;温度变低 <span class="encs">[cool]</span>。如:冷冻;制冷(用人工方法取得低温);太烫了，冷一下再吃;冷藏</p><p><span
