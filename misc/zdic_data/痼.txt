                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>痼</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒(<span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span>)，表示与疾病有关。固声。&ldquo;固&rdquo;(顽固)也具有表意作用。本义:疾病经久难愈的)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chronic]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">痼，久病也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">邓太后</span>以皇子 <span class="diczx2">胜</span>有痼疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;周章传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:痼瘵(积久难治的病。亦指患痼疾的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为根深蒂固的，难改变的 <span class="encs">[inveterate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟霞成痼癖，声价藉</span><span class="diczx2">巢</span><span class="diczx2">由</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot;潘音<span class="smcs">《反北山嘲》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:痼习;痼癖(不能改掉的积习)</span></p>
