                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>倍</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bèi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bèi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>等于原数的两个：加～。事～功半。～道而行（兼程而行）。</li><li>某数的几倍等于用几乘某数：二的五～是十。</li><li>更加，非常：“每逢佳节～思亲”。～加。～儿精神。</li><li>增益：“焉用亡郑以～邻？”</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">背</span>”，背弃，背叛。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">背</span>”，背诵。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">倍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>倍</strong> <span class="dicpy">bèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，( <span class="dicpy">tǒu</span>)声。本义:通<span class="yhcs">“背”</span>。反;背向)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[back towards]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倍，反也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倍正南方。——<span class="smcs">《周髀算经下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵法右倍山陵，前左水泽。——<span class="smcs">《史记·淮阴侯传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 背弃;背叛。后作<span class="yhcs">“背”</span> <span class="encs">[abandon;betray]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无倍畔之心。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·贾谊传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">教而不称师谓之倍。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·大略》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而民不倍。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·大学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愿伯具言臣之不敢倍德也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:倍畔(同背叛);倍上(背叛尊上)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“背”</span>。违反;违背 <span class="encs">[violate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倍本弃事而安怠傲。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·非儒》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倍其公法，损其正心，专听大臣者，危主也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·任法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倍道而妄行，则天不能使之吉。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·天论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:倍言(违背信约);倍时(违时，错过时机);倍情(违背情实)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 加倍，照原数等加 <span class="encs">[double]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为近利市三倍。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘上之急，所卖必倍。——</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:倍日(一天时间当两天用);倍半(超出一倍和仅有其半。谓一多一少，差距甚大);倍多(成倍增加)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“背”</span>。背诵 <span class="encs">[recite from memory]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“倍文曰讽”</span>。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司乐》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">读书倍文，功力兼人。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩信</span><span class="smcs">《韩滂墓志铭》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:倍文(背诵书文);倍讽(背诵);倍读(背诵)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>倍</strong> <span class="dicpy">bèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 倍数 <span class="encs">[times;-fold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尝以十倍之地，百万之师，叩关而攻秦。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span
