                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蝗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huáng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huáng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄤˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 〔～虫〕昆虫，种类很多，躯体绿色或黄褐色。咀嚼式口器，后足适于弹跳，常常成群飞翔，是农业害虫，亦称“蚂（<span class="dicpy">mà</span>）蚱”；简称“蝗”，如“～灾”，“灭～”。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蝗</strong> <span class="dicpy">huáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蝗虫 <span class="encs">[grasshopper;locust]</span>。种类很多。一般指飞蝗，常成群飞翔。又名蚂蚱。吃麦、稻、玉蜀黍等禾苗，是农业上的主要害虫之一</p><p><span class="diczx1">蝗，螽也。从虫，皇声。——说文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蝗螟农夫得而杀之。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蝗螟(蝗与螟。庄稼的两种害虫);蝗螽(即蝗虫)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 蝗灾。蝗虫造成的灾荒 <span class="encs">[plague of locusts]</span>。如:蝗雨(谓蝗群如雨骤至。极言蝗灾之剧);蝗旱(发生蝗灾及旱灾);蝗孽(犹蝗灾)。又叫<span class="yhcs">“蚂蚱”</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蝗虫">蝗虫</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蝗莺">蝗莺</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蝗灾">蝗灾</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
