                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>臬</strong> <span class="dicpy">niè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 箭靶 <span class="encs">[target]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臬，射准的也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> ;引申为目标;准则</p><p><span class="diczx1">所发无臬。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:臬极(准则;标准)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代测日影的标杆 <span class="encs">[guidepost]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陈圭置臬，瞻星揆地。——<span class="smcs">《石阙铭·序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 刑法;法度 <span class="encs">[law]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王曰:<span class="yhcs">“外事，汝陈时臬，司师兹</span><span class="diczx2">殷</span>罚有伦。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《书·康诰》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代主管一省司法的官员 <span class="encs">[provincial judge]</span>。如:臬司(官名。即元的肃政廉访司，掌管一路的司法刑狱和官吏考核的工作。后明、清的提刑按察司，也称臬司，俗称臬台或廉访);臬使(即按察使);臬府(即臬司。指肃政廉访使);臬宪(旧时对按察使的敬称)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 终极 <span class="encs">[terminal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其深不测，其广无臬。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王粲</span><span class="smcs">《游海赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 安装在船侧用以支架橹的木桩 <span class="encs">[spile]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如人摇橹，臬为之碍故也。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/81EC.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】五結切【集韻】【正韻】魚列切【韻會】倪結切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音孽。【說文】射的。从木，自聲。【徐曰】射之高下準的。</p><p>又【廣韻】門橜也。【爾雅·釋宮】樴謂之杙，在地者謂之臬。【註】卽門橜也。【韻會】或作槷。亦作闑。</p><p>又【博雅】臬，灋也。【書·康誥】王曰：外事汝陳時臬。【傳】臬，法也。</p><p>又【多方】爾罔不克臬。【傳】汝無不能用法，欲其皆用法。</p><p>又【小爾雅】臬，極也。</p><p>又【集韻】九芮切，音劂。射的也。</p><p>又【韻補】叶牛例切，音刈。【張衡·東京賦】桃弧棘矢，所發無臬。飛礫雨散，剛彈必斃。【註】臬，射墩的也。【釋文】牛例切。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/81EC_5353.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(臬)</span><span class="ywyy">射<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/214D1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡓑</span>旳也。</span><span class="swdz"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/214D1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡓑</span>旳各本作準的。今正。土部曰。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/214D1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡓑</span>、射臬也。二字互訓。日部曰。旳、明也。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/214D1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡓑</span>準、旳的皆古今字。李善東京賦注引作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/214D1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡓑</span>。可證古本不作俗字矣。臬古假藝爲之。上林賦。弦矢分。藝殪仆。文穎曰。所射準的爲藝。左傳。陳之藝極。皆是也。臬之引伸爲凡標準法度之偁。釋宮曰。樴謂之杙。在牆者謂之臬。康誥曰。陳時臬事。考工記。匠人作槷。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E0F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨸏</span>部曰。賈侍中說陧法度也。皆臬之假借字也。</span><span class="ywyy">從木。自聲。</span><span class="swdz">五結切。十五部。李陽冰曰。自非聲。以去入爲隔礙也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
