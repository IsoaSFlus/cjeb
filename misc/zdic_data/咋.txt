                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎ.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄚˇ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〈方〉代词，怎，怎么：～样。～好。～办。～个。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zé  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zé.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄜˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>大声呼叫。</li>    <li>咬住：～舌（a.把自己的舌头咬住，或忍住不言，形容极度悔恨或畏缩；b.惊讶，害怕，说不出话来）。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhā.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄚˉ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔～呼〕a.吆喝；b.炫耀。均亦作“咋唬”（“<span class="diczx3">呼</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">唬</span>”均读轻声）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">咋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>咋</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 〈方〉∶怎;怎么 <span class="encs">[how]</span>。如:情况咋样?</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 为什么原因、理由或目的 <span class="encs">[why]</span>。如:你咋不去?</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>咋</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><语气></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 多用于句末，表示疑问、命令、惊讶等语气。如:你还会说点个什么咋?;你快走些咋</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhā;zhà</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/咋个">咋个</a></span></div><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>咋</strong> <span class="dicpy">zé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 啃咬 <span class="encs">[bite]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犬彘咋其骨，乌鸢啄其皮。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏舜钦</span><span
