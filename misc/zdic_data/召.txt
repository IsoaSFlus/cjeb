                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">召</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>召</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，刀声。本义:呼唤)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 召唤。用言语叫人来 <span class="encs">[call;summon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召，呼也。从口，刀声。以言曰召，以手曰招。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召，呼也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂二》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召有司。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span><span class="diczx2">御吏</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx1">大府召视儿。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亦召供奉。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林·雅量》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:召呼(呼唤;通知;交代);召致(使之至;唤来)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 招致 <span class="encs">[incur]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故言有召祸也，行有招辱也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召武受辞。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">收召后学。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《答韦中立论师道书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召闹取怒。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:召引(招引;引导);召幸(被招致或宠爱的人);召取(导致;引来)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 邀请 <span class="encs">[invite]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人有烹猴而召其邻人。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·脩务》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“召，犹请也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令召客者酒酣。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·分职》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今召客者，酒酣，歌舞，鼓瑟吹竽。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 征召来授予官职或另有调用 <span class="encs">[recruit]</span></p><p><span
