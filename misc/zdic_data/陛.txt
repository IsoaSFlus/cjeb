                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>陛</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜，坒( <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> )声。从<span class="yhcs">“阜”</span>，表示与地形地势的高低上下有关。本义:帝王宫殿的台阶)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a flight of steps]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陛，升高阶也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，天子之陛九级。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举杰压陛，诛讥罢只。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·大招》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陛者，皆闻焉。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓执兵列于陛侧者。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大王陛下。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪》</span>。按，臣与至尊言不敢指斥，故呼在陛下者告之。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陛高二尺五，广长各三尺，远广各六尺。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·备城门》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至陛下，</span><span class="diczx2">秦武阳</span>色变振恐，群臣怪之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:陛卫(帝王身边的卫士);陛槛(宫殿台阶两旁的栏杆);陛兵(皇宫前殿陛两旁的警卫士兵)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 阶次;品第 <span class="encs">[rank;position]</span>。如:陛级(地位;等级)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>陛</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>执兵器列于陛侧 <span class="encs">[stand in line aside]</span>。如:陛戟(卫士持戟列在皇宫陛下两侧)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/陛下">陛下</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/965B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】傍禮切【集韻】【韻會】部禮切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29A59.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩩙</span>。【說文】升高階也。【玉篇】天子階也。【戰國策】秦舞陽奉地圖匣，以次進至陛。【前漢·東方朔傳】是時朔陛戟殿下。</p><p>又【王莽傳】朱戸納陛。【註】孟康曰：納，內也。謂鑿殿臺際爲陛，不使露也。師古曰：尊者不欲露而升陛，故內之於霤也。</p><p>又天子稱陛下。【史記·秦始皇紀】今海內賴陛下神靈，一統皆爲郡縣。【蔡邕·獨斷】天子必有近臣，執兵立於陛側，以戒不虞。羣臣與天子言，不敢指斥，故呼在陛下者而告之，因<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤰞</span>達尊之意。【集韻】或作㙄。【篇海】別作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28ED8.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻘</span>。</p><p>又【韓愈·曹成王<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥓓</span>辭】王亦有子，處王之所。惟舊之視，蹶蹶陛陛，實取實似。刻詩其<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥓓</span>，爲示無止。【註】陛陛，猶比比，言衆多層次也。一說言王之子孫日益盛大，如歷階陛以升堂也。</p><p>又【正韻】毘意切，音避。義同。【海篇】別作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E7C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨹼</span>。◎按諸韻書陛字皆止上聲，惟正韻有上去二音，今<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>存之。《正字通》以去聲爲正音，非。</p>
