                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>允</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上为<span class="yhcs">“以”</span>(呂)字，下为<span class="yhcs">“儿”</span>(人)字。以是任用，用人不贰就是<span class="yhcs">“允”</span>。本义:诚信)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sincere]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">允，信也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">允，信也;允，诚也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众允。——<span class="smcs">《易·晋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">允恭克让。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">允也天子。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·长发》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命汝作纳言，风夜出纳联命，惟允。——<span class="smcs">《方言》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:允元(信任仁厚之人);允直(诚实正真);允忠(忠信)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 公平 <span class="encs">[fair]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祖父经，为郡县狱吏，案法平允，务存宽恕。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:允正(允当平正);允切(切当);允衷(恰 当);允情(合乎情理);允帖(妥贴，妥当)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>允</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 答应，许可 <span class="encs">[allow;permit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慰留不允。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:允帖(旧时男家向女家求婚，女家表示同意的帖子);允从(允诺，依从)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 符合 <span class="encs">[accord with]</span>。如:允合(符合);允符(符合);允值(相符，相当)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 使人信服;受人敬重 <span class="encs">[believe;respect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你吃我这杯酒，应允我的话，我才起来。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:允孚(得人心，使人信服);允赖(信赖，依靠)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用，使用 <span class="encs">[use]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">允厘百工。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>允</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 确实，果真 <span class="encs">[indeed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度其夕阳，豳居允荒。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·公刘》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:允袭(确实相合);允臧(确实好;完善);允迪(认真履践或遵循)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
