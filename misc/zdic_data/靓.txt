                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">靓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>靓</strong></p>
<p>靚 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 召见;邀请。也作&ldquo;请&rdquo; <span class="encs">[call in a subordinate; invite]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">靓，召也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">靓，呼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span>。按，史记、汉书多以请为之。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 妆饰艳丽 <span class="encs">[make up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">靓妆刻蚀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">昭君</span>丰容靓饰，光明 <span class="diczx2">汉</span>宫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>靓</strong></p>
<p>靚 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 淑静 <span class="encs">[fair and quiet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淡乎若深渊之靓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">意态闲且靓，气若兰蕙芳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贡师泰</span><span class="smcs">《拟古二首》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 艳丽;美好 <span class="encs">[beautiful]</span>。如:靓衣(艳丽的衣饰);靓妆</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 幽静。通&ldquo;静&rdquo; <span class="encs">[quiet and secluded]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">神眇眇兮密靓处，君不御兮谁为荣?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;外戚传下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">澹虖若深渊之靓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">稍暗暗而靓深。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《甘泉赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">li&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>靓</strong></p>
<p>靚 <span class="dicpy">li&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/975A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>疾正切，音淨。【說文】召也。</p>
