                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>棼</strong> <span class="dicpy">fén</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fén.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 栋(屋的正梁)，阁楼的栋 <span class="encs">[ridgepole]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">棼，复屋栋也。从林，分声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，从林者，从二木也。复屋故从二木为意。复屋者，如</span><span class="diczx2">苏</span>俗所云，阁不可居，重屋如楼可居。</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 阁楼 <span class="encs">[attic]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">棼，阁也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:棼橹(楼橹。古时军中用以了望敌军的无顶盖高台)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 麻布 <span class="encs">[sackcloth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">素车，棼蔽。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官·巾车》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>棼</strong> <span class="dicpy">fén</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fén.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“紊”</span>( <span class="dicpy">wěn</span>)。纷乱，紊乱。众多错杂的 <span class="encs">[numerous and disorderly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹治丝而棼之也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">治丝而益棼之。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泯泯棼棼。——<span class="smcs">《书·吕刑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:棼然(扰乱;纷乱);棼丝(乱丝);棼烟(缭乱的烟雾);棼错(纷杂错乱)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/68FC_2861.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(棼)</span><span class="ywyy">複屋棟也。</span><span class="swdz">複屋、考工記謂之重屋。木部曰。檼、棼也。是曰檼、曰棼者、複屋之棟也。曰橑者、複屋之椽也。竹部曰。笮者、在瓦之下棼上者也。姚氏鼐曰。椽棟旣重、軒版垂檐皆重矣。軒版卽屋笮。或木或竹異名。在瓦之下椽之上。檐垂椽端。椽亦謂之橑。考工記重屋。鄭以複笮釋之。而他書所稱曰重檐曰重橑曰重軒曰重、棟、曰重棼、各舉其一爲言爾。按左傳。治絲而棼之。假借爲紛亂字。</span><span class="ywyy">從林。分聲。</span><span class="swdz">符分切。十三部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
