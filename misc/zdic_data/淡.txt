                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">淡</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>淡</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>含盐分少，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">咸</span>&rdquo;相对：味～。～水。～化。</li>
	<li>浅，薄，含某种成分少，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">浓</span>&rdquo;相对：～酒。～绿。～薄。浅～。～雅。天高云～。</li>
	<li>不热心：冷～。～泊（对名利不热心）。～漠。～忘。恬～。</li>
	<li>营业不旺盛：～季。</li>
	<li>无关紧要，无聊：扯～。～话。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> weak, watery; insipid, tasteless</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faible,pâle,léger,doux,clair,indifférent</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">淡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>淡</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，炎声。本义:味淡;味道不浓，不咸)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tastless;weak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淡，薄味也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淡而不厌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子淡以成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span>。注:&ldquo;无酸酢少味也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淡也者，五味之中也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;水地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大味必淡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓无主味也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其水淡而清。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;言语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:淡酒;淡味;淡句(平淡乏味的诗句);淡而不厌(指诗文恬淡而不乏味);淡菜</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 稀薄 <span class="encs">[thin;light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天高云淡，望断南飞雁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">毛泽东</span><span class="smcs">《清平乐&middot;六盘山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:淡水白酒(薄酒);淡蛾(淡眉);淡烟(轻烟);淡粥(稀粥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 颜色浅 <span class="encs">[light;pale]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:淡青;淡绿;淡服(淡素的衣服);淡蒙蒙(浅淡朦胧);淡扫蛾眉(轻画眉毛。形容妇女化妆清雅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 冷淡，不热情 <span class="encs">[indifferent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">钧</span>与人交，始若淡薄，既久乃益固。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《唐书&middot;卢钧传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冷淡(不热情，不亲热);淡悠悠(不介意的样子);淡话(不痛不痒的话;可说可不说的话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 淡雅 <span class="encs">[simple but elegant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲把西湖比西子，淡妆浓抹总相宜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《饮湖上初晴雨后》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:淡净(淡雅素净);淡妆浓抹(淡雅和浓艳两种不同的妆饰打扮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 淡泊，不追求名利 <span class="encs">[not seek fame and wealth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且君子之交淡若水，小人之交甘若醴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;山水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:淡如(恬淡寡欲，不追求名利);淡泊自甘(乐于恬淡生活，不以清贫为苦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 营业不旺盛 <span class="encs">[slack;dull]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:淡月;在淡季里解雇一些职员</span></p>
