                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>饶</strong></p><p>饒 <span class="dicpy">ráo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ráo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，尧声。本义:饱)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be full]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饶，饱也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。 ?</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 富裕，丰足 <span class="encs">[rich;plentiful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饶，多也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饶，益也。</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>时谣曰:<span class="yhcs">“今年尚可后年饶。今苏俗买物请益谓之讨饶头。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">原大则饶，原小则鲜。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">七十子之徒，赐(子贡)最为饶益——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:饶裕(富足;富裕);饶益(富裕);饶美(丰美);饶富(富饶，丰足);饶益(富裕，使人受益);饶余(富足有余);饶足(生活富裕);饶野(富饶的田野)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 余;剩 <span class="encs">[surplus]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饶，余也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子弟衣食自有余饶。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《自表后主》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:饶道(啰嗦，爱说多余的话);饶奢(过分长大);饶言(饶舌)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 安逸 <span class="encs">[easy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沃地之民多不才者，饶也。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 肥沃 <span class="encs">[fertile]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不爱珍器重宝肥饶之地。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掠于饶野，三军足食。——<span class="smcs">《孙子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 如:饶沃;饶广(肥沃广阔)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 众多;多 <span class="encs">[numerous;many;much]</span>。如:饶多(很多，非常多);饶爽(十分豪爽);饶盈(赚钱甚多);饶乏(多少);饶财(多财)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 通“扰”</span>。乱<span class="encs">[confusion;chaos]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疏远微贱者无所告诉，则下饶。——<span class="smcs">《管子·版法解》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>饶</strong></p><p>饒 <span class="dicpy">ráo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ráo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 宽恕;宽容 <span class="encs">[forgive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日月不相饶，节序昨夜隔。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《立秋后题》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:饶了他吧;饶让 (宽恕;让);饶闲(放松;使空闲);饶赦(饶恕赦免);饶情(留情面);饶假(宽恕;宽容);饶借(容让);饶免(宽免)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span>
