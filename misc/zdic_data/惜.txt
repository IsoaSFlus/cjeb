                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>惜</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，昔声。本义:哀痛，哀伤)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be deep sorrow;be grieved]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惜，痛也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惜者，哀也。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·惜誓序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为陛下惜之。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惜其用武不终。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岁月为可惜。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:可惜(值得惋惜);痛惜(沉痛地惋惜);惜伤(哀伤)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 珍惜，爱惜，重视而不糟蹋耗费 <span class="encs">[cherish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为天下惜死。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·长利》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">大禹</span>圣者乃惜寸阴;至于众人，当惜分阴。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·陶侃传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">视之不甚惜。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣辈惜程艺。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林·雅量》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝辈所惜。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朕不自谨惜。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:吝惜(过分爱惜);怜惜(同情爱惜);珍惜(珍重爱惜);惜玉(对女子的温情爱护);惜体面(爱面子);惜福(富有而不肯过分享受);惜护(十分爱护);惜名(爱惜名声)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 惋惜<span class="encs">[feel sorry for sb or about sth.;have pity for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惜其不成，是以就极刑而无愠色。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:爱惜(惋惜、同情)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 哀怜、哀悯<span class="encs">[feel pity for sb.; have compassion for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惜余年老而日衰兮，岁忽忽而不反。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·惜誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杨意不逢，抚凌云而自惜。——<span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 贪婪，贪求<span class="encs">[be avaricious;be greedy for;have an insatiable desire for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惜诵以致愍兮，发愤以抒情。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·惜诵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 吝惜;舍不得 <span class="encs">[pinch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子卿</span>视陵，岂偷生之士，而惜死之人哉?—— <span class="diczx2">李陵</span><span class="smcs">《答苏武书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驱将惜不得。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不惜牺牲生命。——</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:惜吝(吝惜;舍不得);不惜工本;不惜金玉</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
