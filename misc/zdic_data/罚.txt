						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>罚</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">罰</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fá.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄚˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 处分犯罪、犯错误或违犯某项规则的人：～款。～球。惩～。～不当罪（处罚过严或过宽，与所犯的罪行不相当）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>罚</strong></p><p>罰、罸 <span class="dicpy">fá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字，从刀，从詈( <span class="dicpy">lì</span>，骂人)。<span class="yhcs">“刀”</span>指刑法。本义:处罚，惩办)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[penalize;punish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罰，罪之小者。从刀、从詈，会意。未以刀有所贼，但持刀骂詈则应罚。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罚上报下之罪也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三让而罚。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司救》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“挞击之也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小刑宪罚。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司市》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“播其肆也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忠所罪以遇罚兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·惜诵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陟罚臧否，不宜异同。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆不应重罚。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罚所及则思无因怒而滥刑。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:罚规(处罚的规章);罚约(输者受罚的约定);罚弗及嗣(惩罚罪犯，但不延及子孙)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 对有过失、错误的处分 <span class="encs">[be disciplined;take disciplinary action against]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如诗不成，罚依金谷酒数。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《春夜宴桃李园序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:罚爵(宴饮时，罚失礼者喝酒的酒器)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 讨伐;杀 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">致天之罚。——<span class="smcs">《书·汤誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:罚极(因有罪而加以诛杀);罚罪(讨伐或惩处有罪者);罚恶(惩治恶人);罚蔽(罚罪断狱)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 出钱赎罪 <span class="encs">[buy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五刑不筒，正于五罚——<span class="smcs">《书·五刑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:罚锾(纳金赎罪);罚铜(纳铜罚罪);罚赎(出钱赎罪);罚俸(官吏因犯罪而停发薪俸)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 发誓 <span class="encs">[swear;vow;pledge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不是我</span><span class="diczx2">窦娥</span>罚下这等无头愿，委实的冤情不浅。—— <span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">关汉卿</span><span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>罚</strong></p><p>罰 <span class="dicpy">fá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button                           『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7F70_5081.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(罰)</span><span class="ywyy">辠之小者。</span><span class="swdz">辠、犯法也。罰爲犯法之小者。㓝爲罰辠之重者。五罰輕於五㓝。</span><span class="ywyy">从刀詈。</span><span class="swdz">㑹意。房越切。十五部。</span><span class="ywyy">未㠯刀有所賊。但持刀罵詈則應罰。</span><span class="swdz">說從刀詈之意。罰者、但持刀而詈則法之。然則㓝者謂持刀有所賊則法之。別其犯法之輕重也。初學記云。元命包曰。网言爲詈。刀守詈爲罰。罰之爲言内也。陷於害也。注云。詈以刀守之則不動矣。今作罰用寸。寸、丈尺也。言納以繩墨之事。初學記又云。元命包曰。㓝、刀守井也。飮水之人入井爭水。陷於泉。刀守之。割其情也。注云。井飮人、則人樂之不巳。則自陷於泉。故加刀謂之㓝。欲人畏懼以全命也。此二條皆引春秋元命包。今本初學記皆系諸說文。殊誤。觀玄應書卷卄一卄五引春秋元命包說㓝字與此同。可以諟正矣。云刀守詈、刀守井、則㓝罰不分輕重。古五罰不用刀也。故許說罰爲刀詈。犯法之小者。㓝爲井刀。執法之大者。一入刀部。一入井部。所以正緯說也。○唐人諱淵作泉。亦或作川。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
