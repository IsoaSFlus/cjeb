                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>隧</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">suì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>道路，特指门内当中的路。</li><li>郊外的地方：“鲁人三郊三～”。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">燧</span>”，边塞设置的守望烽火的亭子。</li><li>〔～道〕在山中或地下凿成的通路。亦称“隧洞”。</li><li>旋转：“若磨石之～”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> tunnel, underground passageway, path to a tomb</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> unterirdisch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tunnel,passage souterrain</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">隧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>隧</strong> <span class="dicpy">suì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">fù</span>)，遂声。阜指土山，从阜的字有的与建筑有关。本义:墓道，古墓中运送棺材到墓室的通道)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[aisle leading to the coffin chamber of an ancient tomb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以度为丘隧。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·冢人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上数幸</span><span class="diczx2">嶷</span>第， <span class="diczx2">宋</span><span class="diczx2">长宁陵</span>隧道出第前路，上曰<span class="yhcs">“我便是入他家墓内寻人。——<span class="smcs">《南史·豫章文献王嶷传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:隧户(墓道的门);隧口(墓道的入口处)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 地道 <span class="encs">[tunnel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君何患焉?若阙地及泉，隧而相见，其谁曰不然?——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请隧弗许。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十五年》</span>。注:“阙地通路曰隧。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:隧洞(隧道);隧埒(深沟与高墩);隧炭(填在隧道中的炭)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 车辆或人畜通行的道路 <span class="encs">[road]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大风有隧。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑柔》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山无蹊隧。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·马蹄》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">连大车以塞隧。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出入不当门隧。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 人体血气津液等运行分泌的通道 <span class="encs">[channel in the body]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五藏之道，皆出于经隧，以行血气。——<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 钟上受敲击而洼下的地方 <span class="encs">[stricken point of a clock]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凫氏为钟…于上之攠谓之隧。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“遂”</span>。远郊之地 <span class="encs">[suburbs]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>人三郊三隧。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 周代郊外的一种行政区划。五县为隧 <span class="encs">[five counties]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令隧正纳郊保，奔火所。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:隧正(一隧之长)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“燧”</span>。烽火亭 <span class="encs">[beacon tower]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲起亭隧。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·西域传下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">登障隧而遥望兮，聊须臾以婆娑。——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/96A7.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28F61.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨽡</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>徐醉切，音遂。【玉篇】墓道也。【左傳·隱元年】闕地及泉，隧而相見。【註】隧，若今延道。</p><p>又【僖二十五年】晉侯朝王，王饗醴，命之宥，請隧弗許。【註】闕地通路曰隧。【周禮·春官·冢人】及竁以度爲丘隧。【註】隧，羨道也。</p><p>又道也。【詩·大雅】大風有隧。【禮·曲禮】出入不當門隧。【魯語】具舟除隧。【前漢·匈奴傳】起亭隧。【註】隧，謂開小道而行，避敵鈔<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡨥</span>也。</p><p>又【左傳·襄七年】叔仲昭伯爲隧正。【註】隧正，主役徒。</p><p>又【周禮·冬官考工記·鳧氏】于上之攠，謂之隧。【註】隧在鼓中，窐而生光，有似夫隧。</p><p>又草名。【爾雅·釋草】出隧，蘧蔬。【疏】菌類也。一名出隧，一名蘧蔬。</p><p>又【正字通】高麗國左有大穴，曰神隧。</p><p>又地名。【戰國策】吳見伐齊之便，而不知干隧之敗也。【註】干隧，吳地。</p><p>又縣名。【前漢·地理志】河閒國武隧縣。</p><p>又轉也，回也。【莊子·天下篇】若磨石之隧。【註】隧，音遂，回也。</p><p>又【集韻】直類切，音懟。落也。與墜同。【荀子·儒效篇】至共頭而山隧。【班固·幽通賦】眷峻谷曰勿隧。【註】可以免於顚隧。【前漢·王莽傳】不隧如髮。</p><p>又【集韻】雖遂切，音粹。與邃同。深遠也。【周禮·冬官考工記·輿人】參分車廣，去一以爲隧。【註】鄭司農云：隧謂車輿深也，讀如鑽燧攺火之燧。康成謂：讀如邃宇之邃。</p><p>又【集韻】杜罪切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26D8F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦶏</span>。【左傳·襄二十五年】當<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>隧者，井堙木刊。【註】隧，徑也。徐邈讀上聲。　【廣韻】俗作璲。</p><p>
