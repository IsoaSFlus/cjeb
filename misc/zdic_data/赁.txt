                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>赁</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">賃</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>租：租～。出～。～房。～车。～出。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rent, hire; hired person</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anmieten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> louer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赁</strong></p>
<p>賃 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，任声。本义:给人做雇工)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be hired]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷困，赁佣于齐，为酒人保。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;栾布列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赁工(雇工);赁力(出卖劳力);赁作(受雇为人劳作);赁保(受人雇用);赁书(受雇为人抄写);赁佣(受雇为佣工)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 租用、租借 <span class="encs">[rent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">每店皆有驴赁客乘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜佑</span><span class="smcs">《通典》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赁屋;赁书;赁居(租用房屋居住);赁约(租借契约);赁租(出租;租借)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赁</strong></p>
<p>賃 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>工钱，给受雇人的报酬 <span class="encs">[money paid for odd jobs]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赁银(劳动报酬，工钱);赁价，赁钱(租金);赁金(租金)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
