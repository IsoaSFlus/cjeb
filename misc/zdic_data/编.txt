                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>编</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">編</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">biān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>用细条或带形的东西交叉组织起来：～结。～织。～扎。</li><li>按一定的原则、规则或次序来组织或排列：～排。～目（编制目录或指已编成的目录）。～次。～年。～订。～配。～码。</li><li>把材料加以适当的组织排列而成为书籍、报刊、广播电视节目等：～写。～译。～审。～修。～纂。～印。</li><li>创作：～剧。～导。</li><li>捏造：～瞎话。</li><li>成本的书按内容划分的部分：正～。续～。简～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">编</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>编</strong></p><p>編 <span class="dicpy">biān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，扁声。本义:顺次排列，编结在一起)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[put in order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">编，次简也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以绳次物曰编。——<span class="smcs">《声类》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">春秋编年，四时具而后为年。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·桓公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且请编之于令，永为国典。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《驳复仇议》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:编号;编组;编队;编户(编入户籍的普通人家);编民(编入户籍的平民);编人(户籍编制之内的贫民。也称编民);编次(按顺序编排);编蒲(编订蒲叶使成册，以为书写之用)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 交织，编结 <span class="encs">[weave;plait]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">编结沮。——<span class="smcs">《四子讲德论》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“犹编发也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">总编一根大辫，黑亮如漆。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 编辑;创作 <span class="encs">[compile;write]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">编书其罪。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·诸葛丰传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">春秋编年。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·桓公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:编杂志;编述(著述;编辑);编订(编纂修订)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 连接 <span class="encs">[connect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">编町成篁。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“连也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:编町(连绵于畎亩之中)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 虚构，捏造 <span class="encs">[make up]</span>。如:瞎编;胡编;编派(编造故事，借机讥诮别人)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>编</strong></p><p>編 <span class="dicpy">biān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用来穿联竹简的绳子 <span class="encs">[cord]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>晚而喜<span class="smcs">《易》</span>…读<span class="smcs">《易》</span>，韦编三绝。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孔子世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">读之韦编三绝。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·儒林传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">手不停披于百家之编。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《进学解》</span></p><p><span
