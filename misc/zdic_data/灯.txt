                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>灯</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">燈</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dēng.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>照明的器具：电～。路～。～火（泛指亮的灯）。～语（通讯方法之一）。～标。</li>
	<li>其它用途的发光、发热装置：红绿～。指示～。酒精～。</li>
	<li>装饰张挂的彩灯：～节。～市。河～。冰～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">灯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>灯</strong></p>
<p>燈 <span class="dicpy">dēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，登声。本写作&ldquo; 镫&rdquo;。本义:置烛用以照明的器具。&ldquo;镫&rdquo;在古代还作&ldquo;盛熟食的器具&rdquo;解。隶变以后，作照明器具用的写作&ldquo;燈&rdquo;，而&ldquo;镫&rdquo;多作马鞍两旁的铁脚踏讲)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。亦指通电发光的照明器具或做其他用途的发光器具 <span class="encs">[lamp;lantern;light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">月夜见渔灯，孤光一点萤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">查慎行</span><span class="smcs">《舟夜书所见》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:油灯;电灯;日光灯;信号灯;探照灯;灯法(使用灯烛所作的幻术)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 彩灯。特指元宵节挂的彩灯 <span class="encs">[festoon lighting]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:灯市(买卖花灯的市集);灯品(花灯的品类);灯期(元宵节前后赏灯的时节);灯楼(用以张挂花灯的彩楼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[收音机、电视机等的]</span> 电子管的俗名 <span class="encs">[valve]</span>。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9419.svg">                          <p>錠也。从金登聲。都滕切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：錠中置燭，故謂之鐙。今俗别作燈，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9419_7235.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鐙)</span><span class="ywyy">錠也。</span><span class="swdz">祭統曰：夫人薦豆執校，執醴授之執鐙。注曰：校，豆中央直者也。鐙，豆下跗也。執醴者以豆授夫人，執其下跗，夫人受之，執其中央直者。按跗，說文作柎，闌足也。鐙有柎，則無足曰鐙之說未可信。豆之遺制爲今俗用燈盞。徐氏兄弟遂以膏鐙解說文，誤矣。生民傳曰：木曰豆，瓦曰登。豆薦菹醢，登薦大羹。箋云：祀天用瓦豆，陶器質也。然則瓦登用於祭天。廟中之鐙，笵金爲之，故其字从金。</span><span class="ywyy">从金登聲</span><span class="swdz">都滕切。六部</span></p>                          </div>
