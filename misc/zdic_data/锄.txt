                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>锄</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鋤</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>弄松土地及除草的工具：～头。铁～。</li>	<li>耪，弄松土地及除草：～草。～耘。～地。</li>	<li>铲除：～奸。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hoe; eradicate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> biner,sarcler,extirper,éliminer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>锄</strong></p>
<p>鋤、鉏 <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，助声。本义:锄头。一种长柄农具，其刀身平薄而横装，专用于中耕、除草、疏松植株周围的土壤) 同本义 <span class="encs">[hoe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵有健妇把锄犁，禾生陇亩无东西。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:大锄;小锄</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>锄</strong></p>
<p>鋤 <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用锄头整理田地，除草 <span class="encs">[work with a hoe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大儿锄豆溪东，中儿正织鸡笼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《清平乐&middot;村居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锄社(古代北方农村的一种组织。平时协同耕作，有事时则互相帮助);锄耘(耕作农地。也作鉏耘);锄谷(耕作)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 根除，铲除 <span class="encs">[eliminate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又不可使天下之民斫直，删密，锄正，以夭梅病梅为业以术钱也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锄拔(去除);锄理(清理)</span></p>
