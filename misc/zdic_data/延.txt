						<p>● <strong>延</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>引长：～长（<span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng </span>）。～续。蔓～。～年益寿。</li>
	<li>展缓，推迟：～迟。～缓。～宕。～误。</li>
	<li>引进，请：～聘（聘请）。～纳。～师。～医。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">延</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>延</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stretch;extend;prolong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延，长行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，字亦作&ldquo;蜒&rdquo;。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蝮蛇蜒只。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;大招》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延颈而鸣，舒翼而舞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我不敢知曰:不其延。惟不敬厥德，乃早坠厥命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;召诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兹物苟难停，吾寿安得延!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《长歌行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫不延颈举踵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;顺说》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:延瞩(引颈瞩目);延望(引颈远望);延算三纪(延长寿命三十六年);延年(延长寿命);延息(延长寿命);延期(延长或推迟日期)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假借为&ldquo;引&rdquo;。引入;引见;迎接 <span class="encs">[meet;introduce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摈者，延之日升。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主人延客祭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出延射。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;射义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:延接(引进接待);延入(引入);延登(引入而登殿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拖延 <span class="encs">[put off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延至七年(拖延到民国七年，即公元1918年)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略&middot;序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:延挨(拖延);延俄(延迟一会儿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 绵延 <span class="encs">[stretch long and unbroken]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:延袤(绵延;连绵);延昌(绵延昌盛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 延续 <span class="encs">[continue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延及</span><span class="diczx2">孝文王</span>、 <span class="diczx2">庄襄王</span>&hellip;国家无事。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:延寿文疏(祝寿的颂辞);延誉(称扬优点，使其名声远扬)延玩(迟延玩忽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 蔓延 <span class="encs">[spread]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延，遍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言十三》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5EF6.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】以然切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】夷然切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音綖。【說文】長行也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】進也。【禮&middot;射義】孔子使子路執弓矢出延射。【儀禮&middot;覲禮】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22DE4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢷤</span>者延之曰升。【註】從後詔禮曰延。延，進也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】長也。【揚子&middot;方言】延永，長也。凡施於年者謂之延，施於衆長謂之永。【班固&middot;西京賦】歷十二之延祚。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】遠也。【史記&middot;蒙恬傳】延袤萬餘里。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也。【疏】鋪<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】納也。【前漢&middot;公孫弘傳】弘起客館，開東閣，以延賢人。</p>
<p>又【集韻】及也。【書&middot;大禹謨】賞延于世。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】稅也，言也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】遷延也，淹久貌。【左傳&middot;襄十四年】晉人謂之遷延之役。【註】遷延，却退也。【張衡&middot;西京賦】遷延邪睨。【註】李善曰：遷延，引身也。</p>
<p>又盤屈曰宛延。【揚雄&middot;甘泉賦】颺翠氣之宛延。【註】宛延，長曲貌。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】閒也。【疏】謂閒<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻶</span>。今墓道也。【左傳&middot;隱元年隧而相見註】隧若今延道。</p>
<p>又【韻會】州名。漢高奴縣，後魏置延州。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;隱元年】至於廩延。【註】廩延，鄭邑。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>留酸棗縣北有延津。</p>
<p>又【昭二十七年】延州來季子聘於上國。【註】季子本封延陵，後復封州來，故曰延州來。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;地理志】張掖郡有居延縣。【註】居延澤在東北，古文以爲流沙。</p>
<p>又姓。【後漢&middot;延篤傳】篤，南陽人，爲京兆尹。</p>
<p>又【集韻】以淺切，音演。冕上覆也。【禮&middot;玉藻】天子玉藻十有二旒，前後邃延。【集韻】或作綖。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】于線切【集韻】【韻會】延面切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音羨。【集韻】延，及也。【張衡&middot;西京賦】重閨幽闥，轉相踰延。望䆗窱以逕庭，渺不知其所返。【註】延言互相周通。</p>
