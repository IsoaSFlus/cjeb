                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>夺</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">奪</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">du&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&oacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄛˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>抢，强取：抢～。掠～。巧取豪～。强（<span class="dicpy">qiāng </span>）词～理。</li>
	<li>争先取到：～得最后胜利。～魁。～冠（<span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n </span>）。</li>
	<li>冲开：～门而出。</li>
	<li>丧失，削除：剥～。褫～（剥夺）。～志（改变志向或意愿）。</li>
	<li>晃动：光彩～目。</li>
	<li>决定如何处理：请予裁～。</li>
	<li>漏掉（文字）：第八行～一字。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> take by force, rob, snatch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> s'emparer de,arracher,se ruer,gagner,remporter,enlever,prendre de force</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">夺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>夺</strong></p>
<p>奪 <span class="dicpy">du&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，上面象振翅欲飞的鸟，下面是手(又、寸)，其意思是:这只振翅欲飞的鸟，眼看着就要从手中失脱掉。本义:丧失)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夺，手持隹失之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">给夺慈仁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;仲尼燕居》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹乱也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">精气夺则虚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;通评虚实论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三军可夺帅也，匹夫不可夺志也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子罕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勿夺农时。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;富国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:夺宗(始封的国君被尊为诸侯以后，除去原来宗子的名分);夺气(丧失了胆量和勇气);夺魄(精神丧失而无生气);夺胆(丧胆);夺彩(失出光彩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 强取 <span class="encs">[seize;rob]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夺，取也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人夺女妻而不怒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夺其印符。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;淮阴侯列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夺攘矫虔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>王度之，终不可夺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夺</span><span class="diczx2">项王</span>天下者，必 <span class="diczx2">沛公</span>也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:夺转(夺得);夺劝(一边劝一边夺拿);夺情(皇帝特许某些有亲丧的官僚不守孝，或者未满守孝期者复取。意思是夺去了他的哀思之情);夺席(夺取他人的席位)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用力冲开，冲出 <span class="encs">[force one&#39;s way]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:夺门(破门，奋力冲开门);眼泪夺眶而出</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 竞先取得 <span class="encs">[win]</span></p>
