<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>焰</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>火光：光～。</li>
	<li>火苗：火～。烈～。气～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> flame, blaze; glowing, blazing</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anmachen, Anpflaumerei  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">焰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>焰</strong></p>
<p>燄 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，臽( <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n</span>)声。本义:火苗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[flame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟焰迷漫 。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟焰雾雨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span>。又如:内焰(火焰的中间部分);外焰(火焰最外层);焰段(见&ldquo;艳段&rdquo;);焰烘烘(焰腾腾。火焰旺盛的样子;引申为情绪激昂);焰头上(喻指兴旺发达);焰焰(火苗起初的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 火光;辉光 <span class="encs">[flame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凿石有余焰，年命已凋颓矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">葛洪</span><span class="smcs">《抱朴子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:焰红(火红);焰光(光焰;光华);焰然(鲜明貌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 光亮 <span class="encs">[light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雨昏陋巷灯无焰，风过贫家壁有声。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《随园诗话》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 比喻气势 <span class="encs">[momentum]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:妖焰;气焰逼人</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>焰</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 燃烧 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俯而观之，其光自土而出，若焰薪火。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张读</span><span class="smcs">《宣室志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:焰蜡(正在燃烧的蜡烛);焰灰(物体燃烧后的余烬);焰腾腾(火势猛烈貌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 照耀 <span class="encs">[shine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">动鳞甲于鲸鱼，焰光芒于鸣鹤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">北周</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《灯赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">野燎焰天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏源</span><span class="smcs">《圣武记》</span></p>
