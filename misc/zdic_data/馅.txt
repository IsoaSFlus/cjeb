                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>馅</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">餡</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>包在面食或点心等食品里面的肉、菜、糖等：～子。～饼。夹～。肉～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> filling, stuffing; secret</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> farce (viande)​</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>馅</strong></p>
<p>餡 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 面食、糕点里包的豆沙、糖、果仁、果肉或切碎的肉、菜等填料 <span class="encs">[stuffing]</span>。如:饺子馅儿;枣泥馅儿月饼;肉馅;鱼馅;馅草(菜馅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 被包裹的人或物 <span class="encs">[wrapped sb.or sth.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">城外土馒头，馅草在城里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">阮阅</span><span class="smcs">《诗话总龟》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 比喻事情的底细，隐密 <span class="encs">[the ins and outs]</span>。如:漏馅</p>
