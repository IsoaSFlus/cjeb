                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>牒</strong> <span class="dicpy">dié</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dié.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从片，葉( <span class="dicpy">yè</span>)声。古时木片也常用作书写材料，故从<span class="yhcs">“片”</span>。本义:简札)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。古代书写用的木片或竹片 <span class="encs">[writing slip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">版，札也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，小简曰牒，大简曰册。薄者曰牒，厚者曰牍。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受牒而退。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">截以为牒。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·路温舒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王牒石记。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:金牒;玉牒</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指谱籍 <span class="encs">[records of family pedigree]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余读牒记。——<span class="smcs">《史记·三代世表》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“记系谥之书，以牒为之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:谱牒;家牒</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通常由官方颁发的证明某事的文件 <span class="encs">[official document]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昨夜军牒来，电兵万五千。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《行次西郊作》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 如:通牒;牒文(公文;文书);牒旨(命令);牒署(办理;管理)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 书籍，簿册 <span class="encs">[book and noetbook]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壮志异时 (将来) 开史牒。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《送江宁彭给事赴阙》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其高第者上名牒。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·质帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 讼辞，状子 <span class="encs">[certificate]</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>牒</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄉㄧㄝˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>dié <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1166.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代用来书写的小而薄的竹简或木片。</span><span class="gc_yy">《墨子·公输》：「于是见公输盘，子墨子解带为城，以<b>牒</b>为械。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷五一·路温舒传》：「温舒取泽中蒲，截以为<b>牒</b>，编用写书。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">官方文书或证件。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「最后通<b>牒</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左传·昭公二十五年》：「右师不厂对，受<b>牒</b>而退。」</span><span class="gc_yy">金·麻九畴〈许方村即事〉诗：「扑惨官始威，<b>牒</b>烦民更玩。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">各种纪录文件。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷一三○·太史公自序》：「维三代尚矣，年纪不可考，盖取之谱<b>牒</b>旧闻。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">讼状。</span><span class="gc_yy">《魏书·卷四一·源子恭传》：「比加采访，略无证明，寻其表状，又复莫落，案<b>牒</b>推理，实有所疑。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清·纪昀《阅微草堂笔记·卷四·滦阳消夏录四》：「今乃知曾投此<b>牒</b>，岂得罪鬼神，使我受笞耶？」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">绵织品。</span><span class="gc_yy">《后汉书·卷四九·王符传》：「且其徒御仆妾，皆服文组彩<b>牒</b>，锦绣绮纨。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
