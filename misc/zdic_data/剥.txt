                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">剥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>剥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_jzx2.svg" title="旧字形"><span class="diczx3">剝</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>去掉外面的皮或其他东西：～皮。～花生。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>剥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_jzx2.svg" title="旧字形"><span class="diczx3">剝</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄛˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>义同&ldquo;剥&rdquo;（bāo），用于复合词：～夺。～削（<span class="dicpy">xuē</span>）。～落。～蚀。生吞活～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> peel</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> abschälen, schälen; abbalgen, das Fell abziehen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> écorcher,peler,éplucher,peler,écailler</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">剥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>剥</strong> <span class="dicpy">bāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="encs">[口]</span>∶去掉物的外皮或壳(多用于口语) <span class="encs">[shell;skin;peel;peel off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或剥或烹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剥花生;剥碗豆;剥牛皮;剥葱皮</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">bō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/剥壳">剥壳</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/剥皮">剥皮</a></span></div>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>剥</strong> <span class="dicpy">bō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从刀，从录，&ldquo;录&rdquo;又兼作声符。&ldquo;录&rdquo;，<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;刻割也。&rdquo;本义:削;剥离;剥脱，即去掉物体表面上的东西)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pare cut apart]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剥，裂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剥，离也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或剥或亨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然不剥脱，不砥厉，则不可以断绳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;强国》</span>。注:&ldquo;剥脱，谓刮去其生涩。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冬日至，令剥阴木而水之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;秋官》</span>。注:&ldquo;刊、剥互言耳，皆谓斫去次地之皮。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">溪头卧剥莲蓬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《清平乐&middot;村居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剥除(削去);剥割(割削);剥制(剥取或削除外皮，加工制作);剥皮(剥去外皮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 割裂 <span class="encs">[divide;sever;cut apart]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是剥是菹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;信南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 脱落 <span class="encs">[fall off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">实熟则剥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>隶岁久，风雨剥蚀，故其字无复锋芒。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《老学庵笔记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剥落(剥损脱落;落地，没有选上);剥缺(剥落残缺);剥坏(剥落损坏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 强制除去 <span class="encs">[denude]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裸剥士女。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">房玄龄</span><span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剥庐(剥夺他人荫庇之所;穷困的居所);剥放(斥退)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 盘剥;掠夺 <span class="encs">[exploit;rob]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故为吏牧民者竞相剥削。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《梁书&middot;贺琛传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">患在于剥夺之不已。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《钱货议状》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剥害(盘剥伤害);剥褫(革除;褫夺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 罢免，革除 <span class="encs">[recall]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:剥坐(革职治罪);剥辱(谓革职之辱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 伤害 <span class="encs">[injure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剥丧元良。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;泰誓中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剥丧(伤亡);剥戮(伤害杀戮);剥丧(伤亡;丧乱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 衰微;减少 <span class="encs">[decay]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:剥极(时运不利或事物衰败到了极点)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">bāo;pū</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=刀" target="_blank">刀</a>】
                              剝·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/525D.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠚪</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>北角切，音駁。【說文】裂也。从刀，彔聲。【玉篇】削也。【廣韻】落也，割也，傷害也。【楚辭&middot;九思】怫鬱兮肝切剝。</p>
<p>又【增韻】褫也，脫也。</p>
<p>又卦名。【易&middot;剝卦】剝也，柔變剛也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;柞氏】冬日至，令剝隂木而水之。【註】謂斫去次地之皮。</p>
<p>又殺牲體解之名。【詩&middot;小雅】或剝或亨。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;檀弓】喪不剝奠也與。【註】剝者，不巾覆也。脯醢之奠不惡塵埃，故可無巾覆。</p>
<p>又【集韻】普木切【正韻】普卜切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音璞。力擊也。【詩&middot;豳風】八月剝棗。【註】擊也。</p>
<p>又音卜。【魏&middot;劉楨&middot;魯都賦】毛羣隕殪，羽族殲剝。塡崎塞畎，不可勝錄。【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206A9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠚩</span>，亦作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206E7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛧</span>。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/525D.svg">                          <p>裂也。从刀从录。录，刻割也。录亦聲。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206A9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠚩</span>，剥或从卜。北角切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/525D_485.svg" /><span class="swbf">(剝)</span><span class="ywyy">裂也。</span><span class="swdz">衣部曰：裂，繒餘也。謂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239FB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣧻</span>破也。夏小正：二月剝鱓，以爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡔷</span>也。八月剝瓜，畜瓜之時也。剝棗。剝也者，取也。栗零。零也者，降也。零而後取之，故不言剝也。按剝鱓者，謂殘其皮：剝瓜棗者，謂殘其實，其用一也。皮部曰：剝取獸革。與剝鱓合。孔子易傳曰：致飾，然後通則<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧗊</span>矣，故受之以剝。剝者，剝也。物不可以終<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧗊</span>，剝竆上反下，故受之以復也。按此是剝訓<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧗊</span>，裂則將<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧗊</span>矣。豳風假剝爲攴：八月剝棗。毛曰：剝，擊也。音義云：普卜反。故知剝同攴也。小正傳云取，毛傳云擊，此後人訓詁必密於前人也。</span><span class="ywyy">从刀彔。彔，刻也。</span><span class="swdz">說從彔之意。彔下云：刻木彔彔也。破裂之意。</span><span class="ywyy">彔亦聲。</span><span class="swdz">北角切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰剝，割也。</span><span class="swdz">此别一義，與上義相通。按此篆解說，合二徐本及尚書泰誓正義宋刻本參定。</span></p>
