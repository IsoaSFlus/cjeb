                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">替</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>替</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本作&ldquo;竝&rdquo;。从竝，白声。竝( <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;ng</span>)，二人并立。本义:废弃)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[discard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竝，废也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗字作替。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不替</span><span class="diczx2">孟明</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勿替引之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胡不自替。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;召》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唯独居思念前世之崇替。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">謇朝谇而夕替。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:久替(长期废弃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 停止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荐可而替不。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;去也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:替手(停手)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 消亡，泯灭 <span class="encs">[die out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君之冢嗣其替乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;灭也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:替罪(诛灭有罪者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 衰微;衰落 <span class="encs">[decline]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风颓化替。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">房玄龄</span><span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兴替(兴盛与衰亡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 代替<span class="encs">[take the place of;substitute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从此替爷征。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:我替他去;替力(代为出力);替当(顶替充当);替归(轮替而归);替天行道(代行上天的意旨);替罪(代人受过);替下来;替我一下</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 衰亡<span class="encs">[fall]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:朝代兴替</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 松弛;怠惰 <span class="encs">[lax]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是乎下陵上替。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:替懈(替解;懈怠)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>替</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 替身，替代别人的人。对正身而言。常指代人受罪的人 <span class="encs">[a double]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:替僧(明朝，代替皇帝出家的僧人);替头(替身)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;屉&rdquo;。抽屉 <span class="encs">[drawer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及薨，帝常思见之，遂为通替棺，欲见则引替睹尸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南史&middot;宋&middot;孝武殷淑传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">十娘</span>取钥开锁，内皆抽替小箱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>替</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">介</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 为;给 <span class="encs">[for;on behalf of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:替人受罪;替我去商店买东西</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同，和 <span class="encs">[and]</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>替</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p>次、批、起。</p>
