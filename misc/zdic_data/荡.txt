						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>荡</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">蕩</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>清除，弄光：～平。扫～。～除。倾家～产。</li><li>洗涤：洗～。涤～。～口（漱口）。</li><li>摇动：摇～。～漾。～桨。</li><li>行为不检，不受约束：～逸。放～。浪～。～子。～妇。狂～。～检逾闲。</li><li>四处走动：游～。逛～。</li><li>广大平坦的样子：浩～。</li><li>浅水湖：芦花～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>荡</strong></p><p>蕩 <span class="dicpy">dàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从皿，从汤，汤亦声。本义:洗涤)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wash;wash away]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荡亡秦之毒螫。——</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《西都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 动;摇动;震动 <span class="encs">[move;swing;sway]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此四六者不荡，胸中则正。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·庚桑楚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下不能荡也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以荡上心。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·季春纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以其休止不荡也。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">从流漂荡，任意东西。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">波心荡，冷月无声。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">姜夔</span><span class="smcs">《扬州慢》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如;荡突(冲撞);荡心(摇荡心神);荡摇(动摇);荡荡悠悠(摇荡、飘流的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 清除 <span class="encs">[clear away]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荡平塞表。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《表论田畴功》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:荡风(两广的一种风俗。女婿在未见岳父岳母前，依俗饮一大杯酒。有荡除行路风寒之意);荡除(消灭)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 毁坏;破坏 <span class="encs">[ruin;damage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">家乡既荡尽，远近理亦齐。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《无家别》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:荡产(破产，耗尽财产);荡散(消失;毁败);荡灭(毁灭;消灭)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 诱惑;迷惑 <span class="encs">[entice;seduce;lure]</span>。如:荡心(惑乱心志);荡惑(迷惑)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 宽恕 <span class="encs">[forgive]</span>。如:荡宥(宽恕;原谅)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 冲撞;冲杀;触碰 <span class="encs">[collide;bump;rush ahead]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稍近，益狎，荡倚冲冒。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span
