                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>按</strong> <span class="dicpy">àn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/àn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，安声。本义:用手向下压或摁)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[press down]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按，下也。谓手抑物使下。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是天子乃按辔徐行。——<span class="smcs">《史记·绛侯周勃世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">项王</span>按剑而跽曰。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缇骑按剑而前。——</span><span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则以一平板按其面。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:按电钮;按铃;按手印</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 控制;抑止 <span class="encs">[control;restrain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以按徂旅。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·皇矣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按强助弱。——<span class="smcs">《管子·霸言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵简子</span>按兵而不动。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·召类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何不按兵束甲。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若</span><span class="diczx2">街亭</span>有兵把守，即当按兵不行。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:按下此事不提;按不住心头怒火</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 考察，考验 <span class="encs">[examine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按诛五人。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">影胡公</span>按部。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:按杀(查实处死);按讨(查验究治)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 打 <span class="encs">[strike]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按管调弦于茶坊酒肆。——</span><span class="diczx2">孟元老</span><span class="smcs">《东京梦华录序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:按曲(击节唱曲);按键;按拍(打拍子);按板(拍击板眼);按鼓;按丝竹;按乐;按风琴</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 按摩，用手在人身上推、捏、揉 <span class="encs">[massage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其治导引按蹻。——<span class="smcs">《素问·异法方宜论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按金驹，立长沟，枇杷落尽茱萸秋。——</span><span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《拟古乐府》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:按跷(抚摩导引)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 巡视 <span class="encs">[perambulate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂西定河南地，按</span><span class="diczx2">榆溪</span>旧塞。——<span class="smcs">《史记·卫将军骠骑列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:按察(巡行视察);巡按(官名);按察司(官名。提刑按察使司的简称。明、清时一省的司法和检察机关);按临(按察考核;巡行到达)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“安”</span>。安置;安定 <span class="encs">[emplace;resettle;arrange for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余悉除去</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>法，吏民皆按堵如故。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按抚柔氐三千余落，拨徙以实关中。——<span
