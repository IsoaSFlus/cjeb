                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>瘠</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>瘦弱：瘦～。</li>
	<li>土地不肥沃：～土。～田。～薄。贫～。</li>
	<li>薄，简约：&ldquo;若是则～，～则不足欲&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> thin, emaciated; barren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> mager, hager</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> maigre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瘠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瘠</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;ng</span>)，表示与疾病有关，脊声。本义:身体瘦弱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。或作&ldquo;膌&rdquo;<span class="encs">[lean;thin and weak;wispy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牛虽瘠，偾于豚上，其畏不死?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>崇门之巷人服丧而毁甚瘠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;内储说上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长短丰瘠。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老年如脊牛。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瘠弱(瘦弱;衰弱);瘠疲(瘦弱疲惫);瘠色(容貌忧戚憔悴);瘠牛偾豚(瘦瘠的牛仆倒在小猪身上，小猪必死。比喻无德的大国权势虽衰，欺凌羸弱的小国，小国也会灭亡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 贫困 <span class="encs">[poor;impoverished]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫民殷国弱，民瘠国强者，未之有也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;陆逊传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瘠馁(贫困饥饿);瘠贫(贫困);瘠亡(贫穷衰亡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 瘠薄，不肥沃 <span class="encs">[barren;infertile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">处沃土则逸，处瘠土则劳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瘠土(瘠地。贫瘠不肥沃的土地);瘠田(瘠薄而不肥沃的田地);瘠卤(盐碱地。贫瘠的土地);瘠硗(多石不肥沃的土地);瘠壤(瘠土)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 菲薄;简约 <span class="encs">[humble;simple]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">送死不忠厚，不敬文，谓之瘠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瘠墨(俭约，刻薄。因墨家尚俭、薄葬，故有此称)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瘠</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 疾疫 <span class="encs">[disease]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:瘠瘵(因患病而瘦弱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 腐肉;腐烂的尸体 <span class="encs">[rotten corpse]</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中" target="_blank">午集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中&amp;bs=疒" target="_blank">疒</a>】
                              瘠·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7620.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㾊【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>秦昔切，音籍。【說文】瘦也。本作膌。今作瘠。【廣韻】病也。【易&middot;說卦】乾爲瘠馬。【疏】瘠馬，骨多也。【左傳&middot;襄二十九年】何必瘠魯以肥<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233CC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣏌</span>。【書&middot;微子】多瘠罔詔。【傳】紂故使民多瘠病，而無詔救之者。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;樂記】使其曲直繁瘠，廉肉節奏，足以感動人之善心而已矣。【疏】瘠謂省約。</p>
<p>又姓。</p>
<p>又音漬。【前漢&middot;食貨志】故堯有九年之水，湯有七年之旱，而國亡捐瘠者，以畜積多而備先具也。【註】孟康曰：肉腐爲瘠。蘇林曰：瘠，音漬。師古曰：瘠，瘦病也。言無相棄捐而瘦病者耳，不當音漬也。</p>
<p>又或作胔。【前漢&middot;婁敬傳】羸胔老弱。</p>
