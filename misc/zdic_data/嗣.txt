                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>嗣</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sì.mp3"></a></span> ㄙˋ</span></p><ol><li>接续，继承：～后（以后）。～岁（来年）。～响（继承前人之业，如回声之相应）。～徽（继承先人的美德、声誉）。</li><li>子孙：后～。～子。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to connect, inherit; descendants, heirs</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> succéder à,hériter de,descendant,postérité</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">嗣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>嗣</strong> <span class="dicpy">sì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从册，从口，司声。册，帝王对臣下封土授爵的文书。口，下令。甲骨文从大，从子，从册，会意。大子，即嫡长子。本义:诸侯传位给嫡长子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[inherit sovereignty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">嗣，诸侯嗣国也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">舜</span>让于德，弗嗣。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将使嗣位。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:嗣王(继位之王);嗣君(继位的君主);嗣主(继位的国君);嗣位(继承君位);嗣皇(继位的帝王);嗣圣(新继位的皇帝);嗣临(继登皇位)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 继承;接续 <span class="encs">[inherit;continue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">嗣，继也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>乃嗣兴。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“继也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其弟嗣书。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太史书曰:<span class="yhcs">“</span><span class="diczx2">崔杼</span>弑其君。”</span>崔子杀之。其弟嗣书，而死者二人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十五年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今吾嗣为之十二年，几死者数矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:嗣位(继承君位);嗣产(继承子产的事业);嗣徽(继承善美的德行);嗣守(继承并遵守和保存);嗣芳(承继前贤者);嗣承(继承);嗣封(承袭先人而受封);嗣统(继承皇位);嗣武二祖(继承汉高祖、汉光武二祖的事业);嗣音(继续传其音信);嗣事(继续从事);嗣奉(继前人而敬受)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 过继 <span class="encs">[adopt]</span>。如:嗣母(继母)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“司”</span>。主持，掌管 <span class="encs">[take charge of;direct]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阳人有夏商之嗣典，有周室之师旅。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若天之嗣，其事不可识，百姓浅然不识其邻。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·哀公》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远近高下，各得其嗣。——<span class="smcs">《管子·版法》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>嗣</strong> <span class="dicpy">sì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 君位或职位的继承人 <span class="encs">[inheritor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今又不礼于</span><span class="diczx2">卫</span>之嗣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公七年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“新君也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">祁奚</span>请老，晋侯问嗣焉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">长</span>当就国也。(王)立嗣子 <span class="diczx2">融</span>从 <span
