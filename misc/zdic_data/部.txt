						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>部</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>全体中的一份：～分（“<span class="diczx3">分</span>”读轻声）。外～。腹～。局～。全～。～件。～位（位置）。</li><li>机关企业按业务范围分设的单位：外交～。编辑～。～队（军队）。</li><li>具有统属关系：所～五十人。～下。～将。～属。～首。～落（<span class="dicpy">luò</span>）。</li><li>安置安排：～署。</li><li>量词：一～小说。三～汽车。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">部</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>部</strong> <span class="dicpy">bù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从邑，( <span class="dicpy">fǒu</span>)声。从<span class="yhcs">“邑”</span>表示与行政区域有关。本义:古汉地名。约在今甘肃省天水、清水、秦安、两当、礼县、徽县一带)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Bu，an ancient place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">部，</span><span class="diczx2">天水</span><span class="diczx2">狄部</span>。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代军事编制单位，后泛指部队，军队 <span class="encs">[army]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">瑜</span>为前部大督。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·周瑜传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:部阵(队伍);部校(部队;泛称武官);部将(部下的武官;军中偏将)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 中央行政官署。有时也称一般衙署、地方行政官署 <span class="encs">[central government;board;department]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">还部白府君。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:部费(向吏部行贿以便批准任职所用的钱);部尺(由工部制造的标准尺);部曹(旧指京师各部司官);部堂(清代各部尚书、侍郎称部堂。又各省总督多带兵部尚书衔者，也叫部堂);国防部;外交部;财政部</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 地区。古代监察或行政区域名 <span class="encs">[area;district]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">河</span>东二十八县，分为两部。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·尹翁归传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 部落 <span class="encs">[tribe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曾祖</span><span class="diczx2">莫护跋</span>， <span class="diczx2">魏</span>初率其诸部入居 <span class="diczx2">辽西</span>。——<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 分类，门类 <span class="encs">[category]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">名属教坊第一部。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:部索(分部搜索);部居(分门别类的排列);古籍分经、史子、集四部</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 部分 <span class="encs">[part;section]</span>。如:上部;南部</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 单位 <span class="encs">[unit;department]</span>。如:编辑部;批发部</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 军队领导机构 <span class="encs">[headquarters]</span>。如:总参谋部;总政治部;总后勤部;师部</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>部</strong> <span class="dicpy">bù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 统率 <span class="encs">[command]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉王</span>部五诸侯兵。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:部人(被统率的人);部握(统管，掌握);部率(统率)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 安排，布置 <span class="encs">[arrange]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">部署诸将。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“分部而署置。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
