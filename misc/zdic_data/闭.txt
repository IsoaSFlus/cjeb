						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>闭</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">閉</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>关，合：封～。～门。～合。～关锁国。～门思过。～月羞花。</li>    <li>结束，停止：～会。～幕。～市。</li>    <li>堵塞，不通：～气。～塞（<span class="dicpy">sè</span>）。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>闭</strong></p><p>閉 <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从门，从才。<span class="yhcs">“才”</span>是用来闭门的东西。本义:关门，把门合闭起来)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shut the door]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闭，阖门也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">门常闭。——<span class="smcs">《墨子号令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先王以至，日闭关。——<span class="smcs">《易·象传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坚闭门而不出。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天运》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">门已闭矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">古语所谓闭门造车，出门合辙，盖言其法之同也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:闭扫(即闭门欲扫。指闭门谢客);闭影(关门不与外界往来)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指闭合，合拢 <span class="encs">[close;shut]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愿</span><span class="diczx2">陈子</span>闭口，毋复言。——<span class="smcs">《史记·张仪列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:闭目;闭嘴;闭合电路;闭口结舌;闭卷(答题时不能查阅有关资料的考试方法)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 壅塞不通 <span class="encs">[stop up;obstruct]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闭而不通。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:闭绝(堵塞;杜绝);闭心(闭绝欲望)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 禁绝 <span class="encs">[prohibit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">礼者，所以闭淫也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·乐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:闭淫(禁绝邪淫);闭钳(禁制);闭籴(禁止输出，封闭其买入米谷的通道)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 停止;结束 <span class="encs">[stop;end]</span>。如:闭歇(停业);闭经;闭会</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>闭</strong></p><p>閉 <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 门闩的孔 <span class="encs">[hole in bolt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不谨其闭，不固其门，虎乃将存。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·主道》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:键闭</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古时称立秋、立冬为<span class="yhcs">“闭”</span>，意即开始闭藏的气节 <span class="encs">[the Beginning of Autumn or Winter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡分、至、启、闭，必书云物。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/闭谷">闭谷</a></span><span class="crefe"><a                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閉·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：3</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9589.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】博計切【集韻】【韻會】必計切【正韻】必弊切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音嬖。【說文】闔門也。从門才，才所以歫門也。會意，亦像形。俗从下，非。【禮·月令】修鍵閉。【註】鍵，牡。閉，牝也。【疏】何氏曰：鍵是門扇，後樹兩木，穿上端爲孔，閉者將扃關門，以內孔中。【左傳·桓五年】閉蟄而烝。【註】建亥之月，昆蟲閉戸。【釋文】必計反。</p><p>又【左傳·桓五年】凡分至啟閉，必書雲物。【註】閉立秋立冬。</p><p>又【廣韻】掩也。【書·大誥】予不敢閉于天降威用。【傳】言我不敢閉絕天所下威用而不行。【史記·樂書】禮者，所以閉淫也。</p><p>又【淮南子·道應訓】劉氏奪之，若轉閉錘。【註】閉錘，格也。</p><p>又【博雅】閉閉，盛也。</p><p>又【玉篇】塞也。【易·坤卦】天地閉。【疏】謂二氣不相交通，天地否閉。</p><p>又藏也。【淮南子·天文訓】萬物閉藏。</p><p>又【詩·秦風】竹閉緄縢。【傳】閉，紲也。【正字通】弓檠也。</p><p>又服式也。【釋名】反閉襦之小者也，却向著之，領反于背後，閉其襟也。</p><p>又【廣韻】方結切【集韻】【韻會】必結切【正韻】必列切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A500.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪔀</span>。義同。</p><p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9589.svg">                          <p>闔門也。从門；才，所以歫門也。博計切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D75.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨵵</span>門也。</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D75.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨵵</span>下曰：閉也。與此爲轉注。又閟下曰：閉門也。按左傳高其閈閣，疑閈乃閉字之誤。<span class="jieshi">从門。</span>句。<span class="jieshi">才所㠯歫門也。</span>从門而又象撑歫門之形，非扌字也。博計切。十五部。玉裁按扌不成字，云所以距門，依許全書之例，當云扌象所以歫門之形乃合。而無象形之云，則當是合二字會意。攷王逸少書黃庭經三用<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28CF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨳰</span>字，卽今閈也。而中从午，葢許書本作从門午，午，所以歫門。舂字下曰：午，杵省也。然則此午亦是杵省，歫門用直木如杵然。轉寫失眞，乃昧其本始矣。                          </div>
