                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>疤</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄚˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>伤口或疮平复以后留下的痕迹：伤～。疮～。～痕。</li>
	<li>器物上像疤的痕迹：坛子磕了一个～。</li>
</ol>

<p>◎ <strong>疤</strong> <span class="dicpy">bā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;ng</span>)，巴声。从&ldquo;疒&rdquo;表示与疾病有关。本义:疮疤)</p>

<p><span class="diczx3">如:伤疤;疮疤</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">如:盆上有个疤</span></p>
