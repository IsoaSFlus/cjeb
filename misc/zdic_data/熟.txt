                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">熟</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>熟</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˊ</span> 又 <span class="dicpy">sh&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>食物烧煮到可吃的程度：饭～了。</li>
	<li>植物的果实或种子长成，又特指庄稼可收割或有收成：成～。瓜～蒂落。</li>
	<li>程度深：～睡。～思（经久而周密地思考）。深思～虑。</li>
	<li>做某种工作时间长了，精通而有经验：～练。娴～。～习。～能生巧。</li>
	<li>习惯，常见，知道清楚：～人。～悉。～记（强记）。轻车～路。</li>
	<li>经过加工炼制的：～铁。～皮子。～石膏。～石灰。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>熟</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &mdash;&mdash;口语，义同&ldquo;熟&rdquo;（ <span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> ）</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>熟</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，孰( <span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本作&ldquo;孰&rdquo;。&ldquo;熟&rdquo;是后起字。本义:煮熟，食物烹煮到可吃的程度)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cooked]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熟治万物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span>。疏:&ldquo;谓烹煮。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宰夫胹熊蹯不熟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:熟末(指煮得烂熟的食物);熟切店(卖熟肉的店铺);熟水(开水);熟献(指烹熟的上献祭品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 谷物、水果或微生物等成熟 <span class="encs">[harvest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秋，大熟，未收。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;金滕》</span></span></p>
