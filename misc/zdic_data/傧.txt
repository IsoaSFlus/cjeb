						<p>● <strong>傧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">儐</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>接引宾客：～相（<span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;ng </span>）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> entertain guests</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> demoiselle d'honneur (antiquité)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">傧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>傧</strong></p>
<p>儐、擯 <span class="dicpy">bīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，宾声。本义:接引客人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[guide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">傧，导也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 字亦作擯。注:&ldquo;出接宾曰摈。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摈者，请期。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;在主人曰摈，在客曰介。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿为上摈，大夫为承摈，士为绍摈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若有祭祀、宾客、丧纪，则摈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;内小臣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌九仪之宾客摈相之礼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;秋官&middot;司仪》</span>。注:&ldquo;出接宾曰摈，入诏礼曰相。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:傧士(引导初为士者进见于王);傧相(迎宾称傧，赞礼称相)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 陈列，摆 <span class="encs">[display]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">傧尔笾豆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;常棣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;摈&rdquo;。排斥，抛弃 <span class="encs">[repel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">傧厥亲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《逸周书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸儒生相与排傧，不容于齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;主父偃传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;宾&rdquo;。尊敬 <span class="encs">[respect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山川，所以傧鬼神也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>傧</strong></p>
<p>儐 <span class="dicpy">bīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 迎接客人的人 <span class="encs">[waiter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其摈于天子也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼下》</span>。疏:&ldquo;摈，谓天子接宾客之人也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">桓公</span>令傧者延而上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;小问》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">傧者以告</span><span class="diczx2">列子</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:傧相(婚礼中陪伴新郎新娘的男子和女子) 摈</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;n</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“傧”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/儐">儐</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八">卷八</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八&amp;swbs=人">人</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5110.svg">                          <p>導也。从人賓聲。擯，儐或从手。必刃切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5110_336.svg" /><span class="swbf">(儐)</span><span class="ywyy">導也。</span><span class="swdz">導者，導引也。周禮司儀注曰：出接賓曰擯。聘禮：卿爲上擯，大夫爲承擯，士爲紹擯。注曰：擯謂主國之君所使出接賓者也。士冠禮：擯者請期。注曰：擯者，有司佐禮者，在主人曰擯。按擯經典多作擯，史記作賓。廉藺列傳：設九賓於廷是也。○聘禮：賓用束錦儐勞者。又儐之如初。又儐之兩馬束錦。又無儐。凡言儐者九。鄭曰：上於下曰禮，敵者曰儐。上於下曰禮，謂如主國之禮賓是也。敵者曰儐，謂如儐勞者，儐歸饔餼者等是也。鄭據禮經字作儐，是以周禮司儀賓亦如之，賓使者如初之儀皆云賓當爲儐，易賓爲儐，取賓禮相待之義，非擯相之義也。然則合三禮訂之，擯相字當从手，賓禮字當从人。許儐擯合而一，云導也，與二禮及鄭說不合。劉昌宗說聘禮儐與擯同，雖本許，而令學者惑矣。今禮經石本版本於此九儐字內錯出擯字，非是。○又毛詩絲衣繹賓尸也，有司徹賓尸，經典釋文古本皆作賓，又無必刃反之音，而唐宋石本版本賓儐錯出，要之古無作儐尸者，此學者所當知也。○小雅：儐爾籩豆。傳曰：儐，陳也。</span><span class="ywyy">从人賓聲。</span><span class="swdz">必刄切。十二部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5110_337.svg" /><span class="swbf">(擯)</span><span class="ywyy">儐或从手。</span><span class="swdz">今經典作擯相字多從此。莊子徐無鬼注曰：擯，棄也。此義之窮則變也。擯之言屛也。</span></p>                          </div>
