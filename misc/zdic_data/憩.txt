<p>◎ <strong>憩</strong></p>
<p>憇、愒 <span class="dicpy">q&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 休息 <span class="encs">[rest;have a rest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">憩，息也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">召伯所憩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;甘棠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">策扶老以流憩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">憩书斋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:小憩;同作同憩;憩止(歇息，休息);憩休(歇息);憩泊(栖息);憩歇(休息，歇息)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">h&egrave;</span></p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6112.svg">                          <p>息也。从心曷聲。去例切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今別作憩，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6112_2020.svg" /><span class="swbf">(愒)</span><span class="ywyy">息也。</span><span class="swdz">此休息之息。上文息篆訓喘息，其本義。凡訓休息者，引申之義也。釋詁及甘棠傳皆曰：憩，息也。憩者，愒之俗體。民勞傳又曰：愒，息也。非有二字也。又釋言曰愒，貪也。此愒字乃㵣之叚借，如左傳玩歲而愒日，許引作忨歲而㵣日。公羊傳不及時而葬曰愒。愒，急也。亦卽㵣字也。</span><span class="ywyy">从心曷聲。</span><span class="swdz">去例切。十五部。</span></p>                          </div>
