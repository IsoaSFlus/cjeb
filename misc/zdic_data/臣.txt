                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>臣</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chén  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chén.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>君主时代的官吏，有时亦包括百姓：～僚。～子。～服。君～。</li><li>官吏对君主的自称：“王必无人，～愿奉璧往使。”</li><li>古人谦称自己。</li><li>古代指男性奴隶：～仆。～虏。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">臣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>臣</strong> <span class="dicpy">chén</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chén.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象一只竖立的眼睛形。人在低头时，眼睛即处于竖立的位置，字形正表示了俯首屈从之意。本义:男性奴隶)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[male slave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣，事君者也。象屈服之形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仕于公曰臣。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事君不贰是谓臣。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣治烦去惑者也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣妾逋逃。——<span class="smcs">《书·费誓》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“臣妾，厮役之属也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣则左之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·少仪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓囚俘。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽臣虏之劳不苦于此矣。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:臣妾(古时对奴隶的称谓。男曰臣，女曰妾);臣役(泛指奴仆);臣御(臣妾仆御);臣宰(本指奴隶。后亦以称辅佐帝王的臣佐)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 国君所统属的众民 <span class="encs">[subject]</span>。如:臣庶(臣民);臣姓(群臣百姓)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 君主制时的官吏 <span class="encs">[official under a feudal ruler;subject]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今功臣名将，雁行有序。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侍卫之臣不懈于内。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:臣门如市(形容官宦门下钻营者极多，竟像集市一样热闹);臣下(臣子。君主制时代的官吏);臣人(臣下)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 君主制时的高级官员;大臣 <span class="encs">[minister]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侍中、尚书、长史、参军，此悉忠良死节之臣。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:忠臣;奸臣</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 古人自称 <span class="encs">[I]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古代大臣对君的自称</p><p><span class="diczx1">臣窃见先帝欲开西域。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·班超传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:臣下(古时官吏对君主的自称)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 对父的自称</p><p><span class="diczx1">始大人常以臣无赖。——<span class="smcs">《史记·高帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 对一般人的自称。表示自谦</p><p><span class="diczx1">臣少好相人，相人多矣，无如季相。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>臣</strong> <span class="dicpy">chén</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chén.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 役使 <span class="encs">[enslave]</span>。如:臣虏(奴役，役使)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 臣服 <span class="encs">[submit oneself to the rule of;acknowledge allegiance to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匈奴背叛不臣。——<span class="smcs">《盐铁论·本议》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而欲以力臣天下之主。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“服也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span>
