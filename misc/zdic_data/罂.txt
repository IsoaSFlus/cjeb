                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罂</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>罂</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">罌</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>〔～粟〕二年生草本植物，全株有白粉，花有红、紫、白等色，果实球形。未成熟时，果实中有白浆，是制鸦片的原料，果壳可入药，花供观赏。</li><li>古代大腹小口的酒器。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> long necked jar or bottle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Amphore, Tongefäß mit kleiner Öffnung  (S)​,Opiummohn  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> jarre,bouteille</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>罂</strong></p><p>罌、甖 <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大腹小口的瓦器。或作<span class="yhcs">“甇”</span> <span class="encs">[a round jar with a small opening]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罂，缶也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自关而西，</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>之旧都， <span class="diczx2">河</span><span class="diczx2">汾</span>之间，其大者谓之，其中者谓之;自关而东， <span class="diczx2">赵</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>之郊，谓之瓮，或谓之罂。——<span class="smcs">《方言五》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">用瓦木罂，容十升以上者，五十步而十，盛水且用之。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:罂盎(泛指盛酒器);罂瓶(泛指盛酒器)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指小口大腹的瓶 <span class="encs">[bottle with small mouth and big belly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">火药三百罂。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:罂缶(大腹小口的瓶)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 植物名 <span class="encs">[opium poppy]</span>。罂粟科，罂粟属，二年生草本，高约 1.5米</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/罂粟">罂粟</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7F4C.svg">                          <p>缶也。从缶賏聲。烏莖切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">缶也。</span>缶、太平御覽作？。今按作缶爲是。許意缶罌？一物也。方言。㼚瓭？䍃㽀㼻甀瓮瓿甊㽈。甖也。自關而西、晉之舊都、河汾之閒、其大者謂之甀。自關而東、趙魏之郊謂之瓮。或謂之甖。甖其通語也。甀卽？。甖卽罌。瓦部曰。罌謂之㼰。瓮、罌也。康瓠、破罌也。史、漢淮陰侯傳曰。信乃益爲疑兵。乘船欲渡臨晉。而伏兵從夏陽以木罌缻渡軍。襲安邑。木罌缶者、以木爲罌缶狀。實兵於其中。不欲人知。故不爲盆狀。韋昭云。以木爲器如罌缻以渡軍。無船、且尙密也。韋說是也。罌缶器之大者。<span class="jieshi">从缶。賏聲。</span>烏莖切。十一部。                          </div>
