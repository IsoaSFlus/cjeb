						<p>● <strong>彝</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代盛酒的器具，亦泛指古代宗庙常用的祭器：～器。～鼎。～尊。</li>
	<li>常理，法理：～训（指长辈对后辈的日常训诲）。～伦。～常。～章。</li>
	<li>中国少数民族，主要分布于四川省、云南省和贵州省：～族。～人。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">彝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>彝</strong></p>
<p>彜 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，廾( <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span>)持米，彑( <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span>)声。为双手捧丝、米奉献神灵。据甲骨文，象双手捧鸡奉献之意。本义:古代祭祀时常用的礼器的总称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sacrificial vessels]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">官司彝器。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:彝俎(泛指礼器);彝鼎(泛指古代祭祀用的鼎、尊等礼器);彝斝(泛指古代祭祀用的酒器);彝簋(泛指古代祭祀所用的礼器)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 常规 <span class="encs">[cardinal principle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民之秉彝，好是懿德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 彝族，中国少数民族 <span class="encs">[the Yi nationality]</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>彝</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 常 <span class="encs">[ordinary]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:彝序(亦作&ldquo;彝叙&rdquo;。常道);彝典(常典);彝制(常制);彝命(常命。指祖宗遗训);彝法(常法，定法);彝轨(常轨);彝章(常典;旧典);彝教(常教);彝理(常理;正道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 不变的，固定的 <span class="encs">[permanent]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:彝监(永恒不变之龟监);彝议(不变之论);彝准(固定的制度);彝教(永久不变的教化);彝言(指永久传世之言);彝化(永久的教化)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 经常 <span class="encs">[day-go-day]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皇极之敷言，足彝是训。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
