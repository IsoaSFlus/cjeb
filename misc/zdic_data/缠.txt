                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>缠</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">纏</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>绕，围绕：～绕。～绑。～缚。</li>
	<li>搅扰；牵绊：～绵。～磨（<span class="dicpy">m&oacute; </span>）。～搅。纠～。琐事～身。</li>
	<li>应付：这个人真难～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wrap, wind around; tie, bind</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufspulen  (V)​,winden, wickeln  (V)​,herumwickeln, einwickeln ,Rolle,Spule</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> enrouler,entourer,emmêler,mêler,être retenu,importuner,ennuyer</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缠</strong></p>
<p>纏 <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸，廛( <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:围绕，缠绕)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[enwind;wrap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缠，绕也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣有所与供儋缠采薪者九方鄄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;道应》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">动胃缠缘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;扁仓传》</span>。正义:&ldquo;谓脉缰绕胃也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">野田生葡萄，缠绕一支蒿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《葡萄歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缠裹(缠绕裹束;衣著，装束)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 纠缠;搅扰 <span class="encs">[be tangled up;pester]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蛮缠(不讲道理地纠缠);缠讼(纠缠词讼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 应付;对付 <span class="encs">[deal with]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这人真难缠</span></p>
