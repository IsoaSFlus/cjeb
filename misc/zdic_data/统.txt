                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">统</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>统</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">統</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄨㄥˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>总括，总起来：～一。～率（<span class="dicpy">shu&agrave;i</span>）。～帅。～摄（统辖）。～考。～筹。～战。～共。～购～销。</li>
	<li>事物的连续关系：系～。血～。传（<span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;n </span>）～。体～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> govern, command, control; unite</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Zusammenhang, System  (S)​,Tong  (Eig, Fam)​,ganz, gesamt, all</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tout,ensemble,unifier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">统</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>统</strong></p>
<p>統 <span class="dicpy">tǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，充声。本义:丝的头绪)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[main threads of silk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">统，纪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茧之性为丝，然非得工女煮以热汤而抽其统纪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泰族训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:统绪(头绪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 世代相继的系统 <span class="encs">[interconnected system]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求其统类。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;解蔽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:皇统(世代相传的帝系);道统;传统;统承(继承统绪);统系(系统);统贯(系统;条贯);统嗣(帝统的嗣续关系)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 纲纪，准则 <span class="encs">[principle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忠信以为质，端悫以为统。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;臣道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:体统(体制、格局、规矩等);统纪(纲纪);统类(纲纪和条例)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 鞋袜等的筒状部分 <span class="encs">[the tube-shaped part of shoe or stocking]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:袖统(衣袖筒子);统袜(长筒袜);长统马靴;短统女袜</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> <span class="encs">[地质]</span>∶在&ldquo;世&rdquo;的时间内形成的地层叫&ldquo;统&rdquo; <span class="encs">[series]</span>。统的名称是在纪的名称上增加上、中、下等字样。如:上泥盆统;中泥盆统;上二叠统;下二叠统</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 首领 <span class="encs">[chieftain]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:统帅</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 本 <span class="encs">[base]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:万物之统(万物之本)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>统</strong></p>
<p>統 <span class="dicpy">tǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 主管;率领 <span class="encs">[command]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若其所以统之，则无以异于</span><span class="diczx2">桀</span><span class="diczx2">纣</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;强国》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反国统万人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">统兵数万。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">统军略地。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:统会(统率会合);统兵数万;统事(统理事务);统楫(总揽，统领);统监(统率监督;驻于保设国或属地的长官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 总括;合而为一;统一 <span class="encs">[gather into one;unite]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大一统也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">统楫群元。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;儿宽传》</span></span></p>
