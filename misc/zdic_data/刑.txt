                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>刑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>对犯罪的处罚：～罚。～法。～律。～事。执～。服～。</li>	<li>特指对犯人的体罚：～讯。受～。～具。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> punishment, penalty; law</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bestrafung, Strafe  (S)​,Xing  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> peine,torture</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刑</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[kill;cut;dig]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利用刑人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;蒙》</span>。注:&ldquo;兑折为刑。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑人之父子也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;顺说》</span>。注:&ldquo;杀也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀戮之谓刑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;二柄》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阴为刑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;本命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阴将始刑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;音律》</span>。注:&ldquo;杀也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑人如恐不胜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑马作誓。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">膑</span>至， <span class="diczx2">庞涓</span>恐其贤于己，疾之，则以法刑断其两足而黥之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刑牛(古代盟誓时作牺牲用的牛);刑牲(古时为了祭祀或盟约而杀牲畜);刑马(古代结盟要杀马歃血，立誓为信，称&ldquo;刑马&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 惩罚 <span class="encs">[punish torture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑其长吏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五人之当刑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼于刑者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从而刑之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刑吏(行刑的吏卒);刑民(受刑之民);刑臣(古时指受过宫刑的阉人。即后来的宦官，太监)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 征讨 <span class="encs">[go on a punitive expedition]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:刑殄(征讨消灭)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刑</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 刑罚 <span class="encs">[penalty,punishment;torture;corporal punishment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以五刑纠万民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑者，恶之末也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;心术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五刑者，五常之鞭策也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑者，惩恶而禁后者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说苑&middot;政理》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">严断刑罚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公六年》</span>。疏:&ldquo;对文则加罪为刑，收赎为罚。散则刑罚通也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">论其刑赏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怒而滥用刑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因摸地上刑械。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;形&rdquo;。形容，形体 <span class="encs">[appearance;body;form]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死生因天地之刑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;越语》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">刑于寡妻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
