						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>暮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄨˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>傍晚，太阳落山的时候：日～。～气。～色。日～途穷。～鼓晨钟。</li>    <li>晚，将尽：～春。～年。～节。～齿（晚年）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>暮</strong> <span class="dicpy">mù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，莫声。古字作<span class="yhcs">“莫”</span>，像太阳落到草丛中，表示天将晚。<span class="yhcs">“暮”</span>为后起字。本义:日落时，傍晚)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dusk;sunset;evening]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暮者，晚也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·明雩》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝三而暮四。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恐美人之迟暮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旦辞爷娘去，暮宿黄河边。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今朝至，暮变。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·难一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至莫夜月光明。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暮投</span><span class="diczx2">石壕村</span>，有吏夜捉人。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:暮天(傍晚的天空);暮雨(傍晚的雨);暮途(傍晚的路程);暮烟(傍晚的烟霭);暮暮(每天傍晚);暮鼓(鼓楼晚间报时的鼓声);暮暗(日落天暗)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 夜晚 <span class="encs">[the end]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枝封暮雪。——</span><span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《桐赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暮春者，春服既成，冠者五六人。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:暮月(一季的末月);暮末(犹末世);暮冬(冬末)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>暮</strong> <span class="dicpy">mù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 喻指年老，衰老 <span class="encs">[aged]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">藐是流离，至于暮齿。——</span><span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《哀江南赋序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:暮老(指年老);暮古(年老糊涂);暮志(晚年的志向);暮耋(指年老);暮齿(晚年);暮景(比喻垂老之年)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 迟;晚 <span class="encs">[late towards to the end of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫自念斯，学德未暮。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烈士暮年，壮心不已。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《步出夏门行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:暮迟(犹言不及时);暮生(父亲死后才出生)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/暮霭">暮霭</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
