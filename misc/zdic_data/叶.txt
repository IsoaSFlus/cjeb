                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>叶</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">葉</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄝˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>植物的营养器官之一：树～。菜～。～子。～落归根。一～知秋。一～蔽目（喻目光短浅，常被眼前细小事物所遮蔽，看不到远处、大处。亦称&ldquo;一叶障目&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>像叶子的：铁～。百～窗。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/页">页</a>&rdquo;。</li>	<li>世代，时期：初～。末～。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>叶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄝˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>和洽，合：～韵。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">叶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叶</strong> <span class="dicpy">Sh&egrave;</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古邑名 <span class="encs">[She village]</span>。在今河南省叶县南。春秋时属楚领地。汉置县。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叶县凫(指得到皇上眷念的县令)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> (今读 <span class="dicpy">y&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> )姓。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叶公好龙(比喻表面上爱好某事物，实际上并不真爱好)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&egrave;;xi&eacute;</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叶</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 和洽。同&ldquo;协&rdquo; <span class="encs">[be harmonious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犀牛一角叶箜篌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">逢吉</span>与 <span class="diczx2">李程</span>同执政，不叶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叶律(叶韵;韵律);叶韵(协韵;押韵);叶中(犹适中);叶吉(和协吉祥);叶和(和睦;和合;和谐;亦作应和);叶契(协和，配合);叶泰(协和安泰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 协助;帮助 <span class="encs">[assist]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叶佐(辅佐，配合);叶齐(协力一致);叶赞(协同翊赞)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 合，共同 <span class="encs">[together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">股肱叶谋，爪牙宣力。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧五代史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叶力(协力;合力);叶心(同心);叶谋(合谋;共谋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叶</strong></p>
<p>葉 <span class="dicpy">y&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，枽( <span class="dicpy">y&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。植物的叶子。&ldquo;叶&rdquo;，同&ldquo;协&rdquo;，会意字，从十从口。本是两个字。本义:草木之叶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[leaf]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叶，</span>艸<span class="diczx1">木之叶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其叶为胡蝶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;天瑞》</span>。注:&ldquo;散也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枝叶未有害。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;荡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叶尖(植物学名词。叶片的尖端部位);叶序(植物学名词，叶在茎上的排列方式称为叶序);叶芽(植物学名词。与花芽相对。发育后成为茎、枝及叶的芽);叶基(植物学名词。叶片的下端靠近叶柄的部分)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 比喻轻小、轻飘像叶子的东西 <span class="encs">[leaf-like thing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万里风波一叶舟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《无题》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:百叶窗;一叶扁舟;肺叶</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 历史时期的分段 <span class="encs">[part of a historical period]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:唐朝末叶;20世纪中叶</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 世，代 <span class="encs">[generation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勋载二叶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《太尉刘宽碑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">继德前叶，清和挺懿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《豫州从事孔褒碑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">李势</span>在 <span class="diczx2">蜀</span>既久，承藉累叶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叶语(世代相传说)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 量词。轻薄物体的计量单位。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叶叶(片片)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古邑名 <span class="encs">[Ye village]</span>，在今河南叶县南</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公爱</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span>而忽 <span class="diczx2">崔骃</span>，此 <span class="diczx2">叶公</span>之好龙也。(叶公:本名沈诸梁，楚国贵族，叶是他的封地。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;崔骃传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
