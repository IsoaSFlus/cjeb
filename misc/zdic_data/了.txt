                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">了</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>了</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">liǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>明白，知道：明～。一目～然。</li>
	<li>完结，结束：完～。～结。</li>
	<li>在动词后，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">不</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">得</span>&rdquo;连用，表示可能或不可能：看不～。办得～。</li>
	<li>与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">得</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;不得&rdquo;前后连用，表示异乎寻常或情况严重：那还～得！</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>了</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">le  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/le.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄜ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>放在动词或形容词后，表示动作或变化已经完成：写完～。</li>
	<li>助词，用在句子末尾或句中停顿的地方，表示变化，表示出现新的情况：刮风～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">了</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>了</strong> <span class="dicpy">le</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/le.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈助〉</p>
<p>用在动词或形容词后，表示完成 <span class="encs">[used after the verb or adj. to indicate completion]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我已经问了老王;人老了，身体差了;头发白了;这双鞋太小了</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>了</strong> <span class="dicpy">le</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/le.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈语气〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示肯定语气 <span class="encs">[indicates future certainty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:明天又是星期六了;要过新年了，人们都很高兴</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示促进或劝止 <span class="encs">[indicates the speeding up or stopping]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:快躲了;别吵了!闪开了!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示感叹语气 <span class="encs">[indicates interj.]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:好了!别闹了!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">liǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>了</strong> <span class="dicpy">liǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。从子，无臂。小篆字象婴儿束其两臂形。初生的婴儿，往往束其两臂而裹之。本义:束婴儿两臂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 手弯曲 <span class="encs">[(of hand) bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">了，尥也。从子无臂象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，犹交也。手之挛曰了，胫之絷曰尥。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡物二股或一股结纠紾缚不直伸者曰了戾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span><span class="smcs">《说文解字注》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:了尥(手腿弯曲，引申指二物纠结绞缠不直伸的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;憭&rdquo;、&ldquo;悟&rdquo;。懂得，明白其意思<span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嫌人不了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释丘》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽神气不变，而心了其故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3"> 如:了法(领悟法理);了得事(懂行);了利(清楚，明白);了然(明白，清楚)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx2">小乔</span>初嫁了。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇&middot;赤壁怀古》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">明白、懂得。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「一目了然」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;陸游〈醉歌〉：「心雖<b>了</b>是非，口不給唯諾。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">完畢、結束。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「不了了之」、「責任未<b>了</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《老殘遊記&middot;第一九回》：「今日大案已<b>了</b>，我明日一早進城銷差去<b>了</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">完全。與否定語「不」、「無」等連用。有「一點也不&hellip;&hellip;」的意思。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「了無新意」、「了無生趣」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">與「得」、「不」等連用，表示可能或不可能。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「辦得<b>了</b>」、「寫不<b>了</b>」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">聰明、慧黠。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「小時了了，大未必佳。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
