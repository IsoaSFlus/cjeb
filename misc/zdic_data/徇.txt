                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>徇</strong></p><p>侚 <span class="dicpy">xùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从彳( <span class="dicpy">chì</span>)，旬声。<span class="yhcs">“彳”</span>与行有关。本义:迅速，敏捷) 同本义 <span class="encs">[quick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侚，疾也。从人，旬声，字亦误作徇。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黄帝幼而徇齐。——<span class="smcs">《史记·五帝纪》</span>。集解:<span class="yhcs">“疾也，失之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">年逾五十则聪明思虑徇通矣。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:徇通(敏捷通达);徇智(敏慧);徇齐(疾速)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>徇</strong> <span class="dicpy">xùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 巡行 <span class="encs">[perambulate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王乃徇师而誓。——<span class="smcs">《书·泰誓中》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将兵徇</span><span class="diczx2">蕲</span>以东。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:徇铺(巡查街市里巷);徇察(巡行察访)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 巡行示众 <span class="encs">[publicly expose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杀</span><span class="diczx2">颠颉</span>以徇于师。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以徇三军。——<span class="smcs">《史记·司马穰苴传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徇诸市。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:徇首(传首示众);徇罚(示众处罚)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 对众宣示 <span class="encs">[publicly expose;put before the public]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂斩队长二人以徇。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:徇众(示众)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 顺从、依从 <span class="encs">[comply with;give in to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知县又徇了</span><span class="diczx2">顾佥事</span>人情。——<span class="smcs">《古今小说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:徇世(随顺世俗);徇庇(徇私包庇);徇物(曲从世俗);徇俗(顺随时俗)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 舍身 <span class="encs">[sacrifice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陛下舍万乘之躯，而徇小义，古人所不取也。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:徇名(舍身以求名);徇利(不惜身以求利);徇命(捐躯)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 炫耀;夸示 <span class="encs">[show]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徇蹲鸱之沃，则以为世济阳九。——<span class="smcs">《文选·左思·吴都赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:徇陈(遍示列队部伍);徇铎(振铎以警众)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 谋求 <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今不恤士卒而徇其私，非社稷之臣。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span>
