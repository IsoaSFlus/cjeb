                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>噱</strong> <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大笑 <span class="encs">[loud laughter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">噱，大笑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谈关大噱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;叙传》</span>。注:&ldquo;噱噱，笑声也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:噱谈(谈笑);噱噱(嘻嘻。笑声);可发一噱;谈笑大噱</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>噱</strong> <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 口腔 <span class="encs">[mouth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沈沈容容，遥噱虖紘中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《羽猎赋》</span> <span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注:&ldquo;口内之上下名为噱，言禽兽奔走倦极，皆遥张噱吐舌于紘罔之中也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xu&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 〈方〉∶大笑 <span class="encs">[laugh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">噱，大笑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谈笑大噱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;叙传上》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>噱</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄩㄝˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>ju&eacute; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3479.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">大聲的笑。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「便發一<b>噱</b>」、「令人發<b>噱</b>」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">笑。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;皮日休〈吳中苦雨因書一百韻寄魯望〉詩：「入門且抵掌，大<b>噱</b>時碌碌。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
<div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>噱</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄒㄩㄝ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>xuē <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4147.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">令人發笑的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「噱頭」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                              噱·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5671.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】其虐切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】極虐切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音醵。【說文】大笑也。【廣韻】嗢噱，笑不止。【前漢&middot;敘傳】談笑大噱。【註】師古曰：大噱，笑聲也。</p><p>又【揚雄&middot;校獵賦】遙噱虖紭中。【註】師古曰：口內之上下名爲噱，言禽獸奔走倦極，皆遙張噱吐舌於紭罔之中也。</p>
