                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>密</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>稠，空隙小，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">稀</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">疏</span>&rdquo;相对：紧～。稠～。茂～。严～。～封。～布。～集。～匝匝。～锣紧鼓（喻正式活动前的积极而紧张的准备，亦作&ldquo;紧锣密鼓&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>关系近，感情好：～友。～亲。</li>
	<li>不公开，不公开的事物：秘～。保～。～谋。～电。～报。～使。～信。～诏。～旨。～探。</li>
	<li>精致，细致：精～。细～。致～。～实（细密结实）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">密</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>密</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，宓( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:形状象堂屋的山)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(意思是山多) <span class="encs">[hill resembling a hall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">密，山如堂者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">松柏之鼠，不知堂密之有美枞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尸子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 隐蔽的地方 <span class="encs">[concealed place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不窥密。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;少仪》</span>。注:&ldquo;隐曲处也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>密</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为&ldquo;比&rdquo;。稠密;多 <span class="encs">[dense;close;numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">密，稠 也，疏之对也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《增韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">密云不雨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;小畜卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">加密石焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语八》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以</span><span class="diczx2">陈</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>之密迩于 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十七年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">密布字印。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
