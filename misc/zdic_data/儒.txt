                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">儒</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>儒</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">r&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄨˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>指读书人：～生。腐～。通～（指博识多闻的大学者）。～林（儒者之林，旧指学术界）。～雅（读书人所具有的温文尔雅的风貌）。</li>	<li>中国春秋战国时代以孔子、孟子为代表的一个学派：～家。～教（即&ldquo;孔教&rdquo;）。～士。～术。～学。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/懦">懦</a>&rdquo;，懦弱。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">儒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>儒</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人， 需声。本义: 术士)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。春秋时从巫、史、祝、卜中分化出来的、熟悉诗书礼乐而为贵族服务的人 <span class="encs">[scholar server]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儒，术士之称。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">通天地之人曰儒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法言&middot;君子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能说一经者为儒生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;超奇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儒浩居而自顺者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;非儒下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四曰儒，以道得民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太宰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儒之言优也和也，言能安人能服人也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:儒师;儒仙</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 孔子学说(孔教); 孔子的和他的弟子一家的学说，其特点是强调对孝、仁、义、礼、智、信等基本道德的实践和修养，该学说在历史上已成为中国大部分伦理、教育、政治以及宗教的基础 <span class="encs">[Confucianism]</span> 。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:儒者(研究儒家学术的人);儒林(信奉儒家的大众或集团);儒教徒(信奉儒家学说的人); 儒化(儒家的教化);儒风(儒家承传下来的风范); 儒门(犹儒家); 儒相(博通儒学的宰相);儒玄(儒学与玄学);儒史(儒学和史学)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 儒生。中国哲学家孔子的门徒、学生; 孔子学说的信徒 <span class="encs">[Confucianist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女为君子儒，无为小人儒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:儒效(儒者的效用，儒学的功效);儒素(儒者的素行);儒侠(儒生和游侠); 儒素(儒者的素质，谓符合儒家思想的品格德行); 儒吏(儒生出身的吏员); 儒先(儒生); 儒效(儒者的作用;儒学的效用); 儒流(儒士之辈)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 旧时对学者或读书人的称呼 <span class="encs">[scholar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是儒墨之分也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儒者所争，尤在于名实。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谈笑有鸿儒，往来无白丁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:儒馆(文臣的居处);儒巾(古代读书人所戴的一种头巾);儒户(读书人的户籍);儒冠(古代儒生带的帽子);儒俊(才智出众的儒士);儒修(才能出众的儒士);儒服(儒衣，古代儒者的服饰);儒官(古代掌管学务的官员或官学教师)</span></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;懦&rdquo;。懦弱 <span class="encs">[cowardly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劳苦之事，则偷儒转脱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;脩身》</span></span></p>
