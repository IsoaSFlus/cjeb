                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>序</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>次第：顺～。秩～。次～。工～。程～。～数。</li>    <li>排列次第：～次。～列。</li>    <li>开头的，在正式内容之前的：～言。～跋。～曲。～幕。～论。</li>    <li>古代指送别赠言的文字。</li>    <li>指季节：四～。</li>    <li>古代地方办的学校：庠～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> series, serial order, sequence</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Einleitung; Vorwort  (S, Lit)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ordre,préface</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>序</strong> <span class="dicpy">xù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广，予声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，与房屋有关。本义:东西墙)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 堂屋的东西墙 <span class="encs">[east-west wall of a hall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">序，东西墙也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西序东向。——<span class="smcs">《书·顾命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奠爵于序端。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡饮酒礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">直东序。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宾升，立于序内，东方。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·燕礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆馔于西序下。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东西墙谓之序。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若陈衣于序东。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·丧大记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 正屋两侧东西厢房 <span class="encs">[wing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西厢踟蹰以闲宴，东序重深而奥秘。——<span class="smcs">《文选》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代学校的别名 <span class="encs">[locally-run school in ancient times]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">春秋以礼会民，而射于州序。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“序，州党之学也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">殷</span>曰序， <span class="diczx2">周</span>曰庠。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:序室(古代幼童读书处)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 次第，次序 <span class="encs">[order;sequence]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">言有序。——<span class="smcs">《易·艮》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与四时合其序。——<span class="smcs">《易·文言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">内官序当其夜。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日月忽其不淹兮，春与秋其代序。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长幼有序。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·君子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雁行有序。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:工序;次序;岁序;词序;语序;序秩(官序);序成(第等，次序)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 特指官吏等级系列中的位次 <span class="encs">[precedence]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卿大夫以序守之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 教育 <span class="encs">[education]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">神理共契，政序相参。——<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 季节 <span class="encs">[season]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">回忆海棠结社，序属清秋。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 序言 <span class="encs">[preface]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">序以建言，首引情本。——<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丐序于予。——</span><span
