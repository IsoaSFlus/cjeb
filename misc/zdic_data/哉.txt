						<p>● <strong>哉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄞ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>文言语气助词（①表疑问或反诘，相当于&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">吗</span>&rdquo;，&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">呢</span>&rdquo;，如&ldquo;何～？&rdquo;&ldquo;有何难～？&rdquo;②表感叹，相当于&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">啊</span>&rdquo;，如&ldquo;快～！&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/才">才</a>&rdquo;，开始。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">哉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>哉</strong> <span class="dicpy">zāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">助</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:语气词。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;言之间也。&rdquo;表感叹)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[alas]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示感叹，相当于&ldquo;啊&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸甚至哉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《步出夏门行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非人哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;方正》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦旦有是哉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变诈几何哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美哉，我少年</span><span class="diczx2">中国</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:远哉遥遥;快哉此风;命不易哉;谁不为之痛心哉</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 表示肯定语气，相当于&ldquo;啊&rdquo;。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:敢不畏君王哉;不若鸡犬哉</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表示疑问或反问，相当于&ldquo;呢&rdquo;或&ldquo;吗&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安知鸿鹄之志哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂独一琴哉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子&middot;千里马篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂主之子孙不必善哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂独一箸间哉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顾不如</span><span class="diczx2">蜀</span>鄙之僧哉?(哉，表示反问语气，相当于&ldquo;吗&rdquo;。)&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:以此为治，岂不难哉?吾又何能为哉?</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 表示测度，相当于&ldquo;吧&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我其试哉!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:我岂有所失哉!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 表示祈使语气，相当于&ldquo;吧&rdquo;</p>

	<li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/54C9.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕才【唐韻】祖才切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】將來切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音栽。【說文】言之閒也。【註】《論語》君子哉若人。是爲閒隔之辭也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】哉，閒也。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】哉，始也。【疏】哉者，古文作才。《說文》云才，草木之初也，以聲近，借爲哉始之哉。【書&middot;伊訓】朕哉自亳。</p><p>又【康誥】惟三月哉生魄。</p><p>又【玉篇】語助。【書&middot;堯典】僉曰：於，鯀哉。</p><p>又【韻會】柳宗元曰：疑辭也。【書&middot;益稷】禹曰：兪哉。【註】口然而心不然之辭也。</p><p>又與材通。【論語】無所取材。【註】古字材哉同。</p><p>又音載。【書&middot;武成】哉生明。【釋文】徐音載。</p><p>又【詩&middot;大雅】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>錫哉周。【傳】哉，載也。【疏】文王能布<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>大利，以賜子孫。於是又載行周道，致有天下。哉與載古字通。○按《左傳&middot;宣十五年》昭十年引《詩》，哉俱作載。</p><p>又鄭箋云：哉，始也。與毛傳異。</p><p>又叶將其切，音貲。【詩&middot;邶風】已焉哉。天實爲之，謂之何哉。</p><p>又【王風】君子于役，不知其期曷至哉。　【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20CC6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠳆</span>。</p><p>又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22994.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢦔</span>。</p>
