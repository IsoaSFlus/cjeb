                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">备</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>备</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">備</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">b&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄟˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>具备；完备：具～。完～。求全责～（要求全面、完美）。</li>	<li>预备；准备：预～。防～。准～。筹～。～案（向主管机关做书面报告，以备查考）。～注。～忘录。</li>	<li>设备：设～。装～。军～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">备</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>备</strong></p>
<p>備 <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，備( <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:谨慎、警惕)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cautious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事戒不虞曰知备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;小辨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">备者，国之重也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;七患》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且夫备有未至而设之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貌若傥荡不备，然心甚谨密。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;史丹传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:傥荡不备(行为无拘无束，不谨慎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 完备;齐备 <span class="encs">[complete]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">备，具也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">易之为书也，广大悉备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前人之述备矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众妙毕备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣心备焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请备述之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:德才兼备;完备(齐备)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;惫&rdquo;。疲乏;困顿 <span class="encs">[dog-tired;very tired]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修容而以言，耻食以上交，以避农战，外交以备，国之危也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;靳令》</span></span></p>

			<div class="gscdl">青林翠竹，四时俱<span>备</span>。</div><div class="gscdr">南北朝·陶弘景 《答谢中书书》</div><div class="gscdl">乃以宗正刘礼为将军，军霸上；祝兹侯徐厉为将军，军棘门；以河内守亚夫为将军，军细柳：以<span>备</span>胡。上自劳军。</div><div class="gscdr">汉·司马迁 史记. 绛侯周勃世家《周亚夫军细柳》</div><div class="gscdl">此则岳阳楼之大观也，前人之述<span>备</span>矣。</div><div class="gscdr">宋·范仲淹 《岳阳楼记》</div><div class="gscdl">同舍生皆被绮绣，戴朱缨宝饰之帽，腰白玉之环，左佩刀，右<span>备</span>容臭，烨然若神人；余则缊袍敝衣处其间，略无慕艳意。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div><div class="gscdl">今虽耄老，未有所成，犹幸预君子之列，而承天子之宠光，缀公卿之后，日侍坐<span>备</span>顾问，四海亦谬称其氏名，况才之过于余者乎？今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪稍之供，父母岁有裘葛之遗，无冻馁之患矣；坐大厦之下而诵《诗》《书》无奔走之劳矣；有司业、博士为之师，未有问而不告，求而不得者也；凡所宜有之书，皆集于此，不必若余之手录，假诸人而后见也。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
