                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>厥</strong> <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从厂( <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>，山崖)，欮( <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:石块) 同本义 <span class="encs">[stone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥，发石也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相柳之所，抵厥为泽谿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;海外北经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熒惑厥弛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李寻传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">和</span>之璧， <span class="diczx2">井里</span>之厥也，玉人琢之，为天子宝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>厥</strong> <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 病名。指突然昏倒、手足逆冷等症 <span class="encs">[be faint;fall into a coma]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凝于足者为厥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;六节藏象论》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓足逆冷也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:厥冷(指突然晕倒或手足逆冷);厥逆(指突然晕倒或手足厥冷;久久的头痛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> &ldquo;蹶&rdquo;的古字。摔倒;挫败 <span class="encs">[defeat]</span>。如:厥弛(摇动的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 磕头;触碰 <span class="encs">[kowtow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若崩厥角稽首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:角犀厥地;厥角(叩头)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 短，缺 <span class="encs">[be short of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以</span><span class="diczx2">汤</span><span class="diczx2">武</span>至尊严，不失肃祗; <span class="diczx2">舜</span>在假典，顾省厥遗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>厥</strong> <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 其;他的;她的 <span class="encs">[his;her]</span></p>
