						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>押</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yā.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄚˉ</span></p><ol><li>在文书、契约上签名或画记号：画～。签～。</li><li>把财物交给人作保证：～租。～金。～当（<span class="dicpy">dàng</span>）。典～。</li><li>拘留：看（<span class="dicpy">kān </span>）～。拘～。～禁。</li><li>跟随看管：～送。～运。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">压</span>”，用于“押宝”、“押队”、“押韵”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>押</strong> <span class="dicpy">yā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，甲声。本义:签署，在公文、契约上签字或画记号，以做凭信)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sign]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">随即押一纸公文。——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:押了花字(押花);押合同(签订合同);押榜(在官府文告上签字);押敕(签署诏令);押文字(签字);押字(签字);押署(签名;画押)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抵押，典当 <span class="encs">[pawn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暂且押四百两银子。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:押月(租赁房屋时先付给房东两个月租金，其中一个月的租金作为预付);押地(旧时作为抵押品的田地);押款(商业用语。以货物或有价证券等做抵押向银行或钱庄借款);押期(抵押期限);押价(典当或抵押的价格);押保(押出交保)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 拘禁，关押 <span class="encs">[take into custody]</span>。如:押番(专司捕盗衙役);押司(宋时协助地方官办理案牍、官司等事的属吏);拘押(拘禁;关押)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 押送 <span class="encs">[send under escort]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">押正旦带枷上。——<span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:押水(水路运输的押运人);押发(押送)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 下注 <span class="encs">[stake;bet]</span>。如:押注(押上赌注);押宝(猜测宝上所指的方向下注)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 陪伴 <span class="encs">[accompany]</span>。如:押燕(押宴);押伴(陪伴客使);押宴(陪伴宾客，主持宴会)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 执掌 <span class="encs">[take charge of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以六员分押尚书六曹。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·百官志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:押寨夫人(压寨夫人);押班(百官朝会时位在班列之首的官员)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“压”</span>。挤压 <span class="encs">[press]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初生子，便以石押其头使扁。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·东夷·辰韩传》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>押</strong> <span class="dicpy">yā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
