                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>贤</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">賢</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>有道德的，有才能的：～明。～德。～能。～良。～惠。～淑。～哲。～人。圣～。礼～下士。</li>	<li>敬辞，用于平辈或晚辈：～弟。～侄。～契（对弟子或朋友子侄辈的敬称）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> virtuous, worthy, good; able</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> sittliche und rechtschaffene Person  (S)​,gut und weise, würdig  (Adj)​,tugendhaft, pflichtgetreu, talentiert, befähigt  (Adj)​,bieder, würdig  (Adj)​,Xian  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sage,homme vertueux,vertueux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贤</strong></p>
<p>賢 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，与财富有关。本义:多财)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 有德行;多才能 <span class="encs">[virtuous;talented]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贤，多才也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以财分人之谓贤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;徐无鬼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">相如</span>既归， <span class="diczx2">赵王</span>以为贤大夫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲贤臣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贤声远达(好的名声远扬四处);贤歌(品行技艺都好的歌妓);贤才(德才并美之人);时贤(指当代有贤能有声望的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 对人的敬称 <span class="encs">[my dear;used for people of the same generation or lower generation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡与人言&hellip;&hellip;自叔父母以下，则加&ldquo;贤&rdquo;字。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分明是如贤所教，但是小生自小坐书斋，不谙其他生活。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新编五代史评话》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贤宰(知县);贤乔梓(尊称人家父子);贤竹林(魏晋间&ldquo;竹林七贤&rdquo;。后用作对他人叔姪的敬称);贤弟;贤甥</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 良，美善 <span class="encs">[fine;good]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东家有贤女。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多闻其贤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非不贤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贤否(好的与坏的);贤妻(贤淑的妻子);贤慧(称美女子善良而明大义)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 艰难;劳苦 <span class="encs">[work hard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大夫不均，我从事独贤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;北山》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贤</strong></p>
<p>賢 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 有才德的人;人才 <span class="encs">[virtuous person;talented person]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三曰进贤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太宰》</span>。注:&ldquo;有善行也。&rdquo;</span></p>

			<div class="gscdl">——《为政》子曰：“<span>贤</span>哉，回也！一箪食，一瓢饮，在陋巷，人不堪其忧，回也不改其乐。<span>贤</span>哉，回也！”</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孔子弟子及其再传弟子 《《论语》十二章》</div><div class="gscdl">宣室求<span>贤</span>访逐臣，贾生才调更无伦。</div><div class="gscdr">唐·李商隐 《贾生》</div><div class="gscdl">大道之行也，天下为公，选<span>贤</span>与能，讲信修睦。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·礼记 《大道之行也》</div><div class="gscdl">乃重修岳阳楼，增其旧制，刻唐<span>贤</span>今人诗赋于其上。属予作文以记之。</div><div class="gscdr">宋·范仲淹 《岳阳楼记》</div><div class="gscdl">非独<span>贤</span>者有是心也，人皆有之，<span>贤</span>者能勿丧耳。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孟子 《鱼我所欲也》</div><div class="gscdl">既加冠，益慕圣<span>贤</span>之道，又患无硕师、名人与游，尝趋百里外，从乡之先达执经叩问。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
