<p><span class="dicpy">miǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄠˇ</span></p>

<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> far, distant, remote; slight</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hintergründig, tiefdenkend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> profond,distant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">邈</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>邈</strong> <span class="dicpy">miǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，貌声。本义:距离遥远)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，通&ldquo;藐&rdquo; <span class="encs">[remote;be far away]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">振景拔迹，顾邈同列。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《谢平原内史表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">互相轩邈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:邈若山河(形容遥远得如隔山河);邈绵(遥远;旷远);邈廓(辽阔);邈远(遥远);邈然(遥远的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 久远。指时间长 <span class="encs">[long]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邈永(久远);邈殊(久远);邈绝(久远);邈如旷世(仿佛隔了久远的年代)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 渺茫，模糊不清 <span class="encs">[uncertain;not sure]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邈如(虚无飘渺的样子);邈思(遐想);邈渺(渺茫的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 高远，超卓 <span class="encs">[high and far;long-range]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邈行(高远超卓的行为);邈绝(高远);邈志(远大的志向)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>邈</strong> <span class="dicpy">miǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;藐&rdquo;。轻视，小看 <span class="encs">[slight;dispise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">说大人则邈之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;尽心下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:邈视(藐视)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 超越;胜过 <span class="encs">[surmount;surpass]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邈古(超越古人);邈俗(超越世俗);邈逾(超越);邈群(超群);邈世(超脱世俗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用同&ldquo;貌&rdquo;。描绘，摹写 <span class="encs">[discribe]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邈真(描绘图像;描摹);邈掠(犹描摹);邈影(绘画)</span></p>
