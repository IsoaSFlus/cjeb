                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">斋</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>斋</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">齋</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāi.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄞˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>屋舍，常指书房、学舍、饭店或商店：书～。聊～。荣宝～。</li>	<li>祭祀前或举行典礼前清心洁身：～戒。封～（亦称&ldquo;把斋&rdquo;）。～月。～坛。～会。～果。</li>	<li>佛教、道教等教徒、道徒吃的素食：～饭。～堂。吃～。</li>	<li>舍饭给僧人吃：～僧。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> vegetarian diet; study</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Stube, Zimmer, Atelier, Künstlerwerkstatt  (S)​,vegetarisches Essen  (S)​,vegetarisches Essen ( nach den religiösen Regeln z.B. Buddhimus )​  (S, Rel)​,fasten  (V, Rel)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> jeûner,régime végétarien</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">斋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>斋</strong></p>
<p>斎、亝 <span class="dicpy">zhāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从示，斎齐省声。本义:斋戒，旧指祭祀前整洁身心)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fast;abtain from meat，wine，etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斋，戒洁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王斋日三举。盖王日一举。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;膳夫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日中及夕则馂余斋，则每食一太牢也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太牢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斋必变食，至不多食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>虽斋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂许斋五日</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秦王斋五日。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">专意斋醮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斋宿(先一日斋戒，以表诚敬);斋沐(斋戒沐浴);斋事(斋戒祭祀的事);斋舍(古人斋戒时的居所);斋牛(供祭祀用的牛)。又指佛教的进餐用语。小乘禁过午食，以午前、午中进食为斋;大乘禁肉食，以素食为斋。如:长斋;斋食(佛家语。指午前、午中之食);斋时(佛家语。过午不食为斋，故称正午为斋时)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 施舍饭食给僧、道或穷苦人 <span class="encs">[provide Buddhist monks with meals]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">张</span>待诏娘子盛一碗饭，一碗羹，斋这无眼婆婆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《清平山堂话本》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斋僧敬道(对和尚道士虔诚有礼，供食舍钱);斋衬钱(施舍给佛寺的钱币)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 斋宫的简称 <span class="encs">[palace]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 商店用名 <span class="encs">[shop&#39;s name]</span>。今北京有荣宝斋，苏州有采芝斋</p>
