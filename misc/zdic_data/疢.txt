                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>疢</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 烦热;疾病 <span class="encs">[fever;disease]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疢，热病也。亦作疹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美疢不如恶石。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则有疾疢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疢如疾首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小弁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 忧伤;痛苦 <span class="encs">[worry;pain]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疢疾(犹忧患);疢毒(疾疢之毒害;喻祸害)</span></p>
