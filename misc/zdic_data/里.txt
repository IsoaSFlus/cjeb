                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">里</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>里</strong></p><p><strong>（④⑤<span class="diczx3">裏</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>居住的地方：故～。返～（回老家）。</li>    <li>街坊（古代五家为邻，五邻为里）：～弄（<span class="dicpy">lòng </span>）。</li>    <li>中国市制长度单位：一～（等于五百米）。～程牌。</li>    <li>衣物的内层：被～。</li>    <li>内部，与“<span class="diczx3">外</span>”相对，并引申为一定范围以内：～外。心～。这～。那～。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> unit of distance; village; lane</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> in, innerhalb, innerlich  (Präp)​,Heimat  (S)​,innere  (Adj)​,Li (chin. Längenmaß = 1,2 km)​  (Zähl)​,Futter, Innenseite  (S)​,Nachbarschaft, nähere Umgebung  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dans,intérieur,à l'intérieur,li (mesure de longueur d'environ 500 mètres)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">里</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>里</strong></p><p>裏、裡 <span class="dicpy">lǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从衣，里声。本义:衣服的里层)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lining]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">裏，衣内也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。衣外曰表。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">襦在裏也。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·宣公九年》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不罹于裏。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小弁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绿衣黄里。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·绿衣》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:里衣(内衣，汗衫。指贴身的衣报);里外发烧(指里外都是毛皮缝制的衣服)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 内，中。与外相反 <span class="encs">[inside]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">裏急暴痛。——<span class="smcs">《素问·至真要大论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肉里之脉。——<span class="smcs">《素问·刺腰痛篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">约定今夜放火，里应外合。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:里向(靠里边的;里面);里势(里面);里勾外连(内外勾结);里头大叔(里头，指宫内。指慈禧最宠信的大太监李连英);里牵绵(指饮酒下肚时，好像腹内忽有厚绵暖着一样)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 时候 <span class="encs">[time]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妾身年少里，父母俱倾弃。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>·佚名<span class="smcs">《张协状元》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 附在<span class="yhcs">“这”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“那”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“哪”</span>等字后边表示地点;位置;处所 <span class="encs">[used after <span class="yhcs">“这”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“那”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“哪”</span> to indicate direction of place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我这里凭阑望，母亲那里倚门悲。——</span><span class="diczx2">郑德辉</span><span class="smcs">《玉粲登楼》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:这里，那里;哪里;省里;县里</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>里</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从土，从田。从<span class="yhcs">“田”</span>，含有区分界域的意思。本义:里弄;街巷)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[alley;lane]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">里，居也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">里，邑也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“居之邑也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">里尻也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span
