                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>橛</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄝˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>小木桩：～子。</li>
	<li>树或庄稼的残茎：残～。树～。</li>
	<li>木制的马嚼子。</li>
	<li>一小段：&ldquo;伏惟烂木一～。佛与众生不别&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">橛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>橛</strong></p>
<p>橜 <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 木橛子，短木桩 <span class="encs">[peg;short wooden stake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">橜，弋也。从木，厥声。一曰门梱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾处也，若橜株驹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;黄帝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">作厥(橛)株拘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;达生》</span>。注:&ldquo;凡竖木而短者，皆得曰 橜。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苗高二三尺，每路打橛。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋应星</span><span class="smcs">《天工开物》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:橛杙(木桩);橛眼(木桩插入地面留下的孔穴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 门中竖立以为限隔的短木 <span class="encs">[post]</span>。如:橛机(谓门内。亦指&ldquo;内室&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 树木或禾杆的残根 <span class="encs">[stump]</span>。如:橛株驹(树桩)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 即马嚼子。马口中所衔的横木。后亦用金属制成 <span class="encs">[bar bit]</span>。如:橛饰(谓以宝物饰于马衔两端)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>橛</strong> <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 犹翘起，鼓起 <span class="encs">[stick up]</span>。如:橛头船(尖头小船);橛头(橛头船)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 倒，颠仆 <span class="encs">[fall forwards]</span>。如:橛痿(痿蹶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 折断 <span class="encs">[break]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树本浅，根垓不深，未必橛也;飘风起，暴雨至，拔必先矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《说苑》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;撅&rdquo;。敲，打击 <span class="encs">[strike]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以其皮为鼓，橛以雷兽之骨，声五百里，以咸天下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;大荒东经&middot;东海》</span></span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">橛</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>橛</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄩㄝˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>ju&eacute;</rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">小木樁、短木頭。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「木頭橛子」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《水滸傳&middot;第二三回》：「只怕大蟲不死，把棒橛又打了一回。」</span><span class="gc_yx">也稱為「橛子」、「橛兒」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">馬口中所銜的橫木。</span><span class="gc_yy">《韓非子&middot;姦劫弒臣》：「無捶策之威，銜橛之備，雖造父不能以服馬。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">禾稼的殘根。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「稻橛」、「麥橛」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6A5B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>居月切，音厥。【說文】杙也。一曰門梱。【爾雅&middot;釋宮】樴謂之杙。【註】橜也。蓋直一段之木也。【列子&middot;黃帝篇】若橜株駒。【註】斷木。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】旣備乃事。【疏】引漢農書云：孟春，土長冒橛，陳根可拔，耕者急發。</p>
<p>又馬銜曰橜。【莊子&middot;馬蹄篇】前有橛飾之患。【司馬相如傳】銜橛之變。</p>
<p>又【篇海】橜車，鉤心木也。</p>
<p>又擊鼓槌也。【山海經】有獸名夔，以其皮爲鼓，橛以雷獸之管，聲聞五百里。</p>
<p>又同嶡。</p>
<p>又【列子】姑衞切，音劌。盛肉几。【禮&middot;明堂位】俎，夏后氏以嶡。【註】謂中央橫木也。通橛。</p>
<p>又【韻會】其月切，音掘。【正韻】居胃切，音貴。義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。</p>
