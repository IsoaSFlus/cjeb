                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">独</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>独</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">獨</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>单一，只有一个：～唱。～立。～霸。～裁。～创。～特。～辟蹊径（喻独创新风格或新方法）。～具慧眼（形容眼光敏锐，见解高超）。</li>
	<li>老而无子：鳏寡孤～。</li>
	<li>难道，岂：&ldquo;君～不见夫趣（趋）市者乎？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>〔～孤〕复姓。</li>
	<li>语助词，犹&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">其</span>&rdquo;：&ldquo;弃君之命，，～谁受之？&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> alone, single, solitary, only</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> einzig, allein</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> unique,seul,solitaire,singulier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">独</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>独</strong></p>
<p>獨 <span class="dicpy">d&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从犬，蜀声。犬性好斗，多独居，故字从犬。本义:单独;单一)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lonely;single;only]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">独，犬相得而斗也。羊为群，犬为独也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;犬好斗，好斗则独而不群。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子慎其独也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儒有特立而独行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我独南行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;击鼓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天之生是使独也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;养生主》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哀吾生之无乐兮，幽独处乎山中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾以捕蛇独存。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君今何疾，临路独迟回。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span> <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《代放歌行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不见秘书心若失，百年衰病独登台。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《怀元度》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:独角兕( <span class="dicpy">s&igrave;</span>)(一种独角的犀牛);独根孤种(独生子);独幅(性格孤僻，不合群);独脚腿(孤立无援的人) ;独院;独家院</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 独特 <span class="encs">[only]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人所独见，众人焉知其极?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;制乐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:独步(超群出众，独一无二);独拔(超众，特出);独绝(独一无二;无与伦比);独智(过人的才能;独特的智慧);独照(独特的眼光)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>独</strong></p>
<p>獨 <span class="dicpy">d&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 独自 <span class="encs">[alone;only;all by oneself;singly]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:独有(独自安慰);独罟(独吞;独占。罟:网的总称);独擅(独揽;独占。擅:拥有;据有);独吃自疴(喻指顾己不顾人，一人独吞。疴:即&ldquo;屙&rdquo;，拉屎撒尿);独乐(独自欣赏音乐);独觉(一人独睡);独吟(独自吟诗);独言(一人自言);独笑(独自喜笑;自乐);独专(单独占有);独怀(独自思念);独写(独自抒发);独抚(独自安慰);独说(独自解说);独用(单独使用)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 特，特别地 <span class="encs">[very;especially]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:独惨(特惨);独甚(特甚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 唯独，仅仅 <span class="encs">[only]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子所言者，其人与骨皆已朽矣，独其言在耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;老子韩非列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">谢灵运</span>为 <span class="diczx2">永嘉</span>守，凡 <span class="diczx2">永嘉</span>山水，游历殆遍，独不言此山。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不独是城里姓</span><span class="diczx2">贾</span>的多，外省姓 <span class="diczx2">贾</span>的也不少。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">别人来得，独你来不得。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">周立波</span><span class="smcs">《卜春秀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:独言(只说);独独(唯独;独自一个);独说(唯独喜欢。说，通&ldquo;悦&rdquo;);独得(只得到);独惟(唯独;只有);独知(仅一人知道);独树(一株树;一根木);独拍(单掌拍击);独见(坚持己见，固守节操)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 岂，难道 <span class="encs">[could it possibly be&hellip;;does it mean&hellip;]</span></p>

<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 无子孙的老人 <span class="encs">[old people without off-spring]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7368.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】徒谷切【正韻】杜谷切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音犢。【說文】犬相得而<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C1A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩰚</span>。从犬蜀聲。羊爲羣，犬爲獨也。</p>
<p>又獸名。【埤雅】顏從曰：獨一叫而猨散，鼉一鳴而龜伏。或曰鼉鳴夜，獨叫曉。獨，猨類也。似猨而大，食猨。今俗謂之獨猨。蓋猨性羣，獨性特，猨鳴三，獨叫一，是以謂之獨也。</p>
<p>又獨<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2479E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤞞</span>，獸名。詳前<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2479E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤞞</span>字註。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】單獨。【詩&middot;小雅】哀此惸獨。【傳】獨，單也。【又】念我獨兮。【爾雅&middot;釋山】獨者蜀。【疏】蟲之孤獨者名蜀，是以山之孤獨者亦名曰蜀也。【揚子&middot;方言】一蜀也。南楚謂之獨。</p>
<p>又山名。【山海經】獨山，多金玉美石。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】複姓有獨孤氏。【後魏書】西方獨孤渾氏，後攺爲杜氏。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】明獨立，獨善。</p>
