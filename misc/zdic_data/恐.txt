                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>恐</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄨㄥˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>害怕，畏惧：～惧。～怖。～慌。惊～。有恃无～。</li>	<li>恫吓（<span class="dicpy">h&egrave;</span>）：～吓（<span class="dicpy">h&egrave;</span>）。</li>	<li>疑虑：～怕。～不能成功。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">恐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>恐</strong> <span class="dicpy">kǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，巩声。本义:严重害怕，惊恐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fear;dread]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恐，惧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">室如县罄，野无青草，何恃而不恐?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">星队木鸣，国人皆恐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天伦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屠大窘，恐前后受其敌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>其破璧，乃辞谢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恐栗，为儿恒状。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舟人大恐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">魏</span>王恐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恐防(担心，提防);恐虑(担心);恐灼(惊恐);恐怯(畏怯、害怕);恐畏(畏惧);恐急(惊慌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 恐吓，使之害怕 <span class="encs">[terrify;intimidate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伺其危险之陂，以恐其主。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;有度》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">李斯</span>因说 <span class="diczx2">秦王</span>，请先取 <span class="diczx2">韩</span>以恐他国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恐呵(威吓呵斥);恐胁(恐吓威胁);恐逼(恐吓逼迫);恐褐(恫吓威胁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 恐怕 <span class="encs">[I am afraid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恐前后受其敌。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">蒲松龄</span><span class="smcs">《狼三则&middot;其二》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常恐秋节至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;长歌行》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">恐托付不效。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恐其有误;恐不容易;恐非所愿</span></p>
