						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>器</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>用具的总称：～皿。～物。～械。木～。武～。～小易盈。</li>    <li>生物体结构中具有某种独立生理机能的部分：～官。消化～。</li>    <li>人的度量、才干：～度。～量（<span class="dicpy">liàng </span>）。～宇。～质。大～晚成。</li>    <li>重视：～重。～敬。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">器</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>器</strong></p><p>噐 <span class="dicpy">qì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从犬。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“象器之口，犬所以守之。”</span>器物很多，用狗看守。本义:器具)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[utensil;implement;appliance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">器，皿也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“器乃凡器统称。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下神器。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">形乃谓之器。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物周为器。——<span class="smcs">《周书·宝典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其贡器物。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大行人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫冰炭不同器而久。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·显学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">作为食器。斩山木而财之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">守圉之器。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肖于器邪。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《朱文公校昌黎先生集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盆器倾侧。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尽一器。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">器贡(宗庙的器具。九贡之一);器车(器指银瓮丹甑等，车指山车之类。都在太平盛世出现，故象征祥瑞);器小(容器狭小)器备(器物，器具)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指用具 <span class="encs">[utensil;apparatus]</span>。如:银器;锡器;漆器;玉器;器直(木工用的曲尺);器玩(玩具，供玩赏的器物);器仗(亦作<span class="yhcs">“器杖”</span>。武器总称;指帝王所用的仪仗器具)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 器官 <span class="encs">[organ]</span>。如:细胞器;生殖器;泌尿器</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 度量;胸怀 <span class="encs">[capacity;tolerance]</span>。如:器识(器度和见识);器小(器局狭隘);器行(器量品行);器怀(胸怀;度量);器宇(指人的胸襟和度量)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 才能 <span class="encs">[capacity;ability]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庙堂之器。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">器能(器量才能);器分(人所具有的资质和才能);器志(才识和志向);器尚(才具，节操)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 人才 <span class="encs">[talent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大器晚成。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先自治而后治人之称大器。——<span class="smcs">《法言·先知》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>器</strong> <span class="dicpy">qì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>重视 <span class="encs">[think highly of]</span></p><p><span
