                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">愚</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>愚</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>傻，笨：～人。～笨。～蠢。～鲁。～氓（愚蠢的人）。～昧（缺乏知识，文化落后）。～顽。～妄。～不可及。大智若～（有大智慧的人，不卖弄聪明，表面上好像很愚笨，亦作&ldquo;大智如愚&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>欺骗，耍：～弄人。为人所～。</li>
	<li>谦辞，用于自称：～兄。～见。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> stupid, doltish, foolish</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> jdn für dumm verkaufen, jdn hereinlegen  (V)​,dumm, <umg> blöd  (Adj)​,Yu  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bête,idiot,tricher ou tromper</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">愚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>愚</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从心，从禺( <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span>)。从心，与性情有关。禺，猴属，兽之愚者。本义:愚蠢，愚昧)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[foolish;stupid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愚，戆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三赦，曰惷愚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司刺》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">靡哲不愚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;抑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哲人之愚。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非是是非谓之愚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;脩身》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">深知祸福谓之知，反知为愚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以惑愚瞽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使君一何愚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;陌上桑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愚人所为愚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愚者亦明。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:愚若胶柱(胶住调节乐弦的柱再去弹奏，只能弹出单一无变化的音调。比喻愚笨到像去胶柱，拘泥而不知变通);愚戆(愚蠢而刚直);愚騃(痴笨);愚浊(愚笨;糊涂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 自称之谦词 <span class="encs">[my humble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愚以为营中之事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愚谓大计。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愚有片言，望丞相察之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:愚兄(对同辈而年轻于己者的自我谦称);愚老(老人自谦之词);愚臣(大臣对君主自称的谦词);愚意(对自己意见的谦称);愚怀(谦指己见)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 敦厚 <span class="encs">[honest and sincere]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故<span class="smcs">《诗》</span>之失愚，<span class="smcs">《书》</span>之失诬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王肃</span>注:&ldquo;愚，敦厚。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:愚款(单纯朴实);愚鲠(敦厚鲠直)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>愚</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>蒙蔽;欺骗 <span class="encs">[make a fool of;deceive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以愚黔首。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
