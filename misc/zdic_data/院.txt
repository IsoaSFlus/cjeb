                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>院</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>围墙里房屋四周的空地：～子。～墙。庭～。</li>	<li>某些机关、学校和公共场所名称：法～。医～。戏～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> courtyard, yard, court; school</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Anstalt des öffentl. Leben  (S)​,Behörde, Institution  (S, Pol)​,Hof, unzäumtes Grundstück  (S, Arch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cour,(désignation pour certains établissements gouvernementaux ou publics)​,institut</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">院</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>院</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span>)，完声。&ldquo;阜&rdquo;是土山，与土、建筑有关。本义:庭院，院子。房屋围墙以内的空地)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[courtyard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">院，垣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">院，周垣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有垣墙者曰院。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《增韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">深院月明人静。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《西江月》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:前院;后院;深宫大院</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 四周有墙垣围绕、自成部分的房屋 <span class="encs">[compound;courtyard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">院东五里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">院僧夜闻大呼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驰往油画院。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:画院(书院)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 房屋周围的垣墙 <span class="encs">[wall enclosed the house]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:场院</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 林园 <span class="encs">[garden]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">试茗</span><span class="diczx2">蜀井冈</span>，看花 <span class="diczx2">竹西院</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">柳贯</span><span class="smcs">《送郭子昭经历赴淮东诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 娼楼妓馆 <span class="encs">[brothel]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:院门(院子。妓院)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 仆人 <span class="encs">[servant]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:院子(宋、明、清初戏曲小说中称仆人为院子);院公(对他人奴仆的尊称。常见于元、明戏曲小说中)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 官署 <span class="encs">[government office]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">早晨七点钟上院，九点下来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《官场现形记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:殿院;枢密院;行政院;院上行知(保举已得朝廷批准，由巡抚衙门通知本人的文书);院君(有封号的妇人;亦尊称官吏，财主之妻);院长(宋朝指军吏节级。后来也称衙门中的公人);院子家(给皇帝传递饮食的人)</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
