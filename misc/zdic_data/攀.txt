<p>● <strong>攀</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄢˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>抓住东西向上爬：～登。～高。～越。～桂（古代指科举考试登第）。～折（<span class="dicpy">zh&eacute;</span>）。～附。～援。</li>
	<li>拉扯，拉拢，结交：～交。～扯。～谈。～亲。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">攀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>攀</strong> <span class="dicpy">pān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，樊声。本义:拉，牵)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[draw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攀，引也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">右手攀右趾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:攀恋(攀住车辕，恋恋不舍);攀辕(拉住车把)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 攀援，指用握住或抓住某物的方法爬或登 <span class="encs">[climb]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将士皆攀木援崖，鱼贯而进。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;邓艾传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攀草牵棘，&hellip;石崖侧削则援崖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:攀引(攀援);攀延(攀附他物延伸);攀沿(犹攀缘);攀云(攀附青云而上升。后比喻仕进);攀倚(攀援依傍);攀进(攀缘行进)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指跟地位高的人结亲戚或拉关系;高攀 <span class="encs">[seek connections in high places]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两府攀陪十五年，郡中甘雨幕中莲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">罗隐</span><span class="smcs">《乌程》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:攀教(高攀求教);攀风(比喻结交比自己高一等的人);攀亲托熟(认作亲戚熟友)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 攀比 <span class="encs">[compare unrealistically]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窃攀屈宋宜方驾，恐与</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>作后尘。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《戏为六绝句》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:攀引(犹攀比);攀追(攀比追随;攀高追远)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 依附 <span class="encs">[depend on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">逊</span>小心谨慎，徒以攀附至贵显。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史&middot;张逊传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:攀依(攀附，依附);攀陪(依附);攀违(谦词，谓有违于依附);攀鳞(比喻依附帝王以成功名)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 援引 <span class="encs">[cite]</span>。如:攀引(援用;引用);攀扶(援引扶助);攀例(援引为例)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 牵涉;牵扯 <span class="encs">[implicate;involve]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此人曾攀下</span><span class="diczx2">王子服</span>等四人，我已拿下廷尉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p>
