                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>估</strong> <span class="dicpy">gū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，古声。本义:物价)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[price]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃高盐直，贱帛估。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书&middot;陆长源传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;贾&rdquo;。商人 <span class="encs">[dealer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫复临时不寄人，漫道江中无估客。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;梁元帝&middot;别诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:估人(商人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 市场税 <span class="encs">[market tax]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">估税悉除，市无二价。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;甘卓传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:估税(古代的一种市场税)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>估</strong> <span class="dicpy">gū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引申为估价，估量 <span class="encs">[evaluate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:高估其价;估产;估评(估价);估单(估计货物价值运费等的清单。即估价单)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 〈方〉∶逼迫;强迫 <span class="encs">[force]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">硬要估住他赔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">艾芜</span><span class="smcs">《猪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:估打成招;估逼(催逼;强逼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用同&ldquo;鼓&rdquo;。凸起;涨大 <span class="encs">[swell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">卫先生</span>估着眼道:&ldquo;前科没有文章。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">g&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
