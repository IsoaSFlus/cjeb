                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>真</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄣˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>与客观事实相符合，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">假</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">伪</span>&rdquo;相对：～诚。～谛。～挚。～心。逼～。认～。～才实学。～知灼见。</li>
	<li>确实，的确：～好。～正。～切。</li>
	<li>清楚，显明：看得～。咬字很～。</li>
	<li>本性，本原：纯～。天～。</li>
	<li>人的肖像：传（<span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;n </span>）～。写～。</li>
	<li>汉字的楷书：～字。～书。～草隶篆。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">真</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>真</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，从华( <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;</span>)，从目，从乚( <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span>)，从八。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;仙人变形而登天也。&rdquo;匕，变化。目，眼睛。乚，隐藏。八，乘载的工具。本义:道家称存养本性或修真得道的人为真人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。旧时所谓仙人 <span class="encs">[the Immortal (in Taoism)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">真，仙人变形而登天也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按:六经无真字</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫免乎外内之刑者，唯真人能之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">归其真宅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;天瑞》</span>。注:&ldquo;太虚之域。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而已反其真，而我犹为人猗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;大宗师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:真人府(道人居住的地方);真仙(仙人);真君(道教对神仙的尊称);真味(真实的意旨或意味)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 本性;本原 <span class="encs">[natural property]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谨守而勿失，是谓反其真。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:真宰(宇宙的主宰者;造物主);真佛(本身;本人。比喻难以见到的人物);真力(本原之力);真原(本源);真源(本源，本性)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 身 <span class="encs">[body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见利而忘其真。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;山水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 肖像，摹画的人像 <span class="encs">[portrait]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有僧写得师真，呈师，师曰:&ldquo;且道似我不似我?&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《景德传灯录》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 汉字楷书的别称 <span class="encs">[regular style of Chinese calligraphy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝亲书其文，作真、行、草三体。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《续资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:真字(正楷字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Zhen prefecture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广德元年设</span><span class="diczx2">吐蕃</span>，其后 <span class="diczx2">松</span>、&hellip; <span class="diczx2">真</span>、&hellip;等为行州。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>真</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 真实。与假、伪相对 <span class="encs">[real;true]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">假金方用真金镀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李绅</span><span class="smcs">《答章孝标》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使真伪毋相乱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;宣帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此画果真。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而同于真。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">真者而同。</span></p>
