                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>兹</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">茲</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>这，这个，此：～日。～行（x&iacute;ng）。</li>	<li>现在：～聘请某先生为本校教员。</li>	<li>年：今～。来～。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/滋">滋</a>&rdquo;，增益；多。</li>	<li>古代称草席。</li></ol><p>● <strong>兹</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">茲</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">c&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔龟（qiū）～〕见&ldquo;<a href="/hans/龟">龟</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> now, here; this; time, year</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hiermit</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ceci,maintenant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兹</strong></p>
<p>茲 <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>(形声。据<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>，从艸，滋省声。本义:草木茂盛) 同本义 <span class="encs">[grow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兹，草木多益也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五藏之气，故色见青如草兹者死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;五藏生成论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兹兹(增加繁殖)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兹</strong> <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 年<span class="encs">[year]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今兹美禾，来兹美麦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 草席 <span class="encs">[straw mat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蓐谓之兹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:&ldquo;兹者，蓐席也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">毛叔郑</span>奉明水， <span class="diczx2">卫康叔</span>封布兹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 现在;此时 <span class="encs">[now;at present]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兹予大享于先生，尔祖其从与享之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚上》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>兹</strong> <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
