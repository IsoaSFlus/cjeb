                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>饥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">飢</span>、<span class="diczx3">饑</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>饿：～饿。～民。～驱（旧指为衣食而奔走）。～餐渴饮。～寒交迫。～不择食（喻急需的时候顾不得细加选择）。画饼充～。</li>
	<li>庄稼收成不好或没有收成：～荒。～馑（灾荒）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hunger, starving; hungry; a famine</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hungrig</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>饥</strong></p>
<p>饑 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;名，动&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，幾( <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:荒年，五谷无收)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bad havest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饑，谷不熟为饑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五谷不熟谓之饥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;七患》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">降丧饥馑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;雨无止》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>尝大饥，道旁饿死者不可数也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说右上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凶年饥发，君之民老弱转乎沟壑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时不出，天下大饥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今山东饥馑，人庶相食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;翼奉传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饥荒;饥馁(饥饿);饥时得一口(紧要处得人救济，恩德深厚);饥氓(逃荒的人);饥岁(荒年)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="encs">[动]</span>∶通&ldquo;飢&rdquo;。吃不饱，饿 <span class="encs">[hungry;famishing;starving]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黎民不饥不寒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宁一月饥，无一旬饿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牛困人饥日已高。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饥渴而顿踣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风雨霜露饥寒颠踣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>饥</strong></p>
<p>飢 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;名，动&gt;</p>
<p>(&ldquo;飢&rdquo;与&ldquo;饑&rdquo;的区别在于，&ldquo;飢&rdquo;指肚子饿，&ldquo;饑&rdquo;指饥荒。在先秦不相混同，到后来才逐渐通用。&ldquo;飢&rdquo;、&ldquo;饑&rdquo;现在均简化为&ldquo;饥&rdquo;)</p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 吃不饱 <span class="encs">[hungry;famishing;starving]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">飢，饿也。从食，几声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可以乐饥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;陈风&middot;衡门》</span>。笺:&ldquo;饥者，不足于食也.&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家有常业，虽饥不饿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;饰邪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或受之饥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">操</span>军兼以饥疫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饥火(饥饿难忍，如火烧于腹中);饥殍(饿死的人。同饿莩);饥驱(为饥饿所迫，出外奔波谋生);饥火中烧(饥饿难忍。如火烧于腹中)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;饑&rdquo;。荒年，五谷无收 <span class="encs">[bad havest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饥岁之春。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">比年不登，人用饥匮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张奋传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Ji state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">西伯</span>伐 <span class="diczx2">饥国</span>，灭之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;殷本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
