                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坎</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>坎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>低陷不平的地方，坑穴：～井之蛙（喻知识浅陋的人）。～坷。</li>
	<li>自然形成或人工修筑的台阶状东西：土～。田～。</li>
	<li>八卦之一，代表水。</li>
	<li>指最紧要的地方或时机，当口儿：这话可说到～儿上了。</li>
	<li>指坏运气或被迫的处境：今年是他的～儿。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">槛</span>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坎</strong> <span class="dicpy">kǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，欠声。本义:坑，穴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pit;hole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坎，陷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坎者，陷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;序卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其坎深不至于泉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钁其城为坎以先登。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凿地为坎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">东汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坎儿(道路不平。喻指麻烦，变故);坎止(遇险难而止);坎阱(陷阱);坎深(深渊);坎傺(住在土窟里)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 墓穴;墓坑 <span class="encs">[tomb]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">观其葬焉，其坎深不至于泉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通过自然的过程或用人工办法形成或堆成的土堤或土埂 <span class="encs">[bank]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:土坎儿;田坎儿</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 八卦之一，代表水 <span class="encs">[water]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习坎，重险也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;习坎》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坎离(八卦中的水和火);坎德(谦虚之德。<span class="smcs">《周易》</span>以坎属水，水就下处卑下之地。故有此称谓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 光学名词。坎德拉的简称 <span class="encs">[candela]</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>坎</strong> <span class="dicpy">kǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 掘坑，挖洞 <span class="encs">[dig]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坎垣入之，戍者不知也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书&middot;吴少诚传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坎坛(古代挖地为坎，垒木为坛)。又指掘墓穴</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春夏死者，候草木黄落;秋冬死者，候华叶荣茂，然始坎而痉之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 陷;陷落 <span class="encs">[sink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坎，陷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坎陷(塌陷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 扣;戴 <span class="encs">[wear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">晁大舍</span>]</span>随把网巾摘下，坎了浩然巾，穿了狐白皮袄，出去接待。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醒世姻缘传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 忧，恨 <span class="encs">[hate]</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">坑穴，地面凹陷處。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;檀弓下》：「其坎深不至於泉。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷五四&middot;蘇建傳》：「鑿地為坎，置熅火，覆武其上，蹈其背以出血。」</span></p></li>

	<li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/574E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>苦感切，音欿。【說文】陷也，險也。</p>
<p>又穴也。【易】卦名。【彖傳】習坎重險也。【說卦】坎爲水。</p>
<p>又穿穴以葬。【禮&middot;檀弓】其坎深不至于泉。</p>
<p>又穿地以祭。【禮&middot;祭義】祭月于坎。【祭法】相近于坎壇，祭寒暑也。【註】相近當作祖迎。</p>
<p>又擊物聲。【詩&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>風】坎其擊鼓，坎其擊缶。</p>
<p>又用力聲。【詩&middot;魏風】坎坎伐檀兮。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】坎，律銓也。【註】坎卦主法律，所以銓量輕重。</p>
<p>又小罍謂之坎。</p>
<p>又星名。【星經】九坎九星，在牛星南，主溝渠水泉。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;僖二十四年】王遂出及坎欿。【註】周地。</p>
<p>又姓。宋附庸有坎氏。見【統譜】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】苦紺切，音勘。險岸也。</p>
<p>又叶苦簟切，音歉。【歐陽修&middot;梅給事銘】困於翼飛，中垂且斂。一失其塗，進退以坎。　通埳。別作轗輱輡。</p>
