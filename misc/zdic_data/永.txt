                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>永</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄥˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>水流长：“江之～矣”。</li>    <li>久远：～远。～生。隽～。～昼（长昼）。～年（ａ．整年；ｂ．长寿）。～诀。～志不忘。～垂不朽。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">咏</span>”，以诗词等来叙述。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> long, perpetual, eternal, forever</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ewig, fortwährend ,immer ,Yong  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pour toujours,éternellement</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">永</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>永</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，象水流曲曲折折的样子。本义:水流长)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(of stream) long]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">永，水长也。象水巠理之长。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">江</span>之永矣，不可方思。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·汉广》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“永，长。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指长。兼指时间和空间 <span class="encs">[long;distant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">施于众长谓之永。——<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不永所事。——<span class="smcs">《易·讼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日永星火。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维以不永怀。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·卷耳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">永矢弗谖。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·考槃》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且以永日。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·山有枢》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">求永贞。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大祝》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">永乃保之。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以永终誉。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">降年有永有不永。——<span class="smcs">《书·高宗彤日》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斗南当日永，万物显光明。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:永昼(长长的白天);永永(久远);永生(长生);永言(长言);永伤(长久忧伤悲痛);永思(长思，永怀);永夕(长夜);永日(长日，漫长的白天);永宅(长住永守);永安(长久稳固);永住(长住;长存);永命(长命);永巷(宫中长巷;长巷);永贞(长享正命);永宵(长夜);永逸(长久安逸);永岁(长寿);永路(长途，远路)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>永</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 游泳。<span class="yhcs">“泳”</span>的古字 <span class="encs">[swim]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按:此<span class="yhcs">“永”</span>字，即潜行水中之<span class="yhcs">“泳”</span>字之初文。原从人在水中行，由文人做生意，故托以寄游泳之意…后人借用为长永，久而为借意所专，乃加水旁作<span class="yhcs">“泳”</span>以还其原。——</span><span class="diczx2">高鸿缙</span><span class="smcs">《中国字例》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 延长 <span class="encs">[lengthen]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">资富能训，惟以永年。——<span class="smcs">《书·毕命》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“咏”</span>。曼声。长吟，歌唱 <span class="encs">[chant;sing;intone]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">声依永，律和声。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乐郊乐郊，谁之永号。——<span
