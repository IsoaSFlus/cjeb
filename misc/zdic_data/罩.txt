                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>罩</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从网，卓声。本义:捕鱼的竹笼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a bamboo trap for fishing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">持罩入深水，金鳞大如手。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">温庭筠</span><span class="smcs">《罩鱼歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:罩罗(泛指捕鱼或捕鸟的竹器);罩笼(泛指捕鱼的竹器)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 捕鸟的竹笼或掩网 <span class="encs">[bamboo net for catching bird]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弛罩出凤雏。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《孟车篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又指养家禽的竹笼。如:鸡罩</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 罩形的器物 <span class="encs">[cover]</span>。如:灯罩;玻璃罩;保护罩;罩甲(外套，短外衣);灯罩;口罩</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>罩</strong> <span 覆盖;套在外面 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荷塘烟罩小斋虚，景物皆宜入画图。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">司空图</span><span class="smcs">《王官》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:罩笼(笼罩);罩褂(罩衣);罩袖(方言。套在衣袖外面的套袖)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 笼罩或遮蔽 <span class="encs">[wrap]</span>。如:山坡上罩着一层薄雾</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用笼捕取 <span class="encs">[fish with coop]</span>。如:罩汕(泛指用渔具捕鱼)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 超越 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其文博诞空类，大者罩天地之表，细者入毫纤之内。——</span><span
