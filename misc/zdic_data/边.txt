                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">边</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>边</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">邊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">biān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>物体的周围部分，外缘：～缘。～沿。</li>
	<li>国家或地区交界处：～疆。～界。～防。～境。～陲（边境）。</li>
	<li>几何学上指夹成角或围成多角形的直线：等～三角形。</li>
	<li>旁侧，近旁：身～。～锋。</li>
	<li>方面：～干（<span class="dicpy">g&agrave;n </span>）～学。</li>
	<li>表示方位：上～。外～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> edge, margin, side, border</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Grenze, Grenzgebiet, Schranke  (S)​,Schranke, Grenze  (S)​,Seite  (S)​,Seitenrand, Rand, Kante  (S)​,an, nahe bei, neben, voneinander  (Präp)​,Bian  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> côté,bord,marge,frontière,en même temps,simultanément,(suffixe locatif)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">边</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>边</strong></p>
<p>邊 <span class="dicpy">biān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，臱( <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span>)声。本义:山崖的边缘)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[margin;edge;brim]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">边，行垂崖也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">边，垂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">白帝城</span>边足风波， <span class="diczx2">瞿塘</span>五月谁敢过。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《荆州歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:崖边;岸边;水池边;边侧(边沿，旁侧);边涯(边际;边缘)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 边境 <span class="encs">[border;frontier]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其在边邑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span>。注:&ldquo;九洲之外。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顿颡于边。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span>。注:&ldquo;边，边境也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣闻五大不在边，五细不在庭。(五大:一种大人物。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">思念北边之末安。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;利议》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">归来头白还戍边。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:边人(边境的人);边民(边境的人民);边缺(边远地区官职的空缺);边任(驻守边远地区的重要职位);边庭(边疆);边夷(泛指边疆的少数民族);边功(在边境抗敌所建立的功劳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旁边 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">边，方也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">续衽钩边。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;深衣》</span></span></p>
