                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>缮</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">繕</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>修补，整治：～生（保养身体）。～性（修养本性）。～完。修～。</li><li>抄写：～写。～校（<span class="dicpy">jiào </span>）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> repair, mend; rewrite, transcribe</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> réparer,écrire</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缮</strong></p><p>繕 <span class="dicpy">shàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，善声。本义:修补;修整)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mend;repair]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缮，补也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缮完葺墙，以待宾客。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡治，故造新皆谓之缮也。——<span class="smcs">《珠丛》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缮城郭。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:缮治(修补;整治);缮完(修整，营建);缮饰(修理整治;修葺装饰);缮理(修理;修缮);缮修(修缮;修补);缮造(修造);缮性(涵养本性);缮葺(修理房屋、墙垣等)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 制造;整治 <span class="encs">[prepare;renovate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缮兵甲，具卒乘。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:缮甲(整治武器装备);缮兵(整治武备);缮理(整治，整顿)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 工整地抄写 <span class="encs">[copy neatly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然后退扫闲轩，缮写呈上。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《与韩荆州书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:缮录(誊写);缮发(抄写后发出)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 编录文籍 <span class="encs">[edit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是裂纸数千，缮写其兄里系年貌。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《万里寻兄记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 保养;保持 <span class="encs">[keep]</span>。如:缮生(养生)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/缮校">缮校</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/缮写">缮写</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E55.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>時戰切，音膳。【說文】補也。【禮·月令】繕囹圄。</p><p>又【詩·鄭風·叔于田序】繕甲治兵。【箋】繕之言善也。</p><p>又【周禮·夏官·繕人註】繕之言勁也，善也。【疏】以其所掌弓弩，有堅勁而善，堪爲王用者。</p><p>又【左傳·僖十五年】征繕以輔孺子。【註】繕，治也。</p><p>又【前漢·息夫躬傳】繕修干戈。【註】師古曰：繕，備也。</p><p>又【後漢·盧植傳】供繕寫上。</p><p>又與勁同。【禮·曲禮】招搖在上，急繕其怒。【註】繕，讀曰勁。</p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">補也。</span>周禮繕人注曰。繕之言勁也、善也。叔于田序注云。繕之言善也。曲禮。招搖在上。急繕其怒。注曰。急猶堅也。繕讀曰勁。按許言補、其本義也。而中含善勁二義。故鄭云之言。不必如曲禮注之改讀也。<span class="jieshi">从糸。善聲。</span>時戰切。十四部。                          </div>
