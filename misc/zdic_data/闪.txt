                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>闪</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閃</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄢˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>天空的电光：～电。打～。</li>	<li>突然显现：～光。～烁。～耀。～现。</li>	<li>侧转体躲避：躲～。～让。</li>	<li>因动作过猛，使一部分筋肉受伤而疼痛：～了腰。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> flash; avoid, dodge, evade</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Blitz  (S)​,Shan  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> s'esquiver,quitter,briller,étinceler</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>闪</strong></p>
<p>閃 <span class="dicpy">shǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人在门中。本义:自门内偷看)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，引申为偷看 <span class="encs">[peek out through a door]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闪，窥头在门中也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span> <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">裴松之</span>注引<span class="smcs">《魏略&middot;苛吏传》</span>:&ldquo;白日常自于墙壁间閨闪。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故鱼鲔不淰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span>。注:&ldquo;淰之言闪也。&rdquo;疏:&ldquo;闪是忽有忽无，故字从门中人也。经传多以掩为之。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 忽有忽无， 突然显现 <span class="encs">[flash;flicker]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蝄像暂晓而闪屍&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">木华</span><span class="smcs">《海赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;暂见之貌。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为首闪出一将。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脑子闪过一个念头;闪尸(忽隐忽现);闪飒(闪烁);闪缀(闪闪发光);闪然(隐没不见);闪荡(光闪动);闪放(闪烁放光);闪明(天刚明);闪忽(变化不定)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 动摇不定 <span class="encs">[waver]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垂涎闪吞兮，挥霍旁午。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《招海贾文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又指<span class="encs">[身体]</span>猛然晃动。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:闪了闪，差点儿跌倒</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 遮拦 <span class="encs">[block]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因此上裴航要闪住你肖郎路。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">汤显祖</span><span class="smcs">《邯郸记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 抛弃 <span class="encs">[throw;abandon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">教孩儿往帝都，把媳妇闪得苦又孤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">高明</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶记&middot;糟粮自厌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闪了脸(翻了脸，损伤了感情);闪落(撇开不管)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 抛洒 <span class="encs">[shed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闪英雄泪渍盈盈袖，伤心不为悲秋瘦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">汤显祖</span><span class="smcs">《牡丹亭》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 扭伤筋骨 <span class="encs">[sprain]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 欺骗 <span class="encs">[deceive;cheat]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:闪诳(欺骗);闪赚(哄骗);闪揄(奸巧谄媚)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">梦见一人，手执柳枝，闪了他去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">汤显祖</span><span class="smcs">《牡丹亭》</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>闪</strong></p>
<p>閃 <span class="dicpy">shǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 天空的电光。俗称闪电为闪 <span class="encs">[flash of lightning]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千里的雷声万里的闪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李季</span><span class="smcs">《王贵与李香香》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:打闪;闪红(电光)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 闪族 <span class="encs">[semite]</span>，高加索人种的一个分支，多住在中东一带，包括阿拉伯人、犹太人和埃塞俄比亚人</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 闪失;挫折 <span class="encs">[unexpected error]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不想又吃了这一闪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
