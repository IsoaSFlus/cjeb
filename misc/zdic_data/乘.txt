                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>乘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>骑，坐：～马。～车。～客。～警。</li>
	<li>趁着，就着：～便。～机（趁着机会）。～势。～兴（<span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng</span>）。因利～便。</li>
	<li>算术中指一个数使另一个数变成若干倍：～法。～幂（<span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>）。～数。</li>
	<li>佛教的教派或教法：大～。小～。上～。下～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>乘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代称兵车，四马一车为一乘：～舆。千～之国。</li>
	<li>古代称四为乘：～矢。～壶。&ldquo;以～韦先牛十二犒师&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>中国春秋时晋国的史书称&ldquo;乘&rdquo;，后通称一般的史书：史～。野～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ride, ascend; avail oneself of; numerary adjunct for vehicles</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anbringen, befestigen  (V)​,ausnutzen, benutzen, sich jemandes bedienen, von etwas Gebrauch machen  (V)​,ausnutzen, Vorteil wahrnehmen  (V)​,fahren ( mit etw. fahren )​  (V)​,fahren, reiten  (V)​,Gebrauch machen von  (V)​,malen  (V)​,multiplizieren  (V)​,multiplizieren, malnehmen  (V, Math)​,vervielfachen  (V)​,Aufzeichnungen  (S)​,Fahrzeug zum Heil  (S, Buddh)​,Fuhrwerk  (S)​,Geschichte  (S)​,Heilsweg  (S)​,Kriegswagen  (S)​,Wagen  (Zähl)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乘</strong></p>
<p>乗 <span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，从大(人)从木，表示人爬在树上。&ldquo;大&rdquo;象&ldquo;人&rdquo;正面。本义:登，升)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ascend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乘，升也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亟其乘屋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却叔虎将乘城。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">淮阴侯</span>复乘之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高帝纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俱乘高台。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;黄帝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乘槎(神话中指乘木排上天);乘城(登城;守城);乘屋(登上屋顶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 骑;坐 <span class="encs">[ride]</span></p>

<p>又姓。漢煮棗侯乗昌。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【韻會】實證切【集韻】石證切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音剩。車也。【詩&bull;小雅】元戎十乘，以先啓行。</p>
<p>又物雙曰乘。【左傳&bull;僖三十三年】弦高以乘韋先牛十二犒師。【揚子&bull;方言】雙鴈曰乘。</p>
<p>又物四數皆曰乘。【禮&bull;少儀】乘壺酒。【孟子】發乗矢。</p>
<p>又乗丘，地名。【爾雅&bull;釋地註】乗丘，形似車乘也。</p>
<p>又草名。【爾雅&bull;釋草】望乘車。【註】可爲索，長丈餘。</p>
<p>又【韻會】乘者，載也。取載事爲名。【孟子】晋之乗。◎按《韻瑞》引晋乘入平聲，誤。今宗譜曰家乘，義與史乘通。</p>
<p>又【傳燈錄】禪有淺深階級，一小乘，一大乘。頓悟自心無漏智，此心卽佛，曰最上乘。【宋沙門契嵩&bull;原敎篇】五乘皆統之於三藏。一人乗，二天乗，三聲聞乗，四緣覺乘，五菩薩乘。後三乘導其徒出世也。前二乗以欲不可輒去，就其情而制之也。皆去聲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】諸應切，音證。姓也。　【說文】本作椉。</p>
