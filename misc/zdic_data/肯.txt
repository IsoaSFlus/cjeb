                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">肯</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>肯</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kěn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kěn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄣˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>许可，愿意：首～（点头答应）。</li>
	<li>骨头上附着的肉：～綮（<span class="dicpy">qìng</span>）（筋骨结合的地方，喻重要的关键）。中（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）～（喻言论正中要害）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> willing; consent to, permit</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> übereinstimmen, beipflichten ,einwilligen, zustimmen ,bereitwillig  (Adj)​,Ken  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vouloir,accepter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">肯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>肯</strong></p>
<p>肎 <span class="dicpy">kěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(会意。小篆字形从肉，从冎( <span class="dicpy">guǎ</span>)省。本义:着骨之肉) 同本义 <span class="encs">[flesh attached to bone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肎，骨间肉肎肎着也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗字误作肯。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">技经肯綮之未尝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;养生主》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:肯綮</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>肯</strong> <span class="dicpy">kěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为&ldquo;可&rdquo;。表示应允，同意 <span class="encs">[agree;consent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太后不肯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">留为小吏，不肯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劝说了半天，他才肯了;肯认(认可，同意);肯可(赞成，同意);肯首(点头表示同意);肯酒(允婚酒。表示女方应允亲事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 愿意，心甘情愿、乐意 <span class="encs">[be willing to;be ready to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫肯我顾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;硕鼠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客肯为靖郭君末寡人乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹可也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不肯者，可以肯也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;宣公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>不肯击缻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">李伶</span>即又不肯授我。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">清</span><span class="diczx2">侯方域</span><span class="smcs">《壮悔堂文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大将军仁慈不肯发令。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:肯干;他肯吗?肯吃苦;肯心儿(心甘情愿);肯心(心甘情愿;顺心);肯道(愿意)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>肯</strong> <span class="dicpy">kěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 恰恰 <span class="encs">[just]</span>。</p>
