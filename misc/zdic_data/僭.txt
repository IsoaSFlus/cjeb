						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>僭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 超越本分，古代指地位在下的冒用在上的名义或礼仪、器物：～越。～妄。～伪（封建王朝称割据对立的王朝）。～盗。</p></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>僭</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 虚伪 <span class="encs">[sham;hypocritical]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">僭而无征。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">覆为我僭。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:僭乱(虚妄淆乱);僭词(虚妄之辞)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 过分 <span class="encs">[undue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不僭不滥，不敢怠遑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·殷武》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:僭溢(过分);僭奢(过分奢侈)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 差失，罪过;乱 <span class="encs">[fault;crime;in disorder]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不僭不贼。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 表示自谦 <span class="encs">[modest]</span>。如:僭先(越礼先。谦词);僭忝(谓越分愧居上位。用为谦词);僭易(冒昧、轻慢。谦词);僭谈(谦词。所说的超越本分)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/僭盗">僭盗</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/僭妄">僭妄</a></span></div><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>僭</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 超越身分，冒用在上者的职权、名义行事 <span class="encs">[exceed one’s responsibility of office]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸侯僭于天子。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庶位逾节兹谓僭。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:僭越(超出本分行事);僭拟(超越本分，自比于居上位者);僭居(超越身分而窃居);僭位(越分窃据上位)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
