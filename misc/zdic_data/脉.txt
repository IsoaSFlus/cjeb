                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">脉</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>脉</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄞˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>分布在人和动物周身内的血管：～络。～理。</li>
	<li>动脉的跳动：～搏。切～（中医指诊脉）。～口（中医切脉的部位）。～息。～象（指脉搏的形象与动态）。～门（手腕部可以看到的桡动脉跳动的部分）。诊～。</li>
	<li>像血管那样连贯而自成系统的东西：山～。叶～。矿～。～～相承。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>脉</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄛˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>[脉脉]形容用眼神表达爱慕的情意：～～含情。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">脉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>脉</strong></p>
<p>脈、衇 <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。合起来表示身体里的一种支脉。本义:血管)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[arteries and veins]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衺犹分也。衺行体中，谓血脉流转于体中也。&rdquo;</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:&ldquo;按，字俗作脉。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫脉者，血之府也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;脉要精微论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骨著脉通，与体俱生。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王符</span><span class="smcs">《潜夫论&middot;德化》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:动脉;静脉;脉脉相通(血管彼此相通)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指事物如血管连贯有条理者 <span class="encs">[vein]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雪缕青山脉，云生白鹤毛。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《隐者居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:山脉;矿脉;命脉(生命和血脉)。又指植物叶子、昆虫翅膀上像血管的组织</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 脉搏 <span class="encs">[pulse]</span>。心脏收缩时，由于输出血液的冲击引起动脉的跳动</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如今看了脉息，看小弟说的是不是。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脉气(脉搏所呈现的情况);脉候(脉搏变化的情况)</span></p>
