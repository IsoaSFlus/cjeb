						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>套</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄠˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>罩在外面的东西：褥～。手～。外～。</li>
	<li>加罩：～裤。～袖。</li>
	<li>重叠的：～版。～耕。～种（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>装在衣物里的棉絮：被～。</li>
	<li>同类事物合成的一组：一～制服。～路。～数。～曲。配～。</li>
	<li>模拟，照做：～用。～语。生搬硬～。</li>
	<li>用绳子等做成的环：牲口～。圈～（喻陷害人的布置）。</li>
	<li>栓系：～车。</li>
	<li>用谎骗取：～取。～购。～汇。</li>
	<li>河流或山势弯曲之处（大多用于地名）：河～。连环～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">套</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>套</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从大，从长。套在外面的东西比被套者要大、长。本义:罩在外面的东西)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sheath;case;cover]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:枕套;书套;钢笔套;手套</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指已成固定格式的办法或语言 <span class="encs">[convention;formula]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:套文，套言，套括(谓一定的模式、框框);套式(固定的模式);套叙(以客套话交谈);套习(俗套)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 衡量、要求事物的固定模式 <span class="encs">[convention;restriction]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:套数(手续;程式;套子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 地势弯曲的地方。常作地名用字 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">明宗</span>战 <span class="diczx2">胡卢套</span>、 <span class="diczx2">杨村</span>，为 <span class="diczx2">梁</span>兵所败。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新五代史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 特指黄河从宁夏横城堡到陕西府谷县一段或这一段围着的地区。如:河套</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 用绳子等结成的环状物 <span class="encs">[knot;loop;noose]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:牲口套;大车套;绳套</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 圈套 <span class="encs">[trap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">话说</span><span class="diczx2">唐三藏</span>固住元阳，出离了烟花苦套。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:套子</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>套</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 罩在外面 <span class="encs">[cover with;slip on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">下边穿着条香色洋布夹裤，套着双青缎子套裤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 如:套袖(套在衣袖外保护衣服)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 把棉花、丝棉等平整地装入被褥或袄里缝好 <span class="encs">[fit over]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:套棉被;套棉袄;套进去;套件衣服</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用绳索或轭具将牲口拴住或与车辆等用具拴在一起以备使用 <span class="encs">[harness]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:套车;套马;套头(套项。套在牲口脖子上的辖具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用计哄骗或诈诱 <span class="encs">[trick]</span></p>
