                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>思</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> ㄙˉ</span></p><ol><li>想，考虑，动脑筋：～想（ａ．客观存在反映在人的意识中经过思维活动而产生的结果；ｂ．想法，念头；ｃ．思量）。～忖。～索。～维。沉～。寻～。见异～迁。</li><li>想念，挂念：～念。～恋。相～。</li><li>想法：～绪。～致（新颖独到的构思、意趣）。构～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>思</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sāi.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄞˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 〔于～〕多胡须的样子，如“自捋颔下，则～～者如故矣”。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">思</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>思</strong> <span class="dicpy">sāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sāi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 多须的样子 <span class="encs">[heavily bearded]</span>。如:于思(形容胡须很多)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">si</span></p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>思</strong> <span class="dicpy">sī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从心，从囟( <span class="dicpy">xìn</span>)，囟亦声。囟脑子。古人认为心脑合作产生思想。本义:思考，想;考虑)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[think;consider]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">思，容也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">思曰容，言心之所虑，无不包也。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">钦明文思安安。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾尝终日而思矣，不如须臾之所学也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">学而不思则罔，思而不学则殆。——<span class="smcs">《论语·为政》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">老年人常思既往，少年人常思将来。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span
