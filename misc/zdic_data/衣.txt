                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>衣</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人穿在身上用以蔽体的东西：～服。～着（<span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;</span>）。～冠。～架。～锦还（<span class="dicpy">huān</span>）乡。</li>
	<li>披或包在物体外面的东西：炮～。糖～。肠～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> clothes, clothing; cover, skin</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kleidung  (S)​,Yi  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vêtements,peau,enveloppe,s'habiller,porter (un vêtement)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>衣</strong> <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形。上面象领口，两旁象袖筒，底下象两襟左右相覆，为上衣形。&ldquo;衣&rdquo;是汉字的一个部首。从&ldquo;衣&rdquo;的字与衣服有关。本义:上衣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[upper garment;jacket]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣，所以蔽体者也。上曰衣，下曰裳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一戎衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;武城》</span>。传:&ldquo;服也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薄澣我衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;葛覃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东方未明，颠倒衣裳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;东方未明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绿衣黄裳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;绿衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荀九家乾为衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜披衣坐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">披紫衣。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衣祛(衣袖);衣袖(衣服的袖子);衣裤(上衣与裤子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 服装的通称。合上衣下裳而言 <span class="encs">[clothing;clothes;garment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂曰无衣，与子同袍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;无衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无衣无褐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">易衣而出，并日而食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衣衿(秀才穿的衣服;亦指秀才的功名);衣不解带(指和衣而睡);衣不周身(衣不蔽体);衣巾寄学(以士子的身份就学);衣袄(军服);衣饭(借指谋生的职业、技能)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 器物的外罩 <span class="encs">[covering]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:衣甲(铠甲);衣车(前后有遮蔽的车子);弓衣;剑衣;衣袍(古代指覆盖在棺材外面的布罩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指苔藓等 <span class="encs">[moss]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苔衣(苔的总称);地衣(菌类植物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 涂层 <span class="encs">[coating]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:糖衣;防护衣</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 膜，薄软柔韧的片、张或层 <span class="encs">[membrane]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:花生衣</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8863.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>於希切，音依。【說文】上曰衣，下曰裳。【世本】胡曹作衣。黃帝時人。【白虎通】衣者，隱也。【釋名】衣，依也。人所以依以庇寒暑也。【玉篇】所以形軀依也。【類篇】象覆二人之形。【易&middot;繫辭】黃帝、堯、舜垂衣裳，而天下治，蓋取諸乾坤。【禮&middot;玉藻】衣，正色。裳，閒色。【傅<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>&middot;衣銘】衣服從其儀，君子德也。衣以飾外，德以飾內。</p>
<p>又絲衣，祭服也。【詩&middot;周額】絲衣其紑。</p>
<p>又【博雅】寢衣，衾，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293E9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏩</span>服也。</p>
<p>又【釋名】中衣，言在外小衣之外，大衣之中也。</p>
<p>又心衣，抱腹而施鉤肩，鉤肩之閒施一襠，以養心也。</p>
<p>又面衣。【晉書&middot;惠帝紀】尚書高光進面衣。</p>
<p>又耳衣。【唐&middot;邊塞曲】金縫耳衣寒。</p>
<p>又綴衣，掌衣服官名。【書&middot;立政】王左右常伯、常任、準人、綴衣、虎賁。</p>
<p>又白衣，未仕之稱。【後漢&middot;崔駰傳】憲諫以爲不宜與白衣會。</p>
<p>又牛衣，編亂麻爲之。卽今俗呼爲龍具者。【前漢&middot;王章傳】章疾病，無被，臥牛衣中。</p>
<p>又垣衣，苔也。【王融詩】垣衣不可裳。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】見《姓苑》。【正字通】明有衣勉仁、衣祐。</p>
<p>又人名。【高士傳】被衣，堯時人。蒲衣，舜時人。</p>
<p>又靑衣，地名。【史記&middot;彭越傳】處蜀靑衣。【註】今爲臨卭。</p>
<p>又借服膺意。【書&middot;康誥】紹聞衣德言。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】於旣切，讀去聲。服之也。【玉篇】以衣被人也。【增韻】著衣也。【晏子&middot;雜下篇】衣十升之布。【前漢&middot;東方朔傳】身衣弋綈。</p>
<p>又【韻補】於斤切。齊人言衣聲如殷，今姓有衣者，殷之謂歟。　一作㐆。【通志&middot;六書略】卽衣字，从向、身。</p>
