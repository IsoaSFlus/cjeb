                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>奏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zòu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zòu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄡˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>作乐（<span class="dicpy">yuè ㄩㄝˋ</span>），依照曲调吹弹乐器：演～。～国歌。～鸣曲。</li>    <li>封建时代臣子对皇帝陈述意见或说明事情：启～。～议。～疏。～折。～本。～对。</li>    <li>呈现，取得：～效。～功。～捷（取得胜利）。大～奇功。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>奏</strong> <span class="dicpy">zòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。上为<span class="yhcs">“屮”</span>( <span class="dicpy">chè</span>)，初生的草，有上进义;中为双手形;下为<span class="yhcs">“本”</span>( <span class="dicpy">tāo</span>)，行趋之义。本义:奉献，送上)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[present]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奏，进也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敷奏以言。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以奏肤公。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·六月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">各奏尔能。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·宾之初筵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予乘四载，随水刊木，既益奏庶鲜食。——<span class="smcs">《书·益稷》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">相如</span>奉璧奏 <span class="diczx2">秦王</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使奏其技。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以夏进炉，以冬奏扇。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·逢遇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">燕</span>姬奏妙舞， <span class="diczx2">郑</span>女发清歌。—— <span class="diczx2">萧纪</span><span class="smcs">《同萧长史看妓》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俟封奏时潜易之而己。——</span><span
