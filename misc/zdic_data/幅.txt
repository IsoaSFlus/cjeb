                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>幅</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从巾，畐( <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> )声。从<span class="yhcs">“巾”</span>表示与布帛有关。声符<span class="yhcs">“畐”</span>训<span class="yhcs">“满”</span>(参<span class="yhcs">“福”</span>字条)，在字中亦兼有表义作用。布帛的宽度(广)与<span class="yhcs">“满”</span>有相通之处，故可以引申。本义: 布帛的宽度)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。古制一幅为二尺二寸。今为布帛、呢绒等宽度的通称 <span class="encs">[width]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幅，布帛广也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">布帛二尺二寸为幅。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">半幅终幅。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“半幅一尺，终幅二尺。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邪幅在下。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采菽》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“邪缠束，故名邪幅。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幅巾。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·符融传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“以一幅为之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:单幅，双幅;宽幅的白布;幅裂(像布幅一样割裂);幅巾(古代用全幅细绢裹头的头巾。男子用以束发，也叫幞头);幅练(成幅的白练)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申指地面或书画面的广狭 <span class="encs">[width]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幅陨既长。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·长发》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幅者，所以正曲枉也。——<span class="smcs">《列女传·母仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">即连楮为巨幅，广二丈，大书一<span class="yhcs">“屈”</span>字。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:篇幅;振幅</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 界限，限度 <span class="encs">[limit]</span>。如:幅程(范围，尺度);幅利(使贪利之心有所节度)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指布帛或纸张 <span class="encs">[cloth or paper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其男子衣以横幅，但结束相连，略无缝缀。——<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>幅</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p>——记述长条或方形的东西。如:一幅画;一幅对子</p><p>◎ <strong>幅</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>覆盖，用东西遮在…上面 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p><p><span
