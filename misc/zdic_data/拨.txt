                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>拨</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">撥</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄛˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用手指或棍棒等推动或挑动：～动。把钟～准了。～冗（推开繁忙的事物，抽出时间）。～云见日。</li>	<li>分给：～发。～款。～付。</li>	<li>治理：～乱反正。</li>	<li>掉转：～转马头。</li>	<li>量词，用于成批的，分组的：分成两～儿。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">拨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>拨</strong></p>
<p>撥 <span class="dicpy">bō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，发声。本义:治理)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[administer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">撥，治也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玄王桓拨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;长发》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拨乱世，反诸正，莫近诸<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;哀公十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拨烦(治理烦杂的事务);拨乱(治理乱政;平定祸乱);拨畦(整治田畦);拨通(开导;启发)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拨动东西;分开;拨开 <span class="encs">[stir;poke]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">香炉风雪拨帘看。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《香炉峰下新卜山居草堂初成偶题东壁重题》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃奋臂以指拨眦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">试以猪鬣撩拨虫须，仍不动。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拨风(像拨开大风的样子，速度异常之快);拨火儿(拨动火种使火烧得旺，比喻挑拨);拨置(挑拨);拨嘴(拌口舌);拨正(拨动使正;曲与直)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 调拨 <span class="encs">[allocate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾与汝二万五千精兵，再拨一员上将，相助你去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拨款;拨降(发下);拨发(调拨发运);拨换(调换);拨兑(调拨;掉换)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 废弃 <span class="encs">[abandon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>拨古文。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;太史公自序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拨置(废置;挑拨);拨弃(抛开，丢弃);拨捐(犹泼弃，倾弃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 排除 <span class="encs">[get rid of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽然如此，早晚定拨冗而来。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">毕魏</span><span class="smcs">《三报恩》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 断绝，折 <span class="encs">[break]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枝叶未有害，本实先拨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;荡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 弹拨 <span class="encs">[pluck a stringed musical instrument]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/64A5.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>北末切，音鉢。【說文】治也。【詩&middot;商頌】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>王桓撥。【公羊傳&middot;哀十四年】撥亂世，反諸正，莫近於春秋。</p><p>又除也。【前漢&middot;司馬遷傳】秦撥去古文，焚滅詩書。</p><p>又發揚貌。【禮&middot;曲禮】衣毋撥。</p><p>又【增韻】捩開也。</p><p>又轉之也。【戰國策】弓撥矢鉤。【註】撥，弓反也。</p><p>又葬具。【禮&middot;檀弓】廢輴而設撥，竊禮之不中者也。【註】撥可撥引輴車，所謂紼也。</p><p>又鼓弦之物。【唐書&middot;蘇頲傳】皇甫恂使蜀，檄取庫錢，市琵琶捍撥玲瓏鞭，頲不肯予。</p><p>又叱撥，良馬名。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>蒲襏切，音跋。絕也。【詩&middot;大雅】枝葉未有害，本實先撥。箋撥，猶絕也。疏撥去餘根，故猶絕也。</p><p>又【詩&middot;朱傳】叶方吠切，音廢。</p><p>又叶必列切，音龞。義<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。</p><p>又【唐韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>普活切，音潑。芟草也。與發同。亦作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22BF8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢯸</span>。</p><p>又撥剌，張弓貌。【張衡&middot;思<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>賦】彎威弧之撥剌。【註】剌，音力達反。</p><p>又【史記&middot;索隱】房越切，音伐。謂大楯也。【史記&middot;孔子世家】會于夾谷，齊有司請奏四方之樂，于是旍旄羽祓，矛戟劒撥，鼓譟而至。【集韻】作瞂。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
