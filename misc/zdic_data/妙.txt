						<p>● <strong>妙</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">mi&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>美，好：～语。～不可言。美～。～境。～处（<span class="dicpy">ch&ugrave;</span>）（①好的地点；②美妙的方面）。绝～。～趣横生。</li>
	<li>奇巧，神奇：巧～。～计。～用。奥～。～笔生花。灵丹～药。</li>
	<li>青春年少：～年。～龄。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">妙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>妙</strong></p>
<p>玅 <span class="dicpy">mi&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从女，从少，少亦声。美妙少女。本义:美，好)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[beautiful;fine;excellent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尽所历妙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:天镜之句为妙;妙巧(美妙奇巧);妙香(特好的香气);妙丽(容貌美好);妙观(美妙的观察);妙引(佳美的文辞);妙色(庄严或美丽的色彩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 神妙 <span class="encs">[wonderful;marvellous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其当是其妙处不传。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;文学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妙理(神妙的道理);妙略(夺谋，妙计);妙契(神妙的契合);妙用(神妙的作用);妙笔(神妙之笔。指书画或文字的高手)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 精妙;精微 <span class="encs">[exquisite and ingenious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常无欲以观其妙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王</span>注:&ldquo;妙者，微之极也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所知者妙矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;审分》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽妙必效情。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;律书》</span>。注:&ldquo;妙，谓微妙之性也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妙尽琁机之正。(琁机:玉饰的测天仪器。正:则，译为原理)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妙旨(精微幽深的旨意);妙言要道(精到的言论，中肯的道理);妙绪(精妙的思绪，思想);妙思(精妙的构思);妙演(精妙的阐述);妙说(意旨精微的言论)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 巧妙，高明 <span class="encs">[ingenious;subtle;clever;wise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众妙毕备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆服其妙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:以为妙绝;妙着(高明的棋着;好办法);妙品(精巧美妙的物品);妙远(高妙深远);妙迹(指佳妙的书法作品);妙旨(好的意见，计谋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;眇&rdquo;。细微，幼小，年少 <span class="encs">[very small;young]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所知者妙矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;审分》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">终军以妙年使越，欲得长缨占其王。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《求自试表》</span></p>
