<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>探</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>寻求：～求。～讨。～索。～试。勘～。试～。钻～。～幽访胜。～本穷源。</li>
	<li>侦察打听：～问。～听。～询。～查。～察。</li>
	<li>做侦察工作的人：～马（侦察骑兵）。敌～。密～。</li>
	<li>访问，看望：～望。～亲。～监。</li>
	<li>头或上体伸出，手伸入：～身。～头～脑。～囊取物。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">探</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>探</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，罙( <span class="dicpy">shēn</span>)声。本义:摸取)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fish for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">探，远取之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，远取犹深取也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">探，取也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:&ldquo;摸取也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必探筹而定分。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《铨言》</span>。注:&ldquo;捉筹也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则惟尔多方探天之威。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;多方》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见不善如探汤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;季氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不待探筹投钩而公。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;君道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日中如探汤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">探石发穴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:探丸(摸取药丸;又指摸取弹丸以决定谋杀官吏之事);探囊(伸手到袋中摸取)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 探求 <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前来一探。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:探本(探求根本);探真(探求玄理);探意(探求本意);探微(探索微妙的事理);探源(寻求源头;探求本源)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 打听 <span class="encs">[ask about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">探，试也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释言》</span>。注:&ldquo;刺探尝试。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:探声候气(探听消息，等候动静);探事(探听消息);探讯(探信，打听消息);探侦(打听)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 看望 <span class="encs">[pay call on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蓬山此去无多路，青鸟殷勤为探看。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《无题》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:探望;探病(看望病人);探房(新娘过门的次日，娘家人前往看望)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 伸出 <span class="encs">[stretch]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:探爪(探手。伸手);探头探脑(不断伸头张望)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 彻底调查或考察 <span class="encs">[explore]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:探穴;探路;探研(探讨研究);探流;探综(探究贯通)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 预先支付 <span class="encs">[prepay]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:探支(预支);探借(预借)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 侦察 <span class="encs">[scout]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">探颐索隐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span>。疏:&ldquo;窥探。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:探细(侦察);探刺(暗中侦察);探伺(侦察);探虎穴(冒险);探使(负有侦察任务的使者);探候(侦察打听);探报(谍报)</span></p>
