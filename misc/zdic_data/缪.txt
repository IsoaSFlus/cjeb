                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缪</strong></p>
<p>繆 <span class="dicpy">mi&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mi&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 纰缪;错误 <span class="encs">[wrong;false]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多辞缪说，不耕而食，不织而衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;盗跖》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缪舛(舛错，谬误);缪戾(错舛，违背);缪悠(妄诞不经);缪说(荒谬错误的言论);缪妄(荒缪无稽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;穆&rdquo;。穆然，静思 <span class="encs">[solemn and silent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>有所缪然思焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语&middot;辨乐》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;穆&rdquo;。虔诚 <span class="encs">[reverent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">太公</span><span class="diczx2">召公</span>乃缪卜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;鲁周公世家》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缪</strong></p>
<p>繆 <span class="dicpy">mi&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mi&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 诈伪 <span class="encs">[cheat;defraud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故</span><span class="diczx2">相如</span>缪与令相重。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缪数(诈伪之术);缪丑(宋秦桧之谥。借指奸佞)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;樛&rdquo;。绞结 <span class="encs">[twist;wring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其妻鲁人也，衣衰而缪緻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即自缪死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;外戚传下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;勠&rdquo;。合力，并力 <span class="encs">[join in a common effort;unite efforts]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔我先君</span><span class="diczx2">穆公</span>及 <span class="diczx2">楚成王</span>是(寔)缪力同心，两邦若壹，绊以婚姻。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">秦</span>&middot;<span class="smcs">《诅楚文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;缭&rdquo;。缭绕 <span class="encs">[curl up;wind around]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缪绕玉绥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">mi&agrave;o;m&oacute;u</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>缪</strong></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中" target="_blank">未集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中&amp;bs=糸" target="_blank">糸</a>】
                              繆·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E46.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】武彪切【集韻】亡幽切【正韻】莫彪切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䋷。【說文】枲之十絜也。一曰綢繆。【詩&middot;豳風】綢繆牖戶。【傳】綢繆，言纏綿也。【莊子&middot;則陽篇】聖人達綢繆。【音義】綢繆，猶纏綿。</p><p>又云：深奧也。</p><p>又【廣韻】莫浮切，音謀。【集韻】力求切，音劉。義<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。</p><p>又【集韻】居虬切【韻會】居尤切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音樛。【禮&middot;檀弓】衣衰而繆絰。【註】繆，當爲不樛垂也之樛。【疏】繆絰，謂絞麻爲絰。【前漢&middot;孝成趙皇后傳】卽自繆死。【註】師古曰：繆，絞也。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>眉救切，音謬。【禮&middot;大傳】五者一物紕繆。【註】紕繆，猶錯也。【釋文】繆，本作謬。</p><p>又【仲尼燕居】不能詩於禮繆。【註】繆，誤也。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】臨邛令繆爲恭敬。【註】繆，詐也。</p><p>又【于定國傳】何以錯繆至是。【註】繆，違也。</p><p>又姓。【史記&middot;申公傳】蘭陵繆生。【註】索隱曰：繆氏，出蘭陵。【正字通】今姓繆讀若妙，變音，非本音也。</p><p>又【集韻】朗鳥切，音了。繚或作繆。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】繆繞王綏。【註】繆繞，相纏結也。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>莫六切，音穆。與穆同。【禮&middot;大傳】序以昭繆。【註】繆，讀如穆。【公羊傳&middot;隱三年】葬宋繆公。【釋文】繆，左氏作穆。【史記&middot;魯世家】太公召公乃繆卜。【註】徐廣曰：古書穆字多作繆。</p><p>又【集韻】憐蕭切，音聊。繆繆，絲貌。</p><p>又【集韻】力弔切，音嫽。蟉或作繆。蜩蟉，龍首動貌。</p>
