<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>溆</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Xu River]</span>。古名序水，溆水，溆浦，溆川。又名双龙江。源出湖南省溆浦县东南山中，西北流，至溆浦县城东南，又折向西流入沅江</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入</span><span class="diczx2">溆浦</span>余儃佪兮，迷不知吾所如。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 水边 <span class="encs">[edge of a river]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长飚落江树，秋月照沙溆。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">何逊</span><span class="smcs">《赠江长史别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">追欢于林溆之地。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吴锡麒</span><span class="smcs">《游西山记》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
