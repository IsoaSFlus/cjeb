                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戶</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>戶</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>一扇門，門：門～。窗～。～樞不蠹。夜不閉～。</li>
	<li>人家：～口。～主。門～之見（亦指派別上的成見）。</li>
	<li>會計部門稱帳冊上有業務關係的團體或個人：～頭。開～。</li>
	<li>門第：門當～對。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> door; family, household</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Radikal Nr. 63 = Waffe, Tür, Haus, Familie (Varianten: 户, 戸)​  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> famille,foyer,foyer,famille,compte,porte,(classificateur pour les familles, les foyers)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>戶</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象門(門)字的一半。漢字部首之一。从&ldquo;戶&rdquo;的多與門戶有關。本義:單扇門)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本義 <span class="encs">[door]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戶，半門曰戶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《說文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一扇曰戶，兩扇曰門。又在於堂室東曰戶，在於宅區域曰門。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字書》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窺其戶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;豐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">設於戶西。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儀禮&middot;聘禮》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未有入室而不由戶者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《禮記&middot;禮器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木蘭當戶織。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《樂府詩集&middot;木蘭詩》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">當戶理紅妝。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出戶望南山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">幹寶</span><span class="smcs">《搜神記》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出戶南望。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窗戶皆閉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大鐵椎傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:戶下(門邊;戶住的屬下。多指奴婢或門客);戶牡(門鑰);戶門(守門的人);戶鑰(門上開關的鎖鑰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 住戶，人家。一家稱一戶 <span class="encs">[family]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其邑人三百戶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;訟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戶說以眇論。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;貨殖列傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戶裁及萬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵長蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青門剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戶出一男。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爲一戶者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:全戶人口;幾百戶人家;戶帖(登記每戶人口籍貫、名義的冊子);戶版(登記居民戶籍的簿冊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 戶籍(登記戶口的冊籍) <span class="encs">[census register]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:戶版(戶籍;戶口);戶貫(戶籍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 屋室 <span class="encs">[house]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">初九，不出戶庭，無咎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;節卦&middot;象辭》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不出戶知天下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子&middot;四七》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 出入口 <span class="encs">[hole]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:洞戶(洞穴，洞口)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 酒量 <span class="encs">[capacity for liquor;one&#39;s drinking capacity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酒戶年年減，山行漸漸難。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《元稹&middot;春遊詩》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 從事某種職業的人或家庭 <span class="encs">[a person or family of some occupations]</span>。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6236.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㦿<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23958.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣥘</span>【唐韻】【正韻】侯古切【集韻】【韻會】後五切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音祜。【說文】護也。【釋名】所以謹護閉塞也。【六書精薀】室之口也。凡室之口曰戶，堂之口曰門。內曰戶，外曰門。一扉曰戶，兩扉曰門。【易&middot;節卦】不出戶庭。【詩&middot;豳風】塞向墐戶。</p>
<p>又民居曰編戶。【唐六典】戶部掌天下戶口。</p>
<p>又止也。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】屈蕩戶之。【註】止也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;王嘉傳】坐戶殿門失闌免。【註】掌守殿門，止不當入者，而失闌入之。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋地】觚竹北戶。【註】北戶，南方之國。【疏】卽日南郡也。師古曰：言其在日之南，所謂北戶以向日者。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;月令】蟄蟲咸動，啓戶始出。【註】戶，猶穴也。</p>
<p>又飮酒有大小戶。【吳志】孫皓每饗宴，人以七升爲限，小戶雖不入，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>澆灌取盡。【白居易詩】戶大嫌甜酒。</p>
<p>又姓。漢有戶尊。</p>
<p>又與昈通。文采貌。【揚雄&middot;蜀都賦】戶豹能黃。</p>
<p>又與濩通。湯樂名。【揚雄&middot;蜀都賦】戶音六成。</p>
<p>又翁姑切，音烏。【淮南子&middot;時則訓】烏孫國，作戶孫。</p>
