						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>触</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">觸</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>抵、顶：抵～。“羝羊～藩，赢其角”。</li><li>碰，撞：～礁。～电。～动。～摸。～发。～犯。～怒。</li><li>遇着：接～。～觉（<span class="dicpy">jué</span>）。～目惊心。～角（<span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span>）。～景生情。～类旁通。</li><li>因某种刺激而引起感情变化：感～。忽有所～。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>触</strong></p><p>觸 <span class="dicpy">chù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从角，蜀声。本义:以角撞物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用角抵人或物 <span class="encs">[butt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">触，牴也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兽穷则触。——<span class="smcs">《新序杂事》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">触槐而死。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">羝羊触藩。——<span class="smcs">《易·大壮》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:触藩(以角触撞藩篱);触藩羝羊(公羊抵触藩篱，角被挂，无法摆脱很焦急)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 撞，碰 <span class="encs">[strike;hit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">手之所触。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·养生主》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:触损(伤害);触抵(抵触顶撞);触搏(撞击)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 接触 <span class="encs">[touch;contact]</span>。如:触物(接触景物、事物);触桶(便桶);触冒(接触，冒着)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 遇到;遭受 <span class="encs">[meet with;suffer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">触草木，尽死。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:触受(触及，遭受);触值(遭遇);触祸(遭受祸殃)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 触动 <span class="encs">[move one's heart]</span>。如:触情(因遇到外物而兴起的感情或感想);触兴(遇到外物而兴起感想)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 干犯，冒犯 <span class="encs">[offend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">去礼义，触刑法。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·元帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:触突(触犯;刺激);触抵(触犯罪过);触冒(冒犯，冲犯)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>触</strong></p><p>觸 <span class="dicpy">chù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 佛家语。与色、声、香、味、法合称<span class="yhcs">“六尘”</span> <span class="encs">[touch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人之嗜欲，著于声、色、香、味、触、法，而仁义即由嗜欲而起。——</span><span class="diczx2">章炳麟</span><span class="smcs">《菌说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/触处">触处</a></span><span class="crefe"><a                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/89E6.svg">						  <p>【古今注】音紅。白魚赤尾者曰触。一曰魧。或曰雌者曰白魚，雄者曰触魚。亥集有<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29DA5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩶥</span>字，同。魧，原从角从亢作。</p><p>又<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29D78.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩵸</span>，魧字之譌。</p><p>（觸）〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/278BB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧢻</span>【唐韻】尺玉切【集韻】【韻會】樞玉切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>衝入聲。【說文】牴也。【易·大壯】羝羊觸籓。【前漢·元帝紀】去禮義，觸<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法。【荀子·議兵篇】觸之者角摧。【揚子·太<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>經】星辰不相觸。</p><p>又【玉篇】據也。【增韻】污也。</p><p>又觸衣。【本草綱目】褌襠，一名觸衣。</p><p>又人名。【左傳·襄十一年】鄭人賂晉侯以師觸。【註】樂師名。</p><p>又姓。【史記·趙世家】左師觸龍。</p><p>又【集韻】昌句切，芻去聲。亦牴也。【揚雄·羽獵賦】票禽之紲踰，犀兕之牴觸，熊羆之拏攫，虎豹之凌遽。觸叶遽，遽音詎。師古讀。　【集韻】亦作觕。</p><p>（觸）
