						<p>● <strong>捂</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">wǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>严密地遮盖住或封闭起来：～住。～盖子（亦喻掩盖矛盾）。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/迕">迕</a>&rdquo;，逆，对面。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>捂</strong></p>
<p>摀 <span class="dicpy">wǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，吾声。本义:用手扪住)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cover]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:把什么事都捂着;捂眼儿;捂鼻子</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 抵触 <span class="encs">[conflict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人心不同，闻见异辞，</span><span class="diczx2">班</span>氏所谓疏略抵捂者，依违不悉辩也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记集解》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 撑持;斜拄 <span class="encs">[support]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">到得这家计坏了，更支捂不住。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《朱子语类》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 没入;陷入 <span class="encs">[sink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他用手把蒿草下边的沙刨开，果真找见了湿沙子。他把嘴捂在沙子里吸呀吸呀，什么水分也吸不出。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜鹏程</span><span class="smcs">《保卫延安》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>捂</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>同&ldquo;迕&rdquo;。逆;对面 <span class="encs">[contrary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若无器，则捂受之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;既夕礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">贾公彦</span>疏:&ldquo;捂即逆也，对面相逢受也。&rdquo;</p>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">牴觸。</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;張自烈《正字通&middot;手部》：「<b>捂</b>，相抵觸也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;柳宗元〈送元十八山人南遊序〉：「又況楊、墨、申、商、刑名、縱橫之說，其迭相訾毀抵<b>捂</b>不合者，可勝言耶。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">遮掩、遮擋。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「縱有千隻手，難<b>捂</b>萬人口。」</span></p></li>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              捂·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6342.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>五故切，音誤。斜相抵觸也。</p>
<p>又逆也。【儀禮&middot;旣夕】若無器則捂受之。【疏】對面相逆受也。</p>
<p>又斜拄也。【通雅】枝梧，猶支拄抵捂也。史記項籍紀云：莫敢枝梧。註云：小柱爲枝，邪柱爲梧，當作捂。</p>
<p>又漢書司馬遷傳贊云：或有柢梧。註云：柢，觸也。梧，相支拄不安也。亦抵捂譌作柢梧。互詳梧字註。　【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22AF8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢫸</span>。</p>
