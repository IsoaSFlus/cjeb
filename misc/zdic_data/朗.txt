                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>朗</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从月，良声。本义:明亮)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[light;bright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朗，明也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高朗令终。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·既醉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其圣能光远宣朗。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">新而不朗。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朗月垂光。——</span><span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《琴赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">复行数十步，豁然开朗。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天朗气清。——</span><span class="diczx2">王羲之</span><span class="smcs">《兰亭集序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:朗白(明亮发白);朗朗(光明亮洁的样子;声音清脆嘹亮);朗鉴(明鉴);朗亮(雪亮);朗明(明亮);朗净(明净);朗烈(明亮);朗朗烈烈(大大方方;理直气壮)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 声音清晰响亮 <span class="encs">[loud and clear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朗咏清川飞夜霜。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《劳劳亭歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:朗言(响亮的话;说大话;任意胡说);朗然(形容声音响亮);朗吟(高声的吟诵);朗弹(大声弹奏);朗畅(谓声音响亮流畅)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 明察 <span class="encs">[sharp-minded]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公达潜朗。——</span><span class="diczx2">袁宏</span><span class="smcs">《三国名臣序赞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx2">公瑾</span>英达朗心独见。</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:朗心(明察之心;光明之心);朗照(明察，明鉴);朗抱(旷达的胸襟);朗畅(明白畅达);朗朗彻(明白透彻);朗练(明白凝炼)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 高洁 <span class="encs">[noble and unsullied]</span> 。如:朗目清眉(眉清目秀);朗秀(清秀);朗夷(高洁坦荡);朗烈(高洁刚烈)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 清早 <span class="encs">[early]</span>。如:朗旦(清晨)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 清澈 <span class="encs">[limpid]</span>。如:朗清(清澈，清明)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 爽朗 <span class="encs">[bright and clear]</span>。如:朗爽(爽朗;明朗);朗畅(爽朗舒展);朗迈(爽朗超脱)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 颖悟
