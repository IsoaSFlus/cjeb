<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閡</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄜˊ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>阻隔不通：隔～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> blocked or separated; to prevent</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> versperren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faire obstacle,empêcher,obstruer</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阂</strong></p>
<p>閡 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，亥声。( <span class="dicpy">&agrave;i</span>)本义:从门外关门)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 阻隔，不畅通;妨碍 <span class="encs">[hinder;obstruct;separate;block]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不令有所拘阂而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;虞诩传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学而不思，则疑阂实繁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《抱朴子&middot;博喻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阂滞(阻碍);阂目(视线被山所遮蔽);阂绝(隔绝，不相通);阂窒(阻塞);阂山(为山所阻隔);阂塞(阻隔不通)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥形虽大，斯脚则企，跳步雀踊，踵不阂也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《山海经图赞》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 限制 <span class="encs">[limit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阂，限也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恢万里使无阂，通亿载而为津。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《文赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世岂乏材，阂于资格故也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金史》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">閡</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“阂”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/閡">閡</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閡·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95A1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】五漑切【集韻】【韻會】牛代切【正韻】牛蓋切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音礙。【說文】外閉也。从門，亥，意兼聲。【玉篇】止也。【易&middot;蒙卦註】退則困險，進則閡山。【疏】坎在艮下，是山下有險，恐進退不可，故蒙昧也。</p>
<p>又【中孚註】欲進而閡敵，故或鼓也。【左思&middot;吳都賦】寒暑隔閡於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/285C9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨗉</span>宇。【註】閡，五蓋反。【抱朴子&middot;仁明卷】學而不思，則疑閡實繁。</p>
<p>又【廣譬卷】靈鳳所以晨起丹穴，夕萃軒丘，日未移晷，周章九陔，凌風蹈雲，不䟾不閡者，以其六翮之輕勁也。</p>
<p>又【列子&middot;黃帝篇】和者大同於物，物無得傷閡者，遊金石蹈水火，皆可也。【釋文】閡，音艾。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>下攺切，音亥。藏塞也。【前漢&middot;律歷志】該藏萬物，而雜陽閡種。【註】孟康曰：閡，藏塞也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】古代切【集韻】戸代切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音漑。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】紇則切，音劾。礙也。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】苦亥切，音愷。開也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶音攺。【前漢&middot;郊祀歌】厲意逝九閡，紛紜六幕浮大海。【釋文】叶音攺。</p>
