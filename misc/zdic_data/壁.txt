                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>壁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>墙：四～。～报。～画。～挂。～毯。～橱。～灯。铜墙铁～。</li>    <li>指某些物体内部的表层：胃～。肠～。</li>    <li>陡削的山崖：峭～。～立。</li>    <li>军营的围墙：～垒。坚～清野。作～上观（坐观双方成败，不帮助任何一方）。</li>    <li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">壁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>壁</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，辟声。本义:墙壁)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壁，垣也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壁亦土也。——<span class="smcs">《风俗通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">观壁垒于北落兮。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">家徒四壁立。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马相如传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徘徊四顾，见虫伏壁上。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:壁有耳(指机密容易泄露，说话不可不慎。同<span class="yhcs">“隔墙有耳”</span>);壁中书(汉武帝时，鲁恭王为扩建其宫室而坏孔子故居，在夹壁墙中得古文<span class="smcs">《尚书》</span>、<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>、<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>、<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>、<span class="smcs">《孝经》</span>等，凡数十卷，都是周时的古文写的，晋人称为科斗文。今<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>中所收古文，大部分是依据壁中书);壁观(面壁;又指一意禅观，心如墙壁，一切妄想不能侵入)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 物体、身体或生物体等的外围结构的物质层 <span class="encs">[wall]</span>。如:试管内壁;肌壁;腹壁;细胞壁</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 军营;军营的围墙 <span class="encs">[barracks and the enclosing walls of barrack]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>击 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>，诸将皆从壁上观。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帝(</span><span class="diczx2">刘邦</span>)晨驰入 <span class="diczx2">韩信</span>、 <span class="diczx2">张耳</span>壁，夺之军。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《高帝纪上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:壁死(死于营垒之中);壁门(军营大门)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 陡峭的山崖 <span class="encs">[cliff]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山四面峭壁拔起。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《苦斋记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:绝壁;峭壁;壁立千仞(形容山崖陡峻)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 二十八宿之一，是北方七宿之一 <span class="encs">[one of the Chinese zodiacal constellations]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 边，面 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一壁道与红娘:看哥哥行问汤药去也。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">王实甫</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:壁厢(边，旁)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>壁</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>驻扎</p><p><span class="diczx2">魏王</span>恐，使人出 <span class="diczx2">晋鄙</span>，留军壁 <span class="diczx2">邺</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/58C1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】北激切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】必歷切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音繴。【說文】垣也。【釋名】辟也，辟禦風寒也。【孔安國·尚書序】秦始皇焚書，我先人用藏其家書于屋壁。【水衡記】張僧繇于金陵安樂寺畫四龍于壁，不點睛，每曰點之卽飛去。人以爲誕，因點其一，須臾雷電破壁，一龍乗雲上天。</p><p>又軍壘。【前漢·高帝紀】帝晨馳入韓信張耳壁，奪之軍。【周亞夫傳】吳楚反，亞夫救梁，引兵走昌邑，堅壁而守。</p><p>又星名。二十八宿之一。【晉書·天文志】東壁二星主文籍，天下圖書之府。</p><p>又地名。壁州，在三巴，本漢宕渠地，唐高祖武德初置，辟州節度使鄭畋有記。</p><p>又赤壁，在蒲圻。【荆州記】蒲圻縣沿江南岸百里，名赤壁，昔周瑜破曹操處。黃州赤壁乃赤鼻山。【水經注】江水左逕赤鼻山，下爲赤鼻磯。蘇軾赤壁前賦及長短句，人道是三國周郞赤壁，蓋傳疑也。</p><p>又凡石厓之峭削皆曰壁。【馬岌·石壁銘】靑壁千尋。【江淹詩】緬映石壁素。【神仙傳】帛和入西城山，王公令熟視石壁，二年漸覺有文字，三年得神丹方及五嶽圖。　本作壁，十四畫。說文、經史皆同。字彙从省作壁，入十三畫，因之。壁字从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2840C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨐌</span>作。</p>
