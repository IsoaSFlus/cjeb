<p><span class="dicpy">jǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄥˇ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阱</strong></p>
<p>宑 <span class="dicpy">jǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span>)，表示与地形地势的高低上下有关，井声。本义:陷阱，为捕捉野兽的坑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[trap;pitfall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阱，陷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使入陷阱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志下》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;阱，穿地以陷兽也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春令为阱获沟渎之利于民者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;雍氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罟获陷阱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猛虎落陷阱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《君马黄》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:猛虎落阱;阱鄂(捕野兽的陷坑和笼子);阱机(本指捕捉动物的陷阱。喻指陷害别人的计谋)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻害人的圈套 <span class="encs">[pitfall]</span>。如:这是敌人设置的一个阱</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 在地下掘的用来囚拘人的地方 <span class="encs">[pit]</span>。如:阱房(地牢;牢房);阱室(地牢)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>阱</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>设陷阱捕兽 <span class="encs">[trap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">釜兵阱兽，固不久就毙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明清史资料丙编》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9631.svg">                          <p>陷也。从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E0F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨸏</span>从井，井亦聲。穽，阱或从穴。汬，古文阱从水。疾正切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9631_7337.svg" /><span class="swbf">(阱)</span><span class="ywyy">陷也。</span><span class="swdz">穿地陷獸。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E0F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨸏</span>丼。</span><span class="swdz">於大陸作之如井。</span><span class="ywyy">丼亦聲。</span><span class="swdz">疾正切。十一部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9631_7338.svg" /><span class="swbf">(穽)</span><span class="ywyy">阱或从穴。</span><span class="swdz">中庸音義曰：阱本作穽，同。引說文穽或阱字也。今本釋文於或下妄沾爲字。按古本說文多云某或某字，見於經典釋文者往往如是。周禮注所謂古字多或也。今本說文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧗊</span>改之云某或作某，非古也。若讀釋文竄改者，則益可<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23891.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣢑</span>矣。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9631_7339.svg" /><span class="swbf">(汬)</span><span class="ywyy">古文阱，从水。</span><span class="swdz">鍇本作阱或从水。玉篇云：古文作汬。</span></p>                          </div>
