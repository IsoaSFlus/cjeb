                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>使</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˇ</span></p><ol><li>用：～用。～劲。～役。～力。～钱。</li><li>派，差谴：～唤。～命。～女。</li><li>让，令，叫：迫～。</li><li>假若：假～。即～。</li><li>奉命办事的人：～者。大～。公～。～馆。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">使</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>使</strong> <span class="dicpy">shǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，吏声。从人表示人的动作行为。本义:命令)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使，伶也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，伶者，令也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故君使其臣。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·表记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓使之聘问师役之属也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">扶苏</span>以数谏故，上使外将兵。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">怀王</span>使屈原造为宪令。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">魏王</span>使将军 <span class="diczx2">晋鄙</span>将十万众救 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:支使(命令人去做事)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 派遣 <span class="encs">[send;dispatch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃使</span><span class="diczx2">蒙恬</span>北筑长城而守藩篱。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郑穆公</span>使视客馆，则束载，厉兵，秣马矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:差使(差遣;派遣);使转(打发去);使遣(差遣);使会(差遣，使唤)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 致使;让;叫 <span class="encs">[let;make;cause]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不以一己之利为利，而使天下受其利。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">到那时使吾眼睁睁看汝死，或使汝眼睁睁看我死，吾能之乎?——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:一场大火，使他一贫如洗;虚心使人进步</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 运用;使用 <span class="encs">[use;employ]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">联闻，使功不如使过。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·李靖传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">造父</span>巧于使马。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尚贤使能。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:使费(使用钱财);使低嘴(说坏话);使不着(用不着);使民(使用民力);使风(利用风力);使指(使用手指);使官(利用职权);使弊幸(用心计);使贤任能(任用有品德有才能的人);使钱出土</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 使唤;役使;支使 <span class="encs">[order about;work;use]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">节用而爱人，使民以时。——<span class="smcs">《论语·学而》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民之外事，莫难于战，故轻法不可以使之。——<span
