                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">霸</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“䩗”字头，请参考“霸”字：)</span>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=月">月</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9738.svg">                          <p>月始生，霸然也。承大月，二日；承小月，三日。从月䨣聲。《周書》曰：“哉生霸。”<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23378.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣍸</span>，古文霸。普伯切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗作必駕切。以爲霸王字。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9738_7491.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(霸)</span><span class="ywyy">月始生魄然也。</span><span class="swdz">霸魄㬪韵。</span><span class="ywyy">承大月二日。承小月三日。</span><span class="swdz">鄉飮酒義曰。月者三日則成魄。正義云。前月大則月二日生魄。前月小則三日始生魄。馬注康誥云。魄、胐也。謂月三日始生兆胐。名曰魄。白虎通曰。月三日成魄。八日成光。按巳上皆謂月初生明爲霸。而律歷志曰。死霸、朔也。生霸、望也。孟康曰。月二日以往明生魄死。故言死魄。魄、月質也。三統說是。則前說非矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从月。䨣聲。</span><span class="swdz">普伯切。書音義引說文匹革反。古音在五部。漢志所引武成、顧命皆作霸。後代魄行而霸廢矣。俗用爲王霸字。實伯之叚借字也。</span><span class="ywyy">周書曰。哉生霸。</span><span class="swdz">康誥顧命文。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9738_7492.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23378.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣍸</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文或作此。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
