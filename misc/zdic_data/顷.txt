                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>顷</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">頃</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄥˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>中国市制田地面积单位：一～（等于一百亩）。碧波万～。</li>
	<li>短时间：～刻。有～。少～。俄～（很短的时间）。</li>
	<li>刚才，不久以前：～闻。～接来信。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a moment; a measure of area equal to 100 mu or 6.7 hectares; to lean</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> vor kurzem, vorhin</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> un moment,à présent,100 meou (6,11 hectares)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">顷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>顷</strong></p>
<p>頃 <span class="dicpy">qīng</span></p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从匕( <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span>)，从页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>)。匕有排列比较义，也有不正之义。页，头。合起来表示头偏。本义:头不正)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[inclined to side]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顷，头不正也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;顷者，头也。&rdquo; <span class="diczx2">徐灏</span>注:&ldquo;顷、倾古今字。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;倾&rdquo;。倾斜;偏侧 <span class="encs">[inclined;slanting]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妇人擅国兹谓顷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五行志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不盈顷筐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;卷耳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如: 顷筐(倾斜口的竹筐。形似今之畚箕);顷危(倾斜而危险);顷宫(十分高大的宫殿);顷倒(倒下)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">qǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>顷</strong></p>
<p>頃 <span class="dicpy">qǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>田地面积的单位，等于一百亩 <span class="encs">[qing，a unit of area(=6.6667 hectares)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡为田一顷十二亩半。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;宣公十五年》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筑为顷宫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;过理》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一碧万顷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有田一顷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食田一顷。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田百顷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:碧波万顷;顷亩(百亩之地。泛指土地、田地);顷晦(顷亩);顷宫(占地一顷的宫殿);一顷地</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>顷</strong></p>
<p>頃 <span class="dicpy">qǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 少顷，短时间 <span class="encs">[moment;instant;a little while]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莅政有顷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span>。注:&ldquo;言未久。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不为顷久推移。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:俄顷(很短的时间);顷之(未几，不久);顷久(短暂与长久);顷息(喘息的时间);顷顷(片刻);顷然(一会儿)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 往昔;当时 <span class="encs">[past;at that time;then]</span>。</p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“顷”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/頃">頃</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=頁" target="_blank">頁</a>】
                              頃·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：2</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9803.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕頔【廣韻】去穎切【集韻】【韻會】犬潁切【正韻】丘潁切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>傾上聲。【玉篇】田百畝爲頃。【後漢&middot;黃憲傳】叔度汪汪若千頃波。</p>
<p>又俄頃。【禮&middot;三年問】小者至於燕雀，猶有啁噍之頃焉。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;哀十二年】宋鄭之閒有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻶</span>地焉，曰頃丘。【音】苦潁反，又音傾。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】去營切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】窺營切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>與傾同。【說文】頭不正也。</p>
<p>又器名。【詩&middot;周南】采采卷耳，不盈頃筐。《韓詩》云：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22F7D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢽽</span>筐也。</p>
<p>又【諡法】甄心動懼曰頃。敏以敬愼曰頃。</p>
<p>又西頃，山名。【前漢&middot;地理志】隴西郡臨洮，禹貢西頃山在縣西。【師古註】頃讀曰傾。今本禹貢作傾。西頃卽西傾也。○按廣韻作地名，非。</p>
<p>又【正韻】犬蘂切。與跬同。【禮&middot;祭義】君子頃步而弗敢忘孝也。【註】頃當爲跬，缺婢反。</p>
<p>又丘弭反。一舉足爲跬。再舉足爲步。【說文】从匕从頁。【徐鉉曰】匕者，有所比附不正也。</p>
