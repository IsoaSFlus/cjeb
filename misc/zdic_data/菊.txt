                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>菊</strong> <span class="dicpy">jú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，匊( <span class="dicpy">jú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jú.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:植物名，通称<span class="yhcs">“菊花”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chrysanthemun]</span>。多年生草本植物。叶子卵形有柄，边缘有缺刻或锯齿。秋季开花，供观赏，有的品种可入药</p><p><span class="diczx1">采菊东篱下。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《饮酒诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">松菊犹存。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">还束就菊花。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《钱塘湖春行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span>独爱菊。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">菊，花之隐逸者。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">菊之爱，</span><span class="diczx2">陶</span>后鲜有闻。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:菊实(菊花的果实、种子);菊枕(菊花枕头);菊灯(菊花形状的灯笼);菊水(在河南省西南部。古作<span class="yhcs">“鞠水”</span>，也叫<span class="yhcs">“菊潭。”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓。如:菊部头(菊部。宋高宗时官中伶人有菊夫人者，人称<span class="yhcs">“菊部头”</span>。后因以<span class="yhcs">“菊部”</span>为戏班或戏曲界的泛称)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/菊花">菊花</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/菊月">菊月</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
