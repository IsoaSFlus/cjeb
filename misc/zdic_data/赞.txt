                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>赞</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">贊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">z&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>帮助，辅佐：～助。～划。～襄（协助）。～理。～成。</li>
	<li>主持礼仪：～礼。</li>
	<li>夸奖，称扬：～叹。～许。～扬。～和（<span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span>）。～颂。～赏。～词。～誉。称～。</li>
	<li>一种文体，用于颂扬人物：小～。像～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赞</strong></p>
<p>贊、賛 <span class="dicpy">z&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意本义:进财货以求谒见)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[introduce;present]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赞，见也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，玉帛雉羔雁之属皆贝类。贝者，佐见之具。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">进见以见为礼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span><span class="diczx2">锴</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赞谒(进见);赞献(进献祭品);赞见(进见)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 导引 <span class="encs">[guide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太史赞王，王敬从之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赞引(引导);赞见(引见);赞谕(引导教谕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 辅佐，佐助 <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赞，佐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">益赞于禹曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大禹谟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主人之赞者西面北上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡饮酒礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿大夫赞君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;明堂位》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子若能以忠信赞君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赞帷幄之谋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此天以卿二人赞孤也!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赞军校尉。(武官名，相当于参谋。)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赞善(官名。唐始置，为太子属官，掌司从、辅佐);赞元理化(辅佐皇帝推行德化);赞翼(辅助);赞赞(努力帮助)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 称颂;赞美 <span class="encs">[praise;eulogize]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自赞于</span><span class="diczx2">平原君</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心计交赞之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赞拜(臣子朝拜皇帝时，赞礼的人在旁唱礼);盛赞(极力称赞);赞尚(看重别人的长处并加以称扬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 告诉;介绍 <span class="encs">[make known; acquaint one with; inform; introduce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公子引</span><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>坐上坐，遍赞宾客。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 明白 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">总百氏，赞篇章。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赞阅(明察历练)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 参与 <span class="encs">[participate in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子夏</span>之徒不能赞一辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
