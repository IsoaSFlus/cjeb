                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">窟</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>窟</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>洞穴：石～。狡兔三～。～窿。</li>	<li>人聚集的地方，多指歹徒聚集之所：匪～。魔～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hole, cave; cellar; underground</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Höhle  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grotte,caverne,repaire,tanière</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">窟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>窟</strong> <span class="dicpy">kū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，屈声。本义:土穴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窟，兔崛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作窟。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兔之所息谓之窟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广兽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兽穴曰窟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span>。按，凡穴土皆曰堀。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西厌月窟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者先王未有官室，冬则居营窟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窟弄(洞;孔);窟窍(窟窿;洞);窟窦(洞空貌);窟宠(窟窿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 洞穴<span class="encs">[cavity;hole;cave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狡兔有三窟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:石窟;窟穴(洞穴);窟窦(洞穴);窟窖(地窖);:窟居(谓以洞穴为住所);窟穴(动物栖身的洞穴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指人或物汇集处 <span class="encs">[den]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赌窟;贫民窟;窟穴(指隐士的住所);窟藏(隐匿之所);窟薮(寄托或藏身之所)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>窟</strong> <span class="dicpy">kū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>穴居;作巢 <span class="encs">[live in caves]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狐兔窟于殿傍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《西征赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窟守(穴居不出);窟岩(岩穴);窟穴(做巢;盘踞);窟宅(居住;盘踞)</span></p>
