data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从大，亦声。本义:大)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[great]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奕，大也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">往小来奕。——<span class="smcs">《太玄·格》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">唐</span>， <span class="diczx2">尚父</span>宾 <span class="diczx2">周</span>。受氏胙国，重光奕休。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故其华表则镐镐铄铄，赫奕章灼，若日明之丽天也。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">何晏</span><span
