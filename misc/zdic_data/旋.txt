                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>旋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xuán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuán.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>转动：～绕。～转。～舞。～梯。～律。盘～。天～地转。</li><li>回，归：凯～。</li><li>不久：～踵（喻极短的时间，如“～～即逝”）。～即。</li><li>表示与各方来往或来往于各方之间：周～。斡～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">漩</span>”，漩涡。</li><li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>旋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xuàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>打转的：～风。</li><li>临时（做）：～吃～做。</li><li>用车床或刀子转着圈地削：用车床～零件。把瓜皮～下去。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>旋</strong> <span class="dicpy">xuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[revolve;turn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旋，周旋，旌旗之指麾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旋入雷渊。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“旋，转也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旋式正綦。——<span class="smcs">《史记·日者列传》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“旋，转也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鲵旋之潘为渊。——<span class="smcs">《列子·黄帝》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“旋，谓盘旋也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">湍濑旋渊吕梁之深。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·俶真》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水深则回。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·致士》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“回，旋流也。水深不湍峻则多旋流也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">周旋中规，折旋中矩。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">周旋序顺。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“周旋，容止也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">列星随旋，日月递炤。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·天论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:旋辟(进退周旋，环绕);旋式(转动罗盘);旋弄(转动摆弄);旋盘(转碟)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 返回或归来 <span class="encs">[come back;return]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旋，还也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旋，回也。——<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桃今与楼齐，我行尚未旋。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/65CB.svg">                          <p>周旋，旌旗之指麾也。从㫃从疋。疋，足也。似沿切〖注〗徐鍇曰：“人足隨旌旗以周旋也。”</p>                          </div>
