                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>贾</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg"><span class="diczx3">賈</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>作买卖的人；商人。古时特指设店售货的坐商：行商坐～。</li>
	<li>卖:余勇可～（比喻还有多余的力量可以使出）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>贾</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg"><span class="diczx3">賈</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎ.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄚˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贾</strong></p>
<p>賈 <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:做买卖)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[do business]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贾，市也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父母已去，兄嫂令我行贾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;孤儿行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鄙谚曰:&ldquo;长袖善舞，多财善贾。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贾资(做买卖的资金);贾道(做买卖之道);贾贸(买卖;交易);贾贷(买卖)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 坐卖。泛指卖 <span class="encs">[sell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">极赏则民贾其上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;命训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以商贾阜货而行市。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;司市》</span>。注:&ldquo;商店卖物曰贾。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">高固</span>入 <span class="diczx2">晋</span>师，桀石以投人，禽之，而乘其车，击桑本焉，以徇 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>垒。曰:&ldquo;欲勇者，贾余余勇。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公二年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贾欺(兜售谎言);贾用(卖东西);贾勇(出卖勇力)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 买 <span class="encs">[buy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贾余馀勇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">平子</span>每岁贾马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十九年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贾马(买马);贾田(买田);贾官(买取官职)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 谋求;求取 <span class="encs">[strive for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谋于众不以贾好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贾誉(沽名钓誉);贾诚(博取忠诚的名声);贾国(谋取国柄);贾利(求取利益);贾息(经商求利)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 招引，招惹 <span class="encs">[court]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾焉用此以贾害也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公十年》</span></span></p>
