                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>乏</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄚˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>缺少：～味。贫～。不～其人。</li>
	<li>疲倦：～困。～累。疲～。</li>
	<li>无能，无用：～气。～煤。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> lack; poor</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> manquer de,faire défaut,fatigué</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乏</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代射礼唱靶者用以避箭的器具，其形略似屏风 <span class="encs">[shield]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乏，春秋传曰，反正为乏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，容也。从反正，推事。受矢者为正，避矢者为乏。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故文反正为乏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡乏用革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;大射仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乏参侯道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;容谓之乏，所以为获者御矢也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 无功伏安，电抗性伏安单位 <span class="encs">[var]</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乏</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为贬。物质财富短缺 <span class="encs">[be short of;lack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">振乏绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;季春》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孟尝君</span>使人给其食用，无使乏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">农不出则乏其食，工不出则乏其事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">空乏其身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乏资(缺乏费用);乏力(缺少气力);不乏其人</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 荒废;耽误 <span class="encs">[neglect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子往矣，无乏吾事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">自严</span>奏答无滞，不敢安寝，头目臃肿，事幸无乏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 没有，无 <span class="encs">[not have]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:乏趣(无味，没有兴味);乏手(手头不宽裕);乏角儿(没有名气的演员)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>乏</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不正 <span class="encs">[wry]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:乏样子(丑陋的相貌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 疲乏;无力 <span class="encs">[tired]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因其劳乏而乘之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新五代史&middot;周德威传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乏倦(疲倦);乏乏(很疲倦);人困马乏</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 使土地贫瘠的，或使丧失力量或功效的 <span class="encs">[exhausted]</span>。</p>
