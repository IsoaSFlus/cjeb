                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">麟</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>麟</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄣˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔麒～〕古代传说中的一种动物，像鹿，全身有鳞甲，有尾。古代以其象征祥瑞，亦用来喻杰出的人物。简称&ldquo;麟&rdquo;，如&ldquo;凤毛～角&rdquo;，&ldquo;～凤龟龙&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> female of Chinese unicorn</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> weibliches Einhorn</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> licorne</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">麟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>麟</strong></p>
<p>麐 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鹿，粦( <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;n</span>)声。本义:大鹿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[big deer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">麟，大牝鹿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">麐，牝麒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:&ldquo;经典皆以麟为之。&rdquo; <span class="diczx2">段</span>曰:&ldquo;经典无作麐者。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射麋脚麟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《子虚赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">解罘放麟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">薛</span>注:&ldquo;大鹿曰麟。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惧失麟鹿而获艾貑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《潜夫论&middot;贤难》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 麒麟 <span class="encs">[kylin;[Chinese]</span> unicorn]</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">单呼麟者，大牡鹿也;呼麟麟者，仁兽也。麒麟可单呼麟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西狩获麟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋&middot;哀公十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凤毛麟角;麟阁(又称麟台。麒麟阁，汉宣帝时曾在麒麟阁内绘功臣图像，以表其功);麟趾(麟足);麟符(刻画麟状的信物);麟游(麒麟遨游)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 喻皇族、显贵 <span class="encs">[noble]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:麟寺(掌管皇族事物的衙署);麟牒(王族的族谱);麟子凤雏(麒麟之子，凤凰之雏。古人用来比喻贵族的子孙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 喻稀有之物 <span class="encs">[rare and precious things]</span>。</p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集下" target="_blank">亥集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集下&amp;bs=鹿" target="_blank">鹿</a>】
                              麟·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=23" target="_blank">23</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9E9F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>力珍切，音鄰。【說文】大麚也。麕身牛尾，狼額馬蹄，五彩腹下黃，高丈二。【玉篇】仁獸也。【詩&middot;周南】麟之趾。【傳】麟信而應禮，以足至者也。【箋】麟角末有肉，示有武不用。【大戴禮】毛蟲三百六十，麟爲之長。【禮&middot;禮運】麟以爲畜，故獸不狘。【註】狘，驚走也。</p><p>又州名。漢五原，河西二地，唐置麟州。</p><p>又【十洲記】鳳麟州有集弦膠。</p><p>又與燐通。光明也。【揚雄&middot;劇秦美新文】炳炳麟麟。</p><p>又叶陵延切，音連。【韓愈&middot;雜詩】指摘相告語，雖還今誰親。翩然下大荒，被髮騎麒麟。親叶音千。此詩終篇皆先韻。○按經傳皆作麟字。《爾雅》《公羊》《京房易傳》皆作麐。《說文》麐，牝麒也。麟，大牝鹿也。據此，則麐與麟有分。《爾雅註疏》幷州界有麟，大如鹿，非瑞麟也。故司馬相如賦曰：射糜脚麟，謂此麟也。《爾雅》麠大麃牛尾一角，註云：謂之麟者，此是也。然麟麐二字，今俱通用。互詳麐字註。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
