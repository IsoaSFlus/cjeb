                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赌</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">賭</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 用财物作注来争输赢：～博。～注。～场。～徒。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bet, gamble, wager; compete</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Glücksspiel, um Geld spielen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> parier,jeu d'argent,pari</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赌</strong></p><p>賭 <span class="dicpy">dǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，者声。本义:用财物作注比输赢)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gamble]</span>。如:赌东(以做东道赌输赢，输者做东道置酒请客);赌对(赌博，赌赛);赌色(掷色子赌博);赌郡(用郡官作为赌注);聚赌(聚在一起赌博);赌术(赌博的本领);赌钱(以金钱为输赢的赌博);赌运(赌博的运气);赌贩(赌棍);赌经(专记赌术的书籍)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指比胜负;争输赢 <span class="encs">[bet]</span>。如:赌赛马;赌赛(比赛);赌身立誓(用身体、性命起誓);赌狠(发狠;逞凶);赌战(亦作睹战。决战);赌口齿(比口才);赌斗(比试;争斗);赌鳖气(负气;赌气);赌胆(比赛胆量的大小);赌戏(比赛争胜);赌兴(尽兴争胜);赌说(斗嘴，争辩);赌棋(比赛棋艺);赌句(赛诗);赌神发誓(在神灵面前发誓)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌本">赌本</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌博">赌博</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌局">赌局</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌具">赌具</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌风">赌风</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌鬼">赌鬼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌棍">赌棍</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌窟">赌窟</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌气">赌气</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌徒">赌徒</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌窝">赌窝</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌咒">赌咒</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌注">赌注</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌桌">赌桌</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赌资">赌资</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
