                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>为</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">爲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄟˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>做，行，做事：～人。～时。～难。不～己甚（不做得太过分）。</li>
	<li>当做，认做：以～。认～。习以～常。</li>
	<li>变成：成～。</li>
	<li>是：十两～一斤。</li>
	<li>治理，处理：～政。</li>
	<li>被：～天下笑。</li>
	<li>表示强调：大～恼火。</li>
	<li>助词，表示反诘或感叹：敌未灭，何以家～？</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>为</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">爲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>替，给：～民请命。～虎作伥。～国捐躯。</li>
	<li>表目的：～了。～何。</li>
	<li>对，向：不足～外人道。</li>
	<li>帮助，卫护。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">为</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>为</strong></p>
<p>為、爲 <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意字。从爪象。甲金文像手牵象，会劳作意，本义是做事、作为。)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假借为&ldquo;伪&rdquo;。做，作，干，搞 <span class="encs">[do;act;make]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我生之初，尚无为。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;兔爰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子为不知，我将不墜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;定公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为善者，非善也，故善无以为也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;枢言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变化则为生，为生则乱矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;心术上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为，施也。又，成也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有客自云能，帝使为之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;巧艺》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为之难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:&ldquo;犹行也。&rdquo;</p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“为”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/爲">爲</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集中" target="_blank">巳集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集中&amp;bs=爪" target="_blank">爪</a>】
                              爲·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7232.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2696E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦥮</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/244F8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤓸</span>【唐韻】薳支切【集韻】于嬀切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音潙。【說文】母猴也。其爲禽好爪。爪母猴象也。下腹爲母猴形。王育曰：爪象形也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】作，造，爲也。【書&middot;益稷】予欲宣力四方汝爲。【洪範】有猷有爲有守。</p>
<p>又治也。【晉語】疾不可爲也。【註】爲，治也。</p>
<p>又使也。【魯語】其爲後世昭前之令聞也。【註】爲，猶使也。</p>
<p>又語詞。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】何但亡匿幕北寒苦之地爲。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】風俗通云：漢有南郡太守爲昆。【韻會】魯昭公子公爲之後。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>于僞切，音䧦。【廣韻】助也。【增韻】所以也，緣也，被也，護也，與也。【書&middot;咸有一德】臣爲上爲德，爲下爲民。【釋文】爲上爲下之爲，于僞反。徐云：四爲字皆于僞反。</p>
<p>又【多士】惟我下民秉爲。【詩&middot;大雅】福祿來爲。【箋】爲，猶助也。【釋文】于僞反，協句如字。</p>
<p>又叶吾何切，音莪。【詩&middot;王風】有兔爰爰，雉離于羅。我生之初，尙無爲。我生之後，逢此百罹，尙寐無吪。</p>
