                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>谥</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">謚</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古代帝王或大官死后评给的称号：～号。～法。～宝（帝王陵墓中，刻有帝后谥号的印玺）。</li>	<li>叫作，称为：&ldquo;身死无名，～为至愚&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to confer posthumous titles</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> nach dem Tode verliehener Ehrenname (English: posthumous title)​  (S)​,Nachruf  (S)​,Ehrennamen verleihen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Nom posthume</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>谥</strong></p>
<p>謚、諡 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，益声。用言论给予褒贬，故从言。本作&ldquo;諡&rdquo;。本义:古代皇帝、贵族、大臣、杰出官员或其它有地位的人死后所加的带有褒贬意义的称号) 同本义 <span class="encs">[posthumous title (of emperor， distinguished minister)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谥，行之迹也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北堂书钞》</span>引<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谥者，别尊卑，彰有德也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先王谥以尊名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诔生时所行为之谥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;道应》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">增谥美显，荣于身后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:谥法(由主祭者将名号赠给死者，以表彰他一生的功业);谥宝(古代帝王寝墓中，刻有帝后谥号的玺印);谥谱(记载谥号的谱牒);谥名(谥号)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>谥</strong></p>
<p>謚 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 授与，加封;尤指死后追封 <span class="encs">[confer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸德谥为洞箫兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《洞箫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身死无名，谥为至愚，耻及父母，为天下笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:谥告(赠谥的文告);谥典(赠谥的典礼)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">謚</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“谥”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/謚">謚</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三">卷三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三&amp;swbs=言">言</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8B1A.svg">                          <p>笑皃。从言益聲。伊昔切。又，呼狄切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8B1A_6349.svg" /><span class="swbf">(謚)</span><span class="ywyy">行之迹也。</span><span class="swdz">周書謚法解、檀弓樂記表記注皆云：謚者，行之迹也。謚、迹曡韵。</span><span class="ywyy">从言益聲。</span><span class="swdz">按各本作從言兮皿闕，此後人妄改也。攷玄應書引說文：謚，行之迹也。從言益聲。五經文字曰：謚，說文也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27A26.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧨦</span>，字林也。字林以謚爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>聲，音呼益反。廣韵曰：諡，說文作謚。六書故曰：唐本說文無諡，但有謚，行之迹也。據此四者，說文從言益無疑矣。自呂忱改爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27A26.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧨦</span>，唐宋之閒又或改爲諡，遂有改說文而依字林，羼入謚笑皃於部末者。然唐開成石經、宋一代書版皆作謚不作諡，知徐鉉之書不能易天下是非之公也。近宗說文者，不能攷知說文之舊，如汲古閣刊經典依宋作謚矣，而覆改爲諡，可歎也。今正諡爲謚，而刪部末之謚<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>皃，學者可以撥雲霧而覩青天矣。神至切。古音在十六部。</span></p>                          </div>
