                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">樽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>樽</strong></p>
<p>罇 <span class="dicpy">zūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，尊声。本作&ldquo;尊&rdquo;。本义:盛酒器。中国古代的盛酒器具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wine goblet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有酒盈樽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金樽清酒斗十千。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《行路难》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">座上客常满，樽中酒不空。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《全图绣像三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:樽杓(指饮酒之器);樽桂(杯中的桂花酒);樽酌(指饮酒之器);樽酒(杯酒)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>樽</strong> <span class="dicpy">zūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 往酒器里倒酒 <span class="encs">[pour wine into drinking vessel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少倾，摆出酒席，四位樽酒论文。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;撙&rdquo;。抑止 <span class="encs">[save]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">樽流遁之观。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;要略》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杨树达</span>云:&ldquo;樽，经传通作撙。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:樽约(谓节约，樽，同&ldquo;撙&rdquo;);樽节(节省。樽，通&ldquo;撙&rdquo;)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">樽</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              樽·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6A3D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>租昆切，音尊。【說文】作尊。【玉篇】酒器也。【正韻】从木者後人所加。亦作罇。【易&middot;大過】樽酒簋貳用缶。</p><p>又【儀禮&middot;燕禮註】禮法有以壺爲樽者。【左傳&middot;昭十五年】樽以魯壷。</p><p>又止也。【淮南子&middot;要略訓】樽流遁之觀。</p><p>又【韻會】禮運，以尊爲尊<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>之尊，別出樽字。然樽乃林木茂盛之字。</p><p>又叶蹤倫切，音遵。【馬融&middot;東巡頌】圭璋峨峨，犧牲潔純。鬱鬯宗彞，明水元樽。</p><p>又叶且緣切，音詮。【蘇軾&middot;祭單君貺文】何以慰君，中里一尊。人生如夢，何促何延。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">𢍜</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“樽”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/𢍜"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span></a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四">卷十四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四&amp;swbs=酋">酋</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2235C.svg">                          <p>酒器也。从酋，廾以奉之。《周禮》六尊：犧尊、象尊、著尊、壺尊、太尊、山尊，以待祭祀賓客之禮。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2235C_1.svg" />尊，尊或从寸。祖昆切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗以尊作尊卑之尊。别作罇。非是。文二　重一</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2235C_9400.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">酒器也。</span><span class="swdz">凡酒必實於尊以待酌者。鄭注禮曰：置酒曰尊。凡酌酒者必資於尊，故引申以爲尊卑字，猶貴賤本謂貨物而引申之也。自專用爲尊卑字，而別製罇樽爲酒尊字矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从酋，卄㠯奉之。</span><span class="swdz">卄者，竦手也。奉者，承也。設尊者必竦手以承之。祖昆切。十三部。</span><span class="ywyy">周禮六<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>：犧<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、象<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、箸<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、壷<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、大<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、山<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>。㠯待祭祀賓客之禮。</span><span class="swdz">見周禮司尊彝職。犧作獻。鄭司農云：獻讀爲犧。犧尊，飾以翡翠。象尊，以象鳳皇，或曰以象骨飾尊。箸尊，箸略尊也，或曰箸尊箸地無足。壷者，以壷爲尊。春秋傳曰：尊以魯壷。大尊，大古之瓦尊。山尊，山罍也。按毛詩閟之犧尊，卽獻尊也。故許同大鄭作犧，以待祭祀，司尊彝詳之矣。大行人賓客之祼亦必用彝，饗禮食禮亦必用尊，故約之曰以待祭祀賓客之禮。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2235C_9401.svg" /><span class="swbf">(尊)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>或从寸。</span><span class="swdz">此與寺从寸意同。有法度者也。</span></p>                          </div>
