						<p>● <strong>或</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄛˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>也许，有时，表示不定的词：～许。～者（①也许；②连词，用在叙述句里，表示选择关系。均亦单用&ldquo;或&rdquo;）。～然。～则。</li>
	<li>某人，有的人：～告之曰。</li>
	<li>稍微：不可～缓。不可～忽。不可～缺。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">或</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>或</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">代</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形从囗(象城形)，从戈(以戈守之)。表示以戈卫国。本义:国家。用本义时读 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span>)有人，有的&mdash;&mdash;泛指人或事物 <span class="encs">[some;someone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或，有也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或跃在渊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;乾》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殷其弗或乱正四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;微子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或群或友。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;吉日》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庶或饷之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或赐二小人酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或说处杀虎斩蛟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;自新》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或立或卧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><span class="diczx3">又如:或人(某人。不称名而暗指的人。有些人);或时(有时);或一，或种(某种);或日(某日，有一天)</span>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>或</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">连</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用作虚词 <span class="encs">[or]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[1]</span>∶表示选择或列举。 </span><span class="diczx3">如:或是(连词。表示选择或并列关系);或乃(或者。常用于句首表示变换叙述内容) </span><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[2]</span>∶在不同或不相似的事物、状况或行动之间的一个抉择。 </span><span class="diczx3">如:有病或无病，他不都应该在这里 </span><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[3]</span>∶近似、可疑或不肯定。 </span><span class="diczx3">如:在五天或六天之内 </span><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[4]</span>∶两个词或两个短语同义、等义或替换的性质</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或尽粟一石。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君实或见恕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或异二者之为。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或能免乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:跌下悬崖或峭壁</span></p>

			<div class="gscdl"><span>或</span>王命急宣，有时朝发白帝，暮到江陵，其间千二百里，虽乘奔御风，不以疾也。</div><div class="gscdr">南北朝·郦道元 《三峡》</div><div class="gscdl">马之千里者，一食<span>或</span>尽粟一石。</div><div class="gscdr">唐·韩愈 《马说》</div><div class="gscdl">而<span>或</span>长烟一空，皓月千里，浮光跃金，静影沉璧，渔歌互答，此乐何极！登斯楼也，则有心旷神怡，宠辱偕忘，把酒临风，其喜洋洋者矣。</div><div class="gscdr">宋·范仲淹 《岳阳楼记》</div><div class="gscdl">嗟夫！予尝求古仁人之心，<span>或</span>异二者之为，何哉？不以物喜，不以己悲；居庙堂之高则忧其民；处江湖之远则忧其君。是进亦忧，退亦忧。</div><div class="gscdr">宋·范仲淹 《岳阳楼记》</div><div class="gscdl">余立侍左右，援疑质理，俯身倾耳以请；<span>或</span>遇其叱咄，色愈恭，礼愈至，不敢出一言以复；俟其欣悦，则又请焉。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
