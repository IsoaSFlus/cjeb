						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>览</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">覽</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄢˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 看，阅：阅～。游～。展～。～胜（观赏美景）。一～无余（一下子就看得清清楚楚了，形容事物或诗文平淡无奇）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">览</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>览</strong></p><p>覽 <span class="dicpy">lǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从见，从监。<span class="yhcs">“监”</span>亦兼表字音。<span class="yhcs">“监”</span>的本义是借水照形，这里表示看。本义:观看)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[look at;see]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">览，观也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而数览。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皇览揆余于初度兮。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又览累累之昌辞。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洞庭君览毕。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故有所鉴辄省记。——</span><span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:览观(阅览);览读(阅读);览阅(阅览);览核(查阅);览究(阅读研究)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 观赏 <span class="encs">[enjoy the sight of;
