						<p>● <strong>善</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>心地仁爱，品质淳厚：～良。～心。～举。～事。～人。～男信女。慈～。</li>
	<li>好的行为、品质：行～。惩恶扬～。</li>
	<li>高明的，良好的：～策。～本。</li>
	<li>友好，和好：友～。亲～。和～。</li>
	<li>熟悉：面～。</li>
	<li>办好，弄好：～后。</li>
	<li>擅长，长（<span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng </span>）于：～辞令。多谋～断。</li>
	<li>赞许：&ldquo;群臣百姓之所～，则君～之&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>好好地：～待。～罢甘休。</li>
	<li>容易，易于：～变。～忘。多愁～感。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>善</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意，从言，从羊。言是讲话。羊是吉祥的象征。本义:吉祥)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[lucky]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善，吉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">来者以善日邪时，孰与邪日善时?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;翼奉传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:善时(吉时);善祥(吉祥;吉兆);善征(吉兆);善日(吉日)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 好;美好 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">母氏圣善。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;凯风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善，德之建也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善人国之主也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所以善代者乃万故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;长攻》</span>。注:&ldquo;善好也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">择其善者而从之，其不善者而改之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;述而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂人主之子孙则敢不善哉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:善风(良好的风气);善言(美言;说好话);善处(好办法);善马(良马);善少(好少年);善秀(佳穗。比喻资质甚佳，才品出众)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5584.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕譱<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2638D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦎍</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20F84.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠾄</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27B9F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧮟</span>【廣韻】常演切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】上演切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蟺。【說文】吉也。【玉篇】大也。【廣韻】良也，佳也。【書&middot;湯誥】天道福善禍淫。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;鄘風】女子善懷。【箋】善，猶多也。【禮&middot;文王世子】嘗饌善，則世子亦能食。【註】善謂多于前。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮&middot;入國不馳註】馳善躙人也。【疏】善猶好也，車馳則好行刺人也。</p>

<p>又【禮&middot;少儀】問道藝，曰：子習于某乎，子善于某乎。【疏】道難，故稱習。藝易，故稱善。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;西域傳】鄯善國，本名樓蘭王。</p>
<p>又與單通。【前漢&middot;匈奴傳】單于曰善于。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】姓也。【呂氏春秋】善卷，堯師。</p>
<p>又【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>時戰切，音繕。【毛氏曰】凡善惡之善則上聲，彼善而善之則去聲。【孟子】王如善之是也。○按《玉篇》《廣韻》《集韻》《類篇》善字俱無去聲。</p>
<p>又【正字通】與人交讙曰友善。【史記&middot;刺客傳】田光曰：所善荊卿可使也。</p>
<p>又與膳通。【莊子&middot;至樂篇】具太牢以爲善。　【集韻】或作嬗。</p>
