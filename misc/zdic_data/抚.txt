						<p>● <strong>抚</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">撫</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>慰问：～恤（安慰和周济）。～慰。安～。～爱。</li>
	<li>扶持，保护：～养成人。</li>
	<li>轻轻地按着：～摩。～摸。～琴（弹琴）。～今追昔。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/拊">拊</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pat, console, comfort; pacify</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> caresser,élever,nourrir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">抚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>抚</strong></p>
<p>撫 <span class="dicpy">fǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，无声。本义:抚摩)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stroke]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">撫，安也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抚而泣之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语八》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左右抚矢而乘之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妇抚儿乳，儿含乳啼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">东坡</span>右手执卷端，左手抚 <span class="diczx2">鲁直</span>背。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抚弄(抚摩);抚心(用手抚摸胸口。表示安抚);抚拭(抚摩拂拭);抚背(抚摩脊背。表示安慰、关切等)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用手按着 <span class="encs">[press]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抚弦登陴，岂不怆悢&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抚剑(按剑);抚臆(以手按胸);抚手(拍手)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;拊&rdquo;。拍或轻击 <span class="encs">[clap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抚，敷也，敷手以拍之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释姿容》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">权</span>抚掌欢笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;鲁肃传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抚掌(拍手);抚胸(以手捶胸。表示悲痛);抚案(用手指按着节拍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 安抚;安慰 <span class="encs">[console]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西和诸戎，南抚夷越。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及设</span><span class="diczx2">备</span>使抚 <span class="diczx2">表</span>众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抚驭(安抚控制);抚理(安抚治理);抚辑(安抚慰问);抚柔(安抚怀柔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 弹拨<span class="encs">[乐器]</span> <span class="encs">[pluck]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:抚桐(弹琴);抚弄(拨弄;弹奏);抚弦(拨弄琴弦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 照料使健全生长;养育 <span class="encs">[foster]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">依新已五岁，转眼成人，汝其善抚之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抚字(抚养;抚肩);抚孤(抚养、救济孤儿);抚畜(抚养);抚稚(抚养儿女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 占有 <span class="encs">[occupy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西方有九国焉，君王其终抚诸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通&ldquo;幠&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">hū</span>)。掩;盖 <span class="encs">[top;surpass cover;hide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">神与化游，以扶四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Fu prefecture]</span>。汉豫章郡地，陈置抚州。在今江西省临川县治</p>
