                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>怵</strong> <span class="dicpy">chù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，术声。本义:恐惧，害怕)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怵，恐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怵，惧也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">劳形怵心者也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·应帝王》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怵悼栗而耸兢。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆有怵惕恻隐之心。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:怵栗(恐惧);怵悸(恐惧心跳);怵怵(戒惧、警惕的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 伤心 <span class="encs">[feel deeply about over;lament over]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心怵而奉之以礼。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·祭统》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是以君子不怵乎好，不迫乎恶。——<span class="smcs">《管子·心术上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人迫于恶，则失其所好;怵于好，则忘其所恶，非道也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得于好恶，怵于淫物，而后变乱。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·解老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怵迫之徒，或趋西东。——<span class="smcs">《文选·贾谊·鹏鸟赋》</span>。</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/怵惧">怵惧</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/怵目惊心">怵目惊心</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/怵然">怵然</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/怵惕">怵惕</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
