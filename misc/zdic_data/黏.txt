                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>黏</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ni&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>像胶或糨糊的性质：～性。～液。～土。～米。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> stick to; glutinous, sticky; glue</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> klebrig, pappig"</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> collant,glutineux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">黏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>黏</strong> <span class="dicpy">ni&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 粘连;胶合 <span class="encs">[adhere;glue;paste;stick]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黏，相着也。从黍，占声。字亦作粘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黏，合也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则无黏滞之音矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:黏贴(黏附张贴);黏带(黏连牵挂);黏补(修补);黏接(黏合连接)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 贴近;接贴 <span class="encs">[press close to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黏天(贴近天，仿佛与天相连);黏空(耸入高空，仿佛与天相连)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>黏</strong> <span class="dicpy">ni&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>具有黏性的。同&ldquo;粘&rdquo; <span class="encs">[glutinous;sticky]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黏米;黏土;黏湿(黏糊潮湿)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">膠附、黏住。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「黏貼」、「<b>黏</b>照片」、「<b>黏</b>郵票」。</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;王褒〈僮約〉：「<b>黏</b>雀張烏，結網捕魚。」</span></p></li>

	<li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9ECF.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】女廉切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】尼占切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䬯。【說文】相著也。【廣韻】黏麴。</p>
<p>又【韻會】亦作溓。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;輪人】雖有深泥，亦弗之溓也。【註】鄭司農云：溓讀爲黏。</p>
<p>又地名。【前漢&middot;地理志】樂浪郡黏蟬縣。</p>
<p>又【正韻】尼欠切，聶上聲。【集韻】䵒也。【正韻】膠黏。</p>
<p>又稠也。　俗作粘。</p>
