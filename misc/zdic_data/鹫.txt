                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鹫</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>鹫</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鷲</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄡˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 一种猛禽，毛色深褐，体大雄壮，嘴呈钩状，视力很强，腿部有羽毛，捕食野兔，小羊等。亦称“<span class="diczx3">雕</span>”。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> condor, vulture</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kondor  (S, Bio)​,inhuman, schrecklich  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vautour</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鹫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>鹫</strong></p><p>鷲 <span class="dicpy">jiù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 老雕 <span class="encs">[vulture]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鹫鸟，黑色，多子。</span><span class="diczx2">师旷</span>曰，南方有鸟，名曰羌鹫，黄头赤目，五色皆备。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鹫，雕也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生奇材木箭竿就(鹫)羽。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·匈奴传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“大雕也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 产于温带和热带地区的若干种大型猛禽，与鹰类、雕类和隼类都有亲缘关系，但爪较弱，头多秃，主要或全部以腐肉为食，构成鹫科(Aegypiidae)和新域鹫科(Cathartidae)，其中包括一些最大的飞禽。如:鹫羽(老雕的羽毛。又叫<span class="yhcs">“鹫翎”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 灵鹫山的简称 <span class="encs">[Linjiushan mountain]</span>。亦借指佛地。如:鹫峰(即灵鹫山，在中印度。或称鹫岭、鹫山。为佛说法之地);鹫室(鹫山石室。亦指禅房)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中" target="_blank">亥集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中&amp;bs=鳥" target="_blank">鳥</a>】
                              鷲·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=23" target="_blank">23</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9DF2.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>疾僦切，音就。【廣韻】鳥名。黑色，多子。師曠曰：南方有鳥，名羌鷲，黃頭，赤目，五色皆備。【本草】鷲悍多力，盤旋空中，無細不覩，卽白鵰也。一作就。</p><p>又靈鷲，山名。【西域記】耆闍崛山，有兩峰雙立，鷲鳥常居其嶺，山遠望如鷲形，故名靈鷲山。【沈約·石象銘】永言鷲室，棲誡梵宮。</p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四">卷四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四&amp;swbs=鳥">鳥</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2A1A9.svg">                          <p>鳥黑色多子。師曠曰：“南方有鳥，名曰羌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1A9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪆩</span>，黃頭赤目，五色皆備。”从鳥就聲。疾僦切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2A1A9_10389.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1A9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪆩</span>)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1A9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪆩</span>鳥。</span><span class="swdz"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1A9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪆩</span>字今補。</span><span class="ywyy">黑色。多子。</span><span class="swdz">山海經。其獸多鹿<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2A8.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪊨</span>就。郭引廣雅。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1A9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪆩</span>、雕也。李將軍傳。是必射雕者。服虔曰。雕一名鷲。黑色。多子。可以其毛作矢羽。按廣雅？鶚䳻鷲雕也。統言之。許雕？爲一。？爲一。鶚爲一。析言之。</span><span class="ywyy">師曠曰。南方有鳥。名曰羌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1A9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪆩</span>。黃頭赤目。五色皆備。</span><span class="swdz">此別一鳥。名羌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1A9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪆩</span>。非<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1A9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪆩</span>也。藝文志小說家有師曠六篇。豈許所偁與。今世有禽經係之師曠。其文理淺陋。葢因說文此條而僞造。吳都賦。彈鸞鶁。劉注引師曠曰云云。葢本說文。不知字何以作鶁。李音京。廣韵十二庚有鶁字。注。羌鶁也。玄應書引說文。赤目作赤咽。</span><span class="ywyy">从鳥。就聲。</span><span class="swdz">疾僦切。三部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
