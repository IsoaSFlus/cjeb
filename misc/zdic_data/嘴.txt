                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>嘴</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zuǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄟˇ</span></p><ol><li>口，动物吃食，发音的器官，亦指说话：～巴。～头。～快。～严。～直。～软。～笨。～馋。张～。～甜心苦。</li><li>形状或作用像嘴的东西：山～。壶～儿。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">嘴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>嘴</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (嘴，本作觜。嘴是后起的累增字。形声。从口，觜( <span class="dicpy">zuǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǐ.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:同<span class="yhcs">“觜”</span>。猫头鹰之类头上的毛角)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[horn-like hair on the owl-head]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">觜，鸱旧头上角觜。或作嘴。——<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 鸟嘴 <span class="encs">[beak]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">(</span><span class="diczx2">二郎</span>)又变了一只朱绣顶的灰鹤，伸着一个长嘴，与一把尖头铁钳子相似，径来吃这水蛇。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 中古时期，嘴引申指人或动物的口腔器官 <span class="encs">[mouth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有一鱼，可长丈余，粗细大于臂…嘴尖，状如鲟鱼。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王度</span><span class="smcs">《古镜记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">他嘴头子又来得，左话右转，右话左转，翻蛆搭舌头的侪是他说话分。——<span class="smcs">《何典》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:嘴片子(嘴皮子。嘴唇);嘴瓜(嘴部附近的部位);嘴皮(嘴唇);嘴壳子(指鸟喙);嘴犄角(嘴角);嘴筒(嘴巴);嘴卢都(撅着嘴，鼓着嘴)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 从鸟嘴外形突出引申出表示物体的突出部分 <span class="encs">[sth.shaped or functioning like a mouth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两盘一山嘴，转出东南。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·粤西游日记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:山嘴;瓶嘴;茶壶嘴</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 脸 <span class="encs">[face]</span>。如:嘴鼻(面貌);嘴吃屎(脸向下跌倒);嘴啃地(指脸向下跌倒);嘴抢地(嘴啃地)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>嘴</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 说话 <span class="encs">[speak]</span></p><p><span
