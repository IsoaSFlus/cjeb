                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">身</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>身</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄣˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>人、动物的躯体，物体的主要部分：～躯。人～。～材。～段。船～。树～。</li>
	<li>指人的生命或一生：～世。献～。</li>
	<li>亲自，本人：自～。亲～。～教。～体力行。</li>
	<li>统指人的地位、品德：出～。～分（<span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n </span>）。～败名裂。</li>
	<li>孕，娠：～孕。</li>
	<li>量词，指整套衣服：做了一～儿新衣服。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">身</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>身</strong> <span class="dicpy">shēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。象人之形。本义:身躯的总称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身，躬也。象人之形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，身的本义是人的躯干。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必有寝礼，长一身有半。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;乡党》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人自项以下，踵以上，总谓之身。颈以下，股以上，亦谓之身。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王述之</span><span class="smcs">《经义述闻》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人百其身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;黄鸟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身也者，亲之枝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;哀公问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身也者，父母之遗体也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饿其体肤，空乏其身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身首离兮心不惩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九歌&middot;国殇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粉骨碎身浑不怕，要留青白在人间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">于谦</span><span class="smcs">《石灰吟》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">项伯</span>亦拔剑起舞，常以身翼蔽 <span class="diczx2">沛公</span>， <span class="diczx2">庄</span>不得击。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:身边钱(随身带的钱);身品(身材);身伴(身旁);身边人(贴知伺候的奴婢，妾);身首分离(人的头和躯体分开。指被杀);身殃(谓身遭灾殃);身牖(身体的窗子，指耳朵);身无长物(比喻人的穷困)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 物件的主体部分 <span class="encs">[main body of a structure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枞，松叶柏身;桧，柏叶松身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释木》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亥有二首六身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:树身;河身</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 自己，自身 <span class="encs">[oneself;self]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">免不可复得，而身为</span><span class="diczx2">宋国</span>笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">远者数世，近者及身。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾日三省吾身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;学而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是不亦责于身者重以周乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《原毁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:身己(本身，指属于自己的事);身外(自己以外，自身之外);身愆(己身的过失)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 生命，性命 <span class="encs">[life]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国事至此，予不得爱身。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:献身;奋不顾身;舍身救人</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 地位或身分 <span class="encs">[status]</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">軀體的總稱，或專指軀幹。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「搜身」、「翻身」、「隨身攜帶」、「身長七尺」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;告子下》：「餓其體膚，空乏其身。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辭&middot;屈原&middot;九歌&middot;國殤》：「首身離兮心不懲。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">物體的中心或主要部分。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「車身」、「船身」、「機身」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">生命。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「奮不顧身」、「捨身救人」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《石點頭&middot;卷八&middot;貪婪漢六院賣風流》：「五臟六腑，顯出在外，方才氣斷身絕。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">自己。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「以身作則」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;學而》：「吾日三省吾身。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《戰國策&middot;趙策四》：「此其近者禍及身，遠者及其子孫。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">品格、修養。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「修身齊家」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《晏子春秋&middot;內篇&middot;問上》：「稱身就位，計能受祿 。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">身分、地位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「出身寒微」、「身敗名裂」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;微子》：「不降其志，不辱其身。」</span></p></li>
