                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">滩</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>滩</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">灘</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tān.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>河海边淤积成的平地或水中的沙洲：～头。～涂。河～。海～。盐～。</li>
	<li>江河中水浅多石而水流很急的地方：险～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bank, a sandbar, shoal; rapids</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Küste  (S)​,Strand  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> banc de sable,grève,rapide</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">滩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>滩</strong></p>
<p>灘 <span class="dicpy">tān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，难声。本义:河道中水浅流急多沙石的地方)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[beach]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却放轻舟下急滩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《唐崔道融溪夜诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滩，水滩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵&middot;平声寒韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">按，&ldquo;滩濑&rdquo;的&ldquo;滩&rdquo;是后起字，是&ldquo;湍&rdquo;的音转。又如:滩燀(水中沙滩);滩碛(浅水下的沙石滩);滩声，滩响(水激滩石发出的声音)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 滩头。指江、河、湖、海边水涨淹没、水退显露的淤积平地 <span class="encs">[beachhead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云低岸花掩，水涨滩草没。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《江上阻风雨》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惊起一滩鸥鹭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《如梦令》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 等级 <span class="encs">[grade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这种奴隶叫作&ldquo;三滩娃子&rdquo;。滩是等级的意思。管家娃子为头滩，普通娃子为二滩，娃子的娃子为三滩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">郭沫若</span><span class="smcs">《十批判书》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>滩</strong></p>
<p>灘 <span class="dicpy">tān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用于在平面上成灘的東西 <span class="encs">[spot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼻子流出一大灘血。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">楊朔</span><span class="smcs">《亂人坑》</span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“滩”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/灘">灘</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上" target="_blank">巳集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上&amp;bs=水" target="_blank">水</a>】
                              灘·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=23" target="_blank">23</a>　·部外筆画：19</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7058.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕潬【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】他干切【正韻】他丹切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>育攤。水灘也。【增韻】瀨也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】他含切，音貪。【爾雅&middot;釋天】太歲在申曰涒灘。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>他案切，音炭。義同。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】呼旱切【集韻】許旱切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䍐。水濡而乾也。與暵同。【說文】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24169.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤅩</span>。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】奴案切【集韻】乃案切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>難去聲。水奔流貌。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶他涓切，音湍。【白居易詩】手拄靑竹杖，足踏白石灘。漸怪耳目曠，不聞人世喧。</p>
