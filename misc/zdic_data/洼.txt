data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，圭( <span class="dicpy">guī</span>)声。本义:深池)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[deep pond]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窪，深池也。从水，窐声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">马生渥洼水中。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·武帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">似洼者。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泽无洼水。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·览冥》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:洼子(低洼存水的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 低凹的地方 <span class="encs">[depression]</span>。如:洼曲(谦称自己家房舍简陋);水洼儿;洼隆(凹处与凸处)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古水名 <span class="encs">[Wa River]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> <span class="yhcs">“渥洼”</span>的省称。是今甘肃省敦煌县党河的水源之一</p><p><span class="diczx1">丹山彩凤，</span><span class="diczx2">洼</span>水神驹。—— <span <strong>洼</strong></p><p>窪 <span class="dicpy">wā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“窐”</span>。低陷，凹下 <span class="encs">[hollow;low-lying]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窪，下也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窪则盈。——<span class="smcs">《老子·二十二章》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:洼塌(凹隐;凹下);洼坳(低下);洼田(低田);洼坎(凹陷);洼然(凹陷的样子);洼尊(形状凹入如酒尊的岩石)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 深 <span class="encs">[deep]</span>。如:洼池(深池。借指海湾);洼跨脸(额头突出鼻梁上部深凹的脸)</p><p>◎ <strong>洼</strong></p><p>窪
