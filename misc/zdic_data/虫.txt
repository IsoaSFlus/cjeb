                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">虫</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>虫</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">蟲</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>节肢动物的一类：昆～。益～。雕～小技（喻微不足道的技能）。</li>	<li>动物的通称：大～（老虎）。长～（蛇）。介～（有介壳的虫子）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> insects, worms; KangXi radical 142</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Insekt, Wurm  (S, Bio)​,Wurm  (S, Bio)​,Chong  (Eig, Fam)​,Radikal Nr. 142 = Wurm, Insekt  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> insecte,ver</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">虫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>虫</strong></p>
<p>蟲 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。汉字部首之一，从&ldquo;虫&rdquo;的字多与昆虫、蛇等有关。按:&ldquo;虫&rdquo;甲骨文字形象蛇形，本读 <span class="dicpy">huǐ，</span>即虺，是一种毒蛇。后为&ldquo;蟲&rdquo;的简体。本义:昆虫的通称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[insect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虫，有足谓之虫，无足谓之豸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风为虫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语&middot;执辔》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烦气为虫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;精神》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禽兽虫蛾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;黄帝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虫飞薨薨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;桑柔》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古人之观于天地、山川、草木、虫鱼、鸟兽，往往有得。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人民不胜虫蛇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:虫豸(昆虫;虫子);虫蛭(小虫子);虫天(百虫能各自适应所处的环境);虫吊(虫声。其声凄切，有如吊问);虫出(人死后数日则尸体生虫。喻死后不得入土安葬)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肉腐出虫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代泛指所有的动物 <span class="encs">[animal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蛟虫死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;览冥》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:长虫(大虫。方言。指老虎);虫虾(指鱼虾);虫虱(泛指蛀虫);虫网(谓蜘蛛布网)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 虫灾 <span class="encs">[plague of insects]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:虫霜水旱(指农田的四大害);虫妖(虫类造成的灾害。多指蝗灾)</span></p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/866B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】許偉切【集韻】詡鬼切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音卉。【廣韻】鱗介總名。【說文】一名蝮，博三寸，首大如擘指，象其臥形，物之微細，或行、或毛、或蠃、或介、或鱗，以虫爲象。凡虫之屬皆从虫。【玉篇】古文虺字。註見三畫。【佩觿集】蛇虫之虫爲蟲豸，非是。○按《說文》《玉篇》《類篇》等書，虫䖵蟲皆分作三部，虫吁鬼切，䖵古䰟切，蟲持中切，截然三音，義亦各別。《字彙》《正字通》合䖵蟲二部倂入虫部，雖失古人分部之意，而披覽者易于查考，故姑仍其舊。若《六書正譌》以爲虫卽蟲省文，則大謬也。</p>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=虫">虫</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/866B.svg">                          <p>一名蝮，博三寸，首大如擘指。象其臥形。物之微細，或行，或毛，或蠃，或介，或鱗，以虫爲象。凡虫之屬皆从虫。許偉切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/866B_5828.svg" /><span class="swbf">(虫)</span><span class="ywyy">一名<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2741B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧐛</span>。</span><span class="swdz">爾雅釋魚：蝮，虫。今本虫作虺。</span><span class="ywyy">博三寸，首大如擘指。</span><span class="swdz">釋魚文。擘指，大指也。郭云：此自一種蛇，人自名爲蝮虺。今蝮蛇細頸，大頭，焦尾，色如艾綬文，文閒有毛似豬鬛，鼻上有鍼。大者長七八尺，一名反鼻。非虺之類。此足以明此自一㮔蛇。按此注見斯干正義及小顔田儋傳注。郭意爾雅之蝮今無此物，今之蝮蛇非爾雅之蝮蛇也。</span><span class="ywyy">象其臥形。</span><span class="swdz">虫篆象臥而曲尾形。它篆下云虫也，象冤曲垂尾形。許偉切。十五部。</span><span class="ywyy">物之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2304B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣁋</span>細，或行或飛，</span><span class="swdz">或飛二字依爾雅釋文補。</span><span class="ywyy">或毛或<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27779.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧝹</span>，</span><span class="swdz"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27779.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧝹</span>見衣部，但也。俗作蠃，非。</span><span class="ywyy">或介或鱗，㠯虫爲象。</span><span class="swdz">按以爲象，言以爲象形也。从虫之字多左形右聲，左皆用虫爲象形也。月令：春，其蟲䚬。夏，其蟲羽。中央，其蟲倮。虎豹之屬，恆淺毛也。秋，其蟲毛。冬，其蟲介。許云或飛者，羽也。古虫蟲不分，故以蟲諧聲之字多省作虫，如融赨是也。鱗介以虫爲形，如螭虯<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/272A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧊧</span>蚌是也。飛者以虫爲形，如蝙蝠是也。毛<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27779.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧝹</span>以虫爲形。如蝯蜼是也。</span><span class="ywyy">凡虫之屬皆从虫。</span></p>                          </div>
