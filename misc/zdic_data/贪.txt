                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>贪</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">貪</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tān.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄢˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 求多，不知足：～玩。～杯（过分好喝酒）。～婪。～污（利用职权非法地取得财物）。～恋（十分留恋）。～心。～图。～财。～求。～权。～欲。～得无厌。～赃枉法。</p></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贪</strong></p><p>貪 <span class="dicpy">tān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，今声。从<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>，与财物有关。本义:贪财)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be greedy for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪，欲物也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>、 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>河内之比，谓惏曰残。 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>谓之贪。——<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪于饮食，惰于作务。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·非儒下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪货弃命。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爱财曰贪，爱食曰婪。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">虞公</span>贪利其璧与马而欲许之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:贪赃(官吏接受贿赂);贪便宜;贪得无厌;贪冒(贪图财利);贪鄙(贪婪卑鄙);贪汙(贪求私利，取非分的财物);贪墨(贪财受贿);贪夫徇财(贪婪的人为财而死)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 贪婪。多欲而不知满足 <span class="encs">[have an insatiable desire for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪欲无艺。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左右皆恶之，以为贪而不知足。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以贪勤民。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>贪。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:贪虐(贪婪暴虐);贪娼(贪婪而妒忌);贪狠(贪心暴戾);贪残(贪婪残贼);贪花(怜爱美色);贪粗(贪婪粗犷);贪谗(贪婪并进谗言);贪陵(贪婪侵夺);贪悍(贪婪凶横)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 求。同<span class="yhcs">“探”</span> <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪权据凡，则高名自立矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若夫推己以议物，捨状以贪情。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·郭躬传论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:贪祸(取祸);贪黑(摸黑);贪嗜(贪求嗜好);贪贾(贪图小利的商人);贪迷(贪恋，迷恋);贪怯(贪生怕死)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 贪污 <span class="encs">[corrupt;venal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪于财贺。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪慕富贵。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知某不贪。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">官贪吏虐。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:贪滥(贪污渎职);贪黩(贪污);贪洿(贪污);贪官(贪污的官吏);贪罔(贪赃欺罔);贪吏(贪污的官吏)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/贪杯">贪杯</a></span><span
