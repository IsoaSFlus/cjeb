                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>坠</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">墜</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>落，掉下：～马。呱呱～地。</li>
	<li>往下沉：下～。</li>
	<li>系挂在器物上垂着的东西：扇～儿。～子（➊垂着的东西；➋流行于中国河南、山东的一种曲艺，如&ldquo;河南～～&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fall down, drop, sink, go to ruin</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hinfallen  (V)​,niedergehen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tomber,faire une chute,courber</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坠</strong></p>
<p>墜 <span class="dicpy">zhu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，队声。坠落到地上，故从土。本义:落下，掉下)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fall;drop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隊，从高陨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗字作墜。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">墜，落也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">墜，堕也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死而坠也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;文公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝饮木籣之坠露兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矢交坠兮士争先。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九歌&middot;国殇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自舟中坠于水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剑之所从坠。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抗坠疾徐。(坠，声音低沉。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">侯方域</span><span class="smcs">《壮悔堂文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">杞国</span>有人，忧天地崩坠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;天瑞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坠入深渊难以复出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;枚乘传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坠睫(落泪);坠楼(从楼上掉落下地);坠泪(坠睫;掉泪);坠红(落花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 丧失;败坏 <span class="encs">[lose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今惟殷坠厥命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;酒诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自先王莫坠其国，当君而亡之，君之过也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坠亡(丧失);坠失(失去);坠言(失言);坠履(不轻易遗弃旧物或故物失而复得之典)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 毁坏 <span class="encs">[ruin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者</span><span class="diczx2">夫子</span>闵王道之缺，伤斯文之坠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙&middot;史传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坠典(已废亡的典章制度);坠湮(湮没亡失);坠绪(坠遗。行将断绝的皇统)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 垂挂;因分量重而下垂 <span class="encs">[weigh down]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:坠岸(陡岸，险岸);坠脚(置于末尾;吊在下面)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>坠</strong></p>
<p>墜 <span class="dicpy">zhu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>吊在下面的装饰性的东西 <span class="encs">[a hanging object]</span>。</p>
