                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>窖</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，告声。从<span class="yhcs">“穴”</span>，表示与洞穴有关。本义:方形地窖)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 藏谷麦的地穴 <span class="encs">[cellar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窖，地藏也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穿窦窖。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“椭曰窦，方曰窖。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">置入窖中。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:酒窖;菜窖;窖货(为预防腐烂变质而贮藏在地窖内的蔬菜果品等货物)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 坑;穴 <span class="encs">[pit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饥狗盈窖，嗼然。——<span class="smcs">《吕昏春秋·首时》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:窖穴;粪窖</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>窖</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 贮藏;埋藏 <span class="encs">[store;bury]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之败也，豪杰皆争取金玉，而 <span class="diczx2">任氏</span>独窖仓粟。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:啥东西都窖在地下;窖藏(地窖内贮藏或埋藏的财物)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/窖肥">窖肥</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/窖藏">窖藏</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/窖子">窖子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
