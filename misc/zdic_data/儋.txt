                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">儋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>儋</strong> <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 担(擔)的古字。肩荷、用肩挑 <span class="encs">[carry on a shoulder pole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令婢路上担粪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;规箴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:儋石(容器名。儋容一石;一说二石为儋。也作担石，檐石);儋负(担负。肩挑背负);儋何(担荷。肩负责任)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 抬。二人用肩扛 <span class="encs">[carry by two persons]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上人著百尺楼上，儋梯而去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;黜免》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>儋</strong> <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>同&ldquo;聸&rdquo;。耳下垂 <span class="encs">[big-ear]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:儋耳(部族名。此部族俗习是雕刻颊皮，上连耳部，故得其名;又指一种耳垂式装饰品)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>儋</strong> <span class="dicpy">Dān</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Dan prefecture]</span>。汉置儋耳郡，唐改为儋州。民国设县。旧治所在今海南省儋县西北</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 儋县 <span class="encs">[Dan county]</span>，在海南省的西北部。人口71万(1991年)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>儋</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 成担货物的计量单位 <span class="encs">[dan，a unit of weight =50 kilograms]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儋石不储，饥寒交至。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《劝农》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集中" target="_blank">子集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集中&amp;bs=人" target="_blank">人</a>】
                              儋·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/510B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】都甘切【正韻】都監切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音擔。負荷也。</p>
<p>又儋耳，郡名。【前漢&middot;武帝紀應劭註】儋耳者，大耳種也。其渠率耳垂肩三寸。唐立儋州，後爲昌化郡。今儋州屬廣東瓊州府。</p>
<p>又姓。周大夫儋翩。</p>
<p>又人名。司馬遷疑太史儋卽老耼。</p>
<p>又罃也。【揚子&middot;方言】齊之東北，海岱之閒，罃謂之儋。【史記&middot;貨殖傳】漿千儋。【註】一儋，兩罃也。【前漢&middot;蒯通傳】守儋石之祿。【揚雄傳】家無儋石之儲。【註】儋平聲。【通雅】漢書一石爲石，再石爲儋，言人儋之也。　通作擔。別作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27B43.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧭃</span>。</p>
