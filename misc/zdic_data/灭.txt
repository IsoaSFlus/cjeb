                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>灭</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">滅</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">mi&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>火熄：熄～。</li>
	<li>完，尽，使不存在 ：～口。～亡。不可磨～。～族（古代的一种残酷刑罚，一人犯罪，株连他的父母兄弟妻子等亲属，都被一起杀掉）。</li>
	<li>淹没：～顶之灾。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> extinguish; wipe out, exterminate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> abschalten, ausmachen, beenden  (V)​,ertrinken  (V)​,löschen, ausmachen (Licht, Feuer, Kerze)​  (V)​,vernichten  (V)​,erloschen (Licht, Feuer, Kerze)​  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> éteindre,exterminer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">灭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>灭</strong></p>
<p>滅 <span class="dicpy">mi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，烕(<span class="dicpy">mi&egrave;</span>)声。简化字&ldquo;灭&rdquo;为截取&ldquo;烕&rdquo;中下部，被通常附会为会意。本义:消灭;除尽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[extinguish;obliterate;be destroyed;perish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灭，尽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灭，绝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国之灭亡无日矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家有既亡，国有既灭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;襄公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以小畜大灭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;情势》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谁得而族灭也?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六国破灭，非兵不利，战不善，敝在赂</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦皇</span>灭典，亦造仙诗&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙&middot;明诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灭</span><span class="diczx2">韩</span>亡 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灭于绛;一朝而灭;灭亲(断绝亲族间的关系);灭户(全家皆死，宗嗣断绝);消灭(使消灭;除掉;灭亡);毁灭(摧毁消灭);灭夷(消灭);灭劫(毁灭的灾难)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 消失，隐没 <span class="encs">[disappear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何(荷)校(木枷)灭耳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《戏为六绝句》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔曹身与名俱灭。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灭抹(埋没);灭名(名誉尽失);灭身(丧身，毁灭自身);灭没(湮没;隐没);灭景(隐没形影，指隐居)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 熄灭 <span class="encs">[fire or light goes;extinguish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">火不灭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灭却(熄灭);灭息(熄灭)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 淹没 <span class="encs">[drown;submerge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">过涉灭顶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;大过》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 涂抹改动 <span class="encs">[alter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:灭刺(让名刺上的字迹消隐，指不愿投刺干谒);灭青(指古代用刀削改竹简上的字句)</span></p>
