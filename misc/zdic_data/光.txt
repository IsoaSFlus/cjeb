                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>光</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">guāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>太阳、火、电等放射出来耀人眼睛，使人感到明亮，能看见物体的那种东西：阳～。月～。火～。～华（明亮的光辉）。</li><li>荣誉：～临（敬辞，意含宾客来临给主人带来光彩）。～顾。～复。</li><li>使显赫：～大。～宗耀祖。</li><li>景物：春～明媚。</li><li>光滑：～滑。～洁。～泽。</li><li>完了，一点不剩：杀～烧～。吃～用～。</li><li>露着：～膀子。</li><li>单，只：～剩下一口气。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> light, brilliant, shine; only</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Glanz, Ehre, Ruhm  (S)​,Licht  (S)​,alle sein, zu Ende sein  (Adj, vulg)​,bloß, nur, allein  (Adj)​,glatt, blank, nackt, kahl  (Adj)​,Guang  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rayon (de lumière)​,brillant,gloire,nu,épuiser,consommé jusqu'au bout,seulement,rien que,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">光</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>光</strong> <span class="dicpy">guāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，<span class="yhcs">“从火，在人上”</span>。本义:光芒，光亮)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[light;ray]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">光，明也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">光，晃也，晃晃然也。亦言广也，所照广远也。——<span class="smcs">《释名·释天》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与日月兮齐光。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九歌·云中君》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">能游冥冥者与日月同光。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·俶真》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日月淑清而扬光。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国之光。——<span class="smcs">《易·观》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夜未央，庭燎之光。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·庭燎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">推此志也，虽与日月争光可也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">光远而自他有耀者也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公二十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">光明之耀也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">容光必照焉。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山有小口，仿佛若有光。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有红光一缕起土桥，直射城西。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《阎典史传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:阳光;灯光;反光(反射的光线);色光(带颜色的光);晨光(清晨的太阳光);曙光(清晨的日光);光晃(光芒闪烁)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 色泽;光彩 <span class="encs">[color and lustre]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妾有绣腰襦，葳蕤自生光。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蛾脸不舒，中袖无光。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
