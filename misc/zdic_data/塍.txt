						<p>● <strong>塍</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄥˊ</span><span class="dicfq">《廣韻》食陵切，平蒸船。蒸部。</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>田间的土埂子。《説文&bull;土部》：&ldquo;塍，稻中畦也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>小堤。《廣雅&bull;釋宫》：&ldquo;塍，隄也。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a raised path between fields, a dike</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> levée de terre entre les champs</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>塍</strong></p>
<p>堘 <span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，朕( <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n</span>)声。本义:田间的土埂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[earth bank path between fields]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塍，稻中畦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塍，畔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塍者，田中作介画，畜水以养禾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;稻人》</span>疏</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:田塍(田埂);塍区(比喻界限;格式)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 畦田 <span class="encs">[field]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:塍畎(指田地);塍陌(田间小路);塍陇(田间隆起的小路)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 小堤 <span class="encs">[small dike]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:塍岸(小堤;田堤)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/584D_1077.svg" /><span class="swbf">(塍)</span><span class="ywyy">稻田中畦埓也。</span><span class="swdz">集韵、類篇、宋本作稻中畦也。今本及文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨕖</span>注作稻田畦也。韵㑹作稻中畦埓也。今合訂之如此。畦，五十畞之介也。埓者，庳垣，亦所以爲畍。稻田中作介畫以蓄水，取義於此。謂之塍必言稻中者，禾黍不必爲此，惟稻必蓄水以養之。周禮稻人：以遂均水，以列舍水。鄭曰：遂，田首受水小溝也。列，田之畦埓也。開遂舍水於列中，按列讀如遮迾之迾，非人所行之畛陌也。許、鄭說正同。今四川謂之田繩子，江浙謂之田緪，緪亦繩也。西都賦：溝塍刻鏤。</span><span class="ywyy">从土<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26A36.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦨶</span>聲。</span><span class="swdz">食陵切。六部。</span></p>                          </div>
