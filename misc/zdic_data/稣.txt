<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">穌</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˉ</span></p>

</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> revive, to rise again; collect</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> auferstehen, beleben</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> recueillir,revivre</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>稣</strong></p>
<p>穌 <span class="dicpy">sū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾，鱼声。&ldquo;苏&rdquo;的本字。艸、禾义通。&ldquo;稣&rdquo;常用为&ldquo;苏醒&rdquo;义)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;苏&rdquo; <span class="encs">[same as &ldquo;苏&rdquo;]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">稣，把取禾若也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>改为&ldquo;杷取禾若也&rdquo;，并注:&ldquo;杷，各本作把，今正。禾若散乱，杷而取之，不当言把也。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 转意为死而复生，苏醒，复活。今作&ldquo;苏&rdquo; <span class="encs">[revive;come around;recover consciousness]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">留尸十日，平旦喉中有声如雨，俄而稣活&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法苑珠林》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> &ldquo;穌&rdquo;另见 <span class="dicpy">sū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span> (苏)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">穌</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“稣”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/穌">穌</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下" target="_blank">午集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下&amp;bs=禾" target="_blank">禾</a>】
                              穌·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7A4C.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】素孤切【集韻】【正韻】孫租切【韻會】孫徂切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蘇。【說文】把取禾若也。【徐曰】穌，猶部斂之也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】息也，舒悅也。【韻會】死而更生曰穌。通作蘇。【書&middot;仲虺之誥】后來其蘇。【禮&middot;樂記】蟄蟲昭蘇。【註】更息曰蘇。【集韻】俗作甦。</p>
<p>又【職方外紀】耶穌，西國言救世生也。</p>
<p>又叶桑河切，音娑。【黃庭經】臨絕呼之亦復穌，久久行之飛太霞。霞音何。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7A4C_4557.svg" /><span class="swbf">(穌)</span><span class="ywyy">杷取禾若也。</span><span class="swdz">杷各本作把，今正。禾若散亂，杷而取之，不當言把也。離騷：蘇糞壤以充幃兮，謂申椒其不芳。王逸曰：蘇，取也。韓信傳曰：樵蘇後爨，師不宿飽。漢書音義曰：樵，取薪也。蘇，取草也。此皆假蘇爲穌也。蘇，桂荏也。蘇行而穌廢矣。樂記：蟄蟲昭蘇。注云：更息曰蘇。據玉篇云：穌，息也，死而更生也。然則<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2206B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢁫</span>馮所據樂記作穌。</span><span class="ywyy">从禾魚聲。</span><span class="swdz">素孤切。五部。</span></p>                          </div>
