                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>戒</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，上面是<span class="yhcs">“戈”</span>，下面象两只手(即<span class="yhcs">“廾”</span>)。两手持戈，表示戒备森严。本义:警戒，戒备)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[guard against]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戒，警也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岂不日戒。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采薇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以三年之戒。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曾子问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">基浇能戒之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">息民不戒。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怵然为戒。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·养生主》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戒之慎勿忘。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知足以自戒。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:戒慎(戒备谨慎);戒书(用来自我警戒的文字);戒旦(警告天将破晓);戒守(警戒守卫);戒命(禁止某些行为的命令);戒戢(警介禁止);戒诗(自我警戒的诗)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“诫”</span>。告诫 <span class="encs">[warn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主人戒宾。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">三保</span>戒团众。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:戒勉(告诫勉励);戒约(告诫约束);戒谕(告诫;训导);戒朝(告诫无将明)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 留神，当心 <span class="encs">[be careful;caution]</span>。如:戒口(慎言);戒火(慎于用火);戒谨;戒慎</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 预备;准备 <span class="encs">[be ready]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既种既戒。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·大田》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:戒装(准备行装);戒辖(准备车辆)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 登程，出发 <span class="encs">[start]</span>。如:戒行(登程，出发上路);戒途(出发，准备上路);戒程(登程，出发上路);戒道(登程，出发上路)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 戒除 <span class="encs">[abstrain from;give up;stop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戒奢以俭。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span>
