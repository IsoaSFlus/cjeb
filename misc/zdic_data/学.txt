                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">学</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>学</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">學</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xu&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>效法，钻研知识，获得知识，读书：～生。～徒。～习。～业。～友。～者。～阀。～制。～历。～步邯郸（讥讽人只知模仿，不善于学而无成就，亦作&ldquo;邯郸学步&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>传授知识的地方：～校（简称&ldquo;学&rdquo;或&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">校</span>&rdquo;）。～院。～府。中～。大～。上～。</li>
	<li>掌握的知识：～问（简称&ldquo;学&rdquo;）。～术（一切学问的总称）。～位。～士（➊学位名，大学毕业生；➋古代官名）。才～。治～。～识。博～多才。</li>
	<li>分门别类的有系统的知识：～说。哲～。数～。小～（➊古代指文学、音韵、训诂学；➋现指初等学校）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">学</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>学</strong></p>
<p>壆、斈 <span class="dicpy">xu&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本作&ldquo;壆&rdquo;，象双手构木为屋形。后作声符，加&ldquo;子&rdquo;为义符。子，孩子。小孩子是学习的主体。本义:学习)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[study;learn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学，识也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">好学近乎知。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">念终始典于学。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;文王世子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学，效也。近而愈明者学也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尚书大传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学者，学其所不能学也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;庚桑楚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">远寻师学。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学而时习之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学即继以问也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:学书(认真读书);学学乖(经世面，学聪明);学理论;学技术;好学;苦学;教学相长;学文(学习人文学科或社会科学;学习文化知识);学古(学习研究古代典籍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 模仿 <span class="encs">[imitate;mimic]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">巧姐儿</span>]</span>笑嘻嘻走到 <span class="diczx2">凤姐</span>身边学舌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span>。又如:学嘴(学舌);调嘴学舌;学他爸爸走路的样子;学不上来</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 讲述，说 <span class="encs">[speak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一五一十向着</span><span class="diczx2">珍哥</span>、 <span class="diczx2">晁大舍</span>学个不了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醒世姻缘传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见说万山潭，渔童尽能学。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆龟蒙</span><span class="smcs">《鱼具》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:学舌(说闲话;搬弄是非)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 讲学 <span class="encs">[give lectures]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学，教也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">叔仲</span>皮学子柳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡学世子及学士。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;文王世子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">哀王</span>者，帝之少弟，与太子游学相长大。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>学</strong></p>
<p>壆 <span class="dicpy">xu&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 学校 <span class="encs">[school]</span>。又曾称学庐、学教、学馆、学堂、学宫、学院、学屋、学园</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学，官也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释室》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子学以致其道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学则三代共之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万用入学。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《夏小正》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小学在公宫南之左，大学在郊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国子先生晨入太学。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《进学解》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:学俸(教师的薪水);学宪(学台);学台(学政);学里(明清时设立的管理各级地方儒生的机构)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 学问 <span class="encs">[learning]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子积学。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学有未达。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是芋视乃学。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生平为学。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人之为学有难易乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:学耻全牛(惭愧学问未到炉火纯青的地步);学行(学问与品行);才学(才能与学问);绝学(失传的学问);家学(祖传学问)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 学科;某一门类系统的知识 <span class="encs">[subject of study;branch of learning]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今日覩卿词学。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《剧谈录&middot;宣宗夜召翰林学士》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:文学;哲学;地理学;化学;问学于师</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">學</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“学”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/學">學</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=子" target="_blank">子</a>】
                              學·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5B78.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】胡覺切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】轄覺切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音鷽。【說文】覺悟也，本作斆，篆作學。【增韻】受敎傳業曰學。朱子曰：學之爲言效也。【詩&middot;周頌】日就月將，學有緝熙于光明。【書&middot;說命】王人求多聞，時惟建事。學于古訓，乃有獲。【禮&middot;學記】君子之於學也，藏焉，修焉，息焉，游焉，夫然故安其學而親其師，樂其友而信其道。</p>
<p>又學校，庠序總名。【禮&middot;王制】天子命之敎，然後爲學。小學在公宮南之左，大學在郊，天子曰辟雍，諸侯曰頖宮。</p>
<p>又姓。見【姓苑】。</p>
<p>又叶許旣切，音戲。【前漢&middot;匡張孔馬敘傳】樂安裒裒，古之文學。叶下司，司音細。</p>
<p>又叶後敎切，音效。【傅毅&middot;廸志詩】訓我嘉務，惠我博學。叶上誥。　【正字通】與斆別。○按《書&middot;說命》惟斆學半。學與斆別。諸家泥經傳，斆或作學，合學斆爲一，非。今斆音效，而學校字但作學，互詳攴部斆字註。</p>
