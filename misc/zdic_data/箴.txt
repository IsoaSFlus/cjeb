                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">箴</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>箴</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>同“<span class="diczx3">针</span>”。</li><li>劝告，劝戒：～言。～规。～谏。</li><li>古代一种文体，以告诫规劝为主：～铭（“箴”是规戒性的韵文；“<span class="diczx3">铭</span>”是刻在器物或碑石上兼于规戒、褒赞的韵文。因其作用有相似之处，故后人多连称）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> needle, probe; admon</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ermahnen, warnen ,Zhen  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> aiguille,sonde,avertir,exhorter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">箴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>箴</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">名</span></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，咸声。本义:缝衣用的工具。后作<span class="yhcs">“针”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[needle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衣裳破绽，纫箴请补缀。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:箴缕(针线);箴末(喻微细);箴管(指缝缀之事);箴盥(古代妇女承姑奉盥，视为箴规)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 针炙治病所用的针形器具 <span class="encs">[probe]</span>。如:箴石(石制的针。古代治病之具。亦指可用以制针的石头);箴砭(古代用石针治病。后借喻为纠谬;规谏)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 文体的一种，以规戒为表达的主题 <span class="encs">[a type of didactic literary composition]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">箴顿挫而清壮。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《文赋》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:箴铭(文体名。箴是规戒性的韵文;铭在古代常刻在器物上或碑石上，兼用于规戒、褒赞)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>箴</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">动</span></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 规劝，告诫 <span class="encs">[admonish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贤</span><span class="diczx2">袭人</span>娇嗔箴 <span class="diczx2">宝玉</span>，俏 <span class="diczx2">平儿</span>软语救 <span class="diczx2">贾琏</span>。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:箴谏(劝谏别人改正过失);箴砭(指出并纠正错误);箴规(劝谏，规戒);箴诫(规劝告诫);箴诲(规劝教训);箴训(箴戒;规训)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/箴言">箴言</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>箴</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄓㄣ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>zhēn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4428.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">文体名。以表达规劝性质为主题。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷八七·扬雄传下·赞曰》：「<b>箴</b>莫善于虞<b>箴</b>，作州<b>箴</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝梁·刘勰《文心雕龙·铭<b>箴</b>》：「<b>箴</b>者，所以攻疾防患，喻针石也。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">中医用来刺入穴道，以达到医疗效果的针状物。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷三○·艺文志》：「医经者，……而用度箴石汤火所施，调百药齐和之所宜。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">规戒、劝谏。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左传·昭公二十六年》：「父慈而教，子孝而<b>箴</b>。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上" target="_blank">未集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上&amp;bs=竹" target="_blank">竹</a>】
                              箴·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7BB4.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】職深切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】諸深切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音斟。【說文】綴衣箴也。【禮·內則】紉箴請補綴。</p><p>又古者以石爲箴，所以刺病。【前漢·藝文志】用度箴石箴石湯火所施。【註】箴，所以刺病也。石謂砭石，卽石箴也。【山海經】高氏之山，其上多玉，其下多箴石。【註】可以爲砥針治癰腫者。</p><p>又規戒也。【書·盤庚】猶胥顧于箴言。【左傳·襄十四年】工誦箴諫。【前漢·揚雄傳贊】箴莫善于《虞箴》，作《州箴》。【註】九州之箴也。【玉海】箴者，諫誨之辭，若箴之療疾，故名箴。</p><p>又姓。【風俗通】衞有大夫箴莊子。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋器】一羽謂之箴，十羽謂之縳。</p><p>又官名。【左傳·宣四年】子文孫箴尹克黃。【註】箴尹，官名。</p><p>又魚名。【山海經】栒狀之山，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CB5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣲵</span>水出焉，其中多箴魚，其狀如儵，其啄如箴。</p><p>又鳥名。【司馬相如·上林賦】箴疵鵁盧。【註】張揖曰：箴疵，似魚虎而倉黑色。</p><p>又草名。【神異經】桂林有睡草，見之則令人睡。一名醉草，亦呼爲懶婦箴。【韻會補】一曰竹名。亦通作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A201.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪈁</span>。【前漢·司馬相如傳】箴疵鵁盧。箴疵，鳥名。【說文】作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A201.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪈁</span>鴜。</p><p>又【集韻】口減切，音槏。古斬切，音減。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>竹名也。</p><p>又【韻會補】與鍼同。諸韻別出箴爲箴誡字。</p><p>又通作針。</p><p>
