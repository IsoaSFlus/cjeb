                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铙</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>铙</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鐃</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">náo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/náo.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄠˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>铜质圆形的打击乐器，比钹大。</li>    <li>古代军中乐器，像铃铛，但没有中间的锤。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cymbals; hand bell; disturb</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Nao (chin. Bronzeglocke ohne Klöppel)​  (S, Mus)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> clochette militaire pour arrêter les tambours,cymbales</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>铙</strong></p><p>鐃 <span class="dicpy">náo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/náo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 乐器名 <span class="encs">[big cymbals]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铙，小钲也。军法。卒长执铙。从金，尧声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铙，铃也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以金铙止鼓。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·鼓人》</span>。注:如铃无舌、有秉，执而鸣之，以止击鼓。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辨鼓铎镯铙之用。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司马》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“读如讙哓之哓。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 铜制圆形的乐器，常和钹配合演奏。如;铙歌(军中乐歌;泛指军歌);铙挽(铙歌和挽歌)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代击乐器。形制与钹相似:唯中间隆起部分较小。以两片为一副，相击发声。如:铙钹(一种打击乐器。古称铜钹、铜盘、铜钵);铙管(鼓吹中的横笛)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>铙</strong></p><p>鐃 <span class="dicpy">náo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/náo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“挠”</span>。扰乱 <span class="encs">[disturb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">万物无足以铙心者，故静也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天道》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“譊”</span>。喧嚣 <span class="encs">[argue;debate;clamour;hulabaloo]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今年尚可后年铙。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·五行志一》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9403.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】女交切【集韻】【正韻】尼交切【韻會】泥交切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音呶。【說文】小鉦也。軍法，卒長執鐃。【玉篇】似鈴無舌，軍中所用也。【釋名】鐃，聲鐃鐃也。【周禮·地官·鼓人】以金鐃止鼓。【註】鐃如鈴，無舌，有柄，執而鳴之，以止擊鼓。</p><p>又奏樂所用也。【禮·樂記】始奏以文，復亂以武。【註】文謂鼓，武謂金，鐃樂始奏先擊鼓，亂猶言終也。鼓聲爲陽，故謂文。鐃聲爲隂，故謂武。文以始之，武以收之，言節奏得宜也。</p><p>又【博古圖】漢舞鐃二，其形上圜下方，下作疏櫺，中含銅丸，謂之舌鼓，動有聲。</p><p>又樂府有鐃歌，軍中鼓吹曲也。</p><p>又與譊通。【後漢·五行志童謠】今年尚可，後年鐃。○按風俗通作譊。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>女敎切，與橈同。【莊子·天道篇】萬物無足以鐃心者。</p><p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">小鉦也。</span>鉦鐃一物，而鐃較小。渾言不别，析言則有辨也。周禮言鐃不言鉦，詩言鉦不言鐃，不得以大小别之。大司馬：仲冬大閱，乃<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>退，鳴鐃且卻。左傳：陳子曰：吾聞<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>不聞金。亦謂聞<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>進，聞鐃退也。<span class="jieshi">从金堯聲。</span>女交切。二部。<span class="jieshi">軍灋：卒長執鐃。</span>見大司馬職：五人爲伍，五伍爲兩，五兩爲卒。                          </div>
