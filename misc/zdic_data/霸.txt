                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">霸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>霸</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">b&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄚˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>指依杖权势或武力欺压他人的人或集团：～王。称～。恶～。</li>	<li>奉行强力政策，或实行强力占有：～占。～权。～道。～略。</li>	<li>古代称诸侯的盟主：～主（①中国春秋时势力最大并取得首领地位的诸侯；②在某一领域或地区称霸的人或集团）。～业。春秋五～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rule by might rather than right</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Tyrann  (S)​,tyrannisieren  (V)​,herrisch; anmaßend; tyrannisch  (Adj)​,Führer des Bundes der Herzöge im alten China  (S, Gesch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> hégémon,tyran,despote,seigneur,chef féodal,régner par la force,usurper,dominer,tyranniser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">霸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>霸</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从月。①( <span class="dicpy">p&ograve;</span>)本义:阴历每月之初始见的月光。这个意义又写作&ldquo;魄&rdquo;。②古代诸侯之长)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假借为伯。指古代诸侯联盟的盟主 <span class="encs">[chief of feudal princes]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">霸者，伯也，行方伯之职。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">管仲</span>以其君霸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">霸者，长也。言为诸侯之长。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;离娄&middot;丁音》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五霸不同法而霸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;更汉》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:春秋五霸;霸王请客;强留(歇后语)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 依仗权势或实力横行一方的人 <span class="encs">[overlord;tyrant;bully;despot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">薛</span>家系 <span class="diczx2">金陵</span>一霸。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">曹雪芹</span><span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:渔霸;恶霸</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>霸</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 称霸 <span class="encs">[dominate;tyrannize]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共工氏之霸九州也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂霸有天下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:各霸一方;独霸天下;霸道(指行事蛮不讲理)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 霸占 <span class="encs">[seize by force]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">硬把</span><span class="diczx2">桦林山</span>这座天生天化的东西霸成他自己的家产。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕梁英雄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="encs">[文采、才能等]</span> 过人 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主佐合德，文采必霸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></span></p>

<ol>
	<li>

</ol></div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中" target="_blank">戌集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中&amp;bs=雨" target="_blank">雨</a>】
                              霸·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=21" target="_blank">21</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9738.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23378.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣍸</span>【唐韻】【廣韻】【正韻】普伯切【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】匹陌切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音拍。【說文】月始生霸然也。承大月二日，承小月三日。从月䨣聲。【增韻】月體黑者謂之霸。【玉篇】今作魄。【書&middot;武成&middot;旁死魄釋文】魄，普白反。【說文】作霸，匹革反。【前漢&middot;律歷志】四月已丑朔死霸。死霸，朔也。生霸，望也。是月甲辰望，乙巳旁之，故武成篇曰：惟四月旣旁生霸。【師古曰】霸，古與魄同。【韻會補】歐陽曰：俗从西作覇，非。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>必駕切，音灞。【玉篇】霸王也。【孟子】以力假人者霸。【禮&middot;祭法】共工氏之霸九州也。【註】共工氏無錄而王，謂之霸。【左傳&middot;成二年】五伯之霸也。【註】夏伯昆吾，商伯大彭、豕韋，周伯齊桓、晉文。或曰齊桓、晉文、宋襄、秦<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25846.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥡆</span>、楚莊。【疏】伯者，長也。言爲諸侯之長也。鄭康成云：霸，把也，言把持王者之政敎，故其字或作伯，或作霸也。【史記&middot;項羽紀】項羽自立爲西楚霸王。○按毛氏曰：五伯之伯讀曰霸。伯者，取牧伯長諸侯之義，後人恐與侯伯字相溷，故借用霸字以別之。</p><p>又周伯琦曰：今俗以爲王霸字，而月霸乃用<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29CB8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩲸</span>字，非本義。王霸當借用伯字，月魄當用霸字，其義始正。</p><p>又【集韻】一曰月始生也。</p><p>又州名。【韻會】秦上谷郡地，唐置霸州，五代周攺莫州。</p><p>又霸陵，漢縣名，屬京兆尹，故芷陽，文帝更名，見【前漢&middot;地理志】。</p><p>又水名。【前漢&middot;地理志註】霸水，出藍田谷，北入渭。師古曰：兹水秦<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25846.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥡆</span>公更名，以章霸功視子孫。【史記&middot;項羽紀】沛公軍霸上。</p><p>又姓。【韻會】益州耆壽傳有霸栩。</p><p>又叶搏故切，音布。【劉歆&middot;列女贊】非刺虞丘，蔽賢之路。楚莊用焉，功業遂霸。</p><p>又叶補過切，音播。【韋孟&middot;諷諫詩】興國救顚，孰違悔過。追思黃髮，秦繆以霸。</p>
