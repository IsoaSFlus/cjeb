						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>寻</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">尋</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xún  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xún.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>找，搜求：～找。～觅。～机。～问。～访。～衅。追～。～章摘句。</li><li>古代的长度单位（一寻等于八尺）：～常（古代八尺为“寻”，倍寻为“<span class="diczx3">常</span>”，都是平常的长度，后泛指平常、素常）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寻</strong></p><p>尋 <span class="dicpy">xún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>(会意。从工口，从又(彐)寸。本义:八尺为寻) 中国古代的一种长度单位，八尺为寻 <span class="encs">[a measure of length]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尋，绎理也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。度人之两臂为寻，八尺也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寻，长也。周官之法，度广为寻。——<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是寻是尺。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·閟宫》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“八尺曰寻。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">舒肘知寻。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记·主言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度寻舒两肱也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹄间三寻。——<span class="smcs">《史记·张仪传》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“七尺曰寻。按，程氏瑶田云，度广曰寻，度深曰仞。皆伸两臂为度。度广则身平臂直，而适得八尺;度深则身侧臂曲，而仅得七尺。其说精巧，寻仞皆以两臂度之，故仞亦或言八尺，寻亦或言七尺也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">争寻常以尽其民。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无寻尺之禄。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">千寻铁锁沉江底。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">深逾寻丈。——<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>寻</strong></p><p>尋 <span class="dicpy">xún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 寻找 <span class="encs">[look of;search;seek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远寻师学。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寻向所志。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=寸" target="_blank">寸</a>】
                              尋·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5C0B.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B3B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡬻</span>【唐韻】徐林切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】徐心切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音潯。【說文】繹理也，本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/224AB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢒫</span>，从工口，从又寸。工口，亂也。</p><p>又寸，分理之也。彡聲。【增韻】求也。【前漢·黃霸傳】語次尋繹。【註】抽引而出也。</p><p>又度名。【周禮·地官·媒氏註】八尺曰尋，倍尋曰常。【小爾雅】四尺謂之仞，倍仞謂之尋。【孟子】枉尺而直尋，宜若可爲也。</p><p>又仍也，繼也。【左傳·昭元年】日尋干戈，以相征討。【古語】毫末不扎，將尋斧柯。</p><p>又俄也。【晉羊祜讓開府表】以身誤陛下辱高位，傾覆亦尋而至。</p><p>又用也。【左傳·僖五年】將尋師焉。【註】尋，用也。</p><p>又侵尋，猶浸淫也。【前漢·武帝紀】巡郡縣，侵尋太山矣。</p><p>又借作溫燖。【左傳·哀十二年】吳使人請尋盟。子貢曰：若可尋也，亦可寒也。【註】尋，重也，溫也，前盟巳寒，更溫之使熱。與燖義同。</p><p>又長也。【揚子·方言】自關以西，秦晉梁益閒，凡物長謂之尋。</p><p>又俗謂庸常爲尋常。</p><p>又姓。晉尋會，唐劉黑闥將尋相。　【韻會】毛氏曰：从口。俗从几作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B36.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡬶</span>，非。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2065B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠙛</span>。</p>
