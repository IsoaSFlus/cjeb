                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">窥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>窥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">窺</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kuī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuī.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄨㄟˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>从小孔、缝隙或隐蔽处偷看：～探。～伺。～测。～视。管～蠡测（喻见识浅陋，看不清高深的道理）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>peep, watch, spy on, pry</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> guetter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">窥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>窥</strong></p>
<p>窺、闚 <span class="dicpy">kuī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，规声。本义:从小孔或缝里看)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 暗中察看。亦泛指观看 <span class="encs">[peep;spy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窥，小视也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钻穴隙相窥，逾墙相从。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窥见室家之好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子路》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妻从门间而窥其夫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;管晏列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔽林间窥之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窥觑(偷看);窥瞰(窥探;暗中偷看);窥欲(窥伺贪求)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 观察，侦探 <span class="encs">[pry about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窥谷忘反。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窥父不在。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窥究(深究察觉);窥圃(窥园。观赏园景);窥深(观察深入细致);窥睹(观看;观察);窥览(阅览;观察)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 伺机图谋;觊觎 <span class="encs">[watch for one&#39;s chance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禁人之窥伺者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">数通史相窥观。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以窥周室。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自胡马窥</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>去后。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姜夔</span><span class="smcs">《扬州慢》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窥图(窥探谋算);窥攻(伺机进攻);窥取(伺机夺取);窥朝(窥伺朝廷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 企求 <span class="encs">[desire to gain;seek for]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:窥欲(非分希求);窥寻(仔细寻求;探索);窥算(暗算);窥隙(寻找疏漏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 观看 <span class="encs">[see;look]</span></p>
