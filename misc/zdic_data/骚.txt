                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骚</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>骚</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">騷</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>动乱，扰乱，不安定：～乱。～扰。～动。</li>
	<li>忧愁：&ldquo;离～者，犹离忧也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>指中国屈原的《离骚》，后泛指诗文：～体。～人。～客。风～（❶指《诗经》和《离骚》，代指古代诗歌或文化；❷指妇女举止轻佻）。</li>
	<li>举止轻佻，作风下流：～货。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/臊">臊</a>&rdquo;(sāo ㄙㄠ)。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>骚</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">騷</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古通&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">扫</span>&rdquo;，尽其所有，扫数出动：&ldquo;大王宜～淮南之兵。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>骚</strong></p>
<p>騷 <span class="dicpy">sāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，蚤声。本义:刷马)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[brush horse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骚，摩马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;人曰搔，马曰骚，其意一也。摩马，如今人之刷马。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 骚动;动乱<span class="encs">[tumult;disturb;upset]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骚，扰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，谓马扰动也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骚，动也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徐方绎骚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;常武》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">南阳</span>骚动。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;李通传》</span>。注:&ldquo;骚，亦动也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">九年而王室始骚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;郑语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骚害(骚扰，伤害);骚骚(急迫的样子);骚杀(下垂飘动的样子);骚边(骚扰边境)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;搔&rdquo;。抓，挠，以指甲轻刮 <span class="encs">[scratch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其人坚劲，寡有疥骚，终无瘠酲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;地员》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">花县征徭，衡门贫困，两事不堪骚首。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王錂</span><span class="smcs">《寻亲记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 跛行 <span class="encs">[limp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">吴</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>偏蹇曰骚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 忧愁 <span class="encs">[worry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骚，愁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚&middot;序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故忧愁幽思而作<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span>。索隐引应劭:&ldquo;骚，忧也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">作离骚赋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span>。师古曰:&ldquo;忧动曰骚。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小弁之诗作，离骚之辞兴。&mdash;&mdash;《史记&middot;冯奉世传赞</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 传:&ldquo;骚，忧也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德义不行，则迩者骚离，而远者距远。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骚吾心，则固僵仆烦愦愈不可过矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《答韦中立论师道书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骚离(忧愁而离心)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“骚”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/騷">騷</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上" target="_blank">亥集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上&amp;bs=馬" target="_blank">馬</a>】
                              騷·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9A37.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】蘇遭切【韻會】【正韻】蘇曹切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音搔。【玉篇】動也。【說文】擾也。【詩&middot;大雅】徐方驛騷。【註】騷，擾動也。</p>
<p>又【說文】一曰摩馬。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】騷，蹇也。吳楚偏蹇曰騷。【註】蹇，跛行也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】愁也。【正字通】屈原作離騷，言遭憂也。今謂詩人爲騷人。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;檀弓】喪事雖遽，不陵節，騷騷爾則野。【註】騷騷，急疾貌。</p>
<p>又騷殺，飄揚下垂貌。【張衡&middot;東京賦】駙承華之蒲梢，飛流蘇之騷殺。【註】流蘇，五采毛雜之，以爲馬飾，凡下垂者爲蘇。</p>
<p>又騷屑，凄凉也。【謝靈運詩】騷屑出穴風。【杜甫詩】平人固騷屑。【王安石詩】歸夢得蕭騷。【歐陽修詩】休把靑銅照雙鬢，君謨今已白刀騷。</p>
<p>又蒲騷，地名。【左傳&middot;桓十一年】敗鄖師于蒲騷。【註】卽陽臺巫山，在今峽川。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>先彫切，音簫。義同。</p>
<p>又【正韻】蘇老切，音埽。【史記&middot;李斯傳】竈上騷除。</p>
<p>又【黥布傳】大王宜騷淮南之兵。【註】言盡舉之如埽地也。與埽通。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>先到切，音燥。義同。</p>
<p>又古通尤韻。【張衡&middot;思<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>賦】行積冰之皚皚兮，淸泉沍而不流。寒風凄其永至兮，拂雲岫之騷騷。【文選註】音修。騷騷，風動貌。</p>
