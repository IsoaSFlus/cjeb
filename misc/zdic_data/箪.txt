                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>箪</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">簞</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄢˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古代盛饭的圆竹器：～食（<span class="dicpy">sì</span>）壶浆（形容劳军）。～食（<span class="dicpy">sì</span>）瓢饮（喻生活贫苦；亦指安贫乐道）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> small bamboo basket for holding</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> corbeille,panier rond pour le riz cuit</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>箪</strong></p><p>簞 <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，单声。本义:古代盛饭的圆形竹器)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[a bamboo utensil for holding cooked rice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">箪，笥也。从竹，单声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">箪，小筐也。——<span class="smcs">《汉律令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苞苴箪笥。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">栉实于箪。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">箪食与肉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一箪食，一豆羹，得之则生，弗得则死。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:箪笥(盛饭竹器);箪瓢(箪:食器;瓢:饮器。比喻家境贫寒，生活清苦)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 盛物件的小筐 <span class="encs">[small basket]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与之一箪珠，使问</span><span class="diczx2">赵孟</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公二十年》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/箪食">箪食</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/箪食壶浆">箪食壶浆</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/箪食瓢饮">箪食瓢饮</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上" target="_blank">未集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上&amp;bs=竹" target="_blank">竹</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7C1E.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】都寒切【集韻】【韻會】多寒切【正韻】多艱切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音單。【說文】笥也。【漢律令】簞，小筐也。【篇海】竹葦器。鄭康成曰：盛飯者，圓曰簞，方曰笥。【儀禮·士冠禮】櫛實于簞。【論語】一簞食。【註】簞，笥也。【廣韻】簞、笥，小篋。</p><p>又竹名。【嵆含·草木狀】簞竹，葉疎而大，一節相去五六尺。</p><p>又瓢亦曰簞。【揚子·方言】瓥，陳楚宋魏之閒或謂之簞，或謂之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237F5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣟵</span>，或謂之瓢。晉嵇含《南方草木狀》。</p>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7C1E.svg">                          <p>笥也。从竹單聲。漢津令：簞，小筐也。《傳》曰：“簞食壺漿。”都寒切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">笥也。</span>論語孔注同。皇侃曰。以竹爲之。如箱篋之屬。左傳。夫差以一簞珠問趙孟。葢亦簞之小者也。<span class="jieshi">从竹。單聲。</span>都寒切。十四部。<span class="jieshi">漢律令。簞、小匡也。</span>匡俗作筐。匚部曰。匡、飯器、筥也。按筥者、？也。容五<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>。漢律令之簞、謂匡之小者也。與經傳所云簞謂笥者異。葢匡簞皆可盛飯。而匡筥無葢。簞笥有葢。如今之箱盒。其制不同。故小匡爲別一義。<span class="jieshi">傳曰。簞食壷槳。</span>孟子及他儒家書皆有此言。故約之以傳曰也。此證前一義。                          </div>
