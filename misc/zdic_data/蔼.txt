						<p>● <strong>蔼</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">藹</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ǎi.mp3"></a></span> ㄞˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>和气，和善：和～。～然。</li>
	<li>果实、树木繁茂的样子：幽～。～～。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/霭">霭</a>&rdquo;，云气。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> lush; affable, friendly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> freundlich  (Adv)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> amicalement</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蔼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蔼</strong> <span class="dicpy">ǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>藹</p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 树木生长繁茂 <span class="encs">[luxuriant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔼蔼，盛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔼，茂盛貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">离芳蔼之方壮兮，余萎约而悲愁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;宋玉&middot;九辩》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蔼彩(芳鲜茂盛的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 盛多的样子 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔼蔼、济济，止也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔼蔼王多吉士，维君子使，媚于天子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;卷阿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">才夫蔼蔼而曼着兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;逢纷》</span>。注:&ldquo;盛多貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">郁萧条其幽蔼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《河东赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 和气，态度好 <span class="encs">[gentle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笑语蔼然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;王大》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:和蔼(态度温和，容易接近)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蔼</strong></p>
<p>藹 <span class="dicpy">ǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>云气。通&ldquo;霭&rdquo; <span class="encs">[mist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悲风徽行轨，倾云结流蔼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;陆机&middot;挽歌》</span></span></p>

<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋訓】藹藹。【註】賢士盛多之容止。【詩&middot;大雅】藹藹王多吉士。【傳】藹藹，濟濟也。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】齊南海太守藹燠。</p>
<p>又【韻會】倚亥切，音靉。草叢雜貌。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶音翳。【揚雄&middot;羽獵賦】車騎雲會，登降闇藹。泰華爲旒，熊耳爲綴。</p>
