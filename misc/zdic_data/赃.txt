                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赃</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>赃</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">贜</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄤ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>贪污受贿或偷盗所得的财物：～物。～款。～证。追～。退～。人～俱在。分～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> booty, loot, stolen goods; bribe</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Beute, Diebesbeute, Diebesgut, Diebeswaren  (S, Rechtsw)​,gestohlene Ware  (S, Rechtsw)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> objets volés,butin</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赃</strong></p>
<p>贜 <span class="dicpy">zāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。左形，右声。本义:赃物) 同本义 <span class="encs">[stolen goods;booty;swag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以赃获罪，没其先居之财。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;天瑞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赃仗(赃证);赃派(栽赃诬陷);栽赃(坐赃。把赃物或违禁物暗放在别人处，诬告他犯法);窝赃;追赃;分赃;退赃;销赃;赃品(赃物);赃银(赃款)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赃</strong></p>
<p>贜 <span class="dicpy">zāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 贪污;行贿 <span class="encs">[bribe;seduce;accept bribes]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纳贿曰赃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">南阳</span>太守 <span class="diczx2">韩昭</span>坐赃，下狱死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;质帝纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所在虏掠，赃各千万计，并斩之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《魏书&middot;世祖纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赃秽(贪赃纳贿);赃罪(贪污受贿罪);赃贿(贪赃纳贿)</span></p>
