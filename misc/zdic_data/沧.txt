                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">沧</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>沧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">滄</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄤ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>暗绿色（指水）：～海。～浪。～海遗珠（喻被埋没的人才）。～海桑田。～海一粟。</li>
	<li>寒，冷：&ldquo;日初出，～～凉凉，及其日中，如探汤&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> blue, dark green; cold</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> dunkelblau  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bleu foncé,vert azuré,glauque</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">沧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>沧</strong></p>
<p>滄 <span class="dicpy">cāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，仓声。本义:寒冷)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;凔&rdquo;。寒冷 <span class="encs">[cold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沧，寒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天地之间有沧热。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;周祝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾养滄热，滑铍轻重以形体异。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲汤之滄，一人炊之，百人扬之，无益也，不如绝薪止火而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;枚乘传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日初出，沧沧凉凉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:沧凉(寒凉;寒冷);沧热(寒冷与炎热)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;苍&rdquo;。水深绿色 <span class="encs">[dark green]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:沧流(泛指水流。因江水呈青苍色，故名);沧浪(水色青碧;又指水名，在湖北省境内)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>沧</strong></p>
<p>滄 <span class="dicpy">Cāng</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>州名 <span class="encs">[Cang prefecture]</span>。北朝东魏置。治所在今河北省盐山县与山东省乐陵县之间。唐至元因之，治所向北移至今河北省沧州市</p>
