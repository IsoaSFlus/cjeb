                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谏</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">諫</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 旧时称规劝君主或尊长，使改正错误：～劝。进～。～阻。兵～。从～如流。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> remonstrate, admonish</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ermahnen, abmahnen ,Jian  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faire des remontrances</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谏</strong></p><p>諫 <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，柬声。本义:直言规劝)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[admonish;remonstrate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谏，证也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谏者，正也。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·七谏序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谏，犹正也。以道正人行。——周礼·司谏》注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌谏五恶。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·保氏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“以礼义正之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三谏不从。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·庄公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宫之奇</span>之为人也，懦而不能强谏。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">保氏</span>常谏王恶。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·地官》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大臣强谏。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">扶苏</span>以数谏。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忠谏之路。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不为拒谏。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:谏阻纳质(指周瑜直言规劝阻止孙权以其子给曹操为人质);谏鼓(朝廷设置供人民进谏时击用的鼓。又名<span class="yhcs">“朝鼓”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 匡正;挽回 <span class="encs">[rectify;correct;retrieve;redeem]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悟已往之不谏，知来者之可追。实迷途其未远，觉今是而昨非。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“间”</span>。离间 <span class="encs">[sow seeds of discord]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郭开</span>受谏， <span class="diczx2">李牧</span>就诛。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《忧赋》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谏</strong></p><p>諫 <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 谏官<span class="encs">[imperial censors]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">避人焚谏草，骑马欲鸡栖。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《晚出左掖》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:谏垣(谏官的衙署;指朝中大臣及幕僚百官);谏草(谏官的奏稿);谏院(谏官的衙署);谏议大夫(古官名。简称<span class="yhcs">“谏议”</span>。掌管谏诤及议论)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“间”</span>。间谍 <span class="encs">[spy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设谏以纲独为，举错以观奸动。——<span 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              諫·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AEB.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/279C5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧧅</span>【唐韻】古晏切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居晏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音㵎。【說文】証也。【徐曰】閒也。君所謂否，臣獻其可，以閒隔之，於文言柬爲諫。柬者，多別善惡，以<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>於君。【白虎通】閒也，因也，更也。是非相閒，革更其行也。【詩·衞風·淇奧序疏】干也。干君之意而告之。【廣雅】正也。【廣韻】諫諍，直言以悟人也。【書·說命】后從諫則聖。【禮·曲禮】爲人臣之禮，不顯諫，三諫而不聽，則逃之。子之事親也，三諫而不聽，則號泣隨之。【前漢·賈山傳】工誦箴諫，瞽誦詩諫，公卿比諫，士傳言諫過。【舊唐書·職官志】凡諫有五：一曰諷諫，二曰順諫，三曰規諫，四曰致諫，五曰直諫。</p><p>又官名。【周禮·地官】有司諫。【註】諫猶正也，以道正人行。【後漢·百官志註】胡廣曰：武帝元狩五年，置諫大夫爲光祿大夫。世祖中興，以爲諫議大夫。</p><p>又鳥名。【說苑·辨物篇】東方有鳥，名諫珂，文身而朱足，憎鳥而愛狐。</p><p>又果名。【本草綱目】橄欖，一名諫果，出【農書】。</p><p>又姓。【風俗通】漢有治書侍史諫忠。</p><p>又【集韻】居顏切，㵎平聲。【類篇】諍也。</p><p>又【集韻】郞旰切，音爛。與讕同。</p><p>又叶古限切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>。【詩·大雅】王欲玉女，是用大諫。叶上反。</p><p>又叶經<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/290D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩃓</span>切，音見。【卻正·釋譏】譬逌人之有采於市閭，游童之吟詠乎疆畔，庶以增廣福祥，輸力規諫。【註】畔音弁。　【俗書證誤】从東，非。諌dǒng，多言也。。</p>
