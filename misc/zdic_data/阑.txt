                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>阑</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">闌</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lán.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>同“<span class="diczx3">栏</span>”。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">拦</span>”。</li><li>〔～干〕ａ．同“栏杆”；ｂ．纵横交错，参差错落，如“梦啼妆泪红～～”。</li><li>〔～入〕进入不应进去的地方，混进，如“无票不得～～。”</li><li>残，尽，晚：夜～人静。～珊。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> door screen; railing fence</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Geländer, Reling, Balustrade  (S, Arch)​,zu Ende gehen, sich dem Ende neigen  (V)​,erschöpft, abgehetzt  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> toucher à sa fin,rampe,balustrade,exténué</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>阑</strong></p><p>闌 <span class="dicpy">lán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉(形声。从门，柬( <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span>)声。本义:门前的栅栏)</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义。引申为栏杆 <span class="encs">[railing;balustrade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阑，门遮也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽仪之所甚愿为门阑之厮者，亦无先大王。——<span class="smcs">《史记·楚世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今公之牛马老于阑牢，不胜服也。——<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋·内篇谏下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">门阑多喜色，女婿近乘龙。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《李监宅》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怒发冲冠，凭阑处，潇潇雨歇。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">岳飞</span><span class="smcs">《满江红》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:阑槛(栏杆);阑楯(栏杆);阑牢(围着栅栏用来饲养牛马的地方);阑圈(家畜饲养圈)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 环状物 <span class="encs">[loop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你看月阑，明日敢有风也。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">王实甫</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 印刷页纸上的分行线条 <span class="encs">[line]</span>。如:阑道(书箱笺纸四周的框线)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 画的圈儿 <span class="encs">[circle;ring]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">张千</span>，取石灰来，在阶下画个阑儿。—— <span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">李行道</span><span class="smcs">《灰阑记》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>阑</strong></p><p>闌 <span class="dicpy">lán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 残;将尽 <span class="encs">[late;near the end]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白露凝兮岁将阑。——<span class="smcs">《宋孝武宣贵妃诛》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夜阑卧听风吹雨。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《十一月四日风雨大作》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">林黛玉</span>到四更将阑，方渐渐的睡去。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:阑暑(指暑气将尽的夏末);阑单(精疲力尽的样子);岁阑;夜阑人静;阑夕(夜晚)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“拦”</span>。阻隔 <span class="encs">[block]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有河山以阑之，有周</span><span class="diczx2">韩</span>而间之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策三》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 围 <span class="encs">[surround;enclose]</span>。如:阑腰(围腰);阑地(围地，圈地)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 衰退;衰落;消沉 <span class="encs">[fail;decline;down hearted]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白发满头归得也，诗情酒兴渐阑珊。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              闌·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95CC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】洛干切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】郞干切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蘭。【說文】門遮也。【戰國策】晉國之去梁也，千里有餘，有河山以闌之。【史記·楚世家】雖儀之所甚願爲門闌之厮者，亦無先大王。【後漢·明帝紀】勞賜縣掾史及門闌走卒。【註】續漢志曰：五伯鈴下待閣門闌部署街里。</p><p>又車上闌。【左傳·宣十二年·楚人惎之脫扃註】扃，車上兵闌也。</p><p>又【廣韻】晚也。【岑參詩】蕭條芳歲闌。</p><p>又【增韻】褪也，衰也。</p><p>又【玉篇】牢也。</p><p>又【廣韻】希也。飮酒半罷也。【史記·高祖紀】酒闌。【註】闌，言希也。謂飮酒者，半罷半在，謂之闌。【杜甫詩】廚人語夜闌。</p><p>又【廣韻】盡也。【蔡琰·胡笳十八拍】更深夜闌兮夢汝來斯。</p><p>又妄也。【史記·汲黯傳】文吏繩，以爲闌出財物於邊關乎。【註】闌，妄也。</p><p>又無符傳出入爲闌。【史記·高祖功臣年表】陽平侯杜相夫闌出函谷關。【前漢·成帝紀】闌入尚方掖門。【註】應劭曰：無符籍妄入宮曰闌。</p><p>又腕闌。【元氏掖庭記】元靜懿皇后旦日，人獻翠腕闌。【註】闌，手鐲類。</p><p>又闌干。【左思·吳都賦】珠琲闌干。【註】闌干，猶縱橫也。</p><p>又縣名。【前漢·地理志·闌註】屬越巂郡。</p><p>又【集韻】郞旰切。同<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2249E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢒞</span>。文也。或作斕。亦省。</p><p>又【韻補】叶陵延切，音連。【蘇軾·遊東西巖詩】况復情所鍾，感槩萃中年。正賴絲與竹，陶寫有餘歡。常恐兒輩覺，坐令高趣闌。歡音軒。</p><p>
