                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>翼</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>翅膀：～翅。比～双飞。卵～。羽～。～护。～蔽。</li><li>左右两侧中的一侧：左～。侧～。</li><li>帮助，辅佐：～助。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">翌</span>”，明天，明年。</li><li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li><li>〔～～〕a.谨慎，如“小心～～”；b.严整有秩序；c.繁盛，众多。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wings; fins on fish; shelter</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Fittich  (S)​,Flanke  (S)​,Flügel  (S)​,Tragfläche  (S)​,einem Herrscher helfen, beistehen  (V, Lit)​,Yi  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> aile</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">翼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>翼</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从羽，異( <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> )声。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>小篆从飞，異声。本义:翅膀)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翼，翅也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六足四翼。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·楚策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三年不翅，将以长羽翼。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南方有比翼鸟焉。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">振鳞奋翼。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋玉</span><span class="smcs">《高唐赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:翼虎(添上翅膀的老虎);翼张(如鸟展翅。形容分布的样子);翼膀(翅膀);翼翮(翅膀)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鸟的翅膀</p><p><span class="diczx1">明夷于飞，垂其翼。——<span class="smcs">《易·明夷》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 飞机的两翼。如:翼型</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 昆虫的翅膀</p><p><span class="diczx1">以翼鸣者。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 一个队形(如战线、散兵线、行进纵队)左侧或右侧 <span class="encs">[flank]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">韩</span>、 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>翼而击之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:从左右两翼夹攻敌人;翼室(路寝旁的左右室。路寝为天子或诸侯的正室);翼卫(辅翼护卫)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 星名 <span class="encs">[star's name]</span>，二十八宿之一，南方朱雀七宿中的第六宿，有星二十二颗。如:翼宿(星宿名。二十八宿之一);翼轸(二十八宿中的翼宿和轸宿)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 量词。用于禽鸟。如:双翼</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>翼</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 分成两翼或分列左右 <span class="encs">[divide into two wings]</span>。如:翼列(分列两旁);翼夹(如两翼相夹)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 辅助 <span class="encs">[render assistance to a ruler]</span></p><p><span
