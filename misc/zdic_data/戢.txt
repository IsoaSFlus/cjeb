						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>戢</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>收敛，收藏：～翼。～影。载～干戈（把兵器收藏起来）。</li><li>止，停止：～怒。～鳞（喻蓄志待时）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>戢</strong> <span class="dicpy">jí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 收藏<span class="encs">[兵器]</span> <span class="encs">[store up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">载戢干戈。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·时迈》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫兵戢而时动。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫兵戢而时动，动则威。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:戢刃(收起刀剑等利器);戢戢(聚集);戢载(收集装载);戢藏(收藏)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申指停止战争 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弗戢，将自焚。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:戢戈(息兵);戢兵(停用兵器，禁用武力);戢武(息兵)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 收敛 <span class="encs">[converge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戢，敛也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戢其左翼。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·鸳鸯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不戢不难。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·桑扈》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翼翼归鸟，戢羽寒条。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归鸟》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:戢鳞(敛鳞。鱼止息不游。比喻怀志坐待时机);戢羽(敛翅止飞);戢身(敛迹)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 隐居 <span class="encs">[live in seclusion]</span>。如:戢伏(隐居);戢景(戢影。藏匿行踪。比喻退休隐居);戢迹(匿迹)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 约束 <span class="encs">[restrain]</span>。如:戢军(约束士兵);戢制(制止);戢手(束手);戢畏(犹畏服);戢御(约束统治)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/戢翼">戢翼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/戢影">戢影</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
