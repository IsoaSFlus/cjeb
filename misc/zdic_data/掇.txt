						<p>● <strong>掇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">duō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄛˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>拾取；摘取：～拾。～弄。</li>
	<li>用双手拿（椅子，凳子），用手端。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> collect, gather up, pick up</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> recueillir,rassembler,piller,arranger</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">掇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>掇</strong> <span class="dicpy">duō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:拾取)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pick up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掇，拾取也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">患至掇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;讼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">承蜩犹掇之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;达生》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">览取挢掇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;要略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">成</span>益惊喜，掇置笼中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:掇撷(采摘，拾取);掇桂(折桂);掇蜂(喻离间骨肉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 挪;搬取 <span class="encs">[carry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁边只有一块大石头，掇将过来告了门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:掇盆;掇床</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 选取 <span class="encs">[select]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">但二月草已芽，八月苗未枯，采掇者易辨识耳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;采草药》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡所著述五十八篇，掇其切于世事者著于传云。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:掇采(选取，搜集);掇录(选录)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 得到 <span class="encs">[get]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:掇青拾紫(获得高官厚禄);掇醍(受教而觉悟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 端 <span class="encs">[take]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且向店里掇条凳子，坐了两个时辰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:掇臀捧屁(掇:端、捧之意。形容诌媚者的丑态)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 考取 <span class="encs">[pass an entrance examination]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:掇芹(考取秀才)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 哄骗 <span class="encs">[cheat]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:掇赚(哄骗;诱诓);掇骗(哄骗);掇送(劝诱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 掉转 <span class="encs">[turn round]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:掇转(掉转;扭转)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">duo</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              掇·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6387.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>都括切，音剟。讀若朵入聲。【說文】拾取也。【增韻】採也。【易&middot;訟卦】自下訟上，患至掇也。【疏】若手拾掇物然。【詩&middot;周南】采采芣苢，薄言掇之。【史記&middot;張儀傳】秦得燒掇焚杅君之國。【註】索隱曰：掇音都活反，謂焚燒而侵掠也。焚杅音煩烏，謂焚蹂而牽掣也。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】陟劣切【集韻】株劣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音輟。義同。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶昌悅切，音啜。【魏武帝&middot;短歌行】明明如月，何時可掇。憂從中來，不可斷絕。</p>
<p>又【集韻】朱劣切，音拙。與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B74.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠭴</span>同。短也。</p>
<p>又【史記別註】楊愼曰：掇，馬箠也。杅，糞箕也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>存備考。</p>
