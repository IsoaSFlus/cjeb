                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谩</strong></p><p>謾 <span class="dicpy">mán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，曼声。本义:瞒哄;欺骗)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[deceive;hoodwink]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谩，欺也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虔儇，慧也。</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>谓之谩。——<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">偝则谩之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·非相》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谩，欺毁也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是面谩也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·匈奴传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:谩欺(欺骗，诈骗);谩语(谎言，说谎);谩上不谩下(能欺骗上级，但不能欺骗众人);谩辞(欺诳的言辞);谩言(讲假话)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抵赖 <span class="encs">[refuse to admit;deny]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民或祝诅上，以相约结而后相谩，吏以为大逆。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孝文本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">màn</span></p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谩</strong></p><p>謾 <span class="dicpy">màn</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 毁谤;谩骂 <span class="encs">[slander;calumniate;hurl invectives;fling abuses]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乡则不若，偝则谩之，是人之二必穷也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·非相》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">媠谩亡状。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·两龚传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 莫，不要 <span class="encs">[don’t]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谩叹息，谩悒怏。——</span><span class="diczx2">金</span>· <span class="diczx2">董解元</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记诸宫调》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:谩言(休说);谩道(休说，别说);谩且(休得，莫)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 轻慢，对人不尊重 <span class="encs">[disrespectful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻谩宰相。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·翟方进传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故</span><span class="diczx2">桀</span><span class="diczx2">纣</span>暴谩。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·董仲舒传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长书有誖谩。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·外戚传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:谩君(轻慢、失礼之君);谩书(态度轻慢的文书)</p><p>〈形〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“漫”</span>。不切实，散漫 <span class="encs">[free;casual]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太谩，愿闻其要。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天道》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/谩骂">谩骂</a></span></div>                    <div                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8B3E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】母官切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】謨官切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音瞞。【說文】欺也。【史記·秦始皇紀】下懾伏謾，欺以取容。【晉書·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】違忠欺上謂之謾。</p><p>又【廣雅】緩也。【廣韻】慢也。【前漢·兩龔傳】媠謾亡狀。</p><p>又欺毀也。【荀子·非相篇】鄕則不若，背則謾之。</p><p>又媟汙也。【前漢·孝成皇后傳】淳于長書有誖謾。</p><p>又汗漫也。【莊子·天道篇】繙十二經，以說老耼，中其說曰大謾，願聞其要。</p><p>又【玉篇】莫般切【廣韻】莫還切【集韻】謨還切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蠻。【揚子·方言】謾台，懼也。燕代之閒曰謾台。</p><p>又虔儇，慧也，秦謂之謾。</p><p>又【廣韻】武延切【集韻】【韻會】彌延切【正韻】莫堅切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音緜。【類篇】慧黠也。一曰欺也。【前漢·灌夫傳】謾好謝蚡。【師古註】謾，猶詭也。詐爲好言也。【集韻】或作䛲。</p><p>又【玉篇】馬諫切【集韻】【正韻】莫晏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音慢。【前漢·宣帝紀】務爲欺謾，以避其課。【師古註】謾，誑言也。音慢。</p><p>又與慢同。【周禮·秋官·禁暴氏鄭註】民之好爲侵陵。稱詐，謾，誕。此三者，亦<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>所禁也。謾，本或作慢。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>莫半切，音縵。【類篇】欺語也。</p><p>又【增韻】且也。通作漫。　【字彙補】一作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27B12.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧬒</span>。</p>
