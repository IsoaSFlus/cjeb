                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拂</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/拂士">拂士</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拂</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手。弗声。本义:拂拭;掸除)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[whisk;flick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拂，过击也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，随击随过，犹拍也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拂，去也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">授弓拂弓。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射仪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“去尘也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">和弓击摩。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·弓人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“拂之摩之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">商祝拂柩。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·既文礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主人拂几。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以手拂之，其印自落。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:拂掉(拂杓。拂拭;扫除);拂帚(拂尘);拂子(拂尘);拂须(揩拭胡须);拂去桌上的尘土</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 击 <span class="encs">[strike at glancing angle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拂颐，贞凶。——<span class="smcs">《易·颐》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">神武</span>据鞍未动， <span class="diczx2">金</span>以鞭拂马， <span class="diczx2">神武</span>乃还。——<span class="smcs">《北史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 违背;逆 <span class="encs">[disobey]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行拂乱其所为，所以动心忍性，曾益其所不能。——<span
