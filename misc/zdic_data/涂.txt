                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>涂</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">➊-➏<span class="diczx3">塗</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">t&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>使颜色或油漆等附着在上面：～饰。～漆。～抹。</li>	<li>乱写：～鸦。</li>	<li>用笔抹上或抹去：～改。～窜。～乙。</li>	<li>泥泞：～炭。</li>	<li>河流或海流夹杂的泥沙在地势较平的河流入海处或海岸附近沉积而成的浅海滩：海～。滩～。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/途">途</a>&rdquo;。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涂</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，余声。本义:涂水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Tu River]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古水名。即今云南省之牛栏江。发源于寻甸，北流至威宁折向西北，至鲁甸县注入金沙江</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">涂</span>，水。出 <span class="diczx2">益州</span><span class="diczx2">牧靡</span><span class="diczx2">南山</span>，西北入 <span class="diczx2">渑</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 洞涡水支流。在山西省榆次县境</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 道路。也作&ldquo;途&rdquo; <span class="encs">[road]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出于玉之涂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乡也混然涂之人也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涂巷之人也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故辟门除涂，以迎吾人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王霸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涂次(路上;途中);涂夫(路人);涂巷(道路;街坊);涂轨(本指车行之道。引申谓路子，途径);涂数(途程);涂迹(轨迹，途径);涂辙(车轮的痕迹);涂道(路途)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 十二月。古月名 <span class="encs">[December]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十二月为涂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> &ldquo;塗&rdquo;的简化字</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涂</strong></p>
<p>塗 <span class="dicpy">t&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 泥，泥巴 <span class="encs">[mud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塗，泥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见豕负涂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;睽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥土惟塗泥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如塗塗附。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;角弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冻塗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;夏小正》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐于塗炭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涂车(用泥涂制成而饰以彩色的车子。为古时送葬的明器);涂炭(泥淖和炭灰。喻污浊之地。亦谓污浊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 道路 <span class="encs">[road]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道听塗说。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">长塗中宿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下何思何虑?天下同归而殊涂，一致而百虑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">立之涂，匠者不顾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明乎坦涂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七圣皆迷，无所问涂。适遇牧马童子问涂焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;徐无鬼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 此义又作&ldquo;途&rdquo;。又如:涂不拾遗(道不拾遗);涂说(道路传闻之言);涂次(途中停留);涂陌(道路);涂径(道路;路径);涂路(犹道路);涂殚(路走到尽头)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申指途径;门路 <span class="encs">[way]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国乱者，民多私义;兵弱者，民多私勇。则削国之所以取爵禄者多涂，亡国之所以贱爵轻禄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 涂抹;粉刷物品 <span class="encs">[spread on;apply;smear]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 乱写乱画 <span class="encs">[scribble]</span>。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6D82_3355.svg" /><span class="swbf">(涂)</span><span class="ywyy">涂水。出益州牧靡南山，西北入繩。</span><span class="swdz">繩，各本譌作澠。今正。牧前志作收，後志作牧。華陽國志：竟作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦫵</span>。李奇曰：靡音麻。收靡卽<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦫵</span>麻。常璩曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦫵</span>麻縣山出好<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦫵</span>麻。收<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦫵</span>牧三字皆同紐。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23729.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣜩</span>釋益州太守碑牧靡字三見。晉書亦作牧矣。益州郡牧靡，二志同。前志曰：南山臘，涂水所出。西北至越巂入繩，過郡二，行千二十里。水經注若水篇曰：若水，又東，涂水注之。水出建寧郡之收靡縣南山。縣山竝卽草以立名。山在縣東烏句山南五百里，山生收靡，可以解毒。涂水導源臘谷。西北流至越巂入若水。按涂水出臘谷，故漢志謂之臘。涂水，漢志、說文皆云入繩。而水經注云入若水者，善長云若水，又逕越巂大莋縣入繩。繩水出徼外，南逕旄牛道，至大莋與若水合。自下亦通謂之繩水矣。諸書錄記羣水，或言入若，又言注繩，正是異水沿注，通爲一津，隨納通稱也。水道提綱曰：金沙江卽古繩水。鴉龍江一名打沖河，卽古若水。金沙江出番地，至雲南姚安府大姚縣境合鴉龍江，至四川敘州府治宜賓縣西南境入於江。金沙自犛石山發源，至雲南麗江府境已四千二百餘里，自麗江至四川敘州府又二千五百餘里，源遠流長。所受大水數十，小水無數。其爲大江上源無疑也。玉裁謂多以金沙爲大江正源，然非禹貢崏山道江之旨。禹貢於河源江源皆舉其近者，聖人不尙遠略之意。牧靡今何縣，涂水今何水，未審。</span><span class="ywyy">从水余聲。</span><span class="swdz">同都切。五部。按古道塗，塗墍字皆作涂。</span></p>                          </div>
