                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>双</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">雙</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shuāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄨㄤˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>两个，一对：一～鞋。～杠。～重（<span class="dicpy">chǒng </span>）。～方。～管齐下。～豆塞聪（耳被堵塞，一无所闻）。～瞳剪水（形容眼珠的清澈）。智勇～全。盖世无～。</li>	<li>偶，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">单</span>&rdquo;相对：～数。～号。</li>	<li>加倍的：～料。～份。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">双</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>双</strong></p>
<p>雙 <span class="dicpy">shuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从雔，从又，持之。雔( <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span>)，两只鸟。又，手。本义:一对)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 禽鸟二只 <span class="encs">[two birds]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雙，隹二枚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">飞鸟曰双。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言六》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其诸为其双双而俱至者与。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;宣公五年》</span>。正义:&ldquo;双双之鸟，一身二首尾，有雌雄，随便而偶，常不离散。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中有双飞鸟，自名为鸳鸯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 云南等地少数民族计算田亩的单位 <span class="encs">[acre]</span>。田广二亩、四亩、五亩都可称双，各从方俗;无定制</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">官为田，四十双为二百亩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>双</strong></p>
<p>雙 <span class="dicpy">shuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 成双的，一对 <span class="encs">[two;both;double;twin;dual]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云有第三郎，窈窕世无双。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厅旁一室，为吾与汝双栖之所。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:双人舞;举世无双;双红(双红帖。比单帖大一倍，从中折合，用红纸，故名);双林(即沙罗双树，佛祖示寂(死亡)的地方);双阙(古代宫殿、宗庙与陵墓前左右各一个的高台楼观);双套飞车(两头牲口拉的快车);双骞(双飞);双七(指农历七月七日);双丸(指日和月);双角(两角);双杵(古人捣衣，对立执杵如舂米，故名双杵);双枚(屋内的重檐);双关儿透(很透彻);双栖(指禽鸟雌雄同栖息。借喻夫妻形影不离);双靥(脸的两颊);双向沟通(指让意见沟通的双方均能有公平的机会，坦然地表达讯息的意义)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 偶，与&ldquo;单&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[even]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唐朝故事，只日视事，双日不坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史&middot;礼志》</span></span></p>
