                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>愕</strong> <span class="dicpy">è</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/è.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，咢声。本义:惊讶)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[startled]</span>愕，惊也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂一》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愕，然欲殴之。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·张良传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹愕眙而不能阶。——</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《西都赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">成</span>骇立愕呼。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx2">成</span>益愕，急逐趁之，蟆入草间。</p><p><span class="diczx1">先生且喜且愕，舍狼而前。——</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:愕怪(惊奇);愕窒(惊惶得不敢出气);愕梦(惊愕而梦)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 直言 <span class="encs">[blow off]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愕愕者不以天下为忧。——<span class="smcs">《管子·白心》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">万里之朝，日闻唯唯，而后闻诸生之愕愕，此乃公卿之良药针石。——<span class="smcs">《盐铁论·国疾》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">謇愕之操，华首弥固。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·陈蕃传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:愕愕(直言)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/愕然">愕然</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/愕视">愕视</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/愕异">愕异</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
