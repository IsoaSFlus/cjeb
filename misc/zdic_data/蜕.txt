						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蜕</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tuì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>蝉或蛇等脱下来的皮：蛇～。蝉～。</li>    <li>蛇、蝉等动物脱皮：～皮。</li>    <li>鸟换毛。</li>    <li>解脱，变化：～化。～变。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蜕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蜕</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，兑( <span class="dicpy">duì</span>)声。本义:蝉、蛇之类脱皮去壳)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 蝉、蛇等脱皮。亦谓鸟换羽<span class="encs">[slough off;exuviate;moult]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蜕，蝉蛇所解皮也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蝉蜕于浊秽。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蜕蝉(蝉脱壳)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 道家认为修道者死后留下形骸，魂魄散去成仙，称为尸解，也叫<span class="yhcs">“蜕”</span>。后因以蜕为死的讳称 <span class="encs">[die]</span>。如:蜕骨(脱去凡骨。魂升天后的骸骨。多用于道教徒);蜕化(道教谓人死亡解脱成仙);蜕衣(谓肉体仙去留下衣服);蜕壳(指遗骸)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 变化;变质<span class="encs">[degenerate]</span>。如:蜕演(蜕化演变);蜕形(蜕化而成的形式);蜕嬗(蜕变演化)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 脱去，除掉<span class="encs">[get rid of]</span>。如:蜕濯(清除，洗涤)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>蜕</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蛇、蝉等动物从身上脱落下来的壳或皮 <span class="encs">[exuviae]</span>。如:蝉蜕;蛇蜕;蚕蜕</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 尸体 <span class="encs">[corpse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">师素不立文字，临去乃为此，与遗蜕俱存。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 动物的化石 <span class="encs">[fossil]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于</span><span class="diczx2">淮</span>、 <span class="diczx2">济</span>间得一龙蜕，长数十尺。——<span class="smcs">《五杂俎·卷九》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
