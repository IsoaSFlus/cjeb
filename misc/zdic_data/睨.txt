                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>睨</strong> <span class="dicpy">nì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从目，兒( <span class="dicpy">ní</span>)声。本义:斜视)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[look askance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">睨，视也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余与褐之父睨之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">睨而视之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽羿逢蒙不能眄睨也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·山水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">相如</span>持其璧睨柱。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卖油翁释担而立，睨之，久而不去。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:睨注(斜着眼睛注视);睨笑(斜视而笑);睨视(斜视，旁视;傲视)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 视 <span class="encs">[see]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旨酒一盛兮，余与褐之父睨之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陟升皇之赫戏兮，忽临睨夫旧乡。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">玉逸</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“睨，视也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 顾视，回视 <span class="encs">[look around]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 寻视 <span class="encs">[look for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虑也者，以其知有求也，而不必得之，若睨。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 窥伺 <span class="encs">[peep at]</span>。如:睨望(窥望)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 偏斜 <span class="encs">[incline to one side;slant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日方中方睨，物方生方死。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
