                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>接</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，妾声。本义:两手交叉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 交合;会合 <span class="encs">[join;assemble;converge;bring together;meet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">接，交也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">接，合也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昼日三接。——<span class="smcs">《易·晋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两君偃兵接好。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“合也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵刃既接。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">车鉷毂兮短兵接。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九歌·国殇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵不接刃。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·怀宠》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敌皆以走为利，则刃无与接。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·爱士》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 迎接 <span class="encs">[meet face to face;move towards]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">笙箫细乐，却安排接驾。——<span class="smcs">《宣和遗事》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:接场(迎接从科举考场回来的生员);接构(联合);接三(人死后三日举行的迎接魂魄归来的仪式);接煞(为接死人灵魂回家而举行的仪式)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 接见;接待 <span class="encs">[meet;entertain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">接遇宾客。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尽礼相接。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:接伴(接待外国使臣);接待使(陪同官员;担任迎接、陪伴来宾使命的人);接遇(接待);接对(接待应对)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 靠近;接触 <span class="encs">[touch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">车错毂兮短兵接。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九歌·国殇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宾立接西塾。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:接物(与外物接触;与人交往);接膝(膝盖相接。指彼此对坐靠得很近);接仗(交战，打仗);接席(坐席相接。多形容亲近);接翅(翅膀碰着翅膀。形容禽鸟多)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 连续;继续 <span class="encs">[continue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心不怡之长久兮，忧与愁其相接。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九章·哀郢》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">圣人食足以接气。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·精神》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故使工人为木材以接手。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策五》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:接响(接连不断的声响);接轸(车辆相连而行。比喻靠近);接脚(一个跟着一个，紧接在后;接替;接脚夫，接脚婿。妇女丈夫死后再招的后夫);接偈(接诵偈语)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 连接 <span
