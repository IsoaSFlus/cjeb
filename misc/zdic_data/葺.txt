                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>葺</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>原指用茅草覆盖房子，后泛指修理房屋：～墙。～屋（草屋）。修～。</li><li>累积，重叠：～袭（重叠）。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>葺</strong> <span class="dicpy">qì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，揖省声。本义:用茅草盖屋)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[thatch;cover a roof with straw]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">葺，茨也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">葺覆也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">葺屋参分。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·匠人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芷葺兮荷屋。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·湘夫人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:葺茅(用茅草覆盖);葺屋(草屋;修缮房屋)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 修理房屋 <span class="encs">[repair a house;mend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">葺其墙屋。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缮完葺墙，以待宾客。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>陛 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>除，莫不葺复。—— <span class="diczx2">沈炯</span><span class="smcs">《太极殿铭序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尝加营葺。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余稍为修葺。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">复葺南阁子。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:葺治(修建);葺理(修理);葺缮(修补;修缮)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 修饰 <span class="encs">[polish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然皆句读不葺之诗尔。——</span><span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《词论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 整理;整治 <span class="encs">[renovate;dredge]</span>。如:葺治(治理;整治);葺理(整治)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 重叠;累积 <span class="encs">[overlap]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鱼葺鳞以自别兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·悲回风》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">葺鳞镂甲。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">葺袭(重叠的样子)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“缉”</span>。搜捕 <span class="encs">[track down and arrest]</span>。如:葺捕(缉捕)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
