						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>妈</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">媽</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mā.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄚˉ</span></p><ol><li>称呼母亲。</li><li>对女性长辈的称呼：大～。姑～。</li><li>旧时连着姓称中、老年蓬仆：刘～。鲁～。</li></ol>
