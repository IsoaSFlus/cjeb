						<p>● <strong>丽</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">麗</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>好看，漂亮：美～。秀～。明～。绚～。富～。～质（女子美好的品貌）。风和日～。</li>
	<li>附着：附～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>丽</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"> <span class="diczx3">麗</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔高～〕朝鲜历史上的王朝，旧时习惯上沿用指称朝鲜。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/罹">罹</a>&rdquo;，遭遇。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丽</strong></p>
<p>麗 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;罹&rdquo;。遭遇;落入 <span class="encs">[fall into]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鱼丽于罶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鱼丽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丽</strong></p>
<p>麗 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鹿，丽声。其字本作&ldquo;丽&rdquo;，后加&ldquo;鹿&rdquo;，成为形声字。鹿形丽声，鹿成对，并驾。本义:成群;结伴;成对)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 成对。后作&ldquo;俪&rdquo; <span class="encs">[forming a pair or couple;in pair]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丽，两也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丽钩芒与骖蓐收兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传上》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;丽，并驾也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丽马一圉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;夏官&middot;校人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">良马五之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鄘风&middot;干旄》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏引 <span class="diczx2">三国魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王肃</span>曰:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">夏后氏</span>驾两谓之丽。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自</span><span class="diczx2">扬</span>、 <span class="diczx2">马</span>、 <span class="diczx2">张</span>、 <span class="diczx2">蔡</span>，崇盛丽辞。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">刘勰</span><span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丽限(指期限的上限与下限);丽偶(对偶);丽辞(对偶的辞句);丽泽(两个沼泽相连)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 美丽，漂亮 <span class="encs">[fine;beautiful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">状貌甚丽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平津侯主父列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妻子衣服丽都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天生丽质难自弃。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《长恨歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长安水边多丽人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《丽人行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:壮丽(雄壮而美丽);秀丽(清秀美丽);丽象(美丽的景象);丽姝(亦称姝丽。美女);丽春园(又叫丽春院。相传是名妓苏卿的住处，后因以称妓院或艺妓、歌女的住处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 华丽，华靡 <span class="encs">[magnificent;gorgeous;resplendent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恶丽靡而不近，斥芬芳而不御。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绮丽之艳说。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘勰</span><span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丽句(绮靡的文句);丽则(佳美而失法度);丽尔(华丽);丽谯(壮丽的高楼);丽靡(奢靡华丽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;厉&rdquo;。猛烈 <span class="encs">[violent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西北曰厉风。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;地形》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丽</strong></p>
<p>麗 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 结伴而行 <span class="encs">[go or travel in a group]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若其五县游丽辩论之士，街谈巷议，弹射臧否。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 依附;附着 <span class="encs">[stick to;adhere to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日月丽乎天，百谷草木丽乎土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;离》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丽土之毛。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">徐光启</span><span class="smcs">《甘薯疏序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丽水(附着于水中);丽霄(附着在天空);丽紫(着上紫色)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 施;加 <span class="encs">[bring to bear on;impose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">越兹丽刑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丽兵(将武器施加于人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 依法执行 <span class="encs">[punish according to law]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丽法(施行法律);丽徒(服刑的犯人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 射中 <span class="encs">[shoot home]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射麋丽龟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丽龟(射中禽兽中心脊椎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 依托 <span class="encs">[depend on; rely on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人无所丽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 系;拴 <span class="encs">[tie]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君牵牲，&hellip;既入庙门，丽于碑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;罹&rdquo;。遭遇 <span class="encs">[encounter;suffer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鱼丽于罶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鱼丽》</span></span></p>

<p>麗 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>

                            <div class="label"><span class="res_t res_d res_s">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=鹿">鹿</a>】 </span><input type="checkbox" checked="checked" name="twbon" class="lcs_check" autocomplete="on" /></div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">麗</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“丽”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/麗">麗</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=鹿">鹿</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9E97.svg">                          <p>旅行也。鹿之性，見食急則必旅行。从鹿丽聲。《禮》：麗皮納聘。蓋鹿皮也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20019.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀙</span>，古文。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠧥</span>，篆文麗字。郎計切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9E97_8069.svg" /><span class="swbf">(麗)</span><span class="ywyy">旅行也。</span><span class="swdz">此麗之本義，其字本作麗，旅行之象也。後乃加鹿耳。周禮：麗馬一圉，八麗一師。注曰：麗，耦也。禮之儷皮，左傳之伉儷，《說文》之驪駕皆其義也。㒳相附則為麗。易曰：離，麗也。日月麗乎天，百榖艸木麗乎土，是其義也。麗則有耦可觀，㸚部曰：麗<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24568.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤕨</span>猶靡麗也。是其義也。㒳而介其間亦曰麗，離卦之一陰麗二陽是也。</span><span class="ywyy">鹿之性，見食急則必旅行。</span><span class="swdz">此說從鹿之意也。見食急而猶必旅行者義也。小雅：呦呦鹿鳴，食野之蘋。傳曰：鹿得蓱，呦呦然鳴而相呼。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22846.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢡆</span>誠發乎中，以興嘉樂賓客，當有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22846.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢡆</span>誠相招呼以成禮也。北史：裴安祖聞講鹿鳴，而兄弟同食。古文只作麗，後乃加鹿之意如是。</span><span class="ywyy">从鹿丽。</span><span class="swdz">各本丽下有聲字，今正。从鹿，五經文字作从鹿省，葢張氏所據如是，故隸書多作麗少一畫。郞計切。十五部。</span><span class="ywyy">禮：麗皮納聘。葢鹿皮也。</span><span class="swdz">聘禮曰：上介奉幣儷皮。士冠禮：主人酬賓，束帛儷皮。儷卽刺麗之俗。鄭注：儷皮，㒳鹿皮也。鄭意麗為兩，許意麗為鹿，其意實相通。士冠注曰：古文麗為離。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9E97_8070.svg" /><span class="swbf">(丽)</span><span class="ywyy">古文。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9E97_8071.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠧥</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">篆文麗字。</span><span class="swdz">古本皆作篆文，毛刻作籒文。集韻、類篇曰：古作麗、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠧥</span>。玉篇又乖異不同。廣韻則麗、麗各字。疑麗者古文，麗者籒文，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠧥</span>者小篆也。然小篆多用麗為形聲。</span></p>
<p>文二十六今刪<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋝</span>。重六按此部各本<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2D0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋐</span>下麛上有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋝</span>篆，雲鹿跡也，從鹿速聲，桑穀切。無論橫亙令上下二篆不相接。釋獸曰：麋其蹤跡<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/281E0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨇠</span>，鹿其跡速，又作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋝</span>。麇其跡解，兔其跡迒。廣雅緫之曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/281E0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨇠</span>䟱解亢跡也。疎曹憲匹跡反。是可以證古爾雅之作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨒪</span>，不作速。卽加鹿亦當作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋝</span>，不從速。辵部曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨒪</span>，籒文跡。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨒪</span>無妨專為鹿跡之名。卽作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋝</span>，亦必匹跡切，在十六部。不得從速，桑穀切，在三部也。許書有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋝</span>篆，必是淺人據誤本爾雅所羼入，今刪正。集韻：疎或跡字，資昔切。又按麒麟作麐，而麟為別一字，求之古文往往不合。麐為牝麒，何以經傳不<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧦝</span>麒但<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧦝</span>麟，且又何不言牡曰麒也。鳥部以鳳居首，此亦當鹿篆之下出麒篆，解雲：麒麟，仁獸也。麇身牛尾一角。從鹿，其聲。又出麟篆，解雲：麒麟也。從鹿，麟聲。一曰麟大牡鹿也。又出麐篆，解雲：或從吝聲。以下乃次麚篆，乃合。今本恐非古本耳。改<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋝</span>篆為<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A2DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪋝</span>篆，次於麉篆之下，解雲：鹿跡也，從鹿<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨒪</span>，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨒪</span>籒文跡。乃合。</p>                          </div>
