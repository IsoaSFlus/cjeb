                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">馈</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>馈</strong></p><p>饋、餽 <span class="dicpy">kuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，贵声。本义:以食物送人)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[offer food]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">馈，饷也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陈馈八簋。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·伐木》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君有馈焉曰献。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国胡以馈之。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:馈孰(献熟食);馈食(献熟食);馈飱(进献饭食);馈馔(以食物送人);馈献(献熟食)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 进食 <span class="encs">[eat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在中馈。——<span class="smcs">《易·家人》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“食也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:馈养(进食奉养)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 烹调 <span class="encs">[cook]</span>。如:馈灶(烧火做饭);馈膳(烹调膳食);馈人(君王的厨子)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 赠送 <span class="encs">[present]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">馈，遗也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">古者致物于人，尊之则曰献，通行曰馈。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">馈者，上下通称。故祭祀于神而言馈。阳货馈孔子豚而言馈，乡党云朋友之馈，是上下通言馈。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士虞礼》</span>疏</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是诸侯之大于戍</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>， <span class="diczx2">齐</span>人馈之以饩。——<span class="smcs">《左传·桓公六年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王及列士，皆有馈赂。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朋友之馈，虽车马，非祭肉不拜。——<span class="smcs">《论语·乡党》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:馈选(中选);馈岁(旧岁年终亲友互相馈赠);馈饷(赠与财物);馈赆(赠送远行者的财物)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 运送粮食等 <span class="encs">[transport;ship]</span>。如:馈运(运送粮食);馈路(运粮饷、财物的通道);馈挽(运粮);馈饢(运粮饷)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 祭祀 <span class="encs">[offer a sacrifice to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祭祀自孰始曰馈食。馈食者，食道也。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·特牲馈食礼》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:馈奠(丧中祭奠之事);馈祀(以酒饭祭鬼神)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>馈</strong></p><p>饋 <span class="dicpy">kuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 饮食之事 <span class="encs">[food and drink;diet]</span></p><p><span
