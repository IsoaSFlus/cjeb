                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雏</strong></p><p>雛、鶵 <span class="dicpy">chú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)，刍声。本义:小鸡)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雏，鸡子也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃以雏尝黍。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“新鸡也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有人于此，力不能胜一匹雏，则为无力人矣。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为幼禽 <span class="encs">[very young bird]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雏烧。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“鸟之小者也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:雏雉(幼雉);雏禽(幼鸟);雏莺(幼莺。也喻指幼龄的歌妓)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 幼小的动物 <span class="encs">[the young]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乳虎，曾纩息之未定兮，乃敢探其穴，而啖其雏。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《思子台》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 幼儿 <span class="encs">[infant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丈夫生儿有如此二雏者，名位岂肯卑微休!——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《徐卿二子歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挈妇将雏鬓有丝。——</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《七律·无题》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>雏</strong></p><p>雛 <span class="dicpy">chú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>动物幼小的 <span
