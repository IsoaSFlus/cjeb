                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>脚</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">腳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人和某些动物身体最下部接触地面的部分：～心。～掌。～背。～跟。～步。～印。～法（指踢球、踢毽等的技巧）。～镣。～踏实地（形容做事实事求是，不浮夸）。</li>
	<li>最下部：～注。山～。墙～。</li>
	<li>剩下的废料，渣滓：下～料。</li>
	<li>〔～本〕表演戏剧或拍摄影视所依据的底本。</li>
	<li>旧时指与体力搬运有关的：～夫。～行（<span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ng </span>）。～钱。拉～。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>脚</strong></p>
<p>脚 <span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，却声。本义:胫，小腿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shank]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脚，胫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">捶笞膑脚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脚膀(指小腿);脚胫(小腿);腿杆(方言。腿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 足的别称 <span class="encs">[foot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雄兔脚扑朔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脚著</span><span class="diczx2">谢公</span>屐。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脚炉(烘脚的小铜炉);脚踏(炕前供垫脚用的矮凳);脚踪(足迹);脚驴(供人雇用骑行的驴子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 某物的底座(作为它的支承部件)<span class="encs">[base]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水面初平云脚低。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《钱塘湖春行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:根脚;杯脚;山脚;墙脚;脚灯;脚底下人</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 残渣，即最后留下的渣滓 <span class="encs">[dregs;residue]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茶脚;酒脚</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 脚步 <span class="encs">[step]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:脚头(脚步，行动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 担任传递或运输的人 <span class="encs">[porter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:脚家(脚夫);脚子(旧时对搬运工人的蔑称);脚户(脚夫);脚步钱(跑腿钱);脚费(脚钱);脚乘(运载工具，如舟车骡马等;指搬运费)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 帮手 <span class="encs">[helper]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我又不能自去，少不得要他作脚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 末端 <span class="encs">[end]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:脚舰(系在战船尾部备用的小船);太阳光线的末端叫日脚</span></p>
