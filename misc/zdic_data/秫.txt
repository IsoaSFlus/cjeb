                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>秫</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>黏高粱，可以做烧酒，有的地区泛指高粱：～秸。～米（高粱米）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> glutinous variety of millet</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> millet,sorgho,riz glutineux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">秫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>秫</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。从古文术，加禾。本义:谷物之有粘性者。也指不粘者)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a glutinous variety of cereal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秫，稷之黏者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">稻黍秫稷粟麻秔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《急就篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">染羽以朱湛丹秫。盖有赤白二种。今北地谓高粱之粘者为秫，秫亦胡秫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;钟氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春秫作美酒，酒熟吾自斟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《和郭主簿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:秫田(种植黏粟之田);秫酒(用秫酿成的酒);秫蘖(制酒用的糯黍与曲);秫稻(即糯稻);秫谷(黍米);秫黍(高粱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;鉥&rdquo;。长针 <span class="encs">[long needle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黑齿雕题，鳀冠秫缝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
