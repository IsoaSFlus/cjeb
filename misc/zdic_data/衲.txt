                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>衲</strong> <span class="dicpy">nà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉?</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 僧徒的衣服，常用许多碎布补缀而成，因即以为僧衣的代称 <span class="encs">[Buddhist cassock]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自出家来长自在，缘身一衲一绳休。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《赠僧自远禅师》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:衲子(衲为僧衣。故借称僧徒为衲子);衲衣(僧衣);衲袄(和尚所穿的大袖上衣);衲裙(僧人的衣裳);衲帛(丝织物品)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 僧徒自称或代称 <span class="encs">[monk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">老衲供茶碗，斜阳送客舟。——</span><span class="diczx2">戴叔伦</span><span class="smcs">《题横山寺》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:老衲;衲子(指僧徒);衲衣(代称僧人);衲师(僧侣);衲徒(僧人，僧众);衲僧(和尚，僧人)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>衲</strong> <span class="dicpy">nà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 缝补，补缀 <span class="encs">[(of clothes)mend;patch up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拘挛补衲，蠹文已甚。——</span><span class="diczx2">钟嵘</span><span class="smcs">《诗品诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:衲裰(布块补缀成的短袍);纳头(用破布补缀而成的布片子;补丁很多的衣服);衲帛(织绣);衲被(曾经补缀的被子);衲衣(衲头。补缀过的衣服。泛指破旧衣服);衲袍(用碎布料缝缀的袍服)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为缀合而成之意 <span class="encs">[assort]</span>。如:百衲本二十四史</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 密针缝纫。同<span class="yhcs">“纳”</span> <span class="encs">[stitch]</span>。如:衲鞋底;衲线(缝缀用的线)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
