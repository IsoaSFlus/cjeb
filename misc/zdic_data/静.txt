                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>静</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>停止的，与“<span class="diczx3">动</span>”相对：～止。～态。～物。平～。风平浪～。</li><li>没有声音：安～。寂～。僻～。冷～。肃～。～悄悄。～穆。～谧。～默。～观。～听。</li><li>安详，闲雅：～心。～坐。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">净</span>”，清洁。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> quiet, still, motionless; gentle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> still, ruhig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> calme,silencieux,serein,immobile</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">静</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>静</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从青，争声。本义:彩色分布适当)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 安静;宁静 <span class="encs">[quiet;tranquil;peaceful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">静言思之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·柏舟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宵静女德，以伏蛊慝。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">本朝不静。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·音律》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">静乃明几。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·审分》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫不静好。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·女曰鸡鸣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">清和肃穆，莫不静畅。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">支遁</span><span class="smcs">《八关斋会诗序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:静雅(安静);静鞭(帝王仪仗的一种，鞭形，振之作响，令人肃静。也叫<span class="yhcs">“鸣鞭”</span>);静办处(安静的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 静止，物体不运动(跟<span class="yhcs">“动”</span>相反) <span class="encs">[still;motionless]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怒则动，动则手足不静。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">树欲静而风不止，子欲养而亲不待也。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:静物(静止不动的物体);静美(静止形态的美);静风(空气无显著的运动);静海(平静的海);静流(死水)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 没有声响;寂静 <span class="encs">[silent;quiet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茅檐人静。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《夜闻杜鹃》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">静间按些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:静巉巉(静碜碜，静沉沉。形容沉静、静悄悄);静荡荡(寂静空旷的样子);静室(静舍。清静的居室。寺院)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 沉着，冷静 <span class="encs">[sober;calm]</span>。如:静言(沉静地思考);静钝(沉着冷静，不露锋芒);静处(冷静地立身处世)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 贞洁，不轻佻 <span class="encs">[chaste]</span>。如:静女(贞静、不轻佻的女子);静专(贞静专一)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“净”</span>。干净，纯净 <span class="encs">[clean;pure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其告维何，边豆静嘉。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·既醉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">静洁足以享上帝，礼鬼神。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:静扫(打扫干净);静青(纯青);静嘉(洁净美好)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 恬淡;平和 <span class="encs">[peaceful]</span>。如:静退(恬淡谦逊);静意(静心，不动心意);静好(安定美好)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 善 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东门之栗，有静家室。——<span class="smcs">《艺文类聚》</span>引<span class="smcs">《韩诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 倦怠 <span class="encs">[tired]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其静也，日减其所有，而损其所成。——<span 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中" target="_blank">戌集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中&amp;bs=青" target="_blank">青</a>】
                              靜·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/975C.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㣏【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>疾郢切，音穽。【說文】審也。从靑，爭聲。【註】徐鍇曰：丹靑明審也。</p><p>又【增韻】動之對也。【易·坤卦】至靜而德方。</p><p>又【書·堯典】靜言庸違。【傳】靜，謀也。</p><p>又【詩·邶風】靜言思之。【傳】靜，安也。</p><p>又【鄭風】琴瑟在御，莫不靜好。</p><p>又【詩·邶風】靜女其姝。【傳】靜，貞靜也。</p><p>又【廣韻】息也。【禮·月令】百官靜事毋<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>。</p><p>又【韻會】澄也。</p><p>又【廣韻】和也。</p><p>又【韻會】通作靖。亦通作靚。亦通作竫。</p><p>又【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>疾正切，音淨。義同。【前漢·揚雄傳】京師爲之語曰：維寂寞自投閣，爰淸靜作符命。</p><p>又【詩·大雅】籩豆靜嘉。【箋】潔淸而美。【釋文】淸，如字，又才性反。淸靜皆可讀去聲。◎按玉篇、廣韻、集韻、類篇，靜字皆無去聲。韻會始收入敬韻，正韻因之。考大雅、釋文，及揚雄傳京師語，靜字本可讀去聲，但韻會引解嘲爰淸爰靜句，云，顏註合韻，音才性切，則謬甚。解嘲云，爰淸爰靜，游神之庭。卽音才性切，如何與庭叶，是必因爰淸靜句而誤記也。正韻仍其失，今特辨之。</p><p>又叶千廷切，音淸。【六韜】秋道斂，萬物盈。冬道藏，萬物靜。</p>
