						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>撮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cuō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuō.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄨㄛˉ</span></p><ol><li>聚起，多指用簸箕状的器具铲起东西：～成一堆。～土。～合。</li><li>取，摘取：～要。</li><li>用手指捏取细碎的东西：～药。</li><li>中国市制容量单位，一升的千分之一。</li><li>量词：一～米。</li><li>撮东西用的器具：～子。～箕。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>撮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zuǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǒ.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄨㄛˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 量词，用于成丛的毛发：剪下一～头发。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">撮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>撮</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuō.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，最声。本义:用手指抓取粒状物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用三指取物;抓取 <span class="encs">[take up with three fingers]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">撮，四圭也，一曰两指撮也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">量多少者不失圭撮。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·律历志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸱鸺夜撮蚤。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·秋水》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:撮一点儿盐;撮药(抓药);撮土(抓一把土);撮拾(撮取);撮台子(拾掇桌子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 把聚拢的东西用簸箕等物铲起 <span class="encs">[scoop up]</span>。如:撮走一簸箕土</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 拉拢 <span class="encs">[draw sb.over to one's side]</span>。如:撮补(从中帮助)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 玩弄 <span class="encs">[play]</span>。如:撮戏(玩把戏);撮哄(起哄);撮空(戏弄虚假)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 簇拥 <span class="encs">[cluster round]</span>。如:撮拥(簇拥)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 摘要，摘取 <span class="encs">[extract]</span>。如:撮文(摘取文句;咬文嚼字);撮录(采录;摘录);撮略(摘取，摘引);撮述(摘要叙述);撮取(摘取)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 聚合;聚拢 <span class="encs">[gather]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">撮冀州之众。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·袁绍传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我这里正项钱粮，兀自起解不足，东那西撮。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 提，拎 <span class="encs">[carry]</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>撮</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuō</span> <span
