                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>跗</strong> <span class="dicpy">fū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 脚背 <span class="encs">[instep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蹶泥则没足灭跗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:跗注(古代的一种长兵服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 足 <span class="encs">[foot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:跗面(脚面);跗蹋(以足踏地);双跗(双脚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 物体的足部。通&ldquo;柎&rdquo; <span class="encs">[leg]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朱跗黄实，蓄殖果木，不如三土以十分之六。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;地员》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鼓跗(鼓架)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指条状物的末端 <span class="encs">[end]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:剑跗(剑的握手处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 花萼 <span class="encs">[calyx]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衔素蕊于青跗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《郊居赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:跗萼(花萼与子房。亦指花朵);跗萼联芳(比喻兄弟均贵显荣耀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">f&uacute;</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=足" target="_blank">足</a>】
                              跗·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8DD7.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】甫無切【集韻】【韻會】風無切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音膚。足趾也。【玉篇】足上也。【儀禮&middot;士喪禮】乃屨綦結于跗連絇。【註】跗，足上也。【疏】謂足背也。【莊子&middot;秋水篇】蹶泥則沒足滅跗。【音義】跗，足跗也。</p><p>又【左傳&middot;成十六年】有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2939F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩎟</span>韋之跗注。【跓】跗注，戎服，若袴而屬于跗，與袴連。</p><p>又人名。【史記&middot;扁鵲倉公傳】醫有兪跗。【註】黃帝時將也。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>符遇切，音附。義同。</p><p>又與拊通。【詩&middot;小雅】鄂不韡韡。【箋】不音夫，當作拊。【音義】拊亦作跗。</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/67CE_2728.svg" /><span class="swbf">(柎)</span><span class="ywyy">闌足也。</span><span class="swdz">柎蒙上文木<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/271FD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧇽</span>言之。阑字恐有誤。韵會本闌作鄂。柎跗正俗字也。凡器之足皆曰柎。小雅：鄂不<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏬</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏬</span>。傳云：鄂猶鄂鄂然，言外發也。箋云：承華者曰鄂。不當作柎。柎，鄂足也。鄂足得華之光明則<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏬</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏬</span>然盛。古聲不柎同。箋意鄂承華者也，柎又在鄂之下，以華與鄂喻兄弟相依。郭璞云：江東呼草木子房爲柎。草木子房如石橊房，蓮房之類，與花下鄂一理也。</span><span class="ywyy">從木付聲。</span><span class="swdz">甫無切。四部。</span></p>                          </div>
