                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">仁</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>仁</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&eacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>一种道德范畴，指人与人相互友爱、互助、同情等：～义（①仁爱与正义；②通情达理，性格温顺，能为别人着想）。～爱。～政。～人志士（仁爱有节操的人）。～义礼智（儒家的伦理思想）。～至义尽。一视同～（同样看待，不分厚薄）。</li>
	<li>果核的最内部分或其他硬壳中可以吃的部分：核桃～儿。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> humaneness, benevolence, kindness</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Herzensgüte, Gutherzigkeit, Güte  (S)​,Humanität, Menschlichkeit  (S, Philos)​,Kern  (S)​,Wohlwollen  (S)​,empfindlich, sensibel  (Adj)​,human, gütig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> humanité,bienveillance,amande,pépin,bon,bienveillant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">仁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>仁</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&eacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从二。右边的二是重文。本义:博爱，人与人相互亲爱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 中国古代一种含义极广的道德观念。其核心指人与人相互亲爱。孔子以之作为最高的道德标准 <span class="encs">[benevolence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仁，亲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仁者，情志好生爱人，故立字二人为仁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春初&middot;元命苞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上下相亲谓之仁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;经解》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">温良者，仁之本也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仁者，谓其中心欣然爱人也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;解老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仁者，可以观其爱焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;丧服四制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂无居人?不如叔也，洵美且仁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;叔于田》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:仁人(有仁德的人);仁术(施行仁道、仁政的方法);仁宇(在仁德的覆蔽之下);仁瑞(仁德的瑞兆);仁朴(仁爱朴实);仁笃(仁爱笃厚);仁诲(仁爱的教诲);仁鄙(仁爱与鄙薄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 有德者之称 <span class="encs">[the benevolent]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旧指有仁德的人</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予尝求古仁人之心。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:仁人网(传说商汤曾让猎人网开三面。指给予一条生路);仁者(有德行的人);仁里(仁者住地);仁士(仁人。有德行的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指事物中有恩于万物生育者，古代常与五行等相配</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">养之，长之，假之，仁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 完美的道德 <span class="encs">[perfect virtue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫仁者，己欲立而立人，己欲达而达人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 仁政 <span class="encs">[benevolent government]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以德行仁者王。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 恩惠 <span class="encs">[kindness]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则民与子仁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:「恩也。」</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厚泽深仁，遂有天下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《伪自由书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 同情， 怜悯 <span class="encs">[pity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">呼而怨，欲望其哀且仁者，愈大谬矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《天说》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 种子外皮内的部分&mdash;&mdash;常指可以食用的种子和坚果、核果及类似果实的内果皮里边的部分。字本作「人」，明代后改作「仁」 <span class="encs">[kernel]</span>。如:核桃仁</p>
