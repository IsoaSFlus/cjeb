                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">婴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>婴</strong></p><p>嬰 <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从女，賏( <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> )。<span class="yhcs">“賏”</span>是颈项链。本义:妇女颈饰，似现代的项链)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[necklace]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">婴，颈饰也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">喉下称婴。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 初生的女孩 <span class="encs">[baby]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见人方引婴儿欲投之江中。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 亦泛指初生儿</p><p><span class="diczx1">人始生曰婴。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我独泊兮，其未兆，如婴儿之未孩。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自哂同婴孩——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《山寺》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:男婴;女婴;妇婴;婴子(婴儿。又叫<span class="yhcs">“婴人”</span>);婴儿子(幼儿);婴耄(幼儿和老人);婴弱(幼小);婴提(幼儿);婴稚(幼年);婴孺(幼儿);婴年(少年，儿时)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>婴</strong></p><p>嬰 <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 系到颈上;戴 <span class="encs">[wear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辟之，是犹使处女婴宝珠、佩宝玉，负戴黄金，而遇中山之盗也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:婴衿(犹衿缨。结缨于襟)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 缠绕，反复盘绕其上 <span class="encs">[twine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白婴茀。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·天问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">隗乎共相婴。——</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《甘泉赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“婴，绕也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是与天和相婴薄。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·要略》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“婴，绕抱也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">释斤斧之用，而欲婴以芒刃。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·贾谊传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">借问之何之?世网婴我身。——<span class="smcs">《文选·赴洛道中作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:婴怀(萦怀;牵挂在心);婴意(挂心，在意);婴纶(羁缚);婴物(萦怀世务;纠缠于外界事物)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“撄”</span>。触犯 <span class="encs">[offend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">教诲之，调一之，则兵劲城固，敌国不敢婴也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·强国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民和齐则兵劲城固，敌国不敢婴也。——<span
