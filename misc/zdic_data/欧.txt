                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">欧</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>欧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">歐</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄡˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>姓。</li>
	<li>指欧洲，世界七大洲之一。～美国家。</li>
	<li>电阻单位名&ldquo;欧姆&rdquo;的简称，符号&Omega;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> translit.: Europe; ohm; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Abkürzung für Europa</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (abrév.)​ Europe,euro-,(utilisé pour les transcriptions phonétiques)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">欧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>欧</strong></p>
<p>甌 <span class="dicpy">ōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;讴&rdquo;。歌唱 <span class="encs">[sing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百姓欧歌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《隶释&middot;三公山碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:欧歌(讴歌，歌颂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;驱&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或导之以德教，或欧之以法令。。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼&middot;礼察》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 驱使;疾行 <span class="encs">[drive out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:欧牛马</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;殴&rdquo;。殴打<span class="encs">[beat up;hit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">(老父)顾谓</span><span class="diczx2">良</span>曰:孺子下取履! <span class="diczx2">良</span>鄂然，欲欧之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张良传》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>欧</strong></p>
<p>歐 <span class="dicpy">ōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;区&rdquo; <span class="encs">[house]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:欧隅(房屋的角落)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;瓯&rdquo;。种族名 <span class="encs">[O natitonality]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:欧人(古越族的一支);亦为地名。如:欧越(即瓯越。古越人聚居之地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 欧罗巴洲的简称 <span class="encs">[Europe]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:欧人(欧洲人);欧美(欧洲与美洲。泛指西方国家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 欧姆的简称 <span class="encs">[ohm]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">歐</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“欧”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/歐">歐</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下" target="_blank">辰集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下&amp;bs=欠" target="_blank">欠</a>】
                              歐·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6B50.svg">						  <p>【玉篇】【集韻】【韻會】於口切【唐韻】烏后切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音毆。【說文】吐也。或作嘔。【急就篇註】歐逆，吐而不下食也。【前漢&middot;嚴助傳】歐泄，霍亂之病相隨屬。【山海經】薄魚一目，其音如歐。【註】郭璞曰：如人嘔吐聲也。【又】海外歐絲之野，在大踵東有女子跪據樹歐絲。【註】郭璞曰：言噉葉而吐絲，蓋蠶類也。【白氏六帖】跪樹歐絲，生桑得繭。</p>
<p>又與毆通，捶擊也。【史記&middot;留侯世家】良愕然欲歐之。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>烏侯切，音鷗。與謳同。氣出而歌也。</p>
<p>又歐歐，聲也。【魏繆襲&middot;尤射】雞鳴歐歐，明燈皙皙。皙音制。</p>
<p>又姓。歐冶子，古善鑄劒者，見【越絕書】。</p>
<p>又歐侯、歐陽，俱複姓，見【漢書】。</p>
<p>又刀名。【後漢&middot;虞詡傳】寧伏歐刀以示遠近。【註】歐刀，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>人之刀也。</p>
<p>又水名。【山海經】滏水東流，注于歐水。　【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20D30.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠴰</span>㰶<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275BC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧖼</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/238A8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣢨</span>。</p>
