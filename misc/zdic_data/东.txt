                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">东</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>东</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">東</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>方位词，日出的方向，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">西</span>&rdquo;相对：～方。～经（本初子午线以东的经度或经线）。～山再起（喻失势之后，重新恢复地位）。付诸～流。</li>
	<li>主人（古代主位在东，宾位在西）：房～。股～。～道主（泛指请客的主人，亦称&ldquo;东道&rdquo;、&ldquo;作东&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>请客出钱的人：作～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> east, eastern, eastward</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Osten  (S)​,östlich  (Adj)​,Dong  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> est (n.m)​,Orient,hôte (celui qui reçoit)​,propriétaire</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">东</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>东</strong></p>
<p>東 <span class="dicpy">dōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从木，官溥说，从日在木中。本义:东方，日出的方向)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[east]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东，动也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东方者，动方也，万物始动生也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;五行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东方木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文训》</span>。按:&ldquo;日所出也。从日在木中，会意。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东君，日也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释天》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东邻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;既济》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;震为东。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东方者，阳也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;情性》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大儿锄豆溪东，中儿正织鸡笼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《清平乐&middot;村居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:关东;旭日东升;东夏(指中国东部);东朝(东宫);东极(东方边远之处);东君(神话中与西王母相对的东王公);东坦(女婿);东裔(东方边远地方);东村女(即东施);东方作(日出);东记(古代传说中的日出处。亦泛指东方极远之地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 特指东西向，与南北向相对 <span class="encs">[east and west]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:东亩(使田垄东西向)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 东道主的略称。主人 <span class="encs">[host;owner]</span>。在社交或商业活动中接待客人或顾客的人。古时主位在东，宾位在西，所以主人称东。如:房东;股东;今天我们作东;东主(东家;店主;房东)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 春天 <span class="encs">[spring]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:东皇(指春种;司春之神);东君(春神);东作(春耕;指春季作物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指厕所 。即&ldquo;东厕&rdquo;。旧时厕所设于北房左侧，故称东厕<span class="encs">[toilet;lavatory]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:登东(上厕所);东净(厕所);东厮(厕所);东司(厕所)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>东</strong></p>
<p>東 <span class="dicpy">dōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>向东，东去 <span class="encs">[face east]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大江东去，浪淘尽，千古风流人物。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇&middot;赤壁怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:东流(东去的流水);东波(向东流逝之水);东徙(向东迁移)</span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“东”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/東">東</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              東·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6771.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】德紅切【集韻】【韻會】都籠切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蝀。【說文】動也。陽氣動，于時爲春。【書&middot;堯典】平秩東作。【孔傳】歲起於東，而始就耕也。【淮南子&middot;天文訓】東方木也，其帝太皡。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;曆書】日起於東，月起於西。【鄭樵&middot;通志】日在木中曰東，在木上曰杲，在木下曰杳。木，若木也，日所升降。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】東有啓明。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋地】東至于泰遠。</p>
<p>又姓。【聖賢羣輔錄】舜友東不訾。</p>
<p>又叶當經切，音丁。【詩&middot;小雅】念我土宇，我生不辰。逢天僤怒，自西徂東。</p>
<p>又叶都郞切，音當。【楊泉&middot;蠶賦】粵召僕夫，築室于旁。于旁伊何，在庭之東。○按《說文》東自爲部，今倂入。</p>
