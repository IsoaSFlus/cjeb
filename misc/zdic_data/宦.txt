                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">宦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>宦</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从宀 <span class="dicpy">mián</span>，表示与家庭房屋有关。从臣。<span class="yhcs">“臣”</span>本奴隶。合起来的意思是家室奴仆。本义:做奴隶主或帝王的奴仆)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[serve as a slave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与</span><span class="diczx2">范蠡</span>人宦于 <span class="diczx2">吴</span>。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“为臣隶也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:宦女(服务于宫中的女奴)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 学习官吏的事务 <span class="encs">[study government service]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宦，仕也。…犹今试用学习之官也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宦三年矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公三年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“学也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宦学事师。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宦于大夫。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·杂记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:宦学(学习做官与六艺;做官与做学问)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 做官 <span class="encs">[be an official]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">成公</span>即位，乃宦 <span class="diczx2">卿</span>之适而为之田。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与君离别意，同是宦游人。——</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《送杜少府之任蜀州》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:宦成(登上显贵地位);宦况(为官的情况);宦味(做官的况味、感触);宦途(做官的经历)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 阉割 <span class="encs">[castrate]</span>。如:宦牛(阉割的牛)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>宦</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 官吏 <span class="encs">[official]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">本图宦达，不矜名节。——</span><span class="diczx2">李密</span><span class="smcs">《陈情表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">承籍有宦官。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:宦牒(记载官吏姓名爵禄的文书);宦囊羞涩(为官者手头贫乏);宦女(官家女婢);宦族(官宦之家)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 太监，在内宫侍候女眷或被任为宫廷内侍的割去睾丸的男人 <span class="encs">[eunuch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫</span><span class="diczx2">高</span>，故宦人也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李斯列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宦官尚食比郎中。——<span class="smcs">《汉书 ·惠帝纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“阍寺也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宦者，养也。养阉人使其看宫人，此是小臣。——<span class="smcs">《文选·宦者传论》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
