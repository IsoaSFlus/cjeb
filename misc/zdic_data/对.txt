                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">对</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>对</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">對</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">du&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>答，答话，回答：～答如流。无言以～。</li>
	<li>朝着：～酒当歌。</li>
	<li>处于相反方向的：～面。</li>
	<li>跟，和：～他商量一下。</li>
	<li>互相，彼此相向地：～立。～流。～接。～称（<span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;n</span>）。～峙。</li>
	<li>说明事物的关系：～于。～这事有意见。</li>
	<li>看待，应付：～待。</li>
	<li>照着样检查：核～。校（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o </span>）～。</li>
	<li>投合，适合，使相合：～应（<span class="dicpy">y&igrave;ng </span>）。～劲。</li>
	<li>正确，正常，表肯定的答语：神色不～。</li>
	<li>双，成双的：配～。～偶。～仗（律诗、骈文等按照字音的平仄和字义做成对偶的语句）。</li>
	<li>平分，一半：～开。</li>
	<li>搀和（多指液体）：～水。</li>
	<li>量词，双：一～鹦鹉。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">对</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>对</strong></p>
<p>對 <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从口，从&ldquo;丵&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;</span>，即，古&ldquo;丛&rdquo;字，象草木丛生。)，从寸。寸，法度也。汉文帝以为责对而伪，言多非诚，故去其口，以从土。本义:应答)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[answer;reply]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">对，答也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听言则对。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;桑柔》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">叶公</span>问 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>于 <span class="diczx2">子路</span>， <span class="diczx2">子路</span>不对。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;述而》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">唐且</span>对曰:&ldquo;臣未尝闻也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王语</span><span class="diczx2">暴</span>以好乐， <span class="diczx2">暴</span>未有以对也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冠者对。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;应也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">对曰:&ldquo;非礼也敢辞。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;答问也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">操</span>唤 <span class="diczx2">杨修</span>问之， <span class="diczx2">修</span>以鸡肋之意对。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:无言以对;对当(对答;安排);对嘴(争辩;争吵);对理(讲理;对证);对番(对证后理曲);对状(受审问时答述案情);对日(回答有关太阳问题的故事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 两者相对;面对 <span class="encs">[face;mutual;face to face]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼易之，对饮酒，醉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:对食(面对食物;古时宫人相约为夫妇，称为对食);对枰(下棋);对峙(相对峙立);对酒(面对着酒。饮酒);对酌(相对饮酒);对月(向月);对向(应对趋走);对床(两人对床而卧);对棋(相对下棋);对扬(面君奏对)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 相当;相配 <span class="encs">[match]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝作邦作对。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;皇矣》</span>。注:&ldquo;配也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木大而茎叶格对。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;审时》</span>。注:&ldquo;等也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:对头亲(门当户对的亲事);对头(适当的婚配;配偶;冤家;仇敌);对时对景(正符合当时的情景、场合);对合(利息和本钱相等);对越(配称);对坎儿(双方情况正好符合);对治(相匹敌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 核对，比照着检查 <span class="encs">[check;compare;identify]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以两司奏状对勘，以防虚伪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:对读(校对);对笔迹;对数字;对理(对质);对保(出具书面保证后，向保证人核对以证明保证属实的一种手续)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 搀和 <span class="encs">[mix;add]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这酒是对了水的</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 对待，以特定方式待<span class="encs">[人或物]</span> <span class="encs">[treat;deal with;cope with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫一人奋死可以对十。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;初见秦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:他对我不错</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 平分成两份 <span class="encs">[divide into halves]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对股劈;对开;对分</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 扬;显扬;指扬威 <span class="encs">[spread;make known;display one&#39;s power]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以对于天下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;皇矣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">对扬以辟之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭统》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 婚娶 <span class="encs">[wed]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对亲;对值(男女订为婚姻)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 抵押 <span class="encs">[mortgage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我们围着庄子的这几块地，年终不是有二百多银子的租子吗?就把这个对给他，合他说明白了，按月计利，不论年份，银到归赎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 较量，为争夺优势或胜利而激烈斗争 <span class="encs">[have a contest]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对仗(交战)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 拼，冒生命危险去做 <span class="encs">[risk one&#39;s life]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对副(收拾;杀掉);对命(拼命;偿命)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 朝着 <span class="encs">[subtend;betrained on;be directed at]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当窗理云鬓，对镜帖花黄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">对长亭晚，骤雨初歇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斜边对直角;弧对圆心角</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 对人无愧，不辜负 <span class="encs">[be worthy of;not letting down]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对得起，对得住;对不过(对不起);对不住(对不起。对人有愧)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>对</strong></p>
<p>對 <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 配偶 <span class="encs">[spouse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">择对不嫁，至年三十。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;逸民传&middot;梁鸿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 对手，敌对者 <span class="encs">[opponent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而所与对敌，或值人杰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:对儿(对手;对头)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 对策，一种反措施，用以反对、抑制或抵消另一种措施、办法或手段 <span class="encs">[countermeasure]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对子(办法)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 对子，对偶的词句;又指对联 <span class="encs">[antithetical couplet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:喜对;五言对;对笔(写对联的一种笔);对儿(对联)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>对</strong></p>
<p>對 <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 意见、判断或程序上正确 <span class="encs">[right]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他拒绝这一建议很对;你说得对，先生;意见很对;数目不对;猜对了;对，就这么办</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 相互;两人在一起 <span class="encs">[together]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对谈;对饮;对掌(共同掌管);对理(共同审理);对食(共同进餐)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>对</strong></p>
<p>對 <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用于按性别、左右、正反等配合的人和物 <span class="encs">[couple;pair]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他们是天生的一对;一对杯子;一对柜子;一对伙伴</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>对</strong></p>
<p>對 <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈介〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 朝，向&mdash;&mdash;用作虚词，表示动作的对象 <span class="encs">[to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对他的父亲谈及此事;对你的牙是凉了点;对耕作的危害;对财产的权利</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 对待。用法大致同&ldquo;对于&rdquo; <span class="encs">[toward]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:对任何人不怀恶意</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=寸" target="_blank">寸</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5C0D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】都隊切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】都內切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音碓。【說文】應無方也。本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B4A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡭊</span>。【爾雅&middot;釋言】對，遂也。【疏】遂者，因事之辭。【廣韻】答也。【增韻】揚也。【詩&middot;大雅】以對于天下。【註】答天下仰望之心也。【書&middot;說命】敢對揚天子之休命。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】侍坐於先生，先生問焉，終，則對。【又】君子問更端，則起而對。【註】離席對也。</p>
<p>又次對，轉對。【王球貽謀錄】唐百官入閤，有待制次對官。後唐天成中，廢待制次對官，五日一次，內殿百官轉對。</p>
<p>又當也，配也。【詩&middot;大雅】帝作邦作對。【註】言擇其可當此國者而君之也。</p>
<p>又對簿。【史記&middot;李將軍傳】廣年六十餘矣，終不能復對刀筆之吏。</p>
<p>又敵也。【吳陸遜曰】劉備今在境界，此疆對也。</p>
<p>又凡物<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>峙曰對。【杜甫&middot;萬丈潭詩】山危一徑盡，岸絕兩壁對。</p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“对”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/𡭊"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B4A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡭊</span></a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三">卷三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三&amp;swbs=丵">丵</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/21B4A.svg">                          <p>譍無方也。从丵从口从寸。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5C0D.svg" />對，對或从士。漢文帝以爲責對而爲言，多非誠對，故去其口以从士也。都隊切文四，重二</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/21B4A_9335.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B4A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡭊</span>)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27B6D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧭭</span>無方也。</span><span class="swdz">聘禮注曰：對，荅問也。按對、荅古通用。云<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27B6D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧭭</span>無方者，所謂善待問者如撞鐘，叩以大者則大鳴，叩以小者則小鳴也。無方故從丵口。</span><span class="ywyy">从丵口。从寸。</span><span class="swdz">寸，法度也。丵口而一歸於法度也。都隊切。十五部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/21B4A_9336.svg" /><span class="swbf">(對)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B4A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡭊</span>或从士。漢文帝㠯爲責對而面言，</span><span class="swdz">依廣韵訂。</span><span class="ywyy">多非誠對，故去其口㠯從士也。</span><span class="swdz">鍇曰：士，事也。取事實也。按篇、韵皆作土，未知孰是。趙氏明誠曰：據古鐘鼎皆作對。是漢文亦從古耳，非肊更也。</span></p>                          </div>
