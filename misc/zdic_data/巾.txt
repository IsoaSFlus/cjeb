						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>巾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 擦东西或包裹、覆盖东西的用品，多用纺织品制成：手～。围～。领～。枕～。餐～。～帼（“<span class="diczx3">帼</span>”是古代妇女的头巾，指妇女，如“～～英雄”）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">巾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>巾</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象布巾下垂之形。本义:佩巾，拭布，相当于现在的手巾)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[towel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巾，佩巾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沐巾一。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盥卒授巾。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">布巾环幅不凿。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">静其巾幂。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·特牲馈食礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">儿女共沾巾。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">归来泪满巾。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">张俞</span><span class="smcs">《蚕妇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巾短情长。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:花巾;小丝巾;巾栉(梳洗用品，即手巾和梳子;梳洗打扮);巾帚(拭巾和扫帚);巾帨(毛巾)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 缠束或覆盖用的织物 <span class="encs">[a piece of cloth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巾袖无光。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">簌簌衣巾落枣花。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《浣溪沙》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:领巾;围巾;巾带(古代有功之人的冠服，亦代指功名);巾车(有帷幕装饰的车子);巾衣(古代士大夫的装束，服之以示敬礼);又指古代送葬时引柩所用的布</p><p><span class="diczx1">巾待于阼阶下。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 头巾 <span class="encs">[turban]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愆礼巾，所以饰首。——<span class="smcs">《风俗通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巾，本以拭物，后人著之于头。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">羽扇纶巾。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《赤壁怀古》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">首戴方山巾。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《嘲鲁儒》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幅巾藜杖北城头，卷地西风满眼愁。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《秋晚登城北门》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:巾帻(头巾);巾冠(巾和冠);巾絮(头巾);巾囊(包裹，布袋);巾帽(头巾和帽子);巾卷(古代士族所用的头巾和书卷。引申为士族的代称)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 巾箱 <span class="encs">[turban box]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巾卷充街。——<span class="smcs">《文选·皇太子释奠会诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:巾笈(存放巾帛、书卷的小箱子);巾笥(有巾帛覆盖的箱子);巾衍(放置头巾、书卷等物的小箱子);巾笥(即巾箱);巾箧(巾箱)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>巾</strong>
