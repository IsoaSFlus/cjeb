                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>侈</strong> <span class="dicpy">chǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，多声。本义:自高自大，盛气凌人)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[arrogant;selfconceited]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侈，掩胁也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“掩者，掩盖其上;胁者，胁制其旁。凡自多以陵人曰侈。此侈之本义也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侈兮哆兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·巷伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:侈慢(自大傲慢);侈满(骄纵);侈然(骄纵貌;自大貌);侈傲(骄纵);侈邪(言行放肆)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 奢侈，追求过分的享受 <span class="encs">[luxurious;extravagant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侈，一曰奢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于臣侈矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四方之国有侈离之德则必灭。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王霸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">多费谓之侈。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·解老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以侈自败者多矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淫侈之俗日日以长。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自王侯以下莫不逾侈。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:侈服(华丽的衣服);侈饰(奢华的服饰)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 过分;过度 <span class="encs">[excessive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">专淫逸侈靡，不顾国政。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·楚策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">关市之征侈之。——<span class="smcs">《管子·大匡》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓过常也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:侈欲(过分的欲望);侈务(过度的徭役);侈言(夸大不实的言辞);侈论(夸大不实的言论)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>侈</strong> <span class="dicpy">chǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 夸大，吹牛 <span class="encs">[brag]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莽为人侈口。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侈言无验，虽丽非经。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《三都赋序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:侈言(夸大其辞);侈口(夸口)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 放纵 <span class="encs">[indulge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苟无恒心，放辟邪侈，无不为已。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:侈伪(放纵僭为);侈意(放纵之心)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 扩大 <span class="encs">[事物]</span> <span class="encs">[enlarge;aggravate;extend;broaden]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侈苑囿之大。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经训》</span></span></p><p><span
