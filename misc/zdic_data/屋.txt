						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>屋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>房，房间：～子。～宇。房～。</li><li>家：“吴中过客莫思家，江南画船如～里。”</li><li>车盖，覆盖物：黄～（古代帝王所乘车上以黄缯为里的车盖，亦指帝王车）。～社（王朝倾覆的代称）。</li><li>古代井田的区划，一屋合三百亩。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">屋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>屋</strong> <span class="dicpy">wū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (屋的本义是幄，后来<span class="yhcs">“屋”</span>指房屋，另造<span class="yhcs">“幄”</span>字。会意。从尸，从至。尸，与房屋有关，<span class="yhcs">“至”</span>表示来到。人来到这里居住之意。本义:帐幕。<span class="yhcs">“幄”</span>的本字)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屋，居也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐灏</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“古宫室无屋名。古之所谓屋，非今之所谓屋也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尚不愧于屋漏。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“屋，小帐也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">去帝制黄屋左纛。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·南粤王佗传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">去黄屋称制。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·陆贾传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“黄屋，谓车上盖也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:屋幕(帐幕);屋漏(古代室内西北隅施设小帐，安插神主，为人所不见的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代半地穴式住室建筑的顶部覆盖 <span class="encs">[shelter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是故十围之木，持千钧之屋。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 房舍，房屋 <span class="encs">[house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以二人居屋十间。——</span><span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卷我屋上三重茅。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:屋山(屋脊);屋山头(堂屋两端的房檐);屋桷(房屋上的椽子);屋里巷(家里);屋除(阶下，房舍前的台阶);屋庑(房子的顶部);屋头(房屋的上面);屋庐(泛指居室);屋盖(屋顶);屋壁(房屋的夹墙);屋基(房屋的地基);屋山头(屋山。指屋脊或山墙);屋上乌(屋上的乌鸦。指推爱之所及)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 房间<span class="encs">[room]</span>。如:里屋;外屋</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 房顶 <span class="encs">[roof]</span>。如:屋上架屋;屋下架屋</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 室深处 <span class="encs">[inner room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屋，奥也。其中温奥也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 车盖，车篷子 <span class="encs">[canvas top on vehicle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">纪信</span>乘黄屋车。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>屋</strong> <span class="dicpy">wū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button
