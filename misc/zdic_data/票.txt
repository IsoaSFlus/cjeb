                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>票</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pi&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>纸币，通货：钞～。～子。～额。</li>
	<li>印的或写的凭证：～据。～证。股～。车～。选～。</li>
	<li>非职业演戏：～戏。～友。</li>
	<li>量词，相当于&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">批</span>&rdquo;：一～货物。</li>
	<li>被匪绑架做抵押的人：绑～儿。撕～儿。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>票</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">piāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/飘">飘</a>&rdquo;，随风摆动飞扬。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">票</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>票</strong> <span class="dicpy">piāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。本义: 火飞)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[blazing fire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见票如累明。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;沈》</span>。注:&ldquo;飞光也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;飘&rdquo;。轻举的样子 <span class="encs">[act rashly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">票然逝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;礼乐志》</span>。注:&ldquo;轻举意。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:票票(轻浮); 票然(轻举的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>票</strong> <span class="dicpy">piāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 摇动 <span class="encs">[wave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">票昆仑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传下》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹言摇动也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如: 票忽(飘忽); 票轻(飘摇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">pi&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>票</strong> <span class="dicpy">pi&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 迅疾;轻捷 <span class="encs">[fast;agile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为票姚校尉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;霍去病传》</span>。注:&ldquo;劲疾貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">校武票禽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:票勇(行动迅疾勇猛);票健(敏捷强劲);票禽(轻疾的飞禽);票贼(迅猛的盗贼);票轻(轻疾)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 勇猛 <span class="encs">[bold and powerful]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:票帅(勇猛的首领);票将(勇猛的将领);票雄(勇猛强劲者)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
