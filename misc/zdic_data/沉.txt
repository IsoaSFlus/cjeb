                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>沉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄣˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>没（<span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span>）入水中，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">浮</span>&rdquo;相对：～没。～渣。～浮（喻盛衰消长）。石～大海。～鱼落雁。静影～璧。</li>	<li>落下，陷入：～陷。</li>	<li>重量大：～重。</li>	<li>慎重，不轻浮：～着（<span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;</span>）。～毅。</li>	<li>深切长久，程度深：～思。～滞。～吟。～默。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">沉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>沉</strong></p>
<p>沈 <span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，冘( <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;n</span>)声。甲骨文字形，中间是牛，周围是水，表示把牛沉到水中。商代祭祀用牲的方法。本义:没入水中)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[sink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沈，没也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以狸沈祭山林川泽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宗伯》</span>。注:&ldquo;川泽曰沈。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泛泛杨舟，载沉载浮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;箐箐者莪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皓月千里，浮光跃金，静影沉璧，渔歌互答，此乐何极!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山河破碎风飘絮，身世浮沉雨打萍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《过零丁洋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:石沉大海;沉溺(沉没水中)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 埋没 <span class="encs">[cover up;bury]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:沉沦(埋没);沉压(沉抑;埋没)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 投水 <span class="encs">[drown oneself in]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:沉河(投河自尽);沉湘(指屈原沉入湘江支流汨罗江自尽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代祭川泽曰沉。因向水中投祭品故名 <span class="encs">[throw into river]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:沉祠(古代祭水神之礼仪);沉辜(古代宰牲以祭川)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 沉迷，沉溺 <span class="encs">[indulge;be given to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡人不祥，被于宗庙之祟，沉于谄谀之臣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">细腰沉</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>女。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">杨</span><span class="diczx2">敬之</span><span class="smcs">《客思吟》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我用沈酗于酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;微子》</span>。传:&ldquo;沈，湎也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沈酒冒色。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;秦誓上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沈而乐者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《法言&middot;寡见》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:沉饮(沉迷于饮酒);沉酣(沉酒而酣适);沉乱(沉迷昏乱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 隐伏;隐没 <span class="encs">[lie concealed;lie low]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:沉伏(隐伏);沉志(谓潜隐志向);沉汉(隐没);沉祟(隐伏的灾祸);沉晦(隐而不露);沉恶(隐伏的罪恶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 降落、坠落 <span class="encs">[fall;sink]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:沉陨(坠落);沉坠(坠落);沉埃(落下的尘埃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 沉沦;沦落 <span class="encs">[sink into]</span>。 如:沉人(沉沦在下的贤人);沉顿(沉沦顿踬)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 忍住，压制 <span class="encs">[restrain;keep down]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:沉住气;沉下心来</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">沒入水中。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「沉沒」、「石沉大海」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;小雅&middot;菁菁者莪》：「汎汎楊舟，載沉載浮。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">往下降落。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「地基下<b>沉</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;辛棄疾〈生查子&middot;悠悠萬世功〉詞：「紅日又西<b>沉</b>，白浪長東去。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">埋沒、淪落。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;左思&middot;詠史詩八首之二》：「世胄躡高位，英俊<b>沉</b>下僚。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李商隱〈戊辰會靜中出貽同志二十韻〉：「中迷鬼道樂，<b>沉</b>為下土民。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">使降下。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「沉不住氣」、「沉下臉來」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">迷戀、迷於所好。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;微子》：「我用<b>沉</b>酗于酒，用亂敗厥德于下。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《戰國策&middot;趙策二》：「常民溺於習俗，學者<b>沉</b>於所聞。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">潛藏、隱伏不外露。</span><span class="gc_yy">《國語&middot;周語下》：「氣不沉滯，而亦不散越。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國吳&middot;韋昭&middot;注：「<b>沉</b>，伏也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;陸機&middot;漢高祖功臣頌》：「<b>沉</b>跡中鄉，飛名帝錄。」</span></p></li>
