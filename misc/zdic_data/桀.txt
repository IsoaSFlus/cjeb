                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">桀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>桀</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 鸡栖的木桩 <span class="encs">[wood pile on which chickens perch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡栖于桀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;君子于役》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 小木桩 <span class="encs">[small wood pile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">城希裾门而直桀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;杰&rdquo;。杰出的人才 <span class="encs">[find;outstanding person]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">选士厉兵，简练桀俊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:桀俊(才能出众的人);桀士(杰出的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 夏朝末代君主，相传是个暴君 <span class="encs">[Jie， the name of the last ruler of the Xia Dynasty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:桀纣(夏桀和商纣，历史上有名的两个暴君)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>桀</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;傑&rdquo;。特出，高出 <span class="encs">[tall;high]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邦之桀兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;伯兮》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">初之列士桀大夫，慎言知行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;非命中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与其桀相而谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;耕柱》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此小人之桀雄也，不可不诛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;宥坐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人主贤则豪桀归之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;功名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杰然特起。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气杰旺。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:桀异(特出优异);桀立(耸立);桀出(突然出，高出);桀木(高大的树)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 凶悍，横暴 <span class="encs">[fierce and brutal]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:桀虏(凶暴的贱寇);桀逆(凶狠暴逆);桀傲(凶暴骄横);桀黠(凶悍狡猾);桀横(凶暴蛮横)</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">桀</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>桀</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧㄝˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>ji&eacute; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3160.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">雞棲息的小木樁。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;王風&middot;君子于役》：「雞棲于<b>桀</b>，日之夕矣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">才能俊異特出的人。</span><span class="gc_yy">《呂氏春秋&middot;仲春紀&middot;功名》：「人主賢則豪<b>桀</b>歸之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝宋&middot;劉義慶《世說新語&middot;賞譽》：「會稽孔沈、魏顗、虞球、虞存、謝奉，並是四族之俊，于時之<b>桀</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「傑」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人名。夏朝最末的君王，非常凶狠殘暴。參見「<a href='/hans/夏桀' target=_blank>夏桀</a> 」條。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋時楚國有桀溺。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">凶暴。</span><span class="gc_yy">《韓非子&middot;亡徵》：「官吏弱而人民<b>桀</b>，如此則國躁。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《清史稿&middot;卷四九三&middot;忠義傳七&middot;孫家泰傳》：「而鳳臺苗沛霖所部，尤橫<b>桀</b>不可制。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">擔荷、負舉。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;成公二年》：「齊高固入晉師，<b>桀</b>石以投人。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;杜預&middot;注：「<b>桀</b>，擔也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
