                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>见</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">見</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>看到：看～。罕～。～微知著。～义勇为。～异思迁。</li>
	<li>接触，遇到：怕～风。～习。</li>
	<li>看得出，显得出：～效。相形～绌。</li>
	<li>（文字等）出现在某处，可参考：～上。～下。</li>
	<li>会晤：会～。接～。</li>
	<li>对事物观察、认识、理解：～解。～地（见解）。～仁～智（指对同一问题各人从不同角度持不同看法）。</li>
	<li>助词，表示被动或对我如何：～外。～教。～谅（原谅我）。～笑（被讥笑）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>见</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">見</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/现">现</a>&rdquo;，出现，显露。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/现">现</a>&rdquo;，现存。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">见</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>见</strong></p>
<p>見 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面是&ldquo;目&rdquo;，下面是&ldquo;人&rdquo;。在人的头上加只眼睛，就是为了突出眼睛的作用。本义:看见，看到)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[see;catch sight of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见，视也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未见君子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;汝坟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利见大人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;乾》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见龙在田。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;乾》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视而不见，听而不闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见而不见，闻而不闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;主道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昨夜见军帖，可汗大点兵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见两小儿辩斗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儿童相见不相识。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贺知章</span><span class="smcs">《回乡偶书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虎见之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">歧王</span>宅里寻常见。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江南逢李龟年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:见雌雄(看到结果);见人(见证人);见喜(出痘疹的忌讳说法);见不过(看不惯;见不得);见头知尾(比喻聪明透顶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 进见;会见 <span class="encs">[meet;call on]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/898B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【廣韻】古甸切【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】【正韻】經電切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>堅去聲。【說文】視也。从目从儿。【易&middot;乾卦】飛龍在天，利見大人。【疏】德被天下，爲萬物所瞻覩。【書&middot;立政】灼見三有俊心。【禮&middot;王制】問百年者就見之。【周禮&middot;春官】大宗伯以賔禮親邦國，春見曰朝，夏見曰宗，秋見曰覲，冬見曰遇，時見曰會，殷見曰同。【註】此六禮者，以諸侯見王爲義。【史記&middot;五帝紀】舜擇吉月日，見四嶽、諸牧、班瑞。【前漢&middot;東方朔傳】未得省見。【註】言不爲所拔識也。</p>
<p>又【通鑑】漢武帝元光五年，張湯、趙禹定律令，務在深文，作見知法。詳矢部知字註。</p>
<p>又姓。出【姓苑】。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】胡甸切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】形甸切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>賢去聲。【廣韻】露也。【易&middot;乾卦】見龍在田。【疏】陽氣發見，故曰見龍。【儀禮&middot;士相見禮】某也，願見無由達。【註】凡<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>于尊曰見，敵而曰見，謙敬之辭也。【史記&middot;淮隂侯傳】情見勢屈。【師古曰】見，顯露也。</p>
<p>又薦達也。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】齊豹見宗魯於公孟。【註】見，薦達也。謂爲之紹介，猶論語云：從者見之也。</p>
<p>又見在也。【史記&middot;項羽紀】軍無見糧。【註】無見在之糧。【前漢&middot;高五王傳】文帝封悼惠王子列侯見在者六人爲王。【集韻】俗作現。</p>
<p>又【集韻】日朝也。【詩&middot;小雅】見睍曰消。【箋】韓詩作曣，云：見，日出也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】居莧切，音襇。棺衣也。【禮&middot;雜記】實見閒，而後折入。【疏】一解云：鄭合見閒二字共爲覸，苦辯反。【集韻】或作梘。</p>
<p>又雜也。【禮&middot;祭義】建設朝事，燔燎羶薌，見以蕭光。【註】見當爲覸，音閒厠之閒。孔穎達云：覵，謂雜也。</p>
