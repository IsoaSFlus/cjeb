						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>承</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄥˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>在下面接受，托着：～重。～受。</li>
	<li>担当，应允：～担。～当。～包。～做。～认。</li>
	<li>受到，蒙受：～蒙。～恩（蒙受恩泽）。</li>
	<li>继续，接连：继～。～平（指社会比较持久安定的局面）。～前启后。</li>
	<li>顺从，迎合：奉～。～颜候色（顺着别人颜色办事）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">承</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>承</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面象跽跪着的人，下面象两只手。合起来表示人被双手捧着或接着。本义:捧着)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="encs">[hold in the palm;support with the palm;bear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">承，奉也。受也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女承筐无实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;归妹》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;自下受上称承。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">承筐是将。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鹿鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">下有红光动摇承之，或曰，此东海也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外有八龙，首衔铜丸，下有蟾蜍，张口承之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:承印(捧印);承萼(指物件承托的联结部分);承盘(托盘)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 继承 <span class="encs">[succeed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朱明未承夜兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span>。注:&ldquo;续也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">承致多福无疆于女孝孙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;少牢礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹传也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾本寒家，世以清白相承。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:承袭(继承);承家(承继家业);承授(承继传受)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 接受;承受 <span class="encs">[receive;accept;bear;suffer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若不足而不承。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;大宗师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士于大夫承贺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:承派(接受委派);承教(谦词。接受教诲);承做(接受加工);承命(受命)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 奉承 <span class="encs">[flatter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:承附(奉承附和);承旨(逢迎意旨);承志(迎合意旨;继承大志)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 承担，担负;担当 <span class="encs">[undertake]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:承造(负责建造);承局(包办各项事务的人;衙门中的差人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 秉承 <span class="encs">[act on]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:承制(奉旨);承荷(秉承);承运(秉受天命)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 招认 <span class="encs">[confess one&#39;s crime]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:承招(认罪招供);承抵(认罪抵命);承伏(认罪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 侍奉 <span class="encs">[attend]</span>。</p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              承·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/627F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】署陵切【集韻】【韻會】辰陵切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音丞。【說文】奉也。【書&middot;說命】后克聖，臣不命其承。【詩&middot;小雅】承筐是將。</p>
<p>又受也。【禮&middot;禮運】是謂承天之祜。【疏】受天之福也。</p>
<p>又【增韻】下載上也。【易&middot;坤彖】萬物資生，乃順承天。【尚書&middot;大傳】庶人有石承。【註】屋柱下石也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】次也。【左傳&middot;昭十三年】同盟于平丘，子產爭承。【註】承貢賦之次也。</p>
<p>又繼也。【詩&middot;小雅】如松柏之茂，無不爾或承。【疏】新故相承，無彫落也。</p>
<p>又止也。【詩&middot;魯頌】則莫我敢承。【疏】無有於我敢禦止之者。</p>
<p>又通丞。【左傳&middot;哀十八年】楚右司馬子國帥師而行，請承。【註】承，佐也。</p>
<p>又州名。漢牂柯郡地，宋置承州。</p>
<p>又姓。【後漢&middot;承宮傳註】承姓，衞大夫成叔承之後。</p>
<p>又【正韻】時征切，音成。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】諸仍切，音蒸。水名。【前漢&middot;地理志】長沙國承陽縣。【註】承水，出零陵永昌縣界，東流注湘。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>持陵切，音懲。【左傳&middot;哀四年】蔡昭侯如吳，諸大夫恐其又遷也，承。【註】承，蓋楚言。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>蒸上聲，與抍通。【列子&middot;黃帝篇】孔子觀於呂梁，見一丈夫游之，使弟子<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>流而承之。【註】出溺爲承。直作拯。</p>
<p>又【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>昨亙切，音贈。猶送也。【禮&middot;文王世子】賵賻承含，皆有正焉。【註】承讀爲贈，聲之誤也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>諸應切，音證。縣名。【前漢&middot;地理志】東海郡承縣。【註】應劭讀。</p>
<p>又叶辰羊切，音常。【班固&middot;高祖泗水亭<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥓓</span>】休勳顯祚，永永無疆。於皇舊亭，苗嗣是承。　【集韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22ABB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢪻</span>。</p>
<p>又作氶。</p>
