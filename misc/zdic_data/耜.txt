                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">耜</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>耜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">s&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>耒下端铲土的部件。装在犁上，用以翻土。先以木为之，后改用金属。《釋名&bull;釋用器》：&ldquo;耜，似也，似齒之斷物也。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> spade-shaped tool; plow</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Pflug, pflügen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> soc</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">耜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>耜</strong></p>
<p>梩 <span class="dicpy">s&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耒，以声。耒( <span class="dicpy">lěi</span>)，翻土的工具。本义:古代农具名，耒耜的主要部件，似锹) 耒下端铲土的部分，装在犁上，用以翻土 <span class="encs">[spade-like plough]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">字亦作耜。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耜，来下剌臿也。古以木为之，后世以金。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《六书故》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斵木为耜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>亲自操耜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者剡耜而耕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛率》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脩来耜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 民无悬耜，野无奥草。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语中》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">韦昭</span>注:&ldquo;入土曰耜，耜柄曰耒。&rdquo;</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>耜</strong> <span class="dicpy">s&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>引申为以耜铲土 <span class="encs">[spade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薙氏掌杀草&hellip;冬日至而耜之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;薙氏》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中" target="_blank">未集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中&amp;bs=耒" target="_blank">耒</a>】
                              耜·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/801C.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】詳里切【集韻】象齒切【韻會】序姊切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音似。【說文】臿也。本作㭒，今文作耜。【玉篇】耒端木。【廣韻】耒耜，世本曰：倕作耜。古史考曰：神農作耜。【易&middot;繫辭】斵木爲耜。【詩&middot;小雅】以我覃耜，俶載南畝。【禮&middot;月令】季冬之月，命農計耦耕事，修耒耜。【註】耜者，耒之金也。廣五寸。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;匠人】耜廣五寸，二耜爲耦。【疏】耜謂耒頭金，金廣五寸。【釋名】耜，似也，似齒之斷物也。　【說文】或作梩。【集韻】亦或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/264E8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦓨</span>、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233CC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣏌</span>、䎣，又作枱、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28420.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨐠</span>。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枱</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“耜”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/枱">枱</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷六">卷六</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷六&amp;swbs=木">木</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/67B1.svg">                          <p>耒耑也。从木台聲。鈶，或从金。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28420.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨐠</span>，籒文从辝。弋之切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/67B1_2708.svg" /><span class="swbf">(枱)</span><span class="ywyy">耒耑也。</span><span class="swdz">枱，今經典之耜。耒下曰耕曲木也，此云耒耑也，與京房耜耒下耓也，耒耜上句上也，相合。曲木，考工記所謂上句者二尺有二寸也。耒耑，所謂庛長尺有一寸也。許意上曰耒，下曰枱，皆謂木材。錔於庛之金是曰耕曰<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/246FF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤛿</span>。鄭注周禮則云：耒爲木，枱爲金。故云古者耜一金，兩人倂發之。又云庛讀爲刺。刺，耒下前曲接耜。又注月令：於孟春云耒，耜之上曲也。於季冬云耜，耒之金也。說與許異。鄭本匠人耜廣五寸而云。許則謂記文枱<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/246FF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤛿</span>不分，渾言之。記云耜廣五寸者謂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/246FF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤛿</span>也，故於耦下易之云耕廣五寸。</span><span class="ywyy">從木。台聲。</span><span class="swdz">弋之切。按當云詳里切。今俗作耜。鉉本乃以訓臿、訓徙土輂之㭒注云今作耜，則大誤矣。玉篇同誤，後人所改也。一部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/67B1_2709.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鈶)</span><span class="ywyy">或從金台聲。</span><span class="swdz">以其木也，故從木。以其屬於金也，故亦從金。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/67B1_2710.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28420.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨐠</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文從辝。</span><span class="swdz">從木，辝聲也。辝者，籒文辤。</span></p>                          </div>
