						<p>● <strong>厨</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">廚、㕑</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>做饭菜的场所：～房。下～。</li>	<li>厨师：～子。名～。～娘。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>厨</strong></p>
<p>廚 <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，尌( <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span>)声。本义:厨房)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[kitchen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厨，庖屋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>始有厨字，是</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>初名庖， <span class="diczx2">周</span>末名厨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文句读》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厨主食者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以君子远庖厨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饰厨传，俗字误作厨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;宣帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三日入厨下，洗手作羹汤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张籍</span><span class="smcs">《新嫁娘》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厨冷难留乌止屋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《酬梦得贫居咏怀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轩东故尝为厨，人往，从轩前达。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厨下(厨房);厨头(厨房);厨仓(厨房与粮仓);厨帐(账幕式厨房)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 主持烹饪的人 <span class="encs">[head of cooking]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:厨下儿(炊事工);厨人(厨师。又称:厨户，厨司，厨子，厨下儿);厨娘(烹调食物的妇女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 操办官食的官 <span class="encs">[official in charge of mess]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贤母病，长安厨给祠食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;王嘉传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厨吏(厨宰。主持炊事的小吏);厨兵(炊事兵);厨役(担任炊事的仆役)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 菜肴 <span class="encs">[dishs]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫人设厨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《汉武帝内传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厨膳(厨馔，饭菜);厨珍(珍贵美味的食品);厨车(运食物的车)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>厨</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 烹饪;烹调 <span class="encs">[cooking]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竹里行厨，花下提壶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">曹德</span><span class="smcs">《折桂令》</span></p>
