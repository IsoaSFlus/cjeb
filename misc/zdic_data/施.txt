                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>施</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>实行：～工。～政。设～。～展。～教（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o </span>）。～为（<span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span>）。～威。实～。措～（办法）。发号～令。</li>	<li>用上，加工：～肥。～粉。</li>	<li>给予：～礼。～诊。～恩。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">施</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>施</strong> <span class="dicpy">shī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从㫃( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，也声。本指旗帜)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 旗飘动 <span class="encs">[wave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旖施，柔顺摇曳之貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>通训定声</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施，旗貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旖旎(施)从风。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如传》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;犹阿那也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;吔&rdquo;。给，给予。引申为施舍 <span class="encs">[give]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施，予也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德施普也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;乾》</span>。释文:&ldquo;与也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施其功事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;内宰》</span>。注:&ldquo;赋也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐侯</span>好示务施。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;惠也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旅有施舍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王施舍不倦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">留待作遗施，于今无会图。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:施予(给，给别人恩惠、财物);施生(施惠于人，给人生路);施人(施恩于人);施香(施舍香火钱);施食(施舍食物);施报(有所施与，则有所报答);施赈(施舍财物救济贫民或灾民);施遣(施舍赠送);施泽(给予恩惠);乐善好施</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 设置;安放 <span class="encs">[set up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施于中林。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;兔罝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施三川而归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;韩世家》</span>。正义:&ldquo;施犹设也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>政不施。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;蒯通传》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;施，设也，立也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中有都柱，傍行八道，施关发机。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于厅事之东北角，施八尺屏障。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:施髟(安上假发);施灵(设置灵堂);施关(设立关卡);施置(处置;安排)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 施行;实行;推行 <span class="encs">[put into force]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施于有政。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;为政》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">包</span>注:&ldquo;行也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仁义不施而攻守之势异也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时北兵已迫修门外，战、守、迁皆不及施。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今王发政施仁，使天下仕者皆立于王之朝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:施为(施展;作为);施巧(施展巧技);施令，施命(施行政令);施张(施行);施呈(施展);施敬(施行庄敬之教)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 散布;铺陈 <span class="encs">[spread]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云行雨施。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周易&middot;乾卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阴谢阳施。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《闻居赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;布也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:施布(散布;传布);施散(布施散发);施属(散布连缀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 加;施加 <span class="encs">[apply]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">己所不欲，勿施于人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;卫灵公》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苟人事不施于其间，则莽莽榛榛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">面如敷粉，唇若施脂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:施肥;施朱(涂以红色);施粉(涂粉);施检(加印密封)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 判罪 <span class="encs">[convict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施生戮死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十四年》</span>。注:&ldquo;行罪也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:施行(处决，执行死刑;处置，发落)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 陈尸示众 <span class="encs">[display corpse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">文公</span>入， <span class="diczx2">秦</span>人杀 <span class="diczx2">冀芮</span>而施之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子不施其亲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为大戮施之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 用;运用 <span class="encs">[use]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施则行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span>。疏:&ldquo;用也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爪牙之士施。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;臣道》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓展其材也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妾不堪驱使，徒留无所施。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">靡计不施，迄无济。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">未报</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>施，而伐其师，其为死君乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二&mdash;&mdash;三十三年》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx2">楼烦</span>以星施。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《逸周书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓。施琅 <span class="dicpy">Shī L&aacute;ng</span> <span class="encs">[Shi Lang]</span> (1621&mdash;1696) 中国清朝时将领，福建晋江人。字尊侯，号琢公。初为郑芝龙部将，降清后任水师提督。康熙二十二年(1683年)攻灭台湾的郑氏政权。他建议在台湾驻兵屯守，以备御西方殖民者的侵略，为清政府所采纳。封靖海侯</p>

class="k_txt">𢒃</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/201DC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠇜</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20912.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤒</span>【唐韻】式支切【集韻】【韻會】商支切【正韻】申支切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音詩。【說文】旗貌。齊樂施字子旗，知施者旗也。【註】徐鍇曰：旗之逶迤。一曰設也。【書&middot;益稷】以五采彰施于五色。</p><p>又【詩&middot;邶風】得此戚施【傳】戚施，不能仰者。【箋】戚施，面柔下人以色，故不能仰也。</p><p>又【詩&middot;王風】將其來施施。【傳】施施，難進之意。【箋】施施，舒行伺閒，獨來見已之貌。【釋文】施如字。【孟子】施施從外來。【趙岐註】施施，猶扁扁，喜悅之貌。【音義】丁如字，張音怡。</p><p>又【周禮&middot;天官&middot;內宰】施其功事。【註】施，猶賦也。</p><p>又【禮&middot;祭統】施于蒸彝鼎。【註】施，猶著也。</p><p>又【晉語】秦人殺冀芮而施之。【註】施，<img data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>施智切，音翅。惠也，與也。【易&middot;乾卦】德施普也。【又】雲行雨施。【禮&middot;曲禮】太上貴德，其次務施報。【左傳&middot;僖二十四年】報者倦矣，施者未厭。【註】施，功勞也。【增韻】凡施設之施，平聲。施與之施，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>平去通押。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>以豉切，音易。【詩&middot;周南】葛之覃兮，施于中谷。【傳】施，移也。</p><p>又【詩&middot;大雅】施于孫子。【箋】施，猶易也，延也。</p><p>又【儀禮&middot;喪服】絕族無施服。【註】在旁而及曰施。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>賞是切，詩上聲。捨也，攺易也。通作弛。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;少宰】治其施舍。【註】施舍，不給役者也。【論語】君子不施其親。【何晏註】施，易也。不以他人之親易己之親。【後漢&middot;光武紀】將衆部施<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>屯北邊。【註】施，讀曰弛。弛，解也。</p><p>又【集韻】余支切，音移。㢮或作施。【史記&middot;衞綰傳】劒人之所施易。【註】施，讀曰移。</p><p>又【史記&middot;賈生傳】庚子日施兮。【註】施，矢遺反。索隱曰：施，猶西斜也。</p><p>又【韻補】叶詩戈切。【漢&middot;高祖戚夫人歌】橫絕四海，又可奈何。雖有矰繳，尚安所施。</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65BD_2490.svg" /><span class="swbf">(施)</span><span class="ywyy">旗旖施也。</span><span class="swdz">大人賦說旌旗曰：又旖旎以招搖。施字俗改爲旎，从尼聲，殊失音理。</span><span class="ywyy">从㫃也聲。</span><span class="swdz">式支切。古音在十七部。按經傳叚此爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22FAD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢾭</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22EF1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢻱</span>字。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22EF1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢻱</span>之形，施之本義俱廢矣。毛傳曰：施，移也。此謂施卽延之叚借。大雅：施于條枚。吕氏春秋、韓詩外傳、新序皆引作延。上林賦：貤丘陵，下平原。司馬彪曰：貤，延也。按貤丘陵與詩施中谷、施條枚同，貤亦延之叚借。</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AFC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠫼</span>欒施字子旗。</span><span class="swdz">見左氏傳。孔子弟子巫馬施亦字子旗。</span><span class="ywyy">知施者旗也。</span><span class="swdz">自叚施爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22EF1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢻱</span>，而施从㫃之意隱矣，故明之。○此二篆各本轉寫譌舛，據全書連綿字通例更正。</span></p>                          </div>
