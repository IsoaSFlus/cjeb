						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>茫</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">máng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/máng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>模糊不清，对事理全无所知：～然。～昧。</li><li>遥远，面积大，看不清边沿：～～。～漠。渺～。</li></ol>
