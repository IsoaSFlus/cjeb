                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">馑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>馑</strong></p>
<p>饉 <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，堇( <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;n</span>)声。本义:蔬菜和野菜都吃不上)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[vegetable failure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">馑，蔬不熟曰馑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可食之菜，皆不熟为馑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释天》</span></span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或纷扰之际，或荒馑之余;威惠所加，罔不和辑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《除李逊京兆尹制》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 谷物欠收 <span class="encs">[crop failure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一谷不收谓之馑，二谷不收谓之旱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;七患》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二谷不升谓之饥，三谷不升谓之馑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;襄公二十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;殣&rdquo;。饿死。饿死的人 <span class="encs">[person starved to death]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫饿馑流隶，饥寒道路，思有短褐之袭，檐石之蓄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;班彪&middot;王命论》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>馑</strong></p>
<p>饉 <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>缺乏 <span class="encs">[lack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多者不独衍，少者不独馑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
