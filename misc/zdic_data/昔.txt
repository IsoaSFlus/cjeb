                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>昔</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>以前，从前：～者。～人。往～。今～对比。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/夕">夕</a>&rdquo;，夜。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> formerly; ancient; in beginning</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> vergangen, vorüber ,Xi  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> passé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>昔</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。象残肉日以晞之，与俎同意。本义:干肉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dried meat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔，干肉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;昨&rdquo;。从前;过去(与&ldquo;今&rdquo;相对) <span class="encs">[former times;the past]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二曰昔酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;酒正》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔之人无闻知。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;无逸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自古在昔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;那》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者，圣王之作易也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔</span><span class="diczx2">栾武子</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔人已乘</span><span class="diczx2">黄鹤</span>去。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔颢</span><span class="smcs">《黄鹤楼》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔自郡城。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抚今追昔;今非昔比;今胜于昔;在昔(过去，从前);昔来(往日以来);昔士(古之贤人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 昨日 <span class="encs">[yesterday]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者疾，今日愈，如之何不吊?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑下》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">赵岐</span>注:&ldquo;昔者，昨天也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 傍晚<span class="encs">[sunset;dusk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是今日适越而昔至也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;齐物论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日入至于星出谓之昔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;庄公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其乐非特朝昔之乐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;楚世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;夕&rdquo;。夜晚<span class="encs">[evening]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">通昔不寐矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔，夜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏四月辛卯昔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;庄公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔昔梦为国君&hellip;&hellip;昔昔梦为人仆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;周穆王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为一昔之期。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公四年》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6614.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>思積切，音惜。【說文】作㫺。乾肉也。从殘肉，日以晞之。與俎同意。【類篇】隷作昔。【五經文字】後人以爲古昔字。【易&bull;說卦】昔者，聖人之作《易》也。【疏】據今而稱上世，謂之昔者也。【詩&bull;商頌】自古在昔，先民有作。【禮&bull;曲禮】必則古昔稱先王。</p><p>又【詩&bull;陳風】誰昔然矣。【傳】昔，久也。【疏】昔是久遠之事。</p><p>又【禮&bull;檀弓】予疇昔之夜。【註】猶前也。</p><p>又【博雅】昔，夜也。【左傳&bull;哀四年】爲一昔之期。【莊子&bull;天運篇】則通昔不寐矣。</p><p>又姓。【廣韻】漢有烏傷令昔登。</p><p>又【集韻】倉各切，音錯。觕也。【周禮&bull;冬官考工記&bull;弓人】老牛之角紾而昔。【註】昔讀爲交錯之錯，謂牛角觕理錯也。</p><p>又【韻補】叶息約切。【左思&bull;詠史詩】當其未遇時，憂在塡溝壑。英雄有迍邅，由來自古昔。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>

												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=日">日</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6614.svg">                          <p>乾肉也。从殘肉，日以晞之。與俎同意。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6614_1.svg" /><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26821.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦠡</span>，籒文从肉。思積切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6614_2538.svg" /><span class="swbf">(昔)</span><span class="ywyy">乾肉也。</span><span class="swdz">周禮腊人：掌乾肉，凡田獸之脯腊膴胖之事。鄭云：大物解肆乾之謂之乾肉，若今涼州烏翅矣。腊，小物全乾者。鄭意大曰乾肉，小曰腊。然官名腊人，則大物亦偁腊也，故許渾言之。</span><span class="ywyy">从殘肉。</span><span class="swdz">謂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2020C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠈌</span>也。象形。</span><span class="ywyy">日㠯晞之。</span><span class="swdz">昨之殘肉，今日晞之，故从日。鄭注腊人云：腊之言夕也。此可證周禮故作昔字，後人改之。㫺者古文，籒文增肉作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2682A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦠪</span>，於義爲短。昔肉必經一夕，故古叚昔爲夕。穀梁經辛卯昔恆星不見，左傳爲一昔之期，列子昔昔夢爲君皆是。又引伸之則叚昔爲昨，又引伸之則以今昔爲今古矣，今古之義盛行而其本義遂廢，凡久謂之昔。周禮：昔酒。鄭云：今之酋久白酒。周語：厚味實腊毒。韋云：腊，亟也。讀若酋昔酒焉。味厚者其毒亟也。韋意久與亟義相成，積之久則發之亟。思積切。古音在五部。</span><span class="ywyy">與俎同意。</span><span class="swdz">爼从半肉，且薦之。㫺从殘肉，日晞之，其作字之恉同也，故曰同意。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6614_2539.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26821.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦠡</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文从肉。</span><span class="swdz">今隷作腊，專用諸脯腊。</span></p>                          </div>
