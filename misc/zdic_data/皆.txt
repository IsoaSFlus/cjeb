                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>皆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄝˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>全，都：～大欢喜。人人～知。放之四海而～准。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> all, every, everybody</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ganz, allesamt, alle, völlig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tout,tous</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">皆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>皆</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从比，从白。从&ldquo;比&rdquo;，有&ldquo;并&rdquo;的意思。本义:都，全)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[all;every]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆，俱词也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百堵皆兴。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆死皆殡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予及汝皆亡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;汤誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">燕</span>、 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>、 <span class="diczx2">韩</span>、 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>闻之，皆朝于 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小人有母，皆尝小人之食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆以美于</span><span class="diczx2">徐公</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆自于人欤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新五代史&middot;伶官传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所在皆是。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆托</span><span class="diczx2">忠烈</span>之名。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">环村居者皆猎户。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾村十里皆平原</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:皆悉(尽;全都);皆既(日月亏蚀全尽);这件事情人人皆知</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;偕&rdquo;;一同 <span class="encs">[together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆行至于阶让。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹并也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆陈殿下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左右皆流涕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宫中皆恸哭。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一时皆下。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">廷中皆大笑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予及女皆亡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;汤誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亡国之主不自以为惑。故与</span><span class="diczx2">桀</span>、 <span class="diczx2">纣</span>、 <span class="diczx2">幽</span>、 <span class="diczx2">厉</span>皆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;离谓》</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">全、都，統括之詞。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「草木皆兵」、「萬般<b>皆</b>下品，唯有讀書高。」、「眾人<b>皆</b>醉，我獨醒。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;告子下》：「人<b>皆</b>可以為堯舜。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈長恨歌〉：「上窮碧落下黃泉，兩處茫茫<b>皆</b>不見。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">並、同。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儀禮&middot;聘禮》：「大夫奉束帛入，三揖<b>皆</b>行，至于階。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「偕」。</span></p></li>

                              皆·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7686.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古諧切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居諧切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音街。【說文】俱詞也。【小爾雅】同也。【易&middot;解卦】雷雨作，而百果草木皆甲坼。鄭康成讀皆如懈，非。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】居之切，音箕。【前漢&middot;孟喜傳】箕子者，萬物方荄茲也。【師古註】荄，音皆。古皆荄與箕音同。</p>
<p>又叶舉里切，音几。【詩&middot;周頌】以治百禮，降福孔皆。【傳】皆，遍也。與偕通。【荀勗東西廂歌】降福孔偕。　【說文】白字兩見，一在自部，自部之白，疾二切，卽自字。皆字載自部中，則應从白。【集韻】或作皆，非。</p>
