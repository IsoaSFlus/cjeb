                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>标</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">標</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">biāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>树木的末端，引申为表面的，非根本的：～本。治～不治本。</li>
	<li>记号：商～。路～。～记。～志。～尺。～语。</li>
	<li>用文字或其他事物表明：～明。～题。～价。～榜（原为揭示、表明；后引申为宣扬、吹嘘）。</li>
	<li>给竞赛优胜者的奖品，亦指优胜：锦～。夺～。</li>
	<li>对一项工程或一批货物，依照一定的标准，提出价目，然后由竞争厂商选择，决定成交与否：投～。招～。</li>
	<li>准的（<span class="dicpy">d&igrave;</span>），榜样：目～。～领（杰出人物）。</li>
	<li>风度，格调：～格。</li>
	<li>中国清末陆军编制的名称，约相当于后来的一个团；亦用作计量军队的单位：一～人马。～下（➊总督、巡抚、提督等称归自己管辖的军队；➋下级武官对长官的自称）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">标</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>标</strong></p>
<p>標 <span class="dicpy">biāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，票声。本义:树梢)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tip of a tree]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">标，木杪末也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大本而小标。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;霸言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:标枝(树梢的枝条);标末(树梢)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 末梢;事物的枝节或表面。与&ldquo;本&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[tip;symptom]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">本标相应。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘安</span><span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此所谓气之标。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问六&middot;微旨大论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:标季(末季，末期);治标;标本(本末。中医称后起为标，本原为本);标病(表面上的病症)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 顶端 <span class="encs">[top]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">标出(塔顶露出)海云长。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《秋日登扬州西灵塔》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:标颠(顶端，最高点)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 柱竿之类 <span class="encs">[pole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">但立直标，终无曲影。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧唐书&middot;崔彦昭传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:标抹(屋梁上的短柱)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤城霞起而建标。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;孙绰&middot;游天台山赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">标之以翠翳。泛之以游菰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;郭璞&middot;江赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡坑陷井穴皆有标。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书&middot;百官志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:路标(交通标志);商标;浮标;标船(挂着标记的船，一般不会被劫掠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 给优胜者的奖品 <span class="encs">[award;prize]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:标首(首先拿出的赏钱);锦标;夺标</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 承包者向发包一方所标出的自己愿出的价钱 <span class="encs">[bid;tender]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:投标;招标;开标</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 旗帜 <span class="encs">[flag]</span></p>
