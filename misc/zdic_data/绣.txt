                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>绣</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">綉</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄡˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用丝线等在绸和布上缀成花纹或文字：～字。～花。刺～。</li>
	<li>绣成的物品：粤～。苏～。湘～。锦～山河。锦心～口（形容文思、词藻都优美、华丽）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>绣</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>綉、繍</p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从糸，肅声。从&ldquo;糸&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)与丝织有关。本义:刺绣) 同本义 <span class="encs">[embroider]</span>。用彩色线在布帛上刺成花、鸟、图案等</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绣，五采备也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按考工记，以言画缋之事。则凡黹与画之五采备者，皆曰绣也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">絺绣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;益稷》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;刺者为绣。&rdquo;按，谓针缕所紩者，其色备五采。紩者，黹字。郑以刺释黹，非以刺释绣也。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妾自绣腰襦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">著我绣夹裙。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝绣夹裙。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灿若图绣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绣幌(乡花的帷幔，窗帘);绣鹄(锦绣的飞禽图梆);绣鹄(绣花的头巾);绣佛(用彩色的丝刺绣成的佛像);绣被(绣有彩色花纹的衾被)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>绣</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>綉</p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 绣花的衣服 <span class="encs">[embroidered clothing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">富贵不归故乡，如衣绣之夜行，谁知之者?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绣文(彩色绣花的丝织品或衣服);绣衣、绣服(用彩线刺绣的丝绸衣服);绣裳(绣衣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 绣花的物品 <span class="encs">[embroidered article]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绣十匹，锦三十匹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;匈奴列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:湘绣;苏绣</p>
<p>◎ <strong>绣</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>綉</p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 绘饰华美的 <span class="encs">[rich and bright colors]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">文绣有常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黼衣绣裳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;终南》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绣毬(用五色丝绸扎成的球状物);绣错(色彩错杂如绣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 华丽，精致如绣 <span class="encs">[gorgeous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绣羽衔花他自得，红颜骑行我无缘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《草堂诗笺》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骈四俪六，锦心绣口。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《乞巧文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绣口锦心(形容辞句华丽、文思优美);绣阁琼楼(精美秀丽的楼舍);绣闼(雕绘华美的宫中小门);绣草(沿阶草)</span></p>
