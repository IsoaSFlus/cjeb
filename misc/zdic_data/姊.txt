<p>● <strong>姊</strong></p>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>姊</strong></p>
<p>姉 <span class="dicpy">zǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:姐姐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[elder sister]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">姊，女兄也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男子谓女子先生为姊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂及伯姊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;泉水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阿姊闻妹来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小弟闻姊来。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:姊丈(姐姐的丈夫);姊夫(姐姐的丈夫);姊弟(姐姐和妹妹或姐姐和弟弟);姊儿(对妓女的称呼)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指母亲 <span class="encs">[mother]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如今之所谓者，若</span><span class="diczx2">中州</span>名 <span class="diczx2">汉</span>， <span class="diczx2">关</span>右称 <span class="diczx2">羌</span>，易臣以奴，呼母云姊。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">刘知几</span><span class="smcs">《史通&middot;杂说中》</span></p>
