                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>矢</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>箭：流～。弓～。有的（<span class="dicpy">d&igrave;</span>）放～。～镞。</li>
	<li>誓：～志不渝（发誓立志，永不改变）。</li>
	<li>正直：～言（正直的言论）。</li>
	<li>陈列：&ldquo;公～鱼于棠&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>施布：&ldquo;～其文德&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古代投壶（一种娱乐活动）用的筹。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/屎">屎</a>&rdquo;，粪便。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">矢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>矢</strong> <span class="dicpy">shǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形。象镝括羽之形。本义:箭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。以竹为箭，以木为矢 <span class="encs">[arrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矢又谓之箭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矢，弓弩矢也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌六弓、四弩、八矢法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司弓矢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矢，箭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗑得金矢，解得黄矢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;噬》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾如锥矢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侍投则拥矢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;少仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既挟我矢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;吉日》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矢以柘若棘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;投壶》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>无亡矢遗镞之费，而天下诸侯已困矣。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入于太亩，还矢先王。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新五代史&middot;伶官传&middot;序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:飞矢;矢石(箭和礧石。古代守城的武器);矢人(造箭的工匠);矢如雨下(箭像雨一样射来。极言其多);矢房(箭袋);矢书(战争中用箭射至敌方的文字);矢干(箭杆);矢锋(箭的尖端);矢镝;矢镞(箭头);矢刃(箭和刀。泛指兵器)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代投壶用的筹码 <span class="encs">[counter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主人奉矢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;投壶》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:矢跃(谓投壶时筹从壶中跳了出来。亦泛指投壶活动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 几何学名词。弧弦之半径 <span class="encs">[diameter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">值弧与弦之半曰矢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">戴震</span><span class="smcs">《句股割圜记上》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;屎&rdquo;。人或动物排出的粪便 <span class="encs">[excrement]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀而埋之马矢之中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以筐盛矢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;人间世》</span></span></p>

<p>又嚆矢，響箭也。【莊子&middot;在宥篇】焉知曾史之不爲桀紂嚆史也。一作嗃矢。黃庭堅曰：安能爲人作嗃矢。【註】射者必先以嗃矢定其遠近也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也。【虞書序】臯陶矢闕謨。【春秋&middot;隱五年】公矢魚于棠。【詩&middot;大雅】無矢我陵。【傳】矢，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也。【箋】猶當也。【正義】矢實<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>義，欲言威武之盛，敵不敢當，以其當侵而<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>，故言矢猶當也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】誓也。【書&middot;盤庚】出矢言。【詩&middot;衞風】永矢勿諼。</p>
<p>又【博雅】正也。直也。【易&middot;解卦】得黃矢貞吉。【詩&middot;小雅】其直如矢。</p>
<p>又施也。【詩&middot;大雅】矢其文德。</p>
<p>又投壷之籌曰矢。【禮&middot;投壷】主人奉矢。</p>
<p>又星名。【史記&middot;天官書】枉矢，類大流星，虵行而蒼黑，望之如有毛羽然。</p>
<p>又【釋名】齊魯謂光景爲枉矢，言其光行若射矢之所至也。</p>
<p>又鉗矢，蓬矢，諸羌州名。見【唐書&middot;地理志】。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】屎本作矢。【左傳&middot;文十八年】埋之馬矢之中。【史記&middot;廉頗傳】一飯三遺矢。</p>
<p>又複姓。【前漢&middot;馬宮傳】本姓馬矢，宮仕學，稱馬氏云。　【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B18.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬘</span>。</p>
