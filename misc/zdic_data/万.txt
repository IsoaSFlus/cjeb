                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">万</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>万</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">萬</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">w&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄢˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>数目，十个一千：～户侯（中国汉代侯爵的最高一级，享有万户农民的赋税。后泛指高官）。</li>	<li>喻极多：～物。～方（①指全国和世界各地；②指姿态多种多样）。日理～机。气象～千。</li>	<li>极，很，绝对：～～。～幸。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>万</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">m&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄛˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～俟（q&iacute;）〕原为中国古代鲜卑族部落名；后为复姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>ten thousand; innumerable</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Wan  (Eig, Fam)​,zehntausend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dix mille,absolument,un grand nombre de,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">万</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>万</strong></p>
<p>萬 <span class="dicpy">w&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。&ldquo;萬&rdquo;，甲骨文呈蝎子形。本义:蝎)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 虫名。蝎。<span class="encs">[scorpion]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万，虫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="encs">[数词]</span>∶千的十倍;十个一千 <span class="encs">[ten thousand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今日俸钱过十万，与君营奠复营斋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《遣悲怀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:万寿(称皇帝、皇后的生日);万劫(即万世。形容极长的时间);万井(古代以地方一里为一井，万井即一万平方里);万夫(万人);万户(一万户);万户侯(食邑万户之侯);万顷(百万亩。百亩为一顷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代的一种舞名 <span class="encs">[the name of an ancient dance]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:万舞(古代的舞名。先是武舞，舞者手拿兵器;后是文舞，舞者手拿鸟羽和乐器。亦泛指舞蹈)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Wan prefecture]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 南朝梁大同二年置。治所在石城(今四川省达县)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 唐贞观八年改浦州置。治所在南浦(今四川省万县市)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 明洪武元年置。治所在万安(今广东省万宁县)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 县名 <span class="encs">[Wan county]</span>。在四川省东部长江北岸。三国吴置羊渠县，蜀汉改南浦县，元入万州，明改万县</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 倍 <span class="encs">[fold]</span></p>
