/></p><p>◎ <strong>鬲</strong> <span class="dicpy">gé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“隔”</span>。阻隔 <span class="encs">[separate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鬲闭门户，毋得擅上。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志中》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">别异蛮夷，鬲绝器物。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·南越王赵佗传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
