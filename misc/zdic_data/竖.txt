                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">竖</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>竖</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">竪</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>直立，直立的，与“<span class="diczx3">横</span>”相对：～立。～井（一种垂直的矿井）。～琴。～起耳朵听。</li><li>汉字笔形之一，自上往下。</li><li>上下的或前后的方向，与“<span class="diczx3">横</span>”相对：～着写。</li><li>旧称未成年的童仆，小臣，引申为卑贱的：童～。～子（ａ．童仆；ｂ．鄙贱的称呼，如“～～不足与谋”。亦称“竖小子”）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> perpendicular, vertical; erect</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> errichten ,senkrecht, Vertikale  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ériger,dresser,vertical</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">竖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>竖</strong></p><p>竪、豎 <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="diczx1">竖，竖立也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">竖，立也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">槐树自拔倒竖。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·灵帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">(</span><span class="diczx2">佛印</span>)卧右膝，诎右臂支船，而竖其左膝。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>有执长竿入城门者，初竖执之，不可入。—— <span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林·长竿入城》</span></p><p>又如:竖柜(一种直立的较高的柜子，前面开门，有的装有隔板或若干抽屉，多用来存放衣物等);竖一个目标来向它射击</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>竖</strong></p><p>竪 <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 纵。与<span class="yhcs">“横”</span>相反 <span class="encs">[longitudinal;vertical]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">緅色斜临，霞文横竖。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁简文帝</span><span class="smcs">《明月山铭》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:竖井</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 短小 <span class="encs">[short]</span>。如:竖褐(贫民所穿的短窄粗衣)</p><p>◎ <strong>竖</strong></p><p>竪 <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 童仆 <span class="encs">[young servant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">杨子</span>之邻人亡羊，既率其党，又请 <span class="diczx2">杨子</span>之竖追之。——<span class="smcs">《列子·说符》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:竖人(童仆)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 宫中供役使的小臣 <span class="encs">[petty officers in the palace]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>侯之竖 <span class="diczx2">头须</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十四年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:竖臣(小臣);竖宦(宦官)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 书法的直笔画，从上一直向下 <span class="encs">[vertical stroke in Chinese character]</span></p><p><span 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=豆" target="_blank">豆</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8C4E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】臣庾切【集韻】【韻會】上主切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音裋。【說文】豎立也。【徐曰】豆器，故爲豎立。【後漢·靈帝紀】槐樹自拔倒豎。【魏志·鍾繇傳】起偃爲豎。</p><p>又【韻會】貞也。【字彙】直也。</p><p>又【廣韻】童僕之未冠者。【列子·說符篇】鄰人亡羊，請楊子之豎追之。【宋書·周朗傳】婢豎無定科。</p><p>又內廷之小臣也。【周禮·天官】內豎掌內外之通令，凡小事。【註】豎，未冠者之官名。【左傳·僖二十四年】晉侯之豎頭須，守藏者也。【註】豎，左右小吏。</p><p>又凡<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤰞</span>鄙者皆曰豎。【史記·留侯世家】豎儒幾敗乃公事。【晉書·阮籍傳】時無英雄，使豎子成名。</p><p>又姓。【左傳·昭十六年】鄭大夫豎柎。</p><p>又【韻會】通作裋。【史記·秦始皇紀】寒者利裋褐。【註】一作短，一作豎。謂褐衣豎裁爲勞役之衣，短而且狹，故謂之短褐，亦曰豎褐。【荀子·大略篇】衣則豎褐不完。【註】僮豎之褐，亦短褐也。【集韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/277AB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧞫</span>。</p><p>又【正韻】【字彙補】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>殊遇切，音樹。【通鑑】龎涓曰：遂成豎子之名。胡三省讀去聲。　【集韻】籀作䝂。或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2040A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠐊</span>。俗作竪，非。</p>
