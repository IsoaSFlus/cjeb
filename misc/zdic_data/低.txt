                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>低</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>地势或位置在一般标准或平均程度之下，与“<span class="diczx3">高</span>”相对：～空。～地。～谷。～潮。～沉（ａ．云层厚而低；ｂ．声音低；ｃ．情绪低落）。</li>    <li>矮短：身材～矮。</li>    <li>细小，沉重：～微（ａ．声音细小；ｂ．身份或地位低）。～吟。</li>    <li>程度差：～级。～能。眼高手～。</li>    <li>卑贱：～贱。～首下心。</li>    <li>等级在下的：～俗。～档商品。</li>    <li>价钱少：～价出售。</li>    <li>俯，头向下垂：～头从事。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">低</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>低</strong> <span class="dicpy">dī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，氐( <span class="dicpy">dǐ</span>)声。本义:下，与<span class="yhcs">“高”</span>相对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[low]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 向上伸展的长度相对较小的</p><p><span class="diczx1">风吹草低见牛羊。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·杂歌谣辞·敕勒歌》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:低水位;低仰(忽高忽低);低小(低矮窄小);低狭(低矮狭小)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 程度或强度处于正常情况以下的。如:低烧;低大气压</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 声音细微的。如:低声说话;低低(低声，轻声)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 比例、数量或价值在正常标准以下的。如:低收入阶层;低价;低微(低廉;低下)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 接近赤道的低纬度的。如:低北纬地区</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 低下。指身分地位、才能见识等。如:身分低;低搭(卑劣;低贱)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 质量差 <span class="encs">[inferior]</span>。如:低货;低档(等级低的)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 坏，恶劣 <span class="encs">[bad]</span>。如:低人(坏人，品质恶劣的人);低心(坏心肠)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 等级在下的 <span class="encs">[low-grade]</span>。如:低等;低年级</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>低</strong> <span class="dicpy">dī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 向下;向下垂 <span class="encs">[let droop;hang down]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">低，低昂。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">据轼低头，不能出气。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·盗跖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">封君皆低首仰给。——<span class="smcs">《史记·平淮书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举头望明月，低头思故乡。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《静夜思》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:低忖(低头思量);低腰(弯着腰);低眉(眼向下看);低首(低颜，低簪。低着头);低首下心(形容屈从恭顺的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 使向下，放低 <span                     <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">低</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>低</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄉㄧ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>dī <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1127.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">上下距离小，或离地面近。与「高」相对。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「低处」、「低空飞过」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·谢朓·敬亭山诗》：「交藤荒且蔓，樛枝耸复<b>低</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·文天祥〈正气歌·序〉：「单扉<b>低</b>小，白间短窄。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">低下。指身分、能力、程度等而言。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·李白〈登黄山凌歊台送族弟溧阳尉济充泛舟赴华阴〉诗：「空手无壮士，穷居使人<b>低</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">价较便宜的。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·苏轼〈和文与可洋川园池三十首·金橙径〉诗：「金橙纵复里人知，不见鲈鱼价自<b>低</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">形容兴趣不高或情绪不好。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「只要下雨天，我出门的兴致就<b>低</b>了。」、「自从落榜后，他的情绪一直很<b>低</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">形容声音很小。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「你说话的声音太<b>低</b>了，别人可能听不清楚。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">俯、向下弯。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>低</b>身」、「低头不语」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">放<b><b>低</b></b>、垂落。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·谢惠连·雪赋》：「愿<b>低</b>帷以昵帎，念解珮而褫绅。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·李端〈代弃妇答贾客〉诗：「鸣环动珮恩无尽，掩袖<b>低</b>巾泪不流。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">低矮的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「夜幕低垂」、「黄昏时，太阳<b>低</b>悬在海平面上。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">轻声的。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·姜夔〈过垂虹〉诗：「自作新词韵最娇，小红低唱我吹箫。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
