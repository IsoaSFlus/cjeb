						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>堕</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">墮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">du&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨㄛˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 掉下来，坠落：～落。～地。～马。～胎。～甑不顾（喻对已经过去的事，不作无益的惋惜）。</p>

</ol>

<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">墮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">huī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄟˉ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">堕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>堕</strong></p>
<p>墮、隓 <span class="dicpy">du&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，隋声。甲骨文字形，右边是&ldquo;阜&rdquo;(象陡坡)，左边是倒过来的&ldquo;人&rdquo;。会意。表示人从陡坡上掉下。本义:毁坏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fall;sink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堕，败城阜曰堕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万事堕哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;益稷》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堕名城，杀豪杰，收天下之兵聚之</span><span class="diczx2">咸阳</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">西汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周</span>道衰，法度堕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敌人纷堕如落叶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堕废(毁坏;衰落);堕罪(降罪);堕地(落地。指出生);堕局(落入骗避);堕睫(落泪);堕坠(跌落);飞机堕海</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 脱落 <span class="encs">[come off]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:堕齿;堕角;堕指(冻掉手指);堕颠(脱发秃顶)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>堕</strong></p>
<p>墮 <span class="dicpy">du&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为&ldquo;惰&rdquo;。懒散;懈怠 <span class="encs">[sluggish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣下堕而不忠，则卑辱困穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;形势》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">动无堕容，口无虚言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;兵略》</span></span></p>
