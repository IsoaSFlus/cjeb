                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>教</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>指导，训诲：～习。～头。～正。～师。～导。管～。请～。～学相长。因材施～。</li>	<li>使，令：风能～船走。</li>	<li>指&ldquo;宗教&rdquo;：～士。～主。～皇。～堂。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>教</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>传授：～课。你～给我做。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">教</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>教</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū)，</span>从孝，孝亦声。&ldquo;攴&rdquo;，篆体象以手持杖或执鞭。在奴隶社会，奴隶主要靠鞭杖来施行他们的教育、教化。本义:教育，指导)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[teach;instruct;give guidance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上所施下所效也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以教国子弟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;师氏》</span>。注:&ldquo;教之者，使识旧事也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">教也者，长善而救其失者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">教者，民之寒暑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">教，文之施也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
