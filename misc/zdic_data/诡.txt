						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>诡</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">詭</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">guǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄟˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>欺诈，奸滑：～称。～道。～诈。～辩（➊无理强辩；➋逻辑学上指似是而非的论证，如&ldquo;～～论&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>怪异，出乎寻常：～异。～怪。～秘（隐秘难测）。～谲。</li>
	<li>责成：～求（责求，索讨，如&ldquo;～～无已&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>违反：言行相～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>诡</strong></p>
<p>詭 <span class="dicpy">guǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，危声。本义:责成，要求)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[instruct a person or an organization to fulfil an assigned task]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诡，责也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自诡灭贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵充国传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今臣得出守郡，自诡效功，恐未效而死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;京房传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诡求(要求，责求);诡驳(责难驳斥);诡责(责备;责问);诡令(责令)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 违反，自相矛盾 <span class="encs">[contradict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诡，违也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span>注引<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">容服有仪谓之仪，反仪为诡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诡自然之性。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;主术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古今诡趣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;辩亡论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言行相诡，不祥莫大焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;淫辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诡故(违反本心);诡局(同诡句、诡论。哲学名词。加以肯定或否定都会获致相反结果的局面、论点或述句)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 隐蔽;隐藏 <span class="encs">[hide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不得已，变姓名，诡踪迹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>诡</strong></p>
<p>詭 <span class="dicpy">guǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 怪异，奇异 <span class="encs">[eerie;queer;strange]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其名为吊诡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄了&middot;齐物论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诡文回波。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span>。注:&ldquo;奇异也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殊形诡制，每各异观。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《西都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">神仙诡诞之说，谓</span><span class="diczx2">颜太师</span>以兵解， <span class="diczx2">文少保</span>亦以悟 <span class="diczx2">大光明法</span>蝉脱，实未尝死。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诡形(奇异的形状);诡特(奇特怪异);诡喻(奇诡的比喻);诡说(虚妄怪诞的言论)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 欺诈;假冒 <span class="encs">[deceitful;treacherous;crafty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">持诡辩以中伤人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;石显传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求利之诡缓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span>。注:&ldquo;诈也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汉求</span><span class="diczx2">武</span>等，匈奴诡言 <span class="diczx2">武</span>死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;苏武传》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">詭</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“诡”字头，请参考“詭”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              詭·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A6D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】過委切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】古委切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音垝。【說文】責也。【前漢&middot;京房傳】臣出守郡，自詭效功。【師古註】自以爲憂責也。【孔融&middot;薦禰衡表】昔賈誼求試屬國，詭繫單于。【註】自責必繫單于也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】欺也，謾也。【類篇】詐也。【穀梁傳&middot;文六年】詭辭而出。【註】不以實吿人。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】怪也。【莊子&middot;齊物論】是其言也，其名爲弔詭。【註】弔詭，至怪也。</p>
<p>又【增韻】異也。【前漢&middot;劉輔傳】此其言必有卓詭切至當聖心者。【師古註】詭，異於衆也。【後漢&middot;班固傳】殊形詭制。【註】詭，異也。</p>
<p>又違也。【前漢&middot;董仲舒傳】有所詭於天之理與。【師古註】違也。</p>
<p>又毀也。【後漢&middot;班固傳論】固之序事不激詭。【註】激，揚。詭，毀也。</p>
<p>又【廣雅】詭隨，小惡也。【詩&middot;大雅】無縱詭隨。【註】不顧是非，而妄隨人也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】橫射物爲詭遇。【孟子】爲之詭遇。【趙註】橫而射之也。【朱註】不正而與禽遇也。</p>
<p>又譎詭，變化也。【張衡&middot;東京賦】瑰異譎詭。</p>
<p>又【廣雅】詭犀，總也。總，音思。</p>
<p>又【正韻】戾也。</p>
<p>又星名。【前漢&middot;天文志】司詭星出西方。【孟康註】星大而有尾，兩角，熒惑之精也。</p>
<p>又邑名。【史記&middot;秦始皇紀】將軍蒙騖，攻魏氏畼有詭。【註】畼有詭，魏邑名。</p>
<p>又姓。【左傳&middot;莊十六年】晉武公伐夷，執夷詭諸。【註】詭諸，周大夫。夷，采地名。</p>
<p>又人名。【史記&middot;齊世家】桓公長衞姬生無詭。【左傳】作無虧。</p>
<p>又通作恑。【陸機&middot;辯亡論】古今詭趣。【註】詭，變也。同恑。　【集韻】或作謉、譌。【正韻】亦作佹、傀。</p>
