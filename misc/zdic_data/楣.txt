                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>楣</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">méi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/méi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄟˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>门框上的横木：门～。</li>    <li>房屋的横梁，即二梁。</li>    <li>屋檐口，椽端的横板。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">楣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>楣</strong> <span class="dicpy">méi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/méi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，眉声。本义:屋檐口椽端的横板)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lintel over the door]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">楣，屋边联也。</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>谓之檐， <span class="diczx2">楚</span>谓之梠，秦谓之楣。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“按尔雅，‘楣谓之梁’谓门上横梁也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阳气见于眉(楣)宇之间。——</span><span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span>。按，以屋之近前下垂处，喻额前也。</p><p><span class="diczx1">主人阼阶上当楣。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡饮礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公当楣北乡皆是也。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·公食礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苍藤蔽檐楣，楚楚干云势。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《十景园小集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 也指门框上的横木。也叫<span class="yhcs">“门楣”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蜗舍入门楣触额——</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《夏雨叹》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雕琉璃于翠楣。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:楣机(比喻关键、重要之处)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 房屋的横梁，即二梁 <span class="encs">[cross beam]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">楣谓之梁。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“门户上横梁。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剪屏柱楣。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·丧服传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“柱楣所谓梁闇。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盘山越岭，接栋连楣。——</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《大观阁序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:楣梁(房屋的次梁);楣栋(屋的正梁和次梁)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                     <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">楣</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>楣</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄇㄟˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>méi <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0593.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">屋簷口椽端的封簷板。</span><span class="gc_yy">《宋书·卷六七·谢灵运传》：「因丹霞以赪<b>楣</b>，附碧云以翠椽。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">门框的上横部分。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「门<b>楣</b>」、「倒<b>楣</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辞·屈原·九歌·湘夫人》：「桂栋兮兰橑，辛夷<b>楣</b>兮药房。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·陈鸿《长恨传》：「男不封侯女作妃，看女却为门上<b>楣</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">两柱之间所架的横木，即屋梁。</span><span class="gc_yy">《仪礼·乡射礼》：「序则物当栋，堂则物当<b>楣</b>。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6963.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】武悲切【集韻】【韻會】旻悲切【正韻】謨杯切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音眉。本作楣。省作楣。【說文】秦名屋<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2377C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣝼</span>聯也，齊謂之檐，楚謂之梠。徐引《爾雅·釋宮》楣謂之梁，謂門上橫樑也。眉猶際也。【釋名】楣，眉也。近前各兩，若面之有眉。【儀禮·鄕射禮】序則物當棟，堂則物當楣。【註】五架之屋，正中曰棟，次曰楣。【鄕飮酒禮】賔升主人阼階上，當楣北面再拜。【註】楣，前梁也。楣字从睂作。</p><p>
