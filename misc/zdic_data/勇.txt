						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>勇</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>有胆量，敢做：～敢。～毅。～气。～士。英～。奋～。</li><li>中国清代称战争时期临时招募的兵士：兵～。劲～。募～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> brave, courageous, fierce</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> mutig, tapfer</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> brave,courageux</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>勇</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本作勈，或作恿。形声。从力，甬( <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> )声。从力，与力气有关。本义:果敢，胆大)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[brave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇，气也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无拳无勇。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·巧言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尔勇伊何。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇志之所以敢也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子经上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇，文之帅也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知死不辟，勇也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇于就死。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若素名勇。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兴甚勇。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇而多艺。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:忠勇(忠诚而勇敢);英勇(勇敢出众);勇悍(勇敢强悍);勇烈(勇敢猛烈);勇夫(勇敢的人);勇功(勇敢作战立下的功劳);勇沉(勇敢沉着);勇果(勇敢果断)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 勇猛;凶猛 <span class="encs">[bold and powerful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇悍果敢，取众率兵，此下德也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇，天下之凶德也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·论威》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每战，常陷陈却敌，勇冠</span><span class="diczx2">腾</span>军。——<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:神勇(形容人非常勇猛);骁勇(勇猛);勇鸷(勇猛强悍)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>勇</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 勇气，勇力 <span class="encs">[courage;courage and power]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可使有勇。——<span class="smcs">《论语·先进》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇冠三军。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其勇过人。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 清代地方临时招募的兵卒，也泛指士兵 <span class="encs">[soldier]</span>。如:乡勇;勇丁(清代地方招募的士兵);游勇(指失去统属的士兵)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
