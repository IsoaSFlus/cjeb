                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>淑</strong> <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，叔声。本义:水清澈)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[limpid]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淑，清湛也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其何能淑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑柔》</span>。按，清也。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:淑淑(清净);淑清(明朗;纯净);淑然(清纯的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="yhcs">“淑”</span>假借为<span class="yhcs">“俶”</span>。善良;美好 <span class="encs">[kind-hearted]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遇人之不淑矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·中谷有蓷》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淑人君子。——<span class="smcs">《诗·曹风·鳲鸠》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窈窕淑女，君子好逑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·关雎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淑，善也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有私淑艾者。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九侯淑女。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淑人君子，其仪一兮。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未获至论之淑真也。——<span class="smcs">《潜夫论·论荣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将军向宠，性行淑均。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:淑艾(美好;善良);淑人(心地善良之人;明清对三品官之妻的封号);淑慎(美善而敬慎);淑懿(美善;良善);淑景(美景);淑媛(美好善良的女子);淑慝(好坏;善恶);淑女(悠闲洁净的女子);帝王对高官家属的一种封号;淑士(淑人，善人);淑尤(特别美善);淑化(良善的风尚)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 美丽 <span class="encs">[beautiful;fair]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淑好之人，戚施之所妒也。——</span><span class="diczx2">桓宽</span><span class="smcs">《盐铁论·非鞅》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:淑貌(美丽的容貌);淑姿(优美的姿态容貌);淑尤(优美出众，美善过人);淑丽(犹淑美。贤淑美丽);淑美(美丽贤淑);淑令(美丽);淑好(美丽)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
