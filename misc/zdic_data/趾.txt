                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>趾</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，止声。古所谓趾，指足，不指脚趾。脚指叫做指，趾是<span class="yhcs">“止”</span>的后起字。本义:脚)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[foot]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三之日于耜，四之日举趾。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贲其趾。——<span class="smcs">《易·贲卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屦校灭趾。——<span class="smcs">《易·噬嗑卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">麟之趾。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·麟之趾》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今君若步玉趾。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">足趾之所不蹈。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">王力</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“足趾是同义词连用，‘趾’不是足指。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:趾股(腿脚);趾踵(脚);趾踵相接(形容人数之多);趾爪(脚爪)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 脚指头 <span class="encs">[toe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">右手攀右趾若啸呼状。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:脚趾(脚前端的分支);鹅鸭之类趾间有蹼;趾绊(本屐上的趾袢儿);趾趾(足尖轻轻着地行走的声音)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 支撑器物的脚 <span class="encs">[leg]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鼎颠趾。——<span class="smcs">《易·鼎》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“址”</span>。基址<span class="encs">[foundation]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">略基趾。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亭亭峻趾。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">去上西山趾。——</span><span                           <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">止</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“趾”字头，请参考“止”字：)</span>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷二">卷二</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷二&amp;swbs=止">止</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6B62.svg">                          <p>下基也。象艸木出有址，故以止爲足。凡止之屬皆从止。諸市切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6B62_3102.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(止)</span><span class="ywyy">下基也。</span><span class="swdz">與？同部同義。</span><span class="ywyy">象艸木出有阯。</span><span class="swdz">止象艸木生有阯。屮象艸木初生形。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21CFF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡳿</span>象艸過屮枝莖益大。出象艸木益滋上出達也。</span><span class="ywyy">故㠯止爲足。</span><span class="swdz">此引伸假借之法。凡以韋爲皮韋、以朋爲朋黨、以來爲行來之來、以西爲東西之西、以子爲人之偁皆是也。以止爲人足之偁與以子爲人之偁正同。許書無趾字。止卽趾也。詩麟之止。易賁其止、壯于前止。士昏禮北止。注曰。止、足也。古文止爲趾。許同鄭从今文。故不錄趾字。如从今文名、不錄古文銘也。或疑銘趾當爲今文。名止當爲古文。周尚文。自有委曲煩重之字不合於倉頡者。故名止者、古文也。銘趾者、後出之古文也。古文禮今文禮者、猶言古本今本也。古本出於周、從後出之古文。今本行於漢、轉從冣初之古文。猶<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23729.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣜩</span>楷之體、時或有捨小篆用古籒體者也。諸市切。一部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡止之屬皆从止。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
