                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>体</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">體</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄧˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>人、动物的全身：身～。～重。～温。～质。～征（医生在检查病人时所发现的异常变化）。～能。～貌。～魄（体格和精力）。～育。～无完肤。</li>
	<li>身体的一部分：四～。五～投地。</li>
	<li>事物的本身或全部：物～。主～。群～。</li>
	<li>物质存在的状态或形状：固～。液～。～积。</li>
	<li>文章或书法的样式、风格：～裁（文学作品的表现形式，可分为诗歌，散文，小说，戏剧等）。文～（文章的体裁，如&ldquo;骚～&rdquo;、&ldquo;骈～&rdquo;、&ldquo;旧～诗&rdquo;）。字～。</li>
	<li>事物的格局、规矩：～系。～制。</li>
	<li>亲身经验、领悟：～知（亲自查知）。～味。身～力行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>设身处地为人着想：～谅。～贴。～恤。</li>
	<li>与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">用</span>&rdquo;相对。&ldquo;体&rdquo;与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">用</span>&rdquo;是中国古典哲学的一对范畴，指&ldquo;本体&rdquo;和&ldquo;作用&rdquo;。一般认为&ldquo;体&rdquo;是最根本的、内在的；&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">用</span>&rdquo;是&ldquo;体&rdquo;的外在表现。</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">體</span>）</strong></p>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>体</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 軆 <span class="dicpy">tī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">tǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/体己">体己</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>体</strong></p>
<p>軆 <span class="dicpy">tǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (体，会意。从人，从本。古代&ldquo;体、軆&rdquo;是两个字，&ldquo;体&rdquo;是&ldquo;劣&rdquo;，又指粗笨。身体本字是&ldquo;軆&rdquo;，形声。从骨，豊( <span class="dicpy">lǐ</span>)声。今简化为&ldquo;体&rdquo;。本义:身体)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">体，总十二属之名也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，十二属者:顶、面、颐，首属三;肩、脊、臀，身属三;肱、臂、手，手属三;股、胫、足，足属三也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">体，身也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饿其体肤，空乏其身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居五日，</span><span class="diczx2">桓侯</span>体痛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;喻老》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">体有不快。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;华佗传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伤乎体。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">口体之奉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:体态轻盈(形容女子婀娜多姿);上体;下体;体高五尺;体大身强;肉体(人的身体，区别于&ldquo;精神&rdquo;);体骸(躯壳;尸骸);体候(身体状况);体素(敬词。玉体);体段(身段)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 手脚、四肢<span class="encs">[limbs]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轻暖不足于体。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹其有四体也。谓二手二足。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:四体(人的四肢);五体投地</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形体 ;体态<span class="encs">[shape (of a person&#39;s body)physique;body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可怜体无比。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:体段(样子;身分);体相(形体相貌);长方体;立方体;几何体;正方体;角柱体;圆柱体;刚体;磁体;黑体;发光体;导体;晶体</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 牲体 <span class="encs">[body of domestic animal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辨体名肉物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;内飨》</span>。按，牲体有七脊也，左右肋也，左右肱也，左右股也，所谓全軆也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:体解(古代祭祀时将牲体切割成二十一块);体节(带骨的大块牛羊肉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 事物的主要部分 <span class="encs">[main part]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以天演为体。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:体用(事物的本体与作用);体物(体现于万事万物之中);体例(制度的纲领和细则);体要(大体与纲要)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 实体<span class="encs">[substance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天之与地，皆体也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 规格;法式 <span class="encs">[specifications;laws]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">直求输赤诚，所望大体全。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《行次西郊作一百韵》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:体式(法式，格式);体法(指书法的格局法式);体段(体格、结构);体气(诗文的气质格调;人品性格);体统(规矩，制度;诗文或著作中的体裁和条理)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 占卜时的兆象 <span class="encs">[omen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔卜尔筮，体无咎言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:体象(象征)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 本性，本质 <span class="encs">[nature]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:体识(禀性和器识);体器(禀性和器度);体履(禀性和行为)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 准则 <span class="encs">[norm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">平居妄自尊大而临事不知体，此俗人也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陈善</span><span class="smcs">《扪虱新话》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 文体 <span class="encs">[type of writing;literary form]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:古体诗;近体诗;散体;骈体</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 体制 <span class="encs">[system]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>至 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>，四百余年，辞人才子，文体三变。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《谢灵运传论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:政体;国体</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 动词的一套屈折形式、表示动作的性质或关于该动作的开始、持续、完成或重复等方面的情况，但不涉及该动作发生的时间&mdash;&mdash;最初用于斯拉夫语言，后来用于其他语言 <span class="encs">[aspect of a verb]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:完成体;进行体</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 文字的书写形式 <span class="encs">[form;style]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:草体;楷体;宋体;字体;印刷体</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 立体 <span class="encs">[cube]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后见为体。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">体面互见。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舍体而取面。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">体之感觉。</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4F53.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】蒲本切【集韻】部本切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>盆上聲。劣也。</p>
<p>又麤貌。與笨同。【通雅】輀車之夫曰体夫。唐懿宗咸通十二年葬，同昌公主賜酒餠餤四十橐駝，以飤体夫。【註】体，蒲本反。体夫，轝柩之夫。　俗書四體之體，省作體，誤。</p>
<p>（體）〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29A86.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩪆</span>【唐韻】【正韻】他禮切【集韻】【韻會】土禮切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>涕上聲。【說文】總十二屬也。【釋名】體，第也。骨肉毛血表裏大小相次第也。【廣韻】四支也。【禮&middot;中庸】動乎四體。</p>
<p>又【易&middot;文言】君子體仁，足以長人。【疏】體包仁道。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;畢命】辭尚體要。【註】辭以理實爲要。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;衞風】爾卜爾筮，體無咎言。【傳】體，兆卦之體。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】方苞方體，維葉泥泥。【箋】體，成形也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;天官】體國經野。【註】體，猶分也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;天官&middot;內饔】辨體名肉物。【註】體名，脊脅臂臑之屬。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;文王世子】外朝以官體異姓也。【註】體，猶連結也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;學記】就賢體遠。【註】體，猶親也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;中庸】體物而不可遺。【註】猶生也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;中庸】體羣臣也。【註】猶接納也。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;昭二十年】聲亦如味，一氣二體。【疏】樂之動身體者，唯有舞耳。舞者有文武二體。　【廣韻】俗作軆。【集韻】作躰。【增韻】俗作体，非。</p>
