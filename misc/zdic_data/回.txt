                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">回</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>回</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">③迴</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>还，走向原来的地方：～家。</li>
	<li>掉转：～首（回头看）。～顾。～眸。～暧。妙手～春。</li>
	<li>曲折，环绕，旋转：～旋。～肠。～廊（曲折回环的走廊）。</li>
	<li>答复，答报：～信。～话。～绝。</li>
	<li>量词，指事件的次数：两～事。</li>
	<li>说书的一个段落，章回小说的一章：且听下～分解。</li>
	<li>中国少数民族，分布于中国大部分地区：～族。～教（中国称伊斯兰教）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">回</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>回</strong></p>
<p>迴、囬 <span class="dicpy">hu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (囘为回的古字; 廻为迴的俗字。囬、迴同回。象形。甲骨文象渊水回旋之形。本义:回旋;旋转)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[whirl;circle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">回，转也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昭回于天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;云汉》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">图回天下于掌上而辨白黑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诡文回波。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畔回穴其若兹兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;叙传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虎鼓瑟兮鸾回车。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山回路转不见君。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">廊腰缦回。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜枚</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">峰回路转。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:回湍(回旋的急流);回倒(回旋倾倒);回纡(迂回。回旋曲折);回极(无极回旋的枢轴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 环绕; 包围 <span class="encs">[surround]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东道少回远。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:回峰(环绕的山峰);回圆(环绕);回塘(环曲的水池);回缭(围绕);回互(回环交互)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 掉转 <span class="encs">[turn round]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">回车叱牛牵向北。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">回看射雕处。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《观猎》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兴尽晚回舟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《如梦令》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">回头望汝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:回面(转过脸);回眼(回转目光);回天(形容力量很大，能扭转乾坤);回舆(掉转马头，返回);回逆(倒转)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 还，返回 <span class="encs">[return]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">碧水东流至此回。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《望天门山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少小离家老大回。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贺知章</span><span class="smcs">《回乡偶书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余逆脱回。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:回寅(回到农历正月);回九(旧俗新婚后新娘九日后回娘家);回前线;回跸(帝王返驾回宫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 答复;回报 <span class="encs">[report back;reply]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:回盘(旧婚俗。男家行聘礼，女家回礼，把礼物放在托盘、抬盒内，因称回礼为&ldquo;回盘&rdquo;);回风(高官升堂之前，吏役报告准备妥当的一个程式);回诺(回礼);回耗(回音)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 改变 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道苟直，虽死不可回也;如回之，莫若亟去其位。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《与韩愈论史官书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:回心(改变心意);回异(改变;违反);回换(调换;变换);回变(转变)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 违背 <span class="encs">[go against]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥德不回。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;大明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徐方不回。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;常武》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">经德不回，非以干禄也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;尽心下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求福不回。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;苏竟传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二人之不敢请，亦知</span><span class="diczx2">始皇</span>之鸷悍而不可回也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《东坡志林》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 谢绝，推辞不见 <span class="encs">[refuse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:回决(回绝);回倒(回绝驳倒);回听(不听);回出(回绝);回唇答嘴(顶嘴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 回避，避开不喜欢、不欣赏的人 <span class="encs">[averse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:回丧(回避。丧家怕鬼回来害人而躲开);他不回亲戚</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 转卖，转手卖掉 <span class="encs">[resell]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:回买(转买，购买);回钱(售物所得的钱);回些(转让;卖些)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 问 <span class="encs">[ask]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">进得店去，茶房便来回道:&ldquo;客人，用什么夜膳?&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">如:且听下回分解</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 一会儿&mdash;&mdash;用于时间，表示短暂 <span class="encs">[moment]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:回子(一会儿);回一回(停一停)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用于事情，相当于&ldquo;桩&rdquo;、&ldquo;件&rdquo; <span class="encs">[number of times]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:就是这么回事;这完全是两回事</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 某一特定目的或发展的一种形势或某种情况 <span class="encs">[occasion]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:上回，上一回;这一回</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>回</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄨㄟˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hu&iacute; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2894.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">掉轉。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「回首」、「回過身來」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈長恨歌〉：「君王掩面救不得，回看血淚相和流。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">返、歸。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「回國」、「回家」、「一去不回」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;王翰〈涼州詞〉二首之一：「醉臥沙場君莫笑，古來征戰幾人回？」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">改變。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「回心轉意」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷四八&middot;賈誼傳》：「夫移風易俗，使天下回心而鄉道，類非俗吏之所能為也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;柳宗元〈與韓愈論史官書〉：「道苟直，雖死不可回也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">答覆。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「回信」、「回電」、「回答」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《二刻拍案驚奇&middot;卷一一》：「日後他來通消息時，好言回他。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第七回》：「次日，鳳姐梳洗了，先回王夫人畢，方來辭賈母。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">還覆給對方相同或某種動作。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「回敬」、「回他一槍」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">謝絕、辭去。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「一口回絕」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈與開元明師〉二首之一：「謹留筆一束，以領雅意，餘回納，不訝不訝。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第一一九回》：「咱們家遭著這樣事，那有工夫接待人，不拘怎麼回了他去罷。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">避開。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「回避」。</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;徐復祚《紅梨記&middot;第二齣》：「前日兩次訪他，俱不得遇，風塵中人，知他真不在家，還是故意回你。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">中國少數民族之一。主要分布在大陸地區新疆、遼寧、青海、甘肅、寧夏、河北等地區，以信奉回教為主。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞：➊ ​ 計算行為、動作的單位。相當於「次」。如：「前後我共去找了他五回。」宋&middot;辛棄疾〈水調歌頭&middot;帶湖吾甚愛〉詞：「先生杖屨無事，一日走千回。」➋ ​ 計算時間的單位。一陣子稱為「一回」。《喻世明言&middot;卷三&middot;新橋市韓五賣春情》：「八老到門前站了一回，蹔到間壁糶米張大郎門前，閒坐了一回。」➌ ​ 計算長篇小說段落的單位。一章稱為「一回」。如：「一百二十回本《紅樓夢》。」➍ ​ 計算事情的單位。相當於「件」。如：「這是兩回事，不可混為一談。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如明代有回滿注。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">回</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=囗" target="_blank">囗</a>】
                              回·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=6" target="_blank">6</a>　·部外筆画：3</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/56DE.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】戸恢切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】胡隈切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音洄。【說文】从囗，中象回轉之形。【徐鍇曰】渾天之氣，天地相承。天周地外，隂陽五行，回轉其中也。</p>
<p>又【說文】邪也，曲也。【詩&middot;小雅】淑人君子，其德不回。【禮&middot;禮器】禮飾回，增美質，措則正，施則行。</p>
<p>又【正韻】返也。【後漢&middot;蔡邕傳】回途要至，俯仰取容。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】違也。【詩&middot;大雅】求福不回。【又】徐方不回。【註】回猶違也，言不違命也。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】昭回于天。【註】昭，明。回，旋也。</p>
<p>又屈也。【後漢&middot;盧植傳】可加赦恕申宥回枉。【又】抗議不回。</p>
<p>又徘回。【說文】徘徊本作裵回。寬衣也，取其裵回之狀。【張衡&middot;思<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>賦】馬倚輈而徘回。【註】言踟躊不進也。</p>
<p>又低回，紆衍貌。【史記&middot;孔子世家贊】適魯，觀仲尼車服禮器，余低回留之，不能去。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】大道低回。</p>
<p>又姓。【韻會】古賢者方回之後。【正字通】明宣德中御史回續。</p>
<p>又地名。【後漢&middot;郡國志】右扶風有回城，名曰回中。</p>
<p>又通作迴。【荀子&middot;儒效篇】圖迴天下於掌上。</p>
<p>又通作廻。【史記&middot;鄒陽傳】墨子廻車。</p>
<p>又【正字通】回回，國名。西域大食國種也。明丘濬曰：國在玉門關外萬里，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>隋閒入中國。金元以後，蔓延滋甚，所至輒相親守，其所謂敎門者尤篤，今在在有之。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>戸賄切，音悔。繞也。【左傳&middot;襄十八年】右回梅山。徐邈讀上聲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>胡對切，音繢。【前漢&middot;趙充國傳】回遠千里。【註】回謂路迂回也，音胡悔反。</p>
<p>又畏避也。【前漢&middot;王溫舒傳】卽有避回。【註】謂不盡意，捕擊盜賊。</p>
<p>又【蓋寬饒傳】刺舉無所回避。【註】回<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>讀若諱。　俗作囬。</p>
