                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丰</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>丰</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">豐</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>容貌好看：～润。～腴（➊身体丰满；➋富裕；➌丰饶）。～盈。</li>
	<li>风度神采：～采（亦作&ldquo;风采&rdquo;）。～度（美好的举止姿态，风度）。</li>
	<li>盛，多，大：～盛。～碑（高大的石碑，喻不朽的杰作或伟大的功绩）。～富。～沛。～饶。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丰</strong></p>
<p>豐 <span class="dicpy">fēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，上面象一器物盛有玉形，下面是&ldquo;豆&rdquo;(古代盛器)。故&ldquo;豐&rdquo;本是盛有贵重物品的礼器。这由&ldquo;豊&rdquo;字可以得到证明。古文&ldquo;豐与&ldquo;豊&rdquo;是同一个字，<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;豊，行礼之器也。&rdquo;本义:古代盛酒器的托盘)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 中国古代礼器，形状像豆，用以承酒觯 <span class="encs">[bean-like vessel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豐，豆之豐满者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饮酒实于觯，加于丰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;公食大夫礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 蒲草 <span class="encs">[the stem of cattail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东序西向，敷重丰席，画纯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;顾命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 地名</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 周国都名 <span class="encs">[Feng capital]</span>。在今陕西省西安市西南</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 秦沛县之丰邑，汉置县 <span class="encs">[Feng county]</span>。今江苏省徐州市丰县</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 传说中的古侯国名 <span class="encs">[Feng state]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丰侯(传说中的古代因喝酒而亡国的诸侯)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 姓。春秋时鲁有丰愆。又如:丰将(复姓)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丰</strong></p>
<p>豐 <span class="dicpy">fēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 草木茂盛 <span class="encs">[luxuriant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豐，茂也，盛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在彼丰草。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;湛露》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丰殖九薮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">韭曰丰本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树木丛生，百草丰茂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;曹操&middot;步出夏门行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丰容(草木茂盛);丰彤(林木茂密的样子);丰林(丰茂的林木);丰荣(花木繁茂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 大;高大 <span class="encs">[great;tall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丰，大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;丰卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲其丰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记&middot;函人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羽丰则迟。</span></p>
