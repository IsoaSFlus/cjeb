                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>筋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>肌肉的俗称：～力。～肉。～疲力尽。</li><li>肌腱或附着在骨头上的韧带：～骨。～道（“<span class="diczx3">道</span>”读轻声）。蹄～。</li><li>可见的皮下静脉的俗称：～络。～脉。青～暴露。</li><li>像筋的东西：钢～。橡皮～儿。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">筋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>筋</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从肉，从力，从竹。因为竹多筋，故从<span class="yhcs">“竹”</span>。本义:附着在骨上的韧带)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tendon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筋，肉之力也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸筋者，皆属于节。——<span class="smcs">《素问·五藏生成论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">强者在内而摩其筋。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·弓人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">劳其筋骨。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筋骨之强。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先折筋骨。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筋骨尽脱。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:筋皮(筋骨与皮肉);筋厮锁(身体已肢解，只剩筋络相连。比喻山河破碎);筋血(精力与血汗);筋条(身体结实强健);筋马(筋骨强健而不过于肥壮的马)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 肌肉的旧称 <span class="encs">[muscle]</span>。如:筋丝无力(没有一点力气);筋疙疸(凸出的肌肉)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 静脉 <span class="encs">[veins that stand out under the skin]</span>。如:筋脉</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 类似或可比作肋的东西 <span class="encs">[articles resembling tendons or veins]</span>。如:叶筋;丝瓜筋;橡皮筋</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 加强物、加固物 <span class="encs">[reinforcement]</span>。如:钢筋</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 植物的脉络 <span class="encs">[veins]</span>。如:这菜筋多嚼不烂</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7B4B.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕竻【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】舉欣切【正韻】居銀切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音斤。【說文】肉之力也。从肉从力从竹。竹，物之多筋者。【禮·曲禮】老者不以筋力爲禮。【周禮·天官·醫師】凡藥以辛養筋。【釋名】筋，力也。肉中之力，氣之元也，靳固于身形也。　【博雅】北方之美者，有幽都之筋角焉。</p><p>又竹名。【戴凱之·竹譜】筋竹，長二丈許，圍數寸，至堅利，南土以爲矛。其筍未成竹時，堪爲弩絃。</p><p>又藥名。【本草】白茅根，一名土筋。</p><p>又姓，出【姓苑】。</p><p>又【集韻】渠焉切，音乾。大腱也。　【篇海】俗作䈥。</p>
