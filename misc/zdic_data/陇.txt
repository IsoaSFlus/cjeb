						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>陇</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">隴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>中国甘肃省的别称。</li><li>古地名，在今中国甘肃省：得～望蜀（喻人贪得无厌）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">垄</span>”，土埂。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>mountain located between Shanxi</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> autre nom de la province du Gansu</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>陇</strong></p><p>隴 <span class="dicpy">lǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">fù</span>)，表示与地势的高低上下有关，龙声。本义:古地名。在今甘肃东部)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 即陇山。绵延于陕西、甘肃交界的地方 <span class="encs">[Long]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陇，</span><span class="diczx2">天水</span>大阪也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">沈涛</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“今本合三处互订，古本当作‘陇山，天水阪、大阪也。’”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 甘肃省的简称。因古为陇西郡地而得名 <span class="encs">[Gansu province]</span>。如:陇西(古郡名。在今甘肃省东南部一带);陇上(地名。在今甘肃省)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“垄”</span>。田埂 <span class="encs">[ridge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">席陇亩而荫庇。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然</span><span class="diczx2">羽</span>非有尺寸，乘势起陇亩之中。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪赞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:陇邱(田垄;土丘);陇亩(田野，田亩)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 泛指山 <span class="encs">[hill]</span>。如:陇断(土岗子，即垄断。指山脉阻隔。陇，山冈高地)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>陇</strong></p><p>隴 <span class="dicpy">lǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“拢”</span>。凑起，集合 <span class="encs">[gather together]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洪涟陇蹙。——</span><span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《登大雷岸与妹书》</span></p><p>◎ <strong>陇</strong></p><p>隴 <span class="dicpy">lǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“隆”</span>。盛，多 <span class="encs">[rich;abundant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日中而阳陇为重阳。——<span class="smcs">《灵枢·营卫生会》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/陇海">陇海</a></span></div>                    <div                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/96B4.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】力踵切【集韻】【韻會】魯勇切【正韻】力董切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音壠。【說文】天水大阪也。【前漢·地理志】天水郡隴縣。【註】今呼隴城縣者也。</p><p>又【史記·六國表】文公踰隴。【秦本紀】又使司馬錯發隴西，因蜀攻楚黔中。【前漢·地理志】隴西郡。【註】應劭曰：有隴坻，在其西也。師古曰：隴坻，謂隴阪，卽今之隴山也。此郡在隴之西，故曰隴西。【地理直音】漢隴西，今鞏昌府。漢天水，今鞏昌府秦州。</p><p>又【廣韻】州名。漢汧縣，後魏置東秦州，攺爲隴州，因山名之。【地理直音】隋汧陽郡，今鳳翔府隴州。○按今陝西鞏昌府淸水縣，平涼府華亭縣，鳳翔府隴州，皆有隴山，洮州衞有東隴山。</p><p>又【春秋·文二年】盟于垂隴。【註】鄭地，滎陽東有隴城。</p><p>又姓。</p><p>又【正韻】丘壟之壟，亦作隴。【列子·天瑞篇】逆之隴端。【前漢·劉向傳】皆無丘隴之處。</p><p>又壟畝亦作隴。【史記·項羽紀】起隴畝之閒。</p><p>又【荀子·議兵篇】案角鹿埵，隴種東籠而退耳。【註】隴種，遺失貌，如隴之種物然。或曰卽龍鍾。新序作隴鍾。</p><p>
