						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>厮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> ㄙˉ</span></p><ol><li>古代干粗杂活的男奴隶或小役：～役。小～。</li><li>古代对人的称呼（宋代以来的小说中常用）：这～。那～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>厮</strong></p><p>厮 <span class="dicpy">sī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (从广，斯声。广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，就山崖作成的房子。厂( <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>)山石之崖。<span class="yhcs">“斯”</span>也有表义作用。<span class="smcs">《汉书·严助传》</span><span class="yhcs">“厮舆之卒”</span>。颜师古注:<span class="yhcs">“厮，析薪者。”</span><span class="yhcs">“斯、析”</span>都有<span class="yhcs">“劈开”</span>义。本义:古代干粗活的男性奴隶或仆役;服杂役者)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[male servant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厮徒十万。——<span class="smcs">《史记·苏秦传》</span>。正义:<span class="yhcs">“谓炊烹供养杂役。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厮舆之卒。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·严助传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“析薪者也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厮役扈养，死者数百人。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·宣公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">料大王之卒，悉之不过三十万，而厮徒在其中矣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·韩策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:厮役(供差遣的奴仆);厮台(从事劳役的奴仆);厮养(从事析薪炊烹劳役的人);厮舆(砍柴与驾车的人);厮仆，厮竖(仆役);厮滥(出身低下者);女厮。又对男子轻蔑的称呼，犹小子。如:厮舍(仆役居屋)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古时指无身分或下贱的人 <span class="encs">[fellow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">原来我的丈夫也吃这厮杀了。——<span class="smcs">《京本通俗小说·错斩崔宁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">张教头</span>那厮，三回五次托人情去说…，越不肯应承。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:这厮;那厮;厮儿(家伙;小男孩;小子);厮佣(雇工);厮仆(仆役，佣人);厮波(专在酒楼、妓院等娱乐场所侍客索钱的闲汉)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>厮</strong> <span class="dicpy">sī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 相互 <span class="encs">[mutual;together;with each other]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莲子与人长厮类，无好意，年年苦在中心里。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《渔家傲》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">德安</span><span class="diczx2">云梦</span>人，初参 <span class="diczx2">妙喜</span>…， <span class="diczx2">喜</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“我闻你 <span class="diczx2">安州</span>人善厮扑，是否?”</span>——<span class="smcs">《五灯会元·善真禅师》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:厮共(共同);厮勾(互相接近;相亲相爱);厮称(相称);厮闹(吵闹;相互戏弄、胡闹);厮说(彼此说长道短);厮替(相互更替);厮抬厮敬(互相抬举，互相尊敬);厮朴行(专门从事相扑的团体);厮丢厮打(互相打斗);厮遮拦(相拦阻，阻挡);厮合燥(厮合造。混闹，胡闹);厮棵(拉扯，拽);厮揾(依偎，挨靠)</p><p>◎ <strong>厮</strong> <span class="dicpy">sī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 役使
