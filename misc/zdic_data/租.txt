                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>租</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>出代价暂用别人的东西：～房。～用。～借地。</li><li>收取一定的代价，把房屋、土地、器物等借给别人使用：～佃。～价。～让。～赁（ａ．出租；ｂ．租用）。</li><li>出租所收取的钱或实物：房～。地～。</li><li>田赋：～税。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>租</strong> <span class="dicpy">zū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾，且( <span class="dicpy">jū</span>)声。从禾，与谷物有关。本义:田赋，征收的农产品，又指按田地征收的捐税)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[feudal land tax]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">租，田赋也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">租，税也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敛财曰赋，敛谷曰税，田税曰租。——<span class="smcs">《急就篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">租籍者，所以强求也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·国蓄》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其赐天下民今年田租之半。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·文帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">县官急索租。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当其租入。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:租更(指田赋和践更的钱);租委(输纳的田赋);租刍(田赋);租输(上缴的田赋);租限(旧时地主规定佃户交租的期限)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 赋税 <span class="encs">[tax]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">关市之租，府库之征，粟什一。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">李牧</span>为 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>将居边，军市之租，皆自用响士。——<span class="smcs">《史记·张释之冯唐列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使吏致千金与市租，请以奉宾客。——<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:租入(缴纳的赋税);租民(纳税的人);租车(输纳租赋的车辆);租责(所欠赋税)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>租</strong> <span class="dicpy">zū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 租赁;租用 <span class="encs">[rent;hire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">租户于主有连姻。——<span class="smcs">《宋史·刘宰传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:租头(租金，租钱);租摺(租约);租费(又叫<span class="yhcs">“租金”</span>，<span class="yhcs">“租银”</span>)租种(承租他人的土地来耕种)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 征收租税 <span class="encs">[levy taxes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">募民耕</span><span class="diczx2">滦河</span>旷地，十年始租。——<span class="smcs">《辽史·食货志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 积聚 <span class="encs">[gather]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予所蓄租，予口卒暗。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·鸱鴞》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div
