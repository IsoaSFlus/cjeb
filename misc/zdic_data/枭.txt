                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枭</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>枭</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">梟</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>一种与鸱鸺相似的鸟。</li>	<li>勇健：～勇。～健。～将。～雄（①强横而野心勃勃的人；②智勇杰出的人物）。</li>	<li>古代刑罚，把头割下来悬挂在木上：～首。～示（枭首示众）。</li>	<li>旧时指私贩食盐的人：盐～。私～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> owl thus, something evil</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Eule [ lat. Strigiformes ]  (S, Bio)​,Schmuggler  (S)​,enthauptung, köpfen  (V, Gesch)​,mutig, tapfer, tollkühn und rücksichtslos  (Adj)​,Xiao  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> chouette,hibou,brave,contrebandier,dealer de drogue</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>枭</strong></p>
<p>梟 <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从鸟，头在木上。本义:指一种恶鸟，捕捉后悬头树上以示众)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 猫头鹰 <span class="encs">[owlet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枭，不孝鸟也。日至捕枭磔之，从枭头在木上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">用一枭破镜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;郊祀志》</span>。注:&ldquo;</span><span class="diczx2">孟康</span>曰，枭鸟食母，破镜兽食父，黄帝欲绝其类，使百吏祠皆用之。 <span class="diczx2">如淳</span>曰， <span class="diczx2">汉</span>五月五日作枭羹以赐百官。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为枭为鸱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;瞻卬》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射游枭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸱枭群翔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;郊祀志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枭鸣松桂枝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《凶宅》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枭奴(凶狠的奴仆);枭獍(枭镜。相传枭为食母恶鸟，獍为食父恶兽。比喻凶恶忘恩的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 旧时指私贩食盐的人 <span class="encs">[smuggler]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:盐枭</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 使人娱乐、愉快或欢欣的某种方法与手段 <span class="encs">[amusement]</span>。中国古代的一种游戏，叫&ldquo;樗蒱&rdquo;(相当于现在的掷骰子)，一为枭，六为卢。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:枭卢</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>枭</strong></p>
<p>梟 <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/689F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古堯切【集韻】【韻會】堅堯切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音驍。【說文】不孝鳥也。【詩&middot;大雅】爲梟爲鴟。【陸璣疏】自關而西，爲梟爲流離。其子適長大，還食其母。故張奐云：鶹鷅食母。</p><p>又其肉甚美，可爲羹臛。【北戸錄】古人尚鴞羹，意欲滅其族，非以爲美也。</p><p>又縣首木上曰梟首。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】梟故塞王欣頭櫟陽巿。</p><p>又健也。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】北貉燕人來致梟騎助漢。</p><p>又山巓曰梟。【管子&middot;地員篇】其山之梟，多桔符楡。其山之末，有箭與苑。</p><p>又雄也。【淮南子&middot;原道訓】湫漻寂寞，爲天下梟。</p><p>又梟瞯，深目貌。【王褒&middot;四子講德論】燋齒梟瞯，文身裸袒之國，靡不奔走貢獻。</p><p>又梟盧，樗蒱采名。幺爲梟，六爲盧。晉謝艾曰：梟，邀也。六博得邀者勝。【楚辭】成梟而牟呼五白。梟二爲珉采，牟，勝也。勝梟必五白。</p><p>又【本草別錄】桃終冬不落者爲梟桃。</p><p>又姓。【姓譜】隋煬帝誅楊元感，改其姓爲梟氏。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
