                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>袭</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">襲</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">x&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>趁敌人不备时攻击：～击。～扰。夜～。偷～。侵～。</li>	<li>触及：寒气～人。&ldquo;芳菲菲兮～予&rdquo;。</li>	<li>照样做，照样继续下去：因～。沿～。世～。</li>	<li>量词，指成套的衣服：衣一～。</li>	<li>衣上加衣，引申为重迭：&ldquo;～朝服&rdquo;。&ldquo;山三～，陟&rdquo;。</li>	<li>合，和：&ldquo;齐秦～&rdquo;。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">袭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>袭</strong></p>
<p>襲 <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，龖省声。本义:死者穿的衣服，衣襟在左边)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[graveclothes]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">袭，左衽袍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，凡敛死者，左衽，不纽。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陈袭事于房中，西领南上，不綪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士丧礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;袭事，谓衣服也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:袭衣(尸衣;也指古代行礼时穿在裼衣外面的上衣);袭玩(衣着器用)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>袭</strong></p>
<p>襲 <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 衣一套为一袭 <span class="encs">[a suit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赐衣被一袭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;昭帝纪》</span>。注:&ldquo;一称也。犹今一副。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃命留五时衣各一袭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;东平宪王苍传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">袈衣一袭，止用一两。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三宝太监西洋记通俗演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:袭衣(成套衣服);袭衣兼食(成套衣服，多盘菜肴。谓生活优裕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 层，重 <span class="encs">[layer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">棺椁数袭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>袭</strong></p>
<p>襲 <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 因袭，照旧搬用 <span class="encs">[carry on as before]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今袭迹于</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>，欲国安存，不可得也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;孤愤》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五帝不相复，三代不相袭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>兴，仍袭 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>制。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;宦官传论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">袭译欧西人之言。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:沿袭(依照旧传统办理);袭因(依照旧例);袭迹(依照前人的遗迹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 继承 <span class="encs">[succeed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世官九等，皆有袭职，有替职。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;职官志一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">贲瑞</span>早已辞世， <span class="diczx2">贲玺</span>依例袭了侯爵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《一层楼》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:世袭(指帝位、爵位等世代相传);袭职(继承官职);袭位</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 重复 <span class="encs">[repeat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">赵孟</span>曰:&ldquo;吾卜于此起兵，事不再令，卜不袭吉。行也。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十年》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:&ldquo;袭，重也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:袭吉(卜若不吉则止，不可因而重卜;重卜而得吉，叫做&ldquo;袭吉&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 调合;合 <span class="encs">[merge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天地比，</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>袭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:袭从(言两者相合);袭然(合一的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 袭击，乘其不备，偷偷地进攻 <span class="encs">[raid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡师有钟鼓曰伐，无曰侵，轻曰袭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公二十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掩人不备，行不假途，人衔枚，马勒缰，昼伏夜行，为袭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;诛伐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">城上早插</span><span class="diczx2">蜀</span>兵旗号:原来已被 <span class="diczx2">魏延</span>袭了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慧室袭月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">荆</span>人欲袭 <span class="diczx2">宋</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恐</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>袭之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谋袭</span><span class="diczx2">蔡州</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">率众袭之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:偷袭;奔袭;侵袭;奇袭;夜袭;空袭</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 衣上加衣 <span class="encs">[put]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寒不敢袭，痒不敢搔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 穿衣 <span class="encs">[wear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣尸曰袭。袭，匝也，以衣周匝覆之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释丧制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:袭尸(为死者穿衣);袭朝服</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 盖 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">张甲乙而袭翠被。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:袭裘(古代盛礼时，掩上裼衣而不使羔裘见于外)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 触及;熏染;侵袭 <span class="encs">[attack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">芳气袭人是酒香。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:袭人(侵袭到人。薰人)</span></p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8972_6108.svg" /><span class="swbf">(襲)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20087.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂇</span>衽袍。</span><span class="swdz">小斂，大斂，之前衣死者謂之襲。士䘮禮：乃襲三稱。注曰：遷尸於襲上而衣之，凡衣死者，左衽不紐。按喪大記：小斂大斂，祭服不倒，皆左衽結絞不紐，襲亦左衽不紐也。袍，褻衣也。記曰：纊爲繭、緼爲袍也。許曰：袍，襺也。士喪禮：襲衣有爵弁服、皮弁服、褖衣。注：褖衣所以表袍者，子羔之襲繭衣裳與稅衣爲一是也。斂始於襲，襲始於袍，故單言袍也。襲字引申爲凡揜襲之用。若記曰帛爲褶。士喪禮古文作襲。叚借字也。喪大記、玉藻用禮今文作褶。注曰：褶，袷也。有表裏而無著。許依古文禮，故不收褶字。凡經典重襲之義，如筮襲于夢，武王所用。祥襲則行，不襲則增，修德而改⺊，皆當作褶。褶義之引申。</span><span class="ywyy">从衣，龖省聲。</span><span class="swdz">似入切。七部。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8972_6109.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/277DF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧟟</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文襲不省。</span></p>                          </div>
