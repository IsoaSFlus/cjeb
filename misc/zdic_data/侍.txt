						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>侍</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 伺候，在旁边陪着：服～。～立。～者（侍奉左右的人）。～从。～读（给帝王讲学的人）。～坐。～役（仆人）。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>侍</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，寺声。本义:在尊长旁边陪着)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stand at sb.'s side in attendance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侍，承也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曾子侍。——<span class="smcs">《孝经》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“卑在尊者之侧为侍也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侍中。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·邳彤传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“有左右曹，入侍天子。故曰侍中。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中常侍。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·朱穆传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“秦官也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使侍人僚租告公。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂即天子位，群臣以次侍。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·文帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余立侍左右，援疑质理，俯身侧耳以请。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为服侍、侍奉 <span class="encs">[wait upon;attend upon;serve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子路</span>、 <span class="diczx2">曾皙</span><span class="diczx2">冉有</span>、 <span class="diczx2">公西华</span>侍坐。——<span class="smcs">《论语·先进》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">媵侍于户外。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执荐者百人侍西房。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正论·五祀》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:侍面(当面孝敬侍奉);侍祠(陪祭);侍巾巾节(侍侯你梳头洗脸;做妻子的谦称);侍箕帚(服侍洒扫，即做婢妾的意思);侍班阁子(值班室。内侍与禁卫值日办公处);侍御(在天子左右侍奉车驾的人);侍立</p><p><span class="diczx1">恭顺地站立在旁边伺候;侍臣(侍奉帝王的廷臣);侍生 (明清地方官拜访乡绅名流时的谦称;地方官见同辈或晚辈妇女时用的谦称;后辈对前辈的自称);侍中(官名。秦始置，两汉相沿，系正规官职外的加官之一。随侍皇帝)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 进言，进献 <span class="encs">[suggest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迁我京职，则汝朝夕侍母。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">荀欣</span>侍以选练举贤，任官使能。——<span class="smcs">《史记·赵世家》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>侍</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
