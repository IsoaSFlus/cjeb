                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>泥</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ní  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ní.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>土和水合成的东西：～巴。～垢。～浆。～煤。～淖（泥污的洼地）。～泞。～洼。</li><li>像泥的东西：枣～。山药～。印～（盖图章用的印色）。蒜～。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>泥</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>涂抹：～墙。～缝儿（<span class="dicpy">fèngr </span>）。</li><li>固执，死板：拘～。～古（拘泥古代的制度和说法，不根据具体情况加以变通）。～守。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">泥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>泥</strong> <span class="dicpy">ní</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ní.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，尼声。本义:泥水)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 中国古水名，泾水支流，即今甘肃省庆阳地区的东河及其下流马连河 <span class="encs">[Ni River]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泥，</span><span class="diczx2">泥水</span>也。出北地郁郅北蛮中。亦曰 <span class="diczx2">白马水</span>。一名 <span class="diczx2">东河</span>。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">北地郡</span> <span class="diczx2">郁郅县</span>， <span class="diczx2">泥水</span>出北蛮夷中。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泥土;泥巴 <span class="encs">[earth;mud]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥土惟涂泥。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">致远恐泥。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">存者无消息，死者为尘泥。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《无家别》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谁家新燕啄春泥。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《钱塘湖春行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泥中死者。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:泥木(土木);泥水(带泥土的水);泥牛(即土牛);泥冈子(土山冈);泥坂(土坡);泥灰(灰土);泥尘(尘土);泥墩墩(土墩子。比喻呆板无用的人);泥垡头(泥土块)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泥水，稀泥;烂泥 <span class="encs">[mud;mire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">震遂泥。——<span class="smcs">《易·震卦》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“坤土得雨为泥。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胡为乎泥中。——<span
