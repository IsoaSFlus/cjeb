                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">窈</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>窈</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，幼声。本义:幽深，深远)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[far-reaching]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窈，深远也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窈，深也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窈兮冥兮。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“深远之叹。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窈冥昼晦。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至道之精，窈窈冥冥。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·在宥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">由山上五六里，有穴窈然。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:窈杳(幽远的样子);窈纠(幽深曲折);窈悠(深远的样子);窈陷(深凹);窈妙(精微，幽远);窈冥(深远难测);窈坳(幽怨);窈郁(幽深的样子);窈丽(幽深而秀丽);窈蔼(深远;幽暗);窈停(深目高鼻的样子);窈深(幽深;深邃);窈黑(深邃幽暗);窈然(深远的样子;幽深的样子);窈蔚(幽深繁茂)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 幽静 <span class="encs">[quiet and secluded]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高堂俨象设，禅室各深窈。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《与客游道场何山得写字》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:窈寞(幽静);窈峭(高峻峭拨)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“杳”</span>。昏暗 <span class="encs">[dark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天窈窈而昼阴。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《长门赋》</span>。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“杳，冥也，当以杳为正字，窈通字。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窈霭潇湘空。——</span><span class="diczx2">江淹</span><span class="smcs">《杂体诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:窈昧(幽暗);窈窈(深冥的样子;幽暗的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 美好 <span class="encs">[fine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">月华临夜空，青山窈多姿。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">吴光</span><span class="smcs">《泊湘口二妃庙是萧湘二水会处》</span></p><p><span
