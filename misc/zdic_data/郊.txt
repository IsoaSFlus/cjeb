                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>郊</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从邑，交声。从<span class="yhcs">“邑”</span>，表示与城郭、行政区域有关。本义:上古时代国都外百里以内的地区称<span class="yhcs">“郊”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 郊区 <span class="encs">[outskirt;suburb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">郊，距国百里为郊。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，周时距离国都五十里的地方叫近郊，百里的地方叫远郊。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">适彼乐郊。——<span class="smcs">《诗·魏风·硕鼠》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">素服郊次。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洒江郊。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">始知郊田。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邑外谓之郊。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释地》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">郊二旬。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·质人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“远郊也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至于郊。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·觐礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓近郊。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:郊祀(古时帝王在郊外祭祀天地);郊圻(城邑的疆界。圻，地界);郊里(郊外的村落)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 田野 <span class="encs">[field]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">地可垦辟，悉为农郊，以赡萌。——<span class="smcs">《文选·司马相如·上林赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代祭天地的典礼<span class="encs">[worship to Heaven and Earth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">郊社所从来尚矣。——<span class="smcs">《史记·封禅书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:初郊(初即位而祀天);郊社(祭天地);郊赦(天子祭天于郊，而特赦天下的囚犯);郊畤(祭天的地方。畤，神灵居止之处)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Jiao town]</span>。春秋晋地。在今山西省运城县境</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎
