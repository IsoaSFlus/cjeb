                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>挹</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 舀;酌。把液体盛出来 <span class="encs">[ladle out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挹，抒也。从手，邑声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡以器斟酌于水谓之挹。——<span class="smcs">《珠丛》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弟子挹水而注之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·宥坐》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“酌也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维北有斗，不可以挹酒浆。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·大东》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">法流湛寂，挹之莫测其源。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:挹受(汲取，承受);挹盈(舀取盈满的液体);挹酌(汲取);挹彼注此(兹)(将彼器的液体倾注于此器);挹盈注虚(取有余以补不足);挹掬(捧取);挹斗扬箕(谓徒有虚名)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引;牵引 <span class="encs">[draw]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左挹浮丘袖，右拍洪崖肩。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《游仙诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">接倾城之貌，挹希世之人。——<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:挹胜(收取胜景);挹慕(牵念，羡慕)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 谦抑。通<span class="yhcs">“抑”</span> <span class="encs">[modestly restrain]</span>。如:挹降(谦抑);挹退(谦恭，退让)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“抑”</span>。抑制住 <span class="encs">[restrain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">富有四海，守之以谦，此所谓挹而损之之道也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·宥坐》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而久自挹损。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">情存损挹。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·光武帝纪下》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>挹</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 细致的样子 <span class="encs">[detail]</span>。如:挹挹</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 抑郁 <span class="encs">[melancholic]</span>。如:挹郁(愤懑怨恨);挹懕(抑郁不得志)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
