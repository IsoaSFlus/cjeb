                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>忌</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>嫉妒，憎恨：猜～。～才。～能。～妒。～贤妒能。</li><li>害怕，畏惧：顾～。肆无～惮。</li><li>禁戒：～戒。～食。～讳。禁～。</li><li>父母或祖先死亡的日子，迷信称不吉利的日子：～日。～辰。生～（已死父母的生日）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> jealous, envious; fear</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> meiden, sich von etw , jdn fernhalten  (V)​,neiden, beneiden, mißgönnen, Neid empfinden  (V)​,scheuen, fürchen, befürchten  (V)​,sich etw. abgewöhnen, etw. aufgeben  (V)​,eifersüchtig, neidisch  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> être jaloux,envier,s'abstenir de,se passer de</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>忌</strong> <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，己声。本义:憎恨)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忌，憎恶也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而忌处者。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不克不忌。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记·卫将军文子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子将助天为虐，不忌其不祥乎?——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:忌恶(憎恶坏人坏事);忌愤(怨愤，嫉恨)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 嫉妒 <span class="encs">[be jealous of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">操</span>虽称美，心甚忌之。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:忌前(妒嫉贤才);忌妇(妒妇，善妒的妻子);忌克(忌妒他人才能而想超过他);忌才(嫉妒他人的才华或才能)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 禁忌;忌讳 <span class="encs">[taboo;avoid;shun;esehew]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公室卑则忌直言。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说左下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忌怒，则能害己。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说疑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:忌三房(迷信者认为生病的人忌进新房、产房和灵房);忌作(唐宋习俗谓春、秋社日停做针线);忌针(阴历正月忌动针线);忌惮儿(忌讳害怕)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 顾忌 <span class="encs">[scruple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪吏害民无所忌。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《采诗官》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:忌畏(畏惧害怕);忌惮遮拦(顾虑遮掩);忌器(有所顾忌)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>忌</strong> <span
