                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>苕</strong> <span class="dicpy">sháo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sháo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> ——如<span class="yhcs">“红苕”</span><span class="encs">[sweet patato]</span>。甘薯的别名</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">tiáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>苕</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，召声。本义:陵苕。亦名<span class="yhcs">“凌霄”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“紫葳”</span>。<span class="yhcs">“蔓生草”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Chinese trumpet creeper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苕，苕草也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邛有旨苕。——<span class="smcs">《诗·陈风·东门之枌》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苕之华。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·苕之华》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 紫葳科落叶木质藤本，借气根攀附在其他物上。花冠漏斗状钟形，大而鲜艳，橘红色，栽培供观赏，花入药，破血去瘀。</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 如:苕华(花名。即凌霄花);苕荣(陵苕花。亦喻容貌之美);苕颖(草花和禾穗。亦泛指植物的花、穗及其茎)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 苕菜，即紫云英 <span class="encs">[Chinese milk vetch]</span>。豆科。一二年生草本。为优良的绿肥作物，也作蔬菜和家畜饲料</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 本作<span class="yhcs">“芀”</span>。芦苇的花。苕杆可为帚 <span class="encs">[spike of reed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茢，苕帚，所以扫不祥。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·夏宫下·戎右》</span></span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注</p><p><span class="diczx1">系之苇苕。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或苕发颖。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《文赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:苕折(苇苕断折。比喻寄身之地易遭危险);苕帚(用苇花编成的扫把。旧说可以除不祥)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Tiao River]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 如:苕水(水名<span
