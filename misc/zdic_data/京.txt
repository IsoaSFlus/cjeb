                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">京</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>京</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>国都（现特指中国首都北京）：～城。～都。～华（因京都是文物、人才汇集的地方，所以称京都为“京华”）。～畿（国都和国都附近的地方）。～剧。～师（首都的旧称）。～绣。～菜。</li><li>大。</li><li>古代数名，指一千万，亦指一亿兆。</li><li>中国少数民族，主要分布于广西壮族自治区：～族。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">京</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>京</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象筑起的高丘形，上为耸起的尖端。本义:人工筑起的高土堆)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[artificial mound]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">京，人所为绝京丘也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辨京陵。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筑重京。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·览冥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为京邱若山陵。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·禁塞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃觏于京。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·公刘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于堑里筑京，皆高五六丈。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·公孙瓒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:筑京(堆土为小山);京丘(为战死的人所筑的坟墓。即京观);京观(古代战争中，胜者为了炫耀武功，收集敌人尸首，封土而成的高冢);京冢(京观)。泛指高大的山丘 <span class="encs">[tall mourtian]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">望楚与堂，景山与京。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鄘风·定之方中》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:京峙(高丘);京陵(高丘大阜)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 方形大谷仓 <span class="encs">[big ban]</span>。如:京坻(谷米堆积如山。形容丰收);京囷(粮仓);京运(指运往京仓的粮食);京储(京仓的储备粮食)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 国都 <span class="encs">[capital]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">念彼京师。——<span class="smcs">《诗·曹风·下泉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辞帝京。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迁我京职。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">京中有善口技者。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至京。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君之始入京也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:京吏(京师各衙门的都吏。别于外吏而言);京圻(京都);京府(京畿。京城和京都附近的地方;国都所在地);京仓(京师储粮的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 京族 <span class="encs">[Jing nationality]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“鲸”</span> <span class="encs">[whale]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘钜鳞，骑京鱼。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:京鱼(鲸鱼)</p><p><span
