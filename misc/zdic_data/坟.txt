                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坟</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>坟</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">墳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&eacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>埋葬死人筑起的土堆：～墓。～地。～冢。～茔（坟地，坟墓）。</li>
	<li>水边高地：汶～。</li>
	<li>大：～首。～烛。</li>
	<li>古代的典籍：～典。～籍。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> grave, mound; bulge; bulging</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Grab  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tombe,tombeau</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坟</strong></p>
<p>墳 <span class="dicpy">f&eacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，贲( <span class="dicpy">bēn</span>)声。本义:大防)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[embankment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坟，大防。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释丘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坟莫大于</span><span class="diczx2">河</span>坟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅释地》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遵彼</span><span class="diczx2">汝</span>坟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;汝坟》</span>。传:&ldquo;大防也。&rdquo;注:&ldquo;或曰 <span class="diczx2">河</span>坟， <span class="diczx2">汝</span>坟、 <span class="diczx2">淮</span>坟也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 土堆 <span class="encs">[mound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">崇坟夷靡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《射雉赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;今呼为塘。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">登大坟以远望兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《九章&middot;哀郢》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坟家(用土堆成的坟包)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;濆&rdquo;。水边;沿河的高地 <span class="encs">[highland]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坟，地大也，青幽之间，凡土而高大者谓之坟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">背坟衍之广陆兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王粲</span><span class="smcs">《登楼赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遵彼汝坟，伐其条枚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;汝坟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坟延者六施，六七四十二尺而至於泉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;地员》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 特指坟堆，坟墓 <span class="encs">[grave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">墳，墓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，此字本训大防也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坟，冢。</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>之间谓之坟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言十三》</span>。注:&ldquo;坟取名于大防也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古也，墓而不坟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span>。注:&ldquo;土之高者谓之坟。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出郭门直视，但见近与坟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《古诗十九首》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古也墓而不坟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不治坟，欲为省。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;文帝本纪》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/58B3.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/213F6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡏶</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>符分切，音汾。【說文】墓也。【禮&middot;檀弓】古者墓而不墳。【註】土之高者曰墳。【衡山志】楚靈王之世，衡山崩，而祝融之墳壞，中有營丘九頭圖。【張衡&middot;思<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>賦】賭有黎之圮墳。【註】有黎，祝融也。</p>
<p>又水涯曰墳，大防也，所以扞水。【詩傳】辛受無道，商人慕文王而歸之，賦汝墳。</p>
<p>又【白虎通】三墳，分也。論三才之分，天地人之始也。【孔安國&middot;尚書序】伏犧，神農，黃帝之書，謂之三墳，言大道也。【左傳&middot;昭十三年】左史倚相，是能讀三墳，五典，八索，九丘。</p>
<p>又大也。【周禮&middot;秋官】司烜氏共墳燭。</p>
<p>又與羵同。【魯語】土之怪曰墳羊。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【正韻】房吻切【集韻】【韻會】父吻切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音憤。土膏肥也。【書&middot;禹貢】白墳，黑墳，赤埴墳。見土字註。</p>
<p>又【集韻】部本切，音笨。土沸起也。【左傳&middot;僖四年】公祭地，地墳。</p>
