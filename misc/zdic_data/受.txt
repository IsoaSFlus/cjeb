                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>受</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sh&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>接纳别人给的东西：接～。感～。～精。～权。～托。～降。～益。～业（①跟随老师学习；②学生对老师自称）。～教。～领。～聘。～理。</li>	<li>忍耐某种遭遇：忍～。～苦。～制。～窘。～累（受到劳累）。</li>	<li>遭到：遭～。～害。～挫。～屈。～辱。～阻。</li>	<li>适合，中：～吃。～看。～听。～使。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">受</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>受</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象两手（二&ldquo;又&rdquo;）中间有一只舟，&ldquo;舟&rdquo;亦是聲符，表示传递东西。本义:接受;承受)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[receive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受，相付也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span><span class="diczx2">王筠</span>曰:&ldquo;手部授，人部付，皆曰&ldquo;予也。&rdquo;今以付说受，则是受授同字矣。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受其币。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;司书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主妇拜受爵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;特牲馈食礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则受之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司干》</span>。注:&ldquo;取藏之。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受以小功衰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;丧服》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹承也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颛顼受之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span>。注:&ldquo;承也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">釜十五，吾受，而宫出之以百。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;海南》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">志士不饮盗泉之水，廉者不受嗟来之食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣修身洁行数十年，终不以监门困故而受公子财。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:受茶(女子受聘，订立婚约);受宫厅(衙门中的厅堂);受法(伏法);受事(接受教诲);受册(接受册命);受祉(接受天地神明的降福);受胙(接受胙肉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 蒙受;遭受 <span class="encs">[suffer;be subjected to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人又谁能以身之察察，受物之汶汶者乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民到于今受其赐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;宪问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一夫不耕，或受之饥，一女不织，或受之寒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾庐独破受冻死亦足。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
