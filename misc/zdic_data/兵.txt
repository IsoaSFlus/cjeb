                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兵</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>兵</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>武器：～器。～刃。～不血刃（兵器上面没有沾血，指不经过战斗而取得胜利）。</li><li>战士，军队：～士。～卒。～丁。～戎相见（指武装冲突）。</li><li>与军事或战争有关事物的统称：～法。～家。～机。～衅（战争的争端）。～书。～谏（进谏时以武力要挟，迫使必从）。～荒马乱。～贵神速。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>兵</strong> <span class="dicpy">bīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从廾，从斤。甲骨文字形，上面是<span class="yhcs">“斤”</span>，是短斧之类;下面是<span class="yhcs">“廾”</span>( <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span>，双手)，象双手持斤。本义:兵器，武器)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[arm;weapon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵，械也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌五兵。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司兵》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“戈、殳、戟、酋矛、夷矛也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陈五兵。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·庄公二十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“矛、戟、钺、楯、弓矢也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谓五方之兵，东矛、南弩、西戈、北铩、中央剑也。”——<span class="smcs">《匡谬正俗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">离为戈兵。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">修尔车马，弓矢戎兵。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">古之兵，戈、矛、弓、矢而已矣。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·议兵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵刃既接，弃甲曳兵而走。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵者，凶器也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵者，不祥之器。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵者，国之爪也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·七患》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">收天下之兵，聚之</span><span class="diczx2">咸阳</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郑穆公</span>使视客馆，则束载、厉兵、秣马矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃令骑皆下马步行，持短兵接战。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:短兵相接;兵不血刃;动刀兵;坚甲利兵;兵革(兵器甲胄的总称。引申指战争);兵解(古代方士以为学道者死于兵刃，供兵刃解脱躯壳以成仙，称为兵解);兵戈(干戈。借以指战争)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 士兵的群体，即整个军队 <span class="encs">[army;troop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">越</span>人之兵虽多，亦奚益於胜败哉?——<span class="smcs">《列子·虚实》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">必以</span><span class="diczx2">长安君</span>为质，兵乃出。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策四》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举兵伐</span><span class="diczx2">徐</span>，遂灭之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵</span>亦盛设兵待 <span
