                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>想</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>动脑筋，思索：感～。思～。～法。～象（配置组合而创造出新形象的心理过程）。～入非非。异～天开。幻～。</li><li>推测，认为：～必。～见（由推想而知道）。～来（表示只是根据推测，不敢完全肯定）。～当然（凭主观推测，认为事情应该是这样）。不堪设～。</li><li>希望，打算：休～。理～。～望。妄～。</li><li>怀念，惦记：～念。朝思暮～。</li><li>像：云～衣裳花～容。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。心形相声。本义:想念;怀念;羡慕)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[think;miss]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">想，冀思也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">览物想故国。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:想似(深切的怀念);想思(想念)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 思考，思索 <span class="encs">[think deeply;ponder]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入景响之无应兮，闻省想而不可得。——<span class="smcs">《二十年目睹之怪现状》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:冥思苦想;想来想去(反复的多方思考);想度(考虑，衡量)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 希望，想要 <span class="encs">[hope;want]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">那时他老人家正在用功，想中那名进士。——<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:想闻(想望，仰慕)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 料想;猜想 <span class="encs">[anticipate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余读</span><span class="diczx2">孔氏</span>书，想见其本人。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孔子世家论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:想料(料想)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 想象 <span class="encs">[imagine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">想早励良规。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="cino">(11)</span> 好像，如同 <span class="encs">[seem]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">云想衣裳花容，春风指槛露华浓。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《清平调词三首》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 怀念、回想 <span class="encs">[miss;recall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">想当年。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span                     <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>想</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄒㄧㄤˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>xiǎng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4074.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">思索、思考。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>想</b>办法」、「<b>想</b>不出所以然」。</span><span class="gc_yy">元·马致远〈夜行船·酒病花愁何日彻套·离亭宴煞〉：「煮酒烧红叶，<b>想</b>人生有限杯，浑几个重阳节。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">欲、要、打算、希望。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>想</b>结婚」、「<b>想</b>出国」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·刘琨·劝进表》：「四海<b>想</b>中兴之美，群生怀来苏之望。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">推测、猜度。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「料<b>想</b>」、「推<b>想</b>」、「猜<b>想</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《后汉书·卷七○·孔融传》：「以今度之，想当然耳。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">认为、觉得。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「你<b>想</b>这样对不对？」、「我<b>想</b>你应该回家一趟。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">思念、怀念。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「想念」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·李陵·答苏武书》：「望风怀<b>想</b>，能不依依？」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·杜甫〈客居〉诗：「览物<b>想</b>故国，十年别荒村。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">似、像。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·李白〈清平调〉三首之一：「云<b>想</b>衣裳花<b>想</b>容，春风拂槛露华浓。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·杜甫〈东屯月夜〉诗：「数惊闻雀噪，暂睡<b>想</b>猿蹲。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">念头、意念。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「梦<b>想</b>」、「理<b>想</b>」、「不作此<b>想</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·孔稚珪·北山移文》：「萧洒出尘之<b>想</b>。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
