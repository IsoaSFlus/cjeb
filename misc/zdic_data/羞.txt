                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>羞</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄡˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>感到耻辱：～耻。～辱。～恶。</li>	<li>难为情，害臊：害～。～惭。～臊（<span class="dicpy">s&agrave;o </span>）。～愧。含～。～赧（因害臊而红了脸的样子）。～怯。～涩。</li>	<li>使难为情：～人。你别～我。</li>	<li>进献：&ldquo;～玉芝以疗饥&rdquo;。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/馐">馐</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> disgrace, shame; ashamed; shy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> beschämt  (Adj)​,scheu  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> timidité,honte,timide,honteux,être intimidé,avoir honte</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">羞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>羞</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。据甲骨文，以手持羊，表示进献。小篆从羊，从丑，丑亦声。&ldquo;丑&rdquo;是手的讹变。本义:进献)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[offer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羞，进献也。从羊，羊所进也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以式法掌祭祀之戒具，与其荐羞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;宰夫》</span>。注:&ldquo;庶羞，内羞&rdquo;。按，内羞，房中之羞也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共其笾荐羞之实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;笾人》</span>。注:&ldquo;荐羞皆进也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可荐于鬼神，可羞于王公。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:羞豆(古代祭祀宴享时进献食物的一种盛器);羞膳(进献食品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 推荐;进用 <span class="encs">[recommend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有武德以羞为正卿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语九》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 假借为&ldquo;丑&rdquo;。感到耻辱 <span class="encs">[shame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或承之羞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易恒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟口启羞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;缁人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无作神羞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀身无益，适足增羞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李陵</span><span class="smcs">《答苏武书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:羞怍(羞耻，惭愧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 因惭愧而难为情 <span class="encs">[be shy;abash]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾羞，不忍为之下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十四为君妇，羞颜未尝开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《长干行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一则</span><span class="diczx2">贾环</span>羞口难开，二则 <span class="diczx2">贾环</span>也不在意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:羞蛾(美女的双眉);羞人子(羞答答的);羞杀人(羞死人);羞红(因害羞而脸红);羞脚(因怕羞而不敢向前);羞懒(羞愧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 嘲弄;侮辱 <span class="encs">[mock;poke fun at;insult]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那老子信了婆子的言语，带水带浆的羞辱毁骂了儿子几次。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《初刻拍案惊奇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:羞污(玷污);羞戮(羞辱);羞薄(轻视;鄙薄);羞丑(羞辱出丑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 怕 <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">看朱渐成碧，羞日不禁风。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《赠眼医婆罗门僧》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:羞影(怕看影子);羞明</span></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
