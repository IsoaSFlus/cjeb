<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> piebald horse; excellent horse</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schecke (Pferd mit geschecktem Fell)​  (S, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pie (cheval)​</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>骐</strong></p><p>騏 <span class="dicpy">qí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，其声。本义:青黑色有如棋盘格子纹的马)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dark-blue horse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骐，马青骊文如博棋也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四骐翼翼。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采芑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:骐骝(身有青黑斑纹而黑鬣的马)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 骏马 <span class="encs">[fine horse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘骐骥以驰聘兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:骐骥(骏马);骐骥一毛(比喻珍贵物品的极小部分)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“麒”</span>。麒麟 <span class="encs">[kylin;mythical unicorn in Chinese legends]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刳胎焚天，而骐麟不至。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策四》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/骐骥">骐骥</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=馬">馬</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9A0F.svg">                          <p>馬青驪，文如博棊也。从馬其聲。渠之切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">馬靑驪文如綦也。</span>如綦，各本作如博棊，不通。今依李善七發注、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>應書卷二卷四卷八正。凡馬言色者，如下文深黑色、淺黑色，全體之色也。其言某處黑、某處白、發白色、發赤色，一耑之色也。言襍毛者，謂其毛異色相錯，非比異色成片段者也。其曰文者，獨此言而已，謂異色成枝條相交，如文之錯畫然。下文青驪馬爲駽，謂其全體青黑色。此云青驪文如綦，謂白馬而有青黑紋路相交如綦也。綦，糸部作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26070.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦁰</span>，白蒼文也。綦者，青而近黑，秦風傳曰：騏，綦文也。魯頌傳曰：蒼騏曰騏。蒼騏卽蒼綦，謂蒼文如綦也。曹風其弁尹騏傳曰：騏，騏文也。正義作綦文。顧命騏弁，鄭注曰：青黑曰騏，本作綦弁。古多叚騏爲綦。<span class="jieshi">从馬其聲。</span>渠之切。一部。                          </div>
