                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>模</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&oacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄛˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>法式，规范，标准：～范。～式。楷～。～型。～本。～压。</li>
	<li>仿效：～仿（亦作&ldquo;摹仿&rdquo;）。～拟（亦作&ldquo;摹拟&rdquo;）。～写。</li>
	<li>特指&ldquo;模范&rdquo;：劳～。英～。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>模</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄨˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>〔～样〕➊人的长相或装束打扮的样子；➋表示约略的时间、怎么办；➌描摹。</li>
	<li>用压制或浇注的方法使材料成为一定形状的工具：～子。～板。～具。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">模</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>模</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，莫声。本义:铸造器物的模子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[model;pattern;standard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">模，法也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，水曰法，木曰模，土曰型，金曰镕，竹曰笵。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缤乎其犹模绣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大传》</span>。注:&ldquo;模所琢文章之范。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">授全模于梓匠。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者铸器，必先用蜡为模。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">赵希鹄</span><span class="smcs">《洞天清录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:模度(标准);模准(样板，规范);模宪(法则)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 楷式，榜样。特指值得学习的先进人物 <span class="encs">[example;ideal;model]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可为达士模。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《咏史》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劳模;评模;模楷(模范，榜样);模则(楷模，准则);模表(模范，表率);模范(榜样，表率)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>模</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 仿效，效法 <span class="encs">[imitate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彻书之金简，以身模之焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《汉武帝内传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:模次(逐字仿效);模效(模仿效法);模放(模仿);模则(效法);模造(依样制造，塑造)</span></p>
