<p><span class="dicpy">sh&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˊ</span></p>

	<li>俸禄：&ldquo;君子谋道不谋～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>日月亏缺或完全不见的现象：日～。月～。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>食</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从饣皀，饣声。本义:饭，饭食)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cooked cereals，especially rice;food]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食，饣米也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，六谷之饭曰食。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌王之食饮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;膳夫》</span>。注:&ldquo;饭也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">治其粮与其食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;廪人》</span>。注:&ldquo;止居曰食。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共王及后之六食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;馔人》</span>。注:&ldquo;六谷之饭。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小人有母，皆尝小人之食矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甘其食，美其服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孟尝君</span>使人给其食用，无使乏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食非多品，器皿非满案，不敢会宾友。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:食次(酒菜和点心之类;食品);食店(饭店);食垒(一种有几层屉的食盒);食嗓(食道);食忌(饮食的禁忌);食官(古代掌管饮食的官);食膳(膳食，肴馔);食为民天(饮食是人民最重要之事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 粮食 <span class="encs">[grains]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">籍兵乞食于</span><span class="diczx2">西周</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;西周策》</span>。注:&ldquo;粮也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食太阴在卯穰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;谓谷也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食，谓农殖嘉谷，可食之物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卖炭得钱何所营?身上衣裳口中食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百姓孰敢不箪食壶浆以迎将军者乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:口中食(吃的粮食);食地(可种粮食的田地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 食物的通称 <span class="encs">[food]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">廉者不受嗟来之食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐羊子妻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">野兽在深山里没有食吃。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《祝福》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:猪食;鸡食;猫食</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 食禄，俸禄 <span class="encs">[official&#39;s salary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子谋道而不谋食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;卫灵公》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 食墨亦简作&ldquo;食&rdquo;。龟卜的术语。古代龟卜兆与墨画重合叫&ldquo;食墨&rdquo;，为吉兆 <span class="encs">[omen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">召伯相宅，卜惟洛食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张衡&middot;东京赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 亏损。后作&ldquo;蚀&rdquo; <span class="encs">[loss]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">易腐败而食之货勿留。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 特指日蚀或月蚀 <span class="encs">[eclipse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼月而食，则维其常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日中则昃，月盈则食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;丰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三年春王二月，己巳，日有食之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公三年经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是故圣王日食则修德，月食则修刑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;四时》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">长铗归来乎，食无鱼!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾恐</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>人食之不得下咽也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狗彘之畜，无失其时，七十者可以食肉矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:废寝忘食;以食食( <span class="dicpy">s&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )人;食母;食以草具;吞食;食犬(专供食用的狗);食酒(饮酒);食茶(自己饮用的茶);食息(吃饭休息，泛指休息);食藿悬鹑(形容贫穷困苦);食饩(即&ldquo;补廪&rdquo;。生员中的附生递补为廪生);食獐(吃獐子);食肉寝皮(食其肉而寝处其皮。形容痛恨之极)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 靠着吃饭;赖以为生。引申为依赖、依靠 <span class="encs">[depend on;rely on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尊为公侯，食邑万户。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张安世传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:食力(依赖民众的赋税生活);食采(受纳采地的租税);食邑(卿大夫的封地。收封地的租税以供食用，故称&ldquo;食邑&rdquo;，或称&ldquo;采邑&rdquo;);食俸(官吏每月每岁所受的薪俸。又称&ldquo;食禄&rdquo;);食加(靠大夫的田亩增加而生活);食官(靠官府发的粮食而生活);食租衣税(靠收税而生活);食职(靠职务所得而生活)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 享受;受 <span class="encs">[enjoy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居右，食嘉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:自食其果;食征(享用税收);食德(享受先人的德泽);食报(受报答或受报应);食俸(享受俸禄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 垦耕 <span class="encs">[reclaim]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我死则择不食之地而葬我焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:食力(依靠自己劳力而生活)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 背弃<span class="encs">[诺言]</span> <span class="encs">[go back on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔无不信，朕不食言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;汤誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:食言而肥</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 接受，采纳 <span class="encs">[accept]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贤圣不能正不食谏诤之君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 祭献，享祀 <span class="encs">[offer sacrifice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哭汝既不闻汝言，奠汝又不见汝食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:食监(官名。汉置。掌管晦时祭祀)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">食</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=食" target="_blank">食</a>】
                              食·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/98DF.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29683.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩚃</span>餐【唐韻】乗力切【集韻】【韻會】實職切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蝕。【說文】一米也。【玉篇】飯食。【增韻】殽饌也。</p>
<p>又茹也，啗也。【釋名】食，殖也，所以自生殖也。【古史考】古者茹毛飮血，燧人鑽火，而人始裹肉而燔之，曰炮。及神農時，人方食穀，加米于燒石之上而食之。及黃帝，始有釜甑，火食之道成矣。【易&middot;需卦】君子以飮食宴樂。【書&middot;益稷】暨稷播，奏庶艱食，鮮食。【傳】衆難得食處，則與稷教民播種之，決川有魚鱉，使民鮮食之。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;洪範】惟辟玉食。【註】珍食也。</p>
<p>又食祿也。【禮&middot;坊記】君子與其使食浮于人也，寧使人浮于食。</p>
<p>又祭曰血食。【史記&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>涉世家】置守冢三十家碭，至今血食。</p>
<p>又飮酒亦曰食。【前漢&middot;于定國傳】定國食酒，至數石不亂。</p>
<p>又耳食。【史記&middot;六國表】學者牽于所聞，見秦在帝位日淺，不察其終始，因舉而笑之，不敢道，此與以耳食無異。</p>
<p>又目食。【宋史&middot;司馬光傳】飮食所以爲味也，適口斯善矣。世人取果餌刻鏤之，朱綠之，以爲槃案之翫，豈非以目食乎。</p>
<p>又吐而復吞曰食。【書&middot;湯誓】朕不食言。【左傳&middot;僖十五年】我食吾言，背天地也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】食，僞也。【疏】言而不行，如食之消盡，故通謂僞言爲食言，故此訓食爲僞也。</p>
<p>又蠱惑曰食。【管子&middot;君臣篇】明君在上，便嬖不能食其意。</p>
<p>又消也。【左傳&middot;哀元年】伍員曰：後雖悔之，不可食已。【註】食，消也。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;洛誥】乃卜澗水東瀍水西惟洛食。【傳】卜必先墨畫龜，然後灼之，兆順食墨。</p>
<p>又日食，月食。【易&middot;豐卦】月盈則食。【春秋&middot;隱三年】日有食之。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;襄九年】晉侯問於士弱曰：吾聞之，宋災，於是乎知有天道，何故。對曰：古之火正或食於心或食於咮，以出内火。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;檀弓】我死，則擇不食之地而葬我焉。【註】不食，謂不墾耕。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;外戚傳】房與宮對食。【註】應劭曰：宮人自相與爲夫婦，名對食。房宮，二人名。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】食閻，勸也。南楚凡已不欲喜而旁人說之，不欲怒而旁人怒之，謂之食閻。</p>
<p>又寒食，節名。【荆楚歲時記】去冬至一百五日，卽有疾風甚雨，謂之寒食。</p>
<p>又大食，國名，在西域波斯國西，都婆羅門，兵刃勁利，勇于野鬬。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】戲名。博屬。</p>
<p>又姓。漢有食子通。【希姓錄】後漢食于公。</p>
<p>又【集韻】祥吏切【正韻】相吏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音寺。【論語】有酒食，先生饌。【禮&middot;曲禮】食居人之左。【註】食，飯屬也。</p>
<p>又糧也。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;廩人】匪頒賙赐稍食。</p>
<p>又以食與人也。【詩&middot;小雅】飮之食之。【禮&middot;內則】國君世子生，卜士之妻，大夫之妾使食子。【註】食謂乳養之也。【左傳&middot;文元年】穀也食子。【註】食，養生也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>羊吏切，音異。【廣韻】人名。漢審食其，酈食其。【荀悅&middot;漢紀】作異基。</p>
<p>又叶式灼切，音爍。【易林】三河俱合，水怒踊躍。壞我王室，民困于食。</p>

                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷五">卷五</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷五&amp;swbs=食">食</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/98DF.svg">                          <p>一米也。从皀亼聲。或說亼皀也。凡食之屬皆从食。乘力切〖注〗餐、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29683.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩚃</span>，古文食。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2028A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠊊</span>，食本字，从皀亼聲。㿝，古文香。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/29681_10266.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29681.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩚁</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">亼米也。</span><span class="swdz">各本作一米也，玉篇同，葢孫強時已誤矣。韵會本作米也，亦未是。今定爲亼米也，由亼字俗罕用而誤也。以合下云亼口例之，則此當亼米信矣。亼，集也，集衆米而成食也。引伸之，人用供口腹亦謂之食。此其相生之名義也。下文云：飯，食也。此食字引伸之義也。人食之曰飯，因之所食曰飯。猶之亼米曰食，因之用供口腹曰食也。食下不曰飯也者何也，食者自物言，飯者自人言，嫌其義不顯，故不以飯釋食也。飯下何以云食也，自籑篆以下皆自人言，故不嫌也。周禮膳夫職注曰：食，飯也。曲禮食居人之左注：食，飯屬也。凡今人食分去入二聲，飯分上去二聲，古皆不如此分别。</span><span class="ywyy">从皀亼聲。或說亼皀也。</span><span class="swdz">此九字當作从亼皀三字，經淺人竄改不可通。皀者，榖之馨香也，其字从亼皀，故其義曰亼米。此於形得義之例。乘力切。一部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2028A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠊊</span>之屬皆从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2028A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠊊</span>。</span><span class="swdz">鍇本此下有讀若粒三字。衍文。</span></p>                          </div>

			<div class="gscdl">——《为政》子曰：“贤哉，回也！一箪<span>食</span>，一瓢饮，在陋巷，人不堪其忧，回也不改其乐。贤哉，回也！”</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孔子弟子及其再传弟子 《《论语》十二章》</div><div class="gscdl">——《雍也》子曰：“饭疏<span>食</span>饮水，曲肱而枕之，乐亦在其中矣。”</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孔子弟子及其再传弟子 《《论语》十二章》</div><div class="gscdl">杞人有忧天地崩坠，身亡所寄，废寝<span>食</span>者。</div><div class="gscdr">《列子》《杞人忧天》</div><div class="gscdl">便要还家，设酒杀鸡作<span>食</span>。</div><div class="gscdr">魏晋·陶渊明 《桃花源记》</div><div class="gscdl">余人各复延至其家，皆出酒<span>食</span>。停数日，辞去。</div><div class="gscdr">魏晋·陶渊明 《桃花源记》</div><div class="gscdl">虽有嘉肴，弗<span>食</span>，不知其旨也。</div><div class="gscdr">礼记•学记《虽有嘉肴》</div>      </div>
