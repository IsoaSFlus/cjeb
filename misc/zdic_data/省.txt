                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>省</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shěng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shěng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄥˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>地方行政区域：～份。～会。</li>	<li>节约，不费：～钱。～事。～吃俭用。</li>	<li>简易，减免：～略。～称。～写。</li>	<li>中国古官署名：中书～（①魏晋开始设置，总管国家政务，历代有所沿革，唐初设&ldquo;中书、尚书、门下&rdquo;三省共管政事；②元代&ldquo;中书省&rdquo;兼管&ldquo;尚书省&rdquo;的职权，权更重，成为中央最高的官署，称地方最高行政官署为&ldquo;行中书省&rdquo;，简称&ldquo;省&rdquo;，是现在&ldquo;省&rdquo;的来历）。尚书～。门下～。秘书～。</li>	<li>古代称王宫禁地：～中。～闼（禁中）。</li></ol><p>● <strong>省</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄥˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>检查：反～（检查自己）。～察（考察）。吾日三～吾身。</li>	<li>知觉，觉悟：～悟。发人深～。</li>	<li>看望父母、尊亲：～亲。～视。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>省</strong> <span class="dicpy">shěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shěng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从&ldquo;目&rdquo;，从&ldquo;屮&rdquo;（甲骨文或从&ldquo;木&rdquo;），象眼睛观察草木，&ldquo;屮&rdquo;声化为&ldquo;生&rdquo;，&ldquo;生&rdquo;滞古未加中横，隶书底横变撇，&ldquo;丷&rdquo;变&ldquo;八&rdquo;，遂变为&ldquo;少&rdquo;形。( <span class="dicpy">xǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐng.mp3"></a></span> )本义:察看)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 王宫禁署，禁中 <span class="encs">[the emperor&#39;s living quarter in his palace]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禁台省中，连闼对门。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:省中(宫禁之中);省内(宫禁之内)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 官署名称 <span class="encs">[ministry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">投词院直至省，将冤屈叫几声，诉出咱这实情。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《陈州粜米》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 行政区域名 <span class="encs">[province]</span>。元代中央置中书省，于各路设行中书省，称为行省。明代改行省为布政使司，自后即以省为地方行政区域的通称</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>， <span class="diczx2">怀庆</span><span class="diczx2">青华</span>镇人，工技击，七省好事者皆来学。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:省元(礼部考试中的第一名进士。因礼部属尚书省，故称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;眚&rdquo;。灾祸;过失 <span class="encs">[catastrophe;calamity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大省者何?灾省也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;庄公二十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王省惟岁，卿士惟月，师尹惟日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃惟省哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《潜夫论&middot;述赦》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王继培</span>云:&ldquo;省哉，今作眚灾。&rdquo;</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>省</strong> <span class="dicpy">shěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shěng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 减少;精简 <span class="encs">[decrease]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拜至献酬辞让之节繁，及介省矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乡饮酒义》</span>。注;&ldquo;小减曰省。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省囹圄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令&middot;仲春》</span>。注:&ldquo;省，减也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省攻伐之心。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策四》</span>。注:&ldquo;省，减也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省商贾之数。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;富国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">由父省也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;吴王濞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故圣王之诛也綦省矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;仲尼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省徭役，减征赋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴主传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:省刑(减少或减轻刑罚);省笔(减少字的笔画)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 节约 <span class="encs">[economize;save]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今为计莫若先省国用。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:省时间;能省的就省;省工;省口(不说话);省陌(亦作&ldquo;省钱&rdquo;。把不足一百的钱钞当作一百叫作&ldquo;省陌&rdquo;)省衣节食(犹言节衣缩食。指生活节俭)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 免除;非除 <span class="encs">[avoid;remit;remove]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哥哥可省烦恼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:这个字不能省;省却(免去;除掉);省罢(裁减;罢免);省可(省可里。省得，免得，休要);省释(免罪释放)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>省</strong> <span class="dicpy">xǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[examine oneself critically]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省，视也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省，察也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省其文采。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span>。注:&ldquo;审也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">退而省其私。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:&ldquo;视也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省农功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span>。注:&ldquo;观也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王省惟岁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先王以省方观民设教。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;观》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:省方(视察四方);省耕(古代帝王巡视春耕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 检查 <span class="encs">[examine one&#39;s thoughts and conduct]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾日三省吾身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;学而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日省月试。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span>。注:&ldquo;考校其成功也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:省心改过(反省内心，改正错误);内省;省牲之所(屠宰、烹饪牛羊等祭品的地方。祭祀前主祭和助祭人要省查祭品，以示虔诚，叫省牲);反省</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 探望，问候(多指对尊长) <span class="encs">[visit one&#39;s parents or elders living at another place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昏定而晨省。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;问其安否何如。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈子灿</span>省兄 <span class="diczx2">河南</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">次日起来，省过</span><span class="diczx2">贾母</span>，因往 <span class="diczx2">王夫人</span>处来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:省拜(谒见。叩拜父母长辈等);省宫闱(应召入宫探视);省候(探望问候);省晨(早晨问候);省问;省墓(扫墓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 明白 <span class="encs">[be aware]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我方才这般与你说了，你还不省?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:省会(喻示;晓喻);省着(知道;晓得);省谕(通知;告知);省得(懂得);省不的(不明白;不懂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 醒悟 <span class="encs">[be aware;become conscious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽然是眼角儿传情，咱两个口不言心自省。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:不省人事;省得(醒悟);省释(劝化并释放);深省;猛省</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">shěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shěng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7701.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B80.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡮀</span>䁞【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>息幷切，騂上聲。【說文】視也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】察也。【易&middot;觀卦】先王以省方觀民設敎。【論語】吾日三省吾身。</p><p>又【廣韻】審也。【正字通】明也。【列子&middot;楊朱篇】實僞之辨，如此其省也。</p><p>又孟孫陽曰：一毛微於肌膚，肌膚微於一節，省矣。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】善也。【詩&middot;大雅】帝省其山。【箋】省，善也。【禮&middot;大傳】省于其君。【註】善于其君，謂免于大難也。</p><p>又【小爾雅】過也。【史記&middot;秦始皇紀】飾省宣義。【註】飾，文飾也。省，過也。</p><p>又【博雅】省省，不安也。【揚子&middot;方言】秦晉之閒謂不安爲省省。</p><p>又【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>所景切，生上聲。禁署也。【前漢&middot;昭帝紀】帝姊長公主共養省中。【蔡邕云】本爲禁中，避元后父名改曰省中。【師古曰】省，察也。言入此中者當察視，不可妄也。</p><p>又【唐書&middot;百官志】官司之別曰省，曰臺，如尚書，黃門，中書，祕書，殿中，內侍六省是也。【韻會】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A3D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨽</span>。或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D65.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨵥</span>。</p><p>又【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>也。【韻會】少也。【左傳&middot;僖二十一年】貶食省用。【禮&middot;鄉飲&middot;酒義】拜至獻酬，辭讓之節繁，及介省矣。【註】小減曰省。【史記&middot;李將軍傳】莫府省約文書籍事。【註】省，少也。</p><p>又【釋名】省，嗇也。曜嗇約少之言也。</p><p>又藕名。【淸異錄】北戎藕止三孔，漢語轉譯其名曰省事三。</p><p>又姓。【左傳】宋大夫省臧。</p><p>又與眚通。【書&middot;洪範】王省惟歲。【史記&middot;宋世家】作眚。【公羊傳&middot;莊二十二年】春王正月，肆大省。【左傳】【穀梁】作眚。</p><p>又【集韻】息淺切，音蘚。同獮。秋田也。【禮&middot;玉藻】惟君有黼裘，以誓省。【註】省，當爲獮。　【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25171.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥅱</span>。从睂省，从屮。【徐鉉曰】屮，通識也。【同文舉要】舊从目从屮，徹於目者，能省視。</p><p>又从少从目。凡物少經目則省事。眇同意。【字彙補】又作①。①字原作屮下省。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7701.svg">                          <p>視也。从眉省，从屮。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7701_1.svg" /><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B80.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡮀</span>，古文从少从囧。所景切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：屮，通識也。</p>                          </div>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7701_4269.svg" /><span class="swbf">(省)</span><span class="ywyy">視也。</span><span class="swdz">省者，察也。察者，覈也。漢禁中謂之省中。師古曰：言入此中者皆當察視。不可妄也。釋詁曰：省，善也。此引伸之義。大傳曰：大夫有大事省於其君。謂君察之而得其大善也。</span><span class="ywyy">从睂省，从屮。</span><span class="swdz">屮音徹。木初生也。財見也。从眉者，未形於目也。从屮者，察之於微也。凡省必於微。故引伸爲減省字。說文有渻<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2179E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡞞</span>二字。然經傳多作省。所景切。亦息井切。十一部。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7701_4270.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B80.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡮀</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文省，从少囧。</span><span class="swdz">按囧非也。古文目作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/211E1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡇡</span>。此與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2136C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡍬</span>皆從之。从少目者，少用其目省之。用甚微也。</span></p>                          </div>
