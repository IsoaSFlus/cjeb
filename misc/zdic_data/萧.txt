                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">萧</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>萧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">蕭</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>即&ldquo;艾蒿&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>冷落，没有生气的样子：～然。～瑟。～索。～飒。～森。～骚。～疏。～条。</li>
	<li>〔～～〕①象声词，形容马叫声或风声，如&ldquo;风～～兮易水寒&rdquo;；②头发花白稀疏的样子，如&ldquo;华发～～老遂良，一身萍挂海中央&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>〔～墙〕照壁，喻内部，如&ldquo;祸起～～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓，如南朝梁有萧统。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> common artemisia; dejected</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> traurig ,Xiao  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> triste,désert</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">萧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>萧</strong></p>
<p>蕭 <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，肃声。本义:艾蒿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[worm wood]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">萧，艾蒿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">萧萩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:&ldquo;即蒿。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共萧茅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;甸师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼采萧兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;采葛》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:萧艾(臭草名。比喻不肖或平凡无才);萧敷艾荣(比喻凡事委曲求全，以致飞黄腾达)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Xiao state]</span>。春秋时宋的附庸，灭于楚。地在今安徽省萧县西北</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚子</span>伐 <span class="diczx2">萧</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 县名 <span class="encs">[Xiao county]</span>。在安徽省北端，东邻江苏省，西接河南省。秦置县</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">萧君圭</span><span class="diczx2">君玉</span>(萧君圭，字君玉)。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>萧</strong></p>
<p>蕭 <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 萧条，冷落;荒凉 <span class="encs">[desolate;dreary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天寒夜长，风气萧索，鸿雁于征，草木黄落。 &mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《自祭文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蚊蚋归丰草，枯叶散萧林。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘伶</span><span class="smcs">《北芒客舍》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">满目萧然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屋舍萧然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/856D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】蘇彫切【集韻】先凋切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音簫。艾蒿也。【詩&middot;王風】彼采蕭兮。【疏】今人所謂荻蒿者是也。【禮&middot;郊特牲】蕭合黍稷，臭陽達于牆屋。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】蕭蕭馬鳴。【註】聲也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;食貨志】江淮之閒，蕭然煩費。【註】蕭然猶騷然，勞動之貌。</p>
<p>又蕭條，寂寥貌。【宋玉&middot;九辨】蕭瑟兮草木搖落而變衰。【註】隂令促，急風疾暴也。</p>
<p>又【論語】不在顓臾，而在蕭牆之內。【何晏註】蕭之言肅也，牆謂屛也。君臣相見之禮，至屛而加肅敬。</p>
<p>又斧名。【左思&middot;魏都賦】蕭斧戢柯以柙刃。【說文註】蕭斧，芟艾之斧也。</p>
<p>又國名。【左傳&middot;文十四年】宋高哀爲蕭封人。【註】蕭，宋附庸國。</p>
<p>又關名。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】北出蕭關。</p>
<p>又姓。【潛夫論】蕭氏，殷舊姓也。</p>
<p>又叶音修。【毛詩&middot;古音考】冽彼下泉，浸彼苞蕭。叶下周。【屈原&middot;九歌】風颯颯兮木蕭蕭。叶下憂。◎按《周禮&middot;地官&middot;甸師》蕭茅註：鄭讀所六反，至杜子春始讀爲簫。</p>
