                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>禽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qín  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qín.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>鸟、兽的总称：五～戏。</li><li>特指鸟类：家～。飞～走兽。</li><li>古通“<span class="diczx3">擒</span>”：“不～二毛。”</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">禽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>禽</strong> <span class="dicpy">qín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。本义:走兽总名)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[beasts]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禽，走兽总名。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禽者何?鸟兽之总名——<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命主祠祭禽于四方。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“兽之通名也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">傅翼戴角，分牙布爪，仰飞俯走，谓之禽兽。——<span class="smcs">《列子·黄帝》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六禽。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·庖人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“宜为羔豚、犊、麝、雉、鴈。凡鸟兽未孕曰禽。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终日而不获一禽。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾有一术，名五禽之戏。一曰虎，二曰鹿，三曰熊，四曰猿，五曰鸟。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·华陀传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虎亦诸禽之雄也。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡·遭虎》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 鸟类的总称 <span class="encs">[birds]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二足而羽谓之禽。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六禽。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·庖人》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“鴈、鹑、鷃、雉、鸠、鸽也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">田有禽虞。——<span class="smcs">《易·师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执禽者左首。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“鸟也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">越</span>禽不恋 <span class="diczx2">燕</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《左风五十九首》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:鸣禽;禽言(鸟语。指鸟类啼鸣);禽语(鸟语;鸟鸣);禽卵(鸟卵);禽戏(禽鸟嬉戏);家禽</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 鸟兽总称 <span class="encs">[birds and beasts]</span>。如:禽门(鸟兽的境界);
