                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>索</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">suǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄛˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>大绳子或大链子：～子。～道。钢～。绞～。线～。</li>
	<li>搜寻，寻求：～引。思～。搜～。探～。</li>
	<li>讨取，要：～还。～求。～取。勒～。</li>
	<li>尽，毫无：～然无味。</li>
	<li>单独：离群～居。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">索</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>索</strong> <span class="dicpy">suǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。意为用绳子把木头束起。 <span class="diczx2">于省吾</span>认为是绳索。本义:大绳子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[large rope]</span>。后泛指各种绳索</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">索，草有茎叶可作绳索。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其次关木索，被箠楚受辱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鹿裘带索，鼓琴而歌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列士&middot;天瑞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">追讨南度河，使军吏田晏、夏育慕先登，悬索相引。复战于罗亭，大破之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;段颍传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昼尔于茅，宵而索綯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:索苇(用苇作成的绳索);索头(绳头);索桥(用绳索连结两岸的桥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 人的发辫 <span class="encs">[pigtail]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:索葛(头发);索虏(发辫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 法度 <span class="encs">[law]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆启以</span><span class="diczx2">商</span>政，疆以 <span class="diczx2">周</span>索。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;定公四年》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:&ldquo;索，法也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 链条 <span class="encs">[chain]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:索桥</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 中国古地名 <span class="encs">[Suo town]</span>。故地在今河南荥阳县。秦末刘邦与项羽战于京、索之间的索，即此地</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 索县 <span class="encs">[Suo county]</span>。中国汉代建立的县，在今湖南省常德市东。属武陵郡，顺帝时更名汉寿。今为湖南汉寿县</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>索</strong> <span class="dicpy">suǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 搜索 <span class="encs">[search;try to find out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">索，入家搜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7D22.svg">                          <p>艸有莖葉，可作繩索。从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>糸。杜林說：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>亦朱木字。蘇各切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7D22_4834.svg" /><span class="swbf">(索)</span><span class="ywyy">艸有莖葉可作繩索。</span><span class="swdz">當云索繩也，與糸部繩索也爲轉注，而後以艸有莖葉可作繩索發明从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>之意，今本乃淺人所刪耳。爾雅曰：紼，繂也。謂大索。經史多假索爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A61.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡩡</span>字。又木部曰：澌，水索也。索訓盡。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>糸。</span><span class="swdz"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>糸者，謂以艸莖葉糾繚如絲也。詩曰：晝爾于茅，宵爾索綯。以艸者，如<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26CCB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦳋</span>秆茅麻是。以竹者，竹部之筊是。以木者，椶櫐之屬是。穌各切。五部。</span><span class="ywyy">杜林說：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>亦朱木字。</span><span class="swdz">未詳。疑當作索亦朱市字。市者，篆文韍也。杜林說索爲韍字，从糸<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>聲。與杜林說構爲桷字，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26958.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦥘</span>爲貶損字，畁爲麒麐字，鼂爲朝旦字，正同。</span></p>                          </div>
