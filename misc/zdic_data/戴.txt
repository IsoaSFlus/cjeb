                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>戴</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>加在头、面、颈、手等处：～帽子。披星～月。～圆履方。不共～天。</li>
	<li>尊奉，推崇，拥护：～仰。爱～。拥～。感恩～德。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wear on top; support</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> tragen, anziehen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mettre,porter (des lunettes, des gants, un chapeau)​,respecter,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>戴</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:增益)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分物得增益曰戴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;引申之凡加于上皆曰戴。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古国名。故址在今河南省民权县东 <span class="encs">[Dai State]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>人、 <span class="diczx2">蔡</span>人、 <span class="diczx2">卫</span>人伐 <span class="diczx2">戴</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>戴</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 加在头、颈、面、肩上、胸上 <span class="encs">[wear;put on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戴，覆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日抱戴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孝经&middot;援神契》</span>。注:&ldquo;在上曰戴。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戴胜降于桑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垂发戴白。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;邓禹传》</span>。注:&ldquo;戴白，父母也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颁白者(老人)不负戴于道路矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无戴其苍，地履其黄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戴朱缨宝饰之帽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻喜宴独不戴花。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:戴笠(戴斗笠);戴头识脸(有身分，有面子;很有派头);戴巾的(指士大夫阶级);戴假发;戴耳环;戴金戒指;戴盆(将盆覆戴在头上);披星戴月(顶着星星和月亮);戴白(满头都是斑白的发丝。形容人老);她洗了手套，发现它们戴不上了</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6234.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22A07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢨇</span>【唐韻】都代切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】丁代切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A4B4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪒴</span>去聲。【說文】分物得增益曰戴。一曰首戴也。【廣韻】荷戴也。【書&middot;大禹謨】衆非元后何戴。【孟子】頒白者不負戴於道路矣。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋地】途出其前戴丘。【疏】道過丘南，若爲道負戴。</p>
<p>又値也。【禮&middot;喪大記】君纁戴六。【註】戴之言値也。【爾雅&middot;釋地】戴日爲丹穴。【註】値也。【疏】値日之下，其處名丹穴。</p>
<p>又【諡法】典禮無愆曰戴。【左傳&middot;隱三年】其娣戴嬀生桓公。【註】戴謂諡。</p>
<p>又姓。出濟北，本宋戴穆公後。</p>
<p>又或作載。【禮&middot;月令】載靑旂。【詩&middot;周頌】載弁俅俅。【音義】如字。</p>
<p>又與戴同。</p>
<p>又【韻會】作代切，音再。地名。【春秋&middot;隱十年】宋人，蔡人，衞人伐戴。【註】戴國，今<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>留外黃縣東南有戴城。【釋文】戴音再。</p>
