                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">素</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>素</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">s&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>本色，白色：～服。～丝。</li>	<li>颜色单纯，不艳丽：～净。～淡。～妆。～雅。～描。</li>	<li>洁白的绢：尺～（用绸子写的信）。</li>	<li>本来的，质朴、不加修饰的：～质。～养。～性。～友（真诚淳朴的朋友）。</li>	<li>物的基本成分：色～。毒～。维生～。</li>	<li>向来：～来。～常。～志。平～。</li>	<li>白，不付代价：～餐。</li>	<li>非肉类的食品，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">荤</span>&rdquo;相对：～食。～席。～油。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> white (silk); plain; vegetarian; formerly; normally</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Element  (S)​,Gemüse  (S, Ess)​,einfach, schlicht, schmucklos  (Adj)​,einfarbig  (Adj)​,vegetarisch  (Adj, Ess)​,Su  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> simple,blanc,végétarien,maigre,toujours,élément</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">素</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>素</strong> <span class="dicpy">s&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。上是&ldquo;垂&rdquo;，下是糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)。糸，丝。织物光润则易于下垂。本义:没有染色的丝绸)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[white silk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">素，白致缯也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纯以素。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;杂记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">素服哭于库门之外。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十三能织素，十四学裁衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若士必奴，伏尸二人，流血五步，天下缟素，今日是也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:素幭(古代盖在车前横木上的白绸);素服缟冠(丧服，素、缟，指白绘)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用作写字的丝绸或纸张 <span class="encs">[silk or paper for writing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">英骨秀气，洒落毫素。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《化城寺大钟铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又指用绢帛纸张写的书籍或信件</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客从远方来，遗我双鲤鱼。呼儿烹鲤鱼，中有尺素书。&mdash;&mdash;古乐府<span class="smcs">《饮马长城窟行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 本质;本性 <span class="encs">[nature]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如彼梓材，弗勤丹漆，虽劳朴斫，终负素质。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张华&middot;励志诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:根素(根本);素怀(本心)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 带根本性的物质或构成事物的基本成分 <span class="encs">[element]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:核素;毒素;色素;维生素;元素(构成物质的基本成分);毒素(某些有机体产生的有毒物质);因素(构成事物的本质成分);要素(构成事物的必要因素)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 蔬菜瓜果等副食 <span class="encs">[vegetables]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古之民未知为饮食时，素食而分处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;辞过篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">果蓏素食当十石。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;禁藏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:三荤一素;吃素;素什锦(杂取诸种蔬菜配合而成的一种素肴);素膳(素食)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 旧交 <span class="encs">[old friend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">素友俱薄世，屡招清景赏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韦应物</span><span class="smcs">《慈恩伽蓝清会》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:素故(旧交);素情(旧交，旧情);素结(故交);素旧(旧交)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓 <span class="encs">[surname]</span>。三国魏有素利</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>素</strong> <span class="dicpy">s&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 白色，雪白 <span class="encs">[white]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦伯</span>素服郊次，乡向而器。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">服尚素玄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《东都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娥娥红粉妆，纤纤出素手。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《古诗十九首》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">要什么素车白马，断送出古陌荒阡?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">关汉卿</span><span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:素纸(白纸;没有写过字的纸);素衣将敝(比喻人处境艰难，生活困苦);素练(白色的熟绢);素车(以白土涂饰的车);素风(秋风);素秋(即秋季，古代阴阳五行家以金配秋，其色白，故称秋季为&ldquo;素秋&rdquo;);素娥(指月宫中仙女&ldquo;嫦娥&rdquo;。因月色白，故称&ldquo;素娥&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 质朴;不加装饰 <span class="encs">[simple;plain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谈笑有鸿儒，往来无白丁。可以调素琴，阅金经。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众人皆以奢靡为贵，吾心独以俭素为美。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:素碑(没有刻字的石碑);素一(纯朴简约);素冠(素朴无饰的练冠);素室(朴实而不加华饰的房间);素琴(无装饰的琴);素妆(淡妆);素几(不加雕饰的小几);素面朝天(不加妆饰而面见天子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 空，有名无实或有实无名 <span class="encs">[void]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼君子兮，不素飧兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>作<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>以示王意，然则 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>之<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>，素王之业也;诸子之传书，素相之事也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡超奇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:素放(无条件释放);素王(指有王者之道而无王者之位的人);素封(没有官爵封邑但和封君一样富有的人家);素手(空手;不带礼物);素功(素王的功业);素官(没有实际权力的闲官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 平素，往常，旧时 <span class="encs">[ordinary;old]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而五人生于编伍之间，素不闻诗书之训。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即入闾左呼子弟素健者，得数十人，遮豪民于道。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宦官</span><span class="diczx2">黄锦</span>在侧曰:&ldquo;此人素有痴 名。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;海瑞传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:素位(平日所处的地位);素守(平素的操守);素抱(平素的志趣、抱负);素衷(平素的心意);素期(平素所期望的);素意(平素的意愿);素怀(平素的怀抱);素著(一向都很著名);素孚(平时很得人心;一贯深受众望)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 寒素，低微 <span class="encs">[poor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">文帝</span>使人问 <span class="diczx2">诩</span>自固之术， <span class="diczx2">诩</span>曰:&ldquo;愿将军恢崇德度，躬素士之业，朝夕孜孜，不违子道，如此而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;贾诩传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:素士(布衣之士;贫寒的读书人);素姓(平民，百姓);素品(低微的门第);素室(寒门);素流(寒素之辈。指门第低微的人);素族(寒门);素门(清寒之家。与世族豪门相对)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 质数的 <span class="encs">[prime]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:素数(质数);素因子</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 诚心的，真情的 <span class="encs">[honest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫</span><span class="diczx2">公孙</span><span class="diczx2">鞅</span>事 <span class="diczx2">孝公</span>，极身毋二，&hellip;竭智能，示情素。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿诚素之先达兮，解玉佩以要之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>素</strong> <span class="dicpy">s&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 向来;从来就 <span class="encs">[at all]</span>。</p>
