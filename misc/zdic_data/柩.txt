                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>柩</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄡˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>装着尸体的棺材：灵～。棺～。～车。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> coffin which contains corpse</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bahre, Totenbahre  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cercueil</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">柩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>柩</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，从匚( <span class="dicpy">fāng</span>)，盛东西的筐器，久声。本义:装有尸体的棺材)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[coffin with corpse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柩，棺也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古文湨字从匚久，后世又加木旁耳，虚者为棺，实者为柩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先迁柩于庙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;宣公八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">崩薨，柩之为言究也，久也，不复变也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在床曰尸，在棺曰柩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:运柩;枢榇(装有尸体的棺材);柩尸(指灵柩);柩室(停放灵柩之室);柩路(载灵柩之车);柩殡(谓停放灵柩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 铭，汉时谓刻在石碑上的字为柩 <span class="encs">[inscriptions]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">设敖置铭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">贾公彦</span>疏:铭所以表柩，故 <span class="diczx2">汉</span>时谓铭为柩。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">柩</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十二">卷十二</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十二&amp;swbs=匚">匚</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/67E9.svg">                          <p>棺也。从匚从木，久聲。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2096C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠥬</span>，籒文柩。日救切〖注〗匶。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/67E9_2745.svg" /><span class="swbf">(匛)</span><span class="ywyy">棺也。</span><span class="swdz">木部曰：棺者，關也。所以揜屍。此云棺也。二篆義同。曲禮曰：在牀曰屍。在棺曰柩。是棺柩義别。虛者爲棺。實者爲柩。析言之也。柩或可以<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧦝</span>棺。棺不可以<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧦝</span>柩。是以許於柩曰棺也。於棺下不云柩也。以立文不得用考老之例也。</span><span class="ywyy">从匚久聲。</span><span class="swdz">各本有柩無匛。今依玉篇補。玉篇曰：匛，棺也。亦作柩。葢希馮在梁時所據說文如是。以後柩行匛廢。遂變許書之舊。而郭忠恕列匛爲古文。夏竦說匛爲孫強集。夏疑玉篇作匛，不出於希馮而出於孫強也。匛葢古文。而小篆仍之者。檀弓曰：有虞氏瓦棺。夏后氏堲周。殷人棺椁。周人牆置翣。瓦棺，堲周皆以土不以木。易曰：後世聖人易之以棺椁。後世聖人謂黃帝堯舜。然則土棺始於黄帝。堯舜仍之。倉頡造字从匚从久。白虎通云。柩，久也。久不復變也。造字之初。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃔</span>不从木。許言久聲者，以形聲包會意也。巨救切。三部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/67E9_2746.svg" /><span class="swbf">(柩)</span><span class="ywyy">匛或从木。</span><span class="swdz">葢殷人用木以後乃有此字。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/67E9_2747.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2096C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠥬</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文从舊。</span><span class="swdz">从舊猶从久也。舊之言久也。周禮用匶字。</span></p>                          </div>
