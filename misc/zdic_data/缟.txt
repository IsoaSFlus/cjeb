                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缟</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>缟</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">縞</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>未经染色的绢。</li>
	<li>白色：&ldquo;连观霜～，周除冰净&rdquo;。～素。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> white raw silk</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> blanc,simple</p></div>                        
                        <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缟</strong></p>
<p>縞 <span class="dicpy">gǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从糸，表示与线丝有关，高声。本义:细白的生绢) 同本义 <span class="encs">[plain white raw silk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缟，鲜色也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，鲜色当作鲜巵。熟帛曰练，生帛曰缟。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥篚元纤缟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。传:&ldquo;白缯。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与之缟带。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缯之精者曰缟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>人身善织屦，妻善织缟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说林上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乘坚策肥，履丝曳缟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">曹操</span>之众，远来疲敝，闻追 <span class="diczx2">豫州</span>，轻骑一日一夜行三百余里，此所谓&ldquo;强弩之末势不能穿鲁缟&rdquo;者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缟袂(白色绢衣。用以喻白海棠);缟衣綦巾(白绢上衣与浅绿色围裙。古时女子所服);缟冠(白色生绢制的帽子);缟练(白绢)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>缟</strong></p>
<p>縞 <span class="dicpy">gǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>白色 <span class="encs">[white]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其上台观皆金玉，其上禽兽皆纯缟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缟，素也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缟衣綦巾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;出其东门》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵皆缟素。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪》</span>。注:&ldquo;白也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下缟素。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝缟素避正殿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;顺帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">眄隰则万顷同缟，瞻山则千岩俱白。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">谢惠连</span><span class="smcs">《雪赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缟巾(白色头巾);缟带(白色的绢带。喻指雪的洁白);缟羽(白羽);缟衣(白色男服);缟武(白色帽檐)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>缟</strong></p>
<p>縞 <span class="dicpy">gǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>映照 <span class="encs">[shine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缟夜(映照黑夜);月明缟树(月光映照大树)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中" target="_blank">未集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中&amp;bs=糸" target="_blank">糸</a>】
                              縞·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E1E.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古考切，音杲。【說文】鮮色也。【博雅】練也。【小爾雅】繒之精者曰縞。【書&middot;禹貢】闕篚<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>纖縞。【傳】縞，白繒。【詩&middot;鄭風】縞衣綦巾。【傳】縞衣，白色男服。【禮&middot;檀弓】祥而縞。【疏】縞，謂縞冠。</p>
<p>又【玉藻】縞冠素紕，旣祥之冠也。【疏】縞是生絹又。【王制】殷人哻而祭，縞衣而養老。【註】殷尚白，而縞衣裳。【戰國策】强弩之末，不能穿魯縞。【前漢&middot;地理志註】縞，鮮支也，卽今所謂素者也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】古到切【集韻】居號切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音誥。義同。</p>
