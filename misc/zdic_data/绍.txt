                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绍</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>绍</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">紹</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shào.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄠˋ</span></p><ol><li>连续，继承：～复（继承恢复）。～述（继承）。～世（连续几世）。</li><li>指中国浙江省绍兴市：～酒。～剧。～兴师爷（这一职务旧时大多数由绍兴人担任；后来就称刀笔吏、讼棍为“绍兴师爷”，含贬义）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> continue, carry on; join</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> fortsetzen, weiterführen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> continuer,(abrév.)​ Shaoxing,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>绍</strong></p><p>紹 <span class="dicpy">shào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，召声。本义:继承;紧密连续)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[inherit;continue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绍，继也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绍庭上下。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·访落》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弗念厥绍。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使寡君之绍续昆裔。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不绍叶公之明，而使之悦近而来远。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·难三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以此绍殷，是以乱易暴也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·诚廉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绍复先王之大业。——<span class="smcs">《书·盘庚》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:绍真(继承正统;统一天下);绍天明命(承受上天之命);绍衣(承服奉行先人的德化和教言);绍兴(继续发扬光大;另县名)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 介绍，为人引见，使相互认识 <span class="encs">[introduce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绍我</span><span class="diczx2">周王</span>见休。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胜请为绍介。——<span class="smcs">《史记·鲁仲连传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士为绍摈。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 缠绕 <span class="encs">[twine;wind]</span>。如:绍缭(缠绕)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>绍</strong></p><p>紹 <span class="dicpy">Shào</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p>中国浙江绍兴市的简称 <span class="encs">[short for Shaoxing]</span>。如:绍剧;绍酒;绍鸭</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/绍介">绍介</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
