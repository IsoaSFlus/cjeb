                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>曷</strong> <span class="dicpy">hé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 何，什么 <span class="encs">[what]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曷，何也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">呜呼!曷归?——<span class="smcs">《五·五子之歌》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝曷弗告朕?——<span class="smcs">《书·盘庚上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:曷故(何故);曷胜(何胜);曷尝(何尝)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 何日，何时 <span class="encs">[when]</span> 。如:曷归(何日回)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>曷</strong> <span class="dicpy">hé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up class="diczx1">曷不委心任去留。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:曷为(为何;为什么);曷苦(怎么样;怎样)</p><p><span
