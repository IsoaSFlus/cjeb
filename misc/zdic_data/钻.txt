                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">钻</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>钻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鑽</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuān.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄨㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用锥状的物体在另一物体上转动穿孔：～孔。～木取火。～探。</li>
	<li>进入：～心。～营。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>钻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鑽</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>穿孔洞的用具：～床。～头。电～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">钻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>钻</strong></p>
<p>鑽、鑚 <span class="dicpy">zuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，赞声。本义是穿孔的工具，又表示穿孔、刺穿。)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[drill;bore]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">栌梨曰钻之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。按，防有虫于孔，钻视之也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钻龟陈卦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钻燧取火。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:钻一个眼;钻木取火;钻入(穿入);钻山(在山墙上开门或开洞。山墙，指房子两侧的墙，形如山);钻孔(用钻头在器物上钻挖圆孔);钻穴(钻挖洞穴。指男女非礼的结合);钻燧(钻木取火。燧是用来磨擦生热，以取得火种的工具，古分阳燧、木燧两种);钻火(钻木取火)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 钻研，集中精力学习，推究事理 <span class="encs">[study intensively]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仰之弥高，钻之弥坚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:钻仰(探求力索，钻研仰望。用以表示深入研究的意思);钻味(深入体会);钻摩(指努力研究);钻厉(深入研究，发愤勉励。厉通励)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 投机钻营 <span class="encs">[secure personal gain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钱物用多常是解，权门路便不兽钻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张鎡</span><span class="smcs">《南湖集》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 进入 <span class="encs">[get into]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 穿过 <span class="encs">[break through]</span></p>

<p>鑽、鑚 <span class="dicpy">zu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 钻孔的器具 <span class="encs">[drill;auger]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钻，所以穿也。从金，赞声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钻谓之鍴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言九》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其次用钻笮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钻，穿物之锥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:电钻;风钻;台钻;手摇钻;螺旋钻;手钻;弓钻;麻花钻;曲柄钻。又指古代施行膑刑的刑具</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中刑用刀锯，其次用钻凿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注:&ldquo;钻，钻去其膑骨也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 钻石的简称 <span class="encs">[diamond;jewel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钻有五色，金、黑、红者为贵&hellip;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《海国见闻录》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:十七钻的手表;钻戒(镶着钻石的戒指);钻珠(小而圆的钻石)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
