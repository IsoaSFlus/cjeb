                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>宣</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>公开说出，散布：～讲。～传。～战。～称。～言。～叙调。心照不～。</li><li>疏导：～泄。</li><li>古代帝王的大室。</li><li>皇帝命令或传达皇帝的命令：～付。～召（皇帝召见）。～诏（传旨）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>宣</strong> <span class="dicpy">xuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，亘( <span class="dicpy">xuán</span>)声。<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>与房屋有关。一说据甲骨文为云气舒卷自如之象。本义:帝王的宫殿)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代帝王的大室，古宫室名 <span class="encs">[spacious room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宣，天子宣室也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，当训大室也。与宽略同。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">武王破纣牧野，杀之于宣室。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“宣室，殷宫名，一曰狱也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受釐坐宣室。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·孝文纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“未央前正室也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宣室</span>求贤访逐臣， <span class="diczx2">贾生</span>才调更无伦。—— <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《贾生》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 帝王的诏书 <span class="encs">[imperial edict]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黄宣去把团营押。——</span><span class="diczx2">汤显祖</span><span class="smcs">《紫钗记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宣纸的简称 <span class="encs">[Xuan paper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尝至</span><span class="diczx2">琉璃厂</span>购玉版宣，以瓜子金抵其值。——<span class="smcs">《清朝野史大观》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“瑄”</span>。璧玉 <span class="encs">[an ornamental piece of jade about 6.5 inches in diameter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">璧大六寸，谓之宣。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释器》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span>所云瑄玉是也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>嗣王敢用吉玉宣璧。——<span class="smcs">《双剑誃吉金文选·诅楚文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 地名。古州名。治所在今安徽省宣城县 <span class="encs">[Xuan prefecture]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 明代宣府镇 <span class="encs">[Xuan town]</span>，驻所在今河北省张家口市(旧宣化市)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 云南宣威的简称 <span class="encs">[Xuanwei county]</span>。如:宣腿</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>宣</strong> <span class="dicpy">xuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 宣布。宣读 <span class="encs">[declare]</span></p><p><span
