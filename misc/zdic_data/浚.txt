压榨，取出汁水或内含物 <span class="encs">[press]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浚民之膏济以实之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:浚削(榨取;搜刮);浚财(搜刮钱财)</p><p><span
