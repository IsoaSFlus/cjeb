                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>箕</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用竹篾、柳条或铁皮等制成的扬去糠麸或清除垃圾的器具（通常称&ldquo;簸箕&rdquo;）：～帚。</li>
	<li>簸箕形的指纹，不成圆形：斗（<span class="dicpy">dǒu </span>）～。</li>
	<li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sieve; dust pan, garbage bag</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Getreideschwinge, Kornschwinge  (S, Agrar)​,Kehrblech, Kehrschaufel, Worfschaufel  (S)​,Schleife beim Fingerabdruck  (S, Bio)​,Sieb  (S, Agrar)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pelle à ordure,empreintes digitales constituées de lignes non spiralées</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">箕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>箕</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，其声。本义:簸箕，扬米去糠的器具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[winnowing basket]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">箕，簸也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以箕自乡而报之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;箕去弃物。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">良弓之子，必学为箕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:箕帚(簸箕和扫帚。借指家务);箕裘(簸箕和皮袍。借指祖先的事业与遗产);箕舌(指簸箕底伸展向前之广处，其状如舌);箕敛(以箕收取。苛敛民财)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 畚箕。用竹篾、柳条等制成的清除垃圾的器具 <span class="encs">[dustpan]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">箕，箕帚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡为长者粪之礼，必加帚于箕上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:箕畚(畚箕);箕帚(畚箕与扫帚);箕扫(箕帚。扫除之具);箕扫(指执箕清扫);箕帚妾(持箕帚的奴婢，借作妻妾之谦称)</p>

<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 星宿名 <span class="encs">[name of constellation]</span>，指箕宿，二十八宿之一。东方青龙七宿的末一宿。有星四颗</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维南有箕，不可以簸扬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;大东》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7B95.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20020.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀠</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20D29.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠴩</span>䇧<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/262CA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦋊</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25AF6.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥫶</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20DDB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠷛</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2050B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔋</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25ADA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥫚</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2051B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔛</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21FF8.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡿸</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】居之切【正韻】堅溪切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音姬。宿名。【詩&middot;小雅】成是南箕。【傳】南箕，箕星也。正義曰：箕，四星，二爲踵，二爲舌，踵在上，舌在下，踵狹而舌廣。【韻會】箕者，萬物根基，東方之宿，考星者多驗於南方，故曰南箕。【爾雅&middot;釋天】析木之津，箕斗之閒，漢津也。【疏】天漢在箕斗二星之閒，箕在東方木位，斗在北方水位。分析水木，以箕星爲隔。隔河須津梁以渡，故此次爲析木之津。【史記&middot;天官書】箕爲敖客，曰口舌。【註】敖，調弄也。箕以簸揚調弄爲象。</p>
<p>又受物有去來。去來，客之象。箕爲天口，主出氣，是箕有舌，象讒言。【石氏星經】箕四宿，主後宮別府二十七世婦八十一御妻，爲相天子后也。【書&middot;洪範註】好風者箕星，好雨者畢星。【春秋緯】月麗于畢，雨滂沱。月麗于箕，風揚沙。【天官書】箕，燕之分野。</p>
<p>又【篇海】箕，簸箕，揚米去糠之具。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】箕，箕帚。【禮&middot;曲禮】凡爲長者糞之禮必加帚於箕上。【世本】古者少康作箕帚。</p>
<p>又國名。【書&middot;洪範】王訪于箕子。【註】箕，國名。子爵也。</p>
<p>又地名。【山海經】釐山西二百里曰箕尾之山。【春秋&middot;僖三十三年】晉人敗狄于箕。【註】太原陽邑縣南有箕城。【孟子】益避禹之子於箕山之隂。【疏】箕山，嵩高之北是也。【前漢&middot;地理志】琅琊有箕縣。【水經注】濰水出箕屋山。</p>
<p>又姓，晉有大夫箕鄭。</p>
<p>又斯螽別名。【周禮&middot;考工記疏】幽州人謂斯螽爲舂箕。</p>
<p>又木名。【鄭語】檿弧箕服。【韋註】箕，木名。服，矢房也。</p>
<p>又【張衡&middot;思<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>賦】屬箕伯以函風兮。【註】箕伯，風師。</p>
