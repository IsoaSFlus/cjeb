                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蔌</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>〔～～〕a.形容鄙陋；b.风声劲疾，如“棱棱霜气，～～风威”；c.花落的样子，如“又有墙头千叶桃，风动花落红～～”，亦作“簌簌”；d.液体流动的样子，如“清泉～～先流齿”。</li><li>菜肴：山肴野～。</li></ol>
