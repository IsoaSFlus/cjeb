                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>锲</strong></p><p>鍥 <span class="dicpy">qiè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，契( <span class="dicpy">qì</span>)声。本义:镰刀) 同本义 <span class="encs">[sickle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">锲，镰也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刎镰，一曰小镰，南方用以乂谷。——<span class="smcs">《六书故》</span>引蜀本》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刈钩，自关而西，或谓之锲。——<span class="smcs">《方言五》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:锲刀(镰刀)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>锲</strong></p><p>鍥 <span class="dicpy">qiè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“洯”</span>。用刀子刻 <span class="encs">[carve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">锲其轴。——<span class="smcs">《左传·定公四年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“刻也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">锲而舍之，朽木不折。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">锲而不舍。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 截断 <span class="encs">[cut]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尽借邑人之车，锲其轴，麻约而归之。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/锲而不舍">锲而不舍</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
