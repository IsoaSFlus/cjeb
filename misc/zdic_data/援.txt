                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">援</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>援</strong> <span class="dicpy">yuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，爰( <span class="dicpy">yuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:拉，引)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pull by hand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">援，引也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无然畔援。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·皇矣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不援其所不及。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·缁衣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不援上。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">男女授受不亲，嫂溺手援非正。——<span class="smcs">《豆棚闲话》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">右抚剑，左援带，命驱之出。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:援及(动起);援推(引之使来，推之使去)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 攀援 <span class="encs">[climb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">猱蝯善援。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桃李之垂行者，莫之援也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·下贤》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫为四邻之援。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">猿猱欲渡愁攀援。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《蜀道难》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">石崖侧削则援崖。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:援系(犹攀附。旧谓求婚之谦词)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 擢拔，引进推荐 <span class="encs">[promote]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">某官异材秀出，博学名家，…今乃援而进之，论者惜其晚矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《答曾舍人启》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:援进(提拔任用);援拾(提携收录)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 引用 <span class="encs">[cite;quote]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">援赦减等。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:援纳(援例纳银捐官);援例(清朝规定捐一定数额的银子，可得到官职或监生资格);援例监(按照常规捐钱取得国子监监生的资格);有例可援;援验(引证)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 拯救，相救 <span class="encs">[rescue;save]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今天下溺矣，夫子之不援，何也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:抗美援朝;援手(伸手拉人一把以解救其困厄);援拯(援救，帮助);援应(声援接应)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 执持，持 <span class="encs">[hold]</span>。如:援旌(执旗);援桴三鼓(指擂鼓助威);援旌擐甲(执旗穿甲);援持(执持)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>援</strong> <span class="dicpy">yuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span
