<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>兴</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">興</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄥˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>举办，发动：～办。～工。～学。～建。～叹（发出感叹声，如&ldquo;望洋～～&rdquo;）。百废待～。</li>
	<li>起来：夙～夜寐（早起晚睡）。</li>
	<li>旺盛：～盛。～旺。～隆。～衰。复～。～替（兴衰）。天下～亡，匹夫有责。</li>
	<li>流行，盛行：时～。新～。</li>
	<li>准许：不～胡闹。</li>
	<li>或许：～许。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兴</strong></p>
<p>興 <span class="dicpy">xīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从舁，从同。舁( <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span>)，共举;同，同力。本义:兴起;起来)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rise;get up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兴，起也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夙兴夜寐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃寝乃兴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;斯干》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三岁不兴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;同人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其言足以兴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风萧瑟而并兴兮，天惨惨而无色。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王璨</span><span class="smcs">《登楼赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">但国家兴自塑土，徒居平城。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>兴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兴利除弊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水波不兴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怨颇兴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兴居(起居);兴辞(起立致辞);兴寝(起卧);兴舍(兴亡);兴废(盛衰;兴亡);兴毁(兴亡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 使兴盛;流行 <span class="encs">[thrive;prosper;be popular]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8208.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>虛陵切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/287BE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨞾</span>。【爾雅&middot;釋言】興，起也。【詩&middot;小雅】夙興夜寐。【禮&middot;中庸】國有道，其言足以興。【註】興，謂起在位也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】盛也。【詩&middot;小雅】天保定爾，以莫不興。【箋】興，盛也。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】舉也。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;大司馬】進賢興功，以作邦國。【註】興，猶舉也。</p>
<p>又動也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;弓人】下柎之功，末應將興。【註】興，猶動也。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】興迷亂于政。【箋】興，猶尊尚也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;地官&middot;旅師】頒其興積。【註】縣官徵聚物曰興。今云軍興是也。</p>
<p>又【司稼】平其興。【註】所徵賦。</p>
<p>又州名。【五音集韻】漢置武都郡，魏立東西州。梁爲興州，因武興山而名。</p>
<p>又縣名。【李顒&middot;涉湖詩】旋經義興境。</p>
<p>又殿名。【張衡&middot;西京賦】龍興含章。【註】龍興，殿名。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓譜】漢濟陰王，謁者興渠。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>許應切，音嬹。【集韻】象也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】比興。【增韻】興，况意思也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大師】敎六詩，曰風曰賦曰比曰興曰雅曰頌。【詩詁】興者，感物而發，如倉庚于飛，熠燿其羽，昔我往矣，楊柳依依之類。</p>
<p>又【正韻】悅也。【禮&middot;學記】不興其藝，不能樂學。【註】興之言喜也。【殷仲文詩】獨有淸秋日，能使高興盡。</p>
<p>又【正韻】許刃切，音釁。【禮&middot;文王世子】旣興器用幣。【釋文】音同釁。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶火宮切，音凶。【馬融&middot;長笛賦】曲終闋盡，餘弦更興。繁手累發，密櫛<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24D01.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤴁</span>重。重平聲。</p>
<p>又叶虛良切，音香。【徐幹&middot;雜詩】沈隂增憂愁，憂愁爲誰興。念與君相別，乃在天一方。【潘乾<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥓓</span>】實天生德，有漢將興。子子孫孫，俾爾熾昌。</p>
<p>又叶丘侵切，音欽。【詩&middot;大雅】殷商之旅，其會如林。矢于牧野，維予侯興。</p>
