                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朽</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>朽</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄡˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>腐烂：腐～。～木。永垂不～（&ldquo;朽&rdquo;在此引申为磨灭）。</li>
	<li>衰老：衰～。～迈。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> decayed, rotten; rot, decay</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> faul ,morsch ,verfault</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pourri,sénile</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>朽</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，丂( <span class="dicpy">kǎo</span>)声。本义:腐烂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[decayed;rotten]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朽，腐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荼蓼朽止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;良耜》</span>。释文:&ldquo;烂也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其臭朽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span>。注:&ldquo;气若有若无为朽。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">锲而舍之，朽木不折。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朽木不可雕也，粪土之墙不可圬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:朽索(腐朽的绳子);朽壤(腐烂的泥土);朽木不可雕(朽木不雕，朽木难雕，朽棘不雕。比喻人不可造就或事物和局势败坏而不可救药);朽木生花(喻事物得以新生)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 衰老;衰弱 <span class="encs">[senile;old]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">年朽发落。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;隐逸传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">长安</span>有男儿，二十心己朽。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《赠陈商》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣虽朽迈，敢忘往言?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:朽钝(形容年迈笨拙);朽迈(年老);朽人(年迈衰老之人。多作谦词);朽才(衰弱无能之才，不可造就之才。多用于诮人或自谦)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>朽</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 磨灭，消散 <span class="encs">[dissipate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死且不朽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">传世而不朽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略&middot;序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:朽陈(指往昔的朝列);朽散(离散、散去);朽劳(无功之劳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 作国画用土笔勾勒草图 <span class="encs">[delineate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">画之妍丑，岂在朽不朽乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方薰</span><span class="smcs">《山静居画论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:朽炭(朽墨。作国画勾勒草图用的炭笔);朽染(作国画用朽之法);朽笔(用木炭作国画起稿)</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朽</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>朽</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄒㄧㄡˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>xiǔ <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3983.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">腐爛、敗壞。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「朽壞」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈淮陰侯廟記〉：「將軍則與草木同<b>朽</b>，麋鹿俱死。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">磨滅。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「永垂不<b>朽</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;襄公二十四年》：「太上有立德&hellip;&hellip;，雖久不廢， 此之謂不<b>朽</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">敗壞的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「衰<b>朽</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;公冶長》：「朽木不可雕也，糞土之牆不可杇也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">衰老。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老<b>朽</b>」、「年<b>朽</b>齒落」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《三國志&middot;卷九&middot;魏書&middot;曹真傳》：「臣雖朽邁，敢忘往言？」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李賀〈贈陳商〉詩：「長安有男兒，二十心已<b>朽</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">泛指腐敗的東西。</span><span class="gc_yy">《晉書&middot;卷七○&middot;甘卓傳》：「將軍之舉武昌，若摧枯拉<b>朽</b>，何所顧慮乎！」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              朽·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=6" target="_blank">6</a>　·部外筆画：2</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/673D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>許久切，休上聲。木腐也。【詩&middot;周頌】荼蓼朽止。【左傳&middot;襄三十一年】恐燥濕之不時而朽蠹，以重敝邑之罪。</p>
<p>又與殠同。臭也。【列子&middot;周<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25846.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥡆</span>王篇】饗香以爲朽。【仲尼篇】鼻將塞者，先覺焦朽。</p>
<p>又叶喜語切，音許。【陳琳&middot;大荒賦】廓寥寂而無人兮，雖獨存兮何補。追邃古之遐跡兮，惟德音兮爲不朽。</p>
<p>又叶許救切，音齅。【李賀&middot;感諷詩】淒涼梔子落，山舋泣淸漏。下有張仲蔚，披書案將朽。齅字原从二鼻，作鼻鼻。</p>
