						<p>● <strong>懦</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄨㄛˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>胆小软弱：～夫。～弱。怯～。</li>	<li>柔软。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> weak, timid, cowardly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> befangen, furchtsam</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faible,imbécile,timide</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">懦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>懦</strong> <span class="dicpy">nu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，需声。本义:胆小，含有软弱之意;弱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cowardly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">懦，驽弱者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少懦于诸侯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而性懦愚，沉于国家大事，开罪于先生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宫之奇之为人也，懦而不能强谏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众皆笑其懦，不青坚守。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">懦者必怒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《原毁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:懦小(懦弱胆小);懦弛(软弱懈怠);懦退(软弱畏缩);懦恧(软弱);懦软(软弱);懦庸(软弱庸陋);懦谨(胆小谨慎);懦善(软弱善良);懦衿(文弱书生)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 柔软 <span class="encs">[soft]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:懦毛;懦响(懦靡之音);懦靡(柔顺);懦薄(才能薄弱);懦脆(脆弱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 幼小 <span class="encs">[immature]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还是我年懦，容得你。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>懦</strong> <span class="dicpy">nu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 懦夫，软弱无能的人 <span class="encs">[coward]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">声节哀有余，夫何激衰懦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《舟中苦热遣怀》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">懦</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=心">心</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/61E6.svg">                          <p>駑弱者也。从心需聲。人朱切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/611E_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(愞)</span> <span class="ywyy">駑弱也。</span> <span class="swdz">也上本有者，今刪。駑當作奴，許書無駑字。葢衹云奴馬。</span> <span class="ywyy">从心耎聲。</span> <span class="swdz">乃亂切。十四部。此篆各本作懦，从心需聲，人朱切，乃淺人所改，今正。愞與人部偄音義皆同，弱也，本乃亂切，音轉爲乃過切。廣韵獮韵愞而兖切，換韵愞奴亂切，過韵愞乃臥切。玉篇心部愞乃亂、乃過二切，皆訓弱也。此自古相傳不誤之字也。因形近或譌爲懦，再譌爲儒，其始尚分愞懦爲二字二音。故玉藻注云：舒愞者，所畏在前也。釋文云：愞乃亂反，又奴臥反，怯愞也。又作懦，人于反，弱也。皇云學士：是其分别井然，而轉寫愞譌爲懦，故五經文字曰懦人于反，又乃亂反，見禮記注。於是有懦無愞，而以愞之反語入於懦下。廣韵虞韵懦字下人朱切，又乃亂切，其誤正同。又考僖二年左傳愞字、榖梁傳愞字，釋文轉寫皆譌作懦。凡經傳愞字皆譌作懦，不可勝正。愞通作耎，亦或借蝡。漢書西南夷傳<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨕖</span>耎，後書章帝八王傳西羌傳<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨕖</span>愞，史記律書<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨕖</span>蝡，方言注愞<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22C05.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢰅</span>，今無不作懦者，葢需耎二聲古分别畫然。需聲在古音四部，人于切。耎聲在古音十四部，乃亂切。而自張參以來，改耎爲需，不能諟正，《説文》心部之愞、手部之㨎皆經淺人任意竄改，以合里俗。世有好學深思心知其意者，必以愚言爲然也。</span></p>                          </div>
