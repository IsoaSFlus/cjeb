                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>燃</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">r&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>烧起火焰：自～。～料。～烧。～点。～眉之急。</li>	<li>引火点着：点～。～灯。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">燃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>燃</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，然声。&ldquo;然&rdquo;有表意作用。本作&ldquo;然&rdquo;。&ldquo;燃&rdquo;是后起字。本义:燃烧)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">煮豆燃豆萁，豆在釜中泣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《七步诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:燃藜图(劝人勤学苦读的画);燃灰(死灰复燃);燃灼(燃烧;烧灼);燃顶(以香火烧灼头顶，表示虔诚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引火点着 <span class="encs">[light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">&hellip;&hellip;开枪者、燃炮者、&hellip;&hellip;络绎相属。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:燃放(点燃鞭炮等使其爆炸)</span></p>

<ol>
