                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>亩</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">畝</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄨˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>中国市制土地面积单位，一亩等于六十平方丈。十五亩等于一公亩。</li>	<li>田中高处，垄：&ldquo;居于畎～之中&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> Chinese land measure; fields</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> = <a href="./畝">畝</a>mu3 ,Mu (gleich fünfzehntel Hektar)​  (Zähl)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亩</strong></p>
<p>畝、畮 <span class="dicpy">mǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p>(形声。小篆字形。从十，从田，久声。十，四方。本义:中国土地面积市亩的通称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义，一亩约等于667平方米 <span class="encs">[Chinese unit of area mu ( 1 / 15 of a hectare)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畮，六尺为步，百步为畮。秦田二百四十步为畮。畮或从十久。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作畆、畒、畝、畞。如:亩制(以亩为单位划分、计算土地面积的制度);亩级(田地土壤质量好坏的等级);亩积(每一亩田的面积)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亩</strong></p>
<p>畝 <span class="dicpy">mǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 田垄 <span class="encs">[ridge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我疆我理，南东其亩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;信南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艺麻如之何?衡从某亩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亩丘(有垄界的丘地);亩道(古代以国都为中心的大道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指农田，田地 <span class="encs">[field]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又亲操耒耨以修畎亩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说疑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畎亩(泛指田地，田间。析言之，垄上叫亩，垄中叫畎，即田中垄沟);亩陇(田野，农田);亩畹(园圃)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">畝</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“亩”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/畝">畝</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上" target="_blank">午集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上&amp;bs=田" target="_blank">田</a>】
                              畝·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/755D.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C51.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤱑</span>【唐韻】莫厚切【集韻】【韻會】莫後切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>謀上聲。司馬法，六尺爲步，步百爲畝。秦孝公制，二百四十步爲畝。宋程頤曰：古者百畝，止當今之四十畝。今之百畝，當古之二百五十畝。【書&middot;盤庚】惰農自安，不昬作勞，不服田畝。【詩&middot;小雅】南東其畝。【朱註】畝，壟也。</p><p>又丘名。【爾雅&middot;釋地】如畝畝丘。【註】丘有隴界，如田畝。【釋名】畝丘，丘體滿一畝之地也。【詩&middot;小雅】楊園之道，猗于畝丘。【傳】畝丘，丘名。</p><p>又地名。【左傳&middot;桓二年】晉<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25807.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥠇</span>侯之夫人姜氏，以條之役生太子，命之曰仇。其弟以千畝之戰生，命之曰成師。【註】西河界休縣南有地，名千畝。</p><p>又【韻補】叶莫補切，模上聲。【班固&middot;西都賦】士食舊德之名氏，農服先疇之畎畝。商循族世之所鬻，工用高曾之規矩。</p><p>又叶滿彼切，眉上聲。【詩&middot;豳風】饁彼南畝，田畯至喜。【屈原&middot;離騷】余旣滋蘭之九畹兮，又樹蕙之百畝。畦留夷與揭車兮，雜杜衡與芳芷。　【說文】本作畮。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/756E.svg">                          <p>六尺爲步，步百爲畮。从田<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AED.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣫭</span>聲。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/756E_1.svg" />畞，畮或从田、十、久。莫厚切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：十，四方也。久聲。</p>                          </div>
