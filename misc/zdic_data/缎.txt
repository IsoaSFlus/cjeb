						<p>● <strong>缎</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">緞</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>中国特产的质地厚密、一面光滑的丝织品：～子。绸～。软～。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缎</strong></p>
<p>緞 <span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，段声。本义:缝贴于鞋跟的革片、丝绦之类)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a heel piece of shoe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">履舄鞜襞绒缎紃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《急就篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 一种质地厚密而有光泽的丝织物 <span class="encs">[satin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诏赐抚臣名马衣缎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缎疋(缎的总称。亦泛指丝织品);缎匹(即缎疋)</span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“缎”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/𩏇"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏇</span></a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷五">卷五</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷五&amp;swbs=韋">韋</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/293C7.svg">                          <p>履後帖也。从韋段聲。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/293C7_1.svg" />緞，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏇</span>或从糸。徒玩切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/293C7_10229.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏇</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">履後帖也。</span><span class="swdz">帖，帛書署也。引伸爲今俗語帮貼之字。凡履跟必帮貼之，令堅厚，不則易敝。臣瓚曰：躡跟曰跕。按履緊曰跕屣也。跕與帖音同。急就篇：履舄鞜裒䋐緞紃。師古引說文爲注。</span><span class="ywyy">从韋段聲。</span><span class="swdz">此形聲包會意也。段取堅意。古本葢衹有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏇</span>緞二篆，自从段譌爲从叚，而篇、韵皆有䪗縀，音乎加切。此<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏇</span>篆之上亦妄增䪗篆，云履也，从韋叚聲，正如石部碫譌爲碬，各本說文乃作碬耳。今刪䪗篆。徒玩切。篇、韵皆上聲。徒管切。十四部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/293C7_10230.svg" /><span class="swbf">(緞)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/293C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩏇</span>或从糸。</span><span class="swdz">今俗以爲錦繡段之段。</span></p>                          </div>
