                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">去</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>去</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">q&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>离开所在的地方到别处；由自己一方到另一方，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">来</span>&rdquo;相对：～处。～路。～国。</li>	<li>距离，差别：相～不远。</li>	<li>已过的，特指刚过去的一年：～年。～冬今春。</li>	<li>除掉，减掉：～掉。～皮。～势（①阉割；②动作或事情终了时的气势）。</li>	<li>扮演戏曲中的角色：他～男主角。</li>	<li>用在动词后，表示趋向：上～。进～。</li>	<li>用在动词后，表示持续：信步走～。</li>	<li>汉语四声之一：～声（①古汉语四声的第三声；②普通话字调中的第四声）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">去</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>去</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。甲骨文字形。上面是人(大)，下面是口(或作&ldquo;凵&rdquo;)，表示人离开洞口或坑坎而去。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;从大，凵声。&rdquo;凵( <span class="dicpy">qū</span>)。本义:离开)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[go away;leave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">去，人相违也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逝将去女，适彼乐土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;硕鼠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纪侯大去其国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋&middot;庄公四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大去者，不遗一人之辞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武子</span>去所。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能相去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">久而不去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一狼径去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东向驰去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:去离(分离;离开);去任(因故离开职位);去住无门(进退两难);去来(离去;往来);去位(离开官位)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 除去;去掉 <span class="encs">[remove;wipe off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">去死肌，杀三虫。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明日去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俸去书来。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:去绝根株(斩草除根);去疾(除去疾病);去皮;去邪归正(去掉邪恶，归于正道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 相距，远离 <span class="encs">[be apart from;be at a distance of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">连峰去天不盈尺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《蜀道难》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日始出时去人近。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">西蜀</span>之去 <span class="diczx2">南海</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">去村四里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:两地相去50里</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 前往，到别处，跟&ldquo;来&rdquo;相反 <span class="encs">[go]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大江东去，浪淘尽，千古风流人物。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇&middot;赤壁怀古》</span>词</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公然抱茅入竹去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝可去应之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:去住无门(进退两难);去紧(走得快;迅疾);去处(所去的地方);去学校;谁去都一样</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 失掉; 失去 <span class="encs">[lose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大事去矣，可痛惜哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:去失(丢失)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 扮演 <span class="encs">[戏曲里的角色]</span> <span class="encs">[play the part of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:去主角的那位长得并不美</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 赶走;打发走 <span class="encs">[drive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫汇上之处女，有家贫而无烛者，处女相与语，欲去之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 抛弃，舍弃 <span class="encs">[throw away]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是君臣、父子、兄弟尽去仁义，怀利以相接，然而不亡者，未之有也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:去心(心里丢开;放心);去势(舍弃权势)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 去世，死亡 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日月还复周，我去不再阳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《杂诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:去世(死亡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 用在谓词或谓词结构后表示趋向或持续 <span class="encs">[used after a verb or a V&mdash;O construction to indicate that an action is to take place or continue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上去下来船不定，自飞自语燕争忙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梅尧臣</span><span class="smcs">《绝句》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">归去来兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一鸣辄跃去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">跃去尺有咫。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:我去考虑考虑;明年回乡下去;船向前慢慢开去;把东西给他送去</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 逃离，逃亡 <span class="encs">[go into exile;flee from home]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">委而去之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小敌去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纺焉以度而去之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 通&ldquo;驱&rdquo;。驱逐 <span class="encs">[drive out;expel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千乘三去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公十五年》</span></span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>去</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄑㄩˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>q&ugrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3770.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">往、到。與「來」相對。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>去</b>學校」、「<b>去</b>郊遊」、「<b>去</b>餐館吃飯」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈贈韋祕書子春〉詩二首之二：「終與安社稷，功成<b>去</b>五湖。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">離開。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「去職」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷八五&middot;何武傳》：「去後常見思。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">死亡。</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;湯顯祖《牡丹亭&middot;第二七齣》：「杜衙小姐<b>去</b>三年，待與招魂上九天。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">距離。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>去</b>古已遠。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈蜀道難〉：「連峰<b>去</b>天不盈尺，枯松倒挂倚絕壁。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">送、發出。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>去</b>信」、「<b>去</b>電報」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">除掉。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>去</b>一層皮。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;沈括《夢溪筆談&middot;卷三&middot;辯證一》：「不求<b>去</b>礙，而欲見不顛倒，難矣哉！」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">放棄。</span><span class="gc_yy">《莊子&middot;大宗師》：「離形<b>去</b>知。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷九四&middot;匈奴傳上》：「得漢食物皆<b>去</b>之，以視不如重酪之便美也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">失掉。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大勢已<b>去</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷八七&middot;李斯傳》：「胥人者，<b>去</b>其幾也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">過去的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「去年」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;曹操&middot;短歌行》：「譬如朝露，去日苦多。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">助</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示事情的進行。相當於「啊」、「了」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「他睡覺<b>去</b>了。」、「他上班<b>去</b>了。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李賀〈沙路曲〉：「斷燼遺香褭翠煙，燭騎啼烏上天<b>去</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈海棠〉詩：「只恐夜深花睡<b>去</b>，故燒高燭照紅妝。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">平上去入四聲之一。參見「<a href='/hans/去聲' target=_blank>去聲</a> 」條。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如漢代有去卑。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
