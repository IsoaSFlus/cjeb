						<p>● <strong>屡</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">屢</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǚ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄩˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>接连着，不止一次：～次。～年。～见不鲜。～试不爽（多次试验都不错）。～战～捷。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>屡</strong></p>
<p>屢 <span class="dicpy">lǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>(从尸，娄声。本义:多次) 同本义 <span class="encs">[repeatedly;time and again;again and again]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屡当死。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公屡促之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝屡欲召。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屡撩之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屡空(经常贫乏而一无所有。后用以指贫穷、衣食不继);屡常(平常，平时);屡复(多次告白);屡舞(频频歌舞);屡试不售(多次参加科举考试都落榜了);屡迁(多次变易，多次变迁)</span></p>
