                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>型</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>铸造器物用的模子：砂～。～砂（制造砂型的材料）。</li>
	<li>样式：类～。新～。～号。脸～。血～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pattern, model, type; law; mold</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Baureihe  (S)​,Bauweise  (S)​,Gussform  (S)​,Modell  (S)​,Typ  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> type,modèle,moule (n.m)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">型</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>型</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，刑声。本义:铸造器物的模子。用木做的叫模，用竹做的叫范，用泥做的叫型)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">型，铸器之法也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作侀</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑者，侀也。侀者，成也。水曰准，曰法;木曰模，竹曰笵;士曰型。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫纯钩、鱼肠之始下型。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;修务》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凝土亦能型。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">谢灵运</span><span class="smcs">《命学士讲书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纸型;剂型;砂型;造型;铸型;模型</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 楷模 <span class="encs">[model]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽无老成人，尚有典型。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;荡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太夫人仁慈果决如此，其泽物型家，推类可想。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陈康祺</span><span class="smcs">《郎潜纪闻》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:型坊(犹典范，楷模);型范(典范;法式)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 类型;样式 <span class="encs">[type]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:轻型;重型;大、中、小型;新型;脸型;口型;流线型;血型</span></p>
