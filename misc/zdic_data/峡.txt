                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">峡</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>峡</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">峽</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄚˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>两山夹着的水道：三门～（中国黄河中游峡谷之一）。三～（中国长江流经四川到湖北的交界一带所经过的三个峡谷，其中有&ldquo;瞿塘峡&rdquo;、&ldquo;巫峡&rdquo;、&ldquo;西陵峡&rdquo;）。海～（两旁有陆地夹着的形状狭长的海，如&ldquo;台湾～～&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>指两山之间：～谷（狭而深的谷地）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> gorge, strait, ravine; isthmus</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schlucht  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> gorge (montagne)​</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>峡</strong></p>
<p>峽 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，夹声。本义:两山夹水处)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gorge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仿洋于山峡之旁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span>。注:&ldquo;两山之间为峡。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">峡里谁知有人事，世中遥望空云山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《桃源行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仍下峡路。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从峡度栈以上。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:峡口(峡谷之口);峡路(峡谷中的道路或航道)。又特指长江三峡</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">桓公</span>( <span class="diczx2">温</span>)入峡，绝壁天悬，腾波迅急。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:峡口(指西陵峡口);峡江(长江自奉节县瞿塘峡以下，至湖北宜昌一段)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 形容词。狭窄 <span class="encs">[narrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邃岸天高，空谷幽深，涧道之峡，车不方轨，号曰天险。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水经注》</span></span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中" target="_blank">寅集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中&amp;bs=山" target="_blank">山</a>】
                              峽·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5CFD.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】侯夾切【集韻】【韻會】轄夾切【正韻】胡夾切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音洽。【廣韻】巫峽，山名。蜀楚之交，山有三峽。【左思&middot;蜀都賦】經三峽之崢嶸。【註】三峽在巴東永安縣，謂西陵峽，歸鄕峽，巫峽也。【盛弘之荆州記】三峽七百里中，兩岸連山無斷處，重巖疊嶂，隱天蔽日，非亭午不見日月。</p>
<p>又州名。秦將白起攻楚，燒夷陵，卽其地。魏武於此置臨江郡。後魏爲拓州，取開拓之義。周以其居三峽之口，因曰峽州。</p>
<p>又山峭夾水，亦曰峽。見前岬字註。　【集韻】本作陜。或作陿。</p>
