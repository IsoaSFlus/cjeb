						<p>● <strong>旆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代旗末端状如燕尾的垂旒。</li>
	<li>泛指旌旗。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> flag ornament; flags, banners</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Wimpel  (S)​,Banner</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bannière,banderole</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旆</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:古代旐旗末端形如燕尾的垂旒飘带)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[flag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旆，继旐之帛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白旆央央。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;六月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旆旆(旗旒下垂的样子;生长茂盛的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指旌旗 <span class="encs">[banner;flag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白旆，殷旌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释兵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拔旆投衡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span>。注:&ldquo;大旗也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亡大旆之左旃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旆旃;旆旌</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/65C6.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>蒲蓋切，音沛。【說文】作旆。繼旐之旗也，沛然而垂。从㫃，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>聲。【爾雅&middot;釋天】繼旐曰旆。【註】帛續旐，末爲燕尾者。【釋名】雜帛爲旆，以雜色綴其邊爲翅尾也。將帥所建，象物雜也。【詩&middot;小雅】白旆央央。【傳】白旆，繼旐者也。【左傳&middot;昭十三年】建而不旆。【註】旆，游也。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】彼旟旐斯，胡不旆旆。【傳】旆旆，旒垂貌。【朱傳】旆旆，飛揚之貌。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】荏菽旆旆。【傳】旆旆然長也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】蒲掇切，音跋。義同。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶蒲枚切。【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>琳&middot;大荒賦】華蓋建杠，招搖樹旆。攝提運杓，文昌承魁。旆原字从㫃从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/65C6.svg">                          <p>繼旐之旗也，沛然而垂。从㫃<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>聲。蒲蓋切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C6_2501.svg" /><span class="swbf">(旆)</span><span class="ywyy">繼旐之旗也。</span><span class="swdz">旗者，旌旗之總名。下文云旗有衆鈴，旗曲柄，旗旖施皆是。爾雅：繼旐曰旆。郭云：帛續旐末爲燕尾者。何休注公羊云：繼旐如燕尾曰旆。釋名云：旆，以帛繼旐末也。小雅：帛旆央央。毛曰：帛旆，繼旐者也。按帛葢用絳。</span><span class="ywyy">沛然而<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2136E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡍮</span>。</span><span class="swdz">沛旆曡韵，引伸爲凡垂之偁。出車傳曰：旆旆，旒垂皃。左傳昭十三年曰：八月辛未，治兵，建而不旆。壬申，復旆之。杜云：建立旌旗，不曳其旆。旆，游也。按不旆者，卷而不垂。旆之者，垂之也。定四年：晉人假羽旌於鄭，鄭人與之。明日，或旆以會。亦是垂旒之義。大雅：荏菽旆旆。傳曰：旆旆然長也。沛然而垂則長，故毛云爾也。又旆爲旗幟之總名，如左傳狐毛設二旆而退之、晉亡大旆之左旃是也。又假茷爲旆，如左傳綪茷卽蒨旆、詩帛茷央央卽帛旆是也。亦有與末通用者。士喪禮：書銘於末日某氏某之柩。注曰：今文銘皆爲名，末爲旆。</span><span class="ywyy">从㫃<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣎵</span>聲。</span><span class="swdz">蒲葢切。十五部。</span></p>                          </div>
