                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>浩</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄠˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 水大，引申为大和多：～大。～繁。～荡。～瀚。～劫。～淼（亦作&ldquo;浩渺&rdquo;）。～气（盛大刚直之气）。～如烟海。</p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">浩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>浩</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，告声。本义:水势浩大)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[onrushing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浩，浇也。从水，告声。虞书曰:&ldquo;洪水浩浩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，浇者，</span><span class="diczx2">许</span>以声训。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浩，遶也，水大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浩，浩流也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浩浩沅湘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;怀沙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浩浩瀚瀚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;俶真》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曰浩泽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;地形》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:浩波(大波，洪波);浩汗(浩浩，浩然，浩瀚。水盛大的样子);浩洋(水流广阔洪大的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 广远;盛大 <span class="encs">[vast;grand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浩浩，滔天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span>。传:&ldquo;盛大。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陈竽瑟兮浩倡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;东皇太一》</span>。注:&ldquo;大也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:浩壤(广阔的土地);浩渺(广阔无边);浩然(盛大纯一的样子;不可堵塞的样子;浩然之气的省称);浩烦(浩大繁多);浩滂(盛大)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 丰富，众多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丧祭有余曰浩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹饶也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:浩博(广大众多);浩漫(众多的样子;广阔的样子);浩穰(盛大众多的样子);浩侈(繁多)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 远;无边无际 <span class="encs">[faraway;boundless;limitless]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心飞扬兮浩荡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;河伯》</span>。注:&ldquo;志放貌也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:浩乎无际(广大无际);浩浩漫漫(大雾无边无际)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通&ldquo;傲&rdquo;。傲慢 <span class="encs">[arrogant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浩倨者则不亲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语&middot;三怨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:浩居(浩裾，浩倨。傲倨，怠慢不恭貌)</p>
