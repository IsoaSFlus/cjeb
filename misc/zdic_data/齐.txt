                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>齐</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">齊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>东西的一头平或排成一条直线：～整。参差不～。</li>
	<li>达到，跟什么一般平：见贤思～。河水～腰深。</li>
	<li>同时；同样；一起：～名。～声。～心协力。一～前进。</li>
	<li>全；完全：～全。人到～了。</li>
	<li>中国周代诸侯国名，疆域在今山东省北部、东部和河北省的东南部。</li>
	<li>中国朝代名：南朝～。北朝～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>齐</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">齊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>调剂：夫匠者手巧也，而医者～药也。后作&ldquo;剂&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>药剂：处～不过数种。后作&ldquo;剂&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>界限；分际：百年，寿之大～。</li>
	<li>成功；成熟：事能得～。后作&ldquo;济&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>齐</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">齊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> ㄗ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/粢">粢</a>&rdquo;。古代用于祭祀的谷物。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>齐</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">齊</span>）</strong></p>

<ol>

	<li>斋戒：～三日而后行。</li>

<p>◎ <strong>齐</strong></p>
<p>齊 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;剂&rdquo;。调味品 <span class="encs">[flavouring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡齐，执之以右，居之以左。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 合金 <span class="encs">[alloy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金有六齐:六分其金而锡居一，谓之钟鼎之齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记&middot;辀人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;跻&rdquo;。登，升 <span class="encs">[ascend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝命不违，至於汤齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;长发》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>齐</strong></p>
<p>齊 <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象禾麦穗头长得平整的样子。小篆字形下面多了两条线，表示地面。本义:禾麦吐穗上平整)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[neat;even]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齊，禾麦吐穗上平也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">立如齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 整齐 <span class="encs">[in good order;neat;uniform]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其君齐明衷正。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为齐敬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气候不齐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上下方整，前后齐平。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王羲之</span><span class="smcs">《题笔阵图》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">进退得齐焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;礼乐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齐均(整齐均匀);齐贝(整齐的贝壳。即&ldquo;编贝&rdquo;);齐肃(整齐严肃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 平等 <span class="encs">[equal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齐明而不竭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;脩身》</span>。注:&ldquo;无偏无颇也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齐民无藏盖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平准书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齐等(平等)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 全，齐全 <span class="encs">[all present;all ready;complete]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而民不齐出于南亩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平准书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">各屋子里丫头们将灯火俱已点齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齐全人(指家里的长辈及丈夫都全的妇女，被认为是有福气的);人都到齐了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 好 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如时运不齐，命途多舛。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 疾，敏捷 <span class="encs">[quick;fast;speed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齐，疾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齐疾而均，速若飘风。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;弱民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齐给如响。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;臣道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多闻而齐给。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尚书&middot;大传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;斋&rdquo;（ <span class="dicpy">zhai</span>）。肃敬 <span class="encs">[solemn and silence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而又能齐肃衷正。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语下》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>齐</strong></p>
<p>齊 <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同等，相等 <span class="encs">[equal; of the same class，rank，or status]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以给水火之齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;亨人》</span>。注;&ldquo;多少之量也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大时不齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学记 》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与日月齐光。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一日之内，一宫之间，而气候不齐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齐生(指自然中万物生死的本质都是相同的);齐死(指人生在世，无论贵贱贤愚，长寿短命，其死则同，死而必为腐骨亦同);齐同(一致，同等);齐物(万事万物从大道的眼光来看，没有生死、成毁、美丑的不同，但每物都有其存在的价值，每事都有其发生的意义)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 整治 <span class="encs">[govern]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修身齐家治国平天下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《少年中国说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齐正(平定)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 达到同样高度 <span class="encs">[be on a level with;reach a certain point or line]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妻为具食，不敢于</span><span class="diczx2">鸿</span>前仰视，举案齐眉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至</span><span class="diczx2">日观</span>数里内无树，而雪与人膝齐。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 使整齐 <span class="encs">[trim]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">马不齐髦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;既夕礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齐叶</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 集合 <span class="encs">[assemble]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:齐人(把人召集齐全)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 正好对着 <span class="encs">[right down to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:齐头子(劈头盖脸);把玉米秆齐着根儿砍断</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;斋&rdquo;（ <span class="dicpy">zhāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāi.mp3"></a></span> ）。斋戒 <span class="encs">[fast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齐则缁之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使五人齐，而长入拜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十三年》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;资&rdquo;。资助 <span class="encs">[finance;subsidize;support]</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>齐</strong></p>
<p>齊 <span class="dicpy">Q&iacute;</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 朝代名 <span class="encs">[Qi Dynasty]</span>。南北朝时代，南朝萧道成代宋为帝，国号齐，史称南齐(479&mdash;502);北朝高洋代北魏为帝，国号齐，史称北齐(550&mdash;557)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 齐国 <span class="encs">[Qi state]</span>。中国周朝分封的诸候国名(公元前1122&mdash;前265)，在今山东省</p>

<p><span class="diczx1">质于</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>兵乃出。</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>之精英。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;脐&rdquo;。肚脐 <span class="encs">[umbilicus]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若不早图，后君噬齐，其及图之乎!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公六年》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">不知斯齐国几千万里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;黄帝》</span>。注:&ldquo;齐，中也。&rdquo;</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9F4A.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕亝【唐韻】徂兮切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】前西切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音臍。【說文】禾麥吐穗上平也。【註】徐鍇曰：生而齊者，莫如禾麥。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】整也。【正韻】無偏頗也。【荀子&middot;富國篇】必將修禮以齊朝，正法以齊官，平政以齊民。【註】齊，整也。</p>
<p>又等也。【前漢&middot;食貨志】世家子弟富人，或鬭雞走狗馬，弋獵博戲，亂齊民。【註】如淳曰：齊，等也。無有貴賤，謂之齊民。</p>
<p>又【正韻】莊也，肅也。【左傳&middot;文二年】子雖齊聖，不先父食。【註】齊，肅也。</p>
<p>又正也。【詩&middot;小雅】人之齊聖。【註】中正通知之人也。【朱傳】齊，肅也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】殷齊，中也。【註】釋地曰：岠齊州以南。【疏】齊，中也。中州爲齊州。中州猶言中國也。【列子&middot;黃帝篇】華胥氏之國，不知斯齊國幾千萬里。【註】斯，離也。齊，中也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】好也。</p>
<p>又辨也。【易&middot;繫辭】小大者存乎卦。【註】齊，猶言辨也。</p>
<p>又速也。【爾雅&middot;釋言】疾齊，壯也。【註】謂速也。【史記&middot;五帝紀】幼而徇齊。【註】徇疾，齊速，言聖人幼而疾速。</p>
<p>又國名。武王封太公之地，今山東靑州濟南濰縣安樂等處是也。</p>
<p>又乾齊，縣名。屬酒泉郡。見【後漢&middot;郡國志】。</p>
<p>又姓。【風俗通&middot;氏姓篇序】四氏，於國齊魯宋衞，是也。</p>
<p>又放齊，堯臣名。</p>
<p>又【諡法】執心克莊曰齊。資輔就共曰齊。</p>
<p>又與臍通。【左傳&middot;莊六年】後君噬齊。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>在禮切，音薺。【集韻】齊齊，恭慤貌。【禮&middot;玉藻】廟中齊齊。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】在詣切【集韻】【正韻】才詣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音劑。【禮&middot;內則】凡食齊，視春時。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;醬人註】食有和齊，藥之類也。</p>
<p>又酒以度量節作者謂之齊。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;酒正】五齊三酒，亦作齌。</p>
<p>又火齊，珠名。一曰似雲母。重疊而開，色黃赤如金。</p>
<p>又【集韻】子計切，音霽。和也。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;食醫】八珍之齊。</p>
<p>又【正韻】津私切，音貲。【論語】攝齊升堂。孔安國曰：衣下曰齊。【禮曲禮】兩手摳衣去齊尺。【註】齊，謂裳下緝也。</p>
<p>又莊皆切。與齋同。【禮&middot;祭統】齊之爲言齊也，齊不齊以致齊者也。</p>
<p>又牋西切，音齏。與齏同。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;醢人五齊註】齊，當爲齏。五齊，昌本<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26709.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦜉</span>析蜃豚拍深蒲也。【疏】齏葅菜肉之通稱。</p>
<p>又與躋同。【禮&middot;樂記】地氣上齊。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】疾私切，與薺通。【禮&middot;玉藻】趨中采齊。【鄭註】齊，當爲楚薺之薺。【釋文】齊，依註作薺，疾私反。</p>
<p>又【正韻】才資切，音疵。引玉藻采齊當讀疵音。</p>
<p>又【正韻】齋字，古單作齊。詳齋字註。</p>
<p>又【集韻】子淺切，音翦。同剪。【說文】斷也。剪取其齊，故謂齊爲剪。【儀禮&middot;旣夕】馬下齊髦。【註】齊，剪也。</p>
