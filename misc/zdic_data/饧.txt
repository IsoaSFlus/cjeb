                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>饧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">餳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>糖稀。</li>
	<li>糖块、面剂子等变软：糖～了。</li>
	<li>精神不振，眼睛半睁半闭：眼睛发～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sugar, syrup; malt sugar; sticky</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Melassesirup, Melasse  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mélasse</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>饧</strong></p>
<p>餳 <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>用麦芽或谷芽熬成的饴糖 <span class="encs">[maltose;malt sugar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泼火雨初晴。草色青青。傍檐垂柳卖春饧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李彭老</span><span class="smcs">《浪淘沙》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">湖鱼香胜肉，官酒重于饧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《历阳书事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饧糟(熬麦芽糖剩下的渣子);饧饼(一种糖饼);饧粥(甜粥);饧蜜(糖蜜);饧糖(麦芽糖);饧桃(甜桃)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>饧</strong></p>
<p>餳 <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 糖块、面剂子等变软 <span class="encs">[soften]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:糖饧了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 形容眼色朦胧 <span class="encs">[with sleepy eyes]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">八戒</span>饧眼偷看，你道他怎生打扮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">只觉口齿缠绵，眉眼愈加饧涩，忙伏侍他睡下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饧涩(精神涣散，眼色朦胧);饧眼(脒着眼睛，朦胧而视)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9933.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>徒郞切，音唐。【說文】飴和饊也。【揚子&middot;方言】餳謂之餹。【釋文】餳，洋也。煮米消爛，洋洋然也。【急就章】棃<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23408.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣐈</span>柰桃待露霜，棗杏瓜棣饊飴餳。【詩話】劉禹錫曰：詩用僻字，宜有來歷。宋考功云：沈佺期嶺表寒食詩：馬上逢寒食，春來不見餳。常疑之。因讀毛詩，簫管備舉，鄭箋，簫，編小竹管，如今賣餳者所吹，六經惟此中有餳字。吾緣明日重九，押一餻字讀，尋思六經無餻，故不敢爲之。◎按重編廣韻云：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29783.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩞃</span>，徐盈切，當从昜，正韻从易誤。餳，徒郞切，當从易，今混爲一字，非。字彙旣有<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/296FF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩛿</span>字，从徐盈反，復于餳字作徐盈、徒郞二切，尚未了然於字書之誤也。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">𩛿</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“饧”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/𩛿"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/296FF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩛿</span></a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷五">卷五</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷五&amp;swbs=食">食</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/296FF.svg">                          <p>飴和饊者也。从食易聲。徐盈切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/296FF_1.svg" /> <span class="swbf">(餳)</span> <span class="ywyy">飴和饊者也。</span> <span class="swdz">不和饊謂之飴，和饊謂之餳，故成國云飴弱於餳也。方言曰：凡飴謂之餳，自關而東、陳楚宋衛之閒通語也。楊子渾言之，許析言之。周禮小師注：管，如今賣飴所吹者。周頌箋亦云。</span> <span class="ywyy">从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2028A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠊊</span>昜聲。</span> <span class="swdz">各本篆作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/296FF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩛿</span>，云易聲，今正。按鍚从昜聲，故音陽，亦音唐，在十部。釋名曰：餳，洋也。李軌周禮音唐是也。其陸氏音義周禮辭盈反，毛詩夕清反，因之唐韵徐盈切。此十部音轉入於十一部，如行庚觥等字之入庚韵。郭璞三倉解詁曰：楊音盈協韵。晉灼漢書音義反<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/234FE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣓾</span>惲爲由嬰，其理正同耳。淺人乃易其龤聲之偏旁，玉篇、廣韵皆誤从易。然玉篇曰：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/296FF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩛿</span>，徒當切。廣韵十一唐曰：糖，飴也。十四清曰：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/296FF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩛿</span>，飴也。皆可使學者知餳、糖一字，不當从易。至於集韵始以餳入唐韵，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/296FF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩛿</span>入清韵，畫分二字，使人眞雁不分，其誤更甚，猶賴類篇正之。錫，古音如洋，語之轉如唐，故方言曰：餳謂之餹。郭云：江東皆言餹，音唐。</span></p>                          </div>
