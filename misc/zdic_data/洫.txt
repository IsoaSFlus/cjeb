                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>洫</strong> <span class="dicpy">xù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，血声。本义:古井田制，成与成之间的水道。田间的水沟)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[water course in the field]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洫，田间水道也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">方十里为成，成间宽八尺，深八尺，谓之洫。此井田之制。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·匠人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百夫有洫，洫上有涂。此乡遂之制。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·遂人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指河渠 <span class="encs">[rivers and canals]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">昱</span>乃上作方梁石洫，水常饶足。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 护城河 <span class="encs">[moat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">经城洫。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">揣高卑，度厚薄，仞沟洫，物土方…以令役于诸侯。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 小水 <span class="encs">[small water]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大达无畛，不要止洫。——<span class="smcs">《太玄·达》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古水名 <span class="encs">[Xu River]</span>。即今贯河北省东北部的滦河，上源在内蒙，古称<span class="yhcs">“闪电河”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>洫</strong> <span class="dicpy">xù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 空出，使空虚 <span class="encs">[empty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是以长者断之，短者续之，满者洫之，虚者实之。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 败坏 <span class="encs">[corrupt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与世偕行而不替，所行之备而不洫。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 昏惑，迷乱 <span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6D2B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】況逼切【集韻】【韻會】忽域切【正韻】呼狊切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音侐。田閒水道也。【說文】十里爲成。成閒廣八尺，深八尺，謂之洫。</p><p>又城池也。【詩·大雅】築城伊洫。</p><p>又渠也。【後漢·鮑永傳】作方梁石洫。【註】猶今之水門。</p><p>又水名。【前漢·地理志】洫水，出漁陽郡白檀北蠻夷。</p><p>又虛也。【管子·小稱篇】滿者洫之。</p><p>又濫也。【莊子·則陽篇】所行之備而不洫。【註】洫，濫也。王云壞敗也。</p><p>又【集韻】弋質切，音逸。深貌。</p>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6D2B.svg">                          <p>十里爲成。成閒廣八尺，深八尺謂之洫。从水血聲。《論語》曰：“盡力于溝洫。”況逼切</p>                          </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6D2B_3326.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(洫)</span><span class="ywyy">十里爲成。成閒廣八尺、深八尺謂之洫。</span><span class="swdz">考工記匠人文也。匠人溝洫之制、惟歙程氏瑤田通藝錄能發明之。洫亦作淢。韓詩築城伊洫。毛詩作淢。傳曰。淢、成溝也。箋云。方十里曰成。淢其溝也。按門閾之字、古文作？。是或與血異部而音通也。溝洫對文則異。散文則通。故毛曰成溝。</span><span class="ywyy">从水。血聲。</span><span class="swdz">况逼切。按古音在十二部。入聲。今入職韵者、以毛詩作淢之故。</span><span class="ywyy">論語曰。盡力于溝洫。</span><span class="swdz">論語泰伯篇。今本于作乎。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
