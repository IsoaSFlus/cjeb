						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>案</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">àn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/àn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>长形的桌子或架起来代替桌子用的长木板：～子。～板。书～。条～。拍～而起。</li><li>提出计划、方法和建议的文件或记录：档～。备～。议～。提～。方～。有～可查。～卷。～牍。</li><li>事件，特指涉及法律问题的事件：惨～。血～。～件。～例。～犯。破～。</li><li>古代有短脚盛食物的木托盘：举～齐眉（形容夫妻相敬）。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">按</span>”。</li></ol>

vessel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵王</span><span class="diczx2">张敖</span>自持案进食。——<span class="smcs">《史记·田叔列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妻为具食，不敢于</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">成</span>妻纳钱案上。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终朝理文案，薄暮不遑瞑。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《答张士然》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无案牍之劳形。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="encs">[records;archives;dossier;files]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取案末独身无亲戚者二人易汝名。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">竟行之，案末二人立决。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:案记(卷宗文录);案检(案卷)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 按语。同<span class="yhcs">“按”</span> <span class="encs">[note]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">案:</span><span class="diczx2">黄帝</span>葬于桥山，犹曰君臣葬其衣冠。——<span
