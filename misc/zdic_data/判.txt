                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>判</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>区别，分辨，断定：～明。～辨。～据。～读（利用已知的视觉信息符号来判断新获得的视觉信息的含义）。～断。</li><li>分开，截然不同：～然。～若两人。～若鸿沟。</li><li>评定：裁～。谈～。～卷子。</li><li>司法机关对案件的裁决：～词。～决。～案。</li><li>古代官名：通～。～官（中国唐、宋两代辅助地方长官处理公事的人员，传说中借指阎王手下管生死簿的官）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">判</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>判</strong> <span class="dicpy">pàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声兼会意。从刀，半声。从刀，表明其意义与刀有联系。<span class="yhcs">“半”</span>是把牛分开。本义:分，分开)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[divide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">判，分也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">继犹判涣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·访落》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纪于是乎判。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若七德离判，民乃携贰。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语中》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂判为十二，合为七国，威分于陪臣之邦。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《封建论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:判割(分割);判妻(丈夫去世或与丈夫离异后再嫁的妇女);判散(分散);判礼(分离;分开);判迹(分道而行);判裂(割裂;分离)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 判决 <span class="encs">[judge;sentence;condemn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太尉判状辞甚巽(恭顺)。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《段太尉逸事状》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:审判(审理和判决);判词(判决的文词);判牍(判决司法案件的文书);判有罪;判无罪</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 区别;分辨 <span class="encs">[distinguish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">判天地之美，析万物之理。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故不战而强弱胜负已判矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:判别是非;判正(分辨是非曲直)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 裁定 <span class="encs">[decide]</span>。如:公判;评判(判定胜负或优劣);判事(判决事务的是非曲直);判个死日(预知死期。判:断定);判命(判定人的命运;拼命);判析(裁决)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> <span class="encs">[为了评价或强调的目的]</span> 评定做上符号或记号 <span class="encs">[mark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使者呈上书看时，封面上判云:<span class="yhcs">“</span><span class="diczx2">汉大丞相</span>付 <span class="diczx2">周都督</span>开拆。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:判卷子;判阅(批阅)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 球赛中裁定球或球员的情况 <span class="encs">[call]</span>。如:判发球出界;判跑垒员安全进垒</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“拚”</span>。舍弃 <span class="encs">[forsake]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">海波无底珠沉海，采珠之人判死采。——</span><span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《采珠行》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>判</strong> <span class="dicpy">pàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 半 <span
