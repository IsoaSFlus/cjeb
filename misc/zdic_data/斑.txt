						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>斑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄢˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 一种颜色中夹杂的别种颜色的点子或条纹：～点。～纹。～斓。雀～。～秃。～白。～竹。～驳（色彩错落）。管中窥豹，可见一～（喻从看到的一部分，推测全貌）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mottled, striped, freckle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tache,rayure,tacheté,rayé,panaché</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>斑</strong> <span class="dicpy">bān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本作<span class="yhcs">“班”</span>，从文，辡( <span class="dicpy">biàn</span>)声。本义:杂色的花纹或斑点)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[speck;spot;maculation]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辬，驳文也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“今作斑也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">管中窥豹，时见一斑。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·王献之传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">唯余望乡泪，更染竹成斑。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋之问</span><span class="smcs">《晚泊湘江》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:汗斑;斑马;斑污(斑点污渍);雀斑;豹子的斑;油斑</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“班”</span>。行列;位次 <span class="encs">[sequence]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">登斑叙优。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《竹邑侯相张寿碑》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是揖五瑞，斑宗彝。——</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span><span class="smcs">《孔羡碑》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>斑</strong> <span class="dicpy">bān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 色彩驳杂的;灿烂多彩的 <span class="encs">[motley]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斑，驳也，文也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上辬华以交纷。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斑白者不提挈。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拉虎摧斑。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《七启》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中有小斑纹。——</span><span class="diczx2">洪适</span><span class="smcs">《歙砚说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:斑彪(色彩斑杂的小老虎);斑尾(彩色斑烂的羽尾);斑剥(斑驳);斑阑(斑兰，斑斓。色彩错杂灿烂)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 毛色花白的 <span class="encs">[greying]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">塞上长城空自许，镜中衰鬓已先斑。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《书愤》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:斑苍(花白色);斑驺(毛色青白相杂的骏马)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr"
