                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蹂</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>蹂</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>搓揉。《唐寫切韻殘本&bull;尤韻》：&ldquo;蹂，踐穀。&rdquo;《詩&bull;大雅&bull;生民》：&ldquo;或舂或揄，或簸或蹂。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>踐踏。《廣雅&bull;釋詁一》：&ldquo;蹂，履也。&rdquo;《玉篇&bull;足部》：&ldquo;蹂，蹂踐，蹋也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>獸足踐踏地面。也作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/禸">禸</a>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<a href="/hans/厹">厹</a>&rdquo;。《説文&bull;禸部》：&ldquo;禸，獸足蹂地也。蹂，篆文从足，柔聲。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>獸跡。也作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/厹">厹</a>&rdquo;。《集韻&bull;有韻》：&ldquo;厹，獸跡。或作蹂。&rdquo;用暴力欺压、侵凌。元戴良《李仲賓像贊》：&ldquo;蹂强翼弱，名四馳也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>行疾。《廣雅&bull;釋詁一》：&ldquo;蹂，疾也。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蹂</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，柔声。本义:践踏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[trample on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蹂，履也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蹂，蹂践，蹋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余骑相蹂践争</span><span class="diczx2">项王</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蹂若(蹂践、蹂蹈、践踏);蹂蹈(践踏);蹂踏(犹踩踏);蹂抑(践踏压制);蹂促(犹践踏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 欺压、侵凌;侵袭 <span class="encs">[make inroads on;aggression;bully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蹂强翼弱，名四驰也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">戴良</span><span class="smcs">《李促宾像赞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蹂藉(侵害;侵扰);蹂剔(侵害)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 揉擦。通&ldquo;揉&rdquo; <span class="encs">[rub]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或簸或蹂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;生民》</span></span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8E42.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】人又切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】如又切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音輮。【說文】厹，篆文从足柔聲。獸足蹂地也。【博雅】疾也，履也。【玉篇】踐也。【史記&middot;項羽紀】餘騎相蹂踐。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】蹂蕙圃，踐蘭唐。</p>
