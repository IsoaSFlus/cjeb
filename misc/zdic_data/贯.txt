						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>贯</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">貫</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>古代穿钱的绳索（把方孔钱穿在绳子上，每一千个为一贯）：&ldquo;～朽而不可校&rdquo;。腰缠万～。</li>
	<li>穿，通，连：～穿。～串。～通（➊连接，沟通；➋对学术等全部透彻地了解）。连～。～注。～彻。～珠（连珠成串，常用来形容声音的圆润动听）。鱼～而入。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">惯</span>&rdquo;，习惯。</li>
	<li>原籍，出生地：籍～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a string of 1000 coins; to go through</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufreihen, bespannen ,lochen, durchbohren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> traverser,pénétrer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贯</strong></p>
<p>貫 <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从毌( <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> )，从贝(表示与钱财有关)。&ldquo;毌&rdquo;是贯穿之贯，象穿物之形。本义:穿钱的绳子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[string]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贯，钱贝之贯也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都内之钱，贯朽而不可挍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厨有臭败肉，库有贯朽钱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《伤宅》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反尔如贯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;何人斯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贯朽(串钱的绳子朽断，形容积钱多而经久不用)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 旧时用绳索穿钱，每一千文为一贯 <span class="encs">[a string of 1000 cash]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">着落店主人家追要原典身钱三千贯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贯百(贯伯。一贯钱，一些钱。百:一百文钱);钱串一贯(一千文钱);万贯家私</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 事情，事例 <span class="encs">[thing;example]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">闵子骞</span>曰:&ldquo;仍旧贯，如之何?何必改作?&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;先进》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贯利(事功和利益)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 条理;系统 <span class="encs">[system]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鱼贯而进。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;邓艾传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贯类(从同类事物的比较中贯通其理);贯鱼(穿成一串的鱼。比喻有次序)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 罪恶 <span class="encs">[crime]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">商</span>罪贯盈，天命诛之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;秦誓》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贯恶(一贯的罪恶);贯盈(罪恶满盈);贯天(滔天。形容罪恶、灾祸极大)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 祖籍;籍贯 <span class="encs">[native place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翁云贯属</span><span class="diczx2">新丰</span>县，生逢聖代无征战。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《新丰折臂翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贯系(籍贯世系);贯址(籍贯和地址)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 序次;按顺序排列 <span class="encs">[order;arrange in order]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今猥以轻鄙，超伦踰等，上乱聖朝贯鱼之序，下违群士准平立论。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;蔡谟传》</span></span></p>

<p>又【博雅】穿也。【五經文字】旣穿曰貫。【易&middot;剝卦】貫魚。【釋文】貫，穿也。【前漢&middot;董仲舒傳】豈不同條共貫與。【註】貫者，聮絡貫穿。</p>

<p>又【韻府】本貫，鄕籍也。</p>

<p>又【詩&middot;魏風】三歲貫女。【傳】貫，事也。【釋文】古亂反。○按朱傳：貫，習也。音慣。</p>
