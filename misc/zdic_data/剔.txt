						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>剔</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tī.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄧˉ</span></p><ol><li>分解骨肉，把肉从骨头上刮下来：把骨头上的肉～干净。</li><li>从缝隙或孔洞里往外挑拨东西：～牙。</li><li>把不好的挑出来：～除。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，易声。本义:分解骨肉，把肉刮下)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[scrape off]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剔，解骨也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，即为鬄之或体。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">去骨曰剔。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span
