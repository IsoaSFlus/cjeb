                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赴</strong> <span class="dicpy">fù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从走，卜声。从<span class="yhcs">“走”</span>的字、多与<span class="yhcs">“跑”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“走”</span>有关。本义:奔向，奔赴。多指奔向危险的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[go to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赴，趋也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">万里赴戎机，关山度若飞。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下之欲疾其君者，皆欲赴愬于王。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以赴礼者。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赴水则接腋持颐。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·秋水》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:赴履(奔赴);赴质(奔赴目标);赴助(赴救);赴急(往救急难);赴援(赴救)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 前往;去;到;至，到达 <span class="encs">[get to;attend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长子迈将赴</span><span class="diczx2">饶</span>之德兴尉，送之至湖口。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾今且赴府，不久当归还。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:赴吊(前往吊丧);赴市(到市集上去);赴考(前往应试);赴哀(奔丧);赴接(前往救援、接应)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“讣”</span>。报丧(古人讣告字只作赴者，取急疾之意。) <span class="encs">[give obituary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">死必赴。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·文王世子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡崩薨，不赴则不书。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">周烈王</span>崩，诸侯皆吊， <span class="diczx2">齐</span>后往。 <span class="diczx2">周</span>怒，赴于 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>曰。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策三》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赴者未至，则哭于巷，哀于馆。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:赴闻(报丧);赴告(春秋时，各国将贵族丧亡祸福等事告诉别国。凶事叫赴，其他的事叫告。今俗称讣告)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 跳进，举身投入 <span class="encs">[jump into]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">揽裙脱丝履，举身赴清池。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:赴海归神(跳海而死);赴鼎(跳入汤镬而死);赴险(进入险地);赴蹈(赴汤蹈火。献身)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 投身;参与 <span class="encs">[join]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲以客往赴秦军，与赵国俱死。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:赴命(奔命，拼命努力)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 合，顺应 <span class="encs">[accord with]</span>。如:赴拍(应合节拍);赴度(应合节拍);赴趣(钻营;迎合)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 游泳 <span
