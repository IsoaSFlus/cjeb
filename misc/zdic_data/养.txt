                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">养</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>养</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">養</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄤˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>抚育，供给生活品：～育。赡～。抚～。～家。</li>
	<li>饲养动物，培植花草：～花。～殖。</li>
	<li>生育，生小孩儿。</li>
	<li>抚养的（非亲生的）：～子。～父。～母。</li>
	<li>教育，训练：培～。教～。</li>
	<li>使身心得到滋补和休息：～病。～心。～性。休～。营～。～精蓄锐。</li>
	<li>保护修补：～路。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> raise, rear, bring up; support</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufziehen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> nourrir,entretenir,élever,se soigner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">养</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>养</strong></p>
<p>養 <span class="dicpy">yǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，羊声。本义:饲养)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 供养，奉养;抚育 <span class="encs">[support;provide for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">养，供养也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。古文从攴，未详。疑厮养作此字。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡食养阴气也，凡饮养阳气也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特牲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殳能生之不能养之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">养不亏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">监门之养。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不食力而养足。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">养公姥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">供养卒大恩。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">归养。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以养父母。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《伤仲永》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:养娇囡(因父母溺爱而性格骄纵的姑娘);养家人(担负赡养全家生计的人);养济院(孤老院。专收鳏寡孤独老人的慈善机构)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 饲养 <span class="encs">[raise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驯养一虫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于盆而养。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笼养之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:养鸡;养狗;养牛;养马</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 生育 <span class="encs">[give birth to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家家养男当门户。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot;张籍<span class="smcs">《筑城词》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:养孩子</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 培养，修养 <span class="encs">[cultivate;foster]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">立太傅少傅以养之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;文王世子》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹教之。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">养其旁条。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:养息机(修养身心;修炼);养材儿(正处在教养阶段学习干活的少年);养廉银子(清朝按职务等级，在常俸之外另给官吏的银钱);养士(培养人才)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 保养 <span class="encs">[convalesce;recuperate one&#39;s health]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:养身(保养身体。同养生)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:养疴(调养疾病)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">如:养痈贻患(养痈成患);养疥成疮(喻姑息小恶，酿成大患);养女吊妇(勾引妇女乱搞不正当的男女关系);养婆娘(男子与非婚女人发生私情);养女婿(养汉子。女人与非婚男子发生私情);养奸(使奸邪得以滋长)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/990A.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕䍩<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22F1D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢼝</span>【廣韻】餘兩切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】以兩切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音痒。【玉篇】育也，畜也，長也。【易&middot;頤卦】觀頤，觀其所養也。</p>
<p>又取也。【詩&middot;周頌】於鑠王師，遵養時晦。【傳】養，取也。</p>
<p>又養養，憂貌。【詩&middot;邶風】中心養養。【註】憂不定貌。</p>
<p>又【博雅】養，使也。【公羊傳&middot;宣十二年】厮役扈養。【註】艾草爲防者曰厮，汲水漿者曰役，養馬者曰扈，炊烹者曰養。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;儒林傳】兒寬常爲弟子都養。【註】都養，爲弟子造食也。</p>
<p>又姓。養由基，見【左傳&middot;成十六年】。養奮，見【孝子傳】。</p>
<p>又與癢同。【荀子&middot;正名篇】疾養凔熱，滑鈹輕重，以形體異。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【正韻】餘亮切【集韻】弋亮切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音恙。【玉篇】供養也，下奉上也。【禮&middot;月令】收祿秩之不當，供養之不宜者。【註】謂凡恩命濫賜膳服，侈僭踰制者。　【說文】本作養。【字彙】省作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/296EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩛬</span>，附六畫，非。</p>
