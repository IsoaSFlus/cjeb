                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">实</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>实</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">實</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄕˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>充满：～心。充～。虚～。</li>
	<li>符合客观情况，真，真诚：～话。～惠。～际（真实情况）。～践（实行；履行）。～体。～情。～施。～数。～事求是。名～相符。</li>
	<li>植物结的果：果～。开花结～。</li>
	<li>富足：殷～。富～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> real, true; honest, sincere</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> faktisch, real, tatsächlich, wirklich  (Adj)​,Frucht, Samen  (S)​,echt, wahr, ehrlich, wahrhaftig, solide  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> réalité,fait,plein,vrai,réel,fruit</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">实</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>实</strong></p>
<p>實 <span class="dicpy">sh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从宀，从贯。宀，房屋。贯，货物，以货物充于屋下。本义:财物粮食充足，富有)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[prosperous;well-to-do;well-off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">實，富也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">实，满也，塞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">实，荣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子经上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有者为实，故凡中质充满皆曰实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;调经论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而君之仓廪实，府库充。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">管子</span>曰:&ldquo;仓廪实而知礼节。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公家虚而大臣实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;亡征》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:殷实;实核(种子);实谷(子粒饱满的谷类作物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 真实;诚实 <span class="encs">[true;dependable;honest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">实，诚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫图画，非母之实身也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;乱龙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虚则知实之情。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;主道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今人又言心电感应有道，吾亦望其言是实。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:实充(老实);实受(忠厚老实);实业(实际业绩);实迹(真实的证据，事实);实拍(实在的);实丕丕(实呸呸、实坯坯。都指实实在在);实志(真实的心愿);实理(真实的道理);实音(确实的消息);实户(实际户数);实支(实际支出);实年(实际年龄);实务(实际事物);实福(实际的幸福);实祸(实际的祸害)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 广大 <span class="encs">[extensive;vast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">节彼南山，有实其猗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;节南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 坚实;坚强 <span class="encs">[solid;firm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵之形，避实而击虚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;虚实》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:实落(结实;实实在在);实拍拍(形容硬、结实);实秘秘(严严实实);实癖癖(硬鼓鼓);实坚(坚定)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>实</strong></p>
<p>實 <span class="dicpy">sh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="encs">[wealth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚敛积实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令尹问蓄聚积实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">好实无厌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;哀公问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耻费强实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾有卿之名，而无其实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庭实设。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;乘马。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庭实，贡献之物也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;班彪传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其君子尊仁畏义，耻费轻实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:积实(积累财富)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 果实;种子 <span class="encs">[fruit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草木之实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭统》</span>。注:&ldquo;菱芡榛栗之属。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">实函斯活。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;载芟》</span>。笺:&ldquo;种子也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复蒂下生蓬，蓬中结实，亭亭独立。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">用实者成实时采。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:结实</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 实际，事实 <span class="encs">[reality;fact]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今以实校之，彼所将中国人不过十五六万。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">较</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之所得，与战胜而得者，其实百倍。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《权书&middot;六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:实情</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 物资，器物 <span class="encs">[material]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 结果，效果 <span class="encs">[effect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畏惧存想，同一实也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 诚实的人 <span class="encs">[honest person]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 真正地;确实;实在 <span class="encs">[really;certainly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瞻望弗及，实劳我心。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;燕燕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">兰芝</span>仰头答:理实如兄言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 终于 <span class="encs">[finally]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 实际上 <span class="encs">[actually;in fact]</span></p>

<p>實 <span class="dicpy">sh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:实牣(充满;充实);实实窒窒(拥挤不堪的样子);实心(充塞于心);实窒胡同(死胡同);实边(充实边疆);实禀(充实粮仓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 实践;实行 <span class="encs">[practise]</span>。</p>

<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 据实陈报 <span class="encs">[report]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">實</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“实”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/實">實</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=宀" target="_blank">宀</a>】
                              實·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5BE6.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕宲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21F8D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡾍</span>【唐韻】【廣韻】神質切【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】食質切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音失。【說文】實，富也。从宀从貫。貫，貨貝也。【廣韻】誠也，滿也。【增韻】充也，虛之對也。【易&middot;本義】乾一而實，坤二而虛。【孟子】充實之謂美，充實而有光輝之謂大。【宋程頤曰】心有主則實，實則外患不能入。</p>
<p>又華實。【晉語】華則榮矣，實之不知，請務實乎。【史記&middot;商鞅傳】貌言華也，至言實也。</p>
<p>又物成實也。【爾雅&middot;釋草】果臝之、括樓<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>昺疏。實卽子也。【禮&middot;月令】季春，乃爲麥祈實。【註】謂於含秀求其成也。</p>
<p>又品物也。【左傳&middot;莊二十二年】庭實旅百。【註】庭之所實<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>有百品，言物備也。</p>
<p>又【襄三十一年】其輸之，則君之府實也。</p>
<p>又軍實。【左傳&middot;襄二十四年】齊社，蒐軍實。杜註祭社，因閱數軍器。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;特牲饋食】實豆籩。【註】謂取籩豆實之也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;小宗伯註】豆實實於罋，簋實實於篚。</p>
<p>又具數也。【史記&middot;始皇本紀】使黔首自實田。【註】謂令民自具頃畝實數也。</p>
<p>又【唐六典】凡里有手實法，歲終，具民之年與地闊狹爲鄕帳。</p>
<p>又驗也。【後漢&middot;光武紀】使各實二千石，以下至黃綬。</p>
<p>又事跡也。【史記&middot;莊周傳】率皆虛語，無事實。【韓非傳】反舉浮淫之蠹，加之功實之上。</p>
<p>又當也。【書&middot;呂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>】閱實其罪。【註】使與法相當也。</p>
<p>又是也。【詩&middot;大雅】實墉實壑。</p>
<p>又姓。</p>
<p>又【正字通】脂利切，音至。與至同。【禮&middot;雜記】計於適者曰：吾子之外私寡大夫不祿，使某實。【註】言爲計而至此也。　【增韻】實，亦作寔。【韻會】實寔分爲二。</p>
