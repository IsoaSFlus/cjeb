						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>揭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄝˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>把盖在上面的东西拿起，或把粘合着的东西分开：～锅。～幕。</li>    <li>使隐瞒的事物显露：～露。～发。～底。～穿。～短。～晓。</li>    <li>高举：～竿而起。</li>    <li>标示：～橥（<span class="dicpy">zhū</span>）（本是作标记的小木桩，引申为标志。亦作“揭著”）。～示。</li>    <li>扛，持。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>揭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qì.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 提起衣裳：～衣涉水。</p></ol>
