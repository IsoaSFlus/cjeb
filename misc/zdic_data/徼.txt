for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡君愿徼福于</span><span class="diczx2">周公</span><span class="diczx2">鲁公</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">患其徼一时之权。——<span inspection]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">几为巡徼所陵迫死。——</span><span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 边界，边境 <span class="encs">[boundary;border]</span></p><p><span
