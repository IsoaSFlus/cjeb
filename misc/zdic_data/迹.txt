                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>迹</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>脚印：踪～。足～。血～。笔～。</li>    <li>物体遗留下的印痕：印～。</li>    <li>前人遗留下的事物：古～。实～。</li>    <li>追寻踪迹：“汉求将军急，～且至臣家”。</li>    <li>据实迹考知：“～汉功臣，亦皆割符世爵”。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>迹</strong></p><p>跡、蹟 <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[footmark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵</span>主父令工施钩梯而缘 <span class="diczx2">播吾</span>(山名)，刻疏人迹其上。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说左上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:浪迹天涯;人迹(人的足迹);人迹罕至;迹蹈(重复走过的路);迹状(行迹);迹响(踪迹和声响)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 留下的印子 <span class="encs">[mark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蟆入草间，蹑迹披求。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:痕迹;血迹;笔迹;墨迹</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 前人留下的事物(主要指建筑或器物) <span class="encs">[remains]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故平公之迹不可明也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·难一》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:古迹;胜迹;古城墙的遗迹;史迹;迹相(表露出来的不很显著的情况，可借以推断过去或将来)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“绩”</span>。功劳 <span class="encs">[achievement;merit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如是则其迹长矣。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>迹</strong> <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 追踪;追寻 <span class="encs">[shadow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">逻者见之，知非其国人也，迹其所憩执之。——</span><span class="diczx2">岳珂</span><span class="smcs">《桯史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:迹察(寻迹察访);迹盗(跟踪查捕盗贼);迹访(寻访)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 遵循 <span class="encs">[follow]</span>。如:迹附(追随)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/迹象">迹象</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=辵" target="_blank">辵</a>】
                              迹·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8FF9.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2848F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒏</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>資昔切，音積。【說文】步處也。【廣韻】足跡也。【左傳·宣十二年】遷大國之迹于鄭。【前漢·揚雄傳】擬足而投迹。【淮南子·說山訓】足蹍地而爲迹。</p><p>又凡功業可見者曰迹。【書·武成】太王肇基王迹。【前漢·王褒傳】索人求士者，必樹霸迹。</p><p>又凡前人所遺留者曰迹。【莊子·天運篇】《六經》先王之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>迹也。</p><p>又凡有所遵循亦曰迹。【書·蔡仲之命】爾乃邁迹自身。【註】仲無所因，故曰邁迹。【前漢·平當傳】深迹其道，而務修其本。【註】謂求其踪迹。</p><p>又凡有形可見者皆曰迹。【淮南子·說山訓】循迹者，非能生迹者也。【唐書·魏徵傳】豈有君臣同心事形迹者。</p><p>又循實而考之亦曰迹。【前漢·功臣表】迹漢功臣。</p><p>又【杜欽傳】將以求天心，迹得失也。</p><p>又風迹，風化之迹也。【後漢·朱浮傳】頗欲厲風迹。</p><p>又迹射，尋迹而射也。《前漢·王尊傳》將迹射士千人。</p><p>又迹人，官名。【周禮·地官·迹人註】迹之言跡。知禽獸處也。</p><p>又與跡同。【文中子·問易篇】心跡之判久矣。</p><p>又與蹟通。【詩·小雅】念彼不蹟。【註】謂不循故道也。</p><p>又叶卽略切，音爵。【陸機·演連珠】火壯則煙微，性充則情約。是以殷墟有感物之悲，周京無佇立之迹。　【集韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2858A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨖊</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span>。</p><p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8FF9.svg">                          <p>步處也。从辵亦聲。蹟，或从足責。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span>，籒文迹从朿。資昔切〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2848F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒏</span>，古文迹。</p>                          </div>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8FF9_6789.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(迹)</span><span class="ywyy">步處也。</span><span class="swdz">莊子云。夫迹、履之所出。而迹豈履也。</span><span class="ywyy">从辵。亦聲。</span><span class="swdz">迹本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span>。朿聲。故音在十六部。小篆改爲亦聲。則當入五部。而非本部之形聲矣。李陽冰云。李丞相持朿作亦。謂此字也。資昔切。古音在十六部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8FF9_6790.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(蹟)</span><span class="ywyy">或从足責。</span><span class="swdz">責亦朿聲也。小雅。念彼不蹟。毛傳。不蹟、不循道也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8FF9_6791.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文迹。从朿。</span><span class="swdz">釋獸。鹿其跡速。釋文。本又作？。素卜反。引字林鹿跡也。按速正<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span>字之誤。周時古本云其<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span>。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span>之名不嫌專繫鹿也。廣雅。躔？解亢跡也。卽爾雅麋跡躔、鹿跡<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/284AA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨒪</span>、麕跡解、兔跡迒也。曹憲？音匹迹反。集韵云。迹或作？。然則字林從鹿速聲、素卜反之字。紕繆實甚。或以竄入爾雅。又或以羼入鹿部麌麛二字之閒。其誤可不辯自明矣。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
