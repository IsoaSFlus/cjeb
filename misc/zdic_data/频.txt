						<p>● <strong>频</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">頻</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&iacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>屡次，连次：～繁。～仍。～数（<span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span>）（次数多而接连）。～率（<span class="dicpy">lǜ</span>）。～谱。捷报～传。</li>
	<li>危急：&ldquo;国步斯～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>并列：&ldquo;百嘉备舍，群神～行&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/颦">颦</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>频</strong></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">频</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>频</strong></p>
<p>頻 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从步，从页。页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>):人头。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>认为步是&ldquo;涉&rdquo;的省略。人将要渡河，见水深，皱眉而止。本义:皱眉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[knit the brows]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已频顣曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:频蹙(皱眉蹙额，忧愁不乐的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 接近;临近 <span class="encs">[near]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百姓流亡，频于涂炭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">潘岳</span>文</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 并列 <span class="encs">[stand side by side]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:频行(并行)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>频</strong></p>
<p>頻 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 重复，连续 <span class="encs">[continued]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">频，连也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">频年服役。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;杨终传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:频日(连续多日);频世(连续两代);频岁(连年)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 危急;紧急 <span class="encs">[urgent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于科有哀，国步斯频。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;桑柔》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>频</strong></p>
<p>頻 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 在一定时间(常指1秒种)或范围内事物重复出现的次数 <span class="encs">[frequency]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:音频;频带</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假借为&ldquo;滨&rdquo;。水边 <span class="encs">[waterfront;waterside]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/983B.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29524.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩔤</span>【唐韻】符眞切【集韻】【韻會】毗賔切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音顰。【玉篇】急也。【廣雅】比也。【詩&middot;大雅】國步斯頻。【傳】頻，急也。【箋】頻猶比也。國家之政，行此禍害比比然。</p>
<p>又姓。【風俗通】漢有酒泉太守頻暢。</p>
<p>又【說文】水厓。【徐鉉曰】今俗別作水濵，非是。【詩&middot;大雅】池之竭矣，不云自頻。【傳】頻，厓也。【箋】頻，當作濵。</p>
<p>又與顰同。【易&middot;復卦】頻復厲无咎。【註】謂頻蹙之貌。</p>
<p>又果名。【前漢&middot;司馬相如&middot;上林賦】仁頻，幷閭。【註】仁頻，賔榔也。頻或作賔。</p>
