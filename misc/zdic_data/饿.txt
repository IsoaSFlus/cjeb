                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饿</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>饿</strong></p>
<p><img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><strong>（<span class="diczx3">餓</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄜˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>肚子空，想吃东西，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">饱</span>&rdquo;相对：我不～，你吃吧！饥～。～殍（饿死的人）。</li>
	<li>使受饿：注意别～坏了。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hungry; greedy for; hunger</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hungrig sein, Hunger haben, hungern  (Adj, Ess)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> avoir faim,être affamé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>饿</strong></p>
<p>餓 <span class="dicpy">&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，我声。本义:饥之甚。按:在古代，&ldquo;饥&rdquo;与&ldquo;饿&rdquo;存在着程度上的差别。&ldquo;饥&rdquo;指一般的肚子饿;&ldquo;饿&rdquo;是严重的饥饿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hungry]</span>。与&ldquo;饱&rdquo;相对</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饿，饥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饿，无食久馁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《六书故》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饿，甚于饥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有死者曰大饥，无死者曰大饿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传》</span>疏引</span><span class="diczx2">徐邈</span>说</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无一月饿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家有常业，虽饥不饿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;饰邪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饿隶(饥饿的人;形容干瘠的样子);饿损(饿坏);饿杀鬼(方言。贪吃的人);饿病(饥饿致病)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 使受饿;感到饥饿 <span class="encs">[starve;hunger]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饿其体肤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">伯夷</span>、 <span class="diczx2">叔齐</span>饿于 <span class="diczx2">首阳</span>之下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;季氏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孩子饿了;别饿着小猪</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 贫困;缺少 <span class="encs">[poor]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:水田饿水;干田饿水;饿乡(缺吃的乡里)</span></p>
