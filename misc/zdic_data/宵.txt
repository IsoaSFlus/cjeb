data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，肖声。从<span class="yhcs">“门”</span>表夜间昏暗。本义:夜晚)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[night]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宵，夜也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宵中星。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禁宵行者。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司寤氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">甘暝太宵之长。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·精神》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“长夜之中也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宵扈宵。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释鸟》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">樊</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“夜为农驱兽者也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昼尔于茅，宵尔索宵。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晓战随金鼓，宵眠抱玉鞍。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《塞下曲》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今宵酒醒何处。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:宵烟(指夜晚的烟雾);宵宿(住宿;住宿的地方);宵夕(晚上);宵中(夜里，晚上);宵分(半夜，深夜);宵行(夜间出行);宵征(宵行，夜行);宵朗(夜空晴朗)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 前半夜<span class="encs">[the first half of the night(from night-fall to midnight)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禁宵行者，夜游者。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·秋官》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“绡”</span>。丝织品的一种 <span class="encs">[silk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">姆纅笄宵衣。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:宵衣(黑色的丝服。古代妇女助祭时所穿。宵，通<span class="yhcs">“绡”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 小吃、便餐、快餐 <span class="encs">[snack]</span>。如:夜宵</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>宵</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“小”</span>。小 <span class="encs">[small;bad]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宵雅肆三。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“宵之言小也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宵人之离外刑者。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·列御寇》</span>。注:不由明坦之途者，谓之宵人。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋桐好逸，毋迩宵人。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·武五子传》</span>。</span><span
