                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>涉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄜˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>步行过水：～渡。～江。跋～。</li>	<li>到，经历：～世（经历世事）。～历。～险。～足。</li>	<li>牵连，关连：～及。～嫌。～外（涉及与外国关系的）。交～。牵～。～猎。</li>	<li>动，着（<span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;</span>）：～笔。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涉</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从水步。甲骨文字形。中间是水，两边两只脚，象涉水之形。本义:趟水过河)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wade;ford]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涉，徒行濿水也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冯河，徒涉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:&ldquo;无舟而渡水曰徒涉。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">繇膝以上为涉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">过度谓之涉</span><span class="diczx2">济</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言七》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">送子涉淇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">澭水暴益，荆人弗知，循表而夜涉，溺死者千有余人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涉水登山(涉渡溪水并攀登山岭);涉浅(徒步趟过浅水);涉厉(涉水。厉，连衣涉水);跋山涉水</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指渡水 <span class="encs">[cross (a river)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人有涉江者，其剑自舟中坠于水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>师己亥涉河也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《察传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涉人(船夫);涉江(渡河，过河)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 经历，经过 <span class="encs">[experience; go through]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">杭</span>有卖果者，善藏柑，涉寒暑不溃。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后可以刚健强力，涉险而不伤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涉难(经历艰难);涉旬(经过十天);涉手(经手);涉月(经月);涉朔(经月);涉阅(经历)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 牵涉，涉及，关连 <span class="encs">[involve]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不相参涉，皆悉赡举。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋书&middot;刘穆之传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涉疑(有跟某件事情发生牵连的嫌疑);涉虚(假话;空话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 跋涉 <span class="encs">[trudge]</span></p>
