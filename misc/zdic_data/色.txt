                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>色</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄜˋ</span></p><ol><li>由物体发射、反射的光通过视觉而产生的印象：颜～。～彩。～相（<span class="dicpy">xiàng </span>）。～调（<span class="dicpy">diào </span>）。</li><li>脸上表现出的神气、样子：脸～。气～。～厉内荏。</li><li>情景，景象：行～匆匆。景～宜人。</li><li>种类：各～用品。</li><li>品质，质量：音～。成～。足～纹银。</li><li>妇女美貌：姿～。～艺。</li><li>情欲：～情。好（<span class="dicpy">hào</span>）～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">色</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>色</strong> <span class="dicpy">sè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文中象一个人驮另一个人，仰承其脸色。本义:脸色)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[look;countenance;expression]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">色，颜气也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五气五色。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·疾医》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其色必寿。——<span class="smcs">《素问·三部九侯论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">形体色理以目异。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">载色载笑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·泮水》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">太后之色少解。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>视公子色终不变，乃谢客就车。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我言若，王色不许我。——<span class="smcs">《史记·商君列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故以声声怖之。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:面色(脸上的气色);色喜(脸上显出欢喜之色);色笑(开颜欢笑);色沮(脸色露出沮丧的样子);色变振恐(恐惧得脸上变色);色庄(容貌庄重严肃);色智(表现在容色上的才智);喜形于色</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 颜色 <span class="encs">[color]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五色令人目盲。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">满面尘灰烟火色，两鬓苍苍十指黑。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未花时采，则根色鲜泽。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:色衣(色服。除黑白二色以外的各种艳色衣服);色丝(彩色绸缎);色彩(彩色绸缎);古色古香</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姿色，容颜(多就女子而言);美色 <span class="encs">[good looks;woman’s looks;beauty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡人有疾，寡人好色。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">年长色衰，委身为贾人妇。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:绝色佳人;声色;色阵(指冶艳女子聚集之所);国色(一国内容貌最美的女子)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 色欲，好色 <span class="encs">[lewdness;erotic]</span>。如:色荒(过度沉迷于女色);色胆包天(好色之人，胆大妄为，不顾一切);色授(用神色传递内心的感情。多指男女之间的情爱);色长(妓院的男老板;教坊司乐工的头目);色中饿鬼(比喻极其贪色的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 佛家语。指一切物质的存在 <span
