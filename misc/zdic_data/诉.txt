                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>诉</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">訴</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">s&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>叙述，倾吐：告～。～苦。～愿。～衷情。倾～。</li>
	<li>控告：～讼。控～。上～。申～。败～。撤～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> accuse; sue; inform; narrate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> mitteilen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> raconter,accuser,recourir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>诉</strong></p>
<p>訴、愬 <span class="dicpy">s&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:告状;控告)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[accuse;charge;complain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诉，告也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">负</span><span class="diczx2">孝公</span>之周愬天子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;昭公三十一年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必东愬于</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即连楮为巨幅，广二丈，大书一&ldquo;屈&rdquo;字，以两竿夹揭之，走诉行御史台。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:起诉;上诉;公诉;诉呈(诉状);诉权(起诉和诉原的权利);诉辩(申诉并辩白);诉牒(诉状);诉告(上诉;告状)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 告诉，说给人听 <span class="encs">[tell;relate;inform]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">正遇</span><span class="diczx2">延</span><span class="diczx2">平</span>二人，诉说前事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弦弦掩抑声声思，似诉平生不得志。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾与并肩携长，低低切切，何事不语?何情不诉?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诉怨(对人诉说怨愤之情);诉长道短(原原本本地向人诉说);诉事(陈诉事情);诉屈(申诉冤屈);诉言(诉说)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 求，求助 <span class="encs">[resort to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:诉诸武力</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 有事实根据地说人坏话。通&ldquo;愬 <span class="encs">[slander]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">取货于</span><span class="diczx2">宣伯</span>而诉公于 <span class="diczx2">晋侯</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十六年》</span></p>
