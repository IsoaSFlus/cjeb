other]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">八，别也。象分别相背之形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“今 <span class="diczx2">江</span>、 <span class="diczx2">轲</span>，被八创。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">八世。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·崔实传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓三王五帝也。”</span></span></p><p><span
