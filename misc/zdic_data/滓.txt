class="yhcs">“黑也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">脉不粘肤，食不留滓。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘峻</span><span class="smcs">《送橘启》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">滓方:以集诸滓，制如涤方，受五升。——</span><span i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 污浊 <span class="encs">[dirty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皦然泥而不滓者。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:滓敝(肮脏破烂);滓淤(污泥;污浊的东西);滓尘(污浊的尘世);滓窳(污浊)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 低贱 <span
