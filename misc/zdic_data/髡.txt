                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>髡</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>古代剃去男子头发的一种刑罚：～首（剃去头发，光头）。～钳（剃去头发，并用铁圈束颈）。</li><li>古代指和尚。</li><li>古代称修剪树枝。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>髡</strong></p><p>髨、髠 <span class="dicpy">kūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声 。( <span class="dicpy">kūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kūn.mp3"></a></span> )从髟( <span class="dicpy">biāo</span>)，与毛发有关，兀( <span class="dicpy">wù</span>)声。本义:古代一种剃去头发的刑罚)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ancient punishment of shaving off the hair of a criminal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">髡，剃发也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">髡者，使守积。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·掌戮》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辩通凿颠者髡。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">接舆髡兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·屈原·涉江》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃髡钳</span><span class="diczx2">季布</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·季布栾布列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:髡刖(古代刑罚，去发称髡，断足称刖);髡钳(古代刑罚，去发为髡，用铁束颈为钳);髡发(剃去头发);髡首(剃光头发，光头);髡人(被剃去头发的罪人);髡头(光头。同髡首)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 剪去树枝 <span class="encs">[lop off tree branches]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十年以后，髡一树，得一载，岁髡二百树，五年一周。——<span class="smcs">《齐民要术》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>髡</strong> <span class="dicpy">kūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>僧尼 <span class="encs">[monk]</span>。如:髡缁(道士，代指道教);髡人(僧侣，僧人);髡首(指僧尼)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
