                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>握</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄛˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>手指弯曲合拢，执持：～手。～拳。～别。把～。掌～。～力。～笔。～管。～瑜（喻怀有美好的才德）。</li>
	<li>量词，指一把大小或分量：一～粗。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> grasp, hold fast, take by hand</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> etwas in die Hand nehmen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> saisir,tenir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">握</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>握</strong> <span class="dicpy">w&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，屋声。本义:攥在手里，执持)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grasp;grip;hold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">握，搤持也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">握，持也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长尺有握。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">私握臣手。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;怀少》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀瑾握瑜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;怀沙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">手握刀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:握君(如意的别称);握篆(手握官印);握算(执算筹以计数。亦指谋划);握镜(执持明镜);握节(持守符节。不辱君命);握炭流汤(握炽炭，蹈沸汤。不畏危难，敢于用命)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 屈指成拳 <span class="encs">[fist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">终日握而手不掜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;庚桑楚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:握固(屈指成拳);握拳透爪(形容愤怒到极点)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 掌握 <span class="encs">[grasp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">握踣家五十室。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;戒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谨执其柄而固握之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;主道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:握枢(掌握中枢之权);握机(掌握天下的权柄);握雾拿云(驾驭云雾);握柄(掌权);握要(掌握要领);握守(把手)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 控制 <span class="encs">[control]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">握乱以治天下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《原化》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用同&ldquo;捂( <span class="dicpy">wǔ</span>&rdquo;)。遮盖 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">黛玉</span>]</span> 说着，便两手握起脸来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>握</strong> <span class="dicpy">w&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一把的容量</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贻我握椒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;陈风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一拳的长度</p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>握</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄨㄛˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>w&ograve; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/6291.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用手掌執持或抓緊。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>握</b>筆」、「<b>握</b>住」、「緊<b>握</b>雙手」。</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝宋&middot;劉義慶《世說新語&middot;言語》：「庾法暢造庾太尉，<b>握</b>麈尾至佳。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">掌管、控制。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「掌<b>握</b>」、「把<b>握</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;揚雄&middot;解嘲》：「旦<b>握</b>權則為卿相，夕失勢則為匹夫。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;皮日休〈十原系述&middot;原化〉：「率邪以禦眾，<b>握</b>亂以治天下。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞。計算一手抓滿的數量的單位。相當於「把」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「一<b>握</b>沙子」。</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;徐弘祖《徐霞客遊記&middot;卷一下&middot;遊雁宕山日記》：「寺後覓方竹數<b>握</b>，細如枝。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              握·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/63E1.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26946.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦥆</span>【唐韻】烏角切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】乙角切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音渥。【說文】搤持也。陸佃云：持五指也，在外爲持，在內爲握。【詩&middot;小雅】握粟出卜。【儀禮&middot;鄉射禮】箭籌長尺有握。【註】握。本所持處也。【又】大夫之矢，則兼束之，以茅上握焉。【註】握，謂中央也。【禮&middot;王制】宗廟之牛，角握。【前漢&middot;律歷志】算法，用竹徑一分，長六寸，二百七十一枚，而成六觚，爲一握。</p>
<p>又【集韻】烏谷切，音屋。小貌。【易&middot;萃卦】若號一握爲笑，鄭氏讀。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】委瑣握<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A60F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪘏</span>，拘文牽俗。【註】握<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A60F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪘏</span>，局陿也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】握，具也。【疏】握持辦具也。【鄭註】握卽屋字。</p>
<p>又與幄通。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;巾車】翟車貝面組總，有握。【註】有握，則無蓋矣。如今輧車是也。【釋文】握音屋，又烏學反。</p>
<p>又於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>切，音漚。喪用束手者。或作䌂。</p>
