<p>● <strong>店</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">di&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>店</strong> <span class="dicpy">di&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，占声。从&ldquo;广&rdquo;，表示与房屋有关。本义:栈房)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[warehouse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肆，所以陈货鬻之物也;店，所以置货鬻之物也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔豹</span><span class="smcs">《古今注》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 旅舍;客店 <span class="encs">[inn]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:投店宿歇;寡妇店;店都知(对店小二的尊称);店舍(可供旅客住宿且兼售货物的客店);店家(旅舍、旅馆;又指酒馆、店铺的老板或伙计);店账(住旅店应付的费用)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 商店;铺子 <span class="encs">[shop]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:店头(店前路边货摊聚集处);店号(商店的名称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 都邑集镇名 <span class="encs">[fair]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:店头(集市);驻马店;长辛店</span></p>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/576B_1002.svg" /><span class="swbf">(坫)</span><span class="ywyy">屛也。</span><span class="swdz">陳氏禮書曰：坫之別凡有四。記曰：反坫出尊。論語曰：邦君爲兩君之好有反坫。此反爵之坫也。記曰：崇坫康圭，此奠玉之坫也。記又曰：士於坫一，此庋食之坫也。士冠禮：爵弁、皮弁、緇布冠各一匴，執以待於西坫南。大射，將射，工遷於下。東坫之東南，士喪禮：牀<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/7B2B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>笫</span>夷衾，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29739.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩜹</span>於西坫南。旣夕禮，設棜於東堂下南順。齊於坫，此堂隅之坫也。爾雅曰：垝謂之坫。郭云：坫，㙐也。在堂隅，按㙐本作墆，高皃也。以土爲之。高可屛蔽。故許云屛也。其字俗作店。崔豹曰：店，置也。所以置貨鬻物也。</span><span class="ywyy">从土，占聲。</span><span class="swdz">都念切。七部。</span></p>                          </div>
