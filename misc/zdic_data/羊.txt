                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>羊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄤˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>哺乳动物，反刍类，一般头上有一对角，品种很多：绵～。黄～。羚～。～羔。～毫。～肠线。～肠小道。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>羊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄤˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/祥">祥</a>&rdquo;，吉祥。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">羊</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>羊</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，像羊头形。&ldquo;羊&rdquo;是汉字的一个部首。本义:一种哺乳动物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sheep]</span>。反刍类，以食草为生。一般头上有一对角，有许多品种</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羊，羊兽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>曰:&ldquo;半羊之字以形举也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羊曰柔毛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羊曰少牢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;少牢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食麦与羊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兑为羊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杖汉节牧羊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:羊群里跑出骆驼来(喻指平常的人忽然变成了出类拔萃的人物了);羊群蚁阵(形容众多而聚集);羊酒花红(丰厚的赏赐和显赫的荣耀);羊车过市(比喻男子才美绝伦，引人羡慕注目);羊耳风(羊角风);羊脏(调侃语。指好胃口);羊羔(酒名;羊羔酒，一种汾州产的糯米制白酒);羊儿风(羊角风)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 十二生肖之一，与十二地支的未相配 <span class="encs">[sheep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丑、未，亦土也。丑禽牛，未禽羊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>羊</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 吉利。后作&ldquo;祥&rdquo; <span class="encs">[lucky]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">臣愿王与下吏羊计某言而竺(笃)虑之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《马王堆汉墓帛书》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7F8A.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】與章切【集韻】【韻會】余章切【正韻】移章切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音陽。【說文】羊，祥也。从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦫳</span>，象頭角尾之形。孔子曰：牛羊之字，以形舉也。【玉篇】豕屬也。【易&middot;說卦】兌爲羊。【註】其質好剛鹵。【詩&middot;召南】羔羊之皮。【傳】小曰羔，大曰羊。【禮&middot;曲禮】羊曰柔毛。【月令】食麥與羊。【註】羊，火畜也。時尚寒，食之以安性也。</p><p>又麢羊。【爾雅&middot;釋獸】麢，大羊。【註】似羊而大，角圓銳，好在山崖閒。</p><p>又鳥名。【家語】齊有一足之鳥，飛集于公朝。齊侯使使問孔子，孔子曰：此鳥名商羊，水祥也。</p><p>又姓。【左傳&middot;閔二年】羊舌大夫。【註】羊舌，氏也。【公羊傳疏】子夏傳與公羊高。【史記&middot;梁孝王世家】齊人羊勝。</p><p>又官名。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;羊人註】羊屬南方火，司馬火官，故在此。</p><p>又白羊，匈奴國名。【史記&middot;匈奴傳】幷樓煩，白羊，河南。</p><p>又【前漢&middot;禮樂志】雙飛常羊。【註】猶逍遙也。【屈原&middot;離騷】聊逍遙以相羊。【註】逍遙，相羊，皆遊也。</p>
