                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">摭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>摭</strong></p>
<p>拓 <span class="dicpy">zh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，庶声。字又作&ldquo;拓&rdquo;，从手，石声。本义:有选择的拾取)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pick up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拓，拾也。</span><span class="diczx2">陈</span>、 <span class="diczx2">宋</span>语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摭，取也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有顺而摭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃摭于鱼腊俎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;有司彻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹拾遗于涂，摭弃于野。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;逢遇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摭紫贝，搏耆龟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 选取，摘取 <span class="encs">[select;choose;pluck;pick]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摭我华而不食我实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法言&middot;问明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">往往摭<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span>文而反之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:摭华(采摘其文章辞藻);摭裂(采摘细碎);摭实(摘取事实;据实)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挑剔;指摘 <span class="encs">[censure]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:摭赖(挑剔诬赖);摭罗(挑剔、罗织)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 搜集 <span class="encs">[collect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摭其阴事以奏之，</span><span class="diczx2">太祖</span>遂疑之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧五代史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:摭采(搜集采用)</span></p>
