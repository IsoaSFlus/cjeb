                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>依</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>靠，仗赖：～靠。～傍（➊依靠；➋摹仿，多指艺术、学问）。～恋。～偎。～存。～附。归～。</li>
	<li>按照：～照。～旧。～据。～次。</li>
	<li>顺从，答应：～从。～顺。～允。</li>
	<li>亲密的样子：&ldquo;有～其士&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">依</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>依</strong> <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，衣声。甲骨文字形。人在衣中。本义:靠着)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lean on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">依，倚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">依，恃也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白日依山尽，黄河入海流。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王之涣</span><span class="smcs">《登鹳雀楼》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">依柱而笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;刺客列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">空游无所依。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">极贫无所依。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:依山之榭(靠山建造的房屋);依栖(依傍栖息);依山傍水;依着电线杆;偎依(亲密地靠着);依倚(倚靠;依傍);依门卖笑(以色媚人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 倚仗，仗恃，仰赖 <span class="encs">[rely on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无依势作威，无依法以削。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;君陈》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:依仰(依赖仰仗);依阻(凭借;仗恃);依负(倚恃;依附);依乘(凭借);依毗(倚仗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 依靠;托身 <span class="encs">[depend on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必依水草而背众树。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;行军》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勤勤嘱四邻，幸愿相依傍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">梅尧臣</span><span class="smcs">《汝ń贫女诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今去依傍外祖母及舅氏姊妹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:生死相依;无依无靠;唇齿相依;依傍门墙(依靠师父);依仰(依靠;依赖);依贴(依恋体贴);依栖(依靠他人并寄居);依怙(依靠;依赖);依重(依靠;倚重);依荷(依靠);依接(依靠连接);依诉(依靠诉说);依缘(依靠，凭借)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 同意;允许 <span class="encs">[comply with;yield to;agree;consent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有一事肯依，</span><span class="diczx2">窦娥</span>便死而无怨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《元曲选&middot;关汉卿&middot;窦娥冤剧三》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:依心像意(称心如意);依得(依允，依从);依心(称心);依信(信从;依赖);依许(准许)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 服从;听从 <span class="encs">[obey]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:依头顺脑(完全顺从;俯首贴耳);依遵(服从);依随(依顺;听从);依阿(曲从附顺);依阿取容(依附随以取悦于人);依头顺尾(对上下各方都听从)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 原谅，宽恕 <span class="encs">[forgive]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:你要是把这些资料弄丢了，我可不依你</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 遵照，根据 <span class="encs">[in the light of;in accordance with;according too;as]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃拜</span><span class="diczx2">宪</span>车骑将军，金印紫绶，官属依司空。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;窦融传》</span></p>
