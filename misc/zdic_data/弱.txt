                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弱</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>弱</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ruò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ruò.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄨㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>力气小，势力小，与“<span class="diczx3">强</span>”相对：～小。柔～。脆～。薄～。～不禁风。柔心～骨（形容人心地温柔，性格和顺）。</li><li>年纪小：～冠（泛指男子二十岁左右的年纪）。</li><li>不够，差点儿，与“<span class="diczx3">强</span>”相对：三分之一～。</li><li>差，不如：～于。～点。</li><li>丧失（指人死）：又～了一个。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> weak; fragile, delicate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schäche  (S)​,schwach  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faible,fragile,inférieur,un peu moins</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>弱</strong> <span class="dicpy">ruò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ruò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，两把弯曲的弓，<span class="yhcs">“彡”</span>( <span class="dicpy">shān</span>)象毛羽之形，合起来表示柔弱。本义:气力小;势力差)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。与<span class="yhcs">“强”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[weak;feeble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弱，桡也。上象桡曲，彡象毛氂桡弱也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">栋桡，本末弱也。——<span class="smcs">《易·大过》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颉遇王子弱焉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">志弱而事强。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辅隙者则国必弱。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·谋攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众散为弱。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有土者不可以言贫，有民者不可以言弱。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·错法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两争曰弱。——<span class="smcs">《周书·武顺》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然</span><span class="diczx2">操</span>能克 <span class="diczx2">绍</span>，以弱为强者，非惟天时，抑亦人谋也。—— <span class="diczx2">陈寿</span><span class="smcs">《隆中对》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弱者道之用。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弱湍驰文舫，闲谷矫鸣鸥。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《游斜川》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:弱植(软弱无能，无法扶植);弱道(道家语。柔弱之道);弱人(势弱之人);弱湍(舒缓的流水);弱力(力量单薄;能力不强的人);弱才(才能平庸低下);弱孤(力孤力弱);弱门(胆小之人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 纤柔;软弱 <span class="encs">[delicate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苗其弱也欲孤。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·辨士》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弱柳青槐拂地垂。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">卢照邻</span><span class="smcs">《长安古意》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:弱约(柔弱美好);弱骨(骨柔软;伶仃瘦骨);弱劣(软弱无力);弱愿(柔顺善良);弱蒂(细弱的叶柄或花蒂);弱腕(微细的手腕);弱藻(柔弱的水草)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 虚弱;瘦弱 <span class="encs">[infirm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小人弱息，当得一子。——<span class="smcs">《南史·周盘龙传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你妹妹远路才来，身子又弱。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:弱甲(老弱之兵);弱疾(身体瘦弱的疾病);弱息(柔弱的女子)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 年幼，年少 <span class="encs">[young]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝心之固，固不可彻，曾不若孀妻弱子。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>
