                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>途</strong> <span class="dicpy">tú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，余声。本义:道路)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[way;road;rout]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">途，道也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徐亦训道。字古借涂，后变作途。又作塗。——</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">堂途谓之陈。又，路，旅途也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释宫》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当途梧邱。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释邱》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而不利说途也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">经途九轨。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长揖当途人。——</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《游仙诗》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“当仕路也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天明登前途。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仁义之途。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《朱文昌校昌黎先生集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">负者歌于途。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无速其途。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">途中两狼，缀行甚远。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:途轨(路途);途辙(路上之车迹);途毙(因贫病冻饿而死在道路上);途说(道途之说);途陌(道路);途水(取道水路);通途;沿途;归途;长途跋涉</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 生涯，经历。仕途 <span class="encs">[career]</span>。如:宦途;前途远大;途穷(喻走投无路或处境困窘)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 职位 <span class="encs">[position]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时辈多得途，亲朋屡相敕。——</span><span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《寄吴士矩端公五十韵》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 途径;路子 <span class="encs">[way;method]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">古之立国家者，开本末之途，通有无之用。——<span class="smcs">《盐铁论·本议》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span>
