                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">渠</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>渠</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>水道，特指人工开的河道或水沟：～道（亦指途径，门路）。沟～。</li>
	<li>大：～帅。～魁（首领）。</li>
	<li>方言，他：～侬（他，他们）。～辈。</li>
	<li>车辋，古代车轮的外圈。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>渠</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/讵">讵</a>&rdquo;。相当于&ldquo;岂&rdquo;、&ldquo;哪里&rdquo;。&ldquo;掾部～有其人乎？&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">渠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>渠</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，榘( <span class="dicpy">q&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> )省声。本义:水停积处，也指人工开凿的水道)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[canal;channel;ditch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">渠，水所居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">渠，坑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">门闾沟渠必步。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。疏:&ldquo;沟也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:干渠(从水源引水的渠道);支渠;毛渠;渠堑(沟渠);灌溉渠;渠田(水田);渠长(古官名。掌管河川的官;盗贼的首领);渠水(引水的沟;沟渠中的水)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 濠沟 <span class="encs">[ditch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">景霍</span>以为城，而 <span class="diczx2">汾</span>、 <span class="diczx2">河</span>、 <span class="diczx2">涑</span>、 <span class="diczx2">浍</span>以为渠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代指车轮的外圈 <span class="encs">[rim]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:渠冲(古代攻城的大车);渠碗(用车渠壳作的碗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 盾 <span class="encs">[shield]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:渠幨(古时用来御矢的甲名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Qu river]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 渠江，在四川省境内</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 渠水，在湖南省靖县境内。一名渠河</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Qu prefecture]</span>。治所在今四川省渠县</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上" target="_blank">巳集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上&amp;bs=水" target="_blank">水</a>】
                              渠·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6E20.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】彊魚切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】求於切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蕖。【說文】水所居也。【禮&middot;曲禮】溝渠必步。</p>
<p>又水名。【前漢&middot;地理志】渠水首受江，北至射陽入湖。</p>
<p>又渠渠，勤也。【詩&middot;秦風】夏屋渠渠。【箋】渠渠，猶勤勤也。【註】深廣貌。</p>
<p>又大也。【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】誅其渠帥。</p>
<p>又樂章名。【魯語】金奏肆夏樊遏渠。【註】納夏一名渠。</p>
<p>又漸也。【淮南子&middot;汜論訓】渠幨以守。【註】渠，漸也。</p>
<p>又軒渠，笑貌。【後漢&middot;方技傳】軒渠笑自若。</p>
<p>又俗謂他人爲渠儂。</p>
<p>又水名。【水經注】水出鄭縣南巴嶺，流逕宕渠縣。謂之宕渠水。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】謂車輞爲渠。【山海經】韓流麟身渠股。【註】渠，車輞，言跰腳也。</p>
<p>又犀渠，甲名。</p>
<p>又楯也。【吳語】文犀之渠十行。</p>
<p>又芋渠。【馬融&middot;廣成頌】蘘荷芋渠。</p>
<p>又夫渠，荷名。別作蕖。</p>
<p>又姓。【史記&middot;年表】漢渠復縈。</p>
<p>又【集韻】其據切，音遽。與詎同。【字林】未知詞也。【史記&middot;張儀傳】且蘇君在儀，寧渠能乎。</p>
<p>又與遽通。【史記&middot;陸賈傳】使我居中國，何渠不若漢。</p>
<p>又【正韻】臼許切，音巨。義同。</p>
