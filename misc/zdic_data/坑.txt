data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，亢声。本义:地面上凹下去的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hollow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坑，堑也，壑也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或被坑水冲淹。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:刨个坑;挖坑;坑阱(泛指深坑);坑坎(指地面高低不平;洼地;坑穴)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 矿穴，矿场 <span class="encs">[pit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其天下自五岭以北，见捋银坑，并宜禁断。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·食货志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:坑户(唐宋以来采掘金属矿的在籍专业户);坑冶(金属矿的开采与冶炼);坑探(开挖坑道探明矿藏的情况)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 〈方〉∶大小便用的粪坑或缸 <span class="encs">[pit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其天下自五岭以北，见捋银坑，并宜禁断。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·食货志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:茅坑(厕所里的粪坑)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 沟壑 <span class="encs">[gully]</span>。如:坑谷(沟壑山谷);坑堑(地下的陷沟);坑壕(壕沟);坑岸(犹坑堑，沟壑)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>坑</strong> <span class="dicpy">kēng</span> <span up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则被你坑杀人燕侣莺俦。——<span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:坑闪(坑害抛弃);坑陷(坑害;陷害);坑蒙(坑害蒙骗)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 活埋 <span class="encs">[bury alive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>军夜击坑 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>卒二十余人。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:坑降(活埋已经投降的兵将);坑杀(活埋);坑戮(坑杀;活埋);焚书坑儒</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 欺骗 <span class="encs">[cheat]</span>。如:奸商净坑人</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 损人 <span
