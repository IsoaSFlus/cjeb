                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">煽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>煽</strong></p><p>傓 <span class="dicpy">shān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，扇声。本义:火炽盛) 同本义<span class="encs">[flaming]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">傓，炽盛也。从人，扇声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">艳妻煽方处。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·十月之交》</span>。按，傓、煽皆后出字，皆因诗辞而制也。鲁诗:阎妻扇方处，是本字。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">火井沉荧于幽泉，高焰飞煽于天垂。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《蜀都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:煽炽(炽盛);煽虐(肆虐)。引申为气势炽盛</p><p><span class="diczx1">然内宠方煽，太子终以忧死。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:煽行(炽盛，流行)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>煽</strong> <span class="dicpy">shān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 扇火使炽盛 <span class="encs">[blow the fire]</span>。如:煽引(煽动引诱)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 鼓动;蛊惑 <span class="encs">[instigate]</span>。如:煽弄(煽惑玩弄);煽骗(吹嘘诈骗)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/煽动">煽动</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/煽风点火">煽风点火</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/煽惑">煽惑</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/煽情">煽情</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/煽诱">煽诱</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>煽</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄕㄢ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>shān <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5026.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">火焰炽盛。</span><span class="gc_yy">《广韵·平声·仙韵》：「<b>煽</b>，火盛也。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用扇子搧火，使炽盛。</span><span class="gc_yy">《水浒传·第二四回》：「王婆只做不看见，只顾在茶局里<b>煽</b>风炉子，不出来问茶。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">以谗言或手段鼓动，挑拨人事。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「煽动」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《晋书·卷五九·汝南王亮等传·序》：「西晋之政乱朝危，虽由时主，然而<b>煽</b>其风，速其祸者，咎在八王。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
