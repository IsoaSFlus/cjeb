                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>摧</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，崔声。本义:折断)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[break]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先祖于摧。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·云汉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">商旅不行，樯倾楫摧。——</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:摧折(折断毁坏);摧剥(摧折剥落);摧烧(折断而投于火中烧毁)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 摧毁，破坏，毁坏 <span class="encs">[destroy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">列缺霹雳，上峦崩摧。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:摧坚(攻破敌人的坚锐部队);摧坚斩敌(破阵杀敌);摧破(摧陷攻破);摧逼(摧残)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 挫败 <span class="encs">[defeat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">折锐摧矜。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·忧苦》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“挫也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">素疾大户兼并，力摧豪强，抚贫弱。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:摧屈受挫而屈服;摧辱(挫折羞辱);摧败(击破;打败);摧锋(挫败敌军的锐气);摧锋陷阵(破敌深入)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 倒塌;崩裂 <span class="encs">[collapse]</span>。如:地崩山摧壮士死;天摧地塌;摧塌(倒塌)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 悲痛，哀伤 <span class="encs">[grieve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阿母大悲摧。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:摧心(痛心。非常悲痛);摧怆(伤心);摧圮(伤痛之极);摧裂(极言悲痛)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
