                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">復</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>復</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>還，返回。《爾雅&bull;釋言》：&ldquo;復，返也。&rdquo;《説文&bull;彳部》：&ldquo;復，往來也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>恢復；康復。《史記&bull;平原君虞卿列傳》：&ldquo;三去相，三復位。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>報復。《韓非子&bull;内儲説上&bull;七術》：&ldquo;故越王將復吴而試其教。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>報答。《漢書&bull;匈奴傳下》：&ldquo;以復天子厚恩。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>告訴；回答；回覆。《小爾雅&bull;廣言二》：&ldquo;復，白也。&rdquo;《史記&bull;司馬相如列傳》：&ldquo;是以王辭而不復。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>抵償；償還。《禮記&bull;曾子問》：&ldquo;除喪則不復昏禮乎？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>實踐；履行。《論語&bull;學而》：&ldquo;信近於義，言可復也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>事畢。《穀梁傳&bull;文公八年》：&ldquo;未復而曰復，不專君命也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>遏止。《淮南子&bull;時則》：&ldquo;規之為度也，轉而不復，員而不垸。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>免除（徭役或賦稅）。《洪武正韻&bull;屋韻》：&ldquo;復，除也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>寬宥；優待。《吕氏春秋&bull;當賞》：&ldquo;故復右主然之罪。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>安寧；安撫。《左傳&bull;昭公二十七年》：&ldquo;季氏之復，天救之也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>古稱人死後招其魂歸來。《周禮&bull;天官&bull;夏釆》：&ldquo;夏釆掌大喪，以冕服復于大祖，以乘車建綏復於四郊。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>六十四卦之一，卦形為䷗，震下坤上。《易&bull;復》：&ldquo;雷在地中，復。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>副詞。➊表示重複或繼續，相當於&ldquo;再&rdquo;。《論語&bull;述而》：&ldquo;久矣吾不復夢見周公。&rdquo;➋表示反問或加強語氣。《樂府詩集&bull;雜曲歌辭&bull;焦仲卿妻》：&ldquo;兒已薄禄相，幸復得此婦。&rdquo;➌表示頻度。相當於&ldquo;又&rdquo;、&ldquo;也&rdquo;。晋陶潛《讀〈山海經〉十三首》之八：&ldquo;不老復不死，萬歲如平常。</li>
	<li>連詞。表示並列關係。相當於&ldquo;又&rdquo;、&ldquo;與&rdquo;。唐王維《雪中憶李楫》：&ldquo;長安千門復萬户，何處躞蹀黄金羈。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>助詞。起補充或調節音節作用。《樂府詩集&bull;横吹曲辭&bull;木蘭詩》：&ldquo;唧唧復唧唧，木蘭當户織。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/複">複</a>&rdquo;。➊雙重；夾層。如：复姓；复線。《禮記&bull;明堂位》：&ldquo;復廟重檐。&rdquo;➋重複；再來一次。《玉篇&bull;彳部》：&ldquo;復，重也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/覆">覆</a>&rdquo;。➊覆蓋。《荀子&bull;臣道》：&ldquo;以德復君而化之，大忠也。&rdquo;➋傾覆。《易&bull;泰》：&ldquo;城復于隍，勿用師。&rdquo;➌考求。《墨子&bull;尚同中》：&ldquo;方今之時，復古之民始生，未有正長之時。&rdquo;</li>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">復</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>復</strong></p>
<p>復 <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈動〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形聲。小篆字形，下面的意符&ldquo;攵&rdquo;，是甲骨文&ldquo;止&rdquo;字的變形，表示與腳或行走有關。上面是聲符&ldquo;畐&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">f&uacute;</span>)的省形，有&ldquo;腹滿&rdquo;義，在字中亦兼有表義作用。後來繁化，加義符&ldquo;彳&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span>)，表示行走。本義:返回，回來)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本義 <span class="encs">[return to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">復，往來也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《說文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反覆其道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;復》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公歸不復。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《詩&middot;豳風&middot;九罭》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">揚觸捆復。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儀禮&middot;大射儀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">無往不復。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;泰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卒乃復。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《書&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不遠而復，先典攸高。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">樑</span><span class="smcs">《與陳伯之書》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">數請之，</span><span class="diczx2">朱亥</span>故不復謝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;魏公子列傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死者不可復生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊傳&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦復旦兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《書&middot;大傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言不可復也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《論語》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:復還(回返)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申爲恢復 <span class="encs">[restore]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">更若役，復若賦，則何如?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者說》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攘除奸兇，興復</span><span class="diczx2">漢室</span>，還於舊都。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">諸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出師表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:復國(恢復邦國);復性(恢復善良的本性);復正(恢復正規、正常);復樸(恢復真樸);復故(恢復照舊);復籍(恢復學籍;恢復國籍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 迴歸，還原 <span class="encs">[return]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">燕</span>日敗亡，卒至河上，而 <span class="diczx2">齊</span>七十餘城皆復爲 <span class="diczx2">齊</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;田單列傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死者不可復生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊傳&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦復旦兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《書&middot;大傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言不可復也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《論語》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 回答 <span class="encs">[reply]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或遇其叱咄，色愈恭，禮愈至，不敢出一言以復。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送東陽馬生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有復於王者曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王辭而不復。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;司馬相如傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:復書(覆信，答函);復謝(回拜，答謝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 報復 <span class="encs">[retaliate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">縱無被堅執銳者北面復</span><span class="diczx2">匈奴</span>之志。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">漢</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">桓寬</span><span class="smcs">《鹽鐵論本文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:復怨(報仇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 履行，實踐 <span class="encs">[carry out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:復言(實踐諾言);復踐(履行約定之事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古稱人死後招其魂歸來 <span class="encs">[revive]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:復魂(還魂);招魂復魄</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 免除<span class="encs">[賦稅徭役]</span> <span class="encs">[remit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民有東騎馬一匹者，復卒三人。(免除三人的兵役。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">漢</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">晁錯</span><span class="smcs">《論貴粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:復除(免除徭役);復租(免除賦稅);復免(免除徭役)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 覆蓋。引申爲庇護 <span class="encs">[cover;protect;shelter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顧我復我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《詩&middot;小雅&middot;蓼莪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:復土(蓋土);覆載生成(天覆地載，撫育成長。指庇養包容，教育成長);覆瓿(比喻著作沒有價值，只能用來蓋盛醬的瓦罐)</span></p>
<p><strong>詞性變化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>復</strong></p>
<p>復 <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>又，再。中斷再開始 <span class="encs">[resume]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不行不可復者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;牧民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">司馬相如</span>作凡將篇，無復字。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《漢書&middot;藝文志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以一至萬，則世世不相復也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《漢書&middot;賈山傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得復見將軍於此。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;項羽本紀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">超</span>復受使，固欲益其兵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《後漢書&middot;班超樑慬傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一狼得骨止，一狼仍從。復投之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊齋志異&middot;狼三則》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">復前行，欲窮其林。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶淵明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源記》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">輕攏慢捻抹復挑，初爲<span class="smcs">《霓裳》</span>後<span class="smcs">《六幺》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(並序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:復籍;復帳(妓女接待第二個客人);復旦(夜盡復明)</span></p>

<p>又【韻會】答也。【書&middot;說命】說復于王。</p>
<p>又【韻會】白也。【禮&middot;曲禮】願有復也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】反命也。【周禮&middot;天官】諸臣之復。【註】復，報也，反也。【疏】謂羣臣受王命，使臣行之訖，反報於王也。</p>
<p>又招魂曰復。【禮&middot;檀弓】復盡愛之道也。【註】復謂招魂，庶幾其精氣之反。</p>
<p>又興復。【諸葛亮&middot;出師表】興復漢室，還於舊都。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】元有復見心。</p>
<p>又州名。【隋書&middot;地理志】後周置復州，大業初改曰沔州。</p>
<p>又復陶，官名。【左傳&middot;襄三十年】使爲君復陶。【註】復陶，衣服之官也。</p>
<p>又衣名。【左傳&middot;昭十二年】王皮冠秦復陶。【註】秦所遺羽衣也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>方六切，音福。【集韻】重也。【正韻】反覆也。【易&middot;復卦】反復其道。【詩&middot;小雅】顧我復我。【註】謂迴轉反復之也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】復，除也。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】七大夫以下，皆復其身及戸勿事。【註】復其身，及一戸之內，皆不徭役也。</p>
<p>又與複通。【史記&middot;秦始皇紀】爲復道，自阿房渡渭屬之咸陽。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】上從復道上，望見諸將往往耦語。【註】上下有道，故謂之復。</p>
<p>又與覆通。【詩&middot;大雅】陶復陶穴。【音義】復，累土於地上。【說文】作覆。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>浮富切，浮去聲。【集韻】又也。【增韻】再也。【詩&middot;大雅&middot;大明序】文王有明德，故天復命武王也。</p>
<p>又叶房月切，音伐。【韓愈&middot;招揚之罘詩】禮稱獨學陋，易貴不遠復。作詩招之罘，日夕抱饑渴。渴，巨列切。</p>
<p>又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21574.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡕴</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21568.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡕨</span>㚆<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/208FE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠣾</span>。</p>
