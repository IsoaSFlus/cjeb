                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>彻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">徹</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄜˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>通，透：贯～。透～。～底。～骨。～悟。响～。</li>
	<li>治，开发：&ldquo;～田为粮&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>毁坏：&ldquo;～我墙屋&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> penetrate, pervade; penetrating</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> profond,complet,pénétrant,clairvoyant,traverser de part en part</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">彻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>彻</strong></p>
<p>徹 <span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从彳( <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span>)，甲骨文，从&ldquo;鬲&rdquo;(炊具)从&ldquo;丑&rdquo;(举手)，表示吃罢饭用手撤去炊具的意思。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>给古文增加了音符&ldquo;彳&rdquo;，变成了形声字。本义:撤除，撤去)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[remove]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">废彻不迟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">司宫彻之士。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;燕礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彻筮席。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;士冠礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大夫无故不彻县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且虽诸侯相见，军卫不彻，警也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三家者以<span class="smcs">《雍》</span>彻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;八佾上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">解车休马，罢酒彻乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:彻案(撤去食案);彻坐(撤去座位);彻兵(撤军)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拆毁;拆下 <span class="encs">[dismantle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彻我墙屋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伯昌号衰，秉鞭作牧，何令彻彼岐社，命有殷国?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;天问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:彻屋(拆除房屋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通达;明白 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝心之固，固不可彻，曾不若孀妻弱子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:彻鉴(明鉴;洞察)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 垦治 <span class="encs">[reclaim]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彻田为粮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;公刘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:彻田(垦治田地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 穿透 <span class="encs">[penetrate;pierce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日光下彻，影布石上。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:彻札(穿透铠甲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 结束，完结 <span class="encs">[finish;complete]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自经丧乱少睡眠，长夜沾湿何由彻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>

<p>又【廣韻】徹，達也。【左傳&middot;成十六年】養由基蹲甲而射之，徹七札焉。【註】發達七札也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】徹，道也。</p>
<p>又剝取也。【詩&middot;豳風】徹彼桑土。</p>
<p>又治也。【詩&middot;大雅】徹田爲糧。【傳】徹，治也。</p>
<p>又去也。【儀禮&middot;士冠禮】徹筮席。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】軍衞不徹警也。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;膳夫】卒食以樂，徹于造。【疏】天子食終徹器之時，作樂以徹之。</p>
<p>又毀也。【詩&middot;小雅】徹我牆屋。【箋】徹毀我牆屋也。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】徹，列也。【蔡邕&middot;獨斷】羣臣異姓有功封者，稱曰徹侯，避武帝諱，改曰通侯，或曰列侯也。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】丑列切【集韻】【正韻】敕列切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>闡入聲。義同。【集韻】或作〈足<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22FBF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢾿</span>〉。</p>
