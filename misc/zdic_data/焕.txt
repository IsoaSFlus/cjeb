                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>焕</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>(形声。从火，奂( <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:火光) 同本义 <span class="encs">[flame]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">焕，火光也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>焕</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 光亮，鲜明 <span class="encs">[shining;glowing;bright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">焕乎其有文章。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。集解:<span class="yhcs">“明也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:焕乎(光亮的样子);焕炳(光耀明亮);焕发(光彩四现);焕焕(显赫的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 闪耀强烈光亮的 <span class="encs">[brilliant]</span>。如:焕赫(炽盛的火光)</p><p>◎ <strong>焕</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>照耀;焕发光彩，放射光芒 <span class="encs">[shine;glow]</span>。如:焕目(耀眼。光芒映射，使人眼花);焕映(光华映射);焕奕(光彩焕发);焕扬(恢宏光大);焕景(光辉焕发;焕发的光辉)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/焕发">焕发</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/焕然一新">焕然一新</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
