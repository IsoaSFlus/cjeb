						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>掼</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">摜</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>扔，掷：～纱帽。～手榴弹。</li>
	<li>跌，使跌：～交。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> throw ground; know, be familiar</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> kaputtschlagen, zerschmettern</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> avoir l'habitude de,jeter à terre,tomber,porter une armure</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>掼</strong></p>
<p>摜 <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 扔，摔，掷 <span class="encs">[throw away]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劈手把鸡夺了，掼在地下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《范进中举》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:掼纱帽</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 佩带;披带 <span class="encs">[wear]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:顶盔掼甲</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 跌;使跌 <span class="encs">[fall down]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:他掼了一个跟头;把他掼倒了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈方〉∶握住东西的一端而摔另一端 <span class="encs">[fling]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:掼稻</span></p>
