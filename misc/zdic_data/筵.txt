                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>筵</strong> <span class="dicpy">yán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，延声。本义:竹席)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古时铺在地上供人坐的垫底的竹席，古人席地而坐，设席每每不止一层。紧靠地面的一层称筵，筵上面的称席 <span class="encs">[bamboo mat spread on the floor for people to sit on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筵亦席也。铺陈曰筵，藉之曰席。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:筵几(座席和几案);筵第(筵席和床铺)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 席位 <span class="encs">[seat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">张筵列鼎。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:筵席(酒宴时的座位和陈设);筵羞(酒宴美食)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 宴席 <span class="encs">[feast]</span>。如:筵开玳瑁(用玳瑁做装饰坐具的盛宴);筵会(宴会);喜筵;寿筵</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>筵</strong> <span class="dicpy">yán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 铺设坐席 <span class="encs">[spread mat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主人筵于户西。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:筵床(铺设席子的坐榻)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7B75.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】以然切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】夷然切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音延。【說文】竹席也。【周禮·春官】司几筵下士二人。【註】筵亦席也。鋪陳曰筵，藉之曰席。筵鋪于下，席鋪于上，所以爲位也。【釋名】筵，衍也。舒而平之，衍衍然也。【詩·小雅】賔之初筵。【箋】筵，席也。【正字通】經筵，王者講讀之處。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7B75.svg">                          <p>竹席也。从竹延聲。《周禮》曰：“度堂以筵。”筵一丈。以然切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7B75_4674.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(筵)</span><span class="ywyy">竹席也。</span><span class="swdz">周禮司几筵注曰。筵亦席也。鋪陳曰筵。藉之曰席。然其言之筵席通矣。按司几筵掌五几五席之名物。筵席不別也。五席不用竹。惟後鄭說次席是桃枝席。又說顧命蔑席、厎席、豐席、筍席皆爲竹席。許釋筵爲竹席者、其字从竹也。</span><span class="ywyy">从竹。延聲。</span><span class="swdz">以然切。十四部。</span><span class="ywyy">周禮曰。度堂以筵。</span><span class="swdz">匠人職曰。室中度以几。堂上度以筵。</span><span class="ywyy">筵一丈。</span><span class="swdz">此釋周禮也。周禮曰。度九尺之筵。此不合。未詳。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
