						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>升</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄥˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>容量单位。</li>
	<li>量粮食的器具。</li>
	<li>向上，高起，提高：～力（亦称&ldquo;举力&rdquo;）。～格。～华（➊固态物质直接变为气；➋喻事物的提高和精炼）。～迁。～值。～堂入室（喻人的学问造诣由浅入深，循序渐进，达到精深）。晋～。提～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">升</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>升</strong> <span class="dicpy">shēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。本义:容器名。一斗的十分之一)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 容量单位 <span class="encs">[liter， litre]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">升，十龠也。从斗，亦象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，十合也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两匊谓之升。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蕃衍盈升。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;椒聊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 十合为一升，十升为一斗。公制一升为1000毫升，合一市升。今公制与市制相同。如:升斛(升与斛的合称);升合(一升一合。比喻数量很小);升勺(一升一勺之量，比喻数量很少);升合之利(微利)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 量酒的单位</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赐酒日二升，肉二斤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 量器 <span class="encs">[ sheng ， a unit of dry measure for grain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忽见石窠中有二卵大如升。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《搜神后记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代布八十缕为升 <span class="encs">[80 strands]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冠六升，外毕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>升</strong></p>
<p>昇、陞 <span class="dicpy">shēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 上升，升起 <span class="encs">[uprise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昇，日上。本亦作升。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">升假借为&ldquo;登&rdquo;。字亦作昇，作陞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚而上者谓之升。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;序卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如日之升。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;天保》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一人飞升，仙及鸡犬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:升驾(飞升;腾云驾雾);升坠罔知(不知己故者的结局如何);升降揖逊(礼仪制度);升冠升珠(脱下官帽和外衣);升举(修炼成仙，升上天堂);升仙(死亡);升龙(乘龙升天);回升(下降后又往上升)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 登，上 <span class="encs">[ascend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陞，上也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">升，元亨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;升卦》</span>。疏:&ldquo;升者，登也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道存升降。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;毕命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天险不可升也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;习坎》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">由</span>也升堂矣，未入于室也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;先进》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:升阶(拾级而上);升车(登车);升炕(上炕就坐);升殿(登入宫殿理事);升堂睹奥(比喻学有所成，并观察到深奥之处);升陟(升登，攀登);升济(升登，超度);升封(登泰山封禅);升屋(登上屋顶);升座(登上座位)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 提升 <span class="encs">[promote]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">名因文著，位以才升。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《祭卢虔文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以此遂不得升进。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;王符传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既而胡即放</span><span class="diczx2">宁夏</span>知府，旋升 <span class="diczx2">宁夏</span>道。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:升转(官职的提升与调动);升除(提升官职。除:拜官受职);升擢(提升);升补(官吏的升迁与补缺);升扬(提升，升迁);升进(晋升官位);升第(晋级或被录用);升秩(升官);升行(提高行辈)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 谷物登场，成熟 <span class="encs">[ripe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旧谷既没，新谷既升。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 进奉，进献 <span class="encs">[pay tribute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是月也，农乃升谷，天子尝新，先荐寝庙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>升</strong> <span class="dicpy">Shēng</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Sheng prefecture]</span> 故治在今南京市</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓(如宋有昇元中)</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5347.svg">                          <p>十龠也。从斗，亦象形。識蒸切文十七</p>                          </div>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5347_601.svg" /><span class="swbf">(升)</span><span class="ywyy">十合也。</span><span class="swdz">十合各本作十龠，誤，今正。律曆志曰：合龠爲合，十合爲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>，十升爲斗，十斗爲斛。而五量嘉矣，作十龠則不可通。古經傳登多作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>，古文叚借也。禮經注曰：布八十縷爲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>字當爲登。今之禮皆爲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>，俗誤已行久矣。按今俗所用又作陞。經有言<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>不言登者，如周易是也。有言登不言<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>者，左傳是也。</span><span class="ywyy">从斗，象形。</span><span class="swdz">斛左耳爲升。右耳爲合。<img class="lazy" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5347_1.png" src="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5347_1.png">以象耳形也。各本作亦象形。非。識蒸切。六部。</span><span class="ywyy">合龠爲合，龠容千二百黍。</span><span class="swdz">十字各本無，今補。不補則五量惟<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>斗斛見於本書。且失其相絫之數矣。絫下云：增也。一曰十黍之重。正爲銖字張本。鬲下云：鼎屬。實五觳。又云：斗二<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>曰觳。正爲角部觳下無此義，故補之。亼部合下無一曰十龠爲合之文。龠部下無一曰龠容千二百黍之文。此處之當補，正同鬲下，非沾足也。合龠爲合，見律曆志。而尙書正義引作十龠，月令正義引作合，通典引作十，六典說唐制作合。是漢書古本不同。要以下文云合者合龠之量，躍於龠，合於合，廣雅二龠曰合，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣃔</span>之。知十龠之非矣。古者一分一合謂之判合。叚是十龠，則此量不得名合。不得云合於合，合二龠爲合。猶之十二銖㒳之爲兩也。<span class="ywyy">文十七。</span>小徐十六</span></p>                          </div>
