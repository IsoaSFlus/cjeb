						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>扶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>搀，用手支持人或物，使不倒：搀～。～正。～老携幼。</li><li>帮助，援助：～养。～植。～危济困。</li><li>用手按着或把持着：～梯。沙发～手。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>扶</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，夫声。从<span class="yhcs">“手”</span>，表示与手的动作有关。本义:搀扶)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[support with the hand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">危而不持，颠而不扶，则将焉用彼相矣?——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">车至门扶。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·卫策》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“扶，谓下车。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂扶以下。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扶杖望</span><span class="diczx2">朱砂庵</span>而登。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:扶绰(搀扶，扶托);扶策(扶助，搀扶);扶掖(搀扶;扶助);扶挈(以手搀扶);扶将(搀扶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 扶持;护持 <span class="encs">[support;shield and sustain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蓬生麻中，不扶而直。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:扶树(扶持培植);扶舁(扶持;搀扶);扶舁(护持扛抬);扶倾(扶持倾危的建筑物);扶拨(扶持倾斜);扶卫(扶持卫护)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“辅”</span>。辅助;帮助 <span class="encs">[assist;help]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扶，佐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若扶</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>伐 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·宋策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">求太老爷拘拿凶犯，以扶善良。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:扶政(辅佐政事);扶携(扶助提携);扶奖(辅助)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 护送 <span class="encs">[escort]</span>。如:扶灵;扶柩;扶丧(扶送灵枢)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 靠近 <span class="encs">[be closed to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">数披其木，毋使木枝挟疏。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·杨权》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">秋蛩扶户吟，寒妇成夜织。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《拟古八首》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:扶疏(枝叶茂密，高低疏密有致)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 攀缘 <span class="encs">[creep]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扶摇抮抱羊角而上。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“攀也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:扶服(伏在地上爬行);扶伏(伏地爬行);扶匐(伏地爬行)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 勉力撑持<span
