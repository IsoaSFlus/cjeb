						<p>● <strong>耘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&uacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>除草：～田。～锄（除草和松土用的锄头）。耕～。春耕夏～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">耘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>耘</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耒( <span class="dicpy">lěi</span>)，云声。本义:除草)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hoe;weed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">植杖而耘耔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耘艾(耕耘与收割。艾同刈);耘耔(翻土除草，指农耕);耘治(锄草治田);耘爪(一种用于水田的除草工具);耘秽(铲除杂草);耘籽(泛指从事田间劳动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 除去 <span class="encs">[get rid of ;remove]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:耘除(铲除);耘薙(犹刈除)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻辛勤攻读 <span class="encs">[assiduously study]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勉哉耘其业，以待岁晚收。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《送刘师服》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 培土 <span class="encs">[earth up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掘荃蕙与射干兮，耘藜藿与蓑荷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8018.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】王分切【韻會】【正韻】于分切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音雲。【說文】除田閒穢也。本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26510.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦔐</span>。今文作耘。【博雅】除也。【詩&middot;小雅】今適南畝，或耘或耔。【傳】耘，除草也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/264F7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦓷</span>。通作芸。【孟子】不芸苗者也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】王問切，音運。義同。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;東越傳】不戰而耘。【註】徐廣曰：耘，義取耘除。或言耘音于粉反，此楚人聲重耳。耘隕當同音，字有假借，聲有輕重耳。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶曜先切，音延。【韓愈&middot;贈謝自然詩】奈何不自信，反欲從物遷。寒衣及飢食，在蠶績耕耘。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/26510.svg">                          <p>除苗閒穢也。从耒員聲。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/264F7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦓷</span>，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26510.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦔐</span>或从芸。羽文切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/26510_9804.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26510.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦔐</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">除苗閒穢也。</span><span class="swdz">穢，當作薉。艸部薉，蕪也。無穢字。小雅毛傳曰：耘，除草也。耔，雝本也。食貨志云：播種於畎中。苗生三葉以上。稍耨壠草，因<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2148C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡒌</span>其土以附苗根，比成壠盡而根深，能風與旱，故薿薿而盛也。按此古者<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26510.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦔐</span>、耔爲一事也，謂苗初生之始也。旣成已後仍有莠及童蓈生乎其閒，蓐下所云陳艸復生也，則又以槈薅之。薅者，披田艸也，亦謂之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26510.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦔐</span>。吕覽云：其耨六寸，所以閒稼也。注云：耨所以耘苗，刃廣六寸，所以入苗閒。○又按吕覽云：六尺之耜，所以成畞也。謂六尺爲步，步百爲畞，卽車人爲耒，所謂弦其内與步相中也。又云：其博八寸，所以成畎也。八寸者，周之一尺，見夫部、尺部，卽匠人所謂一耦之伐廣尺也。又云：耨柄尺。此其度也，謂耨柄之尺寸，以耒六尺爲度也。又云：其耨六寸，所以閒稼也。此謂耨頭之金廣六寸，入於苗閒，所謂立苗欲疏也。高注：古者以耜耕，六尺爲步，步百爲畞，廣尺爲甽。今本舛誤不可讀。</span><span class="ywyy">从耒員聲。</span><span class="swdz">員，物數也，謂艸之多也。此形聲包會意。羽文切。十三部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/26510_9805.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/264F7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦓷</span>)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26510.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦔐</span>或从芸。</span><span class="swdz">按當云或從耒艸，云聲。今字省艸作耘。</span></p>                          </div>
