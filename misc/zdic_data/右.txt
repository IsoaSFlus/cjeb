                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">右</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>右</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yòu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yòu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄡˋ</span></p><ol><li>面向南时，西的一边，与“<span class="diczx3">左</span>”相对：～手。～边。</li><li>地理上指西方：山～。江～。</li><li>指政治思想上保守或反动的：～倾。～派。～翼。</li><li>古代称等级高的：～族。～职。无出其～（没有高过它的）。</li><li>崇尚，重视：“窃以～文兴化，乃致治之所先”。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">侑</span>”，劝酒，劝食。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">佑</span>”，帮助，偏袒。</li></ol>

</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> droite,côté droit</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">右</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>右</strong> <span class="dicpy">yòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从口，从又(手)。口手并用帮助别人。本义:右助。见<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。这个意义后来写作<span class="yhcs">“佑”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 助;帮助。后多作<span class="yhcs">“佑”</span> <span class="encs">[help]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">右，手口相助也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作佑。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">右者，助也。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以左右刑罚。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·士师》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“左右，助也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王右伯舆。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“助也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">保右命之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·嘉乐》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:右援(援助;引荐);右序(辅助;佑助);右饷(谓享受祭献，佑助降福);右与(佑助，帮助)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 尊崇;崇尚 <span class="encs">[respect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼爱，尚贤，右鬼，非命，</span><span class="diczx2">墨子</span>之所立也。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">高诱</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“右，犹尊也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:右鬼(尊崇鬼神);右贤(尚贤，尊崇贤能);右文(崇尚文治);右武(崇尚武功)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 保佑。后作<span class="yhcs">“祐”</span> <span class="encs">[bless and protect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祐、右实同字。今据</span><span class="diczx2">许</span>书，凡助为右、为佑，神助则为祐。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">保右命尔。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·大明》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维天其右之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·我将》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是天反复右我</span><span class="diczx2">汉国</span>也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·翟义传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“侑”</span>。劝食 <span class="encs">[urge sb. to drink or wait on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一朝右之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·彤弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既右飨之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·我将》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以享右祭祀。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官·大祝》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>右</strong> <span class="dicpy">yòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 右手 <span class="encs">[right hand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">右秉白旄以麾。——<span class="smcs">《书·牧誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">范蠡</span>乃左提鼓，右援，以应使者。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:右口(中医名词。右手寸口);右臂(人大多惯于用右手做事，因以右臂喻事物的要害部分)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 右手一边的方位，与<span class="yhcs">“左”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[the right side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">效驾，奋衣由右上。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">折其右肱。——<span class="smcs">《易·丰》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
