						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>定</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dìng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>不动的，不变的：～额。～价。～律。～论。～期。～型。～义。～都（<span class="dicpy">dū</span>）。～稿。～数（<span class="dicpy">shù</span>）（ａ．规定数额；ｂ．指天命；ｃ．规定的数额）。断～。规～。鉴～。</li><li>使不变动：～案。～罪。决～。确～。</li><li>平安，平靖（多指局势）：大局已～。</li><li>镇静，安稳（多指情绪）：心神不～。</li><li>确凿，必然的：必～。镇～。</li><li>预先约妥：～计。～情。～货。～做。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">定</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>定</strong> <span class="dicpy">dìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，从正。<span class="yhcs">“正”</span>亦兼表字音。本义:安定;安稳)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，与<span class="yhcs">“乱”</span>或<span class="yhcs">“动”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[stable;smooth and steady]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">定，安也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靡所定处。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑柔》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岂敢定居，一月三捷。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采薇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏定而晨省。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以待阴阳之所定。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下属安定，何故反乎?——<span class="smcs">《史记·留侯世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">痛定思痛，痛何如哉!——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录·后序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如其克谐，天下可定也。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:定迭(定叠。安定，太平);定害(定搅，定虐。扰乱，扰害);定帖(稳定;安定);定昏(请晚安)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 镇静;宁静 <span class="encs">[quiet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">磬罢僧初定，山空月又生。——</span><span class="diczx2">李中</span><span class="smcs">《碧云集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:心神不定;入定(佛教徒闭眼静坐，控制身心各种活动);镇定(遇到紧急情况不慌乱)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 固定 <span class="encs">[fixed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我戎未定。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采薇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岂可一切拘以定月哉。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·采草药》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山高风钜，雾气去来无定。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:定数(迷信者认为人生祸福都是命里注定，称为定数);定止(固定的处所);定式(固定的样式);定所(固定的住所、居处)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>定</strong> <span class="dicpy">dìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 使安定 <span class="encs">[stabilize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以定王国。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·六月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">定身以行事。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语二》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>岁定。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语三》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾定而禄爵。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语九》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先王之所以定天下也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·孝行》</span></span></p><p><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5B9A.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㱏【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>徒徑切，庭去聲。【說文】安也。【增韻】靜也，正也，凝也，決也。【易·說卦】天地定位。【書·堯典】以閏月定四時成歲。【禹貢】震澤底定。</p><p>又【禮·王制】論進士之賢者，以告於王，而定其論。【註】謂各署其所長也。</p><p>又止也。【書·洛誥】公定，予往已。【註】成王欲周公止洛，自歸往宗周也。</p><p>又【儀禮·鄕飮酒禮】羹定。【註】定猶熟也。【疏】熟卽止，故以定言之。</p><p>又【諡法】純行不差，安民法古，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>曰定。</p><p>又州名。漢中山郡，唐改定州，以安定天下爲名。</p><p>又丘名。【爾雅·釋丘】左澤曰定。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>丁定切，音訂。營室星也。【詩·鄘風】定之方中，作于楚宮。【註】定星昏而正中，於是時可以營制宮室，故謂之營室。孫炎曰：定，正也。一曰定謂之耨。</p><p>又【詩·周南】麟之定。【註】定，額也。</p><p>又【禮·禮器】羹定詔於堂。【註】羹，肉湆。定，熟肉。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋器】斪斸謂之定。【郭註】鋤屬也。</p><p>又叶唐丁切，音庭。【詩·小雅】亂靡有定。叶下寧成。</p><p>又【韻會】古通正。亦作奠。引周禮瞽矇世奠繫，奠讀爲定，謂帝繫，諸侯世本之屬。</p>
