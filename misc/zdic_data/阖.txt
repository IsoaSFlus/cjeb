						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>阖</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">闔</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄜˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>全，总共：～家。～城。</li>
	<li>关闭：～户。～门静居。</li>
	<li>门扇：仲春之月&ldquo;耕者少舍，乃修～扇&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>close; whole, entire; all; leaf</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ganz, vollständig ,Einlegeboden  (S)​,He  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> porte,fermer,tout,tout</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阖</strong></p>
<p>闔 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，盍声。本义:门扇)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a leaf of a door]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阖，门扇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外阖不闭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span>。注:&ldquo;阖，门扇也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阖扇(门扇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 门 <span class="encs">[door]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:外阖(外门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 盖墙用的草帘子 <span class="encs">[straw curtain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茨墙则剪阖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓。如:阖庐(指吴王夫差之父)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>阖</strong></p>
<p>闔 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 关闭<span class="encs">[close]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是故阖户谓之坤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;阖，闭翕也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲阖口而无言兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;东方朔&middot;七谏谬谏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当且阖閤，勿有所问。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;孙宝传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阖门善俟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以可阖门。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阖门投火死。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长衡</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 如:阖眼;阖户(闭户，关门);阖闭(闭合);阖棺(盖棺);阖辟(开关);阖开(开合)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 符合。通&ldquo;合&rdquo; <span class="encs">[agree]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">意者臣愚而不阖于王心邪?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阖心(符合心意)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>阖</strong></p>
<p>闔 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>全，总 <span class="encs">[the whole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今或至阖郡而不荐一人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;武帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阖属(所有属下);阖座(满座人);阖朝(全朝);阖门百口(全家所有的成员)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>阖</strong></p>
<p>闔 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闔</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“阖”字头，请参考“闔”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              闔·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95D4.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】胡<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26873.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦡳</span>切【集韻】轄<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26873.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦡳</span>切【韻會】曷閤切【正韻】胡閤切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音合。【說文】門扇也。【爾雅&middot;釋宮】闔謂之扉。【疏】闔扇也。【禮&middot;月令】仲春之月，乃修闔扇。【註】用木曰闔。【左傳&middot;襄十八年】以枚數闔。【註】闔，門扇也。【史記&middot;宋微子世家】牧齒著門闔。【註】闔，門扇。</p>
<p>又【韻會】雙曰闔，闔門也。單曰扇，扇戸也。</p>
<p>又【說文】閉也。【易&middot;繫辭】闔戸謂之坤。【疏】謂閉藏萬物，若室之閉闔其戸。【左傳&middot;襄十七年】吾儕小人，皆有闔廬，以辟燥濕寒暑。【註】闔，謂門戸閉塞。</p>
<p>又苫也。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;圉師】茨牆則翦闔。【註】闔，苫也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】摠合也。【後漢&middot;張儉傳】儉見曹氏世德已萌，乃闔門懸車，不與政事。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】今或至闔郡，而不荐一人。【註】總一郡之中，故曰闔郡。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】閶闔。【前漢&middot;禮樂志】游閶闔。【註】閶闔，天門。【淮南子&middot;原道訓】排閶闔，鑰天門。【註】閶闔，始升天之門也。</p>
<p>又風。亦名閶闔。【史記&middot;律書】閶闔風，居西方。</p>
<p>又助語詞。【莊子&middot;列禦<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡨥</span>】闔胡嘗視其良，旣爲秋栢之實矣。【註】闔，語助也。</p>
<p>又【則陽篇】日與物化者，一不化者也，闔嘗舍之。【註】言何不試舍其所爲乎。</p>
<p>又國名。【前漢&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>湯傳】又遣使責闔蘇大宛諸國歲遺。【註】闔蘇，國名。在康居北一千里。</p>
<p>又人名。【莊子&middot;人閒世註】顏闔，魯之賢人隱者。</p>
