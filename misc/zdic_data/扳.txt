                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>扳</strong> <span class="dicpy">bān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，反声。本义:拉，引)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 拉;引;拨动 <span class="encs">[pull;draw;turn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日扳</span><span class="diczx2">仲永</span>环谒于邑人。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《伤仲永》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:扳罾(拉罾网捕鱼);扳倒(用力拧转使之倒翻;翻倒);扳闸</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 扭转;背转 <span class="encs">[turn round]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帝谓能丰己，故扳公议立之。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·则天武皇后传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:扳回一局棋;扳本(方言。翻本)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 论争;辩驳 <span class="encs">[debate]</span>。如:扳驳(辩驳);扳理;有理不怕别人扳</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">pān</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/扳本，扳本儿">扳本，扳本儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/扳道">扳道</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/扳动">扳动</a></span><span class="crefe"><a class="dicpy">pān</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (古通<span class="yhcs">“攀”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 攀援 <span class="encs">[climb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扳，援也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸大夫扳</span><span class="diczx2">隐</span>而立之。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·隐公元年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:扳援(攀着他物向上或向前);扳缘(攀着他物向上爬);扳陟(攀登，抓住或握住某物向上爬);扳跻(攀登)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 结交 <span class="encs">[make friends with;associate with]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若是平常经纪人家;没前程的，</span><span class="diczx2">金老大</span>又不肯扳他了。——<span class="smcs">《喻世恒言》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 攀附 <span class="encs">[seek connections in high places]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">近年牛医儿，城社更扳援。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《行次西郊作一百韵》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:扳附(依附);扳亲(攀附姻亲);扳联(攀附，牵引);扳高(竭力与地位高的人结亲或拉关系);扳龙(比喻依附帝王或有权势的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 牵连 <span class="encs">[involve]</span>。如:扳害(牵扯;连累);扳脏(诬人栽脏);扳连(约束，牵制)</p><p><span 又如:扳蔓(纠缠);扳缠(纠缠;牵制);扳差头(有意找别人的过失);扳厚(拉关系);扳话接舌(攀谈搭腔)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">bān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
