                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">藉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>藉</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 践踏;欺凌 <span class="encs">[tread on;bully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">藉贫孱者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀夫子者无罪，藉夫子者不禁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 进贡 <span class="encs">[pay tribute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无以藉君，与君相忆也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《续齐谐记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 登记;记 <span class="encs">[record]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">藉长幼贫氓之数。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;籍&rdquo;。登记并没收所有的财产 <span class="encs">[confiscated property]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:藉没(没收)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 顾念;顾惜 <span class="encs">[take good care of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:藉不得(顾不得)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用绳缚 <span class="encs">[tie;bind]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:藉靡(缚，捆绑)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>藉</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>杂乱 <span class="encs">[in disorder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公门少推恕，鞭朴恣狼藉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《田家》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>藉</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;籍&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 藉田 <span class="encs">[field]</span>。古代天子、诸侯征用民力耕种的田</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子为藉千亩，诸侯为藉百亩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 耕种藉田 <span class="encs">[till]</span></p>

<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】執玉，其有藉者則裼，無藉者則襲。【註】藉，藻也。【疏】凡執玉必有其藻，以承于玉。【儀禮&middot;聘禮註】藉謂繅也。繅所以蘊藉玉。</p>
<p>又【孟子】助者，藉也。【趙岐註】猶人相借力助之也。【戰國策】藉兵乞食于西周。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;薛廣德傳】廣德爲人溫雅有醞藉。【註】寬博有餘也。</p>
<p>又【後漢&middot;隗囂傳】光武素聞其風聲，報以殊禮，所以慰藉之良厚。</p>
<p>又【綱目集覽】身之所依曰藉。</p>
<p>又【釋名】咀藉也，以藉齒牙也。</p>
<p>又秦昔切，音籍。狼藉，離披雜亂貌。【前漢&middot;江都易王傳】國中口語藉藉，無復至江都。</p>
<p>又【周語】宣王卽位，不藉千畝。【前漢&middot;文帝紀】其開藉田。</p>
<p>又【穀梁傳&middot;哀十三年】其藉于成周，以尊天王。【註】藉謂貢獻。</p>
<p>又【莊子&middot;應帝王】猿狙之，便執斄之狗來藉。【註】藉，繩也。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;武安侯傳】今吾身在也，而人皆藉吾弟。【註】藉，蹈也。</p>
<p>又姓。【國語】藉偃，晉大夫。</p>
<p>又【唐韻古音】讀胙。【史記&middot;商君傳】註新序論：周室歸藉。【索隱】藉音胙。</p>
<p>又【韻補】秦二切。【班固&middot;西都賦】原野蕭條，目極四裔。禽相鎭壓，獸相枕藉。</p>
