                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">咽</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 口腔后部由肌肉和黏膜构成的管子，分三部分，上段与鼻腔相对称“鼻咽”；中段与口腔相对称“口咽”；下段在喉的后部称“喉咽”。咽是呼吸道和消化道的共同通路（亦称“咽头”）：～喉。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 使嘴里的食物或别的东西通过咽头到食道里去：～唾沫。狼吞虎～。细嚼慢～。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yè.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄝˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 阻塞，声音因阻塞而低沉：哽～。呜～。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> throat; pharynx</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Engpass  (S)​,Kehle  (S)​,wichtige Stelle  (V)​, schlucken, verschlucken ,voll pfropfen, würgen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pharynx,gosier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">咽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>咽</strong> <span class="dicpy">yān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，因声。本义:咽头)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pharynx]</span>消化和呼吸的通道，位于鼻腔、口腔的后方，喉的上方，相应地分为鼻咽、口咽和喉咽三部分。通称咽喉</p><p><span class="diczx1">咽，嗌也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“咽者，因也，言食因于是以上下也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咽，又谓之嗌，气所流通，厄要之处也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咽已绝。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·息夫躬传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“喉咙。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">佗</span>尝行道，见有病咽塞者。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·华佗传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾恐</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>人食之不得下咽也。—— <span
