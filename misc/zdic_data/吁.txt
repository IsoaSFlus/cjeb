                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>吁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xū.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>叹息：长～短叹。</li>    <li>叹词，表示惊疑：～，是何言欤？</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>吁</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">籲</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 为某种要求而呼喊：呼～。～天（呼天诉苦）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">吁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>吁</strong> <span class="dicpy">xū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><叹></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，于声。本义:表示惊怪、不然、感慨等) 同本义 <span class="encs">[oh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王曰:<span class="yhcs">“吁!来!有邦有土，告尔祥刑。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《书·吕刑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吁!惊也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王曰:<span class="yhcs">“吁，来!…”</span>——<span class="smcs">《史记·周本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吁!是何言欤?——<span class="smcs">《法言·君子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“吁!骇叹之声。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:吁咈(表示不以为然之意);吁咈都俞(吁咈，吁俞。用以赞美君臣间论政之和洽。见<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span>);吁嗟(表示忧伤或有所感;表示赞美)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>吁</strong> <span class="dicpy">xū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 叹息，叹气 <span class="encs">[sigh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">香车辗尽关山日，弹罢琵琶只自吁。——<span class="smcs">《明珠记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:吁唏(吁嘘。嘘唏，叹息);吁气(叹气);吁嗟(哀叹;叹息);吁嗟(慨叹);吁然(感叹的样子);吁叹(叹息;哀叹);吁嘻(感叹)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 吐 <span class="encs">[breathe out]</span>。如:吁呵(口中默念);吁吸(嘘气与吸气;呼吸);吁呼(指吐气);吁荼(谓散发出温暖之气);吁气(呼气，吐气)</p><p><span class="diczx1">云何吁矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·卷耳》</span></span></p><p><span
