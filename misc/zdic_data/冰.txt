                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>冰</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>水因冷凝结成的固体：～块。～凌。～箱。～窖。～雕。～封。～球。～镇。～释（像冰一样融化，喻嫌隙、怀疑、误会等完全消除）。～淇淋。</li><li>结晶成固体，呈结晶形的：～糖。～晶石。</li><li>使人感到寒冷：～手～脚。</li><li>用冰贴近东西使其变凉：把汽水～上。</li><li>洁白明彻：～绡。～心。～清玉洁。～肌玉骨（a.形容妇女的皮肤；b.形容梅花的高洁）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">冰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>冰</strong></p><p>氷 <span class="dicpy">bīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从仌，从水。金文作<span class="yhcs">“仌”</span>。金文字形表示水凝成冰后，体积增大，表面上涨(上拱)形。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“冻也，象水凝之形”</span>。小篆繁化，增加<span class="yhcs">“水”</span>变成。从<span class="yhcs">“仌”</span>从<span class="yhcs">“水”</span>的会意字，于是<span class="yhcs">“仌”</span>就专用作部首。本义:水冻结而成的固体)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冰，水坚也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">履霜坚冰至。——<span class="smcs">《易·坤》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迨冰未泮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·匏有苦叶》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冰者，阴之盛而水滞者也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冰，水为之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冰解而冻释。——<span class="smcs">《管子·五行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公徒释甲，执冰而踞。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:冰扳(冰镇);冰麝(冰片与麝香);冰清水冷(像冰和水一样的清冷);冰前刮雪(喻雪上加霜之事);冰玉自信(自知冰清玉洁，并无苟且之事);冰轮(比喻月亮)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 冰人，即媒人 <span class="encs">[go-between;match-maker]</span>。如:冰议(指嫁娶之事);冰语(媒人的话);冰斧(媒人);冰媒(媒人)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>冰</strong> <span class="dicpy">bīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 结冰，冻结 <span class="encs">[freeze;ice over]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水始冰，地始冻。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 把东西和冰或冷水放在一起使变凉 <span class="encs">[ice]</span>。如:把两瓶汽水冰一冰</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 使感到极冷 <span class="encs">[feel cold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风月冰人别是乡。——</span><span class="diczx2">杨万里</span><span class="smcs">《六月二十四日病起喜雨闻莺》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:这水冰手</p><p>◎ <strong>冰</strong> <span class="dicpy">bīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 冷，凉 <span class="encs">[cold]</span>。如:冰食(冷食);冰甃(指寒凉的水井);冰澈(寒凉清澈)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 结晶成固体的，成结晶形的 <span class="encs">[crystal]</span>。如:冰糖</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 清白，晶莹 <span
