						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>桌</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhuō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄛˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>几（<span class="dicpy">jī</span>）案，可用于饮食、读书、写字：～案。饭～。方～。</li>
	<li>量词：三～客人。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>桌</strong></p>
<p>棹 <span class="dicpy">zhuō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，卓省声。桌字本作&ldquo;卓&rdquo;。卓有高而直立之义。后来人们又根据&ldquo;棹&rdquo;是木制的特点另制&ldquo;桌&rdquo;字。俗又作&ldquo;棹&rdquo;。本义:桌子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[table]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一桌，一椅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古称几案</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上与讲官俗共一桌，真不啻天颜咫尺矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文隶</span><span class="smcs">《列皇小识》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:桌巾(桌布);桌屏(摆在桌上当作装饰的小屏风);桌帏(亦称&ldquo;桌帷&rdquo;同&ldquo;桌围&rdquo;。围在桌子边的装饰物，多以布或绸缎做成);桌裙(即桌帏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 计酒席的量词。如:一桌酒席，三桌客</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卓</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“桌”字头，请参考“卓”字：)</span>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八">卷八</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八&amp;swbs=匕">匕</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5353.svg">                          <p>高也。早匕爲㔬，匕卪爲卬，皆同義。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/220A6.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢂦</span>，古文㔬。竹角切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5353_607.svg" /><span class="swbf">(㔬)</span><span class="ywyy">高也。</span><span class="swdz">論語：如有所立，卓爾。凡言卓犖，謂殊絕也，亦作卓躒。按稽部<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25894.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥢔</span>，特止也。辵部逴，遠也。人部倬，箸大也。皆一義之引伸。覲禮：匹馬卓上，九馬隨之。注：卓讀如卓王孫之卓，卓猶旳也，以素旳一馬爲上。素旳一馬，謂白馬也。鄭意白馬出衆故謂之卓。史記：多叚淖爲卓。</span><span class="ywyy">早匕爲卓。</span><span class="swdz">此上當有從匕早三字。匕同比。早比之，則高出於後比之者矣。</span><span class="ywyy">匕卪爲卬，皆同意。</span><span class="swdz">意舊作義，今正。此與凡云某與某同，意同也。竹角切。古音在二部。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5353_608.svg" /><span class="swbf">(卓)</span><span class="ywyy">古文㔬。</span><span class="swdz">漢隸及今隸从⺊，用古文而小篆廢矣，又疑古文恐是篆文之誤。</span></p>                          </div>
