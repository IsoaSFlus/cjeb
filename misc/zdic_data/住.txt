                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">住</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>住</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>长期居留或短暂歇息：居～。～宿。～房。～户。</li>
	<li>停，止，歇下：～手。雨～了。</li>
	<li>动词的补语（➊表示稳当或牢固，如&ldquo;站～&rdquo;；➋表示停顿或静止，如&ldquo;他愣～了&rdquo;。➌与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">得</span>&rdquo;或&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">不</span>&rdquo;连用，表示力量够得上，如&ldquo;支持不～了&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">住</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>住</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，主声。本义:停留)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(站住) <span class="encs">[stay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见者呼之曰:&ldquo;</span><span class="diczx2">蓟先生</span>小住。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;蓟子训传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春光已向梅梢住。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">融</span>为中书郎，未有居止，权牵小船于岸上住。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南齐书&middot;张融传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舞余香尚存，歌尽声犹住。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">萧悫</span><span class="smcs">《春日曲水》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:住歇(停止，停歇);住滞(停留);住后(留后;殿后);住锡(僧人在某地居留。锡，锡杖)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 停止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">杜</span>请 <span class="diczx2">裴</span>追之， <span class="diczx2">羊</span>去数里住马，既而俱还 <span class="diczx2">杜</span>许。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;方正》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两岸猿声啼不住，轻舟已过万重山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《早发白帝城》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:住脚(止步);住罢(停止)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 暂居;居住 <span class="encs">[reside]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自言本是京城女，家在虾蟆陵下住。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">住近</span><span class="diczx2">湓江</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人道</span><span class="diczx2">寄奴</span>曾住。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如: 住坐(居住);住室(供人居住的房间)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 住宿;过夜 <span class="encs">[stay;pass the night;put up for the night]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行多有病住无粮，万里还乡未到乡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">卢纶</span><span class="smcs">《逢病军人》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> <span class="encs">[be stationed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家本</span><span class="diczx2">清河</span>住 <span class="diczx2">王城</span>，须凭弓箭得巧各。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">令狐楚</span><span class="smcs">《少年行》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 保留 <span class="encs">[keep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">应似园中桃李树，花落随风子住枝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《母别子》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>住</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;动补&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 做动词的补语</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示牢固或稳当</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众人向前，一个带住婆子，三四个带住</span><span class="diczx2">唐牛儿</span>，把他横拖倒拽，直推进 <span class="diczx2">郓城县</span>里来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">贾政</span>还欲打时，早被 <span class="diczx2">王夫人</span>抱住板子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示停止或静上</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我给你个好主意，</span><span class="diczx2">虎姑娘</span>立住了，面对面地对他说，&hellip;这个事非我自己办不可。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">老舍</span><span class="smcs">《骆驼祥子》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 跟&ldquo;得&rdquo;或&ldquo;不&rdquo;连用。表示能否胜任或能否够得上</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那</span><span class="diczx2">祝龙</span>当敌 <span class="diczx2">秦明</span>不住，拍马便走。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></p>
