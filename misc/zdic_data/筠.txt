                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">筠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>筠</strong> <span class="dicpy">Jūn</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Jun prefecture]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 唐武德七年置。以地产筠篁得名。辖境约当今江西省高安、上高、新昌等县地</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 唐羁縻州，其地即今四川省南部的筠连县</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>筠</strong> <span class="dicpy">yún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，均声。本义:竹子的青皮;竹皮)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[smooth hard skin of bamboo]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筠，竹皮之美质也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">手握青筠之杖。——</span><span class="diczx2">王嘉</span><span class="smcs">《拾遗记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其在人也，如竹箭之有筠也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“筠，竹之青皮也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绮窗唱和，指花月为题，绣阁论情，对松筠为誓。——<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:筠管(竹管);筠筒(竹筒);筠窗(竹窗);筠梯(竹梯);筠席(竹席); 筠床(竹床); 筠纸扇(竹柄纸扇); 筠帘(竹帘)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 竹子的别称 <span class="encs">[bamboo]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">目才到三眠半月强，即时懒意满筠筐。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周煌</span><span class="smcs">《吴兴蚕词》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黛叶轻筠绿，金花笑菊秋。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">钱起</span><span class="smcs">《赋得池上丁香树》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:筠溪(竹丛中的流水);筠斑(斑竹);筠廊(竹丛中的回廊);筠筒(竹筒);筠管(竹管，多用以指笔);筠笼(覆罩在香炉上的竹笼)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 竹制的笛类管乐器 <span class="encs">[bamboo flute]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窃闻</span><span class="diczx2">平阳</span>击石，山谷为之调; <span class="diczx2">大禹</span>吹筠，风云为之动。—— <span class="diczx2">北周</span>· <span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《赵国公集序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">jūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
