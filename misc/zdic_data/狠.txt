                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>狠</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hěn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hěn.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>凶恶，残忍：心～。～戾。～毒。～心。凶～。</li><li>勉强地抑制住难过的心情：～着心把泪止住。</li><li>严厉地：～批他一顿。</li><li>全力以赴：～劲。～抓学习。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">很</span>”。</li></ol>
