                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>菁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>韭菜的花：“秋韭冬～”。</li><li>古代指“芜菁”（即“蔓菁”）。</li><li>华丽：“丽服扬～”。</li><li>水草：“唼喋～藻”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> flower of leek family; turnip</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> grün, frisch, sauber, klar  (Adj)​,prächtig, gut  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fleur de poireau,cyanine</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">菁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>菁</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，青声。本义:韭菜花)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chives flower]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">菁，韭华(花)也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其实菁菹。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·醢人》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“非菹也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以西菁菹。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·公食礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“萱菁也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指盛开的花 <span class="encs">[flower]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">秋兰茝蕙，江离载菁。——<span class="smcs">《文选·宋玉·高唐赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:菁菁者莪(原是<span class="smcs">《诗经·小雅》</span>的篇名，此诗赞美培育人材，后世遂用来比喻乐育英才)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 水草 <span class="encs">[waterweeds]</span>。如:菁茅(植物名。茅的一种，古时用来包裹东西。又香草名。古时用来滤酒，以除去酒中的杂质)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 华采 <span class="encs">[essence]</span>。如:菁华;菁英(精华;精英);菁藻(犹精华;精英)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 泛指蔬菜 <span class="encs">[vegetables]</span>。如:菁羹(菁菜羹。亦泛指穷人所食的粗菜)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/菁菁">菁菁</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>菁</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧㄥ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jīng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3375.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">韭菜花。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·艸部》：「<b>菁</b>，韭华也。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">泛指花朵。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·宋玉·高唐赋》：「秋兰茝蕙，江离载<b>菁</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">植物名。即芜<b><b>菁</b></b>。十字花科芸薹属，一年生或两年生草本。春季开黄色十字花，总状花序，叶抱茎而生，边缘有锯齿，根呈扁球形，可食。</span><span class="gc_yx">俗称为「大头菜」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/83C1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】子盈切【集韻】【類篇】咨盈切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音精。【說文】韭華也。【張衡·南都賦】秋韭冬菁。【註】廣雅曰：韭，其華謂之菁。</p><p>又菁茅。【書·禹貢】包匭菁茅。【管子·輕重丁】江、淮之閒，有一茅而三脊，母至其本，名之曰菁茅。</p><p>又蔓菁。【周禮·天官】醢人菁菹。【註】菁，蔓菁也。</p><p>又蕪菁，【後漢·桓帝紀】種蕪菁以助人食。</p><p>又菁，華英也。【張衡·西京賦】麗服颺菁。</p><p>又【集韻】倉經切，音靑。菁菁，花盛貌。【詩·唐風】有杕之杜，其葉菁菁。</p><p>又地名。【謝靈運·山居賦】三菁五奧。【自註】三菁，在太平之北。</p><p>
