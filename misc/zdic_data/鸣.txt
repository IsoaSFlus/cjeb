                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鸣</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鸣</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鳴</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>鸟兽或昆虫叫：～啭。～唱。～叫。～禽。鸟～。</li>
	<li>发出声音，使发出声音：～响。～奏。孤掌难～。</li>
	<li>声明，发表意见、情感：～谢。～冤。百家争～。</li>
	<li>闻名，著称：&ldquo;以文～江东&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cry of bird or animal; make sound</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> das Singen der Vögel  (V)​,läuten  (V)​,weinen, rufen, schreien  (V)​,zwitschern, krähen, zirpen, trillern  (V)​,Ming  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (animaux, oiseaux)​ crier,chanter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鸣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鸣</strong></p>
<p>鳴 <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从口，从鸟。本义:鸟叫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chirp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸣，鸟声也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸟兽之音曰鸣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管辂别传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸣鹤在阴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;中孚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凤皇鸣矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;卷阿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡既鸣矣，朝既盈矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鸡鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狗吠深巷中，鸡呜桑树巅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鸣管(鸟类的发声装置);鸣凤(凤鸟鸣叫);鸣雁(雁鸣叫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指发声 <span class="encs">[sound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">震为鸣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">萧萧马鸣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;车攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸣玉者，佩玉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南子&middot;贾经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驴一鸣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽大风不能鸣也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2"> 苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鸣石(撞击则会发出声响的石头);鸣玉(古人佩于腰间的玉饰。行走时相互撞击而发声);鸣琴(指琴。又比喻瀑布的声音);鸣鞭(挥鞭发出声响);鸣銮(车上的銮铃鸣响。指天子或贵族出行)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 震惊 <span class="encs">[astonish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿得</span><span class="diczx2">燕</span>弓射天将，耻令 <span class="diczx2">越</span>甲鸣吾君。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《老将行》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 申告 <span class="encs">[redress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">重者鸣官究治。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">祁彪佳</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 言说;称说 <span class="encs">[say]</span></p>

<p>又叶謨郞切，音芒。【前漢&middot;郊祀歌】寒暑不忒況皇章，展詩應律鋗玉鳴。函宮吐角激徵淸，發梁揚羽申以商。【張華&middot;俠曲】孟嘗東出關，濟身由雞鳴。信陵西反魏，秦人<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2274C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢝌</span>其彊。</p>
