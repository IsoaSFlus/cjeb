                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>众</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3"><img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">衆</span>、<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字">眾）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄥˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>许多：与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">寡</span>&rdquo;相对：～人。～多。～矢之的。芸芸～生。</li>	<li>许多人：大～。群～。民～。～口铄金。～目睽睽。～叛亲离。～擎易举。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> multitude, crowd; masses, public</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">众</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>众</strong></p>
<p>衆、 <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。&ldquo;众&rdquo;从三人。&ldquo;三&rdquo;表示众多。&ldquo;众&rdquo;表示众人站立。&ldquo;衆&rdquo;，甲骨文字形。象许多人在烈日下劳动。本义:众人、大家)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[multitude;everyone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人三为众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坤为众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大师之礼用众也，大均之礼恤众也，大田之礼简众也，大役之礼任众也，大封之礼合众也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宗伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安土重居，谓之众庶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;杨终传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庸众而野。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;脩身》</span>。注:&ldquo;众，众人也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众恶之，必察焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;卫灵公》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众怒难犯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则众何为而不汹汹然?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此教我先威众耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群疑满服，众难塞胸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《后出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">激众愤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾村之众。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众皆感奋。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">率众袭之。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:众喙(众人的闲言碎语);众口嚣嚣(众人吵吵嚷嚷);众怨之的(大家怨恨的目标);众力(众人之力);众口(众人的言论);众夫(众人;一般人);众兆(众人);众目(众人的眼睛);众心(众人之心);听众;观众;群众;会众(到会的人);当众(当着大家);民众(人民大众);公众(社会上大多数的人);万众(大众)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指诸事，万事 <span class="encs">[everything]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">政事失其施，加于身而错于前，凡众之动失其宜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;仲尼燕居》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:众名(万物之名);众有(万物);众念(万念);众物(万物;诸物);众圣(诸圣人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 指百官;群臣 <span class="encs">[all official]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子之五官曰司徒、司马、司空、司士、司寇，典司五众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:众士(贵臣以外诸臣。古代泛指诸侯臣僚，各级官吏);众臣(贵臣身份以外之臣。即众士)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 兵;军队。亦指丁壮可为兵者 <span class="encs">[soldier;army]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">率数万之众。(众指军队。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:众力(特指兵力);众卒(一般兵士)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 殷、周从事农业生产的奴隶，或管理奴隶的人 <span class="encs">[slave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">格!尔众庶，悉听朕言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;汤誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 佛家语。用以指教徒人数 <span class="encs">[monk number]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一行三众，辞别</span><span class="diczx2">高老</span>及众亲友，投西而去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 又如:众性(佛教语。众生);众生(泛指人和一切动物;指人以外的各种动物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>众</strong></p>
<p>衆 <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 众多，与&ldquo;寡&rdquo;、&ldquo;少&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[many]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今寇众我寡，难以持久。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;赤壁之战》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">师克在和，不在众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树成荫而众鸟息焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼能且众。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众贼环而进。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众宾团坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众妙毕备。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:众峰(许多山峰);众工(许多工匠);众支(众多的支系);众小(众多小国);众卉(百草);众口纷纭(人多嘴杂，论论纷纷);众毛飞骨(众多的众物能掀起重物);众巧(众多的巧匠);众矢之的(许多箭射的靶子。比喻众人攻击的目标)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 各种 <span class="encs">[various]</span>。如:众艺(各种技艺，艺术);众虑(各种杂念;各方面的考念);众管(各种管乐器);众伪(种种假象);众群(各种邪恶);众善(各种善举)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 广泛;普遍 <span class="encs">[general;common]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是非容貌之患也，闻见之不众，议论之卑尔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杨倞</span>注:&ldquo;闻见不广，议论不高。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:众妙(一切深奥玄妙的的道理);众医(一般医生;庸医)</p>
