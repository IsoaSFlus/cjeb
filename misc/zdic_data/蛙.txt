                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蛙</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>蛙</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">wā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄚˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>两栖动物的一科，无尾，后肢长，前肢短，趾有蹼，善于跳跃和泅水。种类很多：青～（俗称&ldquo;田鸡&rdquo;）。牛～。～泳。～人。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蛙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蛙</strong> <span class="dicpy">wā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从虫，圭声。本义:田鸡类动物。青蛙) 同本义 <span class="encs">[frog]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">&hellip;</span><span class="diczx2">苏</span>俗谓之田鸡， <span class="diczx2">扬州</span>谓之水鸡，亦曰吠蛤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听取蛙声一片。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《西江月&middot;夜行黄沙道中》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蛙市(夜晚蛙群聚集而鸣，称为蛙市);蛙吹(蛙鸣);蛙角(将头发束起，形状宛如青蛙头上左右凸出而对称的部分);蛙怒(形容青蛙叫声很大，有如鼓气发怒);蛙蛤(蛙的一种)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蛙</strong> <span class="dicpy">wā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 淫邪 <span class="encs">[licentious]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蛙咬(淫声俗乐);蛙声(淫声，淫邪的乐声);蛙歌(淫邪之声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 凹隐。扳着脸的样子 <span class="encs">[low-lying]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那</span><span class="diczx2">陈经济</span>把脸儿蛙着不言语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金瓶梅》</span></p>

<ol>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集中" target="_blank">申集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集中&amp;bs=虫" target="_blank">虫</a>】
                              蛙·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/86D9.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕䵷【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>烏瓜切，音哇。【說文】蝦蟆屬。【本草】今處處有之，似蝦蟆而背靑綠色，尖觜細腹，俗謂之靑蛙。亦有背作黃路者，謂之金線蛙。【尹文子&middot;大道上篇】路逢怒蛙而軾之。【前漢&middot;五行志】武帝元鼎五年秋，蛙與蝦蟆羣鬭。</p>
<p>又【韻會】淫也。【前漢&middot;王莽傳】紫色蛙聲。【註】淫蛙之聲。</p>
<p>又烏蝸切，音哇。義同。　本作䖯。或書作鼃。別詳黽部。</p>
