                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>概</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄞˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>大略，总括：大～。～论。～述。～貌。梗～。～要。～算。～括。～念（反映对象的本质属性的思维形式）。～率（概率论的基本概念。用来表示随机事件发生可能性大小的量称为此事件的&ldquo;概率&rdquo;。亦称&ldquo;或然率&rdquo;、&ldquo;几率&rdquo;）。以偏～全。</li>
	<li>情况，景象：胜～（胜景，美丽的景色）。</li>
	<li>一律：一～而论。</li>
	<li>气度，节操；气～。</li>
	<li>刮平斗、斛用的小木板。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">概</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>概</strong></p>
<p>槩 <span class="dicpy">g&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，既声。本义:量米粟时刮平斗斛( <span class="dicpy">h&uacute;</span>)用的木板。量米粟时，放在斗斛上刮平，不使过满)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[scraper]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">同权槩而就衡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;惜誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斛甬，正权概。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">槩者，平量者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:概量(概和斗斛等量谷物的器具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 节操，风度 <span class="encs">[bearing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而不概于王心邪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;范雎传》</span>。按，平也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫</span><span class="diczx2">西河</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>土&hellip;&hellip;漂然皆有节概，知去就之分。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;杨敞传》</span>附 <span class="diczx2">杨恽</span><span class="smcs">《报孙会宗书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:概尚(气节);气概(人遇事表现出来的态度、气势)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 景象;状况 <span class="encs">[circumstance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故山多药物，胜概(美丽的景象)忆桃源。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《奉留赠集贤院崔于二学士》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>概</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引申为刮平，不使过量 <span class="encs">[scrape]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫水，徧与诸生而无为也，似德。&hellip;&hellip;盈不求概，似正。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;宥坐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而不概于王心邪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;范雎传》</span>。按，平也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:概平(用概刮平);概准(用平物的量器刮平)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 概括，总括<span class="encs">[generalize]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:概问(概括询问);以此一端，可概其余;以偏概全</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 衡量;量，限量 <span class="encs">[weigh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食飨不为槩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;量也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:概志(衡量心志);概量(用量器确定谷物的多少;比喻衡量事物的标准)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 蔽，遮盖 <span class="encs">[cover]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:概愆(掩盖前愆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 关切，系念 <span class="encs">[be deeply concerned]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:概怀(系念于心);概然(慨叹的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 抑，抑制 <span class="encs">[restrain; control]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:概了(镇住。有超过所有人的意思)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;溉&rdquo;。灌溉，洗涤 <span class="encs">[irrigation;cleanse;wash]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">澡概胸中，洒练五脏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《枚乘&middot;七发》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如;概盂(洗涤盂钵)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通&ldquo;慨&rdquo;。感慨 <span class="encs">[give vent to one&#39;s fleeling about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫婢妾贱人，感概而自杀，非能勇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;季布栾布田叔传赞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:概然(感慨的样子)</span></p>

<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一律，一概 <span class="encs">[one and all]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余以所闻</span><span class="diczx2">由</span><span class="diczx2">光</span>义至高，其文辞不少概见，何哉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;伯夷列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:概不考虑;概不赊欠;概行(一律施行);概视(一律看待);概用(一概使用)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 大致，大略地 <span class="encs">[generally]</span>。就整个情况而论而不考虑具体事例地。如:概而论之;概举(大略举出);概览(大略地观览)</p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/69E9.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古代切【集韻】【韻會】居代切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音漑。平斗斛木。【說文】作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233D9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣏙</span>，斗斛。【徐曰】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233D9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣏙</span>，戛摩之也。斗斛，量槩也。【禮&middot;月令】仲春之月，正權槪。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】㮚氏槩而不稅。【疏】槩，所以勘諸廛之量器，以取平者。</p>
<p>又平也。【管子&middot;樞言】釜鼓滿則人槪之，人滿則天槪之。</p>
<p>又大槪，大率也。【史記&middot;伯夷傳】其文辭不少槩見。【莊子&middot;天下篇】槩乎皆常有聞者也。【張衡&middot;東京賦】粗爲賔，言其梗槩如此。</p>
<p>又感觸經心也。【史記&middot;范雎傳】臣愚，不槩于王心。【淮南子&middot;精神訓】勢位爵祿，何足以槩志。</p>
<p>又節槩。【左思&middot;吳都賦】俗有節槩之風。【晉書&middot;桓溫傳】豪爽有風槩。</p>
<p>又退槩，幽深不明也。【何晏&middot;景福殿賦】其奧祕則翳蔽曖昧，髣髴退槩。</p>
<p>又口漑切。同慨。【史記&middot;季布傳】婢妾賤人，感槩而自殺，非能勇也，其畫無復之耳。</p>
<p>又許旣切，音餼。亦平木也。</p>
<p>又古對切，音憒。義同。</p>
<p>又古忽切，音骨。哀亂貌。</p>
<p>又叶居氣切，音記。【劉楨&middot;魯都賦】貴交尚信，輕命重氣。義激毫毛，怨成梗槩。</p>
