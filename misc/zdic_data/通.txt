						<p>● <strong>通</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄨㄥˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>没有阻碍，可以穿过，能够达到：～风。～天。～气。～宵。～行。～过。～衢。贯～。四～八达。曲径～幽。</li>	<li>懂得，彻底明了：～晓。～彻。～今博古。～情达理。</li>	<li>传达：～令。～讯。～报。～告。～知。～缉。～谍。</li>	<li>往来交接：～敌。～商。～邮。～融。～假（<span class="dicpy">jiǎ</span>）（汉字的通用和假借）。串～。沟～。</li>	<li>普遍、全：～才（指知识广博，具有多种才能的人）。～论。～体。～身。～读。～常。～病。～盘。普～。</li>	<li>男女不正当的性行为：～奸。私～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">通</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>通</strong> <span class="dicpy">tōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵(<span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，甬(<span class="dicpy">yǒng</span>)声。本义:没有堵塞，可以通过)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[open;through;unimpeded]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">通，达也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">往来不穷谓之通。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">推而行之谓之通。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坎为通。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道远难通。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;至也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">血脉欲其通也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;达郁》</span>。注:&ldquo;利也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凝绝不通声暂歇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中通外直。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:通衢大道(四通八达的大道，指交通要道);通漕(直通水运);通塞(指境遇的顺利与滞涩);通水(通水运;能行船的地域);通畅(通行无阻的);通川(有河川流通的地方);通邑(四通八达的城市)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通往 <span class="encs">[lead to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">指通</span><span class="diczx2">豫</span>南。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道不通。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">才通人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北通</span><span class="diczx2">巫峡</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:通天(通往天界。后用以形容本能非凡);通吊(至丧家吊唁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 传递;传达 <span class="encs">[pass on;communicate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主簿通语言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
