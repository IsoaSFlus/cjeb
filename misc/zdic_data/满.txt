                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>满</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">滿</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄢˇ</span></p><ol><li>全部充实，没有余地：～足。～意。充～。饱～。美～。～腔热血。琳琅～目。～载而归。</li><li>到了一定的限度：～员。～月。不～周岁。</li><li>骄傲，不虚心：自～。志得意～。</li><li>十分，全：～世界（到处）。～堂灌。～天飞。～园春色。</li><li>使满，斟酒：～上一杯酒。</li><li>中国少数民族，主要分布于辽宁、黑龙江、吉林、河北等省和北京市、内蒙古自治区：～族。～文。～汉全席。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">满</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>满</strong></p><p>滿 <span class="dicpy">mǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:填满，布满)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[filled;full of;packed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">满，盈溢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无几何而往，则户外之屦满矣。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·列御寇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荆有云梦，犀兕麋鹿满之。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奸邪满侧。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·扬权》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">溪云初起日沉阁，山雨欲来风满楼。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">许浑</span><span class="smcs">《咸阳城东楼》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">归来泪满巾。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">张俞</span><span class="smcs">《蚕妇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黄四娘家花满溪。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江畔独步寻花》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒌蒿满地。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《惠崇春江晚景》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初闻涕泪满衣裳。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《闻官军收河南河北》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:满口胡柴(满口胡说);满坑满谷(形容积聚得特多);满面生花(满面笑容);满天星(指眼里冒出许多金星);满眼(充满视野);满堂(充满堂上);满衍(布满平野，遍野);满肚子(满肚皮。充满肚皮;充满心中);满实(充盈);满怀信心(心中充满自信心);满脸春色(满脸春风。比喻满脸充满喜悦的笑容);满听(满耳);满溢(太多而漫出);满生生(很满的样子);满心(心中充满某种情绪或意愿);满礼(满理。充足的理由);满顶(不留余地);满饮(斟满而饮)</p><p><span
