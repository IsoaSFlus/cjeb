                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>勿</strong> <span class="dicpy">wù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>(象形。据甲骨文，像一树旗。右边是柄，左边是飘带。本义:州里所建旗帜) 古代大夫、士所建旗帜，半赤半白，用来麾集人众 <span class="encs">[red-white flag]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勿，州里所建旗，…杂帛，幅半异。所以趣民。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“‘州里’当作‘士大夫’。”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>勿</strong> <span class="dicpy">wù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不 <span class="encs">[not]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲勿予。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贤者能勿丧。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">齐人勿附于</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">表否定。如:勿造至于(不至于);勿适意(不舒服;身体欠佳);勿连牵(不连贯;不清楚);勿时路(不时髦;不流行);勿想(不吱声;不说话);勿述(不逆。犹言顺畅);勿庸(无庸，不必，无须);勿论(不追究;不谈);勿药(不服药);勿齿(不愿意提及);勿入调(〈方〉∶不规矩;胡闹)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 不要，别 <span class="encs">[don't]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勿士行枚。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·东山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽欲勿用。——<span class="smcs">《论语·雍也》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">过则勿惮改。——<span class="smcs">《论语·学而》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡人敢勿轼乎?——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·脩务》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">必勿使反。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愿勿遣。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸君勿复言。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将军勿虑。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">表示禁止或劝阻。如:请勿吸烟;勿用(勿动;不可有所作为);勿惮(不要怕);勿然(不要这样)</span></p><p>◎ <strong>勿</strong> <span class="dicpy">wù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>无，没有。与<span class="yhcs">“有”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[not have]</span>。如:勿有(没有);勿碍格(〈方〉∶没什么要紧的);勿着杠(〈方〉∶没得到;落空)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勿谓言之不预">勿谓言之不预</a></span></div>
