                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>果</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">guǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǒ.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄛˇ</span></p><ol><li>某些植物花落后含有种子的部分：～实。～品。～木。结～（ａ．结出果实；ｂ．事情的结局或成效）。</li><li>结局，与“<span class="diczx3">因</span>”相对：因～。成～。</li><li>坚决：～决。～断。</li><li>确实，真的：～真。如～。</li><li>充实，饱足：～腹。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fruit; result</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Frucht  (S)​,Obst  (S)​,Ergebnis, Resultat  (S)​,wirklich, tatsächlich, wie erwartet  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">果</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>果</strong> <span class="dicpy">guǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，田象树上结的果实形，在木之上。本义:果子，果实。这个意义后来曾写作<span class="yhcs">“菓”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fruit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">果，木实也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">艮为果蓏。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而树之果蓏，珍异之物。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·场人》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">张晏</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“有核曰果，无核曰蓏。”</span> <span class="diczx2">臣瓒</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“在地曰蓏，在树曰果。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五果为助。——<span class="smcs">《素问·藏器法时论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓桃李杏栗枣也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡长安豪富人为观游及卖果者，皆争迎取养。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《种树郭橐驼传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">杭</span>有卖果者，善藏柑。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">果止于梨、栗、枣、柿之类。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:果布(果品与布帛);果馔(果品与菜肴);果谷(果类与谷类);果正(正果。亦指转世);果桌(果卓。摆设筵席用的桌子);草果;荚果;浆果;果瓜(供果用的甜瓜);果茹(瓜果蔬菜);果隋(瓜果);果蔗(一种供鲜食的甘蔗)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 结果 <span class="encs">[result]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贵贱虽复殊途，因果竟在何处。——<span class="smcs">《南史·范云传》</span>附</span><span class="diczx2">范缜</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敌人既然敢犯罪，他就该自食其恶果!</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:果验(应验);战果;效果;成果;自食其果;恶果</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“祼”</span>( <span class="dicpy">guàn</span>)。古代帝王以酒祭奠祖先或赐宾客饮之礼 <span class="encs">[sacrificial ceremony of pouring water to irrigate the field]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">大宾客则摄而载果。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以待果将。</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>果</strong> <span class="dicpy">guǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 果敢，果断 <span class="encs">[resolute;courageous and resolute]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">由也果。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">苞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓果敢决断也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其身果而辞顺。——<span
