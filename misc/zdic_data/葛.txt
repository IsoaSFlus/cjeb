                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">葛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>葛</strong> <span class="dicpy">gé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，曷( <span class="dicpy">hé</span>)声。本义:一种植物，纤维可以织布)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 植物名。豆科多年生草本植物( Pueraria lobata )，茎长二三丈，缠绕他物上，花紫红色。茎可编篮做绳，纤维可织葛布。根可提制淀粉，又供药用 <span class="encs">[lobed kudzuvine]</span>。如:葛屦(葛制的鞋子。贫贱人家所穿用。一说夏天所穿用);葛屦履霜(冬天穿夏季的鞋子。比喻非常俭啬);葛面(葛粉)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 葛布。指以葛为原料制成的布、衣、带等 <span class="encs">[kohemp cloth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卒时，佥都御史王用汲入视，葛帏敞籯，有寒士所不堪者，因泣下醵金为敛。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:葛巾(用葛布缝制的头巾);葛衣(葛布制成的衣服);葛帔(用葛制成的披肩);葛纱(以葛的纤维织成的纱布)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 夏衣的代称 <span class="encs">[summer clothing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一葛一裘经岁，一钵一瓶终日，老子旧家风。——</span><span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《水调歌头》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父母岁有裘葛之遗，无冻馁之患矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 表面有横棱的纺织品，用丝或化纤卡丝作经，棉线或毛线作纬 <span class="encs">[a kind of textile fabric]</span>。如:毛葛;华丝葛</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“盖”</span>。掩覆 <span class="encs">[cover]</span>。如:葛沟(古代埋葬尸体的一种方法)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">gě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gě.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/葛布">葛布</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/葛根">葛根</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/葛粉">葛粉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/葛巾">葛巾</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
