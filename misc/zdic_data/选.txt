                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>选</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">選</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xuǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄢˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>挑拣，择：～择。～购。～辑（①挑选并辑录；②选辑成的书）。～拔。～用。～贤任能。</li>	<li>用投票或举手等表决方式推举出代表或负责人：～举。普～。</li>	<li>被选中的（人或物）：入～。人～。</li>	<li>选辑成册的作品：文～。诗～。短篇小说～。</li></ol><p>● <strong>选</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄢ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/先">先</a>&rdquo;，出自战国楚简。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">选</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>选</strong></p>
<p>選 <span class="dicpy">xuǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，巽( <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;n</span>)声。本义:遣送;放遂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[send into exile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">选，遣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其母曰:&ldquo;弗去，惧选。&rdquo;癸卯，</span><span class="diczx2">针</span>适 <span class="diczx2">晋</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;柬&rdquo;。挑选，选择 <span class="encs">[pick and choose;select]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">选，一曰择也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人，十人曰选。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可选也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;柏舟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命乡论秀士，升之司徒曰选士。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昼选男德以象谷明。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">器成卒选，则士知胜矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;七法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">案谨募选阅材伎之士。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大道之行也，天下为公，选贤与能。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">选文以定篇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙&middot;序志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">定选士，胜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:选侍(选入宫中的侍女);选锋(挑选出来的精壮兵卒);选刷(挑选);选人(选用人才);选士(挑选人才);选权(铨选官吏之权);选簿(铨选官吏的簿籍);选职(主管铨选官吏的职务)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 派遣;使令 <span class="encs">[dispatch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">选兵八万人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已选三万人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">更选将官。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:选入外班(派往外地任职的官员)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9078.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】先兗切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】須兗切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25DB7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥶷</span>。【玉篇】擇也。【詩&middot;邶風】威儀棣棣，不可選也。【禮&middot;禮運】選賢與能。【左傳&middot;襄九年】舉不失選。</p><p>又少選，須臾也。【呂覽】少選發而視之。亦曰選閒。</p><p>又【正韻】白選，貨貝名。【史記&middot;平準書】白金三品，其一圜之其文龍，名白選。</p><p>又【玉篇】去絹切【廣韻】思絹切【集韻】【正韻】須絹切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音潠。【禮&middot;王制】命鄕論秀士升之司徒，曰選士。【註】選，宣練反。</p><p>又【正韻】銓官也。【唐書】有《選舉志》。</p><p>又環舞也。【詩&middot;齊風】舞則選兮。</p><p>又【字彙補】萬也。【山海經】五億十選九千八百步。【楊愼云】選與萬，古音通，遂借其字。</p><p>又選懦仁弱也。【後漢&middot;淸河王傳】選懦之恩，知非國典。【註】選懦，仁弱慈戀，不決之意也。</p><p>又選懦、選耎，畏怯之意。【前漢&middot;西南夷傳】議者選耎，復守和議。【後漢&middot;西羌傳】公卿選懦，容頭過身。</p><p>又【廣韻】思管切【集韻】損管切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音算。【集韻】數也。【書&middot;盤庚】世選爾勞。【傳】數也。【左傳&middot;昭九年】弗去懼選。【註】懼數其罪而責之也。</p><p>又與算同。【前漢&middot;公孫賀傳贊】斗筲之徒，何足選。音義與算同。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>數滑切，音刷。金選，銖兩名。【前漢&middot;蕭望之傳】甫<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>之屬小過赦，薄罪贖，有金選之品。【應劭註】選，音刷。【正字通】漢讀率，《史記&middot;周本紀》其罰百率。率，如刷。故劭以刷音之。　【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨕖</span>。俗作⿺辶巺。選字从巺作。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
