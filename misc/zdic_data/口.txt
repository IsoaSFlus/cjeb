                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>口</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kǒu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄡˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>人和动物吃东西和发声的器官（亦称“<span class="diczx3">嘴</span>”）：～腔。～才。～齿。～若悬河。</li>    <li>容器通外面的地方：瓶子～。</li>    <li>出入通过的地方：门～。港～。</li>    <li>特指中国长城的某些关口（多用作地名）：古北～。喜峰～。</li>    <li>破裂的地方：～子。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mouth; open end; entrance, gate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Öffnung (S)​,Mund (S)​,ZEW für Dinge mit einem Mund , einer Öffnung wie Menschen, Haustiere, Brunnen, Schächte, Kanonen (Zähl)​,Kou (Eig, Fam)​,Radikal Nr. 30 = Mund, Öffnung, Eingang, Mündung (S, Sprachw)​,ZEW für Familienmitglieder (Zähl)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bouche,ouverture,entrée,passe,trou,(classificateur pour : les membres d'une famille, les objets à large orifice tels que puits, jarres, cloches, malles)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">口</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>口</strong> <span class="dicpy">kǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象人的口形。本义:口腔器官，嘴)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。人类用来发声和进食的器官 <span class="encs">[mouth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">口，人所以言食也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且夫口三五之门也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">口之为言达也。——<span class="smcs">《春秋·元命苞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">口者心之门户。——<span class="smcs">《鬼谷子·捭阖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勺饮不入口七日。——<span class="smcs">《左传·定公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">下有蟾蜍，张口承之。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卖炭得钱何所营?身上衣裳口中食。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:口腹(指饮食);口分(口福);口滑(指吃东西失去控制;说话不谨慎);口辅(面颊。辅:颊);口眼弗闭(放心不下;死不瞑目);口里摆菜碟儿(比喻嘴上说得很好，但无实惠)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 出入通过的地方 <span class="encs">[passage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山有小口。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">由接官亭至城门口。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俱会大通口。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">彭蠡</span>之口有 <span class="diczx2">石钟山</span>焉。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:井口;矿井口;火山口</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> <span class="encs">[population]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百亩之田，勿夺其时，数口之家可以无饥矣。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">八口之家可以无饥矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">视高曾祖时口已不下五六十倍。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">而户与口之数常处其有余也。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:口赋(古时按人口征收的赋税);口合不敷(口粮不够，难以度日);口井(计口分田，实行井田制)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 言语 <span
