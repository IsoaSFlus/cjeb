                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">晓</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>晓</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">曉</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>天明：～市。拂～。破～。报～。</li>	<li>知道，懂得：～畅。～得。分～。</li>	<li>使人知道清楚：～示。～谕。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dawn, daybreak; clear, explicit</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> aube,savoir,faire savoir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">晓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>晓</strong></p>
<p>暁 <span class="dicpy">xiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，尧声。本义:天明)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。今专指天刚亮 <span class="encs">[dawn;daybreak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓，明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冥冥之中，独见晓焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;俶真》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓驾炭车辗冰辙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓看红湿处。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春夜喜雨》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓当尽戮之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梳晓鬟也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓风残月。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:破晓(刚亮);拂晓(天快亮);晓雾;晓舌(鸟拂晓叫);晓暝(天将亮的时候);晓行;晓鸡初啼;晓星;晓岚;鸡鸣报晓;晓夕(日夜);晓天，晓色(拂晓时的天色);晓月，晓魄(拂晓残月)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 清晨 <span class="encs">[morning]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓光浮野，朝烟承日回。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">简文帝</span><span class="smcs">《侍游新亭应令诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:晓日(朝阳);晓夕;晓光(清晨的日光);晓妆(晨妆);晓昏(朝夕);晓霜(早上的霜露)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>晓</strong></p>
<p>暁 <span class="dicpy">xiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 明白，了解 <span class="encs">[know;understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓，慧也;快也;智也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓然以至道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;臣道》</span>。注:&ldquo;明喻之貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明主不深晓，以为仆沮贰师，而为</span><span class="diczx2">李陵</span>游说。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马迁传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上晓音律。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使使晓武。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
