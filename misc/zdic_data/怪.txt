                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>怪</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>奇异，不平常：～诞（离奇古怪）。～事。～物。～讶。～圈（<span class="dicpy">quān </span>）。～话。～异。奇形～状。</li>
	<li>惊奇：大惊小～。～不得。</li>
	<li>传说中的妖魔之类：鬼～。妖～。</li>
	<li>性情乖僻或行为异样的人：～癖（古怪的癖好）。～僻（古怪）。</li>
	<li>很，非常：～好的天气。</li>
	<li>怨，责备：不～你。～罪。～怨。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">怪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>怪</strong></p>
<p>恠 <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>(形声。从心，圣( <span class="dicpy">k&ugrave;</span>)声。恠曾为怪的俗字。本义:奇异;奇怪)</p>
<p>同本义 <span class="encs">[bewildering;odd;strange;fantastic]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怪，异也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诡于众而突出曰怪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;自纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铅松怪石。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苟床之山多怪石。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;中山经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见怪物&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭法》</span>。疏:&ldquo;庆云之属。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奇服怪民不入宫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;阍人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齐谐者，志怪者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嘻，技亦灵怪矣哉!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝巘多生怪柏，悬泉瀑布，飞潄其间。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水经注&middot;三峡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后小山下，怪石乱卧，针针丛棘，青麻头伏焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怪伟(奇特雄伟);怪物相(奇异的样子;轻蔑、难堪的脸色);怪迂(怪异而不切实际);怪谲(怪异任性)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>怪</strong> <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>妖精，鬼物 <span class="encs">[devil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子</span>不语怪、力、乱、神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;述而》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">土之怪为獖羊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《博物志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山高必有怪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鬼怪;妖怪;精怪;神怪(神仙与鬼怪);魔怪(妖魔鬼怪)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>怪</strong> <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 责怪 <span class="encs">[blame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世果群怪聚骂，指目牵引，而增与为言辞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《答韦中立论师道书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽然，使后之为君者果能保此产业，传之无穷，亦无怪乎其私之也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怪责(责怪);怪恨(责怪怨恨);怪嗔(嗔怪，对别人的言语行为表示不满);怪得(怪底;怪的。难怪，怪不得)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 惊异;觉得奇怪 <span class="encs">[wonder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民怪之，莫敢徒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;商君列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予怪而问之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怪底(惊怪，惊疑);怪怖(惊异恐惧);怪疑(骇异;惊异疑惑);怪慑(惊异害怕)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>怪</strong> <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="encs">[口]</span> 很，非常 <span class="encs">[quite; very]</span>&mdash;&mdash;用在形容词、表示心理状态的动词或动宾词组前面，表示程度很深。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这口箱子怪沉的;经常打扰您，怪不好意思的;怪厉害的;怪似(十分相像);怪热(很热);怪冷(很冷)</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>怪</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄍㄨㄞˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>gu&agrave;i <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2408.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">奇異的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「怪模怪樣」、「奇形怪狀」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;賈島〈暮過山村〉詩：「<b>怪</b>禽啼曠野 ，落日恐行人。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">奇異、不尋常的事物。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;述而》：「子不語：<b>怪</b>、力、亂、神。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">神話傳說中的妖魔之類。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「鬼<b>怪</b>」、「妖<b>怪</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;班彪&middot;王命論》：「震電晦冥，有龍蛇之<b>怪</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如黃帝時有怪義。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">驚訝、疑忌。</span><span class="gc_yy">《墨子&middot;貴義》：「子墨子南遊使衛，關中載書甚多，弦唐子見而<b>怪</b>之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;東方朔&middot;答客難》：「海內定國家安是遇其時者也，子又何<b>怪</b>之邪？」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">責備、埋怨。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「責<b>怪</b>」、「怪罪」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;正論》：「今世俗之為說者，不<b>怪</b>朱、象，而非堯、舜，豈不過甚矣哉？」</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史&middot;第三回》：「論這事，只該<b>怪</b>我們 金老爺。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">很、甚。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「這娃兒<b>怪</b>討人喜歡的！」</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第四二回》：「眾人愛你伶俐，今兒我也<b>怪</b>疼你的了。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/602A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古壞切，乖去聲。【說文】異也。【增韻】奇也。【風俗通】怪者，疑也。【白虎通】異之言怪也。凡行之詭異曰怪。【中庸】索隱行怪。</p>

<p>又氣變常，人妖物孽曰怪。【揚子&middot;太<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>經】怪分靑赤白黑黃，皆物怪也。</p>

<p>又姓。【春秋元命苞】炎帝臣怪義。</p>

<p>又叶古僞切，音貴。【馬融&middot;長笛賦】波散廣衍，實可異也。牚距劫遌，又足怪也。【易林】五心六意，岐道多怪。非君本懷，生我恨悔。</p>
<p>又叶古懷切，音乖。【元稹&middot;痁臥詩】一生常苦節，三省詎行怪。　【周伯琦曰】从心圣聲。圣音怪。俗作恠，非。</p>
