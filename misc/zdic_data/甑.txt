                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">甑</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>甑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zèng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zèng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>古代蒸饭的一种瓦器。底部有许多透蒸气的孔格，置于鬲上蒸煮，如同现代的蒸锅：～尘釜鱼。</li><li>〔～子〕现在蒸饭用的木制桶状物，有屉而无底。</li><li>蒸馏或使物体分解用的器皿：曲颈～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> boiler for steaming rice, pot</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> großer Kessel, Kessel  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> marmite en terre pour cuire le riz</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>甑</strong> <span class="dicpy">zèng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zèng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从瓦，曾声。从瓦，表示是陶制品。本义:古代炊具，底部有许多透蒸汽的小孔，放在鬲上蒸煮 <span class="encs">[食物]</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[an ancient earthen utensil for steaming rice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">甑，甗也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">甑，自</span><span class="diczx2">关</span>而东谓之甗，或谓之甑。——<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>五</p><p><span class="diczx1">许子以釜甄爨，以铁耕乎?——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荷甑堕地，不顾而去。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·孟敏传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝甑米空烹芋粥，夜缸油尽点松明。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《杂题六首》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 蒸米饭的用具，略像木桶，有屉子而无底，亦作<span class="yhcs">“甑子”</span> <span class="encs">[rice steamer]</span>。如:他到灶屋抓了一把甑边饭</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 蒸馏或使物体分解用的器皿 <span class="encs">[an utensil for distilling water， etc.]</span>。如:曲颈甑</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
