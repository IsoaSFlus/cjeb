                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>未</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wèi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wèi.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>不，不曾，没有：～必。～曾（<span class="dicpy">céng </span>）。～来。～尝。～竟（没有完成的）。～及（ａ．没有来得及；ｂ．没有达到）。</li><li>地支的第八位，属羊。</li><li>用于记时：～时（下午一点至三点）。</li><li>放在句末，表示疑问：“君除吏尽～？吾亦欲除吏”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> not yet; 8th terrestrial branch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> 8. Tier im chin. Tierkreis - Ziege <astrol&gt,Jahr der Ziege (S)​,Jahr des Schafs (S)​,Schafsjahre (S)​,Ziegenjahre (S)​,Wei (Eig, Fam)​,nicht; noch nicht (Adv)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ne pas,pas encore,(8ème branche terrestre)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">未</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>未</strong> <span class="dicpy">wèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。基本义:没有;不。<span class="yhcs">“未”</span>字否定过去，不否定将来，与<span class="yhcs">“不”</span>有别。但有时候也当<span class="yhcs">“不”</span>字讲)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 相当于<span class="yhcs">“没有”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“不曾”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“尚未”</span> <span class="encs">[did(have) not;not yet;never]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未，无也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅·广诂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未由也已。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子罕》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未之难矣。——<span class="smcs">《论语·宪问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾与</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>人未有成也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·隐公六年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾未有言之。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·开春》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未果。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有孙母未去。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未百步则返。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将舒未舒。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:未际(未发迹);未的(未必，不一定);未从(未曾，尚未);未傅(未成年的人);未遑(来不及，没有闲暇);未遇(未得到赏识和重用;未发迹);未宾(没有归顺);未集(未能完成);未臻(未到圆满的地步);未极(无穷远处;未到尽头;没有停止);未意之志(没有完成的志向);未第(科举考试中未中);未萌(事情发生之前)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> (<span class="yhcs">“未”</span>字否定过去，<span class="yhcs">“不”</span>字否定将来，但有时候未也当不讲)不 <span class="encs">[not]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众宾未拾取矢。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡射礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“未，犹不也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未若复吾赋不幸。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未为简易。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">游人虽未盛。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹未能也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:未消(不必，不要);未一(不再一一去详细叙述);未敢苟同;未知可否;未审(不知);未的(不确实，不确切);未足(不足，不能);未如(不如);未妨(不妨);未若(不如);未孚(不算大信，不是至诚);未足轻重(无关紧要，不值得重视);未应(不曾，不须);未为不可(不是不可以);未逮(不及;没有达到)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 否 <span
