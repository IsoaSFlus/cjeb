						<p>● <strong>叩</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">k&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄡˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>敲打：～击。～门。～关（➊入国求见；➋攻打关门；➌指足球、冰球等运动中攻打球门）。～诊。</li>
	<li>旧时一种礼节：～拜。～见。～谒。～首。～头。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">叩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叩</strong></p>
<p>敂 <span class="dicpy">k&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>无&ldquo;叩&rdquo;字，只有&ldquo;敂&rdquo;字。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;敂，击也。从攴，句声。读若扣。&rdquo;从&ldquo;攴&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，表示与击打有关。本义:击，敲打)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[knock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叩石垦壤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡四方之宾客，敂关则为之告。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;司关》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扣之，其声清扬而远闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;法行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以杖叩其胫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;宪问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">眾者叩舟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说林》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以杖叩狼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娘以指叩门扉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叩胫(以杖棍击胫骨。是惩罚的一种方式);叩门扉(敲打门扉);叩心(捶胸。表示悔恨交加的样子);叩门(敲门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 叩头、拜 <span class="encs">[kowtow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆叩头，叩头且破，额血流地，色如死灰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叩恳(叩头请求);叩奠(叩拜祭奠);叩赏(磕头谢赏);叩请(恭敬而诚恳地请求)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 举发;询问 <span class="encs">[expose;inquire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扣其乡及姓字，皆不答。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余叩所以。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叩咨;叩问;叩询</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 攻打 <span class="encs">[attack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">引兵叩城。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《刘公墓志铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;扣&rdquo;。拉住 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">伯夷</span><span class="diczx2">叔齐</span>叩马而谏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;伯夷列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叩马(拉住马缰绳，不使前进)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>叩</strong> <span class="dicpy">k&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>诚恳 <span class="encs">[sincere]</span>。</p>
