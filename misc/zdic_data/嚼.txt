<p>● <strong>嚼</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˊ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 用牙齿咬碎：细～慢咽。味同～蜡。</p>
<ol>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>

<p><span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄝˊ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 义同（一），用于某些复合词：咀～。</p>

</ol>

<p>　◎ 〔倒（<span class="dicpy">dǎo</span>）～〕反刍，牛等动物把粗粗咀嚼后咽下去的食物再反回到嘴里细细咀嚼。</p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">嚼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>嚼</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，爵声。本义:以牙磨碎食物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chew]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嚼，削也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嚼，噬嚼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嚼蜡(多指文章或讲话没有滋味，没有意思);嚼吞(咀嚼吞食);嚼咽(咀嚼吞咽);嚼头(经得起咀嚼的厚味(多用比喻义))</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 吃 <span class="encs">[eat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:嚼作(吃喝);嚼食(吃东西，吃喝);嚼啜(吃喝);嚼蔬(吃蔬菜度日，形容饮食粗劣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 剥蚀、蛀蚀、冲蚀或冰川侵蚀 <span class="encs">[erode]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水嚼沙洲树出根。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">真山民</span><span class="smcs">《朱溪涧》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 干杯 <span class="encs">[drink a toast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复嚼者，京都饮酒相强之辞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《续汉书&middot;五行志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">解姊子负解之势，与人饮，使之嚼。非其任疆必灌之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
