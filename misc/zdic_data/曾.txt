                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">曾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>曾</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>指与自己中间隔两代的亲属：～祖父。～孙。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/增">增</a>&rdquo;，增加。</li>
	<li>竟，简直，还（<span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i </span>）：&ldquo;以君之力～不能损魁父之丘，如太山、王屋何？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>曾</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">c&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>尝，表示从前经历过：～经。未～。何～。～几何时。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/层">层</a>&rdquo;，重（<span class="dicpy">chǒng </span>）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> already; sign of past</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> früher einmal  (Adj)​,schon einmal  (Adj)​,jemals</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> arrière-grand-parent,arrière-petit-enfant,jadis,autrefois,une fois</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">曾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>曾</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从八，从曰。本义未明。副词。用来加强语气)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 过去发生过&mdash;&mdash;表示有过某些行为或情况 <span class="encs">[once]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江南好，风景旧曾谙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《忆江南》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斜阳草树，寻常巷陌，人道寄奴曾住。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐&middot;京口北固亭怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曾不(不曾);几年前我曾见过他一面;我曾去过杜坪乡</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 已经 <span class="encs">[already]</span>&mdash;&mdash;表示动作行为已经进行。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:似曾相识</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>曾</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;层&rdquo;。重叠 <span class="encs">[overlapped]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有虞之王，烧曾薮，斩群害，以为民利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;轻重戊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大厦曾架，拟于昆仑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荡胸生曾云。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《望岳》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>曾</strong> <span class="dicpy">zēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 重。指中间隔两代的亲属关系 <span class="encs">[relationship between great-grandchildren and great-grandparents]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自此而曾焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曾元时不分。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">计高曾之时。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视高曾之时。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">高曾时为一户者。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曾翁(称他人的曾祖父);曾玄(曾孙和玄孙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 谦词。犹&ldquo;末&rdquo; <span class="encs">[I]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曾臣</span><span class="diczx2">彪</span>将率诸侯以讨焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曾臣(末臣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 高举的样子 <span class="encs">[high]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[凤皇]</span>曾逝万仞之上。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘安</span><span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;览冥》</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>曾</strong> <span class="dicpy">zēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曾思(反复思考);曾折(曾挠。屈曲貌);曾益(增加，增强);曾巢(在高物上架巢);曾逝(高飞远去)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/66FE.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】昨稜切【集韻】【韻會】徂稜切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音層。【說文】詞之舒也。从八从曰<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/211A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡆧</span>聲。【九經字樣】曾从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/211A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡆧</span>。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/211A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡆧</span>，古文窻字。下从曰，上从八，象氣之分散也。經典相承，隷省作曾。【詩&middot;大雅】曾莫惠我師。【論語】曾是以爲孝乎。【孟子】爾何曾比予於是。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】經也。【增韻】嘗也。【韻會】乃也，則也。</p>
<p>又與層通。【後漢&middot;張衡傳】登閬風之曾城兮。【文選】作層城。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】作滕切【集韻】【韻會】咨騰切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音增。【書&middot;武成】惟有道曾孫周王發。【爾雅&middot;釋親】王父之考爲曾祖，孫之子爲曾孫。【註】曾，猶重也。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;襄十八年】曾臣彪將率諸侯以討焉。【註】曾臣，猶末臣。【疏】曾祖曾孫者，曾爲重義。諸侯之於天子，無所可重。曾臣猶末臣，謙<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>之意耳。</p>
<p>又【楚辭&middot;九歌】翾飛兮翠曾。【註】曾，舉也。</p>
<p>又與橧同。【禮&middot;禮運】夏則居橧巢。【釋文】橧，本又作曾。</p>
<p>又與增同。【孟子】曾益其所不能。【孫奭&middot;音義】曾當讀作增。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓氏急就篇】曾氏出於鄫，姒姓，莒滅鄫，子孫在魯者別爲曾氏。【孫奕&middot;示兒編】曾字除人姓及曾孫外，今學者皆作層字音讀。然經史<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>無音，止當音增。【韻會】今詳曾字有音者，合從本音。餘無音者從層音，亦通。</p>
