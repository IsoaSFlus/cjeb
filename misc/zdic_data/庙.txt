                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>庙</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">廟</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">miào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miào.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄠˋ</span></p><ol><li>供奉祖先的房屋：家～。宗～。太～。</li><li>供神佛或历史上有名人物的地方：孔～。岳～。寺～。</li><li>王宫的前殿，泛指朝廷：～堂。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>庙</strong></p><p>廟 <span class="dicpy">miào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，朝声。<span class="yhcs">“广”</span>与建筑物有关。本义:宗庙，供奉祭祀祖先的处所)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[temple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">廟，尊先祖貌也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，当作宗庙尊先祖貌也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庙祧坛墠，鬼祭先祖也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释天》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">室有东西厢曰庙，无曰寝。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释宫》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寝庙必备。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“前曰庙，后曰寝。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">复于小庙。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·祭仆》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“高祖以下也。始祖曰大庙。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太庙，天子明堂。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·明堂位》</span>。按，周制:天子七庙，…诸侯五庙，大夫三庙，士一庙。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庙成。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一夫作难而七庙堕。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庙在山之西蔍，额曰<span class="yhcs">“惠济”</span>。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至八月朝，</span><span class="diczx2">诣</span>庙中坐。——<span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">藏之于庙。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《伶官传》</span>序</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:少年告庙;庙视道士(庙中管香火的道人);庙主(庙中的牌位;总管全庙事务的僧道);庙见(新妇谒见祖庙);庙祝(管庙里香火的人);庙貌(宗庙中供奉的祖先像);庙堂(宗庙);庙社(宗庙和社稷)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古时供奉神佛的处所 <span class="encs">[temple;shrine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">林教头</span>风雪山神庙。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庙在山之西。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:庙有小姑像;小艇游庙中;方立庙门;土地庙;城隍庙</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古时供奉祭祀历史上有名人物的处所 <span class="encs">[temple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诏为</span><span class="diczx2">亮</span>立庙于 <span class="diczx2">沔阳</span>。——<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:文庙(孔庙);武庙(关公庙);岳庙</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> <span class="yhcs">“朝廷”</span>的代称 <span class="encs">[royal or imperial court]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">居庙堂之高，则忧其民。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5E99.svg">						  <p>【字彙】俗廟字。</p><p>（廟）〔古文〕庿【唐韻】【廣韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>眉召切，苗去聲。【說文】尊先祖貌也。【古今注】廟者，貌也，所以仿佛先人之形容也。【釋名】先祖形貌所在也。【玉篇】宗廟也。【爾雅·釋宮】室有東西廂曰廟。【註】夾室前堂。【疏】凡大室有東西廂夾室及前堂有序牆者曰廟。</p><p>又【六書故】宮前曰廟，後曰寢。今王宮之前殿，士大夫之廳事，是也。虞箴曰：民有寢廟。傳曰：夫鼠不穴寢廟，畏人故也。猶後世言廟朝，廟堂也。【文中子·禮學篇】山澤有廊廟之志。</p><p>又凡祠外神者亦曰廟。【史記·封禪書】趙人新垣平，以望氣見上，言長安東北有神氣，成五采，於是作渭陽五帝廟。</p>
