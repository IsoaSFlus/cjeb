                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>缓</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">緩</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄢˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>慢，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">急</span>&rdquo;相对：～步。～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）。～慢。迟～。</li>	<li>延迟：～刑。～办。～役。～征。刻不容～。</li>	<li>放松，松弛：～和。～冲。～解（<span class="dicpy">jiě</span>）。和～。</li>	<li>苏醒，恢复：～气。～醒。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> slow, leisurely; to postpone, delay</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> faul, träge ,verschieben, aufschieben</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> retarder,se rétablir,modérer,lent,doux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缓</strong></p>
<p>緩 <span class="dicpy">huǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，爰( <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span>)声。本义:宽松;宽大)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[relaxed;be not tense]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缓带(放宽衣带。从容自在的样子);缓服(宽松的服装。对戎装而言);缓带轻裘(宽松的衣带，轻暖的皮衣。形容从容儒雅的风度)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 慢;不急迫。与&ldquo;急&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[slow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缓，舒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫人于事己者过急，于事人者过缓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;卫策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民事不可缓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缓缓(缓慢的样子。同徐徐);缓步代车(慢步行走以代替乘车。同安步当车);缓辔而行(信马由缰地慢走)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 柔软 <span class="encs">[soft]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缓土(柔软而松散的土壤);缓歌慢舞(悠柔美妙的歌声和舞步)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 不陡<span class="encs">[峭]</span> <span class="encs">[gentle]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缓坡</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 软弱;柔弱 <span class="encs">[weak]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缓箭(去势微弱之箭);缓声(柔缓的音乐和歌声)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缓</strong></p>
<p>緩 <span class="dicpy">huǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 延期，延迟 <span class="encs">[delay;postpone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今夫缓于事己者，安能急于事人?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;卫策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子以议狱缓死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;中孚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缓死(宽缓减免死刑;延长寿命);缓召(军事用语。应受动员召集或临时召集的后备军人以及国民兵，因故延缓征召的，称为缓召);缓决(法律名词，对已宣判死刑的犯人，依据特定的状况，于一定时期内暂缓处决)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 下垂 <span class="encs">[hang]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缓耳(缓弛下垂之耳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 苏醒，复活 <span class="encs">[recuperate;revive]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缓气;缓醒</span></p>
