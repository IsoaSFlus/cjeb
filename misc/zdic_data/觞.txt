                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">觞</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>觞</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">觴</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄤˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代酒器：举～称贺。～酌。</li>
	<li>欢饮，进酒：～饮。～咏（饮酒赋诗）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wine vessel; propose toast; feast</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ergötzen, feiern</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> festin,coupe de vin</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">觞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>觞</strong></p>
<p>觴 <span class="dicpy">shāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从角，傷( <span class="dicpy">shāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāng.mp3"></a></span> )省声。金文从&ldquo;爵&rdquo;。本义:古代盛酒器) 同本义 <span class="encs">[drinking vessel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觞，爵实曰觞，虚曰觯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请行觞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;投壶》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">引壶觞以自酌，眄庭柯以怡颜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:觞勺(指觞与勺。都是盛酒器);觞令(即酒令。今俗谓猜拳、划拳);觞豆(酒器与笾豆。皆为饮食的用具。借喻饮食);觞爵(酒器);觞觥(泛指酒器);觞酌(饮酒器)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>觞</strong></p>
<p>觴 <span class="dicpy">shāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 向人敬酒 <span class="encs">[toast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">管仲</span>觞 <span class="diczx2">桓公</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觞酒豆肉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王觞将军。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚王</span>觞之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;徐无鬼》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:觞客(宴飨宾客);觞奠(酹酒祭奠);觞祝(敬酒祝愿);觞行(行觞，传杯)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 饮，喝 <span class="encs">[drink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一觞一咏，亦足以畅叙幽情。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王羲之</span><span class="smcs">《兰亭集序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:觞歌(饮酒放歌);觞弊(饮酒时以为笑乐);觞燕(酒宴;饮宴);觞饮(执杯而饮);觞次(宴饮之所)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 宴请 <span class="encs">[fete;entertain at a banquet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">楚襄王</span>]</span>谓 <span class="diczx2">宋玉</span>曰:&ldquo;寡人欲觞群臣，何以娱之?&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">傅毅</span><span class="smcs">《舞赋》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">觴</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“觞”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/觴">觴</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=角" target="_blank">角</a>】
                              觴·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/89F4.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】式陽切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】尸羊切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音商。【說文】觶實曰觴，虛曰觶。【韻會】酒巵總名。【三禮圖】凡諸觴形皆同，升數則異。【詩&middot;周南&middot;卷耳疏】一升曰爵，二升曰觚，三升曰觶，四升曰角，五升曰散，總名曰爵，其實曰觴。觴者，餉也。【左傳&middot;成二年】奉觴加璧以進。【前漢&middot;外戚傳】酌羽觴兮銷憂。【孟康曰】羽觴，爵也。作生爵形，有頭、尾、羽翼。</p>
<p>又飮人以酒亦曰觴。【左傳&middot;襄二十三年】觴曲沃人。【戰國策】張儀曰：願王賜之觴。王曰：諾。乃觴之。</p>
<p>又濫觴。【家語】江始出于岷山，其源可以濫觴。【王肅註】觴，可以盛酒，言其微也。是濫觴謂始出之微。【唐明皇&middot;孝經序】濫觴於漢，蓋用此義。近世有指爲末流之弊者，誤。</p>
<p>又觴深，淵名。【莊子&middot;達生篇】吾嘗濟乎觴深之淵。　籀文作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2377B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣝻</span>。【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/288A9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨢩</span>。</p>
