                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>歉</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从欠，兼声。本义:吃不饱)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[have nothing to eat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歉，食不满也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">健儿立霜雪，腹歉衣裳单。——</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《行次西郊作诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 收成不好 <span class="encs">[bad harvest;crop failure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一谷不升曰歉。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:歉荒(歉收;灾荒);歉俭(荒歉);歉薄(收成微薄)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 贫困 <span class="encs">[poor]</span>。如:歉弊(贫困);歉褊(贫困，不宽裕);歉迫(困厄，窘迫)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 惭愧。对不住人的心情 <span class="encs">[apology;regret;fell ashamed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俯仰谬恩方自歉。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《酬吴季野见寄》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:抱歉(心中不安，觉着对不住别人);道歉;歉惜(遗憾与惋惜)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 少;不足 <span class="encs">[few]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歉，少也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主信爱之则谨慎而歉。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·仲尼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:歉绌(短缺、不足)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 坏的，歹的 <span class="encs">[bad]</span>。如:歉人(坏人)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“谦”</span>。谦逊 <span class="encs">[modest;humble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主信爱之则谨慎而歉。——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6B49.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>苦簟切，音嗛。【說文】食不滿也。【博雅】少也。【宋書·明帝詔】歲久不登，公私歉敝。【文中子·立命篇】仁生於歉，義生於豐。</p><p>又【玉篇】恨不出也。</p><p>又【廣韻】苦減切【集韻】口減切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音槏。</p><p>又【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>口陷切，音䫡。義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。【博雅】貪也。一曰歉喙。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>詰念切，音傔。義同。【韓愈·喜侯喜至詩】孟生去雖索，侯氏來還歉。叶上店、驗，下豔、贍。【正字通】經傳子史，歉嗛傔謙慊愜嫌，差互各出，無定詁。《孟子》吾何慊乎哉，《穀梁傳》一穀不升謂之嗛，則以慊、嗛爲歉。《大學》此之謂自謙，則以謙爲慊。《漢書·文帝詔》人民未有㥦志，《史記》作嗛志，則以嗛爲㥦。《相如·封禪書》陛下嗛讓弗發，則又以嗛爲謙。《孟子》行有不慊於心則餒，則又以慊爲愜。《呂覽·知士篇》可以傔齊貌辨者吾無辭爲也，則又以傔爲慊。《荀子》信而不處謙，則又以謙爲嫌。諸如此類，皆傳寫錯亂，義難强通。</p>
