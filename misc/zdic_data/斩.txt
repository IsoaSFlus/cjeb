<p>● <strong>斩</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">斬</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>砍断：～断。～首。～决。～除。～草除根。～钉截铁。先～后奏。披荆～棘。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">斩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>斩</strong></p>
<p>斬 <span class="dicpy">zhǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从车，从斤。古有&ldquo;车裂&rdquo;之刑，故从车。斤，斧子，可用于杀人。本义:古代死刑的一种。斩首或腰斩;杀)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[behead;decapitate;chop sb.&#39;s head off;chop;kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斩，截也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斩，杀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国既卒斩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;节南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斫头曰斩，斩要曰要斩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释丧制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌斩杀贼谍而膊之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;秋官&middot;掌戮》</span>。注:&ldquo;斩以斧钺，若今之要斩;杀以刀刃，若今之弃市。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不从令者斩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;备城门》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">法皆斩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">失期当斩。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">借第令毋斩。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀虎斩蛟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;自新》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斩使(斩杀来使);斩棺戮尸(劈开棺木，刺杀尸体);斩立决(处斩刑决不待时);斩馘(杀头并取左耳，以计功);问斩(斩首);斩首</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 砍;砍断 <span class="encs">[chop;cut down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斩荆棘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斩刈杀伐之际。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斩伐(砍伐);斩祀(砍伐神祠近旁的树);斩祛(斩断其袖。借指旧怨);斩绝(斩断;断绝);斩断(砍断;切断);斩关(砍断门闩，泛指攻破城门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 断绝 <span class="encs">[break off;dissociate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>祀不斩，皆出将军之赐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斩夫之祀(断绝夫家的祭祀);斩了(断了;绝了)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 剪裁，特指丧服不缉下边 <span class="encs">[tailor; cut out a garment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">晏桓子</span>卒， <span class="diczx2">晏婴</span>粗缳，斩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十七年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斩衰(粗麻布丧服，不缝边，是五种丧服中最重的，服期三年)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用同&ldquo;眨&rdquo;。眼睛一睁一闭 <span class="encs">[wink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还斩眼动鳞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斩眼(眨眼);斩眉多梭眼(挤眉弄眼)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>斩</strong></p>
<p>斬 <span class="dicpy">zhǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p> 非常;特别 <span class="encs">[very;special]</span></p>
