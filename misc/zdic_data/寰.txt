						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>寰</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄢˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>广大的地域：人～。瀛～（五洲四海）。～海。～球。～宇（亦作&ldquo;环宇&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>古指距京都千里以内的地区，京畿：&ldquo;～内诸侯，非有天子之命，不得出会诸侯&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>寰</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀 <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span>，睘( <span class="dicpy">qi&oacute;ng</span>)声。本义:王畿，古代帝王京城周围千里以内的地方)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[imperial domain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寰，王者封畿内县也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 广大的境域或领域 <span class="encs">[extensive region;a large domain]</span>。如:寰土(疆土);寰域(范围，区域);寰瀛(指疆域)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宇宙;天下 <span class="encs">[world]</span>。如:寰中(宇内，天下);寰内(古谓帝京周围千里之内。后引申指天下);寰县(宇内，天下);寰区(天下;人世间)</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5BF0.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】戸關切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】胡關切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音環。【說文】天子封畿內縣也。</p>

<p>又【廣韻】元甸切【集韻】【類篇】熒絹切。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同縣。【穀梁傳&middot;隱元年】寰內諸侯。【註】寰卽古縣字。</p>
<p>又叶胡官切，音完。【白居易&middot;悟眞寺詩】聞名不可到，處所非人寰。</p>
<p>又有一片石，大如方尺甎。　【說文】本作寰。寰字說文寰，从宀瞏聲。</p>
