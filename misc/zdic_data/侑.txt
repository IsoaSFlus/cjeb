						<p>● <strong>侑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>相助。</li>	<li>在筵席旁助兴，劝人吃喝：～食。～饮。～觞。</li>	<li>报答。</li>	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/宥">宥</a>&rdquo;，宽赦。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">侑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>侑</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，有声。本义:劝食)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[urge sb. to drink or wait on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以为酒食，以享以祀，以妥以侑，以介景福。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:侑劝(劝人吃喝);侑食(劝食，侍奉尊长进食);侑酒(劝酒;为饮酒者助兴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 报答;酬答 <span class="encs">[repay]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:侑缄(附带酬答);侑柬(酬赠礼品);侑酬(酬答)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 配享;从祀 <span class="encs">[accompany and enjoy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少师佐成祖，为靖难首勋，侑享太庙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《帝京景物略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:侑祠(配享，以后死的功臣、贤哲附祭于祖庙);侑饷(配享);侑坐(配坐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 助，佐助 <span class="encs">[help;liven things up]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:侑酒之欢(陪侍进酒的欢乐);侑宴(为宴饮者助兴);侑宾(古代宴命，主人为示殷勤，用财物赠客，以助食兴)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">侑</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>侑</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄧㄡˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>y&ograve;u <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/6058.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">勸人飲食。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>侑</b>酒」、「<b>侑</b>宴」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》：「以為酒食，以享以祀，以妥以<b>侑</b>，以介景福。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《周禮&middot;天官&middot;膳夫》：「以樂侑食，膳夫授祭品嘗食，王乃食。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">報答。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;歐陽修〈祭尹師魯文〉：「子於眾人，最愛予文，寓辭千里，<b>侑</b>此一樽。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">饒恕、寬恕。</span><span class="gc_yy">《管子&middot;法法》：「文有三<b>侑</b>，武毋一赦。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;禮器》：「周坐尸詔<b>侑</b>武方，其禮亦然，其道一也。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「宥」。</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4F91.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】于救切【集韻】【韻會】尤救切【正韻】爰救切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音又。佐也，相也。【周禮&middot;天官】膳夫以樂侑食。</p><p>又侍食於所尊亦曰侑食。【禮&middot;玉藻】凡侑食，不盡食。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】醻酢侑，報也。【郭註】此通謂相報答，不主於飮酒。</p><p>又【文子&middot;守弱篇】三皇五帝有勸戒之器，命曰侑巵。別作酭。</p><p>又與姷通。耦也。</p><p>又寬也。【管子&middot;法法篇】文有三侑，武無一赦。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/59F7.svg">                          <p>耦也。从女有聲，讀若祐。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/59F7_1.svg" />侑，姷或从人。于救切</p>                          </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/59F7_1253.svg" /><span class="swbf">(姷)</span><span class="ywyy">耦也。</span><span class="swdz">耕有耦者，取相助也。故引伸之，凡相助曰耦，姷之義取乎此。周禮宮正：以樂侑食。鄭曰：侑猶勸也。按勸卽助。左傳：王享醴，命晉侯宥。杜云：旣饗又命晉侯助以束帛。以助釋宥。古經多叚宥爲侑，毛詩則叚右爲之，傳曰：右，勸也。</span><span class="ywyy">从女有聲，讀若祐。</span><span class="swdz">于救切。古音在一部。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/59F7_1254.svg" /><span class="swbf">(侑)</span><span class="ywyy">姷或从人。</span><span class="swdz">今通用此體。</span></p>                          </div>
