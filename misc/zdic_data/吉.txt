                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>吉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>好，有利的，幸福的，与“<span class="diczx3">凶</span>”相对：～利。～祥。逢凶化～。～光片羽（喻残存的珍贵的文物）。</li><li>吉利的日子：择～。</li><li>善，贤，美：～人（善良，有才德的人）。～人天相。</li><li>中国吉林省的简称：～剧。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">吉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>吉</strong> <span class="dicpy">jí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上象兵器，下象盛放兵器的器具。合起来表示把兵器盛放在器中不用，以减少战争，使人民没有危难。本义:吉祥;吉利)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lucky]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吉，善也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">礼义顺祥曰吉。——<span class="smcs">《周书·武顺》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">安贞吉。——<span class="smcs">《易·坤》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吉哉。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">文王</span>当兴，赤雀适来;…吉物动飞而圣遇也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·初禀》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 吉服(古代祭祀时穿的礼服);吉帖(婚帖，庚帖);吉谶(预示吉祥的隐语或图记);吉羊(吉利祥瑞。古代器物上多铭刻<span class="yhcs">“吉羊”</span>二字。羊，古祥字);吉卜(吉利的卜兆);吉幸(吉利幸运);吉征(幸运的征兆)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 善;美 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吉事先近日。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令月、吉日。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吉礼大祝。三曰吉祝，五曰吉拜。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吉人之辞寡。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吉日兮辰良。——</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《东皇太一》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 吉士(古时对男子的美称。引申指大华优美之人);吉灵即溜(形容身段灵活);吉吉抖抖(弯曲成串的样子)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>吉</strong> <span class="dicpy">jí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 朔日 <span class="encs">[the first day of the lunar month]</span>。如:吉月(农历每月初一)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 婚礼，通常包括伴随的庆祝活动 <span class="encs">[wedding]</span>。如:吉席(婚礼);吉期</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代祭祀鬼神的礼仪。为五礼(吉、凶、宾、军、嘉)之一 <span class="encs">[sacrificial rites]</span>。如:吉巳(古礼，皇后于每年季春三月之巳日躬亲蚕桑之事，其日卜须吉，故称<span class="yhcs">“吉巳”</span>);吉典(吉礼之仪典);吉服(古祭祀时所著之服)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Ji prefecture]</span>。隋置。唐、五代、宋、清因之。在今江西省吉安市</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 象声词。如:吉蹬蹬(马蹄声);吉丁(金属玉器清脆的碰击声);吉丁当(金属玉器等物碰击或折断声);吉丁丁珰(器物撞击的响声);吉丢古堆(形容波涛汹涌澎湃的声音);吉丁疙疸(坑洼不平的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
