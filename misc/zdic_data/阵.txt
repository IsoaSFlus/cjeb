又如:阵阵赢(仗仗取胜);叫阵;怯阵;出阵;阵败(战败);阵率(战士)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 雷阵雨来临前乌云翻滚、雷电交加的现象 <span class="encs">[lightning accompanied by peals of thunder]</span></p><p><span i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhèn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">动</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 布阵，列阵 <span class="encs">[display battle array]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阵而后战，兵法之常，运用之妙，存乎一心。——<span class="smcs">《宋史·岳飞传》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>阵</strong></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 陣 <span class="dicpy">zhèn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhèn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">量</span></p><p><span
