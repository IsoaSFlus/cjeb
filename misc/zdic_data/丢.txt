						<p>● <strong>丢</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">diū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄡˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>失去，遗落：～失。～了工作。～盔卸甲。</li>
	<li>扔；抛弃：不要乱～垃圾。</li>
	<li>搁置；放：工作～下不管。技术～久了就生疏了。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丢</strong> <span class="dicpy">diū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 丢失;遗失 <span class="encs">[lose;mislay]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丢脱(丧失);丢帽落鞋(形容紧急匆忙的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 抛弃;撇开 <span class="encs">[throw]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丢却(抛弃;除却;舍去);丢搭(糟蹋;抛弃;放开);丢下笆儿弄扫帚(形容有做不完的事情)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用眼色暗示 <span class="encs">[wink]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丢风撒脚(卖弄撒野)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 搁置 <span class="encs">[lay aside]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丢番(放倒);技术丢久了就生疏了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 遗留 <span class="encs">[leave over]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我一生是个无用的人，一块土也不曾丢给你们。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 使出;施展 <span class="encs">[display]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丢门户(摆开架势);丢开解数(放开手脚;使出手段解数:架势;花招);丢抹(打扮;又作&ldquo;丢丢抹抹&rdquo;，&ldquo;抹丢&rdquo;。指羞臊)</span></p>
