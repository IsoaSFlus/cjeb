                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>校</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>学堂，专门进行教育的机构：～园。～长。</li>
	<li>军衔的一级，在&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">将</span>&rdquo;之下，&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">尉</span>&rdquo;之上。</li>
	<li>古代军队编制单位：～尉（统带一校的军官）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>校</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>比较：～场。</li>
	<li>查对、订正：～勘。～订。～对。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">校</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>校</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代刑具。枷械的统称 <span class="encs">[fetters]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">校，木囚也。从木，交声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屦校灭趾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;噬嗑》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何校灭耳。注:&ldquo;若今枷项也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大校重牢，五木被体。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 栅栏 <span class="encs">[railing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子校猎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传》</span>。注:&ldquo;以木相贯穿，总为栏校，遮止禽兽而猎取之。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:校猎(用木栏遮阻而猎取禽兽。校:木栏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 校样 <span class="encs">[proof]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:一校;二校</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> &ldquo;校人&rdquo;的省称。养马官 <span class="encs">[horsekeeper official taking charge of horsekeep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六厩成校。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;校人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">校正属焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十八年》</span>。注:&ldquo;主马官。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:校人(周礼官名。马官之长)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代军营的一种建制。亦指&ldquo;军营&rdquo; <span class="encs">[barrack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常护军傅校获王。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;卫青传》</span>。注:&ldquo;校者，营垒之称。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五校大夫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;中山策》</span>。注:&ldquo;军营也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:校队(古时军队的编制单位。亦指部队，队伍);校联(谓营垒相联);校骑(骑兵队伍);校垒(营垒)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6821_2767.svg" /><span class="swbf">(校)</span><span class="ywyy">木囚也。</span><span class="swdz">囚，繫也。本囚者，以木羈之也。易曰：屨校滅趾，何校滅耳。屨校，若今軍流犯人新到箸木鞾。何校，若今犯人帶枷也。此字似當與下文械杽等篆爲伍矣。周禮校人注曰：校之言挍也。主馬者必仍挍視之。挍人，馬官之長。按此引伸假借之義也。陸德明曰：比挍字當從手㫄。張參五經文字手部曰：挍，經典及釋文或以爲比挍字。案字書無文。張語正謂說文無從手之挍也。故唐石經考挍字皆從木，用張說也。但訂以周禮鄭注，則漢時固有從手之挍矣。比挍字，古葢無正文。較榷等皆可用。</span><span class="ywyy">從木交聲。</span><span class="swdz">古孝切。二部。</span></p>                          </div>
