                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">滞</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>滞</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">滯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>凝积，不流通，不灵活：停～。～留。～销。～空。呆～。～产（产妇临产后总产程超过30小时者）。～针。～下（痢疾的古称）。</li>
	<li>遗落：&ldquo;此有～穗&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> block up, obstruct; stagnant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> blockieren, verhindern, stauen, stagnieren  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> stagner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">滞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>滞</strong></p>
<p>滯 <span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，带声。本义:水流不畅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stagnant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滞，凝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡珍异之有滞者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;廛人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淹回水而凝滞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">流而不滞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;时则训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 积压。长期积存，未作处理 <span class="encs">[overstock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敛市之不售，货之滞于民用者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;泉府》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚书省滞讼不决者，诏征平治。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:滞狱(指因积压或拖延未予审决的案件);滞讼(积压的讼案);滞积(积压);滞货(积压的货物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 停止;阻塞;静止 <span class="encs">[stop;be motionless;be at a standstill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是故能天运地滞，轮转而无废。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:滞用(停止运用);滞淹(闭塞，不通畅);滞越(阻塞);滞著(阻塞;不能宣泄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 郁积 <span class="encs">[smolder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气不沈滞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span>。注:&ldquo;积也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得此佳作，终日喜快，滞闷冰释。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《与王定国书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:滞念(凝结在心中的思念);滞思，滞想(凝聚心头的想念);滞怒(积于心中的愤怒);滞气(郁积之气);滞愤(积愤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 凝聚，积聚 <span class="encs">[condense;congeal;coagulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戚戚多滞念，置酒宴所欢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《拟青青陵上柏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:滞念(心中凝结的思念);滞血(淤积的血液);滞底(凝集)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 陷入;沉抑 <span class="encs">[land oneself in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿方当富贵，必不容久滞屈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:滞羽(喻陷于困境不能施展才能的人);滞抑(受压制);滞屈(贤人沉抑，不得升进);滞淹(人沉抑于下而不得升进)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 暂时停留于某处;逗留 <span class="encs">[stay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几宵因月滞</span><span class="diczx2">三湘</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">姚鹄</span><span class="smcs">《送黄颇归袁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:滞淫(长期旷废;长久停留;久雨不停);滞游(久游未归);滞客(久处下位而未得升迁的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 遗落;遗漏 <span class="encs">[leave over]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 久，长期 <span class="encs">[long-term]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:滞病(久病);滞疾(久病);滞役(长期供职);滞耻(久未洗雪的耻辱)</span></p>
