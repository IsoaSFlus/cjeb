                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">玄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>玄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>深奥不容易理解的：～妙。～奥。～理（① 深奥的道理； ② 魏晋玄学所标榜的道理）。～学。～秘。</li>
	<li>虚伪，不真实，不可靠：～想。～虚。</li>
	<li>黑色：～青（深黑色）。～武（① 道教所奉的北方之神，它的形象为龟蛇合体； ② 二十八宿中北方七宿的合称）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> deep, profound, abstruse; KangXi radical 95</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> mysteriös  (Adj)​,schwarz, düster</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mystérieux,obscur,incroyable</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">玄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>玄</strong> <span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字，下端象单绞的丝，上端是丝绞上的系带，表示作染丝用的丝结。本义:赤黑色，黑中带红)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[red black]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黑而有亦色者为玄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五入为緅，七入为缁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;钟氏》</span>。注:&ldquo;凡玄色者，在緅缁之间，其六入者与。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏纁玄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;染人》</span>。注:&ldquo;玄纁者，天地之色。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">八月载绩，载玄载黄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天玄地黄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">篚厥玄黄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我马玄黄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;卷耳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又泛指黑色 <span class="encs">[black]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玄，黑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">徐州</span>]</span>厥篚玄纤缟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:&ldquo;玄，黑缯。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何草不玄，何人不矜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;何草不黄》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奏之，有玄云从西北方起。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;十过》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄禽(燕子);玄霜(传说中的仙药);玄岫场谷(青山翠谷);玄霄(黑云);玄厉(黑色磨刀石);玄祺(浅黑色旗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为黑暗 <span class="encs">[dark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遗思在玄夜，相与复翱翔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘桢</span><span class="smcs">《公宴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄夜(黑夜);玄窗(阴暗的深坑);玄室(暗室)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 深;厚 <span class="encs">[deep;thick]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临</span><span class="diczx2">沅</span><span class="diczx2">湘</span>之玄渊兮，遂自忍而沈流。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄冰(厚冰);玄包(深藏);玄渊(深渊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 神妙难捉摸;深奥 <span class="encs">[mystic;profound;abstruse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">难!难!难!道最玄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玄之又玄，众妙之门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>第一章</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">异物名实玄纽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学遍书部，特善玄言。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《齐故安陆昭王碑文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄机(神秘的先兆);玄关(佛教称入道的法门);玄术(神秘的法术);玄策(神妙的谋略);玄语(不切实际的话);玄谈(脱离实际的空谈);玄寂(玄虚寂静)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 假借为&ldquo;远&rdquo;或为&ldquo;原&rdquo; <span class="encs">[far]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玄，幽远也。象幽而入覆之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玄古之君天下，无为也，天德而已矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高</span>注:&ldquo;淮南子曰:天也。圣经不言玄妙，至伪尚书有玄德升间之语。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄古(远古);玄睹(远见)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 奇特 <span class="encs">[peculiar]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这个人长的样样都大，大得真玄</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 寂静;清静 <span class="encs">[quiet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天道玄默，无容无则。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄谟(清静无为);玄静(清静无为的思想境界);玄嘿(寂静无为)</span></p>

<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 天，天空 <span class="encs">[sky]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玄，天也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天以不见为玄。地以不形为玄，人以心腹为玄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;玄告》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共玄官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;幼官》</span>。注:&ldquo;主礼天之官也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄都(神仙的居处);玄穹帝(即天帝);玄极(天顶，天空);玄贶(上天的赏赐);玄浑(天)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 道家的学说;道教 <span class="encs">[Taoism]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自中朝贵玄，江左称盛，因谈余气，流成文体。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄门(道教);玄功(道家修道的功夫);玄玄道(道家及其深奥、神妙的教义)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 农历九月的别称 <span class="encs">[the ninth moon of the lunar calendar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至于玄月，王召</span><span class="diczx2">范</span><span class="diczx2">蠡</span>而问焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;越语下》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 玄孙的省称 <span class="encs">[great-great-grandson]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曾孙之子为玄孙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 北方 <span class="encs">[north]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以处玄宫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">陆德明</span>释文:&ldquo;玄宫， <span class="diczx2">李</span>云北方宫也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玄方(北方);玄郊(北郊);玄海(北方之海);玄朔(北方);玄帝(北方之帝)</span></p>

												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四">卷四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四&amp;swbs=玄">玄</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7384.svg">                          <p>幽遠也。黑而有赤色者爲玄。象幽而入覆之也。凡玄之屬皆从玄。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20D53.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠵓</span>，古文玄。胡涓切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7384_3885.svg" /><span class="swbf">(玄)</span><span class="ywyy">幽遠也。</span><span class="swdz">老子曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>之又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>，衆妙之門。高注淮南子曰：天也。聖經不言<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>妙，至僞尚書乃有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>德升聞之語。</span><span class="ywyy">象幽。</span><span class="swdz">謂幺也。小則隱。</span><span class="ywyy">而亠覆之也。</span><span class="swdz">幽遠之意。胡涓切。十二部。</span><span class="ywyy">黑而有赤色者爲爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>。</span><span class="swdz">此别一義也。凡染，一入謂之縓，再入謂之赬，三入謂之纁，五入爲緅，七入爲緇，而朱與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>，周禮、爾雅無明文。鄭注儀禮曰：朱則四入與。注周禮曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>色者，在緅、緇之閒，其六入者與。按纁染以黑則爲緅。緅，漢時今文禮作爵，言如爵頭色也，許書作纔。纔旣微黑，又染則更黑，而赤尚隱隱可見也，故曰黑而有赤色。至七入則赤不見矣。緇與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>通偁，故禮家謂緇布衣爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>端。</span><span class="ywyy">凡玄之屬皆从玄。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7384_3886.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221AF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢆯</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文。</span></p>                          </div>
