                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>津</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>渡水的地方：～渡。关～。～要。问～。</li>
	<li>口液，唾液：～液。～～有味。</li>
	<li>汗：遍体生～。</li>
	<li>滋润，补：～润。～贴。</li>
	<li>中国天津市的简称。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">津</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>津</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，从舟，从淮。&ldquo;淮&rdquo;表示淮水。泛指一般的河流。船停泊在河旁，用来渡河。本义:渡口)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ferry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">津，水渡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又东至于孟津。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自</span><span class="diczx2">黄河</span>泛舟而渡者，皆为津也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水津注&middot;河水》</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风烟望五津。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《杜少府之任蜀州》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>过之，使 <span class="diczx2">子路</span>问津焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;微子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:津梁(渡口的桥梁);津人(在渡口以摆渡为生的人);津门(在河流渡口所设置的关隘);津吏(掌管桥梁及河流渡口的官吏);津主(在关卡或渡口检查商旅货物的官吏);津要(渡口要地。比喻险要的交通据点);津逗(渡水口);津逮(经渡口过河而到达目的地。后常用以比喻为学的门径);津亭驿馆(渡口和驿站的亭馆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 生物的津液;口水 <span class="encs">[saliva]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">津，液也，汁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">津液充郭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;汤液胶醴论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令人望梅生津。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆佃</span><span class="smcs">《埤稚&middot;芥》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:津沫(口中津液;涎沫);津唾(唾液)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 水陆要隘 <span class="encs">[communications hub]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:津通(水路通达。即流通);津隘(关津要隘)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 途径;门径 <span class="encs">[way]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:津路(途径);津涂(亦作&ldquo;津途&rdquo;);津径(门径)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 涯，岸 <span class="encs">[bank]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日出九津。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;求人》</span>。注:&ldquo;崖也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:津岸(涯岸;水边);津涯(岸;水边);津步(码头)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 水 <span class="encs">[water]</span>。</p>
