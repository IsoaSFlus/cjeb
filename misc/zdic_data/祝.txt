                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>祝</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>表示对人对事的美好愿望：～福。～寿。～捷。～辞。馨香祷～。</li><li>古代指男巫。</li><li>在神庙里管香火的人：～融。庙～。</li><li>断：～发（<span class="dicpy">fà</span>）（断发，后指僧尼削发出家）。</li><li>姓。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">注</span>”，敷涂。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">祝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>祝</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhòu</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“咒”</span>。诅咒 <span class="encs">[curse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">否则厥口诅祝。——<span class="smcs">《书·无逸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侯作侯祝。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·荡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民或祝诅上。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孝文纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">南郡</span>极热之地，其人祝树树枯，唾鸟鸟坠。—— <span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:祝诅(祝告鬼神，使加祸别人)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>祝</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象一个人跪在神前拜神、开口祈祷。从示，从儿口。<span class="yhcs">“儿”</span>是古文<span class="yhcs">“人”</span>字。本义:男巫，祭祀时主持祝告的人，即庙祝)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wizard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祝，祭主赞词者。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兑为口为巫。——<span class="smcs">《易》</span>。按，祝所以悦神也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祝将命也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·郊特牲》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祝，以孝告。嘏以慈告。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有大祝、小祝、丧祝、甸祝、诅祝。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·夏官》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">逸祝册。——<span class="smcs">《书·洛诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与巫祝共分其余钱持归。——</span><span class="diczx2">褚少孙</span><span class="smcs">《西门豹治邺》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">工祝招君，背行先些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“男巫曰祝”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:祝人(古代掌祭祀的 );巫祝(巫婆师公);祝伯(掌宗庙祭祀之官);祝宗(古代主持祭祀祈祷者);祝官(古代掌管祭祀祝祷等事宜之官);祝嘏(祭祀时致祝祷之辞和传达神言的执事人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 祭神的祈祷词 <span class="encs">[prayers]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皇帝、皇后诣天神、地祗位，致奠;阖门读使读祝讫，复位坐。——<span class="smcs">《辽史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:祝赞(祝辞，祷告鬼神之文);祝文(古代祭祀神鬼或祖先的文辞);祝规(祝词中劝勉的话)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 祝贺 <span class="encs">[congratulation]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犀首跪行，为仪千秋之祝。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>祝</strong> <span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/795D.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25631.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥘱</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>之六切，音粥。贊主人饗神者。【說文】祝，祭主贊詞者，从人口，从示。一曰从兌省。易曰：兌爲口，爲巫。【徐曰】按易，兌，悅也，巫所以悅神也。【詩·小雅】工祝致告。【周禮·春官】大祝掌六視之辭。</p><p>又屬也。【詩·鄘風】素絲祝之。【箋】祝，當作屬。屬，著也。【毛傳】訓織也。</p><p>又祝融，神名。【虞翻曰】祝，大。融，明也。【韋昭曰】祝，始也。</p><p>又斷也。【公羊傳·哀十四年】子路死，子曰噫：天祝予。【穀梁傳·哀十三年】祝髮文身。</p><p>又丁寧也，請求之辭。</p><p>又國名。【禮·樂記】封帝堯之後於祝。</p><p>又姓。【左傳】鄭大夫祝<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25146.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥅆</span>，後漢司徒祝恬。</p><p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>職救切，音晝。【詩·大雅】侯作侯祝。【傳】祝，詛也。【疏】祝。無用牲之文，口告而祝詛之也。【書·無逸】否則厥口詛祝。【疏】以言告神謂之祝，請神加殃謂之詛。</p><p>又陟慮切，音註。【周禮·天官】瘍醫掌祝藥。【註】祝，讀如注病之注，謂附著藥也。　【集韻】或作呪。亦作詋詶。</p><p>
