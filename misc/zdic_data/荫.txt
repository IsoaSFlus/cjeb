                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>荫</strong></p><p>蔭 <span class="dicpy">yìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，阴声。本义:树阴)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shady]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荫，草阴也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荫，草阴地也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵盂</span>视荫。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公元年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“荫日景也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玉足以庇荫嘉谷。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荫不祥之木。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说林》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“荫，木景也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">武王</span>荫暍人于樾下。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·人间》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">树成荫而众鸟息焉。——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:树成荫而众鸟息;垂荫万亩;荫樊(树荫浓密)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 日影 <span class="encs">[shade]</span>。如:荫影(树木等在阳光照射下所投下的阴影)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指地窖或暗室 <span class="encs">[cellar;darkroom]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则一荫之曲豉，尽美而多量。——<span class="smcs">《潜夫论·德化》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:荫室(谓阳光照射不到的屋舍，如窑洞、地窖之类)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>荫</strong></p><p>蔭 <span class="dicpy">yìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 字亦作廕。遮蔽 <span class="encs">[shelter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若君实庇廕膏泽之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">本根无所庇荫矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公七年》</span></span></p><p><span
