                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>洗</strong> <span class="dicpy">xǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，先声。据甲骨文，上为足形，下为水形。本义:用水洗脚)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wash the feet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洗，洒足也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洗去足垢。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·讥日》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王方踞床洗。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·黥布传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“濯足也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">沛公</span>方踞床，使两女子洗足。——<span class="smcs">《史记·高祖本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为洒。用水涤除污垢 <span class="encs">[wash]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水在洗东。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡饮酒礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“承盥洗者，弃水器也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">上闻</span>后宫欢笑，问其故，左右以贵妃三日洗 <span class="diczx2">禄儿</span>对。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐玄宗天宝十载》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晴雪所洗。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:洗头(宋时礼俗。新婚数日后，女家迎女归，并备礼品送女回婿家);洗耳不听(喻厌闻世事);洗钱(洗儿时赐赠的钱);洗泥(即洗尘。置办酒食招待远行而来的客人)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 免去;除去 <span class="encs">[clear out;purify]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">圣人以此洗心。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">思君厚德委如山，洁诚洗志期暮年。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《代白纻舞歌辞》</span></p><p><span
