                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>轫</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">軔</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">rèn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rèn.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>阻止车轮转动的木头，车开动时，则将其抽走：发～（喻事业开始）。</li><li>柔弱，情怠。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">仞</span>”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a block that keeps a wheel from moving</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Abkantpresse, Bremse  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> frein</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>轫</strong></p><p>軔 <span class="dicpy">rèn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rèn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，刃声。本义:支住车轮使其不能转动的木头)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a piece of wood serving as a brake to halt a carriage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轫，碍车也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">车不发轫。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·兵略》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“车下支。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝发轫于苍梧兮，夕吾至乎县圃。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“仞”</span>。古时的一种长度单位 <span class="encs">[an ancient measure of varying lengths]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掘井九轫而不及泉。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 车轮 <span class="encs">[wheel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水渐轫以凝冱。——<span class="smcs">《文选》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李善</span>注引 <span class="dicpy">rèn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rèn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 柔弱;懒散 <span class="encs">[weak;sluggish]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“韧”</span>。柔软而坚固 <span class="encs">[pliable but tough]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡用兵者攻坚则轫。——<span class="smcs">《管子·制分》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>轫</strong></p><p>軔 <span class="dicpy">rèn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
