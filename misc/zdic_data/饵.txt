                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>饵</strong></p><p>餌 <span class="dicpy">ěr</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ěr.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，耳声。本义:糕饼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cake;pastry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饵，粉饼也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鱼胶饵。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·弓人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:果饵(果饼)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 钓鱼用的鱼食 <span class="encs">[bait]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">临川靡芳饵。——<span class="smcs">《文选·傅长虞诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:鱼饵;钓饵</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 药饵 <span class="encs">[bait]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得而猎之以为饵，可以已大风、挛踠、瘘、疠。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:饵治(以药饵治病)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 食物的总称 <span class="encs">[food]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 筋腱 <span class="encs">[tendons]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">捶反铡之，去其饵。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>饵</strong></p><p>餌 <span class="dicpy">ěr</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ěr.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 吞食 <span class="encs">[swallow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">适足以饵大国耳。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·贾谊传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓为其所吞食。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:饵名钓禄(以不正当的手段取得名誉和地位)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 服食;吃 <span class="encs">[eat]</span>。如:饵药(服药);饵丹(服食金丹);饵术(服食苍术)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引诱 <span class="encs">[induce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此所以饵敌，如何去之!——<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:饵诱(利诱);饵兵(诱敌深入的小部队)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=食" target="_blank">食</a>】
                              餌·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/990C.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>忍止切，音耳。【玉篇】食也，餠也，餻也。【說文】粉餠也。【徐鍇曰】釋名，烝燥屑餠之曰餈，非也。粉米烝屑皆餌也，非餈也。【許愼曰】餈稻餠，謂炊米爛乃擣之，不爲粉也。粉餈以豆爲粉糝餈上也，餌則先屑米爲粉，然後溲之。餈之言滋也，餌之言堅潔若玉餌也。【楚辭·招魂】粔籹蜜餌。</p><p>又【禮·內則】糝取牛羊豕之肉，三如一小切之，與稻米，稻米二肉一，合以爲餌煎之。【註】此周禮糝食也。</p><p>又【周禮·冬官考工記·弓人】魚膠餌。【註】色如餌。</p><p>又【禮·內則】擣珍，取牛羊麋鹿麇之肉，必脄，每物與牛若一，捶反側之，去其餌，孰出之，去其皽，柔其肉。【註】餌，筋腱也。</p><p>又啗魚具。【莊子·外物篇】五十犗以爲餌。</p><p>又隂以利誘人曰餌。【前漢·賈誼傳】五餌三表。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】仍吏切【正韻】而至切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音二。義同。　【說文】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22439.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢐹</span>部作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22439.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢐹</span>。重文从食耳聲，作餌。</p><p>
