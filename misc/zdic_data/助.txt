                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>助</strong> <span class="dicpy">chú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“锄”</span>。除去 <span class="encs">[remove]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">颜不疑</span>归，而师 <span class="diczx2">董梧</span>，以助其色。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>助</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，且( <span class="dicpy">zǔ</span>)声。助人要用力，故从力。本义:帮助)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(在物质上或精神上给予协助) <span class="encs">[help]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">助，左也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">助，佐也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非助我者也。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“犹益也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九一而助。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">助天为虐者，不祥。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予助苗长矣。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跳往助之。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">负薪刍助之。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挟矢以助战。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肤觉之助。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span>
