						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>巩</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鞏</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄥˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>坚固，结实，使牢固：～固。～膜。</li>
	<li>用皮革捆东西：&ldquo;～用黄牛之革&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bind; firm, secure, strong</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gong  (Eig, Fam)​,verzurren, fest, sicher, stark</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> consolider</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">巩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>巩</strong></p>
<p>鞏 <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从革，巩声。从&ldquo;革&rdquo;，表示与皮革有关。本义:用皮革捆东西)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fasten with a leather band]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巩，以韦束也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巩用黄牛之革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;革》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无不克鞏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;瞻卬》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 使坚固 <span class="encs">[consolidate]</span>。如:巩固国防</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>巩</strong></p>
<p>鞏 <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 恐惧 <span class="encs">[terrorful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故君子恭而不难，敬而不巩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;君道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:巩巩(恐惧的样子)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>巩</strong></p>
<p>鞏 <span class="dicpy">Gǒng</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 中国河南省县名 <span class="encs">[Gong county]</span>，以在洛水之间，四面皆山，可以巩固，故名，历代因之</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5DE9.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】與<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2201C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢀜</span>同。</p>
<p>（鞏）【唐韻】【正韻】居竦切【集韻】【韻會】古勇切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音拱。【說文】以韋束也。【易&middot;革卦】鞏用黃牛之革。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】鞏，固也。【又】硈，鞏也。【註】硈然堅固。【詩&middot;大雅】藐藐昊天，無不克鞏。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】鞏，火乾也。凡有汁而乾謂之煎，東齊謂之鞏。</p>
<p>又國名。【左傳&middot;昭十五年】闕鞏之甲，武所以克商也。【註】闕鞏國所出鎧。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】縣名。【左傳&middot;昭二十五年】尹文公涉於鞏。【註】於鞏縣涉洛水也。</p>
<p>又羌名。【前漢&middot;趙充國傳】煎鞏，黃羝之屬。【後漢&middot;順帝紀】討鞏唐羌。</p>
<p>又州名。【韻會】唐渭州，宋攺鞏州。</p>
<p>又姓。【左傳&middot;成二年】晉侯使鞏朔獻齊捷於周。</p>
