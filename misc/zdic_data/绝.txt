                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>绝</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">絶</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>断：～种。～缘。～嗣。～情。～迹。断～。杜～。灭～。空前～后。</li>
	<li>尽，穷尽：～命。～望。～境。～棋。气～。</li>
	<li>极，极端的：～妙。～密。～壁。～无仅有。</li>
	<li>独特的，少有的，没有人能赶上的：～色。～技。～伦。～唱。～代。</li>
	<li>一定的，肯定的：～对。～然。</li>
	<li>越过：&ldquo;假舟楫者，非能水也，而～江河。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>旧体诗的一种体裁：～句。五～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>绝</strong></p>
<p>絶 <span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，表示与线丝有关，从刀，从卩(人)。表示人用刀断丝。本义:把丝弄断)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[break off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝，断丝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冠缨索绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝缨(冠缨断绝。比喻宽宏大量)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为断，断绝 <span class="encs">[sever;cut off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝，断也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举鼎绝膑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;始皇纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必绝其谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>诚能绝 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>。(绝齐，与齐国断绝外交关系。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>之欢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以绝</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>望。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得水则为绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;至乐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝人以玦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>惊，自引而起，袖绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;刺客列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝其望。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;主道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即炉火烧绝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">往来而不绝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝卖(出卖产权;卖掉后再也不许赎回);绝裙(扯断衣襟。指去意坚决，难以挽留);绝祀(断绝祖先的祭祀。比喻国家灭亡，子嗣全无)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 割断;切断 <span class="encs">[cut off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝头刳服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;内储说上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今</span><span class="diczx2">楚国</span>虽小，绝长缩短，犹以数千里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;楚策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七曰绝祭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大祝》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:&ldquo;不循其本直绝肺以祭也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝亢(斩断脖子);绝脰(断颈);绝膑(折断膑骨);绝长补短(本指计量国土大小。后常用为截取有余以补不足)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 气息中止;晕死 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抢呼欲绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝单(狱吏所填写的犯人死亡单子);绝类(灭绝其种类);绝命辞(临死前写的文章)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 断根，无后代 <span class="encs">[discontinue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子绝四。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:&ldquo;无也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝业(已经中断的王业);绝绪(没有后代);绝户计(断绝别人子孙后代的计谋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 横渡;穿越 <span class="encs">[cross]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝江河，横渡过长江黄河。绝，断，横渡。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">假舟楫者，非能水也，而绝江河。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃绝</span><span class="diczx2">漳水</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丈人渡之绝江。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;异宝》</span>。注:&ldquo;过也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝塞(横渡关塞;渡越边塞);绝漠(穿越沙漠);绝险(越过险阻)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 落 <span class="encs">[fall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽萎绝亦何伤兮，哀众芳之芜秽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 杜绝;摒弃 <span class="encs">[completely eradicate;wipe out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝世于良。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十五年》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹言弃也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝弦(断弃琴弦，不再使用);绝学(弃绝学问;抛开学业);绝仁弃义(抛弃世俗的仁义，回复到纯朴的本性)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 超过 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:绝后光前(超越古今);绝俗(超出世俗之外);绝才(过人的才能);绝特(超出寻常)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 停止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗜酒甘而不能绝于口。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;权勋》</span>。注:&ldquo;止也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胞之络脉绝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;奇病论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能绝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜久语声绝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弱者先绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝息(停止);绝脉(脉息停止)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 竭;尽 <span class="encs">[exhaust]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">振乏绝。&mdash;&mdash;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吕氏春秋&middot;季春》。注:&ldquo;居而无食曰绝。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江河山川绝而不流。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span>。注:&ldquo;竭也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝目尽平原。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲍照</span>诗。注:&ldquo;犹尽也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝力(用尽力气);绝目(极目。尽目力之所及);绝叫(大声呼叫);绝阴(阴气竭尽。阴阳家称农历四月戊辰日为绝阴之日，百事不宜)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绝少(极少);绝早(极早);绝峰(绝顶。山的最高峰);绝顶(山的最高峰);绝足(最快的马。喻千里马)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7D55.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇍</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20909.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤉</span>【廣韻】【集韻】情雪切【韻會】徂雪切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音截。【說文】斷絲。从糸从刀从卪，象不連體絕二絲。【廣韻】絕作絶，非。【博雅】斷也。【玉篇】滅也。【書&middot;甘誓】天用勦絕其命。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】終踰絕險。【箋】踰度陷絕之險。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;月令】振乏絕。【疏】不續曰絕。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大祝】辨九祭，七曰絕祭。【註】絕肺以祭，謂之絕祭。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋木】正絕流曰亂。【註】直橫流也。【史記&middot;天官書】絕漢抵營室。【註】索隱曰：絕，度也。【荀子&middot;勸學篇】假舟檝者，非能水也，而絕江河。【註】絕，過也。</p>
<p>又【屈原&middot;離騷】萎絕其何傷兮。【註】絕，落也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】租悅切，音茁。義同。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶此芮切。【司馬相如&middot;哀二世賦】以絕叶勢。</p>
