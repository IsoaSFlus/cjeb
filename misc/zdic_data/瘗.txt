                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>瘗</strong></p><p>瘞 <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 埋物祭地 <span class="encs">[sacrifice to the Earth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有年瘗土，无年瘗土。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高诱</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“祭土曰瘗。年，谷也。有谷祭土，报其功也。无谷祭土，禳其神也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伊年暮春，将瘗后土。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《河东赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:瘗坎(古代行祭地礼时用以埋牲、玉帛的坑穴);瘗地(祭土地神);瘗玉(古代祭山礼仪，治礼毕埋玉于坑);瘗土(祭土地神);瘗毛血(古时祭宗宙和孔庙的一种仪式);瘗位(设瘗坎之处);瘗埋(古代祭地礼仪之一);瘗缯(古代埋缯帛以祭地)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 埋葬 <span class="encs">[bury]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夭赤子于</span><span class="diczx2">新安</span>，坎路侧而瘗之。—— <span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《西征赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:瘗土(用土埋葬);瘗埋(埋葬);瘗葬(埋葬);瘗藏(指殉葬品;埋葬)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 埋藏;隐藏 <span class="encs">[hide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瘗，藏也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瘗，幽埋也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">会人有盗发孝文园瘗钱。——<span class="smcs">《史记·酷吏列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瘗，幽隐也。——<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">寇准</span>瘗谷敌兵畏。—— <span class="diczx2">魏源</span><span class="smcs">《城守篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">寇准</span>瘗谷敌兵畏。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏源</span><span class="smcs">《城守篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:瘗钱(古代葬礼置于墓中陪葬的钱财)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>瘗</strong></p><p>瘞 <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>坟墓 <span class="encs">[grave;tomb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">埋我，以竹杖柱于瘗上，若杖折，掘出我。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p> 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中" target="_blank">午集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中&amp;bs=疒" target="_blank">疒</a>】
                              瘞·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/761E.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28ECF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻏</span>【唐韻】於罽切【集韻】壹計切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音䚷。【說文】幽薶也。【爾雅·釋言】瘞，幽也。【疏】謂埋藏。</p><p>又【釋祭名】祭地曰瘞薶。【註】旣祭埋藏之。【詩·大雅】上下奠瘞。【釋文】瘞，埋也。【禮·禮運】故先王秉著龜，列祭祀瘞繒。【註】埋牲曰瘞。</p><p>又【儀禮·覲禮】祭川沈，祭地瘞。【註】古文瘞作殪。祭地瘞者，祭月也。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋詁】瘞，微也。</p><p>又【集韻】壹計切，医去聲。義同。</p>
