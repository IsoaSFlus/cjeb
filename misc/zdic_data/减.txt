						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>减</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄢˇ</span></p><ol><li>由原有数量中去掉一部分：～价。～员。缩～。削～。偷工～料。</li><li>降低程度，衰退：～轻。～弱。～少。～色。～产。～免。～缓。</li></ol>
