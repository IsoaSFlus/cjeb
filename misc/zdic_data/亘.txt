						<p>● <strong>亘</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">亙</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>空间和时间上延续不断：横～。绵～数千里。～古（整个古代，终古，如&ldquo;～～未有&rdquo;、&ldquo;～～奇闻&rdquo;）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亘</strong></p>
<p>亙 <span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 连绵不断，伸展开去 <span class="encs">[extend;stretch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亘，通也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旌旗亘千里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史&middot;隨本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广数十亩，楼宇连亘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狐嫁女》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其水之大，倍石渠三之一，亘石为底，达于两涯&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《石涧记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:连亘(绵连);亘带(绵延);亘绝(绵延阻绝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 横贯;在空间横过或伸过去 <span class="encs">[span]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这亘古未有的壮举，像一柄锋利的匕首，直刺不共戴天的死敌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《刑场上的婚礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">看桥亘长虹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈暭</span>旦<span class="smcs">《摸鱼子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亘屏(横亘的屏障);亘舒(横空舒展);亘隔(横亘阻隔)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亘</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p>穷尽，终;贯串 <span class="encs">[all over]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亘万古犹一日。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宝</span><span class="smcs">《贾谊新书序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亘天(漫天;连天);亘地(遍地);亘年(历年);亘代(亘古)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亘</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集上" target="_blank">子集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集上&amp;bs=二" target="_blank">二</a>】
                              亘·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=6" target="_blank">6</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E98.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】荀緣切【正韻】息緣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音宣。【說文】求宣也。揚布也。</p><p>又與桓同。烏亘，外國名。◎按亘本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>，與亙字不同。亙从二从舟。舟今作亙。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>从二从囘，囘卽回字，今从日作亘。</p><p>又毛晃曰：紹興二年，禮部看詳姓氏，从水从亘，水名。从木从亘，木名。从木从亘者，皆定讀曰亘。若《晉書》亘彝，亘溫，《書&middot;禹貢》西傾因亘是來之類是也。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亘</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=二">二</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4E98.svg">                          <p>求<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>也。从二从囘。囘，古文回，象<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>回形。上下，所求物也。須緣切〖注〗徐鍇曰：&ldquo;回，風回轉，所以宣陰陽也。&rdquo;</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4E98_74.svg" /><span class="swbf">(亘)</span><span class="ywyy">求回也。</span><span class="swdz">回各本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>。今正。以回釋<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>，以雙聲爲訓也。回者，轉也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>字經典不見。易屯卦磐桓，磐变亦作盤，亦作槃。義當作般，桓義當作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>。般者，辟也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>者，回也。馬融云：槃桓，旋也。是二字皆叚借也。凡舟之旋曰般，旌旗之指<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A3AE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪎮</span>曰旋，車之運曰轉，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24B0C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤬌</span>柄曰斡。皆其意也。</span><span class="ywyy">从二从囘。</span><span class="swdz">會意。須緣切。按古音讀如桓。十四部。</span><span class="ywyy">囘，古文回。</span><span class="swdz">見囗部。</span><span class="ywyy">象<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>回之形。</span><span class="swdz"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20122.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄢</span>回雙聲。猶回轉也。</span><span class="ywyy">上下所求物也。</span><span class="swdz">上下謂二，所求在上，則轉而上所求在下，則轉而下。此說从囘，从二之意。</span></p>                          </div>
