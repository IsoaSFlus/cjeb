                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>锐</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鋭</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ruì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ruì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>锋利，尖，与“<span class="diczx3">钝</span>”相对：～利。～不可当。</li><li>感觉灵敏：敏～。</li><li>勇往直前的气势：～意进取。养精蓄～。</li><li>精良：精～。</li><li>骤，急剧：～减，～增。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sharp, keen, acute, pointed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Rui  (Eig, Fam)​,scharf, intensiv</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pointu,acéré</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>锐</strong></p><p>鋭 <span class="dicpy">ruì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ruì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，兑声。本义:芒)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[awn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">锐，芒也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“芒者，草耑也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 精锐的军队或士卒 <span class="encs">[crack troops]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵王</span>出轻锐以寇其后， <span class="diczx2">秦</span>数不利。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">瑜</span>等率轻锐继其后。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 锐气，勇往直前的气势 <span class="encs">[vigour]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">武夫奋略，战士讲锐。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《释诲》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 力量，劲 <span class="encs">[energy]</span>。如:养精蓄锐</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 锐利的兵器 <span class="encs">[sharp weapons]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫被坚执锐，义不如公。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将军身披坚执锐。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>锐</strong></p><p>鋭 <span class="dicpy">ruì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ruì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 锋利 <span class="encs">[sharp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">锐，利也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">锐喙决吻。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·考工记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长其尾而锐其剽者邪?——<span class="smcs">《荀子·赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔而不刚，锐而不挫。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·时则训》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:锐兵(犀利的兵器);锐锋(利刃);锐居(锐利，锋利);锐喙(尖利的嘴巴)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 精明 <span class="encs">[astute]</span>。如:锐密(敏锐而周密);锐达(敏锐而通达)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 精锐 <span class="encs">[hard-hitting;well-trained]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>特出锐师取地而 <span class="diczx2">韩</span>随之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·存韩》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:锐旅(精锐部队);锐士(精锐的兵士);锐卒(精锐的士卒);锐师(精锐的军队);锐器(精良锐利的兵器);锐骑(精锐勇猛的骑兵)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 物体下大上小 <span class="encs">[taper off at the top]</span>。如:锐头(尖脑袋);锐标(尖端，尖顶);锐上(尖形的额头);锐顶(头额隆起。比喻才华出众)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 勇猛;急速 <span class="encs">[hasty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其进锐者，其退速。——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/92B3.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】以芮切【集韻】【韻會】俞芮切【正韻】于芮切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音叡。【說文】芒也。</p><p>又【書·顧命】一人冕執銳。【傳】銳，矛屬也。【釋文】銳，以稅反。</p><p>又【左傳·成二年】銳司徒免乎。【註】銳司徒，主銳兵者。【釋文】銳，悅歲反。</p><p>又【廣韻】利也。【史記·武安侯傳】魏其銳身爲救灌夫。【前漢·淮南王傳】於是王銳欲發。【註】王意欲發兵，如鋒刃之銳利。</p><p>又【劉向·說苑】哀公問取人。孔子對曰：毋取口銳。口銳者，多誕寡信。</p><p>又【左傳·哀十一年】子羽銳敏。【註】銳，精也。【桓十一年】我以銳師，宵加於鄖。【王褒·講德論】各采精銳，以貢忠誠。</p><p>又【正字通】今凡物鑯利曰銳。【前漢·天文志】下有三星銳，曰罰。【註】上小下大，故曰銳。【爾雅·釋丘】再成銳上爲融丘。【註】纖項者。【釋文】唯歲反。</p><p>又【釋山】銳而高嶠。【疏】銳，鑯也，言山形鑯峻而高者，名嶠。</p><p>又【正韻】細小也。【左傳·昭十六年】且吾以玉賈罪，不亦銳乎。【註】銳，細小也。【疏】銳是鋒鋩。【釋文】銳，悅歲反。</p><p>又姓。【姓苑】升平申鮮里，有御史中丞銳管。</p><p>又【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>徒外切，音兌。矛屬。</p><p>又【集韻】都外切，音祋。義同。</p><p>又【五音集韻】弋雪切，音悅。【揚子·方言】盂，宋楚魏之閒或謂之銚銳。　【集韻】籀作㓹。或作稅。</p><p>
