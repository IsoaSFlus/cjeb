                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>雉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>鸟，雄的羽毛很美，尾长；雌的淡黄褐色，尾较短。善走，不能久飞。肉可食，羽毛可做装饰品。通称“野鸡”。</li>    <li>古代计算城墙面积的单位，长三丈高一丈为一雉。</li>    <li>城墙：～堞。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">雉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雉</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从隹，矢声。隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)，短尾鸟的总称。本义:野鸡)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pheasant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雉有十四种。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雉者，野鸟。——<span class="smcs">《尚书大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雉，耿介之鸟也。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗章句》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">离为雉。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡挚士雉谓其守介节。交有时，别有伦也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雄雉于飞。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·雄雉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无雉兔鲋鱼。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:雉尾扇(野鸡尾部羽毛制作的扇子。亦为仪仗之一);雉尾(皇帝坐朝时左右侍从所执的用野鸡尾羽制的扇障);雉经(吊死;上吊)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代计算城墙面积的单位 <span class="encs">[an ancient measure unit]</span>。长三丈、高一丈为一雉</p><p><span class="diczx1">五楮而雉。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·定公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">都城过百雉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“三堵曰雉。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王宫门阿之制五雉。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·匠人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“长三尺高一丈。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 城墙 <span class="encs">[wall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出没眺楼雉。——</span><span class="diczx2">谢朓</span><span class="smcs">《和王著作八公山》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雉堞">雉堞</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雉鸠">雉鸠</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡨥</span>雉。【註】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡨥</span>雉，一名鵽鳩。</p><p>又【晏子·問篇】鄒滕雉犇而出其地，猶稱公侯。</p><p>又【晉語】雉經於新城之廟。【註】雉經，頭搶而懸也。</p><p>又【周禮·冬官考工記·匠人】王宮門阿之制五雉，宮隅之制七雉，城隅之制九雉。【註】雉，長三丈，高一丈。【左傳·隱元年】都城過百雉。【註】方丈曰堵，三堵曰雉。一雉之牆，長三丈，高一丈。侯伯之城方五里，徑三百雉，故其大都不過百雉。【管子·海王篇】吾欲藉於臺雉。</p><p>又【春秋·定二年】雉門及兩觀災。【註】雉，公宮之南門。</p><p>又【揚雄·甘泉賦】列新雉於林薄。【註】服虔曰：新雉，香草也。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋詁】雉，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24261.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤉡</span>字註。</p><p>又【集韻】演爾切，音酏。縣名。【前漢·地理志】江夏郡下雉縣。【註】雉，羊氏反。【又】南陽郡雉縣。【註】弋爾反。</p><p>又【集韻】口駭切，音鍇。桂林人謂人短爲矲雉，或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/253EA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥏪</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25A64.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥩤</span>。</p><p>又直利切，音稚。野雞也。</p><p>
