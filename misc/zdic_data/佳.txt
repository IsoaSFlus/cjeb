						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>佳</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄚˉ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 美，好的：～美。～妙。～句。～作。～音。～节。～境。～期。～人。～丽（❶美丽；❷美女）。～偶。～酿。美酒～肴。</p></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，圭( <span class="dicpy">guī</span>)声。本义:美，美好)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[beautiful;fine;nice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佳，善也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佳，大也，又，好也。——<span class="diczx1">形夸骨佳。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·脩务》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佳人不同体，美人不同面，而皆说于目。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山气日夕佳。——</span><span
