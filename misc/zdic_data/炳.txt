                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">炳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>炳</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，丙声。本义:光明，明亮)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">炳，明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其文炳也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;革》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">炳炳，明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>之文章炳然与三代同风 。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《两都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:炳著(光明显著);炳焕(光明显耀);炳映(光芒照耀);炳然(光明、明白的样子);炳耀(光耀明亮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 显著;明显;昭著 <span class="encs">[mark able;shining]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炳明(明显、显著);炳炳显显(明显的样子);炳赫(明显突出)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>炳</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 点燃 <span class="encs">[light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古人思炳烛夜游。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《与吴质书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">炳烛之明，熟与昧行乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《说苑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:炳烛(点燃烛火以照明)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 显示，显现 <span class="encs">[show]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炳耀</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 照耀 <span class="encs">[shine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炳映(照射)</span></p>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">顯著、光明。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「功業彪<b>炳</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;革卦&middot;象曰》：「大人虎變，其文<b>炳</b>也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;班固&middot;寶鼎詩》：「寶鼎見兮色紛縕，煥其<b>炳</b>兮被龍文。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">明白。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷三六&middot;楚元王傳》：「決斷狐疑，分別猶豫，使是非炳然可知。」</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝宋&middot;劉義慶《世說新語&middot;文學》：「三乘佛家滯義，支道林分判，使三乘炳然。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
