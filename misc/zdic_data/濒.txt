						<p>● <strong>濒</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">瀕</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>接近，将，临：～近。～危。～死。～于灭亡。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/滨">滨</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> approach, be on verge of; near</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rive,rivage</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">濒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>濒</strong></p>
<p>瀕 <span class="dicpy">bīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从水，频声。本义:水边) 同本义 <span class="encs">[riverside]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是故昔者</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>耕于 <span class="diczx2">历山</span>，陶于河濒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚贤》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行举濒河之郡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;成帝纪》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;滨，水涯也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">濒海之观毕至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾山传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>濒</strong></p>
<p>瀕 <span class="dicpy">bīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> 靠近;临近 <span class="encs">[be close to;border on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">濒</span><span class="diczx2">南山</span>，近 <span class="diczx2">夏阳</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;地理志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:濒危;濒近;濒于(亦作&ldquo;濒於&rdquo;。指临近某种坏的遭遇)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“濒”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/瀕">瀕</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十一">卷十一</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十一&amp;swbs=瀕">瀕</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7015.svg">                          <p>水厓。人所賓附，頻蹙不前而止。从頁从涉。凡頻之屬皆从頻。符眞切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗別作水賓，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7015_3614.svg" /><span class="swbf">(瀕)</span><span class="ywyy">水厓，人所賔附也。</span><span class="swdz">厓今之涯字。附當作駙。馬部曰：駙，近也。瀕賔以曡韵爲訓。瀕，今字作濵。召旻傳曰：瀕，厓也。采蘋、北山傳皆曰：濵，厓也。今字用頻訓數。攷桑柔傳曰頻，急也。廣雅曰頻頻，比也。此從附近之義引申之。本無二字二音，而今字妄爲分别，積習生常矣。</span><span class="ywyy">顰戚不歬而止。从頁从涉。</span><span class="swdz">顰戚各本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29558.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩕘</span>戚，今正。此以顰戚釋从頁之意也。將涉者，或因水深，顰眉蹙頞而止。故字从涉頁。符眞切。按當必鄰切。十二部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29558.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩕘</span>之屬皆从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29558.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩕘</span>。</span></p>                          </div>
