                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>觊</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">覬</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>希望得到：&ldquo;自毁齿已上，父兄鬻卖，以～其利&rdquo;。～觎（非分的希望或企图）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> covet, long for, desire</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> begehren ,ersehnen, verlangen nach</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> espérer,désirer</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>觊</strong></p>
<p>覬 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从见，岂声。本义:希望得到) 同本义 <span class="encs">[covet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自毁齿已上，父兄鬻卖，以觊其利。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觊乎异日，得夷平民，然后裹足西向。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《上杜司徒书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:觊幸(希望能侥幸得到);觊望(非分的希望);觊欲(非分的希求与贪欲);觊夺(希图攘夺);觊欲(企图);觊利(企求利益)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">覬</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“觊”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/覬">覬</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=見" target="_blank">見</a>】
                              覬·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/89AC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】几利切【正韻】吉器切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音冀。【說文】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23886.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣢆</span>幸也。【廣韻】覬覦，希望也。【左傳&middot;桓二年】下無覬覦。【註】下不冀望于上也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作幾。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】庸可幾乎。【疏】幾讀如冀，言用可冀幸而得之乎。【禮&middot;檀弓&middot;吾欲暴尩註】尩者面鄕天，覬天哀而雨之。覬，又作幾，音冀。</p>
<p>又【韻會小補】通作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29949.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩥉</span>。【禮&middot;文王世子&middot;反養老于東序註】州里<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29949.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩥉</span>於邑。【疏】希<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29949.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩥉</span>慕仰，行之於邑也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】虛器切，咦去聲。【類篇】幸也。【韻會小補】垂也。【集韻】亦作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23886.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣢆</span>。【正字通】俗通作冀。【增韻】亦作懻，非。</p>
