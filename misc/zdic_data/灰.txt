                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">灰</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>灰</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>物体燃烧后剩下的东西，经烧制后形成的产品：纸～。～烬。洋～。～飞烟灭。</li>    <li>尘土：～尘。</li>    <li>特指“石灰”：～墙。～膏。</li>    <li>黑白之间的颜色：～色。～质（脑和脊髓的灰色部分）。～沉沉。</li>    <li>志气消沉：心～意懒。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">灰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>灰</strong> <span class="dicpy">huī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从手，从火。意思是火已熄灭，可以用手去拿。本义:火灰)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ash]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">灰，死火余烬也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋烧灰。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“火之灭者为灰。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">火过为灰。——<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">春蚕到死丝方尽，蜡炬成灰泪始干。——</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《无题》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">满面尘灰烟火色。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纸灰之类。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">灰飞烟灭。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇·赤壁怀古》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:灰灭(灰没。像灰烬般地被消灭);灰榇(盛放骨灰的木匣，亦指骨灰);灰罐(灰礶。盛墨的罐子);灰枵(灰烬和腐木。喻消亡);灰火(火灰);灰末(灰粉)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 灰尘 <span class="encs">[dust]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民间食盐，杂以灰土。——<span class="smcs">《宋史·食货志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">已衄而灰。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说林上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">素醰灰丝。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:路上都是灰;灰壤(尘埃);灰朴朴(尘垢满身;土头土脑);灰头灰脸(满面风尘的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 石灰的简称 <span class="encs">[lime]</span>。如:灰户(烧石灰的窑户);灰酒(下少许石灰澄清的酒);灰钉(石灰和铁钉);灰土(石灰土)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 灰浆 <span class="encs">[mortar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">和灰</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>灰</strong> <span class="dicpy">huī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 灰色。白色和黑色的混合色 <span class="encs">[gray;grey]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">面色灰死。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:灰黪(暗灰色);灰鼠(灰色松鼠;也指灰鼠皮);灰不答(形容颜色陈旧暗淡);灰黑(浅黑色)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 沮丧 <span class="encs">[disheartened]</span>。如:灰颓(灰心颓丧);灰气(丧气)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指辈分小 <span class="encs">[junior]</span>——常为贬意。如:灰子灰孙;灰孙子(辈份很低的人;比喻低人一等的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 暗淡;懊丧 <span class="encs">[gloomy]</span>。如:灰念(心志寂静不动。即灰心);灰哑(阴暗沉寂);灰洞洞(灰雾弥漫而昏暗之貌)</p><p><span
