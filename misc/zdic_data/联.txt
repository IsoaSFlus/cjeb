                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>联</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">聯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>连结，结合：～合。～结。～系。～络。～盟。～邦。～袂。～名。～想。～姻。～营。珠～璧合。</li>
	<li>对偶的语句：对～。挽～。楹～。上～。下～。</li>
	<li>古代户口编制的名称，十人为联。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">联</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>联</strong></p>
<p>聯 <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从耳，从丝。战国文字，中间是耳，两边是丝相联。本义:连结)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[join;link]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聯，连也。从耳，耳连于颊也。从糸，糸连不绝也。会意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">周</span>人用联字， <span class="diczx2">汉</span>人用连字，古今字也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三曰官联，以会官治。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;大宰》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:&ldquo;连事通职，相佐助也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聯，聯绵不绝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《声类》</span>。按，凡联续、联合、牵联、结联字，经传皆以连为之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">联袂度危桥，萦回出林杪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《与崔策登西山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:联垂(一种发式。周围头发梳成许多根细辫子);联襟(即连襟。姊妹丈夫之间的亲戚关系);联缀(连系，联集在一块);联珠(连串的珍珠);联华(花开并蒂);联裾(联袂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 联合;联系 <span class="encs">[unite;ally oneself with;contact;relate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣恐</span><span class="diczx2">羌</span>复结联他种，宜未及然为之备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵充国传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三曰联兄弟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹合也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">联名请</span><span class="diczx2">益王</span>即位。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吴研人</span><span class="smcs">《痛史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:联衔(联合签具职衔名位);联拳(群聚的样子);联屯(联合屯兵);联事(联合处理事务);联步(同行);联带(谓互相挈带或联系);联姻，联婚(结亲);联吏(同僚);联曹(同在一署任职);联职(同事);联大(联合国组织的大会。简称联大);联合战线(协力合作。如:甲国与乙国因利害关系相同，团结一致对抗第三者，以求贯彻某种目的。也称统一战线);联教组织(&ldquo;联合国教育科学及文化组织的简称，又称教科文组织);联营公司(与其他公司发生经营关系的公司，其关系或为隶属，或为共同管理，或为同受控制)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 缝 <span class="encs">[sew]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">宋敦</span>]</span>穿了一件新联的洁白湖绸道袍。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">冯梦龙</span><span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一件新联的道袍</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
