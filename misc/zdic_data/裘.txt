                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">裘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>裘</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，求声。本义:皮衣。古代&ldquo;求&rdquo;、&ldquo;裘&rdquo;同字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fur coat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裘，皮衣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裘所以佐女功助温也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;衣裳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌为大裘&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司裘》</span>。注:&ldquo;大裘，黑羔裘。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世以为裘者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛论》</span>。注:&ldquo;裘孤之属也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">良治之子，必学为裘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冬日麑裘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狐裘不暖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裘葛之遗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裘马过世家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裘马甚都(车马衣裘十分华美);裘膻(裘皮和膻气。代指清兵);狐裘;裘氏(周礼官名。制皮衣的工匠);裘冕(上古帝王祭天时所穿戴的衣冠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代制皮工匠的一种 <span class="encs">[furrier]</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>裘</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为&ldquo;求&rdquo;。求取 <span class="encs">[strive for;seek]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熊罴是裘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;大东》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 穿上皮衣 <span class="encs">[put on fur coat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:裘马(坐马车，穿裘衣。比喻权贵的豪华生活);裘马轻肥(衣轻裘，乘肥马。比喻富豪人家的少年)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集下" target="_blank">申集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集下&amp;bs=衣" target="_blank">衣</a>】
                              裘·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/88D8.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕求【唐韻】巨鳩切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】渠尤切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音求。【玉篇】皮衣也。【詩&middot;小雅】舟人之子，熊羆是裘。【又】彼都人士，狐裘黃黃。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;司裘】掌爲大裘，以供王祀天之服。中秋獻良裘，季秋獻功裘。【中論】救寒莫如重裘。</p>
<p>又披裘公，人名。【高士傳】披裘公曰：五月被裘負薪，豈取金者哉。</p>
<p>又菟裘，地名。【左傳&middot;隱十一年】使營菟裘，吾將老焉。【註】在梁父縣南。</p>
<p>又裘氏，亭名。【水經注】沙水又東南逕裘氏鄕裘氏亭西。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】裘氏，衞大夫食采于裘，因氏焉。</p>
<p>又【集韻】渠竹切，音鞠。義同。</p>
<p>又【韻補】渠之切，叶音其。【詩&middot;豳風】取彼狐狸，爲公子裘。　【說文】作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2768D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧚍</span>。</p>
