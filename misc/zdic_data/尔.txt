                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>尔</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">爾</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ěr  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ěr.mp3"></a></span> ㄦˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>你，你的：～父。～辈。～汝（你我相称，关系密切）。～曹（你们这些人）。～虞我诈。</li>	<li>如此：偶～。不过～～。</li>	<li>那，其（指时间）：～时。～后。</li>	<li>而已，罢了（亦作&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">耳</span>&rdquo;）：&ldquo;布衣之怒，亦免冠徒跣，以头抢地～&rdquo;。</li>	<li>词尾，相当于&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">地</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">然</span>&rdquo;：卓～。率～（轻易地）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尔</strong></p>
<p>爾 <span class="dicpy">ěr</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ěr.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:窗格花纹)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[figure;decorative pattern]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 后引作薾。华盛的样子 <span class="encs">[luxuriant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼尔维何?维常之华。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:&ldquo;尔，华盛貌。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丽尔(华丽的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 近。通&ldquo;迩&rdquo; <span class="encs">[near]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戚戚兄弟，莫远具尔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔上敦黍于筵上右之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;少牢馈食礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公降立于阼阶之东南，南乡尔卿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;燕礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">名相近者相远也，实相近者相尔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;肆长》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士不信悫而有多知能，譬之其豺狼也，不可以身尔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;哀公》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尔者(近来);尔语(凑向跟前说话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 浅近 <span class="encs">[shallow]</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尔</strong></p>
<p>爾 <span class="dicpy">ěr</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ěr.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">代</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 第二人称代词 <span class="encs">[you]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 你</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔为尔，我为我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔安敢轻吾射。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尔当(你);尔躲(你身上);尔汝(尊对卑称;贵对贱称;朋友间的亲密互称);尔济(汝辈，你们)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 您;你们&mdash;&mdash;通常放在关系词&ldquo;所&rdquo;之前。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:尔曹(你们大家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 那 <span class="encs">[that]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔夜风恬月朗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尔俸尔禄(那些俸禄。尔:其，那);尔语(那样言语);尔时(那时，彼时)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 这个;此 <span class="encs">[this]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问君何能尔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《饮酒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尔夕(今夕，这个晚上);尔日(当天，这天);尔为(这样，如此)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 如此;这样。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔毋从从尔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:果尔;乃尔;尔馨(六朝时语词。即如此);尔为(如此);尔许(若此，如许。尔，此也。许，助辞);不尔;云尔</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>尔</strong></p>
<p>爾 <span class="dicpy">ěr</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ěr.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">助</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形容词、副词的词尾，相当于&ldquo;然&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼓瑟希，铿尔，舍瑟而作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 相当于&ldquo;了&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非死则徒尔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 应诺声。相当于&ldquo;是&rdquo; <span class="encs">[yes]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:尔尔(应答之词。等于唯唯，是是)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;耳&rdquo;，表示限止用在句末，可译为&ldquo;而己&rdquo;&ldquo;罢了&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唯手熟尔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5C12.svg">                          <p>詞之必然也。从入丨八。八象氣之分散。兒氏切〖注〗段注：尔之言如此也，後世多以爾字為之。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5C12_1.svg" /> <span class="swbf">(尒)</span> <span class="ywyy">䛐之必然也。</span> <span class="swdz">䛐，今作詞。說文字體本作䛐。尒之言如此也。後世多以爾字爲之。凡曰果爾、不爾、云爾、莞爾、鏗爾、卓爾、鼎鼎爾、猶猶爾、聊復爾耳、故人心尙爾皆訓如此。亦有單訓此者，如公羊焉爾之爲於此，孟子然而無乎爾，則亦有乎爾是也。語助有用耳者，與爾絶殊，三國志云生女耳是也。耳之言而已也。近人爾耳不分，如論語女得人焉爾乎，唐石經譌爲焉耳。詩陳風箋梅之樹善惡自爾，宋本譌爲善惡自耳。皆是也。古書尒字，淺人多改爲爾。如手部引《論語》鏗尒，考工記揱尒，小徐本不誤，是也。</span> <span class="ywyy">从丨八，八象氣之分散。</span> <span class="swdz">从丨八，與小同異。</span> <span class="ywyy">入聲。</span> <span class="swdz">今本無此二字。上文作从入丨八，此依韵會所引小徐本訂正。入聲在七部，而尒在十一、十六部閒者，於雙聲求之也。兒氏切。</span></p>                          </div>
