                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>苗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">miáo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miáo.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄠˊ</span></p><ol><li>初生的植物或没有秀穗的庄稼：禾～。麦～。树～。幼～。～子（亦喻继承某种事业的接班人）。～而不秀（只长苗而不秀穗，喻本身条件虽好，但没有成就）。</li><li>形状像苗的：火～儿。</li><li>某些初生的饲养的动物：鱼～。</li><li>能使机体产生免疫力的微生物制剂：疫～。卡介～。</li><li>子孙后代：～裔。</li><li>中国少数民族，主要分布于贵州、湖南、云南、四川、湖北等省和广西壮族自治区：～族。～绣。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">苗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>苗</strong> <span class="dicpy">miáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从田，从艸。田里生长的形状象草的东西。本义:禾苗，未吐穗的庄稼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义;泛指初生的植物 <span class="encs">[seedlings of cereal crops]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苗，草生于田者。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无食我苗。——<span class="smcs">《诗·魏风·硕鼠》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“嘉谷也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无苗。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·庄公七年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“苗者禾也。生曰苗，秀曰禾。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>人有闵其苗之不长而揠之者，芒芒然归。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南门之外有黄犊食苗道左者。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·内储说上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">八月苗未枯。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无苗时采。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有苗时采。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:补苗;间苗;秧苗;麦苗;豆苗;壮苗(健壮的幼苗)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指禾谷之实 <span class="encs">[seed]</span>。如:苗米(漕运上缴的官粮)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 初生的动物 <span class="encs">[the young of some animals]</span>。如:鱼苗;猪苗</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 子孙后代 <span class="encs">[descendant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大王</span><span class="diczx2">刘</span>氏苗族， <span class="diczx2">绍世</span>而起。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其先出自</span><span class="diczx2">箕子</span>苗。——<span class="smcs">《李翊碑》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:苗末(后代子孙);苗胄(后代的子孙);苗胤(子孙相承续。亦指后代子孙);苗嗣(后嗣，后代);苗绪(后代子孙)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 事物的开端;事物的预兆 <span class="encs">[beginning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">言者志之苗，行者文之根。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《读张籍古乐府》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:苗脉(比喻事物的根源);苗头(端绪;预兆;因由)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 露头，岩层露出地表的部分 <span class="encs">[outcrops]</span>。如:矿苗;苗脉(指矿苗和矿脉)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 我国古代部族名 <span class="encs">[Miao
