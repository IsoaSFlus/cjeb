                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>纤</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">纖</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>细小：～人（卑鄙的小人）。～介（细微，细小。亦作&ldquo;纤芥&rdquo;）。～手（指女子细而柔嫩的手）。～尘。～～。～秀。～弱。～毫。～悉（细致而详尽）。～度（天然丝或化纤粗细的程度）。</li>	<li>吝啬：～啬。</li></ol><p>● <strong>纤</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">縴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄢˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>拉船前进的绳子：～夫（在岸上用绳子拉船前进的人）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fine, delicate; minute; graceful</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fin,soie,fibre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纤</strong></p>
<p>縴 <span class="dicpy">qi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用于拉船的绳索 <span class="encs">[rope for towing a boat;towrope;towline]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我比不得他们扯篷拉纤的图银子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纤道(江河两岸纤夫挽船前进的小路);纤路(纤道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 牵牲口的绳索 <span class="encs">[reins]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">马牛有纤，私属有闲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《观市》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/纤夫">纤夫</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/纤绳">纤绳</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/纤手">纤手</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纤</strong></p>
<p>纖 <span class="dicpy">xiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸，千声从糸( <span class="dicpy">mī</span>)，细丝，表微小。本义:细小)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fine;minute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纤，细也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥筐元纤缟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;细也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纤縠蛾飞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">傅毅</span><span class="smcs">《舞赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;细縠也。&rdquo;</p>
