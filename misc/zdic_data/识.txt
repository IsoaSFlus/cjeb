                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">识</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>识</strong></p><p>識 <span class="dicpy">shí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言。戠( <span class="dicpy">zhí</span>)声。善于言谈，表示有知识，故从言。本义:知道;懂得)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[know;realize;be aware]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">識，知也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子是识。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·瞻卬》</span>。笺:<span class="yhcs">“知也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壹宥曰不识。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司刺》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">草荣识节和，木衰知风厉。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知可以战与不可以战者胜，识众寡之用者胜。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·谋攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不识有诸?——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苟有险，余必下推车，子岂识之?——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使汝不识诗书，或未必艰贞若是。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:识味(知味);识空便(知趣;识相);识道(知圣道);识义(知义理)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 认识 <span class="encs">[know;recognize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今为所识穷乏者得我而为之。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">仲永</span>生五年，未尝识书具。—— <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《伤仲永》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">儿童相见不相识，笑问客从何处来。——</span><span class="diczx2">贺知章</span><span class="smcs">《回乡偶书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫愁前路无知己，天下谁人不识君!——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">高适</span><span class="smcs">《别董大》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>                           『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">常也。</span>常當爲意。字之誤也。草書常意相似。六朝以草寫書。迨草變眞。譌誤往往如此。意者、志也。志者、心所之也。意與志、志與識古皆通用。心之所存謂之意。所謂知識者此也。大學。誠其意。卽實其識也。<span class="jieshi">一曰知也。</span>矢部曰。知、識䛐也。按凡知識、記識、標識、今人分入去二聲。古無入去分别。三者實一義也。<span class="jieshi">从言。戠聲。</span>賞職切。一部。                          </div>
