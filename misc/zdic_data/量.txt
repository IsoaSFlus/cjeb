                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>量</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liáng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄤˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>用器物计算东西的多少或长短：用尺～布。用斗～米。车载斗～。～体温。</li>    <li>估量：思～。打～。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>量</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liàng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄤˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>旧指测量东西多少的器物，如斗、升等。</li>    <li>能容纳、禁受的限度：酒～。气～。胆～。度～。</li>    <li>数的多少：数～。质～。降雨～。限～供应。</li>    <li>估计，审度：～力。～入为出。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">量</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>量</strong> <span class="dicpy">liáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:用量器计算容积或长度)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[measure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">量，称轻重也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“称者，铨也。汉志曰:量者，所以量多少也。衡权者，所以均物平轻重也。此训量为称轻重者，有多少斯有轻重，视其多少可辜搉其重轻也，其字之所以从重也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苦称量之不审也兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·惜誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为之斗斛以量之。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·胠箧》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不量凿而正枘兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">量入以为出。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:比量(不用尺而用手、绳、棍等大概地量一量;比试);量柴头数米角比喻做事小气吝啬，斤斤计较;量地(丈量土地);量酒(量酒人，量酒博士。都指酒店中接待顾客的伙计);量珠(以斗量明珠以买侍妾。后指纳妾为量珠之聘);量视(衡量;看待)料量(计量);量校(以量器测定校核)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 衡量，酌量 <span class="encs">[calculate;estimate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事君者量而后入。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·少仪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“量其事意合成否。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">量功名日，分财用，平板干。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则胜负之数，存亡之理，当与</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>相较，或未易量。—— <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span
