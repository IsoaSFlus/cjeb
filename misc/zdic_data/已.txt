                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>已</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>止，罢了：学不可以～。死而后～。</li>
	<li>表示过去：～经。事～至此。～往。业～。</li>
	<li>后来，过了一些时间，不多时：～忽不见。</li>
	<li>太，过：不为～甚。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/以">以</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">已</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>已</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。象蛇形。一说原与&ldquo;子&rdquo;同字。本义:停止)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cease;stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡鸣不已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;风雨》</span>。传:&ldquo;已，止也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德音不已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;南山有台》</span>。传:&ldquo;已，止也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以故事得已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">操蛇之神闻之，惧其不已也，告之于帝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是亦不可以已乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">累寸不已，遂成丈匹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非夫人之物而强假焉，必虑人逼取，而惴惴焉，摩玩之不已。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:不已</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 完成，完毕 <span class="encs">[finish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已，成也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已事遄往。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;损》</span>。注:&ldquo;竟也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且曰吾已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十三年》</span>。注:&rsquo;犹决竟也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有司已于事而竣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span>。注:&ldquo;毕也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:已矣(完了，逝去)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 治愈 <span class="encs">[cure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已大风、挛踠、瘘疠治愈大风、挛踠、瘘、疠(等重病)。大风，麻风病。挛踠，手脚弯曲不能伸展。瘘，脖子肿。疠，恶疮]。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>已</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 已经 <span class="encs">[already]</span>&mdash;&mdash;表示动作变化达到的程度</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北向不能得日，日过午已昏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:已装不卸(喻已成定局，无法改变)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 以前 <span class="encs">[before]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:已先(从前;先前);已事(往事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 罢了，算了 <span class="encs">[well]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:已乎(算了)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 太 <span class="encs">[too]</span>&mdash;&mdash;表示程度</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无已大康，职思其居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死而湮没不足道者，亦已众矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又 <span class="encs">[also]</span>&mdash;&mdash;表示行为的频率</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周子居</span>常云:&ldquo;吾时月不见 <span class="diczx2">黄叔度</span>，则鄙吝之心已复生矣。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 最终，终归 <span class="encs">[finally]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其所以贯理焉，虽亿万已不足以浃万物之变。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 已而，然后 <span class="encs">[then;after that]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庭中始为篱，已为墙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DF3_1642.svg" /><span class="swbf">(巳)</span><span class="ywyy">巳也。</span><span class="swdz">律書曰：巳者，言萬物之巳盡也。律曆志曰：巳盛於巳。淮南天文訓曰：巳則生巳定也。釋名曰：巳，畢布巳也。辰巳之巳旣久用爲巳然巳止之巳，故卽以巳然之巳釋之。序卦傳：蒙者，蒙也。比者，比也。剝者，剝也。毛詩傳曰：虚，虚也。自古訓故有此例。卽用本字，不叚異字也。小雅斯干箋云：似讀爲巳午之巳。巳續妣祖者，謂巳成其宮廟也。此可見漢人巳午與巳然無二音，其義則異而同也。廣雅釋言：巳，㠯也。乃淺人所改。近人興朱氏重刻汲古閣說文改爲己也，殊誤。</span><span class="ywyy">四月昜气巳出。陰气巳臧。</span><span class="swdz">今藏字。</span><span class="ywyy">萬物見，</span><span class="swdz">句。</span><span class="ywyy">成彣彰。</span><span class="swdz">故曰巳也。</span><span class="ywyy">故巳爲它象形。</span><span class="swdz">巳不可像也，故以蛇象之。蛇，長而冤曲垂尾。其字像蛇，則象陽巳出陰巳藏矣。此六字一句讀。巳者，蛇象也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22053.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢁓</span>者，古文豕也。此近十二屬之說，而與論衡物勢篇義各不同。祥里切。</span><span class="ywyy">凡巳之屬皆从巳。</span></p>                          </div>
