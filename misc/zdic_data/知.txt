                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">知</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>知</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>晓得，明了：～道。～名（著名）。～觉（有感觉而知道）。良～。～人善任。温故～新。～难而进。～情达理。</li>	<li>使知道：通～。～照。</li>	<li>学识，学问：～识，求～。无～。</li>	<li>主管：～县（旧时的县长）。～府。～州。～宾（指主管招待宾客的人。亦称&ldquo;知客&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>彼此了解：相～。～音。～近。</li>	<li>彼此了解、交好的人：故～（老朋友）。</li></ol><p>● <strong>知</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/智">智</a>&rdquo;，智慧。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> know, perceive, comprehend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> wissen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> savoir,connaître,connaissances</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">知</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>知</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，从口矢。 <span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>:&ldquo;识敏，故出于口者疾如矢也。&rdquo;意思是:认识、知道的事物，可以脱口而出。本义:知道)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[know]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知，识也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知汝远来应有意。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《左迁至蓝关示侄孙湘》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心徹为知。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;外物》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">留侯</span>曰:&ldquo;陛下不知乎?此谋反耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;留侯世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不知何氏女。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝亦知射。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不知几千里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知其不可而为之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:知不到(不知道);知风(知道情况);知不道(不知道);知兵(通晓军事);知古今(识古通今);知足不辱(自知满足就不会招致羞辱);知命不忧(识天命者无忧虑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 懂得，了解，理会 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百姓闻之，知与不知，无老壮皆为垂涕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;李将军列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食马者不知。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人非生而知之者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不知御。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知幸与不幸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:知道眉高眼低(比喻懂事);知窍(能见机行事;知趣);知局(知趣);知臣莫若君;知子莫若父;知心着意(彼此了解，心意投合);知我(深切了解我);知好歹(明白道理，懂事);知情识趣(懂情意，善体贴);知高识底(说话、做事懂得掌握分寸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 主持;管理 <span class="encs">[administer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有能助寡人谋而退</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>者，吾与之共知 <span class="diczx2">越国</span>之政。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;越语上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜开金殿看星河，宫女知更月明里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《温泉宫行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:知更(主管更漏);知内外事(主持内政和外交事务);知客寮(寺院中的接待客);知印(主持用印);知贡举(唐宋时特派主持进士考试的大臣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 识别;区别 <span class="encs">[distinguish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孪子之相似者，唯其母能知之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:知时达变(识时务，善变通);知人则哲(能识别人的贤愚善恶就聪明)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 赏识 <span class="encs">[appreciate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">含愁更奏绿绮琴，调高弦绝无知音。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">卢仝</span><span class="smcs">《有所思》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:知爱(赏识喜爱);知器(赏识，器重);知遇(赏识;优待);知怜(赏识爱护);知重(赏识;看重)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 要好 <span class="encs">[be close friends]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悲莫悲兮生别离，乐莫乐兮新相知。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九歌》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">张胜相知。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:知慰(关心;抚慰);知契(知心投合)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 感到，感觉，察觉 <span class="encs">[perceive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[君主]</span>又损其生以资天下之人，而终不自知。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;情欲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">识者固知。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 参与 <span class="encs">[participate in;have a hand in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">高</span>、 <span class="diczx2">鲍</span>将不纳君，而立公子 <span class="diczx2">角</span>，国子知之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十七年》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>知</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 知识 <span class="encs">[knowledge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子曰:&ldquo;吾有知乎哉?无知也。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子罕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾生也有涯，而知也无涯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;养生主》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:求知欲;真知灼见;无知;通过努力学习，可以由无知转化为有知</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 知觉 <span class="encs">[consciousness]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草木有生而无知。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死而有知，有几何离;其无知，悲不几时，而不悲者无穷期矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《祭十二郎文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 知己 <span class="encs">[bosom friend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝宾客之知。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天涯占梦数，疑误有新知。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《凉思》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:知心腹(亲信)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> &ldquo;智&rdquo;的古字。智慧;才智 <span class="encs">[wisdom;ability]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故夫知效一官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">择不处仁，焉得知?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;里仁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝多知乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则知明而行无过矣。(知，通&ldquo;智&rdquo;)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大知而察。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:知力(才智能力);知士(才智之士)</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">明白、了解、察覺。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「相<b>知</b>相許」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李紳〈古風〉詩二首之二：「誰<b>知</b>盤中餐，粒粒皆辛苦。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">識別、區別。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;樂記》：「<b>知</b>樂則幾於禮矣。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子&middot;脩務》：「孿子之相似者，唯其母能<b>知</b>之。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">相交、交往。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;昭公四年》：「公孫明<b>知</b>叔孫於齊。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;不苟》：「君子易<b>知</b>而難狎。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">招待、禮遇、賞識。</span><span class="gc_yy">《南史&middot;卷一九&middot;謝靈運傳》：「自謂才能宜參權要，既不見<b>知</b>，常懷慎惋。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;岑參〈北庭西郊候封大夫受降回軍獻上〉詩：「何幸一書生，忽蒙國士<b>知</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">掌管、主持。</span><span class="gc_yy">《國語&middot;越語》：「有能助寡人謀而退吳者，吾與之共<b>知</b>越國之政。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷八五&middot;王世充傳》：「帝愛昵之，拜江都通守，兼<b>知</b>宮監事。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">識見、學問。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「求<b>知</b>」、「真知灼見」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;子罕》：「吾有<b>知</b>乎哉？無<b>知</b>也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">意識、感覺。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;王制》：「草木有生而無<b>知</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;范縝〈神滅論〉：「手等亦應能有痛癢之<b>知</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">知己、朋友。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「故<b>知</b>」、「舊雨新<b>知</b>」。</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/77E5.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221FB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇻</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/253BF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥎿</span>【唐韻】陟离切【集韻】【韻會】珍離切【正韻】珍而切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>智平聲。【說文】詞也。从口从矢。【徐曰】知理之速，如矢之疾也。</p><p>又【玉篇】識也，覺也。【增韻】喩也。【易&middot;繫辭】百姓日用而不知。【書&middot;臯陶謨】知人則哲，能官人。</p><p>又漢有見知法。【史記&middot;酷吏傳】趙禹與張湯論定諸律令，作見知法。【註】吏見知不舉劾爲故縱。</p><p>又相交曰知。【左傳&middot;昭四年】公孫明知叔孫于齊。【註】相親知也。</p><p>又【昭二十八年】魏子曰：昔叔向適鄭，鬷蔑一言而善，執手遂如故知。【楚辭&middot;九歌】樂莫樂兮新相知。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】匹也。【詩&middot;檜風】樂子之無知。【註】匹也。</p><p>又【廣韻】欲也。【禮&middot;樂記】好惡無節於內，知誘於外。</p><p>又猶記憶也。【論語】父母之年，不可不知也。</p><p>又猶主也。【易&middot;繫辭】乾知大始。【左傳&middot;襄二十六年】公孫揮曰：子產其將知政矣。【魏了翁&middot;讀書雜抄】後世官制上知字始此。</p><p>又【揚子&middot;方言】愈也。南楚病愈者，或謂之知。【黃帝素問】二刺則知。【註】上古以小便利腹中和爲知。</p><p>又藥名。【日華志】預知子，取綴衣領上，遇有蠱毒，則聞其有聲。</p><p>又地名。【左傳&middot;昭二十七年】公徒敗于且知。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】知義切。【正韻】知意切。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>與智同。【易&middot;臨卦】知臨大君之宜。【荀子&middot;修身篇】是是非非謂之知。</p><p>又姓。【左傳】晉有知季，卽荀首也。別食知邑，又爲知氏。</p><p>又【諡法】官人應實曰知。</p>
