                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>歧</strong> <span class="dicpy">qí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (同<span class="yhcs">“跂”</span>。形声。从止，支声。或作<span class="yhcs">“岐”</span>。本义:多余的脚趾)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[having extra toes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跂，<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“足多指也。”</span>或作枝歧。——<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歧趾而胪情。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 叉开，事物错出 <span class="encs">[forked;branching]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物两为歧。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歧舌国。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·海外南经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">光歧俨其偕列。——</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《笙赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无为在歧途。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《杜少府之任蜀州》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">多歧路。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《行路难》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:歧歧(禽鸟举翅飞行的样子);歧旨(歧义);歧言(指离别之言);歧意(二心)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 不同，不一致，有差别 <span class="encs">[divergent;different]</span>。如:歧杂(错杂;纷乱);歧趋(不同的趋向)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 聪颖 <span class="encs">[wise]</span>。如:歧嶷(谓幼年聪慧);歧秀(聪慧出众)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>歧</strong> <span class="dicpy">qí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 岔路 <span class="encs">[branch road;forked road]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">路旁一歧东上。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 亦指走入歧路</p><p><span class="diczx1">大道以多歧亡羊。——<span class="smcs">《列子·说符》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:歧旁(旁出的岔路);歧道(歧路，岔路);歧阻(道路阻塞)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
