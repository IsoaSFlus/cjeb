						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>违</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">違</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄟˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>背，反，不遵守：～背。～反。～犯。～法。～抗。～章。事与愿～。</li>
	<li>不见面，离别：久～。</li>
	<li>邪恶，过失。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> disobey, violate, defy; be apart from</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> missachten, nicht gehorchen  (V)​,trennen, ausscheiden  (V)​,verletzen, vergewaltigen  (V)​,Gesetz verletzen ,zuwiderhandeln, verstoßen (gegen das Gesetz, die Regel, die Norm)​</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> désobéir à,enfreindre,violer,être séparé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">违</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>违</strong></p>
<p>違 <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，韦声。本义:离开;背离)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[leave;part]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">违，离也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">违，远也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中心有违。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;谷风》</span>。传:&ldquo;离也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薄违农父。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;酒诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽信美而无礼兮，来违弃而改求。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:违别(离别);违离(离别;分离;背离);违恋(依依惜别);违阔(离别;阔别);违署(离开官署。指辞去官职);违远(远离;离别);违间(离别);违弃(离弃;丢弃);违异(离别;分离)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 避开 <span class="encs">[evade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">纪侯</span>大去其国，违 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>难也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公四年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:违强凌弱(畏避强大，欺凌弱小);违难(避难);违避(避开;背离);违敌(避敌;纵敌);违寒(避开寒冷);违患(避开祸患);违世(避开尘世;去世)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 违背;违反 <span class="encs">[disobey;violate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">违，偝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">静言用违。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且其违者不过数人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而违之俾不通。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不违农时，谷不可胜食也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">违天必有大咎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慎勿违吾语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与我而相违。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫敢违。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君赐不可违。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
