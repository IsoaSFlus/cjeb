						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>雕</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">diāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄠˉ</span></p><ol><li>鸟类的一属，大型猛禽，羽毛褐色，上嘴勾曲，视力很强，利爪，能捕食山羊、野兔等（亦作“<span class="diczx3">鹫</span>”）：～悍。一箭双～。</li><li>刻竹、木、玉、石、金属等：木～。浮～。玉～。～版。～龙（指善于撰写文章）。～镌。～塑。～虫小技。</li><li>有彩绘装饰的：～弓。～鞍。～青（在人体上刺花纹，涂上青色）。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">凋</span>”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">雕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雕</strong></p><p>鵰 <span class="dicpy">diāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)，周声。从<span class="yhcs">“隹”</span>，表示与鸟类有关。本义:大型猛禽。日间活动的大猛禽(鹰科)力大身大，形态优美，视力敏锐，飞行力强。也叫<span class="yhcs">“鹫”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[eagle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流之为雕。——<span class="smcs">《书·太古誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是必射雕者也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李将军列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其状如雕而有角。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·南山经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">回看射雕处，千里暮云平。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《观猎》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:雕悍(凶悍如雕);雕鸷(凶猛如雕);雕鹗(雕和鶚。均为善飞的鸷鸟。比喻人雄放劲健的才力);金雕;海雕;坐山雕</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“碉”</span>。碉堡 <span class="encs">[blockhouse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">依山居止，累石为室。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李贤</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“今彼土夷人呼为‘雕’也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 雕刻的作品;雕刻的图案或人物 <span class="encs">[carving]</span>。如:石雕</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>雕</strong> <span class="dicpy">diāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 治玉。通<span class="yhcs">“琱”</span> <span class="encs">[carve jade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玉栉雕矢磬。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·总目》</span>。谓治玉工也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雕玉仍几。——<span class="smcs">《书·顾命》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:雕人(治玉摩骨的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“彫”</span>。引申为雕刻 <span class="encs">[carve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">下雕辇于东厢。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执雕虎而试象兮。——<span
