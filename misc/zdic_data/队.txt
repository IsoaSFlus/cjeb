                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>队</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">隊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">du&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>排得整齐的行列：～列。～形。</li>
	<li>具有某种性质的集体：～伍。</li>
	<li>量词：一～大学生。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> team, group; army unit</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Geschwader, Gruppe, Mannschaft  (S, Wirtsch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> colonne,rang,équipe,brigade</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">队</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>队</strong></p>
<p>隊 <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:从高处掉下来。是&ldquo;坠&rdquo;的本字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 坠落 <span class="encs">[drop;fall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">队，从高陨也。俗字作坠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殷亩而驰不队。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;轮人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">退人若将队诸渊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公(齐襄公)惧，队于车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈</span>入城，板队而杀人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 失 <span class="encs">[lost]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俾队其师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬不队命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>队</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 隊 <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 队列 <span class="encs">[line]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃分其骑以为四队。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">车按行，骑就队。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《子虚赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:排队;纵队;横队</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 军队或集体的编制单位 <span class="encs">[team;band;contingert;group]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分为二队。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以成一队。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公六年》</span>。注:&ldquo;百人为队。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">襄子疏队而击之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;齐俗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 注:&ldquo;二百人为一队。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲队怪重。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;杨雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:连队;舰队;球队;生产队;军队，部队;队主(一队之主。即队长。六朝时一队军队多由将帅带领)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>队</strong></p>
<p>隊 <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>成群成列的人或物 <span class="encs">[team;group;body of troops]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:一小队士兵;一队巨鳌</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">隊</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“队”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/隊">隊</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中" target="_blank">戌集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中&amp;bs=阜" target="_blank">阜</a>】
                              隊·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/968A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】徒對切【正韻】杜對切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音憝。【說文】从高隊也，失也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】部也。百人也。【廣韻】羣隊也。【左傳&middot;文十六年】楚子乗馹，會師於臨品，分爲二隊。【註】隊，部也。兩道攻之。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】車按行，騎就隊。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>直類切，音懟。【集韻】落也。【禮&middot;檀弓】退人若將隊諸淵。</p>
<p>又【樂記】上如抗，下如隊。【釋文】隊，直媿反。【左傳&middot;成十二年】俾隊其師。【集韻】本作墜。亦作隧。</p>
<p>又【集韻】徐醉切，音遂。與隧同。詳隧字註。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;王莽傳】分爲六尉六隊。【註】隊，音遂。</p>
<p>又【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25846.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥡆</span>天子傳】得絕銒山之隊。【註】隊，謂谷中險阻道也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】杜罪切，憝上聲。羣也。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/968A_7395.svg" /><span class="swbf">(隊)</span><span class="ywyy">從高隊也。</span><span class="swdz">隊墜正俗字，古書多用隊，今則墜行而隊廢矣。大徐以墜附土部，非許意。釋詁：隊，落也。釋文从墜而以隊附見，傎矣。左傳曰：以成一隊。杜注：百人爲隊。葢古語一隊，猶言一堆。物堕於地則聚，因之名隊爲行列之偁。後人以墜入至韵，以隊入隊韵，而莫測其原委矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E0F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨸏</span>㒸聲。</span><span class="swdz">徒對切。十五部。按當云直類切。</span></p>                          </div>
