                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">察</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>察</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">詧</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄚˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>仔细看，调查研究：～看。～核。观～。考～（➊实地观察调查，如&ldquo;科学～～&rdquo;；➋细致深刻地观察，如&ldquo;科学研究要勤于～～和思考&rdquo;）。～觉。～访。～勘。～探。明～秋毫（&ldquo;秋毫&rdquo;指秋天鸟兽身上新长的细毛，喻为人精明，任何小问题都看得清楚）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> examine, investigate; notice</p></div>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">察</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>察</strong></p>
<p>詧 <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span>)，祭声。本义:观察;仔细看)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[examine;look into;scrutinize;observe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">察，复审也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">览察草木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纤微皆审谓之察。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《道术》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">察其所以然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;本味》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">微察公子，公子颜色愈和。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徐而察之，则山下皆石穴罅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:观察(仔细察看客观事物或现象);觉察(发觉，看出来)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 明察，知晓 <span class="encs">[observe;see clearly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小大之狱，虽不能察，必以情。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故察己则可以人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:察士(能明察事理的人);察议(察明情节而议定处分。通常指过失较轻者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 调查;考察 <span class="encs">[investigate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫传言不可以不察。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:察勘(实地调查);察访(详细调查)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 分辨 <span class="encs">[distinguish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">观物弗之察矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹分辨也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视之可察 。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说林》</span>。注:&ldquo;别也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尝百草之实，察酸苦之味。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新语&middot;道基》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 考察后予以推举，举荐 <span class="encs">[recommend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃察举吾弟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;刺客传》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;犹选之也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">察臣孝廉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李密</span><span class="smcs">《陈情表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:察举(选举，选拔);察廉(举廉);察选(察举。选拔)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>察</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>明显;精明 <span class="encs">[apparent;evident;obvious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水至清则无鱼，人至察则无徒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">东方朔</span><span class="smcs">《答客难》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">仔細考核、調查。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「審<b>察</b>」、「考<b>察</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》：「國人皆曰賢，然後<b>察</b>之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷四八&middot;百官志三》：「監察御史十五人，正八品下，掌分<b>察</b>百寮。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">仔細觀看。</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;繫辭上》：「仰以觀於天文，俯以<b>察</b>於地理。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;曹植&middot;洛神賦》：「遠而望之，皎若太陽升朝霞；迫而察之，灼若芙蕖出淥波。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">明辨、了解。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「覺<b>察</b>」、「明察秋毫」。</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;陸賈《新語&middot;道基》：「嘗百草之實，<b>察</b>酸苦之味。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">考察後而保舉。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「察舉」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;李密&middot;陳情表》：「前太守臣逵，<b>察</b>臣孝廉。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋時吳國有察戰。</span></p></li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">察</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=宀" target="_blank">宀</a>】
                              察·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5BDF.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】初八切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】初戛切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音刹。【說文】覆審也。从宀祭聲。【徐鉉曰】祭祀必質明。明，察也。故从祭。【廣韻】諦也，知也。【李陵&middot;答蘇武書】功大罪小，不蒙明察。</p>
<p>又【增韻】考也，廉視也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;士師註】士，察也，義取察理獄訟之事也。</p>
<p>又【唐書&middot;百官志】監察御史，掌司六察。一察官人善惡，二察賦役不均，三察農桑不勤，倉廩耗減，四察妖猾盜賊，五察茂才異等，六察黠吏豪宗，兼幷縱暴。</p>
<p>又昭著也。【中庸】言其上下察也。</p>
<p>又察察，潔淸貌。【史記&middot;屈原傳】安能以身之察察，受物之汶汶。</p>
<p>又苛察也。【老子&middot;道德經】其政察察，其民<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2622B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦈫</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2622B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦈫</span>。</p>
<p>又【晉書&middot;謝安傳】弘以大綱，不存小察。</p>
<p>又【正字通】偏見曰察。【莊子&middot;天下篇】道德不一，天下多得一，察焉以自好。</p>
<p>又姓。吳將軍察戰，明正德中朝城知縣察童。</p>
<p>又讀作祭。【尙書大傳】祭之爲言察也。察者，至也，人事至然後祭。</p>
<p>又叶直列切，音徹。【前漢&middot;郊祀歌】景星顯見，信星彪列。象載昭庭，日親以察。【古詩】置我懷袖中，三歲字不滅。一心抱區區，懼君不識察。</p>
<p>又叶子例切，音穧。【班固&middot;幽通賦】攬葛藟而授余兮，眷峻谷曰勿墜。昒昕寤而仰思兮，心蒙蒙猶未察。　【集韻】或作詧。</p>
