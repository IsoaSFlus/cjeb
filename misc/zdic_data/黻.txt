                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>黻</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从黹( <span class="dicpy">zhǐ</span>)，象缝处纵横交错之形;表示与缝衣或刺绣有关。犮 <span class="dicpy">bó</span>。本义:古代礼服上青黑相间的花纹)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[black and blue figure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子至止，黻衣绣裳。——<span class="smcs">《诗·秦风·终南》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:黻衣(古代礼服的一种)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 绣有这种花纹的礼服 <span class="encs">[ceremonial robe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士服(穿)黻，大夫(服)黼。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《说苑》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:黻班(在宫廷中身穿黻衣的朝班行列。指显贵的地位);黻冕(古代大夫以上祭祀时所穿的礼服。黻:裤裙。冕:帽子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“韨”</span>。古代祭服的蔽膝，用熟皮做成 <span class="encs">[knee hide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衮冕黻珽。——<span class="smcs">《左传·桓公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以黻冕命士会将中军。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恶衣服，而致美乎黻冕。——<span class="smcs">《论语·泰伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“绂”</span>。系印的丝带 <span class="encs">[silk band]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">云</span>装信解黻。—— <span class="diczx2">江淹</span><span class="smcs">《杂体·谢光禄庄郊游》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">委荣轻举，投黻如遗。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《繁阳令杨君碑》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:长黻(印章上长长的丝带)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">黻</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>黻</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄈㄨˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>fú <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0969.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代礼服上所刺绣的花纹，青黑相间，如两己相背之形；一说两弓相背之形。</span><span class="gc_yy">《书经·益稷》：「藻火粉米，黼<b>黻</b>𫄨绣。」</span><span class="gc_yy">汉·孔安国·传：「<b>黻</b>为两己相背。<b>黻</b>音弗，黑与青谓之<b>黻</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代一种系于腰间，遮于官服或礼服前的蔽膝。</span><span class="gc_yy">《论语·泰伯》：「恶衣服，而致美乎黻冕。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·邢昺·疏：「<b>黻</b>，蔽膝也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐书·卷二四·车服志》：「 <b>黻</b>以缯为之，随裳色。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「韨」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">系印的丝带。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·陈子昂〈唐水衡监亟李府君墓志铭〉：「黄<b>黻</b>不贵，拱璧为轻。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「绂」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9EFB.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】分勿切【集韻】分物切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音弗。【說文】黑與靑相次文。【爾雅·釋言】黼黻，彰也。【郭註】黼文如斧，黻文如兩己相背。</p><p>又【左傳·桓二年】袞冕黻珽。【杜註】黻，韋韠，以蔽膝也。</p><p>又【釋名】黻冕，黻，紩也。畫黻紩文綵於衣也。此皆隨衣而名之也。所垂前後珠轉減耳。○按黻之狀如①。①，古弗字。《增韻》云：兩已相背形。《周禮·司服》註疏，黻取臣民背惡向善，亦取合離之義，去就之理。①字两弓相背。</p><p>
