                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">压</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>压</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">壓</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄚˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>从上面加力：～住。～碎。～缩。泰山～顶。</li>
	<li>用威力制服、镇服：镇～。～服。～迫。</li>
	<li>控制，使稳定，使平静：～价。～住阵脚。</li>
	<li>搁置：积～。</li>
	<li>逼近：大兵～境。</li>
	<li>赌博时在某一门上下注：～宝（亦作&ldquo;押宝&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>压</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">壓</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～根儿〕根本，从来，如&ldquo;我～～～就不理解这件事&rdquo;。</li>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">压</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>压</strong></p>
<p>壓 <span class="dicpy">yā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，厌声。本义:崩坏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[collapse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">压，坏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侨将压焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾惧压焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span>。注:&ldquo;笮也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">壤压之变。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:压溺(土崩和水淹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 从上往下增加重力，加压 <span class="encs">[press]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千朵万朵压枝低。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江畔独步寻花》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黑云压城城欲摧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《雁门太守行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:压肩叠背(肩靠肩背挨背，形容人群拥挤);压山探海(形容人数众多);压尺(用金属或玉石做成的压纸用的尺状文具);压枝(果实多，把树枝压低)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 压抑，压制;逼迫 <span class="encs">[restrain;force;coerce;compel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举杰压陛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;大招》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予以大国压之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;文公十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">克压帝心。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《繁阳令杨君碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陵压百姓。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:压倒(压服;制服);压良为贱(掠买平民子女为奴婢)</span></p>
