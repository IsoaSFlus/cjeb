                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">稽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>稽</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾( <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> )，象树木曲头止住不上长的样子，从尤，旨声。本义:停留;阻滞)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。引申为囤积 <span class="encs">[delay;detain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稽，留止也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">司稽，察留连不时去者。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司稽》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之水泔最而稽。——<span class="smcs">《管子·水地》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令出而不稽。——<span class="smcs">《管子·君臣上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稽市物。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“贮滞也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span>
