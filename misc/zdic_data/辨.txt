                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辨</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>辨</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>分别，分析，明察：～别。～认。～析。～正。～识。明～是非。</li>
	<li>古代土地面积单位，九夫为一辨，七辨为一并。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> distinguish, discriminate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> unterscheiden, wahrnehmen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> distinguer,discerner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>辨</strong> <span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，辡( <span class="dicpy">biǎn</span>)声。本义:判别，区分，辨别)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[discriminate;distinguish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辨，判也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辨，别也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辨方正位。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辨是与非。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">目辨白黑美恶，耳辨音声清浊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;荣辱》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不辨牛马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男女辨姓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">双兔傍地走，安能辨我是雄雌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:明辨是非;辨白(分辨清楚);辨色(辨别物色)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;辩&rdquo;。口头上争论 <span class="encs">[argue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾闻穷巷多怪，曲学多辨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;更法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">传曰:析辞而为察，言物而为辨，君子贱之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;解蔽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分争辨讼，非礼不决。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故略上报，不复一一自辨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辨士(善辩的说客);辨给(口才敏捷，能言善辨);辨驳(根据事理加以反驳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;班&rdquo;。颁布 <span class="encs">[promulgate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吏以文法教训辨告，勿笞辱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辨社诸侯，出门见之，著以为戒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;王莽传上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辨告(古代官吏将所订法律颁布告知民众)</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>辨</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄅㄧㄢˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>bi&agrave;n <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0247.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">分別、判別。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「辨別」、「分<b>辨</b>」、「明辨是非」、「雌雄莫<b>辨</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;榮 辱》：「目辨白黑美惡，耳<b>辨</b>音聲清濁。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈秋雨歎〉三首之二：「去馬來牛不復<b>辨</b>，濁涇清渭何當分。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">爭論是非曲直。</span><span class="gc_yy">《戰國策&middot;趙策三》：「鄂侯爭之急，<b>辨</b>之疾，故脯鄂侯。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;陶淵明〈飲酒詩〉二○首之五：「此還有真意，欲<b>辨</b>已忘言。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「辯」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">文體名。如唐代柳宗元的〈桐葉封弟辨〉。也作「辯」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辨</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=辛" target="_blank">辛</a>】
                              辨·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8FA8.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】皮莧切【正韻】備莧切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音辯。【說文】判也。【廣韻】別也。【易&middot;乾卦】問以辨之。【禮&middot;學記】離經辨志。【註】辨，謂考問得其定也。【周禮&middot;天官】弊羣吏之治，六曰廉辨。【註】辨，謂辨然于事分明，無有疑惑也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】牀䏶足笫閒也。【易&middot;剝卦】剝牀以辨。【疏】牀足之上，牀身之下，分辨處也。【程傳】牀之幹也。</p>
<p>又變也。【楚辭&middot;九辨註】辨者，變也。謂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>道德以變說君也。</p>
<p>又井地之數也。【禮&middot;王制&middot;註】京陵之地，九夫爲辨，九辨而當一井。</p>
<p>又姓。</p>
<p>又【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>見切，音徧。與徧通。帀也。【史記&middot;禮書】萬民和喜，瑞應辨至。【註】同徧。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】苻蹇切【集韻】【韻會】平免切【正韻】婢免切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>辯上聲。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】邦免切，鞭上聲。義同。</p>
<p>又與貶通。【禮&middot;玉藻】立容辨<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>，無讇。【註】辨作貶。容雖貶損<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>降，不傾側柔媚也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】普麪切【集韻】【正韻】匹見切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音片。革中斷也。【爾雅&middot;釋器】革中絕謂之辨，革中辨謂之韏。【註】革中斷之名辨，復中分其辨名韏。【集韻】作㸤。</p>
