						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>卷</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>可以舒展和弯转成圆筒形的书画：长～。画～。手～。</li>
	<li>书籍的册本或篇章：上～。第一～。藏书十万～。～帙（书卷成束，用布裹或布囊装起来称&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">帙</span>&rdquo;，即书套。现一般指书籍）。</li>
	<li>考试用的纸：试～。</li>
	<li>机关里分类汇存的档案、文件：案～。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>卷</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">捲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">juǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/juǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>把东西弯转裹成圆筒形：～尺。～帘子。</li>
	<li>裹挟带动：～入。～扬。</li>
	<li>弯转裹成筒形的东西：烟～儿。纸～儿。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卷</strong> <span class="dicpy">juǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/juǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从卩( <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span>，甲骨文象人曲膝而跪的样子)，劵( <span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。( <span class="dicpy">quǎn</span>)本义:膝曲)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 把物弯转成圆筒形 <span class="encs">[roll up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有卷者阿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;卷 阿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赢缩卷舒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span>。注:&ldquo;卷，屈也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">漫卷诗书喜欲狂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《闻官军收河南河北》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5377.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】居倦切【集韻】【韻會】古倦切【正韻】吉掾切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音眷。【說文】厀曲也。</p>
<p>又書卷。【揚子&middot;法言】一卷之書，必立之師。【增韻】可舒卷者曰卷。編次者曰帙。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】居轉切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】古轉切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>眷上聲。【廣韻】舒卷之卷。【韻會】或作捲。</p>
<p>又卷耳，艸名。【詩&middot;周南】采采卷耳。</p>
<p>又卷舌，星名。【前漢&middot;劉向傳】客星見昴卷之閒。【註】師古曰：見於昴與卷舌之閒也。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】巨員切【集韻】【韻會】逵員切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音權。【集韻】曲也。【詩&middot;大雅】有卷者阿。</p>
<p>又【中庸】一卷，石之多。【註】猶區也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】大卷，黃帝樂名。【周禮&middot;大司樂註】卷者，卷聚之義。</p>
<p>又通作惓。【前漢&middot;賈捐之傳】敢昧死竭卷卷。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>驅圓切，音㒽。【集韻】縣名，在河南。【前漢&middot;周勃傳】周勃，沛人，其先卷人也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】一曰冠武。</p>
<p>又【正韻】古本切，音袞。【禮&middot;王制】三公一命卷，天子卷冕。</p>
