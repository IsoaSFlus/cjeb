                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>犯</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>抵触，违反：～规。～法。～罪。～颜（旧时指冒犯君王或尊长的威严）。～讳。</li>
	<li>违反法律的人，有罪的人：～人。罪～。</li>
	<li>侵害，进攻：侵～。秋毫无～。</li>
	<li>触发，发作：～病。～愁。～疑。</li>
	<li>做错事情：～错误。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> commit crime, violate; criminal</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> enfreindre,violer,attaquer,commettre,tomber dans,délinquant,criminel</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">犯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>犯</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[invade;assail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犯，侵也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，字本言犬犯人。转注为凡干陵违逆之称。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">軓又</span><span class="diczx2">义兴</span>水中有蛟，山中有白额虎，并皆暴犯百姓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;自新》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:人不犯我，我不犯人;犯界(侵犯他国领土);犯境(犯边。侵犯边界)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 触犯，冒犯 <span class="encs">[offend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若有作奸犯科及为忠善者，宜付有司论其刑赏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原来</span><span class="diczx2">杨修</span>为人恃才放旷，数犯 <span class="diczx2">曹操</span>之忌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:犯忌讳;犯阙(危害国家，对抗朝廷。阙:指朝廷);犯颜(冒犯他人的尊严。颜:颜面，引申为尊严，威严);犯鳞(因直谏触犯君王);犯众(触犯众人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 袭击 <span class="encs">[attack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">王祖</span>帅诸垒共救之，夜犯 <span class="diczx2">燕</span>军， <span class="diczx2">燕</span>人逆击，走之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 遭遇;顶着;冒着 <span class="encs">[meet;brave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">触风雨，犯寒暑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盖一岁之犯死者二焉。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 侵害 <span class="encs">[prejudice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水火之所犯，犹不可救，而况天乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 伤害，损害 <span class="encs">[damage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">季子皋</span>葬其妻，犯人之禾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 欺凌，污辱 <span class="encs">[bully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后盗欲有犯妻者，乃先劫其姑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 违背;违反 <span class="encs">[violate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犯令陵政则杜之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司马》</span>。注:&ldquo;犯令者，违命也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犷悍无赖，犯法当死。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:犯干(违反;触犯);犯顺(违背情理);犯节(违背礼节);犯教(违背礼教)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 值得(与不连用) <span class="encs">[be worth of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">四丫头</span>不犯罗唣你，却是谁呢?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 发生(多指不好的事) <span class="encs">[incur]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:犯难;犯了什么错事;犯拙(弄僵)</span></p>
