                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">含</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>含</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hán.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄢˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>衔在嘴里，不吐出也不咽下：～一口水。～漱剂。～英咀（<span class="dicpy">jǔ</span>）华（喻反复琢磨体味文章的妙处）。～饴弄孙（含着糖逗小孙子，形容老年人的乐趣）。</li>    <li>藏在里面，包容在里面：包～。～义。～量。～苞。～蕴。～混。～垢纳污（指包容坏人坏事）。</li>    <li>怀有某种感情或意思，不完全表露出来：～怒。～羞。～情。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>hold in mouth; cherish; contain</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufhalten, halten  (V)​,beinhalten, umfassen  (V)​,lutschen  (V)​,inbegriffen  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> contenir,renfermer,garder dans la bouche</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">含</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>含</strong> <span class="dicpy">hán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，今声。本义:含在嘴里)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[keep in the mouth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含，嗛也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含，口也，合口亭之也。衔亦然也。——<span class="smcs">《释名·释饮食》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含哺而熙，鼓腹而游。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·马蹄》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生不布施，死何含珠为?——<span class="smcs">《庄子·外物》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含杜若，握玉环。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·内储说下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾但当含饴弄孙。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·明德马皇后传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">口如含朱丹。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">儿含乳啼。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含乳啼声。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:含哺(口衔食物);含殓(含口;含口垫背。死人入殓时，把珍珠等物放在死人嘴里，叫<span class="yhcs">“含口”</span>。把金钱放在死人身下，叫<span class="yhcs">“垫背”</span>);含咀(衔在口中咀嚼。比喻品味)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 容纳;包含 <span class="encs">[contain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阿女含泪答。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含有音乐。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含文学之趣味。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为之含悲。——</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《<黄花冈七十二烈士事略>序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:含元(包含万物的本原;包含元气);含气(含藏元气);含孕(包含;孕育);含光(内藏光采，包藏美德)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 忍受;宽容 <span class="encs">[bear;endure]</span>。如:含悲茹痛(忍受痛苦);含垢(忍受耻辱);含容(容忍;宽恕);含辱(忍受耻辱);含贷(包涵，宽恕)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 怀着不露;隐藏在内 <span class="encs">[cherish;nurse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">少年别有赠，含笑看吴钩。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《后出塞五首》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使渠弥含憎惧死以侥幸。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·难囚》</span></span></p><p><span
