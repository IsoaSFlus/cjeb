                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饩</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>饩</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">餼</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古代祭祀或馈赠用的活牲畜。</li>	<li>赠送人的粮食或饲料。</li>	<li>赠送食物。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sacrficial victim; gift; grain</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> don de vivres</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>饩</strong></p>
<p>餼 <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，气声。字本作&ldquo;氣&rdquo;。本义:赠送人的谷物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[donative grains]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常从容上席，资饩甚厚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饩食(米粮等食物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 活的牲口 <span class="encs">[live sacrifice stock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脯资饩牵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饩羊(用为祭品的羊);饩牵(活牲口)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 生肉 <span class="encs">[red meat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出牲饩以劳师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧五代史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饩牢(屠宰过的牲口);饩馈(生肉与粮食)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 给养;俸禄 <span class="encs">[grain for rations]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生二人，公与之饩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饩廪(粮食之类的生活物资);饩稍(每月领的俸粮)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/993C.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】許旣切【韻會】於旣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音欷。【玉篇】饋餉也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;司儀】致饔餼。【註】小禮曰飧，大禮曰饔餼。</p><p>又【周語】廩人獻餼。【註】禾米也。</p><p>又牲生也。【儀禮&middot;聘禮】餼之以其禮，上賔大牢，積惟芻禾，介皆有餼。【註】凡賜人以牲生曰餼。餼猶稟也，結也。【疏】按經云：主國使卿歸饔餼。五牢云：飪一牢，腥二牢，餼二牢，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>于門西。鄭註云：餼，生也。牛羊右手牽之，豕東之，是牲生曰餼。論語，告朔之餼羊，鄭註亦云：牲生曰餼。春秋傳，餼臧石牛，服氏亦云：牲生。是凡牲生曰餼。春秋僖三十三年：鄭皇武子云：餼牽竭矣。服氏以爲腥曰餼，以其對牽，故以餼爲腥。詩序云：雖有牲牢饔餼。鄭云：腥曰餼，以其對生是活，故以餼爲腥。</p><p>又【魯語】馬餼不過稂莠。【註】秣也。◎按說文本作氣。餼，饋客芻米也。齊人來氣諸侯，見春秋桓十年。或从食作餼，或从旣作䊠，亦借旣，義<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。今通作餼。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“饩”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/氣">氣</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=米">米</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6C23.svg">                          <p>饋客芻米也。从米气聲。《春秋傳》曰：&ldquo;齊人來氣諸矦。&rdquo;許旣切。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6C23_1.svg" />䊠，氣或从旣。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6C23_2.svg" />餼，氣或从食。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6C23_3191.svg" /><span class="swbf">(氣)</span><span class="ywyy">饋客之芻米也。</span><span class="swdz">聘禮殺曰饔，生曰餼。餼有牛羊豕黍粱稻稷禾薪芻等。不言牛羊豕者，以其字从米也。言芻米不言禾者，舉芻米可以該禾也。經典謂生物曰餼。論語：告朔之餼羊。</span><span class="ywyy">从米气聲。</span><span class="swdz">許旣切。十五部。今字叚氣爲雲氣字，而饔餼乃無作氣者。</span><span class="ywyy">春秋傳曰：齊人來氣諸侯。</span><span class="swdz">事見左傳桓六年、十年。十年傳曰：齊人餼諸侯。許所據作氣。左丘明述春秋傳以古文，於此可見。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6C23_3192.svg" /><span class="swbf">(䊠)</span><span class="ywyy">氣或从旣。</span><span class="swdz">旣聲也。聘禮記曰：日如其饔旣之數。注云：古文旣爲餼。中庸篇曰：旣稟稱事。注云：旣讀爲餼。大戴朝事篇：私覿致饔旣。戴先生曰：旣卽餼字。按三旣皆䊠之省。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6C23_3193.svg" /><span class="swbf">(餼)</span><span class="ywyy">氣或从食。</span><span class="swdz">按从食而氣爲聲，葢晚出俗字，在假氣爲气之後。</span></p>                          </div>
