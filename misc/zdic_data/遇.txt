                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">遇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>遇</strong> <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，禺( <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:相逢，不期而会)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[meet;encounter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遇，逢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遇主于巷。——<span class="smcs">《易·暌》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">崔</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“遇者，不期而会。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃遇汝鸠汝方。——<span class="smcs">《书·序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸侯未及期相见曰遇。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子路</span>从而后，遇丈人。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夏，公及</span><span class="diczx2">宋公</span>遇于 <span class="diczx2">清</span>;遇者何?不期也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:遇庭(和父母相见);遇人不淑(指女子嫁了不好的丈夫);遇缺补正(指遇到员额出缺时，得补任为官)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 遭受 <span class="encs">[suffer;sustain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遇人之艰难矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·中谷有蓷》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">遇人之不淑矣。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公等遇雨，皆已失期。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:遇厄(遭逢灾难);遇疾(生病);遇谗(遭受谗言的陷害);遇谴(遭到谴责);遇难(遭到灾难);遇谤(遭到毁谤);遇乱(遭遇战乱)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 对待、相待 <span class="encs">[treat;entertain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衔恩遇。——<span class="smcs">《广绝交论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公子遇臣厚。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:冷遇(冷淡的待遇);礼遇(尊敬有礼的待遇);优遇(优待)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 抵挡;对付 <span class="encs">[face]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无用吾之所短，遇人之所长。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·大略》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:遇敌(抵挡敌军;对付敌方)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 遇合;投合 <span class="encs">[meet and get along well]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王何不与寡人遇。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“合也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">善仕不如遇合。——<span class="smcs">《史记·佞幸列传序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 接触 <span class="encs">[contact;touch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">方今之时，臣以神遇，不以目视。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·养生主》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">木遇之而叶脱。——</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《秋声赋》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>遇</strong> <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 际遇;机会 <span class="encs">[chance;opportunity]</span></p><p><span
