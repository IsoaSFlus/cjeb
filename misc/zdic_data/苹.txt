                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>苹</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">蘋</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～果〕①落叶乔木，开白花；②这种植物的果实，球形，红色或黄色，味甜或略酸。</li></ol><p>● <strong>苹</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pēng.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄥˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～萦〕回旋的样子，如&ldquo;争湍～～，汨活澎濞&rdquo;。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">苹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>苹</strong></p>
<p>蘋 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，平声。本义:浮萍)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[duckweed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苹，无根浮水而生者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 蒿的一种。即青蒿 <span class="encs">[wormwood;artemisia]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">呦呦鹿呜，食野之苹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鹿鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:苹藻主(妻子);苹敬(礼金)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 同&ldquo;蘋&rdquo;。见&ldquo;苹果&rdquo; <span class="encs">[apple]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苹蘩(指婚仪、婚姻);苹藻(指妇女的言行规范);苹婆(凤眼果)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;軿&rdquo;。兵车的一种 <span class="encs">[boxcar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苹车之萃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;春官&middot;车仆》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>苹</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p>草丛生的样子 <span class="encs">[growing thickly]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苹苹(草聚集丛生的样子。也指丛生的草)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/82F9.svg">                          <p>蓱也。無根，浮水而生者。从艸平聲。符兵切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/82F9_5468.svg" /><span class="swbf">(苹)</span><span class="ywyy">蓱也，無根浮水而生者。</span><span class="swdz">小雅：呦呦鹿鳴，食野之苹。傳曰：苹，蓱也。釋草苹字㒳出，一曰蓱，一曰藾蕭。鄭箋以水中之艸非鹿所食，易之曰苹，藾蕭也。於有令曰蓱，萍也。於周禮萍氏引爾雅萍蓱，似分別萍爲水艸，苹爲藾蕭。鄭所據爾雅自作萍蓱。而毛詩、夏小正以苹爲萍，皆屬叚借。許君則苹蓱萍三字同物，不謂苹爲叚借。○李善注高唐賦引說文：苹苹，艸皃，音平。</span><span class="ywyy">从艸平聲。</span><span class="swdz">符兵切。十一部。</span></p>                          </div>
