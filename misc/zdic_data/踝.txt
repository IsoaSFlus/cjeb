						<p>● <strong>踝</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄞˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～骨〕脚腕两旁凸起的部分。亦称&ldquo;踝子骨&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ankle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Fußknöchel, Knöchel  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cheville (anatomie)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">踝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>踝</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，果声。本义:小腿与脚之间左右两侧突起部分)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ankle;malleolus]</span>，在足和小腿之间的关节，相当于前臂腕关节，马的跗关节，在人类构成屈戌关节，位于上方的胫、腓骨和下方的距骨间</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">踝，足踝也。从足，果声。谓足左右隆然圆起者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:内踝;外踝;踝胫(踝骨与胫骨);踝拐(方言。踝骨);踝子骨(方言。踝骨。内踝和外踝的统称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 脚跟 <span class="encs">[heel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">负绳及踝以应直。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;深衣》</span>。注:&ldquo;跟也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:负绳及踝(将绳带系于脚跟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 脚 <span class="encs">[foot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:削踝(古代砍去双脚的刑罚)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>踝</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;剐&rdquo;。割 <span class="encs">[cut]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:踝舌(割去舌头);踝草(割草)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用同&ldquo;裸&rdquo; <span class="encs">[bare]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:踝蹄婆(对妓女的贬称。踝，用同&ldquo;裸&rdquo;);踝体(裸体)</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>踝</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄨㄞˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>​hu&aacute;i</rt></rtc></ruby><small class="youyin">語音</small> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2881.ogg"></a></span> </div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">小腿和腳底連接處，左右兩旁凸起的圓骨稱為「踝」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「腳<b>踝</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;足部》：「<b>踝</b>，足<b>踝</b>也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;段玉裁&middot;注：「<b>踝</b>者，人足左右骨隆然圜者也。在外者謂之外<b>踝</b>，在內者謂之內<b>踝</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">腳後跟。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;深衣》：「曲袷如矩以應方，負繩及<b>踝</b>以應直。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;鄭玄&middot;注：「<b>踝</b>，跟也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
