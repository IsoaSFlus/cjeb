						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>准</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">➋-➒準</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhǔn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǔn.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄣˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>允许，许可：～许。～予。批～。</li>
	<li>〈介〉依照，依据：～此处理。</li>
	<li>定平直的东西：水～。～绳。</li>
	<li>标准，法则，可以做为依据的：～则。标～。</li>
	<li>箭靶的中心：～的（d&igrave;）。</li>
	<li>正确：～确。～星。瞄～。</li>
	<li>〈副〉一定，确实：～保。～定。</li>
	<li>鼻子：隆～（高鼻子）。</li>
	<li>和某类事物差不多，如同，类似：～尉。～平原。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">准</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>准</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (准本为凖的俗字，现为凖的简化字。但在未简化前，准习用的意义，与凖字有别)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 公文用语 <span class="encs">[permit]</span>。始于唐&middot;五代 <span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示允许，认可</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃于战所，准当时兵士，人种树一株，以旌武功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;文帝纪下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:批准;照准;准如所请 <span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为比照，作某类事物看待。如:准前例办理;准平原;准尉 <span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示依据、根据。如:准某部函</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 决定，必定 <span class="encs">[determine]</span>。公文用语。如:准于某日起程</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这准是使猛了劲，岔了气了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 折充;抵充 <span class="encs">[convert into;compensate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">连身上外盖衣服，脱下准了店钱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醒世恒言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新旧钱暂兼行，新钱千准银一两，旧钱准七钱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《清史稿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我有心看上她，与我家做个媳妇，就准了这四十两银子;岂不两得其便。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:准价(折价);可准(可以抵偿);准算(折算;抵帐)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>准</strong></p>
<p>凖 <span class="dicpy">zhǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，隼( <span class="dicpy">sǔn</span>)声。本义:平，不倾斜)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(取义于水平，侧重于平稳) <span class="encs">[level]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凖，平也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦俗作准。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;谓水之平也。天下莫平于水，水平谓之凖&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">推而放诸东海而凖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭义》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹平也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 亦指其他物体的平</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">輈注则利凖，利凖则久，和则安。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:准望(测绘地图的方法之一)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 准确 <span class="encs">[accurate;precise;exact]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:不知准不准;投篮准;打得准;猜得准;准成(准星;准确可靠);准当(准确恰当);那准不对;没准儿</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 轻重相当;均等 <span class="encs">[equal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与天地准，故能弥纶天地之道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
