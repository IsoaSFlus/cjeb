                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荣</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>荣</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">榮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>草木茂盛，引申为兴盛：～枯（草木盛衰，喻得意失意）。～悴（荣枯）。</li>
	<li>受人敬重，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">辱</span>&rdquo;相对：光～。～升。～誉。</li>
	<li>&ldquo;梧桐&rdquo;的别称。</li>
	<li>草开花，亦泛指草木的花：绿叶素～。～华。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> glory, honor; flourish, prosper</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ehren  (V)​,blühend  (Adj)​,florierend  (Adj)​,Rong  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> prospère,gloire</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>荣</strong></p>
<p>榮 <span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，荧省声。本义:梧桐。又金文字形，象两支如火把相互照耀的花朵或穗头形)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Chinese parasol (tree)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荣，桐木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荣，桐木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:&ldquo;即梧桐。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 草本植物的花。又为花的通称 <span class="encs">[flower]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木谓之华，草谓之荣，不荣而实者谓之秀，荣而不实者谓之英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攀条折其荣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《古诗十九首》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荣悴(草木的开花与凋萎);荣年(百花争艳的季节);荣色(花的色彩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 飞檐，屋檐两头翘起的部分 <span class="encs">[upturned eaves]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">直于东荣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span>。注:屋翼也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">暴于南荣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;南檐也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荣阿(飞檐的曲隅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 荣誉。良好的名声或社会名望 <span class="encs">[honor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且辱者也而荣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;振乱》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">会九世而飞荣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南都赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荣级(荣誉爵位);启荣(死后的荣誉)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>荣</strong></p>
<p>榮 <span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 繁茂，茂盛 <span class="encs">[grow luxuriantly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木欣欣以向荣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荣谢(草木茂盛与凋零);荣芬(茂盛，繁密);荣旺(植物生长旺盛);荣条(茂盛的枝条);荣敷(荣荣。草木茂盛的样子);荣熙(繁盛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 繁荣 <span class="encs">[flourish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">室宫荣与。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span>。注:&ldquo;盛也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荣昌(繁荣昌盛);荣泰(繁荣安定);荣怀(国家繁荣则万民归附);荣茂(繁荣茂盛);荣落(荣盛与衰落);荣畅(荣盛);荣观(荣盛的景观);荣仕(高官显爵);荣逸(显赫安乐);荣国(昌盛之国)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 盛多;丰富 <span class="encs">[abundant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宫室荣与?妇谒盛与?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 光荣，荣耀。与&ldquo;辱&rdquo;相反 <span class="encs">[glory;honor]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:不以为耻，反以为荣;荣近(光荣亲近);荣映(荣耀);荣峻(显耀崇高);荣秩(荣耀与官阶);荣辉(荣耀，光彩);荣福(荣耀与幸福);荣践(荣耀的经历)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 富贵;显荣 <span class="encs">[wealth and rank]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:荣伍(尊显者的行列);显荣(显达荣贵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 荣华 <span class="encs">[splendor]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:荣乐(荣华逸乐);荣冀(对荣华富贵的欲望);荣庆(荣华幸福);荣贵(荣华富贵);荣伸(荣华显耀);荣润(光华润泽)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>荣</strong></p>
<p>榮 <span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 开花 <span class="encs">[bloom]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸越则桃李冬实，朔漠则桃李夏荣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 抛弃 <span class="encs">[throw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荣汝之粮，不若遄归也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 使&hellip;荣耀;使&hellip;光荣 <span class="encs">[make glory]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">足以荣汝身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以</span><span class="diczx2">蓼洲</span><span class="diczx2">周公</span>，忠义暴于朝廷，赠溢美显，荣于身后。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荣除之庆(封官授爵的喜庆);</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;营&rdquo;。经营 <span class="encs">[manage;engage in]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;荧&rdquo;。炫惑<span class="encs">[dazzle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不掩欲以荣君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋&middot;内篇问上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以荣其意而乱其政。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;内储说下》</span></span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“荣”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/榮">榮</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              榮·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/69AE.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】永兵切【集韻】【正韻】于平切【韻會】於營切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音營。【說文】桐木也。見桐字註。</p>
<p>又屋梠之兩頭起者爲榮。【禮&middot;喪大記】升自東榮，降自西北榮。【註】榮屋翼也。</p>
<p>又榮華。【爾雅&middot;釋草】木謂之華，草謂之榮。不榮而實者謂之秀，榮而不實者謂之英。</p>
<p>又【淮南子&middot;時則訓】秋行夏令爲華，行春令爲榮。</p>
<p>又榮者，辱之反。【老子&middot;道德經】知其榮，守其辱。</p>
<p>又人以血爲榮，以氣爲衞。【內經】榮衞不行，五臟不通。</p>
<p>又【諡法】寵祿光大曰榮。</p>
<p>又州名。【前漢&middot;地理志】屬犍爲郡，唐置榮州。</p>
<p>又杜榮，草也。《爾雅》莣，杜榮。註似茅，皮可以爲繩索履屩。</p>
<p>又姓。【史記&middot;聖門弟子傳】榮旂。【莊子】榮啓期。</p>
<p>又【集韻】維傾切，音營。義同。</p>
<p>又叶以中切，音融。【越絕書】種留封侯，不知令終。二賢比德，種獨不榮。【楊愼云】東韻宜收榮字。</p>
<p>又叶爲命切，音詠。【揚雄&middot;太<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>經】宗其高年，鬼待敬也。牽羊于叢，不足榮也。鼎血之蕕，信王命也。</p>
