                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>距</strong> <span class="dicpy">jù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，巨声。本义:鸡爪)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chicken claw]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">距，鸡距也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后阺为之金距。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不鸣不将无距。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“距，鸡附足骨，斗时所用刺之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽有钩针芒距。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长皆及俎距。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·少牢礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“胫中当横节也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">公输</span>之刻凤也，冠距未成。——<span class="smcs">《列子》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 距离 <span class="encs">[distance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">距随长武。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡射礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:苗距;槽距;差距;焦距;行距;株距;距国(介于大国之间，或两面受敌的国家);距随(古人射箭站立时两脚的间隔距离)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 倒刺 <span class="encs">[glochis]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代兵器锋刃上的倒刺</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 钓钩上的倒刺</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>距</strong> <span class="dicpy">jù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="encs">[古]</span>∶同<span class="yhcs">“拒”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 抗拒;抵御 <span class="encs">[resist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">墨子</span>九距之。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">密人不恭，敢距大邦。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·皇矣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">断足以距难。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·君道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">楚闻之，发兵距之</span><span class="diczx2">阳夏</span>，不得前。——<span class="smcs">《史记·高祖本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拒绝 <span class="encs">[refuse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闲先圣之道，距</span><span class="diczx2">杨</span><span class="diczx2">墨</span>，放淫辞。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其无距言。——<span class="smcs">《潜夫论·明闇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受而不距。——<span class="smcs">《风俗通义·愆礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">距关，毋内诸侯。(距，与<span class="yhcs">“拒”</span>通，抵檝、守卫。)——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:距捍(抵抗防御);距谏饰非(不听劝谏，掩饰过错);距关(闭关据守);距难(拒绝并加以责难;抗拒祸患)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 离;距离 <span class="encs">[be apart from;be at adistance from]</span></p><p><span
