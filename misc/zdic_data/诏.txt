                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诏</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">詔</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhào.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄠˋ</span></p><ol><li>告诉，告诫：为人父者，必能～其子。</li><li>帝王所发的文书命令：～书。～令。～谕。奉～。遗～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> decree, proclaim; imperial decree</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> kaiserliches Edikt ,unterrichten, unterweisen, belehren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> proclamer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诏</strong></p><p>詔 <span class="dicpy">zhào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从言从召，召亦声。<span class="yhcs">“言”</span>是言论，<span class="yhcs">“召”</span>是召唤。本义:告知，告诉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tell;let know;inform]</span>。先秦没有此字，秦汉才出现，多用于上告下</p><p><span class="diczx1">诏，告也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">负剑辟咡诏之，出入有诏于国。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上诏书者。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“诏，诰也。有三品。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以诏后世。——<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是上乃使使持节诏将军。——<span class="smcs">《史记·绛侯周勃世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">集</span><span class="diczx2">谢庄</span>少年之精技击者而诏之曰。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:诏众(告诉众人);诏赞(告知和赞助)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 教导;告诫 <span class="encs">[instruct;admonish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫为人父者，必能诏其子。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·盗跖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">多分其教诏。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·审分》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:诏导(教导);诏诲(告诫教导);诏侑(劝告);诏志(教导立身养志);诏相(教导行大礼的言行)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 皇帝下达命令 <span class="encs">[give imperial edict]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诏使儒卿。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">优诏奖瑞。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诏赐抚臣。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:诏求(皇帝下令征求);诏征(皇帝下令征召);诏选(皇帝下令任命);诏用(皇帝下令任用)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 征召 <span class="encs">[recruit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明诏期，前后农夫，以时均修焉，…。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:诏期(征召的日期)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 召见 <span class="encs">[call in]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诏</span><span class="diczx2">伊尹</span>于 <span class="diczx2">亳</span>( <span class="dicpy">bó</span>)郊兮。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·冯衍传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 宣扬 <span class="encs">[advocate; champion; propagate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未可明诏大号。——</span><span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 报告 <span class="encs">[report]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>礼诸职凡言诏者，皆下告上之辞。—— <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不以诏。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽诏于天子无北面。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·学记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故礼有摈诏。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼器》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诏西皇使涉予。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>诏</strong></p><p>詔 <span                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A54.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】之少切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】之笑切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音照。【說文】吿也。【爾雅·釋詁】導也。【註】敎導之也。【又】勴也。【註】謂贊勉。【疏】敎導，卽贊勉也。【韻會】詔者，上下通用之義。【左傳·成二年】欒伯曰：燮之詔也。書何力之有焉。【註】吿也。【周禮·天官·大宰】以八柄詔王馭羣臣。【註】告也，助也。【莊子·盜跖篇】爲人父者，必能詔其子。【如淳註】敎也。</p><p>又【廣韻】上命也。秦漢以下，天子獨稱之。【史記·秦始皇紀】二十六年，秦幷天下，丞相王綰、御史大夫馮劫、廷尉李斯等，議命爲制，令爲詔。【前漢·周勃傳】軍中聞將軍之命，不聞天子詔。</p><p>又【廣韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26620.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦘠</span>也。【正韻】播吿之書也。【文心雕龍】漢命有三品，三曰詔書。詔，吿也。【釋名】詔書。詔，昭也。人暗不見事宜，則有所犯，以此示之，使昭然知所由也。【文中子·問易篇】程元曰：續書之有志，有詔，何謂也。文中子曰：志以成道，言以宣志，詔其見王者之志乎。</p><p>又待詔，官名。【前漢·公孫弘傳】待詔金馬門。【應劭註】諸以才伎召未有正官，故曰待詔。【唐書·百官志】明皇初置翰林待詔。</p><p>又蠻王曰詔。【唐書·南蠻傳】南詔本哀牢夷後，烏蠻別種也。渠帥有六，自號六詔，曰蒙巂詔、越析詔、浪穹詔、邆睒詔、施浪詔、蒙舍詔。</p><p>又【正韻】市召切，音紹。【禮·禮器】禮有<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22DE4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢷤</span>詔。【註】告道賔主者也。詔或爲紹。</p><p>又【集韻】之遙切，照平聲。言誘也。</p><p>又【韻會】時饒切【正韻】時昭切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音韶。【禮·禮器】詔侑武方。【註】詔侑或爲韶囿，武當爲無，方猶常也。</p><p>又叶朱戍切，音注。【班固·東都賦】申舊章下明詔，命有司頒憲度。</p>
