                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>簿</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>本子，册籍：～册。～记。～籍。～录。</li>
	<li>古代称公文、案卷：对～公堂。</li>
	<li>古代的仪仗侍从：～伍。卤～（古代帝王、后妃、王公大臣外出时的仪仗队。次序排列严格并明文著之于簿籍）。</li>
	<li>笏。</li>
</ol>

<p><span class="dicpy">b&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄛˊ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">簿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>簿</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，溥( <span class="dicpy">pǔ</span>)声。本义:登记事物的册子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[book]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上问上林尉诸禽兽簿。(上:皇上;上林尉:官名。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;张释之传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:练习薄;账簿;簿籍(财物出纳的账册);簿钞(簿牒;簿籍文书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 文书 <span class="encs">[document]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儒生所短，不徒以不晓簿书。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;谢短》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:簿责(以文牍相责);簿牒(文书);簿历(履历记录);簿牍(文书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指记录审问材料或罪人供词的文状 <span class="encs">[written confession;deposition]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:簿圆(供状齐备);簿决(断案);簿讼(审理诉讼案件)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 仪仗 <span class="encs">[guard of honour]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:簿伍(仪仗侍从)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 官名。指主簿一类官职，因负责文书簿籍，故多称簿，历朝皆有，如汉代的主簿，唐代的司簿、典簿、掌簿，亦简称簿 <span class="encs">[secterary]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:簿伐，簿阀(先代官籍门阀);簿尉(主簿和县尉)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>簿</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>造册登记 <span class="encs">[register]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:簿列(谓登记入册);簿土(古代指入册的土地)</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">書寫用的本子，或登記事物的冊子。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「帳<b>簿</b>」、「筆記<b>簿</b>」、「作文<b>簿</b>」、「點名<b>簿</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷一○二&middot;張釋之傳》：「上問上林尉諸禽獸<b>簿</b>，十餘問，尉左右視，盡不能對。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷八&middot;宣帝紀》：「上計<b>簿</b>，具文而已。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">文狀、狀子。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「對簿公堂」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷五四&middot;李廣傳》：「大將軍長史急責廣之莫府上<b>簿</b>。廣曰：『諸校尉亡罪，乃我自失道，吾今自上<b>簿</b>。』」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">笏板，古代上朝所持用的手板。</span><span class="gc_yy">《三國志&middot;卷三八&middot;蜀書&middot;秦宓傳》：「宓以<b>簿</b>擊頰。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">職官名。主簿的簡稱。</span><span class="gc_yy">《明史&middot;卷一五八&middot;吳訥傳》：「父遵，任沅陵<b>簿</b>，坐事繫京師。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>

</ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7C3F.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【正韻】裴古切【集韻】【韻會】伴姥切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音部。籍也。【孟子】先簿正祭器。【史記&middot;張釋之傳】上問上林尉諸禽獸簿。【前漢&middot;食貨志】與郡縣通姦，多張空簿。【註】師古曰：簿，計簿也。</p>
<p>又笏也。【蜀志】秦密見太守，以簿擊頰。【註】簿，手版也。</p>
<p>又車駕法從次第爲鹵簿。【史記&middot;黥布傳】布常爲軍鋒。【註】索隱曰：《漢書》作楚軍前簿。簿者，鹵簿。</p>
<p>又百官皆有鹵簿，各分差等。【梁書&middot;王僧孺傳】道遇中丞鹵簿，驅廹溝中。</p>
<p>又主簿，官名。【漢官儀】主簿，主縣之簿書。</p>
<p>又領也。【荀子&middot;正名篇】五官簿之而不知。</p>
<p>又【集韻】伯各切，音博。迫也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】傍各切【集韻】【韻會】白各切【正韻】弼角切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音薄。蠶具。或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25D2E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥴮</span>。</p>
<p>又同箔，簾也。</p>
<p>又【類篇】弼碧切。壁柱。</p>
