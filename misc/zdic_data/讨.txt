                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>讨</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">討</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>查究，处治：检～。</li>
	<li>征伐，发动攻击：～伐。声～（宣布罪行而加以抨击）。</li>
	<li>研究，推求：研～。探～。</li>
	<li>索取：～还（<span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n</span>）。</li>
	<li>求，请求：～教（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o </span>）。～饶。</li>
	<li>惹：～厌。～人喜欢。</li>
	<li>娶：～老婆。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">讨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>讨</strong></p>
<p>討 <span class="dicpy">tǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从言，从寸。言，言论。寸，法度。用言论和法度进行处治。本义:声讨)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[denounce;condemn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">讨，治也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">讨，诛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其君无日不讨国人而训之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人讨 <span class="diczx2">陈</span>叛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公五年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 讨伐;诛戮 <span class="encs">[send armed forces to suppress;kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郑伯</span>为王左卿士，以王命讨之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公九年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">托臣以讨贼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">讨贼督师。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略&middot;序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:征讨(出兵讨伐);讨掩(讨伐掩捕。掩捕，乘人不备而捕捉);讨贰(征讨叛逆不忠的诸侯);讨贼(征讨叛逆或盗贼);讨乱(征伐乱事);讨荡(征讨扫荡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 探究 <span class="encs">[make a thorough inquiry;probe into]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">世叔</span>讨论之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:讨实(摸清;落实);讨究(探讨研究事物的道理);讨索(研讨探索)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 索取 <span class="encs">[ask for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何辱讨焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:讨针线(讨零花钱;靠人过日子;受人节制);讨彩头(以祝贺主人吉利的名义索取财物);讨春(寻春，探春;旧时称卜卦算命)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 乞求 <span class="encs">[beg for]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:讨分上(讨人情;讲情面);讨火(借火);乞讨(向人要钱要饭等)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 得到利益或利润 <span class="encs">[get]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:讨彩(博得喝采);讨乖(博取乖顺的好感);讨野火(占别人便宜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 诛杀 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">讨，诛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是儿小</span><span class="diczx2">秦武阳</span>二岁，而讨杀二豪。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 娶 <span class="encs">[marry a woman]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:讨亲(娶亲);讨老婆</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 招致 <span class="encs">[incur]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:讨口牙(惹口舌);讨气(惹祸;令人厌烦);讨人怜;讨人厌;讨厌</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A0E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】他皓切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】土皓切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>叨上聲。【說文】治也。从言从寸。【徐曰】寸，法也。奉辭伐罪，故从言。會意。【書&middot;臯陶謨】天討有罪。【疏】討治有罪，使之絕惡。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】其君無日不討國人而訓之。【註】討，治也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】誅也。【類篇】殺也。【正韻】訶也。</p>
<p>又除也。【公羊傳&middot;隱四年】其稱人何，討賊之辭也。【註】討者，除也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】去也。【禮&middot;禮器】君子之于禮也，有順而討也。【疏】順序而稍去之也。</p>
<p>又【類篇】求也。【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2146D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡑭</span>韻】探也。【正韻】尋也。【論語】世叔討論之。【註】討，尋究也。【後漢&middot;王充傳】討摘物情。【三國志&middot;袁紹與公孫瓚書】尋討禍源。</p>
<p>又雜也。【詩&middot;秦風】蒙伐有苑。【傳】蒙，討羽也。【箋】討，雜也。畫雜羽之文于伐。</p>
<p>又官名。【舊唐書&middot;職官志】貞元末，置招討使。【金史&middot;百官志】招討司使招懷降附，征討攜離。</p>
<p>又叶他口切，偸上聲。【焦氏&middot;易林】三絲綰弩，無益于輔。城弱不守，郭君受討。</p>
