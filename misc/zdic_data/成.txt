                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>成</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chéng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chéng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>做好，做完：～功。完～。～就。～事。～交。～立。～婚。～仁（儒家主张的成就仁德）。～人之美。玉～其事。</li><li>事物发展到一定的形态或状况：～形。～性。～人。自学～才。蔚然～风。</li><li>变为：长～。变～。</li><li>可以，能行：～，就这么办。</li><li>称赞人能力强：他办事麻利，真～。</li><li>够，达到一定数量：～年累（<span class="dicpy">lěi </span>）月。</li><li>已定的，定形的：～规。～俗。～见。～例。～竹在胸。</li><li>十分之一：增产三～。</li><li>平定，讲和：“会于稷，以～宋乱”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">成</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>成</strong> <span class="dicpy">chéng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chéng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，从<span class="yhcs">“|”</span>(即<span class="yhcs">“杵”</span>)。<span class="yhcs">“斧”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“杵”</span>具备就可以做成事情。本义:完成，成就)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[accomplish;succeed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">成，就也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">成德之终也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">成者功就不可易也。——<span class="smcs">《太元元错》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">箫韶九成。——<span class="smcs">《书·益稷》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祝告曰利成。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·少牢礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及事成。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此织生自蚕茧，成于机杼。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:落成(建筑物完工)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 变成;成为 <span class="encs">[become;turn into]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">累寸不已，遂成丈匹。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:成习(成为习惯);成精;成仙(成为神仙)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 成全 <span class="encs">[help sb.to achieve his aim]</span>。如:成持(帮助，扶持);玉成其事</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 形成 <span class="encs">[form]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">好鸟相鸣，嘤嘤成韵。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:成市(形成市场);成列(形成队列);成体(构成形体)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 事物生长到一定的状态;长成<span class="encs">[ripen]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五谷萎败不成。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·明理》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">用实者成实时采。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">依新</span>已五岁，转眼成人。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 树立 <span class="encs">[set up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以成伯王之名。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“立也。”</span>。又如:成名成家(树立名声，成为专家);成果(造就，创建)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 订立 <span class="encs">[conclude]</span>。如:成言(订约);成券(订立契约);成约，成说(订约)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 平定;讲和 <span class="encs">[pacification;suppression]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">会于</span><span class="diczx2">稷</span>，以成 <span class="diczx2">宋</span>乱。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十一年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂使之行成于</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>。——<span
