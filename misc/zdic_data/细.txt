                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>细</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">細</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>颗粒小的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">粗</span>&rdquo;相对：～沙。～面。～屑。</li>
	<li>长条东西直径小的：～线。～丝。～眉。～水长流。</li>
	<li>精致的：～瓷。～布。～工。～活儿。</li>
	<li>声音小：嗓音～。</li>
	<li>周密详尽：仔～。精～。～致。～密。～目（详细的项目或目录）。～腻。胆大心～。</li>
	<li>微小的：～小。～微。～节。事无巨～。</li>
	<li>俭省：他过日子很～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">细</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>细</strong></p>
<p>細 <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从系( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，囟( <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;n</span>)声。系，细丝。本义:细小)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。和&ldquo;大&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[little;small]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">细，微也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，细者，细之微也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">细，小也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">细人之爱人也以姑息。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">细钧有钟无镈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此吾所谓君子明细而不明大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;天志中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不积细流。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">游鱼细石，直视无碍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斜风细雨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张志和</span><span class="smcs">《渔歌子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不知其细。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:细崽(细作;西崽);细人(细作;姬妾);细探人(多指军队中的探子);细士(小人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 纤细。和&ldquo;粗&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[fine;slender]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者</span><span class="diczx2">楚灵王</span>好士细要。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;兼爱中》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">细笔钩勒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">细若蚊足。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:细腰(细要。纤细的腰身);细娘家(指容貌美好的少女);细袅袅(细而柔软的样子);细毛(纤细的毛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 精致，细密 <span class="encs">[meticulous;delicate;exquisite;fine;close]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还是那年我和你拌嘴的那日起，叫上来做细活的。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
