                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>徙</strong> <span class="dicpy">xǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，止声。本义:迁移)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 (平行或由上而下的移动)<span class="encs">[move from one place to another]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徙，迻也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徙，移也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若徙于他。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·比长》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓出居异乡也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">死徙无出乡。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓爰土易居平肥硗也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百里徙。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·成相》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“迁也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徙</span><span class="diczx2">武</span>北海。—— <span class="diczx2">东汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">号呼而转徙。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非死则徙。雾徙于后。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:徙倚(徘徊;留连);徙贯(迁地居住，更换籍贯);迁徙(迁移);徙居(徙次，即迁居，徙家，徙道。迁居)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 改变，变化 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">具生器以适墓，象徙道也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·礼论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“迁改也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时已徙矣，而法不徙，以此为治，岂不难哉?——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:徙意(改变意念);徙义(见义即改变意念而从之)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 贬谪 <span class="encs">[exile]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">孔明</span>废之为庶人，徙之 <span class="diczx2">汶山</span>。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:徙放(流放);徙逐(放逐);徙废(贬谪废弃);徙锢(贬谪禁锢);徙边(将犯人流放边境服劳役)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 搬走，搬开 <span class="encs">[remove]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徙其大舟。——<span
