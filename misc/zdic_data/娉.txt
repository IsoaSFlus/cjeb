                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>娉</strong> <span class="dicpy">pìn</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，甹( <span class="dicpy">pīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pīng.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:问名。古代婚礼<span class="yhcs">“六礼”</span>之一。即男方请媒人问女方名字和出生年月日)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[betroth;be engaged (to be married)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">娉，问也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">婚姻娉内送逆无礼。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“问名也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:娉命(婚约。即订婚);娉财(行聘礼时所赠财物);娉币(行聘礼时所致币帛财物);娉内(娉纳。古代婚礼中的问名、纳币)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 娶 <span class="encs">[marry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">婚姻娉内。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">再奉朝娉。——<span class="smcs">《樊敏碑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:娉会(聘妻)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">pīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>娉</strong> <span class="dicpy">pīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 美 <span class="encs">[beautiful]</span>。如:娉婷袅娜(形容女子姿态柔美);娉娉(轻盈美好的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">pìn</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/娉婷">娉婷</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
