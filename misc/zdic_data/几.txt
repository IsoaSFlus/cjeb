                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">几</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>几</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">➋➌幾）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>小或矮的桌子：茶～儿。</li>	<li>将近，差一点：～乎。～至。</li>	<li>苗头：知～其神乎。</li></ol><p>● <strong>几</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">幾）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>询问数量多少（估计不太大）的疑问词：～个人？～何（①多少，如&ldquo;人生～～？&rdquo;②研究点线面体的性质、关系和计算方法的学科，如&ldquo;平面～～&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>表示不定的数目：～本书。～百人。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> small table</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> beinahe, fast, annähernd ,kleiner Tisch, Tischchen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> petite table</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">几</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>几</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。本义:古人席地而坐时有靠背的坐具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a long narrow table; bench]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几，坐所以凭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌五几五席之名物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司几筵》</span>。注:&ldquo;五几:左右玉雕彤漆素。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">室中度以几，堂上度以筵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;匠人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几杖，所以扶助衰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;致仕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必操几杖以从之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有司以几筵舍奠于墓左。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span>。疏:&ldquo;依神也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或肆之筵，或授之几。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;行苇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:几杖(供老人依靠的小桌子和支撑用的手杖。古代赐几杖，表示敬老的礼节);几席(几和席，为古人凭依、坐卧的器具);几榻(靠几与卧榻，常用以泛指日用器具);几簟(几席)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 后专指有光滑平面、由腿或其它支撑物固定起来的小桌子 <span class="encs">[a small table]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾妻来归。时至轩中，从余问古事，或凭几学书。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
