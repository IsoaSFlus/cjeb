						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>帛</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bó.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄛˊ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 丝织品的总称：布～。～书。～画。化干戈为玉～（喻变争斗为友善）。竹～（指书籍）。简蠹～裂（书坏了）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> silks, fabrics; wealth, property</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> soierie,tissu de soie</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>帛</strong> <span class="dicpy">bó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从巾，白声。甲骨文字形，上面是<span class="yhcs">“白”</span>字，下面是<span class="yhcs">“巾”</span>字。本义:丝织品的总称)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[silks]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王所赐金帛，归尽藏之。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大帛之冠。——<span class="smcs">《左传·闵公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执玉帛者万国。 ——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孤执皮帛。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">礼云礼云，玉帛云乎哉?——<span class="smcs">《论语·阳货》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃丹书帛曰:<span class="yhcs">“</span><span class="diczx2">陈胜</span>王。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瓦缝参差，如周身之帛缕。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牺牲玉帛，弗敢加也，必以信。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:布帛(棉织品和丝织品的总称);玉帛;财帛;帛召(写在帛上的诏书);帛画(画在丝织物上的图画)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指帛书 <span class="encs">[book copied on silk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">简蠹帛裂，三写易字。——<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/帛画">帛画</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/帛缕">帛缕</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/帛书">帛书</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5E1B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】旁陌切【集韻】【韻會】薄陌切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音白。【說文】繒也。【廣韻】幣帛。【易·賁卦】賁于丘園，束帛戔戔。【書·堯典】五玉三帛。【傳】三帛，諸侯世子執纁，公之孤執元，附庸之君執黃。【周禮·春官·典命註】帛，如今璧色繒也。【左傳·閔二年】衞文公大帛之冠。【註】大帛，厚繒。【後漢·鄧后紀】必書功於竹帛。【註】帛謂縑素。</p><p>又執帛，官名【前漢·曹參傳】乃封參爲執帛。【註】張晏曰：執帛，孤卿也。或曰楚官名。</p><p>又草名。【爾雅·釋草】帛似帛，華山有之。【疏】華山有草，葉似帛者，因以名云。</p><p>又姓。【神仙傳】吳有帛和。【水經注】瀍水西有帛仲理墓。</p><p>又叶僕各切，音薄。【禮·禮運】以烹以炙，以爲醴酪。治其絲麻，以爲布帛。【又】疏布以羃，衣其澣帛。醴醆以獻，薦其燔炙。</p><p>
