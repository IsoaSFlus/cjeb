                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卫</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>卫</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">衛</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>保护，防护：保～。捍～。～道（卫护占统治地位的思想体系，如&ldquo;～～士&rdquo;）。～戍（警备，多用于首都）。～冕。</li>
	<li>防护人员：警～。后～。</li>
	<li>古代称驴：&ldquo;策双～来迎&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>中国明代驻兵的地点（后只用于地名）：威海～（在山东省）。</li>
	<li>中国周代诸侯国名。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> guard, protect, defend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> bewachen, schützen, verteidigen  (V)​,sich wehren  (V)​,sichern, beschützen  (V)​,Wei  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> défendre,garder,protéger,garde du corps</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卫</strong></p>
<p>衛、衞 <span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从韋币从行。甲骨文中&ldquo;卫、韦&rdquo;同字。意思是在大道(行)站岗保卫(韦)。本义:保卫;防护)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[defend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卫，宿卫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卫护也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以卫王宫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">备一夕之卫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然侍卫之臣不懈于内。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卫籍(军籍);守卫(防守保卫);自卫(保卫自己);捍卫(保卫);卫守(保卫防守);卫助(保卫协助);卫足(比喻自全或自卫);卫侍(保卫侍奉);卫社(保卫国家);保家卫国;卫卒(护卫的兵卒);卫养(保护安抚);卫从(护卫侍从);卫蔽(卫护遮掩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 防护使之不受危险 <span class="encs">[guard]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:卫奉(守护侍奉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 谋求 <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有货，以卫身也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语下》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>卫</strong></p>
<p>衛 <span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 卫士，保镖 <span class="encs">[bodyguard]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:卫帮(清代民间秘密结社之一)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 担任卫护、防守之职的人 <span class="encs">[guard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">文公</span>之入也无卫，故有 <span class="diczx2">吕</span>、 <span class="diczx2">却</span>之难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 周代京师以外的行政区域之一 <span class="encs">[an ancient district]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">过了半年，</span><span class="diczx2">松潘卫</span>边外生番与内地民人互市，因买卖不公，彼此吵闹起来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 驴的别称 <span class="encs">[donkey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南中丞卓，</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>游学十余年，衣布缕，乘牝卫，薄游 <span class="diczx2">上蔡</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《云溪友议》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 箭羽 <span class="encs">[arrow&#39;s feather]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span><span class="diczx2">熊渠子</span>出，见寝石，以为伏虎，将弓射之，矢没其卫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;儒增》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 卫生的简称 <span class="encs">[sanitation]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:环卫部门</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Wei state]</span>。周武王弟康叔封地。至懿公为狄所灭。戴公野处漕邑，文公又徙居楚丘。秦始皇既统一全国，独置卫君，为附庸，至二世元年废</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">之</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>过 <span class="diczx2">卫</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎行论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>、 <span class="diczx2">卫</span>、 <span class="diczx2">中山</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 边陲，边远的地方 <span class="encs">[border area]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侯卫之班，藩服之职。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Wei River]</span>。源出河北省灵寿县东北，南流至县东南，合滹沱河</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">恒</span>、 <span class="diczx2">卫</span>既从，大陆既作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 肢体 <span class="encs">[limbs]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四卫变强。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 姓。如:卫青(汉武帝时的大将军，在讨匈战争中，屡立战功);卫夫人(晋卫恒从女，李矩妻，也称&ldquo;李夫人&rdquo;。善书法。王羲之、王献之的书法为她所传授)</p>
