                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">稚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>稚</strong></p><p>穉、稺 <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾，隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)声。本义:晚植的谷类。引申为幼禾)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[young grain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稺，幼禾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作穉，作稚。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稚，幼稼也。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">考灵曜百谷稚熟。——<span class="smcs">《尚书》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“晚熟曰稚。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:稚稼(迟期种植的稻谷)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 孩子，儿童 <span class="encs">[child]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使老稚转乎沟壑。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:稚年(少年;童年;幼年);稚幼(幼儿，幼童);稚乳(婴幼儿;孩童);稚儿(幼儿;幼子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 笋的别名 <span class="encs">[bamboo shoots]</span>。如:稚笋(嫩笋);稚龙(笋的别名)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>稚</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 幼小;年幼(含有晚出生的意思，与<span class="yhcs">“长”</span>相对) <span class="encs">[young;childish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">言皆告稚子王。——<span class="smcs">《书·立政》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">樨朱颜只。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·大招》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“幼也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">怀王</span>稚子。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稚子侯门。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:稚态(幼儿的神情;天真的情态);稚女(幼女;少女);稚小(幼小;年少);稚弟(幼弟;年小的弟弟);稚幼(幼小;幼稚无知)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 物体细小 <span class="encs">[very small]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盖稚水流耳。——<span class="smcs">《水经注》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纯雄其名樨蜂。——<span class="smcs">《列子·无瑞》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“小也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:稚钱(梁武帝时官铸的小钱);稚榻(小床);稚蜂(蜂名;小蜂);稚犬(小狗)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古称妇女年不到五十 <span class="encs">[young woman]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">卫</span>之稚质。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·脩务》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“亦少女也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:稚妻(年少之妻);稚质(年少貌美的女子);稚秀(年轻秀丽)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 晚;暮 <span class="encs">[late]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曙戒勿怠，后稚逢殃。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span
