                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>噫</strong> <span class="dicpy">ài</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 饱食或积食后，胃里的气体从嘴里出来并发出声音 <span class="encs">[belch]</span>。如:噫气(气壅塞而得通;吐气)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 呼气;吹气 <span class="encs">[breathe out;exhale]</span>。如:噫欠(噫气和打哈欠。泛指吐气)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yī;yí</span></p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>噫</strong> <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><叹></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，意声。( <span class="dicpy">ài</span>)本义:出气) 感叹声 <span class="encs">[alas]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示悲痛或叹息</p><p><span class="diczx1">噫!菊之爱，</span><span class="diczx2">陶</span>后鲜有闻。(噫，感叹词，相当于现代汉语中的<span class="yhcs">“唉”</span>。)—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:噫乎(表示慨叹);噫嗟(表示慨叹);噫呜(感慨悲叹貌);噫嘘(叹息貌)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 表示呼告。如:噫歆(祭祀时，发声告神来享用祭品);噫喑叱咤(大声喝叫);噫噫(答应声);噫兴(犹噫歆);噫噫叱咤(大声喝叫)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表示惊异</p><p><span class="diczx2">季恬逸</span>道<span class="yhcs">“噫!那里来的这位太太?”</span>——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:噫——怪!这是谁做的饭?;噫嘻吁(表示惊异或慨叹)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ài;yí</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/噫嘻">噫嘻</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>噫</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span></p><p><连></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“抑”</span>。表示转折 <span class="encs">[or]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">噫，语辞。通作抑。——<span
