                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>畸</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>不规则的，不正常的：～形。～胎。～人。～变。</li>
	<li>残余，零星：～数。～零。～羡（指剩余）。</li>
	<li>偏：～轻～重。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/奇">奇</a>&rdquo;，数目不成双的。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> odd, fractional, remainder, odds</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> einzeln, befremdend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> anormal,étrange</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>畸</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从田，奇声。本义:零片的田地)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[irregular land]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畸，残田也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;残田者，余田不整齐者也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道千乘之国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;学而》</span>。集解:&ldquo;三百一十六里有畸。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畸余(未被侵占的残余土地);畸零(小块零散土地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 数的零头;余数 <span class="encs">[odd]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:畸零;畸羡(剩余。多指剩余之物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古称军队阵形的一翼 <span class="encs">[wing]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:左畸(军队左部)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>畸</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不整齐的，不正规的 <span class="encs">[irregular]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">墨子</span>有见于齐，无见于畸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span>。注:&ldquo;畸，谓不齐也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畸形;畸态(畸形病态)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 偏离;偏颇 <span class="encs">[lopsided]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:畸重畸轻(偏重偏轻，不合常规)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 脱俗的，超群的 <span class="encs">[extraordinary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敢问畸人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;大宗师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畸人(与世俗格格不入的人);畸士(犹畸人。独行拔俗之人);畸行(超俗的、非凡的行为);畸民(畸客。高行拔俗之人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 单数的，奇数的。跟&ldquo;双&rdquo;、&ldquo;偶&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[odd]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畸人者，畸于人而侔于天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;大宗师》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:&ldquo;畸，不耦也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畸则不可为。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畸日(单日。即逢一、三、五、七、九等的日子。跟&ldquo;双日&rdquo;相对)</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畸</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>畸</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jī <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3010.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">不整齊的田畝。</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;張自烈《正字通&middot;田部》：「井田為正，零田不可井者為<b>畸</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">零餘的數目。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「畸零」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《資治通鑑&middot;卷二○八&middot;唐紀二十四&middot;中宗神龍元年》：「是歲，戶部奏天下戶六百一十五萬，口三千七百一十四萬有<b>畸</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">偏、邪。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;天論》：「故道之所善，中則可從，<b>畸</b>則不可為，匿則大惑。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">不正常的、不整齊的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「畸形」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;天論》：「墨子有見於齊，無見於<b>畸</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;楊倞&middot;注：「<b>畸</b>，謂不齊也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上" target="_blank">午集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上&amp;bs=田" target="_blank">田</a>】
                              畸·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7578.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>居宜切，音羈。【說文】殘田也。从田，奇聲。【正字通】井田爲正，零田不可井者爲畸，地勢多邪曲，井田取正方，則田必有畸零。畫井者，必計零以足其數。</p>
<p>又凡數之零餘者皆曰畸。【莊子&middot;大宗師】畸人者，畸於人而侔於天。</p>
<p>又【吳語】董褐將還，王稱左畸。【註】左畸，軍左部稱呼也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】渠羈切，音奇。異也。</p>
