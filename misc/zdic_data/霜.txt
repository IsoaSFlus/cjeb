                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">霜</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>霜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shuāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄨㄤˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>附着在地面或植物上面的微细冰粒，是接近地面的水蒸气冷至摄氏零度以下凝结而成的：～降。～冻。～序（指深秋季节）。～秋。～期。～天。</li>
	<li>像霜一样的东西：柿～。西瓜～。</li>
	<li>形容白色：～鬓。～刃。～锋。</li>
	<li>喻高洁：～操（高洁的节操）。～骨。～情。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> frost; crystallized; candied</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Reif  (S)​,eisig  (Adj)​,Ausschlag  (S)​,Frost  (S)​,Fruchtzucker  (S)​,Niederschlag  (S)​,Raureif  (S)​,ernst  (Adj)​,kühl  (Adj)​,kristallisch  (Adj)​,eingezuckert</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> givre,gelée blanche,cristallisé,candi</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">霜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>霜</strong> <span class="dicpy">shuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从雨，相声。本义:在气温降到摄氏零度以下时，近地面空气中水汽的白色结晶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[frost]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">霜，露所凝也。士气津液从地而生，薄以寒气则结为霜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">霜者，天之所以杀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;董仲舒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">履霜坚冰至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白露为霜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;蒹葭》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羌管悠悠霜满地，人不寐，将军白发征夫泪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《渔家傲》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">每寒夜起立，振衣裳，甲上冰霜迸落，铿然有声。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:霜雪(比喻高洁清白);霜瓦(覆盖着白霜的瓦片);霜旦(下霜的早晨);霜甲(沾着霜的战甲);霜衣(寒衣);霜妃(指霜);霜序(霜降的时序);霜信(寒霜将来的信息。也为雁的别名);霜砧(寒秋时节的捣衣声);霜草(因霜害而枯萎的草;草名，也叫&ldquo;相思草&rdquo;);霜气(寒霜);霜鸟(指在秋冬或日暮时的乌鸦);霜雪(霜和雪;比喻心志清纯洁净);霜雾(寒气);霜黄(指树叶遭受伤害而枯黄的颜色)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 年岁，年岁的代称。犹言秋 <span class="encs">[year]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客舍</span><span class="diczx2">并州</span>己十霜，归心日夜忆 <span class="diczx2">咸阳</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">贾岛</span><span class="smcs">《渡桑乾》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 如霜的粉末 <span class="encs">[frostlike powder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冰盘若琥珀，何以糖霜美?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《送金山乡僧归蜀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:柿霜。亦指润肤的化妆品。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:雅霜;白玉霜</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>霜</strong> <span class="dicpy">shuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 譬喻白色 <span class="encs">[white]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徒霜镜中发，羞彼鹤上人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《古风》</span>之四</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:霜毫(白毫毛笔);霜鬓(耳边白发);霜毛(形容洁白的羽毛;比喻白发);霜衣(形容白鸟的羽毛);霜色(白色);霜侵(指白发渐渐增多);霜翎(白色的羽毛);霜絮(白棉絮);霜蓬(喻白发);霜须(形容须白如霜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 比喻高洁 <span class="encs">[noble and unsullied]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心懔懔以怀霜，志眇眇而临云。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《文赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:霜操(指高洁的操持);霜女(指梅花。比喻其高洁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 譬喻冷酷、严峻 <span class="encs">[severe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">王思远</span>恒如怀冰，暑月亦有霜气。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南史&middot;陆慧晓传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:霜法(严厉的法禁);霜典(严厉的法典);霜威(指寒霜肃杀的威力;比喻严厉的威势);霜烈(比喻严厉);霜台(御史台的别称。御史掌职弹劾，为风霜之任，故称。也称&ldquo;霜署&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 譬喻锋利 <span class="encs">[sharp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钢镞润，霜刃染。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:霜刀(指锋利雪亮的刀);霜刃(锋利雪亮的刀刃);霜爪(指锐利的爪子);霜矛(指锋利雪亮的矛);霜戈(指锋利雪亮的戈)</span></p>
