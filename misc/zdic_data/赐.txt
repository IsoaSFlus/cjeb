                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赐</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赐</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">賜</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘˋ</span></p><ol><li>给，旧时指上级给下级或长辈给小辈：～予。～死。赏～。恩～。</li><li>敬辞：请～教。希～函。</li><li>赏给的东西，给予的好处：厚～。受～良多。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> give, bestow favors; appoint</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> erlauben; gestatten; gewähren  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> accorder,conférer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赐</strong></p><p>賜 <span class="dicpy">cì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，易声。从<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>，表示与钱财有关。本义:赏赐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 给予;上给予下 <span class="encs">[bestow;grant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赐，予也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上予下曰赐。——<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长者赐，少者贱者不敢辞。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">策勋十二转，赏赐百千强。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大王及宗室所赐币帛，尽以与军吏、士大夫。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传·赵将括母》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若从君惠而免之，三年将拜君赐。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:赐光(赏脸，赏光);赐环(将放逐之臣赦罪召还);赐第(皇上敕建的府第);赐田(君主所赐予的田地);赐官(赐予设置家臣以治家邑的权力)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 请给 <span class="encs">[please]</span>。如:赐函;赐示</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赐给">赐给</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赐光">赐光</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赐教">赐教</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赐死">赐死</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赐予">赐予</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">賜</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“赐”字头，请参考“賜”字。)</span>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=貝" target="_blank">貝</a>】
                              賜·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8CDC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>斯義切，思去聲。【說文】予也。【篇海】錫也。【禮·曲禮】三賜不及車馬。【註】三賜，三命也。【疏】受命卽受賜。</p><p>又【玉藻】凡賜，君子與小人不同日。</p><p>又【公羊傳·僖二年】虞郭之相救，非相爲賜。【註】賜，猶惠也。</p><p>又【玉篇】賜，施也，空盡也。</p><p>又姓。【玉海】齊大夫<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>子賜之後。　【正字通】俗作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27DBD.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧶽</span>，非。</p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">予也。</span>釋詁。賚貢錫畀予況賜也。七字轉注。凡經傳云錫者、賜之假借也。公羊傳曰。錫者何。賜也。賜者、與之通稱。禹貢。納錫大龜。乃下與上之詞。又玉藻言賜君子、與小人者、別言之。統言則不別也。方言曰。賜、盡也。此借賜爲澌。澌、盡也。盡之字俗作儩。<span class="jieshi">从貝。易聲。</span>斯義切。十六部。                          </div>
