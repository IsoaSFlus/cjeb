                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>鲜</strong></p><p>鮮、鱻 <span class="dicpy">xiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从鱼，从羊。<span class="yhcs">“鱼”</span>表类属，<span class="yhcs">“羊”</span>表味美。本义:鱼名。出貉国)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假借为鱻。鲜鱼，活鱼 <span class="encs">[live fish;aquatic food]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冬宜鲜羽。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“生鱼也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">治大国若烹小鲜。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">河上公</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“鲜，鱼。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">调鼎他年事，妙手看烹鲜。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">张元干</span><span class="smcs">《水调歌头》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冬宜鲜羽。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:鲜眼(像鱼那样的圆眼);鱼鲜;海鲜;鲜脍(切细的鲜鱼肉);鲜鳞(活鱼);鲜羽(活鱼和活鸟)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鲜美的食物 <span class="encs">[fresh food;delicious food]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟君用鲜。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荐鲜知路近，省敛觉年丰。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐玄宗</span><span class="smcs">《幸凤泉汤》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:时鲜;尝鲜;鲜肥(鱼肉类美味肴馔);鲜食(鲜活的食品);鲜杀(活杀的猎物)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>鲜</strong></p><p>鮮 <span class="dicpy">xiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 新鲜 <span class="encs">[fresh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">炰鳖鲜鱼。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·韩奕》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芳草鲜美。——</span><span
