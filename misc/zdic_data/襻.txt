						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>襻</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>扣住纽扣的套：扣～。纽～。</li>
	<li>功用或形状像襻的东西：鞋～儿。</li>
	<li>扣住，使分开的东西连在一起。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>襻</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 系衣裙的带子 <span class="encs">[band]</span>。如:襻带(系衣裙的带);襻膊(宋代的一种挂在颈项间，用来搂起衣袖方便操作的工具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用布做的扣住纽扣的套 <span class="encs">[loop]</span>。如:纽襻儿</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 外形、用途或功能类似于襻的东西 <span class="encs">[loop]</span>。如:篮子襻儿;车襻儿;鞋襻儿</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 底细 <span class="encs">[ins and outs]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">提起来大概都知道他个根儿襻儿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="encs">[用绳子]</span> 绕住 <span class="encs">[fasten]</span>。如:用绳子襻上，使连在一起;襻上几针</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
