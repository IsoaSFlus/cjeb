                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>红</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">紅</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hóng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>像鲜血的颜色：～色。～叶。～灯。～尘。～包。～烧。～润。～艳艳。～口白牙。～绳系足（旧指男女前生注定的姻缘）。</li><li>象征顺利或受人宠信：～人。～运。～角（<span class="dicpy">jué</span>）儿（受观众欢迎的演员）。走～。</li><li>喜庆：～媒（媒人）。～蛋。～白喜事（结婚和喜丧合称）。</li><li>象征革命：～军。～色根据地，～色政权。</li><li>指营业的纯利润：～利。分～。</li><li>特指对中国古典文学名著《红楼梦》的研究：～学。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>红</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">紅</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄥˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">工</span>”，指妇女的生产作业，纺织、缝纫、刺绣等。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">红</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>红</strong></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 紅 <span class="dicpy">gōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“工”</span>。女工，从事手工劳动的女性 <span class="encs">[female worker;woman worker]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">农夫释耒，红女下机。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·郦食其传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾已食禄，又夺园夫女红利虖。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·董仲舒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“功”</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 功服。丧服名 <span class="encs">[livery of grief;mouring apparel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">服大红十五日，小红十四日。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·景帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 劳绩，功绩 <span class="encs">[merits and achivements]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雕文刻镂，伤农事者也;锦绣纂组，害女红者也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·景帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>红</strong></p><p>紅 <span class="dicpy">hóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，表示与线丝有关，工声。本义:粉红色)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pink]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">红，帛赤白色也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“按，此今人所谓粉红、桃红也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缙红繎。——<span
