                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>副</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>第二位的，辅助的，区别于“<span class="diczx3">正</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">主</span>”：～职。～手。～官。～使。</li><li>附带的，次要的：～业。～品。～食。～刊。～歌。～产品。～作用。</li><li>相配，相称（<span class="dicpy">chèn </span>）：名实相～。其实难～。</li><li>量词（a.一组或一套，如“一～手套”，“全～武装”；b.指态度，如“一～笑脸”）。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>副</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 剖开，裂开：“不坼不～”。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">副</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>副</strong> <span class="dicpy">fù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，畐( <span class="dicpy">fú</span>)声。( <span class="dicpy">pì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pì.mp3"></a></span> )本义:用刀剖开。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“判也。”</span><span class="smcs">《诗·大雅》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“不坼不副。”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 相称，符合 <span class="encs">[correspond;tally with;conform to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盛名之下，其实难副。——</span><span class="diczx2">李固</span><span class="smcs">《遗黄琼书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:名副其实;副顺(顺应，随顺)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 交付，付与 <span class="encs">[hand over;deliver]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">只恐重重世缘在，事须三度副苍生。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《和仆射牛相公寓言》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>副</strong> <span class="dicpy">fù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 非正的(区别于<span class="yhcs">“正”</span>或<span class="yhcs">“主”</span>) <span class="encs">[deputy;assistant;vice-]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">副将军</span><span class="diczx2">史德威</span>慨然任之。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span                           <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/526F_494.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(副)</span><span class="ywyy">判也。</span><span class="swdz">毛詩大雅曰。不坼不副。曲禮曰。爲天子削瓜者副之。匡繆正俗曰。副貳之字本爲福。從衣畐聲。俗呼一襲爲一褔衣是也。書史假借。遂以副字代之。副本音普力反。義訓剖劈。學者不知有褔字。以副貳爲正體。詩不坼不副。乃以朱點發副字。按顏說未盡然也。副之則一物成二。因仍謂之副。因之凡分而合者皆謂之副。訓詁中如此者致多。流俗語音如付。由一部入三部。故韵書在宥韵。俗語又轉入遇韵也。沿襲旣久。其義其音遂皆忘其本始。褔字雖見於龜策傳、東京賦。然恐此字因副而製耳。鄭仲師注周禮云。貳、副也。貝部貳下因之。史記曰。藏之名山。副在京師。漢書曰。臧諸宗廟。副在有司。周人言貳。漢人言副。古今語也。豈容廢副用褔。</span><span class="ywyy">从刀。畐聲。</span><span class="swdz">芳逼切。一部。</span><span class="ywyy">周禮曰。副辜祭。</span><span class="swdz">鄭注周禮作疈。云疈、疈牲胷也。疈而磔之。謂磔禳及蜡祭。許所據作副。葢副者古文、小篆所同也。鄭所據用籒文。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/526F_495.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(疈)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文副。从㽬。</span><span class="swdz">當云重畐。重畐者、狀分析之聲。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
