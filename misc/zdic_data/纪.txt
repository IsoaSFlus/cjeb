                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纪</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>纪</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">紀</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>记载：～年。～元。～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng</span>）。～实。～念。～传（<span class="dicpy">zhu&agrave;n </span>）体（中国传统史书的一种体裁，以人物传记为中心叙述史实，&ldquo;纪&rdquo;是帝王本纪，&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">传</span>&rdquo;是其他人物的列传）。～事本末体（中国传统史书的一种体裁，以重要事件为纲，将一段历史完整地记载下来）。</li>
	<li>记年代的方式：一～（古代指十二年）。世～（一百年）。</li>
	<li>地质年代分期的第二级，纪以下为&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">世</span>&rdquo;，纪以上为&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">代</span>&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>法度：～律。违法乱～。</li>
	<li>散丝的头绪：丝缕有～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>纪</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">紀</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纪</strong></p>
<p>紀 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，表示与线丝有关，己声。本义:散丝的头绪)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[threads of silk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纪，别丝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三纲六纪。纪者，理也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众之纪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span>。注:&ldquo;丝缕之数有纪。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">譬若丝缕之有纪，网罟之有纲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚同上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茧之性为丝，然非得工女煮以热汤而抽其统纪，则不能成丝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泰族训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 开端，头绪 <span class="encs">[threads]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纪，绪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言十》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乱其纪(事情乱了端绪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 要领 <span class="encs">[essentials]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">义也者，万事之纪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故乐者，天地之命，中和之纪，人情之所不能免也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 纲领;纲纪 <span class="encs">[guiding principle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纲纪四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;棫朴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道者，万物之始，是非之纪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;主道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纪经(纲常);纪纲(网罟的纲绳。引申为法纪与政纲;治理;管理);纪序(纲纪次序)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 法则;准则 <span class="encs">[law]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无变天之道，无绝地之理，无乱人之纪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;孟春》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纪法(法律;法规)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 纪律 <span class="encs">[discipline]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:法纪(法度和纪律);军纪;纲纪;党纪</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 终极 <span class="encs">[end;limit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:纪极(极限);纪限(极限)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 仆人 <span class="encs">[servant]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:纪纲(仆人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 历史上的或人类发展，尤指文化发展方面的一个时代或时期 <span class="encs">[age]</span>。</p>

<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 地质上的分期(长于&ldquo;世&rdquo;，包括在一个&ldquo;代&rdquo;中) <span class="encs">[period]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:侏罗纪;二叠纪;石炭纪;寒武纪;震旦纪</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 十二年的一个时期 <span class="encs">[a period of twelve years]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如何四纪为天子，不及</span><span class="diczx2">卢</span>家有 <span class="diczx2">莫愁</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《马嵬》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 杂记、印象或事件的非正式纪录 <span class="encs">[note]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:陕北纪行&mdash;&mdash;义同&ldquo;记&rdquo;，主要用于&ldquo;纪念、纪年、纪要、纪元、纪传&rdquo;等，别的地方多用&ldquo;记&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 旧时史书的一种体裁本纪，专记帝王的历史事迹及一代大事 <span class="encs">[chronological record of events]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖本纪》</span>、<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 中国古国名 <span class="encs">[Ji state]</span>。姜姓，春秋时为齐所灭，故城在今山东省寿光县东南</p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 姓。如:纪信(公元前?&mdash;前204)，汉初将军，赵城人。秦末为刘邦部将，项羽围刘邦于荥阳时，事急纪信伪乘刘邦车出降，刘邦乘隙脱逃，纪信即被项羽杀害;纪昌(古代传说中善于射箭的人)</p>
