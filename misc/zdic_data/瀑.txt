                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>瀑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄨˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 〔～布〕水从高山陡直地流下来，远看好像挂着的白布。简称&ldquo;瀑&rdquo;，如&ldquo;飞～流泉&rdquo;。</p>
<ol>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>瀑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄠˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>〔～河〕水名，在中国河北省。亦作&ldquo;鲍河&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>暴雨。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瀑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瀑</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，暴声。本义:疾雨)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 急雨，暴风雨 <span class="encs">[rainstorm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瀑，疾雨也。<span class="smcs">《诗》</span>曰:&ldquo;终风且瀑&rdquo;。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瀑，瀑雨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 水名。明、清时名宽门、豹河，今名瀑河 <span class="encs">[Bao River]</span>。在河北省东北部，发源于平泉县北，南流经宽城入滦河</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>瀑</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 水飞溅 <span class="encs">[splash]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:瀑沫(飞贱的水沫);瀑泉(喷涌的泉水);瀑流(方言。喷涌的泉水)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一种烹调法。将肉食切好，用开水略煮或用热油快煎，以便食用。今作&ldquo;爆&rdquo; <span class="encs">[quickfry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">煮瀑得熟，也得充饥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">p&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瀑</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，暴声，本义:跌水，瀑布) 瀑布 <span class="encs">[waterfall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瀑，飞泉悬水也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《康熙字典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瀑，瀑布水流下也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
