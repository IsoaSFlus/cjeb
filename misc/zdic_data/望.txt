                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>望</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wàng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>看，往远处看：～见。眺～。张～。～尘莫及（喻远远落后）。～风捕影。</li><li>拜访：看～。拜～。探～。</li><li>希图，盼：期～。欲～。喜出～外。</li><li>人所敬仰的，有名的：～族。名～。声～。威～。</li><li>向，朝着：～东走。</li><li>月圆，农历每月十五日前后：～日。</li><li>埋怨，责备：怨～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to look at, look forward; to hope, expect</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anstarren, besuchen, Blick in Richtung, erwarten, hoffen,  in Richtung zu</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">望</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>望</strong></p><p>朢 <span class="dicpy">wàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面是<span class="yhcs">“臣”</span>象眼睛，下面是<span class="yhcs">“壬”</span>( <span class="dicpy">tǐng</span>)。象一个人站在土地上远望。小篆又加<span class="yhcs">“月”</span>字，表望的对象。本义:远望)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gaze into the distance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">望，出亡在外望其还也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，此字疑当训远视也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">望，远视也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豕望视而交睫腥。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“望视，远视也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邻邑相望，鸡狗之音相闻。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·胠箧》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">望之而不能见也，逐之而不能及也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天运》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾尝跂而望矣，不如登高之博见也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">扁鹊</span>望 <span class="diczx2">桓侯</span>。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举头望明月。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《静夜思》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风烟望五津。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《杜少府之任蜀州》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">潭西南而望。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:望台(指轮船驾驶台);望楼(望远守卫的楼房);望阙(望着皇宫);望乞(希望;乞求);望国(遥望故国);望睹(了望，眺望);望乡(遥望故乡);望祭(望礼。遥望而祭);望拜(远远望见即行叩拜);望祀(遥望祭祀);望眼(远眺的眼睛)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 遥祭，指古代帝王祭祀山川、日月、星辰 <span class="encs">[sacrifice in distance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旅上帝及四望。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“五岳四镇四渎。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹三望。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十一年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“分野之星，国中山川皆因郊祀，望而祭之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:望祀(古代遥祭山川地袛之礼);望拜(遥望拜祭);望表(古代祭祀山川时所立的木制标志)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 希望，期望 <span class="encs">[expect;hope]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日夜望将军至，岂敢反乎?——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王如知此，则无望民之多于邻国也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>所望。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></p><p><span
