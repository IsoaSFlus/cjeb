                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粪</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>粪</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">糞</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>屎：～便。～坑。～土。</li>
	<li>施肥：～地。～田。</li>
	<li>扫除：～除。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> manure, dung, night soil</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> matière fécale,excrément,selles</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>粪</strong></p>
<p>糞 <span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，从采(矢)。合起来表示双手持簸箕清除污秽。本义:扫除)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[clean up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粪，弃除也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堂上不粪，则郊草不芸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;强国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:粪除(扫除);粪洒(洒扫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 施肥 <span class="encs">[apply manure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡粪种。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;草人》</span>。注:&ldquo;凡所以粪种者，皆谓煮取汁也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却是走马以粪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。注:&ldquo;粪田也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:粪治(施肥治田);粪种(古代的一种耕种方法)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>粪</strong></p>
<p>糞 <span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 屎，粪便 <span class="encs">[excrement;dung;droppings]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一亩之稼，则粪溉者先芽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:粪清(粪汁);粪溷(粪坑;厕所);马粪;牛粪;粪棋(低劣的棋艺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 肥料 <span class="encs">[manure]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:粪壤(指拌有肥料的灰土)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上" target="_blank">未集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上&amp;bs=米" target="_blank">米</a>】
                              糞·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7CDE.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25F07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥼇</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25F08.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥼈</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>方問切，音奮。穢也。【左傳&middot;僖二十八年】榮季曰：是糞土也。【史記&middot;貨殖傳】貴出如糞土。</p>
<p>又治也，培也。【禮&middot;月令】可以糞田疇。【疏】壅苗之根也。【荀子&middot;致仕篇】樹落糞本。</p>
<p>又掃除也。【禮&middot;曲禮】凡爲長者糞之禮，必加帚于箕上。【左傳&middot;昭三年】張趯使謂太叔曰：糞除先人之敝廬。【荀子&middot;經國篇】堂上不糞，則郊草不瞻曠芸。【註】糞，除也。</p>
<p>又非問切，音分。掃棄之也。【韓愈文】糞除天下山川。　【集韻】又作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25ECE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥻎</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21422.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡐢</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/213D9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡏙</span>坋<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/214F4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡓴</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23030.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣀰</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/213F0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡏰</span>。【韻會】本作粪，隷作糞。或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22E54.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢹔</span>。亦作拚，又作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21284.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡊄</span>。【玉篇】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25ED4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥻔</span>。【海篇】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25F22.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥼢</span>。【字彙補】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25EEA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥻪</span>。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25F22.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥼢</span>字原作米下黑，或<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25F2F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥼯</span>字。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2891D.svg">                          <p>棄除也。从廾推<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>棄釆也。官溥說：似米而非米者，矢字。方問切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2891D_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2891D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨤝</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">棄除也。</span><span class="swdz">按棄亦糞之誤，亦復舉字之未<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20702.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠜂</span>者。糞方是除，非棄也。與土部<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21285.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡊅</span>音義皆略同。禮記作糞，亦作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22E54.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢹔</span>，亦作拚。曲禮曰：凡爲長者糞之禮。少儀曰：氾埽曰埽，埽席前曰拚。老子曰：天下有道，卻走馬以糞。謂用走馬佗棄糞除之物也。左傳：小人糞除先人之敝廬。許意<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21285.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡊅</span>用帚，故曰埽除。糞用<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>，故但曰除。古謂除穢曰糞，今人直謂穢曰糞，此古義今義之別也。凡糞田多用所除之穢爲之，故曰糞。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠬞</span>推<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>糞釆也。</span><span class="swdz">合三字會意。方問切。古音在十四部。</span><span class="ywyy">官浦說：佀米而非米者，矢字。</span><span class="swdz">此偁官說釋篆上體，釆佀米非米乃矢字，故廾推<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>除之也。矢，艸部作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26CCA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦳊</span>，云糞也，謂糞除之物爲糞。謂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26CCA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦳊</span>爲矢，自許已然矣，諸書多假矢，如亷蘭傳頃之三遺矢是也。許書說解中多隨俗用字。</span></p>                          </div>
