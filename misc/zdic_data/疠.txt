						<p>● <strong>疠</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg"><span class="diczx3">癘</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p>

	<li>恶疮。</li>
	<li>杀。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>疠</strong></p>
<p>癘 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 恶疮;麻风 <span class="encs">[leprosy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疠，恶疾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伯牛有疾，先儒以为疠也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已大风挛踠瘘疠。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疠风(麻风病);疠人(患染恶疾的人);疠痎(恶性疟疾)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 疫病 ［pestilence］</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时皆有疠疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;疾医》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天有菑疠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疠疾(疠瘥。疫病);疠气(能致疫病的恶气);疠疫(瘟疫);疠役(流行性传染病。即瘟疫)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">癘</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“疠”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/癘">癘</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中" target="_blank">午集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中&amp;bs=疒" target="_blank">疒</a>】
                              癘·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7658.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>力制切，音例。惡瘡疾也。【禮&middot;月令】仲冬行春令，民多疥癘。【史記&middot;曹相國世家】時病癘歸國。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】疫氣也。與㾐同。【左傳&middot;昭四年】癘疾不降。【註】癘，惡氣也。【後漢&middot;順帝紀】上干和氣，疫癘爲災。</p>
<p>又【韻會】通作厲。【史記&middot;豫讓傳】桼身爲厲。【註】人體著桼，多生瘡。</p>
<p>又【嚴安傳】民不夭厲。【註】病也。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】借作勵。【漢衡方<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>】砥仁癘義。【帝堯<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>】癘我以仁。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7658_4194.svg" /><span class="swbf">(癘)</span><span class="ywyy">惡疾也。</span><span class="swdz">按古義謂惡病包内外言之，今義别製癩字，訓爲惡瘡，訓癘爲癘疫。古多借厲爲癘。公羊傳作㾐，何注云：㾐者，民疾疫也。大戴禮及公羊何注說七出皆云：惡疾出。何休曰：惡疾棄者不可以奉宗廟也。論語：伯牛有疾。苞氏曰：牛有惡疾，不欲見人，故孔子從牗執其手也。韓詩曰：芣苢，傷夫有惡疾也。薛君曰：芣苢，澤瀉也，臭惡之草。詩人傷其君子有惡疾，人道不通，求己不得，發憤而作，以事興。芣苢雖臭惡乎，我猶采而不已者，以興君子雖有惡疾，我猶守而不離去也。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2456B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤕫</span>，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27363.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧍣</span>省聲。</span><span class="swdz">按大徐厲作蠆，不誤。洛帶切。十五部。</span></p>                          </div>
