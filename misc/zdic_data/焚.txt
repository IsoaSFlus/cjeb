                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>焚</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&eacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>烧：～烧。～毁。～化。～香。～书坑儒。～膏继晷（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">膏</span>&rdquo;，油脂；&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">晷</span>&rdquo;，日影；形容夜以继日地用功读书或努力工作）。忧心如～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> burn</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> brennen, verbrennen, niederbrennen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> brûler</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">焚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>焚</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&eacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从火，从林。甲骨文字形，象火烧丛木。古人田猎，为了把野兽从树林里赶出来，就采用焚林的办法。本义:烧山)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用火烧山林宿草。 <span class="encs">[burn weeds and till]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚，烧田也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚咸丘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;桓公七年》</span>。传:&ldquo;焚之者何，樵之也;樵之者何，以火攻也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚林而田。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 烧<span class="encs">[burn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚，烧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚如死如弃如。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;离》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂从而尽焚之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃焚大槐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;外物》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:&ldquo;谓霹雳时烧大树也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚百家之言，以愚黔首。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚拜如前人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:玩火者必自焚;焚舟(烧毁渡船);焚池(祭祀时焚化祭品的器物);焚黄(烧掉黄表纸。祭告家庙祖坟，祷文写在黄表纸上);焚灼(烧、焚烧);焚帛(焚烧纸帛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旧时的一种酷刑。用火烧死 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚炙忠良。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;泰誓上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡杀其亲者焚之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;掌戮》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 炙;烤 <span class="encs">[roast]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:焚枯(烤煮干鱼);焚枯食淡(烤干鱼，食薄味)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">焚</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集中" target="_blank">巳集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集中&amp;bs=火" target="_blank">火</a>】
                              焚·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/711A.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕燔【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>符分切，音汾。【玉篇】燒也。【集韻】火灼物也。【書&middot;胤征】玉石俱焚。【易&middot;離卦】焚如。【春秋&middot;桓七年】焚咸丘。【註】焚，火田也。【禮&middot;月令】仲春毋焚山林。</p>
<p>又【集韻】方問切，音奮。【左傳&middot;襄二十四年】象有齒，以焚其身。【註】杜預曰：焚，斃也。服虔曰：焚，讀曰僨僵也。【集韻】本亦作僨。</p>
<p>又叶汾沿切。【郭璞&middot;炎山贊】木含陽精，氣結則焚，理其微乎，其妙在傳。</p>
<p>又叶符筠切。【曹植文】季嗣不維，網漏于秦，崩樂滅學，儒坑禮焚。【說文】本作燓。【集韻】或作炃燌。</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/71D3.svg">                          <p>燒田也。从火、棥，棥亦聲。附袁切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/711A_1.svg" /> <span class="swbf">(焚)</span> <span class="ywyy">燒田也。从火林。</span> <span class="swdz">各本篆作樊，解作从火棥，棥亦聲。今正。按玉篇、廣韵有焚無燓，焚，符分切。至集韵、類篇乃合焚燓爲一字。而集韵廿二元固單出燓字，符袁切。竊謂棥聲在十四部，焚聲在十三部，份古文作彬，解云焚省聲。是許書當有焚字。況經傳焚字不可枚舉，而未見有燓，知火部燓卽焚之譌。玄應書引說文焚，燒田也。字从火，燒林意也，凡四見，然則唐初本有焚無燓，不獨篇、韵可證也。</span></p>                          </div>
