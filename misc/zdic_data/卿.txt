                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卿</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>卿</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄥˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古代高级官名：三公九～。～相。</li>	<li>古代对人敬称，如称荀子为&ldquo;荀卿&rdquo;。</li>	<li>自中国唐代开始，君主称臣民。</li>	<li>古代上级称下级、长辈称晚辈。</li>	<li>古代夫妻互称：～～。～～我我（形容男女间非常亲昵）。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> noble, high officer</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> du ,Qing  (Eig, Fam)​,Minister (alte Form)​ ,Ihr (poetisches Du: enspricht dem englischen thou)​  (Pron)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ministre,dignitaire</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卿</strong> <span class="dicpy">qīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，像二人向食之形。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;从卯，皂( <span class="dicpy">bī</span>)声。&rdquo;&ldquo;卯，事之制也。&rdquo;本义:飨食)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古时高级长官或爵位的称谓。汉以前有六卿，汉设九卿，北魏在正卿下还有少卿。以后历代相沿，清末始废 <span class="encs">[a minister or a high official in ancient times]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿，六卿。天官冢宰、地官司徒、春官宗伯、夏官司马、秋官司寇、冬官司空也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸侯之上大夫卿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span>。注:&ldquo;上大夫曰卿。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿大夫豹饰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;揆度》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大国三卿，皆命于天子;次国三卿，二卿命于天子，一卿命于其君;小国二卿，皆命于其君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有卿之名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五大夫三卿。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以</span><span class="diczx2">相如</span>功大，拜为上卿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荐笏言于卿士。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王公卿相。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有卿士。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卿相(指中央行政机构的长官);卿贰(侍郎的别称;次于九卿的少卿。清代一般为三品到五品的虚衔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代用为第二人称，表尊敬或爱意 <span class="encs">[your]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今卿廓开大计，正与孤同。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿今当塗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;汉纪 》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂欲卿治经。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿言多务。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿今者不略。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代君对臣的称谓 <span class="encs">[an emperor&#39;s form of address for a minister]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:爱卿</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/537F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】去京切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】丘京切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音輕。【說文】章也。从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A0D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠨍</span>皀聲。【徐曰】章善明理也。</p><p>又嚮也。言爲人所歸嚮也。【禮&middot;王制】大國三卿，小國二卿。【儀禮疏】諸侯兼官，但有三卿：司徒兼冢宰、司馬兼春官、司空兼秋官。【玉篇】漢置正卿九：太常、光祿、太僕、衞尉、延尉、鴻臚、宗正、司農、少府。【晉書&middot;百官志】古者，天子諸侯皆名執政大臣曰正卿，自周後始有三公九卿之號。</p><p>又【韻會】秦漢以來，君呼臣以卿。【正韻】君呼臣爲卿，蓋期之以卿也。</p><p>又【韻會】凡敵體相呼亦爲卿，蓋貴之也。隋唐以來，儕輩下已，則稱卿，故宋璟卿呼張易之。</p><p>又【前漢&middot;項籍傳】號爲卿子冠軍。【註】文穎曰：卿子，時人相褒尊之稱，猶言公子也。</p><p>又姓。【風俗通】虞卿之後。</p>
