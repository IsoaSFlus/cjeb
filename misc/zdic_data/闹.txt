                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闹</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>闹</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鬧</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nào.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄠˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>不安静：～市。热～。</li>    <li>搅扰：～心。～腾。</li>    <li>戏耍，耍笑：戏～。～洞房。</li>    <li>发生（疾病或灾害）：～病。～事。～水灾。</li>    <li>发泄，发作：～气。～情绪。</li>    <li>生机勃勃，旺盛，声势浩大，热火朝天地搞：红杏枝头春意～。～元宵。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>闹</strong></p><p>鬧、閙 <span class="dicpy">nào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从市，从門(斗)。市场上的争吵、喧闹。本义:嘈杂，喧扰)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[noisy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸣声相呼和，无理只取闹。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《食虾蟆》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召闹取怒。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《答韦中立论师道书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渐不喜闹</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:闹里(闹哄哄的场合中);人声吵闹;喧闹(喧哗吵闹);闹耳朵(形容嘈杂);闹吵(喧闹，大声争吵)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 热闹 <span class="encs">[boisterous;lively;have a joy time]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">门巷不教当闹市，诗篇转觉足工夫。——</span><span class="diczx2">张籍</span><span class="smcs">《奇元员外》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:闹篮(热闹多事的场合);闹杂(热闹嘈杂);闹热(热闹);闹猛(方言。繁忙热闹);闹洋洋(热闹得很);闹盈盈(热热闹闹);闹闹和和(热闹和睦)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 浓盛 <span class="encs">[luxuriant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绿杨烟外晓寒轻，红杏枝头春意闹。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋祁</span><span class="smcs">《玉楼春》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正当十分春色，花开得正闹。——</span><span class="diczx2">杨朔</span><span class="smcs">《荔枝蜜》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>闹</strong></p><p>鬧 <span class="dicpy">nào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 争吵 <span class="encs">[make a noise;stir up trouble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正闹中间，只见一条大汉挑着一担柴来。——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:叫孩子们别闹了;又哭又闹;闹喳(吵闹);闹垓(争吵;吵架)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引起或发泄 <span
