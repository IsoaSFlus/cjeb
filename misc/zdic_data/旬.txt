                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旬</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>旬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&uacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>十日为一旬（一个月分三旬）：上～。中～。下～。～刊。</li>
	<li>十岁为一旬：年过六～。八～老者。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ten-day period; period of time</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Periode von 10 Tagen, Dekade, Monatsdrittel; Jahrzehnt</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> décade</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旬</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从&ldquo;日&rdquo;、&ldquo;螾/蚓&rdquo;初文声。商代金、甲文假借&ldquo;螾/蚓&rdquo;初文、蚯蚓的像形字表示。西周金文加&ldquo;日&rdquo;。战国文字&ldquo;螾/蚓&rdquo;初文或和&ldquo;勹&rdquo;混同，就成了&ldquo;旬&rdquo;，秦文字和今文字继承而来。)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a period of ten-days]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旬，徧也。十日为旬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽旬玄咎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;丰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三百有六旬有六日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旬终则令正日成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筮旬有一日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;少牢礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旬之外曰远某日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">围</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>数旬，则 <span class="diczx2">梁</span>可拔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;初见秦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兼旬(两个十天);旬期(十天);旬修(十天之中所作所为);旬首(一旬之始);旬浃(满十天);旬宴(古天子旬假日赐群臣之宴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 十岁为一旬，多指人的年龄 <span class="encs">[a period of ten years in a person&rsquo;s age]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且喜同年满七旬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《偶吟自慰兼呈梦得》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:八旬老母</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 巡视;游历 <span class="encs">[tour]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王命台虎，来旬来宣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;江汉》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 光阴;时间 <span class="encs">[time]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">量寸旬，涓吉日，陟中坛，即帝位。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;左思&middot;魏都赋》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旬</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>满;整整 <span class="encs">[full]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:旬月;旬岁</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旬</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上" target="_blank">辰集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上&amp;bs=日" target="_blank">日</a>】
                              旬·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=6" target="_blank">6</a>　·部外筆画：2</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/65EC.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/208D9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠣙</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/208E1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠣡</span>【廣韻】詳遵切【集韻】【韻會】松倫切【正韻】詳倫切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音紃。【說文】徧也。十日爲旬。【書&middot;堯典】朞三百有六旬有六日。</p>
<p>又【大禹謨】三旬。【傳】旬，十日也。【禮&middot;曲禮】凡卜筮日，旬之外曰遠某日，旬之內曰近某日。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】來旬來宣。【傳】旬，徧也。</p>
<p>又【易&middot;豐卦】雖旬无咎。【註】旬，均也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;翟方進傳】旬歲閒，免兩司隷。【註】師古曰：旬，滿也。旬歲猶言滿歲也。</p>
<p>又旬始，星名。【史記&middot;天官書】旬始出於北斗旁，狀如雄雞。</p>
<p>又【正韻】須倫切，音荀。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】規倫切。本作均。《周禮》作旬。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;均人】豐年，則公旬用三日焉。【註】旬，均也。讀如㽦㽦原隰之㽦。《易&middot;坤》爲均，今書亦有作旬者。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶松宣切。【柳宗元&middot;祭從兄文】留連遊歡，涉月彌旬。夜<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24454.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤑔</span>膏炬，晝凌風煙。</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65EC_2524.svg" /><span class="swbf">(旬)</span><span class="ywyy">徧也。</span><span class="swdz">小徐無也字。非是。大雅：王命召虎。來旬來宣。傳曰：旬，徧也。菀彼桑柔，其下侯旬。傳曰：旬，言陰均也。周禮均人。豐年則公旬用三日焉。注曰：旬，均也。讀如㽦㽦原隰之㽦。易：坤爲均。今書亦有作旬者。內則。旬而見。注曰。旬當爲均。嫡、妾同時生子。以生先後見之也。易說卦，坤爲均，今亦或作旬。按旬與均音義皆略同。土部曰：均，平徧也。又按許書古文鈞作銞，儀禮今文絢作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25FA1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥾡</span>。知古旬勻二篆，相假爲用。</span><span class="ywyy">十日爲旬。</span><span class="swdz">此徧中之一義也。而必言之者，說此篆从勹日之意也。日之數十，自甲至癸而一徧。</span><span class="ywyy">从勹日，</span><span class="swdz">勹日猶勹十也。詳遵切。十二部。</span></p>
