						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>派</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄞˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>水的支流：九～（指长江支流之多）。</li>
	<li>一个系统的分支：～系。～别。～性。党～。</li>
	<li>作风，风度：正～。气～。～势。～头。</li>
	<li>分配，指定：～赴。～驻。～遣。委～。</li>
	<li>指摘：～不是。</li>
	<li>量词（❶用于派别，如&ldquo;两～的意见争论不休&rdquo;；❷用于景色、气象、语言等，前面用&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">一</span>&rdquo;字，如&ldquo;一～胡言&rdquo;、&ldquo;好一～北国风光&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">派</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>派</strong> <span class="dicpy">pā</span></p>
<p>另见 <span class="dicpy">p&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs">
<p>◎ <strong>派司</strong> <span class="dicpy">pāsi</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="encs">[pass]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 桥牌叫牌中的术语，表示不叫</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 英文 pass的音译，指厚纸印成的或订成本儿的出入证、通行证等</p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>派</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:水的支流)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[branch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">派，别水也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百川派别。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">九派乎</span><span class="diczx2">浔阳</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《江赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茫茫九派流中国，沉沉一线穿南北。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">毛泽东</span><span class="smcs">《菩萨蛮&middot;黄鹤楼》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:九派(一般指长江的很多支流);派流(水的支流)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指江河的流水 <span class="encs">[current]</span>。如:派合(水流汇合)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 人物、事物的系统、流别 <span class="encs">[school;faction]</span>。如:派演天潢(宗族的支派。天潢:皇族);党派;左派;新派;旧派;宗派;死硬派;保守派;宫廷派;派衍(宗教支派，繁衍)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 气派;风度 <span class="encs">[style;manner and air]</span>。如:派势(派头，气派);派式(派头，模样);派阔(派头);文艺复兴派的油画;正派;官僚派</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 由牛奶、奶油、面粉和不同馅料做成的饼。多作为甜食在饭后吃 <span class="encs">[pie]</span>。如:苹果派;巧克力派</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>派</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 派遣;差遣 <span class="encs">[appoint;send;dispatch]</span>。如:派散(分派散发);派人去办;派拨(调遣);选派(挑选派遣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 分配;分摊 <span class="encs">[distribute]</span>。如:派粮;派款;摊派;派索(摊派索取);派开(分配开来);派征(摊派征收);派取(分摊收取);派赏(分配赏给)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 安排 <span class="encs">[arrange]</span>。如:派拨(安排)</p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人、事或学术的分支系统。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「学派」、「党派」、「诗派」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选&middot;刘孝标&middot;广绝交论》：「然则利交同源，派流则异。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">作风或气度。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「派头」、「气派」、「正派」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量词。计算派别的单位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「这两派画家的画风迥然不同。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一种西式烘焙食品。为英语 pie的音译。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「苹果派」、「柠檬派」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
