						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>晨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chén  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chén.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄣˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 清早，太阳出来的时候：早～。凌～。～光。～曦（晨光）。～风。～雾。～炊。</p></ol>
