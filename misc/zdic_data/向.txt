                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>向</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">嚮</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>对着，朝着，与“<span class="diczx3">背</span>”相对：～背（<span class="dicpy">bèi </span>）。～北。</li><li>目标，意志所趋：志～。方～。</li><li>偏袒，袒护：偏～。</li><li>近，临：～晚。秋天漠漠～昏黑。</li><li>从前：～日。～者。</li><li>从开始到现在：～例。一～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> toward, direction, trend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ehedem, ehem. : ehemals</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> direction,orientation,donner sur,tourner vers,être pour,à,vers</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">向</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>向</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，从口。<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>表示房屋，甲骨文中像屋墙，有窗户之形。本义:朝北开的窗户)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[the window facing north]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向，北出牖也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。古宫室北墉，无户牖，民间或有之，命之曰向。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穹窒熏鼠，塞向墐户。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指窗户 <span class="encs">[window]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闭户塞向，密泥，勿使风入漏气。——<span class="smcs">《齐民要术》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刮楹达鄉(向)。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·明堂位》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓夹户窗也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">启牖鄉(向)如初。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士虞礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 方向;方位 <span class="encs">[direction]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明利害之向。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东向驰去。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《魏叔子文钞·大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:风向;反向;去向;山向;向前(犹将来)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 过去，往昔<span class="encs">[past;before]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向也不恕而今也恕，向也虚而今也实。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·山水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向为身死而不受。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向其先表之时可导也。(以前他们设立标记的时候，是可以根据标记渡水的。导，引导，渡水。)——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寻向所志。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">便扶向路。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向吾不为斯役。(假使过去我不当这个差)——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向察众人之议。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向之噌吰。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:向道(原以为);向年(往年)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 从来，向来 <span class="encs">[always;all along]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣向蒙国恩，刻思图报。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:向料(一直认为);向蒙(一贯承蒙)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 刚才 <span class="encs">[just now]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向见年少妇人自水边小径去矣。——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5411.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>許亮切，音蠁。【說文】北出牖也。从宀从口。【註】牖所以通人气，故从口。【玉篇】窻也。【詩·豳風】塞向墐戸。【傳】北出牖也。【疏】士虞禮註云：嚮、牖一名也。北爲寒之備，不塞南窻，故云北出牖也。【廣韻】嚮與向通用。【集韻】亦作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/245BD.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤖽</span>。【正韻】亦作鄕。【禮·明堂位】刮楹達鄕。◎按明堂位註，鄕謂夾戸窻也。每室八窻，爲四達，則凡牖皆名鄕，不獨北出牖矣。</p><p>又【廣韻】對也。【戰國策】西向事秦。【莊子·秋水篇】望洋向若而嘆。</p><p>又或作嚮。【易·說卦】嚮明而治。　亦作鄕。【禮·明堂位】南鄕而立。</p><p>又【集韻】趣也。</p><p>又【韻會】救也。</p><p>又昔也。【莊子·寓言篇】若向也俯，而今也仰。　亦通作鄕。【論語】鄕也，吾見於夫子而問知。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>式亮切，音餉。【廣韻】姓也。【左傳·成五年】諸侯謀復會宋公，使向爲人辭以子靈之難。【釋文】向，舒亮反。</p><p>又【玉篇】地名。【詩·小雅】皇父孔聖，作都于向。【註】向在東都畿內，今孟州河陽縣。【左傳·隱十一年】王與鄭人向。【註】軹縣西有地，名向上。【襄十一年】諸侯會于北林師于向。【註】在<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2949B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩒛</span>川長社縣東北。【後漢·郡國志】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2949B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩒛</span>川郡有向鄕。</p><p>又【集韻】國名。【春秋·隱二年】莒人入向。【註】向，小國也，譙國龍亢縣東南有向城。【史記·褚少孫補三皇紀】怡向申莒，皆姜姓之後，漢爲縣。【前漢·地理志】沛郡向縣。【註】向，故國，姜姓，炎帝後。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>許兩切，音嚮。【集韻】人字。【左傳·襄十一年】晉侯使叔肸告于諸侯。【註】叔肸，叔向也。【釋文】向，許文反。【前漢·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】作叔嚮，顏師古：音許兩反。</p><p>又【正韻】古文嚮字。註詳十六畫。</p><p>
