                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">笑</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>笑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>露出愉快的表情，发出欢喜的声音：～容。～颜。～眯眯。谈～风生。</li>
	<li>讥嘲：～柄。～话。～谈。贻～大方。嘲～。见～。耻～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> smile, laugh, giggle; snicker</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> lachen, lächeln  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rire,sourire,ridiculiser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">笑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>笑</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从竹，从夭。杨承庆曰，&ldquo;竹得风其体夭屈，如人之笑&rdquo;。字或从竹，从夭。本义:因喜悦开颜或出声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[smile; laugh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笑，喜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杨承庆</span>曰:&ldquo;竹得风，其体夭屈，如人之笑也。 <span class="diczx2">李阳冰</span>刊定说文从之。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竹为乐器，君子乐然后笑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字林》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:&ldquo;此字古皆从犬，本义犬狎人声也。从犬，箾省声。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一握为笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;萃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载笑载言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言笑晏晏。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两小儿笑曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乐然后笑，人不厌其笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;宪问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">止增笑耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笑而止之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笑而不顾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笑唏唏(笑嘻嘻);笑咍咍(大声地笑);笑浪(无拘束地大笑);笑面夜叉(外貌和气、心地狠毒的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 讥笑;嘲笑 <span class="encs">[ridicule]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顾我则笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;终风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以五十步笑百步，则何如?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笑端(笑柄);笑骂从汝(我行我素，不顾他人饥笑责骂);笑弄(讥笑;嘲弄);笑侮(嘲笑戏弄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 玩笑;逗乐 <span class="encs">[joke;make fun of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勿以为笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;板》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笑嗷(喜笑;玩闹);笑乐院本(戏曲演出在正戏前加演的小戏);笑耍头回(宋元时说书人在开讲正书前，先讲一个小故事作引子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 喜爱;羡慕 <span class="encs">[like]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:笑眼传心(相视而笑，以目传情);笑悦(欢笑喜悦);笑叹(可笑可叹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 比喻花朵开放 <span class="encs">[flow;open]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">露浓晞晚笑，风劲浅残香。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐太宗</span><span class="smcs">《赋得残菊花》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笑笑(花盛开的样子);笑靥金(菊花的别称)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上" target="_blank">未集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上&amp;bs=竹" target="_blank">竹</a>】
                              笑·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7B11.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕咲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/201C4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠇄</span>【廣韻】私妙切【集韻】【韻會】仙妙切【正韻】蘇弔切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音肖。【廣韻】欣也，喜也。【增韻】喜而解顏啓齒也。</p>
<p>又嗤也，哂也。【易&middot;萃卦】一握爲笑。【詩&middot;邶風】顧我則笑。【毛傳】侮之也。【禮&middot;曲禮】父母有疾，笑不至矧。【註】齒本曰矧，大笑則見。【左傳&middot;哀二十年】吳王曰：溺人必笑。【論語】夫子莞爾而笑。【註】小笑貌。</p>
<p>又獸名。【廣東新語】人熊，一名山笑。</p>
<p>又【韻補】思邀切。【詩&middot;大雅】勿以爲笑，叶上囂、下蕘。【淮南子&middot;汜論訓】不殺黃口，不獲二毛，于古爲義，于今爲笑。【古逸詩&middot;趙童謠】趙爲號，秦爲笑。以爲不信，視地上生毛。</p>
<p>又入宥韻，音秀。【江總詩】玉臉含啼還自笑，若使琴心一曲奏。　或作咲。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】樵夫咲之。　亦省作关。【前漢&middot;薛宣傳】一关相樂。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7B11.svg">                          <p>此字本闕。私妙切〖注〗臣鉉等案：孫愐《唐韻》引《說文》云：&ldquo;喜也。从竹从犬。&rdquo;而不述其義。今俗皆从犬。又案：李陽冰刊定《說文》从竹从夭義云：竹得風，其體夭屈如人之笑。未知其審。文百四十四　重十五 新附</p>                          </div>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/25B07_1.svg" /> <span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>)</span> <span class="ywyy">喜也。从竹从犬。</span> <span class="swdz">徐鼎臣說孫愐唐韵引說文云。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>，喜也。从竹，从犬。而不述其義。攷孫愐唐韵序云：仍篆<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23729.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣜩</span>石經勒存正體，幸不譏煩。葢唐韵每字皆勒說文篆體，此字之从竹犬，孫親見其然，是以唐人無不从犬作者。干祿字書云：咲通，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>正。五經文字力尊說文者也，亦作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>喜也，从竹下犬。玉篇竹部亦作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>。廣韵因唐韵之舊亦作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>。此本無可疑者。自唐<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>度九經字㨾始先笑後<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>，引楊承慶字統異說云从竹从夭。竹爲樂器，君子樂然後笑。字統每與說文乖異，見<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>應書。葢楊氏求从犬之故不得，是用改夭形聲，唐氏從之，李陽冰遂云竹得風，其體夭屈如人之笑。自後徐楚金缺此篆，鼎臣竟改說文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>作笑，而集韵、類篇乃有笑無<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>，宋以後經籍無<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>字矣。今以顧野王、孫愐、顏元孫、張參爲據。復其正始。或問曰：从犬可得其說乎。曰从竹之義且不敢<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/216B6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡚶</span>言，況从犬乎。聞疑載疑可也。假云必不宐从犬，則哭又何以从犬乎。哭之獄省聲乃亦强作解事者爲之也，詳哭下。私妙切。二部。○又按宋初說文本無<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>，鉉增之，十九文之一也。孫愐但从竹从犬，其本在竹部，抑在犬部。鉉不能知，姑綴於竹末。今依之，恐有未協。凖哭从犬求之，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>或本在犬部，而从竹部之字之省聲。未可知也。</span></p>                          </div>
