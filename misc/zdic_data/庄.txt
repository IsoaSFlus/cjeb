                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>庄</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">莊</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄤˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>村落，田舍：村～。～户。～稼。</li>	<li>封建社会君主、贵族等所占有的成片土地：皇～。～主。～客。</li>	<li>商店的一种名称：茶～。饭～。钱～。</li>	<li>某些种类的赌博，局中人轮流为主：～家。坐～。</li>	<li>四通八达的道路：康～大道。</li>	<li>严肃，端重：～严。～重（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）。端～。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>庄</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">p&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>平。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>庄</strong></p>
<p>莊 <span class="dicpy">zhuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从艸，从壮，壮亦声。本义:草大)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grassy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莊，草芽之壮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《六书正伪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莊，草盛貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《唐韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汉明帝</span>名 <span class="diczx2">莊</span>， <span class="diczx2">汉</span>世多以严字为之。<span class="smcs">《左传》</span>、<span class="smcs">《楚莊王国》</span>、<span class="smcs">《莊子》</span>、<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>、<span class="smcs">《卞莊子》</span>，古今人表皆作严，此字从草，壮声，许不著说解，其义失传。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 谨严持重 <span class="encs">[solemn;sober;serious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临之以庄，则敬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;为政》</span></span></p>
