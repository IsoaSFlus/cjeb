up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥民析。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“其民老壮分析也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">逮归，季孟已有析烟之议。——</span><span
