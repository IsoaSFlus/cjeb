						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>拒</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩˋ</span></p><ol><li>抵挡，抵抗：～守。～敌。抗～。</li><li>不接受：～绝。～谏（拒绝劝谏）。来者不～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">矩</span>”，方阵。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拒</strong> <span class="dicpy">jù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，巨声。本义:抵御，抵抗)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[repel;resist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其不可者拒之。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拒敌也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·仲尼》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">内以固城，外以拒难。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·君道》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:拒御(抵御);拒敌(抵御敌人;匹敌，比得上);拒捍(抵抗);拒马(古代的一种防御战具。用以布阵阻塞，使人马止步，不得跨越)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拒绝，抵制 <span class="encs">[decline;deny;refuse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">往者不追，来者不拒。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·尽心下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:拒谏(不肯接受别人的规劝);拒物(拒绝外物，谓不与外界接触);拒却(拒绝，推却);拒闭(闭门拒绝)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 违抗 <span class="encs">[disobey]</span>。如:拒违(违抗);拒逆(违抗)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 据守 <span class="encs">[guard]</span>。如:拒抗(拒守险狭之地);拒塞(扼守边关);拒隘(犹拒险)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 占据 <span class="encs">[occupy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不即安分，却来拒吾之公厅，此又不知耻也。——<span class="smcs">《清平山堂话本》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> <span class="encs">[树枝]</span> 向外岔出，横生 <span class="encs">[fork]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">数披其木，无使木枝外拒。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“矩”</span>。方形阵势 <span class="encs">[square array]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拘环拒折之容。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·齐俗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">从唐侯以为左拒，以从上军。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郑子元</span>请为左拒，以当 <span class="diczx2">蔡</span>人， <span class="diczx2">卫</span>人;为右拒，以当 <span class="diczx2">陈</span>人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·恒公五年》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
