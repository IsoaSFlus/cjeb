                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锁</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>锁</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鎖</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">suǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄛˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>加在门窗、器物等开合处或连接处，必须用钥匙、密码、磁卡等才能打开的金属装置：门～。密码～。～钥。</li>
	<li>用锁锁住：把门～上。</li>
	<li>链子；链条：～链。枷～。长命～。</li>
	<li>关闭；幽闭：闭关～国。经济封～。</li>
	<li>紧皱；蹙紧：愁眉深～。</li>
	<li>一种缝纫方法，用线顺着布边或扣眼密缝：～边。～扣眼。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> lock, padlock; shackles, chains</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schloss  (S, Tech)​,abschließen, verschließen  (V)​,einlochen, einsperren  (V)​,Suo  (Eig, Fam)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>锁</strong></p>
<p>鎖、鎻、鏁 <span class="dicpy">suǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 置于可启闭的器物上，以钥匙或暗码(如字码机构、时间机构、自动释放开关、磁性螺线管等)打开的扣件 <span class="encs">[lock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柄铁折叠环复，如锁上练，引之长丈许。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锁管(锁簧插入的锁身);锁孔(锁眼);锁梃儿(锁的销子);上锁</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 一种用铁环钩连而成的刑具 <span class="encs">[iron chain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若见鬼把椎锁绳纆，立守其旁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;订鬼篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锁靠(束缚手腕的刑具);锁韁(枷锁和韁绳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 链子 <span class="encs">[chain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俄有赤龙长千余尺，电目血舌，朱鳞火鬣，项掣全锁，锁牵玉柱，千雷万霆，激绕其身。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锁子(锁链);锁甲(锁子铠，锁子错甲，即锁子甲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>锁</strong></p>
<p>鎖 <span class="dicpy">suǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 加锁，用锁锁住 <span class="encs">[lock up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长安布衣淮比数，反锁衡门守环堵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《秋雨叹》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锁闭(加锁关闭);锁禁(关锁禁闭);锁锢(关销禁闭);锁院(科举考试时，为防作弊，封锁试场之门，称为&ldquo;锁院&rdquo;。宋代有机密则锁闭院门上奏。因又称机密之奏为锁院)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 紧缩而形成皱纹 <span class="encs">[knit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:锁黛(美女锁眉头);双眉紧锁</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 缝制 <span class="encs">[lockstitch]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:锁边;锁眼</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 束缚 <span class="encs">[tie]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">僧辩</span>既入，背泉而坐，曰:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">鲍</span>郎有罪，令旨使我锁卿，卿勿以故意见期。&rdquo;因出令示 <span class="diczx2">泉</span>，锁之床下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《梁书&middot;鲍泉传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锁缚(用铁链或绳索捆缚);锁索(用铁链系束);锁士(囚禁的士人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 封闭;封锁 <span class="encs">[block;seal;close]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:锁印(年终封印停止办公);锁国</span></p>
