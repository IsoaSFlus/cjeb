                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">沮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>沮</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，且声。①( <span class="dicpy">jū</span>)本义:古水名。在今陕西省。②阻止)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“阻”</span>。阻止，阻遏;终止 <span class="encs">[prevent;stop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乱庶斯沮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·巧言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其次，为赏劝罚沮。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·靳令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">嬖人有臧仓者沮君，君是以不果来也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是以为善者劝，为不善者沮，上下一心，三军同力。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·强国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沮之以兵。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·儒行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">溯流转漕，谷恒输沮。——<span class="smcs">《汉·巴郡太守都亭侯张纳功德叙》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:沮挠(阻挠;阻止);沮丘(背水的山丘);沮格(阻拦);沮厄(阻挠遏止);沮止(阻止，遏止)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 畏惧，恐惧 <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沮之以兵。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·儒行》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓恐怖之也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:沮畏(畏惧);沮恐(畏惧，恐惧);沮喘(恐怖);沮惮(恐惧);沮慑(恐惧)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 破坏，败坏 <span class="encs">[ruin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妄举，则事沮不胜。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·二柄》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:沮咹衂(受挫;挫败);沮坏(中伤;败坏);沮解(破坏而使其离散)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 诋毁，诽谤 <span class="encs">[slander]</span>。如:沮短(谗言毁谤);沮诽(诽谤);沮弃(诋毁放弃);沮陷(毁伤诬陷);沮訾(诋毁);沮薄(贬损，诋毁)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>沮</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 丧气、颓丧或灰心失望 <span
