						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>预</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">預</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˋ</span></p><ol><li>事前：～习。～计。～见。～先。～言。～科。～测。～料。～祝。～知。～谋。～感。～算。</li><li>参与：干～。参～。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xié</span>)， 予声。本义:安乐。经典通用<span advance;beforehand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">预备走舸。(豫备轻快的小船。豫，同<span class="yhcs">“预”</span>，预先。舸，船;走舸，快船，偏正式词组。)——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:预虑(事先考虑);预筮(预卜吉凶);预借元宵(预先点放灯);预日(提前一天);预图(预先考虑);预置(事前陈设)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>预</strong></p><p>預 <span 参与。通<span class="yhcs">“与”</span> <span class="encs">[take part in]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡事相及为预。——<span class="smcs">《珠丛》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且</span><span class="diczx2">灌夫</span>何预也?——<span class="smcs">《汉书·灌夫传》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“预读曰豫。预，干也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">次日设朝，</span><span class="diczx2">董太后</span>降旨，封 <span class="diczx2">皇子协</span>为 <span class="diczx2">陈留王</span>， <span class="diczx2">董重</span>为骠骑将军， <span class="diczx2">张让</span>等共预朝政。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:预席(参加宴会);预选(参与考试选拔);预会(参加集会);预宴(赴宴)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
