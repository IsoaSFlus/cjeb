                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">脑</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>脑</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">腦</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄠˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>高等动物神经系统的主要部分，在颅腔里，主管感觉和运动。人脑又是思想记忆等心理活动的器官：～髓。～子（❶脑；❷指思考、记忆等能力）。～筋。～海。～际。～壳。～颅。～神经。～下垂体。～积水。～溢血。电～。</li>    <li>头：～袋。～壳。头昏～胀。～满肠肥。</li>    <li>形状或颜色像脑的东西：豆腐～儿。</li>    <li>指从物体中提炼出的精华部分：樟～。薄荷～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> brain</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gehirn  (S, Med)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cerveau,cervelle</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">脑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>脑</strong></p><p>腦、匘 <span class="dicpy">nǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:脑髓)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[substance of brain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匘，头髓也。从匕，匕，相比著也。巛象发，囟象脑形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，囟头会匘盖也。字亦作腦。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹙于(脑)而休于气。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·弓人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其荣发也。——<span class="smcs">《素问·五藏生气篇》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“脑为髓海。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人精在脑。——<span class="smcs">《春秋元命苞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋侯</span>梦与 <span class="diczx2">楚子</span>搏， <span class="diczx2">楚子</span>伏己而盐其脑，是以惧。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十八年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 形状或颜色像脑髓的东西 <span class="encs">[sth. resembling brain]</span>。如:豆腐脑儿</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 头部 <span class="encs">[head]</span></p><p><span
