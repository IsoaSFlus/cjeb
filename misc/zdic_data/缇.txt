                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缇</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>缇</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">緹</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄧˊ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 橘红色：～衣（古代骑士的服装）。～帷。～骑（帝王出巡时护卫的骑兵，后指逮捕犯人的骑兵）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> reddish color, red, brown</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> soie rouge orangée</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缇</strong></p><p>緹 <span class="dicpy">tí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，是声。本义:红黄色，丹黄色)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[oranged red]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缇，帛丹黄色。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缇，赤也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缇十重。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·应劭传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“赤色缯也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赤缇用羊。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·草人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“縓色也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四曰缇齐。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·酒正》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“成而红赤。如今下酒也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为治斋宫河上，张缇降帷。——<span class="smcs">《史记·滑稽列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:缇衣(指武士的服装。因衣色丹黄而称之);缇帷(橘红色的绢制帐幕);缇齐(酒名。因其色呈橘红而得名);缇骑(秦设中尉，汉武帝更名执金吾。以其为著橘红色衣装的骑兵，故称之);缇幔(橘红色的帷幕)。特指古代军服的颜色，常用以代指武装人员</p><p><span class="diczx1">凡兵事，韦弁服。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“今时伍伯缇衣，古兵服之遣色。”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>缇</strong></p><p>緹 <span class="dicpy">tí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 橘红色的丝织物 <span class="encs">[orange silk fabrics]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缇骑按剑而前，问<span class="yhcs">“谁为哀者?”</span>——</span><span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:缇(赤色厚缯);缇绣(赤缯与文绣);缇袭(什袭。用赤色缯把物品重重包裹起来)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 莎草的籽实 <span class="encs">[seed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">细茎有三棱，实在茎端，其色赤缇，故曰缇矣。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7DF9.svg">                          <p>帛丹黃色。从糸是聲。衹，緹或从氏。他禮切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">帛丹黃色也。</span>謂丹而黃也下文云。縓、帛赤黃色。丹與赤不同者、丹者如丹沙。與赤異。其分甚微。故鄭注草人曰。赤緹、縓色也。酒正五齊。四曰緹齊。注曰。緹者、成而紅赤。若今下酒矣。按紅赤者、赤而白。緹齊不純赤。故謂之紅赤。緹齊俗作醍。見禮運。<span class="jieshi">从糸。是聲。</span>他禮切。十六部。                          </div>
