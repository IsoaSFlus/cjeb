						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>反</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>翻转，颠倒：～手（ａ．翻过手，手到背后；ｂ．反掌）。～复。～侧。</li>
	<li>翻转的，颠倒的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">正</span>&rdquo;相对：正～两方面的经验。～间（利用敌人的间谍，使敌人内部自相矛盾）。～诉。～馈。适得其～。物极必～。</li>
	<li>抵制，背叛，抗拒：～霸。</li>
	<li>和原来的不同，和预感的不同：～常。</li>
	<li>回击，回过头来：～驳。～攻。～诘。～思。～躬自问。</li>
	<li>类推：举一～三。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">反</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>反</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，从又从厂。&ldquo;厂&rdquo;音 <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>。本义:手心翻转)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。通&ldquo;翻&rdquo;。覆，倾倒 <span class="encs">[turn over]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反，覆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以</span><span class="diczx2">齐王</span>，由反手也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何以知其不反水浆邪?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张安世传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 反覆 <span class="encs">[over and over again;again and again]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安反侧于万物。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不思其反。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">成</span>反复自念。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:辗转反侧;反易(颠倒);反风(风向倒转)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;返&rdquo;。返回;回归 <span class="encs">[go back]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使</span><span class="diczx2">子路</span>反见之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;微子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫迷途知反，往哲是与。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至竟而反。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;卫策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三年而反。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;鲁问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旬有五日而后反。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">经纶世务者，窥谷忘反。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今日往而不反者，竖子也!&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则盍反其本矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:反意(返归的念头);反国(归国);反真(返归淳朴);反根(返回本原)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 未能遵守，违背 <span class="encs">[violate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天反时为灾，地物为妖，民反德为乱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:反背(背叛);反德(违背事物的准则);反道(违反正道);反情(违反人情);反古(违反古训)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 往返于&hellip;之间 <span class="encs">[go there and back]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寒暑易节始一反焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:反往(往返);反报(往还，反复)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 反叛;造反 <span class="encs">[revolt;rebel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">上官桀子</span>安与 <span class="diczx2">桑弘羊</span>及 <span class="diczx2">燕主</span>、 <span class="diczx2">盖主</span>谋反。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日夜望将军至，岂敢反乎!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:反上(背叛君主);反天(造反);反心(背叛之心);反外(背叛，离异)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 反省 <span class="encs">[introspect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知不足，然后能自反。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 又如:反情(内省);反听(自我省察);反观(反省);反躬自问(反问自己)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 归还;送还 <span class="encs">[return]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 又如:反籍(归还天子之位);反璧(归还璧玉;表示不贪取财宝)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 回报;复命 <span class="encs">[report back on what has been done]</span>。如:反始(报答祖先);反命(复命)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(18)</span> 类推 <span class="encs">[analogize]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举一隅，不以三隅反，则不复也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;述而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(19)</span> 又如:反隅(比喻类推。能由此而知彼);反惑(释疑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(20)</span> 反对 <span class="encs">[oppose;be against;fight]</span>。如:反迕(不顺从);反拨(犹言反抗;抵抗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(21)</span> 报复 <span class="encs">[make reprisals;retaliate]</span>。如:反把(反扑);反报(报复怨仇);反杀(报杀人之仇而杀人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(22)</span> 通&ldquo;贩&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">f&agrave;n</span>)。贱买而贵卖 <span class="encs">[buy to resell]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/53CD.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B21.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠬡</span>【唐韻】府遠切【集韻】【韻會】甫遠切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音返。【說文】覆也。从又，厂。【詩&middot;周頌】福祿來反。【註】言福祿之來，反覆不厭也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>勝傳】使者五反。【註】師古曰：反，謂回還也。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】孚袁切【正韻】孚艱切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音幡。【廣韻】斷獄平反。【韻會】錄囚平反之，謂舉活罪人也。【增韻】理正幽枉也。【前漢&middot;食貨志】杜周治之，獄少反者。【註】反，音幡。</p>
<p>又通作翻。【前漢&middot;張安世傳】反水漿。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>部版切，翻上聲。【集韻】難也。【詩&middot;小雅】威儀反反。沈重讀。</p>
