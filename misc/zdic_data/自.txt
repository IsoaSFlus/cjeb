                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>自</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗˋ</span></p><ol><li>本人，己身：～己。～家。～身。～白。～满。～诩。～馁。～重（<span class="dicpy">zhòng </span>）。～尊。～谦。～觉（<span class="dicpy">jué </span>）。～疚。～学。～圆其说。～惭形秽。～强不息。</li><li>从，由：～从。～古以来。</li><li>当然：～然。～不待言。～生～灭。放任～流。</li><li>假如：～非圣人，外宁必有内忧。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>自</strong> <span class="dicpy">zì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形。象鼻形。<span class="yhcs">“自”</span>是汉字的一个部首。本义:鼻子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[nose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自，鼻也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“ <span class="diczx2">许</span>谓自与鼻义同音同，而用自为鼻者绝少也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 始;开头 <span class="encs">[beginning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故法者，王之本也;刑者，爱之自也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 起源 <span class="encs">[origin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知风之自，知微之显，可以入德也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:自顷(近来)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>自</strong> <span class="dicpy">zì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 自己，自我;本身 <span class="encs">[self;oneself;one’s own]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不自为政，卒劳百姓。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅 ·节南山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自引而起，绝袖。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人必自侮，然后人侮之;家必自毁，而后人毁之;国必自伐，而后人伐之。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自比于人。(<span class="yhcs">“人”</span>指信陵君。)——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自度终不能得之于王。(<span class="yhcs">“度”</span>，估计;<span class="yhcs">“得”</span>，指圆满得到;<span class="yhcs">“之”</span>，代援赵的具体措施。)——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自疏濯淖污泥之中。(自疏，自己疏远。濯，通<span class="yhcs">“浊”</span>。淖(</span><span class="dicpy">nào</span>)泥浆。)——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自知其陋而谨护其失。(其:第一人称代词，自己。陋:见闻少，知识浅薄。而:转折连词，可是，却。谨护其失:谨慎小心地掩盖自己的过错。失:过错。)——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自言本是京城女，家在</span><span class="diczx2">虾蟆陵</span>下住。—— <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span
