                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">驿</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>驿</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">驛</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>旧时供传递公文的人中途休息、换马的地方，亦指供传递公文用的马：～站。～馆。～丞（掌管驿站的官员）。～吏。</li>
	<li>现为地名用字：龙泉～（在中国四川省）。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">绎</span>&rdquo;，络绎。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> relay station</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Poststation </p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> poste,relais de poste</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">驿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>驿</strong></p>
<p>驛 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，睪( <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:古代供传递公文或传送消息用的马)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[post horse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驿，置骑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即</span><span class="diczx2">孟子</span>置郵，所谓遽也。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">馹为传车，驿为马骑。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚子</span>乘驿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十六年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使驿谒诸王。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后数日驿至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 驿站 <span class="encs">[post]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驿外断桥边，寂寞开无主。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《卜算子&middot;咏梅》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:驿递夫马(驿马);驿马星(星相家的迷信说法，若占卜得驿马星，表示将远行、赴任、移居);驿吏(管理驿站的官吏);驿亭(古代作为行旅中途休息住宿的处所);驿书(以驿马传送的文书);驿馆(旅馆，驿舍);驿骑(乘驿马传递文书的使者)</span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9A5B_7796.svg" /><span class="swbf">(驛)</span><span class="ywyy">置騎也。</span><span class="swdz">言騎以别於車也。馹爲傳車，驛爲置騎，二字之别也。周禮傳遽注曰：傳遽若今時乘傳騎驛而使者也。葢乘傳謂車，騎驛謂馬。玉藻注云：傳遽，以車馬给使者也。車謂傳，馬謂遽，渾言則傳遽無二，析言則傳遽分車馬。亦可證單騎從古而有，非經典所無。許傳下云遽也，遽下云傳也，此渾言也。驛下云置騎也，馹下云傳也，此析言也。置騎猶孟子言置𨜚，俗用駱驛。</span><span class="ywyy">从馬睪聲。</span><span class="swdz">羊益切。古音在五部。</span></p>                          </div>
