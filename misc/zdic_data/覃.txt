                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>覃</strong> <span class="dicpy">Q&iacute;n</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>覃</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 意味深长 <span class="encs">[meaning;knowing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">覃，长味也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;此与酉部醰，音同义近。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 长，悠长 <span class="encs">[long]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">葛之覃兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;葛覃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:覃恩(深厚广大的恩惠)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>覃</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蔓延，延伸，延伸到 <span class="encs">[spread on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">覃及</span><span class="diczx2">鬼方</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;荡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">葛之覃兮，施于中谷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;葛覃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:覃覃(延展的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 广施<span class="encs">[恩惠]</span> <span class="encs">[widely bestow]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:覃恩(广布恩泽)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>覃</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 深入地 <span class="encs">[deeply]</span></p>
