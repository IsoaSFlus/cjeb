						<p>● <strong>戏</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">戲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>玩耍：游～。儿～。嬉～。～豫（嬉游逸乐）。二龙～珠。</li>
	<li>嘲弄，开玩笑：～言。～弄。～谑（用诙谐有趣的话开玩笑）。</li>
	<li>戏剧，也指杂技：一出～。黄梅～。看～。演～。皮影～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>戏</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 戯、戲 <span class="dicpy">hū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &mdash;&mdash;&ldquo;於戏&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">wūhū</span>):同&ldquo;呜呼&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">wūhū</span>)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;;huī</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>戏</strong></p>
<p>戯、戲 <span class="dicpy">huī</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;麾&rdquo;。将军的旌旗 <span class="encs">[standard of a commander]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:戏戟(有旗的戟);戏下(在主帅的旌麾之下。引申为部下)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>戏</strong></p>
<p>戲 <span class="dicpy">huī</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指挥 <span class="encs">[command;direct]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:戏竹(指挥奏乐的用具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hū;x&igrave;</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>戏</strong></p>
<p>戲、戲 <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从戈。䖒( <span class="dicpy">xī</span>)声。本义:一种兵器)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[weapon]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;麾&rdquo;。军队中的帅旗 <span class="encs">[flag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵罢戏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高帝纪》</span>。正义:&ldquo;大旗也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戏下骑从者八百余人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;项籍传》</span>。注:&ldquo;大将之旗。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 侧翼 <span class="encs">[side wing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戏，三军之偏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;偏为前拒之偏，谓军所驻之一面也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指歌舞杂技等的表演 <span class="encs">[play;sport]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戲</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“戏”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/戲">戲</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=戈" target="_blank">戈</a>】
                              戲·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6232.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】香義切【正韻】許義切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>希去聲。【說文】三軍之偏也。一曰兵也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】戲弄也。【禮&middot;坊記】閨門之內，戲而不歎。【註】戲謂孺子言笑者。</p>
<p>又謔也。【詩&middot;衞風】善戲謔兮。</p>
<p>又嬉也。</p>
<p>又姓。【魏志】穎川戲志才。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】古文呼字。註詳口部五畫。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】許羈切。【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】虛宜切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音羲。【集韻】嗚戲，歎辭。○按《廣韻》旣入虞韻，云古文呼字，又入支韻。亦訓歎辭，則戲字二音，本皆可讀。《正字通》云：詩於戲卽嗚呼，歎辭也或嘉美，或悲傷。末代文士輒爲體例，若哀誄祭文，卽爲嗚呼，封拜冊命，卽爲於戲。謂嗚呼爲哀傷，於戲爲歎美，妄爲穿鑿，其說誠是。然謂呼與戲通，則戲可讀呼，呼仍不可以讀戲，以戲字有羲音，呼字止入七虞也。</p>
<p>又與羲通。伏羲。【莊子】作伏戲。【史記】作虙戲。【荀子&middot;成相篇】文武之道同虙戲。</p>
<p>又地名。【魯語】幽王滅於戲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】呼爲切【韻會】吁爲切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>與麾同。【集韻】旗屬。【周禮&middot;夏官】建大麾以田。或作戲。【史記&middot;項羽紀】諸侯罷戲下，各就國。【註】同麾。</p>
<p>又【集韻】驅爲切，音虧。傾側也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;喪祝註】執披備傾戲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】於宜切，音漪。本作陭。【說文】上黨陭氏阪。或作戲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】桑何切，音娑。娑本作犧。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;兩獻註】酒尊名。飾以翡翠。鄭司農說，或作獻，亦作戲。</p>
