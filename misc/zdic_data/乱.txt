                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乱</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>乱</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">亂</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">luàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/luàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>没有秩序：～套。紊～。凌～。</li><li>社会动荡，战争，武装骚扰：～世。政～。平～。～邦不居。</li><li>混淆：～伦。败常～俗。</li><li>任意随便：～吃。～跑。</li><li>男女关系不正当：淫～。</li><li>横渡：～流。</li><li>治理：～臣。</li><li>古代乐曲的最后一章或辞赋末尾总括全篇要旨的部分：～曰。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> confusion, state of chaos, revolt</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> verwirrt, durcheinander, Unordnung, Chaos</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> désordre,confusion,anarchie, rébellion</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>乱</strong></p><p>亂 <span class="dicpy">luàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/luàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，象上下两手在整理架子上散乱的丝。是<span class="yhcs">“乱”</span>的本字。本义:理丝)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[put silk in order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人以一手持丝，又一手持互以收之，丝易乱，以互收之，则有条不紊，故字训治训理也。——</span><span class="diczx2">杨树达</span><span class="smcs">《积微居小学述林》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 治理 <span class="encs">[administer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予有乱臣十人。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:乱政(治理政务;干扰、败坏政治)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 扰乱;打乱;使乱 <span class="encs">[disturb; trouble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行拂乱其所为。——<span
