                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>狱</strong></p><p>獄 <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="diczx1">狱，确也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而有狱讼者。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司徒》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“争罪曰狱。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">利用狱。——<span class="smcs">《易·噬嗑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋敢折狱。——<span class="smcs">《易·象上传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以两剂禁民狱。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司徒》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谁谓女无家，何以速我狱?——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“狱，讼也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以两剂禁民狱。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“狱，谓相告以罪名者。”</span></p><p>又如:狱诉(告状);狱讼(诉讼);狱犴(诉讼);狱司(管理诉讼、牢狱的机构)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>狱</strong></p><p>獄 <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 讼案 <span class="encs">[lawsuit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫君臣无狱。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以明庶政，无敢折狱。——<span class="smcs">《易·贲》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:狱辞(决狱之词);狱文(判决狱讼的文书);狱主(讼案的当事人)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 刑狱 <span class="encs">[imprisonment]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">教，政之本也;狱，政之末也。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">董仲舒</span><span class="smcs">《春秋繁露》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:文字狱;动辄兴狱;狱牒(刑狱的判决文书);狱牍(刑狱的案卷);狱事(有关刑狱之事);狱法(刑狱之法)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 刑罚 <span class="encs">[penalty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">廷议大辟，上终怜其才，故缓其狱。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">昭连</span><span class="smcs">《啸亭杂录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:狱深(刑罚严酷);狱刑(刑罚)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 监狱，牢房 <span class="encs">[prison]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哀我填寡，宜岸宜狱。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱熹</span>集传:<span class="yhcs">“岸，亦狱也。<span class="smcs">《韩诗》</span>作‘犴’。乡亭之系曰犴，朝廷曰狱。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:狱家院子(狱卒);狱底(古代监禁重犯的地下室);狱级(狱吏);狱牢(监狱)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 罪，罪案 <span class="encs">[crime]</span>。如:狱具(罪案已定)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
