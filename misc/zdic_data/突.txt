                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>突</strong> <span class="dicpy">tū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从穴，从犬。本义:犬从洞穴中突然窜出)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 狗从穴中突然而出 <span class="encs">[dog run out suddenly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">突，犬从穴中暂出也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>系传:<span class="yhcs">“犬匿于穴中伺人，人不意之，突然而出也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是犹以鞿而御駻突。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·刑法志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“恶马也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 急速地向前或向外冲 <span class="encs">[assault forward or outward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铁骑突出刀枪鸣。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">提九突奔客。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驰突巷战。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《复庵记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">突骑。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·光武纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“言能冲突军陈。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:突轶(袭击;冲击);突战(伏兵袭击的战术);突然袭击(军事用语。乘敌方不备发动快速进攻);突骑(冲破敌军阵地的骑兵);突门(正式城门以外的秘密出口);突将(冲锋陷阵的骠悍将卒);突忽(莽撞唐突貌);突冒(冲撞);突撞(冲撞);突冲(冲撞;猛冲);突地(以头触地)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 触犯 <span class="encs">[offend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汗漫突盗以先之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王霸》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“陵触也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:突冒(触犯);突浡(鲁莽;反常)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 欺诈 <span class="encs">[cheat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">突，欺也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 穿;破<span class="encs">[break]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宵突陈城。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“穿也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>
