                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>诱</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">誘</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>劝导，教导：～导。～发。</li>	<li>使用手段引人：引～。利～。</li>	<li>称美之辞：&ldquo;～然与日月争光&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> persuade, entice, induce; guide</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> veranlassen, überreden, anleiten  (V)​,verlocken, verführen, verleiten, ködern  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> guider,instruire,entraîner,leurre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>诱</strong></p>
<p>誘 <span class="dicpy">y&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，秀声。字本作&ldquo;羑&rdquo;，从厶( <span class="dicpy">sī</span>)，从羑( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)。厶，同&ldquo;私&rdquo;。羑，诱导。本义:教导;引导;劝导)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[instruct;teach;guide;advise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诱，引也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不诱于誉，不恐于诽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;非十二子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫子循循然善诱人，博我以文，约我以礼，欲罢不能。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子罕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:诱动(劝诱打动);诱训(诱导教诲);诱进(诱导进取)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引诱 <span class="encs">[lure;seduce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无诱于势利。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《朱文公校昌黎先生集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盖以诱敌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诱兵(战争时诈败以引诱敌人中计的士兵或引诱敌兵);诱敌(诱引敌人进入埋伏的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 向导，引路 <span class="encs">[guide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">步及骤处兮，诱骋先。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诱接(招引接纳;诱纳);诱协(利诱协迫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 欺狂，诱骗 <span class="encs">[deceire;inveigle;trap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼美</span><span class="diczx2">郦生</span>，既为交亲，诱袭军印，岂无他人。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吕温</span><span class="smcs">《繇鹿赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诱略(诱骗，掠夺);诱狎(诱奸);诱哄(诱惑哄骗);诱巧(以狡猾手段欺骗人)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A98.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕羐【唐韻】與久切【集韻】【韻會】以九切【正韻】云九切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音酉。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】進也。</p><p>又【詩&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>&middot;風衡門序】衡門，誘僖公也。【箋】進也。</p><p>又【玉篇】相勸也。【廣韻】道也。【書&middot;大誥】肆予大化，誘我友邦君。【傳】化其固滯，誘其順從。【詩&middot;召南】有女懷春，吉士誘之。【傳】道也。</p><p>又【廣韻】敎也。【儀禮&middot;鄕射禮】誘射。【註】敎也。</p><p>又【說文】相訹呼也。【玉篇】誘，引也。【左傳&middot;僖十年】卻芮曰：幣重而言甘，誘我也。【史記&middot;越王句踐世家】吳太宰嚭貪，可誘以利。</p><p>又通作牖。【詩&middot;大雅】天之牖民。【正義】牖與誘通。【禮&middot;樂記】詩云：誘民孔易。【註】詩本作牖。</p><p>又叶演女切，音雨。【韓愈&middot;元和聖德詩】有恇其凶，有餌其誘。其出穰穰，隊以萬數。數上聲。　【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26376.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦍶</span>。或作䛻。【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25903.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥤃</span>。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26376.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦍶</span>字原从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26B12.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦬒</span>从処作。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/3557.svg">                          <p>相訹呼也。从厶从羑。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/3557_1.svg" />誘，或从言秀。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/3557_2.svg" />䛻，或如此。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/3557_3.svg" />羑，古文。與久切〖注〗臣鉉等案：羊部有羑。羑，進善也。此古文重出。文三　重三</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/3557_8309.svg" /><span class="swbf">(㕗)</span><span class="ywyy">相訹呼也。</span><span class="swdz">呼當作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧦝</span>。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧦝</span>，召也。言部曰：訹者，㕗也。㕗與訹二篆爲轉注，今人以手相招而口言㕗，正當作此字。今則誘行而㕗廢矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从厶羑。</span><span class="swdz">羊部曰：羑者，進善也。訹之若進善然，故从羑，與久切。三部。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/3557_8310.svg" /><span class="swbf">(誘)</span><span class="ywyy">或从言秀。</span><span class="swdz">秀聲也。召南曰：有女懷春，吉士誘之。傳曰：誘，道也。按道卽導字。大雅：天之牖民。傳曰：牖，道也。是則傳謂牖誘同字。大雅牖民，韓詩外傳，樂記作誘民。古二字多通用。釋詁曰：誘，進也。儀禮誘射。鄭曰：誘，猶教也。樂記：知誘於外。鄭曰：誘，猶道也，引也。葢善惡皆得謂之誘，論二字之本義。牖訓窗明，誘訓相訹。固有不同，故㕗必从厶。誂下曰：相<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧦝</span>誘也，許意誘不必以正，似板傳爲正字，野有死麕傳爲假借字，惡無禮之詩，必非誂誘之誘也。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/3557_8311.svg" /><span class="swbf">(䛻)</span><span class="ywyy">或如此。</span><span class="swdz">从盾者，盾下曰所以扞身蔽目，葢取自隱藏以招人之意。宋玉曰：若姣姬揚袂鄣日而望所思。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/3557_8312.svg" /><span class="swbf">(羑)</span><span class="ywyy">古文。</span><span class="swdz">羊部有此，云進善也。此云㕗之古文，說古文以羑爲㕗也。古文本但有羑字，後乃有㕗字。訓爲相訹<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧦝</span>，則羑字專爲進善矣。</span></p>                          </div>
