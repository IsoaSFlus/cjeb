                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赵</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赵</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">趙</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhào.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄠˋ</span></p><ol><li>中国古代国名（ａ．战国时代的“赵”；ｂ．西晋结束，匈奴族、羯族先后在北方建立“赵国”，史称“前赵”、“后赵”）：～客（战国时燕赵多侠士，遂以“赵客”为侠士通称）。燕（<span class="dicpy">yàn</span>）～（燕赵之地，泛指北方）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>surname; ancient state</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Zhào [Familienname]  (Eig)​,Zhao  (Eig, Fam)​,Zhao war ein Staat des chinesischen Altertums während der Zeit der Streitenden Reiche.</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赵</strong></p><p>趙 <span class="dicpy">zhào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从走，肖声。本义:快走) 同本义 <span class="encs">[quickly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趙，趋趙也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趙，及也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天子北征，赵行囗舍。——<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“赵，犹超腾。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 字亦作<span class="yhcs">“踃”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">简惰跳踃般纷絮兮。——</span><span class="diczx2">傅毅</span><span class="smcs">《舞赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">踃，跳也。字之作逍。——<span class="smcs">《埤仓》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>赵</strong></p><p>趙 <span class="dicpy">Zhào</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Zhao state]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 周穆王封造父于赵，故址在今山西省赵城县西南</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 战国七雄之一。在今山西北部、河北西部和南部一带</p><p><span class="diczx2">赵</span>将 <span class="diczx2">括</span>。(赵国的将军 <span class="diczx2">赵括</span>。)—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遗</span><span class="diczx2">赵王</span>书。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵王</span>于是遂遣。</p><p><span class="diczx2">赵主</span>之子孙。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 东晋十六国之一。在今河北、山西、河南、陕西各一部分。史称<span class="yhcs">“前赵”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 东晋十六国之一。羯族石勒灭前赵后称帝，史称<span class="yhcs">“后赵”</span></p><p><span
