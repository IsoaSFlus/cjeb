                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>忿</strong> <span class="dicpy">fèn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fèn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，分声。从<span class="yhcs">“心”</span>，表示与心情有关。本义:愤怒，怨恨)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[anger;hate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忿，悁也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忿，怒也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以惩忿窒欲。——<span class="smcs">《易·象传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身有所忿窒。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·大学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惩违改忿兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·怀沙》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“恨也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忿慉之气。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·达生》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忿，恨也，怒也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伯主约而不忿。——<span class="smcs">《战国制·秦策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将不胜其忿而蚁附之。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·谋攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾意久怀忿，汝岂得自由。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:忿恨(愤怒怨恨);忿气(气愤;生气);忿怒(愤怒);忿疾(愤怒厌恶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用同<span class="yhcs">“奋”</span>。奋力 <span class="encs">[dash ahead]</span>。如:忿不顾身(即<span class="yhcs">“奋不顾身”</span>);忿力(即<span class="yhcs">“奋力”</span>)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/忿恚">忿恚</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/忿然作色">忿然作色</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
