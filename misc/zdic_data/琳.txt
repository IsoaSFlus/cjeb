                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>琳</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lín  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lín.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄣˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 美玉：～珉。～琅（a.美玉，喻美好珍贵的东西，如“～～满目”；b.玉石发出的声音）。</p></ol>
