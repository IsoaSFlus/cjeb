                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贵</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>贵</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">貴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">guì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guì.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>价钱高，与“<span class="diczx3">贱</span>”相对：～贱（ａ．价格的高低；ｂ．指人的地位高低；ｃ．方言，无论如何）。春雨～如油。</li><li>指地位高：～族。～戚。～望（尊贵的门第和声望）。</li><li>敬辞，称与对方有关的事物：～国。～庚（请问别人年龄）。～姓。～干（<span class="dicpy">gàn </span>）（问人要做什么）。</li><li>特别好的，重要的：～重（<span class="dicpy">zhòng </span>）。珍～。尊～。民为～。</li><li>值得看重，重视：可～。</li><li>指中国贵州省：云～高原。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贵</strong></p><p>貴 <span class="dicpy">guì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形，从贝，臾( <span class="dicpy">guì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guì.mp3"></a></span> )声。从<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>，表示与钱物有关。本义:物价高，与<span class="yhcs">“贱”</span>相对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[expensive;costly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贵，物不贱也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贵货而贱土。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不贵难得之货。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国之诸市，屦贱踊贵。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂白令边郡皆筑仓，以谷贱时增其贾(价)而籴，以利农，谷贵时减贾而粜，名曰常平仓。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:春雨贵如油;昂贵(价格很高);贵腾(价格暴涨);贵踊(物价上涨);贵贾(高价)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 社会地位高 <span class="encs">[of high rank;noble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贵，尊也。贵贱以物喻。犹尊卑以器喻。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一曰爵以驭其贵。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民犹犯贵。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·坊记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贵贵为其近于君也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·祭义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苟富贵，无相忘。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:显贵;权贵;亲贵(帝王的近亲或亲信);富贵(有钱又有地位);贵地(显达的地位;尊称他人的乡里);贵途(升官晋爵的途径);贵私(一心想私人占有);贵不期骄(显贵者不锲望染上骄恣专横的习气，但仍在不知不觉中滋长起来了)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 贵重;重要 <span class="encs">[precious;important]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">礼之用，和为贵。——<span class="smcs">《论语·学而》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:贵宝(贵重的宝物);贵献(贵重的献礼);贵器(贵重的器皿。喻大器或高位);贵珍(贵重的珍品)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 敬辞，尊称与对方有关的事物时用 <span class="encs">[your]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贵房师高要县汤公，就是先祖的门生。——<span
