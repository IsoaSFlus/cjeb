                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">垂</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>垂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨㄟˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>东西一头挂下：～杨柳。～钓。～直。～线。～手（①表示容易；②表示恭敬）。～泪。～髫（头发下垂，指儿童）。～头丧气。</li>	<li>敬辞，用于别人（多是长辈或上级）对自己的行动：～爱。～怜。～询。</li>	<li>传下去，传留后世：～范。永～不朽。</li>	<li>接近，快要：～危。～老。～成。功败～成。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">垂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>垂</strong> <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，( <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:边疆)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;陲&rdquo;。边疆;边境 <span class="encs">[frontier]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垂，远边也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，下垂的垂，说文作垂。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垂，疆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡人无良边垂之臣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;宣公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今大国之地，半天下有二垂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少小去乡邑，扬声沙漠垂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《白马篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">边境之臣处，则疆垂不丧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;臣道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 堂檐下靠阶的地方 <span class="encs">[edge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:垂堂(堂边近阶处屋檐下)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>垂</strong> <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 垂挂 <span class="encs">[hang down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垂带悸兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;芄兰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垂发。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;邓禹传》</span></span></p>
