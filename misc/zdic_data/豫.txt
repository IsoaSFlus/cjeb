                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">豫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>豫</strong> <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从象，予声。本义:大象)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[big elephant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豫，象之大者。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豫焉若冬涉川。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">范应元</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“豫，象属。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 地名。豫州，古九州之一 <span class="encs">[Yu prefecture]</span>。其地包括今河南省和湖北省北部</p><p><span class="diczx1">指通</span><span class="diczx2">豫</span>南。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 今河南省的简称 <span class="encs">[Henan province]</span>。因古为豫州地而得名</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>豫</strong> <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“娱”</span>。快乐 <span class="encs">[happy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豫，乐也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豫，悦豫也。——<span class="smcs">《易·豫卦》</span>释文。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“豫，喜豫说乐之貌也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心中和悦谓之豫。——<span class="smcs">《珠丛》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何问之不豫也?——<span class="smcs">《庄子·应帝王》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫子若有不豫色然。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑下》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“颜色不悦也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">说豫娩泽。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·礼论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“豫，乐也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">致令皇帝受惊，圣躬不豫，实属罪大恶极。——<span class="smcs">《痛史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:豫附(心悦诚服而来归附)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 安乐;舒服 <span class="encs">[comfortable]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或乃奢侈逸豫，务广第宅。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·成帝纪》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>豫</strong> <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 出游 <span class="encs">[go on a sightseeing tour]</span>，特指皇上秋日出巡</p><p><span class="diczx1">度秋豫以收成。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span
