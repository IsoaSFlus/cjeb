                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蠹</strong></p><p>蠧、蠹 <span class="dicpy">dù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:蛀虫)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[book eating insect;moth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蠹，木中虫。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌蠹物。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·翦氏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“穿食人器物者，蠹鱼亦是也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">树郁则为蠹。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·达郁》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“蠹蝎木中之虫也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蠹役(欺压百姓的吏役);蠹怪(蠹虫的精怪);蠹薮(蛀虫聚集的地方);蠹贼(蠹，蠹虫;贼，蟊贼);蠹蝎(水中的蠹虫);木蠹;蠹字(蠹虫所蚀如字的痕迹);蠹物(本指穿蚀衣服的蠹虫。引申泛指为害之物)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申以喻侵蚀或消耗国家财富的人或事 <span class="encs">[vermin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">独是</span><span class="diczx2">成氏</span>子以蠹贫，以促织富，裘马扬扬。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:蠹民(指害人的人或事物);蠹政(害民的政令);蠹言(害人的言辞);蠹役书办(腐败，刁钻的吏役书办)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>蠹</strong> <span class="dicpy">dù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蛀蚀 <span class="encs">[bore through]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是勤可以远淫辟地，户枢不蠹，流水不腐。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">罗大经</span><span class="smcs">《鹤林玉露补遗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蠹木(蛀坏的梁木);蠹心(蛀蚀内部);蠹书(蠹册。被虫蛀坏的书);蠹国(侵蚀、危害国家);蠹朽(木材被蠹腐烂)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 损害 <span class="encs">[harm]</span>。如:蠹民(害民);蠹耗(损害;败坏);蠹政(败坏朝政;害国虐民的统治);蠹俗(败坏风化);蠹刻(侵耗刻削);蠹劳(病害劳累);蠹伤(损害);蠹敝(侵蚀破坏)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
