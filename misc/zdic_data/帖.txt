                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">帖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>帖</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 帖服。对约束、控制或权威命令顺从的 <span class="encs">[obedient]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塞外蛮人方帖服，水边鬼卒又猖狂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三侠五义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:伏伏帖帖;俯首帖耳;帖帖(形容帖伏收敛的样子;温顺，服帖);帖伏(折服，顺从不反抗);帖胁(拘束不展)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 安定 <span class="encs">[stable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事不妥帖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王逸</span><span class="smcs">《楚辞序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安置妥帖平不颇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《石鼓歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:帖然(安定);帖泰(安宁;安定)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 安静 <span class="encs">[quiet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帖，静也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:帖息(平静无声);帖妥(静止不动)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>帖</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 粘。同&ldquo;贴&rdquo; <span class="encs">[glue;stick on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">对镜帖花黄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:帖黄(贴黄。唐时敕书用纸。凡有更改的地方，仍以黄纸贴之)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 紧挨着 <span class="encs">[snuggle close]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因帖卧床上，剑至果高。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;假谲》</span></span></p>
