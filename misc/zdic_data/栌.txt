                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>栌</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">櫨</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>落叶灌木，花黄绿色，秋天变成红色，木材黄色，可制家具，亦可做染料。通称&ldquo;黄栌&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>柱上方木，斗拱。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>栌</strong></p>
<p>櫨 <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 柱头承托栋梁的短木。即欂栌、斗栱 <span class="encs">[arch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可以为栌楝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;修务》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">标栋欂栌，以相支持。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 黄栌 <span class="encs">[smoke tree]</span>。一种落叶灌木。花黄绿色，叶子秋天变成红色。木材黄色，可制器具，也可做染料</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">华枫枰栌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传上》</span>。注:&ldquo;栌，今黄栌木也。&rdquo;</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6AE8.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】落胡切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】龍都切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音盧。【說文】柱上柎也。【徐曰】今謂草木枝端花房之蔕爲柎。此櫨象之，卽今之斗栱也。【淮南子&middot;主術訓】短者以爲朱儒枅櫨。</p>
<p>又果名。【呂覽&middot;本味篇】果之美者，箕山之東，靑島之所，有甘櫨焉。或省作盧。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】盧橘夏熟。</p>
<p>又木也。一名黃櫨，一名楊櫨。【本草拾遺】黃櫨，生商洛山谷四川界。【唐本草】楊櫨，又名空疏，所在皆有。生籬垣閒，其子爲莢。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6AE8_3040.svg" /><span class="swbf">(櫨)</span><span class="ywyy">欂櫨也。</span><span class="swdz">依全書通例正。</span><span class="ywyy">從木。盧聲。</span><span class="swdz">落胡切。五部。</span><span class="ywyy">伊尹曰：果之美者。箕山之東。靑鳧之所。有甘櫨焉。夏<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20169.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠅩</span>也。</span><span class="swdz">語見呂覽本味篇。鳧作㠀。不言夏<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20169.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠅩</span>。高誘曰：箕山，在潁川陽城之西。靑㠀，崑崙山之東。二處皆有甘櫨之果。上林賦：盧橘夏<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20169.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠅩</span>。應劭曰：伊尹書云：果之美者，箕山之東，靑鳥之所，有盧橘夏<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20169.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠅩</span>。史、漢注作靑馬。依文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨕖</span>作靑鳥爲長，葢卽山海經之三靑鳥。疑㠀鳧皆鳥之誤也。漢志：道家者流有伊尹五十一篇，小說家者流有伊尹說二十七篇。許葷下、秏下、鮞下及此皆取諸伊尹書。相如用盧橘夏<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20169.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠅩</span>，太沖猶譏其不實。後人以給客橙、枇杷等當之，繆甚。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰宅櫨木，出弘農山也。</span><span class="swdz">鄭注周禮說染艸之屬有橐蘆，未知是不。</span></p>                          </div>
