                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>黥</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qíng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qíng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>古代在人脸上刺字并涂墨之刑，后亦施于士兵以防逃跑：～首。</li><li>在人体上刺文字或图案并涂上颜色。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to tattoo; to brand the face of criminals</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> brandmarken; mit einem Brandzeichen versehen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tatouer un criminel sur le visage</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>黥</strong></p><p>剠 <span class="dicpy">qíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从黑，京声。刻其面以墨窒之。字亦作<span class="yhcs">“剠”</span>。本义:古代一种肉刑，墨刑的异称)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 刺字 <span class="encs">[tattoo;punishment of branding a criminal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黥，墨刑在面也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">放黥者马竿人面也。——<span class="smcs">《书·刑德》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:黥首刖足(刻额染墨，截断双脚);黥布(英布，汉高祖的大将、功臣。因犯罪被黥面);黥首(汉代刑法之一。其法在罪人额上刻字染墨);黥面(在脸上刺字涂墨);黥徒(受黥刑的犯人。引申泛指囚徒，罪犯)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 镌刻 <span class="encs">[carve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">声明走百灵，结构黥幽谷。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏源</span><span class="smcs">《白岳东崖》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>黥</strong> <span class="dicpy">qíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>受过黥刑的人 <span class="encs">[tattooed person]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>失其政，天下乘时，黥髡盗贼。——<span class="smcs">《新五代史》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>黥</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄑㄧㄥˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>qíng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3740.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代的一种刑罚，在犯人脸上刺字涂墨。后亦用于奴婢、士兵，以防其逃跑。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷六五·孙子吴起传》：「膑至，庞涓恐其贤于己，疾之，则以法刑断其两足而<b>黥</b>之，欲隐勿见。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《南史·卷五一·梁宗室传上·临川靖惠王宏传》：「蓄奴僮数百，皆<b>黥</b>其面。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
