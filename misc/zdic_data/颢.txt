                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>颢</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">顥</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄠˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>白的样子：～～。～气（洁白清新之气）。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/昊">昊</a>&rdquo;，昊天。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> luminous; white, hoary</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hell, klar</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> lumineux,nom de lieu)​,poêle</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">颢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>颢</strong></p>
<p>顥 <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从景，从页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>)，头。 <span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span><span class="smcs">《说文解字注》</span>:&ldquo;景者，日光也。日光白，从景页，言白首也。&rdquo;本义:头白的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。因指白头人。引申为白色 <span class="encs">[whitish;white]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颢，白貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天白颢颢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;大招》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">洪</span>注:&ldquo;颢，白貌。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:颢露(白露);颢魄(洁白的月色);颢气(清新洁白之气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 浩大 <span class="encs">[vast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颢，大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;叙传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:颢颢(浩大，博大的样子);颢然(盛大的样子);颢汗(广阔浩大的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 光明 <span class="encs">[bright]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:颢颢(光明，光亮的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>颢</strong></p>
<p>顥 <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;昊&rdquo;。本指西天，泛指天空 <span class="encs">[sky]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">超忽荒，&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;叙传上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西方曰颢天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;有始》</span>。注:&ldquo;金色白。故曰颢天。又为昊。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肇自颢穹生民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:颢天(西天，西方的天，同昊天);颢穹(指天。也作昊穹);颢苍(指天)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">顥</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“颢”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/顥">顥</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九">卷九</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九&amp;swbs=頁">頁</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9865.svg">                          <p>白皃。从頁从景。《楚詞》曰：&ldquo;天白顥顥。&rdquo;南山四顥，白首人也。胡老切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：景，日月之光明，白也。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9865_7633.svg" /><span class="swbf">(顥)</span><span class="ywyy">白皃。</span><span class="swdz">漢郊祀歌曰：西顥沆碭。西都賦曰：鮮顥氣之淸英。顥與㚖音義略同。</span><span class="ywyy">从景<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2944B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩑋</span>。</span><span class="swdz">景者，日光也。日光白，从景<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2944B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩑋</span>，言白首也。按上文當云白，首皃。李善注文選引聲類，顥，白首皃，聲類葢本許書。今許書乃爲淺人刪首字耳。郊祀歌、西都賦及楚辭則皆引伸假借也。胡老切，二部。</span><span class="ywyy">楚詞曰：</span><span class="swdz">詞當作辭。許書皆作楚詞。</span><span class="ywyy">天白顥顥。</span><span class="swdz">見大招。王逸曰：顥顥，光貌。按此當廁白首人也之下。寫者亂之耳。土部堋下引左傳，朝而堋，在前引虞書，堋淫于家，在後。可證許偁古之例。</span><span class="ywyy">南山四顥，</span><span class="swdz">南四八目，廣韵作商。皇甫士，安高士傳曰：四晧皆河內軹人。或在汲。一曰東園公；二曰角里先生；三曰綺里季；四曰夏黃公。秦始皇時退入藍田山。作歌，乃共入商雒。隱地肺山，漢高徵之不至，深自匿終南山，不能屈己。按曰：藍田山，曰：商雒地肺山，曰：終南山。東西相接八百里。實一山也。詩傳曰：終南，周之名山中南也。左傳作中南。史，漢謂之南山。楊雄解嘲曰：四晧采榮於南山。說文作南山不誤。張良傳注商山四晧，宋時浙本作南山。</span><span class="ywyy">顥，</span><span class="swdz">此字今補。</span><span class="ywyy">白首人也。</span><span class="swdz">以是爲白首之證。他書作四晧者，通假字也。</span></p>                          </div>
