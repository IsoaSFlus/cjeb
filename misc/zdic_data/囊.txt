						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>囊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">náng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/náng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>口袋：药～。探～取物。～空如洗。～括。</li><li>〔～生〕藏语，中国西藏农奴主家的奴隶。亦称“朗生”。</li><li>像口袋的东西：胆～。胶～。～肿。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>囊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>〔～膪〕猪的乳部肥而松软的肉。</li><li>〔～揣〕ａ．虚弱，懦弱；ｂ．同“囊膪”。</li></ol>

class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bag;pocket]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">囊，橐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">括囊。——<span class="diczx1">所载不过囊衣。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王吉传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“有底曰囊，无底曰橐。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">药囊提</span><span class="diczx2">轲</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先生如其指，内狼于囊。——</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">置书囊中。——</span><span
