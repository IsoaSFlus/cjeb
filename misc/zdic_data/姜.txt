                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>姜</strong></p>
<p><strong>（➊<span class="diczx3">薑</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄤˉ</span> <span class="dicfq">《廣韻》居良切，平陽見。陽部。</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>多年生草本植物，地下茎黄色，味辣，可供调味用，亦可入药：生～。～汤。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">姜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>姜</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，羊声。本义:水名。姜水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Jiang River]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">岐水</span>经 <span class="diczx2">姜氏城</span>为 <span class="diczx2">姜水</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水经注&middot;渭水》</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">黄帝</span>以 <span class="diczx2">姬</span>水成， <span class="diczx2">炎帝</span>以 <span class="diczx2">姜水</span>成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">姜，</span><span class="diczx2">神农</span>居 <span class="diczx2">姜水</span>，以为姓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:姜太公钓鱼(姜子牙世称姜太公，即吕尚。曾辅佐周文王、周武王灭商。后被封为齐侯。传说姜子牙在渭水边钓鱼的时候，钓绳端的直钓不设饵。歇后用法，愿者上钩，指事情出于自愿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[形]</span>∶通&ldquo;咧&rdquo;。强 <span class="encs">[strong]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:姜姜(逞强争斗的样子。)姜:通&ldquo;咧&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>姜</strong></p>
<p>薑 <span class="dicpy">jiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 生姜 <span class="encs">[ginger]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">楠梓姜桂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一种姜属多年生草本植物( Zingiber officinate )，作一年生栽培。须根不发达，根茎肥大，呈不规则块状，灰白或黄色，有辛辣味，可作蔬菜、调料，亦入药</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不撤姜食，不多食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/59DC.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>居良切，音疆。神農居姜水，以爲姓，其後爲齊甫，申呂，紀許，向芮。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/59DC_1241.svg" /><span class="swbf">(姜)</span><span class="ywyy">神農凥姜水，因㠯爲姓。</span><span class="swdz">晉語：司空季子曰：昔少典娶于有蟜氏，生黃帝、炎帝。黃帝以姬水成，炎帝以姜水成。成而異德，故黃帝爲姬，炎帝爲姜。韋注：成，謂所生長以成功也。渭水篇注曰：岐水又東逕姜氏城南爲姜水。引帝王世紀炎帝神農氏姜姓，母女登遊華陽，感神而生炎帝，長於姜水是其地。按姜姬字，葢後所製。</span><span class="ywyy">从女羊聲。</span><span class="swdz">居良切。十部。</span></p>                          </div>
