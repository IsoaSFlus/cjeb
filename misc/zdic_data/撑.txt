						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>撑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄥˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>支着，支持：～竿跳。支～。～持（勉强支持）。～腰（喻给予有力支持）。～拒。～门面。</li>
	<li>用篙使船前进：～船。</li>
	<li>使张开：～伞。～口袋。</li>
	<li>使保持张开状态的器物：～子。～圈（<span class="dicpy">quān </span>）。</li>
	<li>饱胀到容不下的程度：吃～着了。口袋～破了。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">撑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>撑</strong></p>
<p>橕 <span class="dicpy">chēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，掌声。本义:支撑，支持)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[support from under;sustain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天津流水波赤血，白骨相撑如乱麻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《扶风豪士歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:用两手撑着下巴;撑扶(支撑扶持);撑天拄地(喻顶天立地)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用篙抵住河底使船行进 <span class="encs">[push or move with a pole]</span>。如:撑突(驾船突进);撑驾(点篙行船);撑驾夫(船父)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 维持，保持 <span class="encs">[maintain;keep up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他死活也得把这个门面撑着</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两场球下来，他恐怕撑不住了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:撑场面;撑持</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 抢 <span class="encs">[snatch;grab]</span>。如:撑窝儿(抢地盘)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 积攒;购买 <span class="encs">[save(or collect) bit by bit;buy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我儿花衣裳、头面，多末勿算多，撑得来也勿容易。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《海上孽梅花》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 顶嘴，争辩 <span class="encs">[talk back;argue]</span>。如:撑对(对抗);撑嘴(使人张口说不出);撑硬船儿(硬碰硬，不肯迁就)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 张开 <span class="encs">[open;unfurl]</span>。如:撑舌(张舌不下);撑目(睁大眼睛)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>撑</strong> <span class="dicpy">chēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 过度饱满，充填使饱满 <span class="encs">[fill to the point of bursting]</span>。如:有点撑</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 美丽 <span class="encs">[beautiful]</span>。如:撑达(漂亮;出色)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>撑</strong> <span class="dicpy">chēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="encs">[机]</span>∶撑臂 <span class="encs">[brace]</span>。如:角撑。也指支拄物。如:给草堆打个撑</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6491.svg">						  <p>俗撐字。【說文】樘字註 徐鉉曰：今俗別作撐，非。</p>
<p>（撐）【唐韻】丑庚切【集韻】抽庚切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音瞠。與樘同。【說文】樘衺柱也。【廣韻】撥也。【唐書&middot;朱滔傳】骸撐不掩。【韓愈&middot;月蝕詩】赤龍黑烏燒口熱，翎鬣倒側相搪撐。</p>
<p>又【集韻】中庚切。義同。</p>
<p>又叶抽良切，音倀。【司馬相如&middot;長門賦】刻木蘭以爲榱兮，飾文杏以爲梁。羅丰茸之遊樹兮，離樓梧而相撐。【通雅】䟫卽䠆。一作牚。今作撐。俗呼雌爭反。【正字通】橕撐牚䟫，音義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。</p>
