                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>吹</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chuī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨㄟˉ</span></p><ol><li>合拢嘴唇用力出气：～打。～灯（ａ．把灯火吹灭；ｂ．喻人死亡；ｃ．喻失败、垮台）。～毛求疵。～鼓手（ａ．办婚、丧事时吹奏鼓乐的人；ｂ．胡乱吹捧和宣扬别人的人）。～灰之力。</li><li>说大话：～牛。～嘘。</li><li>类似吹的动作：～拂。风～草动。</li><li>（事情）失败：～台。</li><li>消息流传，鼓动宣传：鼓～。</li></ol>

class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[blow;puff]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吹，嘘也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生物之以息相吹也。——<span class="encs">[blow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夜阑卧听风吹雨，铁马冰河入梦来。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《十一月四日风雨大作》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> class="smcs">《韩非子·内储说上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:吹手(吹鼓手);吹打(吹打乐器。指演奏乐曲);吹唇(吹口哨)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 关系破裂;失败 <span class="encs">[break up]</span>。如:他们俩吹了;原来的计划吹了</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 说大话，自夸 <span class="encs">[boast]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫言非吹也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span
