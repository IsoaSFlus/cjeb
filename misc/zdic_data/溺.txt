<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>溺</strong> <span class="dicpy">n&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 没入水中 <span class="encs">[drown]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嫂溺，则援之以手乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;离娄上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">溺死者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">河中溺死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人马烧溺死者甚众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:溺女(将刚生下的女婴投入水中淹死);沉溺(陷入不良的境地);溺没(沉没);溺溺(沉没);溺毙(淹死)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 沉湎，无节制 <span class="encs">[indulge;give over to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">困于所溺。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《伶官传&middot;序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:溺信(沉迷);溺志(心志沉湎于某种事情);溺情(思想感情沉湎于某个方面);溺意(心志沉湎于某个方面);溺心(潜沉心志);溺惑(沉迷;迷信)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 陷于困境 <span class="encs">[be stick in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下溺，援之以道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;离娄上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 失职 <span class="encs">[neglect]</span>。如溺职</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 假借为&ldquo;弱&rdquo;。水浅，意谓水弱不能胜舟 <span class="encs">[shallow]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ni&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
