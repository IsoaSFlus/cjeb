                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">艮</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>艮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>八卦之一，代表山。</li>	<li>古代指时辰：&ldquo;若依然葬书，多用乾、～二时，并是近半夜，此即文与礼违。&rdquo;</li>	<li>指东北方：～维。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> seventh of eight diagrams</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Viertes der Acht Trigramme im Bagua des I Ging, "Aufrichtigkeit&quot</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> opiniâtre,dur,183e radical</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">艮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>艮</strong> <span class="dicpy">gěn</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指食物不易咬动或嚼烂 <span class="encs">[(of food) tough]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:花生米艮了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 〈方〉∶<span class="encs">[人]</span>坦率直言 <span class="encs">[blunt]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这个人真艮</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 〈方〉∶言语率直无曲折 <span class="encs">[straightforward]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他说的话太艮</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[衣服]</span> 朴素 <span class="encs">[simple]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他穿的衣特艮</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 不松脆 <span class="encs">[stiff]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:艮萝卜</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>艮</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 八卦之一，代表山 <span class="encs">[mountain，a diagram of the Eight Diagrams]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:艮岳(山名。在今河南开封城内东北隅;宋徽宗政和年间在汴京东北隅堆土为山，广集天下奇花异石、珍禽怪兽、佳果文竹于此处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 方位名。东北方 <span class="encs">[north east]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:艮方(东北方);艮岑(位于东北方的高山);艮背(东北隅。背，通&ldquo;北&rdquo;。北方);艮域(东北地区)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>艮</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 止;静止 <span class="encs">[still;stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泉可艮，九天之上，九天之下，何所不艮?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">恽敬</span><span class="smcs">《艮泉图咏记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 坚固;坚硬 <span class="encs">[solid;hard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艮，坚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 艰难 <span class="encs">[difficulty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">象艮有守。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;守》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">gěn</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
