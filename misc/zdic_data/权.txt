                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>权</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">權</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄩㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>职责范围内支配和指挥的力量：政～。～力。～威。～贵。～柄。～势。生杀予夺之～。</li>
	<li>有利的形势：主动～。</li>
	<li>变通，不依常规：～变。～谋（随机应变的计谋）。～术。智必知～。</li>
	<li>暂且，姑且：～且。</li>
	<li>秤锤：～衡。</li>
	<li>衡量，估计：～其轻重。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> power, right, authority</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Amtsbefugnis  (S)​,Recht  (S, Rechtsw)​,genau, richtig  (Adj)​,Quan  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> droit,pouvoir,peser,évaluer,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">权</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>权</strong></p>
<p>権 <span class="dicpy">qu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，雚（ <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span>）声。本义:黄花木)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a kind of tree]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">权，黄华木也。从木，雚声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">权，黄英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释木》</span>。又如:权舆(草木萌发的状态)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于嗟乎，不承权舆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;权舆》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于时冰泮发蛰，百草权舆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;诰志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 秤。测定物体重量的器具 <span class="encs">[scale]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">权，称也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;尧曰》</span>集解</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">锤，谓之权。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">权者，铢两斤钧石也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;律历志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谨权量，审法度。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;尧曰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为之权衡以称之，则并与权衡而窃之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;胠箧》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谨权量、审法度、修废官，四方之政行焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 亦称秤锤</p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[中春之月]</span>正权概。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 权柄，权力 <span class="encs">[reins of power]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大夫执国权。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">权重也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利害之权。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皇上无权。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">真无权矣。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:权牟人主(权力与君王相等);权珰(擅权的宦官);权宠(权势与宠幸);权臣(握有权势而专横的臣子);掌权;专权(独揽大权);兵权</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 权宜、变通 <span class="encs">[expedient]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男女授受不亲，礼也;嫂溺授之以手者，权也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;离娄上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:权数(应变的机智);权巧(权宜善巧);权正(权变与常道);权制(权宜之制);权计(权宜之计);权书(以权宜之策草拟国书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 谋略，计谋 <span class="encs">[plan]</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              權·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=22" target="_blank">22</a>　·部外筆画：18</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6B0A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】巨員切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】逵員切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音拳。【玉篇】稱錘也。【前漢&middot;律歷志】孔子陳後王之法，曰謹權量。量多少者不失圭撮，權輕重者不失黍絫。</p>
<p>又經權。【易&middot;繫辭】巽以行權。【註】權，反經而合道者也。</p>
<p>又平也。【禮&middot;王制】原父子之情，立君臣之義以權之。</p>
<p>又權謀。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】中權後勁。【杜註】中軍制謀，精兵爲殿。</p>
<p>又權柄。【莊子&middot;天運篇】親權者不能與人柄。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】權輿，始也。【詩&middot;秦風】于嗟乎，不承權輿。</p>
<p>又攝官曰權。【鼠璞】權字唐始用之。韓愈權知國子博士，三歲爲眞。</p>
<p>又國名。亦姓。【左傳&middot;莊十八年】楚武王克權。【韻會】楚鬭緡尹權，後因爲氏。</p>
<p>又與爟通。烽火也。【前漢&middot;郊祀志】上宿郊見通權火。</p>
<p>又與顴通，兩頰也。【前漢&middot;高帝紀&middot;隆準註】頰權，準也。【曹植&middot;洛神賦】靨輔承權。</p>
<p>又【說文】權，黃華木也。【爾雅&middot;釋草】權，黃華。【六書故云】以草釋木，似誤。○按《爾雅》木槿，木也，列之于草。殆未可泥。</p>
<p>又【集韻】古玩切，音貫。木叢生也。</p>
