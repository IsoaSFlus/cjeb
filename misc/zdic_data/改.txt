                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>改</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄞˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>变更，更换：～变。更（<span class="dicpy">gēng </span>）～。～革。～造。～善。～弦更张。朝令夕～。～编。～写。纂～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> change, alter; improve, remodel</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ändern, korrigieren  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> changer,transformer,modifier,retoucher,corriger</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">改</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>改</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，左边是&ldquo;己&rdquo;，象一个跪着的小孩子;右边是&ldquo;攴&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，象以手持杖或执鞭。表示教子改过归正之意。本义: 改变)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">改，更也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敝予又改为兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;缁文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盖改葬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;庄公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">回</span>也不改其乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">改取一个挟之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;更也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">改居则请退可也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士相见礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">执政未改。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前图未改。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;怀沙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少小离家老大回，乡音无改鬓毛衰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贺知章</span><span class="smcs">《回乡偶书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">盘庚</span>不为怨者故改其度，度义而后动，是而不见可悔故也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:改容加饰(改变神气而加以奖饰);改适(改嫁);改教(改任执教);改律(改变法规);改视(改变看法;另眼相看);改节(改变节操)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 改正;纠正 <span class="encs">[correct]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三人行必有我师焉，择其善者而从之，其不善者而改之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;述而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">始知为人情所患，有自改意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;自新》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:改励(改过自勉);改悟(醒悟改过);改悛(改悔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 修改;修订 <span class="encs">[revise]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:衣服太大，往小里改一改;改作文;改文章;改纂(改写);改笔(指修改后的文字)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>改</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 重新;再 <span class="encs">[again;anew]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缁衣之好兮，敝予又改造兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风 &middot;缁衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:改卜(重新占卜;另行选择);改口沓舌(重新转动舌头，改变原来的说法);改作(重制;另制);改析(重新划分);改撰(重新撰写)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另行;改任 <span class="encs">[separately]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后公改两广，太监泣别，赠大珠四枚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣<span class="smcs">《</span>记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
