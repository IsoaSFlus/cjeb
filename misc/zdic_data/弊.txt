						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>弊</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>欺蒙人的坏事：作～。营私舞～。</li>
	<li>害处，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">利</span>&rdquo;相对：～病。～端。～害。～政。利～。兴利除～。</li>
	<li>败，疲困。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">蔽</span>&rdquo;，隐蔽。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> evil, wrong, bad; criminal</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Betrug; Schwindel ,Schaden, Übel, Nachteil</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fraude,abus,mal,désavantage,défaite,un dommage</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弊</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弊</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。字本作&ldquo;獘&rdquo;，从犬，敝声。从犬，表示被狗扑倒或遭狗咬而倒下。讹变为&ldquo;弊&rdquo;。本义:仆，向前倒下)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;毙&rdquo;。仆;向前倒下 <span class="encs">[fall forward]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">獘，?顿仆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射之獘一人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">质明，弊旗，诛后至者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司马》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 倒毙;死亡。同&ldquo;毙&rdquo; <span class="encs">[die]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以弊于鄢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:弊仆(倒毙)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;蔽&rdquo;。遮盖，遮挡 <span class="encs">[cover;shield]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">八曰官计以弊邦治。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡庶民之狱讼以邦成弊之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见知不悖于前，赏罚不弊于后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 判决，决定 <span class="encs">[judge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弊御于诸侯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;范蔡传》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;断也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:弊狱(判罪);弊谋(决策)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>弊</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 弊病，弊害 <span class="encs">[evil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举先王之政，以兴利除弊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆指目(指责)朝政之弊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧唐书&middot;黄巢传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:弊孔(产生弊害的漏洞);弊象(弊病的迹象);弊绝风清(形容社会风气特别好，贪污舞弊等坏事完全灭绝)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 欺诈的行为 <span class="encs">[fraud]</span>。如:作弊;营私舞弊;弊幸(舞弊，侥幸;阴谋)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>弊</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 坏，低劣 <span class="encs">[bad]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变更天下之弊法。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《上皇帝万言书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:弊事(恶事);弊人(卑鄙的人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 衰落，疲惫 <span class="encs">[tired out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今三分天下，</span><span class="diczx2">益州</span>疲弊。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:弊世(衰世);弊民(疲惫之民)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;敝&rdquo;。破旧，破损 <span class="encs">[worn-out]</span></p>
