                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7E6B_5022.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(繫)</span><span class="ywyy">繫<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/260C7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦃇</span>也。一曰惡絮。</span><span class="swdz">一曰猶一名也。繫<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/260C7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦃇</span>讀如谿黎。曡韵字。音轉爲縴<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/260C7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦃇</span>。縴苦堅切。廣韵十二齊、一先皆曰。縴<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/260C7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦃇</span>、惡絮。是也。釋名曰。煮繭曰莫。莫、幕也。貧者著衣可以幕絮也。或謂之牽離。煮熟爛牽引使離散如絮也。按此與煮繭絓頭不同物。編太平御覽者合而一之。誤矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从糸。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AA0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣪠</span>聲。</span><span class="swdz">大徐古詣切。非也。此字之本音見周易釋文。云。直作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AA0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣪠</span>下糸者、音口奚反。集韵。繫牽兮切。引說文繫<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/260C7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦃇</span>今惡絮。陸德明、丁度非不言之憭然也。而六朝以後舍系不用、而叚繫爲系。遂使繫之本義薶蘊終古。至鼎臣奉敕校定此書。亦徑云古詣切。何淺率如是。尙自謂用唐韵。不知唐韵霽韵內之繫、非許書之繫<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/260C7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦃇</span>也。十六部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
