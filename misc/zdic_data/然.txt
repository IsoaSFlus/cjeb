                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">然</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>然</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">r&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>对，是：～否。不～。不以为～。</li>	<li>以为对，答应，信守：～纳（以为对而采纳）。～诺（许诺，信守诺言）。</li>	<li>这样，如此：当～。～后。～则。</li>	<li>表示一种语气（①表决定，犹焉，如&ldquo;寡人愿有言～&rdquo;；②表比拟，犹言一般，如&ldquo;如见其肺肝～&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>用于词尾，表示状态：显～。忽～。飘飘～。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/燃">燃</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> yes, certainly; pledge, promise</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> so, richtig, korrekt (Adj)​,aber, doch, dennoch (Konj)​,Ran (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mais,cependant,juste,correct</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">然</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>然</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。下形，上声。四点是火的变形。声符读 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span>，是狗肉的意思，下面加火以烤狗肉。本义:燃烧)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。&ldquo;然&rdquo;是&ldquo;燃&rdquo;的本字 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然，烧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 俗字作&ldquo;燃&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若火之始然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蒸间容蒸，然者处下焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;弟子职》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜然脂照城外。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;刘馥传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:然海(古代传说中的油海，其水可燃);然脂(点油脂或蜡烛照明);然灰(死灰复燃);然除(烧毁);然火(点火);然炬(点燃火炬);然脂(泛指点燃火炬);然犀(传说点燃犀牛的角可以照见怪物。后以明察事务为然犀);然灯(点灯)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 明白 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫</span><span class="diczx2">燧</span>之取火于日，慈石之引铁，蟹之败漆，葵之乡日，虽有明智，弗能然也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;览冥》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 耀;照耀 <span class="encs">[dazzle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然目之绮，裂鼻之馨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杨衒之</span><span class="smcs">《洛阳伽蓝记&middot;正始寺》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 以为&hellip;对;同意 <span class="encs">[agree]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">广</span>以为然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">愬</span>然之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:然纳(同意采纳);然疑(半信半疑，犹豫不决);然赞(赞同);然然可可(犹唯唯诺诺);然可(同意);然诺(答允，许诺);然许(然信。许诺;信守诺言);然信(然诺，许诺);然可(应允)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 形成 <span class="encs">[form]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">楹之铭曰:毋曰胡残，其祸将然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 宜;合适 <span class="encs">[fit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所谓无不治者，因物之相然也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>然</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">连</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 但是，然而 <span class="encs">[but]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[虎]</span>甚恐，然往来视之，觉无异能者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然不自意能先入关破</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:然且(然而;尚且);然如(然而)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 虽然。表示让步关系 <span class="encs">[although]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:然是(虽然);然虽(虽然)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 于是 <span class="encs">[thereupon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>曰:&ldquo;其男子有死之志，妇人有保 <span class="diczx2">西河</span>之志。吾所伐者不过四五人。&rdquo; <span class="diczx2">灵公</span>曰:&ldquo;善。&rdquo;然不伐 <span class="diczx2">蒲</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 然后;才 <span class="encs">[then]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:然乃(然后);然始(犹然后)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>然</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p>是，对 <span class="encs">[yes]</span> 假借为嘫。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然，应也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子曰:然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;阳货》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不侵为然诺者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;张耳陈馀传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公见其妻曰:&ldquo;此子之内子邪?&rdquo;</span><span class="diczx2">晏子</span>对曰:&ldquo;然，是也。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>然</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">代</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 如此，这样，那样 <span class="encs">[so;like that]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽有槁暴，不复挺者，輮使之然也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">河内</span>凶，则移其民于 <span class="diczx2">河东</span>，移其粟于 <span class="diczx2">河内</span>; <span class="diczx2">河东</span>凶亦然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:理所当然;其实不然</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>然</strong> <span class="dicpy">ran</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">助</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用作形容词或副词的词尾，表示状态，有&ldquo;如&rdquo;的意义</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庞然大物也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">康肃</span>忿然曰。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《卖油翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:突然;斐然;欣然;惠然;贸然;蔚然</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用作句末语气词，表示比拟，有&ldquo;&hellip;的样子&rdquo;之意。常与&ldquo;如&rdquo;、&ldquo;若&rdquo;连用，有&ldquo;如&hellip;一般&rdquo;、&ldquo;像&hellip;一样&rdquo;之意</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人之视己，如见其肺肝然，则何益矣?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用作句末语气词，表示断定或决定，相当于古汉语的&ldquo;焉&rdquo;、&ldquo;也&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">穆公</span>召 <span class="diczx2">县子</span>而问然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>然</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄖㄢˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>r&aacute;n <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5195.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">燒。燃的本字。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;公孫丑上》：「若火之始<b>然</b>，泉之始達。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">贊同。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷八&middot;高祖本紀》：「沛公<b>然</b>其計，從之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《三國演義&middot;第一回》：「劉焉<b>然</b>其說，隨即出榜招募義兵。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對、正確。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「不以為<b>然</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;雍也》：「雍之言<b>然</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">代</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">如此。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;憲問》：「子曰：『何必高宗，古之人皆<b>然</b>。』」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;柳宗元〈桐葉封弟辯〉：「周公曰：『天子不可戲。』乃封小弱弟於唐。吾意不<b>然</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">連</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">但是、可是。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;僖公三十年》：「吾不能早用子，今急而求子，是寡人之過也。<b>然</b>鄭亡，子亦有不利焉。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">雖。</span><span class="gc_yy">《董西廂&middot;卷八》：「師言<b>然</b>善，奈處凡浮，遭此屈辱，不能無恨。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">然後。</span><span class="gc_yy">《隋書&middot;卷七○&middot;李密傳》：「待士馬肥充，<b>然</b>可與人爭利。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">助</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">形容詞或副詞詞尾。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「斐<b>然</b>」、「赫<b>然</b>」、「恍<b>然</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;邶風&middot;終風》：「終風且霾，惠然肯來。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用於句末，表肯定、斷定的語氣。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;憲問》：「羿善射，奡盪舟，俱不得其死<b>然</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;檀弓下》：「穆公召縣子而問<b>然</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示比擬的語助詞。</span><span class="gc_yy">《呂氏春秋&middot;孟秋紀&middot;蕩兵》：「夫兵不可偃也，譬之若水火<b>然</b>，善用之則為福，不能用之則為禍。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">歎</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">唯，表應答。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;陽貨》：「子曰：『<b>然</b>！有是言也。』」</span><span class="gc_yy">《晏子春秋&middot;內篇&middot;雜下》：「公見其妻曰：『此子之內子邪？』晏子對曰：『<b>然</b>！是也。』」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如漢代有然溫。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7136.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/244C9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤓉</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>如延切，音䔳。【說文】燒也。【註】徐鉉曰俗作燃。蓋后人增加。【孟子】若火之始然。【管子&middot;弟子職】蒸閒容蒸，然者處下。【文劉績曰】古者，束薪蒸以爲燭。蒸，細薪也。稍寬其束，使其蒸閒可各容一蒸，以通火氣。</p><p>又使已然者居下，未然者居上，則火易然也。【集韻】通作䕼。</p><p>又【玉篇】許也，如是也。譍言也。【史記&middot;張耳陳餘傳】此固趙國立名義不侵爲然諾者也。</p><p>又【廣韻】語助。【禮&middot;檀弓】歲旱，穆公召縣子而問然。【註】然之言焉也。</p><p>又【廣韻】如也。【詩&middot;邶風】惠然肯來。【禮&middot;檀弓】貿貿然來。</p><p>又承上接下語。【禮&middot;曲禮】然後客坐。</p><p>又【檀弓】然則盍行乎。【文王世子】然而衆知父子之道矣。</p><p>又【禮&middot;祭義】國人稱願，然曰：幸哉有子如此。【註】然猶而也。</p><p>又果然，獸名。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;巾車】然。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27700.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧜀</span>髤飾。【註】然，果然也。◎按埤雅作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2486E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤡮</span>。詳犬部<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2486E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤡮</span>字註。</p><p>又率然，蛇名。【孫子&middot;九地篇】率然者，常山之蛇也。擊其首，則尾至。擊其尾，則首至。擊其中，則首尾俱至。</p><p>又連然，縣名。【前漢&middot;地理志】益州郡連然有鹽官。</p><p>又燕然，山名。【前漢&middot;匈奴傳】至速邪烏燕然山。</p><p>又姓。【左傳】楚然丹。鄭然明。</p><p>又叶而鄰切，音人。【劉向&middot;列女贊】齊女傅母，防女未然，莊姜亦材，卒能脩身。【馬融&middot;東巡頌】散齊卽畢，越翼良辰，棫槱增積，烈火燔然。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
