                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">夸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>夸</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">誇</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kuā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄚˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>说大话，自吹：～口。～张。～耀。～嘴（夸口）。浮～。～～其谈。</li>	<li>用话奖励，赞扬：～赞。～许。</li>	<li>奢侈：&ldquo;贵而不为～&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> extravagant, luxurious; handsome</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> renommieren, prahlen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> exagérer,louer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">夸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>夸</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从大，于声。本义:奢侈)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[luxurious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夸，奢也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贵而不为夸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;仲尼》</span>。注:&ldquo;奢侈也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:夸丽(虚美浮华);夸侈(奢侈，浮华);夸恣(奢侈放纵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;誇&rdquo;。自大;炫耀 <span class="encs">[self-important]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">华言无实曰夸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;谥法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">富有天下而不聘夸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;下贤》</span>。注:&ldquo;诧而自大也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">石崇</span>以奢靡夸人。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:夸奓(自大)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[great]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夸，大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夸者死权。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span>。集解:&ldquo;泰也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妾夸布服，粝食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>有狗盗之子与刖危子戏而相夸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左下》</span></p>
