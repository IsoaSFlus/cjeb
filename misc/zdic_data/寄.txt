                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寄</strong> <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，表示与家室房屋有关，奇声。本义:寄居)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[live away from home;visit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寄，托也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寄公者，何失地之君也。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·丧服》</span>传</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可以寄百里之命。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“寄是暂寄有反之目也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">老妻寄异县，十口隔风雪。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《自京赴奉先县咏怀五百字》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:寄籍(寄居外地而附以户籍的叫寄籍);寄庄(在外地买地设庄，收租)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 委托，托付 <span class="encs">[entrust]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令可以寄政。——<span class="smcs">《国语·齐语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先帝知臣谨慎，故临崩寄臣以大事也。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倚南窗以寄傲。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:寄命(委以重任;生命如寄旅，因此比喻短暂的生命);寄付(委托;托付);寄任(指所委托的重要责任;托付重任)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 依赖;依附 <span class="encs">[depend on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">托根附树身，开花寄树梢。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《有木诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蛮烟瘴雨土卑湿，留我寄命与兹乡。——</span><span class="diczx2">黄景仁</span><span class="smcs">《洞庭行赠大归包川》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:寄命(寄身;托身);寄口(依靠别人生活)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 寄放;寄存 <span class="encs">[deposit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前以一匹锦相寄，今可见还。——<span class="smcs">《南史·江淹传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:寄包(寄存行李;喻指寄宿);寄物(寄存的东西;寄托某件物品作为凭证)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 寄托 <span class="encs">[place (hope，etc.) on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">依南窗以寄傲，审容膝之易安。——陶潜<span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先王寄理于竹帛。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·安危》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:寄怀(寄托情志;用真诚的心意待人);寄意(寄托情意)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 托人递送 <span class="encs">[transmit;send;post;mail]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">女寄言于母。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《洹词·记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:寄声(托人口头传达问候);寄外(妻子寄信或他物给丈夫);寄言(寄语、带信);寄奉(敬词。谓通过邮递奉呈)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>寄</strong> <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>非亲生的——指亲属 <span
