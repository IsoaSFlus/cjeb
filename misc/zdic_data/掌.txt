						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>掌</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>手心，脚心：～心。手～。脚～。鼓～。易如反～。</li><li>用巴掌打：～嘴。</li><li>把握，主持，主管：～管。～权。～勺。～柜。</li><li>鞋底或牲口蹄子底下的东西：鞋～。马～儿。</li><li>〔～故〕关于历史人物、典章制度等的故事或传说。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>掌</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 本义:手掌，手心 <span class="encs">[palm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌，手中也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">指其掌。——<span class="smcs">《论语·八佾》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">易于反掌，安于</span><span class="diczx2">泰山</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《谏吴王书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:掌上(手掌之上。极言容易);掌上观纹(比喻极其容易，毫不费力);掌文(手掌纹路所呈现的近乎文字的形状)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 脚的底部 <span class="encs">[sole]</span>。如:脚掌</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 四足动物带爪的脚 <span class="encs">[bottom of certain animals' feet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">熊掌，亦我所欲也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 脚 <span class="encs">[foot]</span>。如:鸭掌</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 掌状物 <span class="encs">[paw-like object]</span>。如:仙人掌</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 钉在马、驴、骡等蹄子底下的蹄铁 <span class="encs">[horseshoe]</span>。如:这匹马该钉掌了</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又指钉或缝在鞋底后部的补钉 <span class="encs">[shoe sole]</span>。如:鞋掌</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>掌</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用手掌打 <span class="encs">[slap;strike with the palm of the hand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹶松析，掌蒺藜。——</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《羽猎赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:掌嘴</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 主管，负责 <span class="encs">[be in charge of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">舜</span>使益掌火， <span class="diczx2">益</span>烈山泽而焚之，禽兽逃匿。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:掌家(管家);掌土(指掌管工部事务。因古代工部负责土木建造和水利工程等，故称);掌文(掌管文翰);掌计(掌握考核);掌节(掌握节令);掌选(主持选拔举荐);掌礼(主持礼仪);掌灶(主持烹调)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 支撑 <span class="encs">[support]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">只觉头重身轻，满眼金星乱迸，实实掌不住。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:掌不住(支撑不住;忍不住)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 忍住 <span class="encs">[endure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">连</span><span class="diczx2">贾珍</span>也掌不住笑了。——<span
