                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亦</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>亦</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>副词，也，表示同样、也是：～无不可。～步～趋。</li>
	<li>又：&ldquo;先君何罪？其嗣～何罪？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>不过，只是：&ldquo;王～不好土也，何患无士？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>表示加强或委婉的语气：&ldquo;呜呼，～盛矣哉！&rdquo;&ldquo;学而时习之，不～说乎？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> also, too; likewise</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> außerdem, auch, ebenso ,und vice versa, das Gegenteil</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> aussi,également</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亦</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(指事。甲骨文字形，在&ldquo;大&rdquo;(人)旁加两点，指示两腋所在。&ldquo;腋&rdquo;的本字。本义:人腋窝) 同本义 <span class="encs">[armpit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦，人之臂亦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字俗作腋</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胳谓之腋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腋在肘后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《埤苍》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亦</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 也;也是 <span class="encs">[also]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦免冠徒跣，以头抢地尔。(也不过是摘了帽子，光着脚，把头往地上撞罢了。抢，撞。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦盛矣哉。(也真是盛大隆重的事啊!)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦以明死生之大。(也就是为了用它表明死生的重大意义。以，用，后面省略了宾语&ldquo;之&rdquo;)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攻一时，敌退，</span><span class="diczx2">三保</span>亦自喜。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亦许(也许);亦然(也是这样)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先君何罪?其嗣亦何罪?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亦且(又，而且);亦复(又)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 也&hellip;也&hellip;;又&hellip;又&hellip; <span class="encs">[both&hellip;and&hellip;]</span>&mdash;&mdash;连用在形容词、动词或名词前面，强调两种动作、行为、状态、事物彼此并列或互相对待。如:亦真亦假</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 不过;仅仅;只是 <span class="encs">[but;only]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡人之从君而西也，亦晋之妖梦是践。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>亦</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄧˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>y&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5885.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">相當於「也」、「也是」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「人云亦云」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;公冶長》：「左丘明恥之，丘<b>亦</b>恥之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李商隱〈無題〉詩：「相見時難別<b>亦</b>難，東風無力百花殘。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">相當於「又」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;文公七年》：「先君何罪？其嗣<b>亦</b>何罪？」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈羌村〉詩三首之一：「鄰人滿牆頭，感歎<b>亦</b>歔欷。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">只是、不過。</span><span class="gc_yy">《戰國策&middot;齊策四》：「王<b>亦</b>不好士也，何患無士？」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈蒹葭〉詩：「江湖後搖落，<b>亦</b>恐歲蹉跎。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">已經。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;王康琚&middot;反招隱詩》：「昔在太平時，<b>亦</b>有巢居子；今雖盛明世，能無中林士？」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈獨立〉詩：「草露<b>亦</b>多濕，蛛絲<b>亦</b>未收。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">連</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">雖然。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈奉先劉少府新畫山水障歌〉：「畫師<b>亦</b>無數，好手不可遇。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;歐陽修〈玉樓春&middot;蝶飛芳草花飛路〉詞：「紅蓮綠芰<b>亦</b>芳菲，不奈金風兼玉露。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">助</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">位於句首或句中，無義。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;盤庚上》：「予<b>亦</b>拙謀，作乃逸。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;召南&middot;草蟲》：「<b>亦</b>既見止，<b>亦</b>既覯止，我心則降。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如明代有亦孔昭。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4EA6.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20143.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠅃</span>【唐韻】羊益切。【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】夷益切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音睪。總也，又也。</p>

<p>又姓。宋開禧進士亦尚節，明參將亦孔昭。</p>
<p>又同奕。【詩&middot;周頌】亦服爾耕。【箋】亦，大也。【正義】亦奕義通。　亦本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/215D5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡗕</span>，象人左右兩腋形。【說文】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/215D5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡗕</span>與掖同。【詩&middot;衡門序】誘掖其君。【釋文】《石經》作亦。亦，古掖字也，左右肘脅之閒曰掖。後从肉作腋。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亦</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=亦">亦</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4EA6.svg">                          <p>人之臂亦也。从大，象兩亦之形。凡亦之屬皆从亦。羊益切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今別作腋，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4EA6_83.svg" /><span class="swbf">(亦)</span><span class="ywyy">人之臂亦也。</span><span class="swdz">玉篇今作掖。按手部掖者，以手持人臂投地也，一曰臂下也。一曰臂下之語，葢淺人據俗字增之耳。徐鉉等曰：亦今別作腋。按廣韵肘腋作此字。俗用亦爲語䛐，乃別造此。肉部曰：胳，亦下也。胠，亦下也。今禮記深衣袼之高下注云：袼，衣袂當腋之縫。袼、腋乃皆俗字。人臂㒳垂，臂與身之閒則謂之臂亦。臂與身有重曡之意，故引申爲重累之䛐。公羊傳：大火爲大辰，伐爲大辰，北辰亦爲大辰。何注云：亦者，㒳相須之意。按經傳之亦，有上有所蒙者，有上無所蒙者。論語不亦說乎，亦可宗也，亦可以弗畔，亦可以爲成人矣，皆上無所蒙。皇侃曰：亦猶重也。此等皆申重贊美之䛐。亦之言猶大也、甚也。若周頌亦有高廪，亦服爾耕。鄭箋云：亦，大也。是謂亦卽奕奕之叚借也。介部曰：奕，大也。又或叚爲射，或叚爲易。</span><span class="ywyy">从大，象㒳亦之形。</span><span class="swdz">謂左右㒳直，所以象無形之形。羊益切。古音在五部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡亦之屬皆从亦。</span></p>                          </div>
