                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>殷</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>富裕，富足：～实。～阜。～富。</li><li>深厚，恳切：情意甚～。～切。～勤。</li><li>众，多：“士与女，～其盈矣”。</li><li>盛，大：～祭。</li><li>中国朝代名，商代的后期，由盘庚起称“殷”：～墟。</li><li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>殷</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 黑红色：～红。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>殷</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>雷声：“～其雷，在南山之阳”。</li><li>震动：“熊咆龙吟～岩泉”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> many, great; abundant, flourishing</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Yin (Familienname)​  (Eig)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rouge foncé,nombreux,grand,régler,(nom de famille)​,roulement de tonnerre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">殷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>殷</strong> <span class="dicpy">yān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 黑红色<span class="encs">[blackish red]</span>。如:殷妍(红艳);殷殷(殷红的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/殷红">殷红</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>殷</strong> <span class="dicpy">yīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从殷从殳。本义:盛乐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grand music]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">殷，作乐之盛称殷。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，殷者，舞之容，殳者，舞之器。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五年而殷祭。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·文公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">服除而后殷祭。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曾子问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">月半不殷奠。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先王以作乐崇德，殷荐之上帝，以配祖考。——<span
