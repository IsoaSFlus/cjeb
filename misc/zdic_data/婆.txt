						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>婆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄛˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>年老的妇女：老太～。苦口～心。</li>
	<li>丈夫的母亲：公～。～媳。～家。～～。</li>
	<li>称长两辈的亲属妇女：姑～。姨～。外～。</li>
	<li>方言，泛指已婚的青年妇女，亦称妻子：～娘。～姨。</li>
	<li>旧时指从事某些职业的妇女：媒～。收生～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">婆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>婆</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&mdash;&mdash;婆娑</p>
<p>&lt;联绵词&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，波声。本义:舞貌)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 舞 <span class="encs">[dancing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婆娑，舞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 盘旋;停留 <span class="encs">[spiraling]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婆娑虖术艺之场，休息虖篇籍之囿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 分散;扶疏 <span class="encs">[disperse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风鸿洞而不绝兮，优娆娆以婆娑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;王褒&middot;洞箫赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 舒展 <span class="encs">[limber up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">醉时眠石上，肢体自婆娑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚合</span><span class="smcs">《游河阳河岸》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>婆</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 年老的妇人 <span class="encs">[old woman]</span>。如:苦口婆心;婆老(老太婆和老头子的合称);婆儿(年老的妇人);婆儿气(老婆子的脾气与作风)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 旧指从事某些职业的妇女 <span class="encs">[woman in a certain occupation]</span>。如:接生婆;媒婆;婆官(女巫);婆塞(和尚);产婆</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 已婚妇女，妻 <span class="encs">[wife]</span>。如:婆姨;婆娘(女人。含贬意);老婆;家主婆;婆子(妻，老婆);婆老(老婆子和老头子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 丈夫的母亲 <span class="encs">[mother-in-law]</span>。如:公婆;婆婆</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 祖母 <span class="encs">[grandmother]</span>。如:太婆;外婆;伯婆;婆婆(方言。奶奶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 母 <span class="encs">[mother]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阿婆不嫁女，那得孙儿抱?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;佚名&middot;折杨柳枝歌》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古代广西少数民族对老人的称谓 <span class="encs">[old man]</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">老年的妇女。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老婆婆」、「老太婆 」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《广韵&middot;平声&middot;戈韵》：「婆，老母称也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">以往对某些职业妇女的称呼。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「媒婆」、「产婆」、「接生婆」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">称丈夫的母亲。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「婆婆」、「公婆」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史&middot;第三回》：「婆媳两个，都来坐著吃了饭。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">祖母辈。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「外婆」、「姑婆」、「姨婆」。</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5A46.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】薄波切【集韻】蒲波切【韻會】【正韻】蒲禾切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音皤。【說文】奢也。一曰老母稱。方俗稱舅姑曰公婆。</p>
<p>又廣西猺俗，男子老者，一寨呼之曰婆，其老婦則呼之曰公。</p>
<p>又黃婆，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26709.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦜉</span>神也。養生家以<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26709.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦜉</span>能母養餘臓，名曰黃婆。</p>
<p>又孟婆，風神也。【楊愼曰】孟婆，宋汴京勾攔語，謂風也。</p>
<p>又鞞婆，琵琶名，見【搜神記】。</p>
<p>又梵言耆婆，華言長壽天神。梵言貧婆，華言叢林。梵言優婆塞，華言善士。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>見【祖庭事苑】。</p>
<p>又國名。闍婆國，卽瓜哇國，元遣史弼征之，封爲瓜哇國王。</p>
<p>又城名。蓬婆城，在吐蕃。【杜甫詩】已收滴博雲閒戍，更奪蓬婆雪外城。　【說文】作媻。</p>
