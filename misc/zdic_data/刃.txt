                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>刃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>刀的锋利部分：刀～儿。～口。</li>
	<li>刀：利～。白～。</li>
	<li>用刀杀：与人～我，宁自～。手～亲仇。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> edged tool, cutlery, knife edge</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Messer, Schwert, Säbel  (S, Mil)​,Schneide, Klinge, Schärfe  (S)​,erstechen, erdolchen, jdn. mit Schwert , Säbel töten  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tranchant,lame,épée,couteau</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刃</strong></p>
<p>刄 <span class="dicpy">r&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。小篆字形，在刀上加一点，表示刀锋所在。本义:刀口，刀锋)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[knife-edge;blade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刃，刀坚也。象刀有刃之形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">张舜徽</span>注:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">许</span>云象形，而实指事。今俗称刀口。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">锻乃戈矛，砺乃锋刃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;费誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延则若莫邪之长刃，婴之者斩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;议兵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今臣之刀十九年矣，所解数千牛矣，而刀刃若新发于硎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;养生主》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刀刃;迎刃而解</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 刀剑一类利器 <span class="encs">[sword;knife]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铸金而为刃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拔刃奋起，率众袭之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀人以梃与刃，有以异乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白刃交于前，视死若生者，烈士之勇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:策马露刃;刃器(刀剑之类的兵器);利刃在手</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;仞&rdquo;。长度单位 <span class="encs">[ren，an ancient measure of varying length]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浚古千刃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《无极山碑》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刃</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 杀死，弄死 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左右欲刃</span><span class="diczx2">相如</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:手刃对手</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 磨 <span class="encs">[grind]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与物相刃相靡，其行尽如驰，而莫之能止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">闻一多</span>校释:&ldquo;刃，亦犹磨也。&rdquo;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>刃</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;韧&rdquo;。柔软而坚固 <span class="encs">[pliable but strong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">服与耜宜用稚材，尚柔刃也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;山虞》</span></span></p>
