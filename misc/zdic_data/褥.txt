						<p>● <strong>褥</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>睡觉时垫在身体下面的东西，用棉絮、兽皮或电热材料等制成：～子。～单。～疮。电～子。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mattress, cushion; bedding</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Matratze  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> matelas,natte</p></div>

<p>◎ <strong>褥</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从衣，辱声。本义:坐卧的垫具) 同本义 <span class="encs">[cotton-padded mattress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">褥，荐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寝必重褥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《苦斋记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:褥甸(小的褥子，供坐卧);坐褥;褥垫;褥席(坐卧用的垫具或席具);褥子;褥位(铺有锦褥的座位);褥草(一种主要的垫料。如稿杆、草皮等;也指垫料的别名)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集下" target="_blank">申集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集下&amp;bs=衣" target="_blank">衣</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8925.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】而蜀切【韻會】如欲切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音辱。氊褥。【類篇】藉也。【正韻】裀褥也。【釋名】褥，辱也，人所坐褻辱也。【詩&middot;秦風疏】茵者，車上之褥。【後漢&middot;王暢傳】暢常布衣皮褥。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/84D0.svg">                          <p>陳艸復生也。从艸辱聲。一曰蔟也。凡蓐之屬皆从蓐。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26E33.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦸳</span>，籒文蓐从茻。而蜀切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/84D0_5662.svg" /><span class="swbf">(蓐)</span><span class="ywyy">陳艸復生也。从艸辱聲。</span><span class="swdz">而蜀切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰蔟也。</span><span class="swdz">此别一義。艸部曰：蔟，行蠶蓐也。蓐訓陳艸復生，引伸爲薦席之蓐，故蠶蔟亦呼蓐。</span><span class="ywyy">凡蓐之屬皆从蓐。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/84D0_5663.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26E33.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦸳</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文蓐，从茻。</span><span class="swdz">此不與艸部五十三文爲類而別立蓐部者，以有薅字從蓐故也。</span></p>                          </div>
