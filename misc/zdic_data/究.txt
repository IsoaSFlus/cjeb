						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>究</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄡˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>推求，追查：研～。推～。讲～。追～。深～。有案必～。</li>
	<li>极，到底：～竟（ ➊ 到底，如&ldquo;～～想干什么？&rdquo; ➋ 结果，如&ldquo;大家都想知道个～～&rdquo;）。终～。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>究</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，九声。本义:穷;尽) 同本义 <span class="encs">[limit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">究，穷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">靡届靡究。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;荡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">害气将究。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此告民究也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;任地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">靡届靡究。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;荡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">害气将究。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此告民究也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;任地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必须天道，<span class="encs">[如日月]</span>周旋无究。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:究途(走完全部的路程。比喻做事有始有终);究年(终年);究归(终归)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>究</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 谋划;研究;探求 <span class="encs">[plan;study carefully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">究，谋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不舒究之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小弁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爰究爰度。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;皇矣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫可究详。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:究讨(研究探讨);究理(推求事物的原理);究心(费力;用心钻研);究味(研究体会);究明(研究清楚);究索(研究探索)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 系统调查或追查 <span class="encs">[investigate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是究是图。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;棠棣》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 达，贯彻 <span class="encs">[carry out]</span></p>
