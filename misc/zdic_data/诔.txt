                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>诔</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">誄</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lěi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lěi.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄟˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代叙述死者生平，表示哀悼（多用于上对下）：贱不～贵，幼不～长（<span class="dicpy">zhǎng </span>）。</li>
	<li>这类哀悼死者的文章：做～。～文。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> eulogize, praise the dead</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Lobpreisung, Lobrede  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> éloge funèbre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>诔</strong></p>
<p>誄 <span class="dicpy">lěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lěi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从言，耒声。本义:叙述死者生前事迹，表示哀悼，亦即为谥法所本。仅用于上对下)同本义，即今之致悼辞 <span class="encs">[pray for the dead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诔，谥也。从言，耒声。累列生时行迹，读之以作谥者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六曰诔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大祝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幼不诔长。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曾子问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诔者，道死人之志也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;鲁问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔丘</span>卒， <span class="diczx2">公</span>诔之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>君的嬖人死， <span class="diczx2">鲁</span>君为之诔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>诔</strong></p>
<p>誄 <span class="dicpy">lěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lěi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>哀悼死者的文章 <span class="encs">[writings eulogizing a dead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遗之日读诔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诔文(悼词。亦简称&ldquo;诔&rdquo;);诔赞(列叙死者生平事迹，并且赞美其德行的文辞)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A84.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】力軌切【集韻】【韻會】魯水切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音壘。【說文】諡也。【釋名】累也。累列其事而稱之也。【廣韻】壘也。壘述前人之功德也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大祝】作六辭以通上下、親疎、遠近，六曰誄。【註】謂積累生時德行以錫之命，主爲其辭也。【左傳&middot;哀十六年】孔丘卒，公誄之。子贛曰：生不能用，死而誄之，非禮也。【禮&middot;檀弓】魯莊公及宋人戰于乗丘，馬驚，敗績，縣賁父卜國死之。公曰：非其罪也。遂誄之。士之有誄，自此始也。</p>
<p>又【曾子問】賤不誄貴，幼不誄長，禮也。惟天子稱天以誄之，諸侯相誄，非禮也。【註】累舉其平生實行爲誄，而定其諡，以稱之也。</p>
<p>又禱祀用誄。【論語】誄曰：禱爾于上下神祇。【疏】累功德以求福。【註】誄者，哀死而述其行之辭也。【說文】引作讄。　【說文】本作䛶。【舉要】載《嚴發<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>》作䚽，非。䚽字原从彡从乚出頭作。</p>
