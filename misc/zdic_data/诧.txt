						<p>● <strong>诧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">詫</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>惊讶，觉得奇怪：～谔。～异。惊～。～为奇事。</li>
	<li>诳，欺骗：甘言～语（甜言蜜语，诳骗人的话）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> be surprised, be shocked</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> être surpris,être étonné</p></div>

<p>◎ <strong>诧</strong></p>
<p>詫 <span class="dicpy">ch&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，宅声。本义:夸耀)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[boast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狐貉安能诧緼袍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《纵笔》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">詫</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“诧”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/詫">詫</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              詫·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A6B.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>丑亞切，音侘。誑也。【玉篇】誇也。【晉書&middot;宗室傳】甘言詫語，方伯襲之以輕兵。【唐書&middot;史思明傳】思明詫曰：朝義怯，不能成我事。【音義】詫，誑也。【宋史&middot;張去華傳】浙人毎迓朝使，必列步騎以自誇詫。</p>
<p>又通作姹。【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】子虛過詫烏有先生。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】作姹。【師古曰】姹，誇誑之也。</p>
<p>又通作侘。【史記&middot;韓長孺傳】以侘鄙縣。【註】誇也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27A6B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧩫</span>。【韻會】亦作咤。</p>
<p>又【集韻】虛訝切，音罅。吿也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】都故切，音妒。與託同。奠爵也。【書&middot;顧命】王三宿三祭三咤。【釋文】咤，亦作宅。馬云：亦作詫。【說文】作㓃。</p>
