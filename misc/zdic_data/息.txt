                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">息</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>息</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从心，从自，自亦声。自，鼻子。古人以为气是从心里通过鼻子呼吸的。本义:喘气;呼吸)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[breathe;pant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">息，喘也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亦孔之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑柔》</span>。笺:<span class="yhcs">“如仰疾风，不能息也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尚不敢惕息。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“出入气也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则心惕息。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马迁传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“喘息也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">武</span>气绝，半日复息。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·苏武传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">随形势为沟墙，庶息响相闻，易于守御。——<span class="smcs">《明史·王复传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:喘息(急促呼吸);息息(呼吸;气息出入);息窒(窒息，呼吸困难);息响(呼吸的声音);息贲(中医病名。指呼吸急促、气逆上奔的症候)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 叹气 <span class="encs">[sigh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">北山愚公长息。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">喟然大息。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">闵王</span>太息。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“长出气也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:叹息(叹气);息悒(叹息忧闷)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 停止，停息 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">息，安也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">息，休也。——<span class="smcs">《释言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">细人之爱人也以姑息。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹安也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而不得息。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·适威》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“安也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣必致死礼以息楚。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫不敢遑息。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·殷其靁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以自强不息。——<span class="smcs">《易·乾》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维子之故，使我不能息兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·狡童》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昼夜不息。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请息交以绝游。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无时而息。——<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵革既未息，儿童尽东征。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《羌村三首》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span>
