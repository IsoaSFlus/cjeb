						<p>● <strong>碍</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">礙</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>妨害，限阻：妨～。阻～。～事。障～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> obstruct, hinder, block, deter</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> stören, behindern, hindern, hemmen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> entraver,gêner,embarrasser,bloquer,obstruer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">碍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>碍</strong></p>
<p>礙 <span class="dicpy">&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石，疑声。本义:妨碍，阻挡)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[prevent;stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">碍，止也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">限至曰碍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孰能碍之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;力命》</span>。注:&ldquo;止也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">碍诸以礼乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法言&middot;问道》</span>。注:&ldquo;限也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">直视无碍&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咱们别在这里碍手碍脚的&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:碍手碍脚</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妨碍别人做事，使人感到不方便;障碍(挡住道路，使不能顺利通过;也指阻挡物);碍着脸(难为情，怕伤情面)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 遮蔽;掩盖 <span class="encs">[cover;screen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云雾不礙其视。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;黄帝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四角碍白日，七层摩苍穹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《与高适薛据同登慈恩寺》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 牵挂 <span class="encs">[worry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">洞同覆载，而无所碍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;缪称》</span>。注:&ldquo;挂也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那时我身入空门，一身无碍，万缘俱寂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>

<p>（礙）【唐韻】五漑切【集韻】【韻會】牛代切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音硋。或作硋。通作閡。南史引浮屠書作㝵。【說文】止也。</p>
<p>又距也，妨也，阻也，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E4E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨹎</span>也。【揚子&middot;法言】聖人之治天下，礙諸以禮樂。</p>
<p>又【集韻】魚其切，音疑。礙<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/255EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥗫</span>，靑石。</p>
<p>又叶魚記切，音詣。【蘇轍&middot;嶽下詩】山林無不容，疲苶坐自礙，自知俗緣深，畢老收闤闠。闠，求位切。</p>
