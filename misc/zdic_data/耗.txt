                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>耗</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>减损，消费：～损。～费。～资。消～。</li>
	<li>拖延：～时间。</li>
	<li>音信，消息：噩～（人死的消息）。凶～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> consume, use up; waste, squander</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> neu, neue ,verschwenden, verschleudern ,(Zeit)​ vertrödeln, herumtrödeln  (V)​,aufbrauchen, verbrauchen  (V)​,ausgeben, aufwenden  (V)​,verschwenden, verbrauchen  (V)​,Hao  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> consommer,utiliser,gaspiller,mauvaise nouvelle</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">耗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>耗</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耒( <span class="dicpy">lěi</span>)，毛声。本义:一种稻子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 消息;音信 <span class="encs">[news]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">赤岭</span>久无耗，鸿门犹合围。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《即日》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耗音(音耗，音信，消息);耗问(消息;音信);耗息(消息);恶耗，凶耗</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 方言称老鼠为&ldquo;耗子&rdquo;。也简称&ldquo;耗&rdquo; <span class="encs">[mouse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:耗耗(方言。老鼠);耗爆(老鼠偷吃东西的声音)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旧时官府征收钱粮时，以损耗为名，在正额外例有附加部分，称&ldquo;耗&rdquo; <span class="encs">[loss]</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>耗</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为&ldquo;消&rdquo;。亏损;消耗 <span class="encs">[consume;cost]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耗，消也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">永尝敢以耗气也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;达生》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:&ldquo;损也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">改煎金锡则不耗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;函人为量》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耗，减也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亏法以利私，耗国以便家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;孤愤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫美下而耗上，妨义之本也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耗荡(消耗毁坏);耗米(历代从水道转运米粮，每担在正额外另加数斗米，作为沿途耗损之用;对定额所收的正米而言，称之为耗米);耗失(减少，损失);耗力(耗费力气、力量)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 损失，受损失;耗费 <span class="encs">[expend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士卒多耗，无尺寸之功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;李斯列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耗敝(耗费损害);耗蠹(耗费损害);耗燥(枯竭)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 〈方〉∶拖延 <span class="encs">[dawdle;waste time]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:别耗着了;耗时间;耗误(耽误);耗磨(消磨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 铲除 <span class="encs">[root out;uproot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:耗稻(方言。锄去稻田的杂草)</span></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 歉收 <span class="encs">[of bad harvest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五谷皆入，然后制国用。用地小大，视年之丰耗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 土壤瘠薄 <span class="encs">[poor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耗土之人丑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;易本命》</span>。注:&ldquo;疏薄之地。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耗土(瘠薄的土地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 空虚 <span class="encs">[hollow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耗，虚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多而乱曰耗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;脩身》</span>。注:&ldquo;虚竭也。凡物多而易尽曰耗。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 零落 <span class="encs">[withered and fallen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秋行夏令，华;行春令，荣;行冬令，耗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高诱</span>注:&ldquo;耗，零落也。&rdquo;</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8017.svg">						  <p>【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>虛到切，好去聲。【說文】本作秏。稻屬。【呂氏春秋】飯之美者，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>山之禾，南海之耗。</p>
<p>又【博雅】耗，減也。【正韻】虛也。【詩&middot;大雅】耗斁下土。【禮&middot;王制】用地大小，視年之豐耗。【抱朴子&middot;微旨卷】夫隂陽之術，高可以治小疾，次可以免虛耗而已。</p>
<p>又【增韻】敗也。【抱朴子&middot;地眞卷】月建煞耗之神。</p>
<p>又惡也。【董仲舒&middot;賢良策】察天下之息耗。【註】息，生也。耗，虛也。一云息耗，善惡也。</p>
<p>又【月令&middot;廣義】每年正月十六日，俗謂之耗磨日。【張說&middot;耗日飮詩】上月今朝減，流傳耗磨辰。但令不事事，同醉俗中人。</p>
<p>又姓。出【何氏姓苑】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>莫報切，音帽。耗亂，不明。【荀子&middot;修身篇】多而亂曰耗。【通鑑&middot;漢景帝後二年詔】二千石，各修其職，不事官職，耗亂者，丞相以聞。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【正韻】謨交切【韻會】謨袍切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音毛。【集韻】獨貌。【增韻】無也，盡也。【前漢&middot;高惠高后文功臣表】靡有孑遺耗矣。【註】孟康曰：謂無有毛米在者也。師古曰：孟解非也。言無有獨存者，至於耗盡也。</p>
<p>又通作毛。【後漢&middot;馬衍傳】飢者毛食。【佩觿集】河朔謂無曰毛。</p>
