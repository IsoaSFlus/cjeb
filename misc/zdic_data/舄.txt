                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>舄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩㄝˋ</span> <span class="dicfq">《廣韻》七雀切，入藥清。鐸部。</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>鸟名。后作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/䧿">䧿</a>（鵲）&rdquo;。《説文&bull;鳥部》：&ldquo;舄，䧿也。&rdquo;《廣韻&bull;藥韻》引文：&ldquo;舄，古鵲字。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。《廣韻&bull;藥韻》：&ldquo;舄，人生。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>舄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒ〡ˋ</span> <span class="dicfq">《廣韻》思積切，入昔心。鐸部。</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>加木底的鞋。《釋名&bull;釋衣服》：&ldquo;複其下曰舄。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>鞋的通称。《廣雅&bull;釋器》：&ldquo;舄，履也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>柱下石。后作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/磶">磶</a>&rdquo;。《墨子&bull;備穴》：&ldquo;二尺一柱，柱下傅舄。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/潟">潟</a>&rdquo;。盐碱地。《漢書&bull;溝洫志》：&ldquo;民歌之曰：&lsquo;鄴有賢令兮為史公（起），决漳水兮灌鄴旁，終古舄鹵兮生稻粱。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>舄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tuō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨㄛ</span> <span class="dicfq">《集韻》闔各切，入鐸透。鐸部。</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>大貌。《詩&bull;魯頌&bull;閟宫》：&ldquo;松桷有舄，路寢孔碩。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>舄</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 重木底鞋(古时最尊贵的鞋，多为帝王大臣穿) <span class="encs">[clog]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舄，履也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤舄几几。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;狼跋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舄，以木置履下，干腊不畏泥湿也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔豹</span><span class="smcs">《古今注》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屦人掌王及王后之服屦，为赤舄黑舄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:舄履(复底厚履)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指鞋 <span class="encs">[shoes]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">发棺视之，止衣舄而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太平广记》</span></span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">舃</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“舄”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/舃">舃</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下" target="_blank">未集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下&amp;bs=臼" target="_blank">臼</a>】
                              舃·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8203.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>思積切，音昔。【博雅】舃，履也。【釋名】複其下曰舃。舃，腊也。行禮久立地，或泥濕，故複其末下，使乾腊也。【古今注】舃以木置履下，乾腊不畏泥濕也。天子赤舃。【詩&middot;豳風】赤舃几几。【傳】赤舃，人君之盛履也。</p>
<p>又【小雅】赤芾金舃。【註】舃，達履也。【疏】履之最上達者也。舃有三等，赤舃爲上，冕服之舃。下有白舃，黑舃。【左傳&middot;桓二年】帶裳幅舃，衡紞紘綖，昭其度也。【註】舃，複履。【疏】謂其複下也。</p>
<p>又大貌。【詩&middot;魯頌】松桷有舃。【傳】舃，大貌。</p>
<p>又【班固&middot;典引】舃奕乎千載。【註】舃奕，光曜流行貌。</p>
<p>又草名。【爾雅&middot;釋草】馬舃，車前。【疏】馬舃，一名車前，一名當道。【莊子&middot;至樂篇】生於陵屯，則爲陵舃。陵舃得鬱栖，則爲烏足。【註】陵屯，阜也。言物因水成而陸產，生於陵屯，化作車前，改名陵舃也。一名澤舃，隨燥濕變也。</p>
<p>又與磶同。【何晏&middot;景福殿賦】玉舃承跋。【註】舃與磶同。廣雅曰：磶，礩也，言以玉礩承柱之跋也。　亦與潟同。【前漢&middot;溝洫志】終古舃鹵兮生稻粱。【註】師古曰：舃鹵，卽斥鹵也。謂鹹鹵之地也。【王融&middot;策文】舃鹵可腴。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【正韻】七雀切【集韻】七約切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>與鵲同。鳥名。【說文】䧿也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】闥各切，音託。大貌。詩松桷有舃，徐邈讀。</p>
<p>又【韻補】履舃。亦叶音鵲。【陸雲&middot;逸民賦】相彼宇宙，方之委舃，夫豈不休，而好是冲漠。【註】委舃，猶弃屣也。磶原字从舃，不从舄。</p>
