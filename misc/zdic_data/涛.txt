                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>涛</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">濤</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄠˉ</span></p><ol><li>大波浪：波～。浪～。惊～骇浪。</li><li>像波涛的声音：松～阵阵。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> large waves</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Vorname  (S)​,Brandungswellen ,Wellen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grandes vagues,flots</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>涛</strong></p><p>濤 <span class="dicpy">tāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，寿声。本义:大波)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[billow;big wave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涛，大波也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬波涛于碣石。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·班彪传上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何必湘渊与涛濑?——<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水泫沄而涌涛。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张衡·思玄赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烟涛微茫信难求。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惊涛拍岸。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇·赤壁怀古》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怒涛排壑。——</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略·序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:涛水(波涛汹涌的大水);涛雪(波涛激汤，水花如雪);涛雷(波涛汹涌，声大如雷);涛波(大波，波涛)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 像波涛一样的声音 <span class="encs">[soughing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阴壑寒生万树涛。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">王世贞</span><span class="smcs">《沸水岩》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/涛声">涛声</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
