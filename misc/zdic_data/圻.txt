						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>圻</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>地的边长。</li>
	<li>方千里之地：&ldquo;今土数～而郢是城，不亦难乎&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">圻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圻</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，斤声。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>中与&ldquo;垠&rdquo;同字。本读 <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span>。本义:边际)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 畿，京畿。古称天子直辖之地。亦指京城所领的地区 <span class="encs">[capital]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矧惟若畴圻父。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;酒诰》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;谓司马主封畿之事。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且昔天子之地一圻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圻父(古官名。掌封畿内军事);圻甸(天子的领地，天子之地方千里，千里之内谓之&ldquo;圻甸&rdquo;);圻郊(都邑的郊野)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 疆界;地域 <span class="encs">[boundary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万千里曰王圻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;职方》</span>。注:&ldquo;界也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今土数圻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">化协殊裔，风衍遐圻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《辩亡论上》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 曲岸。通&ldquo;碕 <span class="encs">[bent shore]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汤泉发云潭，焦烟起石圻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《苦热行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圻岸屡崩奔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">谢灵运</span><span class="smcs">《入彭蠡湖口》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圻岸(曲岸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圻</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;垠&rdquo;。边际 <span class="encs">[boundary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四达无境，通于无圻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;倏真》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>圻</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄑㄧˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>q&iacute; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3535.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">京畿四周一千里以內的地方。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;畢命》：「申畫郊圻，慎固封守，以康四海。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;孔安國&middot;傳：「京圻安則四海安矣。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《逸周書&middot;卷八&middot;職方解》：「方千里曰王圻，其外方五百里為侯服。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">面積千里之地。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;昭公二十三年》：「今土數圻，而郢是城，不亦難乎？」</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;杜預 &middot;注：「方千里為圻。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
<div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>圻</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄧㄣˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>y&iacute;n</rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">邊界、地界。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;土部》：「圻，垠或從斤。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子&middot;俶真》：「四達無境，通于無圻。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「垠」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中" target="_blank">丑集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中&amp;bs=土" target="_blank">土</a>】
                              圻·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=7" target="_blank">7</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/573B.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>渠希切，音祈。界也，王畿千里爲圻。【左傳&middot;昭二十一年】天子之地一圻。</p>
<p>又圻父司馬，掌封畿之兵甲。【書&middot;酒誥】圻父薄違。【註】薄，廹也，廹逐違命者也。</p>
<p>又與垠通。地埒岸也。【淮南子&middot;俶眞訓】四達無竟，通于無圻。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;典瑞疏】瑑有圻鄂，上起若篆之文也。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">垠</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“圻”字头，请参考“垠”字：)</span>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=土">土</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/57A0.svg">                          <p>地垠也。一曰岸也。从土艮聲。圻，垠或从斤。語斤切〖注〗㙬、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/212B7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡊷</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/213F4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡏴</span>，古文。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/57A0_1028.svg" /><span class="swbf">(垠)</span><span class="ywyy">地<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/212F3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡋳</span>㖾也。</span><span class="swdz">咢字各本無。今補。玄應書卷八引圻，地圻咢也。文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨕖</span>七發注引圻，地圻堮也。堮者，後人增土。咢則許書本然。淺人以咢爲怪，因或改或刪耳。按古者邊畍謂之垠咢。周禮典瑞，輈人，禮記郊特牲，少儀，哀公問五注皆云圻鄂。圻或作沂。張平子西京賦作垠鍔。注引許氏淮南子注曰：垠鍔，端厓也。甘泉賦李注曰：鄂，垠鄂也。按垠亦作圻，或作沂者叚借字。淮南書亦作㙬。玉篇曰：古文也。咢作鄂作鍔者，皆叚借字。或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/214D0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡓐</span>作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21447.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡑇</span>者，異體也。咢者，譁訟也。叚借之，毛詩鄂不韡韡。鄂葢本作咢。毛傳曰：咢猶咢咢然，言外發也。箋云：承華者曰咢，不當作柎。柎，咢足也。毛意本謂花瓣外出者。鄭牋則以詩上句爲華，不謂蒂。故謂咢爲下系於蒂，而上承華瓣者。毛云：咢咢猶今人云齾齾。毛、鄭皆謂其四出之狀。長笛賦注，字林始有从卪之鄂，垠咢字之别體也。俗卪阝混殽，故作鄂不作卾，物之邊畍有齊平者。有高起者，有捷業如鋸齒者，故統<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧦝</span>之曰垠咢。有單言垠，單言咢者。如甘泉賦旣云亾鄂，又曰無垠是也。故許以地垠咢釋垠。廣韵曰：圻，圻堮，又岸也。正本說文。</span><span class="ywyy">从土。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/250E9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥃩</span>聲。</span><span class="swdz">語斤切。十三部。廣韵又語巾切。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰岸也。</span><span class="swdz">岸者，水厓陖而高者也。亦曰垠。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/57A0_1029.svg" /><span class="swbf">(圻)</span><span class="ywyy">垠或从斤。</span><span class="swdz">斤聲也。古斤聲與幾聲合韵冣近。故周禮故書畿爲近，田部曰：以遠近言之則言畿也。鄭曰：畿猶限也。是王畿可作王圻。王圻亦可作王垠也。</span></p>                          </div>
