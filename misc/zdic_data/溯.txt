						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>溯</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sù.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>逆着水流的方向走：～流而上。</li><li>追求根源或回想：回～。追～。上～。追本～源。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> go upstream, go against current; formerly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gegen den Strom ,sich etw. ins Gedächtnis zurückrufen, zurückdenken  (V)​,stromaufwärts, flussaufwärts  (Adj)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">溯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>溯</strong></p><p>泝、遡 <span class="dicpy">sù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，朔( <span class="dicpy">sù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sù.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:端直地逆流而上)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[go against the stream]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">逆流而上曰溯洄。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">溯洄从之，道阻且长。——<span class="smcs">《诗·秦风·蒹葭》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至于夏水</span><span class="diczx2">襄陵</span>，沿溯阻绝。—— <span class="diczx2">郦道元</span><span class="smcs">《水经注·江水》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">实以四日半溯流流行七百里云。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:溯涉(逆流跋涉);溯流(逆着水流方向);溯洄(逆流而上);溯游(顺着河流向下)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 追朔 <span class="encs">[recall]</span>。如:推本溯源;上溯(往上回想);溯回思切(追念过去的心情急切)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 探求 <span class="encs">[try to find]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">由此上溯到一千八百四十年，从那时起，为了反对内外敌人，争取民族独立和人民自由幸福，在历次斗争中牺牲的人民英雄们永垂不朽!——<span class="smcs">《人民英雄永垂不朽》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:溯流穷源(推寻原委);溯通(探求疏通)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 追念思慕 <span class="encs">[think of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">学生虽溯洄思切，自念风尘俗吏，末由再睹仙颜，今何幸于此处相遇。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/溯源">溯源</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
