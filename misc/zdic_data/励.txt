                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>励</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">勵</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>劝勉：～志（勉励意志）。～行（<span class="dicpy">xíng </span>）。奖～。勉～。～精图治。</li><li>姓。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">厉</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">砺</span>”，磨炼，振奋。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> strive; encourage</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ermahnen ,Li  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> encourager,récompenser</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>励</strong></p><p>勵 <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，厉声。本义:勉力，努力)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[encourage;exert oneself;try hard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勵，勉力也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">用勵相我邦家。——<span class="smcs">《书·立政》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庶明励翼。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">励于一言。——<span class="smcs">《鬼谷子·决篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:砺翼(勉力辅佐君主，精勤以行君命);励志(勉励其心志;集中心思于某种事业);励节(勉力志行，崇尚气节)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 振作 <span class="encs">[rouse oneself for vigorous efforts to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">处遂改励。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·自新》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">相早励良规。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">励精更始。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·宣帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:励精(振作精神)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/励磁">励磁</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/励行">励行</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
