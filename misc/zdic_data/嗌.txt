                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>嗌</strong> <span class="dicpy">&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 咽喉塞住 <span class="encs">[choke]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗌，噎也&hellip;</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span> <span class="diczx2">晋</span>或曰嗌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:嗌喉(上吊身亡)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>嗌</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 咽喉 <span class="encs">[throat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咽，又谓之嗌，气所流通，厄要之处也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食之已嗌痛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;北山经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使我嗌疾而腰急。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗌于面尘，身无膏泽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 喻指交通要道 <span class="encs">[vital communication line]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗌者，扼也，扼要之处也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文解字注》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 嗌喔 <span class="encs">[sound of flattering]</span>。如:嗌呕(嗌喔。形容奉承取媚的声音)</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">嗌</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】
                              嗌·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/55CC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>伊昔切，音益。【說文】咽也。【穀梁傳&middot;昭十九年】歠飦粥嗌，不容粒。【註】嗌，喉也。【楚辭&middot;大招】四酎幷孰，不歰嗌只。【史記&middot;倉公傳】飮食下嗌。【老子&middot;道德經】終日號而嗌不嗄。</p>
<p>又【集韻】乙革切，音戹。義同。【莊子&middot;大宗師】屈服者，其嗌言若哇。【釋文】嗌，郭音厄。</p>
<p>又【集韻】乙角切，音渥。笑也。【韓詩外傳】疾笑嗌嗌，威儀固陋。</p>
<p>又【集韻】烏懈切，音隘。【揚子&middot;方言】嗌，噎也。秦晉或曰嗌，又曰噎。【註】皆謂咽痛。</p>
<p>又【集韻】壹計切，音翳。與齸同。江東名咽爲齸。</p>

                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷二">卷二</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷二&amp;swbs=口">口</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/55CC.svg">                          <p>咽也。从口益聲。籒文嗌上象口，下象頸脈理也。伊昔切〖注〗《玉篇》<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21050.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡁐</span>，籒文嗌。又䒣，同苒。草盛貌。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/55CC_871.svg" /><span class="swbf">(嗌)</span><span class="ywyy">咽也。</span><span class="swdz">嗌者，扼也。扼要之處也。咽、嗌雙聲。漢書：昌邑王嗌痛。《爾雅》注云：江東名咽爲嗌。</span><span class="ywyy">从口益聲。</span><span class="swdz">伊昔切。十六部。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/55CC_872.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20373.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠍳</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文嗌，上象口，下象頸脈理也。</span><span class="swdz">此象形字，與亢略同。漢百官公<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠨞</span>表曰：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20373.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠍳</span>作朕虞。劭曰：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20373.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠍳</span>，伯益也。師古日：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20373.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠍳</span>，古益字也。按此假借。籒文嗌爲益。如九歌假借古文番爲播也。趙宋時古文尙書益作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20373.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠍳</span>，此本諸漢表耳。○又按凡言項領頸亢胡者，自外言之。言嚨㗋噲呑咽嗌者，自内言之。故皆从口，自口而入也。</span></p>
