                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">从</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>从</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg"><span class="diczx3">從；</span>⑤⑥⑦旧读z&ograve;ng</strong><strong>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">c&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>跟随：愿～其后。</li>
	<li>依顺：顺～。盲～。～善如流。</li>
	<li>采取，按照：～优。</li>
	<li>从事；参加：～业。～政。投笔～戎。</li>
	<li>由，自：～古至今。～我做起。</li>
	<li>跟随的人：侍～。仆～。</li>
	<li>宗族中次于至亲的亲属：～父（伯父、叔父的通称）。</li>
	<li>次要的：主～。～犯。</li>
	<li>中国魏以后，古代官品（有&ldquo;正品&rdquo;和&ldquo;从品&rdquo;之分，宋代龙图阁大学士为从二品）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

<p>◎ <strong>从</strong></p>
<p>従 <span class="dicpy">c&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象二人相从形。本义:随行，跟随)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">従，随行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客从拜辱于朝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或从王事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">啬人不从。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;夏小正》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从以孙子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;击鼓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禽兽可羁而从之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛论》</span></span></p>
