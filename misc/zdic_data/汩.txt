                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">汩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>汩</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，曰( <span class="dicpy">yu&egrave;</span>)声。本义:治水，疏通)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[regulate rivers and watercourses]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汩，治水也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">决汩九川。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不住汩鸿，师何以尚之?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;天问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汩鸿(治理洪水);汩越(治理)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 发咯咯声 <span class="encs">[gurgle]</span>，发出一种近似液体流动的声音。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:崩云霄雨，沥沥汩汩</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>汩</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 弄乱;扰乱 <span class="encs">[disorder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我闻在昔，</span><span class="diczx2">鲧</span>洪水，汩陈其五行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与汩俱出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;达生》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汩乱(扰乱，混乱);汩湮(惑乱湮灭);汩陈(错乱陈列);汩和(扰乱和气);汩泥(搅浑泥沙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 沉迷 <span class="encs">[indulge;wallow]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:汩溺(沉迷;迷惑);汩丧(沦丧);汩振(沦落;没落)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 淹没;湮灭 <span class="encs">[submerge;flood]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羲和，羲和，汝奚汩没于荒淫之波?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《日出入行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汩灭(形迹、印象等消灭;磨灭);汩漱(淹没冲刷);汩暗(湮没不明)</span></p>
