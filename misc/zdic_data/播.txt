                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">播</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>播</strong> <span class="dicpy">bō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bō.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，番声。本义:撒种)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sow the seeds]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">播，种也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其始播百谷。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风 ·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">播时百谷，——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">周</span>弃能播殖百谷蔬。——<span class="smcs">《国语·郑语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:条播;夏播;播田(种田;种植);播植(播种;种植);播谷(播种谷物)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 散布开去，散布得开而远;传布 <span class="encs">[broadcast;spread]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">播于诸侯。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">道大名播。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:广播;播音;播名(传扬名声)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 分佈;散布 <span class="encs">[be distributed over an area;be dispersed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又北播为九河。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">播余香而莫闻。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒸灵液以播云。——<span class="smcs">《文选·嵇康·琴赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:播糠眯目(撒布糠屑，以迷人目。比喻外物虽小，加在自己身上，会伤害本性);播洒(洒水喷地);播馨(散布芳香)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 逃亡 <span class="encs">[become a fugitive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身播国屯。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·献帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">成公</span>播荡。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“流移失所。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:播越(到处流亡);播奔(奔逃)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 迁徙 <span class="encs">[move]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼凌江而建国，始播迁于吾祖。——</span><span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《哀江南赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:播迁(迁徙，迁移);播徙(流亡迁移)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 背弃，舍弃 <span class="encs">[abandon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">播规矩以背度兮，错权衡而任意。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《思古》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:播弃(抛弃，背弃)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“簸”</span>。摇动;簸扬 <span class="encs">[rock;shake;sway]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">播鼗武入于</span><span
