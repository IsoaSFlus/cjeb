                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>瘤</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>身体组织增殖生成的赘生物，多由刺激或微生物寄生而起：～子。肉～。肿～。根～。～胃（反刍动物的胃的一部分）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瘤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瘤</strong></p>
<p>癅 <span class="dicpy">li&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 瘤子，身体组织增生形成的赘生物，多由刺激或微生物寄生而起。分癌瘤、肉瘤、淋巴瘤、血管瘤、脂瘤和骨瘤等 <span class="encs">[tumor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瘤，肿也。从疒，留声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肉凸曰瘤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瘤，息肉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《声类》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瘤，小肿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瘤牛;瘤赘(体表或筋骨间增生的肉疙瘩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 树木干、根外皮隆起的块状物 <span class="encs">[verruca]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:瘤瘿(树木外皮隆起的块状物)</span></p>
