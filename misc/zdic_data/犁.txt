                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>犁</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>耕地的农具：～杖。～铧。～头。木～。</li>
	<li>用犁耕地：～地。～庭扫闾（喻彻底摧毁敌巢。亦称&ldquo;犁庭扫穴&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> plow</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Pflug (S, Agrar)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> charrue,labourer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">犁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>犁</strong></p>
<p>犂 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从牛，利声。本义:牛耕)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[till]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犂，耕也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作犁。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犁其庭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;匈奴传》</span>。注:&ldquo;耕也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犂牛之子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:&ldquo;或音梨，谓耕犂也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古墓犁为田，松柏摧为薪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《古诗十九首》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:犁耕(用犁耕田;耕田);犁花(犁翻的土块);犁春(春耕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 摧毁，平毁 <span class="encs">[destroy]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:犁穴;犁平(扫荡平定)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 割开，划开。通&ldquo;剺&rdquo; <span class="encs">[cut]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:犁面(用刀割脸);犁舌(入犁舌狱割舌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;黎&rdquo;。及，等到 <span class="encs">[till;when;by the time]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犂二十五年，吾冢上柏大矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;晋世家》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>犁</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 耕地的农具。我国春秋时代，就已用牛拉犁耕田 <span class="encs">[plow;plough]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耕者忘其犁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;陌上桑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵有健妇把锄犁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:犁锄(犁和锄);犁锄之厄(死亡的命运)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>犁</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 黑(在黑的意义上，黧、黎、犁实同一词) <span class="encs">[black]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形容枯槁，面目犁黑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犁旦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;南越列传》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;犁，黑也。天未明尚黑时也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:犁眉驹(良马名。毛黄色，眉黑。犁，通&ldquo;黧&rdquo;);犁黑(黧黑，暗黑色。犁，通&ldquo;犂&rdquo;);犁老(指老人。老人面色黧黑，故称犁，通&ldquo;黧&rdquo;);犁明(犁曙黎明。犂，通&ldquo;黎&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;黎&rdquo;。众多之意 <span class="encs">[many]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今商王受，力行无度，播弃犂老。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;泰誓中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:犁元，犁民(黎民百姓。犂，通&ldquo;黎&rdquo;)</span></p>
