                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>绢</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">絹</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">juàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/juàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩㄢˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 一种薄而坚韧的丝织物：～本。～花。～素。手～儿。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> kind of thick stiff silk</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> locker gewobene Seide</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> étoffe légère de soie,lustrine</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>绢</strong></p><p>絹 <span class="dicpy">juàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/juàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>，表示与线丝有关)，肙( <span class="dicpy">yuān</span>)声。本义:生丝织物。厚而疏者称绢)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carbaso;thin，though silk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绢，缯如麦绢者。从糸，肙声。谓粗厚之丝为之。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">治丝麻，捆布绢，以为民衣。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·辞过》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:绢地(书画之绢底);绢帖(以绢作底的书帖);绢丝(织纺工程的产品。光滑润美，手感柔和，适于制造轻软的高级织物)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 麦青色的丝织物 <span class="encs">[deep-green silk]</span>。如:绢扇(用丝绢制成的扇子);绢素(作为书画用的白绢)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 丝织品的通称 <span class="encs">[silk]</span>。如:绢布(泛指丝麻织物);绢绸(以柞蚕丝织成的平绢);绢头(方言。手帕)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 手绢 <span class="encs">[handkerchief]</span>。如:绢绸;天香绢</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指书画、装璜物件 <span class="encs">[painting and calligraph on silk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">堂上绢素开欲裂，一见犹能动毛发。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span>诗</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>绢</strong></p><p>絹 <span class="dicpy">juàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/juàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>使缠绕或包住;使环绕或网住 <span class="encs">[twine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绢猑蹄。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·马融传》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/绢本">绢本</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/绢花">绢花</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/绢丝">绢丝</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/绢子">绢子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">繒如麥䅌色。</span>色字今補。色譌也、而俗刪之耳。自絹至綟卄三篆皆言繒帛之色。而此色字先之。聲類溷縳絹爲一字。由不考其義之殊也。䅌者、麥莖也。繒色如麥莖靑色也。射雉賦曰。麥漸漸以擢芒。又曰。闚䦓？葉。四月時也。繒色似之曰絹。漢人叚爲？字。<span class="jieshi">从糸。肙聲。</span>吉掾切。十四部。                          </div>
