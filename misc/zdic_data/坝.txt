                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坝</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>坝</strong></p>
<p><strong>（③④<span class="diczx3">垻、壩</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>截住河流的构筑物：拦河～。堤～。</li>
	<li>河工险要处、巩固堤防的构筑物：丁～。</li>
	<li>方言，沙滩，沙洲。</li>
	<li>坝子（多用于地名）：平～（在贵州）。留～（在陕西）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> embankment; dam</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Damm, Deich</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> barrage,retenue d'eau,digue</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坝</strong></p>
<p>壩 <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，霸声。本义:堤坝)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 截河拦水的堤堰。堤坝 <span class="encs">[dam]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其河自西坝至东坝十六里有余。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">单锷</span><span class="smcs">《吴中水利书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丁坝;坝埽;顺坝</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 堤岸 <span class="encs">[dyke;embankment]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:坝田(堤岸旁边的田地);坝地(在山沟里打坝，拦流土而淤成的农田)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 平地 <span class="encs">[flatland;plain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君家水茄白银色，殊胜坝里紫彭亨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">黄庭坚</span><span class="smcs">《谢杨履道》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坝子(中国西南地区称平地或平原);坪坝;晒坝</span></p>
