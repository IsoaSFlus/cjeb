                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>畦</strong> <span class="dicpy">qí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从田，圭声。本义:田畦。一说五十亩为畦)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rectangular pieces of land in a field separated by ridges;fifty acre]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">畦，田五十亩曰畦。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">病于夏畦。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">刘</span>注;<span class="yhcs">“今俗以二十五亩为小畦，五十亩为大畦。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">子贡</span>]</span>见一丈人方将为圃畦。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一畦之间。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 亦用作田地的量词</p><p><span class="diczx1">千畦姜韭。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一两畦菜地。——</span><span class="diczx2">茅盾</span><span class="smcs">《送考》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 田块 <span class="encs">[field]</span>。又如:畦堰(田间蓄水的堤岸);畦棱(田间土垄);畦陌(田间的道路);畦径(田间小路);畦沟(畦与畦之间的小沟)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 格式 <span class="encs">[form]</span>。如:畦町(规格;格式);畦畛(格式;常规);畦径(比喻常规)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>畦</strong> <span class="dicpy">qí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>分畦种植 <span class="encs">[plant in a field]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">畦留夷与揭车兮，杂杜衡与芳芷。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">堂下可以畦，呼童对经始。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《种莴苣》</span>诗</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7566.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】戸圭切【集韻】【韻會】懸圭切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音攜。【說文】田五十畝曰畦。【屈原·離騷】畦留荑與揭車兮，雜杜蘅與芳芷。【註】五十畝爲畦。【莊子·人閒世】彼旦爲無町畦，亦與之爲無町畦。</p><p>又【廣韻】菜畦。【史記·貨殖傳】千畦薑韭。【註】徐廣曰：千畦，二十五畝。駰案，韋昭曰：畦猶壠。【前漢·食貨志】還廬樹桑，菜茹有畦，瓜瓠果蓏。【註】師古曰：畦區也。</p><p>又地名。【史記·封禪書】故作畦畤櫟陽，而祀白帝。【索隱註】漢舊儀云：祭人先於隴西西縣人先山，山上皆有土人，上下有畤，如種韭畦，畦中各有二土封，故云畦畤也。</p><p>又【集韻】涓畦切，音圭。田起堳埒也。</p><p>又【集韻】勻規切，音䔺。</p><p>又【五音集韻】息遺切，音綏。義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。</p><p>
