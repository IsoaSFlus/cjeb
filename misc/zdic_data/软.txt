                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">软</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>软</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">軟</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ruǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ruǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>柔，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">硬</span>&rdquo;相对：柔～。～席。～卧。～缎。～话（温和的话）。～绵绵。</li>
	<li>懦弱：～弱。欺～怕硬。</li>
	<li>容易被感动或动摇：心～。耳～。</li>
	<li>不用强硬的手段进行：～磨（<span class="dicpy">m&oacute;</span>）。～禁。～刀子（喻使人在不知不觉中受到折磨或腐蚀的手段）。</li>
	<li>没有气力：两腿发～。货色～。工夫～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> soft, flexible, pliable; weak</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> flexibel, leise, weich  (Adj)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">软</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>软</strong></p>
<p>軟、輭 <span class="dicpy">ruǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ruǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，欠声。本义:柔软)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。质地不硬，与&ldquo;硬&rdquo;相对。也作&ldquo;輭&rdquo; <span class="encs">[soft;flexible;supple]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">軟，柔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">軟，俗輭字。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">輭，柔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">步步金阶上软舆。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《宫词》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婆子们抱着几个软包。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:软壁(可折叠的屏风);软片(指裱好的书画);软柔柔(非常柔软);软设设(软软的);软性(轻柔的事物。与硬性相对);绵软(柔软;又形容身体无力);松软(松散绵软);软甲(软战。用非金属制作的软质战衣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 柔和;温和 <span class="encs">[mild;gentle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">文辞婉软。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《乞巧文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:软咍咍(软绵绵;软软地);软善(和善);软款(娇柔;柔和;温顺地恳求);软局子(软手段);软软款款(用温柔亲切的话体贴、抚慰);软熟(形容人性格柔和，不违世人);软语温香(形容女性细芳的身体);软语温存(用温和婉转的话相抚慰);软和(柔和);软风(和风);软美(柔和美好;温顺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形容人体柔弱无力 <span class="encs">[weak;feeble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">李园</span>，软弱人也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:软刺答(软答刺。软弱无力的样子);软丢答(轻飘无力);软困(疲乏无力);酸软(身体发酸而无力);酥软(肢体软弱无力);软兀剌(形容无力，乏劲);软懒(乏力慵懒的样子);软乎(形容柔弱无力)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 意志不坚定，容易被感动或动摇 <span class="encs">[easily touched or influenced]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心里早软了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:软壳鸡蛋(比喻性情软弱之人);软揣(软弱，不坚强);软怯怯(软弱，羞怯);软厮禁(不硬挣，不坚强);心软(容易被外界事物感动而生怜悯或同情);手软(不忍下手或下手不狠);软脊脊(比喻没有主见，不坚定);软耳朵(比喻无主见、容易轻信人言的人);软商(用软磨的手段商量)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 不用强硬的手段，只是平和地进行 <span class="encs">[soft]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主公软监诸将在内，水食不便。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三刻拍案惊奇》</span></span></p>
