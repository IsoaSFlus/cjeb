                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">草</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>草</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄠˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>对高等植物中除了树木、庄稼、蔬菜以外的茎干柔软的植物的统称；广义指茎干比较柔软的植物，包括庄稼和蔬菜：青～。野～。茅～。水～。花～。～鞋。～堂（茅草盖的堂屋，旧时文人以此自称山野间的住所，有自谦卑陋的意思）。～原。～坪。～行露宿。～菅人命。</li>
	<li>特指用作燃料、饲料的稻麦之类的茎叶：～料。柴～。稻～。</li>
	<li>粗糙，不细致：～率（<span class="dicpy">shu&agrave;i </span>）。～鄙（粗野朴陋）。～具（粗劣的食物）。</li>
	<li>汉字的一种书体：～书。～字（亦为旧时谦称自己的别名）。章～（草书的一种，笔画保存了一些隶书的笔势，因其最初用于奏章，故名&ldquo;章草&rdquo;）。狂～。～体（❶指汉字草书；❷拼音文字的手写体，有大草、小草之分）。</li>
	<li>打稿子，亦指稿子；引申为初步的，非正式的：～拟。～诏（为皇帝草拟诏书）。</li>
	<li>荒野，原野，引申为在野的、民间的：～野。～莽。～寇。～贼。</li>
	<li>雌性的（用于某些家畜、家禽）：～鸡。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">草</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>草</strong></p>
<p>艸 <span class="dicpy">cǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，早声。小篆艸，象两棵草形，是草的本字。今&ldquo;草&rdquo;字系假借字，原是&ldquo;皂&rdquo;的本字。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;草，草斗，栎( <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span>)实也&rdquo;(栎实:栎树的荚果，即皂角)。借为&ldquo;草木&rdquo;之&ldquo;草&rdquo;以后，则另造&ldquo;皂&rdquo;字来代替。本义:栎实)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 草本植物的总称 <span class="encs">[grass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艸，百卉也。从二屮。会意。经传皆以草为之。汉书多以屮为之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大草不生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;任地》</span>。注:&ldquo;草，秽也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草食者羶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;本味》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草苴比而不芳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;悲回风》</span>。注:&ldquo;生曰草。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苔痕上阶绿，草色入帘青。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茅檐低小，溪上青青草。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《清平乐&middot;村居》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:除草;粮草(军用的粮食和草料);野草遍地;寸草不留;青草;牧草;茅草;草厅(草堂;厅堂);草莽(草丛。比喻无用的东西)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指用作燃料、饲料的干草 <span class="encs">[hay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今又盛寒，马无稿草。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:草库伦(指围起来的草场);草料</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 未开垦过的荒地 <span class="encs">[wasteland]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垦草创邑，辟地生粟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:草甸子(方言。长满野草的低湿地);草洼(低洼积水，野草丛生的地方);草间(民间);草茅危言(百姓对国政的剀切言论。草茅，指在野百姓;百姓论庙堂，恐有危险，故谓危言)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 文书的底稿;初稿 <span class="encs">[draft]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">吴</span>中士人家藏其草。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪迈</span><span class="smcs">《容斋续笔》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:草藁(同草稿);起草;奏草(奏张的草稿);草本(原稿的底本)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 一种书写体 <span class="encs">[script type of calligraphy]</span>。如:章草;狂草;草行(书法中的行书兼草体)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>草</strong> <span class="dicpy">cǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 粗糙;粗劣 <span class="encs">[rough;coarse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令人事无大小皆潦草过了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《朱子类语&middot;训门人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:草略(马虎，疏忽);草具(粗劣;粗劣的饭食);草酌(简便的筵席。多用作设宴请客的谦词);潦草(字不工整;不仔细;不认真)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 匆促，急促 <span class="encs">[hasty]</span>。如:草蹙(匆促)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>草</strong> <span class="dicpy">cǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 割草，除草 <span class="encs">[mow]</span>。如:草薙(芟夷，像除草似的加以杀戮)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 创造;创立 <span class="encs">[create]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草，造也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天造草昧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;屯》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;草，草创物也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:草立(创立);草昧(创始;草创);草律(创制法律)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 草拟;起稿 <span class="encs">[draft]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">萧何</span>草律。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;艺文志》</span>。注:&ldquo;创造之。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草立土德时历制度。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;任敖传》</span>。注:&ldquo;创始也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">召今草檄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南史&middot;蔡景立传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:草立(创立);草制(拟订制书);草诏(草拟诏书);草表(草拟章奏)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>草</strong></p>
<p>騲 <span class="dicpy">cǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>雌马 <span class="encs">[mare]</span>。也泛指母畜。如:草狗;草骡(牝骡)</p>

<p>又【史記&middot;陳丞相世家】惡草具進。【註】草，粗也。</p>
<p>又【篇海】苟<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>曰草草。【春秋&middot;隱四年】公及宋公遇于淸。【註】遇者草次之期，二國各<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>其禮也。【疏】草次，猶造次也。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】勞人草草。【傳】草草，勞心也。</p>

<p>又【韻補】脞五切，徂上聲。【徐幹&middot;齊都賦】焚梗林，燎圃草。</p>

<p>又【說文】自保切，音皁。草斗，櫟實也，一曰橡斗子。【徐鉉曰】今俗以此爲艸木之艸，別作皁字，爲黑色之皁。案櫟實可染白爲黑，故曰草，通用。今俗書或从白从十，或从白从七，皆無意義。</p>
