                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>丐</strong></p><p>匃、匄 <span class="dicpy">gài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 乞求，乞讨 <span class="encs">[beg]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">求愿曰匃。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匃，求也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不强丐。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋或匄夺。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丐沐沐我。——<span class="smcs">《史记·外戚世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丐，乞也。——<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不抽屋，不强丐。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:丐饭;丐物;丐食;丐计(乞求计策);丐施(乞求布施);丐贷(乞贷);丐养(给人收养为义子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 求，请求 <span class="encs">[ask for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">邹</span>君 <span class="diczx2">海滨</span>，以所辑黄花冈烈士事略丐序于予。—— <span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《<黄花冈七十二烈士事略>序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:丐命(乞命。请求饶命);丐祠(请求奉祠);丐退(请求辞职);丐留(请求留任);丐贷(请求借贷)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 给予;施舍，把财物给与人 <span class="encs">[give]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尽取善绘，匄诸宫人。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·广川惠王越传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“遗之也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我匄若。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·西域传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“与也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又出库钱一千万，以丐贫民遭旱不能供税者。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《太原王公墓志铭》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沾丐后人多矣。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·<杜甫传>赞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 免除，宽免 <span class="encs">[remit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诏以州镇十一水旱，丐其田租，开仓振恤。——<span class="smcs">《北史》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>丐</strong> <span class="dicpy">gài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 乞丐，以乞讨为生的人 <span class="encs">[beggar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于戏，丐者，言语如斯，可编为丐论。——</span><span class="diczx2">元结</span><span class="smcs">《丐论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:老丐;丐头;丐人;丐夫;丐者;丐棍(以乞讨为业的无赖);丐首(丐头);丐帮(有组织的乞丐团伙)</p>                           <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5303_560.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(匃)</span><span class="ywyy">气也。</span><span class="swdz">气者、雲气也。用其聲叚借爲气求、气與字。俗以气求爲入聲。以气與爲去聲。匄訓气、亦分二義二音。西域傳。气匄亡所得。此气求之義也。當去聲。又曰。我匄若馬。此气與之義也。當入聲。要皆強爲分別耳。左傳。公子棄疾不強匄。又子產曰。世有盟誓。母或匄奪。皆言气求也。通俗文曰。求願曰匄。則是求之曰气匄。因而與之亦曰气匄也。今人以物與人曰給。其實當用匄字。廣韵古達切。其字俗作丐。與丏不同。廣韵曰。二字同。非是。</span><span class="ywyy">亡人爲匄。逯安說。</span><span class="swdz">此稱逯安說、以說字形會意。逯安亦通人之一也。从亡人者、人有所無必求諸人。故字从亡、从人。古代切。按廣韵古太切。亦古達切。十五部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
