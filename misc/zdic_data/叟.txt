                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">叟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叟</strong> <span class="dicpy">sōu</span></p>
<p>&lt;象&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 叟叟:淘米声 <span class="encs">[sou sou;sound of rice-washing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">释之叟叟，烝之浮浮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;生民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">sǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叟</strong> <span class="dicpy">sǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(会意。本作&ldquo;叜&rdquo;。意谓手拿火把在室内搜索。本义:求)搜索;搜查 <span class="encs">[search]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叜，老也。从又从灾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>注:&ldquo;(叟)即搜之古文。从又持火，屋下索物也。会意。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">合群叟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叟</strong> <span class="dicpy">sǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &ldquo;叟&rdquo;。假借为&ldquo;傁&rdquo;，老的男人。也用作对老年男子的尊称 <span class="encs">[old man]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王曰叟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span>。注:&ldquo;叟，长老之称。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叟何人耶?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;在宥》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">河曲智叟笑而止之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">河曲智叟亡以应。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叟，不远千里而来，亦将有以利吾国乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:老叟;童叟无欺</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 叟族 <span class="encs">[Sou nationality]</span>中国汉至六朝时，对今甘肃东南部、四川西部、云南东部、贵州西部等地区部分少数民族的泛称</p>
