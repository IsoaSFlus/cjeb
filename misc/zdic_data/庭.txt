						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>庭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tíng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tíng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄧㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>堂阶前的院子：～院。～园。～除（“<span class="diczx3">除</span>”，台阶）。</li><li>厅堂：～宇。～闱（父母所属内室，借指父母）。～训（父亲的教诲，亦指家教）。家～。</li><li>审判案件的处所或机构：法～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">廷</span>”，朝廷。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>庭</strong> <span class="dicpy">tíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广，廷声。广（ <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>），就山岩架成的屋。本义:厅堂)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庭，宫中也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是君子之所以骋志意于坛宇宫庭也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·儒效》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">藜棘树于中庭。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·刘向·九叹·思古》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“堂下谓之庭。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:庭落(厅堂);庭庑(堂下四周的廊屋);庭炬(古代庭中照明的火炬);庭阶(厅砌。堂前的台阶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 堂阶前的地坪 <span class="encs">[front courtyard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胡瞻尔庭有县貆兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·魏风·伐檀》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徘徊庭树下。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">眄庭柯以怡颜。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缢于庭树。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">严霜结庭兰。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:庭闱(原指父母居住的地方。借指庭院或家庭);庭帏(庭闱);庭燎(举行大典礼时设在庭院中照明的火炬);庭轩(庭院中的小室);庭芜(庭园中丛生的草);庭阈(泛指庭院)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“廷”</span>。朝廷，君主受朝问政的地方 <span class="encs">[the imperial court]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬于王庭，柔乘五刚也。——<span class="smcs">《易·夬》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">龙辂充庭。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张衡·东京赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“庭，朝廷。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拜送书于庭。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使有禄于国，立义于庭。——<span class="smcs">《说苑·立节》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乞以臣此章与三公九卿贤士大夫议之于庭。——</span><span class="diczx2">陈子昂</span><span class="smcs">《谏政理书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奏事入朝，思睹阙庭，将陈所见。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《十渐不克终疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:庭争(在朝廷上据理诤谏);庭论(在朝廷中当众评议);庭庙(朝廷);庭议(议事于朝廷);庭辩(在朝廷上辩论);庭问(上朝问国事);庭会(朝见天子)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 法庭，司法机关审理案件的处所 <span class="encs">[law court]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 如:庭决(公堂判决);民庭;刑庭</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 官署，政府办公的地方 <span class="encs">[government agency]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span>
