                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">宝</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>宝</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">寳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>玉器，泛指珍贵的东西：～贝。～剑。～物。～藏（<span class="dicpy">z&agrave;ng </span>）。国～。财～。珍～。传（<span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;n</span>）家～。如获至～。～贵。</li>
	<li>帝王的印信，借指帝位：～座。登大～（皇帝登基）。</li>
	<li>敬辞，用于称别人的，～地。～刹（称呼庙字）。～号（称呼别人的店铺）。</li>
	<li>指金属货币：元～。</li>
	<li>赌具的一种：开～。押～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> treasure, jewel; precious, rare</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Juwel, Schatz, Preziosen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bijou,trésor,précieux</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>宝</strong></p>
<p>寶 <span class="dicpy">bǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。甲骨文字形，象房子里有贝和玉，表示家里藏有珍宝，会意。在西周金文里，又加上一个声符&ldquo;缶&rdquo;(古音与&ldquo;宝&rdquo;同)。本义:珍宝)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[treasure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宝，珍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">稼穑维宝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;桑柔》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以作尔宝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;崧高》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宝玉者，封圭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;定公八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以其宝来奔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span>。注:&ldquo;玉也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀其宝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>

<p>又瑞也，符也。【易&middot;繫辭】聖人之大寶曰位。【禮&middot;禮運】天不愛其道，地不愛其寶。【詩&middot;大雅】稼穡惟寶。</p>

<p>又凡錢文曰通寶。</p>
<p>又姓。</p>
<p>又叶博古切，音補。【詩&middot;大雅】錫爾介圭，以作爾寶。叶上土。</p>
<p>又【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>琳&middot;瑪瑙賦】帝道匪康，皇鑒元輔。顧以多福，康以碩寶。</p>
<p>又通作葆。【史記&middot;魯世家】毋墜天之降葆命。【註】讀如寶。【留侯世家】見穀城下黃石，取而葆祠之。【徐廣曰】史珍寶字皆作葆。</p>
