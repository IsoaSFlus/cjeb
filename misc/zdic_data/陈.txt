                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>陈</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">陳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>排列，摆设：～列。～兵。</li>
	<li>述说：～述。～情。详～。～诉。～说。</li>
	<li>旧的，时间久的：～旧。～腐。～醋。推～出新。～～相因。～迹。～皮。</li>
	<li>中国周代诸侯国名，在今河南省淮阳县一带。</li>
	<li>中国朝代名，南朝最末的王朝。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>陈</strong></p>
<p>陳 <span class="dicpy">Ch&eacute;n</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜，从木，申声。本义:地名。古宛丘地，春秋时陈国国都，在今河南省淮阳县。引申为姓氏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Chen state]</span>。在今河南省东部和安徽省北部一带</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陈，</span><span class="diczx2">宛丘</span>， <span class="diczx2">舜</span>后妫( <span class="dicpy">gu&iacute;</span>)满之所封。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈</span>， <span class="diczx2">颛顼</span>之族也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公八年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 陈朝 <span class="encs">[Chen Dynasty]</span>。朝代名。南朝之一。公元557年陈霸先代梁称帝，国号陈。建都建康(今江苏南京)，后被隋所灭</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 战阵;行列 <span class="encs">[battle array]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前未到匈奴陈二里所。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;李将军列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>陈</strong></p>
<p>陳 <span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 陈设，陈列 <span class="encs">[put in order;display]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陈，列也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陈，布也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">展器陈告备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;肆师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陈力就列，不能者止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;季氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陈鱼而观之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山肴野蔌，杂然而前陈者，太守宴也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 述说 <span class="encs">[explain;state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾非</span><span class="diczx2">尧</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>之道，不敢以陈于王前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑下》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9673.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E2C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨸬</span>軙敶【唐韻】直珍切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】池鄰切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音塵。【玉篇】列也，布也。【書&middot;咸有一德】乃陳戒于德。【詩&middot;小雅】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>饋八簋。</p>

<p>又【廣韻】故也。【詩&middot;小雅】我取其<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>，食我農人。【史記&middot;平準書】太倉之粟，陳陳相因。</p>
<p>又久也。【書&middot;盤庚】失于政，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>于兹。【傳】今旣失政，而<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>久於此而不徙。【疏】釋詁云：塵，久也。孫炎曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>居之久，久則生塵矣。古者<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>塵同也。故<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>爲久之義。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】張也。【禮&middot;表記】事君欲諫，不欲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>。【註】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>謂言其過於外也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】衆也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋宮】堂途謂之陳。【註】堂下至門徑也。【釋名】言賔主相迎<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>列之處也。【詩&middot;小雅】彼何人斯，胡逝我<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>。【註】北當階，其南接門內霤。</p>
<p>又國名。【廣韻】周武王封舜後胡公滿於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>，楚滅<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>爲縣，漢爲淮陽國，隋爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>州。</p>
<p>又姓。胡公滿之後，子孫以國爲氏。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22F6C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢽬</span>塵。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>直刃切。同陣。軍伍行列也。詳前陣字註。</p>
<p>又叶都年切，音顚。【易林】秋糧未成，無從至<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>。水深難涉，使我不前。【黃庭經】火兵符圖備靈關，前昂後<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>高下<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>。關音涓。</p>
