                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>倨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩˋ</span></p><ol><li>傲慢：前～后恭（先傲慢而后恭敬）。～傲。～固。～慢。</li><li>微曲（<span class="dicpy">qū</span>）：～句（钝角形的称“倨”；锐角形的称“<span class="diczx3">句</span>”）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">踞</span>”，伸开脚坐着。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>倨</strong> <span class="dicpy">jù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，居声。本义:傲慢)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[haughty;arrogant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倨，不逊也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">游毋倨。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“慢也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">嫂何前倨而后恭也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与其倨也宁句。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记·曾子立事》</span>。按，凡侈曰倨，敛曰句。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">体倨固而心执诈。——<span class="smcs">《脩身》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:倨忽(傲慢而疏忽);倨倨(一无思虑、神色傲慢的样子;盛服的样子);倨见(傲慢地接见或往见);倨侮(傲慢自大)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 直而折曲 <span class="encs">[bended]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倨中矩，句中钩。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:倨句(器物弯曲的形状。曲度较小的叫倨，大的叫句);倨佝(倨拘。弯曲的，句为甚曲);倨立(立而不正，前倾侧倚)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“踞”</span>。凭倚 <span class="encs">[rely on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">老聃</span>方将倨堂而应。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天运》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沛公方倨床从两女子洗足。——<span class="smcs">《史记·郦生陆贾列传》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/倨傲">倨傲</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5028_256.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(倨)</span><span class="ywyy">不遜也。</span><span class="swdz">遜當是本作孫。說詳辵部。孫者、逡循恭敬之意。戰國策曰。何前倨而後恭。引伸之凡侈曰倨。凡斂曰句。大戴禮。與其倨也寧句。樂記。倨中矩。句中鉤。左傳。直而不倨。曲而不屈。淮南子。句爪倨牙。凡言侈斂之度謂之倨句。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/264B1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦒱</span>工記曰。倨句一矩有半。又曰。倨句磬折。卽謂一矩有半也。又曰。倨句外博。謂侈於一矩而不及一矩有半也。又曰。句於矩。謂斂於一矩也。管子弟子職曰。倨句如矩。謂正方也。</span><span class="ywyy">从人。居聲。</span><span class="swdz">居御切。五部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
