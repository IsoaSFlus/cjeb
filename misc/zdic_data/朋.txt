                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>朋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">péng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/péng.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>彼此友好的人：～友。～辈。～侪。～俦。宾～。至爱亲～。</li><li>结党：～党（为私利而互相勾结、排斥异己的一帮人）。</li><li>成群：群居～飞。</li><li>古代以贝壳为货币，五贝为一串，两串为一朋。</li><li>比：硬大无～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>朋</strong> <span class="dicpy">péng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/péng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。本义:古代货币单位。相传五贝为一朋。或说五贝为一系，两系为一朋)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代货币单位。五贝为一朋。一说两贝为一朋，也有说十贝为一朋的 <span class="encs">[five shells]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既见君子，锡我百朋。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·菁菁者莪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同学 <span class="encs">[classmate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以朋友讲习。——<span class="smcs">《易·兑》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“同门曰朋，同志曰友。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 朋友 <span class="encs">[friend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朋，比也，朋，类也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或益之十朋之龟。——<span class="smcs">《易·损》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“党也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每有良朋。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·常棣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有朋自远方来。——<span class="smcs">《论语·学而》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:朋故(朋友故旧);朋曹(朋友辈);朋好(朋友，好友);朋伴(朋友，同伴);朋知(朋友;知交);朋侣(朋友;同伴)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 同类 <span class="encs">[same kind]</span>。如:朋从(同类相从);朋类(同类事物)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 朋党 <span class="encs">[clique]</span>。如:朋比(结成私党);朋甲(犹朋党);朋附(结党营私);朋徒(朋党;党徒);朋家(犹朋党);朋头(朋党的首领)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 队;班 <span class="encs">[team;group]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一朋头用杖击弄球子，如缀球子方坠队，两朋争占，供与朋头。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">孟元老</span><span class="smcs">《东京梦华录》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>朋</strong> <span class="dicpy">péng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/péng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 勾结 <span class="encs">[collude with]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">世并举而好朋兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向闻</span><span class="diczx2">杨虞卿</span>兄弟朋比贵势，妨平进之路。——<span class="smcs">《唐书·选举志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:朋谋(结党图谋不轨);朋比(阿附;勾结);朋邪(朋比为奸);朋奸(朋比为奸);朋附(勾结、阿附);朋挺(犹勾结);朋扇(相互勾结煽动);朋援(勾结引援)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 群聚 <span class="encs">[gather]</span>。如:朋酒(谓亲友聚饮);朋宴(聚朋宴饮);朋淫(群聚淫乱);朋饮(聚饮);朋戏(群聚嬉戏)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 合伙 <span class="encs">[form a partnership]</span>。如:朋充(谓合伙蒙混冒充);朋伙(犹合伙)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“崩”</span>。倒塌 <span class="encs">[collapse;crash to the ground]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其状义而不朋。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·大宗师》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>朋</strong> <span class="dicpy">péng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/péng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p>共同，一致
