                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>审</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">審</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shěn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shěn.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄣˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>详细，周密：～慎。～视。</li>
	<li>仔细思考，反复分析、推究：～查。～定。～订。～核。～美。～计。～评。～时度势。</li>
	<li>讯问案件：～理。～判。～讯。公～。</li>
	<li>知道：不～近况如何？</li>
	<li>一定地，果然：～如其言。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">审</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>审</strong></p>
<p>審 <span class="dicpy">shěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从宀番。宀房屋。番，兽足。屋里有兽足印，能仔细分辨。简体字从宀申声。本义:详究;考察)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[study at large]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">审，谓详观其道也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;非相》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻而审，则为福矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故审堂下之阴，而知日月之行，阴阳之变。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">直以不能内审诸己，外受流言，沉迷猖獗，以至于此。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:审时(详察时令);审物(详察物情);审形(详察实情);审决(审察决定);审机而行(详细、周密地进行分析研究，把握好时机再行动);审别(审察辨别);审势(审察形势)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 知道，知悉 <span class="encs">[know]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">审，知也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《类篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">审矣，何足怪乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子审礼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;礼书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陛下连降三诏，召臣回朝，未审圣意为何?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今投使君，共图大事，未审尊意如何?</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">倚南窗以寄傲，审容膝之易安。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:审悉(详细了解)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 审核，审查，检查核对 <span class="encs">[check]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为人主计者，莫如先审取舍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:审考(审查);审合(审查验证);审决(审查决定);审覆(审查;审察);审释(审查释放);审端(检查修正);审分(审定名分、职分);审律(审定乐律);审音(审定字的读音)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 审讯;审判 <span class="encs">[try]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余经秋审，皆减等发配。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:审断(审判裁决);审究(审问查究);审拟(审问拟罪);审鞫(审讯);审录(审讯);审语(审判结论);审质(审问)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 询问，打听清楚 <span class="encs">[inquire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十一娘亦审里居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>审</strong></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">審</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“审”字头，请参考“審”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=宀" target="_blank">宀</a>】
                              審·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5BE9.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕宷【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>式荏切，音嬸。【說文】悉也。本作宷，从宀从釆。【徐鉉曰】宀，覆也。釆，別也。能包覆而深別之也。今从篆作審。【增韻】詳也，熟究也。【書&middot;說命】乃審厥象，俾以形旁，求于天下。【中庸】審問之。【禮&middot;樂記】審聲以知音，審音以知樂，審樂以知政，而治道備矣。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;月令】審卦吉凶。【註】謂省錄也。</p>
<p>又【莊子&middot;徐無鬼】水之守土也審，影之守人也審，物之守物也審。【註】郭象曰：無意，則止於分，所以爲審。循本曰：言此理相守，未嘗相離，如水之守土，影之守人，物之守物，審定而不移也。</p>
<p>又凡鞫事曰審。【書&middot;呂<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>】其審克之。</p>
<p>又束也。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;羽人】十羽爲審。【註】古人徵羽爲旌旄之飾，弓箭之用。審，一束也。</p>
<p>又姓。漢審食其。【正字通】漢郞中審忠，靈帝時上書論曹節等罪惡。</p>
<p>又【韻會】與盤同，水盤旋也。【莊子&middot;應帝王】止水之審爲淵。【註】審音盤。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>文云：蟠，聚也。</p>
