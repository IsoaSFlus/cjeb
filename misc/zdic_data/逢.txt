						<p>● <strong>逢</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄥˊ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>遇到：～遇。久别重～。～凶化吉。狭路相～。</li>
	<li>迎合，巴结：～迎。～君之恶。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">逢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>逢</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，表示与行走有关。夆( <span class="dicpy">fēng</span>)声。本义:遭逢，遇见)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[meet;come upon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逢，遇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">魑魅罔两，莫能逢之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而亲以逢殆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;天问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">萍水相逢，尽是他乡之客。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">落花时节又逢君。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《江南春绝句》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相逢何必曾相识。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:逢辰(遇到好的时刻);逢会(遇到时机;相会，会合);逢场(遇到或到达某场合);逢晤(当面相遇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 迎，迎接 <span class="encs">[meet face to face]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道而得神，是谓逢福。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语上》</span>。注:&ldquo;逢，迎也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逢君之恶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主人能爱客，终日有逢迎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《与卢象集朱家》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 迎合 <span class="encs">[pander to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逢君之恶其罪大。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:逢君(迎合君主的心意);逢意(迎合他人心意);逢合(迎合);逢恶导非(逢迎坏人，助长恶行)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>逢</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大的 <span class="encs">[big]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子孙其逢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">丘</span>少居鲁，衣逢掖之衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:逢掖(宽大的衣袖);逢长(久长);逢衣(古时儒者所穿的一种宽袖衣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;丰&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">fēng</span>)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 壮大，茂盛 <span class="encs">[strengthen;lush]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身其康强，子孙其逢吉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:逢昌(壮大昌盛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宽大 <span class="encs">[loose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逢衣浅带，解果其冠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span></span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=辵" target="_blank">辵</a>】
                              逢·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9022.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>符容切，音縫。【說文】遇也。从辵，峯省聲。【正韻】値也。【左傳&middot;宣三年】不逢不若。【書&middot;洪範】子孫其逢吉。</p>
<p>又【正韻】迎也。【揚子&middot;方言】逢、迎，逆也。自關而西。或曰迎，或曰逢。</p>
<p>又逆也。【前漢&middot;東方朔傳】逢占射覆。【註】逆占事，猶言逆刺也。</p>
<p>又大也。【禮&middot;儒行】衣逢掖之衣。【註】衣掖下寬大也。</p>
<p>又閼逢，歲名。【爾雅&middot;釋天】太歲在甲曰閼逢。【註】言萬物鋒芒欲出，壅遏未通也。</p>
<p>又與縫通。【禮&middot;玉藻】深衣縫齊倍要。【註】縫，或爲逢。</p>
<p>又有逢，國名。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】有逢伯陵因之。【註】逢伯陵，殷諸侯。</p>
<p>又姓。齊逢丑父。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【韻會】蒲蒙切【集韻】【正韻】蒲紅切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蓬。鼓聲也。【詩&middot;大雅】鼉鼓逢逄。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳封禪書】大漢之德，逢涌原泉。【註】逢讀若㷭，言如熢火之升，原泉之流。</p>
<p>又叶符方切，音房。【韓愈詩】蕭條千萬里，會合安可逢。叶上江、下鄕。◎按从夅者，音龎。从夆者，音縫、音蓬。【顏氏家訓】逄、逢之別，豈可雷同。</p>
