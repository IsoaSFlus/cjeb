                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">胄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>胄</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">➊冑</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>盔，古代战士戴的帽子：甲～（甲衣和头盔）。介～（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">介</span>&rdquo;，甲衣）。</li>	<li>帝王或贵族的后代：贵～。王室之～。～裔。～子（古代帝王和贵族的长子，都要进入国学学习，后亦泛称国子学生）。</li>	<li>泛指后代。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">胄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胄</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。&ldquo;胄&rdquo;和&ldquo;冑&rdquo;本为两字，現代大陆规范字&ldquo;冑&rdquo;合并到&ldquo;胄&rdquo;。&ldquo;冑&rdquo;从&ldquo;冃&rdquo;（古&ldquo;帽&rdquo;字，帽子像形字），&ldquo;由&rdquo;声。本义:头盔)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[helmet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胄，兜鍪也。从月，由声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">离为甲胄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟甲胄起戎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;伪说命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贝胄朱綅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂&middot;閟宫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献甲者执胄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左右免胄而下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冠軸(胄)带剑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;议兵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铠胄皆镔铁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:甲胄</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>胄</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。&ldquo;胄&rdquo;从&ldquo;肉&rdquo;，&ldquo;由&rdquo;声。本义:后裔)」</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代称帝王或贵族的后代 <span class="encs">[descendants of emperor or noble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">刘公</span>乃帝室之胄，德广才高，可领 <span class="diczx2">徐州</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《全图绣像三国演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝室之胄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王室之胄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胄子(帝王或贵族的长子);胄监(国子监);胄阀(家世门第)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指世系 <span class="encs">[pedigree;genealogy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂孤人之能胄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《遂志赋》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
