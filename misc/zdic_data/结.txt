<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">結</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>系（<span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span>），绾（<span class="dicpy">wǎn </span>）：～网。～绳。～扎。</li>
	<li>条状物打成的疙瘩：打～。蝴蝶～。</li>
	<li>聚，合：～晶。～识。～盟。～交。～集。～合。～党营私。</li>
	<li>收束，完了（<span class="dicpy">liǎo</span>）：～账。～局。～案。～果。～论。归根～底。</li>
	<li>一种保证负责的字据：具～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">结</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>结</strong></p>
<p>結 <span class="dicpy">jiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>植物生长果实 <span class="encs">[bear fruit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:结瓜;开花结果</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>结</strong></p>
<p>結 <span class="dicpy">jiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 坚硬;牢固 <span class="encs">[solid]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:地很结</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 健壮 <span class="encs">[stout]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:结实</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/结巴">结巴</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/结果">结果</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/结结巴巴">结结巴巴</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/结实">结实</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/结实">结实</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>结</strong></p>
<p>結 <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用绳、线、皮条等绾成的疙瘩 <span class="encs">[knot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:解结;蝴蝶结;装饰结</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 旧时向官府承担责任或承认了结的书面的字据 <span class="encs">[written quarantee]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:保结;具结;印结;甘结;结状(旧时向官府出具的表示证明、担保或了结的文书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 关键 <span class="encs">[key]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诚信者，天下之结也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
