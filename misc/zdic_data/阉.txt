                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>阉</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閹</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>割去男人的或雄性动物的生殖器：～鸡。～割。</li>	<li>太监，封建时代的宦官：～人。～党。～竖。～寺（指宦官）。～宦。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> castrate; eunuch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> entmannen, kastrieren  (V, Med)​,Eunuch  (S, Gesch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> eunuque</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阉</strong></p>
<p>閹 <span class="dicpy">yān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从门，从奄，奄亦声。本义:被阉割的人。古代常用来看守宫门) 同本义 <span class="encs">[eunuch]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 特指宦官</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逆阉防伺甚严，虽家仆不得近。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阉君(主领宫内太监的官);阉奴(指太监);阉竖(太监的贱称。竖，供役使的人);阉党(依附阉官太监而结成的党派);阉官(由太监充任官员的贱称);阉竖(太监的贱称);阉儿(蔑称宦官);阉寺(宦官);阉使(出使的太监);阉狗(骂太监的话);阉尹(太监头头)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阉</strong></p>
<p>閹 <span class="dicpy">yān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 阉割 <span class="encs">[castrate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宦官悉用阉人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阉侍(被阉过的奴仆。也称阉奴、阉阍);阉洁(割除动物的睾丸或卵巢)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95B9.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】英廉切【集韻】【韻會】衣廉切【正韻】衣炎切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音淹。【說文】豎也。宮中閹閽閉門者。从門，奄，意兼聲。【廣韻】男無勢，精閉者。【前漢&middot;敘傳】閹尹之呰。【註】謂宮人爲閹者，謂其精氣奄閉不洩也。一曰主奄閉門者。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋天】太歲在戌曰閹茂。【釋文】閹，音淹。【史記&middot;歷書】游兆，閹茂二年。【註】閹茂，一作淹戊。【管子&middot;幼官篇】春行冬政，肅。行秋政，雷。行夏政，閹。【註】陽氣獨盛。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】衣檢切【正韻】於檢切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音渰。義同。　【集韻】通作奄。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/95B9.svg">                          <p>豎也。宮中奄閽閉門者。从門奄聲。英廉切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/95B9_7299.svg" /><span class="swbf">(閹)</span><span class="ywyy">門豎也。</span><span class="swdz">門字今依御覽補。豎猶孺也。周禮注曰：豎，未冠者之官名。凡文王世子之內豎，左傳之使牛爲豎皆是。司門則曰門豎，故从門。</span><span class="ywyy">宮中奄昏閉門者。</span><span class="swdz">昏，各本作閽，今正。周禮注曰：奄，精氣閉藏者。今謂之宦人。他豎不必奄人，此豎則奄人也，故从奄。一說當依小徐作閹閽閉門者。一說當作宮中掩門者。</span><span class="ywyy">从門奄聲。</span><span class="swdz">此當言从門奄，奄亦聲。英廉切。八部。</span></p>                          </div>
