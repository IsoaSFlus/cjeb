                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>隶</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">隸</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>附属，属于：～属。配～（从属）。直～中央。</li>	<li>封建时代的衙役：～卒。皂～。徒～。</li>	<li>旧时地位低下而被奴役的人：奴～。～仆。</li>	<li>隶书，汉字的一种书体，由篆书简化演变而成：～书。～字。汉～。</li></ol><p>● <strong>隶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄞˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/逮">逮</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> subservient; servant; KangXi radical 171</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> zugeneigt, zugetan ,anreißen, anzeichnen  (V)​,Li  (Eig, Fam)​,Radikal Nr. 171 = ergreifen, fangen, untergeordnet,untergeben sein, unterstehen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dépendre de,être subordonné à,esclave,scribe</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">隶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>隶</strong></p>
<p>隸、隷 <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (隶:又樆尾会意。本义:逮、捕、追及)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[catch;capture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隶，及也，从又从尾省，又持尾者从后及之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 跟从;附属 <span class="encs">[be subordinate to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隶而从者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不隶于宫观之籍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《复庵记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:隶事(以故事相互隶属)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;离&rdquo;。流离 <span class="encs">[live the life of a refugee]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饿馑流隶，饥寒道路。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;叙传&middot;王命论》</span></span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>隶</strong></p>
<p>隸 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 中囯古代对一种奴隶或差役的称谓<span class="encs">[slave;person in servitude]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">輿臣隶，隶臣僚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆得以隶使之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甿隶之人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲效野夫贱隶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:隶人(职位低微的吏役;因罪被罚为官奴而从事劳役的人);隶仆(仆役);隶户(被没入为奴隶的人家);隶农(农奴，佃户，代耕农)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 特指衙役 <span class="encs">[yamen runner]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:皂隶</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 即隶书(秦书八体之一。又名&ldquo;八分体&rdquo;。相传为秦人程邈所作，由小篆省简变化而成) <span class="encs">[official script，an ancient style of calligraphy current in the Han Dynasty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:隸絶(善于写隸书的高手);隸習(練習，演習);隸篆(隸書與小篆)</span></p>
