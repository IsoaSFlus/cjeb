                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>全</strong> <span class="dicpy">quán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/quán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (一作仝。会意。篆文从入，从王(玉)。盖谓交纳的玉完整无缺。本义:纯色玉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pure jade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">全，完也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天子用全，上公用龙。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·考工记·王人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古州名。全州 <span class="encs">[Quan prefecture]</span>。治所在今广西全县</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>全</strong> <span class="dicpy">quán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/quán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 完全<span class="encs">[whole;entire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不明其义，君人不全。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·祭统》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹具也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天地无全功。——<span class="smcs">《列子·天瑞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">唯全人能之。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·庚桑楚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以全争于天下。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·谋攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">墓中全乎为五人。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:全装贯带(全副武装);全副(全套);全家;全甲(全部披甲的士兵);全仗(全部的仪仗兵卫;完全依靠);全兵(军队全部以弓矛等兵器武装);全舍(全部舍弃);全批(统统);全全(全部;统统);全周(周全;全面);全争(不动一卒，不攻一城，完全用智谋使敌降伏);全别(完全变了样，完全不一样);全制(完全控制);全遂(完全遵从);全错;全怪我;全无心肝;全完(全部缴纳);全旨(全部的意蕴、含义);全通(全部通晓);全量(全部，所有);全灶(全能的厨师);全行(全部);全副(整个);全宗(整个宗族);全批(全体);全颗(一群人的全体);全额(全部规定的数目) </p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 完备，完整 <span class="encs">[complete]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">法不平，令不全，是亦夺柄失位之道也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·任法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三年之后，未尝见全牛也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·养生主》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">城郭不备全，不可以自守。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·七患》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牺牲之不全肥。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·明鬼下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">全吴之地。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">全石以为底。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span
