<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> soar, glide, hover; detailed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufsteigen, sich erheben ,Xiang  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> voler,planer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">翔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>翔</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从羽，羊声。本义:翅膀平直不动盘旋地飞)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[circle in the air;soar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翔，回飞也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸢鸟丑其飞也翔。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释鸟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凤以之翔。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“大飞不动曰翔。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽欲翱翔。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·俶真》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“直刺不动曰翔。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">色斯举矣，翔而后集。——<span class="smcs">《论语·乡党》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翦翎送笼中，使看百鸟翔。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《调张籍》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:翱翔(在空中回旋地飞);翔回(回旋飞翔)。亦指行走时张开两臂，像鸟张开翅膀</p><p><span class="diczx1">室中不翔，并坐不横肱。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:翔趋(古代的一种礼容。上体稍前倾，张臂细步趋行)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 悠闲自在地行走 <span class="encs">[walk leisurely]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柴门何萧条，狐兔翔我宇。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植<span class="smcs">《</span>梁甫行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:翔步(缓步。比喻可以从缓进行的事);翔翔(安舒的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 飞后停息 <span class="encs">[rest after flying]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飞不妄集，翔必择林。——<span class="smcs">《鹦鹉赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沙鸥翔集。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 回顾 <span class="encs">[look back]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后弱则翔。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·矢人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“回顾也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凝听翔立，若有所伺。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> <span class="encs">[物价]</span> 上涨 <span class="encs">[go up]</span>。如:翔起(腾踊;上升);翔泳(犹升沉);翔踊(物价腾贵或暴涨)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>翔</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“详”</span>。详尽 <span class="encs">[detailed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自宣元后，单于称藩臣西域服从，其土地山川王侯户数道里远近翔实矣。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·西域传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亦治今文学，博洽翔实。——<span class="smcs">《近代学风之地理的分布》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:翔集(周详无遗地采录收集)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“祥”</span>。吉利 <span class="encs">[lucky]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丰其屋，天际翔也。——<span class="smcs">《易·丰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翔风起，甘露降。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·是应》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:翔洽(上下融洽);翔风(祥风)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/翔实">翔实</a></span></div>
