                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">网</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>网</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">網</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄤˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用绳、线等结成的捕鱼捉鸟的器具：鱼～。～罗。～开一面（喻用宽大的态度来对待）。～漏吞舟（喻法令太宽，使坏人漏网）。</li>	<li>形状像网的东西：电～。发～。</li>	<li>像网一样的纵横交错的组织或系统：～点。通信～。天罗地～。</li>	<li>用网捕捉：～到一条大鱼。</li>	<li>像网似的笼罩着：眼睛里～着红丝。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> net; network; KangXi radical 122</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Netz, Netzwerk  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> filet,réseau,prendre au filet</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>网</strong></p>
<p>網 <span class="dicpy">wǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。&ldquo;网&rdquo;捕鸟兽的叫&ldquo;网&rdquo;，捕鱼的叫罟。笼统地说，两字没有不同。本义:捕鱼鳖鸟兽的工具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[net]</span></p>
