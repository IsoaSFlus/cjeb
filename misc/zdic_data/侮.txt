data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，每声。本义:轻慢，不敬重)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[slight]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不侵侮。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:侮手(动手;对打);侮谇(欺侮斥责);侮夺(侮慢他人，侵夺他人所有之物);侮害(欺侮侵害);侮折(欺侮折辱);侮文(歪曲法律条文以行私作恶)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“捂”</span>。用手遮盖 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p><p><span
