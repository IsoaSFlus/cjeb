						<p>● <strong>弑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古时称臣杀君、子杀父母：～君。～父。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to kill one's superior</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tuer</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弑</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从杀，式声。本义:古代统治阶级称子杀父、臣杀君为&ldquo;弑&rdquo;)同本义 <span class="encs">[murder one&#39;s superior]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弑，臣杀君也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡自虐其君曰弑，自外曰戕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">放弑其君则残之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司马》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君臣未尝相弑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;明堂位》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万乘之国弑其君者，必千乘之家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弑虐(指弑君);弑君;弑母</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弑</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三">卷三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三&amp;swbs=殺">殺</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5F11.svg">                          <p>臣殺君也。《易》曰：&ldquo;臣弒其君。&rdquo;从殺省，式聲。式吏切文二　重四（重三）</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5F11_1770.svg" /><span class="swbf">(弑)</span><span class="ywyy">臣殺君也。</span><span class="swdz">經傳殺、弑二字轉寫旣多譌亂，音家又或拘泥，中無定見，多有殺讀弑者。按述其實則曰殺君，正其名則曰弑君。春秋，正名之書也，故言弑不言殺。三傳，述實以釋經之書也，故或言殺，或言弑，不必傳無殺君字也。許釋弑曰臣殺君，此可以證矣。殺在古音十五部，弑在一部，本不相通也。弑，漢石經公羊作試，二字同式聲也。白虎通引春秋讖曰：弑者，試也。欲言臣子殺其君父不敢卒，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>閒司事，可稍稍試之。釋名曰：弑，伺也。説同。皆本文言傳之意。</span><span class="ywyy">易曰：臣弑其君。</span><span class="swdz">文言傳文。</span><span class="ywyy">从殺省，式聲。</span><span class="swdz">式吏切。一部。</span></p>                          </div>
