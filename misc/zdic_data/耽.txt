data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耳，冘( <span class="dicpy">yín</span>)声。本义:耳朵大而且下垂) 同本义 <span class="encs">[big ear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">夸父</span>耽耳。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·地形》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">高</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“耽耳，耳垂在肩上。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:耽毵(毛长下垂纷披的样子)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>耽</strong></p><p>躭 <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 沉溺 <span class="encs">[indulge in]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于嗟女兮，无与士耽。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士之耽兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“乐也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妻子好合，如鼓瑟琴，兄弟既翕，和乐且耽。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟耽乐之从。——<span class="smcs">《书·无逸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">耽于女乐，不顾国政，则亡国之祸也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且耽田家乐。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《赠闾丘处士》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:耽着(迷恋);耽思深思，潜心研究探讨;耽乐(沈迷于享乐)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 承受;担负 <span class="encs">[take on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我要及时就死，又耽不起吞金服毒的。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:耽险(担风险;危险);耽待(耽带。照顾，帮助;宽容;原谅;担当，承受;等候)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 嗜;喜好 <span class="encs">[be keen on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你本身做亭长，耽几盏酒。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">睢景臣</span><span class="smcs">《高祖还乡》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:耽好(特别爱好);耽志(专心致志);耽尚(爱好，尊崇);耽研(专心研究);耽意(专心)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 停留;延迟 <span class="encs">[delay;hang fire]</span>。如:耽搁;耽误;耽迟(耽误;迟延)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr"
