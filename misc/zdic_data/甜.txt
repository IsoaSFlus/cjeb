                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">甜</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>甜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ti&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ti&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>像糖或蜜的滋味，喻使人感到舒服的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">苦</span>&rdquo;相对：～品。～食。～菜。～瓜。～美。～润。～适。～爽。～言蜜语。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sweet, sweetness</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> süß  (Adj, Ess)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sucré,doux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">甜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>甜</strong></p>
<p>甛 <span class="dicpy">ti&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ti&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从甘，从舌。舌头能品尝甜味。本义:味甘)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 像糖或蜜的味道 <span class="encs">[sweet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甛，美也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甜，甘也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草木不复抽，百味失苦甜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《苦寒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:甜物(甜的食品);甜酒(甘甜的米酒);甜雪(甘甜的雪水);甜酸苦辣(指种种不同的味道);甜香</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 比喻幸福;快乐 <span class="encs">[happy]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:甜沃沃(形容幸福愉快)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 美好 <span class="encs">[fine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:甜差(美差);甜冤对(俏冤家);甜淡(柔和淡雅);甜俏(柔和娇媚);甜俗(软熟媚俗);甜软(甜蜜温柔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 形容睡得酣畅 <span class="encs">[sound;asleep]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:甜睡;甜乡(梦乡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指美言 <span class="encs">[honeyed;unctuous]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:甜口儿(善于说奉承话的嘴);甜句儿(甜言);甜言(美言;好话);甜言软语，甜言媚语(温柔体贴的话);甜话儿(甜言蜜语);甜嘴蜜舌(甜言蜜语)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>甜</strong> <span class="dicpy">ti&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ti&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>酣睡，熟睡 <span class="encs">[sleep soundly]</span></p>
