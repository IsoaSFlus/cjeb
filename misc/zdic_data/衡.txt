                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>衡</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄥˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>秤杆，泛指秤：～器。～镜（借指辨别是非善恶的标准）。～鉴（衡镜）。</li>
	<li>称量：～钧（借指执掌国政之权）。</li>
	<li>反复思索做出决定，比较评定：权～。～情度理。</li>
	<li>平，对等：平～。均～。</li>
	<li>指北斗星：～汉（北斗星和银河）。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">横</span>&rdquo;，纵横。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> measure, weigh, judge, consider</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> abwägen, wägen ,Bedeutung, Gewicht  (S)​,Heng  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> balance,appareil de pesage,peser,mesurer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>衡</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从角，从大，行声。本义:绑在牛角上的横木)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。一说穿于牛鼻的横木 <span class="encs">[crossbar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秋而载尝，夏而福衡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:&ldquo;福衡，设牛角以福之也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡，牛触，横大木其角。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>注:&ldquo;谓牛好抵触，以木阑制之也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 车辕前端的横木 <span class="encs">[croobar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">加之以衡扼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;马蹄》</span>。释文:&ldquo;衡，辕前横木缚轭者也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则见其倚于衡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;卫灵公》</span>。皇疏:&ldquo;衡，车衡，轭也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枉玉衡于炎火兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;刘向&middot;九叹&middot;远逝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:衡辀(车辕与辕前横木);衡轸(辕前横木与车后横木);衡盖(车辕前端的横木和车上的伞盖。亦借指车)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 架在屋梁上或门窗上的横木。即桁条或檩子 <span class="encs">[ridgepole]</span>。如:衡宇(横木为门的房屋。指简陋房屋);衡门(以横木为门的简陋房屋，借指房屋建筑古朴典雅);衡梁(架屋的横梁。比喻重任);衡荜(横木编荜以为门户)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 秤杆，秤 <span class="encs">[the graduated arm of a steelyard weighing apparatus]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡不同于轻重。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;扬权》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:衡石(泛指称重量的器物);衡库(称与仓库);衡玑(即天平);衡鉴(衡器和镜子);衡权(称物之具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 衡阳的简称 <span class="encs">[the Hengyang]</span>。如:京广线衡广段</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 衡山的简称 <span class="encs">[Hengshan Mountain]</span>。此山系五岳之一，在今湖南省中东部</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 水名。衡水的省称 <span class="encs">[the Hengshui River]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求思望</span><span class="diczx2">襄</span>澨，叹息对 <span class="diczx2">衡</span>渚。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">刘义恭</span><span class="smcs">《艳歌行》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>衡</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 称量 <span class="encs">[weigh;measure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡，加重于其一旁，必捶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹衡之于轻重也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;经解》</span>。注:&ldquo;衡，称也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为之权衡以称之，则并与权衡而窃之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;胠箧》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 衡量;评定 <span class="encs">[judge;evaluate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡之于左右。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;主述训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:衡文(品评文章，指主持科举考试);衡定(评定);衡校(品评考核);衡鉴(品评，鉴别)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 横亘，横贯 <span class="encs">[lie across]</span>。如:衡流(横穿水流);衡游(谓横渡)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 违逆 <span class="encs">[disobey]</span>。如:衡命(违逆命令);衡道(违背道义)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 铨选 <span class="encs">[select]</span>。如:衡才(铨选人才);衡尺(喻铨选人才的官职);衡铨(铨衡，铨选);衡综(谓考核铨选官吏)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>衡</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 横行;蛮横。通&ldquo;横&rdquo; <span class="encs">[rude and unreasonable]</span>。如:衡行(横行。不循正道而行);衡击(横行劫击。谓抢劫。衡，通&ldquo;横&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 横。与&ldquo;纵&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[traverse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡，横也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡门之下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;陈风&middot;衡门》</span>。传:&ldquo;衡门，横木为门。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外连衡而斗诸侯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">倚一衡木。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:衡陈(横向排列);横纵(纵横);衡缩(纵横)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 平正 <span class="encs">[fair]</span>。如;衡言(平正之言);衡虚(平正而清虚);衡仪(公平的法则)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 同&ldquo;横&rdquo;，梗塞、不顺 <span class="encs">[block]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡于虑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一人衡行于天下，武王耻之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不官而衡至者，君子慎之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;致仕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有勇力者聚徒而衡击。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡流而方羊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者冠缩缝，今也衡缝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以能合从，又善连衡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非有分于纵横之党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">置於車轅前端的橫木。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;衛靈公》：「在輿，則見其倚於衡也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;陸德明《經典釋文&middot;卷二七&middot;莊子音義中》：「衡，轅前橫木，縛軛者也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">秤，量輕重的器具。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;禮論》：「衡誠縣矣，則不可欺以輕重。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;張衡&middot;東京賦》：「同衡律而壹軌量，齊急舒於寒燠。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國吳&middot;薛綜&middot;注：「衡，稱也。軌，法。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">眉目之間。</span><span class="gc_yy">《後漢書&middot;卷六○下&middot;蔡邕傳》：「胡老乃揚衡含笑，援琴而歌。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;劉孝標&middot;廣絕交論》：「見一善則盱衡扼腕，遇一才則揚眉抵掌。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">星名。北斗七星的第五星。因位於魁與斗杓之間，故以為斗的中央。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;張衡&middot;東京賦》：「攝提揮衡，徐至於射宮。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國吳&middot;薛綜&middot;注：「攝提有六星，玉衡，北斗中星，主迴轉。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">大陸地區衡山的簡稱。為五嶽之一。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;王勃〈滕王閣序〉：「星分翼、軫，地接衡、廬。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如漢代有衡咸。</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8861.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2163B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡘻</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21649.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡙉</span>【唐韻】戸庚切【集韻】【韻會】何庚切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音行。【書&middot;舜典】同律度量衡。【前漢&middot;律歷志】衡，平也。所以任權而均物，平輕重也。【荀子&middot;禮論】衡誠懸矣，則不可欺以輕重。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;舜典】在璿璣玉衡，以齊七政。【傳】璣衡，王者正天文之器，可運轉者。【漢書註】衡謂渾天儀也。</p>
<p>又樓殿邊欄楯也。【前漢&middot;袁盎傳】百金之子不騎衡。</p>
<p>又勺柄，龍頭也。【周禮&middot;冬官&middot;玉人】大璋、中璋九寸，邊璋七寸，衡四寸。</p>
<p>又眉目之閒也。【蔡邕&middot;釋誨】揚衡含笑。【左思&middot;魏都賦】盱衡而誥。</p>
<p>又斗之中央也。【前漢&middot;天文志】衡殷南斗。</p>
<p>又橫也。【前漢&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】合縱連衡。【師古曰】戰國時，齊楚韓魏燕趙爲縱，秦國爲衡。秦地形東西橫長，故爲衡也。</p>
<p>又楅衡，所以楅持牛，使不得抵觸也。【周禮&middot;地官】凡祭祀，飾其牛牲，設其楅衡。</p>
<p>又【小爾雅】斤十謂之衡，衡有半謂之秤。</p>
<p>又維持冠者曰衡。【左傳&middot;桓二年】衡紞紘綖。</p>
<p>又掌山林者謂之衡。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;太宰】虞衡作山澤之材。</p>
<p>又阿衡，官名。【書&middot;太甲】惟嗣王不惠于阿衡。</p>
<p>又地名。【周語】以諸侯朝于衡雝。【註】衡雝，鄭地，在今河內。</p>
<p>又山名。【爾雅&middot;釋山】江南衡。【註】南岳衡山也。</p>
<p>又水名。【水經注】衡水東經阜城縣故城。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】伊尹爲湯阿衡，子孫因以爲氏。一云魯公子衡之後，以王父字爲氏。漢有衡威、衡驃卿。</p>
<p>又與蘅通，杜衡也。【司馬相如&middot;子虛賦】其東則有蕙圃、衡蘭。</p>
<p>又【集韻】胡盲切。與橫通。【詩&middot;齊風】衡從其畝。【疏】衡，古通橫。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;陳風】衡門之下，可以棲遲。【註】衡木爲門也。</p>
<p>又【毛詩古音攷】音杭。《小雅》：約軧錯衡，八鸞瑲瑲。《史記&middot;自序》：維契作商，爰及成湯，太甲居桐，德盛阿衡。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>叶音杭。　【說文】从角、大，从行。【韻會】俗作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2258D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢖍</span>，非。</p>
