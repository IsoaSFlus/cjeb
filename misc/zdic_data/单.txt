<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">單</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>不复杂：～纯。简～。～调（<span class="dicpy">di&agrave;o</span>）。</li>
	<li>独一：～独。～一。～词。</li>
	<li>只，仅：做事～靠热情不够。</li>
	<li>奇（jī）数的：～日。～号。</li>
	<li>薄，弱：～薄。</li>
	<li>衣服被褥只有一层的：～裤。～衣。</li>
	<li>覆盖用的布：被～。床～。</li>
	<li>记载事物用的纸片：～据。传～。名～。</li>
	<li>和尚称禅堂的坐床。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>单</strong></p>
<p>單 <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本义:大)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[great]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">單，大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;当为大言也，浅人删言字。如誣加言也，浅人亦删言字。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明清于单辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span>。按，夸诞之辞。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏禹能单平水土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;郑语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王之威亦单矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;春申君传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">单埢垣兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《甘泉赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;大貌。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 单独 <span class="encs">[single]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">单足以喻则单。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正名》</span>。注:单，物之单名也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:单幅门(独扇的门);单跪(弯屈一膝，行半跪之礼。也叫&ldquo;打千&rdquo;);单绪(只有一个儿子传宗接代。即单传)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 一个 <span class="encs">[one]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">单，一也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;枚乘传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今单车来代之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
