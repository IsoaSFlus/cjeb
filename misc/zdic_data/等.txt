                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>等</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">děng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/děng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>古代指顿齐竹简（书）。</li><li>数量、程度相同，或地位一般高：相～。平～。～于。～同。～值。～量齐观。</li><li>表示数量或程度的级别：～级。～次。～第。～而下之。</li><li>特指台阶的级。</li><li>种，类：这～事。</li><li>表示同一辈份的多数人：我～。尔～。</li><li>表示列举未尽，或用于列举煞尾：北京、上海～地。</li><li>候，待：～候。～待。</li><li>待到：～我写完。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">戥</span>”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rank, grade; wait; equal; 'etc.'</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Klasse, Rang, Grad  (S)​,(auf etwas oder jemanden)​ warten  (V)​,gleich wie, gleichermaßen, identisch  (Adv)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> classe,échelon,etc.,et autres,égal,attendre</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>等</strong> <span class="dicpy">děng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/děng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从竹，寺声。寺官曹之等平也，寺者，简册杂积之地，寺亦声。本义:整齐的简册)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[orderly bamboo slips]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">等，齐简也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 等级;辈分 <span class="encs">[class;grade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士阶三等。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·召类》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆陛下故等夷。——<span class="smcs">《史记·留侯世家》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“言等辈也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">同名同食曰同等。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记·少间》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请自贬三等。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:优等;等例(等级差别，地位高低的差别);等子(宋代的御林军;标本，样本);等分(等级名分);等外品(质量差，不列入等级的产品);等列(等级品位);等别(等级)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 台阶 <span class="encs">[flight of steps leading to the house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出降一等。——<span class="smcs">《论语·乡党》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 类型;样子 <span class="encs">[sort;shape]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爻有等。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“类也”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">呀，真个下雪了，有这等异事。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">关汉卿</span><span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:此等人;等侪，等俦(同类)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 戥子 <span class="encs">[small steelyard]</span>。秤金银和珍贵物品的衡器。如:等子(戥子。小型的秤);等秤(戥子和秤)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>等</strong> <span class="dicpy">děng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/děng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 等同，使一样 <span class="encs">[equate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以等邦国。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹齐等也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与无法等。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·主术》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“同也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我行法，当等贵贱，均贫富。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
