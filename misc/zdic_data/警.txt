						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>警</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄥˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>注意可能发生的危险，戒备，告诫：～卫。～世。～告。～戒。～备。～惕。～省（<span class="dicpy">xǐng </span>）（警惕醒悟）。</li>    <li>需要戒备的事件或消息：～号。～报。～钟。</li>    <li>感觉敏锐，见解独到：～句。～觉（<span class="dicpy">jué</span>）。机～。</li>    <li>国家维持社会秩序和治安的武装力量：～察。～士。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从言，从敬，敬亦声。本义:戒敕)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[warn; admonish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">警，戒也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正岁，则以法警戒群吏。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·宰夫》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所以警众也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·文王世子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃一篇之警策。——<span class="smcs">《文赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今年或者大警</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以警其余。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:警告;警励(告诫策励);警迹人(元代被列入盗贼户籍的人);警鼓(报警之鼓);警动(使人警觉悚动);警新(警策动人，语意新颖);警众(使众人警觉);警省(警觉省悟)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 戒备;防备 <span class="encs">[outpost]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">军卫不彻，警也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:警巡(警戒巡视);警跸(古代为皇帝出行清道，严加戒备，断绝行人);警惧(警戒恐惧);警边(警戒边境)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“惊”</span>。惊恐;惊动 <span class="encs">[start; be frightened; surprise; shock]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">节循虚而警立。——<span class="smcs">《文选·陆机·叹逝赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“警犹惊也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:警动(惊动;惊恐);警唬(吓唬，恐吓)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>警</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" class="encs">[policeman;police]</span>。如:门警;民警;交通警;巡警;乘警;警廷(警察厅);警棍(警察值勤时拿的棍子);岗警;法警;路警</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 警报 <span class="encs">[alarm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盗贼之警。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每有警。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 敏悟 <span class="encs">[intuitive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太祖少机警，有权数。(少:少年。)——<span class="smcs">《三国志·武帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
