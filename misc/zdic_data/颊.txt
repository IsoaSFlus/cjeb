                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>颊</strong></p><p>頰 <span class="dicpy">jiá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xié</span>)，表示与头有关，夹声。本义:面颊，脸的两侧从眼到下颌部分)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cheek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颊，面旁也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咸其辅颊舌。——<span class="smcs">《易·咸》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“耳目之间称辅颊。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未能开笑颊，先欲换愁魂。——</span><span class="diczx2">戎昱</span><span class="smcs">《闺情》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:颊辅(面颊);颊适(容貌调适，没有迎拒的表情);颊车(下牙床骨;牙慧，蹈袭陈言);颊颧(颊权。颧骨)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 侧，旁边 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">湖桥北颊花坞，水阁西头竹村。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文同</span><span class="smcs">《湖桥》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 堂内正室旁边的房间 <span class="encs">[wing-room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">中和堂</span>上东南颊，独有人间万里风。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《中和堂东南颊下瞰海门洞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/颊带">颊带</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
