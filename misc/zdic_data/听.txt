                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">听</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>听</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">聽</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄧㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用耳朵接受声音：～力。～写。～觉。聆～。洗耳恭～。</li>
	<li>顺从，接受别人的意见：言～计从。</li>
	<li>任凭，随：～任（<span class="dicpy">r&egrave;n </span>）。～凭。～之任之。</li>
	<li>治理；判断：～讼（审理案件）。～政。</li>
	<li>量词，指马口铁密封成筒状以贮藏食物、饮料等：一～可口可乐。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hear; understand; obey, comply</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gehorchen, befolgen (V)​,hören (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sourire (arch.)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">听</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>听</strong></p>
<p>聽 <span class="dicpy">tīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本字从耳德，壬( <span class="dicpy">t&igrave;ng</span>)声。从耳德，即耳有所得，今简化为&ldquo;听&rdquo;。&ldquo;听&rdquo;本义&ldquo;笑貌&rdquo;。从口，斤声。本义:用耳朵感受声音)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[listen;hear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听，聆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天视自我民视，天听自我民听。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;泰誓中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无稽之言勿听。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大禹谟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心不在焉，视而不见，听而不闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行人驻足听。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听妇前致词。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜阑卧听风吹雨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听于庭。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听取蛙声一片。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《西江月》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:听知(听到);听受(聆听，用耳感受声音);听言(道听途说，没有根据的话);听真(听得真切);听直(听取曲直)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 接受，听信，接纳 <span class="encs">[heed;obey;have a receptive ear for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不听寡人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故听之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而听细说。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">壹听</span><span class="diczx2">陵</span>言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开张圣听。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>

<p>又魚斤切，音㹞。</p>

<p>又魚衣切，音沂。與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20CBB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠲻</span>同。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20CBB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠲻</span>嗞，媿貌。</p>

<p>（聽）〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26562.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦕢</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>他定切，音侹。【說文】聆也。【釋名】聽，靜也。靜，然後所聞審也。【書&middot;太甲】聽德惟聰。【儀禮&middot;士昏禮】命之曰：敬恭聽，宗爾父母之言。</p>

<p>又斷也。【禮&middot;王制】司寇正<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>明辟，以聽獄訟。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;大宰】凡邦之小治，則冢宰聽之。【前漢&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>法志】一曰辭聽，二曰色聽，三曰氣聽，四曰耳聽，五曰目聽。</p>
<p>又任也。【前漢&middot;景帝紀】其議民欲徙寬大地者，聽之。</p>
<p>又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>也。【戰國策】請爲王聽東方之處。【註】聽，偵<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>之。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】他丁切【集韻】【韻會】湯丁切【正韻】他經切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音㕔。【集韻】聆也，聽受也。中庭曰聽事，言受事察訟於是。俗作㕔。毛氏曰：漢晉皆作聽，六朝以來始加厂。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶儻陽切，音堂。【蘇轍&middot;讀道藏詩】昔者惠子死，莊子笑自傷。微言不復知，言之使誰聽。</p>
