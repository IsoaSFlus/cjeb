                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>阶</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">階</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄝˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>为了便于上下，用砖石砌成的或就山势凿成的梯形的道：～除（台阶）。～墀（台阶）。～级。～下囚。台～。</li>    <li>等级，层次：～层。官～。军～。音～。</li>    <li>凭借：～缘（凭借，依附）。</li>    <li>由来：～祸。</li>    <li>途径：～段。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>阶</strong></p><p>階、堦 <span class="dicpy">jiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">fù</span>)，表示与地形地势的高低上下有关，皆声。本义:台阶)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[steps]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阶，陛也。字亦作堦。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">御史奏鸡鸣于阶下。——<span class="smcs">《尚书·大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浮堦乘虚。——</span><span class="diczx2">何晏</span><span class="smcs">《景福殿赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三揖至于阶。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·乐论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苔痕上阶绿。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庭阶寂寂。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匍匐阶下。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:阶廊(台阶和走廊);阶檐(台阶与屋檐);阶陛(宫殿的台阶);阶除(台阶);阶墀(宫殿台阶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 梯子 <span class="encs">[stairs]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">捐阶。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“梯也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲释阶而登天兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九章·惜诵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:阶梯</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 官阶，官方的衔头 <span class="encs">[rank]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">但以无阶朝廷。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·匡衡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">文阶二十八阶，武阶二十一阶。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·百官志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:阶衔(官位;官衔);阶次(官位的等级);阶品(官吏的等级品位);阶封(官阶与封号)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 缘由，途径 <span class="encs">[way]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫婚姻，祸福之阶也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语中》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:阶祸(引致祸患，祸患的缘由)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 凭借 <span class="encs">[depend on]</span></p><p><span                           <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">階</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中" target="_blank">戌集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中&amp;bs=阜" target="_blank">阜</a>】
                              階·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/968E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古諧切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居諧切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音皆。【說文】陛也。【玉篇】登堂道也，級也。【書·大禹謨】舞干羽于兩階。</p><p>又【釋名】階，梯也，如梯之有等差也。【禮·喪大記】復有林麓，則虞人設階。無林麓，則狄人設階。【註】階，所乗以升屋者，梯也。</p><p>又【易·繫辭】言語以爲階。【詩·大雅】誰生厲階。</p><p>又【禮·少儀】不得階主。【註】階，上進者。【疏】階是等級。人升階，必上進，故以階爲上進。</p><p>又星名。【前漢·東方朔傳】願<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>泰階六符。【註】泰階，三台也。</p><p>又官階。【南史·張融傳】融不知階級，階級亦可不知融。【唐書·百官志】文階二十八階，武階二十一階。</p><p>又烏階，草名。【爾雅·釋草註】欔，一名烏階。</p><p>又州名。【韻會】西戎別種，白馬氐地，魏武州，唐攺階州。</p><p>又叶居夷切，音基。【詩·小雅】彼何人斯，居河之麋。無拳無勇，職爲亂階。</p><p>又【大雅】懿厥哲婦，爲梟爲鴟。婦有長舌，維厲之階。</p><p>又叶堅奚切，音稽。【班固·西都賦】雖輕迅與僄狡，猶愕眙而不能階。攀井幹而未半，目眴轉而意迷。</p>
