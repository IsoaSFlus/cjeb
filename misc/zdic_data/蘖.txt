                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蘖</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>蘖</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ni&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>树木砍去后从残存茎根上长出的新芽，泛指植物近根处长出的分枝：～枝。分～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> stump, sprout</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Spross, Keim  (S, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> talle</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蘖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蘖</strong></p>
<p>櫱 <span class="dicpy">ni&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，薛声。本义:被砍去或倒下的树木再生的枝芽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tiller]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若颠本之有由蘖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苞有三蘖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;长发》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱</span>注:&ldquo;蘖，旁生萌蘖也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非无萌蘖之生焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寻木起于蘖栽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张衡&middot;东京赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;斩而复生曰蘖。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:槎蘖(砍伐幼林)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 植物的芽 <span class="encs">[sprout]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茶之佳品，芽蘖细微，不可多得。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王观国</span><span class="smcs">《学林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蘖芽(草木萌生的新芽)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>蘖</strong> <span class="dicpy">ni&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;糱&rdquo;。高 <span class="encs">[high;tall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋王</span>筑为蘖台。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;过理篇》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蘖</strong></p>
<p>櫱 <span class="dicpy">ni&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 生芽的米 <span class="encs">[budded rice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蘖，芽米也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 酿酒的曲 <span class="encs">[yeast for wine-making]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以蘖为酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;禁藏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蘖酒(曲酒);蘖曲(酒曲)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                              蘖·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=23" target="_blank">23</a>　·部外筆画：17</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8616.svg">						  <p>【正韻】魚列切，音臬。斫木餘。</p>
<p>又肄生曰蘖。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】姓也。【何氏姓苑】本姓薛，東莞人避仇改之。　【字彙】木櫱字有屮無艸，今从艸，似誤。○按《正韻》亦下从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣎴</span>，非从木。疑卽櫱字之譌。</p>
<p>【集韻】博厄切，音擘。【唐韻】俗檗字。【說文】黃木也。或从薛。【本草】李時珍曰：蘗，木名。義未詳。俗作黃柏者，省寫之譌也。【鮑昭&middot;行路難】剉蘗染黃絲，黃絲歷亂不可治。</p>
<p>又與薛通。詳薜字註。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2384C_9531.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2384C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣡌</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">伐木餘也。</span><span class="swdz">商頌傳曰：櫱，餘也。周南傳曰：肄，餘也，斬而復生曰肄。按肄者，櫱之假借字也。韋昭曰：以株生曰櫱。方言。烈枿餘也。陳鄭之閒曰枿，晉衞之閒曰烈，秦晉之閒曰肄，或曰烈。枿者亦櫱之異文。</span><span class="ywyy">從木獻聲。</span><span class="swdz">五葛切。十五部。按獻聲在十四部，合韵也。</span><span class="ywyy">商書曰：若顚木之有㽕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2384C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣡌</span>。</span><span class="swdz">般庚上文。今尚書作由櫱。本又作枿。馬云：顚木而肄生曰枿。</span></p>

<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2384C_9533.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/233B4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣎴</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2384C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣡌</span>。從木無頭。</span><span class="swdz">謂木禿其上而僅餘根株也。</span></p>
