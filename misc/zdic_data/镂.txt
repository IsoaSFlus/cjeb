                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>镂</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鏤</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lòu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lòu.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄡˋ</span></p><ol><li>雕刻：～花。～刻。～空。～骨铭心（喻感激或牢记不忘）。～月裁云（喻巧夺天工的制作）。～尘吹影（喻徒劳）。</li><li>可供刻镂用的刚坚的铁。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">漏</span>”，孔穴。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> carve, inlay, engrave, tattoo</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> einschnitzen, schnitzen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> graver</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">镂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>镂</strong></p><p>鏤 <span class="dicpy">lòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，娄声。本义:可供雕刻的坚铁)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 金刚钻 <span class="encs">[diamond]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥贡璆、铁、银、镂、砮磬。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“镂，刚铁。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 釜，烹饪用的锅 <span class="encs">[cauldron]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">镂，釜也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“漏”</span>。孔穴 <span class="encs">[hole]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生</span><span class="diczx2">禹</span>于 <span class="diczx2">石纽</span>，虎鼻大耳，两耳参镂。——<span class="smcs">《宋书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>镂</strong></p><p>鏤 <span class="dicpy">lòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 雕刻 <span class="encs">[engrave;carve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">锲而不舍，金石可镂。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">器不彤镂。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流苏金镂鞍。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:镂冰(于冰上雕刻。比喻徒劳而无功);镂板(本指雕刻以印书的木板，引申为雕板印刷);镂花(雕刻花纹);镂金(雕刻金箔用为装饰);镂象(雕刻象牙为饰的车子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 凿通 <span class="encs">[tunnel through; bore]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">镂</span><span class="diczx2">灵山</span>，梁 <span class="diczx2">孙原</span>。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马相如传下》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 铭记 <span class="encs">[engrave on one's mind;always bear in mind]</span>。如:镂心(铭记在心)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 雕金为饰 <span class="encs">[carve and polish]</span>。如:镂膺(马胸前的雕花金属饰品带子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/镂簋朱绂">镂簋朱绂</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/镂刻">镂刻</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/镂空">镂空</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/镂月裁云">镂月裁云</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
