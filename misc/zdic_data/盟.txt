						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>盟</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">méng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/méng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>旧时指宣誓缔约，现指阶级的联合，国与国的联合：～军。～友。同～国。～约。山～海誓。</li><li>指结拜弟兄：～兄。～弟。</li><li>中国内蒙古自治区的行政单位。</li><li>发（誓）：～誓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">盟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>盟</strong> <span class="dicpy">méng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/méng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，下面象个盘盂，中间放着牛耳。古代盟会要割牲歃( <span class="dicpy">shà</span>)血，主盟人手执牛耳，掘穴埋牲。本义:在神前发誓结盟)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ally;alliance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">割牛耳盛朱盘，取其血歃于玉敦。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盟，歃血誓也。——<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌盟载之法。凡邦国有疑会同，则掌其盟约之载，及其礼仪，北面谓明神。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司盟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌盟诅。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·诅祝》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“盟诅，主于要誓，大事曰盟，小事曰诅。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">约信曰誓，涖牲曰盟。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">再会而盟。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子屡盟。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·巧言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为坛而盟。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦伯</span>说，与 <span class="diczx2">郑</span>人盟。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屈完及诸侯盟。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:盟主(古代诸侯盟会中的领袖或主持者;泛指同盟首领或倡导者。喻主要的事物;主宰);盟坛(古代缔结盟约时所设的坛场);盟会(犹会盟。古代诸侯间的集会结盟)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 个人向天发誓，永不变心 <span class="encs">[vow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终待说山盟海誓，这恩情到此非容易。——</span><span class="diczx2">赵长卿</span><span class="smcs">《贺新郎》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:盟文(盟誓的文辞);盟契(盟约。又称盟要，盟信);盟心(盟誓在心);海誓山盟(对着山海盟誓。极言男女相爱，坚贞不渝)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>盟</strong> <span class="dicpy">méng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/méng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 盟约;誓辞<span class="encs">[alliance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与结盟好。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·赤壁之战》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">要盟也，神不听。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孔子世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
