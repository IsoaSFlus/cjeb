                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>髯</strong></p>
<p>髥 <span class="dicpy">r&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从髟，冉声。髟( <span class="dicpy">biāo</span>)，长发下垂的样子。本义:两颊上的长须)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义;也泛指胡须 <span class="encs">[whiskers]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">髥，颊须也。从须从冄，会意，冄亦声。字亦作髥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奋髥抵几。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;朱博传》</span>。注:&ldquo;颊毛也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中峨冠而多髯者为</span><span class="diczx2">东坡</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:髯夫(指脸上多须的人);髯公(指年长多须的人);髯奴(指多须的奴仆);髯佛(称仁慈多须的人);髯胡(须髯和颌下的垂肉);髯翁(指年老多须的人);髯须(指胡子);髯髯然(髯须动摇的样子);白发苍髯</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 须多或须长的人 <span class="encs">[a person with bushy whiskers]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">羽</span>美须髯，故 <span class="diczx2">亮</span>谓之髯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 动物的须 <span class="encs">[beard hair]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">上申之山</span>]</span> 其鸟多当扈，其状如雉，以其髯飞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;西山经》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">郭璞</span>注:&ldquo;髯，咽下须也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;蚺&rdquo;。蟒蛇 <span class="encs">[boa;python]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">越人得髯蛇以为上肴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;精神》</span></span></p>
