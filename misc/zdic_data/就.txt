                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">就</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>就</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄡˋ</span></p><ol><li>凑近，靠近：避难～易。～着灯看书。</li><li>到，从事，开始进入：～位。～业。～寝。～任。～绪。～医。高～。</li><li>依照现有情况或趁着当前的便利，顺便：～近。～便。～事论事。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> just, simply; to come, go to; to approach, near</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> plötzlich, augenblicklich, nur, gerade (emphatisch)​, dann ,vollenden  (V)​,schon, bereits</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> déjà,aussitôt,immédiatement,alors,même si,entreprendre,accomplir,s'approcher de,en fonction de</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">就</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>就</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。京尤会意。<span class="yhcs">“京”</span>意为高，<span class="yhcs">“尤”</span>意为特别。本义:到高处去住)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[move to highland]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">就，就高也。从京从尤。尤，异于凡也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">桂馥</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“此言人就高以居也。”</span> <span class="diczx2">孔广居</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“京，高丘也。古时洪水横流，故高丘之异于凡者人就之。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 靠近;走近;趋向 <span class="encs">[come close to;move towards]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">就，即也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主人就东阶，客就西阶。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不就利，不违害。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不知就先，不知就后。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·大宗师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禹趋就下风，立而问焉。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天地》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫子休就舍。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·说剑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">金就砺则利。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">去故乡而就远兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九章·哀郢》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">还来就菊花。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">孟浩然</span><span class="smcs">《过故人庄》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">就吾求寒衣。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">持就火炀之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">就童子试。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:就书(前往从学);就利(趋利，求利);就第(归回宅第;回家);就和(接近)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 归于 <span class="encs">[belong to;come over and pledge allegiance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">处工就官府，处商就井市。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:就化(归顺，向化);就班(按次序归位);就款(归顺臣服)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 担任;开始从事 <span class="encs">[assume the office of]</span>。如:去就(担任或不担任职务);就列(就位。任职);就事(就职);就田(从事耕种)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 下;搭着吃 <span class="encs">[go with]</span>。如:炒鸡蛋就饭;就菜;花生仁就酒</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 完成;成功 <span class="encs">[accomplish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三窟已就，君姑高枕为乐矣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">组已就而效之，其组异善。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说右上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以就懿德。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瞬息可就。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">指物作诗立就。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span
