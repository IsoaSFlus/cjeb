                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谕</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">諭</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˋ</span></p><ol><li>告诉，使人知道（一般用于上对下）：面～。手～。～旨。晓～。</li><li>明白，古同“<span class="diczx3">喻</span>”，明白，理解。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">喻</span>”，比方。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谕</strong></p><p>諭 <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，俞声。本义:旧时上告下的通称。也指告诉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[instruct;tell]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谕，告也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以谕九税之利。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·掌交》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讶士掌四方之狱讼，谕罪刑于邦国。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·秋官》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:谕葬(圣上发旨的葬仪);面谕;谕知(告谕而使人知晓)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 明白、懂得 <span class="encs">[understand;see]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谕，晓也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">教之以事而谕诸德者也。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·师氏》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其言多当矣，而未谕也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·儒效》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又不能谕其内志。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·文帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先生坐!何至于此!寡人谕矣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谕</strong></p><p>諭 <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 旧时用指上对下的文告、指示 <span class="encs">[instruction]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">这可得便来谕，着</span><span class="diczx2">魏子英</span>拿 <span class="diczx2">沙龙</span>。——<span class="smcs">《小五义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:来谕;手谕;赐谕</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 告诫的言辞 <span class="encs">[edict]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻 <span class="encs">[analogy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请以市谕，市朝则满，夕则虚。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AED.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】羊戍切【集韻】【韻會】兪戍切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音裕。【說文】告也。【類篇】曉也。【韻會】及其未悟，告之使曉。【穀梁傳·桓六年】修敎明諭國道也。【禮·祭義】諭其志意。【疏】使祝官啓告鬼神，曉諭鬼神以志意。【周禮·秋官】訝士掌四方之獄訟，諭罪<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>于邦國。【註】告曉以麗罪，及制<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>之本意。【疏】諭爲曉，故曰告曉。【戰國策】寡人諭矣。【註】諭，曉也。【呂覽·離謂篇】言者以諭意也。【淮南子·主術訓】衰絰菅屨，辟踴哭泣，所以諭哀也。【註】諭，明也。</p><p>又【玉篇】譬諭也。【前漢·賈誼傳】誼追傷屈原，因以自諭。【師古註】諭，譬也。</p><p>又【廣雅】諫也。</p><p>又官名。【唐書·百官志】有左右諭德。</p><p>又縣名。【晉書·地理志】新諭縣，屬荆州安成郡，吳置。【唐書·地理志】招諭縣，屬錦州，垂拱三年置。</p><p>又姓。【廣韻】東晉有諭歸，豫章人，撰《西河記》。何承天云：音樹。</p><p>又【玉篇】他口切，偸上聲。誘也。　【集韻】或作喩。</p>
