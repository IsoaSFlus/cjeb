<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">襖</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄠˇ</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>袄</strong></p>
<p>襖 <span class="dicpy">ǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，夭声。本义:有衬里的上衣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 皮衣之类的御寒衣服 <span class="encs">[fur garment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身上穿着银红撇花半旧大袄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:皮袄;袄子(棉袄的俗称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指上衣<span class="encs">[jacket]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:棉袄;夹袄</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
