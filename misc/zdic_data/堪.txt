                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>堪</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kān.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>能，可以，足以：不～设想。～当重任。～以告慰。</li>
	<li>忍受，能支持：难～。不～一击。狼狈不～。疲惫不～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>adequately capable of, worthy of</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> können, dürfen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> capable,digne de,supporter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">堪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>堪</strong> <span class="dicpy">kān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，甚声。本义:地面高起)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mount]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堪，地突也。谓土之坟起者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堪坏得之，以袭昆仑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;大宗师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堪岩(山形幽深)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指天道 <span class="encs">[law of nature and its development]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堪舆徐行。雄以音知雌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">许君</span>注:&ldquo;堪，天道，舆，地道。盖堪为高处，舆为下处。天高地下之义也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屡堪舆以壁垒兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《甘泉赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;天地总名也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">倏然堪舆变。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆龟蒙</span>、 <span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《开元寺楼看雨联句》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堪舆(天地;风水，亦指风水先生);堪舆家(古时为占候卜筮者之一种，后专称以相地看风水为职业者，俗称&ldquo;风水先生&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>堪</strong> <span class="dicpy">kān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 勉强承受<span class="encs">[困难、痛苦或遭遇]</span> <span class="encs">[bear;endure;can stand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人不堪其忧，回也不改其乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国不堪贰，君将若之何?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未堪家多难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;小毖》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">往攻之，予必使汝大堪之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;非攻下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">口弗堪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">零落难堪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众不能堪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非人世所堪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">更哪堪冷落清秋节。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堪忍(忍受);不堪一击</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 能够;可以 <span class="encs">[can;may]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众云并不堪用，正令烧除。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;捷悟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白璧堪裁且作环。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《和友人戏赠》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堪叹(可叹;可惜);堪可(正好;正可以);堪能(能够。才能;擅长);堪火(方言。险些;几乎);堪称佳作;不堪设想</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>堪</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄎㄢ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>kān <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2552.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">勝任、承受。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「難<b>堪</b>」、「足<b>堪</b>重任」、「不堪一擊」、「情何以<b>堪</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;雍也》：「人不<b>堪</b>其憂，回也不改其樂。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;柳永〈雨霖鈴&middot;寒蟬淒切〉詞：「多情自古傷離別，更那<b>堪</b>、冷落清秋節？」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">可以、能夠。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「堪稱大將」、「不堪設想」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜秋娘〈金縷衣〉詩：「花開<b>堪</b>折直須折，莫待無花空折枝。」</span><span class="gc_yy">五代十國南唐&middot;李煜〈浪淘沙&middot;往事只<b>堪</b>哀〉詞：「往事只<b>堪</b>哀　，對景難排。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中" target="_blank">丑集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中&amp;bs=土" target="_blank">土</a>】
                              堪·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/582A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】口含切【集韻】【韻會】枯含切【正韻】苦含切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音戡。【說文】勝也。</p>
<p>又任也，可也。【家語】子貢曰：吳王爲人猛暴，羣臣不堪。【羊祜讓開府表】臣有何功，可以堪之。</p>
<p>又堪輿，天地總名。【范浚心箴】茫茫堪輿，俯仰無垠。</p>
<p>又【孟康曰】堪輿，神名，造輿宅書者。</p>
<p>又山形奇怪也。【揚雄&middot;蜀都賦】堪當隱倚。</p>
<p>又姓。【風俗通】八元仲堪之後。</p>
<p>又【說文】地突也。【徐鉉註】地穴中出也，據此與龕同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】楚錦切，音墋。土也。一曰不淸澄。　通作勘，戡<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2299F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢦟</span>。別作龕。【正字通】經傳錯互者，因聲近而譌。</p>
