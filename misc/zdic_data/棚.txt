                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>棚</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">péng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/péng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄥˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 用竹木搭成架子，上面覆盖席、布等做成的遮蔽风雨日光的东西或简陋的小屋：天～。帐～。窝～。～车。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">棚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>棚</strong> <span class="dicpy">péng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/péng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，朋声。本义:用竹、木搭成的篷架或小屋)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">棚，栈也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，编木横竖为之皆曰栈曰棚，今谓于上以蔽下者曰棚。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span>曰:‘板阁曰栈，连阁曰棚。’析言之也。 <span class="diczx2">许</span>云:‘棚，栈也。’浑言之也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高棚跨路，广幕陵云。——<span class="smcs">《隋书·柳彧传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:窝棚(简陋的小屋);棚井(棚户进庐);棚摊(有棚子的货摊);棚阁(用竹、木等搭建的篷架、陋屋);棚杠(旧时为丧家承办搭棚，扛抬灵柩、冥器等事宜者)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 楼阁。我国传统楼房的一种，供远眺、游憩、藏书、供佛等用。也指类似棚阁的结构与设施 <span class="encs">[pavilion;kiosk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">连阁曰棚。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">负米往来七十步，其二十步上下棚除。——<span class="smcs">《九章算术·商功章》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">刘薇</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“棚，阁也。除邪道也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">棚阁，<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span>:楼阁也。谓重屋复道者也。——</span><span class="diczx2">玄应</span><span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:棚阁(作战时，在城上用木架设的瞭望台);棚帘(棚阁珠帘)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 朋党，帮派 <span class="encs">[clique]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">玄宗</span>时，士子殷盛，每岁进士到省者常不减千余人，在馆诸生更相造诣，互结朋党，以相渔夺，号之为棚。推声望者为棚头。——<span class="smcs">《封氏闻见记·贡举》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 清末陆军编制，兵士十四人为一棚 <span class="encs">[squad]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">钱塘县</span>回了抚台，派了两棚兵带了洋枪出去剿狗。——<span class="smcs">《二刻拍案惊奇》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div                     <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">棚</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
