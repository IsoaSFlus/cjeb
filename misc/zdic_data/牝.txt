						<p>● <strong>牝</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>雌性的鸟或兽，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3"><a href="/hans/牡">牡</a></span>&rdquo;相对：～牛。～马。～鸡。</li>
	<li>锁孔。</li>
	<li>溪谷：&ldquo;丘陵为牡，溪谷为～&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">牝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>牝</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从牛，匕(<span class="dicpy">bǐ</span>)声。依甲骨文，&ldquo;匕&rdquo;为雌性动物的标志。本义:雌性的禽兽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[female bird or animal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牝，畜母也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利牝马之贞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畜牝牛，吉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;离》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牝鸡之晨，惟家之索。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;牧誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:牝朝(唐人称武后当政为牝朝);牝牡(雌雄两性)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指阴性的事物 <span class="encs">[negative]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谿谷为牝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;本命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肾者，牝藏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;水热穴论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牝常以静胜牡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 锁孔 <span class="encs">[lock hole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">键，牡;闭，牝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span></p>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">雌性動物。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「牝牡」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;牛部》：「<b>牝</b>，畜母也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子&middot;時則》：「游<b>牝</b>別其群。」</span></p></li>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">谿谷。</span><span class="gc_yy">《大戴禮記&middot;易本命》：「高者為生，下者為死；丘陵為牡，谿谷為<b>牝</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈贈崔立之評事〉詩：「可憐無益費精神，有似黃金擲虛<b>牝</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">女子陰戶。</span><span class="gc_yy">《二刻拍案驚奇&middot;卷三五》：「馬氏晚間取湯澡<b>牝</b>，正要上床與蔡鳳鳴快活。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">雌性的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>牝</b>馬」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;牧誓》：「牝雞無晨。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/725D.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2090E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤎</span>【唐韻】毗忍切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】婢忍切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29AAF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩪯</span>。【說文】畜母也。从牛，匕聲。【玉篇】牝牡也。【易&middot;坤卦】利牝馬之貞。【書&middot;牧誓】牝雞無晨。【詩&middot;鄘風】騋牝三千。【禮&middot;月令】遊牝於牧。</p>
<p>又【古詩】哀壑叩虛牝。【韓愈&middot;贈崔立之詩】有似黃金擲虛牝。【註】牝，谿谷也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】補履切，音匕。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>婢善切，音楩。亦畜母也。【書&middot;牧誓釋文】徐音扶忍反。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】扶履切【集韻】並履切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2465E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤙞</span>。義同。</p>
