                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">举</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>举</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">舉</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>向上抬，向上托：～头。～手。～重。～棋不定。</li>
	<li>动作行为：～止。轻而易～。</li>
	<li>发起，兴办：～义。～办。创～。</li>
	<li>提出：～要。～例。</li>
	<li>推选，推荐：推～。荐～。</li>
	<li>全：～国。～世。～家。</li>
	<li>古代指科举取士：科～。～人。一～成名。</li>
	<li>攻克：&ldquo;一战而～鄢、郢&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> raise, lift up; recommend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> heben, hochheben  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> lever,soulever,choisir,élire,citer,action,exploit,entier,tout</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">举</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>举</strong></p>
<p>舉、擧 <span class="dicpy">jǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形，从手，舉( <span class="dicpy">yǔ</span>)声。本义:双手托物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[raise;lift up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举，对举也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举，擎也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其坚不能自举也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举所佩玉玦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:举桉(桉同&ldquo;案&rdquo;。借指夫妻相敬);举案(举起托盘以进奉食品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拿起;提起 <span class="encs">[hold up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举全</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>之地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举以予人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:举白(举杯喝尽。干杯);举踵(举起脚跟。比喻切望);举纲持领(提起网的总绳，网眼就张开了;提起皮衣领子一抖，衣上的毛就都顺了。比喻办事要抓关键部分)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 飞，飞起;飘动 <span class="encs">[fly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">九万里风鹏正举。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《渔家傲》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:举翮(展翅起飞);风举(随风飘动)</span></p>
