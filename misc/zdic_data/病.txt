						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>病</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bìng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>生物体发生不健康的现象：疾～。～症。～例。～痛。～情。～源。～愈。～变。～危。～逝。～榻。～残。</li><li>缺点，错误：语～。通～。弊～。</li><li>损害，祸害：祸国～民。</li><li>不满，责备：诟～。</li><li>烦躁，担忧：“郑人～之”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">病</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>病</strong> <span class="dicpy">bìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。疒形丙声。本义:上古时指重病)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be seriously ill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">病，疾加也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疾病外内皆埽。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·既夕礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“疾甚曰病。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坎为心病。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疾病而立之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故得病寝衽，畏惧鬼至。——</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡·订鬼》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与死人同病者，不可生也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·孤愤》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行年四十而有内热之病以死。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·达生》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:病革(病势危急将死);病亟(病革)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 生理上或心理上不正常的状态 <span class="encs">[illness;sickness;disease]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君之病在肌肤。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">性伤谓之病。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">张良</span>多病，未尝特将也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·留侯世家》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:病笃(病势沉重);病疽(毒疮类疾病);病困(受疾病的困扰);病革(病势危急将死);病亟(病革)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 缺点，毛病，瑕疵 <span class="encs">[fault;defect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不如</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>，不如 <span class="diczx2">周公</span>，吾之病也。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《原毁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至于不孚之病，则尤不才为甚。——</span><span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人皆嗤吾固陋，吾不以为病。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然今日庭诘弘，诚中弘之病。——<span class="smcs">《史记·平津侯主父列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">砭切政病。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·杜希全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:病累(指文章中有瑕疵或累赘的字句);语病;通病(一般都有的缺点);毛病(缺点);弊病(事情上的毛病;弊端)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 疾苦，痛苦 <span class="encs">[hardships;painful;sufferings]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长其孤，问其病。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>病</strong> <span
