                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谪</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>谪</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">謫</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄜˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>谴责，责备：众人交～。</li>	<li>封建时代特指官吏降职，调往边外地方：～迁。～降（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng </span>）。～戍。～居。贬～。</li>	<li>变异：&ldquo;庚午之日，日始有～&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>charge, blame; disgrace; demote</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> bemängeln ,schänden, Blamage  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>谪</strong></p>
<p>謫、讁 <span class="dicpy">zh&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，啻( <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span>)声。本义:有意指摘，责备)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[blame;censure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谪，罚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罚罪者曰谪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国子谪我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则自取谪于日月之灾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善言无瑕谪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷年不相谪发。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;力命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公会</span><span class="diczx2">齐侯</span>于 <span class="diczx2">泺</span>，遂及 <span class="diczx2">文姜</span>如 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>。 <span class="diczx2">齐侯</span>通焉。公谪之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公十八年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">室人交遍谪我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;北门》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谪疑(指摘怀疑);谪我(谴责我);谪骂(责骂);谪谴(谴责);众口交谪</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 降职并外放 <span class="encs">[exile;banish;relegate a high official to a minor post in an outlying district]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">谊</span>既以谪去，意不自得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谪守</span><span class="diczx2">巴陵郡</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">只因劝酒蟠桃会，误犯天条谪翠微。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《封神演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谪降(降级下放到边远地方);谪发(发配边疆);谪官(谪宦。谪吏。被贬降的官吏)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>谪</strong></p>
<p>謫 <span class="dicpy">zh&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 被罚戍边的罪人 <span class="encs">[a high official to minor post in an outlying district]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徙谪实之初县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 缺点;过失 <span class="encs">[fault; mistake]</span></p>
