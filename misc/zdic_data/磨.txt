                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>磨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mó.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄛˊ</span></p><ol><li>摩擦：～刀。～墨。～练。～砺（摩擦使锐利，喻经受磨练）。～合。研～。～漆画。</li><li>阻碍，困难：～难（<span class="dicpy">nàn</span>）。好事多～。</li><li>消耗，消灭：～损。～耗。～灭。</li><li>拖延，耗时间：～缠。～功夫。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>磨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mò.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>粉碎粮食的工具：石～。电～。～盘。～坊（亦作“磨房”）。推～。</li><li>用磨将粮食加工成所需的状态：～面。～米。～豆腐。</li><li>掉转：这个胡同太窄，～不过车来。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">磨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>磨</strong> <span class="dicpy">mó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石，麻声。本义:磨治石器)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如切如磋，如琢如磨。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·淇奥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">切磋琢磨，乃成宝器。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·量知》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不曰坚乎，磨而不磷。——<span class="smcs">《论语·阳货》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:切磋琢磨(加工骨称<span class="yhcs">“切”</span>，加工象牙称<span class="yhcs">“磋”</span>，加工玉称<span class="yhcs">“琢”</span>，加工石称<span class="yhcs">“磨”</span>);磨渲(磨治);磨拭(琢磨);磨琢(犹琢磨);磨斫(雕琢;雕饰)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用磨料磨物体使光滑、锋利或达到其他目的 <span class="encs">[polish;sharpen]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小弟闻姊来，磨刀霍霍向猪羊。——<span class="smcs">《木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:磨镜石(传说 <span class="diczx2">轩辕氏</span>铸镜时用以磨镜的石头);磨刀雨(即磨刀水);磨刀水(旧指农历五月十三日所下的雨);磨刀霍霍(磨刀声);磨袭砥砺(四种质地和颜色不同的磨石)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 磨擦 <span class="encs">[rub;wear]</span> 。如:磨拳擦掌(形容斗志昂扬，跃跃欲试的样子);磨牙吮血(磨利牙齿，吮吸鲜血);磨沙(指沙入蚌壳，与蚌肉相摩擦)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 磨炼 <span class="encs">[temper;steel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大开明堂受朝贺，诸侯剑佩鸣相磨。——</span><span
