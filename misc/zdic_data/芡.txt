                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>芡</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>一年生水草，茎叶有刺，亦称“鸡头”：～实（芡的果实；种子的仁可食，经碾磨制成淀粉。亦称“鸡头米”）。～粉。</li><li>烹调时用淀粉加水调成的浓汁：勾～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>芡</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，欠声。本义:水生植物。又名鸡头)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 即<span class="yhcs">“芡实”</span>，俗称<span class="yhcs">“鸡头”</span> <span class="encs">[Gordon euryale]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芡，鸡头也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按，花似鸡冠，实苞如鸡首，故名。生于池沼中的一种一年生大水草，体表有刺，叶圆而大，浮于水面，花茎伸长于水面上，顶生一花，紫色，浆果球形，果内胚乳白粉质，可食用。如:芡珠(芡的子实)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 做菜时用芡粉或淀粉调成的汁 <span class="encs">[sauce made of the seed power of Gordon euryale or starch]</span>。如:勾芡</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芡粉">芡粉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芡实">芡实</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
