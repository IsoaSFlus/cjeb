                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>衙</strong> <span class="dicpy">yá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从行，吾声。( <span class="dicpy">yú</span>)本义:列队行进的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 旧时官署之称 <span class="encs">[local authorities;government office in old China]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸州县长官在公衙亦准此。——</span><span class="diczx2">张昭远</span><span class="smcs">《旧唐书·仪卫志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衙，衙府也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衙门，本牙门之讹，<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>谓之旌门，</span><span class="diczx2">郑氏</span>司常注所云，巡狩兵车之会，皆建太常是也，其旗两边刻绘如牙状，故亦曰牙旗，后时因谓营门曰雅门。——<span class="smcs">《陔余丛考》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每日衙门虚寂，无复诉讼者。——<span class="smcs">《北齐书·宋世良传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:衙头(金人军队的统帅部);衙职(官衙中的职役);衙庭(官署的庭堂);衙堂(官署的大堂);衙里(衙署内);衙子(在衙门中当差的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 排列成行的事物 <span class="encs">[sth.arranging in column]</span>。如:柳衙;松衙</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 唐代谓天子所居之处 <span class="encs">[court]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">群臣始朝于宣政衙。——</span><span class="diczx2">张昭远</span><span class="smcs">《旧唐书·仪卫志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:衙队(宫廷卫队)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>衙</strong> <span class="dicpy">yá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 衙参 <span class="encs">[paying respect to a superior]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">病卧四更后，愁闻报早衙。——</span><span class="diczx2">李洞</span><span class="smcs">《江干即事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:衙日(衙参之日);衙喏(衙参时兵卒的声诺)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 坐衙，官吏坐堂审案 <span class="encs">[hold court trial]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">值城隍早衙，喊冤以投。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
