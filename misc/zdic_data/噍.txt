						<p>● <strong>噍</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˋ</span></p>

<ol>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">噍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>噍</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 咀嚼;吃 <span class="encs">[chew;eat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小饭而亟之，数噍毋为口容。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;少仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦呥呥而噍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;荣辱》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:噍咀(咀嚼);噍嚼(咀嚼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;譙&rdquo;。责备 <span class="encs">[blame]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:噍让(谴责);噍骂(责骂);噍呵(严厉责备)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>噍</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>犹言噍类。特指活着的人或动物 <span class="encs">[living beings]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">襄城</span>无噍类。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪》</span>。注:&ldquo;青州俗呼无孑遗为无噍类。&rdquo;</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">噍</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】
                              噍·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/564D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】才笑切【正韻】在笑切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音誚。【說文】齧也。【玉篇】嚼也。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】襄城無噍類。【註】如淳曰：噍，祚笑反，無復有活而噍食者也。【荀子&middot;榮辱篇】亦呥呥而噍，鄉鄉而飽巳矣。【王充&middot;論衡】口齒以噍食。【集韻】或作嚼。</p>
<p>又【集韻】子肖切，音醮。義同。【禮&middot;少儀】數噍毋爲口容。○按《釋文》噍又作嚼，子笑反，又在笑反，二音皆可讀。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】卽消切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】兹消切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音焦。【廣韻】啁噍，聲也。【集韻】鳥聲。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;樂記】其哀心感者，其聲噍以殺。【註】噍，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20D2B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠴫</span>也。【釋文】噍，子遙反，謂急也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】慈焦切，音樵。義同。【禮&middot;樂記&middot;噍殺釋文】徐在堯反，沈子堯反。○按《釋文》凡三音，獨沈音子堯反，諸韻皆不收。</p>
<p>又【集韻】將由切，音遒。燕雀聲。【禮&middot;三年問】至于燕雀猶有啁噍之頃焉，然後乃能去之。【釋文】噍，子流反。【集韻】通作啾。</p>
<p>又【集韻】疾雀切，音皭。本作嚼。詳嚼字註。</p>
