                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>患</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄢˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>忧虑：～得～失。忧～。</li>	<li>灾祸：～难。祸～。隐～。防～未然。</li>	<li>害病：～病。～者。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">患</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>患</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，串声。本义:担忧，忧虑</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[worry about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">患，忧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不患人之不已知，患其不能也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;宪问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">书文止于一者谓之忠，持二中者谓之患，患人之忠，不一者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋&middot;繁露》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">患货之不足。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">患</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>兵之来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">患志之不立。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;自新》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何患不能。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:患累(忧患);患御(害怕服兵役);患忧(忧患);患失(生怕失去)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 憎恶;讨厌;厌烦 <span class="encs">[detest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">患，恶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">戊</span>不好学患 <span class="diczx2">申公</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;申公传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:患苦(厌恶);患毒(痛恨);患恶(厌恶);患忌(嫌忌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 苦于 <span class="encs">[suffer from]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">患，苦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">近日南方赋重，北方患徭多，民困官贫，急宜省事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《清史稿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 害病 <span class="encs">[suffer from]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:患肝炎;患麻疹;患重伤风</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>患</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 祸患，祸害 <span class="encs">[calamity;trouble;peril]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟事事，乃其有备，有备无患。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;说命中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">论伦无患。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>

    <div class="yczsl-content">
      <div class="yczsl-title">初中古诗词</div>
      <div class="yczsl-description">
			<div class="gscdl">入则无法家拂士，出则无敌国外<span>患</span>者，国恒亡。</div><div class="gscdr">《孟子·告子下》《生于忧患 死于安乐》</div><div class="gscdl">然后知生于忧<span>患</span>，而死于安乐也。[1]</div><div class="gscdr">《孟子·告子下》《生于忧患 死于安乐》</div><div class="gscdl">生亦我所欲，所欲有甚于生者，故不为苟得也；死亦我所恶，所恶有甚于死者，故<span>患</span>有所不辟也。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孟子 《鱼我所欲也》</div><div class="gscdl">如使人之所欲莫甚于生，则凡可以得生者何不用也？使人之所恶莫甚于死者，则凡可以辟<span>患</span>者何不为也？由是则生而有不用也，由是则可以辟<span>患</span>而有不为也。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孟子 《鱼我所欲也》</div><div class="gscdl">既加冠，益慕圣贤之道，又<span>患</span>无硕师、名人与游，尝趋百里外，从乡之先达执经叩问。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div><div class="gscdl">今虽耄老，未有所成，犹幸预君子之列，而承天子之宠光，缀公卿之后，日侍坐备顾问，四海亦谬称其氏名，况才之过于余者乎？今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪稍之供，父母岁有裘葛之遗，无冻馁之<span>患</span>矣；坐大厦之下而诵《诗》《书》无奔走之劳矣；有司业、博士为之师，未有问而不告，求而不得者也；凡所宜有之书，皆集于此，不必若余之手录，假诸人而后见也。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
