                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>剽</strong></p><p>勡 <span class="dicpy">piāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，票声。本义:抢劫)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rob]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勡，劫人也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，古书多以剽为之。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白昼大都之中，勡吏而夺之金。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·贾谊传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俱攻剽为群盗。——<span class="smcs">《史记·酷吏传》</span>。索隐:劫也。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:剽掠(抢劫;掠夺);剽攻(抢劫;掠夺);剽拂(劫掠);剽夺(掳掠);剽虏(掳掠);剽伤(掠夺残害)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[中医]</span>∶砭刺 <span class="encs">[pierce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剽，砭刺也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“砭者，以石刺病也;刺者，直伤也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剽其通。——<span class="smcs">《灵枢经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 攻击 <span class="encs">[attack]</span>。如:剽剥(击杀);剽杀(犹劫杀);剽戮(杀戮)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 窃取，抄袭 <span class="encs">[plagiarize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟古于词必己出，降而不能乃剽贼。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《南阳樊绍述墓志铭》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:剽拾(犹抄袭);剽剥(抄袭窃取);剽掇(抄袭，摘录);剽夺(抄袭);剽裂(摘抄;窃取);剽贼(抄袭);剽缀(抄袭拼凑);剽拟(抄袭摹拟);剽摸(抄袭模仿)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“漂”</span>。浮，浮流 <span class="encs">[float]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五剽之状。——<span class="smcs">《管子·地员》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>剽</strong> <span class="dicpy">piāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 勇猛 <span class="encs">[intrepid]</span>。如:剽轻(矫健勇猛);剽急(勇猛敏捷);剽姚(勇猛劲疾);剽便(谓勇猛敏捷的士卒);剽和(骁勇)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 轻捷 <span class="encs">[agile]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">钟其中者谓之剽。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孙</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“声轻痴。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且其人剽悍。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·陈汤传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:剽迫(轻疾)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 轻浮，轻薄 <span class="encs">[frivolous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其俗剽轻，易发怒，地薄，寡于积聚。——<span
