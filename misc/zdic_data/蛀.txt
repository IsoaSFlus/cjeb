                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蛀</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>〔～虫〕a.指能咬树干、衣服、书籍和粮食等的小虫，如天牛、衣鱼、米象；b.喻侵犯国家、公众利益的坏人。</li><li>被虫子咬坏：书本被虫～了。～蚀。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蛀</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，主声。本义:蛀虫)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[moth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蠹侵嘉树，蛀耗米珠。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">顾大韶</span><span class="smcs">《又后虱赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蛀食(谓蛀虫咬吃);蛀心虫(二化螟、三化螟、大螟、玉米螟等幼虫的统称);蛀书蠹(蛀蚀书卷的蠹鱼。借指只知啃书本读书的人);蛀书虫(蛀书蠹)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用同<span class="yhcs">“疰”</span><span class="encs">[chronic infectious disease]</span>。如:蛀夏(人至夏季，精神倦怠，胃纳不佳，称蛀夏);蛀船(不适应乘船);蛀轿(不适应乘轿)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>蛀</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蠹蚀;被蛀虫咬坏 <span class="encs">[bore (by borer or any wood-boring insect)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">木犀蛀者，用芝麻带壳悬树上。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《格物粗谈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蛀孔(虫蛀咬后留下的孔洞);蛀蚀(因被虫蛀而受损伤。常比喻伤害);蛀牙(龋齿的俗称);蛀齿(即龋齿。齿质受腐蚀的病)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蛀虫">蛀虫</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蛀蚀">蛀蚀</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
