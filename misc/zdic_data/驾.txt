                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>驾</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">駕</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jià  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jià.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄚˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>把车套在牲口身上，使拉（车或农具）：～车。～辕。</li>    <li>古代车乘的总称，亦特指帝王的车，转指帝王：车～。法～（帝王车乘的一种）。</li>    <li>使开动，操纵：～驶。～机。～驭（亦作“驾御”）。</li>    <li>在……上面，超出：～云。凌～（高出、压倒谁或什么）。</li>    <li>对人的敬辞：大～。尊～。劳～。～临。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">驾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>驾</strong></p><p>駕 <span class="dicpy">jià</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jià.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，加声。本义:以轭加于马上)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[draw a cart;harness]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驾，马在轭中。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其乱至矣，不可以驾矣。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·贵因》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驾言兮焉求。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:驾御(驾驭。驾驶车马;引申为驱驶、控制)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 把鞍加在马背上 <span class="encs">[saddle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一马驾一鞍，一女驾一男。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">陈罴</span><span class="smcs">《跃鲤记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 骑;乘 <span class="encs">[ride]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驾，乘也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郤献</span>子闻之，驾往救之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·难一》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:驾士(导引皇帝坐车的人);驾龙(仙道乘龙飞行);驾跨(骑乘);驾鹤西游(死的婉称)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 操纵;驾驶 <span class="encs">[drive;sail;pilot]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晓驾炭车辗冰辙。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驾而宿于朝房。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《洹词·记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:驾飞机;驾轮船;驾着一叶舟;驾长(船长;对船主的尊称);驾娘(划船的女子;女船工)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 用胳膊支撑 <span class="encs">[hold;on shoulder]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驾着鹰雕。——<span class="smcs">《武王伐纣平话》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:驾鹰走犬(带上鹰犬);驾祸害人(制造祸端，加害他人);驾景朝元(魂归天上)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 抵挡 <span class="encs">[keep off;shelter                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上" target="_blank">亥集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上&amp;bs=馬" target="_blank">馬</a>】
                              駕·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/99D5.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2989F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩢟</span>【唐韻】古訝切【集韻】【韻會】居迓切【正韻】居亞切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音嫁。【說文】馬在軛中。</p><p>又馭也。【禮·曲禮】君車將駕。【正字通】唐制，天子居曰衙，行曰駕。</p><p>又車乗也。漢制，六駕屬車八十一乗。【後漢·輿服志】天子出有大駕，有法駕，有小駕。</p><p>又別駕，官名。【通典】漢別駕，從事史一人，刺史行部別乗一乗傳車，故稱曰別駕。唐攺郡丞爲別駕，德宗時復省，今府通判是也。</p><p>又具車馬曰駕。【前漢·高帝紀】詔賢士大夫能從我游者，郡守身勸爲之駕，遣詣丞相。</p><p>又騰駕也。【揚子·法言】仲尼駕說者也。如將復駕其所說，莫若使諸儒金口而木舌。【註】仲尼乗行而贊述之。金口木舌，鐸也。言莫如使諸儒宣揚之也。</p><p>又陵駕也。【左傳·昭元年】子木之信稱於諸侯，猶詐晉而駕焉。【註】駕猶陵也。</p><p>又人名。【左傳】魯大夫榮駕鵞。</p><p>又【集韻】居牙切，音嘉。義同。</p><p>又叶古慕切，音故。【古逸詩·驪駒歌】驪駒在門，僕夫具存。驪駒在路，僕夫整駕。</p>
