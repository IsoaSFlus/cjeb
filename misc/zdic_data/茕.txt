                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>茕</strong></p><p>煢 <span class="dicpy">qióng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qióng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 回旋着飞 <span class="encs">[circling in the air]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茕，回飞也。从凡，煢省声。鸟回转疾飞曰茕。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 孤独，无兄弟 <span class="encs">[solitary;lonely]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无虐茕独。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“单无兄弟也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哀此茕独。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·正月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫何茕独而不余听。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“孤也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:茕子(孤儿);茕孑(孤单);茕困(孤苦);茕妻(寡妇);茕疚(因孤单而感忧伤);茕居(寡居)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 悲愁 <span class="encs">[sad]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">神茕茕以遥思兮，…。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/茕茕">茕茕</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/茕茕孑立">茕茕孑立</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
