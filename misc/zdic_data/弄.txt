						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>弄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&ograve;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄨㄥˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>玩耍，把玩：摆～。玩～。～臣（帝王所亲近狎昵的臣子）。～潮儿。戏～。～瓦（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">瓦</span>&rdquo;是原始的纺锤，古代把它给女孩子玩，意为生女儿）。～璋（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">璋</span>&rdquo;是一种玉器，古代把它给男孩子玩。意为生儿子）。</li>
	<li>做，干：～假成真。～明白。</li>
	<li>设法取得：～点钱花。</li>
	<li>搅扰：这事～得人心惶惶。</li>
	<li>耍，炫耀：搔首～姿。</li>
	<li>不正当地使用：～权。～手段。捉～。</li>
	<li>奏乐或乐曲的一段、一章：～琴。梅花三～。</li>
	<li>古代百戏乐舞中指扮演角色或表演节目。</li>
</ol>

	<li>方言，小巷，胡同：～堂。里～。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弄</strong></p>
<p>衖 <span class="dicpy">l&ograve;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 宫中别道 <span class="encs">[lane in palace]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:东弄;西弄</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 〈方〉∶弄堂，小巷 <span class="encs">[lane]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:里弄;弄口(出入弄堂的地方);弄唐(小巷);弄堂房子</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">n&ograve;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> ;&ldquo;衖&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;ng</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/弄堂">弄堂</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弄</strong></p>
<p>挵 <span class="dicpy">n&ograve;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。上为&ldquo;玉&rdquo;，下为&ldquo;廾&rdquo;(即廾， <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span>)。指双手玩赏玉器。本义:用手把玩;玩弄)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[play around with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弄，玩也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载弄之璋，载弄之瓦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;斯干》</span></span></p>
