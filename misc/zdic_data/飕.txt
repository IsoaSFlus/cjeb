                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>飕</strong></p><p>颼 <span class="dicpy">sōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 象声词，同<span class="yhcs">“嗖”</span> <span class="encs">[whizz]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 形容风雨的声音。如:飕飗(风声);飕飂(风声);飕飋(风声);飕的(形容行动如风)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 形容箭离弦之声</p><p><span class="diczx1">望着为头领的一个马军，飕地一箭，只见翻筋斗射下马去。——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 小风 <span class="encs">[breeze]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">微风曰飕，小风曰飕。——<span class="smcs">《初学记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 寒冷;清凉。也单用作<span class="yhcs">“飕”</span> <span class="encs">[cold]</span>。如:背上有些冷飕飕了</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>飕</strong></p><p>颼 <span class="dicpy">sōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>〈方〉∶风吹 <span class="encs">[blow]</span>。如:洗的衣服飕干了</p><p><span class="diczx1">论今星灿灿，考古寒飕飕。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《中送冀处士东游》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/飕飕">飕飕</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/飕飕作响">飕飕作响</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
