                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>新</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>刚有的，刚经验到的；初始的，没有用过的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">旧</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">老</span>&rdquo;相对：～生。～鲜。～奇。～贵。～绿。～星。～秀。～闻。～陈代谢。</li>
	<li>性质改变得更好，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">旧</span>&rdquo;相对：改过自～。推陈出～。</li>
	<li>不久以前，刚才：～近。</li>
	<li>表示一种有异于旧质的状态和性质：～时代。～社会。～观念。～思维。</li>
	<li>称结婚时的人或物：～娘。～郎。～房。</li>
	<li>中国新疆维吾尔自治区的简称。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">新</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>新</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从斤，从木，辛声。据甲骨文，左边是木，右边是斧子。指用斧子砍伐木材。本义:用斧子砍伐木材。&ldquo;新&rdquo;是&ldquo;薪&rdquo;的本字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 砍伐树木 <span class="encs">[fell (trees)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新，取木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且彼亦知&ldquo;新&rdquo;之为义乎?衣之始裁为之&ldquo;初&rdquo;，木之始伐谓之&ldquo;新&rdquo;。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">章炳麟</span><span class="smcs">《论承用&ldquo;维新&rdquo;二字之荒谬》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 更新，使之新 <span class="encs">[renew;make new]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如何新畬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;臣工》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旧染污俗，咸与唯新。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;胤征》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:新民(使民更新);新美(刷新使之美好);新醮(改嫁);新梢(新长出的树梢)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>新</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 初次出现。与&ldquo;旧&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[new]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于彼新田。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;采芑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宴尔新昏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;谷风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新台有洒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;新台》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谁家新燕啄春呢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《钱塘湖春行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变易新声。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故日新。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="diczx2"><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新夷目。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:新板洋钱(指清代机铸的雕有龙形图案的银元);新发子硎(喻初入学的学生);新文(新闻;新鲜事儿;过分追求新奇的文章;时文)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 新洁;新鲜;清新 <span class="encs">[fresh]</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>新</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄒㄧㄣ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>xīn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4049.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">沒有使用過的。與「舊」相對。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>新</b>辦法」、「<b>新</b>衣」、「<b>新</b>筆」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">剛開始的、始出現的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「新芽」、「新生兒」、「<b>新</b>品種」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">泛指一切新的人、事、物、知識等。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「履<b>新</b>」、「汰舊換<b>新</b>」、「溫故知<b>新</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">剛收成的農作物。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;月令》：「是月也，農乃登穀，天子嘗<b>新</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷一一八&middot;宋務光傳》：「家無接<b>新</b>之儲，國乏俟荒之蓄。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">朝代名。參見「<a href='/hans/新莽' target=_blank>新莽</a> 」條。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">大陸地區新疆省的簡稱。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋時晉國有新穆子。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">改進或使改變，而成為新的、好的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「改過自<b>新</b>」、「一新耳目」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;胤征》：「舊染汙俗，咸與惟<b>新</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;大學》：「苟日<b>新</b>、日日<b>新</b>、又日<b>新</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">不久前、剛才。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>新</b>寫的字」、「<b>新</b>買的衣服」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;不苟》：「<b>新</b>浴者振其衣，<b>新</b>沐者彈其冠。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
