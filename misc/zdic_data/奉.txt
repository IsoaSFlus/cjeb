						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>奉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fèng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fèng.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>恭敬地用手捧着：～觞（举杯祝酒）。～献。～祀。～承（恭维，讨好。“<span class="diczx3">承</span>”读轻声）。</li><li>尊重，遵守：～公守法。～为圭臬（把某些言论或事物当作准则）。～行故事（按老规定办事）。</li><li>敬辞，用于自己的举动涉及对方时：～告。～还（<span class="dicpy">huán </span>）。～陪。～劝。～迎（ａ．敬辞，迎接；ｂ．奉承）。～赠。</li><li>信仰：信～。素～佛教。</li><li>供养，伺候：～养。供～。侍～。</li><li>姓。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">俸</span>”，薪金。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>奉</strong> <span class="dicpy">fèng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fèng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，从手，从収( <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span>，双手)，最上面像一串玉或树盖子。是用手捧物的意思。本义:两手恭敬地捧着。后作<span class="yhcs">“捧”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hold in both hands with respect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奉，承也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">黔敖</span>左奉食，右执饮。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">常山王</span>奉头鼠窜。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·蒯通传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">项伯</span>即入见 <span class="diczx2">沛公</span>， <span class="diczx2">沛公</span>奉卮酒为寿，约为婚姻。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣愿奉璧往使。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">荆轲</span>奉 <span class="diczx2">樊於期</span>头函，而 <span class="diczx2">秦武阳</span>奉地图匣，以次进。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:奉书;奉地图;奉手(捧握长者之手)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 恭敬地接受 <span class="encs">[receive with respect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受任于败军之际，奉命于危难之间。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">近者奉辞伐罪，旌麾南指，</span><span class="diczx2">刘琮</span>束手。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:奉教(接受教导);奉委(接受委任);奉制(接受天子的命令);奉玄(信奉道教)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 进献，下对上送东西 <span class="encs">[offer;present]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请奉盆缻</span><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>，以相娱乐。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玉斗一双，再拜奉大将军足下。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:奉上新书一册;奉纳(交纳);奉呈(奉献，献上);双手奉上;奉贽(进献见面礼品)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 送;给予，赐予 <span class="encs">[give;grant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太子乃使人以千金奉</span><span class="diczx2">庄子</span>， <span class="diczx2">庄子</span>弗受。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·说剑》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奉之弥繁，侵之愈急。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奉违</span><span
