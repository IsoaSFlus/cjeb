						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>眉</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄟˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>眼上额下的毛：～毛。～宇（两眉上面的地方）。～心。～目。～寿（长寿）。～睫。扬～吐气。</li>
	<li>书页上端的空白：书～。～批。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">眉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>眉</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文，小篆字形。象目上毛形。本义:眉毛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 眼上方的弓或嵴，亦指生长在这条嵴上的眉毛 <span class="encs">[brow;eyebrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">眉，目上毛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">螓首蛾眉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;硕人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">报答平生未展眉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《遗悲怀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:眉心(双眉之间);眉间广尺(两眉间隔宽有一尺，表示人的相貌奇特);眉泉(眉头);眉寸(双眉之间);眉下添眉(比喻重复、多余);眉弓(额下长眉毛处突出如弓形的部位);眉尖(双眉附近处);眉柳(细长如柳叶的眉);眉脚(眉梢)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 书页上端的空白 <span class="encs">[top margin of a page]</span>。又泛称上端。如:书眉;眉批</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 借指美女 <span class="encs">[beauty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">眉，媚也，有妩媚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">下眉近戚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仲定碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:眉清目秀，眉目如画(形容容貌俊美清秀，借指美女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Mei prefecture]</span>。故地在今四川省眉山县</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>眉</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形容隆起 <span class="encs">[swelling]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:眉瑑(古代圭、璋等玉制礼器上下两头都有孔，孔与孔之间有沟，沟缘高出部分叫眉瑑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 老 <span class="encs">[old]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">眉，老也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以介眉寿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:眉寿(长寿);眉耇(高寿，长寿)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>眉</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>多用于称新月。如:一眉新月;眉月(指新月)</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7709.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2516E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥅮</span>【唐韻】武悲切【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25110.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥄐</span>悲切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音麋。【說文】目上毛也。【釋名】媚也。有嫵媚也。【春秋&middot;元命包】天有攝提，人有兩眉，爲人表<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>，陽立于二，故眉長二寸。【註】攝提二星頗曲，人眉似之。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】東齊謂老曰眉。【郭璞註】言秀眉也。【詩&middot;豳風】爲此春酒，以介眉壽。【註】豪眉也。</p>
<p>又渠眉，玉飾之溝瑑也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;典瑞】駔圭璋，璧琮，琥璜之渠眉。</p>
<p>又井邊地曰眉。【前漢&middot;游俠傳】揚雄酒箴曰：觀瓶之居，居井之眉。【註】若人目上之有眉也。</p>
<p>又姓。宋眉壽，明眉旭。</p>
<p>又【韻會】通作麋。【大戴禮&middot;主言篇】孔子愀然揚麋。【荀子&middot;非相篇】伊尹之狀，面無須麋。</p>
<p>又通作嵋。峨嵋山，在蜀嘉定府峨眉縣南百里，兩山相對如蛾眉。</p>
<p>又州名。魏置眉州，因峨眉山爲名。○按《說文》本作睂，从目，象睂之形。上象額理也。徐曰：仌，頟理也。指事。隸作眉，《字彙補》又作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25155.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥅕</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/251EE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥇮</span>，非。</p>
