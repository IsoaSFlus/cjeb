                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>睽</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从目，癸声。本义:两眼不看一地方) 斜视 <span class="encs">[strabismus]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">睽，目少精也。——<span class="smcs">《广苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">睽，目不相视也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 目不相视，即二目不能集中视线同视一物</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>睽</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 分离;背离 <span class="encs">[deviate from;depart from]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">睽者，乖也。——<span class="smcs">《易·序卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">睽，外也。——<span class="smcs">《易·杂卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">睽孤见豕负涂。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“睽孤乖剌之意也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">归妹之睽。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“乖离之象。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曩者胶漆契，迩来云雨睽。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《伤友》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:睽违(分离;不在一起);睽离(阔别);睽别(分离，离别);睽阻(离别阻隔);睽隔(别离，分隔);睽阔(久别;远别);睽阕(分离隔断);睽离(分离;离散)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 违背，不合 <span class="encs">[not conform]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">守失其微，天下孤睽。——</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《法言》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 张目注视 <span class="encs">[stare at]</span>。如:睽目(反目;翻脸);众目睽睽(许多人都注视着)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/睽睽">睽睽</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/777D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】苦圭切【集韻】【韻會】傾畦切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音奎。【說文】目不相視也。【玉篇】目少精也。【六書故】反目也，因之爲睽乖。【易·序卦】睽者，乖也。【雜卦】睽，外也。</p><p>又【集韻】涓畦切，音圭。義同。</p><p>又【廣韻】異也。【唐書·柳宗元傳】睽焉而鬭。【宋史·沈起傳】內自睽疑。</p><p>又張目貌。【韓愈·鄆州谿堂詩】萬目睽睽。</p><p>又楚邑名。【左傳·僖二十七年】楚子使子文治兵于睽。</p><p>又【集韻】其季切，音悸。睽睢張目貌。【王延壽·魯靈光殿賦】顄顤顟而睽睢。○按《說文》本作睽。《舉要》別作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202F3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋳</span>，非。《正韻牋》睽孤之睽从目，睽違之睽从日，聾睽之睽从耳。俗溷用，非。《㴑原》又以暌卽睽之譌，亦非。</p>
