						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>迂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yū.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˉ</span></p><ol><li>曲折，绕远：～回。～缓（行动迟缓）。</li><li>言行或见解陈旧不合时宜：～论。～气。～儒。～阔（不切合实际）。～腐（说话、行事拘泥于陈旧的准则，不适应新时代）。～夫子（迂腐的读书人）。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，于声。本义:曲折，绕远)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[circuitous;winding;roundabout]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迂，僻也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，僻远也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迂乃心。——<span class="smcs">《书·盘庚》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“僻也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子之迂也。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“远也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼岂乐为迂阔哉。——</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《答宾戏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡人以为迂言也。”——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·先已》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">却霍见其语迂。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“迂回加诬于人。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迂，曲也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·蔡邕传》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惩山北之塞，出入之迂也。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:迂直(虽迂曲却是最直接有利的);迂折(迂回曲折);迂弘(深远宏大);迂邈(遥远);迂路(绕路);迂深(深远);迂言(迂阔的言辞);迂僻(偏僻);迂狂(迂阔狂放);迂谈(迂阔的谈论);迂傲(迂阔而高傲)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 迂腐，不合事理 <span class="encs">[stubborn adherence to outworn rules and ideas;pedantry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迂儒之议。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厌其为迂。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 如:迂妄(荒诞，不合情理);迂小(腐迂渺小);迂板(迂腐古板);迂癖(迂诞古怪);迂僻(迂诞怪僻，不合情理);迂呆(迂拙愚笨);迂谬(迂腐荒谬);迂薄(迂腐浅薄)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 邪避，不正 <span class="encs">[evil;heretical]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不度而迂求。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“邪也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:迂邪(邪避)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 缓慢 <span
