                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>酿</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">釀</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">niàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄧㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>利用发酵作用制造酒、醋、酱油等：～造。～酒。～醋。</li><li>指酒：佳～。</li><li>蜜蜂做蜜：～蜜。</li><li>喻事情积渐而成：～成水灾。酝～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>酿</strong></p><p>釀 <span class="dicpy">niàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)，襄声。本义:做酒，酿造)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。原专指酿酒，后也指利用发酵作用酿造蜜、醋、酱等 <span class="encs">[make wine;brew]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酿，醖也。作酒曰酿。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">米麦所作曰酿。——<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黑黍舂来酿酒饮。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">曹邺</span><span class="smcs">《田家效陶》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酿泉为酒。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:酝酿(造酒的发酵过程);酿米(酿酒的米);酿具(酿酒的器具);酿秫(用秫黍酿酒);酿制(酿造);酿糯(以糯米酿酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 酝酿，逐渐形成 <span class="encs">[ferment]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早难道救冤反把冤酿。——朱寉<span class="smcs">《十五贯、廉访》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:酿雪(空中水蒸气逐渐凝聚而形成为雪);酿寒(逐渐酿成寒冷的天气);酿祸(酿成祸患);酿乱(酿成祸乱)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 娇宠 <span class="encs">[indulge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">素日皆是你们这些人把他酿坏了，到这步田地还来解劝——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>酿</strong></p><p>釀 <span class="dicpy">niàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 酒 <span class="encs">[wine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伶人倾家酿。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·何充传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
