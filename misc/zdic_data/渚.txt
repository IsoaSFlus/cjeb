                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>渚</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，者声。本义:古水名。渚水)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 水中的小洲 <span class="encs">[islet;small piece of land in waters]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小洲曰渚。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>引<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“渚，小洲也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两涘渚涯之间。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·秋水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渠弭于有渚。——<span class="smcs">《国语·齐语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夕弭节兮北渚。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·湘君》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘</span><span class="diczx2">鄂渚</span>而反顾兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·涉江》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">江有渚，之子归，不我与。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·江有渚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">避渚洲。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《<指南录>后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:渚牙(洲上初生的草芽);渚田(小洲上的田);渚烟(笼罩在小洲上的烟雾);渚泽(洲中积水的洼地)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 水边 <span class="encs">[waterside]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汛洲清川渚，遥望高山阴。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《豫章行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:渚莲(水边荷花);渚宫(春秋楚国的宫名。故址在今湖北省江陵县)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6E1A.svg">                          <p>水。在常山中丘逢山，東入湡。从水者聲。《爾雅》曰：“小洲曰渚。”章与切</p>                          </div>
