                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>堆</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">duī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄟˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>累积在一起的东西：～栈。～房。土～。</li>	<li>累积在一起，聚积在一起：～积。～放。～垒。～摞。～砌。</li>	<li>量词，用于成堆的物或成群的人：一～人。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">堆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>堆</strong> <span class="dicpy">duī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)声。本义:土堆)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 土墩，沙墩或水中聚集的礁石 <span class="encs">[heap pile;hillock;mound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逾陇堆兮渡漠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;疾世》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">激堆埼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">呼水中沙堆为墠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释水》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堆阜(小丘);堆埼(曲折的岸边)&mdash;&mdash;多用于地名。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:滟滪堆(在四川长江中);双堆集(在安徽)</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 常为排列的整齐有序的叠堆 <span class="encs">[stack]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:草堆;柴火堆;堆云(形容密集众多);堆堆(久久不动的样子;形容累积得很多的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>堆</strong> <span class="dicpy">duī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 堆积 <span class="encs">[pile up;heap up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堆，聚土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堆堵(堆积堵塞);堆绢(堆纱花。用彩绢制成花鸟人物形状，缀附于屏障上);堆山塞海(形容财物极为丰富);堆红(漆器上所堆成的朱色花纹);堆案(累积案头。指积压很多文书)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>堆</strong> <span class="dicpy">duī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用来计量成堆的东西 <span class="encs">[heap]</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">積聚成小山的東西。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「土<b>堆</b>」、「瓦礫<b>堆</b>」、「柴火<b>堆</b>」。</span></p></li>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">地名用字。如屏東縣的六堆，大陸地區四川省奉節縣東瞿塘峽口的灩澦堆。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
