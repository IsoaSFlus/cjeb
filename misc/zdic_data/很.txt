                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">很</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>很</strong> <span class="dicpy">hěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从彳( <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span>)，艮( <span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n</span>)声。本义:违逆，不听从)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[disobey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">很，不听从也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美而很。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十六年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;戾不从教。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今王将很天而伐</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:很子(忤逆的儿子);很忤;很触(违犯<span class="encs">[禁令]</span>);很人(执拗乖戾的人);很很(刚愎貌);很刚(暴戾);很恣(乖戾恣肆);很恶(暴戾凶恶);很愎(犹刚愎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 争斗;争讼 <span class="encs">[struggle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">很毋求胜，分毋求多。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 狠命 <span class="encs">[try to one&#39;s utmost]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:很命;很力(狠命用力)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>很</strong> <span class="dicpy">hěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 凶恶，残忍;暴戾，乖戾。假借为&ldquo;狠&rdquo; <span class="encs">[cruel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大子痤美而很。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十六年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:很，今俗作狠。心狠毒。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">很刚而不和，愎谏而好胜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;亡徵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猛如虎，很如羊，贪如狼，彊不使者，皆斩之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">进退自由，傲很天常，若无君父。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《风俗通义&middot;过誉》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而顾私恩，傲很自遂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《风俗通义&middot;愆礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:很心(心肠残忍);很毒(凶狠毒辣);很刻(狠毒忌刻);很戾(凶暴乖戾);很鸷(凶狠阴险)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>很</strong> <span class="dicpy">hěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p>非常，甚，达到一个很高的程度或达到一个相当的范围 <span class="encs">[very]</span>。</p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>很</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄣˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hěn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2780.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">甚、非常，表示程度高。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「雨<b>很</b>快就停了。」、「他<b>很</b>喜歡看書。」、「這主意<b>很</b>好。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>

</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>

</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
