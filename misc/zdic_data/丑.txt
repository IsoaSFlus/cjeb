                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>丑</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">④⑤醜</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chǒu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǒu.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄡˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>地支的第二位，属牛。</li>
	<li>用于计时：～时（凌晨一点至三点）。</li>
	<li>传统戏剧角色名：～角。～旦。</li>
	<li>相貌难看：～陋。</li>
	<li>可厌恶的，可耻的，不光荣的：～化。～恶。～闻。～态百出。跳梁小～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> clown, comedian; 2nd terrestrial branch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hässlich  (Adj)​,scheußlich  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> honteux,laid,vilain,bouffon,le 2e des 12 Rameaux terrestres</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丑</strong></p>
<p>醜 <span class="dicpy">chǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鬼，酉声。古人以为鬼的面貌最丑，故从鬼。本义:貌丑。按:在古代，&ldquo;醜&rdquo;和&ldquo;丑&rdquo;是两个字，意义各不相同，除作地支和时辰用&ldquo;丑&rdquo;以外，都不能写作&ldquo;丑&rdquo;。现在&ldquo;醜&rdquo;字简化为&ldquo;丑&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 可恶 <span class="encs">[hateful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">醜，可恶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耗土之人醜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;易本命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">姱而不醜兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;橘颂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦孔之醜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span>。传:&ldquo;恶也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丑名(恶名);丑言(恶劣的话);丑攀谈(说粗俗话);丑徒(恶徒);丑语(恶劣的话。也作丑话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 污秽 <span class="encs">[dirty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行莫醜于辱先生。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任少卿书》</span>。注:&ldquo;秽也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丑秽(丑陋污秽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 众多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">醜，众也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在醜夷不争。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;众也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丑虏(众多的敌人。也指丑恶的敌人);丑杂(众多杂乱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 形貌陋劣 <span class="encs">[ugly]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丑露(丑陋;难看);丑状(指形貌丑陋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指事物不好 <span class="encs">[bad]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丑田</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 不光彩，可耻 <span class="encs">[disgraceful;scandalous]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丑吒(丑事，不光彩的事);丑行(丑恶的行为)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 类，相同 <span class="encs">[same]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">比物醜类。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">获匪其醜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;离》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丑夷(年辈相同、学业相等的人);丑侪(同类，等辈)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>丑</strong></p>
<p>醜 <span class="dicpy">chǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 厌恶;憎恶 <span class="encs">[detest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">醜类恶物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丑沮(丑诋毁谤)</span></p>
