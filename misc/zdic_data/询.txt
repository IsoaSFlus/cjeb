                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">询</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>询</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">詢</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xún  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xún.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄣˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 问，征求意见：～问。～察。查～。质～。咨～。～事考言（查问考核言行是否相符）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> inquire into, ask about; consult</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> erfragen, abfragen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> s'informer,se renseigner</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>询</strong></p><p>詢 <span class="dicpy">xún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，旬声。本义:询问，请教)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ask about;inquire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今因经无以询为问罪字，遂用讯不用询，其实讯询一字也。——</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">询事考言。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">周爰咨询。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·皇皇者华》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">询尔仇方。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·皇矣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咨亲为询。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">询于八虞。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>大夫不询于我寡君。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十三年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咨才为诹，咨事为谋，咨义为度，咨亲为询。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民俗，事疑必询三老。——</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苟无至诚恻怛忧天下之心，则不能询考贤才，讲求法度。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《上时政疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:询悉(询问后得知);探询(探问);咨询(征求意见);询察(稽查访问)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 查考;打听 <span class="encs">[try to acertain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">燕</span>多剧贼… <span class="diczx2">楚材</span>询察得其姓名，皆留后亲属及势家子，尽捕下狱。——<span class="smcs">《元史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:询质(查考和质正);询索(查访);询按(查核按验);询究(查考，究门)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 谋 <span class="encs">[plan]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帝曰:格汝</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>，询事考言，乃言底可绩。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“询，谋。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:询谋(谋于众人)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 均，协调 <span class="encs">[equal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四时推六律、六吕，询十有二变，而道宏广。——<span class="smcs">《尚书大传》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>询</strong></p><p>詢 <span class="dicpy">xún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p>信实，确实 <span class="encs">[certainly]</span>。如:询訏(实在很大)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/询查">询查</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
