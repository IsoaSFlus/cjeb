                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>狸</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>即“<span class="diczx3">貉</span>”。</li>    <li>哺乳动物，形状与猫相似，毛皮可制衣物。亦称“狸子”、“狸猫”、“山猫”、“豹猫”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">狸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>狸</strong> <span class="dicpy">lí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (狸为貍的俗字。形声。从豸( <span class="dicpy">zhì</span>)，长脊的野兽，里声。本义:狸子，也叫野猫、山猫)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[leopard cat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">捕鼠不知狸狌，此家猫也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·秋水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一之日于貉，取彼狐狸，为公子裘。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 也叫<span class="yhcs">“钱猫”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“山猫”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“豹猫”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“狸猫”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“野猫”</span>。体大如猫，圆头大尾，全身浅棕色，有许多褐色班点，从头到肩部有四条棕褐色纵纹，两眼内缘向上各有一条白纹。以鸟、鼠等为食，常盗食家禽。毛皮可制裘 。又如:狸狌(野猫);狸制(狸兽毛皮制成的服饰);狸力(神兽名)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 黄鼠狼的别名 <span class="encs">[yellow weasel]</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8C8D.svg">                          <p>伏獸，似貙。从豸里聲。里之切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">伏獸。似貙。</span>伏獸，謂善伏之獸。鄭注大射云：貍首。逸詩：貍之言不來也。其詩有射諸矦首不朝者之言，因以名篇。皇偘以爲舊解云：貍之取物，則伏下其頭，然後必得，言射亦必中，如貍之取物矣。上文云貙似貍，此云貍似貙。言二物相似，卽俗所謂野貓。<span class="jieshi">从豸。里聲。</span>里之切。一部。                          </div>
