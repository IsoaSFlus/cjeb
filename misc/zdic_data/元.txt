                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">元</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>元</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>头、首、始、大：～凶。～首。～旦。～年。～勋。～帅。状～（科举考试第一名）。</li><li>基本：单～。～件。～气（精气，根本）。～素。～音。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">圆</span>”。</li><li>未知数：一～二次方程。</li><li>中国朝代名：～代。～曲。</li><li>姓。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">玄</span>”，清代避康熙皇帝（玄烨）名讳，以“元”代“<span class="diczx3">玄</span>”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">元</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>元</strong> <span class="dicpy">yuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从一，从兀。甲骨文字形。象人形。上面一横指明头的部位。上一短横是后加上去的，依汉字造字规律，顶端是一横的，其上可加一短横。本义:头)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[head]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元，始也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，当训首也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元，体之长也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">始加元服。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狄人归其元。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">归国子之元。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇士不忘丧其元。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牛曰一元大武。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“头也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">吐蕃</span>叛换方炽， <span class="diczx2">敬玄</span>失律， <span class="diczx2">审礼</span>丧元，安可更为西方生事?——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:元首(头)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 天 <span class="encs">[Heaven]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执元德于心而化驰若神。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“天也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:元机(天机，指神秘的天意);元神(天帝，天神);元父(天帝);元命(天命);元天(苍天)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 君 <span class="encs">[king;emperor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元首起哉。——<span class="smcs">《书·益稷》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“元首，君也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元，君也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故国斜阳草自春，争元作相总成尘。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《得儿女消息》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:元、恺辅舜(传说高辛氏有才子八人，叫<span class="yhcs">“八元”</span>。高阳氏有才子八人，叫<span class="yhcs">“八恺”</span>。八元八恺辅佐虞舜，把政事治理得很好);元龙(皇帝);元明(佛教指<span class="encs">[本性]</span>清净光明);元后(天子;帝王的嫡妻)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 开始;起端 <span class="encs">[beginning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元年者何?君之始年也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:元始天尊(道教所尊的最高天神。据说他生于太元之先。故名);元初(起初);元由(原由。事情的起始和原因);元因(原因)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 根源;根本 <span class="encs">[source;origin]</span>。如:元本(根本);元序(最根本的秩序，指礼仪);元极(万物之本原)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 指元气 <span class="encs">[vitality]</span>。指天地未分前的混沌之气;指人的精神，精气;中医名词，指人体的正气，与<span class="yhcs">“邪气”</span>相对。如:元炁(元气);元阳(男子的精气)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 道家所谓的道 <span class="encs">[Taoist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元，无所不在也。人能守元，元则舍之;人不守元，元则舍之。——<span class="smcs">《子华子·大道》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:元神(佛道经过修炼的灵魂。成仙得道的人，其元神可以离开肉体自由来往);元君(道教对女子成仙者的尊称);元龙(元阳，道教指<span class="yhcs">“得道”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 指帝王年号 <span class="encs">[the title of an emperor’s
