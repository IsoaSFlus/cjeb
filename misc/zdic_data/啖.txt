                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>啖</strong></p><p>啗、噉、嚪 <span class="dicpy">dàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，炎声。本义:吃，咬着吃硬的或囫囵吞整的食物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[eat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啖，噍啖也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啖，食也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啖啖常欲人之有。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王霸》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“并吞之貌。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啖函启化。——<span class="smcs">《太玄·玄莹》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“含也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">樊哙</span>覆其盾于地，加彘肩上，拔剑切而啖之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日啖荔枝三百颗，不辞长作岭南人。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《食荔枝二首》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茹啖其草木之荑实。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《苦斋记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:啖蔗(吃甘蔗，愈吃愈甜。比喻渐入佳境);啖啖(贪吃的样子);啖指咬舌(恐惧不敢多言的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 给吃 <span class="encs">[feed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">吉</span>妇取枣以啖 <span class="diczx2">吉</span>。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王吉传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先生…又何吝一躯啖我而全微命乎?——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将炙啖</span><span class="diczx2">朱亥</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《侠客行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:啖养(饲养);啖虎(餵虎)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用饵或好象用饵勾引;引诱 <span class="encs">[bait;entice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啖以利，因袭攻武关，破之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·高祖本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:啖围(紧紧包围;引诱并包围);啖以重利</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
