                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>刻</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄜˋ</span></p><ol><li>雕，用刀子挖：～本（雕版印成的书本）。～石。～字。～板。～舟求剑。</li><li>古代用漏壶记时，一昼夜共一百刻。今用钟表计时，一刻等于十五分钟：五点一～。</li><li>时间：此～。即～。顷～（极短时间）。时～。～不容缓。</li><li>形容程度极深：深～。～意。～骨（感受深切入骨）。～苦。</li><li>不厚道：～毒。～薄。尖～。苛～。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">克</span>”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>刻</strong> <span class="dicpy">kè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，亥声。本义:雕刻，在木头上雕刻)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[engrave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刻，镂也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">金谓之镂，木谓之刻。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释器》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">器不刻镂。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·哀公问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二十四年春，刻其桷，皆非礼也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">刻桓宫桷。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指在各种材料上的雕刻</p><p><span class="diczx1">用胶泥刻字，旋刻之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是日观道中石刻，其远方刻尽漫失。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">右刻山高月小水落石出，左刻清风徐来水波不兴。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:刻图章;刻刀(雕刻所用的刀具);刻木(在木质器物上雕刻);刻饰(雕刻装饰);刻铭(在金石等器物上镂刻文字);刻划(雕刻;刻印);刻篆(指雕刻的文字、花纹)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 绘画;修饰 <span class="encs">[draw;adorn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何乃刻画</span><span class="diczx2">无盐</span>，以唐突 <span class="diczx2">西子</span>也?——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·轻诋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:刻句(推敲锻炼词句);刻桷(有绘饰的方椽);刻章琢句(修饰琢磨章句);刻励(琢磨推敲)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 划，割 <span class="encs">[cut]</span>。如:刻蜡板;刻截(割断);刻漆(割漆)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 严格要求 <span class="encs">[rigorously demand]</span>。如:刻己(严格要求自己);刻求(严格要求);刻责(严格要求);刻绳(限制要求)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 限定 <span class="encs">[restrict]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">指日刻期，应时有验。——<span class="smcs">《白石神君碑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:刻纳(限期归还)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 伤害 <span class="encs">[damage;prejudice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山坐金，反自刻。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘安</span><span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span>
