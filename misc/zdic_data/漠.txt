                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">漠</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>漠</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄛˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>面积阔大无人定居，缺水干燥的沙石地带：沙～。～北。</li>
	<li>空旷：广～。荒～。～～。</li>
	<li>冷淡地，不经心地：～视。～不关心。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/寞">寞</a>&rdquo;，寂静无声。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> desert; aloof, indifferent, cool</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> desertieren, im Stich lassen  (S)​,Wüste  (S)​,gleichgültig  (Adj)​,teilnahmslos  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> désert,indifférent</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">漠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>漠</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从水，莫声。本义:沙漠) 同本义 <span class="encs">[desert]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">漠，北方流沙也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逾</span><span class="diczx2">陇</span>堆兮渡漠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;疾世》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大漠孤烟直，长河落日圆。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《使至塞上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:漠北，漠南(蒙古高原大沙漠以南(北)的地区);漠野(我国北方沙漠地区)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>漠</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 淡泊，恬淡，即不追求名利 <span class="encs">[coldly，not seek fame and wealth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">澹而静乎!漠而清乎!调而闲乎!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;知北游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 冷淡，不关心 <span class="encs">[indifferent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我而漠然置之。不可言也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《少年中国说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:漠如(犹漠然。冷淡，不关心);漠置(漠然置之);漠尔(轻视，不在意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;寞&rdquo;。空廓，寂静 <span class="encs">[quite]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫虚静恬淡，寂漠无为者，万物之本也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">野寂漠其无人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;远游》</span></span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上" target="_blank">巳集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上&amp;bs=水" target="_blank">水</a>】
                              漠·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6F20.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】慕各切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】末各切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音莫。【說文】北方流沙也。與幕通。【文選&middot;嘯賦】註沙土曰幕。幕漫之。【程大昌&middot;北邊備對】幕者，漠也。言沙磧廣莫，望之漠漠然也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】漠，淸也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】施也，茂也。</p>
<p>又淡漠，恬靜貌。【文子&middot;上仁篇】非淡漠無以明德。</p>
<p>又與寞通。【楚辭&middot;遠遊】野寂寞其無人。</p>
<p>又與莫通。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】漠，謀也。【詩&middot;小雅】聖人莫之。莫漠音義同。</p>
<p>又漠漠，布列貌。【陸機詩】街巷紛漠漠。</p>
<p>又漠陽，江名。【廣輿記】在肇慶府陽江縣。</p>
<p>又【集韻】莫白切，音陌。與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21EDF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡻟</span>同。或从水，亦作㵹。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶蒙晡切，音模。【李陵詩】徑萬里兮度沙漠，爲君將兮奪匈奴。</p>
<p>又叶莫卜切，音木。【嵆康&middot;阮籍碑銘】怡神大素，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>邁世局。隱處虛室，反眞歸漠。</p>
