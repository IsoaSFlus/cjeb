                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>招</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄠˉ</span></p><ol><li>打手势叫人来：～唤。～呼。～手上车。</li><li>用公开的方式使人来：～集。～收。～纳。～贤。～聘。～兵买马。</li><li>应接：～待宾客。</li><li>引来：～惹。～引。～揽。～致。</li><li>承认自己的罪行：～供。～认。不打自～。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">着</span>”。</li><li>摇动：～摇（故意张大声势，引人注意）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">招</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>招</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从手。召声，呼唤。本义:打手势叫人来)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[beckon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">招，手呼也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">招者，召也。以手曰招，以言曰召。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">招</span><span class="diczx2">樊哙</span>出。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">登高而招，臂非加长也，而见者远。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:招迎(招呼使接待;招引迎接);招求(招引导求);招招(招呼的样子);招诱(招唤劝导);招护(招呼，关照);招应(招呼、理睬)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 招收;招募 <span class="encs">[recruit;enlist;enroll]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上招贤良。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·晁错传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:招拔(召来并加以擢用);招用(招收使用);招军(招募士兵)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 邀请 <span class="encs">[invite]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">招前日宾客。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">侯方域</span><span class="smcs">《壮悔堂文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:招宴(邀请参加酒宴);招要(招邀。邀请);招饮(招人宴饮)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 招致;招惹 <span class="encs">[provoke]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">满招损，谦受益。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《伶官传》</span>引<span class="smcs">《书·大禹谟》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">招而不至。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">招</span><span class="diczx2">越</span><span class="diczx2">蜀</span>呔怪。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:招风惹雨(招惹是非);招风揽火(招惹是非);招尤(招致他人的怪罪或怨恨);招召(招来祸福);招振(招致擢用)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 招供，供认 <span class="encs">[confess]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非法拷打，何罪不招!——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">胡太初</span><span class="smcs">《昼帘绪论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:招状(供状);招款(招状);招稿(招供的文稿);招承(招认;认罪或认错)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 逗引 <span class="encs">[seduce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今夫</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>女 <span class="diczx2">郑</span>姬，设形容，…目挑心招，出不远千里，不择老少者，奔富厚也。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:招蜂引蝶(比喻逗引异性)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 招抚;招安 <span class="encs">[offer amnesty and enlistment to rebels]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仕途捷径无过贼，上将奇谋只是招。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span
