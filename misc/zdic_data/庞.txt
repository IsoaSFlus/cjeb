                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>庞</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">龐</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">páng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/páng.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>大：～大。～然大物。</li><li>杂乱：～杂。</li><li>脸：面～（脸盘）。～眉皓首（眉发花白，年老的样子）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>庞</strong></p><p>龐 <span class="dicpy">páng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/páng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广，龙声。广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，象高屋形。本义:高屋)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[high house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庞，高屋也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓屋之高者也，故字从广。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 脸盘 <span class="encs">[face]</span>。如:庞儿(脸盘)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓。如:庞德(三国魏南安人。字令明。初从马腾，后败降曹操，封关门亭侯);庞涓(战国时魏将。曾与孙膑同学兵法于鬼谷子)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>庞</strong></p><p>龐 <span class="dicpy">páng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/páng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 多而杂乱的 <span class="encs">[innumerable and disordered]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邑居庞杂，号为难理。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·李勉传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:庞杂;庞鸿(天地元气浑成未分，宇宙浑然一体的样子);庞眉(眉毛又粗又黑)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 高大 <span class="encs">[tall;huge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">湛恩庞洪。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马相如传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“厚大也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庞眉皓首无住著。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《戏为韦偃双松图歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">成</span>视之，庞然修伟。——<span class="smcs">《聊齐志异·促织》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 厚实 <span class="encs">[thick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四牡庞庞。——<span class="smcs">《诗·车攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:庞庞(厚实的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr"
