                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赋</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">賦</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>旧指田地税：田～。～税。</li><li>中国古典文学的一种文体。</li><li>念诗或作诗：登高～诗。</li><li>给予，亦特指生成的资质：～予。～有。天～。禀～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">敷</span>”，铺陈，分布。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赋</strong></p><p>賦 <span class="dicpy">fù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，武声。从<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>，表示与财物有关。本义:征收)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tax]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赋，敛也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赋，税也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥赋惟上上错。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“谓土地所生，以供天子。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">七赋之所养。——<span class="smcs">《法言·问道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而令贡赋。——<span class="smcs">《小司徒》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓出车徒给徭役也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太医以王命聚之，岁赋其二。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:赋银(征收税银);赋粟(征收田赋);赋里(征收商业税);赋币(征收财物)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 缴纳赋税 <span class="encs">[pay taxes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讼者平，赋者均。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《送薛存义序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:赋金(交纳税款)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 给予;授予 <span class="encs">[give to;confer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出</span><span class="diczx2">高库</span>之兵以赋民。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为百姓困乏献其田宅者二百三十人，以口赋贫民。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·平帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公属百官，赋职任功。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:赋命(天所授予的命运;给予生命);赋给(分配发给);赋食(布散饮食);赋政(颁布政令);赋质(上天赋予的资质);赋灵(天赋的灵性，生性);赋就(天生的);赋禀(天生具有的智能、性情);赋职(授与职事)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 做诗 <span class="encs">[write poems]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一言均赋，四韵俱成。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衔觞赋诗，以乐其志。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《五柳先生传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 诵读;吟咏 <span class="encs">[sing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不歌而诵谓之赋。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·艺文志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公入而赋。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公子赋<span class="smcs">《河水》</span>，公赋<span class="smcs">《六月》</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">登东皋以舒啸，临清流而赋诗。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span>
