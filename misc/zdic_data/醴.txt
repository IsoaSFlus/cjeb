                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">醴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>醴</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)，豊( <span class="dicpy">lǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐ.mp3"></a></span> )声。从<span class="yhcs">“酉”</span>，表示与酒有关。本义:甜酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sweet wine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醴，酒一宿孰也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醴齐。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·酒正》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“醴，犹體也。成而汁滓相将如今恬酒矣。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或以酏为醴。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醴者，稻醴也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·杂记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且以酌醴。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·吉日》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为酒为醴。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·丰年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酒醴异气，饮之皆醉。——</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:醴杯(酒杯);醴酒(甜酒);醴齐(醴酒，甜酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈形〉 甘，甜 <span class="encs">[sweet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">地出醴泉。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">甘雨时降，万物以嘉，谓之醴泉。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醴泉出山。——<span class="smcs">《尚书·中候》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“醴，甘也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:醴泉侯(酒的别称);醴液(甘美的泉水)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 中药剂型一种 <span class="encs">[drug]</span>。俗称药酒。如:醴酒(药酒);醪醴(中药剂型之一。即药酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 液汁 <span class="encs">[juice; sap]</span>。如:木醴(树流出的液汁)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Li River]</span>。如:醴泉</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“礼”</span>礼法，规范、仪式的总称 <span class="encs">[ceremonial rites and regulations]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出请醴宾。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若不醴，则醮用酒。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醴泉">醴泉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醴酪">醴酪</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=酉" target="_blank">酉</a>】
                              醴·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/91B4.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】盧啓切【集韻】【韻會】里弟切【正韻】良以切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音禮。【說文】酒一宿<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/263E7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦏧</span>也。【玉篇】甜酒也。【釋名】醴，禮也。釀之一宿而成，醴有酒味而已也。【詩·小雅】且以酌醴。【傳】饗醴，天子之飮酒也。【詩詁】酒之甘濁而不泲者。【周禮·天官·酒正】辨五齊之名，二曰醴齊。【註】醴，猶體也。成而滓汁相將，如今恬酒。【前漢·楚元王傳】元王每置酒，常爲穆生設醴。【註】師古曰：醴，甘酒也。少<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29354.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩍔</span>多米，一宿而熟。</p><p>又【廣韻】醴泉，美泉也。狀如醴酒，可養老。【爾雅·釋天】甘雨時降，萬物以嘉，謂之醴泉。【禮·禮運】故天降膏露，地出醴泉。</p><p>又【正字通】木醴。【建康實錄】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>末，覆舟山、蔣山松栢林冬日常出木醴，後主以爲甘露。</p><p>又縣名。【史記·惠景閒侯者年表】醴陵侯越。【註】縣名，屬長沙。【廣韻】醴泉縣，屬京兆府，本漢谷口縣也，屬馮翊。至後魏置寧夷縣，隋攺醴泉，因周醴泉宮得名。【韻會】唐置乾州，宋攺醴州，因醴泉縣名。</p><p>又灃水，亦作醴。【楚辭·九歌】沅有芷兮醴有蘭。【史記·夏本紀】又東至于醴。【註】索隱曰：騷人所歌，濯余佩于醴浦，醴卽灃水也。</p><p>又【字彙補】與禮通。【禮·內則】世子生，宰醴負子，賜之束帛。【註】醴當爲禮。</p><p>
