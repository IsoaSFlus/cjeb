                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>犷</strong></p><p>獷 <span class="dicpy">guǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从犬，广声。本义:犬凶恶，凶猛)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[angry and untamable]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犷，犬犷犷不可附也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犷彼淮夷。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗·泮水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犷犷亡</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犷，猛也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·光武纪》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犷，恶也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·段颎传》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:犷兽(凶猛的野兽);犷狉(凶猛野蛮);犷恶(凶猛，凶恶);犷戾(凶暴而乖张);犷狠(横暴凶狠);犷暴(凶暴);犷犷(凶暴;凶恶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 粗野 <span class="encs">[rude]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且临守偏海，政移犷俗。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·祭肜传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:犷语(粗野的语言);犷卤(粗野愚钝);犷顽(粗野不驯);犷锐(粗野好斗)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 强悍，强壮凶悍 <span class="encs">[brutal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">年十五，犷悍无赖，犯法当死，变姓柳，之盱眙市中为人说书。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:犷野(强悍野蛮);犷横(凶悍蛮横);犷俗(犷悍的习俗);犷敌(强敌)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/犷悍">犷悍</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
