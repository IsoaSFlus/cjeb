                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>盍</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄜˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>何不，表示反问或疑问：&ldquo;～各言尔志？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>何故，为何：&ldquo;～不出从乎？君将有行&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/闔">闔</a>&rdquo;。合，聚合：～簪（&ldquo;勿疑朋～～。&rdquo;意思是待人不疑心，朋友就会聚合而疾来）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>盍</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/蓋">蓋</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">盍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>盍</strong></p>
<p>盇 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(通&ldquo;闔&rdquo;。本义:覆盖) 合 <span class="encs">[gather]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盍簪喧枥马，列炬散林鸦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《杜位宅守岁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:盍簪(友人聚会;朋友);盍朋簪(朋友聚会);盍彻(施行仁政)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>盍</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 何不(盍是「何不」的合音) <span class="encs">[why not]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盍，何不也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盍请济师于王?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子盍从众?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子盍诘盗?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盍杀我?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盍反其本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祭酒计甚善，王盍用之?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盍驰往油画院。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 为何;什么;怎么 <span class="encs">[why]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盍不为行?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;盗跖》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:盍如(何如)</span></p>
<p><strong>基本解释</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>盍</strong></p>
<p>盍 <span class="dicpy">g&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名、动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从皿，去（g&agrave;i）声。甲骨文有二&ldquo;去&rdquo;，一为来去之去，二为蓋子像形字，&ldquo;大&rdquo;像蓋子，&ldquo;口&rdquo;或&ldquo;凵&rdquo;像器皿，甲骨文&ldquo;闔&rdquo;也以此为声。後加&ldquo;皿&rdquo;分化出&ldquo;盍&rdquo;，&ldquo;蓋&rdquo;又被假借別義，所以加&ldquo;艸&rdquo;造&ldquo;蓋&rdquo;。本义:覆蓋)</p>
