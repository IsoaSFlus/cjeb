                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">详</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>详</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">詳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>细密，完备，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">略</span>&rdquo;相对：～细。～略。～情。～谈。周～。～实（详细而确实。亦作&ldquo;翔实&rdquo;）。语焉不～（说得不详细）。</li>
	<li>清楚地知道：内容不～。</li>
	<li>说明，细说：内～。</li>
	<li>审慎：～平（审理案件审慎公平）。～刑（断案用刑谨慎）。</li>
	<li>旧时的一种公文，下级官员对上级的报告：上～。～文。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>详</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">詳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/佯">佯</a>&rdquo;，假装。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> detailed, complete, thorough</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> précis,détaillé,précision,détail</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">详</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>详</strong></p>
<p>詳 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，羊声。本义:审察，审理)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[observe carefully;check;try(a case)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">详，审议也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">度作详刑，以诘四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;审察之也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">凤姐</span>详情度理，说:&ldquo;他们必不敢多说一句话。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿明上详之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:详允(审判得当、公平);详当(详审而允当);详阅(审查批阅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 详细说明 <span class="encs">[explain in detail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:详梦(解梦;析梦);内详;另详</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 清楚地知道 <span class="encs">[know clearly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非君所详。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦不详其姓字。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《五柳先生传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:详典(了解典据)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 旧时下级将案情向上级报告请示 <span class="encs">[report]</span></p>

<p>又善也。【易&middot;大壯】不能退，不能遂，不詳也。【疏】詳者，善也。進退不定，非爲善也。【釋文】王肅作祥。【左傳&middot;成十六年】詳以事神。【註】詳，祥也。</p>

<p>又通作翔。【前漢&middot;西域傳】道里遠近翔實矣。【師古註】翔，與詳同。假借用耳。</p>
