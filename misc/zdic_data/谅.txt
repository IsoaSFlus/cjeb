                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谅</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谅</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">諒</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>宽恕：～解（<span class="dicpy">jiě</span>）。～察。体～。见～。原～。</li><li>信实：～直（诚信正直）。</li><li>推想：～必。～已上车。</li><li>固执，坚持成见。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> excuse, forgive; guess, presume</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> vergeben, verzeihen ,Liang  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pardonner,excuser,supposer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谅</strong></p><p>諒 <span class="dicpy">liàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，京声。本义:诚实;信实)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[honest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谅，信也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众信曰谅。——<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请肆简谅。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">友直友谅。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">易直子谅之心。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·祭义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">私直怜兮何极，心怦怦兮谅直。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九辩》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:谅直(诚信正直) ;谅士(诚信之士);谅节(高尚的节操);谅实(真诚)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 固执，坚持己见 <span class="encs">[stubborn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子贞而不谅。——<span class="smcs">《论语·卫灵公》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谅</strong></p><p>諒 <span class="dicpy">liàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 谅解 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">母也天只，不谅人只。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鄘风·柏舟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未必谅某此心也。——</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《与刁景纯学士书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:本着互谅互让的精神;谅情(体谅实情)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 宽恕或容忍 <span class="encs">[forgive;excuse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谅其至冤。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:谅宥(原谅);尚希见谅</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 料想，认为 <span class="encs">[presume;expect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自谅前事年远，无人推剥。——<span class="smcs">《京本通俗小说·冯玉梅庆团圆》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:谅不见怪;谅必如此;谅来(想来;料想);谅他也不会这样做</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/谅察">谅察</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
