                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>怨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄢˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>仇恨：～恨。恩～。宿～。～仇。～敌。～府（大家怨恨的对象）。～声载道。</li>	<li>不满意，责备：埋（<span class="dicpy">m&aacute;n </span>）～。抱～。～言。任劳任～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hatred, enmity, resentment</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> beschuldigen, Schuld geben  (V)​,Haß, Groll, Feindseligkeit  (V)​,jdn. für etw. verantwortlich machen, tadeln  (V)​,seufzen, bedauern  (V)​,sich beklagen, sich beschweren, jdn. etw. verübeln  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rancune,plainte,reprocher,se plaindre de,en vouloir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">怨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>怨</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，夗( <span class="dicpy">yu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:怨恨;仇恨)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rankle;grudge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怨，恚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曰怨乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:&ldquo;恨也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施行得理，谓之德。反德为怨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不怨人取之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子甚怨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人怨恶其君。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怨颇兴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怨愤所积。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《&lt;黄花冈七十二烈士事略&gt;序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怨怅(怨畅。怨恨);怨谤(怨恨诽谤);怨恶(怨恨憎恶);怨艾(悔恨自己的错误);怨詈(怨恨并咒骂);恩怨(恩惠和仇恨);宿怨(旧有的怨恨);怨痛(怨恨哀痛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 责怪;埋怨 <span class="encs">[blame;reproach;complain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羌笛何须怨杨柳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王之涣</span><span class="smcs">《凉州词》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下怨谤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抱怨(埋怨);怨咎(埋怨责备);怨思(抱怨迂居，怀念故土);怨责(埋怨责怪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 讥讽 <span class="encs">[satirize]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 别离 <span class="encs">[take leave]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:怨旷(长期别离;女无夫，男无妻);怨妇(丧夫或丈夫别离很久的妇人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 哀怨 <span class="encs">[plaintif;sad]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乐不乐者，其民必怨，其生必伤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高诱</span>注:&ldquo;怨，悲。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">痛灵根之夙陨，怨具尔之多丧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《叹逝赋》</span></p>
