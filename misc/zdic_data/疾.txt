                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>疾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">j&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>病，身体不舒适：～病。目～。残～。讳～忌医。</li>	<li>一般的痛苦：～苦。</li>	<li>疼痛：～首蹙额。</li>	<li>恨：～恶如仇。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/嫉">嫉</a>&rdquo;，妒忌。</li>	<li>弊病，缺点：&ldquo;寡人有～&rdquo;。</li>	<li>快，迅速：～步。～走。～进。～驰。～足先得。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> illness, disease, sickness; to hate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Erkrankung  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> maladie,peine,douleur,haïr,détester,rapide,vif</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>疾</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，从大(人)，从矢，字形象人腋下中箭。 <span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span><span class="smcs">《说文解字注》</span>:&ldquo;矢能伤人，矢之去甚速，故从矢会意。&rdquo;本义:受兵伤。泛指疾病)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 轻微的叫疾 <span class="encs">[ill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾，病也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;析言之则病为疾加，浑言之则疾亦病也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君有疾在腠理，不治将恐深。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;喻老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">伯牛</span>有疾， <span class="diczx2">子</span>问之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指病 <span class="encs">[disease]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">司马子</span>反辞以心疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;十过》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疾革(病情加重);疾损(病情减转，病情好转);疾动(发病;疾病发作);疾棘(病危)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 废疾;废疾的人。亦泛指病人 <span class="encs">[disability;patient]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疾瘵(废疾，残疾)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 疫疠;发生疫疠 <span class="encs">[epidemic disease]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾在腠理。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;喻老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡人无疾。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君有疾。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已有疾疫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾疫(发生疫病);疾疠(瘟疫);疾疟(指疟疾);疾厉(即疾疠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 痛 <span class="encs">[ache]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疾首(头痛);疾痒(痛痒);疾心(痛心)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 痛苦;忧患 <span class="encs">[suffering]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问之民所疾苦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身自疾痛。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;订鬼篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾痛惨怛;疾困(疾苦;疾疢);疾吏(病困的小官吏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 缺点，毛病，瑕疵 <span class="encs">[fault]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必有窃疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡人有疾，寡人好色。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>贤者，所刺讥皆中诸侯之疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>疾</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 快速;急速 <span class="encs">[quick]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奋疾而不拔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">征鸟厉疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而疾讨</span><span class="diczx2">陈</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公五年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何来疾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能疾走。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草枯鹰眼疾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《观猎》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾驰入仆营。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾快(快速);疾言(言语急迫);疾进(急速进行);疾疾(迅速的样子;急遽不安的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 敏捷;轻快 <span class="encs">[quick;nimble;sharp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春风得意马蹄疾，一日看尽</span><span class="diczx2">长安</span>花。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">孟郊</span><span class="smcs">《登科后》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾伶(机敏伶俐);疾俏(动作轻快灵活);疾跳(精悍，机灵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 急剧而猛烈 <span class="encs">[rapid;sharp;violent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾风知劲草，板荡识诚臣。&mdash;&mdash;李世民<span class="smcs">《赐萧瑀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾威(暴虐残酷);疾雨(急骤的雨);疾雷(急雷，突然响起的雷。比喻汹涌的波涛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 恶 <span class="encs">[evil;bad]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疾日(恶日，不祥的日子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 怒，强狠 <span class="encs">[angry;fierce]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疾视(瞋目怒视)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 强大，宏大 <span class="encs">[powerful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顺风而呼，声非加疾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>疾</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 患病，生一般的病 <span class="encs">[fall ill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寒暑不能使之疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾色(患病的脸色);疾耗(患病的坏消息);疾作(疟疾发作)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 厌恶;憎恨 <span class="encs">[disgust;hate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔无忿疾于顽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;君陈》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾夫舍曰欲之而必为之辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;季氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生而有疾恶焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有亡而无疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杨倞</span>注:&ldquo;疾同嫉，嫉恨。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾其君者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愤世疾邪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾大户兼并。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾恶(憎恨恶人);疾心(憎恶之心);疾吏(憎恨狱吏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 极力;尽力;努力 <span class="encs">[do one&#39;s utmost;spare no effort]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疾争(极力争辩);疾作(努力劳作);疾耕(努力耕作);疾学(努力学习)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 嫌怨 <span class="encs">[detest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾王听之不聪。(听，名词。聪，耳明。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾怨(怨恨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;嫉&rdquo;。妒忌 <span class="encs">[envy;be jealous of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人之有技，冒疾以恶之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;秦誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公私之分明，则小人不疾贤，而不肖者不妒功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;修权》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">膑至</span>， <span class="diczx2">庞涓</span>恐其贤于己，疾之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;孙子吴起列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疾裘妒枕(夫妻间对双方有外遇的嫉妒);疾夫(妒忌者);疾心(心生妒忌);疾忌(妒忌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 忧虑 <span class="encs">[hesitate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草食之兽，不疾易薮;水生之虫，不疾易水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 非难;毁谤 <span class="encs">[slander]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:疾毁(毁谤)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/75BE.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2457C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤕼</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2458F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤖏</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/253C2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥏂</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2097B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠥻</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24D85.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤶅</span>【唐韻】秦悉切【集韻】【韻會】昨悉切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音嫉。【說文】病也。一曰急也。【徐曰】病來急，故从矢。矢，急疾也。【易&middot;復卦】復亨出入無疾。【書&middot;說命】若藥弗瞑眩，厥疾弗瘳。</p><p>又【玉篇】患也。【左傳&middot;桓六年】謂其不疾瘯蠡也。【疏】不疾者，猶言不患此病也。</p><p>又【玉篇】速也。【廣韻】急也。【易&middot;繫辭】帷神也，故不疾而速。【詩&middot;大雅】昊天疾威。【傳】疾猶急也。【禮&middot;月令】季冬之月，征鳥厲疾。【疏】疾，捷速也。【張衡&middot;南都賦】總括趣欱，箭馳風疾。</p><p>又【增韻】惡也。【左傳&middot;昭九年】辰在子卯，謂之疾日。【注】疾，惡也。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】疾，齊壯也。【疏】急速齊整，皆于事敏速强壯也。</p><p>又虐也。【詩&middot;大雅】疾威上帝。【朱注】疾威，猶暴虐也。</p><p>又怨也。【管子&middot;君臣篇】有過者不宿其罰，故民不疾其威。【注】疾，怨也。</p><p>又毒害也。【左傳&middot;宣十五年】山藪藏疾。【註】山之有林藪，毒害者居之。</p><p>又姓。【姓譜】元魏疾陸眷。</p><p>又車轅前之下垂在地者曰前疾。【周禮&middot;秋官】大行人立當前疾。</p><p>又劉疾，鳥名。【爾雅&middot;釋鳥】鶛，劉疾。</p><p>又與嫉通。【書&middot;君<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>】爾無忿疾于頑。</p><p>又【秦誓】人之有技，冒疾以惡之。【傳】見人之有技藝，蔽冒疾害以惡之。【史記&middot;孫臏傳】龐涓恐其賢於已，疾之。</p><p>又與蒺同。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】及至獲夷之徒蹶，松柏掌疾黎。</p><p>又【韻補】叶才詣切，音劑。【易&middot;無妄】无妄之疾，勿藥有喜。喜，音戲。【詩&middot;大雅】庶人之愚，亦職維疾。叶下戾。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】潏潏淈淈，湁潗鼎沸。馳波跳沫，汨隱漂疾。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
