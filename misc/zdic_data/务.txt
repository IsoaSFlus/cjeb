                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>务</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">務</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>事情：事～。任～。公～。特～。不识时～（不认识时代潮流和当前形势）。</li>
	<li>从事，致力：～工。～实（从事或讨论具体的工作）。～虚。当～之急。</li>
	<li>追求：好（<span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o </span>）高～远。</li>
	<li>必须，一定：～必。～须。除恶～尽。</li>
	<li>旧时收税的关卡（现多用于地名）：曹家～（在中国河北省）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> affairs, business; must, should</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Angelegenheit, Angelegenheiten  (S)​,sich beschäftigen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> affaire,s'occuper,s'appliquer,il faut</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">务</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>务</strong></p>
<p>務 <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，務( <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。从力，表示要致力于某事。本义:致力，从事)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be engaged in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非务相反也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲富国者，务广其地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">务耕织。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:务能(竭力);务精(极力求精;精益求精);务穑(务农);务法(致力于法治);务本(专心致力于根本);务外(谓研究学问，只致力于表面，不求深入);务纯(致力于深钻细研，以达炉火纯青);务本(致力于根本);务于东(在东方致力作战)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 操劳 <span class="encs">[work hard]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:务时(谓及时耕作，不误农时)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 谋求 <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不务文字奇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《寄唐生》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 追求 <span class="encs">[pursue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">务采色。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贪多务得</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 热;焐 <span class="encs">[heat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:务脚(焐脚;暖脚)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>务</strong></p>
<p>務 <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 事业;事务 <span class="encs">[affair;business]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必因此为务。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粟者，王者大用，政之本务。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">方今之务。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">军中多务。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;汉纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿言多务</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:公务;私务;家务;税务;国务;校务</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 紧要的事情 <span class="encs">[important thing]</span>。</p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 职业 <span class="encs">[occupation]</span></p>
