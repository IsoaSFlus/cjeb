                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>豢</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从豕( <span class="dicpy">shǐ</span>，猪)，帣( <span class="dicpy">juàn</span>)声。本义:设围栏以谷物养猪)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[feed pig with grains in sty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豢，以谷圈养豕也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫豢豕为酒。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">案刍豢。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌豢祭祀。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·槁人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:豢圉(饲养牛马的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指喂养;饲养 <span class="encs">[feed;raise]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡畜养禽兽，皆曰豢。——<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">古者畜龙，故国有豢龙氏，有御龙氏。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:豢池(养鱼池);豢牢(兽圈);豢畜(犹豢养);豢圉(养牛马的地方);豢牺(喂养牲畜)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 以利益为饵来引诱人为其服务，任其宰割 <span class="encs">[lure;seduce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豢文臣帷幄无谋，豢武夫疆场不猛。——</span><span class="diczx2">孔尚任</span><span class="smcs">《桃花扇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 贪图 <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豢于游戏酒食之间。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而豢于富贵，安于顺境。——</span><span class="diczx2">俞文豹</span><span class="smcs">《吹剑三录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 供养 <span class="encs">[supply]</span>。如:豢养(养育，供养)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>豢</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 以谷类饲养的家畜，如猪、狗。以别于吃草的牛、羊 <span class="encs">[domestic aminals]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民食刍豢。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:刍豢(刍，指吃草牲 口;豢，指吃谷物的牲 口，如犬，豕);豢牢(泛指家畜);豢龙(古代名马)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=豕" target="_blank">豕</a>】
                              豢·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8C62.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>胡慣切，音宦。【說文】以穀圈養豕也。【廣韻】穀養畜也。【禮·月令】仲秋按芻豢。【註】養牛馬曰芻，犬豕曰豢。【疏】食草曰芻，食穀曰豢。</p><p>又餌之以利，一曰豢。【左傳·哀十一年】吳將伐齊，越子率其衆以朝，王及列士皆有餽賂，子胥懼，曰：是豢吳也夫。【註】豢，養也。</p><p>又官名。【左傳·昭二十九年】董父擾畜龍，以服事帝舜，帝賜之姓曰董，氏曰豢龍。【註】豢龍，官名。以官爲氏。</p><p>又【集韻】通作圂。【禮·少儀】君子不食圂腴。【註】圂，同豢。</p><p>又【韻會】亦作㹖。【莊子·達生篇】祝宗人說彘曰：汝奚惡死，吾將三月㹖汝。</p><p>又【集韻】戸管切，音緩。義同。</p><p>又叶胡玩切，音換。【曹植·孔甲贊】行有順天，龍有河漢。雌雄各一，是擾是豢。</p><p>又叶熒絹切，緣去聲。【歐陽修·讀書詩】淡泊味愈長，始終殊不變。庶幾垂後世，不默死芻豢。　【說文】本从豕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2234F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢍏</span>，手執米以養之，指事也。隷省作豢。《說文》本从豕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2234F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢍏</span>作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2891C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨤜</span>。</p>
