                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闱</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>闱</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">闈</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wéi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wéi.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄟˊ</span></p><ol><li>古代宫室两侧的小门。</li><li>后妃居处：宫～。</li><li>父母居室：庭～。</li><li>科举时代称试院：春～。秋～。～墨（清代每届乡试会试的试卷，由礼部选定录取的文章，编刻成书）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> gate, door; living quarters</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> portes du palais,portes de l'appartement des femmes</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>闱</strong></p><p>闈 <span class="dicpy">wéi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wéi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，韦声。本义:古代宫室、宗庙的旁侧小门)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[side door of the palace]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闱，宫中之门也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使其属守王闱。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·保氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闱门，如今东西掖门。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士虞礼记》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:闱闼(宫中小门);闱门(古代宫室、宗庙的旁侧小门)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 后妃居处 <span class="encs">[imperial concubines' residence]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后正位宫闱，同体天王。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 父母居室 <span class="encs">[one's parents' bedroom]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">眷恋庭闱。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">束晳</span><span class="smcs">《补亡诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 妇女居室 <span class="encs">[women's quarters]</span>。如:闱箔(内室的门帘)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 科举时代对考场、试院的称谓 <span class="encs">[examination hall or room]</span>。如:秋闱(秋试);春闱;闱姓(旧时广东人在科举考试时流行的一种就何姓考取多少的一种赌博);闱战(参加科举考试);闱艺(科举考场上写的诗文)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/闱墨">闱墨</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
