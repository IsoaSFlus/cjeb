                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>绷</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">綳</span> <img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字">繃）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēng.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄥˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>张紧，拉紧：～紧。小褂紧～在身上。</li>	<li>当中用藤皮、棕绳等物绷紧的竹木框：床～。棕～。绣～。～子。</li>	<li>一种缝纫方法，粗粗缝上或用针别上：～被头。</li>	<li>束，包扎：～带。</li>	<li>（物体）猛然弹起：～簧。～弓子。</li>	<li>勉强支持：～场面。</li>	<li>方言，骗财物：坑～拐骗。</li></ol><p>● <strong>绷</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">綳</span> <img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字">繃）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">běng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/běng.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄥˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>板着，强忍着：～劲。～着脸。</li></ol><p>● <strong>绷</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">綳</span> <img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字">繃）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">b&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄥˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>裂开：～瓷儿。</li>	<li>方言，用在某些形容词前面，有&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">很</span>&rdquo;的意思：～硬。～亮。</li></ol>

<p>◎ <strong>绷</strong></p>
<p>綳、繃 <span class="dicpy">bēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，朋声。本义:束缚，捆绑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 缠束;包扎 <span class="encs">[tie;wrap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">繃，束也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>葬 <span class="diczx2">会稽</span>，桐棺三寸，葛以繃之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;节葬篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">襁，即今儿小儿繃也。今俗犹云繃小儿矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;宣帝纪》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:绷吊拷讯(捆绑并吊起来拷问);绷爬吊拷，绷巴吊拷，绷扒吊拷(强行脱衣服，捆好吊起来拷打)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 张紧，在两端或边上用力造成平直的坚挺状态 <span class="encs">[stretch tight]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:弦绷得太紧了;鼓皮绷得真紧</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 猛地弹起 <span class="encs">[jump out]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:盖子一打开，弹簧就绷出来了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 稀疏地缝住或用针别上 <span class="encs">[stitch up]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:把被面绷一绷;袖子上绷着臂章</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 勉强支持;硬撑 <span class="encs">[force oneself]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:绷拽(支撑);绷场面(勉强应付，东拼西凑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 骗 <span class="encs">[财物]</span> <span class="encs">[cheat]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:坑绷拐骗;讹绷(讹诈骗取财物);绷骗(骗人财物);绷子手(骗人财物的人)</span></p>
