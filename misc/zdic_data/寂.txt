                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寂</strong> <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，叔声。本义:静悄悄，没有声音)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[quiet;silent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寂，无人声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寂寥宇宙。——<span class="smcs">《四子讲德论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寂兮寥兮，独立不改。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寂然不动。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四面竹树环合，寂寥无人。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《永州八记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">满坐寂然。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人视端容寂。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俄而寂然。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:寂寂悄悄(悄悄地，不弄出声音地);寂密(隐密);寂天寞地(悄无声息)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 安详闲静，心志淡泊 <span class="encs">[composed and leisurely]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旷然无忧患，寂然无思虑。——</span><span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《养生论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
