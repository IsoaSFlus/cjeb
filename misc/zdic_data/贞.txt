                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贞</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>贞</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">貞</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄣˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>坚定，有节操：忠～不渝。坚～不屈。～节。～士（忠贞之士）。</li>
	<li>旧礼教中束缚女子的一种道德观念，指妇女不改嫁等：～女。～烈。～操（同&ldquo;贞节&rdquo;）。～淑。</li>
	<li>占，卜，问卦：～卜。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">桢</span>&rdquo;，桢干（<span class="dicpy">g&agrave;n</span>）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>virtuous, chaste, pure; loyal</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> rein, keusch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> chaste,pur,droit,vertueux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贞</strong></p>
<p>貞 <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(会意。从卜，从贝(甲骨文作&ldquo;鼎&rdquo;，后省改为&ldquo;贝&rdquo;)。鼎本是食器，这里表火具，即用火具而卜。本义:占卜) 同本义 <span class="encs">[make divination;divine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貞，卜问也。从卜，贝以为贽。会意。京房说，鼎省声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">季冬，陈玉，以贞来岁之恶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;春官&middot;天府》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;问事之正曰贞。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡国大贞，卜立君，卜大封。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贞龟(占卜。古人灼龟甲以卜);贞吝(卜问不吉，其事难行);贞卜(占卜;卜问)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>贞</strong></p>
<p>貞 <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为&ldquo;正&rdquo;、为&ldquo;定&rdquo;。端方正直 <span class="encs">[honest;upright;right]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">元、亨、利、贞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;乾》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥赋贞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。传:&ldquo;正也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求永贞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大祝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万国以贞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;文王世子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一人元良，万邦以贞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;太甲下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子贞而不谅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贞白(正直清白);贞曜(正光);贞一(心正专一);贞期(政治清明的太平盛世);贞明(正大光明);贞确(贞正坚固)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 坚定不移。多指意志或操守 <span class="encs">[firm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言行抱一谓之贞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贞良死节之臣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贞亮死节(坚贞忠诚，能够以死殉节);贞亮(坚贞亮节);贞固(能固守正道，坚贞不变，始终如一);贞人(志节坚定不移、能守正道的人);贞毅(坚贞刚毅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 坚硬;坚劲 <span class="encs">[hard;firm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">严霜识贞木。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">房玄龄</span><span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贞木(坚劲耐寒，经严冬而不凋的树木);贞珉(坚美可做碑铭石刻的石块);贞悦(坚挺劲直);贞柯(坚挺耐塞的枝柯);贞玉(坚美的玉石)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 忠;真诚 <span class="encs">[loyal;faithful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慕古人之贞节。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;诚也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不贰者，则是贞于君也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:忠贞(忠诚而坚定不移);贞臣(廉正有操守的臣子);贞夫(忠直有守的正人君子。也作&ldquo;真夫&rdquo;);贞实(忠信诚实)</span></p>

<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 贞操，节操。旧称女子&ldquo;贞节&rdquo; <span class="encs">[chaste;pure and undefiled]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">清白守节曰贞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;谥法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贞女不更二夫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;甲单列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一念之贞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贞妇(夫死不再改嫁的女子);贞心(坚贞不移的心地);贞艳(有坚贞之节的美女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 碑石 <span class="encs">[an upright stone tablet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:贞碣(碑石的美称);贞碑(碑石的美称);贞琬(碑石的美称);贞珉(石刻碑铭的美称);贞刻(石刻碑铭)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 正轨，正道 <span class="encs">[the right way]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:贞度(符合正道的法度);贞则(符合正道的准则)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8C9E.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A00.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠨀</span>【唐韻】陟盈切【集韻】【韻會】知盈切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音禎。【說文】卜問也。从卜，貝，以爲贄。【徐曰】《周禮》有大貞禮，謂卜人事也。</p>
<p>又【易&middot;乾卦】元亨利貞。【疏】貞，正也。</p>
<p>又【文言】貞者，事之幹也。【書&middot;太甲】一人元良，萬邦以貞。【疏】天子有大善，則天下得其正。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;洪範】曰貞，曰悔。【傳】內卦曰貞，外卦曰悔。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;檀弓】故謂夫子貞惠文子。【疏】諡法，外內用情曰貞。</p>
<p>又【釋名】貞，定也。精定不動惑也。</p>
<p>又木名。【本草】女貞，木名。蘇頌曰：女貞，負霜蔥翠，故貞女慕其名。一名冬靑。</p>
