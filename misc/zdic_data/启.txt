                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>启</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">啓</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>打开：～封。～门。某某～。～齿。</li>    <li>开始：～用。～程。～运。</li>    <li>开导：～迪。～发。～蒙。～示。～明（古代指太阳还没出来的时候，出现在东方天空的金星）。承上～下。</li>    <li>陈述：～事。</li>    <li>书信：书～。小～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">启</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>启</strong></p><p>啓、啟、唘 <span class="dicpy">qǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从户，从口。甲骨文字形，左边是手(又)，右边是户(单扇门);用手开门，即开启的意思。后繁化加<span class="yhcs">“口”</span>，或省去手(又)而成<span class="yhcs">“启”</span>。金文又加<span class="yhcs">“攴”</span>( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)成<span class="yhcs">“啓”</span>。现简化为<span class="yhcs">“启”</span>。本义:开，打开)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[open]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">启，开也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 经传皆作<span class="yhcs">“啟”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公将啟之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疆埸无主，则啟戒心。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡啟塞从时。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“门户道桥谓之啟。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啟户。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士虞礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">门启而入。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">启窗而观。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不能启口。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:启沃(开诚忠告。旧时指用治国之道开导帝王);启扉(开门);启请(开口询问;请教);启户(开门)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 启发;教育 <span class="encs">[enlighten;teach;educate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啟，教也。从攴，启声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不愤不啟。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆啟以商政。——<span class="smcs">《左传·定公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啟古献公。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·祭统》</span></span></p><p><span
