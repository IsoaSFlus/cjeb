                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>晒</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">曬</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄞˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>把东西放在太阳光下使它干燥；人或物在阳光下吸收光和热：～图。冲～。晾～。</li>	<li>〈方〉置之不理，慢待：我让他～在那儿。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>晒</strong></p>
<p>曬 <span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (曬俗作晒。形声。左形，右声。本义:在阳光下曝干或取暖)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 暴晒;晒干 <span class="encs">[expose to the sun;dry in the sun;bask]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曬，暴也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">暴五谷之类。</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之间谓之曬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言七》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">答曰:&ldquo;我晒书。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘义庆</span><span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:太阳晒得人喘不过气来;晒衣服;晒席(晒谷物等用的席子);晒暖(方言。晒太阳取暖);晒腹( <span class="diczx2">晋</span><span class="diczx2">郝隆</span>七月七日出日中仰卧。人问其故，答曰:&ldquo;我晒书&rdquo;。盖自谓满腹诗书也。后用为曝书之典)。又作放射;照耀</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白日晒光，幽隐皆照。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 〈方〉∶置之不理，慢待 <span class="encs">[ignore]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我被晒在那里，心里挺不是滋味</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用阳光或灯光印制 <span class="encs">[print by light]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:晒照片</span></p>
