                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>乙</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>天干的第二位，用于作顺序第二的代称。</li>
	<li>指代一方：甲方～方。～型脑炎。</li>
	<li>中国古代乐谱的记音符号，相当于简谱&ldquo;7&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> second; 2nd heavenly stem</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> zweiter in einer Folge, "B" in "A, "B, ..,der zweite der zehn "Himmelsstämme" des chinesischen Kalenders (S)​,Ethyl, Ethyl-Gruppe (S, Chem)​,Yi (Eig, Fam)​,Radikal Nr. 5 = Sichel  (Varianten: 乚, 乛)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> le 2e des dix Troncs célestes,deuxième,second</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乙</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形。本义:象植物屈曲生长的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[winding]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乙，草木冤曲而出也。象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乙者，物蕃屈有节欲出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">思乙乙其若抽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《文赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;抽也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其於十母为甲乙。甲者，为万物剖符甲而出也;乙者，言万物生轧轧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;律书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:乙乙(难出之貌)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 第二，次一等 <span class="encs">[second]</span>。如:乙部(古代群书四部分类法的第二部)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>乙</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 天干的第二位，与地支相配，用以纪年、月、日 <span class="encs">[the second of the ten Celestial Stems]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其日甲乙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甲乙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝乙归妹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;泰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">微子启帝乙之元子也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 某人 <span class="encs">[one]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甲乙同学。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甲先得举。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:某甲某乙</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鱼腮骨 <span class="encs">[gill bone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狼去肠&hellip;鱼去乙。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;乙，鱼体中害人者名也。今 <span class="diczx2">东海</span>容鱼有骨，名乙，在目旁，状如篆乙，食之鲠不可出。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 虎两肋和尾端有骨，形如乙字，叫虎威 <span class="encs">[tail bone of tiger]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得如虎挟乙，失若龟藏六。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《寄微轩》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>乙</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>碾压;压抑 <span class="encs">[roll]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西太守，乙卯金。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李贤</span>注:&ldquo;乙，轧也。 <span class="diczx2">述</span> 言西方太守能轧绝卯金也。&rdquo;</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乙</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集上" target="_blank">子集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集上&amp;bs=乙" target="_blank">乙</a>】
                              乙·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=1" target="_blank">1</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E59.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】於筆切【集韻】億姞切【韻會】【正韻】益悉切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音鳦。十幹名。東方木行也。【爾雅&middot;釋天】太歲在乙曰旃蒙。月在乙曰橘。【前漢&middot;律歷志】奮軋於乙。【京房易傳】乙，屈也。</p>
<p>又凡讀書，以筆志其止處曰乙。【史記&middot;東方朔傳】朔初上書，人主從上方讀之，止輒乙其處，讀三月乃盡。</p>
<p>又唐試士式，塗幾字，乙幾字。抹去譌字曰塗，字有遺脫，句其旁而增之曰乙。</p>
<p>又【太乙數】有君基太乙，五福太乙諸名。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;藝文志】有天乙三篇。【註】天乙謂湯，其言非殷時，皆假託也。</p>
<p>又姓。漢南郡大守乙世，前燕護軍乙逸，明乙瑄，乙山。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋魚】魚腸謂之乙。【禮&middot;內則】魚去乙。【註】魚餒必自腸始，形屈如乙字。一說魚腮骨，在目旁，如篆文乙，食之鯁不可出，去之乃食。</p>
<p>又【茅亭客話】虎有威如乙字，長三寸許，在脅兩旁皮下，取得佩之，臨官而能威衆。無官佩之，無憎疾者。【蘇軾詩】得如虎挾乙。</p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4E59_54.svg" /><span class="swbf">(乙)</span><span class="ywyy">象春艸木冤曲而出。侌气尙彊。其出乙乙也。</span><span class="swdz">冤之言<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩰪</span>，曲之言詘也。乙乙，難出之皃。史記曰：乙者，言萬物生軋軋也。漢書曰：奮軋於乙。文賦曰：思軋軋其若抽。軋軋皆乙乙之叚借。軋从乙聲，故同音相叚。月令鄭注云：乙之言軋也。時萬物皆抽軋而出。物之出土艱屯。如車之輾地澁滯。</span><span class="ywyy">與丨同意。</span><span class="swdz">謂與自下通上之丨同意也。乙自下出上礙於陰，其書之也宜倒行。於筆切。十二部。按李善：乙音軋。</span><span class="ywyy">乙承甲，象人頸。</span><span class="swdz">以下皆冡大一經曰言之。</span><span class="ywyy">凡乙之屬皆从乙。</span></p>                          </div>
