                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枉</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>枉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>弯曲，弯屈，引申为行为不合正道或违法曲断：～道。矫～过正。贪脏～法。～己正人（自己身子不正，倒去矫正别人）。</li><li>冤屈：～死。冤～。</li><li>屈就，用于别人，含敬意：～驾（ａ．称对方来访自己；ｂ．请对方往访他人）。～顾（称对方来访自己）。～临（称对方来访自己）。</li><li>徒然，空，白：～然。～自。～费心机。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> useless, in vain; bent, crooked</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> vergebens</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> accuser injustement,calomnier,fausser,en vain,courbé,pervers</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>枉</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，王声。本义:弯曲;不正)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[crooked]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枉，邪曲也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是犹立直木而求其景之枉也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王霸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枉道速祸。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枉用三尺。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:枉法营私(曲解、破坏法律，以谋求私利);枉矢(不直的箭);枉径(弯曲的小路);枉策(弯曲的马鞭);枉路(弯曲的道路);枉渚(弯曲之渚);枉木(曲木，弯木);枉直(曲与直。比喻是非、好坏)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 邪恶 <span class="encs">[evil;wicked;vicious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举直错诸枉，能使枉者直。——<span class="smcs">《论语·颜渊》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:枉上(不正之君主);枉邪(邪恶不正，亦指邪恶不正的人);枉情(邪念)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 乱的 <span class="encs">[disorded]</span>。如:枉攘(纷乱的样子)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>枉</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 使受冤屈 <span class="encs">[injustice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恒人见枉。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·问孔》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其枉民也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:枉状(歪曲事实，诬告别人的状文);枉滥(枉法恣肆，使人无辜受害);枉结(冤屈难解的事件);枉抑(冤屈);枉陷(冤枉陷害;无故陷害)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 绕道 <span class="encs">[make a detour]</span>。如:枉帆(谓船绕道而行)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 违背 <span class="encs">[violate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">税外加一物，皆以枉法论。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《重赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:枉戾(谓歪曲颠倒);枉屈(歪曲，违背);枉墨矫绳(比喻违背准绳、准则);枉道事人(本谓违背正道侍奉人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 凌弱 <span class="encs">[insult]</span>。如:枉桡(违法曲断;偏私不公，使有理不申);枉曲(谓违法曲断;不公正)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 谦词。谓使对方受屈 <span class="encs">[be wronged]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枉车骑过之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span
