                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坐</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>坐</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄨㄛˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古人双膝跪地，把臀部靠在脚后跟上，这是其本义，后泛指以臀部着物而止息：席地而～。～待。～垫。～骨。～化（佛教指和尚盘膝坐着死去）。～禅。～功。～骑。</li>	<li>乘，搭：～车。～船。</li>	<li>坚守，引申为常驻，不动：&ldquo;楚人～其北门，而覆诸山下&rdquo;。～庄。</li>	<li>建筑物的位置或背对着某一方向：～落。～北朝南。</li>	<li>把锅、壶等放在火上：～锅。</li>	<li>物体向后施压力：房顶往后～。</li>	<li>介词，因，由于，为着：&ldquo;停车～爱枫林晚，霜叶红于二月花&rdquo;。</li>	<li>副词（①空，徒然，如&ldquo;胡为～自苦，吞悲仍抚膺&rdquo;；②无故，自然而然，如&ldquo;如若此，则盐必～长十倍&rdquo;；③遂，即将，如&ldquo;寒英～销落，何用慰远客&rdquo;；④深，如&ldquo;感此伤妾心，～愁红颜老&rdquo;；⑤正，恰好，如&ldquo;西村渡口人烟晚，～见渔舟两两归&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>定罪：连～。反～。～赃（犯贪赃罪）。</li>	<li>瓜果等植物结实：～瓜。～果。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/座">座</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坐</strong> <span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>古文作象形字，象两人坐在土上。本义:人的止息方式之一。古人席地而坐，坐时两膝着地，臀部压在脚跟上)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">像二人对坐土形。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">林义光</span><span class="smcs">《文源》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐而迁之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲记》</span>。疏:&ldquo;坐通名跪，跪名不通坐也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">退而坐，取屦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受立、授立，不坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;少仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武</span>坐致 <span class="diczx2">右宪王</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span>。疏:&ldquo;坐，跪也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐行而入。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十六年》</span>。注:&ldquo;膝行也。&rdquo;按，坐者，尻也，处也。古席地而坐，膝着席而下其臀曰坐，耸其体曰跪。跪，亦谓之启。跪可言坐，坐不可言跪也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>坐上坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐以待旦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;太早上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐如尸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">项王</span>、 <span class="diczx2">项伯</span>东向坐， <span class="diczx2">亚夫</span>南向坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先生坐!何至于此!寡人喻矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">席不正不坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;乡党》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐语未讫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵尹韩张两王传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 现代在椅、凳出现后，凡将臀部置于椅、凳以支持身体的重量者皆为坐 <span class="encs">[sit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐看霞色晚，疑是赤城标。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孟浩然</span><span class="smcs">《舟中晓望》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坐地(坐下;坐着;坐在地上);坐堂(坐在堂上);坐上客(原指在坐宾客。后称受人礼遇为坐上客);坐啸(闲坐吟啸，无所事事);坐拜(跪拜);坐右(曲右足而坐);坐列(排列而坐。形容无斗志;坐在店铺内);坐思(坐着思念);坐起(安坐或起立;起身而坐);坐静(静坐修持);坐饮(入座宴饮);坐催(坐等催取)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为就坐，就任，包含&ldquo;主持&rdquo;、&ldquo;掌管&rdquo;的意思 <span class="encs">[be seated]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二子在幄，坐射犬于外，既食而后食之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">就打止灵霄宝殿，教他龙床坐不成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坐膺(荣膺;受封);坐办(清制，非常设机构中负责日常事务者);坐馆(任塾师或幕客)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 定罪，由&hellip;而获罪 <span class="encs">[be punished]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使与</span><span class="diczx2">邾大夫</span>坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十三年》</span>。注:&ldquo;讼曲直也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐，罪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遣</span><span class="diczx2">郭威</span>招诱 <span class="diczx2">白承福</span>入居 <span class="diczx2">太原</span>城中，以谋叛坐之，并其部属四百余口尽杀之，不留一个。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新编五代史平话》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妄坐(受冤枉而误判其罪);坐大辟(判为死刑);坐假(因受诬而获罪);反坐;连坐;坐法(犯法获罪);坐死(坐罪而被处死)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">广汉</span>虽坐法诛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵尹韩张两王传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">广汉</span>竟坐要斩。</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>坐要斩。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 因&hellip;犯罪，触犯法律 <span class="encs">[commit a crime]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又坐贼杀不辜、鞠狱故不以实、擅斥除骑士乏军兴数罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵尹韩张两王传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 居留，停留 <span class="encs">[stay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐，止也。从土，从畱省。会意。土所止也。此与留同意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父子坐旅中，惝恍累日，因留过岁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归庄</span><span class="smcs">《黄孝子传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坐窝子(留在原处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 守，防守 <span class="encs">[guard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人坐其北门，而覆诸山下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公十二年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">省外各府州县，皆有坐省家丁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坐索(守候索取;守候催促);坐阵(守卫阵地);坐铺(守卫本铺。谓警戒);坐夜(守夜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 枪炮发射时由力的反作用而使枪炮向后移动 <span class="encs">[recoil]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:步枪的坐劲不小</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 建筑物由于基础不稳固而下沉 <span class="encs">[sink]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这房子向下坐了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 置放。放在或摆在指定的位置上 <span class="encs">[put]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:坐一壶水;坐垫(安放在椅子、凳子上的垫子);坐钟(放在桌案上的时钟);把壶坐上</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 乘，搭 <span class="encs">[机、船、车等]</span> <span class="encs">[travel by]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我走旱路坐车，走水路坐船，走泥路坐撬，走远路坐飞机、坐火车;坐马(供骑坐的马)</span></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">坐中数千人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;陌上桑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请以剑舞，因击</span><span class="diczx2">沛公</span>于坐，杀之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置之坐上。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">设一虚坐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">满坐宾客。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">满坐寂然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:满坐儿;这个剧场有五千个坐儿;坐上(座席上);坐上客(座席上的宾客)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一次连续坐着的时间 <span class="encs">[sitting]</span>。</p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>坐</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄗㄨㄛˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>zu&ograve; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5388.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">彎曲下肢，將臀部附著在座位上休息。與站相對。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「坐下」、「<b>坐</b>在椅子上」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古人席地而坐時兩膝著地，故亦稱跪為「坐」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;玉藻》：「退則<b>坐</b>取屨。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;孔穎達&middot;正義：「<b>坐</b>，跪也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">居、處。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「坐落」、「<b>坐</b>北朝南」、「<b>坐</b>二望一」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">搭乘。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「坐車」、「<b>坐</b>船」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">干犯。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「坐法當死」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">處斷、定罪。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「連<b>坐</b>」、「<b>坐</b>死」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《韓非子&middot;定法》：「公孫鞅之治秦也，設告相<b>坐</b>而責其實。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《隋書&middot;卷八三&middot;西域傳&middot;吐谷渾傳》：「殺人及盜馬者死，餘<b>坐</b>則徵物以贖罪。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">北平、河北、天津方言。指物體重心極力向下壓。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「這房子向下<b>坐</b>了。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">蒞臨。參見「坐殿」、「坐堂」等條。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">席位。</span><span class="gc_yy">《三國志&middot;卷二一&middot;魏書&middot;王粲傳》：「時邕才學顯著，貴重朝廷，常車騎填巷，賓客盈<b>坐</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「座」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">堅守不去。參見「坐鎮」、「坐索」等條。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">比喻平白的、不勞而獲的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「坐享其成」、「坐收漁利」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">介</span>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坐</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中" target="_blank">丑集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中&amp;bs=土" target="_blank">土</a>】
                              坐·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=7" target="_blank">7</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5750.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㘸【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>徂臥切，音座。行之對也。【禮&middot;曲禮】坐如尸。【又】虛坐盡後，食坐盡前。</p><p>又便坐，別坐之處。【前漢&middot;文翁傳】在便坐受事。</p><p>又【後漢&middot;宣秉傳】秉修高節，光武特拜御史中丞，詔與司隸校尉中書令同專席而坐，京師謂之三獨坐。</p><p>又猶守也。【左傳&middot;桓十二年】楚伐絞，軍其南門。絞人爭出，驅楚役徒于山中。楚人坐其北門，而覆諸山下，大敗之。</p><p>又古者謂跪爲坐。【禮&middot;曲禮】先生琴瑟書策在前，坐而遷之。【註】坐，跪也。</p><p>又【律】有罪坐。【前漢&middot;文帝紀】除收帑相坐律令。</p><p>又罪人對理曰坐。【左傳&middot;僖二十八年】鍼莊子爲坐。</p><p>又釋氏大坐曰跏趺。【蓮華經】結跏趺坐。</p><p>又與座通。【前漢&middot;梅福傳】當戸牖之法坐。【註】正座也。</p><p>又姓。見【姓苑】。</p><p>又【集韻】徂果切，音睉。義同。○按坐有上去二音，字韻諸書訓註皆同，惟《轉註古音》坐註引《史記》高帝紀，遂坐上坐。《正義》云：前坐字，在果反，後坐字，在臥反。《字彙》行坐之坐讀上聲，非。《正字通》謂坐字在上聲者，叶音也，亦非。　本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/212F2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡋲</span>。【說文】从土，从畱省，土所止也。隷作坐。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“坐”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/𡊎"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2128E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡊎</span></a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=土">土</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2128E.svg">                          <p>止也。从土，从畱省。土，所止也。此與畱同意。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5750.svg" />坐，古文坐。但臥切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/212F2_9273.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/212F2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡋲</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">止也。</span><span class="swdz">止，下基也。引申爲住止。凡言坐落，坐罪是也。引申爲席地而坐。小雅：不遑啓處。傳曰：啓，跪。處，居也。古謂跪爲啓。謂坐爲凥，爲處。凥俗作居。引申謂凡止箸爲坐。</span><span class="ywyy">从畱省，从土。</span><span class="swdz">會意。徂臥切。十七部。</span><span class="ywyy">土，所止也。此與畱同意。</span><span class="swdz">釋从土之意。从土不必土，猶畱从田不必田，皆謂所止也。故曰同意。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/212F2_9274.svg" /><span class="swbf">(坐)</span><span class="ywyy">古文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/212F2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡋲</span>。</span><span class="swdz">今古文行而小篆廢矣。止必非一人，故从二人。左傳：鍼莊子爲坐。凡坐獄訟，必兩造也。</span></p>                          </div>
