						<p>● <strong>赆</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">贐</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>临别时赠送给远行人的路费、礼物：～道。～仪。～行。</li>
	<li>进贡的财物：纳～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> farewell present</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cadeaux du départ</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赆</strong></p>
<p>贐 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 临别时赠与、赠送或馈赠的财物 <span class="encs">[parting gifts]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赆送一无所取。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《梁书&middot;杨公则传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赆礼(临别时赠送的财物，送行的路费或礼品);赆仪程仪(赆礼);赆赙之仪(赠送给上路或办丧事用的钱物。赙:用钱财资助人办丧事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 进贡的财物 <span class="encs">[articles of tribute;tribute]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:赆货(进贡的财物);赆琛(进贡的财宝);赆贽(进贡用的财货礼物)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>赆</strong></p>
<p>贐 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>尽;全部</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂将此诗赆看，不觉天明。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔象川</span><span class="smcs">《白圭志》</span></p>
