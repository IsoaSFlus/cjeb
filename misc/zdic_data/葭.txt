                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>葭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄚˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>初生的芦苇：～芦。～莩（❶芦苇中的薄膜；❷喻关系疏远的亲戚，如“～～之亲”）。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">笳</span>”，一种乐器。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bulrush, reed; flute, whistle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ried, Rippung (Profil aus mehreren parallelen Holzstäben)​  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> roseau</p></div>
