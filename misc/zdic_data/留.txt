                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>留</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liú.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄡˊ</span></p><ol><li>停止在某一个地方：停～。～学。～任。～级。～步。～守。～驻。</li><li>注意力放在上面：～心。～神。～意。</li><li>不忍舍弃，不忍离去：～连。～恋。</li><li>不使离开：～客。～宿。挽～。拘～。</li><li>接受：收～。</li><li>保存：保～。～存。～别。～念。～后路。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">留</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>留</strong></p><p>畱 <span class="dicpy">liú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从田，从丣，丣亦( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)声。从<span class="yhcs">“田”</span>，表示停留的地方。本义:停留，留下)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(停止在某一处所，含有不动的意思) <span class="encs">[stay]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">畱，止也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令之留于酸枣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">灵连蜷兮既留。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·云中君》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风气留其处。——<span class="smcs">《素问·虐论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃令</span><span class="diczx2">张良</span>留谢。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">城入</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>而璧留 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">留五百人。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雪上空留。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">要留清白在人间。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">于谦</span><span class="smcs">《石灰吟》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">留像于馆。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:逗留(暂时停留);留舍(留宿);留住(对城市年轻人的泛称);留居(保守，维持现状。停住，居留);留身(宋代大臣朝见，请求退朝时独留面奏机宜);留客住(一种头上装有倒钩的长枪)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 使不离开;挽留 <span class="encs">[ask sb. to stay]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">廷椽</span>起矣。状 <span class="diczx2">河伯</span>留客之久，若皆罢去归矣。——<span class="smcs">《史记·滑稽列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昔</span><span class="diczx2">子瑜</span>在 <span class="diczx2">柴桑</span>时， <span class="diczx2">孔明</span>来 <span class="diczx2">吴</span>，孤欲使 <span class="diczx2">子瑜</span>留之。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:留行(挽留，使不离去);留款(挽留款待);留欢(留客欢宴);留饮(留客宴饮;留下宴饮)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 引申为扣留 <span class="encs">[detain;arrest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵高</span>因留所赐 <span
