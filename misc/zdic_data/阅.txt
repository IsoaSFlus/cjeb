						<p>● <strong>阅</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yu&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>看，察看：～览。～读。翻～。传（<span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;n </span>）～。批～。订～。检～。～兵。</li>
	<li>经历：～历。已～三月。</li>
	<li>容，容许：&ldquo;我躬不～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>本钱：折～。</li>
	<li>总聚，汇集：&ldquo;川～水以成川&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阅</strong></p>
<p>閲 <span class="dicpy">yu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，说省声。象在门里清点东西。本义:在门内考察、计算事物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[examine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阅，具数于门中也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为计算 <span class="encs">[calculate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阅，具也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阅，数也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">商</span>人阅其祸败之衅，必始于火。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公九年》</span>。注:&ldquo;阅，犹数也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阅问(逐一询问)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 察看;视察，考查 <span class="encs">[inspect;check]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常以秋、岁末之时阅其民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阅实(查对实事);阅狱(查看监狱);阅审(审核);阅稼(查看禾苗生长情况);阅试(审查考试)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 检阅 <span class="encs">[review]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">马端敏公</span><span class="diczx2">新贻</span>亲临校场阅射。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《庸盦笔记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阅武(检阅军队)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 看;阅读 <span class="encs">[read]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阅金经。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">苏</span><span class="diczx2">黄</span>共阅。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公阅毕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阅卷(评阅试卷);阅荐(审阅荐举);阅看(阅读);阅正(审阅指正)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 经历 <span class="encs">[experience]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那夜跳窗而出，原来就是此楼，思之恍如昨日，不想早阅三年。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《铁花仙史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阅日(过日子);阅月(经过一月);阅岁(经过一年);阅世(经历时世)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 观赏 <span class="encs">[view and admire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">历观今古，无臣下与君上同坐阅戏者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吕道生</span><span class="smcs">《安禄山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阅胜(观赏美景);阅戏(看戏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 总聚;汇集 <span class="encs">[conflow]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">閱</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“阅”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/閱">閱</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閱·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95B1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】弋雪切【集韻】【韻會】欲雪切【正韻】魚厥切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音悅。【說文】具<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22FD9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢿙</span>于門中。【徐曰】春秋大閱，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>車馬也。具數，一一數之也。【玉篇】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>軍實也。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;大司馬】中冬敎大閱。【註】大閱，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>軍實。【春秋&middot;桓六年】秋八月壬午，大閱。【註】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>車馬。</p>
<p>又【說文】一曰察也。出門者，察而數之也。【博雅】閱，數也。【左傳&middot;襄九年】商人閱其禍敗之釁。【註】閱，猶數也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>閱也。【書&middot;多方】克閱于乃邑，謀介。【疏】謂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>閱其事。</p>
<p>又【呂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>】閱實其罪。【疏】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>練核實。</p>
<p>又【正韻】歷也。【前漢&middot;文帝紀】閱天下之義理多矣。【註】閱，猶更歷也。</p>
<p>又【車千秋傳】無伐閱功勞。【師古註】閱，經歷也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】閥閱也。【史記&middot;高祖功臣年表】積日曰閱。【後漢&middot;章帝紀】或起甽畝，不繫閥閱。【註】史記曰：明其等曰閥，積其功曰閱。言前代舉人，務取賢才，不拘門地。</p>
<p>又【韻會】買賣損價也。【荀子&middot;修身篇】良價不爲折閱不市。【註】折閱，謂損其所賣物價也。</p>
<p>又容也。【詩&middot;邶風】我躬不閱。【傳】閱，容也。</p>
<p>又【曹風】蜉蝣掘閱。【傳】閱，容閱也。【箋】掘閱，掘地解閱。謂其始生時也。</p>
<p>又稟也。【老子&middot;道德經】自古及今，其名不去，以閱衆甫。【註】閱，稟也。甫，始也。言道稟與萬物始生，從道受氣。</p>
<p>又遂也。【爾雅&middot;釋宮】桷直而遂，謂之閱。【註】謂五架屋際椽正相當。【疏】屋椽，長直而遂達，五架屋際者名閱。</p>
<p>又【正韻】觀也。</p>
<p>又人名。【左傳&middot;僖三十年】冬王使周公閱來聘。</p>
<p>又【襄九年】華閱。【註】華閱，宋臣華元子。【史記&middot;田敬仲世家】公孫閱謂成侯忌曰：公何不謀伐魏。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/95B1_7295.svg" /><span class="swbf">(閱)</span><span class="ywyy">具數於門中也。</span><span class="swdz">具者，供置也。數者，計也。計者，會也，筭也。云於門中者，以其字从門也。周禮大閱，注曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>軍實也。左氏春秋大閱，傳曰：簡車馬也。引申爲閱歴。又引申爲明其等曰閥，積其功曰閱。手部揲下曰：閱持也。易，揲之以四，謂者四更迭數之也。古叚閱爲穴。詩：蜉蝣堀閱。傳曰：堀閱，容閱也。閱卽穴。宋玉賦：空穴來風。莊子作空閱來風。司馬彪云：門戶孔空，風善從之。道德經：塞其兌，閉其門。兌卽閱之省。詩：我躳不閱。傳云：閱，容也。言我躳不能見容，如無空穴以自處也。</span><span class="ywyy">从門兌聲。</span><span class="swdz">弋雪切。十五部。</span></p>                          </div>
