                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">复</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>复</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">➊-➎<span class="diczx3">復</span>、➎-➏<span class="diczx3">複</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>回去，返：反～。往～。</li>
	<li>回答，回报：～命。～信。～仇。</li>
	<li>还原，使如前：～旧。～婚。～职。光～。～辟。</li>
	<li>副词，又，再：死灰～燃。一去不～返。</li>
	<li>重复，重叠：～习。～诊。～审。～现。～议。</li>
	<li>许多的，不是单一的：重（<span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;ng</span>）～。繁～。～杂。～姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> return; repeat; repeatedly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> vervielfältigen  (V)​,wiederholen  (V)​,erneut, noch einmal  (Adj)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">复</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>复</strong></p>
<p>復 <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形，下面的意符&ldquo;攵&rdquo;，是甲骨文&ldquo;止&rdquo;字的变形，表示与脚或行走有关。上面是声符&ldquo;畐&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">f&uacute;</span>)的省形，有&ldquo;腹满&rdquo;义，在字中亦兼有表义作用。后来繁化，加义符&ldquo;彳&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span>)，表示行走，现在又简化为&ldquo;复&rdquo;。本义:返回，回来)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[return to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">復，往来也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反复其道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;复》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公归不复。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;九罭》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扬触捆復。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;大射仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无往不復。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;泰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卒乃復。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不远而复，先典攸高。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">数请之，</span><span class="diczx2">朱亥</span>故不复谢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死者不可復生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦復旦兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言不可復也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复还(回返)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为恢复 <span class="encs">[restore]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">更若役，复若赋，则何如?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攘除奸凶，兴复</span><span class="diczx2">汉室</span>，还于旧都。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复国(恢复邦国);复性(恢复善良的本性);复正(恢复正规、正常);复朴(恢复真朴);复故(恢复照旧);复籍(恢复学籍;恢复国籍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 回归，还原 <span class="encs">[return]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">燕</span>日败亡，卒至河上，而 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>七十余城皆复为 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;田单列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死者不可复生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦复旦兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言不可复也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 回答 <span class="encs">[reply]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或遇其叱咄，色愈恭，礼愈至，不敢出一言以复。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有复于王者曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王辞而不复。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复书(复信，答函);复谢(回拜，答谢)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 报复 <span class="encs">[retaliate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵无被坚执锐者北面复</span><span class="diczx2">匈奴</span>之志。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">桓宽</span><span class="smcs">《盐铁论本文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复怨(报仇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 履行，实践 <span class="encs">[carry out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:复言(实践诺言);复践(履行约定之事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古称人死后招其魂归来 <span class="encs">[revive]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:复魂(还魂);招魂复魄</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 免除<span class="encs">[赋税徭役]</span> <span class="encs">[remit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民有东骑马一匹者，复卒三人。(免除三人的兵役。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复除(免除徭役);复租(免除赋税);复免(免除徭役)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 覆盖。引申为庇护 <span class="encs">[cover;protect;shelter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顾我复我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;蓼莪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复土(盖土);复载生成(天复地载，抚育成长。指庇养包容，教育成长);复瓿(比喻著作没有价值，只能用来盖盛酱的瓦罐)</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>复</strong></p>
<p>復 <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>又，再。中断再开始 <span class="encs">[resume]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不行不可复者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;牧民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span>作凡将篇，无复字。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;艺文志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以一至万，则世世不相复也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾山传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得复见将军于此。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">超</span>复受使，固欲益其兵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;班超梁慬传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一狼得骨止，一狼仍从。复投之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复前行，欲穷其林。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轻拢慢捻抹复挑，初为<span class="smcs">《霓裳》</span>后<span class="smcs">《六幺》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复籍;复帐(妓女接待第二个客人);复旦(夜尽复明)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>复</strong></p>
<p>復 複 <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 有里的衣服。即夹衣 <span class="encs">[lined garment，dress，etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">複，重衣也。从衣，复声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有里曰複，无里曰襌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释衣服》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏不失复。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">桓宽</span><span class="smcs">《盐铁论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复裈(双层裤，夹裤);复襦(有里的短袄);复衫(短袄);复衾(絮有絮棉的大被);复被(絮有絮绵的被)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 远古人居住的洞穴 <span class="encs">[cave]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:复穴(远古人所居的土窟);复穾(如洞穴一般幽深的屋子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 中医指七方之一的复方 <span class="encs">[compound]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:复药(指由复方配制的成药)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者民泽处复穴，冬日则不胜霜雪雾露，夏日则不胜暑热蚊虻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;氾论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:掏复(掏挖地室)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>复</strong></p>
<p>復 複 <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 重复;繁复 <span class="encs">[compound;complex]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山重水复疑无路，柳暗花明又一村。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《游山西村》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五帝不相复，三代不相袭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span>。又如:复用(重复运用);复沓(重复;重叠堆积)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 夹层 <span class="encs">[double]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从复道上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;上下有道，故曰之复。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">履中有木者，谓之复舄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">红罗复斗帐，四角垂香囊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复道行空，不霁何虹?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:复道(楼阁间架空的通道);复道回廊(高楼间或山岩险要处架空的通道、阁道和曲折迂回的走廊);复哨(军事用语。即二人为一班的步哨);复账(双层的帷账);复壁(两层而中空的墙壁，可以藏匿人和物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 重叠 <span class="encs">[overlapping]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陶复陶穴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;緜》</span></span></p>
