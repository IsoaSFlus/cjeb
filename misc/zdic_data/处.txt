                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>处</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">處</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>居住：穴居野～。</li>
	<li>存在，置身：设身～地。～心积虑。～世。</li>
	<li>跟别人一起生活，交往：融洽相～。</li>
	<li>决定，决断：～理。</li>
	<li>对犯错误或有罪的人给予相当的惩戒：～罚。～决。</li>
	<li>止，隐退：～暑。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>处</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">處</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>地方：～～。～所。</li>
	<li>点，部分：长（<span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng </span>）～。好～。</li>
	<li>机关，或机关、团体、单位里的部门：办事～。筹备～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">处</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>处</strong></p>
<p>處、処 <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从几，从夂，或从处，虍( <span class="dicpy">hū</span>)声。&ldquo;处&rdquo;即&ldquo;處&rdquo;的古字，于六书为会意。金文加声旁&ldquo;虍( <span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;</span>)&rdquo;变成了形声字。本义:中止;停止)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">処，止也。得几而止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子之道，或出或处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">处者处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;射义》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹留也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">譬之如牛马处暑之既至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span>。注:&ldquo;止也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日月不恒处，人生忽若离。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span> <span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《浮萍篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 居住;生活 <span class="encs">[dwell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫敢遑处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;殷其靁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">占右穴居而野处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淖约若处子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span>。释文:&ldquo;在室女也。&rdquo;按，凡士或女未用皆称处。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">处江湖之远则忧其君。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:穴居野处;处妇(居家的妇女);处处(居其所当居的地方);处势(居于有权势的高位);处逸(生活在安逸之中);处安思危(居安思危)</span></p>
