class="encs">[arrive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">到，至也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靡国不到。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·韩奕》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽隆薛之城到于天。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝发</span><span class="diczx2">白帝</span>，暮到 <span class="diczx2">江陵</span>。——<span class="smcs">《水经注·江水》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">豹</span>往到 <span class="diczx2">邺</span>，会长老，问之民所疾苦。——<span class="smcs">《史记·滑稽列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">功施到今。——</span><span class="diczx2">李斯</span><span class="smcs">《谏逐客书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">复到舅家。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span upside down;reverse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">草木之到(倒)植者过半。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·外物》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“锄拔反之更生者曰到植。今字作倒。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公子学去尊，今又王</span><span class="diczx2">齐王</span>，何其到也?——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·爱类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:到植(倒立);到三不着两(行事无准则，颠三倒四)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 往 <span class="encs">[go to;leave for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百川东到海。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·长歌行》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span>
