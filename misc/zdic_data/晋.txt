                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>晋</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">晉</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>进：～级。～升。～谒（进见）。</li>	<li>中国周代诸侯国名。</li>	<li>中国山西省的别称：～剧。</li>	<li>中国朝代名：西～。东～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> advance, increase; promote</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> befördert werden, aufsteigen  (V)​,eintreten, vorgehen, vorrücken  (V)​,Jin  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> avancer,promouvoir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">晋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>晋</strong></p>
<p>晉 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，从日。指追着太阳一直前进。本义:上进)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 进 <span class="encs">[enter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晉，进也。日出，万物进。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晋者，进也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;序卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王提马而走诸侯晋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;田仆》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盍孟晋以迨群兮。(盍:为什么不;孟，勉力;迨，赶上;群:大家。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《幽通赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:晋谒(晋见;谒见);晋接(进见;接见);晋食(进献食品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 晋升。提高地位、级别或荣誉 <span class="encs">[promote]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以功晋知府。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《清史稿&middot;桂中行传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晋封英王。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《陈玉成》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:晋封(晋升加封);晋秩(晋升官职或等级);晋阶(升级);晋号( <span class="dicpy">&mdash;h&agrave;o</span>晋升其称号)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 低，俯 <span class="encs">[low]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见梓晋然实而俯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尚书大传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:晋然(低垂的样子;俯下的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 授予，尤指在公共典礼中授予(如学位或贵族称号) <span class="encs">[confer]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:晋授</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 晋见，正式地会见，拜访 <span class="encs">[call on]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:晋谒</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>晋</strong> <span class="dicpy">J&igrave;n</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 周代诸侯国名 <span class="encs">[Jin state]</span>。周成王封弟叔虞于唐，叔虞子燮父改国号为晋，春秋时居有今山西省大部与河北省西南地区，地跨黄河两岸。后被其大夫韩、赵、魏所分而亡</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子夏</span>之 <span class="diczx2">晋</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎行论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>师三豕过河。</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>师己亥涉河。</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>于是始墨。(晋国从此以黑衣为丧服。墨，活用为动词，意动用法。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二&mdash;&mdash;三十三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:晋用楚材(谓一国聘用别国的人才);晋邦(指春秋时代的晋国);晋阳之甲(喻称地方官吏不满朝廷而举兵向内为兴&ldquo;晋阳之甲&rdquo;);晋棘(指春秋晋国垂棘之地所产的美玉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 朝代名 <span class="encs">[Jin Dynasty]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 司马炎代魏称帝，国号晋，都洛阳，史称西晋(公元265&mdash;316年)，共四帝，为前赵所灭</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span><span class="diczx2">太原</span>中。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无论</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">魏</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>氏以下。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span>独爱菊。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 司马睿即位建康，保有江南之地，史称东晋(公元317&mdash;420年)，共十一帝，为刘裕所取代</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 五代时，石敬瑭灭后唐称帝，国号晋，都洛阳，史称后晋(公元936&mdash;946年)</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">背</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>以归 <span class="diczx2">染</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新五代史&middot;伶官传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:晋年(喻指怀念故朝之思);晋帖(指晋人的书迹或其摹本、刻帖);晋京(晋朝的京城。指洛阳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 山西省的简称 <span class="encs">[Shanxi province]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:晋剧</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Jin River]</span>。源出山西省太原市西南悬瓮山，分北、中、南三渠，东流入汾河</p>
