						<p>● <strong>瞑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～～〕形容昏花迷离。</li>
	<li>闭眼：～目（亦指人死时心里无牵挂）。死不～目。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>瞑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇ〡ㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/眠">眠</a>&rdquo;，睡觉。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瞑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瞑</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>闭上眼睛 <span class="encs">[close the eyes in death]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瞑，翕目也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后得瞑些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">据槁梧而瞑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;德充符》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迅雷不及掩耳，迅電不及瞑目。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《六韬&middot;龙韬&middot;军势》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">内怀殷忧，则达旦不瞑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《养生论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">目似瞑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瞑目蹲身(闭上眼睛，身体后蹲;起跳前的动作);瞑睫(合目);瞑坐(闭目静坐);瞑拜(闭目合掌行跪拜礼以示虔诚)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>瞑</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 昏暗 <span class="encs">[dusk;dark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瞑目。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;气厥论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瞑瞑(昏暗迷乱的样子);瞑眴(昏乱貌);瞑卧(昏睡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 眼睛昏花的 <span class="encs">[dim-sighted]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣耳目聋瞑，不能自励。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">房玄龄</span><span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瞑然(模模糊糊地);瞑士(盲人);瞑臣(春秋晋盲乐师师旷的自称)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>瞑</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;暝&rdquo;。暮:黄昏 <span class="encs">[evening;night]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:瞑色(暮色);瞑光(夜色);瞑氛(夜色);瞑子里(暗地里;暗中)</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瞑</strong> <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;眠&rdquo;。睡觉，打瞌睡 <span class="encs">[sleep;doze off]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瞑</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中" target="_blank">午集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中&amp;bs=目" target="_blank">目</a>】
                              瞑·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7791.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】莫經切【集韻】【韻會】忙經切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音溟。【說文】翕目也。【廣韻】合目瞑瞑也。【後漢&middot;馬援傳】甘心瞑目。【皇極經世】在水者不瞑，在風者瞑。【註】魚在水，其目盡夜不瞑也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】目不明也。【晉書&middot;山濤傳】臣耳目聾瞑，不能自勵。</p>
<p>又【呂覽】瞑者，目無由接也。【汲冢周書】師曠曰：請使瞑臣往與之言。【註】無目故稱瞑。</p>
<p>又【春秋&middot;繁露】民者，瞑也。瞑也者，名其別離分散也。</p>
<p>又瞑瞑視，不審之貌。【荀子&middot;非十二子篇】酒食聲色之中，則瞑瞑然。【淮南子&middot;覽冥訓】其視瞑瞑。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】母迥切【正韻】莫迥切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>溟上聲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>莫定切，溟去聲。義<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】莫賢切【集韻】民堅切【正韻】莫堅切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>麫平聲。與眠通。【玉篇】寐也。【莊子&middot;德充符】據槁梧而瞑。</p>
<p>又弓名。【唐書&middot;南蠻傳】永昌野桑生石上，其材上屈兩向而下植，取以爲弓，不筋漆而利，名曰瞑弓。</p>
<p>又菜名。【本草綱目】瞑菜，一名睡菜，南海人食之思睡，故名。</p>
<p>又盱瞑，與芊眠同。【張衡&middot;南都賦】攢立叢駢，靑麗盰瞑，杳藹蓊鬱于谷底，森䔿䔿而刺天。李善讀，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>詳盰眠二字註。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】莫甸切【集韻】【韻會】眠見切【正韻】莫見切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音麫。瞑眩劇也。</p>
<p>又憒亂也。【書&middot;說命】若藥不瞑眩，厥疾弗瘳。一作眠眩。互詳眠字註。</p>
<p>又【集韻】謨耕切，音萌。與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2532F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥌯</span>同。</p>
<p>又叶武巾切，音旻。【楚辭&middot;招魂】致命於帝，然後得瞑些，歸來歸來。往恐危身些。【註】瞑，臥也。　【正字通】古無眠字，瞑卽眠。今通用眠，正譌从瞑廢眠，泥。</p>
