						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>紧</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">緊</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>密切合拢，与“<span class="diczx3">松</span>”相对：拧～。捆～。</li><li>靠得极近：～邻。</li><li>使紧：把琴弦～～。</li><li>事情密切接连着，时间急促没有空隙：～凑。～密。～缩。加～。抓～。</li><li>形势严重，关系重要：～急。～促。～迫。～要。</li><li>不宽裕：～俏。～缺。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>紧</strong></p><p>緊 <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从臤( <span class="dicpy">qiān</span>)，坚。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，细丝。本义:缠丝急)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[twine urgently]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">紧，缠丝急也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弛紧急之弦张兮。——</span><span class="diczx2">傅毅</span><span class="smcs">《舞赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为物体受拉力或压力后呈现的紧张状态。同<span class="yhcs">“松”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[tight;taut;close]</span>。如:紧峭(扎紧，系紧);紧揪揪(衣服紧绷在身上，指打扮得苗条或干练);紧簇(紧紧的，严严的);紧腾腾(紧紧，严严实实)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指经济不宽裕 <span class="encs">[short of money;hard up]</span>。如:紧巴;紧窄(不宽裕)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 急促;迫切 <span class="encs">[urgent;pressing;tense]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">紧，急也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心紧絭兮伤怀。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·疾世》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:紧事(急事);紧绸(紧凑;急促);紧溜(急流;要紧时刻;紧要关头);紧水(湍急的水流);紧切慢八(慢慢地;一天一天地)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 急躁 <span class="encs">[impetuous;rash]</span>。如:紧三火四(非常急切);紧行无善踪(比喻做事急于求成就不可能完美无缺);紧忙</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 猛烈;激急 <span class="encs">[hard;violently;heavily]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我有一句，这一句就是<span class="yhcs">“一夜北风紧”</span>。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:紧张</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 快速 <span class="encs">[speedy;fast]</span>。如:紧跑(快跑)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 紧要;重要 <span class="encs">[critical;vital]</span>。如:紧溜子(紧溜。紧要关头);紧工(紧要，重要);紧关里(在紧要关头上)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 紧实，牢固 <span class="encs">[fast;firm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戈戟之紧。——<span class="smcs">《管子·问》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“紧，谓其坚彊者。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:紧固(牢固)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 距离近;密接无间 <span class="encs">[close]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其化紧敛。——<span class="smcs">《素问·气交变大论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 又如:紧身(贴身衣服);紧屯屯(方言。形容非常紧密);紧洞洞(方言。形容紧密);紧峭(紧凑);紧健(紧凑有力)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>紧</strong></p><p>緊 <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button
