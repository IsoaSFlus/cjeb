                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>窒</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，至声。洞穴狭小阻塞，故从穴。本义:阻塞，不通)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[obstruct]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窒，塞也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有孚窒。——<span class="smcs">《易·讼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“塞止也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">精不流则气郁，郁处头则为肿为风…处鼻则为鼽为窒。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·尽数》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“不通也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穹窒熏鼠。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:窒塞;窒阂(闭塞，阻隔)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抑止住使不发泄 <span class="encs">[suppress]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">损，君子以惩忿窒欲。——<span class="smcs">《易·损》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:窒抑(阻遏;抑制);窒欲(抑制欲望);窒滞(阻碍);窒沮(阻滞);窒郁(抑郁;沉闷);窒士(不得志的文人)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>窒</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 障碍物 <span class="encs">[obstacle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有窒皆通。——<span class="smcs">《丞相答刺罕赠谥制》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:窒烦(排除烦乱的心情)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/窒息">窒息</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
