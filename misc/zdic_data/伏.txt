                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>伏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>趴，脸向下，体前屈：～卧。～案读书。</li><li>低下去：此起彼～。</li><li>屈服，承认错误或受到惩罚：～法。～输。～辩（伏罪的供状，亦作“伏辨”）。</li><li>使屈服：降～。降龙～虎。</li><li>隐藏：～兵。埋～。</li><li>农历划分夏季最炎热的三个阶段：～日。～旱。～暑。～天。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">伏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>伏</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从犬。意思是:人如狗那样地匍伏着。本义:俯伏;趴下)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lie prostrate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伏，司也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伏瘞亦如之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·犬人》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“课伏犬以王车轹之。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伏，覆也。——<span class="smcs">《释名·释姿容》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寝毋伏。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雌鸡伏子。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志中之上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>子伏己而盐其脑。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一府中皆漝伏，莫敢起。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庑下一生伏案卧。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及敌枪再击，寨中人又鹜伏矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:伏尸(倒在地上的尸体，多指杀死);伏肉(死尸的肉);伏地(俯伏在地上);伏虎;伏奏(俯伏上奏);伏候(俯伏等候)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 身体前倾靠在物体上</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:伏轼(乘车。也作<span class="yhcs">“扶轼”</span>。轼:车厢前面的横木扶手);伏惟(俯伏思惟，常用于书信中的敬词)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 潜藏;埋伏 <span class="encs">[ambush]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伏，藏也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">福兮祸所伏。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物莫伏于蠱。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫大国，难测也，惧有伏焉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入</span><span class="diczx2">武关</span>， <span class="diczx2">秦</span>伏兵绝其后。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:伏莽(藏匿在丛林中的盗匪);伏道(暗藏的地道);伏龙(潜伏的龙;灶神);伏戎(埋伏军队或刺客);伏甲(埋伏武士或军队);伏弩(埋伏弓箭手)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“服”</span>。屈服，顺从。承认罪行或失败 <span class="encs">[surrender;yield;knuckle under;admit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">许</span>既伏其罪矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公十一年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骑皆伏曰:<span class="yhcs">“如大王言!”</span>——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">这个苏小卿怎肯伏低。——</span><span
