                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>钢</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鋼</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄤˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 经过精炼，不含磷砂等杂质的铁，含碳0.15～1.7％，比熟铁更坚硬更富于弹性，是工业上极其重要的原料：～板。～笔（笔头用金属制成的笔）。～铁（“钢”和“<span class="diczx3">铁</span>”，喻坚强，如“～～的意志”）。轧～。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>钢</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鋼</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>把刀在布、皮、石、或缸沿上用力摩擦几下，使锋利些：把刀～一～。</li><li>在刀口上加点儿钢（<span class="dicpy">gāng</span>），重新打造，使更锋利：这口铡刀该～了。</li></ol>
