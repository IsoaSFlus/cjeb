                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>契</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qì.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>证券，证明买卖、抵押、租赁等关系的文书：～约。～据。房～。</li><li>相合，相投：相～。～合。默～。～友。～分（<span class="dicpy">fèn </span>）（投合无间的情分）。～厚。</li><li>用刀雕刻：～刻。～舟求剑。</li><li>〔～机〕指事物转化的关键。</li><li>刻的文字，近代称甲骨文：书～。～文（甲骨文）。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>契</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiè.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄝˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">锲</span>”，用刀子刻。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>契</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiè.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄝˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古人名，中国商朝的祖先，传说是舜的臣，助禹治水有功而封于商。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> deed, contract, bond; engrave</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kontrakt, Vertrag  (S, Rechtsw)​,übereinkommen, mit etw. einverstanden sein,, entgegenkommen  (V)​,eingravieren, schnitzen, gravieren  (V, Lit)​,Zustimmung geben, Einverständnis geben  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> contrat</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>契</strong> <span class="dicpy">qì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="diczx1">后时圣人易之以书契。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌稽布之书契。——<span
