                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>缉</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">緝</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>把麻析成缕连接起来：～麻。</li>
	<li>搜捕，捉拿：～访。～私。～捕。通～。侦～。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/辑">辑</a>&rdquo;，收集。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>缉</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">緝</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>一种缝纫方法，一针对一针地缝：～鞋口。～边儿。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缉</strong></p>
<p>緝 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，表示与线丝、缠织有关。本义:绩，把麻搓成线)</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众妇夜缉灯烘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《次子由诗相庆》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缉绩(纺织，编织);缉取(搓成);缉麻(绩麻);缉妇(从事纺绩的妇女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 搜捕 <span class="encs">[seize;arrest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">访缉纠诘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缉听(向各处去搜集消息);缉凶(搜捕凶犯);缉擒(捉拿;缉拿);缉探人(侦察人员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 继续 <span class="encs">[continue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">授几有缉御。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;行苇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缉熙(本指逐渐达到光明，后因以指光明);缉御(侍者连续更替地侍候着);缉绪(继承传统)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 会合;和睦。通&ldquo;辑&rdquo; <span class="encs">[join; meet; concord; harmony]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">适道已来，四十九载，妙应事多，宜加总缉，共成区畛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广弘明集&middot;沈约&middot;佛记序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有所缉缀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《魏书&middot;高允传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转将深意谕旁人，缉缀疵瑕遣潜说。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《苦乐相倚曲》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">思所以镇定民心，缉宁外内。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴书&middot;陆抗传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还贤与能，群臣缉睦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《陈书&middot;虞荔传》</span></span></p>
