                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">渍</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>渍</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">漬</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zì.mp3"></a></span> ㄗˋ</span></p><ol><li>浸，沤：浸～。淹～。～麻。</li><li>油、泥等积在上面难以除去，亦指积在物体上面难以除去的油、泥等：油～。茶～。</li><li>染，沾染：～染。渐～。</li><li>地面的积水：～水。～涝。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> soak, steep; dye; stains; sodden</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> durchdringen, durchfeuchten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tremper,confire,tache</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">渍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>渍</strong></p><p>漬 <span class="dicpy">zì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本义:短时间浸泡)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ret;steep;soak]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渍，沤也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剥削淹渍以为菹。——<span class="smcs">《诗·楚茨》</span>笺</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水浸曰渍。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="smcs">《神农》</span>、<span class="smcs">《后稷》</span>藏种之方，煮马屎以渍种者。——<span class="smcs">《论语·商虫》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">净淘种子，渍经三宿。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾思勰</span><span class="smcs">《齐民要术》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:渍酒(浸泡在酒里);渍痕(水侵蚀的污迹);渍渍(满是汗水或泪水的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 冲洗 <span class="encs">[wash]</span>。如:渍米(淘米)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 染 <span class="encs">[dye]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">钟</span>氏染羽以朱湛丹秫，三月而炽之，淳而渍之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·考工记·钟氏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:渍浸(浸染)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 沾染 <span class="encs">[contaminate;be soiled with grease，etc.]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飞毛遍绿野，洒血渍芳丛。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜淹</span><span class="smcs">《咏寒食斗鸡应秦王教》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:渍污(污染)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 染<span class="encs">[病]</span> <span class="encs">[catch(a disease)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以视孤寡老弱之渍病，穷困颜色愁悴不赡者，心身自食之。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:渍病(染病)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 陷入 <span class="encs">[get stuck]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行而供冀，非渍淖也;行而俯项，非击戾也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:渍淖(陷入泥沼)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>渍</strong></p><p>漬 <span class="dicpy">zì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 油的沉淀或渣滓 <span class="encs">[sludge]</span>。如:油渍</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 造成污迹的东西 <span class="encs">[stain]</span>。如:水渍;茶渍</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/渍涝">渍涝</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
