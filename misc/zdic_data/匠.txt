						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>匠</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>有手艺的人：～人。木～。画～。能工巧～。</li><li>灵巧，巧妙：独具～心。</li><li>具有某一方面熟练技能，但平庸板滞，缺乏独到之处：～气。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>匠</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从匚( <span class="dicpy">fāng</span>)，盛放工具的筐器，从斤(斧)。工具筐里放着斧头等工具，表示从事木工。本义:木工)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carpenter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匠，木工也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">攻木之工七:轮、舆、弓、庸、匠、车、梓。匠人建国，匠人营国，匠人为沟洫。——<span class="smcs">《考工记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执斧以涖匠师。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·乡师》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“主丰碑之事。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂匠纳车于阶间。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·既夕记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“匠人主载柩窆。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:匠氏(木匠);匠者(木工);匠营(土木工)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 亦泛指工匠 <span class="encs">[artisan]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">能剒削柱梁谓之木匠;能穴凿穴埳谓之土匠，能雕琢文书谓之史匠。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·量知》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:匠作(工匠，以技艺做工谋生的人);匠役(工匠，做工的仆役);匠户(世业的工匠);匠指(工匠);匠门弃材(比喻无用之人，自称的谦词)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指在某一方面造诣高深的人 <span class="encs">[master]</span>。如:匠手(技艺高明的能手);匠石(古代名石的巧匠);匠意(精巧的心思。同<span class="yhcs">“匠心”</span>);匠郢(比喻技艺高明)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>匠</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 治理 <span class="encs">[manage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匠，治也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:匠世(治世);匠理(精心治理)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 教 <span class="encs">[educate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今私门之正匠兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·哀命》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“教也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:匠化(教化);匠成(培养造就)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 制作;创造 <span class="encs">[make]</span>。如:匠费(施工的费用);匠资(施工的费用)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
