                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>户</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>一扇门，门：门～。窗～。～枢不蠹。夜不闭～。</li>
	<li>人家：～口。～主。门～之见（亦指派别上的成见）。</li>
	<li>会计部门称账册上有业务关系的团体或个人：～头。开～。</li>
	<li>门第：门当～对。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">户</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>户</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象门(門)字的一半。汉字部首之一。从&ldquo;户&rdquo;的多与门户有关。本义:单扇门)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[door]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">户，半门曰户。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一扇曰户，两扇曰门。又在于堂室东曰户，在于宅区域曰门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窥其户。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;丰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">设于户西。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未有入室而不由户者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木兰当户织。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当户理红妆。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出户望南山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出户南望。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窗户皆闭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:户下(门边;户住的属下。多指奴婢或门客);户牡(门钥);户门(守门的人);户钥(门上开关的锁钥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 住户，人家。一家称一户 <span class="encs">[family]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其邑人三百户。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;讼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">户说以眇论。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">户裁及万。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">户出一男。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为一户者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:全户人口;几百户人家;户帖(登记每户人口籍贯、名义的册子);户版(登记居民户籍的簿册)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 户籍(登记户口的册籍) <span class="encs">[census register]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:户版(户籍;户口);户贯(户籍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 屋室 <span class="encs">[house]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">初九，不出户庭，无咎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;节卦&middot;象辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不出户知天下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子&middot;四七》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 出入口 <span class="encs">[hole]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:洞户(洞穴，洞口)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 酒量 <span class="encs">[capacity for liquor;one&#39;s drinking capacity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酒户年年减，山行渐渐难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《元稹&middot;春游诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 从事某种职业的人或家庭 <span class="encs">[a person or family of some occupations]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:猎户，农业户</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 门第 <span class="encs">[family status]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:她看来倒是门当户对</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=户" target="_blank">户</a>】
                              戶·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=4" target="_blank">4</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6236.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㦿<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23958.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣥘</span>【唐韻】【正韻】侯古切【集韻】【韻會】後五切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音祜。【說文】護也。【釋名】所以謹護閉塞也。【六書精薀】室之口也。凡室之口曰戶，堂之口曰門。內曰戶，外曰門。一扉曰戶，兩扉曰門。【易&middot;節卦】不出戶庭。【詩&middot;豳風】塞向墐戶。</p>
<p>又民居曰編戶。【唐六典】戶部掌天下戶口。</p>
<p>又止也。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】屈蕩戶之。【註】止也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;王嘉傳】坐戶殿門失闌免。【註】掌守殿門，止不當入者，而失闌入之。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋地】觚竹北戶。【註】北戶，南方之國。【疏】卽日南郡也。師古曰：言其在日之南，所謂北戶以向日者。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;月令】蟄蟲咸動，啓戶始出。【註】戶，猶穴也。</p>
<p>又飮酒有大小戶。【吳志】孫皓每饗宴，人以七升爲限，小戶雖不入，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>澆灌取盡。【白居易詩】戶大嫌甜酒。</p>
<p>又姓。漢有戶尊。</p>
<p>又與昈通。文采貌。【揚雄&middot;蜀都賦】戶豹能黃。</p>
<p>又與濩通。湯樂名。【揚雄&middot;蜀都賦】戶音六成。</p>
<p>又翁姑切，音烏。【淮南子&middot;時則訓】烏孫國，作戶孫。</p>
