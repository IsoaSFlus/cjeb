                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>讲</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">講</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>说，谈：～话。～叙。</li><li>把事情和道理说出来：～说。～学。～武。～演。～义。～师。～坛。</li><li>注重某一方面，并设法使它实现：～求。～团结。</li><li>和解：“而秦未与魏～也”。</li><li>商量，商议：～价儿。～条件。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">讲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>讲</strong></p><p>講 <span class="dicpy">jiǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，冓( <span class="dicpy">gōu</span>)声。本义:和解)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[become reconciled]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讲，和解也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>未与 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>讲也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·西周策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>讲罢兵。——<span class="smcs">《史记·樗里子甘茂传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以中情出，小曰閒，大曰讲。——<span class="smcs">《大载礼记·千乘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卒使公子</span><span class="diczx2">池</span>以三城讲于三国，之兵乃退。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引兵而归，因使人索六城于</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>而讲。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:讲息(和解息争);讲贡(讲和纳贡);讲款(讲和，议款);讲盟(订盟媾和)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 讲习(共同讨论研习学问) <span class="encs">[give lecture and study]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">道士讲火经。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讲中外之故。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:讲颂(讲习诵读);讲读(讲习诵读);讲肄(讲习);讲肆(讲舍);讲贯(讲习);讲帷(讲堂);讲筵(讲席;讲坛。筵:古人席地而坐所铺的席)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 练习;操练 <span class="encs">[train]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">田猎以讲武。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:讲武(讲习武艺)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 用 <span class="encs">[use]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邀见讲钧礼。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 评议;论说 <span class="encs">[appraise sth. through discussion]</span>。如:讲戏(评戏);讲功(评功);讲树(在树下谈论);讲经(论究经义);讲动(传说开来，议论起来);讲古(谈论古人、古事)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 说，用言语表达 <span class="encs">[say;speak;tell]</span>。如:少讲;讲学;讲析(讲话解析);讲英语;讲法语;讲倒(说妥);细讲;讲故事;这节课讲牛顿第一定律;讲课;讲章(高头讲章，八股文读本书眉上面的讲解文字)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 协商、谈判 <span
