                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>刚</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">剛</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>硬，坚强，与“<span class="diczx3">柔</span>”相对：～强。～直。～烈。～劲（姿态、风格等挺拔有力）。～健。～毅。～决。～正不阿（<span class="dicpy">ē</span>）。～愎自用。</li><li>恰好，恰巧：～～（a.恰好；b.才）。～好。</li><li>才：～才。～来就走。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>刚</strong></p><p>剛 <span class="dicpy">gāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，冈声。本义:坚硬)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刚，坚也。——<span class="smcs">《增韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刚，彊断也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挢然刚折。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·臣道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">断之以刚。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔则茹之，刚则吐之。——<span class="smcs">《诗经·烝民》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:刚巨(坚硬锐利的爪);刚条(坚硬的树枝);刚木(木质坚硬的树木);刚土(硬土)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 坚强 <span class="encs">[firm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刚，强也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔弱胜刚强。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刚而塞。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾未见刚者。——<span class="smcs">《论语·公冶长》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">强者必刚斗其意。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·立本》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">鲍叔牙</span>为人，刚愎而上悍。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“刚毅木讷，近仁。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《论语·子路》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:刚明(严明);刚绠(刚强正直);刚气儿(刚强的气质);刚傲(刚强傲岸);刚猛(刚强勇猛)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 刚正，刚直方正 <span class="encs">[upright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瑞生平为学，以刚为主，因自号刚峰，天下称刚峰先生。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">祭彤</span>武节刚方，动用安重。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·祭彤传》</span>论</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:刚特(刚正而不随流俗);刚峭(刚正严峻);刚亮(刚正信实);刚方(刚直方正)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 倔强固执 <span class="encs">[forward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">性刚愎，好自用。——<span class="smcs">《金史·赤盏合喜传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:刚戾(固执凶暴;不讲道理);刚褊(固执而气量狭小);刚求(强求);刚隘(刚愎褊急);刚狠(刚愎)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 刚直 <span class="encs">[outspoken and upright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然而刚肠激发，不遑辞候，惊扰宫中，复忤宾客。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span
