<p>● <strong>迸</strong></p>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>迸</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (从辵，并声。&ldquo;辵&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)意思是在路上行走。本义:奔散，走散)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[run away;flee in disorder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">督将迸走，死伤过半。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;满宠传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迸豆之疾(像豆子倒入锅内时乱迸那样快);迸窜(逃窜);迸逸(逃窜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 涌出;喷射 <span class="encs">[gush;spout]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">银瓶乍破水浆迸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迸泉(涌出的泉水);迸撺(喷涌飞溅);迸放(喷射发出)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 爆开，断裂 <span class="encs">[burst;break]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">打得眼棱缝裂，乌珠迸出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迸穴(迸裂而成的洞穴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 冒出，突然向外发出 <span class="encs">[spout;spurt;burst forth]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:迸竹(突出的竹子);迸云(穿云。喻高)</span></p>
