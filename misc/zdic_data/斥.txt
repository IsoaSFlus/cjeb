                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>斥</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>责备：～责。怒～。驳～。训～。</li>
	<li>指，指出：～谬（指出错误）。</li>
	<li>使退去，使离开：～退。</li>
	<li>开拓：开地～境。</li>
	<li>多，广：充～。</li>
	<li>侦察，伺望：～候（旧时侦察敌情的士兵）。</li>
	<li>盐碱地：～卤。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/尺">尺</a>&rdquo;，尺蠖。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">斥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>斥</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，屰( <span class="dicpy">n&igrave;</span>)声。从&ldquo;广&rdquo;，表示与房屋有关。&ldquo;屰&rdquo;(不顺)也兼表字义，表示人从房屋里逆行而出。本义:驱逐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[drive out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斥，却屋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。谓却退其屋不居。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无益于民者斥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">击吉斥之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;江都易王非传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斥小疏弱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;天官书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">程</span>一日果以眦睚杀人，上大怒，立命斥出，付京兆。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斥放(斥退放逐);斥遣(斥退，驱逐);斥远(摒拒疏远);斥去(排斥并使之离去)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 责备。又称:斥詈，斥遣 <span class="encs">[tongue-lash]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">目</span><span class="diczx2">晋侯</span>斥杀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;僖公五年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 如:申斥(斥责);驳斥(反驳错误的言论或意见)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 开拓，开 <span class="encs">[open up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斥，开也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塞斥之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">除边开益斥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斥地(斥土。开拓土地;开土地为冢圹);斥道(开辟道路)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 黜免 <span class="encs">[dismiss]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是</span><span class="diczx2">孔丘</span>斥逐于 <span class="diczx2">鲁君</span>，曾不用于世也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;利议》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝拜而不道，夕斥之矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《封建论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斥罢(罢免其职务);斥免(斥罢。罢免)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指出 <span class="encs">[point out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:斥谬(指出谬误);斥正(指正)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 侦察 <span class="encs">[reconnoitre]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斥候而服事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斥兵(侦察兵);斥堠(侦察敌情的士兵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 废弃 <span class="encs">[abandon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:斥弃(抛弃);斥女(被抛弃之女)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/65A5.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>昌石切，音尺。同㡿。逐也，遠也。【左傳&middot;昭十六年】大國之求，無禮以斥之。何饜之有。【史記&middot;淮南王傳】王使郞中令斥免。【註】屛斥。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;禹貢】海濱廣斥。【釋文】斥謂地鹹。【前漢&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>法志】除山川沈斥。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;弓人】斥蠖。【註】屈蟲也。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;襄十一年】納斥<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>。【註】不相備也。【史記&middot;李廣傳】然亦遠斥<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>，未嘗遇害。【註】索隱曰：斥，度也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>，視也，望也。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;襄三十一年】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨥</span>盜充斥。【註】充滿斥見，言其多。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】除邊關之益斥。【註】斥，廣也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;惠帝紀】視作斥土者。【註】如淳曰：斥，開也。</p>
<p>又【後漢&middot;孔融傳】擬斥乗輿。【註】斥，指也。</p>
<p>又【張衡&middot;西京賦】絕阬踰斥。【註】斥，澤厓也。</p>
<p>又山名。【爾雅&middot;釋地】東北之美者，有斥山之文皮焉。○按《釋文》音尺。《廣韻》《集韻》入禡韻，充夜切，俱書作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221DB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇛</span>，疑誤。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】見姓纂。</p>
<p>又【集韻】闥各切，音託。【莊子&middot;田子方】揮斥八極。【註】揮斥，猶放縱也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】恥格切。與坼同。詳土部坼字註。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶敕略切。【史記&middot;自序】王遷辟淫，良將是斥。叶上爵。</p>
