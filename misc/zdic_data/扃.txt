						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>扃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiōng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄥˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>从外面关门的闩、钩等：～键（锁钥）。</li>
	<li>上闩，关门：和门昼～。</li>
	<li>门户：步于山～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a door bar placed outside a door</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schließen, geschlossen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Jiong (roi)​,fermer,verrou</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扃</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从户，冋( <span class="dicpy">jiǒng</span>)声。本义:从外面关门的门闩)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bolt for fastening a door from outside]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扃，外闭之关也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入户奉扃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">固扃鐍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;胠箧》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">应门闭兮禁闼扃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;外戚传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">固扃鐍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扃关(门闩);扃鐍(门窗或箱箧上的关锁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 车上用来固定武器和插旗的横木 <span class="encs">[bar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脱扃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span>。注:&ldquo;车上兵阑。&rdquo;</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;横木校轮间，一曰车前横木也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旗不脱扃，结驷方蕲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鼎上贯穿两耳的横杠 <span class="encs">[bar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扃，鼎扃。以木贯鼎，所以闭鼎者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庙门容大扃七个。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;匠人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">设扃鼎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左人抽扃鼎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士虞礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 门，门户 <span class="encs">[door]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扃键(门户的关锁);扃室(墓室);扃禁(宫禁);扃牖(门窗)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扃</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>上闩、关门 <span class="encs">[shut a door]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扃牖而居。(关着门窗呆在里面，关闭。牖，窗户。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扃门(关门);扃户(闭户);扃闭(关闭);扃试(指科举时代考生各闭一室应答试题);扃绢(拘禁，羁绊)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=户" target="_blank">户</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6243.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221FA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢇺</span>【唐韻】古熒切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】涓熒切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音駉。【說文】外閉之關也。【禮&middot;曲禮】入戶奉扃。【註】扃，門關木也。入戶之時，兩手當心，如奉扃然，雖瞻視而不回轉，嫌於干人私也。</p>
<p>又車前橫木。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】楚人惎之脫扃。【註】扃，車上橫木，所以約車上之兵器。</p>
<p>又【集韻】犬迥切，音褧。扃扃，明察也。</p>
<p>又與鉉通。【儀禮&middot;士喪禮】右人左執七抽扃。【註】扃卽鉉字。</p>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6243.svg">                          <p>外閉之關也。从戶冋聲。古熒切文十　重一</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6243_2153.svg" /><span class="swbf">(扃)</span><span class="ywyy">外閉之關也。</span><span class="swdz">關者，以木横持門戶也。曲禮：入戶奉扃。注曰：奉扃，敬也。孔疏曰：奉扃之說多家。今謂禮有鼎扃，所以關鼎。今關戶之木與關鼎相似。凡常奉扃之時，必兩手向心而奉之。今入戶雖不奉扃木，其手若奉扃然。以其手對戶若奉扃，言恭敬也。玉裁謂下文言戶開亦開，戶<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D75.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨵵</span>亦<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D75.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨵵</span>，知戶<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D75.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨵵</span>而入，用兩手推戶爲奉扃。若戶開而入，則兩手不偏可矣。戶扃，葢以木横著於戶爲之機，令外可閉者。鼎關字正作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2056A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠕪</span>。禮古文叚扃爲之。車上所以止旗者亦曰扃。</span><span class="ywyy">从戶冋聲。</span><span class="swdz">古熒切。十一部。</span></p>                          </div>
