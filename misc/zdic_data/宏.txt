data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，表示与家室房屋有关，厷( <span class="dicpy">gōng</span>)声。本义:屋子宽大而深)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[spacious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宏，屋深响也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“屋深也。各本深下衍响字，此因下文‘屋响’而误，今依<span class="smcs">《韵会》</span>、<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span>、<span class="smcs">《类篇》</span>正。…屋深者，其内深广也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宏我邦我家。——<span class="smcs">《毛公鼎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宏，按:深大之屋凡声如有应响。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 大;宏大 <span class="encs">[grand;great]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宏，大也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释诂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若保宏父。——<span class="smcs">《书·酒诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举其宏纲。——<span class="smcs">《书·序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其器宏以弇。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·孟冬》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:宏才(大才);宏硕(大儒硕学。指有学问的人);宏纲(大纲);宏宏(广大的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 宏伟 <span class="encs">[magnificent]</span>。如:宏构(宏伟的建筑);宏整(宏伟整齐;宏伟严谨);宏盛(宏伟美盛);宏规(宏伟的规模)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 博大 <span class="encs">[extensive]</span>。如:宏达(才智广达博通);宏洽(博洽);宏通(博大通彻);宏贯(博大贯通)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 广泛 <span class="encs">[wide]</span>。如:宏扬(广泛宣扬);宏溥(普遍，遍及);宏敷(广布);宏览(广泛浏览)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 远大，深远 <span class="encs">[long-range;profound and lasting]</span>。如:宏规(远大的规划;深远的谋略);宏毅(志向远大，意志坚强)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>宏</strong> <span
