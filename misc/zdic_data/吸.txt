                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>吸</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，及声。本义:吸气入体内)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[inhale]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吹呴呼吸，吐故纳新。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·刻意》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不食五谷，吸风引露。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:吸呼(呼吸);吸留(變滓喘吸的样子);吸风(吸气。道家辟谷养生之术。谓吸食天地之精气;亦作吸西北风。比喻挨饿);吸新吐故(吸进新气，吐出浊气)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 张开口吸，急饮 <span class="encs">[drink]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吸湛露之浮凉兮，漱凝霜之?雰雰。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九章·悲回风》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左相嵧瘤鶹日兴费万钱，饮如长鲸吸百川。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《饮中八仙歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:吸食(用嘴吸进<span class="encs">[某些食物、毒物等]</span>);吸霞(吸食朝霞。道家用以修炼养生);吸露(吸饮露水。喻高洁)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 吸取;吸收;摄取 <span class="encs">[draw into]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久之，则吸纳他族互相同化，则不同血统而同一民族者有之。——</span><span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《政治学大家伯伦知理之学说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:吸纳(吸收容纳);吸集(吸收汇集);吸利(搜括钱财)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 引;吸引 <span class="encs">[attract]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">寿峰</span>手上就像有吸铁石一样，完全都吸到手上。—— <span class="diczx2">张恨水</span><span class="smcs">《啼笑姻缘》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:异性相吸;吸毒石(相传能治毒的药石)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“翕”</span>。聚合<span class="encs">[get together]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风尘吸张。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:吸逮(气聚集而不绝);吸聚(翕聚，汇聚)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 亲近 <span class="encs">[be intimate]</span>。如:吸习(翕习。附和亲近，习以为常)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 动 <span
