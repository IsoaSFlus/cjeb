                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>凭</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">憑</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>靠在东西上：～栏。～吊（对着遗迹怀念）。</li>	<li>依靠，仗恃：～借。～靠。～信。</li>	<li>根据：～票入场。</li>	<li>证据：～据。文～。～空。～证。空口无～。</li>	<li>由着，听任：任～。听～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凭</strong></p>
<p>凴、憑 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从几，从任。几，矮而小的桌子。任，凭借。本义:倚靠)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lean on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恁，依也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">某自幼与</span><span class="diczx2">周郎</span>同窗交契，愿凭三寸不烂之舌，往江东说此人来降。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或凭几学书。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">独自莫凭栏，无限江山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李煜</span><span class="smcs">《浪淘沙》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凭几(凭靠几案);凭肩(以手靠在别人肩上);凭脉(搭脉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 依托;仰仗 <span class="encs">[count on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不凭其子，</span><span class="diczx2">灵公</span>夺而里之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;则阳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凭恃其众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凭附(依附;依靠);凭妖(依托妖言);凭狐(喻依仗他人权势的人);凭城(据城以守);凭系(依托维系);凭负(依恃);凭托(依仗，依托);凭高(登临高处);凭虚(凌空)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 根据 <span class="encs">[base on]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:凭按(据以查验);凭限(官员赴任文凭上规定的期限);凭说(辩白的证据);凭臆(凭主观推测立说);凭应(依据瑞应);凭断(据以判断);凭龟(以龟卜为据)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凭</strong></p>
<p>憑 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>证书 <span class="encs">[certificate]</span>。</p>
