                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>孩</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄞˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>儿童，引申为子女：男～儿。～童。～提（指幼儿时期）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> baby, child; children</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kind  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> enfant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">孩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>孩</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从子，亥声。本义:小儿笑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。同&ldquo;咳&rdquo; <span class="encs">[child laugh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咳，小儿笑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。古文咳从子。内则，孟子则作此字。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孤女藐焉始孩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《寡妇赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:未孩(还不会笑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 当作婴儿看待 <span class="encs">[treat as baby]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人在天下，歙歙为天下浑其心，百姓皆注其耳目，圣人皆孩之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抚爱 <span class="encs">[caress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伏惟陛下，昧旦坐朝，留心政术，明罚以纠诸侯，申恩以孩百姓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北齐书》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>孩</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>幼小;幼稚 <span class="encs">[young]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孩，少也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忆昔十五心尚孩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《百忧集行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孩赤无知(年幼无知);孩幼(幼儿);孩抱(幼小);孩乳(幼小);孩婴(幼小)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>孩</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 幼儿 <span class="encs">[infant;child]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孩，始生小儿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孩提，二三岁之间在襁褓，知孩笑可提抱者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;尽心上》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生孩六月，慈父见背。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李密</span><span class="smcs">《陈情表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孩中颜(幼儿的面色);孩幼(幼儿);孩儿(幼儿);孩稚(幼儿);孩婴(幼儿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 未成年的人;孩子 <span class="encs">[child]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">纣</span>为孩子时， <span class="diczx2">微子</span>诸其不善之性。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;本性》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孩提赤子初心(孩子的纯洁之心);孩子的房儿(衣胞);孩气(孩子般的脾气或神态);男孩;女孩;天生自诩是天才，也把天才奖妇孩</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 胎儿 <span class="encs">[fetus]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:她几个月没来月经了，看来有孩儿了</span></p>
