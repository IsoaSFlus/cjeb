class="encs">[cut;sever]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巀，断也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">截彼淮浦，王师之所。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·常武》</span>。笺:<span class="diczx1">径截辎重。——</span><span class="diczx2">李华</span><span class="smcs">《吊古战场文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> class="encs">[buy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怕无布绢，将现钱去长街上铺内截。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">宫天挺</span><span class="smcs">《范张鸣黍》</span></p><p><span
