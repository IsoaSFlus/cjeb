class="encs">[muddy;turbid]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">溷，浊也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刚柔分动。——<span class="smcs">《易·噬嗑卦》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“不溷乃明。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天气溷浊。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·翼奉传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桥内峡中有池一圆，近流水而不溷。——<span class="diczx1">世溷浊而嫉贤兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事类溷错，易为轻重。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·陈宠传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
