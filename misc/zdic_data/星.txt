                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">星</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>星</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄥˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>天文学上指宇宙间能发光的或反射光的天体；一般指夜间天空中发光的天体：～球。恒～。行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）～。卫～。披～戴月。</li>	<li>细碎的小颗粒东西：火～儿。</li>	<li>秤等衡器上记数的点：定盘～。</li>	<li>军官衣领上的徽记：五～将军。</li>	<li>形容夜间：～行。～奔。</li>	<li>星名，二十八宿之一：～宿。</li>	<li>像星一样排列，分散：～～点点。</li>	<li>喻某一方面新出现的杰出人物：影～。歌～。</li>	<li>古代妇女面上所饰的花点。</li>	<li>以星象推算吉凶祸福的方术：～术。～相（<span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;ng </span>）。</li>	<li>原子核物理学上指高能粒子射入核乳胶或云室、气泡室时发现有许多径迹从一点发出的现象。</li>	<li>国际通用的衡量宾馆、饭店的等级标准：～级。五～饭店。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">星</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>星</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从晶，生声。晶象形。本义:星星)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[star]</span> 宇宙间发射或反射光的天体</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">星，万物之精，上为列星。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，散文则统谓之星，对文则五纬为星，二十八宿为辰。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日月星辰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四曰星辰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;星，五星也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">列星随旋，日月递炤。(炤:照耀)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:星筹(寿算之筹);星霜(比喻年月);星桥(神话传说中的鹊桥。也作银桥);星布(天星密布);星列(天星罗列)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 星宿，中国古代指星座 <span class="encs">[constellation]</span>。共分二十八宿(星群)。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:星宫(星神);星回(星宿回转固位。谓一年将尽);星亡(星宿隐没不见)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 天文 <span class="encs">[astronomy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">文史星历，近乎卜祝之间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:星工(通晓星象的人);星文(星象);星变(星象的异常变化)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古时特指妇女装饰面颊的美容花点 <span class="encs">[tilak]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 戥、秤等衡器上记斤、两、钱的标志点 <span class="encs">[small gradation marks on the arm of a steelyard]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:定盘星;准星;秤星</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 夜，星夜。多用于连夜赶路 <span class="encs">[night]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:星前月下(指男女幽会的时间或处所);星行(早夜急行);星步(凭星而行);星归(连夜赶回)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 用以表示等级 <span class="encs">[class]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:三星级饭店;五星上将</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 星相术 <span class="encs">[astrology]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:星象恶曜(逢着灾星，厄运当头);星宿(列星。代指人的时运，命数);星家(通晓天文术数、算命看相的人);星宿不利(迷信指本命星不吉利);占星;星卜</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 某物质一小块、一小片(断)或数量很小的一份 <span class="encs">[bit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:星星(一点儿;一些儿);一星半点</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>星</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形容多而分散的 <span class="encs">[disperse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:星居(分散布列);星处;零星;星弗(多而乱);星罗棋布;星离(天星分散)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 斑白。多指鬓发 <span class="encs">[white]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诗老相过鬓已星，吟魂未减昔年清。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王伯大</span><span class="smcs">《赠戴石屏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:星鬓(发白的鬓发);星发(花白的头发)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 事物与帝王有关的 <span class="encs">[imperial]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:星舆;星骑;星闱(皇宫);星关(宫禁，皇居);星骑(帝王的使者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 迅速的，跟流星一样的 <span class="encs">[rapid]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:星铄梭光(像星闪烁，像飞梭掠过。形容疾快);星驰(赶快;匆忙);星忙(匆忙);星火(火速)</span></p>
