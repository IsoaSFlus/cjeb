                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>硕</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">碩</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shuò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuò.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>〔～士〕学位名，高于“学士”。</li><li>大：～老。～材。～学（博学，亦指博学的人）。～壮。～果累累（喻巨大的成绩）。～大无朋（形容无比的大）。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>硕</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">碩</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">石</span>”，形容坚固。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>硕</strong></p><p>碩 <span class="dicpy">shuò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xié</span>)，石声。页，头。本义:头大)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[big-headed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">硕，头大也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为大 <span class="encs">[big]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">硕鼠硕鼠，无食我黍。——<span class="smcs">《诗·魏风·硕鼠》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">硕果不食。——<span class="smcs">《易·剥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼其之子，硕大无朋。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·椒聊》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:壮硕;肥硕;硕德(大德);硕见(高见);硕大;硕画;硕望;硕量;硕谋(远大的谋略);硕虑(深远的思虑)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“石”</span>。比喻坚固 <span class="encs">[solid]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孤与将军，恩如骨肉…而忍绝王命，明弃硕交，实为佞人所构会也。——<span class="smcs">《文选·阮瑀·为曹公作书与孙权》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 学识渊博、德高望重的 <span class="encs">[erudite;broad and profound]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又患无硕师名人与游，尝趋百里之外从乡之先达执经叩问。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弱冠游学都下，通儒硕学，必造门质疑。——<span class="smcs">《南史·顾越传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:硕老(年高望重的博学之士);硕彦(品节高尚、学问渊博的名流);硕隽(学识渊博、俊拔出众的人才)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/硕大无朋">硕大无朋</a></span><span
