                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阴</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>阴</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">陰</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>中国古代哲学认为宇宙中通贯所有物质的两大对立面诸一，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">阳</span>&rdquo;相对：～阳。一～一阳谓之道。～差阳错。～盛阳衰。～虚生热。图形：⚋(U+268B)。</li>
	<li>指&ldquo;月亮&rdquo;：太～（月亮）。～历。</li>
	<li>带负电的：～电。～极。～离子。</li>
	<li>云层较厚，遮住阳光：～沉。～雨。～郁（亦指忧郁，不开朗）。～霾。</li>
	<li>不见阳光，亦指不见阳光的地方：～面。～干（<span class="dicpy">gān</span>）。～凉，～影。山～（山的北面，水的南面）。碑～（碑的背面）。</li>
	<li>不露出表面的，暗中的：～沟。～通（秘密往来）。～私。～功（➊暗中做的好事；➋迷信指被阴间记功的好事）。</li>
	<li>背地捣鬼，险恶：～谋。～毒。</li>
	<li>指冥间：～间。～司。～曹地府。</li>
	<li>凹进的：～文图章。～识（即阴文）。</li>
	<li>指时间：光～。惜寸～。</li>
	<li>生殖器：～部。～道。～茎。～囊。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阴</strong></p>
<p>陰、隂 <span class="dicpy">yīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从阜( <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span>)，从侌，侌( <span class="dicpy">yīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span> )亦声。阜，土山，从阜多与地形有关。简化字属会意，表示月夜笼罩山冈，很阴暗。本义:山的北面，水的南面)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[north of a hill or south of river]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陰，闇也。山之北，水之南也。从阜，从侌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸣鹤在阴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;中孚》</span>。按，二在兑泽之中，艮山之下，故称阴。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">壜上张赤挛阴羽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;王会》</span></span></p>
