                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弹</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>弹</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">彈</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄢˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>可以用力发射出去的小丸；装有爆炸物可以击毁人、物的武器：～丸。子～。～雨。枪～。氢～。导～。手榴～。原子～。</li>	<li>古代指以竹为弦的弓：&ldquo;左挟～，右摄丸&rdquo;。</li></ol><p>● <strong>弹</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">彈</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">t&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用手或工具拨动而发射出去，亦指用手指拨弄：～射。～跳。～拨。～指。～琴。～奏。～冠相庆（喻即将作官而互相庆贺，多作贬义）。</li>	<li>检举违法失职的官吏：～劾。～纠。讥～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pellet, bullet, shot, shell</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> jouer du piano ou d'un instrument à corde,lancer,sauter,élastique,balle,boulette,obus,bombe</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弹</strong></p>
<p>彈 <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从弓，单声。本义:弹丸)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bullet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弹，行丸也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">长安</span>大道边，挟弹谁家儿?&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏涣</span><span class="smcs">《变律》</span>其三</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枪弹;弹子涡(卵石);枪林弹雨(形容战斗激烈);弹丸黑子(形容地域狭小)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 形状像弹丸的东西，如圆形的果实;禽鸟的卵等 <span class="encs">[a bullet-shaped thing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其法乃以凫弹数十，黄、白各聚一器。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周密</span><span class="smcs">《齐东野语》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 弹弓 <span class="encs">[slingshot]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 炸弹 <span class="encs">[bomb]</span>。</p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弹</strong></p>
<p>彈 <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从弓，单声。本义:用弹弓发射弹丸)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shoot with the function of a spring;eject]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弹，行丸也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">灵公</span>]</span>从台上弹人，而观其辟丸也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弹力(弹射的力量);弹丸(供弹弓发射用的泥丸、石丸、铁丸。也指子弹弹头);弹射(利用弹力、压力等射出);弹金(发射弹丸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 弹击 <span class="encs">[fluff;strike]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新沐者必弹冠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;渔父》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">倚柱弹其剑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复弹其铗。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复弹其剑铗。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善弹琵琶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弹唱(一边弹奏，一边演唱);弹冠(弹去冠上的灰尘);弹棉花;弹弓(手工弹花的工具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用手指拨动而演奏 <span class="encs">[play]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弹琴看文君，春风吹鬓影。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《咏怀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弹琴;弹击(演奏乐器);弹弦(弹丝。弹奏弦乐器);弹纬(张弦弹奏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 批评，揭发 <span class="encs">[criticize; expose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旌善以兴化，弹邪而矫俗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;陶谦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仆常好人讥弹其文。有不善者，应时改定。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《与杨德祖》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抨弹(抨击);弹议(弹劾评议);弹黜(弹劾罢黜);弹文(弹劾官员过错的奏疏);弹正(纠弹;纠正);弹断(讥评);弹激(激烈率直地予以抨击)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 针刺 <span class="encs">[puncture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弹疽不严，必半复聚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 测定重量 <span class="encs">[weight]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拿来天平上弹着。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot;佚名<span class="smcs">《陈州粜米》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弹算(掂量估模);弹斤估两(形容掂量轻重)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
