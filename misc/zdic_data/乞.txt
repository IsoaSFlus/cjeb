data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>本作<span class="diczx1">操瓢而乞者。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·盗跖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乞食于野人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以钱币乞之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《伤仲永》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乞为囚虏之不暇。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">瑞</span>亦屡疏乞休。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:乞化(乞讨;化缘);乞言(求取可行的善言);乞邻(向邻人求取);乞粮(求人济助粮食);乞活(到有粮之地就食求生);乞募(求募);乞麾(求住地方长官);乞灵(求助于神灵或某种神威);乞请(乞讨)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为请求，希望 <span class="encs">[ask;request;hope]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乞，求也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宋公</span>使来乞师，公辞之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公四年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:乞匄(乞求);乞免(求人赦免;自请免官);乞师(求人出兵相助);乞鞫(请求复审);乞念(乞求考虑);乞哀(乞求怜悯、饶恕);乞和(求和);乞命(请求宽宥生命)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 被;叫 <span class="encs">[be+…-ed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">那和尚猛可地乞他摔住。——<span class="smcs">《清平山堂话本》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乞那婆子缠不过，便道:<span class="yhcs">“你放了手，我去便了。”</span>——<span
