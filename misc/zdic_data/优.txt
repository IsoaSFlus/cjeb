                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">优</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>优</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3"><img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">優</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄡˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>美好的，出众的，与&ldquo;劣&rdquo;相对：～良。～等。</li>
	<li>充足，富裕：～裕。～厚。</li>
	<li>优待：拥军～属。</li>
	<li>旧时指演戏的人：～伶。俳～（滑稽杂耍艺人）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> superior, excellent; actor</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> vorzüglich, ausgezeichnet  (Adj)​,besorgt sein ,Mime, Schauspieler, Schauspielerin  (S)​,bevorzugt behandeln  (V)​,reichlich, um Überfluss  (Adj)​,You  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> excellent,supérieur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">优</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>优</strong></p>
<p>優 <span class="dicpy">yōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，尤声。本义:古代表演乐舞、杂戏的艺人。宋元以后，亦泛称戏曲艺人、演员)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[actor;actress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">优，饶也。一曰倡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，倡者本训，饶者假借。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈氏</span><span class="diczx2">鲍氏</span>之圉人为优。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十八年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公之优曰施。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信谗喜优。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;越语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">优笑在前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span>。注:&ldquo;倡俳也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">优</span><span class="diczx2">孟</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所爱倡优巧匠之属。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;灌夫传》</span>。注:&ldquo;谐戏者也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">优施</span>曰:&ldquo;&hellip;我优也，言无邮。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:优人杂剧(乐舞戏曲);优孤(专演老年男子的优人);优唱(艺人的说唱);优语(优在演戏中戏谑嘲讽的话);优歌(优人唱的歌曲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>优</strong></p>
<p>優 <span class="dicpy">yōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 充足，富裕 <span class="encs">[abundant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">优，饶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">优，多也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">优，渥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维其优矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;瞻夘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汙池渊沼川泽，谨其时禁，故鱼鳖优多而百姓有余用也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:优稳(优裕安逸);优饶(富裕，充裕);优佚(富裕，安逸);优实(富裕);优足(富足);优渥(丰足;优厚);优产(丰富的财产)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 雨水充沛 <span class="encs">[have adequate rainfall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既优既渥，既沾既足，生我百谷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;信南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 优良;美好;优越。跟&ldquo;劣&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[excellent;fine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">优劣得所。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
