                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>活</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄛˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>生存，有生命的，能生长，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">死</span>&rdquo;相对：～人。～体。～检。～物。</li>
	<li>救活，使人活：～人无数。</li>
	<li>在活的状态下：～埋。</li>
	<li>逼真地：～脱。～像。</li>
	<li>不固定，可移动，或处在进去状态的：～塞（<span class="dicpy">sāi </span>）。～扣。～页本。耳软心～（没主见）。</li>
	<li>生动，不呆板，机灵，有生气：～力。</li>
	<li>工作或生产品：～儿。～计。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">活</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>活</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，昏( <span class="dicpy">ku&ograve;</span>)声。本义:流水声。用本义时读( <span class="dicpy">guō</span>))</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 生存。与&ldquo;死&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[live;alive;living]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不我活兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;击鼓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可活。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;太甲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民非水火不生活。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巫何活焉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;天问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:鱼在水里才能活;这个孩子只活三个月;活到高龄而且精力充沛;活头儿(生存的趣味)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 救活 <span class="encs">[save lives]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣活之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:活人(使人活，救活他人);活国(救国)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 养活 <span class="encs">[bring up]</span>。如:活身(养活自身)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 复生 <span class="encs">[become alive]</span>。如:他死了好半天又活了</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>活</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 有生气;生动;活泼 <span class="encs">[lively;vivacious]</span>。如:活脱(活泼，灵活);活跳(活跃;活泼);活喇喇(形容充满生气和活力);活变(活泼;机灵);活意(活力，生机);活健(充满活力，身体强健)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 活动 <span class="encs">[movable;moving]</span>。如:活落(活动;不确定);活泼泼(活动);描绘得活;活支沙(活支剌。活生生地)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 灵活，不呆板 <span class="encs">[vivid;lively]</span>。如:活变(机灵，灵活;活动，走动);活达(灵便);活局(能灵活变化的安排或计谋)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> a <span class="encs">[指火山]</span>∶目前或每隔几年又喷发或火山口含有流动熔岩 <span class="encs">[active]</span>。如:活火山 b <span class="encs">[指冰川]</span>∶其中有些冰正在流动。如:活冰川</p>
<p>◎ <strong>活</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 生计，谋生手段 <span class="encs">[livelihood]</span>。如:做活;这个活做得真好;农活</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 卖力气的工作 <span class="encs">[job]</span>。如:为邻居主妇们做零活</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 劳动 <span class="encs">[美labor;英labour]</span>。如:苦活</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 生活 <span class="encs">[living]</span>。如:全家在一起过活</p>
