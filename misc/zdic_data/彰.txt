data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从彡从章，章亦声。章，显著;彡( <span class="dicpy">shān</span>)，加上修饰。本义:明显，显著)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[evident]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彰，文彰也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彰，明也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彰厥有常。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋陶谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">义理之道彰。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·怀宠》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">嘉言孔彰。——<span class="smcs">《书·伊训》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顺风而呼，声非加疾也，而闻者彰。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得势益彰。——<span 又如:彰弥(更加显著;越发暴露);彰明昭著(彰明较著。形容非常显明);彰著(明显;显著)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 文采美盛鲜明 <span class="encs">[clear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">圣谟洋洋，嘉言孔彰。——<span class="smcs">《书·伊训》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:彰显(昭著的事实);彰施(明施)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>彰</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表扬，表彰 <span 又如:彰往考来(彰明往事，考察未来);彰宣(显扬，宣示);彰扬(宣扬;传扬)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 揭示，表露 <span class="encs">[reveal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">责</span><span class="diczx2">攸之</span>、 <span class="diczx2">瑀</span>、 <span class="diczx2">允</span>等之慢，以彰其咎。—— <span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
