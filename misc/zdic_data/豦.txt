                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8C66.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】强魚切【集韻】求於切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音渠。【說文】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C1A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩰚</span>相丮不解也。从豕虍會意。豕虍之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C1A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩰚</span>不相捨。司馬相如說，豦，封豕之屬。一曰虎兩足舉。</p><p>又【集韻】臼許切，渠上聲。封豕也。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>居御切，音據。獸名。【爾雅·釋獸】豦，迅頭。【註】今建平山中有豦，大如狗，似獮猴，黃黑色，多髥鬣，好奮迅其頭，能舉石摘人，貜類也。　【六書故】虎諧聲，豕虎無<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C1A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩰚</span>理。相如說是。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">豦</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九">卷九</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九&amp;swbs=豕">豕</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8C66.svg">                          <p>鬬相丮不解也。从豕虍。豕虍之鬬，不解也。讀若蘮蒘草之蘮。司馬相如說：豦，封豕之屬。一曰虎兩足舉。强魚切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8C66_6453.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(豦)</span><span class="ywyy">鬬相丮不解也。</span><span class="swdz">鬬當作鬥。兩丮相對也。丮、持也。不言持言丮者、以曡韵爲訓也。</span><span class="ywyy">从豕虍。</span><span class="swdz">此會意。虍者、虎文也。故卽以爲虎字。</span><span class="ywyy">豕虎之鬬</span><span class="swdz">當作鬥。</span><span class="ywyy">不相捨。</span><span class="swdz">說會意之恉。</span><span class="ywyy">讀若蘮蒘艸之蘮。</span><span class="swdz">蘮蒘當作罽挐。之蘮當作之挐。葢本無末二字。後人增之而誤耳。蘮蒘、竊衣。見釋艸。強魚切。五部。</span><span class="ywyy">司馬相如說。豦、封豕之屬。</span><span class="swdz">此別一說也。毛詩傳曰。封、大也。封豕、大豕也。上林賦。擽蜚遽遽。或作虡。廣韵引作廙。其卽豦歟。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰虎㒳足舉。</span><span class="swdz">此又別一義。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
