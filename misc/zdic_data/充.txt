<p><span class="dicpy">chōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>满、足：～足。～实。～分（<span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n </span>）（➊足够；➋尽量）。～沛。～裕。</li>
	<li>填满，装满：填～。～满（➊填满，布满；➋充分具有）。～耳不闻。～电。汗牛～栋。</li>
	<li>当，担任：～当。～军。</li>
	<li>假装：冒～。～数（sh&ugrave;）。</li>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">充</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>充</strong> <span class="dicpy">chōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从儿，育省声。育子长大成人。本义:长，高)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">充，长也，高也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宗人视牲告充。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;特牲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 满 <span class="encs">[full]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">充，塞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">充，满也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冠盗充斥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言充其阳也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;庄公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颜色充盈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;子道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:充多(众多);充车(满车;喻数量众多);充房(满屋);充堂(满堂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 足;实;富厚 <span class="encs">[sufficient]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:充壮(健壮;充沛);充强(充实健壮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 肥胖 <span class="encs">[fat]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:充肥(肥胖，壮实);充腴(肥胖;丰满);充泽(丰润)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>充</strong> <span class="dicpy">chōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 装满;塞满 <span class="encs">[fill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以充府库。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大府》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">近之则钟音充。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">平生衣取蔽寒，食取充腹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狗马实外厩，美人充下陈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:充虚(填满肚子);充肠(充肚。填饱肚子);充庭(古代的一种朝仪。每大朝会，陈皇帝车马仪仗于殿庭)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假冒 <span class="encs">[pretend to be]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:充行家;打肿脸充胖子;滥竽充数;充好汉</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 扩充，扩大;尽量展开 <span class="encs">[enlarge]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:充广(扩充);充扩(扩充，开拓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 凑数;补充 <span class="encs">[serve as a stopgap]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:充赋(凑数);充事(凑合使用)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 备;供应 <span class="encs">[supply]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:充庖(供给膳食);充寒(备寒);充给(供给)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 任职，充任;充当 <span class="encs">[serve as]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂为猾胥报充里正役。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
