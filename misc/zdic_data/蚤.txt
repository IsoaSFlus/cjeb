                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蚤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>蚤</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>昆虫，赤褐色，善跳跃，寄生在人和畜的身体上，吸血液，能传染鼠疫等疾病。通称&ldquo;跳蚤&rdquo;、&ldquo;虼蚤&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/早">早</a>&rdquo;：四之日其～，献羔祭韭。</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/爪">爪</a>&rdquo;：周公乃自揃其～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> flea; louse</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Floh  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> puce</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蚤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蚤</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[flea]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蚤，啮人跳蚤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸱鸺夜撮蚤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得蚤者莫不糜之齿牙，为害身也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《贪恶鸟论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:沙蚤;水蚤;蚤蚊(跳蚤和蚊子。亦泛指小虫);蚤虱(跳蚤和虱子。亦泛指小害虫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;早&rdquo;。指月初或早晨<span class="encs">[early;morning]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日至于曾泉，是谓蚤食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不麾蚤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四之日其蚤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使</span><span class="diczx2">遂</span>蚤得处囊中。乃颖脱而出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蚤亡(早亡);蚤出夜入(早晨出门，晚上归来);蚤作(早晨起身;早起干活);蚤夜(昼夜;早晚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;爪&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">zhǎo</span>)。指甲，脚趾 <span class="encs">[claw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因其蹄蚤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;非乐上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以差论蚤牙之士。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;天志下》</span>。注:&ldquo;蚤作爪。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">争利如蚤甲而丧其掌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蚤牙(爪牙);蚤甲(爪甲)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蚤</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>指时间在先，和&ldquo;迟&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[early]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若皆蚤世犹可。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四之日其蚤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可不蚤虑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刘向传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦日不可不蚤自来谢</span><span class="diczx2">项王</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;龟策传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蚤世(早逝，夭折);蚤食(即早食);蚤达(指年轻时已任高官。早年即已显达得意);蚤知之士(先知，有先见之明的人)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
