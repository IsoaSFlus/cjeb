                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>弘</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hóng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>大（现多用“<span class="diczx3">宏</span>”）：～愿。～图。～业。～谋。恢～（ａ．宽阔、广大，如“气度～～”；ｂ．发扬，如“～～士气”。亦作“恢宏”）。</li><li>扩充，光大：～扬。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> enlarge, expand; liberal, great</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> erweitern, verbreiten, popularisieren  (V)​,groß, großartig, prächtig  (Adj)​,Hong  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grand,libéral</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>弘</strong> <span class="dicpy">hóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从弓。厶( <span class="dicpy">gōng</span>)声。本义:弓声)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sound of bow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弘，弓声也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓。如:弘仁(1610—1664)，清初画家。本姓<span class="yhcs">“江”</span>，名<span class="yhcs">“韬”</span>，安徽人。工画山水;弘法(774—835)，日本留学唐朝的僧侣。日本佛教真言宗之开宗祖师</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>弘</strong> <span class="dicpy">hóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“宏”</span>。大的(现多作宏) <span class="encs">[great]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弘，大也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弘璧。——<span class="smcs">《书·顾命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丧乱弘多。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·节南山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">式虽小子，而式弘大。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·民劳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诚取善之弘大。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:弘功(大功);弘慈(大慈大悲);弘绩(伟大的功绩);弘长(远大;扩大;昌盛)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 广，广大 <span class="encs">[vast]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士不可以不弘毅，任重而道远。——<span class="smcs">《论语·泰伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:弘休(广大的福泽);弘浃(广大普遍);弘致(广泛招来，极力延揽;大致的意思;大旨);弘深(广大渊深)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 宽宏 <span class="encs">[broad-minded;magnanimous]</span>。如:弘方(宽宏方正);弘和(宽宏和善);弘宥(宽宏);弘通(宽宏通大);弘量(宽宏的度量)</p><p>◎ <strong>弘</strong> <span class="dicpy">hóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 扩大;推广;光大 <span class="encs">[carry forward;glorify]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弘，大之也。——<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人能弘道，非道弘人。——<span class="smcs">《论语·卫灵公》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:弘化(推广教化);弘长(光大发扬);弘道(弘扬大道)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 宽容 <span class="encs">[forgive]</span>。如:弘厚(宽大忠厚);弘宥(宽恕);弘恕(宽容)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
