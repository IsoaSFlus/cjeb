                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">貌</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>貌</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>面容：面～。容～。～相。以～取人。</li>
	<li>外表的样子：礼～。～合神离。道～岸然。</li>
	<li>外观：全～。</li>
	<li>古书注解里表示状态、样子，如&ldquo;飞貌&rdquo;指飞的样子。</li>
	<li>描绘，画像：&ldquo;命工～妃于别殿&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> countenance, appearance</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Aussehen, Erscheinung  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> figure,apparence</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">貌</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>貌</strong></p>
<p>皃 <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从豹省，皃( <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> )声。本作&ldquo;皃&rdquo;，从&ldquo;人白&rdquo;，象人面形。儿，古文&ldquo;人&rdquo;字。本义:面容，相貌，容貌)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(外在的仪表，生就的形象) <span class="encs">[facial features;facial appearance;looks]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皃，颂仪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，面之神气曰颂，面之形状曰皃。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫皃情之华也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貌思恭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:&ldquo;动容谓之皃。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貌耸神溢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人之貌有与也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;养生主》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子产</span>蹴然改容更貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;德充符》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貌甚寝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:貌团团(形容面容姣圆、丰润的样子);貌侵(容貌丑陋);貌妍(容貌美丽);貌寝(相貌丑陋、消瘦);貌状(外貌;形象);貌容(容貌);貌色(容貌);貌肖(形貌相似)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 外表的形象，外观 <span class="encs">[outward appearance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貌恭而心不服。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:貌为风厉(表面上做出严厉的样子);全貌(事物的全部情况;全部面貌);貌似(面貌相似。或指事物外表相似而内在不同)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 神态，面部神情 <span class="encs">[expression;countenance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貌若男子所以恭敬，妇人之所以姣好也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说苑&middot;脩文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貌若甚戚者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:貌禅(面部表情如僧徒入神。形容入神的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;緢&rdquo;。细微的丝 <span class="encs">[filament]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">简孚有众，惟貌有稽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span>。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>引作&ldquo;帷緢。&rdquo;</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8C8C.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27CD2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧳒</span>【廣韻】莫敎切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】眉敎切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/296E8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩛨</span>。【說文】皃，頌儀也。从人，白面，象人面形。籀文从豹，省作貌。【書&bull;洪範】五事，一曰貌。【疏】貌是容儀，舉身之大名也。</p>
<p>又【禮&bull;郊特牲】委貌，周道也。【註】或謂委貌爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>冠。【後漢&bull;輿服志】委貌以皁絹爲之。</p>
<p>又【史記&bull;游俠傳贊】諺曰：人貌榮名，豈有旣乎。【註】榮名飾表稱譽無極也。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】《戰國策》：齊有貌辨。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】莫角切，音瞀。【正韻】描畫人物類其狀曰貌。【唐書&bull;后妃傳】命工貌妃於別殿。</p>
<p>又與邈同。遠也。【韓愈&bull;月蝕詩】完完上天東。【考異】完完，諸本作貌貌。　【集韻】或作䫉。</p>
