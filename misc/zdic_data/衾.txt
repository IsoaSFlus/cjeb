						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>衾</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>被子：～枕。《説文&bull;衣部》：&ldquo;衾，大被。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>尸体入殓时盖尸的单被。《孝經&bull;喪親章》：&ldquo;為之棺椁衣衾而舉之。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> coverlet, quilt</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bettdecke  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> couverture</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>衾</strong> <span class="dicpy">qīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (从衣，今声。本义:被子) 同本义 <span class="encs">[bed quilt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衾，大被。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;寝衣为小被(夹被)，则衾是大被(棉被)。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抱衾与椆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;小星》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缁衾赬里，无紞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士丧礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣衾多少厚薄多少之数。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">布衾多年冷似铁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狐裘不暖锦衾薄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以衾拥覆。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生则同衾，死则同穴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:衾褥(殓尸用的被褥);衾衽(指被与席。一说为枕席之间);衾枕(被与枕);衾绸(被;一说为大被和床帐);衾影无愧(暗中不做亏心事。后人引喻人在私生活中无败坏德行的事);衾衣(被子和衣服)</p>
