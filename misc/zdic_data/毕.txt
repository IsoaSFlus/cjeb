                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">毕</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>毕</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">畢</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>完结：礼～。～业。～生。～力。</li>
	<li>完全：～肖（<span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o </span>）（完全相象）。凶相～露。～其全力。</li>
	<li>究竟，到底：～竟。</li>
	<li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li>
	<li>古代田猎用的长柄小网，亦指用长柄网捕取禽兽：&ldquo;鸳鸯于飞，～之罗之&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古代丧祭时穿牲体的横木。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> end, finish, conclude; completed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> beenden  (V)​,das Ganze von, vollkommen  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> finir,achever,terminer,accomplir,compléter,complet,plein,fini</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">毕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>毕</strong></p>
<p>畢 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上端象网形，下端是柄，古时用以捕捉鸟兽、老鼠之类的器具。金文又在上面加个&ldquo;田&rdquo;，意思是田猎所用的网。本义:打猎用的有长柄的网)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a hand-net]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畢，田网也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田守毕弋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span>。注:&ldquo;掩雉兔之网。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其余荷垂天之毕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耒遭民田之毕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;偶会》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:毕戈(毕为捕兽所用之网，戈为射鸟所用的系绳之箭。泛指打猎活动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 毕宿。星名。二十八宿之一，西方白虎七宿的第五宿，有星八颗。以其形状像毕网得名 <span class="encs">[a star name]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>毕</strong></p>
<p>畢 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用毕猎取 <span class="encs">[hunt with a hand-net]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸳鸯于飞，毕之罗之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鸳鸯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 完毕，结束 <span class="encs">[finish;accomplish;conclude]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若入前为寿，寿毕，请以舞剑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此印者才毕，则第二板已具。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老人语未毕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公阅毕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:礼毕;毕婚(完婚);毕世(终其一生);毕老(终其天年);毕事(完事，了事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 全部使出 <span class="encs">[exhaust]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾与汝毕力平险。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:毕气(气数完尽);毕辞(尽所欲言)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>毕</strong></p>
<p>畢 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p> 全部，都，统统 <span class="encs">[fully;completely;altogether]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众妙毕备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忽然抚尺一下，群响毕绝。</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畢</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“毕”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/畢">畢</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上" target="_blank">午集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上&amp;bs=田" target="_blank">田</a>】
                              畢·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7562.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>吉切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】壁吉切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音必。【博雅】畢，竟也。【書&middot;大誥】子曷敢不于前，寧人攸受休畢。【左傳&middot;莊二十九年】日至而畢。【註】日南至，微陽始動，故土功畢。</p>
<p>又皆也。盡也。【詩&middot;小雅】畢來旣升。【禮&middot;月令】仲春之月，乃修闔扇，寢廟畢備。【註】畢，猶皆也。</p>
<p>又【郊特牲】唯爲社田，國人畢作。【疏】畢，盡也。</p>
<p>又月名。【爾雅&middot;釋天】月在甲曰畢。</p>
<p>又星名。【詩&middot;小雅】有捄天畢。【朱註】天畢，畢星也。狀如掩兔之畢。【禮&middot;月令】孟夏之月，日在畢。</p>
<p>又小綱也。【詩&middot;小雅】鴛鴦于飛，畢之羅之。【疏】罔小而柄長謂之畢。【禮&middot;月令】田獵罝罘，羅綱畢翳。【揚雄&middot;校獵賦】荷垂天之畢。</p>
<p>又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>也。【爾雅&middot;釋器】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>謂之畢。【註】今<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>札也。【禮&middot;學記】今之敎者，呻其佔畢。【疏】佔，視也。畢，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>也。不曉經義，但謳吟長咏，以視篇<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>而已。</p>
<p>又貫牲。體木也。【禮&middot;雜記】畢用桑。【註】主人舉肉時，以畢助之。喪祭用桑，吉用棘。畢狀如㐅，博三寸，長八寸，柄長二尺四寸，丹漆兩頭。【儀禮&middot;特牲饋食禮】宗人執畢先入。【註】畢狀如叉，蓋爲其似畢星，取名焉。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;大射儀】司馬正東面，以弓爲畢。【註】畢，所以助敎執事者。【疏】畢是助載鼎實之物，故司馬執弓爲畢以指授。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】車下鐵，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>宋淮楚之閒謂之畢。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】畢門，路門也。</p>
<p>又地名。【爾雅&middot;釋地】畢，堂牆。【註】今終南山道名，畢其邊，若堂室之牆。【詩&middot;秦風&middot;終南何有有紀有堂箋】畢，終南山之道名，邊如堂之牆然。</p>
<p>又國名。【左傳&middot;僖二十四年】畢原酆郇，文之昭也。【史記&middot;建元以來王子侯年表】畢梁侯劉嬰。</p>
<p>又姓。【左傳&middot;閔元年】畢萬爲右。【晉語】得畢陽。【註】畢陽，晉士。</p>
<p>又神名。【博雅】木神謂之畢方。【張衡&middot;東京賦】况鬾<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27312.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧌒</span>與畢方。【註】畢方，老父神，如烏兩足一翼者，常銜火在人家作怪災。</p>
<p>又鳥名。【山海經】章莪之山有鳥焉，其狀如鶴，一足，赤文靑質而白喙，名曰畢方。</p>
<p>又與彃同。【歸藏鄭母經】昔者羿善射，畢十日果畢之。</p>
<p>又與縪同。【儀禮&middot;覲禮】冠六升外畢。【註】外畢者，冠前後屈而出縫於武也。【疏】外畢者，前後兩畢之末而向外攝之也。○按旣夕畢作縪。</p>
<p>又與韠同。【荀子&middot;正論篇】共艾畢。</p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7562_4084.svg" /><span class="swbf">(畢)</span><span class="ywyy">田网也。</span><span class="swdz">謂田獵之网也。必云田者，以其字從田也。小雅毛傳曰：畢所以掩兔也。月令注曰：罔小而柄長謂之畢。按鴛鴦傳云：畢掩而羅之。然則不獨掩兔，亦可掩鳥，皆以上覆下也。畢星主弋獵，故曰畢，亦曰䍑車。許於率下曰：捕鳥畢也。此非别有一畢，亦是掩物之网。特牲饋食禮助載鼎實之噐象之亦曰畢，此則用以上載爲異。</span><span class="ywyy">从田。</span><span class="swdz">各本無此二字，依韵會補。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>象形。</span><span class="swdz">謂以<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>象畢形也。柄長而中可受，畢與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>同，故取<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>象形。各本作象畢形微也，有誤，今正。</span><span class="ywyy">或曰田聲。</span><span class="swdz">上云從田<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦒</span>會意而象其形，則非形聲也。或曰田聲。田與畢古音同在十二部也。各本田誤甶。鉉曰：甶音拂。此大誤也。畢，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>吉切。畢之言蔽也。攴部曰：㪤，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧗊</span>也。今<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧗊</span>義通作畢。</span></p>                          </div>
