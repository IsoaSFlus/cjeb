                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>靳</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从革，斤声。从<span class="yhcs">“革”</span>，表示与皮革有关。本义:套在辕马胸部的皮革，也用作辕马的代称)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[trappings under the neck of a horse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靳，当膺也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾从子，如骖之靳。——<span class="smcs">《左传·定公九年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“车中马也。言如骖马之首，当服马之靳，相随从也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:靳令(靳固，确定。指严格执行法令);靳道(同<span class="yhcs">“劲道”</span>。指面觔吃起来有韧性。喻性格柔中有刚)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 游环 <span class="encs">[hide loop]</span>。在马服背上的皮环，骖马的外辔穿过它，以便起到控制骖马奔离车辕的作用</p><p><span class="diczx1">弸辕谓之靳。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>靳</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 吝惜 <span class="encs">[grudge;be stingy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悔不小靳，可至千万。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·崔石传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:靳惜(靳啬。吝啬，小气);靳色(吝惜的脸色);靳固(吝惜固执);靳令(严格执行法令);靳道(韧性);靳而不与</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 讥笑 <span class="encs">[ridicule]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宋公</span>靳之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公十一年》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“戏而相愧曰靳。”</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9773.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】居近切【集韻】【韻會】居焮切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音㨷。【說文】當膺也。【註】徐鍇曰：靳，固也。靳制其行也。【左傳·定九年】吾從子，如驂之靳。【註】靳，車中馬也。【疏】靳是當胷之皮也。驂馬之首當服馬之胷，胷上有靳。</p><p>又【集韻】吝也。【左傳·莊十一年】宋公靳之。【註】戲而相愧曰靳。【疏】服虔云：恥而惡之曰靳。</p><p>又【孔叢子·唐言篇】靳，取也。</p><p>又姓。【廣韻】楚有大夫靳尙。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9773.svg">                          <p>當膺也。从革斤聲。居近切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9773_7515.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(靳)</span><span class="ywyy">當<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26896.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦢖</span>也。</span><span class="swdz">左傳曰。吾從子如驂之有靳。杜曰。靳、車中馬也。言已從書。如驂馬之隨靳也。正義曰。驂馬之首當服馬之胸。胸上有靳。故云我從子如驂當服之靳。按左傳。晉車七百乗。韅靷鞅靽。杜曰。在胸曰靷。此正在匈曰靳之誤。以秦風傳靳環或作靷環證之。其誤正同矣。游環在服馬背上驂馬外轡貫之。以止驂之出。故謂之靳環。靳者、驂馬止而不過之處。故引伸之義爲靳固。左傳。宋公靳之。吝其寵也。</span><span class="ywyy">从革。斤聲。</span><span class="swdz">居近切。十三部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
