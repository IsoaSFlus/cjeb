						<p>● <strong>翁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄥˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>老头儿：老～。渔～。&ldquo;塞～失马，焉知非福&rdquo;。</li>	<li>父亲：&ldquo;家祭无忘告乃～&rdquo;。</li>	<li>丈夫的父亲：～姑（公婆）。</li>	<li>妻子的父亲：～婿。</li>	<li>姓。</li>	<li>鸟颈毛。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">翁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>翁</strong> <span class="dicpy">wēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从羽，公声。本义:鸟颈毛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ruff]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翁，颈毛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸟头上毛曰翁。翁，一身之最上。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">玄应</span><span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;公&rdquo;。父亲 <span class="encs">[father]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 祖父 <span class="encs">[grandfather]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翁翁(犹公公。老翁，多指祖父)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翁，父也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释亲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾翁及汝翁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;项藉传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:翁媪(年老的父母。同&ldquo;翁妪&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 夫之父或妻之父 <span class="encs">[father-in-law]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翁婿(岳父和女婿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 泛称男性老人 <span class="encs">[old man]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡尊老，</span><span class="diczx2">周</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">陇</span>谓之公，或谓之翁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>六</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卖炭翁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老翁逾墙走。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">独与老翁别。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卖油翁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:塞翁失马;渔艄翁;翁翁(祖父;对老年男子的尊称);翁甫(对老者的尊称);翁长(对老者的尊称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 对男性的敬称 <span class="encs">[man]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:字某曰某翁</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>翁</strong> <span class="dicpy">wēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>通&ldquo;滃&rdquo;。腾涌 <span class="encs">[surge]</span></p>
