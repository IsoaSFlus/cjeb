                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>苓</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">líng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/líng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>指“茯苓”。</li><li>古书上说的一种植物。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">零</span>”，零落。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fungus, tuber; licorice</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Pilz  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tubercule,champignon</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">苓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>苓</strong> <span class="dicpy">líng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/líng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，令声。本义:植物名。苓耳，即卷耳)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 药草名。茯苓、猪苓皆简称苓。茯苓是寄生于松树根下的一种菌类植物。猪苓是枫树苓，生于枫根下的一种植物。二者均可入药 <span class="encs">[the cocklebur]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苓，卷耳也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">失时者苓落。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·叙传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山有榛，隰有苓。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·简兮》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:苓龟(中药名。即茯苓。因上等茯苓乌龟状而得名)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 猪屎 <span class="encs">[pig dung]</span>。如:苓通(喻贱物。猪粪叫苓，马粪叫通)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>苓</strong> <span class="dicpy">líng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/líng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 零落。同<span class="yhcs">“零”</span> <span class="encs">[decayed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得气者蕃滋，失时者苓落。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·叙传上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:苓落(草木凋落。同<span class="yhcs">“零落”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 草木青翠 <span class="encs">[verdant]</span>。如:苓茏(草木青翠茂盛的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/苓耳">苓耳</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/82D3.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>郞丁切，音靈。【說文】卷耳也。【爾雅·釋草】菤耳，苓耳也。【註】卷耳形似䑕耳，叢生如盤。【陸璣疏】可煑爲茹，滑而少味，四月中生子，如婦人耳璫。幽州謂之爵耳。【博雅】苓耳，枲耳也。</p><p>又【詩·邶風】山有榛，隰有苓。【傳】苓，大苦也。</p><p>又茯苓，藥名。【淮南子·說山訓】千年之松，下有茯苓。【魏志】潁川郤儉，能辟穀餌茯苓。</p><p>又豬苓。【本草註】其塊黑，似豬矢，故名。【韓愈·進學解】是猶昌陽引年，而進以狶苓也。</p><p>又地名。【晉書·地理志】扶苓縣，屬九德郡。</p><p>又通零。【說文】草曰苓，木曰落。</p><p>又【集韻】靈年切，音蓮。草名。【枚乗·七發】蔓草芳苓。【註】古蓮字。　【韻會】亦作蘦。</p><p>又作䕘。</p>
