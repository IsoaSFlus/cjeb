                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>屑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>碎末：～子。纸～。</li>
	<li>琐碎：～～。琐～（细微小的事情）。</li>
	<li>认为值得（做）：不～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bits, scraps, crumbs, fragments</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Feilspäne  (S)​,Krume  (S)​,Stanzabfall  (S)​,unbedeutend, geringfügig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> débris,copeaux,pellicules</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">屑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>屑</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从尸，肖声。本义:碎末) 同本义。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时造船，木屑及竹头悉令举掌之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;陶侃传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:米屑;屑涕(屑泣。谓涕泪纷纷下落);屑候(杂有姜桂碎末的干粮)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>屑</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 研成碎末 <span class="encs">[broken to pieces]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客至，屑槟榔、龙脑、香蛤以进。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屑玉(碾碎玉石。多指仙家屑玉为食);屑没(破碎沉没);屑骨(犹言粉身碎骨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 轻忽;轻易 <span class="encs">[neglect]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:屑越(轻易捐弃;糟踏);屑播(轻易抛弃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 顾惜;介意 <span class="encs">[mind;willing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天屑临</span><span class="diczx2">文王</span>慈，是以老而无子者，有所得终其寿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屑意(介意，放在心上);屑临(顾视);屑怀(介意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 常用否定式，不屑，即不惜顾一眼 <span class="encs">[disdain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不屑毁誉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用拳头击打 <span class="encs">[box]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">皇甫殿</span>直掿得拳头没缝，去顶门上屑那厮一椷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《清平山堂话本》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屑一暴(凿击一拳)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>屑</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不安<span class="encs">[intranquil;uneasy;disturbed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不唯迁客须怬屑，见说居人也寂寥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《送人贬信州判官》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 琐碎众多<span class="encs">[trifling;trivial]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:屑役(琐事杂务);屑椊(形容细碎的声音);屑屑(琐碎烦细，忙碌不安);屑细(琐细);屑瑟(细小的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 轻忽的<span class="encs">[light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔乃不大宅天命，尔乃屑播天命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;多方》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屑如(倏忽的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 清洁，整洁 <span class="encs">[neat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宴尔新昏，不我屑以。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;谷风》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5C51.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>先結切，先入聲。【說文】屑，動作切切也。本作㞕，从尸䏌聲。引崔駟達旨辭，吾亦病子，㞕㞕不已。隸作屑。【徐曰】居旣从尸，動亦从尸。屑屑，屢動作也。一曰敬也，不獲己也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】淸也，顧也，勞也。【詩&middot;邶風】不我屑以。【註】屑，潔也。不以我爲潔，而與之也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;王良傳】往來屑屑不憚煩。</p>
<p>又【增韻】輕也。【書&middot;多方】爾乃屑播天命。【註】謂輕棄天命。【正字通】凡遇事物，輕視不加意曰不屑。【孟子】不屑去，不屑就，乞人不屑，是也。</p>
<p>又瑣屑也。【左傳&middot;昭五年】女叔齊曰：禮所以守其國，行其政令，無失其民者也。而屑屑焉習儀以亟，不亦遠乎。</p>
<p>又碎末也。【周禮&middot;天官】王齊共食玉。【鄭註】王齊當食玉屑。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;內則】屑桂與薑。◎按㞕本《說文》屑字。《正韻》㞕、屑分訓，泥。</p>
