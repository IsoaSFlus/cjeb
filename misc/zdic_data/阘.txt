                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>阘</strong></p><p>闒 <span class="dicpy">tà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 楼上小户 <span class="encs">[small door]</span>。如:关阘(关上楼上小门)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 门楼上屋 <span class="encs">[attic]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阘，门楼上屋。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 门上横梁 <span class="encs">[doorhead]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">门上梁谓之瑁，瑁谓之阘。——<span class="smcs">《营造法式》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 长狭而低的坐卧用具。后作<span class="yhcs">“榻”</span> <span class="encs">[bed]</span>。如:阘坐(床前的榻凳)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>阘</strong></p><p>闒 <span class="dicpy">tà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 庸碌;卑下 <span class="encs">[mediocre;low-grade]</span>。如:阘茸(阘亢。庸碌低劣)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 垂貌。也作<span class="yhcs">“塌”</span> <span class="encs">[hang]</span>。如:阘顿(疲惫的样子);阘(委靡不振)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/阘懦">阘懦</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/阘茸">阘茸</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95D2.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】徒盍切【集韻】敵盍切【韻會】敵合切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蹋。【說文】樓上戸也。从門，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26407.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦐇</span>，意兼聲。【廣韻】門樓上屋也。【周禮·夏官·大司馬中軍以鼙令鼓註】司馬法曰：鼓聲不過閶，鼙聲不過闒。【釋文】闒，吐獵反。</p><p>又【廣韻】一曰閭閈里也。【博雅】闒，里也。</p><p>又海外國名。【山海經】闒非，人面而獸身，靑色。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>䚽盍切，音榻。闒茸，意下也。【前漢·司馬遷傳】在闒茸之中。【註】闒，下也。闒茸，猥賤也。</p><p>又【賈誼傳】闒茸尊顯兮。【楚辭·九歎】雜班駮與闒茸。【註】闒茸，駑頓也。</p><p>又【集韻】力盍切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26873.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦡳</span>。義同。</p><p>又䚽合切，音錔。鏜鞳，鐘鼓聲。本作鞳。闛<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D80.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨶀</span>，鐘鼓聲。</p>
