						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>茁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuó.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄛˊ</span></p><ol><li>植物才生长出来的样子。</li><li>壮盛，壮健：～壮。～实（“<span class="diczx3">实</span>”读轻声）。～长（<span class="dicpy">zhǎng </span>）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>茁</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhuó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从艸，从出，出亦声。本义:植物才生长出来的样子)同本义 <span class="encs">[sprouting]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茁，草初生出地貌。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼茁者葭。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·驺虞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向东风种就，一亭兰茁，玉香初茂。——</span><span class="diczx2">陈允平</span><span class="smcs">《过秦楼》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:茁茁(草刚长出的样子);茁茂(茂盛);茁肥(肥壮);茁实(扎实;健壮结实)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>茁</strong> <span 生出;生长 <span class="encs">[grow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牛羊茁壮长而已矣。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“生长貌。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曝干则为干看蕈，今深山穷民以此代耕，殆天茁此品，以遗其利也。——</span><span class="diczx2">王祯</span><span class="smcs">《农书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:茁芽(发芽)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 绽开，开<span class="encs">[花]</span> <span class="encs">[burst]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蠹树高枝茁朵稠，嫩苞开破雪搓球。——</span><span class="diczx2">张建</span><span class="smcs">《咏梨花》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 生<span class="encs">[病]</span> <span class="encs">[get]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">霜髭茁病骨，饥坐听午钟。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span
