						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>唱</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chàng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>依照乐（<span class="dicpy">yuè ㄩㄝˋ</span>）律发声：～歌。～腔。～段。～功。～和（<span class="dicpy">hé ㄏㄜˊ</span>）。歌～。</li><li>高呼，大声叫：～名。～收。</li><li>歌曲：唱个～儿。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">倡</span>”，倡导。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

class="encs">[sing]</span>。如:唱书(说唱演奏小说中的故事。用絃、琴伴奏的，称作唱书);唱诗(吟诗);唱月(对月吟唱)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 表演 <span class="encs">[play]</span>。如:唱白脸</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 叫，呼 <span
