                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>博</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bó.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄛˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>多，广，大：广～。渊～。～学（学问广博）。～览。～爱。～物。</li>    <li>知道得多：～古。</li>    <li>用自己的行动获得：～取。～得。聊～一笑。</li>    <li>古代的一种棋戏；后泛指赌财物：～奕。赌～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">博</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>博</strong> <span class="dicpy">bó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从十，尃( <span class="dicpy">fū</span>)声。<span class="yhcs">“十”</span>意思是四方中央齐备。<span class="yhcs">“尃”</span>有分布之义。本义:大)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。与<span class="yhcs">“小”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[large;big]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">博，大通也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戎车孔博。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·泮水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岂必褒衣博带。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·泛论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丰丽博敞。——</span><span class="diczx2">王逸</span><span class="smcs">《鲁灵光殿赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倚沼畦瀛兮遥望博。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不学博依。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·学记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:宽衣博带;博硕(粗大;宽大);博硕肥腯(古时献牲祭神的祝祷辞，是说六畜肥大)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宽广;广搏 <span class="encs">[extensive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">博闻强志，明于治乱，娴于辞令。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:广博(范围大，方面多);博闻(见闻广博);博引旁搜(考证完备广博，引证资料丰富充实)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 众多;丰富 <span class="encs">[abundant;plentiful;rich]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">博我以文，约我以礼。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子罕》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:地大物博;博祸(多种祸害);博富(丰富广博);博杂(多而杂乱)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 广泛;普遍 <span class="encs">[extensively;generally]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风雨博施。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·天论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:博览群书;博施(普遍施与);博物洽闻(博见广闻);博考(广泛地考查)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 渊博，知道得多 <span class="encs">[profound]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子博学而日参省乎己，则知明而行无过矣。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:渊博(学识深而且广)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>博</strong> <span class="dicpy">bó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 赌，博弈 <span class="encs">[gamble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与</span><span class="diczx2">闵公</span>博。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·庄公十二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则博塞以游。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·骈拇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或以游博持掩为事。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·王符传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不有博奕者乎。——<span class="smcs">《论语·阳货》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或饮或博。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span 			<div class="gscdl">——《子罕》子夏曰：“<span>博</span>学而笃志，切问而近思，仁在其中矣。”</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孔子弟子及其再传弟子 《《论语》十二章》</div><div class="gscdl">权曰：“孤岂欲卿治经为<span>博</span>士邪！但当涉猎，见往事耳。”</div><div class="gscdr">宋·司马光 《孙权劝学》</div><div class="gscdl">今虽耄老，未有所成，犹幸预君子之列，而承天子之宠光，缀公卿之后，日侍坐备顾问，四海亦谬称其氏名，况才之过于余者乎？今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪稍之供，父母岁有裘葛之遗，无冻馁之患矣；坐大厦之下而诵《诗》《书》无奔走之劳矣；有司业、<span>博</span>士为之师，未有问而不告，求而不得者也；凡所宜有之书，皆集于此，不必若余之手录，假诸人而后见也。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
