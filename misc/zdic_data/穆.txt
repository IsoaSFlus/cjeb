                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">穆</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>穆</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄨˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>温和；～清（ａ．清平；ｂ．指上天）。～如清风。</li>    <li>恭敬：严肃：肃～。静～。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">默</span>”，沉默。</li>    <li>古代宗庙次序，父居左为“<span class="diczx3">昭</span>”，子居右为“穆”。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> majestic, solemn, reverent; calm</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ernst, feierlich ,Mu  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> solennel,grave</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">穆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>穆</strong> <span class="dicpy">mù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:禾名)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[standing grain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穆，禾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“盖禾有名穆者也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古时宗庙制度，父居左为昭，子居右为穆。参见<span class="yhcs">“昭穆”</span> <span class="encs">[the right side position in ancestral temple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辩庙祧之昭穆。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小宗伯》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“父曰昭，子曰穆。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 代指右边 <span class="encs">[right side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">只见</span><span class="diczx2">贾</span>府人分了昭穆，排班立定。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:昭穆(左边和右边)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>穆</strong> <span class="dicpy">mù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 恭敬 <span class="encs">[reverent;solemn;respectful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于穆清庙。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·清庙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穆穆皇皇。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·假乐》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我其为王穆卜。——<span class="smcs">《书·金滕》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“敬也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:肃穆(严肃恭敬);静穆(安静庄严);穆穆(端庄恭敬);穆穆纯纯(恭敬诚恳的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 壮美 <span class="encs">[magnificent and beautiful;full of grandeur]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穆穆</span><span class="diczx2">文王</span>。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·文王》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天子穆穆。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:穆穆(端庄盛美的样子);穆行(美行);穆如(和美的样子);穆若(和美的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 肃静 <span class="encs">[solemn silence]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">吴王</span>穆然。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·非有先生论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹默静思貌也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:穆静(安靖);穆然(静思的样子);穆穆(宁静;静默)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 淳和，温和 <span
