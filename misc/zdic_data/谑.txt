                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>谑</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">謔</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xu&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>开玩笑：戏～。～浪（戏言放荡）。谐～。～而不虐（开玩笑但不令人难堪）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> jeer</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Vergnügen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> joie,blaguer,plaisanter,moquer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>谑</strong></p>
<p>謔 <span class="dicpy">xu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，虐声。本义:尽兴地游乐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[banker;tense;crack a joke]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谑，戏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谑，浪笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善戏谑兮，不为虐兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;淇奥》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此乃今之轻薄子，好作谑词，嘲乡里之类，为一乡所疾苦者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《朱子全书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈王</span>昔时宴 <span class="diczx2">平乐</span>，斗酒十千姿欢谑。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《将进酒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谑亲(闹新房);姿欢谑(尽情地欢乐游玩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 取笑作乐 <span class="encs">[mock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伊其相谑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;地理志》</span>。注:&ldquo;戏言也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">调笑来相谑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《陌上桑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗜酒善谑，而好为诗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谑戏(调笑戏弄);谑剧(调笑嬉戏);谑浪(戏谑放荡);谑嘲(戏谑嘲弄);谑弄(戏谑嘲弄);谑词(开玩笑的话)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>谑</strong></p>
<p>謔 <span class="dicpy">xu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p> 喜乐 <span class="encs">[pleased and joy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无然谑谑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;板》</span>。传:&ldquo;喜乐貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈王</span>昔时宴平乐，斗酒十千恣欢谑。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《将进酒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谑谑(喜乐的样子)</span></p>
