                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">建</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>建</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>立，设置，成立：～立（ａ．开始成立；ｂ．开始产生，开始形成）。～树（建立功业，或所建立的功业）。～国。～都（<span class="dicpy">dū</span>）。～党。～军。～交。～设。～功立业。</li><li>造，筑：～造。～筑。修～。新～。兴（<span class="dicpy">xīng</span>）～。筹～。</li><li>提出，倡议：～议。～策（出谋献策）。</li><li>指中国福建省：～兰。</li><li>北斗的斗柄所指的方位。斗柄农历每月所指的方位不同，因此“建”亦转指月份（亦称“月建”、“月尽”）：大～（农历有三十天的月份，亦称“大尽”）。小～（农历有二十九天的月份，亦称“小尽”）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">建</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>建</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从廴( <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span>)，有引长的意思，从聿(意为律)。本义:立朝律)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[work out;draw up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">立朝律也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">建，立也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:建法(制定法律);建中(定立标准);建极(制定至中至正的建国大法)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为建立、创设 <span class="encs">[build;establish;found]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">官惟王建国。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌建邦之宫刑。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小宰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">州建百里之国三十。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皇建其有极。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">择建立卜筮人。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫位政之建也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">足下自以为善</span><span class="diczx2">汉王</span>，欲建万世之业，臣窃以为误矣。——<span class="smcs">《史记·淮阴侯列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">德之不建。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">建</span><span class="diczx2">伊</span><span class="diczx2">皋</span>之业。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">建一官而三物成。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:建官(设置官职);建封(设置爵位，分封诸侯);建竖(建立功绩);建醮净宅(设场建坛，用酒祭神以祛除宅院里的邪气);建本(奠定基础，建立根本)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 封立 <span class="encs">[confer upon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">利建侯。——<span class="smcs">《易·屯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天子建德，因生以赐姓。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">康王</span>息民，并建母弟以蕃屏周。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十六年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:建侯(封立诸侯;封侯建国;立功封侯);建德(封立有德者为诸侯)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 树立、竖起 <span class="encs">[set up ;establish;erect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九十杖而朝见君见杖。——<span class="smcs">《书·大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设此旐矣，建彼旄矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·出车》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">建鼓在阼阶西。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘轺建节。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span
