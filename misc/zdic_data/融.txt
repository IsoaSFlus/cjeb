                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>融</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">róng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/róng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄨㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>固体受热变软或化为流体：～化。～解。消～。</li><li>调合，和谐：～合。～洽。～汇贯通。其乐～～。</li><li>流通：～泄（飘动，浮动）。金～（货币的流通，即储蓄，信贷、汇兑、股票和证券交易等经济活动的总称）。</li><li>长远、永久：～裔（形容声调悠长）。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/róng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鬲，虫省声。鬲( <span class="dicpy">lì</span>)，古代一种烹饪器，与鼎相似。本义:炊气上升)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[belch smoke]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">融融者皆趣热之士，其得炉冶之门者，惟夹炭之子。——<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 融化;消溶 <span class="encs">[melt;dissolve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">融而为川渎。——</span><span class="diczx2">孙绰</span><span class="smcs">《游天台山赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:融流(融化流动);融陶(熔化陶冶);融液(融化成液体);融释(消失;化解);融蚀(消磨;侵蚀);融炼(熔化锤炼)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 融合;融会 <span class="encs">[mix together;blend;fuse]</span>。如:融通(融合通达);融洽无间(融合而没有隔阂抵触);融混(融合混和);融渥(融合滋润)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>融</strong> <span class="dicpy">róng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/róng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 长远;长久 <span class="encs">[permanent;long]</span>。如:融昭(长耀，久照);融远(长久;深远)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 大明，大亮，泛指明亮 <span class="encs">[very bright]</span>。如:融光(明亮的光);融晶(犹光亮);融彻(通明透彻)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通;通达。引申为流通 <span class="encs">[current]</span>。如:金融;融泄(流动貌);融悟(通达颖悟);融达(通达);融散(谓旷达);融畅(明白通畅);融浃(融通和洽)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 显明，昌盛 <span class="encs">[prosperous]</span>。如:融显(显明)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 和乐;恬适 <span class="encs">[harmonious and happy;comfortable]</span>。如:融懿(和美);融浑(犹融和，和顺)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 和煦;暖和 <span class="encs">[pleasantly warm]</span>。如:融晴(温和晴朗);融畅(暖和舒畅);融暖(犹和暖)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
