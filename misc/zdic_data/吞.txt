						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>吞</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>不嚼或不细嚼而咽入：～吐。～咽。～噬。狼～虎咽。气～山河。忍气～声。</li><li>兼并，侵占：～没（<span class="dicpy">mò</span>）。～并。～蚀。～占。</li></ol>

class="smcs">《战国策·西周策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而交吞揆之。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·天问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吞二</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>。—— <span
