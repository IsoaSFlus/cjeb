						<p>● <strong>交</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>付托，付给：～活儿。～卷。～差。</li>
	<li>相错，接合：～点。～界。～错。～相。～辉。～响乐。</li>
	<li>互相来往联系：～流。～易。～涉。</li>
	<li>与人相友好：～朋友。～契。</li>
	<li>一齐，同时：～并。～作。风雨～加。</li>
	<li>两性和合：性～。杂～。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/跤">跤</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

Umgang haben</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> jonction,rencontre,acquitter,donner,livrer,remettre,échanger,se croiser,nouer des relations,s'associer,amitié</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">交</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>交</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，象人两腿交叉形。本义:交叉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 脚胫相交 <span class="encs">[cross]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交，交胫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交足而待。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雕题交趾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:交趾(古郡名。汉置，地处五岭以南一带。相传这里的人入睡时，两足相交)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 交叉;交错，错杂 <span class="encs">[cross;interleave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交臂厯指。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交韔二弓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;小戎》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:交裆(裤裆处);交拐(拐弯走);交戟(执戟相交);交牙(如犬牙相交叉);交歧(交错的路径)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 结交; 交往 <span class="encs">[associate with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交游士林。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:交友(结交朋友);交礼(交往的礼节);交驰(交相奔走，往来不断);交绝(关系断绝，绝交);交善(结交);交援(与人交结而得其援助)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 交接，移交;交换 <span class="encs">[deliver;hand over and take over]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交，易也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥孚交如。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;大有卦》</span>。疏:&ldquo;交，谓交接也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是言上下之交。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span>。注:&ldquo;交谓上下相交接也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">静</span><span class="diczx2">郭君</span>之交。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;知士》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:交盘(点交;交接);交受(犹交易);交点(清点财物，交付与有关的人);交关(交接;紧要关头);交贸(交易)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 交配 <span class="encs">[copulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虎始交。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">备以为交佩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;思美人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:交群(交配);交构(亦作交媾。指性交;阴阳交合);交群(交配);交感(指性交)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 进入<span class="encs">[某个季节]</span>;刚到<span class="encs">[某个时刻]</span> <span class="encs">[set in]</span> 。如:交子时;交冬至了;交九的天气;交节(临近节气。旧俗认为病人这时候易发生不测)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 接触 <span class="encs">[wage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故上兵伐谋，其次伐交。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;谋攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">情交而不感者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《与元九书》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4EA4.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕䢒【廣韻】古肴切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居肴切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音郊。【小爾雅】俱也。【廣韻】共也，合也。【易&middot;泰卦】上下交，而其志同也。</p>
<p>又友也。【易&middot;繫辭】上交不諂，下交不凟。【禮&middot;郊特牲】爲人臣者無外交，不敢貳君也。</p>
<p>又交交，鳥飛貌。【詩&middot;秦風】交交黃鳥。</p>
<p>又交加，參錯也。【前漢&middot;劉向傳】章交公車。</p>
<p>又州名，南越地，漢置交州。【書&middot;堯典】申命羲叔宅南交。【蔡傳】南交，南方交趾地。</p>
<p>又衣領也。【揚子&middot;方言】衿謂之交。</p>
<p>又同蛟。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】則見交龍於上。【史記】作蛟。</p>
<p>又同鵁。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】交精旋目。卽鵁鶄。</p>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=交">交</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4EA4.svg">                          <p>交脛也。从大，象交形。凡交之屬皆从交。古爻切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4EA4_80.svg" /><span class="swbf">(交)</span><span class="ywyy">交脛也。</span><span class="swdz">交脛謂之交，引申之爲凡交之偁。故爻下曰：交也。烄下曰：交木然也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24267.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤉧</span>下曰：交灼木也。㮐下曰：木參交以枝炊䉛者也。衿下曰：交衽也。凡㒳者相合曰交，皆此義之引申叚借耳。楚茨傳：東西曰交，邪行曰逪。䢒、逪字之叚借也。小雅：交交桑扈。箋云：交交猶佼佼，飛往來皃。而黃鳥、小宛傳皆曰：交交，小皃。則與本義不同。葢方語有謂小交交者。</span><span class="ywyy">从大，象交形。</span><span class="swdz">謂从大而象其交脛之形也。古爻切。二部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡交之屬皆从交。</span></p>                          </div>
