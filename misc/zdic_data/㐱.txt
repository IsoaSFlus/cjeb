                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/3431.svg">                          <p>稠髮也。从彡从人。《詩》曰：“㐱髮如雲。” 鬒，㐱或从髟眞聲。之忍切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/3431_8223.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(㐱)</span><span class="ywyy">稠髮也。</span><span class="swdz">稠者多也。禾稠、曰稹。髮稠曰㐱。其意一也。</span><span class="ywyy">从彡。</span><span class="swdz">謂髮。</span><span class="ywyy">人聲。</span><span class="swdz">之忍切。十二部。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰。㐱髮如雲。</span><span class="swdz">庸風君子偕老文。今詩作鬒。葢以或字改古字。傳曰。鬒、黑髮也。疑黑字亦非毛公之舊。許多襲毛。不應有異。左傳。昔有仍氏生女。黰黑而甚美。黰正謂稠髮。髮多且黑而皃甚美也。服、杜皆云美髮爲黰。不言黑髮。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/3431_8224.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(鬒)</span><span class="ywyy">㐱或从髟。眞聲。</span><span class="swdz">黰又其或體。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
