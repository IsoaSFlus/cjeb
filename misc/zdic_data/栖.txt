data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (棲本作<span class="yhcs">“西”</span>。同<span class="yhcs">“栖”</span>。形声。从木，妻声。本义:鸟类歇息)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[perch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸡栖于厅。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山有栖鹘。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:栖竿(捕虫鸟的黏竿);栖山(栖息于山。指隐遁);栖峙(栖止伫立);栖荡(栖息游荡);栖歇(栖止，歇止);栖鸟(栖宿于树上的鸟);栖鸟于泉(使鸟停息于泉中)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 停留 <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:两栖(水陆两处生活);栖迟(游息);栖所(居留之地);栖泊(居留;停泊;寄居)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 居住;寄居 <span class="encs">[dwell;lodge at]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">越王</span><span class="diczx2">勾践</span>栖于 <span class="diczx2">会稽</span>之上。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:栖苴(寄生在树上己枯萎的草);栖住(居住);栖凭(居住安身);栖寓(寄居，暂住);栖处(栖居，寄居)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 隐居 <span class="encs">[withdraw]</span>。如:栖云(隐居);栖遁(归隐);栖崖(隐居山林);栖衡(隐居);栖影(栖踪，隐迹;寄生);栖游(隐迹优游)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 寄托 <span class="encs">[place (hope，etc.) on]</span>。如:栖托(依托，托身);栖诚(寄托心志);栖情(寄托情志);栖志(寄托情志);栖意(寄寓心意)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>栖</strong> <span class="dicpy">qī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button
