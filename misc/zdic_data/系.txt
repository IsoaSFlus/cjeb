						<p>● <strong>系</strong></p>
<p><strong>（➌➏<span class="diczx3">係</span>、➌➍➎➐繫）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>有联属关系的：～统。～列。～数。水～。世～。</li>
	<li>高等学校中按学科分的教学单位：中文～。化学～。</li>
	<li>关联：干～。关～。</li>
	<li>联结，栓：～缚。～绊。～马。维～。名誉所～。</li>
	<li>牵挂：～恋。～念。</li>
	<li>是：确～实情。</li>
	<li>把人或东西捆住上提或向下送：从井下把土～上来。</li>
	<li>某些学科中分类的名称：汉藏（<span class="dicpy">z&agrave;ng </span>）语～。寒武～（地质学名词）。</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>（繫）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˋ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>结，扣：把鞋带～上。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">系</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>系</strong></p>
<p>繋 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 拴;系结 <span class="encs">[tie;fasten;button up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忽风云腾涌，急系缆。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">系之苇召。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:系腰(腰带);系鞋带;把晾衣服绳系在树上;她的头发上系了一根红缎带;腰上系了一根紫色的&hellip;腰带;把卡片系在包上;把衣服扣系上</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 约束;羁绊 <span class="encs">[restrain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愚士系俗兮，窘若囚拘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《鹏鸟赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拘囚 <span class="encs">[arrest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">累及好人，无故系狱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">关汉卿</span><span class="smcs">《裴度还带》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 留意;挂念 <span class="encs">[be careful;look out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:系人情思</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 牵涉;关连 <span class="encs">[involve;drag in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷通各问命，不系才不才。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《谕友》</span></p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>系</strong></p>
<p>繫 <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (&ldquo;系&rdquo;、&ldquo;係&rdquo;、&ldquo;繫&rdquo;三字的意义界限不清楚，故往往通用，且经传多以繫为之，只有世系的意义，只用&ldquo;系&rdquo;字。会意。甲骨文字形上面是&ldquo;爪&rdquo;，下面是&ldquo;丝&rdquo;。丝悬于掌中而下垂。本义:悬，挂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hang]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以羽为巢，而编之以发，系之苇苕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:系璧(系在带间的小玉璧);系滞(悬挂弃置)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拴;绑 <span class="encs">[tie;fasten]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">繫，约束也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">繫，系也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span>释文</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以九两繫邦国之民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;大宰》</span>。注:&ldquo;繫，联缀也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子婴</span>即系颈以组，白马素车，奉天子玺符，降轵道旁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:系足(借指婚姻);系裹(穿戴衣帽);系舟(指船靠岸。也作&ldquo;系船&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拘囚;关进牢狱 <span class="encs">[imprison]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收系张胜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老母系保宫。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利系者之多。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拘系(拘禁);系縻(束缚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 牵挂 <span class="encs">[be anxious about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可怜赫赫</span><span class="diczx2">丹阳</span>尹，数颗槟榔尚系怀。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《读史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:系怀(心中牵挂某事或某人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 依附 <span class="encs">[depend on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者诸侯之有女子也，所以苞苴玩弄系援于大国也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:系援(依附求助);系象(寄托意象);系命(犹言寄托性命)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 关联 <span class="encs">[relate to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">足有系帛书。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">东汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:系亲(联姻，结亲);系络(联结);系联(联系);系属(联缀)</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>系物的带、绳 <span class="encs">[band;rope;cord]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">武冠，俗谓之大冠，环缨无蕤，以青系为绲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;舆服志下》</span></span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7CFB_4801.svg" /><span class="swbf">(系)</span><span class="ywyy">縣也。</span><span class="swdz">縣各本作繫。非其義。今正。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25109.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥄉</span>部曰：縣者，系也。引申爲凡總持之偁。故系與縣二篆爲轉注。系者，垂統於上而承於下也。系與係可通用。然輕傳係多謂束縛。故係下曰絜束也。其義不同。系之義引申爲世系。周禮瞽矇。世帝繫。小史。奠繫世。皆謂帝繫世本之屬。其字借繫爲之。當作系。大傳。繫之以姓而弗别。亦系之叚借。</span><span class="ywyy">从糸。</span><span class="swdz">系，細絲也。縣物者不必麤也。</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20086.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂆</span>聲。</span><span class="swdz"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20086.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂆</span>余制切。抴也。虒字从之。系字亦从之。形聲中有會意也。胡計切。十六部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡系之屬皆从系。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7CFB_4802.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AE6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣫦</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">系或从毄處。</span><span class="swdz">从處而<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AA0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣪠</span>聲也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AA0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣪠</span>亦在十六部。故古係縛字亦多叚<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AA0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣪠</span>爲之。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7CFB_4803.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/260DF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦃟</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文系。从爪絲。</span><span class="swdz">此會意也。覆手曰爪。絲縣於掌中而下垂。是系之意也。</span></p>                          </div>
