                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">傍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>傍</strong> <span class="dicpy">bàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，旁声。本义:靠近，临近)同本义 <span class="encs">[beside;be close to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">傍，近也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">双兔傍地走。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:傍边(靠近边沿);傍通(靠近四方通达之地);傍境(边境);傍户而立</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>傍</strong> <span class="dicpy">bàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 依附，依靠 <span class="encs">[depend on]</span>。如:傍人门户;傍依(依靠之物);傍靠(依靠，紧靠)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 辅佐 <span class="encs">[assist a ruler in governing a country]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四贤傍之。——<span class="smcs">《新书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 顺着;沿着 <span class="encs">[follow]</span>。如:傍照(依照，仿照)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 陪随，陪伴 <span class="encs">[accompany]</span>。如:傍个影儿(露面;亮相)</p><p>◎ <strong>傍</strong> <span class="dicpy">bàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“旁”</span>。旁边，侧 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">傍，近也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，与旁别，四旁四方皆当作此，犹边际也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">傍，侧也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玄应声恸哭，酸感傍人。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·夙惠》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白马金具装，横行辽水傍。——</span><span class="diczx2">孔稚圭</span><span class="smcs">《白马篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两家求合葬，合葬</span><span class="diczx2">华山</span>傍。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:傍门(佛教指学佛而得正误者为正果，指外道为傍门;道家以修炼金丹、全身保真为正道，余皆为傍门);傍支(分支);傍览(从旁观看、打量);傍观(在近旁观看);傍若无人(好像旁边没有人在，形容神情态度高傲自如)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 他人 <span class="encs">[other]</span>。如:傍舍(他人的房舍);傍说(他人的主张)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旁系 <span class="encs">[side]</span>。如:傍尊(指伯父、叔父等旁系尊长);傍荫(旁系亲属袭封官职爵位);傍亲(旁系亲属);傍继(出继旁系亲属)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 唐代州名。在今云南双柏县境 <span class="encs">[Pang prefecture]</span></p><p>◎ <strong>傍</strong> <span class="dicpy">bàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 广博;普遍。同<span class="yhcs">“旁”</span> <span
