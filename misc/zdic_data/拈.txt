                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拈</strong> <span class="dicpy">niān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，占声。本义:用指取物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pick up with the thumb and one or two fingers]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拈，揶也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拈，持也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">女何蚩而三招子。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“拈，指取物也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我二人拈阄，拈着的先去。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:拈毫(拿笔。借指写作或绘画);拈团儿(拈阄);拈撮(用指头取物);拈周试晬(抓周。小儿周岁从罗列的多种物品中拈取，旧俗以为可预测未来的性情志趣);拈酒(唐代口语。拿起酒杯吃酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 摆弄 <span class="encs">[fiddle with]</span>。如:拈弄(摆弄);拈掇(摆弄;提及);拈花弄月(玩赏花月);拈花弄柳(比喻玩弄女性);拈花惹草(比喻挑逗异性;淫乱)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/拈阄，拈阄儿">拈阄，拈阄儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/拈轻怕重">拈轻怕重</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/拈香">拈香</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>拈</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄋㄧㄢˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>nián <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1680.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用手指夹取、捏取。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>拈</b>龟」、「拈香」、「拈花微笑」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·杜甫〈绝句漫兴〉诗九首之七：「舍西柔桑叶可<b>拈</b>，江畔细麦复纤纤。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《红楼梦·第四○回》：「里面盛著一排十根玉簪花棒，<b>拈</b>了一根，递与平儿。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">耍弄、习弄。</span><span class="gc_yy">元·张国宾《合汗衫·第三折》：「自家陈豹，年长一十八岁，膂力过人，十八般武艺，无有不<b>拈</b>，无有不会。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div><div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>拈</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄉㄧㄢ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>diān</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">将物品放置手中，估量轻重。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「他<b>拈</b>了<b>拈</b>小石块，随手打了个水漂儿。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《孤本元明杂剧·大劫牢·第一折》：「也不索昼夜思量心内想，也不索<b>拈</b>斤播两显耀我这英雄猛将。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「掂」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div><div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>拈</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄋㄧㄢˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>niǎn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1684.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用手指搓揉。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>拈</b>线」、「<b>拈</b>灯心」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《红楼梦·第一○五回》：「贾政在外，心惊肉跳，<b>拈</b>须搓手的等候旨意。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「捻」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
