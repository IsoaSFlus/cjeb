class="encs">[twig]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曳梢肆柴。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·兵略》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“小柴也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 如:梢头;梢条;梢棒(亦作<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:眉梢;春梢</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 舵尾 <span class="encs">[rudder end]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">梢，船舵尾也。——<span class="encs">[pole]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饰玉梢以舞歌，体招摇若永望。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·礼乐志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 一伙人 <span class="encs">[group]</span></p><p><span
