						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谨</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">謹</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>慎重，小心：～慎。～严。～防。勤～。～小慎微。</li><li>郑重，恭敬：～启。～祝。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，堇( <span class="dicpy">qín</span>)声。本义:谨慎，小心)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[careful;cautious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谨，慎也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以谨无良。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·怀沙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谨庠序之教。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谨食之。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span 又如:谨畏(小心谨慎);谨肃(严谨认真);谨言(谨慎说话);谨厚(谨慎忠厚);谨介(谨慎耿介)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 恭敬 <span class="encs">[respectful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谨斩</span><span class="diczx2">樊於期</span>头。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谨使臣</span><span class="diczx2">良</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span precise;strict;rigorous]</span>。如:谨急(谨严急刻);谨细(谨严细密);谨质(谨严质朴)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谨</strong></p><p>謹 <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button
