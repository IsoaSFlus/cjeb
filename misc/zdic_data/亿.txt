						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>亿</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">億</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>数目，一万万；古代指十万：～万（泛指极大的数目）。～万斯年（形容无限长远的年代）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hundred million; many</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hundert Millionen (100.000.000, 10^8)​ (Num)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cent millions</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亿</strong></p>
<p>億 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。左形，右声。本义:安宁)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 安宁;安定 <span class="encs">[peaceful;stable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亿，安也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能供亿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心亿则乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我盍姑亿吾鬼神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亿其上下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心亿则乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:共亿(相安);亿康(安康);亿福(安宁幸福)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 极多;极大 <span class="encs">[maximum]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">据亿丈之城。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亿兆(比喻很多，难以计数);亿庶(亿众。亿万众庶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 满 <span class="encs">[full]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可亿逞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亿盈(淆溢);亿廪(亿庾。满仓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>亿</strong></p>
<p>億 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>［数］</p>
<p>数目的名称。古代有时把十万叫亿。今以万万为亿，即以阿拉伯数字100000000标记的可数量 <span class="encs">[a hundred million]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三百亿兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亿丧贝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;震》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;十万曰亿。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为田九十亿亩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亿劫(佛家说从天地形成到毁灭为&ldquo;一劫&rdquo;。意指时间极其久远);亿载(亿年);亿年(万万年)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>亿</strong></p>
<p>億 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;臆&rdquo;。臆测，预料 <span class="encs">[conjecture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亿在屡中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;先进》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">策虑幅亿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;陈汤传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可亿度。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧唐书&middot;李道宗传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亿中(料事能中);亿出(凭臆想而得出);亿度(测度;揣测)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">億</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“亿”字头，请参考“億”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集中" target="_blank">子集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集中&amp;bs=人" target="_blank">人</a>】
                              億·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5104.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】於力切【集韻】【韻會】乙力切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音臆。數名，十萬曰億。一說億數不定。【禮&middot;內則&middot;降德于衆兆民疏】算法，億之數有大小二法。小數以十爲等，十萬爲億，十億爲兆也。大數以萬爲等，萬至萬，是萬萬爲億也。</p>
<p>又安也。【左傳&middot;昭二十一年】伶州鳩曰：心億則樂。</p>
<p>又【三十年】盍姑億吾鬼神。</p>
<p>又料度也。【左傳&middot;襄二十五年】不可億逞。【論語】億則屢中。</p>
<p>又供億，謂供其匱乏，使之安也。【左傳&middot;隱十一年】寡人惟是一二父兄，不能供億。</p>
<p>又【吳幼淸云】億，賭錢也。以意猜度，如漢人射覆之類，故曰億。</p>
<p>又與臆通。【前漢&middot;平都侯相<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥓓</span>】餘悲憑億。</p>
