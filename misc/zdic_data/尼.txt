data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ní.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从尸，匕声。甲骨文字形象两个人亲昵的样子。本义:亲近，亲昵)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。后作<span class="yhcs">“昵”</span> <span class="encs">[very intimate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尼，从后近之也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尼，近也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span>。按，近昵之意，字亦作昵。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不避远尼。——<span class="smcs">《尸子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 安定，平和 <span class="encs">[peaceful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尼，安也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尼，和也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">竟界尼康。——<span class="smcs">《隶释·祝睦后碑》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>尼</strong> <span class="dicpy">ní</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ní.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 尼姑，佛教中出家修行的女子。是梵语<span class="yhcs">“比丘尼”</span>(Bhidsunt)的简称 <span class="encs">[nun]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人道为尼，遂居此寺。——<span class="smcs">《洛阳伽蓝记·胡统寺》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:僧尼(和尚和尼姑);尼寺(尼姑所住的寺院);尼房(尼姑所居处);尼坛(尼姑受戒的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 尼山 <span class="encs">[Ni Hill，believed to be birthplace of Confucius]</span> 。如:尼丘(尼邱。山名，即尼山，在山东曲阜县东南。相传为孔子出生地。故孔子名丘，字仲尼);尼圣(对孔子的尊称);尼轲(孔子和孟子的并称);尼聃(儒家创始人仲尼和道家创始人老聃的并称);尼首(谓人头像尼丘山，中低四方高) </p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span href="/hans/尼罗河">尼罗河</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>尼</strong> <span class="dicpy">nǐ</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 阻止;阻拦 <span class="encs">[impede]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淫嚣不静，当路尼众。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:尼行(停止或阻止前进);尼阻(阻止，阻碍)</p><p><span
