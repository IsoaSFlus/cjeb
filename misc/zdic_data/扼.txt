                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>扼</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄜˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用力掐着，抓住：～杀。～腕（用一只手握住另一只手，表示振奋、失意、惋惜等情绪）。力能～虎。</li>
	<li>抓要点，简要：～要。</li>
	<li>把守，控制：～制。～喉拊背（喻控制要害，制敌于死命）。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">轭</span>&rdquo;，牛马等拉东西时架在脖子上的器具。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> grasp, clutch; choke, strangle</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扼</strong></p>
<p>搤 <span class="dicpy">&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，厄声。本义:抓住;掐住)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[clutch;grip]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">搤，捉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">力扼虎，射命中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李陵传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">搤其喉而不得进。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;荀彧传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不搤其亢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;娄敬传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">搤熊罴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">搤，持也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扼虎(用双手掐住猛虎);扼吭拊背(本指控制咽喉。引申为扼制要冲之意);扼襟(握持衣服的襟领。引申为控制要害，掌握要领);扼臂(手臂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 扼守;据守 <span class="encs">[control;guard]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扼险(控制要害);扼据(控制);</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 阻碍，堵住 <span class="encs">[stop up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因而搤之可也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span>。又如:扼昧(暗中加害);扼塞(阻塞);扼隘(堵住要隘);扼吭(气逆于喉)</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扼</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>扼</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄜˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>&egrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5732.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">捉、持、握。</span><span class="gc_yy">《戰國策&middot;燕策三》：「樊於期偏袒扼腕而進曰：『此臣日夜切齒拊心也，乃今得聞教。』」</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷五四&middot;李廣傳》：「臣所將屯邊者，皆荊楚勇士奇材劍客也。力扼虎，射命中。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">據守、控制。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「扼險」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷一七一&middot;李光進傳》：「密遣田布伏精騎溝下，扼其歸。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">堵住、阻塞。</span><span class="gc_yy">《管子&middot;度地》：「此五水者，因其利而往之可也，因而扼之可也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《晉書&middot;卷一&middot;宣帝紀》：「凡攻敵，必扼其喉而舂其心。」</span></p></li>
