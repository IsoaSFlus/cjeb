						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>尾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wěi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄟˉ</span></p><ol><li>鸟兽虫鱼等身体末端突出的部分：～巴。～鳍。鸟～。</li><li>末端：排～。船～。～声。～骨。～灯。～数。～音。结～。扫～。虎头蛇～。</li><li>在后面跟：～随。～追。</li><li>量词，指鱼：三～鱼。</li><li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>尾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˇ</span></p><ol><li>特指马尾（<span class="dicpy">wěi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěi.mp3"></a></span> </span>）巴上的毛：马～罗。马～儿提豆腐―提溜不起来。</li><li>特指蟋蟀等尾部的针状物：三～儿（雌蟋蟀）。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>尾</strong> <span class="dicpy">wěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从倒毛在尸后。尸，指人。象人长有尾巴。本义:人或动物的尾巴)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tail]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尾，微也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">履虎尾 ，未济，濡其尾。——<span class="smcs">《易·履》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:虎尾;牛尾;尾大(比喻臣下势力强大);尾君子(指猴子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 末端，末尾 <span class="encs">[end]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渤海之尾。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">运于渤海之尾。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">系于其尾。——《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">舟尾横卧一楫。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:首尾;无头无尾;尾末(末尾;尾宿之末);尾句(句尾);尾页(末页)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 水流的下游 <span class="encs">[lower reaches]</span>。如:我住在江尾</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 边际，边界 <span class="encs">[boundary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫边境者，国之尾也。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 后部 <span class="encs">[rear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是我起兵已首尾期月矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">方勺</span><span
