                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>澡</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄠˇ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 沐浴全身，引申为保持节操：洗～。～盆。～堂。～雪（洗涤使清洁）。～身浴德（砥砺志行，使身心纯洁清白）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">澡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>澡</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wash hand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">澡，洒手也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">澡葛綕带。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士虞礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日三澡漱，然后饮食。——<span class="smcs">《魏书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 后泛指洗涤 <span class="encs">[wash]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">常以月旦祓龟，洗以清水澡之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·龟策传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:澡沐(沐浴);澡雪(洗清);澡瓶(寺庙里供僧人洗涤用的水瓶);澡刷(洗刷);澡涤(洗刷。引申指辨白冤屈)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 洗澡或浸泡 <span class="encs">[bath]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">儒有澡身而浴德。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·儒行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时天暑热，</span><span class="diczx2">植</span>因呼常从取水自澡讫，傅粉。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·王粲传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:澡形，澡身(洗身使洁净。引申为修持操行);澡洗(沐浴;盥洗);澡浴(洗澡);澡濯(洗澡)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 改过 <span class="encs">[correct one's errors]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">澡雪垢滓矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">马融</span><span class="smcs">《长笛赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:澡心(改过自新);澡祓(洗刷辨明);澡雪(改正;洗雪);澡荡(洗除)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 修炼 <span class="encs">[refine]</span>。如:澡练(修炼);澡瀹(修炼);澡行(使品行纯真)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/澡盆">澡盆</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/澡堂">澡堂</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/澡塘">澡塘</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
