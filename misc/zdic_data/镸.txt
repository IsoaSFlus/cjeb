						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九">卷九</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九&amp;swbs=長">長</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9577.svg">                          <p>久遠也。从兀从匕。兀者，高遠意也。久則變化。亾聲。　者，倒亾也。凡長之屬皆从長。仧，古文長。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2047F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠑿</span>，亦古文長。直良切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：倒亡，不亡也。長久之義也。〖注〗镸、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20910.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠤐</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2050A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔊</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2047B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠑻</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28C57.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨱗</span>，古文長。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9577_7266.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(長)</span><span class="ywyy">久遠也。</span><span class="swdz">久者、不暫也。遠者、不近也。引伸之爲滋長、長幼之長。今音知丈切。又爲多餘之長、度長之長皆今音直亮切。兄下曰。長也。是滋長、長幼之長也。</span><span class="ywyy">从兀。从匕。</span><span class="swdz">會意。匕呼霸切。</span><span class="ywyy">亾聲。</span><span class="swdz">二字各本在變匕之下。今依韵會正。直良切。十部。</span><span class="ywyy">兀者、高遠意也。</span><span class="swdz">說从兀之意。儿部曰。兀者、高而上平也。</span><span class="ywyy">久則變匕。</span><span class="swdz">匕各本作化。今正。說从匕之意。匕下曰。變也。</span><span class="ywyy">？者、到亾也。</span><span class="swdz">到各本作倒。今正。說？卽亾字。亾而倒、變匕之意。</span><span class="ywyy">凡長之屬皆从長。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9577_7267.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20477.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠑷</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文長。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9577_7268.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2050A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔊</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">亦古文長。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
