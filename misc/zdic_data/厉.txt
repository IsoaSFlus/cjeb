                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厉</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>厉</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">厲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>严格：～禁（➊列队警戒；➋严厉的禁令）。～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）节约。</li>
	<li>严肃：严～。声色俱～。</li>
	<li>凶猛：～害。雷～风行。色～内荏。</li>
	<li>磨，使锋利：～兵秣马。再接再～。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/疠">疠</a>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">癞</span>&rdquo;，恶疮。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> whetstone; grind, sharpen; whet</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ernst, stark  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> strict,dur,sévère</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>厉</strong></p>
<p>厲 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (是&ldquo;砺&rdquo;的本字。从厂( <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;n</span>)，象山崖形，表示与山石有关。本义:磨刀石)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rubstone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;旱石者，刚于柔石者也。字亦作厲、作礪。&rdquo; <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:&ldquo;精者曰厎，粗者曰厉。汉书地理志，述禹贡砥厉砮丹;诗公刘，取厉取锻，礼内则，刀砺;西山经，苕水其中多砥厉;中山经，阴山多砺石。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涉</span><span class="diczx2">渭</span>为乱，取厉取锻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">陆德明</span><span class="smcs">《经典释文》</span>:&ldquo;厉，本又作砺。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 祸患;危险 <span class="encs">[trouble;danger]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">降此大厉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;瞻卬》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厉阶(祸端)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 恶鬼 <span class="encs">[evil ghost]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>侯梦大厉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 疠，瘟疫，传染病 <span class="encs">[pestilence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盗贼公行，而天厉不戒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">漆身为厉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;范蔡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鲜不为厉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《正气歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 河岸，水旁;边 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有狐绥绥，在彼</span><span class="diczx2">淇</span>厉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;有狐》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 带，衣带的下垂部分 <span class="encs">[band]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垂带而厉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;都人士》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鞶厉游缨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">带之垂者谓之厉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广服》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;癞&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i</span>)。恶疮 <span class="encs">[favus]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">豫让</span>又漆身为厉，吞炭为哑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;刺客列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>厉</strong></p>
<p>厲 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 磨，磨快<span class="encs">[sharpen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厉，磨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">砥厉廉隅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></span></p>

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/20A84_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A84.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠪄</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">旱石也。</span><span class="swdz">旱石者，剛於柔石者也。禹貢，厲砥砮丹。大雅：取厲取鍛。引伸之羲爲作也。見釋詁，又危也。見大雅民勞傳，虞注周易，又烈也。見招䰟王注：俗以義異，異其形。凡砥厲字作礪，凡勸勉字作勵，惟嚴厲字作厲。而古引伸假借之法隱矣。凡經傳中有訓爲惡，訓爲病，訓爲鬼者，謂厲卽癘之假借也。訓爲遮列者，謂厲卽迾之假借也。周禮之厲禁是也。有訓爲涉水者，謂厲卽濿之假借，如詩深則厲是也。有訓爲帶之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2136E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡍮</span>者，如都人士<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2136E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡍮</span>帶而厲。傳謂厲卽烈之假借也。烈，餘也。</span><span class="ywyy">从厂，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27363.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧍣</span>省聲。</span> <span class="swdz">按說文萬與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27363.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧍣</span>篆形絕異，厲从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27363.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧍣</span>省聲，則字當作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A84.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠪄</span>。而<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/256EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥛬</span>體<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27363.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧍣</span>作蠆，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A84.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠪄</span>作厲，皆从萬。非也。後人以<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/256EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥛬</span>改篆，則又篆皆从萬矣。漢<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/256EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥛬</span>存者，作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/274F5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧓵</span>作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20ABF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠪿</span>可考也。今篆體及說解皆正。力制切，十五部。</span></p>
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/20ABF_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20ABF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠪿</span>)</span> <span class="ywyy">或不省。</span> <span class="swdz">今各本篆从萬下虫。非是。漢人<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/256EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥛬</span>多作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20ABF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠪿</span>，不省。厥發石也发石故从厂，引伸之凡有撅發皆曰：厥。山海經</span></p>                          </div>
