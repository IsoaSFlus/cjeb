                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>乖</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">guāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guāi.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄞˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>不顺，不和谐：～舛（谬误）。～谬。～戾（性情、言语、行为别扭，不合情理）。～僻（怪僻）。～张（怪僻，不讲情理）。出～露丑。</li>
	<li>机灵，伶俐（多指小孩听话）：～～。～巧。卖～。～觉（<span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span>）。～顺。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乖</strong> <span class="dicpy">guāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，象羊角形，从&ldquo;北&rdquo;。从&ldquo;北&rdquo;，取其分背的意思。本义:背离，违背，不和谐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[perverse;violate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乖，戾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刚柔得适谓之和，反和为乖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾独乖刺而无当兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;怨世》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乖，背也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>执政众而乖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三十年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上下相反，好恶乖迕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唯恐道途显晦，不相通达，致负诚托，又乖恳愿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今墨家非儒，儒家非墨，各有所持，故乖不合。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;薄葬》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乖礼(违背成礼、定制);乖角(违背抵触);乖贰(背离;违异);乖背(违背);乖则(违反法则)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 分离;离别 <span class="encs">[separate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔我同袍，今永乖别。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《朔风》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乖分(分离;分裂);乖别(分离;离别);乖拆(分离);乖析(分离，支离破碎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 断绝;隔绝 <span class="encs">[break off]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:乖绝(隔绝);乖隔(阻隔);乖梗(受阻而分离)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乖</strong> <span class="dicpy">guāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不同;差异 <span class="encs">[different]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人度量之乖舛，何相越之辽迥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《西征赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乖讹(差错;乖僻);乖越(差失，错误;不相当);乖舛(乖殊。乖异;不同)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 反常;谬误 <span class="encs">[abnormal;wrong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朕醉中处事有乖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《辽史&middot;穆宗纪》</span></span></p>
