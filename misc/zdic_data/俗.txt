                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">俗</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>俗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sú.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>社会上长期形成的风尚、礼节、习惯等：～尚。风～。习～。约定～成（指某种事物是由群众通过长期实践而认定形成）。</li><li>大众化的，最通行的，习见的：～名。～语。～曲。雅～共赏。</li><li>趣味不高的，令人讨厌的：～气。～物。鄙～。粗～。庸～。</li><li>凡世间，相对于仙佛僧道：～人。世～。僧～。凡夫～子。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> social customs; vulgar, unrefined</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gewöhnlich, abgeschmackt ,Sitte, Gebräuche</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> coutume,populaire,vulgaire</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">俗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>俗</strong> <span class="dicpy">sú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，谷( <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span>)声。习俗是人的行为，所以从人。本义:风俗)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(长期形成的礼节、习惯) <span class="encs">[custom]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俗，习也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六曰以俗教安。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司徒》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入国而问俗。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循俗未足多也。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·泛论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俗主亏情。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·情欲》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“凡君也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">移风易俗，天下皆宁。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·乐论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亦不敢服垢弊以矫俗干名，但顺吾性而已。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淫侈之俗日日以长，是天下之贼也。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:风俗(风，指一时普遍流行的风气;俗，指长期形成的礼节、习惯);习俗(习惯和风俗);民俗(人民群众的风俗习惯);俗忌(一般习俗所忌讳的事);俗化(民俗教化)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 佛教称尘世间为俗，与出家相对 <span class="encs">[this mortal world]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时有沙门</span><span class="diczx2">释惠林</span>… <span class="diczx2">世祖</span>命使还俗。——<span class="smcs">《宋书·徐堪之传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:俗计(尘世间的生计);俗缘(尘缘。指佛门信徒与世俗的人际关系);俗本(民间通行的版本);俗骨(尘世间人的资质或禀赋);俗界(凡界，尘界);俗纷(尘世的纷扰);俗声，俗喧(尘世的喧扰);俗间(世间，尘间)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 一般人;百姓 <span class="encs">[layman]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俗之所欲，因而予之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·管晏列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是己而非人，俗之同病。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:僧俗(僧尼和一般人);俗家(指一般人家。对出家人而言);俗姓(僧侣未出家前的姓氏);俗父(出家人称生父为俗父);俗众(群众，普通人);俗圣(凡庸人与圣人)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>俗</strong> <span class="dicpy">sú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 平凡;普通;一般 <span class="encs">[mediocre;plain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请回俗士驾，为君谢逋客。——</span><span class="diczx2">孔稚圭</span><span class="smcs">《北山移文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">察其本，乃与俗议之异。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李斯列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不论姚花与魏花，只供俗目陪妖姹。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩琦</span><span
