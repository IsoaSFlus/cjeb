                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>只</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">隻</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>量词：一～鸡。</li>    <li>单独的，极少的：～身。片纸～字。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>只</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">衹</span>、<span class="diczx3">祇</span>、<span class="diczx3">秖</span></strong>）</p><p><span class="dicpy">zhǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>仅仅，惟一：～是（❶仅仅是；❷表示强调限于某个情况或范围；❸但是）。</li>    <li>表示限于某个范围：～顾。～管。～见树木，不见森林。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">只</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>只</strong></p><p>隻 <span class="dicpy">zhī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从又，持隹。持一隹曰隻，持二隹曰雙。本义:鸟一只) 同本义 <span class="encs">[a bird]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">隻，鸟一枚也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如彼翰林鸟，双栖一朝只。——<span class="smcs">《悼亡诗三首》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:只翼(一只鸟。比喻孤单)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>只</strong></p><p>隻 <span class="dicpy">zhī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p>用于计量鸡、禽、牛、羊、球、手、足等的数目。如:两只手;三只鸡</p><p>◎ <strong>只</strong></p><p>隻 <span class="dicpy">zhī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引申为凡物之单者曰只。单一 <span class="encs">[single]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫遣只轮归海窟，仍留一箭射天山。——</span><span class="diczx2">李益</span><span class="smcs">《塞下曲》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匹马只轮无反者。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·僖公三十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:只轮不返(就连一只战车轮子都回不去了。比喻大败，全军覆灭);只鸡架酒(比喻祭品虽菲薄简陋，然情意甚重);只手(单手。转喻为独力);只句(单句，零星散句);只字(单字，零星的字迹或文辞);只曲(指北曲中不成套的小令);只眼(独眼。比喻独特的见地)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 孤独 <span class="encs">[alone]</span>。如:只立(孤立);只凰(无偶的凰鸟。比喻无配偶的男子);只影(孤影。比喻孤单)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 单数;奇数 <span class="encs">[odd]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">肃宗</span>而下，咸只日临朝，双日不坐。——<span
