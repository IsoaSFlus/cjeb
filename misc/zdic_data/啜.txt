data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>另见 <span class="dicpy">chuò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/啜喇">啜喇</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>啜</strong> <span class="dicpy">chuò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:尝、喝)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啜，尝也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子啜其羹。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·非相》</span></span></p><p><span class="smcs">《送穷文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众人皆醉，何不餔其糟而啜其醨?——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:啜茗(品茶);啜饮(饮;喝);啜哺(饮食;吃喝);啜息(饮食休息);啜食(犹言吃喝);啜汁(喝汤。比喻乘机邀功或沾光吃闲饭);啜人贼(犹害人精。啜人，谓吸人精血);啜吒(形容吞咽之声);啜英咀华(比喻品赏、体味诗文的精华)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 哭泣时抽噎 <span class="encs">[sob]</span>。如:啜涕(哭泣);啜血(犹泣血。极其悲痛而无声的哭泣)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 哄骗 <span class="encs">[cheat;hoax]</span>。如:啜持(哄骗，逗引);啜哄(哄骗);啜醋(虚意道歉)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr
