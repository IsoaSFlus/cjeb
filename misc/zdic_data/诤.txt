                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诤</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">諍</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhèng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhèng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>谏，照直说出人的过错，叫人改正：～谏。～言。～臣。～友（能直言规劝的朋友。亦作“争友”）。</li><li>〔～人〕古代传说中的矮小人种。亦称“靖人”。</li><li>纷争，争：～紊（争论是非）。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诤</strong></p><p>諍 <span class="dicpy">zhēng</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“争”</span>。争论;争讼 <span class="encs">[argue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">王凤</span>为平理诤讼。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·刘玄传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诤，讼也。——<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼诚以天下之必无仙，而我独以实有而与之诤，诤之弥久，而彼执之弥回。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">葛洪</span><span class="smcs">《抱朴子》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:诤讼(争论);诤论(争论);诤薮(争讼的渊薮);诤辞(争讼之辞);诤诉(诉讼)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 争奇;竞争 <span class="encs">[contend;forestall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽时时与师友有诤有讲，然师友总不以我为嫌者，知我无诤心也，彼此各求以自得也。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李贽</span><span class="smcs">《与河南吴中丞书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:诤治(竞相采取治国之道);诤心(争高低之心);诤竞(竞争)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“争”</span>。争夺<span class="encs">[contend;vie;strive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有两虎诤人而斗者。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策二》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阘茸勇敢于饕诤。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·王沈传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhèng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhèng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诤</strong></p><p>諍 <span class="dicpy">zhèng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhèng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，争声。因与言论有关，故从言。<span class="yhcs">“诤”</span>字本作<span class="yhcs">“争”</span>，<span class="yhcs">“诤”</span>是后起字，意思是强谏。用则可生，不用则死，程度比<span class="yhcs">“谏”</span>重。本义:直言规劝) 同本义(尤指友谊诚挚的劝告) <span class="encs">[admonish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诤，谏也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士有诤友，则身不离于令名。——<span class="smcs">《孝经·谏诤》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:默诤(背地里直言劝告);诤谏(以直言劝止他人的过失);诤臣(能谏诤的臣子);诤戒(告诫)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhēng</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div
