						<p>● <strong>梓</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>落叶乔木。木材可供建筑及制造器物之用：～器（棺材）。～宫（皇帝的棺材）。</li>
	<li>治木器：～人（古代制造器具的木工）。～匠。</li>
	<li>木头雕刻成印刷用的木板：付～（把稿件交付排印）。～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>指故里：～里。桑～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">梓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>梓</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，宰省声。本义:木名，即梓树)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Chinese catalpa]</span>。一种原产中国的梓树属落叶乔木，高6&mdash;9米，叶对生，宽卵形，先端尖。大的圆锥花序，顶生，黄白色，略带紫色斑点，蒴果长丝状，种子扁平，木材可供建筑及制作木器用</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梓，楸也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树之榛栗，椅、桐、梓、漆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鄘风&middot;定之方中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江南出楠梓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:美国梓;海地梓;四川梓</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 制作木器的人 <span class="encs">[woodworker]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攻木之工，轮、舆、弓、炉、匠、车、梓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:梓人(古代木工的一种);梓匠(两种木工。梓，梓人，造器具;匠，匠人，主建筑);梓师(古代梓人之长);梓器(木工所制的器具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 雕刻印书的木版 <span class="encs">[printing blocks]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻以</span><span class="diczx2">华严</span>合论梓行，此希有功德也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">张居正</span><span class="smcs">《答奉常陆五台书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:梓人(指印刷业的刻版工人);梓行(刻版印行);梓刻(雕版，表示书将印行)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 故乡的代称。桑梓的简称 <span class="encs">[native place]</span> 因为古代宅旁常栽的树，是梓和桑</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾不爱锦衣，荣归夸梓里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘迎</span><span class="smcs">《题刘德文戏彩堂》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:梓里(故乡);梓乡(故乡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>梓</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>刻板，付印 <span class="encs">[print]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故归梓删去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋应星</span><span class="smcs">《天工开物序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其友</span><span class="diczx2">涂伯</span>聚为之梓行。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:付梓(付印)</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">植物名。紫葳科梓屬，落葉喬木。葉對生，呈掌狀淺裂。夏日開淡黃色而帶有暗紫斑點的花。木材供建築及製作器具。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">製作木器的工匠。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;盡心下》：「<b>梓</b>、匠、輪、輿，能與人規矩，不能使人巧。」</span></p></li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋時鄭國有梓慎。</span></p></li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">梓</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              梓·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6893.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】卽里切【集韻】【正韻】祖似切【韻會】祖士切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音耔。【說文】楸也。或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2367C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣙼</span>、榟。【通志】梓與楸相似。【爾雅&middot;釋木】椅，梓。【郭註】卽楸。【陸璣&middot;草木疏】楸之疏理白色而生子者爲梓。【埤雅】梓爲百木長，故呼梓爲木王。羅願云：屋室有此木，則餘材皆不震。</p>
<p>又橋梓。【尚書&middot;大傳】橋者，父道也。梓者，子道也。</p>
<p>又桑梓，父之所樹。【詩&middot;小雅】維桑與梓，必恭敬止。</p>
<p>又《梓材》《周書》篇名。古作杍材。【註】治木器曰梓，治土器曰陶，治金器曰冶。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】攻木之工七：輪輿弓廬匠車梓。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】漑者不寫。【疏】杯盂之屬。亦曰梓。</p>
<p>又姓。梓愼，見【左傳】。</p>
<p>又俗謂鋟文書於板曰梓。</p>
<p>又一種鼠梓，名楰，江東人謂之虎梓。</p>
<p>又【古今註】梓實曰豫章。</p>
