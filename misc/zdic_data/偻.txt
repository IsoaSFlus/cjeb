                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>偻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">僂</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǚ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄩˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>脊背弯曲：伛～（弯腰驼背）。</li>
	<li>迅速：不能～指（不能迅速指出来）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>偻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">僂</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～儸〕➊干练；机灵。➋指绿林或盗贼的部下、仆从。</li>
	<li>〔佝（<span class="diczx3">僂</span>）<span class="dicpy">gōu</span>）～〕见&ldquo;<a href="/hans/佝">佝</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> humpback; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Buckel, Bucklige  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">偻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>偻</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 僂 <span class="dicpy">l&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &mdash;&mdash;见&ldquo;佝偻&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">gōul&oacute;u</span>)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">lǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>偻</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，娄声。本义:弯腰)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偻，尩也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span>:</span><span class="diczx2">周公</span>背偻是为强后。此字本训背曲。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偻者不袒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;问丧》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见疴偻者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;达生》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行步俯偻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;蔡义传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再命而偻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偻巫跛觋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">正考父一命而伛(曲背)，再命而偻(弯腰)，三命而俯，循墙而走，孰敢不轨!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">同行则偻身自卑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;邓后传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:偻背(弓着背);偻偻(弓身貌。形容恭顺);偻行(曲背而行。形容衰老);偻步(驼着背走路);偻人(曲背的人。借指老人);偻身(弯下身子);偻俯(弯下身子，表示恭敬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指弯曲 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽有圣人之知，未能偻指也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />

                              僂·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/50C2.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】落侯切【集韻】【韻會】郞侯切【正韻】盧侯切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音樓。【說文】尫也。【六書故】曲背也。別作瘻。【穀梁傳&middot;成元年】曹公于手僂。【淮南子&middot;地形訓】西方高土，其人面末僂修頸，卬行。【白虎通】周公背僂。【莊子&middot;達生篇】痀僂承蜩。</p>
<p>又屈也。【荀子&middot;儒效篇】雖有聖人之知，未能僂指也。</p>
<p>又曲薄曰聚僂。【莊子&middot;達生篇】聚僂之中，則爲之。【註】曲而可以聚物曰聚僂，畚筥之類是也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>郞豆切，樓去聲。僂佝，短醜貌。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>隴主切，音縷。義同。</p>
<p>又僂句。【左傳&middot;昭二十五年】臧昭伯如晉，臧會竊其寶龜僂句。【註】僂句，龜所出地名。</p>
<p>又【韻會】龍遇切，音慮。義同。　【集韻】作軁。</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/50C2.svg">                          <p>尫也。从人婁聲。周公韈僂，或言背僂。力主切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/50C2_299.svg" /><span class="swbf">(僂)</span><span class="ywyy">厄也。</span><span class="swdz">大徐本作尩也，非是。尩與僂異疾。小徐本作厄也，近是。科厄，木節也。厄與僂雙聲。人背之僂，有似木之科厄。晉語注曰：戚施，僂人也。左傳昭四年注：僂，肩傴也。穀梁傳跛僂並言。管子云：苦水所，多尩與傴人。然僂不得訓尩明矣。公羊傳：夫人不僂，不可使入。何云：僂，疾也，齊人語。夫人稽畱，不肎疾順公。荀卿書亦兩言僂訓疾。按此爲婁之假借字，婁卽屢，與驟通。驟訓數，亦訓疾。○又按大徐作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21BF2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡯲</span>也，葢<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21BF2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡯲</span>是曲脛之名，引伸爲曲脊之名。所以<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21BF2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡯲</span>必釋爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24FD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤿑</span>曲脛者，由其字作尢，从大而偏曲其足，故二疾不妨同名。下文曰周公韤僂，葢<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21BF2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡯲</span>者亦得謂之僂，周公之脛不無少曲。疑大徐本爲長。甲戌二月重訂。</span><span class="ywyy">从人婁聲。</span><span class="swdz">力主切。古音在四部。</span><span class="ywyy">周公韤僂，</span><span class="swdz">韤者，足衣。韤僂者，由足背高隆然如背之僂也。未聞出何書。</span><span class="ywyy">或言背僂。</span><span class="swdz">周公背僂見荀卿、白虎通、論衡諸書。白虎通云：傳曰：周公背僂，是爲強後，成就周道，輔於幼主。僂後主爲韵。</span></p>                          </div>
