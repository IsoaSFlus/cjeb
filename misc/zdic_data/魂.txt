                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>魂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hún  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hún.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>迷信的人指附在人体上主宰人，又可离开肉体而独立存在的实体：～灵。鬼～。～不附体。</li><li>指精神或情绪：～飞魄散（形容极度惊恐）。～不守舍。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">魂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>魂</strong> <span class="dicpy">hún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鬼，云声。本义:灵魂，古人想象的能离开人体而存在的精神)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[soul]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">魂，阳气也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人生始化为魄，既生魄，阳曰魂。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聊乐我魂。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗·出其东门》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">魂者，精气也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·纪妖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">精气为物，游魂为变。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">随神而来往者谓之魂。——<span class="smcs">《灵枢经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">魂去尸长留。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">魂悸以魄动。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:魂识(心灵;魂灵);魂气(灵魂);魂骸(死者的灵魂和躯体);魂质(灵魂和实体)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 精神，神志 <span class="encs">[spirit;mood]</span>。如:断魂;梦魂萦绕;神魂颠倒;魂交(精神相感应，结合);魂鉴(精神理智)。泛指一切事物的精灵。如:花魂;诗魂;柳魂</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 国家、民族崇高的精神 <span class="encs">[the lofty spirit of a nation]</span>。如:民族魂;国魂</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/魂不附体">魂不附体</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/魂不守舍">魂不守舍</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/魂飞魄散">魂飞魄散</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/魂魄">魂魄</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/魂牵梦萦">魂牵梦萦</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>魂</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄨㄣˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hún <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2954.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人的精气。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「灵<b>魂</b>」、「三魂七魄」、「借尸还<b>魂</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《乐府诗集·卷七三·杂曲歌辞十三·古辞·焦仲卿妻》：「我命绝今日，<b>魂</b>去尸长留。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《西游记·第一二回》：「是我太宗皇帝死去三日，还<b>魂</b>复生。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">泛指各种事物的精神。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「国<b>魂</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·温庭筠〈春江花月夜词〉：「千里涵空澄水<b>魂</b>，万枝破鼻飘香雪。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·苏轼〈再用松风亭下梅花盛开韵〉：「罗浮山下梅花村，玉雪为骨冰为<b>魂</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人的神志、意念。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「神魂颠倒」、「黯然<b>魂</b>销」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·许敬宗〈谢𠡠书表〉：「引领天庭，望丹霄而结恋；驰<b>魂</b>魏阙，惧黄落而长违。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
