                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>芽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yá.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄚˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>植物的幼体，可以发育成茎、叶或花的那一部分：发～。嫩～。幼～。萌～。豆～。</li>    <li>形状像芽的东西：肉～（伤口愈合后多长出的肉）。银～（银矿苗）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>芽</strong> <span class="dicpy">yá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，牙声。本义:尚未发育成长的枝、叶或花的雏体)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bud;sprout;shoot]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芽，萌芽也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“古多以牙为芽。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芽，孽也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释草》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂令黄泉下，萌芽夭句尖。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《苦寒诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二月草已芽，八月草未枯。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">同芽者。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粪溉者先芽。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒌蒿满地芦芽短。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《惠崇</p><p><span class="diczx1">春江晚景</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:芽茶(最嫩的茶叶)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 形状像芽的东西 <span class="encs">[sth.resembling a bud]</span>。如:肉芽(伤口愈合后多长出来的肉);银芽(露出地面的银矿石)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芽孢">芽孢</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芽茶">芽茶</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芽豆">芽豆</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芽接">芽接</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芽体">芽体</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芽条">芽条</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芽眼">芽眼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/芽殖细菌">芽殖细菌</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>芽</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄧㄚˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yá <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5952.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">植物初生的苗。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「豆<b>芽</b>」、「麦<b>芽</b>」、「嫩<b>芽</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·白居易〈种桃歌〉：「食桃种其核，一年核生<b>芽</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·辛弃疾〈鹧鸪天·陌上柔桑初破<b>芽</b>〉词：「陌上柔桑初破<b>芽</b>，东邻蚕种已生些。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">形状像<b><b>芽</b></b>的东西。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「肉<b>芽</b>」、「银<b>芽</b>」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋时邹有<b><b>芽</b></b>亶。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">发<b><b>芽</b></b>。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·欧阳修〈重赠刘原父〉诗：「而今春物已烂漫，念昔草木冰未<b>芽</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">开始、发生。</span><span class="gc_yy">汉·蔡邕〈释诲〉：「神疾其邪，利端始萌，害渐以<b>芽</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晋·江统〈函谷关赋〉：「遏奸宄于未<b>芽</b>，殿邪伪于萌渐。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
