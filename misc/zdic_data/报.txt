                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>报</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">報</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>传达，告知：～告。～喜。～捷。～考。～请（用书面报告向上级请示）。～废。</li>
	<li>传达消息和言论的文件、信号或出版物：简～（文字较短、内容简略的书面报告，印发给有关部门）。电～。情～。晚～。画～。～端。</li>
	<li>回答：～答。～恩。～仇。～国（报效祖国）。～酬。</li>
	<li>由于做了坏事而受到惩罚：～应。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">报</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>报</strong></p>
<p>報 <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从帇，从阜，金文字形，左边象刑具形，即&ldquo;帇( <span class="dicpy">ni&egrave;</span>)&rdquo;。右象手按人使之跽跪意，即&ldquo;阜&rdquo;，读 <span class="dicpy">fǔ</span>。组合在一起表示治人罪之意。本义:断狱，判决罪人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[judge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辟报故不穷审。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;胡建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">报囚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;鲁恭传》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓奏请报决也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">报而罪之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻死刑之报，君为流涕。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:报囚(判决囚犯);报当(判罪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 报答，报酬 <span class="encs">[recompense;repay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲报之于陛下也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲略上报。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:报本(报答;回报);报称(报答恩德);报命(报答恩情;执行命令);报捐(捐钱买官);报塞(报答;报效);报谢(报答;答谢)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 报告，答复 <span class="encs">[report]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">列子</span>行泣报 <span class="diczx2">壶子</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;精神》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">荀息</span>操璧牵马而报。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;权勋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阙然久不报，幸勿为过!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任少卿书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请为</span><span class="diczx2">张唐</span>先报 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策五》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求人可使报</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一日晌午，谍报敌骑至。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">具以</span><span class="diczx2">沛公</span>言报 <span class="diczx2">项王</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:报录人(专门向升了官或考中了科的人家里送喜报的人);报辜(报案状子);报喜;报数;报春(报告春天的到来)</span></p>
