                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>放</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fàng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>解脱约束，得到自由：把笼子里的鸟～了。～胆。～诞。～任。～肆。～歌。～怀。豪～。释～。</li><li>散（<span class="dicpy">sàn</span>）：～工。～假。～学。～晴（阴雨后转晴）。</li><li>带牲畜到野外去吃草：～牧。～羊（亦喻任其自由行动，放手不管，含贬义）。</li><li>驱逐到远方去：～逐。～黜。流～。</li><li>到基层去：～官（古时指任命为外地官员）。下～。</li><li>发出：～电。～毒。～光。发～。</li><li>借钱给别人，收取利息：～债。～贷。</li><li>扩展：～大。～宽。</li><li>花开：百花齐～。心花怒～。</li><li>搁、置：这件事情不要紧，先～一～。存～。～弃。～心。～置。</li><li>至：～乎四海。摩顶～踵。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">放</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>放</strong> <span class="dicpy">fàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，方声。本义:驱逐，流放)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[banish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">放，逐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见</span><span class="diczx2">伯夷</span>之放迹。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·悲回风》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卿大夫外淫者放。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">放</span><span class="diczx2">驩</span>兜于 <span class="diczx2">崇山</span>。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">放弑其君则残之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司马》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">放其大夫。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>放其大臣 <span class="diczx2">孟尝君</span>于诸侯。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽放流，眷顾</span><span class="diczx2">楚国</span>，系心 <span class="diczx2">怀王</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">何故怀瑾握瑜而自令见放为?</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:流放(把犯人驱逐到边远地方去);放伐(以武力讨伐并放逐暴君);放徒(放野。放逐于野);放远(远逐);放废(放逐罢黜);放迁(放逐)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 舍弃;废置 <span class="encs">[abandon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">放，弃也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟威惟虐，大放王命。——<span class="smcs">《书·康诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:放丢(扔，丢放);放绝(废弃);放蔑(废弃蔑视)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 解除约束;释放 <span class="encs">[free;release]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昔</span><span class="diczx2">毛宝</span>放龟而得渡。—— <span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">休放往来人闲走。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">关汉卿</span><span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:把俘虏放回去;放赦(宽赦;赦免);放脱(放走);放免(放赦。释放，赦免);放遣(释放;遣散);放释(释放)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 免去;免除 <span class="encs">[exempt from]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">任事者知不足以治职，则放官收。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·八经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span>
