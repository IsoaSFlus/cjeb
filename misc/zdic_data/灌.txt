                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>灌</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>浇地：～溉。～渠。浇～。</li>
	<li>注入液体：～注。～浆。～肠。</li>
	<li>指录音：～唱片。</li>
	<li>炼铸。</li>
	<li>丛生，亦指丛生的树木：～木。～丛。</li>
	<li>古代祭祀的一种仪式，斟酒浇地降神。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pour; water; irrigate, flood</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> füllt ,gießen, einschenken ,übergießen  (V)​,füllen  (V)​,gefüllt  (V)​,Guan  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> irriguer,verser,remplir,enregistrer,inculquer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">灌</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>灌</strong> <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，雚( <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:灌水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Guan River]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灌，灌水也。出庐江雩娄北入淮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 灌水，源出河南省商城县南，东北经固始县西南曰曲河，北合史河流入淮水</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 丛生的树木 <span class="encs">[shrub]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灌木，丛木。又，木族生为灌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灌，丛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">集于灌木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;葛覃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而灌木丛林，蒙茸山麓，未经删治如今者，则无疑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灌木;灌莽;灌丛</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>灌</strong> <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 浇;灌溉 <span class="encs">[irrigate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灌，浇也，渍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时雨降矣，而犹浸灌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以筒水灌之始出，状极俊健。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">引河水灌民田。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;西门豹列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灌植(浇水培植);灌濡(浇灌润泽);灌渎(灌溉用的小沟渠);灌畦(灌溉种菜);灌蔬(栽培菜蔬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 流注 <span class="encs">[pour]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秋水时至，百川灌河。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灌溢(流布);灌澍(灌注，流泻);灌洗(冲洗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 强行使喝下 <span class="encs">[drink by force]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能饮者以筒灌其中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说疑》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/704C.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古玩切，音貫。水名。【說文】水出廬江雩婁，北入淮。</p>
<p>又水名。【山海經】石脆之山，灌水出焉。</p>
<p>又【羅含湘中記】營水，渄水，灌水，皆注湘。</p>

<p>又注也。【莊子&middot;秋水篇】百川灌河。</p>
<p>又飮也。【禮&middot;投壷】奉觴曰賜灌。</p>
<p>又【博雅】灌，聚也。</p>
<p>又木叢生曰灌。【詩&middot;周南】黃鳥于飛，集于灌木。</p>
<p>又盡誠相告曰灌灌。【詩&middot;大雅】老夫灌灌。</p>
<p>又鳥名。【山海經】靑丘山有鳥如鳩，其音若呵，名曰灌灌。</p>
<p>又【騈雅】灌灌，九尾狐。</p>
<p>又與祼通。【周禮&middot;春官】以肆獻祼享先王。【註】祼之言灌也。【疏】以鬱鬯灌地降神，取澆灌之義，故从水。</p>
<p>又【集韻】胡玩切，音換。與渙同。水流盛貌。</p>
<p>又古緩切，音管。與盥同。澡手也。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/704C_3638.svg" /><span class="swbf">(灌)</span><span class="ywyy">灌水。出廬江雩婁，北入淮。</span><span class="swdz">廬江郡雩婁，二志同。前志雩婁下曰：決水，北至蓼入淮。灌水，亦北至蓼入決。過郡二，行五百一十里。過郡二者，廬江、六安國也。水經注：決水自安豐縣故城西北逕蓼縣故城東。又西北，灌水注之。水導源廬江金蘭縣西北東陵鄉大蘇山，東北逕蓼縣故城西而注決水。決水又北入於淮。按前志廬江郡下云：金蘭西北有東陵鄉，淮水出。依酈注，則金蘭者縣名。而志無此縣。志云淮水出，乃灌水出之誤。葢出金蘭東陵鄉逕雩婁至蓼入決也。酈云：灌水俗謂之澮水。又云：決水北入於淮。俗謂之澮口。葢灌澮聲相倫，習俗害眞尒。酈時已與古名全違，今則更難詳矣。方輿紀要曰：雩婁，今安徽潁州府霍丘縣西南八十里有雩婁故城。左傳襄卄六年：楚人侵吳及雩婁。昭五年：薳啓疆待命於雩婁。是其地也。蓼縣城在霍丘縣西北。決水卽今史河。灌水今自河南固始縣東流，經霍丘縣西。合史水入淮是也。</span><span class="ywyy">从水雚聲。</span><span class="swdz">古玩切。十四部。按今字以爲灌注、灌漑之字。</span></p>                          </div>
