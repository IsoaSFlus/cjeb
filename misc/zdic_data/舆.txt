						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>舆</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">輿</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>车中装载东西的部分，后泛指车：～马。肩～。</li>
	<li>古代奴隶中的一个等级，泛指地位低微的人，亦用以指众人的：～台。～论。～情（群众的意见和态度）。</li>
	<li>疆域：～地。～图。堪～（相地，风水）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">舆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>舆</strong></p>
<p>輿 <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，舁( <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:车厢)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cabin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舆，车舆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，车中受物之处。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舆人为车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木材&hellip;曲者宜为轮，直者宜为舆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《潜夫论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 车辆，尤指马车 <span class="encs">[carriage;chariot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">困于金舆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;困》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今乘舆已驾矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽有舟舆，无所乘之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:舆士(车夫);舆服(车子和衣冠的总称);舆几(车子与几案);舆马(车马);舆轮(车轮);舆骑(车骑);舆帅(主管兵车的官吏);舆薪(车子、柴木等大东西。喻指显而易见的东西)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 轿子 <span class="encs">[sedan chair]</span>。如:肩舆;彩舆;舆士(轿夫);舆夫(轿夫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 贱官，仆役 <span class="encs">[servant]</span>。如:舆隶(贱役，奴仆);舆皂(古代十等人中两个低微等级的名称。用以泛称贱役;贱吏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Yu prefecture]</span>。汉置，属临淮郡，东汉改属广陵郡，南朝宋并入江都县。故城在今江苏省江都县西</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>舆</strong></p>
<p>輿 <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大众 <span class="encs">[popular;public]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舆，多也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皂臣舆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公七年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;众也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:舆情不洽(众人反映不好);舆议咸冤(众人都认为冤枉);舆望(众望)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>舆</strong></p>
<p>輿 <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;舁&rdquo;。共同抬<span class="encs">[东西]</span> <span class="encs">[(of two or more persons) carry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂舆机而往。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曾子问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舆豭从之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:舆机(置尸于机上抬之。机，抬尸之床。用指出殡)</p>

                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“舆”字头，请参考“輿”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=車" target="_blank">車</a>】
                              輿·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8F3F.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】以諸切【集韻】【韻會】羊諸切【等韻】雲諸切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音余。【說文】車底也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】輿人爲車。【註】輿人專作輿，而言爲車者，車以輿爲主也。【後漢&middot;輿服志】上古聖人觀轉蓬始爲輪，輪行不可載，因物生智，復爲之輿。【韻會】《詩詁》曰：輈軸之上加板以載物，軫、軾、轛、較之所附植，輿，其總名也。</p>
<p>又乗輿。【蔡邕&middot;獨斷】天子所御車馬、衣服、器械、百物曰乗輿。【註】輿，車也。乗，載也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】權輿，始也。造衡自權始，造車自輿始也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】堪輿，天地之總名。【前漢&middot;藝文志】《堪輿金匱》十四卷。【註】堪，天道。輿，地道。</p>
<p>又載而行之之意。【左傳&middot;僖十一年】敬禮之輿也。【註】謂其載禮以行也。</p>
<p>又【襄二十四年】令名德之輿也。</p>
<p>又負也。【戰國策】百人輿瓢而趨。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】多也。【集韻】衆也。【左傳&middot;僖二十八年】晉文公聽輿人之誦。</p>
<p>又【韻會】扶輿，佳氣貌。</p>
<p>又美稱。【司馬相如&middot;子虛賦】扶輿綺靡。</p>
<p>又輿人，賤官。【左傳&middot;昭四年】輿人納之。</p>
<p>又丘輿，地名。【左傳&middot;成四年】鄭公子偃敗諸丘輿。【註】鄭地。</p>
<p>又平輿。【後漢&middot;郡國志】平輿，屬汝南郡。【註】古沈子國也。</p>
<p>又姓。【韻會】周大夫伯輿之後。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>羊茹切，音豫。【增韻】舁車也。兩手對舉之車。　【集韻】或作轝。【史記&middot;封禪書】作轝。轝字从車从與，作車與。</p>
