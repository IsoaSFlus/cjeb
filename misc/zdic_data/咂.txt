						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄚˉ</span></p><ol><li>舌头与腭接触发声，表示赞叹或羡慕：～嘴。</li><li>吸，小口儿喝：～一口酒。</li><li>仔细辨别：～摸（“<span class="diczx3">摸</span>”读轻声）。～滋味。</li></ol>
