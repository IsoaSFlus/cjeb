                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>剑</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">劍</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>古代的一种兵器：宝～。长～。～鞘。～术。～拔弩张（形容形势紧张，一触即发，后亦喻书法雄健）。刻舟求～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sword, dagger, saber</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schwert (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">剑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>剑</strong></p>
<p>劍、劒 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，佥( <span class="dicpy">qiān</span>)声。本义:古代兵器。长刃两面，中间有脊，短柄)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sword;dagger;sabre]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劒，人所带兵也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。注:&ldquo;今之匕首。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔</span><span class="diczx2">葛卢之山</span>发而出金， <span class="diczx2">蚩尤</span>受而制之为剑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">负剑辟咡诏之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剑寒花不落，弓晓月逾明。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">虞世南</span><span class="smcs">《从军行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剑锷(剑的锋刃);剑首(剑环。用玉或金属制成，上面镂刻有花纹);剑气(剑的光芒;比喻人的声望)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 剑术 <span class="encs">[swordplay]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:剑士(剑术高明的人);剑仙(精于剑术的仙人)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>剑</strong></p>
<p>劍 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 以剑杀人 <span class="encs">[kill with sword]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剑斩</span><span class="diczx2">虞常</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">东汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有</span><span class="diczx2">司马叔</span><span class="diczx2">持</span>者，白日于都市手剑父仇。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《马穫督诔序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 挟在胁下 <span class="encs">[place in under axilla]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">回顾乳者剑汝而立于旁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《泷冈阡表》</span></p>
