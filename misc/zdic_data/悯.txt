						<p>● <strong>悯</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">憫</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">mǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄣˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>哀怜：怜～。～恤。～惜。～恻。</li>
	<li>忧愁：～默。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>悯</strong></p>
<p>憫 <span class="dicpy">mǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从心，闵( <span class="dicpy">mǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:怜恤) 同本义 <span class="encs">[commiserate;take pity on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">隋</span>堤柳，悯亡国也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《新乐府序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人远悲天悯人之怀，岂为一己之不遇乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《朱人远墓志铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:悯人之凶(原谅、可怜凶恶之人。形容看透世情，慈悲为怀，无所计较);悯念(怜悯);悯宥(怜悯宽宥);悯笑(怜悯讪笑;可怜亦复可笑);悯贫(怜恤贫苦的人);悯然(哀怜的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>悯</strong></p>
<p>憫 <span class="dicpy">mǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 忧愁;烦闷 <span class="encs">[worried;sorrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阨穷而不悯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲罢悯然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:悯默(因忧伤而沉默);悯悯(忧伤的样子);悯愧(忧伤怀惭);悯叹(忧伤叹息);悯时病俗(忧虑时俗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 哀伤 <span class="encs">[sad]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:悯凶(为丧事而忧伤。专用以指父母丧亡);悯悼(哀伤悲悼);悯哀(哀伤)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
