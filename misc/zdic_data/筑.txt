						<p>● <strong>筑</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">①②③<span class="diczx3">築</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>捣土的杵：&ldquo;项王伐齐，身负板～，以为士卒先&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>建造，修盖：修～。建～。构～。</li>
	<li>居室：&ldquo;畏人成小～，褊性合幽栖&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古代弦乐器，形似琴，有十三弦。演奏时，左手按弦的一端，右手执竹尺击弦发音。</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">築</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨˊ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">筑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>筑</strong></p>
<p>築 <span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，筑声。本义:筑墙。古代用夹板夹住泥土，用木杆把土砸实)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 捣土使坚实 <span class="encs">[ram earth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">築，捣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不筑，必将有盗。(筑，这里指把坏墙修复。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说难》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:筑踏(捣土并以足踏之，使之坚实)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 修建;建造(仅指建筑物从无到有的建造)<span class="encs">[build]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曰止曰时，筑垒于兹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;绵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>人将筑薛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甸人筑坎坎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;既夕礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筑场圃，十月纳禾稼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北筑长城。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">更筑坚垒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筑石寨土堡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
