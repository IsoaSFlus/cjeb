                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>圮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧˇ</span></p><ol><li>塌坏，倒塌。</li><li>破裂，分裂：“是以悲痛，肝心～裂”。</li><li>毁灭，断绝：“分野殊异，遂用～绝，不图今日乃相得也”。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>圮</strong> <span class="dicpy">pǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，己声。本义:毁;塌坏;坍塌)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[destroy;collapse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">圮，毁也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祖乙圮于耿。——<span class="smcs">《书·序》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“河水所毁曰圮。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">覩有黎之圮坟。——<span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">方命毁族。——<span class="smcs">《虞书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:圮泐(石头毁坏开裂);圮剥(毁分;毁坏剥蚀);圮族(毁害种类);圮坏(毁坏);圮废(毁弃，荒废);圮坏(坍塌);圮倾(坍塌，倾颓);圮毁(坍塌毁坏);圮下(地面塌陷而形成的低洼);圮缺(倾毁，残缺)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 断绝 <span class="encs">[intercept]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故宗绪中圮。——<span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“绝也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:圮绝(断绝);圮隔(隔绝);圮纽(谓不能维系)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 倾覆 <span class="encs">[overturn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">由我则固，不由我则圮。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《梓人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 伤害 <span class="encs">[harm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">圮剥至亲。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
