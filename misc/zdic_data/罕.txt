                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>罕</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>稀少：稀～。～见。～俦（很少与之相比的）。～世之宝。人迹～至。</li>
	<li>古代称捕鸟用的长柄小网。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>罕</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。西汉字形，从&ldquo;罓/冈&rdquo;，&ldquo;干&rdquo;声。本义:捕鸟用的长柄小网)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bird net]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">䍑，网也。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罕车飞扬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《羽猎赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毕曰罕车，主弋猎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;天官书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罕车(载有猎狩等网具的车;星宿名，毕宿的别名。因毕八星排列如网，故名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 旌旗名 <span class="encs">[flag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云罕九游。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罕旗(古代帝王所用的旗帜。旗有九斿，或有九旒);罕毕(古代帝王仪仗之旗)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>罕</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>稀;少 <span class="encs">[rarely;seldom]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叔发罕忌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;大叔于田》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罕见曰闻名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;少仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盖以罕书也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;桓公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则功筑罕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;庄公二十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子罕言利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子罕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人之罕至。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与人罕言语。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罕物(希罕的、名贵的物品);罕事(少有的事);罕伦(很少能够与之相比);罕闻(很少听说);罕发(很少发生)</span></p>

	<li>

	<li>

	<li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7F55.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】呼旱切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】許旱切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音暵。【五經文字】䍐，經典相承。隷省作罕。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】希寡鮮，罕也。【註】罕亦希也。【詩&middot;鄭風】叔發罕忌。【禮&middot;少儀】罕見曰聞名。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】旌旗也。【史記&middot;周本紀】百夫荷罕旗，以先驅。【註】蔡邕獨斷曰：前驅有九流雲罕。東京賦曰：雲罕九旒。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;天官書】畢曰罕車，爲邊兵，主弋獵。【註】畢八星曰罕車。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】鳥網。【後漢&middot;馬融傳】罕罔合部。【註】罕亦罔也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】兔罟也。</p>
<p>又率也。</p>
<p>又地名。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】圍枹罕。【註】師古曰：枹罕，金城之縣也。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】鄭有罕氏，出自穆公，以王父字爲氏，代爲卿大夫。</p>
<p>又羌複姓有罕井氏。</p>
