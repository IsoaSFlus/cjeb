                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">物</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>物</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>人以外的具体的东西：事～。生～。～体。货～。礼～。文～。～价。～质。地大～博。～极必反。</li><li>内容，实质：言之有～。</li><li>指自己以外的人或跟自己相对的环境：～议（群众的批评）。待人接～。～望所归（众望所归）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> thing, substance, creature</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ding, Gegenstand  (Wirtsch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> objet,chose,un être,matière</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">物</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>物</strong> <span class="dicpy">wù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声，从牛，勿声。<span class="yhcs">“勿”</span>是一种杂色旗，表示杂色。本义:万物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[object]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物，万物也。牛为大物，天地之数起于牵牛，故从牛。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物以群分。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四时之散精为物。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·天文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物也者，大共名也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九曰物贡。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:物品(各种财货、物件);物母(万物的本源);物曲(物的性能);物如(事物的本源);物序(事物的规律);物祖(万物之祖);物际(事物的界限);物恺(愿各物安乐);物灵(万物之灵);物用(百物器用)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 物件，东西 <span class="encs">[article;thing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庞然大物。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">润物细无声。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春夜喜雨》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一物无所持。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夺其物。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:物外(有形事物之外);物变(事物变化);物隐(事物的隐秘);物端(东西，物品);物诱(为外物所引诱);物势(事物的趋势、态势);物惑(外物的诱惑);物则(事物的法则);物像(样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 事;事情 <span class="encs">[affair]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有物有则。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·烝民》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“事也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:物务(事务);物事(事情);物格(事理得到穷究)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 社会，外界环境 <span class="encs">[the outside world]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子生非异也。善假于物也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:物外人(尘世以外的人);物道(世道);物运(世运);物外交(超世脱俗的交谊)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 杂色牛 <span class="encs">[varicolored ox]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三十维物，尔牲则具。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·无羊》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“异毛色者三十也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 杂色的旗 <span class="encs">[motley flag]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[九旗]</span> 杂帛为物，以杂色缀其边为燕尾，将帅所建，象物杂色也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">司常掌九旗之物品，各有属以待国事:日月为常，旃龙为旂，通帛为旃，杂帛为物…。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 牲畜的种类、 品级 <span class="encs">[category]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辨六马之属，种马一物，戎马一物……驽马一物。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:物类(同类，物的同类;种类);物汇(物类)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 颜色 <span class="encs">[color]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">比物四骊。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·六月》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 又如:物采(色彩)</p><p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 哲学用语。物质，与<span class="yhcs">“心”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[substance;content]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自其不变者而观之，则物与我皆无尽也。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《赤壁赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(17)</span>
