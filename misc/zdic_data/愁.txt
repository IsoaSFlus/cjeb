						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>愁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chóu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chóu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄡˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 忧虑：忧～。～苦。～楚。～烦。～虑。～郁。～闷。～容。～绪。借酒浇～。多～善感。</p></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chóu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，秋声。字亦作<span class="yhcs">“愀”</span>。本义:忧虑，发愁)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[worry;be anxious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愁，忧也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>愀然作色而对。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·哀公问》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见善愀然。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·脩身》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哀而不愁，乐而不荒。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暝色入高楼，有人楼上愁。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《菩萨蛮》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">江晚正愁余，山深闻鹧胡。——</span><span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《菩萨蛮》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白发三千丈，缘愁似个长。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《秋浦歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫愁前路无知己，天下谁人不识君!——</span><span class="diczx2">高适</span><span class="smcs">《别董大》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:不愁吃，不愁穿;愁坐(含忧默坐);愁思(忧愁的心绪);愁绝(忧愁到极点);愁怀勃勃(心中有万端愁绪;非常忧戚);愁海(比喻愁深)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 使受苦 <span class="encs">[cause sb.suffering]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伤形费神，愁力劳意。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 怨尤;怨恨 <span class="encs">[have a grudge against sb.;hate]</span>。如:愁恨(忧怨)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>愁</strong> <span class="dicpy">chóu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" class="encs">[gloomy]</span>。如:愁红(憔悴的残花。用以比喻女子的愁容);愁戚戚(愁切切，愁凄凄。凄惨愁苦的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 悲哀;哀伤 <span
