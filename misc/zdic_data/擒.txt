<p><span class="dicpy">q&iacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄣˊ</span></p>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>擒</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，禽声。本义:捕捉。古籍本作&ldquo;禽&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[capture;seize]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射人先射马，擒贼先擒王。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《前出塞》</span>诗之六</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:擒捕(捕捉);擒捉(捕捉);擒讨(擒获处治)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 捉拿 <span class="encs">[catch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大敌之擒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子兵法&middot;谋攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">被擒不速死。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:擒捉(捉拿);擒生(活捉敌人);擒捽(捉拿，逮捕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拿 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肩上虽挑却柴担，手里兀自擒着书本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《喻世明言》</span></span></p>
