                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">圣</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>圣</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">聖</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>旧时称所谓人格最高尚的、智慧最高超的人：～人。～哲。</li>
	<li>最崇高的，对所崇拜的事物的尊称：神～。～洁。～地。～经。</li>
	<li>封建时代美化帝王的说法：～上。～旨。～明。</li>
	<li>称学问、技术有特高成就的：～手。棋～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>圣</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代方言，义同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/掘">掘</a>&rdquo;《説文&bull;土部》：&ldquo;圣，汝潁之閒謂致力於地曰圣。&rdquo;清施補華《别弟文》：&ldquo;吾負母而逃，圣野菜充飢。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> holy, sacred</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> sakral  (Adj)​,heilig, sankt  (Adv, Rel)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sacré,saint,sage,saint</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">圣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圣</strong></p>
<p>聖 <span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耳，呈声。甲骨文字形。左边是耳朵，右边是口字。即善用耳，又会用口。本义:通达事理)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[proficient]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣，通也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按:耳顺之谓圣。彼教所言耳根圆通亦此意。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听信之谓圣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;四时》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">睿作圣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span>。传:&ldquo;于事无不通谓之圣。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">母氏圣善。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;凯风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而圣君治国累世而不见者，其所谓忠者不忠，而所谓贤者不贤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圣功(神灵的功力);圣子(超凡入圣的儿子);圣意(神灵的旨意);圣明(英明)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 聪明;才智胜人 <span class="encs">[wise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人莫之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;巧言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是故圣愈圣，愚益愚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圣相(德智才能出众的宰相);圣童(智力特优的儿童);圣通(圣明通达);圣智(亦作&ldquo;圣知&rdquo;。聪明睿智，无所不通);圣善(聪明贤良);圣时(圣明之时);圣子(超凡入圣的儿子或登帝位的儿子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 神圣的。封建时代称颂帝王或与帝王有关的事物之词。亦为宗教圣徒对所崇拜事物的尊称 <span class="encs">[holy;sacred;imperial]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诚宜开张圣听，以光先帝遗德。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圣虑(帝王的思虑);圣裁(皇上的决定;皇帝的裁决);圣治(皇帝的治理);圣眷(皇帝的爱护);圣聪(帝王的听闻);圣朝无阙事(指当代朝廷没有过失);圣寿节(皇帝诞辰纪念日);圣眷隆重(皇帝的深切关怀);圣驾(皇帝或临朝皇后的车乘);圣鉴(指帝王或临朝太后的鉴察);圣子神孙(称皇帝的子孙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 刁钻;精灵、乖觉或敏锐、迅速之意 <span class="encs">[tricky]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:圣觉(犹灵感);圣得知(谓迅速灵敏地知道)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>圣</strong></p>
<p>聖 <span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 精通一事，对某门学问、技艺有特高成就的人 <span class="encs">[master]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:棋圣;诗圣</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 圣人。指儒家所称道德智能极高超的理想人物 <span class="encs">[sage;wise man]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或圣或否。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小宛》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人之所以为圣，愚人之所以为愚，其皆出于此乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积善成德，而神明自得，圣心备焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圣曹(神圣之辈);圣贤(指神佛);圣讳(圣人的名讳);圣经(圣人的经典);圣文(指圣人的文章典籍);圣心(圣人的心怀);圣臣(指德行智能超群出众之臣);圣法、圣制(古代圣人的法则);圣则(圣人所定的法则);圣轨(圣人的规范);圣神(泛称古代的圣人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指传说中的神仙等 <span class="encs">[saint]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:圣诞(泛指神、仙、佛、菩萨的生日);圣贤爷(天上的神灵);圣姑(传说中称得道成仙的女子);圣女(贤慧而有德行的女子;女神)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 神通 <span class="encs">[remarkable ability]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:斗圣;显圣</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 清酒的代称。亦泛指酒 <span class="encs">[clear wine]</span>古时称清酒为圣人，简称&ldquo;圣&rdquo;。如:圣贤</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古之王天下者。亦为对于帝王或太后的极称 <span class="encs">[tmperor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是故有天下七十一圣，其法皆不同。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圣父(对太上皇的尊称指上帝);圣主(对当代皇帝的尊称);圣帝(犹圣主、圣君);圣皇(对皇帝的尊称);圣国(称皇帝)</span></p>
