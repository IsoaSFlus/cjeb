						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>块</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">塊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄨㄞˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>成疙瘩或成团的东西：土～儿。</li>
	<li>量词，用于块状或某些片状的东西：一～地。两～糖。</li>
	<li>量词，用于银币或纸币，相当于&ldquo;元&rdquo;：一～钱。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> piece, lump; dollar</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ZEW für Rechteckige Dinge, Währungseinheit (Zähl)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> motte de terre,morceau,yuan (familier)​,(classificateur des briques, piastres, etc.)​,masse,pièce</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">块</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>块</strong></p>
<p>塊 <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，鬼声。字本作&ldquo;凷&rdquo;，是个会意字，表示土块装在筐器之中。后来写作&ldquo;塊&rdquo;，变成了形声字，现在简化为&ldquo;块&rdquo;。本义:土块)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lump of earth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凷，墣也。从土，一屈象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">张舜徽</span>注:&ldquo;凵象盛土之器，而土在其中也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凷，坚土也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span>引<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寝苫枕块。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;丧服传》</span>。释文:&ldquo;土也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大块噫气。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;齐物论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大块禀群生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选 &middot;张华诗》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓地也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">石块丛起则历。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:块阜(小土丘);块苏(土块和草堆。比喻卑微);块山(土墙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 成块的东西 <span class="encs">[piece;lump;chunk;block]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:把肉切成块;块子(成块状的形体);块聚(凝结成块)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 郁结的心思 <span class="encs">[pented-up]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:块磊(比喻郁结在胸中的愁闷或气愤)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>块</strong></p>
<p>塊 <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 整体的一部分 <span class="encs">[piece;lump;cube]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人运大石一块。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长衡</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一块布;一块石头;一块煤;一块方糖;一块肥皂;一块冰砖</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 中国基本货币单位 <span class="encs">[yuan]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:三块钱</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>块</strong></p>
<p>塊 <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>孤独;孑然 <span class="encs">[lonely]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">块然独立天地间。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;性恶》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5757.svg">						  <p>【篇海類編】音月。</p>
<p>（塊）【唐韻】苦對切【集韻】苦怪切【韻會】苦潰切【正韻】苦夬切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蒯。【說文】墣也。【博物志】徐州人謂塵土爲蓬塊。【左傳&middot;僖二十三年】晉公子重耳出亡，過衞，衞不爲禮，出于五鹿。乞食於野人，野人與之塊。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;喪服】凡喪居倚廬，寢苦枕塊。</p>
<p>又造物之名曰大塊。【莊子&middot;大宗師】大塊載我以形，勞我以生。【郭璞&middot;江賦】煥大塊之流形。</p>
<p>又壘塊，胷中不平也。【世說】晉阮籍胷中壘塊，故須酒澆之。</p>
<p>又銜塊，請罪也。【唐書&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>宗紀】天寶末，安祿山反，帝欲禪位太子。楊貴妃銜塊請死，帝意沮，乃止。</p>
<p>又孑也。一曰楚人謂我曰塊。【楚辭&middot;九辯】塊獨守此無澤兮，仰浮雲而永歎。【陸機&middot;文賦】塊孤立而特峙，非常音之所緯。</p>
<p>又【類篇】苦會切，音檜。義同。</p>
<p>又與蕢通。互見凷字註。</p>
