						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>夭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄠˉ</span></p><ol><li>草木茂盛美丽：～～（ａ．茂盛而美丽，如“桃之～～”；ｂ．颜色和悦的样子，如“～～如也”；ｃ．灾）。</li><li>未成年的人死去：～折。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> young, fresh-looking; die young</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> jung sterben; dicht, wuchernd  (V)</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>夭</strong> <span class="dicpy">ǎo</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 刚出生的兽或禽 <span class="encs">[young]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋覆巢，毋胎夭。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">畋不掩群，不取麛夭;不涸泽而渔，不焚林而猎。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴伯箫</span><span class="smcs">《猎户》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 初生的草木 <span class="encs">[young plant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泽不伐夭。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>夭</strong> <span class="dicpy">yāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，象头部屈曲的样子。本义:屈，摧折) 同本义 <span class="encs">[break]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夭，屈也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，从大而屈其首，指事，申者，腰之直，夭者头之曲。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">申申如也，夭夭如也。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋伐木，毋夭英，毋附竿。——<span class="smcs">《管子·禁藏》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:夭英(折断幼苗)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>夭</strong></p><p>殀 <span class="dicpy">yāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 短命，早死。未成年而死 <span class="encs">[die young]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">少壮而死曰夭。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不殀夭。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夭寿不贰，修身以俟之。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·尽心上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫寿于殇子，而</span><span class="diczx2">彭祖</span>为夭。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夭其稚枝。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夭梅病梅。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:夭札(遭疫病而早死);夭死(早死。又称作:夭枉;夭殁;夭命;夭殂;夭促;夭逝;夭绝;夭昏;夭谢;夭短);夭厉(因遭疾疫而早死)</p><p>◎ <strong>夭</strong> <span class="dicpy">yāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 茂盛 <span class="encs">[luxuriant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥草惟夭，厥木惟乔。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:夭秀(秀丽);夭桃秾李(茂盛艳丽的桃花、李花)</p><p><span
