						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>豁</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">huō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huō.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄛˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>残缺，裂开：～口。～子（残缺的口子）。</li>
	<li>摒弃；舍却：～出性命。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>

<p><span class="dicpy">hu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄛˋ</span></p>
<ol>

	<li>开通；大度：～达。</li>
	<li>显赫；通达：显～。</li>
	<li>排遣；消散：～情散哀。</li>
	<li>免除：～免。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">豁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>豁</strong> <span class="dicpy">huō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>空缺;露出缺口;缺损 <span class="encs">[crack]</span>。如:豁齿(牙齿残缺脱落);豁牙锯齿(器物边口残破缺损，形同锯齿);豁牙(器物边口的残破处)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>豁</strong> <span class="dicpy">huō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 裂开;割裂 <span class="encs">[slit;break;crack]</span>。如:墙上豁了一个口子;豁口截舌(撕开嘴巴，截去舌头。意为命人住嘴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 舍弃，狠心付出某种代价 <span class="encs">[give up;sacrifice]</span>。如:豁出三天时间，也要把它做好;豁出性命;豁脱(豁出;花费);豁撒(抛撒;挥霍);豁平(舍弃)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 〈方〉∶跨 <span class="encs">[stride]</span>。如:豁上马背(跨上马背;骑虎难下，不得不干到底)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈方〉∶指草和庄稼混长在一起，把土地糟蹋了 <span class="encs">[waste]</span>。如:地豁了(地被长在庄稼中的草糟蹋了)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hu&oacute;;hu&ograve;</span></p>
