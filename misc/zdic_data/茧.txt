                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茧</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>茧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">繭</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>某些昆虫的幼虫在变成蛹之前吐丝做成的壳：蚕～。～眉（形容女子秀美的眉毛）。</li>
	<li>手、脚因摩擦而生的硬皮：老～|脚底磨出了～子。同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/趼">趼</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cocoon; callus, blister</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Hühnerauge  (S)​,Hornhaut, Schwiele  (S)​,Insektenpuppe  (S)​,Kokon  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>茧</strong></p>
<p>繭、蠒、絸 <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 许多昆虫幼虫在化蛹前包围身体大部分的由丝组成的外皮，在其中化蛹。家蚕的茧是商业蚕丝的来源 <span class="encs">[cocoon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生自蚕茧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蚕茧;茧儿(喻指秘事;名堂;见不得人的事);茧素(白色的生绢);茧纸(用蚕茧制作的纸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 指茧丝 <span class="encs">[silk]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茧衣(蚕初作茧时在茧外所吐的散丝;以蚕丝织品制成的衣服);茧物(丝织物);茧蚕(吐丝做茧之蚕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 絮丝棉的衣服 <span class="encs">[silk dress]</span>。后作&ldquo;襺&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;趼&rdquo;。手掌或脚掌等部因摩擦而生成的硬皮 <span class="encs">[callosity;callus]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:老茧</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>茧</strong></p>
<p>繭 <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形状如茧的 <span class="encs">[resembling a cocoon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茧栗(形容牛角初生之状，形小如茧似栗);茧卜(古代民俗，于正月十五日夜，抟米、麦粉若茧状，书事置于其中，以占一年之事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 聲气低微貌 <span class="encs">[low]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茧茧(聲气低微的样子)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              茧·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8327.svg">						  <p>【集韻】同<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26B15.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦬕</span>。【玉篇】茧，草衰也。</p>
<p>（繭）〔古文〕絸【廣韻】古典切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】吉典切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音趼。【說文】蠶衣也。【禮&middot;祭義】世婦卒蠶，奉繭以示于君，遂獻繭于夫人。</p>
<p>又【釋名】煑繭曰幕。貧者著衣，可以幕絡絮也。或謂之牽離，煑熟爛，牽引使離散如綿然也。【禮&middot;玉藻】纊爲繭，縕爲袍。</p>
<p>又繭繭，聲氣微也。【禮&middot;玉藻】言容繭繭。【疏】猶綿綿，聲氣微細繭繭然。</p>
<p>又【戰國策】足重繭而不休息。【註】足傷皮皺，如蠶繭也。　【類篇】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/261C2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦇂</span>。俗作蠒，非是。</p>
<p>（繭）</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">繭</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“茧”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/繭">繭</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=糸">糸</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7E6D.svg">                          <p>蠶衣也。从糸从虫，黹省。絸，古文繭从糸見。古典切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7E6D_5023.svg" /><span class="swbf">(繭)</span><span class="ywyy">蠶衣也。</span><span class="swdz">衣者，依也。蠶所依曰蠶衣。蠶不自其衣，而以其衣衣天下，此聖人之所取法也。</span><span class="ywyy">从糸，从虫，从芇。</span><span class="swdz">芇聲。各本作黹省。黹不得爲繭。會意。韵會㒼省聲。㒼上从二十幷，亦非也。五經文字曰：从虫从芇，芇音綿。許書<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20065.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠁥</span>部有芇，字相當也。讀若宀。張參所據本是矣。今據正。虫者，蠶也。芇者，僅足蔽其身也。工殄反。十四部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7E6D_5024.svg" /><span class="swbf">(絸)</span><span class="ywyy">古文繭，从糸見。</span><span class="swdz">見聲也。</span></p>                          </div>
