                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戮</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>戮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>杀：～尸。杀～。</li>	<li>〔～力〕合力，并力，如&ldquo;～～同心&rdquo;。</li>	<li>羞辱，侮辱：～人（罪人）。～民（受刑罚的人）。～辱。～笑（耻笑）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> kill, massacre; oppress</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> löschen, töten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tuer,mettre à mort,massacrer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>戮</strong></p>
<p>剹 <span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:斩，杀)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(表示处以死刑，特指杀死人后陈尸示众) <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戮，杀也。字亦作剹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戮谓膊焚辜肆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;掌戮》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">防淫除邪，戮之以五刑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俘囚为盗耳，晓当尽戮之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:戮佞(铲除奸佞);戮身(杀身);戮挫(诛杀折辱);戮杀(杀戮);戮害(杀害);戮罪(杀戮有罪的人);戮死(受戮而死)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 陈尸示众 <span class="encs">[display killed body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三奸同罪，请杀其生者，而戮其死者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;陈尸为戮。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:戮尸(陈尸示众);戮尸枭首(斩杀死者的尸体，并砍下他的头颅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 惩罚 <span class="encs">[punish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王使执而戮之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:戮人(戮民。受过刑罚的罪人);戮余(刑戮之余。指受刑后的余生);戮死(受戮而死);戮辱(受刑被辱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;僇&rdquo;。羞辱 <span class="encs">[humiliate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不用命戮于社。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;甘誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">贾季</span>戮 <span class="diczx2">臾骈</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公六年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:戮笑(羞辱讥笑)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>戮</strong></p>
<p>勠 <span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:并力，合力)同本义 <span class="encs">[join]</span></p>
