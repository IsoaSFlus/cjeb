						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>嫉</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>因别人比自己好而怨恨：～妒。～恨。～羡。</li>
	<li>憎恨：～恶如仇。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">嫉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>嫉</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，疾声。本义:忌妒才德地位等美好的人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。泛指忌妒 <span class="encs">[envy;be jealous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嫉，妒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">各兴心而嫉妒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世溷浊而嫉贤兮，好蔽美而称恶。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众女嫉予之娥眉兮，谣诼谓余以善淫。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嫉诬(因嫉妒而诬告);嫉病(忌妒指责);嫉心(嫉妒之心);嫉毁(嫉妒毁谤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 憎恨 <span class="encs">[hate;detest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">荀卿</span>嫉浊世之政。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;荀卿列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">屈平</span>既嫉之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嫉恶(憎恨邪恶的人。也作疾恶);嫉邪(憎恨邪恶);嫉俗(憎恨不良的社会习俗)</span></p>
