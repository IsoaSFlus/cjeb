                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>铸</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鑄</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>把金属熔化后倒在模子里制成器物：～铁。～造。～件。熔～。～币。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> melt, cast; coin, mint</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Zhu  (Eig, Fam)​,gießen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fondre (du métal)​,couler (du métal)​,fonderie</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铸</strong></p>
<p>鑄 <span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，寿声。甲骨文字形，会意。上面是双手拿&ldquo;鬲&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span>，古炊具)，下面是&ldquo;皿&rdquo;;鬲、皿表熔化金属的锅炉。中间象被熔铸的金属。小篆变为形声字。本义:铸造)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cast;found]</span>。锤炼，雕琢金属或以液态非金属材料(如塑料)浇制成器的统称</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铸以为金人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以精铜铸成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:铸印(铸造金属印章);铸冶(销冶金属铸造器物);铸兵(铸造兵器);铸作(铸造);铸金(熔铸金属成物);铸铜(炼铜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 造就<span class="encs">[人材]</span> <span class="encs">[train]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或曰:&ldquo;人可铸与?&rdquo;曰:&ldquo;</span><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>铸 <span class="diczx2">颜渊</span>矣。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法言&middot;学行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:铸人(造就人才);铸颜(比喻培养或造就人才)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 锤炼;雕琢<span class="encs">[词句]</span> <span class="encs">[refine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽取熔经意，亦自铸伟辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:铸词(锤炼文辞)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=金" target="_blank">金</a>】
                              鑄·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=22" target="_blank">22</a>　·部外筆画：14</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9444.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/243A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤎧</span>【唐韻】之戍切【集韻】朱戍切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音注。【說文】銷金成器也。【玉篇】鎔鑄也。【左傳&middot;宣三年】鑄鼎象物。【註】象所圖物，著之於鼎。</p>
<p>又【昭二十一年】天王將鑄無射。</p>
<p>又國名。【左傳&middot;襄二十三年】臧宣叔娶于鑄。【註】鑄國，濟北蛇丘縣所治。</p>
<p>又地名。【後漢&middot;郡國志】濟北國有鑄鄕城。</p>
<p>又與祝通。【禮&middot;樂記】封帝堯之後于祝。【註】祝，或爲鑄。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓苑】堯後。以國爲氏。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】照秀切，音呪。【淮南子&middot;俶眞訓】今夫冶工之鑄器。【註】鑄，讀作祝。</p>
