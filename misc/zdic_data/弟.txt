                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>弟</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>同父母（或只同父、只同母）的比自己年纪小的男子：～～。兄～。</li>
	<li>亲戚或亲族间辈分相同的比自己年纪小的男子：表～。堂～。</li>
	<li>称同辈比自己年纪小的男性：老～。师～。</li>
	<li>学生对老师自称或别人指称：～子。徒～。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">第</span>&rdquo;，次序。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">第</span>&rdquo;，但。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弟</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象有绳索围绕于&ldquo;弋&rdquo;，象竖立有杈的短木桩。绳索捆束木桩，就出现了一圈一圈的&ldquo;次第&rdquo;。本义:次第)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[order]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弟，韦束之次第也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乱必有弟。大乱五，小乱三。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;原乱》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 弟弟 <span class="encs">[younger brother]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男子先生为兄，后生为弟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释亲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如兄如弟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;谷风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">称小功以下为兄弟，大功以上为昆弟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;丧服》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">联兄弟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span>。注:&ldquo;兄弟婚姻嫁聚也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:弟老的(排行最小的);弟郎(兄弟);弟兄相狱(兄弟相互诉讼);弟昆(弟兄);弟息(弟弟与儿子);弟道(做弟弟应遵守的道德标准)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指亲戚或亲族中辈分相同的而年纪较小的男子 <span class="encs">[cousin]</span>。如:表弟;堂弟;弟长(年少者与年长者)</p>
