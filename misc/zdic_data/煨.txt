                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">煨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>煨</strong> <span class="dicpy">wēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，畏声。本义:火盆中的火) 同本义 <span class="encs">[fire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">煨，盆中火也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">热灰谓之煻煨。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一班大小徒弟，尽为煨尽之末。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">罗懋登</span><span class="smcs">《三宝太监西洋记通俗演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:煨尘(灰烬;尘埃);煨烬(灰烬，燃烧后的残余物)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>煨</strong> <span class="dicpy">wēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用文火烧熟或加热 <span class="encs">[stew]</span>。如:煨茶;煨牛肉;煨热(用微火加热)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 把生的食物放在火灰里慢慢烤熟 <span class="encs">[roast in fresh cinders]</span>。如:煨栗子;煨芋;煨炙(烧烤);煨罐(供炖、烧用的陶制圆形容器)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 烘干 <span class="encs">[heaten]</span>。如:煨干就湿，煨干避湿(极言抚育孩子的辛苦)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 焚烧 <span class="encs">[burn]</span>。如:煨炙(烧烤);煨燔(烧毁)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 中药炮制法之一。将药材用湿润面粉包裹，在炒热的滑石粉锅内加热至外皮焦黄色为度;或层层隔纸加热，以除去部分油分 <span class="encs">[roast in ashes]</span>。如:煨木香</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/煨烤">煨烤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/煨热">煨热</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>煨</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄨㄟ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>wēi</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">热灰、灰烬。</span><span class="gc_yy">《战国策·秦策一》：「犯白刃，蹈<b>煨</b>炭。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐书·卷八三·懿宗八女传》：「咸通十年薨。……又许百官祭以金贝、寓车、廞服，火之，民争取<b>煨</b>以汰宝。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">烹饪方法：➊ ​ 把生食埋在火灰中烧熟。如：「<b><b>煨</b></b>栗子」、「<b><b>煨</b></b>番薯」。宋·陆游〈初夏野兴〉诗三首之三：「𤎖火就林<b><b>煨</b></b>苦笋，密甖沉井渍青梅。」➋ ​ 用微火慢慢烧煮，使食物熟而软。如：「<b><b>煨</b></b>牛肉」、「<b><b>煨</b></b>蹄膀」。《宋史·卷三七三·洪皓传》：「尝大雪薪尽，以马矢然火<b><b>煨</b></b>面食之。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
