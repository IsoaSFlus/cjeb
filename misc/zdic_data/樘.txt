                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">樘</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>樘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄤˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>门框或窗框：门～。窗～。</li>
	<li>量词，指一套门（窗）框或门（窗）扇：一～玻璃门。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>樘</strong></p>
<p>chēng<span class="dicpy">  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄥ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用于人名。朱祐樘，明代孝宗。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>樘</strong> <span class="dicpy">chēng</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 支柱 <span class="encs">[pillar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">樘，柱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为抵拒，支持。如:樘柱(抵制)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>樘</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，堂声。本义:门或窗的框子) 同本义 <span class="encs">[door or window frame]</span>。如:门樘、窗樘。又作量词。门扇、窗扇、门框、窗框一副称一樘。如:一樘玻璃门</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">chēng</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6A18.svg">                          <p>衺柱也。从木堂聲。丑庚切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗别作橕，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6A18_2971.svg" /><span class="swbf">(樘)</span><span class="ywyy">柱也。</span><span class="swdz">各本柱上有衺字。今刪。文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨕖</span>靈光殿賦、長笛賦李注皆引說文。柱也。長門賦李注引字林。柱也。皆無衺字。惟樘字或作牚、或作撐。皆俗字耳。玉篇云。樘、柱也。亦無衺。葢樘本柱名。如靈光。枝樘杈枒而斜據。枝樘與層櫨曲枅芝栭爲儷。然則訓爲柱無疑也。樘可借爲䟫歫。猶柱可借爲支柱。而支柱遂正釋樘。俗閒謂撐拄必用衺木。遂沾一衺字矣。</span><span class="ywyy">從木。堂聲。</span><span class="swdz">丑庚切。李善引說文恥孟反。古音在十部。</span></p>                          </div>
