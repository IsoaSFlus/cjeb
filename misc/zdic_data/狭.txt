                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">狭</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>狭</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">狹</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄚˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>窄，不宽阔，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">广</span>&rdquo;相对：～窄。～长。～隘。偏～。～邪（指小街曲巷娼妓居住的地方。亦作&ldquo;狭斜&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> narrow, limited; narrow-minded</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> begrenzte, eng</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">狭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>狭</strong></p>
<p>狹、陿 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从犬，夹声。本义:窄，宽度、广度小)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[narrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阜隘狭而幽险兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;怨思》</span>。注:&ldquo;陋也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其生民也陿阨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span>。注:&ldquo;地小也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">险道狭路，可击。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吴子&middot;料敌》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">初极狭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修狭者为之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狭缝;狭路相逢;狭长(窄而长);狭迫(窄小局促)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 心胸狭窄 <span class="encs">[narrow-minded]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小人狭薄，尚为激然之气，战燎于胸中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈亚之</span><span class="smcs">《答冯兄书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾直性狭中，多所不堪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;嵇康&middot;与山巨源绝交书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">张铣</span>注:&ldquo;狭中，谓不能容人也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狭中(心胸狭窄);狭促(心胸、见识狭隘);狭浅(狭薄。狭隘浅薄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 急促 <span class="encs">[hurried]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广则容奸，狭则思欲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span>。注:&ldquo;声急也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 急迫 <span class="encs">[urgent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">&hellip;收租税时，裁阔狭，与民相假货。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 小 <span class="encs">[small]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣见其所持者狭，而所欲者奢，故笑之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狭巷(小巷);狭径(小路);狭斜子(居住陋巷无远识的人);狭庐(狭小简陋的房屋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 少 <span class="encs">[less]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田多可以足其人者为宽乡，少者为狭乡。狭乡授田，减宽乡之半。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书&middot;食货志一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多虚不如少实，广种不如狭收。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《农书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狭学(浅学寡闻之人);狭韵(韵书中字数少的韵部)</span></p>
