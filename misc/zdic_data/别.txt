						<p>● <strong>别</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bi&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>分离：～离。～情。～绪（离别时离别后的情感）。分～。告～。久～重逢。分门～类。</li>
	<li>差别：霄壤之～。</li>
	<li>分类：类～。性～。职～。级～。派～。</li>
	<li>另外的：～人。～号。～字。～墅。～论。～开生面。</li>
	<li>卡住，插住，绷住：～针。～花。</li>
	<li>不要，不准：～动。</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">彆</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bi&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄝˋ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">别</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>别</strong> <span class="dicpy">bi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从冎( <span class="dicpy">guǎ</span>)，从刀。&ldquo;冎&rdquo;，<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;剔人肉置其骨也。&rdquo;&ldquo;别&rdquo;的小篆形体，是一个表示用刀剔骨头的会意字。本义:分解)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cut apart;divide up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">别，分解也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">桀</span>之力，别觡伸钩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;主术训》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:别割(分割);别产(分割家产;分家);别国(从一国分化而成的国家;现常指别的国家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 分离;分开;分出 <span class="encs">[divide;separate;leave;part]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">岷山</span>导 <span class="diczx2">江</span>，东别为 <span class="diczx2">沱</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">久之，颇晓其别蓍布卦意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张禹传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有别券者几何家?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;问》</span>。注:&ldquo;别券，谓分契也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">别其官属常惠等，各置他所。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:别疏(分离疏远);别宥(除去蒙蔽。指排除主观成见);别脚(破绽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 区分;辨别 <span class="encs">[distinguish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知国之安危臧否，若别白黑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;君道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我又欲与若别之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;杨朱》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如丝体变为缕体，缕体即是丝体，有何别焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范缜</span><span class="smcs">《神灭论》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">生人作死别，恨恨那可论。</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 送别 <span class="encs">[see sb off]</span></p>
