                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>飘</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">飄</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">piāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧㄠˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 随风飞动：～扬。～摆。～散（<span class="dicpy">sàn </span>）。～洒。～逸（洒脱自然，与众不同）。～溢（飘荡洋溢）。～拂。～忽（ａ．风云等轻快地移动；ｂ．摇摆，浮动）。～荡。～泊（同“漂泊”）。～渺（同“漂渺”）。～摇。～零（ａ．飘荡；ｂ．漂泊，流落无依）。～～然。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">飘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>飘</strong></p><p>飄、飃 <span class="dicpy">piāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从风，票声。本义:旋风;暴风) 同本义 <span class="encs">[whirlwind;strong wind; storm wind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飘，回风也。…盘旋而起。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">少阳所至为飘风。——<span class="smcs">《素问·六元正纪论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">回风为飘。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其为飘风。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·何人斯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飘至风起。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·蒯通传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飘风不终朝。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“飘风，疾风也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:飘风(旋风);飘骤(疾风骤雨。比喻事物突然骤起);飘风骤雨(突然而来的旋风暴雨。比喻不能长久)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>飘</strong></p><p>飄 <span class="dicpy">piāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 飘扬，飞扬 <span class="encs">[fly in the air;flare]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飘如游云，矫若惊龙。——<span class="smcs">《世说新 语·容止》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山河破碎风飘絮。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《过零丁洋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:飘姚(飘动的样子。即飘摇);飘瓦(飞起的瓦片);飘袖(衣袖随风飘扬。形容雪花飞舞的样子);飘旋(飞舞);飘逝(飞逝);飘翔(飞翔，高飞);飘登(飞升);飘絮(随风飞舞的柳絮);飘瞥(雪花飞舞的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 落 <span class="encs">[fall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一春梦雨常飘瓦，尽日灵风不满旗。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《重过圣女祠》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:飘瓦(自屋顶落下的瓦片。比喻意外的灾祸);飘坠(飘零，落下);飘堕(飘失，坠落);飘殚(凋落);飘淋(流泻)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 流离，浮荡 <span class="encs">[drift about]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飘零何所似，天地一沙鸥。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《旅夜书怀》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:飘兀(随波飘离);飘泊(随水飘流。比喻流离失所);飘流(比喻四处流浪);飘洒(流荡;浪荡);飘零书剑(形容古代文人游学四方，到处漂泊);飘寄(飘流寄寓。即飘泊);飘寓(飘泊旅寄);飘萍(随风飘荡的浮萍。比喻飘泊不定)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 吹 <span class="encs">[blow]</span></p><p><span
