                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>躁</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">z&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>性急，不冷静：～动。～进。～狂（浮躁轻狂）。急～。浮～。骄～。烦～。性情～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> tense, excited, irritable</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> impatient,ardent</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">躁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>躁</strong> <span class="dicpy">z&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rash;impetuous;restless]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">趮，疾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作躁。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羽杀则趮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;矢人》</span>。按，今字作躁。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">躁者不静。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;心术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巽为躁卦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">震为决躁。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">处必掩身毋躁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span>。注:&ldquo;动也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狗赤股而躁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span>。注:&ldquo;举动急疾。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言未及之而言谓之躁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;不安静也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">重为轻根，静为躁君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;喻老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:躁暴(暴躁;急躁);躁妄(急躁轻率);躁忿(急躁易怒);躁扰(急躁好动);躁静(急躁与宁静);躁率(急躁轻率)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 急疾;迅速 <span class="encs">[very fast;rapid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脉三动而躁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;平人气象论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人迎躁盛，喘息气逆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:躁速(迅速);躁疾(迅速;急躁);躁盛(脉博的跳动疾速有力)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 浮躁;不专一 <span class="encs">[flighty and rash;irascible;irritable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">好变动民曰躁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;谥法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">离位之谓躁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;喻老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蟹六跪而二螯&hellip;用心躁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:躁易(浮躁，轻佻);躁戾(浮躁暴戾);躁气(浮躁的气质)</span></p>

<ol>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">性急、不冷靜。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「暴<b>躁</b>」、「煩<b>躁</b>」、「急<b>躁</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;季氏》：「言未及之而言，謂之<b>躁</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">狡猾。</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;繫辭下》：「<b>躁</b>人之辭多。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">動、擾動。</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子&middot;精神》：「七月而成，八月而動，九月而<b>躁</b>，十月而生。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國魏&middot;王基〈戒司馬景王書〉：「心靜則眾事不<b>躁</b>，思慮審定，則教令不煩。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
