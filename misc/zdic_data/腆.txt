						<p>● <strong>腆</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tiǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄧㄢˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>丰厚，美好。</li>	<li>胸部或腹部挺出：～着胸脯。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> prosperous; good; protruding</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> tugendhaft, züchtig</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bomber</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">腆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>腆</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉(月)，典声。本义:丰厚)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rich and generous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腆，设膳腆腆多也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腆，厚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自洗腆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;酒诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 注:&ldquo;善也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不腆敝邑，为从者之淹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:腆仪(厚礼);腆厚(丰厚);腆赠(厚赠);腆盛(丰盛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 平善;美好 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不腆先君之祧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹善也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腆，美也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡君有不腆之酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;燕礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">币必诚，辞无不腆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特性》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:腆养(善养)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 厚颜，厚着脸皮 <span class="encs">[shameless]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:腆冒(厚颜冒昧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 惭愧 <span class="encs">[be ashamed]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:腆然(羞涩);腆愧(羞愧);腆嘿(羞愧不语);腆汗(因羞惭而出汗);腆面(面有羞愧之色);腆瞢(惭愧的样子);腆颜(面带羞愧之色);腼腆事仇(指不知羞耻地侍奉敌人)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>腆</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>胸部或腹部向前挺出 <span class="encs">[protrude]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屠户横披了衣服，腆着肚子去了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:腆胸;腆肚子</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
