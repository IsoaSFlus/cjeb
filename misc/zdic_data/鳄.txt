                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鳄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>鳄</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鰐</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">è  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/è.mp3"></a></span>  ㄜˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 爬行动物的一属，大的体长达三米到六米。身有灰褐色的硬皮，性凶恶。生活在热带、亚热带的河流池沼中，捕食鱼、蛙等，有的也吃人、畜。通称“鳄鱼”，如“～～眼泪”（喻坏人的假慈悲）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鳄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>鳄</strong></p><p>鰐、鱷 <span class="dicpy">è</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/è.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 鳄鱼 <span class="encs">[alligator;crocodile]</span>。热带和亚热带水域的几种大型厚皮长体的水栖爬行动物，组成鳄属和一或两个近缘属 ，包括某些贪婪的种类(如尼罗鳄或东亚和太平洋岛屿的巨型河口湾鳄。)这些种类都会毫不迟疑地袭击人类。如:鳄海(有鳄鱼出没的海滨)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 钝吻鳄的俗称 <span class="encs">[alligator]</span>。两种属于钝吻鳄属的爬行动物之一。如:扬子鳄;密河鳄(密西西比河鳄)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 宽吻鳄的俗称 <span class="encs">[caiman]</span>。如:眼镜鳄</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鳄鱼扳手">鳄鱼扳手</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鳄鱼夹">鳄鱼夹</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鳄鱼钳">鳄鱼钳</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鳄鱼眼泪">鳄鱼眼泪</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
