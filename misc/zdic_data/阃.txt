                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阃</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>阃</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">閫</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kǔn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǔn.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄨㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>门槛，门限：“送迎不越～”。</li><li>特指城郭的门槛：“～以内者寡人制之，～以外者将军制之”。～外。</li><li>统兵在外的将军：“即具以北虚实告东西二～”。～职。</li><li>内室，借指妇女：～闱。～奥。～德（借指妇德）。～范。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> threshold; women's quarters</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Türschwelle  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> seuil,porte de l'appartement des femmes</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>阃</strong></p><p>閫 <span class="dicpy">kǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，困声。本义:门槛)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[threshold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阃以内者寡人制之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·冯唐传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无阃外之非仪也。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语·本命》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 特指城门的门槛</p><p><span class="diczx1">送迎不越阃。——</span><span class="diczx2">李延寿</span><span class="smcs">《南史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:阃阈(门限;门槛);阃奥(门户。引申为要塞)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指郭门 <span class="encs">[gate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阃以内者，寡人制之，阃以外者，将军制之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·张释之冯唐列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:阃外(本指城郭之外，引申为担任要职的人);阃外之寄(战地指挥调度);阃外将军(在都城以外领兵作战的将军)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 借指领兵在外的将帅或外任的大臣 <span class="encs">[a commanding general or minister]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">即具以北虚实告东西二阃。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:阃望(将帅的威望);阃幄(将帅的帐幕);阃幕(将帅的府署);阃帅(地方上的军事长官)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 妇女居住的地方，闺门，指妇女的居处 <span class="encs">[women’s quarters]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">内言不出于阃。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:阃令(阃:闺门。妻子的命令);阃闱(妇女所居的内室。即壸闱)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 妻室;女人 <span class="encs">[wife;woman]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昔阃能臻是，今阃或过之。——<span class="smcs">《镜花缘》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:阃范(女子的风范;妇德);阃德(妇德。阃指妇人居住的内室，故称妇德为阃德);阃仪(妇女的容止);阃政(妻子主持的家政)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 姓</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95AB.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>苦本切，音悃。【廣韻】門限也。【集韻】門橛也。【史記·馮唐傳】閫以內者，寡人制之。【註】此郭門之閫也，門中橛曰閫。【儀禮·士冠禮·布席於門中闑西閾外註】閾，閫也。【易·師卦註】閫外之事，將軍所裁，臨事制宜，不必皆依君命。</p><p>又天閫。【前漢·揚雄傳】天閫決兮地垠開。【註】天閫，天門之閫也。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>苦悶切，音困。義同。　【集韻】本作梱。</p>
