                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蟹</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔螃～〕节肢动物，水陆两栖。全身有甲壳，足有五对，前双足成钳状，称“<span class="diczx3">螯</span>”，横着爬。腹部分节，俗称“<span class="diczx3">脐</span>”，雄性脐呈长尖形，雌性脐呈椭圆形。简称“蟹”，如“河～”，“海～”，“～黄”，“～青”。</p></ol>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/880F.svg">                          <p>有二敖八足，㫄行，非蛇鮮之穴無所庇。从虫解聲。䲒，蟹或从魚。胡買切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/880F_5964.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(蠏)</span><span class="ywyy">有二敖八足。</span><span class="swdz">敖俗作螯、作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29A8B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩪋</span>。廣韵曰。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29A8B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩪋</span>、蟹大腳也。螯、蟹屬。然則俗作螯尤誤也。敖、出游也。故其大腳曰敖。今本大戴禮作螯。非。</span><span class="ywyy">旁行。</span><span class="swdz">攷工記梓人仄行。卽旁行也。鄭亦云蟹屬。</span><span class="ywyy">非它鮮之穴無所庇。</span><span class="swdz">庇者、蔭也。鮮者、今之鱓字。鱓者、魚名。見魚部。魚之似蛇者也。常演切。又作䱇。今大戴禮作䱇。或誤䱉。荀子作蟺。許書古本多作鮮。葢漢人多叚貉國鮮魚之字爲之。本無正字也。玄應曰。鱓又作鱔鮮二形、同。勸學篇曰。蟹二螯八足。非虵䱇之穴而無所寄者。用心躁也。</span><span class="ywyy">从虫。解聲。</span><span class="swdz">胡買切。十六部。廣韵曰。蟹、說文作蠏。知古如此作。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/880F_5965.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(䲒)</span><span class="ywyy">蠏或从魚。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
