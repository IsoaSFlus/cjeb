                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>莘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄣ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>长（<span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng </span>）的样子：&ldquo;鱼在在藻，有～其尾&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>〔～～〕众多,如～～学子。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>莘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄣ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔细～〕即&ldquo;细辛&rdquo;，一种中药草。</li>
	<li>〔～庄〕地名，在中国上海市。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">莘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>莘</strong> <span class="dicpy">shēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 众多的样子 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莘莘征夫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;莘莘，众多。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莘莘将将。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span>。注:&ldquo;莘莘，多貌也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祸之长也兹莘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;徐无鬼》</span>。释文引</span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注:&ldquo;莘，多也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:莘莘</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 长 <span class="encs">[long]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有莘其尾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鱼藻》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传: 莘， 长貌。</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>莘</strong> <span class="dicpy">Shēn</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Shen state]</span>。亦称有辛、有莘、有侁。在今山东省曹县北。如:莘老(指商伊尹。尝耕于有莘之野，故称);莘野(指隐居之所)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/莘莘">莘莘</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>莘</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 植物名。如:细莘(草本植物，多年生)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 地名。如:莘庄(在上海市)</p>

                              莘·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8398.svg">						  <p>【集韻】斯人切，音辛。細莘，藥草。【正字通】莘草，生山澤，如蒲黃，葉如芥。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】所臻切【正韻】疏臻切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音駪。【韻會】虢地名。【詩&middot;大雅】纘女維莘。【傳】太姒國也。【郡國志】郃陽南有古莘國，散宜生爲文王求有莘氏女以獻紂，卽此地也。</p>
<p>又【水經注】漯水又北絕莘道之西，有莘亭。</p>
<p>又莘莘，衆多也。【班固&middot;東都賦】俎豆莘莘。【王褒&middot;靑髥奴文】莘莘翼翼。</p>
<p>又長貌。【詩&middot;小雅】魚在在藻，有莘其尾。</p>
<p>又姓。【潛夫論】祝融之孫分爲八姓：己禿彭姜妘曹斯莘。【通志&middot;氏族略】宋朝有莘融。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26E2F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦸯</span>、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28415.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨐕</span>。</p>
