                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寸</strong> <span class="dicpy">cùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。小篆字形，从又，从一。<span class="yhcs">“又”</span>象手形，<span class="yhcs">“一”</span>指下手腕一寸之处。<span class="yhcs">“寸”</span>是汉字部首之一，从<span class="yhcs">“寸”</span>的字往往与<span class="yhcs">“手”</span>有关。本义:中医切脉，称距离手腕一寸长的部位为<span class="yhcs">“寸口”</span>，简称<span class="yhcs">“寸”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="encs">[中医]</span>∶寸口的简称 <span class="encs">[a person's pulse on the wrist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寸，十分也。人手却一寸，动脉谓之寸口。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，十发为程，而当一分，十分为寸。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">布指知寸。——<span class="smcs">《大载礼记·主言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肤寸而合。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·僖公三十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:寸田(三丹田);寸关尺(中医指寸口、关上、尺中的简称)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 长度单位， 1/10尺为一寸，古代计量长度单位的标准不同，寸的具体数值也有差异 <span class="encs">[cun， a Chinese unit of length]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一丝而累，以至于寸，累寸不已，遂成丈匹。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:寸男尺女(一男半女)。又指用于针灸取穴中的相对长度单位，如两乳头之间是8寸</p><p><span class="diczx1">脐下3寸是关元</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>寸</strong> <span class="dicpy">cùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引申为极短 <span class="encs">[very short]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君与康先生捧诏恸哭，而皇上手无寸柄，无所为计。——</span><span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:寸幅(短信);寸旬(极短的时间);寸铁(形容短小的兵器)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 形容极小的 <span class="encs">[very little]</span>。如:寸土必争;寸愿(微小的心愿);寸田(人胸中方寸之地。用以比喻心);寸进(形容进步极少);寸缕(极少的布制)</p><p>◎ <strong>寸</strong> <span class="dicpy">cùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>协助 <span
