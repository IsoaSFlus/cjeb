                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>久</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 灸灼。<span class="yhcs">“灸”</span>的古字 <span class="encs">[auterize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其腹有久故瘢二所。——<span class="smcs">《睡虎地秦墓竹简》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久，以后灸之，象人两胫后有距也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆木桁，久之。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·既夕礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“当为灸。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 支撑 <span class="encs">[support]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久诸墙以观其桡也。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·庐人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹柱也。今本以灸为之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 堵塞 <span class="encs">[stop up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幂用疏布久之。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 等待 <span class="encs">[await]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轩骄之兵，则恭敬而久之。——<span class="smcs">《银雀山汉墓竹简》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 滞留 <span class="encs">[be detained]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为久也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·庄公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡君以为盟主之故，是以久子。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杨伯峻</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“久子，久留子于 <span class="diczx2">晋</span>也。”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>久</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 长久，时间久(跟<span class="yhcs">“暂”</span>相对) <span class="encs">[for a long time; long]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久行怀思。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夜久语声绝。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久已病矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可久居。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:久羁(长期滞留);久惯牢成(老于世故);久淹(久居于一处)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“旧”</span>。从前的、先前的 <span class="encs">[old]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取完坚，补弊久，去苦恶。——<span class="smcs">《管子·度地》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时暗而久章者。——<span class="smcs">《答宾戏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“久，旧也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:久德(旧时的恩惠);久要(旧日的邀约);久坟(旧坟);久将(旧将;老将);久故(故旧，老朋友)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指经过的时间 <span class="encs">[of a certain duration of time]</span>。如:你来了多久了?久之(多时);久以后(将来，未来);久时(多时，长时间);久淫(久游)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/久别">久别</a></span><span                           『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4E45.svg">                          <p>以後灸之，象人兩脛後有距也。《周禮》曰：“久諸牆以觀其橈。”凡久之屬皆从久。舉友切文一</p>                          </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4E45_48.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(久)</span><span class="ywyy">從後灸之也。</span><span class="swdz">也字今補。久灸㬪韵。火部曰。灸、灼也。灼、灸也。灸有迫箸之義。故以灸訓久。士喪禮。鬲幎、用疏布久之。鄭曰。久讀爲灸。謂以葢塞鬲口也。旣夕。苞筲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26265.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉥</span>甒皆木桁久之。鄭曰。久讀爲灸。謂以葢案塞其口。此經二久字、本不必改讀。葢久本義訓從後歫之。引伸之則凡歫塞皆曰久。鄭以久多訓長久。故易爲灸以釋其義。考工記。灸諸牆以眡其橈之均。鄭曰。灸猶柱也。以柱㒳牆之閒。許所偁作久、與禮經用字正同。許葢因經義以推造字之意。因造字之意以推經義。無不合也。相歫則其<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202B1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠊱</span>必遟。故又引伸爲遟久。遟久之義行而本義廢矣。</span><span class="ywyy">象人㒳脛後有歫也。</span><span class="swdz">歫各本作距。今正。歫、止也。距、雞距也。舉友切。古音在一部。</span><span class="ywyy">周禮曰。久諸牆以觀其橈。凡久之屬皆从久。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
