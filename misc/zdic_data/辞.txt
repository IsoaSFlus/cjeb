                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>辞</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">辭</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">c&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>告别：告～。～诀。～行。～世。～别。</li>
	<li>不接受，请求离去：～职。～呈。</li>
	<li>躲避，推托：不～辛苦。～让。～谢。推～。</li>
	<li>解雇：～退。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/词">词</a>&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>优美的语言：～藻。修～。</li>
	<li>讲话；告诉：&ldquo;请～于军&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>文体的一种：～赋。陶渊明《归去来兮～》。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>辞</strong></p>
<p>辭、辤 <span class="dicpy">c&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。本义:诉讼，打官司)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[legal case;lawsuit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辞，讼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，分争辩讼谓之辞。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善于辞案条教。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;周纡传》</span>。注:&ldquo;辞案犹今案牍也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皇帝请问下民，鳏寡有辞于苗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 口供 <span class="encs">[oral confession]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明清于单辞，无或私家于狱之两辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无情者不得尽其辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其次不辱辞令。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任少卿书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辞布者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司市故书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:&ldquo;辞布，辞讼泉物者也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狱辞无谋故者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有诏即讯，辞服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵君韩张两王传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使犯死者自春而穷其辞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《断刑论下》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 言辞;文辞 <span class="encs">[word;diction;speech;statement]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安定辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。疏:&ldquo;言语也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不以文害辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:&ldquo;诗人所歌咏之辞。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辞也者，兼异实之名，以论一意也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正名》</span>。注:&ldquo;说事之言辞。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲以广主上之意，塞睚眦之辞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其次不辱辞令。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">撰长书以为贽，辞甚畅达。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其文约，其辞微，其志洁，其行廉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辞色(言辞和容色);辞容(表情;面色);辞旨(言语或文章的主旨、含意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 特指政令之词 <span class="encs">[order]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">近者奉辞伐罪，旄麾南指，</span><span class="diczx2">刘琮</span>束手。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span> <span class="diczx2">裴松之</span>注引</p>

<p>又別去也。【楚辭&middot;九歌】入不言兮出不辭。</p>
