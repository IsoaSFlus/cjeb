						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>隅</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˊ</span></p><ol><li>角落：城～。墙～。屋～。～隙（屋角的洞穴）。向～而泣。</li><li>〔～中〕将近中午的时候。</li><li>靠边的地方：海～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>隅</strong> <span class="dicpy">yú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">fù</span>)，禺( <span class="dicpy">yú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> )声。<span class="yhcs">“阜”</span>是土山，有土则可用于建筑，因而从<span class="yhcs">“阜”</span>的字有的与建筑有关。本义:山水弯曲边角处)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[corner of a mountain or river]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">隅，陬也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既登览乎隅椒，复临泛乎汪洋。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐渭</span><span class="smcs">《缇芝赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:山隅;隅椒(山角与山顶);隅隈(角落和弯曲之处);隅陬(角落)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 角，角落 <span class="encs">[corner]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俟我于城隅。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·静女》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宫隅之制七雉，城隅之制九雉。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·匠人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抠衣趋隅。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日出东南隅。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·陌上桑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">采桑城南隅。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">停于大室之隅。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">折过墙隅。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:隅头(墙角;拐角);城隅(城的一角);向隅(面对着屋子的一个角落)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 边远的地方 <span class="encs">[outlying place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咫只雪山路，归飞西海隅。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《送蔡希鲁都尉还陇右》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:隅谷(神话中日落的地方);隅镇(边远小镇);隅夷(神话中的日出处);隅辟(边境，国境);隅官(边地官员)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 事物的部分或片面 <span class="encs">[part]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举端自理，滞隅则失。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:隅见(片面的见解);隅曲(偏狭之见);隅积(部分和总体)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 边;旁 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏将举火，执烛隅坐，错总之法，横子坐所。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:隅坐(坐位的侧边);隅目(斜眼而视，怒视)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9685.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】遇俱切【集韻】【韻會】元俱切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音虞。【說文】陬也。【玉篇】角也。【書·益稷】帝光天之下，至于海隅蒼生。【詩·邶風】俟我於城隅。【禮·曲禮】摳衣趨隅。【註】趨隅，升席也。</p><p>又【檀弓】童子隅坐而執燭。【註】隅坐，不與成人並。【論語】舉一隅，不以三隅反。</p><p>又【玉篇】廉也。【詩·大雅】抑抑威儀，維德之隅。【禮·儒行】砥厲廉隅。</p><p>又海隅，十藪之一。【爾雅·釋地】齊有海隅。</p><p>又【周禮·冬官考工記·匠人】宮隅之制七雉，城隅之制九雉。【註】宮隅，城隅，謂角浮思也。</p><p>又山名。【魯語】汪芒氏之君也，守封隅之山者也。【註】封山，隅山，在今吳郡永安縣。【說文】作嵎。</p><p>又叶語口切，音偶。【詩·唐風】綢繆束芻，三星在隅。今夕何夕，見此邂逅。芻叶側九反，逅叶很口反。</p><p>又叶呼侯反，音齁。【揚雄·反離騷】有周氏之嬋嫣兮，或鼻祖於汾隅。靈宗初諜伯僑兮，流於末之陽侯。　【篇海】同<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EB7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨺷</span>。</p><p>
