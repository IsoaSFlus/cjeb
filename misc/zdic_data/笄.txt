                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">笄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>笄</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古代的一种簪子，用来插住挽起的头发，或插住帽子：发～。弁～。</li>	<li>古代特指女子十五岁可以盘发插笄的年龄，即成年：及～。～年。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hairpin; fifteen year old girl</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (arch.)​ épingle à cheveux,fille de 15 ans</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">笄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>笄</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声本义:古代盘头发或别住帽子用的簪子) 同本义 <span class="encs">[womans&#39; large-sized hairpin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笄，簪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皮弁笄。爵弁笄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中国冠笄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;齐俗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玉笄綦会此冤弁之笄也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">节纵笄总。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">栉设笄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">折委笄，此男子安发之笄也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">设笄珥，衣阿锡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;周穆王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笄珈(原指妇人首饰。亦代指妇女);笄珥(古代妇女常用以装饰发耳的饰件);笄导(即簪导。一种首饰。用以束发)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>笄</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指女子十五岁成年。亦特指成年之礼 <span class="encs">[year in which a girl came of age]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">字而笄之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;僖公九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既笄而孕&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;郑语》</span>。注:&ldquo;女十五而笄。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笄女(指成年的女儿);笄年(女子盘发插笄的年龄;成年);笄冠(女子加笄，男子加冠，指成年)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>笄</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jī <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3011.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
