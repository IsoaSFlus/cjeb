						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>毅</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 果决，志向坚定而不动摇：沉～。刚～。坚～。～力（坚强持久的意志）。～然。～勇。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>毅</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从殳，<span class="yhcs">“殳”</span>指兵器。本义:意志坚强、果断)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[firm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毅，有决也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毅，强而能断也。——<span class="smcs">《论语·泰伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刚、毅、木、讷近仁。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子路》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士不可以不弘毅，任重而道远。——<span class="smcs">《论语·泰伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">性严毅。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:毅武(刚强勇武);毅烈(刚毅节烈)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 坚决 <span class="encs">[resolute]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心毅，则憎心见于下。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·八说》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杀敌为果，致果为毅。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:毅色(严肃坚决的神态)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 残忍，残酷 <span class="encs">[ferous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">强忍犯义，毅也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语下》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/毅力">毅力</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/毅然">毅然</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/毅然决然">毅然决然</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/毅勇">毅勇</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下" target="_blank">辰集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下&amp;bs=殳" target="_blank">殳</a>】
                              毅·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6BC5.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】魚旣切【韻會】疑旣切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音劓。【說文】妄怒也。一曰有決也。从殳<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C4F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧱏</span>聲。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C4F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧱏</span>，豕怒毛豎也。【正譌】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C4F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧱏</span>从辛者，剛也。下从豕會意，故借爲剛毅字。【書·臯陶謨】擾而毅。【正義】《左傳·宣二年》殺敵爲果，致果爲毅。謂爲致果敢殺敵之心是爲强毅。和順者失於不斷，故順而能決，乃爲德也。【論語】剛毅木訥近仁。【又】曾子曰：士不可以不弘毅。【劉劭·人物志】溫直而擾毅，木之德也。剛塞而弘毅，金之德也。</p><p>又州名。【輿地廣記】武州，唐末置，領文德一縣，後唐長興元年改毅州。</p><p>又鳥名。鶡稱毅鳥，謂其性敢於鬭也，見【張華·禽經】。</p><p>又弈法，毅，提也。棋死而結局曰毅，旣毅而隨手曰復毅，俗又謂之提。見【徐鉉·圍棋義例】。　隸作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23ADA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣫚</span>。</p>
