						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>呜</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">嗚</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>〔～呼〕a.文言叹词；b.旧时祭文常用以表示叹息，后借指死亡，如“一命～～”。均亦作“乌呼”、“於呼”、“於戲”。</li><li>象声词：汽笛～～地响。她～～地哭。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>呜</strong></p><p>嗚 <span class="dicpy">wū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><叹></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 口中舒发而出的声音，叹息声。悲叹 <span class="encs">[alas]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及与公卿言国家事，未尝不噫呜流涕。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·袁安传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:呜呃(悲叹);呜乎(呜呼);呜唈(因悲哀、愤懑而抑郁气塞);呜恻(悲泣);呜噎(呜咽。忧伤悲泣);呜呼噫嘻(表示慨叹)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>呜</strong></p><p>嗚 <span class="dicpy">wū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><象></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形容气流刹地经过的声响 <span class="encs">[toot]</span>。如:汽车呜的一声开过去了;轮船上的汽笛鸣呜直叫;呜的一声，它不见了</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 发出喧闹声 <span class="encs">[hoot]</span>。如:呜哨(涨水声);呜哩呜喇(呜哩哇啦。多形容乱嚷嚷声);呜呜(歌咏声;吟咏声);呜轧(吹角声);呜呜咽咽(悲泣声)</p><p>◎ <strong>呜</strong></p><p>嗚 <span class="dicpy">wū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 亲吻 <span class="encs">[kiss]</span>。如:呜咂(亲吻)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 死亡 <span class="encs">[die]</span>。如:一命呜呼</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/呜呼">呜呼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/呜呼哀哉">呜呼哀哉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/呜呜声">呜呜声</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/呜咽">呜咽</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/55DA.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】哀都切【集韻】【韻會】汪胡切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音污。【玉篇】鳴呼，歎辭也。【書·五子之歌】鳴呼曷歸。【集韻】亦作歍。</p><p>又與烏通。【史記·李斯傳】歌呼鳴鳴，快耳目者，眞秦之聲也。亦作烏烏。</p><p>又【集韻】烏故切，音惡。歎傷也。【後漢·袁安傳】噫鳴流涕。【釋文】鳴，一故反。</p>
