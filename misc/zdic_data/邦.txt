                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>邦</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄤˉ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 国：友～。邻～。～交（国和国之间的正式外交关系）。～国（国家）。～人（本国的人）。</p>
<ol>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> nation, country, state</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Land, Staat  (Geo)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pays,État,nation</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">邦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>邦</strong> <span class="dicpy">bāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从邑，丰声。古文从之、从田，会意。与封字从之、从土同意。本义:古代诸侯的封国、国家)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邦，国也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;邦之言封也。古邦封通用。书序云:&lsquo;邦康叔，邦诸侯。&rsquo;论语云:&lsquo;在邦域之中&rsquo;。皆封字也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大宰之职，掌建邦之六典。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;大宰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以佐王治邦国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;大宰》</span>。注:&ldquo;大曰邦，小曰国，邦之所居亦曰国。&rdquo;按，散文邦国亦通。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利用为依迁邦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;益》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">协和万邦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以畜万邦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;节南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后非众罔与守邦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 汉避高祖讳，多以国易之</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且在邦域之中矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;季氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而谋动干戈于邦内。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民惟邦本，本固邦宁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;五子之歌》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及公子返</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>邦、瘵兵伐 <span class="diczx2">郑</span>，大破之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;喻老》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫子至于是邦也，必闻其政。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;学而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:邦畿(古代指直属于天子的地方);邦家(诸侯的封国和大夫之家);邦国(诸侯的封国。大的叫邦，小的叫国，后泛指国家)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指国家 <span class="encs">[country;nation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">姬汉旧邦，无取杂种。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑于寡妻，至于兄弟，以御(治)于家邦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唯求则非邦也与?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;先进》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:邻邦;盟邦;邦典(国家的法令制度)</p>
