						<p>● <strong>挣</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～扎〕尽力支撑或摆脱，如&ldquo;垂死～～&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>挣</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用力支撑或摆脱：～脱。～开。</li>
	<li>出力取得：～钱。～饭吃。</li>
</ol>

(V)​,sich einhandeln, verdienen, erweben  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> lutter,se débattre,se libérer de,se débarrasser de,s'arracher de,gagner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">挣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>挣</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 撞;击 <span class="encs">[strike]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摐金挣玉，吐宫咽征。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">前蜀</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贯休</span><span class="smcs">《长持经僧》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用力张开 <span class="encs">[open by force]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">你挣着口袋，我量与你么。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《陈州粜米》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/挣气">挣气</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/挣扎">挣扎</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>挣</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用力摆脱 <span class="encs">[struggle to get free]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那呆子左挣右挣，挣不得脱手。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郑屠</span>挣不起来，那把尖刀也丢在一边。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:挣开;挣不脱手</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用力支撑 <span class="encs">[support]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">林冲</span>挣的起来，被枷碍了，曲身不得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是有一个媳妇，赛过男子，尽挣得家住。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《初刻拍案惊奇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:挣持(勉强支持);挣扎(用力支撑或摆脱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用力 <span class="encs">[exert one&#39;s strength]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众匠人大半尚兀自在那里挣趱未办的工程。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:挣挫(勉力摆脱);挣脱(奋力摆脱);挣揣(用力获取);挣罗(用力追求);挣趱(勉力赶做)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用力获取;赚取 <span class="encs">[earn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔年先父原没甚所遗，多亏我弟兄，挣得些小产业，只望弟兄相守到老，传至子侄等辈分析。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醒世恒言》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">梳裹箱儿里取明镜，把脸儿挣得光莹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">金</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">董解元</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记诸宫调》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 发呆，发怔 <span class="encs">[stare blankly;be in a daze]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庙倒没去得成，倒把俺婆婆气了个挣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醒世姻缘传》</span></span></p>
