<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>薅</strong> <span class="dicpy">hāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 拔除田草 <span class="encs">[weed;pull up;pull out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:薅马(古代农具的名称。专用于除草);薅草(除草);薅锄(除草锄地);薅耨(除草)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指拔去 <span class="encs">[pull out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:薅胡子(拔胡须);薅菜苗儿;薅秧(拔秧苗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 〈方〉∶揪 <span class="encs">[hold tight]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:薅师傅的胡子;薅下几根头发;薅走了军帽;薅住领子</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
