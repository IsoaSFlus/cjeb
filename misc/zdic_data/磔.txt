                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>磔</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhé  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄜˊ</span></p><ol><li>古代分裂牲体以祭神。</li><li>古代一种酷刑，把肢体分裂：～刑。</li><li>汉字笔形之一，即“捺（<span class="dicpy">nà</span>）”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to dismember, tear apart; downward stroke slanting right</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Zerstückelung des Körpers (als Strafe in alten Zeiten)​  (S, Rechtsw)​,Zhe-Strich: nach rechts fallender Strich (in chinesischen Schriftzeichen)​  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Lingchi</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>磔</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代祭祀时分裂牲畜肢体 <span class="encs">[dismember]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">磔，辜也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。从桀，石声。刳鸡胸、腹而张之，令其干枯不收。故从桀</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">磔，张也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旁磔。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“攘也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祭风曰磔。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“以牲头蹄及皮破之以祭。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[季春之月]</span>九门磔攘。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孙希旦</span>集解:<span class="yhcs">“磔，磔裂牲体也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:磔禳(磔攘。分裂牲体祭神以除不祥);磔鸡(旧历正月初一杀鸡挂门外以除不祥)。又指古代的一种酷刑。以车分裂人体</p><p><span class="diczx2">吴子胥</span>不磔 <span class="diczx2">姑苏</span>东门外乎!——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“车裂也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:磔裂(车裂人体;分割，割裂)。还指五代时始置的一种凌迟酷刑，俗称<span class="yhcs">“剐刑”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">李嗣源</span>遣使部送 <span class="diczx2">潞州</span>叛将 <span class="diczx2">杨立</span>等到阙，并磔于市。——<span class="smcs">《旧五代史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:磔诛(凌迟处死);磔刑(割肉离骨，断肢体，再割断咽喉)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>磔</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>汉字书法之一，右下方的捺笔 <span class="encs">[right-falling stroke (in Chinese characters)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刮视字棱，相其波磔，似是八分书耳。——<span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>磔</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><象></p><p>鸟鸣声，同<span class="yhcs">“吱吱”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">磔磔云霄间。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/78D4.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>陟格切，音摘。張也，開也，裂也，剔也。【前漢·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】諸死<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>皆磔於市。景帝中二年，改磔曰棄市，勿復磔。【師古曰】謂張其尸也。</p><p>又裂牲亦謂之磔。【禮·月令】季春命國難，九門磔攘，以畢春氣。</p><p>又書法，右下爲磔。【崔瑗·永字八法歌】硺騰峻而速進，磔憶昔以遲移。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋天】祭風曰磔。【孫炎曰】旣祭，披磔其牲，以散風也。或省作矺。</p><p>又叶竹棘切，音陟。【蘇轍·除日詩】念同去鄕里，此節已三失。楚人重歲時，爆竹鳴磔磔。</p>
