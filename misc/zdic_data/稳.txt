						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>稳</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">穩</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wěn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěn.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄣˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 安定，固定：～步。～定。～固。～健。～重（<span class="dicpy">zhòng </span>）。安～。站～。～如泰山。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">稳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>稳</strong></p><p>穩 <span class="dicpy">wěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾，隐省声。<span class="yhcs">“禾”</span>与粮食有关，有了粮食，心就安定了。本义:安定，平稳)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[firm;stable;steady]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">地名</span><span class="diczx2">破冢</span>，真破冢而出，行人安稳，布帆无恙。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·排调》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:安稳(稳当;平稳);稳拍拍(稳稳当当);稳惬(稳定使满意);稳善(安稳美好);稳脚(犹稳步);稳疾(安稳而快速);稳平(平稳)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 妥帖;稳妥 <span class="encs">[appropriate;proper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">还须奏过了朝廷，才为稳便。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">罗懋登</span><span class="smcs">《三宝太监西洋记通俗演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:工稳(工整而妥贴);稳顺(妥帖和谐);稳约(稳妥而有准绳);稳善(妥善。作为隐语，指打劫);稳吃三注(指轻而易举地得到全部收获);稳协(工稳而协调)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 沉静;稳重 <span class="encs">[sedate;staid]</span>。如:稳口深藏舌(缄默不语);稳住架(方言。镇静下来，稳定情绪，沉住气);稳住神儿(沉着冷静，不慌乱);稳堰堰(不慌不忙);稳稳沉沉(稳定沉着的样子);稳稳静静(稳重安静);稳审(稳妥安祥)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 匀称，适度 <span class="encs">[well-proportioned;well-balanced]</span>。如:稳身(谓身材匀称，适度);稳称(匀称)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 一定;准定 <span class="encs">[sure;certain]</span>。如:稳受(准定受到);稳请(有把握的求取);稳笃笃(形容很有把握);稳打稳(形容很有把握)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>稳</strong></p><p>穩 <span class="dicpy">wěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蹂谷聚。即蹂践聚集的谷粒，使谷壳和米分开 <span class="encs">[trample on cereals]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稳，持谷聚。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span>。按:</span><span class="diczx2">周祖谟</span><span class="diczx2">校勘</span>记:<span class="yhcs">“持”</span>为<span class="yhcs">“治”</span>。 <span class="diczx2">民国</span><span class="smcs">《续修盐城县志》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“稻秕稃聚者谓之稳。<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span>‘稳’字训‘蹂谷聚’。今谓稻中秕稃扬之使聚者曰稳子，又曰偃子。”</span></p><p><span
