                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">植</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>植</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>栽种：种～。培～。栽～。～树。</li>	<li>树立：～立。～志（立志）。</li>	<li>戮住，竖起：～耳。</li>	<li>生物的一大类，谷类、花草、树木等的统称：～物。～被。～保。</li>	<li>古代军中监督工事的将官：&ldquo;华元为～，巡功。&rdquo;</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> plant, trees, plants; grow</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> pflanzen, anbauen, errichten, setzen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> planter,établir,former</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">植</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>植</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，直声。本义:关闭门户用的直木)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[door post]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">植，户植也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，古门外闭，中竖直木，以铁了鸟关之，可加锁者。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">季孙</span>与邑人争门关，决植。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;非儒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏屋宫驾，县联房植。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 木柱 <span class="encs">[post]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于四角立植而县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;匠人》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">城上百步一楼，楼四植，植皆为通舄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为支柱，柱本 <span class="encs">[pillar;mainstay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">井植生梓，而不容瓮，沟植生条，而不容舟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;览冥》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓材也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子谓坚能用法矣，法者国之植也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">冯梦龙</span><span class="smcs">《智囊补》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 特指筑城墙两端所竖的木柱</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大役，与虑事，属其植。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;夏官&middot;大司马》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又指悬挂蚕箔的柱子</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">槌，</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>、 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>、 <span class="diczx2">陈</span>、 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>、 <span class="diczx2">江</span>、 <span class="diczx2">淮</span>之间谓之植。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 植物，谷类、花草、树木等的统称 <span class="encs">[plant]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:植保(植物保护);植株(包括根、茎、叶等部分的植物体);动植物</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;志&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span>)。志向<span class="encs">[will]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上无固植。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;法法》</span>。注:&ldquo;植，志也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弱颜固植。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
