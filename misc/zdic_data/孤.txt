                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>孤</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>幼年死去父亲或父母双亡：～儿。遗～。托～。～寡鳏独（孤儿，寡妇，无妻或丧妻的人，年老无子女的人）。</li><li>单独：～单。～独。～立。～僻。～傲。～茕（单独无依然）。～介。～身。～危。～芳自赏。～苦伶仃。～掌难鸣。～云野鹤（喻闲逸逍遥的人）。</li><li>古代帝王的自称：～家。～王。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">辜</span>”，辜负。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> orphan, fatherless; solitary</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> einsam  (Adj)​,Gu  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> solitaire,isolé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">孤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>孤</strong> <span class="dicpy">gū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从子，瓜声。本义:幼年丧父，即孤儿)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[orphan]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孤，无父也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如孤子。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·深衣》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“三十以下无父称孤。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">司民协孤终。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">少伶俜而偏孤兮。——</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《寡妇赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">老而无子曰独，幼儿无父曰孤。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">救灾患，宥孤寡。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">备</span>答曰:<span class="yhcs">“ <span class="diczx2">刘荆州</span>临亡，讬我以遗孤，背信自济，吾所不为，死何面目见 <span class="diczx2">刘荆州</span>乎?—— <span class="diczx2">孔衍</span><span class="smcs">《汉魏春秋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:孤驹(无母的小马);孤遗(无父母的子女);孤雏(失去母鸟的幼鸟)。又如:孤疾(孤儿和残疾人);六尺之孤(没有父亲的未成年的孩子);孤终(孤儿和死亡者);孤嫠(孤儿寡妇);孤撮(孤儿)。特指为国事而牺牲者的后代。如:遗孤</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代王侯的自称 <span class="encs">[I]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君名孤寡。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·君守》</span>。注:“人君谦称。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡人有此一德者，足以南面称孤矣。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·盗跖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽贵必以贱为本，虽高必以下为基，是以侯王称孤寡不穀。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸人持议，甚失孤望。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:称孤道寡;孤家(古代君王、诸候的自称)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>孤</strong> <span class="dicpy">gū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 单独;孤独 <span class="encs">[lonely;solitary; isolated; alone]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孤，独也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君名孤寡。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·君守》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且</span><span class="diczx2">燕</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>处 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>革灭殆尽之际，可谓智力孤危。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大漠孤烟直，长河落日圆。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《使至塞上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以区区百人，投身大敌，与之扑斗，何异以孤羊投群狼?——<span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:孤冷(孤独冷清);孤孤恓恓(孤独恓惶);孤恓(孤独不安);孤桩桩(孤零零站着);孤贫(孤独穷困)</p><p><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5B64.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古乎切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】攻乎切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音姑。【說文】無父也。【釋名】孤，顧也，顧望無所瞻見也。【禮·曲禮】君子已孤不更名。</p><p>又窮民之一曰孤。【禮·月令】養幼少，存諸孤。</p><p>又王侯謙稱。【禮·玉藻】凡自稱小國之君曰孤。【曲禮】諸侯與民言，自稱曰寡人。其在凶服，曰適子孤。</p><p>又官名，三公之次也。【書·周官】立少師，少傅，少保，曰三孤，貳公弘化。【註】三孤，雖三公之副貳，非其官屬，故曰孤。</p><p>又凡孑處亦曰孤。【禮·學記】獨學而無友，則孤陋而寡聞。</p><p>又【玉篇】特也。【書·禹貢】嶧陽孤桐。【註】特生之桐也。</p><p>又【集韻】負也。【李陵·答蘇武書】陵雖孤恩，漢亦負德。【毛曰】凡孤負之孤，當作孤。俗作辜，非。</p><p>又獨孤，複姓。　【徐鉉曰】于文子瓜爲孤，瓜聲也。子不見父，則泣呱呱也。會意。</p>
