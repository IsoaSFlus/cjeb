<p>● <strong>作</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄛˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>起，兴起，现在起：振～。枪声大～。</li>
	<li>从事，做工：工～。～息。～业。</li>
	<li>举行，进行：～别（分别）。～乱。～案。～战。～报告。</li>
	<li>干出，做出，表现出，制造出：～恶（<span class="dicpy">&egrave;</span>）。～弊。～梗。～祟。～态。～色。～为。～难。～奸犯科（为非作歹，触犯法令）。</li>
	<li>当成，充当：～罢。～保。～伐（做媒人）。～壁上观（人家交战，自己站在营垒上看，喻坐观别人成败）。</li>
	<li>创造：创～。写～。～曲。～者。</li>
	<li>文艺方面的成品：～品。不朽之～。</li>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">作</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>作</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuō</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 作坊，手工业工场 <span class="encs">[workshop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">明帝</span>方修宫室而节气不合，&hellip;而 <span class="diczx2">礼</span>径至所作，不复重奏，称诏罢民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;魏志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:作房(作坊);作分(作坊);木工作;油漆作;洗衣作</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> (除作坊外，其余都读 <span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> )</p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/作坊">作坊</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>作</strong> <span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从乍。人突然站起为作。甲骨文字形，象衣领初作的形状。本义:人起身)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[get up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">作，起也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明两作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;离》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">作其即位。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;无逸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与子偕作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;无衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或作而行之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡作民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;士师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三献作止爵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;特牲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后圣有作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而后作焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舍瑟而作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;先进》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客作而辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;少仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如日出时，眠者皆作。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《画佛赞》</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>作</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄗㄨㄛˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>zu&ograve; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5391.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">興起、振起。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「興風作浪」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;人部》：「作，起也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;乾卦&middot;九五》：「雲從龍，風從虎，聖人作而萬物睹。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;莊公十年》：「一鼓作氣，再而衰，三而竭。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">造就、培育。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「天作之合」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;康誥》：「亦惟助王宅天命，作新民。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">創作。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「寫作」、「作畫」、「作詩」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;述而》：「述而不作，信而好古。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">進行、舉行。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「作戰」、「作簡報」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">為、當。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「認賊作父」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">假裝。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「裝腔作勢」、「裝模作樣」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">進行某事。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「作媒」、「作證」、「作事」。</span><span class="gc_yx">同「<a href='/hans/做' target=_blank>做</a> 」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">成為。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「作人」、「作官」。</span><span class="gc_yx">同「<a href='/hans/做' target=_blank>做</a> 」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">製造。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;秦韜玉〈貧女〉詩：「苦恨年年壓金線，為他人作嫁衣裳。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">事業。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「工作」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;緇衣》：「毋以小謀敗大作，毋以嬖御人疾莊后。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">詩文書畫與藝術品稱為「作」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「傑作」、「佳作」、「名作」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
<div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>作</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄗㄨㄛˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>zu&oacute; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5384.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item">
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">參見「作摩」、「作料」等條。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>

<p>又造也。【禮&middot;樂記】作者之謂聖。【詩&middot;鄘風】定之方中，作于楚宮。</p>

<p>又姓。漢涿郡太守作顯。</p>

<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>子賀切，音佐。【後漢&middot;廉范傳】廉叔度，來何暮，不禁火，民夜作，昔無襦，今五袴。【韓愈詩】非閣復非船，可居兼可過。君去問方橋，方橋如此作。今方音作讀佐。俗用做。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶總古切，音阻。【韓愈&middot;處州孔子廟<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥓓</span>】惟此廟學，鄴侯所作。厥初庳下，神不以宇。</p>
