                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">汇</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>汇</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">匯</span>、➋➌<span class="diczx3">彙</span>；<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">滙</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>河流会合在一起：～注。～合。</li>
	<li>综合，合并：～总。～编。</li>
	<li>聚合，以类相聚：字～。词～。～报。</li>
	<li>把款项寄到或划拨到别处：～寄。～款。～兑。～率（<span class="dicpy">lǜ</span>）。</li>
	<li>指外汇：出口创～。～市。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> concourse; flow together, gather</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Sammlung, Ansammlung  (S)​,anweisen, überweisen (z.B. Geld)​  (V)​,sammeln  (V)​,zusammenfließen, zusammenlaufen, zusammenströmen  (V)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">汇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>汇</strong></p>
<p>匯、滙 <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从匚( <span class="dicpy">fāng</span>)，筐子，淮声。本义:盛器)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[receiver]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汇，器也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;谓有器名汇也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用外币或本国货币兑换的票据 <span class="encs">[exchange]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:外汇;内汇</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 收集、分类和分送各种信息或其它需要广泛分送的情况或项目 <span class="encs">[things collected;assemblage]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:总汇</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> <span class="encs">[物]</span>∶大量吸收某种物质的器件或系统</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能汇</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中子汇</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>汇</strong></p>
<p>匯 <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 河流会合 <span class="encs">[conflow]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:汇涌(汇集涌流);汇粹(荟萃。会集，聚集);汇濊(水流深广的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 迂回，围绕 <span class="encs">[outflank]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东汇泽为彭蠡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。传:&ldquo;回也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汇行(绕道行走)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 以即期汇票、付账等付款形式送或寄 <span class="encs">[钱]</span> 给某人或某处 <span class="encs">[remit(money);make a remittance]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:给家里汇钱;汇款;汇票庄(即票号。私人经营的金融流通机构，略同于今日的私营银行);汇信(由银行汇款所附的信件)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>汇</strong></p>
<p>彙 <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从韋( <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span>)，长毛兽，胃省声。本义:刺猬)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hedgehog]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彙，彙虫也。似豪猪而小。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彙，毛刺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:&ldquo;今彙，状似鼠。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 类;族类 <span class="encs">[class]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:汇征(以类相从而行);汇类(分类再聚集);汇纂(类集编纂);汇进(连类同进)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>汇</strong></p>
<p>彙 <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
