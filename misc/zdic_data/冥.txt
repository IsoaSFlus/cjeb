                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">冥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>冥</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>昏暗：幽～。～蒙。晦～。</li>	<li>深奥，深沉：～思。～想。～心。</li>	<li>糊涂，愚昧：～顽不灵。</li>	<li>迷信的人称人死后进入的世界：～界。～府。～婚。～衣。～寿。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dark, gloomy, night; deep</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sombre,obscur,profond</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">冥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冥</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声字。小篆字形，从日，从六，冖( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)声。日，太阳，日数十，十六日而月始亏，幽暗也。本义:昏暗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dark;obscure;dim]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冥，幽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冥，暗也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冥者，明之藏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;玄文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维尘冥冥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;无将大车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">擿埴索涂，冥行而已矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法言&middot;修身》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冥火薄天，兵车雷运。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其庙独冥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五行志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥冥(昏暗);冥冥灭灭(昏暗不明的样子);冥蒙(晦暗不明);冥升(暗升;后指不断攀登);冥幻(暗淡虚幻)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 深远;幽深 <span class="encs">[profound and lasting;far-reaching]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临万丈之绝冥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙绰</span><span class="smcs">《游天台山赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;幽深也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">高低冥迷，不知西东。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥绝(杳远隔绝);冥寞(广漠无边);冥缅(幽远的样子);冥翳(高远);冥冥(幽深的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 愚昧 <span class="encs">[dull;muddle-headed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡人惷愚冥烦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;哀公问》</span>。注:&ldquo;言不能明理此事。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不然，则是鳄鱼冥顽不灵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《祭鳄鱼文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥昧(蒙昧);冥迷(迷糊);冥愚(愚昧);冥烦(寓顽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 精妙，深奥 <span class="encs">[wonderful;excellent]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冥言(奥妙的道理);冥迹(玄妙的道理);冥智(玄奥的智慧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 虚空渺茫 <span class="encs">[void;hollow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至道之精，窈冥冥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;在宥》</span>。注:&ldquo;皆了无也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥空(空虚渺茫);冥思(空想);冥浮(依稀渺茫的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 静默 <span class="encs">[quiet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冥然兀坐，万籁有声。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥心危坐(端正地坐着，静静地思考问题);冥寂(幽静);冥漠(静寂);冥默(玄默沉静);冥寞(玄默，冥寂);冥冥(沉默不语的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 潜心，专心 <span class="encs">[be absorbed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无冥冥之志，无昭昭之明。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span>。注:&ldquo;专默精诚之谓也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冥搜未已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥求(潜心探索);冥心(潜心苦思);冥志(静心);冥索(潜心探究)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冥</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 夜晚，黑夜 <span class="encs">[night]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冥火(夜火);冥行(夜间行路);冥色(夜色);冥夜(黑夜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 迷信人称人死后进入的世界，即&ldquo;地府&rdquo;、&ldquo;阴间&rdquo; <span class="encs">[the nether world]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人皆以为冥谪(谪:处罚)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥曹(阴曹地府。迷信者认为，在世间作恶的人，死后要在这里受审治罪);冥阴界(鬼魂活动的阴间世界);冥宅(为死者焚化的纸房子，传说死者能在阴间使用);冥事(阴间的事情)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 神灵，自然 <span class="encs">[Gods;nature]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冥财(给鬼神烧的钱钞等);冥工(神工，神灵的技巧);冥育(自然的化育)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;溟&rdquo;。海 <span class="encs">[ocean;sea]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北冥有鱼，其名为鲲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;消遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是鸟也，海运则将徒于南冥。南冥者，天池也。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>冥</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 泯灭 <span class="encs">[die out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冥一(浑沌成一体);冥心(泯灭俗念，清心静欲);冥昧(混沌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;瞑&rdquo;。闭上眼睛 <span class="encs">[close]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼至人者，归精神乎无始，而甘冥乎无何有之乡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥目(闭上眼睛;死去);冥坐(闭目而坐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 暗合，默契 <span class="encs">[tacit agreement]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">神与理冥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">高允</span><span class="smcs">《征士颂》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥合(暗合);冥契(默契，暗相投合;指意气相投的好友);冥符(默契，暗合)</span></p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/51A5_418.svg" /><span class="swbf">(冥)</span><span class="ywyy">窈也。</span><span class="swdz">窈各本作幽，唐玄應同。而李善思玄賦、歎逝賦、陶淵明赴假還江陵詩三注皆作窈。許書多宗爾雅、毛傳。釋言曰：冥，窈也。孫炎云：深闇之窈也。郭本作幼，釋云：幼稺者多冥昧，頗紆洄。小雅斯干傳曰：正，長也。冥，窈也。正謂宫室之寛長深窈處。王肅本作幼，其說以人之長幼對文，與下君子攸寧不相屬。然則三者互相證，知皆當作窈。穴部曰：窈，深遠也。窈與杳音義同，故杳之訓曰冥也，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26C64.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦱤</span>之訓曰日且冥也，昏之訓曰日冥也。鄭箋斯干曰：正，晝也。冥，夜也。引伸爲凡闇昧之偁。</span><span class="ywyy">从日六，从冖。</span><span class="swdz">从冖，各本作冖聲。下文曰冖亦聲，則此衹當云从冖矣。冖者，覆也。覆其上則窈冥。</span><span class="ywyy">日數十。十六日而月始<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/271FE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧇾</span>，冥也。</span><span class="swdz">冥各本作幽，今依玄應本。冥也二字當作冥之意也四字。此釋从日六之義也。日之數十，昭五年左傳文，謂甲至癸也，歷十日復加六日而月始<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/271FE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧇾</span>，是冥之意，故从日六。</span><span class="ywyy">冖亦聲。</span><span class="swdz">亦字舊奪，依小徐說補。冖今音莫狄切。鼎葢之鼏用爲聲。䖵部<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27585.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧖅</span>又用鼏爲聲。冥在十一部。莫經切。以雙聲爲聲也。</span><span class="ywyy">凡冥之屬皆从冥。</span></p>                          </div>
