                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>怒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>生气，气愤：～色。～视。～叱。～骂。恼～。息～。</li>
	<li>气势盛：～涛。～火。心花～放。</li>
	<li>中国少数民族，主要分布于云南省：～族。</li>
	<li>谴责：&ldquo;若不可教而后～之&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> anger, rage, passion; angry</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ungehalten, entrüstet, empört  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> colère,fureur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">怒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>怒</strong> <span class="dicpy">n&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，奴声。本义:发怒，明显地表形于外的生气)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[anger;fury;rage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怒，恚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怨而不怒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薄言往愬，逢彼之怒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;柏舟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驴不胜怒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吏呼一何怒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王怒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瞋目大怒。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怒烘烘(怒哄哄，怒叿叿，怒吽吽。怒气冲冲的样子);怒恶(嘖怒，发恨);怒心(愤怒之心);怒忿(气愤);怒恚(愤怒);怒猊(愤怒的狮子);怒恼(恼怒);怒愤(愤怒);怒噪(愤怒喧哗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 谴责 <span class="encs">[blame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若不可教，而后怒之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怒怨(责备);怒言(谴责之言);怒谴(责怪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 奋起;奋发 <span class="encs">[do all one can]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[鹏]</span>怒而飞，其翼若垂天之云。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怒行(奋斗疾行);怒马(奋马);怒臂(奋臂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 激怒 <span class="encs">[irritate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自勇其断，则毋以其敌怒之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫唯义可以怒士，士以义怒可与百战。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《权书上》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 超过 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑罚不怒罪，爵赏不踰德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;君子》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>怒</strong> <span class="dicpy">n&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 气势很盛、不可遏止 <span class="encs">[in profusion;burst]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秋高风怒号。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如怒涛排壑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事务 》</span>序</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span><span class="diczx1">飘风怒吹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6012.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22602.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢘂</span>【唐韻】乃故切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】奴故切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>奴去聲。【說文】恚也。【增韻】憤也。【揚子&middot;方言】楚謂怒曰憑。憑，忍盛貌。小怒曰<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A5EE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪗮</span>。言禁<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A5EE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪗮</span>也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>曰苛，言相苛責也。</p>
<p>又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C1A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩰚</span>怒，辨訟也。【周禮&middot;地官】凡有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C1A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩰚</span>怒者，成之。</p>
<p>又馬之肥壯，其氣憤盈曰怒。【後漢&middot;第五倫傳】鮮車怒馬。</p>
<p>又奮也。【莊子&middot;逍遙遊】怒而飛。言大鵬奮起如怒也。</p>
<p>又【外物篇】草木怒生。言乗陽氣奮出而不可遏也。</p>
<p>又威怒也。【禮&middot;曲禮】急繕其怒。【註】堅勁軍之威怒也。</p>
<p>又虎怒則威。【後漢&middot;賈彪傳】彪字偉節，兄弟三人，而彪最優。天下稱曰：賈氏三虎，偉節最怒。</p>
<p>又怒，東方氣也。【史記&middot;天官書】旬始狀如雄雞，其怒靑黑。【註】怒色靑也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】【廣韻】【正韻】奴古切【集韻】【韻會】暖五切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音弩。義同。</p>
<p>又叶乃都切，音奴。【楚辭&middot;九章】憍吾以其美好兮，覽余以其修姱。與余言而不信兮，蓋爲余而造怒。　【顏師古&middot;糾謬正俗曰】怒有二音，詩小雅君子如怒，大雅逢天僤怒，讀爲上聲。邶風逢彼之怒，小雅畏此譴怒，讀爲去聲。今山東河北人但知怒有去聲，不知有上聲，失其眞矣，蓋字有動靜音，人多不講，皆此類也，从心从弩省，怒若强弩之發，人怒則面目張起，凡怒當以心節之，故从心奴爲怒。</p>
