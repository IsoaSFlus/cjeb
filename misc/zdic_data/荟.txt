                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>荟</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">薈</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 草木繁盛，引申为会集：～郁。～蔚（❶草木繁盛的样子，如“林木～～，烟云掩映”；❷云雾弥漫的样子，如“～～云雾”）。～萃（聚集，如“群英～～”）。～集（聚集）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> luxuriant, flourishing, abundant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> blühen, aufblühen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> croissance luxuriante de plantes</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>荟</strong></p><p>薈 <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从芔，会声。本义:草多的样子) 同本义 <span class="encs">[luxuriant growth of grasses]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荟，草多貌。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荟兮蔚兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·曹风·候人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山林翳荟，必谨覆索之，此伏奸之所藏处也。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·行军》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">潜荟葱茏。——</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《江赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:荟蔚(草木茂盛的样子);荟郁(草木繁盛的样子);荟翳(指枝叶繁茂隐翳的草木)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>荟</strong></p><p>薈 <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 聚集 <span class="encs">[gather together]</span>。如:荟蕞(聚集琐碎的东西;聚集精华)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 遮掩;隐翳 <span class="encs">[cover]</span>。如:荟翳(草木丛聚隐翳)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/荟萃">荟萃</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/荟集">荟集</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
