                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骞</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>骞</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">騫</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〈书〉高举，飞起：～举。～腾。～翥。</li>
	<li>亏损：&ldquo;如南山之寿，不～不崩&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/搴">搴</a>&rdquo;，拔取。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/褰">褰</a>&rdquo;，揭起衣服。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> raise, hold high; fly, soar</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> défectueux,déficient,élever</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>骞</strong></p>
<p>騫 <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，寒省声。本义:马腹病，指腹部亏损低陷)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[disease of horse abdomen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骞，马腹垫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。谓马腹低陷不充垫者，下也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闵子骞名损，或曰即左襄廿三传之闵子马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;弟子传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骞，马腹垫也。臣</span><span class="diczx2">锴</span>曰:腹病骞损。<span class="smcs">《诗》</span>曰:&ldquo;不骞不亏&rdquo;。古人名损字骞。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">五代</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐锴</span><span class="smcs">《说文系传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 也指其他动物的肚腹低陷</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小体骞腹若是者，谓之羽属。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记&middot;梓人》</span>。按，鸟形如孵，胸满而复陷。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骞腹(马腹低陷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;蹇&rdquo;。跛足 <span class="encs">[lame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔羊来思，矜矜兢兢，不骞不崩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;无羊》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>骞</strong></p>
<p>騫 <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 惊惧 <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人灵骞都野，鳞翰耸渊丘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;颜延年诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 延误 <span class="encs">[delay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟昔李骞期。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘越石</span><span class="smcs">《扶风歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骞期(失期，约期而失信)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 损坏;亏损 <span class="encs">[ruin;be defective]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不骞不崩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;天保》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外无骞污之名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;晁错传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骞污(亏损污辱);骞崩(亏损坍圮);骞短(亏损缺少)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 同&ldquo;搴&rdquo;。拔取 <span class="encs">[pull up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非有斩将骞旗之实也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;杨仆传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 高举;飞起 <span class="encs">[lift up;fly up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骞，飞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王虺骞只。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;大招》</span>。注:&ldquo;骞，举头貌也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骞翥(展翅高飞的样子);骞举(飞扬的样子);骞骞(飞翔的样子;恣意放肆的样子);骞腾(飞腾)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>骞</strong></p>
<p>騫 <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;愆&rdquo;。过，误 <span class="encs">[fault;sin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长夜漫兮，永思骞兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正名》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9A2B.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】丘乾切【集韻】【韻會】丘虔切【正韻】苦堅切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音愆。【說文】馬腹縶也。【徐曰】馬腹病。</p>
<p>又虧也。【詩&middot;小雅】如南山之壽，不騫不崩。【前漢&middot;鼂錯傳】外無騫污之名。</p>
<p>又輕儇躁進貌。【柳宗元&middot;乞巧文】沓沓騫騫。</p>
<p>又姓。【風俗通】漢金城騫包。</p>
<p>又唐有侍中騫味道。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】怯建切，攐去聲。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】九件切，音蹇。駑也。○按鶱、騫二字音訓不同，以字書正之，鶱音掀，从鳥，註飛貌。騫音愆，从馬，註馬病。</p>
<p>又虧也。文人相承，以騫爲掀舉之義，押人先韻，非也。</p>
