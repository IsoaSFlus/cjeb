                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>魁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kuí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄟˊ</span></p><ol><li>为首的，居第一位的：～首。～星（ａ．北斗星中第一星，又第一星至第四星的总称；ｂ．中国神话中所说的主宰文章兴衰的神）。～元。罪～祸首。夺～。</li><li>高大：身～力壮。～梧。～伟。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">魁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>魁</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从斗，鬼声。<span class="yhcs">“斗”</span>，长柄酒勺。本义:汤勺，调羹。长柄大头)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ladle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">魁，羹抖也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">授以魁柄。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·梅福传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太子洗马</span><span class="diczx2">荀子骥</span>家中以龙铜魁作食欻鸣。—— <span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《易洞林》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古星名。北斗七星的第一至第四星，一说为北斗第一星 <span class="encs">[the four stars in the bowl of the Big Dipper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平旦建者魁。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·天文志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">那北斗正斜倚在紫微垣的西边上面，杓在上，魁在下。——<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:魁星(即<span class="yhcs">“奎宿”</span>，原为二十八宿之一。后被古人附会为主管文运之神);魁杓(指北斗星);魁冈(河魁星与天冈星的合称);魁罡(河魁星与天罡星的合称);魁冈(北斗星的斗魁、天冈二星);魁衡(北斗七星中的第一星和第五星)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 首领，领头人 <span class="encs">[chieftain;leader]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸公之间</span><span class="diczx2">陈遵</span>为雄，闾里之侠 <span class="diczx2">原涉</span>为魁。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·游侠传》</span>。又如:罪魁祸首;盗魁;魁柄(比喻朝廷的大权;借喻宰相之位);魁帅(领袖，首领);魁杰(首领人物)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 小丘 <span class="encs">[hill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>，高山，广川、大薮也，故能生是良材，而 <span class="diczx2">幽王</span>荡以为魁陵、粪土、沟渎。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:魁陵(小土丘);魁父(小丘)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>魁</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 居首位，第一 <span class="encs">[first]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不疾学而能为魁士，名人者，未之有也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:魁甲(古代科举考试，称进士第一名为魁甲);魁解(<span class="yhcs">“明”</span><span class="yhcs">“清”</span>乡试称解试，中式的举人第一名称魁解，也称解元);魁选(选中第一名。即首选);魁品(高档的品级)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 高大，壮伟 <span class="encs">[big and tall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">始以</span><span
