                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">镗</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>镗</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鏜</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄤ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>象声词。钟鼓声或锣声。《説文&bull;金部》：&ldquo;鏜，鐘鼓之聲。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>以铁贯物。《廣韻&bull;唐韻》：&ldquo;鏜，以鐵貫物。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>乐器名，即小镗锣。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>镗</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鏜</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄤˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>加工机械零件内孔的一种方法，工件固定在工作台上，刀具装在镗杆上伸入孔内旋转切削。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> boring tool</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bruit de gong, etc.</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">镗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>镗</strong></p>
<p>鏜 <span class="dicpy">tāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈象〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 钟鼓的声音或敲锣的声音 <span class="encs">[clang]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">镗，钟鼓之声。从金，堂声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">击鼓其镗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;击鼓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:镗鞳(钟鼓声);镗镗(钟鼓声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;ng</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/镗鞳">镗鞳</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>镗</strong></p>
<p>鏜 <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;ng</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>加工零件内孔的一种方法 <span class="encs">[boring]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:镗床;坐标镗床;镗刀</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>镗</strong></p>
<p>鏜 <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;ng</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用镗孔刀内旋切 <span class="encs">[bore]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:镗孔</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">tāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/93DC_7208.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鏜)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>部曰：鼞，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>聲也。引詩擊<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>其鼞，爲其字之从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>也。此引詩擊<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>其鏜，葢有韓、毛之異與。邶風傳曰：鏜，擊<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>聲也。許以其从金，故先之以鐘曰鐘<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>之聲。相如賦作闛<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29348.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩍈</span>。司馬法曰：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>聲不過閶。皆叚借字。</span><span class="ywyy">从金堂聲。</span><span class="swdz">土郞切。十部。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：擊<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔷</span>其鏜。</span></p>                          </div>
