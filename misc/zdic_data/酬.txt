                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>酬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>劝酒：～酢。</li>
	<li>用财物报答：～劳。～谢。～金。报～。</li>
	<li>交际往来：应（<span class="dicpy">y&igrave;ng </span>）～。～对。～和（<span class="dicpy">h&egrave;</span>）（用诗词应答）。～唱（用诗词互相赠答）。</li>
	<li>实现愿望：壮志未～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">酬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>酬</strong></p>
<p>詶、酧、醻 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉，夀声。本义:客人给主人祝酒后，主人再次给客人敬酒作答)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[exchange of toast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酬，主人进客也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。凡主人酌宾曰献，宾还酌主人曰醋，主人又自饮以酌宾曰酬。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酬献(相互劝酒);酬劝(劝酒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 酬答，报答 <span class="encs">[reciprocate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主人酬宾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;饮宾客而从之以财货，曰酬，所以申畅厚意也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交酬好货皆厚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span>。注:&ldquo;交酬相酬之币也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾无以酬之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不有死者，无以酬圣主。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天将以酬长厚者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酬知(酬报知己者);酬赏(报答他人而赏给财物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 应对;对答 <span class="encs">[answer;reply]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可与酬酢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹应对也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酬抗(酬对;对答);酬决(应对决断);酬奉(旧时奉召应对诗文)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 酬诗，以诗文互相赠答 <span class="encs">[present each other]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:酬寄(以诗文酬和寄赠);酬赓(指诗词应和)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 赏赐 <span class="encs">[grant a reward]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:酬偿(报偿;偿还);酬赎(以钱偿值赎回);酬庸(酬劳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 筹划 <span class="encs">[plan and prepare]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:酬想(想像，设想);酬款(筹划借款)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 报复 <span class="encs">[make reprisals;retaliate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:酬阻(因报复而加阻止)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 酹祭 <span class="encs">[offer a sacrifice to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:酬酒(以酒酹祭);酬神(祭谢神灵);酬地(以酒祭地)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>酬</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>报酬 <span class="encs">[reward;payment]</span>。</p>
