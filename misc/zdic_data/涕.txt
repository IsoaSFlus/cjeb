                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>涕</strong> <span class="dicpy">tì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，弟声。先秦时期，<span class="yhcs">“涕”</span>指眼泪，后来出现了<span class="yhcs">“泪”</span>，两字就同义并用了。本义:眼泪)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tears]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涕，泣也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“泣也二字当作目液也三字。转写之误也。 <span class="diczx2">毛</span>传皆云:“自目出曰涕。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悲愁垂涕。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涕流离而从横。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《长门赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涕零如雨。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小明》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长太息以掩涕兮，哀民生之多艰。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">临表涕零。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汪然出涕。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涕泪满衣裳。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《闻官军收河南河北》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">德威</span>流涕。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:涕泗(眼泪和鼻涕);痛哭流涕</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鼻涕 <span class="encs">[snivel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">目泪下落，鼻涕长一尺。——</span><span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《僮约》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">攀柏悲号，涕泪著树，树皆枯。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·王褒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:涕唾(鼻涕和唾液);涕泪(鼻涕和眼泪。亦专指眼泪)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 痰 <span class="encs">[phlegm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肺中寒，吐浊涕。——<span class="smcs">《金匮要略》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>涕</strong> <span class="dicpy">tì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 流泪;哭泣 <span class="encs">[shed tears]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涕，涕流貌。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不敢唾涕。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父母唾涕不见。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因笑</span><span class="diczx2">王</span>、 <span class="diczx2">谢</span>诸人，登高怀远，也学英雄涕。—— <span class="diczx2">陈高</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:涕欷(流泪叹息);涕淫淫(泪流不止的样子);涕洟(涕泪俱下;哭泣);涕血(泣血);涕泗滂沱(沲)(涕泪如雨);涕涟(泪流不断的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
