                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/710C.svg">                          <p>然火也。从火夋聲。《周禮》曰：“遂䶴其焌。”焌火在前，以焞焯龜。子寸切。又，倉聿切</p>                          </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/710C_3683.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(焌)</span><span class="ywyy">然火也。</span><span class="swdz">以火燒物曰然。</span><span class="ywyy">从火。夋聲。</span><span class="swdz">子寸切。又倉矞切。十三部。</span><span class="ywyy">周禮曰。遂䶴其焌。</span><span class="swdz">周禮垂氏曰。凡卜、以明火<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24454.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤑔</span>燋。遂龡其焌契。以授卜師。注云。焌讀如戈鐏之鐏。謂之契柱燋火而吹之也。士喪禮。楚？置於燋。在龜東。楚？卽契。所用灼龜也。燋謂炬。其存火。</span><span class="ywyy">焌火在歬。㠯<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24474.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤑴</span>焯龜。</span><span class="swdz">此許引周禮而釋其焌之義。似有舛誤。依鄭注則契卽楚？。楚？柱於炬然之。用以灼龜。焌者、謂吹而然之也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
