<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a sickle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Mähmesser, Sichel  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faucille</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>铚</strong></p><p>銍 <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一种小镰刀，古时用以割禾 <span class="encs">[small sickle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铚，获禾短镰也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一农之事，必有一耜、一铫、一镰、一鎒、一椎、一铚，然后成为农。——<span class="smcs">《管子·轻重乙》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 割下的禾穗 <span class="encs">[the mowed ear of grain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百里赋纳铚，二百里纳铚。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古县名 <span class="encs">[Zhi county]</span>。在今安徽省宿县西南</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>铚</strong></p><p>銍 <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 收获，刈 <span class="encs">[mow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">截颖谓之铚。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铚铚，断禾穗也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奄观铚艾。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·臣工》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:铚铚(割取稻穗的声音);铚艾(收割。引申为收获);铚权(用武力慑服与用计谋控制对手)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/928D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>陟栗切，音窒。【說文】穫禾短鐮也。【釋名】銍，穫黍鐵也。【詩·周頌】奄觀銍艾。【傳】銍，穫也。【疏】銍器可以穫禾，故云穫也。</p><p>又【小爾雅】禾穗謂之穎，截穎謂之銍。【書·禹貢】二百里納銍。【傳】銍刈，謂禾穗也。【疏】禾穗用銍以刈，故以銍表禾穗也。</p><p>又【廣韻】古縣名。在譙。【前漢·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>勝傳】攻銍酇苦柘譙，皆下之。【註】五縣名。銍音竹乙反。【地理志】沛郡銍縣。</p><p>又通作䬹。【史記·秦本紀】百里奚曰：臣嘗游困於齊，而乞食䬹人。【徐廣曰】䬹，一作銍。</p><p>又【廣韻】之日切【集韻】職日切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音質。義同。　【集韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28AD0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨫐</span>。</p><p>
