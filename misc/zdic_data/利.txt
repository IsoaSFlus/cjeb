						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>利</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>好处，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">害</span>&rdquo;&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">弊</span>&rdquo;相对：～弊。～害。～益。～令智昏。兴～除弊。</li>
	<li>使顺利、得到好处：～己。～用厚生（充分发挥物的作用，使民众生活优厚富裕起来）。</li>
	<li>与愿望相符合：吉～。顺～。</li>
	<li>刀口快，针尖锐，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">钝</span>&rdquo;相对：～刃。～刀。～剑。～落。～口巧辩。</li>
	<li>从事生产、交易、货款、储蓄所得超过本钱的收获：～息。～率（<span class="dicpy">lǜ</span>）。一本万～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">利</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>利</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从刀，从禾。表示以刀断禾的意思。本义:刀剑锋利，刀口快)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sharp;exert oneself;try hard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利铦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其利断金。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国之利器，不可以示人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木受绳则直，金就砺则利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵革非不坚利也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不以兵革之利。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则国之伤也若以利剑刺之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;解老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非兵不利。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求天下之利匕首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又泛指锐利</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾矛之利，于物无不陷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:利爪;利喙(尖利的嘴);利矢(锋利的箭);利口辩给(能言善辩，含贬义);利金(锋利的兵器)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 快;敏捷 <span class="encs">[quick;nimble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">假舆马者，非利足也，而致千里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子贡</span>利口巧辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;仲尼弟子传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:利马(快马);利嗖(轻快利落);利爽(灵活敏捷);利屣(舞屣，头小而尖的鞋子);利便(伶利)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 吉利;顺利 <span class="encs">[lucky;smoothly;without a hitch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时不利兮骓不逝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">探军不利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因利乘便。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:利钝(顺利与挫折);利市酒(为祈祷好运而喝的酒);利时(吉时)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 有利的 <span class="encs">[advantageous;beneficial;favorable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善战者因其势而利导之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 重要的;要害的 <span class="encs">[important]</span></p>
