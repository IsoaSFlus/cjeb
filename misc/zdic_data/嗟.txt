<p>● <strong>嗟</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄝˉ </span> 又 <span class="dicpy">yuē ㄐㄩㄝˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>文言叹词：～乎。～叹。～来之食。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sigh, alas</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Säuseln, Seufzen  (S)​,Seufzer  (S)​,Säuseln, Seufzen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> soupirer,soupirer,hélas!</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>嗟</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;叹&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，差声。本义:叹词。表示忧感)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 感叹声 <span class="encs">[alas]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗟叹使心伤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗟乎，师道之不传也久矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗟乎!&hellip;非此之由乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗟夫，谁知吾卒先汝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嗟乎(嗟呼。表示感叹);嗟讶(感叹，惊叹);嗟异(感叹称异);嗟悼(哀叹，悲悼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示呼唤 <span class="encs">[hello]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗟，我士，听无哗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;秦誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嗟来(来，语助);嗟来之食;嗟嗟(表示招呼)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>嗟</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 叹息 <span class="encs">[sigh;heave a sigh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忽魂悸以魄动，怳惊起而长嗟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嗟呀(叹息);嗟咨(慨叹);嗟怨(悲叹;怨恨);嗟伤(叹息伤感)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 赞叹 <span class="encs">[gasp in admiration，highly praise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见其所为文，嗟赏之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史&middot;王质传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嗟仰(赞叹景仰);嗟尚(赞叹嘉尚);嗟赞(赞叹);嗟异(赞叹称异)</span></p>

                              嗟·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/55DF.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28CA0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨲠</span>差【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>咨邪切，音罝。【玉篇】嗟歎也。【廣韻】咨也。【集韻】一曰痛惜也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】嗟，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28CA0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨲠</span>也。【釋名】嗟，佐也。言不足以盡意，故發此聲以自佐也。【書&middot;甘誓】王曰：嗟六事之人，予誓告汝。【詩&middot;周南】嗟我懷人。</p>
<p>又【齊風】猗嗟昌兮。【疏】猗是心內不平，嗟是口之喑咀，皆傷歎之聲。【禮&middot;檀弓】嗟來食。【廣韻】同<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27A01.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧨁</span>。【集韻】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27AB0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧪰</span>，亦書作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27A98.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧪘</span>。</p>
<p>又【集韻】遭哥切，音䦈。【易&middot;離卦】不鼓缶而歌，則大耋之嗟。【音義】王肅讀遭哥切【阮籍&middot;詠懷詩】李公悲東門，蘇子狹三河。求仁自得仁，豈復歎咨嗟。</p>
<p>又【韻會補】子夜切，音借。同唶。【三蒼詁】咄嗟，易度也。猶言呼吸之閒。字本作唶。古有咄唶歌。世說石崇作豆粥，咄嗟而辦。</p>
