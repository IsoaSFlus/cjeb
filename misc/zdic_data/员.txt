                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>员</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">員</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>人，工作或学习的人：～工。教～。演～。动～。复～。司令～。</li>	<li>指团体组织中的人：成～。团～。党～。会～。</li>	<li>周围：幅～辽阔。</li>	<li>量词，常用于武将：一～小将。</li></ol><p>● <strong>员</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">員</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&uacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄣˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>增加。</li>	<li>表示语气。</li>	<li>古人名用字。</li></ol><p>● <strong>员</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">員</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">员</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>员</strong></p>
<p>員 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，表示俯视鼎，看到鼎口是圆形的。小篆从贝，口声。本义:物的数量;人员的数额。亦指人员)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[specified member of personnel;person engaged in some field of activity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">员，物数也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。木曰枚，竹曰箇，丝曰总，貝曰员。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">员，众也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国人过员。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高惠高后功臣表》</span>。注:&ldquo;数也。&rdquo;</span></p>
