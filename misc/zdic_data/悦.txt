                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>悦</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄝˋ</span></p><ol><li>高兴，愉快：喜～。愉～。和颜～色。心～诚服。取～于人。</li><li>使愉快：～耳。赏心～目。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">悦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>悦</strong> <span class="dicpy">yuè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，说省声。本作<span class="yhcs">“说”</span>。本义:高兴，愉快) 同本义 <span class="encs">[delighted;happy;pleased]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悦，犹说也，拭也，解脱也。若人心有郁结能解释之也。——<span class="smcs">《说文系传统论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="yhcs">“说”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“悦”</span>古今字</p><p><span class="diczx1">客见太子有悦色也。——</span><span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怒可以复喜，愠可以复悦。——<span class="smcs">《孙子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">桓侯</span>又不悦。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">张飞</span>等不悦。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">权</span>大悦。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:悦心(愉悦心情);悦情(欢乐之情);悦笑(欢乐笑谑);悦喜(喜悦);悦畅(和畅，欢畅);悦乐(快乐);悦泽(光润悦目);心悦诚服</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>悦</strong> <span class="dicpy">yue</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 顺服，悦服，从心里佩服 <span class="encs">[heartily admire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悦，服也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释诂》</span>。义疏:<span class="yhcs">“</span><span class="diczx2">孟子</span>所谓“中心悦而诚服”</span>是也。”</p><p><span class="diczx1">理义之悦我心，犹刍豢之悦我口。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:悦仁服德</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 喜，喜欢 <span class="encs">[like]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士为知己者用，女为悦己者容。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任少卿书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悦亲戚之情话。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如;悦义(爱慕道义);悦慕(爱慕敬慕)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 乐于 <span class="encs">[be happy to]</span>。如:悦附(乐于归附);悦使(乐于效劳);悦随(乐于随从);悦谕(乐于接受);悦劝(乐地接受教化);悦赖(悦服信赖);悦伏(同悦服)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
