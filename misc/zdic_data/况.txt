                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>况</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄤˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>情形：情～。状～。近～。～味（境况和情味，如&ldquo;他的处境不好，～～难言&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>比方：比～。每～愈下。</li>	<li>文言连词，表示更进一层：～且。何～。</li>	<li>姓。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/贶">贶</a>&rdquo;，赐予。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> condition, situation; furthermore</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> überdies, ausserdem, zudem</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> situation,cas,de plus</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">况</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>况</strong></p>
<p>況 <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，兄声。本义:寒冷的水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cold water]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">況，寒水也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;未得其证。&hellip;古矧兄、比兄，皆用兄字;后乃用況字，后又改作况。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 情形，景况(侧重于整个事物的概况) <span class="encs">[condition]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乱况斯削。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;桑柔》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客有问</span><span class="diczx2">雁山</span>游况者，聊以塞责，不足观也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《雁山杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:况味(境况情味。有时专指境遇);闲况;状况;实况;战况;情况;盛况</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 兄 <span class="encs">[elder brother]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:况老(方言。称哥哥);况汉(方言。称哥哥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>况</strong> <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 比拟，比喻 <span class="encs">[compare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">案火灭不能复燃以况之，死人不能复为鬼，明矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:每下愈况</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 光顾;访问 <span class="encs">[visit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">足下不远千里，来况</span><span class="diczx2">齐国</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;贶&rdquo;。赐予 <span class="encs">[grant;bestow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">成(盛)名况乎诸侯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北面拜况。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦无况也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">况使臣以大礼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:况施</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>况</strong> <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">连</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示递进关系，相当于&ldquo;何况&rdquo;、&ldquo;况且&rdquo; <span class="encs">[besides;moreover]</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=冫" target="_blank">冫</a>】
                              况·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=7" target="_blank">7</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/51B5.svg">						  <p>【玉篇】俗況字。○按況本从水，亦从二作况。今从冫，當卽況、况二字之譌。况从二从兄。</p><p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>虛放切，音貺。發語辭。【詩&middot;小雅】况也永歎。从二从兄，與況字少異。</p><p>又借作祝。【漢&middot;魯相謁孔廟<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>】祝基作况其。况从二从兄，以况字代替。</p><p>（況）〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21DA2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡶢</span>【唐韻】許訪切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】許放切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音貺。【說文】寒水也。</p><p>又矧也，譬也。【莊子&middot;知北遊】每下愈況。</p><p>又益也。【晉語】衆況厚之。</p><p>又兹也。【詩&middot;小雅】況也永歎。【箋】來兹對之，長歎而已。</p><p>又滋也。【詩&middot;大雅】亂況斯削。</p><p>又賜也。與貺通。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】遭天地況施，著見景象。</p><p>又臨訪曰來況。【司馬相如&middot;子虛賦】足下不遠千里，來況齊國。</p><p>又【廣韻】修況，琴名。</p><p>又姓。【蜀志】有況長寧。◎按佩觿集，况况況<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>虛訪翻，况發語之端，况寒冰也，況形況，今多通用況字。</p><p>又【集韻】或作湟。</p><p>又古與兄通。</p>
