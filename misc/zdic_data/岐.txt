                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>岐</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>〔～山〕山名，在中国陕西省。</li><li>〔～黄之术〕指中医的医术。“岐”，岐伯；“<span class="diczx3">黄</span>”，黄帝，相传他们都是中华民族医家之祖。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">歧</span>”。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>岐</strong> <span class="dicpy">qí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从山，从枝省，支亦声。本义:古地名。陕西岐山县。又山名)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 岐山 <span class="encs">[Qishan mountains]</span>。因山有两枝，故名。岐山在今陕西省岐山县东北。上古称<span class="yhcs">“岐”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王用享于岐山。——<span class="smcs">《易·升》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:岐阳(岐山的南边;旧县名);岐周(西周。周初立国于岐山，所以称岐周)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“歧”</span>。物的分支或事有分歧 <span class="encs">[branch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">即歧趾而胪情。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:岐路(岔路);三岐路口;岐首(两个头);岐旁(岔路，两通的道路);岐路人(宋代称民间卖艺的人。也作岐路或路岐人)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>岐</strong> <span class="dicpy">qí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“崎”</span>。崎岖 <span class="encs">[be rugged and rough]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汝阴</span>太守 <span class="diczx2">曹武</span>，思所以获免，阴蒙避迴，岐岖自列。—— <span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《谢平原内史表》</span></p><p>◎ <strong>岐</strong> <span class="dicpy">qí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“企”</span>。踮起脚跟 <span class="encs">[stand on tiptoe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诞实匍匐，克岐克嶷。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·生民》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中" target="_blank">寅集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中&amp;bs=山" target="_blank">山</a>】
                              岐·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=7" target="_blank">7</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5C90.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2867E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙾</span>㟚【廣韻】巨支切【集韻】【韻會】翹移切【正韻】渠宜切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音跂。【說文】山名。后稷十三世孫古公亶父始居此。【詩·大雅】率西水滸，至于岐下。【一統志】山有兩岐，故名。【六書故】一在今鳳翔府岐山縣，禹貢導岍及岐，是也。</p><p>又山海經，岐山，狐岐之山，在今汾州介休縣，勝水出焉，東北會於汾，禹貢治梁及岐，是也。</p><p>又州名。本雍州地，後爲秦都，漢爲扶風郡，元魏置岐州，唐改鳳翔府，號西京。</p><p>又路岐也。【釋名】物兩爲岐，在邊爲旁。【爾雅·釋宮】二達謂之岐旁。【註】道旁出也。【列子·說符篇】亡羊者曰：岐之中又有岐，不知所之，所以返也。</p><p>又峻也。【詩·大雅】克岐克嶷。【朱傳】岐嶷，峻茂之貌。</p><p>又姓。【正字通】黃帝臣岐伯，唐岐靈岳。◎按《集韻》翹移切，音祁。周文王所封地。</p><p>又常支切，音坻。山名。</p><p>又渠羈切，音奇。地名。</p><p>又章移切，音支。分也。與諸家音切相齟齬。今不从。　【集韻】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。【韻會】或作歧。</p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷六">卷六</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷六&amp;swbs=邑">邑</a>】</div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/28678.svg">                          <p>周文王所封。在右扶風美陽中水鄉。从邑支聲。</p>
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/28678_2.svg" />岐，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>或从山支聲。因岐山以名之也。</p>
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/28678_3.svg" />㟚，古文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>从枝从山。巨支切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/28678_10097.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">周文王所封。在右扶風美陽中水鄉。</span><span class="swdz">經典有岐無<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。惟漢地理志曰。大王徙<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。文王作酆。匈奴傳曰。秦襄公伐戎至<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。師古曰。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>古岐字。岐專行而<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>廢矣。許所見豳岐作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。猶所見薊作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28712.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨜒</span>也。地理志曰。右扶風美陽、禹貢岐山在西北。中水鄉、周大王所邑。魯頌箋曰。大王自　豳徙居岐山之陽。按此云文王所封。要其終而言。皇矣詩曰。度其鮮原。居岐之陽。箋云。乃始謀居善原廣平之地。亦在岐山之南也。文王自<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>遷酆。非文王始國<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>也。下云酆、周文王所都。則此當云大王矣。戴先生曰。美陽今爲陜西鳳翔府岐山、扶風二縣。漢志。美陽。岐山在西北。今岐山縣東七十里岐山是。</span><span class="ywyy">从邑。支聲。</span><span class="swdz">巨支切。十五部。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/28678_10098.svg" /><span class="swbf">(岐)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>或从山。支聲。因岐山㠯名之也。</span><span class="swdz"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>或者、岐之或字。謂岐卽<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>邑之或體也。又云因岐山㠯名之。則又<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>邑岐山畫爲二字矣。考岐山見於夏書、雅、頌、漢志。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>邑因岐山以名。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>邑可作岐。岐山不可作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。薛綜注西京賦引說文。岐山在長安西美陽縣畍。山有兩岐。因以名焉。此說文山部原文也。山有兩岐、當作山有兩枝。山有兩枝、故名曰岐山。疑後人移入於此而刪改之。學者讀此可以刪邑部之岐專入山部矣。○按漢書地理志曰大王徙<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。曰襄公將兵救周有功。賜受<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>酆之地。郊祀志曰大王建國於<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>梁。匈奴傳曰秦襄公伐戎至<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。皆作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>周字也。而岐山字地理志皆作岐。是可證<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>岐之別。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/28678_10099.svg" /><span class="swbf">(㟚)</span><span class="ywyy">古文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>。从枝。从山。</span><span class="swdz">古文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28678.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙸</span>當作古文岐。此亦淺人改山部之文入此部耳。亦當刪此入彼。</span></p>                          </div>
