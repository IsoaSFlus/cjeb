                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>盼</strong> <span class="dicpy">pàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从目，分声。本义:眼睛黑白分明的样子) 同本义。喻美目流转 <span class="encs">[bright lucid(eyes)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盼，目黑白分也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">美目盼兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·硕人》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“盼，白黑分。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盼，美目也。——<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:盼刀(旧时相术用语。凶恶的目光);盼瞩(流转的目光)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>盼</strong> <span class="dicpy">pàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祇宜近盼天都，明日登莲顶。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:盼睇(顾盼);盼睐(欣裳。顾盼和青睐观看);盼恋(顾盼留恋)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 照顾;眷顾 <span class="encs">[look after]</span>。如:盼接(眷顾厚待);盼遇(眷顾，恩遇);盼饰(眷顾奖饰)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 盼望 <span class="encs">[expect;hope for]</span>。如:亟盼(急切希望);盼不的(迫切盼望);盼切(极言盼望殷切);盼眄(盼望);盼盼(急切盼望貌。又指唐代徐州名妓关盼盼)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 探望 <span class="encs">[visit]</span></p><p><span
