                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>际</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">際</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>交界或靠边的地方：无边无～。天～。春夏之～。</li>    <li>彼此之间：校～互助。人～关系。</li>    <li>时候：值此生死存亡之～。</li>    <li>当，适逢其时：～此盛会。</li>    <li>交接，接近：善于交～。</li>    <li>遭遇（多指好的）：遭～。～遇。</li>    <li>中间，里边：胸～。脑～。</li></ol>

<span class="encs">[joint of two walls]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">际，壁会也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:两墙相合之缝也。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 其时;适逢…的时候 <span class="encs">[on the occasion of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">际夜转西壑，隔山望南斗。——</span><span class="diczx2">綦母潜</span><span class="smcs">《春泛若耶溪》</span></p><p><span
