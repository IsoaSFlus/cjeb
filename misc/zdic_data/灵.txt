                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">灵</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>灵</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">靈</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>有效验：～验。～丹妙药。</li>
	<li>聪明，不呆滞：～巧。机～。～慧。</li>
	<li>敏捷的心理活动：～机。～感。～性。</li>
	<li>精神：～魂。心～。英～。</li>
	<li>旧时称神或关于神仙的：神～。精～。</li>
	<li>反映敏捷，活动迅速：～活。～犀。～便（<span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n </span>）。</li>
	<li>关于死人的：幽～。～魂。～柩。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> spirit, soul; spiritual world</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Geist; Seele; Intelligenz  (S)​,wirkungsvoll; effektiv  (Adj)​,gewandt; geschickt; flink</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rapide,alerte (adj.)​,efficace,esprit,âme du défunt,cercueil</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>灵</strong></p>
<p>靈 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从巫，靈( <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:巫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古时楚人称跳舞降神的巫为灵 <span class="encs">[dancing witch;sorceress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵，灵巫也。以玉事神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命灵氛为余占之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵连踡兮既留。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;云中君》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵偃蹇兮姣胶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《东皇太一》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灵子(指巫。以舞导引神的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 神灵 <span class="encs">[gods]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天神曰灵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尸子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵者，神也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《风俗通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阳之精气曰神，阴之精气曰灵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;曾子问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">合五岳与八灵兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;怨思》</span>。注:&ldquo;八方之神也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵之来兮如云。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《九歌&middot;湘夫人》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灵神(神灵);灵祗(神明;神灵);灵霄殿(天帝的神殿);灵怪(神怪);灵圣(灵验，指神鬼显灵);灵圣儿(指神佛塑像中放置的金属作的心脏象征物);灵河(书中虚拟的仙河);灵槎(神仙乘的木筏);灵山(佛家称灵鹫山为灵山。佛祖居处。也泛指仙山)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 灵魂 <span class="encs">[soul]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">经始灵台。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;灵台》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子太庙上可以望气，故谓之灵台。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;明堂位》</span></span><span class="diczx2">卢</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">藏之于灵室。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;气交变大论》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓灵兰室，</span><span class="diczx2">黄帝</span>之书府也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以告先帝之灵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾灵尚依依旁汝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝倘有灵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灵爽(灵魂);灵表(仪表);灵明(指心灵);灵知(指心灵);灵界(佛家语。指云魂所住的世界)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 敬辞。用以称有关鬼神的事物。对死者的敬称 <span class="encs">[sth. retating demons or gods]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在缸内画上灵符。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">钱彩</span>等<span class="smcs">《说岳全传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灵床子(人死后虚设的坐卧之具，供祭尊用;又指供在灵床上写有死者名字的牌位);灵幡(殡葬时所持之幡，上写有表明死者身分的文字);灵符(道士画的图形。迷信者认为可用于驱使鬼神、祭祷和治病等);灵前(灵位之前)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 灵柩的简称 <span class="encs">[coffin;bier]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孤魂翔故域，灵柩寄京师。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《赠白马王彪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:停灵;起灵;灵榇</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指人的精神状态 <span class="encs">[spirit;intelligence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可内于灵台。不可入于灵府。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;德充府》</span>。注:&ldquo;皆谓心也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:心灵(指内心、精神、思想等);灵明(指&ldquo;心&rdquo;，即主观精神)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;軨&rdquo;。窗中竖木 <span class="encs">[lattice work; window lattice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载葱(即窗)灵，寝于其中而逃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;定公九年》</span>。疏:&ldquo;</span><span class="diczx2">贾逵</span>曰:&lsquo;葱灵，衣车也。&rsquo;然则此车前后有蔽，两旁开葱，可以观望;忽中竖木，谓之灵。今人犹名葱木为灵子。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 指天、地、日、月等尊称及物品名 <span class="encs">[the Heaven;sun;the earth;moon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辰之神为灵星。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《风俗通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵星，火星也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灵景(日光);灵汉(银河);灵图(指天子的地位);灵辉(太阳的光辉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 威灵;福气，福分 <span class="encs">[happiness]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若以大夫之灵，得保首领以没。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受天之祐，享鬼神之灵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;董仲舒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 通&ldquo;苓&rdquo;。茯苓 <span class="encs">[tuckahoe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伏灵者，千岁松根也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;策列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 通&ldquo;舲&rdquo;。有窗的小船 <span class="encs">[a small house-boat with lookout windows]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">横大江兮扬灵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九歌&middot;湘君》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 通&ldquo;令&rdquo;。命令、法令 <span class="encs">[laws and decrees;decree]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或问循吏，曰:吏也。游侠，曰:窃国灵也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法言&middot;渊骞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 姓</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2919C_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2919C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩆜</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">巫也。</span><span class="swdz">各本巫上有靈字，乃複舉篆文之未刪者也。許君原書，篆文之下以<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/256EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥛬</span>複寫其字，後人刪之，時有未<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧗊</span>，此因巫下脱也字，以靈巫爲句，失之，今補也字。屈賦九歌：靈偃蹇兮皎服。又靈連蜷兮旣畱，又思靈保兮賢姱。王注皆云：靈，巫也，楚人名巫爲靈。許亦當云巫也無疑矣。引伸之義如謚法曰極知鬼事曰靈，好祭鬼神曰靈。曾子曰：陽之精氣曰神，陰之精氣曰靈。毛公曰：神之精明者稱靈。皆是也。</span><span class="ywyy">以玉事神。</span><span class="swdz">依韻會無也字。</span><span class="ywyy">从王。</span><span class="swdz">巫能以玉事神，故其字从玉。 <span class="ywyy">霝聲。</span><span class="swdz">郞丁切。十一部。</span></span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2919C_2.svg" /><span class="swbf">(靈)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2919C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩆜</span>或从巫。</span><span class="swdz">文一百二十四依鍇本。　重十七鍇十六，今增㻫字則十七。按自璙已下皆玉名也。瓚者，用玉之等級也。瑛，玉光也。璑已下五文记玉之惡與美也，璧至瑞皆言玉之成瑞器者也，璬珩玦珥至瓃皆以玉爲飾也，玼至瑕皆言玉色也，琢琱理三文言治玉也，珍玩二文言愛玉也。玲已下六文，玉聲也。瑀至玖，石之次玉者也。㺿至瑎，石之似玉者也。琨珉瑤，石之美者也。玓至<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24957.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤥗</span>皆珠類也。琀<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24A8E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤪎</span>二文，送死玉也。璗異類而同玉色者。靈謂能用玉之巫也。通乎説文之條理次第，斯可以治小學。 </span></p>                          </div>
