                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>涝</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">澇</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>雨水过多，被水淹，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">旱</span>&rdquo;相对：排～。抗旱防～。～灾。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> inundate, flood; torrent</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> überflutet, überschwemmte</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> inondation</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涝</strong></p>
<p>澇 <span class="dicpy">L&aacute;o</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，劳声。本义:水名。在陕西省。源出秦岭，北流入渭水) 同本义 <span class="encs">[Lao river]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又名潦水。在陕西省户县、周至两县界上，源出秦岭，为关中八川之一</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">涝水</span>北注 <span class="diczx2">甘水</span>，而乱流入于 <span class="diczx2">渭</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水经注》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 汾水支流。源出山西省浮山县西北，西流至临汾市北注入汾水</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涝</strong></p>
<p>澇 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 水淹没;雨多成灾 <span class="encs">[inundate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雨久成水，故其域恒涝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;袁甫传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涝漉(浸润其中);涝疏旱溉(指庄稼受涝应排水，受旱应灌溉);沥涝(沥水淹了庄稼);内涝(地势低洼，积水不能及时排水而造成的涝灾);涝洼地(低洼易淹的田地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 浇水，灌溉 <span class="encs">[water]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公今早晚班春去，强劝涝田补岁饥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《和钱学士喜雪》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>涝</strong></p>
<p>澇 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大水;淹没田地的积水 <span class="encs">[waterlogging;execessive rainwater in the fields]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:排涝(排除田地里过多的积水，使农作物免受涝灾);旱涝无常</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 奶酪 <span class="encs">[milk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">什么叫做涝?&hellip;原来是牛奶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">蘧园</span><span class="smcs">《负曝闲谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&aacute;o</span></p>
