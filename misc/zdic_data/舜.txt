						<p>● <strong>舜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>传说中的上古帝王：尧～（传说均是上古的贤明君主，后泛指圣人）。尧天～日（喻太平盛世）。</li>
	<li>&ldquo;木槿&rdquo;的别称：颜如～华（喻女子容貌美丽）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> legendary ruler</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Shun  (Eig, Fam)​,Shun, Fünfter der Fünf Kaiser (<a href="./五帝">五帝</a>, Wǔ dì - mythische Modell-Herrscher, die das Land vor Beginn der Dynastien beherrscht haben sollen.  (Eig, Pers, ca. 2233 - ca. 2184 v.Chr.)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sage,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">舜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>舜</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形。从舛( <span class="dicpy">chuǎn</span>)，也象蔓连向背之形，舛亦声。本义:一种蔓草)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a kind of grass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舜，舜草也。楚谓之葍，秦谓之蔓，蔓地连华，象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;蕣&rdquo;。木槿 <span class="encs">[shrubalthea]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有女同车，颜如舜华。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;有女同车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:舜华，舜英(木槿花);舜颜(美如木槿花的容貌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 中国传说中父系氏族社会后期部落联盟领袖 <span class="encs">[Shun， the name of a legendary monarch in ancient China]</span>。姚姓，有虞氏，名重华，史称虞舜。相传因四岳推举，尧命他摄政。他巡行四方，除去鲧、共工、饯兜和三苗等四人。尧去世后继位，又咨询四岳，挑选贤人治理民事，并选拔治水有功的禹为继承人</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春风杨柳万千条，六亿神州尽</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span> <span class="diczx2">尧</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">毛泽东</span><span class="smcs">《送瘟神》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:舜恩(像古代圣君虞舜那样的浩荡恩惠);舜韶(传说虞舜所作之乐);舜跖分路(善恶不同);舜巡(对皇帝巡行的美称。传说舜曾南巡至苍梧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
