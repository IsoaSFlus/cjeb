                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>梏</strong> <span class="dicpy">gù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，告声。本义:木手铐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wooden hand-shackles]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">梏，手械也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命有司省囹圄，去桎梏。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·仲春》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在足曰桎，在手曰梏。——<span class="smcs">《易·蒙》</span>疏</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">甫</span>愍然为之改容，乃得并解桎梏。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·范滂传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡囚者，上罪梏拲(两手关械)而桎，中罪桎梏，下罪梏。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·秋官·掌囚》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:梏桎(即桎梏。束缚手足的刑具);梏掠(用刑拷问);梏拲(古代刑具，也指械系)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 为了防牛触人而套在牛角上的横木。字本作告 <span class="encs">[joke]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">告，牛触人，角箸横木，所以告人也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>梏</strong> <span class="dicpy">gù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 戴上手铐。也泛指械系，拘系 <span class="encs">[fetter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执而梏之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主梏扑者亦然。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 监禁 <span class="encs">[imprison]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帝乃梏之</span><span class="diczx2">疏属</span>之山(疏属:山名)。——<span class="smcs">《山海经》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 约束，束缚 <span class="encs">[tie;bind;restrain]</span>。如:梏亡(因受束缚而丧失);梏拲(比喻束缚人的事物)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>梏</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄍㄨˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>gù <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2368.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一种古代的刑具。套在人犯的手上限制其行动。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「桎<b>梏</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·木部》：「<b>梏</b>，手械也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清·徐珂《清稗类钞·武略类·转庵和尚说韩大任就抚》：「置之狱，乃夜毁<b>梏</b>逾垣出，亡走滇南。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">套上、铐住、拘禁。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左传·襄公六年》：「子荡怒，以弓<b>梏</b>华弱于朝。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晋·杜预·注：「张弓以贯其颈，若械之在手，故曰<b>梏</b>。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
