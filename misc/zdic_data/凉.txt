<p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>温度低：～快。～爽。～意。～气。阴～。～丝丝。～亭。荒～。</li>
	<li>喻灰心，失望：听到这消息，我～了半截。</li>
	<li>中国西晋末年至北魏，各族统治者在西北地区建立的割据政权：五～（前、后、南、北、西）。</li>
</ol>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凉</strong></p>
<p>凉 <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，京声。本义:寒)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北风其凉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;北风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北风谓之凉风。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释天》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凉风至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凉雨时降。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;五常政大论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凉州，西方所在寒凉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释州国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水浆，醴凉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;浆人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 微寒，稍冷 <span class="encs">[cool;cold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日初出，沧沧凉凉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span>。注引<span class="smcs">《字林》</span>:&ldquo;凉，微寒。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曰燠，曰寒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span>。疏:&ldquo;燠是热之始，暑是热之极;凉是冷之始，寒是冷之极。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凉风起天末，君子意如何?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《天末怀李白》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冬暖夏凉;凉生生(微凉貌);凉宇(凉秋的天空);凉吹(凉风);凉榻(竹制矮床);凉轿(一种无帷轿);凉衣(凉衫;内衣);凉风(初秋凉爽的西南风。八风之一)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 薄;轻微;刻薄 <span class="encs">[thin;unkind]</span></p>
