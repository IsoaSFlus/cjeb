						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>翳</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用羽毛做的华盖。</li>
	<li>遮蔽，障蔽：荫～。～蔽。～障。～然。</li>
	<li>掩蔽物：&ldquo;兵不解～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>眼角膜上所生障碍视线的白斑：白～。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">殪</span>&rdquo;，树木枯死，倒伏于地。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">翳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>翳</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从羽，殹声。本义:用羽毛做的华盖，舞具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[feather cover]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翳，华盖也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>通训定声:&ldquo;以羽覆车盖，所谓羽葆幢也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 病证名 <span class="encs">[nebula]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指引起黑睛(角膜)混浊或溃陷的外障眼病以及病变愈后遗留于黑睛的疤痕</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 凡眼内、外障眼病所生遮蔽视线影响视力的症状皆可称翳</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 起障蔽作用的东西 <span class="encs">[screen]</span>。如:云翳;氛翳</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 鸟名 <span class="encs">[five-colours bird]</span>。如:翳鸟(五彩神鸟。相传飞行时其翼可蔽一乡)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>翳</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 遮蔽，掩盖 <span class="encs">[screen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翳，障也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百神翳其备降兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span>。注:&ldquo;翳，蔽也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">石嵯以翳日。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《九叹&middot;远逝》</span>。注:&rsquo;翳，蔽也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翳，掩也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言十三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一蝉方得美荫而忘其身，螳裔执翳而搏之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;山水》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:&ldquo;执翳，执草以自翳也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树林阴翳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阴翳蔽日。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:翳景(障蔽日月之光)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 隐藏，藏匿 <span class="encs">[conceal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">意者堂下其有翳憎臣者乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>翳</strong> <span class="dicpy">yi</span></p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>晦暗不明 <span class="encs">[dark]</span>。如:翳昧(隐晦不明的样子)</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7FF3.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【正韻】於計切【集韻】【韻會】壹計切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音殪。【說文】華蓋也。【廣韻】羽葆也。【急就篇註】翳，謂凡鳥羽之可隱翳者也。舞者所持羽翿，以自隱翳，因名爲翳。一曰華蓋，今之雉尾扇，是其遺象。</p>
<p>又【周語】是去其藏，而翳其人也。【註】翳，猶屏也。一曰滅也。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】翳，掩也。【註】謂掩覆也。【廣雅】翳，障也。【廣韻】隱也，蔽也。【類篇】䕃也。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】其菑其翳。【傳】木立死曰菑，自死爲翳。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】雨師謂之荓翳。【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】召屏翳。【註】雷師也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】鳥名也，似鳳。【山海經】北海之內有五采之鳥，飛蔽一鄕，名曰翳鳥。【註】鳳屬也。【屈原&middot;離騷】駟玉虯而乗翳。</p>
<p>又【潘岳&middot;射雉賦序】習媒翳之事。【註】媒翳者，所隱以射者也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】烏奚切【集韻】煙奚切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音鷖。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】一結切【正韻】於歇切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音謁。【左思&middot;魏都賦】桃李䕃翳。叶音咽。</p>
