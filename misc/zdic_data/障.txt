                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>障</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄤˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>阻隔，遮挡：～蔽。～翳。～碍。故～。～眼法。一叶～目，不见泰山。</li>
	<li>中国秦、汉两代边塞上作防御用的城堡：&ldquo;出五原塞数百里，远者千里，筑城～列亭，至庐朐&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>用作遮蔽、防卫的东西：屏～。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/幛">幛</a>&rdquo;，画轴。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> separate; shield; barricade</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> abblocken, blockieren ,aufhalten, behindern ,versperren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> obstacle,barrière,bloquer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">障</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>障</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜，章声。阜，土山。从阜，与高下、险阻有关。本义:阻塞，阻隔)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[block;separate;cut off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">障，隔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蕃隔曰障。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陂障九泽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">障大泽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;陂障其水也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲闻枉而恶直言，是障其源而欲其水也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:障翼(遮挡掩护);障距(阻隔，壅蔽);障塞(阻塞不通);障隔(阻隔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 阻挡;拒绝 <span class="encs">[obstruct;stop;block;resist;refuse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令而不行，谓之障。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:障海(阻挡水流入海);障壅(阻挡);障遏(阻挡，阻止);障害(阻碍，妨碍);障故(障碍变故)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 遮挡;遮蔽 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而障之以手。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;精神》</span>。注:&ldquo;蔽也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">司徒</span><span class="diczx2">褚渊</span>入朝，以腰扇障日， <span class="diczx2">祥</span>从侧过，曰:&ldquo;作如此举止，羞面见人，扇障何益?&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南齐书&middot;刘祥传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">障隙处。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:障日(遮蔽太阳);障覆(遮蔽覆盖);障蔽(遮蔽;遮盖);障袂(扬袖遮日);障庇(遮蔽庇护);障袖(以袖遮面)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 佛教用语。烦恼 <span class="encs">[be vexed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能碍圣道，说以为障。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大乘义章》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:障恼(烦恼);障难(烦恼苦难);障盖(烦恼)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>障</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古时边塞上险要处用以防捍寇盗而另筑的小城 <span class="encs">[border town]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">障，小城也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为置吏筑障。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;朝鲜王满传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居一障间。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;酷吏传》</span>。正义:&ldquo;谓塞上要险之处。&rdquo;</span></p>
