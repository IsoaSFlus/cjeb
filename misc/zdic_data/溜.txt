						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>溜</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄡˉ</span></p><ol><li>滑行，（往下）滑：～冰。</li><li>光滑，平滑，无阻碍：～圆。～光。</li><li>趁人不见走开：～走。～号。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">熘</span>”。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>溜</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄡˋ</span></p><ol><li>迅急的水流：大～。急～。</li><li>顺房檐滴下来的水，房顶上流下的水：檐～。承～。</li><li>房檐上安的接雨水用的长水槽：水～。</li><li>量词，指行（<span class="dicpy">háng </span>）列，排：一～三间房。</li><li>某一地点附近的地方：这～儿的果木树很多。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>溜</strong> <span class="dicpy">liū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 滑动 <span class="encs">[slide;slip;glide]</span>。如:溜缰(悄悄脱开缰绳而跑散);溜眼(溜眼睛。以眼传情);溜嘴(把不该说的话说出了嘴);溜槽;溜下;溜冰</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 偷偷地走 <span class="encs">[sneak off]</span>。如:溜抽(脱身溜走);溜跑(偷偷地跑开);溜溜湫湫(躲躲闪闪，轻手轻脚的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 瞟，偷偷地看 <span class="encs">[steal a glance;peek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">说着，却把眼溜着</span><span class="diczx2">金桂</span>一笑。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 偷偷地拿 <span class="encs">[steal;pilfer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">差人各溜过几件细软东西。——<span class="smcs">《醒世恒言》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 略略热一下 <span class="encs">[heat up]</span>。如:溜冷饭</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 略微煮一下，或加淀粉汁急火快炒 <span class="encs">[quick-fry]</span>。如:醋溜鱼</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 逢迎，拍马;奉承 <span class="encs">[fawn on]</span>。如:溜钩子，亦作<span class="yhcs">“溜沟子”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“溜勾子”</span>(方言。钻营;奉承)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 鸣，叫 <span class="encs">[cry;chirp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闭凝盼，声声燕语明如剪，呖呖莺声溜的圆。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">孔尚任</span><span class="smcs">《桃花扇》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>溜</strong> <span class="dicpy">liū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 倾斜 <span class="encs">[sloping]</span>。如:溜肩</p><p><span
