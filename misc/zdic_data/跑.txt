                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">跑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>跑</strong> <span class="dicpy">páo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/páo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，包声。本义:兽用足刨地) 同本义 <span class="encs">[beasts's digging of soil]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">滕公</span>驾至 <span class="diczx2">东都门</span>，马鸣跼不肯定，以足跑地久之。——<span class="smcs">《西京杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跑，足跑地也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">pǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/跑槽">跑槽</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>跑</strong> <span class="dicpy">pǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 急走 <span class="encs">[run]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跑，趵也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">红缰跑骏马，金镞掣秋鹰。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">马戴</span><span class="smcs">《边将》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:长跑(长距离跑步);跑解马(表演马术);跑冰(滑冰;溜冰);跑竹马(民间娱乐活动之一种);跑搭(奔走，奔忙);跑路(奔走);跑踏(奔走;奔忙)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 逃走;躲避 <span class="encs">[escape]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你当真叫他回去，跑不了一顿饱打。——<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:逃跑(逃走);别让特务跑了;跑走(逃走;走掉)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 走;走路 <span class="encs">[walk;go]</span>。如:跑凌(从不很厚的河水上走过去);跑走(行走;走路;走动);跑路(走路);跑腿(步行;行走)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 为某种事物或利益而奔走 <span class="encs">[run about doing sth.]</span>。如:跑海(到处奔走);跑山(来往于山中做买卖);跑合(旧时指说合生意);跑坡(在野地里跑。意在打猎);跑差(为公家奔走干事);跑船(在船上干活谋生);跑媒(为做媒而奔走)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 丢掉;失去 <span class="encs">[lose]</span>。如:跑气(漏气);跑风(漏风;漏气);跑躁(指精神失去控制的症状);跑掉(夭亡;夭折);跑燥(发脾气;燥热)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 走运;走红 <span class="encs">[be lucky]</span>。如:跑起来(走运)</p><p><span
