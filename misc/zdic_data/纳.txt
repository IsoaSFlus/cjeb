                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纳</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>纳</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">納</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>收入，放进：出～。藏污～垢。</li>
	<li>接受：采～。笑～。～谏。</li>
	<li>享受：～福。～凉。</li>
	<li>缴付：～税。</li>
	<li>补缀，缝补；现多指密密地缝：～鞋底。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> admit, take, receive, accept</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> akzeptieren, annehmen, genießen  (V)​,bezahlen, entrichten  (V)​,Na  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> recevoir,accepter,payer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纳</strong></p>
<p>納 <span class="dicpy">n&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，内声。本义:丝被水浸湿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wet;soak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纳，丝湿纳纳也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣纳纳而掩露。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《九叹&middot;逢纷》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纳纳(潮湿的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 收藏;收入 <span class="encs">[collect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">察纳雅言。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:采纳;容纳;藏垢纳污(包藏容纳污秽之物。比喻包容坏人坏事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> &ldquo;纳&rdquo;假借为&ldquo;接纳&rdquo;;接受 <span class="encs">[receive;admit;accept]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几以不纳死。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂开门纳众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诬守纳己赇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纳钱案上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纳质(接受质证;接受质询);纳采(古婚嫁六礼中的第一件事。即行聘;其他如纳吉，纳聘，纳征，纳币皆为六礼之中);纳贽(初次见面所馈赠的礼物);纳受(接受;收受);纳下(采纳下面的意见)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 缴纳，贡献 <span class="encs">[pay;offer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">九江</span>纳锡大龟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岁终则会其出入，而纳其余。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;泉府》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小臣纳卿大夫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;燕礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纳于太庙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;桓公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十月纳禾稼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命弟子纳射器。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纳之库。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纳监(捐纳财物取得监生资格);纳锡(进贡);纳粟(也作内粟，入粟;捐纳粟米或财货给官，以谋求某种利益);纳官(向官府缴纳谷物);纳质(送纳人质)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 穿;着 <span class="encs">[wear;put on]</span>。以脚跟着入鞋中。如:纳履(穿鞋);纳屦踵决(穿鞋而后跟即破。极言生活贫困)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 使进入 <span class="encs">[enter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三纳之而未克。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 取;娶 <span class="encs">[marry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀三卻而尸诸朝，纳其室以分妇人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纳币(订婚时男方给女方的财物);纳宠(娶妾);纳赘(招上门女婿);纳妇(娶妇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 结交 <span class="encs">[make friends with;associate with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">魏了翁</span>闻之，更纳交焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史&middot;张忠恕传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纳交(结交);纳宗(结交宗室朋友)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 用同&ldquo;捺&rdquo; <span class="encs">[press with force;low]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 通&ldquo;衲&rdquo;。密针缝纫 <span class="encs">[sew close stitches over a patch，etc.]</span></p>

<p>14) 扣压 <span class="encs">[withhold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枢密</span><span class="diczx2">童贯</span>同太师 <span class="diczx2">蔡京</span>，太尉 <span class="diczx2">高俅</span>、 <span class="diczx2">杨戬</span>，商议纳下表章不奏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p>
