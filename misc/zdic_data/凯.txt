                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凯</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>凯</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">凱</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄞˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>军队得胜回来奏的乐曲：～歌。～旋。奏～而归。</li>
	<li>和，柔：&ldquo;～风自南，吹彼棘薪&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> triumphant; triumph, victory</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> erfolgreich, siegestrunken</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> victorieux,triomphant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凯</strong></p>
<p>凱 <span class="dicpy">kǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(会意，从豈，从几。&ldquo;几&rdquo;象架子。字本作&ldquo;豈&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">qǐ</span>)，也作&ldquo;愷&rdquo;。从豆，( <span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i</span>)省声。本义:军队得胜所奏的乐曲) 同本义 <span class="encs">[music of triumph]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豈，还师振旅乐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，经传多以愷为之。亦作凱。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凯以强教之。即以豈胜豈乐为训。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汔可休而凯归。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则令奏恺乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司乐》</span>。注:&ldquo;献功之乐。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恺乐献于社。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司马》</span>。注:&ldquo;兵乐曰恺。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">振旅恺以入于</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十八年》</span>。注:&ldquo;乐也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六军张凯声如雷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘克庄</span><span class="smcs">《破阵曲》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凯入(奏着胜利的乐曲归来);凯乐(演奏胜利的乐曲 );凯声(胜利的乐歌声);凯期(胜利的日期);凯还(胜利归来)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>凯</strong></p>
<p>凱 <span class="dicpy">kǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 和乐;欢乐 <span class="encs">[harmonious and happy;joyous;gay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故凯乐之情，见于金石。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《声无哀乐论》</span>。又如:凯泽(和乐，欢乐);凯乐(和乐，欢乐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 安乐;温和 <span class="encs">[peaceful and happy;mild]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凯风自南。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;凯风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心凯康以乐欢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋玉</span><span class="smcs">《神女赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凯易(平和平易的样子);凯风(和风;南风);凯弟(和顺善良的样子)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>凯</strong></p>
<p>凱 <span class="dicpy">kǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>杀，斩 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叵耐这两个畜生逃走，今日捉将来，我恼了，如何不凯?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《京本通俗小说&middot;碾玉观音下》</span></span></p>
