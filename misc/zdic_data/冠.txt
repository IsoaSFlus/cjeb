						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>冠</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">guān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>帽子：衣～。～戴。～盖（古代官吏的帽子和车盖，借指官吏）。衣～楚楚。</li><li>形状像帽子或在顶上的东西：～子。鸡～。树～。～状动脉。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>冠</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">guàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>把帽子戴在头上：沐猴而～。</li><li>超出众人，居第一位：～军。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">冠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>冠</strong> <span class="dicpy">guān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从<span class="yhcs">“冖”</span>( <span class="dicpy">mì)，</span>用布帛蒙覆。从<span class="yhcs">“元”</span>(人头)，从<span class="yhcs">“寸”</span>(手)。意思是:手拿布帛之类的制品加在人的头上，即<span class="yhcs">“冠”</span>。本义:帽子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冠，弁冕之总名也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冠至尊也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·问丧》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缁布冠。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“今小吏冠。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡甸冠弁服。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司服》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怒发上冲冠。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">新沐者必弹冠。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·渔父》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冠盖相望，乘坚策肥。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小民罢市，丧出江上，白衣冠送者夹岸。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">峨大冠，拖长绅者，昂昂乎庙堂之器也，果能建</span><span class="diczx2">伊</span><span class="diczx2">皋</span>之业耶?—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span
