                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>涤</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">滌</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>洗：洗～。～濯。～除。～荡。～瑕荡秽。</li>
	<li>古代指养祭牲的房子。</li>
	<li>古代指音乐节奏急速。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wash, cleanse, purify; sweep</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> waschen, reinigent<书&gt  (V)​,[bei RÜDENBERG,STANGE nur mit dem dritten und vierten Ton: di3, di4] , waschen, wässern</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> nettoyer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涤</strong></p>
<p>滌 <span class="dicpy">d&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，条声。本义:洗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wash;clense]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涤，洒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射人宿视涤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;大射仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水曰清涤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及执事，视涤濯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太宰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涤篆(洗印。用以检查印是否有缺陷);涤濯(洗涤);涤耳(洗耳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 除，清除 <span class="encs">[clear away]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">进明德而崇业，涤饕餮之贪欲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涤瑕(洗除瑕疵。有改过迁善之意);涤地无类(荡涤无遗。形容清除得彻底)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 打扫 <span class="encs">[wipe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十月涤场。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涤场(打扫场地)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涤</strong></p>
<p>滌 <span class="dicpy">d&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>涤宫，古代宫中饲养祭祀牲畜的房子 <span class="encs">[cowshed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者帝牛必在涤三月，以致严洁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《续资治通鉴》</span></span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">滌</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“涤”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/滌">滌</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上" target="_blank">巳集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上&amp;bs=水" target="_blank">水</a>】
                              滌·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6ECC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】徒歷切【集韻】【韻會】亭歷切【正韻】杜歷切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音狄。【說文】洒也。【詩&middot;豳風】十月滌場。【正義】洗器謂之滌。</p>
<p>又旱氣也。【詩&middot;大雅】滌滌山川。</p>
<p>又煗風也。【歲華紀麗】風惟滌滌，木漸欣欣。</p>
<p>又浩酒也。【周禮&middot;春官】司尊彝凡酒脩酌。【註】脩讀如滌。滌酌，以水和而泲之。今齊人名浩酒曰滌。</p>
<p>又養牲室也。【禮&middot;郊特牲】帝牛必在滌三月。</p>
<p>又【集韻】徒弔切，音調。亦養牲室也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶毒藥切，音鐸。【班固&middot;東都賦】紹百王之荒屯，因造化之盪滌。體元立制，繼天而作。</p>
