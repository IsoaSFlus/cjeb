<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">釧</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨㄢˋ</span></p>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>钏</strong></p>
<p>釧 <span class="dicpy">chu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 臂镯的古称。俗称镯，镯子。用珠子或玉石等穿起来做成的镯子 <span class="encs">[bracelet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">环臂谓之钏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:钏臂(臂镯、手镯)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“钏”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/釧">釧</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=金" target="_blank">金</a>】
                              釧·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：3</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/91E7.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】尺絹切【韻會】【正韻】樞絹切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音竁。【說文】臂環也。【玉篇】釵釧。【正字通】古男女同用，今惟女飾有之。【何偃&middot;與謝尚書】珍玉名釧，因物寄情。【庾信&middot;竹杖賦】玉關寄書，章臺留釧。【墨莊漫錄】唐文宗問宰臣，金條脫何物。宰臣未對。文宗曰：古詩：輕衫穩條脫，卽今臂釧也，別作玔。</p>
<p>又姓。【萬姓統譜】萬曆閒，撫州照磨釧國賢，閬中人。貴陽縣丞釧佩，雲南人。</p>
<p>又【類篇】昌緣切，音穿。車釧。</p>
