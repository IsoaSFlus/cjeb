                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>剜</strong> <span class="dicpy">wān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，宛声。本义:挖)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dig]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">韩当</span>急为脱去温衣，用力剜出箭头。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">罗贯中</span><span class="smcs">《全图绣像三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:剜野菜;剜补(挖出错字，补上正字);剜挑(抠挖);剜改(挖出错字，改成正字);剜肉医疮(比喻只顾眼前之急，不惜采用有害之法)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 以刀子等除去 <span class="encs">[cut out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剜，削也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剜，刻削也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:剜肉生(做，成，补)疮(本想割肉医疮，但被割之处反成新疮。比喻行事只顾一面，结果与预想适得其反)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用手指挖出<span class="encs">[对手的眼睛]</span> <span class="encs">[gouge out]</span>。如:凶残的奴隶主剜出奴隶的眼睛</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/剜肉补疮">剜肉补疮</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
