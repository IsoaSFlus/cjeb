                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>疗</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">療</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liáo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáo.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄠˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 医治：医～。治～。～程。～效。～养。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> be healed, cured, recover</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> heilen  (S, Med)​,behandeln  (V, Med)​,versorgen  (V, Med)​,Liao  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> soigner,traiter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>疗</strong></p><p>療 <span class="dicpy">liáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chuáng</span>)，表示疾病有关，尞( <span class="dicpy">liáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáo.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:医治)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(含有对症下药之意) <span class="encs">[cure; heal; treat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疗，治也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡疗疡，以五毒攻之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官·疡医》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可救疗。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡疗病者，必知脉之虚实。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·王符传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既泣之三日，乃誓疗之。——</span><span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:疗治(治疗，调治);疗疾(疗病。治疗疾病);疗视(诊察治疗);疗护(治疗救护);疗妒(治疗嫉妒的病症);疗病(治病)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 治愈，治好;止<span class="encs">[cure]</span>。如:疗狂(制止狂放或狂妄);疗贪(制止贪婪之心);疗忧(消除忧愁)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疗程">疗程</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疗法">疗法</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疗效">疗效</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疗养">疗养</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疗养院">疗养院</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疗治">疗治</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
