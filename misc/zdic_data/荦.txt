                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>荦</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">犖</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨㄛˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>杂色牛，引申为杂色：怒～（狂怒的杂色牛）。驳～。</li>
	<li>特出，明显：卓～（特出）。～～（分明，明显，如&ldquo;～～大端&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> brindled ox; brindled in color</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>荦</strong></p>
<p>犖 <span class="dicpy">lu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从牛，劳省声。本义:杂色的牛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[brindled ox]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荦，驳牛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黄白杂谓之驳荦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斯为朽关键，怒荦抉以入。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆龟蒙</span><span class="smcs">《杂讽》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Luo town]</span>。在今河南省淮阳县西北</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>荦</strong></p>
<p>犖 <span class="dicpy">lu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>明显，分明 <span class="encs">[salient]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此其荦荦大者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;天官书》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;事之分明也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荦然(明显的样子)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">犖</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“荦”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/犖">犖</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集下" target="_blank">巳集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集下&amp;bs=牛" target="_blank">牛</a>】
                              犖·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7296.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】呂角切【集韻】【韻會】力角切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䃕。【說文】駁牛也。从牛，勞省聲。【廣韻】駁犖，牛雜色。</p>
<p>又【廣雅】丁犖，牛屬。</p>
<p>又【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】赤瑕駁犖。【註】司馬彪曰：駁犖，采點也。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;天官書】此其犖犖大者。【註】索隱曰：犖犖，事之分明也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】卓犖。【韻會】卓犖，超絕也。【班固&middot;典引】卓犖乎方州。</p>
<p>又人名。【左傳&middot;莊三十二年】圉人犖。</p>
<p>又【集韻】力各切，音樂。牛駁色。</p>
