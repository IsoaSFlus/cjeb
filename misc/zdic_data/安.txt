                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>安</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ān.mp3"></a></span> ㄢˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>平静，稳定：～定。～心。～宁。～稳。～闲。～身立命。～邦定国。</li>    <li>使平静，使安定（多指心情）：～民。～慰。～抚。</li>    <li>对生活工作等感觉满足合适：心～。～之若素（遇到不顺利情况或反常现象像平常一样对待，毫不在意）。</li>    <li>没有危险，不受威协：平～。转危为～。</li>    <li>装设：～置。～家立业。</li>    <li>存着，怀着（某种念头，多指不好的）：他～的什么心？</li>    <li>疑问词，哪里：～能如此？</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">安</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>安</strong> <span class="dicpy">ān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从<span class="yhcs">“女”</span>在<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>下，表示无危险。本义:安定;安全;安稳)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[peaceful;quiet;calm;tranquil]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">安，定也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共给之为安。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天地》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">好和不争曰安。——<span class="smcs">《周书·谥法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心皆安下切上。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·少牢礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是故君子安而不忘危。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">居安思危。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风雨不动安如山。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然后得一夕安寝。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">谢庄</span>遂安。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:安睡;安寝;安抵(平安地抵达);心神不安;坐立不安;安枕(安安稳稳地睡觉);安帖(安定;平静;妥帖);安席(安稳地坐着;安静入睡)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 安逸，安乐 <span class="encs">[easy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予出官二年，恬然自安。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子食无求饱，居无求安。——<span class="smcs">《论语·学而》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:安佚(安闲舒适);安堵(安居，不受骚扰);安坦(安心舒坦)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 安详，从容不迫 <span class="encs">[composed]</span>。如:安童(僮仆，小厮);安娴(安详文雅);安谛(安详审慎);安矜(安祥稳重);安俟(安心等待);安宜(稳重)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 安宁 <span class="encs">[peaceful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">毅</span>良久稍安。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:安生(安宁);安豫(安宁快乐);安淳(安宁淳朴);安休</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 缓慢 <span class="encs">[slowly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">安步以当车。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策四》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:安步(缓步徐行);安行</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>安</strong> <span class="dicpy">ān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
