                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">追</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>追</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本义:追赶)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(使追赶对象回来) <span class="encs">[chase after]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">追，逐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以比追胥。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小司徒》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“逐寇也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公追戎于</span><span class="diczx2">济</span>西。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公十八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驰车充国者，追寇之马也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·七臣七主》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">追亡逐北。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">壶子</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“追之!”</span> <span class="diczx2">列子</span>追之不及。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·应帝王》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公使</span><span class="diczx2">阳处</span>父追之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>主追 <span class="diczx2">韩信</span>，苍生起 <span class="diczx2">谢安</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《宴王使君宅题二首》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闻追</span><span class="diczx2">豫州</span>。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">追</span><span class="diczx2">操</span>至 <span class="diczx2">南郡</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx1">追购又急。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《<指南录>后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:穷寇勿追;穷追;猛追;追亡(追逐败亡的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 追求;追取<span class="encs">[pursue;seek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">库藏粮饷，都是民脂民膏，你只顾侵来肥己买笑追欢，败坏国家许多大事。——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:追摄(追取);追欠(追缴亏空的款子、拖欠的赋税等);追欢(求欢)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 赶得上;比配 <span class="encs">[compare;match]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">除两税外，不许差役追拢。——<span
