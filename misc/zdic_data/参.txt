                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>参</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">參</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cān.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>加入在内：～加。～与。～政。～赛。～议。</li>
	<li>相间，夹杂：～杂。～半。</li>
	<li>检验，用其他有关材料来研究，考证某事物：～考。～照。～省（xǐng）（检验省察）。～看。～阅。～检。</li>
	<li>探究，领悟：～悟。～透。～破。～禅。</li>
	<li>旧指下级进见上级：～见。～拜。</li>
	<li>弹劾，向皇帝告状：～奏。～劾。～革。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>参</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">參</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>星名，二十八宿之一：～商（&ldquo;参星&rdquo;和&ldquo;商星&rdquo;，此出则彼没，两不相见；喻亲友隔离不得相见或彼此对立不和睦）。～辰卯酉（&ldquo;辰星&rdquo;即商星，参星酉时现于西方，辰星卯时出于东方；喻互不相关或势不两立）。</li>
	<li>中药名：人～。党～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>参</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">參</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～差（cī）〕长短不齐，如&ldquo;～～不齐&rdquo;、&ldquo;～～错落&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>参</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">參</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sān.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄢˉ</span></p>
