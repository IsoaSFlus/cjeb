                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谢</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>谢</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">謝</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>对别人的帮助或赠与表示感激：～～。～仪。～忱（谢意）。～恩。～意。面～。致～。感～。</li>
	<li>认错，道歉：～过。～罪。</li>
	<li>推辞：～绝。闭门～客。</li>
	<li>凋落，衰退：～顶。～世（去世）。凋～。新陈代～。</li>
	<li>告诉，询问：&ldquo;使人称～：&lsquo;皇帝敬劳将军。&rsquo;&rdquo;</li>
	<li>逊，不如：&ldquo;锦江何～曲江池？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/榭">榭</a>&rdquo;，建在台上的房屋。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>谢</strong></p>
<p>謝 <span class="dicpy">xi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，射声。本义:向人认错道歉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[excuse oneself;make an apologize]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自谢曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长跪而谢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦日不可不蚤自来谢</span><span class="diczx2">项王</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惊谢曰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢咎(引咎自退;悔过;谢罪);谢错(认错赔不是);谢室(待罪之室)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用言辞委婉地推辞拒绝 <span class="encs">[decline;refuse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃召拜</span><span class="diczx2">黯</span>为淮阳太守， <span class="diczx2">黯</span>伏谢不受命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;汲黯列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谢媒人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>而谢。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谢客矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子退而休之，谢医却药。 &mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《盖公堂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢医(谢绝医疗);谢金(谢绝礼金)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 辞去官职 <span class="encs">[resign]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若此儿不救，便当回舟谢职，无复游宦之兴矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南齐书&middot;王僧虔传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当亦谢官去，岂令心事违。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《送张五归山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢老(告老还乡);谢事(卸去官职);谢政(致仕退休);谢役(辞去差使);谢职(卸去官职)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 告辞;告别 <span class="encs">[take leave;leave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">广</span>不谢大将军而起行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;李将军列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>视公子色终不变，乃谢客就车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故不复谢。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不报谢?</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">过谢侯生。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">往昔初阳岁，谢家来贵门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢了扰(告辞);谢别(告别，辞别)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 告诉，告诫 <span class="encs">[tell;warn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谢汉使。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多谢后世人，戒之慎勿忘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 以言辞相问候 <span class="encs">[send one&#39;s respects to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至府，为我多谢问</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;赵广汉传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">界上亭长寄声谢我，何以不为致问?</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 感谢 <span class="encs">[thank]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">休谢之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">哙</span>拜谢，起，立而饮之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且谢曰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽死必谢。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢承(感谢;答谢);谢荷(感谢);谢候(致谢并问候);谢私(感谢私下帮助)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 逝去 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诏曰:弟</span><span class="diczx2">勰</span>所生母 <span class="diczx2">潘</span>早龄谢世，显号未加。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《魏书&middot;彭城王勰传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢宾客(死亡的委婉说法);谢世</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 酬谢 <span class="encs">[thank sb. with a gift]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婚礼不许多用盘盒猪羊糖饼，不许厚礼物谢媒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">海瑞</span><span class="smcs">《禁约》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受人十万，谢客数千，又重馈部吏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《潜夫论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢土(修建完毕，祭祀土神的礼义活动);谢犒(酬谢;犒劳);谢银(酬谢用的银子);谢灶诰(祭灶王爷的青词)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 衰败，衰落 <span class="encs">[decline;wane]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宿觉</span>名未谢，残山今尚存。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">叶适</span><span class="smcs">《宿觉庵》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">花之既谢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 逊让;不如 <span class="encs">[modestly decline]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既鲸鲵折首，</span><span class="diczx2">西夏</span>底定，便宜诉其本怀，避贤谢拙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋书&middot;王宏传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢拙(自谦愚笨而辞职)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 避免，避开 <span class="encs">[avoid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必以俪辞为文，何缘<span class="smcs">《十翼》</span>不能一致，岂波澜既尽有所谢短乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">章炳麟</span><span class="smcs">《文学总略》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谢短(避其所短)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>谢</strong></p>

<p>〈名〉</p>

<p><span class="diczx1">于邑于</span><span class="diczx2">谢</span>，南国是式。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大雅》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">謝</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“谢”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/謝">謝</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              謝·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8B1D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】辭夜切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】詞夜切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音榭。【說文】辭去也。【廣雅】去也。【楚辭&middot;九章】願歲幷謝與長友兮。【註】謝，去也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】絕也。【史記&middot;儒林傳】謝絕賔客。</p>
<p>又【增韻】退也，衰也，彫落也。【南史&middot;範縝傳】形存則神存，形謝則神減。【淮南子&middot;兵略訓】若春秋有代謝。</p>
<p>又【類篇】告也。【前漢&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>餘傳】厮養卒謝其舍。【晉灼註】以辭相告曰謝。</p>
<p>又【韻會】拜賜曰謝。【前漢&middot;張安世傳】安世嘗有引薦，其人來謝。安世以爲舉賢達能，豈有私謝邪。</p>
<p>又【正字通】自以爲過曰謝。【禮&middot;檀弓】從而謝焉。【史記&middot;項羽紀】旦日不可不蚤自來謝項王。</p>
<p>又【韻會】聽也。【正韻】致仕曰謝。【禮&middot;曲禮】大夫七十而致事若不得謝，必賜之几杖。【註】謝，猶聽也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>皓曰：君不許其致事也。如辭謝、代謝，皆却而退去之義。</p>
<p>又鳥名。【張華&middot;禽經】子規啼苦，則倒懸於樹，自呼曰謝豹。</p>
<p>又菜名。【本草綱目】水苦蕒，一名謝婆菜。</p>
<p>又島名。【唐書&middot;地理志】登州東北海有大謝島。</p>
<p>又蠻名。【舊唐書&middot;南蠻傳】東謝蠻，其地在黔州之西。</p>
<p>又國名。【詩&middot;大雅】于邑于謝。【毛傳】謝，周之南國也。【朱傳】在今鄧州南陽縣。</p>
<p>又縣名。【前漢&middot;地理志】謝沐縣，屬蒼梧郡。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】出<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>留、會稽二望。周宣王之舅姜申伯封于謝，以邑爲氏。【左傳&middot;昭七年】謝息，爲孟孫守。</p>
<p>又複姓。【風俗通】周宣王支子食采謝丘，因以爲氏。【前漢&middot;古今人表】謝丘章，魯人。</p>
<p>又通作榭。【左傳&middot;襄三十一年】宮室<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>庳，無觀臺榭。【註】本亦作謝。【荀子&middot;王霸篇】臺榭甚高。【註】與謝同。</p>
<p>又或作繹。亦作射。【爾雅&middot;釋魚】龜仰者謝。【疏】謂行時頭仰。《周禮》地龜曰繹屬是也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;龜人鄭註】仰者繹。【禮&middot;玉藻&middot;鄭註】靈射之屬。【釋文】射音亦。【周禮】作繹。【爾雅】作謝。</p>
<p>又叶祥豫切，徐去聲。【左思&middot;魏都賦】有靦瞢容，神惢形茹。弛氣離坐，㥏墨而謝。</p>
<p>又叶徂賀切，音坐。【韓愈&middot;送窮文】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂹</span>頭喪氣，上手稱謝。燒車與船，延之上坐。　【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27B04.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧬄</span>。</p>
