                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>剟</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">duō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄛ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>刊削；删除。《廣雅&bull;釋詁三》：&ldquo;剟，削也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>割取；选取。《漢書&bull;賈誼傳》：&ldquo;盜者剟寢户之簾，搴兩廟之器。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>刺，戳。《史記&bull;張耳陳餘列傳》：&ldquo;榜笞數千，刺剟，身無可擊者，終不復言。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>击。《廣韻&bull;末韻》：&ldquo;剟，擊也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/贅">贅</a>&rdquo;。无用的。《淮南子&bull;繆稱》：&ldquo;物莫無所不用&hellip;&hellip;是故聖人制其剟材，無所不用矣。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>剟</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/㓼">㓼</a>&rdquo;。《集韻&bull;止韻》：&ldquo;㓼，或作剟。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>剟</strong> <span class="dicpy">duō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:削，删除)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[omit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剟，刊也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剟，削也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吏治榜笞数千刺剟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;张耳陈馀传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剟削(删除，清除);剟除(削除)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 割，割取 <span class="encs">[cut]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盗者剟寝户之帘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span>。注:&ldquo;割取之也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剟材(身有残疾的人材)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 雕刻 <span class="encs">[carve]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">村人曳在道旁西，耶娘父母不知。上剟排书字，此是死不归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《敦煌曲子词》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;掇&rdquo;。掇拾 <span class="encs">[pick up]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:剟拾(摭拾);剟裒(掇集);剟取(摘取，选取);剟剽(摘抄)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=刀" target="_blank">刀</a>】
                              剟·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/525F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】陟劣切【集韻】【韻會】株劣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音輟。【說文】刊也。从刀，叕聲。【商子&middot;定分篇】有敢剟定法令者死。</p>
<p>又刺也。【史記&middot;張耳<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>餘傳】吏治榜笞，數千刺剟，身無可擊者。</p>
<p>又割也。【前漢&middot;賈誼傳】剟寢戸之簾。【註】師古曰：剟謂割取之也。</p>
<p>又與掇通。【前漢&middot;王嘉傳】掇去宋弘。【註】師古曰：掇，讀曰剟。剟，削也。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】丁活切【集韻】【韻會】都活切【正韻】都括切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音掇。【郭璞&middot;爾雅序】剟其瑕礫。【疏】剟削去其疵瑕瓦礫。剟音掇。</p>
