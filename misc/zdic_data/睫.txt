<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>睫</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>眼睑边缘的细毛：～毛。目不交～。迫在眉～。</li>
	<li>眨眼：&ldquo;矢来注眸子而眶不～。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>睫</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从目，疌( <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:眼睫毛) 同本义 <span class="encs">[eyelash]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾不贵其用智之如目见毫毛而不见其睫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;越世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:睫眸(睫毛和眼珠。比喻眼前事理);睫眼间(比喻极短的时间);目不交睫</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>睫</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 迅速而无意地眨眼 <span class="encs">[wink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矢来注眸子，而眶不睫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;仲尼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:睫睫(目动的样子)</p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">长在上下眼睑边缘的细毛。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「迫在眉睫」、「目不交睫」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜牧〈登池州九峰楼寄张祜〉诗：「睫在眼前长不见，道非身外更何求。」</span><span class="gc_yx">也称为「睫毛」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/776B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】子葉切【集韻】卽涉切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音接。同䀹。【說文】目旁毛也。【釋名】揷接也。揷於眼眶而相接也。【禮&middot;內則】豕望視而交睫腥。【註】目睫毛交也。【莊子&middot;庚桑楚】向吾見若眉睫之閒，吾因以得女矣。【皇極經世】走之類，上睫接下。飛之類，下睫接上。類使之然也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】七接切，音妾。義同。</p>
<p>又書涉切，音攝。目動貌。與<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25349.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥍉</span>同。</p>
