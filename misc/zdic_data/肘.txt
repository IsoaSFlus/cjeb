                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>肘</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从肉从寸，寸，腕下<span class="yhcs">“寸口”</span>部位。点明肘所在的范围。本义:上下臂相接处可以弯曲的部位)同本义 <span class="encs">[elbow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肘，臂节也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 肘者，肱与臂相接处。</p><p><span class="diczx1">反诎之，及肘。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·深衣》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“肘或为腕。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俄而柳生其左肘。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·至乐》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自始合，而矢贯余手及肘。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:肘膊(手臂。比喻得力的助手);肘弯(肘关节部位);肘翼(胳膊肘和羽翼，比喻切近之地);肘臂(臂膀。比喻亲信;爪牙);肘腕(肘关节向里凹下去的地方);肘行(以肘前行，表示畏服);肘行膝步(匍匐前行，表示虔诚或哀戚)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>肘</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用肘触人示意;拉住肘部<span class="encs">[hold sb.back by the elbow;hinder]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">柴</span>俯不对，女肘之， <span class="diczx2">柴</span>始强颜笑。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:肘掣(拉住胳膊。比喻牵制或留难);肘足(以肘触肘，以足蹑足。比喻暗中示意，互结其谋)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肘窝">肘窝</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肘见">肘见</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肘腋">肘腋</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肘子">肘子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下" target="_blank">未集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下&amp;bs=肉" target="_blank">肉</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8098.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】陟柳切【韻會】陟肘切【正韻】止酉切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音帚。【說文】臂節也。从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2054E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠕎</span>从寸。寸，手寸口也。【徐曰】寸口，手腕動脈處也。【詩·小雅·如矢斯棘箋】如人挾弓矢，戟其肘。【禮·玉藻】袂可以回肘。</p><p>又【深衣】袼之高下，可以運肘。【左傳·成二年】張侯曰：自始合，而矢貫余手及肘。</p><p>又【釋名】肘，注也。可隱注也。</p><p>又【正字通】爲人捉其肘而留之，亦曰肘。【後漢·孔融傳】欲命駕，數數被肘。【杜甫·遭田家泥飮美嚴中丞詩】久客惜人情，如何拒鄰叟。高聲索果栗，欲起時被肘。</p><p>又書名。【前漢·藝文志】彊弩將軍王圍肘法五卷。【抱朴子·地眞卷】崔文子肘後經。</p><p>又【韻會】一曰一肘二尺，一曰一尺五寸爲一肘，四肘爲一弓，三百弓爲一里。　【集韻】或作䏔。通作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26874.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦡴</span>。</p>
