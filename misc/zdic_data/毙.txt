                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">毙</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>毙</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">斃</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>死：～命。击～。</li>
	<li>仆倒：&ldquo;郑人击简子中肩，～于车中&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> kill; die violent death</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">毙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>毙</strong></p>
<p>斃 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从死，敝声。在先秦古书中的獘，在流传中往往被改成斃。本义:仆倒;倒下去)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fall forward]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郑</span>人击 <span class="diczx2">简子</span>中肩，毙于车中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公二年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多行不义必自斃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射其右，斃于车中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:毙死(仆倒而死);毙踣(倒毙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 垮台，失败 <span class="encs">[collapse;fall from power]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多行不义，必自毙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为死 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与犬，犬斃;与小臣，小臣亦斃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及扑入手，已股落腹裂，斯须就毙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:毙命;毙伤</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> <span class="encs">[口]</span>∶枪杀 <span class="encs">[shoot]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:毙敌三千;愤怒的人们要求毙了这个土皇帝</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 杀死 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼连毙者三。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以刀劈狼首，又数刀毙之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“毙”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/獘">獘</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=犬">犬</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7358.svg">                          <p>頓仆也。从犬敝聲。《春秋傳》曰：&ldquo;與犬，犬獘。&rdquo; 斃，獘或从死。毗祭切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7358_3865.svg" /><span class="swbf">(獘)</span><span class="ywyy">頓仆也。</span><span class="swdz">人部曰：仆者，頓也。謂前覆也。人前仆若頓首然,故曰頓仆。</span><span class="ywyy">从犬，敝聲。</span><span class="swdz">毗祭切。十五部。</span><span class="ywyy">春秋傳曰：與犬犬獘。</span><span class="swdz">僖四年左傳文，引此證从犬之意也。獘本因犬仆製字，叚借爲凡仆之偁，俗又引伸爲利弊字，遂改其字作弊。訓困也，惡也。此與改奬爲㢡正同。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7358_3866.svg" /><span class="swbf">(斃)</span><span class="ywyy">獘或从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239B8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣦸</span>。</span><span class="swdz">經書頓仆皆作此字，如左傳斃於車中、與一人俱斃是也。今左傳犬獘亦作犬斃。葢許時經書斃多作獘。</span></p>                          </div>
