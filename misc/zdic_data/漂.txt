                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>漂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">piāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄠˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 浮在液体上不动或顺着风向、流向而移动：～浮。～流。～没（<span class="dicpy">mò</span>）。～移。～泊（喻职业生活不固定，东奔西走。亦作“飘泊”）。～零。～沦。～游。～萍。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>漂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">piǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄠˇ</span></p><ol><li>用水加药品使物品退去颜色或变白：～白。～染。</li><li>用水冲去杂质：～洗。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>漂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">piào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piào.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄠˋ</span></p><ol><li>〔～亮〕ａ．好看，如“长得～～”；ｂ．出色，如“这件事办得～～”。</li><li>方言，事情、账目等落空：那事没什么指望，～了。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>漂</strong> <span class="dicpy">piāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，票声。本义:浮，浮流)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[float]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">漂，浮也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">血流漂杵。——<span class="smcs">《伪书武成》</span></span></p><p><span
