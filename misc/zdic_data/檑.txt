                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6AD1.svg">                          <p>龜目酒尊，刻木作雲雷象，象施不窮也。从木畾聲。罍，櫑或从缶。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/250C7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥃇</span>，櫑或从皿。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26269.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉩</span>，籒文櫑。魯回切</p>                          </div>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6AD1_3029.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(櫑)</span><span class="ywyy">龜目酒尊。</span><span class="swdz">五經異義。韓詩說。金罍、大器也。天子以玉。諸侯、大夫以金。士以梓。古毛詩說。金罍、酒器也。諸臣之所酢。人君以黃金飾。尊大一碩。金飾龜目。葢刻爲雲靁之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C3C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧰼</span>。許君曰。謹案韓詩說。天子以玉。經無明文。謂之罍者、取<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C3C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧰼</span>雲靁博施。故從人君下及諸臣同。按異義從古毛說。說文同也。故云龜目酒尊、刻本爲雲靁<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C3C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧰼</span>。爾雅。彝卣罍器也。小罍謂之坎。然則櫑有小大。燕禮。罍水在東。則罍亦以盛水。</span><span class="ywyy">刻木作雲靁<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C3C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧰼</span>。</span><span class="swdz">句。</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C3C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧰼</span>施不竆也。從木。從畾。畾亦聲。</span><span class="swdz">此五字今補正。刻木至從畾、此述古毛說。說從木從畾之意也。刻爲龜目。又通體刻爲雲靁。古之刻雲如古文之？。刻靁如古文之？。所以刻爲雲靁者、以雲靁施澤不竆。人君之櫑、爲諸臣取酒自酢者。故<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C3C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧰼</span>之也。畾者、靁之省。凡許言畾聲、皆靁省聲也。魯回切。十五部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6AD1_3030.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(罍)</span><span class="ywyy">櫑或從缶。</span><span class="swdz">葢始以木、後以匋。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6AD1_3031.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/250C7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥃇</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">櫑或從皿。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6AD1_3032.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26269.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉩</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文櫑。從缶囘。</span><span class="swdz">猶籒文䴎作？也。漢書文三王傳。孝王有？尊。如此作。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>
