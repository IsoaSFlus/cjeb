data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，莫声。本义:临摹，照着样子描画、写字)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[copy;trace]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摹，规也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“摹与模义略同。”</span></p><p><span class="smcs">《画记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上见其发秃，又所摹画不能称上意。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《毛颖传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六朝人尚字学，摹临特盛。——<span class="encs">[imitate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若是，三代不摹，圣人未可师也。——</span><span class="diczx2">范晔</span><span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
