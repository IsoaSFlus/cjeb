						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>站</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>立，久立：～立。～岗。～起来。</li><li>停：～住。～住脚。</li><li>中途停留转运的地方：驿～。～台。火车～。起点～。</li><li>分支办事单位：保健～。防疫～。供应～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>站</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从立，占声。本义:直立)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 站立，直立。以基本直立的姿势用双脚或一脚支撑自己 <span class="encs">[stand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡射，或对贼、对把，站定观把子或贼人，不许看扣。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">戚继光</span><span class="smcs">《纪效新书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:站门子(妓女站在门口招人或接客);站眙(久立而不行走);站堂(旧时衙门开审时，差役排列在公堂上以应差);站柜台(指营业员在商品柜台内侧卖货)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 停止前进;直立不动 <span class="encs">[halt;stop]</span>。如:这车中途不站;站住</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 在一场争论中从一方转到另一方 <span class="encs">[come over]</span>。如:站到现实主义方面来</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>站</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蒙古语的音译。驿站，古时传递军政文书的人中途换马、食宿或转递之所 <span class="encs">[post]</span>。元代驿站称<span class="yhcs">“站赤”</span>，省称<span class="yhcs">“站”</span>。明改站为<span class="yhcs">“驿”</span>，清代<span class="yhcs">“驿”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“站”</span>并称</p><p><span class="diczx2">贾琏</span>这番进 <span class="diczx2">京</span>，若按站走时，本该出月到家。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:站夫(驿站的役夫);站驿(元代驿站);站船(旧称在航程有驿站递次接待的官船);站户(元代服役于站驿之户称<span class="yhcs">“站户”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 为某种业务而设置的机构 <span class="encs">[station]</span>。如:车站;供应站;加油站;转运站;中转站;地质站;农业实验站;校外活动站;医疗站</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
