                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>龀</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">齔</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>小孩换牙（乳齿脱落长出恒齿）：童～（年幼）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> lose baby teeth and get adult teeth</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> perte des dents de lait</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">龀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>龀</strong></p>
<p>齔 <span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从齿，𠤎（diān）声，倒人形，<span class="yhcs">“颠”</span>初文。本义:儿童换牙，脱乳齿换恒齿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[drop of milk teeth and growth of permanent teeth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">龀，毁齿也。男八月生齿，八岁而龀;女七月生齿，七岁而龀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未龀不入军门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;小问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:龀髫(换牙和垂发。喻幼年);龀龆(指幼年);始龀(刚换牙，指七八岁)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>龀</strong></p>
<p>齔 <span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 乳齿 <span class="encs">[milk tooth]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指童年 <span class="encs">[child]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:龀童(七、八岁的小孩);龀年(童年);龀岁(童年)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“龀”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/齔">齔</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷二">卷二</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷二&amp;swbs=齒">齒</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9F54.svg">                          <p>毀齒也。男八月生齒，八歲而齔。女七月生齒，七歲而齔。从齒从七。初堇切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9F54_8181.svg" /><span class="swbf">(齔)</span><span class="ywyy">毀齒也。男八月生齒，八歲而齔。女七月生齒，七歲而齔。从齒匕。</span><span class="swdz">各本篆作齔，云从齒，从七。初忍初覲二音。殆傅會七聲爲之。今按其字从齒<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2090E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤎</span>，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2090E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤎</span>，變也。今音呼<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28017.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨀗</span>切，古音如貨。本命曰：陰以陽化，陽以陰變。故男以八月生齒，八歲而毀。女七月生齒，七歲而毀。毀與化義同音近。玄應書卷五：齔，舊音差貴切。卷十一：舊音羌貴切。然則古讀如未韵之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25F42.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥽂</span>，葢本从匕，匕亦聲。轉入寘至韵也。自誤从七旁。玄應云初忍切，孫愐云初堇切，廣韵乃初覲切，集韵乃初問、恥問二切。其形唐宋人又譌齓从乚，絕不可通矣。今當依舊音差貴切。古音葢在十七部。</span></p>                          </div>
