                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>跃</strong></p><p>躍 <span class="dicpy">yuè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，翟( <span class="dicpy">dí</span>)声。本义:跳跃)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[leap;jump]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跃，跳也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涌趯(跃)邪阴。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李寻传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“趯字与跃同。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骐骥一跃。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浮光跃金。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跃掷径出。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若将跃舞。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跃起持之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:跃龙门(比喻科举考试);跃如(踊跃的样子);跃治(比喻人好炫耀以求表现);跃马横戈(骑马奔跃，横握长戈);跃动(跳动);跃跃(跳动的样子。又指跳跃迅速的样子);跃跃欲试(心动而想尝试)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 上竖;上举 <span class="encs">[lift;ascend]</span>。如:跃龙(指皇帝登位)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>跃</strong></p><p>躍 <span class="dicpy">yuè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up
