<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p>

	<li>到，至：～今。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>暨</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从旦，既声。本义:太阳初升略现)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[part sun]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">暨，日颇见也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;颇，头偏也。头偏则不能全见其面，故谓事之略然者曰颇，日颇见者，见而不全也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 限制、遏制或限制的东西 <span class="encs">[limit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:靡暨</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>暨</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;连&gt;</p>
<p>和，与 <span class="encs">[and]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广州府</span><span class="diczx2">余</span>暨、 <span class="diczx2">南</span>、 <span class="diczx2">番</span>二县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:李先生暨夫人</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>暨</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈介〉</p>
<p>直到某时 <span class="encs">[till]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:暨今</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">暨</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上" target="_blank">辰集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上&amp;bs=日" target="_blank">日</a>】
                              暨·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/66A8.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2690C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦤌</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20568.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠕨</span>【唐韻】居豙切【集韻】居氣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音旣。【說文】日頗見也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】暨，與也。【書&middot;堯典】汝羲暨和。【傳】與也。</p>
<p>又【禹貢】朔南暨。【疏】皆與聞天子威聲文敎。【公羊傳&middot;隱元年】會及暨皆與也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;玉藻】戎容暨暨。【註】果毅貌也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋訓】暨，不及也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】至也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】諸暨，縣名，在越。</p>
<p>又【集韻】其旣切，音禨。義同。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>居乙切，音訖。姓也。吳有尙書暨豔。</p>
<p>又戟乙切，音疙。已也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】古文届字。註詳尸部五畫。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/66A8_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(暨)</span> <span class="ywyy">日頗見也。</span><span class="swdz">頗，頭偏也。頭偏則不能全見其面，故謂事之略然者曰頗。日頗見者，見而不全也。釋言曰：暨，不及也。此其引伸之義。乑部曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CFB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣳻</span>者，衆與詞也。引唐書<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CFB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣳻</span>咎繇。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CFB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣳻</span>之叚借多作洎，作暨。公羊傳曰：會及暨，皆與也。暨猶暨暨也。按暨暨猶幾幾，爾雅所謂不及也。</span><span class="ywyy">从旦旣聲。</span><span class="swdz">其冀切。十五部。旣，小食也。日不全見，故取其意，亦舉形聲包會意。</span></p>                          </div>
