                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">燕</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>燕</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>鸟类的一科，候鸟，常在人家屋内或屋檐下用泥做巢居住，捕食昆虫，对农作物有益：～尔（形容新婚夫妇亲睦和美的样子）。～好（常用以指男女相爱）。～侣。～雀处（<span class="dicpy">chǔ</span>）堂（喻居安而不知远虑，临祸却不能自知）。</li><li>轻慢：“～朋逆其师”。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">宴</span>”，安闲，安乐。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">宴</span>”，宴饮。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>燕</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>中国周代诸侯国名，在今河北省北部和辽宁省南部：～赵。～山。～京。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> swallow (bird); comfort, enjoy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ortsname  (S)​,Schwalbe  (S)​,Yan  (Eig, Fam)​,war ein chinesischer Staat während der Zeit der Zhou-Dynastie, der Zeit der Frühlings- und Herbstannalen und der Zeit der Streitenden Reiche. Seine Hauptstadt war Ji (chin. <a href="./薊">薊</a> , 蓟, Jì; heute Peking, auch bekannt als Yanjing, chin. <a href="./燕京">燕京</a>, Yānjīng „Hauptstadt von Yan“)​. ,hinunterschlucken, Schwalbe  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> hirondelle</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">燕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>燕</strong> <span class="dicpy">Yān</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Yan state]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 周代诸侯国名。本作:匽、郾。姬姓;周召公之后，世称北燕，拥有今河北省北部和辽宁省西端，建都蓟(今北京)。战国时成为七雄之一。后灭于秦</p><p><span class="diczx2">燕</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span><span class="diczx2">韩</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲亡</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>走 <span class="diczx2">燕</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">燕</span>畏 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx2">燕</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>之君。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">燕</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>之收藏， <span class="diczx2">韩</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>之经营， <span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>之精英。(互文，收藏、经营、精英均指金玉珍宝。)—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">燕</span>地寒，花朝节后，余寒犹厉。(燕，古代燕国，这里指北京地区。)—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 周代诸侯国名。姞姓;开国君主伯倏。在今河南省延津县东北</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 五代初，刘守光据幽州(今河北省北部)所建国</p><p><span class="diczx1">方其系</span><span class="diczx2">燕</span>父子以组。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新五代史·伶官传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">燕王</span>，吾所立。(燕王，指燕王刘守光的父亲刘仁恭。)—— <span
