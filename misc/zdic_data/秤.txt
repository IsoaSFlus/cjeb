                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>秤</strong> <span class="dicpy">chēng</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用秤量物之轻重 <span class="encs">[weigh]</span>。如:秤量(用秤衡计物体重量);秤薪量水(谓勤俭持家)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 权衡;衡量 <span class="encs">[weigh;measure]</span>。如:秤停(衡量斟酌)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">chèng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chèng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>秤</strong> <span class="dicpy">chèng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chèng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“称”</span>。利用杠杆原理量物体轻重的器具 <span class="encs">[steelyard]</span>。如:秤权(秤锤);秤等(用秤或戥子测量轻重，掂量);秤心(谓心无偏私，公平如秤);杆秤;市秤;磅秤;弹簧秤</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“称”</span>。中国古代重量单位。十五斤为一秤 <span class="encs">[7.5 kilogram]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俺有敕命一道，封</span><span class="diczx2">宋江</span>为镇国大将军，总领 <span class="diczx2">辽</span>兵大元帅，赐与金一提，银一秤，权当信物。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">chēng</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/秤锤">秤锤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/秤房">秤房</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/秤杆">秤杆</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/秤钩">秤钩</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/秤毫">秤毫</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/秤纽">秤纽</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/秤盘子">秤盘子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">稱</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“秤”字头，请参考“稱”字：)</span>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=禾">禾</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7A31.svg">                          <p>銓也。从禾爯聲。春分而禾生。日夏至，晷景可度。禾有秒，秋分而秒定。律數：十二秒而當一分，十分而寸。其以爲重：十二粟爲一分，十二分爲一銖。故諸程品皆从禾。處陵切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7A31_4541.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(稱)</span><span class="ywyy">銓也。</span><span class="swdz">銓者、衡也。聲類曰。銓所以稱物也。稱俗作秤。按爾、幷舉也。偁、揚也。今皆用稱。稱行而爾偁廢矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从禾。爯聲。</span><span class="swdz">處陵切。六部。廣韵又昌孕切。是也、等也。銓義之引伸。</span><span class="ywyy">春分而禾生。</span><span class="swdz">上文云。以二月生。</span><span class="ywyy">日夏至晷景可度。禾有秒。</span><span class="swdz">謂其時禾乃有芒也。</span><span class="ywyy">秋分而秒定。</span><span class="swdz">上文云。以八月孰。孰時芒乃定。</span><span class="ywyy">律數十二。</span><span class="swdz">句。十二謂六律六吕也。</span><span class="ywyy">十二秒而當一分。</span><span class="swdz">十二兩字舊奪。今補。下文云十髮爲程。一程爲分。十分爲寸。然則十二禾秒而當十髮。淮南天文訓作十二蔈而當一粟。</span><span class="ywyy">十分而寸。</span><span class="swdz">天文訓作十二粟而當一寸。</span><span class="ywyy">其㠯爲重。</span><span class="swdz">以衡輕重也。</span><span class="ywyy">十二粟爲一分。</span><span class="swdz">此粟謂禾粟。</span><span class="ywyy">十二分爲一銖。</span><span class="swdz">百四十四粟也。天文訓曰。十二粟而當一分。十二分而當一銖。十二銖而當半兩。衡有左右。因倍之。故二十四銖爲一兩。按金部銖下曰。權十分黍之重也。十分黍有譌。依此則當云十二分粟之重也。許用淮南說。與說苑、律歷志說異。</span><span class="ywyy">故諸程品皆从禾。</span><span class="swdz">度起於十二秒。權起於十二粟。諸程品之字、謂稱以下七篆也。此釋稱从禾之意。倂釋科以下六字从禾之意也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
