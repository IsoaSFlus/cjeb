                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>刘</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">劉</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">li&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄡˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> surname Liu, (classical)​ a type of battle-ax, to kill, to slaughter</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> löschen, töten  (V)​,Liu  (Eig, Fam)​,Liú, Familienname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刘</strong></p>
<p>劉 <span class="dicpy">li&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，从刀，丣( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)声。本义:杀，戮)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(含有大规模杀戮的意思) <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胜</span><span class="diczx2">殷</span>遏刘，耆代尔功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;武》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">重我民，无尽刘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚上》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:&ldquo;刘，杀也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span><span class="diczx2">卫</span>之间谓杀曰刘， <span class="diczx2">晋</span>之北鄙亦曰刘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>卷一</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">芟夷我农功，虔刘我边垂。(虔:杀戮)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 克，战胜 <span class="encs">[conquer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咸刘</span><span class="diczx2">商王</span><span class="diczx2">纣</span>，执矢恶臣百人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《逸周书&middot;世俘》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>刘</strong></p>
<p>劉 <span class="dicpy">li&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 兵器名。斧钺 <span class="encs">[weapon&#39;s name]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一人冕，执刘，立于东堂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;顾命》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:&ldquo;刘，钺属。&rdquo; <span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏引 <span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>曰:&ldquo;刘，盖今鑱斧。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>刘</strong></p>
<p>劉 <span class="dicpy">li&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p>剥落，凋残 <span class="encs">[wither]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5289.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕鎦<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B71.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠭱</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>力求切，音留。【說文】殺也。【書&middot;盤庚】重我民，無盡劉。【詩&middot;周頌】勝殷遏劉。【左傳&middot;成十三年】䖍劉我邊陲。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】劉，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也。【疏】謂敷<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】劉，㬥樂也。【疏】木枝葉稀疎不均爲㬥樂。【詩&middot;大雅】捋采其劉。【毛傳】劉，爆爍而希也。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋木】劉，劉杙。【註】劉子生山中。【疏】劉一名劉杙，其子可食。</p><p>又姓。【韻會】凡二十五望，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>自陶唐氏劉累之後。</p><p>又【集韻】力九切，留上聲。好也。</p><p>又【集韻】龍珠切，音鏤。殺也。漢禮，立秋有貙劉。</p><p>又【同文備考】作鐂。</p>
