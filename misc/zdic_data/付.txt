data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从寸。从<span class="yhcs">“寸”</span>，表示与手的动作有关。意思是:以手持物交付给别人。本义:给予)</p><p><span 又如:付法(传授佛法);付任(把事情交给别人去做);付治(交有司治罪);付推(交付有审问);付度(移交，交代);付命(授以天命)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 支付 <span class="encs">[expend;pay]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当下付了茶钱。——<span class="encs">[entrust]</span>。如:付仗(付托依靠);付属(托付);付嘱(吩咐;叮嘱);付授(嘱托授予)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“敷”</span>。涂;搽 <span class="encs">[apply]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">永不得着绮穿罗，再不能施朱付粉。——<span class="smcs">《金瓶梅词话》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“符”</span>( <span class="dicpy">fú</span>)。符合 <span class="encs">[keeping with;fit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">计凡付终，务本饬末，则富。——<span class="smcs">《管子·幼官》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“附”</span>。归附，附着 <span class="encs">[submit to the authority;stick to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皇天既付中国民。——<span class="smcs">《书·梓材》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">致德，其民和平以静;致道，其民付而不争。——<span
