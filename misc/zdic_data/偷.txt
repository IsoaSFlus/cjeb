						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>偷</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tōu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄡˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>窃取，趁人不知时拿人东西：～窃。～吃。小～儿。</li>
	<li>行动瞒着人：～～。～看。～听。～渡。～袭。～税。～天换日（喻暗中改变重大事物的真相以欺骗别人）。</li>
	<li>抽出时间：～空儿。～暇。～闲。</li>
	<li>苟且：～安。～生。～幸。～合苟容（苟且迎合别人的意思以求容身。亦称&ldquo;偷合取容&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">偷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>偷</strong></p>
<p>婾 <span class="dicpy">tōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，俞声。本义:苟且;马虎)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[drift along]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偷，苟且也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其下偷以幸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安肆曰偷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">存者且偷生。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偷性命于榛莽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸司素偷惰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:偷的锣儿敲不得(喻不能声张);偷存(偷生;苟活);偷合(苟且迎合);偷弛(苟且松弛);偷免(苟免);偷快(苟求快乐);偷幸(苟且侥幸);偷苟(苟且)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 浅薄;不厚道 <span class="encs">[dishonest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则民不偷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;泰伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:偷俗(浅薄的人情风俗);偷风(浅薄的社会风气);偷浅(浅薄)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>偷</strong> <span class="dicpy">tōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 轻视 <span class="encs">[despise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子大叔</span>、 <span class="diczx2">子羽</span>谓 <span class="diczx2">子产</span>曰:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">韩子</span>亦无几术， <span class="diczx2">晋国</span>亦未可以贰。 <span class="diczx2">晋国</span>、 <span class="diczx2">韩子</span>不可偷也。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 取 <span class="encs">[take]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柳占三春色，莺偷百鸟声。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">温庭筠</span><span class="smcs">《太子西池》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 窃取 <span class="encs">[pilfer;steal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偷，盗也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>有善为偷者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;道应训》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偷者，天下之盗也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">专操国柄以偷天下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;陈元传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:偷钱;偷东西;偷嘴;偷吃东西;从停车场偷了一辆车;从银柜偷钱;偷了配方，并开始自己制造这种产品</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 偷情;私通 <span class="encs">[commit adultery]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:偷香(谓女子爱悦男子或谓与妇女私通)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 突然地或急速地抽出 <span class="encs">[snatch]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:偷出右手来，就是一顿饱打</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 设法避免、不履行 <span class="encs">[某种义务]</span> <span class="encs">[evade]</span>。</p>
