                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>昏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>天刚黑的时候：黄～。～晓（朝夕。亦指明暗）。～定晨省（<span class="dicpy">xǐng </span>）（人子侍奉父母的日常礼节，晚上服侍就寝，早上省视问安）。</li><li>暗而无光：～暗。～黑。</li><li>惑乱：～愦。～聩。～庸。</li><li>神智不清楚或失去知觉：发～。～厥（亦称“晕厥”）。～乱。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">婚</span>”，婚姻。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>昏</strong></p><p>昬、曛 <span class="dicpy">hūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从日，从氐( <span class="dicpy">dǐ</span>)省。<span class="yhcs">“氐”</span>是下的意思。合起来表示日已下沉。本义:黄昏) 同本义 <span class="encs">[dusk;evening]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏，日冥也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日至于虞渊，是为黄昏。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·天文训》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏以为期。——<span class="smcs">《诗·陈风·东门之杨》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与曛黄以为期。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·思美人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在昼犹昏。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏晨之往来。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风雨晨昏。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:昏旦(黄昏和早上);昏晚(天刚黑);昏黄(暮色，天色暗淡)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>昏</strong> <span class="dicpy">hūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引申为昏暗;无光 <span class="encs">[dusky;dark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">秋天漠漠向昏黑。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">过午已昏。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:昏邓(昏暗的样子);昏邓邓(昏澄澄、昏昏瞪瞪。形容昏暗);昏曚(昏暗模糊);昏昏(昏暗;阴暗)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 目不明;昏花 <span class="encs">[dim-sighted]</span>。如:昏困(昏倦。头脑昏沉，精神倦怠);昏坎(昏花，模糊);昏腾(头昏眼花或头脑晕转)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 昏聩;糊涂 <span class="encs">[muddled;confused;dateless]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏迷不恭。——<span class="smcs">《书·大禹漠》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我独若昏。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远我昏乎?——<span class="smcs">《庄子·在宥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏微遵迹。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·天问》</span></span></p><p><span
