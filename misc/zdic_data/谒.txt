                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谒</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">謁</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄝˋ</span></p><ol><li>拜见：～见。拜～。进～。</li><li>说明，陈述，告发：请～其故。</li><li>请求：～归。～医。</li><li>名帖：“使者惧而失～，跪拾～”。</li><li>掌管晋见的近待。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谒</strong></p><p>謁 <span class="dicpy">yè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，曷( <span class="dicpy">hé</span>)声。本义:禀告，陈述)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[report (to one's superior)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谒，白也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谒，告也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摈者谒诸天子。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·觐礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">能典谒矣。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟我</span><span class="diczx2">郑国</span>之有请谒焉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公十一年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不谒而归。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣请谒其故。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:谒急(告急);谒言(进谏)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 告发;检举 <span class="encs">[inform against;lodge an accusation against]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伍、官、连、县而邻，谒过赏，失过诛。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·八经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:谒过(揭发罪过)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 请;请求 <span class="encs">[request;ask]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">微太子言，臣愿得谒之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:谒告(告假);谒归(告假归里);谒浆(乞求茶水)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 进见<span class="encs">[地位或辈分高的人]</span> <span class="encs">[call on(a superior or an older person)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谒，访也。——<span class="smcs">《增韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越日偃师谒见王。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父利其然也，日扳仲永环谒于邑人，不使学。(环谒，四处拜访)。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《伤仲永》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乡人子谒余。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谒</span><span class="diczx2">伊藤氏</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">台谒当以属礼。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:谒索(探望;借拜访之机索取财物);谒帝台(进见皇帝或权贵。喻指求官显达);谒选(官员到吏部等候选派);谒刺(谒见人时所用的名刺);谒禁(禁止人来进见);谒托(投见求托)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 到某人的陵墓表示敬意 <span class="encs">[pay homage at sb’s mausoleum]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">城外有新坟者，即往拜扫。禁中亦出车马诣道者院谒坟。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">孟元老</span><span class="smcs">《东京梦华录》</span></p><p><span                         </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              謁·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8B01.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>於歇切，堰入聲。【說文】白也。【爾雅·釋詁】告也，請也。【左傳·隱十一年】唯我鄭國之有請謁焉。【註】謁，告也。【禮·曲禮】問士之子，長曰能典謁矣，幼曰未能典謁也。【註】謁，請也。典謁者，主賔客告請之事。</p><p>又【增韻】訪也，請見也。【後漢·卓茂傳】茂詣河陽，謁見光武。</p><p>又【釋名】謁，詣也，詣告也。書其姓名於上，以告所至詣者也。【正字通】刺名也。古之門狀，今之拜帖。【史記·高祖紀】高祖乃紿爲謁曰：賀錢萬。實不持一錢。【註】謁，謂以札書姓名，若今之通刺。</p><p>又謁舍，今之客舍也。【前漢·食貨志】里區謁舍。</p><p>又官名。【前漢·百官公卿表】謁者，掌賔讚受事。【應劭註】謁，請也，白也，僕主也。【三輔黃圖】內謁者，署在未央宮，屬少府。</p><p>又星名。【宋史·天文志】謁者一星在左執法東北，主賔客，辨疑惑。</p><p>又山名。【山海經】謁戾之山多松柏，有金玉，沁水出焉。【註】山今在上黨郡涅縣。</p><p>又姓。【後漢·方術傳】汝南太守謁煥。</p><p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>乙列切，音䋵。亦白也。李舟說。</p><p>又【正韻】於蓋切，音愛。與䨠同。隂晦也。引韓愈秋雨聯句：牖變景明謁。一本作藹，義同。</p><p>又【揚雄·百官箴】殷以<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>顚，秦以酷敗。獄臣司理，敢告執謁。謁叶敗。</p><p>又【史記·孔子世家】去魯歌云：彼婦之謁，可以死敗。按此歌六句三韻，謁本音愛，與敗叶。【字彙】謁攺音意，敗叶音佩，誤。　【正字通】俗作謁，非。</p>
