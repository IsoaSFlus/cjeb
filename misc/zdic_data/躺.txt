                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>躺</strong> <span class="dicpy">tǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从身，尚声。本义:平卧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lie;recline]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">这无耻的畜生想必是躺尸了。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">忧患余生</span><span class="smcs">《邻女语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:躺在床上;躺着歇歇;躺尸(詈词。指睡觉);躺桥(方言。睡觉)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 停止劳动或努力 <span class="encs">[rest]</span>。如:不要躺在过去的成绩上睡大觉</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 引伸指物体平放或倒伏在地 <span class="encs">[lie flat]</span>。如:荒草躺倒在烂泥里</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 死的婉辞 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先母躺了下来，还是很热闹的。——<span class="smcs">《二十年目睹之怪现状》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/躺倒">躺倒</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/躺卧">躺卧</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/躺椅">躺椅</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
