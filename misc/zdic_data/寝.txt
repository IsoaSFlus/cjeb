                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寝</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>寝</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">寢</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>睡，卧：～室。～车。～宫。安～。</li><li>睡觉的地方：就～。寿终正～。</li><li>皇家宗庙后殿藏先人衣冠之处，亦指帝王的坟墓：～庙。陵～。</li><li>停止，平息：其议遂～（那种议论于是平息）。事～。</li><li>面貌难看：貌～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sleep, rest; bed chamber</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schlafen; etwas bleibt ruhen; Schlafzimmer; Totenkammer  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dormir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寝</strong></p><p>寢 <span class="dicpy">qǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀，侵声。本义:睡觉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sleep;lie]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寢，卧也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寝毋伏。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宰于昼寝。——<span class="smcs">《论语·公冶长》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡人夜者寝而不寐。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·僖公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暮寝而思之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">元济</span>尚寝。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寝必重褥。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既同寝。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:寝不成寐(睡不着。形容心事重);寝不安席(睡觉不能安予枕席。形容心事重);寝卧(睡眠息止);寝床(睡觉用的床);寝息(睡卧休息);寝衾(泛指睡眠所用之衣物);寝宿(睡卧宿息);寝饭(睡觉和吃饭);寝兴(睡下和起床);寝膳(寝食);寝燕(亦作<span class="yhcs">“寝宴”</span>。睡卧与休息)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 卧 <span class="encs">[lie]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">成子</span><span class="diczx2">高</span>寝疾， <span class="diczx2">庆</span>遗于请曰:<span class="yhcs">“子之病革矣。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:寝石(卧石，横躺着的石头);寝伏(卧伏);寝兕(卧着的独角犀。常画于帝王车轮上以壮威);寝病(卧病);寝疾(卧病);寝床(躺卧在床上)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 搁置 <span class="encs">[put]</span>。如:寝格(搁置;阻碍);寝遏(搁置;阻止);寝阁;寝置(犹搁置)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 停止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span>。如:寝谋(停止谋划，停止施行计划);寝罢(废除;停止);寝废(停止;废弃;废除)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 平息 <span class="encs">[put
