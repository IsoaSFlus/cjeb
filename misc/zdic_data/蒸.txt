                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蒸</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>热气上升：～汽。～腾。～发。～气（液体或固体因蒸发沸腾或升华而变成的气体）。～馏。云～霞蔚。～～日上。</li><li>用水蒸气的热力把东西加热或使熟：～饼。～饺。～笼。</li><li>古代以麻秸、竹木制成的火炬：～烛。</li><li>细小的木柴。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> steam; evaporate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> dämpfen, verdampfen, verdunsten  (V, Ess)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> s'évaporer,cuire à la vapeur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蒸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蒸</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。烝声。本义:麻秆)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hemp stalk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒸，析麻中干也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。今俗谓之麻骨棓，古烛用之，故凡用麻干葭苇竹木为烛皆曰蒸。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒸，炬也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故云烛用蒸也。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span>疏</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 细小的木柴 <span class="encs">[small firewood]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共祭祀之薪蒸材木。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·委人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“给炊及燎，粗者曰薪，细者曰蒸。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒸间容蒸然者处下。——<span class="smcs">《管子·弟子职》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“细薪也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以薪以蒸。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:蒸薪(木柴)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 以麻秸、竹木制成的火炬 <span class="encs">[torch]</span>。如:蒸烛(古指以麻秆、竹木等制成的火炬)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 中药炮制法之一 <span class="encs">[steaming]</span>。将药物隔水蒸熟，以便于制剂。如茯苓、厚朴蒸后易于切片。或加酒拌蒸，如大黄、地黄经蒸制后，熟大黄的泻下减弱，熟地黄便成温性而滋肾补血</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>蒸</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“烝”</span>。气体上升 <span class="encs">[evaporate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒸灵液以播云。——</span><span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《琴赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“气上貌。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阴阳陶蒸。——</span><span class="diczx2">张华</span><span class="smcs">《鹪鹩赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“气上山貌。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日气蒸云。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蒸樊(热气郁勃上升);蒸郁(湿气旺盛上升)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“烝”</span>。古代祭祀时以牲之全体置于俎上 <span class="encs">[sacrifice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冬祭曰蒸。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:蒸禋(祭祀)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 烧煮 <span class="encs">[steam]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">素汤米饭蒸卷馒头。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span>
