                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>弯</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">彎</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">wān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>屈曲不直：～曲。～度。～腰。拐～。转～。</li>
	<li>使曲：～弓（拉弓）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bend, curve</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> sich bücken  (V)​,sich beugen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> courbe,tordu,détour,virage,courber,plier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弯</strong></p>
<p>彎 <span class="dicpy">wān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:拉弓)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[draw (a bow)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弯，持弓关矢也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弯，引也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弯綦卫之箭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弯威弧之拔刺兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张衡&middot;思玄赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弯弹(拉弓射弹);弯弓(弯弧。拉弓);弯卒(弓兵);弯弓饮羽(形容勇猛善战)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 折，使弯曲 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">定睛再看时，却是</span><span class="diczx2">史大汉</span>弯跧蹲在东司边。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">冯梦龙</span><span class="smcs">《喻世明言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弯跧(身体蜷缩);弯转(展转搞到);弯踦(弯曲的河岸);弯躬(弯下身子);弯弯扭扭(弯曲;扭动);弯身拾禾;越过岩石海岸线向西弯去</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 停船靠岸，停泊 <span class="encs">[berth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大爷吩咐急急收了口子，弯了船。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>弯</strong></p>
<p>彎 <span class="dicpy">wān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 弯子 <span class="encs">[turn]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:转了一个小弯;急弯(道路突然转折的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;湾&rdquo;。水流弯曲之处 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">望别非新馆，开舟即旧弯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">北周</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《应令》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>弯</strong></p>
<p>彎 <span class="dicpy">wān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用于弯状物。如:一弯新月;一弯牛角弓</p>
