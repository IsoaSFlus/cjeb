                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>贳</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">貰</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>出租，出借：～器店（出租婚丧喜庆应用的某些器物的店铺）。</li>
	<li>赊欠：赊～。</li>
	<li>宽纵，赦免：～赦。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> borrow; pardon; loan</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> entleihen, leihen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> emprunter,acheter à crédit,louer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贳</strong></p>
<p>貰 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，世声。本义:借，借贷)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[borrow or lend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贳，贷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。受者曰赊，予者曰贳。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则赊贳而予之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司市》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民无贷(钱)，则赊贳而予之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司市》</span></span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贳代(借贷);贳贷(借贷;赊欠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 赊欠 <span class="encs">[buy or sell on credit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">县官无钱，从民贳马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;汲郑列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常从</span><span class="diczx2">王</span>媪、 <span class="diczx2">武</span>负贳酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贳酒(赊酒);贳账(赊账)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 租佃，租赁 <span class="encs">[hire out;rent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃贳贷陂田千余顷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;宁成传》</span>。注:&ldquo;假取之也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贳钱(租金);贳廛(租地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 抵押 <span class="encs">[mortgage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">蜀</span>江 <span class="diczx2">吴</span>水不通潮，曾共黄垆贳紫貂。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王士祯</span><span class="smcs">《遥送魏推官赴成都》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 买 <span class="encs">[buy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余乃就火炙衣，贳烧酒饮五杯乃行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 赦免，宽纵 <span class="encs">[pardon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因贳其罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张敞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不贳不忍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span>。注:&ldquo;赦也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂贳宽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刘向传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贳赦(赦免其罪);贳过(宽恕过错);贳患(免除祸患);贳忍(宽恕容忍);贳死(免死)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8CB0.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】舒制切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】始制切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音世。【說文】貸也。【廣雅】賒也。【史記&middot;高祖紀】常從王媼、武負貰酒。【前漢&middot;食貨志】諸賈人末作貰貸。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;文三王傳】得見貰赦。【註】師古曰：貰，謂寬其罪。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>神夜切，音射。</p>
<p>又【集韻】式夜切，音赦。義<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】時制切，音誓。漢侯國名。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶詩戈切。【白居易&middot;效陶詩】家醞飮已盡，村中無酒貰。坐愁今夜醒，其奈秋懷何。</p>
<p>補遺：【字彙補】與貰同。見《佩觿》。貰字上从卋作。</p>
