						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>叫</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiào.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 呼喊：～喊。～嚣（呼喊，吵闹）。～阵。～座（戏曲或演员能吸引观众，看的人多）。～苦不迭。鸣冤～屈。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">叫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>叫</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:呼，喊)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cry;shout]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叫，嘑也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或不知叫号。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·北山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叫我友兮配耦。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·疾世》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“急呼也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叫帝阍使辟扉兮。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戍卒叫，函谷举。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:叫命(骂人话。临死时呼喊声);叫聒(声音吵杂刺耳);叫号(大声呼喊);叫头(戏剧用语。剧中人物之一，在剧情悲痛、情急愤怒之时，高喊对方，称为叫头);叫曲(唱曲时没有蕴藉的韵味、感情)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 招唤，招呼 <span class="encs">[call;greet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或叫于</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>大庙。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“呼也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高声叫道:…——<span class="smcs">《鲁提辖拳打镇关西》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:叫喜(道喜);叫魂</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 鸣叫 <span class="encs">[sing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">候扇举而清叫。——</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《射雉赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“鸣也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百叫无绝。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">双双归蛰燕，一一叫群猿。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《晚泊江口》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:叫嗥(犹吼叫。指豺狼等野兽大声叫)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 诉说 <span class="encs">[complain]</span>。如:叫苦不迭(不断地叫苦);叫阍(旧时吏民因冤屈等原因向朝廷申诉称<span class="yhcs">“叫阍”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 名称是;称为 <span class="encs">[call;name]</span>。如:叫名(名字叫做;叫做)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 雇;到店里订购东西让送来 <span
