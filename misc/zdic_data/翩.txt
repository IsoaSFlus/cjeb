风流潇洒貌 <span class="encs">[admirable and unrestrained;elegant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妹发垂垂弟貌翩。——</span><span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室诗话》</span></p><p><span
