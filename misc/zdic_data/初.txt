                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>初</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>开始时，开始的部分：～始。正月～一。～旬。～稿。～等。～版。～试。～创。～绽。～叶（指某一历史时期的最初一段）。～夜（a.指进入夜晚不久的时候；b.指新婚第一夜）。</li><li>原来的，原来的情况：～衷。和好如～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">初</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>初</strong> <span class="dicpy">chū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从刀，从衣。合起来表示:用刀剪裁衣服是制衣服的起始。本义:起始，开端)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[at the beginning;in the early part of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初，始也。裁者衣之始也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初，舒也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span>。按，谓展帛以就裁。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初吉终乱。——<span class="smcs">《易·既济》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">名曰初虑。——<span class="smcs">《易·书大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我生之初。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·兔爰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初疾畏惊，见鬼之来。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·订鬼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初极狭，才通人。复行数十步，豁然开朗。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:初岁(年初);初枯(秋季里植物开始枯萎);初头(起初;开头);初化(变化之始);初末(始末)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 本原 <span class="encs">[nature]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲知学之力，贤愚同一初。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈<span class="smcs">《</span>符读书城南》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>初</strong> <span class="dicpy">chū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示次序居第一 <span class="encs">[the first in order]</span>。如: 初度(生日);初鼓(一更);初入芦苇(比喻初入仕途);初民(上古时代的百姓)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 本，本来的 <span class="encs">[original]</span>。如: 初愿;初志;初心(本意，最初的心意)</p><p>◎ <strong>初</strong> <span class="dicpy">chū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 往昔;当初 <span class="encs">[in former times，in the past;before]</span></p><p><span                     <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>初</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄔㄨ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>chū <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4802.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">根本、本原。</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子·俶真》：「是故圣人之学也，欲以返性于<b>初</b>，而游心于虚也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷四七·孔子世家》：「进取不忘其<b>初</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">起源、开始。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「年<b>初</b>」、「月<b>初</b>」、「和好如<b>初</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·柳宗元〈封建论〉：「天地果无<b>初</b>乎？吾不得而知之也。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如宋代有<b><b>初</b></b>暐。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">从前、原来。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「初衷」、「初心」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">最先、开头。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「初秋」、「初民」、「初雪」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">最低的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「初级」、「初等」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">阴历每月一日到十日，皆冠上「<b><b>初</b></b>」字，表上旬的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「初一」、「<b>初</b>二」。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">第一次的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「初恋」、「初学」、「初试啼声」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">甫、刚刚。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「初出茅庐」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《书经·召诰》：「呜呼！若生子，罔不在厥<b>初</b>生，自贻哲命。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷四九·外戚世家》：「天下<b>初</b>定未久，继嗣不明。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">全、始终。用于否定句，表范围。</span><span class="gc_yy">《后汉书·卷八一·独行传·彭修传》：「受教三日，<b>初</b>不奉行，废命不忠，岂非过邪？」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
