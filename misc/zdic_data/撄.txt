						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>撄</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">攖</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>接触，触犯：～怒。～其锋。</li><li>扰乱，纠缠：“不以人物利害相～”。～宁（道家所追求的一种修养境界，指心神宁静，不被外界事物所扰）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>撄</strong></p><p>攖 <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，婴声。本义:扰乱，干扰)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[disturb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不以人物利害相撄。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·庚桑楚》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:撄宁(外物的扰乱);撄心(扰乱心神)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 缠绕 <span class="encs">[twine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勿挠勿撄，万物将自称。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高诱</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“撄，缨也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:撄城(环城);撄挽(纠缠)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 触犯 <span class="encs">[offend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杀害既多，必撄鬼神之怒。——<span class="smcs">《绿野仙踪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:撄怒(触怒);撄拂(触犯，拂逆);撄鳞(喻触怒帝王)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 触 <span class="encs">[touch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事势至此，陛下且忍之，不可撄其锋也。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:撄宁(接触外物而不为所动，保持心神宁静);撄锋(触碰锋镝)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 向…挑战或撩斗 <span class="encs">[challenge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虎负嵎，莫之敢撄。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
