                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辣</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>辣</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>像姜、蒜等的剌激性味道：～椒（一年生草木植物，果实有毛笔尖形、灯笼形等，青色，成熟后变成红色。可食，亦可入药。亦称&ldquo;辣子&rdquo;）。辛～。～乎乎。酸甜苦～。</li>
	<li>辣味刺激：～眼睛。</li>
	<li>凶狠，刻毒：毒～。泼～。手段～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> peppery, pungent, hot; cruel</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> scharf  (Adj, Ess)​,scharfer Geschmack</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pimenté,piquant,âcre,cruel</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>辣</strong></p>
<p>辢 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辛，剌( <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> )省声。本义:辛味)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。姜、蒜、辣椒等带刺激性的味道 <span class="encs">[hot;peppery;sharp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辢，辛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辛甚曰辣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">江</span>南曰辣，中国曰辛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《声类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酸甜苦辣;辣菜根子(辣菜的根很辣，喻非常泼辣);辣拐子(辣手的家伙。骂人的话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姜、蒜等含辣味的菜蔬 <span class="encs">[pungent vegetable]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:一如辣末</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 痛;伤痛 <span class="encs">[pain]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:空气使人鼻子里老是作辣，温度无疑是在零下</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>辣</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 猛烈、凶狠 <span class="encs">[ruthless;cruel]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:辣躁(性情凶悍，脾气暴躁);辣浪(浪荡;放荡);辣手狠心(手段凶狠，心肠恶毒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 高温，酷热 <span class="encs">[hot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太阳辣得如火烧，庄稼晒得枯焦焦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白族民歌集》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>辣</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> 辣味刺激 <span class="encs">[bite;sting]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辣椒虽辣，辣不死人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《伪自由书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:切葱头辣眼睛</span></p>
