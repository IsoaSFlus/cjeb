<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> meet or see unexpectedly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> begegnen, treffen  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rencontrer</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>觏</strong></p><p>覯 <span class="dicpy">gòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从见，冓( <span class="dicpy">gōu</span>)声。本义:遇，遇见;看见)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[meet with;see]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">觏，遇见也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亦既觏止。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·草虫》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我觏之子。——诗·豳风·伐柯》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃觏于京。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·公刘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忧心悄悄，愠于群小。觏闵既多，受侮不少。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·柏舟》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:觏闵(遭遇不幸);觏晤(会面);觏面(见面);觏止(相遇)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 遭遇 <span class="encs">[encounter;meet with]</span>。如:觏侮(遭受欺侮)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 构成。通<span class="yhcs">“构”</span> <span class="encs">[form]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郇瑕氏</span>土薄水浅，其恶易觏。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公六年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:觏恶(构成疾疢)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/89AF.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古后切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音遘。【說文】遇見也。【詩·召南】亦旣覯止。</p><p>又【豳風】我覯之子。</p><p>又成也。【左傳·成六年】郇瑖氏土薄水淺，其惡易覯。【疏】言其病易成，由水土惡故也。</p><p>又【正字通】與遘通。長箋同逅。</p><p>又【集韻】下遘切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>。義同。</p><p>又【集韻】胡溝切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>平聲。亦同逅。邂逅，解說貌。</p><p>又【集韻】古項切，音講。與顜同。【史記·曹相國世家】顜若畫一。【註】或从見。通作講。</p><p>又【集韻】訖岳切，講入聲。亦同顜。通作較。</p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">遇見也。</span>覯與遇㬪韵。辵部曰。遘、遇也。覯从見、則爲逢遇之見。召南草蟲曰。亦旣見止。亦旣覯止。傳曰。覯、遇也。此謂覯同遘。鄭箋云。旣覯謂已昏也。引易男女覯精。萬物化生。鄭意以覯卽見。無俟重言。毛云遇也。實含會合之義。故引而伸之。必俟脫纓燭出。昏禮旣成。乃自信可以寧父母心。此申毛、非異毛也。鄭所據易作覯精。今皆作構。葢失之矣。<span class="jieshi">从見。冓聲。</span>古后切。四部。                          </div>
