                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">领</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>领</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">領</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄥˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>颈，脖子：引～而望。</li>
	<li>衣服上围绕脖子的部分：衣～。～口。～结。～带。～章。</li>
	<li>事物的纲要：～袖。要～。提纲挈～。</li>
	<li>带，引，率（<span class="dicpy">shu&agrave;i</span>）：带～。率（<span class="dicpy">shu&agrave;i</span>）～。～头。～衔。</li>
	<li>治理的，管辖的：～海。～空。～土。～域。占～。</li>
	<li>接受，取得：～奖。～命。～款。～教（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o </span>）。招～。</li>
	<li>了解，明白：～悟。～略。心～神会。</li>
	<li>量词，用于衣服、席、箔等：一～席。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/岭">岭</a>&rdquo;，山岭。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">领</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>领</strong></p>
<p>領 <span class="dicpy">lǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>)，令声。从&ldquo;页&rdquo;，表示与头有关。本义:脖子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[neck]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">领，项也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">领，项也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释亲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">领如蝤蛴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;硕人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缅然引领南望。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及君之嗣也，我君</span><span class="diczx2">景公</span>引领西望曰:&ldquo;庶抚我乎!&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:领门儿(衣领;领口);领鬃(鬃毛);领颚(下巴);领腰(脖子与腰部);领圈(项圈)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 领子，衣领 <span class="encs">[collar;neckband]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若挈裘领。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span>。又如:领抹(领系、领戏。外衣领上的系巾);领口;领盘儿(托领);领章;领结</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 要领 <span class="encs">[outline;main point]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:提纲挈领</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 山道，山坡。后作&ldquo;岭&rdquo; <span class="encs">[mountain ridge; slope]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舆轿而逾领。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;严助传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>领</strong></p>
<p>領 <span class="dicpy">lǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 统领，居上领导指挥 <span class="encs">[lead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">各领万人，与</span><span class="diczx2">备</span>俱进。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴主传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:领盘(主持定婚仪式);领家的(妓院老鸨);领护(统治);领御(统率支配);领衔;领袖</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 带领;引导 <span class="encs">[lead;guide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">识以领之，方能中鹄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《续诗品》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:领道(在前引路);领首(带头);领工(带领从事劳动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 兼任 <span class="encs">[hold concurrent posts]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为尚书，领行营节度使。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《段太尉逸事状》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 治理 <span class="encs">[administer]</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=頁" target="_blank">頁</a>】
                              領·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9818.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】良郢切【集韻】【正韻】里郢切【韻會】里整切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音嶺。【說文】項也。从頁令聲。【釋名】領，頸也。以壅頸也。亦言總領衣體，爲端首也。【詩&middot;小雅】交交桑扈，有鶯其領。【傳】鶯鶯然有文章也。領，頸也。【荀子&middot;勸學篇】若挈裘領。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】理也。【韻會】方氏曰：承上令下謂之領。【禮&middot;祭義】先王之敎因而弗改，所以領天下國家也。</p>
<p>又【樂記】領父子君臣之節。【註】領，猶理治也。</p>
<p>又【仲尼燕居】言游進曰：敢問禮也者，領惡而全好者歟。【註】領，猶治也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】統領也。【前漢&middot;魏相傳】總領庶職。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】錄也。【劉公幹詩】沈速簿領書。【註】領，錄也。</p>
<p>又楚官名。【左傳&middot;昭二十七年】鄢將師爲右領。</p>
<p>又晉官名。【晉書&middot;職官志】中領軍將軍，魏官也。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;昭二十二年】樊頃子奉王以追單子及領。【註】領，周地。【前漢&middot;王子侯表】蒲領侯嘉。</p>
<p>又山道，與嶺通。【前漢&middot;嚴助傳】輿轎而踰領。</p>
<p>又【正字通】受也。</p>
<p>又叶離貞切，音鄰。【韓愈&middot;田氏廟<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>】嶪嶪魏土，嬰兒弄兵。吏戎愁毒，莫保首領。</p>
