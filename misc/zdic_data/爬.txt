						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>爬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄚˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>手和脚一齐着地走路，虫类行走：～行。～虫（爬行动物）。～泳。</li>
	<li>攀登：～高。～升。～山。往上～（讽刺追求功名的人）。</li>
	<li>搔：～痒。～搔（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">搔</span>&rdquo;读轻声）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> crawl, creep; climb; scramble</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufsteigen  (V)​,ersteigen, steigen  (V)​,klettern auf  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> avancer en s'aidant des mains,ramper,grimper,escalader</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>爬</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从爪，巴声。爪，手爪。本义:搔，爬梳)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[scratch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爬，搔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怯烦类决痈，惬兴剧爬疥。&mdash;&mdash;韩愈等<span class="smcs">《雨中寄孟刑部几道联句》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">支分闲事了，爬背向阳眠。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《自咏老身示诸家属》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:爬挲(爬搔，摩挲);爬栉(梳理，整理。同爬梳);爬耳搔腮(急切无奈貌);爬拉(频频拨饭菜入口)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 爬行，匍匐。躯体贴地(如虫、蛇、龟)缓慢向前行 <span class="encs">[crawl;creep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爬沙脚手钝，谁使女解缘青冥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《月蚀诗效玉川子作》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:爬蹉(来回爬动);爬沙(缓慢地爬动);爬坑缸弗上(屎尿失控，来不及上厕所;离死不远);爬滩弗动(趴在地上，无法走动)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 攀登 <span class="encs">[climb;clamber;scramble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他们为了出风头，向上爬，却把我们踩在脚底下!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《星火》</span>1979年第二期</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:爬树;爬上高枝儿(依附地位高的人);爬得越高，跌得越惨</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 俯伏 <span class="encs">[lie prostrate]</span>。如:爬蛋(方言。产仔);爬窝(方言。谓牲畜卧圈不起);爬伏(趴下，伏倒)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 整治 <span class="encs">[renovate]</span>。如:爬栉(整治清理);爬剔(剔除，挑剔)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 疏导;发掘 <span class="encs">[dredge]</span>。如:爬罗(发掘搜罗);爬棺材黄鼠狼(盗墓贼)</p>
