                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>战</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">戰</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>打仗：～争。～机。～绩。～略。～术。～国（我国历史上的一个时代）。</li>
	<li>泛指争斗，比高下：论～。争～。</li>
	<li>发抖：～抖。寒～。胆～心惊。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">战</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>战</strong></p>
<p>戰 <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从戈，单(占)声。本义:作战，打仗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[battle;war]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">战，斗也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆陈曰战。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春秋敌者言战。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;庄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忠之属也，可以一战。战则请从。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将军百战死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脱我战时袍。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挟矢以助战。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">技击利巷战。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">普</span><span class="diczx2">法</span>交战图。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时北兵已迫修门外，战、守、迁皆不及施。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:征战(出征作战);战阀(战功);战垒(战争中用以防守的堡垒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指搏斗，争斗，争胜负，比高低 <span class="encs">[fight]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">龙战于野。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">下骑搏战。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:文战;酒战;西瓜战</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 后作&ldquo;颤&rdquo;。发抖 <span class="encs">[shiver]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">战栗，惧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">股战而栗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高五王传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使民战栗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;八佾》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">战战兢兢，如临深渊，如履薄冰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小旻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻鸣镝而股战。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
