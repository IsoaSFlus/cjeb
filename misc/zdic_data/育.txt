						<p>● <strong>育</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>生养：～龄。节～。生儿～女。</li>
	<li>养活：～婴。哺～。培～。抚～。养～。</li>
	<li>按照一定的目的长期地教导和训练：德～。智～。体～。美～。教书～人。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">育</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>育</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象妇女生孩子。上为&ldquo;母&rdquo;及头上的装饰，下为倒着的&ldquo;子&rdquo;。)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[give birth to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">育，生也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无遗育。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至如今不育。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子孙蕃育之谓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妇孕不育，失其道也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;渐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:节育(节制生育);育孕(怀胎足月以至分娩);生儿育女</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 抚养;教育 <span class="encs">[raise;bring up;foster]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">育，养子使作善也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">教育子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载生载育，时维后稷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;生民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:育女(养女);育材(育才。培养人才);育德(培养德性);德育(政治思想和道德品质的教育);智育;体育</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yō</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>育</strong> <span class="dicpy">yō</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &mdash;&mdash; 见&ldquo;杭育&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ngyō</span>)</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">育</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下" target="_blank">未集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下&amp;bs=肉" target="_blank">肉</a>】
                              育·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/80B2.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>余六切，音昱。【說文】養子使作善也。【廣韻】養也。【易&middot;蒙卦】君子以果行育德。【註】育德者，養正之功也。【疏】育養其德。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】育，長也。【詩&middot;衞風】旣生旣育。【箋】育謂長老也。【書&middot;盤庚】我乃劓殄滅之，無遺育。【傳】育，長也。【晉語】正名育類。【註】育，長也。</p>
<p>又生也。【禮&middot;中庸】發育萬物。【註】育，生也。</p>
<p>又覆育也。【詩&middot;小雅】長我育我。【箋】育，覆育也。</p>
<p>又幼稚也。【詩&middot;衞風】昔育恐育鞠。【箋】昔育，昔幼稚之時也。</p>
<p>又地名。【前漢&middot;地理志】育陽。【註】屬南陽郡。</p>
<p>又【集韻】亦姓。</p>
<p>又【集韻】直祐切，音胄。胤也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】通作鬻。【詩&middot;幽風】鬻子之閔斯。【禮&middot;樂記】毛者孕鬻。【註】鬻，生也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作毓。【前漢&middot;五行志】孕毓根荄。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶音亦。【詩&middot;大雅】載生載育。叶上夙下稷。夙音夕。</p>
<p>又叶音乙。【黃伯仁龍馬頌】稟神祇之化靈，乃大宛而再育。資<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>螭之表象，似靈虯之注則。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/80B2.svg">                          <p>養子使作善也。从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AD3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠫓</span>肉聲。《虞書》曰：教育子。毓，育或从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AED.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣫭</span>。余六切〖注〗徐鍇曰：&ldquo;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AD3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠫓</span>，不順子也。不順子亦教之，况順者乎？&rdquo;</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/80B2_5231.svg" /><span class="swbf">(育)</span><span class="ywyy">養子使作善也。</span><span class="swdz">孟子曰：中也養不中，才也養不才。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AD3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠫓</span>，</span><span class="swdz">不从子而从倒子者，正謂不善者可使作善也。</span><span class="ywyy">肉聲。</span><span class="swdz">余六切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">虞書曰：</span><span class="swdz">虞書當作唐書。說在禾部。</span><span class="ywyy">敎育子。</span><span class="swdz">堯典文。今尙書作冑子。攷鄭注王制作冑，注周官大司樂作育，王肅注尙書作冑。葢今文作育，古文作冑也。釋言曰：育，稚也。故史記作敎稚子。邠風毛傳亦曰：鬻子，稚子也。稚者當養以正。二義實相因。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/80B2_5232.svg" /><span class="swbf">(毓)</span><span class="ywyy">育或从每。</span><span class="swdz">周禮、周易蒙卦皆作此字。每，艸盛也。養之則盛矣。</span></p>                          </div>
