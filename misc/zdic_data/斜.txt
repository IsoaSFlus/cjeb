                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>斜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>不正，跟平面或直线既不平行也不垂直的：～坡。～面。～度。～线。～视。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> slanting, sloping, inclined</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span>  lehnen  (V)​,schief, schräg, geneigt  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> oblique,de travers</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">斜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>斜</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从斗，余声。本义:用斗倒出)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 舀出 <span class="encs">[ladle out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斜，抒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斜酒(舀酒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 侧着移动，向偏离正中或正前方的方向移动 <span class="encs">[edge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">门子才斜签着坐下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《葫芦僧判断葫芦案》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">飞腾暮景斜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《杜位宅守岁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斜签(侧着身子);斜躺(斜着下垂)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>斜</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不正，歪斜 <span class="encs">[oblique;slanting;inclined;tilted]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明月斜挂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斜风细雨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张志和</span><span class="smcs">《渔歌子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">青山郭外斜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孟浩然</span><span class="smcs">《过故人庄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟斜雾横。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斜月三星(心字的隐喻);斜射路(抄近路在田中踩踏出来的斜道);斜柯(倾侧;敲侧);斜刺(斜刺里);斜径(歪斜的小路)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;邪&rdquo;。不正当，不正派 <span class="encs">[evil]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:斜局(指行止心态不正常);斜气(邪气，不正之气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同&ldquo;邪&rdquo;。不专诚 <span class="encs">[heretical]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又想:&ldquo;师父万一心斜，走到西方，亦无用处。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>斜</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>巷 <span class="encs">[lane]</span></p>
