                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>抉</strong> <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，夬( <span class="dicpy">guài</span>)声。本义:挑出，挖出)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pickle out;gouge out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抉，挑也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抉人之口。——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抉吾眼。——<span class="smcs">《史记·伍胥传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因自皮面抉眼，自屠出肠。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·韩策二》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:抉目(挖出眼睛);抉眼(挖出眼珠);抉耳(挖清耳朵);抉发(发掘);抉微(谓发掘事物的隐微)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挑开;撬开 <span class="encs">[pry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">郰人纥抉之以出门者。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“撅也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:抉关(撬开城门或关口的大门);抉拾(古代射箭用具。抉，即扳指，戴于右指上，用以钩弦。拾，即皮护臂)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 挑剔;揭发 <span class="encs">[nitpick;expose]</span>。如:抉撾(挑剔;揭发);抉破(揭破，戳破);抉露(揭露)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 掰，用手指使分开 <span class="encs">[break off with the fingers and thumb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">那半个一人抉一块儿，分着吃了。——<span class="smcs">《新儿女英雄传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 挑选;选取 <span class="encs">[choose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先生之作，遇景入咏，不拘奇抉异。——</span><span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《郢州孟亭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:抉剔(摘取剔出);抉撾(抉择;择取);抉摘(选取，揭发);抉搜(搜求挑取);抉挑(挑动)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>抉</strong> <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>扳指 <span class="encs">[thimble]</span>。古代射箭时套在右手拇指上，用以钩弦</p><p><span class="diczx1">百夫抉拾。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">矰弋抉拾。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·缮人》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div
