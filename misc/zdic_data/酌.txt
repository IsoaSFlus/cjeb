                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">酌</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>酌</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhuó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)，从勺，勺亦声。从<span class="yhcs">“酉”</span>与酒有关。本义:斟酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pour out liquor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酌，盛酒行觞也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尸升，坐取爵酌。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·有司彻》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酒曰清酌。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缩酌用茅。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·郊特牲》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盖酌之也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·僖公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及祭，酌仆。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·夏官》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引壶觞以自酌。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因命酌互举。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以我酌油知之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以钱覆其口，徐以杓酌油沥之。——</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《卖油翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无多酌我，我乃酒狂。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·盖宽饶传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:酌献(斟酒以献;酌酒献客;酌酒祭神);酌羽(酌酒。羽，羽觞之省，代指酒);酌言(酌酒。言，助词);酌醴(酌酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 饮酒 <span class="encs">[drink]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">花间一壶酒，独酌无相亲。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《月下独酌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引壶觞以自酌，眄庭柯以怡颜。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《归去来辞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:酌别(饮酒告别);酌杯(饮酒);酌鲁(饮薄酒);酌霞(比喻饮美酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 斟酌，择善而行 <span class="encs">[weigh and consider]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酌今昔而减之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:酌议(斟酌商议);酌责(酌情责罚);酌从(酌情顺从);酌古御今(择古之善者以为治今的借鉴);酌古准今(择取古代之事，用来比照今天的情况);酌估(估计);酌度(酌量;度量)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 舀取 <span class="encs">[ladle out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以杓酌油沥之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:酌饮(挹取流质食物而饮);酌取(挹取，舀取)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>酌</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhuó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 酒的代称 <span class="encs">[wine;liquor]</span></p><p><span
