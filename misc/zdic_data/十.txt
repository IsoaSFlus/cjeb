                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>十</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˊ</span></p><ol><li>数名，九加一（在钞票和单据上常用大写“<span class="diczx3">拾</span>”代）。</li><li>表示多、久：～室九空。</li><li>表示达到顶点：～足。～成。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ten, tenth; complete; perfect</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> zehn</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dix,10</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">十</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>十</strong> <span class="dicpy">shí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>［数］</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。甲骨文象用一根树枝代表十，金文象是结绳记数，用一个结表示十。后来一点变成了一横。本义:九加一的和)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ten]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十，数之具也。—为东西，|为南北，则四方中央备矣。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十是数之小成。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公四年》</span>疏</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十亩之间兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·六月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵也者，审于地图，谋十官。——<span class="smcs">《管子·七法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下欲反，十室九空。——</span><span class="diczx2">葛洪</span><span class="smcs">《抱朴子·用刑》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与吾祖居者，今其室十无一焉。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:十里长亭(古时设在路旁的亭子，常用作送别饯行之处);十番(合奏乐以十种乐器演奏的名称);十牌(十户人家。牌:门牌);十地(佛教称菩萨修行渐近于佛的十种境界);十生九(死的歇后语);十方地面(庙宇。佛教指东、西、南、北、东南、西南、东北、西北和上下);十方常住(寺庙。佛教认为<span class="yhcs">“不变”</span>为常住。寺庙是不变的，故称);十有九就(十成之中有九成可望成功)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指十分;十份 <span class="encs">[topmost]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">比好游者尚不能十一。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:十一(十分之一);十半(十分之五)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 表示约数。犹言十来个 <span class="encs">[about ten]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辱十城之虚寿，奄</span><span class="diczx2">咸阳</span>以取隽。——<span class="smcs">《文选·潘岳·西征赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 特指十倍 <span class="encs">[tenfold]</span>。如:十伯(十倍百倍)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 序数的第十位 <span class="encs">[tenth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请以</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>十五城为 <span class="diczx2">秦王</span>寿。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:十五删(诗韵中上平声第十五个韵目);十四寒(诗韵中上平声第十四个韵目);十三元(依据<span class="smcs">《佩文诗韵》</span><span class="yhcs">“元”</span>字列在上平声第十三位，故称)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>十</strong> <span class="dicpy">shí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示多;齐全;完备 <span class="encs">[many;full;complete]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九十其仪。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·东山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十目所视，十手所指，其严乎!——<span class="smcs">《礼记·大学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:十郎八当(落花流水，七零八落);十变五化(变化多端);十亲九眷(众多亲戚);十相具足(十分美貌);十相俱足(娇妻美妾俱全);十数(十多;十几，表示众多);十荡十决(多次冲击均能破敌);十万(数目。又形容数量极多);十恶五逆(种种不可赦免的大罪);十尧(圣人众多);十朋(许多朋友);十有八九(绝大多数)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“什”</span>。杂 <span class="encs">[miscellaneous]</span>。如:十锦;十样锦(有各种不同花纹的织锦)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
