                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">徐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>徐</strong> <span class="dicpy">xú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从彳( <span class="dicpy">chì</span>)，余声。双人旁与行走有关。本义:慢步走)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[walk slowly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徐，安行也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃徐有说。——<span class="smcs">《易·困》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“坤为徐。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徐其攻而留其日。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·宋策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故其疾如风，其徐如林。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·军争》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摄提运衡，徐至于射宫。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张衡·东京赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 散开 <span class="encs">[spread]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">執徐之岁，岁早旱，晚水。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>徐</strong> <span class="dicpy">xú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 缓慢 <span class="encs">[gently;slowly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不徐不疾，得之于手而应于心。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入而</span><span class="diczx2">徐</span>趋。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此事当徐议之。——<span class="smcs">《宋史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徐有得也。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《朱文公校昌黎先生集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徐而察之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余韵徐歇。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徐以杓酌油沥之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">清风徐来，水波不兴。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《前赤壁赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:徐议(从容商议);清风徐来;徐回(缓慢地回旋运转);徐行(缓慢前行);徐步(缓慢步行);徐呼(发声舒缓的弱音);徐疾(或慢或快)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 安闲的样子 <span class="encs">[leisurely]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宜为人主，安徐而重固。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:徐言(缓言;低声细语);徐婉(舒缓委婉);徐详(舒缓安详)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 全，都。通<span class="yhcs">“俱”</span> <span class="encs">[all]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>人徐伤 <span class="diczx2">归父</span>之无后也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·成公十五年》</span></p><p>◎ <strong>徐</strong> <span class="dicpy">Xú</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古州名。古徐州的简称 <span class="encs">[Xuzhou]</span>，古九州之一。古徐州约在今江苏、山东、安徽的部分地区。汉以后各代皆置徐州，辖地常有变更，大致都在今淮北一带。多以彭城(今江苏徐州市)或下邳(今江苏邳县)为治所</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Xu state]</span></p><p><span
