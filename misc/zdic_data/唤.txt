                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">唤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>唤</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，奂( <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:呼，叫，)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[call out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">唤，呼也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不闻爷娘唤女声。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">千呼万唤始出来。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:唤门(在门外叫内边的人开门);唤应(呼应，照应);唤字叫背(掷钱猜正反面赌博)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 呼唤使来 <span class="encs">[call up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虑诸公不奉诏，于是先唤</span><span class="diczx2">周侯</span>、丞相入。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·方正》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:唤取(呼请);唤集(召集);唤铁(一种响铁器，敲击时可以招唤鸟兽)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 传唤 <span class="encs">[sumnon]</span>。如:唤问(传唤讯问);唤质(传唤质对);唤审(传唤审讯)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 叫做 <span class="encs">[be called]</span>。如:唤作(叫做，称做);唤做(叫做)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 啼叫，禽鸟发出叫声 <span class="encs">[crow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东方欲明星烂烂，汝南晨鸡登坛唤。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 以为 <span class="encs">[think;believe;consider]</span>。如:唤则(当做，以为)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/唤起">唤起</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/唤头">唤头</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/唤醒">唤醒</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
