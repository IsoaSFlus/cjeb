                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>峭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiào.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄠˋ</span></p><ol><li>山又高又陡：陡～。～壁。峻～。～立。～丽（a.陡峭而多姿；b.文辞遒劲华美）。～拔。</li><li>形容严峻：～寒。～急。～劲。～厉。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> steep, precipitous, rugged</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hoch und steil  (Adj)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">峭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>峭</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，肖声。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:从阜。本义:陡直)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[steep]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">峭，陵也。从阜，肖声。斗直曰峭。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">峭，高也。字亦作峭。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上峭山。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·脩务》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涧深，峭如墙。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·内储说上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">峭拔险怪。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高岩峭壁。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">峭拔秀丽。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">峭壁拔起。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">峭壑阴森。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:峭拔(特立高耸);峭岸(陡岸);峭谷(险峻的山谷);峭坂(高陡的山坡);峭卓(高峻陡直);峭岫(陡峭的峰峦);峭削(陡峭如削);峭峙(耸立);峭峭(高峻，高)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挺拔;直立而高耸 <span class="encs">[tall and straight]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白浪如山那可渡，狂风愁杀峭帆人。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《横江词》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">披林入峭茜，攀磴陟崔嵬。——</span><span class="diczx2">张说</span><span class="smcs">《过蜀道山》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:峭帆(耸立的船帆。亦借指驾船);峭危(高耸陡峭);峭崛(刚健挺拔);峭耸(高耸;陡峭);峭秀(挺拔秀丽)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 严峻 <span class="encs">[stern]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">错</span>为人峭直刻深。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·晁错传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宰相刻削(峭)。——<span class="smcs">《四子讲德论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:峭直(严峻刚直);峭鲠(严峻刚直);峭冷(严峻冷漠);峭刑(严刑);峭法(严峻的法令);峭洁(严峻高洁)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 苛刻，严酷 <span class="encs">[harsh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冷露滴梦破，峭风梳骨寒。——</span><span class="diczx2">孟郊</span><span class="smcs">《秋怀》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:冷峭(冷气逼人;比喻为人刻薄，话语尖刻);峭险(严酷阴险);峭薄(刻薄)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 高超不凡 <span class="encs">[outstanding]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西岳高僧名贯休，孤情峭拔凌清秋。——</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳炯</span><span
