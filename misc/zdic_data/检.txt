                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>检</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">檢</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>查：～查。～测。～讨。～举。～校（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o </span>）。～修。～索。～察。</li>
	<li>注意约束（言行）：～点（①注意约束言行，如&ldquo;参加宴会时连吃带拿，太不～～了&rdquo;；②查看是否符合，如&ldquo;把行李～～一遍&rdquo;）。失～。</li>
	<li>古代官名，掌修国史，位次编修。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">检</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>检</strong></p>
<p>檢 <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，佥(<span class="dicpy">qiān</span>)声。本义:书匣上的标签)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bookmark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">检，书署也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，今字作签。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辄皂囊施检。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;公孙瓒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:检封(缄封，封藏);检素(封好的信，书信)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 法式，法度 <span class="encs">[laws;statutes]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">检，法度也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然物有恒姿，而思无定检。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙&middot;物色》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:检式(法式;制度);检押(也作&ldquo;检柙&rdquo;，&ldquo;检狎&rdquo;。规矩，法度;或匡正)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 品行;节操 <span class="encs">[conduct]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">初，</span><span class="diczx2">朗</span>少时虽涉猎文学，然不治素检，以吏能见称。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>检</strong></p>
<p>檢 <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 约束，限制 <span class="encs">[restrain oneself]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德亡首褒不检。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;王莽传》</span>。注:&ldquo;局之。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自此致身绳检外，肯教世路日兢兢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司徒空</span><span class="smcs">《退栖》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:检束(整顿好行装);检勑(检点，整饬);检邪(制止邪气);检勒(检点约束);检局(约束，节制)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 考查，察验 <span class="encs">[examine;inspect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骠骑执法以检下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;周黄徐姜申屠传》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹察也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:检还(查点清楚后归还);抄检(搜查，查抄);检尸(验尸);检视(检查，料理);检详(审查考核)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拣选，挑出 <span class="encs">[select]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:检举(选拔;举荐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 翻阅，查阅 <span class="encs">[check]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:检卷(检阅文书案卷);检书(翻阅书籍);检量(查阅斟酌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 收拾，整理 <span class="encs">[put in order]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:检场(收拾场地;收拾场地的人);检幅(修整边幅);检饬(整治);检晒(整理翻晒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;敛&rdquo;。收敛，约束言行;收聚 <span class="encs">[restrain oneself;gather]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">检身若不及。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;伊训》</span>。<span class="smcs">《正义》</span>云:检，谓自摄敛也。</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6AA2.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>居奄切，兼上聲。【說文】書署也。【徐曰】書函之蓋，三刻其上，繩緘之，然後塡以泥，題書其上而印之也。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;司市註】璽節印章，如今斗檢封。</p>
<p>又玉檢，以玉爲檢也。【前漢&middot;武帝紀封禪註】金泥玉檢。</p>
<p>又書袠之簽曰檢。【後漢&middot;公孫瓚傳】皁囊施檢。【類篇】俗謂之燕尾，又謂之排。</p>
<p>又檢制也。【孟子】狗彘食人食而不知檢。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】檢，同也。【郭註】模範同等。</p>
<p>又檢式也。【淮南子&middot;主術訓】人主立法，先自爲檢式儀表，故令行于天下。</p>
<p>又櫽栝謂之檢。【揚子&middot;法言】蠢迪檢押。</p>
<p>又檢點，官名。【宋史&middot;太祖紀】爲都檢點。</p>
<p>又【韻會】俗謂文書藳曰檢子。</p>
<p>又姓也。【姓苑】漢句章尉檢其明，避諱改作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>。</p>
