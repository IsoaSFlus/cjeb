						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>伎</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>技巧，才能：～痒（人擅长某种技艺，一遇机会，急欲表现，好像皮肤发痒不能自忍，亦作“技痒”）。～俩（<span class="dicpy">liāng </span>）。</li>    <li>古代称以歌舞为业的女子。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>伎</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˊ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔～～〕奔走的样子，如“鹿斯之奔，惟足～～”。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">伎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>伎</strong> <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，支声。本义:党与，同党的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[companion]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伎，与也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“<span class="smcs">《舁部》</span>曰:‘与者，党与也。’此伎之本义也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“妓”</span>。歌女，舞女 <span class="encs">[a female performer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伎女楼上，坐而择食。——<span class="smcs">《洛阳伽蓝记》</span>卷四城西</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凄凉蜀故伎，来舞魏宫前。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《蜀先主庙》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:伎人(女歌舞艺人);伎女(古代指女歌舞艺人)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指歌舞表演 <span class="encs">[performance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杂伎，艺以为珩。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span>。旧注:<span class="yhcs">“手仗曰伎。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:伎船(以歌舞色相为业的游船);伎乐(古代俳优所奏的音乐);伎伎(形容奔走的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 旧指医卜历算之类方术 <span class="encs">[art]</span>。如:伎道(方术);伎数(方伎数术);伎坊(教坊)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“技”</span>。技艺，本领 <span class="encs">[artistry;skill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无他伎。——<span class="smcs">《书·秦誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">案谨募选阅材伎之士。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故圣人之处世，不逆有伎能之士。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·道应》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">积财千万，不如薄伎在身。——<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训·勉学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卜者，世之余伎也。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《非国语上·卜》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">通天地而不通人曰伎。——<span class="smcs">《法言·君子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:伎痒(极想施展本领;动心);伎力(技能与勇力)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 才智;才能 <span class="encs">[ability and wisdom]</span>。如:伎艺(技能;才艺);伎曲(以己之才智曲从无道之君)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
