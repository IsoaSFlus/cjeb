                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>罗</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">羅</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lu&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄛˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>捕鸟的网：～网。</li>
	<li>张网捕捉：～掘（用网捕麻雀，挖掘老鼠洞找粮食。喻用尽办法筹措款项）。门可～雀（形容门庭冷落）。</li>
	<li>搜集，招致，包括：～捕。～致（招请人才）。网～。包～。～织罪名（虚构罪名，陷害无辜）。</li>
	<li>散布：～列。</li>
	<li>过滤流质或筛细粉末用的器具：绢～。</li>
	<li>用罗筛东西：～面。</li>
	<li>轻软有稀孔的丝织品：～绮。～扇。</li>
	<li>量词，用于商业，一罗合十二打。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/脶">脶</a>&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>罗</strong></p>
<p>羅 <span class="dicpy">lu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象网中有隹，表示以网捕鸟的意思。小篆增加了&ldquo;糸&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，表示结网所用的材料。本义:用绳线结成的捕鸟网)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[clapnet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罗，以丝罟鸟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有兔爰爰，雉离于罗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;兔爰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以天下为这罗，则雀不失矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不见篱间雀，见鹞自投罗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《野田黄雀行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罗尉(捕鸟的网);罗落(截捕禽兽的用具);罗弋(捕鸟的工具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 轻软的丝织品 <span class="encs">[a kind of gauze]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遍身罗绮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张俞</span><span class="smcs">《蚕妇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">湿罗幕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">红罗复斗帐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晚成单罗纱。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罗绮(罗与绮皆为丝织品，这里指衣着高贵华丽的人);罗衾(绸被);罗衫(古人夏季时所穿的丝织衣衫;质料柔软轻爽);罗巾(丝制手巾);罗帕(丝织方巾);罗衣(轻软丝织品制成的衣服);罗纨(泛指精美的丝织品);罗带(丝织的衣带)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 一种细密的筛子 <span class="encs">[a close sifter]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:罗床(筛面用的一种器具);绢罗，铜丝罗</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 罗马尼亚的简称<span class="encs">[Romania]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>罗</strong></p>
<p>羅 <span class="dicpy">lu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用网捕捉 <span class="encs">[catch with net]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸳鸯于飞，毕之罗之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鸳鸯》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7F85.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】魯何切【集韻】【韻會】良何切【正韻】朗何切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蘿。【說文】以絲罟鳥。古者芒氏初作羅。【爾雅&middot;釋器】鳥罟謂之羅。【註】謂羅絡之。【詩&middot;王風】雉離於羅。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;羅氏】掌羅烏鳥。</p>
<p>又【類篇】帛也。【釋名】羅文，疎羅也。【戰國策】下宮糅羅紈，曳綺縠。</p>
<p>又【廣雅】羅，列也。【楚辭&middot;九歌】羅生兮堂下。【註】羅列而生。</p>
<p>又國名。【左傳&middot;桓十二年】羅人欲伐之。【註】羅，熊姓國，在宜城縣西山中，後徙。</p>
<p>又水名。【史記&middot;屈原傳】遂自投汨羅。【註】汨水在羅，故曰汨羅。</p>
<p>又新羅，東夷國名。【唐書&middot;東夷傳】新羅，弁韓苗裔也，居漢樂浪地。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓氏急就篇】羅氏，顓頊後，封于羅，今房州也。子孫以爲氏。</p>
<p>又羅羅，獸名。【山海經】北海有獸，狀如虎，名曰羅羅。【駢雅】靑虎謂之羅羅。</p>
<p>又菴羅，果名。【本草】梨之類，色黃，如鵝梨。</p>
<p>又【集韻】鄰知切，音離。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】郞佐切，音囉。邏或省作羅。巡也。</p>
