                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>壮</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">壯</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>大，有力，强盛：强～。～士。～心。～烈。～美。兵强马～。</li><li>增加勇气和力量：～胆。～行（<span class="dicpy">xíng </span>）。以～声势。</li><li>中国少数民族，主要分布于广西壮族自治区及云南、四川、贵州等省（旧作“<span class="diczx3">僮</span>”）：～族。～锦。～歌。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">壮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>壮</strong></p><p>壯 <span class="dicpy">zhuàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从士，爿( <span class="dicpy">pán</span>)声。本义:人体高大，肌肉壮实)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tall and big;srong;robust]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壮，大也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壮，健也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>之间凡人之大谓之奘，或谓之壮。——<span class="smcs">《方言》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三十曰壮。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壮士，赐之卮酒。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">右贤王</span>惊，夜逃，独与其爱妾一人，壮骑数百驰。——<span class="smcs">《史记·卫将军骠骑列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">男气壮力。——<span class="smcs">《逸周书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壮哉我</span><span class="diczx2">中国</span>少年。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三小姐长远勿见，好像壮仔点哉。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩邦庆</span><span class="smcs">《海上花列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:壮浪(健壮);壮盛(强壮旺盛);壮勇(强壮而勇敢);壮武(强壮勇武);年轻力壮;兵强马壮;壮健(强壮，强健)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 豪壮;豪迈 <span class="encs">[grand and heroic]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼不知惧，而学壮语，此之不武，何能为也。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·谯刚王逊传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">七龄思即壮，开口咏凤凰。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《壮游》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:壮夫(豪壮之士，豪杰);壮思(豪壮的情思);壮情(豪壮的情怀、抱负);壮意(豪壮的意气)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 勇壮 <span class="encs">[brave and sturdy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风萧萧兮</span><span class="diczx2">易水</span>寒，壮士一去兮不复还。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策三》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:壮武(勇壮，雄武);壮悍(勇壮强悍);壮毅(勇壮刚毅);壮辞(勇壮的言辞)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 宏伟 <span class="encs">[grandeur]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">克壮其犹。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采芑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且夫天子以四海为家，非壮丽无以重威。——<span class="smcs">《史记·高祖纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>
