                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>淬</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cuì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuì.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>把烧红了的铸件往水或油或其他液体里一浸立刻取出来，用以提高合金的硬度和强度：～火。～砺。～勉。</li>    <li>染：“得赵人徐夫人之匕首，取之百金，使工以药～～之”。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>淬</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>(形声。从水，卒声。本义:盛水以供淬火的器具) 同本义 <span class="encs">[quenching vessel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淬，灭火器也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，贮水以焠刃之器，其实焠、淬同字，刃出于火，故从火。</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>淬</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 淬火 <span class="encs">[quench]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>人 <span class="diczx2">徐夫人</span>之匕首，取之百金，使二以药淬之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">火与水合为淬。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·天文志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以药淬之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·刺客传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:淬淬黑(如钢铁淬过火一样的颜色);淬砺(淬火磨砺。比喻人刻苦锻炼;勤勉奋发);淬刃(刀刃淬火)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 磨 <span class="encs">[grind]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:淬镜(磨镜);淬磨(磨砺;磨炼)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 喻磨炼;勉励 <span class="encs">[temper oneself]</span>。如:淬勉(努力奋勉);淬琢(淬炼，琢磨。比喻自勉上进);淬炼(磨炼);淬励(激励;鞭策)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/淬火">淬火</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/淬亮">淬亮</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>淬</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄘㄨㄟˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>cuì <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5523.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">灭火器。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·水部》：「<b>淬</b>，灭火器也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清·段玉裁·注：「灭火器者，盖以器盛水，濡火使灭，其器谓之<b>淬</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yx">同「<a href='/hans/焠' target=_blank>焠</a> 」。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">打造刀剑时，将金属烧红后浸入水中，以增加其硬度，使其更坚利。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·王襃·圣主得贤臣颂》：「清水<b>淬</b>其锋。」</span><span class="gc_yx">同「<a href='/hans/焠' target=_blank>焠</a> 」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">涂染。</span><span class="gc_yy">《战国策·燕策三》：「得赵人徐夫人之匕首，取之百金，使工以药<b>淬</b>之。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">磨炼。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·柳宗元〈送严公贶下第归兴元观省诗序〉：「端其趋向，以淬砺之。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">没入水中。</span><span class="gc_yy">《西游记·第六回》：「（大圣）​变作一个鱼儿，<b>淬</b>入水。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6DEC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】七內切【集韻】【韻會】取內切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音倅。【說文】滅火器也。【徐曰】淬，劒燒而入水也。與焠通。【前漢·天文志】火與水合爲淬。【王褒·聖主得賢臣頌】淸水淬其鋒。</p><p>又【廣韻】染也，犯也。【史記·司馬相如傳】脟割輪淬。</p><p>又【揚子·方言】淬，寒也。</p><p>又【集韻】卽聿切，音卒。淬，沒水貌。</p><p>又昨律切，音崒。流也。脟字原誤从目从寽作。</p>
