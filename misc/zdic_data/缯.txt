                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缯</strong></p>
<p>繒 <span class="dicpy">zēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，曾声。本义:古代对丝织品的总称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[silk fabrics]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缯，帛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杂帛曰缯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瘗缯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤绨缘缯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;匈奴传》</span>。注:&ldquo;缯者帛之总名。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从弟子女十人所，皆衣缯单衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">灌婴</span>， <span class="diczx2">睢阳</span>贩缯者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;灌婴传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缯服(丝织的衣服);缯絮(丝絮。也指丝絮作成的棉衣);缯纩(丝布与丝棉絮的合称);缯帛(丝绸之统称);缯布(缯帛布疋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;矰&rdquo;。古代射鸟用的箭 <span class="encs">[arrow for shooting birds]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">治其缯缴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;楚策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">好弋者先具缴与缯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缯缴充蹊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;袁绍传注》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缯缴(即矰缴。猎取飞鸟的射具。缴为系在短箭上的丝绳;比喻陷害他人的手段)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Zeng state]</span>。姒姓，相传为夏禹后裔</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">z&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缯</strong></p>
<p>繒 <span class="dicpy">z&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 绑;扎 <span class="encs">[tie;bind;fasten]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E52.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】疾陵切【集韻】【韻會】慈陵切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蹭。【說文】帛也。籀文作緈。【前漢&middot;灌嬰傳】睢陽販繒者也。【註】師古曰：繒者，帛之摠名。</p>
<p>又國名。【穀梁傳&middot;僖十四年】季姬及繒子遇于防。【周語】杞繒由太姒。【註】杞繒二國，姒姓，夏禹之後。</p>
<p>又縣名。【史記&middot;吳大伯世家】敗齊師于艾陵至繒。【註】琅邪繒縣。</p>
<p>又姓。【史記&middot;夏本紀】禹爲姒姓，其後分封，用國爲姓，有繒氏。【前漢&middot;文帝紀】祁侯繒賀爲將軍。</p>
<p>又【集韻】咨騰切，音增。徂棱切，音層。作亙切，音贈。義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。</p>
<p>又與矰同。【三輔黃圖】佽飛具繒繳以射雁。</p>
