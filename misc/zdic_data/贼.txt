                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>贼</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">賊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">z&eacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄟˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>偷东西的人，盗匪：～人。盗～。～赃。～窝。</li>
	<li>对人民有危害的人：国～。民～。工～。～寇。蟊～。</li>
	<li>害，伤害：戕～。&ldquo;淫侈之俗日日以长，是天下之大～也&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>邪的，不正派的：～心不死。</li>
	<li>狡猾：～溜溜。</li>
	<li>副词，很：～冷。～亮。～横（<span class="dicpy">h&egrave;ng </span>）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> thief, traitor; kill</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dieb  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rusé,malin,voleur,traître</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贼</strong></p>
<p>賊 <span class="dicpy">z&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从戈，则声。刀毁贝，如见于西周金文。 本义:残害;伤害)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cruelly injure or kill;harm;hurt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼，败也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">害良为贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;修身》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不僭不贼，鲜不为则。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;抑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寒暑弗能害，禽兽弗能贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毁则为贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十八年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:&ldquo;毁则，坏法也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">下</span><span class="diczx2">广汉</span>廷尉狱，又坐贼杀不辜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵尹韩张两王传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贼毫(书法的败笔。指笔锋之劣);贼伦(毁弃伦常，败坏道德);贼仁(毁弃仁爱);贼蚀(受到的歪曲损害);贼义(毁弃道义);贼害(残害、伤害);贼蠹(危害);贼殃(祸害)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 特指杀害 <span class="encs">[kill;slaughter;murder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼民之主，不忠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宣公</span>骤谏， <span class="diczx2">公</span>患之，使鉏贼之。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀人不忌为贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑杀不正谓之贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《烈女传&middot;辩通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贼斗(残杀格斗);贼残(残害);贼场(讨贼的战场);贼杀(杀害);贼性(残杀之性);贼刑(刑杀);贼诛(杀害)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贼</strong></p>
<p>賊 <span class="dicpy">z&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 先秦两汉时期，贼指作乱叛国危害人民的人 <span class="encs">[traitor;enemy;evildoer;bad person]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">商君</span>， <span class="diczx2">秦</span>之贼。 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>强而贼入 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>，弗归，不可。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;商君列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">讨贼复兴之效。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其实</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>贼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几落贼手死。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼中险易远近。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">西后</span>及贼臣忌益甚。(贼臣，指当时反对变法维新的顽固派。)&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卖国贼;工贼;贼子(反叛，叛乱或叛逆的人);贼臣(叛逆不忠的臣子);贼官(为敌方效力的伪官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 刺客，杀人的人 <span class="encs">[murderer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大索天下，求贼甚急，为张良故也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;留侯世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">燕王</span>昏乱，其太子 <span class="diczx2">丹</span>乃阴令 <span class="diczx2">荆轲</span>为贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 偷窃或抢劫的人(先秦两汉用&ldquo;盗&rdquo;，多指偷窃者，很少指抢劫者;用贼，多指抢劫财物者，后来才指偷窃者) <span class="encs">[thief]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使</span><span class="diczx2">长安</span>丞 <span class="diczx2">龚奢</span>叩堂户晓贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵尹韩张两王传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼二人得我，我幸皆杀之矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大盗积贼，杀人重囚，气杰旺，染此者，十不一二。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼能且众。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一贼提刀。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼应声落马。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令贼知也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:盗贼(强盗和小偷);窃贼(小偷儿);贼不空手(比喻势在必得);贼属(贼寇的家眷亲属)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 骂人的字眼 <span class="encs">[curse word]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老贼欲废汉自立。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与老贼势不两立。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼臣忌益甚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此乃</span><span class="diczx2">荣</span>贼。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 祸害 <span class="encs">[calamity; disaster]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淫侈之欲日日以长，是天下之大贼也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 敌人;仇敌 <span class="encs">[enemy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">环顾国内，贼氛方炽。(敌人的气焰正嚣张。氛，气，气焰。方，副词。炽，火旺盛。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谄谀我者，吾贼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;修身》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>贼</strong></p>
<p>賊 <span class="dicpy">z&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 邪恶，不正派 <span class="encs">[furtive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寒暑不和，贼气相奸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;龟策列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贼贱(卑贱;下贱);贼尘(有害的灰尘。喻欺君罔上者);贼忒忒(形容眼神不正派);贼智(鬼点子，狡猾办法);老鼠真贼</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 残暴;狠毒 <span class="encs">[cruel and ferocious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">内怀诈以御主心，外挟贼吏以为威重。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汲黯列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">郭解</span>]</span>少时阴贼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;游侠列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贼深(心肠残酷，用法深刻);贼暴(残虐;暴虐);贼悍(残暴凶悍)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>贼</strong></p>
<p>賊 <span class="dicpy">z&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p>〈方〉∶很;非常 <span class="encs">[extremely]</span>。多用于令人不满的或不正常的情况。</p>
