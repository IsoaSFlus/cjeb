						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>扬</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">揚</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yáng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yáng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>簸动，向上播散：～水。～场（<span class="dicpy">cháng </span>）。～汤止沸。</li><li>高举，向上：～手。～帆。趾高气～。～眉吐气。</li><li>在空中飘动：飘～。</li><li>称颂，传播：～言。～威。颂～。～弃。～名。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>扬</strong></p><p>揚、敭 <span class="dicpy">yáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，昜( <span class="dicpy">yáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yáng.mp3"></a></span> )声。金文字形，像用双手举起<span class="yhcs">“玉”</span>，是会意字。本义:高举)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[raise;lift]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬，飞举也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬，举也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杜篑洗而扬觯。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盥洗扬斛。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乡饮酒义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南扬弓。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡射礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬其波。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬鞭语曰。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大风扬积雪。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬帆去。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:扬楫(行船);扬袂(举袖);扬眉(举目);扬旌(高举军旗);扬麋(扬眉);扬觯(举起酒器);扬鞭(挥鞭);扬臂(振臂);扬徽(挥动军旗);扬刃(挥动锋刃)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“飞”</span>。飞起，升高 <span class="encs">[fly high]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">只因朝廷无驾驭，遂令草泽有鹰扬。——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飘风起兮扬尘埃，走鬯罔兮乍东西。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王逸</span><span class="smcs">《九思·逢尤》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:扬蕤(彩带飘舞);扬尘(激起尘土);扬饭(扬去饭的热气)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通过风或气流处理 <span class="encs">[谷物等]</span> 以便扇去废物 <span class="encs">[winnow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维南有箕，不可以簸扬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·大东》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:用连枷打谷后，他们又扬谷</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 提高声音 <span class="encs">[aloud]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不吴不扬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·泮水》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:扬言(大声地说);扬音(谓发出高亢的声音);扬歌(高声歌唱);扬嚷(高声叫嚷)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 发扬，发挥 <span class="encs">[develop;make the most of]</span></p><p><span                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              揚·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/63DA.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕敭<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B72.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠭲</span>【唐韻】與章切【集韻】余章切【正韻】移章切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音陽。飛舉也。【詩·王風】揚之水，不流束薪。【傳】揚，激揚也。【疏】謂水急激而飛揚，波流疾之意也。</p><p>又【豳風】以伐遠揚。【疏】謂長條揚起者。</p><p>又【增韻】發也，顯也。【廣韻】舉也。【易·夬卦】揚于王庭。【疏】發揚決斷之事於王者之庭。【書·堯典】明明揚側陋。【禮·文王世子】或以言揚。【疏】能言語應對，亦舉用之。</p><p>又稱說也。【禮·祭統】銘者，自名以稱揚其先祖之美，而明著之後世者也。【前漢·季布傳】使僕游揚足下，名於天下，顧不美乎。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋詁】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/222D0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢋐</span>揚，續也。</p><p>又簸去糠粃也。【詩·小雅】維南有箕，不可以簸揚。</p><p>又眉上下曰揚。【詩·鄘風】揚且之皙也。【傳】揚，眉上廣。【又】子之淸揚。【疏】揚者，眉上之美名。旣名眉爲揚，因謂眉之上，眉之下皆曰揚。</p><p>又鉞也。【詩·大雅】干戈戚揚。</p><p>又州名。【書·禹貢】淮海惟揚州。【疏】江南之氣躁勁，厥性輕揚，故曰揚州。亦曰：州界多水，水波揚也。</p><p>又姓。【揚雄·自序】揚別爲一族。周宣王子尚父封揚侯，因氏。</p><p>又宋揚避舉，明揚光休。　通作颺。</p>
