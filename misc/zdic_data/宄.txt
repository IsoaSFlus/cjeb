                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5B84.svg">                          <p>姦也。外爲盜，内爲宄。从宀九聲，讀若軌。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B1A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠬚</span>，古文宄。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/219EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡧫</span>，亦古文宄。居洧切〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/219CC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡧌</span>，古文。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5B84_1429.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(宄)</span><span class="ywyy">姦也。</span><span class="swdz">姦宄者、通偁。內外者、析言之也。</span><span class="ywyy">外爲盜。內爲宄。</span><span class="swdz">凡盜起外爲姦。中出爲宄。成十七年左傳曰。亂在外爲姦。在內爲宄。外傳晉語亦云。亂在內爲宄。在外爲姦。魯語。竊寶者爲宄。用宄之財者爲姦。亦謂莒太子僕竊莒寶爲內。魯藏姦爲外。三傳無異也。惟亂在內、故字从宀。鄭注尙書云。由內爲姦。起外爲軌。或後人轉寫誤也。宄經史亦假軌爲之。</span><span class="ywyy">从宀。九聲。讀若軌。</span><span class="swdz">居洧切。古音九在三部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5B84_1430.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B1A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠬚</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文宄。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5B84_1431.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/219EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡧫</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">亦古文宄。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
