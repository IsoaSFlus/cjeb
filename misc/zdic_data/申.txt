                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">申</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>申</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄣˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>地支的第九位，属猴。</li>	<li>用于记时：～时（下午三点至五点）。</li>	<li>陈述，说明：～述。～斥。～辩。～冤。三令五～。～请。～报。</li>	<li>重复，一再：&ldquo;～之以盟誓，重之以昏姻&rdquo;。</li>	<li>中国上海市的别称。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to state to a superior, report; extend; 9th terrestrial branch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> 9. Tier im chin. Tierkreis - Affe <astrol&gt,Jahr des Affen,9. der 12 Erdenzweige (V)​,Affenjahre (V)​,ausdehnen, ausweiten (V)​,darlegen, erklären (V)​,Shen (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> le 9e des 12 Rameaux terrestres,affirmer,exprimer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">申</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>申</strong> <span class="dicpy">shēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。闪电的&ldquo;電&rdquo;的象形初文，战国文字闪电末梢讹作&ldquo;𦥑&rdquo;，闪电主体拉直作竖笔。西汉隶书&ldquo;𦥑&rdquo;相连，即成了今文字&ldquo;申&rdquo;。&ldquo;申&rdquo;假借用作地支第九位，也曾假借伸展义、神明义等，后加&ldquo;人&rdquo;、&ldquo;示&rdquo;分化出&ldquo;伸&rdquo;、&ldquo;神&rdquo;，&ldquo;申&rdquo;主要用作地支，字形本意加&ldquo;雨&rdquo;造&ldquo;電&rdquo;。)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义;约束 <span class="encs">[restrain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">申，束身也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">约车申辕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span>。注:&ldquo;束也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畏忌自申。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;韦元成传》</span>。注:&ldquo;言自约束也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勒兵申教令。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;元帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朱绿带申加大带于上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;杂记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">申体防以自持。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:申束(约束)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 舒展。通&ldquo;伸&rdquo;。伸展，伸张 <span class="encs">[stretch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形可劫而使诎申。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;解蔽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大夫执圭而使，所以申信也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特牲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咎犯申其左臂而诎五指。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说苑&middot;正谏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行止屈申，与时息兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班超</span><span class="smcs">《北征赋》</span></p>
