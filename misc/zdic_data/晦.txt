						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>晦</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>农历每月的末一天，朔日的前一天：～朔。</li><li>夜晚：～明。风雨如～。</li><li>昏暗不明：～暝。～暗。～涩（意义隐晦，文字僻拗）。隐～（意义不明显）。</li><li>不顺利，倒霉：～气。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dark, unclear, obscure; night</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> dunkel, Dunkelheit  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> obscurité</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">晦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>晦</strong> <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，每声。本义:阴历每月的最后一天)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[last day of the lunar month]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晦，月尽也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三十日日月合宿，谓之晦。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·四讳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陈不违晦。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝菌不知晦朔。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不知晦朔。——<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戊申晦。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:晦望(农历每月末一日与十五日);晦景(晦日，晦节);晦日(农历每月最后的一天)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 夜;晚 <span class="encs">[night]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晦淫惑疾。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公元年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“夜也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:晦明(从黑夜到天明。即昼夜);晦晓(夜晚与天明);晦魄(夜月);风雨如晦</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>晦</strong> <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 昏暗 <span class="encs">[dark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晦而休。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“冥也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是时雷电晦冥。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幽晦以多雨。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·屈原·涉江》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">道途显晦。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晦明变化。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:晦冥(昏暗);晦蒙(昏暗);晦盲(昏暗不明);晦芒(昏暗)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 义理深微;隐晦;含蓄 <span class="encs">[implicit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">志而晦。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:晦伏(隐晦不显);晦昧(隐晦不明);晦窒(晦涩不通);晦塞(晦涩不畅)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 草木凋零 <span class="encs">[withered，fallen and scattered about]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寂历百草晦。——</span><span class="diczx2">江淹</span><span class="smcs">《诗》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“凡草木华实荣茂谓之明，枝叶凋伤谓之晦。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:晦在(凋零将尽，健在的已经很少了)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 倒霉 <span class="encs">[be unlucky]</span>。如:晦气星(比喻能使人倒霉的人或事);晦气色(难看的、呈青黄色的脸色)</p><p>◎ <strong>晦</strong> <span
