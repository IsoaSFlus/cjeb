						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>熙</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>光明。</li>    <li>兴起，兴盛。</li>    <li>和乐：～怡。～笑。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">禧</span>”，福，吉祥。</li>    <li>〔～～攘攘〕形容人来人往、喧闹纷杂的样子。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">嬉</span>”，嬉戏。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">熙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>熙</strong></p><p>熈、煕 <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声，本义:曝晒;晒太阳)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dry in the sun]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">熙，燥也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仰熙丹崖，俯澡绿水。——<span class="smcs">《文选·卢谌·赠刘琨》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 振兴;兴起 <span class="encs">[develop vigorously;cause to prosper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">允厘百工，庶绩咸熙。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:熙朝(使朝廷兴隆);熙隆(兴盛，盛隆);熙国(振兴国家)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“嬉”</span>。嬉戏 <span class="encs">[play]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王笑曰:<span class="yhcs">“圣人非所与熙也。——<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋·内篇杂下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含哺而熙，鼓腹而游。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·马蹄》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣不敢以死为熙。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·人间》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:熙游(游乐;游戏) ?</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>熙</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 光明、明亮 <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">熙，光也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于缉熙敬止。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·缁衣》</span>。注:“缉熙皆明也。”</span>大学注:<span class="yhcs">“缉熙，光明也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缉熙单厥心。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“熙，光也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">呜呼圣皇及圣相，相与煊赫流淳熙。——</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《韩碑》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:熙皞(熙熙皞皞。光明祥和);熙天(明朗的天空);熙光(辉耀);熙焰(烈焰)</p><p><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7199.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕焈【唐韻】許其切【集韻】【韻會】虛其切【正韻】虛宜切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音僖。【爾雅·釋詁】緝熙，光也。【詩·大雅】於緝熙敬止。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋詁】熙，興也。【詩·周頌】時純熙矣。【朱傳】亦光也。</p><p>又【周語】熙，廣也。【書·堯典】庶績咸熙。【舜典】有能奮庸熙帝之載。【史記·吳世家】歌大雅曰：廣哉熙熙乎。</p><p>又【廣韻】和也。【老子·道德經】衆人熙熙。</p><p>又【說文】燥也。</p><p>又人名。【左傳·昭二十九年】少皥氏有四叔：曰重曰該曰修曰熙，實能金木及水。</p><p>又【韻會】州名。土蕃地。宋置熙州。</p><p>又【正韻】與禧同。【前漢·禮樂志】熙事備成。【註】師古曰：福熙之事也。</p><p>又與嬉通。【宋玉·登徒子好色賦】出咸陽熙邯鄲。【註】熙，戲也。</p><p>又【淮南子·修務訓】木熙者非眇勁。【註】木熙今之走高竿緣繩者，言其援豐條舞扶疎踴躍紆徐自如也。</p><p>又【集韻】盈之切，音飴。【揚子·方言】鬱熙，長也。【註】謂壯大也。</p><p>又人名。【左傳·襄八年】鄭公子熙。◎按釋文熙，許其反，徐音怡，二音俱可讀。【六書故】別作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/241F4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤇴</span>。〇按字彙，《正字通》，熙字音義俱詳十畫熈註。考說文玉篇廣韻，諸書俱作熙，不作熈，則熈當爲俗字。今特將音義備載於此。</p>
