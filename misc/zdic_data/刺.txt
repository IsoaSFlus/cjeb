<p><span class="dicpy">c&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用有尖的东西插入：～绣。～伤。～杀。</li>
	<li>暗杀：～客。被～。行～。</li>
	<li>侦探，打听：～探。</li>
	<li>用尖锐的话指出别人的坏处：讽～。讥～。</li>
	<li>尖锐像针的东西：芒～。鱼～。～丛。</li>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刺</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，朿( <span class="dicpy">c&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:扎入，用尖利的东西刺)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stab;prick;pierce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君杀大夫曰刺。刺，直伤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刺，杀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌三刺三宥三赦之法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司刺》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫</span><span class="diczx2">专诸</span>之刺 <span class="diczx2">王僚</span>也，慧星袭月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刺焚(刺股、焚膏之略词，比喻刻苦攻读);刺子(暗剑);刺心(以刀等刺进心脏。喻刺痛内心);刺血(刺手指出血。表示虔诚的一种苦行);刺臂(古代一种黥刑，在犯者臂部刺字;刺臂出血，用以盟誓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 讽刺 <span class="encs">[satirize;mock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上称帝喾，下道齐桓，中述汤、武，以刺世事&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:讥刺(讥讽);刺邪(讽刺邪恶);刺戒(讥刺告戒);刺美(讽刺邪恶，赞扬美好)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 杀。古时君杀大夫叫刺 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">经刺公子</span><span class="diczx2">偃</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十六年》</span>。注:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">鲁</span>杀大夫皆言刺。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刺</span><span class="diczx2">怀公</span>于 <span class="diczx2">高梁</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刺斫(刺杀);刺虎(杀虎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指责、揭发 <span class="encs">[blame;expose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群臣吏民能面刺寡人之过者，受上赏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刺举(检举;谓检举奸恶，举荐有功);刺打(斥责。方言)</span></p>
