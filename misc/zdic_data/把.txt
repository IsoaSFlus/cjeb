						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>把</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎ.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄚˇ</span></p><ol><li>拿，抓住：～酒（拿着酒杯）。～玩（拿着赏玩）。</li><li>控制，掌握：～握。～舵。</li><li>看守：～守。～门儿。</li><li>自行车、手推车等的手柄：车～。</li><li>可以用手拿的小捆：秫秸～儿。</li><li>专权，一手独揽：～持大权。</li><li>从后托起小孩两腿使之大小便的动作：～尿。</li><li>介词，义为拿，处置，致使：你能～他怎么样。</li><li>量词。</li><li>结盟：拜～子。～兄弟。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>把</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bà  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bà.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄚˋ</span></p><ol><li>物体上便于手拿的部分：刀～儿。</li><li>被人作为说笑资料的言行：话～儿。</li></ol>

Geige, Messer, Regenschirm)​  (Zähl)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tenir,contenir,saisir,prendre en main,manche,poignée,(classificateur pour un instrument ayant un manche ou une anse)​,(particule introduisant le complément d'objet)​,poignée,anse</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>把</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，巴声 。本义:握持;执)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">把，握也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其拱把而上者。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·人间世》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拱把之桐梓。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“以一手把之也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汤</span>自把铖以伐 <span class="diczx2">昆吾</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·殷本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无把铫推耨之劳，而有积粟之实。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">把酒临风。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">把其袖。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">把酒话桑麻。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">孟浩然</span><span class="smcs">《过故人庄》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:把杯(拿着杯子);把刀(掌刀，上灶烧菜);把卷(持卷);把玩(握在手中或置于手中赏玩);把腕(握住手腕)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 把守;看守。站岗放哨 <span class="encs">[guard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">街亭</span>有兵把守。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
