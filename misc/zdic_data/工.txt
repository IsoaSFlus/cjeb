                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">工</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>工</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>个人不占有生产资料，依靠工资收入为生的劳动者：～人。～人阶级。～农联盟。</li><li>制造生产资料和生活资料的生产事业：～业。～业革命。</li><li>从事体力或脑力劳动：～作。～厂。竣～。</li><li>工作量：记～。这个工程需要三十个～。</li><li>技术和技术修养：唱～儿。～夫（ａ．本领，造诣，亦作“功夫”；ｂ．时间）。～力。</li><li>细致，精巧：～巧。～整。～笔（中国画技法，用笔工整，注重细部的描绘）。</li><li>〔～尺（<span class="dicpy">chǐ</span>）〕中国古代一种记谱符号体系，有十个字：合、四、一、上、尺、工、凡、六、五、乙，相当于简谱的５６７1234567。“工”相当于“3”。</li><li>善于，长于：～书善画。～于心计。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">工</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>工</strong> <span class="dicpy">gōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象工具形。<span class="yhcs">“工”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“巨”</span>(矩)古同字，有<span class="yhcs">“规矩”</span>，即持有工具。本义:工匠的曲尺)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carpenter's square]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">工，巧饰也，象人有规榘也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">许</span>君谓工象人有规榘，说颇难通，以巧饰训工，殆非朔义。以愚观之，工盖器物之名也。知者:<span class="smcs">《工部》</span>巨下云:<span class="yhcs">“规巨也，从工，象手持之。”</span>按:工为器物，故人能以手持之，若工第为巧饰，安能手持乎…以字形考之，工象曲尺之形，盖即曲尺也。—— <span class="diczx2">杨树达</span><span class="smcs">《积微居小学述林》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 工匠;工人。古代对从事各种技艺的劳动者的总称;现代为工人和工人阶级的总称 <span class="encs">[craftsman;worker]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡攻木之工七。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·总目》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五日工事之式。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官·大宰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百工居肆以成其事。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子张》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">工欲善其事，必先利其器。——<span class="smcs">《论语·卫灵公》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巫医乐师百工之人，不耻相师。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>人 <span class="diczx2">徐夫人</span>之匕首，取之百金，使工以药淬之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:矿工;临时工;工农联盟;工民(古代从事工艺的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代特指乐官或乐人 <span class="encs">[musicians]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乐工</span><span class="diczx2">罗程</span>者，善弹琵琶，为第一，能变易新声。…他工辈以程艺天下无双，欲以动上意。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林·雅量》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:工师(乐师);工瞽(古代乐官)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古代特指女工 <span class="encs">[woman worker]</span>。如:工容(女工和容貌);工德(指妇工)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 官吏;职事 <span class="encs">[official;duty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">论百工，审时事，辨功苦，尚完剩，便备用，使雕琢文来不敢专造于家，工师之事也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>
