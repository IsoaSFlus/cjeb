                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>圆</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">圓</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>从中心点到周边任何一点的距离都相等的形：～形。～圈。～周。～锥。～柱。</li>	<li>完备，周全：～满。～全。</li>	<li>使之周全：自～其说。～谎。～场。</li>	<li>占梦以决吉凶：～梦。</li>	<li>宛转，滑利：～滑。～润。</li>	<li>运转无碍：～熟。～通。</li>	<li>货币单位。亦作&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">元</span>&rdquo;。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">圆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圆</strong></p>
<p>圓 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从囗( <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span>)，员声。本义:圆形)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[round;circular]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圆，圜全也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圆而神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天道曰圆，地道曰方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;曾子天圆》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中吾规者谓之圆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;天志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水圆折者有珠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;地形》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百工为方以矩，为圆以规。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;法仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">方圆两炮台。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圆丢丢(圆溜溜);圆浑(浑圆);圆领(明朝官员的常礼服。其胸前背后加有不同图案的补子以区别官阶的，叫补服);滚圆(极圆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 圆通;灵活 <span class="encs">[flexible;accommodating;agile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如今到外头去作官，自然非家居可比，总得学些圆通。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圆便(变通的办法);圆媚(处世圆滑，善于逢迎);圆活(圆滑。指处世变通灵活;周全)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 圆满;完整 <span class="encs">[satisfactory;complete;integrated]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蓍之德，圆而神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圆图(录取名单;古时县考时用以公布考生中考的榜牌);圆成(佛教语。成就圆满;圆满成功);圆妙(佛教语。圆满融通);圆明(佛教语。彻底领悟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 丰满;周全 <span class="encs">[full and round]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其粟圆而薄糠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;审时》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圆湛(饱满的样子);圆匀(丰满匀称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 婉转;圆润 <span class="encs">[be sweet and agreeable;mellow and full]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">深圆似轻簧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《题周家歌者》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圆丽(圆润秀丽)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圆</strong></p>
<p>圓 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 圆周 <span class="encs">[circle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">右手画圆，左手画方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 月亮 <span class="encs">[moon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:圆缺(指月亮的盈亏);圆月(中秋节晚上围坐赏月;或指中秋祭月);圆蟾(圆影，圆魄，圆景，圆舒，圆光。都指月亮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指天 <span class="encs">[sky]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载圆履方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圆天(古人认为无呈圆形，故称&ldquo;圆天&rdquo;);圆方(古人认为天圆地方，因此&ldquo;圆方&rdquo;代称天地);圆空(天空，天);圆象(天象);圆盖(圆宰，圆苍，圆精，圆灵。都指天)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 丸，圆而小的东西 <span class="encs">[pill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">炒肉片，煎肉圆，闷青鱼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 圆形的货币。也作&ldquo;元&rdquo; <span class="encs">[round coin]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:银圆(元)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>圆</strong></p>
<p>圓 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 使圆满，成全 <span class="encs">[perfect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">你只依着师傅这话，就算给师傅圆上这个脸了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圆备(完成);圆就(成全;圆满);圆亲(娶亲，成亲);圆谎(掩盖弥补谎话中的漏洞);自圆其说</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 旋转 <span class="encs">[revolve]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">(</span><span class="diczx2">王述</span>)但性急为累，尝食鸡子，以筋刺之，不得，便大怒掷地，鸡子团转不止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圆旋(回旋);圆转(旋转);圆折(水流旋转曲折)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 团圆，散而重聚 <span class="encs">[reunion]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">试问古来几曾见破镜能重圆?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>

                              圓·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5713.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】王權切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】于權切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音員。與圜同。方之對也。【說文】圜，全也。【韻會】古方圓之圓皆作圜，今皆作圓。【易&middot;繫辭】蓍之德圓而神，卦之德方以智。【管子&middot;心術篇】能大圓者，體乎大方。【韓非子&middot;飾邪篇】左手畫圓，右手畫方，不能兩全。【郭璞&middot;江賦】圓淵九迴而懸騰。【註】峽江深急，激岸石而成圓流也。【元結&middot;惡圓論歌】寧方爲皁，不圓爲卿。寧方爲汙辱，不圓爲顯榮。</p><p>又圓夢，占夢以決吉凶也。【秦再思紀異錄】長安興義寺有圓夢堂，禪師智滿，圓夢獲驗，堂因以名。</p><p>又與員同。【孟子】規矩方員之至也。【詩&middot;商頌】景員維河。【說文長箋】員當作圓，言周景山皆大河也。</p><p>又與卵同。【山海經】丹山之陽，有鳳之圓。【緯略】圓，古卵字。【正字通】經本作丸，緯略因聲近，譌爲圓。</p><p>又【集韻】王問切，音運。義同。</p><p>又叶于分切，音雲。【蔡洪&middot;圍棋賦】曲直有正，方而不圓。算徒授卒，三百維羣。円えん、￥（YEN），同圓。</p>
