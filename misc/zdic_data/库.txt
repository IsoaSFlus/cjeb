                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">库</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>库</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">庫</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">k&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>贮存东西的房屋或地方：仓～。国～。～存。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> armory, treasury, storehouse</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Lagerhalle, Lagerhaus  (S)​,Speicher, Magazin, Depot  (S)​,Warenlager  (S)​,Ku  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dépôt</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">库</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>库</strong></p>
<p>庫 <span class="dicpy">k&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，表示与房屋有关，从车。兵车藏在房屋一类的建筑内，表示是储藏武器战车的地方。本义:军械库，收藏兵器和兵车的处所)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[armory]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">库，兵车藏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在库言库。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七月官库。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;时则》</span>。注:&ldquo;兵府也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚库，无聚，将何以守矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:库兵(库藏的兵器)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 后泛指贮物的屋舍 <span class="encs">[storehouse;warehouse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">库，舍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">管库之士。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span>。注:&ldquo;物所藏。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衡库者天子之礼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;七律》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">库门，天子皋门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;名堂位》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百姓虚而府库满。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;富国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使府人、库人各儆其事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">库不受甲兵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;十过》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纳之库。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:库子(库号。掌管、守卫官库的人);库局(佛寺中掌管仓库、财务的部门);库司(佛寺中掌管仓库钱粮的人);库收(藩台衙门库房收银后发给的临时收据);库金(库藏之金帛);库门(仓库的门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 监狱 <span class="encs">[jail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故拘之牖里之库百日，欲令之死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;鲁仲连邹阳列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 宋时酒肆称库 <span class="encs">[wineshop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大抵酒肆除官库、子库、脚店之外，其余谓之&ldquo;拍户&rdquo;，兼卖诸般下酒，食次随意索唤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吴自牧</span><span class="smcs">《梦粱录》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 电量单位&ldquo;库仑&rdquo;的简称 <span class="encs">[coulomb]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庫</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“库”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/庫">庫</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下" target="_blank">寅集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下&amp;bs=广" target="_blank">广</a>】
                              庫·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5EAB.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>苦故切，苦去聲。【說文】兵車藏也。从車，在广下。【釋名】庫，舍也。物所在之舍也，故齊魯謂庫曰舍。【禮&middot;月令】審五庫之量。【蔡邕&middot;章句】一曰車庫，二曰兵庫，三曰祭庫，四曰樂庫，五曰宴庫。</p>
<p>又天庫，星名。【春秋&middot;文曜鉤】軫南四星曰天庫。天庫，五帝車舍也。</p>
<p>又門名。【禮&middot;郊特牲】庫門之內，戒百官也。【註】庫門，在雉門之外。</p>
<p>又姓。【風俗通】古守庫大夫之後，以官爲氏。</p>
