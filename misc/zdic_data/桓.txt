                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>桓</strong> <span class="dicpy">huán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，亘( <span class="dicpy">xuān</span>)声。本义:表柱。古代立在驿站、官署等建筑物旁作标志的木柱，后称华表)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，后也泛指寺、墓、桥梁等用作表识或其他用途的柱子 <span class="encs">[pillar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桓，亭邮表也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三家视桓楹。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“四植谓之桓。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公执桓圭。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“双植谓之桓。桓宫室之象，所以安其上也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:桓楹(古代天子，诸侯葬时下棺所植的大柱子);桓表(即华表)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 木名。即无患木 <span class="encs">[Chinese soapberry]</span>。叶似柳，皮黄白色</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 水名。即今白龙江 <span class="encs">[Huanshui River]</span>。发源于甘肃省西倾山</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 山名 <span class="encs">[Huanshan Mountain]</span>。在今江苏省铜山县东北。如:桓山之泣(谓家人离散的悲痛);桓山鸟(喻离别的痛苦)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>桓</strong> <span class="dicpy">huán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[big]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玄王桓拨。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·长发》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“大也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:桓蒲(祭祀用的大蒲席);桓拨(谓大治)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/桓桓">桓桓</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">桓</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>桓</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄨㄢˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>huán <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2926.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一种植物。</span><span class="gc_yy">《玉篇·木部》：「<b>桓</b>，<b>桓</b>木，叶似柳，皮黄白色。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">《诗经·周颂》的篇名。共一章。根据〈诗序〉：「〈<b><b>桓</b></b>〉，讲武类祃也。〈<b><b>桓</b></b>〉，武志也。」类、祃，皆师祭也。指武王将欲伐殷，治兵祭神，然后克纣。至周公、成王太平之时，诗人追述其事以颂武王之功。<b><b>桓</b></b>者，威武之志。言讲武之时，军师皆武，故取<b><b>桓</b></b>字名篇。本章三句为「绥万邦，娄丰年，天命匪解。」绥，平定。娄，即屡，屡次。解，同「<a href='/hans/懈' target=_blank>懈</a> 」。匪解，不懈怠。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如汉代有桓宽、桓谭。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">参见「[[盘<b><b>桓</b></b>]]」条。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">桓</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              桓·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6853.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>胡官切，音丸。【說文】郵亭表也。【徐曰】表雙立爲桓。漢法，亭表四角建大木，貫以方板，名曰桓表，縣所治兩邊各一。【前漢·尹賞傳】葬寺門桓東。</p><p>又斲木如石<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥓓</span>四植謂之桓，以下棺也。【禮·檀弓】三家視桓楹。</p><p>又【周禮·春官】公執桓圭。</p><p>又桓桓，武貌。【書·牧誓】尚桓桓。【詩·魯頌】桓桓于征。</p><p>又盤桓，難進貌。【易·屯卦】盤桓利居貞。</p><p>又水名。【書·禹貢】西傾因桓是來。</p><p>又【諡法】辟土服遠，克敬勤民，皆曰桓。</p><p>又【方言】桓，憂也。</p><p>又木名。【郭璞云】葉似柳，子似楝。【玉篇】皮黃白色。【山海經】袟周之山木多桓。</p><p>又【酉陽雜俎】無患木，一名桓。</p><p>又盤桓，髻名。【古今註】長安婦人好爲盤桓髻。</p><p>又姓。【姓苑】望出譙郡，漢有桓榮。</p><p>又叶敕倫切，音櫄。【漢高彪詩】古之君子，卽戎忘身。明其果毅，尚其桓桓。</p><p>又叶王權切，音圓。【陶潛·歸去來辭】雲無心以出岫，鳥倦飛而知還。景翳翳以將入，撫孤松以盤桓。還音旋。</p><p>
