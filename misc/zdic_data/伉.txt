class="encs">[match]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下莫之能伉。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">已不能庇其伉俪。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十一年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“敌也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弃其伉俪妃嫔。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“对也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可与乎比伉。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“偶也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:伉合(匹敌相等而配合)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 对敌，抵挡 <span big;noble;lofty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃立皋门，皋门有伉。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·緜》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:伉行(行为高洁;高洁的品德)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 刚直 <span class="encs">[firm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子路</span>性鄙，好勇力，志伉直。——<span class="smcs">《史记·仲尼弟子列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:伉健(刚强健壮);伉侠(刚直仗义);伉直(刚强正直)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 强健 <span class="encs">[strong]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先郡国吏三百石，伉健习骑射者皆从军。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·宣帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:伉王(英明勇健的君王);伉壮(健壮)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 骄傲 <span class="encs">[arrogant;conceited]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太子轻而庶子伉。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·亡征》</span></span></p><p><span
