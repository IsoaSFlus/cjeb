                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">芥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>芥</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～菜〕一年或二年生草本植物，种子黄色，味辛辣，磨成粉末，称&ldquo;芥末&rdquo;，作调味品。按用途分为叶用芥菜（如&ldquo;雪里蕻&rdquo;）；茎用芥菜（如&ldquo;榨菜&rdquo;）；根用芥菜（如&ldquo;大头菜&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>小草，喻轻微纤细的事物：草～。纤～。～舟。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>芥</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～菜〕一年生草本植物，芥菜的变种，叶大，表面多皱纹，叶脉显著，可食。亦作&ldquo;盖菜&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">芥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>芥</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，介声。本义:蔬菜名。即&ldquo;芥菜&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[Indian mustard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">芥，芥菜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天地之行，芥苦味也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脍，春用葱，秋用芥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:芥子(芥菜的种子。颗粒很小，可榨油或制芥末);芥孙(芥菜于霜雪中再发的嫩牙);芥尽(芥子用完。佛教用语。比喻时间之久或劫数之多);芥苔(芥菜开花新生的嫩茎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 小草 <span class="encs">[small grass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">芥为之舟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">似寻针芥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:芥豆(小草和豆子);芥豆大小人家(比喻小户人家);芥舟(比喻小舟);芥拾(比喻轻易地取得)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 梗塞 <span class="encs">[chock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恨无乖崖老，一洗芥蒂胸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《送路都曹》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:芥蒂(细小的梗塞物，比喻积在心中的怨恨或不快);介纳须弥(佛教说法:小小的芥子可包容须弥大山或整个世界。须弥:传说中的古印度山名);芥虿(芥蒂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;介&rdquo; <span class="encs">[knot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:芥羽(指用以角斗的鸡。斗鸡之时，把芥末播撒在鸡的羽毛上，迷蒙对方鸡的眼而取胜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 比喻轻微纤细的事物 <span class="encs">[trifle;petty thing;minor matter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:草芥;纤芥;芥视(轻视，不看重);芥舟(小船)</span></p>

<p>又纖芥，細微貌。【繁露&middot;王道篇】《春秋》記纖芥之失。</p>
<p>又蔕芥，見蔕字註。</p>
<p>又【集韻】訖黠切，音戛。小草。</p>
<p>又叶居吏切，音記。【王粲&middot;浮淮賦】軸轤千里，名卒億計。運兹威以赫怒，淸海隅之舞芥。　【直音】作芥。芥从艸作。</p>
