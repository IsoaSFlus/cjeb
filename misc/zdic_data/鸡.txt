                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鸡</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鸡</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鷄</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>家禽，品种很多，翅膀短，不能高飞；雄性能报晓，雌性能生蛋：公～。母～。～雏。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> chicken</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Huhn  (S, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> poule,coq,poulet</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鸡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鸡</strong></p>
<p>鶏、雞 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从鸟，奚声。本义:家禽名。雄鸡可以报晓) 鸟纲雉科家禽，品种很多，喙短锐，有冠与肉髯，翅膀短，尤指普通家鸡 (Gallus gallus ) <span class="encs">[chicken]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雞，知时兽也。从隹，奚声。籀文从鳥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雞者，小兽，主司时起居人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五行志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食菽与雞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雞曰翰音。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巽为雞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">工商执雞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宗伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡犬相闻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故人具鸡黍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孟浩然</span><span class="smcs">《过故人庄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡栖于厅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鸡男(雄鸡);鸡坊(养鸡场);鸡肤(鸡皮。比喻皮肤粗糙而多皱纹);鸡鹜(鸡和鸭)</span></p>

                              雞·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/96DE.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古兮切【集韻】【韻會】堅奚切【正韻】堅溪切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音稽。【說文】知時畜也。【玉篇】司晨鳥。【爾雅&middot;釋畜】雞大者蜀。蜀子雓，未成雞僆，絕有力奮。【疏】此別雞屬也。【春秋&middot;說題辭】曰：雞爲積陽，南方之象，火陽精物炎上，故陽出雞鳴，以類感也。【易&middot;說卦】巽爲雞。【書&middot;泰誓】牝雞無晨。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大宗伯】六摯，工商執雞。【禮&middot;曲禮】雞曰翰音。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋鳥】鶾，天雞【註】鶾雞赤羽。【逸周書】文鶾若彩雞，成王時蜀人獻之。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋蟲】螒，天雞。【註】小蟲，黑身赤頭，一名莎雞，又曰樗雞。【詩&middot;豳風】六月莎雞振羽。【爾雅翼】一名梭雞。一名酸雞。</p>
<p>又雞人，官名。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;雞人】掌共雞牲，辨其物，大祭祀夜嘑旦，以嘂百官。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;明堂位】灌尊，夏后氏以雞彝。</p>
<p>又地名。【春秋&middot;襄三年】同盟于雞澤。【註】在廣平曲梁縣西南。</p>
<p>又【昭二十三年】吳敗頓胡、沈蔡、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>許之師于雞父。【註】雞父，楚地。【戰國策】負雞次之典。【前漢&middot;地理志】鬱林郡，雍雞縣。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】明正統，陝西苑馬寺監正雞鳴時。　【說文】籀文作鷄。互詳鳥部鷄字註。</p>
