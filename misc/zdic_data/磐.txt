                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>磐</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pán.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>大石，纡回层迭的山石：～石（厚而大的石头）。～互（交相勾连）。～辟（退缩回旋）。～礴（壮大；雄伟）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">盘</span>”，盘桓。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>磐</strong> <span class="dicpy">pán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石，般声。本义:纡回层叠的山石，巨石) 同本义 <span class="encs">[monolith]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">竭磐石。——</span><span class="diczx2">木华</span><span class="smcs">《海赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 字亦作<span class="yhcs">“盤”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">磐，大石。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此所谓盘石之宗也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孝文纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国安于盤石。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:磐陀石(不平的石头);磐固(如磐石般稳固);磐磨(大石，磨)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>磐</strong> <span class="dicpy">pán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“盘”</span>。回旋;盘曲 <span class="encs">[spiral]</span>。如:磐索(盘旋;回绕);磐辟(盘旋回转的样子)</p><p>◎ <strong>磐</strong> <span class="dicpy">pán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[huge]</span>。如:磐礴(雄壮;宏伟);磐磐(大，巨大;层叠盘结);磐维(旧指身负重任、拱卫国家的皇族)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 牢固 <span class="encs">[firm]</span>。如:磐峙(安然耸立);磐结(牢固勾结)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/磐石">磐石</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/78D0.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】薄官切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】蒲官切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音槃。大石也。【易·漸卦】鴻漸于磐。【註】磐山，石之安者。【疏】山中石磐紆，故稱磐也。</p><p>又磐礴。【郭璞·江賦】荆門闕竦而磐礴。【註】磐礴。廣大之貌。</p><p>又磐牙。【後漢·滕撫傳】盜賊羣起，磐牙連歲。【註】磐牙，謂相連結。</p><p>又通作盤。【前漢·孝文紀】磐石之宗。【荀子·富國篇】國安於磐石。</p><p>又或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2544D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥑍</span>。</p>
