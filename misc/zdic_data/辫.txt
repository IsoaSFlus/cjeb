                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>辫</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">辮</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">biàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>把头发分股编成的带状物：发（<span class="dicpy">fà</span>）～。～子（ａ．发辫；ｂ．像辫子的东西；ｃ．喻把柄，如“抓～～”）。</li><li>像辫子的东西：蒜～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> braid; pigtail, plait, queue</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tresser,tresse,natter</p></div>
