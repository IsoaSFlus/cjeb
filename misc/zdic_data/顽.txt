						<p>● <strong>顽</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">頑</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">w&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>愚钝：愚～。冥～不灵（愚昧顽钝不灵活）。</li>	<li>不容易变化或动摇：～固。～强。～敌。～症。刁～。负隅～抗。</li>	<li>淘气，胡闹：～皮。～童。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/玩">玩</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> obstinate, stubborn; recalcitrant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> dumm, unwissend, unsinnig  (Adj)​,frech, ungezogen, rüpelhaft, schelmisch  (Adj)​,starrköpfig, stur, hartnäckig, widerspenstig, eigensinnig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> résistant,têtu,opiniâtre,conservateur (personne)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">顽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>顽</strong></p>
<p>頑 <span class="dicpy">w&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>)，元声。从页。本义:难劈开的囫囵木头)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[log split difficultly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纤枝无萧洒，顽干空突兀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《画松》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 愚顽之人;顽固派 <span class="encs">[stupid and stubborn person;diehards]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:顽军(顽固派的军队)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 凶恶的人 <span class="encs">[ferocious perosn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂惜战斗死，为君扫凶顽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《豫章行》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>顽</strong></p>
<p>頑 <span class="dicpy">w&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引申为浑圆 <span class="encs">[perfectly round]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻</span><span class="diczx2">平乐</span>以下，四顾皆土山，而巉厉之石，不挺于陆而藏于水矣。盖山自此而顽，水自此而险也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 顽固，不驯服 <span class="encs">[headstrong;stubborn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顽童穷固。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;郑语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:顽算(耍弄，算计);刁顽(狡猾顽固);顽户(旧时官府指拖欠赋税之家);顽陋(顽固鄙陋);顽竖(顽劣的小子);顽躯(顽健的身体);顽猾(顽皮狡猾)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 头脑迟钝; 愚蠢 <span class="encs">[insensate;stupid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顽，愚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父顽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疑自苗人顽，恃险不终役。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《自蜀江至洞庭湖口有感而作》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冥顽不灵;顽仙(愚笨无知的仙人);顽昧(顽劣无知);顽疏(愚劣粗疏);顽卤(愚昧钝迟。或作&ldquo;顽鲁&rdquo;);顽贪(顽愚贪婪);顽恶(愚蠢桀骜);顽愚(愚鲁，资质低下迟钝);顽鄙(顽钝鄙拙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 坚硬<span class="encs">[hard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蛮兵如此顽皮，非火攻安能取胜?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三侠五义》</span></span></p>
