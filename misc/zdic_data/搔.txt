                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>搔</strong> <span class="dicpy">sāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，蚤声。本义:用指甲轻刮)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[scratch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搔，括也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而敬仰搔之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“摩也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">足可搔而绝。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·枚乘传》</span>。集注:<span class="yhcs">“谓抓也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白头搔更短，浑欲不胜簪。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春望》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:搔背(以指甲搔脊背);搔掐(以指甲抓刮);搔扪(爬搔抚摸);搔摸(呵痒);搔首(爬搔头部);搔耙(以指爪或他物抓刮爬梳)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“骚”</span>。骚扰;骚乱 <span class="encs">[annoy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">残贼天下，万民搔动。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·兵略训》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">吴起</span>长兵攻取， <span class="diczx2">楚</span>人搔动，相与泣 <span class="diczx2">悼王</span>。——<span class="smcs">《盐铁论·非鞅》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所在搔扰，更为烦苛。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·陆凯传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:搔动(骚动;扰乱);搔扰(动乱不安，扰乱);搔搔(忧虑的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/搔背扒子">搔背扒子</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/搔耳捶胸">搔耳捶胸</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/搔首踟蹰">搔首踟蹰</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/搔首弄姿">搔首弄姿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/搔头">搔头</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/搔头摸耳">搔头摸耳</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/搔头弄姿">搔头弄姿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/搔痒">搔痒</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
