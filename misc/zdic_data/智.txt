                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">智</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>智</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>聪明，见识：～力。～慧。～商。～育。～能。～谋。才～。机～。理～。明～（①考虑问题周到、严密；②有远见）。足～多谋。利令～昏。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wisdom, knowledge, intelligence</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Klugheit, Lebensweisheit (= Lebenserfahrung)​  (S)​,Wissen  (S)​,Wissenschaft  (S)​,Zhi  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sagesse</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">智</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>智</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从日，从知，知亦声。&ldquo;知&rdquo;的后起字。本义:聪明，智力强) 同本义 <span class="encs">[resourceful;wise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后智生于忧患。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span>引<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甚智其子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说难》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可谓智。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">智术浅短。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少年智则国智。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《少年中国说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:智赛隋何，机强陆贾(二人均为汉、楚时代智囊人物。意为聪明经顶，灵活异常);智数(才智;见识)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>智</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 智慧，智谋 <span class="encs">[intelligence wisdom;resourcefulness]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">智，烛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法言&middot;脩身》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">智，性也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;显学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">智者，心之府也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;俶真》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时能变谓之智。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡人之智，能见已然，不能见将然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《治安策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:智着(智慧;知识);才智(智慧和才能);智刃(智慧之刃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 计谋;策略 <span class="encs">[stratagem]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道德上通而智故消灭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;览冥》</span>。注:&ldquo;智故，巧诈也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:智计(计谋;智谋);智术(指君主通晓控制和使用臣下的策略、手段);智量(计策，计谋);智算(谋划，计算);智虑(智谋;谋划)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 知识 <span class="encs">[knowledge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所以知之在人者谓之知。知有所合谓之智。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>通&ldquo;知&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">zhī</span>)。知道，认识<span class="encs">[know]</span></p>
