                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>筹</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">籌</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄡˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>计数的用具，多用竹子制成：～策（➊古代计算用具，以木制成的小棍或小片；➋计策）。～算。～码。略胜一～。竹～。酒～。</li>
	<li>谋划：～款。～备。～措。～划。～集。～建。运～。统～。一～莫展。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">筹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>筹</strong></p>
<p>籌 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，夀声。本义:计数的用具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 壶矢。古代投壶用的签子，形如箭笴<span class="encs">[arrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筹，壶矢也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。谓投壶之矢为 筹。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筹室中五扶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;投壶》</span>。注:&ldquo;矢也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">箭筹八十。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 签筹;算筹 <span class="encs">[chip]</span>。木或象牙等制成的小棍儿或小片儿，用来计数或作为领取的物品的凭证</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射者中，弈者胜，觥筹交错。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:竹筹;筹马(古时投壶计数的用具)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>筹</strong></p>
<p>籌 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 算计 <span class="encs">[plan;map out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">运筹帷幄之中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:筹设(计划设置);筹计(盘算);筹昏(谓无法算计)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 策划 <span class="encs">[plan;plot;scheme]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命康与四卿及同志速设法筹救。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:统筹(统一筹划);筹运(制定策略;谋划。犹运筹);筹历(推算历法)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 筹措。谋划措办;设法筹集 <span class="encs">[plan and prepare]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:筹度(谋划;计划);筹借(设法借用;筹划借款)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>筹</strong></p>
<p>籌 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>条，个，位。用以指人的多少</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">背后只见赶上三筹好汉，也将杀来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
