                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">妇</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>妇</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">婦</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>已婚的女子：～人。少（<span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;o </span>）～。</li>
	<li>妻，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">夫</span>&rdquo;相对：夫～。</li>
	<li>儿媳：～姑（婆媳）。媳～。</li>
	<li>泛指女性：～女。～孺（妇女儿童）。～幼。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> married women; woman; wife</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">妇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>妇</strong></p>
<p>婦、媍 <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，左边是&ldquo;帚&rdquo;，右边是&ldquo;女&rdquo;。从女持帚，表示洒扫。本义:已婚的女子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[married woman]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婦，服也。从女，持帚，洒埽也。会意。谓服事人者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女子谓之妇人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妇人侠床。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士丧礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;妻妾子姓也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士曰妇人，庶人曰妻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听妇前致词，三男</span><span class="diczx2">邺城</span>戍。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:少妇;孕妇;媳妇;妇驵(交易时的女介绍人);妇政(妇人参与政事);妇人家(已婚妇女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指妇女，常指成年女子 <span class="encs">[woman]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵有健妇把锄犁，禾生陇亩无东西。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妇职(妇女应做的事);妇饰(妇女的打扮妆饰);妇师(妇女的模范);妇仪(妇女的容德规范);妇德(妇女贞顺的德行);妇道家(妇道人，妇道人家。女人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 妻子 <span class="encs">[wife]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">门前冷落鞍马稀，老大嫁作商人妇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十七为君妇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;焦仲卿妻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:新妇(新娘);夫妇(夫妻);妇子(妻与子);妇氏(妇家。妻子的娘家);妇翁(妻父)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 儿媳 <span class="encs">[daughter-in-law]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子之妻曰妇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父殁而子嗣，姑死而妇代。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;偶会》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非为织作迟，君家妇难为。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妇姑(婆媳)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>妇</strong></p>
<p>婦 <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">婦</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“妇”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/婦">婦</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集下" target="_blank">丑集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集下&amp;bs=女" target="_blank">女</a>】
                              婦·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5A66.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22F70.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢽰</span>【唐韻】房九切【集韻】【韻會】扶缶切【正韻】房缶切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音阜。【說文】服也。【爾雅&middot;釋親】子之妻爲婦。</p>
<p>又女子已嫁曰婦。婦之言服也，服事於夫也。【禮&middot;昏義】婦人先嫁，三月敎以婦德、婦言、婦容、婦功。</p>
<p>又【郊特牲】婦人，從人者也。幼從父兄，嫁從夫，夫死從子。</p>
<p>又冢婦、長婦、介婦，衆婦也。見【內則】。</p>
<p>又嬪婦，化治絲枲。見【周禮&middot;天官】。</p>
<p>又世婦，后宮官，所謂二十七世婦也。</p>
<p>又屬婦，婦之窮獨，當聯屬者。【書&middot;梓材】至于屬婦。</p>
<p>又嫠婦，寡婦也。【蘇軾&middot;赤壁賦】泣孤舟之嫠婦。</p>
<p>又好貌。【荀子&middot;樂論篇】其容婦。</p>
<p>又物類之隂者亦曰婦。【埤雅】鵓鳩隂則屛逐其婦，晴則呼之。</p>
<p>又【韻府】蟋蟀一名吟蛩，濟南呼爲嬾婦。</p>
<p>又江中有魚名白旂，其脂可然，用之照歌舞則明，照紡績則昏，世謂嬾婦所化。</p>
<p>又鷦鷯名巧婦，赤斑蜘蛛名絡絲婦。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>見【本草綱目】。</p>
<p>又梵言婆利耶，華言婦。</p>
<p>又【正韻】防父切，音附。【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>琳&middot;飮馬長城窟】邊城多健少，內舍多寡婦。作書與內舍，便嫁莫留住。</p>
<p>又叶芳尾切，音斐。【楚辭&middot;天問】水濱之木，得彼小子，夫何惡之，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/217D2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡟒</span>有莘之婦。　通作負。別作媍。</p>
