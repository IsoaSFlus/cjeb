                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>陟</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˋ</span></p><ol><li>登高：～山。</li><li>晋升，进用：黜～（指官吏的进退升降）。“～罚臧否，不宜异同”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">陟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>陟</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形。从阜，从步。左边是山坡，右边是两只向上的脚，表示由低处向高处走。本义:由低处向高处走;升;登高)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，与<span class="yhcs">“降”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[climb up;ascend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陟，登也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陟，陞也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陟降庭止。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·闵予小子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝陟帝位。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">省幽明以黜陟。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陟彼崔嵬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·卷耳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人陟降大艰。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《井铭并序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:陟屺(登屺山。比喻思念母亲。屺，无草木的山);陟岵(登岵山。比喻思念父亲。岵，草木繁茂的山)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 登程，上路 <span class="encs">[set out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若升高，必自下;若陟遐，必自迩。——<span class="smcs">《书·太甲下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:陟遐(远行);陟卓(远行);陟涉(跋涉)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 晋升 <span class="encs">[promote to a higher office]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陟罚臧否，不宜异同。(臧，善。否，恶。臧否，奖善惩恶。臧、否，这里都是动词。)——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:陟罚(提拔与惩罚);陟黜(进用与贬黜);陟劝(提升与奖励);陟明(进用贤明)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 升遐，升天 <span class="encs">[(of emperor) die]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟新陟王，毕协赏罚。——<span class="smcs">《书·康王之诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:陟配(天子升天后，于祭天时配享)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/965F.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20339.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠌹</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>竹力切，音稙。【爾雅·釋詁】陟，陞也。【說文】登也。【書·舜典】汝陟帝位。</p><p>又【太甲】若陟遐，必自邇。</p><p>又【立政】其克詰爾戎兵，以陟禹之迹。【詩·周南】陟彼崔嵬。</p><p>又【廣韻】進也。【書·舜典】三載考績，三考黜陟幽明。</p><p>又【玉篇】高也。【爾雅·釋山】山三襲陟。【疏】山之形若三山重累者名陟。</p><p>又人名。【書·君奭】在太戊時則有若伊陟臣扈。</p><p>又【集韻】的則切，音得。【周禮·春官】大卜掌三夢之灋，一曰致夢，二曰觭夢，三曰咸陟。【註】陟之爲言得也。讀如王德翟人之德。言夢之皆得也。　【集韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20261.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠉡</span>徏徝騭。</p>
