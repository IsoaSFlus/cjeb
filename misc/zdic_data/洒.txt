                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">洒</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>洒</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">灑</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎ.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄚˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>使水或其他东西分散地落下：～水。～扫。～泪。</li>
	<li>东西散落：粮食～了。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>洒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/洗">洗</a>&rdquo;，洗涤。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sprinkle; scatter; pour; to wipe away; to shiver</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Berieselung  (S)​,berieseln, besprengen  (V)​,sprenkeln  (V)​,spritzen  (V)​,verschütten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> répandre,arroser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">洒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>洒</strong></p>
<p>灑 <span class="dicpy">sǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，西声。本义:把水散布在地上)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[splash;ched;sprinkle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">洒，滌水也。古文以为灑掃言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">洒，濯也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一洒之者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">洒扫室堂及庭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:黎明即起，洒扫庭除;洒削(洒水以磨刀);洒泼(胡乱挥霍);扫地先洒些水;洒酒(把酒浇洒在地上);洒削(洒水磨刀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 东西散落 <span class="encs">[drop;spill;scatter;litter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以灰洒毒之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;赤犮氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌五寝之掃除粪洒之事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;隶仆》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弗洒弗掃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;山有枢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父生不得供备洒埽之臣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茅飞渡江洒江郊，高者挂罥长林梢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:洒消毒水;洒敌敌畏;别把汤洒了;洒派(分派);洒口(张口辱骂);洒花(撒花);洒签;洒调(甩开)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挥洒。多指书写 <span class="encs">[wield]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:洒墨(挥毫书写、作画);洒翰(挥笔书写)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
