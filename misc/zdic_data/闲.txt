                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闲</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>闲</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">閑</span>、①&mdash;④閒）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>没有事情；没有活动与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">忙</span>&rdquo;相对：游手好～。没有～工夫。</li>
	<li>房屋、器物等放着不用：～置。～房。机器别～着。</li>
	<li>没有事情做的时候：农～。忙里偷～。</li>
	<li>与正事无关的：～谈。～人免进。～话。</li>
	<li>栅栏。</li>
	<li>防御：防～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fence, guard; defend; idle time</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Freizeit, Muße  (S)​,unbeschäftigt  (Adj)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>闲</strong></p>
<p>閑 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从门中有木。本义:栅栏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[railings]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲，阑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人闲有家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;家》</span>。注;&ldquo;阑也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日闲舆卫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;大畜》</span>。注:&ldquo;阂也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舍则守王闲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;虎贲氏》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;闲，梐枑。&rdquo; <span class="diczx2">贾公彦</span>疏:&ldquo;闲与梐枑皆禁卫之物。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 马厩，关养马的地方 <span class="encs">[stable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子十有二闲，马六种。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;夏官》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">龙与闲驹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;百官表》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲厩(古代皇家养牲口的地方);闲驹(养马之所)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 伦理道德的规范、界限 <span class="encs">[norm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌十有二闲之政教。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;痩人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大德不逾闲，小德出入可也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子张》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 阻隔 <span class="encs">[bounds]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亲非其肤，&hellip;中心闲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲间(间隔)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>闲</strong></p>
<p>閑 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 限制，约束 <span class="encs">[restrict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽收放心，闲之维艰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;毕命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲之以义。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲检(约束检点);闲节(限制;约束)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 熟悉、熟练。通&ldquo;娴&rdquo; <span class="encs">[be familiar;be skillful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲于兵甲，习于战攻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵入则振旅，以闲习之，因于彼狩，存不忘亡，安不忘危。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露&middot;五行逆顺》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">谢安石</span>有庙堂之量，不闲将略。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《肥水之战》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲习(熟习);闲晓(熟悉);闲博(熟习博通)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 防止 <span class="encs">[guard against;hinder;check]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">建极闲邪。(建立准则，防止邪说。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《天论》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>闲</strong></p>
<p>閑、閒 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 闲暇 <span class="encs">[idle;unoccupied]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">止于坐隅兮，貌甚闲暇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《鹏鸟赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲来垂钓。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《行路难》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲驱</span><span class="diczx2">泾水</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人闲桂花落(&ldquo;闲&rdquo;通&ldquo;閒&rdquo;)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="diczx2"><span class="smcs">《鸟鸣涧》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田家少闲月，五月人倍忙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《观刈麦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲男女(糊涂虫，蠢傢伙);闲踢蹬(闲游逛);闲戏(闲嬉。游戏，玩耍);闲遥遥(闲邀邀，闲摇摇。悠闲自在或闲散无聊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 空着，没使用 <span class="encs">[vacant;not in use]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又多闲田。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">野无闲田。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲田闲地;闲屋(无人居住的房屋);闲壤(闲置的土地);闲园(荒园)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 悠闲 <span class="encs">[leisurely and carefree]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲抱琵琶寻旧曲，远山眉黛绿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">前蜀</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韦庄</span><span class="smcs">《谒金门》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲晏(悠闲安逸);闲素(悠闲纯朴);闲肆(悠闲自然);闲蹓(闲逛。散步)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 与正事无关的 <span class="encs">[have no truck with one&rsquo;s proper business]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:闲是闲非(无关紧要的是是非非);闲冤家(与自己利害无关反同人结仇的人);闲账(与己无关的事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 空虚 <span class="encs">[hollow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">向来的幻境安在，回首总成闲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘克庄</span><span class="smcs">《水调歌头&middot;和西外判宗湖楼韵之三》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲宵(寂寞无聊的夜晚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 安静 <span class="encs">[quiet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">质真而素朴，闲静而不躁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲亭独坐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲裕(安静从容);闲旷(安静空旷);闲邃(闲静深远);闲默(安静沉默)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 娴雅。通&ldquo;娴&rdquo; <span class="encs">[refined]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妖冶闲都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美女妖且闲，採桑歧路间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《美女篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲整(闲雅整洁);闲媚(娴雅柔美;闲雅妩媚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[large]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲显轩敞，云屋晧旰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;曹植&middot;七启》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 空阔;宽大 <span class="encs">[empty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">像设居室，静闲安些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟水闲，乾坤大，四面云无遮碍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张养浩</span><span class="smcs">《普天乐》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闲燥(宽敞);闲敞(广阔空旷)</span></p>

<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 空闲的时间 <span class="encs">[leisure]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 闲职 <span class="encs">[job with little work]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">閑</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“闲”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/閑">閑</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閑·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9591.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】戸閒切【集韻】【韻會】何閒切【正韻】何艱切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音閒。【說文】闌也。从門，中有木。【徐曰】閑，猶闌也。以木歫門也。會意。【廣韻】防也，禦也，法也。【易&middot;乾卦】閑邪存其誠。【疏】言防閑邪惡，當自存其誠實也。</p>
<p>又【家人】閑有家。【疏】正義曰：治家之道在初，卽須嚴正立法防閑也。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;畢命】雖收放心，閑之維艱。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】大也。【詩&middot;商頌】旅楹有閑。【疏】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>列其楹，有閑然而大。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;魏風】桑者閑閑兮。【傳】閑閑然，男女無別往來之貌。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】習也。【詩&middot;秦風】遊于北園，四馬旣閑。【傳】閑，習也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;經解】以之田獵有禮，故戎事閑也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】馬闌也。【前漢&middot;百官公卿表】龍馬閑駒。【註】閑闌，養馬之所也。故曰閑駒。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;校人】天子十有二閑。【註】每廐爲一閑。【左傳&middot;成十八年註】每廐爲一閑，閑有二百一十六匹。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;虎賁氏】舎則守王閑。【註】閑，梐枑。【疏】閑與梐枑，皆禁衞之物也。</p>
<p>又閡也。【易&middot;大畜】日閑輿衞。【註】閑，閡也。衞，護也。進得其時，雖涉艱難，而無患也。輿雖遇閑，而故衞也。【疏】進得其時，涉難無患，雖曰有人欲閑閡車輿，乃是防衞見護，故曰閑輿衞也。</p>
<p>又動搖也。【詩&middot;大雅】臨衝閑閑。【傳】閑閑，動搖也。</p>
<p>又【荀子&middot;勸學篇】多見曰閑。</p>
<p>又【博雅】閑，遮也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶音賢。【曹植&middot;王粲誄】發言可詠，下筆成篇。何道不洽，何藝不閑。　【正字通】閒暇、閒宂與閑音同義別。潘岳閒居賦，淸<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25846.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥡆</span>敞閑，本作閒，吳棫韻補引賦攺作閑。字彙引入閑註，譌誤與韻補同。正韻十刪閒註：俗作閑。分閒、閑爲二。閑註，闌也，習也。</p>
<p>又訓散也，宂也，暇也。亦作嫺。嫺註：通作閑。合閒閑爲一，自相矛盾。閑習之閑或借嫺，防閑、馬閑，古今未有攺从嫺者，汎云閑亦作嫺，尤非。</p>
