                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">杏</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>杏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄥˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>落叶乔木，叶卵形，花白色或淡红色，果实称&ldquo;杏儿&rdquo;、&ldquo;杏子&rdquo;，酸甜，可食：～仁儿。～黄。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> apricot; almond</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Aprikose, Marille (lat: Prunus armeniaca)​  (S, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> abricot,abricotier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">杏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>杏</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 木名。杏树 <span class="encs">[apricot;almond]</span>。一种落叶乔木，叶子宽卵形，花单性，白色或粉红色，果实圆形，成熟时黄红色，味酸甜</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杏，杏果也。从木，可省声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，尚省声。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五沃之土，其木宜杏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>游缁帷之林，坐杏坛之上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桃李梅杏，楂梨姜桂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:杏苑(杏树园林;泛指新科进士游宴处);杏林(见杏田典故。以杏林代指良医，并以&ldquo;杏林春满&rdquo;、&ldquo;誉满杏林&rdquo;等称颂医术高明);杏林得意(借指进士及第)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 杏花 <span class="encs">[apricot flower]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桃未芳菲杏未红，冲寒先喜笑东风。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:杏雨(杏花雨);杏腮(指杏花);杏装(杏花的装扮。指粉红色);杏殇(杏的花苞凋落。喻婴儿夭折);杏靥(指杏花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 杏仁 <span class="encs">[apricot kernel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杏粥犹堪食，榆羹已稍煎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《清明日忆诸弟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:杏丹(方士以杏仁为主要原料所制的一种成药。传说食之能令人颜色美好);杏酪(杏仁粥。古代多为寒食节食品)</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">植物名。薔薇科櫻屬，落葉喬木。樹皮帶赤色，葉卵形。三月開淡紅色花朵，花、葉像梅。果實扁圓、肥厚、味稍酸。核裡有仁，可食亦可入藥。花與果實也稱為「杏」。</span></p></li>
