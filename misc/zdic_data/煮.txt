                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>煮</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˇ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 把东西放在水里，用火把水烧开：烹～。～饭。～字（喻书生玩味文字）。～豆燃箕（喻骨肉相残）。～鹤焚琴（喻大煞风景的事）。</p>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>煮</strong></p>
<p>煑 <span class="dicpy">zhǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，者声。本义:把东西放在有水的锅里加热使熟) 同本义 <span class="encs">[boil;cook]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东煮海水为盐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命妪煮芋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妪煮芋进。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时挑野菜和根煮，旋斫生柴带叶烧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜荀鹤</span><span class="smcs">《山中寡妇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若是射得下来，村房人家，讨些水，煮瀑得熟，也得充饥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:煮瀑(即煮泡，放在水中煮);煮爆(煮);煮汤(熬药);煮海(煮海水为盐);煮井(煮盐井之水为盐);煮汁(药物和水熬成汁液);煮酒(烫酒，酿酒;热的酒);煮雪(用雪水烧茶或作饭)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>煮</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>煮盐，熟盐 <span class="encs">[salt obtained by evaporation]</span>。熬干含盐的水分，提取食盐</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>有 <span class="diczx2">渠展</span>之盐， <span class="diczx2">燕</span>有 <span class="diczx2">辽东</span>之煮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;地数》</span></p>
