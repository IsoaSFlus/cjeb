                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赛</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">賽</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sài  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sài.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄞˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>比胜负，比好坏，竞争：比～。竞～。～场。</li>    <li>胜似，比得过：一个～一个，都不一般。</li>    <li>好似，比得上：简直～真的。</li>    <li>旧时祭祀酬报神恩的迷信活动：～神。～会。～社。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，塞省声。本义:行祭礼以酬神)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[give
