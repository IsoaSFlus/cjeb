                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阜</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨字形，像山崖边的石磴形。用以表示地势或升降等意义。阜作左边偏旁，楷书写成阝。本义:土山)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阜，大陆也。山无石者，象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无石曰阜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释丘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如山如阜，如冈如陵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;天保》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸象以鼻破阜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">牛肃</span><span class="smcs">《纪闻》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阜陵(丘陵);阜陆(高地);阜丘(土山);阜垤(小土丘);土阜(土山)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指山 <span class="encs">[mountain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有物于此，生于山阜，处于室堂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阜颠(山顶);阜积(堆积如山)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阜</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 丰富;富有 <span class="encs">[rich;wealthy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是时世平道治，民物阜康。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">常璩<span class="smcs">《</span>华阳国志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阜财(财富殷盛);阜康(富庶安康);阜货(财货盛多)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 高 <span class="encs">[high]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泛泛</span><span class="diczx2">淇</span>右，有堂孔阜。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">虞集</span><span class="smcs">《赋卫节妇王妇人》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阜</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中" target="_blank">戌集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中&amp;bs=阜" target="_blank">阜</a>】
                              阜·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/961C.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠻰</span>【唐韻】【集韻】房九切【韻會】扶缶切【正韻】房缶切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䘀。【爾雅&middot;釋地】大陸曰阜。【說文】山無石者。【釋名】土山曰阜，言高厚也。【詩&middot;小雅】如山如阜。</p><p>又大也。【書&middot;周官】阜成兆民。【註】大成兆民之性命。</p><p>又【玉篇】肥也。【詩&middot;秦風】駟驖孔阜。【疏】馬甚肥大也。</p><p>又盛也。【詩&middot;鄭風】火烈具阜。【傳】阜，盛也。</p><p>又多也。【詩&middot;小雅】爾殽旣阜。【傳】阜，猶多也。</p><p>又長也。【魯語】助生阜也。</p><p>又山名。【左傳&middot;文十六年】楚大饑，戎伐其西南，至于阜山。</p><p>又地名。【禮&middot;明堂位】封周公于曲阜。</p><p>又【左傳&middot;文十五年】置諸堂阜。</p><p>又阜城，屬渤海郡，阜陵，屬九江郡。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>見【前漢&middot;地理志】。</p><p>又阜螽，蟲名。【詩&middot;召南】趯趯阜螽。【傳】阜螽，蠜也。</p><p>又【韻會】佛寺曰香界，亦曰香阜。</p><p>又叶敷救切，音覆。【梁鴻詩】惟季春兮華阜，麥含英兮方秀。哀茂時兮逾邁，愍芳香兮日臭。【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>第&middot;詩古音考】阜字，可上可去。</p><p>又叶房詭切。【劉邵&middot;趙都賦】羣后紛其旣醉，遠人仡以宴喜。悅皇風之舄奕，羨我邦之殷阜。</p><p>又叶符遇切，音附。【裴秀大蜡詩】告成伊何，年豐物阜。禮<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/276D1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧛑</span>孝祀，介兹萬祜。◎按唐韻正，四十四有之半，古與篠小巧皓四韻通爲一韻。詩鄭風，叔于田，乗乗鴇，兩服齊首，兩驂如手，叔在藪，火烈具阜。小雅，田車旣好，四牡孔阜，東有甫草，駕言行狩。</p><p>又吉日維戊，旣伯旣禱，田車旣好，四牡孔阜，易林，倬然遠咎，辟害高阜，田獲三狐，巨貝爲寶。桓驎七說，超絕壑，踰懸阜。馳猛禽，射勁鳥。王粲瑪瑙勒賦，總衆材而課美，信莫臧於瑪瑙，被文采之華飾，雜朱綠於蒼阜。左思魏都賦，矞雲翔龍，澤馬於阜，山圖其石，川形其寶。韻會小補，於紙韻遇韻俱云叶音，非。　【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E0F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨸏</span>。【集韻】同峊。</p>
