                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>我</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">wǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǒ.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄛˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>自称，自己，亦指自己一方：～们。～见（我自己的看法）。～辈。～侪（我们）。自～。～盈彼竭。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> our, us, i, me, my, we</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ich (mir, mich)​ <Personalpronomen 1. Pers.&gt (Pron)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> je,moi</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">我</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>我</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从戈，从戈。&ldquo;我&rdquo;表示兵器。甲骨文字形象兵器形。本义:兵器。基本义:第一人称代词)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 自称;自己 <span class="encs">[I imy;myself]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我，施身自谓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">观我生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;观卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万物皆备于我矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;尽心上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:我行(我这里);我身(我自己;我这个人);我咱(我自。我，我自己);我见犹怜(形容女子容貌姿态美丽动人);我家(自己。我们家);我侬(方言。我)&mdash;&mdash;按:上古时代，&ldquo;吾&rdquo;和&ldquo;我&rdquo;在语法上有分别。&ldquo;吾&rdquo;不用于动词后面作为宾语</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今者吾丧我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 己方;己国 <span class="encs">[we;our]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:敌我友;我每(我们);我伲(方言。我们);我曹(我们);我辈(我等，我们)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>我</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 自己的 <span class="encs">[self]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我生(我之行为);我仪(我的匹配)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示亲密的 <span class="encs">[my dear]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我丈(对老人的亲切称呼);我老彭;我老叶;我东海</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 向一边倾斜的，扭歪的 <span class="encs">[wry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我，顷顿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;谓倾侧也。顷，头不正也。顿，下首也。故引申为顷侧之意。&rdquo;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>我</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>杀 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我伐用张。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;泰誓中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我，古杀字。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>

data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2233F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢌿</span>卜矛不同義。愚謂有我則必及人。故賚<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2233F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢌿</span>卜亦在施身自謂之內也。口部曰：吾，我自稱也。女部曰：姎，女人自稱姎我也。毛詩傳曰：言我也。卬，我也。論語二句而我吾互用。毛詩一句而卬我襍稱。葢同一我義而語音輕重緩急不同。施之於文若自其口出。</span><span class="ywyy">或說我，頃頓也。</span><span class="swdz">謂順側也。頃，頭不正也。頓，下首也。故引申爲頃側之意。賓筵。側弁之俄。箋云。俄，傾貌。人部曰：俄，頃也。然則古文以我爲俄也。古文叚借如此。</span><span class="ywyy">从戈<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20112.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄒</span>。</span><span class="swdz">合二成字不能定其會意形聲者，以<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20112.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄒</span>字不定爲何字也。五可切。十七部。</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20112.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄒</span>，古文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2136E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡍮</span>也。</span><span class="swdz"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2136E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡍮</span>當作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂹</span>。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂹</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2136E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡍮</span>在十七部。然則我以爲形聲也。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰古文殺字。</span><span class="swdz">我从殺，則非形聲。會意亦難說也。殺篆下載古文三。有一略相似者。</span><span
