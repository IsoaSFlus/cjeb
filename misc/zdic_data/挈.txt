						<p>● <strong>挈</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用手提着：提～。提纲～领。</li>
	<li>带，领：～带。～眷。扶老～幼。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/锲">锲</a>&rdquo;，刻。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">挈</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>挈</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;契&rdquo;。契约，书契 <span class="encs">[contract]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今内史稻田租挈重。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;沟洫志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">书挈所录帝王遗事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">卫觊</span><span class="smcs">《魏受禅表》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>挈</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:提起)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lift]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挈，县持也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挈，提也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若挈裘领。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挈壶氏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">班白不提挈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">信</span>挈其手。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;韩信传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">龙尾车者，河滨挈水之器也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《农政全书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:挈瓶(谓提瓶汲水);挈领(提起衣领);提纲挈领;挈壶(悬壶);挈辔(县辔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 带领 <span class="encs">[take along]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挈国之辞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;宣公十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挈其妻子以奔</span><span class="diczx2">曹</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;襄公二十七年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">元</span>兵哨马报知蛮子 <span class="diczx2">海牙</span>，他便挈兵而待。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《英烈传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挈家侨居邑东。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">喊一声，都拖男挈女，呼弟呼兄，一齐跑来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:挈家(携家带眷);挈累(携带家室);挈眷(携带家眷);挈囊(古代尚书携带的紫色袷囊称挈囊，因而也称掌文书之官为挈囊);挈带(带领);挈引(带领引导);挈领(带领)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 提携 <span class="encs">[promote]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">况以两贤王左提右挈，而责杀王，灭</span><span class="diczx2">燕</span>易矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:挈带(提携);挈扶(提携扶助);挈提(提携，扶植);挈维(提携护持);挈携(提携;携带);挈持(扶持)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;契&rdquo;。刻 <span class="encs">[carve]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6308.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】苦結切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】詰結切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>契入聲。【說文】縣持也。【廣韻】提挈也。【禮&middot;王制】班白者不提挈。【周禮&middot;夏官】挈壷氏。【註】世主挈壷水以爲漏。【前漢&middot;張耳<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>餘傳】以兩賢王左提右挈。【註】相扶持也。</p>
<p>又修整也。【荀子&middot;不苟篇】君子挈其辨。而同焉者合矣。【註】謂不煩雜。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】詰計切【正韻】去計切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音契。缺也，又絕也。【司馬相如&middot;封禪書】挈三神之歡。</p>
<p>又以板書之也。【前漢&middot;張湯傳】上所是受而著讞法廷尉挈令。【註】在板挈也。挈獄訟之要也。【史記】作絜。</p>
<p>又與契通。【前漢&middot;溝洫志】內史稻田租挈重，其議減。【註】租挈，收田租之約令也。</p>
<p>又【班固&middot;幽通賦】旦算祀於挈龜。【註】挈，所以然火灼龜者也。【詩&middot;大雅】爰契我龜。【釋文】亦作挈。</p>
<p>又【集韻】奚結切，音頁。亦縣持也。通作絜。</p>
<p>又訖黠切，音戛。【博雅】獨也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>丘八切，音劼。與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26E09.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦸉</span>同。菝<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26E09.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦸉</span>，草名。【禮&middot;月令】王瓜生。【註】萆挈也。卽菝<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26E09.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦸉</span>。萆字原从艸从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20986.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠦆</span>。</p>
