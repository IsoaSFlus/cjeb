                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>咸</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">➋➌鹹</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>〈书〉全，都：～受其益。老少～宜。</li>
	<li>像盐的味道，含盐分多的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">淡</span>&rdquo;相对：～味。～盐。～水湖。</li>
	<li>用盐腌制的：～肉。～鱼。</li>
	<li>六十四卦之一。卦形䷞为艮下兌上。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> together; all, completely; united</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> salzig  (Adj)​,Xian  (Eig, Fam)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">咸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>咸</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。据甲骨文。从戌( <span class="dicpy">xū</span>)，从口。戌是长柄大斧，&ldquo;口&rdquo;指人头。合起来表示大斧砍人头。本义:杀)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咸刘厥敌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;君奭》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 毕;终 <span class="encs">[finish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迄</span><span class="diczx2">始皇</span>三载而咸，时、激、地、保、人事乎?&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《法言》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 感应 <span class="encs">[sense]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">&hellip;窕则不咸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:咸腓(妄动);咸感(阴阳相感，夫妇相和)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>咸</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 普遍都，全部 <span class="encs">[all]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咸，皆也，悉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庶绩咸熙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">周邦咸喜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;崧高》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">村中闻有此人，咸来问讯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咸怃然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">京城学者咸怪其无征。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">属吏咸伏渴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:咸叨福庇(都借光沾了福气);咸宥(全部宽恕);咸臻(全都到达);咸五登三(帝德广被，同于五帝而超于三王)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 共;同 <span class="encs">[together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敦商之旅，克咸厥功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂&middot;閟宫》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>咸</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 普遍 <span class="encs">[general]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小赐不咸，独恭不优。不咸，民不归也;不优，神弗福也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 协和，调和 <span class="encs">[harmonic]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舟中鸣鼓若竞渡，挽人从山石中闻鼓声而咸力。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《天工开物》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 和睦 <span class="encs">[concord]</span>。<span class="diczx3">如:咸平(和平;太平);咸和(协和;和睦)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>咸</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 咸阳(今为地级市，位于陕西西安西北20公里)。中国古代文化的发祥地。公元前350年秦孝公都咸阳，城市宫殿林立。公元前206年为楚项羽焚毁，简称咸 <span class="encs">[Xianyang city]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古乐曲名。即咸池 <span class="encs">[xingci]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:咸英(尧乐<span class="smcs">《咸池》</span>与帝喾乐<span class="smcs">《六英》</span>的并称)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>咸</strong></p>
<p>鹹 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>含盐的 <span class="encs">[salted;salty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:咸鱼;咸肉;咸水;咸菜;咸泪</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】
                              咸·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/54B8.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】胡監切【集韻】【韻會】胡讒切【正韻】胡嵒切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音諴。【說文】皆也。【玉篇】悉也。【書&middot;堯典】庶績咸熙。【左傳&middot;僖二十四年】周公弔二叔之不咸。【註】咸，同也。【魯語】小賜不咸。【註】咸，徧也。【莊子&middot;知北遊】周徧咸三者異名同實，其指一也。【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】上咸五，下登三。【註】師古曰：與五帝皆盛也。</p>
<p>又卦名。【易&middot;咸卦】彖曰：咸，感也。</p>
<p>又【易&middot;雜卦】咸，速也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋丘】左高曰咸丘。</p>
<p>又樂名。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大司樂】大咸。【註】大咸，咸池，堯樂也。</p>
<p>又地名。【史記&middot;秦本紀】孝公十二年，作爲咸陽。</p>
<p>又星名。【前漢&middot;天文志】咸漢星出西北。</p>
<p>又【王充&middot;論衡】任氣卒咸，不揆於人。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓苑】巫咸之後，今東海有之。</p>
<p>又【集韻】居咸切，音緘。【禮&middot;喪大記】凡封，大夫士以咸。【註】咸讀爲緘，今齊人謂棺束爲緘繩。咸或爲椷。【釋文】咸，一本作緘。</p>
<p>又【集韻】古斬切，音鰔。與減同。損也。</p>
<p>又一曰水名，詳水部減字註。</p>
<p>又姓。【前漢&middot;酷吏傳】咸宣，楊人也。【註】咸音減省之減。</p>
<p>又【集韻】公陷切，音䫡。亦與減同。詳水部減字註。</p>
<p>又音憾。【左傳&middot;昭二十一年】窕則不咸。【註】不充滿人心。【釋文】咸，本亦作感，戸暗反。○按感同憾。</p>
<p>又叶餘針切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>。【張衡&middot;舞賦】歌以詠志，舞以旌心。細則聲窕，大則不咸。</p>
