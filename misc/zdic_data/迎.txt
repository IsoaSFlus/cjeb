                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>迎</strong> <span class="dicpy">yíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)。本义:遇，相逢)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[meet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迎，逢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“夆，悟也;逢，遇也，其理一也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以迎岁于东郊。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·时则》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“逆春也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迎日推策。——<span class="smcs">《史记·五帝纪》</span>。正义:<span class="yhcs">“逆也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:迎霜(遇霜)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 迎接;欢迎 <span class="encs">[welcome;greet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冕而亲迎。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·昏义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亲迎于渭。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·大明》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">哀公</span>迎 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·非儒下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每迎女婿固不遣。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:迎风板子(迎风。指新官上任，为树权威先访拿数人杖责四十。然后视情节再行发落);迎猪(赶猪回家);迎枕(迎手。中医号脉时垫在病人腕部的枕头);迎年(迎接新年);迎气(迎接祭祀五帝，祈求五谷丰收);迎将(迎接);迎劳(迎接慰劳);迎新送旧(迎接新来的，辞送离去的);迎銮(迎接皇帝);迎宾(迎接宾客)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 迎合;逢迎 <span class="encs">[pander to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则民严而不迎。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语·入官》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“奉也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">群臣迎阿，独</span><span class="diczx2">怀道</span>执不可。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:迎阿(逢迎阿谀);迎意(测知他人心意，迎合他人心意);迎望(迎合，迎承);迎时(迎合时势);迎逢(迎合;逢迎);迎附(迎合)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 预测;推算 <span class="encs">[forecast;calculate]</span>。如:迎日推筴(经过推算而预知未来的节气历数)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 投降 <span class="encs">[surrender;capitulate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愚谓大计不如迎之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>迎</strong> <span 面向着，正对着 <span class="encs">[be directed at;face]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迎风收栗子，过雨剪花头。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span
