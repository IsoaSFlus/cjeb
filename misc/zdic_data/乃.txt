                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>乃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">nǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǎi.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄞˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>才：今～得之。&ldquo;断其喉，尽其肉，～去&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>是，为：～大丈夫也。</li>
	<li>竟：～至如此。</li>
	<li>于是，就：&ldquo;因山势高峻，～在山腰休息片时&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>你，你的：～父。～兄。&ldquo;家祭无忘告～翁&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乃</strong></p>
<p>廼、迺 <span class="dicpy">nǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 你，你的 <span class="encs">[your]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余嘉乃勋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几败乃公事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谁谓乃公勇者?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;淮南衡山传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王师北定中原日，家祭无忘告乃翁(父亲)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《示儿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乃父(乃翁。你的父亲);乃祖(你的祖父;先祖);乃公(你的父亲);乃兄</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 他的 <span class="encs">[his]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃心在</span><span class="diczx2">咸阳</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《蒿里行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乃眷(他的妻子);乃尊(尊称别人的父亲);乃老(他的父亲);乃堂(他的母亲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 此，这个 <span class="encs">[this]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾闻之，五子不满隅，一子可满朝，非乃子耶?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乃今(如今;从今);乃者(往日;从前)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 这样，如此 <span class="encs">[so]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子无乃称。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;德充符》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:因山势高峻，乃在山腰休息片刻;乃尔(如此;这样);乃若(至于)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>乃</strong> <span class="dicpy">nǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> 是，就是 <span class="encs">[be]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以其乃</span><span class="diczx2">华山</span>之阳名之。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾乃与而君言，汝何为者也?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:真乃英雄好汉;失败乃成功之母;乃是(却是)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>乃</strong> <span class="dicpy">nǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 刚刚，才，表示事情发生得晚或结束得晚 <span class="encs">[just now]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">乃悟前狼假寐，盖以诱敌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 只，仅仅 <span class="encs">[only then]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:唯虚心乃能进步</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 竟，竟然 <span class="encs">[unexpectedly;actually]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 却 <span class="encs">[at the same time]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E43.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2010E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄎</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209E4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠧤</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/223E9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢏩</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/223A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢎧</span>【唐韻】奴亥切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】囊亥切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>柰上聲。語辭。【莊子&middot;逍遙遊】而後乃今培風。</p>
<p>又承上起下之辭。【爾雅&middot;序疏】若乃者，因上起下語。</p>
<p>又繼事之辭。【書&middot;堯典】乃命羲和。</p>
<p>又辭之難也。【公羊傳&middot;宣八年】而者何，難也。乃者何，難也。曷爲或言而，或言乃，乃難乎而也。</p>
<p>又辭之緩也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;小司<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨥</span>】乃致事。【註】乃，緩辭。</p>
<p>又語已辭。【韓愈&middot;鬭雞聮句】一噴一醒然，再接再礪乃。【註】用費誓礪乃鋒刃語也。</p>
<p>又【王禕詩】兹焉舍我去，契闊將無乃。</p>
<p>又爾汝之稱。【書&middot;大禹謨】惟乃之休。【註】乃，猶汝也。</p>
<p>又某也。【禮&middot;雜記】祝稱卜葬虞子孫曰哀，夫曰乃。【註】乃某卜葬其妻某氏。</p>
<p>又彼也。【莊子&middot;大宗師】孟孫氏人哭亦哭，是自其所以乃。</p>
<p>又【唐書&middot;南蠻傳】昔有人見二羊<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28DD6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨷖</span>海岸，彊者則見，弱者入山，時人謂之來乃。來乃者，勝勢也。</p>
<p>又地名。【元史&middot;地理志】新添葛蠻安撫司，都鎮馬乃等處。</p>
<p>又果名。【桂海虞衡志】特乃子，狀似榧，而圓長端正。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】或作廼。【詩&middot;大雅】廼慰廼止，廼左廼右。【前漢&middot;項籍傳】必欲烹廼公。</p>
<p>又【正韻】依亥切，哀上聲。【字彙】款乃，棹船相應聲。黃山谷曰：款乃，湖中節歌聲。【正字通】款乃，本作欸乃。今行船搖櫓，戛軋聲似之。【柳宗元詩】欸乃一聲山水綠。【元結&middot;湖南欸乃曲】讀如矮靄是也。【劉蛻&middot;湖中歌】靄廼。【劉言史&middot;瀟湘詩】曖廼皆欸乃之譌。◎按欸，亞改切，應也。後人因柳集註有云：一本作襖靄。遂直音欸爲襖，乃爲靄，不知彼註自謂別本作襖靄，非謂欸乃當音襖靄也。《正韻》上聲解韻乃音靄，引柳詩：欸乃讀如襖靄。而上聲巧韻襖部不收款。去聲泰韻，乃音愛，亦引柳詩：欸乃讀如懊愛。而去聲效韻奧部不收款。至若旱韻，收款音窾，絕不註明有襖懊二音，此可證款不音襖懊，而欸之譌作款明矣。</p>
<p>又乃有靄音，無愛音。《正韻》增音愛，非。</p>
<p>又《字彙》《正字通》旣明辨款不音襖，欸譌作款，而《字彙》欠部款音襖，棹船相應聲。《正字通》櫓聲，自相矛盾，尤非。</p>
