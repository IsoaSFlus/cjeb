                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>消</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>溶化，散失：烟～云散。～融（亦作&ldquo;消溶&rdquo;）。～失。～逝。～亡。～沉。～极。～化。</li>
	<li>灭掉，除去：～除。～灭。～毒。～炎。</li>
	<li>把时间度过去：～夜。～夏。～闲。～遣。～磨（<span class="dicpy">m&oacute;</span>）。</li>
	<li>减少，损失，耗费：～耗。～损。～退。</li>
	<li>需要：不～说。</li>
	<li>中医学病名：～疾。～渴。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">消</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>消</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，肖声。本义:消除;消灭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[get rid of;eliminate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">消，尽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">消息阴阳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span>。注:&ldquo;消，灭也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">消雰埃于中宸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;消，散也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">热多则筋弛骨消。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;皮部论》</span>。注:&ldquo;消，烁也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸟兽之害人者消，然后人得平土而居之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诏求隐学之士能消灾复异者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;魏志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娘娘一肚子气没地方散去，借酒消愁。&mdash;&mdash;京剧<span class="smcs">《贵妃醉酒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:消索(灭尽，散亡);消炎(消除炎症;消除炎热);消弭(消灭停止)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 消失，完全不存在 <span class="encs">[disappear;vanish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">内君子而外小人，君子道长，小人道消也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;泰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">观其不合而散逐消藏，则未尝不恨其道之难行也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">曾巩</span><span class="smcs">《上杜相公书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:消败(消失，衰败);消挫(消散)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 减削 <span class="encs">[decrease]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">消，减也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心思消缩，齿发凋耗，常恐卒填沟壑，独遗恨于无穷也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">曾巩</span><span class="smcs">《再乞登对状》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其间有户消落之家。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:消折(耗损;亏损);消兵(减少兵员);消沮(削减;减弱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 消磨，打发 <span class="encs">[use;get through]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无酒能消夜，随僧早闭门。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方干</span><span class="smcs">《冬夜泊僧舍》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乐琴书以消忧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;陶潜&middot;归去来辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:消脱(消磨);消日(消磨时光);消取(排遣。取，语助词)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 享受;受用 <span class="encs">[enjoy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一片绿衫消不得，腰金拖紫是何人?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《哭从弟诗》</span></p>
