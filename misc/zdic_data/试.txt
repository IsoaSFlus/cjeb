                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>试</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">試</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>按照预定的想法非正式地做：～车。～图。～航。～问。尝～。～金石。</li>
	<li>考，测验：～场。～卷。～题。笔～。考～。口～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> test, try, experiment</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> experimentieren, probieren, versuchen, testen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> essayer,éprouver,examen</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">试</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>试</strong></p>
<p>試 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，式声。本义:用;任用)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[use;try out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">试，用也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明试以功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今予将试以汝迁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">师干之试。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;采芑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵革不试。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾不试。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">私人之子，百僚是试。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;大东》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵革不试，五刑不用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:试兵(用兵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 尝试 <span class="encs">[try]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">试，尝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可试也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;无妄》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣请试之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我虽不敏，请尝试之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日高人渴漫思茶，敲门试问野人家。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《浣溪沙》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:试啼(试着啼哭);试药郎中(方言。庸医诊病时以病试药);试试缩缩(即畏畏缩缩，不敢大胆前进);试水(尝试品尝茶水)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 初次使用 <span class="encs">[first use]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">思试之斗以觇之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:试酌(初饮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 试验 <span class="encs">[test]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">试用于今日，先帝称之曰能。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又试之鸡，果如</span><span class="diczx2">成</span>言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:试守(试任某一官职);试儿(旧时试测小儿性情和志趣的习俗。也称&ldquo;抓周&rdquo;、&ldquo;试周&rdquo;);试晬(周岁测试小孩);试艺(试验才艺);试年庚(古时风俗在除夕夜聚博，以胜负来作为来年运气的征兆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 考查;考试 <span class="encs">[examination;examine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老人延入坐，知从郡城就童子试归。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及试吏呼名至</span><span class="diczx2">史公</span>， <span class="diczx2">公</span>瞿然注视。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:试验(考查;验证);试判(考试);试官(主持考试的官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 试探;刺探 <span class="encs">[sound out;spy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白发不相负，青阳始试旬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王夫之</span><span class="smcs">《元日折梅次定山韵》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:试风头(剌探情况);试旬(探求;探寻)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;弑&rdquo;。旧称臣杀君、子杀父母等行为 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受命之臣，专征云试。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
