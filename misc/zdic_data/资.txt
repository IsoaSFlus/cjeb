                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>资</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">資</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗˉ</span></p><ol><li>财物，钱财：～源。物～。～财。～金。～费。～料。工～。川～（路费）。</li><li>经营工商业的本钱和财产：～本。外～。独～。合～。～方。</li><li>供给，帮助：～助。～送。～敌。可～借鉴。</li><li>智慧能力：～质。天～。</li><li>出身和经历：～格。～历。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">资</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>资</strong></p><p>資 <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，次声。从贝，与财富有关。本义:钱财)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[money;fund]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">资，货也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">资，川也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怀其资——<span class="smcs">《易·旅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丧乱蔑资。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·板》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">资用乏绝，去</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>而归。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策一》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既至</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>，持千金之资币物，厚遗 <span class="diczx2">秦王</span>宠臣中庶子 <span class="diczx2">蒙嘉</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">应得之资。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄·种植部》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">富者捐资。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中家以上，皆竭资取得。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厚资而遣之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:投资(把资金投入企业);内资;外资;集资;薪资(工资);茶资(茶钱);资业(资产);借资</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 费用 <span class="encs">[expenses]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">问几月之资。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">致马资于有司。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·少仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">军资所出，国以富饶。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:资斧不裕(旅费盘缠不宽裕);资装(盘缠与行装);川资(旅费);邮资(邮费);资值(衣食之值);开资</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 资历;资格 <span class="encs">[qualifications]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昔先君</span><span class="diczx2">仲尼</span>与君先人 <span class="diczx2">伯阳</span>有师资之尊。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·言语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">愔</span>自以资望少。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·郄愔传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">资轻望浅，舆论不孚。——<span class="smcs">《大波》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:资履;资任(资格;资历);资名(资历名望);资级(资格和品级);资秩(资历和品质);资籍(资格履历)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 天赋;天资 <span class="encs">[endowment]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然大王资侮人。——<span
