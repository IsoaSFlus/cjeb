                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>岚</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">嵐</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>山间的雾气：夕～。山～。晓～。～岫（雾气笼罩的山峰）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mountain mist, mountain haze</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dunst im Gebirge  (S, Met)​,feiner Nebel  (S, Met)​,Höhenrauch  (S, Met)​,Arashi ( japanische Boygroup )​  (Eig, Mus)​,Sturm ( in den Bergen )​  (S, Met)​,Unwetter ( in den Bergen )​  (S, Met)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> brouillard,vapeur</p></div>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>岚</strong></p>
<p>嵐 <span class="dicpy">l&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从山，葻( <span class="dicpy">lǎn</span>)声。本义:山林中的雾气) 同本义 <span class="encs">[mountain mist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未夜青岚入，先秋白露团。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《新栽竹》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:岚气(山中雾气);岚光(山间雾气经日光照射而发出的光彩);岚峰(雾气缭绕的山峰);岚雾(山中雾气);岚霭(山中的云雾。岚，山林中的雾气。霭，云气)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5D50.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>盧含切，音婪。【說文】岢嵐，山名。近太原，有渥洼池，出良馬。</p>
<p>又州名。本樓煩王所居，漢爲太原地，後魏置嵐州。</p>
<p>又山氣烝潤也。【謝靈運&middot;晚出西射堂詩】夕曛嵐氣隂。【王維&middot;送方尊師詩】夕陽彩翠忽成嵐。</p>
