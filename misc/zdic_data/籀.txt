                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">籀</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>籀</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～文〕汉字的一种字体。春秋战国时流行于秦国，今存石鼓文是其代表。亦称&ldquo;大篆&rdquo;。</li>	<li>阅读：～读（读书）。～绎（阅读并理出文章的脉络）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> recite, read; style of calligraphy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> entwickeln, entfalten</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">籀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>籀</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[read]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">籀，读书也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卜籀云。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋传》</span>。按，今本<span class="smcs">《左传》</span>凡卦兆之词皆以繇为之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">重籀<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>诸书。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">章炳麟</span><span class="smcs">《检论&middot;订孔下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">睲河海之行、帝王、妃后、臣宰、农工、徒隶之法，无不籀也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《知归子赞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:籀读(诵读);籀书(即籀文)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;抽&rdquo;。抽取;引出<span class="encs">[take out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挥兔豪而匪固，籀鱼网而终灭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李俨</span><span class="smcs">《益州多宝寺道因法师碑文》</span>序</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:籀演(引申;演绎)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>籀</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 汉字的一种文体。一名大篆 <span class="encs">[a style of calligraphy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周宣王</span>史名籀，其所著文字曰籀文。亦曰大篆。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">籀，史籀，</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>太史造大篆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仇校篆籀，篇章毕觌。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:籀文(我国古代书体的一种);籀篆(古代的一种书体。即大篆)</span></p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“籀”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/籒">籒</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上" target="_blank">未集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上&amp;bs=竹" target="_blank">竹</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7C52.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【韻會】【正韻】直又切【集韻】直祐切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音胄。史籒，周宣王太史名，造大篆。【法書攷】籒文者，史籒所作也，與古文大篆小異，後人以名稱書，謂之籒文。《七略》曰：卽周時史官敎學童書也，與孔壁古文異體，卽奇字也。其跡則《石鼓文》存焉。【學古編】李斯旣作小篆，遂以籒文爲大篆。</p><p>又【說文】讀書也。</p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“籀”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/籒">籒</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷五">卷五</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷五&amp;swbs=竹">竹</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7C52.svg">                          <p>讀書也。从竹㩅聲。《春秋傳》曰&ldquo;卜籒&rdquo;云。直又切〖注〗卜籒，占卜認讀。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7C52_4758.svg" /><span class="swbf">(籒)</span><span class="ywyy">讀書也。</span><span class="swdz">言部曰：讀，籒書也。敘目曰：尉律學僮十七巳上始試。諷籒書九千字，乃得爲吏。試字句絕。諷籒連文，謂諷誦而抽繹之，滿九千字皆得六書之恉，乃得爲吏也。此籒字之本義。經傳尟用。周宣王時大史以爲名，因以名所箸大篆曰籒文。迄今學者絕少知其本義者，故於讀下籒書改爲誦書，於敘目釋爲籒文九千字，重悂貤繆，可勝嘆哉。毛傳曰：讀，抽也。方言曰：抽，讀也。抽皆籒之假借。籒者，抽也。讀者，續也。抽引其緒相續而不竆也。亦假紬字爲之，大史公自序：紬史記石室金匱之書。如淳云：抽徹舊書故事而次述之也。亦借繇字爲之，春秋傳⺊筮繇辭，今皆作繇。又俗作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26178.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦅸</span>。據許則作籒。服䖍曰：繇，抽也。抽出吉凶。</span><span class="ywyy">从竹㩅聲。</span><span class="swdz">此形聲包會意。直又切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">春秋傳曰：⺊籒云。</span><span class="swdz">左傳⺊筮皆云繇。此言⺊以該筮也。</span></p>                          </div>
