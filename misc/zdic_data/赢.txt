                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赢</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">贏</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yíng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yíng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>有余利，获利：～利。～余（盈余）。</li>    <li>赌博或比赛获胜：赌输～。</li>    <li>担负：“～粮而景从”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赢</strong></p><p>贏 <span class="dicpy">yíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从贝，从贏，赢( <span class="dicpy">yíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yíng.mp3"></a></span> )省声。贝，古货币，与财富买卖有关。本义:有余)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[have a surplus]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赢，贾有余利也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贾而欲赢而恶嚣乎。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鬻者兼赢。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仓廥空虚，而群县无复赢畜以待用。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">吴莱</span><span class="smcs">《欧阳氏急就章解后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:赢衍(丰盈;盈余);赢财(余财);赢畜(多余财物的积蓄);赢副(多余的东西);赢绌(盈余和亏损);赢落(帐外结余);赢费(积余和耗费);赢数(余数);赢阙(赢余和短缺);赢粮(余粮;粮食有余);赢缩(有余和不足);赢藏(蓄藏的余财)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宽缓;松懈 <span class="encs">[relaxed;slack;loose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大夫君子，昭假无赢。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·云汉》</span>。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“赢，缓也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">严断刑，天地始肃，不可以赢。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 充裕 <span class="encs">[abundant]</span>。赢获(丰富。充实);赢储(充裕的准备)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>赢</strong></p><p>贏 <span class="dicpy">yíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 经商盈利 <span class="encs">[reap profits;earn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赢钱的都已溜回家去睡钱。——<span class="smcs">《官场现形记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:赢绌(赢与亏);赢得猫儿亏了牛(比喻因小失大);赢钱</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 博赛获胜。与输相对 <span class="encs">[win;defeat;beat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">傻大舅输家，没心绪，吃了两碗，便有些醉意，嗔着两个娈童只赶着赢家不理输家了。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:赢家(游戏、赌赛等中获胜的一方)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 天文上五星趋舍而前为赢，退舍为缩 <span class="encs">[advance;waxing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岁星赢缩，以其舍命国…其趋舍而前曰赢，退舍曰缩。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:赢缩(天文学上称岁星的运行较历法所算超前为赢;落后为缩)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 博取，获得 <span class="encs">[gain]</span></p><p><span
