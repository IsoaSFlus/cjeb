						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>半</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>二分之一：～圆。～百（五十，多指岁数）。</li><li>不完全的：多～。～岛。～透明。～脱产。</li><li>在中间：～空。～路上。～夜。</li><li>喻很少：一星～点。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>半</strong> <span class="dicpy">bàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从八，从牛。<span class="yhcs">“八”</span>是分解的意思;牛大，易于分割，所以取<span class="yhcs">“牛”</span>会意。本义:一半，二分之一)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[half;semi-]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">半，物中分也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>有天下太半。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士卒食半。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·项籍传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先帝创业未半而中道崩殂。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">半匹红绡一丈绫。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身已半入，只露尻尾。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:太半(三分之二);半日;半工;半升</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 中 <span class="encs">[in the middle;halfway]</span>。如:半山腰;半大;半路;半道;半截腰</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 部分的，不完全的 <span class="encs">[partly;about half]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹抱琵琶半遮面。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:半自动;半老头;半工半读;半半拉拉;半死;半信半疑;一知半解</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 比喻极少的 <span class="encs">[very little]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而</span><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>曾无一言半辞送我。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:一星半点;半歇(一会儿);半星儿(半点儿);半丝麻线(些微私弊)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div
