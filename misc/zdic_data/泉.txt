						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>泉</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩㄢˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>从地下流出的水源：～源。～瀑。甘～。温～。喷～。黄～（旧时称人死后所在的地方。亦作&ldquo;九泉&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>古代钱币的名称。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> spring, fountain; wealth, money</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Quelle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> source</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">泉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>泉</strong> <span class="dicpy">qu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形。象水从山崖泉穴中流出的样子。本义:泉水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[spring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泉，人原也。象水流出成川形。字亦作洤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如彼泉流。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小旻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泉之竭矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;召旻》</span>。传:&ldquo;泉水从中以益者也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泉涓涓而始流。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酿泉为酒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁事记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泉香而酒洌。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泉而茗者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有泉可汲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《复庵记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:温泉;矿泉;清泉;泉根(泉源);泉脉(地下伏流的泉水);泉涌(泉川喷涌);泉鱼(渊鱼);泉绅(从高山上飞泻下来的泉水);泉韵(泉水声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 地下水 <span class="encs">[underground water]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若阙地及泉，隧而相见，其谁曰不然?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泉下，指人死后所在的地方 <span class="encs">[dwelling place of the dead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有志者入泉，无为者住世，岂佳事乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《书信》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:泉台之望(指死者的希望);泉厥(黄泉;地下);泉乡(泉下);泉扇(墓门。亦指阴间);泉里(黄泉之下)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 帛币。古钱币名 <span class="encs">[ancient coins]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">货泉径一寸，重五铢，右文曰货，左文曰泉，直一也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;外府》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:泉儿(古代钱币的通称);泉币(钱币，货币。又称泉货);泉金(金钱);泉布(帛币和布币);泉刀(泉币与刀币);泉布先生(钱币的谑称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 泉眼 <span class="encs">[hole of spring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熊咆龙吟殷岩泉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">水的源头。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选&middot;曹冏&middot;六代论》：「夫泉竭则流涸。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;欧阳修〈醉翁亭记〉：「峰回路转，有亭翼然，临于泉上者，醉翁亭也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">地下水。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;苏轼〈潮州韩文公庙碑〉：「譬如凿井得泉，而曰水专在是，岂理也哉？」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人死后所在的地方。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「黄泉」、「九泉」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选&middot;王粲&middot;七哀诗二首之一》：「悟彼下泉人，喟然伤心肝。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈夜遇微之于峡中〉诗：「往事渺茫都似梦，旧游流落半归泉。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代的钱币。钱的流行像泉水一样，故称为「泉」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「泉布」、「刀泉」、「货泉」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《管子&middot;轻重丁》：「凡称贷之家，出泉参千万，出粟参数千万锺。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如唐代有泉男生。</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6CC9.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕洤【唐韻】疾緣切【集韻】【韻會】從緣切【正韻】才緣切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音全。【說文】水原也。象水流成川形。【易&middot;蒙象】山下出泉。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋水】濫泉正出。正出，涌出也。沃泉縣出。縣出，下出也。氿泉穴出。穴出，仄出也。</p>
<p>又同出異歸曰肥泉。【詩&middot;衞風】我思肥泉，兹之永歎。</p>
<p>又醴泉。【禮&middot;禮運】天降膏露，地出醴泉。</p>
<p>又泉有光華曰榮泉。【前漢&middot;郊祀歌】食甘露，飮榮泉。</p>
<p>又瀑布曰立泉。【班固&middot;終南山賦】立泉落落。</p>
<p>又州名。【廣輿記】周時爲七閩地，隋曰溫陵，唐曰泉州。</p>
<p>又天泉，星名。【甘氏星經】天泉十星，在鼈東。一曰大海，主灌漑溝渠之事。</p>
<p>又龍泉，劒名。卽龍淵也。【杜甫詩】三尺獻龍泉。</p>
<p>又姓。南史有泉企。</p>
<p>又貨泉，卽錢也。【周禮&middot;天官】外府掌布之出入。【註】布，泉也。其藏曰泉，其行曰布，取名于水泉，其流行無不徧也。</p>
<p>又【地官&middot;泉府註】泉，或作錢。</p>
<p>又【集韻】疾眷切，全去聲。義同。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶才勻切，音秦。【李尤&middot;東觀銘】房闥內布，疏綺外陳。是謂東觀，書籍林泉。【集韻】或作灥。</p>
<p>又<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24F44.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤽄</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24FC1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤿁</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24181.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤆁</span>。</p>
