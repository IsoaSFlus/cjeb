                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">隽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>隽</strong></p>
<p>隽 <span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>，短尾鸟)，从弓。&ldquo;弓&rdquo;用来射鸟。本义:鸟肉肥美，味道好)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。也指美味 <span class="encs">[(bird) plump;fat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隽，肥肉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指肥美之肉，美味</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爱滑卷青绡，香袅冰丝细。山人隽味。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李彭老</span><span class="smcs">《摸鱼儿》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[言论、诗文]</span> 意味深长 <span class="encs">[meaningful;fine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:隽语(意味深远的语句);隽德(德行崇高深厚);隽壮(优美雄健);隽洁(优美简洁);隽蔚(意味深长)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古时以小鸟为射的，射中为隽 <span class="encs">[shooting at bird]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得隽为雄，唯能是与。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《观兵部马射赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 科举时代喻称考中 <span class="encs">[passing an examination]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">名高场屋已得隽，世有</span><span class="diczx2">龙门</span>今复登。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《送徐生之渑池》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>隽</strong></p>
<p>隽 <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;俊&rdquo;。优秀，才智出众 <span class="encs">[outstanding]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有三隽才。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十五年》</span></span></p>
