                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贺</strong></p><p>賀 <span class="dicpy">hè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 奉送礼物表示庆祝 <span class="encs">[send a present with congratulation]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贺，以礼相奉庆也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不然，则重币帛，以贺其福而吊其凶。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受天之祜，四方来贺。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·下武》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">叔向</span>贺之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是以贺。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何贺之有。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人皆贺之。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·人间训》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:贺敬(贺礼);贺吊往还(庆贺吊唁往来应酬);贺冬(庆祝冬至节);贺厦(庆祝大厦落成)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 嘉奖;犒劳 <span class="encs">[award;reward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今文余一人贺之。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·觐礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:贺彩(奖品);贺功(庆功);贺军(犒劳将士);贺酒(表示庆贺的酒宴)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 赞许;附和 <span class="encs">[praise]</span>。如:贺曰(赞许地说);齐贺(齐声附和);赞贺(赞许)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 增加，增益 <span class="encs">[increase;add]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贺之结于后。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:贺人(增加人员)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>贺</strong></p><p>賀 <span class="dicpy">hè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 锡的别名 <span class="encs">[tin]</span>。因古时以临贺(今广西贺县)出的锡最出名，因而称锡为贺</p><p><span class="diczx2">时珍</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“…方术家谓之贺，盖锡以 <span class="diczx2">临贺</span>出者为美也。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《本草纲目》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓。如:贺拔(复姓);贺若(复姓)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
