                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>瘦</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shòu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shòu.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄡˋ</span></p><ol><li>体内含脂肪少，肌肉不丰满，与“<span class="diczx3">胖</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">肥</span>”相对：～溜（“<span class="diczx3">溜</span>”读轻声）。～弱。～小。～削。～俏。～长（<span class="dicpy">cháng </span>）。～瘪。消～。清～。～骨嶙峋。</li><li>衣服鞋袜等窄小：这条裤子太～了。</li><li>细削，单薄：字小而～。</li><li>土地瘠薄：～瘠。</li><li>遒劲有骨力：书贵～硬。水清石～。</li></ol>

Variante von 痩  (Adj)​,schlank  (Adj)​,siehe <a href="./瘦">瘦</a>shou4</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> maigre,mince,étroit</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瘦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>瘦</strong> <span class="dicpy">shòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chuáng</span>)，叟声。从疒，表明与疾病有关。本义:肌肉不丰满)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[thin;skinny;slender]</span>。与<span class="yhcs">“胖”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“肥”</span>相对</p><p><span class="diczx1">瘦，臞也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毁瘠羸瘦。——<span class="smcs">《孝经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久则瘦臞腐败。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·廛人》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有书生避雨檐下，衣湿袖单，影乃益瘦。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:瘦怯(瘦弱;瘦削);瘦怯怯(瘦弱的样子);瘦亭亭(亦作<span class="yhcs">“瘦棱楼”</span>。十分瘦削的样子);瘦岩岩(纤细瘦削的样子);瘦恹恹(瘦厌厌。形容瘦弱消瘦);瘦伶伶(形容瘦的样子);瘦伶仃(十分瘦弱的样子);瘦客(消瘦衰弱之人);瘦悴(消瘦憔悴);瘦容(消瘦的容的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 细小;不茁壮 <span class="encs">[tight;small]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">落松满地金钗瘦，远树黏天菌子孤。——</span><span class="diczx2">杨万里</span><span class="smcs">《刘村渡》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
