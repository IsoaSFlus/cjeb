						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>窜</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">竄</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>乱跑，逃走（用于敌军、匪徒、野兽等）：～犯。～扰。～逃。～踞。流～。抱头鼠～。</li>
	<li>放逐：～逐。</li>
	<li>修改文字：～改。～定（删补改定）。点～（删减涂改）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> run away; revise, edit; expel</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> fliehen; davonlaufen; davonstürzen; abhauen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fuir,s'enfuir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">窜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>窜</strong></p>
<p>竄 <span class="dicpy">cu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从鼠，从穴。老鼠在洞穴里，表示&ldquo;隐匿&rdquo;、&ldquo;逃窜&rdquo;。简化为&ldquo;窜&rdquo;，形声，从穴，串声。本义:躲藏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hide]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:窜迹(遁迹;隐迹);窜身(藏身)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 奔逃 <span class="encs">[scuttle;scurry off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:逃窜(逃跑流窜);窜亡(逃亡;逃跑);窜走(逃跑;潜逃。奔走;乱跑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 放逐 <span class="encs">[exile;banish]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:窜投(放逐);窜死(贬逐而死);窜官(流放的官员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 删改 <span class="encs">[adulterate;distort]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:窜句(改易文字);窜削(犹删改);窜易(改动);窜附(改动和增益);窜益(改动和增益);窜寄(更改并混入)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 羼杂;混入 <span class="encs">[mix]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:窜言(参与议论);窜杂(搀杂;混杂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 骚扰 <span class="encs">[disturb]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:窜挠(犹&ldquo;骚扰&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 跳;往上冲 <span class="encs">[spring;jump]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:窜动(激烈地跳动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 怂恿 <span class="encs">[instigate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:窜掇(劝诱;怂恿);窜谋(暗中谋划)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">竄</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“窜”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/竄">竄</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下" target="_blank">午集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下&amp;bs=穴" target="_blank">穴</a>】
                              竄·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7AC4.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25A12.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥨒</span>【唐韻】七亂切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】取亂切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音爨。【說文】匿也，逃也。【周語】不窋自<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥨥</span>于戎翟之閒。【前漢&middot;蒯通傳】奉頭䑕竄。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】放也，誅也。【書&middot;舜典】竄三苗于三危。</p>
<p>又藏也，隱也。【賈誼&middot;弔屈原文】鸞鳳伏竄兮，鴟鴞翱翔。【晉語】可以竄惡。【註】隱也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】竄，微也。【疏】竄行之微也。【晉語】敏能竄謀。【註】微也。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;倉公傳】卽竄以藥，旋下病已。【註】竄，以藥薰之也。</p>
<p>又改易也。【韓愈詩】漬墨竄古史。</p>
<p>又【廣東新語】增城謂屋曰竄。</p>
<p>又【集韻】七丸切，爨平聲。入穴也。</p>
<p>又【韻會小補】誘人爲惡曰竄。俗曰攛掇。</p>
<p>又叶倉甸切，音茜。【蘇轍&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/215DD.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡗝</span>中詩】江流日益深，民語漸以變。遙想彼中人，狀類麖鹿竄。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>取外切，音襊。亦逃也。</p>
