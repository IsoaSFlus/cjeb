<p>● <strong>强</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄤˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>健壮，有力，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">弱</span>&rdquo;相对：～壮。～健。～人。～力。～大。～劲。刚～。富～。列～。～弩之末。年富力～。</li>
	<li>程度高：～手。～烈。～酸。能力～。</li>
	<li>胜过，优越，好：～干。争～好（<span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o </span>）胜。</li>
	<li>有余，略多于某数：～半（超过一半）。</li>
	<li>使用强力，硬性地：～占。～加。～攻。～悍。～横（<span class="dicpy">h&egrave;ng </span>）。～梁（强横霸道）。</li>
	<li>着重，增加分量：～化。增～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">强</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>强</strong></p>
<p>強、彊 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 倔强;固执 <span class="encs">[stubborn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">申生</span>甚好仁而彊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></p>
