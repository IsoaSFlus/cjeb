						<p>● <strong>森</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>树木众多，引申为众多、繁盛：～林。～立。～～。～然。～郁。</li>
	<li>幽深可怕的样子：阴～。～邃。</li>
	<li>严整的样子：～严。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">森</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>森</strong> <span class="dicpy">sēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从林从木。本义:树木丛生繁密)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tall and dense of trees;be full of trees]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">森，木多貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">林多木长貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">森槮柞朴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马融</span><span class="smcs">《长笛赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弹言鸟于森木。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《蜀都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:森标(树丛的顶端);森木(高耸繁茂的树木);森沉(林木繁茂幽深);森阴(林木幽深繁密);森萃(茂密);森朗(繁密而又清朗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 满;罗列 <span class="encs">[full]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其中戈矛森聚，京露其颖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:森布(繁密排列);森聚(聚集，密集)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 众多，众盛 <span class="encs">[multitudinous;in multitudes]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">森奉璋以阶列。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杨雄</span><span class="smcs">《甘泉赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百神森其备从兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;众貌。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">溪涧树林，森然布列。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:森列(形容数量众多，如森林一般排列);森罗(形容数量众多，如森林一般排列);森罗宝殿(迷信传说中阴间阎罗王处理公务的殿堂);森仗(众多的仪卫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 高耸。峙立 <span class="encs">[towering]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秭归</span>正面有一大片铁青色礁石，森然耸立江面。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《长江三峡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:森悚(因惊惧而耸立);森立(耸立;森严地排列);森森张(伸张耸竖);森动(耸动);森霄(直立于云端);森幻(高耸而奇幻);森拱(高大);森挺(高耸直立);森峭(高耸峭拔);森梢(高耸挺拔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 阴沉，幽暗 <span class="encs">[gloomy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">眇默诸境森。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">顾况</span><span class="smcs">《游子吟》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:森敞(幽暗而宽敞);森薄(阴凉逼人);森岑(形容幽暗阴冷);森森森然(昏眩);森邃(幽深);森寂(幽深岑寂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 森严，严峻 <span class="encs">[forbidding]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">快剑长戟森相向。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《李潮八分小篆歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:森逼(森严逼人);森卫(警卫森严);森肃(森严，严肃);森凛(森严可畏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 因惊愕而毛发耸起 <span class="encs">[be blood curdling]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:森竖(感觉恐怖而毛发直立);森耸(因恐怖而毛发耸立)</span></p>
