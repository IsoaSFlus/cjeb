                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">颠</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>颠</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">顛</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">diān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diān.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄧㄢˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>头顶：华～。</li>	<li>最高最初的部分：～末（本末）。山～。</li>	<li>倾倒，跌：～沛。～倒（<span class="dicpy">dǎo </span>）。～踬。～覆。～扑不破。</li>	<li>上下跳动：～簸。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/癫">癫</a>&rdquo;，精神错乱。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> top, peak, summit; upset</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ballotter,cahoter,sommet,cime</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">颠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>颠</strong></p>
<p>顛 <span class="dicpy">diān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>)，真声。本义:头顶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[crown of the head]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颠，顶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">班序颠毛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诲于华颠胡老。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;蔡邕传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:颠毛(头发);颠顶(头顶);颠童(形容老人头发稀少)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指物体的顶部 <span class="encs">[top]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:颠日(天日，天空的太阳);颠杪(顶端末梢);颠委(指水的上源和下游)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 马的额头 <span class="encs">[front of the horse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有车邻邻，有马白颠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;车邻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 本，始 <span class="encs">[origin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聊书其颠委以为之记。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王士祯</span><span class="smcs">《西城别墅记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古县名 <span class="encs">[Dian county]</span>。指滇池县，西汉置。治所在今云南省晋宁县东。两汉为益州郡治所</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>颠</strong></p>
<p>顛 <span class="dicpy">diān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &ldquo;颠&rdquo;假借为&ldquo;蹎&rdquo;。仆倒，倒下 <span class="encs">[fall forward]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人亦有言，颠沛之揭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;荡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颠越不恭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">造次必如是，颠沛必于是。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;里仁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">危而不持，颠而不扶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;季氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">杜回</span>踬而颠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十五年》</span></p>
