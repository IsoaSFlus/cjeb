<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>癞</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">癩</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄞˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>麻风病。</li>
	<li>癣疥等皮肤病：～子。～皮狗（喻不要脸的人）。</li>
	<li>表皮凸凹不平或有斑点的：～瓜（即&ldquo;苦瓜&rdquo;）。～蛤蟆。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> leprosy, scabies, mange; shoddy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Krätze  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> maladie de peau,lèpre,gale</p></div>

<p>◎ <strong>癞</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 癩 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>癞</strong></p>
<p>癩 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;ng</span>)，表示与疾病有关，赖声。本义:病名。即&ldquo;麻疯&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;疬&rdquo;。麻风病 <span class="encs">[leprosy]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:癞可(指宋僧祖可。因身披恶疾，故称);癞菌(麻风病的病原体)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 〈方〉∶恶疮;顽癣 <span class="encs">[favus]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:癞皮(身患顽癣，毛秃皮厚);癞肉顽皮(身患顽癣，皮肉丧失知觉);癞疮(恶疮;顽癣);癞头(长黄癣的头);癞癣(泛指黄癣、白癣及其他癣疮)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>癞</strong></p>
<p>癩 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 坏;低劣 <span class="encs">[bad]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:癞子(俗指无耻、唯利是图的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 脏 <span class="encs">[dirty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">但凡客商在路，早晚安歇，有两件事免不得，吃癞碗，睡死人床。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
