                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">翠</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>翠</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cuì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>绿色：～绿。苍～。～微（青绿的山色，亦泛指青山）。</li><li>〔～鸟〕属鸣禽类，形似杜鹃，嘴长，头部深橄榄色，有青绿色斑纹，背青绿色，腹赤褐色，尾短，捕食小鱼。</li><li>指“翡翠”（硬玉）：～玉。～镯。珠宝～钻。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> color green; kingfisher</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> bläulich grün</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bleu-vert,vert jade</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>翠</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从羽，卒声。本义:翠鸟)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 鸟名，翠鸟。又专指雌性的翠鸟 <span class="encs">[kingfisher]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:翠以羽自残(翡翠鸟因羽色之美而被残杀。比喻人因有才而遭嫉害);翠毛(翠鸟的羽毛);翠羽(翠鸟的羽毛。古代多用作饰物);翠禽(翠鸟)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 翠鸟的羽毛，可作装饰品 <span class="encs">[feathers of kingfishers]</span>。如:翠翘(古时女子首饰名。形似翠鸟尾上的长羽);翠华(指皇家仪仗中用翠鸟羽毛装饰而成的旗帜)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又指青、绿、碧色的玉石 <span class="encs">[green jade]</span>。如:翠纯珠围(绕以翡翠，围以珠玉);翠冠(翠玉所饰之冠);翠佩(翠玉的佩饰。亦借指美女)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>翠</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 青、绿、碧之类的颜色 <span class="encs">[bluish green]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两个黄鹂鸣翠柳，一行白鹭上青天。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《绝句(其三)》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青树翠蔓。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:翠樾埭(绿荫覆盖的堤坝);翠筠(绿竹);翠微(青葱的山色);翠屏(山名。亦指山色苍翠如屏);翠羽(青绿色的鸟羽)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 与美人有关的。一般为美丽的意思 <span class="encs">[beautiful]</span>。如:翠眉(用黛螺画的眉。比喻美人的眉毛);翠蛾(用黛点色的蛾属。指美人的眉毛。也指美人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 色调鲜明 <span class="encs">[bright]</span>。如:翠旃(色泽鲜明的曲柄旗);翠墨(色泽鲜明的墨;色泽鲜明的字迹);翠灿(鲜明的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
