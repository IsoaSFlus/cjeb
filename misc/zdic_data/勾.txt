						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>勾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄡˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>用笔画出符号，表示删除或截取：～销。～乙（在报刊书籍的某些词句两端画上像“<span class="diczx3">乙</span>”的记号，表示作为资料）。</li>    <li>画出形象的轮廓，描画：～画。～勒。</li>    <li>用灰、水泥等涂抹建筑物的缝（<span class="dicpy">fèng </span>）：～缝。</li>    <li>调和使黏，调味：～芡。</li>    <li>牵引，招引：～引。～通。</li>    <li>停留，逗留：～留。</li>    <li>摄取：～魂摄魄（形容使人心神摇荡，控制不住）。</li>    <li>中国古代数学著作中称不等腰直角三角形中较短的直角边：～股定理。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>勾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gòu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gòu.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄡˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>事情，多指坏事：～当。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">够</span>”，达到，探取。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">勾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>勾</strong> <span class="dicpy">gōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 本作<span class="yhcs">“句”</span>。弯曲 <span class="encs">[bend]</span>。如:勾垂(弯曲低垂);勾着背;勾曲</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 删除或截取 <span class="encs">[cancel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">谢安</span>已令吏部郎 <span class="diczx2">袁彦伯</span>撰策文，文成， <span class="diczx2">安</span>辄勾点，令更治改。——<span class="smcs">《魏书·司马叡传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">把报上的新词勾出来</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:勾除(勾销，除掉);勾抹(勾除;抹掉);勾却(勾销，取消)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 勾勒(用笔先勾一个轮廓) <span class="encs">[delineate]</span>。如:勾染(勾勒渲染);勾填(勾勒填描)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 勾结 <span class="encs">[collude with]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">都是这些和尚勾合光棍，在外诈作客商模样。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">艾衲居</span><span class="smcs">《豆棚闲话》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:勾哄(勾结哄骗);勾合(勾结;串通，结合)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 勾引，引诱 <span class="encs">[seduce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">他在</span><span class="diczx2">王婆</span>茶房里，和 <span class="diczx2">武大娘子</span>勾搭上了。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:勾将(引杀;纠集);勾惹(引逗，挑逗);勾挑(勾引挑逗);勾戏(勾引调戏);勾肆(勾拦瓦肆的简称。宋元时说书演戏的场所，后来也指妓院);勾拽(勾引)</p><p><span
