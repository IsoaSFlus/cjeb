                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">號</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>號</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>名稱：國～。年～。字～。</li>
	<li>舊指名和字以外的別號：如&ldquo;李白字太白，～青蓮居士&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>記號，標誌：信～。暗號。</li>
	<li>排定的次序或等級：編～。～碼。</li>
	<li>揚言，宣稱：～稱。項羽兵四十萬，～百萬。</li>
	<li>指某種人員：病～。傷～。</li>
	<li>標上記號：～房子。把這件東西～上。</li>
	<li>號令，命令：發號施～令。～召。</li>
	<li>軍隊或樂隊裏所用的西式喇叭：吹～。～兵。</li>
	<li>量詞，用於人數：昨天去了幾十～人。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>号</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>拖長聲音大聲呼叫：呼～。～叫。</li>
	<li>大聲哭：哀～。～喪。～啕大哭。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">號</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>號</strong></p>
<p>號 <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈動〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (會意。从虎，从號。&ldquo;號&rdquo;亦兼表讀音。本義:大聲喊叫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本義 <span class="encs">[howl;yell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">號，呼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《爾雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">誰之永號?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《詩&middot;魏風&middot;碩鼠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公號慶</span><span class="diczx2">鄭</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《國語&middot;晉語》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">號呼而轉徙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者說》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因大號，一墟皆驚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童區寄傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陰風怒號。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳陽樓記》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">號泣載道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:號噪(呼叫，喧嚷);號躑(號叫跳頓);號佛(高聲唸佛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 哭而有言 <span class="encs">[cry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">號，痛聲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《說文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以哭有言者爲號。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《顏氏家訓&middot;禮》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">老聃</span>死， <span class="diczx2">秦失</span>吊之，三號而出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《莊子&middot;養生主》</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集中" target="_blank">申集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集中&amp;bs=虍" target="_blank">虍</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/865F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】胡刀切【集韻】乎刀切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音豪。大呼也。【詩&middot;大雅】式號式呼。【小雅】載號載呶。【傳】號呶，號呼讙呶也。</p>
<p>又哭也。【易&middot;同人】先號咷而後笑。【周語】夫婦哀其夜號也。而取之以逃于褒。</p>
<p>又雞鳴也。【晉書&middot;律歷志】雞始三號。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】胡到切，音号。名號也。【公羊疏】春秋貴賤不嫌同號。【註】通同號稱也。【白虎通】春秋傳曰：王者受命于王，必擇天下之美號，以爲號也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大祝】掌辨六號。【註】號謂尊其名，更爲美稱。</p>
<p>又【夏官&middot;大司馬】家以號名。【註】鄕遂之屬謂之名，家之屬謂之號。</p>
<p>又【冢人】詔其號。【註】謂諡號。</p>
<p>又號令也。【易&middot;渙卦】渙汗其大號。</p>
<p>又號召也。【齊語】使周游四方，以號召天下之賢土。</p>
<p>又叶胡溝切。【皮日休&middot;悼賈文】臨汨羅之漾漾兮，想懷沙之幽憂。森樛羅以蓊鬰兮，時逬狖以相號。　【集韻】本作号，又作唬。　毛氏曰：从口从丂，丂音考，俗从号，非。</p>
<p>又作號，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>俗字。號字从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2719E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧆞</span>作。</p>
