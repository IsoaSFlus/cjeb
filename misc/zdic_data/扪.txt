                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>扪</strong></p><p>捫 <span class="dicpy">mén</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mén.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，门声。本义:执持)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扪，抚持也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫扪朕舌。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“扪，持也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:扪舌(握住舌头，使不能说话);扪虱(捉住虱子);扪月(捉月)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抚摸 <span class="encs">[stroke;touch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃扪足曰:<span class="yhcs">“虏中吾指。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《史记·高祖本纪》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“扪，摸也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">必先扪而循之。——<span class="smcs">《素问·离合真邪论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“扪，谓手摸。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以手扪之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:扪心(抚摸胸口);;扪心自揣(按着胸口自我估量);扪循(按摩);扪摸(触摸;摸索);扪腹(抚摸腹部);扪索(摸索，寻求);扪足(以手摸足);扪参(抚摸参宿);扪天(摸天。极言其高);扪膝(摸膝)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 攀;挽 <span class="encs">[climb]</span>。如:扪涉(谓攀山涉水);扪萝(攀援葛藤)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/扪心自问">扪心自问</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/扪诊">扪诊</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/636B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【正韻】謨奔切【韻會】謨昆切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音門。【說文】撫持也。【詩·大雅】莫捫朕舌。</p><p>又摸也。【史記·高祖紀】漢王傷胷，乃捫足。【楚辭·九章】遂儵忽而捫天。</p><p>又【韻補】叶眉貧切，音珉。【歐陽修·菱溪石詩】嗟予有口莫能辨，歎息但以兩手捫。皆云女媧初鍛鍊，融結一氣凝精純。　俗作䦐，非。</p>

                          <span class="jieshi">撫持也。</span>撫、安也。一曰揗也。謂安揗而持之也。大雅。莫捫朕舌。傳曰。捫、持也。渾言不分析也。若王猛捫蝨之類。又專謂摩挲。<span class="jieshi">从手。門聲。</span>莫奔切。十三部。<span class="jieshi">詩曰。莫捫朕舌。</span>大雅抑文。                          </div>
