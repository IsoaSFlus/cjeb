						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>访</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">訪</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>向人询问，调查：～求（探访寻求）。～员（记者）。搜奇～古。</li><li>探问，看望：～视。～问。～晤（探访会晤）。探亲～友。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> visit; ask, inquire</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> visiter,rendre visite,enquêter</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>访</strong></p><p>訪 <span class="dicpy">fǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，方声。本义:广泛地征求意见)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[seek advice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">訪，泛谋也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">訪，谋也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王访于</span><span class="diczx2">箕子</span>。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">访予落止。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·访落》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受纳访——<span class="smcs">《周礼·内史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使访物官。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">穆</span>公访诸 <span class="diczx2">蹇叔</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十二年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:访断(咨询决疑);访覃(问及);访讯(讯问;打听)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为访问、拜访(敬词) <span class="encs">[visit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此行殊访戴，自可缓归桡。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《陪从祖济南太守泛鹊山湖》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时余方访君寓。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">径造庐访</span><span class="diczx2">成</span>。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:访戴(访友的代称);出访(到外国访问);回访(在对方来拜访后去拜访对方);过访(访问);走访(访问;拜访);造访(上门访问)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 寻求 <span class="encs">[seek by inquiry;try to get;search]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">访风景于崇阿。——</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span><span class="diczx2">李渤</span>始访其遗踪。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使人访其夫妇，载以来。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:寻访(寻求查访);访觅(访寻;寻找);访论稽古(探求讨论古事)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“方”</span>。初始 <span class="encs">[start]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">访以</span><span class="diczx2">吕氏</span>故，几乱天下。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 谋议 <span class="encs">[consult]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">教之令，使访物官。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">韦昭</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“访，议也。物，事也。使议知百官之事业。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 调查;查处 <span class="encs">[investigate;look into]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又九门提督所访缉纠诘，皆归刑部。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:访缉(访查缉捕);访办(调查处理);访俗(察访民俗)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/访查">访查</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
