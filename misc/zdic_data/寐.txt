                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>寐</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mèi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mèi.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄟˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 睡，睡着（<span class="dicpy">zháo</span>）：～语。假（<span class="dicpy">jiǎ</span>）～。梦～以求。夙兴（<span class="dicpy">xīng</span>）夜～（早起晚睡）。夜不能～。</p></ol>
