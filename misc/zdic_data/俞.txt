                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>俞</strong> <span class="dicpy">yú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>(会意。从亼( <span class="dicpy">jí</span>)，从舟，从刂，刂水也。”本义与造船有关，不详)古代挖空树木做船 <span class="encs">[make a boat by hollowing the log]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俞，空中木为舟也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:空中木者，舟之始</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>俞</strong> <span class="dicpy">yú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">叹</span></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 文言叹词。犹言<span class="yhcs">“然”</span>。表示应答或肯首。是;对 <span class="encs">[assent;consent;permit]</span>。如:俞允(允诺。多用于君主);俞音(帝王表示允可的诏令)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 答应;允许 <span class="encs">[consent;promise]</span>。如:俞纳(应允;接受);俞旨(表示同意的圣旨);俞咈(赞成和反对)</p><p>◎ <strong>俞</strong> <span class="dicpy">yú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 安定 <span class="encs">[stable]</span>。如:俞然(安然，安定的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 愉快 <span class="encs">[pleasant]</span>。如:俞俞(和乐愉快的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>俞</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄩˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yú <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/6450.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">挖空树木以作舟。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·舟部》：「<b>俞</b>，空中木为舟也。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示答应、允诺的意思。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷五七·司马相如传下》：「于是天子沛然改容，曰：『<b>俞</b>乎，朕其试哉！』」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如汉代有<b><b>俞</b></b>连。</span></p></li></ol></div></div><div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>俞</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄕㄨ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>shū</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">国名。为汉代封与栾布的侯国，其治所约在今大陆地区山东省平原县西南。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">参见「<a href='/hans/俞儿' target=_blank>俞儿</a> 」条。</span></p></li></ol></div></div><div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>俞</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄩˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yù</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">病好了。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子·解蔽》：「故伤于湿而击鼓，鼓痺，则必有敝鼓丧豚之费矣，而未有<b>俞</b>疾之福也。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「愈」。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">更加。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷二四上·食货志上》：「德泽加于万民，民<b>俞</b>勤农。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「愈」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4FDE.svg">                          <p>空中木爲舟也。从亼从舟从巜。巜，水也。羊朱切</p>                          </div>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4FDE_223.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(俞)</span><span class="ywyy">空中木爲舟也。</span><span class="swdz">淮南氾論訓。古者爲窬木方版以爲舟航。高曰。窬、空也。方、並也。舟相連爲航也。按窬同兪。空中木者、舟之始。並板者、航之始。如椎輪爲大路之始。其始見本空之木用爲舟。其後因刳木以爲舟。凡穿窬、廁牏皆取義於兪。中孚傳曰。利涉大川。乘木舟虛也。</span><span class="ywyy">从亼。从舟。从巜。</span><span class="swdz">合三字會意。羊朱切。古音在四部。</span><span class="ywyy">巜、水也。</span><span class="swdz">巜下曰。水流澮澮也。說从巜之意。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
