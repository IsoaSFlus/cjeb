data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，者声。本义:炎热) 同本义 <span class="encs">[heat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暑，热也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。暑近湿如蒸，热近燥如烘，</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一寒一暑。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六月徂暑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·四月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">土润溽暑。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寒暑燥湿风火。——<span class="smcs">《《素问·天玄纪大论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冬日则寒冻，夏日则暑伤。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·人间训》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乌兽稀毛，其性能暑。——</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《言守边备塞疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">触风雨，犯寒暑，呼嘘毒疠，往往而死者相藉也。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻霜露而狎风雨，是故寒暑不能为之毒。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:暑溽(夏天闷热的气候);暑岁(炎热干旱的年岁);暑夏(炎热的夏季);暑寒(天气炎热与寒冷);暑湿(炎热潮湿)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>暑</strong> <span class="dicpy">shǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a weather]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寒往则暑来，暑往则寒来。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寒暑不兼时而至。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·显学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 夏季 <span class="encs">[summer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寒暑易节，始一返焉。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">杭</span>有卖果者，善藏柑，涉寒暑不溃。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:暑月(夏月);暑雨(夏季的雨);暑伏(夏季中的伏时。是一年之中最热的时期);暑雨祁寒(夏大雨，冬大寒);暑夜(炎夏之夜)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/暑假">暑假</a></span><span
