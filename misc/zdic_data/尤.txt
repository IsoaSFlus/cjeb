                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>尤</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>特异的，突出的：～为（<span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i </span>）。～异。无耻之～。</li>
	<li>更加，格外：～其。</li>
	<li>过失：效～（学着别人做坏事）。</li>
	<li>怨恨，归咎：怨天～人。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> especially, particularly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> besonders, insbesondere ,You  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> surtout,en particulier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尤</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声，小篆字形，从乙，又声。乙象植物屈曲生长的样子，受到阻碍，则显示出它的优异。本义:最优异)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[outstanding]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尤，异也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:此字当之猶之古文，犬子也。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尤，怪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尤，异也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫有尤物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公八年》</span>。注:&ldquo;异也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而视之尤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十六年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;甚也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然有知强弱之所尤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;侈靡》</span>。注:&ldquo;殊绝也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫子，物之尤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尤功(功绩最卓著。亦指功绩最卓著的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 惊异 <span class="encs">[staring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公饮之酒，厚酬之，赐及从者。司马亦如之。张丐尤之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尤</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 责备;怪罪 <span class="encs">[blame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尤其室。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十五年》</span>。注:&ldquo;责过也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畜君何尤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:&ldquo;何尤，无过也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不怨天，不尤人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;宪问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尤苦(苛责;污蔑);尤恶(谴责和憎恶);尤怨(埋怨;怨恨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 缠绵;爱昵 <span class="encs">[linger;show tender affection for]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尤殢(尤云殢雨;也指放娇撒赖，纠缠不清);尤红殢翠(比喻男女间的缠绵亲昵);尤云殢雨(喻缠绵于男女欢爱)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>尤</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 过失，罪过 <span class="encs">[mistake]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5C24.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕怣【唐韻】【集韻】羽求切【韻會】疑求切【正韻】于求切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音郵。【說文】尤，異也。从乙又聲。【徐曰】乙欲出而見閡，則顯其尤異者也。【司馬相如&middot;封禪文】未有殊尤絕迹可考於今者。【註】尤，異也。一曰甚也，過也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】怨也。【詩&middot;鄘風】許人尤之，衆稚且狂。【左傳&middot;僖二十四年】尤而效之，罪又甚焉。</p>
<p>又最也。【莊子&middot;徐無鬼】夫子物之尤也。【註】言於人物之中稱之爲最。</p>
<p>又蚩尤，黃帝臣。帝與戰於涿鹿，殺之。今畫其形於旗上，名蚩尤旗。</p>
<p>又彗星亦名蚩尤旗。</p>
<p>又姑尤，齊東界二水名。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】聊攝以東，姑尤以西。</p>
<p>又姓。見【姓苑】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】通作郵。【詩&middot;小雅】不知其郵。【前漢&middot;成帝詔】以顯其郵。</p>
<p>又叶戸恢切，音回。【詩&middot;小雅】廢爲殘賊，莫知其尤。叶上梅。</p>
<p>又叶與之切，音怡。【詩&middot;鄘風】大夫君子，無我有尤。叶下之。</p>
<p>又叶以諸切，音余。【道藏歌】日月粲華暉，如光照眞符。億兆不同劫，千載莫之尤。</p>
