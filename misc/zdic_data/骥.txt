                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>骥</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">驥</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 好马，喻贤能：～足（ａ．喻杰出的才华；ｂ．喻才华出众的人）。～尾（喻依附他人而成名）。～途（喻锦绣前途）。～服盐车（喻埋没人才）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> thoroughbred horse; refined and</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Vollblutpferd  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bon cheval,pur-sang,excellent coursier,homme distingué,talent</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>骥</strong></p><p>驥 <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从马，冀声。按左传，冀之北上，马之所生，是冀亦兼会意。本义:好马;良马)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a thoroughbred horse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骥，千里马也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骥不称其力，称其德也。——<span class="smcs">《论语·宪问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:骥子(良马;比喻才能特出的人);骥尾(千里马的尾巴。比喻追随在德行好、地位高或能力强的人的后面);骥足(千里马的脚。比喻高才)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻杰出的人才 <span class="encs">[a cultured，well-bred person]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">世不乏骥，求则可致。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·虞预传》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/骥骜">骥骜</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9A65.svg">                          <p>千里馬也，孫陽所相者。从馬冀聲。天水有驥縣。几利切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">千里馬也。孫陽所相者。</span>孫陽字伯樂。秦穆公時人。其所與有九方臯。卽九方歅。戰國䇿汗明說春申君、是伯樂相驥事。按小徐說伯樂卽王良、卽郵無恤。大繆。<span class="jieshi">从馬。冀聲。</span>几利切。古音在一部。<span class="jieshi">天水有驥縣。</span>地理志天水郡冀縣。郡國志漢陽郡冀縣。漢陽卽天水也。故城在今陝西鞏昌府伏羌縣東。史皆作冀、不作驥。左傳。冀之北土。馬之所生。許葢援此說字形。从冀馬會意。許本作冀縣。謂此卽左傳生馬之地。淺人改之。                          </div>
