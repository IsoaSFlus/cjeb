                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>姣</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，交声。本义:修长美丽)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[graceful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">姣，好也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“姣谓容体壮大之好也。”</span> <span class="diczx2">徐灏</span>笺:<span class="yhcs">“凡从交声之字其义多为长…壮佼亦谓其壮而高长，非谓大也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弃位而姣，不可为贞。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">灵偃蹇兮姣服。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·东方太一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">古者桀纣长巨姣美，天下之杰也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·非相》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前有楼阙轩辕，后有长姣美人。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 容貌美好的 <span class="encs">[beautiful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">娇丽施只。——<span class="smcs">《大招》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公姣且丽。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·达郁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左右言其姣好。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·东方朔传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“美丽也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:姣艳(美好艳丽);姣娥(美貌的女子);姣姬(美女);姣人(美人);姣色(美色);姣冶(美丽);姣妙(美妙);姣服(谓服饰华美);姣怯怯(娇弱);姣羞(妩媚害羞);姣弱(娇嫩细弱);姣滴滴(娇媚);姣贵(娇贵)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 对儿女的爱称 <span class="encs">[my dear]</span>。如:姣姣(乖乖。对小孩的爱称);姣娃(犹娇女)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/姣好">姣好</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/姣姣者">姣姣者</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/姣丽">姣丽</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/姣美">姣美</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
