                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>仪</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">儀</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>人的外表或举动：～态。～表。威～（使人敬畏的严肃容貌和举止）。</li><li>按程序进行的礼节：～式。～仗。司～。</li><li>礼物：贺～。谢～。</li><li>供测量、绘图、实验用的器具：～器。</li><li>法制，准则：～刑。“设～立度，可以为准则”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">仪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>仪</strong></p><p>儀 <span class="dicpy">yí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，义声。本义:容止仪表)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[appearance;bearing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令仪令色，小心翼翼。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>笺:<span class="yhcs">“善威仪，善颜色。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心质平理，其仪安闲。——<span class="smcs">《人物志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:仪状(容貌形状);仪观(容貌威仪);仪宇(仪容;器量);仪缀(威仪，外观);仪止(仪表举止);仪采(仪表风采);仪相(仪表容貌);仪形(仪容;形体);仪状(仪容形状);仪望(仪容;外表);仪干(仪容体格);仪操(仪容节操);仪举(仪容举止)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 法度;礼法 <span class="encs">[law;moral standard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仪，度也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>系传:<span class="yhcs">“度，法度也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">置此以为法，立此以为仪，将以量度天下之王公大人、卿大夫之仁与不仁，譬之犹分黑白也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所以宣布哲人之令德，示民轨仪也。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:仪刑(法式，作为模范);仪令(法令);仪准(法度标准);仪则(法则);仪司(泛称地方司法机构)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 典范;表率 <span class="encs">[model]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">望三五以为像兮，指</span><span class="diczx2">彭咸</span>以为仪。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上者，下之仪也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:仪范(作为典范);仪象(以为模式);仪形(典范;楷模);仪型(楷模，典范)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 礼仪。仪式;礼节 <span class="encs">[ceremony;rite;etiquette]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">礼仪卒度。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·楚茨》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肄仪为位。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官·小宗伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十六知礼仪。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:礼仪(礼节和仪式);仪部(礼部);仪文(礼仪的形式);仪位(礼仪、爵位);仪检(礼仪法式);仪台(行礼仪的高台);仪序(礼仪的秩序);仪制(礼仪制度及其具体规定)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 仪仗 <span class="encs">[guard of honor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仪马之设，自</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>始也。——<span class="smcs">《事物纪原》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span>
