                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>敝</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[worn-out;old and shabby]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敝，一曰败衣。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苟有衣必见其敝。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·缁衣》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>训败衣。</p><p><span class="diczx1">邻有敝舆而欲窃之。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·谋攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>摄敝衣冠。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余则缊袍敝衣处其间。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:敝鼓丧豚(击破鼓，宰杀猪以求神治病。指徒费而无益);敝帷不弃(不轻易丢弃破旧的帷幕);敝衣(破旧衣服)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 疲惫，困乏，衰败 <span class="encs">[tired]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">还师以敝</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公九年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“疲也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">土敝则草木不长。——<span class="smcs">《史记·乐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">曹操</span>之众远来疲敝。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吏民凋敝。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·张敞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:敝卒(疲惫的士卒);敝敝(疲困的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 败坏;衰败 <span class="encs">[decayed;corrupt]</span>。如:经久不敝;敝肠(坏心肠，恶性情);敝人(德行浅薄的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 对自己或自己一方的谦称 <span class="encs">[my]</span>。如:敝处(本处。敝，破旧;谦称);敝房(谦词。敝室。对人说自己妻子);敝国(自己的国家)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>敝</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 失败 <span class="encs">[defeat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敝于</span><span class="diczx2">韩</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“败也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 弃 <span class="encs">[cast away]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冠而敝之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·郊特牲礼》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“弃也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 损害<span class="encs">[harm;hurt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因人之力而敝之，不仁。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盖供过求，时价劣经价，则供者必受敝，受敝则迁。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《生计学学说沿革小史》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
