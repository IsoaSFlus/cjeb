                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>沛</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄟˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>水势湍急，行动迅疾的样子：～然。～～。</li>
	<li>充盛的样子：充～。丰～。</li>
	<li>有水有草的地方。</li>
	<li>跌倒，倾仆：颠～（挫折困顿）。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">旆</span>&rdquo;，旌旗。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> abundant, full, copious; sudden</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> reichlich</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> abondant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">沛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>沛</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，巿声。本义:沛水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Pei River]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沛，</span><span class="diczx2">沛水</span>。出 <span class="diczx2">辽东</span>番汗塞外，西南入海。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泽名。沛泽 <span class="encs">[Pei lake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐侯</span>田于 <span class="diczx2">沛</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭二十年》</span>。注:&ldquo;沛，泽名。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 多水草的沼泽地 <span class="encs">[marsh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大陷于沛泽之中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;僖公四年》</span>。注:&ldquo;草棘曰沛。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹逸禽之赴深林，蝱虫内之趣大沛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;崔骃传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 蓄积用来灌田的水 <span class="encs">[water for irrigation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">浙中</span>&hellip;少水，人家多于山上置闸蓄水，遇旱岁开以灌田，名之曰沛，取沛然之义也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三余赘笔》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;旆&rdquo;。旗，幡 <span class="encs">[flag;banner]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丰其沛，日中见沫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;丰》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>沛</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 水势湍急的样子，泛指盛，大 <span class="encs">[copious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沛，水波流也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民归之，由水之就下，沛然谁能御之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚沛泽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;揆度》</span>。注:&ldquo;大泽也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沛焉竞溢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《洞箫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">河水沛沛，比夫众川，孰者为大?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;自纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:充沛;沛沛(水盛大的样子);沛若(盛大的样子);沛然(充盛的样子;盛大的样子);沛发(大量涌出);沛泽(盛大的恩泽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;霈&rdquo;。雨盛大的样子 <span class="encs">[coprous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天油然作云，沛然下雨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冻雨沛其洒涂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">沛</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上" target="_blank">巳集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上&amp;bs=水" target="_blank">水</a>】
                              沛·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6C9B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>普蓋切，音霈。水名。【說文】沛水出遼東番汗塞外，西南入海。</p>
<p>又澤名。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】齊侯田于沛。</p>
<p>又草生水曰沛。【公羊傳註】草棘曰沛。【風俗通&middot;山澤篇】沛者，草木之蔽茂，禽獸之所匿也。</p>
<p>又縣名，屬沛郡。【漢書註】秦泗水郡之屬縣。</p>
<p>又行貌。【楚辭&middot;九歌】沛吾乗兮桂舟。【左思&middot;吳都賦】直衝濤而上瀨，常沛沛而悠悠。</p>
<p>又有餘貌。【公羊傳&middot;文十四年】沛若有餘。</p>
<p>又大貌。【前漢&middot;五行志】沛然自大。</p>
<p>又雨貌。【張衡&middot;思<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>賦】凍雨沛其灑塗。</p>
<p>又滂沛，雨盛貌。別作霈。</p>
<p>又旛幔也。【易&middot;豐卦】豐其沛。通作旆。</p>
<p>又蓄水灌田之名。【三餘贅筆】浙中少水人家，多于山上置閘蓄水，遇旱歲開以灌田，名之曰沛。</p>
<p>又竹名。【神異經】南方荒中有沛竹，可以爲大船。【僧贊寧&middot;筍譜】又沛竹筍。</p>
<p>又沛艾，姿容俊偉貌。【司馬相如&middot;大人賦】沛艾赳螑。</p>
<p>又滯沛，奔揚貌。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】奔揚滯沛。</p>
<p>又姓。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>博蓋切，音貝。義同。</p>
<p>又疾也。【前漢&middot;郊祀歌】靈之來，神哉沛。【註】沛，補蓋反。</p>
<p>又偃仆也。【詩&middot;大雅】顚沛之揭。【傳】沛，拔也。【正義】遽離根本之言。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶方吠切，音避。【揚雄&middot;甘泉賦】雲飛揚兮雨滂沛，于胥德兮麗萬世。</p>
<p>又叶蒲迫切，音白。【左思&middot;魏都賦】揆旣往之前跡，叩將來之後轍。成都迄以傾覆，建業則亦顚沛。</p>
