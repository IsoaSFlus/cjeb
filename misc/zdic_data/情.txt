                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>情</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qíng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qíng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>外界事物所引起的喜、怒、爱、憎、哀、惧等心理状态：感～。～绪。～怀。～操。～谊。～义。～致。～趣。～韵。性～。～愫（真情实意）。～投意合。～景交融。</li><li>专指男女相爱的心理状态及有关的事物：爱～。～人。～书。～侣。～诗。殉～。～窦初开（形容少女初懂爱情）。</li><li>对异性的欲望，性欲：～欲。发～期。</li><li>私意：～面。说～。</li><li>状况：实～。事～。国～。～形。～势。～节。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">情</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>情</strong> <span class="dicpy">qíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，青声。本义:感情)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[feelings;affection;sentiment]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">情，人之阴气有欲者也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何谓人情?喜怒哀惧爱恶欲七者，弗学击能。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span>又</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人情者，圣王之田也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">情伪相感。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“情阳也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">情者，阴之化也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通·情形》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">情者，性之质也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天若有情天亦老。——</span><span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《金铜仙人辞汉歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">览物之情——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">情所欲居。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂利之情。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:情熟(亲密);情款(情意诚挚融洽);情悃(情意;感情真挚，诚心诚意);情热(感情深厚);情肠(感情;情意);情悰(情愫;感情，本心;真情实意);无情(没有感情;不留情);友情(朋友的感情;友谊);情交(情感相通);情好(情谊，交情);情志(情感志趣);情思(思念之情);情切(感情真切)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 本性 <span class="encs">[a person's character;innate quality]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">情，性也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·上德》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫物之不齐，物之情也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:情性(天赋的本性);情心(本性;性情);情尚(性情与爱好);情品(性格);情素(真情;本心);情行(犹品行)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 情欲，性欲 <span class="encs">[lust sexual passion]</span>。如:情天欲海(情大如天，欲深如海);色情(性欲方面表现出来的情绪);发情期;情尘(指情爱，情欲)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 爱情 <span class="encs">[love]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">唯将旧物表深情。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《长恨歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">落日故人情。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《送友人》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愿天下有情人皆成眷属。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巾短情长。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:情窦(爱情的萌芽);情谈款叙(慢慢地谈情说爱);情天(爱情的境界);情田(播种爱情之田)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 实情，情况 <span class="encs">[the state of
