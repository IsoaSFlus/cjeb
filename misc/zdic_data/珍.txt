                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">珍</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>珍</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>珠玉等宝物：～宝。～珠。奇～异宝。席～待聘（“席珍”，坐席上的宝石，喻怀才待用）。</li><li>宝贵的，贵重的：～贵。～奇。～稀。～闻。～玩（贵重的供赏玩的东西）。</li><li>重视，爱惜：～视。～爱。～重（<span class="dicpy">zhòng</span>）。～存。～藏（<span class="dicpy">cáng</span>）。</li><li>精美的食物：～羞（亦作“珍馐”）。八～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> precious, valuable, rare</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> wertvoll, selten  (Adj)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">珍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>珍</strong></p><p>珎 <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本义:珠玉等宝物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[treasure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">珍，宝也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">珍圭。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·典瑞》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“王使之瑞节。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">多珍怪些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“金玉为珍。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">珍怪奇物。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·主术》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远方莫致其珍。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·解蔽》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">希世之珍。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子·千里马篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">堂有炼珍。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:珍积(积蓄的财宝);珍翰(墨宝);珍币(珍宝财物);珍赂(珍赀。珍宝财物);珍瑰(珍玮。珍宝)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻难得的人才 <span class="encs">[talented person]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此固国家之珍，而社稷之佐也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 精美的食品 <span class="encs">[delicacy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">交广市鲑珍。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">食必珍美。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:珍杂(珍错，山珍海味);珍鲑(山珍海味);珍滋(美味);珍异(珍贵奇特的食物);山珍海味</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>珍</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 珍贵的，宝贵的 <span class="encs">[valuble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">珍器重宝。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奇草珍木。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/73CD.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕錱【唐韻】陟鄰切【集韻】知鄰切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音眞。【說文】寶也。【玉篇】貴也，美也，重也。【禮·儒行】儒有席上之珍以待聘。【盧諶詩】不待卞和顯，自爲命世珍。</p><p>又瑞也。【詩·周頌·將受厥明傳】我周家大受其光明，謂爲珍瑞，天下所休慶也。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋詁】珍，獻也。</p><p>又奇也。【書·旅獒】珍禽奇獸，不育于國。【公羊傳·昭三十一年】食必坐二子於其側而食之，有珍怪之食。【註】珍怪，猶奇異也。</p><p>又【正字通】食之美者亦曰珍。【禮·王制】八十常珍。【註】常食皆珍味也。【又】九十者天子欲有問焉，則就其室以珍從。【註】九十者，專指有爵者，言天子就而問，珍味從之以往，致尊養之義也。</p><p>又【周禮·天官·膳夫】珍用八物。【註】謂淳熬淳母炮豚炮牂擣珍漬熬肝膋也。【陸佃云】珍用八物，牛羊麋鹿麕豕狗狼。</p><p>又坤珍，洛書也。【後漢·班固傳】聖皇乃握乾符，闡坤珍。</p><p>又【正字通】州名。漢牂牁郡，唐置珍州。</p><p>又【字彙補】讀作鎭。【周禮·春官·典瑞】珍圭以徵守，以恤凶荒。【註】杜子春云：珍，當作鎭。</p><p>又【韻補】叶張連切，音邅。【<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>琳馬瑙勒賦】遭時顯價冠世珍兮，君子窮達亦時然兮。　【玉篇】俗作珎。【五音集韻】俗作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2493D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤤽</span>。</p><p>
