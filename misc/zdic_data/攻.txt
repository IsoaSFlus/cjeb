                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>攻</strong> <span class="dicpy">gōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，工声。本义:进攻，攻打)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(与<span class="yhcs">“守”</span>相对) <span class="encs">[assail;attack;assault]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">攻，击也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">攻</span><span class="diczx2">灵公</span>于 <span class="diczx2">桃园</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宽则两军相攻。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以此攻城，何城不克?——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">公输盘</span>为 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>造云梯之械，成，将以攻 <span class="diczx2">宋</span>。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其次伐兵，下政攻城，攻城之法为不得已。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·谋攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仁义不施而攻守之势异也。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子曰:<span class="yhcs">“非吾徒也，小子鸣鼓而攻之可也。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《论语·先进》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:攻车(古代的一种兵车。用于进攻);攻钞(攻打劫掠);攻围(攻敌时切断敌人要塞与外界的交通，予以四面包围);攻无不取(只要进攻，必能夺取)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指责 <span class="encs">[censure;accuse;blame;bring charge against;rail at]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五曰攻，六曰说。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太祝》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“以辞责之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">攻者责也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·顺鼓》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:攻瑕指失(批评缺点，指出失误);攻瑕索垢(批评不足，寻找缺点);攻摘(批评指摘)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 引申为以药物治疗疾病 <span class="encs">[cure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡疗疡，以五毒攻之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官·疡医》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:攻疗(治疗);攻治(医治);攻熨(原指以药物炒热敷患处，散寒止痛。引申为改正);攻泻(饮服烈性药物使之宣泻)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 致力研究，专心从事 <span class="encs">[assiduously study;specialize in]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闻道有先后，术业有专攻，如是而已。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:攻苦食淡(致力于勤苦之事，食用无味之物。引申为艰苦自励，一心求学);攻苦(从事于劳苦之事。多指苦心求学);攻习(攻读;奋力学习)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 治理，加工 <span class="encs">[administer;process;work]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">攻，治也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庶民攻之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">农攻粟，工攻器，贾攻货。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·上农》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">贤</span>第新成，攻坚。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·董贤传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span>
