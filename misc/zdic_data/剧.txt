                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">剧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>剧</strong></p><p>劇 <span class="dicpy">jù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:甚;厉害，严重)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[acute;severe;intense]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剧，甚也。——<span class="smcs">《文选》</span>注引<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“即勮字之误文。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何其剧欤。——<span class="smcs">《剧秦美新》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">口吃不能剧谈。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">即疾剧，留屯毋行。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·赵充国传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不以梦剧乱知谓之静。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·解蔽》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:剧论(激烈的论辩);剧难(激切地质问论难);剧疵(严重的过失);剧暑(酷暑);剧寒(严寒);剧艰(激烈诘难);剧药(毒性轻于毒药，但作用剧烈，容易产生中毒、成瘾或如腐蚀、起火爆炸等其他事故的一类药品。如:山道年，可待因，乙醚，火棉，盐酸等)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 繁多;繁忙 <span class="encs">[numerous and diverse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹然而材剧志大。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·非十二子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“繁多也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事剧而功寡。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·算地》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:剧月(农忙之月);剧繁(繁忙的事物);剧烦(繁剧。亦指繁杂的事物);剧事(艰巨，繁杂的事务);剧地(繁杂难治之地)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 艰难 <span class="encs">[hard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执务私事，不辞剧易。——</span><span class="diczx2">范晔</span><span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:剧地(形势险要之地);剧口(讲述烦难);剧易(艰难);剧韵(险韵)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 巨，大 <span class="encs">[powerful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">材剧志大，闻见杂博。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·十二子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:剧贼(大盗;强贼);剧寇(剧贼);剧恶(大恶);剧虏(势力强大的敌寇)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 急促;疾速 <span class="encs">[fast]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二世而亡，何其剧与?——</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《剧秦美新》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:剧气(气喘，呼吸急促);剧然(突然，猛然);剧读(疾读，流畅的朗读)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>剧</strong></p><p>劇 <span class="dicpy">jù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>戏耍 <span class="encs">[play and joke]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妾发初覆额，折花门前剧。——李白<span
