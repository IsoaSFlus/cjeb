                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">薰</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>薰</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xūn.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>同“<span class="diczx3">熏</span>”。</li><li>古书上说的一种香草，又泛指花草的香气：～莸不同器（香草和臭草不能放在一个器物里，喻好和坏不能共存。亦称“薰莸异器”）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a medicinal herb; to cauterize</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> duften, riechen [ auch <a href="./熏">熏</a>, <a href="./燻">燻</a> ]  (V)​,etw. räuchern [ auch <a href="./熏">熏</a>, <a href="./燻">燻</a> ]  (V, Ess)​,verräuchern, ausgasen, desinfizieren [ auch <a href="./熏">熏</a>, <a href="./燻">燻</a> ]  (V)​,Xun  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vapeur,suffoquer,plantes aromatiques</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">薰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>薰</strong> <span class="dicpy">xūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，熏声。本义:蕙草)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 香草名。即<span class="yhcs">“蕙草”</span>，又名<span class="yhcs">“零陵香”</span> <span class="encs">[holy basil]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薰，香草也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薰草，蕙草也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span>。按，即今零陵香。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叶曰薰，根曰薰是也。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span><span class="diczx2">王逸</span>注</p><p><span class="diczx1">一薰一蕕。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浮山有草焉，名曰薰。麻叶而方茎，赤华而黑实，臭如蘼芜，佩之可以已疠。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·西山经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">腐鼠在坛，烧薰于宫。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说林》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:薰莸(薰和莸。一种香草和一种臭草;比喻美丑、善恶或君子、小人等);薰燧(焚烧香草以取香);薰草(即蕙草。俗名佩兰)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 香气、香味 <span class="encs">[aroma;fragrance of flowers]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陌上草薰——</span><span class="diczx2">江淹</span><span class="smcs">《别赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“香气也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:薰薰(酒器弥漫的样子)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>薰</strong> <span class="dicpy">xūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 以气味或烟气烤制物品。通<span class="yhcs">“熏”</span> <span class="encs">[treat meat，fish，etc. with smoke]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薰以香，自烧，此用其根也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·龚胜传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:薰鱼;薰鸡;薰服(香熏的衣服。指妓女或妓乐。古代妓女用香薰其服，故名);薰沐(薰香和沐浴。表示虔敬)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 感化。潜移默化地受影响 <span class="encs">[exert]</span>。如:薰染(感化，薰陶习染);薰修(佛家语。指修行。因用得修身，好比用香薰衣服，故名)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 烧灼，熏炙 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燎薰炉兮炳明烛。——</span><span class="diczx2">谢惠连</span><span class="smcs">《雪赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“火烟上出也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薰胥以刑。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·叙传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓相薰蒸。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:薰戒(焚香斋戒);薰灼(用烟、火熏烤);薰心(心受熏灼。常形容愁苦)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用香物涂身 <span class="encs">[burn joss sticks and take a
