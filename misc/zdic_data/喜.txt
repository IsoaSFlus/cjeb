						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>喜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>高兴，快乐：欢～。～悦。～讯。～剧。～气。～色。～幸。～乐（<span class="dicpy">l&egrave; ㄌㄜˋ</span>）。～洋洋。欢天～地。欣～若狂。</li>
	<li>可庆贺的，特指关于结婚的：～事。～酒。～糖。～蛋。～联。～幛。～雨。～报。～庆。贺～。报～。</li>
	<li>妇女怀孕：害～。她有～了。</li>
	<li>爱好：～爱。～好（<span class="dicpy">好</span>）。～欢。好（<span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o ㄏㄠˋ</span>）大～功（热衷于做大事，立大功，现常用以形容浮夸的作风）。</li>
	<li>适于：～光植物。海带～荤。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">喜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>喜</strong> <span class="dicpy">xǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文上面是&ldquo;鼓&rdquo;本字，下面是&ldquo;口&rdquo;。&ldquo;鼓&rdquo;表示欢乐，&ldquo;口&rdquo;是发出欢声。本义:快乐;高兴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(乐之见于谈笑曰喜。喜，形于色) <span class="encs">[happy;pleased]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">喜，乐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人喜则斯陶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其志为喜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;五远行大论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既见君子，我心则喜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;菁菁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈胜</span>、 <span class="diczx2">吴广</span>喜，念鬼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嘿然大喜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虎因喜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不以物喜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">三保</span>亦自喜。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">喜容原好，愁容也好，蓦地间怒容越好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《二十年目睹之怪现状》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:喜洽(喜恰。和颜悦色;热情);喜之不尽(无限欢喜);喜匆匆(喜欢，兴奋的样子);喜欢不杀(很喜欢;喜欢死了);喜上眉梢(眉宇间露出喜悦的表情)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 容易 <span class="encs">[easy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人命难知，计算喜错。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《百喻经&middot;婆罗门杀子喻》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>喜</strong> <span class="dicpy">xǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 喜爱;爱好 <span class="encs">[be fond of;like]</span></p>
