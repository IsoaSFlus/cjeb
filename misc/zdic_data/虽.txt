                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>虽</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">雖</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">suī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄟˉ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 连词，把意思推开一层，表示“即使”或是“纵然”的意思，后面多有“可是”、“但是”相应：～然。～则。麻雀～小，五脏俱全。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> although, even if</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> obschon, obwohl, selbst wenn, zwar  (Konj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bien que,malgré que</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>虽</strong></p><p>雖 <span class="dicpy">suī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><连></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，唯声。古读 ( <span class="dicpy">huī</span>)。本义:虫，似蜥蜴而大)</p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>兵旦暮渡 <span class="diczx2">易水</span>，则虽欲长侍足下，岂可得哉?——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">齐国虽褊小，吾何爱一牛!——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:虽故(虽然);他虽身负重伤，但坚决不下火线</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 即使…也;纵使 <span class="encs">[even if]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽人有百口，手有百指，不能指其一端。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先王之法，经手上世而来者也，人或益之，人或损之，胡可得而法?虽人弗损益，犹若不可得而法。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 仅，只 <span class="encs">[only]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽有明君，能决之，又能塞之。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 原本，本 <span class="encs">[originally]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你那里休夸口，你虽是一人为害，我与那</span><span class="diczx2">陈州</span>百姓每分忧。——<span class="smcs">《陈州粜米》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/虽然">虽然</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/虽说">虽说</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/虽死犹荣">虽死犹荣</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/虽死犹生">虽死犹生</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/虽则">虽则</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">佀蜥易而大。</span>易各本作蜴，誤。今正。此字之本義也。自借以爲語䛐，尟有知其本義者矣。常棣云：每有良朋。又云：雖有兄弟。傳云：每，雖也。凡人竆極其欲曰恣睢。雖卽睢也。按方言守宫在澤中者，東齊海岱謂之螔䗔。注云：似蜥易大而有鱗。䗔字疑雖之誤。<span class="jieshi">从虫唯聲。</span>息遺切。十五部。                          </div>
