                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>柙</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiá.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄚˊ</span></p><ol><li>关闭猛兽的笼槛，亦指押解犯人的囚笼或囚车。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">匣</span>”，收藏东西的器具。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cage, pen for wild animals</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Käfig  (S)​,Steg, Kabine (eines Aufzugs)​  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> enclos</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>柙</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (古文象形。从木，甲声。本义:关兽的木笼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wooden cage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柙，槛也，以藏虎兕。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虎兕出于柙。——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">实则禽槛豕匣也。——<span class="smcs">《菩提寺疏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">杨幺</span>虽有忠义之心，其余虎性岂能易驯?幸得入柙，留之必遗后患。——<span class="smcs">《后水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:柙板(一种刑具);柙床(扣住重犯手脚的睡床)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“匣”</span>。箱匣 <span class="encs">[box]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柙而藏之，不敢用也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·刻意》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子纯终领闻，蠢迪检柙。——<span class="smcs">《法言·序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:柙匮(箱柜)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>柙</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="encs">[用囚笼、囚车]</span> 关押，押解 <span class="encs">[lock up or escort]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂生束缚而柙以予</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>。——<span class="smcs">《管子·小匡》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拱柙天人矣。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
