                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>梁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liáng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>架在墙上或柱子上支撑房顶的横木，泛指水平方向的长条形承重构件：房～。栋～。～上君子（指窃贼）。</li><li>桥：桥～。津～（渡口和桥梁，借指学习的门径）。</li><li>器物、身体或其他物体上中间高起的部分：鼻～。山～。车～。</li><li>中国战国时期国名，魏国于公元前361年迁都大梁（今河南省开封市）后，改称“梁”。</li><li>中国朝代名（ａ．南朝之一；ｂ．五代之一）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">梁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>梁</strong></p><p>樑 <span class="dicpy">liáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从木，从水，刅( <span class="dicpy">chuāng</span>)声。从<span class="yhcs">“木”</span>从<span class="yhcs">“水”</span>，表示用木料在水上造桥。本义:水桥)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bridge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">梁，水桥也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">梁丽可以冲城。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·秋水》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“小船也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">除道梁溠。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谨关梁。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十一月舆梁成。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十月成梁。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">津梁之上。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">造桥为梁，不显其光。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·大明》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兹</span><span class="diczx2">郑子</span>引辇上高梁而不能支。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说右下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泽无舟梁。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:河梁(桥，又借指送别之地);津梁(渡和桥梁);桥梁(桥);梁缘(为得到好的报应而做的修桥补路之类的好事);梁头(桥头);梁津(桥梁与渡口);梁栈(桥梁)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 房梁 <span class="encs">[girder;roof beam]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兔从狗窦入，雉从梁上飞。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·十五从军征》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">架梁之椽。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:横梁;悬梁(在房梁上上吊);梁楹(屋<span class="yhcs">“梁和大柱);梁倚(如屋梁一般的相依附、倚靠);梁木其坏(以梁木的折毁来比喻贤哲的死亡)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 稻粱。通“粱”</span> <span class="encs">[millet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">养以刍豢黍梁。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·人间》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:梁肉(粱肉。泛指美酒佳肴。梁，通<span class="yhcs">“粱”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 山谷之间的延绵高地 <span
