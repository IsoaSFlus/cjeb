                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叨</strong> <span class="dicpy">dāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>话多 <span class="encs">[chatter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叨缕(叨叨缕缕。说话啰嗦冗长);叨蹬(捣腾，闹腾);絮叨(念叨);唠叨(没完没了地说)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>叨</strong> <span class="dicpy">dāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 〈方〉∶临死前气息急促的样子 <span class="encs">[gasping]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">等他爬起来一看呀，小兔子正在叨气儿呢，胸脯里的心跳得一撞一撞的。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杨朔</span><span class="smcs">《春子姑娘》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">tāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叨</strong> <span class="dicpy">tāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 贪婪。饕的俗字 <span class="encs">[be greedy for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂横叨天功以为己力乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;卢植传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叨食(贪食);叨冒(叨贪。贪婪);叨懫(贪而暴戾);叨秽(贪婪卑鄙);叨沓(贪婪而荒于政务);叨昧(贪恋;贪婪);叨富贵</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 承受 <span class="encs">[get the benefit of]</span>。古汉语中用于对受人恩惠及礼物表示感谢的谦词</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">位窍和羹重，恩叨醉酒深。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张说</span><span class="smcs">《恩赐丽正殿书院宴应制得林字》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叨福(承受福庇);叨庇(承受庇佑);叨沫(蒙受);叨位(忝居官位);叨名(虚有其名);叨忝(忝列;叨光);叨受(承受);叨承(忝受;承受)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">dāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
