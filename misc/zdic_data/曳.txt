                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>曳</strong> <span class="dicpy">yè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从申，从丿，丿( <span class="dicpy">yì</span>)亦声。申，伸展。本义:拖，牵引)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[drag;haul]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曳，臾曳也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。臾曳:束缚捽摔之意。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">车轮曳踵。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">武举前曳踵。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士相见礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“古文作抽。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曳彗星之皓旰兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·怨思》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“引也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曳笏却立。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">负箧曳屣。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曳屋许许声。(曳屋，指众人把被火烧着的房子拉倒。许许声，指拉倒房子时众人一齐用力的呼哧声。)——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:弃甲曳兵;曳尾泥涂(比喻污秽卑鄙的行为);曳步(脚步轻缓挪移);曳脚慢坡(斜坡):曳布拖麻(曳布披麻。披麻带孝);曳开(迈开;拉开);曳兵弃甲(拖着兵器，丢掉铠甲。形容打败仗逃跑时的狼狈相)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 穿着 <span class="encs">[dress]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子有衣裳，弗曳弗娄。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“娄、曳俱是著衣之事。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骄侈不循法度，伎妾数百，曳罗纨，甘粱肉，放于声乐以自娱。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:曳缟(穿白绢衣服);曳娄(穿戴)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 飘摇 <span class="encs">[sway]</span>。如:摇曳;曳曳的(飘飘悠悠);曳曳(飘动的样子);曳动(摇动，飘荡);曳影(犹摇影)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 率领 <span class="encs">[lead]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孙子</span>命 <span class="diczx2">章子</span>曳兵与 <span class="diczx2">燕</span>兵对阵。——<span class="smcs">《乐毅图齐平话》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>曳</strong> <span class="dicpy">yè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>困顿，精力衰竭 <span class="encs">[tired out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贫而不衰， 贱而不恨， 年虽疲曳， 犹庶几名贤之风。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
