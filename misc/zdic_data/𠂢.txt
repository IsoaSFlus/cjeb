                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>𠂢</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄞˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>水的支流。后作&ldquo;派&rdquo;。《説文&bull;𠂢部》：&ldquo;𠂢，水之衺流别也。&rdquo;段玉裁注：&ldquo;𠂢與《水部》派音義皆同，派蓋後出耳。&rdquo;《集韻&bull;卦韻》：&ldquo;𠂢，水分流也。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/200A2.svg">                          <p>水之衺流，別也。从反永。凡<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200A2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂢</span>之屬皆从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200A2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂢</span>。讀若稗縣。匹卦切〖注〗徐鍇曰：&ldquo;永，長流也。反卽分<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200A2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂢</span>也。&rdquo;</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/200A2_9158.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200A2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂢</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">水之衺流別也。</span><span class="swdz">流別者，一水岐分之謂。禹貢曰：漾東流爲漢。沇東流爲沱。江東別爲沱。此言流別之始。釋水詳之。自河出爲灉，濟爲濋已下是也。流別則其勢必衺行，故曰衺流別。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200A2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂢</span>與水部派音義皆同，派葢後出耳。衺流別，則正流之長者較短而巠理同也，故其字从反永。</span><span class="ywyy">从反永。</span><span class="swdz">匹卦切。十六部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200A2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂢</span>之屬皆<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200A2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂢</span>。讀若稗縣。</span><span class="swdz">禾部曰：琅邪有稗縣。今地理志作椑縣，誤也。小徐本作蜀稗縣，非。蜀衹有郫縣，音疲。</span></p>                          </div>
