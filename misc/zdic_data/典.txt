                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>典</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">diǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>可以作为标准的书籍：～籍。字～。词～。经～。引经据～。</li>
	<li>标准，法则：～章。～制。～故（ ➊ 典制和掌故； ➋ 诗文里引用的古书中的故事或词句）。～范。～雅。～礼。～型。</li>
	<li>指典礼：盛～。大～。</li>
	<li>主持，主管：～试（主持科举考试之事）。～狱。</li>
	<li>活买活卖，到期可以赎：～卖。～押。～契。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">典</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>典</strong> <span class="dicpy">diǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面是&ldquo;册&rdquo;字，下面是&ldquo;廾&rdquo;。本义:重要的文献、典籍)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[work of standard scholarship;classics]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">典，五帝之书也&hellip;庄都说，典，大册也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，谓从册，从大，古文从古文册。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">典，经也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可为典要。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是能读三坟、五典、八索、九丘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">司</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>之典籍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十五年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不远而复，先典攸高(重贵)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自书典所记，未之有也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:药典;字典;释典(佛经);法典;佛典;引经据典;典志(记载政典的文籍);典坟(<span class="smcs">《三坟》</span>和<span class="smcs">《五典》</span>的省称。都是上古的典籍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 常道，准则 <span class="encs">[norm]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:典型(常刑;常规);常典(常法);典要(不变的标准);典式(典范，法式)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 法律;法规 <span class="encs">[canon;law]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌建邦之三典，轻典、中典、重典也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:典例(可依为准则的成例);典常(常法，常规);典诰(古代文体名。典指常法;诰指诏令)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 典礼，仪节 <span class="encs">[ceremony]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:盛典;开国大典;庆典(庆祝典礼);典重涂山(比喻典礼重要，规模宏大。相传涂山是夏禹娶涂山氏及会见诸侯的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 典故。诗文等作品中引用的古代故事和有来历出处的词语 <span class="encs">[literary quotation]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:语出何典;典实(典故，史实);典证(出典和依据)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 典章，法令制度。又称&ldquo;典度&rdquo; <span class="encs">[decrees and regulations]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:典则(典章法则);典册(记载典章制度等的重要册籍);典律(典章律令)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>典</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄉㄧㄢˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>diǎn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1198.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">五帝時的書，指重要的文獻、簡冊。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;昭公十二年》：「是能讀三墳五典，八索九丘。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">國家的常法。</span><span class="gc_yy">《周禮&middot;天官&middot;大宰》：「大宰之職，掌建邦之六典，以佐王治邦國。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">可作為依據或模範的書。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「字典」、「引經據典」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">可遵守的法度、準則。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;劉琨&middot;勸進表》：「中興之兆，圖讖垂典，自京畿隕喪，九服崩離，天下囂然，無所歸懷。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">典禮、儀式。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「慶典」、「大典」、「盛典」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">前代的故事、制度、文物。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「用典」、「數典忘祖」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如三國時魏國有典韋。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">掌管、治理。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「典試」、「典獄」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《三國志&middot;卷六二&middot;吳書&middot;是儀傳》：「到見親任，專典機密。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">以物品質押貸款。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「典當」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈曲江〉詩二首之二：「朝回日日典春衣，每日江頭盡醉歸。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
