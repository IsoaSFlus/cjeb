                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">邮</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>邮</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">郵</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄡˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>由国家专设的机构传递信件、款项、物件等：～递。～包。通～。</li>	<li>有关邮务的：～政。～购。～船。</li>	<li>特指&ldquo;邮票&rdquo;：～展。～坛（集邮界）。集～。</li>	<li>古代传递文书的驿站：～吏。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> postal, mail; post office</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> etw. mit der Post schicken  (V)​,postalisch  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> envoyer par la poste,postal</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">邮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>邮</strong></p>
<p>郵 <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从邑垂。垂，边郵;邑，城邑。&ldquo;邮&rdquo;，从邑，由声。本义:古地名。)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[You village]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邮，左冯翊高陵，从邑，由声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，邮亭，邮乡，在今陕西西安府高陵县。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 传递文书的驿站 <span class="encs">[post;mail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德之流行，速于置邮而传命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因邮上封事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缮治邮亭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵充国传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:邮吏(驿战管邮递的小官);邮馆(设在驿站的旅店);邮驿(古时办理传送公务信件的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指传送文书的人 <span class="encs">[postman;mailman]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">殷洪乔</span>不为致书邮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;殷浩传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:邮人(传递公文书信的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代井田间田官督耕所居的庐舍 <span class="encs">[house]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邮舍(古代田官督耕居住的庐舍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;尤&rdquo;。过失，罪过 <span class="encs">[fault;crime]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不知其邮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;宾之初筵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邮又甚焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语四》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">韦昭</span>注:&ldquo;邮，过也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以显朕邮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;成帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:邮罚(判人罪过，处罚其身)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>邮</strong></p>
<p>郵 <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 传递;邮寄 <span class="encs">[post]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邮子(驿卒。传送文书、公物的差役);邮书(寄递的书信);信已经邮去;这月给母亲邮三十元</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;尤&rdquo;。责怪 <span class="encs">[blame]</span></p>
