						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>浍</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">澮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄨㄞˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 田间水沟：涓～（小流）。《周禮&bull;地官&bull;稻人》：&ldquo;以列舍水，以澮寫水。&rdquo;</p>
<ol>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>浍</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">澮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>水名。（➊浍水。源出山西省&middot;翼城县东，西流经曲沃县、侯马市注入汾河。➋浍河。源出河南省，流经安徽省入淮。）</li>
	<li>古城名。在今山西省翼城县。</li>
</ol>

<ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>浍</strong></p>
<p>澮 <span class="dicpy">Hu&igrave;</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 河名，浍河 <span class="encs">[Huihe River]</span>。源出河南省，流经安徽省北部，上游为东沙河和包河，在临涣汇合后，向东南流经固镇、边城，又向东流至五河县北与沱河汇合，最后流注洪泽湖</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>浍</strong></p>
<p>澮 <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，会声。( <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )本义:水名。也称浍河。源出河南省，流入安徽省) 田的排水沟 <span class="encs">[water ditches in field]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以浍写水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;稻人》</span>。注:&ldquo;田尾去水大沟。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而深山卡谷之水，四面而出，沟渠浍川，十百相通。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《上杜学士言开河书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6FAE.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕巜<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23D2F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣴯</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CB8.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣲸</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>古外切，音儈。水名。【說文】水出靃山，西南入汾。【水經注】澮水，出詳高山。</p>
<p>又涓澮，小流也。【郭璞&middot;江賦】商搉涓澮。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋水】水注溝曰澮。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;遂人】千夫有澮。【疏】澮，廣二尋，深二仞。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>古邁切，音夬。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】烏外切，音薈。與濊同。深廣也。</p>
<p>又戸八切，音滑。兩水合也。</p>
