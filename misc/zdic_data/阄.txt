                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>阄</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鬮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄡˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>[抓～]为了赌胜负或决定事情而各自抓取做好记号的纸团等。</li>
	<li>抓阄时用的纸团等。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> lots (to be drawn); draw lots</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Los</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tirer au sort</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阄</strong></p>
<p>鬮 <span class="dicpy">jiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>写上字或画上记号的小纸团，供赌胜负或决定事情时抓取 <span class="encs">[(cast a) lot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:抓阄;纸阄;阄儿(即&ldquo;阄&rdquo;);钻阄(抓阄)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阄</strong></p>
<p>鬮 <span class="dicpy">jiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>拈阄，抓阄 <span class="encs">[cast a lot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:阄开(以抓阄的方式分开);阄题(以抓阄确定题目);阄定(用抓阄的办法定);阄戏(抓阄游戏)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
