                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>纽</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">紐</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">niǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄧㄡˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>器物上可以提起或系挂的部分：秤～。印～。</li>
	<li>可以扣合衣物的球状物、片状物或其它形状的东西：～扣。</li>
	<li>操纵的机键；关键：电～。枢～。～带。</li>
	<li>瓜果等刚结的果实：瓜～儿。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纽</strong></p>
<p>紐 <span class="dicpy">niǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，丑声。从糸，与丝线有关。本义:指可解的结)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a knot that can be undone by a pull;slipknot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纽，系也。一曰结而可解。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，结而不可解曰缔。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">并纽约。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span>。疏:&ldquo;带之交结之处。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冠帻簪簧结发纽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《急就篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纽约(用以扣合衣服的纽结)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 比喻事物的根本，关键 <span class="encs">[pivot;key]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span> <span class="diczx2">舜</span>之所纽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;人间世》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:电纽;枢纽</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 纽绊。器物上用以提携或系绳带的部件 <span class="encs">[button loop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延纽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;弁师》</span>。注:&ldquo;小鼻在武上，笄可贯也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:秤纽;印纽</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 钮扣 <span class="encs">[button]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:纽门(纽扣的套孔，纽攀儿);纽门儿(纽门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 汉语音韵学名词，指辅音或声母。如&ldquo;声母&rdquo;又称&ldquo;声纽&rdquo; <span class="encs">[initial consonant]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>

<p>又姓。【隋書&middot;孝義傳】紐回，字孝政，河東安邑人。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶碾與切。【陳琳&middot;大荒賦】建皇極以連衡兮，布辰機而結紐。陽幹曜于乾門兮，隂氣伏于地戸。</p>
