						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>封</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄥˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>密闭，使跟外面隔绝：～闭。密～。～口。～存。～条。～贮。～面。～底。～锁。～顶。冰～雪飘。～山育林。</li>
	<li>用来装信、钱等物的纸套：信～。赏～。</li>
	<li>帝王把土地或爵位给予亲属或臣僚：～侯。～君（领有封地的贵族）。～邑。～拜（拜官授爵）。～典。～建。</li>
	<li>疆界：～疆。～域。～界。</li>
	<li>限制：故步自～。</li>
	<li>帝王筑坛祭天：～禅（祭天为&ldquo;封&rdquo;，祭地为&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">禅</span>&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>量词，用于装封套的东西：一～信。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">封</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>封</strong> <span class="dicpy">fēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，左边象土上长着丰茂的树木形，右边是一只手(又，后来写作&ldquo;寸&rdquo;)，表示聚土培植。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;封，爵诸侯之土也。从之从土从寸。&rdquo; <span class="diczx2">郭沫若</span>曰:&ldquo;古之畿封实以树为之也。此习于今犹存。然其事之起，乃远在太古。太古之民多利用自然林木以为族与族间之畛域，西方学者所称为境界林者是也。&rdquo;。 <span class="diczx2">李孝定</span>曰:&ldquo;封之本义当以 <span class="diczx2">郭</span>说为是， <span class="diczx2">许</span>训乃后起之义。字象植树土上，以明经界。爵诸侯必有封疆，乃其引申义。&rdquo;本义:疆界;田界)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[boundary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所封封域。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;春官&middot;保章氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田有封洫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使田有封洫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;乐成》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既东封</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>，又欲肆其西封。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:封内，封外(天子或诸侯的领地之内或外);封守(边防;封疆);封界，封略(疆界;边境);封洫(区分田界的水沟);封畔(国界)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 坟堆;土堆 <span class="encs">[mound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而五人者亦得以加其土封。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 土堆或形状像坟的堆积物 <span class="encs">[mound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所谓平原者，下泽也。虽有小封，不得为高。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 国土，土地 <span class="encs">[land of a country;land]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">土地之大，封内千里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 泛指书信 <span class="encs">[letter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">封来江渺渺，信去雨冥冥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《酬令狐郎中见寄》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 封建主义的简称 <span class="encs">[feudalism]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 封山，山名 <span class="encs">[Feng mountain]</span>。在今浙江省德清县西南</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>封</strong> <span class="dicpy">fēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 分封土地 <span class="encs">[present vavasory]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以赂</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之地，封天下之谋臣。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故</span><span class="diczx2">太公望</span>封于 <span class="diczx2">营丘</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot; 货殖列传序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:封国(封土立国);封爵(封土授爵);封赋(受封田地的赋税);封邑(赐给领地以为食邑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 帝王把爵位及土地赐给臣子也叫封 <span class="encs">[confer upon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">安緼王</span>即位，封公子为 <span class="diczx2">信陵君</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">刘良佐</span>者，故弘光四镇之一，封 <span class="diczx2">广昌伯</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《阎典史传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:分封(古代帝王社祭，以五色土为坛，分封诸侯则割方色之土，以茅草包之，授予受封者，作为分封土地的表示);封名(封爵赐姓);封拜(赐爵授官);封侯(封拜侯爵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 堆土植树为界;培土 <span class="encs">[earth up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将何以封矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是封之，崇四尺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开阡陌封疆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;商君书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:封植(封土植树);封树(堆土植树以固疆界);封表(堆土以作标记);封殖(壅土培育)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代帝王打胜仗筑土坛以祭天也叫封 <span class="encs">[build soil altar to worship heaven in ancient Emperor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">元嘉草草，封狼居胥，赢得仓皇北顾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐&middot;京口北固亭怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 堆土筑坟 <span class="encs">[stack soil to build a grave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">封王子</span><span class="diczx2">比干</span>之墓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用加盖印章的纸条贴在门、箱或其他容器的口上以防开启 <span class="encs">[seal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">籍吏民，封府库，而待将军。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公拆袄，出珠授之，封识宛然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:封押(缄封签押);封包(封缄);封册(封缄玉册);封志(封识，封记);封检(加盖印记的封口)。又指用密封物或结扎物把容器弄严，防止空气流通。如:把酱菜坛子封好</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 界限，局限 <span class="encs">[limit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余不敢以麋鹿自封也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">徐光启</span><span class="smcs">《甘薯疏序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民族者，非封于</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>属而已。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">章炳麟</span><span class="smcs">《复仇是非论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 封闭(以封记关闭，使不能动用、通行或随便打开);堵塞 <span class="encs">[close]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂收盛</span><span class="diczx2">樊於期</span>之首，函封之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:封贮(封存，贮藏);封藏;封锢(严密关锁);封关(封锁关口);封灵(盖棺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 用新加燃料或其他物质把火盖住 <span class="encs">[bank up]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炉子封了吗?</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">包好的或用來裝東西的紙袋。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「賞封」、「信封」、「拆封」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞。計算包裹或裝上封套物件的單位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「一封信」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;高適〈送李少府貶峽中王少府貶長沙〉詩：「巫峽啼猿數行淚，衡陽歸雁幾封書。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第二回》：「至次日早有雨村遣人送了兩封銀子&hellip;&hellip;答謝甄家娘子。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">疆界。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;僖公三十年》：「（晉）​又欲肆其西封。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;杜預&middot;注：「封，疆也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。唐代有封演。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">密閉、緘合。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大雪封山」、「封住洞口」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《後漢書&middot;卷六&middot;順帝紀》：「而遠獻大珠，以求幸媚，今封以還之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈秦中吟&middot;買花〉：「水灑復泥封，移來色如故。」</span></p></li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">限制。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「故步自封」。</span></p></li>

	<li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">富厚、增加。</span><span class="gc_yy">《國語&middot;楚語上》：「是聚民利以自封而瘠民也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國吳&middot;韋昭&middot;注：「封，厚也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈風疾舟中伏枕書懷三十六韻奉呈湖南親友〉詩：「春草封歸恨，源花費獨尋。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">圍棋中以破壞對手的通道而對其實施封鎖的下法。</span></p></li>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=寸" target="_blank">寸</a>】
                              封·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5C01.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21258.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡉘</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/212BD.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡊽</span>【唐韻】府容切【集韻】【韻會】方容切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音崶。【說文】爵諸侯之土也。从之从土从寸。【徐曰】各之其土也，寸守其法度也。本作封，隸作封，从圭所執也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大宗伯】王大封，則告后土。【註】封，土地之事也。</p>
<p>又【大戴禮】五十里而封。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】同十爲封，封十爲畿，畿方千里。</p>
<p>又封疆之官曰封人。【周禮&middot;地官】封人掌設王之社壝，爲畿封而樹之。</p>
<p>又【大司徒之職】凡造都鄙，制其地域，而溝封之。【註】封，起土界也。土在溝上謂之封，封上樹木以爲固也。</p>
<p>又大也。【詩&middot;商頌】封建厥福。【書&middot;舜典】封十有二山。【孔傳】封，大也。每州之名山殊大者，以爲其州之鎮。</p>
<p>又封禪，祭名。積土增山曰封，爲墠祭地曰禪。【孝經緯】封於泰山，禪於梁甫。【白虎通】王者封禪以告太平。金泥銀繩，封以印璽。孔子登泰山，觀易姓而王可得而數者，七十餘封是也。</p>
<p>又聚土曰封。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;冢人】以爵等爲丘封之度與樹數。</p>
<p>又培也。【吳語】封殖越國。【註】壅本曰封。</p>
<p>又富厚也。【史記&middot;貨殖傳】無秩祿之奉，爵邑之入，而樂與之比者，命曰素封。</p>
<p>又凡專利自私曰封。【詩&middot;周頌】無封靡于爾邦。【禮&middot;王制】名山大澤不以封。【鄭註】與民同財，不得障管也。</p>
<p>又緘也。漢制，奏事皁囊封板，以防宣泄，謂之封事。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;平帝紀】諸乗傳者，持尺五木傳信，封以御史大夫印。</p>
<p>又國名。【禮&middot;明堂位】封父龜。【註】封父，國也。</p>
<p>又州名。蒼梧郡地，隋爲封州。</p>
<p>又姓。黃帝時有封鉅。</p>
<p>又賁氏改封氏。</p>
<p>又穿封，複姓。</p>
<p>又土精。【白澤圖】物如小兒手，無指，名封。食之多力。</p>
<p>又封豕，大豕名。</p>
<p>又去聲。【廣韻】芳用切，音葑【書&middot;蔡仲之命】往卽乃封。徐邈讀。</p>
<p>又叶府良切，音方。【韓愈&middot;李道古銘】本支于今，其尚有封。叶下亡。封字从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2125A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡉚</span>从寸作。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">封</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=土">土</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5C01.svg">                          <p>爵諸矦之土也。从之从土从寸，守其制度也。公侯，百里；伯，七十里；子男，五十里。㞷，古文封省。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21258.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡉘</span>，籒文从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21D00.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡴀</span>。府容切〖注〗徐鍇曰：各之其土也。會意。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5C01_1515.svg" /><span class="swbf">(封)</span><span class="ywyy">爵諸侯之土也。</span><span class="swdz">謂爵命諸侯以是土也。詩毛傳：之子，嫁子也。之事，祭事也。莊子之人也，卽是人也。然則之土言是土也。其義之土，故其字从之土。引申爲凡畛域之偁。大司徒注曰：封，起土畍也。封人注曰：聚土曰封，謂壝堳埒及小封疆也。冡人注曰：王公曰丘，諸臣曰封。又引申爲大也。又引申爲緘固之偁。</span><span class="ywyy">从之土。从寸。</span><span class="swdz">合三字會意。府容切。九部。</span><span class="ywyy">寸，守其制度也。</span><span class="swdz">此說从寸之意。凡法度曰寸，尊字，寺字下皆曰有法度。</span><span class="ywyy">公、侯百里，伯七十里，子、男五十里。</span><span class="swdz">此用孟子及王制之說。所謂制度也。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5C01_1516.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21258.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡉘</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文封，从丰土。</span><span class="swdz">从土，丰聲也。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5C01_1517.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2125A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡉚</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文封省。</span><span class="swdz">無寸也。从㞢土，則與<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21CFF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡳿</span>部讀若皇者同字，猶豕亥古文同作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22053.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢁓</span>也。艸木<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/216B6.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡚶</span>生字之在土上，之之本義也。爵諸侯之土字从之土，之之引申叚借義也。</span></p>                          </div>
