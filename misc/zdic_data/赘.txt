                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赘</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>赘</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">贅</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>多余的，多而无用的：累（<span class="dicpy">l&eacute;i </span>）～。～述。～言。～词。</li>
	<li>招女婿：～婿。入～。招～。</li>
	<li>会聚。</li>
	<li>方言，使受累赘：这孩子～得我什么也干不了。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/缀">缀</a>&rdquo;，连结。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> unnecessary, superfluous</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> überflüssig, unnötig</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> répéter,joindre,excroissance,parasite,superflu</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赘</strong></p>
<p>贅 <span class="dicpy">zhu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从敖贝。敖犹&ldquo;放&rdquo;，贝与财富有关。本义:抵押)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pawn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赘，以物质钱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赘，得也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span>。按，以物得钱也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卖子与人作奴婢名曰赘子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;俗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">间者，数年岁比不登，民待卖爵赘子以接衣食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;严助传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赘子(旧时穷苦人家把孩子卖给富家做奴婢的，叫做&ldquo;赘子&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 聚会 <span class="encs">[assemble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">梁王</span>赘其群臣而议其过。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说苑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赘聚(会聚);赘结(聚集，集结)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;缀&rdquo;。连缀，附着 <span class="encs">[join together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君若赘旒然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;襄公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲赘天下之兵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;存韩》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虎贲赘衣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《西都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如;赘附(依附);赘旒(连缀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 增添;附加 <span class="encs">[add;attach]</span>。如;赘名(附列其名)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 累赘;祸害 <span class="encs">[burdensome]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赘累(拖累);赘冗(累赘繁冗);赘脚(拖累)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赘</strong></p>
<p>贅 <span class="dicpy">zhu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 多余;无用 <span class="encs">[superfluous]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赘木(树木上隆起的节目);赘人(多余无用的人);赘土(无用之地);赘叙(多余的叙述);赘物(多余的东西)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 恶的 <span class="encs">[evil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有独见之虑，见赘于人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>赘</strong></p>
<p>贅 <span class="dicpy">zhu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 就婚于女家与改为女家姓的男子称为&ldquo;赘婿&rdquo;。对男家来说，出去当赘婿称为&ldquo;出赘&rdquo;。对女家来说，招女婿称为&ldquo;招赘&rdquo;。男子到女家当赘婿称为&ldquo;入赘&rdquo;<span class="encs">[a son-in-law who marries into wife&rsquo;s family and take her family name]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赘而不赎，主家配以女，则谓之赘婿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">发诸赘婿贾人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;始皇纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">土将军</span>，你若早破 <span class="diczx2">西岐</span>，吾将弱女赘公为婿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《封神演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赘入(入赘);赘娶(入赘);赘亲(入赘成亲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 颈椎骨 <span class="encs">[cervical vertebra]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肩高于顶，句赘指天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;大宗师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 赘瘤;肉瘤 <span class="encs">[anything superfluous or useless]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赘，属也。横生一肉属着体也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">具赘卒荒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;桑柔》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赘疵(肉瘤。比喻多余无用之物);赘木(有赘瘤状物的树木);赘行(赘瘤)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8D05.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27DCC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧷌</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27E06.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧸆</span>【唐韻】之芮切【集韻】【韻會】朱芮切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B65.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠭥</span>。【說文】以物質錢，从敖貝。敖者，猶放貝當復取之也。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】具贅卒荒。【傳】贅，屬也。【疏】贅，猶綴也。謂繫綴而屬之。</p>
<p>又【莊子&middot;大宗師】彼以生爲附贅縣疣。【釋名】贅，屬也。橫生一肉，屬著體也。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;滑<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25874.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥡴</span>傳】淳于髠者，齊之贅壻也。【註】索隱曰：贅壻，女之夫，比于子，如人疣贅是餘剩物也。【前漢&middot;賈誼傳】家貧子壯則出贅。【註】師古曰：贅，質也。家貧無有聘財，以身爲質也。</p>
<p>又會也。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】毋贅聚。【註】如淳曰：贅，會也。</p>
<p>又行不當也。【老子&middot;道德經】其于道也，若餘食贅行。【註】行之無當曰贅。</p>
<p>又言煩也。【曾鞏&middot;講官議】問一告二謂之贅。</p>
<p>又【博雅】贅，定也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】最也，得也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】牛交切，音敖。【類篇】頝贅，不媚。</p>
