                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>娥</strong> <span class="dicpy">é</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/é.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，我声。本义:女子容貌美好。常<span class="yhcs">“娥媌”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“娥娥”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“娥姣”</span>等连用，形容女子美貌) 同本义 <span class="encs">[beautiful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">娥媌靡曼者。——<span class="smcs">《列子·周穆王》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“妖好也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:娥姣(容貌美好);娥苗(妖艳美好)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>娥</strong> <span class="dicpy">é</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/é.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 美女 <span class="encs">[beautiful woman]</span>。如:娥流(女流);娥妆(美女的妆饰);娥苗(轻盈美丽的女子);娥辉(女子的光彩)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 借指眉毛 <span class="encs">[eyebrows]</span>。如:娥翠(女子细长而黑的眉);娥绿(即螺黛。妇女画眉用的青黑色颜料)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 传说中尧女舜妻的字 <span class="encs">[E，one of the two daughters of the emperor Yao]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">娥，帝</span><span class="diczx2">尧</span>之女 <span class="diczx2">舜</span>妻娥皇字也。 <span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>谓好曰姪娥。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:娥皇女英(相传是尧的两个女儿，同嫁给虞舜为妃。后舜出巡时死于苍梧之野，她二人奔丧到南方，投湘水而死)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 嫦娥的简称 <span class="encs">[Chang’e，the goddess of the moon]</span>。如:娥轮(月亮的别称);娥月(月亮的别称。传说月中有嫦娥，故称);娥魄(月亮);娥轮(月亮);娥影(月光。指镜中美人的影子或月中的嫦娥)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/娥眉">娥眉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/娥眉月">娥眉月</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
