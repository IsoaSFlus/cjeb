                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>老</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄠˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>年纪大，时间长，有经验，陈旧的：～当益壮。～朋友。～练。～化。少年～成。～马识途。</li>
	<li>对年纪大的人的尊称：吴～。～人家。～大爷。</li>
	<li>极，很：～早。～羞成怒。</li>
	<li>老年人：敬～院。扶～携幼。～有所为（<span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i </span>）。</li>
	<li>晚年：～年。～境。</li>
	<li>敬老，养老：&ldquo;～吾老，以及人之老。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>总是，经常：～是生病。</li>
	<li>原来的：～地方。</li>
	<li>与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">嫩</span>&rdquo;相对：黄瓜长～了。</li>
	<li>词头，用于表排行，用于表相互尊称，或加在某些动植物名前构成多音节词：～大。～鹰。～倭瓜。</li>
	<li>老子（中国先秦思想家）及其学说的简称。</li>
	<li>死的讳称：～了。</li>
	<li>〔～板〕指业主或企业的经营者。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">老</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>老</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象一个手里拿着拐杖的老人形。本义:年老，衰老)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 五十至七十岁的高龄 <span class="encs">[old;aged]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老，考也。七十曰老。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七十以上曰老。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有父母耆老而无昆弟者以告。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span>。注:&ldquo;六十曰耆，七十曰老。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使帅一二耋老而绥焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;宣公十二年》</span>。注:&ldquo;六十称耋，七十称老。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老冉冉其将至兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六十以上为老男，五十以上为老女。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;海王》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷当益坚，老当益壮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:老钝(年老迟钝);老家人(老仆人);老院子(老仆人);老丹青(老画家);老行(老年男子);老姐(年老的女仆);老杜(指杜埔。与后来的杜牧相对而言);老羸(年老瘦弱);老口(年老的人;亦指牲口年龄老)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 历时长久 <span class="encs">[long-standing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百年老屋，尘泥渗漉，雨泽下注。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:老工厂，老房子;老亲(多年的或世代的姻亲关系);老晴(天晴得牢，不会很快转阴);老酒(陈年的酒);老桧(老刺柏);老牧(旧称久任地方官者);老相识(相识已久的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 娴熟，富有经验，阅历深 <span class="encs">[experienced]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:老辣(老练而有锋芒);老落(成熟，老练);老道(老练);老粉嘴(老驴。即在拉磨时老是偷吃面粉的驴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 厚 <span class="encs">[thick]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:老脸皮(老面皮。脸皮厚);老着脸(厚着脸皮);老趼(手掌或脚掌上因摩擦而生的硬皮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[great]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:老大小(偌大;老大的);老汗(大汗);老劲(很大的气力)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 排行在最后的 <span class="encs">[youngest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我那太公有个老女儿，年方二十岁，更不曾配人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:老生女儿(最后的一个女儿);老女儿(最小的女儿)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>老</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 老年;晚年 <span class="encs">[old age]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆羸老之卒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老妇出门看。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">独与老翁别。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲言国之老少。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:老生(老年之人);老况(老年的景况);老耋(老迈)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 老年人 <span class="encs">[old people;the aged]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:男女老幼;敬老院;老儿(老人家);老老(对老年男子的尊称);老先(对年长者的尊称，老先生);老老头(老头儿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 对先辈、年长者的尊称 <span class="encs">[your]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 对老人的尊称。如:老太(老太太。对老年妇女的尊称);老官(尊称年长的人);老底(父亲);老阿妈(女真语称祖父);老郎(对前辈艺人的尊称;教头;老练);老先儿(老先。老先生，对老年男子的尊称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 敬词。多不表示年岁。如:老办(老板);老先生(对年高望重者的敬称);老大人(尊称年老位尊的人);老相公(旧时对上层社会年老男子的敬称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 自称 <span class="encs">[I]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:老妾(年老妇女的谦称);老媳妇(老妇人的谦称);老爹(自称是对方父亲。詈词)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古时对某些臣僚的尊称 <span class="encs">[term of honor and respect for some feudal officials]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">指上公</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">指上卿</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">指大夫</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">指大夫的家臣</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">指群吏之尊者</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 指父母或兄长 <span class="encs">[parents or brother]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老吾老，以及人之老。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱熹</span>注:&ldquo;吾老，谓我之父兄。人之老，谓人之父兄。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:老母(年老的母亲);老子娘(指父母双亲);老亲(年老的父母)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 老子及其哲学的省称 <span class="encs">[Lao <span class="dicpy">zi</span>]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:老氏(指老子);老生(指老子);老易(老子与周易的并称);老庄(老子与庄子的并称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>老</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 死的讳称 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老于户牖之下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以备</span><span class="diczx2">京</span>中老了人口，在此停灵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:老去(死去);老家(指阴间。俗语以&ldquo;回老家&rdquo;比喻死亡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 敬爱，敬重 <span class="encs">[respect and love;honor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老吾老，以及人之老。(指第一个老。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 告老 <span class="encs">[retire from age]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余将致政焉&hellip;乃老。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">范武子</span>将老。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十七年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 变老;衰老 <span class="encs">[be old and feeble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天若有情天亦老。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《金铜仙人醉汉歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 衰老;衰颓 <span class="encs">[old and feeble;senil;decrepit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣之壮也，犹不如人;今老矣，无能为也已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:老色(衰老之色);老拙(老朽愚拙);老气(暮气);老背悔(老背晦。老胡涂);老货(骂老人的话)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>老</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用在动词前面，表示某种动作、行为或状态在一段较长时间里一直持续不断发生或时常重复出现，有&ldquo;经常&rdquo;、&ldquo;时常&rdquo;的意思;有时&ldquo;老&rdquo;和&ldquo;是&rdquo;连用，有强调的意味 <span class="encs">[always]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:小孩子，别老问这个啊;你老说这些，有啥用!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用在形容词前面，表示程度深，相当于&ldquo;很&rdquo;、&ldquo;极&rdquo; <span class="encs">[very]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:老早;老长;老咎晚(很晚);老大(极，甚。如今俗语老大不愿意、老大不甘心);老咱晚(很晚);老大无成(年岁已很大，毫无建树)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 很久 <span class="encs">[for a long time]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:老没见你啊</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>老</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈前缀〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 加在姓、名和某些称谓的前面。如:哎呀!原来是老弟;老杨，快走哇!</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 加在兄弟姐妹排行次序上。如:老二去了哪里?;老大还没回来</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 加在某些动植物名词前面。如:老虎;老鼠;老玉米</p>
<p>◎ <strong>老</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈后缀〉</p>
<p>代指人(含有轻视意)。也作&ldquo;佬&rdquo;。如:庄稼老;外国老</p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>老</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄌㄠˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>lǎo <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1792.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">年長的人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「扶老攜幼」、「敬老尊賢」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對長輩的尊稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「劉老」、「于老」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">老子及其哲學的簡稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老莊」、「佛老」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷六三&middot;老子韓非傳》：「申子之學本於黃老而主刑名，著書二篇，號曰申子。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如宋代有老佐、老麻。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">二一四部首之一。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">尊敬。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》：「老吾老以及人之老，幼吾幼以及人之幼。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">退休。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;隱公三年》：「桓公立，（石碏）​乃老。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;杜預&middot;注：「老，致仕也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《金史&middot;卷六六&middot;始祖以下諸子傳》：「勗髭鬢頓白，因上表請老。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">年紀大。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老兵」、「老人」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈吉祥寺賞牡丹〉詩：「人老簪花不自羞，花應羞上老人頭。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">熟練、經驗豐富。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老手」、「老練」、「老江湖」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">陳舊、過時。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老米」、「老式」、「老套」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">年久的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老交情」、「老主顧」、「老字號」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">原來的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老地方」、「老規矩 」、「老話題」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">加在稱呼、姓氏上，表示尊敬或親暱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老師」、「老闆」、「老李」、「老王」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">總是、常常。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老是頭痛。」、「你老是挑我的毛病。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">很、極。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老遠」、「老早」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">綴</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">加在兄弟姊妹排行的次序上。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老大」、「老二」、「老么」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">加在動物名稱上。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老鷹」、「老虎」、「老鼠」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中" target="_blank">未集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中&amp;bs=老" target="_blank">老</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8001.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20130.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠄰</span>【廣韻】盧皓切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】魯皓切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音栳。【說文】考也。七十曰老。从人毛匕，言須髮變白也。【禮&middot;曲禮】七十曰老而傳。【公羊傳&middot;宣十一年】使帥一二耋老而綏焉。【註】六十稱耋，七十稱老。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;鄭風】與子偕老。【疏】沒身不衰也。【禮&middot;祭義】貴老。爲其近于親也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;地官&middot;鄕老註】老，尊稱也。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;聘禮】授老幣。【註】老賔之臣。【疏】大夫家臣稱老。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】國君不名卿老。【註】卿老亦卿也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;王制】天子之老。【註】老謂上公。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;禮運】三老在學。【註】乞言，則受之三老。【左傳&middot;昭三年】三老凍餒。【註】三老，謂上壽、中壽、下壽，皆八十已上。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;隱三年】桓公立，乃老。【註】老，致仕也。</p>
<p>又【列子&middot;天瑞篇】老，耄也。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】宋有老佐。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶朗口切。【釋名】老，朽也。【史記&middot;律書】酉者，萬物之老也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶滿補切，姥當以此得聲。【班固&middot;西都賦】若臣者，徒觀迹於舊墟，聞之乎故老。十分未得其一端，故不能徧舉也。</p>

      <div class="yczsl-description">
			<div class="gscdl">枯藤<span>老</span>树昏鸦，小桥流水人家，古道西风瘦马。</div><div class="gscdr">元·马致远 《天净沙·秋思》</div><div class="gscdl">一<span>老</span>河兵闻之，又笑曰：“凡河中失石，当求之于上流。”</div><div class="gscdr">清·纪昀 《河中石兽》</div><div class="gscdl"><span>老</span>骥伏枥，志在千里；烈士暮年，壮心不已。</div><div class="gscdr">汉·曹操 《龟虽寿》</div><div class="gscdl">故人不独亲其亲，不独子其子，使<span>老</span>有所终，壮有所用，幼有所长，矜、寡、孤、独、废疾者皆有所养，男有分，女有归。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·礼记 《大道之行也》</div><div class="gscdl">南村群童欺我<span>老</span>无力，忍能对面为盗贼。</div><div class="gscdr">唐·杜甫 《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</div><div class="gscdl">今虽耄<span>老</span>，未有所成，犹幸预君子之列，而承天子之宠光，缀公卿之后，日侍坐备顾问，四海亦谬称其氏名，况才之过于余者乎？今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪稍之供，父母岁有裘葛之遗，无冻馁之患矣；坐大厦之下而诵《诗》《书》无奔走之劳矣；有司业、博士为之师，未有问而不告，求而不得者也；凡所宜有之书，皆集于此，不必若余之手录，假诸人而后见也。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
