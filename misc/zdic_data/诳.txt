                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诳</strong></p><p>誑、誆 <span class="dicpy">kuáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，狂声。本义:欺骗，迷惑) 同本义(隐瞒事实真相迷惑人) <span class="encs">[cheat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诳，欺也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诳，诳惑也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幼子常视无诳。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以诳刘子。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">田单</span>后与骑劫战，果设诈诳 <span class="diczx2">燕</span>军。——<span class="smcs">《史记·乐毅列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民疾其态，天子诳之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语二》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将军</span><span class="diczx2">纪信</span>乃乘王驾，诈为 <span class="diczx2">汉王</span>，诳 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>， <span class="diczx2">楚</span>皆呼万岁。——<span class="smcs">《史记·高祖本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:诳驾(因失陪而向客人说的道歉的话);诳谬(狂妄荒谬);诳上(欺骗君上、尊长);诳世(欺骗世人);诳动(欺骗煽动)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/诳诞">诳诞</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/诳惑">诳惑</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/诳语">诳语</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A91.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】居況切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】古況切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音㤮。【說文】欺也。【玉篇】惑也。【禮·曲禮】幼子常視無誑。【釋文】欺也。【史記·高祖紀】紀信乗王駕，詐爲漢王誑楚。【舊唐書·姚崇傳】遞相欺誑。【子牙子·論將篇】信而喜信人者可誑也。【註】惑也。</p><p>又【集韻】或作迋。【詩·國風】無信人之言，人實迋女。【傳】迋，與誑同。</p><p>又【韻會】亦作誆。【史記·鄭世家】晉使解揚誆楚。　【說文】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27AB4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧪴</span>。【集韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27976.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧥶</span>、逛。</p><p>
