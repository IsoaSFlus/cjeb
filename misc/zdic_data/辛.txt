                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>辛</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>辣：～辣。五～。</li><li>劳苦，艰难：～苦。～劳。</li><li>悲伤：～酸。</li><li>天干的第八位，用于作顺序第八的代称：～亥革命。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>辛</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。据甲骨文，像古代刑刀。本义:大罪)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[crime]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辛，大罪也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“辛痛泣出，罪人之象。凡辠(罪)、宰、辜、辭皆从辛者由此。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辛所以煞伤之也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">隆科多</span>]</span> 凡四十一款，当斩，妻子入辛者库，财产入官。——<span class="smcs">《清史稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 葱蒜等带刺激性的蔬菜 <span class="encs">[pungent vegetable]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以辛养筋。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·疡医》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辛甘行些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓椒姜也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">江南曰辣，中国曰辛。——<span class="smcs">《声类》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大蒜勿食，荤辛害目。——<span class="smcs">《养生要》</span></span><span class="diczx2">李善</span>注</p><p><span class="diczx1">五辛菜，乃元旦立春，以葱、蒜、韭、蓼、蒿、芥辛嫩之菜，杂和食之，取迎新之义。——<span class="smcs">《五辛菜》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 天干第八位与地支相配，用以纪年、月、日 <span class="encs">[the eigh of the ten Heavenly Stems]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其日庚辛。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“辛之言新也，因以为日名焉。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔月辛卯。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小邪·十月之交》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太岁在辛曰重光。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 商帝号 <span class="encs">[Xin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子</span><span class="diczx2">辛</span>立，是谓 <span class="diczx2">帝辛</span>，天下谓之 <span class="diczx2">纣</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:辛甲(纣臣，屡谏纣王不听，去而至周，为太史);辛癸(商纣与夏桀)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>辛</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 辣味 <span class="encs">[hot(in taste，flavor，etc.);pungent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大苦咸酸，辛甘行些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曲直作酸，从草作辛。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苦而微辛。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>、 <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:辛刻(辛辣峻刻);辛凉(辛辣清凉)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 劳苦;艰苦 <span class="encs">[hard;laborious;toil;hard work;arduous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔武以匡辛苦。——<span class="smcs">《周书》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“辛苦，穷也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">萦苦辛。——<span
