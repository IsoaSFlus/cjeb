                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>忘</strong> <span class="dicpy">wàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从心，从亡，亡亦声。本义:忘记，不记得)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[forget]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忘，不识也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">德音不忘。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·有女同车》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中年病忘。——<span class="smcs">《列子·周穆王》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“不记事也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忘路之远近。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宠辱偕忘。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忘身于外。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:忘魂(忘怀);忘昏(忘怀);忘反(流连忘归);忘年(忘记年岁);忘味(忘记美食的滋味);忘食(忘了吃饭);忘却(不记得);忘生舍死(忘却性命，不怕牺牲);忘先(忘记祖先)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 玩忽，怠忽 <span class="encs">[negllect;trifle with;idle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夙夜不敢忘怠。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《潮州祭神文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“亡”</span>。逃亡;遗失;灭亡 <span class="encs">[flee;lost;perish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乐隤心其如忘。——<span class="smcs">《文选·陆机·叹逝赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敷前人受命，兹不忘大功。——<span class="smcs">《书·大诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佩玉将将，寿考不忘。——<span class="smcs">《诗·秦风·终南》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不愆不忘，率由旧章。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·假乐》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今日不为，明日忘贷。——<span class="smcs">《管子·乘马》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣闻子胥尽忠而忘其号。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·武五子传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 舍弃 <span class="encs">[give up]</span>。如:忘私(不关心自己的得失，无私心);忘身(奋不顾身;置生死于度外);忘命(不怕死;竭尽全力);忘倦(专注于某物或被其吸引而忘却疲倦);忘劳(不感觉劳累;不知疲倦);忘想(不关心，不去想)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 无，没有 <span class="encs">[not have]</span>。如:忘翼(谓不飞去);忘机瓮(比喻没有机心)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/忘本">忘本</a></span><span class="crefe"><a                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5FD8.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】武方切【正韻】無方切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音亡。【說文】不識也。【增韻】忽也。</p><p>又遺也。【書·微子之命】予嘉乃德，曰篤不忘。謂不遺也。</p><p>又【儀禮·士冠禮】壽考不忘。【註】長有令名，不忽然而遽盡也。</p><p>又善忘，病也。【莊子·達生篇】氣下而不上，則使人善忘。</p><p>又坐忘，無思慮也。【莊子·大宗師】回坐忘。</p><p>又【廣韻】【正韻】巫放切【集韻】【韻會】無放切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音妄。【韻會】棄忘也。【增韻】遺忘也。【周禮·地官·司刺】三宥，一曰遺忘。</p><p>又志不在也。【左傳·隱七年】鄭伯盟，歃如忘。【註】志不在于歃血也。</p><p>又【韓愈·別竇司直詩】中盤進橙栗，投擲傾脯醬。歡窮悲心生，婉戀不能忘。　【說文】从心从亡。會意。</p>
