                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">根</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>根</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>高等植物茎干下部长在土里的部分：～植。～茎。～瘤。～毛。～雕。须～。块～。扎～。叶落归～。</li><li>物体的基部和其他东西连着的部分：～底。～基。墙～儿。</li><li>事物的本源：～源。～由。～本。知～知底。</li><li>彻底：～除。～究。～治。</li><li>依据，作为根本：～椐。</li><li>量词，指长条的东西：两～筷子。</li><li>数学上称一数开平方所得的值为“平方根”，开立方所得的值为“立方根”。</li><li>数学上指代数方程式内未知数的值。</li><li>化学上指带电的基：氨～。硫酸～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> root, base(d on); foundation</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Wurzel  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> racine (botanique)​,racine (math.)​,radical chimique,base,fondement,pied (d'une montagne)​,origine des choses,(classificateur des objets longs et minces : cigarettes, troncs d'arbre, cordes, ceintures, épingles, aiguilles, etc.)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">根</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>根</strong> <span class="dicpy">gēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，艮( <span class="dicpy">gèn</span>)声。本义:草木之根)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 植物生长于土中或水中吸收营养的部分 <span class="encs">[root]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">根，木株也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蔓根为根，直根为柢。——<span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">根深，则视久。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·解老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是谓深根、固柢。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有根株于下，有荣叶于上。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·超奇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其无宿根者，候苗成而未有花时采，则根生已足而又未衰。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斯亦伐根以求木茂，塞源而欲流长也。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:根荄(根。荄:草根;又指根本，根源);根茇(植物的根部);根柢(草木的根);根垓(根核。植物的根;植根)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 事物的本源，根由，依据 <span class="encs">[origin;source;cause;root]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">根，始也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">根者，书之所谓柢也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·解老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">万物有所生，而独知守其根。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盖志士仁人所为根心者也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·宋弘传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然病之生也，有根有柢。——</span><span class="diczx2">陈亮</span><span class="smcs">《与彭子寿祭酒》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">近岁市人转相摹刻诸子百家之书，……其文词学术当倍蓰于昔人，而后生科举之士，皆束书不观，游谈无根，此又何也?——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《李君山房记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:祸根(祸事的根源;引起灾祸的人或事物);根浅门微(根基浅，门第低微);根儿(根本);根气(根基。指禀赋、气质);根儿里(根本;本来);根苗(兆头，开头)</p><p><span
