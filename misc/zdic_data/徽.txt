                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">徽</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>徽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>标志，符号：国～。校～。帽～。～章。～记。～帜（<span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span>）。</li>	<li>美好的：～号。</li>	<li>〔～州〕旧地名，在今中国安徽省歙县。简称&ldquo;徽&rdquo;，如&ldquo;～墨&rdquo;。</li>	<li>系琴弦的绳，后用做抚琴标记的名称，古琴全弦共十三徽。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a badge, insignia</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Abzeichen, Rangabzeichen  (S)​,Logo  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> insigne,emblème,badge,logo,blason</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">徽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>徽</strong> <span class="dicpy">huī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，表示与线丝有关，微省声。本义:三纠绳)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[three strands of rope]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徽，三股绳也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">系用徽纆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坎》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">刘</span>注:&ldquo;三股为徽。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 绳索 <span class="encs">[rope]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">免于徽索。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span>。注:&ldquo;绳也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:徽索(徽绳。捆绑俘虏、罪人的绳索);徽纆(绳索;捆绑);徽缠(绳索。亦比喻束缚，牵累);徽绳(徽索。指捆绑罪人的绳索)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 琴徽，系琴弦的绳 <span class="encs">[cord for the string of a stringed instrument]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今夫弦者，高张急徽，追趋逐耆，则坐者不期而附矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 后亦指七弦琴琴面十三个指示音节的标识</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弦以园客之丝，徽以</span><span class="diczx2">钟山</span>之玉。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《琴赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 标志;符号 <span class="encs">[emblem;sign]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:徽车(饰有徽帜的车);徽物(指区别等级的标志物，如旌旗、服饰等);徽识(古代朝廷或军中用以识别的标志);国徽;校徽;帽徽</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 旗帜 <span class="encs">[flag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">易服色，殊徽号。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 绑腿布 <span class="encs">[bandage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徽，袤幅也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;即<span class="smcs">《诗》</span>之邪幅也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 徽州的省称 <span class="encs">[Huizhou prefecture]</span>。旧府治在今安徽省歙县。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:徽墨</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 安徽省的简称 <span class="encs">[Anhui]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:徽调</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>徽</strong> <span class="dicpy">huī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下" target="_blank">寅集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下&amp;bs=彳" target="_blank">彳</a>】
                              徽·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：14</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5FBD.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22FB0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢾰</span>【唐韻】許歸切【集韻】【韻會】吁韋切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音揮。【說文】衺幅也。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】善也。【疏】徽者，美善也。【書&middot;舜典】愼徽五典。【詩&middot;小雅】君子有徽猷。</p><p>又太姒嗣徽音。</p><p>又【正字通】琴節曰徽。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】高張急徽。【註】琴徽也，所以表發撫抑之處。【嵆康&middot;琴賦】徽以鍾山之玉。【註】以玉爲徽也。</p><p>又【說文】三糾繩也。【玉篇】徽，大索也。【易&middot;坎卦】繫用徽纆。【揚雄&middot;解嘲】折脅拉髂，免於徽索。</p><p>又徽嫿，奔馳貌。【馬融&middot;廣成頌】徽嫿霍奕，別騖分奔。</p><p>又州名。【宋史&middot;地理志】宣和三年，改歙州爲徽州。</p><p>又與幑通。幟也。【禮&middot;大傳】聖人南面而治天下，必改正朔，殊徽號。【註】徽號，旌旗之屬也。【揚雄&middot;羽獵賦】徽車輕武。【註】徽車，有徽幟之車也。</p><p>又與褘通。【張衡&middot;思<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>賦】揚雜錯之袿徽。【註】爾雅曰：婦人之徽謂之褵。郭璞云：卽今之香纓也。◎按《爾雅&middot;釋器》本作褘。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
