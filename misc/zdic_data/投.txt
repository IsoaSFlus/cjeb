                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>投</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tóu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tóu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄡˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>抛，掷，扔（多指有目标的）：～篮。～弹（<span class="dicpy">dàn </span>）。</li>    <li>跳进去：～河。自～罗网。</li>    <li>放进去，送进去：～入。～资。～标。</li>    <li>走向，进入：～宿（找地方住宿）。～奔。～靠。～降。～师（从师学习）。～诉。</li>    <li>发向：～射。～影。</li>    <li>寄，递送：～递。～稿。</li>    <li>合：～合。意气相～。</li>    <li>临，在…以前：～明。～暮（天黑以前）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">投</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>投</strong> <span class="dicpy">tóu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tóu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从手，从殳。殳( <span class="dicpy">shū</span>)，古兵器。合起来表示手拿兵器投掷。本义:投掷)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[throw]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">投，擿(掷)也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受其书而投之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">投我以桃。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·柳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋投与狗骨。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">投之深渊些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲投鼠而忌器。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·贾谊传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以肉投馁虎。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">作投击势。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">投以骨。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">复投之。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孤羊投群狼。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:投兵(向人投掷兵刃。谓诛杀);投注(投掷);投食(投掷食物);投绂(弃去印绶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 跳进去;投入(专指自杀行为) <span class="encs">[throw oneself into]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">务光</span>因自投于河。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说林上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有过于江上者，见人方引婴儿而欲投之江中。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:投河;投江;投井</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 投弃，抛弃 <span class="encs">[dump;abandon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">投诸</span><span class="diczx2">渤海</span>之尾。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平生怀仗剑，慷慨即投笔。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘希夷</span><span class="smcs">《从军行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">停杯投箸。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《行路难》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有怀投笔，慕</span><span class="diczx2">宗悫</span>之长风。—— <span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弃甲投戈。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:投簪(丢掉固定帽子的簪子。比喻弃官);投传(投弃符信);投檄(投弃征召的文书);投纶(投弃钓具);投笔(弃文而就他业);投策(弃鞭;弃杖);投畀(抛弃)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 使射影于，特指向一定方向 <span class="encs">[cast]</span>。如:树影投在窗户上;投视(投射目光);把眼光投到来访者身上</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 递送 <span
