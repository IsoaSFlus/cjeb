                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>汀</strong> <span class="dicpy">tīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，丁声。本义:水平)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[level]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汀，平也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓水之平也。水平谓之汀，因之洲渚之平谓之汀。”</span> <span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“水岸平处。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 水边平滩 <span class="encs">[waterside plat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岸芷汀兰。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:汀渚(水中小洲或水边平地);汀葭(水边的芦苇);汀喷(水涯，水滨)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 河流名。汀江 <span class="encs">[Ting River]</span>。在福建省西南部</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Ting prefecture]</span>。唐置。故州治在福建省长汀县。辖境约当今福建省汀江、九龙溪流域</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/汀线">汀线</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/汀洲">汀洲</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
