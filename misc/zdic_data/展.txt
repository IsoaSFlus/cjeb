                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">展</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>展</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形假卧的人形。从尸，与人体动作有关。本义:转动)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[turn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">展，转也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">展，转，反侧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">展转伏枕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩诗泽陂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忧心展转。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;惜贤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">展眼已是夏末秋初。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:展动(翻动);展轮(转轮。多指启程出行);展思(展转思念)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 舒展;伸展 <span class="encs">[stretch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侈必展。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">盗跖</span>大怒，两展其足，案剑瞋目。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;盗跖》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:展放(舒展);展眉舒眼(眼、眉舒展开来。形容称心如意而高兴的样子);展舒(舒展);展怀(舒怀，开怀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 张开;展现或进入视野 <span class="encs">[unfold;unfurl;open;spread out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">破笼展翅当远去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《鹧鸪词》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:展卷(展开卷状物);展目(犹放眼);展阅(展读，观览);展诵(开卷诵读);展拓(开辟，扩充)展见(展现);展陈(显示、呈现)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 延长;放宽 <span class="encs">[prolong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令冬月益展一月，卒吾事矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;王温鉥列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:展赈(指延长赈济的日期);展延(延展);展日(延期)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 扩大，扩展 <span class="encs">[expand]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:展业(谓扩充发展产业);开疆展土;那块荒地已展到七八亩</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 陈述;申述 <span class="encs">[state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子非展义不巡守。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:话已跟大家展直了;展谢(致谢，陈谢);展仪(申其礼仪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 施展，发挥 <span class="encs">[put to good use]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儿童惯识呼鸠曲，闾巷曾闻展骥才。(骥:千里马)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">罗贯中</span><span class="smcs">《全图绣像三国演义》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:展爪(施展手段);展施(施展发挥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 陈列;展览 <span class="encs">[display;exhibit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百官之属，各展其物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">展车马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敢展谢其不恭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公二十年》</span>。注:&ldquo;展，陈也。&rdquo;</span></p>
