                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>诺</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">諾</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄨㄛˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>答应的声音，表示同意：唯唯～～。</li>	<li>答应，允许：～言。许～。承～。一～千金。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> promise; assent, approve</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Versprechen  (S)​,Zusage  (S)​,versprechen, zusagen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> consentir,promettre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>诺</strong></p>
<p>諾 <span class="dicpy">nu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，若声。本义:表示)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(一般用于上对下、尊对卑或平辈之间，卑对尊用&ldquo;谨诺&rdquo; <span class="encs">[yes]</span>)</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诺，应也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，应词也。缓应曰诺，疾应曰唯。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫敢不诺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂&middot;閟宫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太后曰:&ldquo;诺。恣君之所使之。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孟尝君</span>不说，曰:&ldquo;诺，先生休矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诺诺(连声应诺);诺唯(应诺);诺许(许诺;应允)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古时批字于公文之尾，表示许可叫&ldquo;诺&rdquo;，犹今签字 <span class="encs">[put &ldquo;okay&rdquo;on a document]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是谁画诺谁传诏，一纸明贻万国羞!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄遵宪</span><span class="smcs">《述闻》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AFE.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕喏【唐韻】【正韻】奴各切【集韻】【韻會】匿各切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>囊入聲。【說文】譍也。【玉篇】答也。【正韻】應聲。【詩&middot;魯頌】莫敢不諾。【箋】應辭也。【禮&middot;玉藻】父命呼唯而不諾。【註】唯速而恭，諾緩而慢。</p><p>又【投壷】大師曰諾。【疏】承領之辭也。【吳志&middot;顧雍傳】顧悌每得父書，拜跪讀之，每句應諾。</p><p>又【韻會】以言許人曰諾。【左傳&middot;襄十八年】獻子許諾。【論語】子路無宿諾。【史記&middot;季布傳】楚諺：得黃金百斤，不如得季布一諾。【老子&middot;道德經】輕諾必寡信。【荀子&middot;王霸篇】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>賞己諾，信乎天下矣。【註】諾許也。己不許也。</p><p>又自輺語。【公羊傳&middot;僖元年】慶父曰：此奚斯之聲也，諾已。【註】諾已，皆自輺語。</p><p>又【南史&middot;江夏王鋒傳】鋒五歲，齊高帝使學鳳尾諾，一學卽工。【潘遠&middot;紀聞談】諸侯箋奏皆批曰諾，諾字有尾若風也。</p><p>又【字彙補】諾臯，神名。【酉陽雜俎】有《諾臯記》。</p><p>又水名。【前漢&middot;匈奴傳】韓昌、張猛與單于及大臣，俱登匈奴諾水東山。【師古註】今突厥地，諾眞水也。</p><p>又州名。【唐書&middot;地理志】諾州，屬靜邊郡，貞觀五年置。</p><p>又姓，見【姓苑】。</p>
