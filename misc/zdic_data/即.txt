                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>即</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>就是：知识～力量。</li>
	<li>当时或当地：～日。～刻。～席。～景。在～。～兴（<span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>就，便：黎明～起。</li>
	<li>假如，倘若：～使。～便（<span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n </span>）。～或。～令。</li>
	<li>靠近：不～不离。</li>
	<li>到，开始从事：～位。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">即</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>即</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文作坐人形(后讹为卩)面对食器(皀)会意。本义:走近去吃东西)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[come near to eat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即，就食也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">席末取粮即稻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;公食礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼎有实，我仇有疾，不我能即。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;鼎》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高亨</span>注:&ldquo;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&lsquo;即，就食也。&rsquo;此用其本义。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 基本义是接近、靠近、走向，与&ldquo;离&rdquo;对举。 <span class="encs">[approach;be near]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将即席。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">匪来贸丝，来即我谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">神即形也，形即神也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范缜</span><span class="smcs">《神灭论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜半，</span><span class="diczx2">童</span>自转，以缚即炉火烧绝之。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:若即若离</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 登上;走上 <span class="encs">[ascend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公即位。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋&middot;桓公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山行即桥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;河渠书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">庄公</span>即位，为之请制。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:即阼(即位。皇帝登极叫即阼。因古时新天子登基，有升登东阶而主祭的仪式，所以叫即阼。阼:东阶);即真(正式就皇帝位。后世以官职由代理而改为实授，也称即真);即禽(打猎，在野外猎取禽兽);即祚(皇席即位登基)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>即</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;连&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 虽然 <span class="encs">[though; although]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小即小，天生的口不曾合。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西厢记诸宫调》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假若 <span class="encs">[if]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即有不称。(如果不能称将军之职。意即打败仗。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即不幸有方二三千里之旱，国胡以相恤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 即使 <span class="encs">[even if]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">公子</span>即合符。(即，即使。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
