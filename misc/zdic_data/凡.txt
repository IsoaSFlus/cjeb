                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凡</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>凡</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">f&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>平常的，不出奇的：平～。～庸。～夫俗子。</li>	<li>指人世间（宗教或迷信的说法）：～尘。～心。</li>	<li>所有的：～年满十八岁的公民，都有选举权与被选举权。～是。</li>	<li>总共：全书～二十八卷。</li>	<li>大概，要略：大～。～例。发～（陈述全书或某一学科的要旨）。</li>	<li>中国古代乐谱的记音符号，相当于简谱&ldquo;4&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> all, any, every; ordinary, common</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gewöhnlich, weltlich, vergänglich, der materiellen Welt zugehörig, was auch immer ,Note in der chinesischen Tonleiter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凡</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形，象造器之模范形。①本义:铸造器物的模子。②引申义:凡是，表示概括)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 凡是，一切 <span class="encs">[all]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡，皆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡，数之总名也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">深察名号，凡者，独举其大事也。号凡而略，名目而详。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡有辜罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;微子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;犹皆也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡内女之有爵者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;春官序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡所应有。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡四方之士无有不过而拜且泣者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡</span><span class="diczx2">永嘉</span>山水，游历殆遍。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凡有(一切;所有);凡此(所有这些)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 总共 <span class="encs">[altogether]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">计凡付终。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;版法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡如是十许字。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪迈</span><span class="smcs">《容斋续笔》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轩凡四遭火。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡六百一十六言。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:全书凡六卷，五十章;孙中山先生致力国民革命凡四十年;凡几(共计多少)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凡</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 平常;普通 <span class="encs">[ordinary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他近日所见的这几个三等的仆妇，吃穿用度，已是不凡了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尽众人凡士。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:平凡(平常;不稀奇);凡材(普通材料);凡常(普通，一般);凡费(平常的费用);凡等(普通人);凡浅(平凡浅陋)</span></p>

      <div class="yczsl-title">初中古诗词</div>
      <div class="yczsl-description">
			<div class="gscdl">一老河兵闻之，又笑曰：“<span>凡</span>河中失石，当求之于上流。”</div><div class="gscdr">清·纪昀 《河中石兽》</div><div class="gscdl">如使人之所欲莫甚于生，则<span>凡</span>可以得生者何不用也？使人之所恶莫甚于死者，则<span>凡</span>可以辟患者何不为也？由是则生而有不用也，由是则可以辟患而有不为也。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孟子 《鱼我所欲也》</div><div class="gscdl">今虽耄老，未有所成，犹幸预君子之列，而承天子之宠光，缀公卿之后，日侍坐备顾问，四海亦谬称其氏名，况才之过于余者乎？今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪稍之供，父母岁有裘葛之遗，无冻馁之患矣；坐大厦之下而诵《诗》《书》无奔走之劳矣；有司业、博士为之师，未有问而不告，求而不得者也；<span>凡</span>所宜有之书，皆集于此，不必若余之手录，假诸人而后见也。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
