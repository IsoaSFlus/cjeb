						<p>● <strong>嚣</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">囂</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>喧哗：叫～。喧～。～～（喧哗声）。～张（放肆；跋扈）。～谤（为众人所谤议）。～风。～然。～尘。甚～尘上。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> be noisy; treat with contempt</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> toben, Geschrei  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> crier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">嚣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>嚣</strong></p>
<p>嚻 <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从㗊，从页。㗊( <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span>)，众口。页，人头。表示众口喧嚣。本义:喧哗，声音响而闹哄)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[noisy;loud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嚣，嚣声也。气出头上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禁其门嚣者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司武虎》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在陈而嚣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">湫隘嚣尘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而以金石匏竹之昌大嚣庶为乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">选徒嚣嚣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;车攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甚嚣，且尘上矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叫嚣乎东西，隳突乎南北。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嚣争(吵闹;争夺);嚣湫(尘嚣渊隘);嚣鄙(喧闹鄙俗);嚣号(喧嚷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 害臊;害羞 <span class="encs">[feel ashamed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">店主起来作揖，说与</span><span class="diczx2">谭绍闻</span>道喜， <span class="diczx2">绍闻</span>嚣的耳朵稍都是红的。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《歧路灯》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>嚣</strong></p>
<p>嚻 <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 轻浮;轻薄 <span class="encs">[blatant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形色嚣然，自矜得遇滋甚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嚣幸(轻怠);嚣了头(薄了面子;搁不住脸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 饥饿 <span class="encs">[hungry]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">囂</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“嚣”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/囂">囂</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】
                              囂·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=21" target="_blank">21</a>　·部外筆画：18</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/56C2.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20F78.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠽸</span>【唐韻】許嬌切【集韻】【韻會】虛嬌切【正韻】吁嬌切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音枵。【說文】聲也，气出頭上。从㗊从頁。頁，首也。【玉篇】喧譁也。【集韻】聲也。【左傳&middot;昭三年】湫隘囂塵。【釋文】囂，許嬌反，一音五高反。【詩&middot;小雅】選徒囂囂。【傳】囂囂，聲也。【釋文】囂，五刀反，亦許驕反。</p>
<p>又【孟子】人知之亦囂囂，人不知亦囂囂。【註】囂囂，自得無欲之貌。</p>
<p>又人名。【史記&middot;黃帝紀】嫘祖生二子，其一曰<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>囂，是爲靑陽。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>牛刀切，音敖。義同。【周禮&middot;秋官】銜枚氏掌司囂。【註】察囂譁者。【釋文】囂，五羔反。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】讒口囂囂。【箋】囂囂，衆多貌。【釋文】五刀反，韓詩作嗸嗸。【前漢&middot;董仲舒傳】此民之所以囂囂苦不足也。【註】師古曰：與嗸同。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】我卽爾謀，聽我囂囂。【傳】囂囂猶嗸嗸也。【釋文】囂，五刀反。</p>
<p>又【字彙】山凹之地曰囂。【梁宣帝七山寺賦】神囂嵒嵒而特立。</p>
<p>又鳥名。【山海經】梁渠之山有鳥，狀如夸父，四翼一目犬尾，名曰囂。</p>
<p>又獸名。【山海經】羭次之山有獸，狀如禺，長臂善投，名曰囂。</p>
<p>又水名。【山海經】嶓冢之山，囂水出焉。</p>
<p>又山名。【山海經】北囂之山無石，其陽多碧，其隂多玉。</p>
<p>又【集韻】地名。通作隞敖。詳前嚻字註。○按《經傳》《釋文》囂多讀敖，惟《小雅》車攻及《左傳&middot;昭三年》《釋文》兼敖枵二音。</p>
