                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>失</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˉ</span></p><ol><li>丢：遗～。坐～良机。收复～地。流离～所。</li><li>违背：～约。～信。</li><li>找不着：迷～方向。</li><li>没有掌握住：～言。～职。～调（<span class="dicpy">tiáo </span>）。</li><li>没有达到：～望。～意。</li><li>错误：～误。～策。过～。～之毫厘，谬以千里。</li><li>改变常态：惊慌～色。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">失</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>失</strong> <span class="dicpy">shī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，乙声。表示从手中丢失。小篆字形。本义:失掉;丢失)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">失，纵也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“在手而逸去为失。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">失得勿恤。——<span class="smcs">《易·晋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故人情不失。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹去也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子不失足于人。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·表记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“失其容止之节也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌国中失之事。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·师氏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“失，失礼者也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">塞翁失马，今未足悲。——</span><span class="diczx2">魏泰</span><span class="smcs">《东轩笔录》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">失道者寡助。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">累官故不失州郡也。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此之谓失其本心。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:失状(丢失财物的清单);失脱(丢失;失掉);失国(丧失国家);失鉴(失去检点);失瞻(失敬);失翁马(比喻暂时遭受损失，却因此得到好处);失民(丧失民心);失本(失去根本);失正(失其正道);失名(丧失名节);失合(失去配偶);失偶(丧失配偶);失鹿(失去天下);失道(失去准则;违背道义);失众(失去民心;失去众心);失柄(丧失统治权力)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 错过 <span class="encs">[miss]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸡、豚、狗、彘之畜，无失其时，七十者可以食肉矣。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奉不可失，敌不可纵。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:失眼(没有看准;看错了);失人(错过人才;错用人才);失候(错过适当的时刻)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 不自禁，忍不住 <span class="encs">[be out of control]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">庄公</span>失言。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·庄公二十五年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丞相何故失惊?——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:失喜(喜欢到极点，不能自禁);失手;失笑;失足;失惊(吃惊)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 违背 <span class="encs">[violate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">论功计劳，未尝失法律。——</span><span class="diczx2">章炳麟</span><span class="smcs">《原法》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:失礼;失约;失命(违误命令)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 遗漏 <span class="encs">[leave out by mistake]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">细过不失。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·开塞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:失恩(不顾恩义);失脱(疏忽失误)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 迷失 <span
