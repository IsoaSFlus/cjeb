                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">临</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>临</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">臨</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>从上向下看，在高处朝向低处：照～。～渊羡鱼（看着深潭里的鱼，很希望得到；喻只作空想，不做实际工作）。</li>
	<li>到，来：光～。莅～。亲～。</li>
	<li>遭遇，碰到：～时。面～。</li>
	<li>挨着，靠近：～近。～街。～终。～危。～阵磨枪。</li>
	<li>照样子摹仿字画：～摹。～帖。～写。</li>
	<li>旧时指帝王上朝：～朝。～政。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> draw near, approach; descend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> neben, nahe  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> approcher de,être près de,être sur le point de,donner sur,se présenter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">临</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>临</strong></p>
<p>臨 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，右边是人，左上角象人的眼睛，左下角象众多的器物。整个字形象人俯视器物的样子。本义:从高处往低处察看)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[look down from above]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临，监临也。从卧，品声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，隐几视下之称。》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临，视也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不临深谿，不知地之厚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上帝临女。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;大明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有亭翼然临于泉上。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:居高临下;如临深渊;临轩(在殿前平台上接见臣属);临下(从高望下);临见(居上视下看见)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为从上监视着 <span class="encs">[keep watch on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临长</span><span class="diczx2">晋国</span>者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;监也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今陛下君临四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史&middot;赵普传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:临引(居上察下。今称监察);临制(监临控制;指皇后临朝称制);临政(亲理政务)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 面对(上对下，尊对卑) <span class="encs">[face;confront]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临众发政而治民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚贤下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">把酒临风。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临表涕零。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">执策而临之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:临川(面对川流);临穴(面对自己的圹穴。亦指面对(别人的)墓穴);临敌(面对敌人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 碰上， 逢着 <span class="encs">[meet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临财无苟得，临难毋苟免。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:临头(落到头上;当头);临风(迎风;当风);临谷(身临深谷。喻危险恐惧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 到，至。后常用作敬辞<span class="encs">[attend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临其穴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;黄鸟》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/81E8.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/268F7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦣷</span>【唐韻】力尋切【集韻】【韻會】犂針切【正韻】犂沉切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音林。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】臨，視也。【詩&middot;衞風】日居月諸，照臨下土。</p>
<p>又【大雅】上帝臨女，無貳爾心。【箋】臨，視也。【禮&middot;曲禮】臨諸侯，畛於鬼神。【疏】以尊適<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>曰臨。【穀梁傳&middot;哀七年】春秋有臨天下之言焉，有臨一國之言焉，有臨一家之言焉。【註】徐乾曰：臨者，撫有之也。</p>
<p>又【博雅】臨，大也。</p>
<p>又【戰國策】縣隂以甘之，循有燕以臨之。【註】臨，猶制也。</p>
<p>又易卦名。</p>
<p>又車名。【詩&middot;大雅】以爾鉤援，與爾臨衝，以伐崇墉。【傳】臨，臨車也。【疏】臨者，在上臨下之名。【釋名】臨，如字。韓詩作隆。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;哀四年】荀寅奔鮮虞，趙稷奔臨。【註】臨，晉邑。</p>
<p>又丘名。【爾雅&middot;釋丘】右高名臨丘。</p>
<p>又姓。【後趙錄】秦州刺史臨深。【孔融傳】有臨孝存。</p>
<p>又門名。【左思&middot;吳都賦】左稱彎崎，右號臨硎。【註】彎崎，臨硎，閽闥名。吳後主起昭明宮，於太初之東開彎崎臨硎二門，彎崎宮東門，臨硎宮西門。</p>
<p>又【韻會】力鴆切【正韻】力禁切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>林去聲。【增韻】喪哭。【顏師古曰】衆哭曰臨。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】楚子圍鄭，旬有七日。鄭人卜行成，不吉。卜臨于大官，且巷出車，吉。【註】臨，哭也。【釋文】臨，力鴆切。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶盧東切，音隆。【詩&middot;大雅】后稷不克，上帝不臨。耗斁下土，寧丁我躬。【司馬相如&middot;長門賦】奉虛言而望誠兮，期城南之離宮。修薄具而自設兮，君不肯乎幸臨。</p>
<p>又叶力陽切，音良。【秦始皇祠洛水歌】洛陽之水，其色蒼蒼。祠祭大澤，倏忽南臨。洛濱醊禱，色連三光。</p>
