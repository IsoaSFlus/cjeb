                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冈</strong></p>
<p>岡 <span class="dicpy">gāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>(形声。从山，网声。本义:山脊，山岭) 同本义 <span class="encs">[ridge(of hill)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岡，山脊也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗亦误作崗。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">览高冈兮峣峣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;守志》</span>。注:&ldquo;山岭曰冈。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山脊曰冈。冈，亢也，在上之言也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陟彼高冈，我马玄黄(病)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;卷耳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:山冈;冈陀(山冈);冈子(低矮而坡度较小的山丘);冈岑(小山峦);冈崖(山岩);冈岭(山岭)</span></p>

<p>（網）【廣韻】文兩切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】文紡切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音罔。【說文】本作网。或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2627D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦉽</span>，隷省作㒺。今文从糸作綱。【易&middot;繫辭】作結繩而爲㒺罟，以佃以漁。【朱子&middot;本義】㒺，與綱同。【書&middot;盤庚】若綱在綱。【詩&middot;邶風】魚綱之設。</p>
<p>又【楚辭&middot;招魂】綱戸朱綴。【註】綱戸，綺文縷也。</p>
