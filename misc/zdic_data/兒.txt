                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>兒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&eacute;r  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;r.mp3"></a></span>  ㄦˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>小孩子：嬰～。～戲。</li>
	<li>年輕的人（多指青年男子）：男～。～女情。</li>
	<li>兒子，男孩子：～子。生～育女。</li>
	<li>父母對兒女的統稱，兒女對父母的自稱。</li>
	<li>助詞：❶多用作名詞後綴。❷用於形容詞後。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>兒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ㄋ〡ˊ.mp3"></a></span>  n&iacute;</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>老人牙齒落盡後更生的細齒。後作&ldquo;齯&rdquo;。《集韻&bull;齊韻》：&ldquo;齯，通作兒。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>弱小。後作&ldquo;倪&rdquo;、&ldquo;婗&rdquo;。《古今韻會舉要&bull;齊韻》：&ldquo;兒，弱小也。通作倪。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;郳&rdquo;。《集韻&bull;齊韻》：&ldquo;郳，或作兒。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。《廣韻&bull;齊韻》：&ldquo;兒，姓也。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兒</strong></p>
<p>兒 <span class="dicpy">&eacute;r</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;r.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，下面是&ldquo;人&rdquo;字，上面象小兒張口哭笑。&ldquo;兒&rdquo;是漢字部首之一，从&ldquo;兒&rdquo;的字大都與&ldquo;人&rdquo;有關。本義:幼兒。古時男稱兒，女稱嬰，後來孩童都稱兒)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本義 <span class="encs">[child， pl. children]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兒，孺子也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《說文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">專氣致柔，能如嬰兒乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>第九章</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老人兒啼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;循吏列傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男曰兒，女曰嬰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《蒼頡篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">見兩小兒辯鬥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;湯問》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兒女子(孩童);兒劇(猶兒戲);兒話(孩子話);兒撬(柺棍兒);兒拜(孩童對大人所行的禮拜);兒啼(小孩哭);兒孩(孩童)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 兒女，父母所生的子女，第一代後裔 <span class="encs">[child]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兒孫滿堂;小兒;兒女夫妻(結髮夫妻，從小的夫妻);兒女之情(指青年男女之間相愛的情感)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 長輩對幼輩的稱呼。亦用作輕蔑之稱 <span class="encs">[child]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兒郎(將帥對士兵的稱呼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 父母對兒子的稱呼 <span class="encs">[son]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兒，子也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《廣雅&middot;釋親》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">湯</span>爲兒守舍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《漢書&middot;張湯傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外黃令舍人兒年十三。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《漢書&middot;項藉傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大兒鋤豆溪東，中兒正織雞籠。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛棄疾</span><span class="smcs">《清平樂&middot;村居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阿爺無大兒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《樂府詩集&middot;木蘭詩》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兒媳(即兒媳婦);兒子花(兒子的影子);兒曹(兒輩);兒息(子息)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 多用作名詞後綴 <span class="cino">(1)</span>∶表示小。如:盆兒;棍兒;小貓兒;小車兒 <span class="cino">(2)</span>∶表示詞性變化。動詞名詞化，如:唱兒;逗笑兒。形容詞名詞化，如:亮兒;零碎兒 <span class="cino">(3)</span>∶表示具體事物抽象化。如:口兒;門兒;根兒 <span class="cino">(4)</span>∶表示詞義變化。如:白麪兒(海洛因);老家兒(對人稱自己或別人的父母)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 動詞的後綴。如:玩兒;火兒</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形容詞後綴。如邵雍<span class="smcs">《首尾吟》</span>:&ldquo;天聽雖高只些子，人情想去沒多兒。&rdquo;</p>
