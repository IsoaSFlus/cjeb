                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>匡</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kuāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄨㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>纠正；～正。～谬。</li><li>救：～救。～复。～时济世。</li><li>辅助，帮助：～助。～扶。～我不逮（帮助我所做不到的）。</li><li>粗略计算，估计，预料：～算。～计。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">筐</span>”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">匡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>匡</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从匚( <span class="dicpy">fāng</span>)，表示与筐器有关，王声。本义:盛东西的方形竹器)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="yhcs">“筐”</span>的古字。容器名 <span class="encs">[basket]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匡，饭器，也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既齐既稷，既匡既勑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·楚茨》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蚕则绩而蟹有匡。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="yhcs">“框”</span>的古字。边框;围子;框框 <span class="encs">[frame]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行人夜宿墙匡月。——</span><span class="diczx2">韦庄</span><span class="smcs">《秦妇吟》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:匡子(框子)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> <span class="yhcs">“眶”</span>的古字。眼眶 <span class="encs">[orbit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刺匡上陷骨中脉，为漏为盲。——<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 春秋时齐国简册名 <span class="encs">[bamboo slips]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《管子》</span>有<span class="smcs">《大匡》</span>、<span class="smcs">《中匡》</span>、<span class="smcs">《小匡》</span>三篇</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 螃蟹的背壳 <span class="encs">[shell]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">海岛有蟹，其匡宛具一人面。——<span class="smcs">《拜经楼诗话》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Kuang town]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 春秋时卫地，在今河南省长垣县西南</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 春秋时郑地。在今河南省扶沟县</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 山名，即江西省的庐山 <span class="encs">[Lushan mountain]</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>匡</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 纠正，匡正 <span class="encs">[correct]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">善则赏之，过则匡之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一匡天下。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今</span><span class="diczx2">范中行</span>氏之臣，不能匡相其君。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语九》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:匡言(匡正的言论);匡弼(匡正辅助);匡救(匡正挽救);匡翼(匡正辅助);匡邪(纠正邪恶)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拯救 <span class="encs">[save]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匡乏困，救灾患。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:匡危(拯救危难);匡立(拯救国家，建立大业);匡攘(拯救国家，驱逐敌人);匡困(救助贫困);匡时(挽救艰危的时局);匡难(匡救国家的灾难)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 帮助;辅助，辅佐 <span
