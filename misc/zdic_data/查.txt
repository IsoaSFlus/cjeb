                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">查</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>查</strong></p>
<p>査 <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，且声。本义:木筏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。同&ldquo;楂&rdquo; <span class="encs">[raft]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有巨查浮于西海。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王嘉</span><span class="smcs">《拾遗记&middot;唐尧》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:查影(筏影，船影);查头(船头)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 树桩 <span class="encs">[stump]</span>。如:查儿(短而硬的胡子或头发)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 树杈 <span class="encs">[crotch of a tree]</span>。如:查卉(树木砍伐后的再生枝)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>查</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 审查，考查;检查 <span class="encs">[check;examine;inspect]</span>。如:查票;查勘(访查勘验)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 调查 <span class="encs">[look into(a matter); investigate]</span>。如:查看(实地调查);查视(查看)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 在参考书中寻找 <span class="encs">[consult;look up]</span>。如:查字典;查书</p>
