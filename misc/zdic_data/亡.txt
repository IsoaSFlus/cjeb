                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>亡</strong></p><p>亾 <span class="dicpy">wáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字从入，从乚。<span class="yhcs">“入”</span>是人字。乚( <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span>)隐蔽。合起来表示人到隐蔽处。本义:逃离，出走)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[flee;run away]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亡，逃也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋公子</span>生十七年而亡。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语四》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子牟</span>有罪而亡。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怀其璧，从径道亡。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今亡亦死，举大计亦死。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或以为亡。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">数言欲亡。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亡而入胡。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·人间训》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奴惊，各亡去。——</span><span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是时</span><span class="diczx2">桓楚</span>亡在泽中。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:亡归(逃回);逃亡(逃走在外);亡虏(逃亡的犯人);亡民(逃亡在外的百姓);亡子(亡人。逃亡的人);亡奔(逃奔);亡臣(逃亡之臣);亡逸(逃亡);亡匿(逃跑并躲藏起来)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 死 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">存亡不可知。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">刘表</span>新亡，二子不协。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身在身亡。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长者先亡。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:亡人(死人。骂人的话);阵亡(在作战中牺牲);亡醮(为死者设坛祭祷);亡友(死去的友人);亡死(死亡);亡殁(死亡);亡化(死亡;去世)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 丢失;丧失 <span
