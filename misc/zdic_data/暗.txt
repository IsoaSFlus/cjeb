                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">暗</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>暗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">àn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/àn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>不亮，没有光，与“<span class="diczx3">明</span>”相对：黑～。～淡（ａ．不光明；ｂ．喻景象悲惨）。阴～。～无天日。</li><li>不公开的，隐藏不露的：～藏。～号。～杀。～娼。～沟。～含（做事、说话包含某种意思而未明白说出）。～流（ａ．流动的地下水；ｂ．喻潜伏的思想倾向或社会动态）。明察～访。</li><li>愚昧，糊涂：～昧。～弱（愚昧软弱）。兼听则明，偏信则～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">暗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>暗</strong> <span class="dicpy">àn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/àn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，音声。本义:昏暗)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。与<span class="yhcs">“明”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[dim;dark;dull]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暗，日无光也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日中光明，故其出入时光暗，故大。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·说日》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">体之感何自起?曰，起于远近之比例，明暗之掩映。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:昏暗(光线不足);暗中摸索(在黑暗中寻找探索)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 昏昧，愚昧;不明白 <span class="encs">[dull;stupid;unclear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上暗则政险。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·天率》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:暗昧(真假不明;暧昧，含糊不清);暗主(昏庸的君主)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 隐秘 <span class="encs">[hidden;secret]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">别有幽愁暗恨生，此时无声胜有声。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:暗记儿;暗号</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 地下 <span class="encs">[underground]</span>。如:暗河;暗沟(地下的排水沟)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>暗</strong> <span class="dicpy">àn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/àn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 默默地<span class="encs">[do not say a word;keep silent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暗算乡程隔数州，欲归无计泪空流。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜荀鹤</span><span class="smcs">《旅寓诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孺人半夜觉寝，促</span><span class="diczx2">有光</span>暗诵。—— <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《先妣事略》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:暗诵(默诵，背诵);暗数(默默记数);暗写(默写)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 秘密;暗中 <span class="encs">[in secret]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曹节</span><span class="diczx2">王甫</span>，暗杀太后。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·宦者传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孤犬无猛噬，长箭不暗射。——</span><span class="diczx2">元好问</span><span class="smcs">《驱猪行》</span></p><p>◎ <strong>暗</strong> <span class="dicpy">àn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
