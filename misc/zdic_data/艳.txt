                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>艳</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">艷</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>色彩鲜明：～丽。～冶。鲜～。娇～。百花争～。</li>
	<li>羡慕：～羡。</li>
	<li>旧时指关于爱情方面的：～事。～诗。～史。</li>
	<li>美丽，亦指美女：&ldquo;吴娃与越～，窈窕夸铅红。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> beautiful, sexy, voluptuous</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> zauberhaft</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">艳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>艳</strong></p>
<p>艷、豓、豔 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从豐( <span class="dicpy">fēng</span>)，从色。豐，丰大。色，色彩。丰大而有色彩。本义:鲜艳，艳丽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[colorful;gandy;be bright-colored and beautiful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艷，好而长也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">目逆而送之，曰，美而艳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艳妻煽方处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艳陆离些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span>。注:&ldquo;艳，好貌也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窈窕艳城郭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:艳色(常用以指美女、美色);艳段(宋元杂剧中，在正剧之前表演的一段短剧);艳质(美丽的姿质)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 文辞华丽 <span class="encs">[flowery]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信言不艳，贯居于好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:艳才(写作美妙文章的才能);艳采(绮丽的文采)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 过于艳丽的色彩，极为俗气花俏的服饰 <span class="encs">[gaudy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那日，果然来了几个女人，都是艳装丽服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:这布的花色太艳了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 光彩;光泽和颜色 <span class="encs">[bright]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:艳发(鲜明焕发);艳阳(形容光艳美丽);艳晶晶(光彩闪耀的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 浓 <span class="encs">[strong]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:艳饰(浓妆打扮)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>艳</strong></p>
<p>艷 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 美女 <span class="encs">[beauty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">吴</span>娃与 <span class="diczx2">越</span>艳，窈窕夸铅红。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《李太白诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:艳波(美女的眼波);艳姝(美女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 有关男女爱情方面的事情或著作 <span class="encs">[amorous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当她年轻的时候，他们还共同制造过一些动人的艳闻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">沙汀</span><span class="smcs">《淘金记》</span></p>
