                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>愈</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>更，越：～甚。～加。～发。～益。～演～烈。</li>
	<li>较好，胜过：孰～（哪个好）？彼～于此。</li>
	<li>病好了：痊～。病～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> more and more, even more</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> <a href="./愈">愈</a><a href="./多">多</a><a href="./愈">愈</a><a href="./好">好</a> je mehr, desto besser ,bessern, besser werden  (V)​,entdecken, wiederherstellen  (V)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">愈</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>愈</strong></p>
<p>瘉、癒 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从心，俞声。本义:病好了。这个意义后来又写作&ldquo;瘉&rdquo;、&ldquo;癒&rdquo;) 同本义 <span class="encs">[recover]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瘉，病瘳也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸上病瘉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;卢绾传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至日中间差瘉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李寻传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周王</span>病瘉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;周策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武</span>益愈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因天气和暖，</span><span class="diczx2">黛玉</span>之疾渐愈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兼旬愈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:愈合(伤口长好)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>愈</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>胜过 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一暴之功，犹愈于十日之寒也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《与潘次耕札》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>愈</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 更加;越发 <span class="encs">[more]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">政事愈蹙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愈束缚牢甚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻而愈悲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雪愈甚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入之愈深。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:我愈觉得这些孩子可爱了;家乡离我愈近了;愈更(更加);愈扇(更加发扬);愈愈(益甚);愈出愈奇(越来越奇异)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 连用，与&ldquo;越&hellip;越&hellip;&rdquo;义同 <span class="encs">[the more&hellip;the more&hellip;]</span>。</p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集上" target="_blank">卯集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集上&amp;bs=心" target="_blank">心</a>】
                              愈·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6108.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】以主切【集韻】【韻會】勇主切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音庾。【玉篇】勝也。【廣韻】賢也。【增韻】過也。【孟子】丹之治水也，愈於禹。</p>
<p>又進也，益也。【詩&middot;小雅】憂心愈愈。【蘇氏曰】愈愈，益甚之意。</p>
<p>又差也。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】相從爲愈。【註】愈，差也。【正義】病差謂之愈。</p>
<p>又【正韻】偶許切。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】容朱切【韻會】羊朱切【正韻】雲俱切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音于。【老子&middot;道德經】動而愈出。【音義】羊主反，又羊朱反。</p>
<p>又通作兪。【吳語】越閒兪章。【荀子&middot;仲尼篇】兪務而俞遠。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>讀作愈。【前漢&middot;禮志】兪甚亡益。　亦通作瘉。【晉語】東方之士孰爲瘉。【註】賢也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;藝文志】不猶瘉於野乎。</p>
<p>又與愉通。【荀子&middot;正論篇】天子者，勢至重而形至怢，心至愈而志無所詘。【註】愈，讀爲愉。◎按廣韻，集韻，韻會，上聲俱切喩母，而正韻獨切疑母，蓋北音以疑爲喩，故又以喩爲疑也。然於平聲又切喩母，則其不安於疑母可知矣。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6109_2015.svg" /><span class="swbf">(愉)</span><span class="ywyy">薄也。</span><span class="swdz">薄本訓林薄、蠶薄，而叚爲淺泊字。泊水部作洦。凡言厚薄皆<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/231AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣆪</span>泊之叚借字。此薄也當作薄樂也，轉寫奪樂字。謂淺薄之樂也。引申之，凡薄皆云愉。唐風：他人是愉。傳曰：愉，樂也。禮記曰：有和氣者，必有愉色。此愉之本義也。毛不言薄者，重樂不重薄也。鹿鳴：視民不恌。傳曰：恌，愉也。許書人部作佻，愉也。周禮：以俗教安，則民不愉。鄭注：愉謂朝不謀夕。此引申之義也。淺人分别之，别製偷字，从人，訓爲偷薄，訓爲苟且，訓爲偷盜，絕非古字，許書所無。然自山有樞鄭箋云愉讀曰偷。偷，取也。則不可謂其字不古矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从心俞聲。</span><span class="swdz">羊朱切。古音在四部。</span><span class="ywyy">論語曰：私覿愉愉如也。</span><span class="swdz">鄉黨篇文。覿者，儥之俗字。愉愉，聘禮作俞俞。論語鄭注云：愉愉，容色和也。正薄樂之義。</span></p>                          </div>
