                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>疫</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chuáng</span>)，役省声。本义:瘟役)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。急性传染病流行的通称 <span class="encs">[epidemic disease]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疫，民皆疾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疫，病流行也。——<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">氐为天根生疫。——<span class="smcs">《史记·天官书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鬻棺者欲岁之疫。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·刑法志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">果实早成，民殃于疫。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:时疫;疫死(因染患流行的传染病而死);疫旱(传染病与旱灾);疫症(疫病);疫疠(瘟疫);疫疾(疫病;疫病流行)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 疫鬼，古代迷信称施瘟疫的鬼 <span class="encs">[demon of playue]</span>。如:疫神(疫鬼);疫鬼(散布瘟疫的鬼神)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疫病">疫病</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疫疠">疫疠</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疫苗">疫苗</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疫情">疫情</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
