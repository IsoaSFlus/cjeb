                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">壹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>壹</strong></p><p>弌 <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形。从壶，吉声。<span class="yhcs">“一”</span>的大写。本义:专一)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[single-minded]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壹，专一也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子壹教，凝子壹学，亟成。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·大略》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与人之壹也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公三年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“壹，无贰心。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若琴瑟之专壹，谁能听之?——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭二十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:壹心(同心;专心);壹德(谓一心一意);壹意(专心致志);壹定(一贯不变，固定不变)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 统一;一致 <span class="encs">[united;identical]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">圣人之为国也，壹赏，壹刑，壹教。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·赏刑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:壹同(统一);壹统(一统);壹意(统一意志);壹体(一致，如同一个整体);壹气(元气，统一不杂之气)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 皆;一概;一律 <span class="encs">[all;one and all]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自天子以至于庶人，壹是皆一修身为本。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·大学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:壹是(一概，一律);壹概(一概，犹言齐一);壹切(一切;全部);壹匡(一匡。使一切得到匡正)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 一样;等同;同一 <span class="encs">[same;equal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">镇静者修之，则壹。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语七》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:壹齐(齐一，划一);壹体(如同一个整体)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>壹</strong> <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
