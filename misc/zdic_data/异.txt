                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>异</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg"><span class="diczx3">異</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>不同的：～乎。～说。～常。～己（与自己意见不同或利害相冲突的人）。～端（旧时指不符合正统思想的主张或教义，如&ldquo;～～邪说&rdquo;）。～化。～性。～样。大同小～。～曲同工。</li>
	<li>分开：离～。～居。</li>
	<li>另外的，别的：～日。～地。～国。～乡。～类。</li>
	<li>特别的：奇～。～闻。～彩。奇才～能（特殊的才能）。</li>
	<li>奇怪：惊～。诧～。怪～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> different, unusual, strange</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Verschiedenheit, Differenz (S)​,ungewöhnlich, außergewöhnlich, fremd, fremdartig (Adj)​,ungleich, verschieden, anders (Adj)​,iso, iso- (Eig, Chem)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> différent,étrange,exceptionnel</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">异</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>异</strong></p>
<p>異 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。&ldquo;異&rdquo;，甲骨文字形，象个有手、脚、头的人形。从廾( <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span>)从畀( <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span>)。畀，予也。本义:奇特;奇异;奇怪)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[strange;queer;odd]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">异，怪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">异，奇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何以异哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;杨朱》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">异乎交益之士。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妇人异甚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">产异蛇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觉无异能。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父异焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《伤仲永》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:异疾(奇怪的病症);异形(怪异或奇特的形状)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 不同 <span class="encs">[different]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">礼者为异。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓别贵贱也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事为异别。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">执心各异。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得无异乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或异二者。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何异以孤羊投群狼?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:异俗(风俗异于汉族的少数民族);异姓陌路(既不同姓也不认识);异手(指不同之人);异世(不同时代);异目(另眼)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>异</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p>其他，别的 <span class="encs">[other]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贾不敢为异事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;上农》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 区别;分开 <span class="encs">[distinguish;divide]</span></p>
