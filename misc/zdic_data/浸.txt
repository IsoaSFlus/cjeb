                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">浸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>浸</strong> <span class="dicpy">Jìn</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，籀文寝声。本义:古水名。浸水) 同本义 <span class="encs">[Jin River]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浸，</span><span class="diczx2">浸水</span>也。出 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>郡 <span class="diczx2">武安</span>东北，入 <span class="diczx2">呼沱</span>水。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>按，出今 <span class="diczx2">河南</span><span class="diczx2">彰德府</span><span class="diczx2">武安县</span>，入 <span class="diczx2">虖沱</span>河。</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:巨浸(大湖)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>浸</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 泡在水里，被水渗入 <span class="encs">[immerse;steep;soak]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冽彼下泉，浸彼苞稂。——<span class="smcs">《诗·曹风·下泉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其浸五湖。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·职方氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浸渊之草。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·木味》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:浸淬(把金属工件烧红，然后浸入水中急速冷却，以增加其硬度或改变其物理、化学性能);浸蚀(因液体浸泡而腐蚀或损伤);浸濡(因受水渍而湿透)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 灌溉 <span class="encs">[irrigate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一日浸百畦。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天地》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:浸溉(灌溉);浸水(用水灌溉，泡水，置于水中)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 渗入;渗透 <span class="encs">[permeate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浸润之谮。——<span class="smcs">《论语·颜渊》</span>。皇疏:<span class="yhcs">“浸渊，犹渐渍也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上漏下湿，润浸北房。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span
