                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蕉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˉ</span></p><ol><li>指某些有像芭蕉那样的大叶子的植物：香～。美人～。</li><li>生麻，未沤治的麻。</li><li>〔芭～〕见“<span class="diczx3">芭</span>”。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">焦</span>”，枯焦。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蕉</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，焦声。本义:生麻。麻未沤称蕉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hemp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">覆之以蕉。——<span class="smcs">《列子·周穆王》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蕉布(蕉麻纤维织成的布);蕉衣(麻布制的衣服);蕉衫(用麻布缝制的衣衫)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指蕉布 <span class="encs">[hemp cloth]</span>。如:蕉纱(蕉布);蕉葛(蕉布)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 植物名。芭蕉、香蕉、美人蕉等芭蕉科植物的简称 <span class="encs">[banana]</span>。如:蕉叶(芭蕉叶);蕉园(芭蕉园);蕉扇(用芭蕉叶制成的扇子);蕉书(用芭蕉叶代纸作书)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">qiáo</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蕉农">蕉农</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蕉</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiáo</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“樵”</span>。柴 <span class="encs">[firewood]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">覆之以蕉。——<span class="smcs">《列子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">殷敬顺</span>释文:<span class="yhcs">“蕉，与樵同。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蕉萃">蕉萃</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
