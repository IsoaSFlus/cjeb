						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>大</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄚˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>指面积、体积、容量、数量、强度、力量超过一般或超过所比较的对象，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">小</span>&rdquo;相对：～厅。～政。～气候。夜郎自～。～腹便便。</li>
	<li>指大小的对比：这间房有那间两个～。</li>
	<li>规模广，程度深，性质重要：～局。～众。</li>
	<li>用于&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">不</span>&rdquo;后，表示程度浅或次数少：不～高兴。</li>
	<li>年长，排行第一：老～。</li>
	<li>敬辞：～作。～名。～手笔。</li>
	<li>时间更远：～前年。</li>
	<li>〔～夫〕古代官职，位于&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">卿</span>&rdquo;之下，&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">士</span>&rdquo;之上。</li>
	<li>超过事物一半，不很详细，不很准确：～概。～凡。</li>
</ol>

<p>◎ <strong>大</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象人的正面形，有手有脚。&ldquo;大&rdquo;是汉字部首之一，从&ldquo;大&rdquo;的字往往与人类或人事有关。本义:大小的&ldquo;大&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 形容体积、面积、数量、力量、规模、程度等方面超过一般或超过所比较的对象。与&ldquo;小&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[big;large;great]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大，天大地大人亦大，故大象人形。古文大也，籀文介，改古文亦象人形。凡大人、大夫、太子、太君，皆尊词。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遵彼大路兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;遵大路》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大有。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易》</span>。释文:&ldquo;大有，包容丰富之象。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大报天而主日也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特牲》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其朝践用两大尊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司尊彝》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:&ldquo;大，古之瓦尊。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而祭大山之邑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;桓公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后会于大庙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭统》</span>。注:&ldquo;始祖庙也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大，小大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奠高山大川。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古</span><span class="diczx2">田常</span>上请爵禄而行之群臣，下大斗斛而施于百姓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;二柄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此不为远者小而近者大乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忽一人大呼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一只大口袋;山上的大白楼;大浸(大水);大美(大功德，大功业);大蔡(大龟);大泉(大钱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 年辈较长或排行第一的 <span class="encs">[senior;eldest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大兄言办饭，大嫂言视马。&mdash;&mdash;汉乐府<span class="smcs">《孤儿行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">诸葛恢</span>大女适太尉 <span class="diczx2">庾亮</span>儿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;方正》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:大妇(长子之妻);大宗(宗法社会以嫡系长房为&ldquo;大宗&rdquo;;余子为小宗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 重要，重大 <span class="encs">[important;main]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故临崩寄臣以大事也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿牢定大计，莫用众人之议也!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:大头脑(主脑;主旨);大八丈(大人物);大柄(大权);大趣(大旨;主要的旨趣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 德高望重的 <span class="encs">[be of noble character and high prestige;with great virtue and high prestige]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾长见笑于大方之家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王年小而位大。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;排调》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利见大人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;乾》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:大操(高尚的操守);大贤(才德超群的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 超过一般 <span class="encs">[heavy;strong;loud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陶公少有大志，家酷贫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;贤缓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 学识渊博的 <span class="encs">[be learned;have great learning]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大匠不为拙工改废绳墨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;尽心上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 规模大 <span class="encs">[general;main;major]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胡人大入塞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;人间训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公子乃置酒大会宾客。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:大手术;大反攻</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 性质严重 <span class="encs">[serious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欺君不忠，母病不孝，不忠不孝，其罪莫大。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;政事》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 尊称对方的事物 <span class="encs">[your]</span>。敬语。如:大作;尊姓大名</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 用在时令、时间或节日前表示强调。如:大白天;大清早;大喜的日子</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>大</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 达到很广范围或很高程度，大肆 <span class="encs">[greatly;fully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">约以连兵大举。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此臣所课大患也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:大长志气;大吃一惊;大大增加;大办农业</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 很，太，非常 <span class="encs">[very]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居简而行简，无乃大简乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大谩，愿闻其要。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">告之以大古，教之以至顺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;非十二子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少年大骇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">门者答揖大喜奔出。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何竟日默默在此，大类女郎也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span>。又如:不大好;不大舒服;大肯(非常愿意)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 经常 <span class="encs">[often]</span>&mdash;&mdash;加&ldquo;不大&rdquo;则表示不经常 <span class="encs">[not often]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:不大吃面;不大回家</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 前天或后天的前一天或后一天 <span class="encs">[further ahead or behind]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:大前天;大后天</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>大</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大人 <span class="encs">[adult;grown-up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">刘道生</span>日夕在事，大小殊快。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;赏誉》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小大稽首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 大小 <span class="encs">[size]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今年杀诸贼奴，当取金印如斗大。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;尤悔》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 〈方〉∶父亲 <span class="encs">[father]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:俺大叫俺来上海看看你</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈方〉∶指伯父或叔父 <span class="encs">[uncle]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:俺三大是个劳模</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>大</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 长大 <span class="encs">[be brought up]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 超过 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦无大大王。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策二》</span></span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">大</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>大</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄉㄚˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>d&agrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1015.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">在體積、面積、容量、數量、強度、深度、力量等方面超過一般，或超過比較的對象。與「小」相對而言。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大山」、「大河」、「大雨」、「眼睛大」、「力氣大」、「雷聲大，雨點小。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">最年長的、排行第一的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大哥」、「大伯」、「大媽」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示尊敬之詞。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「拜讀大作」、「尊姓大名」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">不平常、重要的、偉大的。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;石崇&middot;思歸引序》：「余少有大志，夸邁流俗。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;袁宏&middot;三國名臣序贊》：「雖大旨同歸，所託或乖。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">時間上更前或更後，有強調的意味。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大前天」、「大後天」、「大年初一」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表程度深。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大紅大紫」、「大吃一驚」、「大快人心」、「大搞活動」、「天已大亮」、「大有出息」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">與「不」字連用，表次數少或程度淺。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「她不大出門見人的。」、「他不大愛抽菸。」、「小侄兒還不大會講話。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">很、非常。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷六八&middot;霍光傳》：「長公主大以是怨光。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">差不多、不很精確。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大約」、「大概」、「大略」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">與「小」相對之詞。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》：「小國不可以敵大。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">年紀較大的人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「你們一家大小都要來吃喜酒喔！」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如唐代有大明，金代有大興國。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">二一四部首之一。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">誇耀、自以為是。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「誇大」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;表記》：「是故君子不自大其事，不自尚其功。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
<div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>大</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄊㄞˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>t&agrave;i</rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">至高無上的。</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;江沅《說文釋例&middot;卷上&middot;釋字例&middot;大》：「古兄作『大』，不作『太』、『泰』。《易》之『大極』，《春秋》之『大子』、『大上』，《尚書》之『大誓』、『大王王季』，《史》、《漢》之『大上皇』、『大后』，後人皆讀為太。或徑改本書，作『太』及『泰』。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「太」、「泰」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5927.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20632.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠘲</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】徒蓋切。【正韻】度柰切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音汏。小之對。【易&middot;乾卦】大哉乾元。【老子&middot;道德經】域中有四大，道大，天大，地大，王亦大。【莊子&middot;天地篇】不同同之謂大。【則陽篇】天地者，形之大。隂陽者，氣之大。</p>
<p>又初也。【禮&middot;文王世子】天子視學，大昕鼓徵。【註】日初明，擊鼓徵召學士，使早至也。</p>
<p>又徧也。【禮&middot;郊特牲】大報天而主日。</p>
<p>又肥美也。【儀禮&middot;公食大夫禮】士羞，庶羞皆有大，贊者辨取庶羞之大，以授賓。【註】大，以肥美者特爲臠，所以祭也。</p>
<p>又過也。【戰國策】無大大王。</p>
<p>又長也。【爾雅&middot;釋器】珪大尺二寸謂之玠。【疏】大，長也。</p>
<p>又都大，官名。宋制有兩都大，一提舉茶馬，一提點坑冶鑄錢與提<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>序官。</p>
<p>又措大，士也。【書&middot;言故事】窮措大，眼孔小，與錢十萬貫，塞破屋子矣。</p>
<p>又唐大，弓名，見【周禮&middot;夏官】。</p>
<p>又四大，地、水、火、風也，見【梵書&middot;圓覺經】。</p>
<p>又姓大。廷氏之後，見【風俗通】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>他蓋切，音忲。易：大和大極。書、詩：大王大師。禮：大羹大牢。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音泰。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>唐佐切，音䭾。【杜甫&middot;天狗賦】不愛力以許人兮，能絕目以爲大。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>吐臥切，音拕。猛也，甚也。【禮&middot;童子不衣裘裳註】鄭康成爲大溫也。徐邈大音唾。</p>
<p>又叶徒計切，音第。【詩&middot;大雅】戎雖小子，而式弘大，叶厲泄愒敗。　【正字通】楊愼曰：大無音一駕切者，韻書二十二禡不收。考淮南子，宋康王世，有雀生鸇。占曰：小而生大，必霸天下。大叶下，古亦有一駕切之音。　【說文】天大，地大，人亦大。象人形。【徐曰】本古文人字。一曰他達切，經史大太泰通。</p>
