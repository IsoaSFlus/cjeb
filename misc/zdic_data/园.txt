                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>园</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">園</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>种植果蔬花木的地方：～子。～丁。～艺。～圃。</li>
	<li>原指别墅游息之所，现指供人游玩、娱乐的公共场所：圆明～。公～。</li>
	<li>旧指历代帝王以及亲王、妃嫔、公主之墓：～庙（帝王墓地所建之宗庙）。～陵（帝王的墓地）。</li>
</ol>

Erholungsstätte  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> jardin,parc,terre utilisée pour la culture de plantes,site utilisé pour les loisirs publics</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>园</strong></p>
<p>園 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从囗( <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span>)，袁声。形符为&ldquo;囗&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span>)，表示范围。本义:种蔬菜、花果、树木的地方)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[garden]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">园，所以树果也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">种树曰园。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">园圃毓草木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以场圃任园地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;载师》</span>。注:&ldquo;樊圃谓之园。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于丘园。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;贲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">园有桃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;园有桃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">青青园中葵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;长歌行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田园将芜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">园日涉以成趣。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:园头(禅寺内管菜园的人);果园;植物园;园公(即东园公。商山四皓之一;又指管理花园的仆人);园户(指唐宋时种植、制作茶叶的民家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 庭园;供人憩息、游乐或观赏的地方 <span class="encs">[flower garden;grounds;place for public recreation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白菟素鸠，游君园庭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《隶释》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:园叟花丁(园丁与花匠);园叟(年老的园丁);公园;戏园;动物园;园客(游园的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 帝王、后妃的墓地 <span class="encs">[cemetery]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:园陵(祖先的坟茔);园寝(建在帝王墓上的庙);园庙(帝王墓地所建的宗庙)</span></p>
