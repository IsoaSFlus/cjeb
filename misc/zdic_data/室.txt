                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">室</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>室</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p><ol><li>屋子，房间，亦指家：居～。教～。会客～。温～。引狼入～。</li><li>家，家族：皇～。女有家，男有～。</li><li>机关团体内部的工作单位：档案～。</li><li>古指妻子（亦指为子娶妻或以女嫁人）：妻～。继～。</li><li>刀剑的鞘。</li><li>墓穴。</li><li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">室</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>室</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从宀从至。人到屋中就停止了。本义:内室)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[room behind hall;room]</span>。堂后之正室。古人房屋内部，前叫<span class="yhcs">“堂”</span>，堂后以墙隔开，后部中央叫<span class="yhcs">“室”</span>，室的东西两侧叫<span class="yhcs">“房”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">室，实也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“古者前堂后室。释名曰:“室，实也，人物实满其中也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上古穴居而野处，后世圣人易之以宫室，古者宫室贵贱同称。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span>传</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">室，堂之内，人所安止也。——<span class="smcs">《说文系传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若通而言之，则宫室通名，故<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>云，<span class="yhcs">“宫谓之室，室谓之宫。”</span>别而言之，论其四面穹隆则曰宫，因其贮物充实则曰室。”——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span>疏</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入室又弗见也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·问丧》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:室间(室内);室庑(居室);室温(室内温度);室隅(室内角落);登堂入室;室匣(内室中贮藏衣物的柜)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 房屋;房间 <span class="encs">[house;room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筑室百堵，西南其户。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·斯干》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">户庭无尘杂，虚室有余闲。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厅旁一室，为吾与汝双栖之所。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:室家(居所，住宅;夫妇所居为室，一门之内为家;妻子);室宇(房舍;屋宇);室宅(房舍住宅);室事(建房用材);室制(宫殿、屋宇的规模样式);室舍(房屋;住所);室第(住宅);室闾(房舍);室庐(居室，房舍)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 家 <span class="encs">[home]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">室人交遍谪我。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·北门》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曩与吾祖居者，今其室十无一焉。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">室中更无人，惟有乳下孙。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:室老(家相，家臣中的长者);室族(家族);室祭(家祭);室人(家中的人);室怒市色(生家中人的气，却以怒色对待市人，谓迁怒于人)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 机关、工厂、学校等内部的工作单位 <span class="encs">[work unit of an organization，institution，factory，etc.]</span>。如:办公室;研究室;手术室;收发室</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 指妻子 <span class="encs">[wife]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三十曰壮，有室。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:室妾(妻妾);室氏(妻子);正室;侧室</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 墓穴 <span class="encs">[grave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百岁之后，归于其室。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·葛生》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 刀剑的鞘 <span class="encs">[sheath]</span>:与刀刃、猎刀或者其它器械紧密配合的护套</p><p><span class="diczx1">拔剑，剑长操其室。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 家人;家族 <span class="encs">[members
