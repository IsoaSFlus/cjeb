                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蔬</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>蔬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>可做菜吃的植物（多属草本）：～菜。菜～。布衣～食。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> vegetables, greens</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gemüse  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> légumes</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蔬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蔬</strong> <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从艸，疏声。本义:蔬菜) 同本义 <span class="encs">[vegetables;greens]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔬，菜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span>。先秦无&ldquo;蔬&rdquo;字，用&ldquo;疏&rdquo;表示。&ldquo;蔬&rdquo;是汉魏间字。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令发五苑之蓏、蔬、枣、栗，足以活民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说右下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">八曰臣妾，聚敛疏材。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太宰》</span>。注:&ldquo;疏材，百草根实可食者。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饭疏食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>注:&ldquo;菜食也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能植百谷百蔬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">菜谓之蔬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁</span><span class="diczx2">淳安</span>知县。布袍脱粟，令老仆艺蔬自给。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;海瑞传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蔬菲(指果菜类粗食);蔬笋(蔬菜和笋);蔬饭(蔬菜饭食。指粗食);蔬蓏(蔬菜瓜果);蔬蔌(蔬菜之统称);蔬圃(菜园);蔬茹(蔬菜)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蔬</strong> <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>种菜 <span class="encs">[grow vegetables]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有松可荫，有地可蔬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《复庵记》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/852C.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】所葅切【韻會】山於切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音疏。【說文】菜也。【爾雅註】凡草菜可食者，通名爲蔬。【禮&middot;曲禮】稻曰嘉蔬。【註】稻，菰蔬之屬也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋草】蘧蔬。【註】似土菌，生菰草中。</p>
<p>又通疏。【周禮&middot;天官】臣妾聚斂疏材。</p>
<p>又通疎。【荀子&middot;富國篇】葷菜百疎。</p>
<p>又【集韻】爽舉切，音所。粒也。【莊子&middot;天道篇】䑕壤有餘蔬。【註】蔬讀若糈，粒也。</p>
