                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>铺</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鋪</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>把东西散开放置，平摆：～开摊平。～床。～垫（①卧具；②衬托，陪衬，写作技巧之一）。～张。平～直叙。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>铺</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鋪</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>商店：饭～。肉～。～面。</li>
	<li>床：床～。搭～。卧～。</li>
	<li>旧时的驿站：三十里～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铺</strong></p>
<p>鋪、舖 <span class="dicpy">pū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，甫声。本义:衔门环的底座。又称&ldquo;铺首&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[knocker-holder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铺，箸门铺首也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挤玉户以撼金铺兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《长门赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金琐银铺。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">何晏</span><span class="smcs">《景福殿赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铜龟蛇铺首鸣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;哀帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金铺交映。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">西晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《蜀都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:铺手(铺首)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 铜器名。豆属。其上为一平的直壁浅盘。铺与豆的区别在于它没有细长的把柄，在盘下连铸一较宽的高圈足 <span class="encs">[Pu vessel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铺，亦古器名，有公刘铺，有天尹养铺，形亦如簠，但簠方而铺圆耳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杨慎</span><span class="smcs">《丹铅总录》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铺</strong></p>
<p>鋪 <span class="dicpy">pū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 把东西展开或摊平 <span class="encs">[spread;extend]</span></p>

                              鋪·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/92EA.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】普胡切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】滂模切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25835.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥠵</span>。【說文】著門鋪首也。从金甫聲。【增韻】所以銜環者，作龜蛇之形，以銅爲之，故曰金鋪。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】排玉戸而颺金鋪兮。【註】鋪，門首也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也，布也。【詩&middot;周南序疏】賦之言鋪，直鋪<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>今之政敎善惡。</p>
<p>又【小雅&middot;乃安斯寢箋】乃鋪席，與羣臣安燕以樂之。【大雅】鋪敦淮濆。【註】鋪，布也。布其師旅也。【禮&middot;樂記】鋪筵席，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>尊俎。</p>
<p>又病也。與痡通。【詩&middot;周南&middot;我僕痡矣釋文】痡，音敷。</p>
<p>又普烏反。本又作鋪。</p>
<p>又【大雅】淮夷來鋪。【傳】鋪，病也。</p>
<p>又徧也。【詩&middot;小雅】淪胥以鋪。【箋】鋪，徧也。【釋文】鋪，普烏反。王云：病也。</p>
<p>又【正字通】古器曰鋪，豆屬，有鋪<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>薦獻之義。博古圖：周劉公鋪，高五寸五分，深一寸六分，口徑七寸六分，容二升三合。銘云：劉公作杜嬬尊鋪，永寶用。名雖爲鋪，其器則豆也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>芳無切，音敷。【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也，設也。○按詩小雅、大雅、禮樂記、釋文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>兼<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25835.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥠵</span>、敷二音。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】鋪，止也。【註】鋪，芳孤反。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】揄鋪，毳也。荆揚江湖之閒曰揄鋪。【註】謂物之行蔽也。鋪音敷。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】鋪頒，索也。東齊曰鋪頒，猶秦晉言抖擻也。【註】鋪，音敷。</p>
<p>又【集韻】奔模切，音逋。設也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>普故切，音怖。義同。一曰鋪首，著門銜環者。</p>
<p>又【韻會】賈肆也。【正字通】俗作舗。資暇集市肆中，筐筥等鱗次其物以粥者曰星貨鋪，言羅列繁密如星。今呼星火舗，非。</p>
