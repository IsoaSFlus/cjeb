                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诰</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诰</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">誥</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gào.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄠˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>古代帝王对臣子的命令：～命。～封。</li>    <li>告诫，勉励：自～。～诫。</li>    <li>帝王任命或封赠的文书：～敕（官吏受封的文书）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> inform, notify, admonish, order</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> auferlegen, vorschreiben ,bewilligen, gestatten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> annonce,informer un inférieur,décret</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诰</strong></p><p>誥 <span class="dicpy">gào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，告声。本义:告诉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，上告下曰诰 <span class="encs">[tell;inform]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诰，告也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“以言告人，古用此字，今则用告字。以此诰为上告下之字。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按，上告下之义，古用诰，</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>复造诏安当之。——<span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">伊尹</span>申诰于王。——<span class="smcs">《书·太甲下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后以施命诰四方。——<span class="smcs">《易·姤》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">告上曰告，发下曰诰。——<span class="smcs">《列子·杨朱》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雅告奥义。——<span class="smcs">《书·序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诰誓不及五帝。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·大略》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“诰誓以言辞相诫约也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至秦又造诏字以当之。——<span class="smcs">《书·大诰》</span>释文</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:诰命夫人(特指受封赠的夫人);诰授(以诰命授与);诰文(帝王的祷告文字)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 告诫;劝勉 <span class="encs">[admonish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">近臣谏，远臣谤，舆人诵，以自诰也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:诰毖(告诫);诰教(告诫教育)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>诰</strong></p><p>誥 <span class="dicpy">gào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 告诫之文。帝王任命或封赠的文书 <span class="encs">[imperial mandate]</span>。古者上下有诰，秦废古制称制、诏。唐称制不称诰。宋始以诰命庶官，凡追赠大臣、贬谪有罪、赠封其祖父妻室，不宜于廷者，皆用诰，通谓之制</p><p><span class="diczx1">一品至五品，皆授以诰命，六品至九品，皆授以敕命。——<span class="smcs">《清会典事例》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:诰敕(旧制官吏受封的文书);诰券(皇帝任命和封赠的文书);诰策(诰令策书);诰章(诏令)</p><p><span class="diczx1">颁诰</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诰令</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/诰命">诰命</a></span></div>                    <div class="div                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              誥·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AA5.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2234E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢍎</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/279DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧧞</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/279D9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧧙</span>【唐韻】【廣韻】古到切【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】【正韻】居号切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音告。【說文】告也。【徐曰】以文言告曉之也。【廣雅】敎也。【易·姤卦】后以施命誥四方。【周禮·春官】大祝作六辭，以通上下、親疏、遠近，三曰誥。【註】杜子春曰：誥，當爲告。書亦可爲告。</p><p>又【韻會】告上曰告，發下曰誥。【爾雅·釋言】謹也。【註】所以約勤謹戒衆。【疏】以六義諭衆謂之誥，尚書誥誓之類是也。【孔安國·尚書序】夏商周之書，雖設敎不倫雅誥奧義，其歸一揆。【疏】三王訓、誥、誓、命、歌、貢、征、範類猶有八，獨言誥者，以別而言之，其類有八，文從要約，一誥兼焉。以此八事，皆有言以誥示，故總謂之誥。【穀梁傳·隱八年】誥誓不及五帝。【周禮·秋官】士師掌五戒，二曰誥，用之于會同。【文心雕龍】其在三代，事兼誥誓，誓以訓戒，誥以敷政。</p><p>又【正字通】古者上下有誥，秦廢古稱制詔。漢武元狩六年，初作誥，然不以命官。唐稱制不稱誥，宋始以誥命庶官，明命官用敕不用誥。三載考績，則用誥以褒美。洪武十七年奏定有封爵者給誥，如一品之制，二十六年定一品至五品皆授以誥命，六品至七品，皆授以敕命。</p><p>又鳥名。【揚子·方言】布穀，自關東西梁楚之閒謂之結誥。</p><p>又【集韻】姑沃切，音梏。亦告也。同告。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8AA5.svg">                          <p>告也。从言告聲。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/279D9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧧙</span>，古文誥。古到切〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/279DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧧞</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2234E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢍎</span>，亦古文誥。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">告也。</span>見釋詁。按以言吿人，古用此字，今則用吿字，以此誥爲上吿下之字。又秦造詔字，惟天子獨稱之。文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨕖</span>注卅五引《獨<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣃔</span>》曰：詔猶吿也。三代無其文，秦漢有也。據此可證秦已前無詔字，至倉頡篇乃有幼子承詔之語，故許書不錄詔字。鉉補之，非也。<span class="jieshi">从言告聲。</span>古到切。古音在三部。<span class="jieshi"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/279D9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧧙</span>，古文誥。</span>按此从言肘聲，是古音在三部之證。〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/279DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧧞</span>，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2234E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢍎</span>，亦古文誥。                          </div>
