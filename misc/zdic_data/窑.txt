						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>窑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄠˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>烧砖、瓦、陶瓷器的构筑物：砖～。瓷～。</li>
	<li>为采煤而凿的洞：煤～。</li>
	<li>在坡上特为住人挖的洞：～洞。～葬。</li>
	<li>旧时称妓院：～子。～姐（妓女）。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>窑</strong></p>
<p>窯、窰 <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从穴，从缶( <span class="dicpy">fǒu</span>)。穴，土室;缶，瓦器。表示烧制瓦器的地方。本义:烧砖瓦陶瓷器的灶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[kiln]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窯，烧瓦灶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陶灶曰窯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">文采纂组者，燔功之窑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;七臣七主》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穴内口为灶，令如窑，令容七八员艾，左右窦皆如此。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:窑头土坯(窑头上未经烧制的砖瓦坯。比喻土里土气，未曾受教化的人);窑神(旧称主宰砖瓦窑的神灵);窑烟(烧制砖瓦陶瓷等的灶里冒出的烟)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指陶瓷器 <span class="encs">[pottery;earthen ware]</span>。如:窑器(陶瓷器);窑人(烧制砖瓦陶瓷器的工人);窑户(陶瓷工匠);窑务(主管陶瓷业务的机构)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 近代泛指硅酸盐工业常用的高温设备 <span class="encs">[hot-temperature equipment]</span>。如:竖窑;回转窑;坩埚窑</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 方言。指妓院 <span class="encs">[brothel]</span>。如:窑姐(旧称娼妓);窑调(旧时在妓院里流行的小调)</p>
