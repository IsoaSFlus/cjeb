                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>家</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄚˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>共同生活的眷属和他们所住的地方：～庭。～眷。～长（<span class="dicpy">zhǎng </span>）。～园。～谱。～塾。～乡。～风。～训。～规。～喻户晓。如数～珍。</li>
	<li>家庭所在的地方：回～。老～。安～。</li>
	<li>居住：&ldquo;可以～焉&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>对人称自己的尊长、亲属：～祖。～父。～翁。～母。～慈。</li>
	<li>家里养的，不是野生的：～畜。～禽。</li>
	<li>经营某种行业的人家或有某种身份的人家：酒～。农～。</li>
	<li>掌握某种专门学识或有丰富实践经验及从事某种专门活动的人：专～。行（<span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ng </span>）～。作～。科学～。</li>
	<li>学术流派：儒～。法～。道～。墨～。纵横～。诸子百～。</li>
	<li>量词，用于计算家庭或企业：一～人家。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>家</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jia  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jia.mp3"></a></span> &bull;ㄐㄧㄚ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>词尾，指一类的人：老人～。</li>
	<li>用在男人的名字或排行后面，指他的妻：水生～</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>家</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jie  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jie.mp3"></a></span> &bull;ㄐㄧㄝ</span></p>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>家</strong> <span class="dicpy">gū</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古通&ldquo;姑&rdquo; <span class="encs">[the mother of one&#39;s husband]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:阿家(称婆婆);阿家阿翁</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>家</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面是&ldquo;宀&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span>)，表示与室家有关，下面是&ldquo;豕&rdquo;，即猪。古代生产力低下，人们多在屋子里养猪，所以房子里有猪就成了人家的标志。本义:屋内，住所)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[home;domicile;dwelling place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家，居也。从宀，豭省声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家人内也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;杂卦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上地家七人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;小司徒》</span>。注:&ldquo;有夫有妇，然后为家。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夙夜浚明有家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;皋谟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开国承家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">室为夫妇所居，家谓一门之内。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;桃夭》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">便要还家，设酒杀鸡作食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申表示住宅、房屋 <span class="encs">[house;residence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">平原君</span>家楼临民家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:家堂(正屋中间的堂屋);家所(家居处所);家堂菩萨(家中供奉的佛祖神灵);家第(家宅;私邸);家间(家里;家中)。借指部队或机关中某个成员工作的处所。如:连长开会去了不在家;所长在家主持工作</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 家庭，人家 <span class="encs">[family]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">数口之家，可以无饥矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">治天下之国若治一家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚同下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家半三军。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视吾家所寡有者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长铗归来乎，无以为家!</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家绘像祀之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:养家;持家;安家;家第(对对方家庭的敬称);家数(家族;武艺招数、招式);家庙(供奉祭祀祖先的祠堂);家缘(家业，家产);家私(家产);家赀(家私财产);家计(家业，家产)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指夫或妻 <span class="encs">[husband or wife]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女有家，男有室，无相渎也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">固乱流其鲜终兮，</span><span class="diczx2">浞</span>又贪夫厥家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:家婆(家主婆;当家婆);家公(丈夫);家花(喻自己的妻子);家婆(妻子;主妇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 家家;每家 <span class="encs">[every family]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:家户(每家每户，家家户户);家至户到(到每家每户;遍及每家每户)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 大夫统治的政治区域，即卿大夫或卿大夫的采地食邑 <span class="encs">[minister and high official or theirs feud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">丘</span>也闻有国有家者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;季氏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王曰:何以利吾国?大夫曰:何以利吾家?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:家甲(古时卿大夫的私人武装);家主(春秋时对卿大夫的称谓);家老(上古大夫家臣中的长者);家臣(春秋时各国卿大夫的臣属);家邦(本指家和国，亦泛指国家);家国(家与国，亦指国家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 娘家 <span class="encs">[married woman&#39;s maiden home]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿但暂还家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谢家来贵门。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今日还家去。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且暂还家去。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 学术或艺术流派 <span class="encs">[school]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焚百家之言，以愚黔首。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:家数(流派风格;家法传统。多用于诗、文、技艺等);家言邪学(自成一家的偏见。儒家指儒家以外的学说)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 经营某种行业的人家或具有某种身分的人 <span class="encs">[a person or family engaged in a certain trade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">农家少闲月。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《观刈麦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:农家;厂家;渔家;行家;东家;庄家</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 掌握某种专门学识或从事某种工作、担任某种职务的人 <span class="encs">[expert;specialist in a certain field]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地学之家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:政治家;艺术家;专家;科学家;社会活动家;作家</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 民族 <span class="encs">[nation]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苗家;侗家;傣家</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 指某一集团或某一方面 <span class="encs">[group]</span>。 如: 三家条约</p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 家财，财产 <span class="encs">[property]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆没其家而迁之蜀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;吕不韦列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:分家;发家;家破人亡;抄家</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 朝廷 <span class="encs">[court]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">争先入公家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;贵卒》</span>。注:&ldquo;公之朝也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汉家(即汉朝);随家(即隋朝);家历(皇朝的历数)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 都城 <span class="encs">[capital]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其国家，宫室、车旗、衣服、礼仪，皆以九为节。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;国家，国之所居，谓城方也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">紫泉</span>宫殿锁烟霞，欲取 <span class="diczx2">芜城</span>作帝家。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《隋宫》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(18)</span> 帝王或太子 <span class="encs">[emperor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三家，三皇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;马融传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:家吏(皇后或太子的官属)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(19)</span> 某人 <span class="encs">[a person]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:家伙;家头(一个人叫一家头)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(20)</span> 古代医书上指患某种病的人 <span class="encs">[patient]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">喘家，作桂枝汤，加厚朴、杏子佳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《伤寒论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(21)</span> &ldquo;傢&rdquo;的古字 <span class="encs">[utensil]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:家生;家伙点(锣鼓家什的节奏);家器(家庭日常用具)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>家</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 人工饲养或驯养在家中的。与&ldquo;野&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[domestic]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:家羽(家养的鸟);家兔;家禽;家兽(人类为了经济或其它目的而驯养的兽类，如猪、牛、羊、马、骆驼、家兔、猫、狗等)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 私家的，个人的 <span class="encs">[individual]</span>，与&ldquo;公&rdquo;相对</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今大道既隐，天下为家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家祭勿忘告乃翁。&mdash;&mdash;陆游<span class="smcs">《示儿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 我的 <span class="encs">[my]</span>&mdash;&mdash;谦辞，用于对别人称自己的辈分高或年纪大的亲属 。如:家父家母(对人称自己父母的谦词);家岳(对人称自己的丈人);家尊(称自己的父亲，家父);家堂(对母亲的称呼)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 内部的;家中的 <span class="encs">[internal]</span>。与&ldquo;外&rdquo;相对。如:家生哨(亦作&ldquo;家中哨&rdquo;、&ldquo;家生肖&rdquo;。詈词。犹言家盗内贼);家贼(家庭内部的败类。亦指汉奸)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>家</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用来计算家庭或企业的多少。如:三家商店;两家人家</p>
<p>◎ <strong>家</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 娶妻;成家 <span class="encs">[marry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">少康</span>之未家兮，留有 <span class="diczx2">虞</span>之二姚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:家道(成家之道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 安家;定居 <span class="encs">[settle down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家，居其地曰家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正志通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既去职，遂家于</span><span class="diczx2">汝州</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《崔评事墓志铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 养家，持家 <span class="encs">[support a family]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:家克计(持家之道);家法(治家的礼法)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;稼&rdquo;。稼穑。种谷曰稼，收获曰穑 <span class="encs">[sow grains]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弊也者家也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;侈靡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陶家事亲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼&middot;五帝德篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">jie</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jie.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">家</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>家</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧㄚ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jiā <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3119.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">眷屬共同生活的場所。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「家庭」、「回<b>家</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;賀知章〈回鄉偶書〉詩二首之一：「少小離<b>家</b>老大回，鄉音無改鬢毛衰。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一門之內共同生活的人。</span><span class="gc_yy">《呂氏春秋&middot;先識覽&middot;察微》：「吳人應之不恭，怒殺而去之，吳人往報之，盡屠其<b>家</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">學術流派。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「儒<b>家</b>」、「道<b>家</b>」、「百家爭鳴」、「一家之言」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">經營某種行業或具有某種身分的人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「農<b>家</b>」、「商<b>家</b>」、「店<b>家</b>」、「少東<b>家</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">尊稱學有專長或有專門技術的人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「專<b>家</b>」、「文學<b>家</b>」、「科學<b>家</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">自稱或稱別人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「自<b>家</b>」、「咱<b>家</b>」、「哀<b>家</b>」、「姑娘<b>家</b>」、「小孩子<b>家</b>」、「婦道人<b>家</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代大夫所統治的政治區域。與「國」相對。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;季氏》：「丘也聞有國有<b>家</b>者，不患寡而患不均，不患貧而患不安。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">私有財產。</span><span class="gc_yy">《韓非子&middot;外儲說左上》：「夫賣庸而播耕者，主人費<b>家</b>而美食。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷八五&middot;呂不韋傳》：「諸嫪毐舍人皆沒其<b>家</b>而遷之蜀。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞。計算家庭、店鋪、大型企業等的單位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「住戶不滿十<b>家</b>」、「三<b>家</b>報社」、「只此一<b>家</b>，別無分號。」、「開了三<b>家</b>分公司。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如周代有家伯。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">居住。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;江淹&middot;別賦》：「又若君居淄右，妾<b>家</b>河陽。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對人謙稱自己的親長。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「家父」、「家母」、「家兄」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">家中的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「家事」、「家人」、「家產」、「家業」、「家僮」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">家中飼養的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「家畜」、「家禽」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">助</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">置於句中。相當於「地」、「的」。</span><span class="gc_yy">元&middot;蕭德祥《殺狗勸夫&middot;第二折》：「他道俺哥哥十分<b>家</b>沉醉，且吃些兒熱湯熱水。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《西遊記&middot;第五一回》：「大碗<b>家</b>寬懷暢飲。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
<div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>家</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄍㄨ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>gū <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2342.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對女子的尊稱。</span><span class="gc_yy">《後漢書&middot;卷八四&middot;列女傳&middot;曹世叔妻傳》：「扶風曹世叔妻者，同郡班彪之女也，名昭&hellip;&hellip;。帝數召入宮，令皇后諸貴人師事焉，號曰大<b>家</b>。　」</span><span class="gc_yx">同「<a href='/hans/姑' target=_blank>姑</a> 」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5BB6.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20594.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠖔</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A45.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡩅</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/219DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡧚</span>【唐韻】古牙切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居牙切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音加。【說文】家居也。【爾雅】戸牖之閒謂之扆。其內謂之家。【詩&middot;周南】宜其室家。【註】家謂一門之內。</p>
<p>又婦謂夫曰家。【孟子】女子生而願爲之有家。</p>
<p>又一夫受田百畝，曰夫家。【周禮&middot;地官】上地家七人，中地家六人，下地家五人。【註】有夫有婦，然後爲家。</p>
<p>又大夫之邑曰家，仕於大夫者曰家臣。【左傳&middot;襄二十九年】大夫皆富，政將在家。</p>
<p>又天家，天子之稱。【蔡邕&middot;獨斷】天子無外，以天下爲家。</p>
<p>又居其地曰家。【史記&middot;陸賈傳】以好畤田地，善往家焉。</p>
<p>又著述家。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】表章六經，罷黜百家。</p>
<p>又【太史公自序】成一家之言。</p>
<p>又家人，易卦名。</p>
<p>又姓。漢劇令家羨，宋家鉉翁。</p>
<p>又【集韻】古胡切，音姑。【詩&middot;豳風】予未有室家。叶上据荼。</p>
<p>又【小雅】復我邦家。叶上居樗。</p>
<p>又與姑同。大家，女之尊稱。漢曹世叔之妻班昭稱大家，卽超妹。</p>
<p>又叶古俄切，音歌。【古雉朝飛操】我獨何命兮未有家，時將暮兮可奈何。</p>
<p>又【孔臧&middot;蓼賦】苟非德義，不以爲家。安逸無心，如禽獸何。</p>
<p>又叶古暮切，音固。【焦氏&middot;易林】三足孤鳥，靈明爲御。司過罰惡，自殘其家。　【說文】从宀，豭省聲。周伯溫曰：豕居之圈曰家，故从宀从豕。後人借爲室家之家。○按《六書故》作①，人所合也。从㐺，三人聚宀下，①之義也。乑古族字，乑譌爲豕，《說文》謂从豭省，無義。①字从宀从㐺，或从宀从乑。乑字从亻从人人，與㐺形近。</p>
<p>又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/219BC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡦼</span>。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5BB6_1468.svg" /><span class="swbf">(家)</span><span class="ywyy">凥也。</span><span class="swdz">凥各本作居，今正。凥，處也。處，止也。釋宫：牖戶之閒謂之扆，其內謂之家。引伸之天子諸侯曰國，大夫曰家。凡古曰家人者，猶今曰人家也。家人字見哀四年左傳夏小正傳及史記、漢書。家凥曡韵。</span><span class="ywyy">从宀，豭省聲。</span><span class="swdz">古牙切。古音在五部。按此字爲一大疑案。豭省聲讀家，學者但見从豕而巳，从豕之字多矣，安見其爲豭省耶。何以不云叚聲，而紆回至此耶。竊謂此篆本義乃豕之凥也，引申叚借以爲人之凥，字義之轉移多如此。牢，牛之凥也，引伸爲所以拘罪之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EFC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻼</span>牢，庸有異乎。豢豕之生子冣多，故人凥聚處借用其字，久而忘其字之本義，使引伸之義得冒據之，葢自古而然。許書之作也，盡正其失，而猶未免此。且曲爲之說，是千慮之一失也。家篆當入豕部。</span></p>
