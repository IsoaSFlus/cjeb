                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">党</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>党</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">黨</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄤˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>为了政治目的结合起来的团体：～派。～团。～委。～员。～章。～阀。～风。～纪。</li>
	<li>意见相合的人或由私人利害关系结成的团体：～羽。朋～。死～。</li>
	<li>指亲族：父～。母～。妻～。</li>
	<li>古代地方组织，以五百家为一党。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> political party, gang, faction</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Partei  (S, Pol)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> parti,parti politique,clique,faction</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">党</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>党</strong></p>
<p>黨 <span class="dicpy">dǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从黑，尚声。&ldquo;党&rdquo;、&ldquo;黨&rdquo;在古代是两个字，现在&ldquo;党&rdquo;是&ldquo;黨&rdquo;的简化字。简化字&ldquo;党&rdquo;，从儿(人，尚声)本义:晦暗不明。&ldquo;党&rdquo;指集团时，在古代一般只用于贬义，与现代汉语不同)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 不鲜明 <span class="encs">[not bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黨，不鲜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">嘉兴</span>谓衣物敝垢不鲜曰烟党。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">蒋礼鸿</span><span class="smcs">《义府续貂》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 美，善，正直。后作&ldquo;谠&rdquo; <span class="encs">[good]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">党，善也;美也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">元而致实，博而党正，是士君子之辩者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>党</strong></p>
<p>黨 <span class="dicpy">dǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 知晓，解悟 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">法先王，顺礼义，党学者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 偏私，偏袒 <span class="encs">[be partial to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子党于师人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群而不党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>注:&ldquo;助也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾闻君子不党。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>注:&ldquo;相助匿曰党。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 结伙 <span class="encs">[gang involvement]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">性贪而狠，党豺为虐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>党</strong></p>
<p>黨 <span class="dicpy">dǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代地方户籍编制单位。五百家为党 <span class="encs">[five-hundred families]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五族为党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span>。注:&ldquo;百家，党正，各掌其党之政令教治。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君实乡州藏焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;山权数》</span>。注:&ldquo;三千五百家为党，此非</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>制。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">操</span>当以 <span class="diczx2">肃</span>还付乡党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:党人(同一乡里的人);党正(周代所设地方组织的长官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 朋辈。指意气相投的人 <span class="encs">[kinsfolk;relative]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辨说得其党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;仲尼燕居》</span>。注:&ldquo;类也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">睦于父母之党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;坊记》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹亲也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗟哉吾党二三子，安得至老不更归。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《山石》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:党友(指志道相近、立场相似的人);党援(指与自己同道而给予援助的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 朋党;由私人利害关系结成的小集团 <span class="encs">[clique faction]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无偏无党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span>。注:&ldquo;朋党。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">各于其党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;里仁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟党人之偷乐兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使其党入索财物。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:死党;余党(未消灭尽的党羽);党恶(结党作恶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 政党 <span class="encs">[political party]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:共产党;共和党;国民党;工人党</span></p>
