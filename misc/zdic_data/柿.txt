                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>柿</strong></p>
<p>柹 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 柿树 <span class="encs">[persimmon]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 柿树属落叶乔木，品种很多，叶子椭圆形或倒卵形，背面有绒毛，花黄白色。结浆果，扁圆形或圆锥形，橙黄色或红色，可以吃。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:柿叶(柿树的叶子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 这种植物的果实。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:柿糕(用糯米和干柿制成的一种食品)</span></p>
