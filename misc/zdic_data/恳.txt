						<p>● <strong>恳</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">懇</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kěn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kěn.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄣˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>真诚：诚～。～求。～托。～切。～谈。～请。～辞。勤～。</li>
	<li>请求：～情。哀～再三。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>恳</strong></p>
<p>懇 <span class="dicpy">kěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p> (形声。从心，( <span class="dicpy">kěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kěn.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:诚恳，真诚)同本义 <span class="encs">[earnestly;genuinely]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恳，信也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至诚曰恳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span>四引<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">意气勤勤恳恳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马迁传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;恳恳，至诚也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又乖恳愿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恳悃(真心诚意);恳款(殷切诚恳);恳曲(诚恳委婉);恳言(诚挚之言);恳直(诚挚切直)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>恳</strong></p>
<p>懇 <span class="dicpy">kěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> 请求，表露愿望和要求 <span class="encs">[request;beseech]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恳恩拯救。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恳乞(请求);恳央(恳求);恳祈(祈求);恳告(恳求)</span></p>

<p>又或作貇。【前漢&middot;劉向傳】貇貇數奸死亡之誅。【師古註】款誠之意。　亦作頎。【禮&middot;檀弓】頎乎其至也。【註】頎音懇，惻隱之貌。</p>
