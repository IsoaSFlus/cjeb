                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>形</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>实体：～仪（体态仪表）。～体。～貌。～容。～骸。～单影只。～影相吊。</li>
	<li>样子：～状。～式。～态。～迹。地～。情～。</li>
	<li>表现：～诸笔墨。喜～于色。</li>
	<li>对照，比较：相～见绌。</li>
	<li>状况，地势：～势。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/型">型</a>&rdquo;，模子。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/刑">刑</a>&rdquo;，刑罚。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">形</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>形</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shape;form]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形，象形也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">物成生理谓之形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雷雨之动满形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;屯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在天成象，在地成形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形色天性也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形者，生之具也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;太史公自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">良马可形容筋骨相也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;说符》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形似酒尊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸟兽之形。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形若土狗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因势象形。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:形物(有形之物);条形;线形;方形;圆形;球形;梯形;三角形;正方形;多角形;多边形;口形;体形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 形体，实体 <span class="encs">[body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可以隐形。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无案牍之劳形。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山岳潜形。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钩勒形廓。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:形寂(死亡);形响(形体和声响);形解(形体超脱现实;道教指尸解);有形;无形;整形;形生(身体与性命);形性(形体和性质);形训(用文字形体的分析来释字义)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 容色，容貌 <span class="encs">[appearance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">望远者，察其貌，而不察其形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:形藏(模样、身分);形质(容貌与性情);形秽(容貌不洁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 各种自然环境或地表的自然特征 <span class="encs">[terrain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>，形胜之国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:形便(地形有利);形要(形势险要);形局(地理格局)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 情势，形势 <span class="encs">[situation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">由此言之，勇怯，势也;强弱，形也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仆未之得验，然其形必然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《铸钱》</span></p>
