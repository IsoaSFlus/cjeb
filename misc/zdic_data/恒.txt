						<p>● <strong>恒</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>持久：～心。～久。～定。～齿。永～。～星。～温。</li>	<li>经常的，普通的：～言。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> constant, regular, persistent</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ständig, dauerhaft ,Heng  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> persévérance,permanent,persévérant,constant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">恒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>恒</strong></p>
<p>恆 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文，从心，从月，从二。&ldquo;二&rdquo;，表示天地。本义:永久，永恒)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lasting;permanent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恒，常也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恒者，久也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;序卦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恒，德之固也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无恒安处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以求其恒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恒牙(恒齿。永久齿);恒风(持续吹佛的风);恒舞(历时长而不中断的舞);恒制(长久不变的法则)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 寻常;普通 <span class="encs">[constant;common]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形貌奇伟，骨体不恒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴主传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人恒状。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恒态(常态);恒恣(经常的状态);恒情(常情);恒品(常类;常物);恒士(常人，平凡的人);恒常(正常的规律);恒医(普通的医生);恒言(常用的言语)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>恒</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 恒心，持之以恒的决心 <span class="encs">[perseverance]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:有恒</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 规律;法则 <span class="encs">[law]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:恒式(常规;常法);恒典(常典;常制);恒例(常规，惯例);恒规(常规)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 即恒山 <span class="encs">[Hengshan Mountain]</span>。古山名，在今河北省曲阳西北与山西接壤处。五岳中的北岳</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五岳:</span><span class="diczx2">华</span>、 <span class="diczx2">岱</span>、 <span class="diczx2">恒</span>、 <span class="diczx2">衡</span>、 <span class="diczx2">嵩</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">张华</span><span class="smcs">《博物志》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>恒</strong></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">恆</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“恒”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/恆">恆</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=二">二</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6046.svg">                          <p>常也。从心从舟，在二之閒上下。心以舟施，恆也。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6046_1.svg" /><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20128.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄨</span>，古文恆从月。《詩》曰：&ldquo;如月之恒。&rdquo;胡登切〖注〗篆作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/226E2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢛢</span>。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6046_1924.svg" /><span class="swbf">(恆)</span><span class="ywyy">常也。</span><span class="swdz">常當作長。古長久字衹作長。淺人稍稍分別。乃或借下帬之常爲之。故至集韵乃有一曰久也之訓。而篇、韵皆無之，此俗字之不可不正者也。時之長與尺寸之長，非有二義。</span><span class="ywyy">从心舟在二之閒上下。</span><span class="swdz">上下猶往復也。</span><span class="ywyy">心㠯舟施，恆也。</span><span class="swdz">謂往復遙遠，而心以舟運旋，歷久不變，恆之意也。宙下曰：舟車所極復也。此說會意之恉。胡登切。六部。俗本心上增一字。非。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6046_1925.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20128.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄨</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文恆，从月。</span><span class="swdz">此篆轉寫譌舛。旣云从月，則左當作月，不當作夕也。若汗<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>則左作舟，而右亦同此。不可曉。又按門部之古文閒作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28CE2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨳢</span>，葢古文月字略似外字，古文恆直是二中月耳。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：如月之恆。</span><span class="swdz">小雅天保文。此說从月之意。非謂毛詩作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20128.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠄨</span>也。傳曰：恆，弦也。按詩之恆本亦作緪。謂張弦也。月上弦而就盈，於是有恆久之義，故古文从月。</span></p>                          </div>
