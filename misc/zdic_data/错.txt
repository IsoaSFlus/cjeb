                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">错</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>错</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">錯</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄨㄛˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>不正确，与实际不符：差～。过～。～讹。～谬。～觉。～怪。不～。～爱。将～就～。</li>	<li>交叉着：交～。～乱。～杂。～综（纵横交叉）。～动。～落（交错纷杂）。盘根～节。</li>	<li>叉开：～开。～车。～过机会。</li>	<li>用来打磨玉石的石头：它山之石，可以为～。</li>	<li>打磨玉石：攻～。</li>	<li>镀金、银，涂饰：～金。～银。～彩镂金。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">错</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>错</strong></p>
<p>錯 <span class="dicpy">cu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，昔声。本义:用金涂饰，镶嵌)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[inlay with gold，silver，etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">错，金涂也。今所谓镀金。俗字作镀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金银要饰谓之错镂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼎错日用而不足贵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:错臂(用丹青画饰手臂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 互相磨擦 <span class="encs">[grind]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">错，磨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">错者所以治锯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列女传&middot;仁智》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">八卦相错。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;摩也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:错牙</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 杂 <span class="encs">[mix]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">错，杂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">错综其数。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交错以辩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;特牲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">车错毂兮短兵接。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;国殇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:错石(彩石，错杂叠积各种石头而成文彩);错列(错杂排列);错戾(错杂，交杂);错行(交替运行);错落(交错纷杂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 废弃 <span class="encs">[abandon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殷既错天命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;微子序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 分开;岔开 <span class="encs">[separate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:错口儿(开口);错缝儿(微小的过失)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 转动 <span class="encs">[turn]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:错眼珠;错身</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>错</strong></p>
<p>錯 <span class="dicpy">cu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 琢玉用的粗磨石 <span class="encs">[coarse wetstone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">锡贡磬错。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:&ldquo;治玉石曰错。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 适于磨光或磨快刀具的石头;磨石 <span class="encs">[grindstone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">它山之石，可以为错。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鹤鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 锉刀。磋治骨角铜铁的工具 <span class="encs">[file]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:错刀(还作古钱币名。王莽时所铸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 错误 <span class="encs">[error;mistake]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:错事(错误的事);错缪(谬误，差误。也作错谬);错见(失算);错上了坟(看错了人)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>错</strong></p>
<p>錯 <span class="dicpy">cu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 杂乱;交错 <span class="encs">[in disorder;alternate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觥筹交错。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而境界危恶，层见错出，非人世所堪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:错文(交错为文);错落(不一致);错合(交互配合)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 坏的;差的 <span class="encs">[bad]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这件事办得还不错</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">錯</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“错”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/錯">錯</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=金" target="_blank">金</a>】
                              錯·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/932F.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕䥘【唐韻】【集韻】倉各切【韻會】淸各切【正韻】七各切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音厝。【說文】金涂也。【前漢&middot;食貨志】錯刀，以黃金錯其文，一刀直五千，與五銖錢<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>行。【註】錯刀，王莽錢。【張衡詩】何以贈之金錯刀。</p><p>又【詩&middot;大雅】簟茀錯衡。【傳】錯衡，文衡也。【史記&middot;趙世家】剪髮文身，錯臂左衽。【註】錯臂亦文身，謂以丹靑錯畫其臂。</p><p>又【玉篇】鑢也。【書&middot;禹貢】錫貢磬錯。【疏】治玉石曰錯。磬有以玉爲之者，磬錯，謂治磬錯也。</p><p>又【正韻】厲石也。【詩&middot;小雅】他山之石，可以爲錯。【傳】錯，石也，可以琢玉。【釋文】錯，厲石也。</p><p>又【玉篇】雜也。【書&middot;禹貢】厥賦惟上上錯。【傳】錯，雜出。【疏】交錯是閒雜之義，故錯爲雜也。</p><p>又亂也。【尚書&middot;孔安國序】錯亂磨滅，勿可復知。</p><p>又【易&middot;繫辭】錯綜其數。【疏】錯謂交錯。【禮&middot;文王世子】禮樂交錯于中。</p><p>又【祭義】不錯則隨。【註】錯，鴈行也。父黨隨行，兄黨鴈行。【詩&middot;小雅】獻醻交錯。【傳】東西爲交，邪行爲錯。</p><p>又【集韻】乖也。【增韻】舛也，誤也。【五代史&middot;羅紹威傳】帥魏博牙軍驕恣，盡殺之，由此勢弱，曰：聚六州四十二縣鐵，鑄一个錯不成。【註】借義，喻錯悞也。</p><p>又【前漢&middot;五行志】宣元之後，劉向治穀梁春秋，數其旤福，傳以洪範，與仲舒錯。【註】錯，謂牴牾不合也。</p><p>又【廣韻】厠也，言相閒厠也。</p><p>又【禮&middot;中庸】辟如四時之錯行。【朱註】錯，猶迭也。</p><p>又【易&middot;離卦】履錯然。【註】錯然，敬愼之貌。</p><p>又【馬融&middot;廣成頌】嵎峗錯崔。【註】錯崔，高峻貌。</p><p>又【山海經】帶山有獸，其狀如馬，一角，有錯。【註】言角有甲錯也。</p><p>又神名。【後漢&middot;禮儀志】錯斷食巨。【註】逐疫之神也。</p><p>又姓。【廣韻】宋太宰之後。</p><p>又人名。【史記&middot;高祖功臣年表】稾侯<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>錯。【索隱註】漢表作鍇，音揩。</p><p>又蟲名。【字彙補】莎雞，幽州人謂之蒲錯。</p><p>又與厝通。【前漢&middot;地理志】五方雜厝，風俗不純。【註】厝、錯古通。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>倉故切，音措。【集韻】金塗謂之錯。亦姓。本作䥄。</p><p>又【博雅】藏也。</p><p>又【楚辭&middot;九章】萬民之生，各有所錯兮。【註】錯，安也。</p><p>又【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】展采錯事。【註】展其官職，設厝其事業也。錯，音措。</p><p>又【易&middot;繫辭】苟錯諸地，而可矣。【疏】錯，置也。【釋文】錯，音措。【史記&middot;周本紀】成康之際，天下安寧，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>錯四十餘年不用。【註】錯，置也。</p><p>又【史記&middot;張儀傳】秦魏之交，可錯矣。【註】錯，停止也。音措。</p><p>又【後漢&middot;寒朗傳】二人錯愕不能對。【註】錯，七故反。錯憚，猶倉卒也。</p><p>又【揚子&middot;方言】滅也。周秦曰錯。【註】音酢。</p><p>又與醋同。【管子&middot;弟子職】置醬錯食。</p><p>又【集韻】七約切，音碏。物理麤也。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
