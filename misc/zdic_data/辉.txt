						<p>● <strong>辉</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">輝</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">huī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>闪射的光彩：光～。满室生～。～煌。</li>
	<li>照耀：～映。～耀。～照。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> brightness, luster, brilliance</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Glanz, heller Schein</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> splendeur,éclat,briller (sur)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>辉</strong></p>
<p>輝、煇 <span class="dicpy">huī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从光。军声。本义:光;光辉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[light;radiance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">煇，光也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庭燎有煇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;庭燎》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故德煇动于内。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">观国之煇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;韦玄成传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辉辉(光耀的样子);辉烂(光辉灿烂);辉风(光辉的风范);辉容(光辉的仪容)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 晨光 <span class="encs">[the light of the early morning sun;dawn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝旦为辉，日中为光。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;魏志》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>辉</strong></p>
<p>輝 <span class="dicpy">huī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> 产生光彩;照耀 <span class="encs">[shine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">石韫玉而山辉，水怀珠而川媚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《文赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辉丽(照亮);辉张(发扬光大);辉焕(照耀，映照)</span></p>
