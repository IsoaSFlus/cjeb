						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>隆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>盛大，厚，程度深：～冬。～重（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>兴（<span class="dicpy">xīng </span>）盛：兴～。～盛（<span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>高，高起：～起。～穹。～准（高鼻梁）。</li>
	<li>尊崇：～师。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> prosperous, plentiful, abundant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> groß, erhaben, feierlich  (Adj)​,stark, gewaltig  (Adj)​,Long  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> proéminent,profond,grandiose,solennel,prospère,intense,proéminent</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">隆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>隆</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从生，降省声。本义:高)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 丰大;高 <span class="encs">[grand;high]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隆，丰大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宛中隆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释山》</span>。注:&ldquo;山中央高。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽隆薛之城。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隆准而龙颜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">段干木之隆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;秋期贤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">降冲以攻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德隆望尊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:隆穷(隆起的样子);隆颓(高低不平的样子);隆岳(高山);隆恩(皇帝给予的特大的恩典)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 国家逢勃发展;隆盛 <span class="encs">[prosperous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陛下都</span><span class="diczx2">洛阳</span>，岂欲与 <span class="diczx2">周</span>室比隆哉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;刘敬叔孙通列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>室之隆。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兴隆(兴盛);隆替(兴废);隆替无步(即好坏各不相关)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 丰厚 <span class="encs">[rich;abundant]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:隆杀(指尊卑、厚薄之别);隆眄(即厚顾，器重);隆爱(厚爱);隆遇(优厚的待遇)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9686.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】力中切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】良中切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音癃。【說文】豐大也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】中央高也。【爾雅&middot;釋山】宛中隆。【疏】山形中央蘊聚而高者名隆。</p>
<p>又【戰國策】雖隆薛之城到於天，猶之無益也。</p>
<p>又與窿通。【正韻】穹窿，天勢。本作隆。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】穹隆雲橈。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】盛也。【禮&middot;檀弓】道隆則從而隆，道汙則從而汙。【史記&middot;封禪書】文王攺制，爰周郅隆。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】迫隆冬至。【註】隆冬，猶言盛冬也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;祭義】頒禽隆諸長者。【註】隆，猶多也。</p>
<p>又【荀子&middot;致士篇】君者，國之隆也。【註】隆，猶尊也。【史記&middot;平準書】方隆貴用事。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;王莽傳】臣莽夙夜隆就孺子。【註】隆，長也。言成就之，使其長大也。</p>
<p>又厚也。【後漢&middot;皇后傳】使後世不見隆薄進退之隙。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;五行志】沛城鐵官鑄鐵，鐵不下，隆隆如雷聲。</p>
<p>又地名。【史記&middot;晉世家】齊伐魯，取隆。【註】隆，卽龍也。魯地有隆山。</p>
<p>又州名。後周陵州，宋升隆州。</p>
<p>又豐隆，雷師。【屈原&middot;離騷】吾令豐隆乗雲兮，求宓妃之所在。【正韻】靊霳，本作豐隆。</p>
<p>又與南通。【淮南子&middot;俶眞訓】終南作終隆。</p>
<p>又叶盧王切，音郞。【道藏歌】但聞仙道貴，不聞鬼道隆。謠歌參天氣，賈生元正章。</p>
<p>又叶閭承切，音棱。【陸雲&middot;贈孫世顯詩】制動以靜，祕景在隆。雲根可棲，樂此隈岑。</p>
