                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>度</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dù.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>计算长短的器具或单位：尺～。刻～。～量衡。</li><li>事物所达到的境界：程～。高～。风～。</li><li>分角的单位，一圆周角分为360度：角～。</li><li>依照计算的一定标准划分的单位：温～。湿～。经～。纬～。浓～。</li><li>电能的单位，一千瓦小时电量的通称。</li><li>法则，应遵行的标准：制～。法～。</li><li>哲学上指一定事物保持自己质的数量界限。</li><li>能容受的量：气～。</li><li>考虑，打算：置之～外。</li><li>过，由此到彼：～日。～假。欢～新春。</li><li>量词，次：一～。再～。</li><li>僧尼道士劝人出家：剃～（剃发出家）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>度</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">duó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duó.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨㄛˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 计算，推测：忖～。揣～。审时～势。～德量力。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">度</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>度</strong> <span class="dicpy">dù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从又，庶省声。<span class="yhcs">“又”</span>即手，古代多用手、臂等来测量长度。本义:计量长短的标准，尺码)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[linear measure;size;measures]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度，法制也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，五度，分寸尺丈引也。度起于人手取法，故从又。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">同律度量衡。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度然后知长短。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执鞭度守门。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司市》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾忘持度。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:尺度</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 法制;法度 <span class="encs">[rule;law]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公室无度。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度不可改。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九曰以度教节。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司徒》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓宫室车服之制。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度作刑以诘四方。——<span class="smcs">《书·吕刑》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“法度也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:度轨(法度轨范);度矩(法度;法则)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 限度，限额 <span class="encs">[limit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生之有时而用之亡度，则物力必屈。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span
