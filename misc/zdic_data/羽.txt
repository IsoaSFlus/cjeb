                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>羽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˇ</span></p><ol><li>鸟的毛：～毛。～绒。～书。～衣。～扇。～族（指鸟类）。～旗。～檄（羽书）。</li><li>鱼类或昆虫的翅膀，道教传说中指会飞的仙人：～翼。～翰（翅膀）。～士（a.会飞的仙人；b.道士）。</li><li>箭上的羽毛，借指箭：～猎。</li><li>古代五音之一，相当于简谱“6”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">羽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>羽</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象羽毛形，即鸟的长翎( <span class="dicpy">líng</span>)形。<span class="yhcs">“羽”</span>是汉字的一个部首，从<span class="yhcs">“羽”</span>的字多与羽毛有关。本义:鸟毛，特指鸟的长毛)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[feather]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">羽，鸟长毛也。象形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">羽虫三百六十，而凤为之长。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语·执辔》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">教羽舞。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·舞师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初献六羽。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">染羽。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·钟氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剑盾为羽旗，车为龙旗。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·旗帜》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:羽葆鼓吹(仪仗乐队);羽葆翠盖(以翠羽连缀为饰的华丽车盖);羽葆花旌(仪仗旗帜);羽仪廊庙(指受到朝廷重视，其德行为满朝文武之楷模);羽旆(用羽毛装饰的旌旗);羽佩(以翠羽为饰的佩带)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鸟类 <span class="encs">[bird]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡人之有</span><span class="diczx2">仲父</span>也，犹飞鸿之有羽翼也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·霸形》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:羽族(泛指鸟类);羽物(鸟类);羽皮(鸟兽的毛皮);羽翔(首翼色青的鸾鸟);羽翮(泛指鸟类);羽类(鸟类)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 鸟虫的翅膀 <span class="encs">[wing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">双燕戏云崖，羽翰始差池。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《咏双燕》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:羽肢(肢膀);羽翎(鸟翼)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 喻相辅翼 <span class="encs">[assistance]</span>。如:羽翮已就(比喻辅佐的人甚多，势力已巩固。羽翼已成);羽翮(比喻辅翼或辅佐者);羽翼(辅佐;维护;辅佐的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 古代箭杆上的羽毛。亦指箭 <span class="encs">[arrow]</span>。如:羽队(背着弓箭的队伍);羽箭(箭。因尾部缀鸟羽，故称);羽镞(指箭)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古代用雉羽制成的舞具，文舞者所持 <span class="encs">[plume]</span>。如:羽翟(瞿羽所制的舞具);羽舞(古代一种文舞，舞者执羽)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 指旌旗 <span class="encs">[flag]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翠凤翔文螭，羽节朝玉帝。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span
