                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>闽</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">閩</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄣˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 中国福建省的别称。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fujian province; a river; a tribe</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Abkürzung für Fujian Provinz (Eig, Geo)​,Reich Min (eins der Zehn Köngsreiche im historischen China, 909-945)​ (Eig, Gesch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> autre nom de la province de Fujian</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>闽</strong></p><p>閩 <span class="dicpy">mǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，门声。本义:古种族名。生活在浙江南部和福建一带，后因称福建为闽)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[another name for Fujian Province]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">閩，东南越也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">七閩九貉。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·职方式》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">閩在海中。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·海内经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">选自閩禺。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:闽中(泛指福建省地);闽江(水名。为福建省第一大河流);闽关(指福建通内地的关隘);闽岭(福建北部的山岭)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 五代十国之一 <span class="encs">[Min state]</span>。933年王延钧在长乐(今福州市)称帝，国号闽。历三十七年，后被南唐所灭</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/闽北话">闽北话</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/闽南话">闽南话</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
