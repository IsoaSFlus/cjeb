                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>尚</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄤˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>还（<span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i </span>），仍然：～小。～未。～不可知。</li>	<li>尊崇，注重：～武。～贤（①崇尚贤人；②《墨子》篇名，内容阐述墨子的一种政治主张）。</li>	<li>社会上共同遵从的风俗、习惯等：风～。时～。</li>	<li>矜夸，自负：自～其功。</li>	<li>古，久远：&ldquo;故乐之所由来者～矣，非独为一世之所造也&rdquo;。</li>	<li>庶几，差不多：～飨（希望死者来享用祭品之意）。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尚</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从八，向声。本义:尚且)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[still;yet;even]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚，曾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚无为。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;免爰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚求其雌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小弁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦尚一人之庆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;秦誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">元济</span>尚寝，笑曰:&ldquo;俘囚为盗耳，晓当尽戮之。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣以布衣之交尚不相欺，况大国乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尚有不少;尚来得及;尚未娶亲;尚待研究;尚兀自(还;尚且);尚尔(尚且这样);尚自(尚且);尚然(尚且);尚犹(犹，还)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 还;仍然 <span class="encs">[still;yet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">赵王</span>使使(派使者)视 <span class="diczx2">廉颇</span>尚可用否。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如仆尚何言哉?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 希望 <span class="encs">[wish]</span> 表示命令或希望</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸣呼哀哉!伏惟尚飨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《为韦京兆祭太常崔少卿文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 差不多 <span class="encs">[nearly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">灵王</span>卜曰:&ldquo;余尚得天下。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 往上;向上。通&ldquo;上&rdquo; <span class="encs">[upper;up]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尚论(往上追论古人的行事。尚，通上);尚同(墨子的重要思想之一。即在政体中，下位者，必须对上服从，必须向上认同)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尚</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 尚假借为&ldquo;上&rdquo;，尊崇<span class="encs">[worship;revere]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚，上也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以其上古之书谓之尚书。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;孔安国序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚有晋国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学者多称五帝，尚矣!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;五帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚论古之人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;万章下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚三王只。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;大招》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚左。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;觐礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宣德</span>间，宫中尚促织之戏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且五方土音，乡俗好尚，习见习闻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:崇尚(尊重;推崇);尚茅茨(崇尚茅屋);尚齿(尊崇年老的人);尚年(尊崇年长者);尚左(以左为尊);尚右(以右为尊);尚白(崇尚白色)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 重视 <span class="encs">[pay attention to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣欲使士大夫尊尚武勇，讲习兵法。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尚文(重视文治);尚气(重气节，重义气;负气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 仰慕 <span class="encs">[hold sb. in high esteem]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚前良之遗风兮，恫后辰而无及。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄曲》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 佑，佑助 <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">象曰:包荒得尚于中行，以光大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;泰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 给帝王管理事物 <span class="encs">[administer (things for emperor)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今日堂有炼珍，朝分尚食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尚食(掌帝王膳食);尚宝(明代官名。掌理宝玺、金银、符牌等事);尚衣(古官名。掌管帝王衣服);尚席(官名。掌理宫中筵席);尚宫(宫中女官名。为宫官的首长，掌管导引皇后及闺阁廪赐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 夸耀 <span class="encs">[show off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子不自大其事，不自尚其功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 超过;高出 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得闻先生之余论，则</span><span class="diczx2">大庭氏</span>何以尚兹?&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 奉;承 <span class="encs">[present]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿赐问而自进兮，得尚君之玉音。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;司马相如&middot;长门赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 仰攀婚姻 <span class="encs">[match]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">卓王孙</span>喟然而叹，自以得使女尚 <span class="diczx2">司马长卿</span>晚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 加上，往&hellip;上补充东西 <span class="encs">[increase]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚之以琼华乎而。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;著》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尚絅(穿锦衣时加上罩袍，以使华美不显耀于外)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 荐举;选拔 <span class="encs">[value highly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚者，<span class="smcs">《五制》</span>:&ldquo;上贤以崇德&rdquo;，&ldquo;上贤谓举贤也&rdquo;。上与尚通。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span><span class="diczx2">王念孙</span>疏证</p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 喜欢;爱好 <span class="encs">[like]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尚诙谑(喜欢戏谑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 娶帝王之女为妻 <span class="encs">[marry a princess]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸男皆尚</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>公主。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;李斯列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尚主(娶公主为妻)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=小" target="_blank">小</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5C19.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】時亮切【正韻】時樣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音上。【說文】曾也，庶幾也。从八向聲。【爾雅註】郉昺曰：尙，謂心所希望也。【詩·小雅】不尙息焉。【書·大禹謨】爾尙一乃心力。</p><p>又【廣韻】加也，飾也。【論語】好仁。　無以尙之。【詩·齊風】充耳以素乎而，尙之以瓊華乎而。</p><p>又崇也，貴也。【禮·檀弓】夏后氏尙黑，殷人尙白，周人尙赤。</p><p>又主也。　　司進御之物者皆曰尙。【漢官儀】尙食，尙醫，尙方等是也。</p><p>又尙書，主大計。</p><p>又【增韻】尊也。【詩·大雅】維師尙父。【註】太公望，太師而尊爲尙父者也。</p><p>又猶也。【詩·大雅】雖無老成人，尙有典<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>。</p><p>又娶公主謂之尙。言帝王之女尊，而尙之，不敢言娶。【前漢·王吉傳】娶天子女曰尙公主，娶諸侯女曰承翁主，尙承皆<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>下之名。一曰配也。【司馬相如傳】卓王孫自以使女得尙司馬長卿晚。【註】尙，配也。義與尙公主同。</p><p>又【易·泰卦】得尙乎中行。【註】謂合乎中行之道也。</p><p>又奉也。【司馬相如·長門賦】願賜問而自進兮，得尙君之玉音。</p><p>又矜伐也。【禮·表記】君子不自尙其功。</p><p>又姓。戰國尙靳，唐尙衡。</p><p>又與上通。【詩·魏風】上愼旃哉，猶來無止。【註】上猶尙也，言愼之可以來歸，無止於彼也。【尚書序】尚者，上也。言此上代以來書，故曰尚書。</p><p>又叶辰羊切，音常。【詩·大雅】肆皇天弗尚。叶亡方。</p>
