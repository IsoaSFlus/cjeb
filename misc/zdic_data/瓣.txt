                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>瓣</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>组成花冠的各片：花～。</li>
	<li>植物的种子、果实或球茎可以分开的片状物：豆～儿。蒜～儿。</li>
	<li>物体破裂分成的部分。</li>
	<li>量词。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> petal; segment; valves</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Blumenblatt (S, Bio)​,Klappe, Lamelle (S, Bio)​,Scherbe, Bruchstück (S, Tech)​,ZEW Segment, Zehe, Stück (z.B. Knoblauchzehe, Apfelstück)​ (Zähl)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> morceau,segment,section,fragment,pièce,pétale,gousse,(classificateur pour les pièces, segments, etc.)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瓣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瓣</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从瓜，辡( <span class="dicpy">biǎn</span>)声。本义:瓜类的籽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[melon seeds]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瓣，瓜中实也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瓠栖瓣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。按，今曰瓜子仁。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水中有甘蔗，及梅李核瓜瓣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">谢惠连</span><span class="smcs">《祭古冢文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 瓜果的分瓤 <span class="encs">[pulp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朱橘香苞数瓣分。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《贬江陵途中寄乐天》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:橘瓣，瓜瓣</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 组成花冠的各片 <span class="encs">[petal]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:花瓣</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 植物的种子、果实或球茎可以分开的片状物 <span class="encs">[segment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">面如桃瓣，目若秋波。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:豆瓣;蒜瓣</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 物体自然地分成或破碎后分成的部分 <span class="encs">[section]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:七棱八瓣;摔成几瓣</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瓣</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用于花瓣、叶片或种子、果实、球茎分开的小块儿 <span class="encs">[segment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">种柳，先于土坑中置蒜一瓣，甘草一寸，永不生虫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《格物粗谈》</span></span></p>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">瓜類的種子。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;瓜部》：「<b>瓣</b>，瓜中實也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;傅玄〈瓜賦〉：「細肌密理，多瓤少<b>瓣</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;干寶《搜神記&middot;卷一》：「（徐光）​從人乞瓜，其主勿與。便從索<b>瓣</b>，杖地種之。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">瓜果或球莖等中有薄膜隔開或可依其紋理分開的部分。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「橘<b>瓣</b>」、「蒜<b>瓣</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">花片。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「花<b>瓣</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《警世通言&middot;卷三&middot;王安石三難蘇學士》：「他也不曉得黃州菊花落<b>瓣</b>，也怪他不得！」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">瓣膜的簡稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「三尖<b>瓣</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞：➊ ​ 計算花瓣、葉片或水果、球莖等所分成小塊的單位。如：「這個橘子一共有十二瓣。」➋ ​ 計算物件成為瓣狀或破碎後的部分的單位。如：「將蘋果切成四瓣。」、「這瓷瓶被摔成好幾瓣。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
