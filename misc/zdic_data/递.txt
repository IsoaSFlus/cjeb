                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">递</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>递</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">遞</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>传送，传达：传～。投～。～送。～交。～眼色（以目示意）。呈～国书。</li>
	<li>顺着次序：～补。～变。～增。～减。～升。～降（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>古代指驿车。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hand over, deliver; substitute</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> überreichen; aushändigen; übergeben  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> remettre,passer,transmettre,par ordre,successivement</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">递</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>递</strong></p>
<p>遞 <span class="dicpy">d&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，虒( <span class="dicpy">sī</span>)声。本义:轮流;交替)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[take turns;do sth.in turn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">递，更易也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国之递甚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策六》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二八侍宿射递代些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诈术递用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;先巳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">合场递进。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">傅毅</span><span class="smcs">《舞赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日月递炤(照)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:递嬗(不断地更迭、变化);递代(互相替换);递衣(轮流换穿同一件衣服);递兴(交替兴起);递迁(更易变化)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 传;传送 <span class="encs">[pass;hand over;transmit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:恭敬地递上;递钟(传杯，把盏);递传帖(递送帖子);递局(递眼色;示意);递夫(古代在驿站间运送官方货物的役仆);递铺(传送官方文书或货物的驿站);递禀(向上级呈关报告);递盏(传杯饮酒);递盗(转移偷盗之物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指通过驿站传递公文、货物等 <span class="encs">[deliver]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驿传旧有三等，曰步递，马递，急脚递。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:递运(转运;驿站运输)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 押送 <span class="encs">[escort]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:递解还乡(押送犯人返回原籍);递发(挥送);递流(放逐)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>递</strong></p>
<p>遞 <span class="dicpy">d&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>顺次地;一个一个地 <span class="encs">[in proper order;successively]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">向使</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>复爱六国之人，则递三世可至万世而为君，谁得而族灭也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:递加;递减;递相(顺次，一个接一个，各自);递转(依次转动);递演(逐步演变);递毁(依次毁废);递化(顺次改变);递日(一日接一日)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>递</strong></p>
