						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>笃</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">篤</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨˇ</span></p><ol><li>忠实，一心一意：～学。～信。～志。～情。～厚。</li><li>厚实，结实：“彼其之子，硕大且～”。</li><li>病沉重：病～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>笃</strong></p><p>篤 <span class="dicpy">dǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，竹声。本义:马行迟顿)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(of horse) slow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">笃，马行顿迟也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">笃癃无家属。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·先武纪》</span>。注引<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“笃，困也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:笃么(笃笃末末，笃笃寞寞。转来转去，徘徊不前)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 忠实，不虚伪 <span class="encs">[sincere]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朋友不笃，非孝也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·孝行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慎思之，明辨之，笃行之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">笃公刘。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·公刘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尧九男，皆益笃。——<span class="smcs">《史记·五帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:笃人(忠实厚道的人);笃仁(笃实而仁厚);笃亮(笃实亮直);笃美(笃实美善);笃敏(诚笃而敏锐);笃教(竭诚于教化);笃烈(诚厚刚正)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> <span class="encs">[病势]</span>沉重 <span class="encs">[serious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孙权</span>病笃。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:笃速速(笃簌簌。发抖、哆嗦的样子);笃疾(疾病沉危);笃剧(指病情沉重危急);笃癃(重或残疾之人);笃患(严重的病患);笃灾(严重的灾害)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 厚 <span class="encs">[thick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">笃，厚也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释诂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">硕大且笃。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·椒聊》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予嘉乃德，日笃不忘。——<span class="smcs">《书·微子之命》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 专一 <span class="encs">[single-minded]</span>。如:笃慎(专心谨慎);笃向(专诚向往);笃重(专一深重);笃习(专一于学习);笃意(专心致意)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 切实;确凿 <span class="encs">[reliable;true]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">论笃是与，君子者乎?——<span class="smcs">《论语·先进》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:笃论(确当的言论);笃见(确切的见解)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 丰厚 <span class="encs">[rich and generous]</span>。如:笃生(上天独厚于其人，使生而不同于凡常);笃降(笃生);笃顾(犹厚念);笃雅(厚重典雅);笃睦(淳厚和睦)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 〈方〉∶安稳;确定 <span class="encs">[stable]</span>。如:笃悠悠;笃笃定定</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 深厚 <span class="encs">[deep]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仆道不笃，业甚浅近。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《答韦中立论师道书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 又如:笃好(交情深厚);笃正(纯厚正直);笃古(纯厚古朴;好古);笃至(深厚到了极点);笃尚(纯厚高尚);笃恭(纯厚恭敬)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>笃</strong></p><p>篤 <span class="dicpy">dǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǔ.mp3"></a></span>
