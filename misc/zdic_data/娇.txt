                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">娇</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>娇</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">嬌</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>美好可爱：～儿。～女。～艾（年轻貌美的女子）。～娆。～艳。～嗔。～逸（潇洒俊美）。</li>
	<li>爱怜过甚，过分珍惜：～养。～惯。</li>
	<li>柔弱：～弱。～小。～嫩。～气。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> seductive and loveable; tender</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> zart, empfindlich  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> charmant,tendre,délicat,exigeant,gâter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">娇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>娇</strong></p>
<p>嬌 <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，乔声。本义:妩媚可爱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(柔嫩美态) <span class="encs">[sweet and charming]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娇，姿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娇，女字，亦态。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娇，娇姿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娇逸未有婚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自在娇莺恰恰啼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江畔独步寻花》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娇儿恶卧踏里裂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">娇姿欲滴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:娇影(娇美动人的影子);娇娇痴痴(故作娇痴之态);娇婢侈童(娇贵奢侈的婢女童仆);娇羞(妩媚含羞的样子);娇娥(美丽的妇女);娇逸(俊美飘逸);娇颜(美丽的容貌);娇冶(艳丽);娇慵(娇懒;精神不振);娇奶奶(娇惰的富家妇人);娇懒(懒散倦怠);娇喘(娇柔嫩细的喘声);娇喉(柔美的歌喉);娇语(柔声细语);娇脆(嫩脆);娇狞(婉转而细弱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 柔弱 <span class="encs">[delicate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侍儿扶起娇无力。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《长恨歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:娇怯(柔美;柔弱);娇妍(柔美);娇荑(喻柔嫩纤细的手指);娇怯怯(柔弱的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 任性 <span class="encs">[capricious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小姐，你性儿忒惯得娇了。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王实甫</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 同&ldquo;骄&rdquo;。骄横 <span class="encs">[over-bearing]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:娇傲(骄傲;受宠而倨傲);娇奢(骄横奢侈);娇阳(骄阳);娇妇(娇横之妻)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>娇</strong></p>
<p>嬌 <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指年轻女子。对美女的称谓 <span class="encs">[beauty]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:娇蝉(女子的鬓发);娇艾(美貌的少女);娇姝(美人);娇倩(指少女);娇淑(指美人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 对儿童的称谓 <span class="encs">[child]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜相思，投壶不停箭，忆欢作娇时。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>娇</strong></p>
