data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，木声。本义:洗头发)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wash one's hair;shampoo]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沐，濯发也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予发曲局，薄言归沫。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采绿》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共王之沐浴。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·宫人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沐则心覆。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸侯皆有汤沐之邑也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·隐公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫子助之沫椁。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且沐者，去首垢也，洗去足垢，盥去手垢，浴去身垢，皆去一形之垢，其实等也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·讥日》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">新沐者必弹冠，新浴者必振衣。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:沐发(洗发);沐澡(洗头洗澡);沐濯(洗头洗澡);沐芳(用香草水洗头);沐巾(洗发用的巾);沐盆(盥洗盆);栉风沐雨</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 芟除 <span class="encs">[remove]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沐涂树之枝。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:沐树(芟除树枝，使之无荫)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 整治 <span class="encs">[renovate]</span>。如:沐椁(整治棺材)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 润泽 <span class="encs">[moisten]</span>。如:沐日浴月(受日月光华的润泽)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 受润泽。引申为蒙受 <span class="encs">[suffer]</span>。如:沐恩(清代官场中下级对上级的自称，意为沐浴着上级恩惠之人);沐泽(蒙受恩泽);沐浴;沐仁浴义(受仁义的熏陶);沐露沾霜(饱经霜露辛苦)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>沐</strong> <span class="dicpy">mù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指休假 <span class="encs">[take a holiday]</span>。如:沐日(休假日)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 米汁。古人常用作洗头之用 <span class="encs">[thin rice gruel]</span>。如:沐粱(用淘粱的汁水洗头发);沐稷(用淘洗稷的汁水洗头发)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 水名。即中国山东省弥河
