						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>感</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>觉出：～触。～觉。～性。～知（客观事物通过感觉器官在人脑中的直接反映）。～官。</li>
	<li>使在意识、情绪上起反应；因受刺激而引起的心理上的变化：～动。～想。反～。好～。情～。敏～。～染。～召。～慨。～喟。～叹。自豪～。～人肺腑。百～交集。</li>
	<li>对人家的好意表示谢意：～谢。～恩。～激。～愧。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">感</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>感</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，咸声。本义:感动)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。思想感情受外界事物的影响而激动 <span class="encs">[move]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">感，动人心也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">感而遂通，天下之故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;动也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝感其诚，命</span><span class="diczx2">夸娥氏</span>二子负二山。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">羊子</span>感其言，复还终业。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:感格(感动，感化);感咽(受感动而悲伤，泣不成声);感涕(感动而涕泪俱下);感慕(感动而思慕);感悦(感动悦服);感人肺腑(使人的内心深受感动);感天动地(感动天地);感哽(感动得泣不成声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 感应，影响 <span class="encs">[response]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:感制(感应制约);感会(感应会合);感验(应验);感变(感应变动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 感触;触动感慨 <span class="encs">[touch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天地感而万物化生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;成》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">登斯楼也，则有去国怀乡，忧谗畏讥，满目萧然，感极而悲者也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:感目(触目);感事(受外界事物的触动);感逆(触犯，冒犯);感悔(受到触动而悔改)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 感谢;感激 <span class="encs">[thank;feel grateful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新妇谓府吏:&ldquo;感君区区怀!&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">感荷君子德，恍若乘朽栈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《赠张籍》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">远辱专人惠书，辅以药物，极济所乏，衰疾有赖矣，感刻，感刻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《与张元明书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:感刻(深深感激);感荷(感谢，感佩);感篆(感激而铭记在心);感仰(感戴敬仰);感喜(感激喜悦);感颂(感激颂扬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 感慨，感伤 <span class="encs">[sigh with emotion]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">感时花溅泪，恨别鸟惊心。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春望》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至于长者之抱才而困，则又令我怆然有感。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:感忾(感叹。同&ldquo;感慨&rdquo;);感切(伤感凄切);感时(感慨时事的变迁或时势的变化);感悼(感伤哀悼，伤感);感恻(感伤悲痛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 思念 <span class="encs">[miss]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">感物众而思深。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">何晏</span><span class="smcs">《景福殿赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹思也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:感思(思念);感逝(感念往昔);感逝山阳(表示对亡友的哀念)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 感染，感受。多用于疾病 <span class="encs">[infect;be affected by]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太夫人并无别症，偶感了些风寒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:感风(谓中风;宋代太学诸生请假外宿，例以&ldquo;感风&rdquo;为辞);感疾(染病);感通(有感于此而通于彼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 迷惑 <span class="encs">[puzzle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使人不能执一者，物感之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通&ldquo;减&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span>)。减损，减少 <span class="encs">[lessen;decrease]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其满为感，其虚为亡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;侈靡》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 感触;情绪;意念 <span class="encs">[thoughts and feelings]</span>。</p>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對別人所給的好處表示謝意。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「感恩」、「感激」、「銘感五內」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;張華&middot;答何劭詩二首之二》：「是用感嘉貺，寫心出中誠。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;歐陽修〈代贈田文初〉詩：「感君一顧重千金，贈君白璧為妾心。」</span></p></li>
