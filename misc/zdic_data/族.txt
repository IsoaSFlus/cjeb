                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">族</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>族</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zú.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>亲属，泛指同姓之亲：～规。～长。家～。</li><li>指具有共同起源和共同遗传特征的人群：种～。</li><li>指在历史上形成的人群的稳定共同体，他们有共同的语言、经济生活以及表现于共同文化上的心理素质：民～。</li><li>聚居而有血统关系的人群的统称：宗～。氏～。部～。</li><li>事物有共同属性的一大类：芳香～。</li><li>封建时代的一种残酷刑罚，一人有罪，把全家或包括母亲、妻家的人都杀死：～灭。</li><li>聚合，集中：云气不待～而雨。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a family clan, ethnic group, tribe</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Familie, Volksgruppe, Klasse (gesellschaftlich)​, Nationalität  (S, Pol)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> clan,ethnie,famille</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">族</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>族</strong> <span class="dicpy">zú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。本义:箭头)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[arrowhead]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">族，矢锋也。束之族族也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 家族，氏族 <span class="encs">[clan]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">克明俊德，以亲九族。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">陆德明</span>释文:<span class="yhcs">“上自高祖，下至玄孙，凡九族。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:大族(人口多、分支繁的家族);族氏(宗族姓氏);族正(清时聚族而居的地方;挑选族中地位名望最高的人主持和裁决本族事务，名为<span class="yhcs">“族正”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓氏，表明家族、宗族系统的称号 <span class="encs">[surname]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亡</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>族矣。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其诗以养父母、收族为意。(收:聚，团结)——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《伤仲永》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">繁衍之族。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:族地(一姓一族的土地);族姓(同姓的亲族;家族姓氏;世族大姓)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 民族 <span class="encs">[nationality]</span>。如:汉族;回族;维吾尔族</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古代百家为一族 <span class="encs">[a hundred families]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四闾为族，使之相葬。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“闾，二十五家;族，百家。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:族长</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 品类，种类 <span class="encs">[species]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士大夫之族。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫不族布。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 动植物分类学中相当于亚群或子群，或在此之下，但经常次于亚科的单位;亦指不按分类学的自然群 <span class="encs">[tribe]</span>。如:猫族;玫瑰族</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 筋骨交错聚结的地方 <span class="encs">[knot]</span></p><p><span
