                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">伦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>伦</strong></p><p>倫 <span class="dicpy">lún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，仑( <span class="dicpy">lún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lún.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:辈，类)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[kind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伦，辈也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人伦并处。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">儗人必于其伦。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毛犹有伦。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">廉颇</span><span class="diczx2">赵奢</span>之伦。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:伦辈(伦匹，伦党，伦伍);伦序(流辈;等类);伦表(流辈之上);伦品(等类品第);伦侯(秦爵名。与列侯相类而无封邑);伦望(流辈中的声望);伦族(所属之族)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 条理，顺序 <span class="encs">[order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悌乃知序，序乃伦;伦不腾上，上乃不崩。——<span class="smcs">《逸周书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:伦叙(有条理，有顺序);伦次(条理次序);伦绪(条理秩序)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 人伦，人与人之间的道德关系。伦常，纲纪，封建礼教规定的人与人之间正常关系，特指尊卑长幼之间的关系 <span class="encs">[human relations]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">只是父亲伯叔兄弟之伦，因是圣人遗训，不敢违忤。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲洁其身，而乱大伦。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:天伦(古代指父子、兄弟等关系);伦物(人伦物理);伦经(天道人伦的常则);伦质(人伦道德之理);伦谊(人伦大义)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 道理，义理 <span class="encs">[ration]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我不知其彝伦攸叙。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:伦脊(道理;条理)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 意义 <span class="encs">[meaning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫祭有十伦焉。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·祭统》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“论”</span> <span class="encs">[theory]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有伦有义。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>伦</strong></p><p>倫 <span class="dicpy">lún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 顺其纹理 <span class="encs">[comform to grain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">析开必伦。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 类比，匹敌 <span class="encs">[analogize; be equal in force; be well-matched]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">德伦三五，配皇作烈。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span> <span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《学宫颂》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暴殄天物之罪，岂有伦哉!——</span><span
