                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>围</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">圍</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wéi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wéi.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄟˊ</span></p><ol><li>环绕，四周拦挡起来：～攻。～城。突～。解～。</li><li>圈起来作拦阻或遮挡的东西：～巾。～墙。～裙。</li><li>四周：外～。周～。</li><li>量词（ａ．两手姆指和食指合拢的长度，如“腰大十～”；ｂ．两两臂合拢的长度，如“树大五～”）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">围</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>围</strong></p><p>圍 <span class="dicpy">wéi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wéi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从囗，韦声。<span class="yhcs">“囗”</span>是<span class="yhcs">“围”</span>的古字。本义:环绕)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[enclose;surround]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">囗，回也，象回币之形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“按，围绕， 周围字当用此。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">围，裹也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">范围天地之化。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以其一为之被而围之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·考工记·庐人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:围堑(围绕营垒的壕沟);围圆(圆形围墙);围地(四周山川环绕，形势险峻的地方);围屏(屏风);围落(篱笆，栅栏)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 包围 <span class="encs">[encompass;surround]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之围 <span class="diczx2">邯郸</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·平原君虞卿列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兵围</span><span class="diczx2">邯郸</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">江都</span>围急。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四面围住。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:围歼;围守(包围监守);围取(包围夺取)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 防守 <span class="encs">[defense]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">围，守也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">围不言战。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·庄公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:围落(藩篱。借指防卫)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“违”</span>。违背;离开 <span class="encs">[violate;leave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其功顺天者天助之，其功逆天者天围之。——<span class="smcs">《管子·形势》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则王以春围其农，夏食其食，秋取其刈，冬陈其宝。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·徕民》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>围</strong></p><p>圍 <span class="dicpy">wéi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wéi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 围子，用土、石、荆棘等围成的防御设施 <span class="encs">[defensive wall surrounding village]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敕军营更筑严围。——<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 打猎的围场;围猎 <span class="encs">[exclosure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百官戎服骑从，鼓行入围。——<span class="smcs">《隋书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 封建帝王出外所圈设的禁区 <span class="encs">[forbidden zone]</span></p><p><span
