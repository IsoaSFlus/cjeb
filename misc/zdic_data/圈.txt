                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圈</strong> <span class="dicpy">juān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/juān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 把禽畜关在栅栏里 <span class="encs">[shut in a pen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼻赤象，圈巨狿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张衡&middot;西京赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:把羊群圈起来</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 把犯人拘禁起来 <span class="encs">[put in jail]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:圈闭(禁闭);圈禁(禁闭于空房);圈留(挽留)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 闷在;关闭 <span class="encs">[confine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我只恨天天圈在家里，一点儿做不得主，行动就有人知道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n;quān</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圈</strong> <span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
