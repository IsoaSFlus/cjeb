                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">职</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>职</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">職</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>职务，分（<span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n </span>）内应做的事：～业。～务。～守。～权。天～。尽～。</li>
	<li>职位，执行事务所处的一定地位：～工。～位。～员。～称。以身殉～。</li>
	<li>旧时公文用语，下属对上司的自称：卑～。～当奉命。</li>
	<li>掌管：～掌。</li>
	<li>由于：～此而已。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> duty, profession; office, post</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dienst, Pflicht, Beruf, Amt</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fonctions,poste,devoir,tâche,charge,gérer,se charger de</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">职</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>职</strong></p>
<p>職 <span class="dicpy">zh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耳，只(戠 <span class="dicpy">zh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。&ldquo;职&rdquo;是&ldquo;记&rdquo;的意思。 <span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>:&ldquo;凡言职者，谓其善听也。&rdquo;故从耳。本义:识;记。职为识之本字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[remember]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">职，记微也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">章画职墨兮，前度未改。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 主管;任职 <span class="encs">[be responsible for;be in charge of;take office]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天职生覆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;天端》</span>。注:&ldquo;主也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">职于</span><span class="diczx2">南京</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:职称言路(做谏官);职任民社(管理人民与国家);职客(红、白喜事的主持人);职内(古官名。掌邦赋收入)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 尽职;称职 <span class="encs">[fulfil one&#39;s duty;be competent for one&#39;s job or position]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其不职或贪暴，免归田里，以明赏罚之信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:职办(称职而能办事)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>职</strong></p>
<p>職 <span class="dicpy">zh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 职务;职业;职责 <span class="encs">[duty;job;occupation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸侯朝于天子曰述职。述职者，述所职也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">设官分职。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太宰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自去史职。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">代百司之职。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将尽厥职。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《复庵记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:专职(由专人担任的职务);革职(撤职);职役(犹职事。多指较为低贱的职务);职事僧(寺院中分管各项职务的僧人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 职位;执行事务所 <span class="encs">[post;office]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">解辫清职。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span>梁&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁我京职。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宰相之职。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8077.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】之弋切【集韻】【韻會】質力切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音織。【說文】記微也。【徐曰】國有六職，皆主記事之微也。【增韻】執掌也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】職，主也。【博雅】職，事也。【書&middot;周官】六卿分職，各率其屬，以倡九牧。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;大宰】九曰閒民，無常職。【註】謂無事業者。【史記&middot;張丞相傳】沛公以周昌爲職志。【註】職，主也。志，旗幟也。謂掌旗幟之官也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】職，常也。</p>
<p>又貢也。【左傳&middot;襄二十八年】共其職貢。【淮南子&middot;原道訓】海外賓伏，四夷納職。【註】職，貢也。</p>
<p>又專也。【詩&middot;大雅】民之罔極，職涼善背。【註】專由小人，名爲直諒。而實善背也。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】憐職，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22674.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢙴</span>也。言相<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22674.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢙴</span>憐者，吳越之閒謂之憐職。</p>
<p>又職職，多也。【莊子&middot;至樂篇】萬物職職。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓譜】周禮有職方氏，後因官爲氏。【風俗通】漢山陽令職洪之後。</p>
<p>又【集韻】敵德切，音特。杙也。周官有職人。戚兗讀。或作樴樲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】逸織切，音弋。義同。</p>
<p>又與幟同。【史記&middot;叔孫通傳】於是皇帝輦出房，百官執職傳警。【註】職，音幟。</p>
