                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阚</strong></p>
<p>闞 <span class="dicpy">k&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 望 <span class="encs">[look over]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阚，望也。从门，敢声。望或倚门倚闾，故从门。字亦作瞰，作矙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阚，视也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俯阚海湄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《琴赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:俯阚(俯览，向下看)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 探望，看望 <span class="encs">[visit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>于是疏 <span class="diczx2">河</span>决 <span class="diczx2">江</span>，十年未阚其家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尸子&middot;君治》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阚</strong></p>
<p>闞 <span class="dicpy">K&agrave;n</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Kan village]</span>。在今山东省汶上县</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95DE.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>苦濫切，音瞰。【說文】望也。</p><p>又【博雅】闞，視也。</p><p>又【玉篇】臨也。</p><p>又【廣韻】魯邑。【左傳&middot;昭二十五年】叔孫昭子如闞。【註】闞，魯邑。【穀梁傳&middot;昭三十二年】公在乾侯取闞。</p><p>又亭名。【後漢&middot;郡國志】東平陸，六國時曰平陸，有闞亭。</p><p>又姓。【史記&middot;齊太公世家】闞止有寵焉。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>虎檻切，音㺖。虎聲。【詩&middot;大雅】闞如虓虎。【傳】闞然如虎之怒。【釋文】火斬反。【前漢&middot;敘傳】於是七雄虓闞。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】許鑒切【韻會】胡懺切【正韻】胡監切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音㒈。【廣韻】犬聲。【集韻】獸怒聲。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>虎覽切，音喊。義同。</p>
