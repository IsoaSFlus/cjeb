                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>丫</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yā.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄚˉ</span></p><ol><li>分杈的：树～。～杈。</li><li>像树枝的分杈：～头（ａ．古代女孩子头上梳双髻，如树丫杈，因以称女孩子；ｂ．指受役使的女孩子，亦称“丫鬟”。“<span class="diczx3">头</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">鬟</span>”均读轻声）。脚～子。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>丫</strong> <span class="dicpy">yā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。本义:树木或物体的分叉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bifurcation;fork of a tree]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丫，象物开之形。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span>。按，这是中古所造的字。泛指上端分出的东西。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十个指头八个丫。——<span class="smcs">《五灯会元》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:丫叉(树木两枝分歧处;两手交叉);丫子(物体分叉的地方);丫木(上端分叉的木头);丫枝(枝桠);丫钯(叉草用的农具);丫槎(丫叉，植物的枝丫);丫路(叉路)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 像树枝的分叉，女孩子头上梳着双髻犹如树叉，因即以称女孩子 <span class="encs">[girl]</span>。如:丫头片子(方言。对女孩子的戏称);丫环(婢女)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/丫巴儿">丫巴儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/丫杈">丫杈</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/丫鬟">丫鬟</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/丫髻">丫髻</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/丫角">丫角</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/丫头">丫头</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
