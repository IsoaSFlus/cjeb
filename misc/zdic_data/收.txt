	<li>接到，接受：～发。～信。～支。～讫。～益。</li>
	<li>藏或放置妥当：这是重要东西，要～好了。</li>
	<li>割断成熟的农作物：～割。～成。麦～。</li>
	<li>招回：～兵。～港。</li>
	<li>聚，合拢：～容。～理。～集。</li>
	<li>结束：～尾。～煞。～盘。</li>
	<li>逮捕，拘押：～捕。～监。～押。～审。</li>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">收</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>收</strong></p>
<p>収 <span class="dicpy">shōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，收( <span class="dicpy">jiū</span>)声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[arrest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收，捕也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女反收之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;瞻卬》</span>。传:&ldquo;拘收也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">单于</span>壮其节，朝夕遣人候问 <span class="diczx2">武</span>，而收系张胜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一时收禽，上下肃然，称为政理。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:收领(捉拿);收系(逮捕下狱);收付(拘捕罪犯，交付办案);收考(拘捕考问);收夷(捕杀);收劾(拘捕弹劾);收案(拘捕案问);收掩(收捕);收执(捉拿);收禁(拘押监禁);收领(拘禁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 收拾;收取 <span class="encs">[receive;accept;accomodate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收，取也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收以奔褒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;郑语》</span>。注:&ldquo;取也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勤者有事则收之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span>。疏:&ldquo;谓敛持在手。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">责毕收乎?来何疾也!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必死是间，余收尔骨焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古来白骨无人收。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:收发报;收信;收什(收拾);收局(收拾局面);收捉(方言。收拾;修建);收略(收拾;安排);收解(当铺收进的抵押品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 聚集;收集 <span class="encs">[gather;collect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收，敛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我其收之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;维天之命》</span>。传:&ldquo;聚也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既蜡而收。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪记&middot;郊特牲》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓收敛积聚也。&rdquo;</span></p>
