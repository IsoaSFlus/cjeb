                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>柘</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄜˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>落叶灌木或乔木，树皮有长刺，叶卵形，可以喂蚕，皮可以染黄色，木材质坚而致密，是贵重的木料：～黄。～蚕。～丝。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">蔗</span>”，甘蔗。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">柘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>柘</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 柘树 <span class="encs">[three-bristle cudrania]</span>。落叶灌木或乔木，树皮灰褐色，有长刺，叶子卵形或椭圆形，花小，排列成头状花序，果实球形。叶子可以喂蚕，木材中心为黄色，质坚而致密，是贵重的木料</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 唐教坊舞曲<span class="smcs">《柘枝舞》</span>的省称 <span class="encs">[Zhe-Zhi dance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柘因零落难重舞，莲为单开不并头。——<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:柘袖(柘枝舞者的衣袖);柘舞(柘枝。即柘枝舞);柘枝花帽(舞柘枝时所带的帽子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“蔗”</span>。甘蔗<span class="encs">[sugarcane]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有柘浆些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王夫之</span>云:<span class="yhcs">“柘，与蔗通。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泰尊柘浆析朝酲。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·礼乐志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸柘巴苴。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《子虚赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 注:<span class="yhcs">“诸柘，甘蔗也。”</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
