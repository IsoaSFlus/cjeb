                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>鉴</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鑒</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>镜子。</li>
	<li>照：光可～人。</li>
	<li>观察，审察：～别。～定。～赏。～于（看到，觉察到）。台～（书信用语，表示请人看信。亦作&ldquo;惠鉴&rdquo;、&ldquo;钧鉴&rdquo;）。～往知来。</li>
	<li>可以使人警惕或引为教训的事情：借～。～戒。前车之～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mirror, looking glass; reflect</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Beispiel, Leitbild  (S)​,Messing- oder Bronzespiegel  (S)​,nachdenken, widerspiegeln  (S)​,ermahnen, warnen  (V)​,inspizieren, beschauen  (V)​,prüfen, einsehen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> miroir,avertissement,refléter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鉴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鉴</strong></p>
<p>鑒、鑑、鍳 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，监声。本义:古代用来盛水或冰的青铜大盆)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[basin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鑑，大盆也，一曰鑑诸，可以取明水于月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐灏</span>曰:&ldquo;鑑，古祇作堅，从皿以盛水也。其后范铜为之，而用以照形者，亦谓之鑑，声转为镜。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鑑谓之镜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">借明于鑑以炤之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;主术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我心匪鑒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;柏舟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">卫灵公</span>有妻三人，同鑑而浴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春始治鑑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鉴诸(古代承露取水的器具);鉴燧(方诸和阳燧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 中国古代用青铜制成的镜子 <span class="encs">[ancient bronze mirror]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:龟鉴;鉴台(镜台);鉴明(镜面明净);鉴洼(凹面镜);鉴团(凸面镜);鉴台(镜台，梳妆台)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 借鉴，可以作为警戒或引为教训的事 <span class="encs">[warning]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">槛外诸鉴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:引以为鉴;前车之覆，后车之鉴;鉴戒(以过去的经验为教训)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 编年史的一种名称 <span class="encs">[history]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span>;<span class="smcs">《了凡纲鉴》</span>;鉴书(历史;史书);通鉴;年鉴;图鉴</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>鉴</strong></p>
<p>鑒 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 明察，审查 <span class="encs">[scrutinize]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谿极则不鑒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;适音》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能鉴听。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鉴彻(明察);鉴达人(鉴事人。明察事理的人);鉴貌辨色(察颜观色;看脸色行事);鉴事(明察事理)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 照看 <span class="encs">[reflect]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鉴人(照人);水清可鉴;鉴微(照见细微的东西)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 旧时书信套语，表示请对方看信 <span class="encs">[may I draw your attention to the following]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:某先生台鉴;惠鉴;钧鉴</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 借鉴，鉴戒 <span class="encs">[warn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哀之而不鉴之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9451.svg">                          <p>大盆也。一曰監諸，可以取明水於月。从金監聲。革懺切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9451_7249.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鑑)</span><span class="ywyy">大盆也。</span><span class="swdz">盆者，盎也。凌人：春始治鑑。注云：鑑如甀，大口，以盛冰，置食物於中，以禦温氣。春而始治之。按鄭云如甀，醢人作醢云塗置甀中，則鑑如今之甕。許云大盆，則與鄭說不符，疑許說爲是。且字从金，必以金爲之也。</span><span class="ywyy">从金監聲。</span><span class="swdz">革懺切。八部。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰鑑諸，可㠯取朙水於月。</span><span class="swdz">鑑諸當作鑑方諸也，轉寫奪字耳。周禮司烜氏：以夫遂取明火於日，以鍳取明水於月。注：夫遂，陽遂也。鍳，鏡屬。取水者，世謂之方諸。淮南書：方諸見月則津而爲水。高注：方諸謂陰燧大蛤也，孰摩令<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24360.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤍠</span>。月盛時以向月下則水生，以銅盤受之，下水數滴。高說與許、鄭異。考工記以鑒燧之齊倂言，則鑑之爲鏡可知也。鄭云鏡屬。又注考工記云：鑒亦鏡也。詩云。我心匪鑒。毛傳曰：鑒所以察形。葢鏡主於照形，鑑主於取明水，本系二物。而鏡亦可名鑒，是以經典多用鑑字，少用鏡者。鑑亦叚監爲之，是以毛詩宜鑒於殷，大學作儀監。鄭箋詩云：以殷王賢愚爲鏡。注大學云：監视殷時之事。各依文爲說而已。尚書監字多有同鑒者。</span></p>                          </div>
