						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>屈</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>使弯曲，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">伸</span>&rdquo;相对：～曲（<span class="dicpy">qū</span>）。～折。～膝。～伸（弯曲和伸直，引申为失意和得意）。首～一指。卑躬～膝。</li>
	<li>低头，降服：～服。～从。威武不～。</li>
	<li>冤枉，叫人不痛快：冤～。委～。～辱。～才。～就（受委屈而担任某种职务，常用于请人任职的客套话）。～驾。～己待人。</li>
	<li>理亏：～心（亏心，昧心）。理～词穷。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">屈</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>屈</strong> <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从尸，出声。尸，古文字中是假卧着的人形。本义:弯曲)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屈，拗曲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡曲而不伸者皆曰屈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尺蠖之屈，以求信也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猬缩蠖屈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可屈伸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安能屈豪杰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屈膝叩首。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不当屈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:屈指询算(迷信指用手指占算);屈律(弯曲的样子);屈卮(有弯曲把手的酒杯);屈申(屈伸。屈曲和伸展);诎屈(曲折;弯曲);屈伸(屈曲);屈屈(弯弯曲曲);屈蠖(曲身的尺蠖)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 委屈;冤屈 <span class="encs">[feel wronged;put sb. to great inconvenience]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可屈致。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大书一屈字。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《凫藻集&middot;书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求一屈己。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">被诬负屈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:屈官司(冤枉官司);屈致(随便招请，使人受到委屈);屈事(冤枉事);屈驰(委屈);屈气(怨气);屈漾(屈央，屈恙。冤枉);屈厄(委屈困顿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 屈服 <span class="encs">[subdue;yield]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屈人之兵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;谋政》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屈人之兵而非战。</span></p>
