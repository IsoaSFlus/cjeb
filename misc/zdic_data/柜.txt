                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">柜</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>柜</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">櫃</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>一种收藏东西用的家具，通常作长方形，有盖或有门：～子。～橱。电视～。掌～（称商店老板或掌管商店的人。亦称&ldquo;掌柜的&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>柜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～柳〕落叶乔木，羽状复叶，小叶长椭圆形，枝韧，可以编筐。性耐湿、耐碱，可固沙，多栽植在路旁做行道树。亦称&ldquo;元宝枫&rdquo;、&ldquo;杞柳&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cabinet, cupboard; shop counter</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schrank, Kabinett</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> variété de saule (botanique)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">柜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>柜</strong></p>
<p>匮、櫃 <span class="dicpy">gu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，匮( <span class="dicpy">gu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:收藏衣物、书籍的家具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 小匣 <span class="encs">[box]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">匮，匣也。从匚，贵声。俗作櫃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将为胠箧探囊发匮之盗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;胠箧》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玉与石而同匮兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;谬谏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人有卖其珠于 <span class="diczx2">郑</span>者，为木兰之柜，董以桂椒，缀以珠玉，饰以玫槐，辑以悲翠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储左上》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 后泛指盛放衣物、书籍、文件等用的器具，方形或长方形，一般为木制或铁制 <span class="encs">[cabinet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:衣柜;钱柜;碗柜;壁柜</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 四周高起以蓄水的地方 <span class="encs">[quadrate basin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江边有田以柜称，四起封圉皆力成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《元诗选&middot;王祯&middot;柜田》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:柜田</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 柜房，账房，商人保存账册和处理商务的房间或办公室 <span class="encs">[countinghouse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:柜身(柜身子。店铺的柜台);柜吏;柜坊;现款都交柜了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 老板;掌柜的 <span class="encs">[boss; shopkeeper]</span></p>
