						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>甬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄥˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>中国浙江省宁波市的别称：～剧（流行于宁波一带的地方戏曲剧种）。</li>
	<li>钟柄。</li>
	<li>花蓓蕾的样子：草木花～～然。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">桶</span>&rdquo;，古量器名。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">甬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>甬</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文&ldquo;甬&rdquo;象鐘形，上象钟悬，下象钟形。①本义:乐钟。②钟柄)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代钟柄名 <span class="encs">[one of bell&#39;s handle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甬本是钟，乃后人用字变迁，缩小其义为钟柄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杨树达</span><span class="smcs">《积微居小学述林》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代两旁有墙垣遮蔽的通道 <span class="encs">[road flanked by walls]</span>。如:甬路(楼房之间有篷顶的通道;院落中的砖石路)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 仆役;佣工 <span class="encs">[servant]</span>。如:甬官(古代掌管徭役的官)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 甬江 <span class="encs">[Yong river]</span>。在浙江，流过宁波</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 宁波市的别称 <span class="encs">[Ningbo city]</span>。因境内有甬江而得名</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;桶&rdquo;古斗斛一类量器 <span class="encs">[a measure of capacity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谷口铜甬，容十斗，重四十斤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《集古录》</span></span></p>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代的一种量器。即斛。</span><span class="gc_yy">《玉篇&middot;丂部》：「甬，斛也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《吕氏春秋&middot;仲秋纪&middot;仲秋》：「平权衡，正钧石，齐斗甬。」</span></p></li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">甬</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上" target="_blank">午集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上&amp;bs=用" target="_blank">用</a>】
                              甬·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=7" target="_blank">7</a>　·部外筆画：2</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/752C.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】余隴切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】尹竦切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音勇。【說文】草木華甬甬然也。【徐曰】甬之言涌也，若泉涌出也。</p>
<p>又甬道。【史記&middot;秦始皇紀】築甬道。【註】應劭曰：謂馳道外築牆，天子於中，外人不見也。【項羽紀註】應劭曰：恐敵鈔輺重，故築牆垣如街。【淮南子&middot;本經訓】修爲牆垣甬。道相連。【註】甬道，飛閣複道也。【韓愈詩】雲韶凝禁甬。【註】宮禁巷道也。【正字通】按甬道之名雖同，或馳道外，或軍伍中，或宮巷道，其用不一。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;鳧氏】鳧氏爲鍾，舞上謂之甬，甬上謂之衡。【註】此二名者，鍾柄。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】自關而東，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>魏宋楚之閒，保庸謂之甬。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;哀二十二年】越滅吳，請使吳王居甬東。甬東，越地，會㮷句章縣，東海中洲也。</p>
<p>又量名。【禮&middot;月令】仲春之月，日夜分，則同度量，鈞衡石，角斗甬。【註】甬，今斛也。</p>
<p>又【博雅】甬，常也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>杜孔切，音動，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>管也。與筩同。</p>
