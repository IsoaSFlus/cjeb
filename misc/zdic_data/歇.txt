                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">歇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>歇</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从欠。曷( <span class="dicpy">hé</span>)声。歇息与出气有关，故从<span class="yhcs">“欠”</span>。本义:休息)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(侧重于放松身体的紧张状态以消除疲劳) <span class="encs">[rest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歇，息也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牛困人饥日已高，市南门外泥中歇。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼时</span><span class="diczx2">黛玉</span>自在床上歇午，丫环们皆出去自便，满屋内静悄悄的。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:歇泊(休息);歇子(小歇;稍憩);歇午(歇中，歇晌。皆指午间休息);歇夏(歇伏，伏天休息);歇晚(晚上休息);歇热(乘凉)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 停止，中止活动 <span class="encs">[stop work]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余韵徐歇。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">声暂歇。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骤雨初歇。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">要歇业的店铺，暂时不准歇。——</span><span class="diczx2">茅盾</span><span class="smcs">《动摇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:歇令(停止，住手);歇浪(关掉;打烊)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 睡眠，躺下来休息 <span class="encs">[sleep]</span>。如:他在朋友家里歇了一夜</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 留宿 <span class="encs">[lodge]</span>。在旅店等处住宿</p><p><span class="diczx2">张主管</span>闲坐半晌，安排歇宿。——<span class="smcs">《京本通俗小说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:歇家(旅舍;住宿处;兼搞跑腿、牵线的客店;客店主人);歇卧(睡觉，过夜);歇店(住客店;客店)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 竭，尽，到了尽头 <span class="encs">[come to an end]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">难未歇也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">灯青兰膏歇，落照飞蛾舞。——</span><span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《伤心行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:歇绝(消失);歇微(衰退消失);歇灭(息灭，消失);歇落 (消失)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 凋零，花、木、草枯萎 <span class="encs">[wither]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">春木有荣歇，此节无凋零。——</span><span class="diczx2">陈子昂</span><span class="smcs">《与东方左史虬修竹篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 气味散发;消散 <span class="encs">[diffuse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歇，气越泄。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>歇</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>曾经 <span class="encs">[once]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我屋里家主婆从来勿曾说歇，耐倒要管起我来哉。——<span class="smcs">《海上花列传》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>歇</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
