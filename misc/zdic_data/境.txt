                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>境</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>疆界，边界：～界。国～。入～。出～。边～。</li><li>地方，区域，处所：无人之～。佳～。环～。身临其～。</li><li>状况，地步：～况。顺～。逆～。困～。事过～迁。～遇。意～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>境</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，竟声。本义:边境，国境)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[boundary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">境，疆也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>境。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·赞能》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 字本作<span class="yhcs">“竟”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亡不越竟。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">边竟有人焉。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其竟关之政尽察。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与</span><span class="diczx2">燕王</span>会境上。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">廉颇</span>送至境。</p><p><span class="diczx1">屯于境上。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">起视四境。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《权书·六国论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:出境(离开国境);入境(进入国境);境宇(国境，疆土);境人(境内之人;本地人);境头(结果;亦作<span class="yhcs">“景头”</span>。神佛点化人制造的幻境);境土(疆域;领地);境会(在边界上聚会)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 境况 <span class="encs">[circumstance]</span>。如:境物(四周的环境与景物);家境(家庭的经济状况);境象(景象;情境)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 居所，地方 <span class="encs">[place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">结庐在人境，而无车马喧。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《饮酒诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士大夫出其境。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 地步，境界 <span class="encs">[condition; state; extent reached]</span>。如:处境(所处的境地);困境(困难的处境);佳境(美好的境界)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 佛教指成为心意对象之世界 <span class="encs">[condition]</span>。如:尘境;色境;法境等;境头(所谓前身的情景。多指神佛点化的梦兆)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 景象，景物 <span class="encs">[lanscape]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">槛外诸境。——<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/境地">境地</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/境界">境界</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/境况">境况</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/境遇">境遇</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/境域">境域</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
