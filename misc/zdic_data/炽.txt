同本义。引申为凶猛，激烈;气焰高涨 <span class="encs">[burning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">炽，盛也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三月而炽之。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·钟氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">环顾国内，贼氛方炽。——</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略·序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:炽火(炽燎。烈火);炽焰(烈焰);炽火(燃点灯烛);炽腾(火势猛烈，火苗上窜)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 兴盛，昌盛 <span class="encs">[powerful and prosperous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>虽炽盛，不如如此。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·艺增》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:炽昌(昌盛);炽富(昌盛富足);炽茂(茂盛);炽发(勃发)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>炽</strong></p><p>熾 <span class="dicpy">chì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 烹煮 <span class="encs">[cook]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">钟氏染羽，以朱湛丹秫，三月而炽之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·考工记·钟氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">湛炽必洁。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">畅草可以炽酿。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·异虚》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:炽爨(烧煮)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr
