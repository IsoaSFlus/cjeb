                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>劫</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jié  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jié.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄝˊ</span></p><ol><li>强取，掠夺：～掠。洗～。～道。～富济贫。</li><li>威逼，胁制：～持（要挟，挟持）。～制。</li><li>灾难：～数（<span class="dicpy">shù</span>）（佛教指注定的灾难）。～难（<span class="dicpy">nàn</span>）。浩～（大灾难）。遭～。～后余生。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> take by force, coerce; disaster</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Katastrophe, Unglück, Verhängnis  (S)​,drohen, zwingen  (V)​,rauben, berauben, plündern, überfallen, Raubzug machen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> voler,piller,saisir par la force,contraindre,calamité,catastrophe</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">劫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>劫</strong></p><p>刼、劫 <span class="dicpy">jié</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jié.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从力，去声。本义:以强力使对方欲去而不得)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 威胁;威逼 <span class="encs">[threaten;coerce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">劫，人欲去，以力胁止，曰劫。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">劫之以众。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·儒行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">已珉欲以</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>劫 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故口可劫而使</span><span class="diczx2">墨</span>云。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·解蔽》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可劫以死生。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·精神》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因以劫众。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">积威所劫。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:劫杀(劫持并加以杀害);劫略(以威力胁迫于人);劫盟(以暴力逼人缔结盟约);劫质(挟持人质，借此勒索)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抢夺;强取 <span class="encs">[plunder;raid;rob]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃欲以生劫之，必得约契以报太子。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谋劫</span><span class="diczx2">单于</span>母。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行劫缚者。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:劫掳(抢劫掳掠);劫鞘(抢劫鞘银);劫磷(劫难;灾难。磷:薄;损伤);劫剞(抢劫掠夺);打劫(抢劫);行劫(进行抢劫)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>劫</strong> <span class="dicpy">jié</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jié.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 盗贼，劫匪 <span class="encs">[robber]</span>。如:劫人(强盗);劫帅(盗贼的首领);劫盗(强盗;劫贼);劫贼(强盗;土匪)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 佛教名词。<span class="yhcs">“劫波”</span>(或<span class="yhcs">“劫簸”</span>)的略称。意为极久远的时节。古印度传说世界经历若干万年毁灭一次，重新再开始，这样一个周期叫做一<span class="yhcs">“劫”</span>。后人借指天灾人祸 <span class="encs">[calamity]</span>。如:劫灰(本为佛教所谓<span
