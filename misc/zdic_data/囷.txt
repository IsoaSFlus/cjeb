                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>囷</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>古代一种圆形谷仓：“（大荒）而～鹿（方形仓）空虚。”</li><li>样子像囷仓的事物：“……少宝之山，百草木成～。”</li><li>积聚；聚拢。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> round-shaped storage bin for grain</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Getreidespeicher, Kornkammer  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grenier</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>囷</strong> <span class="dicpy">ｑūn</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从禾在囗(古围字)中。本义:圆形的谷仓) 同本义 <span class="encs">[round granary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">囷，禀之圆者。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">囷窌仓城。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·匠人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">修囷仓。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而囷鹿空虚。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“圆曰囷，方曰鹿。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胡取禾三百囷兮?——<span class="smcs">《诗·魏风》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:囷头儿(囤顶);囷京(储存谷物的仓库);囷仓(粮仓);囷窌(谷仓与地窖);囷庚(粮仓);囷鹿(粮仓);囷窖(粮仓);囷鹿(粮仓)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>囷</strong> <span class="dicpy">qūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 积聚，聚拢 <span class="encs">[gather]</span>。如:囷府(国家库藏钱粮物资的处所)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 回旋，围绕 <span class="encs">[circle round;revolve round]</span>。如:囷囷(曲折回旋的样子);囷轮(轮囷。屈曲的样子)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/56F7.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】去倫切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】區倫切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音箘。【說文】廩之圓者。从禾，在囗中。圓謂之囷，方謂之京。【周禮·冬官考工記·匠人】囷窌倉城。【註】地上爲之，圓曰囷，方曰倉，穿地曰窌。【詩·魏風】不稼不穡，胡取禾三百囷兮。【註】圓廩也。</p><p>又【周語】市無赤米，而囷鹿空虛。【註】先儒以爲圓曰囷，方曰鹿。鹿善聚，亦善散，故囷亦謂之鹿也。</p><p>又輪囷，屈曲盤戾貌。【前漢·鄒陽傳】蟠木根柢，輪囷離奇，爲萬乗器，以左右爲之先也。【左思·吳都賦】重葩掩葉，輪囷虬蟠。【註】謂木如龍之盤屈也。</p><p>又星名。【石氏星經】天囷十二星，主倉廩之屬。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>巨隕切，音窘。義同。</p>
