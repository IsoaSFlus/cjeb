                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">捍</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>捍</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>保卫，抵御：～卫。～御。</li>
	<li>古代射者左臂佩戴的皮制袖套。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/悍">悍</a>&rdquo;，强悍。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ward off, guard against, defend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> défendre,sauvegarder</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">捍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>捍</strong></p>
<p>扞 <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(古字作&ldquo;扞&rdquo;，后作&ldquo;捍&rdquo;。形声。从手，旱声。本义:抵御;保卫)同本义 <span class="encs">[defend;guard;protect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扞，以手扞，又卫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亲帅扞之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此公侯之所以扞城其民也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而设以国为王扞</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;西周策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若手足之扞头目。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能扞大患则祀之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">守一城，捍天下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《张中函传后叙》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:捍塞(防堵，阻塞);捍难(抵御外侮);捍遏(以武力阻隔);捍城(护卫城池);捍救(捍卫;救援);捍护(防卫;护卫);捍屏(捍蔽。屏藩)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>捍</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>古代射者左臂所著的皮制袖套 <span class="encs">[leather oversleeve]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:铠捍(铠甲与皮袖套)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>捍</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;悍&rdquo;。勇猛;强悍 <span class="encs">[fierce and tough;intrepid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼近吾死而我不听，我则悍矣，彼何罪焉!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;大宗师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无衔橛而御捍马也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;刑德》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:捍马(凶悍的烈马)&ldquo;扞&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">保衛、護衛。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「捍衛國土」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《集韻&middot;去聲&middot;翰韻》：「扞，衛也。或作<b>捍</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈張中丞傳後敘〉：「守一城，<b>捍</b>天下，以千百就盡之卒，戰百萬日滋之師。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">阻止、抵禦。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;祭法》：「能禦大菑而祀之，能<b>捍</b>大患而祀之。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">違抗。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>捍</b>命不降」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《北齊書&middot;卷四七&middot;酷吏傳&middot;宋遊道傳》：「對<b>捍</b>詔使，無人臣之禮，大不敬者死。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古時射手左臂上所帶的皮製袖套，具有保護作用。</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;王念孫《廣雅疏證&middot;卷八上&middot;釋器》：「拾、<b>捍</b>、韝，韘也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">疏證：「拾、<b>捍</b>、韝，皆謂遂也，著於左臂所以扞弦也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">凶暴、勇猛。</span><span class="gc_yy">《韓非子&middot;五蠹》：「無私劍之<b>捍</b>，從斬首為勇。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷一二九&middot;貨殖傳》：「上谷至遼東&hellip;&hellip;大與趙、代俗相類，而民雕<b>捍</b>少慮，有魚鹽棗栗之饒。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;司馬貞&middot;索隱：「人雕<b>捍</b>，言如雕性之捷<b>捍</b>也。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「悍」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              捍·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/634D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>侯幹切，同扞。【禮&middot;祭法】能捍大患則祀之。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;內則】右佩玦捍。【註】捍謂拾也，言可以捍弦也。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;貨殖傳】燕與趙代俗相類，而民雕捍少慮。【註】言如雕性之捷捍也。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>下赧切，音僩。捍摌，搖動也。</p>
<p>又止也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】戸版切，患上聲。義同。</p>
