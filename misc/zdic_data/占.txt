                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">占</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>占</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从卜，从口。以口问卜。本义:推测吉凶，即察看甲骨的裂纹或蓍草排列的情况取兆推测吉凶)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[divine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">占，视兆问也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未占有孚。——<span class="smcs">《易·萃》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">极数知来之谓占。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三人占。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">占者三人。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“三人，掌玉兆、瓦兆、原兆者也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">占衿兆。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以制器者尚其象，以卜筮者尚其占。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">史苏</span>占之，曰:不吉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公十五年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:占旺相(占卜时运);占人(掌占卜的官员);占工(占者。占家。专门从事占卜的人);占术(占卜之术);占书(占卜的书)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 窥察 <span class="encs">[ovserve; peek]</span>。如:占天(观测天象);占步(观测推算)</p><p><span class="diczx1">天象</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 占状(报告观测天象结果的奏状);占视(观察);占气(观云气风色以测吉凶)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 推料，推测 <span class="encs">[guess]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">史以天占人，圣人以人占天。——<span class="smcs">《法言·五百》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:占候(推测气候变化);占射(猜测);占覆(猜度隐藏之物)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 估计;揣度 <span class="encs">[estimate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度食不足，令民各自占家五种石斗数，为其期。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·号令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:占费(估量费用);占卖(估价出卖)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>占</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 征兆 <span class="encs">[omen]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山崩川竭，国土将亡之占也。——<span
