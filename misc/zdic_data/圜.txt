                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">圜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圜</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从囗 <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span>，睘( <span class="dicpy">qi&oacute;ng</span>)声。本义:围绕)同本义。通&ldquo;环&rdquo; <span class="encs">[surround]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">火似圜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记&middot;画缋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">动一亲戚，天下圜视而起。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圜流九十里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;说符》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲内圜凿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;孟荀传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余圜视大骇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《梓人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圜山(环山)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>圜</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sky]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圜，天体也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，浑圆为圜，平圆为圆。圆之规为圆。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圜，天道也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;圜道》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大圜在上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;序意》</span>。注:&ldquo;天也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乾为天，为圜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圜则九重，孰营度之?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圜邱(圜丘);圜方(指天地);圜宰;圜道(天道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 牢狱 <span class="encs">[prison]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">司圜中士六人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:圜土(牢狱);圜扉(狱门);圜墙(牢狱);圜门(圆门;狱门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 圆 <span class="encs">[circle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圜，一中同长也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;经上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刓方以为圜兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;怀沙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">石苍黑色，多平方，少圜(圜:同圆)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=囗" target="_blank">囗</a>】
                              圜·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/571C.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】王權切。【集韻】于權切。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>與圓同。【說文】天體也，全也，周也。【易&middot;說卦】乾爲天，爲圜。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】輿人爲車，圜者中規，方者中矩。【又】規之以眡其圜也。【禮&middot;深衣】袂圜以應規，曲袷如矩以應方。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;梅福傳】高祖從䛫如轉圜。【註】與圓同。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大司樂】圜鍾，爲宮。【註】圜鐘，夾鐘也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大司樂】冬日至，于地上之圜丘，夏日至，于澤中之方丘。【註】因高以事天，故於地上取自然之丘，圜者應天圜也。因下以事地，故於澤中取方丘，水中不可以設祭，故亦取自然之方丘，象地方故也。</p>
<p>又圜法，錢也。【前漢&middot;食貨志】齊太公爲周立九府圜法。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;地官&middot;比長】以圜土納之。【註】圜土，獄城也。獄必圜者，規主仁，以仁心求其情，古之治獄，閔於出之。【又】司圜掌收敎罷民，凡害人者，弗使冠飾，而加明<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>焉，任之以事，而收敎之。【疏】收敎者，謂入圜土，見收使困苦攺悔。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】戸關切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】胡關切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音還。繞也，圍也。【賈誼&middot;治安策】動一親戚，天下圜視而起。【註】驚視也。【通鑑】漢明帝幸辟雍，冠帶縉紳之徒圜橋門，而觀聽者蓋億萬人。</p>
<p>又圜陽，縣名。見前圁字註。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/571C_978.svg" /><span class="swbf">(圜)</span><span class="ywyy">天體也。</span><span class="swdz">圜，環也。吕氏春秋曰：何以說天道之圜也。精氣一上一下，圜周復襍，無所稽畱，故曰天道圜。何以說地道之方也。萬物殊類殊形，皆有分職，不能相爲，故曰地道方。按天體不渾圜如丸。故大戴禮云：參嘗聞之夫子曰：天道曰圓，地道曰方。盧云：道曰方圓耳，非形也。淮南子曰：天之圓不中規，地之方不中矩。白虎通曰：天，鎮也，其道曰圓。地，諦也，其道曰方。許言天體，亦謂其體一氣循環，無終無始，非謂其形渾圜也。下文云：圓，圜全也。斯爲渾圜。許書圓圜㘣三字不同。今字多作方圓、方員、方圜，而㘣字廢矣。依許則言天當作圜，言平㘣當作㘣，言渾圓當作圓。</span><span class="ywyy">从囗瞏聲。</span><span class="swdz">王權切。十四部。</span></p>                          </div>
