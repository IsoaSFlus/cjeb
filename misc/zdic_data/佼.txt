                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>佼</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，交声。本义:美好)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[graceful;supernormal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佼人僚兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·陈风·月出》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“佼人，美人也。”</span>释文:<span class="yhcs">“佼，本作姣。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子由之佼以好。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·成相》</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“佼，亦好也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">养壮佼。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓形容佼好。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">量之无佼民之辞。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语·入官》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹好也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">面目佼好则使之。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·尚贤中》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">陈平</span>贫而饮食不足，貌体佼好。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·骨相》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侗长佼好。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·上世之人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卿所谓铁中铮铮，庸中佼佼者也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·刘盆子传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李贤</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“言佼佼者，凡佣之人稍为胜也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:佼人(美人);佼好(美好);佼易(美好和气);佼健(美好和善)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“狡”</span>。狡诈的 <span class="encs">[artful;crafty;cunning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而燕雀佼之，以为不能与之争于宇宙之间。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·览冥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">好佼反而行私请。——<span class="smcs">《管子·七臣七主》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓很诈也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:佼诡(狡诈诡谲);佼黠(狡猾奸诈)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>佼</strong> <span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4F7C.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】古卯切【集韻】吉巧切【韻會】【正韻】古巧切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音攪。好也。【詩·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>風】佼人僚兮。陸氏作姣。【揚子·方言】自關而東，河濟之閒，凡好謂之佼。【禮·月令】仲夏養壯佼。【註】壯，形體碩大。佼，形容佼好。擇此類養之，亦順長養之令。【後漢·劉盆子傳】光武曰：卿所謂鐵中錚錚，庸中佼佼。【王充·論衡】上世之人，侗長佼好，堅强老壽。</p><p>又健也。【淮南子·覽冥訓】草木不搖，而燕雀佼之。【註】燕雀自以爲能佼於鳳凰也。</p><p>又姓。漢佼彊，南北朝佼長生。</p><p>又【廣韻】古肴切。與郊同。【史記·趙世家】宜爲上佼而今乃抵辠。</p>
