                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">莱</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>莱</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">萊</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lái  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lái.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄞˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>藜。</li>    <li>〔～菔〕萝卜的别称。</li>    <li>古代指郊外轮休的田，亦指田废生草：“政烦赋重，田～多荒”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> goosefoot, weed; fallow field</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Weißer Gänsefuß ,Lai  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (nom de certaines plantes sauvages)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">莱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>莱</strong></p><p>萊 <span class="dicpy">lái</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lái.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，来声。本义:草名。即藜)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 草名，又名藜 <span class="encs">[lamb’s-quarters]</span>。一种一年生草本植物。嫩苗可食，生田间、路边、荒地、宅旁等地，为古代贫者常食的野菜</p><p><span class="diczx1">莱，蔓华也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南山有台，北山有莱。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·南山有台》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 郊外休耕的田 <span class="encs">[unrotated land]</span>。如:莱田(荒地)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Lai state]</span>。今山东省黄县东南有莱子城即古莱国旧址。如:莱夷(古国名。殷周时分布在今山东半岛东北部。鲁襄公六年为齐所灭)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><span class="diczx2">莱驹</span>为右。(莱驹作晋侯的车右武士。)——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>莱</strong></p><p>萊 <span class="dicpy">lái</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lái.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 长满杂草 <span class="encs">[overgrow with weeds]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莱五十亩。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·遂人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莱，野也。——<span class="smcs">《越绝书·吴内传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">田卒污莱。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·十月之交》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 除草 <span class="encs">[weed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若大田猎，则莱山田之野。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·地官·山虞》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/莱茵河">莱茵河</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/840A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】洛哀切【正韻】郞才切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音來。【說文】蔓華也。【玉篇】藜草也。【詩·小雅】北山有萊。【疏】萊，草名。其葉可食。</p><p>又【周禮·地官·縣師】辨其夫家人民田萊之數。【註】萊，休不耕者。郊內謂之易，郊外謂之萊。</p><p>又【周禮·地官·山虞】若大田獵，則萊山田之野。【註】萊，除其草萊也。【詩·小雅】田卒汙萊。【註】萊，草穢。</p><p>又地名。【書·禹貢】萊夷作牧。【齊語】通齊國之魚鹽于東萊。【註】東萊，齊東萊夷也。</p><p>又山名。【山海經】萊山，其木多檀楮。</p><p>又姓。【孟子】苦伊尹、萊朱。【趙岐註】萊朱，湯賢臣仲虺是也。【左傳·文二年】萊駒爲右。</p><p>又【韻補】音黎。【郭璞·遊仙詩】朱門何足榮，未若託蓬萊。臨泉揖淸波，陵岡掇丹荑。</p><p>又【廣韻】落代切，音賴。義同。　【爾雅】作釐。</p>
