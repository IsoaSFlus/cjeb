                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">爷</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>爷</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">爺</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yé  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yé.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄝˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>父亲：“军书十二卷，卷卷有～名”。</li>    <li>祖父：～～。姥～。</li>    <li>对长辈或年长男子的敬称：张大～。</li>    <li>旧时对官僚、财主等的称呼：县太～。少（<span class="dicpy">shào </span>）～。</li>    <li>对佛、神的称呼：佛～。财神～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> father, grandfather</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Großpapa, Großvater, Opa  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grand-père,père</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">爷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>爷</strong></p><p>爺 <span class="dicpy">yé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从父，耶声。简体字<span class="yhcs">“卩”</span>只是个符号。本义:父亲)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。也作<span class="yhcs">“耶”</span> <span class="encs">[father]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愿为市鞍马，从此替爷征。——<span class="smcs">《木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卷卷有爷名。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旦辞爷娘去。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爷娘闻女来。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:爷娘皇天(呼爹叫娘，嚎天喊地)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 爷爷 <span class="encs">[grandfather]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 祖父——多叠用</p><p><span class="diczx1">爷爷会织布、会种田、还会养牛</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 对与祖父辈分相同、年龄相仿的男子的敬称。如:张爷爷</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 对长一辈或年长男子的尊称 <span class="encs">[uncle]</span>。如:大爷;二爷;赵爷</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 旧时对主人、上官或尊贵者的称呼 <span class="encs">[master]</span>。如∶王爷;相爷;老爷;少爷</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 迷信的人对神佛的称呼 <span class="encs">[god]</span>。如:佛爷;老天爷;土地爷;阎王爷</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 自矜之称 <span class="encs">[I]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">祥子</span>明知道上工辞工都是常有的事，此处不留爷，自有留爷处。—— <span class="diczx2">老舍</span><span class="smcs">《骆驼祥子》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/爷儿们">爷儿们</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/爷们">爷们</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/爷爷">爷爷</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
