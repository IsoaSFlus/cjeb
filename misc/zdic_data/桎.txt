                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>桎</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，至声。本义:古代拘系罪人的木制脚镣) 同本义 <span class="encs">[fetters]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桎，足械也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。在手曰梏。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九家坎为桎梏。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“手足各一木也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">利用刑人，用说桎梏。——<span class="smcs">《易·蒙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中罪桎梏。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·秋官·掌囚》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“在手曰梏，在足曰桎。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命有司省囹圄，去桎梏。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·仲春》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:桎拲(刑具。拲，两手手铐)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>桎</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 给脚带上刑具 <span class="encs">[put on fetters]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帝乃梏之疏属之山，桎其右足。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·海内北经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 束缚，约束 <span class="encs">[bind;tie]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">指与物化而不以心稽，故其灵台一而不桎。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·达生》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">儒学自桎。——<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:桎槛(囚禁，囚于槛车)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/桎梏">桎梏</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
