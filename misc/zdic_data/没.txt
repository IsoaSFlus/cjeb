                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>没</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">沒</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄟˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>无：～有。～用。～关系。～词儿。～精打采。～心～肺。</li>
	<li>不曾，未：～有来过。</li>
	<li>不够，不如：汽车～飞机快。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>没</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄛˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>隐在水中：沉～。～顶之灾。</li>
	<li>隐藏，消失：埋～。～落。</li>
	<li>漫过，高过：水～了头顶。淹～。</li>
	<li>财物收归公有或被私人侵吞：～收。抄～。</li>
	<li>终，尽：～世。～齿不忘。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/殁">殁</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> not, have not, none; drown, sink</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> nicht (haben)​, (es gibt)​ nicht, nicht haben (V)​,bis ans Ende ,etw überschwemmen, überfluten  (V)​,etw konfiszieren  (V)​,sich verlieren  (V)​,sterben  (V)​,tauchen  (V)​,untergehen, versinken  (V)​,untertauchen, verschwinden  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ne pas (avoir)​,noyer,inonder,mourir,sombrer,submerger,disparaître,noyé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">没</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>没</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不领有，不具有，不存在 <span class="encs">[be without;have not;there isn&#39;t]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恐怕没来由走去撞在乱兵之手，死了也没说处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《二刻拍案惊奇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:没谱(没有标准);没根蒂(没有根据);没脚手(没有雨具);没个了局(没有了结;没完没了);没个道理(没有办法);没仁没义(没有一点情义);没家亲引不出外鬼来(没有内患，引不来外敌);没事(无端;无缘无故);没对(无敌;无与匹敌);没来历(无缘由);没空生有(犹无中生有，凭空胡诌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 不及;不到 <span class="encs">[not as good as;inferior to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:哥哥没弟弟胖;他回家没两天又走了</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>没</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 没有，不曾，未 <span class="encs">[not，no]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用在动词或形容词前面，表示对过去的行为、动作或状态的否定。如:没大(没有怎么);没巧不成话(没有巧合就没有故事);没走完的路;没等你请，我自个来了;天还没亮，我们就上路了</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用在选择问句末，代表选择问句的否定方面。如:你吃饭没?这本书你看完没?</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>没</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意 表示入水有所取。本义:沉没水中)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[submerge;drown]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">没，沉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其子没于渊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若赴水火，入焉焦没耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;议兵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梦为鱼而没于渊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;大宗师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或浮或没。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;自新》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浅草才能没马蹄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《钱塘湖春行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水不没顶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">没水而死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:沉没(没入水中);淹没(大水漫过;盖过);没胫(没过脚胫);没不煞(方言。沉没不了);没溺(沉没);洪水没过了大坝;没漂(淹没冲荡);没石(暗礁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 潜游水中 <span class="encs">[dive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若乃夫没人，则未尝见舟而便操之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;达生》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南方多没人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《日喻》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:没人(熟知水性能潜水的人);没水(潜水);没头蹲(扎猛子，以头朝下钻入水中)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 消失，不见;隐没 <span class="encs">[disappear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">良久，羽旄之影渐没。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未闻刃没而利存。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范缜</span><span class="smcs">《神灭论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 覆灭;败亡 <span class="encs">[destroy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂没其军。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 沉迷。糊涂 <span class="encs">[indulge in]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:没没(沉迷不醒);没汩(糊涂，不明事理)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 没收 <span class="encs">[confiscate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">财产没官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:没官(没收入官);没入(没收财物、人口等入官);没有(犹没收)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;殁&rdquo;。死 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庖牺氏没。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逮至昔三代圣王既没。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;明鬼下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣王已没。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以没其身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆为戮没。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孝公</span>既没。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:没化(死亡);没世(终身;一辈子);没地(人死埋葬于地下);没命(舍命。丧身，死亡);没陈(阵亡);没寿(谓死)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 沦没，落入 <span class="encs">[fall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侯没胡中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 隐没 <span class="encs">[conceal;hide under cover]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">没为银海。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出没于鲸波万仞之中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">周密</span><span class="smcs">《观潮》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:出没(出现和隐藏);没矢(谓箭射进石头后隐没不见)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>没</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>

<p><span class="diczx3">如:没想到</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>没</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 尽;终 <span class="encs">[all]</span>。</p>
