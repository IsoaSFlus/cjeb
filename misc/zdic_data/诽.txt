						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诽</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">誹</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fěi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fěi.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄟˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 捏造事实，说别人坏话：～谤。～章。腹～心谤。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诽</strong></p><p>誹 <span class="dicpy">fěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fěi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，非声。本义:背地议论，指责他人)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[blame]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诽，谤也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，放言曰谤，微言曰诽、曰讥。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诽，明恶也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子经上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至于怨诽之多，则固前知其如此也。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如;诽怨(责备怨恨);诽议(责难，非议)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 毁谤 <span class="encs">[calumniate;slander]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">经诽誉。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贤者诽。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·决胜》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而废格沮诽。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓非上所行。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赏者有诽焉，不足以劝。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·八经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:诽诽(众说纷纷的样子);诽章(谤书，谤毁他人的奏章);诽讥(毁谤讥讽);诽誉(毁谤和赞誉);诽诋(诽谤诋毁)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/诽谤">诽谤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/诽谤罪">诽谤罪</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AB9.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】敷尾切【集韻】【韻會】妃尾切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音斐。【說文】謗也。【博雅】䛼也。【史記·高祖紀】誹謗者族。【莊子·刻意篇】高論怨誹。【戰國策】國必有誹譽，忠臣令誹在己，譽在上。</p><p>又【集韻】或省作非。【前漢·鼂錯傳】非謗不治。【師古註】非讀曰誹。</p><p>又【集韻】府尾切，非上聲。義同。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】匪微切【正韻】芳微切。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>與非通。【類篇】謗言也。【增韻】非議也。【史記·平準書】張湯奏顏異當九卿，見令不便，不入言而腹誹。【前漢·食貨志】作腹非。【註】口不言，心非之也。</p><p>又【廣韻】方味切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】方未切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>非去聲。義同。【史記·文帝紀】誹謗之木。【索隱】誹，音非，亦音沸。尸子云：堯立誹謗之木。韋昭云：慮政有缺失，使書于木，後代遂因以爲飾。今宮牆橋樑頭四柱木是也。</p>
