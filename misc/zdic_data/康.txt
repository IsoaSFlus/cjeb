<p>● <strong>康</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄤˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>安宁：～乐（<span class="dicpy">l&egrave; </span>）。～平。～宁。</li>
	<li>空，空虚：萝卜～了。</li>
	<li>宽阔：～庄。～衢（四通八达的大路）。</li>
	<li>无病：～健。～复。～泰。健～。</li>
	<li>丰盛：小～。～年。</li>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">康</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>康</strong> <span class="dicpy">kāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。康为穅的本字。从禾，康声。本义:谷皮;米糠)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bran;rice polishings]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">康，谷皮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作穅。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尘垢粃穅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦食穅何覈耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;陈平传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">播康迷目。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 五路通达的大路，泛指大路 <span class="encs">[broad road]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五达谓之康，六达谓之庄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公驱及之康内。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋&middot;内篇谏上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">微服游于康衢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;仲尼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>康</strong> <span class="dicpy">kāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 安乐，安定 <span class="encs">[easy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民康乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无己大康，职思其居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;蟋蟀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上下和洽，海内康平。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;宣帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日康娱以自忘兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:康乐(安乐);康阜(安和富足);康娱(欢娱安乐);康福(安乐，富裕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 健康 <span class="encs">[health;well-being]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命如南山石，四体康且直。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:康健(安康强健);康胜(康健安泰);康宁(健康平安)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 太平;安宁 <span class="encs">[peaceful;stable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">海</span>、 <span class="diczx2">沂</span>之康，实赖 <span class="diczx2">王祥</span>，邦国不空，别驾之功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:康时(太平盛世);康乂(安治，太平无事。乂:治理);康适(安适，安舒);康日(谓太平岁月)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 丰足;富裕 <span class="encs">[rich;abundant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迄用康年。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;周臣臣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:康年(丰年)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 四通八达的;四面八方的 <span class="encs">[all round;extended in all directions]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为开第康庄之路。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;孟子荀卿列传》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5EB7.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B73.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠭳</span>【唐韻】苦岡切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】丘岡切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音穅。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】樂也。【詩&middot;唐風】無巳大康。</p>
<p>又【周頌】迄用康年。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】康，安也。【書&middot;益稷】庶事康哉。</p>
<p>又【洪範】五福，三曰康寧。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋宮】五達謂之康，六達謂之莊。【疏】孫炎曰：康，樂也，交會樂道也。【釋名】五達曰康。康，昌也，昌盛也，車步倂列並用之，言充盛也。【列子&middot;仲尼篇】堯遊於康衢。【史記&middot;騶衍傳】爲列第康莊之衢。</p>
<p>又【易&middot;晉卦】康侯用錫馬蕃庶。【註】康，美之名也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;祭統】康周公。【註】康，猶褒大也。</p>
<p>又【諡法】淵源流通曰康，溫柔好樂曰康，令民安樂曰康。</p>
<p>又國名。【書&middot;康誥疏】命康叔之誥。管蔡郕霍皆國名，則康亦國名，在圻內。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;西域傳】安息國王治番兜城，北與康居接。</p>
<p>又州名。【唐書&middot;地理志】康州，析端州之端溪置。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】衞康叔之後。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋器】康瓠謂之甈。【註】瓠，壺也。【賈誼&middot;弔屈原賦】寶康瓠。</p>
<p>又與穅通。【說文】穅或省作康。</p>
<p>又與漮通。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】漮，虛也。【詩&middot;小雅】酌彼康爵。【箋】康，空也。漮康音義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】苦浪切，音抗。【禮&middot;明堂位】崇坫康圭。【註】康讀爲亢。【疏】亢，舉也。</p>
