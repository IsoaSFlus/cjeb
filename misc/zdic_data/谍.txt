                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谍</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">諜</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dié  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dié.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄝˊ</span></p><ol><li>秘密探察军、政及经济等方面的消息：～报。间（<span class="dicpy">jiào </span>）～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">牒</span>”，谱录。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">喋</span>”，喋喋。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> an intelligence report; to spy; spying</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> espionnage,espion</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谍</strong></p><p>諜 <span class="dicpy">dié</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dié.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，枼( <span class="dicpy">yè</span>)声。本义:间谍，细作)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 间谍 <span class="encs">[spy;espionage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谍，军中反间也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌斩杀贼谍而搏之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·掌戮》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏贼谍。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·环人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“反间为国贼。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>人获 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>谍。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谍报敌情至。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:防谍;谍贼(暗探和叛国贼);谍人(间谍)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 传递的情报 <span class="encs">[spy's report]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宁边军</span>数日间连受八十余谍。——<span class="smcs">《续资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“牒”</span>。书札 <span class="encs">[writing slip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">太史公</span>读春秋历谱谍。——<span class="smcs">《史记·十二诸侯年表序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">宫童效异，披图案谍。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·礼乐志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故一介之策，各有攸建，子长谍之，爓然有第。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谍</strong></p><p>諜 <span class="dicpy">dié</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dié.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 暗中窥伺敌情或秘密 <span class="encs">[spy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">罗</span>人欲伐之，使 <span class="diczx2">伯嘉</span>谍之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·恒公十二年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8ADC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】徒叶切【集韻】【韻會】達協切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音牒。【說文】軍中反閒也。【玉篇】伺也。【左傳·桓十二年】楚師分涉於彭，羅人使伯嘉諜之。【註】諜，伺也。【疏】謂詐爲敵國之人，入其軍中，伺<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>閒隙，以反報其主，兵書謂之反閒。</p><p>又【宣八年】晉人獲秦諜。【註】諜，往來閒諜者，今謂之細作。</p><p>又【哀元年】使女艾諜澆。【註】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>也。【周禮·夏官】環人巡邦國搏諜賊。【吳志·孫皓傳】宜遣閒諜，以觀其勢。</p><p>又【字林】通作牒。【廣韻】譜諜也。【史記·三代世表】余讀諜記，黃帝以來皆有年數，稽其歷譜諜終始五德之傳，古文咸不同，乖異。【後漢·張衡傳】子長諜之，爛然有第。【註】諜，譜第也。與牒通。【文心雕龍】百官詢事，則有關刺解諜。諜者葉也，短<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>編諜，如葉在枝。諜亦書作牒。</p><p>又通作喋。【史記·張釋之傳】豈斆此嗇夫，諜諜利口捷給哉。【索隱曰】漢書作喋喋，多言也。</p><p>又【集韻】託協切，音帖。【說文】義同。【類篇】安也。</p><p>又【集韻】悉協切，音燮。【類篇】言相次也。　【增韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27A2F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧨯</span>，譌作〈言<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21D6C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡵬</span>〉。【字彙補】又省作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27A5C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧩜</span>，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>非。</p><p>
