                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">保</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>保</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄠˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>看守住，护着不让受损害或丧失：～卫。～管。～健。～障。～密。明哲～身。朝不～夕（早晨保不住晚上会发生什么情况。形容形势危急）。</li>	<li>维持原状，使不消失或减弱：～持。～洁。～质。～墒。</li>	<li>负责：～证。～荐。～修。～险。确～。担～。</li>	<li>旧时户口的一种编制，若干甲为一保。</li>	<li>旧称佣工：酒～（酒店服务人员）。佣～。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">保</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>保</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象用手抱孩子形。金文写作从&ldquo;人&rdquo;从&ldquo;子&rdquo;。后来为了结构的对称，小篆变成&ldquo;保&rdquo;，使人不能因形见义了。本义:背子于背)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carry on one&#39;s back]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">负子于背谓之保，引申之，则负之者为保;更引申之，则有保养之义。然则保本象负子于背之义，</span><span class="diczx2">许</span>君误以为形声，遂取养之义当之耳。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐兰</span><span class="smcs">《殷墟文字记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 护养;育 <span class="encs">[bring up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">保，养也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若保赤子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;康诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">保抱携持厥妇子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;召诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长之养之，如保赤子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;议兵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儒者之道，古之人若保赤子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;藤文公上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:保艾(安养;养育);保赤(抚育幼孩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 保佑 <span class="encs">[bless and protect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天地格保。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;召诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:保佐(保佑;佐助);保禳(祈祷消除灾祸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 保护;保全;守住 <span class="encs">[protect;defend;guard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父子相保全。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;人间训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少有强者不可保。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《祭十二郎文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有朕位几不保。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 保持 <span class="encs">[keep;preserve;maintain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至今保藏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:保值(保持价值);保光(保持并发扬光大)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 保证;担保 <span class="encs">[guarantee;ensure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">保为将军破之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:保修(保养修理);旱涝保收;保官(担任保护职能的官员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 占有;拥有 <span class="encs">[possess]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽慈父不能保其子。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:保据(占据);保宁(保有并使之安宁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 保举;保荐 <span class="encs">[recommend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都保你二位做大官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:保题议叙(写奏本保荐有功人员);保题(向上推荐);保案(保举下属的文件)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 依靠;仗持 <span class="encs">[depend on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">保君父之命而享其生禄，于是乎得人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:保山(依山固守);保栖(据山以守)</span></p>
