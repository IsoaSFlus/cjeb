                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>懿</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从壹，恣声。<span class="yhcs">“壹”</span>表示专一。本义:美好)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">懿，美也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">好是懿德。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·庶民》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斯则前世之懿事。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·吴主传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茂学懿文。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《斐度中书舍人制》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以就懿德。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:懿盛(因皇室宗亲而名位显盛);懿范(美好的风范。多用于赞扬妇女美德);懿明(德高智明);懿士(有美德的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[great]</span>。如:懿度(大度);懿伟(伟大)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 深 <span class="encs">[deep]</span>。如:懿筐(深筐)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/懿德">懿德</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/懿行">懿行</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/懿旨">懿旨</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/61FF.svg">                          <p>專久而美也。从壹，从恣省聲。乙冀切文二</p>                          </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/61FF_2114.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(懿)</span><span class="ywyy">嫥久而美也。</span><span class="swdz">嫥者、壹也。釋詁、詩烝民傳皆曰。懿、美也。周書謚法曰。柔克爲懿。温柔聖善曰懿。許益之以專久者、爲其字从壹也。專壹而後可久。可久而後美。小爾雅及楚辭注。懿、深也。詩七月傳曰。懿、深筐也。深卽專壹之意也。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21539.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡔹</span>。从恣省聲。</span><span class="swdz">从恣省聲四字、葢或淺人所改竄。當作从心、从欠、壹亦聲。从心从欠、所謂持其志、無暴其氣。美在其中而暢於四支也。壹亦聲者、國語衞武公作懿戒以自儆。韋注懿讀曰抑。大雅之抑詩也。大雅。懿厥哲婦。箋云。有所痛傷之聲也。金縢。對曰信懿。馬云。猶噫也。小雅。抑此皇父。箋云。抑之言噫。古懿抑同用。懿抑壹三字同音。可證。古音讀如一。十二部。今乙冀切。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
