                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>宥</strong> <span class="dicpy">yòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，有声。<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>表示房屋。本义:广厦容人曰宥)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 宽仁;宽待 <span class="encs">[treat with leniency]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宥，宽也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宥，宽也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闻在宥天下，不闻治天下也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·在宥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知其冤而宥其失。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:宥世(以宽仁之政治理国家);宥德(宽仁之德)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宽恕 <span class="encs">[forgive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天尊岂可违天擅杀，望天尊暂宥。——<span class="smcs">《梼杌闲评——明珠缘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中间驰至九天，以告上帝。帝知其冤，而宥其失，前所谴责，因而获免。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:宥免(宥恕，宥赦，宥过。宽恕别人的过错);宥愆(宽恕罪过)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 赦免 <span class="encs">[remit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宥，赦也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流宥五刑。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“宽也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌三刺、三宥、三赦之法。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司刺》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犯臣姬昌罪犯忤君，赦宥免死，暂居里。——<span class="smcs">《封神演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以赦过宥罪。——<span class="smcs">《易·解》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“宥谓宽宥，罪谓故犯，过轻则赦，罪重则宥，皆解缓之义也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:宥善(赦免善良的人);宥罪(赦免罪过);宥释(赦免释放)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>宥</strong> <span class="dicpy">yòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 宏深;深邃 <span class="encs">[secret]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夙夜基命宥密。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·昊天有成命》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:宥地(机要之地);宥密(深密;机密);宥弼(形容职司机密、重要)</p>                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5BA5.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】于救切【集韻】【韻會】尤救切【正韻】爰救切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音又。【說文】寬也。【徐鉉曰】寬之而已，未全放也。【易·解卦】君子以赦過宥罪。</p><p>又【周禮·秋官】司刺掌三刺三宥之法，一宥曰不識，再宥曰過失，三宥曰遺忘。</p><p>又【書·大禹謨】宥過無大。【註】謂不識而誤犯，雖大必赦宥也。【君<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>】狃于姦宄，敗常亂俗，三細不宥。【註】犯此三者，雖小罪不可宥也。</p><p>又宏深也。【詩·周頌】夙夜基命宥密。【註】言其夙夜積德，以承藉天命者，宏深而靜密也。</p><p>又助也。【左傳·僖二十五年】晉侯朝於王，王饗醴，命之宥。【註】又加之以幣帛，以助勸也。</p><p>又以樂勸食也，與侑同。【周禮·春官·大司樂】王大食三宥，皆令奏鐘鼓。</p><p>又姓。</p><p>又通作又。【禮·王制】王三又，然後制<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>。</p>
