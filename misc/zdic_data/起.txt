                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>起</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>由躺而坐或由坐而立等：～床。～立。～居。～夜。</li>    <li>离开原来的位置：～身。～运。～跑。</li>    <li>开始：～始。～码（最低限度，最低的）。～步。～初。～讫。～源。</li>    <li>拔出，取出：～锚。～获。</li>    <li>领取（凭证）：～护照。</li>    <li>由下向上，由小往大里涨：～伏。～劲。～色。</li>    <li>发生，产生，发动，提出：～风。～腻。～敬。～疑。～义。～诉。</li>    <li>长出：～痱子。</li>    <li>拟定：～草。</li>    <li>建造，建立：～房子。白手～家。</li>    <li>群，组，批：一～（一块儿）。</li>    <li>量词，指件，宗：一～案件。</li>    <li>自，从：～小儿就淘气。</li>    <li>用在动词后，表示动作的趋向：想～。掀～。兴（<span class="dicpy">xīng </span>）～。</li>    <li>用在动词后，与“<span class="diczx3">来</span>”连用，表示动作开始：唱～来。</li>    <li>用在动词后，常与“<span class="diczx3">不</span>”或“<span class="diczx3">得</span>”连用，表示胜任；亦表示达到某一种标准：看不～。经得～检验。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从走，己声。本义:由躺而坐;由坐而立)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stand up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">起，能立也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曩子坐，今子起。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">载起载行。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·沔水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请业则起。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子</span><span class="diczx2">墨子</span>起。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾恂恂而起。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫起大呼。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">妇亦起大呼。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:起在(起来);起舞(起身而舞。欢欣的样子);起坐(起立与坐下);起谢(起立谢过)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 起床 <span class="encs">[get up;get out of bed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">起视四境。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不敢同卧起。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:起去(指起床);起炕(起床)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 产生;发生 <span class="encs">[produce;happen;take place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自</span><span class="diczx2">董卓</span>以来，豪杰并起，跨州连郡者，不可胜数。—— <span class="diczx2">陈寿</span><span class="smcs">《隆中对》</span></p><p><span
