                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>婢</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，卑声。本义:旧社会里被迫受剥削阶级役使的女子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代罪人的眷属没入宫为婢，后通称受役使的女子 <span class="encs">[slave girl;servant-girl]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">婢，女之卑者也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使婢子侍执巾栉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十二年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“婢子，妇人之卑称也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使吾二婢子夹我。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“妾也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父母有婢子。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“所通贱人之子。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先大母婢也。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为婢仆所惑。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:婢婿(与婢女私通的人);婢妾(妾与使女)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代妇女的谦称 <span class="encs">[servant-girl;humble girl]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自世妇以下自称曰婢子。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“婢之言卑也。”</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/婢学夫人">婢学夫人</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/婢女">婢女</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/婢子">婢子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集下" target="_blank">丑集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集下&amp;bs=女" target="_blank">女</a>】
                              婢·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5A62.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】便俾切【集韻】【韻會】部弭切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音庳。【說文】女之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤰞</span>者。【禮·曲禮】自世婦以下皆稱曰婢子。</p><p>又夫人負罪而有所請，亦曰婢子。【左傳·僖十五年】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25807.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥠇</span>姬曰：晉君朝以入，則婢子夕以死。</p><p>又【二十二年】羸氏對太子圉曰：寡君使婢子侍執巾櫛。</p><p>又有罪而沒入于官曰官婢。【前漢·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】太倉令淳于公罪當<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>，小女緹縈上書，願沒入爲官婢，以贖父。</p><p>又小魚曰魚婢，見【爾雅·釋魚】。</p><p>又金鳳花，別名菊婢，見【草木譜】。</p><p>又婢曰上淸。【司馬光·考異】引柳珵上淸傳，竇參知敗，屬上淸，定爲宮婢。一曰上淸或婢本名。【正字通】當時通稱婢爲上淸。</p><p>
