						<p>● <strong>劲</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">勁</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>力气，力量：～头。费～。干～。</li>
	<li>精神，情绪，兴趣：干活儿起～儿。这部电影真没～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>劲</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">勁</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>坚强有力：～敌。～旅。～拔。～悍。～挺。～秀。～直。～松。强～。刚～。疾风知～草。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">劲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>劲</strong></p>
<p>勁 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，坙声。①( <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> )本义:强劲有力。②力气)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 力量，力气 <span class="encs">[strength;energy]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:劲兵(精锐部队);牛劲儿(大力气);加劲(增加力量);用劲(用力);带劲(有力量);脚劲(两腿的力气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 精神;情绪 <span class="encs">[spirit;vigor;drive;zeal]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:上劲(精神振奋，劲头儿大);起劲(情绪高，劲头儿大);干得非常起劲;他们真有这股劲儿;要鼓实劲，不要鼓虚劲</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 神情;态度 <span class="encs">[air;manner;expression]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他们都显出高兴劲;骄傲劲儿;精明劲儿;别扭劲儿</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 趣味;兴趣 <span class="encs">[interest;relish;gusto;mood]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:打扑克没劲;他们两不对劲</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指某种程度，限度 <span class="encs">[limit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:熟过了劲;白劲儿;香劲儿;够劲儿</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>劲</strong></p>
<p>剄 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，巠声。本义:强劲有力)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[strong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劲，彊也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劲，强也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">廉直劲正庄诚之音作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫梁兵劲而权重。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;宋策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>之劲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;说符》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弓先调而后求劲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说林》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">良将劲弩守要塞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《新书&middot;过秦论上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下之强弓劲弩，皆自</span><span class="diczx2">韩</span>出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风力虽尚劲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此劲敌也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刚劲;劲弩(坚强有力的弓弩);劲酒(烈酒);劲节(强劲的枝节;坚贞不移的操守);劲武(刚健勇武);劲猛(强悍勇猛);劲兵(战斗力很强的部队);劲卒(精锐的士兵);劲勇(精壮的勇士);劲锐(精锐的士卒或军队)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 笔法遒劲 <span class="encs">[vigorous]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:劲骏(挺拔俊逸);劲丽(挺拔秀丽);劲妙(刚健神妙);劲利(形容书法雄健流利)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 猛烈;风势强劲 <span class="encs">[violent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风劲角弓鸣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《观猎》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筋力越劲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;非相》</span>。注:&ldquo;勇也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劲秋(秋气肃杀，能凋零草木，所以称秋天为劲秋);劲气(寒冷之气);劲厉(风势猛烈而寒冷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 坚固;坚硬 <span class="encs">[firm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">材虽劲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;顺说》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木益枯则劲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;别类》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">真心凌晚桂，劲节掩寒松。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">骆宾王</span><span class="smcs">《浮槎》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劲节(竹木丫枝出生的坚固之处);劲利(坚固锐利);劲质(坚硬的质地;坚强的质性)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 强壮，对疾病或恶劣天气有抵抗力 <span class="encs">[sturdy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其气急疾坚劲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;腹中论》</span>。注:&ldquo;刚也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劲草;劲松</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 坚强;刚强 <span class="encs">[tenacious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">某度之，必其兵未集而有是言。使之集，则使者健而言必劲矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《九议》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劲直(刚毅正直);劲切(刚强峻急);劲果(刚毅果敢);劲廉(刚正廉洁);劲躁(刚强而急躁)</span></p>
