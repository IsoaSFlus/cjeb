						<p>● <strong>荔</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～枝〕①常绿乔木，果实球形，果实外壳有瘤状突起，熟时紫红色，果肉白色半透明，多汁，味甜美；②这种植物的果实。均亦称&ldquo;离枝&rdquo;、&ldquo;丹荔&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>荔</strong></p>
<p>茘 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 草名。即&ldquo;马蔺&rdquo; <span class="encs">[Chinese iris]</span>。又名&ldquo;马荔&rdquo;。一种多年生草本植物，须根长而坚硬，叶片狭线形，花蓝色。花及种子可入药，叶可造纸，根可制刷子</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 荔枝 <span class="encs">[litchi]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:荔子(即荔枝)</span></p>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">植物名。即馬藺。鳶尾科鳶尾屬，多年生草本。根鬚長而堅硬，葉條形，質硬無劍脊。葉可造紙，根可製為刷子，種子為止血藥。</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;王念孫《廣雅疏證&middot;卷一○上&middot;釋草》：「馬薤，<b>荔</b>也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">疏證：「蠡、藺、<b>荔</b>，一聲之轉，故張氏注子虛賦謂之馬<b>荔</b>、馬<b>荔</b>猶言馬藺也。<b>荔</b>，葉似薤而大，則馬薤之所名也。」</span></p></li>
