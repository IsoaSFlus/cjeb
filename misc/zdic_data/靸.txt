						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>靸</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄚˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>〔～鞋〕ａ．一种草制的拖鞋；ｂ．鞋帮纳得很密、前面有皮脸的布鞋。</li>    <li>方言，把布鞋后帮踩在脚后跟下；穿（拖鞋）：～着鞋。</li></ol>
