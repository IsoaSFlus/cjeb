                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">驴</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>驴</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">驢</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lǘ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǘ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄩˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 哺乳动物，像马，比马小，能驮东西、拉车、耕田、供人骑乘：～骡。～皮胶（亦称“阿胶”）。驴皮影。～唇不对马嘴。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>驴</strong></p><p>驢 <span class="dicpy">lǘ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǘ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，盧声。本义:驴子。家畜名)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ass;burro;donkey]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驴，似马，长耳。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“按，‘驴、骡、駃騠’ <span class="diczx2">太史公</span>皆谓为 <span class="diczx2">匈奴</span>奇畜，本中国所不用，故字皆不见经传，盖 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>人造之耳。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">腾驾罢牛，骖蹇驴兮。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《吊屈原文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骐骥不能与罢驴为驷。——<span class="smcs">《史记·日者列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">黔</span>无驴。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">主将适蝼而麓不闻。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驴不胜怒。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:驴头(驴马头。骂人的话);驴王(指凶残恶狠的人);驴年(古代以生肖纪年，十二肖中没有驴。比喻没有期限);驴券(比喻为文或说话不得要领);驴颓(骂男子的粗话。指公驴的生殖器);驴辇(用驴驾引的车子);驴生戟角(比喻不可能发生的事);驴鸣犬吠(比喻文章的鄙俗);驴前马后(贱称仆役、随从。饥笑人自甘落后)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 詈词。如:驴鸟(骂男人的粗话);驴熊(孬种);驴颓(驴鸟)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
