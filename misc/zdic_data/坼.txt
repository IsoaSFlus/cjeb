                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坼</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从土，斥声。本义:裂开，分裂) 同本义 <span class="encs">[crack;split open]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坼，裂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，土裂。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天旱地坼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span>。注:&ldquo;燥裂也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不坼不副。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;生民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百果草木皆甲坼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;解》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坼甲(草木种子外皮开裂而萌芽);坼腹(剖腹。指难产。剖腹而生)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>坼</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>裂缝 <span class="encs">[crack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卜人占坼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;占人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坼兆(龟甲、兽骨占卜时，裂纹所显示的预兆);坼罅(裂缝)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坼</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中" target="_blank">丑集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中&amp;bs=土" target="_blank">土</a>】
                              坼·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/577C.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】丑格切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】恥格切。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/245B4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤖴</span>。裂也。【禮&middot;月令】仲冬，地始坼。【後漢&middot;安帝紀】日南地坼長百餘里。【晉史&middot;天文志】惠帝中，張華爲司空，三台星坼。</p>
<p>又植物房初開也。【易&middot;解彖傳】雷雨作，而百果草木皆甲坼。</p>
<p>又坼副，難產也。【詩&middot;大雅】先生如達，不坼不副。【註】言姜嫄生后稷之易也。【史記&middot;楚世家】陸終妻女嬇生子六人。坼剖而產。</p>
<p>又龜坼。【周禮&middot;春官】史占墨卜人占坼。今江淮閒天旱田裂亦曰龜坼。</p>
<p>又或作宅。【揚雄&middot;蜀都賦】百果甲宅。【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22BEA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢯪</span>。【九經字樣】坼<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21369.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡍩</span>拆<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。坼原字从土从厈。</p>
