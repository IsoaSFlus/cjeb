                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>掣</strong> <span class="dicpy">chè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，制声。本义:牵引，拉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[pull]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掣，拽也。——<span class="smcs">《经典释文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见舆曳，其牛掣。——<span class="smcs">《易·睽》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流火掣空飞。——</span><span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《雪夜感怀同黄道元作》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纷纷暮雪下辕门，风掣红旗冻不翻。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:掣后腿;掣手;掣脱(决裂;弄僵);掣撰(掣曳，牵引之意)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 牵制;控制 <span class="encs">[control]</span>。如:掣制(牵制);掣搦(拘牵，牵制)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 抽;拔 <span class="encs">[draw]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">羲之</span>密从后掣其笔不得。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·王献之传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:掣签(清代制度，候补的地方官吏抽签确定任职省分。具备一定条件的，可以自选省分，称<span class="yhcs">“指分”</span>);掣兵(抽调兵马);掣开(抽出);掣身(抽身)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/掣电">掣电</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/掣肘">掣肘</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/掣子">掣子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/63A3.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>尺制切，音懘。【爾雅·釋訓】甹夆，掣曳也。【易·睽卦】見輿曳，其牛掣。【註】滯隔不進也。【唐書·陸贄傳】可任，則當要之於終，不宜掣肘於內也。【劉邵·飛白贊】素翰冰解，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>墨電掣。直準箭馳，屈擬蠖勢。或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22CC5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢳅</span>。</p><p>又作摯。</p><p>又【唐韻】昌列切【集韻】【韻會】尺列切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>滯入聲。義同。亦挽也。</p><p>又揭也，取也。【晉書·王獻之傳】七八歲時學書，羲之從後掣其筆不得。</p><p>又【正韻】敕列切，音徹。義同。【類篇】通作挈。</p><p>又【玉篇】掣同<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24E2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤸪</span>。牽也。【說文】引縱曰<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24E2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤸪</span>。从手，瘛省聲，尺制切。【六書故】瘛瘲，謂小兒風驚，乍掣乍縱。掣，搐也，縱則掣而乍舒也。瘛瘲因掣縱立文，今乃作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24E2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤸪</span>，更从瘛，失之甚矣。</p>
