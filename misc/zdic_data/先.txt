                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">先</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>先</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄢ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>时间在前的，次序在前的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">后</span>&rdquo;相对：～前。～期。原～。～驱。～河（中国古代帝王先祭黄河，后祭海，以河为海的本源，后称倡导在先的事物）。～觉（<span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span>）。～见之明。～发制人。</li>	<li>家族或民族的较早的一代或几代：～人。～世（祖先）。～民。</li>	<li>对死去的人的尊称：～祖。～父。～哲（指已去世的有才德的思想家）。～烈。～贤。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">先</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>先</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。据甲骨文，上面是&ldquo;止&rdquo;(脚)，下面是&ldquo;人&rdquo;。意思是脚已走在人的前面。本义:前进，走在前面)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[advance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先，前进也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旌蔽日兮敌若云，矢交坠兮士争先。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《九歌&middot;国殇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恐为</span><span class="diczx2">操</span>所先。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:身先士卒;争先恐后;争先(争着赶到别人前头)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 尊崇;重视 <span class="encs">[attach importance to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安宁则长庠序，先本绌末，以礼义防于利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;本准书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:先本(重视根本)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 首创;开始 <span class="encs">[initiate;begin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不为福先，不为祸始。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;刻意》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:先志(先立志向);先事(先行其事)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>先</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 当初;先前 <span class="encs">[first]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丢三忘四，惹人抱怨，竟不大象先了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:先起头(从前);先来(先前，原来);先日(从前;日前)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 先世;祖先 <span class="encs">[ancestry;ancestors]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仆之先非有剖符丹书之功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行莫丑于辱先。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任少卿书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">思厥先祖父。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先典攸高。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先西域人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>、 <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">侯方域</span><span class="smcs">《壮悔堂文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先茔在杭(先茔:祖先的坟墓。杭:浙江省杭州市)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">行为士先。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>先</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄒㄧㄢ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>xiān <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3998.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">祖先。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;司馬遷&middot;報任少卿書》：「行莫醜於辱<b>先</b>，詬莫大於宮刑。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">首要的事務。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;學記》：「建國君民，教學為<b>先</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">原先、先前。</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第七二回》：「這兩日比<b>先</b>又添了些病，所以支持不住。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">先生的簡稱。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷六七&middot;梅福傳》：「夫叔孫<b>先</b>非不忠也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋時晉國有先軫。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對已去世者的尊稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「先父」、「先人」、「先聖先賢」、「革命先烈」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">時間或次序在前。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「先發制人」、「近水樓臺<b>先</b>得月」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;衛靈公》：「工欲善其事，必先利其器。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈惠崇春江晚景〉詩二首之一：「春江水暖鴨<b>先</b> 知。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">暫時。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「你<b>先</b>不要慌，我們慢慢想辦法來解決。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">倡導、先行。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;子路》：「衛君待子而為政，子將奚<b>先</b>？」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;曹植&middot;求自試表》：「突刃觸鋒，為士卒<b>先</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">時間居前。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈師說〉：「生乎吾前，其聞道也，固<b>先</b>乎吾，吾從而師之。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">事前聯繫、介紹。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷四三&middot;酈食其傳》：「此真吾所願從游，莫為我<b>先</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">領導、率導。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;郊特牲》：「天<b>先</b>乎地，君<b>先</b>乎臣。」</span></p></li>
