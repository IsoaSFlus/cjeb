                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>祷</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">禱</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄠˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>教徒或迷信的人向天、神求助、求福：～文。～告。～念。祈～。</li>    <li>祝愿，敬辞（书信用语）：为～。盼～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">祷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>祷</strong></p><p>禱 <span class="dicpy">dǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从示，寿声。向神求福的一种迷信行为)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 祈祷，祈神求福 <span class="encs">[pray]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祷，告事求福也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祷，祭也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五曰祷。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大祝》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“祷，贺庆言福祚之辞。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">候禳祷词之祝号。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小祝》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“求福谓之祷，报赛谓之祠。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既伯既祷。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·吉日》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子</span>疾病， <span class="diczx2">子路</span>请祷。——<span class="smcs">《论语·述而》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦襄王</span>病，百姓为之祷;病愈，杀牛塞祷。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说右下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以身祷于桑林之际。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·主术》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:祷巫(犹言巫祝祈祷);祷祀(有事祷求鬼神而致祭);祷雨(祈神降雨);祷祈(祈祷);祷牲(祈祷时所用的祭牲)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 盼望——书信中用作敬词，表示期望和请求 <span class="encs">[ask earnestly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君臣不同道，下以名祷。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·扬权》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:切祷;是所至祷;祷切(书信客套用语，表示祈求急切);祷盼(书信客套用语，表示祈求、盼望)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/祷词">祷词</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/祷告">祷告</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/祷念">祷念</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/祷文">祷文</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/祷祝">祷祝</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
