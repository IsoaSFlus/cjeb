                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缚</strong></p><p>縛 <span class="dicpy">fù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，表示与线丝或缠织等有关。尃( <span class="dicpy">fū</span>)声。本义:捆绑)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缚，束也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">战之明日，</span><span class="diczx2">晋襄公</span>缚 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>囚。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主缚者亦然，不如所欲，缚时即先折筋骨。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:缚人(捆绑人);缚绑(捆绑);缚住(捆住);缚虎(捆住猛虎。比喻征服极难征服之人);缚送(捆住押送);缚扎(捆扎)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 约束;限制 <span class="encs">[tie up]</span>。如:缚束(裹扎);缚裤(扎紧套裤脚管，以便骑乘。泛指戎装)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>缚</strong></p><p>縛 <span class="dicpy">fù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 绳索 <span class="encs">[string;rope]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夜半，童自转，以缚即炉火烧绝之。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:释缚(解缚。解绳)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 量词。用于捆起来的东西。如:一缚书(一捆书)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
