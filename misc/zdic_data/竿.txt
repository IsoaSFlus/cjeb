						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>竿</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>竹子的主干：竹～。立～见影。揭～而起。</li><li>特指“钓鱼竿”：垂～。投～。～钓。</li><li>古代指竹简：～牍（书信）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bamboo pole; penis</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> perche,canne</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>竿</strong> <span class="dicpy">gān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，干声。本义:竹竿)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。竹子的主干 <span class="encs">[bamboo pole]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">竿，竹梃也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">籊籊竹竿。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·竹竿》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禁藏母拊竿。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">竿殳之所揘毕。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大书一<span class="yhcs">“屈”</span>字，以两竿夹揭之，走诉行御史台。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:立竿见影;揭竿为旗;竿木(古代艺人借以在其上表演各种技艺动作的长竿);竿殳(竹杖);竿首(竹竿顶端);竿头(竹竿的顶端)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“简”</span>。竹简 <span class="encs">[bamboo script]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不离苞苴竿牍。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·列御寇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">阳翟</span><span class="diczx2">田望</span>勤于竿牍……日发数十函不倦。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">朱彧</span><span class="smcs">《萍洲可谈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:竿牍(指书信，古代用竹简书写，故称竿牍)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 特指钓竿 <span class="encs">[fishing rod]</span>。如:垂竿;竿纶(钓竿与钓线)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指竹篙 <span class="encs">[punt-pole]</span>。如:竿枻(竹篙和船桨)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>竿</strong> <span class="dicpy">gān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 计算长状物的单位，竹一根为一竿</p><p><span class="diczx1">一径野花香袭体，数竿幽竹绿依依。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如果开窗正对着白色墙壁，太单调了，给补上几竿竹子或几棵芭蕉。——<span class="smcs">《苏州园林》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 方言。用于抽旱烟</p><p><span class="diczx1">约摸咂完一竿叶子烟的时候，…听见…有人朝房外跑走的脚步声。——<span class="smcs">《大波》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>竿</strong> <span class="dicpy">gān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 以竿悬挂 <span class="encs">[hang with a pole]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">应元</span>乘月黑，束槀为人，人竿一灯，立陴郳间。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:竿旌(旗杆顶端所饰的雉羽);竿首(用竿悬首)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“干”</span>。请托 <span class="encs">[request another’s help]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">竿摩，谓相逼近也。今俗以事干人者谓之相竿摩。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·董卓传》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:竿摩(干求，请托)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr
