                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>漱</strong></p><p>潄 <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形声。# 同本义 <span class="encs">[gargle;rinse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">漱，盪口也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咸盥漱。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡内外，鸡初鸣，咸盥漱。——<span class="smcs">《礼·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:漱盂(盛漱口水的器皿);漱流(以流水漱口。比喻隐居生活);漱盥(漱口盥洗)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 洗涤 <span class="encs">[wash]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冠带垢，和灰清漱。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:漱涤(洗涤);漱澣(洗涤);漱浣(洗涤);漱濯(洗涤)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 冲刷;冲荡 <span class="encs">[erode]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悬泉瀑布，飞漱其间。——</span><span class="diczx2">郦道元</span><span class="smcs">《水经注·江水》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">善沟者水漱之。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·匠人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">石泉漱琼瑶。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《招隐诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沈液漱陈根。——</span><span class="diczx2">张协</span><span class="smcs">《杂诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:漱石(冲刷岩石);漱石枕流(枕石漱流。比喻士人的隐居生活);漱玉(形容山泉激石，飞流溅白，晶莹如玉);漱啮(侵蚀，冲荡)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 吮吸，饮 <span class="encs">[suck]</span>。如:漱腴(汲取精华);漱墨(汲墨)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/漱口">漱口</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
