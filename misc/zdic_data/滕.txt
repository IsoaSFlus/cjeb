<p><span class="dicpy">t&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄥˊ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>滕</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，朕( <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n</span>)声。本义:水向上腾涌)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[surge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滕，水超涌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百川沸滕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滕，<span class="smcs">《诗》</span>曰:&ldquo;百川沸滕&rdquo;。水上涌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为张口说话 <span class="encs">[open-mouth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滕口说也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;咸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">范君</span>独判于心，不畏滕口。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韦瓘</span><span class="smcs">《宣州南陵县大农陂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:滕口(张口放言)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>滕</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 西周分封的侯国名 <span class="encs">[Teng state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春秋</span><span class="diczx2">滕</span>侯， <span class="diczx2">文王</span>子 <span class="diczx2">叔绣</span>之后。国在今 <span class="diczx2">山东</span><span class="diczx2">衮州府</span><span class="diczx2">滕县</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 在今山东省滕县一带</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 县名 <span class="encs">[Teng county]</span>。在山东省</p>
