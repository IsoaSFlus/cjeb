                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朴</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>朴</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">樸</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄨˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>没有细加工的木料，喻不加修饰：～素。～实。～厚。～质。</li>	<li>朴实，朴素：质～。俭～。</li></ol><p>● <strong>朴</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">p&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄛˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>朴树，落叶乔木，花淡黄色。木材可制器具。</li></ol><p>● <strong>朴</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pō.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄛ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～刀〕古代的一种武器，窄长有短把的刀，双手使用。</li></ol><p>● <strong>朴</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pi&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pi&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧㄠˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> simple, unadorned; sincere; surname; a tree</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Pu  (Eig, Fam)​,schlicht und einfach  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> microcoulier de Chine,Celtis sinensis var. japonica,(nom de famille Piao)​,(nom de famille Pak en coréen)​</p></div>
