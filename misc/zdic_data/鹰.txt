                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鹰</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>鹰</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鷹</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 鸟类的一科，猛禽类，嘴钩曲，趾有钩爪，十分锐利，捕食小兽和其他鸟类，猎人可驯养帮助打猎：～犬。～隼（鹰和隼，喻凶猛或凶猛的人）。～视狼步（喻人举止凶狠）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> falcon; Accipiter species (various)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Adler (S, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> aigle,milan</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鹰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>鹰</strong> <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鸟。本义:鹰属的各种鸟) 同本义 <span class="encs">[hawk]</span>许多白天活动的隼形目(Falconiformes)隼亚目(Falcones)猛禽，是鹰科(Accipitridae)部分种类鸟的通称，一般指鹰属各种鸟</p><p><span class="diczx1">鹰翅疾如风，鹰爪利如锥。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《放鹰》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:鹰鸷(猛禽);鹰人(驯鹰的人);鹰师(训练老鹰的人);鹰架(用竹木制成、用来提取重物的支架;亦指老鹰栖立的架子);鹰拿燕雀(比喻容易攫取捕捉);鹰觑鹘望(形容眼光如鹰鹘般锐利)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 比喻爪牙 <span class="encs">[cat’s paw]</span>。如:鹰儿爪(爪牙;帮凶);鹰犬(比喻为虎作伥供人指使行恶的爪牙)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形容勇猛，激进 <span class="encs">[of valour;intrepid]</span>。如:鹰胜(喻指勇士);鹰派(国际名词。好战派)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 形容凶狠，贪戾 <span class="encs">[ferocious;violent]</span>。如:鹰视狼顾(形容窥视而欲有所攫取);鹰扬(喻大逞淫威);鹰视(飞鹰欲攫取食物时，露出凶狠的目光。比喻贪戾之人，瞻视如鹰);鹰视狼步(视物如鹰，行步如狼。形容外貌凶狠);鹰瞵鹗视(视物如鹰鹗。形容威猛凶悍，伺机猎取的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 威武，雄健 <span class="encs">[powerful;mighty]</span>。如:鹰武(威武。比喻大展雄才);鹰扬(威武，逞威，如鹰之飞扬)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鹰鼻鹞眼">鹰鼻鹞眼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鹰派人物">鹰派人物</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鹰犬">鹰犬</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鹰隼">鹰隼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鹰洋">鹰洋</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鷹</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“鹰”字头，请参考“鷹”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中" target="_blank">亥集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中&amp;bs=鳥" target="_blank">鳥</a>】
                              鷹·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=23" target="_blank">23</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9DF9.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>於陵切，音膺。【玉篇】鷙鳥。李時珍曰：鷹以膺擊，故謂之鷹。陸佃云：一歲曰黃鷹，二歲曰鴘鷹，鴘次赤也。三歲曰鶬鷹，今通謂之角鷹，頂有毛角微起。一曰題肩，一曰征鳥，一曰爽鳩。【左傳·昭十七年】爽鳩氏。【註】鷹也，一作鶆鳩。【爾雅·釋鳥】鷹鶆鳩。</p><p>又【本草】虎鷹，翼廣丈餘，能搏虎。【裴氏新書】鷹在衆鳥閒，若睡寐然，故積怒而後全剛生焉。詩大雅：維師尚父，時維鷹揚。言其武之奮揚也。【正字通】鷹雄形小雌體大，生於窟者好眠，巢於木者常立，雙骹長者起遲，六翮短者飛急。禮月令，季夏，鷹乃學習。註：鶵學飛。</p><p>又季秋，鷹乃祭鳥。註：欲食鳥，先殺鳥不食。毛氏曰：本作䧹，後人加鳥字，不知䧹已从隹矣。徐鍇曰：鷹隨人指縱，故从人。</p>

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/28FF3_10193.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28FF3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨿳</span>)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24E30.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤸰</span>鳥也。</span><span class="swdz">左傳。如鷹鸇之逐鳥雀。釋鳥。鷹來鳩。郭云。來當爲爽。按左傳。爽鳩氏司宼也。杜曰。爽鳩、鷹也。</span><span class="ywyy">从隹。从人。</span><span class="swdz">鍇曰。鷹隨人所指䡮。故從人。按雁鴈亦从人。</span><span class="ywyy">瘖省聲。</span><span class="swdz">此七字依韵會訂。瘖在七部而？在六部者、合韵冣近也。於陵切。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/28FF3_10194.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A1FF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪇿</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28FF3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨿳</span>。从鳥。</span><span class="swdz">按？葢古文也。小篆從之。从隹、从瘖省聲。籒文則从鳥而應省聲。非兼用隹鳥也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
