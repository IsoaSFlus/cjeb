                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>酥</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>酪，用牛羊奶制成的食物：～酪。～油。</li>
	<li>松脆，多指食物：～脆。～糖。桃～。</li>
	<li>柔腻松软：～胸。～松。</li>
	<li>身体酸软无力：～软。～麻。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> butter; flaky, crispy, light, fluffy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> flockig, flockige ,knusprig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> croquant,croustillant,sablé,mou,flasque</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">酥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>酥</strong> <span class="dicpy">sū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从禾，从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)。酥指食品，因而与禾、酉(代表酒)有关。本义:酪类。由牛羊乳制成。又称&ldquo;酥油&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[butter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十万军城百万类，酥油香暖夜如烝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛能</span><span class="smcs">《影灯夜》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酥团(用牛羊乳制成的酪类食品，色白光润);酥酡(古印度以乳酪作成的食品);酥酪(以牛羊乳所制成的一种食品);酥蜜(酥酪与蜂蜜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 酒 <span class="encs">[wine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天竺国谓酒为酥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">窦平</span><span class="smcs">《酒谱&middot;异域酒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酥酒(古时酒名);酥醪(奶酒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 面粉加酒、糖等制成的一种点心，松而易碎 <span class="encs">[shortbread]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已倾潘子错著水，更觅君家为甚酥。(为甚酥，一种油果名)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《戏刘监食求米粉饼》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塞北送酥一盒至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:桃酥;芝麻酥;酥饼;酥糖(用面粉、芝麻与糖制成的食品);酥签(一种酥松的糕点)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 搽脸的油脂 <span class="encs">[paste]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">暖酥消，腻云亸，终日厌厌倦梳</span><span class="diczx2">裹</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《定风波》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>酥</strong> <span class="dicpy">sū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 松脆而易碎的 <span class="encs">[crisp]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:酥脆;香酥鸡</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 肢体松软的。缺乏力量、肌肉松弛的 <span class="encs">[limp;soft;weak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">休道冲动那厮，这一会儿连</span><span class="diczx2">小闲</span>也酥倒了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《元曲选&middot;关汉卿&middot;救风尘》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酥懈(酸软倦怠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 具有缎子般的光泽和光滑的;光洁细腻的 <span class="encs">[(of the skin)satiny]</span>。</p>
