                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蜇</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhé  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄜˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 〔海～〕海里生的一种腔肠动物，形状像张开的伞，可供食用。简称“蜇”，如“～头”，“～皮”。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> jellyfish</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> stechen (Insekt)​  (V, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> piquer,méduse</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蜇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蜇</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhē</span></p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 毒虫叮刺 <span class="encs">[sting]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苦咸酸辛，虽蜇吻裂鼻，缩舍涩齿，而咸有笃好之者。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《读韩愈所著毛颖传后题》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蜇杀(毒虫叮咬致死);蝎子蜇人;蜇螫(毒蛇咬)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 刺痛;刺伤 <span class="encs">[stab]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乡豪取而尝之，蜇于口，惨于腹。——<span class="smcs">《列子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:切洋葱蜇眼睛;蜇鼻(刺激鼻子而感到微痛)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 折磨;毒害;苦楚 <span class="encs">[rack;torment;torture;suffering]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如来!我师徒受了万蜇千魔，自东土拜到此处，蒙如来吩咐传经。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蜇</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 腔肠动物。即<span class="yhcs">“水母”</span>。俗称<span class="yhcs">“海蜇”</span> <span class="encs">[jellyfish]</span>。身体作半球形，上面有伞状部分，俗称<span class="yhcs">“海蜇皮”</span>。下面有口腔八条，俗称<span class="yhcs">“海蜇头”</span>。可食。如:海蜇;蜇花(指海蜇腹下的众多的脚)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhē</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
