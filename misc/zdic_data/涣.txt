						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>涣</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄢˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 散开：～散。～然冰释（像冰遇到热，一下子消融；喻疑虑、困难或误会完全消除）。</p></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>涣</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，奂( <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:流散，离散)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[disperse;disappear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涣，流散也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">方涣涣兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·溱洧》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涣者离也。——<span class="smcs">《易·序卦传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则涣然离矣。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·刑法志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涣兮。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“涣者解散。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涣乎成功。——<span class="smcs">《刘熊碑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">功德涣彰。——<span class="smcs">《戚伯著碑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:涣然冰释(如冰之溶解消散。多用以指误会、疑难的消散);涣驰(涣散。分散，不集中);涣泮(消释，融化);涣衍(蔓延，扩散)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 帝王发布号令 <span class="encs">[issue imperial edict]</span>。如:涣汗(流汗。比喻帝王的圣旨、号令，有如汗出于肤，无法收回);涣命(帝王的诏命);涣诏(帝王的恩诏);涣发(特指帝王发布号令);涣号(指帝王的旨令，恩旨)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>涣</strong> <span class="dicpy">huàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 水很大的 <span class="encs">[overflowing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">清音应空谷，潜波涣寒塘。——</span><span class="diczx2">吕同老</span><span class="smcs">《丹泉》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又泛指盛貌</p><p><span class="diczx1">纂辞奋笔，涣若不思。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span>文</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 鲜明的。同<span class="yhcs">“焕”</span> <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涣烂兮其溢目也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·延笃传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:涣烂(光明灿烂的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
