                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>式</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p><ol><li>物体外形的样子：～样。样～。</li><li>特定的规格：格～。程～。</li><li>典礼，有特定内容的仪式：开幕～。阅兵～。</li><li>自然科学中表明某些关系或规律的一组符号：分子～。算～。公～。</li><li>一种语法范畴，表示说话者对所说事情的主观态度：叙述～。命令～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">式</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>式</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从工，弋( <span class="dicpy">yì</span>)声。工有<span class="yhcs">“矩”</span>的意思。本义:法度;规矩)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[law;moral standard;established practics;rule]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">式，法也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">式，法也。——<span class="smcs">《周书·谥法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">下士之式。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·下武》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“法也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三曰筮式。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·篔人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓筮制作法式也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌妇式之法。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·典妇功》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九式。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓用财之节度。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:式度(规制，法式);式则(法则;规范)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 榜样;楷模 <span class="encs">[example;model]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">世世享德，百邦作式。——<span class="smcs">《书·微子之命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孝敬之准式。——</span><span class="diczx2">箫统</span><span class="smcs">《文选序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">式箕之门。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·韩良传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“亦表也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:式范(模范，楷模)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 规格;样式 <span class="encs">[norm;specification;standard;fashion;form;figure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正以音律调韵，取高前式。——</span><span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《谢灵运传论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:新式;旧式;式盘(中国古代推<span class="yhcs">“算”</span>历数或占卜的工具。分天地盘。天盘为圆形，地盘为正方形)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 仪式，典礼 <span class="encs">[ceremony;ritual]</span>。如:开幕式;毕业式</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 自然科学中表现某种规律的一组符号 <span class="encs">[formula]</span>。如:方程式;分子式;公式;样式</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 时态 <span class="encs">[tense]</span>。如:过去式;现在式;将来式</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 动词的一种语气 <span class="encs">[mood]</span>。如:叙述式</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>式</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 效法 <span class="encs">[follow the example of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仪式刑</span><span class="diczx2">文王</span>之典。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·我将》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使诸大夫国人皆有所矜式。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使人主师</span><span class="diczx2">五帝</span>而式 <span class="diczx2">三王</span>。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·崔寔传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:式型(效法，取法);式瞻(敬仰，景慕)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 示范，作为榜样 <span class="encs">[set an example]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王命</span><span class="diczx2">申伯</span>，式是南邦。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·崧高》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:示规(示范);示望(仰望);式慰(安慰);式好(兄弟和好)</p><p><span
