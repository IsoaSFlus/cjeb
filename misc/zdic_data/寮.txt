						<p>● <strong>寮</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄠˊ</span></p>

	<li>小屋：茅～。茶～酒肆。</li>
	<li>小窗。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/僚">僚</a>&rdquo;，官。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>寮</strong> <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span>)，表示与房屋有关，尞( <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:小窗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[small window]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寮，穿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寮，小窗也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">交绮豁以疏寮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皦日笼光于绮寮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原来是大人家，有两层窗寮，外面为窗，里面为寮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金瓶梅词话》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;僚&rdquo;。百官;官吏。后多作&ldquo;僚&rdquo; <span class="encs">[official]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">同官为寮，吾尝同寮，敢不尽心乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今吾子之教官寮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勿寮勿从。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:寮佐(僚属佐吏);寮寀(百官。同僚寀);寮人(做官的人，官员);寮庶(百官，众官);寮位(官位);寮吏(属吏);寮司(各衙署长官);寮属(僚属;属官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 小屋 <span class="encs">[small house]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:草寮(小茅屋);僧寮(僧人居住的小屋);茶寮(茶舍);寮子(小屋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 民族名 <span class="encs">[Liao nationality]</span>。老挝国内的主要民族。泰国北部及东北部也有寮人</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">小窗。</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝梁&middot;簡文帝〈序愁賦〉：「玩飛花之入戶，看斜暉之度<b>寮</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;孔尚任《桃花扇&middot;第四○齣》：「問秦淮舊日窗<b>寮</b>，破紙迎風，壞檻當潮。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">簡陋的屋舍。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「菜<b>寮</b>」、「工<b>寮</b>」、「菇<b>寮</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;陸游〈貧居〉詩：「囊空如客路，屋窄似僧<b>寮</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">官吏。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;皮日休〈何武傳〉：「雖劇<b>寮</b>貴吏，皆得辱殺之。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「僚」。</span></p></li>

<p>又叶凌如切，音閭。【傅毅&middot;洛都賦】革服朔，正官寮。辨方位，摹八區。</p>
