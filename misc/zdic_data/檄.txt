                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">檄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>檄</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古代官府用以征召或声讨的文书：“此臣之所谓传～而千里定者也”。～文。羽～。～移（文体名，“檄文”与“移文”的合称。檄文多用于声讨和征伐；移文多用于晓喻或责备）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> call arms; urgency</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anordnen, gliedern</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> déclaration de guerre</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>檄</strong> <span class="dicpy">xí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:檄文)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a call to arms in ancient times]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">太祖</span>得书，便命移檄。——<span class="smcs">《英烈传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">制府檄下。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所有文檄，幕下儒生设意修词(<span class="yhcs">“文檄”</span>即檄文。古代用以征召、晓喻或声讨的文书)。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奉檄守御。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:传檄(传布檄文);羽檄(上插鸟羽的古代征兵军书)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指信函 <span class="encs">[letter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使君子年善?来檄可求。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《寄丁中允》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>檄</strong> <span class="dicpy">xí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用檄文晓谕或声讨 <span class="encs">[announce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">按察司行文檄了知县去。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">檄其人士，于政治宗教，皆欲有所更革，顾终不成。——</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《坟·摩罗诗力说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:檄调(行檄调动);檄委(用檄文晓谕、托咐)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/檄文">檄文</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>檄</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄒㄧˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>xí <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3869.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代用于征召、声讨等的官方文书。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷九二·淮阴侯传》：「今大王举而东，三秦可传檄而定也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·曹植·白马篇》：「羽<b>檄</b>从北来，厉马登高堤。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用檄文饬令、告知等。</span><span class="gc_yy">《聊斋志异·卷一一·王者》：「于是急<b>檄</b>属官，设法补解讫。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史·第五回》：「汤知县把这情由细细写了个禀帖，禀知按察司。按察司行文书<b>檄</b>了知县去。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6A84.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】胡狄切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>狄切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音薂。【說文】下尺書也。【顏師古曰】檄者，以木<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>爲書，長尺二寸，用徵召也。【前漢·申屠嘉傳】爲檄召通。</p><p>又有急，則加以雞羽揷之，示速疾也。【史記·漢高紀】以羽檄徵天下兵。</p><p>又【韻會】陳彼之惡，說此之德，曉諭百姓之書也。</p><p>又曰：檄，皎也，明言此使令皎然而識也。漢《司馬相如·諭巴蜀檄》、魏《陳琳·討曹操檄》皆是。</p><p>又【逸雅】檄，激也。下官所以激迎其上之書文也。</p><p>又長檄，印封長牒也。【後漢·安帝紀】民窮困道路，欲歸本郡，在所爲封長檄。</p><p>又檄櫂直上。【爾雅·釋木】無枝爲檄。</p><p>又檄糴，疾貌。【嵆康·琴賦】懰檄糴以奔邀。</p><p>又【唐韻】胡敎切，音效。【郭璞·蜜蜂賦】誅戮峻于鈇鉞，招徵速乎羽檄。集不謀而同期，動不安而齊約。</p><p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6A84_3007.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(檄)</span><span class="ywyy">尺二書。</span><span class="swdz">各本作二尺書。小徐繫傳已佚。見韵會者作尺二書。葢古本也。李賢注光武紀曰。說文以木<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>爲書。長尺二寸。謂之檄。以徵召也。與前書高紀注同。此葢出演說文。故語加詳。云尺二寸與鍇本合。但漢人多言尺一。未知其分別之詳。後漢書、水經注皆曰。李雲上書云。孔子言帝者、諦也。今尺一拜用。不經御省。是帝欲不諦乎。又後漢書儒林傳云。詔曰。乞楊生師。卽尺一出<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26AF5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦫵</span>。文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨕖</span>注引蕭子良古今篆<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23729.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣜩</span>文體曰。鶴頭書、偃波書俱詔板所用。在漢時則謂之尺一<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>。獨<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣃔</span>曰。策書長二尺。以命諸侯王三公。三公以罪免。亦賜策。以尺一木。</span><span class="ywyy">從木。敫聲。</span><span class="swdz">胡狄切。古音在二部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
