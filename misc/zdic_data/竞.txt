class="encs">[compete;contest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">競，逐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">职竞用力。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑柔》</span></span></p><p><span class="encs">[argue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前在修文令曹，有</span><span class="diczx2">山东</span>学士与 <span class="diczx2">关中</span>太史竞历。——<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训》</span>。 <span class="encs">[strong]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心则不竞。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:竞朗(刚正爽利);竞爽(精明强干)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 繁忙的<span class="encs">[busy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敝邑有社稷之事，使肥与有职竞焉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 小心谨慎 <span
