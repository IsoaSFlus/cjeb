						<p>● <strong>剖</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pōu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄡˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>破开：～开。解～。～面。～腹。</li>
	<li>分析，分辩：～白。～解。～析。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>剖</strong> <span class="dicpy">pōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，咅( <span class="dicpy">pǒu</span>)声。本义:破开，中分)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cut open;split open]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剖，判也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剖，分也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剖伛之背。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;宋策》</span>。注:&ldquo;劈也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">比干</span>剖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;胠箧》</span>。释文:&ldquo;谓割心也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剖刑而莫邪已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;彊国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剖冰济水，不惮艰难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吴子&middot;料敌》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我先君</span><span class="diczx2">惠公</span>有不腆之田，与女剖分而食之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十四年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予贸得其一，剖之，如有烟扑口鼻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剖心(破开胸腹以割心;开布诚心，竭诚相见);剖判(分割，破开;辨析);剖列(分列);剖割(分割);剖肝沥胆(比喻以赤诚之心相待)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 分辨;分析 <span class="encs">[analyze]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">通人暗于好恶兮，岂昏惑而能剖?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:剖玄(解说深奥的道理);剖决(判决，断事);剖断(分析决断);剖白(分析表白);剖豁(解释);剖决机宜(适时、准确地分析决策);剖明(辨明)</span></p>
