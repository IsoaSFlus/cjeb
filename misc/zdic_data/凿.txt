                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凿</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>凿</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鑿</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">z&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>挖槽或穿孔用的工具，称&ldquo;凿子&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>穿孔，挖掘：～孔。～井。～通。</li>
	<li>器物上的孔，是容纳枘（榫头）的。</li>
	<li>明确，真实：～～。证据确～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> chisel; bore, pierce</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> graben  (V)​,meißeln  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> burin,creuser,percer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凿</strong></p>
<p>鑿 <span class="dicpy">z&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，鑿( <span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;</span>)省声。从金，表示与金属制品有关。本义:凡穿物使通都称凿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cut a hole;chisel or dig]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凿，穿木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">重木刊凿之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士丧礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆是水凿之穴。(凿，这里是冲刷的意思。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凿巾(在覆盖死者脸部的巾上，正对口部的位置，剪开一通孔);凿穴(开造洞穴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 挖掘;开凿 <span class="encs">[dig]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凿地为坎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水凿之穴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水凿之处。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千锤万凿出深山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">于谦</span><span class="smcs">《石灰吟》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凿溉(开渠灌溉);凿龙(开凿龙门。指大禹治水，凿龙门以导流);凿饮(掘井而饮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 开通 <span class="encs">[open up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然</span><span class="diczx2">张骞</span>凿空，其后使往者皆称 <span class="diczx2">博望侯</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;张骞列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 穿凿附会 <span class="encs">[give strained interpretations and draw far fetched analogies]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所恶于智者，为其凿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;离娄下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凿说(穿凿附会之说);凿脱(穿凿附会造成失误);凿言(穿凿附会地说)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 戳，用手指头猛地一推 <span class="encs">[jab]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那婆子揪住</span><span class="diczx2">郓哥</span>，凿上两个栗暴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 舂米使之精白 <span class="encs">[polish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粢食不凿，昭其俭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凿八(指捣米一石，可得八斗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 引申为冲刷 <span class="encs">[wash]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如大小龙湫、水帘、初月谷之类，皆是水凿之穴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 敲击，捶打 <span class="encs">[beat;strick]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鑿</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“凿”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/鑿">鑿</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=金" target="_blank">金</a>】
                              鑿·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=27" target="_blank">27</a>　·部外筆画：19</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/947F.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28C12.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨰒</span>【唐韻】在各切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】疾各切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音昨。【廣韻】鏨也。【古史考】孟莊子作鑿。【師古曰】鑿所以穿木也。</p>
<p>又【說文】穿木也。【易&middot;繫辭&middot;刳木爲舟疏】刳鑿其中。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;豳風】二之日鑿冰冲冲。【朱註】鑿冰，取冰於山也。</p>
<p>又黥<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>。【前漢&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>法志】其次用鑽鑿。【註】鑿，黥<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>也。</p>
<p>又開也。【前漢&middot;張騫傳】然騫鑿空。【註】鑿，開也。空，通也。騫始開通西域道也。</p>
<p>又造也。【公羊傳&middot;成十三年】公鑿行也。【註】鑿，猶更造之意。【釋文】鑿，在洛反。猶造意也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】六情曰六鑿。【莊子&middot;外物篇】心無天游，則六鑿相攘。【註】六情也。</p>
<p>又【正字通】恣意不求合義理，謂之鑿。【孟子】爲其鑿也。【前漢&middot;禮樂志】以意穿鑿。</p>
<p>又書名。緯書有【乾坤鑿度】。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】則落切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】卽各切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音作。【集韻】鮮明貌。【詩&middot;唐風】白石鑿鑿。【傳】鑿鑿，鮮明貌。【釋文】鑿，子洛反。</p>
<p>又【九章算術】粟率五十，鑿二十四。言粟五斗，爲米二斗四升。【左傳&middot;桓二年】粢食不鑿。【註】不精鑿。【釋文】鑿，子洛反。【杜甫詩】秋菰成黑米，精鑿傳白粲。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>昨木切，音族。【廣韻】鑿鏤花葉。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>在到切，漕去聲。【集韻】穿空也。【增韻】孔寵。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;輪人】凡輻，量其鑿深以爲輻廣。【釋文】鑿，曹報反。</p>
<p>又如字。【前漢&middot;楚元王傳】羊入其鑿。【師古註】鑿，在到反。謂所穿冢藏者。【楚辭&middot;九辯】何時俗之工巧兮，滅規矩而攺鑿。獨耿介而不隨兮，願慕先聖之遺敎。</p>
<p>又【集韻】七到切，音操。宂也。</p>
<p>又【唐韻正】平聲，音漕。【水經注】桓<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>有問鼎之志，乃漕一洲，以充百數。漕卽鑿字。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶脞五切，租上聲。【易林】鉛刀攻玉，無不鑽鑿。龍體具舉，魯班爲輔。</p>
