						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>膏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄠˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>肥，肥肉：～粱（肥肉、细粮）。～腴。～沃。</li>    <li>脂油：春雨如～。～泽（ａ．滋润作物的及时雨；ｂ．喻给予恩惠）。</li>    <li>中医指心尖脂肪，认为是药力达不到的部位：病入～肓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>膏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gào.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄠˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>把油抹在车轴或机械上：～油。</li>    <li>把毛笔蘸上墨汁在砚台边上掭：～笔。～墨。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">膏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>膏</strong> <span class="dicpy">gāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，高声。从肉，表示与肉体有关。本义:溶化的油脂，无角动物的油脂)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fat;grease;oil]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">膏，肥也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，膏者，脂也。凝者曰脂，释者曰膏。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无角者膏。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记·易本命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">膏者，神之液也。——<span class="smcs">《春秋·玄命苞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">内热溲膏。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·则阳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">膏之下。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“心下为膏。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雉膏不食。——<span class="smcs">《易·鼎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自伯之东，首如飞蓬。岂无膏沐，谁适为容?——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·伯兮》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">实以薪草，膏油灌其中。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·周瑜传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">根之茂者其实遂，膏之沃者其光晔。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《答李翊书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:膏液(动植物体内或植物果实内的油脂);膏泽脂香(指油脂类的化妆品);膏髓(脂膏与骨髓);膏馥(膏脂的香味)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 肥肉 <span class="encs">[fat meat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《诗》</span>云:<span class="yhcs">“既醉以酒，既饱以德。”</span>言饱乎仁义也，所以不愿人之膏粱之味也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:膏鲜(指鱼肉一类食物);膏蟹(食用蟹的一种);膏肉(泛指脂肉)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 特指油灯 <span class="encs">[oil lamp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">养其根而竢其实，加其膏而希其光。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《答李翊书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:膏明(灯火照明);膏映(谓灯光照射);膏晷(灯光与日影)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 浓稠的糊状物。引申为药膏 <span
