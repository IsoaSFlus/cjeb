                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诵</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诵</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">誦</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sòng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sòng.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄨㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>用有高低抑扬的腔调念：～读。背～。～诗。</li><li>称述，述说：“王之为都者，臣知五人焉，知其罪者，惟孔距心，为王～之”。</li><li>诗歌：作～（作诗）。</li><li>怨谤。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> recite, chant, repeat</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> laut lesen, vortragen, vorlesen  (V)​,Song  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> lire ou réciter à haute voix</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诵</strong></p><p>誦 <span class="dicpy">sòng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sòng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，甬( <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span>)声。本义:背诵、朗读)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[recite;read aloud]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诵，讽也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“临文为诵。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兴道讽诵言语。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司乐》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“背文曰讽，以声节之曰诵。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">春诵夏弦。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·文王世子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宴居有师工之诵。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子路</span>终身诵之。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子罕》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十五弹箜篌，十六诵诗书。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坐大厦之下而诵<span class="smcs">《诗》</span><span class="smcs">《书》</span>，无奔真诚之劳矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:熟读成诵;过目成诵;背诵;诵经;记诵(默记和背诵);诵笃笃(叽叽咕咕，嘟嘟噜噜);诵咏(诵读吟咏);诵诗(诵读<span class="smcs">《诗经》</span>);诵说(诵读解说);诵数(诵读熟习经书);诵谏(诵读诗歌以作为劝戒);诵忆(背诵并记住);诵号(高声诵经)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 述说，发表详细内容 <span class="encs">[state]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聊为足下诵其所闻。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩佥</span><span class="smcs">《答陈生书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王之为都者，臣知五人焉，知其罪，惟</span><span class="diczx2">孔距心</span>，为王诵之。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑下》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“颂”</span>。颂扬 <span class="encs">[extol]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">本原事业，只诵功德。——</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="smcs">《泰山刻石》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使百姓歌诵，史官纪德。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·何敞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">垂流亿载，世世叹诵。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《司隶校尉杨孟文石门颂》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:诵德(颂功。颂扬功德);诵法(称颂效法);诵烈(颂扬功业)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“讼”</span>。公开 <span class="encs">[declare]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">平阳侯</span>驰语太尉 <span class="diczx2">周勃</span>， <span class="diczx2">勃</span>尚恐不胜，赤敢诵言诛之。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高后纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:诵言(公然言说)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 以婉言、隐语讽谏 <span class="encs">[satirize]</span>。如:诵言(讽劝自己的言语);诵训(古代掌管百工的工师所讽诵的谏言)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>诵</strong></p><p>誦 <span class="dicpy">sòng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sòng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>诗篇 <span class="encs">[poem]</span></p><p><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AA6.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>似用切，音頌。【說文】諷也。【徐曰】臨文爲誦。誦，從也。以口從其文也。【廣韻】讀誦也。【周禮·春官·大司樂】以樂語敎國子：興、道、諷、誦、言、語。【註】倍文曰諷，以聲節之曰誦。【詩·大雅】誦言如醉。【禮·文王世子】春誦夏絃。【史記·儒林傳】兒寬行常帶經，止息則誦習之。</p><p>又【廣雅】論也，言也。【韓非子·難言篇】時稱詩書，道法往古，則見以爲誦。【註】誦，說舊事也。【王融·策秀才文】進講誦志，以沃朕心。【註】誦，言也。</p><p>又【正字通】怨謗亦曰誦。【左傳·襄四年】臧紇侵邾，敗于狐駘，國人誦之。</p><p>又【晉語】惠公入，而背內外之賂。與人誦之，皆怨辭也。</p><p>又官名。【周禮·地官】誦訓。【鄭註】能訓說四方所誦習，及人所作爲久時事。</p><p>又鳥名。【山海經】開明南有誦鳥。</p><p>又通作訟。詳訟字註。</p><p>又叶侯容切，音慵。【詩·小雅】家父作誦，以究王訩。</p><p>又叶牆容切，音從。【楚辭·九辯】欲循道而平驅兮，又未知其所從。然中路而迷惑兮，自壓按而學誦。</p>
