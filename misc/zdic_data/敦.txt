<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>敦</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄣˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>厚道，笃厚：～朴。～厚。～实。</li>
	<li>诚心诚意：～聘。～请。</li>

	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">敦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>敦</strong> <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代食器 <span class="encs">[ancient eating vessel]</span>。青铜制。盖和器身都作半圆球形，各有三足或圈足，上下合成球形，盖可倒置。流行于战国时期</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">有虞氏</span>之两敦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;明堂位》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">dūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>敦</strong> <span class="dicpy">dūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从&ldquo;攴&rdquo;，表示与以手持械的动作有关。本义:投掷)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 厚道。诚朴宽厚;厚重;笃实 <span class="encs">[honest;sincere]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">说礼乐而敦诗书。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乞言，从求善言，可以为政者，敦使受之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;行苇&middot;序》</span></span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>笺</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尽力莫如敦笃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敦祗恭厚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乐者敦和。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>

<p>又【五經文字】敦，厚也。【易&middot;臨卦】敦臨吉。【疏】厚也。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;邶風】王事敦我。【釋文】韓詩云：敦，迫也。鄭都回反，猶投擲也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】敦，勉也。【疏】敦者，厚相勉也。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】敦衆神使式道兮。【註】師古曰：敦，勉也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋天】太歲在午曰敦牂。【史記&middot;歷書註】敦，盛也。</p>

<p>又【詩&middot;魯頌】敦商之旅。【箋】敦，治也。</p>
<p>又【莊子&middot;說劒篇】今日試使士敦劒。【註】敦，斷也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】度官切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】徒官切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音團。【詩&middot;豳風】有敦瓜苦。【傳】敦，猶專專也。【疏】敦，是瓜繫蔓之貌，言瓜繫於蔓，專專然也。【釋文】徒丹反。【朱傳】音堆。</p>

<p>又【周禮&middot;天官&middot;玉府】若合諸侯，則共珠槃玉敦。【註】敦，槃類，古者以槃盛血，以敦盛食。</p>
<p>又【集韻】大到切，音道。幬，或作敦。覆也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;司几筵】每敦一几。【註】敦讀曰燾。覆也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】杜皓切，音稻。</p>

<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>都困切。通作頓。【爾雅&middot;釋丘】丘一成爲敦丘。【疏】詩衞風氓篇，至于頓丘，是也。</p>
