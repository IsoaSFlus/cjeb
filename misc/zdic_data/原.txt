                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">原</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>原</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>最初的，开始的：～本。～告。～稿。～籍。～理。～料。～色。～始。～著。</li>
	<li>本来：～样。～型。～址。～主。</li>
	<li>谅解，宽容：～宥。～谅。</li>
	<li>宽广平坦的地方：～野。平～。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/塬">塬</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> source, origin, beginning</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ursprünglich  (Adj)​,eigentlich  (Adv)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> primitif,originel,initial,pardonner,excuser,plaine,brut,original,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">原</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>原</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。象泉水从山崖里涌出来。从厂( <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>)，象山崖石穴形。从泉。本义:水源，源泉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &ldquo;源&rdquo;的古字。水源，水流起头的地方 <span class="encs">[fountainhead;source of a river]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原，水泉本也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗字作&ldquo;源&rdquo;。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原泉混混。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原流、泉浡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹塞川原为潢洿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有本有原。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《原毁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:原泉(源泉);原流(源流)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 起源;根本;根由 <span class="encs">[origin;cause;source]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必达于礼乐之原。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;孔子闲居》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹本也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原小则鲜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣食之原。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原大则饶。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:原故(原因);原凶(凶杀案的主犯);原曲(原委和底细)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 原野 <span class="encs">[open country]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广平曰原。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拘诸原。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">川原无际。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南望原隰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:原燎(原野上大火延烧);原色(原野的景色);原陵(平原与丘陵)</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>原</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">本於、發生於。</span><span class="gc_yy">《莊子&middot;天下》：「聖有所生，王有所成，皆<b>原</b>於一。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;王制》：「凡聽五刑之訟，必<b>原</b>父子之親，立君臣之義。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">推究根源。</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;繫辭下》：「易之為書也，原始要終，以為質也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;沈括《夢溪筆談&middot;卷二四&middot;雜誌一》：「磁石之指南，猶柏之指西，莫可<b>原</b>其理。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">寬恕、諒解。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「原諒」、「情有可<b>原</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《後漢書&middot;卷八一&middot;獨行傳&middot;范冉傳》：「冉首自劾退，詔書特<b>原</b>不理罪。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">根本。</span><span class="gc_yy">《呂氏春秋&middot;孟冬紀&middot;異用》：「萬物不同，而用之於人異也，此治亂存亡死生之<b>原</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;王符《潛夫論&middot;務本》：「游業末事以收民利，此貧邦之<b>原</b>也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">廣大而平坦的地方。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「平<b>原</b>」、「高<b>原</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;盤庚上》：「若火之燎于<b>原</b>，不可嚮邇，其猶可撲滅。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈賦得古<b>原</b>草送別〉詩：「離離<b>原</b>上草，一歲一枯榮。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋時魯國有原壤。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">最初的、本來的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「原文」、「原著」、「原油」、「物歸原主」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">再。</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子&middot;泰族》：「<b>原</b>蠶一歲再收，非不利也，然而王法禁之者，為其殘桑也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《資治通鑑&middot;卷二七一&middot;後梁紀六&middot;均王貞明六年》：「閏月、庚申朔，蜀主作高祖原廟于萬里橋。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">本來。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;薛能〈贈隱者〉詩：「甘貧原是道，苦學不為名。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第四二回》：「賈母<b>原</b>沒有大病，不過是勞乏了，兼著了些涼。」</span></p></li>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=厂" target="_blank">厂</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/539F.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AA5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠪥</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28621.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨘡</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AB0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠪰</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>愚袁切，音元。【說文】高平曰原，人所登。【爾雅&middot;釋地】大野曰平，廣平曰原。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;大司徒】辨其山林澤墳衍原隰之名物。【禮&middot;月令】孟夏，令野虞出行田原，爲天子勞農。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;食貨志】農漁商賈四者，衣食之原。【董仲舒傳】道之大，原出於天。【司馬相如傳】爾陿游原。【註】孟康曰：原，本也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】原，再也。【疏】重再也。【易&middot;比卦】原筮元永貞。【朱傳】必再筮，自審有元善長永正固之德。【禮&middot;文王世子】命膳宰曰：末有原。【註】末，勿也。謂所食之餘，不可再進也。【前漢&middot;禮樂志】以沛宮爲原廟。【註】師古曰：原，重也。言已立正廟，更重立也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】推原也。【易&middot;繫辭】原始要終。【前漢&middot;薛宣傳】原心定罪。【註】師古曰：原，謂尋其本也。【管子&middot;戒篇】春出，原農事之不本者，謂之遊。【註】原，察也。</p>
<p>又宥罪曰原。【晉書&middot;潘岳傳】會詔原之。</p>
<p>又通作源。【前漢&middot;食貨志】猶塞川原爲潢洿也。【註】師古曰：原，謂水泉之本也。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】孔子弟子有原憲。【前漢&middot;趙廣漢傳】潁川大姓原褚。【註】原褚，二姓也。</p>
<p>又州名。【韻會】漢高平縣，魏爲鎮州，又攺原州。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶虞雲切。【史記&middot;敘傳】懷王客死，蘭咎屈原。好諛信讒，楚幷于秦。</p>
