                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>粳</strong></p>
<p>粇、秔、稉 <span class="dicpy">jīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从禾，亢声，或从更声。本义:粳稻，稻之不黏者。今指介于籼稻、糯稻之间的一种晚稻品种，米粒短而粗，米质黏性较强，胀性小) 同本义 <span class="encs">[japonica rice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秔，稻属。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秔，不黏稻也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《声类》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">更为秔稻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;沟洫志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水澍稉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粳香等炊玉，韭美胜炮羔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《新凉》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7CB3.svg">						  <p>【集韻】居行切，音庚。【玉篇】稻不黏者。【爾雅&middot;釋草疏】秔糯甚相類，黏不黏爲異。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;食醬註】稌稉也。【梁&middot;庾肩吾集】有《謝賚粳米啓》。　【韻會補】粳，俗秔字。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">秔</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“粳”字头，请参考“秔”字：)</span>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=禾">禾</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/79D4.svg">                          <p>稻屬。从禾亢聲。稉，秔或从更聲。古行切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/79D4_4500.svg" /><span class="swbf">(秔)</span><span class="ywyy">稻屬。</span><span class="swdz">凡言屬者，以屬見别也。言别者，以别見屬也。重其同則言屬，秔爲稻屬是也。重其異則言别，𥟑爲禾别是也。周禮注曰：州黨族閭比鄉之屬别。介次市亭之屬别。小者屬别並言，分合並見也。稻有至黏者，稬是也。有次黏者，稉是也。有不黏者，稴是也。稉比於稬則爲不黏，比於稴則尚爲黏。稉與稴爲飯。稬以釀酒，爲餌餈。今與古同矣。散文稉亦偁稻，對文則别。魏都賦：水澍稉稌，陸蒔稷黍。蜀都賦：黍稷油油，稉稻莫莫。皆稉稻並舉。本艸經秔米稻米殊用。陶貞白乃不能分别，其亦異矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从禾亢聲。</span><span class="swdz">古行切。古音在十部。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/79D4_4501.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2581B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥠛</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">俗秔。</span><span class="swdz">更聲也。陸德明曰：稉與粳皆俗秔字。</span></p>                          </div>
