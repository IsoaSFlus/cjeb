                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">觉</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>觉</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">覺</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ju&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人或动物的器官受刺激后对事物的感受辨别：感～。知～。触～。视～。～察。</li>
	<li>睡醒；醒悟：～悟。～醒。&ldquo;～今是而昨非&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>觉</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">覺</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>睡眠（从睡着到睡醒）：午～。睡了一大～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to wake up from sleep; conscious</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schlaf  (S)​,fühlen， spüren, empfinden  (V)​,Sinn, Empfindung  (S)​,erwachen, aufwachen, sich bewusst werden, zur Einsicht kommen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> éprouver,sentir,penser,éveiller,dormir,s'éveiller</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">觉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>觉</strong></p>
<p>覺 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 睡醒 <span class="encs">[wake up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觉，悟也。从见，學省声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上欲起贤，未觉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;董卓传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觉而之渐台。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;邓通传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:觉卧(觉醒时和寝卧时);觉寤(睡醒);觉寝(自睡中觉醒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;挍&rdquo;、&ldquo;校&rdquo;。比较，较量 <span class="encs">[have a contest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如此贤不肖相觉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;离娄下》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我才不及卿，乃觉三十里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>觉</strong></p>
<p>覺 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 俗称睡眠为睡觉 <span class="encs">[sleep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且有大觉而后知此其大梦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;齐物论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觉见卧闻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;订鬼篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一觉黄粱之梦，百年大槐之宫。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄公绍</span><span class="smcs">《施经斋会戒约榜》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/89BA.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕覐<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2782D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧠭</span>【唐韻】古岳切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】訖岳切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音角。【說文】寤也。【廣韻】曉也。【書&middot;說命】念終始典于學，厥德修罔覺。【公羊傳&middot;昭三十一年】叔術覺焉。【註】覺，悟也。【莊子&middot;齊物論】且有大覺，而後知此大夢也。【白虎通】學之爲言覺也，悟所不知也。</p>
<p>又發也。【前漢&middot;高帝紀求賢詔】有而勿言覺免。【註】發覺者，免其官。</p>
<p>又明也。【左傳&middot;文四年】以覺報宴。【註】以明報功宴樂。</p>
<p>又大也，直也。【詩&middot;小雅】有覺其楹。【傳】有覺，言高大也。【箋】直也。【左傳&middot;襄二十一年】夫子覺者也。【註】較然正直。</p>
<p>又【釋名】告也。一曰自上敕下。一曰告，告覺也。</p>
<p>又【博雅】哲也。</p>
<p>又佛曰覺王。【舊唐書&middot;高祖詔】自覺王遷謝，像法流行。</p>
<p>又【姚崇傳】佛者，覺也，在乎方寸。【魏書&middot;釋老志】浮屠正號曰佛陀，華言譯之則謂淨覺。</p>
<p>又星名。【晉書&middot;天文志】妖星三曰天棓。一名覺星。</p>
<p>又姓，見【姓苑】。</p>
<p>又【韻會】通作梏。【禮&middot;緇衣】有梏德行。【註】梏，音角。【詩&middot;大雅】本作覺。【疏】梏與覺字異音同。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】古孝切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居效切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音敎。【增韻】夢醒曰覺。【詩&middot;王風】尚寐無覺。【史記&middot;高帝紀】後人至高祖覺。【註】覺謂寢寐而寤也。</p>
<p>又叶訖力切，音棘。【列子&middot;力命篇】楊朱歌曰：天其弗識，人胡能覺。</p>
<p>又叶吉列切，音孑。【蘇軾&middot;補龍山文】驥騄交騖，駑蹇先蹶，楚狂醉亂，隕帽莫覺。</p>
<p>又叶古爻切，音交。【左傳&middot;哀二十一年】齊人歌：魯人之皋，數年不覺，使我高蹈。【音學五書】覺叶㡍。</p>
<p>又【正字通】按郭璞菵草讚：菵草赤莖，實如蘡薁。食之益智，忽不自覺，殆齊生知，功奇于學。薁音約，與覺、學叶。字彙不考郭讚上下文，泥。吳棫《韻補》薁音育，覺攺音谷，非。</p>
