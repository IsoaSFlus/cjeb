<p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>诚实，不欺骗：～用。～守。～物。～货。～誓旦旦。</li>
	<li>不怀疑，认为可靠：～任。～托。～心。～念。</li>
	<li>崇奉：～仰。～徒。</li>
	<li>消息：～息。杳无音～。</li>
	<li>函件：～件。～笺。～鸽。～访。</li>
	<li>随便，放任：～手（随手）。～步（随意走动，散步）。～笔。～意。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/芯">芯</a>&rdquo;。</li>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">信</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>信</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从言。人的言论应当是诚实的。本义:真心诚意)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[honest sincere]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信，诚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有诸已之谓信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信，言合于意也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信者，诚也。专一不移也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;情性》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">定身以行事谓之信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">期果言当谓之信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民不求其所欲而得之谓之信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;经解》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信誓旦旦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反贼无信!吾不幸误中汝奸计也!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牺牲玉帛，弗敢加也，必以信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信而见疑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则是无信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;方正》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:信行(信用;守诺言);信人(讲守信用的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 真实，不虚伪<span class="encs">[true;sure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信言不美，美言不信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谓为信然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其事信。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4FE1.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㐰䚱【唐韻】息晉切【集韻】【正韻】思晉切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音訊。愨也，不疑也，不差爽也。【易&middot;繫辭】人之所助者，信也。【左傳&middot;僖七年】守命共時之謂信。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋地】大蒙之人信。【註】地氣使然也。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;莊三年】一宿爲舍，再宿爲信。【詩&middot;豳風】于女信處。</p>
<p>又【周頌】有客信信。【註】四宿也。</p>
<p>又符契曰信。【前漢&middot;平帝紀】漢律，諸乗傳者持尺五木轉信。【註】兩行書繒帛，分持其一。出入關，合之乃得過。或用木爲之。【後漢&middot;竇武傳】取棨信閉諸禁門。【註】棨，有衣戟也。</p>
<p>又古人謂使者曰信。與訊通。【史記&middot;韓世家】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>軫說楚王，發信臣，多其車，重其幣。【司馬相如&middot;諭巴蜀檄】故遣信，使曉諭百姓。</p>
<p>又州名。唐置信州，卽今廣信府。</p>
<p>又姓。信陵君無忌之後。</p>
<p>又複姓。【何氏姓苑】有信都，信平二氏。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>升人切。與申同。【易&middot;繫辭】往者，屈也。來者，信也。【詩&middot;邶風】于嗟洵兮，不我信兮。</p>
<p>又同身。【周禮&middot;春官】侯執信圭，伯執躬圭。【註】信圭，刻人形伸也。躬圭，刻人形屈也。</p>
<p>又叶斯鄰切，音新。【詩&middot;小雅】庶民弗信。叶上親。◎按《正韻》云：韓王信本與淮隂侯同名，嫌誤讀作新。今《敘傳》韓信音新，是信本有平、去兩音，其讀平者亦音，而非叶矣。</p>
