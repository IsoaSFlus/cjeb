                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">芴</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>芴</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄨˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>wù</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一种可供观赏或食用的野菜。</span><span class="gc_yy">《诗经·风邶·谷风》「采葑采菲」句下孔颖达正义引陆机曰：「菲，似葍，茎麤，叶厚而长，有毛。三月中烝。三月中烝鬻为茹，滑美可作羹。幽州人谓之<b>芴</b>，尔雅谓之蒠菜，今河内人谓之宿菜。」</span><span class="gc_yx">俗称为「宿菜」、「土瓜」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div><div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>芴</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄨ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hū</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">恍惚。</span><span class="gc_yy">《庄子·至乐》：「芒乎<b>芴</b>乎，而无有象乎？」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·成玄英·疏：「恍惚芒昧，参差难测。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">忽然。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子·正名》：「故愚者之言，<b>芴</b>然而粗。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·杨倞·注：「<b>芴</b>与忽同。忽然，无根本貌。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「忽」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
