                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>雍</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>和谐。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">壅</span>”，遮蔽，壅塞。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">拥</span>”，拥有。</li><li>〔～～〕ａ．鸟和鸣声，如“～～鸣雁”；ｂ．乐声和谐，如“听庙中之～～”。</li><li>〔～容〕文雅大方，从容不迫的样子，如“～～大雅”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雍</strong> <span class="dicpy">yōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 水被壅塞而成的池沼 <span class="encs">[pond]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">振鹭于飞，于彼西雍。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·振鹭》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋雍泉。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·僖公九年》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“雍，塞也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今臣雍阏不得闻。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·中山靖王胜传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“雍读曰壅。雍，塞也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代掌烹饪之官 <span class="encs">[official]</span>。如:雍人(古代掌宰杀烹饪之人);雍正(古代宫中掌筵席的长官)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古天子祭祀宗庙毕撤俎豆时所奏的乐章，亦用为撤膳时之乐 <span class="encs">[Yong]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三家者以<span i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 和谐;和睦 <span class="encs">[harmonious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雍睦亲族。——<span class="smcs">《新五代史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:雍熙于变(和谐兴盛，国家稳定);雍恬(和洽安乐);雍雍(声音和谐;鸟和鸣声);雍齿封侯(不计宿怨的典故)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 雍容。形容态度温文大方;舒缓;从容不迫 <span class="encs">[elegant]</span>。如:雍容揖逊(文雅谦虚的样子);雍容典雅(形容文气舒缓，词藻优美而不俗);雍容雅步(神态从容，举止斯文);雍容闲雅(雍容尔雅。神态从容不迫，举止文雅大方)</p><p>◎ <strong>雍</strong> <span class="dicpy">yōng</span> <span <span class="encs">[block up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维尘雍兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·无将大车》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“雍，字亦作壅。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雍以沙幕。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·匈奴传赞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上下雍隔故也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·梁冀传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:雍防(壅土堵防);雍害(阻挠妨害);雍遏(阻塞，壅塞);雍蔽(蒙蔽;隔绝)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雍睦">雍睦</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
