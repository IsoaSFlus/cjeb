<span class="encs">[exhaust]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">殚，极尽也。从歹，单声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">殚，尽也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span
