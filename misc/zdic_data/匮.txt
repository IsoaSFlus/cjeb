                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>匮</strong></p><p>匱 <span class="dicpy">kuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从匚( <span class="dicpy">fāng</span>)，表示与盛放东西有关，贵声。( <span class="dicpy">guì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guì.mp3"></a></span> )本义:柜子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 运装土的畚。通<span class="yhcs">“篑”</span> <span class="encs">[dung's basket]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纲纪咸张，成在一匮。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“辟如为山，未成一匮。止，吾止也。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《汉书·礼乐志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>匮</strong></p><p>匱 <span class="dicpy">kuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 缺乏，空乏 <span class="encs">[deficient;short of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匱，乏也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">财财不匮。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令仲秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其财匮。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虞不出财财匮少。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">财匮少而山泽不辟矣。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:匮竭(穷尽，缺乏财货);匮粮(缺乏粮食);匮少(缺少);匮饿(缺粮饥饿);匮矮(缺粮饥饿);匮阕(缺乏)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“潰”</span> <span class="encs">[rout]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">貴而不傲，富而不骄，两而不爭，闻而不遥，远而不绝，穷而不匮者鲜矣。——<span
