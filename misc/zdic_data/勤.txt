                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>勤</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qín  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qín.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>做事尽力，不偷懒：～劳。～快。～奋。～政（勤奋于政事）。～谨。～勉。～恳。克～克俭。</li><li>经常：～密（频繁）。来往很～。</li><li>上班，事务：～务。～杂。出～。后～。</li><li>担心，忧虑：～恤（忧虑怜惜）。</li><li>帮助：～王。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">勤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>勤</strong> <span class="dicpy">qín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。右形，左声。本义:劳累，劳苦)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 尽力多做，不断地做 <span class="encs">[assiduous;diligent;industrious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勤，劳也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">文王</span>既勤止。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·赍》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勤者，有事则收之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“执劳辱之事也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥父母勤劳稼穑。——<span class="smcs">《书·天逸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何勤子屠母，而死兮竟地?——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·天问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">会数而礼勤。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勤且艰若此。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忧勤国事。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">好学而不勤问。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四体不勤。——<span class="smcs">《论语·微子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勤而无所。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以贪勤民。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昼夜勤作息。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:勤力得(勤劳的人);勤儿(能手);勤紧(勤劳);勤学(努力学习);勤勤(劳苦的样子);勤能(勤勉而又有才能)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 为某人某事尽力;帮助 <span class="encs">[do one's utmost;help]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>人勤我也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今君其不勤民。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十八年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“尽心尽力，无所爱惜为勤。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勤天子之难。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·不广》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:勤民(尽心尽力于民事);勤事(尽心尽力于职事)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 忙于;致力于 <span class="encs">[endeavour]</span>。如:勤兵(率兵出征);勤苦(勤劳辛苦);勤属(勤于职守);勤人(操劳政事，致力于百姓)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“尽”</span>。竭，完 <span class="encs">[exhaust;finish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绵绵若存，用之不勤。——<span class="smcs">《老子·六章》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">力勤则匮。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·主术》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>勤</strong> <span class="dicpy">qín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
