<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">擬</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">nǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>打算：～去信联系。～议。</li>
	<li>初步设计编制或起草：～定（➊起草制定；➋揣测断定）。～订。草～。</li>
	<li>仿照：～古之作。～人（修辞方式，把事物人格化）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">拟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>拟</strong></p>
<p>儗、懝 <span class="dicpy">nǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，以声。本义:揣度，猜测)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[conjecture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拟，度也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行止而拟度焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;射人》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拟之而后言，议之而后动。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先生说的可单是拟题?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:悬拟(凭空虚构);拟足(揣度脚步);拟度(揣度;推测);拟迹(揣度足迹);拟题(应试举子揣度命题)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 类似;比拟 <span class="encs">[similar;analogous;compare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复举剑拟之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客以剑拟王，王头随堕汤中。(拟:比划，作砍的样子。&ldquo;拟&rdquo;后有&ldquo;于&rdquo;省略。客用剑向楚王一比划，楚王的人头就落到开水里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span>)</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非它山可拟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可拟小孤。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拟之如天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拟主(自比作主人);拟制(比拟其规模制度);拟物(修辞方法之一。把人拟作物或把物拟作人，或把甲物拟作乙物);拟伦(比拟;伦比);拟容(形貌上的比拟);拟圣(比拟圣人);拟质(比拟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 效法 <span class="encs">[follow the example of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:拟制(仿效帝王制度);拟法(效法);拟则(效法;模仿);拟迹(仿效);拟效(仿效);拟圣(仿效圣人);拟范(效法);拟学(仿效);拟宪(效法)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 模仿 <span class="encs">[imitate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">衡</span>乃拟 <span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《两都》</span>作<span class="smcs">《二京赋》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:模拟(模仿);拟经(模仿经典);拟水(古代祭祀仪式中模拟盥洗的动作);拟托(模拟假托);拟象(谓模仿其状);拟貌(摹拟描摹);拟仪(模仿其法度;仿效)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 打算 <span class="encs">[intend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拟用左通政。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拟作(准备有所作为);拟待(打算);拟踪(打算达到);拟托(打算委托)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 拟定 <span class="encs">[work out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:拟用(拟定使用);拟论(指拟立为太子之说);拟题(拟定题目)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 起草 <span class="encs">[draw up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命君拟旨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
