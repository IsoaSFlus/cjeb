                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>本</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">běn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/běn.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄣˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>草木的根：～草（泛指中药）。无～之木。</li>
	<li>事物的根源，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">末</span>&rdquo;相对：～末（头尾；始终）。根～（根源；彻底；本质上）。</li>
	<li>草的茎，树的干：草～植物。</li>
	<li>中心的，主要的：～部。～体。</li>
	<li>原来：～来。～领。</li>
	<li>自己这方面的：～国。～身。～位。～分（<span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n </span>）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> root, origin, source; basis</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ZEW für gebundene Druckerzeugnisse (z.B. Buch, Heft)​ (Zähl)​,Stamm (S)​,Ursprung, Quelle, Grundlage, Basis (fig)​ (S)​,Wurzel (S, Bio)​,ursprünglich (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> racine,tige,fondation,origine,capital,principal,base,celui-ci,propre,personnel,(classificateur pour les livres, cahiers, carnets, etc.)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">本</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>本</strong> <span class="dicpy">běn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/běn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。小篆字形，从&ldquo;木&rdquo;，下面的一横是加上的符号，指明树根之所在。本义:草木的根或靠根的茎干)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[root of a plant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">本，木下曰本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">本实先拨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;荡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木水之有本原。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伐木不自其本，必复生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是岁，有禾生景天中，三本一茎九穗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;吉验》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摇其本以观其疏密。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《种树郭橐驼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:水有源，木有本;本干(草木的根干)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 本宗族 <span class="encs">[patriarchal clan]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">本支百世。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;文王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几房的本家大约已经搬走了。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《呐喊》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:本宗(犹祖籍;本宗族);本种(自己的种族)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 事物的根基或主体 <span class="encs">[foundation;basis;origin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子务本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;学而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今背本而趋末。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然墨之道，兼爱为本。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:治本(从根本上加以处理);根本(事物的根源或最重要部分)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 母金，本钱 <span class="encs">[capital;principal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子本相侔。(子:利息，侔:相等)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《柳子厚墓志铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亏本(损失本钱);本钿(方言。本钱);本银(本钱，资本)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 根源，本源 <span class="encs">[source;origin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">物有本末，事有始终。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盖亦反其本矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/672C.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕楍㮺<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21D21.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡴡</span>【唐韻】【正韻】布忖切【集韻】【韻會】補衮切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>奔上聲。【說文】木下曰本。从木，一在其下，草木之根柢也。【左傳&middot;昭元年】木水之有本原。【班固&middot;西都賦】元元本本，殫見洽聞。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】始也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】舊也，下也。【禮&middot;禮器】反本修古，不忘其初。【爾雅&middot;釋器疏】柢本也，凡物之本，必在底下。</p>
<p>又【左傳註】豫爲後地曰張本。</p>
<p>又【曲禮】韭曰豐本，菖蒲根曰昌本。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>逋昆切。同奔。喻德宣譽曰本走。</p>
<p>又叶方典切，音匾。【班婕妤&middot;擣素賦】調非常律，聲無定本。或連躍而更投，或暫舒而常斂。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/672C.svg">                          <p>木下曰本。从木，一在其下。楍，古文。布忖切〖注〗徐鍇曰：&ldquo;一，記其處也。本末朱皆同義。&rdquo; 〖注〗㮺、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21D29.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡴩</span>，亦古文本。</p>                          </div>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/672C_2640.svg" /><span class="swbf">(本)</span><span class="ywyy">木下曰本。從木從丅。</span><span class="swdz">此篆各本作本。解云從木，一在其下。今依六書故所引唐本正。本末皆於形得義。其形一從木丄，一從木丅，而意卽在是，全書如此者多矣。一記其處之說，非物形也。大雅以本奏爲奔走，假借也。布忖切。十三部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/672C_2641.svg" /><span class="swbf">(楍)</span><span class="ywyy">古文。</span><span class="swdz">此從木<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C3C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧰼</span>形也。根多竅似口。故從三口。</span></p>                          </div>
