                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>苑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>古代养禽兽植林木的地方，多指帝王的花园：鹿～。御～。～囿。</li><li>学术、文艺荟萃之处：文～。艺～。学～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>苑</strong> <span class="dicpy">yuàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，夗( <span class="dicpy">yuàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuàn.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:养禽兽植树木的地方。后来多指帝王游乐打猎的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gardens]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苑，所以养禽兽囿也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">养牛马林木曰苑。——<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有垣曰苑。——<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">值林为苑。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">刘</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“有木曰苑。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指园林，花园</p><p><span class="diczx1">会幸苑中。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林·雅量》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:苑囿兵(禁兵);苑马(苑囿和马厩;亦指苑囿中的马)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> <span class="encs">[学术、文艺]</span>荟萃之处 <span class="encs">[centre(of art and literature，etc.)]</span>。如:文苑;艺苑</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/苑囿">苑囿</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">苑</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>苑</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄩㄢˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yuàn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/6561.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">畜养禽兽或种植草木果蔬的地方，古代多指帝王游乐狩猎的园林。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「鹿<b>苑</b>」、「上林<b>苑</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·艸部》：「<b>苑</b>，所以养禽兽。」</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝宋·谢灵运〈石门岩上宿诗〉：「朝搴<b>苑</b>中兰，畏彼霜下歇。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·刘子翚〈汴京纪事〉诗：「内<b>苑</b>珍林蔚绛霄，围城不复禁刍荛。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">事物会集的地方。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「艺<b>苑</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝梁·刘勰《文心雕龙·才略》：「观夫后汉才林，可参西京，晋世文<b>苑</b>，足俪邺都。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div><div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>苑</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄩˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yù</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">积聚、胶著、停滞。</span><span class="gc_yy">《礼记·礼运》：「故事大积焉而不<b>苑</b>，并行而不缪。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·孔颖达·正义：「虽复万机辐凑而应之有次序，不使<b>苑</b>积也。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「郁」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div><div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>苑</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄩㄢˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yuán</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋时齐国有<b><b>苑</b></b>何忌。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              苑·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/82D1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】於阮切【集韻】【韻會】委遠切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音婉。【說文】所以養禽獸也。【周禮·地官·囿人疏】古謂之囿，漢謂之苑。【前漢·高帝紀】故秦苑囿園池，令民得田之。【註】養禽獸曰苑。【白虎通】苑囿所以在東方，謂養萬物。東方，物所生也。</p><p>又縣名。【史記·高祖功臣侯年表】高苑侯丙倩。【索隱註】縣屬千乗。</p><p>又善苑，國名。見【洞冥記】。</p><p>又【莊子·天地篇】諄芒將東之大壑，適遇苑風於東海之濵。【註】苑風，扶搖大風也。</p><p>又書名，如《文苑》《類苑》》《藝苑》《說苑》之類。</p><p>又宮室名。如內苑、禁苑、西苑、南苑之類。</p><p>又星名。【史記·天官書】句曲九星，二曰天苑。</p><p>又藥名。蕀苑，遠志也。見【博雅】。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>紆願切，音怨。【周禮·地官】禁山之爲苑。劉昌宗讀去聲。【左思·吳都賦】遭藪爲圃，値林爲苑。異荂蓲蘛，夏曄冬蒨。</p><p>又【類篇】于袁切，音鴛。人姓。【左傳】苑何忌，齊大夫。【魏志】渤海苑康。</p><p>又音鬱。【詩·小雅】我心苑結。【箋】苑，猶屈也，積也。【釋文】音鬱。</p><p>又【正韻】委粉切，讀如蘊。義同。</p><p>又苑、菀互通。【前漢·百官表】牧師菀令。【晉語】人皆集于苑。</p><p>又【韻補】叶音氳。【詩·秦風】蒙伐有苑。【註】苑，文貌，讀平聲，叶上羣韻。</p><p>
