                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">泽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>泽</strong></p><p>澤 <span class="dicpy">zé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水。睪( <span class="dicpy">yì</span>)声。本义::光泽，润泽)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lustre]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泽，光润也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共饭不泽手。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泽剑首。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·少仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">实诸泽器。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·慌氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顺非而泽。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·礼论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芳与泽其杂糅兮。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">热多则淖泽。——<span class="smcs">《素问·经络论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">色沃不泽。——<span class="smcs">《素问·玉机真藏论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">色泽莹润。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">根色鲜泽。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仁义不足泽其性，礼乐不足锢其情。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《太古》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:泽雨(润泽万物之雨);泽器(润泽光滑的器皿);色泽(颜色和光泽)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 水深的湖泽或水草丛杂的湖泽 <span class="encs">[marsh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">下而有水曰泽，言润泽也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水草交厝名之为泽。言其润泽万物以阜民用也。——<span class="smcs">《风俗通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泽水之钟也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">川壅为泽。——<span
