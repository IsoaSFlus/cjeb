                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>两</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">兩</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>数目，二。一般用于量词和“个、半、千、万、亿”前：～个黄鹂。～本书。</li><li>双方：～可。～边。～便（客套用语，彼此方便）。～旁。～侧。～袖清风。～败俱伤。</li><li>中国市制重量单位：十～（一市斤。旧制为十六两一市斤）。半斤八～（喻彼此一样，不相上下，含贬义）。</li><li>表示不定数目：～下子。～着儿（<span class="dicpy">zhāor </span>）。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>两</strong></p><p>兩 <span class="dicpy">liǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从一，兩平分。兩亦声。本义:二十四铢为一两)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[liang，a unit of weight]</span> 16两为1斤。今市制折合国际单位制0。05千克，十钱一两，十两一斤</p><p><span class="diczx1">两，二十四铢为一两。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衡权…本起于黄钟之重。一龠容千二百黍，重十二铢，两之为两，二十四铢为两，十六两为斤。…两者，两黄钟律之重也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·律历志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 双。用于鞋娄 <span class="encs">[two]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一两棕鞋八尺藤，</span><span class="diczx2">广陵</span>行遍又 <span class="diczx2">金陵</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">戴叔伦</span><span class="smcs">《忆原上人》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 匹(长四丈)</p><p><span class="diczx1">归夫人鱼轩，重锦三十两。——<span class="smcs">《左传·闵公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“辆”</span>。车一乘 <span class="encs">[used for buses，carts，etc.]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">武王戎车三百两。——<span class="smcs">《书·牧誓·序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">之子于归，百两御之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·鹊巢》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>两</strong></p><p>兩 <span class="dicpy">liǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>［数］</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 二 <span class="encs">[two]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">参天两地而倚数。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以九两系邦国之民。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官·大宰》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“两，犹耦也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两小儿辩斗。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">陈胜</span>佐之，并杀两尉。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">途中两狼。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两军人马。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两骑来是谁。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span
