                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>陷</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>掉进，坠入，沉下：～落。～没（<span class="dicpy">mò</span>）。～溺。～于。～入。</li><li>为捉野兽挖的坑：～坑。～阱。</li><li>凹进：双眼深～。</li><li>设计害人：～害。诬～。～人于罪。</li><li>攻破，占领：失～。沦～。</li><li>缺点：缺～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">陷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>陷</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">fù</span>);从臽，臽( <span class="dicpy">xiān</span>)亦声。据金文，象人掉进陷阱形。本义:坠入，掉进)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fall into]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陷，高下也。一曰陊也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可陷也。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“下也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上陷而不振。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“坠也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一拥而入，人马俱陷。——<span class="smcs">《三国志通俗演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:陷狱(入狱);沉陷(地面陷下去)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 陷溺，淹没 <span class="encs">[submerge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陷没而死。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《<指南录> 后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:陷溺</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 陷害 <span class="encs">[frame up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三长史皆害</span><span class="diczx2">汤</span>( <span class="diczx2">张汤</span>)，欲陷之。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:诬陷(诬告陷害);陷灭(陷害诛灭)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 攻破 <span class="encs">[capture;break]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">灌孟</span>]</span> 战常陷坚。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:陷敌(陷队。冲入敌阵);陷坚(攻破敌军坚强的阵地。同陷锐)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 陷入，落在不利的境地 <span class="encs">[sink into;land oneself in;get bogged down in]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身虽陷败，彼观其意，且欲得其当而报于</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任少卿书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:陷败(遭到失败);陷误(陷入误区);陷身(身陷泥坑、火坑)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>陷</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 陷阱 <span class="encs">[trap]</span>。诱捕猎物或诱杀动物用的坑</p><p><span class="diczx1">鱼可使之吞钩，虎可使之入陷。——<span class="smcs">《齐丘子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:陷车(古时押解犯人的囚车)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 缺点;过失 <span class="encs">[faults]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陷而入于恭。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:缺陷(欠缺或不够完善的地方)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/陷害">陷害</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
