                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谐</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谐</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">諧</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xié  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xié.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>和，配合得当：和～。～调（<span class="dicpy">tiáo </span>）。～音。</li>    <li>滑稽：诙～。～趣。～谑（诙谐逗趣）。亦庄亦～（既严肃又诙谐）。</li>    <li>（事情）商量好，办妥：事～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谐</strong></p><p>諧 <span class="dicpy">xié</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xié.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，皆声。本义:和谐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(强调配合得匀称) <span class="encs">[harmonious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谐，和也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">克谐以孝。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌司万民之难而谐和之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·调人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如乐之和，无所不谐。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如其克谐，天下可定也。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·赤壁之战》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫妻谐老。——<span class="smcs">《初刻拍案惊奇》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如: 谐当(妥当);谐和(各组成部分之间协调地相互联系在一起的);谐调(和谐);谐奏(和协地演奏);谐律(谐合于音律)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 言语或行为有趣而引人发笑 <span class="encs">[humorous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上以朔口谐辞给，好作问之。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·东方朔传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心亲不复异新旧，使脱巾屦相谐嬉。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《和刘贡甫燕集之作》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醉中共笑语，往往杂谐谚。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《赠杨荥阳》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:谐易(诙谐平易);谐辞(戏谑之辞);谐谚(诙谐谚语);谐画(富于谐趣的话)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谐</strong></p><p>諧 <span class="dicpy">xié</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xié.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 办妥，办成功 <span class="encs">[succeed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恐事不谐，反遭其害。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今事不谐，不过赤族，为社稷死，岂不快乎?——<span class="smcs">《宋史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:谐当(妥当)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 商定 <span class="encs">[settle;come to an agreement;bargain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当之官者，皆先至</span><span class="diczx2">西园</span>谐价，然后得去。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:谐价(议价成交)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              諧·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AE7.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】戸皆切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】雄皆切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音骸。【說文】詥也。</p><p>又【廣雅】耦也。【玉篇】合也，調也。【書·堯典】克諧以孝。</p><p>又【舜典】八音克諧。【左傳·襄十一年】如樂之和，無所不諧。【禮·禮器】君子有禮，則外諧而內無怨。</p><p>又【文心雕龍】諧之言皆也，辭淺會俗，皆悅笑也。【前漢·敘傳】東方贍辭，詼諧倡優。【晉書·顧愷之傳】愷之好諧謔，人多愛狎之。</p><p>又平論定其價也。【後漢·宦者張讓傳】當之官者，皆先至西園，諧價，然後得去。</p><p>又古書名。【莊子·逍遙遊】《齊諧》者，志怪者也。</p><p>又鳥名。【博雅】鴆鳥，其雄謂之運日，其雌謂之隂諧。【淮南子·繆稱訓】隂諧知雨。【註】天將隂雨則鳴。</p><p>又叶弦雞切，音奚。【後漢·五行志】更始時童謠曰：諧不諧，在赤眉。</p><p>又【儒林周澤傳】時人爲之語曰：生世不諧，作大常妻。</p><p>
