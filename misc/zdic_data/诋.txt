data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，氐( <span class="dicpy">dǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button class="diczx1">诋，毁也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂二》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诋毁也，辱也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·刘向传》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">历诋公卿大臣。——<span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录·后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 欺 <span class="encs">[cheat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">朔</span>擅诋欺天子从官，当弃市。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 抵赖，抵讳。用同<span class="yhcs">“抵”</span> <span class="encs">[deny]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">上</span>责( <span class="diczx2">史)万岁</span>， <span class="diczx2">万岁</span>诋谰。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
