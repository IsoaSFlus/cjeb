                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>醉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zuì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>饮酒过量，神志不清：～汉。～鬼。～意。～拳（一种拳术，步法上突出东倒西歪的醉态）。～生梦死。～翁之意不在酒。</li><li>沉迷，过分爱好：～心。沉～。陶～（很满意地沉浸在某种境界或思想活动中）。</li><li>用酒泡制的：～枣。～虾。～蟹。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>醉</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)，从卒。<span class="yhcs">“酉”</span>表示酒，<span class="yhcs">“卒”</span>表示终结。酒喝到不能再喝的时候，就醉了。本义:醉酒)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(饮酒过量，神志不清) <span class="encs">[drunk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醉，酒卒也。各卒其度量，不至于乱也。一曰酒溃也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既醉而出，并受其福。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·宾之初筵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">对饮酒醉。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醉里吴音相媚好。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《清平乐·村居》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沉醉不知归路。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《如梦令》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醉能同其乐，醒能述以文者。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苍颜白发，頺然乎其间者，太守醉也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醉醇醲。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酉，为酒所酣曰醉。——<span class="smcs">《洪武正韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:醉是醒时言(醉后说的常是真心话);醉饱(酒足饭饱);醉沈(指酒后所作的书画);醉魔咕咚(醉呼呼);醉兴勃勃(醉后神情亢奋);醉呼卢(醉呼呼睡大觉);醉沉沉(沉醉的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用酒浸制的 <span class="encs">[liquor-saturated]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瓮中取醉蟹，最忌用灯。——<span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:醉蟹(酒渍的螃蟹);醉虾</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 昏愦，糊涂 <span class="encs">[muddleheaded]</span>。如:醉梦(指人糊里糊涂如醉如梦);醉命(胡乱下的命令);醉痴(精神恍惚不安)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 沉酣入迷;爱好 <span class="encs">[be fascinated]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">目断南浦云，心醉东郊柳。——<span class="smcs">《送赵六贞固》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:醉乡砚田(沉缅于美酒与文墨之间);醉飞吟盏(在喝酒的同时作诗);醉侯(称喜欢喝酒而又有酒量的人);醉翁(喜好喝酒的老人)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醉步">醉步</a></span><span
