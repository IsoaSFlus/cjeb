                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>缗</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">緡</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">m&iacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄣˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古代穿铜钱用的绳子。</li>	<li>钓鱼绳。</li>	<li>古代计量单位：钱十～（即十串铜钱，一般每串一千文）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fishing-line; cord; string of coi</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kordel, Kord  (S)​,Min  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> corde</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缗</strong></p>
<p>緡 <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。又作&ldquo;緍&rdquo;。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，昏声。本义:钓鱼绳)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fishing line]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其钓维何?维丝伊缗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;何彼秾矣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:振缗(挥动钓丝);缗纶(钓鱼用的丝线)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代穿铜钱的绳子 <span class="encs">[string of threading a copper]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贾人缗线。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缗钱(用丝线串起来的钱);缗繦(穿钱的绳子。引申为钱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Min state]</span>。夏时之缗国，春秋时属宋，汉置东湣县，故址在今山东省金乡县东北</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缗</strong></p>
<p>緡 <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用于成串的铜钱，每串一千文 <span class="encs">[string]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钱千万缗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:钱十万缗</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>缗</strong></p>
<p>緡 <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 钓取鱼类 <span class="encs">[angle;go fishing]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缗鱼(钓鱼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 安装<span class="encs">[弦线]</span> <span class="encs">[fix]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言缗之丝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;抑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缗丝(安装弦线)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 以衣物相覆 <span class="encs">[cover with cloth]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:缗系(脱衣相覆)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“缗”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/緡">緡</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中" target="_blank">未集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中&amp;bs=糸" target="_blank">糸</a>】
                              緡·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7DE1.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】武巾切【集韻】眉貧切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音珉。【正韻】彌鄰切，音民。【說文】釣魚<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2617E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦅾</span>也。【詩&middot;召南】其釣維何，維絲伊緡。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】緡，綸也。【註】繩也。江東謂之緡。</p><p>又【說文】吳人解衣相被謂之緡。【詩&middot;大雅】言緡之絲。【註】緡，被也。</p><p>又錢貫也。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】初筭緡錢。【註】緡，絲也，以貫錢也。</p><p>又邑名。【左傳&middot;僖二十三年】齊侯伐宋圍緡。【註】緡，宋邑。高平昌邑縣東南有東緡城。</p><p>又【集韻】呼昆切，音昏。義同。</p><p>又【集韻】彌延切，音棉。通作緜。</p><p>又【集韻】弭盡切，音泯。【莊子&middot;則陽篇】雖使丘陵草木之緡。【音義】緡，盛也。</p><p>又【莊子&middot;在宥篇】當我緡乎。【音義】緡，泯合也。</p><p>又【集韻】美隕切。愍或作緡。痛也。</p>
