                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锡</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>锡</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">錫</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>一种金属元素，银白色，质软，富延展性：焊～。～矿。～石。～纸。～箔。</li>    <li>赏赐：～命。～赉。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>锡</strong></p><p>錫 <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 化学名词。一种略带蓝色的白色光泽的低熔点金属元素，在化合物内是二价或四价，不会被空气氧化，主要以二氧化物(锡石)和各种硫化物(例如硫锡石)的形式存在 <span class="encs">[tin]</span>——元素符号Sn</p><p><span class="diczx1">金锡连丹。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:锡古子(形如鼓的有盖锡锅)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“緆”</span>。细麻布 <span class="encs">[fine gunny;sackcloth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">用锡若絺。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>女曼姬，被阿锡。——<span class="smcs">《史记·司马相如传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:锡衣(细布所制的衣服);锡衰(由平滑的细麻所制成的衣服)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 和尚所用锡杖的简称 <span class="encs">[monk’s cane]</span>。如:锡杖</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>锡</strong></p><p>錫 <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“赐”</span>。给予;赐给 <span class="encs">[grant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">申锡无疆，及尔斯所。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·烈祖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人有见</span><span class="diczx2">宋王</span>者，锡车十乘。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·列御寇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诗曰:“孝子不匮，永锡尔类。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">老亲母若肯见教，胜予锡我百朋矣。——<span class="smcs">《宛如约》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:锡赉(赏赐);锡命(天子赐与诸侯爵位、服饰等诰命)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
