						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>爻</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yáo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yáo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄠˊ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 组成八卦中每一卦的长短横道：～象（❶卦的形象；❷喻形迹、真相）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> diagrams for divination</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Radikal Nr. 89 = mischen, durchkreuzen, knüpfen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> entrelacer,changer,89e radical</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">爻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>爻</strong> <span class="dicpy">yáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。本义:组成八卦的长短横道。卦的变化取决于爻的变化，故爻表示交错和变动的意义) 同本义 <span class="encs">[interwinled]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爻，交也。象易六爻，头交也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，乂，古文五，二五天地之数。会意。凡从爻之字，皆错杂意。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">道有变动，故曰爻。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:爻分(交错分开);爻错(交叉错杂)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>爻</strong> <span class="dicpy">yáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="smcs">《周易》</span>中组成卦的符号。 <span class="encs">[line in eight trigrams]</span><span class="yhcs">“—”</span>为阳爻，<span class="yhcs">“--”</span>为阴爻。每三爻合成一卦，可得八卦;两卦(六爻)相重则得六十四卦，称为别卦。爻含有交错和变化之意</p><p><span class="diczx1">六爻熟透，八卦精通。——<span class="smcs">《梼杌闲评——明珠缘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爻者，言乎变者也。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:爻律(变动音律);爻象(<span class="smcs">《周易》</span>中六爻相交成卦所表示的事物形象;形迹)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 爻辞的省称 <span class="encs">[explanations of diagrams for divination]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽抱<span class="smcs">《中孚》</span>爻，犹劳见锦诗。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">谢灵运</span><span class="smcs">《初发石首城》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:爻画(指<span class="smcs">《易》</span>卦);爻系(爻辞);爻辞(说明<span class="smcs">《易》</span>六十四卦各爻象的文辞);爻变(变卦)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
