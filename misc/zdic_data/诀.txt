                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诀</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">訣</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jué  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄝˊ</span></p><ol><li>高明的方法：～窍。秘～。</li><li>用事物的主要内容编成的顺口的便于记忆的词句：口～。</li><li>辞别，多指不再相见的分别：永～。～别。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诀</strong></p><p>訣 <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，夬( <span class="dicpy">guài</span>)声。本义:辞别，告别。特指长别)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[part with sb.]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与死者辞曰诀。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">相诀而去。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孔子世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与王诀曰。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诀妻子。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">梦汝来诀。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早知诀汝。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:诀行(告别而行);诀去(话别离去);诀音(遗言);诀绝(诀别，长别)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“决”</span>。自杀 <span class="encs">[commit suicide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帝大怒，遂赐后死，逼令引诀。——<span class="smcs">《周书·宣帝杨皇后传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未能引诀。——<span class="smcs">《隋书·薛道衡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">天元</span>大怒，遂赐 <span class="diczx2">后</span>死，逼令引诀。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·陈宣帝太建十二年》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>诀</strong></p><p>訣 <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 就事物主要内容编成的顺口押韵的、容易记忆的词句 <span class="encs">[rhymed formula]</span>。如:歌诀;十六字诀;口诀</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 做某事的窍门、方法 <span class="encs">[knack]</span>。如:秘诀;妙诀</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div
