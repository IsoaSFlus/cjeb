                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>字</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zì.mp3"></a></span> ㄗˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>用来记录语言的符号：文～。汉～。～符。～母。～典。～句。～里行（<span class="dicpy">háng </span>）间。～斟句酌。</li>    <li>文字的不同形式，书法的派别：草～。篆～。颜～。柳～。欧～。赵～。</li>    <li>书法的作品：～画。～幅。</li>    <li>字的音：～正腔圆。</li>    <li>人的别名，亦称“表～”，现多称“<span class="diczx3">号</span>”；商店的名称，亦称“～号”。</li>    <li>合同，契约：～据。</li>    <li>旧时称女子出嫁：待～闺中。</li>    <li>生子，乳，爱：～乳（生育）。～孕（怀孕）。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>字</strong> <span class="dicpy">zì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)从子，子亦声。在屋内生孩子。本义:生孩子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[give birth to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">字，乳也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">字，生也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妇人疏字者子活，数乳者子死。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·气寿》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六畜遂字。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“字，生也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苦山有木，服之不字。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·中山经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亭有畜字马，岁课息。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:字乳(生育);字育(生育、蕃育)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 怀孕，体内怀着胎儿 <span class="encs">[be pregnant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">女子贞不字，十年乃字。——<span class="smcs">《易·屯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘字牝者，傧而不得聚会。——<span class="smcs">《史记·平准书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:字牝(怀孕的母畜);字孕(怀胎;生殖);字马(怀孕的马)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 抚养;养育;教养 <span class="encs">[bring up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其僚无子，使字敬叔。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不能字人之孤而杀之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牛羊腓字之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·生民》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">字而幼孩，遂而鸡豚。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《种树郭橐驼传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:字养(抚养;养育);字民(养育人民);字育(化生);字孤(抚养孤儿)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 爱 <span class="encs">[love]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于父，不能字厥子。——<span class="smcs">《书·廉诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乐王鲋字而敬。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公元年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“字，爱也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:字孤(抚爱孤儿);字爱(抚爱)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 教育;教课;传授知识 <span class="encs">[teach]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天会间，充女直字学生，学问通达，观书史，工为诗。——<span class="smcs">《金史·温敦兀带传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 治理 <span class="encs">[govern]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">防民之理甚周，而不至皎察;字民之方甚裕，而不至使侵蛑。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《答饶州之使君书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span>
