						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>罄</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qìng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>本义为器中空，引申为尽，用尽：告～。～其所有。～竹难书。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">磬</span>”，打击乐器。</li><li>显现：“夫犬马，人所知也，旦暮～于前”。</li><li>严整的样子：“师旷～然”。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>罄</strong> <span class="dicpy">qìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:器中空)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[empty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罄，器中空也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缾之罄矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·蓼莪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罄无不宜。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·天保》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:罄身人(身无分文的人);罄然(空无一物的样子);罄身(空着身子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 严整 <span class="encs">[serious and neat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">师旷罄然。——<span class="smcs">《周书·太子晋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:罄然(严肃整饬的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 满;全 <span class="encs">[full;all]</span>。如:罄山采木(把山上的树木全部采光);罄地(所有地区);罄宇(犹言整个世界);罄尽，罄净(全尽无余)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>罄</strong> <span 用尽;消耗殆尽 <span class="encs">[use up;exhaust]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罄，尽也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罄南山之竹，书罪未穷。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·李密传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贫亦罄衣装。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:罄其所学(用尽平生所学一切);罄空尽竭(罄尽);罄舍(施舍净尽);罄净(罄尽);罄绝(尽绝，全绝);罄竭(用尽无余，竭尽心力);罄尽(竭尽。同罄竭);罄地(所有的地)</p><p><span class="diczx1">夫犬马，人所知也，旦暮罄于前，不可类之，故难。——<span
