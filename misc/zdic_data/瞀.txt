						<p>● <strong>瞀</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>目眩，眼花：～病（头目晕眩的病症）。～～（垂目下视的样子；形容眼睛昏花，引申为昏昏沉沉）。～瘛（中医指看不清，手脚拘挛的症状）。雀（<span class="dicpy">qiǎo</span>）～（古时称夜盲症）。</li>
	<li>心绪紊乱。～乱。</li>
	<li>愚昧：&ldquo;世俗之沟犹～儒&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to look at closely; nearsighted; dim; indistinct vision</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> dunkel, trüb</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vision indistincte,faible</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瞀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瞀</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从目，敄( <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span>)声。本义:眼睛昏花)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dim-sighted]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予少而游于六合之内，予适有瞀病。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;徐无鬼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昏瞀垫溺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;益稷传》</span>。疏:&ldquo;眩惑之意。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瞀眩(眼花头晕;迷糊);瞀瞑(目眩昏晕;晕厥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 乱;错乱 <span class="encs">[confusion;in disorder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中闷瞀瞀忳忳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《惜诵》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瞀见(愚昧错乱的见解)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 愚昧无知貌 <span class="encs">[ignorant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世俗之沟犹瞀儒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;非十二子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瞀儒(愚昧无知的儒生)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瞀</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 俯视，低目谨视 <span class="encs">[look down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瞀，低目谨视也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汤</span>又因瞀光而谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;让王》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怨咽对风景，闷瞀守闺闼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《绍古辞七首》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 〈方〉∶看望 <span class="encs">[visit]</span>。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瞀</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中" target="_blank">午集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中&amp;bs=目" target="_blank">目</a>】
                              瞀·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7780.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>莫<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>切，音茂。【說文】低目謹視也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】目不明貌。【晉書&middot;天文志】眼瞀精絕，故蒼蒼也。【揚子&middot;太<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>經】視也見，而晦也瞀。【亢倉子&middot;全道篇】夫瞀視者，以黊爲赤。【註】黊同黃。【韓愈&middot;南山詩】淚目苦矇瞀。</p>
<p>又亂也。【北史&middot;房豹傳】是非瞀亂。【楚辭&middot;九章】中悶瞀之忳忳。【註】煩亂也。</p>
<p>又不敢正視貌。【荀子&middot;非十二子篇】子弟之容瞀瞀然。</p>
<p>又被髮也。【淮南子&middot;道應訓】去其瞀而載之木。【註】木騖鳥冠也。</p>
<p>又蟲名。【埤雅】蜹似蚊而小。一名瞀芮。【列子&middot;天瑞篇】瞀芮生乎腐蠸。</p>
<p>又姓。【左傳】莒有瞀胡。</p>
<p>又通作貿。【禮&middot;檀弓】貿貿然來。【註】貿貿，不明貌。與瞀同。</p>
<p>又通作霿。【廣韻】䐨瞀。【集韻】作䐨霿，鄙吝心不明也。【正韻】無識也。【荀子&middot;儒效篇】愚陋傋瞀。【註】傋音寇，愚也。瞀，無知也。【前漢&middot;五行志】作傋霿。</p>
<p>又通作愗。【楚辭&middot;九辯】直怐愗以自苦。【註】愗音茂，愚貌。與瞀通。互詳䐨字註。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>迷浮切，音謀。義同。</p>
<p>又【王逸&middot;九思】復顧兮彭瞀，叶上憂下投。本作務。務光，古介士也。一作牟光。古務瞀牟<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>通。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】莫角切。【集韻】墨角切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>尨入聲。目不明也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>亡遇切，音務。義同。</p>
<p>又俯視也。【黃帝素問】民病肩臂瞀重。【註】低目俯首曰瞀。【靈樞經】交兩手而瞀爲臂厥。【註】瞀，目垂貌。</p>
<p>又【集韻】微夫切，務平聲。【前漢&middot;地理志】雊瞀，縣名，屬上谷郡。【註】孟康音句無。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】莫到切，音冒。視眩易也。【莊子&middot;徐無鬼】予適有瞀病。</p>
<p>又【埤雅】莫卜切，音木。雀目夕昏，人有至夕昏不見物者，謂之雀瞀。　【說文】本作瞀。【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25206.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥈆</span>。瞀字原刻从攴，不从攵。</p>
