						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>粥</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhōu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄡˉ</span></p><ol><li>用米面等食物煮成的半流质食品：小米～。</li><li>像粥的东西：泥～。乱成一锅～。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>粥</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˋ</span></p><ol><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">育</span>”，生养。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">鬻</span>”，卖。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>粥</strong> <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“鬻”</span>。卖 <span class="encs">[sell]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则使其贾粥之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·巫马》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不粥祭器。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">田里不粥。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">戎器不粥于市。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:粥画(卖画的)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 养育 <span class="encs">[bring up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初俊羔助厥母粥。俊也者大也，粥也者养也。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 嫁出 <span class="encs">[marry off]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请粥庶弟之母。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“粥，谓嫁之也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>粥</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。字本作<span class="yhcs">“鬻”</span>。从鬻(古文<span class="yhcs">“鬲”</span>字)，米声。鬲，古烹饪器。本义:稀饭) 同本义 <span class="encs">[gruel;porridge;congee]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粥，驙也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驙粥之食。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“厚曰驙，稀曰粥。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">崇</span>为客作豆粥。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·石崇传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恃粥耳。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粥以糊口。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上" target="_blank">未集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上&amp;bs=米" target="_blank">米</a>】
                              粥·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7CA5.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>之六切，音祝。糜也。【釋名】粥濯于糜，粥粥然也。【禮·月令】仲秋行糜粥飮食。【風土記】天正日南，黃鐘踐長，是日始牙動，爲饘粥以養幼。【南越志】盧陵城中有井，半靑半黃，黃者甜滑，宜作粥，色如金，似灰汁，甚芬馨。</p><p>又豆粥。【後漢·馮異傳】光武至饒陽蕪蔞亭，異上豆粥。【晉書·石崇傳】崇爲客作豆粥，咄嗟便辦。</p><p>又茗粥。【茶錄】吳人采茶煑之，名茗粥。</p><p>又楡粥。【唐書·陽城傳】隱中條山，歲饑，屑楡爲粥。</p><p>又天有粥㕑。【天文集要】玉井主粥㕑。</p><p>又凉州以粥爲羅闍。【異物志】高昌僻土，有異于華，寒服冷水，暑啜羅闍。</p><p>又【說文】本作鬻。今俗作粥。【前漢·文帝紀】吏廩當受鬻者，或以陳粟。【註】鬻與粥同。師古曰：黃帝始烹穀爲粥，周謂之饘，宋、衞謂之餰。</p><p>又姓。鬻熊，周文王時人，省作粥。楚有粥權，卽熊後。</p><p>又粥粥，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤰞</span>謙貌。【禮·儒行】粥粥若無能也。【疏】粥粥，柔弱專愚之貌。【註】亦作羊六反。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>余六切，音育。【前漢·禮樂志】粥粥音送。【註】粥粥，敬懼貌。師古曰：粥，弋六反。</p><p>又北狄名。【史記·五帝紀】黃帝北遂葷粥。【註】粥，音育。</p><p>又賣也。【禮·王制】田里不粥。【荀子·儒效篇】魯之粥牛馬者不豫賈。</p><p>又【韻會小補】靡爲切。䭈也。通作靡。</p><p>又叶職律切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B74.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠭴</span>。【蘇轍·鍾山詩】老僧一身泉上住，十年掃盡人閒迹。客到惟燒柏子香，晨饑坐視山前粥。</p>
