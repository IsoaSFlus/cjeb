                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>敷</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>布置，铺开，摆开：～设。～陈（铺陈，详细叙述）。～衍（ａ．叙述并发挥，亦作“敷演”；ｂ．做事不够负责或待人不恳切，只做表面上的应付；ｃ．勉强维持）。</li><li>涂上，搽上：～药。～粉。</li><li>足够：入不～出。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">肤</span>”，表面的。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">敷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>敷</strong> <span class="dicpy">fū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，表示与动作有关。<span class="yhcs">“搏”</span>是<span class="yhcs">“布列”</span>的意思，在字中也具有表意作用。本义:施予;给予)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grant;carry out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勘定厥功，用敷遗后人休。——<span class="smcs">《书·康王之诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敷政优优，百禄是道。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·长发》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">舜</span>不忍伐，而敷之文德。—— <span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《原弈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:敷民(施于人民。敷:布施);敷施(布施);敷锡(施赐);敷教(布施教化)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 铺展;铺开 <span class="encs">[spread out;unfold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>敷土，随山刊木，奠高山大川。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牖间南向，敷重篾席。——<span class="smcs">《书·顾命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敷宴席。——<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">离城三里，止营傅陈。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·陈汤传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“傅，读曰敷。敷，布也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 搽，涂上 <span class="encs">[apply]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">头上周围一转的短发…越显得面如敷粉，唇若施脂。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:敷粉;敷药</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“布”</span>( <span class="dicpy">bù</span>)。宣告;陈述 <span class="encs">[narrate in detail]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不能敷扬五教，翼赞日月。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张网传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">善敷者辞殊而义显。——<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙·鎔裁》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:敷布(陈述);敷文(铺陈文藻);敷畅(敷陈发扬);敷奏(敷陈上奉);敷释(铺叙阐释);敷布(叙陈)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 开<span class="encs">[花]</span> <span class="encs">[open]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">用花者取花初敷时。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:敷英(开花);敷秀(敷英;敷披。开放);敷荣(繁荣;开花);敷华(开花)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 传布 <span class="encs">[propagate]</span>。如:敷弘(传布弘扬);敷展(展布，传播);敷扬(传播宣扬);敷显(传布显扬)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 宣布;公布 <span class="encs">[make public to]</span>。如:敷宣(宣布);敷政(推行政事);敷纶(宣诏);敷露(公布，披露)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 足，够 <span class="encs">[be sufficient
