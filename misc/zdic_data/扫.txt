                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扫</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>扫</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">掃</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>拿笤帚等除去尘土或垃圾：～地。</li>
	<li>清除，消灭：～雷。～盲。～荡。一～而空。</li>
	<li>全，尽，尽其所有：～数（<span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span>）。</li>
	<li>低落，丧失：～兴（<span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>描画：～描。～眉。</li>
	<li>迅速横掠而过：～射。～视。</li>
	<li>结束，了结：～尾。</li>
	<li>祭奠：～墓。祭～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>扫</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">掃</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">s&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～帚〕以竹枝等扎成的扫地用具。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扫</strong></p>
<p>掃、埽 <span class="dicpy">sǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从手，从帚。手拿扫帚表示打扫。本义:打扫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[clean up;sweep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">埽，弃也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作掃。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">埽，除也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌五寝之埽除粪洒之事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;隶仆》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子有廷内，弗洒弗扫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;山有枢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">花径不曾缘客扫，蓬门今始为君开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《客至》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扫径(清洁路径);扫尘(扫去尘垢;比喻扫荡残敌，平定乱事);扫脑儿(秃子);扫道(踩出来的路);扫桃(扫面子);扫花以待(表示主人待客的诚意);扫眉才子(指有文才的女子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 掠过<span class="encs">[sweep away]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千骑飚扫，万乘雷奔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《大猎赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扫望(扫视);扫射</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 清除，消灭 <span class="encs">[eliminate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扫</span><span class="diczx2">项</span>军于 <span class="diczx2">垓下</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扫白(除去白发);扫穴(清除巢穴);扫穴擒渠(扫荡巢穴，擒拿贼首);扫殄(消灭);扫清(平定廓清);扫定(平定):扫难(平定患难)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 画，染 <span class="encs">[paint]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淡扫蛾眉朝至尊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张祜</span><span class="smcs">《集灵台》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扫黛(画眉;用黛描画);扫眼(描眼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 祭扫 <span class="encs">[sacrifice and renovate]</span></p>
