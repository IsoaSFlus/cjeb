                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">埙</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>埙</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">塤</span>；<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">壎</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xūn.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代用陶土烧制的一种吹奏乐器，圆形或椭圆形，有六孔。亦称&ldquo;陶埙&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> instrument</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">埙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>埙</strong></p>
<p>塤、壎 <span class="dicpy">xūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p> (形声。从土，熏声。本义:古代用陶土烧制的一种吹奏乐器，大小如鹅蛋，六孔，顶端为吹口。又叫&ldquo;陶埙&rdquo;) 同本义 <span class="encs">[an egg-shaped， holed wind instrument]</span>。也有用石、骨、象牙制成的</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">壎，乐器也。以土为之，六孔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作塤。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">埙箫管。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;小师》</span>。注:&ldquo;大如雁卵。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">调竽笙壎箎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伯氏吹壎，仲氏吹箎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;何人斯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如壎如箎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;板》</span>。注:&ldquo;其声平下，与箎相谐。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">礼乐壎坎音也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡六孔，上一、前三、后二。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新定三礼图&middot;投壶图&middot;埙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:埙箎(埙为土制乐器，篪为竹制乐器，埙篪合奏声音和谐。后用以表示兄弟和睦，也代指兄弟)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
