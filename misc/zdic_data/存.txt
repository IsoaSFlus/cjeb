                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">存</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>存</strong> <span class="dicpy">cún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:生存;存在)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[live;exist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">存，存在也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“存即在。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有天子存。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·隐公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽存乎人者。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以其存心也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·离娄下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召寇则无以存矣。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·应同》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽我之死，有子存焉。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而吾以捕蛇独存。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:存济(存活;活命);存殁(活着的和死去的)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 恤问，劳问 <span class="encs">[comfort;console;soothe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">存，恤问也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无一介之使以存之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“劳问也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:存候(存问，问候);存问(安慰，问候);存抚(存问安抚);存养(安抚养育)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 访问;问候 <span class="encs">[visit;express regards and concern for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">存问长老。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·文帝纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“省视也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣乃市井鼓刀者，而公子亲数存之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:存见(探望慰问);存访(探望问候);存谕(存问、慰谕)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 抚育;保护 <span class="encs">[foster;nurture;protect]</span>。如:存神(存养精神);存孤(恤养孤儿)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 思念;怀念 <span class="encs">[miss]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五曰存爱。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其存君兴国而欲反复之，一篇之中致志焉。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:存眷(思念，关注);存想(考虑，思索，想像);存注(意念，注意力);存追(追念，追慕)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 关心;关怀 <span class="encs">[concern]</span>。如:存神(留神);存顾(关心照顾);存覆(关怀爱护);存润(关怀接济)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 储存;保存;保全 <span class="encs">[store;preserve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或仅存姓名而无事迹。——</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略·序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span>
