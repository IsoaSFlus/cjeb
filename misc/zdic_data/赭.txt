data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhě.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 红土 <span class="encs">[red soil]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赭，赤土也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">偷长以赭污其衣裙。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·张敞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其土则丹青赭垩。——<span class="smcs">《文选·司马相如·子虚赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:赭垩(红土与白土)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 赤红如赭土的颜料，古人用以饰面 <span class="encs">[ocher dye]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赫如渥赭，公言赐爵。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·简兮》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="yhcs">“赭衣”</span>的简称 <span class="encs">[red worn by convicts dress]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">田叔</span>钳赭，志于夷戮。——<span class="smcs">《宋书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:赭徒(囚徒，犯人。因身穿赭衣，故称)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 赤地，寸草不生的土地。亦指田野寸草不生的荒芜景象 <span class="encs">[barren land]</span>。如:赭尽(谓使山野草木空尽而成赤地)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 山名 <span class="encs">[Zhe mountain]</span>。在浙江省萧山县东北。山石呈赭色，故名</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>赭</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhě.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 染成红色 <span class="encs">[dye red]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或赭其垣。——</span><span class="cino">(3)</span> 因羞愧而脸红 <span class="encs">[blush]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人戏称曰:<span class="yhcs">“公之<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>，亦权倚阁乎?”</span></span><span class="diczx2">秩</span>色颇赭。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">彭乘</span><span class="smcs">《墨客挥犀》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 施加罪罚 <span class="encs">[punish]</span>。如:赭墨(服赭衣，受墨刑。指刑罚)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 伐尽树木，使山岭赤裸呈赭色。如:赭山(伐尽山上的树木)</p><p>◎ <strong>赭</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="encs">[reddish-brown]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赭，赤也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释器》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桃汤赭鞭。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传》</span>。</span><span
