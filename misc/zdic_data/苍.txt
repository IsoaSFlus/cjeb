                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">苍</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>苍</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄤˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>青色（包括蓝和绿）：～翠。～松。～天。～穹（苍天）。～龙。</li>
	<li>灰白色：～白。～～（➊灰白；➋苍茫）。～老。～劲（苍老挺拔，多指树木形态或书画笔力）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">苍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>苍</strong></p>
<p>蒼 <span class="dicpy">cāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，仓声。本义:草色)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，引申为青黑色 <span class="encs">[green]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苍，草色也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苍，青也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在色为苍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;阴阳应象大论》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓薄青色。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼苍者天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;黄鸟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春为苍天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释天》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东方曰苍天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;有始》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驾苍龙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令孟春》</span>。注:&ldquo;苍亦青也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">染于苍则苍，染于黄则黄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;所染》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:苍山(青山);苍苔(青苔);苍头(旧指仆人。汉时奴仆皆以深青色巾包头，故名;又指老年人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 灰白色 <span class="encs">[ashy;grey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">满面尘灰烟火色，两鬓苍苍十指黑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:苍髯;苍浪(花白);苍华(形容头发灰白)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 苍老 <span class="encs">[old;aged]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苍颜(苍老的容颜)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>苍</strong></p>
<p>蒼 <span class="dicpy">cāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 百姓 <span class="encs">[common people]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苍生(指百姓，众生);苍黔(百姓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>

<p>又【博雅】茂也。【書&middot;益稷篇】至于海隅蒼生。【傳】蒼蒼然生草木。</p>
<p>又老也。【詩&middot;秦風】蒹葭蒼蒼。【釋文】物老之狀。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;陳勝傳】蒼頭。【註】士卒靑帛巾。</p>
<p>又【綱目集覽】蒼黃，急遽貌。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】蒼氏。【註】《風俗通》云：八愷，蒼舒之後。</p>
<p>又【韻會】采朗切，倉上聲。莽蒼，寒狀。一曰近郊之色。【莊子&middot;逍遙遊】適莽蒼者，三飡而反。【唐書&middot;韋述傳】蒼卒犇逼。【白居易詩】寒銷春蒼茫。　亦作倉。【禮&middot;月令】駕倉龍，服倉玉。【史記&middot;蕭望之傳】倉頭廬兒。　【直音】作蒼。蒼字从艸作。</p>
