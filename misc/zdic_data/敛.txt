                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">敛</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>敛</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">斂</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">liǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>收拢，聚集：～钱。～足（收住脚步，不住前进）。～容。～衣（用收集来的碎布制成的衣）。收～。聚～。</li>
	<li>征收：横征暴～。</li>
	<li>收束，约束：～迹。～手（➊缩手，表示不敢恣意妄为；➋拱手，表示恭敬）。～袂（整理衣袖，表示敬服）。～抑。～步。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">敛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>敛</strong></p>
<p>斂、歛 <span class="dicpy">liǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，佥( <span class="dicpy">qiān</span>)。本义:收集，聚集)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(收集的对象是物，常是财物) <span class="encs">[gather]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敛，收也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既射则敛之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;夏官&middot;缮人》</span>。注:&ldquo;敛，藏也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狗彘食人之食不知敛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鞭挞其夫家，聚敛贡城阙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《自京赴奉先县咏怀五百字》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敛赀财以送其行。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敛局(邀人聚赌，从中抽头);敛衣(聚集零碎布头所制成的衣服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 收获 <span class="encs">[harvest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自种自敛，服箱以走。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《牛赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敛材(采集可食的百草根实);敛实(收割庄稼);敛获(收获)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 征收 <span class="encs">[levy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">豹</span>因重敛百姓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">邺</span>三老、 <span class="diczx2">廷椽</span>尝岁赋敛百姓，收取其钱得数百万。&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赋敛之毒有甚是蛇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">稍敛众以去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高君</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">成</span>不敢敛户口。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:征敛(旧指官府向民间征捐敛财);聚敛(重税搜刮);敛法(收税的准则)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 收缩;退缩 <span class="encs">[contract;shrink back;flinch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>、 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>合而为一以临 <span class="diczx2">韩</span>， <span class="diczx2">韩</span>必敛手。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;春申君列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敛额(皱眉);敛退(退缩);敛策(收束马鞭。比喻隐退不仕);敛手(缩手不敢乱动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 停止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:敛兵(收兵);敛辔(息驾，收起车马);敛祸(停止作祸);敛毫(停笔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 入殓 <span class="encs">[put a body into coffin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣尸棺曰敛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释丧制》</span></span></p>
