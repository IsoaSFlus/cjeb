                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">笋</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>笋</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">筍</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sǔn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǔn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄣˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>竹子初从土里长出的嫩茎、芽，又称&ldquo;竹笋&rdquo;，可以做菜吃。可食用者主要有&ldquo;毛竹笋&rdquo;、&ldquo;慈竹笋&rdquo;、&ldquo;麻竹笋&rdquo;等。</li>	<li>竹子的青皮：～席（用竹青编成的席子）。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/榫">榫</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bamboo shoots</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bambussprossen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pousse de bambou</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">笋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>笋</strong></p>
<p>筍 <span class="dicpy">sǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，尹( <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span>)声。本义:竹笋)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 竹子初从土里长出的嫩芽，味鲜美，可以做菜，也叫&ldquo;竹笋&rdquo; <span class="encs">[bamboo shoot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筍，竹胎也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作笋。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绚笋及蒲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;韩奕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苞笋抽节。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如筀竹笋，有二月生者，有三四月生者，有五月方生者谓之晚筀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笋玉(喻指女人的手指);笋剥(像剥笋似地脱掉，剥光);笋枯(笋干);笋鞭(竹的地下茎);笋尖(笋的尖嫩部分);笋衣(笋壳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 竹的青皮 <span class="encs">[bamboo&#39;s skin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敷重笋席。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;顾命》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;析竹青皮也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如竹箭之有筠也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笋席(用篾青所编的竹席);笋鞋(以竹皮或笋壳做成的鞋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代悬挂钟磬等乐器的横木 <span class="encs">[bar]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:笋业(悬钟磬的架子);</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 同&ldquo;榫&rdquo;。器物利用凹凸方式相接处凸出的部分 <span class="encs">[tenon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:笋里不知卯里(这一方面不知那一方面);笋头(榫头。竹、木等器物或构件利用凹凸方式相接处凸出的部分)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>笋</strong> <span class="dicpy">sǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p>嫩的 <span class="encs">[tender]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:笋鸡;笋鸭</span></p>
