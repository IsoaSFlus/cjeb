                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>檠</strong> <span class="dicpy">qíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> ( 形声。从木，敬声。本义:矫正弓弩的器具)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[adjuster]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">檠，榜也。…弛弓防损伤，以竹若木辅于里绳约之。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不能绁檠巧用。——<span class="smcs">《诗·角弓》</span>传</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">檠不正而可以正弓。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弓待檠而后能调。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·脩务》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">檠弓弩。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·苏武传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:檠枻(矫正弓弩的器具);檠榜( 矫正弓弩的器具)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 灯台 <span class="encs">[lampstand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">梦断酒醒山雨绝，笑看饥鼠上灯檠。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《侄安节远来夜坐》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 灯 <span class="encs">[lamp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">投僧避夜雨，古檠昏无膏。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《自州追送朱氏女弟》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">檠</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>檠</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄑㄧㄥˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>qíng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3738.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">辅正弓弩的器具。</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子·修务》：「故弓待<b>檠</b>而后能调，剑待砥而后能利。」</span><span class="gc_yy">汉·刘向〈劝学〉：「操弓不返于<b>檠</b>，木受绳则直。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">灯架、烛台。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·苏轼〈姪安节远来夜坐〉诗三首之二：「梦断酒醒山雨绝，笑看饥鼠上灯<b>檠</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">灯。</span><span class="gc_yy">北周·庾信〈对烛赋〉：「莲帐寒<b>檠</b>窗拂曙，筠笼熏火香盈絮。」</span><span class="gc_yy">元·翁森〈四时读书乐〉诗四首之三：「近床赖有短<b>檠</b>在，对此读书功更倍。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">矫正。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷五四·苏建传》：「武能网纺缴、<b>檠</b>弓弩。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">拿、托。</span><span class="gc_yy">《喻世明言·卷一七·单符郎全州佳偶》：「空手忽<b>檠</b>双块玉，污泥挺出并头莲。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「擎」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
