                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缶</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，上面是<span class="yhcs">“午”</span>字，即<span class="yhcs">“杵”</span>。下面是<span class="yhcs">“缶”</span>的本体。<span class="yhcs">“杵”</span>是棒子，可用来制坯。<span class="yhcs">“缶”</span>又是秦乐器，<span class="yhcs">“杵”</span>可以敲击成曲。本义:瓦器， 圆腹小口，用以盛酒浆等)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 盛酒浆的瓦器。大腹小口，有盖。也有铜制的 <span class="encs">[earthen jar with big belly and small mouth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缶，瓦器所以盛酒浆，</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>人鼓之以节歌。象形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盎谓之缶。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贰用缶。——<span class="smcs">《易·坎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以二缶钟惑。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天地》</span>。按，钟聚也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有孚盈缶。——<span class="smcs">《易·比》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“汲器。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坎其击缶。——<span class="smcs">《诗·陈风·宛丘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">击瓮叩缶。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李斯传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五献之尊，门外缶，门内壶。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼器》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 汲水的瓦器 <span class="encs">[earthware for sucking water]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">具绠缶，备水器。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[考古]</span>∶一种陶瓷乐器 <span class="encs">[ fou ，a clay musical instrument]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不鼓缶而歌。——<span class="smcs">《易·离》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>缶</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一种古容量单位，等于十六斗。一说三十二斗 <span class="encs">[ fou ]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薮二有半谓之缶，缶二谓之钟。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:缶米(一缶的米。即十六斗米)</p>                    <div                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7F36.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】方久切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】俯九切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音否。【說文】瓦器，所以盛酒漿，秦人鼓之以節歌。【爾雅·釋器】盎謂之缶。【註】盆也。【疏】缶是瓦器，可以節樂，如今擊甌，又可以盛水盛酒，卽今之瓦盆也。【急就篇註】缶卽盎也，大腹而斂口。【易·比卦】有孚盈缶。【釋文】鄭云：缶，汲器也。</p><p>又【坎卦】樽酒簋貳用缶。【註】瓦缶之器。</p><p>又【離卦】不鼓缶而歌。【前漢·楊惲傳】仰天撫缶。【註】缶，瓦器也。</p><p>又【小爾雅】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2894F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨥏</span>二有半謂之藪，藪二有半謂之缶，缶二謂之鍾。【註】缶，四斛也。【魯語】其歲收田一井，出稯禾，秉芻，缶米。【註】缶，庾也。</p><p>又【前漢·高帝紀】上破布軍於會缶。【註】蘇林曰：音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2136E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡍮</span>。師古曰：此字本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26248.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉈</span>，而轉寫誤爲缶字耳。黥布傳則正作垂字。</p><p>又【正字通】宥韻，讀若覆，義同。○按諸韻書無去聲。</p>
