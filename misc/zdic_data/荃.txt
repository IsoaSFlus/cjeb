                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>荃</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">quán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/quán.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄩㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>古书上说的一种香草，亦用以喻国君：～察（旧时书信中请人原谅的敬辞，“荃不察余之中情兮”）。～鉴。～宰（喻君臣）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">筌</span>”，捕鱼的竹器。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>荃</strong> <span class="dicpy">quán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/quán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，全声。本义:香草名，即荪)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 香草名。即<span class="yhcs">“菖蒲”</span>，又名<span class="yhcs">“荪”</span>。古用以比喻君主 <span class="encs">[calamus]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荃不揆余之中情兮。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“香草也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荃者，所以在鱼得鱼而忘荃。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·外物》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“香草也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荃蕙蔽匿兮，胡久而不芳?——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《吊屈原文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:荃宰(指君臣);荃荪(香草。古代常用以喻贤良的人);荃察(旧时书信中常用为希望对方鉴谅的敬辞);荃芜(香草名)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 细切的腌芥菜 <span class="encs">[pickled leaf mustard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荃，芥脃也。从草，全声。谓以芥为斋、鲜脃也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“筌”</span>。捕鱼具 <span class="encs">[fishtackle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荃者所以在鱼，得鱼而忘荃。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·外物》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:荃蹄(鱼苟和兔网)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“絟”</span>。细布 <span class="encs">[fine cloth]</span>喻君主 <span class="encs">[emperor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">繇王</span><span class="diczx2">闽侯</span>亦遣建荃。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·景十三王传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:荃宰(喻君臣);荃察(荃，指国君;察，明察)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
