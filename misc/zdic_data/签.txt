                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">签</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>签</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">簽</span><span class="diczx3">籤</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>亲自写姓名或画上符号：～名。～字。～到。～发。～收。～署。～押。～订。</li><li>简要地写出意见：～注。～呈。～证。</li><li>用竹木等物做成的细棍或片状物：牙～儿。</li><li>书册里作标志的纸片或其他物体上作标志的东西：书～。标～。</li><li>粗粗地缝合起来。</li><li>用于占卜或赌博的细长竹片或细棍：～筒。求～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">签</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>签</strong></p><p>簽 <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，佥声。本义:署名押字)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sign one's name on]</span>。如:请你签个字;签合同;签押房(官员批阅公文的地方);签任(受签署任用的人;官吏)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用简单的文字拟具意见 <span class="encs">[make brief comments on a document]</span>。如:签揭(贴签写出要点);签贴(签注意见贴在正文上的字条)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 粗粗地缝合起来 <span class="encs">[tack]</span>。如:把袖口签上</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 引，导引 <span class="encs">[lead]</span>。如:签合(导引会合);签河(为分水护堤而开筑的引河)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 下令征调 <span class="encs">[call up]</span>。如:签军(战时征调兵员)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 刺;插 <span class="encs">[stick]</span>。如:签钉(栽插)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>签</strong></p><p>簽 <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 旧时官府交给差役拘捕犯人的片状凭证，多用竹、木做成 <span class="encs">[bamboo slips]</span>。如:朱签;火签;签军(金元时一遇战事，多强迫汉人壮男当兵，因非正军，只服苦役，称为<span class="yhcs">“签军”</span>);签解(持签牌押送);签票(旧时官府交给差役拘捕犯人的凭证)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 公文体制的一种 <span class="encs">[a kind of document]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 签子 <span class="encs">[pick，slender pointed pieces of bamboo，wood or iron wire]</span>。一种用竹木或铁丝制成的尖细棍子。亦用作量词，犹串。如:签堤(用竹桩加固的防水堤)</p><p>◎ <strong>签</strong></p><p>籖、籤 <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 标识，标签 <span class="encs">[label]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">籤，验也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">记识曰籤。与检略同。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:书签;签题(书册的标签);签筹(古代滴水计时器中指示时刻的指针);标签;浮签;签帙(标签和书套);签轴(加有标签便于检取的卷轴);签牌(标签)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 漏箭 <span class="encs">[bamboo slip]</span>，古代漏壶上记时的竹签，上有刻度。如:签筹(更筹，古代计时报更的竹签);签声(古代晚间报更时，更签掷地的响声)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代官府拘捕、惩罚犯人的凭证 <span class="encs">[a kind of document]</span>。以竹片制成，上有文字等标记。如:签疏(签呈和疏奏);签票(捕人的文书);签告(经检复后发给的授职证书)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 寺庙中求神占卜凶吉所用的竹片，其上写有凶吉祸福的词句(多以诗的形式) <span class="encs">[bamboo slips used for divination]</span>。如:求签;神签(求神占卜吉凶所用的竹片);签诗(寺庙中供卜问吉凶所编的诗句);签诀(犹签诗);签谱(犹签诗)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 背面涂有胶，用时润湿即压贴于某表面的纸片 <span class="encs">[sticker]</span>。如:航空邮签</p><p><span
