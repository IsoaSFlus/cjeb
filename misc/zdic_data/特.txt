                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>特</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄜˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>不平常的，超出一般的：～殊。～色。～产。～权。～性。～征。～需。～技。～务。～区。～价。～例。～效。</li>
	<li>单，单一：～为（<span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i </span>）。～设。～地。～惠。～辑。～使。～赦。～约。</li>
	<li>只，但：不～如此。&ldquo;相如度秦王～以诈佯为予赵城，实不可得&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>三岁的兽，一说四岁的兽：&ldquo;不狩不猎，胡瞻尔庭有悬～兮！&rdquo;</li>
	<li>公牛，亦用以借指公马和雄性的牲畜。</li>
	<li>配偶，匹配：&ldquo;不因旧姻，求尔新～&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">特</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>特</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从牛，寺声。本义:雄性的牛马)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bull]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">特，朴特，牛父也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡马，特居四之一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;夏官&middot;校人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">特，牡牛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:特处士(赋予公牛以处士头衔);特牛(一头公牛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指牛 <span class="encs">[ox]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">高辛氏人家生一犬，初如小特。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">樊绰</span><span class="smcs">《蛮书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 三岁兽 <span class="encs">[three-year old beast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胡瞻尔庭有县特兮?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 敌特;特务 <span class="encs">[spy]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:反特;特工</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>特</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 独特，特异;突出;特别<span class="encs">[special;particular;out of the ordinary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士之特立独行，适于义而已。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《伯夷颂》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何其无特操与?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;齐物论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后知是山之特立，不与培塿为类。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《始得西山宴游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣</span><span class="diczx2">超</span>区区，特蒙神灵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;班超传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:特达(特别通晓事理);特支(特别支出。源于宋时朝廷发给军士的特别赏赐);特等;特旨(特诏;帝王的特别诏令);特命(特别命令);特绝(卓绝);特表(非凡的仪表)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="encs">[single-handed;by oneself]</span> 。如:特操(独立的操守);特识(独立的见解);特笔(独特的笔法);特见(独特的见解);特士(有独特志向、操行的人物)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>特</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>单独;单单;特地 <span class="encs">[alone;by oneself;specially]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫国岂特为车哉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;君守》</span></span></p>
