<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧˋ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>辅佐：～士（辅佐的人）。～匡（辅佐匡正的人）。～导。～谐。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弼</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tool for rectifying crossbow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弼，辅也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，当训弓辅也。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 辅佐君王的大臣 <span class="encs">[assister]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柱石之臣，宜民辅弼。(左称辅，右称弼。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;伏湛传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梦帝赉予良弼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;说命上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一日去良弼，如亡左右手。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弼</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>辅助，特指臣下辅佐君王 <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">择其能正色。弼违。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;武帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能弼宁</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>室，辅予一人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;元帝纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予违汝弼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;益稷》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">建立辅弼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《求言令》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辅弼(辅佐);弼匡(辅佐改正);弼教(辅助教化)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>弼</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄅㄧˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>b&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0202.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">輔助。</span><span class="gc_yy">《字彙&middot;弓部》：「<b>弼</b>，輔也，助也，正也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;益稷》：「予違汝<b>弼</b>，汝無面從。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;孔安國&middot;傳：「我違道，汝當以義輔正我。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷九七&middot;魏徵傳》：「徵蹈履仁義，以<b>弼</b>朕躬。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">矯正過失。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「匡<b>弼</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《晉書&middot;卷三&middot;武帝紀》：「擇其能正色弼違匡救不逮者，以兼此選。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古時用來矯正弓弩的器具。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;臣道》「謂之拂」句下唐&middot;楊倞&middot;注：「拂，讀為<b>弼</b>。<b>弼</b>，所以輔正弓弩者也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">職官名。輔佐元首的官員。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;文王世子》：「設四輔及三公。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;孔穎達&middot;正義：「其四輔者，案《尚書大傳》云：『古者天子必有四鄰，前曰疑，後曰丞，左曰輔，右曰<b>弼</b>。』」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弼</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下" target="_blank">寅集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下&amp;bs=弓" target="_blank">弓</a>】
                              弼·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5F3C.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20842.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠡂</span>㢸<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A12.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠨒</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2260D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢘍</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/223C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢏇</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22F25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢼥</span>【唐韻】房密切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】薄密切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>頻入聲。【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22400.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢐀</span>。㐁，舌也。舌柔而弓剛，以柔从剛，輔弼之意。【韻會】所以輔正弓弩者。【正韻】正弓器也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】俌也。【註】俌猶輔也。【書&middot;大禹謨】明于五<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>，以弼五敎。</p>
<p>又【說命】夢帝賚予良弼。【越語】憎輔遠弼。【註】相道爲輔，矯過爲弼。</p>
<p>又戾也。【前漢&middot;五行志】君臣故弼，兹謂悖。【註】弼，猶相戾也。悖，惑也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】重也。【註】弼輔，所以爲重疉也。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】弼，高也。</p>
<p>又通作拂。【孟子】入則無法家拂士。</p>
<p>又叶平祕切，音備。【李嵩&middot;述志賦】赳赳干城，翼翼上弼。志馘奔鯨，截彼醜類。　【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22421.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢐡</span>。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5F3C.svg">                          <p>輔也。重也。从弜㐁聲。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22421.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢐡</span>，弻或如此。㢸、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/223C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢏇</span>，並古文弻。房密切〖注〗徐鍇曰：&ldquo;㐁，舌也，非聲。舌柔而弜剛，以柔从剛，輔弻之意。&rdquo; 文二　重三</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5F3C_1796.svg" /><span class="swbf">(弼)</span><span class="ywyy">輔也。</span><span class="swdz">輔者，車之輔也。引申爲凡左右之偁。釋詁曰：弼，俌也。人部曰：俌，輔也。俌輔意義皆同也。詩曰：交韔二弓。竹閉緄滕。傳云。交韔，交二弓於韔中也。閉，紲。緄，繩。滕，約也。小雅。騂騂角弓。翩其反矣。傳曰：騂騂，調利皃。不善紲檠巧用則翩然而反也。士喪禮注曰：柲，弓檠。驰則縛之於弓裏備損傷。以竹爲之。詩所謂竹柲緄滕。木部曰：榜，所以輔弓弩檠榜也。然則曰檠，曰榜，曰柲，曰閉者竹木爲之。曰紲，曰滕者，縛之於驰弓以定其體也。弓必有輔而後正。人亦然。故輔謂之弼。</span><span class="ywyy">从弜㐁聲。</span><span class="swdz">徐鍇曰：㐁非聲。按非也。㐁正曰：一讀若誓。弼字从此。誓與弼同十五部也。房密切。玉篇曰。今字作弼。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5F3C_1797.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22421.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢐡</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22400.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢐀</span>如此。</span><span class="swdz">从重㐁者，取會意。㐁，舌皃也。弛弓之檠，如口中之舌。二弓則二舌矣。重㐁以見二弓也。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5F3C_1798.svg" /><span class="swbf">(㢸)</span><span class="ywyy">亦古文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22400.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢐀</span>。</span><span class="swdz">攴，小擊也。榜之則不無扑擊。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5F3C_1799.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/223C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢏇</span>)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22400.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢐀</span>或如此。</span><span class="swdz">弗者，矯也。故从弗。弗亦聲。</span></p>                          </div>
