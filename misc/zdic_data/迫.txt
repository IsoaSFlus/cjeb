                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>迫</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄛˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用强力压制，硬逼：逼～。～害。压～。强～。胁～。～降（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng </span>）。～降（<span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;ng </span>）。～不得已。</li>
	<li>接近：～近。～冬。</li>
	<li>急促：急～。～切。～不及待。</li>
	<li>狭窄：地势局～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>迫</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǎi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄞˇ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>〔～击炮〕一种火炮。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">迫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>迫</strong></p>
<p>迫 <span class="dicpy">p&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，白声。本义:逼近)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[approach]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迫，近也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迫，逼也，近也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">望崦嵫而勿迫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span>。注:&ldquo;附也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而侮所迫之国者，可亡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;亡征》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涉旬月，迫季冬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时北兵已迫修门外。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迫措(犹逼近)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 硬逼，逼迫 <span class="encs">[compel;force;press]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">贾家庄</span>几为巡徼所陵迫死。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">里胥迫我纳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《重赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胁迫(威胁强迫);强迫(施加压力使服从);迫劫(逼迫);迫蹙(逼迫强制);迫阨(逼迫;胁迫);迫弄(逼迫并戏耍);迫拶(逼迫);迫索(强迫索取);迫胁(逼迫、威胁);迫勒(强迫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 催促 <span class="encs">[urge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迫生而下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;贵生》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迫</span><span class="diczx2">孔悝</span>于厕，强盟之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十五年》</span></p>
