                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>抡</strong></p>
<p>掄 <span class="dicpy">lūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 挥动或挥舞 <span class="encs">[brandish;swing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那后生抡着棒又赶入来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抡打(挥舞);抡大铁锤;抡荤的(说下流话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 任意浪费家财 <span class="encs">[spend freely]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:万贯家财，禁不住胡抡</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 猛拉 <span class="encs">[switch]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:绳子可能从你的手里抡出去</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>抡</strong></p>
<p>掄 <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，仑( <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:挑选，选拔) 同本义 <span class="encs">[select]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抡，择也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡邦工入山林而抡材不禁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;山虞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君抡贤人之后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;择也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抡材(抡才，选拨人才);抡选(选拔，挑选);抡择(挑选);抡元(科举考试中选第一名);抡魁(科举考试的第一名。亦指中选第一名)</span></p>
