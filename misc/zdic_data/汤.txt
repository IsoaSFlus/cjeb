                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">汤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>汤</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">湯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄤˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>热水：～雪。赴～蹈火。扬～止沸。</li>
	<li>煮东西的汁液：米～。参（<span class="dicpy">shēn </span>）～。</li>
	<li>烹调后汁特别多的食物：鸡～。菜～。清～。</li>
	<li>专指温泉（现多用于地名）：～泉（温泉）。～山（在中国北京市）。</li>
	<li>中药的剂型：～剂。～药。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>汤</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">湯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄤˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～～〕大水急流的样子，如&ldquo;河水～～&rdquo;，&ldquo;浩浩～～&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hot water; soup, gravy, broth</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Suppe (S, Ess)​,Brühe (S, Ess)​,Tang (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bouillon,eau chaude,soupe,potage,échauder,chauffer,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">汤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>汤</strong></p>
<p>湯 <span class="dicpy">tāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，昜( <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;ng</span>)声。本义:热水;开水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[boiling water]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汤，热水也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见不善如探汤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;季氏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薪火水汤以济之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;备梯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日中如探汤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">踔出汤中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王头随堕汤中。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分其汤肉葬之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汤羊(经过开水处理的整只羊);汤火(滚水与烈火);汤炭(滚水与炭火);汤婆子(充以热水放在被中取暖的铜制扁圆形容器);汤浴(洗热水澡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 菜汤 <span class="encs">[soup]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三日入厨下，洗手作羹汤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《新嫁娘》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汤脚(剩汤);汤玉(汤饼);汤饭</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 把草药放在水里熬得的液态药剂 <span class="encs">[liquid decoction of medicinal herbs]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:汤粥(汤药和稀饭);汤散(汤药和散剂);汤引(药引子)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6E6F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】土郎切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】他郎切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音鏜。【說文】熱水也。【楚辭&middot;九歌】浴蘭湯兮沐芳。</p>
<p>又水名。【山海經】上申之山，湯水出焉。</p>
<p>又【水經注】江水，東逕瞿巫灘，左則湯谿水註之，源出朐忍縣北。</p>
<p>又谷名。【張衡&middot;南都賦】湯谷涌其後。</p>
<p>又州名。【韻會】廣南化外，唐置湯州，天寶時爲湯泉郡。</p>
<p>又縣名。【廣輿記】湯隂縣，屬彰德府。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;虞書疏】除殘去虐曰湯。馬融又云：雲行雨施曰湯。</p>
<p>又【風俗通&middot;王霸篇】湯者，攘也，昌也。言其攘除不軌，天下熾盛。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】式羊切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】尸羊切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音商。湯湯，流貌。一曰波動之狀。【詩&middot;大雅】江漢湯湯。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>他浪切，音儻。熱水沃也。【禮&middot;月令】如以熱湯。</p>
<p>又【集韻】余章切，音陽。與暘同。暘谷，日所出也。亦作陽。【淮南子&middot;天文訓】日出于湯谷。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】他浪切，音盪。與蕩通。【詩&middot;陳風】子之湯兮。【傳】湯，蕩也。【箋】言游蕩無不爲也。</p>
<p>又與盪同。【前漢&middot;天文志】四星若合，是謂大湯。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶透空切，音通。【東方朔&middot;七諫】何靑雲之流瀾兮，微霜降之蒙蒙。徐風至而徘徊兮，疾風過之湯湯。</p>
