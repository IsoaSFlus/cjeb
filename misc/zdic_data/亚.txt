                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>亚</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">亞</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄚˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>较差：他的体力不～于你。</li>	<li>次一等：～军。～圣。</li>	<li>原子价较低的，酸根或化合物中少含一个氢原子或氧原子的：硫酸～铁。</li>	<li>指&ldquo;亚细亚洲&rdquo;（位于东半球的东北部，简称&ldquo;亚洲&rdquo;）：东～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>Asia; second</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schwächer als, unterlegen, zweitrangig (Adj)​,untere, untergeordnet (Adj)​,Asien <Abkürzung für 亚<a href="./洲">洲</a> [<a href="./亞">亞</a><a href="./洲">洲</a>]&gt (Eig, Geo)​,sub- (z.B. Subtyp, Substruktur,...)​ <Suffix&gt (Med)​,sub-, unter- <Suffix&gt (Chem)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> inférieur,second,(abrév.)​ Asie</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亚</strong></p>
<p>亞 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆作&ldquo;亞&rdquo;。许慎认为像人驼背形。甲骨文一说像花边形。本义:丑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ugly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫地有山有泽，有黑有白，有美有亚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马王堆</span>汉墓帛书<span class="smcs">《十六经》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亚，丑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;此亚之本义。亚与恶音义皆同&hellip;衣驾切。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 次;次于。表示时间或空间的先后 <span class="encs">[second]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言天下之至赜而不可亚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span>荀本</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亚旅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;牧誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侯亚侯旅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;载芟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为亚卿焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">龟亚之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士虞礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亚父南向坐(亚父者，范增也。)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亚献(古代祭祀举行三次供物仪式，第二次叫&ldquo;亚献&rdquo;);亚元(名列第二);亚父(仅次于父);亚帅(副帅);亚相(官位次于丞相的大臣);亚将(次将，副将);亚饭(古代天子、诸侯第二次进食时奏乐佐食的乐师;古代天子、诸侯的第二次进食);亚圣(道德才智仅次于圣人);亚魁(古代泛指科举考试第二名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 低于;低。表示等级的高低 <span class="encs">[inferior;lower than]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">圭妫</span>之班，亚 <span class="diczx2">宋</span>子而相亲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十九年》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:&ldquo;亚，次之。&rdquo;</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亚</strong></p>
<p>亞 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 垂;低垂 <span class="encs">[hang down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">松装粉穗临窗亚，水结冰锤簇溜悬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">前蜀</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韦庄</span><span class="smcs">《对雪献薛常侍》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有几枝还低亚着，护定他从打枣的竿梢所得的皮伤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《野草》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 俯，偃俯 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舟人渔子入浦溆，山木尽亚洪涛风。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《戏题王宰画山水图歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言天下之至赜而不可亚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span>荀李</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亚旅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;牧誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侯亚侯旅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;载芟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为亚卿焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鱼亚之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士虞礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亚身受取白玉羁，开口衔将紫金勒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">元稹</span><span class="smcs">《望云骓马歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亚地(俯于地;倒于地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 压;低压 <span class="encs">[press down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可怜把个行者头，勒得似个亚腰葫芦，十分疼痛难忍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叶重如将青玉亚，花轻疑是红绡挂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《渔家傲》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挨着，靠近 <span class="encs">[be near to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武松</span>在轿上看时，只见亚肩叠背，闹闹嚷嚷，屯街塞巷，都来看迎大虫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亚肩叠背(压肩叠背)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 掩，闭 <span class="encs">[close]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">花笼淡月，重门深亚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡伸</span><span class="smcs">《丑奴儿慢》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;压&rdquo;。垂压 <span class="encs">[heavily press]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鬓毛垂领白，花蕊亚枝红。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《上巳日徐司录林园宴集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">花亚欲移竹，鸟窥新卷帘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《入宅三首》</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集上" target="_blank">子集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集上&amp;bs=二" target="_blank">二</a>】
                              亞·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E9E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>衣駕切，鴉去聲。【說文】醜也。象人局背之形。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】亞，次也。【蜀志】諸葛亮管蕭之亞。</p><p>又【增韻】少也。【廣韻】就也。</p><p>又姻亞，壻之父曰姻，兩壻相謂曰亞。【詩&middot;小雅】瑣瑣姻亞，則無膴仕。別作婭。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>於加切，音鴉。【前漢&middot;東方朔傳】伊優亞者，辭未定也。</p><p>又【趙古則曰】物之岐者曰亞。俗作丫椏。</p><p>又【正韻】烏落切。【正譌】與堊同，塗飾牆也。</p><p>又與惡同。【史記&middot;盧綰傳】綰孫他人封亞谷侯。【漢書】作惡谷。【語林】宋人有獲玉印，文曰周惡夫印。劉原父曰：漢條侯印，古亞惡二字通用。</p><p>又與滹通，滹沱。【禮&middot;禮器】作惡池。【秦&middot;詛楚文】作亞駝。</p><p>又與稏通，稻也。【韻會】䆉稏通作罷亞。</p><p>又【郝敬讀書通】壓通作亞。【杜甫&middot;上巳宴集詩】花蕊亞枝紅。</p><p>又【人宅詩】花亞欲移竹。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>與壓同。　【字彙】正譌云：亞本涂飾字，餘皆借義，旣爲借義所奪，小篆遂从土作堊字，又从心作惡字以別之。亞、堊、惡本一字，《秦&middot;詛楚文》以亞駝代滹沱，則因聲借用明矣。◎按《字彙》說是，但俗旣習用借義已久，姑載本義於後，以備一字原委云。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
