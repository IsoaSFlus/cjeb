                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>济</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">濟</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>渡，过河：同舟共～。</li>	<li>对困苦的人加以帮助：～世。救～。赈～。周～。接～。</li>	<li>补益：无～于事。</li></ol><p>● <strong>济</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">濟</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～水〕古水名，源于今中国河南省，流经山东省入渤海。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>济</strong></p>
<p>濟 <span class="dicpy">Jǐ</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，齐声。本义:水名，即济水。古四渎之一)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。<span class="encs">[Ji River]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">济，</span><span class="diczx2">济水</span>也。出 <span class="diczx2">常山</span><span class="diczx2">房子</span><span class="diczx2">赞皇山</span>东入 <span class="diczx2">汦</span>&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">济水</span>源出 <span class="diczx2">赞皇山</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阴谷皆入</span><span class="diczx2">济</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6FDF.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕泲㴉【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>子禮切，音秭。水名。【說文】水出常山郡房子縣贊皇山。【釋名】濟，濟也，源出河北濟河而南也。【風俗通&middot;山澤篇】濟者齊，齊其度量也。【書&middot;禹貢】導沇水，東流爲濟。【傳】泉源爲沇，流去爲濟。</p><p>又水名，出酈縣。【山海經】支離之山，濟水出焉。</p><p>又濟南，郡名。【廣輿記】漢初爲濟南國，景帝時分郡。</p><p>又濟濟，衆盛之貌。一曰多威儀也。【詩&middot;大雅】濟濟多士。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>子計切，音霽。渡也。【揚子&middot;方言】過渡謂之涉濟。【詩&middot;邶風】濟有深涉。</p><p>又止也。【詩&middot;鄘風】旣不我嘉，不能旋濟。</p><p>又與霽通。亦止也。【爾雅&middot;釋天】濟，謂之霽。【疏】濟，止也。今南陽人呼雨止爲霽。</p><p>又成也。【左傳&middot;僖二十年】以欲從人則可，以人從欲鮮濟。</p><p>又利用也。【易&middot;繫辭】臼杵之利，萬民以濟。</p><p>又益也。【左傳&middot;桓十一年】莫敖曰：盍請濟師于王。</p><p>又賙救也。【易&middot;繫辭】知周乎萬物，而道濟天下。</p><p>又相助也。【易&middot;謙卦】天道下濟而光明。</p><p>又【揚子&middot;方言】濟，憂也。陳楚或曰溼，或曰濟。</p><p>又讀作擠，滅也。【晉語】二帝用師，以相濟也。【註】濟，當讀爲擠。</p><p>又【集韻】前西切，音齊。濟濟，祭祀容。</p><p>又在禮切，音薺。與霽同。</p>
