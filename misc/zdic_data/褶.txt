						<p>● <strong>褶</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhě  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhě.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄜˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>衣服摺叠而形成的印痕：百～裙。</li>
	<li>泛指摺皱重复的部分：～子。～皱。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pleat, crease, wrinkles</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Falte (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pli,robe de cour (archaïque)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">褶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>褶</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 裤褶服中的上衣 <span class="encs">[court dress]</span>。始为左衽骑服，后亦改为右衽，用作常服、朝服。如:褶衣(裤褶服中的上衣);褶裤(骑服)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代的一种便服 <span class="encs">[informal dress]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:褶子(古代的一种便服。后常专指传统戏装中的一种便服，也作穿蟒袍的衬衣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 旧时女子的膝袜 <span class="encs">[stockings]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:褶裤(旧时女子的膝袜。也称膝裤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhě.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>褶</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhě.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，习( <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;</span>)声。本义:衣服折叠而形成的印痕)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pleat;crease;wrinkle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">休将包袱做枕头，&hellip;我只怕干时节熨不开褶皱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卷底衣褶中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:百褶裙;褶纹(纹痕);褶祬(衣服上的折皱);褶褶(皱叠的样子);褶绉(衣服上的皱纹);褶叠(折叠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 脸上的皱纹 <span class="encs">[wrinkles;lines]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:褶皱</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集下" target="_blank">申集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集下&amp;bs=衣" target="_blank">衣</a>】
                              褶·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8936.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】徒協切【韻會】達協切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音牒。【類篇】襲也。【儀禮&middot;士喪禮】襚者以褶。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;玉藻】帛爲褶。【註】衣有表裏而無著也。【喪大記】君褶衣褶衾。【註】褶，袷也。【急就篇註】褶，謂重衣之最在上者也。其形若袍，短身而廣袖。一曰左衽之袍也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】席入切，音習。【類篇】袴褶，騎服。【晉書&middot;輿服志】弓弩隊各五十人，黑袴褶。</p>
<p>又寔入切，音十。義同。</p>
<p>又【韻經】古襲字。註見十六畫。</p>
