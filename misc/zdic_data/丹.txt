                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丹</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>丹</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>红色：～砂（朱砂）。～桂（观赏植物，花为橘红色）。～心碧血（赤诚的忠心，珍贵的热血）。～青。</li>
	<li>依成方制成的颗粒状或粉末状的中药：丸散膏～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cinnabar (native HgS); vermilion (artificial HgS used as pigment)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kügelchen, Pulver ,Rot, Zinnoberrot</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rouge,vermillon,remède</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丹</strong> <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，外面框框象矿井形，里边的一横是加上的符号，表示那里有丹砂。本义:辰砂，朱砂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cinnabar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丹，巴越之赤石也。象采丹井。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">砺砥砮丹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有始州之国，有丹山。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;大荒北经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">加之以丹矸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃丹书帛曰&ldquo;</span><span class="diczx2">陈胜</span>王&rdquo;，置入所罾鱼腹中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丹矸(丹砂);丹砂(一种红色矿物，也叫&ldquo;朱砂&rdquo;);丹砾(矿物之丹砂，可为药用);丹铅(丹砂和铅粉，是古人校勘文字的用具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 道家炼制的所谓长生不老药。又称丹头;丹液 <span class="encs">[pills of immortality]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炼丹;丹剂(丹药，丹饵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代用作染色的颜料 <span class="encs">[red pigment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡画者丹质。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指南方。古代五行说以五色配五方，南方属火，火色丹，故称 <span class="encs">[southern]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丹山(南方当日之地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 帝王的，帝王居住的 <span class="encs">[imperial]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丹诏(皇帝的诏书);丹跸(帝王的车驾);丹书铁券(皇帝颁给功臣使其世代享受免罪特权的诏书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 依成方制成的颗粒状或粉末状的中成药 <span class="encs">[pellet;powder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山海丹</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵丹妙药</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>丹</strong> <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 红色，赤色 <span class="encs">[red]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">染羽以朱湛丹秫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颜如渥丹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;终南》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日上正赤如丹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丹秫(赤粟);丹陛云墀(雕刻着云彩的红色台阶);丹阙(宫殿前两边用红色染饰的楼台);丹毫(红颜色的笔);丹陛(红色的台阶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 赤诚 <span class="encs">[whole hearted]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丹悃(真诚;赤诚之心);丹诚(丹赤诚衷之心);丹禁(天子居住的禁城)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 炼丹的 <span class="encs">[making pills of immortality]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丹鼎(道家语。一种炼丹的器具);丹丘(神话中的神仙之境。日夜皆明);丹台(道家语。神仙居住的地方)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E39.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2007F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠁿</span>㣋<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20551.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠕑</span>【唐韻】都寒切【集韻】【韻會】多寒切【正韻】都艱切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音單。赤色丹砂也。【書&middot;禹貢】礪砥砮丹。【山海經】丹以赤爲主，黑白皆丹之類。【陶弘景曰】卽朱砂也。</p>
<p>又道家以烹鼎金石爲外丹，吐故納新爲內丹。【黃庭經】九轉八瓊丹。【註】八者：朱砂，雄黃，空靑，硫黃，雲母，戎鹽，硝石，雌黃也。</p>
<p>又【博物志】和氣相感，則陵出黑丹。仁主壽昌，民延壽命，天下太平。</p>
<p>又以朱色塗物曰丹。【揚雄&middot;解嘲】朱丹其轂。</p>
<p>又容美曰渥丹。【詩&middot;秦風】顏如渥丹。</p>
<p>又赤心無僞曰丹。【謝朓詩】旣秉丹石心，寧流素絲涕。</p>
<p>又姓。漢丹玉，宋丹山，明丹衷。</p>
<p>又丹陽，郡名。漢武帝攺鄣郡爲丹陽郡。晉武帝分立宣城，毗陵二郡。</p>
<p>又州名。本赤翟地，元魏置汾州，後改丹州。</p>
<p>又【崔豹古今註】丹徼，南方徼，色赤，故稱丹徼，爲南方之極也。</p>
<p>又丹丹，國名。見【南史】。</p>
<p>又【山海經】鳳凰產于丹穴。</p>
<p>又竊丹，鳥名。爲九鳳之一。</p>
<p>又牡丹，花名。【本草】一名鼠姑。</p>
<p>又木丹，梔子花別名。紫丹，茈草別名。</p>
<p>又叶都懸切，音顚。【陸機&middot;羅敷歌】南崖充羅幕，北渚盈輧軒。淸川含藻景，高岸被華丹。　【說文】丹巴，越赤石。外象丹井，中象丹形，靑彤雘等字从此。</p>
