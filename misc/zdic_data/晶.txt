data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，从三日，表示光亮之意。本义:光亮，明亮)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[brilliant;glittering]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晶，精光也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐灏</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“晶即星之象形文。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“精”</span> <span class="encs">[clear]</span>。如:晶灵(精灵)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 清明;晴朗 <span class="encs">[clear and bright;fine;sunny]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">八月凉风天气晶，万里无云河汉明。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋之问</span><span class="smcs">《明河篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 水晶的简称 <span class="encs">[rock crystal;crystal]</span>。如:茶晶;墨晶;晶印(水晶印章);晶帘(水晶帘子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 日，太阳 <span class="encs">[sun]</span>。如:晶辉(光辉)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
