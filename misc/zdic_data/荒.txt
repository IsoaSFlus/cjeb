                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>荒</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>年成不好，收成不好：～年。灾～。防～。备～。</li><li>长满野草，或无人耕种：～芜。～地。开～。</li><li>废弃：～废。～疏。～置。业精于勤，～于嬉。</li><li>冷落偏僻：～村。～郊。～落（<span class="dicpy">luò</span>）（ａ．荒凉冷落；ｂ．荒疏衰退）。～颓。</li><li>严重缺乏，不够用：煤～。</li><li>不实在的，不正确的：～信。～唐（ａ．浮夸，不实在；ｂ．行为放荡。“<span class="diczx3">唐</span>”均读轻声）。</li><li>放纵，迷乱：～淫。～腆（沉湎于酒）。</li><li>远，边远的地方：～远。～遐。八～。</li><li>扩大：“天作高山大王～之”。</li><li>包有：“奄有龟蒙，遂～大东，至于海邦”。</li><li>工业上指没有经过精细加工的：～子（毛坯）。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>荒</strong> <span class="dicpy">huāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从丱，巟( <span class="dicpy">huāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huāng.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:荒芜)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[waste;lie waste]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荒，芜也，一曰草荒地也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我居圉卒荒。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·召》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">野荒民散则削之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司马》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">地广大荒而不治。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三径就荒。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">开荒南野际。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">极于荒残。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span
