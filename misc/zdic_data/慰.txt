						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>慰</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wèi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wèi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>使人心里安适：～问。～劳。～勉（安慰鼓励）。～唁（慰问死者家属）。～留。～藉。安～。劝～。抚～。</li><li>心安：～志（宽慰自己的心情）。宽～。欣～。</li></ol>

class="encs">[comfort]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慰，安也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有子七人，莫慰母心。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·凯风》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以慰长想。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慰留不允。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:慰眼(观赏，饱眼福);劝慰(劝解安慰);自慰(自己安慰自己);抚慰(安慰);慰存(安抚慰问);慰唁(安慰勉励);慰喭(对遭丧事者的慰问;安慰，慰勉);慰肃(慰问;整肃);慰纳(安抚招纳或接纳)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 居住 <span class="encs">[live]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慰，居也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 问 <span class="encs">[ask]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时时慰风俗，往往出东田。——</span><span data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 愤怒 <span class="encs">[angry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慰，一曰恚怒也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 段玉裁注:<span class="smcs">《小雅》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“以慰我心。”</span> <span class="diczx2">毛</span>曰:<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾心甚慰。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span
