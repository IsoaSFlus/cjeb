                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>啦</strong> <span class="dicpy">lā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;拉&rdquo;。闲扯，闲谈 <span class="encs">[chat]</span>。如:啦呱(闲聊);人人都愿意和他啦</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">la</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/la.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/啦啦队">啦啦队</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>啦</strong> <span class="dicpy">la</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/la.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈语气〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 是语气词&ldquo;了&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">le</span>)和&ldquo;啊&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">a</span>)的合音，兼有语气词&ldquo;了&rdquo;和&ldquo;啊&rdquo;的作用:</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用在句末表示喜悦、赞叹、惊奇、气愤。如:我们胜利地完成任务啦!这个比方妙极啦!这可使人感到奇怪啦!你瞧，他连优良作风都丢啦!</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用在句末，表示情况已经变化或将要出现新的情况。如:队长!我没找到老杨，他上工地去啦;他又要演出啦</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用在句末，表示肯定、解释、劝止。如:你都听明白啦;有错，改了就行啦;这就别提啦</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用在句末，表示疑问。如:你怎么啦?他往哪里走啦?</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用在列举的事项之后，表示停顿。如:口号声啦，锣鼓声啦，鞭炮声啦，响彻云霄;书啦，报纸啦，笔记本啦，钢笔啦，墨水啦，堆得满桌子都是</p>
