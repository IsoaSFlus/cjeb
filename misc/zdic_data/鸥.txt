                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鸥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鸥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鷗</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄡˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>鸟类的一科，羽毛多为白色，嘴扁平，前趾有蹼，翼长而尖。生活在湖海上，捕食鱼、螺等：海～。～盟（与鸥鸟为友，借指退隐）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> seagull, tern; Larus species (various)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Möwen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mouette,goéland,laridae</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鸥</strong></p>
<p>鷗 <span class="dicpy">ōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鸟，区( <span class="dicpy">ōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ōu.mp3"></a></span> )声。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;水鸮也。&rdquo;本义:水鸮)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 水鸟名 <span class="encs">[gull]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惊起一滩鸥鹭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《如梦令》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸥鹭灭没。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 鸥科(Laridae)。此类鸟善飞，能游水，常随潮而翔，有海鸥、银鸥、燕鸥等种类。为长翼蹼足水鸟，形体较大，身体较粗壮，喙较厚，喙端略呈弯钩状，通常呆在岸边或内陆水域附近，是港口重要的食腐动物 。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鸥汀(群鸥栖息的沙洲);鸥没(鸥鸟没入水中);鸥眠(鸥鸟息眠);鸥鹭忘机(指人无机心，鸥鸟也能和其相亲近);鸥水相依(比喻离不开赖以生存的环境);鸥闲(鸥鸟悠闲自在)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[动]</span>∶比喻隐居 <span class="encs">[withdraw from society and live in solitude]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鸥没(鸥鸟逍遥飞翔于水面。比喻隐居者生活的悠闲自在);鸥盟(与鸥为友。指隐居江湖);鸥社(同&ldquo;鸥盟&rdquo;);鸥梦(隐居的志趣);鸥情(退隐者的心情)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
