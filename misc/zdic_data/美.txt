                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>美</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">měi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/měi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄟˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>好，善：～德。～学。～谈。审～。～丽。～容（美化容貌）。～不胜收。</li>	<li>得意，高兴：～滋滋的。</li>	<li>称赞，以为好：赞～。～言。～誉。</li>	<li>指&ldquo;亚美利加洲&rdquo;（简称&ldquo;美洲&rdquo;）：北～。南～。</li>	<li>指&ldquo;美国&rdquo;：～元。～籍华人。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">美</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>美</strong> <span class="dicpy">měi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/měi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，从羊，从大，古人以羊为主要副食品，肥壮的羊吃起来味很美。本义:味美)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[delicious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美，甘也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后天地之美生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;五行》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓甘露醴泉之类也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食必珍美。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:美酒;美甘甘(形容非常甘美或甜蜜);美味(美好的滋味;可口的食品);美食(味美的食品;吃味美的食物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 形貌好看，漂亮 <span class="encs">[beautiful;good-looking;handsome;pretty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美</span><span class="diczx2">孟姜</span>也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鄘风&middot;桑中》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美</span><span class="diczx2">孟弋</span>矣。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我孰与城北</span><span class="diczx2">徐公</span>美?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梅以曲为美，直则无姿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:美姬(美丽的女子);美眷(美丽的女眷;美人);美色(姣美的姿色);美劭(俊美，秀美)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 美好 <span class="encs">[fair;good;nice;fine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋侯</span>之美也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;庄公十二年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我孰与城北徐公美?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王为建取妻于</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>而美。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其民愿，其俗美。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王霸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">芳草鲜美。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下之美。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子&middot;千里马篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">才美不外现。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>

      <div class="yczsl-title">初中古诗词</div>
      <div class="yczsl-description">
			<div class="gscdl">山川之<span>美</span>，古来共谈。</div><div class="gscdr">南北朝·陶弘景 《答谢中书书》</div><div class="gscdl">忽逢桃花林，夹岸数百步，中无杂树，芳草鲜<span>美</span>，落英缤纷，渔人甚异之，复前行，欲穷其林。</div><div class="gscdr">魏晋·陶渊明 《桃花源记》</div><div class="gscdl">土地平旷，屋舍俨然，有良田<span>美</span>池桑竹之属。</div><div class="gscdr">魏晋·陶渊明 《桃花源记》</div><div class="gscdl">是马也，虽有千里之能，食不饱，力不足，才<span>美</span>不外见，且欲与常马等不可得，安求其能千里也？策之不以其道，食之不能尽其材，鸣之而不能通其意，执策而临之，曰：“天下无马！”</div><div class="gscdr">唐·韩愈 《马说》</div><div class="gscdl">环滁皆山也。其西南诸峰，林壑尤<span>美</span>，望之蔚然而深秀者，琅琊也。</div><div class="gscdr">宋·欧阳修 《醉翁亭记》</div><div class="gscdl">万钟则不辩礼义而受之，万钟于我何加焉！为宫室之<span>美</span>，妻妾之奉，所识穷乏者得我与？乡为身死而不受，今为宫室之<span>美</span>为之；乡为身死而不受，今为妻妾之奉为之；乡为身死而不受，今为所识穷乏者得我而为之；是亦不可以已乎？此之谓失其本心。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孟子 《鱼我所欲也》</div>      </div>
