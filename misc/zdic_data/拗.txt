                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拗</strong></p><p>抝 <span class="dicpy">ǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，幼声。本义:折断)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[break]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">捣麝成尘香不灭，拗莲作寸丝难绝。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">温庭筠</span><span class="smcs">《达摩支曲》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十分小心的拗开了，焦皮里面窜出一道白气，白气散了，是两半个白面的馒头。——</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《药》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:拗折(折断)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 转折;弯转 <span class="encs">[turn;bend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">读到这里，他总是微笑起来，而且将头仰起，摇着，向后面拗过去，拗过去。——</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《朝花夕拾》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ào;niù</span></p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拗</strong> <span class="dicpy">ào</span> <span class="ptr"><a
