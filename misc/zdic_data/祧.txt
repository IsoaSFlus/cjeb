                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>祧</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从示，兆声。从<span class="yhcs">“示”</span>与祭祀有关。本义:祀远祖、始祖之庙) 同本义 <span class="encs">[ancestral temple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祧，祭也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祧，祭先祖也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">守祧八人，掌先王先公之庙祧。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">文武为祧，四时祭之而已。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远庙为祧。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 如:祧迁(把隔了几代的祖宗的神主迁入远祖之庙);祧庙(远祖庙)。泛指祖庙。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:祧祊(祭祀祖先的祠堂)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>祧</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 迁庙。帝王对世数远隔之祖，依制将其神主迁入远祖之庙，故迁移神主也称祧 <span class="encs">[move]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">已祧之主，不得复入太庙。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 超越 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非先有词而后有</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>人之诗，亦不能祧诗而言词。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">江顺诒</span><span class="smcs">《词学集成》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 承继为后嗣 <span class="encs">[be or become heir to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">守器承祧。——</span><span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《立太子诏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:祧绪(世代相承的统绪);祧续(祧绪。指子孙传宗接代)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指继承 <span class="encs">[inherit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宿草敢祧</span><span class="diczx2">刘礼部</span>，东南绝学在 <span class="diczx2">昆陵</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《己亥杂诗三百十五首》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>祧</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄊㄧㄠ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>tiāo <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1461.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">祖庙。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左传·襄公二十三年》：「纥不佞，失守宗<b>祧</b>，敢告不吊。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代远祖神位所迁往的宗庙称为「<b><b>祧</b></b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《周礼·春官·守桃》：「守桃掌守先王先公之庙<b>祧</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">汉·郑玄·注：「迁主所藏曰<b>祧</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">继承上代的人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「承<b>祧</b>」、「守<b>祧</b>」。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">迁庙。</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐书·卷一三·礼乐志》：「已<b>祧</b>之主，不得复入太庙。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《宋史·卷一二三·礼志二十六》：「僖祖及文懿皇后神主既<b>祧</b>，准礼不讳，忌日亦请依唐睿宗<b>祧</b>迁故事废之。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">承继。</span><span class="gc_yy">《聊斋志异·卷二·侠女》：「且身已向暮，旦夕犯雾露，深以<b>祧</b>续为忧耳。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《老残游记二编·第六回》：「不怕等二老归天后再还宗，或是兼<b>祧</b>两姓俱可。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
