                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>涧</strong></p><p>澗 <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，间声。本义:夹在两山间的水沟)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gully;ravine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涧，山夹水也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于涧之中。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·采蘩》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时鸣春涧中。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《鸟鸣涧》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">陕西</span>大涧。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">溪涧。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:涧谷(溪涧山谷);涧籁(山涧的水声)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 涧谷，山谷 <span class="encs">[valley]</span>。如:涧水(山谷中的溪水);涧流(山谷中的水流);涧井(山谷，山凹);涧栖(居住在山谷中)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代数词。万万沟为涧，即10 64 <span class="encs">[美ten vigintillion;英ten thousand decillion]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Jian River]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一曰</span><span class="diczx2">涧水</span>，出宏农西安东南入 <span class="diczx2">洛</span>。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 源出河南渑池县东北，东南流会渑水，东流经新安县南，到洛阳市西折，东南入洛河</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
