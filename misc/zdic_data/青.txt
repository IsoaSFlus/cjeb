                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">青</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>青</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>深绿色或浅蓝色：～绿。～碧。～草。～苔。～苗。～菜。～葱。～山绿水。～云直上。万古长～（喻高尚的精神或深厚的友情永远不衰）。</li><li>绿色的东西：踏～。～黄不接。</li><li>靛蓝色：靛～。～紫。～出于蓝，胜于蓝。</li><li>黑色：～布。～线。～衫。～衣。</li><li>喻年轻：～年。～春。～工。</li><li>竹简：～简。～史（原指写在竹简上的记事，后指史书，如“永垂～～”）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> blue, green, black; young</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> grün, blau, türkisfarben  (V)​,Qing  (Eig, Fam)​,Radikal Nr. 174 = blaugrün, blau, grün, grünes Gras, junge Saat, jung</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bleu ou vert,jeune,herbe verte</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">青</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>青</strong> <span class="dicpy">qīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从生，从丹。金文字形上面是个<span class="yhcs">“生”</span>字;下面是<span class="yhcs">“丹”</span>字，丹是井字之变。<span class="yhcs">“青”</span>是汉字部首之一。本义:蓝色)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[blue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青，东方色也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正东曰青州。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·职方氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时则有青眚青祥。——<span class="smcs">《洪范五行传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“木色也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其色青。——<span class="smcs">《素问·风论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“肝色。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌凡金玉锡石丹青之戒令。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·职金》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“青，空青也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青取之于蓝，而青于蓝。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:青磷(磷火。俗称鬼火);青靛(深蓝色);青焰焰(青蓝色);青云万里(比喻前程远大);青云干吕(庆云翔集，应乎六吕。是一种吉祥的征兆);青冥(青天);青霄(青空，碧空)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 深绿色 <span class="encs">[green]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两岸青山相对出。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《望天门山诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">草色入帘青。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青箬笠。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">张志和</span><span class="smcs">《渔歌子》</span>词</p><p><span class="diczx1">青树翠蔓。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p><p><span
