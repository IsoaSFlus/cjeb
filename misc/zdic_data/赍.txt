                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赍</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赍</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">賫</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p><ol><li>怀抱着，带着：～恨。～持（拿着）。～志而没（<span class="dicpy">mò</span>）（志未遂而死去）。</li><li>把东西送给别人：～发（资助）。～赏。～盗粮（把粮食送给盗贼，喻帮助敌人做坏事）。</li><li>旅行的人携带衣食等物：“行者～，居者送”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赍</strong></p><p>賫、繼 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 拿东西给人，送给 <span class="encs">[give]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">齌，持遗也。从貝，齊声。俗字作賫。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共其财用之币齌。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·外府》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设道齌之奠。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小祝》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹送也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受其将币之齌。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小宗伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又齌皮、马。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王何不以地齌</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>最。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·西周策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赍钱三百万。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:赍助(接济;资助);赍赏(赏赐);赍钱(持赠金钱);赍盗粮(赠送粮食给盗寇)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 携带;持 <span class="encs">[bring; hold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行者齎。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓将衣食之具以自随也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">齎盗粮。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·大略》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赍黄金千斤。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赍叶入市。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:赍酒(携酒，带酒);赍带(携带);赍排(置办安排);赍刺(持名片往谒)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 怀抱着 <span class="encs">[cherish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赍志没地，长怀无己。——</span><span class="diczx2">江淹</span><span class="smcs">《恨赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:赍志(心怀大志);赍志而殁(心怀未遂的志愿死去);赍子(男性生殖器)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赍发">赍发</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赍恨">赍恨</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赍赏">赍赏</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9F4E_8178.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(齎)</span><span class="ywyy">持遺也。</span><span class="swdz">周禮掌皮。歲終則會其財齎。注。予人以物曰齎。今時詔書或曰齎計吏。鄭司農云。齎或爲資。外府。共其財用之幣齎。注。齎、行道之財用也。聘禮曰。問幾月之齎。鄭司農云。齎或爲資。今禮家定齎作資。玄謂齎資同耳。其字以齊次爲聲。從貝變易。古字亦多或。玉裁按。此鄭君不用許書說。謂齎資一字。聲義皆同也。許則釋資爲貨。釋齎爲持而予之。其義分別。不爲一字。近人則訓齎爲持矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从貝。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AFC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠫼</span>聲。</span><span class="swdz">祖雞切。十五部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
