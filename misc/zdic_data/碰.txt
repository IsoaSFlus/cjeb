<p><span class="dicpy">p&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄥˋ</span></p>

	<li>撞击：～击。～撞。～壁（喻事情办不成）。～～车。～钉子。</li>

	<li>试探：～运气。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>碰</strong></p>
<p>踫、掽 <span class="dicpy">p&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石，并声。本义:两物相触或相撞)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[collide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掽，蒲孟切，彭去声，搕掽，撞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;今俗谓卒然相遇曰掽。&rdquo;按，掽是晚起字，今字作&ldquo;碰&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:小车碰到电线杆;鸡蛋碰石头;碰头(磕响头，表示惶恐服罪);碰关(方言。充其量;到了顶点);碰磕(相撞，冲撞)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用手、脚或器械触及 <span class="encs">[bump;touch]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:碰手(方言。触手、触疼手);碰触(接触);蛇你如果碰它，它就要咬你</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 凭机遇撞上 <span class="encs">[take a chance]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:碰着法(方言。碰巧;偶然);碰和(碰巧，偶然;打麻将牌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 偶然相遇 <span class="encs">[run into]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:碰着(遇见);在街上碰到一个熟人</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 触犯 <span class="encs">[offend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俺们这个</span><span class="diczx2">玉大人</span>真是了不得，赛过活阎王，碰着了就是个死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></p>
