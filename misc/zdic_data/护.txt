                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">护</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>护</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">護</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>使不受侵犯和损害：保～。～卫。～理。～士。～航。～林。辩～。守～。</li>
	<li>救助：～护。</li>
	<li>掩蔽，包庇：～短。庇～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> protect, guard, defend, shelter</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> beschützen, schützen, hüten, bewachen, sichern  (V)​,jdn, in Schutz nehmen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> protéger,garder,défendre,soigner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">护</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>护</strong></p>
<p>護 <span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，蒦( <span class="dicpy">hu&oacute;</span>)声。本义:保卫;保护)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[defend;guard;safeguard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吏护还之乡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾欲护汝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从我杀贼护家室。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贯铁絙护之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:护队(行军所设防卫的军队);护藏(保护经藏);护梁(城楼上作护卫用的横梁);护镜(护心镜。衣甲上用以防护前心和后心的铜镜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 爱护 <span class="encs">[take good care of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">备极护爱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:护书(收放文书用的长方形木匣子);护头(小孩子不肯剃头发);护槽(马吃草时，人难接近)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 袒护;包庇 <span class="encs">[be partial to;shield from censure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谨护其失。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:护庇(袒护;包庇);护相容隐(庇护容忍);护失(袒护自己的过错)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 监视;监督 <span class="encs">[supervise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">护，救视也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有白马将出护其兵。</span><span class="diczx2">李广</span>上马车十余骑奔射杀胡白马将。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 救助 <span class="encs">[help sb. in danger or difficulty;succor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">高祖</span>为布衣时， <span class="diczx2">何</span>数以吏事护 <span class="diczx2">高祖</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;萧相国世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 总领 <span class="encs">[command]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">乐毅</span>于是并护 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>、 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>、 <span class="diczx2">韩</span>、 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>、 <span class="diczx2">燕</span>之兵以伐 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>，破之 <span class="diczx2">济</span>西。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;乐毅传》</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“护”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/護">護</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              護·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=21" target="_blank">21</a>　·部外筆画：14</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8B77.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>胡故切，音瓠。【說文】救視也。【廣韻】助也。【增韻】擁全之也。【書&middot;畢命序孔傳】成定東周郊境，使有保護。【史記&middot;蕭相國世家】高祖爲布衣時，何數以吏事護高祖。【前漢&middot;西域傳】凡遣使送客者，欲爲防護<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨥</span>害也。【晉書&middot;紀瞻傳】帝使謂瞻曰：卿雖病，但爲朕臥護六軍，所益多矣。</p>
<p>又官名。【前漢&middot;百官公卿表】護軍都尉，秦官。</p>
<p>又【西域傳】武帝時，西域內屬者三十六國，置使者校尉領護之，宣帝攺爲都護。【唐書&middot;百官志註】龍朔閒攺左右庶子爲左右中護。</p>
<p>又樂名。【呂覽&middot;古樂篇】湯乃命伊尹作《大護》。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大司樂賈疏】作大濩。【字彙補】與頀同。</p>
<p>又曲名。【唐書&middot;禮樂志】《丁都護》，晉宋閒曲也。</p>
<p>又草名。【本草綱目】景天一名護火。</p>
<p>又薺一名護生草。</p>
<p>又【名醫別錄】神護草。李時珍曰：物類志謂之護門草，王筠詩，風驚護門草，卽此也。</p>
<p>又地名。【爾雅&middot;釋地】周有焦護。【郭註】今扶風池陽縣瓠中是也。</p>
<p>又諸羌州名。【唐書&middot;地理志】隴右道有護卬、久護、護川三州，俱開元十七年置。</p>
<p>又叶黃郭切，音穫。【庾闡&middot;弔賈誼文】雖有惠音，莫過《韶護》。雖有騰蛇，終仆一壑。</p>
