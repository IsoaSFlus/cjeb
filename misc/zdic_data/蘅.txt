                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>蘅</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄥˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>〔～芜〕古书上说的一种香草。</li>
	<li>〔杜～〕多年生草本植物，野生在山地，开紫色小花。根茎可入药。亦作&ldquo;杜衡&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蘅</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>杜衡( Asarum forbesii )的简称。又名苦叶细辛，南细辛。古称香草 <span class="encs">[forbes wild ginger]</span>。马兜铃科。多年生草本。花被筒钟状，暗紫色。全草入药，并可提取芳香油</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蘅，杜蘅，香草。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8605.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】戸庚切，音行。【玉篇】香草也。【爾雅&middot;釋草】杜，土鹵。【註】杜衡也，似葵而香。【唐本草註】杜衡，葉似葵，形如馬蹄，故俗云馬蹄香。【屈原&middot;離騷】雜杜蘅與芳芷。俱作衡。</p>
<p>又微蘅，草名。【述異記】魏興錫山多生微蘅草。</p>
<p>又【唐韻古音】音杭。【屈原&middot;九歌】白玉兮爲瑱，疏石兮爲芳。芷葺兮荷蓋，繚之兮杜蘅。【宋玉&middot;風賦】獵蕙草，離秦蘅，被辛夷，槩稊楊。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衡</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“蘅”字头，请参考“衡”字：)</span>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四">卷四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四&amp;swbs=角">角</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8861.svg">                          <p>牛觸，橫大木其角。从角从大，行聲。《詩》曰：&ldquo;設其楅衡。&rdquo;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2164F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡙏</span>，古文衡如此。戶庚切〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2163B.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡘻</span>，亦古文衡。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8861_6004.svg" /><span class="swbf">(衡)</span><span class="ywyy">牛觸，橫大木。</span><span class="swdz">各本大木下有&ldquo;其角&rdquo;二字，今依韵會所據鍇本。按許於告字下曰：牛觸，角箸橫木所以告也。是設於角者謂之告。此云牛觸橫大木，是闌閑之謂之衡。衡與告異義，大木<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣃔</span>不可施於角，此易明者。魯頌傳曰：楅衡，設牛角以楅之也。箋云：楅衡其牛角，爲其觸觝人也。許說與毛、鄭不同。毛、鄭謂設於角，許不云設於角也。木部云：楅以木有所畐束也。亦不言角。云大木者，字從大也。古多假衡爲橫。玉人注曰：衡古文橫，假借字也。〇鄭注周禮云：楅設於角，衡設於鼻，如椵狀。楅衡爲二。許於衡不言楅，於楅不言衡，葢亦二之。</span><span class="ywyy">从角大，行聲。</span><span class="swdz">戸庚切。古音在十部。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：設其楅衡。</span><span class="swdz">詩曰當作周禮曰。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8861_6005.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A257.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪉗</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文衡如此。</span></p>
