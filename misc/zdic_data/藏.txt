                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>藏</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声，臧声。本义:把谷物保藏起来)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 储积，收藏 <span class="encs">[store]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">农夫春耕夏耘，秋敛冬藏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;三辩》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子不闻藏书者乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:矿藏;收藏(收集保藏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 隐匿 <span class="encs">[hide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">藏，匿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慢藏海盗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即藏其尸，持童抵主人所。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见瓶水之冰，而知天下之寒，鱼鳖之藏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
