                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>述</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>讲话，陈说，叙～。～评。描～。论～。综～。～职。～而不作。</li><li>修纂：著～。</li><li>循，顺行：“报我不～”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">述</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>述</strong> <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，术声。本义:遵循)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">述，循也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">报我不述。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·日月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂述命曰。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·少牢礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">述</span><span class="diczx2">大禹</span>之戒以作歌。——<span class="smcs">《书·五子之歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:述遵(遵循);述旧(遵行旧规);述祖(遵循祖训)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 陈述，记叙 <span class="encs">[state;narrate;relate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">识礼乐之文者能述。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小有述职。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸侯朝于天子曰述职。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后常流涕述其事以语人，曰:<span class="yhcs">“吾师肺肝，皆铁石所铸造也。”</span>——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">成</span>述其异，宰不信。试与他虫斗，虫尽靡。又试之鸡，果如 <span class="diczx2">成</span>言。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:综述(综合叙述);口述;重述一遍;述知(陈述;告诉);述事(陈述事理);述载(陈述记载);述圣(陈述圣人之道);述历(一一陈述);述而不作(传述先哲的理论，不再自创新意)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 传述;传承;阐述前人成说 <span class="encs">[continue from the former generations to those succeeding us]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">述而不作，信而好古。——<span class="smcs">《论语·述而》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:述宣(继承和发扬);述修(修治;修明);述附(承袭)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>述</strong> <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 泛指叙述的文字 <span class="encs">[works]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醉能同其乐，醒能述以文者，太守也。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此则</span><span class="diczx2">岳阳楼</span>之大观也，前人之述备矣。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">德琏常斐然有述作之意，其才学足以著书。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">曹丕</span><span class="smcs">《与吴质书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:述作(著作);述撰(撰述;著作);述制(记述;著作);述造(著作)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/述评">述评</a></span><span class="crefe"><a                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8FF0.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>食律切，音術。【說文】循也。【論語】述而不作。【中庸】父作之，子述之。</p><p>又【廣韻】著述也。【正韻】修也，纘也，譔也。凡終人之事，纂人之言，皆曰述。【禮·樂記】識禮樂之文者能述。【又】述者之謂明。【儀禮·士喪禮】筮人許諾述命。【註】旣受命而申言之曰述。</p><p>又<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>也。【孟子】諸侯朝於天子曰述職。</p><p>又冠名。【後漢·輿服志】通天冠前有山展筩，爲述。記曰：知天者冠述。</p><p>又通作術。【禮·祭義】結諸心，形諸色，而術省之。【註】術，當爲述。</p><p>又姓。【風俗通】魯大夫仲述之後。</p><p>又叶樹僞切，音瑞。【曹植·酒賦】安沈湎而爲娛，非往聖之所述。闢酒誥之明戒，同元凶之三季。</p>
