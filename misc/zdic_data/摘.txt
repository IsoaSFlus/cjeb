                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>摘</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形，从手，啻( <span class="dicpy">chì</span>)声。本义:用手采下或取下)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pick;pluck;take off ]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摘，摭果树实也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摘，取也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一摘使瓜好，再摘令瓜稀。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·承天皇帝传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以摘之。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摘而藏之。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄·种植部》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:摘胆剜心(形容杀人者的凶残);摘肩儿(摘下肩上的担子);摘青(谓果实等未成熟即采摘);摘瓜抱蔓(喻一扫而空);摘桃子(比喻坐享其成);摘棉花;摘苹果</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 除去，去掉 <span class="encs">[remove]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">过了三天再不去，叫人来摘门下瓦。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:把灯泡摘下来;摘帽(脱下帽子);摘头(除去头上首饰);摘不开(摆脱不了)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 摘录 <span class="encs">[take passages]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寻章摘句老雕虫，晓月当帘挂玉弓。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《南国》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:摘句(摘录文章诗歌之句);摘钞(择要抄录);摘句寻章(摘取搜寻文章的片断词句。指读书局限于文字的推求)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 调拨 <span class="encs">[allocate]</span>。如:摘那(调拨);摘拨(调派)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 〈方〉∶借 <span
