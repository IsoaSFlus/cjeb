                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>表</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">➎錶</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">biǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄠˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>外部，外面，外貌：～面。外～。仪～。～象。～层。～皮。</li>	<li>显示：～示。～态。～征。～达。～露。～演。～情。略～心意。</li>	<li>中医指用药物把感受的风寒发散出来：～汗。</li>	<li>分类分项记录事物的文件：～册。～格。～报。调查～。</li>	<li>计时间的器具，通常比钟小，可以带在身边：钟～。手～。怀～。</li>	<li>计量某种量的器具：电～。</li>	<li>标志，榜样：～率（<span class="dicpy">shu&agrave;i </span>）。为（<span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i </span>）人师～。</li>	<li>称呼父亲或祖父的姊妹、母亲或祖母的兄弟姊妹生的子女，用来表示亲属关系：～亲。～兄弟。</li>	<li>测量的标尺：～尺。圭～（古代测日影的器具）。</li>	<li>封建时代称臣子给君主的奏章：～章。诸葛亮《出师～》。</li>	<li>树梢：林～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">表</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>表</strong> <span class="dicpy">biǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从毛，从衣，&ldquo;毛&rdquo;又兼作声符。小篆字形，衣字中间加个毛字。古人穿皮衣，毛朝外面，所以&ldquo;表&rdquo;从&ldquo;毛&rdquo;。本义:外衣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[coat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">表，上衣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:&ldquo;古者衣裘，以毛为里。按，古衣裘皆外毛，礼服必加裼衣其上。许所谓上衣也，故曰以毛为田，言有表，则毛在里也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">表裘不入公门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必表而出之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:&ldquo;谓加上衣也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中绀而表素。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;让王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表裘(外衣);表礼(作为礼物的衣料);表里(衣服的面子与里子;里外;也指作为礼物的衣料)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 外面(与&ldquo;里&rdquo;相对) <span class="encs">[outside;surface]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">表里山河。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表海(临海，滨海);表薄(泛指出身贫贱。表:野外;薄:山林);表里相应(指内外互相应和);表壮不如里壮(丈夫有才能，还不如妻子善能持家，可为内助。表指丈夫，里指妻子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 外表，外貌 <span class="encs">[appearance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虚有其表耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明皇实录》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表相(外貌);表征</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;标&rdquo;。表率，榜样 <span class="encs">[example]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仁者，天下之表也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">廉者，民之表也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">包拯</span><span class="smcs">《乞不用赃吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表正(证据;表率;榜样);表俗(作为世人的表率)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;幖&rdquo;。表帜，标志 <span class="encs">[mark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祭之日表。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;肆师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君法明，论有常，表仪既设民知方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;成相》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表旗(边境上的标帜);表识(标记，标帜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 给皇帝上的奏章 <span class="encs">[memorial to an emperor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今当远离，临表涕零，不知所言。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出师一表真名世，千载谁堪伯仲间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《书愤》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表函(上呈天子的函件);表奏(表文奏章);表草(表文的草稿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 表格 <span class="encs">[list;form;table]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为十表。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:度量衡表;对数表;乘法表;编制表;一览表;价目表</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 作标记的木柱 <span class="encs">[marking pillar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">吴起</span>治 <span class="diczx2">西河</span>，欲谕其信于民，夜日置表于南门之外。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎小》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">城上千步一表。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;备城门》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 表亲 <span class="encs">[the relationship between the children or grandchildren of a brother and a sister or of sisters]</span>。一种亲戚关系，有姑表和姨表之分，都叫表，姑母的子女互为姑表，姨母的子女互为姨表。如:表兄弟，表兄妹，表姊弟，表姊妹(姑母、舅父、姨母的子女);表叔，表伯，表舅(父母的表兄妹);表侄，表甥(表兄弟姐妹的孩子);表婶(表叔之妻)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 边界线 <span class="encs">[boundary]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:表旗(边界线上的旗帜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 直立于地面，用以测日影的标杆 <span class="encs">[ancient Chinese sundial]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏至日中立八尺之表，其景尺有五寸，谓之地中。(景:影。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">阮元</span><span class="smcs">《畴人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 石碑 <span class="encs">[stele;stone tablet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千里立表，万里连纪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 测量仪器 <span class="encs">[gage;meter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:温度表;水表;电表;表座(日影表的底座)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 树梢 <span class="encs">[tip of a tree]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:林表</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>表</strong> <span class="dicpy">biǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表彰，显扬 <span class="encs">[cite;commend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刻石表功兮炜煌煌。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韦应物</span><span class="smcs">《石鼓歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旌表(立石碑、匾额以颂扬功德);表章(显扬;宣扬;古代臣子上君王的奏章);表显(显扬);表式(表彰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 设立标记;标出，标明 <span class="encs">[mark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表木(立木为标志);表目(标名，命名);表揭(标志)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表白 <span class="encs">[indicate;express]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或援誓以表心。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘知几</span><span class="smcs">《史通》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表异(表明特异之处);表微(表明微细之事);表心意;按下不表;表抒(表达，抒发)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 启奏，上表章给皇帝 <span class="encs">[present memorial to a emperor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">亮</span>自表后主。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">礼官辄表贺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;海瑞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">曹公</span>表 <span class="diczx2">权</span>为骠骑将军。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴书&middot;吴主传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:表救(上表援救);表闻(上表申闻于上);表荐(上表推荐);表谢(上表谢恩);表劝(上表劝即帝位)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表现，显示 <span class="encs">[show]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:表德(佛教用语。直接显示真理叫表德。与遮情相对);表候(表现在外的征象)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 记载 <span class="encs">[record]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:表勒(刻石记载);表载(记载)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 用药物等把感受的风寒发散出来。如:表寒气;表汗</p>
<p>◎ <strong>表</strong></p>
<p>錶 <span class="dicpy">biǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>一种轻便的可以戴在手腕上或放在口袋内的计时器 <span class="encs">[watch]</span>。又如:手表;怀表</p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>表</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄅㄧㄠˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>biǎo <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0225.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">外衣。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;衣部》：「<b>表</b>，上衣也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;段玉裁&middot;注：「上衣者，衣之在外者也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;袁宏道〈雨後遊六橋記〉：「諸友白其內者皆去<b>表</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">標識。</span><span class="gc_yy">《管子&middot;君臣上》：「民有疑惑貳豫之心，而上不能匡，則百姓之與間，猶揭<b>表</b>而令之止也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;尹知章&middot;注：「<b>表</b>，謂以本為標，有所告示也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">事物外在的一面。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「外<b>表</b>」、「江<b>表</b>」、「虛有其<b>表</b>」、「出人意<b>表</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;僖公五年》：「虢，虞之<b>表</b>也。虢亡虞必從之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;潘岳&middot;西征賦》：「或從容傅會，望<b>表</b>知裡。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">模範、榜樣。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「表率」、「為人師<b>表</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;班固&middot;幽通賦》：「保身遺名，民之<b>表</b>兮。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代臣子呈給帝王的一種奏章。如三國蜀諸葛亮的〈出師表〉、李密的〈陳情表〉。</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;徐師曾〈文體明辨序說&middot;<b>表</b>〉：「古者獻言於君，皆稱上書。漢定禮儀，乃有四品，其三曰<b>表</b>，然但用以陳請而已。後世因之，其用寖廣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">石碑。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷七五&middot;李尋傳》：「千里立<b>表</b>，萬里連紀。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">墓表、墓誌。如宋代歐陽修的〈瀧岡阡表〉。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">分格或分項以列記事物的文件。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「報<b>表</b>」、「課<b>表</b>」、「統計<b>表</b>」、「調查<b>表</b>」、「史記十二諸侯年<b>表</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人除正名以外的其他字號。</span><span class="gc_yy">《醒世恆言&middot;卷七&middot;錢秀才錯占鳳凰儔》：「（高贊）​接口就問說：『此位就是令親顏大官人？前日不曾問得貴<b>表</b>。』錢青道：『年幼無<b>表</b>。』」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">計時器或計量器。如：「手表」、「電表」。《紅樓夢&middot;第一九回》：「寶玉命取表來看時，果然針已指亥正。」也作「錶」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表親。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「姑<b>表</b>」、「姨<b>表</b>」、「老<b>表</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">泛指行為不檢點的婦女，也多專指妓女。</span><span class="gc_yx">通「婊」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如明代有表貢。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">加上外衣。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;鄉黨》：「當暑，袗絺綌，必<b>表</b>而出之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國魏&middot;何晏&middot;箋：「必<b>表</b>而出之，加上衣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">顯揚、獎勵。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷五五&middot;留侯世家》：「今陛下能封聖人之墓、<b>表</b>賢者之閭、式智者之門乎？」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;司馬遷&middot;報任少卿書》：「恨私心有所不盡，鄙陋沒世而文彩不<b>表</b>於後世也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8868.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2761D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧘝</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/277A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧞧</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2772B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧜫</span>【唐韻】陂矯切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】彼小切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䔸。【說文】表，上衣也。【玉篇】衣外也。</p><p>又外也。【書&middot;立政】方行天下，至于海表，罔有不服。</p><p>又表異。【書&middot;畢命】表厥宅里。【傳】表異其宅里。</p><p>又標也。【晉語】置茅蕝，設望表。【註】謂立木以爲表，表其位也。</p><p>又箋表。【釋名】下言於上曰表。【蔡邕&middot;獨斷】表者不需頭，上言臣某，下言臣某，誠惶誠恐，頓首。</p><p>又【韻會】杪也，末也。</p><p>又表表，偉也。</p><p>又地名。【晉書&middot;地理志】表氏縣，屬酒泉郡。</p><p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】表氏，見《姓苑》。【正字通】明有表貢。</p><p>又【集韻】俾小切，音褾。義同。</p><p>又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>遙切，音飇。識也。</p><p>又【韻補】博舉切。【前漢&middot;班固敘傳】春秋之占，咎徵是舉。告往知來，王事之表。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2761D.svg">                          <p>上衣也。从衣从毛。古者衣<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2768D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧚍</span>，以毛爲表。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2761D_2.svg" /><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/277A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧞧</span>，古文表从麃。陂矯切〖注〗<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2772B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧜫</span>，古文表。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2761D_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2761D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧘝</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">上衣也。</span><span class="swdz">上衣者，衣之在外者也。論語：當暑袗絺綌，必表而出之。孔曰：加上衣也。皇云：若在家則裘葛之上亦無别加衣，若出行接賓客皆加上衣。當暑絺綌可單，若出不可單，則必加上衣也。嫌暑熱不加，故特明之。玉藻：表裘不入公門。鄭曰：表裘，外裘也。外裘，今本作外衣，誤。襌絺綌，外裘二者形且褻，皆當表之乃出。引伸爲凡外箸之稱。</span><span class="ywyy">从衣毛。</span> <span class="swdz">會意。毛亦聲也。陂矯切。二部。</span><span class="ywyy">古者衣裘，故㠯毛爲表。</span><span class="swdz">說从衣毛之意也。古者衣裘謂未有麻絲，衣羽皮也。衣皮時毛在外，故裘之制毛在外。以衣毛製爲表字，示不忘古。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2761D_2.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/277A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧞧</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文表，从麃。</span><span class="swdz">麃聲。〖注〗<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2772B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧜫</span>，古文表。</span></p>                          </div>
