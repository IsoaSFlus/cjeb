<p>● <strong>覆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>遮盖，蒙：～盖。～溺。～蔽。～被（遮盖，喻恩荫）。～庇。</li>
	<li>翻，倾倒，败，灭：～舟。颠～。～灭。～辙（翻过车的道路，喻曾经失败的做法）。～没（<span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span>）。～水难收。～巢无完卵（喻灭门之祸，无一得免，亦喻整体覆灭，个人不能幸存）。前车之～，后车之鉴。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">复</span>&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">覆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>覆</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。覆盖，復声。本义:翻转，倾覆)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[c apsize;overturn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见若覆夏屋者矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓茨瓦也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载舟覆舟，所宜深慎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">覆舟斟寻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;天问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">覆杯水于坳堂之上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽天地覆坠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;德充符》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前车覆，后车诫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:覆盆(翻扣的盆子。比喻蒙冤不能辩白);覆手(把手反转过来。比喻事情容易办到);覆杯(倒置的杯子;倾杯畅饮);覆水(已倒出的水。喻事已成定局);覆篑(倒一筐土);覆醢(倒去肉酱);覆酱烧薪(极言著作无价值或不受重视);覆瓶(喻著作毫无价值，不被重视)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 覆盖;遮蔽 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庑下一生伏案卧，文方成草。公阅毕，即解貂覆生，为掩户。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以衾拥覆，久而乃和。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:被覆(遮盖;蒙);覆荐(覆盖铺垫);覆罩(覆盖笼罩);覆裹(覆盖包裹);覆墓(把土覆在墓上。也指扫墓);覆帱(遮盖)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 颠覆;灭亡 <span class="encs">[destroy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">括</span>既行，代 <span class="diczx2">廉颇</span>三十余日， <span class="diczx2">赵</span>兵果败， <span class="diczx2">括</span>死军覆。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:覆邦(亡国);覆亡(灭亡);覆陈(覆军败阵，败仗);覆军(覆灭全军);覆族(灭族);覆国(亡国);覆师(覆灭全军)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 保护;庇护 <span class="encs">[protect;shelter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诞置之寒冰，鸟覆翼之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;生民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:覆庇(关怀;照顾;袒护;包庇);覆庇(庇护;保佑);覆荫(庇护);覆帱(犹覆被。谓施恩，加惠);覆露(荫庇)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 伏击;袭击 <span class="encs">[ambush;assault]</span>。如:覆荡(谓扫荡平定);覆陷(攻占);覆拔(攻占)</p>
