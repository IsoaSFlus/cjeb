                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>更</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>改变，改换：～正。～生（重新获得生命，喻复兴）。～衣（ａ．换衣服；ｂ．婉辞，上厕所）。～定（改订）。～迭（轮流更换）。～递。～番。～新（旧的除去，新的建起）。～张（调节琴弦，喻变更或改革）。</li><li>经历：少（<span class="dicpy">shào </span>）不～事（年龄小，没有经历过多少事情）。</li><li>旧时夜间计时单位，一夜分为五更：～时。～阑。～夫。～鼓。～漏（原指计时用的漏壶，后泛指时辰）。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>更</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gèng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gèng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄥˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 愈加，再：～加。～好。～上一层楼。</p></ol>

</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> changer,modifier,plus,davantage,encore plus,bien que,même si,de nouveau,encore,cependant,comment se pourrait-il que...</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">更</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>更</strong> <span class="dicpy">gēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，<span class="yhcs">“更”</span>的小篆形是个形声字。丙声。 本义:改变)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">更，改也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">更也，人皆仰之。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子张》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">更爵洗。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">更皮币。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">更姓改物。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">应国之称号亦更矣。——<span class="smcs">《管子·侈靡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国更立法以典民则祥。——<span class="smcs">《管子·任法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">更葬也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·晋世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">景公</span>欲更 <span class="diczx2">晏子</span>之宅。——<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋·内篇杂下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将军若能翻然改图，易迹更步，古人不难追，鄙士何足宰哉!——<span class="smcs">《三国志·吕凯传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">曲沃</span><span class="diczx2">武公</span>已即位三十七年矣，更号曰 <span class="diczx2">晋武公</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·晋世家》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:更故(更改;变改);更号(改变名位与称号);更步(改变行止);更元(更改年号);更移(变迁;改变);更徙(变迁);更法(变法);更革(变革;改革)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 改，改正 <span class="encs">[make correction]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此则寡人之罪也，寡人请更。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既云常赦，不免皆赦除之，此非直赦其有罪，亦是与天下断，当许其更新(改过自新)。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·孙伏伽传》</span></span></p><p><span
