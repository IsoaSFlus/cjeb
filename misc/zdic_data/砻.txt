                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">砻</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>砻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">礱</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>去掉稻壳的农具，形状略像磨，多以木料制成。</li>
	<li>用砻去掉稻壳：～谷舂米。</li>
	<li>磨（<span class="dicpy">m&oacute;</span>）：&ldquo;造兹宝刀，既～既砺&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> grind, sharpen; mill</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schleifen, abschleifen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> frotter,meule</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">砻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>砻</strong></p>
<p>礱 <span class="dicpy">l&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 磨 <span class="encs">[grind]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">磨砻底厉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;枚乘传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钝金必将待砻厉然后利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;性恶》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">造兹宝刀，既砻既砺。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《宝刀铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:砻刻(磨光雕琢);砻淬(磨炼刀刃，比喻刻苦锻炼);砻琢(磨炼);砻斫(磨和砍削);砻错(磨治;磨炼;切磋研讨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用砻脱出稻谷的壳 <span class="encs">[hull (rice)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡既砻，则风扇以去糠秕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《天工开物》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:砻坊;砻糠</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>砻</strong></p>
<p>礱 <span class="dicpy">l&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 磨石 <span class="encs">[wet stone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡稻去壳用砻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋应星</span><span class="smcs">《天工开物》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:砻砺(磨石)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 脱出稻壳的农具 <span class="encs">[rice huller]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:砻磨(碾稻去壳的农具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;垄&rdquo;。坟墓 <span class="encs">[grave]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:砻石(墓碑);砻冢(坟墓)</span></p>
