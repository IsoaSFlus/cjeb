                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">该</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>该</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">該</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāi.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄞˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>应当：应～。～当。</li>
	<li>那，着重指出前面说过的人或事物：～地。～书。</li>
	<li>欠，欠账：～账。～他钱。</li>
	<li>表示肯定或推测：～你值班。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/赅">赅</a>&rdquo;，完备。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> should, ought to, need to</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> der bewusste, der betreffende; derartig, besagt, erwähnt ,müssen. sollen, sich gehören, sich geziemen; dürfen; würde, wäre  (V)​,schulden, schuldig bleiben  (V)​, an der Reihe sein  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> devoir,falloir,mériter,ce,ledit,(adverbe employé pour marquer l'emphase)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">该</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>该</strong></p>
<p>該 <span class="dicpy">gāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，亥声。本义:军中互相戒守的约言。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;该，军中约也。&rdquo;引申为&ldquo;完备&rdquo;。引申义:完备，包括一切。通&ldquo;赅&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同引申义 <span class="encs">[comprehensive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此该之变而道之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;哀公元年》</span>。注:&ldquo;备也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">招具该备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又撰<span class="smcs">《西京记》</span>三卷，引据该洽，世称其博闻焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;卢寊传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:该洽(完备周详);该备(完备，详备);该尽(完备无遗);该详(完备详尽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 广博 <span class="encs">[(of a person&rsquo;s knowledge) extensive;wide]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:该广(广博);该深(广博精深);该通(博通);该茂(广博深厚)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>该</strong></p>
<p>該 <span class="dicpy">gāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 包容;包括 <span class="encs">[contain;conclude]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">该秉季德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;天问》</span>。注:&ldquo;包也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁该终始。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;云图》</span>。注:&ldquo;兼也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《广韵》</span>一书可以该六书之学，其用宏矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">潘来</span><span class="smcs">《重刊古本广韵序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:该臧(包藏和充塞);该遍(包容一切);该兼(包容，兼容);该天(遮天);该括(包罗，概括)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 应该;应当(宋、元时才出现此义) <span class="encs">[should;ought to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">(</span><span class="diczx2">凤姐</span>)说道:天也不早了，我也该起来了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:不该拒绝;该当;该办;我们该走了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 应当轮到 <span class="encs">[be one&rsquo;s turn to do sth.]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:该房(值班);下一个该谁发言;该世(方言。来世;再生);该班坐夜(值班守夜);该吏(当班的吏员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 欠 <span class="encs">[owe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人家该咱们的，咱们该人家的&hellip;算一算，看看还有几个钱没有。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:我不该他钱</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>该</strong></p>
<p>該 <span class="dicpy">gāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指示代词。指称上文说过的人或事物(多用于公文) <span class="encs">[such]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:该员;该处;该案;该公司;该同志;该校是所重点中学</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 〈方〉这个。如:该号(这种);该首(这一边;这);该桩(这一件);该搭(这里;这儿)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>该</strong></p>
<p>該 <span class="dicpy">gāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用于强调 <span class="encs">[used for emphasis]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:妈妈又该唠叨了!要是今天不下雨那该多好啊</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示范围，相当于&ldquo;全&rdquo;、&ldquo;都&rdquo; <span class="encs">[all，overythere]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">招具该备，永啸呼些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如万事该悉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史》</span></span></p>
