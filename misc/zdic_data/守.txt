                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>守</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shǒu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǒu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄡˇ</span></p><ol><li>保持，卫护：～成（在事业上保持前人的成就）。～御。～身（爱护自身，保持自己的品节）。～节。～恒。～望相助。墨～成规。</li><li>看管：看～。～护。</li><li>在一个地方不动：～株待兔。留～。</li><li>遵照：遵～。～法。～时。</li><li>维持原状，不想改变：保～。因循～旧。</li><li>呆在一起：厮～。</li><li>节操：操～。</li><li>靠近，依傍：～着水的地方，可多种稻子。</li><li>古代官名：太～。～祧（中国周代掌管祭祀宗庙的官）。～刺（太守，刺史）。～令（指太守、刺史、县令等地方官）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">守</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>守</strong> <span class="dicpy">shǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从宀，从寸。宀表示房屋;寸是法度。合起来表示掌管法度。本义:官吏的职责，职守)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[duty;post]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">守，官守也。从门，寺府之事也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。寺，廷也;府，文书藏也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五载一巡守。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王巡虢守。——<span class="smcs">《左传·二十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">均地守。——<span class="smcs">《周礼均人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“地守，衡虞之属。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纠其守。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·内宰》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“宿卫者。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽守者益。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王制》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓地也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">官司之守，非君所及也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我无官守，我无言责也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 节操;操守 <span class="encs">[high moral principle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妾为情缘，葳蕤之质，一朝失守。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 秦代郡的长官名，汉代更名太守。后时作州、府地方长官的省称。被委任在某一方或某一区域负责实施法律或管理的政府主要官员 <span class="encs">[magistrate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">守者，</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>置也。 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>兼天下，置三川守。 <span class="diczx2">伊</span>、 <span class="diczx2">河</span>、 <span class="diczx2">洛</span>也。—— <span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">袁</span>有守多惠政，民甚爱之。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:郡守;吴起为河西守</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 州郡地方政府所在地 <span class="encs">[government place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维梁之西，其蔽曰某山，某守曰</span><span class="diczx2">兴州</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《兴州江运记》</span></p><p><span
