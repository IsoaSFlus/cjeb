                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>冲</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">衝；<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字">沖）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨㄥˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用水或酒浇注，水撞击：～茶。～剂。～洗。～荡。</li>	<li>向上钻，直上：～腾。～入云霄。</li>	<li>破解不祥：～喜。</li>	<li>空虚，谦虚：&ldquo;大盈若～，其用不穷&rdquo;。～挹（<span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span>）。谦～。</li>	<li>幼小：～昧。～弱。～龄。</li>	<li>方言，山区的平地：韶山～。</li>	<li>通行的大路，重要的地方：要～。首当其～。</li>	<li>不顾一切，一直向前：～锋。横～直撞。</li>	<li>猛烈地撞击：～力。～突。</li>	<li>收支账目互相抵销：～账。</li>	<li>情感强烈：～动。兴（<span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng</span>）～～。</li>	<li>太阳系中，除水星和金星外，其余的某一个行星进行到与地球、太阳成一条直线而地球正处在这个行星与太阳之间的位置时称&ldquo;冲&rdquo;。</li></ol><p>● <strong>冲</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">衝）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ch&ograve;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨㄥˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>对着，向着：～着太阳。</li>	<li>凭，根据：～你这么好学，一定能有成就。</li>	<li>猛烈；强烈：～劲。这酒真～。</li>	<li>一种使金属板成形或在金属板上打孔的专用设备称&ldquo;冲床&rdquo;。</li>	<li>在冲床上加工金属工件：～压。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">冲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冲</strong> <span class="dicpy">chōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，中声。本义:向上涌流)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[surge up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冲溶沅瀁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">木华</span><span class="smcs">《海赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 直上 <span class="encs">[shoot up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冲天(直上天空);冲汉(直上霄汉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 以水、酒等浇注调制 <span class="encs">[pour boiling water]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冲鸡蛋;用酒冲服;冲茶</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> <span class="encs">[会计]</span>收支账目互相抵消，或两户应支付的款项互相抵消 <span class="encs">[cancel out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸而后手里渐渐翻过来了，除了冲账的，反赢了好些&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:红字冲销</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 使曝光过的照相材料显影 <span class="encs">[develop]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冲胶卷</span></p>
