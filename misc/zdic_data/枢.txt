                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>枢</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">樞</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>门上的转轴：户～不蠹。</li><li>重要的或中心的部分，起决定性作用的部分：～纽。中～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> door hinge; pivot; center of power</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Achse  (S)​,Kernpunkt  (S)​,Drehpunkt  (S)​,Hauptsache  (S)​,Mittelpunkt  (S)​,Türangel  (S)​,Zapfen  (S)​,grundlegend  (Adj)​,unentbehrlich  (Adj)​,wesentlich  (Adj)​,wichtigste  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pivot en bois d'une porte,axe,centre,fondamental</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>枢</strong></p><p>樞 <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，区声。本义:户枢。旧式门的转轴或承轴臼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[door hinge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枢，户枢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枢谓这椳。又枢达北方谓之落时。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枢机之发。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“制动之主。”</span>释文:<span class="yhcs">“门臼也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">视门枢下当有白发。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然</span><span class="diczx2">陈涉</span>瓮牖绳枢之子，甿隶之人，而迁徙之徒也。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流水不腐，户枢不蝼，动也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·尽数》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:枢户(解脱门枢)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 木名，即刺榆 <span class="encs">[ Hemiplelea davidii ]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山有枢，隰有榆。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·山有枢》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指转轴 <span class="encs">[pivot]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">半弦弧不满圜半径者，以矢为枢，以半弦弧规之，成浑圜之小规。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">阮元</span><span class="smcs">《畴人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又指主制动的机关</p><p><span class="diczx1">其枢在水。——<span class="smcs">《管子·水地》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“主运转者也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">琴氏</span>乃横弓着臂，施机设枢。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">赵晔</span><span class="smcs">《吴越春秋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:枢干</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 中心;枢纽;关键 <span class="encs">[centre]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今夫</span><span class="diczx2">韩</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>，中国之处，而天下之枢也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谓之道枢。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“要也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:枢柄(主要的权柄);枢奥(中枢堂奥的所在);枢秘(关键;纲要);枢括(关键，中心);枢管(枢辖。关键);枢中(枢要中心)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 旧指国家政权或天子之位 <span class="encs">[central authority]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予除右丞相兼枢密使。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录·后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:枢垣(朝廷中重要的、接近皇帝的官署);枢近(在天子近侧或中央机要的职位);枢臣(指朝廷中枢的大臣);枢府(国政机要之所。宋多指枢密院。宋朝枢密院与中书省分掌文武，号为二府);枢使(枢密使的简称);枢相(枢密使);枢务(国家的重要政务。多指宰臣所掌的政事);枢幄(枢密院);枢衡(指中央行政机关。也指行政中枢的主要职权);枢廷(政权中枢);枢秘(指中央机要部门，犹枢要);枢辖(指中央政权的机要部门);枢辅(旧时指中央掌军权的大臣)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 星名 <span class="encs">[star’s name]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 指北斗七星第一星。又称天枢星。如:枢斗(即天枢。北斗七星第一颗);枢光(天枢星的光芒)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span>
