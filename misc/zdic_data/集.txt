<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>集</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>群鸟栖止于树上：&ldquo;黄鸟于飞，～于灌木&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>聚合，会合：聚～。～合。～会。～体。～团。～训。～散。～资。～中。～大成。～腋成裘。</li>
	<li>会合许多著作编成的书：～子。文～。诗～。选～。全～。</li>
	<li>大型图书中可以相对独立的一部分，或一部小说、一部电影、电视剧中相对独立的段落：上～。第五～。</li>
	<li>定期交易的市场：～市。赶～。</li>
	<li>中国古代图书的四部分类法：～部。经史子～。</li>
	<li>成就，成功：大业未～。</li>
	<li>数学基本概念之一，指若干具有共同属性的事物的总体：子～。交～。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">辑</span>&rdquo;，和睦。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">集</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>集</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[perch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">集，群鸟在木上也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">集，会也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">集于苞栩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;鸨羽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黄鸟于飞，集于灌木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;葛覃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:集隼(栖息的隼鸟);集凤(凤停于树)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为停留 <span class="encs">[stay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人皆集于苑，己独集于枯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齐集有其一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沙鸥翔集。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旋见鸡伸颈摆扑;临视，则虫集冠上，力可不释。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 集合;聚集;收集 <span class="encs">[assemble;collect;gather]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人皆集于苑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今乘诸军未集，宜速击之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆集于此。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四面集。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:集凑(收集拼凑);集叠(收取，打叠);集贤院(唐文学三馆之一。掌秘书图籍等事);集缀(聚集联缀，编辑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 依就 <span class="encs">[depend on;attach oneself to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有命既集。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;大明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:集服(顺从，服从);集附(归心，顺服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 至;遭受 <span class="encs">[arrive;suffer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不其集亡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;至也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:集蓼(遭遇苦难);集枯(遭到冷遇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 成功 <span class="encs">[accomplish]</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>集</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>j&iacute; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3056.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">棲息。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;唐風&middot;鴇羽》：「肅肅鴇羽，集于苞栩。」</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝宋&middot;鮑照〈贈傅都曹別詩〉：「輕鴻戲江潭，孤鴈集洲沚。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">把散開的事物聚合在一起。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「集合」、「聚集」、「籌集」、「集資」、「收集」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;賈誼&middot;過秦論》：「天下雲集響應，嬴糧而景從。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">市場、人口聚集舉行交易的地方。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「市集」、「趕集」、「年集」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">地名用字。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「張家集」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史&middot;第二回》：「話說山東兗州府汶上縣有個鄉村，叫做薛家集。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">輯錄詩文等的書籍。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「詩集」、「文集」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;曹丕&middot;與吳質書》：「頃撰其遺文，都為一集。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞。計算叢書或影劇集系列的單位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「這齣戲今天播出第三集。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">中國傳統圖書四部分類的第四部，包括詩文等的作品稱為「集」。</span></p></li>
