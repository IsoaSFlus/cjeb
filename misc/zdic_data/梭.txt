                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>梭</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">suō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄛˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>织布时往返牵引纬线（横线）的工具，两头尖，中间粗，像枣核形：～子（ ➊ 织布工具； ➋ 机关枪、冲锋枪的子弹夹子； ➌ 量词，如&ldquo;打了一～～子弹&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>形状像梭的：～镖。～子蟹。</li>
	<li>喻不断地来往：～巡。穿～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> weaver's shuttle; go to and fro</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> pendeln, Pendelverkehr  (S)​,Weberschiffchen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> navette</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">梭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>梭</strong> <span class="dicpy">suō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:织机的构件) 梭子 <span class="encs">[shuttle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梭，织具也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">机梭声札札，牛驴走纷纷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《朱陈村》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:梭布(土布。因用梭子织出);穿梭;无梭织机;梭杼(机杼。指织布机);梭肚(梭子中粗部分);梭布(家庭木机所织之布)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>梭</strong> <span class="dicpy">suō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 织布梭往复的次数 <span class="encs">[shuttle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一梭声尽重一梭，玉腕不停罗袖卷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《织锦曲》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 夹住子弹装入步枪弹仓用的器具 <span class="encs">[clip]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:三梭子弹</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>梭</strong> <span class="dicpy">suō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 穿梭 <span class="encs">[shuttle]</span>。比喻往来频繁，运行快速</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纷纷织女梭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《代堂上歌行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:日月如梭;梭梭(往复如梭。形容频繁);梭过(很快地穿过);梭天摸地(上窜下跳。喻到处逃窜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 滑动 <span class="encs">[slide]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:从马上梭下来</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用眼一扫 <span class="encs">[glance]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:偷偷地梭了他一眼</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 悠荡 <span class="encs">[sway]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人人吹唱人人欢，高打秋千好快乐。梭罗梭，梭着</span><span class="diczx2">李婆</span>!&mdash;&mdash;梨园戏<span class="smcs">《陈三五娘》</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">織布時用來牽引橫線的器具，兩頭尖，中間粗，絲束放於中空部分。</span><span class="gc_yy">《晉書&middot;卷六六&middot;陶侃傳》：「侃少時漁於雷澤，網得一織梭，以掛于壁。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;華岳〈田家〉詩：「老農鋤水子收禾，老婦攀機女織梭。」</span><span class="gc_yx">也稱為「梭子」。</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/68AD.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】私閏切【集韻】須閏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音濬。木也。一曰木茂也。</p>
<p>又徐鉉曰：別作蘇禾切，音蓑。【正韻】桑何切，機杼之屬，所以行緯。【晉書&middot;陶侃傳】侃少漁雷澤，網得一梭，挂壁上，雷雨暴至，化龍而去。本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23724.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣜤</span>，亦作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B5F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥭟</span>，今文作梭。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】且泉切，音悛。木名。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>松倫切，音旬。木名。</p>
<p>又【類篇】逡緣切。木如餘甘。</p>
