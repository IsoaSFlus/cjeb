                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>缨</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">纓</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>用线或绳等做的装饰品：帽～子。红～枪。～穗。</li><li>像缨的东西：萝卜～子。</li><li>带子，绳子：长～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a chin strap; tassel; to annoy, bother</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Quast  (S)​,Quaste  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cordon de chapeau,gland</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缨</strong></p><p>纓 <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸，婴声。本义:系在脖子上的帽带)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cap or hat ribbon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缨，冠系也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鲜冠组缨，绛衣博袍。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公孟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正冠而缨绝。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·让王》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沧浪之水清兮，可以濯吾缨。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·渔父》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戴朱缨宝饰之帽。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:缨冠(把帽带连同帽子一齐加于头上。形容极为急迫，来不及整束)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 彩带，古代女子许嫁时所佩 <span class="encs">[coloured ribbon]</span>。亦用以系香囊。如:缨徽(妇女所佩的香囊)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 套马的革带，驾车用。引申指绳索 <span class="encs">[leather ribbon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咸龙旂而繁缨。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">薛</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“缨，马鞅也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鞶厉游缨。——<span class="smcs">《左传·桓公二年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“缨，在马膺前如索帬。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以缨拾矢可也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼下》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“缨，缨卷也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:缨缴(被系有绳子的箭所射中);缨铰(捆绑人的绳子与枷锁。比喻拘限)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 丝、线等做成的穗状饰物 <span class="encs">[tasse1]</span>。如:红缨;帽缨;缨佩(以缨纽为佩饰);缨蕤(帽子的垂饰);缨徽(妇女所佩带的香囊)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>缨</strong></p><p>纓 <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 缠绕、系牵。通<span class="yhcs">“婴”</span> <span class="encs">[twine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而不缨垢氛。——</span><span class="diczx2">谢灵运</span><span class="smcs">《述祖德》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">李善</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“缨，绕也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:缨情(犹系心)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 遭受 <span class="encs">[suffer from]</span>。如:缨缴(中箭。缴( <span class="dicpy">zhuó</span>):箭上的丝绳。借指箭)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/缨络">缨络</a></span><span class="crefe"><a                           『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7E93.svg">                          <p>冠系也。从糸嬰聲。於盈切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">冠系也。</span>冠系、可以系冠者也。系者、係也。以二組系於冠卷結頣下是謂纓。與紘之自下而上系於筓者不同。冠用纓。冕弁用紘。纓以固武。卽以固冠。故曰冠系。玉藻之記曰。玄冠朱組纓。天子之冠也。緇布冠繢緌。諸侯之冠也。玄冠丹組纓。諸侯之齊冠也。玄冠綦組纓。士之齊冠也。許此冠字專謂冠、不該冕弁。<span class="jieshi">从糸。嬰聲。</span>於盈切。十一部。                          </div>
