                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">餐</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>餐</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>吃：～具。～厅。聚～。风～露宿。</li>
	<li>饭食：早～。西～。</li>
	<li>量词，指一顿饭：一日三～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> eat, dine; meal; food</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> manger,prendre le repas,aliment,repas</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">餐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>餐</strong></p>
<p>飱、湌 <span class="dicpy">cān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，( <span class="dicpy">c&aacute;n</span>)声。本义:吃)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[eat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">餐，吞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">餐，食也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相谒而餐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span>。注:&ldquo;昼饭为餐，晚饭为飧。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼君子兮，不素餐兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">列侯幸得赐餐钱奉邑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:进餐;会餐;野餐;就餐;聚餐;餐玉(吞食玉屑。古时认为服食玉屑可以延寿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 吞食 <span class="encs">[swallow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此甘餐毒药，戏猛兽之爪牙也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>餐</strong> <span class="dicpy">cān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 饮食，食物 <span class="encs">[food]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令其裨将传餐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;韩信传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以一壶餐得士二人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;中山策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:餐卫(指饮食调养);早中晚三餐;素餐;中餐;西餐;夜餐</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="encs">[量词]</span>∶一顿饭叫一餐 <span class="encs">[meal]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:一日三餐</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=食" target="_blank">食</a>】
                              餐·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9910.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】七安切【集韻】【韻會】千安切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>粲平聲。【說文】吞也。【詩&middot;魏風】不素餐兮。【釋文】餐，七丹反。【說文】或从水作湌。【韻會】俗作飡。</p>
<p>又飮饌曰餐。【前漢&middot;高后紀】賜餐錢。【註】厨膳錢也。</p>
<p>又【韓信傳】令其裨將傳餐。【註】服虔曰：立騎傳餐食也。如淳曰：小飯曰餐。師古曰：餐，古食字。千安反。</p>
<p>又採也。【王儉褚淵<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>】餐輿誦于丘里，瞻雅詠于京國。【註】採輿論，稱述其德也。</p>
<p>又古通先。【古樂府&middot;君子行】周公下白屋，吐哺不足餐。一沐三握髮，後世稱聖賢。</p>
<p>又【集韻】蘇昆切，音孫。與飧同。餔也。【爾雅&middot;釋言】粲，餐也。【釋文】餐，音飧。</p>
<p>又【集韻】蒼案切，音粲。餠也。　【正韻】作䬸。【字彙】作餐，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>非。</p>
