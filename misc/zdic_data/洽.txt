                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>洽</strong> <span class="dicpy">qià</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qià.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，合声。本义:沾湿，浸润)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[soak]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洽，沾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仁洽道丰。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是泽南洽而威北畅也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·终军传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">内洽五藏。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·要略》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:洽濡(沾湿，滋润);洽润(润泽);洽衿(沾湿衣襟)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 合，符合 <span class="encs">[fit;suit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以洽百礼。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·载芟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则无以祖洽于众也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·仲尼燕居》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:洽愿(称心如愿);洽意(合意;中意);洽悉(与事切合而详尽)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 商量，协商意见 <span class="encs">[consult with;arrange with]</span>。如:洽办;面洽;洽商</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“给”</span>。供应 <span class="encs">[supply]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不洽而爟之，使客无得以助攻备。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·兵守》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>洽</strong> <span class="dicpy">qià</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qià.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 周遍;广博 <span class="encs">[extensive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元元本本，殚见洽闻。——</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《西都赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“洽，遍也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:洽化(普遍受到薰陶教化);恰闻(见识广博);洽人(学识渊博的人);洽客(广见博闻者);洽浃(广博;周遍)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 和谐;融洽 <span class="encs">[harmonious;uniform;in harmony]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洽比其邻。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·正月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民之洽也。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·板》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:洽平(太平盛世，四方融洽);洽同(协和统一);洽和(和睦); 洽悦(和睦欢乐)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/洽办">洽办</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
