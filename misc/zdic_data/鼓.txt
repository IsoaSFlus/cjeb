                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鼓</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鼓</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>打击乐器，圆柱形，中空，两头蒙皮：～乐（<span class="dicpy">yu&egrave;</span>）。～角（<span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span>）。大～。</li>
	<li>形状、声音、作用像鼓的：耳～。石～。</li>
	<li>敲击或拍打使发出声音：～吹。～噪。</li>
	<li>发动，使振作起来：～励。～动。～舞。一～作气。</li>
	<li>高起，凸出：～包。～胀。</li>
	<li>古代夜间击鼓以报时，一鼓即一更。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> drum; beat, top, strike</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Trommel  (S, Mus)​,Radikal Nr. 207 = Trommel, trommeln, schlagen, Musik spielen, ermuntern, Luft hineinblasen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tambour,bomber,207e radical</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鼓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鼓</strong></p>
<p>皷 <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，左边是鼓的本字，右边是&ldquo;支&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，表示手持棒槌击鼓。本义:鼓，一种打击乐器)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[drum]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼓，郭也。春分之音。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼓，廓也。张皮以冒之，其中空也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释乐器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">击鼓其镗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;击鼓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皮曰鼓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;律历志上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抱玉枪兮击鸣鼓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;国殇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">瑜</span>等率轻锐继其后，雷鼓大震。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百姓阗王钟鼓之乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 打击乐器之一。一般由两端绷紧皮面的空心圆筒构成，以一根或一对木槌敲击时发出深沉的咚咚声。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鼓钹(鼓和铙钹。打击乐器);鼓鞞(乐器，分大鼓和小鼓，进军时以励战士);鼓吏(掌鼓的官吏);鼓床(鼓架，鼓座)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鼓面</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼓长八尺，鼓四尺，中围加三分之一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 形状、作用、声音像鼓的东西。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:石鼓;耳鼓;蛙鼓</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指鼓声</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可堪回首，</span><span class="diczx2">佛狸祠</span>下，一片神鸦社鼓。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐&middot;京口北固亭怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 钟磬被敲击之处 <span class="encs">[stricken point]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铣间谓之于，于上谓之鼓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令二人蹲踞而背倚之，漏鼓移则番代。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四鼓，愬至城下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古国名。春秋时鼓国，在今河北省晋县，白狄的一支，为晋国所灭 <span class="encs">[Gu state]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 古代量器名。四钧为石，四石为鼓 <span class="encs">[Gu， a kind of measure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献米者操量鼓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>

<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 击鼓进攻 <span class="encs">[drum]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鼓，击鼓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">夫战，勇气也。一鼓作气，再而衰;三而竭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 激发 <span class="encs">[rouse;pluck up]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鼓舞;鼓励</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 拍打;振动 <span class="encs">[flap;shake]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 凸起，涨大 <span class="encs">[bulge]</span>。</p>

<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 扇动 <span class="encs">[fan]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鼓风</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9F13.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29272.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩉲</span>【唐韻】工戸切【集韻】【韻會】果五切【正韻】公土切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音古。革音之器。伊耆氏造鼓。【說文】鼓，郭也。春分之音，萬物郭皮甲而出，故謂之鼓。【徐鍇曰】郭者，覆冒之意。【玉篇】瓦爲椌，革爲面，可以擊也。樂書，鼓所以檢樂，爲羣音長。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;鼓人】掌敎六鼓。【註】六鼓：靁鼓八面，靈鼓六面，路鼓四面，鼖鼓，皋鼓，晉鼓，皆兩面。</p>
<p>又夏后氏足鼓，置鼓於趺上，謂之節鼓。殷楹鼓，以柱貫中，上出而樹之也。周縣鼓，植簨虡而縣之也。</p>
<p>又星名。【爾雅&middot;釋天】河鼓謂之牽牛。【郭註】荆楚人呼牽牛爲擔鼓。擔者，荷也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;五行志】天水冀南山大石鳴，曰石鼓，鳴則有兵。</p>
<p>又國名。春秋鼓國，白狄別種。【左傳&middot;昭二十三年】晉襲鼓，滅之。【後漢&middot;郡國志】鉅鹿下曲陽有鼓聚，故翟鼓子國。</p>
<p>又量名。【禮&middot;曲禮】獻米者操量鼓。【廣雅】斛謂之鼓。【荀子&middot;富國篇】瓜桃棗李，一本數以盆鼓。【註】鼓，量也。謂數度以盆量也。</p>
<p>又【後漢&middot;東夷傳】扶餘國，正月，國中連日大會飮樂，名曰迎鼓。　【集韻】俗作皷，非是。</p>
