                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">汗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>汗</strong> <span class="dicpy">hán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 可汗，古代鲜卑、柔然、突厥、回纥、蒙古等族对其统治者的称号 <span class="encs">[king]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汗，可汗。蕃王称。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">咸丰</span>四年， <span class="diczx2">土谢图汗</span>、 <span class="diczx2">车臣汗</span>两部汗、王、公、台吉等请捐助军需，温旨郤之。——<span class="smcs">《清史稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>汗</strong> <span class="dicpy">hàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，干声。本义:汗腺的分泌物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 由人或高等动物皮肤内腺体分泌的一种含盐的液体 <span class="encs">[sweat;perspiration]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汗，身液也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汗者，精气也。——<span class="smcs">《素问·评热病论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五藏化液心为汗。——<span class="smcs">《宣明五气篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出令如出汗，汗出而不反也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·刘向传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汗衣，近身受汗垢 之衣也。——<span class="smcs">《释名·释衣服》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汗出浃背。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:汗巾(擦汗用的手巾);汗血(汗与血;流汗流血;汗出如血);汗汪汪(形容汗水多的样子);汗星(细小的汗珠);汗迹(汗水的痕迹)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指青竹被火烤后像出汗一样冒出的水分。亦指以火烤干青竹之水分 <span class="encs">[sweat]</span>。如:汗筒(古人用竹筒书写，竹筒在使用前，先在火上烤出汁，用以防蛀，称为汗筒，也称汗青);汗竹(借指史籍、书册);汗青头白(书成人老)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 旧时维吾尔族一些上层男子名字后面所加的称号 <span
