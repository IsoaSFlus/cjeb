                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">澍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>澍</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从水，尌( <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:及时雨) 同本义 <span class="encs">[timely rain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">澍，时雨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群生澍濡。雨润万物名曰澍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《难蜀父老文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">连获甘澍，岁时丰稔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;段熲传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:澍雨(及时雨);澍降(降雨);澍霖(及时雨)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>澍</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 降<span class="encs">[雨]</span> <span class="encs">[rain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未及还宫而澍雨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:澍雨(降雨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 雨水滋润 <span class="encs">[moisten]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若春雨之灌万物也，浑然而流，沛然而施，无地而不澍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘安</span><span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泰族》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:澍泽(沾濡滋润。亦指滋润万物的及时雨);澍濡(雨水滋润万物。引申指善施恩泽，或指承受恩泽)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>澍</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄕㄨˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>sh&ugrave;</rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">及時的雨。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;陸游〈喜雨〉詩：「樂哉甘<b>澍</b>及時至，九衢一洗塵沙黃。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">降、落。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;王禹偁〈對雪示嘉祐〉詩：「秋來連<b>澍</b>百日雨，禾黍漂溺多不收。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《水滸傳&middot;第九四回》：「喬冽揭榜上壇，甘霖大<b>澍</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">潤澤、滋潤。</span><span class="gc_yy">《淮南子&middot;泰族》：「若春雨之灌萬物也，&hellip;&hellip;無地而不<b>澍</b>，無物不生。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;王充《論衡&middot;雷虛》：「說雨者以為天施氣。天施氣，氣渥為雨，故雨潤萬物，名曰<b>澍</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上" target="_blank">巳集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上&amp;bs=水" target="_blank">水</a>】
                              澍·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6F8D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】常句切【集韻】【韻會】朱戍切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音注。【說文】時雨澍生萬物。</p>
<p>又借作霑濡滋植意。【司馬相如&middot;難蜀父老文】羣生澍濡。</p>
<p>又與注同。水流射也。【王褒&middot;洞簫賦】聲礚礚而澍淵。【註】澍與注，古字通。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>殊遇切，音樹。義同。</p>
