                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>踪</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">蹤</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄨㄥˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>人或动物走过留下的脚印：～影。～迹。追～。失～。跟～。无影无～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> footprints, traces, tracks</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Fußabdruck, Fährte, Spur</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> trace,empreinte</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">踪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>踪</strong></p>
<p>蹤 <span class="dicpy">zōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，从(宗)声。本义:脚印，踪迹)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[trace]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草合人踪断，尘浓鸟迹深。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《谒老君庙》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千山鸟飞绝，万径人踪灭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《江雪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">访其遗踪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝无踪响。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胆寒潜踪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踪尘(尘世的事情);踪绪(踪迹，头绪);踪响(踪迹和声响);踪辙(踪迹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 事物的痕迹 <span class="encs">[track]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翠华一去寂无踪，玉楼歌吹，声断己随风。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《鹿虔扆&middot;临江仙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踪由(事情的始末);跟踪</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>踪</strong> <span class="dicpy">zōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>追随 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朕欲远追</span><span class="diczx2">周文</span>，近踪 <span class="diczx2">光武</span>，使宗庙有太山之安。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;刘曜载书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">质菲薄而难踪，心恬愉而去惑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《隋书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踪行(跟着走)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">蹤</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“踪”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/蹤">蹤</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=足" target="_blank">足</a>】
                              蹤·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8E64.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】卽容切【集韻】【韻會】將容切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音從。【玉篇】跡也。【釋名】從也。人形從之也。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】躡三皇之高蹤。○按《說文》無蹤字，古皆以縱爲蹤。詳糸部縱字註。</p><p>又【韻補】叶卽王切，音匡。【陸雲&middot;陸府君誄】耽精遐奧，肆志篇章。仰咨遺訓，思齊曩蹤。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">𨌰</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“踪”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/𨌰"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28330.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨌰</span></a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四">卷十四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四&amp;swbs=車">車</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/28330.svg">                          <p>車迹也。从車，從省聲。卽容切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗別作蹤，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/28330_10069.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28330.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨌰</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">車迹也。</span><span class="swdz">㒳輪之迹也。迹者，步處也，因引申爲凡迹之偁。㒳輪之迹亦謂之軌徹，而非軌徹之體也。莊子曰：夫迹，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21CD0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡳐</span>之所出。而迹豈<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21CD0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡳐</span>也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28330.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨌰</span>之言從也，有所從來也，又可從是以求其質也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28330.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨌰</span>古字衹作從。羔羊傳曰：委蛇委蛇，行可從迹也。君子偕老傳曰：委委，行可委曲從迹也。俗變爲蹤，再變爲踪。固不若用許書<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28330.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨌰</span>字矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从車，從省。</span><span class="swdz">大徐有聲字。非也。此以會意包形聲。卽容切。九部。</span></p>                          </div>
