                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>贩</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">販</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>买货出卖：～私。～毒。～卖。～运。</li><li>买货物出卖的行商或小商人：商～。摊～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> peddler, hawker, street merchant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Straßenhändler  (S)​,kaufen für Wiederverkauf  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> trafiquer,transporter des marchandises,petit commerçant,marchand ambulant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贩</strong></p><p>販 <span class="dicpy">fàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，反声。本义:贱买而贵卖的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[peddler;pedlar;pedler]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贩，买贱卖贵也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贩夫贩妇。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司市》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽负贩者，必有尊也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:菜贩;摊贩;贩商(商贩);贩人(商贩)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Fan state]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贩，古国名。——<span class="smcs">《六书故》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>贩</strong></p><p>販 <span class="dicpy">fàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 买货出售 <span class="encs">[buy to resell]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">资用乏，以贩马自业。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·吴汉传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:贩粜(贩卖粮食);贩鲜(贩卖鱼虾之类);贩易(做买卖);贩肆(贩运货物，开店买卖)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 也单指买进货物 <span class="encs">[buy]</span>。如:贩籴(收买粮食);贩买(买进);贩官(买得官位)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻叛卖 <span class="encs">[betray]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽</span><span class="diczx2">吕布</span>贩君， <span class="diczx2">郦寄</span>卖友，万之斯人，未足为酷。——<span class="smcs">《宋书·沈攸之传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:贩君(卖主求荣);贩国(卖国);贩交买名(卖友求荣)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 卖出 <span class="encs">[sell out]</span>。如:贩路(销路);贩贴(出卖劳力，依附于人);贩质(典卖)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/贩毒">贩毒</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
