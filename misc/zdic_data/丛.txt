                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丛</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>丛</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">叢</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">c&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>聚集，许多事物凑在一起：～生。～聚。～密。</li>
	<li>聚在一起的（人或物）：人～。草～。～刊。为～驱雀（喻不善于团结人，把可以依靠的力量赶到敌人方面去）。～祠。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bush, shrub; thicket; collection</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gebüsch; Gesträuch; Gestrüpp; Dickicht, Busch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> touffe,bouquet</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丛</strong></p>
<p>叢、樷 <span class="dicpy">c&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从丵，取声。&ldquo;丵&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;</span>)，意思是&ldquo;丛生草&rdquo;。本义:聚集)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[crow together;gather]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丛，聚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其植物宜丛物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树木丛生，百草丰茂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《观沧海》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丛物(聚生于一处的植物);丛灌(丛生的灌木);丛丛(聚集的样子);丛育(聚集生长);丛毛(丛生的草);丛森(树木繁茂幽深样子);丛薄(草木丛生的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 许多事物凑在一起 <span class="encs">[crowd together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">石块丛起则历块。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则百恶并起，而万灾丛至矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;达郁》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丛</strong></p>
<p>叢 <span class="dicpy">c&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 众多;繁杂 <span class="encs">[miscellaneous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">网密事丛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;酷吏传赞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恶丛巧之乱世兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;冯衍传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丛顇(繁多杂乱的样子);丛谈(各种轶事杂说);丛委(杂谈的堆积);丛丛杂杂(杂乱而繁多的样子);丛细，丛琐(繁多琐碎)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>丛</strong></p>
<p>叢 <span class="dicpy">c&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 丛林;丛生的树木 <span class="encs">[chump;grove;jungle;scrub]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兽走丛薄之中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;俶真》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又间令</span><span class="diczx2">吴广</span>之次所旁丛祠中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:桉树丛;松树丛;丛祠(草野间的神祠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 聚集在一起的人 <span class="encs">[depth]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:消失在人丛中</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 收集在一起出版的文章 <span class="encs">[collection]</span>。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/53E2.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】徂紅切【集韻】【韻會】徂聰切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>族平聲。【說文】聚也。从丵从取。【書&middot;無逸】是叢于厥身。</p>
<p>又姓。南北朝滁州刺史叢鐇。</p>
<p>又臺名。在邯鄲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作樷。【前漢&middot;東方朔傳】樷珍怪。</p>
<p>又作藂。【前漢&middot;息夫躬傳】藂棘棧棧。【註】詩葛覃註：灌木曰藂。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶徂黃切，音牀。【韓愈&middot;此日足可惜詩】蕭條千萬里，會合安可逢。淮之水舒舒，楚山直叢叢。逢音房。【韻會】後人誤作菆，或作蕞，二字皆非。毛氏曰：丵，上从四直，兩長兩短。从丱，非。丵原刻下从羊。</p>
