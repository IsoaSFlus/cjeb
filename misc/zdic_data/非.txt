                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>非</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fēi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēi.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄟˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>不，不是：～凡。～法。～分（<span class="dicpy">fèn </span>）。～礼。～但。～同小可。啼笑皆～。</li>    <li>不对，过失：痛改前～。文过饰～。习～成是（对于某些错的事情习惯了，反认为是对的）。</li>    <li>与“<span class="diczx3">不</span>”呼应，表示必须（有时后面没有“<span class="diczx3">不</span>”字）：我～看这本书。</li>    <li>责怪，反对：～难（<span class="dicpy">nàn </span>）。～议。无可厚～。</li>    <li>指“阿非利加洲”（位于东半球的西南部。简称“非洲”）：东～。西～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">非</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>非</strong> <span class="dicpy">fēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。金文作<span class="yhcs">“兆”</span>，像<span class="yhcs">“飞”</span>字下面相背展开的双翅形，双翅相背，表示违背。本义:违背;不合)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[violate;run counter to;not conform to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非，违也。从飛，下翅取其相背也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非刀匕是共。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇氏</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“不也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非礼也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹失也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">傲主人，非礼也;以贵骄人，非道也。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·简傲》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:非常异义(违背经文正义的异说);非度(违反法度);非道(不合道义);非德(不合道德;违背道德);非理(不合常理;违背情理)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 责怪;非难;反对 <span class="encs">[blame;censure;reproach]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">私田稼不善则非吏。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·宣公十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“责也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今诸生不师今而学古，以非当世。——<span class="smcs">《史记·秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苟或不然，人争非之，以为鄙吝。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:非驳(非难驳斥，诘责缺失);未可厚非</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 讨厌 <span class="encs">[detest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾岂厌喧而求静，吾岂好丹而非素?——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《白鹤吟示觉海元公》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 讥刺 <span class="encs">[satirize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非世而恶利。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李斯传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其观于人，不知其非笑之为非笑也。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《答李翊书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当时士大夫家皆然，人不相非也。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:非讥(讥评，议论);非笑(讥笑)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“诽”</span>( <span class="dicpy">fěi</span>)。诽谤，诋毁 <span class="encs">[slander]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此山谷之士，非世之人，枯槁赴渊者之所好也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·刻意》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民释实事而诵虚词，则力少而非多。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·慎法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">誉者不能进，非者弗能退。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·有度》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:非誉(诽谤与赞誉);非而不用(诽谤而不予重用);非世(诋毁世俗);非孝(非难毁诋孝行);非怨(诋毁，怨恨)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“避”</span>。躲开 <span class="encs">[avoid]</span></p><p><span
