                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>咤</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&agrave;</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>诧异;惊奇 <span class="encs">[be surprised]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那怪看得眼咤，小龙丢了花字，望妖精劈一刀来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>咤</strong></p>
<p>吒 <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，宅声。本义:吃东西时口中作声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[smack one&rsquo;s lips]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毋咤食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 痛惜 <span class="encs">[deeply regret]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">痛惜曰咤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span>引<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 怒吼 <span class="encs">[bellow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那</span><span class="diczx2">行者</span>一生性急， 那里容得， 大咤一声， 现了本相。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:咤叱(叱咤，怒斥);咤叱喑鸣(厉声怒喝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 慨叹，叹息声 <span class="encs">[sign]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哀咤良久。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> &ldquo;吒&rdquo;</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">咤</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】
                              咤·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/54A4.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>陟嫁切，音奼。【玉篇】與吒同。噴也，叱怒也。【史記&middot;淮隂侯傳】項王喑惡叱咤。【前漢&middot;王吉傳】口倦乎叱咤。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】毋咤食。【疏】謂於口舌中作聲，似嫌主人之食。</p>
<p>又悲也。【蔡琰&middot;悲憤詩】怛咤靡肝肺。</p>
<p>又與詫通。【孟子&middot;顧鴻鴈麋鹿註】其心以爲娛樂誇咤。【後漢&middot;王符傳】轉相誇咤。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作喥，詳後喥字註。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>都故切，音妒。【書&middot;顧命】三祭三咤。【傳】三祭酒三奠爵。【釋文】咤音妒。【集韻】本作㓃。或作宅詫。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>陟加切，音奓。【玉篇】與吒同。【集韻】或作喥。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】達利咤，出《釋典》。</p>
<p>又【集韻】闥各切，音託。奠爵也。書三咤。徐邈讀。或省作宅。○按書《釋文》咤，陟嫁反，又音妒，又音託，又豬夜反，凡重音四。一作宅，一作詫，凡重文三。</p>
