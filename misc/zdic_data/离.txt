                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">离</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>离</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">離</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>相距，隔开：距～。太阳是～地球最近的恒星。</li>
	<li>离开，分开：分～。～别。～家。～散（<span class="dicpy">s&agrave;n</span>）。～职。～异。～间（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n </span>）。支～破碎。</li>
	<li>缺少：办好教育～不开教师。</li>
	<li>八卦之一，符号是&ldquo;☲&rdquo;，代表火。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/罹">罹</a>&rdquo;，遭受。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/缡">缡</a>&rdquo;，妇女的佩巾。</li>
	<li>〔～～〕形容草木茂盛，如&ldquo;～～原上草，一岁一枯荣&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">离</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>离</strong></p>
<p>離 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)，表示与鸟类有关，离声。&ldquo;离&rdquo;是&ldquo;鹂&rdquo;的本字，因而声符亦兼表字义。本义:鸟名。即黄鹂，也称仓庚，鸣声清脆动听)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Chinese oriole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">离，离黄仓庚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">離为雉、九家，離为鸟，为飞、为鹤、为黄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">离则配禹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">流離之子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;旌丘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两个黄鹂鸣翠柳，一行白鹭上青天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《绝句》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 长离，传说中的凤鸟，比喻有才华的人 <span class="encs">[a legendary bird]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">双鸾游兰渚，二离扬清晖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">傅长虞</span><span class="smcs">《赠何劭王济》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 八卦之一，象征火 <span class="encs">[fire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">离，为火，为日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;缡&rdquo;。古代女子出嫁时系的佩巾 <span class="encs">[scarf]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">申佩离以自思。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;外戚传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;螭&rdquo;。古代传说中没有角的龙 <span class="encs">[dragon without horn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如虎如罴，如豺如离。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;周本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;樆&rdquo;。山棃 <span class="encs">[rowan]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桂椒栏，檗离朱杨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《子虚赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;蓠&rdquo;。香草 <span class="encs">[sweetgrass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扈江离与辟芷兮，纫秋兰以为佩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>离</strong></p>
<p>離 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 离假借为&ldquo;剺&rdquo;。离开;离别 <span class="encs">[part;leave each other]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">正大夫离居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;雨无止》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少小离家老大回。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《回乡偶书》</span></span></p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/79BB.svg">                          <p>山神，獸也。从禽頭，从厹从屮。歐陽喬說：离，猛獸也。呂支切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：从屮，義無所取，疑象形。〖注〗<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21F01.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡼁</span>，古文。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/79BB_4485.svg" /><span class="swbf">(离)</span><span class="ywyy">山神也。</span><span class="swdz">也字今補。</span><span class="ywyy">獸形。</span><span class="swdz">形，各本作也，今正。左傳：螭魅罔兩。杜注：螭，山神，獸形。周禮：地示物魅。正義引服䖍左傳注螭，山神，獸形。上林賦：蛟龍赤螭。如淳注曰：螭，山神也，獸形。按山神之字本不从虫。从虫者，乃許所謂若龍而黃者也。今左傳作螭魅，乃俗寫之譌。東京賦作魑，亦是俗字。徐鉉於鬼部增魑字，誤矣。薛綜二京解云：魑魅，山澤之神也。與許、服說同。本是山神而形如獸，故其字从厹。若今本作神獸，則大誤矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从禽頭。</span><span class="swdz">謂凶也。</span><span class="ywyy">从厹，</span><span class="swdz">獸形則頭足皆獸矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从屮。</span><span class="swdz">从屮，若巂字之首，像其冠耳。竊謂當从山。从山者，謂其爲山神也。音丑知切。古音在十七部。大徐吕支切。</span><span class="ywyy">歐陽喬說：离，猛獸也。</span><span class="swdz">此别一義。西都賦：拕熊螭。李注引歐陽尚書說曰：螭，猛獸也。漢書儒林傳：歐陽事伏生，世世相傳。至會孫高字子陽，傳孫地餘，地餘子政。由是尚書世有歐陽氏學。㙯文志：歐陽章句三十一卷。許云歐陽喬者，葢卽高。古喬高通用。許作离，李善作螭者，俗亂之也。此葢說今文坶誓。史記作如豺如離，可證。离離古通用。周禮正義引服氏左傳注：螭，山神，獸形，或曰如虎而噉虎。二說並列，正同許氏。若俗本說文前說改爲山神獸也，則與後說不别矣。</span></p>                          </div>
