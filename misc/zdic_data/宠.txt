                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>宠</strong></p><p>寵 <span class="dicpy">chǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，龙声。本义:尊崇)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[revere]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宠神其祖。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“尊也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宠，尊居也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宠名皆弃。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十年》</span>。谓族也。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:宠存(尊崇慰问);宠重(尊崇重视);崇厉(尊崇勉励)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宠爱 <span class="encs">[bestow favor on;dote on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君以为忠，而甚宠之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不宜爱而受宠。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·幸偶》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">少姜</span>有宠而死。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公三年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宠辱若惊。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上官大夫与之同列，争宠而心害其能。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:一家人很宠他;宠冠一时(备受宠爱，冠绝一时);宠拔(宠爱提拔)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>宠</strong></p><p>寵 <span class="dicpy">chǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 荣耀 <span class="encs">[honor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">承天宠也。——<span class="smcs">《易·师》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“光耀也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其宠大矣。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“荣也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宠有孝悌长幼顺明之节。——<span class="smcs">《史记·赵世家》</span>。正义:<span class="yhcs">“贵也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心旷神怡，宠辱偕忘。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:宠辱(荣耀与耻辱);宠借(给予荣宠和奖励)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 恩惠 <span class="encs">[kindness]</span>。如:宠灵(恩宠);宠光(恩宠光耀);宠利(恩宠与利禄)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 受宠爱的人 <span class="encs">[pet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐侯</span>好内，多内宠。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公十七年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:内宠;爱宠</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 皇帝所施的 <span class="encs">[imperial]</span>。如:宠劳(皇帝的慰劳);宠赐(帝王的恩赐);宠惠(帝王的恩泽)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/宠爱">宠爱</a></span><span
