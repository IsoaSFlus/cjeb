						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>榻</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tà  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tà.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄚˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 狭长而较矮的床，亦泛指床：竹～。藤～。卧～。下～（客人住宿）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cot, couch, bed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gepolsterte Liege, Polsterliege  (S)​,langes, schmales, niedriges Bett  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> lit</p></div>
