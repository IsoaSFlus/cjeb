                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">广</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>广</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">廣</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">guǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄤˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>指面积、范围宽阔，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">狭</span>&rdquo;相对：宽～。～博。～义。～漠。～袤（东西称&ldquo;广&rdquo;，南北称&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">袤</span>&rdquo;，指土地面积）。～厦。</li>
	<li>多：大庭～众。</li>
	<li>扩大，扩充：推～。～开言路。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>广</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/庵">庵</a>&rdquo;。草屋。清桂馥《説文解字義證&bull;广部》：&ldquo;馥謂广即庵字，隸嫌其空，故加奄，變象形為諧聲。&rdquo;元袁桷《次韻瑾子過梁山濼》：&ldquo;土屋危可緣，草广突如峙。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>广</strong></p>
<p>yǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄢˇ</p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>依山崖建造的房屋。《説文&bull;广部》：&ldquo;广，因广為屋，象對刺高屋之形。&rdquo;徐灝注箋：&ldquo;因厂為屋，猶言傍巖架屋。此上古初有宫室之為也。&rdquo;宋李誡《營造法式&bull;總釋上&bull;宫》:&ldquo;因巖成室謂之广。&rdquo;唐韓愈《陪杜侍御遊湘西兩寺獨宿有題因獻楊常侍》:&ldquo;剖竹走泉源，開廊架崖广。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>屋脊。《古今韻會舉要&bull;琰韻》:&ldquo;广，《增韻》：&lsquo;棟頭曰广。&rsquo;&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wide, extensive, broad; rad. 53</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> breit, umfangreich, weit reichend ,weit ,Radikal Nr. 53 = Schrägdach, Schutz, Unterschlupf, Unterbringung  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vaste,immense,nombreux</p></div>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>广</strong> <span class="dicpy">ān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;庵&rdquo;。草屋 <span class="encs">[hut]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">guǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>广</strong></p>
<p>廣 <span class="dicpy">guǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，黄声。从&ldquo;广&rdquo;，表示与房屋有关。按:&ldquo;广&rdquo;和&ldquo;廣&rdquo;本不同义:是两个字。&ldquo;广&rdquo;，本读 <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>，甲骨文和金文的写法象屋墙屋顶，其含义是依山崖建造的房屋。现在&ldquo;广&rdquo;是&ldquo;廣&rdquo;的简化字。本义:宽大的房屋)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 四周无壁的大屋 <span class="encs">[wallless hall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广，殿之大屋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:殿谓堂无四壁&hellip;覆乎上者曰屋，无四壁而上有大覆盖，其所通者宏远矣，是曰广。 <span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:&ldquo;堂无四壁者。秦谓之殿，所谓堂皇也，覆以大屋曰广。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子之堂广九雉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尚书大传》</span>。注:&ldquo;荣间相去也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居天下之广居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是求马于唐肆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;田子方》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:&ldquo;广庭也，以唐为之。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朱涂广，夷石为堂。(涂:粉饰。夷石:平整的石头做堂基)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉郊祀歌》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 宽度，横向尺寸 <span class="encs">[width]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广袤丰杀，一称心力。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《庐山草堂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今蛭广有分数，长有寸度。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;福虚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沈机长二丈，广八尺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;备城门》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即连楮为巨幅，广二丈，大书一&ldquo;屈&rdquo;字，以两竿夹揭之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前有平地，轮广十丈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《庐山草堂记》</span>。轮:南北距离</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:广运(宽广。东西距离为广，南北距离为运)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 广州的省称 <span class="encs">[Guangzhou (Canton)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杂彩三百匹，交</span><span class="diczx2">广</span>市鲑珍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:广交会;广疮(性病。此病似从国外经广州传入的，故称);广里(指广州)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 广东、广西两省的略称 <span class="encs">[Guang dong (Kwangtung) or Guangxi(Kwangsi)]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:两广;广花(颜料。广东产的花青);广云胶(胶名。产于广东，无臭味)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>

<p>◎ <strong>广</strong></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 广阔，宽阔;分布广;辽阔 <span class="encs">[wide;vast;extensive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广，阔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狄之广漠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公二十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地广而益重。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;西周策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者圣王为大川广谷之不可济，于是利为舟楫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;节用中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">平原广野，此车骑之地。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《言兵当疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汉亡广矣，不可泳思。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;汉广》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃为赋以自广。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原&middot;贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:广通(广泛传播);广交声气(多方交接意气相投的朋友);广平(宽阔平坦);广庄(宽阔的大道);广狭(宽广与狭窄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为大 <span class="encs">[huge;great]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四牡修广。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;六月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安得广厦千万间，大庇天下寒士俱欢颜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明道德之广崇，治乱之条贯，靡不毕见。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:广厦细旃(高大的房屋);广车(大车);广舶(大型远洋船);广肆(大店铺);广衢(大道);广眉(大眉，厚眉);广渊(弘大深远)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 盛大，以非常富丽、堂皇、丰盛或庄重为标志的 <span class="encs">[grand]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:广宴(盛宴);广筵(盛宴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 远大，将来要成就一番大事业的 <span class="encs">[far-reaching]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:君子贫穷而志广;才疏意广</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 多，众多，数目很多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自迎</span><span class="diczx2">赢</span>于众人广坐之中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竟敢在大庭广众之中，光天化日之下，横行不法，岂不怪哉</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:广众;广交(交友极多);广坐(众人聚会列坐的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 普通;广泛 <span class="encs">[general]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸可广问讯，不得便相许。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;旷&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;ng</span>)。荒废，耽搁 <span class="encs">[rusty;be out of practice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不譲事，不广闲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;大匡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:广广(荒废的状况);广土(荒废的土地)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5E7F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】魚檢切【韻會】疑檢切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>醃上聲。【說文】因广爲屋。象對刺高屋之形。【徐鉉曰】因广爲屋，故但一邊下。【增韻】棟頭曰广。【韓愈&middot;遊湘西寺詩】剖竹走泉源，開廊架屋广。</p>
<p>又【釋名】衾，广也。其下廣大，如广受人也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】魚掩切，音隒。義同。</p>
<p>（廣）【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古晃切，光上聲。【說文】殿之大屋也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】廣，大也。【廣韻】廣，闊也。【易&middot;繫辭】廣大配天地。【疏】大以配天，廣以配地。</p>
<p>又州名。【唐書&middot;地理志】嶺南道有廣州。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓譜】出丹陽，廣成子之後，宋有廣漢。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】古曠切，光去聲。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;大司徒】周知九州之地域廣輪之數。【疏】馬融云：東西爲廣，南北爲輪。【釋文】廣，古曠反。</p>
<p>又車名。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;車僕】廣車之萃。【註】廣車，橫<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>之車也。【釋文】廣，古曠反。【左傳&middot;僖二十八年】西廣東宮。【疏】楚有左右廣，蓋兵車之名。【宣二年】分爲二廣。【註】十五乗爲一廣。</p>
<p>又【正字通】音曠。【荀子&middot;王霸篇】人主胡不廣焉。【註】廣，開泰貌。</p>
<p>又與曠通。【前漢&middot;五行志】師出過時之謂廣。</p>
<p>又姑黃切，音光。【爾雅&middot;釋獸】回毛在背，闋廣。【疏】伯樂相馬法，旋毛在背者名闋廣。【音義】廣，音光。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶果五切，音古。【禮&middot;樂記】今夫古樂，進旅退旅，和正以廣，弦匏笙簧，會守拊鼓，始奏以文，復亂以武。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5E7F.svg">                          <p>因广爲屋，象對剌高屋之形。凡广之屬皆从广。讀若儼然之儼。魚儉切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5E7F_1712.svg" /><span class="swbf">(广)</span><span class="ywyy">因厂爲屋也。</span><span class="swdz">厂各本作广，誤，今正。厂者，山石之厓巖。因之爲屋，是曰广。廣韵<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2494E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤥎</span>儼二韵及昌黎集注皆作因巖，可證。因巖卽因厂也。</span><span class="ywyy">从厂，</span><span class="swdz">各本無此二字，今補。</span><span class="ywyy">象對刺高屋之形。</span><span class="swdz">刺各本作剌，今正。讀七亦切，謂對面高屋森聳上刺也。首畫象巖上有屋。</span><span class="ywyy">凡广之屬皆从广，讀若儼然之儼。</span><span class="swdz">魚儉切。八部。</span></p>                          </div>
