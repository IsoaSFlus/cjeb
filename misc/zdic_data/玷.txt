                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>玷</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">diàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>白玉上面的斑点，亦喻人的缺点、过失：小～（小过失）。～缺（人有缺点，如玉有斑点）。白圭之～（“<span class="diczx3">圭</span>”，玉器）。</li><li>使有污点：～污。～辱。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>玷</strong> <span class="dicpy">diàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:白玉上的斑点)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a flaw in a piece of jade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玷，玉瑕。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白圭之玷，尚可磨也。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“玷，缺也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不知其玷。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·召旻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">元成</span>复作自荐复玷缺之艰难。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·韦元成传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“玉缺曰玷。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:玷缺(白玉有斑点、缺损。比喻人的品德有缺点);玷翳(玉上斑痕)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为过失;缺点 <span class="encs">[defect]</span> 。如: 玷邮(缺点，过错);玷瑕(疵点;毛病);玷漏(过失)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 污点 <span class="encs">[stain]</span>。如: 玷污</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>玷</strong> <span class="dicpy">diàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 玷污，污辱 <span class="encs">[smear;stain]</span>。如:玷言玷语(污秽的言语);玷毛(濡笔);玷名(玷污名声);玷秽(玷污，污辱);玷染(污染;弄脏)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 谦辞。犹<span class="yhcs">“忝”</span>。屈辱 <span class="encs">[be an incompetent person]</span>。如:玷冒(忝冒。自谦之辞。滥竽充数)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/玷辱">玷辱</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/玷辱门庭">玷辱门庭</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/玷污">玷污</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">玷</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>玷</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄉㄧㄢˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>diàn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1205.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">玉上的瑕疵、斑点。</span><span class="gc_yy">《诗经·大雅·抑》：「白圭之<b>玷</b>，尚可磨也。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">缺点、过失。</span><span class="gc_yy">《诗经·大雅·抑》：「斯言之<b>玷</b>，不可为也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《喻世明言·卷二七·金玉奴棒打薄情郎》：「却拜个团头做岳丈，可不是终身之<b>玷</b>！」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">辱、弄污。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「玷污」、「有<b>玷</b>家声」。</span><span class="gc_yy">元·郑光祖《倩女离魂·第二折》：「你今私自赶来，有<b>玷</b>风化，是何道理？」</span><span class="gc_yy">清·蒋士铨《冬青树·第○出》：「惜朝内无人匡谏，以致成彼高名，<b>玷</b>俺君德。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">谦词。忝辱之意。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「久<b>玷</b>官职，又无政绩，实在汗颜！」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·杜甫〈春日江村〉诗五首之三：「岂知牙齿落，名<b>玷</b>荐贤中。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
