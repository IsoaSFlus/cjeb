						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>欲</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>想得到某种东西或想达到某种目的的要求：～念。～望。～火。食～。情～。禁～。纵～。</li>    <li>想要，希望：～盖弥彰。～罢不能。～速不达。</li>    <li>需要：胆～大而心～细。</li>    <li>将要：摇摇～坠。山雨～来风满楼。</li>    <li>婉顺的样子。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>欲</strong></p><p>慾 <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从欠，谷( <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> )声。<span class="yhcs">“欠”</span>表示有所不足，故产生欲望。本义:欲望;嗜欲) 同本义 <span class="encs">[desire;pleasure-seeking desire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲，贪欲也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗字亦作慾。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲者，情之应也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六欲。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·贵生》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“生死耳目口鼻也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以欲竭其精。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“乐色曰欲。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然则诸侯之地有限，暴</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之欲无厌。—— <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厉而不刚者，则欲夺之也。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘劭</span><span class="smcs">《人物志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:欲令智昏(欲火使人丧失理智);欲障(嗜欲);欲海(佛教语。情欲令人迷本性，沉沦于生死大海);欲界(佛教语。三界之一，在色界之下，包括欲天、人间和地狱等);欲心(情欲之心);欲事(男女情欲之事);欲尘(佛教语。佛家谓财、色、食、名、睡五欲污身如尘埃，故称)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>欲</strong> <span class="dicpy">yù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 想要;希望 <span class="encs">[want;wish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无欲速，无见小利。欲速，则不达。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子路》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲洁其身而乱大伦。——<span class="smcs">《论语·微子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋鄙</span>…曰:<span class="yhcs">“…今单车来代之，何如哉?”</span>欲无听。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孤岂欲卿治经为博士耶!——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·汉纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故人远我，欲以火器困我也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:欲踵(想要放效);欲待(想要、打算);欲人勿知，莫若勿为(想要别人不知道，最好自己不去做);欲速则不达(性急求快反而不能达到目的);欲益反损(想把事情做好，结果反而弄坏)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 爱好;喜爱 <span class="encs">[like]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲，爱也。——<span class="smcs">《增韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人情欲厚恶薄。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·案书》</span></span></p><p><span
