                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>爵</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jué  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄝˊ</span></p><ol><li>古代饮酒的器皿，三足，以不同的形状显示使用者的身份。</li><li>君主国家贵族封号（中国古代分为“<span class="diczx3">公</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">侯</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">伯</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">子</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">男</span>”五等）：～位。官～。～禄（爵位和俸禄）。～士。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">雀</span>”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> feudal title or rank</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Adelsfamilien, Adelsgeschlecht  (S)​,Adelsstand  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> titre de noblesse,rang</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">爵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>爵</strong> <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象古代饮酒器。本义:古代酒器。青铜制)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 盛酒的礼器，形似雀，青铜制，有流、两柱、三足，用以温酒或盛酒，盛行于殷代和西周初期 <span class="encs">[an ancient wine vessel with three legs and a loop handle]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">爵，礼器也。象爵之形，中有鬯酒，又持之也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">实二爵。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·特牲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">殷</span>以斝， <span class="diczx2">周</span>以爵。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·明堂位》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洗爵奠斝。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·行苇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">虢公</span>请器，王与之爵。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公二十一年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 爵位，爵号，官位 <span class="encs">[the rank of nobility;peerage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王者之制禄爵。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受爵不让。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·角弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请卖爵子。——引</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span
