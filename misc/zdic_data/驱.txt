                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>驱</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">驅</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩˉ</span></p><ol><li>赶牲口：～马。～策。～驰。</li><li>赶走：～动。～赶。～寒。</li><li>强行、逼迫：～使。～遣（ａ．强迫；ｂ．赶走；ｃ．消除，排除情绪）。</li><li>快跑：并驾齐～。先～。前～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> spur a horse on; expel, drive away</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ausweisen, ausschließen ,lenken, antreiben</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pousser,expulser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">驱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>驱</strong></p><p>驅、駈 <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，区声。本义:奔驰;疾行)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[run quickly;speed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驱，马驰也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驱，奔也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释室》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王用三驱。——<span class="smcs">《易·比》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驱马悠悠。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鄘风·载驰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">负而前驱。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新五代史·伶官传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">并驱如故。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:驱弛;驰驱(骑马快跑);长驱(迅速地向很远的目的地走);驱侵(驱车侵袭);驱劳(奔走辛劳);驱突(驱驰奔跑);驱骇(奔走惊骇)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 赶马 <span class="encs">[whip up;drive a horse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">并驱从两肩兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·齐风·还》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">并驱从两牡兮。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驱而之</span><span class="diczx2">薛</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策四》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">冯谖</span>先驱。</p><p><span class="diczx1">被驱不异犬与鸡。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闲驱</span><span class="diczx2">泾水</span>之涘。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:驱驱(策马快奔);驱马(策马奔驰);驱良(驱策良马);驱驽(驱策劣马);驱肥(驱策肥马);驱鑣(驱策奔驰);驱放(驱策奔驰)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 驱逐;赶走 <span class="encs">[drive
