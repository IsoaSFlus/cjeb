                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8526.svg">                          <p>寄生也。从艸鳥聲。《詩》曰：&ldquo;蔦與女蘿。&rdquo;樢，蔦或從木。都了切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8526_5696.svg" /><span class="swbf">(蔦)</span><span class="ywyy">寄生艸也。</span><span class="swdz">艸字各本脫，依毛詩音義及韵會補。小雅傳曰：蔦，寄生也。陸璣曰：蔦一名寄生，葉似當盧，子如覆盆子。本艸經：桑上寄生，一名寓木，一名宛童。按寓木、宛童見釋木。</span><span class="ywyy">从艸。</span><span class="swdz">毛、陸皆曰寄生耳，許獨云寄生艸者，爲其字之從艸也。</span><span class="ywyy">鳥聲。</span><span class="swdz">詩音義云：說文音弔。唐韵都了切。二部。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：蔦與女蘿。</span><span class="swdz">小雅頍弁文。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8526_5697.svg" /><span class="swbf">(樢)</span><span class="ywyy">蔦或從木。</span><span class="swdz">艸屬故從艸，寓木故從木，廣雅釋木作樢字。</span></p>                          </div>
