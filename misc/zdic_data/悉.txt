						<p>● <strong>悉</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>知道：洞～（很清楚地知道）。尽～。获～。来函敬～。</li>
	<li>尽，全：～力。～心。～数（<span class="dicpy">shǔ</span>）（完全列举，如&ldquo;不可～～&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>know, learn about, comprehend</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> wissen, kennen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> savoir,apprendre</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>悉</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>(会意。从心，从采。采（ <span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n</span>），辨别。心中加以辨别，很详细。本义:详尽) 同本义 <span class="encs">[detailed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悉，详尽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悉，尽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">占不悉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平准书》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;尽也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时上所问禽兽簿甚悉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张释之传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至孅至悉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">词不悉心。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纤悉(详细;详尽);详悉(详细而全面);悉数(一一列举)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>悉</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="encs">[一个个]</span>全都，<span class="encs">[一件件]</span>全部 <span class="encs">[all;entire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悉如外人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悉以咨之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悉吾村之众。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悉使羸兵负草填之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;赤壁之战》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:悉知底里(对事情的内情底细完全清楚);悉皆(全都);悉备(齐备)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>悉</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 详尽地叙述 <span class="encs">[elaborate;expound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">书不能悉意。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 详尽的知道，了解 <span class="encs">[know;learn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">文亦宜然，随时变改，难可详悉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">萧统</span><span class="smcs">《文选》</span>序</p>
