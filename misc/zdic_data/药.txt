                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">药</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>药</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">藥</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>可以治病的东西：～材。～物。补～。毒～。草～。中～。西～。～剂。～膳。～到病除。良～苦口。</li>
	<li>有一定作用的化学物品：火～。炸～。杀虫～。</li>
	<li>用药物救治：不可救～。</li>
	<li>毒死：～老鼠。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/约">约</a>&rdquo;，缠。</li>
	<li>草名，即&ldquo;白芷&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> leaf of Dahurian angelica plant; medicine</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Medikament  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> médicament</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">药</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>药</strong></p>
<p>藥 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，乐声。本义:治病的物品。药物;药材。一般是植物，故从艸)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[medicinal herbs;drug;medicament;remedy;medicine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">药，治病草也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以五味五谷五药养其病。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;疾医》</span>。注:&ldquo;五药，草木虫石谷也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勿药有喜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;无妄》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求仙人不死之药。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:药肆(药店);药贴(处方单);药封(用红纸袋封送的诊断费);药案(药方);药王(指治病用药如神的人);药引(中医处方中先选某种药物作为其他药物的导引，使药力可以达到病处，称为药引);药局(专司配药剂的机构;俗称出卖药品的部门为药局);药饵(药物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指某些有化学作用的物质 <span class="encs">[certain chemicals]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">药稍熔，则以一平板按其面，则字平如砥(药稍稍化开，此指松脂、腊等)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:药水龙(化学灭火器);药骰(在骰子中封入水银或铅，庄家可以任意操纵得到的点数，以欺骗押赌者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指火药 <span class="encs">[gunpowder;powder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡鸟铳长约三尺，铁管载药。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋应星</span><span class="smcs">《天工开物》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 芍药的简称 <span class="encs">[Chinese herbaceous peony]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:药蔓(芍药花的蔓藤);药栏(芍药之栏。泛指花栏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 泛指能使人改过迁善，有益身心的东西 <span class="encs">[good medicine]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:药石之言(金玉良言;劝人改过的话);药言(良药苦口之言)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>药</strong></p>
<p>藥 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用药治疗 <span class="encs">[cure with medicine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多将熇熇，不可救药。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;板》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不如吾闻而药之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语&middot;正论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼得之不足以药伤补败。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:药师(药工、医师之古称);药捻(带药的纸捻或纱布条，外科治疗时用来放入伤口或疮口内);药膳(配有中药做的菜肴或食品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 使中毒或毒杀 <span class="encs">[poison;kill with medicine]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:药老鼠;药死(毒死)</span></p>
