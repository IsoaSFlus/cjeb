                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>攘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">rǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄤˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>侵夺，偷窃：～羊（指暴露亲人的过失，语出《论语&middot;子路》中的&ldquo;其父～羊，而子证之&rdquo;）。～夺。～窃。～善（掠人之美）。</li>
	<li>推，排斥：～斥。～除。～夷（抵御外国侵略）。</li>
	<li>烦乱：～～（纷乱）。扰～。</li>
	<li>捋：～袂。～臂。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">攘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>攘</strong> <span class="dicpy">rǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，襄( <span class="dicpy">xiāng</span>)声。本义:推让，退让)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[politely and modestly decline;make a concession;give in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攘，推也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。谓推手使前拱揖之容也，字亦作儴。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左右攘辟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小子何敢攘焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;太史公自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">随流而攘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传》</span>。注:&ldquo;却退也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盛揖攘之容。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;礼乐志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">合于尧之克攘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;艺文志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 排斥，努力消去 <span class="encs">[reject]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攘，除也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攘夷狄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;僖公四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反离谤而见攘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;七谏&middot;沈江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攘白翟之地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攘弃仁义。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;胠箧》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:攘斥(排斥;驱除);攘抑(排除抑止);攘患(排除祸患);攘贪(排除贪欲);攘讨(驱除讨伐);攘却(驱除);攘夷(攘狄。抗拒异族入侵);攘狱(拒捕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既从竖而横构，亦风除而雨攘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《郊居赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 侵略 <span class="encs">[invade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸侯暴乱，擅相攘伐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;渔父》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南夷相攘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;严助传》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓相侵夺也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:攘伐(侵夺交战);攘肌及骨(比喻侵及腹地);攘争(争夺);攘服(征服);攘杀(掠夺毁坏);攘掊(掠夺搜括);攘略(侵夺);攘剽(掠夺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 偷，盗窃 <span class="encs">[steal]</span></p>
