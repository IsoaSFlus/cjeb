                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">办</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>办</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">辦</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>处理：～公。～事。～理。</li>
	<li>处分：惩～。法～。首恶必～。</li>
	<li>置备：～置。～货。</li>
	<li>创设：创～。兴（<span class="dicpy">xīng</span>）～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> manage, do, handle; deal with</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> handeln, erledigen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> régler,s'occuper de,se débrouiller,établir,créer,préparer,punir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">办</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>办</strong></p>
<p>辦 <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，辡声。力取致力之意。本义:办理;治理)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[handle;manage;do]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">办，致力也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿能办之者诚决。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:办案;办事;办理</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 做，干 <span class="encs">[do]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾已无事可办，惟待死期耳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怎么办;群众发动起来了，事情就好办了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 创设，经营，管理 <span class="encs">[found;run]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是年，</span><span class="diczx2">谢庄</span>办团。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:办工厂;办学校;办报;办卖(变卖)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 准备，操办 <span class="encs">[prepare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">船、粮、战具俱办。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:办酒(备办酒席);办丧事</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 惩罚 <span class="encs">[punish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君信可人，必能办贼者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;费祎传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:办罪;严办;首恶必办</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 做成;做好;能做到 <span class="encs">[accomplish;achieve]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食未为而</span><span class="diczx2">景仁</span>为 <span class="diczx2">玄</span>所召。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南史&middot;谢裕传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">趋为诸将军办装。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;彭宠传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>办</strong></p>
<p>辦 <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>&ldquo;办公室&rdquo;的简称 <span class="encs">[office]</span>。</p>
