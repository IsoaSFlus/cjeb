                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>畏</strong> <span class="dicpy">wèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。据甲骨文意思是鬼手拿杖打人，使人害怕。本义:害怕)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(对威势、威严的害怕) <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">畏，惧也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂二》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">畏，恐也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不畏不怒。——<span class="smcs">《列子·黄帝》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天明畏。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋陶谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以小事大者，畏天者也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公子畏死邪?何泣也?——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妾之美我者，畏我也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然畏愬。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忧谗畏讥。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巍巍乎可畏。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">畏其唇吻。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:畏威(畏惧权势);畏威怀德(害怕他的威严，怀念他的恩德);畏之如虎(像怕老虎那样害怕);畏天知命(谓知天命，识时务);畏死贪生(害怕死亡，贪恋生存);畏戒(畏惧戒惕);畏附(畏惧依附)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 敬;敬服 <span class="encs">[respect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心畏相公。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:畏天恤民(敬畏上天，爱怜下民);畏天悯人(敬畏天命，悯惜世人的困苦);畏伏(敬服);畏俯(犹敬服);畏敬(尊敬;敬重);畏钦(尊敬钦佩);畏爱(敬佩爱戴);畏羡(敬重羡慕)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 吓唬 <span class="encs">[frighten]</span>。如:畏语(指恫吓他人的言语)</p><p><span
