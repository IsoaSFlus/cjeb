                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">席</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>席</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xí.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>用草或苇子编成的成片的东西，古人用以坐、卧，现通常用来铺床或炕等：～子。草～。苇～。竹～。凉～。～地而坐。～卷（<span class="dicpy">juǎn </span>）。</li><li>座位：～位。～次。出～。列～。</li><li>酒筵，成桌的饭菜：筵～。宴～。酒～。</li><li>特指议会中当选的人数：四～。</li><li>职位：主～。西～（塾师）。</li><li>量词：一～酒。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">席</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>席</strong></p><p>蓆 <span class="dicpy">xí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (<span class="yhcs">“蓆”</span>的古字。会意。从巾，庶省声。天子诸侯的席有刺绣镶边，故从巾。席用来待广大宾客，故从<span class="yhcs">“庶”</span>省。本义:供坐卧铺垫的用具。后又曾写作<span class="yhcs">“蓆”</span>) 同本义 <span class="encs">[mat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">席，藉也。礼天子诸侯席有黼绣纯饰。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，即筵也。方幅如巾，故从巾。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设莞筵纷纯，加缫，席画纯。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司几筵》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“初在地者一重，谓之筵。重在上者，即谓之席。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设之曰筵，坐之曰席。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·祭统》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我心匪席，不可卷也。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·柏舟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">结发同枕席。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">觉时之枕席。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">席地而卧。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">席地倚墙。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:席包(以苇席或高粱篾席制的包);席具(铺垫用具);席户(悬席为户。比喻房子的简陋);席号(以草席搭建的临时棚屋);席荐(席子与草荐，都是草编的坐卧用具)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>席</strong> <span class="dicpy">xí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 座位;席位 <span class="encs">[place;seat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">席而无上下，则乱于席次矣。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">变色离席。(大惊失色离开座位)。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:席舍(贡院中的试场座位、考舍);席尊(首席);上席;下席;硬席;专席;来宾席;被告席</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 成桌的饭菜，酒筵 <span class="encs">[banquet;feast]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饮酒酣，</span><span class="diczx2">武安</span>起为寿，坐皆避席优。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏其武安侯列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所赐者兴，受爵，降席下，奠爵，再拜稽首。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·燕礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“席下，席西也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
