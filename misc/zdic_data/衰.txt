                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>衰</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代用粗麻布制成的毛边丧服 <span class="encs">[mourning garments of hemp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共丧衰亦如之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;内司服》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无衰麻之服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子墨</span>衰緻， <span class="diczx2">梁弘</span>御戎， <span class="diczx2">莱驹</span>为右。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衰衣(衰服。古代的丧服);衰杖(居丧用的麻緻与哭丧棒);衰冠(丧服，衰衣丧冠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 下，从上到下的次序 <span class="encs">[order from top downward]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:衰杀(等差);衰构(分门别类构拟文辞)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>衰</strong> <span class="dicpy">cuī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 由大到小依照一定的标准递减 <span class="encs">[decrease progressively]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:衰分(古代数学名词。谓从大渐差而小);衰序(衰次。按一定比数递减的次序);衰征(衰政。视土地之差等以征税)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 减少 <span class="encs">[reduce]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:衰少(减少);衰乏(减少缺乏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">shuāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>衰</strong> <span class="dicpy">shuāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从衣，从冄( <span class="dicpy">rǎn</span>)。&ldquo;冄&rdquo;象草毛茸茸的样子。是&ldquo;蓑衣&rdquo;的&ldquo;蓑&rdquo;的本字。（ <span class="dicpy">suǒ</span>）本义:蓑衣。)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 力量减退，衰落，没落。与&ldquo;盛&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[decline;wane]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衰则气复反入。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;疟论》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓病衰退也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一鼓作气，再而衰，三而竭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">呜呼!盛衰之理，虽曰天命，岂非人事哉!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新五代史&middot;伶官传&middot;序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衰末(衰落微末);衰旺(衰落与旺盛);衰季(衰微末世);衰废(衰败颓废);衰宗(殁落残败的宗族);衰门(寒门);衰薄(颓废浇薄。多指道德风尚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 减少;削弱 <span class="encs">[fail;decline]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其周德之衰乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十九年》</span>。注:&ldquo;小也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔周道衰微。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传序》</span>。疏:&ldquo;谓衰弱陵迟。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余幼好此奇服兮，年既老而不衰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日食饮得无衰乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:风势渐衰;衰颓(衰败);衰止(衰减而止息);衰乏(衰退疲乏);衰色(指姿色衰减的女子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 枯萎;凋谢 <span class="encs">[wither]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明朝风起应吹尽，夜惜衰红把火看。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《惜牡丹花》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是处红衰翠减，苒苒物华休。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《八声甘州&middot;对潇潇雨洒江天》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常恐秋节至焜黄华叶衰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗辞&middot;长歌行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衰红(凋谢的花);衰条(枯枝);衰黄(枯黄)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>衰</strong> <span class="dicpy">shuāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 衰老 <span class="encs">[old and feeble;decrepit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">年衰志悯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;主术》</span>。注:&ldquo;老也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人之老也形盖衰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;去宥》</span>。注:&ldquo;肌肤消也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而臣衰，窃爱怜之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;战策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其无宿根者，即候苗成而未有花时采，则根生已足而又未衰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衰颜(衰老的容颜);衰暮(老年;晚年);衰冗(衰老冗废);衰年(衰老之年);衰枯(衰老干枯);衰耄(衰老;年老糊涂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 人老时鬓发疏落变白 <span class="encs">[greying]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塞上长城空自许，镜中衰鬓已先斑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《剑南诗稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衰白(人老时鬓发疏落变白);衰鬓(年老而疏白的鬓发。多指暮年)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 衰弱 <span class="encs">[weak;feeble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老妪力虽衰，请从吏夜归。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衰癃(衰弱多病);衰迈(衰弱老迈);衰困(衰弱困顿);衰窿(衰弱抱病)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 懈怠 <span class="encs">[slack;sluggish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">年既老而不衰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;涉江》</span>。注:&ldquo;懈也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衰葸(懈怠畏惧);衰惰(怠惰);衰堕(懈怠)</span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8870_6012.svg" /><span class="swbf">(衰)</span><span class="ywyy">艸雨衣。</span><span class="swdz">雨衣有不艸者。左傳：成子衣製杖戈。杜曰：製，雨衣。按言製則非艸爲，若今油布衣。</span><span class="ywyy">秦謂之萆。</span><span class="swdz">艸部曰：萆，雨衣，一曰衰衣。此則著萆爲秦語也。小雅：何蓑何笠。傳曰：蓑所以僃雨，笠所以禦暑。公羊傳：不蓑城也。何云：若今以艸衣城。齊語注云：襏襫，蓑襞衣也。襞或萆字，亦作薜。六韜：蓑薜䔲笠，衰俗从艸作蓑。而衰遂專爲等衰，衰絰字。衰絰本作縗，衰其假借字也。以艸爲雨衣，必層次編之。故引伸爲等衰，後世異其形，異其音。古義茫昧矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从衣象形。</span><span class="swdz">謂<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2033A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠌺</span>也。穌禾切。十七部。</span></p>
