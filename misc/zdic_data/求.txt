                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>求</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄡˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>设法得到：～生。～成。～知。～索。～证（寻求证据，求得证实）。～实（讲求实际）。～同存异。～全责备。～贤若渴。实事～是。</li>
	<li>恳请，乞助：～人。～告。～乞。～医。～教。～助。</li>
	<li>需要：需～。供过于～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> seek; demand, request; beseech</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> bitten für, fragen für, suchen, verlangen nach  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> demander,implorer,supplier,rechercher</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">求</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>求</strong></p>
<p>裘 <span class="dicpy">qi&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>以&ldquo;求&rdquo;为&ldquo;裘&rdquo;之古文。省衣，象形。金文与战国古文作&ldquo;求&rdquo;。本是&ldquo;裘衣&rdquo;的&ldquo;裘&rdquo;。古人皮衣一般毛朝外，所以甲骨文在&ldquo;衣&rdquo;字外加毛。表示裘衣。本义:皮衣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &ldquo;裘&rdquo;的古字。皮衣 <span class="encs">[fur coat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乐正求。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>求</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 请求;干请;乞助 <span class="encs">[ask;beg;request]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求，乞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《增韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因留</span><span class="diczx2">怀王</span>以求割地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求救于</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有求于我也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求其能千里也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求救于</span><span class="diczx2">孙</span>将军。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:求浼(请求;央求);求神福(祈求神灵保佑;祝福);求张良，拜韩信(到处求人;求托有用的人);求化(乞求人施舍财物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 追求，谋求;寻求;寻找 <span class="encs">[strive for;seek]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求，索也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求则得之，舍则失之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入水求之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求剑若此。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求人可使报</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不求闻达。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求利以污其行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求钱之民。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以求钱也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以求重价。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求太监后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《洹词&middot;记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:求代(迷信指吊死鬼诱人当&ldquo;替死鬼&rdquo;，以求投胎托生);力求(极力追求;尽力谋求);求友(寻求朋友);求正(寻求正道);求合(寻求志同道合者);求采(搜求选取);求觅(寻找);求访(寻觅探访)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 索取 <span class="encs">[ask for;demand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我之求也，此何罪?请杀我乎!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公十六年》</span></span></p>
