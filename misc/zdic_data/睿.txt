						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>睿</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ru&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ru&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>深明，通达：～智。～哲（明智，英明）。</li>
	<li>古代颂扬帝王用语：～旨。～览（御览）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> shrewd, astute, clever, keen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> weit vorausschauend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> perspicace</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>睿</strong></p>
<p>叡 <span class="dicpy">ru&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ru&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p> (会意。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo; 从目，从谷省。空虚的山洼，有畅通义。&lsquo;目&rsquo;的作用是明察。合起来表示&lsquo;明智&rsquo;、&lsquo;智慧&rsquo;&rdquo;。本义:通达;明智) 同本义 <span class="encs">[wise and farsighted]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叡，深明也，通也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">睿，智也，明也，圣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子羽睿敏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">睿而为愚者也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《愚溪诗序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德幼而叡齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;五帝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:睿圣(聪明通达，明晓事理);睿日(神圣的太阳);睿化(圣明的教化);睿祖(神圣的祖先);睿姿(指圣明的资质);睿思(圣明的思虑);睿性(圣明的天性);睿岳(比喻深广的恩德);睿明(智慧;明智)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>睿</strong> <span class="dicpy">ru&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ru&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古时臣下对君王、后妃等所用的敬词 <span class="encs">[emperor]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:睿鉴(请求帝王明察指示);睿藻(对帝王所作诗文的称颂用语);睿文(指皇帝的文德);睿幄(指皇宫;朝廷);睿图(皇帝的谋划);睿断(皇帝的决定);睿谟(皇帝圣明的谋略);睿谋(指皇帝的谋划);睿德(皇帝的恩德)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 特指孔子 <span class="encs">[Confucius]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:睿图(指孔子的画像)</span></p>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/53E1.svg">                          <p>深明也。通也。从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239BC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣦼</span>从目，从谷省。睿，古文叡。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/214DD.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡓝</span>，籀文叡从土。以芮切〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B89.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠮉</span>。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/53E1_691.svg" /><span class="swbf">(叡)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2594D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥥍</span>明也。</span><span class="swdz">鉉本此下有通也二字，雖合古訓，然恐俗增。馬注尚書、鄭注尚書大傳皆曰：睿，通也。許則於叡曰深明也，於聖曰通也。叡從目，故曰明。聖從耳，故曰通。此許意也。周書謚法解曰：叡，聖也。邶風毛傳曰：聖，叡也。古文尚書睿作聖，故周書、毛傳叡、聖互訓。楚語：謂之睿聖武公。韋曰：睿，明也。按韋但曰明，許曰深明者，許主解字，爲其字之從<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239BC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣦼</span>也。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239BC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣦼</span>从目。</span><span class="swdz">故曰深明。</span><span class="ywyy">从谷省。</span><span class="swdz">谷以皃其深也。按叡實從㕡省。從㕡者，皃其能容也。能容而後能明。古文尚書：思曰睿。今文尚書：思心曰容。義實相成也。許不云從㕡者，不立㕡部也。五經文字曰：易作叡。是可證尚書作睿也。以芮切。十五部。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/53E1_692.svg" /><span class="swbf">(睿)</span><span class="ywyy">古文叡。</span><span class="swdz">見古文尚書。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239B5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣦵</span>者，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239BC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣦼</span>之省。䜭下曰：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239B5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣦵</span>，殘也。按殘者謂殘穿。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/53E1_693.svg" /><span class="swbf">(壡)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文叡。从土。</span><span class="swdz">此亦從㕡省也。玉部籒文璿從此。</span></p>                          </div>
