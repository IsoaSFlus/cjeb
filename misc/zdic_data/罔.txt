                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>罔</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄤˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>蒙蔽，诬：～民（陷害人民）。～己（受人诳骗）。欺～。</li>	<li>无，没有：～极（①没有定准，变化无常；②无穷，久远）。置若～闻。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/惘">惘</a>&rdquo;，失意。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/网">网</a>&rdquo;，用绳线等结成的捕鱼捉鸟器具。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> net; deceive; libel; negative</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> täuschen, jn. hinters Licht führen  (V)​,Wang  (Eig, Fam)​,kein; ohne; nicht</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> filet,ne pas,en vain,tromper</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>罔</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从网，亡声。本义:渔猎用的网)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。同&ldquo;网&rdquo; <span class="encs">[net]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">伏羲</span>]</span>作结绳而为罔罟，以佃以渔，盖取诸离。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">死于罔罟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">列卒满泽，罘罔弥山。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是罔民也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罔民而可为也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:罔置(泛指捕猎的网具);罔罟(渔猎时所用的罗网);罔罗(渔猎所用的网。同罗网)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 法网 <span class="encs">[the net of justice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天之降罔，维其优矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>罔</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 迷惑。失意。通&ldquo;惘&rdquo; <span class="encs">[puzzle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学而不思则罔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;为政》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罔流涕以聊虑兮，惟著意而得之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;宋玉&middot;九辩》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罔然若酲，朝罢夕倦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张衡&middot;东京赋》</span></span></p>
