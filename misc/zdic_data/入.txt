                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>入</strong> <span class="dicpy">rù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象个尖头器具，尖头器具容易进入。本义:进来，进去)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[enter;come into]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入，内也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">他人入室。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·山有枢》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">就不欲入。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·人间世》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有蛇自泉宫出，入于国。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君为我呼入，吾得兄事之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>有执长竿入城门者。—— <span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:入门问讳(到别人家里，先需了解人家先祖名讳，以便谈话);入对(进宫回答皇帝的问题);入览(看到);入迁(从外地迁到京城做官);入学(童生考取秀才);入山;入口;入帘(科举时期，考官进场阅卷)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 参加， 加入 <span class="encs">[join;be a dmitted to;become a member of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">室人入又。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·宾之初筵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自四卿入军机，然后皇上与</span><span class="diczx2">康先生</span>之意始能少通。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:入舍布袋(上门女婿);入舍女婿(入舍);入释(参加佛教;进入佛门);入舍(做上门女婿);入脚(进身;指搭上关系);入队;入伙;入金马、登玉堂(考进翰林院。金马、玉堂指汉代的金马门和玉堂殿);入局(加入赌局);入团</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 交、交纳 <span class="encs">[pay]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入其社稷之臣于</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岁恶不入，请卖爵子。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:入粟(交纳一定数目的钱捐取功名)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 接纳;采纳 <span class="encs">[accept]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡嫁子娶妻，入币纯帛，无过五两。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·地官·媒氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其臣箴谏以不入。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">野人莫敢入王。——<span class="smcs">《史记·楚世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">商君</span>亡 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>归 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>， <span class="diczx2">魏</span>怒不入。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时上颇厌兵，入其言。——</span><span class="diczx2">罗大经</span><span class="smcs">《鹤林玉露》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 与…相适应 <span class="encs">[conform to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曲直之不相入。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·主术》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“中也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:入式(合乎程式);入道;入彀</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 入朝，指属国、外国使臣或地方官员谒见天子 <span class="encs">[go to court]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">强国请服，弱国入朝。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">项伯</span>即入见 <span
