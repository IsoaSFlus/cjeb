                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>医</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">醫</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>治病：～疗。～术。～务。～道。</li>	<li>治病的人：～士。～生。～德。</li>	<li>治病的科学：西～。中～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cure, heal; doctor, medical</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Arzt, Doktor  (S, Med)​,Medizin  (S, Med)​,behandeln  (V, Med)​,heilen  (V, Med)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> médecine,médecin,docteur,traiter (une maladie)​,soigner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">医</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>医</strong></p>
<p>醫、毉 <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从匚( <span class="dicpy">fāng</span>)，从矢，矢亦声。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;盛弓弩矢器也。&rdquo;&ldquo;醫&rdquo;，会意，从&ldquo;殹&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> )，从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)。&ldquo;殹&rdquo;，治病时的扣击声。&ldquo;酉&rdquo;，用以医疗的酒。二字各有本义，今用&ldquo;医&rdquo;为简体字。本义:治病的人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[doctor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">医，治 病工也。殹，恶姿也。醫之性得酒而使。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将免者以告，公令医守之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;越语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可以作巫医。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">譬之若良医。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驰召医。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又无医药。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:医国(指良医);医家有割股之心(喻医生有治病救人的虔诚愿望);缺医少药;医家(医生，医学家);医师(官名。为众医之长);医婆(女医师);医人(医生);医工(原为官名，指医官。后泛指一般医生);医流(医生，医家);医算(医生和卜人);医巫(治病的人。古代医生往往兼用巫术治病，故称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 医术;医学 <span class="encs">[medicine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原来尊夫人懂得医理，明日就请来看看罢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《二十年目睹之怪现状》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:医手(医术高明者);行医;学医;医王(医术极精的人。多用以比喻诸佛或高僧等);医部(医学书籍);医理(医学道理或理论);医道(医术)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 粥加曲蘖酿成的甜酒 <span class="encs">[sweet wine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:凉医(凉甜酒)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>医</strong></p>
<p>醫 <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 治疗，治愈 <span class="encs">[cure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚毒药以共医事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:医药局(集防疫、治疗、司药为一体的医疗机构);医缘(迷信的人认为疾病好坏与医药有命定的关系，称作医缘);医济(医治)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 救治 <span class="encs">[treat and cure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上医医国，其次疾人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:医国(比喻救治国家)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=匸" target="_blank">匸</a>】
                              医·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=7" target="_blank">7</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/533B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】于計切【集韻】【韻會】壹計切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音翳。【說文】盛弓弩矢器。从匸，从矢亦聲。【齊語】兵不解医。</p><p>又【玉篇】所以蔽矢也。或作翳。</p><p>（醫）【唐韻】【韻會】於其切【正韻】於宜切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音翳。【說文】治病工也。【禮&middot;曲禮】醫不三世，不服其藥。【史記&middot;扁鵲傳】爲醫或在齊，或在趙。</p><p>又官名。【周禮&middot;天官】醫師掌醫之政令，聚毒藥以供醫事。註醫師，衆醫之長也。【後漢&middot;百官志】太醫令一人六百石。【註】掌諸醫。</p><p>又蟲名。【崔豹&middot;古今注】蝘蜓，一名蛇醫。</p><p>又【集韻】或作毉。【後漢&middot;郭玉傳】毉之爲言意也。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>隱綺切，音倚。【韻會】飮也。【五音集韻】梅漿也。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;酒正】辨四飮之物，二曰醫。【註】醴濁釀酏爲之則少淸矣。</p><p>又【集韻】或作醷，亦作臆。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;酒正註】鄭司農說，《內則》漿水臆，醫與臆音亦相似，文字不同，記之者各異耳。此皆一物。【釋文】醷，本又作臆。</p><p>又【集韻】壹計切，音医。【周禮&middot;六飮】一曰醫。徐仙民讀。</p><p>（醫）</p>
