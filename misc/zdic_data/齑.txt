						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>齑</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">齏</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>捣碎的姜、蒜、韭菜等。</li>    <li>细，碎：～粉。～音（细碎的声音）。</li></ol>
