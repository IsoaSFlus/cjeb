                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>厂</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">厰</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄤˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>工厂：机械～。造纸～。纱～。</li>	<li>有空地可以存货或进行加工的地方：煤～。</li>	<li>棚舍：&ldquo;枳篱茅～共桑麻。&rdquo;</li>	<li>中国明代为加强专制统治而设的特务机关：东～。西～。</li></ol><p>● <strong>厂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄢˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>山边岩石突出覆盖处，人可居住的地方。</li></ol><p>● <strong>厂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yăn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yăn.mp3"></a></span>  〡ㄢˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/䉷">䉷</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol><p>● <strong>厂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄢˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/庵">庵</a>&rdquo;，多用于人名。</li></ol>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，敞声。从&ldquo;广&rdquo;，表示与房屋有关。本义:棚舍)</p>

<p><span class="diczx3">如:厂臣(明东厂、西厂的主官);厂狱(指明代东厂、西厂囚禁犯人的牢狱)</span></p>
