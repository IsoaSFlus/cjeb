						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>崩</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>倒塌：～塌。～坍。～毁。～解（<span class="dicpy">jiě</span>）。～溃。～决。～颓。分～离析。</li><li>破裂：～裂。把气球吹～了。</li><li>崩裂的东西击中：放爆竹～了手。</li><li>败坏：礼坏乐（<span class="dicpy">yuè</span>）～。</li><li>称枪毙：拉出去～了。</li><li>君主时代称帝王死：驾～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">崩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>崩</strong> <span class="dicpy">bēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，朋声。本义:山倒塌)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[landslide;landslip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">梁山</span>崩。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公五年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:山崩地裂</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 崩裂;倒塌 <span class="encs">[collapse;crash to the ground;burst apart]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中间力拉崩倒之声，火爆声，呼呼风声，百千齐作。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:崩拆(倒塌断裂);崩陷(倒塌陷落);崩陨(塌陷);崩损(崩塌损坏);崩坠(倒塌坠落)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代把天子的死看得很重，常用山塌下来比喻，由此从周代开始帝王死称<span class="yhcs">“崩”</span> <span class="encs">[death of an emperor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故临崩寄臣以大事也。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越二月，帝崩。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:崩驾(帝王之死);崩殂(崩背，崩逝。又指帝王之死)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 崩溃;垮台;败坏 <span class="encs">[collapse;fall in ruin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不义不昵，厚将崩。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三年不为乐，乐必崩。——<span class="smcs">《论语·阳货》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:崩阙(败坏);崩动(煽动败坏)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 破裂，迸裂 <span class="encs">[break;down burst]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天崩地塌壮士死。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴士玉</span><span class="smcs">《玉带生歌奉和漫堂先生》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:谈崩了;崩裂(物体突然破裂);崩云(破裂的云彩);把气球吹崩了</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 炸伤;枪毙 <span class="encs">[go off in;hit by shooting]</span>。如:爆竹崩了他的手;咋不崩了他?</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 血崩，指妇科<span class="yhcs">“崩症”</span> <span
