                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>亏</strong></p><p>虧 <span class="dicpy">kuī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从亏，( <span class="dicpy">hū</span>)声。本义:气损)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[damage of vital energy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亏，气损也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“引申凡损皆曰亏。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:亏柔(犹虚弱);亏退(衰退止息)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 欠缺，短少(应该有的而缺少)。与<span class="yhcs">“盈”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“满”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[be short of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽监门之服养不亏于此。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为山九仞，巧亏一篑。——<span class="smcs">《书·旅獒》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日极则仄，月满则亏。——<span class="smcs">《管子·白心》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:亏失(缺失);亏欠(欠了人情);亏累(债务;亏损);亏紊(亏缺紊乱);亏替(亏缺衰败)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 损耗，损害 <span class="encs">[loss;wear and tear;damage;harm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贿</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>而力亏。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《权书·六国论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:亏缺(亏空);亏污(亏损沾污);亏误(失误);亏输(失败;损失)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 输 <span class="encs">[lose;be defeated]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请与对弈，互有盈亏。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 毁坏 <span class="encs">[ruin;destroy;damage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不亏不崩。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·閟宫》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:亏污(亏损污染);亏害(损害);亏损(亏待，损害);亏图(图谋损害);亏名(损害名誉)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 减损;减少 <span class="encs">[reduce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厚者亏之，薄者靡之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·扬权》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:亏成(缺损与完满;失败与成功);亏全(缺损与完满)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 违背 <span class="encs">[violate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有善於前，有过於后，不为亏法。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·赏刑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 又如:亏负(亏待;做对不起他人的事);亏心事(违背良心的事;问心有愧的事);亏制(违制。放宽法度)</p><p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 相异;不适应 <span class="encs">[differ from]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其时已与先王之法亏矣。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 损失，丧失 <span class="encs">[loss]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恩爱苟不亏，在远分日亲。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《赠白马王彪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 又如:吃亏;使出版家受亏;自负盈亏</p><p><span class="cino">(18)</span> 月相从全月到新月的时期 <span class="encs">[wane]</span></p><p><span
