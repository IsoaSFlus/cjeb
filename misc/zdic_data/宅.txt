						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>宅</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&aacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄞˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>住所，房子（多指较大的）：住～。内～（指住宅内女眷的住处）。～门。～邸。～第。</li>
	<li>葬地，墓穴：～兆（坟墓的四界）。</li>
	<li>居住：～心仁厚（居心仁义厚道）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> residence, dwelling, home; grave</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Haus, Wohnung  (S, Arch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> résidence,demeure,(fam.)​ rester enfermé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">宅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>宅</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span>)，乇( <span class="dicpy">zh&eacute;</span>)声。本义:住所;住处)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[residence;house]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宅，所托居也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">小徐</span>本<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上以厚，下安宅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;象传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五亩之宅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">便利田宅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">方宅十余亩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">岐王</span>宅里寻常见。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江南逢李龟年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">住近湓江地低湿，黄芦苦竹绕宅生。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卜宅(卜择住处建都地或葬地);宅司(宅子;宅院);宅券(房契);宅相(住宅风水之相);宅神(住宅里的神鬼);宅屋(住房)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 家，常指大家族的家 <span class="encs">[family]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当日</span><span class="diczx2">宁</span><span class="diczx2">荣</span>两宅，人口也极多，如何便萧索了呢?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:宅库(私家财库);宅上(犹府上。敬称对方的家);宅家(唐代对皇帝的一种称呼。也称官家);宅田(古时官员告老还家，公家给以养老的禄田);宅眷(家眷;家属。多指女眷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 葬地 <span class="encs">[graveyard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大夫卜宅与葬日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;杂记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:宅兆(墓地)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>宅</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 居，居住 <span class="encs">[reside]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我王来，既爰宅于兹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:宅居(居住);宅土(居住在平地上;所居住的土地);宅者(古代指退休家居的官员);宅道(所居之界域);宅中图大(居于中心，谋划四方。谓得地势之利)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 居于;处于某种境地 <span class="encs">[be at]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使宅百揆。(使它居统领百官的位置。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王宅忧，亮阴三祀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;说命上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:宅忧(处于父母丧事期间)</span></p>
