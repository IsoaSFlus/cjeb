                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">拓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>拓</strong></p>
<p>搨 <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 拓印 <span class="encs">[make rubbings from inscriptions， pictures， etc. on stone tablets or bronze vessels]</span>。在刻铸有文字或图像的器物上蒙一层纸，捶打后使凹凸分明，涂上墨，显出文字图像来</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其相承传拓之本，犹在秘府。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《隋书&middot;经籍志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拓工(拓印的工艺);拓手(摹印石碑上文字、图画的技艺);拓本(拓片装订成的本子);拓印(摹印石碑或器物上的文字、图画);拓墨(拓取用的墨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 以纸片依样制作 <span class="encs">[copy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新拓来的鞋样子的鞋，刚刚上脚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">丁玲</span><span class="smcs">《母亲》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 贴 <span class="encs">[stick on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">就是前日被瘟官打的棒疮，在暗地狱里讨个烂膏药拓了。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张南庄</span><span class="smcs">《何典》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 涂抹 <span class="encs">[daub;smear;paint]</span></p>
