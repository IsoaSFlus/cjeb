						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>巧</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄠˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>技能好，灵敏：～妙。～思。～劲。～干（<span class="dicpy">gàn </span>）。精～。轻～。乖～。心灵手～。～发奇中。</li>    <li>美好：～笑（指美好的笑貌）。</li>    <li>虚伪（特指语言）：～言。～诈。～辩。～言令色。</li>    <li>恰好：～合。～遇。恰～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">巧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>巧</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从工，丂( <span class="dicpy">kǎo</span>)声。<span class="yhcs">“工”</span>有精密、灵巧义。本义:技艺高明、精巧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[skillful;ingenious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巧，技也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">利于人，谓之巧。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·贵义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刻雕众形，而不为巧。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">工人巧士。——</span><span class="diczx2">马融</span><span class="smcs">《长笛赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">明</span>有奇巧人曰 <span class="diczx2">王叔远</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:巧思(精巧的构思、设计);巧工(技艺高超的工匠);巧任(技巧，技能);精巧;工巧(能很快发明的创造力量和品质);巧夺天孙(比喻比织女更巧);巧舌(灵巧的舌头);巧便(灵便;灵巧);小巧(小而灵巧);手巧(手灵巧，手艺高)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 机巧 <span class="encs">[adroit;ingenious]</span>。如:巧诈(机巧诈伪);巧谲(机巧诡诈);巧心(机巧之心);巧佞(奸诈机巧);巧媚(机灵而善谄媚)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 巧妙;精妙 <span class="encs">[artistic;smart;adept]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巧者有余。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牙机巧制。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">明</span>有奇巧人曰 <span class="diczx2">王叔远</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:巧招(巧妙的计策或手段);巧劲(巧妙的手法);巧法(巧妙的方法)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 美好;美丽 <span class="encs">[fine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巧言如流。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·雨无止》</span>。笺:<span class="yhcs">“犹善也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为奇巧声。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林·雅量》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 如:巧笑(美好的笑容);巧丽(美妙华丽);巧媚(形容姿态美好);巧地方</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 虚浮不实，伪诈 <span class="encs">[false;cunning;deceitful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余犹恶其佻巧。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“利也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋或作为淫巧。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓奢伪怪好也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">对人前巧语花言。——</span><span class="diczx2">王实甫</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绝巧弃利。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。注:<span
