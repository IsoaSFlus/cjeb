                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">断</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>断</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">斷</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>长形的东西从中间分开：～裂。～层。～面。截～。～肠。～魂。～线风筝。</li>
	<li>不继续，禁绝：～粮。～水。～炊。～奶。～档。～流。～种（<span class="dicpy">zh&oacute;ng </span>）。～交。～片。～续。～子绝孙。</li>
	<li>判定，决定：判～。诊～。～狱（审理和判决罪案）。</li>
	<li>一定，绝对：～乎不可。～然施行。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>sever, cut off; interrupt</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> absolut ,entschieden ,zerbrechen, abbrechen, aufgeben, unterlassen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> certainement,couper,casser,rompre,interrompre,renoncer à,décider</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">断</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>断</strong></p>
<p>斷 <span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。本义:截断，截开)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cut off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">断，截也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">断木为杵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三日断五匹，大人故嫌迟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">公孙弘</span>断发而为 <span class="diczx2">越王</span>骑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说林下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是断手而续以玉也，故世有易身之患。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;用人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:断梗(断枝。比喻飘泊无定;指微贱的东西);断金(同心协力，坚固难移);断袖(旧时指男宠;比喻友情深厚);断颡(断头;砍头);断袖之宠(男宠);断头话(决绝的话);断裳(截断裳衣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 断开;断绝 <span class="encs">[break;snap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">断者架木通之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">切肉肉断而发不断。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;内储说下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">天门</span>中断 <span class="diczx2">楚江</span>开。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《望天门山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:断纹(琴上涂漆的裂纹);断头香(燃至中途熄灭的香。迷信者以为再烧此香，即对神佛不敬，来世必遭报应)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拦截;拦劫 <span class="encs">[intercept]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐断兵马禀缣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;杜茂传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因军兴，断盗数千万。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;盖勋传》</span>。注:&ldquo;断，谓割截。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:断取(截取);断盗(拦截抢劫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 判断;裁决 <span class="encs">[judge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事不目见耳闻，而臆断其有无，可乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:断配(判决发配);断决(判决);断冤(审理冤狱);断遣(断决，处理);断没(判断没收归公)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 分;区分;划分 <span class="encs">[divide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">断，段也，分为异段。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:断限(划定的年代界限);断方(划为方形)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 禁止 <span class="encs">[inhibit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:断酒(禁止酿酒);断尽(禁绝);断渡(禁渡，停渡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 戒掉，有意地抑制自己不行动 <span class="encs">[abstain from]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:断酒</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 斩杀，宰杀 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大者立断。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;淮南历王长传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:断除(除灭，杀掉);断杀(砍杀);断首(砍头)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 治理，治，管理&hellip;的事物 <span class="encs">[administer]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:断遗(处理;处分)</span></p>
