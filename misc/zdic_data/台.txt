                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">台</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>台</strong></p><p><strong>（➊&mdash;➎<span class="diczx3">臺</span>、➏<span class="diczx3">檯</span>、➐<span class="diczx3">颱</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">t&aacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄞˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>高平的建筑物：亭～楼阁。</li>	<li>像台的东西，器物的座子：井～。窗～。灯～。</li>	<li>量词：一～戏。</li>	<li>台湾省的简称：～胞。～币。</li>	<li>姓。</li>	<li>桌子、案子：写字～。</li>	<li>发生在太平洋西部热带海洋上的一种极猛烈的风暴，称&ldquo;台风&rdquo;。</li>	<li>〔三台〕星名，古代用来比喻三公。</li>	<li>敬辞，用于称呼对方或与对方有关的事物：～鉴。～甫。</li></ol><p>● <strong>台</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tāi.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄞˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔天～〕①山名，在中国浙江省；②地名，在中国浙江省。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> platform; unit; term of address</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span>  Station  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">台</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>台</strong> <span class="dicpy">Tāi</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 地名。&ldquo;台州&rdquo;的简称 <span class="encs">[Taizhou prefecture]</span>。唐武德五年(公元622年)，改海州为台州，以境内天台山得名，治所在今浙江省临海县。元改为路，明改为府</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南发交</span><span class="diczx2">广东</span><span class="diczx2">温台</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《和王微之登高斋二首》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>台</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形。从口，(以)声。从口，与表示喜悦有一定的联系。本义:喜悦。读( <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span>))</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 三台。星名。古代用三台来比喻三公 <span class="encs">[one of three stars in Ursa Major;one of the three highest dignitaries of State]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:台鼎(古代称三公或宰相，意为职位显要);台宿(三台星);台光(三台星光);台阶(三台星亦名泰阶，故称台阶);台斗(比喻宰辅重臣);台司(指三公等宰辅大臣);台臣(指宰辅重臣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 鱼名。背上有黑的花纹。后作&ldquo;鲐&rdquo; <span class="encs">[chub mackerel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黄</span>耇<span class="diczx1">台背，以引以翼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;行苇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>台</strong></p>
<p>臺 <span class="dicpy">t&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从至，从之，从高省，与室屋同意。按积土四方高丈曰台，不方者曰观曰阙。本义:用土筑成的方形的高而平的建筑物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[platform]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">台，观四方而高者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可以处台榭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">层台累榭，临高山些!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">歌台暖响，春光融融。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百年多病独登台。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《登高》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多少楼台烟雨中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《江南春绝句》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">台阁相向。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">守台军士。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:台门(古代诸侯所筑用来了望守卫的土堆高台);台榭(垒土高起的平方地叫台，台上的亭子叫榭);台阁(台榭楼阁);台观(楼台宫观);台下(台榭的下面);台馆(楼台馆阁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通常高于附近区域的平面(地面的或地板的) <span class="encs">[stage;terrace]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:站台;讲台;检阅台;台城(古代守城拒敌的设备);台门(古代天子、诸侯宫室的门。因以土为台基，故称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 器物的底座 <span class="encs">[stand;support]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:台盏(一种酒盏，有托盘);砚台;镜台;灯台;船台;锅台;烛台</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 站;单位 <span class="encs">[station]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今从宽将</span><span class="diczx2">贾赦</span>发往台站效力赎罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:气象台;电视台</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 敬辞。用于称呼对方或跟对方有关的行为 <span class="encs">[term of respectful address in letters，etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">台，侍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仆臣台。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公七年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;给台下征召也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:台讳(对人官名的尊称);台坐(敬辞。坐于尊位);台表(敬辞。用于称呼人的字);兄台;台鉴(请对方阅览的敬词);台屏(敬辞。尊称对方的家);台甫(敬辞。旧时用于问人的表字);台安(敬辞。多用于书信结尾，表示对收信人的问候);台候(敬辞。用于问候对方寒暖起居);台席(古以三公取象三台，故称宰相的职位为台席)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 莎草 <span class="encs">[nutgrass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">南山</span>有台， <span class="diczx2">北山</span>有莱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;南山有台》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古代中央官署名 <span class="encs">[central office]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚书初入台为郎中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉官仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">台谒当以属礼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> (台，御史台，作状语)。又如:台垣(高级官署);台辅(高级官署或官位);台臣(台阁之臣，国家的大臣;谏官);台官(官名。汉代以尚书为中台，御史为宪台，后世因此又称尚书或御史为台官);台郎(尚书郎);台省(汉时称尚书为台省。因尚书省的办事处中台在禁省中而得名)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 台湾省的简称 <span class="encs">[Taiwan Province]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:台胞</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 姓</p>
