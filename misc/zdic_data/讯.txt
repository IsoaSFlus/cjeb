                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>讯</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">訊</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xùn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xùn.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>问，特指法庭中的审问：审～。刑～。～问。</li><li>消息，信息：通～。音～。～息。</li><li>告，陈诉：“夫也不良，歌以～之。”</li><li>中国西周时对俘虏的称谓：执～（意思是捉到俘虏）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">迅</span>”，迅速。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">讯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>讯</strong></p><p>訊 <span class="dicpy">xùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[interrogate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讯，问也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，字亦作询。汉宣帝名询。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讯之占梦。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·正月》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“问也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">多其讯。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·学记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“或为訾，按，訾犹咨也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执讯而与之书。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公十二年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“通问之官。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君尝讯臣矣。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·僖公十年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“上问下曰讯。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以讯首或告。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讯鞫论报。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·张汤传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“考问也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卒从吏讯。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·邹阳传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓鞠问也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃讯申胥。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昼夜搒讯。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">木讯者三人。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:讯定(审问判决);讯治(审问，处罚);讯责(审问罪状而加谴责)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 询问;请教 <span class="encs">[question]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">广问讯。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咸来问讯。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泛讯之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你如今也讯听着，不管他根基富贵，只要模样儿配的上，就来告诉我。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:讯听(打听)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 责问，诘问 <span class="encs">[call sb. to account;closely question]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歌以讯之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·陈风·墓门》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讯予不顾。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诏大臣至</span><span class="diczx2">西宁卫</span>讯理，按例赔偿。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">俞正燮</span><span class="smcs">《癸已类稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span>                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A0A.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27A3C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧨼</span>訙<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27AD3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧫓</span>䛜<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20C56.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠱖</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>思晉切，音信。問也。【詩·小雅】召彼故老，訊之占夢。【傳】訊，問也。【公羊傳·僖十年】荀息曰：君嘗訊臣矣。【註】上問下曰訊。</p><p>又【玉篇】辭也。【爾雅·釋言】言也。【郉疏】訊，問以言也。【詩·小雅】執訊獲醜。【傳】訊，辭也。【箋】言也。【疏】謂其有所知識，可與之爲言辭。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋詁】告也。【疏】訊者，告問也。【詩·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>風】夫也不良，歌以訊之。【傳】訊，告也。韓詩曰：諫也。</p><p>又讓也。【吳語】乃訊申胥。【註】告讓也。</p><p>又書問。【左傳·文十七年】鄭子家使執訊而與之書，以告趙宣子。【註】執訊，通訊問之官。【荀卿·雲賦】行遠疾速，而不可託訊者與。【註】訊，書問也。</p><p>又鞫罪。【周禮·秋官·小司<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡨥</span>】以三刺斷庶民獄訟之中，一曰訊羣臣，二曰訊羣吏，三曰訊萬民。【禮·王制】出征執有罪，反釋奠于學，以訊馘告。</p><p>又治也。【禮·樂記】訊疾以雅。【註】訊，亦治也。雅，樂器，奏此以治舞者之疾。</p><p>又【博雅】動也。【韻會小補】振訊，整理之義，見【左傳·隱四年孔疏】。</p><p>又通作誶。【詩·大雅】執訊連連。【箋】訊，言也。亦作誶。</p><p>又【史記·賈誼傳】訊曰已矣。【註】李奇曰：訊，告也。張晏曰：《離騷》丁寧亂辭也。索隱曰：重宣其意也。【漢書】作誶。</p><p>又通作迅。【禮·樂記】訊疾以雅。【註】奮訊也。【釋文】訊，本又作迅。</p><p>又【前漢·揚雄傳】猋駭雲訊。【師古註】訊，亦奮訊也。【文選】作迅。</p><p>又【集韻】須閏切，音濬。【說文】義同。</p><p>又【集韻】雖遂切，音隧。與譢同。問也，告也，讓也，諫也。亦通作誶。</p><p>又【詩·小雅】凡百君子，莫肯用訊。【朱傳】訊，音息悴切。叶上瘁下退。</p><p>又叶息七切，音悉。【左思·魏都賦】翩翩黃鳥，銜書來訊。人謀所尊，鬼謀所秩。李善讀。</p>
