                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>旧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">舊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄡˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>过时的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">新</span>&rdquo;相对：～式。～俗。</li>
	<li>东西因经过长时间而变了样子：～衣服。～书。</li>
	<li>原先曾有过的，过去很长时间的：～居。～友。</li>
	<li>有交情，有交情的人：故～（指老朋友）。念～（怀念旧日情谊）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> old, ancient; former, past</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> alt, veraltet, überholt  (Adj)​,früher, ehemals, ehemalig  (S)​,Jiu  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vieux,ancien,usé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旧</strong></p>
<p>舊 <span class="dicpy">ji&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。甲骨文字形，上面是&ldquo;萑&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">huān</span>)，鸱鸟类;下面是&ldquo;臼&rdquo;，作声符。本义:鸟名)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 鸺鶹，即猫头鹰 <span class="encs">[owl]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 故交，老交情 <span class="encs">[old friendship]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">待之若旧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与之有旧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与相国有旧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:怀旧;有旧;念旧;亲戚故旧;旧雨</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 原有的典章制度 <span class="encs">[old institutions]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不必循旧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:复旧;照旧</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指世族 <span class="encs">[aristocratic family]</span>。如:旧家(世家望族);旧家子(世家子弟)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;丘( <span class="dicpy">qiū</span>)&rdquo;。丘冢，坟墓 <span class="encs">[mond;grave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">越王</span>不隳旧冢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说苑&middot;尊贤》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">越王</span>不颓旧冢，而 <span class="diczx2">越</span>人服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼&middot;保傅》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>旧</strong></p>
<p>舊 <span class="dicpy">ji&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 过时的;陈旧的(跟&ldquo;新&rdquo;相对) <span class="encs">[old;used;worn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">保守也故永旧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:旧牌儿(旧谱子。旧规矩;老样子);旧楮(旧纸币);旧垒(旧的堡垒、营垒);旧矩(老规矩);旧车;旧衣服;旧流丢(破旧的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 从前的;原先的 <span class="encs">[bygone;past;former original]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">告尔旧止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;抑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其旧如之何?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;东山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非旧器者举毁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还于旧都。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风景旧曾谙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《忆江南》</span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下" target="_blank">未集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下&amp;bs=臼" target="_blank">臼</a>】
                              舊·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/820A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】巨救切【正韻】巨又切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音柩。【說文】鴟舊，舊留也。【徐曰】卽怪鴟也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】故也。【增韻】對新之稱。【詩&middot;豳風】其新孔嘉，其舊如之何。【左傳&middot;僖二十八年】輿人誦，原田每每，舍其舊，而新是謀。【公羊傳&middot;莊二十九年】新延廄者何。修舊也。</p>
<p>又久也。【詩&middot;大雅】於乎小子，告爾舊止。【箋】舊，久也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】昔也，老宿也。</p>
<p>又姓。漢上黨太守舊彊。</p>
<p>又與柩同。【金史&middot;蔡珪傳】燕靈王舊。舊，古柩字通用。</p>
<p>又【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>巨九切，音臼。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】許尤切，音休。與鵂同。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶巨己切，音技。【詩&middot;大雅】匪上帝不時，殷不用舊。時音氏。【又】維今之人，不尚有舊。叶上里。</p>
<p>又叶巨寄切，音忌。【韋<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>成詩】天子我監，登我三事。顧我傷墜，爵復我舊。</p>
<p>又【管子&middot;牧民篇】不恭祖舊，則孝弟不備。◎按唐韻正，舊音忌，則忌亦正音，非叶音。</p>
<p>又叶去九切，音糗。【前漢&middot;敘傳】三枿之起，本根旣朽。枯楊生華，曷惟其舊。</p>
