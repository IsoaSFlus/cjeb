						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>杠</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">槓</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄤˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>一种较粗的棍子：～子。～杆。</li>
	<li>一种体育器材：～杠。～杠。高低～。</li>
	<li>在阅读或批改文字中作标记而画的粗直线。</li>
	<li>吵嘴，自以为是而好与人争论：抬～。～上了。</li>
	<li>磨擦：～刀。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>杠</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄤˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>旗杆。</li>
	<li>小桥。</li>
	<li>床前横木。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">杠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>杠</strong></p>
<p>槓 <span class="dicpy">gāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，工声。( <span class="dicpy">gāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> )本义:床前横木)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">床，其杠，</span><span class="diczx2">北燕</span>、 <span class="diczx2">朝鲜</span>之间谓之树，自 <span class="diczx2">关</span>而西， <span class="diczx2">秦</span> <span class="diczx2">晋</span>之间谓之杠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杠，床前横木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指旗杆 <span class="encs">[flag staff]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">素锦绸杠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释天》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比较小的桥 <span class="encs">[small bridge]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:石杠(石桥);杠彴(泛指桥);杠梁(桥梁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">g&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>杠</strong></p>
<p>槓 <span class="dicpy">g&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 抬重物的粗棍。亦指门闩 <span class="encs">[stout carrying pole]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:木杠;铁杠;门杠;杠房头(杠房的把头);杠架(抬尸体用的床)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 锻炼身体用的一种器械 <span class="encs">[bar]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:高低杠;杠铃;双杠;单杠</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 批改文字或阅读中作为标记所画的粗直线 <span class="encs">[thick line]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:书上画了好多杠杠;在稿子上扛了许多杠子</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 机床上带螺纹的棍状零件 <span class="encs">[screw]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丝杠</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>杠</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 把不通的文字、错字或多余的字用直线划去或标出 <span class="encs">[cross out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他把作文中的许多词句都杠掉了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 在布、皮或石头上磨擦使锋利些 <span class="encs">[sharpen]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:把刀或剃刀杠一下;杠刀</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6760.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】古雙切【正韻】居郞切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音江。【說文】牀前橫木也。【徐曰】今人謂之牀桯。【急就篇】妻婦聘嫁齎媵僮，奴婢私隷枕牀杠。【方言】秦晉之閒謂之杠。</p>

<p>又【博雅】杠，舉也。</p>
<p>又星名。【晉書&middot;天文志】大帝上九星曰華蓋，下九星曰杠，華蓋之柄也。</p>
