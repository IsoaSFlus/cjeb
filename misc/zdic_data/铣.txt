                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铣</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>铣</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">銑</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>有光泽的金属：～铁（铸铁，生铁）。</li>
	<li>小凿。</li>
	<li>古代钟下的两角。</li>
	<li>以金装饰弓的两头。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>铣</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">銑</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>一种用圆形能旋转的多刃刀具切削金属的专用设备，称&ldquo;铣床&rdquo;，上有&ldquo;铣刀&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>在铣床上加工金属工件：～削。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铣</strong></p>
<p>銑 <span class="dicpy">xǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用一种能旋转的圆形多刃刀具切削金属工件 <span class="encs">[mill]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:铣削;铣刀</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/铣刀">铣刀</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/铣工">铣工</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/铣削">铣削</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铣</strong></p>
<p>銑 <span class="dicpy">xiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，先声。本义:富有光泽的金属)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 富有光泽的金属 <span class="encs">[bright metal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金之泽者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绝泽谓之铣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:&ldquo;即美金，最有光泽也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而玦之金铣者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黄离踵曜，太阳分铣树之辉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《乾元殿颂序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:铣泽(光泽);铣树(闪烁金光之树)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9291.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>穌典切，音跣。【說文】金之澤者。【爾雅&middot;釋器】絕澤謂之銑。【疏】金之最有光澤者名銑。</p>
<p>又【說文】一曰小鑿也。</p>
<p>又【晉語】銑者，寒甚矣。【註】銑，灑也。灑灑，寒貌，言不和潤也。</p>
<p>又【說文】一曰鍾兩角謂之銑。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】鳧氏爲鍾，兩欒謂之銑。【註】銑，鍾口兩角也。【疏】古樂器，應律之鍾，如今之鈴不圜，故有兩角。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋器】弓以金者謂之銑。【註】以金飾弓之兩頭也。【疏】飾弓以金者名銑。</p>
