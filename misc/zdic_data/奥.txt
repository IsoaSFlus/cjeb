                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>奥</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>含义深，不易理解：深～。～妙。～秘。～旨。</li>
	<li>室内的西南角，泛指房屋及其他深处隐蔽的地方：堂～。经堂入～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>奥</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>浊。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/燠">燠</a>&rdquo;，暧。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>奥</strong> <span class="dicpy">&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:古时指房屋的西南角。古时祭祀设神主或尊者居坐之处)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[southwest corner of house]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奥，宛也。室之西南隅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">燔柴于奥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼器》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西南隅谓之奥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">司宫筵于奥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;少牢礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">设于奥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士丧礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">经堂入奥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奥阼(室之西南隅，为尊者、主人之位)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指室内深处 <span class="encs">[the depths of the room]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">室无奥阼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仲尼燕居》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无能老蝙蝠，乘夜出堂奥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张耒</span><span class="smcs">《夏日杂感》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堂奥</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宫廷内机密的地方 <span class="encs">[hidden recesses]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出入往来禁奥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;董昭传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奥主(国内之主。比喻国君。奥有深居内室之意)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>奥</strong> <span class="dicpy">&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 深 <span class="encs">[deep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">野无奥草。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span>。注:&ldquo;深也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">结根坚且奥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《塘上行》</span></p>
