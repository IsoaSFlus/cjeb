                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>滃</strong> <span class="dicpy">Wēng</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p>滃江 <span class="encs">[Wengjiang River]</span>，水名。在广东</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>滃</strong> <span class="dicpy">wēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 浓染 <span class="encs">[painted strongly]</span>。如:难滃(纸面油光，难以染上颜色)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">wěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>滃</strong> <span class="dicpy">wěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 云气腾涌的样子;青烟弥漫的样子 <span class="encs">[cloudy]</span>。如:滃然(云气腾涌、烟雾弥漫的样子);滃渤(滃浡、滃勃。云蒸雾涌的样子);滃滃(滃滃澹澹云气腾涌的样子);滃滃翳翳(云烟笼罩、昏暗晦冥的样子);滃溶(云气飘浮貌);滃郁(云烟弥漫)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 大水沸涌的样子 <span class="encs">[surging]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水耗初而釜治，火增壮而力均，滃嘈杂而廉清，信净美而甘分。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《菜羹赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 浓 <span class="encs">[strong]</span>。如:滃染(中国绘画技法一种，即用水墨淡彩润画面，不露或少露笔痕);滃泱(水弥漫)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">wēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
