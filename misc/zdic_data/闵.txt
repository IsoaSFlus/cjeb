<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閔</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">mǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄣˇ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>忧忠，凶丧：～凶。</li>

	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/悯">悯</a>&rdquo;，忧虑。</li>

	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>闵</strong></p>
<p>閔 <span class="dicpy">mǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，文声。本义:吊唁)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[condole;offer one&rsquo;s condolenees]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闵，吊者在门也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同&ldquo;悯&rdquo;。怜恤，哀怜 <span class="encs">[sympathize]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闵予小子，遭家不造。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;闵予小子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闵察(怜悯审察);闵悼(怜恤哀悼);闵悔(怜恤而痛悔);闵笑(怜悯嘲笑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 勉。黾勉 <span class="encs">[encourage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予惟用闵于天越民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;君奭》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闵免(勤免不息。同黾勉。也作闵勉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 忧虑 <span class="encs">[worry;be anxious about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>人有闵其苗之不长而揠之者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闵闵焉如农夫之望岁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闵凶(忧伤的事。常指父母之丧);闵悔(伤痛后悔);闵急(令人担忧的严重情势)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9594.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】眉隕切【集韻】【韻會】美隕切【正韻】弭盡切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音敏。【說文】弔者在門也。【徐曰】今別作憫，非。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】病也。【詩&middot;衞風】覯閔旣多。【傳】病也。【禮&middot;儒行】不閔有司。【釋文】閔，本亦作愍。</p>
<p>又傷念也。【詩&middot;周南&middot;遵彼汝墳序】婦人能閔其君子。【註】傷念也。</p>
<p>又勉也。【書&middot;君奭】子惟用閔于天越民。【傳】閔，勉也。</p>
<p>又地名。【穀梁傳&middot;僖二十三年】齊侯伐宋，圍閔。【釋文】閔，左氏作緡。</p>
<p>又姓。【韻會】孔子弟子閔損。</p>
<p>又閔馬父，見左傳。</p>
<p>又人名。【前漢&middot;哀帝紀】立魯頃王子郚鄕侯閔爲王。</p>
<p>又【集韻】眉貧切，音珉。【說文】秋天也。引虞書，仁閔覆下，則稱旻天。或書作閔。通作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29509.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩔉</span>。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;豳風】恩斯勤斯，鬻子之閔斯。【註】亦病也。</p>
