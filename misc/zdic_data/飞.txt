                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>飞</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">飛</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fēi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēi.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄟˉ</span></p><ol><li>鸟类或虫类等用翅膀在空中往来活动：～天（佛教壁画或石刻中的空中飞舞的神）。～跃。～鸟。</li><li>在空中运动：～雪。火箭～向太空。</li><li>形容极快：～驶。～黄腾达（喻人骤然得志，官位升迁快）。</li><li>极，特别地：刀子～快。</li><li>无根据的、无缘无故的：流言～语。</li><li>像架在空中的形状：～桥。～阁。</li><li>感情的表达与传递：～眼。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">飞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>飞</strong></p><p>飛 <span class="dicpy">fēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，下面象展开的双翼，上面象鸟首。本义:鸟飞)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(bird) fly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飛，鸟翥也。象张翼之形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飞鸟遗之音。——<span class="smcs">《易·小过》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明珠弹于飞肉。——<span class="smcs">《太玄·唐》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“飞肉，禽鸟也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西塞山前白鹭飞，桃花流水鳜鱼肥。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">张志和</span><span class="smcs">《渔歌子》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸟倦飞而知还。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸢飞戾天者。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:飞凫(飞翔的凫鸟);飞翰(飞翔的小鸟);飞鸿(飞翔中的鸿雁)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指飞翔 <span class="encs">[fly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飞龙在天。——<span class="smcs">《易·乾》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彗孛飞流。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·天文志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“绝迹而去也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:飞虫(能飞的昆虫类);飞蝗(指善飞的蝗虫);飞鱼钩(叉鱼的钩子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 物体随风在空中飘游浮荡 <span class="encs">[hover or flutter in the air]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桃李阴阴柳絮飞。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《酬部给事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胡天八月即飞雪。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茅飞渡江洒江郊。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">樯橹灰飞烟灭。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇·赤壁怀古》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:飞絮(空中飘摇的柳絮);飞天光棍(手段高强的地痞无赖);飞火(爆竹的声音与火光);飞星(流星般迅疾)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 矿物药或颜料，研成细末，置于水中以漂去其浮于水面的粗屑 <span class="encs">[skim off]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">这些颜色，咱们淘澄飞跌着。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>飞</strong></p><p>飛 <span class="dicpy">fēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a
