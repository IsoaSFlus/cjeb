                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">山</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>山</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>地面形成的高耸的部分：土～。～崖。～峦。～川。～路。～头。～明水秀。～雨欲来风满楼（喻冲突或战争爆发之前的紧张气氛）。</li><li>形状像山的：～墙（人字形房屋两侧的墙壁。亦称“房山”）。</li><li>形容大声：～响。～呼万岁。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mountain, hill, peak</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Berg ,Radikal Nr. 46 = Berg, Gebirge  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> montagne,colline,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">山</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>山</strong> <span class="dicpy">shān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文和金文字形，象山峰并立的形状。<span class="yhcs">“山”</span>是汉字的一个部首。本义:地面上由土石构成的隆起部分)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mountain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山，土有石而高。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山，土之聚也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山人取之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">夏后代</span>山。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·明堂位》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">太行</span>、 <span class="diczx2">王屋</span>二山，方七百里，高万仞。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如山如阜，如冈如陵。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·天保》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生于山阜，处于室堂。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为山九仞，功亏一篑。——<span class="smcs">《书·旅獒》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山重水复疑无路，柳暗花明又一村。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《游山西村》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:山霭(山上的云气);山灵(山神);山阿(山中凹曲的地方);山岩(山峰;山崖);山隈(山角);山啜(山岔);山落(山岔;山角落);山孤钉(小山包);山头(绿林好汉占据的山寨;坟地;山墙);山长(科举时代的书院主讲及总领院务者);山主(寺院的主持);山观(山中道观);山关(依山而建的城堡)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形状像山的东西 <span class="encs">[anything in the shape of a mountain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刃树剑山。——<span class="smcs">《南齐书·高逸传伦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:冰山;鳌山;山枕(枕头。古代枕头多用木、瓷制作，中凹，形似山);山堆阜积(东西堆积得像山一样);山棚(结彩的牌楼;比武台，戏台);山家(佛寺)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 特指<span class="yhcs">“五岳”</span> <span class="encs">[the Five mountains]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奠高山大川。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“高山，五岳。大川，四渎。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:山斗(<span class="yhcs">“泰山北斗”</span>的缩略。借指卓有成就令人仰慕的人物);山右(指山西省，西在太行山之右);山左(山东的别称)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 山墙 <span class="encs">[gable wall]</span>。如:山架(把几层木板镶嵌在墙里的架子)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 指酒肆的楼上 <span class="encs">[storey of a bar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酒阁名曰厅院，若楼上则又或名为山，一山、二山、三山之类。牌额写过山，非特有山，谓酒力高远也。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">灌圃耐得翁</span><span class="smcs">《都城纪胜》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 山中。指隐居之处 <span class="encs">[hermitage]</span>。如:山谷臣(隐士的自称);山谷之士(隐士);山囚(隐居山野不得志的士人);山巾(山野隐士的便帽)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 坟 <span class="encs">[mound]</span>。如:山陵(帝王的陵墓);山园(坟地);山门(墓门)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 蚕簇。由禾杆、油菜杆搭成的小簇 <span class="encs">[a small bundle of straws，etc.，for silkworms to spin cocoons on]</span>。如:蚕上山了 </p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span>
