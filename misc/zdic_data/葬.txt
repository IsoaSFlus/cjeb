                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>葬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">z&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄤˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>掩埋死人，泛指处理死者遗体：安～。埋～。土～。火～。海～。～送。～礼。～仪。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>葬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">z&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。从&ldquo;死&rdquo;，在&ldquo;茻&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">mǎng</span>)中，&ldquo;一&rdquo;其中，所以荐之。指人死后盖上草席埋藏在丛草中。荐，草席覆盖。本义:人死用草覆盖埋葬，后用棺木埋入土中)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bury]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">葬，臧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">葬也者，藏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故葬埋敬藏其形也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赐钱二百万以葬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分其汤肉葬之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而卒葬之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以衣冠葬之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:葬堂(为古代我国南方少数民族处置死者遗骨的一种方式);葬式(葬礼);葬虞(葬礼和虞祭。虞祭行于葬日，故用&ldquo;葬虞&rdquo;借指下葬的日期)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指处理尸体的方式 <span class="encs">[form of inhumation]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:火葬;海葬</span></p>
