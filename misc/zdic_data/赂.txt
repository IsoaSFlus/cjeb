                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赂</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">賂</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>赠送的财物，亦泛指财物：～之甚厚。“货～将甚厚”。</li><li>用财物买通公职人员：贿～。“六国破灭，非兵不利，战不善，弊在～秦。”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bribe; give present</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bestechung  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faire cadeau de,corrompre,cadeau,pot-de-vin</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赂</strong></p><p>賂 <span class="dicpy">lù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，各声。从<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>，表示与钱财有关。本义:赠送财物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[send gift]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赂，遗也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大赂南金。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·泮水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">骊姬</span>赂二王。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因厚赂</span><span class="diczx2">单于</span>，答其善意。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·苏武传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">割国之锱铢而赂之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仅</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>， <span class="diczx2">汉</span>之赂遗王财物，不可胜言。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·张骞传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是从散约解，争割地而赂</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:赂贿(用财物买通别人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 行贿，用钱、物买通别人 <span class="encs">[bribe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">六国破灭，非兵不利、战不善，弊在赂</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">富者赂数十百金。——</span><span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:贿赂(古代一般用<span class="yhcs">“赇”</span>字表示。如<span class="yhcs">“受赇枉法。”</span>中古时，<span class="yhcs">“赇”</span>与<span class="yhcs">“赂”</span>可以同义连用，这时的赂就是带贬义的<span class="yhcs">“赂”</span>了);赂门(行贿的途径);赂息(行贿以求息事);赂买(行贿买通);赂结(以行贿而结交);赂赠(行贿)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>赂</strong></p><p>賂 <span class="dicpy">lù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 赠送的财物和钱 <span class="encs">[goods]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">货赂将甚厚。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">数之王命，取赂而还。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公二十八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">家贫，货赂不足以自赎。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王及列士皆有馈赂。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:赂田(赠送的田);赂地(赠送的土地);赂贿(用来买通别人的财物);赂器(指受贿得来的器物)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“璐”</span>。美玉 <span class="encs">[fine jade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元龟象齿，大赂南金。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·泮水》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
