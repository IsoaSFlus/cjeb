                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">搜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>搜</strong> <span class="dicpy">sōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 聚集 <span class="encs">[gather]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:搜斥(广为积聚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 搜索;搜查 <span class="encs">[hunt after;track down and arrest;ransack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">搜，求也。</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>之间曰搜，就室曰搜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言二》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">惠子</span>恐，搜于国中，三日三夜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:搜弊(搜查弊端，弄清欺诈行为);搜猜(搜查，搜寻);搜筒(搜检，搜查);搜看(搜索检查，搜查);搜阅(搜查，查看);搜山煮海(严密搜查各个角落);搜伏(搜索隐伏之敌);搜刷(搜索)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 从事搜劫和掠夺 <span class="encs">[rifle]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:遍搜她的书桌，拼命地寻找书写的材料</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 寻求 <span class="encs">[search;poke about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时太子詹事</span><span class="diczx2">周捨</span>撰<span class="smcs">《礼疑义》</span>，自 <span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>至于 <span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>，并加搜采。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《梁书&middot;孔休源传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:搜剔(搜寻，寻找);搜遗(寻找、检查遗漏之处);搜须捉虱(竭力搜寻);搜刷(搜捕，清查);搜才(寻求贤才);搜句(寻求佳句);搜抉(搜求选择);搜吟(寻觅诗句);搜拔(寻求选拔);搜奇;搜采</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 检查，检点 <span class="encs">[check]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不肯搜自己狂为，只待要觅别人破绽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 选择 <span class="encs">[choose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">王安丰</span>选女婿，从挽郎搜其胜者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 挖，掏 <span class="encs">[dig]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">着这逼绰刀子搜开这墙阿，磕掉我靠倒这墙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《珠砂担》</span></span></p>
