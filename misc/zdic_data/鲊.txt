                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>鲊</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鮓</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄚˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>一种用盐和红曲腌的鱼：“江南人好作盘游饭，～脯脍炙，无有不埋在饭中”。</li>    <li>用米粉、面粉等加盐和其他作料拌制的切碎的菜，可以贮存：茄子～。扁豆～。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>鲊</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鮓</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhà  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhà.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄚˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 海蜇，水母的一种。</p></ol>
