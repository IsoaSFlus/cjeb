                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谗</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>谗</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">讒</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄢˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>在别人面前说陷害某人的坏话：～言。～害。～佞。进～。信～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> slander, defame, misrepresent</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> médire,calomnie</p></div>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>谗</strong></p>
<p>讒 <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> (形声。从言，毚( <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:说别人的坏话) 同本义 <span class="encs">[backbite;slander]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谗，谮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谗，诞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谗口嚣嚣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">败言为谗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谗鼎之铭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;疾谗之鼎。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">取彼谗人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;马援传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">屈平</span>属草稿未定，上官大夫见而欲夺之， <span class="diczx2">屈平</span>不与。因谗之曰&hellip;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谗谤(诋毁，诽谤);谗言冷语(毁谤、讥刺的冷言冷语)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>谗</strong></p>
<p>讒 <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 说别人坏话的人 <span class="encs">[slanderer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谗与佞，俱小人也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;答佞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谗人(喜欢恶意攻击、陷害贤良的人);谗夫(谗害他人的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 谗言，陷害别人的坏话 <span class="encs">[slander]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乱之又生，君子信谗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;巧言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">采苓，刺</span><span class="diczx2">晋献公</span>也。 <span class="diczx2">献公</span>好听谗焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;采苓序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">安帝</span>信谗，无辜死者多。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;五行志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">去国怀乡，忧谗畏讥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">洎</span><span class="diczx2">李牧</span>以谗诛。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谗箭(谗言伤人像箭那样，说它既锐利又难以防备);谗阅(互相毁谤、争斗)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              讒·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=24" target="_blank">24</a>　·部外筆画：17</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8B92.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】士咸切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】鋤咸切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音饞。【說文】譖也。【玉篇】佞也。【正字通】崇飾惡言，毀善害能也。【書·舜典】朕堲讒說殄行。【莊子·漁父篇】好言人之惡謂之讒。【荀子·修身篇】傷良曰讒。【說苑·臣術篇】蔽善者，國之讒也。</p><p>又星名。【晉書·天文志】卷舌六星中，一曰天讒，主巫醫。</p><p>又鼎名。【左傳·昭三年】讒鼎之銘。【疏】讒鼎，疾讒之鼎，《明堂位》所云崇鼎是也。一云讒，地名。禹鑄九鼎於甘讒之地，故曰讒鼎。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】士懺切【韻會】【正韻】士監切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>饞去聲。義同。</p><p>又叶鉏弓切，音崇。【楚辭·九歎】吸精粹而吐氛濁兮，橫邪世而不取容。行叩誠而不阿兮，遂見排而逢讒。</p><p>又叶銀炎切，音嚴。【梁鴻·適吳詩】欲乗策兮縱邁，疾吾俗兮作讒。競舉枉兮錯直，咸先佞兮唌唌。唌音延。</p><p>又【俗書證誤】从二免，非。䜛。</p>
