						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>穗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">suì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>禾本植物聚生在茎的顶端的花和果实：麦～。稻～。谷～。～轴。～状花序。</li><li>用丝线、布条或纸条等扎成的、挂起来往下垂的装饰品：灯笼～儿。</li><li>中国广东省广州市的别称。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">穗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>穗</strong> <span class="dicpy">suì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从禾，惠声。稻麦等禾本科植物的花或果实聚生在茎上顶端部分。亦泛指穗状花实)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[the ear of grain;spike]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赪茎素毳，并柯共穗之瑞。——<span class="smcs">《曲水诗序》</span>。注;<span class="yhcs">“共穗，嘉禾了。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼黍离离，彼稷之穗。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·黍离》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼有滞穗。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·甫田》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:穗肥(禾谷作物幼穗分化期施用的追肥，可使幼穗有充足养分，穗大粒多，增加产量);麦穗</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 穗状之物 <span class="encs">[tassel]</span>。如:穗带(如穗状的带子);穗缰(带穗的缰绳);穗头(穗状物)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 烛花或灯花 <span class="encs">[snuff]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时复见残灯，和烟坠金穗。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩偓</span><span class="smcs">《懒卸头》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 中国广州市的别称 <span class="encs">[another name for Guangzhou city]</span>。古代传说中有五仙人乘五色羊执六穗至此，故称其为<span class="yhcs">“穗”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“繐”</span>。细而疏的麻布 。古时多用作丧服 <span class="encs">[fine-textured cloth]</span>。如:穗帷(灵帐。同繐帐);穗屦(细疏布所制，丧事时穿的鞋子);穗布(细而稀疏的麻布)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/穗选">穗选</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/穗轴">穗轴</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/穗子">穗子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
