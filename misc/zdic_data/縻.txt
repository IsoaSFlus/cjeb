data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，麻声。本义:牛缰绳) 同本义 <span class="encs">[halter for ox]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">縻，牛辔也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">縻，牛缰也。——<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p><p><span <strong>縻</strong> <span class="dicpy">mí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="yhcs">“靡”</span> <span class="encs">[consume]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坐縻廪粟。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span
