						<p>● <strong>源</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>水流所从出的地方：河～。泉～。发～。～远流长。～头。</li>
	<li>事物的根由：来～。资～。渊～。能～。起～。策～地。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> spring; source, head; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Quelle, Ursprung eines Flusses  (S, Geol)​,Ursprung, Ursache, Grund, Quelle  (S)​,Yuan  (Eig, Fam)​,Ursprung</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> source</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>源</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，原声。&ldquo;原&rdquo;有表义作用，指水源。本义:水源，源泉。字本作&ldquo;原&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fountain head;source of a river]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或源也，或委也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祈祀山川百源。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹浊其源而求其清流。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼乐志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岸势犬牙差互，不可知其源。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:源委(指水的发源和归宿。引申为事情的本末和底细);源理(水的本源和支流)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 来源;根源 <span class="encs">[origin;source;cause]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">启生人之耳目，穷法度之本源。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧唐书&middot;儒学传序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:源本(根本);源由(根由);源起(事物发生的根源)</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">源</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>源</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄩㄢˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yu&aacute;n <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/6552.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">水流的出處。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「水源」、「泉源」、「飲水思源」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《國語&middot;晉語一》：「伐木不自其本，必復生；塞水不自其源，必復流。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李嶠〈河〉詩：「源出崑崙中，長波接漢空。」</span></p></li>
	<li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6E90.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>愚袁切，音元。【說文】水泉本也。【禮&middot;月令】爲民祈祀山川百源。【註】衆水始出爲百源。</p>
