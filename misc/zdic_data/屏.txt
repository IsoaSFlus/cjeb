<p><span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧㄥˊ</span></p>

<ol>

	<li>字画的条幅，通常以四幅或八幅为一组：画～。四扇～。</li>
</ol>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">屏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>屏</strong> <span class="dicpy">bīng</span></p>
<p>另见 <span class="dicpy">bǐng;p&iacute;ng</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/屏营">屏营</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>屏</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 退避;隐退 <span class="encs">[withdraw and keep off;go and live in seclusion]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>乃屏人间语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屏居(辞官隐居，不问世事);屏语(避开他人，私下交谈);屏伏(隐退，避匿);屏移(退隐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 隐藏 <span class="encs">[hide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔不许我，我乃屏璧与圭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;金縢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屏匿(隐藏);屏迹(隐避);屏藏(隐藏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 摈弃;除去，去掉<span class="encs">[某人、某物]</span>。同&ldquo;摒&rdquo; <span class="encs">[get rid of;discard]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:屏绝(屏弃断绝，不相来往);屏弃(屏除废弃);屏除(除去，放弃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 保护 <span class="encs">[protect]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:屏卫(护卫);屏翰(保卫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 抑制。抑止不出气 <span class="encs">[hold (one&#39;s breath)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋将军</span>屏息观之。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屏息(止住声息。即屏气);屏气(暂时抑制呼吸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 掩蔽 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p>

<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从尸，并声。这里的&ldquo;尸&rdquo;是由&ldquo;广&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)演变来的，表示与宫室有关的事物。本义:宫殿当门的小墙。又称&ldquo;照壁&rdquo;)</p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 成组的条幅 <span class="encs">[set of scrolls]</span>。</p>
