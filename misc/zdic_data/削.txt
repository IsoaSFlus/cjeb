                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>削</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用刀斜着去掉物体的表层。～苹果皮。～铅笔。</li>
	<li>打乒乓球时用球拍平而略斜地击球：～球。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>削</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xuē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuē.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄝˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>义同削（xiāo），用于一些复合词：～铁如泥。～足适履。</li>
	<li>减少；减弱：～减。～弱。</li>
	<li>除去：～职为民 。</li>
	<li>搜刮；掠取：剥～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">削</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>削</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本义读 <span class="dicpy">qi&agrave;o</span>。装刀剑的套子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 曲刀，书刀，削刀 <span class="encs">[knife]</span>?</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">削书刀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《曲礼&middot;金工》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 东周和秦汉时用来除去书写在木牍或竹简上的错字。又如:削刀(书刀)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 简札 <span class="encs">[bamboo&#39;s script]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:削青(古人制简记事，先以火炙竹令其出汗变青，谓之&ldquo;削青&rdquo;。后泛指书籍)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>削</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 斜着刀略平地切去物体的表层 <span class="encs">[pare with a knife;peel with a knife]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">削则削。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;礼乐志》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓有所删去，以刀削简牍也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为天子削瓜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">公输子</span>削竹木以为鹊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;鲁问》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">指如削葱根，口如含朱丹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:削铅笔;切削;削瓜(削去瓜皮。古代的一种礼节;又指削去皮的瓜);削杖(古代母丧时子服齐衰所执的木杖，用桐木削成)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 删除。指删改文字 <span class="encs">[delete]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">削而投之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有司请定法，削则削，笔则笔，救时务也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注:&ldquo;削者，谓有所删去，以刀削简牍也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:削改(删改);削抹(删改抹去)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 削除;削减 <span class="encs">[cut down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外削幅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;丧服礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寇至度必攻。主人先削城编，唯勿烧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
