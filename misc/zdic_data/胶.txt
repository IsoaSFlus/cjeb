                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">胶</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>胶</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">膠</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>黏性物质，有用动物的皮或角等熬成的，亦有植物分泌的和人工合成的：～质。～原。鳔～。乳～。万能～。如～似漆。～合板。</li>
	<li>用橡胶树的分泌物制成的有弹性的物质：橡～。～鞋。～皮。～布。</li>
	<li>有黏性像胶的：～泥。～水。</li>
	<li>粘着，粘合：～合。～着（<span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;</span>）。～柱鼓瑟。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> glue, gum, resin, rubber; sound; shin bone</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gummi  (S)​,Kleber, Leim  (S)​,leimen  (V)​,leimig, klebrig, zähflüssig  (Adj)​,Jiao  (Eig, Pers)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> coller,caoutchouc,colle,collant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">胶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胶</strong></p>
<p>膠 <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。古代&ldquo;胶&rdquo;用动物的皮煮制而成，所以从&ldquo;肉&rdquo;。 <span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>说:&ldquo;皮近肉，故字从肉。&rdquo;本义:粘性物质，用动物的皮角或树脂制成)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[glue;gum]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胶也者，以为和也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;弓人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胶鳔(用鱼鳔煮治而成的胶);胶漆(胶与漆。比喻事物的结合非常紧密;或比喻交情深厚);胶青(胶清。已制成的色质纯正的胶质);胶革(用皮革熬成的胶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代大学之称，后泛指学校 <span class="encs">[college]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:胶序(殷学名序，周学名胶;后用为学校的通称);胶庠(古代学校名，其中胶为周时大学名，庠为周时小学名，庠为商学名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鸡鸣声，泛指禽类鸣声<span class="encs">[chirp]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:胶胶;胶胶角角;胶胶膊膊</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 橡胶 <span class="encs">[rubber]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 酸性橡胶，天然橡胶。如:乳胶</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 合成橡胶，人造橡胶。如:胶轮</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 在某些方面类似树胶的物质 <span class="encs">[gum]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:香口胶</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 特指制成胶制的药物 <span class="encs">[gelatin]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:驴皮胶(阿胶)、虎骨胶、龟板胶、鹿角胶等</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">胶鬲</span>举于鱼盐之中。(胶鬲起初是鱼盐贩子。西伯(文王)发现了他，举荐给商纣王。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胶</strong></p>
<p>膠 <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 牢固 <span class="encs">[firm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德音孔胶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;隰桑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胶固(牢固)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 诡辩的 <span class="encs">[sophistry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胶，诈也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胶，欺也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牵胶而喻侈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胶言(诡辩不合法度的言论)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 固执 <span class="encs">[stubborn]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:胶固(固执);胶执(固执己见);胶滞(拘泥);胶让(坚辞)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 纠缠纷扰的样子 <span class="encs">[entangled]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:胶加(乖戾，纠缠);胶扰(胶胶扰扰。纷乱;纠结);胶葛(交错的样子)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>胶</strong></p>
<p>膠 <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用胶粘牢;粘住;使不能移动 <span class="encs">[stick with glue]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:胶续(用胶粘接断弦。后转指续娶);胶附(胶合。比喻志趣相投);胶致(解送犯人时，槛车四周以木版密封，不能开启，以防脱逃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 船搁浅 <span class="encs">[be stranded]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:胶浅(搁浅);胶溺(搁浅沉溺)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/81A0.svg">                          <p>昵也。作之以皮。从肉翏聲。古肴切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/81A0_5321.svg" /><span class="swbf">(膠)</span><span class="ywyy">昵也。</span><span class="swdz">弓人說膠曰：凡昵之類不能方。注：故書昵或作樴。杜子春云：樴讀爲不義不昵之昵，或爲䵑。䵑，黏也。玄謂樴，脂膏䐈敗之䐈，䐈亦黏也。按此經杜作昵，又作䵑，後鄭作䐈。其意則同也。許從杜作昵。日部曰：暱，日近也，或作昵。是則昵亦訓黏也。一說此昵也，及周禮注不義不昵之昵，三昵字皆當作䵒。</span><span class="ywyy">作之㠯皮。</span><span class="swdz">考工記：鹿膠靑白，馬膠赤白，牛膠火赤，鼠膠黑，魚膠餌，犀膠黃。注云：皆謂煑用其皮，或用角。按皮近肉，故字從肉。</span><span class="ywyy">从肉翏聲。</span><span class="swdz">古肴切。古音在三部。</span></p>                          </div>
