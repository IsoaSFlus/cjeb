						<p>● <strong>担</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">擔）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄢ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用肩膀挑：～水。</li>
	<li>承当，负责：～负。～任。～当。～待（①原谅；②担当责任）。～重任。～风险。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>担</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">擔）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>挑东西的用具，多用竹、木做成：扁～。</li>
	<li>一挑东西：～子。勇挑重～。</li>
	<li>中国市制重量单位，一担等于一百斤。</li>
	<li>量词，用于成担的东西：一～水。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>担</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄢˇ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>担</strong></p>
<p>擔 <span class="dicpy">dān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，詹声。本义:用肩挑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carry on a shoulder pole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儋，何也。从人，詹声。以背曰负，以肩曰儋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作&ldquo;擔&rdquo;。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">担，负也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">担囊行取薪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《苦寒行》</span></p>
