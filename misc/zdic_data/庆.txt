                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庆</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>庆</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">慶</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&igrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>祝贺：～贺。～祝。～幸。～典。～功。</li>
	<li>可祝贺的事：国～。大～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> congratulate, celebrate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> feiern, gratulieren, Festtag  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fête,célébration,célébrer,fêter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>庆</strong></p>
<p>慶 <span class="dicpy">q&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，左边是个&ldquo;文&rdquo;字，中间有个心，表示心情诚恳;右边是一张鹿皮。合起来表示带着鹿皮，真诚地对人庆贺。小篆字形上面是鹿字省略一部分;中间是&ldquo;心&rdquo;字，表心意;下边是&ldquo;攵&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">suī</span>脚)，表示&ldquo;往&rdquo;。意思跟甲骨文相同。本义:祝贺;庆贺)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[celebrate;congratulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庆，行贺人也。&hellip;吉礼以鹿皮为挚，故从鹿省。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。段注:&ldquo;谓心所喜而行也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庆，贺也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庆子家驹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;昭公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故庆其喜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以有庆矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;裳者华》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">蜀</span>遣卫尉 <span class="diczx2">陈震</span>庆 <span class="diczx2">权</span>践位。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴主传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">苏秦</span>见 <span class="diczx2">齐王</span>，再拜，俯而庆，仰而吊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;苏秦列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:庆忭(欢庆鼓掌);庆寿(祝贺生日);庆问(庆贺问候);庆官酒(庆祝升官的酒宴);庆成宴(庆贺祭祀典礼圆满成功的宴会);庆松(庆贺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 赏赐 <span class="encs">[award]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">农夫之庆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;甫田》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:庆赏(行赏奖励)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>庆</strong></p>
<p>慶 <span class="dicpy">q&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 喜;福庆，吉庆 <span class="encs">[occasion of celebration]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孝孙有庆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一人有庆，兆民赖之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:寿庆;校庆;国庆;庆色(喜色);庆会(喜庆宴会);庆门(福庆之家);庆殃(福庆与祸殃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 福泽。有幸的事 <span class="encs">[blessing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有庆未尝不怡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span>。注:&ldquo;福也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:余庆;庆云(庆烟。祥瑞的云气);庆霄(吉祥的云气);庆灵(景云与灵芝);庆绪(福善的事业);庆延(福泽绵延);庆祉(福泽);庆室(吉宅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;卿&rdquo;。卿士，大臣掌事者 <span class="encs">[a minister or a high official in ancient times]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">慶</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“庆”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/慶">慶</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集上" target="_blank">卯集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集上&amp;bs=心" target="_blank">心</a>】
                              慶·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6176.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/227A2.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢞢</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2264E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢙎</span>【唐韻】丘竟切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】丘正切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>卿去聲。【說文】行賀人也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;宗伯】以賀慶之禮，親異姓之國。【疏】謂侯國有喜可賀，王使大夫以物慶賀之也。</p>
<p>又【秋官&middot;大行人】賀慶以贊諸侯之喜是也。</p>
<p>又善也。【書&middot;呂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>】一人有慶。【正義】天子有善事也。【詩&middot;大雅】則篤其慶。【毛傳】善也。【正義】福慶爲善事，故爲善也。</p>
<p>又休也。【禮&middot;月令】行慶施惠。【註】慶謂休其善也。休，美也。</p>
<p>又福也。【易&middot;履卦】大有慶也。【詩&middot;小雅】孝孫有慶。</p>
<p>又賜也。【詩&middot;小雅】是以有慶矣。【箋】謂有慶賜之榮也。</p>
<p>又發語詞。【揚雄&middot;反離騷】慶天悴而喪榮。</p>
<p>又州名。隋立慶州。</p>
<p>又姓。【左傳】齊慶封，晉慶鄭。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>丘京切，音卿。【易&middot;大畜五上兩爻傳】有慶也，道大行也。慶與行叶。</p>
<p>又【睽卦四五兩爻傳】志行也，往有慶也。行與慶叶。【班固&middot;白雉詩】容潔朗兮於淳精，發皓羽兮奮翹英。彰皇德兮侔周成，永延長兮膺天慶。慶與精英成叶，皆庚韻也。蓋慶乃卿字去聲，轉平聲卽卿也。故卿雲亦曰慶雲。近昆山顧氏未達音韻，乃曰易經慶字俱讀羌音，行字俱讀杭音，此偏論也，不知易自有叶羌字者。未可棄慶字本聲之卿音也。</p>
<p>又叶虛羊切，音羌。亦福也。【易&middot;坤卦】積善之家，必有餘慶。叶下殃。【書&middot;伊訓】萬邦惟慶。叶上祥。【詩&middot;大雅】則篤其慶。叶下光又。【小雅】孝孫有慶。叶下彊。　从心从久。久者，行也。吉禮以鹿皮爲贄，从鹿省，會意。</p>
