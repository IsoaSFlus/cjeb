						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>愧</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ku&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 羞惭：羞～。惭～。～色。～怍（惭愧）。～疚。～赧（因羞愧而脸红）。～悔。问心无～。</p>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>愧</strong> <span class="dicpy">ku&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，鬼声。本义:惭愧)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。同&ldquo;媿&rdquo; <span class="encs">[ashamed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小则如愧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚不愧于屋漏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;抑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不愧于人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;何人斯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽颜惭未知心愧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《魏书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵上不杀我，我不愧于心乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;张耳陈余列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">谌</span>虽暴抗，然闻言则大愧流汗。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《段太尉逸事状》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能不愧老人之言否。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">下恐愧吾师矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兹游快且愧矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兼愧贵家子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:愧心(内心惭愧);愧悔(惭愧后悔);愧服(衷心佩服他人，自惭不如);愧灼(极言惭愧之意);愧天作人(上愧对天理，下愧对人事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 折辱人;使羞惭 <span class="encs">[ashame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">昭王</span>闻之，欲愧之以辞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:愧辱(羞辱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 责人罪咎 <span class="encs">[blame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是故君子不以其所能病人，不以人之所不能者愧人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;表记》</span>。注:&ldquo;病、愧谓罪咎之。&rdquo;</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>愧</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄎㄨㄟˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>ku&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2621.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">因理虧或做錯事，而感到難為情。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「慚愧」、「愧不敢當」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈自京赴奉先縣詠懷五百字〉詩：「所愧為人父，無食致夭折。」</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;宋濂〈秦士錄〉：「兩生素負多才藝，聞弼言，大愧，下樓足不得成步。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">辱人而使之感到羞慚。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;表記》：「是故君子不以其所能者病人，不以人之所不能者愧人。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《後漢書&middot;卷二四&middot;馬援傳》：「季孟嘗折愧子陽而不受其爵。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">辜負、對不起。</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;方苞〈左忠毅公軼事〉：「吾上恐負朝廷，下恐愧吾師也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">羞慚。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「面有愧色」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
