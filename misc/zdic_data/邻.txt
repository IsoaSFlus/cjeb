                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">邻</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>邻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鄰</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>住处接近的人家：四～。左～右舍。</li>
	<li>接近，附近：～国。～家。～座。～里。～邦。</li>
	<li>接近，亲近：&ldquo;～以理者，知也&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古代五家为一邻，五邻为一里。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> neighbor; neighborhood</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> angrenzend, benachbart ,Lin  (Eig, Fam)​,Nachbar</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">邻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>邻</strong></p>
<p>鄰、隣 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从邑，粦( <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;n</span>)声。从&ldquo;邑&rdquo;，表示与行政区域有关。本义:古代的一种居民组织。五家为邻)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 周朝地方基层组织的户口单位 <span class="encs">[basic community unity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邻，五家为邻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五家为邻，五邻为里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;遂人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">渔翁家苇间，蜗舍无邻伍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陈造</span><span class="smcs">《泊慈湖北岸》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:邻伍(古制一邻有五家，五家为伍，故称邻伍);邻里</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 相连接的家户。邻居;邻国 <span class="encs">[neighbor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东邻杀牛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;坊记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">武义动于南邻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邻人京城氏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其邻人之父。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说难》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邻有敝舆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邻有短褐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恃交援而简近邻，怙强大之救，而侮所迫之国者，可亡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;亡征》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:邻党(邻里乡亲);邻佑(邻右。邻居);邻舍家(邻比。邻居);邻曲(邻居，左邻右舍的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 车行的声音 <span class="encs">[carriage&rsquo;s sound]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邻邻(众车行走的声音。同辚辚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;燐&rdquo;。磷火 <span class="encs">[phosphorescent light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">马血之为转邻也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;天瑞》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>邻</strong></p>
<p>鄰 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 五家为一组，彼此相距不远，因此引申为邻近;近邻 <span class="encs">[neighboring;adjacent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五家谓伍，又谓之邻。邻，连也，相接也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释州国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邻，近也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣哉邻哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;益稷》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恰比其邻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;正月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善谋邻国敌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;新序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">海内存知己，天涯若比邻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王勃</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邻有西塾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与国邻接。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:邻敌(邻近的敌国);邻境(地域相接);邻村(邻近的村舍);邻近(接近;靠近，在附近)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 众多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邻熟(谷物丰收，结实累累);邻邻(众多的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 亲密，亲近 <span class="encs">[intimate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邻好(睦邻友好);邻和(邻国或邻居相和好)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>邻</strong></p>
<p>鄰 <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 位置很接近，邻近 <span class="encs">[be near to;close to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">荆州</span>与国邻接，江山险固，沃野千里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无与福邻，祸乃不存。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;初见秦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;吝&rdquo;。顾惜，舍不得 <span class="encs">[stint]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谦卑而不邻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;刺复》</span></span></p>
