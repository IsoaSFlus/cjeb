                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>戎</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">róng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/róng.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>古代兵器的总称。</li><li>军队，军事：兵～。投笔从～（指文人从军）。～装。～马。</li><li>古代称兵车：御～。</li><li>大：～功。</li><li>称（方言，音如“<span class="diczx3">农</span>”）：“～有良翰”。</li><li>中国古代称西部民族：西～。～狄。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> arms, armaments; military affair</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Militärwesen  (S)​,Schwert  (S)​,Jung (Eigenname von 张<a href="./戎">戎</a>=Jung Chang)​  (Eig)​,Rong  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> armée,affaires militaires</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>戎</strong> <span class="dicpy">róng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/róng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从戈，从十。<span class="yhcs">“戈”</span>是兵器，<span class="yhcs">“十”</span>是铠甲的<span class="yhcs">“甲”</span>。本义:古代兵器的总称。弓、殳、矛、戈、戟为古代五戎)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[weapons]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戎，兵也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以习五戎。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戎器不粥于市。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“军器也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伏戎于莽。——<span class="smcs">《易·同人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弓矢戎兵。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:戎仗(兵器，军械);戎器(兵器);戎储(兵器储备);戎钺(泛指兵器。钺，古兵器名);戎具(兵器)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 兵车 <span class="encs">[chariot]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">元戎十乘。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·六月》</span>。传:夏曰钩车，殷曰寅车，周曰元戎。</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">梁弘</span>御戎。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:元戎(大战车);戎御(兵车的驾御者);戎轸(兵车);戎轩(兵车);戎路(古代帝王军中所乘的车)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 军队 <span class="encs">[army]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戎右少师。——<span class="smcs">《左传·桓公七年》</span>。注;<span class="yhcs">“军右也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以脩我戎。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·常武》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:投笔从戎;戎士(将士;兵士);戎级(军职);戎帅(军队的统帅);戎重(军事重任);戎威(军威);戎柄(军权);戎门(军门);戎政(军政);戎戍(行伍，军队);戎略(军事谋略);戎寄(军事任务);戎兵(军服和兵器)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 战争。敌对双方的军事行动 <span class="encs">[warfare]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戎成不退。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·雨无止》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:戎舰(战船);戎烬(遭受战火的破坏);戎缮(备战与修缮宫室之事);戎场(战场);戎戒(战备);戎捷(战利品)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 戎羌，中国古代称西部民族 <span class="encs">[an ancient name for the people in the west]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西和诸戎。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:戎狄(西戎和北狄);戎羌(借指我国少数民族地区);戎荒，戎落(戎族聚居地);戎骑(戎族的军队);戎羯(戎和羯);戎虏(古时对西方或北方少数名族的蔑称)</p><p><span
