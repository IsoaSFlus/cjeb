                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">讹</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>讹</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">訛</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄜˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>错误：～字。～误（文字、记载错误）。～谬。～传（<span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;n </span>）。以～传～。</li>
	<li>敲诈，假借某种理由向人强迫索取财物或其他权利：～诈。</li>
	<li>谣言：～言（➊诈伪的话，谣言；➋胡言乱语）。</li>
	<li>感化，变化：&ldquo;岁月迁～。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>野火烧。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> swindle, cheat; erroneous, wrong</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">讹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>讹</strong></p>
<p>訛、譌 <span class="dicpy">&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，化声。字本作&ldquo;譌&rdquo;。本义:谣言)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rumor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">譌，伪言也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 字亦作&ldquo;讹&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民之讹言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;沔水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">讹言大水至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;成帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:讹言(谣言)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 差错 <span class="encs">[error]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:讹舛(差错;谬误);讹阙(错误残缺);讹头(过失，污点，把柄);讹替(错误)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>讹</strong></p>
<p>訛 <span class="dicpy">&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 讹诈 <span class="encs">[black-mail]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:讹人(讹诈人);讹头(讹诈的钱财)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 改变;感化 <span class="encs">[change;move]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或降于阿，或饮于池，或寝或讹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;无羊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 变化 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">式讹尔心。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;节南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岁月迁讹，斯风渐笃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 不正确的 <span class="encs">[incorrect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">平秩南讹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:讹音</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">訛</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“讹”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/訛">訛</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              訛·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A1B.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】五禾切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】吾禾切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音囮。【玉篇】與譌同。僞也，謬也，舛也。【詩&middot;小雅】民之訛言，寧莫之懲。【傳】訛，僞也。【說文】【宋書&middot;五行志】引《詩》<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>作譌言。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】言也。【註】世以妖言爲訛。【前漢&middot;翟方進傳】民人訛謠。【吳志&middot;孫皓傳】妖訛橫興。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】化也。【書&middot;堯典】平秩南訛。【傳】訛，化也。【史記&middot;五帝紀】作南譌。【索隱】作南爲，謂所當爲之事也。【讀書通】又通作僞。【前漢&middot;王莽傳】毎縣則薅，以勸南僞。【師古註】僞，讀曰訛。化也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】動也。【詩&middot;小雅】或寢或訛。【傳】訛，動也。【韓詩】作譌。覺也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】通作吪。【詩&middot;王風】尚寐無吪。【傳】吪，亦動也。本亦作訛。</p>
<p>又獸名。【神異經】西南荒中出訛獸，狀若兔，人面，能言，常欺人，言東而西，言惡而善。肉美，食之言不眞。一名誕。</p>
<p>又蛇名。【埤雅】恩平郡譜，蛇謂之訛。</p>
<p>又火名。【柳宗元&middot;述舊言懷詩】訛火亟生煆。【註】野火也。【山海經】作譌火。</p>
<p>又姓。【唐書&middot;南蠻傳】巂州新安城旁有六姓蠻，三曰訛蠻。【宋史&middot;眞宗紀】夏國監軍訛勃囉。</p>
<p>又【集韻】牛何切，音莪。亦動也。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">譌</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“讹”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/譌">譌</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三">卷三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三&amp;swbs=言">言</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8B4C.svg">                          <p>譌言也。从言爲聲。《詩》曰：&ldquo;民之譌言。&rdquo;五禾切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8B4C_6380.svg" /><span class="swbf">(譌)</span><span class="ywyy">譌言也。</span><span class="swdz">疑當作僞言也。唐風：人之爲言。定本作僞言。箋云：爲，人爲善言以稱薦之，欲使見進用也。小雅：民之訛言。箋云：訛，僞也。人以僞言相陷入。按爲、僞、譌古同，通用。尚書南譌，周禮注、漢書皆作南僞。</span><span class="ywyy">从言爲聲。</span><span class="swdz">五禾切。十七部。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：民之譌言。</span><span class="swdz">今小雅作訛。說文無訛有吪。吪，動也。訛者俗字。</span></p>                          </div>
