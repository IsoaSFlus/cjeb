                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贻</strong></p><p>貽 <span class="dicpy">yí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，台声。从<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>，与财富有关。本义:赠送)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[present]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贻我彤管。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·静女》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">美人之贻。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余嘉其能行古道，作<span class="diczx2">苏</span>泛 <span class="diczx2">赤壁</span>云。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古经传中诒、贻见互。 behind;bequeath]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贻我来牟。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·思文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坐贻聋瞽。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
