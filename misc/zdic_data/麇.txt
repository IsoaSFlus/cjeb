						<p>● <strong>麇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄣ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>獐子。</li>
	<li>春秋时国名。都今陕西省白河县东南。</li>
	<li>春秋时地名。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>麇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&uacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>成群：～至。～集（聚集，群集）。</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/稛">稛</a>&rdquo;。捆绑。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> general name for the hornless deer; to collect to band together</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> daim</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">麇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>麇</strong></p>
<p>麕 <span class="dicpy">jūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 獐子 <span class="encs">[river deery]</span>，哺乳动物，形状像鹿而较小，身体上面黄褐色，腹部白色，毛较粗，没有角。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:麇惊(麇性怯弱易惊。比喻像麇一样受到惊吓);麇聚(像獐子那样依类群居);麇包(用包茅包的死獐子);麇獐(即獐子);麇窜(像獐子那样惊慌逃窜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[name of an ancient country]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 春秋时麇国之地，在今湖北省郧县西</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚子</span>代 <span class="diczx2">麇</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 春秋时楚邑，在今湖南省岳阳东南</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚王</span>使由于城 <span class="diczx2">麇</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">q&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>麇</strong></p>
<p>麕 <span class="dicpy">q&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 成群 <span class="encs">[flock together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求诸侯而麇至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公五年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:&ldquo;麇，群也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:麇聚(麇集);麇至(成群而来);麇居(群居)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">jūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集下" target="_blank">亥集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集下&amp;bs=鹿" target="_blank">鹿</a>】
                              麇·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9E87.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】居筠切【集韻】【韻會】俱倫切【正韻】規倫切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音頵。【說文】麞也。似鹿。麞性驚，又善聚散，故又名麇，一物二名也。【左傳&middot;哀十四年】逢澤有介麇焉。</p>
<p>又國名。【左傳&middot;文十年】厥貉之會，麇子逃歸。</p>
<p>又【文十一年】楚子伐麇，敗麇師於防渚。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;定五年】吳師居麇。【註】麇，地名。</p>
<p>又【集韻】拘云切【韻會】拘雲切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音君。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】衢雲切【韻會】【正韻】渠云切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音羣。【左傳&middot;昭五年】求諸侯而麇至。【註】麇，羣也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】丘粉切【集韻】去粉切【韻會】苦粉切【正韻】苦隕切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27F10.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧼐</span>。束縛也。【左傳&middot;哀二年】羅無勇，麇之。</p>
