						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>聘</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pìn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pìn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>访问：～问（古代指代表国家访问友邦）。通～。～使往来。</li><li>请人担任职务：～书。～用。～请。招～。</li><li>定婚或女子出嫁：～礼（彩礼）。～金。～姑娘。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>聘</strong> <span class="dicpy">pìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耳，甹( <span class="dicpy">pīng</span>)声。探问消息要靠耳朵，故从<span class="yhcs">“耳”</span>。本义:访问，探问)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[call on;drop in ]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聘，访也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聘，问也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靡使归聘。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采薇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">季冬</span>聘王梦。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·占梦》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聘</span><span class="diczx2">申叔</span>于 <span class="diczx2">陈蔡</span>兮。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·冯衍传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸侯使大夫问于诸侯曰聘。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡诸侯之邦交岁相问也，殷相聘也。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大行人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:聘弓(古代聘问时持用之弓);聘贡(聘问朝贡);聘享(聘问献纳);聘使(奉命聘问)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 聘请 <span class="encs">[engage;employ]</span>。如:礼聘(用尊敬的方式聘请);招聘(用广告的方式聘请);聘君(即以币帛或重金聘请而来的聘士、征士);聘召(以礼征召贤能);聘纳(以礼娶亲);聘币(聘人或订婚所订的礼物)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 旧时称订婚、迎娶之礼 <span class="encs">[betroth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聘则为妻。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:聘定(犹婚约)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“娉”</span>。女子订婚或出嫁 <span class="encs">[(of girl) be married or be married off]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">声伯</span>之母不聘。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十一年》</span></p><p><span
