                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>恢</strong> <span class="dicpy">huī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，灰声。本义:志气大;大;广大)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(ambition) great;high]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恢，大也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不恢于夏家。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恢郭也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·文公十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">配稷契兮恢唐功。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·守志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恢恑谲怪。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酒量犹恢。——</span><span class="diczx2">李曾伯</span><span class="smcs">《沁园春》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以无厚入有间，恢恢乎其于游刃必有余地矣。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·养生主》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:恢伟(壮伟，魁伟);恢奇(雄伟杰出的样子);恢台(广大的样子);恢卓(宽大，高明);恢恢(宽大广阔的样子);恢郭(宽大的外城)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“诙”</span>。诙谐;怪诞 <span class="encs">[humorous]</span>。如:恢谐(诙谐);恢谑(诙谐戏谑);恢诡(荒诞怪异);恢诞(浮夸怪诞)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>恢</strong> <span class="dicpy">huī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button class="diczx1">恢，大之也。——<span class="smcs">《增韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恢弘志士之气。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:恢拓(扩大，开拓);恢张(推广，扩展);恢大(弘大);恢拓(拓展，开拓扩展);恢宣(广为发扬)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 恢复 <span class="encs">[restore]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">就西</span><span class="diczx2">粤</span>恢中原，则一隅甚大。—— <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span>等辑<span class="smcs">《东明见闻录》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
