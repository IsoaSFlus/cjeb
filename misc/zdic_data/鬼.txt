                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鬼</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鬼</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">guǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄟˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>某些宗教或迷信的人所说的人死后的灵魂：～魂。～魅。～蜮（①鬼怪；②阴险害人的）。</li>
	<li>阴险，不光明：～话。～黠。～胎。</li>
	<li>对人的蔑称或憎称：酒～。烟～。～子。吝啬～。</li>
	<li>恶劣，糟糕（限做定语）：～天气。</li>
	<li>机灵，敏慧（多指小孩子）：～精灵。</li>
	<li>表示爱昵的称呼：小～。机灵～。</li>
	<li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鬼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鬼</strong> <span class="dicpy">guǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，下面是个&ldquo;人&rdquo;字，上面象一个可怕的脑袋(非&ldquo;田&rdquo;字)，是人们想象中的似人非人的怪物。&ldquo;鬼&rdquo;是汉字部首之一，从&ldquo;鬼&rdquo;的字大多与迷信、鬼神有关。本义:迷信的人认为人死后有&ldquo;灵魂&rdquo;，称之为&ldquo;鬼&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[devil;apparition;ghost]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鬼，人所归为鬼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众生必死，死必归士，此之谓鬼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载鬼一车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;睽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为鬼为蜮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;何人斯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">苍颉</span>作书鬼夜哭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身既死兮神以灵，子魂魄兮为鬼雄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;国殇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾作此书时，尚是世中一人;汝看此书时，吾已成为阴间一鬼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鬼伯(鬼王);鬼雄(鬼中的强者);鬼物(鬼怪，鬼魅。有别于生人，故称鬼物);鬼教(说阴间鬼神之事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 万物的精怪 <span class="encs">[goblin;demon;spirit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鬼者，老物之精也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;订鬼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庶人庶士无庙，死曰鬼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭法》</span></span></p>

<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】虔儇，慧也。自關而東趙魏之閒謂之黠。或謂之鬼。</p>
