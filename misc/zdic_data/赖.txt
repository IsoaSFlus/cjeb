                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>赖</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">賴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lài  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lài.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄞˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>倚靠，仗恃：依～。仰～。百无聊～（精神上无所寄托，感到什么都没意思）。</li>    <li>留在某处不肯走开：～着不走。</li>    <li>不承认：抵～。～账。～婚。</li>    <li>刁钻泼辣，不讲道理；游手好闲，行为不端的人：～子。无～。</li>    <li>诬，怪罪：诬～。</li>    <li>不好，劣：好～。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赖</strong></p><p>賴、頼 <span class="dicpy">lài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，剌( <span class="dicpy">là</span>)声。本义:得益;赢利)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[profit;gain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赖，赢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赖，取也。——<span class="smcs">《方言十三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先王岂有赖焉。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“利也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">相语以利，相示以赖，相陈以知贾。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>则益，为 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>则不赖矣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:赖利(得利)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 依靠;依赖;依恃、凭藉 <span class="encs">[rely on;depend on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奸人赖赏而富。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·诡使》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王室之不坏，繄伯舅是赖。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以有补于人君，人君赖之。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·逢遇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而赖吾之德。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">赖子存之。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾赖是以食吾躯。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赖肤觉之助。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:赖衣求食;赖天(依靠上天的庇卫);赖庇(依靠别人的庇卫)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 拒绝承认，抵赖 <span class="encs">[deny]</span>。如:赖债(不承认所欠的债务，或故意拖延不还)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 耍赖。亦指无赖的作风和行为 <span class="encs">[make a scene]</span>。如:赖子(赖皮子，赖皮狗。刁钻撒泼、不讲道理的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 留在某处不肯离开 <span class="encs">[hang on]</span>。如:赖着不走;赖地(躺在地上耍赖)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 责怪，应该受责<span class="encs">[to be to blame for]</span>。如:这事全赖我</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 〈方〉∶逃 <span class="encs">[escape]</span>。如:赖学</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎
