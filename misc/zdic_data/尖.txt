						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>尖</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>锐利的末端或细小的部分：～锐。～刻（说话尖酸刻薄）。～酸。打～。～端。</li><li>感觉敏锐：眼～。</li><li>声音高而细：～脆。～嫩。</li><li>出类拔萃的人或事物：技术～子。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>尖</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从小，从大。一头小一头大为尖。本义:物体的末端细削而锐利)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sharp-pointed;pointed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尖，锐也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 按，<span class="yhcs">“尖”</span>是后起的会意字。又如:尖山(尖而高的山);尖屁股(借指坐不安稳);尖担担柴两头脱(比喻两头落空，什么也没捞着);尖顶木驴(尖头木驴。古代攻城战车)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 音调细而高，刺耳 <span class="encs">[shrill;high-pitched]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">促织声尖尖似针，更深刺着旅人心。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾岛</span><span class="smcs">《客思》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:尖声;尖嗓子;尖儿(戏曲中指忽然高亢的唱腔);尖脆(形容声音尖细清脆);尖厉(形容声音高而刺耳)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 新颖别致 <span class="encs">[novel and unique]</span>。如:尖新(新颖别致);尖巧(尖新奇巧);尖恰恰(形容尖而巧);尖颖(犹新颖;新奇)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 感觉灵敏 <span class="encs">[sensitive]</span>。如:尖恰恰(尖巧可爱的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 尖刻;刻薄 <span class="encs">[acrimonious]</span>。如:尖快(说话锋利尖刻);尖言冷语(言语尖刻);尖灵(尖刻机灵);尖酸(言辞尖锐刁钻)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 雄性的、与雄性有关的、具有雄性特征的<span class="encs">[male]</span>。如:尖脐</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 〈方〉∶奸滑的;圆滑的 <span class="encs">[crafty]</span>。如:这个人可尖了</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>尖</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 细长的手指 <span class="encs">[slender finger]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歌彻阳春酒半醺，玉尖搦管蘸香云。——</span><span class="diczx2">杨维桢</span><span class="smcs">《学书诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 细削的末端 <span class="encs">[point;tip]</span>。如:笔尖，刀尖;针尖儿;塔尖儿;牛角尖;尖儿(物体细小锐利的尖端;脚尖;出众的;最好的;戏曲中忽然高亢的唱腔)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 超出同类的人或物 <span class="encs">[the best of it's kind]</span>。如:冒尖;拔尖;尖儿货</p><p>◎ <strong>尖</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 钻进，进入 <span class="encs">[get into]</span>。如:她把身尖到两个男人之间，坐了下来;有一阵风打窗缝里尖进来</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 工间或旅途中小憩并略进饮食 <span class="encs">[take a rest]</span>。如:尖站(大路上隔一定里程供休息吃饭的地方);尖后(途中打尖以后)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
