						<p>● <strong>免</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">miǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧㄢˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>去掉，除掉：～除。～税。～费。～官。～检。罢～。豁～。</li>	<li>不被某种事物所涉及：避～。～疫。～不了。在所难～。</li>	<li>不可，不要：闲人～进。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/勉">勉</a>&rdquo;，勉励。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/娩">娩</a>&rdquo;，分娩。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">免</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>免</strong> <span class="dicpy">miǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，下面是&ldquo;人&rdquo;，上面象人头上戴帽形，是冠冕的&ldquo;冕&rdquo;本字。由于假借为&ldquo;免除&rdquo;义，另造&ldquo;冕&rdquo;字。假借义:免除，避免)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 脱掉;脱落 <span class="encs">[take off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">免，脱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左右免胄而下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其将不免乎!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">免使者而复鼓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">免胄而听命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语六》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>师过 <span class="diczx2">周</span>北门，左右免胄而下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谓身不死得免去皮肤也?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;道虚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:免胄(脱下头盔);免丧(脱去丧服);免绖(脱去麻带而仅着丧服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 赦免，释放 <span class="encs">[release;set free]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">暂而免诸国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因而获免。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:惠而免之;免囚(被赦免的囚犯);免坐(对罪犯亲属免予治罪);免放(释放);免赦(赦免)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 逃避灾难或罪过 <span class="encs">[avoid;refrain from]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诚得如此，臣免死罪矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;内储说上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以免于难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于溷藩以免。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即免死。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五国既丧，</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>亦不免。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/514D_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(免)</span><span class="ywyy">兔逸也。从免不見足會意。</span><span class="swdz">許書失此字，而形聲多用爲偏旁，不可闕也。今補。免免之異，異於其足。能鹿㲋从比，鳥烏兔从匕。兔象其蹲居之形，有足有尾，其字當橫視之。兔之走最迅速，其足不可諟見，故免省一畫。兔不見於人則謂之免。孫子云：始如處女，敵人開戶。後如脫兔，敵不及拒是也。此二字之別也。引伸之，凡逃逸者皆謂之免。假借爲袒免，爲免薧。依毛詩與洒韵，古音在十三部，轉入十四部也。今音忙辨切。錢氏大昕云：兔免當是一字，漢人作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23729.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣜩</span>誤分之。似未然。俛从免，自是會意。</span></p>                          </div>
