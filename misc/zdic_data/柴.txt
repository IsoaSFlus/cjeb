						<p>● <strong>柴</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄞˊ</span></p>

	<li>瘦，不松软：～鸡。～心。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">柴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>柴</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，此声。本义:捆束的细木小柴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[faggot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大者可析谓之薪，小者合束谓之柴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃取蒙冲斗舰十艘，载燥荻枯柴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指木柴，小木散材，也指作燃料的木柴<span class="encs">[firewood;wood]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柴，小木散材也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树枳棘与薪柴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;愍命》</span>。注:&ldquo;枯枝为柴。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是时东郡烧草，以故薪柴少。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;沟洫志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:柴荆(乡里用木板、荆条编制而成的门);柴禾(木柴;柴草);木柴;劈柴;柴水(打柴汲水);柴市(木柴交易处);柴山(生长矮小灌木杂草的山)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 枯枝，老木 <span class="encs">[fuzz stick;old wood]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:柴立(有如枯木的站立。也用来形容人清瘦的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>柴</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 烧柴祭天。后又写作&ldquo;祡&rdquo; <span class="encs">[burn firewood and worship heaven]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岁二月，东巡守，至于</span><span class="diczx2">岱宗</span>，柴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柴望秩于山川。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柴于上帝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柴而望祀山川。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 注:&ldquo;柴，祭天告至也。&rdquo;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>柴</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>〈方〉∶ 干瘦，皮包骨 <span class="encs">[bony]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:柴毁(身体衰瘦如枯柴);柴瘠(骨瘦如柴)</span></p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>柴</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;i</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 编木维护四周 <span class="encs">[fence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">周武王</span>]</span>乃封 <span class="diczx2">比干</span>之墓，表 <span class="diczx2">商容</span>之闾，柴 <span class="diczx2">箕子</span>之门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></p>

<p>又【說文】徐鍇曰：師行野次，豎散材爲區落，名曰柴籬。後人語譌轉入去聲。</p>

<p>又【集韻】鋤加切，音查。小木也。</p>
<p>又【類篇】杈宜切，音差。柴池，參差也。【張揖&middot;上林賦】作柴虒。【註】不齊也。</p>
<p>又【說文】作㧘。【集韻】子智切【正韻】資四切，音恣。積也，謂積禽也。【詩&middot;小雅】助我舉柴。</p>
<p>又【集韻】一曰搣，頰勞也。</p>
<p>又仕懈切，音砦。柴藩落也。</p>
<p>又士邁切，音寨。義同。</p>
<p>又叶七何切，音蹉。【劉向&middot;九歎】折芳枝與瓊華兮，樹枳棘與薪柴。掘荃蕙與射干兮，耘藜藿與蘘荷。</p>
