data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xié.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>(形声。从人，皆声。本义:共同在一起)同本义<span class="encs">[be together with;be in the company of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">偕，俱也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与雨偕也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夙夜必偕。——<span class="smcs">《诗·魏风·陟岵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故由由然与之偕而不自失焉。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙</span><span class="diczx2">丑</span>上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">俄人</span>偕 <span class="diczx2">英</span>、 <span class="diczx2">法</span>、 <span class="diczx2">美</span>三国合兵犯 <span class="diczx2">天津</span>。——<span class="smcs">《清史稿·奕山传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">偕数友出。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">即偕洋商。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·女日鸡鸣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与子偕行。——<span class="smcs">《诗·秦风·无衣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">客不得已与偕行。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:偕隐(同居山林，不求官禄);偕作(一起行动);偕适(同趋一个方向);偕生之疾(与生俱来的病)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div
