                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">悲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>悲</strong> <span class="dicpy">bēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，非声。本义:哀伤;痛心)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grieved;sad]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悲，痛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">悲，伤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忧心且悲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鼓钟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我心伤悲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;草虫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">并于肺则悲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;宣明五气篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至于悲谷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文》</span>。注:&ldquo;西南方之大壑也。言深峻，临其上令人悲思，故名。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何哭之甚悲也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少壮不努力，老大徒伤悲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;长歌行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不以物喜，不以己悲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:悲怆(悲伤);悲怨(悲痛怨恨);悲惋(悲伤惋惜);悲吟(悲伤哀叹);悲忧(悲伤忧虑);悲郁(悲伤忧郁);悲涕(悲痛的眼泪);悲哭(悲痛号哭);悲怒(悲痛愤怒);悲疚(悲痛);悲戚(悲痛忧伤);悲恻(悲痛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 悲哀 <span class="encs">[doleful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">故遂忍</span>悲为汝言之。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:悲雨(因悲哀而泪下如雨);悲哽(悲哀哽咽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 悲壮;悲凉 <span class="encs">[solemn and stirring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戍角悲吟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姜夔</span><span class="smcs">《扬州慢》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:悲角(悲壮的号角声);悲风(凄厉的风声);悲默(忧伤沉默)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 悲愤 <span class="encs">[grieved and indignation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余闻而愈悲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>悲</strong> <span class="dicpy">bēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 眷念;怅望 <span class="encs">[think of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">游子悲故乡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注:&ldquo;悲谓顾念也。&rdquo;</p>
