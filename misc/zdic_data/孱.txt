<p><span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>软弱，弱小：～王（软弱无能的君王）。～弱。～～。</li>
	<li>卑微：～琐（卑贱无能）。～微（地位低微）。</li>
	<li>窘迫。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>孱</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">c&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>只用于&ldquo;孱头&rdquo;（方言，软弱无能的人）。</li>
</ol>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>孱</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从尸，犹从屋省，本义:狭窄)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[narrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孱，迮也，&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孱，窄也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 浅陋，低劣 <span class="encs">[meagre;mean;inferior]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">术学肤孱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋祁</span><span class="smcs">《授龙图阁谢恩表》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形容谨小慎微的样子 <span class="encs">[be cautious and meticulous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子博学而孱守之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;曾子立事》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 懦弱 <span class="encs">[cowardly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若素名勇，徒能藉贫孱者耳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孱夫(懦弱的人);孱王(懦弱的君王)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 瘦弱 <span class="encs">[fragile]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:孱肌(瘦弱的肌体);孱质(瘦弱的身体)</span></p>

                              孱·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5B71.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】士連切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】鋤連切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音潺。【說文】迮也，从孨在尸下。一曰呻吟也。【玉篇】弱也。【廣韻】劣也。【史記&middot;張耳傳】趙相貫高曰：吾王，孱王也。【註】孟康曰：冀州人謂愞弱爲孱。</p>
<p>又【集韻】昨閑切。窄也。</p>
<p>又子仙切，音煎。窘也，孱蹙也。今俗有孱蹙語。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】【集韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>士限切，音棧。孱陵，古地名。【前漢&middot;地理志】在武陵郡。</p>
<p>又與巉通。【司馬相如&middot;大人賦】放散畔驤以孱顏。【註】孱顏，卽巉巖。【蘇軾詩】攝衣步孱顏。【註】山額曰顏。</p>
