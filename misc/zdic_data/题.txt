                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>题</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">題</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>写作或讲演内容的总名目：～目。主～。话～。～材。～旨。</li>
	<li>练习或考试时要求解答的问题：试～。问答～。</li>
	<li>写上，签署：～名。～字。～壁。～诗。～辞。～跋。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">题</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>题</strong></p>
<p>題 <span class="dicpy">t&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>)，是声。页，头。本义:额头)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[front]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">题，额也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雕题交趾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤眉圆题。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫加之以衡扼，齐之以月题。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;马蹄》</span>。释文引</span><span class="diczx2">司马崔</span>云:&ldquo;月题，马额上当颅如月形者也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">文题白身，名曰孟极。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;北山经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雕题黑齿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">连缓耳，琐雕题。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;杜笃传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="smcs">《雕题交阯》</span>&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;南蛮西南夷传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:题注(以头额撞击);黑牛白题(黑牛白额)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 物品的前端或顶端 <span class="encs">[end]</span> 堂高数仞，榱题数尺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:榱题(屋檐的椽头。榱，椽子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 题目;标题 <span class="encs">[subject;title;examination question]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣尝私习此赋，请试他题。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">脱脱</span>等<span class="smcs">《宋史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:试题(考试时让应考者作答的问题);题头(标题，篇目);题意(题目的旨意);题号(标题名称);题位(题目的要求，作文的规则)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 书签;标签 <span class="encs">[label]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">委之在深箧，蠹鱼坏其题。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《感兴八首》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 标志 <span class="encs">[mark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲垦荒田，先立表题。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 奏章。明、清两代公文用语之一。又指上奏 <span class="encs">[memorial to the throne]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 如:题参(参奏;奏本弹劾);题本(上奏章);题估(清代公文用语);题奏(题本用奏本);题留(清代公文用语);题补(清代公文用语);题销(上奏报销);题请(上奏呈请)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>题</strong></p>
<p>題 <span class="dicpy">t&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 书写;题署 <span class="encs">[inscribe]</span></p>
