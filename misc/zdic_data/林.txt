                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>林</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄣˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>长在一片土地上的许多树木或竹子：树～。森～。～海。～薮（➊山林小泽；➋喻丛集的处所）。</li>
	<li>聚集在一起的同类的人或事物：书～。艺～。碑～。儒～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> forest, grove; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Wald  (S)​,Lin  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (petite)​ forêt,bois (n.m)​,bosquet</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">林</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>林</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从二木。表示树木丛生。本义:丛聚的树木或竹子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[forest;grove;woods]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">林，平土有丛木曰林。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">野外谓之林。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有鹤在林。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;白华》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">每大林麓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;序官&middot;林衡》</span>。注:&ldquo;竹木生平地曰林。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山中丛木曰林。林，森也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忽逢桃花林，夹岸数百步。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔽林间窥之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《黔之驴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:竹木林;山林(有山有树林的地方);深山老林;防护林;林落(树林，丛林);林浪(林琅、琳琅、林郎。丛林);林麓(森林与山脚);林泉(林木与泉石);林错(林中树木交错，形容众多)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指野外或退隐的地方 <span class="encs">[secluded place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">富贵还乡国，光辉满旧林。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张说</span><span class="smcs">《和魏仆射还乡》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:林木(指山林田野退隐之处);林下人(退隐之人。林，指退隐之地);林丘(指隐居的地方)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 泛指人或事物的会聚，汇集处 <span class="encs">[circle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士有此五者，然后可以托于世，而列于君子之林矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:林府(事物众多之处);林薮(比喻事物聚集的处所)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 郊外，野外 <span class="encs">[surbur]</span>。如:林坰(郊野。野外称林，林外称垌)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 林业 <span class="encs">[forestry]</span>。如:农林牧副渔各行各业</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>林</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 众多的样子 <span class="encs">[various]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">林者，众也。万物成熟，种类众多也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟人之初，总总而生，林林而群。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《贞符》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:林戈(如林之多。极言其多);林立(如同树林一样密密麻麻地竖立着，形容数量很多)</p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

                              林·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6797.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】力尋切【集韻】【韻會】犂針切【正韻】犂沈切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音臨。【說文】平土有叢木曰林。徐曰：叢木，故从二木。平土，故二木齊。【詩&middot;小雅】依彼平林。</p>
<p>又野外謂之林。【詩&middot;周南】施于中林。</p>
<p>又山木曰林。【穀梁傳&middot;僖十四年】林屬于山爲麓。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;地官&middot;林衡註】竹木曰林，水衡曰衡。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】林，君也。</p>
<p>又盛貌。【詩&middot;小雅】有壬有林。</p>
<p>又林鐘，律名。【禮&middot;月令】季夏之月律中林鐘。《周禮》作函鐘。</p>
<p>又羽林，星名。應劭曰：天有羽林，大將軍之星也。林喩若林木，羽翼，鷙擊之意，故以名武官。【前漢&middot;宣帝紀】取從軍死事者之子，養爲羽林軍，號羽林孤兒。</p>
<p>又綠林，荆州山名。【後漢&middot;劉元傳】諸亡命集于綠林。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓譜】殷比干後，避難長林山，因氏。</p>
<p>又平王世子林開之後，望出南安。○按《說文》林自爲部，棼、楚等字从之，今倂入。</p>
