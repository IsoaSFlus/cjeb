                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>芟</strong> <span class="dicpy">shān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从殳( <span class="dicpy">shū</span>)。殳，兵器。以殳除草。本义:铲除杂草)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mow grass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芟，刈草也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尝之日，涖卜来岁之芟。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·肆师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芟蕰崇之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">载芟载柞。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·载芟》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“除草曰芟。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:芟夷(除草，割草);芟荑(同<span class="yhcs">“芟夷”</span>。刈除);芟柞(指耕作);芟翦(芟剪。剪除;修剪);芟薙(刈除);芟柞(割除草木);芟秽(割除杂草。后用以比喻除去祸害)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 斩杀;消灭;清除 <span class="encs">[kill;eliminate;clear away]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芟野菼。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“杀也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今</span><span class="diczx2">操</span>芟夷大难，略已平矣。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:芟讨(讨灭敌人);芟锄(除去，铲除);芟刈(割，引申为杀戮);芟夷(铲除;除草;亦指杀戮);芟定(平定)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 删除 <span class="encs">[delete;strike out]</span>。如:芟削(删除);芟翦(芟剪。删除)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>芟</strong> <span class="dicpy">shān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大镰刀 <span class="encs">[scythe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">权节其用，耒、耜、耞、芟。——<span class="smcs">《国语·齐语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 如:芟割(用镰刀收割)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="encs">[用作防汛护堤]</span>砍下的芦荻 <span class="encs">[reed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡伐芦荻谓之<span class="yhcs">“芟”</span>，伐山木榆柳枝叶谓之<span class="yhcs">“梢”</span>，辮竹纠芟为索。——<span class="smcs">《宋史》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
