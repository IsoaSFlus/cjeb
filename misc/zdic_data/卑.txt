                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>卑</strong> <span class="dicpy">bēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (椑之古字，本义是圆搕，一种酒器)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 地位低微 <span class="encs">[low and degrading;humble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卑，庳也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">文王</span>卑服。——<span class="smcs">《书·无逸》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人皆赴高，己独赴下卑也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·水地》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天尊地卑。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">位卑则足羞。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先帝不以臣卑鄙。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:卑弁(下级武职对上级的自称);卑寒(谦词。谓位卑家贫);卑陋(低矮简陋);卑辱(卑微屈辱);卑人(地位低下的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 地势低下。与<span class="yhcs">“高”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[low]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谓山盖卑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·正月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卑高已陈。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闻</span><span class="diczx2">长沙</span>卑湿。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:卑小(矮小);卑隘(低矮狭窄);卑洼(低洼)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 衰微，衰弱 <span class="encs">[feeble;weak]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王室其将卑乎?——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 素质低下 <span class="encs">[inferior]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非天质之卑，则必不若余之专耳。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 谦恭 <span class="encs">[modest and courteous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卑礼厚币以招贤者。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:卑谨(谦卑恭敬);卑顺(谦恭驯顺)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>卑</strong> <span class="dicpy">bēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 轻视，小看 <span class="encs">[look down on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何以卑我?——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吏之所卑，法之所尊也。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:卑侮王室(贱视并凌辱王室成员)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 低俯 <span class="encs">[bow]</span>。如:卑身贱体(弯腰俯首，屈从奉迎);卑躬(卑体，卑身。低身，屈身)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr"
