						<p>● <strong>蓉</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用某些植物的果肉或种子制成的粉状物：椰～。豆～。</li>
	<li>中国四川省成都市的别称：～城。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蓉</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 四川成都市的简称 <span class="encs">[another name for Chengdu]</span>。五代后蜀孟昶于宫苑城上遍植木芙蓉，因名成都为芙蓉城，后简称蓉城或蓉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 豆类、瓜果煮熟晒干后磨粉做的糕点馅儿 <span class="encs">[mash]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:豆蓉;莲蓉;椰蓉</span></p>
