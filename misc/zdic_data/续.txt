                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>续</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">續</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩˋ</span></p><ol><li>连接，接下去：连～。继～。陆～。狗尾～貂（喻不好的东西连接在好的东西的后面，亦喻事物的前后优劣不相称；又常用为自谦之辞，表示不敢与人等列并美的意思）。</li><li>在原有的上面再加：～编。～集。把茶～上。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> continue, carry on; succeed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> andauern, fortdauern</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> continuer,suivre,ajouter,additionner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">续</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>续</strong></p><p>續 <span class="dicpy">xù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，卖( <span class="dicpy">yù</span>)声。本义:连接起来，接上)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[link]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">续，联也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">续，继也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">续衽钩边。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·深衣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">貂不足，狗尾续。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·赵王伦传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是断手而续以玉也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·用人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一时人急智生，把自己头发拔下一绺，登时把弓弦续好。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:续弦;续篇;续编</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 继续 <span class="encs">[continue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岁时更续。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·巾车》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刑者不可复续。——<span class="smcs">《史记·扁仓传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">亡秦</span>之续。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以夜续昼，由是获声誉。——<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">续发众人。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:续终(继续到底);续续(相继不绝，连续不断);持续(延续不断)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 继承 <span class="encs">[inherit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝复为太史，则续吾祖矣。——<span class="smcs">《史记·太史公自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:续世(谓世代继承)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 添，加 <span class="encs">[add;supply more]</span>。如:续添(增加，后援);往灶里续柴;给客人续水;续短(补充不足);续寿(添寿)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 妻子死后再娶 <span class="encs">[remarry after one’s wife dies]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">才死了老婆…他在</span><span class="diczx2">奶奶庙</span>大会上见过 <span class="diczx2">小芹</span>一面，愿意续她。—— <span class="diczx2">赵树理</span><span class="smcs">《小二黑结婚》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:续母(继母);续弦;续继(妻死再娶)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 传递 <span class="encs">[transmit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">教顺施续，而知能流通，由此观之，学不可已明矣。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·脩务》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 后同于前，旧事重演 <span class="encs">[repeat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谗言繁兴，延及寡君之绍续昆裔。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:续约(当一个条约的时效将终，再拟一个条约，作为辅助性条款，用以延续正约的期限或补充正约的不足者，谓之)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
