                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">皱</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>皱</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">皺</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>脸上起的褶纹，物体上的褶纹：～纹。～褶。～痕。～襞。防～。</li>	<li>使生褶纹：～眉头。眉头一～，计上心来。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wrinkles, creases, folds</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Falten werfen, falten, knittern, kniffen, zerknittern  (V)​,knicken  (V)​,runzeln  (V)​,faltig, gerunzelt</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> plisser,froisser,pli,ride</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">皱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>皱</strong></p>
<p>皺 <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从皮，刍( <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span>)声。本义:皮肤因松弛而起的纹路)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wrinkle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫道韶华镇长在，发白面皱专相待。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《嘲少年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">面皮嘴唇烧的紫绛皱裂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:皱皮(皮肤上的皱纹);皱文(皱纹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 衣、物等经折叠而显出痕迹<span class="encs">[fold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前低划开阔，烂漫堆众皱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《南山诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:皱褶(褶皱。指衣服上折叠的纹路);皱彀(喻指水面的微波)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>皱</strong></p>
<p>皺 <span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 收缩;紧蹙 <span class="encs">[wrinkle;crease;crumple]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">琼窗春断双蛾皱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李煜</span><span class="smcs">《采桑子》</span></p>
