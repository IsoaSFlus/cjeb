						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>履</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lǚ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄩˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>鞋：西装革～。削足适～。</li>    <li>践踩，走过：～历（a.个人的经历；b.记载履历的文件）。～任。～险如夷（走在险峻的地方，像走在平坦的路上，喻身处险境而不畏惧，又喻安全地度过险境）。如～薄冰（身临其境）。</li>    <li>步伐：步～维艰。</li>    <li>执行，实行：～行。～约。</li>    <li>〔～带〕在拖拉机、坦克车等的车轮上围绕的钢质链带。</li>    <li>指领土：“赐我先君～”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">履</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>履</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字，从尸，即人;从彳( <span class="dicpy">chì</span>)，表示与行走有关。履在战国以前一般只作动词用。一般用<span class="yhcs">“屦”</span>称鞋子。用<span class="yhcs">“鞋”</span>是唐以后的事了。本义:践踏)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tread on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">履霜，履虎尾。——<span class="smcs">《易·坤》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可以履霜。——<span class="smcs">《诗·魏风·葛屦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">履蒯席。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">地履其黄。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">足之所履。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·养生主》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:履危(蹈践高危之处);履亩(勘查田亩);履虎尾(踩踏虎尾);履迹(踏着前人的足迹);履霜(踩踏霜地。比喻由眼下迹象而警戒未来);涉水如履平地;履冰(行于冰上);履冰狐(踏冰而过的狐狸);履坦(行于坦途);履虚(行于虚空之中);履薄行走(于薄冰上)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 穿鞋 <span class="encs">[put on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自履藤鞋收石蜜。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《南园》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 登位 <span class="encs">[ascend the throne]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">履其位而不履其事。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·表记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">履至尊。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:履任(到任，就任);履位(就位)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 经历某种景况 <span class="encs">[experience]</span>。如:履炭(比喻经历艰难);履运(遭逢时运)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 实行 <span class="encs">[fulfill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">处其位而不履其事，则乱也。——<span
