                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>翻</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fān.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>歪倒（<span class="dicpy">dǎo </span>），反转，变动位置，改变：推～。～车。～卷。～滚。～腾。～工。～阅。～身。～地。～修。～建。～改。～脸。人仰马～。～江倒海（形容水势浩大，多喻力量或气势非常壮大）。～云覆雨（喻反覆无常或玩弄手段）。</li>
	<li>数量成倍的增加：～番。</li>
	<li>越过：～越。</li>
	<li>飞。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> flip over, upset, capsize</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> umkehren, umdrehen, kippen; sich überwerfen  (V)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">翻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>翻</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 飜、繙 <span class="dicpy">fān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="diczx1">〈动〉</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> (形声。从羽，番声。本义:鸟飞)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">青菰临水映，白鸟向山翻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《辋川闲居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:翻泊(或飞或止);翻翔(翻飞，飞翔);翻簸(飞驰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 翻转;翻腾 <span class="encs">[turn over;toss]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">波翻晓霞影。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《姑熟十咏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:翻滚滚(上下急速滚动的样子);翻海(形容声响如海浪翻腾);翻雪(形容白浪翻滚);翻扑(翻腾)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 反转，倾倒，变动位置 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钿头银篦击节碎，白色罗裙翻酒污。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多少好汉被蒙汗酒麻翻了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:翻席(吃完一席，再到他处吃另一席);翻台(嫖客在一妓院宴毕，再到另一妓院饮宴);翻手(翻转手掌。形容时光迅速或处事轻便)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 推翻;改变 <span class="encs">[reverse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翻变(翻悔，变卦);翻异(事后改变主意);翻样(变换花样);翻盘(推翻承诺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 翻过;越过 <span class="encs">[cross over]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翻墙(翻过墙壁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 回返 <span class="encs">[return]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翻回(回返)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 翻阅，披览 <span class="encs">[browse;look over]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翻披(翻阅，披览);翻撷(翻检)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 翻译 <span class="encs">[translate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翻经(翻译佛经);翻绎(犹推演)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 反复研讨 <span class="encs">[study]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翻空(形容作文构思时奇想联翩);翻思(反复思考)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 谱写;改编;移植 <span class="encs">[write;adapt;rearrange]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:翻梓(翻刻;翻印);翻謄(改作;抄写誊录);翻意(变换文意)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 数量成倍增加 <span class="encs">[multiply]</span>。</p>
