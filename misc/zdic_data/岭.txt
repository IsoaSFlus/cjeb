                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">岭</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>岭</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">嶺</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄥˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>山，山脉：山～。分水～。～脊（山脊）。</li>
	<li>特指中国大庾岭等五岭：～南（指五岭以南的广东、广西一带。亦称&ldquo;岭外&rdquo;、&ldquo;岭表&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>岭</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～嵤〕山深貌。亦作&ldquo;岭巆&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mountain ridge, mountain peak</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bergkette  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> chaîne de montagnes</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">岭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>岭</strong></p>
<p>嶺 <span class="dicpy">lǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，领声。本义:山道;山坡)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mountain road;slope]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岭，山道也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日末涧增波，云生岭逾叠。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span> <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">谢灵运</span><span class="smcs">《登上戍石鼓山》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 顶上有路可通行的山，亦泛指山峰 <span class="encs">[mountain ridge;mountain peak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予登岭上。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岭外望之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:翻山越岭;岭隘(山岭上险要的隘口);岭北(山峰北面;五岭之北)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 相连的山，山脉 <span class="encs">[mountains]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">横看成岭侧成峰，远近高低各不同。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《题西林壁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:岭阴(岭北。山的北面);岭坫(山岭屏障、边缘)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 高大的山脉;山脉的干系 <span class="encs">[mountain range]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:秦岭;大兴安岭;葱岭;岭梅(大庾岭上的梅花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 五岭的简称 <span class="encs">[the Five ridges]</span>。大庾、始安、临货、桂阳、揭阳为五岭。也专指大庾岭(别名&ldquo;梅岭&rdquo;)。如:岭外(岭表，岭南。岭徼。五岭以南的地区);岭峤(泛指五岭地区);岭海(指广东和广西。因两广在五岭之南，临近南海，故称岭海)</p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
