                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>华</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">華</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄚˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>美丽而有光彩的：～丽。～艳。～彩。～贵。～章。～表（亦称&ldquo;桓表&rdquo;）。～盖。</li>
	<li>精英：精～。含英咀（jǔ）～。</li>
	<li>开花：～而不实。春～秋实。</li>
	<li>繁盛：繁～。荣～富贵。</li>
	<li>奢侈：浮～。奢～。</li>
	<li>指中国或汉族：～夏。中～。～裔。～胄（ ➊ 华夏的后裔； ➋ 贵族的后裔）。～工。</li>
	<li>敬辞，用于跟对方有关的事物：～翰（称人书信）。～诞（称人生日）。～居（称人住宅）。</li>
	<li>头发花白：～发（f&agrave;）。～颠（指年老）。</li>
	<li>指时光：年～。韶～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>华</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">華</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～山〕山名，五岳中的西岳，在中国陕西省。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">華</span>）</strong></p>

<p>◎ <strong>华</strong></p>
<p>華 <span class="dicpy">huā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从芔，从芌( <span class="dicpy">xū</span>)。&ldquo;華&rdquo;的本字，上面是&ldquo;垂&rdquo;字，象花叶下垂形。本义:花)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;花&rdquo;，花朵 <span class="encs">[flower]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">华，荣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木谓之华，草谓之荣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释草》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桃之夭夭，灼灼其华。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;桃夭》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焜黄华叶衰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;长歌行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今言华如华实之华者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:华英(指花);华叶(花与叶);华胜(花胜。古代妇女的一种花形首饰);华实(花和果实;亦指开花结果)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 轻浮柔弱如花的脉象 <span class="encs">[flower pulse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脉至如华者，令人善恐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王冰</span>注:&ldquo;谓似华虚弱，不可正取也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>华</strong></p>
<p>華 <span class="dicpy">huā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 开花 <span class="encs">[flower]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桃李始华。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;时则训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:华而不实(只开花不结果。比喻外表好看，内容空虚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 昏花 <span class="encs">[dazzle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">僧问:&ldquo;拨尘见佛时如何?&rdquo;师曰:&ldquo;莫眼华。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《五灯会元》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 当中剖开 <span class="encs">[dissect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瓜曰华之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为国君者华之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;中裂之。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;;hu&agrave;</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>华</strong></p>
<p>華 <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 华丽;光彩美丽 <span class="encs">[magnificent;gorgeous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金银华美之服。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">华堂之上。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">华衣乘马。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:华扁(华丽的匾额);华彩(华美;光彩);华服(华丽的服装);华舆(华美的车辆或轿子);华筵(华美的筵席);华观(华丽的观阙，即宫门前的望楼);华毂(华丽的车)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 豪华 <span class="encs">[luxurious;sumptuous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">况我</span><span class="diczx2">大魏天王</span>，个为华侈。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">杨衒之</span><span class="smcs">《洛阳伽蓝记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 称美之词。通常用于跟对方有关的事物 <span class="encs">[your]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:华名(美名);华宗(对同族或同姓者的美称);华笺(对他人来信的敬称);华缄(对他书信的美称或敬称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 灰白 <span class="encs">[grey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多情应笑我，早生华发。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《赤壁怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:华首(头发花白。指老人);华颠(白发);华鬓(鬓发花白);华发(花白头发)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 年轻;年少 <span class="encs">[young]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:华年</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 浮华 <span class="encs">[flashy;extravagant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">处其实，不居其华。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:华辞(虚浮巧饰的言辞);华誉(不实的声誉);华风(浮华的文风)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 汉语 <span class="encs">[Chinese]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:华言(指中原地区的语言。后泛指汉语)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 荣华;光耀 <span class="encs">[glory]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:华伍(指达官贵人);华序(显要的官阶);华使(显贵的官吏);华秩(显位;高阶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 显耀 <span class="encs">[splendor]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:华近(显贵而亲近君上的官职);华要(显要尊贵的官职);华省(显贵而职务上能亲近君主的官署);华重(显贵而重要的官职)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 繁盛 <span class="encs">[luxuriant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其街市之繁华，人烟之阜盛，自与别处不同。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:华滋(草木茂盛的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 鲜美 <span class="encs">[delicious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其民华食而脂肥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王冰</span>注:华谓鲜美，酥酪骨肉之类也。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 彩色。特指雕绘或装饰 <span class="encs">[color]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:华轩(饰有文采的曲栏);华衮(古代王公贵族的多采的礼服。常用以表示极高的荣宠);华纳(有雕饰的台阶。纳，即纳陛);华幄(帝王所居的华丽的帷帐)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>华</strong></p>
<p>華 <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 中国 <span class="encs">[China]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:来华访问;华中;华北</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 我国古称华夏，今称中华。省称&ldquo;华&rdquo; <span class="encs">[the Han nationality]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:华夷(华是汉族，夷指非汉族的其他少数名族);华风(指汉族或中原的风俗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 光辉;光彩 <span class="encs">[brilliant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日月光华，旦复旦兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《卿云歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:华景(明亮的阳光);华月(皎洁的月亮);华焕(光彩绚丽);华魄(光彩;亦指皎洁的月光)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泉中的矿物由于沉积而形成的物质 <span class="encs">[sinter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:钙华;硅华</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 借升华而得的化学品 <span class="encs">[flowers]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:硫华;锌华</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 车盖 <span class="encs">[cover of a carriage]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:华芝(车盖。因车盖的外形象芝而得名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 时光 <span class="encs">[time]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:年华，岁华，韶华</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 声望 <span class="encs">[prestige]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客游</span><span class="diczx2">梁朝</span>，则声华籍甚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;任彦升&middot;宣德皇后令》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 精华 <span class="encs">[essence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">物华天宝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:华甸(精华荟聚之区。常以指京都、中原、中国);华英(精华);华髓(精髓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 文才 <span class="encs">[talent for literature]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔</span><span class="diczx2">庚无规</span>才华清英。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">刘勰</span><span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 瓜类植物的果实 <span class="encs">[fruit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子树瓜华，不敛藏之种也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 光环 <span class="encs">[corona]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薄云敝日时，由于无数水滴的衍射，在太阳、月亮或其他发光体四周形成的一组或几组小半径的同心彩色光环</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 矿脉或煤层已发生氧化或分解的露头 <span class="encs">[blossom;bloom]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">huā;hu&agrave;</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓。如:华佗;华扁(古代名医华陀、扁鹊的并称)</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/83EF.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕䔢【唐韻】戸花切【集韻】胡瓜切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音划。【書&middot;舜典】重華協于帝。【傳】華謂文德。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;檀弓】華而睆。【疏】凡繪畫，五色必有光華，故曰華畫也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】草盛也。</p>
<p>又粉也。【曹植&middot;洛神賦】鉛華弗御。</p>
<p>又髮白也。【後漢&middot;陳蕃傳】蹇諤之操，華首彌固。</p>
<p>又華林，園名。【魏志】芳林園卽今華林園。</p>
<p>又地名。【戰國策】說趙王于華屋之下。【史記&middot;秦本紀註】華陽，地名。【吳志&middot;孫皓傳】皓舉大衆出華里。</p>
<p>又【水經注】河水東南徑華池。</p>
<p>又華表。【古今註】堯設誹謗木，今之華表。</p>
<p>又星名。【晉書&middot;天文志】大帝九星曰華蓋。</p>
<p>又【韻會】胡化切，音話。【書&middot;禹貢】至于太華。【爾雅&middot;釋山】華山，爲西嶽。</p>
<p>又姓。【潛夫論】華氏子，姓也。【通志&middot;氏族略】宋戴公子者，食采于華，因氏焉。</p>
<p>又【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】華楓枰櫨。【註】華皮可以爲索。</p>
<p>又【集韻】呼瓜切，音譁。【禮&middot;曲禮】爲國君者華之。【註】華，中裂之，不四拆也。【爾雅&middot;釋木】瓜曰華之。</p>
<p>又與花同。【爾雅&middot;釋草】華，荂也。【揚子&middot;方言】齊楚之閒或謂之華，或謂之荂。【佩觿集】華有戸瓜，呼瓜二翻，俗別爲花。</p>
<p>又【韻補】呼戈切。【邊讓&middot;章華賦】體迅輕鴻，榮曜春華。進如浮雲，退如激波。</p>
<p>又胡戈切。【徐鍇&middot;說文繫傳】華，本音和，故今人謂華表爲和表。【棗據詩】矯足登雲閣，相伴步九華。徙倚憑高山，仰攀桂樹柯。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;本音】灼灼其華。【註】音敷。【考】《詩》如常棣之華，顏如舜華，維常之華，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>叶車韻。隰有荷華，叶下都韻。黍稷方華，叶下途韻，凡七見，皆讀敷。</p>
<p>又【唐韻古音】亦音敷。郭璞曰：江東謂華爲敷。陸德明曰：古讀華如敷，不獨江東也。漢光武曰：仕宦當作執金吾，娶妻必得隂麗華。</p>
<p>又【韻會】苦蛙切【正韻】枯瓜切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音誇。不正也。　或作蕐。詳䓙字註。䓙又作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26D8E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦶎</span>。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
