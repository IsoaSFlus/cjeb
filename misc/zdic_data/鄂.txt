<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>鄂</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄜˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>中国湖北省的别称。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">谔</span>&rdquo;，正直的话。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">愕</span>&rdquo;，惊讶。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">萼</span>&rdquo;，花托。</li>
	<li>边界：&ldquo;纷被丽而亡～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鄂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鄂</strong> <span class="dicpy">&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[E county]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 湖北省的简称 <span class="encs">[another name for Hubei Province]</span>。因清代省会武昌是隋以后鄂州的治所而得名</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 边界 <span class="encs">[boundary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纷被丽而亡鄂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;扬雄&middot;甘泉赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;愕&rdquo;。惊愕 <span class="encs">[stunned;stupefied]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">象鄂不怿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;五帝本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">&ldquo;孺子，下取履!&rdquo;</span><span class="diczx2">良</span>鄂然，欲殴之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;留侯世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群臣皆惊鄂失色，莫敢发言，但唯唯而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;霍光传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:鄂然(吃惊的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;萼&rdquo;。花托 <span class="encs">[receptacle]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:鄂鄂(花开放的样子);鄂不(花萼和花托);鄂足(花托)</p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>鄂</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄜˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>&egrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5739.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">地名：➊ ​ 春秋时楚国都邑，故城约在今湖北省武昌县。➋ ​ 春秋时晋国都邑，故城约在今山西省乡宁县南方。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">湖北省的简称。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">边际。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书&middot;卷八七&middot;扬雄传上》：「并闾与茇䒷兮，纷被丽其亡鄂。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三国魏&middot;徐干&middot;齐都赋：「南望无垠，北顾无鄂。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">托住花朵的部分。</span><span class="gc_yy">《诗经&middot;小雅&middot;常棣》：「常棣之华，鄂不韡韡。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「萼」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如汉代有鄂千秋。</span></p></li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鄂</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=邑" target="_blank">邑</a>】
                              鄂·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9102.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】五各切【集韻】【正韻】逆各切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音諤。國名。【史記&middot;殷本紀】以西伯、九侯、鄂侯爲三公。</p>
<p>又楚地。【史記&middot;楚世家】中子紅爲鄂王。【註】《九州記》曰：鄂，今武昌。【輿地記】今鄂州武昌，楚之東鄂也。</p>
<p>又晉邑。【左傳&middot;隱六年】逆晉侯于隨，納諸鄂，晉人謂之鄂侯。【註】晉別邑。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】南陽有西鄂。【前漢&middot;地理志】南陽郡西鄂縣。【註】江夏有鄂，故加西。</p>
<p>又鄂然，外見貌。【詩&middot;小雅】鄂不韡韡。</p>
<p>又鄂鄂，辨厲也。【大戴禮&middot;立事篇】君子出言以鄂鄂。</p>
<p>又垠也。【揚雄&middot;甘泉賦】紛被麗其亡鄂。</p>
<p>又幽州人謂額曰鄂。【釋名】額，鄂也。有垠鄂也。</p>
<p>又與諤通。【史記&middot;趙世家】不聞周舍之鄂鄂。【註】《韓詩外傳》：周舍立門下三日夜，簡子使人問之，對曰：願爲鄂鄂之臣。</p>
<p>又與愕通。【史記&middot;五帝紀】象鄂不懌。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;霍光傳】羣臣皆驚鄂失色。</p>
<p>又與噩通。【爾雅&middot;釋天】歲在酉曰作噩。【史記&middot;天官書】作作鄂。</p>
<p>又姓。漢安平侯鄂千秋。【說文】本作䣞。俗作鄂。</p>
