                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>郎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>对年轻男子的称呼：大～。～才女貌。</li>
	<li>对某种人的称呼：货～。女～。</li>
	<li>旧时妻称夫或情人：～君。</li>
	<li>封建时代的官名：～中（①古官名；②中医医生）。侍～。员外～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>郎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄤˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔屎壳～〕&ldquo;蜣螂&rdquo;的俗称。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> gentleman, young man; husband</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> jeune homme,monsieur,maître</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">郎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>郎</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从邑，良声。从&ldquo;邑&rdquo;，表示与行政区域有关。本义:古邑名。在今山东金乡县境)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古地名。春秋鲁邑 <span class="encs">[Lang town]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">郎，</span><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>邑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按， <span class="diczx2">鲁</span>有二 <span class="diczx2">郎</span>。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 在今山东省鱼台县东北，是费伯的食邑</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">费伯</span>帅师城 <span class="diczx2">郎</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 在今山东省曲阜县附近</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏，城</span><span class="diczx2">郎</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公九年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 郎，古廊字。原指宫殿廷廊，置侍卫人员所在。官名 <span class="encs">[an official title under the monarchy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">郎官，谓三中郎将下之属官也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;桓帝纪》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 战国始置。帝王侍从官侍郎、中郎、郎中等的通称。其职责原为护卫陪从、随时建议，备顾问差遣等侍从之职。郎官一直沿用到清朝。如:郎官(郎中及员外郎的泛称);郎中(郎官。汉代称中郎、侍郎、郎中为郎官，掌星宿之职);郎将(官名);郎署(宿卫官的官署)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 旧时妇女对丈夫或情人的昵称 <span class="encs">[my darling;husband]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">郎骑竹马来，绕床弄青梅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《长干行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:郎伯(妇人称丈夫为郎、伯，并称郎伯)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 女婿，女儿的丈夫 <span class="encs">[son-in-law]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我哥哥有两个郎;郎婿(女婿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 称别人的儿子 <span class="encs">[son]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:令郎;大郎、二郎、三郎;郎子(对他人之子的爱称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 汉魏以后少年的美称 <span class="encs">[youth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">瑜</span>时年二十四， <span class="diczx2">吴</span>中皆呼为 <span class="diczx2">周</span>郎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 旧时对从事某种职业者的称呼 <span class="encs">[worker]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:货郎;牛郎;卖油郎;郎不郎，秀不秀(形容不伦不类)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 旧时对一般男子的尊称 <span class="encs">[man]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">王</span>家诸郎亦皆可嘉，闻来觅婿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:伴郎;郎不郎，秀不秀(指不成材);郎子(对英俊少年的爱称);郎秀(明初乡里间称呼男子因出身而异，大家豪族称秀;平凡家庭称郎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 仆人称主人为郎 <span class="encs">[landlord]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为两郎僮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孰若为一郎僮。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">正副郎好事者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:郎主(门生家奴称其主;对外族首领的称呼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 通&ldquo;廊&rdquo;。室外有顶的过道 <span class="encs">[veranda;covered corridor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今陛下累郎台，恐其不高也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;东方朔传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盖闻</span><span class="diczx2">虞舜</span>之时，游于岩郎之上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;董仲舒传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 通&ldquo;廊&rdquo;。朝廷 <span class="encs">[court]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茤荛之言，可择郎庙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">钟繇</span><span class="smcs">《宣示帖》</span></p>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>郎</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄌㄤˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>l&aacute;ng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1835.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">秦漢時起，官多稱為「郎」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「侍<b>郎</b>」、「尚書<b>郎</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷六八&middot;霍光傳》：「時年十餘歲，任光為<b>郎</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對男子的美稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「周<b>郎</b>」、「少年<b>郎</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝宋&middot;劉義慶《世說新語&middot;雅量》：「王家諸<b>郎</b>，亦皆可嘉。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈橫江詞〉六首之五：「<b>郎</b>今欲渡緣何事？如此風波不可行。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">婦女對丈夫或情人的稱呼。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>郎</b>有情，妹有意。」</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝宋&middot;劉義慶《世說新語&middot;賢媛》：「生縱不得與郗<b>郎</b>同室，死寧不同穴！」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈長干行〉：「<b>郎</b>騎竹馬來，遶床弄青梅。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">僕役對主人的稱呼。</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷一二四&middot;宋璟傳》：「君非其家奴，何<b>郎</b>之云？」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">女子也稱為「郎」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「女<b>郎</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如漢代有郎顗。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
