                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>并</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄥˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>合在一起：～拢。合～。兼～。</li>
	<li>一齐，平排着：～驾齐驱。～重（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）。～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>连词，表平列或进一层：～且。</li>
	<li>用在否定词前，加强否定的语气，表不像预料的那样：～不容易。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>并</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄥˉ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 中国山西省太原市的别称。</p>
<ol>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> combine, annex; also, what's more</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> und ,verschmelzen, verbinden, zusammenlegen, zusammenfassen, zusammenschließen, fusionieren, vereinigen</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">并</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>并</strong> <span class="dicpy">bīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古地名。并州 <span class="encs">[Bing prefecture]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 相传禹治洪水，分天下为九州。并州为九州之一，其地在今河北保定、正定和山西大同、太原一带。沿用为太原的别称</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 汉置并州，其地在今内蒙古、山西(大部)、河北(一部)。东汉时并入冀州。三国魏复置。其地在今山西汾水中游</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">并</span>、 <span class="diczx2">汾</span>乔木，望秋先陨。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>并</strong></p>
<p>並、併、竝 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;从二立。&rdquo;金文字形，为二人并立之形。本义:并行，并列)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[side by side]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">並，併也。从二立，会意。今隶作並。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">並行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">並立则乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俄而並乎</span><span class="diczx2">尧</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">並纽约用组。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
