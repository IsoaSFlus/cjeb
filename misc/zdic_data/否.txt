                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>否</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fǒu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǒu.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄡˇ</span></p><ol><li>表示不同意，不认可：～定。</li><li>不，用在表示疑问的词句里：可～？。</li><li>不如此，不是这样，不然：～则。学则正，～则邪。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>否</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 不好，坏，恶：～极泰来（“否”和“<span class="diczx3">泰</span>”，前者是坏卦，后者是好卦。指事情坏到了极点，就会向好的方向转化）。未知善～。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>否</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="encs">[古]</span>∶不，不然，不如此 <span class="encs">[nay;no]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">否，不也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">否臧凶。——<span class="smcs">《易·师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">否德黍帝位。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“皆训不。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">唐雎</span>对曰:<span class="yhcs">“否，非若是也。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王曰:<span class="yhcs">“否，吾何快于是，将以求吾所大欲也”</span>。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:这是妥当的办法吗?否</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 非，不是 <span class="encs">[not]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大人否亨。——<span class="smcs">《易·否卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">否则熟虑而从之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·表记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓非己志也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫建国设都，乃作后王君公，否用泰也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> ——用于<span class="yhcs">“能否”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“可否”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“是否”</span>中。如:明日能否出发，需视天气而定</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用在疑问句末，构成是非问句</p><p><span class="diczx1">是不必问其果解脱否也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">廉颇</span>老矣，尚能饭否?—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐·京口北固亭怀古》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:汝知之否</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">pǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5426.svg">                          <p>不也。从口从不。方九切</p><p>不也。从口从不，不亦聲。方久切1895〖注〗徐鍇曰：“不可之意見於言，故从口。” 文二 </p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5426_733.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(否)</span><span class="ywyy">不也。从口不。</span><span class="swdz">按否字見不部。此誤增也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>              <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5426_734.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(否)</span><span class="ywyy">不也。</span><span class="swdz">不者、事之不然也。否者、說事之不然也。故音義皆同。孟子萬章曰。然則舜僞喜者與。孟子曰。否注。孟子言舜不詐喜也。又咸丘蒙問舜南面而立。瞽瞍亦北面而朝之。孟子曰。否。注。言不然也。又萬章曰。堯以天下與舜、有諸。孟子曰。否。注。堯不與之。又萬章問曰。人有言。伊尹以割烹要湯。孟子曰。否然也。萬章又問孔子於衞主癰疽。孟子曰。否然也。萬章又問百里奚自鬻於秦養牲者。孟子曰。否然注皆曰。否、不也。不如是也注以不如是釋否然。今本正文皆譌作否不然。語贅而注不可通矣。否字引申之義訓爲不通。如易之泰否、堯典之否德、小雅之否難知也、論語之子所否者皆、殊其音讀符鄙切。要之古音則同在弟一部。</span><span class="ywyy">从口不。</span><span class="swdz">會意。</span><span class="ywyy">不亦聲。</span><span class="swdz">方久切。古音在一部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
