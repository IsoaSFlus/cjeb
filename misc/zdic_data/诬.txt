<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">誣</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">wū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人没有做坏事，硬说他做了坏事；把没有的事说成有：～蔑。～赖。～陷。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>诬</strong></p>
<p>誣 <span class="dicpy">wū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 欺骗 <span class="encs">[cheat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因为诬上，卒从吏议。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">佞誉诬谀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诬禄(假冒有功而受禄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 无中生有，捏造事实害人 <span class="encs">[accuse falsely]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诬善之人，其辞游。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诬守纳己赇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诬去贤使君。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诬太守者。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诬扳(犯罪的人诬告攀扯他人);诬罪(诬陷使人入罪);诬系(捏造事实，使人获罪);诬良为盗;诬言(诬罔之言。虚伪骗人的言辞);诬罔(诬蔑冤枉);诬诋(捏造事实以诋毁人);诬善(诬陷，欺骗善良)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 抹杀 <span class="encs">[blot out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而高人所述，不可诬矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">隋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王度</span><span class="smcs">《古镜记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 冤屈 <span class="encs">[torture indiscriminately]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其刑矫诬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“诬”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/誣">誣</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              誣·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AA3.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】武扶切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】微夫切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音無。【說文】加也。【徐曰】以無爲有也。【玉篇】欺罔也。【廣韻】誣，枉也。【正韻】詐也，衊也，謗也。【易&middot;繫辭】誣善之人其辭游。【疏】誣罔善人，其辭虛漫。【禮&middot;表記】故其受祿不誣。【註】不信曰誣。【周語】其<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>矯誣。【註】加罪無辜曰誣。【荀子&middot;大略篇】不能而居之，誣也。</p>
<p>又【讀書通】通作憮。【論語】君子之道焉，可誣也。【前漢&middot;薛宣傳】引作憮。【晉灼註】憮同誣。　【篇韻】譌作誈。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">誣</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“诬”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/誣">誣</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三">卷三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三&amp;swbs=言">言</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8AA3.svg">                          <p>加也。从言巫聲。武扶切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8AA3_6277.svg" /><span class="swbf">(誣)</span><span class="ywyy">加也。</span><span class="swdz">玄應五引皆作加言。加言者架言也。古無架字，以加爲之。淮南時則訓：鵲加巢。加巢者，架巢也。毛詩箋曰：鵲之作巢，冬至加之。劉昌宗：加音架。李善引呂氏春秋注曰：結，交也。構，架也。云加言者，謂憑空構架聽者所當審愼也。按力部曰：加，語相增加也。從力口。然則加與誣同義互訓，可不增言字。加與誣皆兼毁譽言之，毁譽不以實皆曰誣也。方言：誣䛳與也。吳越曰誣，荆楚曰䛳與，猶齊晉言阿與。表記：受禒不誣。注曰：於事不信曰誣。</span><span class="ywyy">从言巫聲。</span><span class="swdz">武扶切。五部。</span></p>                          </div>
