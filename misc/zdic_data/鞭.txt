                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鞭</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鞭</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">biān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>驱使牲畜的用具，柔软像绳子：～杆。～长莫及。</li>
	<li>用鞭子抽打：～打。～扑。～责。～策。</li>
	<li>形状细长类似鞭子的东西：教～。</li>
	<li>一种古代兵器，铁制有节，无锋刃：钢～。竹节～。</li>
	<li>编连成串的爆竹：～炮。小～。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鞭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鞭</strong> <span class="dicpy">biān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从革，便声。本义:皮制的马鞭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[leather-thonged whip]</span> 泛指鞭子</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鞭，驱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其左执鞭弭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽鞭之长，不及马腹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北市买长鞭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骏马不劳鞭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《赠友人》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鞭影(马鞭的影子。后以比喻警戒策勉);鞭镫(马鞭与马镫);鞭弭(马鞭和弓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 竹根 <span class="encs">[root of bamboo]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新鞭暗入庭，初长两三茎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张蠙</span><span class="smcs">《新竹》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鞭笋(植物名。指竹子的根茎生长于地下);鞭茁(初生的鞭笋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形状细长类似鞭子的东西 <span class="encs">[pointer]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:教鞭</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代一种兵器 <span class="encs">[a kind of weapon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:三棱鞭;钢鞭;九节鞭</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>鞭</strong> <span class="dicpy">biān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 打马。泛指鞭打 <span class="encs">[whip]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使</span><span class="diczx2">造父</span>操右革而鞭笞之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说右下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鞭牛(鞭打春牛以示迎春);鞭石(用鞭击石);鞭草(以鞭击草);鞭楚(用黄荆木条鞭打人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古刑名。鞭笞罪人的刑罚 <span class="encs">[lash]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃掘</span><span class="diczx2">楚平王</span>墓，出其尸，鞭之三百，然后曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;伍子胥列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鞭尸(以鞭抽打尸体。指严厉责罚已死的罪人);鞭墓(即鞭尸);鞭刑(古时用鞭子抽打犯人的刑法)</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一種皮製的長軟器具，用來驅使牲口或打人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「馬<b>鞭</b>」、「皮<b>鞭</b>」、「鞭長莫及」。</span><span class="gc_yx">也稱為「鞭子」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一種古代的兵器。以鐵打造，有節，無刀刃。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「鋼<b>鞭</b>」、「竹節<b>鞭</b>」、「三棱<b>鞭</b>」。</span></p></li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">指長條形類似鞭子的物品。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「教<b>鞭</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
