                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>砭</strong> <span class="dicpy">biān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石，乏声。本义:治病刺穴的石针)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stone needle used in acupuncture]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">砭，以石刺病也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其病皆为痈疡，其治宜砭石。——<span class="smcs">《素问·异法方宜论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:砭割(用石针治病。比喻忍痛除恶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[地质]</span>∶曲流凹岸由于水流拥挤而发生侵蚀的地点 <span class="encs">[nip]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 〈方〉∶山坡 <span class="encs">[hillside]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">他们这时正走在一道砭上，上头是山崖，下边是石岩，光秃秃的没一点遮拦。——</span><span i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代用石针扎皮肉治病。后用金属针以治病，也称<span class="yhcs">“砭”</span> <span class="encs">[pierce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风上逆，砭头血可愈。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·则天武皇后传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:砭磨(用石针刺磨患处。引申为救时除弊);砭熨(用石针刺穴，用药物熨贴患处。指救治病痛)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 救治 <span class="encs">[treat and cure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士病吾能砭。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《舟中望九华山》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:砭俗(救治庸俗)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 刺 <span class="encs">[stab]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其气栗冽，砭人肌骨。——</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《秋声赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:砭骨</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div                     <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>砭</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄅㄧㄢ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>biān <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0231.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古时用来治病的石针。参见「<a href='/hans/砭石' target=_blank>砭石</a> 」条。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古人用石针扎刺肌肤治病。</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐书·卷七六·后妃传上·则天武皇后传》：「帝头眩不能视，侍医张文仲、秦鸣鹤曰：『风上逆，<b>砭</b>头血可愈。』」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">救治。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·韩愈〈喜侯喜至赠张籍张彻〉诗：「又如心中疾，针石非所<b>砭</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·苏轼〈择胜亭铭〉：「我铭斯亭，以<b>砭</b>世盲。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">刺、刺痛。宋·欧阳修〈秋声赋〉：「其气栗冽，<b><b>砭</b></b>人肌骨」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
