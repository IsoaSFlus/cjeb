<p>● <strong>咧</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">liē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liē.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄝˉ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>〔大大～～〕形容随随便便，满不在乎的样子。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>咧</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄝˊ</span></p>

	<li>〔～～〕方言，乱说乱讲，如&ldquo;瞎～～&rdquo;（后一个&ldquo;咧&rdquo;读轻声）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>咧</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lie  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lie.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄝ</span></p>

<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> stretch mouth, grimace, grin</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Mund verziehen, grinsen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grimacer,babil,grimace,(particule modale exprimant l'exclamation)​</p></div>
