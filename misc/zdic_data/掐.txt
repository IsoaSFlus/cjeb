                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>掐</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qiā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄚˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用指甲按或截断：～算。～花。～尖儿。</li>
	<li>割断，截去：～头去尾。</li>
	<li>用手的虎口及手指紧紧握住：一把～住。</li>
	<li>量词，一只手或两只手指尖相对握着的数量：一～儿松枝。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hold; gather with hand; choke</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> auswählen, abpflücken ,kneifen, zwicken ,Prise  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> couper,saisir,étrangler</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">掐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>掐</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用指甲按或切入 <span class="encs">[pinch;nip]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:掐出水来(用指甲轻按即流出汁来。形容极稚嫩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用指甲切断或截取，摘 <span class="encs">[pinch off;nip off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">香菱</span>等在山石边掐凤仙花呢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:把杈子掐掉</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用有力的外部压力按住喉部使窒息 <span class="encs">[choke to death;throttle]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:被一残忍的强盗掐死了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 镶嵌 <span class="encs">[inlay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">黛玉</span>换上掐金挖云红香羊皮小靴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:掐牙(在衣服滚边镶上一条极细的滚条，稍稍露在外面作装饰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用拇指点着别的手指进行暗记或推算 <span class="encs">[count on one&#39;s fingers]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:掐指(用拇指轻点其他指头计算)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>掐</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>拇指和另一手指尖相对握着的数量。如:一掐儿韭菜</p>
