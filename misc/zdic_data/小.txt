                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">小</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>小</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>指面积、体积、容量、数量、强度、力量不及一般或不及所比较的对象，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">大</span>&rdquo;相对：～雨。矮～。短～精悍。</li>
	<li>范围窄，程度浅，性质不重要：～事。～节。～题大作。～打～闹。</li>
	<li>时间短：～坐。～住。</li>
	<li>年幼小，排行最末：～孩。</li>
	<li>谦辞：～弟。～可。～人（①谦称自己，指地位低；②指人格卑鄙的人；③指子女；④小孩儿）。</li>
	<li>妾：～房。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> small, tiny, insignificant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> klein (Adj)​,jung (Adj)​,Radikal Nr. 42 = klein (S, Sprachw)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> petit,jeune</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">小</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>小</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。据甲骨文，象沙粒形。小篆析为会意。从八，从|。本义:细;微。与&ldquo;大&rdquo;相对)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[small;little;minor]</span>。形容事物在体积、面积、数量、力量、强度等方面不及一般的或不及比较的对象</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小，物之微也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小往大来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;泰》</span>。疏:&ldquo;阴生消耗，故称小。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼所小言尽人毒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span>。注:&ldquo;细巧入人为小言。&rdquo;?</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嘒彼小星。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;小星》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受小球大球。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;长发》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怨不在大，亦不在小。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;康诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:小鹿儿(喻指因紧张而剧烈跳动的心脏);小录(新科进士的题名录);小伙伙(小巧，娇小);小话(短小的故事或寓言);小单拱儿(手推车);小唱(由乐器协奏的歌曲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;少&rdquo;。年幼 <span class="encs">[young]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝廷之事，小者不毁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;饬令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小妹语曰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">龙君小女。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:小孽障(小孩子);小猴子(小孩);小堂名(旧时为婚丧喜庆雇用的由小孩组成的乐队);小娘子(侍女;侍儿);小娘儿(妓女);小鬼头(小孩子);小妮子(婢女;女孩子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 稍，略 <span class="encs">[slightly;a little]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汔可小康。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;民劳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汔可小安。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汔可小休。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小不如意。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:小傲怄儿(带有一点玩笑意味的抬杠);小节不足(不顺心;不能称心如意);小厮扑(徒手对打);小意儿(小殷勤);小有(稍有一些)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 将近 <span class="encs">[nearly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">归了包堆花了小一百，还剩四百来块。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">老舍</span><span class="smcs">《骆驼祥子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:小日中(将近中午的时候);我今年小(奔)六十了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用在姓、名、排行等之前，表示爱称 <span class="encs">[young brother]</span>。</p>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>小</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄒㄧㄠˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>xiǎo <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3963.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">與「大」相對：➊ ​體積、數量、力量等方面不大的。如：「小城」、「小人物」。《荀子&middot;勸學》：「不積小流，無以成江海。」唐&middot;韓愈〈原道〉：「坐井而觀天，曰：『天小者，非天小也。』」➋ ​ 年輕、幼稚的。如：「年紀小」、「小伙子」。《晉書&middot;九二&middot;文苑傳&middot;趙至傳》：「我小，未能榮養。」➌ ​ 排行最後或地位較低的。如：「小官」、「這兩位是我二弟和小妹。」、「小妹！請你把帳單拿過來。」➍ ​對自己或與自己有關的人事物的謙稱。如：「恕小民直言。」、「小的不敢！」、「小店」、「小兒」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">邪惡的人、壞人。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷八九&middot;循吏傳&middot;龔遂傳》：「今大王親近群<b>小</b>，漸漬邪惡所習。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">小孩、年幼者。如：「一家老小」。《詩經&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》：「既醉既飽，小大稽首」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">妾。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「嫁給人家當小的。」</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;湯顯祖《牡丹亭&middot;第二五齣》：「你可知老相公年來因少男兒，常有娶<b>小</b>之意。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">二一四部首之一。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">輕視。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;桓公十三年》：「將自用也，必<b>小</b>羅。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈送長沙陳太守〉詩二首之二：「莫<b>小</b>二千石，當安遠俗人。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">稍微。表示程度不深。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「牛刀小試」、「不無小補」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">助</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">詞頭，表對年紀輕者的暱稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>小</b>王」、「小老弟」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
