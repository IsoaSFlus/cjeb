						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>加</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄚˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>增多：增～。追～。～倍。～封。</li>    <li>把本来没有的添上去：～注解。～冕。</li>    <li>把几个数合起来的算法：～法。</li>    <li>施以某种动作：～以。不～考虑。</li>    <li>使程度增高：～工。～强。～剧。</li>    <li>超过：～人一等（形容学问才能超过常人）。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">加</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>加</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从力，从口。本义:添枝加叶说假话、虚报)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[make a false report;slander]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加，语相增加也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子称其功以加小人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我不欲人之加诸我也。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弗敢加也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:加叶添枝(加油添醋，加油加醋，加油添酱。为了夸张或渲染的需要，在叙述或转述时，增添原来没有的内容);加诸(诬谤;凌驾于;乱说);加诬(虚构诬陷)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 增加<span class="encs">[increase;add]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臂非加长。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">声非加疾。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何以复加。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加其土封。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:加注解;加符号;加一钱(收到本金十分之一的高利贷);加二(二成);加绪含容(增加功绩，包含宽容。绪:功绩);加纳(加官受职);加年(年龄增长);加兵(增加兵力)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 放在上面，加上 <span class="encs">[put on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加彘肩上。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加以金银。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三缢加别械。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:加额(把手放在额头，表示庆幸或敬意);加冠(古代男子二十岁行加冠礼，表示成年;著冠);加笄(以簪束发。古代女子十五岁开始加笄，表示成年)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 施加;强加 <span class="encs">[impose;exert]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加之以师旅。——<span class="smcs">《论语·先进》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加诸彼。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加兵于</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卒不加诛。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:加功(施工);加惠(施加恩惠)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 参加;加与 <span class="encs">[take part in]</span>。如:加盟(参加某一团体或组织)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 凌驾，侵凌 <span class="encs">[bully]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加胜于</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>加</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示程度，相当于<span class="yhcs">“更加”</span>，<span class="yhcs">“愈加”</span> <span class="encs">[more]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山不加增。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span                     <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">加</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>加</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧㄚ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jiā <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3107.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">算法上，指两个数目的结合。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「三<b>加</b>五等于八。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">将一物放在另一物的上面。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「花生米<b>加</b>豆腐干，滋味胜火腿。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《庄子·马蹄》：「夫<b>加</b>之以衡轭。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">置（刑）​或施（恩）​。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·韩愈〈送李愿归盘谷序〉：「车服不维，刀锯不<b>加</b>，理乱不知，黜陟不闻。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·欧阳修〈泷冈阡表〉：「逢国大庆，必<b>加</b>宠锡。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">增益。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「增<b>加</b>」、「<b>加</b>股」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《论语·子路》：「既富矣，又何<b>加</b>焉？」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·韩愈〈答李翊书〉：「<b>加</b>其膏而希其光。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">参见「<a href='/hans/加以' target=_blank>加以</a> 」条。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">更。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子·梁惠王上》：「邻国之民不<b>加</b>少，寡人之民不<b>加</b>多。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·王安石〈游褒禅山记〉：「盖其又深，则其至又<b>加</b>少矣。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">数学上的运算方法之一。参见「<a href='/hans/加法' target=_blank>加法</a> 」条。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如汉代有<b><b>加</b></b>特奴。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
