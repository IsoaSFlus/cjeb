                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>照</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄠˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>光线射在物体上：日～。～耀。～射。</li>	<li>对着镜子或其他反光的东西看自己或其他人物的影像：～镜子。</li>	<li>摄影：～相。拍～。</li>	<li>画像或相片：小～。写～。</li>	<li>看顾：～管。～顾。</li>	<li>按着，依着：依～。遵～。～搬。～本宣科。</li>	<li>凭证：护～。牌～。执～。</li>	<li>知晓：心～不宣。肝胆相～。</li>	<li>通知，通告：知～。～会。</li>	<li>对着，向着：～壁。～敌人开枪。</li>	<li>查对：对～。查～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">照</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>照</strong></p>
<p>炤 <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，昭声，字亦作炤。本义:明亮;光明) 同本义 <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">照，明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦孔之炤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;正月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">月出照兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;陈风&middot;月出》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者十日并出，万物皆照。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">远近照闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《郯令景君阙铭》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盛德炤明。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谯敏碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">炤之以祸灾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;修身》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:照彻(透明晶莹 );照澄(明朗清爽)&ldquo;炤&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhāo</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>照</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 照射，照耀 <span class="encs">[shine;illuminate;light up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">照耀煇煌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
