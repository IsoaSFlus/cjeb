						<p>● <strong>燎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄠˊ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>延烧：～荒。～原烈火。</li>
	<li>烫：～泡。</li>
	<li>照明。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>燎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">liǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄠˇ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>挨近火而烧焦：把头发～了。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>燎</strong> <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 放在地上的火炬，大火把 <span class="encs">[torch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜未央，庭燎之光。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;庭燎》</span>。疏:&ldquo;庭燎者，树之于庭，燎之为明，是烛之大者。&ldquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">火在地曰燎，执之曰烛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士丧礼》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:燎火(火炬);燎炬(火把);燎烛(烛炬)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>燎</strong> <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 烫 <span class="encs">[scald]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:燎浆泡</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">liǎo;li&agrave;o</span></p>
