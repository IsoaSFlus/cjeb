                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">岛</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>岛</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">島</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>江海或湖泊里四面被水围着的陆地：～屿。半～。海～。～链（排列成一串的许多岛）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> island</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Insel (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> île,îlot</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">岛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>岛</strong></p>
<p>島 <span class="dicpy">dǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从山，鸟声。本义:江、湖、海洋中被水所包围而比大陆要小的一片陆地) 同本义 <span class="encs">[island]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入海居岛中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;田儋传》</span>。集解引</span><span class="diczx2">韦昭</span>:&ldquo;海中山曰岛。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山岛竦峙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《步出夏门行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">英伦诸岛乃属冰天雪海之区。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:岛峙(似海岛般耸峙);岛夷(古指中国东部近海一带及海岛上的居民;南北朝时南北双方封建统治者各以正统自居，互相诋毁，北朝称南朝为岛夷;也指倭寇)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中" target="_blank">寅集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中&amp;bs=山" target="_blank">山</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5CF6.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【廣韻】【正韻】都皓切【集韻】【韻會】都老切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>刀上聲。【說文】海中往往有山可依止曰島。从山鳥聲。《釋名》島，到也。人所奔到也。《書&bull;禹貢》島夷皮服。孔傳海曲謂之島。居島之夷還服其皮。【木華&bull;海賦】崇島巨鼇。</p>
