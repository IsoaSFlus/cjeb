                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">冯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冯</strong></p>
<p>馮 <span class="dicpy">F&eacute;ng</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Feng village]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郑</span>于是乎伐 <span class="diczx2">冯</span>、 <span class="diczx2">滑</span>、 <span class="diczx2">胥靡</span>， <span class="diczx2">负</span><span class="diczx2">黍</span>、 <span class="diczx2">狐</span><span class="diczx2">人</span>、 <span class="diczx2">阙外</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;定公六年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/冯梦龙">冯梦龙</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冯</strong></p>
<p>馮 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，冫声。冫，古冰字的楷书写法。本义:马跑得快)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[galloppingly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冯，马行疾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冯冯(马行疾速的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 大，盛 <span class="encs">[great]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今君奋焉震电冯怒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冯弱犯寡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司马》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝冯怒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冯戎(富盛);冯怒(盛怒，震怒);冯隆(高大的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>冯</strong></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">馮</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“冯”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/馮">馮</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上" target="_blank">亥集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上&amp;bs=馬" target="_blank">馬</a>】
                              馮·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：2</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/99AE.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕淜【廣韻】扶冰切【集韻】【韻會】皮冰切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音憑。【說文】馬行疾也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】乗也，陵也，登也。【易&middot;泰卦】包荒用馮河。【疏】用馮河者，無舟渡水，馮陵於河，是頑愚之人。此九二能包含容受，故曰用馮河也。【爾雅&middot;釋訓】馮河，徒涉也。【疏】小雅小旻，不敢馮河。毛傳云：馮，陵也。然則空涉水陵波而渡，故訓馮爲陵也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;大司馬】馮弱犯寡則眚之。【註】馮，謂乗陵也。</p>
<p>又馮馮，牆堅聲。【詩&middot;大雅】削屢馮馮。【傳】削牆鍛屢之聲馮馮然。【朱傳】削屢，牆成而削治重複也。馮馮，牆堅聲。</p>
<p>又借爲馮依字。【詩&middot;大雅】有馮有翼。【傳】可馮依，以爲輔翼也。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】馮，怒也。楚曰馮。【註】馮，恚盛貌。【左傳&middot;昭五年】今君奮焉，震<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/290D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩃓</span>馮怒。</p>
<p>又恃也，矜也。【史記&middot;伯夷傳】衆庶馮生。【註】馮者，恃也。言衆庶之情，蓋馮恃矜其生也。</p>
<p>又【莊子&middot;盜跖篇】富人侅溺於馮氣，若負重行而上也。【註】呂吉甫曰：馮，恃多資氣驕滿也。舊註馮讀如憤，非。</p>
<p>又不滿之意。【張衡&middot;西京賦】惟帝王之神麗，懼尊<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>之不殊。雖斯宇之旣坦，心猶馮而未攄。【註】宮室神嚴，所以別尊<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>，故此宇雖寬坦，心未攄散也。</p>
<p>又古郡名。【前漢&middot;地理志】左馮翊註，故秦內史，太初元年更名。</p>
<p>又馮乗縣，屬蒼梧郡。</p>
<p>又官名。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;宗伯】馮相氏。【註】馮，乗也，相視也。</p>
<p>又馮夷，神名。</p>
<p>又【集韻】披耕切，音怦。馮，閎大也。一曰虛廓。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】房戎切【集韻】【韻會】符風切【正韻】符中切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音逢。【玉篇】姓也。【韻會】鄭馮<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>子。</p>
<p>又【集韻】皮命切，音病。據也。</p>
<p>又父吻切。與憤同。懣也。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/99AE.svg">                          <p>馬行疾也。从馬冫聲。房戎切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：本音皮冰切。經典通用爲依馮之馮。今别作憑，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/99AE_7717.svg" /><span class="swbf">(馮)</span><span class="ywyy">馬行疾也。从馬仌聲。</span><span class="swdz">皮冰切。六部。按馬行疾馮馮然，此馮之本義也。展轉他用而馮之本義廢矣。馮者，馬蹏箸地堅實之皃，因之引伸其義爲盛也、大也、滿也、懣也。如左傳之馮怒，離騷之馮心以及天問之馮翼惟象，淮南書之馮馮翼翼，地理志之左馮翊，皆謂充盛，皆畐字之合音叚借。畐者，滿也，或叚爲凭字。凡經傳云馮依，其字皆當作凭，或叚爲淜字。如易、詩、論語之馮河皆當作淜也。俗作憑，非是。</span></p>                          </div>
