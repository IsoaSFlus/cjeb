                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>雅</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄚˇ</span></p><ol><li>正规的，标准的：～言。～正（ａ．规范的；ｂ．正直；ｃ．客气话，用于赠给他人的书画题款上，请对方指正）。</li><li>美好的，高尚的，不粗俗的：文～。高～。典～。～观。～教（<span class="dicpy">jiào </span>）。～兴（<span class="dicpy">xìng </span>）。～座。～俗。</li><li>平素，素来：～爱。～善鼓琴。</li><li>极，甚：～以为美。～不欲为。</li><li>交往：无一日之～。</li><li>酒器名：～量（<span class="dicpy">liàng </span>）（ａ．大的酒量；ｂ．宽宏的气度）。</li><li>中国周代朝庭上的乐歌：风～颂。～声（泛指诗歌）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">鸦</span>”，乌鸦。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">雅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雅</strong> <span class="dicpy">yā</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“鸦”</span> <span class="encs">[crow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雅，楚乌也。一名鷽，一名卑居，秦谓之雅。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按:<span class="yhcs">“大而纯黑反哺者乌，小而不纯黑不反哺者雅。雅即乌之转声。字亦作鸦，作鵶。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流之为鵰。——<span class="smcs">《书·古太誓》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“鸦乌也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸱鸦耆鼠。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雅片">雅片</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雅</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从隹，牙声。从隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)，与鸟有关。( <span class="dicpy">yā</span>)本义:鸟名，乌鸦的一种。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“楚乌也。”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 正确;合乎规范 <span class="encs">[correct，right;standard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雅者，正也。言王政之所由废兴也。——<span class="smcs">《毛诗序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">文章尔雅。——<span class="smcs">《史记·三王世家》</span>。索隐:皆训正也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雅之为言正也。——<span class="smcs">《风俗通·声音》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雅者，古正也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通·礼乐》</span>。按:说文疋下古文，以为诗大雅字。疋字隶体似正，故傅会训正，其实古文借疋为諝，后又借雅为諝也。风，讽也。雅，諝也，颂，诵也。此四始之本字。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辞令就得谓之雅。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子安雅。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·荣辱》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“正而有美德者谓之雅。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">察纳雅言。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:雅道(正道，大道);雅音(正音);雅算(正确的决策);雅学(正道之学。指儒家经典之学)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 高尚;美好 <span class="encs">[fine;refined;elegant]</span></p><p><span
