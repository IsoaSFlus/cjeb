                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>猛</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">měng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/měng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>气势大，力量大：～将。～士。～烈。勇～。</li><li>忽然，突然：～然。～省（<span class="dicpy">xǐng </span>）（亦作“猛醒”）。～可（突然，陡然）。～不防。</li><li>严厉：宽以济～。</li><li>凶暴：苛政～于虎。～禽。～兽。～戾。～悍。</li><li>古哺乳动物，长毛的象：～犸。</li></ol>

class="diczx1">猛，健犬也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驱猛兽。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">猛虞趪趪。——</span><span class="dicpy">měng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/měng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 凶恶可怕 <span class="encs">[fierce;ferocious;violent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公之狗甚猛。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粗厉猛起。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苛政猛于虎。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如猛兽奇鬼。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:猛利(犹凶暴);猛戾(凶暴乖张);猛急(犹暴躁);猛暴(凶残暴戾)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 勇猛;威猛 <span class="encs">[valiant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">威而不猛。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">便捷猛鸷。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命猛将统兵。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·赤壁之战》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雄发指危冠，猛气冲长缨。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《咏荆轲》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:猛进(勇猛精进);猛决(勇猛果决);猛勇(威猛勇敢);猛气(勇猛的气势和气概)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 严厉 <span class="encs">[severe]</span>。如:猛法(严酷的法令);猛峻(严峻);猛厉(严厉刚烈;严酷，严厉)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 猛烈(强烈;刚烈) <span class="encs">[fierce;violent;vigorous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">火烈风猛。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">猛浪若奔。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:猛火(猛烈燃烧的火);猛迅(猛烈而迅速);凶猛(凶恶强大)</p><p>◎ <strong>猛</strong> <span class="dicpy">měng</span> <span
