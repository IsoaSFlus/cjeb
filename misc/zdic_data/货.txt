						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>货</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">貨</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huò.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>商品：～物。～主。～位。～栈。</li><li>钱币：～币。通～。</li><li>卖：～殖（经商）。～卖。</li><li>贿赂。</li><li>骂人或开玩笑的话：蠢～。宝～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">货</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>货</strong></p><p>貨 <span class="dicpy">huò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，化声。本义:财物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 财物，金钱珠玉布帛的总称 <span class="encs">[property]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">货，财也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二曰货。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“掌金帛之官。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">商贾阜通货贿。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“金玉曰货，布帛曰贿。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">司货。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">多货则伤于德。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“货，天地所化生谓玉也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">假货居贿。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">患货之不足。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而货财寡。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贪于财货。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">治行则货积。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·立本》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">居为奇货。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:货儿(货郎);货易(即贸易);货财(财物);货贿(金玉布帛，即财物)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 钱币 <span class="encs">[money]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日中为市，致天下之民，聚天下之货，交易而退。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先是汉川悉以绢为货。——<span class="smcs">《宋书·刘秀之传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:货布(古代钱币名。王莽时所铸。又泛指货币);货贝(古代用贝壳作的货币);货法(货币流通的法令);货泉(王莽时货币名。亦指货币的通称)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 货物，具有经济用途或者能满足经济上的某种需要的东西 <span class="encs">[goods;cargo]</span>。如:百货;送货上门;上等货;货真价实;货全部合格;发货;到货;卸货;装货;货包(货物包裹)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 对人的贬称(多为詈语或玩笑语) <span class="encs">[person used as a term of revile with an abusive prefix]</span>。如:蠢货;宝货;货头(货色，东西。多用于对人蔑称) </p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>货</strong></p><p>貨 <span class="dicpy">huò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8CA8.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕䞈<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23C77.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣱷</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>呼臥切，火去聲。【說文】財也。【廣韻】貨者，化也。變化反易之物，故字有化也。【易·繫辭】日中爲市，致天下之民，聚天下之貨，交易而退。【書·洪範】八政，二曰貨。【傳】貨，寶用物。【周禮·天官·大宰】商賈阜通貨賄。【註】金玉曰貨。【前漢·食貨志】貨，謂布帛可衣及金刀龜貝，所以分財布利通有無者也。</p><p>又賂也。【左傳·僖二十八年】晉侯有疾，曹伯之豎侯獳貨筮史，使曰：以曹爲解。【註】貨，猶賂也。</p><p>又【玉篇】貨，賣也。　【六書故】作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27D26.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧴦</span>。</p>
