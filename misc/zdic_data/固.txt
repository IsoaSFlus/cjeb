						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>固</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>结实，牢靠：坚～。稳～。巩～。凝～。加～。</li><li>坚定，不变动：～执。～定。～本。</li><li>本，原来：～有。～步自封（亦作“故步自封”）。</li><li>鄙陋（见识浅少）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">固</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>固</strong> <span class="dicpy">gù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从囗( <span class="dicpy">wéi</span>)，<span class="yhcs">“围”</span>的古体字，象四周围起来的样子，古声。本义:坚，坚固)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[solid;firm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">固，四塞也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亦孔之固。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·天保》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“固，坚也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">脩陈固列。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弱颜固植。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筋骨欲其固也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·达郁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦孝公</span>据殽函之固。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如是则兵劲城固，敌国畏之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王霸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">良马固车。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·难势》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:固项(牛皮制的护领);固阴(隆冬时阴气固结);固植(根基巩固。比喻意志坚定不移)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指地势险要和城郭坚固 <span class="encs">[strategically located and difficult of access]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">荆州</span>与国邻接，江山险固，沃野万里。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今夫</span><span class="diczx2">颛臾</span>，固而近于 <span class="diczx2">费</span>，今不取，后世必为子孙忧。——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:固塞(坚固的要塞)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 久 <span class="encs">[of long time]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">固，久也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅·广诂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣固闻之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语六》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆以太子为非固相也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策二》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 疾病经久难治的。后作<span class="yhcs">“痼”</span> <span class="encs">[chronic]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">季冬行春令，则<span class="yhcs">“国多固疾。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太后前闻</span><span class="diczx2">商</span>有女，欲以备后宫， <span class="diczx2">商</span>言有固疾。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王商传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 安定，稳固 <span class="encs">[stable]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">根不固而求木之长，德不厚而思国之安，臣虽下愚，知其不可。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国可以固。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语二》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:固色(安定的情绪和神色)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 固执，专一 <span class="encs">[single-minded]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝心之固，固不可彻。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">独夫之心，日益骄固。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:固护(精神专一，意志坚定)</p><p><span
