data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，扁声。本义:衣带或衣服狭小)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cramped;narrow and small]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">褊，衣小也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">带其褊矣。——<span 又如:褊衫(袈裟);褊衣(狭小的紧身衣)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 狭小 <span class="encs">[narrow and small]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以敝邑褊小，不足以容从者。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:褊能(能力不足);褊小(狭小。指地域、车船等不宽大)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 匮乏 <span class="encs">[poor]</span>。如:褊乏(匮乏);褊短(贫乏)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“惼”</span>。气量狭小;急躁 <span class="encs">[narrow-minded;irritable]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维是褊心，是以为刺。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·葛履》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然卓性刚而褊。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·吕布传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:褊迫(气量小;性子急);褊心(心地狭窄);褊忌(心胸狭小)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“扁”</span>。物体厚度小于长度和宽度 <span class="encs">[flat]</span>。如:褊桃(桃子的一种。即扁桃)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr
