                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">燧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>燧</strong> <span class="dicpy">suì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从火，遂声。本义:古代取火器)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[flint]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燧，塞上亭守烽火者。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燧，以取火于日。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有圣人作，钻燧取火，以化腥臊，而民悦之，使王天下，号之曰</span><span class="diczx2">燧人氏</span>。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">钻燧取火。——<span class="smcs">《论语·阳货》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命夙驾载燧。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">钻燧取火，以化腥臊。——<span class="smcs">《人类的出现》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幽王为熢燧。——<span class="smcs">《史记·周本纪》</span>。正义:<span class="yhcs">“炬火也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:燧(铜)镜(古代用以向日取火)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 火炬之类 <span class="encs">[torch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前驱举燧，后乘抗旌。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《应诏诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 烽火台 <span class="encs">[beacon tower]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">南朝</span>自 <span class="diczx2">武昌</span>至 <span class="diczx2">京口</span>，列置烽燧，此山当是其一也。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代边防报警的信号。白天放烟告警叫<span class="yhcs">“烽”</span>，夜间举火告警叫<span class="yhcs">“燧”</span> <span class="encs">[beacon fire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">幽王</span>欲悦之，数为举燧火。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·孽嬖传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有寇则燃火，举之以相告，曰烽。又多积薪，寇至即燔之望其烟，曰燧。昼则燔燧，夜则举烽。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·光武纪》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:燧火(古代边防报警的烟火);燧堡(烽火台)。后泛指报警的烽火，不分昼夜</p><p><span class="diczx1">与城上烽燧相望。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·号令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 守望烽燧的基本组织 <span class="encs">[beacon group]</span>。燧是最基本的组织，专司本烽燧的守望职责，设燧长，其秩位相当于亭长</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>燧</strong> <span class="dicpy">suì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>燃 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燧松明而识浅，散星宿于亭皋。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span
