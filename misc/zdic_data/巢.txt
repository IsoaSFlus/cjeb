                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>巢</strong> <span class="dicpy">cháo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cháo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，下边是树木，木上是三只鸟和鸟窝。合起来表示鸟栖于树窝上。本义:鸟窝)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[birds' nests]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巢，鸟在木上曰巢，在穴曰窠。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸟之所乳谓之巢。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夏则居橧巢。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语·问礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维鹊有巢，维鸠居之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·鹊巢》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又泛指其他动 物的巢穴 <span class="encs">[nest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">长安</span>城南有鼠衔黄蒿、柏叶，上民冢柏及榆树上为巢。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又泛指蜂、蚁、兽的窝。如:巢车(古代的一种兵车。用以瞭望敌军。车上有用辘轳升降的瞭望台，人在台中，如鸟在巢，故名);巢聚(谓聚集在巢穴中)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古人住的巢穴 <span class="encs">[house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有圣人作，构木为巢以避群害。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 敌人或盗贼盘踞的地方 <span class="encs">[den]</span>。如:巢窠(盘踞之地);巢薮(指盗匪或敌人盘踞之地)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>巢</strong> <span class="dicpy">cháo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cháo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 筑巢 <span class="encs">[build nest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燕雀乌鹊，巢堂坛兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·屈原·涉江》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:巢幕(筑巢于帷幕之上。喻处境危险)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 居住;栖息 <span class="encs">[dwell]</span>。如:巢居子(泛指隐士);巢栖(指隐居)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/巢毁卵破">巢毁卵破</a></span><span
