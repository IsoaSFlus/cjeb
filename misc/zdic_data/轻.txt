                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>轻</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">輕</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>分量小，与“重（<span class="dicpy">zhòng </span>）”相对：～重。～型。～便（<span class="dicpy">biàn </span>）。～于鸿毛。～尘栖弱草（喻人生渺小短暂）。</li><li>程度浅，数量少：年～。工作～。</li><li>用力小：～放。～声。～闲。～描淡写。</li><li>负载少，装备简省：～装。～骑。～锐（轻装的精锐部队）。</li><li>认为容易，不以为重要：～视。～蔑。～生。</li><li>随便，不庄重：～率（<span class="dicpy">shuài</span>）。～佻。～浮。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">轻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>轻</strong></p><p>輕 <span class="dicpy">qīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:车名)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 轻车 <span class="encs">[light carriage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻，轻车也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“轻本车名，故字从车。引申为凡轻重之轻。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻车之萃。谓驰敌致师之车也。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·车仆》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使轻车锐骑冲雍门。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 轻工业的简称 <span class="encs">[light industry]</span>。如:均衡发展农、轻、重</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 化学元素氢的旧译 <span class="encs">[hydrogen]</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>轻</strong></p><p>輕 <span class="dicpy">qīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉?</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 分量不大。与<span class="yhcs">“重”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[light]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻重同。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓斤两。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">权然后知轻重，度然后知长短。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渭城朝雨浥轻尘。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《送元二使安西》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:轻单(<span class="encs">[衣着]</span>单薄);轻省(负担轻，不费力);轻肥(穿轻裘，乘肥马。比喻显贵的人或豪奢的生活);轻狐(轻暖的狐裘)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 微小 <span class="encs">[small;little]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有轻罪者，赎以金分。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·泛论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无势之谓轻。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·解老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻者无罪者。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:轻小(细小);轻文(微小的水波);轻汗(些微的汗);轻雨(小雨)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 轻快，轻便 <span class="encs">[light;portable]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">师轻而骄。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓超乘也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻锐僄狡。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span>。注:<span
