						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>枪</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">槍</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>刺击用的长矛：长～。标～。红缨～。</li><li>发射子弹的武器：手～。步～。机关～。～杀。～林弹雨。</li><li>像枪的东西：焊～。烟～。</li><li>两头尖的竹木片，供编篱笆用。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> spear, lance; gun, rifle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Brenner, Schusswaffe, Speer, pistolenähnlicher Gegenstand  (S)</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>枪</strong></p><p>槍、鎗 <span class="dicpy">qiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，仓声。本义:二树的枝条互相抵拒)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古时长柄有尖头的刺击武器 <span class="encs">[spear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枪，歫也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，歫人之械也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枪，谓木两 头锐者也。——<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剡木伤盗曰枪。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挟其枪刈耨刈。——<span class="smcs">《国语·齐语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“桩也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铁骑突出刀枪鸣。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枪刺</span><span class="diczx2">应元</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:红缨枪;枪缨(枪上用丝、线等做成的穗状饰物)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 武器名。火铳，火枪;发射子弹的火器。同<span class="yhcs">“鎗”</span> <span class="encs">[gun;rifle;firearms]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">开枪者。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枪声隆然。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《青稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众枪齐发。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">发枪之号。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">都带着洋枪，还怕什么呢?——<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:枪火(指枪支弹药);枪把子(枪柄。比喻权柄);枪材(制作枪柄的木材);枪枝(同<span class="yhcs">“枪支”</span>);枪竿(竖立的枪身);枪疤(枪伤后留下的伤疤);枪珠(炮弹);枪柱(枪上可以推送子弹进枪膛的机件)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指枪形的东西 <span class="encs">[a gun-shape thing]</span>。如:枪蒲(菖蒲);烟枪;茶枪(茶叶的嫩芽);电子枪;注油枪，注干油枪;水泥喷枪;焊枪</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 削尖的竹木片 <span class="encs">[bamboo or wood chip]</span>。如:枪篱(竹篱笆);枪累(用尖竹木相累而成的藩篱、栅栏)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>枪</strong></p><p>槍 <span class="dicpy">qiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“抢”</span>。触，撞，碰撞 <span class="encs">[knock]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枪榆坊而止。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见狱吏则头枪地。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任少卿书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:枪榆(形容识浅志小，亦指识浅志小的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 请人代替考试 <span class="encs">[substitute for another during test]</span>。如:枪替</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/69CD.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】七羊切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】千羊切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音鏘。【說文】距也。【周禮·秋官·職金】國有大故，而用金石，則掌其令。【註】用金石者，作槍雷椎椁之屬。【揚雄·長楊賦】木雍槍纍，以爲儲胥。</p><p>又【玉篇】木兩頭銳也。【類篇】剡木傷盜曰槍。</p><p>又曰器也。見槈字註。</p><p>又抵也。【前漢·路溫舒傳】見獄吏，則頭槍地。</p><p>又【前漢·天文志】紫宮左之星曰天槍。</p><p>又姓。【姓苑】漢槍傳。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】楚耕切【正韻】抽庚切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音崢。【爾雅·釋天】彗星爲欃槍。亦叶音鏘。【宋·謝瞻·張子房詩】婉婉幙中畫，輝輝天業昌。鴻門銷薄蝕，垓下隕欃槍。</p><p>

                          <span class="jieshi">歫也。</span>止部曰。歫、止也。一曰槍也。按槍有相迎鬬爭之意。通俗文曰。剡木傷盜曰槍。今俗作鎗。<span class="jieshi">從木。倉聲。</span>七羊切。十部。<span class="jieshi">一曰槍攘也。</span>三字句。攘各本從木。誤。今正。莊子在宥。傖囊。崔譔作戕囊。云戕囊猶搶攘。晉灼注漢書曰。搶攘、亂皃也。按許無從手之搶。凡槍攘、上從木、下從手。                          </div>
