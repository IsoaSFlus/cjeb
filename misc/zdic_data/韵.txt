                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">韵</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>韵</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">韻</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>汉语字音中的元音或元音加收尾音，即声母以外的部分，或声母和介音以外的部分，称&ldquo;韵母&rdquo;。如&ldquo;娘&rdquo;ni&aacute;ng的韵母是iang，其中i是韵头，a是韵腹，ng是韵尾。～文（有韵律节奏的文学体裁，亦指用这种体裁写成的文章，包括诗、词、歌、赋等。区别于&ldquo;散文&rdquo;）。押～。～腹（韵母中主要元音）。～脚（韵文句末押韵的字）。～律（诗词中的平仄格式和押韵的规则）。</li>	<li>和谐而有节奏的：～白。琴～悠扬。</li>	<li>风度，风致，情趣，意味：风～。气～。～味。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">韵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>韵</strong></p>
<p>韻 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从音，员(匀)声。本义:和谐悦耳的声音)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[musical sound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余韵徐歇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">短笛无声，寒砧不韵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:韵悠悠(声音悠扬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 归本于喉的音。即语音学所称的韵母 <span class="encs">[rhyme]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:韵符(标注韵母的符号)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 诗赋中的韵脚或押韵的字 <span class="encs">[rhythm]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:韵牌匣子(近体诗所用之诗韵，即平水韵。将韵字做成小牌，置于匣内并按韵部分匣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 气韵;风度 <span class="encs">[charm;poise]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:韵度(风韵态度)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>韵</strong></p>
<p>韻 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 风韵雅致 <span class="encs">[charm]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:韵人(有才华的文人雅士)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 美，标致 <span class="encs">[beautiful]</span>?</p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">韵</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>韻</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄩㄣˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>y&ugrave;n <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/6592.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">聲韻學將漢字音節分為前後兩部分，開頭部分為聲，聲之後即為韻。最複雜的韻包括韻首、韻腹、韻尾三部分。參見「<a href='/hans/韻母' target=_blank>韻母</a> 」條。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">詩賦文學中的韻腳，或押韻之字，詩韻只考慮韻腹、韻尾是否相同。如東、公同屬一韻。</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝梁&middot;劉勰《文心雕龍&middot;聲律》：「異音相從謂之和，同聲相應謂之<b>韻</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;王勃〈滕王閣序〉：「一言均賦，四<b>韻</b>俱成。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">和諧的聲音。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「琴<b>韻</b>悠揚」。</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;蔡邕〈琴賦〉：「于是繁絃既抑，雅<b>韻</b>復揚。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">神態、風度、情趣。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「風<b>韻</b>猶存」、「神<b>韻</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;陶淵明〈歸園田居〉詩五首之一：「少無適俗<b>韻</b>，性本愛丘山。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《南史&middot;卷二○&middot;謝弘微傳》：「康樂誕通度，實有名家<b>韻</b>，若加繩染功，剖瑩乃瓊瑾。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">風雅、風趣。</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝宋&middot;劉義慶《世說新語&middot;言語》：「或言道人畜馬不<b>韻</b>，支曰：『貧道重其神駿。』」</span><span class="gc_yy">《醒世恆言&middot;卷二五&middot;獨孤生歸途鬧夢》：「卻恁般淒楚，忒煞不<b>韻</b>！該罰！該罰！」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
