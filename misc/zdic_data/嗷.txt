<p><span class="dicpy">&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>象声词，愁叹声，嘈杂声：～～（哀号声）。～～待哺（形容饥饿时急于求食的样子）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> loud clamor; sound of wailing</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vive clameur,acclamation</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>嗷</strong> <span class="dicpy">&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;象&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，敖声。本义:哀鸣声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[make a hubbub;whine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗷，众口愁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸿雁于飞，哀鸣嗷嗷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鸿雁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嗷然(大喊大叫的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 叫呼声;喧杂声 <span class="encs">[cry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">声嗸儗以寂寥兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;惜贤》</span>。注:&ldquo;呼声也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谗口嗸嗸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刘向传》</span>。注:&ldquo;众声也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嗷嘈(吵闹);嗷骚(喧扰，不平静)</span></p>
