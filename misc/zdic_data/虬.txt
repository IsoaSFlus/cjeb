                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>虬</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiú.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄡˊ</span></p><ol><li>古代传说中有角的小龙：～龙。</li><li>拳曲：～曲（盘绕弯曲）。～须。～髯（拳曲的胡须，特指两腮上的胡须）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> young dragon</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> junger Drache</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> frisé,ondulé,jeune dragon légendaire à cornes</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">虬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>虬</strong></p><p>虯 <span class="dicpy">qiú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代传说中的有角的龙 <span class="encs">[small dragon without horns]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虯，龙子有角者。从虫，按，俗字作<span class="yhcs">“虬。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有鳞曰蛟龙，有翼曰应龙，有角曰虯龙，无角曰螭龙。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释鱼》</span>。按，龙“雄有角，雌无角，龙子一角者蛟，两角者虯，无角者螭也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">母龙曰蛟，子曰虯，其状鱼身如蛇尾，皮有珠。——<span class="smcs">《抱朴子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驾青虬兮骖白螭。——</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《涉江》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:虬立(如虬龙般耸立。形容恣态骁勇矫健);虬虎(龙和虎。比喻英雄豪士);虬柱(雕绘虬龙的柱子);虬龙片甲(虬龙是罕见的动物，虽是一片鳞甲，也很难得。比喻贵重的物品)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>虬</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 盘曲，卷曲的样子 <span class="encs">[curling]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弓欺猿臂秋无力，剑泣虬髯晓有霜。——</span><span class="diczx2">罗邺</span><span class="smcs">《老将》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:虬眉(卷曲的眉毛);虬髯(卷曲的胡须);虬文(纠绕盘曲的花纹);虬屈(盘屈纠结的样子)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/866F.svg">                          <p>龍子有角者。从虫丩聲。渠幽切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/866F_5829.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(虯)</span><span class="ywyy">龍無角者。</span><span class="swdz">各本作龍子有角者。今依韵會所據正。然韵會尙誤多子字。李善注甘泉賦引說文虯、龍無角者。他家所引作有角、皆誤也。王逸注離騷天問㒳言有角曰龍、無角曰虯。高誘注淮南同。張揖上林賦注、後漢書馮衍傳注、玉篇、廣韵皆曰。無角曰虯。絕無龍子有角之說。惟廣雅云。有角曰？。卽虯字。無角曰？。卽螭字。其說乖異。恐轉寫之譌。不爲典要。</span><span class="ywyy">从虫。丩聲。</span><span class="swdz">渠幽切。三部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
