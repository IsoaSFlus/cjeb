						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>悬</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">懸</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xuán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuán.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>挂，吊在空中：～垂。～吊。～梯。～河。～心。～念（ａ．挂念；ｂ．文艺作品对故事情节发展和人物今后命运的伏笔）。～梁刺股。</li><li>没有着落，没有结束：～断。～案。～而未决。</li><li>距离远：～隔。～殊。</li><li>危险：～崖。～乎（“<span class="diczx3">乎</span>”读轻声）。</li><li>公开提示：～赏。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">悬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>悬</strong></p><p>懸 <span class="dicpy">xuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，县声。本作<span class="yhcs">“县”</span>，象断首倒挂，后加<span class="yhcs">“系”</span>。假借为行政区域的<span class="yhcs">“县”</span>后，另加<span class="yhcs">“心”</span>字表示悬挂。本义:吊挂)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hang;suspend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悬者植梯接之。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以叵幅悬之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倒悬之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:悬丝诊脉(传说古代中医诊脉法之一。不见面将丝线系于病人腕上诊脉治病);悬壁(相学术语。指脸部接近耳垂下端处的部位)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 牵挂;挂念 <span class="encs">[feel anxious;worry about]</span>。如:悬结(悬念;惦记);悬悬(挂念);悬肠挂肚(担心;挂念)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 凭空设想;揣测 <span class="encs">[imagine without foundation]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你们做的事，须自己承认。怎么悬定得罪?——<span class="smcs">《梼杌闲评——明珠缘》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:悬度(无根据地揣测、估计);悬料(凭空臆测);悬猜(揣测，猜想)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 揭示 <span class="encs">[reveal]</span>。如:悬金(出具赏格)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 公布 <span class="encs">[publish]</span>。如:悬令(公布法令);悬名(张榜公布名姓);悬法(公布法令)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 系连，关联 <span class="encs">[correlate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吏者，民之所悬命也。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王之命悬于遂手。——<span class="smcs">《史记·平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>悬</strong></p><p>懸 <span class="dicpy">xuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 久延不决 <span class="encs">[unresolved]</span>。如:悬调(长期拖欠的赋税);悬租(长期拖欠的租赋)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 孤立，无所依傍 <span class="encs">[alone;sole]</span>。如:悬然(无所依傍貌;空虚貌);悬师(远征的孤军);悬守(犹孤守)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 空虚，匮乏 <span class="encs">[empty]</span>。如:悬虚(凌空;空虚、空洞)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形容高耸，陡峭 <span class="encs">[steep]</span>。如:悬崖峭障(陡直的山崖，高险的山峯);悬峰(陡峭的山峰)</p><p><span
