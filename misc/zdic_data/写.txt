						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>写</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">寫</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiě  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiě.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>用笔作字：～字。～作。编～。</li>    <li>描摹，叙述：～生。～实。～照（❶画人物的形象；❷描写刻画）。轻描淡～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">写</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>写</strong></p><p>冩 <span class="dicpy">xiě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiě.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀，舃( <span class="dicpy">xì</span>)声。本义:移置;放置)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[replace;pour]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">写，置物也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗字亦作泻。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以浍写水。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·稻人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牛弗进，则置虚命彻。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公四年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“写器令空。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">御食于君，君赐余，器之溉者不写，其余皆写。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“写者，传已器中，乃食之也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 输送 <span class="encs">[carry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">发</span><span class="diczx2">北山</span>石椁，乃写 <span class="diczx2">蜀</span>、 <span class="diczx2">荆</span>地材皆至。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 倾吐，倾诉;抒发 <span class="encs">[express;pour out (one’s heart，troubles，etc.)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驾言出游，以写我忧。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·泉水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">写，除也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我心写也。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·裳裳者华》</span>。笺:<span class="yhcs">“则我心所忧，写而去矣。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何以含忍，寄之此诗;何以写思，记之斯辞。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆云</span><span class="smcs">《失题》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:写心(抒发内心感情);写志(发抒情志);写情(抒发感情);写怀(抒发情怀);写念(抒发思念之情);写思(抒发情思);写神;写忧(发抒排除忧闷)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 传抄地书写(汉以前只用<span class="yhcs">“书”</span>，汉以后<span class="yhcs">“书”</span>与<span class="yhcs">“写”</span>并用) <span class="encs">[write with a pen，etc.]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为官写书受直，以养老母。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·班超传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">武帝</span>置写书官。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·艺文志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可笑此公何太惑，读书写字到三更。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文同</span><span class="smcs">《可笑口号》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:写卷头(乡试时，考生在考前领取空白试卷，填写姓名、年龄、籍贯、祖宗三代履历后交回。入场时到二门口再发给);写字洞(办公室);写染(指书法、绘画、写诗、作文等笔墨之事)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 抄写 <span class="encs">[copy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《三都赋》</span>成，竞相传写。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·左思传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">困乏写手，一时未得奉寄。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《答俞右吉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:写白(誊清);写手(抄手);写书(抄写书籍);写经(抄经);写录(抄录)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 仿效;描绘 <span class="encs">[follow;describe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雷震之声，可以放钟写也。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坟墓写状以孤出，哭泣含声而相召。——</span><span class="diczx2">隋</span>· <span class="diczx2">李播</span><span class="smcs">《周天大象赋》</span></p><p><span
