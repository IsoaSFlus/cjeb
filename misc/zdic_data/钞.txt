						<p>● <strong>钞</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鈔</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/抄">抄</a>&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>纸币：～票。现～。兑换外～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> paper money, bank notes; copy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> billet de banque</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">钞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>钞</strong></p>
<p>鈔 <span class="dicpy">chāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 掠取;抢掠。后作&ldquo;抄&rdquo; <span class="encs">[rob]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钞，叉取也。从金，少声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗字作抄。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遮取谓之抄掠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攻钞郡县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;公孙瓒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:钞掠(劫掠骚扰;劫夺他人财物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 誊写。也作&ldquo;抄&rdquo; <span class="encs">[copy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">溥</span>幼嗜学，所读书必手钞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;张溥传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:钞白(抄录的公文副本);钞胥(担任誊录钞写工作的小吏。也作&ldquo;抄胥&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>钞</strong></p>
<p>鈔 <span class="dicpy">chāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 纸币名。现在称纸币为钞票，简称钞 <span class="encs">[paper money]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">递制交钞，与钱并用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金史&middot;食货志三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:外钞(外国的钞票);冥钞(迷信人给死人烧的假钞票，有些地方叫纸钱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指钱 <span class="encs">[money]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:破钞(请客送礼破费钱);钱钞(旧时泛指钱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 文学作品等经过选录而成的集子 <span class="encs">[collection]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:<span class="smcs">《北堂书钞》</span>、<span class="smcs">《章太炎文钞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9214.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】【正韻】楚交切【集韻】初交切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䜈。【說文】叉取也。【徐鉉曰】今俗別作抄。【廣韻】略也。【後漢&middot;公孫瓚傳】尅會期日，攻鈔郡縣。</p>
<p>又【韻會】或作摷。【張衡&middot;東京賦】摷昆聊。</p>
<p>又作抄。【杜甫詩】飯抄雲子白。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】明鈔秀，鈔奇。</p>
<p>又【增韻】謄寫也。【抱朴子&middot;金丹卷】余令略鈔金丹之都，較以示後之同志者。</p>
<p>又【博雅】强也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】初敎切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】楚敎切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音勦。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;射鳥氏敺烏鳶註】烏鳶善鈔，盜便汙人。【釋文】鈔，初敎反。</p>
<p>又【正字通】楮貨名。宋史，紹興二十四年，女眞以銅少，循宋交子法，造鈔引一貫、二貫、三貫、五貫、十貫五等謂之大鈔。一百、二百、三百、五百、七百五等謂之小鈔。與錢<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>用，以七年爲限，納舊易新，諸路置交鈔庫官受之，每貫取工墨錢十五文，公私便焉。</p>
<p>又【韻會定正】官收物而給印信文憑也。卽今鈔關。</p>
<p>又與杪同。【管子&middot;幼官篇】敎行於鈔。【註】鈔，末也。【又】聽於鈔，故能聞未極。【註】鈔，深遠也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】齒紹切，音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A351.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪍑</span>。取也。</p>
