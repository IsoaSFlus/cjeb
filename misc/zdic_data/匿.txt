                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>匿</strong> <span class="dicpy">nì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从匚( <span class="dicpy">xǐ</span>)，若声(上古读音与匿声相近)。<span class="yhcs">“匚”</span>表示有所藏。本义:隐藏，躲藏)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匿，亡也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匿，藏也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瑾瑜匿瑕。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“亦藏也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而知匿其暱。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“藏也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匿怨而友其人。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“藏也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引车避匿。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">君畏匿之。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匿于溷藩以免。(藏在厕所里。溷，厕所。藩，篱、墙。)——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:隐匿(隐藏;躲起来);藏匿(藏起来不让人发现);匿光(隐藏其光华。比喻才德不外露);匿怨(内心隐藏怨恨);匿意(隐藏真情);匿谋(隐藏其谋略)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 隐瞒 <span class="encs">[conceal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闻</span><span class="diczx2">崔烈</span>集门生讲传，遂匿姓名，为 <span class="diczx2">烈</span>名人质作食。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·文学》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">过举不匿，则官无邪人。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·垦令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:匿户(隐瞒不报的户籍);匿心(隐瞒真实思想);匿年(不肯把真实年龄告人);匿作(隐瞒姓名而劳作);匿訑(隐瞒欺诈);匿悃(隐瞒实情);匿情(隐瞒真情);匿善(隐瞒才能)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 〈形〉 假借为<span class="yhcs">“慝”</span>。恶，坏 <span class="encs">[bad]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">常令不审，则百匿胜。——<span class="smcs">《管子·七法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故道之所善，中则可从，畸则不可为。匿则大惑。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·天论》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>匿</strong> <span class="dicpy">nì</span> <span class="ptr"><a
