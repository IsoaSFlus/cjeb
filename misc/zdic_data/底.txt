						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>底</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄧˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>最下面的部分：～层。～座。～下（➊下面；➋以后）。海～。～肥。</li>
	<li>末了：年～。月～。到～。</li>
	<li>根基，基础，留作根据：刨根问～。～蕴。～稿。～版。</li>
	<li>图案的基层：白～蓝花的瓶子。</li>
	<li>何，什么：～事伤感。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">抵</span>&rdquo;，达到。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">底</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>底</strong> <span class="dicpy">de</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/de.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &ldquo;底&rdquo;在五四时期至三十年代用于领属关系，现已不用</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">dǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>底</strong> <span class="dicpy">dǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，氐( <span class="dicpy">dǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǐ.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:止住;停滞)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">底，止居也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勿使有壅蔽湫底。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">底禄以德。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有所底止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盟以底信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戾久将底。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:底止(结局);底着(停滞;滞留);底遏(遏止);底滞(滞留)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 隐藏 <span class="encs">[hide]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:底伏(隐伏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 达到 <span class="encs">[arrive]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:底定(稳定，平定);底豫(由不悦变成欢乐);底平(底定);底成(取得成功)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 引致 <span class="encs">[incur]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:底力(致力;尽力);底服(致使臣服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 磨砺 <span class="encs">[grind]</span>。</p>
