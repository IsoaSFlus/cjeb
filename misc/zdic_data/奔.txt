                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>奔</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄣˉ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 急走，跑：～跑。～驰。～突（横冲直撞；奔驰）。～流。～腾。～忙。～波（劳苦奔走）。～放（疾驰。喻气势雄伟，不受拘束）。私～（女子私自投奔所爱的人，或跟他一起逃走）。</p>
<ol>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>奔</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄣˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>直往，趋向：投～。～东走。他都～六十了（将近六十岁）。</li>
	<li>为某种目的而尽力去做：～命。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>奔</strong> <span class="dicpy">bēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，上面从&ldquo;大&rdquo;(人)，象人挥动双手，下面从&ldquo;止&rdquo;(趾)，而且是三个&ldquo;止&rdquo;，表示快跑。本义:快跑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[run quickly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中庭谓之走，大路谓之奔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释宫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将奔走之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三十一年》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹赴趣也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弗迓克奔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;牧誓》</span></span></p>

			<div class="gscdl">屠乃<span>奔</span>倚其下，弛担持刀。</div><div class="gscdr">蒲松龄《狼三则·其二》</div><div class="gscdl">或王命急宣，有时朝发白帝，暮到江陵，其间千二百里，虽乘<span>奔</span>御风，不以疾也。</div><div class="gscdr">南北朝·郦道元 《三峡》</div><div class="gscdl">急湍甚箭，猛浪若<span>奔</span>。</div><div class="gscdr">南北朝·吴均 《与朱元思书》</div><div class="gscdl">今虽耄老，未有所成，犹幸预君子之列，而承天子之宠光，缀公卿之后，日侍坐备顾问，四海亦谬称其氏名，况才之过于余者乎？今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪稍之供，父母岁有裘葛之遗，无冻馁之患矣；坐大厦之下而诵《诗》《书》无<span>奔</span>走之劳矣；有司业、博士为之师，未有问而不告，求而不得者也；凡所宜有之书，皆集于此，不必若余之手录，假诸人而后见也。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
