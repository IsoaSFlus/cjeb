                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>彘</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形。矢声，其余象猪头、脚。本义:彘本指大猪，后泛指一般的猪)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pig;swine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彘，豕也。后蹏废谓之彘。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彘，猪也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">食黍与彘。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“水畜也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彘者，北方之牲也。——<span class="smcs">《贾子胎教》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">竹山有兽焉，名曰毫彘。——<span class="smcs">《西山经》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“貆猪也。</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>呼鸾猪。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故明主表信，如曾子杀彘也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说左上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸡豚狗彘。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赐之彘肩。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">与一生彘肩。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">加彘肩上。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:彘狗不如;如彘牢;猪圈)，(彘肩(即肘子。作为食物的猪腿的最上部分);彘肩斗酒(形容英雄豪壮之气)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 地名，故址在今山西霍县东北 <span class="encs">[Zhi，anc.place's name]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三年，乃流</span><span class="diczx2">王</span><span class="encs">[ <span class="diczx2">厉王</span>]</span>于 <span class="diczx2">彘</span>。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
