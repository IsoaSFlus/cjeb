                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">带</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>带</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">帶</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用皮、布或线等做成的长条物：～子。皮～。领～。一衣～水。</li>
	<li>像带子的长条物：～钢。～鱼。声～。</li>
	<li>车轮胎：车～。外～。</li>
	<li>区域：地～。温～。</li>
	<li>佩戴，披挂：～孝。～剑。</li>
	<li>随身拿着：携～。～挈。～着钱。</li>
	<li>捎，连着，顺便做：连～。～职。～累（<span class="dicpy">lěi </span>）（连累）。话中～刺。</li>
	<li>含有，呈现：～电。～伤。</li>
	<li>率领，引导：～领。～头。～动。以点～面。</li>
	<li>女子阴道分泌的白色黏液：白～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">带</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>带</strong></p>
<p>帶 <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，上面表示束在腰间的一根带子和用带的两端打成的结。下面象垂下的须子，有装饰作用。本义:大带，束衣的腰带)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[broad belt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">带，绅也。上象系佩之形。佩必有巾，从重巾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不说带。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士虞礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">之子无带。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;有狐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">带裳幅舄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王遂披襟解带，留连不能已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;文学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子</span><span class="diczx2">墨子</span>解带围城。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:带冕(大带和冠冕);带围(腰围，腰带);带钩(皮革腰带上的金属钩。形状一端曲直，另一端为圆钮以承钩。有动物形的，也有铸花纹的);带头(旧时衣袍外腰带上的扣头，常镶以金、玉等装饰物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指狭长形条状物 <span class="encs">[belt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而半山居雾若带然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裤带;鞋带</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 根据纬度和温度把地球表面分为5个大带的任一带 <span class="encs">[zone]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:温带</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 生物地理区的典型带状区。如:海洋滨岸带;南方生物带</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 以某种特殊性质、作用或成分为特征的地球物质(如岩石)的分带、分层或层系。如:饱和带</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 轮胎 <span class="encs">[car tire]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:车带;里带;外带</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 妇科病名 <span class="encs">[gyrecological disease]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:白带;赤带</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>带</strong></p>
<p>帶 <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中" target="_blank">寅集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中&amp;bs=巾" target="_blank">巾</a>】
                              帶·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5E36.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>當蓋切，音㯂。【說文】紳也。男子搫帶，婦人帶絲，象繫佩之形。佩必有巾，故帶从巾。【徐鉉曰】卌，其帶上連屬固結處。【釋名】帶，蔕也。著於衣，如物之繫蔕也。【易&middot;訟卦】或錫之鞶帶。【疏】鞶帶大帶也。【禮&middot;玉藻】凡帶有率，無箴功。【疏】謂其帶旣襌，亦以箴緶緝其側，但繂襵之，無別裨飾之箴功。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】厲謂之帶。【註】小爾雅，帶之垂者曰厲。【詩&middot;小雅】垂帶而厲。</p>
<p>又佩也。【禮&middot;月令】帶以弓䪅。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】行也。【註】隨人行也。</p>
<p>又蟲名。【莊子&middot;齊物論】螂蛆甘帶。</p>
<p>又書帶，草名。【三齊記】鄭康成山下生草，大如䪥，葉長一尺餘，土人名康成書帶草。</p>
<p>又姓。【賈誼&middot;過秦論】帶佗。</p>
<p>又叶音蒂。【楚辭&middot;九歌】荷衣兮蕙帶，儵而來兮忽而逝。夕宿兮帝鄉，誰須兮雲之際。</p>
