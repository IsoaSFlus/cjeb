                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>痴</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>傻，无知：～人说梦。～钝。～愚。白～。</li>
	<li>精神失常，疯癫：发～。～癫。</li>
	<li>入迷，极度迷恋：～心。～情。</li>
	<li>谦辞，白白地：～长（<span class="dicpy">zhǎng </span>）（说自己白白地比对方大若干岁）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> foolish, stupid, dumb, silly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dummkopf  (S)​,dumm, sentimental, stupid, töricht  (Adj)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">痴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>痴</strong></p>
<p>癡 <span class="dicpy">chī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;ng</span>)，疑声。本义:不聪慧;迟钝)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[silly;idiotic;stupid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">癡，不慧也。字俗作痴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:痴哑(痴呆喑哑);痴呆呆(傻乎乎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 疯癫，癫狂 <span class="encs">[mentally deranged]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此人素有痴名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;海瑞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 持久不止 <span class="encs">[lasting]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:痴雨(久雨。指天气久不放晴，长年阴雨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 天真的昵称 <span class="encs">[naive]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:痴孩子;痴小(年少无知)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 佛教语。贪、瞋、痴&ldquo;三毒&rdquo;之一。梵语 <span class="dicpy">moha</span>，也译作&ldquo;无明&rdquo;。谓愚昧无知，不明如实之事理<span class="encs">[stupid]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:痴定(枯坐无慧);痴箭(害人害己的邪念)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>痴</strong> <span class="dicpy">chī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 发呆 <span class="encs">[stupify]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那边哭的自己伤心，却不道这边听的早已痴倒了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 入迷 <span class="encs">[be crazy about]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:痴魂(痴情);痴儿女(沉迷于爱情的男女)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>痴</strong> <span class="dicpy">chī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>颠狂病 <span class="encs">[general term of manic-depressive psychosis]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今子独坏形貌，蒙耻辱，为狂痴，光曜晻而不宣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
