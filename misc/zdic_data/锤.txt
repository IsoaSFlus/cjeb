                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>锤</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">錘</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨㄟˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>配合秤杆称（<span class="dicpy">chēng</span>）轻重的金属块：秤～（秤砣）。</li>	<li>敲打物件的器具：～子。铁～。</li>	<li>用锤敲打：～打。～炼（①磨炼；②刻苦钻研，反复琢磨使技艺等精炼、纯熟）。千～百炼。</li>	<li>古代的一种兵器，柄的上头有一个金属圆球：铜～。</li>	<li>古代重量单位，一锤等于八铢（一说六铢）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> balance weight on scale; hammer</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hämmern ; Hammer</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> marteau,massue,poids,marteler</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>锤</strong></p>
<p>錘、鎚 <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，垂声。本义:古代重量单位) 同本义 <span class="encs">[ancient weight unit]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 八铢</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">锤，八铢也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有千金之璧，而无锱锤之礛诸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;诠言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 十二两</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽割国之锱锤以事人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;诠言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 六铢</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铢六则锤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《风俗通义》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>锤</strong></p>
<p>錘 <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 槌子，俗称榔头 <span class="encs">[hammer]</span>。如:铁锤;钉锤;锤骨(内耳听骨之一;形状似锤子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古兵器名。柄的上头有一个金属圆球 <span class="encs">[mallet]</span>。如:铜锤</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 秤砣 <span class="encs">[weight]</span>。如:秤锤;锤钩(指衡器。钩指秤钩)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 锤形物 <span class="encs">[sth.shaped like a mallet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曾招渔侣下清浔，独茧初随一锤深。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆龟蒙</span><span class="smcs">《顷自桐江得一钓车》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>锤</strong></p>
<p>錘 <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 打;击 <span class="encs">[hit;strike]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千锤万凿出深山，烈火焚烧若等闲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">于谦</span><span class="smcs">《石灰吟》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:锤锻(锤击;锻造);锤淬(锤击并淬火);锤炉(锻打)</p>
