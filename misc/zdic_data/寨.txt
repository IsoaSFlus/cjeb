						<p>● <strong>寨</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>防守用的栅栏：～子。鹿～（军事上常用的一种障碍物，古时多用削尖的竹木或枝杈，现多用铁蒺藜等做成）。</li>
	<li>旧时驻兵的营地：营～。山～。水～。安营扎～。～主。</li>
	<li>村庄：村～。苗～。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>寨</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，赛省声。本义:防卫所用的木栅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stockade;camp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敌军已近寨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寨中人踡伏。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">借寨墙为蔽。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 多指四面环围的驻军处，营垒</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时</span><span class="diczx2">巴山</span><span class="diczx2">陈定</span>亦拥兵立寨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《陈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:寨落(有栅栏或围墙的村落);寨垛(寨墙上的垛口);寨屋(营房)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 寨子，村庄 <span class="encs">[stockaded village]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:山寨;边寨(边疆地区的寨子);寨壕(环绕村寨的壕沟)</span></p>
