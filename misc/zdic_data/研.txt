                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>研</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>细磨（<span class="dicpy">m&oacute;</span>），碾：～磨。～药。～墨。</li>	<li>深入地探求：～究。钻～。～京练都（<span class="dicpy">dū</span>）（晋代左思作《三都赋》构思了十二年，后遂用&ldquo;研京练都&rdquo;形容经年累月地构思文章）。</li></ol><p>● <strong>研</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄢˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/砚">砚</a>&rdquo;，砚台。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> grind, rub; study, research</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> broyer,étudier,examiner à fond,rechercher</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">研</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>研</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石，幵( <span class="dicpy">jiān</span>)声。本义:细磨)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grind;pestle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">打取杏仁，以汤脱去黄皮，熟研，以水和之，绢滤取汁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《齐民要术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晚上把这药用酒研开，替他敷上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:研石(磨墨的用具);研弄(磨墨弄笔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 研究;探讨 <span class="encs">[study]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能研诸侯之虑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span>。注:&ldquo;研，俞思虑也。&rdquo;疏:&ldquo;精也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">研核阴阳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范晔</span><span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:研榷(研究;商订);研述(研究并阐述);研味(研究玩味);研治(研究);研省(研究省察);研赏(研究赏析);研究(细致审问);研务(探究事理)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>研</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 详尽;精细 <span class="encs">[in detail]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:研务(细审);研深(精深);研疏(精细和粗细);研校(仔细校对);研讯(仔细审讯)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 妍好。也作&ldquo;妍&rdquo; <span class="encs">[fine]</span>。</p>
