                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>裁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">c&aacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄞˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用剪子剪布或用刀子割纸：～剪。～缝。～衣。对～。</li>	<li>减除，去掉一部分：～减。～军。～员。</li>	<li>决定，判断：～判。～夺（考虑决定）。～决。～度（<span class="dicpy">du&oacute;</span>）。～断。</li>	<li>安排取舍：体～。别～。独出心～。</li>	<li>节制，抑止：制～。独～。</li>	<li>杀：自～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">裁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>裁</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，( <span class="dicpy">zāi</span>)声。本义:裁制，剪裁)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cut]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裁，制衣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夷衾质杀之，裁犹冒之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;丧大记》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹制也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十四学裁衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裁此百日功，唯将一朝舞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韦应物</span><span class="smcs">《杂体》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:套裁;剪裁;对裁;裁衣(裁剪衣料制作衣服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 裁定;判断 <span class="encs">[decide;judge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大王裁其罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span>。注:&ldquo;制也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裁大者众之所比也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;地形》</span>。注:&ldquo;断也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">公羊</span>辨而裁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;序》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓善能裁断。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今请姑娘裁夺着，再添些也使得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裁示(裁决并指示);裁答(裁决答复)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 删除;削减 <span class="encs">[reduce;cut down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而裁其贾以招民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓减省之也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又裁节邮传冗费。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;海瑞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裁革(减免);裁人(裁员。裁减人员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 割裂;割断 <span class="encs">[cut apart;separate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:裁剖(切开);裁截(切割成段;切截整齐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 安排取舍(多指文学艺术)<span class="encs">[arrange;edit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:裁长补短(取长补短);裁红点翠(比喻选择华丽的辞藻)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 杀戮 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">跪而自裁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用刀剪等把紙或布割裂、剪開。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>裁</b>布」、「<b>裁</b>紙」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;衣部》：「<b>裁</b>，制衣也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《南齊書&middot;卷三二&middot;張岱傳》：「恕未閑從政，美錦不宜濫<b>裁</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">縫製。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;古詩十九首&middot;客從遠方來》：「文綵雙鴛鴦，<b>裁</b>為合歡被。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">削減。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「裁軍」、「裁員」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第三六回》：「如今說因為襲人是寶玉 的人，<b>裁</b>了這一兩銀子，斷乎使不得。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">決斷、判斷。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>裁</b>罪」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;僖公十五年》：「夕以入則朝以死，唯君<b>裁</b>之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《韓非子&middot;初見秦》：「臣願悉言所聞，唯大王<b>裁</b>其罪。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量度。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈感春〉詩五首之二：「孤吟屢闋莫與和，寸恨至短誰能<b>裁</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">控制。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「獨<b>裁</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辭&middot;賈誼&middot;惜誓》：「神龍失水而陸居合，為螻蟻之所<b>裁</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
