                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>验</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">驗</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>检查，察看：～核。～血。～尸。～光。～证。</li>
	<li>效果，有效果：～方。灵～。应～。效～。屡试屡～。</li>
	<li>证信，凭据：何以为～？</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> test, examine, inspect; verify</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> prüfen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> contrôler,examiner,vérifier,éprouver,mettre à l'épreuve,essayer,preuve</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">验</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>验</strong></p>
<p>驗、騐 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马。佥( <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span>)声。本义:马名)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[horse&rsquo;s name]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">验，马名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 证据;凭证 <span class="encs">[proof]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何以为验。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;晋世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">商君</span>之法，舍人无验者坐之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;商君列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:验左(佐证;证据)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 效验，灵验 <span class="encs">[produce the expected result;prove effective]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自然之验。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:验方(有效验的药方);明效大验(极为显著的效验)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 征象;征兆 <span class="encs">[omen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黄为物熟验，白为人老效。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>验</strong></p>
<p>驗 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 检查;检验 <span class="encs">[examine;check;test]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦验其辞于正前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">验之以理。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;填行论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">验之以事(用事实验证它)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲验之，但取芦菔、地黄辈观(验:检验，证明)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">历验各种僵石。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:验煤;验金;验护照;验数(查数);验复(复查);验实(检查核实);验试(检查试验);验解(查实后解送);验票(查单据、票证)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 试验 <span class="encs">[test]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">赵高</span>欲为乱，恐群臣不听，乃先设验。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上" target="_blank">亥集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上&amp;bs=馬" target="_blank">馬</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9A57.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>魚窆切，黏去聲。【玉篇】馬名也。</p>
<p>又證也，效也。【史記&middot;晉世家】獻公曰：何以爲驗。荀息對曰：使死者復生，生者不慙，爲之驗。</p>
<p>又考視也。【前漢&middot;楊惲傳】惲下廷尉，于定國考問，左驗明白。【註】左，證左也。言當時在其左右見此事者也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;平帝詔】其當驗者，卽驗問。【註】就其所居而問。　俗作騐驗，非。</p>
