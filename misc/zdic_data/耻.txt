                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>耻</strong></p><p>恥 <span class="dicpy">chǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，耳声。本义:耻辱，可耻的事情)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(因声誉受损害而至的内心羞愧) <span class="encs">[disgrace]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">耻，辱也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君不使无耻。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·襄公二十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行己有耻。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">越王</span>苦 <span class="diczx2">会稽</span>之耻。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·顺民》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而患其志行之少耻也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后世且行之而不知以为耻者多矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="diczx1">坐縻廪粟而不知耻。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:耻愧(羞愧);耻惧(羞愧恐惧)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ class="cino">(1)</span> 羞辱;侮辱 <span class="encs">[humiliate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不醉反耻。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·宾之初筵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">左丘明</span>耻之， <span
