                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>诟</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">詬</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄡˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>耻辱：&ldquo;～莫大于宫刑&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>辱骂：～骂。～病（指责，辱骂）。～谇（辱骂指斥）。～厉。～詈。～辱。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> abuse, scold, berate, insult</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> missbrauchen, beschimpfen ,Schamgefühl  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> honte,outrage,injure,insulte,outrager,faire honte,couvrir de honte,injurier avec colère,insulter,s'emporter contre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>诟</strong></p>
<p>詬 <span class="dicpy">g&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p> (形声。从言，后声。本义:耻辱) 同本义 <span class="encs">[disgrace;shame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">詬，謑詬耻也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常以儒相詬病。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余不忍其詬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诟，耻辱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诟莫大于宫刑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公以</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>诟语之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诟耻(耻辱);诟丑(耻辱)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>诟</strong></p>
<p>詬 <span class="dicpy">g&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诟，骂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">曹</span>人诟之，不行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公八年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">投龟诟天而呼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">重丘人闭门而诟之。&mdash;&mdash;《左传&middot;襄公十七年。注:&ldquo;骂也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予自度不得脱，则直前诟虏帅失信。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p>又如:诟怒(怒骂);诟叱(责骂呵叱);诟悖(辱骂、悖逆);诟谩(辱骂，谩骂);诟谇谣诼(辱骂斥责，造谣中伤)</p>
