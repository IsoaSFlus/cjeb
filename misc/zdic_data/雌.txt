data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>名</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)，此声。从<span class="yhcs">“隹”</span>，表示与鸟有关。本义:母鸟。引申为母的)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 母鸟，能产卵的鸟 <span class="encs">[a female，the female of bird]</span></p><p><span class="dicpy">cí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 张露，露出。通<span class="yhcs">“龇”</span> <span class="encs">[show]</span>。如:雌牙(露出牙齿;笑);雌牙扮齿(裂开嘴笑)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 叱人曰雌 <span
