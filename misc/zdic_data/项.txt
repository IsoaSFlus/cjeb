                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>项</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">項</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄤˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>颈的后部，泛指脖子：颈～。～链。～缩（缩脖子，形容羞怯、畏缩的样子）。强～。</li>
	<li>量词，分类的条目，：～目。事～。</li>
	<li>钱款，经费：款～。进～。存～。</li>
	<li>数学用语，代数式中不用加、减号连接的单式，如&ldquo;3a&sup2;b,ax&sup2;&rdquo;等。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">项</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>项</strong></p>
<p>項 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页，工声。从页，与头有关。本义:脖子的后部)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[nuque;nape of the neck]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">项，头后也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延颈秀项，皓质呈露。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">项背相望。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;左雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:项髻(挽发髻于颈后);项窝(脖子后部的凹处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指人颈 <span class="encs">[neck]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老魔将芭蕉扇插在后项衣领。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">项掣金锁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">青项金翅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:项头(头与脖项);项领(指颈部);项缩(缩颈。形容畏惧的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 代数中不用加、减号连接的单式 <span class="encs">[term]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:内项;外项</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 款项 <span class="encs">[sum (of money)]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:进项;欠项;出项;项头(项目)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 春秋时国名 <span class="encs">[Xiang state]</span>。在今河南省项城县东北</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 冠的后部 <span class="encs">[hat back]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宾右手执项，左手执前进客。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>项</strong></p>
<p>項 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用于分项目的事物 <span class="encs">[item;subparagraph]</span>。</p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“项”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/項">項</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=頁" target="_blank">頁</a>】
                              項·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：3</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9805.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】胡講切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】戸講切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>學上聲。【說文】頭後也。【釋名】項，确也。堅确受枕之處。【儀禮&middot;士冠禮】緇布冠缺項。【註】缺讀如有頍者弁之頍，緇布冠無筓者，著頍圍髮際，結項中，隅爲四綴，以固冠也。</p>
<p>又冠後爲項。【儀禮&middot;士冠禮】賔右手執項。【註】項，結纓也。</p>
<p>又國名。【春秋&middot;僖十七年】夏滅項。【註】項國，今汝隂項縣。【前漢&middot;地理志】汝南郡縣，項。故國。</p>
<p>又【唐書&middot;西域傳】党項，漢西羌別種。</p>
<p>又姓。【韻會】本姬姓國，齊滅之，子孫以國爲氏。【史記&middot;項羽本紀】項氏世世爲楚將，封於項，故姓項氏。</p>
<p>又大也。【詩&middot;小雅】四牡項領。【傳】項，大也。【箋】但養大其領，不肯爲用。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】正古胡孔切，鴻上聲。【張衡&middot;西京賦】鮪鯢鱨鯋，修頟短項。大口折鼻，詭類殊種。</p>
