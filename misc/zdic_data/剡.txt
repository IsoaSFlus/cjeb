River]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明月照我影，送我至</span><span class="diczx2">剡溪</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span>诗</p><p><span
