                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>丞</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄥˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>帮助，辅佐：～相（古代辅佐帝王治理国家大事的统率百官的最高大臣）。</li>
	<li>封建时代辅佐主要官员做事的官吏：府～。县～。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">承</span>&rdquo;，秉承。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丞</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面象两只手，下面象人掉在陷阱里，合起来表示救人于陷阱之中。( <span class="dicpy">zhěng</span>)本义:拯救)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 辅佐;辅助 <span class="encs">[assist]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如:丞弼(辅佐;也指辅佐的大臣或职位);丞辅(辅助)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;承&rdquo;。接受、秉承 <span class="encs">[bear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前疑后丞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孝经&middot;谏诤章》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是丞上指，请造白金及五铢钱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;酷吏列传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丞</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古官名 <span class="encs">[auxiliary official]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 传说为帝王的四辅之一，是辅佐帝王的最高官吏</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 佐官名。秦始置。汉以后，中央和地方官吏的副职有大理丞、府丞、县丞等</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E1E.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】署陵切【集韻】【韻會】辰陵切【正韻】時征切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音承。【玉篇】繼也。【廣韻】佐也。【正韻】副貳也。【禮&middot;文王世子】虞、夏、商、周有師保，有疑丞。</p>
<p>又【戰國策】堯有九佐，舜有七友，禹有五丞，湯有三輔。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;百官表】丞相秦官金印紫綬，掌丞天子助理萬機。【註】應劭曰：丞者，承也。相者，助也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;淳于長傳】扶丞左右，甚有甥舅之恩。</p>
<p>又【宋史&middot;天文志】紫微垣西蕃七星，第七星爲上丞。東蕃八星，第八星爲少丞。</p>
<p>又與承通。【史記&middot;張湯傳】於是丞上指。今本或作承。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】常證切，承去聲。縣名。在沂州，匡衡所居。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶之郢切。與拯通。【揚雄&middot;羽獵賦】丞民於豐桑。</p>
