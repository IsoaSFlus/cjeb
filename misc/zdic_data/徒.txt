                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>徒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>步行：～步。～涉。</li>
	<li>空：～手。</li>
	<li>白白地：～然。～劳无益。</li>
	<li>只；仅仅：家～四壁。</li>
	<li>从事学习的人：～弟。～工。学～。师～。</li>
	<li>同一派系或信仰同一宗教的人：信～。教～。党～。</li>
	<li>人（多指坏人）：匪～。暴～。赌～。叛～。</li>
	<li>剥夺犯人自由的刑法：～刑。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">徒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>徒</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，土声。本义:步行) 同本义 <span class="encs">[go on foot;walk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徒，步行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我徒我御。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;黍苗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子耆老不徒行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徒递来告。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无徒骥于锱坛之宫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;徐无鬼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">败其徒兵于</span><span class="diczx2">洧上</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公元年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以吾从大夫之后，不可徒行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;先进》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贲其趾，舍车而徒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;贲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自京师徒步入</span><span class="diczx2">华山</span>为黄冠。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《复庵记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:徒徙(无舟船乘载而徒步过水);徒跣(赤足步行);徒践(赤足步行);徒杠(供徒步行走的小桥);徒负(徒步负荷);徒行(步行;无事闲行)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>徒</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 步兵，兵卒(周代盛行车战，车上的兵称&ldquo;甲士&rdquo;，车后跟着步行的兵叫&ldquo;徒&rdquo;) <span class="encs">[soldier]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帅徒以往。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:徒骥(步兵与骑兵);徒兵，徒卒(步兵);徒士(步兵。没有兵器、铠甲装备的战士);徒甲(徒卒和甲士);徒卫(卫兵);徒侯(徒卒侯吏);徒师(步卒，步军)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 徒党，同一类或同一派别的人 <span class="encs">[clique]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原屏咎之徒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂为我徒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人之徒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外之有徒，祸乃始牙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;版法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郯子</span>之徒。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">佞誉诬谀之徒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得志之徒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与其徒置酒酣歌。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:党徒;僧徒;教徒;徒属(随从人员);徒侣(徒伴，朋辈;同伴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 服徭役的人 <span class="encs">[slave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁徙之徒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:徒御(拉车和驾车的人);徒役(服劳役的人);徒奴(服劳役的罪犯和奴隶);徒夫(服劳役的罪人);徒士(服役的人);徒隶(服劳役的犯人);徒孥(泛指罪犯，犯人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 门徒，弟子 <span class="encs">[pupil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">仲尼</span>之徒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">传诸其徒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《朱文昌校昌黎先生集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:徒侣(门徒;学生);徒友(门徒和朋友);徒羽(门徒;党羽);徒党(门徒;党羽);徒属(门徒;部属);门徒</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 地位低的人 <span class="encs">[person]</span>。多指坏人。如:奸徒(奸险的人);无耻之徒;匪徒</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 奴仆 <span class="encs">[housemaid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时望甚崇，家徒甚殷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白行简</span><span class="smcs">《李娃传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 徒刑，古代刑法名。即拘禁使服劳役 <span class="encs">[imprisonment]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:徒罪(徒刑之罪);徒系(囚犯);徒流(徒刑与流刑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 众，很多的人 <span class="encs">[crowd]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:徒舆(众人);徒众(属下群众;门徒，弟子);徒附(依附于豪门、权势人家的人众);徒庶(民众)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 独，仅仅 <span class="encs">[only]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">徒慕君之高义。</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:徒苦(徒然受苦;白吃苦);徒恨(徒然抱恨);徒食(空坐以食而无所事事);徒善(空有善心);徒尔;徒说(白说;空说);徒语(徒言，白说);徒处(无所为);徒设(虚设)</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">步行。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;辵部》：「<b>徒</b>，步行也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;賁卦&middot;初九》：「賁其趾，舍車而<b>徒</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">步卒、兵卒。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;魯頌&middot;閟宮》：「公<b>徒</b>三萬。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;隱公四年》：「諸侯之師，敗鄭徒兵，取其禾而還。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人，多指壞人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「暴<b>徒</b>」、「匪<b>徒</b>」、「不法之<b>徒</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">服膺、信仰某一宗教或學說的人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「信<b>徒</b>」、「教<b>徒</b>」、「基督<b>徒</b>」、「佛教<b>徒</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">門人、弟子。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「學<b>徒</b>」、「門<b>徒</b>」、「徒弟」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;先進》：「非吾<b>徒</b>也。小子鳴鼓而攻之，可也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一種拘禁犯人，強服勞役的刑罰。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「無期徒刑」、「他被判有期徒刑二年，得併科罰金。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">空的，不憑藉什麼的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「徒手搏虎」、「徒手體操」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>

	<li>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下" target="_blank">寅集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下&amp;bs=彳" target="_blank">彳</a>】
                              徒·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5F92.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AE0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠫠</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同都切，音塗。【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28452.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨑒</span>。步行也。【易&middot;賁卦】舍車而徒。</p>
<p>又步卒也。【詩&middot;魯頌】公徒三萬。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋訓】徒輦者也。【疏】會同田獵，人挽輦以徒行也。【詩&middot;小雅】徒御不驚。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】衆也。【書&middot;仲虺之誥】實繁有徒。【前漢&middot;東方朔傳】人至察，則無徒。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】空也。【爾雅&middot;釋訓】暴虎，徒搏也。【註】空手執也。</p>
<p>又【廣雅】徒，袒也。【史記&middot;張儀傳】秦人捐甲，徒裼以趨敵。</p>
<p>又【正韻】但也。【孟子】徒善不足以爲政。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】黨也。【張衡&middot;思<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>賦】朋精粹而爲徒。</p>
<p>又弟子曰徒。【論語】非吾徒也。【後漢&middot;鄭康成傳】扶風馬融，門徒四百餘人。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】徒隷也。【周禮&middot;天官】冢宰胥十有二人，徒百有二十人。【疏】胥有才智爲什長，徒給使役，故一胥十徒。</p>
<p>又司徒，官名。【書&middot;舜典】汝作司徒。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;司徒疏】司徒，主衆徒也。</p>
<p>又丹徒，縣名。【前漢&middot;地理志】會稽郡有丹徒縣。【註】卽春秋云朱方也。【地志】秦望氣者言其地有王氣，始皇使赭衣徒三千鑿京峴山，以敗其勢，因名丹徒。</p>
<p>又罪名。【唐書&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>法志】用<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>有五，其三曰徒。徒者，奴也。蓋奴辱之，量其罪之輕重，有年數而捨。</p>
<p>又申徒，登徒，司徒，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>複姓。【風俗通】申屠氏，隨音改爲申徒氏，夏有申徒狄。【宋玉&middot;好色賦序】大夫登徒子，侍於楚王。【註】登徒，姓也。【姓譜】舜嘗爲堯司徒，支孫氏焉。</p>
<p>又叶唐何切，音駝。【道藏歌】運役自然氣，於是息三徒。一暢萬劫感，慶賀西王那。</p>
