						<p>● <strong>粘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ni&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄧㄢˊ</span></p>

</ol>
<p>● <strong>粘</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhān.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>黏的东西互相附着连结在一起：糖～牙。</li>
	<li>用胶水或糨糊把一种东西胶合在另一种东西上：～贴。～连。～接。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/沾">沾</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> viscous, mucous; glutinous</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> visqueux,collant,coller</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>粘</strong> <span class="dicpy">ni&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从米，占声。本义:具有粘性) 同本义 <span class="encs">[glutinous;sticky]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:粘粥(粘稠的粥);踩在一些粘东西上;粘涎(口涎;亦在方言中谓言语、举止不爽利，令人乏味);粘稠(浓厚而带粘性);粘粘涎涎(形容言语不简要)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhān.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>粘</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 粘( <span class="dicpy">ni&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )的东西互相连接或附着在别的东西上 <span class="encs">[glue;stick;paste]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:粘合(粘贴胶合);粘竿(一种顶端涂粘质，用以捕鸟的竹竿);粘着(用胶质把物体固定在一起。亦指执着，不能超脱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;沾&rdquo;。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ni&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ni&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">附著。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「麥芽糖<b>粘</b>的她衣服都是。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈贈王二十四侍御契四十韻〉：「稍稍息勞筋，網聚<b>粘</b>圓鯽。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;王禹偁〈杏花〉詩七首之一：「紅芳紫萼怯春寒，蓓蕾<b>粘</b>枝密作團。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「黏」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">糊貼。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>粘</b>海報」、「<b>粘</b>郵票」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《喻世明言&middot;卷二&middot;陳御史巧勘金釵鈿》：「有些磨壞的去處，再把些飯兒<b>粘</b>得硬硬的，墨兒塗得黑黑的。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《初刻拍案驚奇&middot;卷二一》：「恐店主人忘了，遂於沿路上令王吉于牆壁<b>粘</b>手榜。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「黏」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>

<ol>
