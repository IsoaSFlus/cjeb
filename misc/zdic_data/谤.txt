                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谤</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">謗</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>恶意攻击别人，说别人的坏话：～讥。～讪。～议。～毁。诽～。</li><li>责备：～木（传说中舜设立的供人写谏言的木牌，后代仿效。亦称“华表木”）。“厉王虐，国人～王”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> slander, libel</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> médire,vilipender,diffamer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谤</strong></p><p>謗 <span class="dicpy">bàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声兼会意。从言，旁声。本义:在背后公开地议论或批评某人的短处)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[criticise openly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谤，毁也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，谤者道人之实，事与诬谮不同。大言曰谤，小言曰诽，曰讥。</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">厉王</span>虐，国人谤王。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有能谤讥于市朝。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以致天下怨谤也。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:谤书(攻击别人或揭发别人隐私的文书;亦专指<span class="smcs">《史记》</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 诽谤 <span class="encs">[defame;slander]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">信而见疑，忠而被谤，能无怨乎?——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是故事修而谤兴。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《原毁》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:谤言(公开指责他人的话。同谤议);谤誉(毁谤或赞誉)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 诅咒 <span class="encs">[curse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">进胙者莫不谤令尹。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十七年》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/谤讪">谤讪</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/谤议">谤议</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
