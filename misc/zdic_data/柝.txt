                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>柝</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，橐声。本义:巡夜打更用的梆子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[watchman's clapper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夕击柝而比之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官·宫正》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>击柝闻于 <span class="diczx2">邾</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公七年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抱关击柝而不以为寡。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·荣辱》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大夫击门，士击柝。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·庄公二十五年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">范宁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“柝，两木相击。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔气传金柝。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">留击柝者。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:柝击(击柝巡夜);柝汲(巡夜和汲水。泛指劳作)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 象声词 <span class="encs">[clang]</span>。如:柝柝</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>柝</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 判，分开 <span class="encs">[divide]</span>。如:柝居(分家，分居)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“拓”</span>。开拓 <span class="encs">[open up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">廓四方，柝八极。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">武皇帝攘四夷广土柝境，面数千里。——<span class="smcs">《潜夫论·救边》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:柝境(开拓境域)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
