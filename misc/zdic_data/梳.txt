						<p>● <strong>梳</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>整理头发的用具：木～。角～。</li>
	<li>用梳子整理头发：～头。～洗（梳头洗脸）。～妆。～辫子（喻把纷繁的事项、问题等进行分析归类）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">梳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>梳</strong> <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从木，疏省声。本义:梳子) 同本义 <span class="encs">[comb]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梳，理发也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梳，栉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梳，言其齿疏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝有讽谏，犹发之有梳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书&middot;吴兢传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:木梳;梳枇(齿稀的叫梳，齿密的叫枇);梳掠(梳子。小的叫梳，大的叫掠);梳行(买卖梳子的牙行);梳背(压发梳一类妆饰品);梳掌(梳子的柄)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>梳</strong> <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用梳整理头发 <span class="encs">[comb one&#39;s hair]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">头蓬不暇梳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杨雄</span><span class="smcs">《长杨赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼如梳，军如篦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;洪钟传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绿云扰扰，梳晓鬟也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予九岁，憩书斋，汝梳双髻，披单缣来，温<span class="smcs">《缁衣》</span>一章。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:梳云(梳整像乌云一般的头发);梳裹(梳理头发包裹头巾。指化妆);梳沐(梳洗);梳雪(梳理白发);梳栉(梳理头发)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 整理 <span class="encs">[put in order]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白鸟梳翎立岸莎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">温庭筠</span><span class="smcs">《游南塘》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:梳爬(整理);梳翎(鸟类梳理自身羽毛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 梳笼;梳拢;梳弄 <span class="encs">[prostitute for the first time]</span>。指妓女首次按客伴宿</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这等就说标致;他家里还有一个粉头，排行三姐，号&ldquo;玉堂春&rdquo;，有十二分颜色。鸨儿索价太高，还未梳栊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/68B3.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】所菹切【集韻】【韻會】山於切【正韻】山徂切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音疏。【說文】理髮也。【徐曰】梳之言導也。【揚雄&middot;長楊賦】頭蓬不暇梳。《文選》作梳，《漢書》作疏。</p>
<p>又【博雅】經梳謂之枃。【廣韻】櫛也。【釋名】梳，言其齒疏也。【唐書&middot;吳兢傳】朝有諷諫，猶髮之有梳。　【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2340C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣐌</span>、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/234DC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣓜</span>。</p>
