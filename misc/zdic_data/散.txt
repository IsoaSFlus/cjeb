data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 逍遥，懒散 <span class="encs">[free and unfettered]</span></p><p><span
