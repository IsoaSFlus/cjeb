                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>莫</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mò.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>不要：～哭。</li><li>没有，无：～大。～非。～名其妙（亦作“莫明其妙”）。</li><li>不，不能：～如。～逆。～须有。～衷一是（不能得出一致的结论）。爱～能助。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">漠</span>”，广大。</li><li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>莫</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄨˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">暮</span>”。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">莫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>莫</strong> <span class="dicpy">mò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示否定，相当于<span class="yhcs">“不”</span> <span class="encs">[not]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫相知。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫敢过其门。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故莫能知。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫能得。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:莫得(没有);爱莫能助;一筹莫展;莫可奈何(犹言无可奈何);莫之奈何(对它们无可奈何);莫或(没有);莫奈何(无可奈何)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示劝戒。不要;不可;不能 <span class="encs">[don’t]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慎莫留。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫相忘。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫愁前路无知己。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">高适</span><span class="smcs">《别董大》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫用众人。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:闲人莫入;莫怪(不要怪罪);莫教(休教，别让);莫得(休得，不得);莫道(休说，不要说)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表示揣测。或许;大约;莫非 <span class="encs">[perhaps;about;can it be that]</span>。如:莫不成(难道说);莫成(莫非，莫不是);莫是(莫非是;或许是);莫须(也许)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>莫</strong> <span class="dicpy">mò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span>
