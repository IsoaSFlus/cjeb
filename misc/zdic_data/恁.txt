                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">恁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>恁</strong> <span class="dicpy">n&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>(形声。从心，任声。本义:思;念) 同本义 <span class="encs">[think of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宜亦勤凭旅力，以充厥道。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《典引》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恁约(思忖，思量)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>恁</strong> <span class="dicpy">n&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">代</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;您&rdquo; <span class="encs">[you]</span> 多见于早期白话</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恁不去出力，息生教娘娘和番?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马志远</span><span class="smcs">《汉宫秋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恁儿(您儿子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 这样;那样 <span class="encs">[such;that]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君非我，任功名意气莫恁徘徊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《沁园春》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恁般(恁的。这样;如此);恁地(如此，这样);恁样(如此，这般);恁么(这样，如此)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 这，那 <span class="encs">[that]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">早知恁般么，恨当初不把雕鞍锁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《定风波》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:恁时(那时候)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 谁;保，什么 <span class="encs">[who;what]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:恁地(什么);恁人(谁，何人);恁么(怎么样;什么)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 怎，怎么 <span class="encs">[how]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却恁地教什么人在间壁吱吱的哭，搅俺弟兄们吃酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>恁</strong> <span class="dicpy">n&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
