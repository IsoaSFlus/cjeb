						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>瓢</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">piáo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piáo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄠˊ</span></p><ol><li>舀水或取东西的工具，多用对半剖开的匏瓜或木头制成：～泼大雨。</li><li>形状像瓢的：～虫。～儿菜。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>瓢</strong> <span class="dicpy">piáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 瓠的一种。也称<span class="yhcs">“葫芦”</span> <span class="encs">[gourd;calabash]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瓢，蠡也。从瓠省，票声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瓢，瓠勺也。——<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一瓢饮。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:瓢冠(瓜瓢形的僧帽);瓢堂(简陋的堂室)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用葫芦干壳做成的勺 <span class="encs">[gourd ladle]</span>。如:瓢杓(剖开葫芦制成的酒器);瓢壶(盛酒的器具);瓢把子(江湖黑话。土匪头子);瓢箪(饮食只有一瓢水，一篮饭。比喻清寒穷困);瓢勺(把老熟的葫芦剖成两半所做成的勺子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 量词。用以量水、酒 <span class="encs">[gourd ladle]</span>。如:一瓢水</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/瓢泼">瓢泼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/瓢泼大雨">瓢泼大雨</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/瓢儿菜">瓢儿菜</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
