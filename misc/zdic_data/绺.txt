                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>绺</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">綹</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">liǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄡˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>量词，指一束理顺了的丝，线、须、发等：一～青丝。</li>
	<li>衣服因下垂而起直皱：打～儿。</li>
	<li>丝缕的组合物。</li>
</ol>
