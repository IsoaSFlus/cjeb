                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>镬</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鑊</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huò.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>锅：～盖。</li><li>古代的大锅：鼎～（常作为烹人的刑具）。～亨（“<span class="diczx3">亨</span>”，同“<span class="diczx3">烹</span>”）。汤～（古代酷刑，把人投入盛着沸水的锅中煮死）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">镬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>镬</strong></p><p>鑊 <span class="dicpy">huò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形如大盆，用以煮食物的铁器 <span class="encs">[caldron]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">羊镬豕镬。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·少牢礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">省牲镬。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“烹饪器也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌共鼎镬。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·亨人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“所以煮肉及鱼腊之器。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尝一脔肉，知一镬之味。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说山》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“无足曰镬。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一镬之味。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:镬煮(放在镬里烹煮)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指烹人的刑具 <span class="encs">[big caldron used in ancient China as a punishment of boiling criminals alive]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">有凿颠抽胁镬亨之刑。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·刑法志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:镬亨(古代的一种酷刑。把人放在镬中烹煮。亨即烹)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 锅子 <span class="encs">[pot]</span>。如:镬釜(大锅);镬灶(锅灶);镬子(锅子)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=金" target="_blank">金</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/944A.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】胡郭切【集韻】【韻會】黃郭切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音穫。【說文】鐫也。从金蒦聲。【廣韻】鼎鑊。【增韻】釜屬。【周禮·天官·亨人】掌共鼎鑊。【註】鑊，所以煑肉及魚腊之器。【前漢·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】有鑿顚抽脅鑊亨之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>。【師古註】鼎大而無足曰鑊，以<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C4D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩱍</span>人也。</p><p>又【韻補】叶胡麥切，音獲。【柳宗元·懲咎賦】進與退吾無歸兮，甘脂潤兮鼎鑊。幸皇鑒之明宥兮，纍郡印而南適。</p><p>
