						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>凶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>不幸的，不吉祥的：吉～。～信。</li><li>庄稼收成不好：～年饥岁。</li><li>恶：～暴。～恶。～顽。～相（<span class="dicpy">xiàng </span>）。～神恶煞。</li><li>关于杀伤的：行（<span class="dicpy">xíng </span>）～。帮～。</li><li>厉害，过甚：雨～风狂。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>凶</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。小篆字形，<span class="yhcs">“凵”</span>象地陷形，读 <span class="dicpy">kǎn</span>。<span class="yhcs">“乂”</span>表示这里可陷人。<span class="yhcs">“兇”</span>是会意字，从儿(人)，在凶下。本义:不吉利)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[inauspicious;ominous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凶，恶也。象地穿交陷其中也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凶，咎也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吉凶者，言乎其得也。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一曰凶短折。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日月告凶。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·十月之交》</span>。笺:<span class="yhcs">“告天下以凶亡之徵也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="yhcs">“此孰吉孰凶?”</span>——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·卜居》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:凶兆(不祥的预兆);凶宿(凶星);吉凶未卜;凶星(不吉祥的星)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 凶恶可怕 <span class="encs">[ferocious;fierce]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">作凶事，为凶人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顽凶不用。——<span class="smcs">《史记·五帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">向高坐者，故是凶物。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·赏誉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">除奸凶。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:凶人(凶恶之人);凶凶(凶恶的样子);凶竖(凶恶的小人);凶焰(凶恶的气焰);凶豪(凶恶强横);凶惨(凶恶残酷)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 闹饥荒的，歉收的 <span class="encs">[crop failure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三谷不收谓之凶。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·七患》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大札大凶。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司乐》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凶荒杀礼。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·掌客》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">河内凶，则移其民于河东。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凶年免于死。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:凶穰(歉岁与丰年);凶馑(灾荒、饥馑);凶乱(灾荒祸乱);凶俭(灾荒);凶歉(歉收，灾荒);凶灾(凶年灾荒)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 与死人有关，丧葬 <span class="encs">[funeral]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乡共吉凶礼乐之器。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·乡师》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“凶器，若族丧器者也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:凶耗(死讯);凶肆(出售丧葬用品的商铺);凶具(棺材);凶疚(丧事的悲痛)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 厉害 <span
