                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">肌</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>肌</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 人或动物体内附着在骨头上或构成内脏的柔软物质，由许多纤维组成（通称“肌肉”）：～肤。～理。心～。随意～。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> muscle tissue; meat on bones</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Fruchtfleisch, (lebendes)​ Fleisch  (S)​,Muskel  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> muscle,chair</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>肌</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，几声。本义:肌肉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(先秦时期，<span class="yhcs">“肌”</span>表示人的肉;<span class="yhcs">“肉”</span>表示禽兽的肉) <span class="encs">[muscle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肌，肉也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度制，肌肤血气之情也。——<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">病在肌肤。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃割皮解肌，诀脉结筋。——<span class="smcs">《史记·扁鹊列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:肌肥骨重(形容身躯壮实魁悟);肌肌(肌肉强直的样子);肌肪(肌肤);肌革(肌肤，皮肉);肌骨(肌肉与骨骼)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申指皮肤 <span class="encs">[skin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肌理细腻骨肉匀。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《丽人行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一肌一容。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肌肤之所浸渍。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:肌衄(皮肤出血)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌肤">肌肤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌腹">肌腹</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌隔">肌隔</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌腱">肌腱</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌觉">肌觉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌肉">肌肉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌体">肌体</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌质">肌质</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/肌注">肌注</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
