                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疆</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>疆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄤˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>地域，领域，边界：～土。～宇（国土）。～界。～场（战场）。～陲（边境）。边～。海～。</li>
	<li>极限：万寿无～。</li>
	<li>划分界限：&ldquo;楚子～之&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>疆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;彊（强）&rdquo;，强大。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> boundary, border, frontier</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Grenze  (S)​,Jiang  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> limite,frontière</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>疆</strong></p>
<p>畺 <span class="dicpy">jiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。金文从土，彊声。彊，从弓，从畺( <span class="dicpy">jiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāng.mp3"></a></span> ，田界)。从&ldquo;弓&rdquo;，表示以弓记步，即以弓来丈量土地。本义:田界)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[field]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畺，界也。从畕、三，其界畫也。指事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疆埸有瓜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;信南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有掌疆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;夏官》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疆陇(田界);疆畛(地界;界限);疆畎(指田地，垄亩沟渠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为国界、边界 <span class="encs">[border]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出疆必请。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">固封疆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疆宇(疆界的四陲。引申为国土);疆吏(驻防边疆的官吏);疆事(边疆的事故。多指边界的冲突、争执而言);疆略(疆界;境界);疆圻(疆界)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 疆域 <span class="encs">[territory]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">制其畿疆而沟封之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禳于畺及郊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;肆师》</span>。注:&ldquo;五百里。&rdquo;按，王畿界也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疆易(疆土，领土);疆壤(地域);疆地(疆土，领土)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 止境;穷尽 <span class="encs">[limit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以逞无疆之欲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疆理(境界;界限);疆顿(停留不能前进)</span></p>
