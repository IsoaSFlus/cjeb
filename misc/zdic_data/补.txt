                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>补</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">補</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄨˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>把残破的东西加上材料修理完整：缝～。～葺。亡羊～牢。</li>
	<li>把缺少的东西充实起来或添上：弥～。～充。贴～。～习。滋～。</li>
	<li>益处：不无小～。于事无～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mend, patch, fix, repair, restore</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ausbessern, ergänzen, flicken  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> réparer,rapiécer,combler,suppléer,compléter,tonifier,fortifier,avantage</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">补</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>补</strong></p>
<p>補 <span class="dicpy">bǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，甫声。本义:补衣服)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mend;patch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">补，完衣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田赞衣补衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;顺说》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">补褐防寒岁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《村居卧病》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:补衣;补袜;补衬(打补丁，衬里子);补衣(打过补丁的衣服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 修补;整修破旧的东西 <span class="encs">[repair]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牵萝补茅屋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《佳人》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修桥补路;补天济世(修补天道，救济世人);补天(传说古代之时，苍天西北角有破缺之处，于是女娲氏炼石以补苍天，使无缺损;比喻挽回时运)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 补充 <span class="encs">[add;fill;replenish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿令得补黑衣之数。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:填补(补足空缺或缺欠);弥补(把不足的部分填足)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 弥补 <span class="encs">[fill;make up for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维仲山甫补之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;烝民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必能裨补阙漏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">稀豁之处，锄而补之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《齐民要术&middot;种谷》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:补刖(弥补被刖的脚跟。比喻矫正缺失，使归真返朴);补拙(弥补愚钝);补苴(弥补缺漏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 济助 <span class="encs">[help]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收孤寡，补贫穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:补益(裨补助益);补报(增补);补裨(增益补阙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 补养;滋补 <span class="encs">[nourish; take a tonic]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">静然可以补病。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;外物》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:补理(滋补调理);补导(采补和导引。道家的养生术)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 官有缺位，选员补充 <span class="encs">[fill a vacancy]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:补外(京官调外地就职);补官(补授官职)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>补</strong></p>
<p>補 <span class="dicpy">bǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/88DC.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2248F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢒏</span>【唐韻】【正韻】博古切【集韻】【韻會】彼五切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音圃。【說文】完衣也。【急就篇註】修破謂之補。【詩&middot;大雅】袞職有闕，惟仲山甫補之。</p>
<p>又助也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;小行人】若國札䘮，則令賻補之。</p>
<p>又益也。【前漢&middot;董仲舒傳】凡所爲屑屑，夙興夜寐，務法上古者，又將無補與。</p>
<p>又數也。【韻會】十兆曰經，十經曰垓，十垓曰補。</p>
<p>又邑名。【鄭語】鄢、蔽、補、丹、依、㽥、歷、莘，君之土也。【註】八邑也。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】補氏。【正字通】唐中常侍補眞。㽥字比照考証蔽字條改正。</p>
