                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>椎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhuī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄟˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>构成高等动物背部中央骨柱的短骨：～骨。脊～。颈～。胸～。尾～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>椎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨㄟˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>敲打东西的器具：铁～。木～。鼓～。</li>
	<li>敲打，用椎打击：～鼓。～杀。～心泣血（形容极度悲痛的样子）。</li>
	<li>愚钝，朴实：～鲁。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">椎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>椎</strong> <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuī.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:槌子，敲击的器具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 捶击的工具。后亦为兵器 <span class="encs">[mallet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">椎，所以击也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">椎，棒椎也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杼上终葵首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;玉人》</span>。疏:&ldquo;终葵，椎也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:椎拍(用椎拍击);椎斧(椎和斧);椎凿(槌子和凿子);椎锻(锤子和打铁用的砧石);椎额(额头突出如椎);椎塘(臼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 椎髻。盘在头顶上的一种椎形高髻 <span class="encs">[mallet-like chignon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:椎髫(满族人留的发辫);椎结(将头发编束成锥形的髻);椎头(椎形发式)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>椎</strong> <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用椎打击 <span class="encs">[beat with mallet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">椎，击也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五日一椎牛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;张释之冯唐传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">椎</span><span class="diczx2">秦始皇</span><span class="diczx2">博浪</span>之中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;冯衍传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:椎楚(杖刑);椎牛(击杀牛);椎坐(捶击坐具);椎秦(椎击秦皇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指重力撞击 <span class="encs">[beat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:椎冰(砸冰);椎破(击破;砸破);椎胸跌足(椎胸顿足)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 杀 <span class="encs">[kill]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:椎埋(杀人而加以掩埋);椎埋穿掘(杀人埋尸，发冢盗墓)</span></p>
