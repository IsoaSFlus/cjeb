                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">颖</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>颖</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">穎</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>禾的末端，植物学上指某些禾本科植物小穗基部的苞片：～果。</li>	<li>东西末端的尖锐部分：锋～。</li>	<li>才能出众：聪～。～悟。～慧。～异。新～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rice tassel; sharp point; clever</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> intelligent</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">颖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>颖</strong></p>
<p>穎、頴 <span class="dicpy">yǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾，顷声。从禾与农作物有关。本义:谷穗。亦称&ldquo;颖片&rdquo;。指禾本科植物小穗基部的二枚苞片)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[glume]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颖，禾末也。从禾，顷声。谓贯于穗及近于穗之芒秒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禾穗谓之颖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">异亩同颖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;归禾序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">实颖实贾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;生民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嘉禾重颖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">应贞</span><span class="smcs">《晋武帝华林园林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:颖果(干果的一种，种皮和果皮合而为一，里面只有一粒种子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 锥芒 <span class="encs">[awl]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃颖脱而出，非特其末见而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:颖脱(将锥放在囊中，终将全部脱出。比喻能充分显露内在的才能);短颖羊毫笔</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>颖</strong></p>
<p>穎 <span class="dicpy">yǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 聪敏 <span class="encs">[intelligent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灵运幼便颖悟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南史&middot;谢灵运传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君性颖异。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">邓文原</span><span class="smcs">《苏府君墓表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:颖拔绝伦(聪敏过人，找不出第二个人);颖秀(聪慧出众);颖哲(聪慧)</span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“颖”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/穎">穎</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下" target="_blank">午集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下&amp;bs=禾" target="_blank">禾</a>】
                              穎·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7A4E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】餘頃切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】庾頃切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音潁。【玉篇】禾末也。【詩&middot;大雅】實穎實栗。【傳】穎，垂穎也。【疏】穎是禾穗之挺，言其穗重而穎垂也。【書序】唐叔得禾，異畝同穎。【傳】穎，穗也。禾各生一莖，而合爲一穗。</p><p>又鐶也。【禮&middot;少儀】刀卻刃授穎。</p><p>又錐鋩也。【史記&middot;平原君傳】如錐之處囊中，乃穎脫而出。</p><p>又警枕也。【禮&middot;少議】穎杖琴瑟。【註】穎，警枕也。【疏】穎是穎發之義，故爲警枕。【釋文】穎，京領反。</p><p>又【韻會】筆頭也。韓愈有毛穎傳。</p><p>又【正字通】士才能拔類者亦曰穎。【陸機&middot;文賦】拔尤取穎。</p><p>又姓。【左傳&middot;隱元年】穎考叔爲潁谷封人。後遂以爲氏。【急就篇】亦作潁。○按穎當依《說文》隷禾部，《字彙》《正字通》譌入頁部，今改正。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">穎</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“颖”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/穎">穎</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=禾">禾</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7A4E.svg">                          <p>禾末也。从禾頃聲。《詩》曰：&ldquo;禾穎穟穟。&rdquo;余頃切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7A4E_4559.svg" /><span class="swbf">(穎)</span><span class="ywyy">禾末也。</span><span class="swdz">穎之言莖也，頸也，近於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25769.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥝩</span>及貫於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25769.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥝩</span>者皆是也。大雅：實穎實栗。毛曰：穎，垂穎也。禹貢鄭注曰：百里賦入總。謂入刈禾也。二百里銍，銍，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃔</span>去稿也。三百里秸。秸，又去穎也。四百里入粟，五百里入米者，遠彌輕也。禮器：稾鞂之設。鄭注：穗去實曰鞂。鞂與秸同物。鄭注尙書曰：去穎，謂用其<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25769.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥝩</span>也。注禮器曰：去實，謂用其穎也。史記曰：錐處囊中，穎脫而出，非特其末見而巳。少儀：刀卻刃授穎。是則穎在錐則卻於末，在刀則卻於刃，在禾則卻於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25769.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥝩</span>也。渾言之則穎爲禾末，析言之則禾芒乃爲秒。</span><span class="ywyy">从禾頃聲。</span><span class="swdz">余頃切。十一部。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：禾穎穟穟。</span><span class="swdz">大雅生民文。今詩作禾役。毛曰：役，列也。玉裁按：役者，穎之叚借字。古支耕合韵之理也。列者，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/257A5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥞥</span>之叚借，禾穰也。此穎通穰言之，下章之穎則專謂垂者。</span></p>                          </div>
