                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">常</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>常</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄤˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>长久，固定不变：～数。～量（亦称&ldquo;恒量&rdquo;）。～项。～任。～年。～驻。～住。～备不懈。</li>	<li>副词，经常，时时（叠），不只一次：～～。～客。时～。经～。</li>	<li>普通的，一般的：～识。～务。～规。～情。～人。平～。反～。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> common, normal, frequent, regular</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> allgemein, oft</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> souvent,fréquemment,ordinaire,commun,constant,invariable</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">常</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>常</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从巾，尚声。本义:旗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[flag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纪于太常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;君牙》</span>。传:&ldquo;王之旌旗画日月曰太常。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载是常服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;六月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 裙子 <span class="encs">[skirt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">叔旦</span>泣涕于常，悲不能对。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《逸周书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常，下裙也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:&ldquo;常裳二字，经传截然分开，并不通借。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 规则，规律 <span class="encs">[routine;law]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陈常于时夏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;思文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天行有常，不为</span><span class="diczx2">尧</span>存，不为 <span class="diczx2">桀</span>亡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:常古(旧法，常法);常故(旧例;常例);常式(法式)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 封建社会宣扬的所谓恒久不变的人与人之间的关系准则，即伦常;纲常 <span class="encs">[feudal order of importance or seniority in human relationships]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:常伦(伦常);三纲五常</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>常</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p>古代长度单位。一丈六尺为常 <span class="encs">[sixteen feet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寻舒两肱也，倍寻谓之常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广度》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蒲筵常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;公食礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;丈六尺曰常，半常曰寻。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酋予常有四尺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">争寻常以尽其民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不过墨丈寻常之间。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寻常之沟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;庚桑楚》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>常</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 恒久;长久不变 <span class="encs">[constant;fixed;invariable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常，质也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未变常也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易象下传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;恒也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>邦是常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂&middot;閟宫》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天命靡常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;文王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日月以为常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;越语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故官无常贵，而民无终贱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚贤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人无常师。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:常德(始终不变的品德);常心(固执不变的心);常安(永久的平安)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一般;普通;正常 <span class="encs">[ordinary;common;normal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其有常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;鸨羽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则维其常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顾人之常情，由俭入奢易，由奢入俭难。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且欲与常马等不可得。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白乐天游大林寺诗云:&ldquo;人间四月芳菲尽，山寺桃花始盛开。&rdquo;盖常理也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:常时(平时;平日);常言(平常的话;普通的话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 一定 <span class="encs">[fixed;established]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:常刑(一定的刑罚);常序(一定的次序)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>常</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 经常，常常 <span class="encs">[frequently;often]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常生常化者，无时不生，无时不化。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汉王</span>急，推堕 <span class="diczx2">孝惠</span>、 <span class="diczx2">鲁元</span>车下; <span class="diczx2">滕公</span>常下，收载之，如是者三。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">良因异之，常习诵之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;留侯世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千里马常有，而</span><span class="diczx2">伯乐</span>不常有。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:常见面;常则是(经常是，总是);常时(时常，常常);常参官(日常参朝的官吏);常惺惺(佛教语。指头脑经常或长久保持清醒);常经(经常);常赁(经常受雇于人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;尝&rdquo;。曾经 <span class="encs">[have already]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子常语诸梁也曰:凡事若小若大，寡不道以权成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;人间世》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主父常游于此。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫日、月之有蚀，风雨之不时，怪星之常见，是无世而不常有之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">高祖</span>为亭长时，常告归之田。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">项伯</span>常杀人，从良匿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;留侯世家》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
