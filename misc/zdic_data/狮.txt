                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>狮</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">獅</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˉ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 哺乳动物，雄的脖子上有长鬣，多产于非洲及印度西北部（通常称“狮子”；古亦作“师子”）：～子舞。～子搏兔（喻对小事情也拿出全部力量，不轻视）。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>狮</strong></p><p>獅 <span class="dicpy">shī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 犬生二子称<span class="yhcs">“狮”</span> <span class="encs">[the dog gives birth to two children]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狮，犬生二子。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 猛兽名 <span class="encs">[lion]</span>主要是夜行性的猫科大型食肉类一种哺乳动物，现在大部分产于非洲的空旷或多岩石的地区，也产于亚洲南部，身体黄褐色，有带丛毛的尾，雄性的有黑色或暗褐色的粗糙鬣毛。如:狮儿(喻雄视一世的俊杰);狮子口(狱门);狮子座(狮子坐。佛坐之处);狮子灯(狮子状的花灯)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/狮虎当道">狮虎当道</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/狮舞">狮舞</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/狮子鼻">狮子鼻</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/狮子搏兔">狮子搏兔</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/狮子大开口">狮子大开口</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/狮子头">狮子头</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/狮子舞">狮子舞</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
