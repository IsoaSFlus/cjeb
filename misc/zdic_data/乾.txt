                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>乾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>八卦之一，代表天：～坤（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">坤</span>&rdquo;，代表地）。</li>	<li>旧时称男性的：～造。～宅。</li></ol><p>● <strong>乾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄢˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>见&ldquo;<a href="/hans/干">干</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dry; first hexagram; warming principle of the sun, penetrating and fertilizing, heavenly generative principle (male)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> trocken  (Adj)​,Erstes der Acht Trigramme im Bagua des I Ging, "Trockenheit&quot</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> homme,mâle,masculin,le souverain,l'empereur,l'un des huit trigrammes représentant le ciel,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乾</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>(俗作乹。形声。从乙，倝声( <span class="dicpy">g&agrave;n</span>)。乙，象植物屈曲生长的样子。本义:上出) 同本义 <span class="encs">[go up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乾，上出也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:此乾字之本义也。自有文字以后，乃用为卦名，而 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>释之曰健也。健之义生于上出，上出为乾，下注则为湿，故乾与湿相对，俗别其音，古无是也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乾</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 八卦的首卦;天 <span class="encs">[the first of the Eight Diagrams;sky]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">达于上者谓之乾。凡上达者莫若气，天为积气，故乾为天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乾，天也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乾，阳物也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乾刚坤柔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;杂卦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乾为天、为圜、为君、为父、为玉、为金、为寒、为冰、为大赤、为良马、为老马、为瘠马、为驳马、为木果。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乾方(天方);乾元(天);乾施(上天的施予);乾首(天的高处);乾刚(天道刚健);乾象(天象);乾启(上天的启示);乾钧(天钧，北极之地，泛指北方);乾道(天道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 指君主 <span class="encs">[monarch]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:乾化(帝王的教化);乾心(帝心);乾居(帝王);乾符(帝王受命于天的吉祥征兆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 太阳 <span class="encs">[sun]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:乾晖(阳光);乾曜(太阳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 代表西北方 <span class="encs">[north-west]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:乾冈(西北方位的山冈);乾风(西北风);乾雷(西北方的响雷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 代表男性 <span class="encs">[man]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:旧时婚姻中称男方为&ldquo;乾造&rdquo;，男家为&ldquo;乾宅&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">gān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
