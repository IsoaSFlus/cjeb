<p>● <strong>滋</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>生出，长：～生。～芽。～事。～扰（骚扰生事）。潜～暗长。</li>

	<li>汁液，润泽：～润。</li>
	<li>味道：～味。</li>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">滋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>滋</strong> <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 滋水 <span class="encs">[Zi River]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滋水出</span><span class="diczx2">牛饮山</span><span class="diczx2">白陉谷</span>，东入 <span class="diczx2">呼沱</span>。从水，兹声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一名兹水，今名磁河。源出河北省阜平县西部南坨山东，今为潴龙河支流</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 浆，液汁 <span class="encs">[thick liquid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">墨井盐池，玄滋素液。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">流光驿驿，甘滋泥泥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">邹阳</span><span class="smcs">《酒赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 滋味 <span class="encs">[taste]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丧有疾，食肉饮酒，必有草木之滋焉，以为姜桂之谓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>滋</strong> <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;孳&rdquo;。滋生;繁殖 <span class="encs">[multiply;grow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滋，益也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无使滋蔓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;益也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草木庳小不滋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;明理》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事滋无成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树德务滋，除恶务本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;泰誓下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:滋疑(引起怀疑);滋乳(滋生繁衍);滋漫(滋生蔓延);滋演(滋生演变);滋蕃(滋生繁育);滋茂(植物生长繁茂);滋硕(竹长茂盛);滋荣(生长繁茂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 滋补。供给身体以养分;补养 <span class="encs">[tonic]</span>。如:滋阴(滋补阴虚);滋膳(补养的膳食);滋助(滋补)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 栽种 <span class="encs">[plant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">播江离与滋菊兮，愿春日以为糗芳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:滋培(栽培;养育);滋植(培植)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 浸染 <span class="encs">[permeate]</span>。如:滋沥(汁液渗滴);滋液(渗透的汁液);滋穴(喷涌泉水的洞穴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 增长 <span class="encs">[increase]</span>(今国之疵，民之病，有滋而无损焉，乌所谓言之效邪?)&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《上田正言书二》</span></p>
