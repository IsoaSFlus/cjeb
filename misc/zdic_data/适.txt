						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>适</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">適</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>切合，相合：～当。～龄。～销。～度（<span class="dicpy">d&ugrave;</span>）（程度适当）。～应（适合客观条件或需要）。</li>
	<li>舒服：～意。舒～。</li>
	<li>刚巧：～中。～值（恰好遇到）。～可而止。</li>
	<li>刚才，方才：～才（刚才）。～间。</li>
	<li>往，归向：无所～从。</li>
	<li>旧称女子出嫁：～人。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">适</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>适</strong></p>
<p>適 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，啻( <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span>)声。本义:往，到)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[go]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">適，之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>曰:&ldquo;往自发动言之，适自所到言之。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十一月，</span><span class="diczx2">沛公</span>引兵之 <span class="diczx2">薛</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辍耕之垄上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">适，往也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民不适攸居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爰其适归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;四月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">适彼乐土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;硕鼠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使适四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;小行人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以适父母舅姑之所。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逝将去女，适彼乐国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;硕鼠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余自齐安舟行适临汝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:无所适从;何适;适秦;适远(到远处去);适野(前往野外)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 归向 <span class="encs">[turn towards]</span></p>
