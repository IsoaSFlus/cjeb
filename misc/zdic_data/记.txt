                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>记</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">記</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>把印象保持在脑子里：～忆。～取。～性。博闻强～。</li>
	<li>把事物写下来：～录。～功。～者。</li>
	<li>记载事物的书册或文字：游～。日～。大事～。</li>
	<li>符号，标识（<span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span>）：印～。标～。～号。</li>
	<li>古时的一种公文：奏～。笺～。</li>
	<li>皮肤上的生下来就有的深色斑：胎～。</li>
	<li>量词，指打一下：给他一～耳光。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">记</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>记</strong></p>
<p>記 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，己声。本义:记住)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[remember;bear in mind]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">记，疏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">记，识也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">记有成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">记间之学。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡所经履，莫不暗记。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;应奉传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常记溪亭日暮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《如梦令》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾记</span><span class="diczx2">天圣</span>中。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">望中犹记。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐&middot;京口北固亭怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:记混了;记错了;切记;牢记;铭记;记不清;记省(回忆，记忆);记心(记在心上，常用于叮嘱);记念(挂心;惦念);记忆(记在心里)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 记载，记录 <span class="encs">[record;write down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">记，书也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宫之</span>奇果谏，记曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;僖公二年》</span>。注:&ldquo;史记也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受记考事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张敞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">属予作文以记之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">记之而去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予在患难中，间以诗记所遭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《&lt;指南录&gt; 后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">览则省记。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span>记之。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:摘记(摘要记录);笔记(用笔记录);手记(亲手记录);记言(记录言论)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>记</strong></p>
<p>記 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 记载事物的书册或文章 <span class="encs">[note]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:记书(传记及诸家之书);游记;日记;杂记;摘记</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 按时间顺序记述历史史实或事件 <span class="encs">[chronicle]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:大事记;史记;记传(历史传记);记牒(史册;文字记录);记府(古代国家收藏文书史策的府署)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 标志，记号 <span class="encs">[mark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仓廒内自有官司封记。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:标记;以红色为记;记色(凭据;证据)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 胎记 <span class="encs">[birthmark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生那儿时，脊背下有一搭红记。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:他身上有块记</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古时的一种公文 <span class="encs">[accounts]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:奏记;残记。又指记述或解释典章制度的文字。如:十三经中的<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记》</span>和<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 印章 <span class="encs">[seal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">正其名，请铸铜记给之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史&middot;职官志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:戳记;图记;钤记;记室(官名。掌管章表书记文檄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 记叙文，一种以叙事为主的文体 <span class="encs">[a written narration]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:<span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span>;<span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              記·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：3</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A18.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>居吏切，音冀。【說文】疏也。【徐曰】謂一一分別記之也。【博雅】識也。【釋名】紀也。【玉篇】錄也。【廣韻】志也。【書&middot;益稷】撻以記之。【傳】使記識其過也。【禮&middot;王制】太史典禮執<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>記。【註】國有禮事，則豫執<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>策，記載所當行之禮儀。【唐書&middot;于志寧傳】左有記言之史，右有記事之官。【關尹子&middot;五鑑篇】昔遊再到，記憶宛然。</p>
<p>又【博雅】書也。【前漢&middot;蕭望之傳】待詔鄭朋奏記于蕭望之。【註】記，書也。奏記自朋始。【文心雕龍】後漢始有公府奏記，記之言志，進己志也。</p>
<p>又【正字通】文符也。【後漢&middot;鍾離意傳】少爲郡督郵，時部縣亭長有受人酒禮者，府下記案考之，意封還記，太守甚賢之。</p>
<p>又官名。【後漢&middot;百官志】記室令史，主上表章報書記。</p>
<p>又與其通。【字彙補】助語辭。【詩&middot;王風】彼其之子。【箋】其或作記。或作己。讀聲相似。</p>
<p>又叶苟起切，音几。【崔琰&middot;述初賦】望高密以亟征，戾衡門而造止。覿游夏之峩峩，聽大猷之篇記。一說本作紀，紀與止叶，譌作記。</p>
