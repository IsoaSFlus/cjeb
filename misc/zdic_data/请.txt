                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>请</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">請</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǐng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>求：～求。～示。～假（<span class="dicpy">jiǎ </span>）。～命。～战。～教（<span class="dicpy">jiào </span>）。～愿。～君入瓮。～缨（喻请战杀敌）。</li><li>敬辞，用于希望对方做某事：～进。～坐。～安。～便。</li><li>延聘、邀、约人来：～客。～柬。邀～。</li><li>谒见、会见：“造～诸公，不避寒暑”。</li></ol>

class="cino">(8)</span> 召;邀请 <span class="encs">[invite;engage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">战则请从。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>
