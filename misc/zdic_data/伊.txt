                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>伊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>彼，他，她：～说。～人（那个人，多指女性）。</li>    <li>文言助词：下车～始。～谁之力？～于胡底（到什么地步为止，不堪设想的意思）。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">伊</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>伊</strong> <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从尹。尹，治理。合起来指伊尹，殷治理天下者。后假借为那)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示远指，相当于<span class="yhcs">“那”</span> <span class="encs">[that]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所谓伊人，在水一方。——<span class="smcs">《诗·秦风·蒹葭》</span>。笺:<span class="yhcs">“伊，当作繄，犹是也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示第三人称，相当于<span class="yhcs">“她”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“他”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“彼”</span> <span class="encs">[he;she]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">薛蟠</span>因伊倔强，将酒照脸泼去。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">江</span>家我顾伊， <span class="diczx2">庾</span>家伊顾我。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾见</span><span class="diczx2">张</span>时，伊已六十。——<span class="smcs">《南史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:伊行(她那里;他们);伊曹(彼辈，他们);伊俦(彼辈，其类);伊拉(方言。他们)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表示第二人称，相当于<span class="yhcs">“你”</span> <span class="encs">[you]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勿学汝兄，汝兄自不如伊。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我这里吐胆倾心说与伊，难道你不解其中意。——佚名<span class="smcs">《马陵道》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:伊咱(你);伊行(你这里;你们);伊家(你)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>伊</strong> <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示判断，常与<span class="yhcs">“匪”</span>连用，相当于<span class="yhcs">“却是”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“即是”</span> <span class="encs">[be]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪莪伊蒿。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·蓼莪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昔卫鞅因景监以见，有识知其不终。今得臣举者，匪荣伊辱。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:匪朝伊暮;匪朝伊夕;匪荣伊辱;匪愚伊耄</p><p>◎ <strong>伊</strong> <span class="dicpy">yī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><语></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 语助词。用于句中，无义</p><p><span class="diczx1">旨酒既清，嘉荐伊脯。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 发语词，无义。如:伊何(为何，为什么);伊谁(谁，何人)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“繄”</span>。是 <span class="encs">[yes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可畏也，伊可怀也。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·东山》</span>。笺:<span
