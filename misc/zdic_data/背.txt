                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>背</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人体后面从肩到腰的部分：～脊。～包。～影。</li>
	<li>物体的后面或反面：～面。刀～。～后。～景。</li>
	<li>用背部对着，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">向</span>&rdquo;相对：～光。人心向～。</li>
	<li>向相反的方向：～地性（植物向上生长的性质）。～道而驰。</li>
	<li>避开，离开：～地。～井离乡。</li>
	<li>凭记忆读出：～书。～诵。～台词。</li>
	<li>违反：违～。～离。～信弃义。</li>
	<li>不顺：～运。～兴（<span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>偏僻：～静。</li>
	<li>听觉不灵：耳～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>背</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bēi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄟˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人用背驮（<span class="dicpy">tu&oacute;</span>）东西，引申为负担：～负。～包。～黑锅（喻受冤枉代人受过）。～包袱（喻有沉重的思想负担或经济负担）。</li>
</ol>

<p>◎ <strong>背</strong></p>
<p>揹 <span class="dicpy">bēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bēi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 人用脊背驮 <span class="encs">[东西]</span> <span class="encs">[have on one&#39;s shoulder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">早背胡霜过戍楼，又随寒日下汀州。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">罗邺</span><span class="smcs">《雁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:背粮食;背头(男子头发由鬓角起都向后梳的发式);背锅(方言。驼背)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 负担;承受 <span class="encs">[bear]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:背了一身债;背利(负担很重的或长期的利息);背罪(承担罪责)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[back of the body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">背，脊也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">背者，胸中之府。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;脉要精微论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牛足出背上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;梁孝王世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然徒步则汗出浃背。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左手抚</span><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>直背。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不呼则杖其背。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:芒刺在背;背袋(背在背上的口袋);背花(旧时刑罚，即用木棒打背脊，伤破处称背花);背子(半袖上衣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 事物的后面或反面 <span class="encs">[back of an object]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其船背稍夷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即以斧破其背，得剑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剑在其背</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乘在浪背上前进;刀背;锯背;斧背</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 正北堂屋 <span class="encs">[north hall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焉得谖草(萱草)，言树(种)之背。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;伯兮》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/80CC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】補妹切【正韻】邦昧切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音輩。【說文】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/267DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦟝</span>也。从肉北聲。【韻會】身北曰背。【玉篇】背脊。【正字通】身之隂也。【釋名】背，倍也，在後稱也。【易&middot;艮卦】艮其背。【註】背者，無見之物也。</p>
<p>又手背。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;弓人】合灂若背手文。</p>
<p>又壽也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】背，壽也。【詩&middot;大雅】黃耉台背。</p>
<p>又【魯頌】黃髮台背。【傳】台背，皆壽徵也。</p>
<p>又日旁氣也。【前漢&middot;天文志】暈適背穴。【註】孟康曰：皆日旁氣也。背形如背字也。如淳曰：凡氣向日爲抱，向外爲背。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】堂北曰背。【詩&middot;衞風】焉得諼草，言樹之背。【傳】背，北堂也，又人名。【穀梁傳&middot;成十年】衞侯之弟黑背，帥師侵鄭。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】蒲昧切【正韻】步昧切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音旆。【廣韻】弃背。【集韻】違也。【正韻】棄也，孤負也，反面也。【詩&middot;小雅】噂沓背憎。【書&middot;太甲】旣往背師保之訓。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】君爲秦吏，今欲背之。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】奴對切，音內。義同。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶音必。【詩&middot;大雅】民之罔極，職涼善背。叶下克力。　【正韻】亦作偝倍。</p>
