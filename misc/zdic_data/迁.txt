                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">迁</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>迁</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">遷</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄢˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>机关、住所等另换地点：～居。～移。～徙。</li>	<li>变动，转变：变～。～就（改变自己的意见，凑合别人）。～延（拖延）。～怒。事过境～。</li>	<li>贬谪，放逐：～谪（贬官远地）。～客（流迁或被贬谪到外地的官）。</li>	<li>古代称调动官职，一般指升职：升～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> move, shift, change; transfer; relocate a capital city</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> austauschen, tauschen ,erregen, verschieben</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> déplacer,changer</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>迁</strong></p>
<p>遷 <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵，&ldquo;辵&rdquo;表移动。形声字声旁有时有表意作用，此即一例。本义:向高处迁移)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ascend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁，登也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁，徙也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁，移也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既其女迁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;巷伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出自幽谷，迁于乔马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;伐木》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迁莺(指迁升飞翔的黄莺。喻登第)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 晋升或调动 <span class="encs">[promote]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝迁明德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;皇矣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁至栘中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁为太史令。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁我如振落叶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁</span><span class="diczx2">淳安</span>知县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迁于乔木(比喻升官);迁除(指官吏的升调委派);迁乔(官吏由低职升到高职位);迁封(加封爵位);迁官(晋升官爵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 徙居;搬动 <span class="encs">[move;migrate;change one&#39;s residence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二曰询国迁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;小司寇》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓徒都改邑也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毁其宗庙，迁其重器。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">盘庚</span>之迁。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">战守迁皆不及施。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《&lt;指南录&gt;后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迁次(移居，迁移);迁幸(旧指天子迁居他处);迁神(移灵，移柩);迁海(从濒海之地向内地迁徙);迁祔(迁徙墓地);迁冗(迁徙逃散);迁京(迁都);迁座(迁移灵座);迁土(迁离乡土);迁宅(犹迁居);迁邻(迁居择邻)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 变更，变动 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有百世不迁之宗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记大传》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹变易也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饬令，则法不迁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;饬令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其迁极渐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:事过境迁;迁正(变异历制);迁易(改变);迁染(人的性情被习俗沾染而有所改变);迁兰变鲍(在无形中渐渐受影响而同化);迁日(更改日期)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 死亡 <span class="encs">[die]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:迁神(死亡);迁形(死亡);迁徂(离去);迁殂(犹去世);迁神(僧人逝世);迁逝(犹死去;离去)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 流放;放逐 <span class="encs">[banish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怒而迁之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及夫大逆不道，然后掩捕而迁之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《封建论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迁放(流放);迁逐(贬斥放逐);迁窜(迁逐流放)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 贬谪，降职 <span class="encs">[banish from the court]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迁客骚人，多会于此。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迁斥(贬斥，放逐);迁臣(指贬斥远地的官吏);迁削(谪降削职);迁凑(迁就凑合);迁寺(谪降削职)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 离散 <span class="encs">[disperse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">成而不迁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;离散也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迁逃(逃走;逃离);迁逝(消逝，流失);迁摇(离散动摇)</span></p>
