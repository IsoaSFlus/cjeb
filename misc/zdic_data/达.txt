                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>达</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">達</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dá.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄚˊ</span></p><ol><li>通：四通八～。～德（通行天下的美德）。～人。～士（达人）。</li><li>通晓：洞～。练～。</li><li>遍，全面：～观（对不如意的事情看得开，不计个人的得失）。</li><li>到：到～。抵～。通宵～旦。</li><li>实现：目的已～。</li><li>传出来：传（<span class="dicpy">chuán</span>）～。转（<span class="dicpy">zhuǎn </span>）～。</li><li>得到显要的地位：显～。～官贵人。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>达</strong></p><p>達 <span class="dicpy">dá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，羍声。本义:道路畅通)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。泛指畅通 <span class="encs">[lead to;go to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">达，行不相遇也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">达，通也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左右达为夹室，所以相隔。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡人其达王于甬句东。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">达四聪。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">达于河。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">七曰达吏。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">下达。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下达道。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">唯恐道途显晦，不相通达。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屋极有窗以达气。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:四通八达;达越(疏通瑟底之孔使其声迟重);达乡(相对而畅达的窗户)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 到达，通到 <span class="encs">[reach;achieve;attain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">凉州</span>之兵始达 <span class="diczx2">咸阳</span>。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">路旁一歧东上，乃昔所未至者，遂前趋直上，几达天都侧。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">万人凿盘石，无由达江浒。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《丁都护歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">指通</span><span class="diczx2">豫南</span>，达于 <span class="diczx2">汉阴</span>。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日与其徒置酒酣歌达曙。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span
