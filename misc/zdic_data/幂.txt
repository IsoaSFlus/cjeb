						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>幂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>覆盖东西的巾。</li><li>覆盖，遮盖。</li><li>数学上指一个数自乘若干次形式：～次（方次）。乘～（乘方）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cover-cloth, cover with cloth</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Potenz, Kraft  (S, Math)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> exposant (math.)​,puissance (algèbre)​</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>幂</strong></p><p>幂 <span class="dicpy">mì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从巾，冥声。本义:盖东西用的巾)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cloth cover]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大巾谓之幂。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅·广诂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幂人，掌共巾幂。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官·幂人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“共巾，可以覆物。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幂用锡若絺。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“幂，覆尊巾也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幂用疏布。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·既夕礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">簠有盖幂。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·公食大夫礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:幂首(古代妇女障面的一种头巾);幂人(<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>官名。掌共巾幂);幂篱(古代少数民族的一种头巾)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 数学名词。表示一个数自乘若干次的形式，如a自乘n次的幂为a n <span class="encs">[power]</span>。如:立方是一个数的三次幂</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 云南少数民族计算布帛的单位 <span class="encs">[Mi]</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>幂</strong> <span class="dicpy">mì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用布覆盖 <span class="encs">[to cover with cloth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祭祀，以疏布巾幂八尊。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:幂历(分布覆被的样子;弥漫笼罩的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 遮;蒙 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幂窗用纸。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《庐山草堂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“塓”</span>。涂刷 <span class="encs">[plaster]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">葺墙幂室，房庑杂袭。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/幂级数">幂级数</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
