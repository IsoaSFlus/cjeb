<p><span class="dicpy">m&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄠˊ</span></p>

<p><span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄠˋ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旄</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ancient flag with yak&#39;s tail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旄，幢也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。本用妈犛牛尾，注于旗之竿首，故曰旄。后又用羽，或兼用氂与羽焉。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">右秉白旄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;牧誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">建彼旄矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;出车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见羽旄之美。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">总光耀之采旄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乐师有旄舞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;旄人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上将拥旄西出征。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《轮台歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旄钺(本为旗帜和兵器。代指军权);旄麾(即旌麾。帅旗);旄象(饰以象牙的旌旗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 牦牛尾 <span class="encs">[yak&#39;s tail]</span>。古代常作饰物</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羽旄齿革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旄羽(牦牛尾和雉羽);旄狄(即旄羽);旄端(尾端)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 牦牛 <span class="encs">[yak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骍旄之盟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旄尘(牦牛和尘);旄毡(用牦牛毛制成的毛织品);旄牛(即牦牛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旄丘。前高后低的山丘 <span class="encs">[yak-like hill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旄丘之葛兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;旄丘》</span>。传:&ldquo;前高后下曰旄丘。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旄敦(旄丘与敦丘)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 星名 <span class="encs">[a kind of star]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:旄星(即旄头星);旄头(即昂星)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;髦&rdquo;。俊杰 <span class="encs">[outstanding person]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:旄俊(俊杰之士)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;毛&rdquo;。<span class="encs">[hair;feather;down;wool]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粪除其颠旄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;小匡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旄丘之葛兮，何诞之节兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;旄丘》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/65C4.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】莫袍切【集韻】【韻會】謨袍切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音毛。【說文】幢也。【書&middot;泰誓】右秉白旄以麾。【釋文】白旄，旄牛尾。【詩&middot;鄘風】孑孑干旄。【傳】注，旄於干者，大夫之旃也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;旄人註】旄，旄牛尾，舞者所持以指麾。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;邶風&middot;旄丘傳】前高後下曰旄丘。</p>
<p>又星名。【史記&middot;天官書】昴曰旄頭。【註】昴七星爲旄頭。</p>
<p>又騎名。【後漢&middot;光武紀】賜東海王旄頭。【註】秦文公時，梓樹化爲牛，以騎擊之，騎不勝，或墮地，髻解披髮，牛畏之，入水，故秦因是置旄頭騎。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>莫報切，音帽。麕毛㺜長也。</p>
<p>又與耄通。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;司刺】再赦曰老旄。【孟子】反其旄倪。</p>
<p>又【集韻】亡遇切，音務。嵍，或作旄。山名。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶蒙晡切【傅毅&middot;洛神賦】昆山美玉，濤海明珠，金銀璆琳，翠<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29989.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩦉</span>貂旄。</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C4_2498.svg" /><span class="swbf">(旄)</span><span class="ywyy">幢也。</span><span class="swdz">劉煕曰：幢，童也。其皃童童也。廣雅曰：幢謂之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2649B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦒛</span>。爾雅曰：翢，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/261E8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦇨</span>也。毛傳曰：翿者，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/261E8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦇨</span>也，翳也。羽部曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26487.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦒇</span>者，翳也，所以舞也。人部曰：儔者，翳也。按或用羽，或用犛牛尾，或兼用二者。翢儔翿實一字。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/261E8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦇨</span>俗作纛，亦卽<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26487.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦒇</span>字，爾雅、毛傳皆以今字釋古字耳。幢亦卽<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26487.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦒇</span>字，古<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/223DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢏚</span>聲、周聲與童聲轉移，如詩以調韵同，漢縣鮦陽讀如紂之比。其始衹有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26487.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦒇</span>字，繼乃有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/261E8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦇨</span>，繼乃有幢，皆後出，故許書不列<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/261E8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦇨</span>幢二篆。此釋旄必云幢，不云<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26487.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦒇</span>者，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26487.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦒇</span>嫌舞者所持。旄是旌旗之名。漢之羽葆幢，以犛牛尾爲之，如斗，在乘與左騑馬頭上。用此知古以犛牛尾注竿首，如斗童童然，故詩言干旄，言建旄，言設旄。有旄則亦有羽，羽或全或析。言旄不言羽者舉一以晐二。其字从㫃从毛，亦舉一以晐二也。以犛牛尾注旗竿，故謂此旗爲旄。因而謂犛牛尾曰旄，謂犛牛曰旄牛，名之相因者也。禹貢㒳言羽旄，周禮旄人、旄舞，皆謂犛牛尾曰旄也。</span><span class="ywyy">从㫃毛聲。</span><span class="swdz">舉形聲包會意。莫袍切。二部。</span></p>                          </div>
