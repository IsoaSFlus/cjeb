						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>杖</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>扶着走路的棍子：手～。拐～。</li><li>泛指棍棒：擀面～。禅～。</li><li>古代刑罚之一，用棍打：～脊。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">仗</span>”，恃，凭倚。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">杖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>杖</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，丈声。本义:执;持)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hold; grasp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖，持也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王左杖黄钺。——<span class="smcs">《书·牧誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖剑从之。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·韩信传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖斧。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·武帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:杖斧(持斧以为威权);杖楫(持杖);杖斧之臣(掌握兵权的武臣);杖策(执马鞭);杖节(执持旌节)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“仗”</span>。凭倚;依靠 <span class="encs">[rely on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖信以待</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>，不亦可乎?——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">近臣已下足杖矣。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李寻传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仗父兄之烈。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仗舟楫。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仗汝扶持。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:杖力(依靠武力);杖仁(依仗仁德);杖任(依靠，信任);杖威(凭恃权威);杖信(凭靠信义)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用棍子打;拷打 <span class="encs">[flog with a stick]</span>。自隋开皇新律始定为五刑(笞、杖、徒、流、死)之一。用大荆条、大竹板或棍棒抽击人的背、臀或腿部</p><p><span class="diczx1">尝受守杖。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖汝。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖之百。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予之杖。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宰严限追比，旬余，杖至百，两股间脓血流离。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖其背。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖杀其父</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:杖楚(以棍棒拷打);自杖三十;杖牛(打春牛);杖徒(五刑之。用大竹板、棍棒、荆条抽打犯人背部);杖钱(给施杖刑衙役的贿赂钱);杖刑(古代刑罚之一。用荆条拷打犯人);杖治(以杖刑治罪)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 拄着 <span class="encs">[support]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">必操几杖以从之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖名齐其心。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·丧服》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杖汉节牧羊。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·苏武传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遥望老子杖藜而来。——<span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五十杖于家，六十杖于乡，七十杖于国，八十杖于朝。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>杖</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
