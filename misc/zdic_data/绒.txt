                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>绒</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">絨</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>柔软细小的毛：鸭～。羽～。驼～。</li>
	<li>棉、丝或毛制成的上面有一层细毛的纺织品：～布。～毯。～衣。丝～。</li>
	<li>细布。</li>
	<li>刺绣用的细丝：红绿～儿。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> silk, cotton, or woolen fabric</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aus Wolle, Wollware ,Samt  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> duvet,laine fine</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>绒</strong></p>
<p>絨、毧 <span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，戎声。本义:鸟兽身上的柔毛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[down]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:绒毛(毛纤维类型的一种。生长在兽类毛被的内层，是毛纺织工业的贵重原料);绒羽(也称&ldquo;羽绒&rdquo;。鸟羽的一种。柔软细小，有护体、保温作用。可制御寒被服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 丝、棉纱或羊毛等的纺织物 <span class="encs">[fine cloth]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:绒毯;绒花;绒绳</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 熟丝 <span class="encs">[boiled-offsilk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绒，熟练丝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 绒布，上面有一层绒毛的纺织品 <span class="encs">[cloth with pile on one or either side]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丝绒;长毛绒;灯芯绒</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> <span class="encs">[刺绣用的]</span> 细丝 <span class="encs">[velvet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:绒线;绒线铺(卖丝线的店铺)</span></p>
