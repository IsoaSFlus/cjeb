                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>则</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">則</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zé  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zé.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄜˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>模范：以身作～。</li>    <li>规程，制度：规～。总～。原～。细～。</li>    <li>效法：～先烈之言行。</li>    <li>表示因果关系，就，便：闻过～喜。</li>    <li>表示转折，却：今～不然。</li>    <li>表示肯定判断，乃，是：此～余之罪也。</li>    <li>用在一、二、三……等数字后，列举原因或理由：一～房子太小，二～参加的人数多。</li>    <li>与“<span class="diczx3">做</span>”相近（中国宋、元、明代小说、戏剧里常用）：～甚（做什么）。</li>    <li>量词，指成文的条数：新闻两～。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>则</strong></p><p>則 <span class="dicpy">zé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文从鼎，从刀。古代的法律条文曾刻铸在鼎上，以便让人遵守。本义:准则，法则)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[criterion;norm;standard;rule]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则，等画物也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则，法也;则，常也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">根天地之气，寒暑之和，水土之性，人民鸟兽草木之生物，虽不甚多，皆均有焉，而未尝变也，谓之则。——<span class="smcs">《管子·七法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">均守平则。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司马》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">法则以驭其官。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌则以逆都鄙之治。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太史》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“亦法也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有物有则。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·烝民》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天不变其常，地不易其则。——<span class="smcs">《管子·形势》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:则天(以天为法，治理天下);则度(法度);则效(则象。效法)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 等级(由则的划分等级引申而来) <span class="encs">[grade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《坤》</span>作坠势，高下九则。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·叙传下》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注引 <span class="diczx2">刘德</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“九州土田上中下九等也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古指三百平方里以下的采邑 <span class="encs">[district about 300 square li]</span>。王莽时以土方五十里为一则，为子男封邑</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 标准权衡器 <span class="encs">[standard weighing device]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王者制事、立法、物度、轨则，壹禀于六律。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 榜样 <span class="encs">[example]</span>。如:以身作则</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>则</strong></p><p>則 <span
