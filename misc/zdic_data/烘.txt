                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>烘</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hōng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>用火或蒸气使身体暖和或使物体变热、干燥：～箱。～手。～干（<span class="dicpy">gān</span>）。～烤。～焙。</li><li>烧。</li><li>衬托，渲染：～衬。～托。～染。～云托月（喻从侧面加以点染以烘托所描绘的事物）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">烘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>烘</strong> <span class="dicpy">hōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，共声。本义:烧，焚烧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烘，燎也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卬烘于歁。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·白华》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:烘眼(角膜炎之类的眼病，俗称<span class="yhcs">“红眼”</span>);烘焰(光焰);烘腾腾(火旺盛貌)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 烤干;烤热 <span class="encs">[dry by fire;roast]</span>。如:烘火(烤火，向火取暖);烘咖啡豆;烘衣服;烘鸡</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 衬托;渲染 <span class="encs">[set off by contrast]</span>。如:烘晕(烘托)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 映照 <span class="encs">[shine upon]</span>。如:烘明(通明);烘霁(霁光映照);烘晴(阳光映照晴空);烘影(映照成影)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 用同<span class="yhcs">“哄”</span>。人同时发出声音 <span class="encs">[roar]</span>。如:烘堂(众人同时大笑);烘然(喧闹貌)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烘焙">烘焙</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烘干">烘干</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烘烤">烘烤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烘笼，烘笼儿">烘笼，烘笼儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烘炉">烘炉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烘染">烘染</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烘托">烘托</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烘云托月">烘云托月</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>烘</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄨㄥ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hōng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2982.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用火烤干或藉火取暖。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「烘干」、「<b>烘</b>手」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《水浒传·第一○回》：「小人是牢城营差使人，被雪打湿了衣裳，借此火<b>烘</b>一<b>烘</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">衬托、陪衬。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「烘托」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·范成大〈春后微雪一宿而晴〉诗：「朝暾不与同云便，<b>烘</b>作晴空万缕霞。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·辛弃疾〈满江红·可恨东君〉词：「更天涯、芳草最关情，<b>烘</b>残日。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
