                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/21562.svg">                          <p>瑙蓋也。象皮包覆瑙，下有兩臂，而夊在下。讀若范。亡范切〖注〗瑙當作匘。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/21562_9286.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21562.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡕢</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">匘葢也。</span><span class="swdz">司馬彪輿服志。乗輿金錽。劉昭引蔡邕獨<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣃔</span>曰。金錽者、馬冠也。高廣各五寸。上如五華形。在馬髦前。薛綜注東京賦同。按在馬髦前、則正在馬之匘葢。其字本作金<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21562.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡕢</span>。或加金旁耳。馬融廣成頌。揚金<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21562.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡕢</span>而拖玉瓖。字正作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21562.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡕢</span>可證。西京賦。璿弁玉纓。薛曰。弁、馬冠叉髦也。徐廣說金錽云。金爲馬叉髦。然則弁也叉髦也、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21562.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡕢</span>也、一也。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21562.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡕢</span>或誤作㚇。錽或誤作鍐。玉篇又誤作金騣。皆音子公反。非也。匘葢者、人<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26953.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥓</span>也。見<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26953.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥓</span>部。</span><span class="ywyy">象皮包覆匘。</span><span class="swdz">謂不<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25605.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥘅</span>。</span><span class="ywyy">下有㒳<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2684D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦡍</span>。</span><span class="swdz">謂人。</span><span class="ywyy">而夊在下。</span><span class="swdz">人足也。</span><span class="ywyy">讀若范。</span><span class="swdz">亡范切。七部八部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
