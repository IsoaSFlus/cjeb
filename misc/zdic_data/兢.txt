                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兢</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形，象二人头顶重物形。头上戴着重物，故常戒惕小心。本义:小心谨慎的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[careful;cautious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">战战兢兢，如临深渊，如履薄冰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小旻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兢惕(小心谨慎);兢持(矜持，拘束);兢兢切切(兢兢业业，小心翼翼);兢危(戒慎忧惧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 强劲 <span class="encs">[strong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔羊来思，矜矜兢兢，不骞不崩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;无羊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兢兢(强壮的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兢</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>战栗;恐惧 <span class="encs">[twitch]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兢灼(戒惧焦灼);兢悚(恐惧);兢惕(戒惧);兢悸(犹惊悸)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兢</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=儿" target="_blank">儿</a>】
                              兢·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5162.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/204C6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠓆</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>居陵切，音矜。【說文】競也。【徐曰】競，强也。一曰敬也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋訓】兢兢，戒也。【玉篇】戒愼也。【書&middot;臯陶謨】兢兢業業，一日二日萬幾。【註】言當戒懼萬事之微。【前漢&middot;外戚傳】唯婚姻爲兢兢。【註】戒懼也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】不自安貌。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】人凌兢。【註】師古曰：寒凉戰栗處。</p>
<p>又【韻會】通作矜。【文選&middot;韋孟詩】矜矜先王。【註】戒也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】巨興切，音殑。兢兢，堅彊貌。</p>
