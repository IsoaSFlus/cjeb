                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>番</strong> <span class="dicpy">fān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，上面象野兽的足掌和爪，下面的<span class="yhcs">“田”</span>象兽足踩出的印子。①本义:兽足。②更替，替代，轮流)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 更替，轮值 <span class="encs">[take turns]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顿兵一万，番代往来。——<span class="smcs">《北史·贺弼传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">漏鼓移则番代。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:番代(轮流更换);番休(轮流休息);番更(轮流巡夜)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用同<span class="yhcs">“翻”</span> <span class="encs">[turn]</span>。如:番将转来(翻转过来);番修(翻修);番身(翻身)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>番</strong> <span class="dicpy">fān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 次，回 <span class="encs">[time]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迭为三番。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:几番风雨;番把(几次);番番是福(每件事都吉利)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 种 <span class="encs">[a kind of]</span>。如:别有一番天地;别有一番滋味;一番江水一番鱼</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 片;枚;块 <span class="encs">[piece]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宫中造</span><span class="diczx2">清思院</span>新殿，用铜镜三千片，黄白金薄十万番。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代计算纸张的单位 <span class="encs">[sheet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">杜暹</span>]</span>秩满归，吏以纸万番赆之， <span class="diczx2">暹</span>为受百番。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 倍 <span
