                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">儿</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>儿</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">兒</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&eacute;r  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;r.mp3"></a></span> ㄦˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>小孩子：婴～。～戏。</li>
	<li>年轻的人（多指青年男子）：男～。～女情。</li>
	<li>儿子，男孩子：～子。生～育女。</li>
	<li>父母对儿女的统称，儿女对父母的自称。</li>
	<li>助詞：❶多用作名词后缀。❷用于形容词后。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> son, child; KangXi radical 10</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Sohn ,silbenloses Diminutiv-Suffix (das "e" wird nicht gesprochen)​</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> enfant,(suffixe phonétique)​,enfant,fils</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">儿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>儿</strong></p>
<p>兒 <span class="dicpy">&eacute;r</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;r.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，下面是&ldquo;人&rdquo;字，上面象小儿张口哭笑。&ldquo;儿&rdquo;是汉字部首之一，从&ldquo;儿&rdquo;的字大都与&ldquo;人&rdquo;有关。本义:幼儿。古时男称儿，女称婴，后来孩童都称儿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[child， pl. children]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儿，孺子也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">专气致柔，能如婴儿乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>第九章</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老人儿啼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;循吏列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男曰儿，女曰婴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见两小儿辩斗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:儿女子(孩童);儿剧(犹儿戏);儿话(孩子话);儿撬(拐棍儿);儿拜(孩童对大人所行的礼拜);儿啼(小孩哭);儿孩(孩童)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 儿女，父母所生的子女，第一代后裔 <span class="encs">[child]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:儿孙满堂;小儿;儿女夫妻(结发夫妻，从小的夫妻);儿女之情(指青年男女之间相爱的情感)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 长辈对幼辈的称呼。亦用作轻蔑之称 <span class="encs">[child]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:儿郎(将帅对士兵的称呼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 父母对儿子的称呼 <span class="encs">[son]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儿，子也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释亲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汤</span>为儿守舍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张汤传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外黄令舍人儿年十三。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;项藉传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大儿锄豆溪东，中儿正织鸡笼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《清平乐&middot;村居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阿爷无大儿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:儿媳(即儿媳妇);儿子花(儿子的影子);儿曹(儿辈);儿息(子息)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 男青年 <span class="encs">[young man]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:儿孩儿(男孩子);儿男(男孩，男子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 年轻人;青年 <span class="encs">[youngster]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:健儿</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 我 <span class="encs">[I]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 子女对父母的自称</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">兰芝</span>惭阿母:&ldquo;儿实无罪过。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古代年轻女子的自称</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">送儿还故乡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:儿家(古代年轻女子的自称;你，称女子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 弟对兄的自称</p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[帝]</span> 乃以</span><span class="diczx2">延宗</span>为相国、 <span class="diczx2">并州</span>刺史，总 <span class="diczx2">山西</span>兵事。 谓曰∶&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">并州</span>阿兄取，儿今去也。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史&middot;齐安德王延宗传》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>儿</strong></p>
<p>兒 <span class="dicpy">&eacute;r</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;r.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 雄性&mdash;&mdash;多指牲畜 <span class="encs">[male]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:儿猫(公猫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 自己的 <span class="encs">[my]</span> 用于古代妇女称自己丈夫</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宣和</span>中士女观灯者赐酒。有夫妇并游，不获同进，其妇蒙赐，辄怀酒杯，谢词曰:&ldquo;归来恐被儿夫怪，愿赐金杯作证盟。&rdquo;上赐之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《月令广义》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>儿</strong></p>
<p>兒 <span class="dicpy">&eacute;r</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&eacute;r.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;后缀&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 儿化。汉语后缀的&ldquo;儿&rdquo;不自成音节，而和前面一个音节合在一起构成带卷舌韵母r的音节</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 多用作名词后缀 <span class="cino">(1)</span>∶表示小。如:盆儿;棍儿;小猫儿;小车儿 <span class="cino">(2)</span>∶表示词性变化。动词名词化，如:唱儿;逗笑儿。形容词名词化，如:亮儿;零碎儿 <span class="cino">(3)</span>∶表示具体事物抽象化。如:口儿;门儿;根儿 <span class="cino">(4)</span>∶表示词义变化。如:白面儿(海洛因);老家儿(对人称自己或别人的父母)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 动词的后缀。如:玩儿;火儿</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形容词后缀。如邵雍<span class="smcs">《首尾吟》</span>:&ldquo;天听虽高只些子，人情想去没多儿。&rdquo;</p>
