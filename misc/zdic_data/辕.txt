                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辕</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>辕</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">轅</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>车前驾牲畜的两根直木：～马。车～。驾～。南～北辙。</li><li>旧时指军营、官署的外门，借指衙署：～门。行（<span class="dicpy">xíng </span>）～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> axle; magistrate's office; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Deichsel  (S)​,Yuan  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> brancard,limon (longues pièces de bois entre lesquelles on attelle un animal)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>辕</strong></p><p>轅 <span class="dicpy">yuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，袁声。本义:车辕子，车前驾牲口的直木)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shafts of a cart]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辕，辀也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，大车、柏车、羊车皆左右两木，曰辕，其形直。一牛在辕间;田车、兵车、乘车皆居中。一木穹隆而上，曰辀，其形曲。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今夫大车之辕挚，其登又难，既克其登，其覆车也必易。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·辀人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡为辕，三其轮崇。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·考工记·车人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令尹南辕返旆。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:辕轭(车前驾牲口的直木和套在牲口脖子上的曲木。借指车子)辕缚(指辂木。因其缚于辕上，故名)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代帝王外出止宿时，于险阻处置车为屏藩，又仰两车使辕对峙如门，称辕门 <span class="encs">[outer gate(of a commander's or emperor's temporary residence)formed by two carriages]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">置旃以为辕门。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·昭公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设车宫辕门。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:辕垣(官署)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 后指军营之门或行馆，即长官战场司令部或官方的衙署 <span class="encs">[office of magistrate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">吕奉先</span>射戟辕门， <span class="diczx2">曹孟德</span>败师淯水。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">城中有何新闻?抚军在辕否?——</span><span class="diczx2">沈浮</span><span class="smcs">《浮生六记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:辕门抄(清朝督抚官署抄寄属下州、府、县的公文或情报。后改为木板印刷)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 车 <span class="encs">[cart]</span>。如:辕下(车下);辕辙(车迹);辕议(车夫的议论。泛指街谈巷议)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 犁辕 <span class="encs">[shaft of a plough]</span>。如:辕长(犁辕的长度)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Yuan village]</span>。在今山东省禹城县境内</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 姓 <span class="encs">[surname]</span>。春秋陈有辕涛涂</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr                           <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“辕”字头，请参考“轅”字。)</span>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=車" target="_blank">車</a>】
                              轅·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8F45.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】雨元切【集韻】【韻會】于元切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音袁。【說文】輈也。【釋名】轅，援也。車之援也。【韻會】詩詁曰：車前曲木上鉤衡者謂之輈，亦曰轅。從軫以前稍曲而上至衡，則居衡之上而向下鉤之，衡卽輈端橫木，以駕馬者。</p><p>又轅門。【周禮·天官·掌舍】掌王會同之舍，設車宮轅門。【註】王者出行于外，次車爲藩，仰車以轅相向表門，故曰轅門。</p><p>又軒轅，黃帝號。【前漢·古今人表】黃帝作軒冕之服，故謂之軒轅氏。</p><p>又軒轅，星名。【史記·天官書】軒轅十二星。</p><p>又姓。【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>大夫轅濤塗之後。</p><p>又軒轅，複姓。</p><p>又通作爰。【前漢·地理志】商鞅作轅田。【註】轅，與爰同。</p><p>又【集韻】于眷切【等韻】羽眷切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音媛。地名。【左傳·哀十年】取犂及轅。【註】齊地。</p><p>又轘轅，縣名。【史記·樊噲傳】從攻長社、轘轅。【註】許州所屬縣也。</p>
