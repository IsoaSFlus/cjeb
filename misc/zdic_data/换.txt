						<p>● <strong>换</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>给人东西同时从他那里取得别的东西：交～。对～。～工。～文。兑～。</li>
	<li>更改，变：变～。更（<span class="dicpy">gēng </span>）～。～马（喻撤换担负某项职务的人，含贬义）。～样。～气。～言之（换句话说）。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>换</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，奂( <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:互易)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[trade;exchange;barter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">换，易也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">擅相换易。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;桓公元年》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:以帛换酒;换过子(互相调换);换了帖(异姓的人结拜为兄弟时交换写有年龄、籍贯、家世等的帖子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 变易，改变 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几移几换。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:换衣服;换手(更换操作的人);换脚手(换鞋、衣服、手套等);换易(调换、更换);换朝(改朝换代)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/63DB.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>胡玩切，音逭。讀若完去聲。【說文】易也。【晉書&middot;阮孚傳】嘗以金貂換酒。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;敘傳】項氏畔換。【註】孟康曰：畔，反也。換，易也。師古曰：畔換，强恣貌。猶言跋扈也。【左思&middot;魏都賦】雲散叛換。【註】叛換，猶恣睢也。【集韻】作愌。</p>
<p>又于眷切，音院。【蘇轍&middot;送孫提<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>詩】依依東軒竹，凜凜故人面。詔書遂公私，使節許新換。从手从側人从穴从廾。俗作换，非。</p>
