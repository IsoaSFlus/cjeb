						<p>● <strong>蓐</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>陈草复生，引申为草垫子，草席：～妇（产妇）。～母（接生婆）。坐～（临产）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> straw bed mat; rushes</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hetzt, saust ,Matte, Abbaumatte  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> natte,tapis,jonc,paille</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蓐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蓐</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，辱声。本义:陈草复生)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grow aftergrass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蓐，陈草复生也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>系传:&ldquo;陈根更生繁缛也。&hellip;言草繁多也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 吃饱 <span class="encs">[have eaten one&#39;s fill;be full]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蓐马饷军(让马吃饱，让士兵吃好);蓐食(吃饱吃好;丰厚的饮食)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蓐</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 草席;草垫 <span class="encs">[straw mat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蓐谓之兹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注;&ldquo;兹者，蓐席也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左追蓐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span>。注:&ldquo;在左者追求草蓐为宿备。&rdquo;疏:&ldquo;蓐，谓卧土之草。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">范</span>乃令军中蓐食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;廉范传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蓐母(产婆，接生婆);蓐妇(产妇;生产之妇女);蓐劳(中医病名。此病因产时不顺，疲累过度，产后气血衰弱，调养失宜，导致虚邪侵入，阴虚成劳);蓐食(早晨未起身，在床席上进餐。谓早餐时间很早)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;褥&rdquo;。坐卧时铺在床椅上面的垫子，褥子 <span class="encs">[mattress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有重疾，卧蓐七年。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;赵岐传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 蚕蔟。用麦秆等做成，蚕在上面做茧 <span class="encs">[a small bundle of straw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蓐，蔟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Ru State]</span>。地在 <span class="diczx2">汾水</span>流域，春秋时为 <span class="diczx2">晋国</span>所灭</p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              蓐·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/84D0.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】而蜀切【韻會】如欲切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音辱。【說文】陳草復生繁縟也。</p>
<p>又【篇海】猶蠶蔟也。</p>
<p>又薦也。【禮&middot;少儀】《註》：茵著，蓐也。【爾雅&middot;釋器】蓐謂之兹。【註】《公羊傳》屬負兹。兹者，蓐席也。</p>
<p>又馬藉草曰蓐。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;圉師】春除蓐釁廄，始牧。【註】蓐，馬兹也。馬旣出而除之。</p>
<p>又【博雅】厚也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;月令】孟秋，其神蓐收。【左傳註】秋物摧蓐而可收也。</p>
<p>又國名。【左傳&middot;昭元年】沈、姒、蓐、黃。【註】四國臺駘之後。</p>
<p>又姓。見【氏族畧】。</p>
