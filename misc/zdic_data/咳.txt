                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">咳</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>咳</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">k&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄜˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～嗽〕呼吸器官受刺激而引起一种反射作用。把吸入的气急急呼出，同时发声，能清除呼吸道中的异物或痰，亦是某些病的症状。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>咳</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hāi.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄞˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>叹息：～声叹气。</li>
	<li>叹词，表示惋惜或后悔：又表示招呼人，提醒人注意：～！我昨天没去医院。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cough</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> husten, Husten  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tousser,toux,(onom.)​ soupir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">咳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>咳</strong> <span class="dicpy">hāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈叹〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 啊 <span class="encs">[hi]</span>&mdash;&mdash;用以表示各种感情(如欢欣、宽慰、遗憾或轻蔑等)。如:咳!我怎么忘了?</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 哎呀，唉，呜呼 <span class="encs">[alas]</span>&mdash;&mdash;用以表示不幸、悲痛、惋惜或关切。如:咳!这真是意料不到的事</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 该死!糟了! <span class="encs">[damn it]</span>&mdash;&mdash;用作轻微的诅咒。如:咳，我怎么这么糊涂!</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 表示感叹 <span class="encs">[what]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:咳，提起话来就长啦;咳，真有这种怪事儿!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 嗨 <span class="encs">[hi]</span>&mdash;&mdash;用以表示问候或引起注意。如:咳，到这儿来!;咳咳(戏曲中起加强乐曲节奏的衬字)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>咳</strong> <span class="dicpy">hāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 叹息 <span class="encs">[sigh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">黛玉</span>只&ldquo;咳&rdquo;了一声，眼中泪直流下来，回身便走。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i;k&eacute;</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/咳声叹气">咳声叹气</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>咳</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 小儿笑 <span class="encs">[child laugh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咳，小儿笑也。从口，亥声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 后泛指笑貌。如:咳咳(喜笑貌)，咳笑(小儿笑);</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;阂&rdquo;。阻隔 <span class="encs">[obstruct]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颈尾咳于天地乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>咳</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>

                              咳·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/54B3.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕孩【唐韻】戸來切【集韻】【正韻】何開切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音頦。【說文】小兒笑也。【史記&middot;扁鵲傳】曾不可以告咳嬰之兒。【註】咳嬰，言嬰兒初知笑者。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;內則】父執子之右手，咳而名之。【疏】謂以一手執子右手，一手承子之咳而名之。【集韻】或作㜾<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20E5B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠹛</span>。</p>
<p>又【集韻】柯開切，音該。【史記&middot;倉公傳】受其脈書上下經，五色診奇咳術。【註】奇咳，言奇祕非常術也。【集解】奇音羈，咳音該。○按《前漢&middot;藝文志》有五音奇胲用兵二十六卷，《淮南子&middot;兵略訓》<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>德奇賌之數，《廣韻》作奇侅，亦作奇賅。咳胲賌侅賅五字皆通。</p>
<p>又與該同。【晏子&middot;外篇】頸尾咳於天地，然而漻漻不知六翮之所在。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】苦代切，音慨。【禮&middot;內則】不敢噦噫嚏咳。【釋文】咳，苦愛反。【莊子&middot;漁父篇】幸聞咳唾之音。【前漢&middot;宣元六王傳】大王誠賜咳唾。【正韻】謦欬，亦作咳。</p>
