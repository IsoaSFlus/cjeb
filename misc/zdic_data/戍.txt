class="encs">[guard the frontier]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戍，守边也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，伐者左人右戈，人持戈也。戍者，下人上戈，人何戈也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">武王</span>立重泉之戍。——<span class="smcs">《管子·地数》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼其之子，不与我戍</span><span class="diczx2">申</span>。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·扬之水》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">二世</span>元年七月，发闾左适戍 <span class="cino">(4)</span> 驻守 <span class="encs">[garrison]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三男</span><span class="diczx2">邺城</span>戍。—— <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">僵卧孤村不自哀，尚思为国戍轮台。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《十一月四日风雨大作》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:戍鼓(驻边军士所击的鼓声);戍主(古代驻守一地的长官);戍堡(边防驻军的营垒、城堡);戍御(防守御敌)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>戍</strong> <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a soldiers]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃归诸侯之戍。——<span class="smcs">《左传·定公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:戍傜(兵役及劳役);戍屋(戍卒屋舍);戍妇(戍卒的妻子);戍歌(戍卒唱的歌)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 守边之事 <span class="encs">[garrison]</span>。如:戍漕军需用品之运输。特指水运。又指边防区域的营垒、城堡</p><p><span class="diczx1">其</span><span class="diczx2">谢尚</span>、 <span
