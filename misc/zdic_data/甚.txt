<p><span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄣˋ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>很，极：～好。～快。</li>
	<li>超过：日～一日。</li>
	<li>方言，什么：～事？</li>
	<li>表示进一层的意思：～至。～或。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>甚</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&eacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄣˊ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">甚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>甚</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&eacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 疑问代词。什么 <span class="encs">[what]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:甚般(哪般，什么;怎样);甚的(甚底、甚迭。什么);甚末(甚么，什么);甚娘(甚末娘，什么娘。骂人的话。什么);甚生(什么);甚实(确实，很实);甚人(什么人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 为什么，怎么 <span class="encs">[why]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:甚地(怎么)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>甚</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，从甘，从匹。甘是快乐，匹，匹耦。沉溺于男女欢情。本义:异常安乐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[abandon oneself to;indulge in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甚，尤安乐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐灏</span>注:&ldquo;甚，古今字。女部。&lsquo;乐也。&rsquo;通作耽、湛。<span class="smcs">《卫风&middot;氓篇》</span>:&lsquo;无与士耽。&rsquo;<span class="smcs">《小雅&middot;常隶篇》</span>:&lsquo;和乐且湛。&rsquo;皆甚字之本义。从甘匹，会意，昵其匹耦也;甘亦声&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玄甚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓贪淫声色。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 过分 <span class="encs">[go too far;overdo]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甚矣，汝之不惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此不亦畏之太甚而养之太过欤?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:甚泰(衣着过于宽大)</span></p>
