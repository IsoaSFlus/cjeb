                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">落</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>落</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄚˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>丢下，遗漏：丢三～四。～了一个字。</li></ol><p>● <strong>落</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄛˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>掉下来，往下降：降～。～下。零～（①叶子脱落，如&ldquo;草木～～&rdquo;；②衰败，如&ldquo;一片～～景象&rdquo;；③稀疏，如&ldquo;枪声～～&rdquo;）。脱～。～叶。～泪。～潮。～英。～日。～体。～座。陨～。～井下石（形容乘人之危，加以打击陷害）。～雁沉鱼。叶～归根。</li>	<li>衰败：没（<span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span>）～。破～。衰～。沦～。流～。～泊（①潦倒失意；②豪迈，不拘束，均亦作&ldquo;落魄（<span class="dicpy">b&oacute; </span>）&rdquo;）。</li>	<li>遗留在后面：～后。～伍。～选。</li>	<li>停留，留下：～户。～荒。～笔。～款。</li>	<li>停留或居住的地方：村～。部～。院～。</li>	<li>归属，得到某种结果：～得。～空。</li>	<li>陷入不利境地：～网。～难（<span class="dicpy">n&agrave;n</span>）。</li>	<li>古代宫室建成时举行的祭礼，现泛指建筑物完工：～成。</li>	<li>稀少：疏～。稀稀～～。</li>	<li>屋檐上的滴水装置（俗称&ldquo;檐滴水&rdquo;）：&ldquo;矗不知其几千万～。&rdquo;</li>	<li>死亡：殂～。</li>	<li>〔～～〕①豁达，大方，如&ldquo;～～大方&rdquo;；②孤独，不苟合，如&ldquo;～～寡合。&rdquo;</li>	<li>篱笆：&ldquo;凿井浚渠，缚～锄园。&rdquo;</li></ol><p>● <strong>落</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄠˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用于一些口语词，如&ldquo;落枕&rdquo;、&ldquo;落炕&rdquo;等。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fall, drop; net income, surplus</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hinterlassen, zurücklassen  (V)​,gleich wie <a href="./落">落</a> (luo)​; wird in manchen umgangsprachlichen Wörtern verwendet (V, vulg)​,absetzen, fallen ,fallen, absinken ,landen ,Luo  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> laisser derrière,abandonner,oublier,baisser,tomber,descendre</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>落</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 丢了，漏掉 <span class="encs">[be missing]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丢三落四;这里落了两个字</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 忘了拿，没注意取 <span class="encs">[forget to bring sth.;leave sth.]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我忙着出来，把票落在家里了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 落后 <span class="encs">[lag behind]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落下很远;落在后面</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 没有上，误 <span class="encs">[miss]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落了一个星期的课</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;o;luō;lu&ograve;</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>落</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>曲艺名。北方对&ldquo;莲花落&rdquo;的俗称。又泛指各种曲艺杂耍 <span class="encs">[laozi，Lotus laozi]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落子馆(演北方曲艺杂耍的场所)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>落</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 捞，赚 <span class="encs">[earn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">把东西开个花账儿，落他二三两银子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落褒贬(落下不是);落亏欠(欠下人情债)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 脱落;退去 <span class="encs">[fade]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落后(退色)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 错失 <span class="encs">[fault]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落枕(又名失枕。因睡觉时受寒或枕枕头的姿势不合适，以致脖子疼痛，转动不便)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 倒，倒下 <span class="encs">[fall]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落炕(方言。病得不能起床)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;;luō;lu&ograve;</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>落</strong> <span class="dicpy">luō</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &mdash;&mdash;&ldquo;大大落落&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;da-luōluō</span>):方言。形容态度大方</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;;l&agrave;o;lu&ograve;</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>落</strong> <span class="dicpy">lu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，洛声。本义:叶落，花落)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wither or fall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">落，凡草曰零，木曰落。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草木黄落。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及荣华之未落兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唯草木之零落兮。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桑之未落，其叶沃若。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人闲桂花落。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《鸟鸣涧》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">落英缤纷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叶落树下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纷堕如落叶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落红(落花);落花媒人(不正当男女关系的撮合人);落英缤纷(落地零乱的样子);落木(落叶);落花时节(指暮春季节)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 脱离;脱身 <span class="encs">[separate one-self from;break away from]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落荒而走(逃离战场，奔向荒野)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指下落;下坠 <span class="encs">[descend;fall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山高月小，水落石出。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《后赤壁赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牙齿半落左耳聋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《复阴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">飞流直下三千尺，疑是银河落九天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《望庐山瀑布》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其印自落。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡鸣月落。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼应声落。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贼应声落;落土(指太阳或月亮下山);落山(指太阳下山);落羽(指受伤坠地的鸟);涨落;落生(射落生灵，即猎取鸟类);落帽(帽子为风吹落);落絮(落下的丝絮。比喻飘落的柳絮;比喻飘落的雪花);落潮(退潮);落雁沉魚(形容女子貌美所用之詞);落坐(坐下);落台(下台;了結);落地罩(可以坠到地面的帷幕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 掉进;进入 <span class="encs">[fall into]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可落于敌人之手。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭記》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">几落贼手死。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落局(落入骗局);落夜(入夜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 耽误;荒废 <span class="encs">[delay;hold up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫子阖行邪?无落吾事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落索(萧索);落然(荒废的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 衰败 <span class="encs">[decay; decline; wane]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盛而不落者，未之有也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;宙合》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落漠(落拓，潦倒);落羽(羽毛摧落。比喻失意);落薄(落魄;潦倒失意);落拓(落魄;穷困失意;放荡不羁);落膘(指牲畜变瘦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 除去;特指免去职务等 <span class="encs">[remove sb. from office;dismiss]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落台(卸去公职)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 止息;停留;亦指留下 <span class="encs">[stop;stay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众婆子步下围随至一垂花门前落下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span>。又如:落市(歇市，停止营业);落草(停止);落夜(住宿，过夜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 遗留在后面 <span class="encs">[lag behind]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落卷(没有录取的卷子);落名(考试落第);落脚货(剩下来的布头零碎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 归属 <span class="encs">[belong to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落着(可以依靠或指望的来源);落台(結束，告一段段)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 得到 <span class="encs">[get;obtain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俺若是一心行正，落一个万古名扬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《射柳捶丸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落不的(得不到);落来(得到某种结果);落定(成为定局);落保(担保)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 克扣，中饱钱财 <span class="encs">[fish for;gain]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:落钞(经手银钱时私下克扣一小部分);落腰(收进腰包);落阁(中饱私囊;从中渔利);落钱(侵吞经手钱财)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 古代宫室建成时举行祭礼 <span class="encs">[be completed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚子</span>成 <span class="diczx2">章华</span>之台，愿与诸侯落之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公七年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落成</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 通&ldquo;络&rdquo;。羁勒，用网状物兜住 <span class="encs">[hold sth. in place with a net]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">落马首，穿牛鼻，是谓人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">成玄英 疏：&ldquo;牛鼻可穿，馬首可絡。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 中医的经络系統 <span class="encs">[system of the channels and collaterals]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原人血脉经落骨髓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;艺文志》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 衰败;零落;稀疏 <span class="encs">[withered and fallen]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落解粥(极稀的粥);落然(冷落);落堕(零落);落驿(稀疏，零落)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>落</strong> <span class="dicpy">lu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 居处 <span class="encs">[residence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矗不知几千万落。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜枚</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:院落;部落;村落</span></p>

<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 叠，摞 <span class="encs">[pile]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:公公拿着一落砖</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">脫落、凋墜。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈古風〉詩五九首之一四：「木<b>落</b>秋草黃，登高望戎虜。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;秦觀〈一斛珠&middot;碧雲寥廓〉詞：「江山滿眼今非昨，紛紛木葉風中<b>落</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">掉下、降低。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「落淚」、「落價」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李商隱〈常娥〉詩：「雲母屏風燭影深，長河漸<b>落</b>曉星沉。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈後赤壁賦〉：「山高月小，水落石出。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">陷入、掉入。</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;陶淵明〈歸園田居詩〉五首之一：「誤落塵網中，一去三十年。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《晉書&middot;卷五四&middot;陸雲傳》：「於水中顧見其影，因大笑落水，人救獲免。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">除去。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;劉長卿〈戲贈干越尼子歌〉：「厭向春江空浣沙，龍宮落髮披袈裟。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《宋史&middot;卷三○四&middot;劉師道傳》：「幾道既擢第，事泄，詔<b>落</b>其籍，永不預舉。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">掉在後面。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈流夜郎贈辛判官〉詩：「氣岸遙凌豪士前，風流肯<b>落</b>他人後。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">衰敗。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「衰<b>落</b>」、「家道中<b>落</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;蔣士銓〈鳴機夜課圖記〉：「越二載，生銓，家益<b>落</b>，歷困苦窮乏，人所不能堪者。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">停留。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「落腳」、「話音未<b>落</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;劉長卿〈入桂渚次砂牛石穴〉詩：「片帆<b>落</b>桂渚，獨夜依楓林。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">留下。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「落款」、「不<b>落</b>痕跡」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">得到。</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第三四回》：「伏侍一場，大家<b>落</b>個平安，也算是造化了。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">歸屬。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「重擔<b>落</b>在誰身上？」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈將適吳楚留別章使君〉詩：「不意青草湖，扁舟<b>落</b>吾手。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">掉下的、凋零的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「落英繽紛」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈長恨歌〉：「西宮南苑多秋草，落葉滿階紅不掃。」</span></p></li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">稀疏、冷清的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「寥<b>落</b>」、「疏<b>落</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷一二○&middot;鄭當時傳》：「家貧，賓客益<b>落</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">伶俐。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「俐<b>落</b>」。</span></p></li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">停留、停頓的地方。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「下<b>落</b>」、「著<b>落</b>」。</span></p></li>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/843D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】盧各切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】歷各切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音洛。【禮&middot;王制】草木零落，然後入山林。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】落，始也。</p><p>又【左傳&middot;昭七年】楚子成章華之臺，願與諸侯落之。【註】宮室始成，祭之爲落。</p><p>又叔孫爲孟鐘，饗大夫以落之。【註】以豭豬血釁鐘曰落。</p><p>又【博雅】居也。【綱目集覽】人所聚居，故謂之村落、屯落、聚落。</p><p>又【後漢&middot;竇憲傳】躡冒頓之區落。</p><p>又蕃籬曰虎落。【前漢&middot;鼂錯傳】爲中周虎落。【註】以竹篾相連遮落之也。</p><p>又廢也。【莊子&middot;天地篇】子高曰：夫子盍行耶，無落吾事，耕而不顧。</p><p>又落落，不相入貌。【老子&middot;道德經】落落如石。</p><p>又牢落。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】牢落陸離。</p><p>又陸落。【論衡】見文吏便而儒生陸落，則詆訾儒生，以爲淺短，稱譽文吏，謂之深長。</p><p>又拓落。【揚雄&middot;解嘲】何爲官之拓落也。</p><p>又【韻會】錯落，閒厠貌。</p><p>又歲名。【爾雅&middot;釋天】太歲在巳曰大荒落。</p><p>又星名。【史記&middot;天官書】軍西爲壘，旁有一大星爲北落。</p><p>又山名。【水經注】伊水東北過郭落山。</p><p>又草名。【本草】土落，生嶺南山谷。</p><p>又角落，生江西山谷，似茱萸，獨莖。</p><p>又木名。【爾雅&middot;釋木】檴，落。【疏】檴，一名落，可作杯圈，皮韌，繞物不解。</p><p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】漢有落下閎。</p><p>又與絡通。【莊子&middot;秋水篇】落馬首，穿牛鼻。【前漢&middot;李廣傳】上召禹刺虎，禹從落中斫絕纍，上壯之。【師古註】謂當時繦絡之而下也。</p><p>又【唐韻古音】讀路。【晉庾闡&middot;弔賈誼文】張高弦悲聲激柱落。叶下濩韻。</p>
