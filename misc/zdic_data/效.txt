                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>效</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>摹仿：～法。仿～。上行下～。～尤（明知别人的行为是错的而照样去做）。</li>
	<li>功用，成果：～验。～果。成～。有～。功～。～益。～用。～应。～率。</li>
	<li>尽、致：～力。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">效</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>效</strong></p>
<p>俲 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，交声。&ldquo;攴&rdquo;有致力的意思。本义:献出;尽力)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[present;devote to;render (service)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">司城</span><span class="diczx2">荡意诸</span>来奔，效节于府人而出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">异日</span><span class="diczx2">韩王</span>纳地效玺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:效力;效绩(呈献功绩);效玺(呈献国君的印章，表示臣服);效己(贡献一己之力);效芹(自称贡献微薄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 明白 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">由此效之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乡也，效门室之辨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">效，明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言十三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;校( <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;o</span>)&rdquo;。考查。考核 <span class="encs">[examine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼将任我以事而效我以功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:效试(考核试验);效志(查核心志);效情(验以情理)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 授还，授 <span class="encs">[confer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宣王</span>有志，而后效官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十六年》</span>。注:&ldquo;授也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:效官;效业(授官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 显示;呈现 <span class="encs">[take on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信者效其忠。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:效祉(呈现福祉);效祥(呈露祥瑞)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 效验，验证 <span class="encs">[desired result]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何以效之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;订鬼篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 效劳，效忠 <span class="encs">[offer one&#39;s services]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀身自效。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>效</strong></p>
<p>俲、傚 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 摹仿;师法 <span class="encs">[imitate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">效，像也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作俲</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">效者，为之法也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;小取》</span></span></p>
