                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鍪</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代炊器。似釜而反唇。流行于汉代。</li>
	<li>古代武士的头盔。秦、汉以前称&ldquo;胄&rdquo;，后来称&ldquo;兜鍪&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古指形似兜鍪的帽子。</li>
	<li>披散头发。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> an iron pan; a metal cap</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pot de fer,capuchon métallique</p></div>

<p><span class="diczx1">鍪，釜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代武士戴的头盔 <span class="encs">[helmet]</span>。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鍪</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=金" target="_blank">金</a>】
                              鍪·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/936A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】莫浮切【集韻】迷浮切【正韻】莫侯切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音謀。【說文】鍑也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】兜鍪，首鎧。【書&middot;說命&middot;惟甲冑起戎註】冑，兜鍪也。【釋文】鍪，莫侯反。【韻會】譌作䥐。</p>
<p>又【正韻】冠卷。【荀子&middot;禮論篇】冠有鍪而無縱。</p>
<p>又【集韻】莫<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>切，音茂。【博雅】釜也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶蒙晡切，音模。【急就章】弓弩箭矢鎧兜鍪，鐵棰撾杖梲柲殳。殳音殊。　【正字通】兜鍪，形似釜而反脣，非炊具。說文長箋云：軍士以首鎧爲炊具，故曰兜。兜爲冑名，鍪則釜名，一物兼二義。按軍伍鐎斗，晝炊夜擊，未有以首鎧炊者。凡被圍，糧匱援絕，煮弩鎧，食筋革，皆勢窮使然，非軍士例用首鎧爲炊也。箋說誤。</p>
