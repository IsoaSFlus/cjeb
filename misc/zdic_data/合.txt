                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">合</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>合</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄜˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>闭，对拢：～眼。～抱。珠连璧～。貌～神离。</li>	<li>聚集：～力。～办。～股。～资。</li>	<li>不违背，一事物与另一事物相应或相符：～格。～法。情投意～。</li>	<li>应该：～该。～当。&ldquo;文章～为时而著，诗歌～为时而作&rdquo;。</li>	<li>总共，全：～家欢乐。</li>	<li>计，折算：～多少钱。</li>	<li>中国古代乐谱的记音符号，相当于简谱中的低音&ldquo;5&rdquo;。</li></ol><p>● <strong>合</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gě  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gě.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄜˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>中国市制容量单位，一升的十分之一。</li>	<li>旧时量粮食的器具，容量为一合，木或竹制，方形或圆筒形。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> combine, unite, join; gather</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schließen, passen, zusammen  (V)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">合</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>合</strong> <span class="dicpy">gě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gě.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 容量单位， 市制十合为一升 <span class="encs">[a unit of dry measure for grain (=1 decilitre)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十合为升，十升为斗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>合</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从亼( <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span>)，三面合闭，从口。本义:闭合，合拢)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[close;shut]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">合，合口也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">公孙龙</span>口呿而不合。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为开合之称</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">合则成体。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;达生》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">合盖隆起。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四面竹树环合。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绿树村边合。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孟浩然</span><span class="smcs">《过故人庄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">暝色已合。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蚌合;合上书;合祛(开闭);合喙(闭口);合盘(犹合掌);合瞑(闭目;闭目入睡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 会聚，聚合 <span class="encs">[assemble]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">合</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】
                              合·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=6" target="_blank">6</a>　·部外筆画：3</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5408.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>閤切【集韻】【韻會】曷閤切【正韻】胡閤切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音盒。【說文】合口也。</p><p>又【玉篇】同也。【易&middot;乾卦】保合太和。【詩&middot;小雅】妻子好合。</p><p>又配也。【詩&middot;大雅】天作之合。【前漢&middot;貨殖傳】蘗麴鹽豉千合。【註】師古曰：蘗麴以斤石稱之，輕重齊則爲合。鹽豉以斗斛量之，多少等亦爲合。合者，相配耦之言耳。</p><p>又會也。【禮&middot;王制】不能五十里者，不合于天子。【註】合，會也。</p><p>又聚也。【論語】始有曰：苟合矣。【註】合，聚也。</p><p>又答也。【左傳&middot;宣二年】旣合而來奔。【註】合，答也。</p><p>又閉也。【前漢&middot;兒寬傳】封禪告成，合祛于天地神明。【註】李奇曰：祛，開散。合，閉也。</p><p>又六合。【莊子&middot;齊物論】六合之外，聖人存而不論。【梁元帝&middot;纂要】天地四方曰六合。</p><p>又黍名。【禮&middot;曲禮】黍曰薌合。</p><p>又【文中子&middot;問易篇】黃帝有合宮之聽。</p><p>又合黎，地名。【書&middot;禹貢】導弱水，至于合黎，餘波入于流沙。</p><p>又州名。【廣韻】秦爲巴郡，宋爲宕渠郡，後魏置合州，蓋涪漢二水合流之處，因以名之。</p><p>又參合，代地。【史記&middot;韓王信傳】入居參合。</p><p>又鉅合在平原，合騎在高城，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>漢侯國。見【史記&middot;建元以來王子侯者年表】。</p><p>又重合縣屬勃海郡，合鄕縣屬東海郡。</p><p>又合浦，郡名。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>見【前漢&middot;地理志】。</p><p>又【廣韻】器名。【正韻】合子，盛物器。</p><p>又【廣韻】姓也。【左傳】宋大夫合左師。</p><p>又子合，西域國名。【前漢&middot;西域傳】西夜國王，號子合王。【後漢&middot;西域傳】子合國，去疏勒千里。</p><p>又通作闔。【戰國策】意者，臣愚而不闔於王心耶。【註】闔合同。</p><p>又與郃通。【史記&middot;魏世家】築雒隂合陽。【註】郃水之北。</p><p>又【高祖功臣侯表】高祖兄仲廢爲合陽侯。</p><p>又【唐韻】【正韻】古沓切【集韻】【韻會】葛合切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音閤。【廣韻】合，集也。</p><p>又【集韻】兩龠爲合。【前漢&middot;律歷志】量者，龠合升斗斛也，所以量多少也。</p><p>又合龠爲合，十合爲升。【註】合龠一作十龠，又合者，合龠之量也。</p><p>又叶許及切，音翕。【張衡&middot;思<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>賦】何孤行之焭焭兮，孑不羣而介立。感鸞鷖之特棲兮，悲淑人之希合。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
