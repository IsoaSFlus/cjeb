                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">村</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>村</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">邨</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄨㄣ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>乡村；村庄：～子。～塾（旧时农村中的私塾）。～民。</li>	<li>粗野；粗俗：～野。～俗。～话。～气。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> village, hamlet; uncouth, vulgar</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dorf</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">村</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>村</strong></p>
<p>邨 <span class="dicpy">cūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从邑，屯声。本义:村庄)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[village;hamlet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邨，地名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐铉</span>曰:&ldquo;今俗作村。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">村中闻有此人，咸来问讯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">僵卧孤村不自哀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《十一月四日风雨大作》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">环村居者皆猎户。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:村坊(村庄);村疃(村庄);村上(村子里);村井(村庄);村公(村中长者);村老(村中父老);村戍(村里守卫之所);村垣(村庄围墙)。又指农村基层组织。如:村正(村司、村吏、村胥，都指村长);村伍(村户);村邑(村镇。引申指故里);村郭(村镇);村范(村规)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 乡村，乡下，农村 <span class="encs">[country]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂无山歌与村笛，呕哑嘲哳难为听。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:村田乐(宋元明时表现农村生活的民间舞蹈);村泊(村野，乡村);村务(乡村小酒店;农家);村野匹夫(身居乡野的鄙陋无知之人);村醪浊酒(乡下人自家酿的酒);村姑(农村少女)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>村</strong> <span class="dicpy">cūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 粗俗;土气 <span class="encs">[vulgar;rustic]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:村蛮(粗野);村獠(粗笨的人);村夫(乡下人);村言(粗俗的话);村妆(粗俗的打扮);村纣(粗野，粗俗);村顽(粗野，愚蠢);村性(野性，粗野的脾气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 蠢，傻 <span class="encs">[stupid;foolish]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:村势煞(傻相，蠢样);村沙样势(村村势势。土头土脑，傻里傻气);村厮(傻瓜，乡巴佬);村愚(愚蠢，粗笨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 朴实 <span class="encs">[simple]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:村憨(朴实憨厚);村朴(朴实)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>村</strong> <span class="dicpy">cūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>冒犯，用不好听的话伤人 <span class="encs">[offend]</span></p>
