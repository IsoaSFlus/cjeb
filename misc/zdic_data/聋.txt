                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">聋</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>聋</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">聾</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lóng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lóng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄥˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 耳听不见声音：～子。耳～。发～振聩（发出的声音很大，使耳聋人也能听见；喻用语言文字唤醒糊涂的人，使他们清醒。亦作“振聋发聩”）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> deaf</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gehörlosigkeit  (S, Med)​,gehörlos, taub, hörbehindert  (Adj, Med)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sourd</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">聋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>聋</strong></p><p>聾 <span class="dicpy">lóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耳，龙声。本义:丧失听觉能力)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[deaf;be hard of hearing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聋，无闻也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聋者无以与乎钟鼓之声。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">耳不听五声之和为聋，目不别五色之章为昧。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:聋聩(耳聋或天生的聋子。比喻愚昧无知，不明事理);聋昧(耳聋目盲);聋瞢(聋盲)。又指聋子</p><p><span class="diczx1">发瞽披聋。——</span><span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 愚昧;不明事理 <span class="encs">[stupid]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郑</span>昭， <span class="diczx2">宋</span>聋。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十四年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:聋言(比喻遮蔽，欺骗);聋俗(比喻愚昧无知的流俗，就如有耳而无闻);聋昧(比喻迟钝、愚昧无知)。又指不明事理的人</p><p><span class="diczx1">一兴异论，群聋和之。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答曾公立书》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/聋哑">聋哑</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/聋子">聋子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/807E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】盧紅切【集韻】【正韻】盧東切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音籠。【說文】無聞也。【釋名】籠也。如在蒙籠之內，不可察也。【左傳·僖二十六年】耳不聽五聲之和爲聾。</p><p>又【宣十四年】鄭昭宋聾。【註】聾，闇也。【禮·王制】瘖聾跛躃斷者。【疏】聾謂耳不聞聲。</p><p>又葱聾，獸名。【山海經】符禺之山，其獸多蔥聾，其狀如羊而赤鬛。【註】葱聾，如羊，黑首赤鬛。</p><p>又【韻補】叶盧黃切，音郞。【易林】遠視無光，不知靑黃。黈纊塞耳，使君闇聾。</p><p>
