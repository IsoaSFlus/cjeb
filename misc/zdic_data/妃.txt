                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">妃</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>妃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fēi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄟˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>帝王的妾，位次于皇后；亦指太子、王、侯的妻：～子。～嫔。</li>
	<li>对神女的尊称：天～。宓～。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/绯">绯</a>&rdquo;，粉红色。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>妃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/配">配</a>&rdquo;，婚配。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wife, spouse; imperial concubine</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> (königl. od. kaiserl.)​ Konkubine  (S)​,Ehefrau (eines Fürsten oder Prinzen)​  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> concubine impériale,princesse</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">妃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>妃</strong> <span class="dicpy">fēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从女，己声。本义:婚配，配偶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;配&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> )。同本义 <span class="encs">[spouse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妃，匹也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嘉耦曰妃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故</span><span class="diczx2">黄帝</span>作君臣上下之义，父子兄弟之礼，夫妇妃匹之合。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;画策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:吉妃(美满的婚配)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指妻子 <span class="encs">[wife]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以某妃配某氏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;少牢礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;某妃，某妻也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">嫘祖</span>为 <span class="diczx2">黄帝</span>正妃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 帝王的妾 <span class="encs">[imperial concubine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宿有妃，嫱嫔御焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妃妾(皇帝的妾);妃嫔(帝王的妾侍。妃，位次于后;嫔，位又次于妃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 特指太子、王侯之妻 <span class="encs">[the wife of the Crown Prince]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子之妃曰后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;配也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皇帝纳皇后。&hellip;皇太子纳妃。&hellip;亲王纳妃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 女神的尊称 <span class="encs">[the honorific title of goddess]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从南</span><span class="diczx2">湘</span>之二妃。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">三国</span> <span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 某一事物的对立面。因其既相反又相成，故称 <span class="encs">[opposite]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水，火妃也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">妃</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>妃</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄈㄟ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>fēi</rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">配偶。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;桓公二年》：「嘉耦曰<b>妃</b>，怨耦曰仇，古之命也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">稱古代天子的配偶，且地位僅次后者為「妃」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「貴<b>妃</b>」、「妃子」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">太子或諸侯的配偶。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「王<b>妃</b>」、「太子<b>妃</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;哀公元年》：「今聞夫差，次有臺榭陂池焉，宿有<b>妃</b>嬙嬪御焉。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷一八&middot;禮樂志八》：「皇太子納<b>妃</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對女神的尊稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「天<b>妃</b>」、「湘<b>妃</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辭&middot;劉向&middot;九歎&middot;愍命》：「逐下袟于後堂兮，迎宓<b>妃</b>于伊雒。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;王逸&middot;注：「宓<b>妃</b>神女，蓋伊洛水之精也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
<div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>妃</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄆㄟˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>p&egrave;i</rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">婚配。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;文公十四年》：「子叔姬<b>妃</b>齊昭公，生舍。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;陸德明《經典釋文&middot;卷一六&middot;春秋左氏音義之二》：「<b>妃</b>，音配，本亦作配。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5983.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】芳非切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】芳微切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音霏。【說文】匹也。【總要】女與已身儔也。古<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/218D5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡣕</span>御之貴，次于后者曰妃。【禮&middot;檀弓】舜葬於蒼梧之野，蓋三妃未之從也。</p>
<p>又太子之嫡室亦曰妃。</p>
<p>又天妃，水神。【司馬光曰】水隂類，其神當爲女子。</p>
<p>又湘妃，竹名。其斑如淚痕，出古辢者佳，出陶虛山中次之。見【竹譜】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】盈之切，音怡。與姬同。衆妾總稱。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>滂佩切，音配。【禮&middot;曲禮】天子之妃曰后。【註】妃，配也。【衛風&middot;氓詩序】喪其妃耦。【前漢&middot;呂后紀】高祖微時妃。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>與配同。　或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2175E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡝞</span>。別作婜。</p>
<p>又與婓通。詳婓字註。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5983_1202.svg" /><span class="swbf">(妃)</span><span class="ywyy">匹也。</span><span class="swdz">匹者，四丈也。禮記：納幣一束，束五兩，兩五尋。注云：十箇爲束。兩兩合其卷，是謂五兩。八尺曰尋。按四丈而兩之，各得二丈。夫婦之片合如帛之判合矣。故帛四丈曰兩，曰匹。人之配耦亦曰匹。妃本上下通偁，後人以爲貴偁耳。釋詁曰：妃，媲也。引申爲凡相耦之偁。左傳曰：嘉耦曰妃。其字亦叚配爲之。太<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2175E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡝞</span>，其云<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2175E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡝞</span>㐜者，卽左傳之嘉耦曰妃，怨耦曰仇也。</span><span class="ywyy">从女己。</span><span class="swdz">各本下有聲字，今<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206F9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛹</span>。此會意字，以女儷己也。芳非切。十五部。</span></p>                          </div>
