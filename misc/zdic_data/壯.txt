						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>壯</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuàng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄤˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 见“<span class="diczx3">壮</span>”。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> big, large; robust; name of tribe</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> stark, kräftig, robust</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> robuste,vigoureux,fort,renforcer,fortifier,grand,grandiose</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>壮</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄓㄨㄤˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>zhuàng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4595.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">强盛、强健。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷一一一·卫将军骠骑传》：「右贤王惊，夜逃，独与其爱妾一人、<b>壮</b>骑数百驰，溃围北去。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">雄豪。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「壮志」、「豪言壮语」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷六五·东方朔传》：「拔剑割肉，壹何<b>壮</b>也！」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">扩大。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「壮大声势」、「借酒壮胆」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">赞许、钦服。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·韩愈〈新修滕王阁记〉：「及得三王所为序赋记等，<b>壮</b>其文辞，益欲往一观而读之，以忘吾忧。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人到三、四十岁的时期称为「<b><b>壮</b></b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《礼记·曲礼上》：「人生十年曰幼学；二十曰弱冠；三十曰<b>壮</b>，有室。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">农历八月的别名。参见「<a href='/hans/壮月' target=_blank>壮月</a> 」条。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋时晋国有<b><b>壮</b></b>驰兹。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
