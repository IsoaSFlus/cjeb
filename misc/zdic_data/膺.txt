                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>膺</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>胸：义愤填～。</li>
	<li>接受，承当：～选（当选）。～赏。～受。荣～。</li>
	<li>讨伐，打击：～惩。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> breast, chest; undertake, bear</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> entgegennehmen, erhalten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> poitrine,recevoir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">膺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>膺</strong> <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉。本义:胸腔，胸)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">膺，胸也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无搯膺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">背膺拌合以交痛兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;惜诵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">编愁苦以为膺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;悲回风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无拊膺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语&middot;子夏问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旧事填膺，思之凄梗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:膺门(马的胸膛);膺臆(胸臆。多指个人的思想或见解);义愤填膺</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 乳上骨 <span class="encs">[upper breastbone]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 胸傍，胸部两侧的肌肉隆起处 <span class="encs">[chest sides]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有病膺肿颈痛胸满腹胀，此为何病?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:膺中(胸前两旁高处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 心间;胸臆 <span class="encs">[heart]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">回</span>之为人也，择乎中庸，得一善，则拳拳服膺而弗失之矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:膺服(谨记在心;衷心信服);膺肺(内心);膺堂(心间)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>膺</strong> <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 担当，接受重任 <span class="encs">[bear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣有何德何能，敢膺圣眷如此!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:膺天明命(承受天命);膺期(承受期运。指受天命为帝王);膺运(膺期);膺荐(承受荐举)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 因被任命、提升或被选举而担任<span class="encs">[某职]</span> <span class="encs">[hold]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:膺此重任</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 接受 <span class="encs">[receive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子受四海之图籍，膺万国之贡珍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:膺命(接受天命)</span></p>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>膺</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄧㄥ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yīng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/6189.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">胸、內心。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「義憤填<b>膺</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈蜀道難〉：「捫參歷井仰脅息，以手撫<b>膺</b>坐長歎。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷一○○&middot;封倫傳》：「然善矯飾，居之自如，人莫能探其<b>膺</b>肺。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">馬匹胸前的帶子。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;秦風&middot;小戎》：「蒙伐有苑，虎韔鏤<b>膺</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;毛亨&middot;傳：「<b>膺</b>，馬帶。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「纓」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">接受。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;班固&middot;東京賦》：「天子受四海之圖籍，<b>膺</b>萬國之貢珍。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">承擔。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「榮<b>膺</b>大任」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;武成》：「誕<b>膺</b>天命，以撫方夏。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">膺</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下" target="_blank">未集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下&amp;bs=肉" target="_blank">肉</a>】
                              膺·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=19" target="_blank">19</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/81BA.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/268BB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦢻</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>於陵切，音應。【說文】胷也。【史記&middot;趙世家】大膺，大胷，修下而馮。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】親也。【禮&middot;少儀】執箕膺擖。【註】膺，親也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】當也。【書&middot;武成】誕膺天命，以撫方夏。【傳】膺，當也。</p>
<p>又受也。【楚辭&middot;天問】撰體協脅。鹿何膺之。【註】膺，受也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】馬帶也。【詩&middot;秦風】虎韔鏤膺。【傳】膺，馬帶也。</p>
<p>又【大雅】鉤膺濯濯。【傳】鉤膺，樊纓也。</p>
<p>又【增韻】擊也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】於證切，英去聲。胷也。【禮&middot;中庸】得一善，則拳拳服膺，而弗失之矣。【釋文】徐邈讀。</p>
<p>又【釋名】膺，壅也。氣所壅塞也。【正字通】黃庭經，舌下<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>膺生死岸。陶弘景讀壅。</p>
