						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>豺</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chái  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chái.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄞˊ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 哺乳动物，贪食，残暴，常成群侵袭家畜。分布于中国及俄罗斯西伯利亚、印度尼西亚等地（亦称“豺狗”）：～狼。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>豺</strong> <span class="dicpy">chái</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chái.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从豸( <span class="dicpy">zhì</span>)，才声。从<span class="yhcs">“豸”</span>，表示与野兽有关。本义:野兽名。形似犬而残猛如狼，俗名豺狗) 同本义 <span class="encs">[jackal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豺，狼属，狗声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作犲。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豺，狗足。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豺似狗，白色，有爪牙。迅捷，善搏噬也。——<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇·解诂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豺则祭兽。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·季秋》</span>。注:“似狗而长毛，其色黄。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏豺狼。——<span class="smcs">《史记·司马相如传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">曹</span>公，豺虎也。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 按，一种野犬，比狼小，毛色较黄，胆子也较狼小，有时夜间成群猎食，但多半是单独或成对出猎，主要吃腐肉或小动物(如家禽)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/豺狼">豺狼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/豺狼当道">豺狼当道</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
