                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>習</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">x&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>鳥類頻頻練飛。《説文&bull;習部》：&ldquo;習，數飛也。&rdquo;《禮記&bull;月令》：&ldquo;鷹乃學習，腐草為螢。&rdquo;</li>	<li>學習。《禮記&bull;學記》：&ldquo;五年視博習親師，七年視論學取友。&rdquo;</li>	<li>複習；溫習。《論語&bull;學而》：&ldquo;學而時習之。&rdquo;</li>	<li>累積；重疊。《易&bull;坎》：&ldquo;習坎，入于坎。&rdquo;</li>	<li>熟悉；通曉。《國語&bull;周語上》：&ldquo;是皆習民數者也。&rdquo;</li>	<li>習慣；習染。《商君書&bull;慎法》：&ldquo;見訾言無損，習相憎，不相害也。&rdquo;</li>	<li>近習；親信。《韓非子&bull;孤憤》：&ldquo;凡當塗者之於人主也，希不信愛也，又且習故。&rdquo;</li>	<li>教習；訓練。《吴子&bull;治兵》：&ldquo;習其馳逐，閑其進止，人馬相親，然後可使。&rdquo;</li>	<li>作為。《易&bull;坤》：&ldquo;直方大，不習无不利。&rdquo;</li>	<li>調節。《大戴禮記&bull;子張問入官》：&ldquo;既知其以生有習，然后民特從命也。&rdquo;</li>	<li>副詞。常常、經常。《漢書&bull;董仲舒傳》：&ldquo;習聞其號，未燭厥理。&rdquo;</li>	<li>姓。《廣韻&bull;緝韻》：&ldquo;習，姓。出襄陽。晋有習鑿齒。&rdquo;</li>	<li>通&ldquo;<a href="/hans/襲">襲</a>&rdquo;。相因；重複。《書&bull;大禹謨》：&ldquo;龜筮協從，卜不習吉。&rdquo;</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">習</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>習</strong></p>
<p>習 <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">動</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (會意。从羽 。从羽，與鳥飛有關。本義:小鳥反覆地試飛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本義 <span class="encs">[fly frequently]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">習，數飛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《說文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鷹乃學習。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《禮記&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">習習籠中鳥，舉翮觸四隅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《詠史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 反覆練習，鑽研 <span class="encs">[review;practise;exercise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子以朋友講習。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;象下傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是皆習民數者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《國 語&middot;周語》</span>。注;&ldquo;習，簡習也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">學而時習之，不亦說乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《論語&middot;學而》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇侃</span>義疏:&ldquo;習是修故之稱也。言人不學則已，既學必因仍而修習，日夜無替也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:習容(練習舉止，整飭儀表);習肄(猶練習);習戰(練習作戰);習非勝是(對錯誤的事物相習既久，無法矯正，反以爲是)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通曉，熟悉 <span class="encs">[become familiar with;be used to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不習於誦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《戰國策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">誰習計會，能爲文收責於</span><span class="diczx2">薛</span>者乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《戰國策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆通習之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韓愈</span><span class="smcs">《師說》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">益習其聲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不習水戰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《資治通鑑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">習見習聞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黃宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">習方俗</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:習流(熟悉水流。又指熟悉水戰的兵士);習士(熟悉法制的人);習事(熟諳事理);習知(熟知);習水(熟習水性)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 學習 <span class="encs">[learn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">始習於大豆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《呂氏春秋&middot;造父》</span>。注:&ldquo;學也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">習其句讀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韓愈</span><span class="smcs">《師說》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自幼好武術，習無不精。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗類鈔&middot;戰事類》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">不習水土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《資治通鑑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家人習奢。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">習於鐘鼓。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">蘇軾</span><span class="smcs">《教戰守》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
