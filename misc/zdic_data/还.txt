                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>还</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">還</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>回到原处或恢复原状：～乡。～俗。衣锦～乡。返老～童。</li>	<li>回报别人对自己的行动。～手。～击。以眼～眼。以牙～牙。</li>	<li>偿付：归～。偿～。～本。原物奉～。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/环">环</a>&rdquo;，环绕。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>还</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">還</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄞˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>依然，仍然：这本书～没有看完。</li>	<li>更加：今天比昨天～冷。</li>	<li>再，又：锻炼身体，～要注意休息。</li>	<li>尚，勉强过得去：身体～好。</li>	<li>尚且：他～搬不动，何况我呢？</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">还</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>还</strong></p>
<p>還 <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &mdash;&mdash;表示某些语气，有时兼有连接前后分句的作用。&ldquo;还&rdquo;表示的语气大体上可以分成平、扬、抑三类</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示平的语气，不含轻重抑扬的意思</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示行为动作或状况不变，跟&ldquo;仍旧、依然&rdquo;相当 <span class="encs">[still;yet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白头还戍边。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">取酒还独倾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还在原位;我的脚还痛</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 跟&ldquo;虽然、尽管&rdquo;等连用，表示行为状况不因别的条件改变而改变。如:虽然已经宣布散会，人们还在继续争论(表示行为重复，有&ldquo;重新、再&rdquo;的意思。如:京剧团明天还演一场)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表示扬的语气，把事情往大里、高里、重里说</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 更加 <span class="encs">[even more]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如: 二勇比他哥哥大勇还壮</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 表示项目、数量增加，范围扩大 <span class="encs">[as well]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:除了他们三个以外，小组里还有我</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 不但(不仅，不光)&hellip;还&hellip; <span class="encs">[also]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:小伙子不仅会开拖拉机，还会修理电器</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 表示抑的语气，把事情往小里、低里、轻里说</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 表示勉强过得去 <span class="encs">[fairly]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这根绳子还较结实;还算不错，电话最后打通了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 表示数量小，时间不到，等等 <span class="encs">[still]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:人还太少，编不成队</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 还&hellip;就&hellip; <span class="encs">[only]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:还不过五点钟，他就已经起床了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 还+没(不到)&hellip;就&hellip; <span class="encs">[yet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我还没说话，他就说&ldquo;知道了&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 尚且 <span class="encs">[even]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:小车还通不过，更别提大车了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 表示感情为主，意思有的可以用前面三项来解释，但那是次要的</p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 表示超出预料，有赞叹的语气 <span class="encs">[notwithstanding]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:下这么大雨，没想到你还真准时到了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 表示应该怎样而不怎样，名不副实，有责备或讥讽的语气 <span class="encs">[unworth of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:亏你还是大哥哥呢，也不让着点妹妹!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(18)</span> 用于反问 <span class="encs">[why]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:都十二点了，你还说早!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(19)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>还</strong></p>
<p>還 <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&oacute;</span>，表示与行走有关)，瞏( <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:返回)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[come back;go back;return]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还，復也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还，返也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还而不入。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;何人斯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾其还也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">势还不远，可先为起第。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既罢，还内。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还部白府君。(回到府里报告太守。府君，太守。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">送儿还故乡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">便要还家。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复还终业。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自南海还。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还首(自动回来请罪);还云(飘回的云朵;指回信);还函(回信);还转(回转，返回);还复(返回)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 回头;回顾 <span class="encs">[turn around;turn round]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">羽</span>还叱之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;项籍传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视无还。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还顾(回顾;回头看);还睇(回头看);还视(回头看)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;环&rdquo;。环绕 <span class="encs">[surround]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左还授师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还辟再拜稽首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将还玩</span><span class="diczx2">吴国</span>于股掌之上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span>。注:&ldquo;还，转也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">引车右还。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道还公宫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸侯之师，还</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>而来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还庐树桑，菜茹有畦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三人还射。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;李将军传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还柱而走。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">方还柱走。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以地还之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水潦所还，埒丘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释丘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还绕(环绕);还会(交互缠绕的样子);还旋(环绕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 恢复;还原 <span class="encs">[recover;restore]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吏以过误罢者还其职。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;太祖本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还惺(歇息一会而恢复精力);还质(恢复丽质);还睦(恢复和睦);还童(恢复青春);还级(恢复到原等级)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 来，到来 <span class="encs">[come;arrive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">舜</span><span class="diczx2">禹</span>还至，王业还起。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王霸》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:春还(春天到来;春天来了);时还(时来运转)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 交还;归还 <span class="encs">[give back;return]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当奉还土地民人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴主传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还矢先王。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新五代史&middot;伶官传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">率夺还之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还璧(归还玉璧;归还原物或辞谢馈赠之物);还珠(物归原地与原主);还归(归还)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 偿还;交付 <span class="encs">[give sth. in return;repay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">割慈忍爱还租庸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《岁晏行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还付(归还交付);还偿(偿还);还款(归还所欠的债款或偿付所欠的贷款);还直(偿还价值)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 回报;回击 <span class="encs">[repay;fight back]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:还报(还手，报复;报应;报答);还兵(用兵器还击);还骂(挨骂者回骂对方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 后退 <span class="encs">[draw back;fall back]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主人答拜还。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡饮酒礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还走(返身后退而走);还流(回流;回旋或倒流之水)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 罢歇;止息 <span class="encs">[pause;stop]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:雨还(雨止);流弊不还(流弊不止息)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 包;保证 <span class="encs">[guarantee]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:还你名实兼收(包你名利双收);还有雨下(保证有雨下)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 通&ldquo;营&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 经营;谋画 <span class="encs">[administer;manage;plan;scheme]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">内则大夫自还而不尽忠，外则诸侯连朋合舆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;山至数》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还秩(谋求私利);还私(谋私)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 惑乱 <span class="encs">[in disorder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国多私，比周还主党与施。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;成相》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">周环中规，折还中矩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:还主(惑乱君主)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">子孙日以长，世世还复然。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《田家》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">鬼魅不能自还兮，半长途而下颠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传上》</span></span></p>
