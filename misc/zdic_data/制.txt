                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">制</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>制</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">➍<span class="diczx3">製</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>规定：因地～宜。～定。～式。～宪。</li>
	<li>限定，约束，管束：～止。～裁。专～。～约。抵～。节～。～动。～海权。</li>
	<li>法规，制度：民主集中～。公有～。</li>
	<li>造，作：～造。～做。～品。～图。～革。～版。如法炮（<span class="dicpy">p&aacute;o </span>）～。</li>
	<li>依照规定的标准做的：～钱（中国明、清两代称本朝的铜钱）。～服。</li>
	<li>古代帝王的命令：～诰。</li>
	<li>古代父母死亡守丧；守～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">制</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>制</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 制、製本同一词，后来分化，&ldquo;製&rdquo;用于具体的制 造，&ldquo;制&rdquo;且于抽象的制作。(会意。小篆字形，左边是&ldquo;未&rdquo;，右为刀。&ldquo;未&rdquo;即有滋味，乐于裁制。本义:裁断;制作)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cut;make]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">制，裁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贤主之用人也，犹巧工之制木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;主术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">制彼裳衣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;东山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可使制梃，以挞</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>之坚甲利兵矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:制割(裁剪切割);制义(制宜，裁断适宜);轧制;机制;仿制;自制;试制;研制(研究制造);配制;提制(提炼制造);精制;中国制</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 制定，用文字规定 <span class="encs">[formulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泽上有水，节，君子以制数度，议德行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;节》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今也，制民之产。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故王者之制法，昭乎如日月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;刑德》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周</span>之王也，制礼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公七年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:制宜(谓区别不同的情况而制定适宜的方式方法);制则(制定法度);制立(建立制度);制改(改制。谓制定典章革除弊端)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 控制;制服 <span class="encs">[overpower;control]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水因地而制流，兵因敌而制胜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;虚实》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从天而颂之，孰与制天命而用之?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">威制豪强。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵广汉传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">制其兵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">制六合。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>有余力而制其弊。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受制于人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自制足之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:制屈(制伏;使屈服);制地(控制土地);制驭(控制驾驭);制御(统治，控制)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 抑制，限制，忍住 <span class="encs">[control;restrict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣服有制，饮食有节，则出寡矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;画策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:制劫(牵制);制使(制约);制遏(抑制);制约(限制约束);制限(限制);制缚(制约束缚);制防(限制防范)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 主管 <span class="encs">[be in charge of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:制兵(统辖军队);制局(职掌内府器杖兵役的机构)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;质&rdquo;。抵押<span class="encs">[mortgage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔不谷先委制于</span><span class="diczx2">越</span>君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5236_467.svg" /><span class="swbf">(制)</span><span class="ywyy">裁也。</span><span class="swdz">衣部曰：裁，製衣也。製，裁衣也。此裁之本義。此云制，裁也。裁之引伸之義。古多假折爲制。吕㓝：制以㓝。墨子引作折則㓝。魯論：片言可以制獄。古作折獄。羽獵賦：不制中以泉臺。制或爲折。又吕㓝：折民惟㓝。四八目引作制民。</span><span class="ywyy">从刀未。</span><span class="swdz">㑹意。征例切。十五部。</span><span class="ywyy">未，物成有滋味可裁<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃔</span>。</span><span class="swdz">說從未之意。未下曰味也。有滋味可裁<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃔</span>，故刀、未爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206D0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛐</span>。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰止也。</span><span class="swdz">前義可包此義。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
