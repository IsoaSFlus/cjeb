                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>卯</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">mǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>地支的第四位，属兔。</li>
	<li>用于记时：～时（早晨五点至七点）。～正（早晨六点）。点～。画～。</li>
	<li>器物接榫的地方凹入的部分：～眼。～榫。</li>
	<li>期限：比～（中国清代催征钱粮，分期追比）。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卯</strong></p>
<p>夘 <span class="dicpy">mǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。象两扇门打开之形。本义:门开着)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 地支的第四位 <span class="encs">[number four of the duodecimal cycle]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:子、丑、寅、卯</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 与天干相配，用以纪年。如:卯君(生在卯年的人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用以纪月，即农历二月</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 十二时辰之一，早晨五时至七时。也泛指早晨。如:卯睡(早晨睡觉);卯酉(早晚。卯，卯时，早晨;酉，酉时，傍晚);卯困(谓因吃卯酒而困倦昏睡)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 报到。旧时官署例定在卯时开始办公时，进行点名报到等活动。其点名册叫&ldquo;卯簿&rdquo;，&ldquo;卯册&rdquo;。俗称&ldquo;到一下&rdquo;叫&ldquo;点个卯&rdquo;或&ldquo;打个卯&rdquo; <span class="encs">[roll call]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卯册(即卯簿);卯簿(旧时官署中的名册。点卯时用之，故称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 木器上安榫头的孔眼 <span class="encs">[mortise]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卯笋(即卯榫。比喻两部分的连接处);卯眼</span></p>
