<p><span class="dicpy">yōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄡˉ</span></p>

	<li>微细;微小。《説文&bull;幺部》：&ldquo;𢆶，微也。&rdquo;段玉裁注：&ldquo;二幺者，幺之甚也。&rdquo;《廣韻&bull;尤韻》：&ldquo;𢆶，微小。&rdquo;北周衛元嵩《元包經&bull;孟陰》：&ldquo;俶么𢆶，卒飄飍。&rdquo;李江注：&ldquo;𢆶，微也。&rdquo;</li>
