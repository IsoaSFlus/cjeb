                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>载</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">載</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎi.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄞˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>年；岁：千～难逢。三年两～。</li>    <li>记录；刊登；描绘：记～。连～。转～。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>载</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">載</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zài  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zài.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄞˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>装，用交通工具装：～客。～货。～重。～体。装～。满～而归。</li>    <li>充满：怨声～道。</li>    <li>乃，于是（古文里常用来表示同时做两个动作）：～歌～舞。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>载</strong></p><p>載 <span class="dicpy">zǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 年(唐虞时代用于年名) <span class="encs">[age]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">夏</span>曰岁， <span class="diczx2">商</span>曰祀， <span class="diczx2">周</span>曰年， <span class="diczx2">唐</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>曰载。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释天》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朕在位七十载。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>兴，至 <span class="diczx2">孝文</span>四十有余载。——<span class="smcs">《史记·文帝本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:载祀(年)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>载</strong></p><p>載 <span class="dicpy">zǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"                           <span class="jieshi">椉也。</span>乗者，覆也。上覆之則下載之，故其義相成。引申之謂所載之物曰載，如詩載輪爾載，下載音才再反是也。引申爲凡載物之偁，如詩：汎汎楊舟，載沈載浮。中庸：天地之無不持載。是也。又叚借之爲始，才之叚借也。才者，艸木之初也。夏曰載，亦謂四時終始也。又叚借爲事。詩：上天之載。毛傳曰載，事也是也。又叚爲語䛐。詩載馳載驅。毛傳：載，辭也。春日載陽。箋云：載之言則也。<span class="jieshi">从車<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22992.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢦒</span>聲。</span>作代切。一部。韵會此下有易曰大車以載六字。                          </div>
