data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，盾声。本义:逃避)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[evade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遁，迁也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遁，避也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遁，犹回避也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·杜林传》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阳遁而不能蒸。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平当逡遁有耻。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·隽疏干薛平传赞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:遁隐(避世隐居);遁天(逃遁天理，违背自然);遁命(逃避任命);遁俗(犹言逃避世俗);遁避(犹逃避)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 逃跑，逃走 <span class="encs">[flee;escape]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一曰逃也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晋将遁矣。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敌弃炮仓皇遁。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虎大骇，远遁。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:遁世离群(逃离人世，过隐居生活);遁亡(逃亡);遁退(逃跑退却);遁奔(逃奔)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 迁移 <span class="encs">[move]</span>。如:遁思(迁移;离去)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 隐匿 <span class="encs">[hide]</span>。如:遁疚(隐藏的疾病);遁隐(隐藏);遁影(隐藏形影);遁疾(隐藏的疾病)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 隐避;隐居 <span class="encs">[live in seclusion]</span>。如:遁夫(隐居之人);遁身(隐居);遁居(隐居)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 放纵;淫逸 <span class="encs">[loose;wanton]</span>。如:遁乐(淫纵逸乐);遁逸(放纵;不可约束)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 失 <span class="encs">[lose]</span>。如:遁逸(散失);遁舍(犹舍弃)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 欺 <span
