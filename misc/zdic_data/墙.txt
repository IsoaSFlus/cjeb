						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>墙</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">墻</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiáng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiáng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>用砖石等砌成承架房顶或隔开内外的建筑物：砖～。土～。院～。城～。～垣。～头。～头草。铜～铁壁。</li><li>门屏：萧～（喻内部，如“祸起～～”）。</li><li>古代出殡时张于棺材周围的帏帐。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>墙</strong></p><p>墻、牆 <span class="dicpy">qiáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从啬，从土。<span class="yhcs">“啬”</span>有节俭收藏的意思。垒土为墙，意在收藏。本义:房屋或园场周围的障壁)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">墙，垣蔽也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无逾我墙。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·将仲子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粪土之墙不可圬也。——<span class="smcs">《论语·公冶长》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">止如堵墙。——<span class="smcs">《尉缭子·战威》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">老翁逾墙走。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span>诗</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:墙阙(院落;门庭);墙宇(房舍，住宅;喻指人的气度);墙篱(藩篱，篱笆);墙东(比喻隐居不仕，以避乱世);墙茨(墙上生长蒺藜，意欲清除，又恐墙坏家毁。比喻阖门淫乱，家丑难除);土墙;围墙</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 门屏 <span class="encs">[screen]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而在萧墙之内也。——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span>。按:萧墙之内，指国君塞门之内，喻内忧</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:萧墙(古代宫室内当门的小墙)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代出殡时柩车上覆棺的装饰性帷幔 <span class="encs">[curtain]</span>。如:墙合(即官悬);墙柳(出殡时覆盖在柩车上的装饰性帷幔)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
