                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>注</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>灌进去：～入。～射。大雨如～。</li>
	<li>（精神、力量）集中在一点：～视。～目。～意。</li>
	<li>用文字来解释词名：～解。～释。～音。夹～。</li>
	<li>解释词句所用的文字：～疏（注解和解释注解的文字的合称）。</li>
	<li>记载，登记：～册。～销。</li>
	<li>赌博时所下金钱财物：下～。赌～。</li>
	<li>量词，多用于款项或交易：一～钱。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> concentrate, focus, direct</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gießen, Anmerkung  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> note,commentaire,annoter,commenter,enjeu,mise,verser,fixer,concentrer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">注</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>注</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，主声。本义:灌入，注入)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pour]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">注，灌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">振盪注流。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丰水东注。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;文王有声》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">声如震雷破山，泪如倾河注海。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;言语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而泽下注。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大雨如注。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">暴雨下如注，水潦成流。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《东观汉记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">解州</span><span class="diczx2">盐泽</span>，方百二十里，久雨，四山之水悉注其中。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:把酒注到瓶里;注溉(灌溉);注萤(以水浇灌萤火);注集(流泻汇集);注溜(倾泻的水流)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 集中;聚集 <span class="encs">[concentrate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及弊田，令禽注于虞中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;兽人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">谢</span>注神倾意，不觉流汗交面。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;文学》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:注心(集中心意，专心);注耳(倾耳);注仰(抬头注视);注盼(凝视);注神(凝神);注眼(集中目光看);注眸(凝眸);注睇(凝眸斜视)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 解释古书原文意义为&ldquo;注&rdquo;(解释前人注文的意义为&ldquo;疏&rdquo;) <span class="encs">[note]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其有明圣人之道，尽六经之意，而不能诵疏与注，一切弃之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳冕</span><span class="smcs">《与权德舆书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">景纯</span>注<span class="smcs">《雅》</span>，动植必赞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙&middot;颂赞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>欲注<span class="smcs">《春秋传》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;文学》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">注曰&ldquo;不好&rdquo;，改为&ldquo;过&rdquo;。(注曰，在旁边注明。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪迈</span><span class="smcs">《容斋续笔》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:注本(带有注释的版本);注述(注释);注训(注解);注家(注解古籍的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 投;击 <span class="encs">[throw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以瓦注者巧，以钩注者惮，以黄金注者昏&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;达生》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 连为一个整体;连接 <span class="encs">[join]</span></p>
