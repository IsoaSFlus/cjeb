                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>揠</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，匽( <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n</span>)声。本义:拔起)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tug upward]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">揠，拔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拔心曰揠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅&middot;广物》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>人有闵其苗之不长而揠之者，芒芒然归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:揠苗助长</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 提拔 <span class="encs">[promote]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">德</span>与 <span class="diczx2">琼</span>素不相下，一旦揠之在上，则必争。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋书&middot;岳飞传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抓着马鞍 <span class="encs">[hold the saddle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀得</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span>盔斜发乱，发甲捶胸，揠鞍吐血。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></p>
