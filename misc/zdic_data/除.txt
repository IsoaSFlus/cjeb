                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>除</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chú.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>去掉：～害。～名。～根。铲～。废～。排～。～暴安良。</li><li>改变，变换：岁～（农历一年的最后一天）。～夕。</li><li>不计算在内：～非。～外。</li><li>算术中用一个数去分另一个数，是“<span class="diczx3">乘</span>”的反运算：～法。</li><li>台阶：阶～。庭～。</li><li>任命官职：～拜（授官）。～授。～书（授官的诏令）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>除</strong> <span class="dicpy">chú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜，余声。从<span class="yhcs">“阜”</span>，表示与地形地势的高低上下有关。本义:宫殿的台阶)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[steps of palace]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">除，殿阶也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，阶级如山石之高下，故从阜。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自前殿南下椒除。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">登自东除。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苔痕上除绿，草色入帘青。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 一般建筑的台阶 <span class="encs">[steps leading to a house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扶辇下除，触柱折辕。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指台阶 <span class="encs">[step]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">听事前除，雨后犹湿。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·政事》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">除谓之阶。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">李诫</span><span class="smcs">《营造法式》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>除</strong> <span class="dicpy">chú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 拜受官位 <span class="encs">[confer a official]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初除之官。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·景帝纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“凡言除者，除故官就新官也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予除右丞相兼柩密使，都督诸路军马。——</span><span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:除授(授予<span class="encs">[官职]</span>);除召(为任官而被召);除官(授官，除去旧职以任新职)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 清除，去掉 <span class="encs">[eliminate;get rid of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">除恶务本。——<span class="smcs">《书·泰誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然则将军之仇报，而燕国见陵之耻除矣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:根除(彻底铲除);除恶务尽(清除邪恶势力，务必彻底);消除(使不存在，除去)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 修治、修整 <span class="encs">[repair;mend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">郡之贤士大夫请于当道，即除魏阉废祠之址以葬之。——</span><span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 打扫 <span class="encs">[sweep;clean]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">由帅其属而修除。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·典祀》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:除舍(打扫房舍);除宫(打扫宫殿。比喻宫廷更换主人)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 诛灭 <span class="encs">[exterminate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诛乱除害。——<span class="smcs">《史记·秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>
