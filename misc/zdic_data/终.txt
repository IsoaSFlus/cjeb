                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">终</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>终</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">終</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>末了（<span class="dicpy">liǎo </span>），完了（<span class="dicpy">liǎo</span>），与“<span class="diczx3">始</span>”相对：年～。～场（末了一场）。～极。～审（司法部门对案件的最后判决）。～端。靡不有初，鲜克有～（人们做事无不有开头，而很少能坚持到底）。</li><li>从开始到末了：～年。～生。饱食～日。</li><li>人死：临～。送～。</li><li>到底，总归：～归。～究。～于。～将成功。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> end; finally, in the end</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> finalement,en fin de compte,fin,mort,finir,achever</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>终</strong></p><p>終 <span class="dicpy">zhōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，冬声。甲骨文字形，象一束丝，两头象结扎的末端。本义:把丝缠紧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wind silk tightly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终，絿丝也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旋终在项。——<span class="smcs">《睡虎地秦墓竹简》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 终了;结束。与<span class="yhcs">“始”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[end;finish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终，极也;终，穷也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天禄永终。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以永终知敝。——<span class="smcs">《易·象下传》</span>。</span><span
