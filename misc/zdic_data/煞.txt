                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">煞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>煞</strong> <span class="dicpy">shā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;杀&rdquo;。弄死，杀死 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">立性凶暴，多行煞戮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杨衒之</span><span class="smcs">《洛阳伽蓝记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:令杖煞之;打煞人了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 停止;结束 <span class="encs">[stop;halt;check]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">煞住脚往里细听。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:收煞;煞住(止住，收住);煞果(方言。最后;结果);煞后(殿后，走在最后面);煞场(科举考试结束;一出戏结束);煞落(干脆利落);煞年(年终)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 削弱，损坏 <span class="encs">[spoil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此刻的话，很近于大煞风景。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《且介亭杂文二集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:煞水(驱除湿气);煞手锏(杀手锏。喻出奇制胜的手段和方法);煞渴(消渴;解渴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 出气;发泄 <span class="encs">[vent]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:煞火(痛快;过瘾);煞性(索性;干脆);煞性子(出气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 刺痛 <span class="encs">[smart]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:碘酒涂在伤口上真煞得慌</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 使紧或更紧 <span class="encs">[tighten]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:煞腰带</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>煞</strong> <span class="dicpy">shā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 甚么，啥 <span class="encs">[what]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">刘姥姥</span>心中想着:这是什么东西，有煞用处呢?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
