                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>此</strong> <span class="dicpy">cǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从止，从人。甲骨文字形，左边是一只脚(止)，右边是<span class="yhcs">“人”</span>，意思是一只脚踩在别人身上，是<span class="yhcs">“跐”</span>( <span class="dicpy">cǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cǐ.mp3"></a></span> )的本字。①本义:踩，踏。②指示代词，这)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 这;这个 <span class="encs">[this]</span>。表示近指的代词，与<span class="yhcs">“彼”</span>相对</p><p><span class="diczx2">陈</span><span class="diczx2">康肃公</span><span class="diczx2">尧咨</span>善射，当世无双，公亦以此自矜。—— <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《卖油翁》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:此等(这样);此生(这辈子);此地(这里);此个(这个);此家(此人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 表示此时或此地 <span class="encs">[here]</span>。如:由此往南;此外;此发彼应</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 如此，这般 <span class="encs">[so;such]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虎因喜，计之曰:<span class="yhcs">“技止此耳!”</span>——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《黔之驴》</span></p><p><span
