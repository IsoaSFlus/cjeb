                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缦</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>缦</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">縵</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">màn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>没有彩色花纹的丝织品。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">慢</span>”，不经心。</li><li>〔～立〕久立，如“～～远视而望幸焉。”</li><li>〔～～〕a.萦回舒卷的样子；b.沮丧的样子。</li><li>琴弦：操～（拨弄琴弦，指学琴的人调弦听音）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> plain silk; simple, plain</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> bedächtig, langsam</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> soie unie mince,sans fioritures</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缦</strong></p><p>縵 <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，曼声。从糸，与丝织品有关。本义:无花纹的丝织品)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[plain thin silk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缦，绘无文也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庶人衣缦。——<span class="smcs">《春秋·繁露》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘缦不举。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">廊腰缦回。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:缦布(粗朴的布帛);缦帛(没有文采的布帛);缦胡(武士所系的素色无纹的缨带);缦阁(用布帛搭成的楼阁)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 帷幔;帏幕 <span class="encs">[curtain]</span>。如:布缦(布制帷幕)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>缦</strong></p><p>縵 <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“慢”</span>，宽缓;惰慢 <span class="encs">[slow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缦，缓缦。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缦者，窖者、密者。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“宽心也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">礼缓缓兮。——<span class="smcs">《尚书大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 注:<span class="yhcs">“教化广远也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:缦缦(沮丧的样子);坚缦(步履坚定而缓慢)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 没有花纹 <span class="encs">[colourless]</span>。如:缦胡(粗而没有文理的帽带，武士冠缨);缦缨(缦胡);缦胡缨(武士冠缨。亦指武服);缦布(粗布)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E35.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>莫半切，音幔。【說文】繒無文也。漢律曰：賜衣者，縵表白裏。</p><p>又【周禮·春官·巾車】卿乗夏縵。【註】五采畫無瑑。【疏】言縵者，亦如縵帛無文章。【左傳·成五年】絳服乗縵。【註】車無文。</p><p>又【前漢·食貨志】一歲之收，常過縵田。【註】縵田，謂不爲甽者也。</p><p>又【廣韻】謨晏切【集韻】莫晏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音幔。【周禮·春官·磬師】敎縵樂。【註】縵，謂雜聲之和樂者也。【禮·學記】不學操縵，不能安弦。【註】操縵，雜弄弦屬。</p><p>又【莊子·齊物論】縵者，窖者，密者。【音義】縵，寛心也。【又】大恐縵縵。【音義】齊死生貌。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>謨官切，音瞞。縵或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22FDC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢿜</span>。</p><p>又【韻補】叶莫半切，緩縵也。【舜卿雲歌】卿雲爛兮，糺縵縵兮。日月光華，旦復旦兮。</p>
