                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>遘</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，表示与行走有关，冓( <span class="dicpy">gōu</span>)声。本义:遇，遇见)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[encounter;meet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遘，遇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遘厉虐疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;金滕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫何予生之不遘时!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;严忌哀时命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是疾易传染，遘者虽戚属，不敢同卧起。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遘屯(遭遇困难);遘时(逢时，遇到好的机运);遘难(遭遇灾难);遘慝(遭遇灾祸);遘愍(遭遇忧患);遘时(遇到好时机);遘辰(遇到好时机)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;构&rdquo;。构成，造成 <span class="encs">[form;make up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西京乱无象，豺虎方遘患。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王粲</span><span class="smcs">《七哀诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遘祸(构祸，造成灾祸);遘患(作乱);遘扇(构扇。挑拨煽动)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
