                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>讪</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">訕</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>讥笑：～笑。～骂。</li><li>不好意思，难为情的样子：～～。搭～（为把尴尬局面敷衍过去而找话说）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> abuse, slander; vilify; ridicule</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> beschimpfen, sexuell missbrauchen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> médire,ravaler</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>讪</strong></p><p>訕 <span class="dicpy">shàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，山声。本义:毁谤)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[slander]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讪，谤也。与姗略同。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讪，诽毁也。——<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恶居下流而讪上者。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">居下而讪上，处贫而非富。——<span class="smcs">《盐铁论·地广》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有谏而无讪。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·少仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:讪上(毁谤在上位的人);讪议(毁谤议论);讪刺(毁谤讽刺)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 讥刺，挖苦 <span class="encs">[mock;diride;ridicule]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见朝廷有所任使，非其资序，则相议而讪之。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《上仁宗皇帝言事书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">偏加些恶谑毒讪。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">孔尚任</span><span class="smcs">《桃花扇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:讪人(让人下不了台;小看人);讪语(说讽剌话);讪嘴(斗嘴);讪侮(讥笑轻侮);讪诮(讥笑嘲讽);讪驳(讥笑批驳)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>讪</strong></p><p>訕 <span class="dicpy">shàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>羞惭，难为情 <span class="encs">[embarrassed;awkward]</span>。如:听到别人的批评，他脸上有些发讪;讪筋(恼羞成怒);讪不搭的(形容难为情);讪脸(厚脸皮)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/讪谤">讪谤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/讪骂">讪骂</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/讪讪">讪讪</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/讪笑">讪笑</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
