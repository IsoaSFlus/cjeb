                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>纴</strong></p><p>紝、絍 <span class="dicpy">rèn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rèn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 织布帛的纱缕 <span class="encs">[silk and thread]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纴，机缕也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">织纴组紃。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执针织纴，皆百人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妇人不得纺绩织纴。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·严助传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 指纺织 <span class="encs">[spinning and weaving]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侍儿五三，可充纴织;家僮数四，足代耕耘。——<span class="smcs">《北史·萧大圜传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 缯帛 <span class="encs">[silk fabric]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其妇女庄栉织纴皆取成于婢仆。——<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>纴</strong></p><p>紝 <span class="dicpy">rèn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rèn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 绕线。泛指纺织 <span class="encs">[sew;stitch]</span>。如:纴织(织纴。纺纱织布)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 今也指缝制衣服 <span class="encs">[sew]</span>。如:缝纴</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 穿，引 <span class="encs">[thread]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">石斛数条，去根如筒子，一边纴入耳中。——<span class="smcs">《本草纲目》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
