                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>呼</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hū.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>喊：～喊。～声。～吁。～天号（<span class="dicpy">háo </span>）地。</li><li>唤，叫：～唤。～叫。～应。～朋引类（招引同类的人，共同做坏事）。</li><li>往外出气，与“<span class="diczx3">吸</span>”相对：～气。～吸。</li><li>象声词：～地跳起来。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">呼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>呼</strong></p><p>嘑、虖、謼 <span class="dicpy">hū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，乎声。本义:吐气，与<span class="yhcs">“吸”</span>相对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[exhale;breathe out]</span>。生物体把体内的气体排出体外——与<span class="yhcs">“吸”</span>相对</p><p><span class="diczx1">呼，外息也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>(外息，指呼气)</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阴盛则呼吸万物而藏之内也。——<span class="smcs">《书·大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">候呼引针。——<span class="smcs">《素问·离合真邪论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:呼哈(呼吸吞吐);呼噏(吐云纳气);呼息(呼吸，喘气);呼翕(呼气和吸气)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 大声叫喊 <span class="encs">[cry out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">呼，唤也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">式号式呼。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·荡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">城上不呼。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">媵侍于户外，呼则闻。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">呼</span><span class="diczx2">河伯</span>妇来。——<span class="smcs">《史记·滑稽列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一人大呼。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫起大呼。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妇亦大呼。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">千百人大呼。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:呼噪(嘈杂地叫喊;喧嚷);呼天(向天呼喊以求助);呼天吁地(呼喊天地以求救助)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 召唤 <span class="encs">[call]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">呼</span><span class="diczx2">张良</span>与俱去。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前者呼。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奋呼攻打。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">忠烈</span>大呼。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span
