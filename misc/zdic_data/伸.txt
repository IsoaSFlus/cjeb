						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>伸</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>舒展开：～直。～手（ａ．伸出手；ｂ．喻向别人或上级要东西、荣誉、照顾；ｃ．指插手）。～张（扩大，如“～张正义”）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本作<span class="yhcs">“申”</span>。形声。从人，申声。本义:伸直，伸展)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stretch;extend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伸，不屈也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伸，展也。——<span class="yhcs">“志倦则见，体倦则伸。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">熊经鸟伸。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·精神》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“频伸也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">便有妇人惊觉欠伸，其夫呓语。——<span class="encs">[wipe out a disgrace;right a wrong]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小事尚不伸，况大事乎?——<span class="smcs">《宋史·韩绛传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:伸雪(诉说冤情以求昭雪);伸诉(向上级官员说明情由)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 陈述，表白 <span class="encs">[state]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">第为上者不能察，使匹夫攘袂群起以伸其愤。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:伸问(亦作<span class="yhcs">“申问”</span>。查问，询问);伸诉(详细说明);伸覆(陈说禀告)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr                           『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4F38.svg">                          <p>屈伸。从人申聲。失人切</p>                          </div>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4F38_143.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(伸)</span><span class="ywyy">屈伸。</span><span class="swdz">屈者、無尾也。伸之反也。屈伸、謠俗共知之語。故以爲訓。屈亦作詘。所謂隨體詰詘也。伸古經傳皆作信。周易。詘信相感而利生焉。又尺蠖之詘。以求信也。又引而信之。韋昭漢書音義云。信古伸字。謂古文假借字。許書冣目曰。近而申之。以究萬原。古本申作信。又虫部。尺蠖、屈伸蟲也。太平御覽引作曲信蟲。</span><span class="ywyy">从人。申聲。</span><span class="swdz">疑此字不古。古但作詘信。或用申爲之。本無伸字。以屈伸訓伸篆。亦非說解之體。宋毛晃曰。古惟申字。後加立人以別之。失人切。十二部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
