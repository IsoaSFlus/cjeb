                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鲍</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>鲍</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鮑</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bào.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄠˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>〔～鱼〕❶咸鱼，如“如入～～之肆，久而不闻其臭”；❷鳆鱼的俗称。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> abalone; dried fish; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bao  (Eig, Fam)​,Seeohr, Ohrschnecke  (Eig, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ormeau</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>鲍</strong></p><p>鮑 <span class="dicpy">bào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 盐腌的鱼 <span class="encs">[salted fish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如入鲍鱼之肆，久而不闻其臭。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语·六本》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:鲍舍(存放鲍鱼的房舍);鲍室(存放鲍鱼的房室);鲍肆(鲍鱼之肆。卖盐渍鱼的商店。比喻小人聚居之所)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 鲍鱼，鳆鱼的别名。即石决明 <span class="encs">[abalone]</span>。腹足纲软体动物，海味珍品，贝壳入药</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 腹足纲前鳃亚纲鲍科鲍属海产贝类 <span class="encs">[ear shell]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代鞣制皮革的工人 <span class="encs">[tanner]</span>。如:鲍人(主管治皮革之官。又用以称治皮革的工匠)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鲍老">鲍老</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9B91.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】薄巧切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】部巧切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音抱。【說文】饐魚也。【玉篇】漬魚也。今謂裛魚。【釋名】鮑魚，鮑，腐也，埋藏奄使腐臭也。【急就篇註】鮑亦海魚，加之以鹽，而不乾者也。【周禮·天官·籩人】膴，鮑魚，鱐。【註】鮑者，於楅室中糗乾之，出於江淮也。【史記·貨殖傳】鮑千鈞。【註】鮑，膊魚也。謂破開中，頭尾不相離爲鮑，謂之膊關者也。【周語】鮑魚不登俎豆。【家語】與不善人居，如入鮑魚之肆，久而不聞其臭。</p><p>又姓。【左傳·莊十四年註】鮑叔牙，小白之傅。【齊語】鮑叔牙，齊大夫姒姓之後。</p><p>又與鞄同。【周禮·冬官考工記】攻皮之工，函，鮑。【註】鮑，或書爲鞄。蒼頡篇有鞄<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20926.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠤦</span>。</p><p>又【集韻】披交切，音拋。魚名。</p><p>又【集韻】班交切，音包。人名。楚有申鮑胥。通作包。</p>

                          <span class="jieshi">饐魚也。</span>饐、飯傷溼也。故鹽魚溼者爲饐魚。周禮籩人有鮑。注云。鮑者、於<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24427.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤐧</span>室中<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24427.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤐧</span>乾之。出於江淮。師古注漢書曰。鮑、今之䱒魚也。鄭以爲於煏室乾之。非也。秦始皇載鮑亂臭、則是䱒魚耳。而煏室乾者、本不臭也。䱒於業反。按玉篇作裛魚。皆當作浥耳。浥、溼也。釋名曰。鲍、腐也。埋藏淹使腐臭也。<span class="jieshi">从魚。包聲。</span>薄巧切。古音在三部。                          </div>
