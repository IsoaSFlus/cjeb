						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>损</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">損</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sǔn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǔn.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄨㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>减少：～失。～耗。～益。增～。亏～。～兵折将。</li><li>使失去原来的使用效能：～坏。破～。易～。</li><li>使蒙受害处：～害。满招～，谦受益。</li><li>用刻薄的话挖苦人：～人。</li><li>刻薄，毒辣：这话太～了。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">损</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>损</strong></p><p>損 <span class="dicpy">sǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，员声。本义:减少)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，与<span class="yhcs">“益”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[decrease]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">损，减也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">损偏去也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">损德之脩也。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">损益，盛衰之始也。——<span class="smcs">《易·杂卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以君之力。曾不能损魁之丘，如太行王屋何?——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先王之法，经乎上世而来者也，人或益之，人或损之，胡可得而法?——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请损之，月攘一鸡。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:损年(短报年岁;寿命减少);损膳(减膳);损薄(降低标准，减损);损夺(削减);损减(节制;减少)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 损失;丧失 <span class="encs">[lose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">满招损，谦受益。——<span class="smcs">《书·大禹谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大军围城者二十四万，死者六万七千，巷战死乾又七千，凡损卒七万五千有奇。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《阎典史传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:损折(损失);损败(损失;损坏);损阴(折损了阴德);损身(丧身);损书(对人书札的敬辞。是说对方不惜贬抑身分写信给自己);损挹(谦退)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 伤害或损害 <span class="encs">[harm;injure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">益者三友，损者三友。——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小人之誉人反为损。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说山训》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">做的个上梁不正，只待要损人利己惹人憎。——<span class="smcs">《元曲选·陈州粜米》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:损人肥己，损人害己;损友(有害的朋友);损生(损害身体)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 毁，坏，损坏 <span class="encs">[damage]</span>。又如:破损;损污(损坏玷污);损缺(损坏残缺)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 病情减轻 <span class="encs">[change for the better]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而母</span><span class="diczx2">杨夫人</span>忽病，谒医久未损。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">叶适</span><span class="smcs">《故大宗丞高公墓志铭》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 轻蔑地讥笑或愚弄 <span class="encs">[deride]</span>。如:你别损人了</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/损本逐末">损本逐末</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
