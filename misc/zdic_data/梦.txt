                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">梦</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>梦</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">夢</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄥˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>睡眠时身体内外各种刺激或残留在大脑里的外界刺激引起的景象活动。</li>
	<li>做梦：～见。</li>
	<li>比喻幻想或愿望：～想。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dream; visionary; wishful</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> träumen  (V)​,Traum  (S)​,Wunschtraum, Illusion  (S)​,Meng  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rêve,songe</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">梦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>梦</strong></p>
<p>夢 <span class="dicpy">m&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，由&ldquo;宀&rdquo;(房子)、&ldquo;爿&rdquo;(床)、&ldquo;夢&rdquo;(不明也)三字合成。意为夜间在床上睡觉，眼前模糊看不清，即作梦。&ldquo;夢&rdquo;由&ldquo;苜&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span>，眼看不清)、&ldquo;宀&rdquo;(人的变形)、&ldquo;夕&rdquo;(晚间)三字会意。本义:睡眠中的幻象)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dream]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妇梦中咳嗽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铁马冰河入梦来。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《十一月四日风雨人作》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形诸梦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梦中得我。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:梦迷(梦后刚醒，尚未完全摆脱梦境);梦梦铳铳(形容睡得迷迷糊糊);梦梦查查(迷迷糊糊);梦思(梦中的思念);梦中梦(喻幻境。极言虚幻);梦中(睡梦之中);梦语(睡梦中说的话);梦啼妆泪红阑干(梦中啼哭，擦了脂粉的脸上流满了一道道红色的(泪痕)。妆，这里指脸上的脂粉。阑干，纵横错乱的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 湖泽 <span class="encs">[lake]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:梦泽(即云梦泽);梦溪(地名。在今江苏省丹阳县，宋沈括故宅所在地。<span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span>的省称)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>梦</strong></p>
<p>夢 <span class="dicpy">m&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 做梦 <span class="encs">[dream;have a dream]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王梦见一儿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卧则梦闻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;订鬼篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我欲因之梦吴越。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜深忽梦少年事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:梦枕(传说中会使人做梦的枕头);梦花(梦见花朵。指 <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">马融</span>梦食花，文思大进事)</span></p>
