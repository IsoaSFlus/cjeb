						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蹶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jué  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩㄝˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>跌倒。</li>    <li>挫折，失败：一～不振。</li>    <li>竭尽，枯竭：天下财产，何得不～？</li>    <li>踏，踩。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蹶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">juě  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/juě.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩㄝˇ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ [尥蹶子](liào juě zi)骡、马等用后腿向后踢。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蹶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蹶</strong></p><p>蹷 <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，厥声。本义:倒下，跌倒)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹶，僵也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小人之行，不蹶于山。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·慎行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">处足则为萎，为蹶。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·尽数》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毅恐蹶仆地。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span>。又如:蹶角(额角叩地，表示恭敬诚恳);蹶踬(跌倒，颠仆);蹶躄(颠跌，奔波);蹶踣(颠仆，跌倒);蹶跌(跌倒)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 折损 <span class="encs">[suffer a setback;suffer loss of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹶上将军。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孙吴传》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“毙也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:一蹶不振;蹶衂(衰败;损伤)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用一脚或双脚猛踢，尤指马、骡等动物用后腿向后踢 <span class="encs">[kick]</span>。如:蹶踢(指骡马跳起来用后腿向后踢)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 疾行，跑 <span class="encs">[run]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣闻从时者，犹救火追亡人也，蹶而趋之，唯恐弗及。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 踏;蹈 <span class="encs">[tramp]</span></p><p><span
