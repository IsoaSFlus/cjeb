                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>闻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">聞</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&eacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>听见：～诊。～听。～讯。博～强记。～过则喜。～鸡起舞（听到荒鸡鸣而起舞，喻志士及时奋发）。</li>
	<li>听见的事情，消息：新～。传～。见～。</li>
	<li>出名，有名望：～人。～达。</li>
	<li>名声：令～（好名声）。丑～。</li>
	<li>用鼻子嗅气味：你～～这是什么味儿？</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hear; smell; make known; news</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> riechen, hören (V)​,Wen (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> entendre,sentir (une odeur)​,information</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>闻</strong></p>
<p>聞 <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耳，门声。本义:听到)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻，知声也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻耳之聪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子经上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心不在焉，视而不见，听而不闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻佳人兮召予。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《九歌&middot;湘夫人》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜闻</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>军四面皆 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>歌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妾闻志士不饮盗泉之水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻屏障中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻舟中夜弹琵琶者，听其音，铮铮然有京都声。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闻声不食(听到动物的叫声就不忍心吃它的肉);闻所不闻(听到从未听过的事情。也说&ldquo;闻所未闻&rdquo;);闻风响应(听到风声，便起而响应);闻雷失箸(比喻假借其他不相关的事来掩饰自己的实情);闻鸡起舞(祖逖与刘琨共被同寝，夜半闻荒鸡鸣，乃与琨起身舞剑。后世比喻有志之士及时奋起)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 听说;知道 <span class="encs">[be told;know]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻君行仁政。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其闻道也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻道有先后。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可得闻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闻问(通音讯，获得音讯);闻道(闻知领会道理);闻说(听说);闻得(听说);闻知(听说;知道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 接受 <span class="encs">[accept]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:闻教(受教;领教);闻命(接受命令或教导);闻令(接受教诲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 传布，传扬 <span class="encs">[propagate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">贺兰山</span>下阵如云，羽檄交驰日夕闻。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《老将行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闻诵(传布与称道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 报告上级<span class="encs">[report]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:闻达(向皇帝报告);闻问(通消息)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 趁，乘 <span class="encs">[take advantage of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:闻早(趁早，赶早)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 闻名，出名 <span class="encs">[well-known]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">名闻天下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻于诸侯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻达于诸侯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以义闻于东南。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 嗅;嗅到 <span class="encs">[smell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与善人居，如入芝兰之室，久而不闻其香，即与之化矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语&middot;六本》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罗襦襟解，微闻芗泽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:自不可闻</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通&ldquo;问&rdquo;。询问;问候 <span class="encs">[inguire;extend gleetings to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丧牛之凶，终莫之闻也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;旅象》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谓他人昆，亦莫我闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;葛藟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群公先正，则不我闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;云汉》</span></span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中" target="_blank">未集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中&amp;bs=耳" target="_blank">耳</a>】
                              聞·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/805E.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26541.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦕁</span>䎽䎹<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/265AB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦖫</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25E62.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥹢</span>【唐韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>無分切，音文。【說文】知聞也。【書&middot;堯典】帝曰：兪，予聞如何。【禮&middot;少儀】聞始見君子者。【疏】謂作記之人，不敢自專制其儀，而傳聞舊說，故云。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;玉藻】凡於尊者，有獻而弗敢以聞。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】舉吏民能假貸貧民者以聞。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】宋咸平進士聞見。明尚書聞淵。</p>
<p>又聞人，複姓。【後漢&middot;靈帝紀】太僕沛國聞人襲爲太尉。【註】姓聞人，名襲，風俗通曰：少正卯，魯之聞人，其後氏焉。</p>
<p>又獸名。【山海經】杳山有獸焉，其狀如彘，黃身白頭白尾，名曰聞<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C82.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧲂</span>，見則天下大風。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】亡運切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】文運切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音問。【韻會】聲所至也。【詩&middot;小雅】聲聞于天。【書&middot;呂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>發聞惟腥。【釋文】聞，音問，又如字。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】名達。【書&middot;微子之命】爾惟踐修厥猷，舊有令聞。【詩&middot;大雅】令聞令望。【朱註】令聞，善譽也。</p>
<p>又通作問。【前漢&middot;匡衡傳】淑問揚乎疆外。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶無沿切，音近眠。【楚辭&middot;九章】孤臣唫而抆淚兮，放子出而不還。孰能思而不隱兮，昭彭咸之所聞。還音旋。</p>
