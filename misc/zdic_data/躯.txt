<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">軀</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩˉ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">躯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>躯</strong></p>
<p>軀 <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从身，区声。本义:身体)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[the human body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">躯，体也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">籍柔覆温之躯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赖是以食吾躯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:躯老(躯劳。身体，身段);血肉之躯;躯量(身材);躯躬(身体。引申指生命);躯材(身材);躯身(身躯)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 身孕 <span class="encs">[pregnancy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其母怀躯，阳气内养。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> &ldquo;躯口&rdquo;的省称。宋元时代女真族和蒙古族称被俘获并强迫服劳役的汉族人为&ldquo;躯口&rdquo; <span class="encs">[captive laborer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">你道要女儿着钱赎个婢，要厮儿着钞买一个躯。&mdash;&mdash;佚名<span class="smcs">《刘弘嫁婢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指生命 <span class="encs">[life]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:躯命(生命)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>躯</strong></p>
<p>軀 <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 身体的数目 <span class="encs">[body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中有丈八金像一躯，中长金像十躯，绣珠像三躯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">北魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杨衒之</span><span class="smcs">《洛阳伽蓝记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一躯死尸</span></p>
