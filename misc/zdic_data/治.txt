						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>治</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>管理，处理：～理。～家。～丧。～标。～本。～国安邦。自～。统～（ａ．管理；ｂ．分配）。</li>    <li>整理：～河。～水。</li>    <li>惩办：～罪。处（<span class="dicpy">chǔ</span>）～。</li>    <li>医疗：～病。～疗。医～。</li>    <li>消灭农作物的病虫害：～蝗。～蚜虫。</li>    <li>从事研究：～学。～史。</li>    <li>安定：～世。～安（社会的秩序）。天下大～。</li>    <li>旧称地方政府所在地：府～。～所。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">治</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>治</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，台声。①( <span class="dicpy">chí</span>)本义:水名。②引申义:治水;整治;修治)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同引申义 <span class="encs">[prevent flood by water control;dredge a river;rebuild;repair]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">昔</span><span class="diczx2">禹</span>治洪水。—— <span class="diczx2">郦道元</span><span class="smcs">《水经注》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民治渠。——</span><span class="diczx2">褚少孙</span><span class="smcs">《西门豹治邺》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂治别院，使夫妇成礼。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·小翠》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其后治装行，东入海求其师云。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孝武本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:治酒(置办酒食);治步(修整仪容，举止合乎法度);治任(整理行装);治行(治严。整理行装);治缮(修缮);治茸(修缮)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 治理;管理;统治 <span class="encs">[administer;govern]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">治国无法则乱。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或劳心，或劳力。劳心者治人，劳力者治于人。治于人者食人，治人者食于人。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">伯乐</span>善治马。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·马蹄》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">衡</span>下车，治威严，整法度。(治威严，树立威信。)——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:天下大治;法治;人治;自治;根治;治公(治理公务;办公);治戎(治军，用兵);治具(治理国家的各项措施);治术(治理国家的方法);治制(治理国家的法制、体制);治道(治理国家的方针、政策、措施等);治乱(治理混乱的局面，使国家安定、太平);治掌(掌管);治市(古代掌管市场贸易的官员)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 办理;处理 <span class="encs">[handle;treat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后频历</span><span class="diczx2">武</span><span class="diczx2">威</span>二郡太守，随俗化导，各得治宜。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·廉范传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆尚威严，有治办名。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·酷吏传·尹赏》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:治办(善于办理事物);治剧(办理繁杂的工作);治事(办理公事);治数(处理各种事务);治宜(处理事务合宜);治干(处理政务的才干)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 经营 <span class="encs">[manage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父子治产，居无几何，致产数十万。——<span class="smcs">《史记·越王勾践世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:治产(经营产业)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 治;治疗 <span class="encs">[cure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如人有疾，不治则寝以深。——<span class="smcs">《盐铁论·世务》</span></span></p><p><span
