data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，去声。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:本从犬。本义:胆小，畏缩)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cowardly;timid]</span>怯，多畏也。从犬，去声。 <span class="diczx2">杜林</span>说，从心或曰劫省声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">持节不恐谓之勇，反勇为怯。——<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怯者不得独退。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·军事》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五不行，示</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>弱且怯也。——<span class="encs">[rustic]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怕人说他怯，笑他不开眼。——<span class="smcs">《负曝闲谈》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 虚弱 <span class="encs">[weak]</span></p><p><span afraid]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勇士不怯死而灭名。——<span class="smcs">《史记·鲁仲连邹阳传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黄花不怯西风冷，只怕诗人两鬓霜。——</span><span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span
