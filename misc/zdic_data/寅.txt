						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>寅</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yín  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yín.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>地支的第三位，属虎：～吃卯粮（喻入不敷出）。</li><li>用于计时：～时（夜三点至五点）。</li><li>敬：～饯（恭敬地送）。～畏（敬畏）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to respect, reverence; respectfully; 3rd terrestrial branch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Jahr des Tigers ,Tigerjahre  (S)​,Tigerjahre ,3-5 Uhr morgens ,3. der 12 Erdenzweige ,3. Tier im chin. Tierkreis - Tiger <astrol&gt ,Tigerjahre ,Jahr des Tigers  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> respect,car. cycl.</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寅</strong> <span class="dicpy">yín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。据甲骨文为矢形。小篆讹变。本义:恭敬) 同本义 <span class="encs">[respectful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寅，居敬也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寅，敬也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夙夜惟寅。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">严恭寅畏。——<span class="smcs">《书·无逸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寅哉寅哉。——<span class="smcs">《周书·祭公》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寅宾出日。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:寅亮(恭敬信奉);寅畏(恭敬畏惧);寅清(言行恭谨，持心清正)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>寅</strong> <span class="dicpy">yín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 地支的第三位 <span class="encs">[the third of the twelve earthly branches]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寅，髌也。正月易气动，去黄泉欲上出，侌尚强也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“髌，字之误也，当作ń。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:星次序数之一。古代天文学十二星次中的<span class="yhcs">“析木”</span>为寅</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 在历法中用以纪年，太岁(反木星)运行到析木为寅年，太岁的名摄提格。如:寅皮(老虎皮，亦指属虎的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用以纪月，指夏历正月。如:寅月;寅正(农历正月)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用以纪时，指凌晨三至五时</p><p><span class="diczx1">寅不通光，而卯则日出。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:寅正一刻(四点一刻)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 同僚 <span class="encs">[fellow officer]</span>。如:寅翁(对在同一衙门做事者的敬称);寅家(同僚);寅丈(对同僚的尊称);寅兄(旧时同僚之间的的敬称);寅生(同寅，同官)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 五行属木 <span class="encs">[wood]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寅，木也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/寅吃卯粮">寅吃卯粮</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
