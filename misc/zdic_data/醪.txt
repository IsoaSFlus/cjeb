                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>醪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">láo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/láo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄠˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>浊酒。</li>    <li>〔～糟儿（<span class="dicpy">zāor</span>）〕江米酒。</li>    <li>〔～醴〕中药剂型之一，即药酒。</li>    <li>醇酒。</li></ol>
