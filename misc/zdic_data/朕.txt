						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>朕</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄣˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>我，我的。中国秦始皇时起专用作皇帝自称：&ldquo;～为始皇帝&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>征兆，迹象：～兆。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pronoun 'I'</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Wir (pluralis majestatis)​  (Pron)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> moi</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>朕</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="diczx1">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 缝隙 <span class="encs">[chink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舟之缝理曰朕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">戴震</span><span class="smcs">《考工记图&middot;函人注》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视其朕，欲其直也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 征兆;先兆 <span class="encs">[omen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">体尽无穷，而游无朕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;应帝王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变化之朕焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《鬼谷子》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:朕兆(征兆;预兆);朕垠(征兆，迹象);朕迹(征兆;痕迹)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>朕</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="diczx1">代</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 我。秦以前，不论尊卑，皆自称朕 <span class="encs">[I，my]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朕，我也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朕，我也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝能庸命巽朕位。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无废朕命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;韩奕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朕皇考曰伯庸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者尊卑共称朕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">回朕车以复路兮，及行迷之未远。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:朕皇考(我的已故父亲的名字)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 皇帝的自称。秦灭六国以后，天子始自称朕 <span class="encs">[the royal &ldquo;I&rdquo; (used exclusively by the emperor or king to mean &ldquo;I&rdquo;)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朕位几不保。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朕非约耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:朕躬(我，我身。多用于天子自称);朕违(帝王称自己的过错)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朕</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上" target="_blank">辰集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上&amp;bs=月" target="_blank">月</a>】
                              朕·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6715.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26A4E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦩎</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】直稔切【正韻】呈稔切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音㼉。【說文】我也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】朕，我也。【註】古者貴賤皆自稱朕。【疏】《大禹謨》云：帝曰朕宅帝位，禹曰朕德罔克。屈原亦云：朕皇考曰伯庸。秦始皇二十六年定爲至尊之稱，漢因不改，以迄於今。</p>
<p>又【莊子&middot;應帝王註】朕，兆也。【淮南子&middot;俶眞訓】欲與物，接而未成兆朕。【註】兆朕，形怪也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】丈忍切，音紖。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記函人】眡其朕，欲其直也。【註】謂革制。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朕</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八">卷八</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八&amp;swbs=舟">舟</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6715.svg">                          <p>我也。闕。直禁切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6715_2627.svg" /><span class="swbf">(朕)</span><span class="ywyy">我也。闕。</span><span class="swdz">此說解旣闕。而<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/216B6.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡚶</span>人補我也二字，未知許說字之例也。按朕在舟部，其解當曰舟縫也。从舟，灷聲。何以知爲舟縫也。考工記函人曰：視其朕，欲其直也。戴先生曰：舟之縫理曰朕，故札續之縫亦謂之朕，所以補許書之佚文也。本訓舟縫，引伸爲凡縫之偁。凡言朕兆者，謂其幾甚微。如舟之縫，如龜之坼也。目部瞽字下云：目但有朕也，謂目但有縫也。釋詁曰：朕，我也。此如卬吾台余之爲我，皆取其音，不取其義。趙高之於二世，乃曰天子所以貴者，但以聞聲，羣臣莫得見其面，故號曰朕。比傅朕字本義而言之，遂以亡國。凡說文字不得其理者，害必及於天下。趙高、王安石是也。云从舟灷聲者，人部<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2022A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠈪</span>字亦云灷聲。今說文雖無灷字，然論例當有之。凡勝騰滕螣黱皆以朕爲聲，則朕古音當在六部矣。今音直禁切。六部與七部合韵冣近也。</span></p>                          </div>
