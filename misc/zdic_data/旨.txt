                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>旨</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形。上象<span class="yhcs">“匕”</span>( <span class="dicpy">bǐ</span>)即匙形，下面是口，以匙入口，表示味道好。”<span class="yhcs">“口”</span>隶变为<span class="yhcs">“日”</span>。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:“从甘，匕声。本义:味美)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[delicious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旨，美也。从甘，匕声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既饮旨酒。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·泮水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">告旨。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡饮礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽有嘉肴，弗食，不知其旨也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·学记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:旨甘(美好的食物);旨味(美味);旨肴(美味的菜肴)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 美好 <span class="encs">[fine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">垂功烈而不刊，不亦旨哉!——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《与李睦州论服气书》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>旨</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 美味的食品 <span class="encs">[delicious food]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我有旨畜，亦以御冬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·谷风》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">食旨不甘，闻乐不乐。——<span class="smcs">《论语·阳货》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:甘旨(美味的食物);旨蓄(贮藏的美好食品);旨嘉(美酒佳肴)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 意思;意义 <span class="encs">[meaning;alm;purpose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妙达此旨，始可言文。——<span class="smcs">《宋书·谢灵运传论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:主旨(主要的意义、用意或目的);大旨(主要的意思);宏旨(大旨;要旨);微旨(不是明说，而是含蓄表达出来的意思)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 意图;宗旨 <span class="encs">[intention]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其旨远，其辞文。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">肃</span>宣 <span class="diczx2">权</span>旨，论天下事势，致殷勤之意。——<span
