                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>披</strong> <span class="dicpy">pī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>(形声。从手，皮声。本义:引柩帛) 古夹具。即用在柩车两旁牵挽的帛 <span class="encs">[silk ribbon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">披，从旁持曰披。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设披周也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“披，柩行夹引棺者。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执披。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司士》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“柩车行所以披持棺者，有纽以结之，谓之戴。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执披者旁四人。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设披。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·既夕礼》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>披</strong> <span class="dicpy">pī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 分开;裂开 <span class="encs">[divide;split]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而披其地。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十八年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹分也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又披其邑。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“析也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不折必披。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏其安侯传》</span>。正义:分析也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柳条将舒未舒，柔梢披风。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:竹竿披了;披磔(开割);披砍(撇开);披榛(砍去丛生之草木)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 打开;开发 <span class="encs">[open]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">披，张也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">披重攘以诞兮。——</span><span class="diczx2">嵇良</span><span class="smcs">《琴赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“开也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">哙</span>遂入，披帷西向立。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:披卷(开卷阅读);披帙(打开卷帙);披对(开诚相对);披肝胆(表示以真诚相见);披展(披诚展拜，拜见);披情(剖露情怀);披示(揭示，显示);披决(劈剖);披荆(比喻在前进道路上或创业过程中扫除障碍，克服困难)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 拨开 <span class="encs">[push aside]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹑迹披求，见有虫伏棘根。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:披究(拨开观赏);披草(拨开荒草);披抉(拨开挑剔);披视(拨开来看);披云雾(拨开云雾得见青天);披拨(犹推开)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 覆盖或搭衣于肩 <span class="encs">[drape over one's shoulder]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孰居无事而披拂是。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天运》</span>。释文:<span
