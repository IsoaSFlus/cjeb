                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">互</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>互</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。是一种绞绳用的工具。本义:绞绳用的工具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a implement that use to wind up cord]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;今绞绳者尚有此器。从竹，象形，谓其物象工字;中象人手推握也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 差错 <span class="encs">[error;mistake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">各有乘互。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆法言</span><span class="smcs">《切韵序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代挂肉的木架 <span class="encs">[meatrack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共其牛牲之互。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;牛人》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;互，若今屠家县肉格。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置互摆牲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张衡&middot;西京赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 门，巷门 <span class="encs">[gate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:闾互(闾里巷门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 甲壳动物的总称 <span class="encs">[crustancean]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:互物(指有甲壳动物的总称)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>互</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 交替;相互 <span class="encs">[mutually;each other]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">互，交互。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胡笳互动。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李陵</span><span class="smcs">《答苏武书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以参互考日成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司会》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宗族盘互。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刘向传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">渔歌互答。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">互芬齿颊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">体面互见。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">更互用之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:互易(法律名词。即当事人双方约定互相移转金钱以外财产权的契约。古代以物易物，便是互易);互保(互相保证);互训(两个同义词彼此互相解释);互结(人民对官署，须具两个人或两家以上的店铺互相保证的信约)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>互</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E92.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】胡誤切【韻會】【正韻】胡故切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音護。差也。</p><p>又交互。【周禮&middot;天官】司會以參互考日成。【又】鼈人掌取互物。【註】龜，鼈，蜃，蚌，甲㱿交合也。</p><p>又【周禮&middot;地官】牛人凡祭祀，供其牛牲之互。【註】互者，楅衡之屬，若今屠家縣肉格。</p><p>又【周禮&middot;秋官】野廬氏，凡道路之舟車轚互者，敘而行之。【疏】謂水陸之道，舟車往來，狹隘之所，更互如轚。</p><p>又【周禮&middot;秋官】修閭氏掌比國中宿互<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237C4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣟄</span>者。【註】宿，宿衞也。互謂行馬，所以障互禁止人也。</p><p>又【前漢&middot;劉向傳】向上封事言，兄弟據重，宗族磐互。</p><p>又【左思&middot;吳都賦】長干延屬，飛甍舛互。【註】棟宇交互也。</p><p>又【後漢&middot;蔡邕傳】初朝議，以州郡相黨，乃制婚姻之家，及兩州人士，不得對相監臨，至是復有三互法。【註】三互，謂婚姻之家及兩州人不得交互爲官。　別作㸦。【北史&middot;文苑傳序】彼此好尚㸦有異同，互、㸦形相近，故變作㸦，从互爲正。</p>
