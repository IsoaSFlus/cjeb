						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>卸</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>把东西去掉或拿下来：～车。～装（演员除去化装时穿戴涂抹的东西）。～妆（妇女除去身上的装饰）。拆～。</li>    <li>解除，不肯承担：～任。～肩（卸去肩上的负担，喻卸掉责任）。推～。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从卩，从止，从午。卩( <span class="dicpy">jié</span>)，像人形;止，息止;午，指马。合起来表示停车解马。本义:解马卸车)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[unharness;unhitch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卸，舍车解马也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卸，卸马去鞍。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">空烦卸马鞍。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《携酒高亦同过用寒字》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:卸车;卸鞍</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 解除;解下。亦指褪下;脱下 <span class="encs">[remove;strip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你快卸了装束，归顺天恩。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">功高马卸黄金甲。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄滔</span><span class="smcs">《李相公》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:卸剥(脱);卸头(妇女卸去头上的装饰);卸除(解除，拆除)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 折下;取下 <span class="encs">[remove;disassemble]</span>。如:折卸</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 交卸;推卸 <span class="encs">[shirk]</span>。如:卸过(推卸过失);卸印(辞去官职)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 降落;落 <span class="encs">[fall]</span>。如:卸帆(降帆);卸顶(秃顶)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 凋谢 <span class="encs">[wither]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寿星台上，有千年不卸的名花。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">月光昏，隔水人断孤村;深巷小，黄叶纷纷都卸。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">史震林</span><span class="smcs">《西青散记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 倒塌 <span
