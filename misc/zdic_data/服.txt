						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>服</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>衣裳：～装。制～。</li><li>穿衣裳：～丧。～用（ａ．指穿衣服、用器物；ｂ．吃药）。</li><li>作，担任：～务。～刑。～兵役。</li><li>顺从：信～。佩～。降（<span class="dicpy">xiáng </span>）～。～辩（旧指认罪书）。～膺（牢记在胸中，衷心信服）。</li><li>习惯，适应：不～水土。</li><li>吃（药）：内～。</li><li>乘，用：～牛乘（<span class="dicpy">chéng </span>）马。</li><li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>服</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 量词，指中药（亦作“<span class="diczx3">付</span>”）：两～药。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">服</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>服</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。古文从舟，兼做声符。本义:舟两旁的夹木)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 服从，顺服 <span class="encs">[obey;submit (oneself) to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敌已服矣。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·论威》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以一服八。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以力服人者，非心服也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当舜之时，有</span><span class="diczx2">苗</span>不服。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">授之政，西土服。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·尚贤》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远人不服而不能来也。——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span></span></p><p><span
