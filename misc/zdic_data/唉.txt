                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>唉</strong> <span class="dicpy">āi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/āi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈叹〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，矣声。本义:表示答应)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[yes;well]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">唉，应也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">唉，予知之。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·知北游》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“应声。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勤唉厥生。——<span class="smcs">《韦贤讽谏诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:<span class="yhcs">“你一定要去呀!”</span><span class="yhcs">“唉，我是要去的。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 假借为诶。表示叹息 <span class="encs">[alas]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">亚父</span>受玉斗，置之地，拔剑撞而破之，曰:<span class="yhcs">“唉!竖子不足与谋”</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:<span class="yhcs">“唉!”</span>他又长长地叹了口气;唉!管不了他!</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>唉</strong> <span class="dicpy">ài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈叹〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示伤感或惋惜 <span class="encs">[oh，well]</span>。如:唉!他不小心摔伤了</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示同情 <span class="encs">[alas]</span>。如:唉，他病得怪可怜的!</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">āi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/āi.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
