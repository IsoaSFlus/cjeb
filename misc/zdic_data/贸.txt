                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>贸</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">貿</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>交换财物等商业活动：～易。～迁（贩运买卖）。财～。外～。</li>
	<li>冒冒失失或轻率的样子：～然而来。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> trade, barter; mixed; rashly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Handel  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> commerce,acheter,faire du commerce</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贸</strong></p>
<p>貿 <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，卯( <span class="dicpy">mǎo</span>)声。从贝，与财物有关。本义:交换财物，交易)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[trade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贸，易财也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贸，市也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">氓之蚩蚩，抱布贸丝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span>。传:&ldquo;买也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">工商贸贩于道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;苻坚载记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿以潭上田贸财以缓祸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《永州八记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予贸得其一。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:以臣等贸马;贸籴(买进粮食);贸迁(贩运买卖);贸载(指贩运货物的车子);贸买(购买);贸售(贩卖，出售);贸货(买卖货物);贸贩(贩卖)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 改变 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男女贸功以长生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;上农》</span>。注:&ldquo;易也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古今一揆，先后不贸，焉知来者之不如今。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">吴质</span><span class="smcs">《在元城与魏公子笺》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫事有贸迁，而言无变革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史通&middot;因习》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贸名(改变名称的含义);贸理(事情的道理或规则有所改变);贸换(交换);贸位(变动位置);贸功(换工)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 买 <span class="encs">[buy]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:贸首(悬赏买头。比喻双方积怨很深。都想要对方的性命)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贸</strong></p>
<p>貿 <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
