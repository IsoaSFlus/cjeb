                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">雾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>雾</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">霧</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>接近地面的水蒸气，遇冷凝结后飘浮在空气中的小水点：～气。～霭。迷～。云～。～淞（通称“树挂”）。</li><li>像雾的东西：烟～。～剂。喷～器。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fog, mist, vapor, fine spray</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dunst  (S, Met)​,Nebel  (S, Met)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> brouillard</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">雾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雾</strong></p><p>霧 <span class="dicpy">wù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 接近地面的空气中的水蒸气 <span class="encs">[fog]</span>。由于接触较冷的地表，因而凝结成小水滴或冰晶，使能见度不足一公里，就叫做<span class="yhcs">“雾”</span>，与云的区别只是雾较贴近地面;有时区别于霭的是不太透明。如:大雾;浓雾;雾合(如云雾般的聚合);雾析(雾气消散);雾岑(云雾缭绕的山峰);雾卷(雾气离散);雾杳(雾气深广的样子);雾会(如云雾般聚集在一起。形容其多);雾驳(形容事物像雾色一样斑斓驳杂)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 像雾的许多小水滴 <span class="encs">[spray]</span>。如:喷雾器</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>雾</strong></p><p>霧 <span class="dicpy">wù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 比喻轻细的 <span class="encs">[light]</span>。如:雾绡(像薄雾一般的轻纱)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 比喻浓密的 <span class="encs">[dense]</span>。如:雾鬣(细密的鬣毛)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 昏晦，昏暗 <span class="encs">[dim]</span>。如:雾塞(形容昏昧)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻头发的堆卷盘旋，有如雾气的舒腾 <span class="encs">[scattered]</span>。如:雾鬟(形容女子头发像云雾般的堆卷盘旋);雾鬓风鬟(形容女子发髻松散的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雾沉沉">雾沉沉</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雾化">雾化</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雾里看花">雾里看花</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雾茫茫">雾茫茫</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雾气">雾气</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雾腾腾">雾腾腾</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
