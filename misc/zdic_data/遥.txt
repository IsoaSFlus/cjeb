						<p>● <strong>遥</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>远：～远。～望。～想。～瞻。～指。～瞩。～相呼应。路～知马力。～测。～感。～控。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">遥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>遥</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>))</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[faraway]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遥，远也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遥，远也。</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>曰遥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言六》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遥遥，远也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">倚沼畦瀛兮遥望博。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span>。注:&ldquo;遥，远也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可登而路遥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遥闻深巷中犬吠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遥闻汝哭声。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遥语</span><span class="diczx2">应元</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遥天(远天);遥祭(向着远方而祭祀);遥领(间接治理，由远处控制监督);遥遥华胄(指远古时候的名门后裔);遥心(心向远方);遥目(远望);遥仰(对远地的人表示敬仰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 时间长 <span class="encs">[long]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勤思终遥夕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">何劭</span><span class="smcs">《杂诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遥夕(长夜);遥久(长久)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;姚&rdquo;。妖艳;美好 <span class="encs">[pretty and coquettish;fine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:遥冶(姚冶;艳丽);遥艳(妖艳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;摇&rdquo; <span class="encs">[rock;sway]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:遥曳(摇荡)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>遥</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>飘荡 <span class="encs">[drift;wave]</span></p>
