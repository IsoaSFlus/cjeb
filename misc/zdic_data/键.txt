                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">键</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>键</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鍵</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>安装在车轴头上管住车轮或使轴与齿轮等连接固定的零件，一般是用钢制的长方条（亦称“<span class="diczx3">辖</span>”）：关～。</li><li>插在门上关锁门户的金属棍子：“掌授管～以启闭国门”。</li><li>琴或机器上使用时用手按动的部分：～盘。～子。琴～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> door bolt, lock bolt; key</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bindung, Keil, Taste  (S, Chem)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> clé,clef,touche (de piano ou clavier)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">键</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>键</strong></p><p>鍵 <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，建声。本义:鼎上贯通两耳的横杠)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">键，铉也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓鼎扃也。以木横关鼎耳而举之。非是则既炊之鼎不可举也。故谓之关键。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 车辖。安在车轴两端，管住车轮不脱离轴的铁棍 <span class="encs">[linchpin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">键，一曰车辖也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">题无四寸之键，则车不行。——<span class="smcs">《尸子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 钥匙 <span class="encs">[key]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诚九流之津涉，六艺之铃键。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《尔雅序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">户钥，自关而东，</span><span class="diczx2">陈</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>之间谓之键。——<span class="smcs">《方言五》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 竖着插的门闩 <span class="encs">[bolt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">横曰关，竖曰键。——<span class="smcs">《字书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五寸之键，制开阖之门。——<span class="smcs">《说苑·谈丛》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:键闭(门闩;锁钥)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 锁簧，也称<span class="yhcs">“锁须”</span> <span class="encs">[locking spring]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌授管键，以启闭国门。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司门》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> <span class="yhcs">“关”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“键”</span>连用，泛指门闩，后来比喻事物中最紧要的部分，起决定性的因素 <span class="encs">[bolt;key]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">善闭，无关键而不可开。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">神居胸臆，而志气统其关键。——<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙·神思》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 一种机械零件<span class="encs">[key]</span>。如:轴键;导向平键;琴键;键盘</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 表示原子价的短横线 <span class="encs">[bond]</span>。如:共价键;单键;双键</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>键</strong></p><p>鍵 <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 闩门 <span class="encs">[bolt the door]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是键其门而去。——</span><span class="diczx2">张读</span><span class="smcs">《宣室志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生丁其时命所存，六淫汹涌无键阍。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span
