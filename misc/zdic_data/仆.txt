                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">仆</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>仆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄨ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>向前跌倒：～倒。前～后继。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>仆</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">僕</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>被人雇佣差遣服务的人，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">主</span>&rdquo;相对：～人。～从。</li>
	<li>旧谦称&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">我</span>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fall forward; lie prostrate, prone; servant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Diener, Dienstmädchen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tomber en avant,se prosterner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">仆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>仆</strong> <span class="dicpy">pū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 向前跌倒 <span class="encs">[fall forward]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仆，顿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏</span>声曰:&ldquo;前覆曰仆，后仰曰偃。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仆表决漏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马穰苴传》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;卧其表也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">度水跌仆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;经脉别论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卒仆</span><span class="diczx2">济</span>北。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;邹阳传》</span>。注:&ldquo;僵仆也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卫士仆地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:前仆后继;仆然(扑通倒下的样子);仆顿(精疲力竭而倒仆);仆僵(跌倒;翻倒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 下坠;倾倒 <span class="encs">[fall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">距洞百余步，有碑仆道，其文漫灭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旧碑断折，其半仆地。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《祭湘君夫人文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:仆旗(放倒战旗。借指休战)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 败灭 <span class="encs">[be shattered;vanish]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:随起随仆(随时发生，随遭败灭)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">p&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>仆</strong></p>
<p>僕 <span class="dicpy">p&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
