                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">证</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>证</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">證</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用人物、事实来表明或断定：～明。保～。～实。作～。对～。论～。人～。物～。</li>
	<li>凭据，帮助断定事理的东西：～据。凭～。～书。出入～。～章。有诗为～。～券。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> prove, confirm, verify; proof</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bestätigung  (S)​,bestätigen, demonstrieren, prüfen, zertifizieren  (V, Rechtsw)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> certificat,permis,papiers,prouver,attester,démontrer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">证</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>证</strong></p>
<p>証 <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>谏正 <span class="encs">[remonstrate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">证，谏也。从言，正声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士尉以证</span><span class="diczx2">靖郭君</span>， <span class="diczx2">靖郭君</span>不听。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">高诱</span>注:&ldquo;证，谏也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:证谏(直言规劝)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>证</strong></p>
<p>証 <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;症&rdquo;。病症 <span class="encs">[disease;illness]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后先生之言，为</span><span class="diczx2">思陵</span>对证之药也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《子刘子行状》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假借为&ldquo;徵&rdquo;。今亦用为證验字。证据 <span class="encs">[evidence;proof]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">索证正不在远。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>证</strong></p>
<p>證 <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，正声。繁体为&ldquo;登&rdquo;声。本义:告发)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[inform against]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">證，告也。从言，登声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其父攘羊，而子证之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子路》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:证父(告发父亲)</span></p>
