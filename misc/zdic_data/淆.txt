<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，肴( <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span>)声。本义:错杂;混杂)</p>

<p><span class="diczx1">澄之不清，淆之不浊，不可量也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
