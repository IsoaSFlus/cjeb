                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>沾</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄢ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>浸湿：～衣。～润（沾湿浸润，喻沾得利益）。</li>
	<li>因接触而附着（<span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute; </span>）上：～水。～手。～污。</li>
	<li>染上：～染。</li>
	<li>指因凭借关系而得到：～光。～包。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>沾</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">di&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉ〡ㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古水名。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">沾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>沾</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为&ldquo;霑&rdquo;。浸润;浸湿 <span class="encs">[moisten;soak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沾，一曰益也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惠沾渥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉曹全碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">澍雨沾洽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白石神君碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汗出沾背。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈丞相世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泣下沾衿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长夜沾湿何由彻!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泣下沾襟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《伶官传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不终朝日，而澍雨沾洽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白石神君碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十八日，大雨沾足，乃种豆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:沾足(雨水充足);沾洽(雨水充足;渊博)。又如:沾浃(浸透);沾渥(浸润);沾臆(沾膺。泪水浸湿胸前);沾濡(浸湿);沾襟(浸湿衣襟);沾洒(水珠洒落浸湿。多指流泪);沾缨(泪水浸湿冠缨。指痛哭、悲伤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 牵扯，牵连 <span class="encs">[involve]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:沾扯(牵扯);沾带(牵连);沾恋(牵念留恋);沾连(沾带。牵连)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 接触 <span class="encs">[touch with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沾水则高下不平。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 受赏 <span class="encs">[be rewarded]</span>。</p>
