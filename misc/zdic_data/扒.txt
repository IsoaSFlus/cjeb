<p><span class="dicpy">bā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bā.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄚˉ</span></p>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扒</strong> <span class="dicpy">ba</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 刨，挖 <span class="encs">[dig]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扒了好几天，才扒出来这个大洞子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《高玉宝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">用前足的钩扒掘地面。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《蝉》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扒坑;扒开浮土;扒土;扒堤</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 攀援 <span class="encs">[climb]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他明朝就扒在我头上来哩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">朱京藩</span><span class="smcs">《风流院传奇&middot;投罗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扒车;扒得高，跌得重</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拆除 <span class="encs">[pull down]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扒了旧房盖新房</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 脱掉 <span class="encs">[strip off]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他把棉袄一扒就干起活来</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 剥去或除去&hellip;的表层(如皮) <span class="encs">[skin]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:把兔子皮扒下来</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">p&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扒</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用手或用工具把东西聚拢或散开 <span class="encs">[rake up;gather up;rake together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">姐夫，去请你爹进来扒口子饭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金瓶梅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扒草;把枯树叶扒在一起</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 煨烂或用微火炖 <span class="encs">[stew;braise]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扒羊肉;扒鸡</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 从别人身上摸窃财物<span class="encs">[steal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他送给我一只旧怀表&hellip;不知道它是在什么时候给扒手拿走的。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">巴金</span><span class="smcs">《我的哥哥李尧林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扒钱包</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈方〉∶搔，抓 <span class="encs">[scratch]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扒痒</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 挣<span class="encs">[钱]</span> <span class="encs">[earn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">社会在变，舞文弄墨者群也在变，能够在这领域里纵横驰聘的人，有不少已不赞成严肃认真，他们也称写作为&ldquo;扒分&rdquo;。就像个体户卖牛仔裤一样。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙少琪</span><span class="smcs">《他在写作》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扒分(挣钱，捞外快)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 划动 <span class="encs">[row]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扒捍(船名);扒艇(舢板船);扒龙(清末一种装备武器的手划的走私快艇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 用同&ldquo;爬&rdquo;。爬行 <span class="encs">[crawl]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扒山虎(山行便桥);扒沙(爬行);扒推(亦作&ldquo;杷推&rdquo;、&ldquo;爬推&rdquo;。眼泪成串下流的样子)</span></p>
