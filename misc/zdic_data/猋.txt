						<p>● <strong>猋</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">biāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄠ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>犬跑的样子。</li>	<li>奔跑；急速前去：&ldquo;灵皇皇兮既降，～远举兮云中。&rdquo;</li>	<li>暴风；旋风。后作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/飇">飇</a>&rdquo;，也作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/飙">飙</a>&rdquo;：&ldquo;～风暴雨总至。&rdquo;</li>	<li>草名。古又称为&ldquo;<a href="/hans/荼">荼</a>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<a href="/hans/苕">苕</a>&rdquo;。</li>	<li>贝名。也作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/贆">贆</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>
