                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>掀</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>揭起，打开：～开。～涌。～起。～动。</li><li>发动，兴起：～风鼓浪（喻煽动情绪，挑起事端）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>掀</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，欣声。本义:举起)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lift;raise]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掀，举出也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃掀公以出于淖。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:掀掀(高举的样子);掀然(高举的样子;飞扬的样子);掀擢(举拔，拔擢);掀拳裸袖(高举拳头，卷起衣袖。表示心情激动)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 撩起;揭开 <span class="encs">[lift up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">刘老老</span>掀帘进去。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:掀揭(掀起，揭开)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 翻;推 <span class="encs">[stir up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有觉南方重，无疑厚地掀。——</span><span class="diczx2">李咸用</span><span class="smcs">《庐山诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:掀天(翻天。极言声势之大);掀扬(折腾，磨折)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 揭发，把…公开出来 <span class="encs">[expose]</span>。如:掀人罪状;掀他老底</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 攻取 <span class="encs">[conquer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">记少年骏马走</span><span class="diczx2">韩卢</span>，掀 <span class="diczx2">东郭</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《满江红·和廓之雪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 向上翘起 <span class="encs">[turn upwards]</span>。如:掀鼻(鼻孔朝上翘);掀屋角;浓眉掀鼻;掀髯(笑时启口张须的样子;激动的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/掀掉">掀掉</a></span><span
