                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>穹</strong> <span class="dicpy">qióng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qióng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，弓声。本义:穷尽)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[completely;thoroughly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穹，穷也。从穴，弓声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:穹窒(完全堵塞)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 高 <span class="encs">[high]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">郁并起而穹崇。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《长门赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:穹枝(犹高枝);穹穹(高大的样子);穹官(高官);穹居(高大的住所);穹宫(高大的宫室，祠堂)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[big]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穹，大也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释诂》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“穹隆亦为大也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穹崖巨谷。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:穹石(大岩石);穹穹(高而大貌);穹龟(大龟)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 漫远 <span class="encs">[distant;far]</span>。如:穹古(上古;远古)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 深<span class="encs">[deep]</span>。如:穹谷(深谷);穹林(幽深的树林);穹壑(深谷)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>穹</strong> <span class="dicpy">qióng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="encs">[vault;dome]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以念穹苍。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑柔》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穹苍，苍天也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释天》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洒扫穹窒。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·东山》</span>。笺:<span class="yhcs">“穹窒，鼠穴也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:穹隆(形容天的形状，中央高而四周下垂的形状);穹窿垂像(天象);苍穹;穹天(天空);穹旻(即天);穹昊(上天);穹灵(上天的神灵);穹玄(苍天)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 穹庐;毡帐 <span class="encs">[tent]</span>。如:穹帐(穹闾;穹庐;毡帐);穹宇(拱形的屋宇)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/穹苍">穹苍</a></span><span
