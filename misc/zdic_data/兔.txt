                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>兔</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>哺乳动物，耳长，尾短，上唇中间裂开，后肢较长，跑得快：～子。～脱（迅速地逃走）。～毫笔。～起鹘落（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">鹘</span>&rdquo;，打猎用的猛禽。兔子才起来而鹘已经扑下去，喻动作敏捷。亦喻作书画或写文章下笔迅捷）。</li>
	<li>古称娈童（指被当作女性玩弄的美貌男子）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rabbit, hare</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Hase, Kaninchen  (S, Bio)​,Wildkaninchen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> lapin</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兔</strong></p>
<p>兎 <span class="dicpy">t&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。象踞后其尾形。本义:哺乳类动物，通称兔子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hare;rabbit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兔，兽名。象踞后其尾形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。其兽前足短后足长，俗字作菟。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兔舐毫而孕，及其生子，从口而出也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;奇怪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肃肃兔罝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;兔置》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兔曰明视。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兔走触株。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冀复得兔。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兔不可复得。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雄兔脚扑朔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雌兔眼迷离。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">双兔傍地走。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 头部略像鼠，耳大，上唇中间分裂，尾短而向上翘，前肢比后肢短。善于跳跃，跑得很快。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兔走乌飞(玉兔走，金乌飞。指日月的消长);兔角(佛教用语。比喻不可能有的事物);兔缺(称上嘴唇中裂的人);免脱(形容逃脱迅速);兔毛(亦指细嫩茶叶上的白毫，借指茶叶);兔纤(兔肉制成的食品，略似今之肉松);兔苑(兔园)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 传说中的月中玉兔 <span class="encs">[jade rabbit in the moon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兔乌(玉兔、金乌的简称。指月和日。古代传说太阳中有金乌，月亮中有玉兔);兔影(玉兔的影子);兔房(传说中玉兔捣药之屋。借指仙家药室);兔儿爷(月中的玉兔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 月亮的别称 <span class="encs">[moon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兔魄，兔轮，兔月(月亮的别名);兔钩(弯月);兔宫(月宫);兔起鸟沉(月出日落);兔辉(月光);兔阙(月宫);兔华(明月)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代车制 <span class="encs">[a carriage brake]</span>。舆下方木，伏于毂上轴内两旁，用以承舆者。名伏兔，省称为兔</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 制笔的兔毫。借指毛笔 <span class="encs">[writing brush]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兔毫，兔颖(用兔毛制成的笔);兔楮(笔和纸。犹言笔墨);兔翰(毛笔);兔管(毛笔的别称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 詈词 <span class="encs">[curse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兔强盗;兔崽子;兔毛大伯(土财主)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>兔</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>捕兔，猎兔 <span class="encs">[rabbit]</span>。</p>
