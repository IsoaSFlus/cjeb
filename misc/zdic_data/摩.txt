						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>摩</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mó.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄛˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>擦，蹭，接触：～擦。～天。～崖（山崖上刻的文字、佛像等）。～肩接踵。～顶放踵。</li>    <li>摸，抚：～弄。～挲（<span class="dicpy">suō</span>）</li>    <li>研究，切磋：观～。揣～（a.研究，仔细琢磨；b.估量，推测）。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">磨</span>”，磨擦。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>摩</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄚˉ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔～挲（<span class="dicpy">suō</span>）〕用手轻轻按着一下一下地移动。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>摩</strong> <span class="dicpy">mā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>另见 <span class="dicpy">mó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/摩挲">摩挲</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>摩</strong> <span class="dicpy">mó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，麻声。本义:摩擦，磨蹭)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rub;scrape]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摩，研也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刮摩之工。——<span class="smcs">《考工记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鎋击摩车而相过。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人肩摩。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">濯手以摩之，去其皱。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:摩戛(摩擦。也作磨戛);摩厉(磨物使锐。引申有磨炼、钻研的意思);摩肩接毂(肩挨着肩，车子擦着车子);摩肩(肩挨着肩);摩拭(揩擦)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 迫近，接近 <span
