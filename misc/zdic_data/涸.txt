						<p>● <strong>涸</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄜˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>水干：干～。～泽。～竭。～辙之鲋（喻处境十分危难、急待救助的人）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dried up; exhausted, tired; dry</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> abtrocknen, dörren ,ausdorren, stecken bleiben</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dessécher</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>涸</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，固声。本义:失去水而干枯)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涸，竭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仲秋之月&hellip;水始涸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">商涸旱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎大》</span>。注:&ldquo;涸，枯也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兀若枯木，豁若涸流。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《文赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涸辙(鱼在干涸的车辙中。比喻处于困境之中，急待救援);涸泽(干涸的沼泽;抽干沼泽的水);涸流(枯竭的水流);涸溜(干枯的小水流)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 竭;尽 <span class="encs">[exhaust]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">措国于不倾之地，积于不涸之仓，藏于不竭之府。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;牧民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:涸思干虑(犹言绞尽脑汁)</span></p>
