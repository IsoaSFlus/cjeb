                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>郁</strong></p>
<p><strong>（①②<span class="diczx3">鬱</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>树木丛生：～葱（➊青翠茂盛；➋形容很盛）。～闭。</li>
	<li>忧愁，愁闷：～闷。～悒（苦闷）。～愤。～怒。～积。～结。～忧。抑～。</li>
	<li>有文彩：～～（➊文彩显著；➋香气浓厚；➌草木茂密；➍心中苦闷）。</li>
	<li>香气浓厚：～烈。馥～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sweet smelling, rich in aroma; (Cant.) to move, hit</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> elegant, elegante ,Yu  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> triste,mélancolique,stagnant,réprimé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">郁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>郁</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 郁夷 <span class="encs">[Yu county]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">郁，右</span><span class="diczx2">扶风</span><span class="diczx2">郁夷</span>也。从邑，有声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古地名。汉置县。在今陕西省陇县西</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 物实无中核者谓之郁 <span class="encs">[core]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">物实无中核者谓之郁，无刀斧之斫者谓之朴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:郁朴(本指没有内核的果实或未加工的木料，借喻缺乏教养的人)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>郁</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 文采美盛 <span class="encs">[rich and bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周</span>监于二代，郁郁乎文哉!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;八佾》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纷郁郁其远承兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;思美人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:郁郁桓桓(文采明盛、勇武出众的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 丰盛 <span class="encs">[abundant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蜜房郁毓被其阜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《蜀都赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;盛多也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:郁毓(丰盛的样子);郁霭(云彩盛多的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 美好 <span class="encs">[fine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:郁穆(和穆美好的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 香气浓郁 <span class="encs">[strong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">践椒涂之郁烈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:郁芬(浓烈的香气);郁攸(火气;灼热之气);郁郁青青(香气馥郁，青葱繁茂的样子)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>郁</strong></p>
<p>鬱 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从邑，有声。&ldquo;郁&rdquo;本是地名，又作姓。&ldquo;鬱&rdquo;为形声，从林，鬱( <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> )省声。本义:繁盛的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[luxuriant;rush]</span></p>
