						<p>● <strong>屹</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>山势高耸，喻坚定不可动摇：～～（高耸的样子）。～立。～然不动。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to rise high; to stand erect</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> haut et abrupt</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>屹</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，乞声。本义:山势直立高耸的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。后泛指耸立的 <span class="encs">[towering]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屹山峙以迂郁。(迂郁:盘曲的样子。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王延寿</span><span class="smcs">《鲁灵光殿赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:屹仡(挺拔雄劲的样子);屹峙(耸立)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 坚定不移的 <span class="encs">[firm]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:屹若长城</span></p>
