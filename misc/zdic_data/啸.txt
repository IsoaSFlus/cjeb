                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">啸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>啸</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">嘯</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiào.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>撮口作声，打口哨：～歌（吟咏）。～傲。～聚（互相招呼，聚集成集，如“～～山林”）。呼～。仰天长～。</li>    <li>动物拉长声叫：虎～。猿～。</li>    <li>自然界发出的声音：北风呼～。海～。</li>    <li>飞机或子弹掠过时发出的声音：飞机尖～着冲上蓝天。炮弹呼～而过。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> roar, howl, scream; whistle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> fauchen, zischen ,pfeifen, flöten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> siffler,hurler,mugir,rugir</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>啸</strong></p><p>嘨 <span class="dicpy">xiào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，肃声。本义:撮口作声，打口哨)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[whistle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啸，吹声也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其嘨也歌。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·江有汜》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不嘨不指。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">激于舌端而清谓之啸。——<span class="smcs">《封氏闻见记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">登东皋以舒啸。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俯仰啸歌。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若啸呼状。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倚修木而啸。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:啸傲林泉(在幽静的山林泉水环抱中隐居);啸吟(长啸哀叹);啸指(以指夹唇吹之作声)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 呼召，号召 <span class="encs">[call]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">嘨者，谓若有所召命，若</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">庄</span>抚楹而歌耳。——<span class="smcs">《匡缪正俗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">招具该备，永啸呼些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:啸引;啸召(呼唤;召唤);啸合(召唤聚集);啸侣(呼叫同类;召唤同伴);啸侣命俦(召唤同伴);啸命(高声命令)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 鸟兽等的长声鸣叫 <span class="encs">[howl;roar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虎啸猿啼。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乳虎啸谷。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:鸟啸;啸萃(鸣叫而聚集);啸吼(长声吼叫);啸风(呼风);风嘶雨啸</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/562F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】蘇弔切【集韻】【韻會】先弔切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音熽。【說文】吹聲也。【詩·召南】其嘯也歌。【箋】嘯，蹙口而出聲。</p><p>又【小雅】嘯歌傷懷。【司馬相如·上林賦】長嘯哀鳴。【拾遺記】前漢，西方有因霄之國，人皆善嘯。丈夫嘯聞百里，婦人嘯聞五十里。【世說新語】蘇門山中忽有眞人，阮籍往觀，對之長嘯良久，乃笑曰：可更作籍。後嘯意盡退，還半嶺許，聞上唒然有聲，如數部鼓吹，林谷傳響，顧看，乃向人嘯也。</p><p>又【集韻】息六切，音肅。吹氣若歌。【詩·王風】有女仳離，條其歗矣。【釋文】歗，本亦作嘯。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>尺栗切。與叱同。【禮·內則】不嘯不指。詳前叱字註。唒字从口从酋。嘯字十六畫。</p>
