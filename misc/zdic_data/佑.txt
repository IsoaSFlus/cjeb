                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>佑</strong> <span class="dicpy">yòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，右声。字本作<span class="yhcs">“右”</span>。表右助。因<span class="yhcs">“右”</span>用为左右之<span class="yhcs">“右”</span>，又造<span class="yhcs">“佑”</span>字表示本义。本义:保护;右助)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bless and protect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天佑下民，作之君，作之师。——<span class="smcs">《书·泰誓上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天命反侧，何罚何佑?——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:保佑(迷信的人称神力保护和帮助);佑助(保佑扶助;庇护)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 辅助;帮助 <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佑，助也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> <span class="smcs">《说文》</span>无<span class="yhcs">“佑”</span>字。 <span class="diczx2">徐铉</span>在<span class="yhcs">“右”</span>字下注云:<span class="yhcs">“今俗别作佑。”</span>按:又、右、佑当为一字分化</p><p><span class="diczx1">高祖为亭长，常佑之。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:佑庇(帮助;保护)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/53F3.svg">                          <p>助也。从口从又。于救切〖注〗徐鍇曰：“言不足以左，復手助之。”</p><p>手口相助也。从又从口。于救切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗别作佑。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/53F3_706.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(右)</span><span class="ywyy">助也。从口又。</span><span class="swdz">者手也。手不足。以口助之。故曰助也。今人以左右爲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20087.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂇</span>又字。則又製佐佑爲左右字。于救切。古音在一部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>              <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/53F3_707.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(右)</span><span class="ywyy">助也。</span><span class="swdz">李燾本及集韵如是。今二徐本皆作手口相助也。</span><span class="ywyy">从又口。</span><span class="swdz">按又下曰厷、曰叉、曰叉、自臂指言之。以又下象臂、上象指也。不當早廁從口之字。口部有此字、云助也。從口又。主謂以口助手。不當入此謂手助口。宜刪。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
