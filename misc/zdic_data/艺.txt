                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">艺</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>艺</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">藝</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>才能，技能，技术：工～。技～。文～。～人。～术（①戏剧、曲艺、音乐、美术、建筑、舞蹈、电影、诗和文学等的总称；②指富有创造性的方式、方法；③形状独特而美观的）。～苑奇葩。～高人胆大。</li>
	<li>种植：园～。</li>
	<li>准则、法度、限度：&ldquo;骄泰奢侈，贪欲无～。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> art; talent, ability; craft</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kunst  (Kunst)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> art,habileté,talent</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">艺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>艺</strong></p>
<p>藝 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(会意。甲骨文字形。左上是&ldquo;木&rdquo;，表植物;右边是人用双手操作。又写成&ldquo;埶&rdquo;，从坴( <span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;</span>)，土块;从丮( <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span>)，拿。后繁化为&ldquo;藝&rdquo;。&ldquo;艺&rdquo;从&ldquo;帅&rdquo;，乙声。本义:种植) 同本义 <span class="encs">[plant;grow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艺，种也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艺麻之如何?衡从其亩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能艺稷黍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;鸨羽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艺山林也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树艺五谷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以艺粟菽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艺蔬自给。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:艺植(耕种;栽植);艺人(耕种的人);艺圃(种植花卉园圃)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>艺</strong></p>
<p>藝 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 技能;才能 <span class="encs">[skill;craftsmanship]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">求</span>也艺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艺天下无双。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艺天下第一。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惜罗程艺。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以三保勇而多艺，推为长。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/85DD.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕秇【唐韻】魚祭切【韻會】倪祭切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蓺。才能也。【禮&middot;禮運】月以爲量，故功有藝也。【註】藝猶才也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;天官&middot;宮正】會其什伍，而敎之道藝。【註】藝謂禮、樂、射、御、書、數。</p>

<p>又【左傳&middot;文六年】陳之藝極。【註】藝，準也。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】藝殪仆。【註】所射準的爲藝。</p>
<p>又【家語】合諸侯而藝貢事禮也。【註】藝，分別貢獻之事也。</p>
<p>又【晉語】貪欲無藝。【註】藝，極也。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】有藝氏。</p>
<p>又與蓺通。【韻會】種也。【書&middot;禹貢】蒙羽其藝。【傳】兩山巳可種藝。【孟子】樹藝五穀。</p>
<p>又【韻補】五結切，叶音臬。【丘遲&middot;思賢賦】備百行之高致，談九流之洪藝。諒可雜而非染，跡每同而常別。</p>
