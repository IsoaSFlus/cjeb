                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>得</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">d&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄜˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>获取，接受：～到。～失。～益。～空（<span class="dicpy">k&ograve;ng</span>）。～便。～力。～济。心～。</li>	<li>适合：～劲。～当（<span class="dicpy">d&agrave;ng </span>）。～法。～体。</li>	<li>满意：～意。扬扬自～。</li>	<li>完成，实现：饭～了。～逞。～志（多指满足名利的欲望）。</li>	<li>可以，许可：不～随地吐痰。</li>	<li>口语词（①表禁止，如&ldquo;～了，别说了&rdquo;；②表同意，如&ldquo;～，就这么办&rdquo;）。</li></ol><p>● <strong>得</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">děi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/děi.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄟˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>必须，须要：可～注意。</li>	<li>极舒服，极适意：这时要能洗上凉水澡，就～了。</li></ol><p>● <strong>得</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">de  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/de.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄜ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用在动词后表可能：要不～。拿～起来。</li>	<li>用在动词或形容词后的连接补语，表示效果或程度：跑～快。香～很。</li></ol>

<p>◎ <strong>得</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，右边是&ldquo;贝&rdquo;(财货)加&ldquo;手&rdquo;，左边是&ldquo;彳&rdquo;，表示行有所得。手里拿着财货，自然是有所得。本义:得到，获得)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(本来没有而争取得来成为己有) <span class="encs">[get;obtain;gain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得，行有所得也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三人行必得我师焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。释文:&ldquo;本或作必有。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缘木求鱼，虽不得鱼，无后灾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">佳婿难得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;假谲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卖炭得钱何所营。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">工之侨</span>得良桐焉。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子&middot;千里马篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:得人心;各得其所;得肆(得志，肆意);得闲儿(有空闲时间;得空);得胜回头(宋元说书人在开讲正书前先说一段小故事作引子，叫得胜回头);得职(获得官职);得情报;得钱;得奖</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 找到 <span class="encs">[discover;obtain;find out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知得而不知丧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;文言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虑而后后能得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓得事之宜也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至德不得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既而得其尸于井。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 得知 <span class="encs">[hear of;learn about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故无术者得于不用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">礼得其报则乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓晓其义。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武王</span>得之矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;义赏》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹知也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 捕获 <span class="encs">[catch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然得而腊之以为饵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 亦指被捕获;被逮捕。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:得俊(俘获敌方的勇猛将士)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 成功;完成 <span class="encs">[succeed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已经传人画图样去了。明日就得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:得算(计谋成功);得策(谋略得当)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 适，合 <span class="encs">[fit;befit;suit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">傲啸东轩下，聊复得此生。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《饮酒》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 具备 <span class="encs">[have;possess]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积善成德，而神明自得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 同&ldquo;德&rdquo;，恩惠，感恩 <span class="encs">[be grateful;feel grateful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所识穷乏者得我欤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地广而不得者国危，兵强而凌敌者身亡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;击之》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 助动词。能，能够 <span class="encs">[can]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>下，欲与之言，趋而避之，不得与之言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;微子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以是人多以书假余，余因得遍观群书。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">进退不得，为之奈何?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吴子&middot;应变》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">沛公</span>军 <span class="diczx2">霸上</span>，未得与 <span class="diczx2">项羽</span>相见。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 得意，满足 <span class="encs">[be proud of;revel in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">意气扬扬，甚自得也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;管晏列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常使两情皆得，彼此俱畅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;文学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:洋洋得意</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 看到 <span class="encs">[see]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">林尽水源，便得一山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>得</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 收获，心得 <span class="encs">[what one has learned]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古人之观于天地、山川、草木、虫鱼、鸟兽，往往有得。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开卷有得，便欣然忘食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南史&middot;陶潜传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 德，道德，有德之人 <span class="encs">[virtue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚得推贤不失序。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>得</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p>必须，应该 <span class="encs">[must]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君为我呼入，吾得兄事之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>得</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p>合适;正确 <span class="encs">[suitable;right]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于彼计则得。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">历古今之得失。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>得</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">叹</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用在口语中表示同意或禁止 <span class="encs">[all right]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:得，就这么办;得了，别说了;得也么(算了吧;又作答应之词)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用在口语中，瞧。在情况变坏时表示无可奈何 <span class="encs">[look，look!]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:得，又搞错了;得，全完了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">de;děi</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>得</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄉㄜˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>d&eacute; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1016.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">獲、取。與「失」相對而言。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「鷸蚌相爭，漁翁得利。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;告子上》：「求則<b>得</b>之，舍則失之。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">演算產生結果。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「三三<b>得</b>九。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">遇。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>得</b>便」、「得空即前往拜訪。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">契合、適宜。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「得體」、「得法」。</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;王襃〈聖主<b>得</b>賢臣頌〉：「聚精會神，相<b>得</b>益章。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">快意、滿足。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷六二&middot;管晏傳》：「意氣揚揚，甚自<b>得</b>也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">可以、能夠。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「得過且過」、「不<b>得</b>抽菸！」、「得饒人處且饒人」、「所有員工均<b>得</b>摸彩。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用於談話終了之時，表反對、禁止或同意。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「得了，別再出餿主意了！」、「<b>得</b>，我們就照你的方法去做。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">相當於「何」、「豈」、「那」、「怎」、「難道」等，表示反詰的語氣。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈後苦寒行〉二首之二：「巴東之峽生凌澌，彼蒼迴斡人得知。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">助</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">置於動詞之後，無義。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈絕句漫興〉詩九首之二：「恰似春風相欺<b>得</b>，夜來吹折數枝花。」</span></p></li>

                              得·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5F97.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22536.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢔶</span>䙷<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22528.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢔨</span>【唐韻】【正韻】多則切【集韻】【韻會】的則切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音德。【說文】行有所得也。【玉篇】獲也。【韻會】凡有求而獲皆曰得。</p><p>又賦受亦曰得。【易&middot;乾卦】知得而不知喪。【禮&middot;曲禮】臨財毋苟得。【左傳&middot;定九年】凡獲器用曰得，得用焉曰獲。【孟子】求則得之。</p><p>又貪也。【論語】戒之在得。</p><p>又【韻會】與人契合曰相得。【王褒&middot;聖主得賢臣頌】聚精會神，相得益章。</p><p>又得得，唐人方言，猶特地也。【全唐詩話】貫休入蜀，以詩投王建曰：一瓶一鉢垂垂老，千水千山得得來。</p><p>又叶都木切，音篤。【老子&middot;道德經】罪莫大於可欲，禍莫大於不知足，咎莫大於欲得。【易林】入市求鹿，不見頭足，終日至夜，竟無所得。　【集韻】或作㝶。</p>
