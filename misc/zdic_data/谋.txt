                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>谋</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">謀</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">m&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄡˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>计划，计策，主意：计～。出～划策。</li>	<li>设法寻求：～求。图～。～面。～篇。～生。～幸福。</li>	<li>商议：～议。～划。不～而合。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> plan, scheme; strategem</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> projeter,comploter,rechercher,chercher à obtenir,projet,complot,stratagème</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>谋</strong></p>
<p>謀 <span class="dicpy">m&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，某声。本义:考虑;谋划)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(侧重于商议出办法或盘算出主意) <span class="encs">[consider;plan]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虑难曰谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聪作谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咨难为谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谁适与谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;苍伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在事为诗，未发为谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋&middot;说题辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心思为谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;超奇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谋及子孙，可谓死君乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未能远谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肉食者谋之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚室而谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谋诸漆工。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子&middot;千里马篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谋谟殚神(筹谋划策，竭尽全力);不谋而合;合谋(共同策划);谋及庶人(与百姓共商国事);思谋(方言。思索;考虑);谋始(于开始时慎重考虑);谋章(考虑文章的篇章结构)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 计议;商议 <span class="encs">[confer;discuss]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谋，议也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚室而谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谋迹(议其行事);谋阙(商议补救缺失);谋合(合伙同谋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 图谋;营求 <span class="encs">[plot;conspire;seek]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子谋道不谋食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;卫灵公》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦宜自谋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>

      <div class="yczsl-description">
			<div class="gscdl">——《学而》曾子曰：“吾日三省吾身：为人<span>谋</span>而不忠乎？与朋友交而不信乎？传不习乎？”</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孔子弟子及其再传弟子 《《论语》十二章》</div><div class="gscdl">惩山北之塞，出入之迂也，聚室而<span>谋</span>曰：“吾与汝毕力平险，指通豫南，达于汉阴，可乎？”杂然相许。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·列御寇  列子《愚公移山》</div><div class="gscdl">是故<span>谋</span>闭而不兴，盗窃乱贼而不作，故外户而不闭，是谓大同</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·礼记 《大道之行也》</div><div class="gscdl">其乡人曰：“肉食者<span>谋</span>之，又何间焉？”</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·左丘明 《曹刿论战》</div><div class="gscdl">刿曰：“肉食者鄙，未能远<span>谋</span>。”乃入见。问：“何以战？”</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·左丘明 《曹刿论战》</div><div class="gscdl">若无兴德之言，则责攸之、祎、允等之慢，以彰其咎；陛下亦宜自<span>谋</span>，以咨诹善道，察纳雅言，深追先帝遗诏，臣不胜受恩感激。</div><div class="gscdr">三国·诸葛亮 《出师表》</div>      </div>
