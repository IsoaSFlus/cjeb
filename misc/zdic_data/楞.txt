                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>楞</strong> <span class="dicpy">léng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/léng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (同<span class="yhcs">“棱”</span>。本义:四方木;棱角)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[square timber;arris;edge]</span>。如:楞子(棱角);楞缘(楞角边缘);楞角(物体的边角或尖角)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 物体上一条条凸起来的部分 <span class="encs">[ridge]</span>。如:楞坎(地面突起的陡坎子);楞楞(凸起显露的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">lèng</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/楞层">楞层</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>楞</strong> <span class="dicpy">lèng</span></p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 失神、发呆 <span class="encs">[stupified]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">班惊楞，逡巡来答。——</span><span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:楞子眼(因酒醉或其他原因而眼神呆滞);楞楞 (傻傻的模样);楞神(失神、发呆);楞然(惊奇发呆貌);楞中楞(发呆貌);楞柯柯(亦作<span class="yhcs">“楞磕磕”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“楞呵呵”</span>。形容发呆的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 凶狠;蛮横 <span class="encs">[ferous]</span>。如:楞层(威猛、严厉的样子);楞不讲理</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 冒失，鲁莽 <span class="encs">[rash]</span>。如:楞人(冒失莽撞的人);楞伙子(冒失的小伙子);楞子(指粗鲁莽撞的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">léng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/léng.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
