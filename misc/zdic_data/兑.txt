                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兑</strong> <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从囗，八象气之舒散，兄者与祝同意。从八，与曾同意，今字作悦。本义:喜悦)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[happy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兑，说也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兑为口。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兑者说也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;序卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">佞兑而不曲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;脩身》</span>。注:&ldquo;兑，悦也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见由则兑而倨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;不苟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豫通而不失乎兑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;德充符》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多兑道以为上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;七臣七主》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祷祠而求福，雩兑而请雨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泰族》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兑悦(喜悦)</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>兑</strong> <span class="dicpy">du&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 更换 <span class="encs">[convert;commute]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兑转(交换);兑拨(调拨;调给);兑支(支给);兑便(唐代的一种汇兑方式);兑命(抵命);兑坊(小当铺);把外币兑本国货币</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用天平称银子 <span class="encs">[weigh]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兑准(称准，秤足);兑头(零头，称兑金银中的小差额);兑银子</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="smcs">《易》</span>卦名。八卦之一;又六十四卦之一。象征沼泽 <span class="encs">[one of the eight trigrams of baguah]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兑人(巫师)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 西方。古人认为兑为西方之卦，故亦用以称西方 <span class="encs">[western]</span>。</p>
