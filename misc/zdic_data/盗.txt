                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>盗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dào.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄠˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>偷窃，用不正当的手段营私或谋取：～窃。～贼。～伐。～掘。～运。～卖。～用。～名欺世（亦称“欺世盗名”）。</li>    <li>偷窃或抢劫财物的人：海～。强～。窃国大～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">盗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>盗</strong></p><p>盜 <span class="dicpy">dào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，意思是:看到人家的器皿就会贪婪地流口涎，存心不善。本义:盗窃，偷东西)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[steal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盜，私利物也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窃货曰盗。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·脩身》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子不为盗，贤人不为窃。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·山水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窃人之财犹谓之盗。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盗器为奸。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公十八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盗名不如盗货。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·不苟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如姬果盗兵符与公子。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>国人善盗乎?——<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋·内篇杂下》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:偷盗(偷窃);盗跖下惠(跖和柳下惠，两人虽是兄弟，但跖被诬为大盗，而柳下惠却被视为圣人);盗名(偷取美名)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抢掠劫持 <span class="encs">[rob]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂共盗而残之。——<span class="smcs">《列子·说符》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 窃据;篡夺。用不正当的手段营私或谋取 <span class="encs">[usurp]</span>。如:盗恩(冒取他人的功勋和奖赏);盗篡(盗窃夺取。特指臣子取代君位);盗据(割据;强占)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 诈骗;骗取 <span class="encs">[defraud]</span>。如:欺世盗名;盗言(巧诈甜美的言辞);盗铸(私自铸钱)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 侵犯 <span class="encs">[invade]</span>。如:盗边(侵犯边境)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 私通 <span class="encs">[have illicit intercourse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闻</span><span class="diczx2">平</span>居家时，盗其嫂。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·陈平传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 逃避 <span class="encs">[escape]</span>。如:盗税(逃避纳税)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>盗</strong> <span class="dicpy">dào</span> <span class="ptr"><a
