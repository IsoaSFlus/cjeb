                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>唱，声调有抑扬地念：～赞。歌～。吟～。～唱。</li><li>用诗词等来描述，抒发感情：～梅。～史。～怀。～叹。～叹调（富于抒情的独唱曲）。</li><li>指诗词：佳～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sing song or poem, hum, chant</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ode, Gedicht  (S, Lit)​,in poetischer Form erzählen  (V, Lit)​,rezitativisch singen  (V, Mus)​,rezitieren  (V)​,Lied, Gesang  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> réciter,chanter</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>咏</strong></p><p>詠 <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，永声。又作<span class="yhcs">“詠”</span>。本义:曼声长吟;歌唱)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chant;sing;intone]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">詠，歌也。或从口。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“尧典曰:‘歌永言。’乐记曰:‘歌为之言也，长言之也。说之，故言之;言之不足，故长言之。’”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏拊琴瑟以咏。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓歌诗也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五曰夷则，所咏歌九则，平民无二也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人喜则斯陶，陶斯咏。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咏而归。——<span class="smcs">《论语·先进》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咏殷周之<span class="smcs">《诗》</span>。——</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《东都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:咏士(诗人);咏雪(咏絮。泛指有诗才的女子);咏哦(歌咏吟哦);咏吟(曼声诵读);咏言(吟诗)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用诗词等形式来写景抒情 <span class="encs">[narrate in poetic form]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若是而不从，动而不悛，则文咏物以行之，求贤良以翼之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歌以咏志。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·曹操·步出夏门行》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:咏桑寓柳(喻表面上赞美此事物，实际是为寄托对他事物的情感);咏志(以诗歌抒发心志);咏风嘲月(诗人写诗，常以风月为题材，故常泛称写诗为咏风嘲月)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 歌颂 <span class="encs">[sing the praise of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">含淳咏德之声盈耳。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《四子讲德论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:咏颂(歌颂);咏仁(歌颂仁德);咏德(歌颂高尚的品德)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>咏</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>指诗歌等韵文作品 <span class="encs">[poetry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不有佳咏，何伸雅怀。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《春夜宴从弟桃花园序》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/548F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>爲命切，音泳。【說文】本作詠，歌也。【前漢·東方朔傳】彈琴其中，以咏先王之風。【禮·文王世子】適饌省醴，養老之珍具，遂發咏焉。【註】發咏，謂以樂納之。</p><p>又【集韻】或作永。【詩·魏風】誰之永號。【釋文】作咏。○按《玉篇》《廣韻》諸書本作咏。《字彙》作咏，从二水，附六畫，非，今攺正。咏字右从二水作，不从永。</p><p>（詠）【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>爲命切，音泳。【說文】歌也。【玉篇】長言也。【增韻】詠歌謳吟也。【書·益稷】戛擊鳴球，搏拊琴瑟以詠。【傳】以合詠歌之聲也。【爾雅序】敘詩人之興詠。【疏】詠者，永言也。【前漢·藝文志】詠其聲謂之歌。</p><p>又鳥鳴亦曰詠。【陸機·悲哉行】耳悲詠時禽。【註】禽聲應時而變也。</p><p>又通作咏。【史記·樂書】歌詠其聲也。【禮·樂記】作歌咏。</p><p>又【說文長箋】省作永。【書·舜典】詩言志，歌永言。</p>
