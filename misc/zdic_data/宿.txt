                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>宿</strong> <span class="dicpy">sù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。据甲骨文，象人睡在室内席子上。本义:住宿;过夜)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stay overnight]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宿，止也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出宿于干。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·泉水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">言就尔宿。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·我行其野》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戒及宿之日。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孟子</span>去 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>，宿于昼。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">止</span><span class="diczx2">子路</span>宿，杀鸡为黍而食之。——<span class="smcs">《论语·微子》</span></p><p><span
