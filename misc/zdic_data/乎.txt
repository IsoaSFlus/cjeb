                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>乎</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hū.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>文言助词，表示疑问：汝识之～（吗）？</li><li>文言叹词：陛下与谁取天下～（呀）！</li><li>文言介词，相当“<span class="diczx3">于</span>”（用在动词或形容词后）：在～。无须～。异～寻常。</li><li>古形容词或副词后缀：巍巍～。郁郁～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">呼</span>”，呜呼。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>乎</strong> <span class="dicpy">hū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面的符号表示声音上扬，下面的符号表示舒气。<span class="yhcs">“乎”</span>是<span class="yhcs">“呼”</span>的本字。当<span class="yhcs">“乎”</span>借作语气词、介词用之后，就另造了一个从<span class="yhcs">“口”</span>的形声字<span class="yhcs">“呼”</span>。本义:吐气)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[exhale]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="yhcs">“呼”</span>的古字。呼唤;召唤 <span class="encs">[call]</span>。如:乎号</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>乎</strong> <span class="dicpy">hū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈语〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示疑问或反诘</p><p><span class="diczx1">汝识之乎?——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为人谋而不忠乎?与朋友交而不信乎?传不习乎?——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">近者大乎。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远者凉乎。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝多知乎。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">污其行乎。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">断斯织乎。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不亦精乎。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 相当于<span class="yhcs">“吗”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">学而时习之，不亦说乎!——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可乎。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不闻藏书者乎。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下事有难易乎?——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">学有难易乎。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示感叹语气或呼告</p><p><span class="diczx1">中庸之为德也，其至矣乎!——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二十年之外，</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>其为沼乎!——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长铗归来乎!食无鱼。——<span class="smcs">《史记·平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贤人乎，贤人乎!非质有其内，恶能用之哉?——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有甚是者乎。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天乎。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人乎。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 表示祈使或命令语气。相当于<span class="yhcs">“啊”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“呀”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勉速行乎!无重而罪!——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表示推测语气</p><p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>其亡乎?幸而后败。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p><p><span
