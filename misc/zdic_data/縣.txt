						<p>● <strong>縣</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄢˋ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">縣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>縣</strong></p>
<p>縣 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (會意。从系持倒首。象懸掛的樣子。( <span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )本義:懸掛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 縣假借爲「寰」，爲州縣的縣 <span class="encs">[county]</span>。行政區劃單位。周代縣大於郡，秦以後縣屬於郡。今爲一級行政區劃，隸屬於地區、自治州、直轄市之下</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂縛詣縣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">三國</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邯鄲淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南番二縣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《廣東軍務記》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">龍泉</span>縣。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">劉基</span><span class="smcs">《誠意伯劉文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">開道置縣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:縣尹(一縣的長官);縣學(舊制設在縣裏的學校，以培養人才，教育百姓，稱縣學);縣委(中國共產黨縣一級委員會的簡稱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 官府 <span class="encs">[local authorities]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:縣君(宋代官員妻子的一種封號);縣治(縣衙;縣政府)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代天子所治之地，在京都周圍千里之內，即王畿 <span class="encs">[feud]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:縣內(儒家之說，以四海之內爲九州，其一爲畿內，天子所治);縣主(皇族女子的封號);縣公主(漢代皇女的封號)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另見 <span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p>〈動〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 系掛 <span class="encs">[hang]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">縣，系也。從系，持縣會意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《說文》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">驪姬</span>請使申，處 <span class="diczx2">曲沃</span>以速縣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《國語&middot;晉語》</span>。注:「縊也。」</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不狩不獵，胡瞻爾庭有縣鶉兮?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《詩&middot;魏風&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">爾庭有縣鶉兮。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:縣令(高懸的命令。即「高名」、「虛名」);縣車(懸掛繮繩，停放車子不用。比喻退隱不再出仕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 維繫;拴系;系聯 <span class="encs">[tie]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">縣聯房植。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本經》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">縣棺而封。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《禮記&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 稱量;衡量 <span class="encs">[weigh]</span>。</p>
