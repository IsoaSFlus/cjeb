                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庶</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>庶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>众多：～务。～物。～绩。富～。</li><li>平民，百姓：～民。～人。黎～。</li><li>宗法制度下家庭的旁支，与“<span class="diczx3">嫡</span>”相对：～子（妾生的儿子）。～母（嫡出子女称父亲的妾）。～姓（古代称与帝王没有亲属关系的异姓诸侯）。</li><li>表示希望发生或出现某事，进行推测；但愿，或许：“～竭驽钝，攘除奸凶”。～免于难。～几（ａ．也许可以，表示希望；ｂ．近似，差不多；ｃ．旧指贤者）。～乎可行。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> numerous, various; multitude</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gewöhnlich, üblich ,Shu  (Eig, Fam)</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>庶</strong></p><p>庻 <span class="dicpy">shù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 众多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庶，屋下众也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既庶且多。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·卷阿》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明庶风至。——<span class="smcs">《春秋考异郵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庶众也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庶明厉翼。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">制义庶乎以行之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寒暑不时，以伤庶物。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·渔夫》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且地学之家，历验各种僵石，知动植庶品，率皆递有变迁。——<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:庶常(众多有养德的官员);庶类(众多物类);庶官(百官，众官);庶士(众士，多士;军士);庶事(诸事，众事);庶草(百草众草);庶征(许多迹象);庶尹(百官之长。同<span class="yhcs">“庶正”</span>);庶卉(众草);庶兄弟(众兄弟);庶邦(诸侯众国)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 各种 <span class="encs">[various]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以清酌庶羞之奠，致祭于亡友</span><span class="diczx2">曼卿</span>之墓下。—— <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《祭石曼卿文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:庶羞(各种佳肴);庶政(一切政务，各种政务);庶僚(朝中百官);庶吉士(官名。翰林院的庶常馆，选擅长文学书法之新进士，入院学习，称之);庶绩(各种功绩，众多的功业)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 几乎，将近，差不多 <span
