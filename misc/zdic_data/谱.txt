                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谱</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谱</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">譜</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄨˇ</span></p><ol><li>依照事物的类别、系统制的表册：年～。家～。食～。菜～。～系（ａ．家谱上的系统；ｂ．物种变化的系统）。</li><li>记录音乐、棋局等的符号或图形：歌～。乐（<span class="dicpy">yuè</span>）～。棋～。画～。～号。～表。</li><li>编写歌谱：～曲。～写。～制。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> list, table; musical score</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bandbreite, Skala  (S, Math)​,Liste  (S)​,Spektrum  (S)​,zwanzig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> partition,spectre,certitude,composer</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谱</strong></p><p>譜 <span class="dicpy">pǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，普声。本义:记载事物类别或系统的书)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[table;chart;book]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谱，牒也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谱，籍录也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span>。朱骏声曰:<span class="yhcs">“桓君山云，太史公三代世表，旁行斜上，普效周谱，是谱之名起于周代也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十二曰谱系，以纪世施继序。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·经籍志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:食谱;歌谱;谱学(研究氏族或宗族世系的学科);谱录(用谱表的方式记录世系);家谱;棋谱</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 符号或文字列记乐歌的音节、声调，以便奏唱的籍录 <span class="encs">[music;music score]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自历代至于本朝，雅乐皆先制乐章而后成谱，崇宁以后乃先制谱后命词。——<span class="smcs">《宋史·乐志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:简谱;五线谱;工尺谱;乐谱</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 大致的把握 <span class="encs">[a considerable degree of assurance;a fair amount of confidence]</span>。如:做事有谱儿 </p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 将放射物或波的诸分量按某种可变特征(如波长、质量或能量)分开并排列而成的一种系列 <span class="encs">[spectrum]</span>。如:光谱;能谱</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谱</strong></p><p>譜 <span class="dicpy">pǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 按照事物的类别或系统编排记录 <span class="encs">[excerpt and edit;take notes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谱，布也。布列见其事也，亦曰绪也，主绪人世类相继如统绪也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自</span><span class="diczx2">殷</span>以前诸侯不可得而谱。——<span class="smcs">《史记·三代世表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谱汝诸孙中。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:谱列(按照事物的系统和类别编排);谱注(记叙记载);谱状(记载家族及历史的书籍)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 写曲 <span class="encs">[compose music;set to music]</span>。如:把这首诗词谱成歌曲;这首歌是谁谱的曲</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/谱表">谱表</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
