                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">销</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>销</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">銷</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>熔化金属：～金。～毁。</li>
	<li>去掉：～案。～账。～脏。～魂。～蚀。～声匿迹（形容藏起来，不在公开场合出现）。报～。</li>
	<li>开支，花费：开～。</li>
	<li>出卖货物：～售。～路。供～。</li>
	<li>机器或器物上像钉子的零件：～子。～钉。插～。</li>
	<li>把机器上的销子或门窗上的插销推上。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/消">消</a>&rdquo;，消散，消失。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> melt, fuse; market, sell</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> annulieren, verschmelzen, schmelzen, absetzen, verkaufen  (V)​,Keil</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> verrou,brûler,fondre,vendre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">销</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>销</strong></p>
<p>銷 <span class="dicpy">xiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，肖声。本义:熔化金属)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[melt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">销，铄金也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">销车以斗七命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收天下兵，聚之咸阳，销以为钟鐻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">销锋镐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今出黄金银物百五十种，千八百余斤，销冶以供军用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;魏志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女娲销炼五色石以补苍天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;谈天》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:销冶(熔炼，熔铸，熔冶);销炼(熔化冶炼);销钱(熔化钱币)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 溶化;消融 <span class="encs">[molten]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雪霜顿销释，土脉膏且黏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《苦寒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一天大事，竟如此瓦解冰销。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《官场现形记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;消&rdquo;。清失;消除;消散 <span class="encs">[eliminate;dispel;dissipate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是圣人仆也，是自埋于民，自藏于畔，其声销，其志无穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;则阳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">销忧者莫若酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;东方朔传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫明者起福于无形，销患于未然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刘向传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:销患(化除祸患。也作&ldquo;销祸&rdquo;);销距(化除敌人的抵抗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 耗尽，毁灭 <span class="encs">[exhaust;use up;destroy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薰以香自烧，膏以明自销。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;龚胜传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:销刃(销毁兵刃);销化(消失而化为无形);销失(即&ldquo;消失&rdquo;);销沉(消失沉没。指时光的流逝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 排遣，打发 <span class="encs">[use;get through]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饱食醉酒，忽忽无事，以此销日，以此终年。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">北齐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">颜之推</span><span class="smcs">《颜氏家训》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:销日(排遣时间);销夏(排遣炎热的夏天)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 衰敝;衰残 <span class="encs">[decline;wane;depress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周公</span>遗化销微， <span class="diczx2">孔氏</span>庠序衰坏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;地理志下》</span></p>
