                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>躬</strong></p><p>躳 <span class="dicpy">gōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从身，弓声。从<span class="yhcs">“身”</span>的字多与身体有关。本义:整个身体)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[body]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">躬，身也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">信圭矦守之，躬圭伯守之。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·玉人》</span>。按，琢为人形，信直躳曲。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾党有直躬者。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“直身而行。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">躬腠胝无胈。——<span class="smcs">《史记·司马相如传》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“体也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我鞠躬不敢息。——</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百姓愁苦，靡所错躬(指安身)。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·元帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣鞠躬尽瘁，死而后已。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《后出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:躬体(身体);躬先士卒(身先士卒);躬擐(身穿。擐:穿)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 生命 <span class="encs">[life]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见南</span><span class="diczx2">郢</span>之流风兮，殒余躬于 <span class="diczx2">沅</span>、 <span class="diczx2">湘</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《九叹·远游》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“肱”</span>。手臂从肘到腕的部分。泛指手臂 <span class="encs">[arm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歌终，顾而流涕，张躬而舞。——<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋·内篇谏下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倍中以为躬。倍躬以为左右舌，谓侯之上下幅布也。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡射礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:长躬(衣服的中间部分较长)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>躬</strong> <span class="dicpy">gōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 自身;自己 <span class="encs">[oneself;self]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">已躬命之。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹亲也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">静言思之，躬自悼之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百官之非，宜由联躬。——<span class="smcs">《史记·教本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王若行之，将妨于国家，靡王躬身。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:躬自(自己);躬化(以自身的德行感化别人);反躬自问</p><p>◎ <strong>躬</strong> <span class="dicpy">gōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 亲身;亲自 <span class="encs">[personally]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣本布衣，躬耕于</span><span class="diczx2">南阳</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span
