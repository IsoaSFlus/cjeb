<p>● <strong>柏</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎi.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄞˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>常绿乔木，叶鳞片状，结球果，有&ldquo;扁柏&rdquo;、&ldquo;侧柏&rdquo;、&ldquo;圆柏&rdquo;、&ldquo;罗汉柏&rdquo;等多种。木质坚硬，纹理致密。可供建筑及制造器物之用：～露（柏树上的露水，据说用以洗眼，有明目的作用）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">柏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>柏</strong></p>
<p>栢 <span class="dicpy">bǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，白声。本义:木名，柏树，也称&ldquo;椈&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 柏科柏木属植物的通称 <span class="encs">[cypress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柏，鞠也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">柏，椈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鬯臼以椈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;杂记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岁寒，然后知松柏之后凋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子罕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 柏属约20种。如:侧柏、圆柏、刺柏、台湾扁柏、福建柏等，常绿树，可供观赏和材用，分布于欧亚和北美暖温带和亚热带地区</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 柏叶 <span class="encs">[cypress leaf]</span>。如:柏酒(以柏树叶浸泡的酒)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 特指柏台(御史台的别称。汉御史府中列植柏树，固称) <span class="encs">[cypress terrace]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俱蟠使下之柏，俱擅乙中之二。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄滔</span><span class="smcs">《祭陈侍御》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古国名。故地在今河南省西平县 <span class="encs">[Bai state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">江</span>、 <span class="diczx2">黄</span>、 <span class="diczx2">道</span>、 <span class="diczx2">柏</span>方睦于 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
