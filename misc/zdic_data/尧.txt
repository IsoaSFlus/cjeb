						<p>● <strong>尧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">堯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>传说中上古帝王名：～舜（&ldquo;尧&rdquo;和&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">舜</span>&rdquo;，均为传说中上古的贤明君主。后泛指圣人）。～天舜日（旧时喻太平盛世）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a legendary ancient emperor-sage</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Yao  (Eig, Fam)​,Yáo, Vierter der Fünf Kaiser (<a href="./五帝">五帝</a>, Wǔ dì - mythische Modell-Herrscher, die das Land vor Beginn der Dynastien beherrscht haben sollen.  (Eig, Pers, ca. 2333 - ca. 2234 v.Chr.)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> haut,éminent</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尧</strong></p>
<p>堯 <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>(会意。从垚( <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> )，从兀。&ldquo;垚&rdquo;是土高，兀( <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span>)高耸突出，所以&ldquo;尧&rdquo;表示高。本义:高) 同本义 <span class="encs">[high]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尧，高也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曰若稽古帝尧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:&ldquo;翼善传圣曰尧。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尧亦美谥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;须颂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尧犹荛荛也，至高之貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;号》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尧者，高也，饶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《风俗通&middot;皇霸》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尧</strong></p>
<p>堯 <span class="dicpy">Y&aacute;o</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span>中国古代的皇帝陶唐氏之号。生于伊，嗣后耆，故称伊耆氏;初封陶，后徙唐，又称&ldquo;伊唐氏&rdquo; <span class="encs">[Yao]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长此戴尧天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜审言</span><span class="smcs">《蓬莱三殿传宴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">运生世治，劫生世危，</span><span class="diczx2">尧</span>、 <span class="diczx2">舜</span>、 <span class="diczx2">禹</span>、 <span class="diczx2">汤</span>&hellip;皆应运而生者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:堯(草名，相传当初生于尧的厨房，能自己摇动生风，防止食物变质);尧天(比喻太平盛世);尧封(疆域。传说尧舜时划定我国疆土为十二州，故称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“尧”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/堯">堯</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中" target="_blank">丑集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中&amp;bs=土" target="_blank">土</a>】
                              堯·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/582F.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/212F0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡋰</span>【廣韻】五聊切【集韻】倪幺切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音僥。【說文】高也。从垚，在兀上。高遠也。【白虎通】堯猶嶢也。嶢嶢，至高貌。古唐帝。【書&middot;堯典】曰若稽古帝堯。</p>
<p>又姓。魏堯暄，上黨人，以武功著。</p>
<p>又【諡法】翼善傳聖，善行德義，皆曰堯。</p>
<p>又人名。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】帝擢趙堯爲御史大夫，曰無以易堯。【宋史】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>堯叟，堯咨，堯佐，兄弟皆有聲，世謂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>氏三堯。</p>
<p>又山名。【山海經】美山東北百里曰大堯山，今直隸眞定唐山，縣亦名堯山以堯始封得名。　或作㚁。本作垚，小篆加兀，作堯。兀，會高意。一曰从三土積累而上，象高形。</p>
