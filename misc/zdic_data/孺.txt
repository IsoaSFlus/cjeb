class="encs">[child]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孺，乳子也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孺子早寝晏起。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="encs">[affectionate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">和乐且孺。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·常棣》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:孺慕</p><p>◎ <strong>孺</strong> <span
