                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>糟</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>做酒剩下的渣子：酒～。～粕（亦喻无价值的东西）。～蛋。～糠之妻（指贫穷时共患难的妻子）。</li>
	<li>腐朽，腐烂：～烂。～朽。</li>
	<li>坏：事情办～了。～糕。</li>
	<li>作践，损害：～蹋。～践（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">践</span>&rdquo;读轻声）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sediment, dregs; pickle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pourri,gâté,mauvais,désastreux,lie,céréales fermentées,gâchis</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">糟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>糟</strong> <span class="dicpy">zāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从米，曹声。从米，与制酒有关。本义:古指未漉清的带滓的酒。后指酒渣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[distillers&#39; grains;draff]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">糟，酒滓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共后之致饮于宾客之礼醫酏糟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;酒正》</span>。注:&ldquo;糟，醫酏之不醫者，醫者曰清。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">稻醴清糟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span>。注:&ldquo;糟，醇也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邻有糠糟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梁肉之与糠糟。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何不餔其糟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:糟鼻子(酒糟鼻;红鼻子);糟油(加入甜糟或酒糟调制的油。用来浇拌凉菜或做醮食用);糟魄(酒滓。同&ldquo;糟粕&rdquo;);糟坛(饮酒聚会的地方。即&ldquo;酒坛&rdquo;);糟头(酒鬼，斥骂喜饮酒的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指粗恶的食物 <span class="encs">[coarse food]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:糟食(粗劣的食物)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>糟</strong></p>
<p>蹧 <span class="dicpy">zāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 以酒或酒糟渍物 <span class="encs">[be pickled with distillers&#39; grains or in wine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公不见肉糟淹更堪久邪?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;孔群传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:糟鱼(腌后用酒糟渍之，再经烹烧而成的鱼);糟腌(糟淹，糟醃。用酒或糟加上盐及其他调味品腌制食品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 糟蹋 <span class="encs">[insult;ruin;waste]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:糟趾(糟蹋;蹂躏);糟扰(骚扰)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>糟</strong> <span class="dicpy">zāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 俗称事物败坏为糟 <span class="encs">[in a wretched state;in a mess;bad luck]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这事更糟了，怎么和尚脑袋上长出辫子来呢?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 腐烂 <span class="encs">[rotten;decayed]</span>。</p>

<ol>
	<li>

</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>

	<li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">不好、敗壞。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「糟糕」、「他這學期的成績很<b>糟</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《兒女英雄傳&middot;第一一回》：「這事更<b>糟</b>了，怎麼和尚腦袋上會長出辮子來呢？這不是野忿兒嗎！」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">腐爛、腐朽。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「布<b>糟</b>了。」、「椅腿兒<b>糟</b>了。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《孤本元明雜劇&middot;裴度還帶&middot;第 三折》：「我則見梁漕椽爛柱根<b>糟</b>，這的是欠九分來待倒。」</span></p></li>
