                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>化</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huà  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huà.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄚˋ</span></p><ol><li>性质或形态改变：变～。分～。僵～。教（<span class="dicpy">jiào </span>）～。熔～。融～。潜移默～。～干弋为玉帛。。</li><li>佛教、道教徒募集财物：～缘。～斋。</li><li>用在名词或形容词后，表示转变成某种性质或状态：丑～。绿～。</li><li>习俗，风气：有伤风～。</li><li>特指“化学”：～工。～纤。～肥。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>化</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huā.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄚˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 同“<span class="diczx3">花</span>”。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> change, convert, reform; -ize</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> verändern, verwandeln, umwandeln (V)​,-isierung <Substantivsuffix&gt,auflösen (einer Substanz)​ (V)​,schmelzen (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> changer,transformer,fondre,digérer,se transformer en imitant un modèle,éduquer,transformer les moeurs,convertir,(suffixe)​ -fier,-iser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">化</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>化</strong> <span class="dicpy">huā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用掉、耗费。同<span class="yhcs">“花”</span> <span class="encs">[spend]</span>。如:化钱;化工夫</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">huà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/化子">化子</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>化</strong> <span class="dicpy">huà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (古字为<span class="yhcs">“匕”</span>。会意。甲骨文，从二人，象二人相倒背之形，一正一反，以示变化。本义:变化，改变)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[turn;change]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匕，变也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐灏</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“匕化古今字。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知变化之道。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“在阳称变，在阴称化，四时变化。”</span> <span class="diczx2">荀</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“春夏为变，秋冬为化，坤化为物。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">和故百物化焉。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若欲其化也。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·柞氏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹生也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">状态而实无别而为异者谓之化。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“化者改旧形之名。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胜败若化。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“言转化无常也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因时而化。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终不能化。——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5316.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2090E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠤎</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20BD2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠯒</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/203C1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠏁</span>【唐韻】【正韻】呼霸切【集韻】【韻會】火跨切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>花去聲。【說文】化，敎行也。【增韻】凡以道業誨人謂之敎。躬行于上，風動于下，謂之化。【老子·道德經】我無爲而民自化。</p><p>又以德化民曰化。【禮·樂記】化民成俗。</p><p>又【韻會】天地隂陽運行，自有而無，自無而有，萬物生息則爲化。</p><p>又泛言攺易，亦曰變化。【易·繫辭】擬議以成其變化。</p><p>又貨賄貿易曰化。【書·益稷】懋遷有無化居。</p><p>又革物曰化。【周禮·春官·大宗伯】合天地之化。【註】能生非類曰化。【疏】鳩化爲鷹之類。皆身在而心化。若䑕化爲鴽，雀化爲蛤蜃之等，皆據身亦化，故云能生非類。</p><p>又【正韻】告誥諭使人回心曰化。【書·大誥】肆予大化，誘我友邦君。</p><p>又【公羊傳·桓六年】化我也。【註】行過無禮謂之化，齊人語也。【疏】哀六年傳云：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>乞曰：常之母有魚菽之祭，願諸大夫之化我也。亦是行過無禮之事。</p><p>又州名。【韻會】漢屬合浦郡，宋攺化州。</p><p>又【正韻】呼瓜切，音花。【後漢·馮衍傳】與時變化。【章懷太子註】音花。</p><p>又【韻補】叶胡隈切，音回。【三略】變動無常，因敵變化。不爲事先，動而輒隨。【<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>琳·大荒賦】越洪寧之蕩蕩兮，追<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>漠之造化。跨三五其無偶兮，邈卓立而獨奇。</p><p>又叶呼戈切，音訶。【離騷】初旣與余成言兮，後悔遁而有他。余旣不難夫離別兮，傷靈脩之數化。</p><p>又叶許旣切，希去聲。【後漢·傅幹皇后箴】在昔明后，日新其化。匪惟訓外，亦訓于內。</p><p>又叶呼臥切，呼去聲。【白虎通】火之爲言化也。陽氣用事，萬物變化也。【釋名】火，化也，消化物也。【漢書·敘傳】偪上幷下，荒殖其貨。侯服玉食，敗俗傷化。</p><p>又【總要】化音吪，差錯也，謬言也。从人匕會意。小篆與匕混，故加言作訛。【史記·天官書】其人逢俉化言。</p>
