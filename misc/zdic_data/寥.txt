                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寥</strong> <span class="dicpy">liáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" class="diczx1">上下不相宁，吏民不相寥。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·兵略》</span>。按，与国策民无所聊。</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>寥</strong> <span class="dicpy">liáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本作廫。形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，本义:空虚;寂静)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[empty;silent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">廫，空虚也。字亦作寥。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃入于寥天。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·大宗师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹焦明已翔乎寥廓。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《难蜀父老》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今欲使天下寥廓之上。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·邹阳传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寂兮寥兮，独立而不改。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恣心目之寥朗。——</span><span class="diczx2">孙绰</span><span class="smcs">《游天台山赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:寥朗(空虚明朗);寥天(道教的所谓虚无之境。即太虚);寥廓(空虚);寥然(寂静的样子);寥寥(空寂;孤单);寥阒(寂静);寥天一(清静无为的人入于寂寥，和无形的天合而为一)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 冷清，冷落，寂寞 <span class="encs">[lonely]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寥落悲前事，支离笑此生。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">戴叔伦</span><span class="smcs">《除夜宿石头驿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:寥萧;寥落(荒凉冷落);寥寞(冷清;孤单)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 辽阔;空旷 <span class="encs">[vast;broad]</span>。如:寥宇(广阔的天空);寥汉(辽阔的天空);寥泬(开阔清朗);寥廓(宽广空阔;又形容度量的远大)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 稀少，稀稀拉拉 <span class="encs">[few;scanty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晓星正寥落。——</span><span class="diczx2">谢元暉</span><span class="smcs">《京路夜发诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:寥梢(亦作<span class="yhcs">“寥稍”</span>。稀少);寥寥(形容数量很少)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 深远 <span class="encs">[profound and lasting]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寥，深也。——<span
