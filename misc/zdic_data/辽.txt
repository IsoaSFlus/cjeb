                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>辽</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">遼</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>远：～远。～阔。</li>
	<li>中国朝代名：～代。</li>
	<li>中国辽宁省的简称。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> distant, far</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> entfernt, weit weg  (Adj)​,Liao He (Fluss in der Mandschurei)​  (Eig, Geo)​,Tartaren-Dynastie</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> éloigné,lointain</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>辽</strong></p>
<p>遼 <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，尞( <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:遥远)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[distant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辽，远也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山修远其辽辽兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九叹&middot;忧苦》</span>。注:&ldquo;辽辽，远貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>师辽远，粮食将尽，必将速归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公八年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">眷故乡之辽隔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《登虎牢山赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辽绝(远隔);辽险(遥远险要);辽天(远天);辽阻(辽远阻隔);辽荒(荒凉遥远)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 开阔 <span class="encs">[broad and open]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江山辽落，居然有万里之势。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;言语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辽落(通&ldquo;寥落&rdquo;。寂寥空阔);辽缓(宽缓);辽辽(广远的样子);辽阔(远大广阔);辽空(辽阔的天空);辽原(辽阔原野)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 久远 <span class="encs">[distant; faraway]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人生乐长久，百年自言辽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">阮籍</span><span class="smcs">《咏怀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:辽远(久远);辽廓(久远)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>辽</strong></p>
<p>遼 <span class="dicpy">Li&aacute;o</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 中国辽宁省的简称 <span class="encs">[Liaoning Province]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:辽东之豕(比喻少见多怪。亦比喻平庸无奇);辽饷(明神宗万历四十六年，以对辽左用兵为名，加征田赋银三百万两，叫&ldquo;辽饷&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Liao River]</span>。即今辽河，古名辽水，又名大辽水。如:辽口(辽河入海口)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 朝代名 <span class="encs">[Liao dynasty]</span>。公元916年，契丹族领袖耶律阿保机创建，国号契丹，两年后建都皇都(今内蒙巴林左旗南波罗城)。公元947年改国号为辽(983&mdash;1066年间曾重称契丹)，改皇都为上京。疆域东北至今日本海黑龙江口，西北至蒙古中部，南以今天津海河、河北霸县、山西雁门关一线与宋接界</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;僚&rdquo;。同为官吏，或朋辈 <span class="encs">[colleague;associate in office]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遼党感动。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《外黄令高彪碑》</span></span></p>
