                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>苻</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 即<span class="yhcs">“白英”</span>( Solanum lyratum )。又名鬼目草，茎似葛，叶圆有毛，子如耳珰，赤色，丛生 <span class="encs">[bittersweet]</span>。如:苻蓠(植物名。即莞蒲。白芷的别称)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“莩”</span>，芦苇杆里的薄膜 <span class="encs">[membrane]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芦苻之厚，通于无垠。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·俶真》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓(今读 <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> )。本作<span class="yhcs">“蒲”</span>，至苻坚更改为<span class="yhcs">“苻”</span>。如:苻秦(晋时五胡十六国中之前秦。为苻氏所建，故称);苻朗皂白(谓晋苻朗善识味，食鹅能知其毛色之事)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“符”</span>。符契 <span class="encs">[document]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">即上尚书，参以苻验。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《史晨乡孔庙后碑》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">精通皓穹，三纳苻银。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《李翕析里桥郙阁颂》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“符”</span>。符命，预言未来的神秘文书 <span class="encs">[spell]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">运天苻命。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《北军中侯郭仲奇碑》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/苻雄">苻雄</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
