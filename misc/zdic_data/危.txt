                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>危</strong> <span class="dicpy">wēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形上面是人，中间是山崖，下面腿骨节形。人站在山崖上，表示很高。本义:在高处而畏惧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[phobia in high place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">危，在高而惧也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">决危而美名者。——<span class="smcs">《鬼谷子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“山高也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">榣木不生危。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“高险也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高而不危。——<span class="smcs">《孝经》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“殆也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 恐惧;忧惧 <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窃为君危之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·西周策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:人人自危;危心(心存戒惧);危怖(恐惧不安);危栗(恐惧战栗);危气(气馁;危惧);危悚(危悰，危惧);危视(惊视);危睇(俯视而感到惊恐)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 威胁，摧败 <span class="encs">[threaten]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">能危</span><span class="diczx2">山东</span>者，强 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策一》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 使危险;伤害;损伤 <span class="encs">[injure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今先生之立法术，设度数，臣窃以为危于身而殆于躯。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·问田》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">危士臣，构怨于诸侯。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:危国(危害国家)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 败亡，灭亡 <span class="encs">[be defeated;perish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">魏</span>必危。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策四》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 怀疑，疑虑 <span class="encs">[doubt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫妻相冒，日以相危。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·明理》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>危</strong> <span class="dicpy">wēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 高<span
