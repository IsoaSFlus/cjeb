                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缘</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>缘</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">緣</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>因由，因为：～由。～何（为何，因何）。～故。～起。</li>
	<li>宿命论认为人与人之间命中注定的遇合机会，泛指人与人或人与事物之间发生联系的可能性：～分（<span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n </span>）。化～。姻～。一面之～。</li>
	<li>沿，顺着：～法（沿袭旧法）。～木求鱼。</li>
	<li>边：边～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hem, margin; reason, cause; fate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Grund, Anlaß, Ursache  (S)​,Rand, Kante  (S)​,vorherbestimmte Beziehung ( Verbindung )​, Schicksalsfügung, die Menschen zusammenführt  (S)​,entlang  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> raison,cause,bord,bordure,sort,destin</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缘</strong></p>
<p>緣 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 器物的边沿 <span class="encs">[edge;brink;fringe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">低下头去把嘴唇搁在杯缘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">茅盾</span><span class="smcs">《蚀&middot;追求》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缘石(砌筑在车行道与人行道之间的长条形石块或混凝土块，用以保护人行道并使车行道的路边水流通畅);缘海(边缘海;沿海，接海);缘边(沿边。指边境)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 原故，理由 <span class="encs">[cause;reason]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">璞</span>皆知其名姓及巧诈缘由。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《宋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 因缘;缘分 <span class="encs">[lucky chance;lot or luck by which people are brought together;predestined relationship]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">渠会永无缘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言谈大自缘。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">画与书法为缘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">画与建筑雕刻为缘。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缘悭(无缘分);缘业(姻缘);缘会(缘分);缘契(缘分);缘便(机缘便利);缘悭一面(无缘相见);缘悭分浅(缺少缘分)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缘</strong></p>
<p>緣 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;连&gt;</p>
<p>因为;由于 <span class="encs">[because]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缘物之情。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎行论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缘愁似个长。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《秋浦歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缘土气有早晚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缘何(因何，为何);缘坐(因牵连而获罪。同连坐);缘底(因何;为什么)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>缘</strong></p>
<p>緣 <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 向上爬，攀援 <span class="encs">[climp up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以若所为，求若所欲，犹缘木而求鱼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缘竿(杂技的一种。即爬竿的特技);缘木(爬树);缘心(攀援事物之心);缘竿(杂技中的爬竿节目);缘跻(攀登)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 牵连 <span class="encs">[involve]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百姓有罪，皆案之以法，其缘坐则老幼不免。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《隋书&middot;刑法志》</span></span></p>
