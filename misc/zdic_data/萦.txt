						<p>● <strong>萦</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">縈</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>缭绕：～回。～绕。～纡。～怀。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">萦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>萦</strong></p>
<p>縈 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，荧( <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> )省声。糸，细丝。本义:回旋缠绕)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[coil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">萦，收卷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收绩曰萦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">萦，绕也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">葛藟萦之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;寥木》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百步九折萦岩峦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《蜀道难》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">峻赠怪石列，崎岖曲道萦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:萦抱(环抱);萦环(环绕;佩带环饰);萦萦(缠绕的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 牵缠;牵挂 <span class="encs">[worry;care]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:萦萦(挂念);萦心(牵挂心间);萦挂(牵挂);萦牵(旋绕牵挂);萦绊(牵缠;牵挂);萦惹(牵缠;招引)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;荧&rdquo;。迷惑 <span class="encs">[puzzle;confuse]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:萦悸(迷惑恐惧);萦惑(迷惑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 弯曲 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">断梗桔槎无拍处，一川寒碧自萦回。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">朱熹</span><span class="smcs">《偶题》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:萦薄(草木丛生的弯曲地带)</span></p>
