						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>趋</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">趨</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩˉ</span></p><ol><li>快走：～走。～进。～前。～奉。～翔（快走像鸟展翅飞翔）。～炎附势（奔走于权贵，依附有权势的人）。～之若鹜（像野鸭子一样成群地争着去，含贬义）。</li><li>归向，情势向着某方面发展：～向。～势。大势所～。</li><li>鹅或蛇伸头咬人。</li><li>追求，追逐：～时（追求时髦）。～利。～光性。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>趋</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">趨</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄨˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">促</span>”，催促；急速。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">趋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>趋</strong></p><p>趨 <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从走，刍声。本义:快步走)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[run]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趋，走也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，疾行曰趋，疾趋曰走。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">走而不趋。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王藻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帷薄之外不趋。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则负匮揭箧担囊而趋。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·胠箧》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">急趋之，折过墙隅，迷其所往。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">步趋动作。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:趋蹑(快速尾随);趋行(急行;赶路);趋走(疾走);趋风(快走如风。比喻快步向前走或表示向对方致敬);趋趋(小步急行的样子);趋拜(急往拜见)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 步，行走 <span class="encs">[walk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盈盈公府步，冉冉府中趋。——<span class="smcs">《古乐府·陌上桑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:趋趄(抬腿做出要走的架势);趋跄(步行快慢有节奏，合乎礼节);趋蹲(趋锵。行动整齐而有节奏)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代的一种礼节，小步快走，表示恭敬 <span class="encs">[zip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赐带剑履上殿，入朝不趋。——<span class="smcs">《史记·萧相国世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span>
