                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>乡</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鄉</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄤˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>泛指城市外的区域：～村。穷～僻壤。</li>
	<li>自己生长的地方或祖籍：家～。故～。～井。～里（ ➊家庭久居的地方； ➋同乡的人）。～党（乡里）。～试。</li>
	<li>中国行政区划基层单位，属县或县以下的行政区领导。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> country; rural; village</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bürgerschaft, Gemeinde  (S)​,Dorf, Heimat, Geburtsort  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> village,campagne,canton (division administrative)​,village natal</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乡</strong></p>
<p>鄉 <span class="dicpy">xiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。据甲骨文，像二人对食形。&ldquo;&lsquo;鄉&rsquo;和&lsquo;饗&rsquo;原本是一字&hellip;整个字像两个人相向对坐，共食一簋的情状。本义是用酒食款待别人，是&ldquo;饗&rdquo;的古字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &ldquo;乡&rdquo;假借为行政区域名 <span class="encs">[township]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乡，国离邑民所封乡也。啬夫别治封圻之内六乡六卿治之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五州为乡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span>。注:&ldquo;万二千五百家。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十邑为乡，是三千六百家为一乡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习乡尚齿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行比一乡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其乡人曰:&ldquo;肉食者谋之，又何间焉?&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 唐、宋至今指县以下的行政区划。所辖规模历代不同。又如:乡荐(唐宋时由地方官推荐应进士考试的人);乡科(乡试);乡贡(由州县选拔出应科举的士子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 乡村，城市外的区域 <span class="encs">[countryside;rural area;country]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">传一乡秀才。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《伤仲永》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遍捉乡民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">各乡逆夷</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乡里迓鼓(泛指民间音乐);乡曲之誉(乡里的声望);乡先生(辞官居乡或在乡执教的长者);乡瓜子(见识少的乡下人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 家乡，故乡。在客地称原籍为乡 <span class="encs">[native place;home village]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国者，乡之本也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;权修》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">登斯楼也，则有去国怀乡，忧谗畏讥，满目萧然，感极而悲者矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从乡之先达执经叩问。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乡音无改。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贺知章</span><span class="smcs">《回乡偶书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">采吾乡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扣其乡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乡心(思念故乡的心情);乡味(家乡饮食的风味);乡信(家乡亲友寄来的书信)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 地方;处所 <span class="encs">[place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三世居是乡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:他乡;外乡;异乡;侨乡;帝乡(京城)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 某种超现实的境界 <span class="encs">[an unreal delightful country]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:梦乡;醉乡</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 同一籍贯的人(在外地时说) <span class="encs">[villager]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:乡中(同乡);老乡;同乡</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 用作动词，读 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;ng</span>。通&ldquo;向&rdquo;。面对着 <span class="encs">[face to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东乡坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;田单列传》</span></span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鄕</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“乡”字头，请参考“鄕”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=邑" target="_blank">邑</a>】
                              鄕·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9115.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】許良切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】虛良切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音香。【釋名】鄕，向也，衆所向也。【廣韻】萬二千五百家爲鄕。【前漢&middot;食貨志】五家爲鄰，五鄰爲里，四里爲族，五族爲黨，五黨爲州，五州爲鄕，是萬二千五百戸也。</p>
<p>又上聲。【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>許兩切，與響同。【前漢&middot;董仲舒傳】如影鄕之應形聲。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】與饗通。【前漢&middot;文帝紀】尃鄕獨美其福。</p>
<p>又去聲。【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>許亮切。與嚮同。【禮&middot;曲禮】則必鄕長者所視。【又】請席何鄕。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋宮】兩階閒謂之鄕。【註】人君南鄕當階閒。</p>
<p>又窻牖名。【禮&middot;明堂位】刮楹達鄕。【疏】每室四戸八窻，窻戸皆相對。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;士虞禮】祝從啓牖鄕如初。【註】鄕，牖一名也。【疏】北牖名鄕，鄕亦是牖，故云一名也。</p>
<p>又【正字通】昔也，曩也。往者在前，來者從後，故往者謂之鄕者，往日謂之鄕日。【論語】鄕也，吾見於夫子而問知。</p>
<p>又姓。【集韻】通作向。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/287B0.svg">                          <p>國離邑，民所封鄉也。嗇夫別治。封圻之内六鄉，六鄉治之。从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/286DC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨛜</span>皀聲。許良切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/287B0_10132.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/287B0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨞰</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">國離邑。</span><span class="swdz">離邑，如言離宫别館。國與邑名可互偁。析言之則國大邑小，一國中離析爲若干邑。</span><span class="ywyy">民所封鄉也。</span><span class="swdz">封猶域也。鄉者今之向字。漢字多作鄉。今作向。所封謂民域其中，所鄉謂歸往也。釋名曰：鄉，向也。民所向也。以同音爲訓也。</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20F82.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠾂</span>夫別治。</span><span class="swdz">别彼列切。别治謂分治也。百官公<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠨞</span>表曰：縣大率十里一亭，亭有長。十亭一鄉，鄉有三老，有秩嗇夫、游徼。三老掌敎化。嗇夫職聽訟，收賦稅。游徼徼循禁盜賊。司馬彪百官志曰：鄉置有秩三老游徼。鄉小者置嗇夫一人。風俗通云：嗇者，省也。夫，賦也。言消息百姓，均其役賦。按許不言三老游徼者，舉一以該其二。亦謂鄉小者但置嗇夫，不置三老游徼也。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/286DC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨛜</span>皀聲。</span><span class="swdz">許良切。十部。</span><span class="ywyy">封圻之內六鄉、六卿治之。</span><span class="swdz">按封圻上當有周禮二字。上云嗇夫别治，言漢制。此云六鄉六𠨞治之，謂周禮也。封圻卽邦畿。周禮：方千里曰國畿。六鄉地在遠郊以内。五家爲比，五比爲閭，四閭爲族，五族爲黨，五黨爲州，五州爲鄉，鄉老二鄉則公一人，鄉大夫每鄉<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠨞</span>一人。許先舉漢制，後言周禮者，許書凡言郡縣鄉亭皆漢制。漢表云：凡縣道國邑千五百八十七，鄉六千六百二十二，亭二萬九千六百三十五。許全書所舉某縣某鄉某亭皆在此都數之中。</span></p>                          </div>
