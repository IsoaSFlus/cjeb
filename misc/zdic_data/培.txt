						<p>● <strong>培</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">p&eacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄟˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>为保护植物或墙堤等，在根基部分加土：～土。～种（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）。～修。～植。</li>	<li>帮助和保护人的成长：～养。～育。～训。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>培</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，咅( <span class="dicpy">pǒu</span>)声。本义:给植物或墙堤等的根基垒土)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[earth up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">培，培敦土田山川也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，垒土也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若见垒培。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;垒墼曰培。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故栽者培之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凿培而遁之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;齐俗》</span>。注:&ldquo;培屋后墙也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">高培则拔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;辨土》</span>。注:&ldquo;培田侧也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:培户(昆虫把土堆在洞穴周围)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 增益;增添 <span class="encs">[add]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:培敦(增加其封地);培堆(层层叠起;堆高)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 培养，教育 <span class="encs">[cultivate;educate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:栽培(种植;培养);培壅(培养)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 把东西埋在灰、沙中保存 <span class="encs">[bury in sand or ash]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:以灰培瓜子;放在沙中培着</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;凭&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;ng</span>)</p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>凭借、依靠 <span class="encs">[by force of;depend on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而后乃今培风。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
