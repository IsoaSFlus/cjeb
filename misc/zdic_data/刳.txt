                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>刳</strong> <span class="dicpy">kū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，夸声。本义:剖，剖开)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cut open]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刳，判也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刲之刳之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刳剔孕妇。——<span class="smcs">《伪泰誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引刀自刳其腹。——</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:刳割(剖割);刳木(剖凿大木，使其中空);刳肝(剖挖肝脏);刳胎(剖割孕妇胎儿)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挖，挖空 <span class="encs">[hollow out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刳木为舟，剡木为楫。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:刳心(挖出心脏);刳腔(挖空的腹腔)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 杀，割 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刳白马而盟。——<span class="smcs">《史记·苏秦列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:刳羊(古时外交结盟时杀羊宣誓);刳割(切割)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 洗除，消除 <span class="encs">[eliminate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫道，覆载万物者也，洋洋乎大哉!君子不可以不刳心焉。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 遭受残害 <span class="encs">[cruelly injure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">江东</span>新刳于兵，郡旱，饥民交走死无吊。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《曹成王碑》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/刳刀">刳刀</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
