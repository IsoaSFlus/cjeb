						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>扭</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">niǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄧㄡˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>转动，扳转：～过脸来。～头。</li>
	<li>拧断：强～的瓜不甜。</li>
	<li>拧伤：～了腰。</li>
	<li>身体摇摆转动：～动。～捏。～秧歌。</li>
	<li>揪住：～打。</li>
	<li>违拗：胳臂～不过大腿。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> turn, twist, wrench; seize, grasp</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> drehen, rotieren  (V)​,festnehmen, (einander)​ packen  (V)​,ringen  (V)​,sich wenden, sich drehen  (V)​,tänzeln, tänzelnd gehen  (V)​,verdrehen, verrenken  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tourner,tordre,se fouler,twist</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扭</strong> <span class="dicpy">niǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，丑声。本义:用手拧)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wring]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:把衣扭干;在他脸上扭了一下;扭绞(用两股以上的条状物向相反的方向转动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 揪住 <span class="encs">[grapple with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大众见他二人扭在一起。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《官场现形记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扭殴(揪打);扭拽(拉扯);扭拽(揪住);扭搜(硬挤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 扭动;摇摆 <span class="encs">[swing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">只消把腰儿扭一扭就过去了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扭扭屹屹(躯体扭动的样子);扭扭捏捏(身体摆动的样子);扭秧歌(跳秧歌舞);扭搐(抽搐);扭造(做作;不自然)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 歪曲;生硬编造 <span class="encs">[distort]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扭曲作直(谓颠倒是非);扭捻(谓生硬编造)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 拧伤筋骨 <span class="encs">[sprain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可扭了腰没有。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扭筋(因用力过猛，导致筋肉受挫伤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 争执;违拗 <span class="encs">[dispute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那里扭得过他。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扭别(别扭，不顺遂);别扭(不顺心;难对付;意见不相投);胳膊扭不过大腿</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 掉转，转动 <span class="encs">[turn round;turn away]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">杨志</span>听得弓弦响，扭回身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扭头向后看;一扭身跑了</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>扭</strong> <span class="dicpy">niǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>手铐 <span class="encs">[handcuffs]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抱钳扭，徙幽裔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;蔡邕传论》</span></span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>扭</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄋㄧㄡˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>niǔ <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1675.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">手握緊東西而旋轉。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「扭乾衣服」、「扭開門把」。</span><span class="gc_yy">元&middot;無名氏《硃砂擔&middot;第二折》：「我這領布衫著雨淋溼了也，你與我扭一扭。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">回轉、掉轉。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「扭頭就走」、「扭過臉來」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《水滸傳&middot;第一三回》：「周謹在馬上聽得腦後弓弦響，扭轉身來，便把傍牌來迎，卻早接個空。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">抓、揪住。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「扭打在一起」、「警察扭住小偷不放」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《官場現形記&middot;第三一回》：「大眾見他二人扭在一處，只得一齊住手，過來相勸。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">身體左右搖擺。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「她走路時喜歡扭腰擺臀的賣弄風騷。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《西遊記&middot;第二二回》：「這條河若論老孫去時，只消把腰兒扭一扭，就過去了。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用力過猛使筋骨或肌肉折轉而受傷。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「小心別扭了腰。」、「扭傷腳踝」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">違逆、不順從。</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第四回》：「薛蟠見母親如此說，情知扭不過，只得吩咐人夫，一路奔榮國府而來。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
