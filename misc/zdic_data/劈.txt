                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">劈</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>劈</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用刀斧或强力破开：～杀。～刺。～成两半。</li>
	<li>雷电击坏或击毙：天打雷～。</li>
	<li>冲着，正对着：～头盖脑。</li>
	<li>两个斜面合成的纵截面呈三角形的简单机械，刀、斧、楔子等各种切削工具的刃都属于这一类（亦称&ldquo;尖劈&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>劈</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>分开：～柴。～成三股。</li>
	<li>分裂，使从原物上分开：把菜帮儿～下来。</li>
	<li>腿或手指等过分叉开：～叉。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">劈</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>劈</strong> <span class="dicpy">pī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，辟声。本义:破开;分割)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chop;cleave;split]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劈，破也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劈，分也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劈，剖也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《埤苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以刀劈狼首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劈心(剖心);劈半儿(分做两半);劈拉(成八字分开);劈笺(裁纸);劈空(划破长空)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 俗称雷击为&ldquo;劈&rdquo; <span class="encs">[strike]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:劈雷(打雷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 正对着; 冲着 <span class="encs">[be right against (one&rsquo;s face)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">便把鱼汁劈脸泼将去，淋那酒保一身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劈胸只一提，丢翻在雪地上。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劈心(对准心口);劈腰(当腰，正对着腰);劈脊(正对着脊背);劈口(正对着嘴);劈头劈脸(正对着头和脸而来);劈先里(首先;开头);劈头(立即;马上);劈空(突然;忽然)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 辨 <span class="encs">[distinguish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举其理，则皆谟明渊沉，剖微穷深，劈析是非，校度古今。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《答问》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>劈</strong> <span class="dicpy">pī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>简单机械，由两个斜面合成，纵截面呈三角形，木工、金工用的楔子和刀、斧等各种切削工具的刃都是劈 <span class="encs">[wedge]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尖劈</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>劈</strong> <span class="dicpy">pī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 〈方〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 不能自持 <span class="encs">[lose self-control]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:高兴劈了;喜欢劈了</span></p>
