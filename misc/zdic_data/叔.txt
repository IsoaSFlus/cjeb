                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">叔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>叔</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>兄弟排行次序第三：伯、仲、～、季。</li>
	<li>父亲的弟弟，亦称跟父亲同辈而年纪较小的男子：～父。大～。</li>
	<li>丈夫的弟弟：小～子。～嫂。</li>
	<li>拾：&ldquo;九月～苴&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> father's younger brother</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Onkel  (S)​,tertiär  (Adj, Chem)​,Shu  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> oncle</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">叔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叔</strong> <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>会意兼形声。同本义 <span class="encs">[pick up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叔，拾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。汝南名收芋为叔。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">九月叔苴，采荼薪樗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叔</strong> <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为&ldquo;少&rdquo;。年幼的 <span class="encs">[young]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叔者，少也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;姓名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父之兄弟后生为叔父，父之弟妻为叔母。又夫之弟为叔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伯某甫，仲叔季，唯其所当。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古时兄弟长幼顺序常用&ldquo;伯、仲、叔、季&rdquo;或&ldquo;孟、仲、叔、季&rdquo;表示，叔表示排第三的</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仲叔继幽沦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《哭连州凌员外司马》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 末;衰 <span class="encs">[last;weak;feeble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">同姓小邦，则曰叔父，异姓小邦，则曰叔舅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;觐礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆叔世也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span>。注:&ldquo;晚世也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然所遇之时，实是叔世。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;刘颂传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叔世(末世，衰乱的年代);叔末(叔世、末世的合称);叔季(末也，国家衰乱将亡的时代，又指少年时代);叔代(衰乱的时代)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>叔</strong> <span class="dicpy">shū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 称父亲的弟弟 <span class="encs">[uncle;father&#39;s younger brother]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">数</span><span class="diczx2">吕师孟</span>叔侄为逆。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叔伯姆(妯娌);叔翁(父亲的叔父，即&ldquo;叔祖&rdquo;)。或常用于对男性年长者(如对家庭的密友)的客气的亲切的称呼。如:叔侄;大叔;对中年男子的尊称。犹言&ldquo;阿叔&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 女性对其丈夫的弟弟的称呼 <span class="encs">[husband&#39;s younger brother]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我们山里人，小户人家，这算得什么?她有小叔子，也得娶老婆。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《祝福》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叔舅(母亲的弟弟;周天子对异姓小邦诸侯的称呼);叔丈人(妻子的叔父);叔丈母(妻子的婶母);叔郎(丈夫的弟弟，即小叔);叔妹(丈夫的妹妹;即&ldquo;小姑&rdquo;);叔姑(丈夫的叔母，即&ldquo;婶婆&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;菽&rdquo;。豆类的总称 <span class="encs">[beans]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子见夫牺牛乎?衣以文绣，食以刍叔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御冠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以叔粟当赋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;昭帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p>
