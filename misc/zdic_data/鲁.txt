                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鲁</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鲁</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">魯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>愚拙，蠢笨：愚～。粗～。～莽。</li>
	<li>周代诸侯国名，在今山东省西南部。</li>
	<li>山东省的别称。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> foolish, stupid, rash; vulgar</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> grob, derb ,Lu  (Eig, Fam)​,Kleiner Staat während der Zeit der Streitenden Reiche (<a href="./戰國">戰國</a> Zhànguó, 475 - 221 v. Chr.)​ im Alten China; Heimatstaat des Konfuzius (<a href="./孔子">孔子</a> Kǒngzǐ)​  (Gesch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> stupide,grossier,rude</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鲁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鲁</strong></p>
<p>魯 <span class="dicpy">lǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文从鱼，从口，&ldquo;口&rdquo;象器形。整个字形象鱼在器皿之中。本义:鱼味美，嘉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(fish) tasty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">(古鲁字)从曰，不从白，彝器每言&ldquo;鲁休&rdquo;，&ldquo;纯鲁&rdquo;，</span><span class="diczx2">阮氏元</span>云:&ldquo;鲁本义盖为嘉，从鲁入口，嘉美也。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">林义光</span><span class="smcs">《文源》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 迟钝，愚钝 <span class="encs">[dull;slow-witted]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">参</span>也鲁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鲁人以为敏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">容居，鲁人也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鲁拙(笨拙);鲁人(鲁钝的人，愚钝的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 莽撞，粗野 <span class="encs">[hot-headed;reckless;rash]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:粗鲁(粗暴鲁莽)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鲁</strong></p>
<p>魯 <span class="dicpy">Lǔ</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 中国春秋时国名 <span class="encs">[Lu state]</span>，在山东省南部。周武王封其弟周公旦于鲁。战国时为楚所灭</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 山东省的简称 <span class="encs">[Shandong province]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:鲁人(山东人);鲁鸡(鲁地所产的一种大鸡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">魯</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“鲁”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/魯">魯</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中" target="_blank">亥集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中&amp;bs=魚" target="_blank">魚</a>】
                              魯·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9B6F.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23950.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣥐</span>【廣韻】【正韻】郞古切【集韻】【韻會】籠五切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音虜。【說文】鈍詞也。【論語】參也魯。【何晏註】魯，鈍也。曾子性遲鈍。</p>
<p>又國名。【詩&middot;魯頌譜】魯者，少昊摯之墟也。【前漢&middot;地理志】周興，以少昊之虛曲阜封周公子伯禽爲魯侯，以爲周公主。【釋名】魯，魯鈍也。國多山水，民性樸魯也。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】伯禽之後，以國爲姓，出扶風。</p>
<p>又複姓有魯步氏。</p>
<p>又【集韻】旅，古作魯。註詳方部六畫。</p>
