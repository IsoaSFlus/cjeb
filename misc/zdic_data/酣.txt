						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>酣</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>酒喝得很畅快：～饮。半～。～醉。酒～耳热。</li><li>尽量，痛快：～畅。～赏（恣意游赏）。～眠。～然。</li><li>浓，盛：～春（春色正浓）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>酣</strong> <span class="dicpy">hān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从酉，从甘，甘亦声。从<span class="yhcs">“酉”</span>，表示与酒有关。<span class="yhcs">“甘”</span>也具有表意作用。本义:酒喝得很畅快)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[drink to one’s heart’s content]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酣，酒乐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酣，乐也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酒酣，上击筑自歌。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">代君至酒酣。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·长攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>饮酒酣。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酒酣，公子起，为寿</span><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>前。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宴酣之乐。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:酣湑(畅饮欢乐);酣饫(酒醉饭饱);酣歌(畅饮乐极而歌唱);酣兴(尽情喝酒的乐趣);酣叫(畅饮且大声地呼叫);酣放(嗜酒畅饮而行为放纵);酣酣(畅饮醉酒的样子);酣宴(纵情饮宴)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 浓盛;盛美 <span class="encs">[flourishing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荷花落日红酣。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《题西太一宫壁》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:酣春(春色最盛美的时候);酣酣(形容景色极盛美)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 舒畅痛快 <span class="encs">[merry and lively]</span>。如:酣边(畅快，高兴);酣歌(忘情高歌);酣笑(大笑);酣足(痛快满足);酣悦(畅快喜悦)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 睡眠甜浓 <span class="encs">[soundly]</span>。如:酣沉(形容睡眠浓甜而深沉);酣卧(熟睡);酣呼(酣睡时打呼噜);酣寝(熟睡)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 充足;饱满 <span class="encs">[satisfied]</span>。如:酣畅(感情饱满，表达尽意);酣边(兴头上，兴趣正浓时)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>酣</strong> <span class="dicpy">hān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button
