						<p>● <strong>闼</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">闥</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>门，小门：排～直入（推开门就进去）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> door; gate</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>闼</strong></p>
<p>闥 <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，达声。本义:小门)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(small) door]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">哙</span>乃排闼直入。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;樊哙传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窗户闼(窗户上的小门);闼门(小门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 门内 <span class="encs">[inside door]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在我闼兮，履我发兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;东方之日》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 门楼上的小屋 <span class="encs">[a small house over gateway]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上飞闼而仰眺。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闥</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“闼”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/闥">闥</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              闥·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=21" target="_blank">21</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95E5.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>他達切，音撻。【說文】門也。【博雅】闥謂之門。【詩&middot;齊風】彼姝者子，在我闥兮。【傳】闥，門內也。【釋文】闥，他達反。韓詩云：門屛之閒曰闥。</p>
<p>又【韻會】漢號禁門曰黃闥。【前漢&middot;高后紀】不出房闥。【註】闥，中小門。【樊噲傳】噲乃排闥直入。【註】闥，土曷反。宮中小門也。一曰門屛也。</p>
<p>又闈也。【後漢&middot;桓帝紀】乙未，南宮承善闥火。【註】闈謂之闥。</p>
<p>又【集韻】巨偃切，音寋。拒門木。【玉篇】飛闥，突出方木也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶音脫。【崔駰&middot;達旨】攀台階闚紫闥，據高軒望朱闕。</p>
<p>又叶音隊。【曹植&middot;王粲誄】我王建國，百司俊乂，君以顯舉，秉機省闥。</p>
