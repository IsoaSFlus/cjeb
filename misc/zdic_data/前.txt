                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">前</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>前</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>指空间，人面所向的一面；房屋等正门所向的一面；家具等靠外的一面，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">后</span>&rdquo;相对：～面。～边。～方。面～。～进。～程。</li>
	<li>指时间，过去的，往日的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">后</span>&rdquo;相对：以～。～人。～此。～科。～嫌。～言。～车之鉴。</li>
	<li>顺序在先的：～五名。</li>
	<li>向前行进：勇往直～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> in front, forward; preceding</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> vor, vorne, bevor</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> avant,devant,précédent,ancien,pré-</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">前</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>前</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本义:前进)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[go forward;go ahead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前，进也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然而驱之不前，却之不止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说右上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">相如</span>视 <span class="diczx2">秦王</span>无意偿 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>城，乃前曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">罗敷</span>前致词。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;陌上桑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听妇前致词。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狼不敢前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驰而前。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>下车而前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:前却(进退);前迈(前行，前进);前旌(仪仗中前行的旗帜)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引导 <span class="encs">[guide;lead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尸谡祝前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;特牲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹导也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">张若</span>、 <span class="diczx2">謵</span>朋前马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;徐无鬼》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:前王(古代举行仪式时在前面引导帝王);前马(在马前引导或护卫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 进见 <span class="encs">[call on (sb.holding high office)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">允</span>乞更一见，然后为诏。诏引前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《魏书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;翦&rdquo;。剪裁 <span class="encs">[cut;trim]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无前则徵多，徵多故富。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;八经》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>前</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 前面，与&ldquo;后&rdquo;相对;正面 <span class="encs">[ahead;forward;front]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在前上处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;简兮》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百夫荷素质之旗于王前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;克殷》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">近出前后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犬坐于前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恐前后受敌。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:前队(前锋);前途(前面;前边);前件(前已述及的人或事物;逻辑学用语。与&ldquo;后件&rdquo;对称。假言判断中规定条件的判断叫&ldquo;前件&rdquo;，以前件为条件的判断叫&ldquo;后件&rdquo;);前行(前锋);前和(枪的前额);前轨(犹前辙);前军(先头部队)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 与&ldquo;后&rdquo;相对，时间在先 <span class="encs">[ago;before;former]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我未之前闻也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹故也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前事之不忘，后事之师也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇纪赞》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">前人之述。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">说前代事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:前倨后恭(先傲慢，后谦恭);前光(先人的光辉);前番(前一次;上一次);前埭(上次);前限(前次的限期);前半三世(前半生);前家(前妻);前家儿(前妻之子);前星(指太子);前行(旧时美称吏员);前绪(前人的功业)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 预先;事前 <span class="encs">[in advance;beforehand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可以前知。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span>。注:&ldquo;亦先也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至于怨诽之多，则固前知其如此也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">在正面的、脸所向的一面。与「后」相对。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「前门」、「前方」、「面<b>前</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《论语&middot;子罕》：「瞻之在<b>前</b>，忽焉在后。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈静夜思〉诗：「床<b>前</b>明月光，疑是地上霜。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">次序在先的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>前</b>三名」、「请大家坐到<b>前</b>排来。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">过去的、较早的。与「后」相对。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「前天」、「前人种树，后人乘凉。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记&middot;卷六&middot;秦始皇本纪》云：「前事之不忘，后事之师也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">已成历史的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>前</b>清」、「<b>前</b>苏联」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">前任的简称。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「前妻」、「<b>前</b>总理」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">未来的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「前程」、「前途」、「前景看好」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">向<b><b>前</b></b>走。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「勇往直<b>前</b>」、「畏缩不<b>前</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韩愈〈左迁至蓝关示姪孙湘〉诗：「云横秦岭家何在？雪拥蓝关马不<b>前</b>。」</span></p></li>
