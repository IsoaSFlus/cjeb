                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">皓</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>皓</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄠˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>洁白，明亮：～白。～齿。～首穷经。～月当空。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/昊">昊</a>&rdquo;，昊天。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bright, luminous; clear; hoary</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hell, klar</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">皓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>皓</strong></p>
<p>皜 <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从白，告声。本作&ldquo;晧&rdquo;。本义:光明)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bright;luminous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日出皓兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;陈风&middot;月出》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皓月千里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:皓月(明月);皓旰(光明亮丽);皓颢(明亮洁白);皓洁(明亮洁白)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 白;洁白 <span class="encs">[white]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皓，白也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白石皓皓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;扬之水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皓皓乎不可尚已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有五六老叟，庞眉皓发。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;刘庞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曼理皓齿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;杨权》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延颈秀项，皓质呈露。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绛皓驳色。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:皓腕(素腕，指女子洁白的手腕);皓皓(洁白的样子;光明的样子);皓手(洁白的手);皓然(洁白的样子);皓齿明眸(洁白的牙齿，明亮的眼睛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;昊&rdquo;。广大的样子 <span class="encs">[vast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闇乎天下之晦盲也，皓天不复，忧无疆也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皓天舒白日。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《咏史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太皓悦和，雷声乃发 。又如:皓然(元气盛大流行的样子);皓天(天的泛称;即昊天);皓荡(广阔天边的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>皓</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>借指老翁 <span class="encs">[old man]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">送尔</span><span class="diczx2">长江</span>万里心，他年来访 <span class="diczx2">南山</span>皓。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《金陵歌送别范宣》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">白、潔白。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「皓髮」、「明眸皓齒」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷四○&middot;張良傳》：「年皆八十有餘，須眉皓白，衣冠甚偉。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韋莊〈菩薩蠻&middot;人人盡說江南好〉詞：「爐邊人似月，皓腕凝雙雪。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">光明、明亮。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「皓月當空」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜牧〈長安夜月〉詩：「寒光垂靜夜，<b>皓</b>彩滿重城。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;姜夔〈踏莎行&middot;燕燕輕盈〉詞：「淮南皓月冷千山，冥冥歸去無人管。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">皓</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中" target="_blank">午集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中&amp;bs=白" target="_blank">白</a>】
                              皓·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7693.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】胡老切【集韻】下老切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音昊。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】光也。【小爾雅】白也。【博雅】皓皓，明也。【詩&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>風】月出皓兮。【揚子&middot;淵騫篇】明星皓皓。</p><p>又【集韻】潔白也。【詩&middot;唐風】揚之水，白石皓皓。【朱註】又胡暴反，叶下鵠。鵠，音告。</p><p>又虛曠貌。【大戴禮】常以皓皓，是以眉壽。</p><p>又皓膠，水凍貌。【楚辭&middot;大招】霧雨淫淫，白皓膠只。</p><p>又【廣韻】顥，今作皓。【說文】商山四顥，白首老人也。【史記&middot;留侯世家】四人從太子，鬚眉皓白。【師古註】所以謂之四皓。</p><p>又通作昊。太皓，天也。【後漢&middot;郞顗傳】太皓悅和，靁聲乃發。【荀子&middot;賦篇】皓天不復。【註】皓，同昊。</p><p>又天皓，星名。【史記&middot;天官書】歲隂在丑，星居寅，以十二月與尾箕晨出，曰天皓。【前漢&middot;天文志】作天昊。</p><p>又與皞同。【楚辭&middot;遠遊】歷太皓以左轉。【註】卽太皞也。</p><p>又【唐韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古老切，音杲。皓皓，潔白精瑩貌。</p><p>又姓。【吳越春秋】句踐大夫皓進。</p><p>又【集韻】呼回切，音灰。髮皓落也。劉昌宗說。　【玉篇】同皜。【集韻】或作皞暠䯫。【廣韻】从日作晧。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
