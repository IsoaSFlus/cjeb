                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>绊</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">絆</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄢˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 行走时被别的东西挡住或缠住，引申为束缚或牵制：～住。～倒。羁～（束缚）。～脚石（喻阻碍前进的东西）。～手～脚。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>绊</strong></p><p>絆 <span class="dicpy">bàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸，半声。本义:驾车时套在牲口后部的皮带)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[crupper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绊，马絷也。字亦作跘。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靽，半也，拘使半行不得自纵也。——<span class="smcs">《释名·释车》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指系鸟兽的绳子</p><p><span class="diczx1">饰五采之华绊，结璇玑之金环。——</span><span class="diczx2">傅玄</span><span class="smcs">《鹰赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 喻阴谋、圈套 <span class="encs">[snare]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">连兄弟</span><span class="diczx2">燕青</span>也着绊了。——<span class="smcs">《燕青博鱼》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 亦用以比喻误中圈套而受害</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 装束，穿戴 <span class="encs">[dress]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国人之绊。——<span class="smcs">《瀛涯胜览》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>绊</strong></p><p>絆 <span class="dicpy">bàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>动</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 羁绊;拘束;束缚 <span class="encs">[tie]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">细推物理须行乐，何用浮荣绊此身。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《曲江二首》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今绊</span><span class="diczx2">姜维</span>于沓中，使不得东顾。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用绳子把足系住 <span class="encs">[tie the foot]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不羁不绊。——</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《交州牧箴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:绊脚(用绳子绑住脚)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 走路或跑步时被别的东西挡住或缠住 <span class="encs">[stumble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">被门槛子绊倒。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:绊倒</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
