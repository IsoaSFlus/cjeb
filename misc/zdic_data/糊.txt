<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>糊</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨˉ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 涂抹或粘合使封闭起来：～了一层泥。</p>
<ol>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>糊</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>粘合，涂附：裱～。～墙。～窗户。</li>
	<li>粥类：～口。</li>
	<li>〔～涂〕➊不明事理；认识模糊混乱，如&ldquo;他太～～了&rdquo;；➋内容混乱的，如&ldquo;～～账&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">煳</span>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

<ol>
	<li>像粥一样的食物：面～。辣椒～。</li>
	<li>欺骗，蒙混，敷衍：～弄。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>糊</strong> <span class="dicpy">hū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 涂抹 <span class="encs">[plaster]</span>。如:糊墙缝;糊窟窿;糊上一层泥</p>
