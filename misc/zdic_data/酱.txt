                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">酱</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>酱</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">醬</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄤˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用发酵后的豆、麦等做成的一种调味品：甜面～。豆瓣～。</li>
	<li>用酱或酱油腌制：～菜。～瓜。</li>
	<li>像酱的糊状食品：果～。芝麻～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> any jam-like or paste-like food</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Sauce</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pâte de soja fermenté,sauce de soja,purée</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">酱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>酱</strong></p>
<p>醬 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从肉，从酉。酒以和酱也。爿声。古文从酉，爿声。籀文从酉，从皿，爿声。本义:用盐醋等调料腌制而成的肉酱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用麦、面、豆等发酵制成的调味品 <span class="encs">[thick paste made from fermented soya beans]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酱用百有二十瓮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;膳夫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不得其酱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">濡鸡醢酱，濡鱼卵酱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:黄酱;炸酱;甜酱;豆瓣酱;酱坊;酱豆(盛酱的食器);酱瓜(即越瓜);酱清(古代调味品。相当于今之酱油)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 将鱼、肉、蔬、果捣烂制成的糊状食品 <span class="encs">[paste;jam]</span>。如:果子酱;花生酱</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>酱</strong></p>
<p>醬 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用酱或酱油腌制 <span class="encs">[pickle in soya sauce]</span>。如:把萝卜酱一酱;把豆腐酱了再吃;酱瓜儿(用酱腌制的瓜)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 搅;混杂 <span class="encs">[mix]</span>。如:酱泥(沾染了污泥)</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/91AC.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/287FB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨟻</span>【廣韻】【正韻】子亮切【韻會】卽亮切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音將。【說文】醢也。【韻會】醢，肉醬也。</p>
<p>又豉醬。</p>
<p>又菜茹亦謂之醬。【正字通】麥麪米豆皆可罨黃，加鹽曝之成醬。【禮&middot;曲禮】醯醬處內。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;膳夫】醬用百有二十罋。【註】醬，謂醯醢也。【史記&middot;西南夷傳】南越食蒙蜀枸醬。【註】劉德云：枸樹如桑，其椹長二三寸，味酢，取其實以爲醬。【枚乗&middot;七發】熊蹯之臑，芍藥之醬。　【說文】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24595.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤖕</span>。臑字原从足从而作。</p>
