                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>缩</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">縮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">suō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄛˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>向后退，往回收：退～。畏～。～手（喻不敢再做下去）。～手～脚（喻做事顾虑多，不大胆）。</li>
	<li>由大变小，由长变短：收～。伸～。压～。～减。～小。～编。～微（指利用照相技术等把文字图像缩成极小的胶卷复制品）。</li>
	<li>捆束：&ldquo;～版以载&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>直，理直：&ldquo;自反而～，虽千万人，吾往矣&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>滤酒去渣：～酒。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>缩</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">縮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">s&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～砂密〕多年生草本植物，种子棕色，亦称&ldquo;缩砂&rdquo;。种子入中药，称&ldquo;砂仁&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>缩</strong></p>
<p>縮 <span class="dicpy">suō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，宿声。糸，细丝，与绳索有关。本义:捆束)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bind;tie]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其绳则直，缩版以载。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;緜》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缩，乱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱</span>曰:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">许</span>书之乱字皆治也，理也。此说解及尔雅之乱，正谓治理。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">磬阶闲缩霤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡饮酒礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十纯则缩而委之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼&middot;大射仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绞横三缩一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士丧礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冠缩缝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">布绞缩者一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;丧大纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 收缩 <span class="encs">[contract;shrink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">论战斗之事，则缩颈而股慄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缩栗(冷缩颤栗);缩气(收敛气焰);缩瑟(瑟缩。畏缩貌);缩首(点头)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 退缩;退，减 <span class="encs">[draw back;withdraw;recoil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是日风静，舟行颇迟，又秋深潦缩，故得尽见</span><span class="diczx2">杜老</span>所谓&ldquo;幸有舟楫迟，得尽所历妙&rdquo;也。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缩恧(因羞惭而畏惧瑟缩。恧，惭);缩伏(畏惧屈服);缩退(畏缩退却);缩栗(形容畏惧颤朒;枝叶枯萎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 节约，节省 <span class="encs">[economize]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:节衣缩食</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 亏欠，不足 <span class="encs">[shorten]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">由是以天下公赋为人君私藏，有司不复得窥其多少，校其赢缩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐&middot;大历十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缩囊(日渐贫困);缩腹(忍受饥饿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 滤酒去渣 <span class="encs">[filter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缩酌用茅，明酌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特牲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缩酒(古代祭祀时，捆束包茅立于前，灌酒于茅束，酒渗而下，视为神饮，名为缩酒，一说为滤酒去掉渣滓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 取，采取 <span class="encs">[take]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若于目观则美，缩于财用则匮，是聚民利以自封而瘠民出，胡美之焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:缩弗转(方言。收不回);缩屋称贞(称颂人在妇女有危难时，能守礼而不加侵犯凌辱之词。按:缩屋而继之，指薪尽，又拆取屋木以继)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E2E.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>所六切，音蹜。【說文】亂也。一曰蹴也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】縱縮，亂也。【註】縱放掣縮，皆亂法也。【儀禮&middot;鄕飮酒禮】磬階閒縮霤北面鼓之。【註】縮，從也。霤以東方爲從。古文縮爲蹙。【禮&middot;檀弓】古者冠縮縫，今也衡縫。【註】縮，從也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋器】繩之謂之縮之。【註】縮者，約束之。【詩&middot;大雅】縮版以載。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;郊特牲】縮酌用茅。【註】縮，去滓也。【左傳&middot;僖四年】無以縮酒。</p>
<p>又【孟子】自反而縮。【趙岐註】縮，義也。【朱子&middot;集註】縮，直也。</p>
<p>又【戰國策】縮于財用則匱。【註】歉也，贏之反也。</p>
<p>又【釋名】齊人謂車枕以前曰縮，言局縮也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】退也，止也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】斂也，短也。</p>
<p>又姓。【戰國策】安陵人縮高。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶式律切。【班固&middot;幽通賦】神先心以定命兮，命隨行以消息。斡流遷其不濟兮，故遭罹而贏縮。</p>
