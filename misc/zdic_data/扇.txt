                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扇</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>扇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>摇动生风取凉的用具：～子。～坠（扇柄下端的装饰物）。～面儿。～形。</li>
	<li>指板状或片状的屏：门～。隔～。</li>
	<li>量词，用于门窗等：一～门。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>扇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄢˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>摇动扇子或其它东西，使空气加速流动成风：用扇子～。</li>
	<li>鼓动别人去做不应该做的事：～动。～风点火。</li>
	<li>用手掌打：～耳光。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扇</strong></p>
<p>搧 <span class="dicpy">shān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 摇扇 <span class="encs">[fan]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吹，扇助也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言十二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艳妻扇方处。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span>。毛本作煽。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一扇息火，二扇生风，三扇下雨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扇风(摇扇以生风);扇暍(传说周武王曾替中暑人扇风取凉);扇枕温衾(对父母老人极尽孝道之意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 以掌批面 <span class="encs">[slap]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扇耳光;扇了几巴掌</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 施展 <span class="encs">[put to good use;give free play to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>官御史，坐行赇免，居林下，大扇威虐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>扇</strong> <span class="dicpy">shān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 煽动、鼓动<span class="encs">[他人做坏事]</span> <span class="encs">[instigate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奸谄颇相扇构。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;谢安传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扇构(煽动众人借机迫害);扇诱(煽惑引诱);扇惑(煽动蛊惑);扇聚(煽动纠合)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 振翼欲飞 <span class="encs">[flap the wings]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扇动(指鸟禽飞虫鼓翅);扇翅(振翅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="encs">[风]</span> 起，吹 <span class="encs">[blow]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扇风(起风，鼓风)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宣扬;传播 <span class="encs">[propagate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:扇扬(宣扬，张扬);扇和(宣扬附和)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 遮蔽 <span class="encs">[shadow]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叶死不扇(树叶枯死，不会遮阴)</span></p>
