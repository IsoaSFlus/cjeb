						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>让</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">讓</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄤˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>不争，尽（<span class="dicpy">jǐn</span>）着旁人：～步。～位。谦～。</li>
	<li>请：～茶。</li>
	<li>许，使：不～他来。</li>
	<li>任凭：～他闹去。</li>
	<li>被：～雨淋了。</li>
	<li>索取一定代价，把东西给人：出～。转（<span class="dicpy">zhuǎn </span>）～。</li>
	<li>闪避：～开。当仁不～。</li>
	<li>责备，谴责：&ldquo;二世使人～章邯&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">攘</span>&rdquo;，侵夺。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">让</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>让</strong></p>
<p>讓 <span class="dicpy">r&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，襄( <span class="dicpy">xiāng</span>)声。本义:责备)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[blame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">让，相责让也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诘责以辞谓之让。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">让，责也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且让之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">让不贡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>人以为让。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;齐世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众知有为，因让之曰:&hellip;&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:让书(有责备言语的书信);让勖(既责备又勉励);让诮(责让讥诮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 退让;谦让 <span class="encs">[give away;give ground;modestly decline]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">让者，礼之主也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">让文之材也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">让，推贤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">允恭克让。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;推贤尚善曰让。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">退让以明礼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">尧</span>让天下于 <span class="diczx2">许由</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">侯生</span>摄敝衣冠，直上载公子坐，不让，欲欢公子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:让梨觅枣(比喻兄弟间的手足情爱);见困难就上，见荣誉就让;各不相让;寸步不让;让棋;让衢(让路);让再让三(三番五次地推让);让客(礼让客人);让德(谦让的品德)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 推举 <span class="encs">[elect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哥哥三打</span><span class="diczx2">祝家庄</span>身亡之后，众兄弟让我为头领。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">李致远</span><span class="smcs">《还牢末》</span>楔子</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 以一定代价将东西的所有权转给他人 <span class="encs">[let sb.have sth.at a fair price;make over]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">把房子让给远房几家族人来住。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">薛</span>家伏势倚情，偏不相让。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:让与(把财物或权利移转于别人);让禄(把利禄移转给别人);让国(把国君的地位让给他人);让名(把名誉让给他人);让价;让利销售</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 请，邀请 <span class="encs">[invite;usher]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:把他让进门来;把客人让进里屋</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 允许 <span class="encs">[let;allow;permit]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:公共场所不让吸烟;让他走了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;攘&rdquo;。推辞;推让;拒绝 <span class="encs">[decline;refuse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃惧距天用而让有用也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《潜夫论&middot;明闇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:让职(辞让官职);让爵(让爵位)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 要求 <span class="encs">[ask]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:是他让我来的;她妈妈让她每天练钢琴八个小时</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 躲避，避免碰到(如突然转身) <span class="encs">[dodge]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:幸亏我让得快，要不早给那辆车撞倒了;让威(避开敌人的锋芒);让开(避开);让辟(退让避开)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 用烟酒款待 <span class="encs">[entertain;treat;offer]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:让酒(请人喝酒);让烟(请人抽烟);让坐(请客人入座)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 通&ldquo;攘&rdquo;。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 窃夺 <span class="encs">[steal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">治斧钺者，不敢让刑;治轩冕者，不敢让赏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;君臣下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 骚扰 <span class="encs">[disturb]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巨涂则让，小涂则殆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;荣辱》</span></span></p>

                              讓·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=24" target="_blank">24</a>　·部外筆画：17</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8B93.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/269AC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦦬</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】人樣切【正韻】而亮切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>壤去聲。【說文】相責讓也。【小爾雅】詰責以辭謂之讓。【左傳&middot;僖五年】公使讓之。【註】譴讓之。【周禮&middot;地官】司救掌萬民之衺惡過失而誅讓之。【史記&middot;張耳<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>餘傳】張耳責讓<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>餘以不肎救趙。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】謙也。【類篇】退也。【字彙】先人後己謂之讓。【書&middot;堯典】允恭克讓。【左傳&middot;文元年】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤰞</span>讓，德之基也。</p>
<p>又【襄十三年】讓，禮之主也。【禮&middot;曲禮】君子恭敬撙節退讓以明禮。【疏】應受而推曰讓。【儀禮&middot;聘禮】賔入門皇升堂讓。【鄭註】讓，謂舉手平衡也。</p>
<p>又【鄕飮酒禮註】事同曰讓，事異曰辭。</p>
<p>又木名。【左思&middot;蜀都賦】交讓所植。【註】交讓，木名。兩樹對生，一樹枯則一樹生。出岷山，在安都縣。</p>
<p>又諸羌州名。【唐書&middot;地理志】劒南道有讓川州，開元後置。</p>
<p>又【讀書通】通作攘。【史記&middot;司馬相如&middot;封禪書】進讓之道，何其爽與。【漢書】作進攘。</p>
<p>又【韻會小補】通作襄。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;保氏&middot;五射&middot;鄭註】一曰襄尺。【釋文】襄音讓，本作讓。</p>
<p>又叶如羊切，音穰。【詩&middot;小雅】受爵不讓，至于已斯亡。　【說文】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27BA8.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧮨</span>。</p>
