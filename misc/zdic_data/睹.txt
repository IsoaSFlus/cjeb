						<p>● <strong>睹</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>

</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>睹</strong></p>
<p>覩 <span class="dicpy">dǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从目，者声。本义:见，看见)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[see]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">睹，见也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人作而万物睹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;文言传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今我睹子之难穷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云霞明灭或可睹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令人目不忍睹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:有目共睹;睹睐(看见;发现);睹闻(亲见亲闻);睹始知终(看见事物的开始阶段，就预见到它的最终结果);睹记(见闻与记忆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 察看 <span class="encs">[watch;observe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">赵简子</span>将袭 <span class="diczx2">卫</span>，使 <span class="diczx2">史默</span>往睹之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;召类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耳闻目睹;秋毫必睹</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 明白;懂得 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不务深迂而难睹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:睹事(懂事，明晓事理)</span></p>
