                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">聪</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>聪</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">聰</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄨㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>听觉：失～。</li>
	<li>听觉灵敏：耳～目明。</li>
	<li>心思灵敏：～明。～睿。～慧。～颖。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> intelligent, clever, bright</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> (ein gutes)​ Gehör; hellhörig</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> intelligent,(ouïe)​ fine</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">聪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>聪</strong></p>
<p>聰 <span class="dicpy">cōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从耳，总声。本义:听力好)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(of hearing) acute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聪，察也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听曰聪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻审谓之聪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;宙合》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耳彻为聪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;外物》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚寐无聪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;兔爰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聪耳(听觉灵敏的耳朵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 明察。指耳闻声音而心能辨别其是真假 <span class="encs">[astute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耳不能两听而聪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">屈平</span>疾王听之不聪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聪察(擅长审辨);聪察轩昂(明察真伪，仪态不凡);聪听(明于听取;明于辨察)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 聪明，有才智 <span class="encs">[clever]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听曰聪，聪者，能闻事而审其意也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露&middot;五行五事》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">瞻</span>今已八岁，聪慧可爱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聪者不得用其诈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;有度》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不聪敬止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;敬之》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聪哲(聪明多智);聪明自误(天资聪明的人，反被聪明所误);聪智(指聪明而有才智)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>聪</strong></p>
<p>聰 <span class="dicpy">cōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>听;听觉 <span class="encs">[faculty of hearing]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:左耳失聪</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8070.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】倉紅切【集韻】麤叢切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音驄。【說文】察也。【廣韻】聞也，明也，通也，聽也。【書&middot;洪範】聰作謀。【史記&middot;商君傳】反聽之謂聰，內視之謂明。【管子&middot;宙合篇】耳司聽，聽必順聞，聞審謂之聰。【註】耳之所聞，旣順且審，故謂之聰。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶千剛切，音倉。【陳琳&middot;柳賦】穆穆天子，亶聽聰兮。德音允塞，民所望兮。宜爾嘉樹，配甘棠兮。望平聲。</p>
