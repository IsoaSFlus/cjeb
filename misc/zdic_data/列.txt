						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>列</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>排成的行：罗～。行（<span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ng </span>）～。队～。～岛。</li>
	<li>众多，各：～位。～强。～传（<span class="dicpy">zhu&agrave;n </span>）。</li>
	<li>摆出：～举。</li>
	<li>安排到某类事务之中：～席。</li>
	<li>量词，用于成行列的事物：一～火车。</li>
	<li>类：不在此～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">烈</span>&rdquo;，强烈，猛然。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">裂</span>&rdquo;，分裂。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">列</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>列</strong> <span class="dicpy">li&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，本义:割，分)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &ldquo;裂&rdquo;的古字。分割;分解 <span class="encs">[break up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">列，分解也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者列地建国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大袂列。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;五辅》</span>。注:&ldquo;决之也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两骖列两服入厩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;哀公》</span>。?</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:列断(裂断);列地(列土。分地封侯);列缺(高空中闪电所现出的空隙闪电);列土分茅(古代帝王用茅土分封诸侯的仪式)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 排列 <span class="encs">[arrange]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">列敌度宜谓之义。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;玄摛》</span>。注:&ldquo;序也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">森然布列。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">张筵列鼎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奇峰错列。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江岸列营。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:列树(成列的树木);列石(排列成行的石块);列待(排列待立);列炬(排列火炬);列秧(按品级排列)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 收列;列入 <span class="encs">[list]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:代表姓名列后;列入议程</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 陈列;摆开 <span class="encs">[display]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小者坐列贩卖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:列缋(陈列绘画);列兵(陈兵，驻扎军队);列罗(陈列);列卒(陈兵布阵);列陈(摆开阵势。陈，通&ldquo;阵&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 安排;给予 <span class="encs">[make arrangement;give]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如谥，臣子所谏列也，诔生时所行，为之谥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 布置 <span class="encs">[assign; give in struction about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太尉列卒取(捉拿)十七人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《段太尉逸事状》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 对立 <span class="encs">[be antagonistic to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故下与官列法，上与君分威。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>

			<div class="gscdl">今虽耄老，未有所成，犹幸预君子之<span>列</span>，而承天子之宠光，缀公卿之后，日侍坐备顾问，四海亦谬称其氏名，况才之过于余者乎？今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪稍之供，父母岁有裘葛之遗，无冻馁之患矣；坐大厦之下而诵《诗》《书》无奔走之劳矣；有司业、博士为之师，未有问而不告，求而不得者也；凡所宜有之书，皆集于此，不必若余之手录，假诸人而后见也。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div><div class="gscdl"><span>列</span>国周齐秦汉楚，赢，都变做了土；输，都变做了土。</div><div class="gscdr">元·张养浩 《山坡羊·骊山怀古》</div>      </div>
