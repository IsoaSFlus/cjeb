                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>饯</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">餞</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>设酒食送行：～行。～别。</li><li>用蜜或糖浸渍果品，又指蜜或糖浸渍的果品：蜜～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> farewell party; see off, send off</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> bewahrt, eingemachte</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> conserves,salaisons,repas d'adieu</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>饯</strong></p><p>餞 <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，戋( <span class="dicpy">qiān</span>)声。本义:设酒食送行)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[give a farewell dinner]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">餞，送去食也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饯，送行馈饥也。——<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饮饯于祢。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·泉水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃饯。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士虞礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">季文子</span>饯之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郑六卿</span>饯宣子于郊。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十六年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:饯路(外出时所给予的馈赠);饯花会(为花举行的饯别会);饯席(饯别的酒席);饯客(设酒为客人饯行)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 送行 <span class="encs">[see sb.off]</span>。如:饯泪(送别的眼泪);饯腊(送别残冬腊月)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>饯</strong></p><p>餞 <span class="dicpy">jiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>以蜜、浓糖浆浸渍的果品 <span class="encs">[candied fruit]</span>。如:蜜饯;果饯</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/饯别">饯别</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/饯行">饯行</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/991E.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>慈演切，音踐。【說文】送去也。【徐曰】以酒食送也。【詩·邶風】飮餞于禰。【註】祖而舍軷飮酒其側曰餞。【釋文】餞，音踐。【爾雅·釋詁】餞，進也。【疏】餞者，進飮食之名也。</p><p>又凡送皆曰餞。【書·堯典】分命和仲宅西曰昧谷，寅餞納日。【註】寅，敬也，餞禮送行者之名。納日，方納之日也。蓋以秋分之暮夕方納之日，而識其景也。【釋文】餞，賤衍反。</p><p>又【廣韻】疾箭切【集韻】【韻會】才線切【正韻】在線切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音賤。義同。詩邶風，飮餞，徐邈讀。</p><p>又【集韻】子賤切，音箭。義同。</p>
