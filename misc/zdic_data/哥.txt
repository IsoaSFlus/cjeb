                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">哥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>哥</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gē.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄜˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>兄，同父母（或只同父，只同母）或同族同辈而年龄比自己大的男子：～～。</li>    <li>称呼年龄跟自己差不多的男子：李二～。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">歌</span>”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> elder brother</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> älterer Bruder</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> frère aîné</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>哥</strong> <span class="dicpy">gē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从二可。<span class="yhcs">“可”</span>，有<span class="yhcs">“快乐”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“欢乐”</span>的意思。<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·正月》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“哿矣富人。”</span> <span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“哿，可。”</span> <span class="diczx2">王引之</span><span class="smcs">《经义述闻》</span>卷六:<span class="yhcs">“哿与哀对文，哀者忧悲，哿者欢乐也。……<span class="smcs">《毛传》</span>训哿为可，可亦快意惬心之称。”</span><span class="yhcs">“哥”</span>似即<span class="yhcs">“歌”</span>之本字。本义:歌)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“歌”</span>。咏，唱，奏乐 <span class="encs">[song]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">召公</span>卒，而民人思 <span class="diczx2">召公</span>之政，怀裳树不敢伐哥咏之，作<span class="smcs">《甘棠》</span>之诗。——<span class="smcs">《史记·燕召公世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诚信著于天下，醇德流乎四海，则近者歌讴而乐之，远者执禽而朝之。——<span class="smcs">《盐铁论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:哥钟(即歌钟，古代打击乐器);哥咏(歌咏)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 哥哥 <span class="encs">[elder brother]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哥，声也。从二可。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，发声之语，如可而平，今以称兄之词。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">再拜跪奠大哥于座前，伏维哥孝友慈惠，和易谦恭。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《祭浮梁大兄文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:大哥;二哥;哥嫂;哥子(哥哥;对男孩的称呼)。亦用于对同族和亲戚中兄长的称呼。如:叔伯哥</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 对年稍长者或年龄跟自己差不多的男子的尊称或昵称 <span class="encs">[brother]</span>。如:张大哥;李大哥;哥们(男子之间的昵称)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 唐代常称父为哥 <span class="encs">[father]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 对丈夫的昵称。尤其是小两口之间 <span class="encs">[husband]</span>。如:送哥参军</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 宋代著名瓷窑<span class="yhcs">“哥窑”</span>的简称 <span class="encs">[Ge Kiln]</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/哥白尼">哥白尼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
