                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>往</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>去，到：～返。～复。～还（<span class="dicpy">huán </span>）。～来。交～。向～。勇～直前。</li><li>过去：～昔。～日。～事。～古。～常。以～。一如既～。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">望</span>”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">往</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>往</strong></p><p>徃 <span class="dicpy">wǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，从止，从土。意为从这个地方走向目的地。本义:去，到…去)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[depart (head，leave，start) for;go to somewhere]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">往，之也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大夫有所往。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">憧憧往来。——<span class="smcs">《易·咸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾言既往矣。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">往而不返。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">往见老聃。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跳往助之。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">随其往。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欣然前往。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子何恃而往。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:前往(前去;去);往造(前去拜访);往教(老师前往学生处施教);往旋(往复回旋);往蹇来连(往来皆难);往人(派往的人);往至(前往);往程(去的路程);往夫(一往无前的战士)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 彼此间来往 <span class="encs">[contact]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">往复一月间事，过城池已固。——<span class="smcs">《三侠五义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:往回(来回;往返);往往来来(多次往返)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 亡去 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无以蓄之则往而不可止也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·权修》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓亡去也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:往化(死亡)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 给予，送 <span class="encs">[give]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愧彼赠我厚，惭此往物轻。——</span><span class="diczx2">泰嘉</span><span
