                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>购</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">購</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄡˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>买：～买。～置。～销。收～。采～。</li>
	<li>悬赏征求：悬～。～募（悬赏募求）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> buy, purchase; hire</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anschaffen, einkaufen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> acheter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">购</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>购</strong></p>
<p>購 <span class="dicpy">g&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝。从&ldquo;贝&rdquo;，表示与财物有关。本义:悬赏征求)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[offera reward for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">购，以财有所求也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">购，偿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾闻汉购我头千金，邑万户，吾为若德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穷饿无聊，追购又急，天高地迥，号呼靡及。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:购求(设置奖金以捉捕犯人);购问(悬赏征询);购募(悬赏募求);购悬(悬赏缉拿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 原指用重金收买，后泛指买 <span class="encs">[purchase;buy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝方博购</span><span class="diczx2">王羲之</span>贴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧唐书&middot;褚遂良传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予购三百盆，皆病者，无一完者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:购求(寻求购买);购粮;购房;购地;赊购;购辑(购买收集);购请(购买)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;媾&rdquo;。讲和 <span class="encs">[make peace with a foreign country]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃警</span><span class="diczx2">公仲</span>之行，将西购于 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;韩世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:购于秦(与秦国媾和;向秦讲和)</span></p>
