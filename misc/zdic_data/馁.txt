                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>馁</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">餒</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">něi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/něi.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄟˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>饥饿：冻～。</li>
	<li>没有勇气：气～。自～。～怯。</li>
	<li>鱼腐烂：&ldquo;鱼～而肉败&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hungry, starving, famished</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hungrig</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> avoir faim,perdre courage</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">馁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>馁</strong></p>
<p>餒、餧 <span class="dicpy">něi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/něi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，委声。本义:饥饿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[famished;hungry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">餒，饥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">餒，饥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">餒，饿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闵其粥糜冻餒之患。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《王纯碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今民馁而君逞欲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾有馁而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十年》</span>。注:&ldquo;饿也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我馁甚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冻馁之患。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:馁殍(饿殍);馁疾(饥饿病困);馁毙(饿死);馁馑(饿死);馁士(饥饿之士);馁腹(饿着肚子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 气馁;泄气，丧气 <span class="encs">[disheartened;discouraged;dispirited]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其为气也配义与道。无是，馁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:馁堕(空虚，落下。指怒气已消失);胜勿骄，败勿馁;馁怯(气馁胆怯);馁荏(气势软弱);馁却(因气馁而退却);馁弱(气馁软弱)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>馁</strong></p>
<p>餒 <span class="dicpy">něi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/něi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指鱼腐烂变质 <span class="encs">[(of fish)putrid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鱼馁而肉败，不食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;乡党》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指食物腐烂变质</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食物馁败，生虫，欣然食之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">黄叔璥</span><span class="smcs">《台海使槎录》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:馁败(腐烂变质)</p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=食" target="_blank">食</a>】
                              餒·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9912.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】奴罪切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】弩罪切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音鮾。【玉篇】餓也。【左傳&middot;襄二十年】吾有餒而已。</p>
<p>又魚爛也。【爾雅&middot;釋器】魚謂之餒。【註】肉爛。【疏】魚爛從內發，故云內爛。今本內作肉，恐誤。</p>
<p>又同餧。【楚語】民之羸餧日日已甚。【前漢&middot;魏相傳】振乏餧。　【說文】本作餧。</p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">飢也。从食。妥聲。</span>各本篆作餧。解作委聲。非也。今正攷論語音義曰。餒、奴罪反。說文魚敗曰餒。本又作餧。字書同。爾雅音義亦云。餒、奴罪反。說文魚敗曰餒。字書作餧。別字書於說文、則陸所據說文从妥明矣。按妥聲乃與奴罪切音相近。猶挼必妥聲、乃與女禾切相近。綏必妥聲、乃得妥綏爲古今字也。若五經文字曰。餧、飢也。經典相承。別作餒爲飢餒。以餧爲餒餉。葢張時說文巳改从委聲。與陸所據說文不同。故其字各異。餒古音十七部。餧爲餧餉俗字。許艸部作萎。<span class="jieshi">一曰魚敗曰餒。</span>論語。魚餒而肉敗。釋器曰。肉謂之敗。魚謂之餒。按魚爛自中。亦飢義之引伸也。                          </div>
