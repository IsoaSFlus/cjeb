                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">再</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>再</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">z&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄞˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>两次；第二次：～次。一而～，～而三。</li>	<li>重复；继续：良辰难～。青春不～。</li>	<li>副词。（①表示又一次：～接～厉。一错～错。②表示事情或行为重复、继续，多指未然：明天～来。雨不会～下。③用在两个动词之间，表示动作的先后关系：做完作业～去玩。④更，更加：～好没有了。⑤表示加外有所补充：～则。）</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> again, twice, re-</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> noch einmal, wieder, nochmals  (Adv)​,ferner, außerdem, überdies  (Adv)​,noch mehr  (Adv)​,nochmal, wieder  (Adv)​,(erst ...)​, dann ...  (Konj)​,wiederkehren, zurückkehren  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> à nouveau,de nouveau,encore,une fois de plus,puis,continuer,revenir,re-</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">再</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>再</strong> <span class="dicpy">z&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>［数］</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆:从一，冓( <span class="dicpy">g&ograve;u</span>)省。&ldquo;冓&rdquo;是&ldquo;构&rdquo;的初文，甲骨文字形，象两部分材木架起的样子。本义:第二次)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[another time]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再，一举而二也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，冓者，加也。对耦之词曰二，重叠词曰再。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再刺再宥再赦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司刺》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">樊缨十有再就。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;巾车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酒肉之赐弗再拜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">过言不再。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;儒行》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹不更也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一鼓作气，再而衰，三而竭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再战而烧</span><span class="diczx2">夷陵</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再迁为</span><span class="diczx2">太史令</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敌枪再击。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再则曰老大帝国。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:再录一堂(复审一次);再速(再请);再二(第二次，再次);再之(第二次，第三次);再眠(指蚕第二次蜕皮时的不食不动状态)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 两次 <span class="encs">[two times;doubly;twice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女工再税。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;本议》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">击赵者再。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日再食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再夺门。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至再三。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>再</strong> <span class="dicpy">z&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 事情或行为重复，继续 <span class="encs">[again;afresh;once again]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">她不敢再看一看那大庙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">叶紫</span><span class="smcs">《星》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:再试一次;拿来再商量;再议;再思(再度思考);再聘(再度婚娶);再阐(再度显扬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 重，重新 <span class="encs">[anew]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勿复再言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再火令药用。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">再眺山下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">寰</span>再上疏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:再计(重新谋划);再审(重新审查)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 更，更加 <span class="encs">[once more]</span>&mdash;&mdash;表示程度</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">你会觉得世界末日也不会比这再坏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">茅盾</span><span class="smcs">《雷雨前》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:再高点儿;再多点儿</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另，另外 <span class="encs">[another]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:再吃一点菜;再创新纪录;再行(另外进行某项活动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 与否定词配合使用，强调情况的普遍性</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">进了门，找</span><span class="diczx2">周嫂子</span>，再找不着。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用于让步句，常同&ldquo;也&rdquo;、&ldquo;都&rdquo;配合使用，有&ldquo;即使多么&rdquo;、&ldquo;不管怎样&rdquo;的意思 <span class="encs">[in any case]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水再深脚也能过，山再高手也能攀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《赶车传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 表示转折，相当于&ldquo;却&rdquo;、&ldquo;也&rdquo; <span class="encs">[but;yet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">你只怨人行动嗔怪你，你再不知道你怄的人难受。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 表示两种行为或情状并举、并存、相当于&ldquo;且&hellip;且&rdquo;、&ldquo;又&hellip;又&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其词旨乃典乃文，再恳再切，实可警策未悟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《全唐文纪事》</span></span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">第二次、又一次、兩次。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「再見」、「再嫁」、「<b>再</b>鞠躬」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷六九&middot;蘇秦傳》：「秦趙五戰，秦<b>再</b>勝而趙三勝。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示動作繼續重複。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「再接再厲」、「再版」、「失敗了<b>再</b>努力。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈自京赴奉先縣詠懷五百字〉詩：「榮枯咫尺異，惆悵難<b>再</b>述。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示一個動作接續在另一動作結束之後。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「吃完飯<b>再</b>走！」、「先擦窗戶，<b>再</b>洗地板。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">更。表示行為程度的加深。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「你能這麼想，真是<b>再</b>好不過了。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示另有補充。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>再</b>則」、「再說」、「再不然」。</span></p></li>
