                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">变</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>变</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">變</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>性质状态或情形和以前不同；变化；改变：情况～了。～了样。</li>
	<li>变成：沙漠～良田。后进～先进。</li>
	<li>使改变：～废为宝。</li>
	<li>能变化的；已变化的：～数。～态。</li>
	<li>变卖：～产。</li>
	<li>变通：通权达～。</li>
	<li>有重大影响的突然变化：事～。政～。</li>
	<li>指变文：目连～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> change, transform, alter; rebel</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ändern  (V)​,sich entwickeln  (V)​,sich verändern  (V)​,umsteigen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> changer,devenir,transformer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">变</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>变</strong></p>
<p>變 <span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，孌( <span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n</span>)声。本义:变化，改变)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[transform;change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变，更也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变，易也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变者，非常也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一阖一辟谓之变。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">病变而药不变。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是宾客无不变色离席。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无下有变&hellip;百姓熟敢不箪食壶浆以迎将军者乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不得已，变姓名。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:变文协韵(古文修辞术语。用变换文字或颠倒词序等手段使句子的音调和谐);天气突变;巨变(巨大的变化);变豹(即豹变，指人发迹而富贵);变宫(中国古代七音的一种。即宫的变声，比高宫低半音)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 变通 <span class="encs">[be flexible]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善言而不知变，未可谓能说也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">桓宽</span><span class="smcs">《盐铁论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 发动事变 <span class="encs">[rebel;revolt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">待</span><span class="diczx2">吕</span>氏变而共诛之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高后纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:变谋(变乱的图谋);变震(变乱震惊)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>变</strong></p>
<p>變 <span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 事变，有重大影响的突发事件 <span class="encs">[unexpected turn of events]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">变遂发。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:哗变(称军队突然叛变);变事(突然发生的重大事件)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 灾异，异常的自然现象 <span class="encs">[catastrophe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灾异愈甚，天变成形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五行志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天变不足畏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史&middot;王安石传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:变怪(灾变怪异)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8B8A.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2249F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢒟</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/224AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢒪</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/224A6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢒦</span>【唐韻】祕戀切【廣韻】彼眷切。【集韻】【韻會】彼卷切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>鞭去聲。【說文】更也。【小爾雅】易也。【廣韻】化也，通也。【增韻】轉也。【正韻】攺也。【易&middot;乾卦】乾道變化。【易解】自有而無謂之變，自無而有謂之化。【禮&middot;王制】一成而不可變。【註】更也。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;司爟】四時變國火，以救時疾。【註】變，猶易也。【前漢&middot;循吏傳】漢家承敝通變。</p>
<p>又動也。【禮&middot;檀弓】夫子之病革矣，不可以變。【註】變，動也。【荀子&middot;議兵篇】機變不張。【註】謂器械變動攻敵也。</p>
<p>又權變。【前漢&middot;魏豹傳贊】張耳、韓信皆徼一時之權變，以詐力成功。【文中子&middot;述史篇】非君子不可以語變。【註】變，權也。反經合道之謂也。</p>
<p>又災異曰變。【前漢&middot;五行志】災異愈甚，天變成形。</p>
<p>又【諸葛豐傳】災變數見。</p>
<p>又死喪曰變。【穀梁傳&middot;昭十五年】君在祭樂之中，大夫有變以聞，可乎。</p>
<p>又姓，見【姓苑】。</p>
<p>又【韻會】平免切，音辨。正也。【禮&middot;禮運】大夫死宗廟謂之變。【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>註】變讀爲辨。猶正也。</p>
<p>又叶<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>眠切，音邊。【莊子&middot;田子方】夫至人者，上闚靑天，下潛黃泉，揮斥八極，神氣不變。</p>
<p>又叶必愼切，音鬢。【陸雲詩】羽儀未變，叶有客來信。○按《說文》本从攴䜌，載攴部。徐曰：攴，有爲也。精薀从久，俗譌作攵，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>非。字彙已載攴部，是言部重出，下攺从攵，非。</p>
<p>又集韻俗作〈木變〉<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23035.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣀵</span>，亦非。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23035.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣀵</span>。</p>
