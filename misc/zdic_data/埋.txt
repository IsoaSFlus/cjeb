                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>埋</strong> <span class="dicpy">mái</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mái.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声.从土，里声.字本作<span class="yhcs">“薶”</span>，甲骨文字形，象在坑中埋狗的形象，是向地神祭献的活动。本义:藏入土中)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bury;cover up with earth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祭器敝则埋之，龟筴敝则埋之，牲死则埋之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生女犹得嫁比邻，生男埋没随百草。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我死谁埋。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毁其盆，悉埋于地。——</span><span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:埋地雷;埋玉(比喻英才或美女之死);埋书(把盟书埋在地下。古代盟誓时的一种礼仪);埋狱(埋没在监狱屋基下的宝剑。比喻埋没人才)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代特指简陋不依礼制而落葬。也泛指葬埋 <span class="encs">[bury]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">葬不如礼曰埋。(如:按照)——<span class="smcs">《释名·释葬制》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:埋骨(人死而埋葬屍骨);埋匿(埋窆。掩埋尸体);埋堙(埋藏。掩埋尸体);埋魂(埋葬英魂);埋蛇(<span class="yhcs">“埋蛇”</span>为善行仁爱之典);埋掩(掩埋，埋葬)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 藏，掩盖 <span class="encs">[hide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狐埋之而狐搰之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:埋冤(含冤);埋情(昧心);埋忧(隐藏忧愁);埋身(埋名;隐居);埋匿(埋藏);埋翳(掩埋;掩盖);埋覆(掩蔽;覆盖)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 低，低下去 <span class="encs">[low]</span>。如:埋头顾影(形容矫饰者暗中窥察外界的反应);埋首(埋头)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>埋</strong> <span class="dicpy">mái</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mái.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 犹暮或末 <span class="encs">[old]</span>。如:埋年(方言。年老);埋暮(迟暮。喻年岁大)</p><p><span
