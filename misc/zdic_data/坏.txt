                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>坏</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">壞</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄞˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>不好的；恶劣的，与&ldquo;好&rdquo;相对：～人。～事。～习惯。</li>	<li>东西受了损伤，被毁：破～。败～。</li>	<li>坏主意：使～。</li>	<li>用在某些动词或形容词后，表示程度深：忙～了。</li></ol><p>● <strong>坏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆ〡</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/坯">坯</a>&rdquo;。古文字同《说文》&ldquo;坯&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rotten, spoilt, bad, broken down</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schlecht, kaputt, zerbrochen, zerbrechen  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mauvais,méchant,cassé,très,terriblement,fort,s'abîmer,se détériorer,se détruire,mauvaise intention,mauvais tour</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坏</strong></p>
<p>壞 <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，褱( <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;i</span>)声。古代建筑物主要以土建造，故从&ldquo;土&rdquo;。本义:倒塌(指建筑物遭到破坏)。按:古代&ldquo;坏&rdquo;与&ldquo;壞&rdquo;是音义都不相同的两个字。&ldquo;坏&rdquo;，本读作 <span class="dicpy">pēi</span>，是个从土、不声的形声字，指&ldquo;未烧过的砖瓦、陶器&rdquo;。现在&ldquo;壞&rdquo;简化为&ldquo;坏&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[collapse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坏，败也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，当训毁也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坏，毁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坠坏城郭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span>。正义:&ldquo;坼也，自颓曰坏。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大室之屋坏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">恭公</span>坏孔子宅以为宫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;佚文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天雨墙坏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说难》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而坏都舍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">室坏不修。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坏山(倒塌的山);坏决(倒塌;破败);坏溃(坍塌);坏压(坍塌);坏裂(崩溃);坏颓(崩毁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 破坏;毁坏 <span class="encs">[ruin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">门坏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坏大门及寝门而入。公惧，入于室，又坏户。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有基无坏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坏舛(毁坏错乱);坏乱(败坏错乱);坏乱(败坏;混乱);坏废(败坏;废弛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 衰亡 <span class="encs">[decline and fall]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 战败 <span class="encs">[defeat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">擂鼓大进，北军大坏，</span><span class="diczx2">曹</span>军退走。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴志》</span></p>
