                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>汲</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>从井里打水：～水。～引（ａ．汲水；ｂ．吸取）。</li>
	<li>〔～～〕形容心情急切、努力追求，如&ldquo;～～于富贵&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> draw water from well; imbibe</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> puiser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">汲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>汲</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，及声。本义:从井里打水，取水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[draw water]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汲，引水于井也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汲，取也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可用汲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;井》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而出溉汲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎行论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而谷汲者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">每汲用。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有泉可汲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《复庵记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:汲古(钻研古书，如汲水于井);汲水(引水;取水)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 牵引 <span class="encs">[pull;drag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡任，索约，大汲其版，谓之无任。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记&middot;匠人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 引导 <span class="encs">[lead;guide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汲</span><span class="diczx2">郑伯</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;襄公十年》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:汲善(引人向善)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 引荐;提拔 <span class="encs">[recommend]</span>。如:汲扬(汲引，引荐而显扬之)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>汲</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用同&ldquo;急&rdquo;。急剧 <span class="encs">[impatient]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不汲汲于富贵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:汲汲忙忙(十分急迫繁忙);汲汲营营(急迫追求的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用同&ldquo;岌&rdquo; <span class="encs">[dangerous]</span>。如:汲汲</p>
<p>◎ <strong>汲</strong> <span class="dicpy">J&iacute;</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 中国县名 <span class="encs">[Ji county]</span>。在河南省</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6C72.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】居立切【集韻】【韻會】訖立切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音急。【說文】引水於井也。【易&middot;井卦】可用汲，王明<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>受其福。【莊子&middot;至樂篇】綆短者，不可以汲深。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】引也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】匠人大汲其版。【註】汲，引也。【郭璞&middot;江賦】幷吞沅灃，汲引沮漳。</p>
<p>又汲汲，不休息貌。【博雅】孜孜汲汲，劇也。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】不汲汲於富貴。【文子&middot;上德篇】君子日汲汲以成煇。</p>
<p>又縣名。屬河內郡。【史記&middot;魏世家】城垝津以臨河內，河內共汲必危。【廣輿記】汲縣，故汲郡，治今屬衞輝府。</p>
<p>又姓。漢汲黯。</p>
<p>又【集韻】極入切，音及。與伋同。伋伋，虛詐貌。或作汲。</p>
