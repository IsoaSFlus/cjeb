						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>夕</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˉ</span></p><ol><li>日落的时候：～阳。～照。朝（<span class="dicpy">zhāo ㄓㄠ</span>）～相处（<span class="dicpy">chǔ ㄔㄨˇ</span>）。</li><li>泛指晚上：前～。除～。一～谈。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>夕</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。从月半见。本义:黄昏，傍晚)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sunset;dusk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夕，莫也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日之夕矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·君子于役》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日东则景夕多风。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司徒》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓日跌景乃中立表之处，大东近日也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初昏为夕。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范·五行》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夕市，夕时而市，贩夫贩妇为主。 ——<span class="smcs">《周礼·地官·司市》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瘦影写微月，疏枝横夕烟。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《置酒梅花下作短歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝晖夕阴，气象万千。——</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:夕晖(夕阳的余晖);朝发夕至;夕霭(黄昏的云气);夕市(傍晚进行的集市贸易)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 夜 <span class="encs">[evening;night]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今夕何夕。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·绸缪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以永今夕。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·白驹》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝而不夕。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以朝夕燕出入。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·道仆》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子终日乾乾，夕惕若厉无咎。——<span class="smcs">《易·乾》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不知天上宫阙，今夕是何年。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《水调歌头》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:朝乾夕惕(形容终日勤奋工作，戒勉不已);终夕不寝;通夕不寐;终夕;风雨之夕;住宿一夕;除夕(一年最后一天的夜晚);前夕</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指一年的最后一季或一个月的下旬 <span class="encs">[last period]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晡时至黄昏为日之夕;下旬为月之夕;自九月尽至十二月为岁之夕。——<span class="smcs">《洪范·五行传》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">月夕卜宅。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·礼论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“汐”</span>。晚潮 <span class="encs">[nighttide;erening tide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">官赋轨符，乘四时之朝夕。——<span class="smcs">《管子·国蓄》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>夕</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>傍晚朝见君主<span class="encs">[have an audience with an emperor in the evening]</span></p><p><span
