                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>料</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liào.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄠˋ</span></p><ol><li>估计，猜想：～想。预～。</li><li>可供制造其他东西的物质：材～。～子。备～。</li><li>喂牲口用的谷物：草～。</li><li>一种熔点较低的玻璃，用来制造器皿或工艺品：～器。</li><li>烹调时的调味品：调～。</li><li>整理，处理：～理。</li><li>量词，用于中药配制丸药，处方剂量的全份：配一～药。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>料</strong> <span class="dicpy">liào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从斗，从米，表示用斗量米。本义:称量)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 计数;计量;核计 <span class="encs">[count;calculate;assess]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">料，量也。从斗，米在其中，会意。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宣王</span>料民于 <span class="diczx2">太原</span>。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“数也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>师可料也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫古者不料民而知其多少。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:料校(计数检核);料民(计点人数);料数(计数);料算(估算)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 估量;揣度;料想 <span class="encs">[estimate;appraise;assess;conjecture;expect;think;presume]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">捭之者，料其情也。——<span class="smcs">《鬼谷子·捭阖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不复料其虚实。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">料大王士卒足以当</span><span class="diczx2">项王</span>乎。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大王自料，勇悍仁强，孰与</span><span class="diczx2">项王</span>?——<span class="smcs">《史记·淮阳侯列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:料知(犹估计);料来(估计;料想);料量(估计，测度);料道(估计到，预料);料得(预测到;估计到);料敌(估量、判断敌情);料知(料想到);料然(料想);料莫(想来);料猜(猜测);料世(揣度时势)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 选择，挑选 <span class="encs">[select]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">逊</span>料得精兵八千余人。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·陆逊传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:料人(选择人才);料取(选取);料选(挑选;拣选);料择(选择);料拣(选择，拣择)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 安排，料理;管理 <span class="encs">[arrange;manage; take care of; make arrangement]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">料，理也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">比当料理。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·王徽之传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">导后料检中书故事。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·周顗传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">料检太祖巾箱。——<span class="smcs">《宋书·王僧绰传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">琴童料持下晌午饭。——</span><span class="diczx2">王实甫</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:料持(料理，安排;对付，收拾);料吏(指管理胥吏);料度机宜(料理事务);料实(清点，查实);料检(犹查点;清理);如:料视(照料);料高(看守;登高守望。是一种苦役)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 小看 <span class="encs">[look down upon;belittle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">常言<span class="yhcs">“海水不可斗量”</span>，你休料我。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">冯梦龙</span><span class="smcs">《喻世明言》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span>
