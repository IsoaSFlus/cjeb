                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>蓑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">suō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄛˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用草或棕毛做成的防雨器：～衣。～笠。</li>
	<li>用草覆盖。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rain coat made of straw, coir, etc.</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> manteau d'herbes contre la pluie</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蓑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蓑</strong></p>
<p>簑 <span class="dicpy">suō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(象形。从艸，衰声。古文上象笠，中象人面，下象衰形。字亦作&ldquo;蓑&rdquo;。&ldquo;蓑&rdquo;是后起字。本义:雨具名。即蓑衣) 同本义 <span class="encs">[straw or palm-back rain cape]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衰，草雨衣也。</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>谓之萆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作蓑。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何蓑何笠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;无羊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">橐车载蓑笠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;既夕礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">被蓑以当铠鑐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;禁藏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地里还有工作的农民，披着蓑戴着笠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蓑笠(蓑衣和斗笠);蓑褐(蓑衣短褐。比喻衣着粗劣);蓑翁(穿蓑衣的老人。指渔翁)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蓑</strong> <span class="dicpy">suō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>用草覆盖;掩 <span class="encs">[cover with straw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">仲几</span>之罪何?不蓑城也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用草或棕櫚葉做成的雨具。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;小雅&middot;無羊》：「爾牧來思，何蓑何笠。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;毛亨&middot;傳：「蓑，所以備雨。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;柳宗元〈江雪〉詩：「孤舟蓑笠翁，獨釣寒江雪。」</span></p></li>

<p>又【韻會】覆也。【公羊傳&middot;定元年】仲幾之罪，何不蓑城也。【註】不以蓑苫城也。</p>
<p>又【唐韻古音】初危切，音摧。【郭璞&middot;山海經註】蓑，辟雨之衣也，音催。</p>
<p>又【集韻】蘇回切，音毸。華蘂下垂貌。【張衡&middot;南都賦】敷華蘂之蓑蓑。</p>
<p>又蘇煎切，音暹。【郭璞&middot;山海經贊】江疑所居，風雲是潛。獸有獓<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24771.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤝱</span>，毛如披蓑。</p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8870_6012.svg" /><span class="swbf">(衰)</span><span class="ywyy">艸雨衣。</span><span class="swdz">雨衣有不艸者。左傳：成子衣製杖戈。杜曰：製，雨衣。按言製則非艸爲，若今油布衣。</span><span class="ywyy">秦謂之萆。</span><span class="swdz">艸部曰：萆，雨衣，一曰衰衣。此則著萆爲秦語也。小雅：何蓑何笠。傳曰：蓑所以僃雨，笠所以禦暑。公羊傳：不蓑城也。何云：若今以艸衣城。齊語注云：襏襫，蓑襞衣也。襞或萆字，亦作薜。六韜：蓑薜䔲笠，衰俗从艸作蓑。而衰遂專爲等衰，衰絰字。衰絰本作縗，衰其假借字也。以艸爲雨衣，必層次編之。故引伸爲等衰，後世異其形，異其音。古義茫昧矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从衣象形。</span><span class="swdz">謂<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2033A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠌺</span>也。穌禾切。十七部。</span></p>
