                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>褫</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>剥夺：～夺（依法剥夺）。～革（革除，剥夺）。～职（革职）。～魄（使人失魂落魄）。</li>    <li>脱去，解下：解佩而～绅。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>褫</strong> <span class="dicpy">chǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，虒( <span class="dicpy">sī</span>)声。本义:夺去衣服)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[strip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终朝三褫。——<span class="smcs">《易·讼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">念解佩而褫绅。——<span class="smcs">《雪赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃褫豪民衣自衣。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:褫衿(剥去衣冠。旧时生员等犯罪，必先由学官褫夺衣冠，革除功名之后，才能动刑拷问)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 夺去 <span class="encs">[take away from]</span>。如:褫气(夺气，丧失气势)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 革除 <span class="encs">[abolish;get rid of]</span>。如:褫夺(剥夺);褫官(革除官职)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 扯住 <span class="encs">[pull]</span>。如:褫住(扯住)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 废弛 <span class="encs">[abandon]</span>。如:褫散(解散);褫落(脱落;掉下;废弛败落;解脱)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/褫夺">褫夺</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/褫革">褫革</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
