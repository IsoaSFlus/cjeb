                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>琢</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuó.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄛˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 雕刻玉石，使成器物，常喻用心推敲考虑、刻意求工：～磨。雕～（a.雕刻玉石等；b.过分地修饰文字，使人觉得不自然，如“～～之风不可长”）。～刻。～句。玉不～，不成器。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>琢</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zuó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuó.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄛˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 〔～磨〕思索、考虑，如“他的话我～～了很久，不明白什么意思”（“<span class="diczx3">磨</span>”读轻声）。</p></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从玉，豖( <span class="dicpy">chù</span>)声。本义:雕刻加工玉石)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carve and polish;chisel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">琢，治玉也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雕谓之琢。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玉不琢，不成器。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·学记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">和之璧，井里之厥也。玉人琢之，为天子宝。——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如琢如磨。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·淇奥》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“玉曰琢，石曰磨。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:琢玉(雕刻加工玉石);琢火(琢石冒出火星);琢治(雕琢玉石等);琢克(雕刻);琢雕(雕琢，指雕刻金玉为器)。泛指雕刻加工其他物品</p><p><span class="diczx1">以其所书于竹帛，镂于金石，琢于盘盂，传遗后世子孙者知之。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 撰文时的修改加工，琢磨 <span class="encs">[polish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">良工存旧笔，老叟琢新诗。——</span><span class="diczx2">赵汴</span><span class="smcs">《游青城山》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浅斟杯酒红生颊，细琢歌词稳称声。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《和致仕张郎中春书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:琢句(修饰文句);琢刻(修饰文辞)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr
