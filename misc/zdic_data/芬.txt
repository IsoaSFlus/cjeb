data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从艸，从分，分亦声。本义:香气)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fragrance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芬，草初生其香分布也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芬，芬香也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天地之行分、苦味也。——<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苾苾芬芬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·信南山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芬馥肸蚃。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芳菲菲而难亏兮，芬至今犹未沬。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:芬苾(芳香);芬烈(香气郁烈);芬郁(香气浓郁;亦谓芳香和腐臭)。使感到芬芳</p><p><span class="diczx1">至其可人之口者，则莲实与藕皆并列盘餐而互芬齿颊者也。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄·种植部》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特别好闻的香气 <span class="encs">[aroma]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流芬赋采，风靡云旋。——</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《芙蓉赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 比喻盛德或美名 <span class="encs">[moral;good reputation]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬芬千载之上。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·桓彝传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:扬芬千载(扬名千年);芬烈(喻功业盛美)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“粉”</span>( <span class="dicpy">fěn</span>)。米细末，化妆用的粉末 <span class="encs">[face powder]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五隐之状，黑土黑苔，青怵以肥，芬然若灰。——<span class="smcs">《管子·地员》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>芬</strong> <span class="dicpy">fēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" class="encs">[disorderly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘玄四龙，回驰北行，羽旄殷盛，芬哉芒芒。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·礼乐志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:芬芬(纷纷。杂乱的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“坟”</span>。隆起的样子 <span class="encs">[mound-like]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五壤之状，芬然若泽、若屯土。——<span
