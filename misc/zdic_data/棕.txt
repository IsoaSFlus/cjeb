                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">棕</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>棕</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zōng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～榈〕常绿乔木，茎直立不分枝，叶大，木材可制器具，通称&ldquo;棕树&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>〔～毛〕棕榈叶鞘的纤维，简称&ldquo;棕&rdquo;，如&ldquo;～绳&rdquo;，&ldquo;～绷&rdquo;，&ldquo;～帚&rdquo;，&ldquo;～编&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>〔～熊〕哺乳动物，体大，毛棕褐色。掌和肉可食，皮可制皮褥，胆可入药。亦称&ldquo;马熊&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">罴</span>&rdquo;；通称&ldquo;人熊&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>棕</strong></p>
<p>椶 <span class="dicpy">zōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 植物名。棕榈的省称 <span class="encs">[palm]</span>。本作椶</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">椶，栟榈也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:龙棕;蜡棕;扇棕;棕片(棕衣。棕皮);棕车(以棕为盖顶的车);棕笋(棕榈的花苞)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 棕毛或棕片 <span class="encs">[palm fiber]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:棕笠;棕床;棕冠;棕帚;棕匠</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 棕色的染料 <span class="encs">[brown dye]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:酸性棕;碱性棕;直接棕</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>棕</strong> <span class="dicpy">zōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>棕毛的颜色，即褐色 <span class="encs">[brown]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:红、黄、棕等各种颜色的布;棕人(指棕色人称);棕绿(绿中泛棕色的一种颜色)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">椶</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“棕”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/椶">椶</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷六">卷六</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷六&amp;swbs=木">木</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/6936.svg">                          <p>栟櫚也。可作萆。从木㚇聲。子紅切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6936_2882.svg" /><span class="swbf">(椶)</span><span class="ywyy">栟櫚也。</span><span class="swdz">互訓也。蜀都賦椶枒楔樅，四木名也。</span><span class="ywyy">從木㚇聲。</span><span class="swdz">子紅切。九部。按椶與召南之緫音義略同。毛曰：緫，數也。數讀數罟之數。</span><span class="ywyy">可作萆。</span><span class="swdz">艸部曰：萆，雨衣。一名衰衣。按可作萆之文，不系於栟下而系椶下者。此樹有葉無枝，其皮曰椶，可爲衰。故不系栟下也。椶本皮名，因以爲樹名，故栟閭與椶得互訓也。張揖注上林賦曰：并閭，椶也。皮可以爲索，今之椶繩也。玉篇云：椶櫚一名蒲葵。今按南方艸木狀云：蒲葵如栟櫚而柔薄，可爲簦笠，出龍川。是蒲葵與椶樹各物也。謝安之蒲葵扇，今江蘇所謂芭蕉扇也。椶葉縷析，不似蒲葵葉成片可爲笠與扇。</span></p>                          </div>
