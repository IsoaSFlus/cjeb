                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>闿</strong></p><p>闓 <span class="dicpy">kǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，豈声。本义:开门;开)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[open]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闿，开也，——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">开户，楚谓之闿。——<span class="smcs">《方言六》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今欲与汉闿大关。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·匈奴传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:闿关(开关);闿拓(开展);闿门(敞开门);闿置(开设);闿彻(开通);闿阖(开合)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 启发，开导 <span class="encs">[inspire;enlighten]</span>。如:闿导(启发引导);闿阐(解说;开导)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>闿</strong></p><p>闓 <span class="dicpy">kǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 开阔;明朗 <span class="encs">[open;wide;bright and clear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闿，明也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:闿明(开阔明朗);闿爽(开朗);闿敏(开朗聪敏);闿疏(开朗通达)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“恺”</span>。和乐 <span class="encs">[happy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昆虫闿怿，回首面内。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马相如传》</span>。又如:闿悦(欢乐);闿怿(和乐的样子);闿悌(和易近人)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95D3.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】苦亥切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】可亥切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音愷。【說文】開也。【正韻】闢也，解也。【揚子·方言】東齊開戸謂之閻笘，楚謂之闓。【前漢·匈奴傳】今欲與漢闓大關。【曹植·矯志詩】門機之闓，楛矢不追。</p><p>又【博雅】闓，欲也。</p><p>又通作凱。【前漢·司馬相如傳】昆蟲闓懌。【史記】作凱澤。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>丘哀切，音開。【集韻】明也。</p><p>又決拾也。【儀禮·鄕射禮·袒決遂註】決，猶闓也，以象骨爲之，著右大擘指，以鈎弦闓體也。【釋文】闓，音開。</p><p>又人名。【後漢·劉表傳】遂起立學校，博求儒術，綦母闓、宋忠等。【註】闓，音開。</p><p>又【廣韻】苦蓋切【集韻】【韻會】口漑切【正韻】丘蓋切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音慨。義同。</p>
