						<p>● <strong>阎</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閻</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>里巷的门，亦指里巷。</li>
	<li>〔～罗〕佛教称鬼王，主宰地狱。亦称&ldquo;阎王&rdquo;、&ldquo;阎罗王&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> village gate; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Eingang zu einer Gasse  (S, Arch)​,Yan  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> porte,hameau,rue,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阎</strong></p>
<p>閻、閆 <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，臽( <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n</span>)声。本义:内巷内的门，亦指里巷)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gate of a lane]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">閻，里中门也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隐于穷阎漏屋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span>。注:&ldquo;里门也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兴于闾阎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;循吏传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">便旋闾阎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;里中门曰阎。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阎闾(里巷内外的门);阎术(里巷);阎亲(乡亲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">閻</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“阎”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/閻">閻</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閻·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95BB.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>余廉切，音鹽。【說文】里中門。【後漢&middot;班固傳】閭閻且千。【註】字林曰：閻，里中門也。【史記&middot;越世家】莊生雖居窮閻，然以廉直聞於國。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】巷也。【博雅】閻謂之衖。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】閻笘，開也。東齊開戸謂之閻笘。</p>
<p>又勸也。【揚子&middot;方言】食閻，勸也。南楚，凡已不欲喜，而旁人說之，不欲怒，而旁人怒之，謂之食閻。或謂之慫慂。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】姓也。閻職。見【左傳&middot;文十八年】閻敖，楚大夫。見【莊十八年】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>徐廉切，音燅。鬼閻，地名。在潁川。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】戰於鬼閻。【註】潁川長平縣西北有閻亭。【釋文】閻，似廉反。</p>
<p>又縣名。【左傳&middot;昭九年】周甘人與晉閻嘉爭閻田。【註】閻嘉，晉閻縣大夫。【釋文】閻，以廉反。</p>
<p>又【集韻】以贍切，音爓。【說文】好而長也。【五音集韻】美色也。</p>
<p>又衣長也。【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】眇閻易以戍削。【註】閻易，衣長貌。</p>
<p>又【集韻】以冉切，音琰。地名。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】鬼閻。【釋文】又以冉反。　【集韻】或作壛。</p>
