                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">繁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>繁</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>字本作&ldquo;緐&rdquo;.马头上的饰物.同本义<span class="encs">[horse decoration]</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">緐，马髦饰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;引申为緐多。又俗改其字作繁，俗形行而本形废，引申之义行而本义废矣。&rdquo;</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>繁</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 众多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">正月繁霜，我心忧伤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;正月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奉之弥繁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《权书&middot;六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善始者始繁，克终者盖寡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:繁剧(事务繁重);繁伙(众多);繁星(众多的星星);繁霜(浓霜，借喻为白色);繁言(多言);繁虑(很多的忧虑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 盛大;旺盛 <span class="encs">[grand; vigorous]</span>。如:繁昌(繁荣昌盛);繁俎(丰盛的肴馔);繁盛(繁荣兴盛;繁多昌盛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 复杂 <span class="encs">[complicated]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见简即用，见繁即变，此乃通术也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:繁务(杂事);繁句(凡句子中，文法结构比较复杂的，称为繁句);繁思(繁杂的思绪)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 茂盛 <span class="encs">[luxuriant]</span></p>
