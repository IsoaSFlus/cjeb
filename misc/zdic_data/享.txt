						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>享</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄤˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>受用：～福。～乐。～誉。～年（敬辞，享有的年岁，对人或朝代而言）。～受。～用。～有。～国（帝王在位年数）。安～。分～。</li>
	<li>贡献（指把祭品，珍品献给祖先、神明或天子、侯王），上供：～堂。～殿。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">享</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>享</strong></p>
<p>亯 <span class="dicpy">xiǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。本作&ldquo;亯&rdquo;。从&ldquo;高&rdquo;省，&ldquo;曰&rdquo;象进献熟物形。本义:祭献，上供。用物品进献人，供奉鬼神使其享受)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sacrifice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">享，献也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">享于祖考。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;信南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是用孝享。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;天保》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以享以祀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸侯侯以享天子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;玉人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王用享于西山。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;随》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">享尝乃止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相夺予享。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兹予大享于先王。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀牛宰马，祭天享地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:享厅(供奉祖宗木主或神佛偶像的地方);享牛(供祭祀的牛)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 人享受福禄 <span class="encs">[enjoy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大臣享其禄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而享其生禄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子孙享之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《刘熊碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">享无穷逸致。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不享其利。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受享无穷。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滋味之享。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常只是遇节令，设蔬品奉祭，未卜享否?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:享强寿(享受到长寿之年);共享太平</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 献，像进贡那样地交付、献出 <span class="encs">[贡奉品]</span> <span class="encs">[tribute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫敢不来享。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;殷武》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:享觐(朝见天子进献贡品);享礼;享上(奉承上级)</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4EAF.svg">                          <p>獻也。从高省，曰象進孰物形。《孝經》曰：&ldquo;祭則鬼亯之。&rdquo;凡亯之屬皆从亯。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20156.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠅖</span>，篆文亯。許兩切。又，暜庚切。又，許庚切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4EAF_86.svg" /><span class="swbf">(亯)</span><span class="ywyy">獻也。</span><span class="swdz">下進上之䛐也。按周禮用字之例，凡祭亯用亯字，凡饗燕用饗字。如大宗伯吉禮下六言亯先王，嘉禮下言以饗燕之禮親四方賓客，尤其明證也。禮經十七篇用字之例，聘禮內臣亯君字作亯。士虞禮、少牢饋食禮尙饗字作饗，小戴記用字之例。凡祭亯、饗燕字皆作饗，無作亯者。左傳則皆作亯，無作饗者。毛詩之例，則獻於神曰亯，神食其所亯曰饗。如楚茨以亯以祀，下云神保是饗。周頌我將我亯，下云旣右饗之。魯頌亯祀不忒、亯以騂犧，下云是饗是宜。商頌以假以亯，下云來假來饗。皆其明證也。鬼神來食曰饗，卽禮經尙饗之例也。獻於神曰亯，卽周禮祭亯作亯之例也。各經用字自各有例，周禮之饗燕，左傳皆作亯宴。此等葢本書固尒，非由後人改竄。</span><span class="ywyy">从高省，</span><span class="swdz">獻者必高奉之。曲禮曰：執天子之器則上衡。國君則平衡。後世亦以舉案齊眉爲敬。</span><span class="ywyy">曰象孰物形。</span><span class="swdz">禮經言饋食者，薦孰也。許㒳切。十部。亯象薦孰，因以爲飪物之偁，故又讀普庚切。亯之義訓薦神，誠意可通於神，故又讀許庚切。古音則皆在十部。其形，薦神作亨，亦作享。飪物作亨，亦作烹。易之元亨，則皆作亨。皆今字也。</span><span class="ywyy">孝經曰：祭則鬼亯之。</span><span class="swdz">孝經孝治章文。</span><span class="ywyy">凡亯之屬皆从亯。</span></p>
