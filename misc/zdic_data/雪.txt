                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>雪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xuě  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuě.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄝˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>天空中飘落的白色结晶体，多为六角形，是天空中的水蒸气冷至摄氏零度以下凝结而成：～花。～山。～中送炭（喻在别人遇到困难时及时给予帮助）。</li>    <li>洗去，除去：报仇～恨。为国～耻。平反昭～。</li>    <li>擦拭：“晏子独笑于旁，公～涕而顾晏子”。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">雪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雪</strong> <span class="dicpy">xuě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuě.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从雨，从彗，彗( <span class="dicpy">huì</span>)省声。本义:空气中的水汽，冷却到摄氏零度以下时，就有部分凝结成冰晶，由空中降下，叫做雪)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[snow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今我来思，雨雪霏霏。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采薇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">春洩气为雨，雨凝为雪。——</span><span class="diczx2">谢惠莲</span><span class="smcs">《雪赋》</span>题注引<span class="smcs">《五经通训》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平地尺为大雪。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">麻衣如雪。——<span class="smcs">《诗·曹风·蜉虫游》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[孟冬之月]</span>行秋令，则雪霜不时，小兵时起，土地侵削。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平生所娇儿，颜色白胜雪。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《北征》</span>诗</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:雪案萤窗(比喻勤学苦读);雪里鸿(即雪泥鸿爪);雪浪纸(一种有波浪形暗纹的宣纸)</p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 借喻白色 <span class="encs">[white]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雪衣雪发青玉嘴，群捕鱼儿溪影中。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>·杜牧<span class="smcs">《鹭鸶》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君不见高堂明镜悲白发，朝如青丝暮成雪。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《将进酒》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:雪厾(白银);雪练(色泽像雪白的绢一样);雪花银(白银);雪肌(白细如雪的肌肤);雪色(白色)</p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 比喻高洁 <span class="encs">[noble and unsillied]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">松品落落，雪格索索。——</span><span class="diczx2">前蜀</span>· <span class="diczx2">贯休</span><span class="smcs">《送姜道士归南岳》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一别高人又十年，霜筋雪骨健依然。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">杨万里</span>诗</p><p><span class="diczx1">松品落落，雪格索索。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">贯休</span><span class="smcs">《送姜道士归南岳》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:雪格(比喻高洁的品格);雪淡(态度淡漠，不热心)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>雪</strong> <span class="dicpy">xuě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuě.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 下雪 <span class="encs">[snow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于时始雪，五处俱贺。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:雪夜访普(宋太祖赵匡胤曾于雪夜到大臣赵普家商谈国事的故事);雪峰(积雪的山峰);雪云(降雪的阴云)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 洗刷，昭雪 <span class="encs">[wipe out;rehabilitate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大夫</span><span
