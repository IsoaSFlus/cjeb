						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>寡</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">guǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎ.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄚˇ</span></p><ol><li>少，缺少：～薄。～恩。～陋。～趣。优柔～断。～不敌众。孤陋～闻。～廉鲜（<span class="dicpy">xiǎn </span>）耻（不廉洁，不知耻）。清心～欲。</li><li>淡而无味：～味。清汤～水。</li><li>妇女死了丈夫：～妇。～母。～居。守～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寡</strong> <span class="dicpy">guǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 少;缺少 <span class="encs">[few]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡，少也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">曹操</span>比于 <span class="diczx2">袁绍</span>，则名微而众寡。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不患寡而患不均。——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:寡尤(少犯错误);寡功(功效少);寡见(见识少);寡交(与人交往少)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 孤独;孤单 <span class="encs">[lonely;alone]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡，独也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生成及彊而寡。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南面称寡。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·士容》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自谓孤寡。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:寡老(孤独的老人);寡孤(孤独，孤寂);寡女(独居无偶的妇女);寡立(犹独立;孤单);寡草(孤生的草)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 弱小 <span class="encs">[weak]</span>。如:寡力(力量弱小);寡弱(势孤力小);寡萌(小民)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 倒霉;不吉利 <span class="encs">[unlucky]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今早空肚皮进城，就吃了这一肚寡气。——<span class="smcs">《古今小说·沈小霞相会出师表》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 淡而无味 <span class="encs">[wishy-washy;be like milk and water]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">清汤寡水</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>寡</strong> <span class="dicpy">guǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 寡妇，丈夫死去后还未再嫁的女人 <span class="encs">[widow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哀鳏寡(此指老而无夫的女人);恤孤独。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无夫曰寡。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爰及矜人，哀此鳏寡。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·鸿雁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行人驻足听，寡妇起彷徨。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:鳏寡</p><p>◎ <strong>寡</strong> <span class="dicpy">guǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，从<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，从<span
