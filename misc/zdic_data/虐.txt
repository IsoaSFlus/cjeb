                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">虐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>虐</strong> <span class="dicpy">nüè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nüè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。从虍( <span class="dicpy">hǔ</span> 指虎头)，爪人。即虎足反爪伤人。隶变后省略<span class="yhcs">“人”</span>字。本义:凶恶，残暴)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[brutal;cruel;and ferocious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虐，残也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">厉王</span>虐，国人谤王。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民之憔悴于虐政，未有甚于此时者也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">加以官贪吏虐，民日贴妇卖儿，更无休止。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:虐世(残暴的时代);虐人(残暴凶狠的人);虐暴(暴虐，残暴酷虐);虐焰(残暴的气焰);虐行(残暴的行为);虐刻(残暴苛刻);虐烈(残暴酷烈);虐遇(虐待)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 暴烈;险恶 <span class="encs">[violent;perilous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遘厉虐疾。——<span class="smcs">《书·金縢》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“暴也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">到州日亭午，余暑秋更虐。——</span><span class="diczx2">陈基</span><span class="smcs">《通州》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:虐疾(重病;恶疾);虐暑(酷暑;酷热)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 无节制;纵情 <span class="encs">[to one’s heart’s content]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">善戏谑兮，不为虐。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱熹</span>集传:<span class="yhcs">“善戏谑不为虐者，言其乐易而有节也。”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>虐</strong> <span class="dicpy">nüè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nüè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 侵害;残害 <span class="encs">[abuse;mishandle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">方命虐民，饮食若流。——孟子·梁惠王下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奸官污吏虐民。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:虐人(残害人民);虐戕(残害);虐害(残害，残酷迫害);虐割(残害)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span>
