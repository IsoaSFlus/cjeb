                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>扎</strong></p><p>紥、紮、 <span class="dicpy">zā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 捆绑;缠束;拴;系 <span class="encs">[tie;bind;fasten]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抄扎家私，分俵众军。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:扎抹(缠绑;收拾);扎爪(拴绑;缠绑);扎一根红头绳;扎花环;扎包(捆在腰间的长布);扎缚(捆扎;包扎);扎脚(缠足);扎把(成捆的草把)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 编 <span class="encs">[plait]</span>。如:扎小辫儿</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挽上，卷起来 <span class="encs">[roll up]</span>。如:扎高裤脚;扎起袖子;扎脚勒手(卷起裤脚和衣袖)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 趁人之危，勒索财物 <span class="encs">[blackmail;extort under false pretences]</span>。如:扎火囤(用女色设骗局诈取财物);扎诈(讹诈)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 捉<span class="encs">[迷藏]</span> <span class="encs">[hide and catch]</span>。如:扎盲盲(捉迷藏);扎朦(捉迷藏)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 准备 <span class="encs">[be ready]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">阿里奇</span>]</span>传令教番兵扎掂已了，来日出 <span class="diczx2">密云县</span>，与 <span class="diczx2">宋江</span>交锋。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span>
