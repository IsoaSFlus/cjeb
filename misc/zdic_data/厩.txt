                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>厩</strong></p>
<p>廄、廏 <span class="dicpy">ji&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。古文字中，&ldquo;厂&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>)与&ldquo;广&rdquo;常常通用，都表示与房舍、居住有关。本义:马圈，马棚)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厩，马舍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六系为厩，厩一仆夫，计马二百十六匹也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;校人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">新延厩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公二十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厩焚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狗马实外厩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厩中仆马。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所立厩中。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厩长(掌管马厩的官吏);厩苑(养马场);厩率(马夫);厩副(马厩的副职官长);厩圉(马棚); 厩闲(马房)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指牲口棚 <span class="encs">[catble-shed;pen]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:厩肥</span></p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“厩”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/廏">廏</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九">卷九</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九&amp;swbs=广">广</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5ECF.svg">                          <p>馬舍也。从广㲃聲。《周禮》曰：&ldquo;馬有二百十四匹爲廏，廏有僕夫。&rdquo; <img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20919.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤙</span>，古文从九。居又切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5ECF_1735.svg" /><span class="swbf">(廏)</span><span class="ywyy">馬舍也。从广㲃聲。</span><span class="swdz">居又切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">周禮曰：馬有二百十四匹爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/222C1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢋁</span>，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/222C1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢋁</span>有僕夫。</span><span class="swdz">四當爲六，字之誤也。夏官校人曰：乘馬一師四圉。三乘爲皁，皁一趣馬。三皁爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AA0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣪠</span>。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AA0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣪠</span>一馭夫。六<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23AA0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣪠</span>爲廏，廏一僕夫。六廏成校。校有左右。注曰：二耦爲乘，四匹也。自乘至廏，其數二百一十六匹。易，乾爲馬。此應乾之策也。</span></p><hr class="swhr" /><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5ECF_1736.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20919.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤙</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文从九。</span><span class="swdz">此从古文叀，而九聲也。</span></p>                          </div>
