                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绽</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>绽</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">綻</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>衣缝脱线解开，引申为裂开：～裂。～开。～露。～放。破～（漏洞）。皮开肉～。</li><li>缝补，缝纫：“故衣谁为补，新衣谁当～。”</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>绽</strong></p><p>綻 <span class="dicpy">zhàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，定声。糸，细丝。本义:衣服裂开)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[burst;split]</span>。俗称<span class="yhcs">“开线”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衣裳绽裂。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:绽破(衣缝裂开);将绽(衣缝快要裂开)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 皮肉破裂 <span class="encs">[open;the skin is torn]</span>。如:肉绽皮开(皮肉破裂);绽口(开口)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 饱满、开裂。特指花果 <span class="encs">[break open as flowers burst]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">春色方盈野，枝枝绽翠英。——</span><span class="diczx2">北周</span>· <span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《杏花》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:绽花(花苞绽放);绽蕊(开放的花)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 缝补 <span class="encs">[sew and mend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茅茨覆宫殿，封章绽帷帐。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《感怀》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:绽衣(缝衣)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 破费;花费 <span class="encs">[go to some expense]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">多参白水江湖酒，少绽黄边风月钱。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">汤显祖</span><span class="smcs">《还魂记》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
