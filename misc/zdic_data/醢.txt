                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">醢</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>醢</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎi.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄞˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用肉、鱼等制成的酱。</li>
	<li>古代的一种酷刑，把人杀死后剁成肉酱。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> minced pickled meat; mince</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Hackfleisch  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (supplice)​ se faire hacher menu,se faire réduire en bouillie</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>醢</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉。本义:肉酱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[minced and hashed meat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脯醢菜羹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:醢脯(肉酱和干肉);醢酱(肉酱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 酱 <span class="encs">[sauce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">醢，酱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自酒米至于盐醢百有余品，皆尽时味。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《魏书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡国家大事、兵农礼乐，以及钱刀醯醢之细，无不经营贯串。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">毛奇龄</span><span class="smcs">《王君慎斋诗集序》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>醢</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>古代一种酷刑，将人剁成肉酱 <span class="encs">[mince up sb.into meat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀</span><span class="diczx2">梅伯</span>而醢之，杀 <span class="diczx2">鬼侯</span>而脯之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎行论》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=酉" target="_blank">酉</a>】
                              醢·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/91A2.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】呼攺切【集韻】許亥切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音海。【說文】肉<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24595.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤖕</span>也。【詩&middot;大雅】醓醢以薦。【疏】醢，肉汁也。【禮&middot;曲禮】毋歠醢。【疏】醢，肉醬也。醬宜鹹，客若歠之，則是醬淡也。【周禮&middot;天官】醢人掌四豆之實，醓醢、蠃醢、蠯醢、蜃蚳醢、兔醢、魚醢、鴈醢。【註】凡作醢者，必先膊乾其肉，乃後莝之，雜以粱麴及鹽，漬以美酒，塗置甁中，百日則成。鄭司農曰：無骨曰醢。</p>
<p>又烹也。【史記&middot;魯仲連傳】吾將使秦王烹醢梁王。【前漢&middot;班固敘傳】然卒潤鑊伏質，亨醢分裂。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶虎李切，音喜。【楚辭&middot;九章】忠不必用兮，賢不必以。伍子逢殃兮，比干葅醢。</p>
