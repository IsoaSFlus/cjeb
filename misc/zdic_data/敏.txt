                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>敏</strong> <span class="dicpy">mǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，每声。甲骨文字形，像用手整理头发的样子。本义:动作快)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[quick;agile;nimble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敏，疾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曾孙不怒，农夫克敏。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·甫田》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子欲纳于言而敏于行。——<span class="smcs">《论语·里仁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敏于事而慎于言。——<span class="smcs">《论语·学而》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:敏给(敏捷);敏赡(敏捷而多智);敏睿(敏捷聪慧);敏疾(敏捷，迅速);敏思(才思敏捷);敏才(敏捷的才思)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 思想敏锐，反应快 <span class="encs">[intelligent;clever]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">回</span>虽不敏，请事斯语矣。——<span class="smcs">《论语·颜渊》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是故聪与敏，可恃而不可恃也。——</span><span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我虽不敏。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">礼成而加之以敏。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“审当于事也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:敏悟(聪敏伶俐;善解人意);敏博(聪明博学);敏达(敏捷而通达事理);敏隽(聪明俊秀);敏智(聪敏机智);敏识(聪明博识);敏瞻(机灵多智;敏捷而丰富)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 勤勉 <span class="encs">[perseveringly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敏而好学，不耻下问。——<span class="smcs">《论语·公冶长》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人道敏政。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹勉也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:敏行(指勉力修身);敏求(勉力以求);敏学(勤勉好学)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>敏</strong> <span class="dicpy">mǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up
