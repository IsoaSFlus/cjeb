                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>跻</strong></p><p>躋 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，齐声。本义:升，登)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ascend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跻，登也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跻于九陵，勿逐。——<span class="smcs">《易·震》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跻彼公堂，称彼兕觥。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跻踬连绝。——</span><span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《洞箫赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:跻踬(或上或下);跻览(登高眺望);跻险(登上高险处);跻登(攀登;登上);跻峰造极(登上山峰绝顶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 达到 <span class="encs">[arrive;reach]</span>。如:跻致(使达到安居乐业的境地)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 晋升 <span class="encs">[promote]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大事于大庙，跻</span><span class="diczx2">僖公</span>，逆祀也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公二年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:跻陟(晋升，升迁);跻升(升迁)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 下坠 <span class="encs">[fall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今尔无指告予颠跻。——<span class="smcs">《书·微子》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/跻身">跻身</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
