                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">夯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>夯</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;笨&rdquo;。笨拙 <span class="encs">[stupid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小儿蠢夯，自幼失学。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:夯汉(干粗笨体力活的人);夯货(笨货;蠢人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>夯</strong> <span class="dicpy">hāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(会意。从大，从力。表示劳动时需要出大气力。本义:劳动中使劲时发出的呼声。引申筑实地基的工具)同引申义 <span class="encs">[ramming tool;rammer;punner]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:用夯筑堤</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>夯</strong> <span class="dicpy">hāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用重物把地或其他粒状材料砸密实 <span class="encs">[ram]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:把土夯实;夯硪(砸压)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 〈方〉∶用力猛击 <span class="encs">[pun]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:举起拳头向下夯</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用力扛东西 <span class="encs">[shoulder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夯，大用力以肩举物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自家闺阁中物，不肯放下，反累及他人担夯，无乃大劳乎!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">净善集</span><span class="smcs">《禅林宝训》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 捞取 <span class="encs">[fish for;gain]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:夯市(纵兵大闹街市)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 充胀，胀满 <span class="encs">[swell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有口难言，气夯破胸脯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王实甫</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>夯</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄤ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hāng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2783.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用來敲打地基，使其結實的工具。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「木<b>夯</b>」、「石<b>夯</b>」、「鐵<b>夯</b>」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">脹滿、鼓脹。</span><span class="gc_yy">元&middot;石君寶《秋胡戲妻&middot;第四折》：「怎敢來俺莊上，不由的忿氣<b>夯</b>胸膛。」</span><span class="gc_yy">元&middot;關漢卿《調風月&middot;第二折》：「氣<b>夯</b>破肚別人行怎又不敢提？獨自向銀蟾底。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">北方方言。指用力以肩扛物。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>夯</b>行李」。</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;凈善重集&middot;禪林寶訓一：「自家閨閣中物不肯放下，返累及他人擔<b>夯</b>，無乃大勞乎！」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用夯砸地。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>夯</b>實」、「<b>夯</b>地」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
