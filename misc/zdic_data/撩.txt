bottom]</span>。如:撩窗帘;撩衣(掀起衣襟);撩牙(启齿;开口);撩天(朝天) </p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用手舀水浇洒 <span class="encs">[sprinkle]</span>。如:在沟里撩水洗手</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，尞( <span class="dicpy">liáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="cino">(5)</span> 摘取 <span class="encs">[pick up]</span>。如:撩摘(摘取);撩罟(捕鱼的网)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 挑，拨 <span class="encs">[incite;provoke;stir up]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 引申为挑逗，招引，逗引 <span class="encs">[banter;tease]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屡撩之。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>
