                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">股</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>股</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>大腿，自胯至膝盖的部分：～骨。～肱（亦喻左右辅助得力的人）。</li>
	<li>事物的分支或一部分（①资金的一份，如&ldquo;～份&rdquo;，&ldquo;～东&rdquo;，&ldquo;～票&rdquo;；②机关团体中的一个部门；③其他，如&ldquo;钗～&rdquo;，&ldquo;八～文&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>中国古代称不等腰三角形构成直角的较长的边。</li>
	<li>量词（①指成条的，如&ldquo;七～大水&rdquo;；②指气味，如&ldquo;一～香味&rdquo;；③指力气，如&ldquo;拧成一～劲&rdquo;；④批、部分，如&ldquo;一小～敌军&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">股</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>股</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，殳( <span class="dicpy">shū</span>)声。本义:大腿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[thigh]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">股，髀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">膝上曰股，膝下曰胫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;宪问》</span>疏</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">读书欲睡，引锥刺其股。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余皆股栗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;酷吏列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屠自后断其股，亦毙之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻鸣镝而股战，对穹庐以屈膝又何劣邪!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:股掌(大腿与手掌);股弁(大腿发抖)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 事物的分支或一部分 <span class="encs">[section]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">注:&ldquo;</span><span class="diczx2">如淳</span>曰:股，支别也。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;沟洫志》</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:人事股;军需股</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 合股纱、线或绳的一组成部分 <span class="encs">[strand]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:六股的绣花丝线</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 资本或财物的一份 <span class="encs">[stock]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:普通股;优先股</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 不等腰直角三角形构成直角的较长的边 <span class="encs">[the longer leg of a right triangle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故折矩，以为句(即勾)广三，股修四，径隅(即弦)五。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周髀算经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:勾股弦</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>股</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一阵气味或一阵随风飘至的气味或味道 <span class="encs">[whiff]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:一股香味;一股烟悬在休眼火山上空</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 喷出或吹出的气体或液体 <span class="encs">[puff]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:从烟斗喷出的股股烟</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 一队武装人员 <span class="encs">[band]</span>。</p>
