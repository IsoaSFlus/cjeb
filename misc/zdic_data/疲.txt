                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>疲</strong> <span class="dicpy">pí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒，皮声。病字旁念 <span class="dicpy">chuáng，</span>据甲骨文，象人躺在床上害病的样子。以它作形旁的字一般与疾病有关。本义:疲乏;困倦)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tired]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疲，劳也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疲，乏也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苶然疲役。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·齐物论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疲马之死也。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·俶真》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士民疲病于内。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·初见秦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫以疲病之卒御狐疑之众。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:精疲力尽;疲敝(疲乏困苦);疲玩(疲塌轻忽);疲曳(疲弱困顿，比喻年老);疲钝(疲倦困顿);疲顽(疲困倦怠);疲匮(疲累困乏);疲弊(困苦穷乏，是疲敝);疲人(疲困之人);疲耗(困顿耗损);疲匮(劳累穷乏);疲羸(困苦穷乏)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 衰老;衰弱 <span class="encs">[weak; in poor health]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以疲马犬羊为币。——<span class="smcs">《管子·小筐》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓瘦也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:疲癃(衰老龙钟或有残疾的人);疲冗(衰弱无能);疲劣(衰弱恶劣);疲羸(衰弱)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 厌倦 <span class="encs">[be bored with]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我自乐此，不为疲也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·光武帝纪》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>疲</strong> <span class="dicpy">pí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 停止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余欣</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>土，忽有此经。…载玩载咏，欲疲不能，遂佐对校。—— <span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">道安</span><span class="smcs">《鞞婆沙序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 疲软 <span class="encs">[slump;weaken]</span>。如:橡胶期货交易在疲了一阵之后，收盘时又稍稍坚稳了一点</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 衰败;凋敝 <span class="encs">[decline;depressed]</span>。如:疲暮(暮年;衰老);疲老(衰老);疲瘵(凋敝);疲罢(疲敝，衰败)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲惫">疲惫</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲敝">疲敝</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲病">疲病</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲怠">疲怠</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲顿">疲顿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲乏">疲乏</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲倦">疲倦</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲困">疲困</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲劳">疲劳</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲累">疲累</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲软">疲软</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲弱">疲弱</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲塌">疲塌</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/疲于奔命">疲于奔命</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
