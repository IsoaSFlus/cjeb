						<p>● <strong>纵</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg"><span class="diczx3">縱</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">z&ograve;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄨㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>放：～虎归山。～火。</li>
	<li>放任；不拘束：放～。～目四望。～情。～观全局。</li>
	<li>身体猛然向前或向上：～身。</li>
	<li>即使：～然。～使。～令。</li>
	<li>竖，直，南北的方向，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">横</span>&rdquo;相对：～横交错。～横捭阖（指在政治、外交上运用手段进行联合或分化）。～贯。～深。</li>
	<li>起皱纹：～花（用有皱纹的纸做成的花）。这张纸都～了。</li>
	<li>指连队编制上的&ldquo;纵队&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纵</strong></p>
<p>縱 <span class="dicpy">z&ograve;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，从声。本义:松缓)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[loose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵，缓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蓬发施纵，无形仪，不治家业。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《南齐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纵缓(松懈);纵弛(松懈;放松)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 南北称纵，东西称横。古作&ldquo;从衡&rdquo; <span class="encs">[from north to south]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不别横之与纵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;沈江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵有千古，横有八荒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纵贯南北的京九铁路</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 跟物体的长的一边平行的 <span class="encs">[longitudinal;lengthwise]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:纵波;纵目(直竖的眼睛);纵理入口(面部有竖纹衔接至口边。命相者以为饿死之相);纵剖面</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 广泛 <span class="encs">[extensively]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E31.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25F9E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥾞</span>【廣韻】子用切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】足用切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>蹤去聲。【說文】緩也。一曰舍也。【博雅】置也。【玉篇】恣也，放也。【書&middot;太甲】縱敗禮。【詩&middot;大雅】無縱詭隨。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;鄭風】抑縱送忌。【註】發矢曰縱。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;仲尼燕居】縱言至于禮。【註】縱言，汎說事。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;襄二十六年】敢有二心乎。縱有，共其內，莫共其外。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】縱，亂也。</p>
<p>又省作從。【論語】從之純如也。【前漢&middot;王吉傳】放從自若。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】祖動切【正韻】作孔切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音總。【禮&middot;檀弓】喪事欲其縱縱爾。【註】讀如總領之總。急遽趨事貌。</p>
<p>又【集韻】足勇切，音䙕。慫或作縱。【前漢&middot;衡山王傳】日夜縱臾。【註】師古曰：縱臾，謂獎勸也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】卽容切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】將容切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蹤。【廣韻】縱橫也。【韻會】或作從。【詩&middot;齊風】衡從其畝。【類篇】東西曰衡，南北曰從。【賈誼&middot;過秦論】合從締交。【註】從，與縱同。</p>
<p>又與蹤通。【前漢&middot;蕭何傳】發縱指示獸處者，人也。【註】師古曰：讀者乃爲蹤蹟之蹤，非也。○按隷釋袁良<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>：往者王尊發縱于平陽。楊著<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>：追縱魯參。魯峻<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>：比縱豹產。皆以縱爲蹤。師古之說非是。</p>
<p>又【集韻】徂聰切，音叢。髻高大貌。</p>
