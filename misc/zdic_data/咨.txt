						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>咨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗˉ</span></p><ol><li>商议，询问：～问。～访。～询。</li><li>用于同级机关的一种公文：～文。</li><li>叹气的声音：～嗟（a.叹息；b.赞叹）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> inquire, consult, discuss; plan</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> konsultieren, Befragen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> consulter</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>咨</strong> <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，次声。本义:商议;征询)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[take counsel;consult]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咨，谋事曰咨。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作谘。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咨，谋也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">周爰咨诹。——<span class="smcs">《诗·皇皇者华》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咨于故实。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">访问于善为咨。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咨亲为询。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">悉以咨之。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">咨臣以当世之事。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">以咨诹善道。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂随使者倒军，署司空户曹掾，引见咨议。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·魏志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:咨叩(咨询求教);咨考(询问查考);咨度(咨询;商酌);咨问(咨询;请教);咨禀(请教;禀告)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 叹息;赞叹 <span class="encs">[sigh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">文王</span>流涕而咨之。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">微微老夫，咨既迁绝。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·韦贤传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">对曰:<span class="yhcs">“</span><span class="diczx2">巢</span>、 <span class="diczx2">许</span>狷介之士，不足多慕。”</span>王大咨嗟。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·言语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:咨叹(叹息);咨闵(叹息怜恤);咨惋(叹惜);咨咨(叹息;叹息声)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 用同<span class="yhcs">“龇”</span>。露出<span class="encs">[牙齿]</span> <span class="encs">[bare]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">大圣</span>喝了一口，即咨牙咧嘴道:<span class="yhcs">“不好吃!不好吃!”</span>——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>咨</strong> <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><叹></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示赞赏，相当于<span class="yhcs">“啧”</span> <span class="encs">[appreciate;admire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帝曰:<span class="yhcs">“咨!四岳!朕在位七十载，汝能庸命，巽朕位。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">尧</span>曰:“咨!尔 <span class="diczx2">舜</span>!天之历数在尔躬，允执其中”</span>。——<span class="smcs">《论语·尧曰》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:咨嗟(赞叹之声);咨玩(赞叹玩赏)</p><p>◎ <strong>咨</strong> <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 旧时公文的一种。咨文的简称 <span class="encs">[official document]</span>。用于同级机关</p><p><span class="diczx1">少保据着</span><span class="diczx2">萧云仙</span>的详文，咨明兵部。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:咨称(公文上说);咨呈(具文呈报);咨注(载入官牒)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/咨文">咨文</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
