                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>丧</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">喪</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄤ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>跟死了人有关的事：～事。～礼。～亡。～假（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;</span>）。～乱。治～。吊～。</li></ol><p>● <strong>丧</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">喪</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">s&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄤˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>丢掉，失去：～失。～生。～偶。～胆。～气（不吉利，倒霉。&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">气</span>&rdquo;读轻声）。颓～（情绪低落，精神委靡）。懊～。沮～。～权辱国。～尽天良（良心全部失去了）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mourning; mourn; funeral</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Trauer ,etw. verlieren  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> deuil,funérailles,perdre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">丧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>丧</strong></p>
<p>喪 <span class="dicpy">s&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，上面是&ldquo;哭&#39;，下面是&ldquo;亡&rdquo;。表示哭已死去的人。本义:丧失)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[forfeit;lose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">喪，亡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东北丧朋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坤》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:&ldquo;失也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受禄无丧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;皇矣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问丧无夫子乎。又，丧人无以为宝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宣王</span>既丧南国之师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非独贤者有是心也，人皆有之，贤者能勿丧耳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偃王行仁义而丧其国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丧明(眼睛失明);丧检(丧失德行，失去检点);丧履(失去庇护);丧精(失神);丧心(心理反常;丧失理智);丧志(丧失心志);丧律(丧失军纪);丧师(失去民心);丧师辱国(军队损失，国家蒙受耻辱);丧资(失去财产)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 死去 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小人多欲则多求妄用，败家丧身。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丧躯(丧身);丧元(掉头颅);丧仆(亡身);丧身(丧命);丧没(灭亡，死亡);丧明之痛(丧子之痛);丧亲(父或母亡);丧室(妻室丧亡);丧家子(丧失其家无所依存的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 灭亡;失败 <span class="encs">[perish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六国互丧，率赂</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>耶?&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:丧师(战败);丧国之社(国家灭亡，无人祭祀的宗庙);丧师(战败而损失军队);丧陷(失陷);丧败(因失败而受损失)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 逃亡;流亡 <span class="encs">[escape]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丧人(逃亡到他国的人);丧物(耗竭财物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 忘记，忘掉 <span class="encs">[forget]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今者吾丧我，汝知之乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 灰心丧气 <span class="encs">[be discouraged]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:丧惘(怅惘失神);丧沮(灰心失望);丧门(丧家之门。或称恶人或使人倒霉的人)</span></p>
