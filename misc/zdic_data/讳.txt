                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">讳</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>讳</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">諱</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>避忌，有顾忌不敢说或不愿说：～言。～匿。～饰。忌～。隐～。直言不～。～疾忌医。</li><li>古时称死去的皇帝或尊长的名字：名～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> conceal; shun; regard as taboo</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> für tabu erklären, tabuisieren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cacher,taire,tabou</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">讳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>讳</strong></p><p>諱 <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，韦声。本义:避忌。有顾忌而躲开某些事或不说某些话)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[avoid as;taboo，tabu]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讳，誋也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讳，避也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卒哭乃讳。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">舍故而讳新。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">昭王</span>之忌讳。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小史》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">讳名不讳姓。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·尽心下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>为尊者讳，为亲者讳，为贤者讳。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·闵公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:直言不讳;讳名(对尊长避免说写其名，表示尊敬的心意);讳恶(先王的名讳与国家的凶恶);讳亡(忌言亡国)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 隐;隐蔽 <span class="encs">[conceal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恐犯忌而干讳。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·谬谏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“所隐为讳。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:讳疾(隐瞒缺失);讳匿(隐匿;藏匿);讳过(隐瞒过错或失误);讳恶不悛(隐恶不改悔)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 畏，怕 <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">击断无讳。——<span class="smcs">《史记·范蔡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不讳强大。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹辟也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 死的婉辞 <span class="encs">[die]</span>。如:讳所(死难之地);讳日(死亡之日);讳问(死讯)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>讳</strong></p><p>諱 <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 忌讳的事情 <span class="encs">[forbidden word;taboo，tabu]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有公讳。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入门而问讳。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:犯讳</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 旧时指死去的帝王或尊长的名字 <span class="encs">[the name of deceased emperor or head of a family]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>讳 <span class="diczx2">武帝</span>名<span class="yhcs">“ <span class="diczx2">彻</span>”</span>为通。—— <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《讳辩》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">子厚</span>讳 <span class="diczx2">宗元</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《柳子厚墓志铭》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/讳疾忌医">讳疾忌医</a></span><span                               諱·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AF1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>許貴切，音卉。【說文】誋也。【廣韻】避也。【玉篇】隱也，忌也。【春秋序】隱諱以辟患。【公羊傳·閔元年】春秋爲尊者諱，爲親者諱，爲賢者諱。【戰國策】罰不諱强大。【註】諱，猶避也。【史記·秦始皇紀】秦俗多忌諱之禁。</p><p>又畏也。【史記·商君傳】華陽、涇陽等擊斷無諱。</p><p>又【增韻】護短曰諱。</p><p>又生曰名，死曰諱。【左傳·桓六年】周人以諱事神，名終將諱之。【疏】自殷以往，未有諱法，諱始於周。【禮·檀弓】卒哭而諱。【疏】古者生不相諱，卒哭，乃有神諱也。</p><p>又不諱，謂死也。【史記·商君傳】公叔病，如有不可諱，將奈社稷何。【後漢·桓榮傳】如有不諱，無憂家室也。【註】死者人之常，故言不諱也。</p><p>又叶呼韋切，音揮。【東方朔·七諫】願乗閒而效志兮，恐犯忌而干諱。卒撫情以寂漠兮，然怊悵而自悲。</p>
