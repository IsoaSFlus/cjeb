						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>击</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">擊</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p><ol><li>敲打，或做类似敲打的动作：～打。～鼓。～中（<span class="dicpy">zhòng</span>）。～破。～毙。～水（a.拍打水面；b.指游泳）。旁敲侧～。</li><li>攻打：～败。～毁。打～。声东～西。</li><li>碰，接触：撞～。目～。肩摩毂（<span class="dicpy">gǔ</span>）～（亦作“摩肩击毂”）。</li></ol>
