                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>碗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄢˇ</span></p><ol><li>盛（<span class="dicpy">chéng </span>）饮食的器皿，上面口大而圆：饭～。茶～。盖～。～橱。</li><li>像碗的东西：轴～儿。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>碗</strong></p><p>椀 <span class="dicpy">wǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一种圆形凹心盛食品的器具，在形式上通常接近半球形，一般比盘子深，比茶杯大和重 <span class="encs">[bowl]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芙蓉玉碗，莲子金杯。——</span><span class="diczx2">北周</span>· <span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《春赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:饭碗;瓷碗;搪瓷碗;摆碗筷;碗泥(烧制瓷器的粘土);碗窑(烧制瓷器的窑)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 像碗的东西 <span class="encs">[sth.resembling a bowl]</span>。如:橡碗子，轴碗儿;碗脱(方言。窝头)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>碗</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用来计量用碗装的饮食 <span class="encs">[bowl]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">滑出出，水冷冷，两碗来素匾食。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">朱有</span><span class="smcs">《豹子和尚自还俗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 宋朝以来灯笼一盏叫一碗 <span class="encs">[lantern]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正劝不开，只见两三碗灯笼飞也似来。——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/碗柜">碗柜</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/碗盏">碗盏</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
