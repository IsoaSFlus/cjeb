						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>遗</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">遺</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>丢失：～失。～落。</li>
	<li>漏掉：～忘。～漏。</li>
	<li>丢失的东西，漏掉的部分：补～。路不拾～。</li>
	<li>余，留：～留。～俗。～闻。～址。～风。～憾。～老（➊经历世变的老人；➋仍然效忠前一朝代的老人）。</li>
	<li>死人留下的：～骨。～言。～嘱。</li>
	<li>不自觉地排泄：～尿。～精。</li>
	<li>生物体的构造和生理机能由上一代传给下一代：～传。</li>
	<li>抛弃：～弃。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>遗</strong></p>
<p>遺 <span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 给予;馈赠 <span class="encs">[make a present;offer as a gift]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相遗以水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲厚遗之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置币遗</span><span class="diczx2">单于</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裘葛之遗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗贼(给予戕害;遭害);遗赂(赠送财物);遗劳(馈赠;犒劳);遗酌(济助);遗送(赠送);遗金(赠送金钱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 送交;交付 <span class="encs">[turn over;hand over;pay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遗</span><span class="diczx2">赵王</span>书。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遗</span><span class="diczx2">魏王</span>及公子书。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遗</span><span class="diczx2">权</span>书曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以书遗</span><span class="diczx2">操</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗书(投书;寄信);遗使(遣使);遗秉(发放粮食);遗大(赋予重大任务)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 输送 <span class="encs">[carry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">强国之民，父遗其子，兄遗其弟，妻遗其夫，皆曰:&ldquo;不得，无返!&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商召书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 加给 <span class="encs">[add]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王事敦我，政事一埤遗我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>遗</strong></p>
<p>遺 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，贵声。本义:遗失)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遗，亡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遗，离也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不遐遗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;泰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君惟不遗德刑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左伟&middot;成公十六年》</span>。注:&ldquo;失也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得人遗契者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;说符》</span>。注:&ldquo;弃也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亡矢遗镞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羊子尝行路，得遗金一饼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小学而大遗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行若遗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《朱文公校昌黎先生集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗簪坠屡(失落的簪和鞋。比喻旧有的东西);遗佚(散失亡佚);遗逸(指散失之物);遗宝(遗落的宝物);遗简(失散的书籍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 遗漏，因疏忽而漏掉 <span class="encs">[omit;leave out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刑过不避大臣，赏善不遗匹夫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗珠弃璧(喻遗弃俊才);遗才(有才能而未被发现或重用的人);遗贤(受埋没而不为世所知的贤人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 遗弃;舍弃 <span class="encs">[abandon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故旧不遗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;泰伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必遗乎今。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《朱文公校昌黎先生集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗世(抛弃世事，避世);遗荣(放弃荣华富贵，辞官归隐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 不自主地排泄 <span class="encs">[involuntary discharge of urine，etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">朔</span>尝醉入殿中，小遗殿上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗溺(遗尿。不自由的排尿);遗精;遗溲(小便失禁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 残存。留下 <span class="encs">[hand down;leave behind at one&#39;s death]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地有遗利，民有余力。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗孽(残留下来的坏人);遗噍(残存未死的人);遗黎(亡国或改朝换代后不事新朝的人民)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 缺失 <span class="encs">[lack;be short of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:遗火(失火);遗计(失计，失策);遗策(失策，失算)遗逸(缺失);遗算(失算，失策);遗筹(失策，失算);遗兆(失算);遗帙(残缺的书套);遗计(失策);遗紊(失误而紊乱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 遗忘 <span class="encs">[forget]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三日遗忘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span>。注:&ldquo;忽忘也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">审毫厘之小计，遗天下之大数。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗失(遗忘;忘记);遗倦(忘记疲倦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 遗留 <span class="encs">[leave over;hand down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因遗策。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">访其遗踪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忠烈遗骸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗安(指隐居田园，淡泊自守，不以官禄遗留子孙，而遗之以德行);遗下(留下);遗讥(让后人讥笑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 专指死人留下的 <span class="encs">[left by the deceased]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:遗光(遗留的德泽);遗表(大臣临死前写给皇帝的奏章);遗芳(死后留传的美好名声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 离开，脱离 <span class="encs">[leave]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:遗体(身体为父母所生，故称自己身体为父母的&ldquo;遗体&rdquo;);遗世(脱超尘世;避世隐居)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 堕，落下;下垂 <span class="encs">[fall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">目眇眇而遗泣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:遗英(落花);遗粒(遗落的谷粒);遗坠(掉落)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 废止 <span class="encs">[abolish;annul]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:遗寺(废旧的佛寺);遗块(废弃的土块);遗壤(荒废的土地)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/907A.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/285EE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨗮</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/285BD.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨖽</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/285ED.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨗭</span>【唐韻】以追切【集韻】【韻會】夷隹切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音夷。【說文】亡也。【易&middot;泰卦】不遐遺。【註】用心弘大，無所遺棄也。【詩&middot;小雅】棄予如遺。【註】言忘去不復存省也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;司刺】三曰遺忘。</p>
<p>又【正韻】失也。【前漢&middot;賈誼傳】功不遺矣。【後漢&middot;桓榮傳】慮無遺計。</p>
<p>又【集韻】餘也。【禮&middot;樂記】有遺音者矣。【註】有不盡之音。【屈原&middot;離騷】依彭咸之遺則。【註】遺，餘也。</p>
<p>又留也。【書&middot;大誥】寧王遺我大寶龜。【左傳&middot;昭十九年】古之遺愛也。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】便旋也。【前漢&middot;東方朔傳】小遺殿上。【南史&middot;謝幾卿傳】小遺沾令史。【註】卽俗言小便也。</p>
<p>又【正字通】遺遺，與委蛇通。【戰國策】出遺遺之門。【註】言其路逶迤也。【管子&middot;樞言篇】遺遺乎若有從治。</p>
<p>又姓。魯費宰南遺之後。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>以醉切，遺去聲。投贈也，餽也。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;遺人註】以物有所餽遺也。【左傳&middot;隱元年】請以遺之。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】余恚切【正韻】于位切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27E3D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧸽</span>。</p>
<p>又【集韻】徐醉切，音遂。盈之切，音怡。愈水切，音唯。義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】旬爲切【等韻】似爲切。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>與隨同。謙以下人也。【詩&middot;小雅】莫肯下遺。【註】遺，音隨。</p>

			<div class="gscdl">攀条折其荣，将以<span>遗</span>所思。</div><div class="gscdr">汉·佚名 《庭中有奇树》</div><div class="gscdl">邻人京城氏之孀妻有<span>遗</span>男，始龀，跳往助之。</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·列御寇  列子《愚公移山》</div><div class="gscdl">今虽耄老，未有所成，犹幸预君子之列，而承天子之宠光，缀公卿之后，日侍坐备顾问，四海亦谬称其氏名，况才之过于余者乎？今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪稍之供，父母岁有裘葛之<span>遗</span>，无冻馁之患矣；坐大厦之下而诵《诗》《书》无奔走之劳矣；有司业、博士为之师，未有问而不告，求而不得者也；凡所宜有之书，皆集于此，不必若余之手录，假诸人而后见也。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div><div class="gscdl">诚宜开张圣听，以光先帝<span>遗</span>德，恢弘志士之气，不宜妄自菲薄，引喻失义，以塞忠谏之路也。</div><div class="gscdr">三国·诸葛亮 《出师表》</div><div class="gscdl">侍中、侍郎郭攸之、费祎、董允等，此皆良实，志虑忠纯，是以先帝简拔以<span>遗</span>陛下。</div><div class="gscdr">三国·诸葛亮 《出师表》</div><div class="gscdl">若无兴德之言，则责攸之、祎、允等之慢，以彰其咎；陛下亦宜自谋，以咨诹善道，察纳雅言，深追先帝<span>遗</span>诏，臣不胜受恩感激。</div><div class="gscdr">三国·诸葛亮 《出师表》</div>      </div>
