                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>丈</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄤˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>中国市制长度单位，十尺：万～高楼。</li>    <li>测量长度和面积：～量（<span class="dicpy">liàng</span>）。</li>    <li>对老年男子的尊称：～人（❶古代对老人的尊称；❷岳父。“<span class="diczx3">人</span>”均读轻声）。老～。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。从又(手)持<span class="yhcs">“十”</span>。十，十尺。本义:长度单位，十尺) 同本义 <span class="encs">[unit of length(=3.3 meters)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丈，十尺也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度五尺为墨，倍墨谓之丈。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂成丈匹。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白发三千丈。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《秋浦歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百丈冰。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">半匹红绸一丈绫，系向牛头充炭直。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span 又如:丈尺(以丈、尺为单位来计量);丈六(一丈六尺);丈二(一丈二尺);丈数(一丈多;丈把)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>丈</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古时对长辈男子的尊称 <span class="encs">[elder person]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丈者，长也。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:丈人行(父辈;长辈);丈母(丈母娘，丈妈，岳母);丈丈(对尊长的敬称);老丈;岳丈</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 丈夫 <span class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 扶，倚。后作<span class="yhcs">“杖”</span> <span class="encs">[support with the hand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丈，借为扶行之丈。老人持丈，故谓之丈人。别用杖通。——<span class="smcs">《六书正伪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 测量土地 <span
