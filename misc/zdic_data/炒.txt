                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">炒</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>炒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>把东西放在锅里搅拌着弄熟：～菜。～米。～面。～冷饭（喻办事不讲效率，说话做事只是简单地重复过去，没有新的内容）。～鱿鱼（方言，因鱿鱼一炒就卷起来，因以借指卷铺盖，解雇。亦简作&ldquo;炒&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>倒买倒卖：～黄鱼（指倒买倒卖黄金）。～地皮。～买～卖（指对股票外汇的买卖）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fry, saute, roast, boil, cook</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> etwas in der Pfanne kurz braten, sautieren; hochzüchten,  Krach schlagen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faire sauter,sauté (cuisine)​,frit,jeter au feu,livrer aux flammes</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">炒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>炒</strong> <span class="dicpy">chǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，少声。本义:煎炒，火干)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一种烹调方法。把食物或其他东西放在锅里加热翻动使熟或使干，一般先要放少量的食油 <span class="encs">[stir-fry]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炒肉丝;炒花生</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 中药炮制法之一。将药材放在锅内加热、翻动至一定要求 <span class="encs">[parch]</span>。炒时不加辅料叫清炒;加辅料同炒的有:麸炒、土炒、米炒、酒炒、醋炒等</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 吵闹。通&ldquo;吵&rdquo; <span class="encs">[make a noise]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:炒炒(吵吵，许多人乱说话);炒刺(吵闹)</span></p>
