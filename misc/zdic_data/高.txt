                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">高</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>高</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄠˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>由下到上距离大的，与“<span class="diczx3">低</span>”相对：～峰。～空。～踞。～原。～耸。～山流水（喻知己、知音或乐曲高妙）。～屋建瓴（形容居高临下的形势）。～瞻远瞩。</li>    <li>高度：他身～一米八。</li>    <li>等级在上的：～级。～考。</li>    <li>在一般标准或平均程度之上：～质量。～消费。～价。～档。～手。～能物理。</li>    <li>声音响亮：引吭～歌。</li>    <li>敬辞，称别人的事物：～见。～就。～论。～寿。～堂。～徒。</li>    <li>热烈、盛大：～昂。兴～采烈。</li>    <li>显贵，道德水平高：崇～。清～。～风亮节。～尚。～雅。～洁。</li>    <li>宗族中最在上之称：～祖。</li>    <li>酸根或化合物中比标准酸根多含一个氧原子的：～锰酸钾。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">高</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>高</strong> <span class="dicpy">gāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象楼台重叠之形。汉字部首之一。从<span class="yhcs">“高”</span>的字多与高大或建筑有关。本义:离地面远;从下向上距离大)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[high;tall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高，崇也，像台观高之形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巽为高。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span>传</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穷高极远，而测深厚。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不登高山，不知天之高也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>、 <span class="diczx2">韩</span>之患，则大王高枕而卧，国必无忧矣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高树多悲风，海水扬其波。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《野田黄雀行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">城非不高也，池非不深也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·公孙丑下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">最高</span><span class="diczx2">日观峰</span>，在 <span class="diczx2">长城</span>南十五里。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:高平(指土地高而平);高丘(高的土山);高坛(高耸的祭坛);高燥(指土地较高而又干燥)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 等级在上，超越流俗或标准 <span class="encs">[above the average;of a high degree;superior;advanced]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻辞天子，非高也，势薄也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是高材疾足者先得焉。——<span class="smcs">《史记·淮阴侯列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则今之高爵显位，一旦抵罪，或脱身以逃，不能容于远近。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">居庙堂之高，则忧其民。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:高世(高出世人);高仙(崇高的仙人);高作(高着;绝招);高轨(高尚的规范、崇高的典范);高品(品德高尚的人，技艺高超的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 敬称，称别人的事物 <span class="encs">[your]</span></p><p><span
