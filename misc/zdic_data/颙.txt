                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>颙</strong></p><p>顒 <span class="dicpy">yóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>(形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xié</span>)，禺( <span class="dicpy">yù</span>)声。页，头。本义:大头) 同本义 <span class="encs">[big head]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颙，大头也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>颙</strong></p><p>顒 <span class="dicpy">yóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形容大的样子 <span class="encs">[grand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四牡修广，其大有颙。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·六月》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 温和肃敬的样子 <span class="encs">[respectful;solemn and just]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有孚颙若。——<span class="smcs">《易·观》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“敬也。”</span>虞注:<span class="yhcs">“君德有威容貌。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颙颙夘夘。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·卷阿》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颙候来报，以慰我思。——</span><span class="diczx2">辽</span>· <span class="diczx2">李良嗣</span><span class="smcs">《与李处温使为辽内应书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:颙俟(颙候;颙竢。恭敬地等候);颙祈(恭敬地祈求);颙颙夘夘(肃敬轩昂的样子)</p><p>◎ <strong>颙</strong></p><p>顒 <span class="dicpy">yóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>仰慕，盼望 <span class="encs">[hold sb.in high esteem]</span>。如:颙望(盼望);颙戴(仰慕拥戴);颙想(仰慕怀念);颙仰(仰慕)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
