                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>枥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">櫪</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>马槽：&ldquo;老骥伏～，志在千里&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/栎">栎</a>&rdquo;，木名。</li>
	<li>[～㯕]古时刑具，即&ldquo;拶指&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> type of oak; stable</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> auge d'écurie</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>枥</strong></p>
<p>櫪 <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，厉声。本义:喂马的槽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[manger]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">养马器也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言五》</span>枥注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老骥伏枥，志在千里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《步出夏门行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骈死于槽枥之间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枥马(拴在马槽上马);枥厩(饲马器和养马房);枥骥(俯首马槽上的骏马)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 马房 <span class="encs">[stable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气劳者，缓系之枥上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《齐民要术&middot;养牛马驴骡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古书上指栎树 <span class="encs">[oak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时见松枥皆十围。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《山石》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“枥”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/櫪">櫪</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              櫪·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：16</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6AEA.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】郞擊切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】郞狄切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音歷。【說文】櫪㯕，椑指也。【徐鍇曰】以木椑十指而縛之。【廣韻】櫪，馬櫪也。【晉&middot;桓溫風魏武詩】老驥伏櫪，志在千里。亦作歷。【前漢&middot;梅福傳】伏歷千駟。</p>
<p>又木名。【張衡&middot;南都賦】楓柙櫨櫪。【李善註】與櫟同。</p>
<p>又【韻會】引《韓愈&middot;山石》詩時見松櫪皆十圍。</p>
<p>又【正韻】蠶薄曰櫪。</p>
