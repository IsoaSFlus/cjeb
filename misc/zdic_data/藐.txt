<p>● <strong>藐</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">miǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧㄠˇ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">藐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>藐</strong> <span class="dicpy">miǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，貌声。本义:小，幼稚)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[petty;small]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孤女藐焉始孩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《寡妇赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:藐孤(弱小的孤儿);藐躬(孱弱的躯体);藐然(幼小的样子);藐藐(幼小的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 孤独的 <span class="encs">[lone]</span>。如:藐艰(流离孤单，遭遇艰苦)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;邈&rdquo;。远 <span class="encs">[far]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">藐蔓蔓之不可量兮，缥绵绵之不可纡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;悲回风》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>藐</strong> <span class="dicpy">miǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;眇&rdquo;。轻视 <span class="encs">[slight;despise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曰:&ldquo;以是藐诸孤辱在大夫，其若之何?&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">说大人则藐之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;尽心下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:藐法;藐视;藐玩(轻视玩忽)</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">藐</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              藐·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：14</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/85D0.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】亡沼切，音眇。【博雅】小也。【左傳&middot;僖九年】以是藐諸孤，辱在大夫。</p>
<p>又【孟子】說大人則藐之。【註】輕視貌。</p>
<p>又【莊子&middot;逍遙遊】藐姑射之山，有神人居焉。【註】遠也。</p>
<p>又【張衡&middot;西京賦】眳藐流盼。【註】藐，好視容也。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】漸也。</p>
<p>又【博雅】廣也。</p>
<p>又莫角切，音邈。【爾雅&middot;釋草】藐，茈草。【註】可以染紫，一名茈䓞。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】藐藐，美也。【詩&middot;大雅】寢廟旣成，旣成藐藐。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】誨爾諄諄，聽我藐藐。【傳】藐藐然不入也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶音密。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】長眉連娟，微睇綿藐。色授魂與，心愉于側。</p>
<p>又眉敎切，音貌。亦染草。</p>
