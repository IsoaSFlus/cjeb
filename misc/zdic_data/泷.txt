<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>泷</strong></p>
<p>瀧 <span class="dicpy">l&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 湍急的流水 <span class="encs">[rapids]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中夜前泷吼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏源</span><span class="smcs">《阳朔舟行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:泷船(在急流中行驶的船)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古水名 <span class="encs">[Long River]</span> 。即今山东省孝妇河</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">shuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>泷</strong></p>
<p>瀧 <span class="dicpy">Shuāng</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 水名。即今武水，又名武溪 <span class="encs">[Shuang River]</span>，源出湖南省临武县境，流入广东省，经乐昌县至韶关市，与浈水合为北江，又至三水，与西江相通</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7027.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】盧紅切【集韻】【韻會】盧東切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音籠。【說文】雨瀧瀧貌。</p>
<p>又瀧涷，沾漬也。【揚子&middot;方言】瀧涿，謂之霑<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23FD9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣿙</span>。</p>
<p>又水名，在梁鄒縣。【水經注】瀧水，卽古袁水也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】力鍾切【正韻】盧容切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音龍。義同。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】所江切【集韻】【韻會】疎江切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音雙。水名。【水經注】桂陽藍豪山，廣圓五百里，悉曲江縣界，巖嶺干天，交柯雲蔚，霾天晦景，謂之瀧中。</p>
<p>又州名。【廣輿記】廣東羅定州，梁曰瀧州，隋曰瀧水，今州有瀧水縣。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】呂江切【集韻】【韻會】閭江切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音䮾。奔湍也。南人名湍曰瀧。</p>
<p>又【集韻】盧貢切，音弄。瀧涷，溼也。義同。</p>
