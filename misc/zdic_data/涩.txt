                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>涩</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">澀</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sè.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄜˋ</span></p><ol><li>不光滑，不滑溜：轮轴发～。</li><li>一种使舌头感到不滑润不好受的滋味：苦～。这个柿子很～。</li><li>文字难读难懂，不流畅：晦～。生～。～讷。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">涩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>涩</strong></p><p>澀、濇、澁 <span class="dicpy">sè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，歰声。声符(歰 <span class="dicpy">sè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sè.mp3"></a></span> )为本字，象四只脚两两相抵，表示不滑，是会意字。本义:不滑溜)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[unsmooth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">濇，不滑也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">短而濇。——<span class="smcs">《素问·至真要大论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">濇，文歰，今作澀，不滑也。——<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">始余有佩刀甚良，至是涩不可拔。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《砥石赋·序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:涩噎(因不滑润而梗噎);涩道(刻有花纹的倾斜石砌);涩巴巴(形容不滑润);涩浪(古代宫墙基垒石凹入，作文纹状);涩脉(脉动往来不流利，虚细而迟)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 味不甘滑 <span class="encs">[puckery;astringent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">酸涩如棠梨。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《病桔》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:涩苦(又涩又苦);涩赤(干涩发红);涩重(干涩沉重)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 说话、写文章迟钝艰难、生硬、不流畅 <span class="encs">[obscure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生而舌短，涩于言论。——<span class="smcs">《宋书·南郡王(刘)义宣传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:生涩(言词、文字等不流畅);文句艰涩;涩笔(看似拟古不流利，实则庄茂而沉着有力的笔法);涩僻(文词生涩冷僻);涩辞(言语迟钝);涩体(艰涩难读、自成一格的文章体式)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 道路险阻;不通畅的 <span class="encs">[difficult]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冰泉冷涩弦凝绝，凝绝不通声暂歇。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:涩难(险阻);涩道(无级次的石砌陡斜小路);涩滞(险阻;不通畅)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 急<span class="encs">[fast]</span>。如:涩奈(涩耐。心意不安，羞渐，差涩)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
