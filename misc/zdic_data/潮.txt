						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>潮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cháo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cháo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄠˊ</span></p><ol><li>海水因为受了日月的引力而定时涨落的现象：～水。～汐。涨～。落～。～汛。～流。～涌。</li><li>像潮水那样汹涌起伏的：思～。热～。新～。～红。～热。心～澎湃。</li><li>湿：～气。返～。～湿。</li><li>方言，技术不高：手艺～。</li></ol>
