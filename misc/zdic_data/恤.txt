                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">恤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>恤</strong></p><p>卹、賉 <span class="dicpy">xù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，血声。本义:忧虑)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[worry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恤，忧也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勿恤其孚。——<span class="smcs">《易·泰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不印自恤。——<span class="smcs">《书·大诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">告而忧恤。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑柔》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不恤国事。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所图者国恤家仇，所期望者豪杰事功。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张煌言</span><span class="smcs">《复郎廷佐书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:恤功(担忧民众的事务);恤恤(忧虑的样子);恤民(忧虑人民的疾苦)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 体恤 <span class="encs">[favor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝廷竟无恤赠之典。——<span class="smcs">《隋唐演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恤病讨贰。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不知存恤。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:恤近忽远(体恤亲近的人而忽略疏远的人);恤金</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 怜悯 <span class="encs">[pity;sympathize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">司寇宜哀狱，台庭幸恤辜。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈佺</span>期<span class="smcs">《移禁司刑》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:恤矜(怜悯)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 周济 <span class="encs">[help out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以恤礼哀寇乱。——<span class="smcs">《周书·大宗伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胡以相恤。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">存恤贫无告者。——<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">隋</span>主闻知大怒，厚恤 <span class="diczx2">麦铁杖</span>等，杀监军 <span class="diczx2">刘士龙</span>，囚 <span class="diczx2">于仲文</span>。——<span class="smcs">《隋唐演义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:恤邻(救济近邻);恤贫(救济贫民);恤嫠(救济);恤病(救援有危难的人);恤患(济人于患难)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 安置 <span class="encs">[install]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不遂我遣，恤我九列。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 顾及;顾念 <span class="encs">[take into account]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">战胜</span><span class="diczx2">宜阳</span>，不恤 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>交，忿也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:恤刑(慎用刑法)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 抚养 <span class="encs">[nurse]</span></p><p><span
