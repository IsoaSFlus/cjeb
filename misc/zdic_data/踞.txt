                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">踞</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>踞</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>蹲，坐：龙蟠虎～（形容地势险要）。～坐。箕～（古人席地而坐把两腿像八字形分开）。</li>	<li>占据：～守。盘～（亦作&ldquo;盘据&rdquo;）。雄～一方。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> crouch, squat; sit, occupy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> hocken, kauern</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> s'accroupir,s'asseoir sur les talons</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">踞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>踞</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，居声。本义:蹲坐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[crouch;squat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">踞，蹲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，踞为居的俗字。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆踞转而鼓瑟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">释甲执冰而踞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">钟山</span>龙盘， <span class="diczx2">石头</span>虎踞。此帝王之宅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太平御览》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踞炉炭上(蹲在炭炉上。比喻处于危险的境地);踞蹲(蹲坐);踞伏(蹲伏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 伸开腿坐 <span class="encs">[crouch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">沛公</span>方踞床，使两女子洗足。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">箕踞见贾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;陆贾传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;箕踞，谓伸其两脚而坐。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踞门限;踞厕(张开两腿，坐在床边)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 盘据 <span class="encs">[occupy]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:踞盘(盘踞);踞蟠(踞盘);踞住(占据)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 倚，依靠 <span class="encs">[rely on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">据</span><span class="diczx2">渭</span>踞泾。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">兹郑</span>踞辕而歌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说右下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踞觚(倚着灶角)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>踞</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;倨&rdquo;。傲慢 <span class="encs">[haughty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃用妇人之言，何有踞肆于朝?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;叙传上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反赂遗而尚踞敖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;结和》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">捐贫贱之故旧，轻人士而踞傲也，骄人也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《抱朴子&middot;行品》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">单于</span>骄踞，自比 <span class="diczx2">冒顿</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;南匈奴传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踞肆(骄傲放肆);踞慢(傲慢不恭);踞嫚(踞慢);踞见(踞坐而见客。形容待人傲慢)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 形如锯齿的 <span class="encs">[sawlike]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:踞齿(锯牙);踞牙(形如锯齿之牙)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=足" target="_blank">足</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8E1E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>居御切，音據。【說文】蹲也。【大戴禮】獨處而踞。【註】踞，蹲也。</p><p>又【左傳&middot;襄二十四年】皆踞轉而鼓琴。【註】轉衣裝。【疏】踞，謂坐其上也。</p><p>又【前漢&middot;高帝紀】沛公方踞牀。【註】踞，反企也。</p><p>又【前漢&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>餘傳】高祖箕踞罵詈，甚慢之。【註】箕踞者，謂申兩脚，其形如箕。【類篇】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27FC3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧿃</span>。詳<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27FC3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧿃</span>字註。</p><p>又【集韻】斤於切，音居。義同。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">踞</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷二">卷二</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷二&amp;swbs=足">足</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8E1E.svg">                          <p>蹲也。从足居聲。居御切</p><hr class="swhr"><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5C45.svg" /><p>（居）蹲也。从尸古者，居从古。 <img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5C45_1.svg" />踞，俗居从足。九魚切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：居从古者，言法古也。〖注〗㞐、㝒，古文居。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5C45_1546.svg" /><span class="swbf">(居)</span><span class="ywyy">蹲也。</span><span class="swdz">足部曰蹲，居也。二字爲轉注。今足部改居爲踞，又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/216B6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡚶</span>添踞篆，訓云蹲也。總由不究許書條理，㒺知古形古義耳，立部竣下亦曰居也，亦同義而譌爲偓竣也。葢俗本之紛亂如此。說文有凥，有居。凥，處也。从尸得几而止，凡今人居處字古衹作凥處。居，蹲也。凡今人蹲踞字古衹作居。廣雅釋詁二凥也一條，釋詁三踞也一條。畫然分别。曹憲曰：按說文今居字乃箕居字，近之矣。但古人有坐、有跪、有蹲、有箕踞，跪與坐皆厀著於席，而跪聳其體，坐下其<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/267A0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦞠</span>，詩所謂啟處。四牡傳曰：啟，跪也。處，居也。四牡不遑啟處。采薇，出車作不遑啟居，居皆當作凥，許凥下云處也。正本毛傳引伸之爲凡凥處字也。若蹲則足底著地，而下其<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/267A0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦞠</span>，聳其厀曰蹲。其字亦作竣，原壤夷俟，謂蹲踞而待，不出迎也。若箕踞，則<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/267A0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦞠</span>著席而伸其腳於前，是曰箕踞。趙佗箕踞見陸賈，聞賈言乃蹷然起坐是也。箕踞爲大不敬。三代所無。居篆正謂蹲也。今字用蹲居字爲凥處字。而凥字廢矣。又別製踞字爲蹲居字，而居之本義廢矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从尸古聲。</span><span class="swdz">各本作古者居从古，乖於全書之例。淺人因下云俗居从足而竄改譌謬耳。今正。九魚切。五部。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5C45_1547.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27FC3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧿃</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">俗居从足。</span><span class="swdz">小徐本如此，不誤。大徐本篆作踞。非也。小徐云：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27FC3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧿃</span>一本从居。則小徐時固有兩本。</span></p>                          </div>
