                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>赤</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>红色，比朱色稍暗的颜色：～血。～字。</li>
	<li>真诚，忠诚：～诚（极其真诚）。～忱。～子（纯洁无暇的初生婴儿，古代亦指百姓）。～胆忠心。</li>
	<li>空无所有：～手空拳。～地千里。</li>
	<li>裸露：～脚（光脚）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> red; communist, 'red'; bare</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> nackt, unbekleidet ,rot, feuerrot, scharlachrot</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> nu,rouge</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">赤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赤</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文，从大(人)从火。人在火上，被烤得红红的。一说&ldquo;大火&rdquo;为赤。本义:火的颜色，即红色)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[red]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤，南方色也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤者，火色也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范&middot;五行传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其色赤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;风论》</span>。注:&ldquo;赤者，心色也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤刀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;顾命》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">武王</span>诛 <span class="diczx2">纣</span>时刀赤为饰。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">色赤椒好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《齐民要术&middot;种椒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">困于赤绂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;困卦》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;朱深曰赤。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日上，正赤如丹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span>。又如:赤刀(刀口赤色的宝刀);赤衣(红色衣服);赤丸(红色弹丸);赤日(红日;烈日);赤石(红色的石头);赤泥(呈红色的泥土)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 忠诚;真纯 <span class="encs">[loyal;pure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">推赤心于天下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赤心(忠心);赤衷(赤诚的心意);赤情(真诚的心意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 裸露 <span class="encs">[bare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当流赤足蹋涧石，水声激激风吹衣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《山石诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赤剥(光身子);赤巴巴(赤裸裸;毫无掩饰);赤脚大罗仙(传说中得道的李君);赤条精光(全身赤裸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 空;尽;一无所有 <span class="encs">[empty;extreme]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晋国大旱，赤地三年。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;十过》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当年老使君赤手降於菟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《送范纯粹守庆州》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赤淋淋(赤条条，一丝不挂);赤白白(一无所有);赤洒洒(形容无牵无挂)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赤</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古以赤为南方之色，后因以赤指南方 <span class="encs">[south]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赤帝(神话中的南方之神。代指汉高祖刘邦);赤方(指南方);赤位(指南方);赤天(南方的天)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 谶纬家谓汉以火德王，火色赤，后因以借指汉朝 <span class="encs">[Han Dynasty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赤德(指汉朝的气运)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> &ldquo;赤子&rdquo;的简称。指婴儿 <span class="encs">[baby]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赤襁(指婴孩);赤子苍头(泛指老人小孩。苍头:原指老仆，此指老人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指鲜血 <span class="encs">[blood]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赤津津(鲜血渗流的样子);赤臭(血污腐臭之气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 共产党 <span class="encs">[communist party]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这里现亦大讨其赤，中大学生被捕者有四十余人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《书信&middot;致李霁野》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>赤</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>除掉，诛灭 <span class="encs">[remove]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此祸水也!指日赤吾族矣!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;小翠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赤族(诛灭全族);赤诛(诛杀)</span></p>

<p>又【書&middot;康誥】若保赤子。【疏】子生而赤色，故言赤子。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;五行志】赤地千里。【註】空盡無物曰赤。</p>

<p>又六赤。【正字通】今骰子別名。【李洞&middot;贈李郞中詩】微黃喜兆莊周夢，六赤重新擲印成。</p>
<p>又姓。【呂氏春秋】赤冀作臼。【列仙傳】赤斧，巴人。</p>
<p>又【集韻】七迹切，音戚。【周禮&middot;秋官】赤犮氏。【註】赤犮，猶言捇拔也。【疏】捇拔，除去之也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶敕略切。【郭璞&middot;猙贊】章義之山，奇怪所宅。有獸似豹，厥色惟赤。宅，徒洛切。　【說文】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2418D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤆍</span>。</p>
