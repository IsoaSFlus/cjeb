                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">早</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>早</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，上面是<span class="yhcs">“日”</span>，下面是<span class="yhcs">“甲”</span>。<span class="yhcs">“甲”</span>的最早写法象<span class="yhcs">“十”</span>，指皮开裂，或东西破裂。<span class="yhcs">“早”</span>即天将破晓，太阳冲破黑暗而裂开涌出之意。本义:早晨)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[early morning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早，晨也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晨初为早。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·小星》</span>疏</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是谓老服。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早夜以思。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《原毁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早莺争暖树。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《钱塘湖春行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早暮咈吾耳。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早出暮归。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:清早(清晨);明早(明天早上);早出晚归(整日在外);起早贪黑;早天(早晨的天空);早角(早晨的号角声);早朝(早晨朝参);早膳(吃早饭);早霞(朝霞);早堂(旧时官府早晨坐衙治事称<span class="yhcs">“早堂”</span>);大清早</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>早</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 本来;已经 <span class="encs">[already]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">媳妇儿守寡又早三个年头，服孝将除了也。——</span><span class="diczx2">关汉卿</span><span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:早来(本是;已经);早为(已是)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 幸亏，幸而 <span class="encs">[luckily]</span>。如:早则(幸而;早该;早已)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比一定的时间靠前 <span class="encs">[early;beforehand;in advance]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晨往，寝门辟矣，盛服将朝。尚早，坐而假寐。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早与之绝。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早定大计。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:他早走了;早死了;早世(过早地死去;夭死)</p><p>◎ <strong>早</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p>何时;何日 <span class="encs">[what time]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山水朝来笑问人:“翁早归来也?——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《卜算子》</span></p><p>◎ <strong>早</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 在平生的早期;在年轻时 <span
