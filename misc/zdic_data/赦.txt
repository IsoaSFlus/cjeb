                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>赦</strong> <span class="dicpy">shè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，赤声。本义:宽免罪过)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[remit;pardon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赦，置也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“赦与捨音义同。非专谓赦罪也。后捨行而赦废。赦专为赦罪矣。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌三刺三宥三赦之法。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司刺》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赦止者免止之罪辞也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·昭公十八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先时者杀无赦。——<span class="smcs">《书·伪允征》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以赦过宥罪。——<span class="smcs">《易·象卦》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“谓放免。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣从其计，大王亦幸赦臣。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:赦书(宽免刑罚的文书);赦贷(宽免罪过);赦命(赦免的命令);赦宥(宽恕;赦免);赦恕(宽免，饶恕);赦过(赦免过错)。又如:赦释(免罪释放);赦放(免罪释放)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 宽容 <span class="encs">[pardon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先有司，赦小过，举贤才。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子路》</span></span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p>姓</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赦令">赦令</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赦免">赦免</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/赦罪">赦罪</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8D66.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24185.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤆅</span>【廣韻】始夜切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】式夜切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/286ED.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨛭</span>。【說文】置也，釋也。或从亦作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22F1C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢼜</span>。【廣韻】赦，宥也。【易·解卦】君子以赦過宥罪。【書·湯誓】罔有攸赦。</p><p>又【呂<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>】五<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>之疑有赦。【周禮·秋官·司刺】掌三刺、三宥、三赦之法。【註】赦，舍也。【前漢·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】三赦，一曰幼弱，二曰老眊，三曰惷愚。</p><p>又姓。【說苑】趙<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>子臣赦厥。</p><p>又通作舍。【前漢·朱博傳】常<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>不舍。</p><p>又【集韻】測革切。本作敕。擊馬也。</p><p>又【韻補】叶詩戈切。【韓愈·東方朔詩】羣仙急乃言，百犯庸不科。向觀睥睨處，事在不可赦。○按詩戈切，吳才老讀。</p><p>
