                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>褐</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄜˋ</span></p><ol><li>粗布或粗布衣服：短～。</li><li>黑黄色：～煤（煤的一种，多为褐色，除可作燃料外，还可以提炼汽油、煤油、焦油等。亦称“褐炭”）。～藻（藻类植物的一大类，褐色，是海底最主要的藻类，富含碘质和胶质，海带就是供食用的褐藻）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>褐</strong> <span class="dicpy">hè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，曷声。本义:用粗麻织成的袜子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hempen socks]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">褐，编枲袜。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“取未绩之麻，编之为足衣，如今草鞵之类。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指粗布或粗布衣;最早用葛、兽毛，后通常指大麻、兽毛的粗加工品，古时贫贱人穿 <span class="encs">[coarse cotton garments or coarse hempen garments]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无衣无褐，何以卒岁。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>笺:<span class="yhcs">“褐，毛布也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">从者衣褐。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:褐巾(用粗布缝成的头巾);褐衣(粗布衣。借指平民);褐衫(粗布衣);褐夫(穿粗布衣服的人。指贫贱的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 卑贱的人 <span class="encs">[the humilia ted]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旨酒一盛兮，余与褐之父睨之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十三年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“褐，寒贱之人。”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>褐</strong> <span class="dicpy">hè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>黄黑色 <span class="encs">[brown]</span>。如:褐煤;褐铁矿</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/褐色">褐色</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
