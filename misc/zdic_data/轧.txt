                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>轧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">軋</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>圆轴或轮子等压在物体上转：～场（<span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng </span>）。～轹（➊碾，滚压：➋排挤倾轧）。</li>
	<li>排挤：倾～（在一个组织中排挤不同派系的人）。</li>
	<li>象声词，形容机器开动时发出的声响：机声～～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>轧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">軋</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&aacute; ㄗㄨㄛˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用机器把钢坯压成一定形状的钢材：～钢。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>轧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">軋</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&aacute; ㄍㄚˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>挤；拥挤。</li>
	<li>结交：～朋友得小心。</li>
	<li>结算；核对：～账。</li>
	<li>观察；揣测：～苗头。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">轧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>轧</strong></p>
<p>軋 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，本义:辗压)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[roll]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轧，辗也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罪小者轧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;匈奴传》</span>。汉书注:&ldquo;辗辗其骨节。若今之压踝者。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鳃鳃然常恐天下之一合而轧己也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:把路面轧一下</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 排挤，倾轧 <span class="encs">[oust;push out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">名也者，相轧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;人间世》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 压倒，胜过 <span class="encs">[overwhelm;prevail over]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:轧窒(压制阻碍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 委曲 <span class="encs">[indirect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轧，辞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;宣公十九年》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓委曲也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:轧辞(委曲之辞)</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>轧</strong></p>
<p>軋 <span class="dicpy">g&aacute;</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 〈方〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 挤入;拥挤 <span class="encs">[press hard against each other;break into]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:轧不上去;车上很轧;轧米(方言。在拥挤的情况下争购食米)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 结交 <span class="encs">[take up with]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:轧朋友(结交朋友)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 核查 <span class="encs">[check]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:账轧不平;轧账(核算查对账目)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 象声词。如:轧拉(形容物体摩擦或受压力后而发出的声音)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;;zh&aacute;</span>(轧钢;轧辊)</p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"> <span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/轧轧">轧轧</a></span> </div>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>轧</strong></p>
<p>軋 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;象&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一种嘈杂声 <span class="encs">[ya]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:轧轧(车行的声音;摇桨的声音);轧然(形容物之间相磨擦发出的声音)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“轧”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/軋">軋</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=車" target="_blank">車</a>】
                              軋·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：1</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8ECB.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】於黠切【韻會】【正韻】乙黠切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音扎。【說文】車輾也。【六書故】車載重，蹍軋有聲也。</p>
<p>又<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>名。【前漢&middot;匈奴傳】罪小者軋，大者死。【註】謂輾轉轢其骨節，若今之厭踝也。</p>
<p>又勢相傾也。【莊子&middot;人閒世】名也者，相軋也。【唐書&middot;李宗閔傳】樹黨相磨軋。</p>
<p>又軋芴，緻密也。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】縝紛軋芴。【史記】作軋沕。</p>
<p>又軋忽，長遠之貌。【前漢&middot;禮樂志】淸風軋忽。</p>
<p>又軮軋，無涯際也。【揚雄&middot;甘泉賦】軮軋無垠。</p>
<p>又【集韻】膺眼切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/259D1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥧑</span>。義同。</p>
<p>又叶乙役切，音鬱。【張衡&middot;南都賦】流湍投濈，砏汃輣軋。長輸遠逝，漻淚淢汨。【註】輣軋，波相激之聲。</p>
