<span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:棉被;夹被;毛巾被;被窝(覆以棉被，容人安睡的地方);被卧(睡眠用的被子)</p><p><span i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 被覆;遮盖 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">被袗衣。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·尽心下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">被练三千。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三年》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“被是被覆衣着之名。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皋兰被径兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃祖</span><span class="diczx2">吾离</span>被苫盖。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十四年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凝霜被野草。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">阮籍</span><span class="smcs">《咏怀》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如被冰雪。——<span from]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>复击 <span class="diczx2">轲</span>，被八创。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其被轰击者，则断壁危楼。——</span><span
