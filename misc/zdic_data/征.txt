                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>征</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">➌-➐<span class="diczx3">徵</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>远行：长～。～途。～夫。～人。～衣。～帆（远行的船）。</li>
	<li>用武力制裁，讨伐：～服（用力制服）。～讨。～伐。～战（出征作战）。南～北战。</li>
	<li>召集：～兵。～募（招募兵士）。～集兵马。</li>
	<li>收集：～税。～粮。</li>
	<li>招请，寻求：～求。～稿。～婚。～聘（招聘）。～询（征求意见）。</li>
	<li>证明，证验：～引（引用，引证）。信而有～。</li>
	<li>表露出来的迹象：特～。～候。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">征</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>征</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从彳( <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span>)，正声。从彳，表示与行走有关。甲骨文从彳，从足。本义:到很远的地方去，远行)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[go on a journey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徴，正行也。从辵，正声。或从彳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">征，行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">壮于趾征凶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;大壮》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而月斯征。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小宛》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先王卜征五年。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十三年》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓巡狩征行。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肃肃宵征，夙夜在公。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;小星》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿为市鞍马，从此替爷征。&mdash;&mdash;古乐府<span class="smcs">《木兰词》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孤蓬万里征。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《送友人》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:征鼙(征战的军鼓);征衣(行旅在在外的御寒衣服);征客(游子。同征人);征鸟(远飞的鸟);征盖(远行的车);征夫(远行的人;出征的士兵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 征伐，发兵讨伐 <span class="encs">[go on a punitive expedition]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">征者，上伐下也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奉辞伐罪曰征。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;允征》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桓桓于征。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂&middot;泮水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一曰征。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太卜》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:&ldquo;谓征伐人也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挟天子以征四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:征诛(征讨诛戮);征阵(战阵);征讨(讨伐);征略(征战略地);征剿(犹征讨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 争夺，争取 <span class="encs">[contend for;fight for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不为征利。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 征收 <span class="encs">[levy]</span></p>
