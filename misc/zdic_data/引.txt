                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">引</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>引</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>拉，伸：～力。～颈。～而不发。～吭高歌。</li><li>领，招来：～见。～子。～言。～导。～荐。抛砖～玉。</li><li>拿来做证据、凭据或理由：～文。～用。援～。</li><li>退却：～退。～避。</li><li>旧时长度单位，一引等于十丈。</li><li>古代柩车的绳索：发～（出殡）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to pull, draw out, attract; to stretch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ableiten, ablenken  (V)​,führen, leiten, jmd. den Weg weisen  (V)​,ziehen, anspannen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> conduire,guider,attirer,provoquer,citer,emprunter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">引</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>引</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从弓丨。丨表示箭。箭在弦上，即将射发。本义:拉开弓)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[draw a bow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引，开弓也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子引而不发，跃如也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引弓而射。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说林训》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣为王引弓，虚发而下鸟。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·楚策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长城以后，引弓之国，受命单于。——<span class="smcs">《史记·匈奴列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丁壮者，引弦而战。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·人间训》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柄铁折叠环复…引之长丈许。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:引手(引箭手);引弓(拉弓);引满(拉弓至满)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拉，牵挽 <span class="encs">[draw]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人方引婴儿而欲投之江中。婴儿啼。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引</span><span class="diczx2">相如</span>去。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引车避匿。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">友人惭，下车引之。</span><span class="diczx2">元方</span>入门，不顾(引之)，拉他(表示好感)。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·方正》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:引日(拉转日轮);引手(拉回手);引曳(牵拉)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 延长;延续 <span class="encs">[lengthen;continue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子子孙孙，忽替引之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·楚茨》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:引日(拖延时日);引年(延长年寿);引时(迁延时日)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 伸着 <span class="encs">[stretch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引首顾曰。——</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使者持刀趋前，</span><span class="diczx2">济</span>引颈待之。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·卓行传·甄济》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:引首(伸长头颈，抬起头。形容殷切盼望);引手(伸手);引颈(伸长颈项);引臂(伸臂)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 牵连;攀供 <span class="encs">[involve in]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">果引</span><span class="diczx2">张胜</span>。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:引附(攀附);引逮(株连捕)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 引导 <span class="encs">[guide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引之表仪。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公六年》</span></span></p><p><span 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下" target="_blank">寅集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集下&amp;bs=弓" target="_blank">弓</a>】
                              引·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=4" target="_blank">4</a>　·部外筆画：1</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5F15.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㧈【唐韻】余忍切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】以忍切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蚓。【說文】開弓也。【徐鉉曰】象引弓之形。【周禮·冬官考工記】維體防之，引之中參。</p><p>又【廣雅】演也。【易·繫辭】引而伸之。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋詁】長也。【釋訓】子子孫孫引無極也。【書·梓材】引養引恬。</p><p>又相牽曰引。【禮·檀弓】喪服，兄弟之子，猶子也。蓋引而進之也。【註】牽引進之，同于己子。【史記·秦始皇紀】諸生轉相告引。</p><p>又【集韻】導也。【史記·韓長孺傳】奉引墮車，蹇。【註】爲天子導引而墮車，跛。</p><p>又卻也。【禮·玉藻】侍坐，則必退席。不退，則必引而去君之黨。【註】引，卻也。</p><p>又相薦達曰引。【史記·魏其侯傳】兩人相爲引重。【註】相薦達爲聲勢。【後漢·張皓王龔傳論】顯登者以貴塗易引。</p><p>又服氣法曰道引。【莊子·刻意篇】道引之士，養形之人。【史記·留侯世家】道引不食穀。</p><p>又治疾法有撟引。【史記·扁鵲傳】鑱石撟引。【註】謂爲按摩之法，夭撟引身，如熊顧鳥伸也。</p><p>又十丈爲引。【前漢·律歷志】其法用竹爲引，高一分，廣六分，長十丈。引者，信也。【註】信讀曰伸，言其長。</p><p>又【廣韻】【正韻】羊晉切【集韻】【韻會】羊進切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>蚓去聲。【集韻】牽牛綍也。【禮·檀弓】弔於葬者，必執引。【疏】引，柩車索也。</p><p>又【集韻】一曰曲引。【蔡邕·琴操】有思歸引。</p><p>又引古作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22A89.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢪉</span>。</p>
