                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">狂</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>狂</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kuáng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuáng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>本称狗发疯，后亦指人精神失常：～犬。疯～。癫～。发～。～人。</li><li>纵情任性或放荡骄恣的态度：轻～。～妄（极端自高自大）。～吠（狗狂叫，借指疯狂的叫嚣）。～乱。～野。～躁。～恣。～草（草书的一种，风格狂放无羁）。</li><li>气势猛烈，超出常度：～风。～飙。～热。力挽～澜。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> insane, mad; violent; wild</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> irre, verrückt ,Manie  (S)​,Kuang  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fou,furieux,arrogant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">狂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>狂</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:狗发疯)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。也指狗发疯 <span class="encs">[dog was mad]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狂，狾犬也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">值狂犬之暴怒，加楚害于微躯。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">阮籍</span><span class="smcs">《鸠赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:狂犬(凶猛的狗)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申指<span class="yhcs">“人的精神失常”</span>，<span class="yhcs">“疯癫”</span> <span class="encs">[mad;crazy;insane]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狂夫瞿瞿。——<span class="smcs">《诗·齐风·东方未明》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狂走者累日。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">漫卷诗书喜欲狂。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《闻官军收河南河北》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忽忽如狂。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佯狂不知所之者。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:狂死(因疯疾致死);狂易(精神失常);狂惑(精神错乱;疯癫);狂徒(狂人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 狂妄 <span class="encs">[too arrogant;stiff-necked]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幼而狂。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其蔽也狂。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“妄抵触人。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愈以是得狂名。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:狂谬(狂妄荒谬);狂且(行动轻狂之人);狂乖(狂妄狡猾);狂纵(狂妄放肆);狂轻(狂妄而轻浮);狂悖(狂妄背理;猖獗);狂且(行动轻狂);狂话(狂妄的话);狂狡(狂妄狡诈);狂戾(狂妄暴戾);狂惑(狂妄昏惑)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 狂放，任性放荡 <span
