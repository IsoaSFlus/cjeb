                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">连</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>连</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">連</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>相接：～日。～夜。～年。～亘（接连不断）。～襟（姐妹的丈夫之间的亲戚关系）。～载。～缀。～理。烽火～天。～篇累（<span class="dicpy">lěi </span>）牍。</li>
	<li>带，加上：～带。～坐（一个人犯法，他的家属、亲族、邻居连带受处罚）。</li>
	<li>就是，既使，甚至于：～我都不信。</li>
	<li>联合：外～东吴。</li>
	<li>军队的编制单位，&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">排</span>&rdquo;的上一级：～长。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/琏">琏</a>&rdquo;，古代宗庙盛黍稷的器具。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/链">链</a>&rdquo;，铅矿。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">连</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>连</strong></p>
<p>連 <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，从车。本义:人拉的车)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[man-drawn carriage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">連，负车也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;连即古文輦也。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">连车组輓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;故书巾车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与其輂连。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;乡师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行服连轺辇者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管者&middot;海王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民相连而从之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;让王》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>君、 <span class="diczx2">房</span>君、 <span class="diczx2">司马</span>君皆云:&ldquo;读为辇。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[<span class="diczx2">太后</span>]</span>曰:&lsquo;老妇持连而瞏(還)。&rsquo;&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">马王堆</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>墓帛书<span class="smcs">《战国纵横家书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姻亲关系 <span class="encs">[affinity;relationship by marriage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[<span class="diczx2">吕嘉</span>]</span>及 <span class="diczx2">苍梧</span>(地名) <span class="diczx2">秦王</span>有连。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;南越列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:连亲(连姻);连昏(连婚。本有姻亲关系而又结亲。即俗称亲上加亲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 由若干个排组成的军队一级组织，通常隶属于营 <span class="encs">[company]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:连长;连指导员</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 连词的简称 <span class="encs">[conjunction]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Lian prefecture]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 在今广东连县一带</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 在今四川筠连县</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通&ldquo;兰&rdquo;。兰草 <span class="encs">[fragrant thoroughwort (Eupatorium fortunei )]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薜荔白芷、蘼芜椒连。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;地员》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通&ldquo;链&rdquo;。铅 <span class="encs">[lead ( plumbum )]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">长沙</span>出边锡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 通&ldquo;辇&rdquo;。用人拉的车 <span class="encs">[a mandrawn carrriage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">往蹇来连。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;蹇》</span>。注:&ldquo;连，辇也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 同时获得 <span class="encs">[gain simultaneous]</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=辵" target="_blank">辵</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9023.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】力延切【集韻】【韻會】陵延切【正韻】靈年切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音漣。【說文】員連也。【玉篇】合也，及也。【廣韻】續也，還也。【集韻】屬也。【正韻】接也。【詩&middot;大雅】執訊連連。【朱傳】屬續貌。【禮&middot;王制】十國爲連，連有帥。【齊語】四里爲連，十連爲鄕。【後漢&middot;韓康傳】連徵不至。</p>
<p>又姻親爲連。【史記&middot;尉佗傳】及蒼梧秦王有連。【註】有連者，連姻也。</p>
<p>又黏鳥曰連。【淮南子&middot;覽㝠訓】連鳥于百仞之上。</p>
<p>又連尹、連敖，皆楚官名。【左傳&middot;襄十五年】屈蕩爲連尹。【史記&middot;淮隂侯傳連敖註】楚司馬官也。</p>
<p>又連山，易名。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大卜】掌三易之法，一曰連山。【註】其卦以純艮爲首，山上山下，是名連山。</p>
<p>又鉛之未鍊者曰連。【史記&middot;貨殖傳】長沙出連錫。</p>
<p>又祈連，山名。【前漢&middot;霍去病傳】出北地，至祈連山。【註】卽天山也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】州名。漢屬桂陽郡，隋置連州。</p>
<p>又姓。【左傳&middot;莊八年】齊有連稱。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>力展切，音斂。難也。【易&middot;蹇卦】往蹇來連。【註】連，亦難也。</p>
<p>又遲久之意。</p>
<p>又【集韻】連彥切，音摙。【禮&middot;玉藻】連用湯。【註】連，猶釋也。以湯洗足垢，乾潔其體也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】郞旰切，音爛。連石，山名。【淮南子&middot;天文訓】日至于連石。</p>
<p>又叶離珍切，音鄰。【陸雲&middot;夏府君誄】祈祈縉紳，泣涕留連。故作斯誄，著之不泯。泯音民。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9023_6819.svg" /><span class="swbf">(連)</span><span class="ywyy">負車也。</span><span class="swdz">負車，各本作貟連，今正。連卽古文輦也。周禮：鄉師輂輦。故書輦作連，大鄭讀爲輦。巾車連車，本亦作輦車。管子海王：服連軺輂。立政，㓝餘戮民。不敢服絻，不敢畜連。負車者，人輓車而行，車在後如負也。字从辵車會意，猶輦从㚘車會意也。人與車相屬不絕，故引伸爲連屬字。耳部曰：聯，連也。大宰注曰：古書連作聯。然則聯連爲古今字，連輦爲古今字。假連爲聯，乃專用輦爲連。大鄭當云連今之輦字，而云讀爲輦者，以今字易古字，令學者易曉也。許不於車部曰連古文輦而入之辵部者，小篆連與輦殊用。故云聯連也者，今義也。云連負車也者，古義也。</span><span class="ywyy">从辵車。會意。</span><span class="swdz">依韵會訂。力延切。古力展切。十四部。</span></p>                          </div>
