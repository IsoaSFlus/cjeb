                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">齿</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>齿</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">齒</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄔˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人和动物嘴里咀嚼食物的器官（通常称&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">牙</span>&rdquo;）：牙～。～腔。～髓。～龈。～冷（笑必开口，笑的时间长了，牙齿就会感到冷。因谓讥笑于人，如&ldquo;令人～～&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>排列像牙齿形状的东西：～轮。锯～。梳子～儿。</li>
	<li>因幼马每岁生一齿，故以齿计算牛马的岁数，亦指人的年龄：马～徒增（旧时自谦年长无能）。</li>
	<li>并列：不～（不能同列或不与同列，表示鄙弃）。</li>
	<li>谈到，提及：～及。不足～数。</li>
	<li>触：～剑（触剑受刀，指被杀或自刎）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">齿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>齿</strong></p>
<p>齒 <span class="dicpy">chǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文，象嘴里的牙齿，象形字。战国文字在上面加了个声符&ldquo;止&rdquo;，成为形声字。小篆同。本义:牙)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tooth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男以八月而生齿，八岁而龀，女七月生齿，七岁而龀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;易本命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">发堕齿槁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;上古天真论》</span>。注:&ldquo;齿为骨余。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齿吻(齿及唇);齿颊(牙齿与脸颊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 排列如齿状的物品 <span class="encs">[tooth-like thing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齿革羽毛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。传:&ldquo;象牙也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锯齿;梳齿;羊齿(一种植物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指年龄 <span class="encs">[age]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齿路马有诛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;数年也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者谓年龄，齿亦龄也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;文王世子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">退而甘食其土之有，以尽吾齿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齿序(年龄的大小顺序);齿力(年龄和体力)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>齿</strong></p>
<p>齒 <span class="dicpy">chǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 并列，次列 <span class="encs">[juxtapose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不敢与诸任齿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公十一年》</span>。注:&ldquo;列也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百官以此相齿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齿列(与人同等并列);齿遇(以同等相对待);齿班(并列)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 谈说，重视 <span class="encs">[utter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:齿及(说及，挂齿);齿牙余论(言词之力;口舌之劳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 录用，收纳 <span class="encs">[employ]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">终身不齿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹录也。&rdquo;按，齿有行列者。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齿召(予以录用征召);齿用(录用);齿旧(录用旧臣)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">吾谋适合意，几亦齿奸锋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span>文</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">齒</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“齿”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/齒">齒</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集下" target="_blank">亥集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集下&amp;bs=齒" target="_blank">齒</a>】
                              齒·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9F52.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20692.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠚒</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20512.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠔒</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26952.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦥒</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2396B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣥫</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2696A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦥪</span>【唐韻】【廣韻】昌里切【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】醜止切【正韻】昌止切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䊼。【說文】口齗骨也。象口齒之形。牙，牡齒也。【字彙】上曰齒。下曰牙。【顏師古&middot;急就篇註】齒者總謂口中之骨，主齰齧者也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;小司<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨥</span>之職】自生齒以上，登於天府。【鄭註】人生齒而體備，男八月，女七月而生齒。</p>
<p>又【釋名】齒，始也。少長別，始乎此也。以齒食多者長也，食少者幼也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】齯齒，壽也。註：齒墮更生細者。通作兒。【詩&middot;魯頌】黃髮兒齒。</p>
<p>又年也。</p>
<p>又列也。【左傳&middot;隱十一年】寡人若朝於薛，不敢與諸任齒。【杜註】齒，列也。【疏】禮記文王世子曰：古者謂年齡，齒亦齡也。然則齒是年之別名，人以年齒相次列，以爵位用次列，亦名爲齒，故云齒列也。【左傳&middot;昭元年】使后子與子干齒。【杜註】以年齒高下而坐。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】齒路馬有誅。【疏】論量君馬歲數，亦被責罰，皆廣敬也。</p>
<p>又類也。【管子&middot;弟子職】同嗛以齒。【註】齒，類也。謂食盡則以其所盡之類而進。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】錄也。</p>
<p>又金齒，地名。</p>
<p>又魚齒，山名。在潁川郡。見【後漢&middot;郡國志】。</p>
<p>又鑿齒，獸名。【揚雄&middot;長楊賦】鑿齒之徒。【註】獸齒似鑿，能食人。</p>
<p>又羊齒，草名。【爾雅&middot;釋草】緜馬羊齒。【郭註】草細葉，葉羅生而毛，似羊齒，今江東呼爲雁齒。</p>
<p>又黑齒，外國姓。</p>
<p>又鑿齒，人名。【山海經】羿與鑿齒戰於壽華之野。【郭註】鑿齒，人齒如鑿，長五六寸，因以爲名。</p>
<p>又習鑿齒，晉人名。</p>
<p>又【集韻】稱拯切，稱上聲。齒也。</p>
