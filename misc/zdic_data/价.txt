                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>价</strong></p>
<p>價 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，贾声。本义:价格，价值)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[price]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">经千载以待价兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;嵇康&middot;琴赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国贫而用不足，请以平价取之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;轻重》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以求重价。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:价贯(价关。以铜钱计算的价钱);价一不择主(价钱一样，卖得谁都可以);价比(是一种物品计算期价格与其基期价格之比);价色(价格)</span></p>
