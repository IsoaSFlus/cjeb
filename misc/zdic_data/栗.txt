                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">栗</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>栗</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>落叶乔木，果实为坚果，称&ldquo;栗子&rdquo;，味甜，可食：～色。火中取～。</li>
	<li>发抖，因害怕或寒冷肢体颤动：战～。</li>
	<li>坚实：&ldquo;缜密以～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> chestnut tree, chestnuts; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Frucht der Kastanie  (S, Bio)​,Kastanie  [ lat. Castanea ]  (S, Bio)​,Maronen, Maroni [ Schweiz ]  (S, Bio)​,Li  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> châtaigne,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">栗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>栗</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文象结了果实的栗树形。本义:木名。果实也称栗，可食)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一种落叶乔木，果实叫栗子，果仁味甜，可以吃。木材坚实，供建筑和制器具用;树皮可供鞣皮及染色用;叶子可喂柞蚕 <span class="encs">[Chinese chestnut;hairy chestnut]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">栗，栗木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树之榛栗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鄘风&middot;定之方中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">练主用栗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;文公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">哀公</span>同社于 <span class="diczx2">宰我</span>， <span class="diczx2">宰</span><span class="diczx2">我</span>对曰:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">夏后氏</span>以松， <span class="diczx2">殷</span>人以柏， <span class="diczx2">周</span>人以栗。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:栗主(用栗木做成的神牌位);栗跗(栗花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 任一种栗属的乔木或灌木结的可食的甜味坚果 <span class="encs">[chestnut]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">果至于梨栗枣之类。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:栗黄(即栗子。因其肉色鲜黄而得名);栗爆(栗子在火炉中烘煨而爆裂);栗犊(小牛。因小牛初生时角如栗而得名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>栗</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 谷实饱满 <span class="encs">[plump-eared]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">实坚实好，实颖实栗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;民劳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡苗自函活以至颖栗，早者食水三斗，晚者食水五斗，失水即枯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《天工开物》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 坚实 <span class="encs">[solid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缜密以栗，知也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;聘义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:栗理(坚实有文理);栗然(坚实密致貌);栗密(谨严，缜密);栗温(质地密致而润泽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 庄敬，严肃 <span class="encs">[respectful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命汝典乐，教胄子，直而温，宽而栗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:栗斯(谨慎恭敬的样子)</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>栗</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄌㄧˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>l&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1906.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">植物名。殼斗科板栗屬，落葉喬木。葉作披針形，有鋸齒，互生。夏日開花，花小，雌雄同株。果實為堅果，在囊狀之殼斗內。其殼斗全面帶刺，成熟後，則殼裂散出果實。種子可供食用。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如漢代有栗融。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">畏懼。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;八佾》：「哀公問社於宰我&hellip;&hellip;，曰使民戰<b>栗</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷六六&middot;楊敞傳》：「眾毀所歸，不寒而<b>栗</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「慄」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">堅固。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;聘義》：「夫昔者君子比德於玉焉，溫潤而澤，仁也；縝密以<b>栗</b>，知也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;鄭玄&middot;注：「<b>栗</b>，堅貌。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">敬謹、戒慎。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;舜典》：「直而溫，寬而<b>栗</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;孔穎達&middot;正義：「謂矜莊嚴。<b>栗</b>者，謹敬也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">威嚴。</span><span class="gc_yy">周&middot;司馬穰苴《司馬法&middot;嚴位》：「凡戰之道，位欲嚴，政欲<b>栗</b>。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
