						<p>● <strong>盖</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">蓋</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>有遮蔽作用的东西：～子。锅～。瓶～。膝～。天灵～。</li>
	<li>伞：雨～。</li>
	<li>由上往下覆，遮掩：覆～。遮～。掩～。～浇饭。</li>
	<li>压倒，超过：～世无双。</li>
	<li>方言，超出一般地好：这本书真叫～！</li>
	<li>用印，打上：～章。～戳子。</li>
	<li>造（房子）：～楼。翻～。</li>
	<li>文言虚词（①发语词，如&ldquo;～闻&rdquo;；②表大概如此，如&ldquo;～近之矣&rdquo;；③连词，表示原因，如&ldquo;有所不知，～未学也&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>盖</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">蓋</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gě  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gě.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄜˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古地名，在今中国山东省沂水县西北。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>盖</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">蓋</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄜˊ</span></p>

      <div class="yczsl-description">
			<div class="gscdl">乃悟前狼假寐，<span>盖</span>以诱敌。</div><div class="gscdr">蒲松龄《狼三则·其二》</div><div class="gscdl"><span>盖</span>石性坚重，沙性松浮，水不能冲石，其反激之力，必于石下迎水处啮沙为坎穴，渐激渐深，至石之半，石必倒掷坎穴中。</div><div class="gscdr">清·纪昀 《河中石兽》</div><div class="gscdl">庭下如积水空明，水中藻荇交横，<span>盖</span>竹柏影也。</div><div class="gscdr">宋·苏轼 《记承天寺夜游》</div><div class="gscdl">尝贻余核舟一，<span>盖</span>大苏泛赤壁云。</div><div class="gscdr">明·魏学洢 《核舟记》</div><div class="gscdl"><span>盖</span>简桃核修狭者为之。</div><div class="gscdr">明·魏学洢 《核舟记》</div><div class="gscdl">以中有足乐者，不知口体之奉不若人也。<span>盖</span>余之勤且艰若此。</div><div class="gscdr">明·宋濂 《送东阳马生序》</div>      </div>
