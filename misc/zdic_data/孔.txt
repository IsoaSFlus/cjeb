                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">孔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>孔</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄥˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>小洞，窟窿：～穴。～眼。～洞。～方兄（指钱，因旧时的铜钱有方形的孔，恢谐含鄙意）。</li>
	<li>很：～急。～武有力。</li>
	<li>量词，用于窑洞：一～土窑。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> opening, hole, orifice; great</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bohrung, Loch  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> trou,ouverture</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">孔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>孔</strong> <span class="dicpy">kǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>(象形。金文字形，象小儿食乳之形。婴儿吃奶容易过量，因以表示过甚之意。本义:甚，很) 同本义 <span class="encs">[very]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其新孔嘉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;东山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孔亟(甚急);孔多(很多);孔明(很完备;很洁净;很鲜明);孔疚(很痛苦);孔虔(非常虔诚);孔圣(很圣明)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>孔</strong> <span class="dicpy">kǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 小窟窿 <span class="encs">[hole;opening;aperture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孔，窍也，空也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孔德之容。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>二十一章</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="diczx2">王</span>注:&ldquo;孔，空也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">反古之道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓晓一孔之人。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自钱孔入。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:钥匙孔;鼻孔;孔口(洞口);孔窍(洞孔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 孔夫子的省称 <span class="encs">[Confucius]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:孔老(孔子与老子);孔林(孔子及其后裔的墓园);孔周(孔子与周公的并称);孔府(孔子后裔直系子孙&ldquo;衍圣公&rdquo;的住宅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 孔雀的省称 <span class="encs">[peafowl]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孔翠(翠鸟)群翔，犀象竞驰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孔盖(以孔雀的羽毛装饰的车盖);孔翠(孔雀和翠鸟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓。</p>
<p>◎ <strong>孔</strong> <span class="dicpy">kǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 嘉，美 <span class="encs">[good; fine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故古人名嘉，字子孔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">许慎</span><span class="smcs">《说文解字》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通达 <span class="encs">[open;clear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辟在西南，不当孔道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;西域传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[great]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:孔硕(硕大);孔德(大德)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>孔</strong> <span class="dicpy">kǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>洞穴、窑洞、油井、石桥等的量名。如:一孔石桥;一孔油井</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5B54.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2193F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡤿</span>【唐韻】【正韻】康董切【集韻】【韻會】苦動切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>空上聲。【說文】通也。从乙从子。乙，請子之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>鳥也。乙至而得子，嘉美之也。故古人名嘉字子孔。</p>
<p>又甚也。【詩&middot;小雅】德音孔昭。【書&middot;禹貢】九江孔殷。【註】殷正也。言水道甚得其正也。</p>
<p>又穴也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】孔，閒也。【疏】謂閒<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻶</span>也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;圅人】眡其鑽空而惌。【史記&middot;舜本紀】爲匿空旁出。【註】空卽孔。</p>
<p>又空也。通也。【老子&middot;道德經】孔德之容，惟道是從。【註】謂空虛能容也。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;太<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>經】孔道夷如。【註】孔道，通道也。</p>
<p>又鳥名。【山海經】南方多孔烏。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】殷湯之後。本自帝嚳<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/216B1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡚱</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>狄吞乙卵而生契，賜姓子氏。至成湯，以其祖吞乙卵而生，故名履，字太乙。後代以子加乙，始爲孔氏。至宋孔父嘉遭華父督之難，其子奔魯，故孔子生於魯。</p>
