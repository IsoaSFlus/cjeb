                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">帏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>帏</strong></p><p>幃 <span class="dicpy">wéi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wéi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从巾，韦声。<span class="yhcs">“巾”</span>与丝织品有关。本义:佩带的香囊)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fragrant bag]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帏，囊也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苏粪壤以充帏兮，谓申椒其不芳。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 帐子;幔幕 <span class="encs">[curtain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帏，一说单帐也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帷屏无彷彿。——<span class="smcs">《文选·潘岳·悼亡诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如素车白马帷盖之张。——<span class="smcs">《文选·枚乘·七发》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:帏盖(帏幕和篷盖);帏幔(帐幕);帏箔，帏薄(帐幕和帘子);帏帘(帐幕);帏幌(帷幌);帏帐(惟帐);帏室(张挂帷幔的内室);帏屏(亦指寝息之所);帏帽(帷帽);帏幄(帐幔，帐幕;军队的帐幕，借指指挥中心;内庭，内室)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 裙正面的一幅 <span class="encs">[skirt's front]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王使妇人不帏而噪之。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">韦昭</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“裳正幅曰帏。”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>帏</strong></p><p>幃 <span class="dicpy">wéi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wéi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>遮蔽 <span class="encs">[cover]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其人无衣与裳，惟以布一幅束其阴，上体以被一方，帏而裹之。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=巾">巾</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5E43.svg">                          <p>囊也。从巾韋聲。許歸切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">囊也。</span>離騷。蘇糞壤以充幃。王逸曰。幃謂之幐。幐、香囊也。按凡囊皆曰幃、曰幐。王依文爲說則謂之香囊耳。或曰爾雅婦人之褘亦作幃。是許所云幃也。今按此與爾雅之褘無渉。釋器曰。婦人之褘謂之縭。縭、緌也。郭云。卽今之香纓。女子旣嫁之所著。示繫屬於人。義見禮記。攷士昏禮注曰。婦人十五許嫁。筓而禮之。因著纓。明有繫也。葢以五采爲之。其制未聞。內則。婦事舅姑衿纓。注曰。婦人有纓。示繫屬也。詩。親結其縭。毛云。縭、婦人之褘也。母戒女施衿結帨。孫炎釋爾雅婦人之褘云。帨巾也。禮之纓必以采繩。詩、爾雅之褘縭乃帨巾。其不相涉明甚。景純注非。許以囊釋幃。亦<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣃔</span>非釋器及毛詩之褘也。<span class="jieshi">从巾。韋聲。</span>許歸切。十五部。                          </div>
