                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>署</strong> <span class="dicpy">shǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从网，者声。部署任务，各有系统，故从网。本义:布置;部署)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[arrange;dispose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">署，都署有所系属。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">署，置也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">署位之表也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">部署诸将。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:部署(安排;布置);署用(署理任用);署事(处理事务);署置(设置官署任用官吏)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 签名，签署 <span class="encs">[sign]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吏呼名至</span><span class="diczx2">史</span>公，公瞿然注视，呈卷，即面署第一。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">冯湲</span>署曰<span class="yhcs">“能”</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:署笺(署名于章奏);署券(签署券约);署押(在文书上签名，押字);署榜(开列姓名，张榜示人);署议(上书议事。因上书须署名，故称);署衔(加书官职品爵于姓名上)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 代理、暂任或试充官职 <span class="encs">[act as deputy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">署</span><span class="diczx2">南平</span>教谕。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太守</span><span class="diczx2">宗资</span>先闻其名，请署功曹，委任政事。—— <span class="diczx2">范晔</span><span class="smcs">《后汉书·范雎传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:署印(代理官职);署任(暂摄原任);署缺(本官出缺，由他官暂代其职);署事(官员任职，以一年为期的叫署事;代理管事);署院(代理抚台);署印老爷(代理官员)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>署</strong> <span class="dicpy">shǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 公署;官署。办理公务的机关 <span class="encs">[official bureau]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">署，官舍曰署。——<span class="smcs">《篇海类篇》</span>。署使用于战国时期，常称官署，公署。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫署，所以朝夕虞君命也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宣帝即时召武待诏宦者署。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·苏武传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">学士入署，常视日影为候。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·李程传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:署府(泛指官署)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
