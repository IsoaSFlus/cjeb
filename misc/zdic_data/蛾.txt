<span class="encs">[moth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禽兽虫蛾。——<span class="smcs">《列子·黄帝》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朱蛾其状如蛾。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·海内北经》</span></span></p><p><span
