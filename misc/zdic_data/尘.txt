                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尘</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>尘</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">塵</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>飞扬的灰土：～土。～埃。～垢。～芥（尘土和小草，喻轻微的事物）。粉～。烟～。甚嚣～上。望～莫及。</li>
	<li>佛家、道家指人间：红～。～世。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>dust, dirt, ashes, cinders</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Erde ,Staub</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> poussière,saleté,terre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尘</strong></p>
<p>塵 <span class="dicpy">ch&eacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&eacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>从三&ldquo;鹿&rdquo;，从&ldquo;土&rdquo;，表示鹿群行扬起尘土的意思。楷书简去重迭的部分，只保留一个&ldquo;鹿&rdquo;。现行简化字&ldquo;尘&rdquo;，也是一个从&ldquo;小&rdquo;从&ldquo;土&rdquo;的会意字。本义:尘土)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dust]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">湫隘嚣尘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三年》</span>。注:&ldquo;土也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尘埃也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而游于尘垢之外。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;齐物论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朱尘筵些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">满面尘灰烟火色。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">渭城</span>朝雨浥轻尘。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《送元二使安西》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:浮尘;降尘;灰尘(尘土);一尘不染;尘封(盖满灰尘);尘涓(尘埃与一滴水。比喻细微);尘芥(尘土与草芥。比喻无价值的东西);尘沙(因风飞扬的灰沙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 喻庸俗肮脏或指庸俗肮脏的事物 <span class="encs">[dirty thing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春不得避风尘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惊尘蔽天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尘陋(凡俗浅陋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 尘世，人世间，俗间。又称:尘间;尘浊;尘市;尘域;尘堀;尘区;尘海 <span class="encs">[the world]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:红尘(旧称繁华的社会，也泛指人世间);尘凡(凡尘;尘世)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 踪迹;业迹 <span class="encs">[trace]</span>。</p>
