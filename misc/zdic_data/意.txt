                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>意</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>心思：～思。～见。～义。～味。～念。～志（为了达到既定目的而自觉努力的心理状态）。注～。同～。～在笔先。～在言外。</li>    <li>心愿，愿望：～愿。愿～。～向。～图。～皆。好～。“醉翁之～不在酒”。</li>    <li>人或事物流露的情态：春～。诗～。惬～。情～。～境。</li>    <li>料想，猜想：～料。～想。～外。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">意</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>意</strong> <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从心从音。本义:心志。心意)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[meaning;idea]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">意，志也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心之所谓意。——<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露·循天之道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今者</span><span class="diczx2">项庄</span>拔剑舞，其意常在 <span class="diczx2">沛公</span>也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">通其意。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中有真意。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《饮酒》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不快我意。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无意则已。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">蔡伦</span>立意造纸。——<span class="smcs">《齐民要术》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醉翁之意不在酒。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其意深长。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:文章大意;词不达意。亦指念头，想法。如:意投道合(心意投合，志向一致);意故(意思，缘故);意定(认定);意头(心意;想法);意马(意念的马。比喻意念像野马般奔驰，难以驾驭)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 意志;愿望。亦引申为志向 <span class="encs">[desire;intention;will]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明君在上，便僻不能食其意。——<span class="smcs">《管子·君臣下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:意缓心劳(冀求不执着;三心二意);意足心满(非常满足);意树(喻意念。佛教认为人的一切善果、恶果都由意念所生);意宪(意志，意思);意望(愿望，希望)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 胸怀;内心 <span class="encs">[heart]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">除尔贪图，祗慎意业。——<span class="smcs">《云门颂》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:意业(佛教语。指内心的贪、淫、痴等心理行为);意气超迈(器宇轩昂，抱负远大);意概(气概，胸怀);意会(内心领会)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 情意;感情 <span class="encs">[affection;emotion]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">醉翁之意不在酒，在乎山水之间也。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久之，目似瞑，意暇甚。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span>
