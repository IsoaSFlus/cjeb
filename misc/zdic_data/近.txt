                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">近</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>近</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jìn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>距离短，与“<span class="diczx3">远</span>”相对：接～。附～。靠～。～路。～景。舍～求远。～在眉睫。～朱者赤，～墨者黑。～水楼台。</li><li>现在以前不久的时间：～况。～来。～代。～岁。～闻。～照。～体诗。</li><li>亲密：亲～。～亲。～臣。平易～人。</li><li>差别小，差不多：接～。相～。</li><li>浅显：言～旨远。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> near, close; approach; intimate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> nahe; nahebei  (Adj)​,nahezu  (Adj)​,Jin  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> proche,approximativement,intime</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">近</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>近</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，表示与行走有关，斤声。本义:走近，接近)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[approach;be close to;draw near]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">近，附也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为其近于道也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·祭义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小大近丧。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·荡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾入关，秋毫不敢有所近。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稍出近之。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">近出前后。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稍近。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时敌军已近寨。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:逼近(靠近;接近);挨近;近傍(接近;靠近);近晚(傍晚);近火(靠近火)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申追求，希求 <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为近利市三倍。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:近名(追求名誉);近利(逐利)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 逼近 <span class="encs">[press on towards]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二与四同功而异位，其善不同。二多誉，四多惧，近也。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞下》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">韩康伯</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“位逼于君，故多惧也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 得宠 <span class="encs">[find favor with sb.]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">襄子</span>必近 <span class="diczx2">幸子</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有七孺子皆近。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽有贵戚近习。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:近上(接近上司);近上的(接近上层的;等级高的);近珰(皇帝宠信的太监);近爱(帝王所亲近宠爱)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 位于边缘;濒于 <span class="encs">[verge on]</span>。如:已近老年;近天(天边，极远之处);近正(接近正确;接近标准);近边(接近边疆)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 知晓 <span class="encs">[know]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慈亲不能传于子，忠臣不能入于君，唯有其材者为近之。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎
