                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">于</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>于</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>介词（➊在，如&ldquo;生～北京&rdquo;；➋到，如&ldquo;荣誉归～老师&rdquo;；➌对，如&ldquo;勤～学习&rdquo;；➍向，如&ldquo;出～自愿&rdquo;；➎给，如&ldquo;问道～盲&rdquo;；➏自，从，如&ldquo;取之～民&rdquo;；➐表比较，如&ldquo;重～泰山&rdquo;；➑表被动，如&ldquo;限～水平&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>后缀（➊在形容词后，如&ldquo;疏～防范&rdquo;；➋在动词后，如&ldquo;属～未来）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> in, on, at; go to; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> an, als, aus, bei, in, von, zu</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dans,à,de,par,que,(nom de famille Yu)​,en,dans,pour,par</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">于</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>于</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，表示气出受阻而仍越过。本义:超过)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 往;去 <span class="encs">[go]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">之子于归，宜其室家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;桃夭》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:&ldquo;于，往也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予惟以尔庶邦于伐</span><span class="diczx2">殷</span>，逋播臣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大诰》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 取 <span class="encs">[take]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昼尔于茅，宵尔索陶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 如;好像 <span class="encs">[look]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《易》</span>曰:&ldquo;介于石，不终日，贞吉。&rdquo;介如石焉，宁用终日，断可识矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:于何(如何)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>于</strong></p>
<p>於 <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈介〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引进动作、行为的时间、处所，意义相当于&ldquo;在&rdquo;、&ldquo;到&rdquo;或&ldquo;在&hellip;方面(上、中)&rdquo; <span class="encs">[in， at， etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">捐金于野。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">成于机杼。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射于家圃。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">葫芦置于地。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">力足以至焉，于人为可讥，而在己为有悔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于时冰皮始解，波色乍明。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">越长城之限，至于</span><span class="diczx2">泰安</span>。(于:介词，到。)&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:第一次世界大战爆发于1914年;于以(在何处);于兹(在此);于时(在此)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引进动作、行为的对象，相当于&ldquo;向&rdquo;、&ldquo;对&rdquo;、&ldquo;对于&rdquo; <span class="encs">[for， etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">告之于帝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何有于我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于其身也，则耻师焉，惑矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言于</span><span class="diczx2">李愬</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E8E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】羽俱切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】雲俱切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音迂。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】于，曰也。</p>
<p>又往也。【書&middot;大誥】民獻有十夫，予翼以于。【詩&middot;小雅】王于出征。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;士冠禮】宜之于假。【註】于，猶爲也。宜之見爲大矣。</p>
<p>又【聘禮】賄在聘于賄。【註】于讀曰爲。言當視賓之禮，而爲之財也。</p>
<p>又【司馬相如&middot;長門賦敘】因于解悲愁之辭。</p>
<p>又未定之辭。【公羊傳&middot;僖二十八年】歸于者何。歸于者，罪未定也。</p>
<p>又行貌。【韓愈&middot;上宰相書】于于焉而來矣。</p>
<p>又于于，自足貌。【莊子&middot;應帝王】其臥徐徐，其覺于于。</p>
<p>又鐘兩口之閒曰于。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】鳧氏爲鐘，兩欒謂之銑，銑閒謂之于。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;元后傳】衣絳緣諸于。【註】大掖衣也。</p>
<p>又【唐書&middot;元德秀傳】明皇命三百里內刺史縣令，以聲樂集，德秀惟樂工十人，聮袂歌于蔿于。</p>
<p>又草名。【爾雅&middot;釋草】茜，蔓于。【註】生水中。一名軒于。【司馬相如&middot;子虛賦】菴閭軒于。</p>
<p>又木名。【爾雅&middot;釋木】棧木，于木。【註】僵木也。江東呼木觡。</p>
<p>又淳于，縣名。今密州安丘縣，古淳于國。</p>
<p>又姓。周武王第二子邘叔之後，以國爲氏，後因去邑爲于。</p>
<p>又淳于，宣于，鮮于，多于，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28DD6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨷖</span>于，皆複姓。</p>
<p>又三氏姓。勿忸于，阿伏于，見【魏書&middot;官氏志】。</p>
<p>又助語辭。【詩&middot;召南】于沼于沚。【朱傳】于，於也。【周易】【毛詩】於皆作于。于於古通用。</p>
<p>又【集韻】邕俱切，音紆。廣大貌。【禮&middot;檀弓】邾婁考公之喪，徐君使容居來弔含。有司曰：諸侯之來辱敝邑者，易則易，于則于，易于雜者，未之有也。</p>
<p>又【文王世子】仲尼曰：周公抗世子法于伯禽，所以善成王也。聞之曰：爲人臣者，殺其身有益於君，則爲之，况于其身以善其君乎。俱與迂通。</p>
<p>又【正韻】休居切，與吁通。歎辭。【詩&middot;周南】于嗟麟兮。</p>
