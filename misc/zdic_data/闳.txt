                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>闳</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閎</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>巷门。</li>
	<li>宏大：～大广博。～言崇议（指议论宏远）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> gate, barrier; wide, vast, expand</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schwer, wichtig ,Hong  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> porte,barrière,large,vaste,étendu</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>闳</strong></p>
<p>閎 <span class="dicpy">h&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，厷( <span class="dicpy">gōng</span>)声。本义:巷门)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gate of a lane]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闳，巷门也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入于闳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及闳中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十年》</span>。注:&ldquo;曲门中。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闳门(皇门。古代天子、诸侯等宫殿的左门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 门 <span class="encs">[door]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:九闳(九天之门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓。如:闳天(周文王四友之一。纣囚文王于羑里，闳天求得美女和名马献给纣，纣很高兴，便释放文王。后来辅佐武王灭纣)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>闳</strong></p>
<p>閎 <span class="dicpy">h&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;宏&rdquo;。大，宏大 <span class="encs">[grand;great]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其器圆以闳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其语闳大不经，必先验小物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;孟子荀卿列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闳言崇议。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闳言(博大的言论);闳闳(宏大的样子);闳诞(闳大荒诞，大而无当);闳丽(宏伟壮丽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;宏&rdquo;。宽广，博大 <span class="encs">[wide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">布濩闳泽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人则乡</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>之闳道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;张山拊传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闳耀(广大光耀);闳旷(宽宏豁达);闳豁(宽敞);闳廓(博大)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;泓&rdquo;。水深 <span class="encs">[deep]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:闳流(深流)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閎·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/958E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】戸萌切【集韻】乎萌切【韻會】乎盲切【正韻】胡盲切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音宏。【說文】巷門也。从門，厷，意兼聲。【爾雅&middot;釋宮】衖門謂之閎。【註】閎，鬨頭門。【左傳&middot;成十七年】乗輦而入干閎。【註】巷門。</p>
<p>又【襄三十一年】高其閈閎。【註】閎，門也。</p>
<p>又【昭二十年】使華齊御公孟，宗魯驂乗，及閎中。【註】閎，曲門中。</p>
<p>又天門也。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】騰九閎。【註】九閎，九天之門。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】門辟旁長橛。【爾雅&middot;釋宮】所以止扉，謂之閎。【註】閎，長杙，卽門橜也。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;襄三十一年&middot;閈閎釋文】又云：所以止扉，謂之閎。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】姓也。【書&middot;君奭】有若閎夭。【傳】閎氏，文王弟。</p>
<p>又人名。【史記&middot;歷書】落下閎運算轉歷。【註】姚氏按益都耆舊傳，閎字長公，明曉天文，隱于落下。</p>
<p>又【集韻】呼宏切，音訇。閎廓，深遠也。</p>
<p>又中寬曰閎。【禮&middot;月令】其器圜以閎。【註】閎，讀如紘。紘謂中寬，象土含物。【韓愈&middot;進學解】閎其中，肆其外。</p>
<p>又虛廓也。【莊子&middot;知北遊】彷徨乎馮閎，大知人焉，而不知其所窮。【註】閎，虛廓之謂也。</p>
<p>又敦美也。【揚子&middot;太<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>經】大圈閎閎。【註】閎閎，敦美之意。</p>
<p>又【集韻】一曰大也。【楚辭&middot;九歌】山峻高以無垠兮，遂曾閎以迫身。【註】曾，重也。閎，大也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】胡肱切，音弘。義同。</p>
