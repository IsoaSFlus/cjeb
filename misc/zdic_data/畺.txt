                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>畺</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄤ</span> <span class="dicfq">《廣韻》居良切，平陽見。陽部。</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/疆">疆</a>&rdquo;。《玉篇&bull;畕部》；&ldquo;畺，《説文》曰&lsquo;界也。从畕，三，其界畫也。&rsquo;亦作疆。&rdquo;《廣韻&bull;陽韻》：&ldquo;畺，《説文》界也。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>畺</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄤˋ</span> <span class="dicfq">《集韻》居亮切，去漾見。</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>死不朽。《集韻&bull;漾韻》：&ldquo;畺，死不朽也。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
