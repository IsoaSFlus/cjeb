                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>骊</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">驪</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>纯黑色的马。</li>    <li>传说中黑色的龙：～珠。</li>    <li>并列：“～驾四鹿”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a pure black horse; a pair of horses</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Rappe (schwarzes Pferd mit schwarzer Mähne)​  (S, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cheval noir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骊</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>骊</strong></p><p>驪 <span class="dicpy">lí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 深黑色的马 <span class="encs">[black horse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骊，马深黑色。从马，丽声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有骊有黄。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·駉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戎事乘骊。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盗骊。——<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四骊济济，垂辔沵沵。——<span class="smcs">《诗·齐风·载驱》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:骊歌(告别的歌。<span class="yhcs">“骊驹之歌”</span>的简称);骊驹(纯黑色的马;逸<span class="yhcs">“诗”</span>篇名);骊黄(黑马和黄马)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 山名。骊山 <span class="encs">[Li mountain]</span>。又名蓝田山。在今陕西省临潼县东南。古代骊戎居此，故名骊山。如:骊山会(西周幽王为博褒姒一笑，于骊山举烽火戏诸侯，失信天下而导致大乱);骊山老母(骊山之姥。神话中的女仙)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古邑名 <span class="encs">[Li，ancient city]</span>。在今陕西省临潼县。周初骊戎所居，秦置骊邑。北宋时改称临潼</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>骊</strong></p><p>驪 <span class="dicpy">lí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 黑色 <span class="encs">[black]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骊，黑也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白马从骊驹。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·陌上桑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:骊马(黑色的马;齐驱并驾的马);骊龙(古代传说中颔下有珠的黑龙);骊珠(宝珠。传说出于骊龙颔下);骊驹(黑色的小马)</p><p>◎ <strong>骊</strong></p><p>驪 <span class="dicpy">lí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“丽”</span>。附丽;并列，用两匹马驾车 <span class="encs">[be drawn by two horses]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骊马二驷。(并驾之马八匹。)——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:骊驾(车马并排而行);骊马(齐驱并驾的马)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr                               驪·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=29" target="_blank">29</a>　·部外筆画：19</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9A6A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】呂支切【集韻】【韻會】鄰知切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音離。【玉篇】盜驪，千里馬也。【爾雅·釋畜】小領盜驪。【註】郭註《穆天子傳》曰天子之駿盜驪，綠耳，又曰右服盜驪。【詩·齊風】四驪濟濟。【禮·檀弓】夏后氏尚黑，戎事乗驪。</p><p>又【月令】冬駕鐵驪。</p><p>又駕兩馬曰驪。【後漢·寇恂傳】光武北征時，軍食急乏，寇恂以輦車驪駕轉輸，前後不絕。【註】驪駕，倂駕也。</p><p>又山名。【後漢·郡國志】京兆尹新豐有驪山。【註】杜預曰：古驪戎國。韋昭曰：戎成居此山，故號驪戎。</p><p>又高句驪，國名。</p><p>又縣名。【前漢·地理志】右北平郡驪成縣。</p><p>又【廣韻】郞奚切【韻會】憐題切【正韻】鄰溪切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音黎。義同。</p><p>又【集韻】陳尼切，音馳。驪靬，縣名。【前漢·地理志】張掖郡驪靬縣。【註】李奇曰：音遲虔。師古曰：驪，力遲反。靬音虔。令其土俗人呼驪靬，疾言之曰力虔。</p><p>
