                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>循</strong> <span class="dicpy">xún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从彳，盾声。彳( <span class="dicpy">chì</span>)，双人旁，与行走有关。本义:顺着，沿着)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[along]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循，行顺也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循道而趋。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循墙而走。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·列御寇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">率循大下。——<span class="smcs">《书·顾命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五星循轨。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循天者，与道游者也。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循山而南。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循表而夜涉。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循陵而走，见蹲石鳞鳞。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循以入。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:循轨(依轨道而行);循箫(沿街叫卖的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 按照;依序 <span class="encs">[according to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因任而授官，循名而责实。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·定法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奉事循公姥，进止敢自专。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:循例(依照常例);循名责实(按其名而求其实);循次(依次);循依(依照)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 遵循，仿效 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昏微循迹。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·天问》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“循，遵也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循绳墨而不颇。——</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无有作好，遵王之道。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:循古(遵循古制);循道(遵循正道);循桡(遵行);循检(遵照规矩);循蹈(遵循)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 遵守 <span class="encs">[abide by]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卿大夫以循法为节。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·射义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大人作而弟子循。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·汜论训》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:循律(遵循法律、制度);循分(遵守本分)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 循环 <span class="encs">[circulate]</span>，往复回旋。指事物周而复始地运动或变化。如:循环往复，循环无端</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“巡”</span>。巡视 <span class="encs">[tour]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循行国邑。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">循，巡也。——<span class="smcs">《珠丛》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徼循长杨以东。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·东方朔传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“行视也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:循览(浏览)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 步行 <span class="encs">[walk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今重甲循兵，不可以逾险。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></span></p><p><span
