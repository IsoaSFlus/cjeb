						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>崖</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yá.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄚˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>高地的边，陡立的山边：山～。悬～。～谷。～壑。～壁。～葬（悬棺葬）。～岸。悬～勒马。</li>    <li>边际：～略（大略，梗概）。</li></ol>
