                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刍</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>刍</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">芻</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>喂牲畜的草，亦指用草料喂牲口：～秣（饲养牛马的草料）。反～。</li>
	<li>割草：～荛（割草称&ldquo;刍&rdquo;，打柴称&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">荛</span>&rdquo;。指割草打柴的人。后常用作向人陈述意见的谦辞）。～言（常用来谦称自己的言论）。～议（同&ldquo;刍言&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>草把：～灵（古代送葬用的茅草扎的人马）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> mow, cut grass; hay, fodder</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> geschnittenes Gras ,Heu, Stroh</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> foin,paille,couper de l'herbe</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刍</strong></p>
<p>芻 <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形兼会意。甲骨文字形，从又(手)从草，表示以手取草。俗作芻。本义:割草)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">芻，刈草也。象包束草之形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，象断草包束以饮马牛者也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七曰刍粖之式。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太宰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淫刍荛者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禁刍牧采樵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刍莝养马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《越绝书&middot;外传本事》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刍牧(割草放牧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用草喂牲口 <span class="encs">[feed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刍之三月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;充人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刍养(饲养)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>刍</strong></p>
<p>芻 <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 喂牲畜的草 <span class="encs">[fodder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食以刍叔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">馈之刍米。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民争负薪刍助之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卒有取民麻一缕以束刍者，立斩以徇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刍菽(饲养牛马 的草料);刍粟(粮草);刍薪(柴草);刍藁(饲养牲畜的干草);刍豆(草和豆);刍茭(干草);刍粮(刍食，刍粟，粮草);刍厩(饲料屋);刍灵(用茅草扎成的人马);刍藁(干草);刍菽(刍豆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 谷类植物的茎杆 <span class="encs">[straw]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 割草的人 <span class="encs">[mower]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陛下不废刍言，则端士贤者必当自效。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 吃草的牲口 <span class="encs">[animals that feed on grass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民食刍豢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;齐物论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刍豢(指牛羊猪狗。牛羊食草，称刍;犬豕食谷，称豢。也泛指祭祀用的牺牲)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>刍</strong></p>
<p>芻 <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              芻·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/82BB.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】側愚切【正韻】楚徂切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音初。【說文】刈草也。【詩&middot;大雅】詢于芻蕘。【疏】芻者飼牛馬之草。【孟子】猶芻豢之悅我口。【趙註】草食曰芻。【韻會】羊曰芻，犬曰豢，皆以所食得名。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;祭統】士執芻。【註】藁也。【詩&middot;小雅】生芻一束。【箋】苽草刈取以用曰芻，故曰生芻。</p>
<p>又草名。【小雅】終朝采綠。【箋】綠，王芻也。</p>
<p>又梵語謂僧曰苾芻。</p>
<p>又芻尼。【許彥周詩話】嘗作七夕詩，押潘尼字，難于屬和，後讀《藏經》，有呼喜鵲爲芻尼。</p>
<p>又姓。見【何氏姓苑】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】葘尤切，音鄒。【韓愈&middot;駑驥詩】力小若易制，價微良易酬。渴飮一斗水，饑食一束芻。　【六書正譌】芻象包束草之形，俗作蒭，非。【干祿字書】通作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26DDD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦷝</span>、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26C6A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦱪</span>。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26DDD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦷝</span>字原从艸从口从丑作。</p>
