                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>菅</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>多年生草本植物，多生于山坡草地。很坚韧，可做炊帚、刷子等。杆、叶可作造纸原料：草～人命（比喻轻视人命）。</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;蕑&rdquo;。兰草。</li>
	<li>通&ldquo;姦&rdquo;：野蕪曠則民乃～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> coarse grass, themedia forskali</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Jian  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Themeda forskali</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">菅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>菅</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，官声。本义:菅茅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[villous themeda]</span>。一种多年生草本植物 (Themeda gigantea var. villosa )，叶子细长而尖，花绿色，结颖果，褐色</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白华菅兮，白茅束兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;白华》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:菅茅(茅草的一种);菅菲(菅履，草鞋);菅筲(用菅草编的盛饭器);菅荐(草席)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 也表示茅草的统称 <span class="encs">[cogongrass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽有丝、麻，无弃菅、蒯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:菅蒯(茅草之类，可编绳索;亦指草鞋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 中国古地名 <span class="encs">[Jian town]</span>。春秋宋地。在今山东单县北</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公败</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>师于 <span class="diczx2">菅</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/83C5.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26B42.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦭂</span>【唐韻】古顏切【集韻】居顏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音姦。【玉篇】茅屬也。【詩&middot;小雅】白華菅兮。【疏】已漚爲菅。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】無棄菅蒯。【註】菅似茅，滑澤無毛，筋宜爲索，漚與曝尤善。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;昭二十七年】或取一編菅。【杜註】苫也。【山海經】白菅爲席。</p>
<p>又【管子&middot;牧民篇】野蕪曠，則民乃菅。【註】菅當爲姦。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】漢有菅禹，唐有菅崇嗣。</p>
<p>又古頑切，音關。【春秋&middot;隱十年】公敗宋師于菅。【註】菅，宋地。</p>
<p>又【韻補】圭<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>切，音涓。【詩&middot;陳風】東門之池，可以漚菅。叶下晤言韻。</p>
