                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>烹</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>煮：～调（<span class="dicpy">tiáo </span>）（烹炒调制）。～饪。～茶。</li><li>一种做菜的方法，先用热油略炒之后，再加入液体调味品，迅速搅拌，随即盛出：～对虾。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>烹</strong> <span class="dicpy">pēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，亨声。本义:烧煮)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[boil;cook]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烹，煮也。——<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故曰:“治大国者若烹小鲜。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·解老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卒买鱼烹食。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:烹炮(烹调的手艺);烹鲜(比喻治理国家);烹龙炮凤(形容山珍海味);烹石(煎药);烹炙(烹煮煎烤);烹庖(烹治;烹煮);烹茗(煮茶或沏茶);烹宰(宰杀烹煮牲畜)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 冶炼 <span class="encs">[forge]</span>。如:烹炼(冶炼、提炼、锤炼);烹锻(烧炼);烹金(冶金;炼金);烹银(炼银)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 一种烹饪方法，先用热油略炒，然后加入酱油等作料迅速搅拌，随即盛出 <span class="encs">[fry quickly in hot oil and stir in sauce]</span>。如:烹对虾</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 浸泡在热水中以制成(如一种饮料或其它液体) <span class="encs">[brew]</span>。如:烹茶</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烹茶">烹茶</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烹饪">烹饪</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/烹调">烹调</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
