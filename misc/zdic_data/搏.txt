						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>搏</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bó  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bó.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄛˊ</span></p><ol><li>对打：～斗。～击（奋力进击）。～战。～膺（捶胸，表示愤怒）。肉～。拼～。</li><li>跳动：～动。脉～。</li><li>捕捉：～噬。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>搏</strong> <span class="dicpy">bó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，尃( <span class="dicpy">fū</span>)声。本义:搏斗，对打)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[combat;fight;struggle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏，索持也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏，击也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屏左右而相搏。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·僖公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋侯</span>梦与 <span class="diczx2">楚子</span>博。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸷虫攫博。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·儒行》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“以脚取之为攫，以翼击之为搏。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏扶摇羊角而上者。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏战获王。——<span class="smcs">《史记·卫将军骠骑传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以坚毅不挠之精神，与民贼相搏。——</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 捕捉 <span class="encs">[catch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏谍贼。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·夏宫·环人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:搏执(拘捕);搏获(捕获);搏撠(揪住);搏狩(狩猎)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 执持;拾取，击取 <span class="encs">[get;pick up;seize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摭紫贝，搏耆龟。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">螳螂执翳而搏之。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·山水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">益习其声，又近出前后，终不敢搏。(扑上去抓。)——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:搏杖(扶杖，抱杖);搏弊(人虽疲困，但也自持完成礼仪)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“拍”</span>。轻击;击 <span class="encs">[beat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搏埴之工二。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·总叙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弹琴博髀。——</span><span class="diczx2">李斯</span><span class="smcs">《谏逐客书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因搏心大哭。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水石相搏。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:搏鸷(猛烈拍击);搏膺(捶胸，表示愤怒);搏影(拍击影子。影随物动，故不能拍到。比喻不能成功或不易捉摸)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 夺取 <span class="encs">[take by force;capture]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铄金百镒，</span><span class="diczx2">盗跖</span>不搏。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李斯列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:搏斗;搏击</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 跳动 <span
