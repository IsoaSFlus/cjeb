                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>远</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">遠</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˇ</span></p><ol><li>距离长，与“<span class="diczx3">近</span>”相对：～方。～道。～程。～景。～足（较远的徒步旅行）。～见（远大的眼光）。～虑。～谋。～客。遥～。～走高飞。舍近就～。</li><li>时间长：～古。～祖。长～。永～。</li><li>关系疏，不亲密：～亲。疏～。～支。</li><li>深奥：言近旨～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>远</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">遠</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 避开：近君子，～小人。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">远</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>远</strong></p><p>遠 <span class="dicpy">yuǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，袁声。本义:走路走得长)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[far;distant]</span>。引申距离大，相隔远。与近相对</p><p><span class="diczx1">远，辽也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远，遐也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屏之远方。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“九州之外也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔远人也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穷高极远。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">念悲其远。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远主备之，无乃不可乎?——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">劳师以袭远，非所闻也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·三十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缦立远视。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">火器利袭远，技击利巷战。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">极目远望。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:远行无急步(走长路不能走得太急);远游之戒(指孔子<span class="yhcs">“父母在，不远游”</span>的戒训);远招近揖(热情相邀、礼貌相待远近友人);远水近火(即远水不救近火。比喻缓不济急)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 长久;长远 <span
