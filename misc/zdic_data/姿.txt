data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，次声。本义:姿态)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(<span class="diczx1">娇姿欲滴。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">直则无姿。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 容貌 <span class="encs">[appearance;looks]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">王汝南</span>少无婚，自求 <span class="diczx2">郝普</span>女。…既婚，果有今姿淑德。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·贤缓》</span></p><p><span
