						<p>● <strong>摈</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">擯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>排除，抛弃：～弃。～除。～黜（罢黜并放逐）。～诸门外。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/傧">傧</a>&rdquo;，迎宾。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> exclude, expel, reject; usher</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> recevoir,rejeter</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>摈</strong></p>
<p>擯 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 排斥;弃绝 <span class="encs">[discard;get rid of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摈，相排斥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贤者摈于朝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说林训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六国从亲以摈</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已摈忧患寻常事，留得豪情作</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>囚。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">恽代英</span><span class="smcs">《狱中诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:摈兑(除掉;以命换命);摈落(排去，弃而不用);摈压(受到排斥压迫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;傧&rdquo;。导引宾客 <span class="encs">[guide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宗人摈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;有司彻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君召使摈，色勃如也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;乡党》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌九仪之宾客摈相之礼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡四方之使者，大客则摈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;小行人》</span></span></p>

<p>又與儐同。導賓也。主國之君所使出接賓者，在主曰擯，在客曰介。【禮&middot;聘義】卿爲上擯，大夫爲承擯，士爲紹擯。【疏】承者，承副上擯也。紹者，繼續承擯也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;小行人】凡四方之使者，大客則擯。【註】擯而見之王，使得親言也。</p>
