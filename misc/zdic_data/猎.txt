						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>猎</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">獵</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>打猎；捕捉禽兽：狩～。渔～。～捕。～取。～人。～户。～狗。～枪。</li>
	<li>搜寻；物色：～奇。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>猎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒ〡</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>古代传说中一种像熊的兽。《廣韻&bull;昔韻》：&ldquo;猎，獸名，似熊。出《山海經》。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">猎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>猎</strong></p>
<p>獵 <span class="dicpy">li&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从犬，巤( <span class="dicpy">li&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:打猎，捕捉禽兽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hunt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猎，放猎逐禽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不狩不猎，胡瞻尔庭有县貆兮?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">执弓挟矢以猎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">楚蒍艾猎，字叔敖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吏所猎也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;毅》</span>。注:&ldquo;捕也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将军猎</span><span class="diczx2">渭城</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《观猎》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">单于</span>出猎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">教我猎虫所。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:猎火(打猎时焚山以驱兽的火);猎围(猎捕禽兽的围子);猎士(打猎的壮士);猎夫(猎手、猎民、猎者、猎客、猎师、猎徒。猎人);猎食(捕捉或寻找食物;谋食)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 搜求，追求 <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p>
