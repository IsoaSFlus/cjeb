                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>左</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zuǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǒ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄛˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>面向南时，东的一边，与“<span class="diczx3">右</span>”相对：～手。～方。～右。～膀右臂。</li>    <li>地理上指东方：山～。江～。</li>    <li>指政治思想上进步或超过现实条件许可的过头思想和行动：～派。～翼。～倾。</li>    <li>斜，偏，差错：～脾气。～嗓子。</li>    <li>降低官职：～迁。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">佐</span>”，佐证。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> left; east; unorthodox, improper</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> links, linke Seite ,Zuo  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> gauche,opposé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">左</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>左</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象手的象形。本义:辅佐，从旁帮助。后作<span class="yhcs">“佐”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左，手相左助也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。俗字作<span class="yhcs">“佐”</span>。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以左右民。——<span class="smcs">《易·象上传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予欲左右有民。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">实左右商王。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·长发》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以左右刑罚。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·士师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左右就养无方。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓扶持之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辅相天地之宜，以左右民。——<span class="smcs">《易·泰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朕且继礼左助听政。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·殇帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 贬谪，从朝廷下放到地方为官 <span class="encs">[banish from the court]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左，谪官为左迁。自</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>起至 <span class="diczx2">唐</span>，亦谓去朝廷为州县曰左迁。——<span class="smcs">《增韵》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:左除(降级授职);左降(贬官);左退(降官;贬职);左宦(降官;贬职);左授(降官;贬职);左转(降官;贬职);左黜(降官;贬职);左迁(降官;贬职);左官(降官;贬职)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 见外，把某人当外人看待 <span class="encs">[regard sb. as an outsider]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今居分之土而官之，是左之也。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>左</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 左手 <span class="encs">[left]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子阳阳，左执簧，右招我由房，其乐只且。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·君子阳阳》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:左书(左手写字);左癖(左撇子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 方位名。与<span class="yhcs">“右”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[left]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虚左。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:左侍下(左边);左势下(左边);左符(符契的左半);左驸(车箱左侧的立木);左骖(左马。三马驾车中左边的马)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 地理上常以东为左 <span class="encs">[east]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疾逸道左。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">淮</span>左名都。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>·
