                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>示</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>表明，把事物拿出来或指出来使别人知道：～警。～范。～弱。～威。～众。～意。告～。指～。请～。</li>    <li>对来信的敬称：赐～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> show, manifest; demonstrate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> zeigen  (V)​,Radikal Nr. 113 = zeigen, verkünden, demonstrieren (Variante: 礻)​; frühere Bedeutung: verehren, opfern  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> montrer,indiquer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">示</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>示</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，<span class="yhcs">“二”</span>是古文<span class="yhcs">“上”</span>字，三竖代表日月星。甲骨文本作<span class="yhcs">“T”</span>，象祭台形。<span class="yhcs">“示”</span>是汉字的一个部首，其义多与祭祀、礼仪有关。本读( <span class="dicpy">qí</span>)。本义:地神。引申义:让人看，显示)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 显现;表示 <span class="encs">[notify;show]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">示，现也。——<span class="smcs">《华严经音义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天垂象见吉凶所以示人也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。古文三垂，日月星也。观乎天文以察时变，示神事也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">示，现也。——<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">武王</span>示之病。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穷不得所示。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·怀沙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王不行，示</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>弱且怯也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斩使以示威。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二子心计，公无从办，特示故人意耳。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:示像(显露外形);示疾(佛教语。佛菩萨及高僧得病);示现(菩萨应机缘而现种种化身);示贬于褒(寓批评于表扬);示重(表示器重);示问(指表示问候的简札书启之类);示俭(表现节俭);示优(表示优厚);示惩(表示惩戒);示怀(表示恩德);示导(启示开导);示诲(开导教诲)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指示;让人看;把事物摆出来或指出来让人知道 <span class="encs">[point out;demonstrate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">示，语也，以事告人曰示也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">示，垂示。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">示我周行。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·鹿鸣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">言示之事。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·抑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则不能毋举物示之乎?——<span class="smcs">《墨子·尚贤下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国之利器不可以示人。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">袒而示之背。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召桑田巫，示而杀之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">璧有瑕，请指示王。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">权</span>以示群下，莫不响震失色。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">归以示成。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:示仰(指示;传达);示梦(灵魂在梦中以事示人);示下(指示);示导(启示开导);示众(给大家看，特指当众惩罚人);示及(见示、谈到);示化(启示化导);示世(昭示于世人);示唆(启示);示覆(书牍中请对方作答之用语);示知(信函用语。告知);示谕(告知;晓示)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 暗示;示意解释 <span class="encs">[hint]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">范增</span>数目 <span class="diczx2">项王</span>，举所佩玉玦以示之者三。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>示</strong>
