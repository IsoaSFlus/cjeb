                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">巨</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>巨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>大，很大：～大。～人（①身材异常高的人；②童话里指比一般人高大，有神力的人；③喻有巨大影响和贡献的人）。～匠（泛称在科学或文艺上有极大成就的人）。～子（①中国战国时墨家学派领袖的尊称；②在某方面卓有成就，有声望的人）。艰～。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> large, great, enormous; chief</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> groß, riesig, gewaltig, enorm, gigantisch  (Adj)​,Ju  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grand,énorme,immense,gigantesque</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">巨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>巨</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形，是&ldquo;矩&rdquo;的本字，象人持矩形。本义:画直角方形用的工具)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。后作&ldquo;矩&rdquo; <span class="encs">[carpenter&rsquo;s rule]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巨，规巨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以君子有挈巨之道也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">成功之术，必有巨矱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;宙合》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:巨矱(法度。同&ldquo;矩矱&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;距&rdquo;。距离 <span class="encs">[distance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">守为台城，以临羊黔，左右出巨，各二十尺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>巨</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大;很大 <span class="encs">[great;huge;gigantic;tremendous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巨，大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">力士举巨囊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;哀公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">右巨指钩弦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;大射仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">穹崖巨谷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巨石岿然。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巨幅悬之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巨弹堕地。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劝输巨室。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:巨响(极大的响声);巨指(大指);巨港(大港);巨室(大厦，大屋;公卿大夫之家);巨蠹(喻祸国殃民的大奸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 多，数量大 <span class="encs">[enormous]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:巨万(万万之数。形容数目很大);巨金(大批钱财;大锭银子);巨千(为数甚多)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 粗;粗大;粗略 <span class="encs">[coarse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:巨屦(粗糙的鞋子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 高超、杰出 <span class="encs">[super]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:巨川材(济世大才);巨手(高手，喻指杰出的人物);巨公(皇帝的特称;太师;大人师)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>巨</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;讵&rdquo;。岂，难道 <span class="encs">[Does it mean&hellip;? Shouldn&#39;t it be&hellip;?]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">沛公</span>不先破关中兵，公巨能入乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:巨能(岂能)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 最 <span class="encs">[very]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言不尽于此，颇难悉载，故粗举其巨怪者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《辩道论》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5DE8.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22011.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢀑</span>㠪<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20AD5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠫕</span>【唐韻】其呂切【集韻】臼許切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>渠上聲。【說文】䂓巨也。从工，象手持之。</p><p>又【玉篇】大也。【孟子】爲巨室。</p><p>又巨萬，數也。【史記&middot;平準書】京師之錢累巨萬。【註】巨萬，今萬萬。</p><p>又姓。【何氏姓苑】巨氏出南昌。【廣韻】漢有巨武，爲荆州刺史。</p><p>又巨毋，複姓。【前漢&middot;王莽傳】有奇士，長丈，大十圍，自謂巨毋霸。莽更其姓，曰巨母氏。謂因文母而霸王符也。【後漢&middot;光武紀】作巨無霸。</p><p>又【韻會】通作鉅。【前漢&middot;食貨志】庶人之富者累鉅萬。【李賀&middot;高軒過詩】文章鉅工。</p><p>又與詎同。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】公巨能入乎。【師古曰】巨讀曰詎，猶豈也。</p><p>又【類篇】求於切，音蕖。未央也。【韻會】亦作渠。詩小雅庭燎註，夜未渠央。義與遽同，言未便至夜也。</p>

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DE8_1633.svg" /><span class="swbf">(巨)</span><span class="ywyy">規巨也。</span><span class="swdz">周髀筭經曰：圜出於方，方出於矩，矩出於九九八十一。故折矩以爲句廣三，股脩四，徑隅五。旣方其外，半之一矩，環而共盤，得成三四五，㒳矩共長二十有五，是謂積矩。用矩之道，平矩以正繩，偃矩以望高，覆矩以測湥，臥矩以知遠，環矩以爲圓，合矩以爲方。方屬地，圓屬天，天圓地方，方數爲典，以方出圓。按規矩二字猶言法度，古不分別。規圜矩方者，圜出於方，圜方皆出於矩也。夫部曰：規，有法度也。不言圜曰規。考工記：斬轂之道必矩其陰陽。注：矩，謂刻識之也。凡識其廣長曰矩，故凡有所刻識皆謂之矩。</span><span class="ywyy">从工，象手持之。</span><span class="swdz">謂コ也。其吕切。五部。按後人分別巨，大也；矩，法也，常也。與說文字異。其吕切，唐韵也。廣韵作矩、榘，入九麌，俱雨切。又云：說文又其吕切，此出說文音隱。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DE8_1634.svg" /><span class="swbf">(榘)</span><span class="ywyy">巨或从木矢，矢者其中正也。</span><span class="swdz">矢部曰：有所長短，以矢爲正。按今字作矩省木。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DE8_1635.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22013.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢀓</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文巨。</span><span class="swdz">此爲象手持之。小篆變之，取整齊耳。大學：絜矩之道。注云：矩或作巨。此古文之遺也。</span></p>                          </div>
