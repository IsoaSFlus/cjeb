                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鹄</strong></p>
<p>鵠 <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 箭靶的中心 <span class="encs">[bull&#39;s-eye]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鹄子(箭靶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 目标，目的 <span class="encs">[target]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此其鹄惟在于刑，其刑惟在于任威斩断，而五官之大法无与焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">章炳麟</span><span class="smcs">《商鞅》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">h&uacute;;h&egrave;</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/鹄的">鹄的</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鹄</strong></p>
<p>鵠 <span class="dicpy">h&egrave;</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;鹤&rdquo;。鸟名。鹤科各种禽类的泛称 <span class="encs">[crane]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫鹄不日浴而白，乌不日黔而黑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">越鸡不能伏鹄卵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;庚桑楚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王昭楚妃，千里别鹄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《琴赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大仪鹄发。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;吴良传赞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">h&egrave;;gǔ</span></p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鹄</strong></p>
<p>鵠 <span class="dicpy">h&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鸟，告声。本义:鸟名。指鸿鹄，又名&ldquo;黄鹄&rdquo;，俗称天鹅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[swan]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">青雀白鹄舫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">鵠</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“鹄”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/鵠">鵠</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中" target="_blank">亥集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中&amp;bs=鳥" target="_blank">鳥</a>】
                              鵠·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9D60.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>胡沃切，音翯。【說文】鴻鵠也。【顏師古曰】鵠，水鳥，其聲鵠鵠。【本草】鵠大於鴈，羽毛白澤，其翔極高，而善步。一名天鵝。【史記&middot;陳涉世家】燕雀安知鴻鵠之志哉。</p><p>又黃鵠。【玉篇】黃鵠仙人所乗。【前漢&middot;賈誼傳】黃鵠之一舉知山川之紆曲，再舉知天地之圓方。</p><p>又鵠立。【後漢&middot;袁紹傳】瞻望鵠立。【註】企佇之狀，如鵠立也。</p><p>又鵠蒼，犬名。見【博物志】。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋器】象謂之鵠。【郭註】鵠，治樸之名，謂治其樸未成器也。</p><p>又【後漢&middot;吳良傳】大儀鵠髮。【註】鵠髮卽鶴髮。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>姑沃切，音牿。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;司裘】王大射，則共虎侯豹侯，設其鵠。【鄭司農云】鵠，鵠毛也。四尺曰鵠，謂之鵠者，取名於鳱鵠。鵠小鳥難中，是以中之謂雋。一說鵠，鳥之大者，有力飛遠，故正鵠取此。【禮&middot;射儀】循聲而發，不失正鵠。【註】畫布曰正，棲皮曰鵠。鵠之言梏也。梏直，言人正直乃能中也。</p><p>又姓。見【萬姓統譜】。</p><p>又【正字通】音告。地名。鵠澤。【詩&middot;唐風】從子于鵠。【毛傳】鵠，曲沃邑也。【前漢&middot;地理志】鵠澤。【註】孟康讀告。</p><p>又【呂覽】鵠乎其羞周智慮也。【註】鵠讀如浩，大也。○按《正字通》云：《轉註古音》《讀書通》俱云鵠通作鶴。《淮南子&middot;覽冥訓》：鴻鵠，鶬鶴，莫不驚憚伏竄。《班固&middot;西都賦》：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>鶴白鷺，黃鵠鵁鸛。《左思&middot;吳都賦》：鳥則鸘鵠、鶄鶴。皆分鵠、鶴爲二，至於《別鶴操》雄鵠、雌鵠，《劉孝標&middot;辨命論》龜鵠千歲，《費昶&middot;擣衣》詩開韞舒龜鵠，古本皆作鶴，俗譌爲鵠，豈可據此謂鵠卽鶴也。鶴不過叶音同鵠耳，《嵆康&middot;琴賦》鶴與曲叶讀如鵠，豈可言鵠卽是鶴，故鶴不宜與鵠通。</p>
