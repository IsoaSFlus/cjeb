                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>给</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">給</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gěi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gěi.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄟˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>交付，送与：～以。～予。送～。献～。</li>
	<li>把动作或态度加到对方：～他一顿批评。</li>
	<li>替，为：～大家帮忙。</li>
	<li>被，表示遭受：房子～火烧掉了。</li>
	<li>把，将：请你随手～门送上。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>给</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">給</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>供应：供～。补～。～养。自～自足。</li>
	<li>富裕，充足：家～人足。</li>
	<li>敏捷：言论～捷。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">给</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>给</strong></p>
<p>給 <span class="dicpy">gěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gěi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="encs">[口]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 使对方得到或遭受到 <span class="encs">[give;grant;hand]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:给他一张票;我给他字典;给我一片面包;给脸(给面子;给以礼遇);给个炭篓鬼戴(抹黑;使人难堪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 让;使;叫 <span class="encs">[let]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:给我看看;别叫风给刮散了</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>给</strong></p>
<p>給 <span class="dicpy">gěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gěi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈介〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示对象、目的，相当于&ldquo;为&rdquo;、&ldquo;替&rdquo; <span class="encs">[for;for the benefit of ]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:为给人类带来利益而工作;给饥饿者所需要的食物;寄给我的信</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">给伤员包扎</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引进动作行为的主动者，或表示被动语态，相当于&ldquo;被&rdquo; <span class="encs">[by]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:机器给弄坏了;屋子里给弄得乱七八糟</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示方向，相当于&ldquo;朝&rdquo;、&ldquo;对&rdquo;、&ldquo;向&rdquo; <span class="encs">[to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:给这儿灌水;给他送礼;给老师行礼;给他使了个眼色</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>给</strong></p>
<p>給 <span class="dicpy">gěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gěi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈助〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用在某些动词前面，用以加强语气 <span class="encs">[used before some verbs，giving stress to the tone]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:保不住给忘了;风把门给吹开了;您给找个人</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">碗给打碎了;裤腿都叫露水给湿透了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">jǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ample;be well provided for;abundant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">给，相足也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事之供给。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">则日不足，力不给。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;有度》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">御人以口给，屡憎于人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:给口(口才敏捷);给捷(敏捷)</span></p>
