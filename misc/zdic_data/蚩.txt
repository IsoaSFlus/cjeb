						<p>● <strong>蚩</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˉ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蚩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蚩</strong> <span class="dicpy">chī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，之声。本义:蚩虫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a kind of insect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蚩，蚩虫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 传说中的一种海兽 <span class="encs">[marine beast]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蚩尾(即鸱尾。古代屋脊上的鱼尾形饰物);蚩吻(传说中的怪兽名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如: <span class="diczx2">蚩尤</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蚩尤惟始作乱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:&ldquo;少昊之末九黎君名。&rdquo;按， <span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">蚩尤</span>霸天下， <span class="diczx2">黄帝</span>所伐者，学 <span class="diczx2">蚩尤</span>为此者，九黎之君在少昊之代也，是 <span class="diczx2">黄帝</span>擒于涿鹿者。</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蚩</strong> <span class="dicpy">chī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 痴呆，愚蠢 <span class="encs">[ignorant;stupid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蚩，痴也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儿大黠宗室无蚩者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;刘盆子传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蚩骏(痴呆笨拙);蚩伫(鲁钝庸劣);蚩人(愚人);蚩蚩蠢蠢(愚笨蠢动的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;媸&rdquo;。丑陋;丑恶 <span class="encs">[ugly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孰知辨其蚩妍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张壹传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蚩妍(丑与美。同媸妍);蚩鄙(粗野拙劣)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蚩</strong> <span class="dicpy">chī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 欺侮 <span class="encs">[bully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蚩眩边鄙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;侮也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蚩辱(侮辱;欺压);蚩弄(犹侮弄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;嗤&rdquo;。讥笑 <span class="encs">[sneer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蚩，笑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他日与</span><span class="diczx2">蒙</span>会，又蚩辱之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吕蒙传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">终蚩弄而交泄兮，虽近习而不亲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">阮籍</span><span class="smcs">《猕猴赋》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一種蟲。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;虫部》：「<b>蚩</b>，蟲也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如古時有蚩尤。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">嘲笑。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;阮籍&middot;詠懷詩一七首之一一》：「乃誤羨門子，噭噭今自<b>蚩</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「嗤」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">欺侮。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;張衡&middot;西京賦》：「鬻良雜苦，<b>蚩</b>眩邊鄙。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">無知、痴笨。</span><span class="gc_yy">《後漢書&middot;卷一一&middot;劉盆子傳》：「兒大黠，宗室無<b>蚩</b>者。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">醜惡。</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;趙壹〈刺世疾邪賦〉：「榮納由於閃榆，孰知辨其<b>蚩</b>妍？」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「媸」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集中" target="_blank">申集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集中&amp;bs=虫" target="_blank">虫</a>】
                              蚩·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/86A9.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】赤之切【韻會】充之切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音妛。蟲名。【六書正譌】凡無知者，皆以蚩名之。</p>
<p>又蚩尤，人名。【書&middot;呂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>】蚩尤惟始作亂。【註】九黎之君，號曰蚩尤。</p>
<p>又星名。【晉書&middot;天文志】蚩尤旗，類彗而後曲，象旗主所見之方下有兵。</p>
<p>又侮也。【張衡&middot;西京賦】蚩眩邊鄙。</p>
<p>又騃也。【陸機&middot;文賦】姸蚩好惡，可得而言。</p>
<p>又蚩蚩，敦厚貌。【詩&middot;衞風】氓之蚩蚩。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】蚩氏，蚩尤之後也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】敕豸切，音弛。蟲伸行。【廣韻】从㞢。【六書正譌】別作媸、嗤，非。</p>
<p>【集韻】同蚩。詳蚩字註。蚩字右下从冫不从丶。</p>
