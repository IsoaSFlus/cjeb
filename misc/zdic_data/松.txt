                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">松</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>松</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">②-⑤<span class="diczx3">鬆</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄨㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>种子植物的一属，一般为常绿乔木，脂可提取松香或松节油等。种子可榨油和食用：～针。～脂。～香。～子。</li>
	<li>稀散，不紧密，不靠拢，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">紧</span>&rdquo;相对：捆得太～。土质～软。</li>
	<li>宽，不紧张，不严格：规矩太～。～懈。</li>
	<li>放开：～手。～绑。～心。</li>
	<li>用瘦肉做成的茸毛或碎末形的食品：肉～。鱼～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pine tree; fir tree</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kiefer (lat: Pinus)​  (S, Bio)​,lockern  (V)​,locker  (Adj)​,schlaff  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> desserrer,lâcher,détendu,mou,pin (emblème de longévité, de constance et de fidélité)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">松</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>松</strong> <span class="dicpy">sōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，公声。本义:松科植物的总称) 同本义 <span class="encs">[pine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">松，松木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山有乔松。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;山有扶苏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如松茂矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;斯干》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出户望南山，松生石上，剑在其背。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《搜神记&middot;干将莫邪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一般为常绿乔木，很少为灌木，树皮多为鳞片状，叶子针形，花单性，雌雄同株，结球果，卵圆形或圆锥形，有木质的鳞片，木材和树脂都可利用。又如:松塔;松涛;松针;松脂;松亭(旁边有松树的亭子);松活(以松柏的枝叶扎成人、鹤、鹿、亭等形状，作为冥器，叫做松活);松肪(松脂);松扇(用柔嫩松皮制成的一种古扇);松钗(松树的枝叶);松腴(松脂);松花绿(亦作&ldquo;松花&rdquo;、&ldquo;松绿&rdquo;。偏黑的深绿色，墨绿);松篁(松树与竹子)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>松</strong></p>
<p>鬆 <span class="dicpy">sōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 发乱;乱发貌 <span class="encs">[tangled]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">首蓬松以半散，支棘瘠而枯踈。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆龟蒙</span><span class="smcs">《自怜赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 不紧 <span class="encs">[loose;slack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蜂须蝉翅薄松松，浮动搔头似有风。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《宫词》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:松宽(亦作宽松。使行动有较大的自由，放松);松松爽爽(轻松爽快);松怠怠(赖散的样子);松通(宽松，放宽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 不坚实 <span class="encs">[light and flaky;soft]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:松脆;这点心松脆可口</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 经济宽裕 <span class="encs">[not hard up]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:&ldquo;手头松&rdquo;;松通(舒散，流通，宽裕)</span></p>
