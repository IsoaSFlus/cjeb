                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">杂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>杂</strong></p><p>雜、襍 <span class="dicpy">zá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，集声。本义:五彩相合)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[multicoloured]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">画绘之事，杂五色。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·考工记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 混合;搀杂 <span class="encs">[mix; mingle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故先王以土与金木水火杂，以成百物。——<span class="smcs">《国语·郑语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼此错杂。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:杂烩菜(将各种剩菜合并在一起的菜淆);夹杂(搀杂);杂就(参杂而成);混杂(混合搀杂)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 聚会;聚集 <span class="encs">[get together; assemble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杂，聚也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四方来杂，远乡皆至。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:杂物(聚集事物)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>杂</strong></p><p>雜 <span class="dicpy">zá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 驳杂不纯 <span class="encs">[heterogeneous;mixed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夹岸数百步，中无杂树。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:杂面(杂合面粉作的面条)</p><p><span class="diczx1">杂彩三百匹。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杂花生树。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昧没而杂。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杂然而前陈。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">少杂树。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:杂裳(前黑后黄的下衣)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 紊乱 <span class="encs">[disorderly;chaotic]</span>。如:杂错(交错混杂);嘈杂(声音杂乱;喧闹);庞杂(多而杂乱)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 繁琐;细碎 <span class="encs">[(of writings and speech) many
