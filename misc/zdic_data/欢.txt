                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>欢</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">歡</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">huān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huān.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>快乐，高兴：～乐。～庆。～会。～快。～颜。尽～而散。郁郁寡～。</li>
	<li>喜爱，亦指所喜爱的人：心～。新～旧识。</li>
	<li>活跃，起劲，旺盛：～蹦乱跳。～实。机器转得真～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> happy, pleased, glad; joy; enjoy</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">欢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>欢</strong></p>
<p>歡、懽、驩 <span class="dicpy">huān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从欠，雚( <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span>)声。本义:喜悦，高兴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[happy;cheerful;joyful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欢，喜乐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子不尽人之欢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫妇不得不驩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若果养乎?予果欢乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;至乐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">许子而大欢，彼将知君利之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说林上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而大国与之懽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饮酒则欢乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;渔父》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今日之欢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众宾欢也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欢动一城。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奉一日之欢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">侯方域</span><span class="smcs">《壮悔堂文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:欢容(喜悦的容颜);欢洽(快乐和睦);欢哄(欢乐，哄闹);欢情(喜悦的心情)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 欢迎，殷勤地或诚挚地迎接 <span class="encs">[welcome]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻其声，争交欢</span><span class="diczx2">解</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;游侠列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:欢门(宋代酒馆、店铺用彩色纸帛装饰门窗，以招徕顾客，谓之欢门)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈方〉∶起劲;活跃 <span class="encs">[with great drive;vigorous]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:你不是跑得挺欢吗?</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>欢</strong></p>
<p>歡 <span class="dicpy">huān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古时男女相爱，女子对情人的称呼 <span class="encs">[lover]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唱尽新词欢不见，红霞映树鹧鸪鸣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《踏歌词》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻欢下</span><span class="diczx2">扬州</span>，相送 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>山头。&mdash;&mdash;古乐府<span class="smcs">《莫愁乐》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 欢爱。指男女交合之事 <span class="encs">[sexual union]</span>。</p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下" target="_blank">辰集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下&amp;bs=欠" target="_blank">欠</a>】
                              歡·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=22" target="_blank">22</a>　·部外筆画：18</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6B61.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27192.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧆒</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>呼官切，音讙。【說文】喜樂也。【徐曰】喜動聲氣，故从欠。【禮&middot;檀弓】啜菽飮水盡其歡。</p>
<p>又【樂記】欣喜歡愛，樂之官也。亦作懽。【孝經&middot;孝治章】故得萬國之懽心，以事其先王。</p>
<p>又作驩。【孟子】驩虞如也。【前漢&middot;王褒傳】驩然交欣。</p>
<p>又合歡，漢殿名。【班固&middot;西部賦】後宮則有合歡增成。</p>
<p>又樹名。【崔豹&middot;古今注】合歡樹似梧桐，枝葉繁，互相交結，樹之階庭，使人不忿。</p>
<p>又竹名。【僧贊寧&middot;筍譜】雙稍竹出九疑山，筍長，獨莖。及生枝葉卽分爲兩梢，謂之合歡竹。</p>
<p>又橘名。【廣輿記】荆州江陵有合歡橘。</p>
<p>又歡伯，酒也。【焦氏易林】酒爲歡伯，除憂來樂。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶許元切，音暄。【魏韋誕&middot;親蠶頌】同碩慶于生民，發三靈之永歡。苞繁祐于萬國，卷福釐以言旋。　【集韻】或作孉。亦作〈女歡〉。</p>
