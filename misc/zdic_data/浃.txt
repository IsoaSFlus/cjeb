                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>浃</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">浹</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄚ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>湿透：汗流～背。</li>
	<li>周匝：～日（古代以干支为&ldquo;浃日&rdquo;）。～辰（称自子至亥一周十二日为&ldquo;浃辰&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>深入，融洽：～洽。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> saturate, drench; damp, wet</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> sättigen, durchtränken  (V)</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>浃</strong></p>
<p>浹 <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，夹声。本义:湿透)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[saturate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汗出浃背。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汗流浃背;浃髓沦肌，浃髓沧肤，浃沦肌髓(深入骨髓，浸透肌肤;喻感受深切)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通达，理解 <span class="encs">[understand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其所以贯理焉，虽亿万已不足以浃万物之变，与愚者若一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>浃</strong></p>
<p>浹 <span class="dicpy">jiā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 整个儿的 <span class="encs">[full]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浃辰之间而</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>克其三都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公九年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:浃岁(一整年);浃时(一季);浃月(一个月);浃辰(十二天);浃旬(一旬，十天)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 融洽 <span class="encs">[on friendly terms]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其岁九月，人吏浃和。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《新修滕王阁记》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6D79.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】子協切【集韻】卽協切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/205C9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠗉</span>。【爾雅&middot;釋言】徹也。【疏】謂潤澤浹洽，相霑徹也。【前漢&middot;禮樂志】教化浹洽。</p>
<p>又周浹，猶周匝也。【荀子&middot;君道篇】先王審禮，以旁皇周浹于天下。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;宣九年】浹辰之閒，楚克三都。【註】浹辰，謂自子至亥，周匝十二日也。【楚語】近不過浹日。【註】浹日，謂從甲至癸也。</p>
<p>又通作挾。【周禮&middot;天官】使萬民觀治象，挾日而斂之。</p>
<p>又【集韻】轄夾切，音洽。浹渫，水貌。【郭璞&middot;江賦】長波浹渫。【註】浹渫，水滂溏也。</p>
