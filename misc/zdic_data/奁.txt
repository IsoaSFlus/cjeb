                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奁</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>奁</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">奩</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>古代盛梳妆用品的匣子。《説文&bull;竹部》：&ldquo;籢，鏡籢也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>泛指盛放器物的匣子。《説苑&bull;尊賢》：&ldquo;臣笑臣隣之祠田也，以一奩飯、一壺酒、三鮒魚，祝曰：&lsquo;蟹堁者宜禾，洿邪者百車，傳之後世，洋洋有餘。&rsquo;臣笑其賜鬼薄，而請之厚也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>古时盛放香炉的笼子。亦称&ldquo;香笼&rdquo;。《廣韻&bull;鹽韻》：&ldquo;匳，盛香器也。俗作奩。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>嫁妆。《正字通&bull;大部》：&ldquo;今以物送女嫁曰妝奩。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>奁</strong></p>
<p>奩、匳、匲 <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代盛梳妆用品的匣子 <span class="encs">[mirrored dressing case]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弹棋始自</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>宫内用妆奁戏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;巧艺》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凝走弄香奁，拔脱金屈戌。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《骄儿诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妆奁(香奁。放梳妆用品的器具)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指盛放器物的匣子 <span class="encs">[box]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">&hellip;并两部书，茶奁、茶杯而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一奁饭(一盒饭);茶奁(茶壶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 陪嫁的衣物等 <span class="encs">[dowry]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:奁田(陪嫁的田产);奁匣(陪嫁的镜匣);奁币(陪嫁的财物);奁具(嫁妆);奁箱(嫁妆)</span></p>
