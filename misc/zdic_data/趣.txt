                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>趣</strong> <span class="dicpy">cù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (古通<span class="yhcs">“促”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 督促;催促 <span class="encs">[urge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趣，疾也。从走，取声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趣其稼事。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·县止》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趣民收敛。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趣为我语。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·周勃传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趣</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>兵亟入关。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">王</span>夫人又趣使大臣立 <span class="diczx2">栗姬</span>为皇后。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·外戚传上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:趣使(督促);趣董(催促);趣督(督促)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 赶快;从速 <span class="encs">[at once;quickly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">趣执之，无使得遁。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:趣装(速整行装);趣袭(急袭，快速攻击)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">qù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>趣</strong> <span class="dicpy">qù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>(形声。从走，取声。本义:急，赶快) 同本义 <span
