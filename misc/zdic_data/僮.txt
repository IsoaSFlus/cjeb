						<p>● <strong>僮</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄨㄥˊ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">僮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>僮</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，童声。本义:未成年的人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[young]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">僮，未冠也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按:&ldquo;十九以下，八岁以上也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">僮子备官，而未之闻邪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为两郎僮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孰若为一郎僮耶。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与其杀是僮。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不当为僮。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:僮子(童子。指未成年的人);僮女(童女，少女);僮男(童男，少年);僮儿(男孩);僮谣(即童谣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 奴婢 <span class="encs">[servant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而</span><span class="diczx2">卓王孙</span>家僮数百人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今民卖僮者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;贾谊传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:僮使(奴婢);僮奴(奴仆);僮史(仆隶);僮役(仆役);僮牧(犹僮仆);僮指(称僮仆，奴婢);僮客(奴仆);僮娃(指童婢)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>僮</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 无知 <span class="encs">[ignorant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">僮昏不可使谋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:僮昏(昏愦无知。亦指昏愦无知的人);僮蒙(昏昧不明事理)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 低贱 <span class="encs">[humble]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:僮妇(泛称平民妇女);僮夫(犹言匹夫。指平民男子);僮讼(指民事诉讼)</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/僮奴">僮奴</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/僮仆">僮仆</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>僮</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhu&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/50EE.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】徒紅切【集韻】【韻會】徒東切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音同。【說文】未冠也。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;衞靑傳註】僮者，婢妾之總稱。【史記&middot;貨殖傳】卓王孫家僮八百人。【前漢&middot;張安世傳】僮七百人，皆有手技。【玉篇】僮幼迷荒者。【詩&middot;鄭風】狂僮之狂也且。【傳】狂行僮昏所化也。【廣雅】僮，癡也。今爲童。</p>
<p>又無知貌。【揚子&middot;太玄經】物僮然未有知。</p>
<p>又竦敬貌。【詩&middot;召南】被之僮僮，夙夜在公。</p>
<p>又縣名。【前漢&middot;地理志】徐，僮，取慮三縣名，屬臨淮郡。</p>
<p>又姓。漢交趾刺史僮尹。【鄭樵&middot;族略】卽童姓。或从人，以別其族。　【韻會】《說文》童，孥也。僮，幼也。今以僮幼字作童，童僕字作僮，相承失也。</p>
