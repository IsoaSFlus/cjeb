                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>法</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǎ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄚˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>体现统治阶段的意志，国家制定和颁布的公民必须遵守的行为规则：～办。～典。～官。～规。～律。～令。～定。～场。～理。～纪。～盲。～人（&ldquo;自然人&rdquo;的对称。指依法成立并能以自己的名义独立参与民事活动，享有民事权利和承担法律义务的社会组织）。～制。～治。犯～。守～。合～。宪～（国家的根本大法）。政～。奉公守～。逍遥～外。</li>	<li>处理事物的手段：办～。设～。手～。写～。</li>	<li>仿效：效～。</li>	<li>标准，规范，可仿效的：～式。～帖。</li>	<li>佛家的道理：佛～。～号。～轮。～像。～门。</li>	<li>道家佛家的所谓拿妖捉怪的技术：～师。～器。～事。</li>	<li>指&ldquo;法国&rdquo;：～文。～式大菜。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">法</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>法</strong></p>
<p>灋 <span class="dicpy">fǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从&ldquo;水&rdquo;，表示法律、法度公平如水;从&ldquo;廌&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span>)，即解廌，神话传说中的一种神兽，据说，它能辨别曲直，在审理案件时，它能用角去触理曲的人。基本义:刑法;法律;法度)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同基本义 <span class="encs">[act;law passed by legislative body;penal law;law]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灋，刑也。平之如水。从水，廌所以触不直者去之，从去，会意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利用刑人，以正法也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;蒙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟作五虐之刑曰法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">法者，刑罚也。所以禁强暴也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;诏圣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杀戮禁诛谓之法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;心术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">礼者禁于将然之前，而法者禁于已然之后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">失期，法当斩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故治国无法则乱，守法而弗度则悖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:合法;犯法;法禁(刑法和禁令)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 法令;规章;制度 <span class="encs">[rules and regulations]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汉法令非行也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《治安策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:法日(制度规定的日子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 标准;模式 <span class="encs">[norm;standard;model]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">匹夫而为百世师，一言而为天下法。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《潮州韩文公庙碑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:法戒(楷式和监戒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 方法;办法 <span class="encs">[method]</span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/704B_3635.svg" /><span class="swbf">(灋)</span><span class="ywyy">㓝也。</span><span class="swdz">㓝者，罰辠也。易曰：利用㓝人，以正法也。引伸爲凡模笵之偁。木部曰：模者，法也。竹部曰：笵者，法也。土部曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21312.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡌒</span>者，鑄器之法也。</span><span class="ywyy">平之如水。从水。</span><span class="swdz">説从水之意。張釋之曰：廷尉，天下之平也。</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22281.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢊁</span>所㠯觸不直者去之。从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22281.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢊁</span>去。</span><span class="swdz">下<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22281.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢊁</span>字今依韵會補。此説从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22281.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢊁</span>去之意。法之正人，如<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22281.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢊁</span>之去惡也。方乏切。八部。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/704B_3636.svg" /><span class="swbf">(法)</span><span class="ywyy">今文省。</span><span class="swdz">許書無言今文者，此葢隸省之字，許書本無，或增之也。如艸部本有㪿無折。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/704B_3637.svg" /><span class="swbf">(佱)</span><span class="ywyy">古文。</span></p>                          </div>
