                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>衔</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">銜</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xián  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xián.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>马嚼子。</li><li>用嘴含，用嘴叼：～枚。燕子～泥。</li><li>含在心里：～恨。～冤。～恤（含哀，怀着忧伤）。～戢（敛藏于心，表示衷心感激）。</li><li>接受，奉：～命。</li><li>指前后相接：～接。</li><li>职务和级别的名号：学～。军～。官～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bit; hold in mouth, bite; gag</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> (amtlicher,militärischer)​ Rang oder Titel (S)​,Trense (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tenir dans la bouche (aux lèvres)​,titre,grade</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>衔</strong></p><p>銜、啣 <span class="dicpy">xián</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xián.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 口含 <span class="encs">[hold in the mouth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蟾蜍衔之。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:燕子衔泥;衔环相报(衔环);衔刀(口中叼刀);衔土(口含泥土);衔口垫背(古代一种殓葬习俗:给死人口含珠、玉或米。死尸褥下放钱)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 怀藏 <span class="encs">[harbor;cherish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衔之次骨。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 如:衔恩(感恩);衔悔(怀有悔恨之心);衔匿(深藏在心里);衔恤(心怀忧伤);衔哀(心怀哀痛)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 奉接，接受 <span class="encs">[accept]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">贾昌</span>衔保家活命之恩，无从报效。——<span class="smcs">《醒世恒言》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:衔命(奉行命令)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 把两物互相接起来或缚住 <span class="encs">[connect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且把粮车首尾衔，权做寨栅。——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 包含，蕴含 <span class="encs">[cherish;contain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">衔远山。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/衔恨">衔恨</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/衔接">衔接</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/衔枚">衔枚</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/衔头">衔头</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/衔尾">衔尾</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/衔尾相随">衔尾相随</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/衔冤">衔冤</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/929C.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】戸監切【集韻】【韻會】乎監切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音咸。【說文】馬勒口中。从金从行。銜，行馬者也。【徐曰】馬銜所以制之行也。會意。【戰國策】伏軾撙銜，橫歷天下。【前漢·張敞傳】利其銜策。</p><p>又【正字通】凡口含物曰銜。【詩·豳風·勿士行枚箋】初無行<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>銜枚之事。【東京夢華錄】車駕登宣德樓，下赦，旋立雞竿，竿尖大木盤上有金雞，口銜紅幡子，書皇帝萬歲字。【盧照隣詩】龍銜寶蓋迎朝日。</p><p>又【正字通】奉君命而行曰銜命。</p><p>又官吏階位曰銜。【語林】近代選曹補授，先具舊官於前，次書擬官於後，新舊相銜也。【白居易·贈張籍詩】獨有詠詩張大祝，十年不攺舊官銜。【陸遊詩】頭銜字字敵冰淸。</p><p>又感也。【管子·法法篇】法立而民樂之，令出而民銜之。【林景熙詩】心銜造化仁。</p><p>又憾也。【前漢·外戚傳】栗姬怒不應，言不遜，景帝心銜之。【唐書·薛藝傳】藝頻爲李景所辱，深銜之。</p><p>又人參，一名人銜。【本草】其成有階級，故名。【蘇軾詩】舊聞人銜芝，生此羊腸嶺。</p><p>又馬銜，海神也。【木華·海賦】海童邀路，馬銜當蹊。【註】海童，馬銜，皆神名。馬銜，馬首，一角，龍形。</p>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">馬勒口中也。</span>也當作者。革部曰。勒、馬頭落銜也。落謂絡其頭。銜謂關其口。統謂之勒也。其在口中者謂之銜。落以<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2929A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩊚</span>爲之。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2929A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩊚</span>、生革也。銜以鐵爲之。故其字从金。引申爲凡口含之用。<span class="jieshi">从金行。</span>會意。戶監切。葢金亦聲。在七部。<span class="jieshi">銜者、所㠯行馬者也。</span>所以字今補。凡馬提控其銜以制其行止。此釋从行之意。                          </div>
