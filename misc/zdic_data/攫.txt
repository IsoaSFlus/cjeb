                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>攫</strong> <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，矍( <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:鸟用爪迅速抓取)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[snatch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">攫，执也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸷虫攫搏。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·儒行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盗跖弗攫。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李斯传》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“凡鸟翼击物曰搏，足取曰攫。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸟攫其肉。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·黄霸传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:攫取(抓取;拿取);攫捷(抓取;猎获);攫啄(以爪抓取，以嘴啄食);攫搏(鸟兽以爪翅猎物);攫噬(抓取而吞噬)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 夺取 <span class="encs">[grab;seize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">备兽触攫。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·兽人》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乳犬攫虎。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·庄公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左手攫之，而右手废。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·让王》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:攫攘(争夺);攫窃(窃夺);攫昼(白昼抢劫);攫盗(盗取，窃取)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/攫夺">攫夺</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>攫</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄩㄝˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jué <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3483.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">鸟兽用爪捕取猎物。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「老鹰<b>攫</b>鸡」、「饿虎<b>攫</b>羊」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷八九·循吏传·黄霸传》：「吏出，不敢舍邮亭，食于道旁，乌<b>攫</b>其肉。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">扑取、夺取。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>攫</b>搏」、「<b>攫</b>为己有」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《庄子·让王》：「左手<b>攫</b>之则右手废，右手<b>攫</b>之则左手废。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·柳宗元〈愚溪对〉：「见交趾宝货之多，光溢于目，思以两手左右<b>攫</b>而怀之。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
