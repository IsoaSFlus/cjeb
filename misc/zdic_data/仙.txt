                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">仙</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>仙</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>神话中称有特殊能力、可以长生不死的人：～人。～女。～子。～界。～境（①仙人居住的地方； ②形容景物美好的地方）。～风道骨。～山琼阁（喻虚无缥缈的美妙幻景）。</li>
	<li>具有高超才能的人：诗～。酒～。</li>
	<li>婉称死：～去。～逝。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> Taoist super-being, transcendent, immortal</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Unsterblicher  (S)​,Cent  (S, Wirtsch)​,Xian  (Eig, Fam)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">仙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>仙</strong></p>
<p>僊 <span class="dicpy">xiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。本作&ldquo;僊&rdquo;。右边的意思是人爬到高处取鸟巢，加上&ldquo;人&rdquo;旁，表示人升高成仙。隶书作&ldquo;仙&rdquo;，表示仙人多住在高处。本义:仙人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[celestial being;immortal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">僊，长生迁去也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求僊人羡门之属。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;郊祀志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仙圣为之臣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;黄帝》</span>。注:&ldquo;仙寿考之迹。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 神仙，古代神话和宗教中指修炼得道长生不死的人，或指能达到至高神界的人物</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山不在高，有仙则名。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仙之人兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如听仙乐。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仙及鸡犬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:仙机玄奥(深奥不可知晓);仙人劝驾(指有威望的人从中调解);仙醪(仙酒);仙闼(神仙的住处);仙机(奥秘);仙车(驾临);仙缘(可成仙的缘分)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 超越凡品的人或事。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:自称臣是酒中仙;诗仙;歌仙;仙郎;仙卫;仙手</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用于称道教的人和事物。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:仙箓(道仙的名册);仙翁(对老年道士的尊称);仙长(对道士的尊称);仙师(对道士、和尚的尊称)</span>&lt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 仙女 <span class="encs">[fairy]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 民间传说故事中住在仙境(有时下凡)但又常善意地干预人间事务的年轻貌美的仙女。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:仙姿</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 中国古代用为艳妇、美女、妓女、女道士等的代称。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:怀仙;梦仙诗;仙妓</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 旧时对美分(百分之一美元)音译名生脱的简称，现在香港、台湾仍用;香港货币的分亦称&ldquo;仙&rdquo; <span class="encs">[cent]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 用于死或死者的婉词 <span class="encs">[dead]</span>。</p>
