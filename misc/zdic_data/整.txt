                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>整</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhěng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhěng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>有秩序，不乱：～齐。～洁。～然有序。</li><li>治理：～治。～改。～编。～饬（ａ．使有条理，整顿；ｂ．整齐，有条理）。～装待发。</li><li>修理，修饰：～形。～旧如新。</li><li>完全无缺，没有零头：～体。完～。</li><li>使人吃苦头：～人。</li><li>搞，弄：咋～的？</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">整</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>整</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhěng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)从束从正，正亦声。攴是敲打，束是约束，使之归于正。合起来表示整齐。本义:整齐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[neat;tidy;in good order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">整，齐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">整设于屏外。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“整，正列也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以乱易整，不武。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:整娖(整齐，条理分明);整立(整齐地矗立);整扮(打扮整齐);整设(整齐阵列);整蔚(整齐而华丽)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 端正，端庄 <span class="encs">[regular]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">王夷甫</span>容貌整丽，妙于谈玄。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:整心(正心，端正思想);整秀(端庄俊秀);整身(身体端正，穿戴整齐);整美(端正美好)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 整个;完整;全部在内 <span class="encs">[whole;complete;total;entire]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小姐越发闷上加闷，整日眉头不展。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:十二点整;整夜不眠;整年;整月;整一(统一)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>整</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhěng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 整理;整治 <span class="encs">[put in order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">整法度。(整顿法令制度。)——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">整羁鞘。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:整旅(整顿军队);整比(整理排比);整弁(整冠);整休(一面整顿，一面休息);整次(整理编次);整抖(整理);整束(整理收拾);整兵(整饬军队)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 修补，修理 <span class="encs">[mend;renovate;repair]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">庆基殿</span>如的有损漏，只令三司差官整补，不得理为劳绩。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span
