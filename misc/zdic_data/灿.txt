                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>灿</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">燦</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">càn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/càn.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>〔～烂〕光彩，耀眼，如“阳光～～”。亦简称“灿”，如“～若晨星”。</li><li>（<span class="diczx3">燦</span>）</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> vivid, illuminating; bright</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> brillant,étincelant</p></div>
