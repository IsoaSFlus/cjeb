						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>泄</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˋ</span></p><ol><li>液体或气体排出：排～。～洪。～泻。</li><li>漏，露：～劲。～露。～漏。～密。</li><li>尽量发出（情绪、情欲等）：～愤。～恨。发～。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>泄</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 〔～～〕ａ．缓飞的样子，如“雄雉于飞，～～其羽”；ｂ．闲散自得，如“十亩外兮，桑者～～兮”；ｃ．弛缓的样子，如“天之方噘，无然～～”。</p></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>泄</strong></p><p>洩 <span class="dicpy">xiè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，世声。本义:泄水。在江西)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“歇”</span>。发泄;发散 <span class="encs">[divulge;give away;let out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">运物之泄也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·山木》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“发也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穷岫泄云。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹出也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宗气泄也。——<span class="smcs">《素问·平人气象论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓发泄也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">精泄于目。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惠此中国，俾民忧泄。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·民劳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蓄泄数千载。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《历阳壮士勤将军名思齐歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:泄宣(宣泄，宣露);泄冤(表白或洗雪冤屈);泄写(宣泄，倾吐。写，同<span class="yhcs">“泻”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 排出 <span class="encs">[discharge;release;drain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">振河海而不洩。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泻出于两峰之间。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:泄洪;泄水道;泄水坝;泄水闸</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 腹泻 <span class="encs">[have diarrhoea]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">金郁泄之。——<span class="smcs">《素问·六元正经大论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓渗泄也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:泄溺(大小便);泄下(水泻，腹泻);泄利(泄痢。水泻;痢疾。利，通<span
