                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>轮</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">輪</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄣˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>安在车轴上可以转动使车行进的圆形的东西（亦称&ldquo;车轱辘&rdquo;）：～子。车～。～胎。</li>
	<li>安在机器上能旋转并促使机器动作的东西：齿～儿。</li>
	<li>指&ldquo;轮船&rdquo;：江～。拖～。</li>
	<li>像车轮的：日～。月～（指圆月）。年～。</li>
	<li>依次更替：～班。～训。～休。～作。</li>
	<li>转动：间或一～。</li>
	<li>量词：一～红日。头～影院。他比我大一～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wheel; revolve, turn; recur</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dampfer  (S)​,Durchgang  (S, Sport)​,Rad  (S)​,Ring  (S)​,Runde  (S)​,Turnus  (S)​,drehen  (V)​,rotieren  (V)​,Runde  (Zähl)​,Scheibe  (Zähl)​,Lun  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> roue,disque,alterner,(classificateur pour le soleil et la lune)​,(classificateur pour les événements et phases)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">轮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>轮</strong></p>
<p>輪 <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，仑( <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:车轮，轮子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wheel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轮，有辐曰轮，无辐曰辁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，考工有轮人。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坎为弓轮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">輮以为轮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金车玉作轮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:轮指(弹奏乐器的一种指法:一手的几个手指接连动作，犹如车轮的转动);轮轭(车轮和车轭);轮崇(车轮的高度)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为车子 <span class="encs">[carriage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">椎轮抵</span><span class="diczx2">长乐</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">梁</span><span class="diczx2">简文帝</span><span class="smcs">《长安道》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:轮轩(显贵者所乘坐的车子);轮鞅(车马。比喻访客);轮盖(车盖)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 凡平圆形像车轮似的东西 <span class="encs">[sth.resembling a wheel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未央前殿月轮高。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王昌龄</span><span class="smcs">《春宫曲》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叶轮;花轮;月轮;收卷钓丝的转轮</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 树的年龄 <span class="encs">[ring]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:年轮</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 轮船的省称 <span class="encs">[steamer]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:江轮;轮舟(轮船);轮舶(即轮船)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指月亮 <span class="encs">[moon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:轮彩(指月亮);轮辉(指月光或日光)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 边缘，外围 <span class="encs">[edge]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:轮周(圆周);轮廓(指钱的内外边缘)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 制作车轮的工匠 <span class="encs">[craftsman]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梓、匠、轮、舆，能与人规矩，不能使人巧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;尽心下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:轮人(制作车轮的工匠);轮舆(轮人和舆人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>轮</strong></p>
<p>輪 <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 高大的样子 <span class="encs">[tall and big]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宫阙方轮焕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《和望晓》</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下" target="_blank">酉集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集下&amp;bs=車" target="_blank">車</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8F2A.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】力迍切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】龍春切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音倫。【廣韻】車輪也。【說文】有輻曰輪，無輻曰輇。【釋名】輪，綸也。言瀰綸周匝也。【周禮·冬官考工記】察車自輪始。</p><p>又輪人，車官名。【周禮·冬官考工記】輪人爲輪。</p><p>又廣輪，地形縱橫也。【周禮·地官·大司徒】周知九州之地域廣輪之數。【註】東西爲廣，南北爲輪。</p><p>又大也。【禮·檀弓】美哉輪焉。【註】言輪囷高大也。</p><p>又【史記·鄒陽傳】輪囷離佹。【註】委曲盤戾也。</p><p>又輪轉，廻旋也。</p><p>又輪氏，地名。</p><p>又叶閭員切，音憐。【陸機·羽扇賦】夫創始者恆樸而飾，終者必姸。是故烹飪起于熱石，玉輅基于椎輪。</p><p>又叶良愼切，音吝。【王濬·平吳詩】馬怠其銜，車曳其輪。飛龍旣造，天命有晉。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8F2A_6721.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(輪)</span><span class="ywyy">有輻曰輪。</span><span class="swdz">云有輻者、對無輻而言也。輪之言倫也。从侖。侖、理也。三十輻㒳㒳相當而不迆。故曰輪。</span><span class="ywyy">無輻曰輇。</span><span class="swdz">鄭注周禮蜃車云。禮記或作槫。或作輇。注士喪禮曰。載柩車。周禮謂之蜃車。襍記謂之團。或作輇。或作槫。聲讀皆相同耳。未聞其正。注襍記大夫載以輲車云。輲讀爲輇。或作槫。注喪大記士葬用國車云。輇字或作團。是以又誤爲國。輇車、柩車也。又士喪禮注云。其車之轝狀如牀。中央有轅前後出。設前後輅。轝上有四周。下則前後有軸。以輇爲輪。按鄭於禮經、襍記㒳引許叔重說文解字曰有、輻曰輪。無輻曰輇。而以輲蜃團槫皆卽輇字。但許不言喪車無輻也。戴先生曰。輇者、輪之名。輲者、車之名。不宜溷而一之。注喪大記改輴爲輇。亦誤。詳見釋車。</span><span class="ywyy">从車。侖聲。</span><span class="swdz">力屯切。十三部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
