                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">酤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>酤</strong> <span class="dicpy">gū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)，古声。从&ldquo;酉&rdquo;，表示与酒有关。本义:一夜酿成的酒)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wine made within one night]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酤，一宿酒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:清酤(一夜酿成的美酒);酤酿(酿酒出售)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指酒 <span class="encs">[wine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既载清酤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;烈祖》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:清酤(薄酒)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>酤</strong> <span class="dicpy">gū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 买，买酒 <span class="encs">[buy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无酒酤我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;伐木》</span>。注:&ldquo;酤，一说，一宿酒，即速熟之酒。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">高祖</span>每酤留饮，酒讐数倍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酒酤于市，果止于梨、栗、枣、柿之类。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酤榷(官府专卖酒类。酤:通&ldquo;沽&rdquo;。买酒或卖酒);酤酒(买酒或卖酒);酤买(买酒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为谋取 <span class="encs">[seek for]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:酤名(谋取名誉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 卖酒 <span class="encs">[sell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出于屠酤之肆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周丘</span>者， <span class="diczx2">下邳</span>人也，亡命 <span class="diczx2">吴</span>，酤酒无行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;吴王濞传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>人有酤酒者，为器甚洁清，置表甚长，而酒酸不售。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋&middot;问上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:酤坊(酒店);酤家(酒家，酒店);酤肆(酒肆;酒店);酤卖(出卖酒类等)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">賣酒。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷一一七&middot;司馬相如傳》：「盡賣其車騎，買一酒舍酤酒，而令文君當鑪。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《資治通鑑&middot;卷一六&middot;漢紀八&middot;景帝中二年》：「夏，四月，地震。旱，禁酤酒。」</span><span class="gc_yy">元&middot;胡三省&middot;注：「<b>酤</b>，謂賣酒也。」</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9164.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古乎切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】攻乎切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音孤。【說文】一宿酒也。【徐曰】謂造之一夜而熟，若今雞鳴酒也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】賣酒也。【淮南子&middot;氾論訓】出於屠酤之肆。【史記&middot;吳王濞傳】周丘者，下邳人，亡命吳，酤酒無行。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】初榷酤。【註】以木渡水曰榷，謂禁民酤釀，獨官開置，如道路設木爲榷，獨取利也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】一曰買酒。【詩&middot;小雅】無酒酤我。【傳】酤，一宿酒也。【箋】酤，買也。【史記&middot;高祖紀】高祖每酤留飮酒讐數倍。</p>
<p>又通作沽。【論語】沽酒市脯不食。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【正韻】侯古切【集韻】【韻會】後五切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音戸。【廣韻】一宿酒。【詩&middot;商頌】旣載淸酤。【傳】酤，酒也。【釋文】音戸。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】果五切【正韻】公土切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音古。【集韻】一宿酒。【韻會】又賣酒也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古慕切，音顧。【廣韻】賣也。【集韻】略也。</p>
