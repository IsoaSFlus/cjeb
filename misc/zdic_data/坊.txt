                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>坊</strong> <span class="dicpy">fāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，方声。本义:城市中街市里巷的通称)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代把一个城邑划分为若干区，通称为坊 <span class="encs">[lane]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两京及州县之郭内为坊，郊外为村。——<span class="smcs">《唐元典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坊隅众人，慌忙拢来。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:坊隅(街巷，街坊);坊厢(古代城市区划，城中曰坊，近城曰厢);坊市(街市);坊郭(城郭市街)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 官署名 <span class="encs">[office]</span>。隋代太子官署有左右坊、门下坊、典书坊等，唐以后易为太子左春坊、右春坊</p><p><span class="diczx1">明年春，大疫，为病坊，处疾病之无归者。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">曾巩</span><span class="smcs">《越州赵公救灾记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:坊局(詹事府属下的左、右春坊和司经局的合称)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 牌坊 <span class="encs">[memorial archway]</span>。如:三元坊;贞节坊</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 铸造器物的土模 <span class="encs">[earthy mold]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 店铺 <span class="encs">[shop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茶坊每五更点灯博易。——</span><span class="diczx2">孟元老</span><span class="smcs">《东京孟华录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:坊肆(商店);坊店(店铺);坊贾(书商)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 别屋;专用的房舍 <span class="encs">[another room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">里边有十数家，都是开坊子吃衣饭的。——<span class="smcs">《金瓶梅词话》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:坊子(妓院;私娼的屋子);坊陌(妓女居所名);坊曲(唐代妓女居住的地方。也指小街曲巷)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Fang prefecture]</span> 在今陕西省黄陵县</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">fáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/坊本">坊本</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/坊巷">坊巷</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>坊</strong> <span class="dicpy">fáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a
