<ol>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">舂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>舂</strong> <span class="dicpy">chōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 把东西放在石臼或钵里捣去皮壳或捣碎 <span class="encs">[pound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舂，捣粟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">城曰舂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;明帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水舂河漕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;西羌传》</span>。注:&ldquo;即水碓也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:舂堂(又叫舂塘。舂谷的木槽);舂谷(舂捣谷物);舂揄(指舂米。捣米于臼曰舂，自臼取出曰揄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;冲&rdquo;。冲击 <span class="encs">[rush]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舂其喉以戈，杀之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;鲁周公世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舂，撞也。以舂筑地为节也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:舂击(冲击);舂撞(冲撞;冲击)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>舂</strong> <span class="dicpy">chōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 臼 <span class="encs">[mortar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刚勇有力，能举石舂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代因犯罪或被俘等成为舂膳的奴隶 <span class="encs">[pounding slave]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:舂市(古代舂人所役女犯劳作之处);舂槁(又叫舂槁。 <span class="diczx2">周</span>代舂人、槁人的合称。职掌罪人服苦役之事)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">舂</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下" target="_blank">未集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下&amp;bs=臼" target="_blank">臼</a>】
                              舂·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8202.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>書容切，音摏。【說文】擣粟也。黃帝臣雍父作舂。【詩&middot;大雅】誕我祀如何，或舂或揄。【莊子&middot;逍遙遊】適百里塡宿舂糧。【百里奚&middot;妻扊扅歌】舂黃藜，搤伏雞。</p>
<p>又官名。【周禮&middot;地官】舂人掌共米物。</p>
<p>又樂器。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;笙師註】舂牘以竹，大五六寸，長七尺，短者一二尺。其端有兩空髤畫，以兩手築地。【釋名】舂，撞也。牘，筑也。以舂築地爲節也。</p>
<p>又縣名。【前漢&middot;地理志】南陽郡舂陵縣。【後漢&middot;光武紀】舂陵節侯買。【註】舂陵，鄕名，本屬零陵，在今永州唐興縣北。</p>
<p>又山名。【淮南子&middot;天文訓】日至虞淵，是謂高舂。至于連石，是謂下舂。</p>
<p>又鳥名。【爾雅&middot;釋鳥】鷺，舂鉏。【疏】齊魯之閒，謂之舂鉏。</p>
<p>又【博雅】獨舂，䳚瞗也。</p>
<p>又舂容。【禮&middot;樂記】善待問者如撞鐘，待其舂容，然後盡其聲。【韓愈&middot;送權秀才序】寂寥乎短章，舂容乎大篇。</p>
<p>又與衝通。【史記&middot;魯世家】獲長狄，富父終生舂其喉以戈殺之。【後漢&middot;西羌傳】水舂河漕。</p>
<p>又【正字通】音窻。八蠻之類。一曰旁舂。見墨子。</p>
<p>又【集韻】諸容切【韻會】職容切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音鍾。荆山別名。【張正見&middot;白頭吟】彈珠金市側，抵玉舂山東。</p>
