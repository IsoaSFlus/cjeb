                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>魑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chī.mp3"></a></span> ㄔˉ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔～魅〕传说中指山林里能害人的怪物，如“～～魍魉”（指各种各样的坏人）。</p></ol>
