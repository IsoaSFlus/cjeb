                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>戾</strong> <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从犬，从户。犬从关着的门中挤出，必曲其身。本义:弯曲)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戾，曲也。从犬，出户下身曲戾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饮必小咽，端直无戾。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:戾契(曲折辗转)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 至，到达 <span class="encs">[come to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翰飞戾天。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·四月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">鲁侯</span>戾止。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·泮水》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戾于敝邑。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">古者天灾降戾。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸢飞戾天者。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:戾止(来临);戾于(至于)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 违背，违反 <span class="encs">[violate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如子之说，文虽相反，义不相戾。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《论语笔解》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举事戾苍天。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·览冥训》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 破裂 ［break;split］</p><p><span class="diczx1">曲得其宜，无所击戾。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·主术训》</span>。注：<span class="yhcs">“戾，破也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“捩”</span>。扭转 <span class="encs">[reverse;turn back;turn round]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戾翳旋把，萦随所历。——</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《射雉赋》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>戾</strong> <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 乖张;违逆 <span class="encs">[perverse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戾，乖背也。——<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">降此大戾。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·节南山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又仰而呼天曰:<span class="yhcs">“何为使至此极戾也?——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《天说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">果敢而很，猛贪而戾。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·荣辱》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:乖戾(别扭，不合情理);戾沓(乖戾多言);戾悖(乖戾背离);戾谬(乖谬)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 暴戾 <span class="encs">[ruthless and tyrannical]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诛严不为戾。(戾残暴。)——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
