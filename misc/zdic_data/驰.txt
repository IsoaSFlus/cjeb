                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>驰</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">馳</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>车马等奔跑，快跑：～驱。～骋。风～电掣。</li>    <li>向往：神～。心～神往。～念。～思。</li>    <li>传播，传扬：～名。～誉。</li>    <li>驱车马追逐：“齐师败绩，公将～之”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> go quickly or swiftly; hurry</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Geschwindigkeit  (S)​,Tempo  (S)​,schnell rennnen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> galoper,passer à toute vitesse</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">驰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>驰</strong></p><p>馳 <span class="dicpy">chí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><动</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，也声。本义:车马疾行)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[speed;gallop]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驰，大驱也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弗驰弗驱。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·山有枢》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">车驰卒奔。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">好驰马试剑。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啬夫驰。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">项伯</span>乃夜驰之 <span class="diczx2">沛公</span>军。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又驱使</p><p><span class="diczx1">公诚能驰一介之使，加咫尺之书，则</span><span class="diczx2">孝章</span>可致，友道可弘矣。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">孔融</span><span class="smcs">《论盛孝章书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:驰车(古代战场上快速奔走的马车、轻车;驾车行走);驰马(骑马快跑);驰道(为帝王行驶马车所筑的马路);驰报(疾驰传报);驰夫(骑马送信的差役)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 疾行 <span class="encs">[run;rush to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每冒风驰行，未百步辄反。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山舞银蛇，原驰蜡象。——</span><span class="diczx2">毛泽东</span><span class="smcs">《沁园春·雪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:风驰电掣;驰说(往来劝说);驰求(奔走追求);驰兵(迅速进兵)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 追逐 <span class="encs">[pursue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>师败绩，公将驰之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公十年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:驰竞(追逐，竞争)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 向往 <span class="encs">[look forward to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抑志而弥节兮，神高驰之邈邈。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身在边隅，情驰</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>阙。——<span class="smcs">《隋书·史祥传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:驰结(挂念，想念);驰义(向往忠义而奔驰来归;归义，臣服)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 消逝迅速 <span class="encs">[pass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">年与时驰，意与日去。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《诫子书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:驰年(快速流逝的岁月);驰晖(疾驰的日光)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 传播 <span class="encs">[spread]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">名驰三江外，峻节贯云霄。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/99B3.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27093.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧂓</span>【廣韻】直離切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】陳知切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音池。【玉篇】走奔也。【廣韻】馳騖也，疾驅也。【左傳·莊十年】公將馳之。</p><p>又【玉篇】天子道也。【正字通】馳道，天子所行路，若今之中道。</p><p>又姓。【姓苑】明馳九垓，仁壽縣舉人。</p><p>又【集韻】唐何切，音駝。亦走也。</p><p>又叶徒臥切，音隋。【詩·小雅】不失其馳，舍矢如破。</p><p>又叶尺里切，音恥。【劉向·九歎】周流覽於四海兮，志升降以高馳。徵九神於回極兮，建虹采以招指。</p><p>又叶直詈切，音滯。【蔡邕·彈棊賦】於是列象，彫華逞麗。豐腹斂邊，中隱四企。輕利調博，易使騁馳。</p>
