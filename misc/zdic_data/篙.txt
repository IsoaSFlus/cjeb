                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>篙</strong> <span class="dicpy">gāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，高声。从<span class="yhcs">“竹”</span>，表示与竹有关。本义:撑船的竿) 同本义 <span class="encs">[punt-pole]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尝发所在竹篙，有一官长连根取之，仍当足，乃超两阶用之。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·政事》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自忆</span><span class="diczx2">东吴</span>榜舟日，蓼花沟水半篙强。—— <span class="diczx2">李郢</span><span class="smcs">《山行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大家跳下船，双喜拔前篙，阿发拔后篙。——<span class="smcs">《社戏》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:篙眼(篙痕。篙在岸上留下的孔穴);篙楫(篙桨等行船的工具);杉篙(杉木撑船篙);篙橹(撑船摇船的工具);篙痕(篙撑船时留下的痕迹)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>篙</strong> <span class="dicpy">gāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>用篙撑船 <span class="encs">[push with a punt-pole and cause a ship to move]</span>。如:篙手(掌篙的船工);篙律(撑船的方法)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/篙竿，篙杆">篙竿，篙杆</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/篙头">篙头</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/篙子">篙子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
