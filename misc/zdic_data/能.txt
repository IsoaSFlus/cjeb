                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>能</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>才干，本事：～力。～耐。才～。</li>
	<li>有才干的：～人。～手。贤～。～工巧匠。～者为师。</li>
	<li>胜任，善于：～够。～柔～刚。力所～及。欲罢不～。～动。</li>
	<li>会（表示可能性）：小弟弟～走路了。</li>
	<li>应该：你不～这样说他。</li>
	<li>物理学名词，&ldquo;能量&rdquo;的简称：电～。热～。</li>
	<li>和睦：&ldquo;（萧）何素不与曹参相～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>传说中的一种兽，似熊。</li>
	<li>古代称一种三足鳖。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>能</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/耐">耐</a>&rdquo;，受得住。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">能</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>能</strong> <span class="dicpy">n&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文作字形象熊形。本义:熊)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 传说中的一种像熊的兽 <span class="encs">[bear-like beast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能，熊属。足似鹿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梦黄能入于寝门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公七年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">贾</span>注:&ldquo;兽也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">近者三奸悉破碎，羽窟无底幽黄能。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《忆昨行和张十一》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 才能，能力 <span class="encs">[ability]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">各奏尔能。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;宾之初筵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无异能者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">募有能捕之者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有千里之能。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">表恶其能。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:能处(本事;能耐);无能(没有能力);低能(能力低下);逞能(显示自己能干);各尽所能;能解(犹能耐，才能)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 有才能的人 <span class="encs">[a talented person]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将能而君不能御。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;谋攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贤能为之用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">智能之士。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼能且众。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>能</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄋㄥˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>n&eacute;ng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1630.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">傳說中的動物。如熊，足似鹿。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;<b>能</b>部》：「<b>能</b>，熊屬，足似鹿。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈憶昨行和張十一〉詩：「近者三姦悉破碎，羽窟無底幽黃<b>能</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">能力、才幹。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「逞<b>能</b>」、「智<b>能</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;子罕》：「夫子聖者與？何其多<b>能</b>也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;沈約&middot;宋書謝靈運傳論》：「並摽<b>能</b>擅美，獨映當時。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人才、有才能的人。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;禮運》：「選賢與<b>能</b>，講信脩睦。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;司馬遷&middot;報任少卿書》：「招賢進<b>能</b>，顯巖穴之士。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用途、功用。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;柳宗元〈愚溪詩序〉：「可以染也，名之以其<b>能</b>，故謂之染溪。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一種日本古典戲劇。由猿樂發展的歌舞劇，以動作、表情和音樂表現。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「夢幻<b>能</b>」、「修羅<b>能</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">物質運動的能量。因運動形式不同而有電能、熱能、機械能、化學能、原子能等。能量間可互相轉換，以功率為計算單位。</span><span class="gc_yx">也稱為「能量」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">有才幹的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「能人」、「能者多勞」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子&middot;公孫丑上》：「賢者在位，<b>能</b>者在職。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">投契、睦。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;大雅&middot;民勞》：「柔遠能邇，以定我王。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷五三&middot;蕭相國世家》：「何素不與曹參相<b>能</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">及、到。</span><span class="gc_yy">《戰國策&middot;趙策一》：「韓與秦接境壤界，其地不<b>能</b>千里。」</span></p></li>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/80FD.svg">                          <p>熊屬。足似鹿。从肉㠯聲。能獸堅中，故稱賢能；而彊壯，稱能傑也。凡能之屬皆从能。奴登切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：㠯非聲。疑皆象形。文一</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/80FD_5263.svg" /><span class="swbf">(能)</span><span class="ywyy">熊屬。</span><span class="swdz">左傳、國語皆云晉侯夢黃能入於寝門。韋注曰：能似熊。凡左傳、國語能作熊者，皆淺人所改也。</span><span class="ywyy">足佀鹿。</span><span class="swdz">故皆从比也。㲋足、彘足亦同。</span><span class="ywyy">从肉，</span><span class="swdz">猶龍之从肉也。</span><span class="ywyy">㠯聲。</span><span class="swdz">奴登切，古者在一部，由之而入於咍則爲奴來切，由一部而入於六部則爲奴登切，其義則一也。</span><span class="ywyy">能獸堅中，故偁賢能。</span><span class="swdz">賢，古文作臤。臤，堅也。</span><span class="ywyy">而彊壯，偁能傑也。</span><span class="swdz">此四句發明叚借之恉。賢能，能傑之義行而本義幾廢矣。子下曰：十一月陽氣動。萬物滋，人以爲偁。亦此例也。韋朋來西烏五篆下說解皆此例。</span><span class="ywyy">凡能之屬皆从能。</span></p>                          </div>
