                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蔗</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蔗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄜˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔甘～〕多年生草本植物，茎直立，有节，含甜汁很多，可生吃，亦可制糖。副产品糖蜜可酿酒、制酒精。简称“蔗”，如“～农”。“～糖”。“～渣”。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sugar cane</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Zuckerrohr  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> canne à sucre</p></div>
