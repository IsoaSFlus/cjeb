                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>璧</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>平圆形中间有孔的玉，古代在典礼时用作礼器，亦可作饰物。</li>    <li>美玉的通称：～人（即“玉人”，指容貌秀美的人）。～日（像璧玉一样圆而亮的太阳）。～月。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>璧</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从玉，辟声。本义:平而圆，中心有孔的玉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a round flat piece of jade witha hole in it]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">璧，瑞玉环也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肉倍好谓之璧。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释器》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“肉边好孔。玉人，璧好三寸。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">璧者，方中圆外。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宏璧。——<span class="smcs">《书·顾命》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“大璧，度尺二寸。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以苍璧礼天。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如圭如璧。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·淇奥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愿以十五城请易璧。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">相如</span>奉璧奏 <span class="diczx2">秦王</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:璧不可以防寒(比喻物品虽然贵重，但无实际的用处);璧雍(璧堂。古代天子所设的太学。外形好像圆璧，周围以水绕之)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 玉的通称 <span class="encs">[jade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柱以白璧，砌以青玉。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:璧玉(上等美玉);璧散(古玉爵名。为祭祈时所用的礼器);璧人(仪容美好的人);璧门(用玉装饰的门)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 喻月亮 <span class="encs">[moon]</span>。如:璧采(比喻月光，月色)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>璧</strong> <span class="dicpy">bì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>退回赠送的礼品或归还借用之物 <span class="encs">[return]</span>。如:璧帖拜门(退还以前结拜为兄弟的蓝帖，另具门生帖子拜在门下，改做师生关系);璧回(敬词。表示退还赠礼或归还借物)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
