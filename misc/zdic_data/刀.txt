                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>刀</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄠˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用来切、割、斩、削、砍、刺、铡的工具：～子。～兵。～法。剪～。镰～。</li>	<li>中国的纸张计量单位：一刀合一百张。</li>	<li>古代的一种钱币，因其形如刀故称：～币。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> knife; old coin; measure</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span>  (<a href="./刀">刀</a>状<a href="./的">的</a>东<a href="./西">西</a>)​ messerförmiges Gerät  (S)​, (<span class="punct"><a href="./切">切</a>、</span><a href="./割">割</a><a href="./的">的</a><a href="./工具">工具</a>)​ Messer  (S)​, (<a href="./指">指</a><a href="./兵器">兵器</a>)​ (einschneidige)​ Hiebwaffe, Säbel, Schwert  (S)​,hundert Bogen (Papier)​  (Zähl)​,Dao  (Eig, Fam)​,Radikal Nr. 18 = Messer, Schwert, Hiebwaffe, Säbel (Varianten: <a href="./刁">刁</a>, 刂)​  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> couteau,sabre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">刀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刀</strong> <span class="dicpy">dāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，象刀形。汉字部首之一。本义:古代兵器名)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[knife;soldier&#39;s sword;sabre]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刀，兵也。象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刀，到也，以斩伐到其所乃击之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刀光照塞月，阵色明如昼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔国辅</span><span class="smcs">《从军行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刀枪;刀兵;刀瘢(刀伤的瘢痕)。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指用来斩、割、切、削、砍、铡的工具 <span class="encs">[knife]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中刑用刀锯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有斧钺刀墨之民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝自捉刀立床头。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;容上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妻乃引刀趋机而言曰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屠乃奔倚其下，弛担持刀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刀锯(古代的刑具。割刑用刀，刖刑用锯);刀环(刀头的环);刀墨(古黥刑。先用刀在额头上刻划，然后再染上墨);刀斧手(刽子手;又指持刀拿斧、准备捉拿后杀人的军士)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形状像刀的东西 <span class="encs">[sth.shaped like a knife]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冰刀;刀圭(指古代量取药物的小匙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 即刀币 <span class="encs">[knife money]</span>，中国的一种古钱币，其形如刀</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余刀布。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;荣辱》</span>。注:&ldquo;钱也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及金刀龟贝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓钱币也。利于刀&rdquo;。注:&ldquo;名钱为刀者，以其利于民也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刀儿(古代钱币的通称。因其形状如刀，故有此称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;舠&rdquo;。小船 <span class="encs">[boat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谁谓河广?曾不容刀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;河广》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">武器名。一種用來砍殺，頗具殺傷力的兵器。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大<b>刀</b>」、「寶<b>刀</b>」、「軍<b>刀</b>」、「武士<b>刀</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">切、削、剪、刻、割、斬的工具。多以鋼鐵製成。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「菜<b>刀</b>」、「鍘<b>刀</b>」、「剪<b>刀</b>」、「鐮<b>刀</b>」、「雕刻<b>刀</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代的錢幣。外形如刀。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷三○&middot;平準書》：「農、工、商交易之路通，而龜貝金錢刀布之幣興焉。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞：➊ ​ 計算切割次數的單位。如：「老屠戶在那塊豬肉上劃了幾刀。」➋ ​ 計算紙張的單位。一百張為一刀。如：「一刀稿紙」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如明代有刀幹孟。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">二一四部首之一。</span></p></li>
