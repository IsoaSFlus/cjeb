                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>阔</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">闊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ku&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄛˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>宽广，或指时间的长久：广～。辽～。～别。～步。高谈～论。海～天空。</li>
	<li>富有，豪奢：～气。～绰。</li>
	<li>粗疏，不细密：～略。～达。疏～。</li>
	<li>离别，分离：～情。久～。叙～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> broad, ample, wide; be apart</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> reichlich, reich  (Adj)​,weit, breit, geräumig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> large,riche</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阔</strong></p>
<p>闊、濶 <span class="dicpy">ku&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，活声。本义:阔大，很开阔)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[vast;wide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阔，疏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阔，稀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;沟洫志》</span>集注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阔，远也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缓步阔视。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;黄帝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于嗟阔兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;击鼓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阔大渊深，不可测也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;论人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">湖阔数十里。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《陪从祖济南太守泛鹊山湖》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">广厦阔屋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;齐俗训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">暮霭沈沈楚天阔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阔绝(隔绝，差别很大);阔略(疏略;粗疏);阔落(宽疏，不细密);阔寥(稀少);阔疏(粗疏);阔希(稀疏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 宽横的距离大 <span class="encs">[broad;vast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有江千里阔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《寄微之》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阔狭(广狭，宽窄);阔叶树;阔达(旷达不拘小节);阔涧(较宽的山间小溪);阔朗(宽阔明亮);阔颡(宽阔的额部);阔臆(宽阔的胸部)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 迂阔，不切实际 <span class="encs">[high-sounding and impracticable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王道迂阔而莫为。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李华</span><span class="smcs">《吊古战场文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阔论( 不切实际的大话);阔迂(拘泥而不切实际)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 侈大，荣显，富有 <span class="encs">[wealthy;rich]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何阴阳之难测，伟二仪之奓阔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;成公绥传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阔老;阔天阔地(指大手大脚，大肆挥霍);阔客(有钱客);阔拓(阔绰);阔少爷(有钱人家的子弟);阔人家(富家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 久不相见 <span class="encs">[long]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阔别稍久，眷与时长。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王羲之</span><span class="smcs">《问慰诸帖》</span></p>
