						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>歌</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gē.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄜˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>唱：～唱。～咏。～颂。～坛。～台舞榭。～舞。能～善舞。</li>
	<li>能唱的文词：唱～。～谱。～词。～诀。民～。诗～。～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）（旧诗的一种体裁，音节、格律比较自由）。诗言志，～咏言。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> song, lyrics; sing, chant; praise</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Lied  (Mus)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">歌</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>歌</strong></p>
<p>謌 <span class="dicpy">gē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从欠，哥声。从&ldquo;欠&rdquo;，表示与口有关系。本义:唱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">歌，咏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">歌者，长引其声以诵之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文系传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不鼓缶而歌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;离》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">歌永言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">歌咏其声也，长言也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲合乐曰歌，徒歌曰谣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;园有桃》</span>传</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者，舜鼓五弦，歌<span class="smcs">《南风》</span>之诗而天下治。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>狂 <span class="diczx2">接舆</span>歌而过 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;微子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃歌夫&ldquo;长铗归来&rdquo;者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如</span><span class="diczx2">优孟</span>摇头而歌，而后可以得志。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:对歌(一问一答地唱歌);高歌(放声歌唱);歌吹(歌唱，吹奏);歌鼓(歌唱并击鼓);歌戏(歌唱嬉戏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 歌颂，赞美 <span class="encs">[sing the praises of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故近者歌讴而乐之，远者竟竭蹶而趋之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;儒效》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诗人歌功。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《赵充国颂》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:歌功(颂扬他人的功德);歌思(歌颂思慕);歌叹(歌颂赞叹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 奏乐;伴奏 <span class="encs">[play music]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">歌于斯，哭于斯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">往往会倚嫔御歌，必为奇巧声动上，由是得幸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王谠</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又指<span class="encs">[禽鸟]</span>鸣叫 <span class="encs">[chirp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸟歌花舞太守醉，明日酒醒春已归。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《丰乐亭游春三首》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 作歌;作诗 <span class="encs">[poet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫也不良，歌以讯止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;陈风&middot;墓门》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因为长句，歌以赠之，凡六百一十六言，命曰<span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">岂无山歌与村笛，呕哑嘲哳难为听。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古用同&ldquo;哥&rdquo;。一指兄长;一指父亲 <span class="encs">[brother;father]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:歌歌(同&ldquo;哥哥&rdquo;。指父亲)</span></p>

	<li>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下" target="_blank">辰集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下&amp;bs=欠" target="_blank">欠</a>】
                              歌·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6B4C.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕可哥【唐韻】古俄切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】居何切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音柯。【說文】詠也。【徐曰】長引其聲以詠也。【釋名】人聲曰歌。歌者，柯也。以聲吟詠上下，如草木有柯葉也。【揚子&middot;方言】兗冀言歌，聲如柯。【書&middot;舜典】詩言志，歌永言。【正義曰】直言不足以申意，故令歌詠其詩之義以長其言。【禮&middot;樂記】詩言其志也，歌詠其聲也。【又】歌之爲言也，長言之也。言之不足，故長言之。</p>
<p>又曲合樂也。【詩&middot;魏風】我歌且謠。【傳】曲合樂曰歌，徒歌曰謠。【疏】正義曰：謠旣徒歌，則歌不徒矣，故曰曲合樂曰歌。歌謠對文如此，散則歌爲總名，未必合樂也。</p>
<p>又【韓詩&middot;章句】有章曲曰歌，無曰謠。</p>
<p>又【古樂府註】齊歌曰謳，吳歌曰歈，楚歌曰豔。奏樂曰登歌，曰升歌。</p>
<p>又鐘名。【左傳&middot;襄十一年】鄭人賂晉侯歌鐘二肆，晉侯以樂之半賜魏絳。</p>
<p>又山名。【廣輿記】歌山，在廣西平樂府富川縣。</p>
<p>又朝歌，地名，紂所都也。漢爲縣，屬河內郡。見【前漢&middot;地理志】。</p>
<p>又叶古賀切，音過。【左貴<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/218D5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡣕</span>&middot;晉元后誄】內敷隂敎，外毗陽化。綢繆庶政，密勿夙夜。恩從風翔，澤隨雨播。中外禔福，遐邇詠歌。說見【顏氏&middot;刊謬正俗】。</p>
<p>又叶居之切，音姬。【屈原&middot;遠遊】張樂咸池奏承雲兮，二女御九韶，歌，使湘靈鼓瑟兮，令海若舞馮夷。</p>
<p>又叶斤於切，音居。【柳宗元&middot;饒娥<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥓓</span>辭】鄱民哀號，或以頌歌。齊女色憂，傷槐罷誅。【說文】或作謌。【集韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/210ED.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡃭</span>。</p>
