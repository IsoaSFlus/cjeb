						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>横</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">héng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/héng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄥˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>跟地面平行的，与“<span class="diczx3">竖</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">直</span>”相对：～梁。～楣。～额。～幅。～批。～披（长条形横幅字画）。～匾。～标。～空。</li>    <li>左右向的，跟目视方向垂直的，与“<span class="diczx3">竖</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">直</span>”、“<span class="diczx3">纵</span>”相对：～写。～排。～向。～斜。纵～。</li>    <li>地理学上指东西向的：与“<span class="diczx3">纵</span>”相对：～贯东西。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>横</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hèng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hèng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄥˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>凶暴，不讲理；蛮～。强～。～暴。～蛮。</li>    <li>意外的，不寻常的：～财。～祸。～事。～死。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>横</strong> <span class="dicpy">héng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/héng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，黄声。本义:门框下部的横木)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[middle rail]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">横，闌木也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作桁。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以横为之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·陈风·横门之下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:横轼(古代车前供人凭倚的横木)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 侧，旁边 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时横隤以阳遂。——</span><span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《洞箫赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“旁决貌。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:横头(正面两侧的位置，或长方形物体较短两侧的位置);横屋(正屋前两侧的房屋);横道(旁道);横逸(犹旁出，旁行)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代负有督察记录权责的官员，可以入人于罪者 <span class="encs">[surpervisor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">下有五横，以揆其官。——<span class="smcs">《管子·君臣》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 学堂 <span class="encs">[school]</span>。如:横舍;横塾</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 汉字由左至右的笔形<span class="yhcs">“一”</span> <span class="encs">[horizontal stroke]</span>。如:王字是三横一竖</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>横</strong> <span class="dicpy">héng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/héng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 地理上指东西向。与<span class="yhcs">“纵”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[east to west]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">横田其亩。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗南山》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“东西曰横。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不别横与纵。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·沈江》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“纬曰横。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纵横间之。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·览冥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坐横弓。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射仪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“南踣弓也。"</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曰从与横。——<span class="smcs">《太玄卷·七玄莹》</span>。注:“东西曰横。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6A6B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】戸盲切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】胡盲切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音黌。【說文】闌木也。</p><p>又【唐韻】縱橫也。東西曰縱，南北曰橫。亦作從橫。【史記·蘇秦傳】合從連衡。【漢書音義】利合爲從，威權相脅爲橫。</p><p>又【呂覽·離謂篇】失從之意，失橫之事。【註】關東六國爲從，關西爲橫。一曰以六攻一爲從，以一離六爲橫。</p><p>又【楚辭·招隱】不知橫之與縱。【註】緯曰橫，經曰縱。</p><p>又星名。【前漢·天文志】王梁之旁有八星絕漢，曰天橫。</p><p>又縣名。【前漢·地理志】橫縣，屬琅邪郡。</p><p>又學舍也。與黌通。【後漢·鮑德傳】修起橫舍。</p><p>又姓。【風俗通】韓王子成號橫陽君，其後爲氏。</p><p>又【唐韻古音】胡光切，音黃。【楚辭·九辯】收恢台之孟夏兮，然欿傺而沈藏。葉菸邑而無色兮，枝煩挐而交橫。</p><p>又【集韻】草名。【爾雅·釋草】傅，橫目。【註】一名結縷。俗謂之鼓箏草。</p><p>又【集韻】姑黃切，音光。漢門名。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>戸孟切，衡去聲。不順理也。【孟子】待我以橫逆。【前漢·吳王傳】吳王日益橫。</p><p>又卜兆名。【前漢·文帝紀】卜之，兆得大橫。</p><p>又【唐韻】【集韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>古曠切，音桄。盛氣充滿也。【禮·樂記】鐘聲鏗鏗以立號，號以立橫，橫以立武。</p><p>又【類篇】俎跗，橫木也。</p>
