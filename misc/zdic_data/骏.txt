                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>骏</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">駿</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jùn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jùn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>良马：～马。～足。～骨。</li><li>迅速：～奔。～发。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">峻</span>”，高大。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">俊</span>”，才智超群。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> excellent horse, noble steed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> feuriges Pferd, edles Pferd</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cheval fougueux</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>骏</strong></p><p>駿 <span class="dicpy">jùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:良马)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[steed;fine horse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骏，马之良材也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天子八骏。——<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天子赐许男骏马十六。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东市买骏马。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将胡骏马而归。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·人间训》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:骏骨(骏马的骨头。比喻贤才);骏驭(良马拉的车驾);骏乘(骏良，骏马，骏骁，骏珍，骏骑，骏驹。都指良马)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 才能出众的人 <span class="encs">[outstanding person]</span>。如:骏足(比喻贤能才俊);骏步(比喻贤才的足迹);骏民(贤人);骏才(才智杰出的人)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>骏</strong></p><p>駿 <span class="dicpy">jùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 迅速 <span class="encs">[speedy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骏奔走在庙。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·清庙》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:骏发(迅速耕作);骏逸(本指马行急速。引申为超凡出众、才华纵逸);骏奔(急走，快跑);骏骥(良马)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 高大，大 <span class="encs">[great]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骏，大也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骏命不易。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·文王》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遹骏有声。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·文王有声》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:骏命(上天或天子重大的命令);骏庞(笃厚，大厚);骏业(宏伟的事业);骏功(大功);骏烈(盛业)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“俊”</span>。才智杰出 <span class="encs">[outstanding]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">收天下之豪杰、有天下之骏雄。——<span class="smcs">《管子·七发》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雄骏不创寿于旗幢。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·大体》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诽骏疑杰兮，固庸态也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:骏异(优异);骏概(英武的气概);骏彩(华美);骏徒(优秀的学生)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 长 <span class="encs">[long]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浩浩昊天，不骏其德。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·雨无止》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/骏马">骏马</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
