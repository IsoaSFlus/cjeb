                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">最</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>最</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，从冃( <span class="dicpy">mào</span>)，从取。本义:冒犯夺取)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[offend and fetch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">最，犯而取之。从曰，从取。会意。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公既诸将一禀睿筭，与贼遌，最，遂下之。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘三吾</span><span class="smcs">《许国襄简王公神道碑铭》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 聚，聚合 <span class="encs">[assemble;gather]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得其常心，物所为最之哉?——<span class="smcs">《庄子·德充符》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冬收五藏，最万物。——<span class="smcs">《管子·禁藏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“最，聚也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民举所最粟。——<span class="smcs">《管子·地数》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 总计;合计 <span class="encs">[amount to;total]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">最其行能，多不及中。——<span class="smcs">《潜夫论》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>最</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代考核政绩或军功时划分的等级，以上等为最。跟<span class="yhcs">“殿”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[first]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹无益于殿最也。——</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《答宾戏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“上功曰最。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有里课之，最者，赐田典日旬。——<span class="smcs">《睡虎地秦墓竹简·厩苑律》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:最吏(表现特别，考绩最优的官吏);最尤(特异，突出);最吏(考绩优秀的官吏);最殿(功绩最劣等者);最课(官吏考课的最高成绩)</p><p>◎ <strong>最</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“绝”</span>。表示程度，相当于<span class="yhcs">“极”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“尤”</span> <span class="encs">[most;best;to the highest degree]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之水泔最而稽。——<span class="smcs">《管子·水地》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“绝也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中侯仍拜稽首受最。——<span class="smcs">《尚书》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“要言也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">最从高帝。——<span class="smcs">《史记·绛侯周勃世家》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“都凡也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">最下者与之争。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">最爱湖东行不足。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《钱塘湖春行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">最喜小儿无赖，溪头卧剥莲蓬。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《清平乐·村居》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">最著前。(排在最前头。著，居于。)——<span
