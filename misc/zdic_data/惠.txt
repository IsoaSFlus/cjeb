                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">惠</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>惠</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>恩，好处：恩～。～泽。～和。～爱。～握（恩惠深厚）。仁～。实～。施～于人。</li>
	<li>给人财物或好处：～政。互～互利。～及远人。～而不费（给人好处自己又没有什么耗费；常用以形容有实利而不多费钱财）。</li>
	<li>敬辞，用于对方对待自己的行动：～赠。～临。～顾。～存。～允。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">慧</span>&rdquo;，聪明。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">惠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>惠</strong></p>
<p>僡、叀 <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，叀(<span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span>)声。本义:仁爱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[kindheartedness]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惠，仁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爱民好与曰惠。柔质慈民曰惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;谥法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分人以财谓之惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心省恤人谓之惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孙讨虏</span>聪明仁惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫慈者不忍，而惠者好与也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;内储说上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其养民也惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;公冶长》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">彭</span>有守多惠政。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:惠声(仁爱而有德泽的美名);惠育(用仁心爱德加以养育);惠化(仁爱的教化);惠心(仁慈的心)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 柔顺;顺从 <span class="encs">[mild]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">义征不惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">荆南</span>怀惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:惠音(和谐的乐音;对他人书信的尊称);惠气(和顺之气);惠来(招抚使之归顺)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;慧&rdquo;。聪慧 <span class="encs">[clever]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巧文辩惠则贤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语九》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先言而后当者，惠也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋&middot;外篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惠者知其不可两守，乃取一焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;宙合》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甚矣，汝之不惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
