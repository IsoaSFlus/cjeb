                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>枕</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhěn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhěn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>躺着时垫在头下的东西：～头。落（<span class="dicpy">lào </span>）～。～席。</li><li>躺着的时候，把头放在枕头上或器物上：～戈待旦。～藉（纵横相枕而卧）。</li><li>垫着：～木（铁路上承受铁轨的横木）。～腕（写字运笔的一种方法，右手垫在左手上写）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>枕</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhěn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">名</span></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木。本义:枕头)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pillow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枕，卧所荐首也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辗转伏枕。——<span class="smcs">《诗·陈风·泽陂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">险且枕。——<span class="smcs">《易·坎》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“木在首曰枕”</span>。 <span class="diczx2">陆绩</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“枕闲碍险害之貌。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三窟已就，君姑高枕为乐矣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:枕上之花(比喻女色);枕箱(可作枕头使用的长方形扁箱。出门旅行时放置零物);枕山(谓垫得很高的枕头);枕中术(藏在枕中的秘术);枕中书(珍秘之书)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 枕骨 <span class="encs">[occipital bone]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">头横骨为枕。——<span class="smcs">《素问·骨空论》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>枕</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhěn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">动</span></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 枕着 <span class="encs">[pillow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曲肱而枕之。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾枕戈待旦，志枭逆虏。——<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尸骸枕藉。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:枕弓(犹枕戈);枕戈汗马(枕着武器，疾驰战马。形容时刻准备杀敌立功);枕石(枕于石头。多指隐居山林);枕甲(枕着铠甲)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 靠近，临 <span class="encs">[be near to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">会稽</span>东接于海，南近 <span class="diczx2">诸越</span>，北枕大江。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·严助传》</span>。</p><p><span
