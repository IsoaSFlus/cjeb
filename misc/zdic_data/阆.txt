						<p>● <strong>阆</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閬</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">l&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄤˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔闶～〕见&ldquo;<a href="/hans/闶">闶</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol><p>● <strong>阆</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">閬</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄤˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～中〕地名，在四川省。</li>	<li>门高的样子。</li>	<li>空旷。</li>	<li>没有水的城壕。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阆</strong></p>
<p>閬 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 门高大 <span class="encs">[(of door) high]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">閬，门高也。从门，良声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闶阆其寥廓兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span>。注:&ldquo;闶阆，空虚也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阆阙(高大的门阙);阆阆(高大的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 空旷 <span class="encs">[spacious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">郭外为之土阆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;度地》</span>。注:&ldquo;空旷也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:闶阆(建筑物中空阔的部分)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阆</strong></p>
<p>閬 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 隍，无水的城壕 <span class="encs">[dry moat outside a city wall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">城外为之郭，郭外为之土阆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胞有重阆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;外物》</span>。注:&ldquo;阆谓隍，又为朗。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 阆风巅的省称 <span class="encs">[langfengdian mountain]</span>，山名。传说中神仙居住的地方，在昆仑之巅，又称&ldquo;阆风岑&rdquo;、&ldquo;阆风&rdquo;、&ldquo;阆邱&rdquo;、&ldquo;阆风台&rdquo;、&ldquo;阆山&rdquo;等</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">閬</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“阆”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/閬">閬</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閬·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95AC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】來宕切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】郞宕切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音浪。【說文】門高也。</p><p>又空虛也。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】閌閬閬其寥廓兮。【註】閬，音浪。閬閬，空虛也。</p><p>又空曠也。【莊子&middot;外物篇】胞有重閬，心有天遊。【註】閬，空曠也。音浪。</p><p>又土閬。【管子&middot;度地篇】城外爲之郭，郭外爲之土閬。</p><p>又山名。【屈原&middot;離騷】朝吾將濟於白水兮，登閬風而緤馬。【註】閬風，山名。在崑崙之上。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】登閬風而遙集兮。【註】張揖曰：閬風山在崑崙閶闔之中。</p><p>又地名。巴郡有閬中縣。【前漢&middot;地理志】閬中。【註】屬巴郡。閬音浪。</p><p>又【集韻】里黨切，音朖。爣閬，寬明貌。</p><p>又人名。【史記&middot;周本紀】釐王子惠王閬立。</p><p>又【集韻】里養切，音兩。【說文】蝄蜽也。【史記&middot;孔子世家】木石之怪夔罔閬。【註】夔，音逵。閬，音兩。家語作魍魎。</p><p>又【廣韻】魯當切【集韻】盧當切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音郞。亦門高也。</p><p>又【後漢&middot;張衡傳】集太微之閬。【註】閬，明大也。【揚雄&middot;甘泉賦文選註】閬音郞。閬閬，高大貌。</p><p>又【集韻】徒郞切，音唐。高門謂之閬。或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D88.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨶈</span>。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/95AC.svg">                          <p>門高也。从門良聲。巴郡有閬中縣。來宕切</p>                          </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/95AC_7293.svg" /><span class="swbf">(閬)</span><span class="ywyy">門高也。</span><span class="swdz">文選甘泉賦注引作門高大之皃也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28E0F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨸏</span>部曰：阬，閬也。此曰閬，門高皃。相合爲一義。凡許書異部合讀之例如此。大雅：迺立皋門，皋門有伉。傳曰：王之郭門曰皋門。伉，高皃。按詩伉當是阬之譌。甘泉賦：閌閬閬其寥郭兮。閌亦卽阬字。許書無閌。</span><span class="ywyy">从門良聲。</span><span class="swdz">來宕切。十部。</span><span class="ywyy">巴郡有閬中縣。</span><span class="swdz">見漢地理志。</span></p>                          </div>
