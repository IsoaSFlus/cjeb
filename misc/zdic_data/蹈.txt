						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蹈</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄠˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>践踏，踩：～袭（走别人走过的老路，沿用前人旧例）。～节（信守节操）。～海（跳到海里自杀）。赴汤～火。循规～矩。</li>    <li>跳动：舞～。手舞足～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蹈</strong> <span class="dicpy">dǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，舀( <span class="dicpy">yǎo</span>)声。本义:踩，踏)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(往往有冒险的意味) <span class="encs">[tread;stamp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹈，践也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹈，履也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">足之蹈之。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹈腾昆仑。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“蹑也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至人潜行不窒，蹈火不热。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·达生》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">羸兵为人马所蹈藉。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹈死不顾。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蹈火(踩火);蹈藉(践踏);蹈跃(践踏奔跳);蹈冰(踏冰)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 顿足踏地 <span class="encs">[stamp one's foot]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">发扬蹈历之己蚤。——<span class="smcs">《史记·乐书》</span>。正义:<span class="yhcs">“顿足蹋地也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:手舞足蹈;蹈舞(手舞足蹈，表示欢乐);蹈咏(舞蹈和吟唱)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 遵循 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后之君子，蹈常而习故。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《伊尹论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:蹈矩(投身;依托);蹈节(克守节操);蹈节死义(遵守节操，为正义而死)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 实行 <span class="encs">[carry out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一理长成，遽躬蹈之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而蹈之者常十之八九。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:蹈险(经历危险);蹈义(履行正义);蹈道(履行正道)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 朝某方向走;行 <span class="encs">[go]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故无首虏之获，无蹈难之赏。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·儒效》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:蹈难(身赴危难);蹈敌(赴敌)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 乘;利用 <span
