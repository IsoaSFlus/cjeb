                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>粗</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>疏忽，不周密：～心。～疏。～略。～率（<span class="dicpy">shuài </span>）。</li><li>不精致，工料毛糙：～糙。～劣。～料。～纸。～粮。～制滥造。去～取精。</li><li>长条东西直径大的：～大。～壮。～重。～实。～线条（ａ．指毛道粗的线条：ｂ．喻粗率的性格、作风，亦喻文章的粗略的构思）。～枝大叶。</li><li>颗粒大的：～沙子。</li><li>声音低而大：～哑。～声～气。</li><li>鲁莽：～鲁。～暴。～野。～犷。～俗。～笨。～人。～手～脚。</li><li>略微：～略。～具规模。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粗</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>粗</strong></p><p>麤 <span class="dicpy">cū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从米，且声。本义:糙米)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[unpolished rice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粗，疏也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，粝米也。禾黍粟十六斗大半斗为米一斛。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粮则无矣，麤则有之。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:粗糠(糠。稻、麦，谷子等农作物子实的皮或壳)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 粗粮 <span class="encs">[coarse food grains]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡九谷，皆随精粗，差其耗损而供焉。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 中国古代哲学范畴，指事物的表面现象 <span class="encs">[phenomenon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可以言论者，物之粗也;可以意致者，物之精也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通过一物体中心的直线长度 <span class="encs">[diameter]</span>。如:树干底部粗为两米的树</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>粗</strong> <span class="dicpy">cū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不精，粗糙 <span class="encs">[rough;coarse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粗，大也。凡不精者皆曰粗。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其器高以粗。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其声粗以厉。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">粗布之衣。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:粗粝(糙米;粗俗简陋的饭食);粗恶(粗糙，低劣);粗食(粗糙不精的食物);粗粗(即粗糙);粗腿;粗砂;粗重(粗大笨重的家具)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 粗疏;粗略 <span class="encs">[careless;inattentive]</span></p><p><span
