                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">扛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扛</strong></p>
<p>摃 <span class="dicpy">gāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用两手举重物 <span class="encs">[lift with both hands]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扛，横关对举也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作掆.</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两人对举为扛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;费长房传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翘关扛鼎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">扛，举也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">力能扛鼎，才气过人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:扛鼎(举鼎);扛舁(举抬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 两人或两人以上共抬一物 <span class="encs">[(of two or more people) carry together]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:四个兵把他扛到驴背上;扛帮(聚合众人结成帮派);扛哄(合伙哄骗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 磕;碰 <span class="encs">[impact]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:吃倒好吃，只怕有些扛牙;扛了扛团长的肩膀</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 阻拦 <span class="encs">[block]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:八戒扛住行者说&hellip;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 横架 <span class="encs">[bar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这时城门已经上了大铁锁，扛了大木闩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李连璧</span><span class="smcs">《古城怒火》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 顶撞;抬杠 <span class="encs">[contradict; argue for the sake of arguing; bicker]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">呆子慌了，往山坡下筑了有三尺深，下面都是石脚石根，扛住钯齿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">k&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>扛</strong> <span class="dicpy">k&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
