                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">习</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>习</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">習</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>学过后再温熟反复地学，使熟练：练～。学～。实～。</li>
	<li>学：～文。～武。</li>
	<li>对某事熟悉：～见。～闻。～以为常。</li>
	<li>长期重复地做，逐渐养成的不自觉的活动：～惯。积～。陈规陋～。</li>
	<li>相因：世代相～。～～相因。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> practice; flapping wings</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> üben, vertraut sein ,Xi  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> étudier,apprendre,pratiquer,habitude,s'exercer,s'habituer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">习</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>习</strong></p>
<p>習 <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从羽 。从羽，与鸟飞有关。本义:小鸟反复地试飞)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fly frequently]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">習，数飞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鹰乃学习。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习习笼中鸟，举翮触四隅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《咏史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 反覆练习，钻研 <span class="encs">[review;practise;exercise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子以朋友讲习。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;象下传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是皆习民数者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国 语&middot;周语》</span>。注;&ldquo;习，简习也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学而时习之，不亦说乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;学而》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇侃</span>义疏:&ldquo;习是修故之称也。言人不学则已，既学必因仍而修习，日夜无替也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:习容(练习举止，整饬仪表);习肄(犹练习);习战(练习作战);习非胜是(对错误的事物相习既久，无法矫正，反以为是)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通晓，熟悉 <span class="encs">[become familiar with;be used to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不习于诵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谁习计会，能为文收责于</span><span class="diczx2">薛</span>者乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆通习之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">益习其声。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不习水战。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习见习闻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习方俗</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:习流(熟悉水流。又指熟悉水战的兵士);习士(熟悉法制的人);习事(熟谙事理);习知(熟知);习水(熟习水性)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 学习 <span class="encs">[learn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">始习于大豆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;造父》</span>。注:&ldquo;学也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习其句读。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自幼好武术，习无不精。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:习学(学习);习文练武;讲习(讲授与学习);习业(攻习学业，钻研学问)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 习惯;习惯于，习以为常 <span class="encs">[get accustomed to;be used to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人者，明于治乱之道，习于人事之终始者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民习以力攻难，故轻死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君法&middot;战法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人习于苟且非一日。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不习水土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家人习奢。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习于钟鼓。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:习熟(习惯熟悉);习闲(游手好闲者);习闻;习安(习惯于安定)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 训练 <span class="encs">[train]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不习，则民不可使也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:习教(犹训练);习勒(严格训练);习练(练习;训练);习用(演习);习兵(操演军事);习阵(演习战阵之事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 习染 <span class="encs">[fall into a bad habit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习善而为善，习恶而为恶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;本性》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">习其家风。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>

<p>習 <span class="dicpy">x&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 习性;习惯 <span class="encs">[habit;custom;usual practice]</span></p>
