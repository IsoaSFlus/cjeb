class="diczx1">掩以绝灭。——</span><span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《洞箫赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“止息貌。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掩细柳而抚剑。——</span><span
