                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辐</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>辐</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">輻</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>连结车辋和车毂的直条：车～。～条。～（亦作&ldquo;辐凑&rdquo;）。～射。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> spokes of wheel</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Speiche  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> rayon</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>辐</strong></p>
<p>輻 <span class="dicpy">f&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，畐( <span class="dicpy">f&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。&ldquo;畐&rdquo;有&ldquo;腹满&rdquo;义(参&ldquo;福&rdquo;字条)，而&ldquo;辐&rdquo;为圆形，与人腹满之形相类，因而&ldquo;畐&rdquo;在字中亦兼有表义作用。本义:辐条。插入轮毂以支撑轮圈的细条)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[spoke]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辐，轮轑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辐也者，以为直指也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;轮人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三十辐共一毂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众辐凑于前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刘向传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坎坎伐辐兮，置之河之侧兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:轮辐;辐集(聚集);辐聚(聚集)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 聚集如辐 <span class="encs">[in swarm]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:辐至(齐至);辐裂(分崩离析);辐解(分解)</span></p>
