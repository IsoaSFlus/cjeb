						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>冒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄠˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>向外透或往上升：～烟（❶烟往上升；❷发怒）。～汗。～尖。</li>
	<li>不顾（恶劣的环境或危险等），顶着：～雨。～险。～死。</li>
	<li>不加小心，鲁莽，冲撞：～失。～昧。～进（不顾具体条件，急躁进行）。</li>
	<li>用假的充当真的，假托：～牌。～充。～名顶替。</li>
	<li>复盖：&ldquo;先设一铁板，其上以松脂、蜡和纸灰之类～之&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>贪污：&ldquo;贪于饮食，～于货贿&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">帽</span>&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">瑁</span>&rdquo;，玳瑁。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

<p>● <strong>冒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄛˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 〔～顿（d&uacute;）〕中国汉初匈奴族的一个君主名。</p>
<ol>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> risk, brave, dare</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> emporsteigen (Rauch, Qualm)​  (V)​,quellen (Wasser)​  (V)​,vorschnell, betrügerisch  (Adj)​,ohne Rücksicht auf</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> émettre,brave,courageux,oser</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冒</strong></p>
<p>冐 <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。上为帽子，下边是眼睛。本义:帽子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &ldquo;帽&rdquo;的古字。帽子 <span class="encs">[hat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">著黄冒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;儁不疑传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白纱冒者，视朝、听讼、宴见宾客之服也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冒絮(头巾)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代殓尸的布囊，由上下两截合成 <span class="encs">[sack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冒:缁质，长与手齐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 冒与芼古字通。芼，菜也 <span class="encs">[vegetables]</span></p>
