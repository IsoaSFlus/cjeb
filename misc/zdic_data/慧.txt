						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>慧</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 聪明，有才智：聪～。智～。颖～。～黠（聪明而狡猾）。～心。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>慧</strong> <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，彗声。本义:聪明，有才智)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[intelligent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慧，儇也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知或谓之慧。——<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>三</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 注:<span class="yhcs">“慧，儇，皆意精明。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亟见窕察谓之慧。——<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔质受谏曰慧。——<span class="smcs">《周书·谥法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">周子</span>有兄而无慧。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而慧者不以藏书箧。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:慧侠(有才华，有胆略);慧给(聪敏而有口才);慧种(优良的天赋秉性);慧黠(聪敏机智);慧美(聪明美丽)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 狡黠 <span class="encs">[crafty]</span>。如:慧齿(灵巧的口齿)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> <span class="encs">[中医]</span>∶眼睛清明 <span class="encs">[clear and bright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身冷若冰，眼睛不慧，语言不休。——<span class="smcs">《伤寒论》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>慧</strong> <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>佛教名词 <span class="encs">[Prajna (般若)]</span>。意译为慧，智慧。如:慧目(指智慧的眼目能洞察世间的一切现象);慧光(智慧的光，能了彻一切);慧寂(佛教谓智慧和禅定);慧业(指智慧的业缘)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
