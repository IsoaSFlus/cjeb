						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>峻</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄣˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>山高而陡：高～。险～。～峭。～拔。</li>
	<li>高大：～德。～节。</li>
	<li>严厉苛刻：～刻。～厉。严～。严刑～法。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> high, steep, towering; stern</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> abschüssig, einweichen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> élevé,dur,rigoureux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">峻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>峻</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，雋( <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:高而陡峭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[high and precipitous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山峻高而蔽日兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山道峻隘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北齐书&middot;高昂传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是犹上高陵之颠堕峻溪之下而求生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;奸劫弑臣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:峻危(高耸险峻);峻朵(高峻的山峰);峻岭(崇高的山岭);峻绝(极为陡峭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 严酷，严厉 <span class="encs">[harsh;stern]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃峻责租调。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书&middot;李实传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">严刑峻法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;非韩》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因以峻文诋之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;朱浮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:峻政(苛政);峻责(严厉责求);峻拒(断然拒绝);峻辞(断然辞绝)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 高超 <span class="encs">[outstanding;talent]</span>。如:峻绝(高超);峻迈(高超出众);峻论(高论);峻格(高超的格调);峻立(高超特出)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 大;高大 <span class="encs">[great;tall and big]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">克明峻德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:峻宇(高大的屋宇);峻德(大德);峻命(大命，指天帝或帝王的命令);峻博(博大)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 高雅;高贵;高尚 <span class="encs">[lofting;noble]</span>。如:峻雅(高雅);峻阀(高贵的门第);峻爵(崇高的爵位)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 严正 <span class="encs">[solemn and just]</span>。如:峻整(严正庄重);峻网(严正的法网);峻介(严正耿直);峻切(文词、语言严正切直)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 猛烈 <span class="encs">[fierce]</span>。如:峻剂(猛烈的药剂);峻药(猛烈的药物);峻利(猛烈);峻烈(猛烈);峻利(味道浓烈而爽口)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 指文笔刚劲挺拔 <span class="encs">[vigorous]</span>。如:峻利(形容笔力刚劲雄健);峻健(刚劲有力);峻洁(诗文刚劲凝练)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>峻</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
