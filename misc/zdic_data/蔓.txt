                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蔓</strong> <span class="dicpy">mán</span></p><p>另见 <span class="dicpy">màn;wàn</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蔓菁">蔓菁</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蔓</strong> <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>(形声。从艸，曼声。本义:藤蔓，草本蔓生植物的枝茎) 同本义 <span class="encs">[vine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蔓，葛属。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青树翠蔓。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丹藤翠蔓。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蔓广则歧多，歧多则饶子。——<span class="smcs">《齐民要术·种瓜》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兔丝附蓬麻，引蔓故不长。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《新婚别》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>蔓</strong> <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蔓延;滋长 <span class="encs">[creep]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">野有蔓草。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·野有蔓草》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“延也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敛蔓于野。——<span class="smcs">《诗·唐风·葛生》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不蔓不枝。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无使滋蔓，蔓，难图也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蔓生(蔓延生长);蔓衍(扩展延伸);蔓草(蔓延滋生的草);蔓连(延伸串联);蔓蔓(滋长延伸的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 攀引，缠绕 <span class="encs">[twine]</span>。如:蔓引(如瓜蔓相互的牵连);蔓蔓(纠缠难察的样子)</p><p>◎ <strong>蔓</strong> <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span
