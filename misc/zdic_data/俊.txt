                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>俊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jùn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jùn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>才智出众的人：～杰。～伟。～彦（才智杰出的人）。～爽。～造（学识造诣很深的人）。</li><li>容貌美丽：～俏。～美。～秀。～逸（俊美洒脱，不同凡俗）。英～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">峻</span>”，大。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">俊</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>俊</strong></p><p>儁 <span class="dicpy">jùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，雋( <span class="dicpy">jùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jùn.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:才智超群的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[elite]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俊，材千人也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十人者曰豪，百人者曰杰，千人者曰俊，万人者曰英。——<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露·爵国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">德万人者谓之俊。——<span class="smcs">《鹖冠子·能天》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俊士。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赞杰俊。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:俊选(俊士和选士);俊良(贤能优良之士);俊物(杰出人物);俊群(才能卓越一类人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 光彩 <span class="encs">[lustre]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">那就算你在世街路上留了朋友，俊了师傅了。——<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>俊</strong> <span class="dicpy">jùn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“峻”</span>。大;高尚 <span class="encs">[great;fine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俊乂在官。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋陶谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">克明俊德。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时有俊风。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记·夏小正》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俊鹘抟水禽。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:俊伟(伟大;高大);俊德(大德);俊风(大风);俊马(好马，快马)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 杰出;卓越;出众 <span class="encs">[outstanding]</span>。如:俊民(优秀的人);俊艾(贤才之士);俊髦(才能出众的人);俊烈(杰出刚正);俊眼(杰出的眼力);俊声(才智出众的名声)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 雄健，英武 <span class="encs">[robust]</span>。如:俊朗(英俊潇洒);俊敏(英俊聪敏);俊捷(雄健洒脱);俊鹘(矫健之鹘);俊爽(雄健敏捷)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 漂亮，美丽 <span class="encs">[handsome]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">状极俊健。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:俊刮(俊俏;漂亮);俊快(俊美而流畅);俊庞(容貌俊秀、漂亮);俊生(俊俏，俊美)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 味美;可口 <span
