                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>好</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄠˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>优点多或使人满意的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">坏</span>&rdquo;相对：～人。～汉。～歹。～事多磨。</li>	<li>身体康健，疾病消失，生活幸福：您～。安～。</li>	<li>友爱，和睦：友～。相～。</li>	<li>容易：～办。～使。～懂。</li>	<li>完成，完善：办～了。</li>	<li>表示应允、赞成：～！你干得不错。</li>	<li>很，甚：～冷。～快。～坏。～一会儿。</li>	<li>便于：场地清理干净～打球。</li>	<li>反话，表示不满意：～，这下可坏了！</li></ol><p>● <strong>好</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄠˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>喜爱，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">恶</span>&rdquo;（<span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span>）相对：～奇。～色。～尚（爱好和崇尚）。～高骛远（亦作&ldquo;好高务远&rdquo;）。～逸恶劳。</li>	<li>常常，容易（发生事情）：刚学会骑车的人～摔跤。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>好</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从女，从子。本义:美，貌美)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 指女子貌美 <span class="encs">[beautiful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">好，美也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡美色或谓之好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可谓好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;美也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是女子不好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鬼侯有子而好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>氏有好女。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;陌上桑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:好皮囊(好看的外貌);好鸟(美丽的鸟);好不丑(反语。即好不俊，俊得很);好美(美貌);好闲(容貌美丽，举止闲雅);好妇(美貌的妇女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 善，优良，良好 <span class="encs">[good;fine;nice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">领恶而全好者与。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;仲尼燕居》</span>。注:&ldquo;善也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父信谗而不好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;惜诵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又是江南好风景。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江南逢李龟年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">好雨知时节。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春夜喜雨》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江南好。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《忆江南》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">注曰不好。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪迈</span><span class="smcs">《容斋续笔》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:好天良夜(美好的时节);好日(吉日;好天;又指结婚佳期);好头脑(好对象;好人物);好言(善言;好话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 交好;友爱 <span class="encs">[friendly;kind]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妻子好合。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;常棣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲与王为好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">结好</span><span class="diczx2">孙权</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">情好日密。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">游处相好。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:好会(诸侯间友好的会盟);好达达(妇女对男子的昵称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 健康 <span class="encs">[in good health;get well]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他看起来很好;他的健康状况仍然是很好的;好身手(体格雄壮，身手矫健)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 容易 <span class="encs">[be easy (to do);simple;likely]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这个问题好回答;这事好办;好吃的果儿(容易对付的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 完成;完毕 <span class="encs">[complete;finished]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田车既好。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;车攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">凤生</span>将书封好，一同玉蟾蜍交付 <span class="diczx2">龙香</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《二刻拍案惊奇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:工具都准备好了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 表示赞许、同意或结果;是，同意 <span class="encs">[all right;OK，okay]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:好，我十点钟不找你</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 便于;宜于 <span class="encs">[be easy to;be convenient for;suitable;fitting]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缁衣之好兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;缁衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:好合(志意相合)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>好</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 很，甚，太 <span class="encs">[very]</span>&mdash;&mdash;表示程度，多含感叹语气。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:好大的眼睛;好深的沟;好乔(十分古怪);好杀(极言其好);好是(真是，很是)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用在数量词、时间词前面，表示多或久 <span class="encs">[how]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:等了好半天;来了好多人;过了好久;好歇(好一会儿);好几时(很长时间);好早晚(时候很晚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用在某些动词前面，表示效果好 <span class="encs">[good]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:好看;好听;好吃</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>好</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈助动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 可以 <span class="encs">[may]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:我好进来吗?</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 应该 <span class="encs">[ought to ]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:天快要下雨了，你好走了吧</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 宜于;便于;以便 <span class="encs">[so as to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">青春作伴好还乡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《闻官军收河南河北》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:想法打蛇的头，好打晕它;你作个决定，我好回他;你交代下来，我好去叫他</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">好</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>好</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄠˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hǎo <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2727.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">美、善，理想的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「好東西」、「<b>好</b>風景」、「花好月圓」、「好人好事」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韋莊〈菩薩蠻&middot;人人盡說江南<b>好</b>〉詞：「人人盡說江南<b>好</b>，遊人只合江南老。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">友愛的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「好朋友」、「<b>好</b>同學」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">完整的、沒壞的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「完好如初」、「修<b>好</b>了。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">相善、彼此親愛。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「友<b>好</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;高適〈贈別晉三處士〉詩：「知己從來不易知，慕君為人與君<b>好</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《紅樓夢&middot;第二七回》：「誰和我<b>好</b>，我就和誰<b>好</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">痊癒。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「病<b>好</b>了！」</span><span class="gc_yy">《警世通言&middot;卷一六&middot;小夫人金錢贈年少》：「孩兒感些風寒，這幾日身子不快，來不得。傳語員外得知，一<b>好</b>便來。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">很、非常。表示程度深。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「好久」、「<b>好</b>冷」、「<b>好</b>笨」、「<b>好</b>厲害」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">完成、完畢。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「交代的工作做<b>好</b>了。」、「稿子寫<b>好</b>了。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史&middot;第四三回》：「都梳<b>好</b>了椎髻，穿<b>好</b>了苗錦。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">容易。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「這事好辦。」、「這問題<b>好</b>解決。」、「這小孩<b>好</b>帶。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">以便、便於。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「快準備行李，<b>好</b>早點上路。」、「請告訴我你的住處，我<b>好</b>去找你。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈聞官軍收河南河北〉詩：「白日放歌須縱酒，青春作伴<b>好</b>還鄉。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">可以、應該。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「只<b>好</b>如此」、「正<b>好</b>試試」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《官場現形記&middot;第五一回》：「刁邁彭屈指一算，後任明天<b>好</b>到，便約張太太三天回音。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">置於某些動詞之前，表效果佳。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「好看」、「好玩」、「好吃」、「好笑」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">置於數量詞或時間詞之前，表示多或久的意思。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「好些個」、「<b>好</b>幾處」、「好半天」、「好一會兒」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">歎</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示稱讚或允許。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>好</b>！就這麼辦。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《京本通俗小說&middot;碾玉觀音》：「郡王道：『<b>好</b>！正合我意。』」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">表示責備或不滿意的語氣。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>好</b>！這下子事情愈來愈棘手了。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
