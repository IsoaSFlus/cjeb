						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>昭</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄠˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>光明：～明。～～（➊明亮的样子，如&ldquo;日月～～&rdquo;；➋明白事理。如&ldquo;贤者以其～～，使人～～&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>明显，显著：～著。～彰。～布。～雪（揭明真情，洗清冤枉）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bright, luminous; illustrious</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> deutlich  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> clair,évident</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>昭</strong></p>
<p>炤 <span class="dicpy">zhāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，召声。本义:明亮)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昭，日明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">倬彼云汉，昭回于天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;云汉》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">青春受谢，白日昭只。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;大招》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昭式(光明的法度);昭列(光明);昭衍(光明广布);昭亮(明亮);昭美(光明美好)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 彰明，显著 <span class="encs">[clear;obvious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所以昭炯戒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昭升(显著地升闻);昭名(显著的声名);昭灼(明显;显著);昭明(显明;显著);昭速(明显地招引)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>昭</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 光;亮光 <span class="encs">[light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">倬彼云汉，昭回于天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;云汉》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昭回(晨辰光耀回转);昭耀(光耀);昭晳(光亮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古时宗法制度 <span class="encs">[an order in patriarchal clan system]</span>，宗庙次序，始祖居中，二世、四世、六世位于始祖之左方，称&ldquo;昭&rdquo;;三世、五世、七世，位于右方，称&ldquo;穆&rdquo;。又坟地葬位的左右次序也按此规定排列</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>昭</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 显扬;显示 <span class="encs">[show]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昭陛下圣明。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昭畅(大大发扬);昭文(显扬文采);昭武(显扬武威)&ldquo;炤&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;o</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昭</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上" target="_blank">辰集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上&amp;bs=日" target="_blank">日</a>】
                              昭·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/662D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】止遙切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】之遙切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音招。【說文】日明也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】昭，見也。【博雅】明也。【玉篇】光也。【廣韻】著也，覿也。【易&middot;晉卦】君子以自昭明德。【書&middot;堯典】百姓昭明。【詩&middot;大雅】於昭于天。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;王制】天子七廟，三昭三穆與太祖之廟而七。</p>
<p>又【祭統】夫祭有昭穆。昭穆者，所以別父子、遠近、長幼、親疏之序而無亂也。【魯語】明者爲昭，次者爲穆。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;樂記】蟄蟲昭蘇。【註】昭，曉也。蟄蟲以發生爲曉，更息曰蘇。</p>
<p>又姓。【戰國策】楚有昭奚恤。【屈原&middot;離騷註】三閭之職，掌王族三姓，曰：昭、屈、景。</p>
<p>又【集韻】時饒切【正韻】時昭切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音韶。廟中佋穆，或作昭。【佩觽集】說文自有佋穆之字，以佋爲昭，蓋借音耳。李祭酒涪說爲晉諱昭，改音韶，失之也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>止少切，音沼。【詩&middot;魯頌】其馬蹻蹻，其音昭昭。【釋文】昭，之繞反。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>之笑切。照，或省作昭。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶蚩於切。【前漢&middot;敘傳】受命之初，贊功剖符。奕世弘業，爵土乃昭。</p>
<p>又叶之由切。【楚辭&middot;九章】臨沅湘之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>淵兮，遂自忍而沈流。卒沒身而絕名兮，惜壅君之不昭。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/662D_2547.svg" /><span class="swbf">(昭)</span><span class="ywyy">日朙也。</span><span class="swdz">引伸爲凡明之偁。廟有昭穆，昭取陽明，穆取陰幽，皆本無正字，叚此二字爲之。自晉避司馬昭諱，不敢正讀，一切讀上饒反。而陸氏乃以入經典釋文，陋矣。又别製佋字，注云：廟昭穆，父爲佋南面，子爲穆北面。从人召聲。此冣爲不通。昭穆乃鬼神之偁，其字當从示，而从人何也。無識者又取以竄入說文人部中，其亂名改作有如此者。今人部删佋。</span><span class="ywyy">从日召聲。</span><span class="swdz">止遙切。二部。</span></p>                          </div>
