                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弦</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>弦</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>系在弓背两端的、能发箭的绳状物：弓～。～韦（&ldquo;弦&rdquo;指弓弦，&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">韦</span>&rdquo;是兽皮，弦紧皮软，喻性子急缓不同。古人佩弦来警戒自己的性缓，佩韦以警戒自己的性急；后遂用&ldquo;弦韦&rdquo;喻朋友的规劝）。～月（农历每月初七、八或二十二、三，月亮半圆，形似弓弦，故名）。上～（农历每月初七或初八的月相）。下～（农历每月二十二或二十三的月相）。应～而倒。</li>
	<li>乐器上发声的线：琴～。～子（①指琴弦；②乐器&ldquo;三弦&rdquo;的通称）。～歌。管～。续～（古人以琴瑟喻夫妻，故又以&ldquo;断弦&rdquo;喻丧妻，以&ldquo;续弦&rdquo;喻再娶）。～外之音。</li>
	<li>中国古代称不等腰直三角形的斜边。</li>
	<li>中医脉象名：～脉。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弦</strong></p>
<p>絃 <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。左为&ldquo;弓&rdquo;，右为&ldquo;丝&rdquo;。弓弦是用丝做成的。本义:弓弦)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bowstring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弦，弓弦也。从弓，象丝轸之形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无待韦弦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;王文宪集序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故佩弦以自急。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;观行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">引弦而战。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;人间训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抚弦登陴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弦箭(弦上之箭);弦栝(弓弦和箭末扣弦处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 弓弩 <span class="encs">[bow]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:弦刃(弓和刀);弦木(指弓);弦矢(弓箭)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 乐器上的肠线或金属丝 <span class="encs">[string of a musical instrument]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弦而鼓之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子&middot;千里马篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝歌夜弦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转轴拨弦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小弦切切</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弦徽(琴弦与琴徽;系琴弦之绳。后也指琴面上的标志);弦乐(丝弦乐器);弦子(胡琴);弦吹(泛称弦管乐);弦柱(乐器上绾丝的柱子);弦桐(琴的别名。以桐木为造琴良材，故名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 弦乐器 <span class="encs">[strings]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>烈然返瑟而弦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弦子(胡琴);弦吹(指弦乐器和管乐器);弦柱(乐器绾丝之柱);弦乐(丝弦乐器);弦子(三弦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 弦乐器弹奏出的声音 <span class="encs">[sound of strings]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">管清疑警鹤，弦巧似娇莺。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《历阳书事七十韵》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弦歌(歌泳诵读，也指儒家的礼乐教化);弦节(琴瑟之节拍);弦外之音(同&ldquo;絃外之音&rdquo;。喻指含蓄不尽的言外之意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> <span class="encs">[数]</span>∶连结曲线上两点的直线;特指曲线上正割的两个交点之间的线段 <span class="encs">[chord]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 直角三角形的斜边(直角所对的边) <span class="encs">[hypotenuse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勾三、股四、弦五。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周髀算经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 四分之一 <span class="encs">[quarter]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 地平圈按四分之一划分的一个部分或相当于这一部分的基本方位点</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 月球公转的四分之一。即上弦是月球在太阳之东90&deg;时的位相，下弦是月球在太阳之西90&deg;时的位相。如:上弦;下弦</p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 半圆形的月亮 <span class="encs">[semicircular moon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">月中分谓之弦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;四讳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弦朔(指农历每月的初七、初八、廿二、廿三日(弦日)和初一(朔日));弦晦;弦望(月相变化)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> <span class="encs">[天文]</span>∶形成直角的三体的一种组态，尤指太阳系中太阳位于90&deg;角顶点的组态 <span class="encs">[dichotomy]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 周代国名 <span class="encs">[Xian state]</span>。春秋时为楚所灭。故城在今河南省潢川县西南</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人灭 <span class="diczx2">弦</span>， <span class="diczx2">弦子</span>奔 <span class="diczx2">黄</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 〈方〉∶发条 <span class="encs">[clockwork spring]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:上弦;钟弦</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 弦脉。中医指脉气紧张，是脉象之一 <span class="encs">[tight]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六脉皆弦，因平日郁结所致。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弦怯(指弦脉虚弱);弦芤(切脉时如同指按丝弦、浮大中空)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 姓</p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">緊繫在弓上的索、線，具有彈性。即弓弦。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「箭在弦上不得不發。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷一○九&middot;李將軍傳》：「度不中不發，發即應<b>弦</b>而倒。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">張於樂器上的絲、線，可用於彈奏發出樂音。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「琴<b>弦</b>」、「箏<b>弦</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">半圓形的月亮。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「上<b>弦</b>」、「下<b>弦</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈初月〉詩：「光細<b>弦</b>初上，影斜輪未安。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">弦樂或弦樂器的簡稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「弦歌不輟」。</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;王羲之〈三月三日蘭亭詩序〉：「雖無絲竹管<b>弦</b>之盛。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">數學上指圓周或曲線上任意二點的連接線段。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋時鄭國有弦高。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">比喻妻子。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「斷<b>弦</b>」、「續<b>弦</b>」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5F26.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【正韻】戸田切【集韻】【韻會】戸千切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音賢。【說文】弓弦也。从弓，象絲軫之形也。【儀禮&middot;鄕射禮】有司左執弣，右執弦而授弓。</p>
<p>又半月曰弦。【釋名】弦，半月之名也。其形一旁曲，一旁直，若張弓施弦也。【前漢&middot;律歷志】淳于陵渠復覆太初歷，晦朔弦望皆最密。</p>
<p>又國名。【春秋&middot;僖五年】楚人滅弦，弦子奔黃。【註】弦國在弋陽軑縣東南。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】風俗通云：弦子後。【左傳&middot;僖二十三年】鄭商人弦高。【哀四年】齊<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>乞弦施救范氏。【哀十一年】使問弦多以琴。</p>
<p>又脈數曰弦。【史記&middot;倉公傳】脈長而弦。</p>
<p>又弦蒲，弦中，皆地名。【前漢&middot;地理志】正西曰雍州，藪曰弦蒲。【註】在汧縣，又雍州山北有蒲谷鄕弦中谷。</p>
<p>又與絃通。【禮&middot;文王世子】春誦夏弦。【樂記】舜作五弦之琴，以歌南風。</p>
