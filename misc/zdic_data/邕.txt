                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9095_6884.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(邕)</span><span class="ywyy">邑四方有水自邕成池者是也。</span><span class="swdz">邑各本無。依韵會補。成各本作城。誤。依廣韵、韵會正。自邕當作自<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22E6C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢹬</span>。轉寫之誤。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22E6C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢹬</span>者、抱也。池沼多由人工所爲。惟邑之四旁有水來自擁抱旋繞成池者、是爲邕。以擁釋邕、以㬪韵爲訓也。故其字从川邑。引申之、凡四面有水皆曰邕。周頌曰。于彼西雝。傳曰。雝、澤也。大雅曰。於樂辟雝。鎬京辟雝。傳曰。水旋丘如璧曰辟雝。水經注釋漁陽郡雍奴曰。四方有水爲雍。不流爲奴。皆邕字之叚借也。</span><span class="ywyy">从巛邑。</span><span class="swdz">川圍邑。會意。</span><span class="ywyy">讀若雝。</span><span class="swdz">於容切。九部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9095_6885.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21FF7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡿷</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文邕如此。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
