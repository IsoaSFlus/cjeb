						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>莽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>草，密生的草：～原。草～。</li><li>广大，辽阔：～苍。～～（ａ．形容原野辽阔，无边无际；ｂ．形容草木茂盛）。</li><li>古书上指一种短节竹。</li><li>粗鲁，冒失：～汉。～撞。鲁～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>莽</strong> <span class="dicpy">mǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从犬，从茻。原意是犬跑到草丛中逐兔，假借为茻，草丛)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 草丛 <span class="encs">[grass cluster]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莽，草也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暴骨如莽。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在野曰草芒之臣。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罗千乘于林莽。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">地饶广荐草莽水泉。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·景帝纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“草稠曰荐，深曰莽。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伏戎于莽。——<span class="smcs">《易·同人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:莽林(泛指草木密集连绵而不能通过的森林;专指热带雨林);莽渺(深远而渺茫的样子);莽草(一种有毒植物，又称<span class="yhcs">“水莽草”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 草 <span class="encs">[grass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">食莽饮水，枕块而死。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>莽</strong> <span class="dicpy">mǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 茂密;盛多 <span class="encs">[dense;numerous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">滔滔孟夏兮，草木莽莽。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九章·怀沙》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:莽然(草木茂盛的样子;也指众多的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 无涯际的样子 <span class="encs">[boundless]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莽，大也。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何以为之莽莽也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·知接》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“长大貌。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平原莽千里。——</span><span class="diczx2">谭嗣同</span><span class="smcs">《出潼关渡河》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:莽沆(水广大的样子);莽泱(辽阔广远的样子);莽莽苍苍(无边无际);?</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 渺茫;迷茫 <span class="encs">[vast and hazy]</span>。如:莽眇(迷茫);莽莽漠漠(模模糊糊);莽卤(模糊不明);莽苍苍(迷茫)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 粗疏;鲁莽 <span class="encs">[rough and careless;boorish]</span>。如:莽莽广广(莽莽撞撞);莽壮(卤莽);莽卤(粗疏;马虎)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 大;猛烈 <span class="encs">[big;fierce]</span>。如:莽壮(形容声音、气力粗壮有力)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/莽苍">莽苍</a></span><span                   <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>莽</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄇㄤˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>mǎng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0671.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">植物名。八角茴香科莽草属，常绿灌木。叶椭圆形，有香气，叶面有细微小点。花瓣细长，黄白色。种子褐色、平滑，有剧毒，误食能致死。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">丛生的草地。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·宋玉·风赋》：「蹶石伐木，梢杀林<b>莽</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《聊斋志异·卷一·贾儿》：「儿薄暮潜入何氏园，伏<b>莽</b>中，将以探狐所在。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">粗率、冒失的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「莽夫」、「莽汉」、「鲁<b>莽</b>」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              莽·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/83BD.svg">						  <p>【玉篇】【唐韻】【廣韻】莫朗切【集韻】【類篇】模朗切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蟒。【說文】南昌謂犬善逐兔草中爲莽。</p><p>又【揚子·方言】草，南楚之閒謂之莽。【孟子】在野曰草莽之臣。【趙岐註】莽亦草也。【屈原·離騷】夕攬洲之宿莽。【註】草冬生不死者。</p><p>又【周禮·秋官·剪氏】掌除蠹物，以莽草薰之。【山海經】朝歌之山有草，名曰莽草，可以毒魚。</p><p>又【拾遺記】有草名莽煌，炙人衣則焦，刈之爲席，方冬彌溫，以枝相摩則火出。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋草】莽，數節。【疏】凡竹節閒促數者名莽。</p><p>又【小爾雅】大也。</p><p>又草深貌。【楚辭·天問】草木莽莽。</p><p>又【莊子·則陽篇】君爲政焉勿鹵莽。【註】猶粗率也。</p><p>又莽蒼，見蒼字註。</p><p>又國名。【列子·周穆王篇】西極之南隅，名古莽之國。</p><p>又姓。【前漢書武帝紀莽何羅註】孟康曰：本姓馬，明德皇后惡其先人有反者，易姓莽。</p><p>又【廣韻】莫厚切，音某。義同。</p><p>又【唐韻古音】莫補切，音媽。【楚辭·九章】陶陶孟夏兮，草木莽莽。傷懷永哀兮，汨徂南土。</p><p>又【集韻】謨郞切，音茫。莽蒼，亦讀平聲。　俗作莾、莽。</p><p>
