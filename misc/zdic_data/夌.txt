                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/590C_1133.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(夌)</span><span class="ywyy">越也。</span><span class="swdz">凡夌越字當作此。今字或作淩、或作凌、而夌廢矣。檀弓。喪事雖遽。不陵節。鄭曰。陵、躐也。躐與越義同。廣韵陵下云。犯也。侮也。侵也。皆夌義之引伸。今字槪作陵矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从夊<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21D06.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡴆</span>。</span><span class="swdz">越之義也。力膺切。六部。</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21D06.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡴆</span>、高大也。</span><span class="swdz">說从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21D06.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡴆</span>之意。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰夌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2254C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢕌</span>也。</span><span class="swdz">彳部曰。徲、久也。凡言陵遟、陵夷當作夌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2254C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢕌</span>。今字陵遟、陵夷行而夌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2254C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢕌</span>廢矣。玉篇云。夌遟也。廣韵云。陵遟也。遟與<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2254C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢕌</span>同也。匡謬正俗釋陵遟曰。陵爲陵阜之陵。而遟者、遟遟微細削小之義。古遟夷通用。或言陵夷。其義一也。言陵阜漸平。喻王道弛替耳。玉裁謂。許書作夌。則知說陵爲陵阜、非也。夌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2254C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢕌</span>爲邌之反語。古遟邌通用。夌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2254C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢕌</span>言時久則弛替。遟古讀如夷。夌夷㬪韵字耳。以左傳下陵上替說之。許前義於下陵近。後義於上替近。下陵與上替、其事常相因也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
