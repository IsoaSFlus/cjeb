同本义 <span class="encs">[argue;debate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辩，治也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辩其狱讼。——<span class="yhcs">“辨”</span> <span class="encs">[distinguish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辩其功苦。——<span class="smcs">《国语·齐语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若白墨之于目辩。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·滫务》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">目能辩色，耳能辩声。——</span><span class="diczx2">东汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">仲长统</span><span class="smcs">《昌言·理乱》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:辩章(辨别彰明。同辨章);辩析(辨别分析);明辩是非;辩白(申辩明白。同辨白)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 治理，办理 <span class="encs">[manage;handle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辩治百官，领理万事。——<span class="diczx1">子言非不辩也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:辩武(善辞令的人。即辩士);辩人(善于辞令的人。如说客之流);辩捷(能言善辩，口才敏捷);辩口(口才便捷，善于辩论)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr
