                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>矛</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">m&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄠˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古代用来刺杀敌人的长柄兵器：～头。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">矛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>矛</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形，是古代用来刺杀敌人的进攻性武器。本义:长矛) 同本义 <span class="encs">[spear lance;pike]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矛，酋矛也。建于兵车，长二丈，象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，矛者，刺兵也，其饰县毛羽，兵车左人持弓，右人持矛，中人御。又有夷矛者，长二丈四尺，兵车所不建，不常用。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二矛重英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;清人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脩我戈矛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;无衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">进矛戟者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">操戈执矛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;王会》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矛若林立。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:矛戈(矛与戈。攻击;指责);矛弧(矛与弓。泛指凶器);矛子(梭镖。装有长柄的双刃单尖刀);矛戟(矛和戟。泛指兵器);矛叉(月牙铲);矛舛(矛盾，乖谬);矛戟(矛盾。矛与盾)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">矛</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中" target="_blank">午集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中&amp;bs=矛" target="_blank">矛</a>】
                              矛·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=5" target="_blank">5</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/77DB.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28968.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨥨</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/229A7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢦧</span>鉾<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25365.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥍥</span>【唐韻】莫浮切【集韻】【韻會】迷浮切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音謀。【說文】酋矛也。建於兵車，長二丈，象形。【徐曰】鉤兵也。【書&middot;牧誓】立爾矛。【傳】矛長，故立之於地。【詩&middot;秦風】厹矛鋈錞。【傳】三隅矛也。【禮&middot;曲禮】進矛戟者前其鐓。【疏】矛如鋋而三廉也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;廬人】酋矛常有四尺，夷矛三尋。【註】八尺曰尋，倍尋爲常。酋夷，長短名。【史記&middot;仲尼弟子傳】越使大夫種以屈盧之矛賀吳王。【尉繚子制談篇】殺人於五十步之內者，矛戟也。【揚子&middot;方言】矛，吳揚江淮南楚五湖之閒謂之鍦，或謂之鋋，或謂之鏦。</p><p>又言不相副曰矛盾。【韓非子&middot;難一篇】楚人譽其盾之堅曰：物莫能陷也。</p><p>又譽其矛之利曰：物無不陷也。或曰以子之矛，陷子之盾，何如。其人弗能應。此矛盾之說也。</p><p>又星名。【史記&middot;天官書】杓端有兩星，一內爲矛招搖，一外爲盾天鋒。【註】招搖爲天矛，近北斗者也。</p><p>又藥名。【本草綱目】衞矛，一名鬼箭。【李時珍曰】齊人謂箭羽爲衞，此物幹有直羽如箭羽，矛刃自衞之狀，故名。</p>
