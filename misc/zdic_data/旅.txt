                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>旅</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǚ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄩˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>出行的，在外作客的：～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）。～馆。～次。～居。～客。～伴。商～。差（<span class="dicpy">chāi</span>）～。～社。</li>
	<li>军队的编制单位，在师与团之间。</li>
	<li>泛指军队：军～。强兵劲～。</li>
	<li>共同：～进～退。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/稆">稆</a>&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/膂">膂</a>&rdquo;，脊梁骨。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> trip, journey; travel; traveler</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ausflug  (S)​,Reise</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> voyager,voyage,troupes,brigade</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旅</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象众人站在旗下。旗，指军旗;人，指士兵。小篆字形，表示旌旗;从&ldquo;从&rdquo;，表示众人，即士兵。本义:古代军队五百人为一旅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 军队编制单位。上古一般以五百人为旅，齐制二千人为旅。现代指师以下的单位，基本上由司令部和两个或者两个以上的团或大队组成的战术和行政单位 <span class="encs">[brigade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旅，军之五百人为旅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孙策</span>以天下为三分，众才一旅。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《哀江南赋序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">全旅为上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;谋攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">破旅次之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旅长;两旅兵</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 军队 <span class="encs">[army]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">加之以师旅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;先进》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所以佐百姓之急，足军旅之费。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">桓宽</span><span class="smcs">《盐铁论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:劲旅(强有力的队伍);振旅(休整军队)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 在外的人，旅人 <span class="encs">[traveller]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行旅者皆欲出于王之涂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">商旅不行，樯倾楫摧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旅颜(旅人困顿愁苦的面色);旅怀(羁旅者的情怀);旅尘(旅人身上的尘土);旅愁(羁旅者的愁闷心情);旅梦(旅人思乡之梦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 旅行;旅途 <span class="encs">[journey;trip]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羁旅长堪醉，相留畏晓钟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">戴叔伦</span><span class="smcs">《江乡故人偶集客舍》</span></p>

<p>又【博雅】旅。客也。【易&middot;復卦】商旅不行。</p>
<p>又【旅卦疏】旅者，客寄之名，羈旅之稱，失其本居而寄他方，謂之爲旅。【詩&middot;大雅】于時廬旅。【箋】廬舍其賓旅。【左傳&middot;莊二十二年】羈旅之臣。【註】旅，客也。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;禹貢】蔡蒙旅平。【傳】祭山曰旅。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;掌次】王大旅上帝。【註】大旅上帝祭於圜丘。國有故而祭，亦曰旅。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;牧誓】亞旅。【傳】亞，次也。旅，衆也。衆大夫其位次卿。【左傳&middot;文十五年】請承命於亞旅。【註】亞旅，上大夫也。</p>

<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】旅力方剛。【傳】旅，衆也。【儀禮&middot;士冠禮】旅占卒。【註】旅，衆也。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】殽核惟旅。【傳】旅，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也。</p>

<p>又【周禮&middot;天官&middot;小宰】掌官府之徵令，四曰旅，掌官常以治數。【註】旅辟下士也。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;地官&middot;司徒】旅師。【註】旅，猶處也。</p>
