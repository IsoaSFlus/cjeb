<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">攣</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄢˊ</span></p>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>挛</strong></p>
<p>攣 <span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，羉( <span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:维系，牵系)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tie]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挛，係也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有孚挛如。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;小畜》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:&ldquo;连也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:挛缀(联结);挛结(纠结);挛牵(牵制;束缚);挛索(系绳);挛格(捆缚;束缚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 抽搐，痉挛;手足蜷曲不能伸直 <span class="encs">[contraction]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">痿躄为挛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;疏五过论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">筋散则不挛，故辛可以养筋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《洪范传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:挛曲(蜷曲);挛卧(蜷曲而卧);挛屈(蜷曲)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">攣</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“挛”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/攣">攣</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              攣·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=23" target="_blank">23</a>　·部外筆画：19</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6523.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】呂員切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】閭員切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>戀平聲。【說文】係也。凡拘牽連繫者皆曰攣。【易&middot;中孚】有孚攣如。【疏】相牽繫不絕之名也。【前漢&middot;鄒陽傳】越攣拘之語，馳域外之議。【韓愈&middot;元和聖德詩】解脫攣索。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>龍眷切，音戀。手足曲病也。【史記&middot;蔡澤傳】蹙齃膝攣。</p>
<p>又與戀通。眷也。念也。【前漢&middot;外戚李夫人傳】攣攣顧念我。【註】攣，讀曰戀。</p>
