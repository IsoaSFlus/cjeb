						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>笼</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">籠</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lóng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lóng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>用竹篾、木条编成的盛物器或罩物器：灯～。熏～。</li><li>用竹篾、木条或金属丝等编插而成的养鸟或虫的器具：鸡～。鸟～。蝈蝈～。</li><li>旧时囚禁犯人的东西：囚～。牢～。</li><li>用竹木或金属材料制成的有盖的蒸东西器具：～屉。蒸～。</li><li>泛指包络之物：～头。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>笼</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">籠</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>较大的箱子：箱～。</li><li>遮盖，罩住：～罩。烟～雾罩。</li><li>概括而不分明，不具体，称“笼统”。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>笼</strong></p><p>籠 <span class="dicpy">lóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，龙声。本义:竹笼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用竹片编成的盛物的器具 <span class="encs">[bamboo box]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">笼，举土器也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共丘笼。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·遂师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">貂裘而负笼。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荷笼负锸。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青丝为笼系。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·陌上桑》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">桂枝为笼钩。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:笼臿(盛土的笼和铲挖泥土的锹);笼篰(竹笼和小竹篓);笼屉(蒸笼)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 饲养鸟、虫、家禽等的笼子 <span class="encs">[cage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闭以雕笼。——</span><span class="diczx2">祢衡</span><span class="smcs">《鹦鹉赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以天下为之笼，则雀无所逃。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·庚桑楚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若脱笼之鹄。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span
