rob]</span>。如:割割剥(残害，掠夺);割剥元元(残害百姓。元元:百姓)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 买 <span class="encs">[buy]</span>。如:割肉(上街买肉)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割爱">割爱</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割地">割地</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割断">割断</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割肚牵肠">割肚牵肠</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割鸡焉用牛刀">割鸡焉用牛刀</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割胶">割胶</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割炬">割炬</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割据">割据</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割开">割开</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割礼">割礼</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割裂">割裂</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割弃">割弃</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割青">割青</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割情">割情</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割让">割让</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割舍">割舍</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割尾巴">割尾巴</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割席">割席</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/割线">割线</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
