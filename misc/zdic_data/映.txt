                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">映</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>映</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>照射：～射。～照。</li>
	<li>反照，照射而显示：反～（➊反照，反射；➋把容观事物表现出来；➌向上级转达，如&ldquo;～～群众意见&rdquo;）。～衬（映托使显现）。～雪读书（形容家贫而苦读）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> project; reflect light</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> reflektieren, spiegeln  (V)​,Ying  (Eig, Fam)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">映</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>映</strong></p>
<p>暎 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，央声。本义:照)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">映，明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日阴曰映。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冠盖相映。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">映日荷花别样红。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杨万里</span><span class="smcs">《晓出净慈寺送林子方》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">映日不可逼视。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:映耀(辉映;照耀);映日(映照着日光);辉映(照耀，映射)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 反映，因光线照射而显出 <span class="encs">[reflect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狭石分花径，长桥映水门。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">北周</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《咏画屏风》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:垂杨柳倒映在水里;放映(利用强光装置把图片或影片上的形象照射在幕上或墙上);映入眼帘</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 映衬 <span class="encs">[set off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千里莺啼绿映红，水村山郭酒旗风。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《江南春绝句》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:映协(互相映衬和协);映媚(互相映衬而成一种美的景致)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 遮;隐藏 <span class="encs">[shade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疏条交映。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:映掩(掩映);映身(隐身);映蔽(遮蔽)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>映</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 阳光 <span class="encs">[sunshine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山岗有余映，岩阿增重阴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王粲</span><span class="smcs">《七哀》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 未时。午后一到 三时 <span class="encs">[afternoon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">梁元帝</span><span class="smcs">《纂要》</span>云:&ldquo;日在午曰亭，在未曰映。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杨慎</span><span class="smcs">《丹铅续录》</span></p>
