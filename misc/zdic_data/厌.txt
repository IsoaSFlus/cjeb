                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厌</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>厌</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">厭</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>嫌恶，憎恶：～恶（w&ugrave;）。讨～。～倦。喜新～旧。不～其详。学而不～。</li>
	<li>满足：贪得无～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dislike, detest, reject; satiate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> verabscheuen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> détester,en avoir assez de,répugner,être dégouté</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厌</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>厌</strong></p>
<p>厭 <span class="dicpy">yā</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一物压在另一物上 <span class="encs">[press]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厌，笮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。段注:&ldquo;此义今人字作压，乃古今字之殊。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地震陇西，厌四百余家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五行志下之上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如墙厌之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;彊国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厌目而视者，视一以为两。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;解蔽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厌覆(车翻人压)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指压制;抑制 <span class="encs">[restrain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东厌诸侯之权，西远羌胡之难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;翼奉传》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;厌，抑也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故贤人立朝，折冲厌难，胜于亡形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;辛庆忌传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;厌，抑也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厌抑(压制)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 以迷信的方法，镇服或驱避可能出现的灾祸，或致灾祸于人 <span class="encs">[pray for suppressing or driving away]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦始皇帝</span>常曰:&ldquo;东南有天子气&rdquo;，于是因东游以厌之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高祖纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厌法(厌胜法。是一种用咒符来制服对手的巫术)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 堵塞 <span class="encs">[block up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厌其源，开其渎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;修身》</span>。注:&ldquo;厌，塞也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>厌</strong></p>
<p>厭 <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从厂( <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>)，猒( <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。厂，象山崖石穴形。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;厌，笮也。&rdquo;意思是&ldquo;压&rdquo;。由&ldquo;犬、肉、甘&rdquo;三部分合起来，会意，表示&ldquo;吃饱&rdquo;、&ldquo;满足&rdquo;。本义:吃饱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 饱，满足。后作&ldquo;餍&rdquo; <span class="encs">[be satisfied]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学而不厌，诲人不倦，何有于我哉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不厌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>之欲无厌。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求之者无厌。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厌极(满足穷尽);厌饫(吃饱;吃腻;满足);厌塞(满足);厌伪(对奸邪巧伪感到满意);厌恋(以此为满足而眷恋);厌副(满足);厌足(满足)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 憎恶;嫌弃 <span class="encs">[loathe;abhor;detest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人常厌事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟厌事也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹厌言兵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姜夔</span><span class="smcs">《扬州慢》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无厌其为迂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这儿我住厌了。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹禺</span><span class="smcs">《雷雨》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厌世(现世;出丑露乖);厌厌地(无精打采地;微弱的样子);厌物(骂人话，指讨人厌的东西);厌钝(讨厌，扫兴);厌色(厌烦、不满的脸色);厌苦(厌烦以为苦事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yā</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厭</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“厌”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/厭">厭</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=厂" target="_blank">厂</a>】
                              厭·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：12</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/53AD.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】於葉切【集韻】【韻會】益涉切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>饜入聲。【說文】笮也。从厂，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/247A3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤞣</span>聲。【徐曰】笮，鎭也。壓也。一曰伏也。【左傳&middot;昭二十六年】將以厭衆。【前漢&middot;杜鄴傳】折衝厭難。【註】厭者，壓也。鎭壓<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨥</span>難，使之銷靡也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】順從貌。【荀子&middot;儒效篇】天下厭然猶一也。</p>
<p>又【增韻】禳也，當也。【史記&middot;高祖紀】於是因東游以厭之。</p>
<p>又合也。【周禮&middot;春官巾車】王后厭翟。【註】次其羽，使相迫也。</p>
<p>又損也。【左傳&middot;文二年】及晉處父盟以厭之。【註】厭猶損也。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;鄕飮酒禮】賔厭介入門左。【註】推手曰揖，引手曰厭。【疏】引手曰厭者，以手向身引之。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】惡夢也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】厭次，地名。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作壓。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>於豔切，饜去聲。【集韻】足也。【詩&middot;周頌】有厭其傑。【註】厭，受氣足也。【前漢&middot;王莽傳】克厭上帝之心。【註】厭，滿也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】厭，斁也。【詩&middot;葛覃】服之無斁。註：厭也。</p>
<p>又通作饜。【禮&middot;曾子問】孔子曰：有隂厭，有陽厭。【註】厭是饜飫之義。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】【集韻】於琰切。【正韻】於檢切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音掩。【荀子&middot;解蔽篇】厭目而視者，視一以爲兩。</p>
<p>又【正韻】惡也。【論語】天厭之。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】厭，魅也。別作魘。【韓愈&middot;游湘西寺詩】怵惕夢成魘。</p>
<p>又【正韻】與黶同。與掩藏之掩同音。【禮&middot;大學】見君子而后厭然。【註】厭讀爲黶。厭，閉藏貌。</p>
<p>又【集韻】於鹽切，音㤿。同懕。【說文】安也。【詩&middot;小雅】厭厭夜飮。【秦風】厭厭良人。【註】安靜也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】同<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/247A3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤞣</span>。【左傳&middot;昭二十八年】屬厭而已。【杜預註】言小人之腹飽，猶知厭足。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>乙甲切，音押。【前漢&middot;劉向傳】抑厭遂退。【註】師古曰：厭，音乙甲反，謂不伸也。</p>
<p>又【荀子&middot;儒效篇】厭旦於牧之野。【註】厭，猶臨也。謂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>於將旦之先也。</p>
<p>又【正韻】通作壓。</p>
<p>又【集韻】乙及切，音邑。厭邑，濕意。</p>
<p>又【集韻】鄔感切，音暗。沈溺意。【莊子&middot;逍遙遊】其厭也如緘。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/53AD.svg">                          <p>笮也。从厂猒聲。一曰合也。於輒切。又，一<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2494E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤥎</span>切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/53AD_655.svg" /><span class="swbf">(厭)</span><span class="ywyy">笮也。</span><span class="swdz">竹部曰：笮者，迫也。此義今人字作壓。乃古今字之殊。土部壓訓：壞也，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A04.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨄</span>也。無笮義，凡喪服言尊之所厭皆笮義。喪冠謂之厭冠，謂冠出武下也。周禮：巾車，王后厭翟。注云：次其羽使相迫也。禮經：推手曰揖，引手曰厭。厭卽尚書大傳，家語之葉拱。家語注云：兩手薄其心。古文禮揖，厭分別。今文禮厭皆爲揖。鄭不之從，而禮經有厭譌作撎者。周禮大祝疏竟作引手曰撎，斷不可從。撎爲跪而舉頭下手，與厭爲立而引手箸<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26644.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦙄</span>不相涉也。檀弓，死而不弔者厭。注：行止危險之下，巳上皆笮之義。其音於輒切。</span><span class="ywyy">从厂猒聲。</span><span class="swdz">於輒切，又一剡切。八部，按厭之本義笮也，合也。與壓義尚近，於猒飽也義則遠。而各書皆假厭爲猒足，猒憎字。猒足猒憎失其正字，而厭之本義䍐知之矣。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰合也。</span><span class="swdz">周語：克厭天心。韋注：厭，合也。韋注、漢書、敘傳亦同。按蒼頡篇云：伏合人心曰厭。字苑云：厭，眠內不祥也。此合義之一耑。㝥下云：寐而厭也，是也。俗字作魘，徐鉉用爲新附字。誤矣。山海經，服之使人不厭。郭云：不厭，不厭夢也，此厭字之冣古者也。其音一剡切。</span></p>                          </div>
