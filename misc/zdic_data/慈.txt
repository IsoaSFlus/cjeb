                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>慈</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">c&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>仁爱，和善：～爱。～善。～悲。～祥。仁～。～和。～眉善目。</li>
	<li>特指&ldquo;慈母&rdquo;，多用于对人称自己的母亲：家～。～闱。～颜。～命。</li>
	<li>对父母的孝敬奉养：孝子～孙。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> kind, charitable, benevolent</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gutmütig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bon,tendre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">慈</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>慈</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，兹声。本义:慈爱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[love;show affection or kindness to;kindly love]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慈，爱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">尧</span>不慈 <span class="diczx2">舜</span>不孝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;盗跖》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫慈者不忍，而惠者好与也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;内储说上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一曰慈幼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span>。注:&ldquo;爱幼少也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慈和遍服曰顺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十八年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">服</span>注:&ldquo;上爱下曰慈。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其次为慈母。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span>。注:&ldquo;慈母知其嗜欲者也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慈者，父母之高行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;形势解》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亲爱利子谓之慈，恻隐怜人谓之慈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父母威严而有慈，则子女畏慎而生孝矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:慈幼(慈爱幼儿);慈亲(慈爱双亲);慈泪(父母哀痛子女所流的眼泪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 对父母孝敬奉养 <span class="encs">[give presents to one&rsquo;s parents]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慈以旨甘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span>。注:&ldquo;爱敬进之也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽孝子慈孙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不慈孝于父母。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事亲则慈孝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;渔父》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:慈良(孝顺);慈孝(孝敬);慈命(对尊上命令的敬称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 佛教用语。佛、菩萨爱护众生，给予欢乐称为慈 <span class="encs">[give happiness]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:慈光(菩萨大慈大悲的光辉);慈悲;慈航(佛、菩萨以慈悲之心度人，如航船之济众，使脱离生死苦海)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>慈</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>和气，善良 <span class="encs">[kind;loving]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:慈父(慈祥的父亲);慈容(慈祥和蔼的容颜);慈颜(慈祥和蔼的容颜);慈明(犹慈光);慈景(犹慈颜)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>慈</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>慈母的省称 <span class="encs">[mother]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:慈旨(慈母的教诲);慈教(慈母的教诲)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6148.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】疾之切【集韻】【韻會】牆之切【正韻】才資切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音磁。【說文】愛也。从心兹聲。【精薀】父母之愛子也。【左傳&middot;昭二十六年】父慈子孝，姑慈婦聽。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;喪服】慈母如母，謂養母也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;內則】慈以甘旨。【註】慈，謂愛敬進之也。</p>
<p>又慈和，服物也。【左傳&middot;文十八年】宣慈惠和。【註】慈者，愛出於心，恩被於物也。</p>
<p>又【增韻】柔也，善也，仁也。</p>
<p>又石名。【精薀】山產鐵於其陽者，慈石產其隂。【郭璞&middot;慈石贊】慈石吸鐵，母子相戀也。俗作磁，非。</p>
<p>又竹名。【竹譜】竹之叢生，子母相依，曰慈竹。</p>
<p>又果名。【羣芳譜】慈姑，一根，歲生十二子，歲閏則十三子，如慈母之乳諸子也。</p>
<p>又州名。唐置慈州。</p>
<p>又姓。漢有慈仁，明有慈止。　从心从兹。【魏校曰】兹也者，養其子而使之長，心乎愛之。俗或譌作㤵。</p>
