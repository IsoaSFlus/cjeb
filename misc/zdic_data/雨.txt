class="smcs">《上大法颂表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 譬喻密集 <span class="encs">[dense]</span>。如:雨矢(箭矢像雨一样落下。比喻密集);雨注(像雨一样下降。比喻密集)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 譬喻离散 <span class="encs">[scattered]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风流云散，一别如雨。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">王粲</span><span
