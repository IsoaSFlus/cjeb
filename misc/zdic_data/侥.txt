                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>侥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">僥</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～幸〕➊希望得到不应该得的，如&ldquo;存在～～心理&rdquo;。➋获得意外的利益或意外地免去不幸的事，如&ldquo;～～成功&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>侥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">僥</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔僬～〕见&ldquo;<a href="/hans/僬">僬</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> be lucky; by chance, by luck</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fourbe</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">侥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>侥</strong></p>
<p>僥、儌 <span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，尧声。( <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ) 本义: 僬侥( <span class="dicpy">jiāoy&aacute;o</span>): 古代传说中的矮人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 贪求不止 <span class="encs">[be greedy for;request endlessly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不敢复有侥冀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:侥速(贪求速度);侥滥(贪得无厌不守法度);侥望(对财利的非分奢望)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申指希求意外成功或幸免 <span class="encs">[lucky]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此以人之国侥倖也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;在宥》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:侥天之幸(极其侥幸);侥利(侥幸所得之利);侥会(侥幸遇合)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 侥薄，即浇薄。指民俗浮薄 <span class="encs">[flighty;superficial]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不修廉隅以侥名当世。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答龚深父书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/侥幸">侥幸</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>侥</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 僥 <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &mdash;&mdash;&ldquo;僬侥&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">jiāoy&aacute;o</span>) <span class="encs">[Jiao yao state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南方有焦侥人，长三尺，短之极，从人，尧声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从中州以东，四十万里，得僬侥国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
