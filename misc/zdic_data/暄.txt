						<p>● <strong>暄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>温暖，太阳的温暖：～妍（天气和暖，景物明媚）。～暖。</li>
	<li>松软，松散：～土。馒头很～。～腾。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> warm, comfortable; warm, genial</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tiède,chaleur</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>暄</strong> <span class="dicpy">xuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，宣声。本义:温暖)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[warmth of the sun]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其性为暄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;五运行大论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叙温郁则寒谷成暄，论严苦则春丛零叶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘峻</span><span class="smcs">《广绝交论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:喧和(温暖和畅);暄风(和风，春风);暄妍(天气暖和，景色明媚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 炎热 <span class="encs">[hot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:暄气(暑热之气);暄寒(犹寒暑。亦指年岁);暄热(炎热)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈方〉∶松散柔软 <span class="encs">[fluffy]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:暄肿(松软胀肿);这馒头真暄;沙土暄</span></p>
