                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>孑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>单独，孤单：～然一身。茕茕～立。～遗生物（活化石）。</li>
	<li>〔～孓〕蚊的幼虫。通称&ldquo;跟头虫&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">戟</span>&rdquo;，古代兵器名。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> remaining, left-over; lonely</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> klein, unbedeutend ,Mückenlarven  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> seul</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">孑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>孑</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，象人缺右臂形。本义:缺少右臂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[without right-hand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孑，无右臂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 孤单，单独 <span class="encs">[lonely]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胡有孑然其效戎翟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何孤行之茕茕兮，孑不群而介立。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:孑居(孑栖。独居);孑弦(单弦);孑轮(一辆车)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 小 <span class="encs">[small]</span>。如:孑义(小仁小义);孑盾(兵车上用的小盾);孑孑(细行，小惠)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 剩余 <span class="encs">[residual]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孑，余也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:孑黎(遗民;残存的百姓);孑余(剩余;残余)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 特立，出众。多指才能、人品等 <span class="encs">[outstanding]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孑，俊也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孑孑干旄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鄘风&middot;干旄》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:孑杰(卓立，特出);孑然(特立;又指孤立，孤单)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>孑</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>戟。古代的一种兵器 <span class="encs">[trident]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚武王</span><span class="diczx2">荆</span>尸，授师孑焉，以伐 <span class="diczx2">随</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公四年》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:&ldquo;尸，陈也。 <span class="diczx2">荆</span>亦 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>也，更为 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>陈兵之法。 <span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《方言》</span>:&lsquo;孑者，戟也。&rsquo;&rdquo;</p>

                              孑·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=3" target="_blank">3</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5B51.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】居列切【集韻】【韻會】吉列切【正韻】古屑切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音結。【說文】人無右臂形。【玉篇】單也，餘也，後也，短也。【集韻】健也。【詩&middot;大雅】靡有孑遺。言周之民無復有遺者。</p>
<p>又孑孑，特出貌。【詩&middot;鄘風】孑孑干旌。</p>
<p>又戟也。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;大司馬司戈盾註】戈有旁出者爲句孑。【左傳&middot;莊四年】楚武王荆尸，授師孑焉以伐隨。【揚子&middot;方言】凡戟而無刅謂之孑。俗作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28942.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨥂</span>。</p>
<p>又孑孒，水中赤蟲。游水際，遇人則沈。俗呼沙蟲。一名蜎蠉。【淮南子&middot;說林訓】孑孒爲蟁。【註】倒跂蟲也，化爲蟁。蟁卽蚊也。</p>
<p>又姓。見【奇姓通】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】激質切，音吉。義同。</p>
