                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>怕</strong> <span class="dicpy">pà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，白声。怕就是怖，<span class="yhcs">“怕”</span>是中古的新字。本义:畏惧)同本义 <span class="encs">[fear;dread;be afraid of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怕，怕惧。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鬼神怕嘲咏。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span 又如:惧怕(害怕);害怕(遇到困难、危险等而心中不安或发慌);怕怯(害怕)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>怕</strong> <span class="dicpy">pà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" class="yhcs">“也许”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“或许”</span>;在现代汉语里，句尾常带有<span class="yhcs">“吧”</span> <span class="encs">[I’m afraid;perhaps;maybe]</span>。如:怕是有什么意外，平时他总是准时来的;怕不(恐怕，也许);怕敢(恐怕也许)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 岂，难道 <span class="encs">[as]</span>。如:怕不待(岂不，难道不)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如其，倘 <span
