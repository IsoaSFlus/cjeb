                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>饔</strong> <span class="dicpy">yōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 熟食 <span class="encs">[cooked food]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">酒车酌礼，方驾授饔。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饔餐(饭食)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 专指早餐 <span class="encs">[breakfast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贤者与民并耕而食，饔飧而治。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公》</span>。注:&ldquo;饔飧，熟食也，朝曰饔，夕曰飧。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">但是</span><span class="diczx2">贾昌</span>在家，朝饔夕餐，也还成个规矩，口中假意奉承几句。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醒世恒言》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代诸侯正式行聘礼时所送的肉类、已杀之牲 <span class="encs">[gift of cooked meat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:饔饩(古代祭品。已杀的称饔，生牲称饩)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>饔</strong> <span class="dicpy">yōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 做饭;烹煮 <span class="encs">[cook]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亲二宫之饔膳，致晨昏之定省。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;杜周传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饔人(官名。掌切割烹调之事);饔夫(饔子。厨工);饔膳(备办酒饭)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=食" target="_blank">食</a>】
                              饔·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=22" target="_blank">22</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9954.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>於容切，音邕。【玉篇】熟食也。【詩&middot;小雅】有母之尸饔。【傳】熟食曰饔。【孟子】饔飧而治。【註】饔飧，熟食也。朝曰饔，夕曰飧。</p><p>又【周禮&middot;天官&middot;內饔註】饔，割烹煎和之稱。【疏】饔，和也，熟食曰饔。熟食須調和，故號曰饔。</p><p>又【儀禮&middot;聘禮】君使卿韋弁歸饔餼五牢。【註】牲殺曰饔，生曰餼。</p><p>又通作雝。【周語】佐雝者嘗焉。</p><p>又【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>於用切，雍去聲。義同。</p>
