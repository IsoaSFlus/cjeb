                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">响</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>响</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">響</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄤˇ</span></p><ol><li>声音：～声。～箭。～马（旧称在路上抢劫财物的强盗，因抢劫时先放响箭而得名）。音～（a.声音，多就声音所产生的效果说；b.泛称收音、录音、扩音等设备）。</li><li>发出声音：钟～了。</li><li>声音高，声音大：～亮。～彻云霄（响声直达高空，形容声音十分嘹亮）。</li><li>回声：～应（<span class="dicpy">yìng </span>）。如～斯应（喻反应迅速）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> make sound, make noise; sound</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ertönen; Echo, Geräusch; laut</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sonner,retentir,bruit,bruyant,sonore</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">响</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>响</strong></p><p>響 <span class="dicpy">xiǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，鄉声。本义:回声)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[echo]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">響，声也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">炎光飞响。——<span class="smcs">《剧秦美新》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黄玉响应。——<span class="smcs">《史晨奏铭》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其受命也如响。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">空谷传响，哀转久绝。——<span class="smcs">《水经注·江水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">云集响应。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绝无踪响。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:响臻(响应纷至);响彻;响效(同响应)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 声音 <span class="encs">[sound]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泠泠作响。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">歌台暖响。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">枹止响腾，余韵徐歇。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">群响毕绝。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:响丁丁(响泠泠。形容清脆的响声);响卜(借听到声音来占卜吉凶);响钞(指硬币)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 音讯 <span class="encs">[message]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">(</span><span class="diczx2">邓</span>) <span class="diczx2">艾</span>得书，大喜，即报书。——<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">裴松之</span>注引<span class="smcs">《蜀书》</span>:思闻嘉响。</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>响</strong></p><p>響 <span class="dicpy">xiǎng</span> <span
