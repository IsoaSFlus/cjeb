						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>拢</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">攏</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄥˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>凑起，总合：～共。～总。归～。</li>
	<li>靠近，船只靠岸：～岸。拉～。</li>
	<li>使不松散或不离开：收～。～音。把孩子～在怀里。</li>
	<li>梳，用梳子整理头发：～一～头发。</li>
	<li>合上，聚集：她笑得嘴都合不～了。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> collect, bring together</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> sammeln, einsammeln</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> approcher,attacher,lier,peigner</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>拢</strong></p>
<p>攏 <span class="dicpy">lǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，龙声。本义:聚合，合拢)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gather together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拢万川乎巴梁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《江赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拢钱</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 总计;合计 <span class="encs">[sum up]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:拢一拢帐;拢统(总共)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 梳理;整理 <span class="encs">[comb]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">湘云</span>]</span>便在石蹬上重新匀了脸，拢了鬓，连忙起身，同着来至 <span class="diczx2">红香圃</span>中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 傍靠 <span class="encs">[come alongside]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知郎旧时意，且请拢船头。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;丁仙芝&middot;江南曲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拢家(回家);拢岸;拢船(撑船靠岸);拢边(靠近旁边)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 接近 <span class="encs">[be near]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:拢帐(走近)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 弹奏弦乐器的一种指法。用指在弦上上下按捺 <span class="encs">[make a light tap on pipa string]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轻拢慢捻抹复挑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p>
