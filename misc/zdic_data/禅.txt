						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>禅</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">禪</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chán.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>佛教指静思：坐～。参（<span class="dicpy">cān</span>）～。～心。～机（佛教禅宗启发门徒悟道时使用的隐语、比喻以及带有暗示性的动作等）。～宗。～定。</li><li>特指佛教的：～师。～杖。～林。～堂。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>禅</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">禪</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>帝王的祭地之礼：封～。</li><li>帝王让位给别人：～位。～让。受～。</li><li>事物更（<span class="dicpy">gēng </span>）代。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>禅</strong></p><p>禪 <span class="dicpy">chán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> )本义:古代帝王辟基祭地) 佛教语。梵语<span class="yhcs">“禅那”</span>之略。原指静坐默念 <span class="encs">[deep meditation]</span>。如:禅法(佛法);禅坐(指僧侣端坐静修)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>禅</strong></p><p>禪 <span class="dicpy">chán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示与佛教有关的事物 <span class="encs">[Buddhist]</span>。如:禅门(佛教禅宗的教门);禅床(和尚用来打坐和睡觉的床)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 指禅房 <span class="encs">[a monastic room]</span>。如:禅室(禅房);禅阁(禅房)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">shàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禅房">禅房</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禅机">禅机</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禅林">禅林</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禅师">禅师</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禅堂">禅堂</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禅院">禅院</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禅杖">禅杖</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禅宗">禅宗</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>禅</strong></p><p>禪 <span class="dicpy">shàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从示，单声。从<span class="yhcs">“示”</span>的字多与鬼神祭祀有关。本义:古代帝王祭地礼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[worship]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禅，祭天也。——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/79AA.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>時戰切，音繕。封禪。【韻會】築土曰封，除地曰禪。古者天子巡守，至於四岳，則封泰山而祭天，禪小山而祭山川。舜典，歲二月東巡守至于岱宗，柴望秩于山川是也。齊桓公欲封泰山，管仲設辭拒之，謂非有符瑞，不可封禪。至秦始皇惑於神仙之說，欲禱祠以求長生，遂以封禪爲異典。項氏曰：除地爲墠，後改曰禪，神之矣。</p><p>又代也。禪讓，傳與也。【孟子】唐虞禪。　一作嬗。【前漢·律歷志】堯嬗以天下。【師古往】嬗，古禪讓字也。</p><p>又通作擅。【荀子·正論篇】堯舜擅遜。【註】與禪同。</p><p>又作儃。【揚子·法言】允哲堯儃舜之重。【註】同禪。</p><p>又漢書禪多作䄠。詳䄠字註。</p><p>又【廣韻】市連切【集韻】【韻會】時連切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蟬。靜也。浮圖家有禪說。【傳燈錄】禪有五，外道禪，凡夫禪，小乗禪，大乗禪，最上乗禪。【杜甫詩】虛空不離禪。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/79AA.svg">                          <p>祭天也。从示單聲。時戰切</p>                          </div>

                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">祭天也。从示。單聲。</span>凡封土爲壇。除地爲墠。古封禪字葢衹作墠。項威曰。除地爲墠。後改墠曰禪。神之矣。服虔曰。封者、增天之高。歸功於天。禪者、廣土地。應劭亦云。封爲增高。禪爲祀地。惟張晏云。天高不可及。於泰山上立封。又禪而祭之。冀近神靈也。元鼎二年紀云。望見泰一。修天文䄠。䄠卽古禪字。是可證禪亦祭天之名。但禪訓祭天。似當與祡爲伍。不當廁此。時戰切。十四部。                          </div>
