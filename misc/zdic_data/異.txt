                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>異</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>不同的：～乎。～說。～常。～己（與自己意見不同或利害相衝突的人）。～端（舊時指不符合正統思想的主張或教義，如&ldquo;～～邪說&rdquo;）。～化。～性。～樣。大同小～。～曲同工。</li>
	<li>分開：離～。～居。</li>
	<li>另外的，別的：～日。～地。～國。～鄉。～類。</li>
	<li>特別的：奇～。～聞。～彩。奇才～能（特殊的才能）。</li>
	<li>奇怪：驚～。詫～。怪～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">異</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>異</strong></p>
<p>異 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (會意。&ldquo;異&rdquo;，甲骨文字形，象個有手、腳、頭的人形。從廾( <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span>)從畀( <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span>)。畀，予也。本義:奇特;奇異;奇怪)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本義 <span class="encs">[strange;queer;odd]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">異，怪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">異，奇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《廣韻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何以異哉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;楊朱》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">異乎交益之士。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都賦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婦人異甚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《戰國策&middot;趙策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">產異蛇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者說》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">覺無異能。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父異焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《傷仲永》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:異疾(奇怪的病症);異形(怪異或奇特的形狀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 不同 <span class="encs">[different]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">禮者爲異。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《禮記&middot;樂記》</span>。注:&ldquo;謂別貴賤也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事爲異別。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《禮記&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">執心各異。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">漢</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">劉向</span><span class="smcs">《列女傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得無異乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳陽樓記》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或異二者。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何異以孤羊投羣狼?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗類鈔&middot;戰事類》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:異俗(風俗異於漢族的少數民族);異姓陌路(既不同姓也不認識);異手(指不同之人);異世(不同時代);異目(另眼)</span></p>
<p><strong>詞性變化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>異</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 其他，別的 <span class="encs">[other]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">賈不敢爲異事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《呂氏春秋&middot;上農》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">無它異也。(意思是，沒有別的意外的事)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《後漢書&middot;列女傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:異域茅草(蠻荒之地的鄉野村夫);異土(他鄉;別處)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>異</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈動〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 護翼 <span class="encs">[shelter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古天異臨子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盂鼎》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 區別;分開 <span class="encs">[distinguish;divide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">異是非，明好惡，檢奸邪，消佚亂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新語》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:異分(區別);異居(分居)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 覺得奇怪;詫異 <span class="encs">[be surprised;be astonished]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">漁人甚異之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶潛</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源記》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特別優待;特別重視 <span class="encs">[give preferential treatment;pay attention to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:異擢(破格提拔);異待(特別禮遇);異恩(特殊恩遇);異遇(特別優待);異謀(特別的計謀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;翼&rdquo;。恭敬 <span class="encs">[respect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">極明與與，有畏勸汝，何異非汝，何畏非世。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《逸周書》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;翼&rdquo;。輔助 <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令行禁止王始也&hellip;出三日無適異;出四日無適與。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《逸周書》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>異</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指以往，以前 <span class="encs">[before;formerly;in the past]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:異時(往時;從前);異代(前代;前世)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 指以後，將來 <span class="encs">[after;later;future]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:異日(來日;以後);異時(以後;他時);異世(後代;後世);異代(後代;後世)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 怪異不祥之事;災異 <span class="encs">[strange]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">己巳，日有食之。何以書?記異也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指特殊本領、才能 <span class="encs">[special skill;special ability]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">覺無異能者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舉天下所貢蝴蝶&hellip;一切異狀遍試之，無出其右者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊齋志異&middot;促織》</span></span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>異</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄧˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>y&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5917.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">特別的、不平常的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>異</b>士」、「<b>異</b>術」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷六七&middot;仲尼弟子傳》：「孔子曰：『受業身通者七十有七人。』皆異能之士也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《後漢書&middot;卷五八&middot;臧洪傳》：「洪體貌魁梧，有<b>異</b>姿。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">另外的、其他的。</span><span class="gc_yy">《呂氏春秋&middot;士容論&middot;上農》：「農不敢行，賈不敢為異事。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;王維〈九月九日憶山東兄弟〉詩：「獨在異鄉為異客，每逢佳節倍思親。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">不同的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「異口同聲」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;旅獒》：「王乃昭德之致於異姓之邦，無替厥服。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;盧照鄰〈南陽公集序〉：「異議蜂起，高談不息。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">分開。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;曲禮上》：「群居五人，則長者必<b>異</b>席。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷六八&middot;商君傳》：「民有二男以上不分<b>異</b>者，倍其賦。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">奇怪。</span><span class="gc_yy">《後漢書&middot;卷四四&middot;徐防傳》：「防體貌矜嚴，占對可觀，顯宗<b>異</b>之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot; 陶淵明〈桃花源記〉：「忽逢桃花林，夾岸數百步，中無雜樹，芳草鮮美，落英繽紛，漁人甚<b>異</b>之。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如唐代有異牟尋。</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7570.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22116.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢄖</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】羊吏切【正韻】以智切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>移去聲。【說文】分也，从廾从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2233F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢌿</span>。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2233F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢌿</span>予也。【博雅】異分也。【史記&middot;商君傳】民有二男以上，不分異者，倍其賦。</p>
<p>又不同也。【書&middot;旅獒】王乃昭德之，致于異姓之邦。【禮&middot;儒行】同弗與異弗非也。【疏】謂彼人與己之疏異，所爲是善，則不非毀之也。　　又怪也。【釋名】異者，異於常也。【左傳&middot;昭二十六年】據有異焉。【註】異猶怪也。【史記&middot;屈賈傳】化爲異物兮，又何足患。</p>
<p>又奇也。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;質人】掌成市之貨賄，人民，牛馬，兵器，珍異。【註】珍異，四時食物。【史記&middot;仲尼弟子傳】受業身通者七十有七人，皆異能之士也。</p>
<p>又違也。</p>
<p>又姓。唐異牟尋歸唐，冊封南詔王，今白水蠻有此姓。</p>
<p>又異，翹草名。【爾雅&middot;釋草】連異翹。</p>
<p>又無名異藥名，主治金創折傷。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶延知切，音怡。【詩&middot;邶風】洵美且異。叶下貽。</p>
<p>又叶弋質切，音逸。【詩&middot;小雅】亦祇以異。【朱註】逸織反。【無名氏樂德歌】所見奇異，叶甘美酒食。</p>
