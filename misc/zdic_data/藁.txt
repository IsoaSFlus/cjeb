<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>藁</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;稾(稿)&rdquo;。禾秆 <span class="encs">[stalk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">儿馁嗔郎罢，妻寒望稾砧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">唐庚</span><span class="smcs">《自笑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:藁砧(稻草与砧板，古代行刑时的用具);藁人(用草扎成的人形);藁秸(稻、麦等的秆子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;稾(稿)&rdquo;。稿子，草稿 <span class="encs">[draft]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:藁草(草稿)</span></p>

<p>〈形〉</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">藁</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              藁·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：14</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/85C1.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古老切，音杲。【唐韻】俗槀字。【正字通】木枯也。</p>
<p>又藁席。【荀子&middot;正名篇】屋室廬宇葭藁蓐，尚機筵，而可以養形。【註】以藁爲席，貧賤人之居也。</p>
<p>又藁本，藥名。【管子&middot;地員篇】五臭疇生蓮與蘼蕪、藁本、白芷。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;屈原傳】屬草藁。【索隱註】創制憲令之本。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;陳湯傳】斬郅支及名王以下，縣頭藁街。</p>
<p>又【韻補】車御切。【易林】蝗齧我稻，驅不可去。實穗無有，但見空藁。　【正字通】引《漢書&middot;馬援傳》藁葬註，草也。○按藳葬，藁字从禾，不从木。</p>
