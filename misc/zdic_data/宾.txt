                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">宾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>宾</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">賓</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>客人：～客。来～。～馆。～主。贵～。～至如归。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/傧">傧</a>&rdquo;，傧相。</li>
	<li>服从，归顺：～服。～附。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">宾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>宾</strong></p>
<p>賓 <span class="dicpy">bīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，冥( <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span>)声。甲骨文字形，会意。上面象屋形，下面是&ldquo;人&rdquo;和&ldquo;止&rdquo;。表示客人来到屋下，即宾客到门。金文将&ldquo;止&rdquo;改为&ldquo;贝&rdquo;，小篆从之。王国维说:&ldquo;金文及小篆易从止为从贝者，乃后起之字。&rdquo;本义:地位尊贵、受人尊敬的客人，贵客)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[guest;visitor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">賓，所敬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，从貝者，宾礼必有贽。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宾者，接人以义者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乡饮酒义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谋宾介。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡饮礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;贤者为宾，其次为介，又其次为众宾。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸侯、诸伯、诸子、诸男之相为宾也。诸公之臣相为国客，是散文宾客通称，对称则宾尊而客卑，宾大而客小。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我有嘉宾，鼓瑟吹笙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鹿鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宾出，主人拜送。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相待如宾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众宾团坐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:来宾;宾鸿(大雁。因是侯鸟，往来守时，有如宾客，故称);宾东(雇员和东家);宾人(宾相;赞礼的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">率土之宾，莫非王臣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;王莽传中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Bin prefecture]</span>。汉郁林郡地。唐贞观五年置宾州，亦曰安城郡。至德二年改为领方郡。元初为宾州路，寻复为宾州。清属广西思恩府。今为宾阳县</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>宾</strong></p>
<p>賓 <span class="dicpy">bīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">賓</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“宾”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/賓">賓</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=貝" target="_blank">貝</a>】
                              賓·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8CD3.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27D89.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧶉</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21AC5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡫅</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2598E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥦎</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/219CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡧍</span>【唐韻】必鄰切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>民切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音濱。【說文】所敬也。【玉篇】客也。【易&middot;觀卦】利用賓于王。【書&middot;舜典】賓于四門。</p>
<p>又【洪範】八政，七曰賓。【傳】禮賓客無不敬。【儀禮&middot;士冠禮】主人戒賓。【註】賓，主人之僚友。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】賓，服也。【疏】賓者，懷德而服。【新序】先王所以拱揖指揮，而四海賓者，誠德之至已形于外，故《詩》曰：王猷允塞，徐方旣來。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;堯典】寅賓出日。【傳】賓，導也。【釋文】從也。</p>
<p>又律名。【禮&middot;月令】律中蕤賓。【註】仲夏氣至，則蕤賓之律應。【白虎通】蕤者，下也。賓者，敬也，言陽氣上極，隂氣始賓敬之也。</p>
<p>又罽賓，西域國名。【前漢&middot;西域傳】罽賓國王治循鮮城。</p>
<p>又姓。【玉海】賓氏，周有賓滑、賓起，齊有賓胥無、賓媚人。</p>
<p>又官名。【唐書&middot;百官志】司賓、典賓、掌賓各二人。</p>
<p>又龍賓。【陶家瓶餘事】明皇御案墨，一日見小道士如蠅，呼萬歲。曰：臣，墨之精，黑松使者也。凡世有文者，墨上有龍賓十二。上神之，乃以分賜掌文官。</p>
<p>又野賓。【王氏見聞錄】王仁裕有猿，小而慧黠，名曰野賓。</p>
<p>又【集韻】必刃切，音殯。【書&middot;多士】予惟四方罔攸賓。【釋文】徐音殯，馬云：卻也。【史記&middot;蘇秦傳】其次必長賓之。【註】次計長擯棄關西。【莊子&middot;徐無鬼】先生居山林，以賓寡人。【音義】賓或作擯，棄也。</p>
