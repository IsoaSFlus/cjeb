                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>轰</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">轟</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>形容大的声响：～～。～响。～鸣。～动。</li>
	<li>用大炮或炸弹破坏：～击。～炸。炮～。</li>
	<li>驱逐，赶走：～走。～出去。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rumble, explosion, blast</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> knallen, bumsen  (V)​,vertreiben, aufscheuchen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (onom.)​ boum!,gronder,bombarder,explosion,attaquer,chasser (au loin)​,expulser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">轰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>轰</strong></p>
<p>轟、輷 <span class="dicpy">hōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;象&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从三车。本义:群车行驶声)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[roll;rumble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轰，群车声也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">轰轰殷殷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;苏秦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:轰隐(成群车队的喧闹声);轰輵(象声词。形容车声、雷声等);轰轰阗阗(形容车马声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 雷鸣、炮击、爆破等的隆隆声 <span class="encs">[boom;bang]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:轰!轰!轰!一连串爆破声震撼山谷;轰雷掣电(雷声炸响，闪电劈空。形容心灵受到巨大冲击)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>轰</strong></p>
<p>轟 <span class="dicpy">hōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 枪炮、雷电等对准或瞄准爆发 <span class="encs">[explode;rumble;bombard]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:炮轰;雷轰;雷轰电闪;轰打(用炮火轰击);轰炮(开炮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 猛烈攻击 <span class="encs">[attack]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:他在议会上轰市长;写文章轰他</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 驱赶;赶开 <span class="encs">[shoo away;expel;drive]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:轰麻雀;轰魄(驱赶魂魄。迷信);轰蚊(驱赶蚊虫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 笑闹;狂放 <span class="encs">[roar]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:轰豗(喧哗吵闹的声音);轰醉(狂饮而大醉);轰腾(形容气势旺盛);轰斗(群聚哄闹打斗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 眩晕 <span class="encs">[feel dizzy;feel giddy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请大夫把病来调，我涩的难行立，轰的则待倒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张国宾</span><span class="smcs">《罗李郎》</span></p>
