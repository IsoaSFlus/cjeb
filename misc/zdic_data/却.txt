                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">却</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>却</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩㄝˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>退：～步（因畏惧或厌恶而后退，如&ldquo;望而～～&rdquo;）。退～。</li>	<li>退还，不受：盛情难～。</li>	<li>表示转折：我来了，他～走了。</li>	<li>去掉：失～。了（<span class="dicpy">liǎo</span>）～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> still, but; decline; retreat</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aber, jedoch, dennoch ,zurückweisen, ablehnen, ausschlagen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mais,cependant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">却</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>却</strong></p>
<p>却 <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本作&ldquo;却&rdquo;，俗字作&ldquo;却&rdquo;。从卩，谷( <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。卩( <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span>)，象人下跪的样子，即腿骨节屈曲的样子。从&ldquo;卩&rdquo;与脚的活动有关。本义:退)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[step back;retreat;withdraw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却，节欲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按:&ldquo;退也&rdquo;。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却行仄行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;梓人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾行却曲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;人间世》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怒战栗而却。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却下而载之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;既夕礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却骐骥以转运兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;愍命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北救</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>而却秦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已却</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>存 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">持璧却立。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曳笏却立。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:退却(向后撤退;畏难后退);却走(退避;退走);却还(退还);却避(退避);却归(退归);却略(退身);却去(后退;离去);却生(后退贪生);却立(后退站立)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 除;去 <span class="encs">[get rid of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:却扇(古代婚礼行礼时，新娘以扇遮面，交拜后去扇);却惑(去邪;消除疑难);了却(了结)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 避;避免 <span class="encs">[avoid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是时</span><span class="diczx2">李少君</span>亦以祠灶谷道却老方见上，上尊之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;封禅书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:却老(避免衰老);却死(避死)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 拒绝 <span class="encs">[refuse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:却情勿过(碍着情面推辞不掉);却绝(拒绝);却妆(拒受嫁妆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 回转;返回 <span class="encs">[turn back]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:却回(回转);却望(回头远望);却顾(回顾，回转头看)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 反复 <span class="encs">[repeat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:却论(反复论难);却顾(反复考虑)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>却</strong> <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">连</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用在偏正复句的正句中，提出跟偏句相反或不一致的动作、行为或状况，表示转折，相当于&ldquo;但&rdquo;、&ldquo;但是&rdquo;、&ldquo;可是&rdquo; <span class="encs">[but]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一连数日，</span><span class="diczx2">施恩</span>来了大牢里三次，却不提防被 <span class="diczx2">张团练</span>家心腹人见了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &ldquo;反而&rdquo;&ldquo;然而&rdquo; <span class="encs">[at the same time]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:这个吓得胆战心惊、惶惶不安并满腹牢骚的人&hellip;却具有掌权和发号施令的能力</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>却</strong> <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">助</span></p>
<p>用在动词后，相当于&ldquo;掉&rdquo;，&ldquo;去&rdquo; <span class="encs">[off;out;away]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冷却;忘却;失却信心;抛却旧包袱</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>却</strong> <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 固然 <span class="encs">[admittedly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">好却十分好了，只是一件，这孩儿生下来，昼夜啼哭，乳也不肯吃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《喻世明言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 再 <span class="encs">[then]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却与小姑别。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却看妻子愁何在。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《闻官军收河南河北》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却坐促弦弦转急。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:却又(再);却再(再)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 还 <span class="encs">[still]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:却是(还是)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 才 <span class="encs">[just]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:却才(方才)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 恰;正 <span class="encs">[just]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:却好(恰好;正好);却如(恰如;好像);却待(恰待;正要)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 刚刚 <span class="encs">[a moment ago]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:却才(刚才)</span></p>
