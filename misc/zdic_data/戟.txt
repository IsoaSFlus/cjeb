                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>戟</strong></p><p>戟 <span class="dicpy">jǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从戈，从榦( <span class="dicpy">gàn</span>)省。本义:古代兵器。青铜制，将矛、戈合成一体，既能直刺，又能横击)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[halberd]</span>。一种可勾可刺的兵器， 出现于商、周，盛行于战国、汉晋各代。戟是一种分枝状兵器</p><p><span class="diczx1">戟，有枝兵也。…今字误作戟。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戟广寸有半。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·冶氏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“今三锋戟也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">修我矛戟。——<span class="smcs">《诗·秦风·无衣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">交戟之卫士。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刀戟纵横。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:戟戈(戟和戈，泛指兵器);戟盾(戟和盾);戟牙(戟上横出的刃);戟吏(仪仗中持戟的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 仪仗名 <span class="encs">[guard of honour's name]</span>。如:戟节(戟级。戟仗和符节);戟槊(门戟和长矛);戟幢(门戟和饰以羽毛的旗帜)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>戟</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 伸出食指和中指来指人<span class="encs">[point at another and scold him]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拱此而揖人，人莫不喜;戟此而詈人，人莫不怒。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《后怪石供》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:戟手(伸出食指和中指指人，其状似戟，表示愤怒或勇武的情状)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 刺激 <span class="encs">[stimulate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其根辛苦，戟人咽喉。——<span class="smcs">《本草纲目·大戟》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:戟口(刺口，唇受刺激如戟刺);戟喉(喉受刺激)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                     <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戟</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>戟</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jǐ <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3068.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">武器名。戈和矛的合体，兼有勾、啄、撞、刺四种功能。装于木柄或竹柄上。出现于商、周，盛行于战国、汉、晋各代。南北朝后渐被枪取代，转而为仪仗、卫门的器物。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷六五·孙子吴起传》：「孙子分为二队，以王之宠姬二人各为队长，皆令持<b>戟</b>。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/621F.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕屰【廣韻】几據切【集韻】訖逆切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音㻷。有枝兵也。【增韻】雙枝爲戟，單枝爲戈。【釋名】戟，格也，傍有枝格也。【典略】周有雍狐之戟。【周禮·冬官考工記】戟廣寸有半寸，內三之，胡四之，援五之。【註】戟，今三鋒戟也。內長四寸半，胡長六寸，援長七寸半。</p><p>又地名。【戰國策】秦舉安邑，而塞女戟。【註】女戟在太行西。</p><p>又與棘通。【周禮·天官·掌舍棘門註】以戟爲門。【左傳·隱十一年】子都拔棘以逐之。【註】棘戟也。【禮·明堂位】越棘大弓。【註】棘戟同。</p><p>又【韻補】叶訖約切，音腳。【詩·秦風】脩我矛戟，與子偕作。【揚子·太玄經】比札爲甲，冠矜爲戟。被甲荷戟，以威不恪。　【說文】作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/229E2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢧢</span>。</p><p>
