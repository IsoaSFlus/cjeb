                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>败</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">敗</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bài  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bài.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄞˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>输，失利，不成功，与“<span class="diczx3">胜</span>”相对：～北。～退。～绩（❶连队溃败；❷事业的失利）。～诉。～笔。～局。两～俱伤。</li>    <li>战胜，使失败：大～敌军。</li>    <li>毁坏：～坏。～露。</li>    <li>解除，消散：～火。～毒。</li>    <li>破旧，衰落，腐烂：～絮。～落。～兴（<span class="dicpy">xìng </span>）（情绪低落）。腐～。叶残花～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">败</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>败</strong></p><p>敗 <span class="dicpy">bài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从贝，从攴。攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，甲骨文象以手持杖，敲击的意思，汉字部首之一。在现代汉字中，<span class="yhcs">“攴”</span>大多写成<span class="yhcs">“攵”</span>，只有极少数字保留着<span class="yhcs">“攴”</span>的写法。从<span class="yhcs">“攴”</span>的字多与打、敲、击等手的动作有关。败，甲骨文左边是<span class="yhcs">“鼎”</span>字(小篆简作<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>)，右边是<span class="yhcs">“攴”</span>，表示以手持棍击鼎。本义:毁坏，搞坏)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ruin;fail;spoil]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">败，毁也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无俾正败。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·民劳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若唇之与击，空柔相摩而不致败。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">能全天之所生而勿败之。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·尊师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">法败则国乱。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">败家丧身。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">以侈自败者多矣。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是纵散约败。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:败辕裂犁(比喻把事情弄坏);败名(败坏名声);败好(败坏盟好);败事(败坏事情);败家相(败坏家业的情状);败俗(败坏风俗)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 损害;损伤 <span class="encs">[damage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">死而形体朽，精气散，犹囊橐穿败，粟米弃出也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蠹鱼败书编，萍草粘户半。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《久雨喜晴十韵》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:败足(伤脚);败物(破损之物);败面(毁伤面容);败群(危害集体)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 失败;战败 <span class="encs">[lose the battle;be defeated]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>师败矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故善战者，立于不败之地。——<span class="smcs">《孙子兵法·形篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">赵</span>尝五战于 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>，二败而三胜。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6557.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕贁貝<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24590.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤖐</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>簿邁切，音浿。【說文】毀也。【爾雅·釋言】覆也。【釋名】潰也。</p><p>又壞也。【玉篇】破也。【增韻】損也。</p><p>又頹也。【易·需卦】敬愼不敗也。【書·大禹謨】反道敗德。</p><p>又【左傳·文十年司敗註】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>楚名司<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡨥</span>爲司敗。【論語】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>司敗問，昭公知禮乎。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋器】肉謂之敗。【註】臭壞。【論語】魚餒而肉敗。</p><p>又【廣韻】補邁切【集韻】北邁切【韻會】【正韻】布怪切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音拜。破他曰敗。【增韻】凡物不自敗而敗之，則北邁切。物自毀壞，則薄邁切。【詩·召南】勿翦勿敗。【音義】敗，必邁反。</p><p>又【左傳·莊十一年】凡師敵未<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>曰敗，大崩曰敗績。【顏氏家訓】江南學士讀左傳，口相傳述，自爲凡例。軍自敗曰敗，打破人軍曰敗，讀補敗反，諸記傳未見補敗反。徐仙民讀左氏，唯一處有此音。</p><p>又不言自敗敗人之別，此爲穿鑿耳。</p><p>又【韻補】叶蒲昧切。【荀子·賦篇】功立而身廢，事成而家敗。棄其耆老，收其後世。</p>
