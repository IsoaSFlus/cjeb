class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[love]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">媚，说也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">思媚</span><span with]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人服媚之如是。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若是乃能媚于神。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲媚上官。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">甘言媚词。——</span><span class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 姿态可爱，侧重于婀娜多姿，逗人爱 <span class="encs">[charming;enchanting;fascinating]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">媚，美也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">媚，好也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烟视媚行。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·不屈》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">石韫玉而山辉，水怀珠而川媚。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《文赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绿筱(竹)媚清涟。——</span><span class="diczx2">谢灵运</span><span class="smcs">《过始宁墅》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:明媚(景物鲜明可爱;眼睛明亮动人);媚好(美好;娇美);媚秀(娇美秀丽);媚曼(娇美);媚景(谓春景;美好的景物);媚娇(容貌声音妩媚动人);媚趣(优美动人的意趣)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“魅”</span>。具有魅力，具有诱惑力或吸引力的 <span
