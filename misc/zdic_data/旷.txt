                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旷</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>旷</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">曠</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄨㄤˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>空阔：空～。～远。～野。地～人稀。</li>
	<li>开朗，心境阔大：～达。心～神怡。</li>
	<li>相互配合的东西之间空隙过大：这双鞋穿着太～了。</li>
	<li>荒废，耽误：～工。～时持久。～日经年。</li>
	<li>长时间所无：～代伟人（当代无人比得上的伟大人物）。～古绝伦。盛世～典（兴盛时代的罕见难逢的隆重典礼）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> extensive, wide, broad; empty</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> öde, überflüssig ,Kuang  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> manquer,négliger,vaste,étendu,désert</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旷</strong></p>
<p>曠 <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会义兼形声。从日，广声。本义:光明;明朗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旷，明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旷，光明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此人谓照旷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旷若发矇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旷旷(光明;明亮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 心境开阔，开朗 <span class="encs">[free from worries and petty ideas]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则有心旷神怡。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旷度(宽大的气度);旷澹(心胸豁达，不重名利);旷怀(开阔的心胸);旷逸(心胸开阔，性情超脱);旷士(胸襟开阔之士);旷如(开阔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 空旷;开阔 <span class="encs">[vast;spacious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旷兮其若谷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>十五章</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居于旷林。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">率彼旷野。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;何草不黄》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">独观于昭旷之道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;邹阳传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">土地平旷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其下平旷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">星光照旷野。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旷平(宽广平坦);旷廊(空旷;空阔);旷僻(空阔而偏僻);旷宇(广阔的原野;宽广的胸怀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 久 <span class="encs">[long-time]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自夏涉玄冬，弥旷大余旬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘桢</span><span class="smcs">《赠五官中郎将》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旷劫(佛教指过去时间极长);旷代(隔世，谓历时长久)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 遥远 <span class="encs">[distant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旷如望羊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语&middot;辨乐》</span>。注:&ldquo;用志广远。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旷迥(遥远之处);旷望(极目眺望，远望);旷绝(僻远)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 无妻的，有时也指无夫的 <span class="encs">[single;unmarried]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:旷女(无夫的成年女子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 罕见;超绝 <span class="encs">[rare;outstanding]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:旷恩(罕见的恩德)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>

<p>◎ <strong>旷</strong></p>
<p>曠 <span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 空，使空闲 <span class="encs">[leave vacant;idle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祥车旷左。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">无旷其众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旷虚(虚空;空缺);旷绝(空缺);旷额(空额)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 耽误;荒废 <span class="encs">[neglect;waste]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃德旷宗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今得强</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>之兵，以杜 <span class="diczx2">燕</span>将，旷日持久数岁，令士大夫余子之力尽于沟垒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策四》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:旷班;旷任;旷惰(怠惰，荒废职守);旷官(官吏荒废职守，才能不足称其职;悬缺待补的官职);旷土(荒芜的土地);旷学(荒废学业)</span></p>
