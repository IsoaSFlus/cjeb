                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">慝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>慝</strong> <span class="dicpy">tè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从匿，从心。匿( <span class="dicpy">nì</span>)，隐藏。把心隐藏起来，存有邪念。本义:邪恶，恶念)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wickendness]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">崇谗慝也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“慝，言隐匿其情以饰非。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">察军慝。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·环人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“慝，阴奸也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">展氏</span>有隐慝焉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“阴恶，非法所得。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日入慝作。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“奸恶也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匡邦国而观其慝。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·匡人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“奸伪之恶也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天子端然少旁求，股肱耳目皆奸慝。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘叉</span><span class="smcs">《雪车》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:慝人(邪恶的人);慝作(奸恶之人乘机而起);慝伪(奸伪);慝礼(不正之礼)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 灾害 <span class="encs">[calamity]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以伏蛊慝。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 恶人;坏人 <span class="encs">[villain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如此，则国平而民无慝矣。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 阴气 <span class="encs">[overcast]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">唯正月之朔，慝未作。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“慝，阴气也。”</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“人情爱阳而恶阴，故谓阴为慝。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 瘴气蛊毒之类 <span class="encs">[miasma]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">道地慝。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·土训》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“地慝，若障蛊然也。”</span></span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“地所生恶物害人者。若虺蝮之属。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 禁讳的语言 <span class="encs">[tabooed words]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌道方慝，以诏辟忌，以知地俗。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“方慝，四方言语所恶也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 差错 <span class="encs">[mistake]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有过慝之度。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>慝</strong> <span class="dicpy">tè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 变更，差错。通<span class="yhcs">“忒”</span> <span class="encs">[change]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">之死矢靡慝。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鄘风·伯舟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岂曰不极?伊胡为慝!——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·瞻卬》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有过慝之度。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人用侧颇辟，民用僭慝。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王嘉传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>云:<span class="yhcs">“慝，恶也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“匿”</span>。隐藏 <span class="encs">[hide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">隐慝良道，而不相教诲。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·尚贤下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔而月见东方，谓之仄慝。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志下之下》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/615D.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】他德切【集韻】惕得切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音忒。【廣韻】慝，惡也。【書·畢命】旌別淑慝。</p><p>又穢也。【禮·樂記】世亂，則禮慝而樂淫。</p><p>又邪也。【詩·鄘風】之死矢靡慝。</p><p>又隱惡也。【書·周官篇】司<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡨥</span>詰姦慝。</p><p>又地慝，若瘴蠱也。【周禮·地官】土訓掌道地圖，以詔地事，道地慝以辨地物。【晉語】宵靜女德，以伏蠱慝。</p><p>又方慝也。【周禮·地官】誦訓掌道方慝，以詔辟忌。【註】方慝，四方言語所惡也。</p><p>又仄慝也。【前漢·五行志】朔而月見東方，謂之仄慝。【孟康曰】仄慝者，月行遲在日後，當沒而更見也。</p><p>又【集韻】昵力切，音匿。隱情飾非曰慝。　从匿从心，蓋惡之慝於心者。</p><p>
