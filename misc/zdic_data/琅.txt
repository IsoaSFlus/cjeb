						<p>● <strong>琅</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～～〕①象声词，金石相击声；②象声词，响亮的读书声，如&ldquo;书声～～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>〔～玕〕像珠子的美石。</li>
	<li>〔～玡〕山名，在中国山东省。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a variety of white carnelian; pure</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Lang  (Eig, Fam)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">琅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>琅</strong></p>
<p>瑯 <span class="dicpy">l&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从玉，良声。本义:似玉的美石或青色的珊瑚)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a stone resembling pearl]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">琅，琅玕似珠者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥贡惟球琳琅玕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。传:&ldquo;石似玉。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:琳琅(美玉，比喻优美珍贵的东西);琅花(琅华。琅玕树所开之花，常以美称白花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 门环<span class="encs">[door ring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木门仓琅根，谓宫门铜锾，言将尊贵也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>琅</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;象&gt;</p>
<p>形容清朗、响亮的声音。如:琅玕(用铁链锁人。又指象声词);琅然(声音清朗的样子);琅诵(朗诵)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>琅</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>洁白、华美如玉 <span class="encs">[resembling pearl; white carnetian]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已过重阳半月天，琅华千点照寒烟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《奉和鲁望白菊诗》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">琅</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上" target="_blank">午集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上&amp;bs=玉" target="_blank">玉</a>】
                              琅·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7405.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24A1C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤨜</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/249B4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤦴</span>【唐韻】魯當切【集韻】【韻會】盧當切【正韻】魯堂切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音郎。【說文】琅玕，似珠者。【玉篇】琅玕，石似玉。【廣韻】琅玕，玉名。【書&middot;禹貢】厥貢惟球琳琅玕。【傳】琅玕，石而似珠。【山海經】崐崘山有琅玕樹。【本草】寇宗奭曰：西域記云天竺出琅玕。蘇恭云：是琉璃之類，琉璃乃火成者，非琅玕也。李時珍曰：山海經云開明山北有珠樹。淮南子云：曾城九重有珠樹，在其西，珠樹卽琅玕。在山爲琅玕，在水爲珊瑚。</p>
<p>又琅琊，郡名。今沂州俗作瑯。【山海經】琅琊臺在渤海閒，琅琊之東。【註】今琅琊在海邊，有山嶕嶢特起，狀如高臺。【括地志】琅琊山在密州諸城縣東南，始皇立層臺於山上，謂之琅琊臺。</p>
<p>又姓。【五音集韻】齊有大夫琅過。</p>
<p>又倉琅，宮門縮首銅鐶。【前漢&middot;趙后傳】木門倉琅根。</p>
<p>又琅當，長鏁也。【前漢&middot;王莽傳】以鐵鎖琅當其頸。或作琅璫。</p>
<p>又琳琅，玉聲。【楚辭&middot;九歌】撫長劒兮玉珥，璆鏘鳴兮琳琅。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;司馬振鐸註】司馬法曰：鼓聲不過閶，鼙聲不過闒，鐸聲不過琅。【疏】鼓鼙與鐸，聲之有異也。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】力宕切，音浪。【管子&middot;宙合篇】以琅蕩凌轢人。</p>
