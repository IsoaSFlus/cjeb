						<p>● <strong>刈</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>割（草或谷类）：～除。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>cut off, reap, mow; sickle</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> mähen, abschneiden  [ hist. <a href="./艾">艾</a>  ]  (V, Agrar)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> couper (de l'herbe)​,moissonner,faucher</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刈</strong></p>
<p>苅、 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本作乂。形声。从刀，乂( <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本作&ldquo;义&rdquo;。本义:割草)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mow;cut down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刈，断也。又，杀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是刈是濩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;葛覃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腰镰刈葵藿，倚杖牧鸡豚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《代东武呤》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:刈获(收割;收获);刈熟(指收割庄稼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 杀 <span class="encs">[kill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而又刈亡之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及后世贪者之用兵也，以刈百姓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>刈</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄧˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>y&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5882.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">割取。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>刈</b>草」、「<b>刈</b>麥」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辭&middot;屈原&middot;離騷》：「冀枝葉之峻茂兮，願俟時乎吾將<b>刈</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">砍殺。</span><span class="gc_yy">《呂氏春秋&middot;慎大覽&middot;順說》：「<b>刈</b>人之頸，刳人之腹。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《新唐書&middot;卷八五&middot;竇建德傳》：「我無罪，王何信讒，自<b>刈</b>左右手乎？」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">消滅。</span><span class="gc_yy">《國語&middot;吳語》：「今天王既封植越國，以明聞於天下，而又<b>刈</b>亡之，是天王之無成勞也。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">割草的農具。即鎌刀。</span><span class="gc_yy">《國語&middot;齊語》：「時雨既至，挾其槍、<b>刈</b>、耨、鎛，以旦暮從事於田野。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國吳&middot;韋昭&middot;注：「<b>刈</b>，鎌也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如五代時宋有刈懷。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=刀" target="_blank">刀</a>】
                              刈·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=4" target="_blank">4</a>　·部外筆画：2</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5208.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206C4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛄</span>【唐韻】魚肺切【集韻】魚刈切【韻會】疑刈切【正韻】倪制切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音乂。【說文】芟草也。【徐曰】本作乂，後人又加刀作刈。【屈原&middot;離騷】願竢時乎吾將刈。【註】刈，穫也。草曰刈，穀曰穫。</p>
<p>又通艾。【前漢&middot;匈奴傳】艾，朝鮮之旃。【註】師古曰：艾讀曰刈。刈，絕也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】取也，殺也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】絕也。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4E42_43.svg" /><span class="swbf">(乂)</span><span class="ywyy">芟艸也。</span><span class="swdz">艸部曰：芟，㐅艸也。二篆爲轉注。周南曰：是刈是濩。周頌曰：奄觀銍艾。艾者，㐅之叚借字。銍者，所以㐅也。禾部曰：穫，㐅榖也。是則芟艸穫榖總謂之㐅。鄭箋詩云。芟末曰艾。刀部有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206CE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛎</span>。金部有鎌，有鍥，所以芟艸也。銍則穫禾短鎌也。引申之，㐅訓治也。見諸經傳。許辟部云。䢃，治也。引唐書有能俾䢃。則䢃爲正字。</span><span class="ywyy">从丿乀相交。</span><span class="swdz">象左右去之會意也。魚廢切。十五部。</span></p>
