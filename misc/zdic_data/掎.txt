                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>掎</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>拖住，牵引：～止（从后截获）。～角（分兵牵制或夹击敌人）。</li>    <li>发射：“机不虚～”。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">倚</span>”，支撑。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">掎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>掎</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="encs">[从旁或从后]</span>用力拉住，拖住 <span class="encs">[drag]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掎，偏引也。从手，奇声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伐木掎矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小弁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸戎掎之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十四年》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“言戾其足也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掎止晏莱焉。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“从后曰掎。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">躬</span>掎禄曰。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·息夫躬传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“从后引之也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昔</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>失其鹿， <span class="diczx2">刘季</span>遂而掎之。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·叙传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:掎汩(犹牵动);掎拔(提引而出;挺拔);掎鹿(拉着鹿);掎裳连袂(牵裙连袖)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 发射 <span class="encs">[shoot]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">机不虚掎。——</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《西都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 牵制，拖住使不能自由行动 <span class="encs">[pin down]</span>。如:掎角(分兵牵制或夹击敌人);掎掣(牵制);掎挈(牵制)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指摘 <span class="encs">[censure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>西行不到 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>，掎摭星宿遗羲娥。—— <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《古鼓歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:掎挈(掎契。指摘);掎夺(指摘摈弃)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/掎摭">掎摭</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              掎·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/638E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】居綺切【集韻】【韻會】舉綺切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>羈上聲。【說文】偏引也。【廣韻】牽一腳也。【周禮·秋官】翨氏掌攻猛鳥，各以其物爲媒而掎之。【註】鳥來下則掎其腳。【魯語】掎止晏萊焉。【註】從後曰掎。【前漢·班固敘傳】秦失其鹿，劉季逐而掎之。【註】掎，偏持其足也。</p><p>又發也。【班固·西都賦】機不虛掎。</p><p>又【正韻】居里切，音几。義同。</p><p>又【唐韻】【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>居宜切，音羈。亦偏引也。</p><p>又古音讀若哿。【音學五書】古音居我反，後人誤入四紙韻。【詩·小雅】伐木掎矣，析薪扡矣。</p><p>又【正字通】掎，居何切，讀若柯。扡，䚽何切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>古音也。◎按詩釋文掎，寄彼反。</p><p>又疏云：掎者，倚也，以物掎其巓峰也。音義亦从紙韻。</p><p>又【集韻】隱綺切，音倚。掎匜，不正也。與<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22DD4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢷔</span>同。</p>
