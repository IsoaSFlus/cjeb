                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>腐</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" putrid;corrupt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">腐，烂也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">腐，败也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人死，五藏腐朽。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡·订鬼篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流水不腐。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·尽数》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肉腐出虫，鱼枯生蠹。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:腐散(腐烂消散);腐坏(腐烂损坏);腐秽(腐烂肮脏);腐浊(腐臭浑浊)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“拊”</span>。拍;击 <span class="encs">[clap]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此臣之日夜切齿腐心也，乃今得闻教!——<span data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指思想或文章陈旧、古板 <span class="encs">[pedant;stale]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为天下安用腐儒?——<span class="smcs">《史记·黥布列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:腐板(迂腐古板);腐俗(陈腐庸俗);腐音(陈腐的音调);腐局(陈腐的观念)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 气味恶臭 <span class="encs">[of rotten smell]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流水不腐。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·尽数》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>腐</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 豆腐的省称 <span class="encs">[short for beancurd]</span>。如:腐衣(豆腐衣);腐竹</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 宫刑 <span class="encs">[castration]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">死罪欲腐者许之。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·景帝纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“宫刑也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:腐史(指<span class="smcs">《史记》</span>。因作者司马迁是在受了腐刑的情况下完成这本书的);腐夫(宦官，太监。即受宫刑的人);腐罪(应受腐刑之罪);腐身(遭受腐刑的身躯);腐庸(指太监。太监腐身而为庸仆。故称);腐人(因受刑或饥寒而死于监狱的人)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
