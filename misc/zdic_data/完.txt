                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>完</strong> <span class="dicpy">wán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，元声。<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>与房屋有关。本义:完备，完整)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[whole;complete]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">完，全也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巢非不完也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">完全富足而趋</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·议兵》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">完璧归</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">果得一死男，手足完具。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·华佗传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不如伐蜀之完也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">功少而最完，势疏而最忠。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《治安策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不能独完。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无一完者。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:完计(周密完善的计策);完完(完整无缺的样子);完充(完备充裕);完锐(完备精良);完块(完整的田土);完篇(完整的篇章);完丽(完整华美);完垒(完整的营垒);完名(完美的名节);完书(完美的书;完整的书);完德(完美的品德)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 坚固 <span class="encs">[firm]</span>。如:完利(坚固适用);完牢(坚固)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 充足;充实 <span class="encs">[full]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今天水完富，士马最强。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>完</strong> <span class="dicpy">wán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 完成，结束 <span class="encs">[finish;complete;run out;use up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">那夜</span><span class="diczx2">安公子</span>早已完卷。——<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:完卷(完成答卷);完房(完婚);完娶(结为夫妻，完婚);完就(成就，成功);完饭(婚俗。婚后三日，女家送食物到男家，男家则以茶饭招待);完了那纂(做完那些事情)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 偿付或缴纳 <span class="encs">[pay]</span>。如:完钱粮(缴纳粮食和税款);完租(交纳田赋);完捐(交纳捐税);完逋(交纳拖欠的赋税);完纳(交纳);完就(完成;成功);完案(了结案件)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 重新整修 <span class="encs">[renovate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父母使</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>完廩。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·万章上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缮完葺墙。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:完缮(修理;修缮);完补(补救;修复);完葺(修缮，修葺);完理(修整)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 保全，保护，使不受损失 <span class="encs">[preserve]</span>。如:完身(保全自身);完免(保全;幸免);完活(保全养育);完惜(保全);完辑(保全;安定)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 保守 <span class="encs">[keep]</span>。如:完护(保护);完节(保持贞节;保全节操)</p><p><span
