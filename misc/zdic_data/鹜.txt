<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鶩</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˋ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鹜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鹜</strong></p>
<p>騖 <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鸟，敄( <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:野鸭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wild duck]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鹜，野凫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太平御览》</span>引<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">庶人之挚匹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼下》</span>。疏引</span><span class="diczx2">李巡</span>:「凫，家鸭名;鹜，野鸭名。」</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">邹穆公</span>有令，食凫鴈者必以粃。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《春秋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">落霞与孤鹜齐飞，秋水共长天一色。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 家鸭 <span class="encs">[duck]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寨中人又鹜伏焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鹜列(如鸭排列成行);鹜舲(鹜形的小舟);鹜溏(大便水粪青黑如鸭粪的一种病症);鹜没(如鸭潜没水中)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鹜</strong></p>
<p>騖 <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 奔驰。通「鹜」 <span class="encs">[gallop]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鹜置(以马疾驰传递);鹜行(疾驰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为追求 <span class="encs">[seek for]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:好高鹜远;鹜新(求新)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 游水 <span class="encs">[swim]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">领水兵十余人，鹜水直抵南岸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吴趼人</span><span class="smcs">《痛史》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">鶩</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“鹜”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/鶩">鶩</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中" target="_blank">亥集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中&amp;bs=鳥" target="_blank">鳥</a>】
                              鶩·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9DA9.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>莫卜切，音木。【說文】鶩，舒鳧也。郭璞曰：鴨也。方氏曰：以爲人所畜，不善飛，舒而不疾，故曰舒鳧。【禮&middot;曲禮&middot;疏】野鴨曰鳧，家鴨曰鶩。</p>
<p>又毛氏云：可畜而不能高飛曰鴨，野生高飛曰鶩。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;襄二十八年】公膳日雙雞，饔人竊更之以鶩。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大宗伯】庶人執鶩。【鄭註】鶩取其不飛遷。</p>
<p>又【後漢&middot;馬援傳】刻鵠不成尙類鶩。</p>
<p>又【楚辭&middot;九歌】鼂騁鶩兮江臯。【註】鶩，亂馳也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>亡遇切，音務。義同。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9DA9.svg">                          <p>舒鳧也。从鳥敄聲。莫卜切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9DA9_8012.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鶩)</span><span class="ywyy">舒鳧也。</span><span class="swdz">几部曰：舒鳧，鶩也。與釋鳥同。舍人、李巡云：鳧，野鴨名。鶩，家鴨名。許於鳧下當云鳧，水鳥也。舒鳧，鶩也。文乃備。左傳疏云：謂之舒鳧者，家養馴不畏人，故飛行遟，別野名耳。某氏注云在野舒飛遠者爲鳧，非是。詞章家鳧亦呼鶩，此如今野人雁亦呼雁䳘也。春秋繁露：張湯問祠宗廟。或以鶩當鳧，可用否。仲舒曰：鶩非鳧。鳧非鶩也。以鶩當鳧名實不相應，以承大廟不可。此舒鳧與鳧之判。廣雅云：鳧鶩<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A057.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪁗</span>也。此統言而未析言之也。</span><span class="ywyy">从鳥敄聲。</span><span class="swdz">莫⺊切。三部。</span></p>                          </div>
