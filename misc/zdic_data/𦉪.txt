                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7F51.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20543.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠕃</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2627D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉽</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/262DF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦋟</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2626E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉮</span>【廣韻】文兩切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】文紡切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音網。【說文】庖犧所結繩以漁。从冂，下象網交文。【註】今經典變隷作罒。【玉篇】羅罟總名。亦作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26052.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦁒</span>、罔、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20548.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠕈</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2626F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉯</span>。【廣韻】與網同。【篇海】网字有三譌：本作网而譌作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26273.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉳</span>，亦作冈而又譌作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2053F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔿</span>，又亦作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26270.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉰</span>而譌作罒。罒乃橫目，惟睪眔睘等字從之，其他但屬羅網義者，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>係<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26270.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉰</span>字下橫畫，不連兩旁。</p><p>（網）【廣韻】文兩切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】文紡切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音罔。【說文】本作网。或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2627D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦉽</span>，隷省作㒺。今文从糸作綱。【易·繫辭】作結繩而爲㒺罟，以佃以漁。【朱子·本義】㒺，與綱同。【書·盤庚】若綱在綱。【詩·邶風】魚綱之設。</p><p>又【楚辭·招魂】綱戸朱綴。【註】綱戸，綺文縷也。</p>
