                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>艾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ài  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ài.mp3"></a></span> ㄞˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>多年生草本植物，嫩叶可食，老叶制成绒，供针灸用：～子。～蒿。～绒。</li>    <li>年老的，也指老年人：耆～。</li>    <li>止，绝：方兴未～。</li>    <li>美好：少（<span class="dicpy">shào </span>）～（年轻美好的女子）。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>艾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 治理。[自怨自艾]本义是悔恨自己的错误，自己改正。现仅指悔恨。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">艾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>艾</strong> <span class="dicpy">ài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:草名)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 即艾蒿 <span class="encs">[Argy wormwood;mugwort wormwood]</span>。一种菊科的多年生草本植物( Artemisia argyi )，叶制成艾绒，供针灸用</p><p><span class="diczx1">艾，冰台也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">削冰令圆举以向日，干艾于后，承其景则得火，故曰冰台。——<span class="smcs">《博物志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼采艾兮，一日不见，如三岁兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·采葛》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">求三年之艾也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:艾人(用艾草结成的草人。旧俗在端午节以艾人悬挂于门上，可以避邪除毒)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 老年，对老年人的敬称 <span class="encs">[the old]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有幼、壮、艾之期。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《送鸿举师游江南引》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五十曰艾，服官政。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“艾，老也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">搜索稚与艾，惟存跛无目。——</span><span class="diczx2">梅尧臣</span><span class="smcs">《田家语》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:艾老(五十岁以上的老人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 漂亮的人 <span class="encs">[handsome man;pretty girl;beanlty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国君好艾(此指男色)，大夫殆。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">积得些金帛，娶了些娇艾。——<span class="smcs">《桃花扇》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:艾色(美丽;漂亮。多指女色)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 小人 <span class="encs">[person of low position]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何昔日之芳草兮，今直为此萧艾也。——</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>艾</strong> <span class="dicpy">ài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button
