						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诈</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">詐</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhà  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhà.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄚˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>欺骗，用手段诓骗：欺～。～骗。～取。～财。～哄。尔虞我～。～语（骗人的话）。</li>    <li>假装：～死。～降。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">乍</span>”，突然。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，乍声。从言，说明与言论有关。本义:欺骗)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[blackmail;cheat;swindle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诈，欺也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诈，伪也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匿行曰诈。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·脩身》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有掎挈伺诈。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">繁战之君，不足于诈。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·义赏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无功伐而求荣富，诈也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·务本》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">相如</span>度 <span class="diczx2">秦王</span>特以诈佯为予 <span class="diczx2">赵</span>城，实不可得。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禽兽之变诈。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:诈端(讹诈的理由);诈掯(敲诈勒索);诈哄(欺骗);诈伪(欺骗);诈力(诈术与暴力);诈人(专务诈欺作伪的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 假装;冒充 <span class="encs">[feign;pretend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诈自称公子。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃诈称公子。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诈云欲降。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:诈酒三分醉(喝酒不多却装做醉酒);诈使命(假使节;伪装的使节);诈允(假装允诺);诈狂(假装颠狂);诈死(假装死去);诈痴不颠(假装痴呆)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用语言试探，诱使对方露真情 <span class="encs">[sound out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谁不知道</span><span class="diczx2">李十太爷</span>是能事的!把我一诈，就吓毛了!——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:他是拿话诈我</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>诈</strong></p><p>詐 <span class="dicpy">zhà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhà.mp3"></a></span> class="cino">(2)</span> 矜夸;神气 <span class="encs">[cocky]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">只待要弄柳拈花，马儿上扭捏着身子儿诈。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">郑廷玉</span><span
