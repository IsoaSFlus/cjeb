                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>潜</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qián  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qián.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>隐在水面下活动：～水。～泳。～坝。～行（ａ．在水面以下行动；ｂ．在外边秘密行动）。</li><li>隐藏的，秘密地：～藏。～伏。～遁。～逃。～力。～心。～师。～在。～台词。～移默化。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">潜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>潜</strong></p><p>潛 <span class="dicpy">qián</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qián.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:没入水中，而且在水下活动)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[go under water;move under water]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">潜，涉水也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>按:没水以涉曰潜。</p><p><span class="diczx1">潜龙。——<span class="smcs">《易·乾龙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">潜虽伏矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·正月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">游鱼潜绿水。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《情诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沈潜相克。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">潜龙腾渊。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:潜水</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 隐藏 <span class="encs">[hidden;latent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">潜服贼器不入宫。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·阍人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">潜醢以食</span><span class="diczx2">夏</span>后。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十九年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日星隐耀，山岳潜形。——</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胆寒潜踪。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span>又</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">潜避两炮台中。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:潜晦(潜藏养晦。又指隐藏才能);潜德(韬养隐藏的内涵、美德);潜逸(隐逸);潜户(隐藏的洞穴);潜包，潜苞(包藏);潜光(隐藏光彩);潜名(隐姓埋名)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 深隐;隐居 <span class="encs">[hermit]</span>。如:潜居草野(隐居在民间);潜而不用(小人当道，圣人只宜潜藏，不可有为);潜夫(隐者);潜升(谓或隐或显);潜户(深隐的门户)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 专心 <span class="encs">[make a concentrated effort]</span>。如:潜志(专心致志);潜研(专心钻研);潜修(专心修养);潜神(犹专心)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 潜入水中 <span class="encs">[dive]</span>。如:一只大水鼠从此岸潜下</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 埋葬 <span class="encs">[bury]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西望大泽，后稷所潜也。——<span class="smcs">《山海经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 暗流 <span class="encs">[underflow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东望</span><span class="diczx2">泑</span>泽， <span class="diczx2">河水</span>所潜也。——<span class="smcs">《山海经》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:潜演(水在地下流动);潜源(潜伏着的水源)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 测量;探测 <span class="encs">[measure;survey]</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>潜</strong> <span class="dicpy">qián</span> <span class="ptr"><a                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6F5C.svg">						  <p>【字彙】俗潛字。</p><p>（潛）【唐韻】昨鹽切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】慈鹽切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24135.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤄵</span>。【說文】涉水也。【揚子·方言】潛涵，沉也。</p><p>又游也。【註】潛行水中，亦曰游。</p><p>又藏也。【易·乾卦】陽氣潛藏。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋言】潛，深也。【詩·小雅】潛雖伏矣。</p><p>又漢水之別名。【爾雅·釋水】水自江出爲沱，漢爲潛。【書·禹貢】沱潛旣道。</p><p>又丘名。【爾雅·釋丘】晉有潛丘。</p><p>又地名。【春秋·隱二年】公會戎于潛。</p><p>又邑名。【史記·楚世家】吳取楚之六潛。</p><p>又潛江，縣名。【廣輿記】屬安陸府。</p><p>又牛名。【酉陽雜俎】句漏縣大江中有潛牛。</p><p>又姓。宋安撫使潛說友。</p><p>又【小爾雅】魚之所息謂之潛。【詩·周頌】潛有多魚。【傳】潛，糝也。</p><p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>慈艷切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25333.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥌳</span>。義同。一曰伏流。</p>

                          <span class="jieshi">涉水也。</span>上文云潛行水中。對下文浮行水上言之。邶風傳云。由厀以上爲涉。然則言潛者、自其厀以下<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CDA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣳚</span>於水言之。所謂泳也。左傳哀十七年。越子以三軍潛涉。又按潛汓等字、後人不甚分明。若水經注江水篇云。有潛客泳而視之。見水下有兩石牛。此則謂潛全<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CDA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣳚</span>水中矣。<span class="jieshi">一曰藏也。</span>此今日通行義。釋言曰。潛、深也。方言曰。潛、涵、沈也。<span class="jieshi">从水。朁聲。</span>昨鹽切。七部。<span class="jieshi">一曰漢爲潛。</span>釋水文。劉逵注蜀都賦云。禹貢梁州、沲潛旣道。有水從漢中沔陽縣南流。至梓潼漢壽縣。入大穴中。通岡山下。西南潛出。今名伏水。舊說云禹貢潛水也。郭樸爾雅音義同此說。                          </div>
