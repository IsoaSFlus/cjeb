                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>榆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>落叶乔木，实扁圆，木材坚实，可制器具或供建筑用：～荚。～钱儿（即&ldquo;榆荚&rdquo;，像小铜钱）。～面（以榆皮磨制成粉，用为黏剂）。～塞（古称边塞植榆，故称边塞为&ldquo;榆塞&rdquo;）。桑～暮景。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>榆</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 榆树<span class="encs">[elm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">榆，白枌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，榆皮色赤，其白者为枌也。荚可食，亦可为酱。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堇萱枌榆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我决起而飞抢榆枋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 落叶乔木，叶卵形，花有短梗，木材可供建筑或制器械、家具等。如:榆枋(借喻见识浅陋;也指眼界狭小);榆荚(古代钱币名称，汉代铸造)</p>
