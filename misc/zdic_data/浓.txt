                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>浓</strong></p><p>濃 <span class="dicpy">nóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，农声。本义:露多)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dewy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浓，露多也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">零露浓浓。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·蓼萧》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:浓浓(露多的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 稠密;厚;多 <span class="encs">[dense;thick;many]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浓雾半作。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">那和尚生得浓眉大眼。——<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:浓抹(厚施脂粉);浓福(厚福);浓烟;浓雾;浓繁(郁郁葱葱);浓圈密点(在文字旁加上许多圆圈或点。常以表示值得注意或精采之处)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 特指液体或气体中所含某种成分多。与<span class="yhcs">“淡”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“薄”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[concentraed;thick;strong]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宵寒药气浓。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《昌谷读书示巴童》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:浓咖啡;浓洌(浓郁清洌);浓腴(指味厚和肥美的食物);浓醇(醇厚浓烈);浓醪(浓烈的浊酒);浓酾(指兴味浓厚);浓酽(汁液稠，味道厚)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 艳丽 <span class="encs">[rich and gaudy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">态浓意远淑且真，肌理细腻骨肉匀。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《丽人行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:浓姿(艳丽的姿态);浓绮(浓艳绮丽);浓缛(浓艳繁丽);浓桃艳李(桃花浓丽，李花鲜艳。比喻人容貌俊美，神采焕发)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 颜色重 <span class="encs">[dark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浓朱衍凡唇。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《娇女诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:浓翠(深绿);浓黛(深黛);浓睡(酣睡，沉睡)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 程度深 <span class="encs">[deep]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浓睡不消残酒。——</span><span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《如梦令》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">更谁家横笛，吹动浓愁。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《满庭芳》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:露浓花瘦;游兴很浓;得意浓时趁早休</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr"
