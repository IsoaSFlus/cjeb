                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">世</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>世</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄕˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>一个时代，有时特指三十年：～代（ ➊ 很多年代； ➋ 好几辈子）。～纪（指一百年）。流芳百～。</li>
	<li>一辈一辈相传的：～袭。～家（ ➊ 封建社会中门第高，世代做官的人家； ➋ 《史记》中诸侯的传记）。</li>
	<li>人间，以与天上相区别：～上。～俗（ ➊ 流俗； ➋ 非宗教的）。～故（➊处事待人圆滑，&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">故</span>&rdquo;读轻声； ➋ 处世经验）。～态炎凉。</li>
	<li>自然界和人类社会一切事物的总和；全地球、人间或宇宙；宇宙的一部分：～界。举～瞩目。公之于～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">世</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>世</strong></p>
<p>丗、卋 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。金文字形。古人以三十年为一世。&ldquo;止&rdquo;上加三个圆点，表三十年;止，到此为止。本义:三十年)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[thirty years]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世，三十年为一世。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如有王者，必世而后仁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子路》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 父子相继为世。一代(古礼规定，男子三十岁结婚生孩子，产生新一代) <span class="encs">[generation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父子相代为一世。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《字汇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世室世世不毁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;文公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世相朝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大行人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔我先王世后稷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有</span><span class="diczx2">蒋氏</span>者，专其利三世矣。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孙权</span>据有 <span class="diczx2">江东</span>，已历三世，国险而民附。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:世功(累代的功绩);世世(代代);世治(世世代代地统治);世济(世代继承);世卿(指世代承袭的卿大夫);世职(父子相袭的官职);世爵(世代继承爵位);世谱(世系家谱);世将(世代为将);祖传世医</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 一生;一辈子 <span class="encs">[lifetime;life]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">负刍必以</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>，殁世事 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span>。注:&ldquo;身也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡君之所毕世而经营者，为天下也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:今生今世;尘世;来世;出世;入世;永世不忘</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 人世，世间;世界 <span class="encs">[world]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世有</span><span class="diczx2">伯乐</span>，然后有千里马。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《马说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽才高于世，而无骄尚之情。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此世所谓上下相孚也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一世之雄;举世闻名;世故;世尊(佛祖。佛教徒对释迦牟尼的尊称);世涂(光天化日之下;亦常比喻处世的经历);世界路上(人世交际场中);世外桃源</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 时代;朝代 <span class="encs">[times;era;epoch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问今是何世，乃不知有</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>，无论 <span class="diczx2">魏</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而圣君治国累世而不见者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:近世;治世;浊世;盛世;乱世;先世;世屯(时世艰难);世务(当世有关国计民生的大事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 岁;年 <span class="encs">[age]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">志国三世。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世之有饥穰，天之行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:世远事乖(年代久远，事情不顺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 地质年代的划分，短于一个纪而长于一个代的地质年代的分期 <span class="encs">[epoch]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:古新世</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 世俗 <span class="encs">[mundane]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">龙德而隐者也，不易乎世。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;乾》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 后嗣;后人 <span class="encs">[descendant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">卫</span><span class="diczx2">瑞木叔</span>者，子贡之世也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;杨朱》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:后世;官人以世(官人不以贤才而以父兄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 家世 <span class="encs">[family property]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陵</span>不引决，忝世灭姓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 世族 <span class="encs">[noble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以族论罪，以世举贵，虽欲无乱，得乎哉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;君子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:世家子弟(泛指世代做官人家的子弟);世家军籍(世代都是军人);世胄(贵族后裔;泛指门第高、世代做官的人家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 姓</p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>世</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄕˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>sh&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4924.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">三十年稱為「一世」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;十部》：「<b>世</b>，三十年為一<b>世</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;子路》：「如有王者，必<b>世</b>而後仁。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">父子相繼稱為「一世」，即一代。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「五世其昌」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《字彙&middot;一部》：「<b>世</b>，父子相代為一<b>世</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;大雅&middot;文王》：「文王孫子，本支百<b>世</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">朝代。</span><span class="gc_yy">晉&middot;陶淵明〈桃花源記〉：「問今是何<b>世</b>，乃不知有漢，無論魏、晉。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">時代。</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;繫辭下》：「易之興也，其當殷之末<b>世</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;王充《論衡&middot;逢遇》：「伯夷，帝者之佐也，出於王者之<b>世</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">年、歲。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;曲禮下》：「去國三<b>世</b>，爵祿有列於朝，出入有詔於國。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞。計算一生、一輩子的單位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「七<b>世</b>夫妻」、「三生三<b>世</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">世界、世間、世人。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「傳<b>世</b>」、「避<b>世</b>」、「世外桃源」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《莊子&middot;天地》：「千歲厭<b>世</b>，去而上僊；乘彼白雲，至於帝鄉。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辭&middot;屈原&middot;漁父》：「舉<b>世</b>皆濁我獨清，眾人皆醉我獨醒。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如戰國時秦國有世鈞。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">與先輩有交誼的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>世</b>姪」、「世誼」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史&middot;第三回》：「貴房師高要縣湯公，就是先祖的門生，我和你是親切的世弟兄。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">世間的、世俗的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「世局」、「人情世故」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">累代的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「世交」、「世仇」。</span></p></li>
