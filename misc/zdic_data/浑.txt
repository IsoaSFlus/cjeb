                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>浑</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">渾</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hún  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hún.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>水不清，污浊：～水摸鱼。～浊。</li><li>骂人糊涂，不明事理：～人。～话。～蛋。～～噩噩。</li><li>全，满：～身。～然。</li><li>天然的，淳朴的：～古。～朴。～厚。</li><li>简直：“白头搔更短，～欲不胜簪。”</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> muddy, turbid; blend, merge, mix</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> trouble,stupide</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">浑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>浑</strong></p><p>渾 <span class="dicpy">hún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，军声。本义:大水涌流声)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[surging sound]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浑，水喷涌之声也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浑，混流声也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">溟海浑濩涌其后。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张协·七命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浑浑若川。——<span class="smcs">《法言·问神》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“洪流也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">财货浑浑如泉源。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 河名 <span class="encs">[Hun River]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 桑干河上游支流，亦名浑源川，发源于山西省北端西麓</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 卢沟河在元明后的别称，因河水浑浊得名，即今永定河</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 小辽河，辽河最大的支流</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 浑江，又名佟家江，发源于吉林省龙岗山脉</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 中国古代少数民族吐谷浑的省称 <span class="encs">[Tuguhun nationality]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 浑天说的简称 <span class="encs">[Hun，the ancient theory that the Earth is inside the heaven as a yoke in an egg]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">先是儒者论天，互执浑、盖二义，论盖不合于浑，论浑不合于盖。</span><span class="diczx2">灵恩</span>立义，以浑盖为一焉。——<span class="smcs">《梁书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>浑</strong></p><p>渾 <span class="dicpy">hún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 混浊 <span class="encs">[muddy;turbid]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浑，浊也。——<span class="smcs">《切韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浑兮其若浊。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浑沦者，言万物相浑沦未相离也。——<span class="smcs">《列子·天瑞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贤不肖浑殽。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·董仲舒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:浑水(浑浊不清的水);浑黄(浑浊而发黄);浑浑(浑浊的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[torrent;vast]</span>。如:浑大(宏大;博大);浑芒(广大无边的样子);浑洪(水流盛大的样子);浑浩(水势盛大的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 糊涂 <span class="encs">[stupid]</span>。如:浑同三拍(含糊);浑虫(糊涂虫)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 质朴;朴实 <span class="encs">[simple;honest]</span>。如:浑素(朴素，不华丽);浑纯(朴质纯正);浑雅(质朴高雅);浑质(纯厚朴实);浑穆(质朴淳和)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 纯，无杂质 <span class="encs">[pure]</span>。如:浑花(浑化。骰子掷出得同一彩色);浑黄(纯黄色);浑粹(纯粹);浑铁(纯铁)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 全，整个 <span
