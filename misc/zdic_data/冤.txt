                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>冤</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄢ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>屈枉，无故受到指责或处分：～枉。～屈。～案。～狱。～愤。伸～。鸣～叫屈。</li>	<li>仇恨：～头。～家。～孽。</li>	<li>欺骗：不许～人。</li>	<li>吃亏，上当：～大头。花～钱。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">冤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冤</strong></p>
<p>寃 <span class="dicpy">yuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从兔，从冖( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)。&ldquo;冖&rdquo;表示覆盖。兔子被覆盖住了，卷曲不能伸。或从冖，表示在房屋内。本义:屈缩;不舒展)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冤，屈也。从兔，在冂下不得走，益屈折也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冤，曲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冤颈折翼，庸得往兮!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;息夫躬传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>注:&ldquo;冤，屈也&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冤曲(弯曲);冤伏(屈伏);冤延(宛延。长曲的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 上当;不合算 <span class="encs">[be taken in]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:花冤钱;白跑一趟，真冤</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈方〉∶欺骗 <span class="encs">[cheat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:你别冤人</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>冤</strong> <span class="dicpy">yuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 枉曲，冤屈 <span class="encs">[injustice;wrong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哀枯杨之冤雏兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离世》</span>。注:&ldquo;冤，烦冤也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我今日负屈衔冤哀告天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">关汉卿</span><span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:冤苦钱(囚犯送给狱卒的贿赂);冤业(含冤的鬼魂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;怨&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">yu&agrave;n</span>)。冤仇，仇敌 <span class="encs">[foe;enmity;hatred;rancour]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无冤尤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;毁学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掠拷冤滥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;杨终传》</span></span></p>
