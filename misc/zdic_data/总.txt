                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>总</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">總</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄨㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>聚合，聚在一起：～之。～数。～体。～结。汇～。</li><li>概括全部，主要的：～纲。～则。</li><li>为首的，最高的：～司令。～裁。～经理。～统。</li><li>束系，束头发：～角（<span class="dicpy">jiǎo </span>）。</li><li>经常，一直：～是这样。</li><li>一定，无论如何：～归。“万紫千红～是春”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">总</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>总</strong></p><p>總、縂 <span class="dicpy">zǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，悤( <span class="dicpy">cōng</span>)声。糸是细丝，细丝常聚合成一束，故从糸。本义:聚束;系扎)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tie into a bundle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">总，聚束也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">总，结也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">素丝五总。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·羔羊》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">总角之宴。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令徒隶衣七总布。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孝景帝》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">总布二匹。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸡初鸣，咸盥漱，栉、縦，笄、总。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以麻总发。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:总发(指童年，幼年。同总角);总角之好(指幼年相契的朋友。也称<span class="yhcs">“总角之交”</span>);总丱(古时儿童束发为两角)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 聚合;汇集 <span class="encs">[get together;collect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无总于货宝，生生自庸。——<span class="smcs">《书·盘庚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">功名之总也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·礼书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">总揽英雄。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:总萃(会合聚集);总集(聚集，集合;汇集许多人的作品而成的诗文集);总猥(聚合的样子);总聚(综合聚集);总总(聚合的样子);归总;汇总;拢总</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 结，系 <span class="encs">[tie]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饮余马于</span><span
