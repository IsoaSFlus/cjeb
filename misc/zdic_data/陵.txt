                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>陵</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>大土山：山～。丘～。～谷迁变（简作&ldquo;陵谷&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>高大的坟墓：～园。～墓。～寝。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/凌">凌</a>&rdquo;，侵犯，欺侮。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hill, mound; mausoleum</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Abwurfstelle, Aufschüttung  (S)​,Hügel; hügelige Landschaft  (S)​,Mausoleum; große Grabanlage  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> coline,tombeau</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">陵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>陵</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span>)，表示与地形地势的高低上下有关。夌( <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:大土山)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[high mound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陵，大阜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如山如阜，如冈如陵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;天保》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殽有二陵焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陵阜陆墐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸿渐于陵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;渐卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古陵蔚起。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">循陵而走。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:陵谷(山岭和深谷);陵谷变迁(高山深谷变换位置。比喻世事变化);陵丘(后高前低平的大丘);陵居(居住在高陵地区);陵隰(山陵和低湿之地);陵阿(山陵);陵衍(高而广的丘陵地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申指帝王陵墓 <span class="encs">[mausoleum]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乐游原上望昭陵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《将赴吴兴登乐游原》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃强起扶杖，执图诣寺后，有古陵蔚起。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举一滴可包陵谷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:其南陵，其北陵;陵屋(帝王墓地的建筑);陵田(陵墓旁的田地);陵表(墓碑;墓表);陵户(看守帝王陵墓的人);陵寝;陵庙</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 山，山头 <span class="encs">[mountain; top]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故用兵之法，高陵勿向。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;军争》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山无陵，江水为竭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;鼓吹曲辞一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓。如:陵尹(复姓)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>陵</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 升，登 <span class="encs">[ascend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陵重巘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:陵云(升上云霄。比喻超尘绝俗或神仙的境界);陵霄(升上云霄。比喻志气高远。通凌云)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 超越，逾越 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陵水经地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:陵忽(超越;欺侮);陵架(超越其上);陵越(超越。也作凌越);凌迈(超越)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 欺侮;欺压 <span class="encs">[bully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在上位，不陵下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为巡缴所陵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录&middot;后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见陵之耻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修成子仲骄恣陵折，吏民皆患苦之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《加急记&middot;外戚世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:陵折(欺压，折辱);陵虐(欺侮虐待。也作凌虐);陵侮(欺侮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 日渐衰微 <span class="encs">[decline]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝王之道，日以陵夷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;成帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:陵夷(渐次衰颓。同陵迟);陵弛(渐次衰颓、废弛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 凌驾 <span class="encs">[override]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">(</span><span class="diczx2">宁成</span>)好气，为人小吏，必陵其长吏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;酷吏列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凌架(凌驾);陵跨(跨越;凌驾)</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">大土山。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「丘陵」、「山陵」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;僖公三十二年》：「殽有二陵焉。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國魏&middot;曹植〈鰕䱇篇〉：「駕言登五嶽，然後小陵丘。」</span></p></li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如明代有陵茂。</span></p></li>

	<li>

                              陵·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9675.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕夌<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EEA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻪</span>䧙【唐韻】力膺切【集韻】【韻會】閭承切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音凌。【爾雅&middot;釋地】大阜曰陵。【釋名】陵，崇也，體崇高也。【書&middot;堯典】蕩蕩懷山襄陵。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋丘】後高陵丘。【又】如陵陵丘。</p>
<p>又東陵，地名。【書&middot;禹貢】過九江，至于東陵。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】冢也。【齊語】陵爲之終。【註】以爲葬也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】犯也。【廣韻】侮也，侵也。【易&middot;賁象】永貞之吉，終莫之陵也。【書&middot;畢命】以蕩陵德。【傳】陵，邈也。【禮&middot;學記】不陵節而施之謂孫。【註】陵，躐也。</p>
<p>又【荀子&middot;宥坐篇】百仞之山，任負車登焉，何則陵遲故也。【註】王肅曰：陵遲，陂阤也。【史記&middot;平準書】選舉陵遲。【前漢&middot;成帝紀】日以陵夷。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】慄也。</p>
<p>又馳也。</p>
<p>又草名。【爾雅&middot;釋草】苕，陵苕。【註】一名陵時。</p>
<p>又淬也。【荀子&middot;君道篇】兵刃不待陵而勁。</p>
<p>又【荀子&middot;致仕篇】凡節奏欲陵，而生民欲寬。【註】峻也。</p>
<p>又州名。【唐書&middot;地理志】劒南道陵州仁壽郡。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】永樂中濼州同知陵茂，高郵人。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】漢複姓六氏，吳季子之後有延陵氏，高士傳有於陵子仲，戰國策有安陵丑，呂氏春秋有鉛陵卓子，漢有高陵顯，秦高陵君之後，楚有公子，食采於鄧陵，後以爲氏。</p>
<p>又叶力中切，音隆。【胡廣&middot;侍中箴】國有學校，侯有泮宮。各有攸敎，德用不陵。</p>
<p>又叶落胡切，音盧。【柳宗元&middot;佩韋賦】雲岳岳而專强兮，果黜志而乖圖。咸觸屛以拒訓兮，肆隕越而就陵。</p>
<p>又與鯪同。【楚辭&middot;天問】陵魚何所。一作鯪。</p>
<p>又與凌通。【史記&middot;秦始皇紀】陵水經地。【註】正義曰：陵作凌，歷也。　【玉篇】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2086D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠡭</span>。【<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25846.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥡆</span>天子傳】作䧙。</p>
