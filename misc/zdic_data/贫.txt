                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贫</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>贫</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">貧</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pín  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pín.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄧㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>穷，收入少，生活困难，与“<span class="diczx3">富</span>”相对：～穷。～寒。～民。清～。</li><li>缺乏，不足：～乏。～血。～瘠。</li><li>絮烦可厌：～相（<span class="dicpy">xiàng </span>）。～气（ａ．絮烦可厌；ｂ．行动态度不大方。“<span class="diczx3">气</span>”均读轻声）。</li><li>僧道谦称：～道。～僧。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> poor, impoverished, needy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pauvre,bavard,ennuyeux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贫</strong></p><p>貧 <span class="dicpy">pín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从贝从分，分亦声。<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>是古货币，一个<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>还要分开，表示贫困。本义:缺少财物，贫困。与<span class="yhcs">“富”</span>相对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[poor;poverty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贫，财分少也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无财谓之贫。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·让王》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贫者，士之常也。——<span class="smcs">《说苑·杂言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">分贫振穷。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十四年》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“贫、穷相类。细言，穷困于贫。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贫而无谄。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">强本而节用，则天下不能贫。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·天论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人贫居。—— <span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">家贫，无从致书以观。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:贫难(贫苦，困难);贫胎(骂人的话。穷鬼);贫窭(贫苦);贫贱之交(贫困患难中结交的朋友);贫户(贫穷人家);贫交(贫贱者相交友);贫巷(贫民居住的地方);贫病(又穷又病);贫汉(贫穷的男子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为缺少，不足 <span class="encs">[be deficient in;be poor in，be scanty of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">富于万篇，而贫于一字。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘勰</span><span class="smcs">《文心雕龙·练字》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:贫阙(不足，短缺);贫薄(浅陋，短浅);贫腐(贫乏陈腐);贫微(贫乏微薄);贫寒(贫乏单薄);贫空(谓空无所有);贫辛(贫乏艰涩)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 絮叨可厌 <span class="encs">[loquacious;garrulous]</span>。如:他的嘴真贫;贫嘴恶舌(话多而尖刻);贫嘴贱舌(形容人说话轻薄);贫嘴饿舌(形容人话多令人讨厌)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 僧道、尼姑自谦之称 <span class="encs">[a humble term of self-address of a Buddhist monk or Taoist priest]</span>。如:贫僧;贫尼(尼姑对自己的谦称);贫家(谦称自己的家) </p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>贫</strong></p><p>貧 <span class="dicpy">pín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 使贫穷 <span
