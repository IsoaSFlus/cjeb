                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">锻</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>锻</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg"><span class="diczx3">鍛</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>把金属放在火里烧，然后用锤子打：～工。～件。～接。～炼。～压。～造。</li>
	<li>锤击：&ldquo;取石来～之&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> forge metal; temper, refine</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schmieden  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> forger,marteler</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>锻</strong></p>
<p>鍛 <span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，段声。本义:打铁)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[forge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">锻，小冶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，熔铸金为冶，以金入火焠而椎之为小冶。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">锻练之吏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;韦彪传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:锻菜刀;锻锄头。又指文句的加工</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百锻为字，千炼成句。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《刘枣强碑》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 锤击 <span class="encs">[hammer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其父谓其子曰:&ldquo;取石来锻之&rdquo;。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;列御寇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 罗织罪状，陷人于罪 <span class="encs">[cook up charges]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:锻炼(编造罪名，加害于人)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>锻</strong></p>
<p>鍛 <span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>锻铁用的砧石 <span class="encs">[anvil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涉</span><span class="diczx2">渭</span>为乱，取厉取锻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;公刘》</span></p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“锻”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/鍛">鍛</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=金" target="_blank">金</a>】
                              鍛·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/935B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】丁貫切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】都玩切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音碫。【說文】小冶。从金段聲。【徐曰】椎之而已，不消，故曰小冶。【倉頡篇】椎也。【廣韻】打鐵。【增韻】冶金曰鍛。【書&middot;費誓】鍛乃戈矛。【傳】鍛鍊戈矛也。【晉書&middot;嵆康傳】康好鍛，每於柳樹下鍛，鍾會造之，康鍛如故。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;路溫舒傳】上奏畏卻，則鍛鍊而周內之。【註】精熟周悉，致之法中也。【後漢&middot;韋彪傳】鍛鍊之吏，持心近薄。【註】鍛鍊，猶言成熟也。言深文之吏，入人之罪，猶工冶陶鑄鍛鍊，使之成熟也。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;喪服】鍛而勿灰。【疏】冠用六升布，加以水濯，勿用灰而已。</p>
<p>又【士喪禮&middot;功布註】功布，鍛濯灰治之布也。</p>
<p>又【馬融&middot;長笛賦】靁叩鍛之岌峇兮。【註】叩鍛，岌峇，爲聲。倉頡篇曰：鍛，椎也。</p>
<p>又與腶通。【左傳&middot;哀十一年&middot;腶脯釋文】腶，丁亂反。亦作鍛。【穀梁傳&middot;莊二十四年】婦人之贄，棗栗鍛脩。【註】鍛，丁亂反。脯也。鍛而加薑桂曰脩。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】取厲取鍛。【傳】鍛，石也。【箋】鍛厲斧斤之石。【釋文】鍛本又作碫，丁亂反。《說文》云碫，厲石。</p>
<p>又矢名。【前漢&middot;衡山王傳】作輣車鍛矢。</p>
