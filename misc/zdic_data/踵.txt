                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>踵</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǒng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>脚后跟：举～。接～而至。</li><li>走到：～见（常去相见）。～谢。～门相告。</li><li>追随，继承：～继。～武（跟着别人的脚印走，喻继承前人的事业或是效法前人）。～事增华（指继承前人的事业并更加发展）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">踵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>踵</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，重声。本义:追逐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pursue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">踵，追也。从足，重声。一曰往来貌。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">踵二皇之遐武。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">踵见</span><span class="diczx2">仲尼</span>。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·德充符》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有司靡踵。——<span class="smcs">《史记·太史公自叙》</span>。索隐曰:<span class="yhcs">“继也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">踵，迹也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·皇后纪》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">骑者，军之司候也，所以踵败军，绝粮道，击便寇也。——<span class="smcs">《六韬·均兵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">吴</span>踵 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>，而疆场无备，邑能无亡乎?——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十四年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:踵呼(追随呐喊)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 跟随 <span class="encs">[follow close behind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">步兵踵军后数十万人。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·武帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:踵至(接踵而来);踵蹑(相继;接连)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 至;亲到 <span class="encs">[personly arrive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远近闻知，俱踵其门观看。——<span class="smcs">《包公案》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:踵谢(亲自登门道谢);踵门(登门;亲到其门)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 继承 <span class="encs">[inherit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">迎新在此岁，惟恐踵前迹。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《田家》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:踵古(沿袭古代);踵迹(犹继承);踵美(谓继承前人的功业、美德);踵袭(犹沿袭);踵续(连续不断)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>踵</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 脚后跟 (书面语词)。亦泛指脚 <span class="encs">[heel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">踵，足后曰跟，又谓之踵。踵，钟也。钟，聚也，体之所钟聚也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及前王之踵武。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">车轮曳踵。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">去一以为踵围。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·輈人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“后承轸者也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">踵狭而舌广。——<span class="smcs">《诗·苍伯》</span>笺</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">踵门而告</span><span class="diczx2">文王</span>。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></p><p><span
