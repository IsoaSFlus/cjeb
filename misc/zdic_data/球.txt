                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">球</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>球</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qi&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄡˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>圆形的立体物：圆～。～茎。～体。气～。煤～。</li>	<li>指球形的体育用品，球类运动：～艺。～员。～坛。～迷。</li>	<li>星体，特指&ldquo;地球&rdquo;：月～。星～。誉满全～。</li>	<li>美玉。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">球</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>球</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从玉，求声。本义:美玉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fine jade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">球，玉磬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，古以为磬，亦为笏，亦为刀室饰。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">球琳琅珰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;美玉也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天球。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;顾命》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;雍州所贡之玉色如天者。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:球琳(球与琳，都是美玉名);球玉(美玉);球琲(玉串);球府(玉府。古代皇室藏宝之府库);球琳器(比喻贤才);球璧(泛指珍宝);球玉(玉磬);球音(玉磬的声音);球琳(玉磬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同&ldquo;毬&rdquo;。鞠， 古代的一种游戏用具。今泛指某些圆球形的体育用品 <span class="encs">[ball]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:篮球;足球;乒乓球。亦指球类运动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 以半圆的直径为轴，使半圆旋转一周而成的立体 <span class="encs">[sphere]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:球体</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指球形或接近球形的物体 <span class="encs">[anything shaped like a ball]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:眼球;气球;棉球;球球(兽角弯曲貌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指星球 <span class="encs">[globe;sphere]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:月球;地球;球籍(指生存地球上的资格)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;捄&rdquo;。法 <span class="encs">[act]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">受大球小球，为下国缀旒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;长发》</span></span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">美玉。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;玉部》：「<b>球</b>，玉也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;禹貢》：「厥貢惟<b>球</b>、琳、瑯玕浮于積石，至于龍門西河，會于渭汭。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">玉製的打擊樂器。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;益稷》：「戛擊鳴<b>球</b>，摶拊琴瑟以詠。」</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝梁&middot;劉勰《文心雕龍&middot;原道》：「泉石激韻，和若<b>球</b>鍠。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">數學上指以半圓的直徑為軸，使半圓旋轉一周而成，且由中心至表面各點距離皆相等的立體。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「球體」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">球形或似球形的物體。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「地<b>球</b>」、「彩<b>球</b>」、「煤<b>球</b>」、「紅血<b>球</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">球類運動中所用的主要器材。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「籃<b>球</b>」、「棒<b>球</b>」、「乒乓<b>球</b>」、「橄欖<b>球</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞。計算球狀物的單位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「兩<b>球</b>毛線」、「兩<b>球</b>冰淇淋」。</span></p></li>
