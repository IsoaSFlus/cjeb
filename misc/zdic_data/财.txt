                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>财</strong></p><p>財 <span class="dicpy">cái</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cái.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，才声。本义:财物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(多指日常生活必需品，包括米粟在内) <span class="encs">[wealth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">财，人所宝也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">财，货也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以九赋敛财贿。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“泉谷也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与其财用。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·职方式》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“帛谷货贿也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先财而后礼。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·坊记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“币帛也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此轻财而重礼之义也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·聘义》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓璧琮享币也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">财之为言才也。凡粟米丝麻材木可用者曰财。——<span class="smcs">《六书故》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘其财用之出入。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官·宰夫》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暮而果大亡其财。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说难》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苟粟多而财有余，何为而不成?——</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:资财(资金与财物);生财;地财;浮财;洋财;邪财</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 财富;财产 <span class="encs">[money]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终不以监门困故而受公子财。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">务本节用财无极。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·成相》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:钱财;敛财(搜括钱财)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>财</strong></p><p>財 <span class="dicpy">cái</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cái.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“才”</span>。仅仅 <span class="encs">[only]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">财令</span><span class="diczx2">陵</span>为助兵。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李陵传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士财有数千。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广利传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“纔”</span>。刚刚 <span class="encs">[just]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃为小冠，高广财二寸。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·杜钦传》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>财</strong></p><p>財 <span class="dicpy">cái</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cái.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“裁”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 裁成，裁制 <span
