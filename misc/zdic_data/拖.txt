                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">拖</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>拖</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tuō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨㄛˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>牵引，拉，拽：～车。～船。～累（<span class="dicpy">lěi </span>）。～儿带女。</li>
	<li>耷拉着：～着辫子。</li>
	<li>延长时间：～延。～欠。～债。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> drag, tow, haul; delay, prolong</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ziehen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tirer,traîner,retarder</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">拖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>拖</strong></p>
<p>拕 <span class="dicpy">tuō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本作&ldquo;拕&rdquo;，左形，右声。本义:曳引)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[drag;haul;pull]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拕，引也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵体拖发。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;齐俗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拖其衣被。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;人间世》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拖舟而入水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;南越传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拖上州拔下县(被拖到州里，拉到县上);拖罟(拖网);拖钩(拔河的别称);拖刀计(假装败走将刀垂下，乘机回击之计);拖扯(拉扯;拉住)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 牵累，牵制 <span class="encs">[tie down]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:拖带(连带;附带);拖空子(受累亏空)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 下垂 <span class="encs">[hang]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">峨大冠拖长绅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:拖天扫地(形容衣服过长);拖玉(衣襟下垂带玉佩。喻指显贵);拖地锦(女子结婚时的披红);拖挂(下垂。亦指小竽之物);拖露(下垂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 拖延 <span class="encs">[delay;defer]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:拖水夹桨(拖泥带水，不爽快);拖腔(歌唱时字句后的延长音调);拖缠(拖延纠缠);这项工作的完成时间拖得太长了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 沉重或困难地负担着 <span class="encs">[burden]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:拖了一身债</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 强夺或费力取得 <span class="encs">[wrest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦牛缺</span>径于山中而遇盗&hellip;拖其衣被。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 披散 <span class="encs">[dishevel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵体拖发，箕倨反言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 掷 <span class="encs">[throw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔闻</span><span class="diczx2">杨伯起</span>，拖金振清风。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吴均</span>诗</p>

<p>又【禮&middot;少儀】僕者負良綏，申之面，拖諸幦。【疏】拖，猶擲也。亦引也。綏申於面前而引之，可置車幦上也。【論語】加朝服拖紳。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】宛虹拖於楯軒。【註】拖謂申加於上也。讀上聲或去聲。</p>
