                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>庵</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>圆形草屋（文人的书斋亦多称“庵”，如“老学～”，“影梅～”）。</li><li>小庙（指尼姑居住的）：～堂（尼姑庵）。～子。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>庵</strong> <span class="dicpy">ān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (闇的俗字作庵或菴)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 圆顶草屋 <span class="encs">[small thatched hut]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">草圆屋谓之庵。——<span class="smcs">《释名·释宫室》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庵，舍也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亲人菴庐。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·皇甫规传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">居河之湄，结划为庵。——<span class="smcs">《神仙传·焦先传》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:庵舍(设在墓旁的草屋);庵庐(草舍)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 尼姑的住所 <span class="encs">[nunnery]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一定另外或村庄或尼庵寻个下处。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:水月庵;庵堂(尼姑庵)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 庙宇 <span class="encs">[temple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既登峰头，一庵翼然，为文殊院。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:庵舍(小寺庙)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 旧时文人多用作字号或书斋名 <span class="encs">[study]</span>。如:老学庵;影梅庵</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/庵子">庵子</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5944_1164.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(奄)</span><span class="ywyy">覆也。大有餘也。</span><span class="swdz">釋言曰。荒、奄也。弇、同也。弇、葢也。古奄弇同用。覆葢同義。詩皇矣傳曰。奄、大也。執競傳曰。奄同也。鄭箋詩奄皆訓覆。許云覆也、大有餘也。二義實相因也。覆乎上者、往往大乎下。故字从大。周官經謂宦者爲奄。以精氣閉藏名之。覆葢義之引申也。</span><span class="ywyy">又欠也。</span><span class="swdz">三字未詳。方言曰。奄、息也。李密陳情表曰。氣息奄奄。</span><span class="ywyy">从大申。</span><span class="swdz">㑹意。依檢切。八部。</span><span class="ywyy">申、展也。</span><span class="swdz">說从申之意。申下曰神也。古屈伸多作詘信。不作伸、申。今則作申。俗又作伸。申本義不訓展也。故必特釋之。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
