<p>● <strong>填</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ti&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ti&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>把空缺的地方塞满或补满：～塞。～补。～充。～空（<span class="dicpy">k&ograve;ng </span>）。义愤～膺。</li>
	<li>在空白表格上按项目写：～表。～词。</li>
	<li>形容声音巨大：～然。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">填</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>填</strong> <span class="dicpy">ti&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ti&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，真声。本义:充塞)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fill;stuff]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">填，塞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">填坑满谷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">填如也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宾客亦填门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;郑当时传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">负草填之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">门人弟子填其室。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">填塞门户。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:填馅(填空;代替);填引(加引);填坑满谷(塞满坑谷);填街(充塞街道);填咽(充满空间);填盈(充满);填宫(古代犯官家属没入宫廷);填门(填户。形容登门人多);填噎(堵塞，拥挤)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 填写，写入 <span class="encs">[write;fill in]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 如:填纳税表;填报(填写上报);填造(填写编制);填红(旧时官员用红笔填写年月日);填具(填写具呈);填画(填写描画)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 补充 <span class="encs">[replenish]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 如:填替(补充代替);填纳(补缴)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 涂饰，涂抹 <span class="encs">[paint]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 如:填金;填白(在瓷器的深色图案中填月白色瓷);填漆</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通&ldquo;颠&rdquo;。跌落 <span class="encs">[fall]</span>。</p>
