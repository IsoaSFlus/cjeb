                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">稍</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>稍</strong> <span class="dicpy">shāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾，肖声。本义:禾末)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tip of standing grain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稍，出物有渐也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，此字当训禾末，与秒为谷芒者别。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:稍秣(给牛马吃的草料)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指事物的末端，枝叶 <span class="encs">[tip;the thin end of a twig]</span>。如:稍芟(指树枝芦荻等塞河之物);稍房(亦作<span class="yhcs">“梢间”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“梢房”</span>。距主房较远的房间);稍瓜(菜瓜)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 俸禄，官吏的薪水 <span class="encs">[an offical's salary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">均其稍食。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·内宰》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“吏禄禀也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今诸生学于太学，县官日有廪之供。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:稍食(月俸，按月而给的廪食);稍饩(月俸，廪食);稍房(堆放稻粮的屋子)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 赌本 <span class="encs">[money to gamble with]</span>。如:翻稍(翻本);稍物(做赌本的财物，金钱);稍长胆壮(赌本足，胆子大);稍挽稍(拿自己的赌资来博取别人的银钱)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 用同<span class="yhcs">“艄”</span>。称船家 <span class="encs">[boat man]</span>。如:稍婆(船家妇女;撑船的女子);稍工(艄公);稍子(艄公;船夫);稍公(撑船的人，掌舵的人)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>稍</strong> <span class="dicpy">shāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 逐渐 <span class="encs">[gradually]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">献孝之后，稍以蚕食六国。——<span class="smcs">《史记·秦楚之际月表》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稍，渐也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·郊祀志》</span>集注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渐渐，犹稍稍也。——<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吏稍侵辱之。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·周勃传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稍出近之，慭慭然，莫相知。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其后稍蚕食</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:稍杀(渐衰;稍差)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 略微;稍微 <span class="encs">[a little;slightly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稍，稍小也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span
