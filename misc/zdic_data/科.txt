                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>科</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kē.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄜˉ</span></p><ol><li>动植物的分类单位：狮子属于猫～。槐树是豆～。</li><li>机关内部组织的划分：～室。财务～。</li><li>学术或专业的类别：～目。学～。文～。外～。</li><li>古代分科考选文武官吏后备人员的制度：～举。～甲。～第。登～。</li><li>判定（刑罚）：～处（<span class="dicpy">chù</span>）。～罚。</li><li>古典戏剧本中指示角色表演动作时的用语：笑～。～白。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">棵</span>”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">科</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>科</strong> <span class="dicpy">kē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从禾，从斗。<span class="yhcs">“斗”</span>的意思是<span class="yhcs">“量”</span>。合起来指衡量、分别谷子的等级品类。本义:品类，等级)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sort]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">科，程也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为力不同科。——<span class="smcs">《论语·八佾》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与此同科。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·幸偶》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君歌且休听我歌，我歌今与君殊科。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《八月十五夜赠张巧曹》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:科品(种类;等级);科荣(科第荣华);科爵(指封建王朝官员的品级爵位)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 法令;刑律 <span class="encs">[laws and regulations]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三仪同科。——<span class="smcs">《太玄·玄离》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“法也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">从水之科满。——<span class="smcs">《太玄·从》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“法也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若有作奸犯科及为忠善者。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:科防互设(条律禁令交相设置);科文(法令条文);科令(法令;律条);科戒(修道的戒律、法规)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 科举考试的科目或等第 <span class="encs">[imperial examination]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设程试之科，重金爵之赏。——</span><span class="diczx2">韦昭</span><span class="smcs">《博奕论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:科分(科举中式之年份);科甲出身(由科举及第而取得入仕的资格);科法(科举取士之法);科贡(泛指科举)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 条款;段落 <span class="encs">[article]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">科条既备。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:科段(指文章的段落或部分);科条(科目，项目;条例;谓分类整理成条款、纲目)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 法式;规制 <span class="encs">[norms;rule]</span>。如:科式(法式。指宗教仪式);科制(制度;程式)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 课程或业务的分类 <span class="encs">[branch of study]</span>。如:文科;工科;理科;科本(课本，教科书)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 官署或机关中按工作性质分设的部门 <span class="encs">[section;department]</span>。如:科研所(从事科学研究的机构);科业(指行业中的某一部门);科道(指科道两衙门)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 指科斗文字。我国古代字体之一。以其笔划头圆大、尾细长、状似蝌蚪而得名 <span class="encs">[tadpole script]</span>。如:科斗文(科斗文字);科斗字(科斗文字);科斗书(科斗文字);科斗籍(古文经籍)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 传统戏剧角色的动作 <span class="encs">[act]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鼓三通，锣三下科。——<span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 又如:打科;笑科</p><p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 草丛 <span
