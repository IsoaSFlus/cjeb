                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>会</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">會</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>聚合，合在一起：～合。～审。～话。</li>	<li>多数人的集合或组成的团体：～议。开～。</li>	<li>城市，通常指行政中心：都（dū）～。省～。</li>	<li>彼此见面：～面。～见。</li>	<li>付钱：～账。～钞。</li>	<li>理解，领悟，懂：～心，体～。</li>	<li>能，善于：～游泳。～英语。</li>	<li>机会，时机，事情变化的一个时间：机～难得。</li>	<li>一定，应当：长风破浪～有时。</li>	<li>恰好，正好：～天大雨。</li>	<li>一小段时间：～会儿。</li></ol><p>● <strong>会</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">會</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ku&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄨㄞˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>总计：～计（①管理和计算财务的工作；②管理和计算财务的人）。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>会</strong></p>
<p>會 <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。古文从合，从彡。按彡亦众多意。本义:会合)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[get together;assemble]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">会，合也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">会，聚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">会同有绛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;车攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">会言近止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;杕杜》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">会朝清明。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;大明》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">会诸其币。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以会天地之藏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令&middot;季秋》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:会定(见到);会少离多(相会少，别离多);昨天我没有会着他</span></p>
