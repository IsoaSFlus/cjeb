                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>脩</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄡˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>干肉：&ldquo;凡肉～之颁赐，皆掌之。&rdquo;</li>	<li>旧时指教学的酬金：束～。～金。</li>	<li>干枯。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/修">修</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dried meat (used as teachers payment in ancient times)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ausbessern, reparieren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cultiver,réparer,paye d'un professeur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">脩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>脩</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，攸( <span class="dicpy">yōu</span>)声。本义:干肉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(加姜桂等香料，捶制而成) <span class="encs">[dried meat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脩，脯也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三问皆脩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;掌客》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">取糗与腶脩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;有司彻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枣栗段脩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;昏义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自行束脩以上，吾未尝无诲焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;述而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡肉脩之颁赐，皆掌之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脩脯(干肉);脩脡(即束脩，十条干肉)。又指用香料制成的干肉</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">析言之，则薄析曰辅，捶而施姜桂曰段脩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文解字注》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指学生致教师的酬金 <span class="encs">[salary for a teacher]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小儿跟随肄业，以房资作为脩金。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《镜花缘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脩脯(付给教师的酬金);脩金(送给老师的酬金)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;羞&rdquo;。美味的食品 <span class="encs">[fine food]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祀五帝，则掌百官之誓戒，与其具脩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脩，爵无数。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乡饮酒义》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>脩</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 高;长 <span class="encs">[long;tall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脩，长也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四牡脩广。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;六月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颈脩七寸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;投壶》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则及其大脩也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;弓人》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹久也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朱儒问径天高于脩人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山》</span>。注:&ldquo;长人也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脩垌(遥远的田野);脩眉(远山);脩路(长路;远道);脩途(长途，远路);脩隔(遥远而阻隔);脩久(长久);脩长(长远)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 美好 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8129.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】息流切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】思留切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音羞。【說文】脯也。【正字通】肉條，割而乾之也。【釋名】縮也。腊脯乾燥而縮。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;膳夫】凡肉脩之頒賜皆掌之。【註】脩，脯也。</p><p>又【內饔】凡掌共羞脩<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>膴胖骨鱐，以待共膳。【註】脩，鍛脯也。【左傳&middot;莊二十四年】女贄不過榛栗棗脩。【註】脩，脯也。【釋文】鍛脯加薑桂曰脩。</p><p>又治也，習也。【書&middot;說命】爾交脩子。【疏】令其交更脩治己也。【詩&middot;大雅】脩爾車馬。【禮&middot;禮運】講信脩睦。</p><p>又【韻會】長也。【詩&middot;小雅】四牡脩廣。【傳】脩，長也。</p><p>又【大雅】孔脩且張。【屈原&middot;離騷】路曼曼其脩遠兮，吾將上下而求索。【註】長也。</p><p>又掃除也。【周禮&middot;天官】掌百官之誓戒，與其具脩。【註】脩，掃除糞酒。【禮&middot;祭義】宮室旣脩。【註】脩，設，謂除及黝堊。</p><p>又備也。【周語】脩其簠簋。【註】備也。</p><p>又久也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;弓人】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230AA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣂪</span>目不荼，則及其大脩也，筋代之受病。【註】脩，猶久也。</p><p>又儆也。【魯語】吾冀而朝夕脩我。【註】儆也。</p><p>又乾也。【詩&middot;王風】中谷有蓷，暵其脩矣。【傳】脩，且乾也。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋樂】徒鼓鍾謂之脩。</p><p>又草名。【山海經】賈超之山，其中多龍脩。【註】郭曰：龍須也。似莞而細，生山石穴中。</p><p>又魚名。【山海經】橐山，橐水出焉，其中多脩辟之魚。</p><p>又姓。【韻會】漢有屯騎校尉脩炳。</p><p>又人名。【屈原&middot;離騷】吾令蹇脩以爲理。【註】蹇脩，伏羲氏之臣也。</p><p>又【正韻】云九切，音有。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;鬯人】廟用脩。【註】脩，器名，漆尊也。鄭康成曰：脩讀爲卣。</p><p>又【集韻】他彫切，音祧。縣名，在信都。周亞夫封邑。【前漢&middot;恩澤侯表序】孝景將侯王氏，脩侯犯色。【註】脩，讀曰條。</p><p>又【類篇】思邀切，音宵。脩脩，羽敝也。　【正字通】說文脩，脯也。修，飾也。分爲二。今脩修通。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
