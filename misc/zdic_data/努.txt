                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">努</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>努</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">nǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>尽量地使出力量：～力。</li>
	<li>突出，撅着：～着嘴。～嘴。</li>
	<li>因用力太过，身体内部受伤：用力不要太猛，别～着。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to exert, strive, make an effort; to pout</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anwenden, gebrauchen ,bestreben, erstreben</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faire des efforts,s'efforcer,s'exercer</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>努</strong> <span class="dicpy">nǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，奴声。本义:勉力，出力)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[exert effort;put forth strenggth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少壮不努力，老大徒伤悲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;长歌行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:努筋拔力(竭尽全力;费了很大的力)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 向外突出 <span class="encs">[protrude;bulge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却才见押司努嘴过来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:努唇胀嘴(撅着嘴，表示不高兴的样子);努咀(撅嘴;以嘴示意);努目(努眼。把眼睛张大，使眼球突出);努嘴胖唇(鼓嘴凸唇。形容不高兴);努臂(努膊。伸臂)</span></p>
