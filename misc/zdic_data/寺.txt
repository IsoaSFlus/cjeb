						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>寺</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sì.mp3"></a></span> ㄙˋ</span></p><ol><li>古代官署名：太常～（古代掌管宗庙礼仪的官署）。鸿胪～（略同于现代的礼宾司）。</li><li>佛教出家人居住的地方：佛～。～观（<span class="dicpy">guàn </span>）。</li><li>伊斯兰教徒礼拜、讲经的地方：清真～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寺</strong> <span class="dicpy">sì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形，从寸，之声。寸，与法度有关。本义:古代官署的名称。秦以官员任职之所，通称为寺。如:大理寺(职掌审核刑狱案件))</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[government house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寺，廷也，有法度者也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寺，治也，官舍也。——<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寺，官舍也。——<span class="smcs">《三苍 》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寺，官也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span>。按，朝中官曹所止理事之处。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令骑奴还至寺门。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·何竝传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“诸官曹之所，通呼为寺。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">城郭官寺。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·元帝纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“凡府廷所在，皆谓之寺。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">列寺七里。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:寺正(大理寺正卿的略称);寺舍(官舍。官署办公的房子;佛教僧侣的房舍);寺库(古时寺观里设的当铺);寺省(古时中央行政机构<span class="yhcs">“省”</span>和<span class="yhcs">“寺”</span>的合称);寺曹(九卿官署);寺署(官署);寺卿(九寺大卿的简称);寺棘(大理寺的别称);寺丞(官署中的佐吏)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 佛教的庙宇 <span class="encs">[temple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寺，</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="diczx2">西域</span>白马驼经来，初止于 <span class="diczx2">鸿胪寺</span>，遂取寺名，创置 <span class="diczx2">白马寺</span>。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南朝四百八十寺，多少楼台烟雨中。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《江南春绝句》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五里，至汤寺，浴于汤池。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孤山寺</span>北贾亭西，水面初平云脚低。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《钱塘湖春行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">左忠毅公</span>视学京畿，一日，风雪严寒，从数骑出，微行入古寺。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:寺宇(寺院);寺刹(寺和塔);寺主(主管佛寺事务的僧人);寺庵(僧和尼所居的寺庙);寺寝(祠庙的后殿)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 某些宗教供礼拜、讲经的处所 <span class="encs">[monastery]</span>。如:礼拜寺;摩尼寺;特指伊斯兰教礼拜讲经的地方</p><p><span class="diczx1">清真寺</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 奄人，内监。古代皇宫内供使令的小臣 <span class="encs">[eunuch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未见君子，寺人之令。——<span class="smcs">《诗·秦风·车邻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪教匪诲，时维妇寺。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·瞻卬》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>寺人 <span class="diczx2">貂</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阍，门者也，寺人也。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·襄公二十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:寺人(太监，古代宫内供使的小臣，东汉后专指宦官)</p><p><span
