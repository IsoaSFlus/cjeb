                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">国</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>国</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">國</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gu&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄛˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>有土地、人民、主权的政体（古代指诸侯所受封的地域）：～家。～土。～体（ ➊ 国家的性质； ➋ 国家的体面）。～号。～度（指国家）。～策。～情。～法。～力。～防。～威。～宝（➊ 国家的宝物；➋ 喻对国家有特殊贡献的人）。～格。～魂。～是（国家大计，如&ldquo;共商～是&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>特指中国的：～产。～货。～粹。～乐（<span class="dicpy">yu&egrave; ㄩㄝˋ</span>）。～药。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">国</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ 國</p>
<p>囯 <span class="dicpy">gu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从&ldquo;囗&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span>)，表示疆域。从或(即&ldquo;国&rdquo;)。&ldquo;或&rdquo;亦兼表字音。本义:邦国)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 周代，天子统治的是&ldquo;天下&rdquo;，略等于现在说的&ldquo;全国&rdquo; <span class="encs">[state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国，邦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以佐王治邦国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;太宰》</span>。注:&ldquo;大曰邦，小曰国。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">方千里曰国畿，诅祝以叙国之信用，以资邦国之剂信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司马》</span>。注:&ldquo;国谓王之国;邦国，谓诸侯国也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都城过百雉，国之害也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">丘</span>也闻有国有邦者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;微子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫大国，难测也，惧有伏焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>人开关延敌，九国之师，逡巡而不敢进。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《新书&middot;过秦论上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:齐国;晋国;郑国</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 国都，一国最高政权机关所在地。又称国城，国邑 <span class="encs">[capital]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国中九经九纬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;匠人》</span>。注:&ldquo;城内也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三曰国禁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;士师》</span>。注:&ldquo;城中也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在国曰市井之臣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓都邑也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">土国地漕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;击鼓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先王之制，大都不过参国之一，中五之一，小九之一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">武夫力而拘诸原，妇人暂而免诸国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿君顾先王之宗庙，姑反国统万人乎!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">登斯楼也，则有去国怀乡，忧馋畏讥，满目萧然，感极而悲者矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:国中(王城之内;国内);国人(国都中的人);国迁(国都迁徙);国阴(都城北郊);国郊(国都周围地名);国禁(古代国都中的禁令);国邑(城邑);国刑(城中施行的刑罚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 国家 <span class="encs">[country;state;nation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聊以行国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;园有桃》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国无有残。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;民劳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">僵卧孤村不自哀，尚思为国戍</span><span class="diczx2">轮台</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《十一月四日风雨大作》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">思国之安者，必积其德义。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国人皆以诸先烈之牺牲精神为国奋斗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:国纪(旧指国家礼制与法令);国帑(国库中的钱币);国禄(国家的俸禄);国课(国税;国家税收);国器(可主持国政的人才)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代王、侯的封地 <span class="encs">[feud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汉王之国，</span><span class="diczx2">良</span>送至 <span class="diczx2">褒中</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;留侯之家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孟尝君</span>就国于 <span class="diczx2">薛</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:国租(封地的田赋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 帝王 <span class="encs">[emperor&#39;s]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:国孝(为皇帝、后妃或皇帝父母服丧);国丈(帝王的岳父);国太(帝王之母的俗称);国姻(帝王的姻亲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 部落 <span class="encs">[tribe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">韩</span>]</span>凡七十八国。 <span class="diczx2">伯济</span>是其一国焉。大者万余户，小者数千家，各在山海间。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 地方 <span class="encs">[place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">荆州</span>北据 <span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="diczx2">沔</span>，利尽南海，东连 <span class="diczx2">吴会</span>，西通 <span class="diczx2">巴</span><span class="diczx2">蜀</span>，此用武之国。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陈寿</span><span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逝将去女，适彼乐国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;硕鼠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 家乡 <span class="encs">[hometown]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">侃</span>]</span>欲逊位归国，佐吏等苦留之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 姓</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">國</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“国”字头，请参考“國”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=囗" target="_blank">囗</a>】
                              國·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/570B.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕囗囶圀<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/229CC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢧌</span>䆐【唐韻】古或切【集韻】骨或切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>觥入聲。【說文】邦也。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;量人】掌建國之法，以分國爲九州。</p>
<p>又【冬官&middot;考工記】匠人營國，旁三門，國中九經九緯，經涂九軌，左祖右社，面朝後市。【禮&middot;王制】五國以爲屬，十國以爲連，二十國以爲卒，二百一十國以爲州。【孟子】大國，地方百里，次國，地方七十里，小國，地方五十里。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;地官&middot;掌節】山國用虎節，土國用人節，澤國用龍節。【註】山國多山者，土國平地也，澤國多水者。</p>
<p>又滅人之國曰勝國。【左傳註】勝國者，絕其社稷，有其土地也。</p>
<p>又九州之外曰外國。亦曰絕國。【後漢&middot;班超傳】君侯在外國三十餘年。【又】遠處絕國。</p>
<p>又兩國相距曰敵國。【孟子】敵國不相征也。</p>
<p>又外國來附者曰屬國。【李陵&middot;答蘇武書】聞子之歸位，不過典屬國。【註】典，掌也。卽掌屬國之事者。</p>
<p>又城郭國，行國。【宋程大昌備北邊對】漢西域諸國，有城郭國，有行國。城郭國，築城爲守者，行國不立城，以馬上爲國也。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓苑】太公之後。齊有國氏，世爲上卿，宋有國卿。</p>
