                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蹇</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>蹇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>跛，行走困难：～足。～步。</li>
	<li>迟钝，不顺利：～涩。～滞。～拙。～运。时乖命～。</li>
	<li>傲慢。</li>
	<li>穷困。</li>
	<li>驽马，亦指驴。</li>
	<li>文言语助词：～谁留兮中洲？</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> lame, crippled; unlucky</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schwierigkeiten, Schwierigkeit  (S)​,Jian  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> boiter,malheur</p></div>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蹇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蹇</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，寒省声。本义:跛足)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蹇，跛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蹇膝伸不屈、易蹇、往蹇来连。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;骨空论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驾蹇驴而无策兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;谬谏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郤</span>克偻，而鲁使蹇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;晋世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">策蹇驴，囊图书。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">红装而蹇者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蹇兔(不能急走的跛脚兔。比喻弱小者);蹇驴(走不快的羸弱之驴);蹇人上天(跛子登天。比喻不可能的事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 艰阻，不顺利 <span class="encs">[hard;untoward]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蹇侘傺而含戚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;哀郢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蹇拙(艰险;不走运);蹇蹇(艰难、不顺遂的样子);蹇剥(艰困，比喻时运不好);蹇步(行走艰难);蹇连(艰难的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;謇&rdquo;。口吃 <span class="encs">[stutter]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蹇言</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 傲慢 <span class="encs">[proud]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:偃蹇;骄蹇;蹇纵(傲慢放纵);蹇傲(高傲;傲慢)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 正直的样子 <span class="encs">[upright]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蹇辞(忠正耿直之言);蹇蹇(忠正耿直的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 行动迟缓 <span class="encs">[slow]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蹇蹶(步履缓慢的样子);蹇缓(步履缓慢)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>蹇</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 劣马或跛驴 <span class="encs">[jade or lame donkey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">策蹇赴前程。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孟浩然</span><span class="smcs">《唐城馆中早发寄杨使君》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蹇味儿(驴子的别称;蠢驴);蹇驴一鸣，荒鸡三号(天亮时。比喻事情见了分晓);蹇驴(跛蹇驽弱的驴);蹇蹄(劣马);蹇瘠(瘦弱的驴马)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>蹇</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>骑驴 <span class="encs">[ride a donkey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">红装而蹇者，亦时时有。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蹇</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=足" target="_blank">足</a>】
                              蹇·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8E47.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】居偃切【集韻】【韻會】紀偃切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音犍。【說文】跛也。【釋名】蹇，跛蹇也，病不能執事役也。【史記&middot;晉世家】卻克僂，而魯使蹇，衞使眇。【前漢&middot;敘傳】駑蹇之乗。【莊子&middot;達生篇】聾盲跛蹇。</p>
<p>又【易&middot;蹇卦】蹇，難也，險在前也。【又】王臣蹇蹇。【疏】涉蹇難而往濟蹇，故曰王臣蹇蹇也。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;哀六年】彼皆偃蹇，將棄子命。【註】偃蹇，驕敖。【屈原&middot;離騷】望瑤臺之偃蹇兮。【註】高貌。【又】何瓊珮之偃蹇兮。【註】衆盛貌。</p>
<p>又【九歌】靈偃蹇兮姣服。【註】舞貌。【補註】委曲貌。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】蹇產溝瀆。【註】蹇產，屈折也。</p>
<p>又【管子&middot;四時篇】毋蹇華絕芋。【註】蹇，拔也。</p>
<p>又【屈原&middot;離騷】蹇將憺兮壽宮。【註】蹇，詞也。</p>
<p>又【博雅】蹇，擾也。</p>
<p>又姓。【左傳&middot;僖三十二年】穆公訪諸蹇叔。【註】秦大夫。【屈原&middot;離騷】吾令蹇脩以爲理。【註】蹇脩，伏羲之臣也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】九輦切【集韻】【韻會】九件切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>搴上聲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】巨偃切，音鍵。義<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。</p>
<p>又與褰同。【莊子&middot;山木篇】蹇裳躩步。【楚辭&middot;九章】憚蹇裳而濡足。【補註】蹇，讀若褰。</p>
<p>又與謇同。【屈原&middot;離騷】余固知謇謇之爲患兮。【註】謇謇，忠貞貌。易曰：王臣謇謇。【補註】今《易》作蹇蹇。先儒引經多如此，蓋古今本不同耳。【六書正譌】蹇从足，寒省聲。跛爲正義，借爲蹇難、蹇直字。別作謇，非。</p>
