						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>厢</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>在正房前面两旁的房屋：东～房。西～房。</li><li>边，方面：这～。</li><li>靠近城的地区：城～。关～。</li><li>戏院或影院里特别隔开的座位：包～。</li><li>车里容纳人或货的地方：车～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>厢</strong></p><p>厢 <span class="dicpy">xiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从厂( <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>)相声。从厂，与房屋有关。本义:东西廊)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[eastern or western gallary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厢，廊也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">室有东西厢曰庙。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释宫》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孙</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“厢，夹室前堂。”</span> <span class="diczx2">李</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“厢，谓宗庙殿有东西小堂也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">下雕辇于东厢。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徘徊东西厢，孤妾谁与俦。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韦应物</span>诗</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 厢房，在正房前面两侧的房屋 <span class="encs">[wing-room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吕后侧耳于东厢听。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·周昌传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“正寝之东西室皆曰箱，言似箱箧之形。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">月暗西厢，凤去秦楼。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">王实甫</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东厢扶案。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:东厢;西厢;厢房;厢库(纸扎的房屋);厢悬(古代礼乐制度。悬钟磬类乐器于厢室中)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 堂屋的东西墙 <span class="encs">[eastern or western wall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒺藜蔓乎东厢。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 边;旁 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怕不是我身厢有甚么缠魂不定?——</span><span class="diczx2">汤显祖</span><span class="smcs">《南柯记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:两厢;这厢;那厢;让开大路，占领两厢</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 宋代划分京城地区为若干厢，相当今日的区 <span class="encs">[district]</span>。如:厢禁军(对厢军、禁军的混合称呼;泛指士兵)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 靠近城的地方为厢 <span class="encs">[the vicinity outside of the city gate]</span>。如:关厢;城厢;厢村(城郊的乡村)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 车厢 <span class="encs">[carriage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是先生乃舒虹霓以蕃尘，倾雪盖以蔽明，倚瑶厢而徘徊，总众辔而安行。——</span><span class="diczx2">阮籍</span><span class="smcs">《大人先生传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 用同<span class="yhcs">“箱”</span>。箱箧 <span class="encs">[box]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又把豆子磨了一厢豆腐，也都卖了。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">打开这药厢，将小舍人放在里面。——</span><span class="diczx2">纪君祥</span><span class="smcs">《赵氏孤儿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:厢子(箱子);一厢豆腐</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 类似厢的隔间 <span class="encs">[certain places resembling a compartment]</span>。如:包厢; <span class="encs">[火车]</span>
