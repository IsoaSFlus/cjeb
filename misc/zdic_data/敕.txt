<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>敕</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>帝王的诏书、命令：～命。～书。～封。奉～。宣～。</li>
	<li>告诫：申～。戒～。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">饬</span>&rdquo;，整顿。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> an imperial order or decree</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> édit</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">敕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>敕</strong></p>
<p>勅 <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，束声。本义:告诫，嘱咐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[admonish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敕，诫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敕天之命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敕晓(告戒晓谕);敕谕天下(朝廷告谕全国);敕戒(警戒，教诫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;饬&rdquo;。整治;整饬 <span class="encs">[put in order]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既匡既敕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子以明罚勅法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;噬嗑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敕身齐戒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;礼乐志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惧余身之未敕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敕法(整饬法令);敕设(整饬周备)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 委任，任命 <span class="encs">[appoint]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:敕目(任命官员的名单)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 皇帝下命令 <span class="encs">[order imperially]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:敕赐(皇帝命令赐与);敕许(敕命准许)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>敕</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>皇帝，帝王自上命下之词(汉时凡尊长或官长告诫子孙或僚属，皆称敕。南北朝以后专指皇帝诏书) <span class="encs">[imperial]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">手把文书口称敕，回车叱牛牵向北。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敕书(皇帝行文给臣僚的文书);敕命(皇帝颁赐爵位或物品的诏命);敕符(书有皇帝命令的凭证)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">敕</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集下" target="_blank">卯集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集下&amp;bs=攴" target="_blank">攴</a>】
                              敕·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6555.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】恥力切【集韻】【韻會】蓄力切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音勅。【說文】試也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】敕勞。【註】敕者，相約敕也。亦爲勞苦。【釋名】敕，飭也，使自警飭，不敢廢慢也。【史記&middot;樂書】余每讀虞書，至於君臣相敕。【後漢&middot;安帝紀】能敕身率下。【韓非子&middot;主道篇】賢者敕其才，君因而任之。</p>
<p>又【後漢&middot;光武紀註】漢制度曰：帝之下書有四。一曰策書，二曰制書，三曰詔書，四曰誡敕。誡敕者，謂敕刺史太守。【正字通】宋敕或用之於獎諭，非敕初意。明制，差遣諸臣，予敕行事，備載職守，申以勉詞。凡褒嘉責讓，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>用敕，詞皆散文。六品以下官贈封稱敕命，始用四六。</p>
<p>又【博雅】理也，謹也，語也，進也。【小爾雅】正也。【廣韻】固也，急也。</p>
<p>又【說文】臿地曰敕。　【說文】从攴作敕。【玉篇】本作勑，今相承皆作勅。互詳力部勅字註。敕字原从攴作。</p>
