						<p>● <strong>傥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3"><img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">儻</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">tǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄤˇ</span></p>

	<li>〔倜～〕见&ldquo;<a href="/hans/倜">倜</a>&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>失意：&ldquo;文侯～然，终日不言&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">傥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>傥</strong></p>
<p>儻 <span class="dicpy">tǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，党声。本义: 洒脱不拘，不拘于俗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[unconventional]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赞陈汤傥荡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;傅常郑甘陈段传》</span>。注:&ldquo;无行检也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貌若傥荡不备。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;史丹传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:倜傥(洒脱;不拘束)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 怅然自失，恍惚 <span class="encs">[absentminded]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">文候</span>傥然，终日不言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;田子方》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:傥然(失志的样子);傥莽(怅然自失)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 正直 <span class="encs">[upright]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:傥言(正直的言论);傥论(堂皇正大的言论)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 广大 <span class="encs">[broad]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:傥漭(广阔无边);傥阆(广大宽敞);倘莽(旷远);傥朗(晦暗不明的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 安闲自得 <span class="encs">[leisurely]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:傥佯(安闲自得地行走);傥傥(舒闲自适地)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>傥</strong></p>
<p>儻 <span class="dicpy">tǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈连〉</p>
<p>表示假设，相当于&ldquo;倘若&rdquo;、&ldquo;如果&rdquo; <span class="encs">[if]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">傥所谓天道，是邪?非邪?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;伯夷传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:傥若;傥使</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>傥</strong></p>
<p>儻 <span class="dicpy">tǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>偶然，意外地 <span class="encs">[by accident]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">况荣宠贵盛，傥来物也，可恃以凌人乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span>。又如:傥来(意外得来);傥然(偶然;侥幸)</p>
