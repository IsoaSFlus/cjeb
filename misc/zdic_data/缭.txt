                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缭</strong></p><p>繚 <span class="dicpy">liáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，表示与丝有关尞( <span class="dicpy">liáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáo.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:缠绕)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wind round]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缭，缠也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">再缭四寸。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“绕也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弗缭。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡饮酒礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹紾也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肠纷纭以缭转兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·怨思》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缭之兮杜衡。——</span><span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《九歌·湘夫人》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">林麓薮泽陂池连乎</span><span class="diczx2">蜀汉</span>，缭以周墙，四百余里。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:缭肘(即<span class="yhcs">“镣ń”</span>。囚禁犯人的脚镣、手铐);缭绕(缠绕);缭纠(缠绕相连貌)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 围绕;绕道而行 <span class="encs">[make a detour]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">矜纠收缭之属。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·议兵》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“缭绕，言委曲也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每一门内，四缭连屋。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·金和尚》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:缭曲(迂回曲折;婉转含蓄);缭眺(谓声清而长)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 祭祀名 <span class="encs">[offer a sacrifice to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">八月缭祭。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大祝》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“以手纵持肺本，循之至于末，乃绝以祭也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:缭祭(周礼九祭之一。祭以左手纵持肺根，右手取肺尖，缭绕使断，取以为祭，故称)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 缝纫法之一。用针斜着缝缀 <span class="encs">[stitch]</span>。如:缭衣缝;缭贴边</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“撩”</span>。撩乱，纷乱 <span class="encs">[disorder;wind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缭乱边愁听不尽。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王昌龄</span><span class="smcs">《从军行》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>缭</strong></p><p>繚 <span class="dicpy">liáo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liáo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用以固定船帆的绳索 <span class="encs">[sail rope]</span>。如:帆缭;缭手</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">lǎo;liǎo</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E5A.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】落蕭切【集韻】【韻會】憐蕭切【正韻】連條切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音聊。【說文】纏也。【類篇】繞也。【禮·玉藻】再繚四寸。【班固·東都賦】修袖繚繞而滿庭。</p><p>又祭名。【周禮·春官·大祝】辨九祭，八曰繚祭。【儀禮·鄉飮酒禮】弗繚，右絕末以祭。【註】繚，猶紾也。</p><p>又人名。【前漢·藝文志】尉繚二十九篇。【註】師古曰：尉姓，繚名也。</p><p>又縣名。【前漢·地理志】淸和郡有繚縣。</p><p>又【集韻】朗鳥切【正韻】盧皎切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音了。義同。</p><p>又【集韻】離昭切，音髎。力照切，音燎。義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。</p><p>又【廣韻】力小切，音燎。【莊子·盜跖篇】繚意絕體而爭。【音義】繚，理也。</p><p>又【集韻】爾紹切，音擾。人名。莊子有黃繚。</p><p>又【韻補】叶力虯切。【楚辭·招隱士】偃蹇連卷兮枝相繚。【註】繚，紐也。</p><p>
