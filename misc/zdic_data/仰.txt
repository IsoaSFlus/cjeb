                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>仰</strong> <span class="dicpy">áng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/áng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“昂”</span>。高 <span class="encs">[high]</span>。如:仰仰(同<span class="yhcs">“昂昂”</span>。气概轩昂的样子);仰首伸眉(意气高昂的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>仰</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从人，从卬，卬亦声。卬，小篆字形。一个人站着，一个人跪着，跪者抬头看站者，为抬头仰望的<span class="yhcs">“仰”</span>的本字。后借为<span class="yhcs">“我”</span>，遂加<span class="yhcs">“人”</span>作<span class="yhcs">“仰”</span>。<span class="yhcs">“卬”</span><span class="yhcs">“仰”</span>古今字。本义:抬头，脸向上)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。跟<span class="yhcs">“俯”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[face upward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仰，举也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，仰即卬之或体。汉书皆作卬，不作仰。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驷马仰秼。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仰足以事父母。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仰头相向鸣。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仰药而伏刃。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·息夫躬传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“仰首而饮药。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无敢仰视。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span
