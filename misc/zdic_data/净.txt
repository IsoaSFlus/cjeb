                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">净</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>净</strong></p><p>凈 <span class="dicpy">jìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，争声。本义:春秋时鲁国北城门<span class="yhcs">“争门”</span>的护城河)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Jing moat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">净，</span><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>北城门池也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，北城之门曰净门，当因池水名净而命也。公羊传所谓鹿门、争门、吏门，盖以争为之。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 京剧中的一类角色 <span class="encs">[actor with a painted face]</span>。如:生旦净末;净角(净脚。戏剧角色之一，一般扮演性格刚烈粗鲁或奸险的人物，俗称<span class="yhcs">“花脸”</span>)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>净</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“瀞”</span>。清洁，洁净。亦用于形容抽象事物。亦指明净 <span class="encs">[clean]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风烟俱净。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亭亭净植。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:窗明几净;白净(白而洁净);净本(唐人称誊写清楚的文书);净袜(白色布袜);净色(明净的月色);净衣(洁净的衣服)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 空无所有，无余 <span class="encs">[exhausted]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">北累既除，表里俱净。——</span><span class="diczx2">萧子良</span><span class="smcs">《开物归信门》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:净办(净辨。消静，干净利索);净光(一点不剩);净尽(一点不剩)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 纯，纯粹 <span
