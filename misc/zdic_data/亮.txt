                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>亮</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄤˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>明，有光：天～了，敞～。明～。豁～。～光。～度。</li>
	<li>光线：屋子里一点～儿也没有。</li>
	<li>明摆出来，显露，显示：～相。</li>
	<li>明朗，清楚：心里～了。</li>
	<li>声音响：洪～。响～。</li>
	<li>使声音响：～开嗓子唱。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亮</strong> <span class="dicpy">li&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>一说本作&ldquo;倞&rdquo;，后移&ldquo;人&rdquo;于&ldquo;京&rdquo;下，又变作&ldquo;儿&rdquo;，并省去&ldquo;京&rdquo;中一竖。本义:明亮)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亮，明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;古人名亮者字明。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:诸葛亮，字孔明。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皎皎亮月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;嵇康&middot;杂诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寸心若不亮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">谢灵运</span><span class="smcs">《初发&middot;石首城诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且火德承</span><span class="diczx2">尧</span>，虽昧必亮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;苏竟传》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">李贤</span>注:&ldquo;亮，明也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亮钟(更楼上报天亮的钟声);亮隔(亮槅。有透光花格的窗户、门窗等);亮色(明亮的色彩);亮油油(形容光亮而润泽);亮闪(明亮);亮莹莹(形容光亮透明);亮藿藿(形容明光耀眼);亮灼灼(犹亮晶晶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;谅&rdquo;。诚信;忠诚 <span class="encs">[sincere]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孟子</span>曰:君子不亮，恶乎执?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亮直(诚实正直;诚实正直的人);亮节(清高的节操);亮怀(忠直、坦荡的胸怀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 响亮<span class="encs">[loud and clear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其鸣高亮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鹤鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亮溜(指声音嘹亮溜圆);亮彻(指声音响亮清脆);亮节(高亢之声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 明白，清楚 <span class="encs">[obvious;plain;as clear as day]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">即蒙亮许，当赐矜擢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋书&middot;王僧达传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亮拔(明达章理、才能出众);亮许(明察允许);亮察(明鉴);亮照(亮察)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亮</strong> <span class="dicpy">li&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 辅佐 <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亮，佐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟时亮天工。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p>
