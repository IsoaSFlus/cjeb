<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鬃</strong></p>
<p>鬉 <span class="dicpy">zōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 某些哺乳动物颈部周围生长的又长又密的毛 <span class="encs">[mane;bristle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鬃白似披</span><span class="diczx2">梁苑</span>雪，颈肥如扑 <span class="diczx2">杏园</span>花。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">韦庄</span><span class="smcs">《代书寄马诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:鬃帽(用马、猪等颈毛所制的帽子);马鬃;猪鬃</p>
