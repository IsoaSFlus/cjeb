                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>季</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>兄弟排行次序最小的：～弟（小弟）。～父（小叔叔）。</li><li>末了：～世。～春（春季末一月）。明～（明代末年）。～军（体育运动竞赛的第三名）。</li><li>一年的四分之一：一年分春夏秋冬四～。～度。～风。</li><li>一段时间：～节。～候。～相（<span class="dicpy">xiàng </span>）（某个地区在某个季节的自然景象）。雨～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">季</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>季</strong> <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从子，从稚省。<span class="yhcs">“稚”</span>亦兼表读音。本义:即排行最后的)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(与<span class="yhcs">“老”</span>相对) <span class="encs">[youngest among brothers]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">季，少称。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曰伯某甫仲叔季，唯其所当。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“伯、仲、叔、季，长幼之称。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">季者，有中之辞也。—<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">季者，幼也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有齐季女。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·采蘋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生四子，太祖其季也。——<span class="smcs">《明史·太祖本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:季女(最小的女儿);季父(父亲的幼弟);季指(小指头);季弟(最小的弟弟)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 一年四季中，每季的最后一个月 <span class="encs">[the last month of a season]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">季春之月。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">季冬讲武，习射御。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《时令论上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:季月(每季的最后一月，即农历三、六、九、十二月);季考(宋代太学中每一季度末举行的考试);季商(农历九月)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 下等<span class="encs">[inferior]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">季绢。——<span class="smcs">《管子·乘马》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“三等其下者曰季。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:季祖母(祖父之妾);季绢(下等的丝绢)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 物之幼嫩者 <span class="encs">[young]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡服耜斩季材。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·山虞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:季材(幼嫩的木材)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 以第四级取代为特点的 <span class="encs">[quaternary]</span>。如在有机基团中与四个碳原子相联结的。如:季碳原子</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>季</strong> <span class="dicpy">jì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 三个月为一季，一年分春夏秋冬四季 <span
