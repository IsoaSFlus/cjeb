                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>痕</strong> <span class="dicpy">hén</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hén.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chuáng</span>)，表示与疾病有关，艮( <span class="dicpy">gèn</span>)声。本义:疮伤痊愈后留下的疤)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[scar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">痕，胝瘢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">创瘢曰痕。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:痕瑕(瘢痕，斑点);痕挞(伤痕);痕瘕(疤痕。比喻曾犯过罪);伤痕</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指痕迹 <span class="encs">[imprint;track;mark]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸟归沙有迹，帆过浪无痕。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾岛</span><span class="smcs">《江亭晚望》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苔痕上阶绿。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">空余泪痕。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:痕垢(污垢);痕印(痕迹;烙印);痕沫(水沫的痕迹);痕秽(污秽的痕迹);痕影(痕迹)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 影子。特指阴影 <span class="encs">[shadow]</span>。如:痕累(因事牵连而受累);痕路(指物体镶嵌的纹路)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/痕迹">痕迹</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
