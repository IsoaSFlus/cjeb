                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>艰</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">艱</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>困难：～难。～辛。～险。～深。～涩（形容晦涩难懂的文词）。～贞（处境艰难而能忠贞不移）。～苦卓绝（艰苦程度超过一切，无可比拟）。</li><li>旧时指父母亲丧事：丁～。母～。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>艰</strong></p><p>艱 <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。表示与土有关，艮( <span class="dicpy">gèn</span>)声。本义:土难治理)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">艰，土难治也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“引申之，凡难理皆曰艰。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 艰难;困难 <span class="encs">[difficult;hard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">艰，难也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">艰则无咎。——<span class="smcs">《易·大有》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遗大投艰于朕身。——<span class="smcs">《书·大诰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">固国之艰急是为。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长太息以掩涕兮，哀民生之多艰。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">艰其昏晨之往来。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勤且艰若此。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:艰虞(艰难困苦);艰屯(艰苦险阻);艰患(艰难祸患);艰急(困难);艰深(艰难深奥，晦涩难解);艰苦奋斗(以巨大的努力克服艰难困苦而进行);艰苦朴素(不怕苦累、生活俭朴、不追求享受的态度和行为);艰疚(忧困，苦难);艰厄(艰阨。饥乏;困乏);艰棘(困苦，危难)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 险恶，危难 <span class="encs">[dangerous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼何人斯，其心孔艰。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·何人斯》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:艰贞(指人处境艰危之时能守正不屈);艰地(险境);艰难(危险);艰关(崎岖辗转，历尽千辛万苦)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>艰</strong></p><p>艱 <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 旧指父母之丧事 <span class="encs">[(of parents) die;deceased]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又以居母艰去官。——</span><span class="diczx2">王俭</span><span class="smcs">《褚渊碑文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如::丁艰(父或母丧);外艰(父丧);内艰(母丧);艰疚(丧亲之痛);艰棘(亲丧);艰酷(亲丧。亲亡为惨痛之事，故称)</p><p>◎
