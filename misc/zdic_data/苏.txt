                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">苏</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>苏</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">➊-➐<span class="diczx3">蘇</span>、➑囌）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>植物名（&ldquo;紫苏&rdquo;或&ldquo;白苏&rdquo;的种子，称&ldquo;苏子&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>指须头下垂物：流～。</li>
	<li>昏迷中醒过来：～生。～醒。死而复～。</li>
	<li>缓解，解除：以～其困。</li>
	<li>特指&ldquo;江苏省&rdquo;、&ldquo;苏州市&rdquo;：～剧。～绣（苏州的刺绣）。</li>
	<li>前&ldquo;苏联&rdquo;的简称。中国第二次国内革命战争时期曾把当时的工农民主政权组织称为&ldquo;苏维埃&rdquo;；把当时的根据地称为&ldquo;苏区&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
	<li>见&ldquo;<a href="/hans/噜">噜</a>&rdquo;字&ldquo;噜苏&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>苏</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">s&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>朝向：&ldquo;～刃者死&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">苏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>苏</strong></p>
<p>蘇、蘓 <span class="dicpy">sū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，稣( <span class="dicpy">sū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span> )声。从艸，与植物有关。本义:植物名，即紫苏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[perilla]</span>。唇形科。一年生草本植物。茎方形，叶两面或背面带紫色，夏季开红花或淡红色花。茎、叶、种子入药，嫩叶古用以调味，种子可榨油。如:苏子(紫苏和白苏的种子。可以入药、榨油)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 柴草 <span class="encs">[faggot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">樵苏脂烛，莫非种植之物也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 须状下垂的饰物 <span class="encs">[pendant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">金车玉作轮，踯躅青骢马，流苏金缕鞍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 苏维埃的简称 <span class="encs">[short for Soviet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苏区</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 江苏省的简称 <span class="encs">[short for Jiangsu province]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苏剧</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 苏州市的简称 <span class="encs">[short for Suzhou city]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:苏杭(苏州和杭州的并称);苏裱(苏州裱字画的技艺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 原苏联国名 <span class="encs">[short for Soviet Union]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:中苏关系</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>苏</strong></p>
<p>蘇、甦、穌 <span class="dicpy">sū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 更生 <span class="encs">[revive]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蘇</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“苏”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/蘇">蘇</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              蘇·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=22" target="_blank">22</a>　·部外筆画：16</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8607.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】素姑切【集韻】【正韻】孫租切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音酥。【說文】桂荏也。【本草】紫蘇。【註】蘇，从穌，舒暢也。蘇性舒暢，行氣和血，故謂之蘇。蘇乃荏類，而味辛如桂，故《爾雅》謂之桂荏。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】蘇，芥草也，江淮南楚之閒曰蘇。</p>
<p>又木名。【詩&middot;鄭風】山有扶蘇。【傳】扶蘇，扶胥，小木也。</p>
<p>又流蘇。【西京雜記】昭陽殿壁帶往往爲黃金釭，皆銜五色流蘇。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】【註】蘇，析羽也。</p>
<p>又息也。【書&middot;仲虺之誥】后來其蘇。</p>
<p>又死更生。【戰國策】勃然乃蘇。</p>
<p>又取也。【屈原&middot;離騷】蘇糞壤以充幃兮。【綱目集覽】取草曰蘇。</p>
<p>又【韻會】蘇蘇，氣索貌。【易&middot;震卦】震蘇蘇。【註】恐懼不安之貌。【王註】躁動貌。</p>
<p>又臺名。【吳語】高高下下，以罷民于姑蘇。【註】姑蘇，臺也。</p>
<p>又亭名。【後漢&middot;郡國志】襄國有蘇人亭。</p>
<p>又國名。【魏志&middot;東夷傳】諸國各有別色，名之爲蘇塗。</p>
<p>又姓。【書&middot;立政】司<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨥</span>蘇公。【傳】忿生爲武王司<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨥</span>，封蘇國。</p>
<p>又【集韻】山於切，音疋。詩扶蘇之蘇，徐邈讀疋。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】蘇故切，音傃。【荀子&middot;議兵篇】蘇刃者死。【註】蘇讀作傃，謂相向格鬭者。</p>
