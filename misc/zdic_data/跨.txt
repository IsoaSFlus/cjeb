                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>跨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kuà  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuà.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄚˋ</span></p><ol><li>抬腿向前或向旁移动越过，迈过：～进。～入。～度。</li><li>骑，两脚分在器物的两边坐着或立着：～马。小孩～着门槛。</li><li>超过时间或地区之间的界限：～越。～年度。～国公司。</li><li>附在旁边：～院。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">胯</span>”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">跨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>跨</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，夸声。本义:迈步，越过)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[step;stride]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跨，渡也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓大其两股间，以有所越也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不跨其国。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">康王</span>跨之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十三年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:跨进大门;跨过小沟;跨蹑(跨踏;跨步);跨巽(跨到或达到<span class="yhcs">“巽”</span>的方位);跨火(跨过火堆。为一种民俗);跨踪(跨越)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 超过 <span class="encs">[go beyond]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃览</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>制，跨 <span class="diczx2">周</span>法。—— <span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:跨灶(比喻儿子胜过父亲);跨俗(超越世俗);跨年(从旧年底到新年初的时间)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 骑 <span class="encs">[bestride]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:跨战马去草原;跨鹤(乘鹤。比喻成仙);跨青牛(相传老子骑青牛出函谷关仙去)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 横于上 <span class="encs">[span]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跨谷弥阜。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张衡·西京赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:跨街楼(联结街道两旁楼房而横跨在街道上空的楼。又称过街楼);跨所(跨院，正院旁边的院子);跨虹(喻指拱形桥)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 挎 <span class="encs">[carry on the arm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">智深</span>到座前领了法贴，辞了长老，背上包裹，跨了戒刀禅杖。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:跨菜篮子;跨刀(挎着腰刀)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 占据 <span class="encs">[occupy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跨州连郡者不可胜数。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">若跨有荆益。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 又如:跨蹈(占有);跨据(据有);跨有(据有)</p><p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 兼有 <span class="encs">[have
