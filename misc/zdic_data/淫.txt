                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>淫</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&iacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>过多，过甚：～辞。～雨。～威。</li>
	<li>渐浸，浸渍：浸～。</li>
	<li>迷惑：富贵不能～。</li>
	<li>在男女关系上态度或行为不正当：奸～。～荡。～乱。～秽。～猥。～书。</li>
	<li>放纵，沉溺：～湎（沉溺于酒）。骄奢～逸。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">淫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>淫</strong></p>
<p>滛 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，侌( <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:浸淫;浸渍)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[soak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淫，侵淫随理也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>注:&ldquo;随其脉理而浸渍也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善防者水淫之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;匠人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女娲积芦苇以止淫水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;览冥》</span>。注:&ldquo;平地出水为淫水。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 涂染 <span class="encs">[paint]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谏帛，以栏为灰，渥淳其帛，实诸泽器，淫之以蜃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;淫，薄粉之，令帛白&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:淫溢(逐渐浸染)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指通奸; 奸淫 <span class="encs">[have illicit sexual relations]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">男女不淫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;小匡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:淫奔(淫乱放荡;私跟男子交往);淫垢(淫媾。奸污);淫污(奸污)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 放纵;恣肆 <span class="encs">[let sb.have his own way;allow to run wild]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其无淫于观。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;无逸》</span>。疏:</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>云:&ldquo;淫，放恣也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 引申为骄纵。如:淫汰(骄纵奢侈);淫行(纵欲放荡的行为);淫放(纵欲放荡);淫暴(放纵暴戾);淫纵(纵欲淫乱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 贪欲;贪心 <span class="encs">[be greedy for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾闻国之昏，不由声色，必由奸利好乐声色者淫也，贪奸者惑也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《说苑&middot;反质》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:淫心(贪心);淫思(淫欲之念)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 惑乱，沉湎;沉浸 <span class="encs">[indulge in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">富贵不能淫，贫贱不能移。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:淫溺(迷恋沉溺。多指酒色。耽于积习;沉迷于癖好)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 僭越 <span class="encs">[exceed]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:淫名(超越本分的称号);淫黩(超越分际而亵读轻慢)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 侵淫;侵犯 <span class="encs">[invade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">足於性者，天损不能入;贞於期者，时累不能淫。</span><span class="diczx2">李善</span>注:&ldquo;淫，犹侵也&rdquo;。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;陆机》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 通&ldquo;游&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span>)。遨游 <span class="encs">[travel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不淫意于法之外。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;明法》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孙星衍</span>云:&ldquo;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;有度》</span>篇淫作游。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">归来兮，不可以久淫些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">洪兴祖</span>补注:&ldquo;淫，游也。&rdquo;</p>

<p>◎ <strong>淫</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 过度，无节制;滥。引申为过失 <span class="encs">[excessive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罔淫于乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大禹谟》</span>。传:&ldquo;淫，过也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不淫其色。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;关雎序》</span>。疏:&ldquo;淫者，过也，过其度量谓之为淫。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骄奢淫泆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公三年》</span>。疏:&ldquo;淫，谓耆欲过度。&rdquo;</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 邪恶，奸邪 <span class="encs">[evil]</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">浸漬。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;水部》：「<b>淫</b>，浸<b>淫</b>隨理也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;劉熙《釋名&middot;釋言語》：「<b>淫</b>，浸也。浸<b>淫</b>旁入之言也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;匠人》：「善防者水<b>淫</b>之。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;鄭玄&middot;注：「謂水淤泥土留著，助之為厚。」</span></p></li>

<p>又放也。【禮&middot;哀公問】淫德不倦。</p>
<p>又貪也。【禮&middot;樂記】聲淫及商。</p>
<p>又溢也，過也。【書&middot;大禹謨】罔淫于樂。</p>
<p>又甚也。【列子&middot;黃帝篇】黃帝曰：朕之過淫矣。</p>
<p>又大也。【詩&middot;周頌】旣有淫威。</p>
<p>又僭也。【吳語】掩王東海，以淫名聞於天子。</p>
<p>又久也。【晉語】底著滯淫。【註】滯廢淫久也。</p>
<p>又邪也。【禮&middot;王制】志淫好僻。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】毋淫視。【疏】謂流移也。</p>
<p>又【小爾雅】男女不以禮交，謂之淫。</p>
<p>又歲星失次亦曰淫。【左傳&middot;襄二十八年】歲在星紀，而淫于元枵。</p>
<p>又淫淫，去遠貌。【揚雄&middot;羽獵賦】淫淫與與。【註】往來之貌。</p>
<p>又水名。【淮南子&middot;覽冥訓】女媧氏積蘆灰以止淫水。【註】平地出水爲淫。</p>
<p>又泉名。【王子年&middot;拾遺記】日南之南，有淫泉之浦。</p>
<p>又樹名。【採蘭雜志】遜頓國有淫樹。</p>
<p>又魚名。【淮南子&middot;釋山訓】瓠巴鼓瑟，而淫魚出聽。</p>
<p>又【集韻】以贍切，音豔。巴東有淫預堆。【古歌】淫預大如馬。今作灩澦。</p>
<p>又讀作涅。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】㡛氏湅帛，淫之以蜃。【註】淫當爲涅。</p>
<p>又讀作瑤。【山海經】爰有淫水，其淸洛洛。【註】淫音遙，與瑤同。</p>
