						<p>● <strong>英</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄥ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>花：落～缤纷。</li>	<li>才能出众，才能出众的人：～俊。群～荟萃。～才。～雄。～烈。</li>	<li>精华，事物最精粹的部分：精～。～华。含～咀华。</li>	<li>用羽毛做的矛饰：二矛重（<span class="dicpy">chǒng </span>）～。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/瑛">瑛</a>&rdquo;，似玉的美石。</li>	<li>指&ldquo;英国&rdquo;：～文。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">英</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>英</strong> <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，央声。本义:花)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[flower]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">英，草荣而不实者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有女同行，颜如舜英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;有女同车》</span>。传:&ldquo;华也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秋菊之落英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《离骚&middot;夕餐》</span>。注:&ldquo;华也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗣若英于西冥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">谢庄</span><span class="smcs">《月赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;若木之英也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毋夭英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;禁藏》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓草木之初生也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">落英缤纷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:落英(花落);英华(言花木之美)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 矛上的羽饰 <span class="encs">[spear&rsquo;s feather]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二矛重英，河上乎翱翔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;清人》</span>。传:&ldquo;矛有英饰也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三英粲兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;羔裘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 杰出的人 <span class="encs">[hero]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">才能过人曰英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">尧</span>、 <span class="diczx2">舜</span>者，天下之英也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">智过万人者谓之英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泰族》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德过千人曰英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;辨名记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与三代之英。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span>。注:&ldquo;俊选之尤者。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:英耆(年老有德有智的人);英人(智慧和才能超群的人);英士(才智或武艺出众之士);英少(才华出众的青少年);英台(才能杰出的台阁官员);英材(英才。具有出众才智的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 精华 <span class="encs">[essence;the best part]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">含英咀华。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《进学解》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/82F1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>於驚切，音瑛。【爾雅&middot;釋木】華而不實者謂之英。【詩&middot;鄭風】有女同車，顏如舜英。【註】英，猶華也。</p><p>又木名。【爾雅&middot;釋木】權，黃英。</p><p>又葉亦謂之英。【屈原&middot;離騷】夕餐秋菊之落英。【西溪叢語】《宋書&middot;符瑞志》沈約云：英，葉也。《離騷》餐落英，言食秋菊之葉也。據《玉函方》甘菊三月上寅採葉，名曰玉英，是英亦謂之葉也。</p><p>又【禮&middot;禮運】大道之行也，與三代之英。【註】倍<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨕖</span>曰俊，千人曰英。【孟子】得天下英才而敎育之。</p><p>又【博雅】美也。【晉書&middot;荀闓傳】京師語曰：洛中英英荀道明。</p><p>又【爾雅&middot;釋山】再成，英。【疏】山形兩重者名英，今南郡英山縣，蓋取此名也。</p><p>又瓊英，美石似玉者。【詩&middot;齊風】尚之以瓊英乎而。【水經注】《孫盛&middot;魏春秋》曰：文帝愈增崇宮殿，取白石英及紫石英。</p><p>又英英，雲貌。【詩&middot;小雅】英英白雲，露彼菅茅。</p><p>又【詩&middot;小雅】二矛重英。【註】以羽飾矛。【魯頌】朱英綠縢。【疏】蓋絲纒而朱染之。</p><p>又樂名。【前漢&middot;禮樂志】帝嚳作《五英》。英，華茂也。</p><p>又國名。【史記&middot;陳杞世家】臯陶之後，或封英、六。【楚世家註】英國在淮南，蓋蓼國也。</p><p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】英氏，以國爲氏，漢有九江王英布。</p><p>又於䖍切，音閼。【班固&middot;西京賦】翡翠、火齊。流耀含英。縣黎、垂棘，夜光在焉。</p><p>又【唐韻古音】于良切，讀央。【屈原&middot;九歌】浴蘭湯兮沐芳，華采衣兮若英。【毛詩&middot;本音】舜英、重英、瓊英、美如英，俱叶韻讀央。【管子&middot;地員篇】五色雜英，各有異章。【集韻】英，稻初生未移者，亦讀央。</p><p>又於慶切，音映。飾也。</p>
