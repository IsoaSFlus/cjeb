						<p>● <strong>榜</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄤˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>张贴出来的文告或名单：～帖（官府的公告）。红～。张～。光荣～。～文。发～。～眼（科举时代称殿试考取一甲第二名的人）。～书（原指写在宫阙门额上的大字，后泛指招牌一类的大型字）。</li></ol><p>● <strong>榜</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">b&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄤˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>摇船的用具：～人（船工）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">榜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>榜</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 木片 <span class="encs">[wood fiber]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载米三十万斛，钱布数十舫，竖榜为城。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 匾额 <span class="encs">[horizontal inscribed board]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">魏</span>时， <span class="diczx2">凌云殿</span>榜未题而匠者误钉之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;王献之传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:榜题(匾额题字);榜字(写在匾额上的大字)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 告示应试取录的名单 <span class="encs">[published list of successful canditates]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既而试榜出，时所推誉洞房花烛夜，金榜挂名时。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汪洙</span><span class="smcs">《喜》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆不在榜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史&middot;选举志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:放榜，榜上无名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 公开张贴的名单、文书、告示 <span class="encs">[notice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">鲁达</span>看见众人看榜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:光荣榜;榜示(文告，告示)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 屋栋 <span class="encs">[ridgepole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屋栋谓之甍。注:&ldquo;今谓之榑，亦谓之檩，又谓之榜。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《营造法式》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>榜</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 公开张贴文书、告示 <span class="encs">[put up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昨日里胥方到门，手持尺牒榜乡村。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《新乐府&middot;杜陵叟》</span></p>
