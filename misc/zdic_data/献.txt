                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>献</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">獻</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>恭敬庄严地送给：奉～。贡～。捐～。～身。～礼。借花～佛。</li>
	<li>表现出来：～技。～艺。～媚。～丑。</li>
	<li>古代指贤者，特指熟悉掌故的人：文～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> offer, present; show, display</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> offerieren  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> offrir,dédier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">献</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>献</strong></p>
<p>獻 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从犬，鬳( <span class="dicpy">y&agrave;n</span>)声。&ldquo;犬&rdquo;代表进献之物。本义:献祭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sacrifice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献，宗庙犬，名羹献。犬肥者以献之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犬曰羹献。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。按，礼凡荐腥谓之献。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献禽以祭祖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司马》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献羔祭韭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荐晡醢，三献。(三献:陈祭品三次献酒。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:献尸(向代表死人的尸者进献祭品);献帛(祭祀礼仪之一，献上巾帕);献爵(祭祀礼仪之一，献酒);献台(祭台)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 下对上、卑对尊的进献 <span class="encs">[offer;present]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献，进也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春献鳖蜃，秋献龟鱼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;鳖人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有献于公。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">称献于公。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">称远近而等贡献。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献俘，授馘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谨使臣</span><span class="diczx2">良</span>奉白璧一双，再拜献大王足下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请献十金。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时而献焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献之太常。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子&middot;千里马篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献诸朝。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:献馘(杀敌立功);献新(进奉应时的新鲜食品);献曲(进献乐曲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 呈现;现露 <span class="encs">[show oneself]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽惠心妍状，愈献丑焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;皇后纪上》</span></span></p>
