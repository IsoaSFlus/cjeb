						<p>● <strong>瞬</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>眨眼，眼球一动：&ldquo;尔先学不～，而后可言射矣&rdquo;。一～即逝。</li>	<li>极短的时间：转～。～间。～时。～即。～华（指短暂的时光）。～息。～时速度。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瞬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瞬</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从目，舜声。本义:眨眼)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔先学不瞬，而后可言射矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一瞬(一眨眼工夫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 眼珠转动 <span class="encs">[turn eyeball]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">直视，不能瞬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张机</span><span class="smcs">《金匮要略》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 视，看 <span class="encs">[see;gaze at]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:瞬视(目眨动;注视)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 惊恐。同&ldquo;眴&rdquo; <span class="encs">[be frightened]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">适见豚子食于其死母者，少焉眴若，皆亲之而走。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">陆德明</span>释文:&ldquo;眴若，本亦作&lsquo;瞬&#39;。 <span class="diczx2">司马</span>云:惊貌。&rdquo;</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瞬</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>一眨眼的工夫 <span class="encs">[twinkling]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:瞬华(一眨眼的时光);瞬霎(谓眨眼之间)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中" target="_blank">午集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集中&amp;bs=目" target="_blank">目</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/77AC.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】舒閏切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】輸閏切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音舜。目自動也。【列子&middot;湯問篇】紀昌學射於飛衞，衞曰：爾先學不瞬，而後可言射矣。【宋史&middot;韓世忠傳】目瞬如電。</p><p>又【埤雅】木槿朝生夕隕，一名舜。蓋瞬之義取諸此。【陸機&middot;文賦】觀古今之須臾，撫四海於一瞬。【司馬法嚴位篇】一人之禁，無過瞬息。　【玉篇】與瞚同。【集韻】亦作眴䀢瞤。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">瞚</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“瞬”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/瞚">瞚</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四">卷四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四&amp;swbs=目">目</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/779A.svg">                          <p>開闔目數搖也。从目寅聲。舒問切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗别作瞬，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/779A_4333.svg" /><span class="swbf">(瞚)</span><span class="ywyy">開闔目數搖也。</span><span class="swdz">開闔目，玄應本作目開閉。闔一曰閉也。左傳：臾駢曰：使者目動而言肆，懼我也。目動者，開閉數搖也。吕覽曰：夫死其視萬歲猶一瞚也。莊子：兒子終日視而目不瞚。此皆瞚字本義。凡謂與公羊䀢同者，非也。䀏爲目搖，瞚爲目數搖，皆不必以目使人。惟䀢主以目通指。</span><span class="ywyy">从目寅聲。</span><span class="swdz">舒閏切。按寅聲當在十二部。莊釋文因或作瞬，統音舜耳。</span></p>                          </div>
