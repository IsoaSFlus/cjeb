						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>兼</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>加倍，把两份并在一起：～旬（两个十天）。～程。～并。</li><li>同时涉及或所具有的不只一方面：～而有之。～收并蓄。～顾。～职。～任。～课。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>兼</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，从又(手)，从秝( <span class="dicpy">lì</span>)。字形象一手持两棵庄稼。本义:一手执两禾。引申为同时进行几桩事情或占有几样东西)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同时具有或涉及几种事务或若干方面 <span class="encs">[hold two or more…concurrently]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼，并也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼执之，以进。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹两也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼巾之。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“六豆共巾也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼诸弣。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡射礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“并矢于弣。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可得兼。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丞相兼枢密使。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《<指南录> 后序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼百花之长。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄·种植部》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼筑雕刻。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:兼祧(一子兼作两房的后嗣。祧继承上代);兼资(与通;并有);兼署(官吏除本职外，又兼理其他职务);兼味(指两种以上的菜肴)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 合并，由部分合成整体 <span class="encs">[merge;combine;amalgamate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">约从离衡，兼韩、魏、燕、楚、齐、赵、宋、卫、中山之众。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 并吞;兼并 <span class="encs">[annex (territory) by force;swallow up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其兼天下不难矣。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·亡征》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孺子长矣，而相吾室，欲兼我也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:兼列(犹兼并);兼畜(犹兼并，并吞);兼弱攻昧(吞并弱者攻打昏者)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 倍，加倍 <span class="encs">[double]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王馈兼金一百。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“其价兼倍于常者。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">利不兼，赏不倍。——<span class="smcs">《马王堆汉墓帛书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">读书倍文，功力兼人。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《韩滂墓志铭》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼旬愈。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:兼只(几只，数只);兼日(连日，不止一日);兼旬(二十天);兼通(通晓两门或两门以上的学问或技艺)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 重复;累积 <span
