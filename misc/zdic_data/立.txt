                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>立</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>站，引申为竖起来：～正。～柜。～足（ａ．站得往脚；ｂ．处于某种立场）。～场。屹～。顶天～地。</li><li>做出，定出：建～。设～。树～。～意。～此存照。</li><li>存在，生存：自～。独～。势不两～。</li><li>马上，即刻：～时。～刻。～等。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">立</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>立</strong> <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><动、名></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文象一人正面立地之形。本义:笔直的站立)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">立，侸也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以立不易方。——<span class="smcs">《易·恒》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">哙</span>遂入，披帷西乡立。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">持璧却立。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">立有间。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">卖油翁</span>释担而立。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田来》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或立或卧。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:挺立(直立);坐立不安;把电线杆立起来;肃立(恭敬庄严地站着);立托(依托);立容(站立时的仪态容表);立戟(卫士持戟侍立守卫);立谈(停立交谈)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 耸立;树立;建立;竖起 <span class="encs">[erect;set upright]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大石侧立千尺。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃立宗庙于</span><span class="diczx2">薛</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且立石于基墓门。——</span><span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且人患志之不立，何忧令名之不彰雅。——<span class="smcs">《世说新》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:立事(建功立业);立方(立德);立言(指著书立说);立业(建树功烽);立勋(建立功勋);立石(树立碑石);立权(树立权威);立眉嗔目(竖眉瞪眼);立德(树立德业);立德立言(儒家认为，为了匡时济世，应树立圣人之德，宣谕圣人之言);立碑垂成(刻文于石碑留作规戒)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 设置;设立 <span class="encs">[set up;put up;establish;found]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故立君。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·荡兵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">商君</span>佐之，内立法度。—— <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:立元(建立年号);立仗(设立仪仗);立本(建立根本;确立根基);立表下漏(设置日晷、漏刻以计时);立事(设置治事小臣);立制(建立制度)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 制定;订立 <span class="encs">[lay down;draw up;conclude]</span>。如:立文书;立军令状;立券(订立契约);立格(订出标准)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 确定;决定 <span class="encs">[define;decide;determine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人之立志。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">澎端叔</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人患志之不立。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·自新》</span></span></p><p><span
