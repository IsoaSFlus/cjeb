						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>黛</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄞˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>青黑色的颜料，古代女子用来画眉：～色。～眉。粉～。～绿。～蓝。～紫。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> blacken eyebrows; black</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schwarze Augenbrauenfarbe, schwarze Augenbrauentusche  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fard noir pour les sourcils</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">黛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>黛</strong></p>
<p>黱 <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从黑，代声。本义:青黑色的颜料。古代女子用以画眉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a dark pigment used by women in ancient times to paint their eyebrows]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黱，画眉也。从黑，朕声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。锴本作画眉墨。字亦作黛。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">染青石谓之点黛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黛，代也。灭眉毛去之，以此画代其处也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六宫粉黛无颜色。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《长恨歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西方有石名黛，可代画眉之墨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:黛岑(青黑如黛的山峰);黛螺(绘画或画眉所使用的青黑色颜料)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 女子眉毛的代称 <span class="encs">[women&#39;s eyebrows]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黛眉(眉毛);黛笔(古代妇女画眉之笔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 美女 <span class="encs">[beauty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黛娥(比喻美女);黛绿年华(比喻女子的青春时代)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>黛</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>青黑色 <span class="encs">[black]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">霜皮溜雨四十围，黛色参天二千尺。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《古柏行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两岸的青山&hellip;都装成了深黛的颜色。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《故乡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:黛烟(青黑色的烟);黛青(青黑如眉黛);黛色(青黑色)</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">青黑色的顏料，古時女子用以畫眉。</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辭&middot;屈原&middot;大招》：「粉白黛黑，施芳澤只。」</span></p></li>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">婦女的眉。</span><span class="gc_yy">南朝梁&middot;元帝〈代舊姬有怨〉詩：「怨黛舒還斂，啼紅拭復垂。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">美女。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈長恨歌〉：「回眸一笑百媚生，六宮粉黛無顏色。」</span></p></li>
