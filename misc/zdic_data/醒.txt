						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>醒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xǐng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄥˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>睡眠状态结束或尚未入睡：如梦方～。</li>
	<li>酒醉、麻醉或昏迷后神志恢复正常状态：～酒。</li>
	<li>泛指头脑由迷糊而清楚：～悟。觉（<span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span>）～。清～。提～。猛～。</li>
	<li>明显、清楚：～目。～眼。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">醒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>醒</strong> <span class="dicpy">xǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉，星声。酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)与酒有关。本义:酒醒)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sober up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">醒能述以文。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今宵酒醒何处。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:醒酒石(可以醒酒的石头);醒酒汤(可以醒酒的羹汤);醒药(醒酒的药);醒狂(不醉而狂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 醒悟 <span class="encs">[be clear in mind]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:猛醒(猛然觉悟;忽然明白过来);醒心(神志清醒;清醒的认识);醒素(清醒);梦醒</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 睡眠状态结束或尚未睡着 <span class="encs">[awake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既而儿醒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫亦醒。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大儿醒。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大儿初醒声。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:醒睡(睡眠中保持警觉，不沉睡);醒困(消除困倦);醒松(惺松。刚苏醒的样子);醒觉(由睡眠或昏迷而苏醒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 动植物的恢复生机或由蛰伏而活动 <span class="encs">[recover]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:醒水(秧苗插下后复苏存活);醒绽(方言。树木返青发芽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 方言。知道，懂得 <span class="encs">[know;understand]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:醒得(懂得，知道);醒事(懂事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 消除<span class="encs">[eliminate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">等她气醒了，再回家来。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">周立波</span><span class="smcs">《山乡巨变》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 用同&ldquo;擤&rdquo; <span class="encs">[blow (one&#39;s nose)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摸出手帕醒鼻涕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">巴金</span><span class="smcs">《电》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:醒鼻涕(擤鼻涕)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>醒</strong> <span class="dicpy">xǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 明白;明显 <span class="encs">[clear;obvious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">童奶奶</span>道:&ldquo;这话我不醒的。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醒世姻缘传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:醒醒白白(明明白白);醒豁(清醒，明白);醒眼</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用同&ldquo;腥&rdquo; <span class="encs">[fishy odor]</span>。</p>
