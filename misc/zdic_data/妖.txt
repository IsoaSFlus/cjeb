                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">妖</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>妖</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄠˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>迷信的人指异于常态而害人的东西：～魔鬼怪。～精（①妖怪；②喻姿色迷人的女子。&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">精</span>&rdquo;均读轻声）。～氛。</li>	<li>装束或神态不正派：～里～气。～态。</li>	<li>媚，艳丽：～女。～娆。～艳。～冶。～妍。</li>	<li>邪恶而迷惑人的：～言。～人。～术。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">妖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>妖</strong> <span class="dicpy">yāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，夭声。)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gorgeous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妖，妍也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妖夫曳衔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;天问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妖冶闲都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传》</span>。注:&ldquo;妖冶，美好也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妖姬静女。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">侯方域</span><span class="smcs">《壮梅堂文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妖妍(艳丽);妖姿(艳丽的姿容);妖异(美艳奇异);妖丽(艳丽);妖靡(艳丽华美);妖蛊(艳丽);妖姬(美女);妖玩(美女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 怪诞，怪异 <span class="encs">[weird]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:妖蜃(传说中的海中怪物，能吐气成海市蜃楼);妖幻(怪异的幻术);妖邪(妖异怪诞)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 妖媚。淫邪，不正 <span class="encs">[seductively charming]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">梁冀</span>妻 <span class="diczx2">孙寿</span>，色美而善为妖态，作愁眉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妖红(妇女勾引男子并与其私通);妖妙(妖娆美好);妖惑(惑人的媚态);妖韶(妖绕美好);妖娇(娇美);妖服(妖冶的服装)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 不祥 <span class="encs">[ominous]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:妖氛(不祥的云气);妖星(古代指预兆灾祸的星，如彗星);妖祥(凶兆和吉兆);妖服(不祥之服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 邪恶;品质恶劣 <span class="encs">[evil]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:妖童(邪恶的小子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 装束奇特、作风不正派&mdash;&mdash;多指女性 <span class="encs">[bewitching;coquettish]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:妖妖调调(妖里妖气;俏艳而轻佻);妖里妖气</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>妖</strong> <span class="dicpy">yāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古称一切反常怪异的事物或现象 <span class="encs">[abnormal thing or phenomena]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人无衅焉，妖不自作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心和而出，且为声为名，为妖为孽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;人间世》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妖变(指反常、怪异的现象和事物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 迷信传说中称害人的怪物 <span class="encs">[goblin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">菩萨又怕那妖无礼，却把一个箍儿，丢在那妖头上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 借指美女 <span class="encs">[beauty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:妖玩</span></p>
