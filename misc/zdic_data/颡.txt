                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>颡</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">顙</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄤˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 额，脑门儿：稽～（古代一种跪拜礼，屈膝下拜，以额触地。居丧、请罪、投降时行之）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> the forehead; to kowtow</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Stirn  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> front</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>颡</strong></p><p>顙 <span class="dicpy">sǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 额头 <span class="encs">[forehead]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颡，额也。从页，桑声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中夏谓之额，东齐谓之颡。——<span class="smcs">《方計》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可使过颡。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">河目龙颡。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语·困誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">靥辅在颊则好，在颡则丑。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘安</span><span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:颡骨(额骨);颡泚(额上汗淋淋。借指心中惶惶不安);颡汗(额头上冒汗)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 头 <span class="encs">[head]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">修鳞脱远枝，巨颡拆老拳。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《义鹘》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 喉咙;嗓子 <span class="encs">[throat]</span>。如:颡根(喉的后部);颡子眼(喉咙眼);颡根轴子(即人体咽喉与食管部位);颡子(喉咙)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>颡</strong></p><p>顙 <span class="dicpy">sǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="yhcs">“稽颡”</span>的省称，即叩头、磕头 <span class="encs">[kowtow;kotow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拜稽颡。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“头触地。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">再拜颡。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
