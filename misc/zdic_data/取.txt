                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">取</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>取</strong> <span class="dicpy">qǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从又，从耳。甲骨文字形。左边是耳朵，右边是手(又)，合起来表示用手割耳朵。古代作战，以割取敌人尸体首级或左耳以计数献功。本义:<span class="encs">[捕获到野兽或战俘时]</span>割下左耳)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cut off left-ear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取，捕取也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大兽公之，小禽私之，获者取左耳。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">且今之勍者，皆吾敌也，虽及胡者，获则取之，何有于二毛?——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取</span><span class="diczx2">吴元济</span>。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 斩获敌人的首级为取 <span class="encs">[behead]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恨君不取</span><span class="diczx2">契丹</span>首，金甲牙旗归故乡。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《阳关词》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拿 <span class="encs">[take;fetch;get]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取数斗归。——</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">对面取人物。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取一葫芦。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取器械。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《洹词·记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:从架上取书;从巢里取蛋;取受(拿取和收受);取则(取作准则，规范或榜样);取将(拿取)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“娶”</span>。娶妻 <span class="encs">[marry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取妻如之何?——<span class="smcs">《诗·齐风·南山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君取于</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>，为同姓。——<span class="smcs">《论语·述而》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勿用取女。——<span class="smcs">《易·蒙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可以冠子取妻。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·杂记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">昭公</span>取于 <span class="diczx2">吴</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十二年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终老不复取。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">还必相迎取。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我近来取得一个老小，</span><span class="diczx2">清河县</span>人。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:取女(娶妻);取亲(娶妻);取室(娶妻)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 选取;选拔 <span class="encs">[adopt;choose;select]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">深思而慎取。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人取之。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span
