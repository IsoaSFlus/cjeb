                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>缁</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">緇</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 黑色：～衣。～帷。～素（a.黑和白；b.借指僧人和俗人，因僧尼穿黑衣，而白衣是平常人穿的衣服）。～黄（指和尚与道士）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缁</strong></p><p>緇 <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，甾( <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> )声。糸，细丝，可以染上各种颜色。本义:帛黑色) 同本义 <span class="encs">[silks black]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缁，帛黑色也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缁衣之宜兮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·缁衣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天雨，解素衣，衣缁衣而反。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说林下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">素衣虽成缁，不为京路尘。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《自小云顶上云顶寺》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:缁布(黑色的布帛);缁衣(本为古代用黑色的布帛做的朝服，后借以泛称黑色衣服;僧尼所穿青黑色的衣服);缁林(僧人聚集之处。缁，指僧人穿的黑色衣服)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>缁</strong></p><p>緇 <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 僧侣 <span class="encs">[monks and priests]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">游食缁黄，往来寄宿。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:缁锡(僧人);缁子(和尚);缁林(僧界，僧众);缁徒(僧侣)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 黑色僧服。亦指僧侣或作僧侣 <span class="encs">[robes of monks]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自此更姓改名，削发披缁，去了。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:缁服(僧服);缁衲(僧衣。借指僧侣);缁锡(僧衣锡杖。僧人所用)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7DC7.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】側持切【集韻】莊持切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音菑。【說文】帛黑色。【博雅】緇謂之皁。【釋名】緇，滓也。泥緇黑色者曰滓，此色然也。【詩·鄭風】緇衣之宜兮。【傳】緇，黑色。【周禮·冬官考工記·鍾氏】七入爲緇。【註】緅又復再染以黑，乃成緇矣。</p><p>又【正韻】旨而切，音枝。義同。</p><p>又【集韻】側几切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23408.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣐈</span>。【韻會】壯仕切，音滓。【前漢·班固敘傳】涅而不緇。【註】師古曰：合韻音上聲。</p><p>又【集韻】側吏切，音胾義同。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7DC7.svg">                          <p>帛黑色。从糸甾聲。側持切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">帛黑色也。</span>黑者、北方色也。火所熏之色也。考工記。三入爲纁。五入爲緅。七入爲緇。鄭注曰。玄色者、在緅緇之閒。其六入者與。<span class="jieshi">从糸。？聲。</span>側持切。一部。按玉藻大夫佩水蒼玉而純組綬注。純當爲緇。古文緇字、或作糸旁才。又周禮媒氏純帛注。純實緇字也。古緇以才爲聲。祭統王后蠶於北郊以供純服注。純以見繒色。論語今也純。鄭讀爲緇。鄭意今之䊷字、俗譌爲純耳。然則許書當爲䊷篆。解云古文緇。从糸、才聲。而缺者、豈從今書不從故書之例與。                          </div>
