						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>吼</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hǒu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǒu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄡˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>兽大声叫：～叫。狮～。</li>    <li>人在激怒时的呼喊：大～。</li>    <li>（风、汽笛、大炮等）发出巨大的声响：～鸣。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>吼</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，孔声。本义:<span class="encs">[野兽]</span>大声叫)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[roar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吼，声也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:虎吼;狮子吼;大炮一再地吼</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 人因愤怒或情绪激动而大声呼喊 <span class="encs">[shout;cry out]</span>。如:吼咤(大声怒喝);吼怒(怒极而大声咆哮);吼喊(大声叫喊);吼号(大声哀号)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 风、雷等发出的巨响 <span class="encs">[roar;thunder]</span>。如:吼啸(呼啸;发出巨响);吼沫(发出巨响的激流浪花);吼雷(打响雷，雷鸣)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 〈方〉∶叫，呼唤 <span class="encs">[人]</span> <span class="encs">[call]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">意马</span>，吼你大大去。——<span class="smcs">《秧歌剧选》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>吼</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 虹 <span class="encs">[rainbow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虹谓之吼。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李翊</span><span class="smcs">《俗呼小录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 哮喘病 <span class="encs">[asthma]</span>。如:吼儿病;吼疾</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/吼叫">吼叫</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/吼鸣">吼鸣</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/吼三喝四">吼三喝四</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">吼</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>吼</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄡˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hǒu <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2742.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">猛兽大声的叫。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「河东狮<b>吼</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《晋书·卷四三·王戎传》：「猛兽在槛中，虓<b>吼</b>震地。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·杜甫〈复阴〉诗：「江涛簸岸黄沙走，云雪埋山苍兕<b>吼</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">因愤怒或情绪激动而大声叫喊。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「大<b>吼</b>大叫」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《南史·卷八○·贼臣传·侯景传》：「丑徒数万，同共吹唇，唱<b>吼</b>而上。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">自然界发出巨响。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「北风怒<b>吼</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·岑参〈走马川行奉送出师西征〉诗：「轮台九月风夜<b>吼</b>，一川碎石大如斗。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·王庭珪〈和周秀实田家行〉：「大臣鼻息如雷<b>吼</b>，玉帐无忧方熟眠。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/543C.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】呼后切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】許后切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音㸸。【玉篇】牛鳴也。【增韻】虓聲。【後漢·童恢傳】一虎低頭閉目，狀如震懼，卽時殺之，其一視，恢鳴吼踊躍自奮。【梵書·大智論】辟如獅子，百獸之王，爲小蟲吼，則爲衆所笑。【玉篇】亦作吽。【廣韻】亦作呴。</p><p>又【廣韻】呼<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20F07.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠼇</span>切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】許<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蔲。【廣韻】聲也。</p><p>又【集韻】【類篇】厚怒聲。本作㖃。○按《玉篇》《廣韻》吼、㖃分載，音義各別。《集韻》《類篇》非。</p><p>
