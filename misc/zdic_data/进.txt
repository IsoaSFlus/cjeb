                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">进</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>进</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">進</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jìn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>向前或向上移动、发展，与“<span class="diczx3">退</span>”相对：前～。上～。推～。跃～。～退。～取。～击。～驻。～行（<span class="dicpy">xíng </span>）。～而。</li><li>入，往里去：～见。～谒。～谗。</li><li>吃，喝：～食。～餐。滴水未～。</li><li>收入或买入：～账。～货。日～斗金。</li><li>奉上，呈上：～言。～奉。～献。</li><li>旧式房院层次，这所宅子是两～院。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">进</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>进</strong></p><p>進 <span class="dicpy">jìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面是<span class="yhcs">“隹”</span>，象小鸟形，下面是<span class="yhcs">“止”</span>(趾)。鸟脚只能前进不能后退，故用以表示前进。本义:前进，与<span class="yhcs">“退”</span>相对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[advance;move forward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">进，登也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巽为进退。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">进退维谷。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑葇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徒衔枚而进。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司马》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子三揖而进。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·表记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">进则引之，退则策之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说右下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">进兵击</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>军。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以次俱进。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水陆并进。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贼环而进。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">猱进鸷击。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:迈进(大踏步地前进);奋进(奋勇前进);进不来出不去(比喻进退两难或处境尴尬);进旅退旅(进退一致，整齐合一);进退中绳(举动合乎规矩)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 超过 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣之所好者，道也，进乎技矣。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·养生主》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 入，走入 <span class="encs">[一个地方]</span>。跟<span class="yhcs">“出”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[enter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">府尹叫进后堂来说。——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:进门;进口;进屋;进洣(上学);进了学(上学读书);进镇(进驻镇守);进壁(进驻并筑工事固守);进营(进驻)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 奉献 <span class="encs">[offer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">群臣进谏。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">进尽忠言。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">煮芋以进。——</span><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9032.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2865F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨙟</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/285C3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨗃</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>卽刃切，音晉。【說文】登也。【玉篇】升也。【廣韻】前也。【禮·曲禮】遭先生于道，趨而進。【表記】君子三揖而進。【註】人之相見，三揖三讓，以升賔階。【書·盤庚】乃登進厥民。【疏】延之使前而告之也。</p><p>又【正韻】薦也。【禮·儒行】推賢而進達之。</p><p>又【正韻】效也。【禮·樂記】禮減而進，以進爲文。【註】自勉强也。【易·乾卦】君子進德修業，欲及時也。</p><p>又近也。【禮·檀弓】兄弟之子猶子也。蓋引而進之也。</p><p>又進士。【禮·王制】大樂正論造士之秀者，以告于王，而升諸司馬，曰進士。【註】進士，可進而受爵祿也。</p><p>又特進。【後漢·和帝紀】賜諸侯王公將軍特進。【註】諸侯功德優盛，朝廷所敬異者賜位特進。</p><p>又與餕同。【禮·祭統】百官進徹之。【註】進同餕。</p><p>又【字彙補】與盡同。【列子·黃帝篇】竭聰明，進智力。</p><p>又通作薦。【列子·湯問篇】穆王薦之，张註薦當作進。</p><p>又【集韻】徐刃切【正韻】齊進切。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>與贐同。會禮也。【前漢·高帝紀】蕭何爲主吏主進。【註】主賦斂禮錢也。師古曰：進本作贐，聲轉爲進。</p><p>又叶資辛切，音津。【揚子·太<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>經】陽引而進，物出溱溱。</p><p>
