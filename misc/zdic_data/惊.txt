                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>惊</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">驚</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>骡马等因为害怕而狂奔起来不受控制：～车。～群。马～车败。</li>
	<li>害怕，精神受了突然刺激而紧张不安：～恐。～骇。～愕。～惶。～诧。～遽。～厥。～悟。～心动魄。～惶失措。～世骇俗（言行出奇，使世人惊恐）。</li>
	<li>震动：～动。～扰。～堂木。打草～蛇。</li>
	<li>出人意料的：～喜。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> frighten, surprise, startle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ängstigen, beunruhigen  (V)​,sich erschrecken, bestürzt sein, stören  (V)​,überrascht sein  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> être effrayé,prendre,surprendre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">惊</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>惊</strong></p>
<p>驚 <span class="dicpy">jīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，敬声。本义:马受惊)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[the horse be frightened]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驚，马骇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">襄子</span>至桥而马惊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策一》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:惊嘶(马受惊而嘶叫);惊尘(车马疾驶所扬起的尘土);惊镳(惊马)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 惊动;震惊<span class="encs">[alarm;disturb;start;be startled]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宫庭震惊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其生若惊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎大》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">震惊百里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;震卦》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;惊之言警戒也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆惊忙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">月出惊山鸟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《鸟鸣涧》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惊问信之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惊为生人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:惊唬(惊动，吓唬);惊耍(用惊吓的办法开玩笑;耍笑);惊闺(惊闺叶。货郎为招徕顾客而使用的一种手摇响器)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 动 <span class="encs">[move]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">波澜不惊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 惊慌，恐惧 <span class="encs">[alarmed;scared;fear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>惊，自引而起。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻人声亦惊起。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:惊恍(惊慌，害怕);惊怖(惊慌恐怖，惊吓);惊报(灾祸的先兆);惊疑(惊慌疑惑);惊嘬嘬(非常提心、害怕的样子);惊怖惕息(战战兢兢，恐惧不安的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 惊讶;惊奇<span class="encs">[surprised;amazed]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:惊厄(惊险;惊困);惊诧(惊讶诧异);惊乍(惊恐不安);惊悼(惊异痛伤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 纷乱 <span class="encs">[helter-skelter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫敢直言，其生若惊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">细尘障路起，惊花乱眼飘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">北周</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《侠客行》</span></p>
