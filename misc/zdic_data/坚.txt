                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>坚</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">堅</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>牢固，结实，硬：～固。～实。～如磐石。</li>	<li>不动摇，不改变：～决。～信。</li>	<li>牢固、结实的东西或阵地：中～。攻～。披～执锐。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hard, strong, firm; resolute</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> fest, hart  (V)​,Jian  (Eig, Fam)​,entschlossen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ferme,dur,fermement,résolument</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坚</strong></p>
<p>堅 <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从臤( <span class="dicpy">qiān</span>)，从土。&ldquo;臤&rdquo;，金文象以手拉&ldquo;臣&rdquo;(屈服的奴隶)。从&ldquo;土&rdquo;，表示土质坚硬。本义:泥土坚硬)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。泛指坚硬;结实 <span class="encs">[hard;solid;firm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坚，刚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坚，坚土也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四为壤五，为坚三。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《九章算术&middot;穿地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铠甲不坚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剑坚，故不可立拔。(剑坚，剑插得紧。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非不坚利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">披坚执锐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">火烧令坚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">砚冰坚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坚瓠(坚厚而实心的葫芦。不可剖以盛物，因喻无用的东西);坚冰(多以喻积过成祸，困难重重);坚顽(极其坚硬);坚确(坚硬);坚滑(坚硬滑溜);坚润(坚硬而润泽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 牢固，坚固，强固有力而不易摧毁 <span class="encs">[firm;solid;secure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坚，强也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坚穷廉直。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;审分》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">誉其盾之坚，物莫能陷也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难势》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坚闭门而不出。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坚巧(牢固而精巧);坚城(坚固不易攻破的城池);坚勚(结实);坚壁(坚固的墙壁或营垒;坚守壁垒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[植物的种子]</span>饱满 <span class="encs">[full]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其谷不坚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;贵信》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坚成(结实;成熟)</span></p>
