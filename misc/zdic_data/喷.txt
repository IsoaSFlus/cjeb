						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>喷</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">噴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄣˉ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 散着射出：～出。～涌。～发。～饭（形容极其可笑）。～薄（形容气势壮盛，激荡喷涌而出，如“一轮红日～～而出”）。～洒。～泉。～壶。井～。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>喷</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">噴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pèn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pèn.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄣˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>香气扑鼻：～香的热馒头。</li>    <li>蔬菜、鱼虾、瓜果等上市正盛的时期：西瓜～儿。</li>    <li>量词，指开花结实或成熟收割的次数：头～棉花。</li></ol>
