                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衅</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>衅</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">釁</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代用牲畜的血涂器物的缝隙：～钟。～鼓。</li>
	<li>缝隙，感情上的裂痕，争端：～隙。～端（争端）。挑～。寻～。</li>
	<li>以香熏身：～浴。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> consecrate sacrificial vessels by smearing blood; rift</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Xin  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bagarre,rixe,dispute,oint de sang (arch.)​,offrir du sang en sacrifice</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>衅</strong></p>
<p>釁 <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从爨省，爨( <span class="dicpy">cu&agrave;n)</span>，灶。从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)，与酒有关，表祭祀。分，表示分布。合起来表杀牲以祭。本义:古代血祭新制的器物。杀牲，用其血涂于器物缝隙中来祭祀)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sacrifice with blood]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">釁，血祭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隋衅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大祝》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓荐血也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大师掌衅祈号祝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;小祝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不以累臣衅鼓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">车甲衅而藏之府库。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将以衅钟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衅鼓旗甲兵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎大》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桓公践位，令衅社塞祷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;小问》</span>。注:&ldquo;杀牲以血浇落于社曰衅社。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缚之，杀以衅鼓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衅宝镇及宝器;衅钟;衅宝刀;衅社(杀牲取血以祭社神);衅鼓(杀人或杀牲以血涂鼓行祭)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 涂 <span class="encs">[smear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">比至，三衅、三浴之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">韦昭</span>注:&ldquo;以香涂身曰衅。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衅尸(古代丧礼以鬯酒涂尸);衅浴(用芳香涂身后沐浴以除秽恶。引申为认真研究，用精取宏);衅鬯(涂尸的香酒)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>衅</strong></p>
<p>釁 <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 缝隙;裂痕 <span class="encs">[crack]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">会</span>闻用师，观衅而动。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏:&ldquo;衅是间隙之名。今人谓瓦裂龟裂皆为衅。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为室三重，户闭，涂衅必周。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;律历志上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衅会(有衅可乘的时机)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 祸患;祸乱 <span class="encs">[calamity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫功全则誉显，业谢则衅生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衅首(祸乱之首);衅逆(叛乱);衅辱(蒙祸受辱);衅累(祸患与事端的牵连);衅祸(祸乱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 罪过 <span class="encs">[crime]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衅门子弟不得居宿卫近侍之官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衅情(罪情，罪恶的用心);衅恶(罪恶);衅稔恶盈(罪大恶极);衅故(罪情，罪行)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 过失 <span class="encs">[fault]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人无衅焉，妖不自作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衅咎(过失，罪过);衅眚(过失);衅过(过失，罪);衅弊(缺失，弊端)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 争端。引起争执的事端 <span class="encs">[dispute;quarrel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">想此事大半衅由</span><span class="diczx2">姚</span>婆，毒谋出于 <span class="diczx2">方</span>贼，今已父子并诛，斩草除根，大仇已报，余人无罪，不可妄及。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《石点头》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:边衅;挑衅(借端生事，企图引起冲突或战争);寻衅</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 征兆 <span class="encs">[omen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">近览</span><span class="diczx2">刘氏</span>倾覆之衅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衅兆(征兆)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">衅</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集下" target="_blank">申集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集下&amp;bs=血" target="_blank">血</a>】
                              衅·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8845.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】許覲切【集韻】許愼切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音舋。與釁同。【玉篇】牲血塗器祭也。【禮&middot;樂記】車甲衅而藏之府庫，而弗復用。【註】衅，釁字也。許靳反。</p>
<p>（釁）【唐韻】虛振切【集韻】許愼切【韻會】許刃切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>興去聲。【說文】血祭也。象祭竈也。【廣韻】牲血塗器祭也。【禮&middot;月令】孟冬之月，命大史釁龜筴。【疏】謂殺牲以血塗釁其龜及筴。</p>
<p>又【雜記】成廟則釁之。【疏】謂宗廟初成，則殺牲取血以釁之，尊而神之也。【史記&middot;高祖紀】祭蚩尤於沛庭而釁鼓。【註】釁，祭也。殺牲以血塗鼓曰釁。</p>
<p>又罪也。【左傳&middot;宣十二年】觀釁而動。【註】釁，罪也。</p>
<p>又瑕也。【左傳&middot;桓八年】讐有釁，不可失也。【註】釁，瑕隙也。【史記&middot;李斯傳】成大功者，在因瑕釁而遂忍之。【註】索隱曰：言因諸侯有瑕釁，則忍心而剪除也。</p>
<p>又兆也。【魯語】若鮑氏有釁，吾不圖矣。【註】釁，兆也。【陸機&middot;答賈長淵詩】天厭霸德，黃祚告釁。</p>
<p>又塗也。一曰熏也。【齊語】比至，三釁三浴之。【註】以香塗身曰釁。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;肆師】共其釁鬯。【註】以鬯塗尸，使之香美也。【周禮&middot;春官】女巫掌歲時祓除釁浴。【註】釁浴，謂以香薰草藥沐浴。【前漢&middot;賈誼傳】釁面吞炭。【註】漆面以易貌。一曰熏也，以毒熏入之。</p>
<p>又動也。【左傳&middot;襄二十六年】夫小人之性釁於勇。【註】釁，動也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋獸】獸曰釁。【疏】獸之自奮迅動作名釁。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】周有釁夏。</p>
<p>又【正字通】通作舋。【韓非子】旣蓄王資而承敵國之舋。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】乗舋而運。</p>
<p>又或作衅。【禮&middot;樂記】車甲衅而藏之府庫。【註】衅，釁字也。包干戈以虎皮，明能以武服兵也。</p>
<p>又或作興。【禮&middot;禮器】旣興器用幣。【註】興，當爲釁字之誤。</p>
<p>（釁）</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/91C1.svg">                          <p>血祭也。象祭竈也。从爨省，从酉。酉，所以祭也。从分，分亦聲。虚振切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：分，布也。文三　重一</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/91C1_7058.svg" /><span class="swbf">(釁)</span><span class="ywyy">血祭也。</span><span class="swdz">周禮大祝注云：隋釁謂薦血也。凡血祭曰釁。孟子梁惠王趙注曰：新鑄鐘，殺牲以血塗其釁郤，因以祭之曰釁。漢書高帝紀釁<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡔷</span>，應劭曰：釁，祭也，殺牲以血塗<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡔷</span>釁呼爲釁。呼同罅。按凡言釁廟、釁鐘、釁<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡔷</span>、釁寶鎭寶器、釁龜策、釁宗廟，名器皆同，以血塗之因薦而祭之也。凡坼罅謂之釁。方言作璺。音問。以血血其坼罅亦曰釁。樂記作衅。</span><span class="ywyy">象祭<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2B058.svg"><span class="k_txt">𫁘</span>也。从爨省，</span><span class="swdz">祭<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2B058.svg"><span class="k_txt">𫁘</span>亦血塗之。故從爨省。爨者<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2B058.svg"><span class="k_txt">𫁘</span>也。</span><span class="ywyy">从酉。酉，所㠯祭也。</span><span class="swdz">酉者酒之省。</span><span class="ywyy">从分，</span><span class="swdz">取血布散之意。</span><span class="ywyy">分亦聲。</span><span class="swdz">分聲故釁，或爲薰。如齊語三釁三浴，或爲三薰。吕覽：湯得伊尹，釁以犧豭。風俗通作熏以萑葦。漢書豫讓釁面吞炭。顏云：釁，熏也。皆是也。釁又讀爲徽，如周禮女巫鬯人注先鄭說是也。分聲讀徽，此卽煇旂人微韵之比。古音十三部，在問韵。今韵虛振切，非也。</span></p>                          </div>
