						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>返</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄢˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 回，归：往～。～航。～工。～青（某些植物的幼苗移栽或越冬后，由黄转绿并恢复生长）。～销。～修。～还（<span class="dicpy">huán </span>）。流连忘～。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>返</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵，反声。字本作<span class="yhcs">“反”</span>。本义:回归，返回)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[return;go back]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">返，还也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">返，归也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祖伊返。——<span class="smcs">《书·西伯勘黎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黄鹤一去不复返，白云千载空悠悠。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔颢</span><span class="smcs">《黄鹤楼》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公子往而臣不送，以是知公子恨之复返也。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久在樊笼里，复得返自然。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">返字本作<span class="yhcs">“反”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">福禄来反。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·执竞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而反其侵地。——<span class="smcs">《国语·齐语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:返命(办完事情后，回来复命);返书(回信);返棹(船只返回)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 归还 <span class="encs">[return]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其子长而返其璧。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·观表》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俟汝至石头城，返汝簪。——<span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:返璧(送还别人馈赠的礼品);返金(退钱，还钱)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 更换 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">返瑟而弦。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·慎人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:返瑟而弦(换瑟而弹奏);返嘴(反口，改口);返易(倒换，掉换)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“反”</span>。违反 <span class="encs">[violate]</span>。如:返倒(违逆，抗拒)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr
