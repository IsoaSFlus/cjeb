又如:窘步(急步);窘惶(窘惧。急迫惶恐)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 尴尬;难为情 <span class="encs">[embarrassing]</span>。如:窘境;窘态</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>窘</strong> <span 使为难，迫使对方陷入困境 <span class="encs">[embarrass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">项籍</span>使将兵，数窘 <span class="diczx2">汉王</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·季布栾布列传》</span></p><p><span 要;切要 <span class="encs">[want]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窘乎哉，消者瞿瞿，孰知其要!——<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拘禁;局限 <span class="encs">[define]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飞鸣彼何乐，窘束此何冤!——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《赎鸡》</span></p><p><span
