                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>共</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gòng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gòng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>相同，一样：～性。～同。同甘～苦。</li><li>彼此都具有、使用或承受：患难与～。休戚与～。</li><li>一起，一齐：～鸣。～勉。～议。～处（<span class="dicpy">chǔ</span>）。</li><li>总计，合计：～计。总～。</li><li>与，和：“落霞与孤鹜齐飞，秋水～长天一色。”</li><li>“共產党”的简称。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>共</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">恭</span>”，恭敬。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">供</span>”，供奉，供给。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">共</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>共</strong> <span class="dicpy">gōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 供奉 <span class="encs">[piously worship;offer respectfully]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不共神祗。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尔贡包茅不入，王祭不共，无以缩酒。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 恭敬。通<span class="yhcs">“恭”</span> <span class="encs">[respect;venerate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共武之服。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·六月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虔共尔位。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·韩奕》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">公卑杞</span>， <span class="diczx2">杞</span>不共也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十七年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共承嘉惠。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span>。集解:<span class="yhcs">“敬也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:共命(敬从命令);共勤(恭谨勤劳);共承(恭敬地承奉);共奉(敬奉，供奉)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 供给;供应。通<span class="yhcs">“供”</span> <span class="encs">[supply]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共其羊牲。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·羊人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事之共给。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不能共亿。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行李之往来，共其乏困。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三辅长无共张繇役之劳。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·成帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:共给(供给);共张(供应、置备);共待(备用);共具(用以摆设酒食的器具)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 供职;奉职 <span class="encs">[hold office]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卿等不必言，其各共乃事。——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5171.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26C79.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦱹</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>渠用切，蛩去聲。【說文】同也。从廿卄。【徐曰】廿音入，二十共也，會意。【玉篇】同也，衆也。【廣韻】皆也。【增韻】合也，公也。【禮·王制】爵人于朝，與士共之。【史記·張釋之傳】法者，所與天下共也。</p><p>又【唐韻】九容切【集韻】【韻會】居容切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音恭。【前漢·王褒傳】共惟秋法，五始之要。【註】服虔曰：共，敬也。師古曰：共，讀曰恭。</p><p>又姓。【氏族略】以國爲氏。</p><p>又地名。【詩·大雅】侵阮徂共。【韻會】阮國之地，在河內共城。</p><p>又【集韻】古勇切【正韻】居竦切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>恭上聲。【前漢·百官公卿表】垂作共工，利器用。【註】應劭曰：垂，臣名也。爲共工，理百工之事。共讀曰龔。</p><p>又向也。【論語】居其所而衆星共之。</p><p>又姓。【前漢·匈奴傳】太守共友。【註】師古曰：共友，太守姓名也。共讀曰龔。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>居用切，恭去聲。【左傳·僖四年】敢不共給。【前漢·成帝紀】無共張繇役之勞。【註】師古曰：共音居用反，謂共具張設。</p><p>又【律歷志】共養三德爲善。◎按，共給，共養，有平、去二音，音別義同，故《正韻》東、送二韻共供兩存之。</p><p>又【正韻】忌遇切，音具。【周禮·天官·內饔】掌共羞修<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>膴胖骨鱐，以待共膳。【註】掌共，共當爲具。</p><p>又【集韻】胡公切，音洪。共池，地名。【字彙】本作卄，象兩手合持之形。今作共。</p><p>
