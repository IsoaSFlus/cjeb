                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闺</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>闺</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">閨</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">guī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄟˉ</span></p><ol><li>上圆下方的小门：～门。～阃（内室）。</li><li>旧时指女子居住的内室：深～。～阁。～女。～秀。～范。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> small entrance; women's quarters</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> chambre de femme,petite porte voûtée,boudoir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">闺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>闺</strong></p><p>閨 <span class="dicpy">guī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，圭声。<span class="yhcs">“圭”</span>亦具有表意作用。本义:上圆下方的小门;小门)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[small door]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">闺，特立之户也。上圆下方，有似圭。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俯而出城门，以为小之闺也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·解蔽》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筚门闺窦之人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">公孙戍</span>趋而去，未出，至中闺， <span class="diczx2">君</span>(指 <span class="diczx2">孟尝君</span>)召而反之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:闺窦(穿墙小门);闺閤(宫中小门，内宫深处);闺牖(宫内的门窗);闺壸(内宫)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 内室 <span class="encs">[inner apartment]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">室西连于中闺，先妣尝一至。——</span><span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今夫贵人之子，必宫居而闺处。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:闺门(内室的门。有时也称家门);闺闱(内室);闺閤(内室小门);闺禁(宫中后妃居住处)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 特指女子居住的内室 <span class="encs">[a woman's bedroom]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">杨</span>家有女初长成，养在深闺人未知。—— <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《长恨歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可怜无定河边骨，犹是春闺梦里人。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">陈陶</span><span class="smcs">《陇西行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:闺閤(女子的卧房。卧房，内室。也作闺阁);闺阃(妇女所居的内室);闺阈(女子居处的内室。即闺房);闺声(来自闺房的笑声)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 借指妇女 <span class="encs">[woman]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人间不见因谁知，万家闺艳求此时。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘言史</span><span class="smcs">《七夕歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:春闺;闺阃所无(比喻女子中少有的人才);闺人(妇女);闺妮(方言。指女儿);闺艳(美丽的少女)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                         <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閨·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95A8.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古攜切【集韻】【韻會】涓畦切【正韻】居爲切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音邽。【說文】特立之戸。上圜下方，有似圭。【左傳·襄十年】蓽門閨竇之人。【註】閨竇，小戸，穿壁爲戸。【廣韻】閨閤。【前漢·循吏傳】傳敎令出入閨閤。【註】閨閤，內中小門也。【玉篇】宮中門小者曰閨。【杜甫·贈李白詩】李侯金閨彥，脫身事幽討。【註】東方朔、公孫弘，待詔金馬門，故云金閨。【謝朓詩】旣通金閨籍。【註】列名仕版也。</p><p>又【南史·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>文帝紀】每夜刺閨取外事分判者，前後相續，敕雞人司漏，傳籤殿中。【註】夜有急報，投刺於宮門也。</p><p>又女稱閨秀，所居亦曰閨。【李白詩】揮鞭萬里去，安得念春閨。【後漢·皇后紀贊】班政蘭閨。【劉珊詩】石家金谷妓，妝罷出蘭閨。</p><p>又通作圭。【禮·儒行】蓽門圭窬。【註】小戸如圭形。</p>
