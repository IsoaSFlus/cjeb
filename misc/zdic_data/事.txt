                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">事</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>事</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄕˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>自然界和社会中的现象和活动：～情。～件。～业。</li>	<li>变故：～故（出于某种原因而发生的不幸事情，如工作中的死伤等）。～端。</li>	<li>职业：谋～（指找职业）。</li>	<li>关系和责任：你走吧，没你的～了。</li>	<li>办法：光着急也不是～儿，还得另找出路。</li>	<li>做，治：不～生产。无所～事。</li>	<li>服侍：～父母。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> affair, matter, business; to serve; accident, incident</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Angelegenheit, Ereignis, Geschäft; Unfall  (S)​,Angelegenheit  (S)​,Sache  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> être occupé,être engagé dans,affaire,chose (immatérielle)​,accident,trouble,travail,responsabilité,implication,empêtrement</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">事</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>事</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从史，之省声。史，掌管文书记录。甲骨文中与&ldquo;吏&rdquo;同字。本义:官职)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[government post]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事，职也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无功而受事，无爵而显荣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:事宦(仕宦。事通&ldquo;仕&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为职守;政事;事务 <span class="encs">[post;duty;powers of office]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事，勤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为政事庸力行务。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十五年》</span>。注:&ldquo;在君为政，在臣为事。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事蚤济也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事至佚而功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王霸》</span>。注:&ldquo;任也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陉山之事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span>。注:&ldquo;役也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公侯之事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;采蘩》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王事多难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;出车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">赵太后</span>新用事， <span class="diczx2">秦</span>急攻之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余将告于莅事者，更若役，复若赋，则何如?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:事分(职分;名分)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 职业 <span class="encs">[occupation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">舞阳侯</span><span class="diczx2">樊哙</span>者， <span class="diczx2">沛</span>人也。以屠狗为事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 事情 <span class="encs">[matter;thing;business;affair;job;work]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子</span>入太庙，每事问。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;八佾》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敏于事而慎于言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;学而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事无大小悉以咨之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下之事有难易乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻其师为弟子说前代事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:事不过三(同样的事不宜连作三次);事头(事因，事由;事情);事因(因由，事情来由);事苗(事情的根源);事机(时机;事情成功的机会);事有分定(凡事情总有定数);事在呼吸(比喻事情临近眼前，情况非常紧急);事序(事情的条理、秩序)。亦指天子、诸侯的国家大事，如祭祀、盟会、兵戎等</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子无事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">范宁</span>注:&ldquo;事谓巡守、崩葬、兵革之事。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 事业 <span class="encs">[achievements]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若事之不济，此乃天也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他日继吾志事，惟此生耳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:事绩(业绩，重大的成就);事济(事业完成)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 事故，事件 <span class="encs">[accident]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">延及</span><span class="diczx2">孝文王</span>、 <span class="diczx2">庄襄王</span>，享国之日浅，国家无事。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而士大夫亦未尝言兵，以为生事扰民，渐不可长。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:事款则圆(遇事要从长计议，才能圆满解决)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 交媾、性交 <span class="encs">[intercourse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:房事不宜多</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 典故;故事 <span class="encs">[allusion]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝来床前，为说稗官野史可喜可愕之事，聊资一欢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 情况，情形 <span class="encs">[condition;state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">论战斗之事，则缩颈而股栗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:事色(情况，情势)</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>事</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄕˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>sh&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4930.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">工作、職務。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;子路》：「居處恭，執<b>事</b>敬，與人忠，雖之夷狄不可棄也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;祭法》：「以死勤<b>事</b>則祀之。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人類所作所為及自然界的一切現象、活動，都稱為「事」。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「人<b>事</b>」、「國<b>事</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;大學》：「物有本末，<b>事</b>有終始，知所先後，則近道矣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">變故。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「多事之秋」、「平安無<b>事</b>」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">侍奉。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;學而》：「<b>事</b>父母能竭其力，<b>事</b>君能致其身。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">做、為。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;顏淵》：「回雖不敏，請<b>事</b>斯語矣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">插入。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷四五&middot;蒯通傳》：「慈父孝子所以不敢<b>事</b>刃於公之腹者，畏秦法也。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「剚」。</span></p></li>
