                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">无</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>无</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3"><img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">無</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>没有，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">有</span>&rdquo;相对；不：～辜。～偿。～从（没有门径或找不到头绪）。～度。～端（无缘无故）。～方（不得法，与&ldquo;有方&rdquo;相对）。～非（只，不过）。～动于衷。～所适从。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> negative, no, not; KangXi radical 7</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Nichts  (Philos)​,es hat nicht ,fehlen, un- ,keiner,keine ,nein,nicht,  ohne</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> non,71e radical</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">无</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>无</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 無 <span class="dicpy">m&oacute;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &mdash;&mdash;&ldquo;南无&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">nām&oacute;</span>):佛教用语，表示对佛尊敬或皈依</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">w&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>无</strong></p>
<p>無 <span class="dicpy">w&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。据甲骨文字形，象一个人持把在跳舞。卜辞、金文中&ldquo;无、舞&rdquo;同字。本义:乐舞)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sing and dance]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 哲学范畴，指无形、无名、虚无等，或指物质的隐微状态 <span class="encs">[nihility]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下万物生于有，有生于无。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>无</strong></p>
<p>無 <span class="dicpy">w&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>没有，跟&ldquo;有&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[not have;there is not]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无，不有也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无若丹朱傲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;益稷》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无偏无党。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无内人之疏，而外人之亲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;法行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事不耳闻目睹而臆断其有无，可乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无不伸颈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无不变色。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无不毕肖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无不惊为生人。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:无颜落色(面色发白，没有颜色);无头面(没有头绪);无是处(毫无办法;没有一点儿好处);无纤掐(没有一点);无气歇(没歇一口气，没有停息);无回豁(没有反应);没干净(不罢休，没完没了);无存济(无办法，难以应付);无笆壁(无依靠;无办法);无路求生;无干(没关系;不相干);无方(无常;没有固定的行止、住处等);无虞(无误)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>无</strong></p>
<p>無 <span class="dicpy">w&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不，表示对动词或形容词的否定 <span class="encs">[not]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣是以无请也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;喻老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请无攻</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不若无闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎行论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可以无悔矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">无</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集下" target="_blank">卯集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集下&amp;bs=无" target="_blank">无</a>】
                              无·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=4" target="_blank">4</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/65E0.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20476.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠑶</span>【唐韻】武夫切，音巫。【說文】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞣</span>，亡也。奇字，无通<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞣</span>。王育說，天屈西北爲无。【易&middot;乾卦】无咎。【釋文】无音無。易內皆作此字。【藝苑雄黃】无亦作亡。古皆用亡无，秦時始以蕃橆之橆爲有無之無。詩、書、春秋、禮記、論語本用无字，變篆者變爲無，惟易、周禮盡用无。然論語亡而爲有，我獨亡，諸無字，蓋變隸時誤讀爲存亡之亡，故不改也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】莫胡切，音模。南无，出《釋典》。</p>

                        <div class="nr-box-header">
                          <div class="zi-b-container zib-title">
                            <div class="label"><span class="res_t res_d res_s">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十二">卷十二</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十二&amp;swbs=亾">亾</a>】 </span><input type="checkbox" checked="checked" name="twbon" class="lcs_check" autocomplete="on" /></div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">𣠮</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“无”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/𣠮"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2382E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣠮</span></a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十二">卷十二</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十二&amp;swbs=亾">亾</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2382E.svg">                          <p>亡也。从亡無聲。</p>
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2382E_2.svg" />无，奇字无，通於元者。王育說：天屈西北爲无。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2382E_1.svg" /> <span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2382E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣠮</span>)</span> <span class="ywyy">亡也。</span> <span class="swdz">凡所失者，所未有者皆如逃亡然也。此有無字正體。而俗作無。無乃<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞤</span>之隷變。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞤</span>之訓豐也。與無義正相反。然則隷變之時。昧於亡爲其義。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞤</span>爲其聲。有聲無義。殊爲乖繆。古有叚<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞤</span>爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2382E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣠮</span>者。要不得云本無二字。漢隷多作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2382E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣠮</span>可證也。或叚亡爲無者，其義同。其音則雙聲也。</span> <span class="ywyy">从亡<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞤</span>聲。</span> <span class="swdz">按不用莫聲而用<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞤</span>聲者，形聲中有會意。凡物必自多而少而無。老子所謂多藏必厚亡也。武夫切。五部。古音武夫與莫胡二切不别。故無，模同音。其轉語則水經注云燕人謂無爲毛，楊子以曼爲無，今人謂無有爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CDA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣳚</span>有皆是也。</span></p>
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2382E_2.svg" /> <span class="swbf">(无)</span> <span class="ywyy">奇字無也。</span> <span class="swdz">謂古文奇字如此作也。今六經惟易用此字。</span> <span class="ywyy">通於元者。</span> <span class="swdz">元俗刻作无。今依宋本正。禮運曰：是謂合莫。注引孝經說曰：上通元莫。正義云。上通元莫者，孝經緯文。言人之精靈所感。上通元氣寂寞。引之者證莫爲虛無也。正本元字作无。謂虛無寂寞。義或然也。按此注疏今本譌誤不可讀。而北宋本可據正。疏正本字當是定本之誤。謂鄭引上通元莫，顔師古定本作无莫也。依許云通於元者，虛无道也。則孝經緯必作元莫矣。葢其義謂上通元始。故其字形亦用元篆。上毌於一。</span> <span class="ywyy">虛无道也。</span> <span class="swdz">謂虛无之道上通元氣寂寞也。玉篇曰：无，虛无也。奇字之无與篆文之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/237A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣞤</span>義乃微别。許說其義。非僅說其形也。</span> <span class="ywyy">王育說：天屈西北爲无。</span> <span class="swdz">此稱王育說又无之别一義也。亦說其義。非說其其。屈猶傾也。天傾西北。地不满東南。見列子及素問。天傾西北者，謂天體不能正圜也。</span></p>                          </div>
