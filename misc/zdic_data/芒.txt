                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">芒</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>芒</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">máng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/máng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>某些禾本科植物种子壳上的细刺：针尖对麦～。初露锋～。</li><li>像芒的东西：光～。</li><li>多年生草本植物，叶细长有尖，叶除可作绿篱和布置庭园外，又可作造纸原料和编织草鞋，嫩叶可做牛的饲料。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> Miscanthus sinensis</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Chinaschilf ,Mang  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> barbe d'épi,pointe,fatigué</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">芒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>芒</strong> <span class="dicpy">máng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/máng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，亡声。本义:谷类植物种子壳上或草木上的针状物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[awn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芒，草耑也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“谓麦谷为芒种是也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">种之芒种。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·地官·稻人》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑众</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“芒种，稻麦也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芒之为言萌也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通·五行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">麦渐渐以擢芒。(擢芒:长出麦芒。)——</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《射雉赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:芒茅(茅草的一种);芒履(芒屣，芒屦，芒鞋);芒鞋(用芒茎外皮编成的鞋。亦泛指草鞋);芒刺(草木茎叶和果谷上的小刺。比喻使人极度不安的感觉);芒角(植物的尖叶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 一种多年生大草本植物 <span class="encs">[Chinese silvergrass]</span>，秋天开花，黄褐色。如:芒草(又名莽草。形状像石楠而叶稀，有毒)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 光芒 <span class="encs">[ray]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扬芒熛而绛天兮。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张衡·思玄赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有作其芒。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:芒炎(星星的光芒);芒彩(光彩;光芒)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“铓”</span>。毫末;锋刃 <span class="encs">[blade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芒刀不顿。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·贾谊传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫不衄锐挫芒。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:芒锷(锋芒。比喻人的锐气);芒刃(刀剑锐利处。俗称刀尖、刀口)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>芒</strong> <span class="dicpy">máng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/máng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 广大;众多 <span class="encs">[vast;numerous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芒芒其稼。——</span><span class="diczx2">束晳</span><span class="smcs">《补亡诗·华黍》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:芒芒(通茫茫。广大辽远的样子;形容众多);芒洋(也作茫洋。浩翰无边际的样子);芒荒(大荒。辽阔的荒野)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“茫”</span>。模糊不清 <span class="encs">[dim]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">目芒然无见。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·盗跖》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:芒昧(混沌不明的样子);芒芠(混沌的样子);芒然(茫然);芒然自失(茫然自失)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
