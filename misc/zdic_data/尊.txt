                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>尊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄣˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>地位或辈分高：～长（<span class="dicpy">zhǎng </span>）。～卑。～贵。～严。</li>	<li>敬重：～敬。自～。～重。</li>	<li>敬辞，称与对方有关的人或事物：～府。令～。～姓。～驾。</li>	<li>量词：一～佛像。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/撙">撙</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尊</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尊</strong> <span class="dicpy">zūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象双手捧着尊，从酉，从収( <span class="dicpy">gǒng</span>)。小篆从酋，从収。或作寸。本义:酒器)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wine vessel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尊，酒器也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作罇。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辩六尊之名物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宗伯》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:&ldquo;献尊、象尊、著尊、壶尊、大尊、山尊。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尊用牺象山罍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;明堂位》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侧尊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;士冠礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">形似酒尊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆隐在尊中。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尊则振龙。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一尊还酹江月。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇&middot;赤壁怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:携尊(端起酒器);尊觯(尊谭。泛指酒器);尊爵(泛指礼器或酒器);尊彝(尊、彝均为古代酒器);尊中弩(酒尊中的弩影)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 行使行政的和常常是司法的职责的地方官员 <span class="encs">[magistrate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:邑尊;县尊</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>尊</strong> <span class="dicpy">zūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 敬重;推崇 <span class="encs">[respect;revere]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尊贤而重士。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尊五美，屏四恶，斯可以从政矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尊人(尊敬他人);尊上(尊敬长上);尊明(尊敬地对待并明示于人);尊贤(尊敬贤者);尊优(尊敬优待);尊齿(敬重年长者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 重视 <span class="encs">[pay attention to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牟夷非卿而书，尊地也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置诗、书、重攻战，尊首级。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子思</span>言&ldquo;尊德性&rdquo;，而归于&ldquo;道问学，问且先于学也。(<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span>:&ldquo;故君子尊德性而道问学。&rdquo;意思是君子尊崇高尚的道德品质，然后归结到由勤问好学入手。言:论。德性:高尚的道德品质。道:经由，名词用如动词。且:况且、并且、而且，表递进的连词。先:指先提出来，动词。子思:孔伋，孔子的孙子，传说<span class="smcs">《中庸》</span>是他作的。)&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尊用(重用);尊异(格外重视)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 减损 <span class="encs">[reduce;cut down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子深其深，浅其浅，益其益，尊其尊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 撙。节制;谦抑 <span class="encs">[restrain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谦尊而光。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;谦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大者不难卑身尊位以下之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说疑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尊节(克制;遵守法度);尊让(克制谦让)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 同&ldquo;遵&rdquo;。遵行，遵从 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尊五美，屏四恶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;尧曰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行既已尊之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">守者必善，而君尊用之，然后可以守也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 置酒，把酒倒入杯、碗、尊中 <span class="encs">[fill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫奥老妇之祭也，盛于盆，尊于瓶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尊醮(酌酒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 尊奉 <span class="encs">[worship;venerate;treat respectly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见其尊己也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《原毁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尊尚勇武。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>尊</strong> <span class="dicpy">zūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 尊贵;高贵 <span class="encs">[respectful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子者，执位圣尊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">位尊而无功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">媪尊</span><span class="diczx2">长安君</span>之位。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">履至尊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非尊于</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>&hellip;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">始吾读</span><span class="diczx2">孟轲</span>书，然后知 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>之道尊。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《读〈荀〉》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">德隆望尊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尊大(尊贵伟大;至高至大);尊安(尊贵安泰);尊客(尊贵的宾客);尊遂(尊贵显达);尊胜(尊贵;尊严);尊肃(尊贵庄严);尊优(地位尊贵，生活优裕);尊爵(尊贵的爵位)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 高，高出 <span class="encs">[high]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天尊地卑，乾坤定矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">虞庆</span>为屋，谓匠人曰:&ldquo;屋太尊。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尊远(高远);尊位(高位);尊者(称辈分或地位高的人);尊盛(位高势盛);尊属(辈分高的亲属);尊官(高官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 年龄大 <span class="encs">[elder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">间有年尊人不识羞耻，亦复为之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">吴自牧</span><span class="smcs">《梦梁录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一族之中，惟</span><span class="diczx2">李清</span>年齿最尊，推为族长。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醒世恒言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尊老(称年高的长辈);尊年(高龄);尊宿(年老而有名望的高僧);尊齿(高龄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 庄重;隆重 <span class="encs">[serious;selemon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尊礼(隆重的礼仪);尊严(庄重肃穆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 重要;紧要 <span class="encs">[important]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为客治饭而自藜藿，名尊于实也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尊要(重要);尊秘(重要而深秘)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>尊</strong> <span class="dicpy">zūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">代</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 敬词 <span class="encs">[your]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 对帝王、官吏的称呼。如:尊王、尊君(称帝王);尊官(对官员的敬称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 称呼父亲<span class="cino">(1)</span> 称别人的父亲。如:尊上，尊大人(称人父母);尊大君(称人父亲);尊甫、尊公(称别人父亲);尊侯(敬称别人父亲);尊堂(敬称他人母亲);令尊 <span class="cino">(2)</span> 称自己的父亲、母亲。如:尊慈，尊萱(对自己母亲的敬称);家尊。亦以称&ldquo;伯叔&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 对长辈的称呼。如:尊上(尊长)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 对一般人的敬称。如:尊篆(您的大名，客气说法);尊人(旧时称他人父母的敬词);尊官(尊敬的客官);尊壶(尊夫人);尊兄(对同辈年长者或己兄、他兄或朋友、共事者之间的敬称);尊姓(对人姓氏敬称);尊门(对人家族敬称)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>尊</strong> <span class="dicpy">zūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 称盛酒器 <span class="encs">[vessel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何时一尊酒，重与细论文?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春日怀李白》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 称塑像 <span class="encs">[statue]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">龛中各有人像一尊，手中各持种种乐器作吹奏式。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">郭沫若</span><span class="diczx2"><span class="smcs">《女神之再生》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一尊佛像</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 称大炮 <span class="encs">[piece]</span>。</p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“尊”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/𢍜"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span></a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四">卷十四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四&amp;swbs=酋">酋</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2235C.svg">                          <p>酒器也。从酋，廾以奉之。《周禮》六尊：犧尊、象尊、著尊、壺尊、太尊、山尊，以待祭祀賓客之禮。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/2235C_1.svg" />尊，尊或从寸。祖昆切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗以尊作尊卑之尊。别作罇。非是。文二　重一</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2235C_9400.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">酒器也。</span><span class="swdz">凡酒必實於尊以待酌者。鄭注禮曰：置酒曰尊。凡酌酒者必資於尊，故引申以爲尊卑字，猶貴賤本謂貨物而引申之也。自專用爲尊卑字，而別製罇樽爲酒尊字矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从酋，卄㠯奉之。</span><span class="swdz">卄者，竦手也。奉者，承也。設尊者必竦手以承之。祖昆切。十三部。</span><span class="ywyy">周禮六<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>：犧<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、象<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、箸<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、壷<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、大<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>、山<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>。㠯待祭祀賓客之禮。</span><span class="swdz">見周禮司尊彝職。犧作獻。鄭司農云：獻讀爲犧。犧尊，飾以翡翠。象尊，以象鳳皇，或曰以象骨飾尊。箸尊，箸略尊也，或曰箸尊箸地無足。壷者，以壷爲尊。春秋傳曰：尊以魯壷。大尊，大古之瓦尊。山尊，山罍也。按毛詩閟之犧尊，卽獻尊也。故許同大鄭作犧，以待祭祀，司尊彝詳之矣。大行人賓客之祼亦必用彝，饗禮食禮亦必用尊，故約之曰以待祭祀賓客之禮。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/2235C_9401.svg" /><span class="swbf">(尊)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2235C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢍜</span>或从寸。</span><span class="swdz">此與寺从寸意同。有法度者也。</span></p>                          </div>
