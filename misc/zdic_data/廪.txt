                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>廪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>米仓，亦指储藏的米：仓～。～生（即“廪膳生员”，中国明、清两代称由府、州、县按时发给银子和补助生活的生员）。～饩（旧指由官府供给的粮食）。</li><li>积聚，郁结：“～于肠胃”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">廪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>廪</strong></p><p>廪 <span class="dicpy">lǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼象形。从人回，象屋形，中有户牖。又作<span class="yhcs">“廪”</span>。<span class="yhcs">“廪”</span>是俗字。本义:米仓)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[store house for rice; granary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亦有高廪。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·丰年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盛米曰廩。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·廪人》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余刀布，有囷廪。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·荣辱》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“圆曰囷，方曰廪。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">御廪者，粢盛委之所藏也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·桓公十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">御廪，夫人八妾所舂米，藏以奉宗庙也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谷藏曰仓，米藏曰廪。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父母使</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>完廪。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·万章上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">管子</span>曰<span class="yhcs">“仓廪实而知礼节。”</span>—— <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:廪囷(米仓。方的称廪，圆的称囷);廪粟(米仓中的粮食);廪庚(米仓)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指粮食仓库 <span class="encs">[granary; barn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">昭侯</span>令人复廪。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·内储说下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">广蓄积，以实仓廪。——</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如廪人(古代管理粮仓的官吏)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 米粟类;粮食 <span class="encs">[grains]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坐縻廪粟而不知耻。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:廪禄(官府供给的俸米和俸钱);廪库(粮仓;仓库);廪庾(粮仓);廪饷(粮饷)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 俸禄 <span class="encs">[salary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">廪食以数百人。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·内诸说上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:廪给(官吏的俸给);廪秩;廪俸;廪赐(俸禄和赏赐)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> <span class="yhcs">“廪生”</span>的简称 <span class="encs">[scholars who live on government grants]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">他家大老那宗笔下，怎得会补起廪来。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:禀贡(以禀生的资格做了贡生)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“林”</span>。林甸。树林与郊野 <span class="encs">[secluded place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桓公明日弋在廪。——<span class="smcs">《管子·戒》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>廪</strong> <span class="dicpy">lǐn</span>                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5EE9.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2223A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢈺</span>【唐韻】力甚切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】力錦切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音凜。【說文】本作㐭。穀所振入，宗廟粢盛，倉黃㐭而取之，故謂之㐭。从入，回，象屋形中有戸牖。或从度从禾。【爾雅·釋言】廩，廯也。【玉篇】倉廩也。【釋名】廩，矜也。實物可惜者，投之其中也。【詩·周頌】亦有高廩，萬億及秭。【禮·明堂位】米廩，有虞氏之庠也。【註】庠序亦學也。魯謂之米廩，虞帝上孝，令藏粢盛之委焉。【周語】廩於藉東南，鍾而藏之。【註】廩，御廩。一名神倉，東南生長之處。鍾，聚也。爲廩以藏所藉田，以奉粢盛。【荀子·富國篇】垣窌倉廩者，財之末也。【註】穀藏曰倉，米藏曰廩。</p><p>又給也。【後漢·章帝紀】恐人稍受廩，往來煩劇。【註】廩，給也。</p><p>又天廩，星名。【隋書·天文志】天廩四星在昴南。【張衡·周天大象賦】天廩備稷以祈歆。</p><p>又官名。【周禮·地官】廩人掌九穀之數，以待國之匪頒賙賜稍食。【後漢·和帝紀】復置廩犧官。【註】漢官儀曰：廩犧令一人秩六百石。</p><p>又與懍通。【前漢·食貨志】可以爲富安天下，而直爲此廩廩也。</p><p>又與<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21484.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡒄</span>通。【集韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21484.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡒄</span>或作廩。</p><p>
