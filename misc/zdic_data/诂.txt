                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>诂</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">詁</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用通行的话解释古代语言文字或方言字义：训～。解～。字～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> exegesis, explanation; explain</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> darlegen, erklären ,Erläuterung  (S)​,Kommentar  (S)​,Kommentator  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> expliquer des textes anciens en termes modernes</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>诂</strong></p>
<p>詁 <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从言，从古，古亦声。本义:用通行的话解释古代语言文字或方言字义) 同本义 <span class="encs">[explain archaic or dialectal words in current language]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诂，训故言也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 后汉桓谭传郑兴传注皆引说文、训古言也</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诂者，古也。古今异言，通之使人知也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《毛诗&middot;周南&middot;关睢》</span>诂训传疏</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诂，训故言也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;桓谭传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">雄</span>少而好学，不为章句，训诂通而已，博览无所不见。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:诂解(用当代语言解释古语);诂训(解释古书的文义。也用以指古言古义。同故训)</span></p>

<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>古言古义;词语的意义 <span class="encs">[archaism;old saying;the meaning of a word]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">同字而异诂，同辞而异义。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《万卷楼记》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">詁</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“诂”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/詁">詁</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              詁·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A41.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】果五切【正韻】公土切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音古。【說文】訓故言也。引《詩》詁訓孔穎達疏：詁、訓、傳者，註解之別名。詁者，古今異言，通之使人知也。【徐曰】按《爾雅》：詁，古也，言有古今也。會意。【博雅】言也。【正韻】通古今之言，而明其故也。【前漢&middot;揚雄傳】雄少而好學，不爲章句訓詁通而已。【舊唐書&middot;經籍志】詁訓以紀六經讖<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古慕切，古去聲。義同。</p>
<p>又通作故。【詩&middot;周南&middot;關雎詁訓傳第一】陸德明音義曰：詁訓，舊本多作故，今或作詁。【前漢&middot;藝文志】詩經魯故二十五卷。【師古註】故者，通其指義也。今流俗毛詩攺故訓傳爲詁字，失其眞耳。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">詁</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“诂”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/詁">詁</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三">卷三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三&amp;swbs=言">言</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8A41.svg">                          <p>訓故言也。从言古聲。《詩》曰詁訓。公戶切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8A41_6213.svg" /><span class="swbf">(詁)</span><span class="ywyy">訓故言也。</span><span class="swdz">故言者，舊言也。十口所識前言也。訓者，說教也。訓故言者，說釋故言以教人是之謂詁。分之則如爾雅析故訓言爲三。三而實一也。漢人傳注多偁故者，故卽詁也。毛詩云故訓傳者，故訓猶故言也，謂取故言爲傳也。取故言爲傳，是亦詁也。賈誼爲左氏傳訓故。訓故者，順釋其故言也。</span><span class="ywyy">从言古聲。</span><span class="swdz">公戸切。五部。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰詁訓。</span><span class="swdz">此句或謂卽大雅古訓是式，或謂卽毛公詁訓傳，皆非是。按釋文於抑，告之話言下云：戸快反。說文作詁。則此四字當爲“詩曰：告之詁言”六字無疑。毛傳曰：詁言古之善言也。以古釋詁正同許以故釋詁，陸氏所見說文未誤也。自有淺人見詩無告之詁言，因改爲詩曰詁訓，不成語耳。</span></p>                          </div>
