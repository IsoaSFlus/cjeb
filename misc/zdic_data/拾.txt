                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拾</strong> <span class="dicpy">shè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蹑足而上 <span class="encs">[tiptoe up]</span>。如:拾级</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">shí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/拾级">拾级</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拾</strong> <span class="dicpy">shí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，合声。本义:捡取，从地上捡起来)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pick up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拾，掇也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹拾遗于涂，摭弃于野。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·逢遇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故周之夺殷也，拾遗于庭。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·安危》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犹拾瀋也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俯有拾，仰有取。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">志士不饮盗泉之水，廉者不受嗟来之食，况拾遗求利以污其行乎!——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拾视之，非字而画。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:拾粪;拾麦穗儿;拾毛(拣界外球。毛:用毛充塞内部的球);拾芥(拾取地上的小草。比喻取之极易);拾石为砚(从地上拾起一块石头用作砚台);拾夺(拾掇;收拾);拾拣(拣取);拾翠(拾取翠鸟羽毛以为首饰);拾沈(拾取汁水)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 收敛，收集 <span class="encs">[collect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妇人拾蚕。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说林下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拾发以将乘矢。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请拾投。——<span
