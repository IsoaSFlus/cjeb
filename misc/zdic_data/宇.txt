                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>宇</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˇ</span></p><ol><li>屋檐，泛指房屋：～下（屋檐之下，喻在他人庇护之下）。屋～。庙～。～宙（ａ．屋檐和栋梁；ｂ．空间与时间；ｃ．天地）。</li><li>国土，疆土：故～。</li><li>整个空间世界：～内（指天下）。玉～。～航。</li><li>风度，仪表：眉～（指人的样子，仪表）。器～轩昂。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">宇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>宇</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，于声。<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>表示房屋。本义:屋檐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[eaves]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宇，屋边也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宇，屋檐也。——<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span>引<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上栋下宇。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">八月在宇。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“屋四垂为宇。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">置于宇西阶上。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五帝庙同宇。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·郊祀志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而燕雀佼之，以为不能与之争于宇宙之间。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·览冥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">权</span>起更衣， <span class="diczx2">肃</span>追于宇下。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:宇堂(屋檐下的正房前);宇达(屋檐和窗户);宇溜(屋檐)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 房屋 <span class="encs">[house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">聿来胥宇。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·緜》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高堂邃宇。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟恐琼楼玉宇，高处不胜寒。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《水调歌头》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:屋宇(房屋);宇庭(屋室和庭院);宇室(房舍。借指家庭)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 空间。上下四方，天地之间 <span class="encs">[space]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四方上下曰宇，古往今来曰宙。——<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">神覆宇宙。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·下贤》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“四方上下曰宇。以屋喻天地也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">威振八寓。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“八方区宇也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">云霏霏而承宇。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·屈原·涉江》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:宇甸(宇县，宇寰，宇宙。天下);寰宇;宇内</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 地域;疆土 <span class="encs">[territory]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">复</span><span class="diczx2">周公</span>之宇。</p><p><span
