                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">胜</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>胜</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">勝</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>在斗争或竞赛中打败对方或事业达到预定目的：～利。得～。～券（指胜利的把握）。～负。无往不～。</li>
	<li>超过，占优势：～似。优～。以少～多。</li>
	<li>优美的：～地。～景。～境。～迹。～状（胜景）。</li>
	<li>古代妇女的饰物：花～。彩～。</li>
	<li>能承担，能承受（旧读<span class="dicpy">shēng </span>）：～任。不～其烦。</li>
	<li>尽（旧读<span class="dicpy">shēng </span>）：不～感激。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> victory; excel, be better than</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Sieg, Erfolg, besiegen, übertreffen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> remarquable,excellent,supérieur,être égal à,être à la hauteur de,pouvoir supporter,victoire,succès,vaincre,remporter la victoire sur,surpasser,dépasser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">胜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胜</strong></p>
<p>勝 <span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，朕( <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n</span>)声。本义:胜任，禁得起)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[can bear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胜，任也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胜，克也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫之胜说。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;遯》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;能也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武王</span>靡不胜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;烈祖》</span>。传:&ldquo;任也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子有幼弱不胜养为累者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;入国》</span>。注:&ldquo;堪也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白头搔更短，浑欲不胜簪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春望》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言讫，歔欷流涕，悲不自胜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:不胜其苦(苦得受不住);胜任愉快(有能力承担并能愉快地完成任务);胜兵(能充当士兵参战的人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 战胜，打败 <span class="encs">[defeat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胜殷遏刘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;武》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胜，克也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其势弱于</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>，而犹有可以不赂而胜之势。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
