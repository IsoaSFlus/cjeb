                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>饬</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">飭</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˋ</span></p><ol><li>整顿，使整齐：整～纪律。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">敕</span>”，告诫，命令。</li><li>谨慎：“程元凤谨～有余，而乏风节”。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">饰</span>”，巧饰。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> order; command; give command</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ordonner,commander,arranger</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>饬</strong></p><p>飭 <span class="dicpy">chì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，从力，食声。本义:修整，整治)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[readjust]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饬，致坚也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">周</span>军饬垒。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“治也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饬曲岸之际。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戎车既饬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·六月》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:整饬;饬装(整备行装);饬兵(整顿军队)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 命令 <span class="encs">[order]</span>。如:饬差(责令派人);饬知(上级官署给属官的告谕公文);饬查(命令下属调查)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 告诫。通<span class="yhcs">“敕”</span><span class="encs">[admonish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">信饬百官，众功皆兴。——<span class="smcs">《史记·五帝本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">修德束躬，以自申饬，所以检其邪心，守其正意也。——<span class="smcs">《说苑·修文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">双饬众官，各慎其职。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲令戒饬。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·杨恽传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:饬厉(告诫勉励。也作敕厉);饬勉(告诫勉励)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 指责 <span class="encs">[reproach]</span>。如:饬责(斥责)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>饬</strong></p><p>飭 <span class="dicpy">chì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 谨慎;恭敬 <span class="encs">[respectful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饬者，谨也，敬也。——<span class="smcs">《匡缪正俗》</span>八</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:饬备(谨慎防备);饬愿(谨慎诚实)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                           <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">飭</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“饬”字头，请参考“飭”字：)</span>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=力">力</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/98ED.svg">                          <p>致堅也。从人从力，食聲。讀若敕。恥力切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">致臤也。</span>臤者、堅也。致者、送詣也。致之於堅。是之謂飭。攷工記曰。審曲面勢以飭五材。謂五材皆必堅緻也。又曰。飭力以長地材。謂整頓其人力也。凡人物皆得云飭。飭人而筋骸束矣。飭物而器用精良矣。其字形與飾相似。故古書多有互譌者。飾在外。飭在內。其義不同。竊謂許書工下云巧飾也。當作巧也、飭也。飭卽記所謂飭五材。辨民器。謂之百工。不徒修飾其外而已。凡經傳子史之譌皆可以意正之。飭與敕義略相近。敕、誡也。<span class="jieshi">从人力。食聲。讀若敕。</span>恥力切。一部。                          </div>
