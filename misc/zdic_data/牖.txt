                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>牖</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从片户甫。片，锯开的木片，<span class="yhcs">“户”</span>指窗。先秦多用牖，窗少见。本义:窗户) 同本义 <span class="encs">[window]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牖，穿壁以木为交窗也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“交窗者，以木横直为之，即今之窗也。在墙曰牖，在屋曰窗。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宗室牖下。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·采》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众美人把四周窗牖俱开。——<span class="smcs">《隋唐演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然</span><span class="diczx2">陈涉</span>瓮牖绳枢之子。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">扃牖而居。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牖其前以通明。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:牖下(家中)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=片">片</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7256.svg">                          <p>穿壁以木爲交窻也。从片、戶、甫。譚長以爲：甫上日也，非戶也。牖，所以見日。與久切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7256_3788.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(牖)</span><span class="ywyy">穿壁㠯木爲交窗也。</span><span class="swdz">交窗者、以木横直爲之。卽今之窗也。在牆曰牖。在屋曰窗。此則互明之。必言以木者、字从片也。古者室必有戶有牖。牖東戶西。皆南鄉。毛詩曰。向、北出牖也。北或有穴通明。至冬塞之。然士虞禮祝啓牖鄉注云。鄉、牖一名。明堂位達鄉注。牖屬。是南牖亦名向。士喪禮。寢東首於墉下。喪大記作北墉下。今本墉皆譌牖。非也。牖所以通明。故叚爲誘。召南吉士誘之、大雅天之牖民傳皆訓曰。道也。道卽導。</span><span class="ywyy">从片。戶甫聲。</span><span class="swdz">葢用合韵爲聲也。與久切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">譚長㠯爲</span><span class="swdz">譚長者、博采通人之一。</span><span class="ywyy">甫上。</span><span class="swdz">句。</span><span class="ywyy">日也。非戶也。牖所㠯見日。</span><span class="swdz">說从日之意也。許篆作牖。而偁譚說者、字久从戶作。譚說有理。故偁之。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
