                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">时</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>时</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">時</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>一切事物不断发展变化所经历的过程：～间。～不我与。</li>
	<li>较长的时间：～代。古～。</li>
	<li>泛指一段时间：～光。～令。～分。</li>
	<li>现在的，当前的：当～。～下。～务。～宜。</li>
	<li>常常：～常。学而～习之。</li>
	<li>一年中的一季：～序。四～如春。</li>
	<li>旧时的记时单位，一昼夜十二分之一为一&ldquo;时辰&rdquo;，深夜十一点至次日凌晨一点为&ldquo;子时&rdquo;，中午十一点至一点为&ldquo;午时&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>现在的记时单位，一昼夜的二十四分之一：～钟。～差（<span class="dicpy">chā</span>）。</li>
	<li>某一时刻：按～上班。</li>
	<li>不定时，有时候：～而。～阴～晴。</li>
	<li>机会：～机。待～而动。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">时</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>时</strong></p>
<p>時、旹 <span class="dicpy">sh&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，寺声。从&ldquo;日&rdquo;与时间有关。本义:季度;季节)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[quarter (of a year);season]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时，四时也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时，四方各一时，时，期也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谓其三时不害。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公六年》</span>。注:&ldquo;春夏秋也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三时者，成岁之要时。又，天时不如地利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;篇叙》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓时曰支干五行相孤虚之属也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斧斤以时入山林。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时事之征也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;闵公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时者，所以记岁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;山权数》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时者，天之吏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时和谓之宝烛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释天》</span>。注:&ldquo;道光照也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬授人时。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:&ldquo;羲和四子掌四时。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时之景不同，而乐亦无穷也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谓其三时(指春夏秋)不害，而民和年丰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公六年》</span></span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">時</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“时”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/時">時</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上" target="_blank">辰集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上&amp;bs=日" target="_blank">日</a>】
                              時·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6642.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕旹【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】市之切【正韻】辰之切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蒔。【說文】四時也。【釋名】四時，四方各一時。時，期也，物之生死各應節期而至也。【書&middot;堯典】敬授人時。【傳】敬記天時以授人也。【又】朞三百有六旬有六日，以閏月定四時成歲。【禮&middot;孔子閒居】天有四時，春秋冬夏。【淮南子&middot;天文訓】隂陽之專精爲四時。【又】三月而爲一時。</p>
<p>又【韻會】辰也，十二時也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】是也。【書&middot;堯典】黎民於變時雍。【傳】時，是也。【詩&middot;大雅】曰止曰時，築室于兹。【朱傳】可以止于是，而築室矣。</p>
<p>又【博雅】伺也。【論語】孔子時其亡也，而往拜之。【疏】謂伺虎不在家時而往謝之。</p>
<p>又【博雅】善也。【廣韻】中也。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;莊九年】戰于乾時。【註】乾時，齊地。時水在樂安界，岐流旱則竭涸，故曰乾時。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】良吏傳有時苗。《何氏姓苑》云：今鉅鹿人。</p>
<p>又與塒同。【詩&middot;王風】雞棲于塒。【釋文】塒，本亦作時。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶上紙切。【王粲&middot;七釋】不以志易道，不以身後時。進德修業，與世同理。</p>
<p>又叶側吏切。【屈原&middot;離騷】忳鬰邑余侘傺兮，吾獨窮苦乎此時也。叶下態，態音替。</p>
