						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>幸</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄥˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>意外地得到成功或免去灾害：～运。侥～。～存。～免。～未成灾。</li>
	<li>福气：～福。荣～。</li>
	<li>高兴：庆～。欣～。～甚。</li>
	<li>希望：～勿推却。～来告语之。</li>
	<li>宠爱：宠～。得～。</li>
	<li>指封建帝王到达某地：巡～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">幸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>幸</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸，吉而免凶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸而至于旦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不幸短命死矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">皇</span>疏:&ldquo;凡应死而生曰幸。应生而死曰不幸。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸甚。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《步出夏门行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生固幸而遇予。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知幸与不幸。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸获名成。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民之遭水旱疾疫而不幸者，不过十之一二矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p>又如:三生有幸;荣幸(光荣而幸运);万幸(非常幸运);得此幸，不幸中的大幸;庆幸;天幸;不幸;万幸</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>幸</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;倖&rdquo;。侥幸 <span class="encs">[luckily]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非分而得谓之幸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝无幸位。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;富国》</span>。注:&ldquo;无德而禄谓之幸位。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小人见人之厄则幸之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;宣公十五年》</span>。注:&ldquo;侥倖也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸得脱矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸皆杀之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朕自得丞相，幸成帝业。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:幸心(侥幸心理);幸生(侥幸偷生);幸民(侥幸于万一之民);幸位(侥幸得位);幸得(幸而);幸诡(侥幸和诡诈);幸赏(侥幸得赏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 幸亏 <span class="encs">[fortunately]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸大雪踰岭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸诚门未有烧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幸彼苍默佦</span></p>
