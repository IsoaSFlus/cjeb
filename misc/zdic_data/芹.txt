                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">芹</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>芹</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qín  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qín.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>〔～献〕对人谦称所赠东西不好。亦称“献芹”。</li><li>〔～意〕谦辞，微薄的情意。</li><li>〔～藻〕古代喻贡士或有才学之士。</li><li>菜名，一年或二年生草本植物，茎可食。亦称“水芹”。还有一种“草芹”，有特殊香味，俗称“药芹”。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>芹</strong> <span class="dicpy">qín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 芹菜 <span class="encs">[celery]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芹，楚葵也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 即今之水芹菜》</p><p><span class="diczx1">言采其芹。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采菽》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薄采其芹。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·泮水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">菜之美者，</span><span class="diczx2">云梦</span>之芹。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·本味》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">芹菹兔醢。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·醢人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 多年生草本植物，夏天开白色花，茎叶可以吃。亦名<span class="yhcs">“水芹”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“水靳”</span>，古名<span class="yhcs">“楚葵”</span>。又有一种旱芹，有特殊的香气，俗名<span class="yhcs">“药芹”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>芹</strong> <span class="dicpy">qín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 喻微薄 <span class="encs">[meagre]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邀兄到敝斋一饮，不知可纳芹意否?——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 如:芹敬(微薄的礼物);芹诚(谦词。微薄的诚意);芹意(谦词。微薄的情意);芹曝(谦词。所献微不足道)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
