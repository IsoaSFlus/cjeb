						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>粤</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yu&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄝˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>中国广东省的别称：～语。～剧。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">聿</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">越</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">曰</span>&rdquo;，文言助词，用于句首或句中。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> Guangdong and Guangxi provinces; initial particle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Guangdong, Kanton ( Akz. )​  (S, Geo)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> autre nom de la province du Guangdong</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>粤</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈助〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 助词。古与&ldquo;聿&rdquo;、&ldquo;越&rdquo;、&ldquo;曰&rdquo;通用，用于句首或句中</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粤，于也。审慎之词也。从于，从寀，会意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粤，曰也。又，于也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粤三日丁亥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;召诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粤詹(瞻)</span><span class="diczx2">雒伊</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;周本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚粤其几，沦神域兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《幽通赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:粤若(发语词。用于句首以起下文)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>粤</strong> <span class="dicpy">Yu&egrave;</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 广东的简称 <span class="encs">[Guangdong; Kwangtung]</span>。如:粤绣</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 旧地名 <span class="encs">[the provinces of Guangdong and Guangxi]</span>。百粤之地，即今广东和广西等地</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予弱冠</span><span class="diczx2">粤</span>行。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:粤东(广东省的别称);粤寇(清朝统治阶级对太平天国起义者的污蔑之词);粤海(指中国南部广东一带的海域，又作为广东和广州的代称);粤峤(指五岭以南地区)</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7CA4.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>王伐切，音越。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】粤，曰也。【註】語辭發端。【說文】審愼之詞。【徐曰】凡言粤者，皆在事端句首，未便言之，駐其言以審思之。書召誥，粤三日丁巳是也。心中暗數其日數，然後言之，其聲氣舒亏，故从亏會意。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】於也。【註】語之韻絕歎辭也。</p>
<p>又與曰通。【書&middot;堯典】曰若稽古帝堯。</p>
<p>又與越通。【書&middot;召誥】惟太保先周公相宅越若來。</p>
<p>又地名。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】從百粤之兵以佐諸侯，誅暴秦。</p>
<p>又【地理志】粤地，牽牛婺女之分野。</p>
<p>又厚也。【管子&middot;五行篇】天爲粤宛，草木養長。【註】天爲厚順，不逆時氣也。《說文》粵，从亏从宷。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7CB5.svg">                          <p>亏也。審愼之詞者。从亏从宷。《周書》曰：&ldquo;粤三日丁亥。&rdquo;王伐切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">亏也。</span>釋詁曰。粤于爰曰也。爰粤于也。爰粤于那都繇於也。粤與于雙聲。而又从亏。則亦象气舒于也。詩書多假越爲粤。箋云。越、於也。又假曰爲粤。<span class="jieshi">宷愼之䛐也。</span>也各本作者。今正。此說从宷之意。粤亏皆訓於。而粤尤爲宷度愼重之䛐。故从宷。<span class="jieshi">从宷亏。</span>宷愼而言之也。王伐切。十五部。<span class="jieshi">周書曰。粤三日丁亥。</span>今召誥越三日丁巳。亥當作巳。                          </div>
