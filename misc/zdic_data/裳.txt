                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">裳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>裳</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，尚声。本义:下衣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古人穿的遮蔽下体的衣裙，男女都穿，是裙的一种，不是裤子 <span class="encs">[skirt worn by a woman or man in ancient China]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裳下饰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黄裳元吉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坤》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绿兮衣兮，绿衣黄裳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;绿衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">制芰荷 以为衣兮，集芙蓉以为裳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">整顿衣裳起敛容。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裳衣(裳与衣。泛指衣服);裳服(衣服);裳裾(衣襟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指衣服 <span class="encs">[dress]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脱我战时袍，著我旧时裳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裳袂(衣袖);裳裙(衣襟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">shɑng</span></p>
