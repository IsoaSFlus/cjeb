                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>机</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">機</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>事物发生的枢纽：生～。危～。转（<span class="dicpy">zhuǎn </span>）～。契～。</li>
	<li>对事情成败有重要关系的中心环节，有保密性质的事件：军～。～密。</li>
	<li>合宜的时候：～会。～遇。时～。</li>
	<li>由许多零作组成可以做功或有特殊作用的装置和设备：～器。～动。～关。</li>
	<li>有生命的生物体器官的作用：～能。有～体。</li>
	<li>灵活，能迅速适应事物变化的：～智。～敏。～巧。～变。</li>
	<li>指&ldquo;飞机&rdquo;：客～。～场。～组。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">机</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>机</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 木名。桤木树 <span class="encs">[long penduncled alder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">机，机木。从木，几声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">单狐之山</span>多机木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;北山经》</span>。注:&ldquo;似榆，可烧，以粪稻田。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">春机杨柳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《蜀都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;几&rdquo;。几案，小桌子 <span class="encs">[small table]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奔其机。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;涣》</span>。注:&ldquo;承物者也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公子牟隐机大息，仰天而笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">围巾几(机)筵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:机杖(几案与手杖);机案(泛指案桌);机筵(几案和坐席);机榻(几案与床榻)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>机</strong></p>
<p>機 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，幾( <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:弓弩上的发射机关。按:&ldquo;机&rdquo;与&ldquo;機&rdquo;古代是两个字。&ldquo;机&rdquo;本木名，即桤( <span class="dicpy">qī</span>)木树。现在&ldquo;机&rdquo;是&ldquo;機&rdquo;的简化字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[engine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主发谓之機。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耳目，心之枢机也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span>。注:&ldquo;枢机，发动也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">施关发机。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一龙发机。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一龙机发。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">机发吐丸。(机关发动吐出铜丸。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范晔</span><span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:机牙(机互。弩上发箭的含矢处和钩弦制动的机件);机括(机栝。弩上发矢的机件;可开关的机械装置;圈套，计谋;机密，关键);机郭(弩上机牙的外壳);机发(谓弩机发射)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指机械，器械 <span class="encs">[machine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在璇机玉衡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:&ldquo;机，浑天仪可旋转，故曰机。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">公输班</span>为 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>设机。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;宋策》</span></p>
