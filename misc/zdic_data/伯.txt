                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>伯</strong> <span class="dicpy">bà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“霸”</span>。原指春秋时诸侯的首领 <span class="encs">[chief of feudal princes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">穆公</span>任之，强配五伯、六卿施。(五伯，即五个盟主，五霸:齐桓公、晋文公、宋襄公、秦穆公、楚庄王。)——<span class="smcs">《荀子·成相》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:伯气(霸气)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>伯</strong> <span class="dicpy">bà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 称霸 <span class="encs">[dominate;tyrannize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一朝而伯。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·儒效》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">bǎi;bó</span></p><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>伯</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 数目。十的十倍 <span class="encs">[hundred]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亡(无)农夫之苦，有仟伯之得。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
