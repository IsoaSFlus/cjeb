                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>君</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>封建时代指帝王、诸侯等：～主。～子（ａ．旧指贵族、统治者及其代言人；ｂ．指品行好的人，如“正人～～”）。～王。～上。国～。～权。～临。～侧。</li><li>古代的封号：商～。平原～。信陵～。长安～。</li><li>对对方的尊称：张～。诸～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sovereign, monarch, ruler, chief, prince</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gebieter  (S)​,Jun  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> souverain,monarque,gentleman,dirigeant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">君</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>君</strong> <span class="dicpy">jūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从尹，从口。<span class="yhcs">“尹”</span>，表示治事;从<span class="yhcs">“口”</span>，表示发布命令。合起来的意思是:发号施令，治理国家。本义:君主，国家的最高统治者)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代大夫以上据有土地的各级统治者的通称 <span class="encs">[sovereign;ruler]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君，尊也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赏庆刑威曰君。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君也者，掌令者也。——<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君者，治辨之主也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·礼论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君帝清问下民。——<span class="smcs">《书·吕刑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">克长克君。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·皇矣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二十余君。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">思得明君。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则忧其君。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身名君宠。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:尧舜之君;欺君罔上;贤君;暴君;昏君;国君;君臣;明君;君王(古时对帝王的一种尊称);君伐(君王的功绩);君位(国君之位);君长(国君与卿大夫;天子或诸侯);君体(人主的体统);君德(人主的德行或恩德);君侯(古代对列侯的尊称);君公(诸侯);君父(对父为国君者的称呼);君子医(术精德高的名医)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 封建制度的一种尊号，尤指君主国家所封的称号或封号 <span class="encs">[lord]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君以十五之地存。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:平原君;春申君;武安君</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 引申为人的尊称，相当于<span class="yhcs">“您”</span> <span class="encs">[your]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君有疾。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君之病在肌肤。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君之病在肠胃。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">况君前途尚可。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·自新》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">落花时节又逢君。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江南逢李龟年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:诸君;李君;王君;祝君早安;请君光临;君子不羞当面(君子不以当面把话说清楚为羞)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 夫妇之间的尊称 <span class="encs">[sir;husband;wife]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君行虽不远，守边赴</span><span class="diczx2">河阳</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span
