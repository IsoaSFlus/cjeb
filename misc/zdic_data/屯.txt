                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>屯</strong> <span class="dicpy">tún</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tún.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从屮贯一。屮( <span class="dicpy">chè</span>)，草。一，土地。象草木初生的艰难。①( <span class="dicpy">zhūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhūn.mp3"></a></span> )本义:艰难。②引申义:聚集)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 聚集 <span class="encs">[assemble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屯，聚也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">胜</span><span class="diczx2">广</span>皆为屯长。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·陈胜传》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“人所聚曰屯。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">火与日，吾屯也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·寓言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屯骑罗而星布。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:屯聚(会合，聚集);屯屯(聚集);屯合(聚集);屯行(集中行进);屯萃(聚集);屯结(聚集;集结);屯塞(聚拢堵塞);屯师(集结军队);屯居(聚居)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 戍守，驻扎 <span class="encs">[garrison]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屯大泽乡。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屯于境上。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">屯</span><span class="diczx2">新野</span>。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:屯封(屯守之地);屯堡(军队戍守的保垒);屯卫(派兵驻守护卫);屯籍(登载屯兵的名册);屯夫(守卒);屯列(布防，屯驻);屯次(驻扎);屯防(驻兵防御);屯住(屯驻。驻守);屯泊(驻扎)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 屯田 <span class="encs">[have garrison troops open up wasteland and grow food grain]</span>。如:屯政(有关屯田的事务);屯丁(屯田之人);屯田客(募来屯田的农民);屯弁，屯衙(管理屯田的小吏);屯收(屯田的收益);屯利(屯田之利);屯官(掌管屯田事务的官) </p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 积聚。聚集储存起来 <span class="encs">[store up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蜂屯蚁杂，不可爬搜。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span
