                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>庸</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōng.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>平常，不高明的：平～。～医。～言。～俗。～人。昏～。～主（平庸或昏庸的君主）。～夫。～暗（平凡，愚昧）。～～碌碌（没有志气，没有作为）。</li><li>需要：无～细述。无～讳言。</li><li>岂，怎么：～讵（岂，何以，怎么，亦作“庸遽”）。</li><li>中国唐代一种赋税法：租～调。</li><li>功劳：～绩（功绩）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">佣</span>”，雇佣。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> usual, common, ordinary, mediocre</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> mittelmäßig  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> médiocre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">庸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>庸</strong> <span class="dicpy">yōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。从用，从庚。<span class="yhcs">“庚更”</span>同音，表更换。先做某事，然后更换做别的事。本义:用;需要)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[need;adopt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庸，用也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">畴咨若时登庸。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我生之初，尚无庸。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·兔爰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">齐子</span>庸止。——<span class="smcs">《诗·齐风·南山》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 靖潜庸回。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公十八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庸動长老。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语中》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请始无庸战。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 常与否定副词<span class="yhcs">“无”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“勿”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“弗”</span>连用</p><p><span class="diczx1">无稽之言勿听，弗询之谋勿庸。——<span class="smcs">《书·大禹谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我无庸去，只是教阁下个至良极美的法则。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:无庸讳言;无庸细述;毋庸讳言;庸释(舍弃)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 任用 <span class="encs">[employ]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">名之所在，上之所庸。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《日知录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:庸庸(任用应受任用的人);登庸(选拔重用)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 受雇用，出卖劳动力 <span class="encs">[be hired]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">买庸而决窦。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 如:庸作(受雇而为人劳作);庸仆(佣工、仆役);庸僦(雇佣租赁)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 酬其功劳 <span class="encs">[reward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亲亲故故，庸庸劳劳，仁之杀也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·大畧》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:庸勋(酬劳有功的人)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>庸</strong> <span class="dicpy">yōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 平常 <span
