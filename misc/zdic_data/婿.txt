						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>婿</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>丈夫：夫～。</li>
	<li>女儿、妹妹及其他晚辈的丈夫：女～。妹～。侄～。甥～。贤～。翁～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> son-in-law; husband</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schwiegersohn  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> gendre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">婿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>婿</strong></p>
<p>壻 <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，胥声。或作&ldquo;壻&rdquo;。本义:古时女子称夫为婿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[husband]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">壻，夫也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女子之夫为壻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">壻执雁入。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;昏义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">壻御轮三周。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;有女同车》</span>笺</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">赵</span>有侧室曰 <span class="diczx2">穿</span>， <span class="diczx2">晋君</span>之壻也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十二年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东房少妇婿从军，每听乌啼知夜分。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李瑞</span><span class="smcs">《乌栖曲》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>贵将独有奉车都尉，天子姊婿，爵为通侯，当先降之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若是姻缘早，把风流婿招，少什么美夫妻图画在碧云高。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汤显祖</span><span class="diczx2"><span class="smcs">《牡丹亭&middot;写真》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:夫婿(丈夫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 成年男子的美称 <span class="encs">[an adult male human being]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桓温有英雄之才，愿陛下勿以常人遇之，常婿畜之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;桓温传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 女儿、妹妹或其他晚辈的丈夫 <span class="encs">[son-in-law]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婿固不遣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婿竟不调。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">本来就没有儿子&hellip;</span><span class="diczx2">祥子</span>的确不错，但是提到儿婿两当，还差得多呢!&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">老舍</span><span class="smcs">《骆驼祥子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赘婿(入赘的女婿);女婿(女儿的丈夫)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>婿</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>作女婿;作夫婿 <span class="encs">[become son-in-law]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">况</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>婿于天子，顽嚣嫚戾者独不畏之，又从而杀之耶?&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆龟蒙</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晟</span>请以一子婿 <span class="diczx2">延赏</span>女， <span class="diczx2">延赏</span>不许。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孔平仲</span><span class="smcs">《续世说》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/58FB.svg">                          <p>夫也。从士胥聲。《詩》曰：&ldquo;女也不爽，士貳其行。&rdquo;士者，夫也。讀與細同。婿，壻或从女。穌計切</p>                          </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/58FB_1124.svg" /><span class="swbf">(壻)</span><span class="ywyy">夫也。</span><span class="swdz">夫者，丈夫也，然則壻爲男子之美稱，因以爲女夫之稱。釋親曰：女子子之夫爲壻。</span><span class="ywyy">从士胥。</span><span class="swdz">鉉本有聲字，誤。周禮注、詩箋皆曰：胥，有才知之稱。又曰：胥讀如諝，謂其有才知爲什長。説文言部曰：諝，知也。然則从胥者，从諝之省。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：女也不爽，士貳其行。士者，夫也。</span><span class="swdz">此引衞風而䆁之，明从士之意。</span><span class="ywyy">讀與細同。</span><span class="swdz">穌計切。古音當在十六部。</span></p>
