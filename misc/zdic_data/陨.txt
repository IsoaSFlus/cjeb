                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>陨</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">隕</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǔn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔn.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>坠落：～落。～灭。～星。～石。～铁。</li><li>毁坏。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">殒</span>”，死亡。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fall, slip; let fall; die</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> fallen, absinken ,Meteor  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tomber du ciel</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">陨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>陨</strong></p><p>隕 <span class="dicpy">yǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">fù</span>)，员声。<span class="yhcs">“阜”</span>指土山，与<span class="yhcs">“高下”</span>义有关。本义:从高处掉下，坠落)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fall from the sky or outer space]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陨，从高下也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陨，坠也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陨，落也，堕也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冬十二月陨霜不杀菽。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·内储说上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其黄而陨。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涕既陨之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小弁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">并</span>、 <span class="diczx2">汾</span>乔木，望秋先陨。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">启陨箨。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:陨坠(坠落);陨泗(流泪);陨涕(落泪);陨集(下落聚集)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 丧失，失去 <span class="encs">[lose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天诱其衷，</span><span class="diczx2">成王</span>陨命， <span class="diczx2">穆公</span>是以不克逞志于我。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公十三年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陨子辱矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而陨失其国者。——<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋·内篇问上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:陨获(困迫失志);陨命(丧失生命);陨失(丧失;失落);陨职(失职)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 死 <span class="encs">[die]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">巢</span>陨 <span class="diczx2">诸樊</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十一年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则陨身灭国矣。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说疑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如何不吊，事临垂克，遘疾陨丧!——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:陨殁(死亡);陨丧(死亡);陨谢(死的婉词);陨隧(死亡的婉称);陨国(殉国，为国而死);陨世(逝世)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 毁坏 <span class="encs">[destroy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">世济其美，不陨其名。——<span class="smcs">《史通》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:陨圮(倒塌崩坏);陨绝(毁灭;灭绝);陨覆(覆灭)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a                               隕·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9695.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】于敏切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】羽敏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音殞。【說文】从高下也。【爾雅·釋詁】隕，墜也。【又】隕，磒落也。【註】磒，猶隕也。方俗語有輕重耳。【書·湯誥】慄慄危懼，若將隕于深淵。【易·姤卦】有隕自天。【春秋·莊七年】夜中星隕如雨。【公羊傳】作霣。</p><p>又【禮·儒行】不隕穫於貧賤。【註】隕穫，困迫失志之貌也。</p><p>又同殞。【左傳·襄三十二年】巢隕諸樊。</p><p>又【集韻】王問切，音運。義同。</p><p>又【詩·小雅】心之憂矣，涕旣隕之。【釋文】音蘊。</p><p>又【集韻】于倫切，音筠。義同。【詩·衞風】桑之落矣，其黃而隕。自我徂爾，三歲食貧。【註】隕叶于貧反。</p><p>又【集韻】王權切，音圓。均也。【詩·商頌】幅隕旣長。【傳】隕，均也。【箋】隕，當爲圓。圓謂周也。</p>
