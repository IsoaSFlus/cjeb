                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>汛</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xùn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xùn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>江河定期的涨水：～期。～情。防～。桃花～（桃花盛开时发生的河水暴涨。亦称“桃汛”）。</li><li>洒：～扫（ａ．洒扫；ｂ．扫除，清除）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">讯</span>”：～地（中国清代兵制，凡千总、把总、外委所统率的绿营兵均称“汛”，其驻防巡逻的地区称“汛地”）。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xùn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sprinkle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汛，洒也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，此字经传多误作汛滥之汛。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汛埽反道。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·郊特性》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汛拚席。——<span class="smcs">《管子·弟子职》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">况尽汛扫前圣数千载功业，专用己之私，而能享佑者哉?——<span class="smcs">《文选·扬雄·剧秦美新》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:汛扫(洒扫);汛逐(清扫驱除)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 防守 <span class="encs">[defend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大军汛黄河而角其前，</span><span class="diczx2">荆州</span>下宛、叶而犄其后。——<span class="cino">(2)</span> 明清时称军队驻防地段 <span class="encs">[station]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">三镇各释小嫌，共图大事，速速回汛，听候调谴。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">孔尚任</span><span class="smcs">《桃花扇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又称:汛地</p><p><span class="diczx1">须俟汛报紧急，先以分守之兵，统赴汛地。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李颐</span><span class="smcs">《条陈海防疏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:汛弁(汛地官兵);汛守(汛地防守岗位);汛防(巡逻防守);汛兵(汛地的士兵);汛房(汛地营房)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
