                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>翦</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 初生的羽毛 <span class="encs">[first feather]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翦，羽初生也。一曰矢羽。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王筠</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“翦者谓新生之羽，整齐之状也。”</span> <span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“羽初生如前齐也。前，古之翦字。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓。</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>翦</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 剪整齐 <span class="encs">[cut;trim]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">实始翦商。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·閟宫》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翦，齐也。——<span class="smcs">《释言》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茅茨不翦。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:翦缀(裁剪缝纫);翦发(修剪头发);翦灯(修剪灯芯。后常指夜谈)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 割截;杀戮 <span class="encs">[cut out;kill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不翦其类也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·文王世子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“割截也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:翦刈(铲除);翦草除根(比喻彻底清除，不留后患);翦翎(剪除羽翎。常以喻因受羁缚，才能不得伸展);翦棘(斩除荆棘)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 歼灭 <span class="encs">[wipe out;remove]</span>。如:剪除(消灭);剪翕(剪灭)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 削减 <span class="encs">[reduce;cut down]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋是翦弃。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十四年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“削也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">卫</span>人翦 <span class="diczx2">夏</span>戊。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十一年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“削其爵邑也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其翦以赐诸侯，使臣妾之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“翦，削也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:翦抑(削弱;压制);翦弱(削弱)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
