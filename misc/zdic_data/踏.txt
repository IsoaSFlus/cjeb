						<p>● <strong>踏</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用脚踩：～步。～板。～青（春天到郊外散步。亦称&ldquo;踏春&rdquo;）。～雪。～月。践～。糟～。脚～实地。</li>
	<li>亲自到现场去：～看。～勘。～查。～访。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">踏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>踏</strong> <span class="dicpy">tā</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 落下 <span class="encs">[fall]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蹋顿(跌倒);蹋衍(指斜平的下坡路)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 下垂 <span class="encs">[hang]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蹋翼(耷拉着翅膀。颓丧无所作为的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/踏实">踏实</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/踏踏实实">踏踏实实</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>踏</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，沓( <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:足着地;踩 )</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[step on;tread]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">踏，足著地也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">足踏使坚平。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《齐民要术&middot;种葵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡子圆转不止，便下床以屐齿踏之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;王述传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踏步不前;踏雪(在雪地上行走);把火踏灭;踏踏(马蹄声，也作蹋蹋);踏破菜园(吃素的人，破了荤戒);踏蹑(织布时踩踏织机的踏板)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 边走边唱;打拍 <span class="encs">[beat time]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">到明辛苦无处说，齐声腾踏牵船歌。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《水夫谣》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踏场(按照乐调节拍舞蹈);踏臂(连臂而歌，踏地打节拍);踏蹄(歌舞时以足踏地为节)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 查，勘察 <span class="encs">[survey]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:踏逐(寻求，觅取);踏验(实地勘察);踏灯(元宵节上灯市看灯);踏缉(察访搜捕);踏察(勘察，探测)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 迈步 <span class="encs">[take a step]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:踏罡步斗(道家作法时以特定方位的步子移动);踏踵(漫步);踏屣(犹行步);踏实(行步稳健;落实)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 吞咽 <span class="encs">[swallow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">县官踏飧去，簿吏复登堂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李贺</span><span class="smcs">《感讽五首》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 跟随 <span class="encs">[follow]</span>。</p>
