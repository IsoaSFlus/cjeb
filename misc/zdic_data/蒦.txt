                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蒦</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四">卷四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四&amp;swbs=萑">萑</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/84A6.svg">                          <p>規蒦，商也。从又持萑。一曰視遽皃。一曰蒦，度也。彠，蒦或从尋。尋亦度也。《楚詞》曰：“求矩彠之所同。”乙虢切〖注〗徐鍇曰：“商，度也。萑，善度人禍福也。”</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/84A6_5642.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(蒦)</span><span class="ywyy">規蒦、</span><span class="swdz">逗。</span><span class="ywyy">商也。从又持萑。</span><span class="swdz">規蒦二字葢古語。釋之曰商也。葢毛持萑則恐其奪去。圖所以處之。是曰規蒦。乙虢切。五部。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰視遽皃。</span><span class="swdz">矍下云。一曰視遽皃。蒦與矍形聲皆相似。故此義同。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰蒦、</span><span class="swdz">逗。</span><span class="ywyy">度也。</span><span class="swdz">度、徒故切。漢志曰。寸者、忖也。尺者、蒦也。故蒦爲五度之度。鳥飛起止多有中度者。故雉、蒦皆訓度。度高廣皆曰雉。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/84A6_5643.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(彠)</span><span class="ywyy">蒦或从尋。尋亦度也。</span><span class="swdz">寸部曰。度人之㒳臂爲尋。八尺也。</span><span class="ywyy">楚辭曰。求矩彠之所同。</span><span class="swdz">見離騷。王曰。榘、法也。彠、度也。高注淮南曰。榘、方也。彠、度法也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
