                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>府</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˇ</span></p><ol><li>储藏文书或财物的地方：～库。天～。</li><li>旧时封建贵族和官僚的主宅，泛指一般人的住宅：～邸。～第。王～。～上。</li><li>中国唐代至清代的行政区域名，等级在县和省之间：开封～。～尹。～治。～试。首～。</li><li>国家行政机关：政～。官～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">腑</span>”，脏腑。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">府</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>府</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，表示与房屋有关，付声。本义:府库，府藏。古时国家收藏文书或财物的地方)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[repository]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">府，文书藏也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">开府库，出币帛。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·时则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在府言府。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">籍吏民，封府库，而待将军。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以问公卿，亦以为虚费府帑。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·匈奴传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遇有水旱疾疫，则开仓廪悉府库以赈之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:府室(国家书库);府库(官府储存财物、兵甲的仓库);府实(府库中的财物);府帑(国库;国库所藏的金帛);府钱(府库的钱财)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代管理财货或文书的官吏 <span class="encs">[financial or documental official]</span>。如:府吏(古时管理财货文书出纳的小吏)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 官署。汉至南北朝多指高级官员及诸王治事之所 <span class="encs">[government agency;seat of government]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以八法治官府。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“百官所居曰府。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宫中府中，俱为一体。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缙绅大夫士萃于左丞相府，莫知计所出。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《< 指南录>后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:府上(官府，衙门;今尊称对方家宅或家乡);府厅(官署的厅堂);府朝(官署;王府);府幕(府署的幕僚)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 第宅。地方行政长官或达官贵人的官邸 <span class="encs">[mansion;offlcial residence]</span>。如:王府(有王爵封号的人的住宅);府寺(古代公卿的官舍);府舍(官舍，官邸)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 唐朝至清朝的行政区划，比县高一级 <span class="encs">[prefecture]</span>。如:常德府，济南府;府元(府试头名);府庠(州府里的学校)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 对别人住宅的敬称 <span class="encs">[your home]</span>。如:造府请教</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“腑”</span>。脏腑 <span class="encs">[internal organs of human body;viscera]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所谓上喘而为水者，阴气下而复上，上则邪客于藏府间，故为水也。——<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">五藏已伤，六府不通。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:六府(中医指胃、小肠、大肠、膀胱、胆和三焦)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>府</strong> <span class="dicpy">fǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
