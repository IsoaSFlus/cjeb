                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>薪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄣˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 柴火：～苏（打柴割草）。～尽火传（<span class="dicpy">chuán </span>）（柴火烧完，又引燃了后一根柴，火永远不灭。原指人形骸有尽而精神未死，后亦用以喻思想学问、技艺代代相传）。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>薪</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grass;hay]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薪，荛也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">收秩薪柴。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“大者可析谓之薪。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薪蒸材木。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·委人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“粗者曰薪，细者曰蒸。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以薪以蒸。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·无羊》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">农夫得居装而卖其薪荛。——<span class="smcs">《管子·轻重甲》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">某有负薪之忧。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毁伤其薪木。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顾野有麦场，场主积薪其中。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:薪火(柴火);薪桂(薪贵于桂。形容柴火昂贵);薪柴(柴火);薪樵(柴火)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 可以劈开来用的粗大木柴 <span class="encs">[firewood;faggot;fuel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明足察秋毫之末，而不见舆薪。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卖炭翁，伐薪烧炭南山中。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">负薪刍助之。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·唐纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抱薪救火。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:薪采(采樵，砍柴);薪桂(薪贵于桂。比喻物价昂贵);薪蒸(木材);薪火相传(木柴虽有烧尽的时候，但火却可以传衍不息。比喻为师徒以道术递相传授不绝，或种族血统及文化精神之绵衍无尽)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 薪水、薪金的省称 <span class="encs">[salary]</span>。又如:加薪;薪工(薪金，工资);薪津(薪金);薪给</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>薪</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 取以为薪;打柴 <span class="encs">[collect firewood]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今遣此力，助汝薪水之劳。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="diczx2">肖统</span><span class="smcs">《陶渊明传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
