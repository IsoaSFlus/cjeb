                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>葆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄠˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>草茂盛的样子，草木丛生的样子：&ldquo;头如蓬～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古书上说的一种菜。</li>
	<li>车盖：羽～。</li>
	<li>藏，蔽：～光（隐蔽其光不让人知道，喻才智藏而不露）。</li>
	<li>保持：～真。永～青春。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">宝</span>&rdquo;，珍贵。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">保</span>&rdquo;，保护。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> reserve, preserve; conceal</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> conserver,garder,luxuriant,buissonneux,touffu,dense</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>葆</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。保声。本义:草木茂盛的样子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(grass) clustered;luxuriant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">葆，草盛貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">头如蓬葆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;燕刺王旦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;宝&rdquo;。珍贵 <span class="encs">[precious;rare;valuable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子之葆龟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;乐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天之降葆命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;周公世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">取而保祠之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;留侯世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:葆龟(即宝龟。占卜的用具);葆爱(珍爱;珍视)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>葆</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 丛生的枝、芽 <span class="encs">[teller]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">得时之稻，大本而茎葆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 车盖 <span class="encs">[hood]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垂翟葆，建羽旗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:葆车(有五彩羽毛编成车盖的坐车);葆羽(仪仗名。以鸟羽为饰)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>葆</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;保&rdquo;。保持;保护;守卫 <span class="encs">[keep;defend;guard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小城不自守通者，尽葆其老弱粟米畜产。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;号令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人貌而天，虚缘而葆真。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;田子方》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五粟之土，干而不格，湛而不泽，无高下葆泽以处，是谓粟土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;地员》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:永葆青春</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">丛生的草。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书&middot;卷六三&middot;武五子传&middot;燕剌王刘旦传》：「当此之时，头如蓬葆。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;颜师古&middot;注：「草丛生曰：『葆』。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">鼓上的装饰物。</span><span class="gc_yy">《三国演义&middot;第四九回》：「竿尖上用鸡羽为葆，以招风信。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">通「堡」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《吕氏春秋&middot;慎行论&middot;疑似》：「与诸侯约，为高葆，祷于王路。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如明代有葆光先。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">隐藏、掩蔽。</span><span class="gc_yy">《管子&middot;水地》：「故其民谄谀葆诈，巧佞而好利。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">守。</span><span class="gc_yy">《墨子&middot;非攻》：「济三江五湖，而葆之会稽。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「保」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">保持。通「保」。参见「<a href='/hans/葆真' target=_blank>葆真</a> 」条。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              葆·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8446.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】補抱切，音保。【前漢&middot;燕刺王旦傳】頭如蓬葆。【註】葆，草木叢生之貌。</p>
<p>又【韻會】梓上苗也。【字彙補】關中謂桑楡孽生曰葆。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;雜記&middot;羽葆疏】葆謂蓋也。【張衡&middot;西京賦】垂翟葆。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;禮器】不樂葆大。【註】葆之言褒也。</p>
<p>又【素問】治數之道，從容之葆。【註】葆，平也。</p>
<p>又藏也。【莊子&middot;齊物論】此之謂葆光。</p>
<p>又【韻會】旅葆，野菽，實可食。【史記&middot;天官書】觜觿爲虎首，主葆旅事。【晉灼曰】葆，菜也。野生曰旅。</p>
<p>又通褓。【史記&middot;留侯世家】成王少在襁葆之中。【註】葆，小兒被也。</p>
<p>又通寶。【史記&middot;留侯世家】見穀城山下黃石，取而葆祠之。【註】《史記》珍寶字皆作葆。</p>
<p>又與堡通。【史記&middot;匈奴傳】侵盜上郡葆塞。</p>
<p>又【集韻】博毛切，音褒。廣也。</p>
