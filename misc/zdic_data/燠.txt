                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>燠</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，奥( <span class="dicpy">yu&egrave;</span>)声。本义:温暖)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[warm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">燠，热在中也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">燠， 煖也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曰燠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span>。传:&ldquo;火气也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安且燠兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;无衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问衣燠寒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸟何燠之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;天问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">八，庶征:曰雨，曰暘，曰燠，曰寒，曰风，曰时。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:燠日(温暖的太阳);燠沐(温暖湿润)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 热 <span class="encs">[hot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若乃炎风之燠，夏日之长，寻头扑面，入袖穿裳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三宝太监西洋记通俗演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:燠疾(热症);燠暑(炎热);燠蒸(闷热如蒸);燠热(炎热;闷热)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>

<p>◎ <strong>燠</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>使温暖;使热 <span class="encs">[warm]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">燠</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集中" target="_blank">巳集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集中&amp;bs=火" target="_blank">火</a>】
                              燠·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=17" target="_blank">17</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/71E0.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕灹<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24230.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤈰</span>【廣韻】於六切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】乙六切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音郁。【說文】熱在中也。【爾雅&middot;釋言】燠煖也。【註】今江東通言燠。【書&middot;洪範】曰燠曰寒。【前漢&middot;王褒傳】不苦盛暑之鬱燠。</p>
<p>又通作奧。【詩&middot;唐風】不如子之衣，安且燠兮。【註】奧本又作燠。</p>
<p>又【小雅】日月方奧。【禮&middot;內則】問衣燠寒。【註】燠本又作奧。◎按漢書五行志李尋王莽傳，燠皆作奧。</p>
<p>又與隩同。【書&middot;堯典】厥民隩。【史記&middot;堯本紀】作其民燠。【集韻】或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/232C9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣋉</span>。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】烏到切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】於到切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音奧。義同。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】燠釜以水添釜。</p>
<p>又【集韻】威遇切，音嫗。燠休，痛念聲。或作噢。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【類篇】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>於求切，音憂。義同。【左傳&middot;昭三年】民人痛疾，而或燠休之。【註】燠休，痛念之聲。【疏】燠，厚也。休，美也。〇按釋文休，虛喩反。燠，徐音憂。</p>
<p>又於到反。</p>
<p>又乙六反。三音皆可讀。【集韻】或亦作奧。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>烏皓切，音襖。【廣韻】甚熱也。</p>
