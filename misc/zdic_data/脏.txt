                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">脏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>脏</strong></p>
<p>髒 <span class="dicpy">zāng</span></p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 玷污，不干净或不纯洁 <span class="encs">[dirty;filthy;smudged]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:脏衣服;脏地毯</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="encs">[指语言]</span>不文明，骂人 <span class="encs">[bad]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:责备这个孩子说脏话</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>脏</strong></p>
<p>髒 <span class="dicpy">zāng</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 弄污 <span class="encs">[smear;stain;defile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">咱一来是为行好，二来也怕脏了我的店。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">z&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脏话">脏话</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脏乱">脏乱</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脏煤">脏煤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脏水">脏水</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脏土">脏土</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脏污">脏污</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脏字">脏字</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>脏</strong></p>
<p>臟 <span class="dicpy">z&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。左形，右声。本义:中医学以心、肝、脾、肺、肾为五脏。后因以为身体内脏的通称)同本义 <span class="encs">[viscus( pl. viscera);internal organs of luman body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脏，五脏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &ldquo;脏的本字是藏。脏是&ldquo;藏&rdquo;的后起分别字。如:脏躁症(中医病名。主要症为神躁不宁，哭笑无常);脏象(指人体脏腑正常机能及发生病态变化时反映于外的征象);内脏;五脏六腑</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zāng</span></p>

<p>又或作臧。【前漢&middot;藝文志】五臧六府。◎按臧、藏、臟一字。後人加艸，又加肉。</p>
