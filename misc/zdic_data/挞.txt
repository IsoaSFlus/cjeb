                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>挞</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">撻</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tà  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tà.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄚˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 用鞭棍等打人：鞭～。大张～伐（a.使用武力大规模征讨；b.指对人进行声讨或攻击）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">挞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>挞</strong></p><p>撻 <span class="dicpy">tà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，达声。本义:用鞭子或棍子打)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[whip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挞，乡饮酒，罚不敬，挞其背也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌其比觵挞罚之事。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·闾胥》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“扑也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而挞其怠慢者。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小胥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而挞之流血。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斫挞无伤痛。——<span class="smcs">《列子·黄帝》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鞭挞黎庶令人悲。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">高适</span><span class="smcs">《封丘作》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:鞭挞(鞭打);挞市(在市朝上受到鞭打的刑罚);挞辱(鞭打侮辱);挞笞(用鞭和竹板子打);挞讯(拷问);挞通(打通);挞架(打架)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为攻打 <span class="encs">[attack]</span>。如:挞责(攻击指责);挞煞(结局)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 拍打 <span class="encs">[pat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">便脱下鞋底，将字迹挞没了。——<span class="smcs">《古今小说》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/挞伐">挞伐</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/64BB.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28608.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨘈</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22F5E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢽞</span>【唐韻】【集韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>他達切，音闥。打也，抶也。【書·益稷】撻以記之。</p><p>又【說命】若撻于市。【周禮·地官·閭胥】凡事，掌其比觵撻罰之事。【註】撻，扑也。【疏】有失禮，輕者以觵酒罰之，重者以楚撻之。【儀禮·鄉飲酒禮】罰不敬，撻其背。</p><p>又疾也。【詩·商頌】撻彼殷武。【疏】撻是速疾之意。【釋文】韓詩云：達也。</p><p>又弣側矢道也，以韋爲之。【儀禮·士喪禮】設依撻焉。【註】今文撻爲銛。</p><p>又【崔豹·古今註】蛺蝶，江東呼爲撻末。本从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28536.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨔶</span>。【集韻】或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22E82.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢺂</span>。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28608.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨘈</span>原字誤从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26352.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦍒</span>作。</p>

                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/64BB.svg">                          <p>鄉飲酒，罰不敬，撻其背。从手達聲。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28608.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨘈</span>，古文撻。《周書》曰：“<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28608.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨘈</span>以記之。”他達切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">鄉㱃酒罰不敬。撻其背。</span>周禮閭胥。凡事掌其比。觵撻罰之事。故書或言觵撻之罰事。鄭云。觵撻者、失禮之罰。觵用酒。其爵以兕角爲之。撻、扑也。按鄭但云失禮。許必系之鄉飲酒者、禮莫大於此。惟此可登時行觵撻也。<span class="jieshi">从手。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28536.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨔶</span>聲。</span>他<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28536.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨔶</span>切。十五部。                          </div>
