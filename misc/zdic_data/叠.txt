						<p>● <strong>叠</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">di&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>重复，累积：重（<span class="dicpy">chǒng </span>）～。层见～出。～罗汉。～韵。～嶂。～翠。</li>
	<li>摺：摺～。铺床～被。</li>
	<li>乐曲的重复演奏：阳关三～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">叠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叠</strong></p>
<p>曡、疊 <span class="dicpy">di&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，象物体叠放在&ldquo;俎&rdquo;上。本义:重叠)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pile(up);overlap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曡，重也，积也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阳始出奥舒、曡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;太元乐》</span>。注:&ldquo;积也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽累叶百叠，而富强相继。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;左思&middot;吴都赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叠石为山;叠暴(显露;指人激动时青筋鼓出来);叠字(单字的重叠);叠雪(重叠飞扬的雪花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 折，折叠，交叠，指用对折或交叠的方法减少长度或宽度 <span class="encs">[fold]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叠被子;叠衣服</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 振作 <span class="encs">[bestir]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">只好叠着精神，稳着步儿，探着腰儿，挺着胸儿&hellip;望前而行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《镜花缘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 轻击 <span class="encs">[鼓]</span> <span class="encs">[knock at]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叠鼓(轻轻地连续击鼓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 惧怕 <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫不震叠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;时迈》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>叠</strong> <span class="dicpy">di&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>连续;接连 <span class="encs">[on end;in succession]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:叠次(迭次。一次又一次);叠连(重复;连接);叠接(连续收到)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>叠</strong> <span class="dicpy">di&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 堆在一起或逐个堆放的大量东西 <span class="encs">[pile]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:一叠纸;一叠钞票</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一簇结合着的但并非捆在一起的相似的条状物 <span class="encs">[sheaf]</span>。</p>
