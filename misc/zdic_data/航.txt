                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>航</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〈书〉船。</li>
	<li>行船或飞行：～海。～空。～天。～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）。～班。民～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sail; navigate; ship, boat</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Luftfahrt  (S)​,mit dem Schiff fahren, fliegen  (V)​,Schiff, Schifffahrt  (V)​,Navigation, navigieren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bateau,vaisseau,naviguer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">航</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>航</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 船;方舟 <span class="encs">[boat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">航，船也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">航，方舟也。本作斻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大者以为舟航柱梁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;主术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">譬临河而无航。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长鲸吞航。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;左思&middot;吴都赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:航人(船夫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 两船相并，因指连船而成的浮桥 <span class="encs">[pontoon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:航桥(渡桥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 航空，飞机的飞行活动 <span class="encs">[aviation]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:民航</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>航</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 船行;航行 <span class="encs">[navigate]</span>。今飞机、飞船等飞行器在空中飞行也称航。如:航空;宇航;航业(船舶往来一港口输送货物或旅客的营业)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 渡过 <span class="encs">[cross]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">峡甚逼仄，故一木航之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">譬临河而无航。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长鲸吞航。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;左思&middot;吴都赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:航人(船夫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 两船相并，因指连船而成的浮桥 <span class="encs">[pontoon]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:航桥(渡桥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 航空，飞机的飞行活动 <span class="encs">[aviation]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:民航</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>航</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 船行;航行 <span class="encs">[navigate]</span>。今飞机、飞船等飞行器在空中飞行也称航。如:航空;宇航;航业(船舶往来一港口输送货物或旅客的营业)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 渡过 <span class="encs">[cross]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">峡甚逼仄，故一木航之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记》</span></span></p>

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/230DA_9494.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">方舟也。</span><span class="swdz">舟字葢衍。衞風：一葦杭之。毛曰：杭，渡也。杭卽<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>字，計謂一葦可以爲之舟也。舟所以渡，故謂渡爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>。始皇臨浙江，水波惡，乃西百二十里從狹中渡，其地因有餘杭縣。杜篤論都賦，造舟於渭，北杭涇流。章懷後漢書作北<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>。注云：說文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>字在方部。今流俗不解。遂與杭字相亂者，誤也。是說誠然。然<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>之作杭久矣。章懷偶一正之，而不能盡正也。李南傳：向度宛陵浦里，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>馬踠足，亦係章懷改杭爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>。而地理、郡國二志餘杭縣未之或改也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣃚</span>亦作航。方言曰：舟或謂之航，杭者說文或抗字。</span><span class="ywyy">从方亢聲。</span><span class="swdz">胡郞切。十部。</span><span class="ywyy">禮：天子造舟，諸侯維舟，大夫方舟，士特舟。</span><span class="swdz">大雅詩傳及釋水同。李巡曰：比其舟而渡曰造舟，中央左右相維持曰維舟。倂兩船曰方舟。一舟曰特舟。孫炎曰：造舟，比舟爲梁也。維舟，連四舟也。釋水及公羊傳注此下又有庶人乘泭句。</span></p>                          </div>
