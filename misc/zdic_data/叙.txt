                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>叙</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>述说：～说。～事。～话。～亲。～旧。</li>	<li>评议等级次第：～功。～奖。～用（分级进用）。～擢（分级提拔，授予宜职）。</li>	<li>〈书〉同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">序</span>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> express, state, relate, narrate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> erzählen, begleitend kommentieren  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> raconter,narrer,parler,bavarder</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">叙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叙</strong></p>
<p>敘、敍 <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，余声。本义:秩序，次序)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(强调一个挨一个的序列) <span class="encs">[order]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敘，次弟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。经传亦以序为之。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百揆时敘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行其秩敘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;宫伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以官府之六叙正群吏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时不失其叙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叙齿(序齿);叙齿而坐(按年齿大小的顺序入座)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同&ldquo;序&rdquo;。书籍的序言(早期写作&ldquo;叙&rdquo;，后多写作&ldquo;序&rdquo;) <span class="encs">[preface]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如: <span class="diczx2">许慎</span><span class="smcs">《说文解字&middot;叙》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>叙</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 依次序排列 <span class="encs">[order]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惇叙九族。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;皋陶谟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以敘其财。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 叙述，述说 <span class="encs">[tell;narrate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纪言以叙之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自叙少小时欢乐事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王乃具叙</span><span class="diczx2">宣王</span>创业之始。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;尤悔》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畅叙幽情。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王羲之</span><span class="smcs">《兰亭集序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:自叙;追叙;倒叙;插叙;铺叙;补叙;叙事;叙心(抒怀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 谈话 <span class="encs">[talk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大家散了，</span><span class="diczx2">王夫人</span>姊妹不免又叙了半夜的话儿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:叙意(表达心意);叙觏(会面叙谈);叙会(会面叙谈)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 记述，著述 <span class="encs">[narrate]</span></p>
