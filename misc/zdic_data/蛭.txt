                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蛭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔水～〕环节动物，身体长形，稍扁，墨绿色，尾端有吸盘，雌雄同体生活在池沼或水田中，能吸人畜的血。唾液中含有水蛭素，医学上能发挥抗凝血作用。虫体经干燥炮制后入中药。俗称“蚂蟥”“马鳖”。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蛭</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 蚂蟥。环节动物。体一般长而扁平，略似蚯蚓，前后各有一个吸盘。生活在淡水或湿润处，能吸人畜的血 <span class="encs">[leech]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蛭，虮也。从虫，至声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">楚惠王</span>食寒菹而得蛭，遂吞之，久病心腹之疾皆愈。——<span class="smcs">《贾子春秋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蛭之性食血，</span><span class="diczx2">惠王</span>殆有积血也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·福虚》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弥六合，泽万物，而虾与蛭不离尺水。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《晋问》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蛭虮(水蛭);蛭蝚(水蛭。一名至掌);蛭类(动物学名词)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 小山丘。通<span class="yhcs">“垤”</span> <span class="encs">[little mound]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人莫迹于山，而迹于蛭。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·人间》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
