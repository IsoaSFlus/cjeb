                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厚</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>厚</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hòu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hòu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄡˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>扁平物体上下两个面的距离：～度。～薄（厚度）。</li>    <li>扁平物体上下两个面距离较大的，与“<span class="diczx3">薄</span>”相对：～纸。～重（<span class="dicpy">zhòng </span>）（ａ．又厚又重；ｂ．丰厚；ｃ．厚道而庄重）。</li>    <li>深，重（<span class="dicpy">zhòng </span>），浓，多：～望。</li>    <li>不刻薄，待人好：～道（诚恳，宽容，“<span class="diczx3">道</span>”读轻声）。宽～。</li>    <li>重视，推崇：～此薄彼。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>厚</strong> <span class="dicpy">hòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从厂( <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>)，表示与山石有关。本义:地壳厚。与<span class="yhcs">“薄”</span>相对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(of the earth’s crust)thick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谓地盖厚。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·正月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穷高极远而测深厚。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“深厚，山川也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厚将崩。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见于厚土。——<span class="smcs">《素问·王常政大论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:厚土(覆土厚);厚地(大地);厚坤(大坤);厚厚(大地)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 扁平物体上下两面之间距离大的。 与<span class="yhcs">“薄”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[thick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刀刃者无厚。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·养生主》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以无厚入有间。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:厚木板;厚嘴唇;厚膊(厚的肉干);厚皮(脸皮厚);厚大(又厚又大);厚皮馒头(比喻不切题的文章、言论);厚厚(很厚)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[large;great]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不厚其栋。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“大也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:厚幸(大幸);厚福(大福);厚德(大德)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 丰厚 <span class="encs">[rich and generous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">告之者其赏厚而信。——<span                     <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>厚</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄡˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>hòu <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2745.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">扁平物体上下之间距离较大的。与「薄」相对。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>厚</b>纸」、「<b>厚</b>唇」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·白居易〈新制布裘〉诗：「布重绵且<b>厚</b>，为裘有余温。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·李贺〈苦昼短〉诗：「吾不识青天高、黄地<b>厚</b>，唯见月寒日暖，来煎人寿。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">多、大。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「厚利」、「厚礼」、「厚望」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·元稹《崔莺莺传》：「兄之恩，活我之家，<b>厚</b>矣。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·钱公辅〈义田记〉：「公虽位充禄<b>厚</b>，而贫终其身。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">浓、深。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「酒味<b>厚</b>」、「深情厚谊」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《仪礼·士冠礼》：「甘醴惟<b>厚</b>，嘉荐令芳。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">不刻薄的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「仁<b>厚</b>」、「宽<b>厚</b>」、「憨<b>厚</b>」、「忠厚老实」。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">扁平物体表面与底面间的距离。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「这块木板约有二寸<b>厚</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《礼记·檀弓上》：「天子之棺四重，水兕革棺被之，其<b>厚</b>三寸。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">重视、推崇。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「厚古薄今」、「厚此薄彼」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷五·秦本纪》：「遂复三人官秩如故，愈益<b>厚</b>之。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">副</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">优待。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「厚待」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《礼记·中庸》：「<b>厚</b>往而薄来，所以怀诸侯也。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
