                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">太</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>太</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>过于：～长。</li>
	<li>极端，最：～甚。～平。</li>
	<li>高，大：～空。～学。</li>
	<li>很：不～好。</li>
	<li>身分最高或辈分更高的：～老伯。～夫人（旧时尊称别人的母亲）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> very, too, much; big; extreme</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> allzu, äußerst, übermäßig, zu sehr (Adj)​,ältest, ranghöchster (Adj)​,höchst, allergrößt (Adj)​,sehr, außerordentlich (Adj)​,Tai (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> trop,très,extrêmement</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">太</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>太</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。古作&ldquo;大&rdquo;，后语音分化，在&ldquo;大&rdquo;字下添加符号，成指事字。本义:过于)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 极大。古作&ldquo;大&rdquo;，也作&ldquo;泰&rdquo;。凡言大而以为形容未尽，则作太 <span class="encs">[the greatest;the highest;maximal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太，大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂一》</span></span><span class="diczx2">段</span>曰:&ldquo;后世还言，而以为形容未尽，则作太。如大宰俗作太宰。大子俗作太子，周大王俗作太王是也&rdquo;。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太亦大也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;五行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">易有太极。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span>。注:&ldquo;大极者。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王入太室裸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洛诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太上贵德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饮而不损兹曰太。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;樊准传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:太虚幻境(天上虚幻的境界);太清(天空);太乙(古代形成天地万物的元气);太极(指整个世界的总和);太原;太侈(骄泰奢侈);太无(空旷虚无之境)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 身分最高或辈数更高的 <span class="encs">[the more or most senior]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太史。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;光武纪》</span>。注:&ldquo;史官之长也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:太夫人;太老师;太老伯;太老爷;太母(祖母);太后(帝王的母亲);太翁(曾祖父)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 大的，广大的 <span class="encs">[great]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太牢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公六年》</span>。疏:&ldquo;牢之大者。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:太仓(京城里贮粮的大仓库);太息(亦作&ldquo;大息&rdquo;。长长的叹息)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代记数有所超过称&ldquo;太&rdquo; <span class="encs">[greater]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>有天下太半，而诸侯皆附之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 安宁的，秩序正常的。也作&ldquo;泰&rdquo; <span class="encs">[peaceful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不乱离，不知太平之难。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">魏源</span><span class="smcs">《默觚上》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 亨通;顺利 <span class="encs">[smooth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命险太甚靡常，道离隆而匪易。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆云</span><span class="smcs">《九愍》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 与古代皇家、官家有关的 <span class="encs">[about emperor and official]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:太尊(明清时对府的长官知府的尊称);太府(原为官名，亦指掌管库藏财物的官府);太常卿(汉景帝中元六年改秦奉常为太常，为九卿之一，司礼乐郊庙社稷事宜。历代相沿，清末始废)</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;台&rdquo;。泰阶，星名，即三台。又比喻三公 <span class="encs">[one of three stars in Ursa Major;one of the three highest dignitaries of state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以玉衡正而太阶平也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惜乎不登太阶，以尹天下。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孔融</span><span class="smcs">《张俭碑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抚剑西南望，思欲赴太山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《杂诗六》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>太</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 过于;过分 <span class="encs">[too]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太区区。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言其太隘。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又</p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:太半(大半，过半);太甚(太过);会议不要开得太长;屋里太闷热了，你出去凉凉吧;我对这个问题太没有认识了;他们待我太好了;这个问题确实是太重要了</span></p>

<p>又【集韻】他達切，音獺。太末，漢縣名。在會稽西南。　亦作太。</p>
