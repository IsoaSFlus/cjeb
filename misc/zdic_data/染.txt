                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">染</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>染</strong> <span class="dicpy">rǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，杂声。一说从木、从水、从九。会意。古染料多来源于植物，故从木;染料须加工成液体，故从水;染须反复进行，故从九。本义:使布帛等物着色)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dye]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">染，以缯为色也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌染草。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·序官》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“兰ň象斗之属，掌染草，掌以春秋敛染草之物。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:染博士(染色的工匠);染服(僧侣所穿的缁衣。因缁衣由黑色染成，故称);染户(操染色业的人家);染茜(染成大红色);染采(将织物染成彩色);染事(染色的事务);染网(用牲畜血等染网);一股暖流染红了她的两颊;染指甲;染头发</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 传染，感染 <span class="encs">[catch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此所以染者众也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:染疾(患病);染渍(感染，传染);染惹(感染，沾上);染恙(染病，传染);染薰(沾染香气);染上了流感</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 渲染 <span class="encs">[apply colors to a drawing]</span>。如:染写(渲染描绘);染渲(用水墨或淡彩烘染画面);染画(绘画);染削(润色削减);染翰(以笔蘸墨);染毫(濡墨挥笔)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 熏染;影响 <span class="encs">[influence]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">舜</span>染于 <span class="diczx2">许由</span>、 <span class="diczx2">伯阳</span>。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·当染》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渐染砥砺，几乎道真。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《与吕道州温论非国语书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:染尚(濡染崇尚);染化(薰陶教化);染神乱志(思想受到迷惑扰乱);染风习俗(受风俗影响而有所习染);染丝之变(比喻受环境影响而变得不同);染惑(外物的影响和迷惑);染上不良习惯;染习(习染)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 污染，沾染 <span class="encs">[be contaminated by]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予独爱莲之出淤泥而不染。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:染染手儿(沾点光);染戕(血染戈矛);染洁(操行受到污染);染痘</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> <span class="encs">[在书法时]</span> 落笔 <span class="encs">[make strokes]</span>。如:染毫(染翰。沾笔书写)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 连累;牵连 <span class="encs">[implicate]</span>。如:染逮(沾污连累);染涉(参与，涉足);染累(染逮。牵连，连累);染惹(沾染;牵扯)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>染</strong> <span class="dicpy">rǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>豆豉酱 <span class="encs">[fermented and seasoned soybeans in paste form]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是具染而已。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高诱</span>注:<span
