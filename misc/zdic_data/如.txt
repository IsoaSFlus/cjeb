                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>如</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">rú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rú.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>依照顺从：～愿。～意。～法炮制。</li><li>像，相似，同什么一样：～此。～是。～同。～故。～初。游人～织。</li><li>比得上，及：百闻不～一见。自叹弗～。</li><li>到，往：～厕。</li><li>假若，假设：～果。～若。假～。</li><li>奈，怎么：～何。不能正其身，～正人何？</li><li>与，和：“公～大夫入”。</li><li>或者：“方六七十，～五六十”。</li><li>用在形容词后，表示动作或事物的状态：突～其来。</li><li>表示举例：例～。</li><li>应当：“若知不能，则～无出”。</li><li>〔～月〕农历二月的别称。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">如</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>如</strong> <span class="dicpy">rú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从女，从口。本义:遵从，依照)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如，从随也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，此字疑从女，若省声。女子从人者也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有律以如己也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“如，从也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所不与舅氏同心者，有如白水!——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">犬皆如人意。——<span class="smcs">《柳宗元《三戒》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:如命(遵从命令);如志(随顺意愿;实现志愿);如令(从令，遵令)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 好像， 如同 <span class="encs">[like;as if]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日初出大如车盖。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">状貌如妇人女子。——<span class="smcs">《史记·留侯世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两狼之并驱如故。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:如皋雉(比喻男子以才华博得女子青睐);如许(似这般);如今晚儿(现在);如许豚犊(这样的不肖之子);如应如响(十分灵验)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 比得上，及 <span class="encs">[can be compared with]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不如无生。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·苕之华》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窥镜而自视，又弗如远甚。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣之壮也，犹不如人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:我不如他</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 去，往 <span class="encs">[go]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如，往也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坐须臾，</span><span class="diczx2">沛公</span>起如厕。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以一</span><span class="diczx2">仪</span>而当汉中地，臣请往如 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如</span><span class="diczx2">扬州</span>过 <span class="diczx2">瓜洲</span><span class="diczx2">扬子桥</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 遭遇，际遇 <span class="encs">[meet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">淮阳</span><span class="diczx2">叶生</span>者，失其名字。文章词赋，冠绝当时;而所如不偶，因于名场。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 表示举例 <span class="encs">[for instance;for example;such as]</span>。如:大文学家、大诗人，如李白、杜甫;工人都很勤劳，如挖土</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 相敌;抵挡 <span class="encs">[resist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>之不足如 <span class="diczx2">梁</span>也，寡人知之矣。——<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 应当 <span class="encs">[should]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君若爱司马，则如亡。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>如</strong> <span class="dicpy">rú</span> <span
