                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>积</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">積</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>聚集：～少成多。处心～虑。～储。～愤。～郁。～怨。～愿。～累（<span class="dicpy">lěi </span>）。～攒。</li>
	<li>数学上指乘法运算的得数：～数。乘～。体～。容～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> accumulate, store up, amass</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufhäufen (S)​,ansammeln (V)​,Multiplikation (Math)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> accumuler,amasser</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">积</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>积</strong></p>
<p>積 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从禾，责声。从&ldquo;禾&rdquo;，表示与农作物有关。本义:堆积谷物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[amass;store up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积，聚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，禾谷之聚曰积。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令野修道委积。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span>。注:&ldquo;少曰委，多曰积。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌邦之委积。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;遗人》</span>。疏:&ldquo;三十里言委，五十里言积。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故人主积其食，守其用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;错币》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:积谷(存积米谷以备灾荒);积居(聚积;囤积);积仓(贮存谷物的粮仓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 累积;堆叠 <span class="encs">[accumulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积土成山，风雨兴焉。(积土成为高山，风雨就能从山里兴起，即指积土成山能使气侯发生变化而兴风作雨。兴，起，产生。焉，于是，从这里。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积水成渊。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积薪其中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转视积薪后。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大风扬积雪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积善成德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能已积热。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祖父积子孙弃者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:积石(堆聚的石头);积委(累聚，储存);积威(累积而成的威势);积重(财物的累聚、蓄积);积气(积聚的气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 蕴积，积蓄 <span class="encs">[hold in store;be latent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">商贾以币之变多积货逐利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平准书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃积乃仓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;公刘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:积中(蕴积于心中);积伐(蕴蓄才能并自我夸耀);积志(蓄志，夙愿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 滞留<span class="encs">[stagnate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天道运而无所积。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;无道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:积污(积滞污秽);积塞(积滞堵塞);积抱(郁积的怀抱);积忿(忿恨郁积于心);积怒</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 累计 <span class="encs">[add up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积于今。(算到现在。积，累计。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>积</strong></p>
<p>積 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指贮积起来的钱物等 <span class="encs">[accumulated]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主国五积。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共其牢礼积膳之牛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;牢人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居则具一日之积。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span>。注:&ldquo;刍米禾薪。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">完者，使守积。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">畜积足恃。(积，动词用作名词，积蓄的东西。蓄同积。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:积陈(蓄积陈粮);积滞(滞积的水流);积实(指谷粟财货等);积委(指积贮的财物);积苏(聚积的柴草)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 中医指儿童消化不良的病 <span class="encs">[indigestion]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:积痞(中医名词。指体腔内可以摸得到的硬块);这孩子有积了;奶积</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 乘积的简称，即由两个或两个以上的数或量相乘所得出的数或量 <span class="encs">[product]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:求积</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;襀&rdquo;。衣裙的褶子 <span class="encs">[wrinkles of the clothing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">襞积褰绉，纡徐委曲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美襞积以酷烈兮，允麈邈而难。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张衡&middot;思玄赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;绩&rdquo;。功劳 <span class="encs">[achievement;merit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">积厚者流泽广，积薄者流泽狭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;礼论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">考积幽穷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉&middot;北海相景君碑》</span></span></p>
