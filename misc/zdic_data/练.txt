                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>练</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">練</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>白绢：素～。江平如～。</li>
	<li>把生丝、麻或布帛煮熟，使柔软洁白：～漂（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">漂</span>&rdquo;，漂白）。</li>
	<li>反复学习，多次操作：～习。～笔。～操。训～。～功。</li>
	<li>经验多，精熟：老～。熟～。干（<span class="dicpy">g&agrave;n </span>）～。～达（阅历多而通达人情世故）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">练</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>练</strong></p>
<p>練 <span class="dicpy">li&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸(<span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，表示与线丝有关，柬声。本义:把生丝煮熟;亦指把麻或织品煮得柔而洁白)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[boil and scour raw silk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">练，湅缯也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冬则练帛之中，足以为轻且煖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;辞过》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡染，春暴练。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;染人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主练染，作采色。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:练丝(未染色的熟丝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 练习;操演 <span class="encs">[practice;drain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">练之禾定。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">胡仔</span><span class="smcs">《苕溪渔隐丛话》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:练阅(训练检阅);练句(写作时斟酌遣词用句的工夫。也作炼句);练字(写作时用字技巧的推敲琢磨。也作炼字)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 漂洗;洗涤 <span class="encs">[wash]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:练衲(漂染过的僧衣);练漂(纺织物精练和漂白的总称。即退浆、精练、漂白、丝光等加工过程的统称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 熔炼 <span class="encs">[melt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">方士欲练以求奇药。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:练丹(炼丹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;拣&rdquo;。选择 <span class="encs">[select]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">选士厉兵，简练俊杰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span>。又如:练日(选择日期);练材(精选的优质材料);练择(精选)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 染 <span class="encs">[dye]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">白纱入缁，不练自黑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:练肆(染坊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;柬&rdquo;。选择 <span class="encs">[select]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既不能昭练贤鄙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《潜夫论&middot;本政》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>练</strong></p>
<p>練 <span class="dicpy">li&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 洁白的熟绢 <span class="encs">[white silk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使</span><span class="diczx2">邓廖</span>帅组甲三百被练三千，以侵 <span class="diczx2">吴</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">文绣素练。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;节葬下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">澄江静如练。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">谢朓</span><span class="smcs">《晚登三山远望京邑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:江白如练;练文(形容澄澈的波纹。有如柔软洁白的生丝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代祭名。因古时于父母去世十三月时戴练冠祭于家庙而得名 <span class="encs">[an ancient sacrificial name]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>大夫练而床，礼邪?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;子道》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:练主(丧礼小祥之祭时所立的神主，藏于祖庙以供奏祀);练冠(丧祭所戴的冠);练衣(丧祭时所穿的衣)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Lian River]</span>。在今广东省普宁县至潮阳县一带</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;链&rdquo;。链子 <span class="encs">[chain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如锁上练。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>练</strong></p>
<p>練 <span class="dicpy">li&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 白，素色 <span class="encs">[white]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">墨子</span>见练丝而泣之，为其可以黄，可以墨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说林训》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:练巾(白色的头巾);练衣(白色布衣);练文(比喻像白练飘动般的波纹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为经验多，精熟 <span class="encs">[experienced]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">世事洞明皆学问，人情练达即文章。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:干练(又有才能又有经验);练才(文才练达);练甲(精兵)</span></p>
