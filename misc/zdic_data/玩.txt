						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>玩</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wán.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>游戏：～耍。～笑。～兴（<span class="dicpy">xìng </span>）。～具。～偶。</li><li>戏弄，搬弄：～弄。～狎。～花招儿。</li><li>观赏：～赏。～味。瞻～。～物丧志。</li><li>可供观赏的东西：古～。</li><li>轻视，忽视：～忽职守。～世不恭。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">玩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>玩</strong> <span class="dicpy">wán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从玉，元声。本义:以手玩弄<span class="encs">[玉]</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 玩耍;玩弄 <span class="encs">[play with;dally with]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玩，弄也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将还玩吾国于股掌之上。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民狎而玩之，则多死焉，故宽难。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岂惟玩景物，亦欲摅心素。——<span class="smcs">《白氏长庆集·读谢灵运诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玩古知今，特可宝爱。——<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训·杂艺》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玩天地于掌握之中。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·精神》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">摩玩不已。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:玩服(玩赏佩用之物);玩爱(玩赏珍爱);玩饰(供玩赏的佩饰)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 玩赏;欣赏 <span class="encs">[enjoy;appreciate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">展玩不可晓。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:玩咏(玩赏吟咏);玩幽(玩赏幽景);玩好(玩赏与爱好);玩月(赏月)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 从事或投入<span class="encs">[某种游戏或其他文娱活动]</span> <span class="encs">[engage in some kinds of sports or recreational activities]</span>。如:玩戏(非专业演员演戏，玩票;耍闹，开玩笑);玩棒球;玩雪;玩适(娱乐);玩之不足(观赏不尽);玩影(弄影) </p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 戏弄 <span class="encs">[make fun of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玩人丧德，玩物丧志。——<span class="smcs">《书·旅獒》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:为人所玩;玩巧(玩弄巧诈手段);玩奸(玩弄奸诈的手段);玩谑(戏弄;开玩笑);玩是玩，笑是笑(俗语。开玩笑是开玩笑，该正经时要正经)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 研讨;反复体会 <span class="encs">[savor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顾玩所见。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡·订鬼篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而玩其辞。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玩其文也久矣。——<span class="smcs">《列子·黄帝》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:玩索(体味探索);玩习(玩味研习);玩占(占卜;研究卦象、卜辞);玩思(研究，探索);玩耽(潜心玩味);玩咏(玩味讽咏);玩意(玩味，体味)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 轻慢;轻视 <span class="encs">[slight;neglect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">究属玩视， 着记大过三次。——<span class="smcs">《官场现形记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:玩令(玩命。玩忽命令);玩延(玩忽延误);玩弛(玩忽松懈);玩法(玩忽法令);玩泄(玩忽，轻慢);玩俗(玩忽法令的风气);玩怠(玩忽，懈怠)</p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 不认真对待;轻率无礼地谈话、写作、行事等 <span class="encs">[trifle with;treat
