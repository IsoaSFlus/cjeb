                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诛</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">誅</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˉ</span></p><ol><li>把罪人杀死：害民者～。～灭。～锄。～戮。伏～。</li><li>责罚：～意（不问罪行，只根据其用心讹定罪状）。口～笔伐。</li><li>责求：～求无已（一味索取，没有止境）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> execute, kill, put to death; punish</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> strafen, bestrafen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mettre à mort,critiquer et punir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诛</strong></p><p>誅 <span class="dicpy">zhū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，朱声。本义:声讨、谴责)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[reproach]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诛，讨也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诛，代。诛犹责也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诛以驭其过。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诛求无厌。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">齿路马有诛。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“罚也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">得志于诸侯，而诛无理，曹其首也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于</span><span class="diczx2">予</span>与何诛?——<span class="smcs">《论语·公冶长》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">甚悖逆不轨，宜诛讨之日久矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">桓宽</span><span class="smcs">《盐铁论·本议》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:诛放(责斥其罪并予以放逐);诛远(斥退疏远);诛惩(声讨惩处);诛心(揭露、指责人的用心)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 杀戮。夺去生命<span class="encs">[kill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">立诛杀</span><span class="diczx2">曹无伤</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">诛有功之人。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不诛之则为乱。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·慎行论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">广德</span>素闻 <span class="diczx2">超</span>在 <span class="diczx2">鄯善</span>诛灭虏使，大惶恐。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·班超梁慬传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">能诛戮清流之朋，莫如</span><span class="diczx2">唐昭宗</span>之世。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《朋党论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不当诛之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既而以</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>民之乱请于朝，按诛五人。—— <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以诛奸贼。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:诛论(以罪论死);诛流(杀戮和流放);诛害(杀害);诛赦(杀戮与赦免)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 治罪，惩罚 <span class="encs">[punish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诛严不为戾。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">夫离法者罪，而诸先生以文学取;犯禁者诛，而群侠以私剑养。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">而诛罚不必其后也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故卒不加诛。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">原情定过，赦事诛意。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·霍胥传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:诛意(惩罚、制裁其心意。指加罪于人，不论其事迹、行事，只寻究其动机或心意);诛恶(惩治作恶者);诛率(惩治首恶)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 要求;索取 <span class="encs">[ask for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诛屡于徒人费，弗得。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人人自制，是以贪财诛利，不畏死亡。——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A85.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】陟輸切【集韻】【韻會】追輸切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音株。【說文】討也。【廣雅】殺也。【書·胤征】以干先王之誅。【禮·月令】詰誅暴慢。【註】誅者，戮其人。【前漢·<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>法志】征暴誅悖，治之威也。【莊子·庚桑楚】爲不善乎顯明之中者，人得而誅之，爲不善乎幽暗之中者，鬼得而誅之。</p><p>又【釋名】罪及餘人曰誅。誅，株也。如株木根，枝葉盡落也。【周禮·秋官·司烜氏】軍旅修火禁，邦若屋誅，則爲明竁焉。【註】鄭司農云：屋誅，謂夷三族。</p><p>又【易·雜卦】明夷誅也。【註】誅，傷也。</p><p>又【晉語】小國敖，大國襲焉，曰誅。</p><p>又【玉篇】罰也。【禮·曲禮】以足蹙路馬芻有誅，齒路馬有誅。【註】誅，罰也。</p><p>又【類篇】責也。【左傳·襄三十一年】誅求無時。【註】誅，責也。【周禮·天官·大宰】誅以馭其過。【疏】人有過失，非故爲之者，則以言語責讓之。</p><p>又翦除也。【晉語】故以惠誅怨。【註】誅，除也。【楚辭·卜居】寧誅鋤草茅，以力耕乎。【杜甫·岳麓山道林二寺詩】傍此煙霞茅可誅。【正字通】翦茅爲屋。借用誅字。</p><p>又叶之由切，音周。【華覈·自責文】不敢違敕，懼速罪誅，冒承詔命，魂逝形留。</p><p>又叶株遇切，株去聲。【蔡邕·釋誨】下獲熏胥之辜，高受滅家之誅，前車已覆，襲軌而騖。</p><p>
