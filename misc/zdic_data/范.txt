						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>范</strong></p>
<p><strong>（①-④<span class="diczx3">範</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>模（m&uacute;）子：铜～。铁～。</li>
	<li>榜样：模～。典～。</li>
	<li>一定界限：～围。～畴（➊类型；范围；➋概念性最高的基本概念，如&ldquo;化合&rdquo;、&ldquo;分解&rdquo;是化学的范畴；&ldquo;矛盾&rdquo;、&ldquo;质&rdquo;和&ldquo;量&rdquo;等是哲学的范畴）。规～。</li>
	<li>限制：防～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">范</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>范</strong></p>
<p>範 <span class="dicpy">f&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，笵( <span class="dicpy">f&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> )省声。本义:古代遇大事出车，先辗过祭坛及祭牲的一种祭祀活动，祭路神)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a ceremony of sacrifice to the Road God]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为笵。法则 <span class="encs">[rule]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">範，法也。範，常也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾为之範我驰驱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:&ldquo;法也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:范防(防范);范轨(法则，模范)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 典范 <span class="encs">[model]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宇文新州之懿范。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:规范;示范;范轨(模范，法则);范型(典型，范例)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 模子 <span class="encs">[pattern]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则以一铁范置铁板上，仍密布字印，满铁苑为一板。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:铁范;钱范;铜范;范金合土(用模子浇铸金属和泥)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>范</strong></p>
<p>範 <span class="dicpy">f&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用模子浇铸，引申纳入规范 <span class="encs">[cast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">范金合土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:范型(使合乎某种模式);范溶(铸造器物);范铜(铸铜)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 效法;取法 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾为之范我驰驱，终日不获。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:范水模山(喻效法模仿他人);范围(效法)</p>
<p>◎ <strong>范</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 草名 <span class="encs">[grass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">范，草也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 蜂 <span class="encs">[bee]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">范则冠而蝉有婑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓下》</span></span></p>

<p>又通軓。《少儀》祭左右軌范。</p>
<p>又通範。【揚子&middot;太<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>經】矩范之動，成敗之效也。　【正字通】㔾从弓，音頷。</p>
