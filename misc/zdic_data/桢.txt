                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>桢</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">楨</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>坚硬的木头。</li><li>古代打土墙时所立的木柱，泛指支柱：～干（<span class="dicpy">gàn </span>）（喻能胜重任的人）。</li><li>古书上指女贞树。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">桢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>桢</strong></p><p>楨 <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，贞声。本义:木名，即女贞)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hardwood]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桢，刚木也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">文欀桢橿。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“桢、橿，二木名。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又东二百里，曰</span><span class="diczx2">太山</span>，上多金玉、桢木。——<span class="smcs">《山海经》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代筑墙时竖立在两端的木柱 <span class="encs">[terminal posts used in building a wall in ancient times]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桢，干也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">舍人</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“桢，正也。筑墙所立两木也。”</span>按，植于两端者曰桢，植于两边者曰干。</p><p><span class="diczx1">维</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>之桢。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·文王》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:桢干</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 支柱，主干 <span class="encs">[mainstay;pillar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">峙乃桢干。——<span class="smcs">《书·费誓》</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“题曰桢。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:桢臣(栋梁之臣);桢材(干才);桢固(支柱;骨干)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/桢干">桢干</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/桢木">桢木</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
