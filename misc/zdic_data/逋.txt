                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">逋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>逋</strong> <span class="dicpy">bū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，甫声。逋的原始意义是奴隶逃亡。本义:逃亡，逃跑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[abscond;flee]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逋，亡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃惟四方之多罪逋逃，是崇是长。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;牧誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于伐殷逋播臣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为天下逋逃主。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;武臣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六年其逋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狼失声而逋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:逋臣(逃亡的大臣);逋逃薮(逃亡的罪犯或流亡者聚集的地方);逋客(逃亡的人;避世的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拖欠，欠税 <span class="encs">[be behind in payment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逋租宿债勿复收。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">沈约</span><span class="smcs">《宋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:逋债(拖延付债);逋租(拖欠租税)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 懈怠;拖延 <span class="encs">[slack;delay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">久逋王命。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">房玄龄</span><span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;蔡谟传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:逋壅(拖延积压);逋慢(怠慢不敬;不遵法令)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>逋</strong> <span class="dicpy">bū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>逃亡者 <span class="encs">[fugitive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可以怀远，可以柔逋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/900B.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】博孤切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】奔謨切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音哺。【說文】亡也。【書&middot;大誥】于伐殷逋播臣。【註】逋亡播遷之臣也。【易&middot;訟卦】歸而逋其邑人三百戸。象曰：不克訟，歸逋<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥨥</span>也。【左傳&middot;文六年】趙宣子爲政，董逋逃。【註】督<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥨥</span>逃有罪之人也。</p><p>又【廣韻】懸也。【正韻】欠也。凡欠負官物，亡匿不還，皆謂之逋。【前漢&middot;昭帝紀】三年以前逋更賦未入者，皆勿收。【後漢&middot;光武紀】爲舂陵侯家訟逋租。</p><p>又【段熲傳】洗雪百年之逋負。</p>
