                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>画</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">畫</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄚˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>绘图：绘～。～图。～像。～地为牢。～脂镂冰（在凝固的油脂或冰上绘画雕刻，一融化就都没有了，喻劳而无功）。</li>
	<li>图像：～面。～片。～坛。～板。～卷（<span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n </span>）（①成卷轴形的画；②喻壮丽的景色或动人的场面）。～外音。图～。诗中有～。</li>
	<li>写，签押，署名：～到。～卯。～押。～供。</li>
	<li>用手、脚或器具做出某种动作：比～。指手～脚。&ldquo;曲终收拨当心～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>字的一笔称一画：笔～。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/划">划</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">画</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>画</strong></p>
<p>畫 <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼指事。金文。上面是&ldquo;聿&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;</span>)，象以手执笔的样子，是&ldquo;笔&rdquo;的本字;下面象画出的田界。整个字形，象人持笔画田界之形。本义:划分，划分界线)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[divide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">画，界也，象田四界，聿所以画之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">画为九州。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">画壄分州。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;地理志上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:画野分疆(划分疆域而治);画土分贡(中国古代实行分封制，天子划分封地给诸侯，诸侯定期向天子朝贡);画地(在地上画界线);画界(划定疆界)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 绘画;作画 <span class="encs">[draw;paint]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为蛇画足。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善画者多工书。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善画者或兼建筑。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:画学(绘画的学问);画壁(在墙壁上作画);画革(在皮革上书写);画癖(喜爱绘画艺术成癖)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用画装饰的或以图案等装饰的 <span class="encs">[be decorated with paintings or pictures]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:画桥(彩饰的桥梁);画烛(有彩饰的蜡烛);画楼(彩饰的楼);画刹(有彩绘装饰的佛寺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 签署;签押 <span class="encs">[sign]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:画敕(画押);画可(帝王在奏章上批可字，表示允准可行);画卯(旧时官署规定卯时开时办公，史胥差役按时赴官署签到)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 书写文字。亦特指写出带有标记性的文字 <span class="encs">[write]</span> 。如:画灰(在灰上写字);画沙(古代书家以为笔锋如锥画沙，方为高妙);画沙印泥(古代书法家推崇的一种用笔方法);画札(写字);画拉(方言。写)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 比画 <span class="encs">[gesticulate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:指手画脚;画符(道士用咒语画成符录);指天画地</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 截止;停止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">力不足者，中道而废，今女画。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">何晏</span>集解引 <span class="diczx2">孔安国</span>曰:&ldquo;画，止也&hellip;&hellip;自女自止耳，非力极。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百川学海而至于海，丘陵学山不至于山，是故恶夫画也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《法言》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">李轫</span>注:&ldquo;画，止。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 谋划;策划 <span class="encs">[plan;plot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">画，计也，策也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孝公</span>平画。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;更法》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">助画方略。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:画策(计划;筹划);划事(筹划事情);画制(筹画制度);画计(谋画)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通&ldquo;化&rdquo;。教化 <span class="encs">[educate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:画外(化外，指政令教化所不及之地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 横划过去 <span class="encs">[a horizontal stroke]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲终收拨当心画。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
