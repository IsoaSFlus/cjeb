                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>乳</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">rǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨˇ</span></p><ol><li>分泌奶的器官：～房（亦称“乳”、“奶子”）。～峰。～罩。</li><li>乳房中分泌出来的白色甜汁：～汁（亦称“<span class="diczx3">奶</span>”）。哺～。～母（奶妈）。～臭（<span class="dicpy">xiù</span>）未干（对年幼人表示轻蔑）。</li><li>像乳汁的东西：豆～。～胶。～腐（亦称“腐乳”）。</li><li>像乳头的东西：钟～（钟上可敲打的突出物）。钟～石。</li><li>生，生殖：孳～。</li><li>初生的、幼小的：～燕。～牙。～名（小名）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>乳</strong> <span class="dicpy">rǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从孚)。甲骨文中象手抱婴儿哺乳形。<span class="yhcs">“孚”</span>是以爪抱子哺乳。本义:生子，生产</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[give birth to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人及鸟生子曰乳，兽曰产。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乳化曰孽。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span>传</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胎生曰乳。——<span class="smcs">《尸子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">菑川</span><span class="diczx2">王</span>美人怀子而不乳。——<span class="smcs">《史记·扁仓传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主人方乳。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·音初》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使牧羝，羝乳乃得归。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:乳子(产子;分娩);乳舍(产房);乳妇(产妇);乳卵(产(下)蛋);乳抱(繁殖)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 喂奶 <span class="encs">[suckle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妪，先大母婢也，乳二世。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妇抚儿乳。——</span><span class="diczx2">林嗣环</span><span class="smcs">《口技》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:乳狗(育子的母狗);乳育(哺育);乳哺(哺育;养育);乳养(哺育);乳雏(哺育幼雏);乳兽(育子的猛兽);哺乳</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 孵化 <span class="encs">[hatch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">立春，鸡始乳。——<span class="smcs">《魏书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 研磨 <span class="encs">[grind]</span>。如:乳钵(研制粉末的小臼)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 饮 <span class="encs">[drink]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乳血飧肤。——</span><span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《芜城赋》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>乳</strong> <span class="dicpy">rǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 乳汁 <span class="encs">[milk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">常饮牛乳，色如处子。——<span class="smcs">《魏书·王琚传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:乳花(奶花。指年幼无知);乳白;乳茶;乳酒;乳畜;乳食;乳汁;乳牛;炼乳;乳臭小子(对年轻人的鄙称);乳腥气;乳臭</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 乳房 <span class="encs">[breast]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">儿含乳啼。——<span
