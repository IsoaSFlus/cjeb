                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>急</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>焦燥：～燥。着～。焦～。</li><li>气恼，发怒：～眼。</li><li>使着急：这件事真～人。</li><li>匆促：～促。～功近利（急于求目前的成效和利益）。</li><li>迅速，又快又猛：～流。～进。～先锋。～风暴雨。</li><li>迫切，要紧：～切。～诊。～事。</li><li>严重：告～。</li><li>对大家的事情或别人的困难尽快帮助：～人之难。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">急</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>急</strong> <span class="dicpy">jí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形，从心，及声。本义:狭窄)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 紧，紧缩 <span class="encs">[tight; compact]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">遂生</span>缚 <span class="diczx2">布</span>， <span class="diczx2">布</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“缚太急，小缓之。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·吕布传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:急亲戚(最接近的亲戚);急持(紧紧抱持);急窄(犹紧缩);急装(扎缚紧凑的装束);急绷绷形容把东西拉得很紧</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 急躁，着急 <span class="encs">[annoyed;irritated;impatient;anxious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曰急。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:“急促自用也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">急缮其怒。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国无六年之蓄曰急。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">西门豹</span>之性急，故佩韦以缓己。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·观行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:急吼吼(性急慌忙的样子);急撮(因受人摆弄而急躁);急屎狗(性急的狗);急杀人(极其着急);急脚鬼(性情特别急躁的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 迫切;紧急 <span class="encs">[urgent;pressing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时恐急。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">甚急。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事急而不断。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">江都</span>围急。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事急矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:急口(急忙开口);急脚(亦作<span class="yhcs">“急脚子”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“急足。送紧急文书的仆役);急递铺(元代在军事紧急时，设置急递铺作传递文书的驿站，每天最快要跑四百里);急且(急切，仓猝);急处从宽(在紧急情况下并不紧张)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 疾速;快捷;匆忙 <span class="encs">[fast;rapid;at high speed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>急攻之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">急应</span><span class="diczx2">河阳</span>役。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">急挥帜。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span
