                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">垄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>垄</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">壟</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄥˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>田地分界高起的埂子：田～。～沟。</li>
	<li>农作物的行（<span class="dicpy">h&aacute;ng </span>），或行与行间的空地：宽～密植。</li>
	<li>像垄的东西：瓦～。</li>
	<li>坟冢：&ldquo;由是观之，生王之头，曾不若死士之～也&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> grave, mound; ridge in field</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> monopolisieren, an sich reißen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tertre,tombeau</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">垄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>垄</strong></p>
<p>壟、壠 <span class="dicpy">lǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，龙声。本义:坟)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生王之头，曾不若死士之垄也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:垄墓(坟墓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 土埂 <span class="encs">[ridge]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 田埂</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辍耕之垄上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:垄次(田间);垄亩之臣(辞官归耕田野的臣子);垄亩(阡陌田野)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 成行种植农作物的土埂</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 像垄的东西。如:瓦垄;垄灶(土灶，就地砌起的灶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 高丘，高地 <span class="encs">[high hill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">封</span><span class="diczx2">比干</span>之丘垄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;东方朔&middot;七谏沉江》</span>。注:&ldquo;小曰丘，大曰垄。垄，一作陇。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:垄岗沙(沙漠中广泛分布的一种沙丘，也称&ldquo;沙垄&rdquo;或&ldquo;纵向沙丘&rdquo;)</span></p>
