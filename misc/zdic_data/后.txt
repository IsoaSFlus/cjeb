                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">后</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>后</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">➍-➑<span class="diczx3">後</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">h&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>君主；帝王：商之先～（先王）。</li>	<li>帝王的正妻：皇～。太～。</li>	<li>诸候。《書&bull;舜典》：&ldquo;肆覲東后。&rdquo;</li>	<li>指空间在背面，反面的，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">前</span>&rdquo;相对：～窗户。～面。～学。～缀。～进。</li>	<li>时间较晚，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">先</span>&rdquo;相对：日～。～福。～期。</li>	<li>指次序，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">前</span>&rdquo;相对：～排。～十名。</li>	<li>子孙：～辈。～嗣。～裔。～昆。无～（没有子孙）。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">后</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>后</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。象人之形，施令以告四方，古之，从一口，发号者君后也。按从坐人，从口，与君同意。本义:君主，帝王)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sovereign]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后，继体君也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">班瑞于群后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">允王维后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;时迈》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后王命冢宰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔我先世后稷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皇皇后帝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">夏</span>后 <span class="diczx2">皋</span>之墓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:后王(君王;天子);后帝(天帝;上帝);后夷(上古有穷国的国君);后辟(君主;帝王)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 帝王的妻子 <span class="encs">[empress;queen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子之妃曰后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝嫡妃曰皇后，帝母曰皇太后，帝祖母曰太皇太后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《独断下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:后族(皇后的亲族);后党(清末慈禧一派;皇后、太后的亲族或与皇后、太后利害关系一致的人所结成的政治集团)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代指列国诸侯 <span class="encs">[dukes or princes under an emperor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肆觐东后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;东后，东方之诸侯也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代对长官、郡守或将领的尊称 <span class="encs">[high official]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赫赫明后，柔嘉维则。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李翕</span><span class="smcs">《西狭颂》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂知吾后意，忧此</span><span class="diczx2">毗陵</span>最。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">前蜀</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贯休</span><span class="smcs">《上孙使君》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指后土。对大地的尊称 <span class="encs">[the earth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王大封，则先告后土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宗伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:后皇(后土与皇天);后缗(地神)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓。如:后缗(夏少康的母亲)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>后</strong></p>
<p>後 <span class="dicpy">h&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文，从&ldquo;彳&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">ch&igrave;</span>)，表示与走路有关，从&ldquo;幺&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">yāo</span>，小)，从&ldquo;攵&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">suī</span>)，是&ldquo;足&rdquo;的反写，有&ldquo;行路迟缓&rdquo;的意思)。 <span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span><span class="smcs">《说文解字注》</span>:&ldquo;幺者小也，小而行迟，后可知矣。&rdquo;本义:迟到，走在后</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be(or come，arrive)late]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">後，迟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非敢後也，马不进也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子路</span>从而後。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;微子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兄弟之後生者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;有司後》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣闻命後。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;定公八年》</span>。注:&ldquo;晚也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">收气乃後。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;五常政大论》</span>。注:&ldquo;後，不及时也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知古则可知後。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;长见》</span>。注:&ldquo;来也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:随后就来;落后;殿后(行军走在最后)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 承继 <span class="encs">[inherit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陷队之士知疾斗，不得斩首队五人，则陷队之士，人赐爵一级，死则一人后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>后</strong></p>
<p>後 <span class="dicpy">h&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 后代，子孙 <span class="encs">[male offspring]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">式救尔後。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;瞻卬》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为人後者为之子也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;杂记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下初发难时，假立诸侯，後以代</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求太监后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:无后;绝后;后叶(后代);后胤(子孙，后裔);后戒(后人的鉴戒);后哲(后代的哲人);后绝(没有后代)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 后世 <span class="encs">[posterity]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:后镜(后世的借镜);后后烈(流传后世的业绩);后叶(后世);后报(来世受报应)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 次序在后 <span class="encs">[later]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子路从而后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;微子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后岁余。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从诸君子之后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟恐或后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指肛门 <span class="encs">[anus]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:后庭(肛门);后窍(肛门)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>后</strong></p>
<p>後 <span class="dicpy">h&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 时间较迟或较晚。与&ldquo;先&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[later;after]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君还而后退。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然后国之良士，亦将可得而众也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚贤上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知止而后有定，定而后能静。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以五戒先後刑罚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;士师》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事至而後虑者谓之後。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后遂无问津者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后遂不复至。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">花朝节后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其后为之者较少。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:后时(后来;以后);后次(下次;下回);后儿(后天);后番(下次;下回)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 后面，位置在后 <span class="encs">[behind;back]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事成而後。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓位在下也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以後路与冕服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;杂记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">近出前后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后狼止。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恐前后受其敌。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转视积薪后。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以攻其后。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:后山(后面的山);后应(居后作接应 的部队);后廷(后宫);后户(后门);后言(背后的议论);后阁(在后面 的殿阁);先斩后奏</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>后</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄡˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>h&ograve;u <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2743.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">君王。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;商頌&middot;玄鳥》：「商之先<b>后</b>，受命不殆，在武丁孫子。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;僖公三 十二年》：「其南陵，夏<b>后</b>皋之墓也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">諸侯。</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;舜典》：「班瑞于群<b>后</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;柳宗元〈封建論〉：「周有天下，裂土田而瓜分之，設五等，邦群<b>后</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">君王的嫡妻。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「王<b>后</b>」、「皇<b>后</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《漢書&middot;卷五&middot;景帝紀》：「朕親耕，<b>后</b>親桑，以奉宗廟粢盛祭服，為天下先。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;陸次雲〈費宮人傳〉：「<b>后</b>自縊，袁貴妃亦自縊。帝拔劍刃嬪妃數人。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如春秋時齊國有孔子的弟子后處，漢代有后蒼。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>

<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">時間上較晚的。與「先」、「前」相對。</span><span class="gc_yy">《禮記&middot;大學》：「知止而<b>后</b>有定。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「後」。</span></p></li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">后</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】
                              后·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=6" target="_blank">6</a>　·部外筆画：3</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/540E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【正韻】胡口切【韻會】很口切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音後。【說文】繼體君也，象人之形。施令以告四方，故<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20086.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂆</span>之从一，口。發號者，君后也。【書&middot;仲虺之誥】徯我后。【易&middot;泰卦】后以財成天地之道。【禮&middot;檀弓】夏后氏堲周。【疏】夏言后者，白虎通云以揖讓受于君，故稱后。</p><p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】天子有后。【疏】后，後也。言其後于天子，亦以廣後胤也。【白虎通】商以前皆曰妃，周始立后。正嫡曰王后，秦漢曰皇后，漢祖母稱太皇太后，母稱皇太后。</p><p>又諸侯亦稱后。【書&middot;舜典】班瑞于羣后。</p><p>又古者君稱臣亦曰后。【書&middot;舜典】汝后稷，播時百穀。【疏】國語云：稷爲天官，單名爲稷，尊而君之，稱爲后稷。</p><p>又【畢命】三后協心。【註】謂周公君<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>畢公也。</p><p>又【書&middot;武成】告于皇天后土。【傳】后土，社也。【左傳&middot;昭二十九年】土正曰后土。【註】土爲羣物主，故稱后也。其祀句龍焉，在家則祀中霤，在野則爲社。【正韻】后土，亦取厚載之義。</p><p>又姓。【史記&middot;仲尼弟子傳】后處字子里。【前漢&middot;儒林傳】后倉字近君。</p><p>又與後通。【禮&middot;曲禮】再拜稽首，而后對。</p><p>又【廣韻】胡遘切【集韻】下遘切【韻會】【正韻】胡茂切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>。義同。</p><p>又叶後五切，音戸。【蔡邕&middot;胡黃二公頌】允兹漢室，誕育二后。曰胡曰黃，方軌齊武。【陸雲&middot;漢高盛德頌】咸陽克殄，旣係秦后。峩峩阿房，乃淸帝宇。○按詩本音云：《周頌》宣哲維人，文武維后，燕及皇天，克昌厥後。后後俱音戸，後人誤入四十五厚韻，故於《唐韻》正中歷引經集証之。然自《玉篇》以後，后在厚韻相沿已久，不得不以後五切爲叶音矣。</p>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九">卷九</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九&amp;swbs=后">后</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/540E.svg">                          <p>繼體君也。象人之形。施令以告四方，故厂之。从一口。發號者，君后也。凡后之屬皆从后。胡口切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/540E_720.svg" /><span class="swbf">(后)</span><span class="ywyy">繼體君也。</span><span class="swdz">釋詁、毛傳皆曰：后，君也。許知爲繼體君者，后之言後也。開<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛝</span>之君在先，繼體之君在後也。析言之如是，渾言之則不别矣。易象下傳曰：后以施命誥四方。虞云：后繼體之君也。此許說也。葢同用孟易，經傳多假后爲後。大射注引孝經說曰：后者，後也。此謂后卽後之假借。</span><span class="ywyy">象人之形。</span><span class="swdz">謂上體<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2008B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂋</span>也。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2008B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠂋</span>葢入字横寫，不曰从入。而曰象人形者，以非立人也。下文卮解亦曰象人。</span><span class="ywyy">从口，</span><span class="swdz">胡口切。四部。</span><span class="ywyy">易曰。后㠯施令告四方。</span><span class="swdz">此用遘象傳說从口之意。傳曰：天下有風，遘，后以施命誥四方。鄭作詰。許作吿。按此條各本作象人之形，施令以吿四方，故厂之从一口，發號者君后也。淺人所竄，不成文理。上體既旣象人，又何得云从余制切之厂，且从一乎，今以下卮字解文法更定，則宜有易曰審矣。</span><span class="ywyy">凡后之屬皆从后。</span></p>                          </div>
