                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>慢</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">màn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>迟缓，速度小，与“<span class="diczx3">快</span>”相对：～车。～件。～腾腾。～条斯理。缓～。迟～。～性。</li><li>态度冷淡，不殷勤，不礼貌：～待。轻～。傲～。怠～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> slow(ly), leisurely, sluggish</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> langsam  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> lent,lentement</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">慢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>慢</strong> <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，曼声。本义:轻慢;对人无礼貌)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[despise;look down on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慢，一曰不畏也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慢，惰也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我远而慢之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公三十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可敬不可慢。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·缁衣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而长不简慢矣。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·处方》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君使民慢，乱将作矣。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敬贤者存，慢贤者亡。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·君子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">荆成王</span>慢焉。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·上德》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其慢若彼之甚也;见贤人若此其肃也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·则阳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">管家，实是多慢了你。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:慢骂(信口乱骂);慢亵(慢待;招待不周);慢狎(轻侮);慢视(轻视);慢公(怠忽公务);慢令(轻慢上司的命令);慢心(轻慢之心)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 怠慢 <span class="encs">[slight;cold-shoulder]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">责</span><span class="diczx2">攸之</span>、 <span class="diczx2">伟</span>、 <span class="diczx2">允</span>等之慢。—— <span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:慢客(怠慢客人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 放纵;无节制 <span class="encs">[indulge]</span>。如:慢散(不严肃;不整齐);慢泄(轻浮);慢淫(浪荡遨游)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“谩”</span>。欺骗 <span class="encs">[deceive;hoodwink]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">田</span>驷东慢 <span class="diczx2">齐侯</span>，南欺 <span class="diczx2">荆</span>王。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说林上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“墁”</span>。涂抹 <span class="encs">[plaster]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">郢人垩慢其鼻端。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·徐无鬼》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>慢</strong> <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 懒惰 <span class="encs">[idle;lazy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">慢，惰也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">政宽则民慢，慢则纠之以猛。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">啴谐慢易。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:慢憧(懈怠动摇);慢志(怠懈的心智);慢官(闲散的官职)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 不及时，迟了 <span class="encs">[late;tardy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不及时日，渴葬也;不及时而不日，慢葬也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·隐公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 从慢走引申为缓慢 <span class="encs">[slow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叔马慢忌，叔发罕异。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·大叔于田》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻拢慢捻抹复挑。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p><p><span
