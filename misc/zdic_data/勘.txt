                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>勘</strong> <span class="dicpy">kān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，甚声。本义:校订，核对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(考订核实异同) <span class="encs">[read and correct the text]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勘，校也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每获一书，即共同勘校。——</span><span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《金石录后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:勘合(验证，核对。文书上加盖印信，一分为二，当事双方各执一半，作为凭证);勘对(核对;注定);勘婚(婚前男女两家互相查考生辰八字、门第等)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 审问 <span class="encs">[interrogate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当堂对勘。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:勘正(审问核实);勘问(盘问;审问);勘鞠(审讯);勘当(审问核察)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 查看;调查 <span class="encs">[survey]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又推勘妖魔作怪之由。——</span><span class="diczx2">洪仁玕</span><span class="smcs">《太平天日》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:勘灾(查看灾情);勘破(看破);堪舆(看风水)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 判断 <span class="encs">[judge; decide]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天也，你错勘贤愚枉做天!——<span class="smcs">《窦娥冤》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘测">勘测</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘察">勘察</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘定">勘定</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘校">勘校</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘探">勘探</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘探">勘探</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘误">勘误</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘误表">勘误表</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/勘正">勘正</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
