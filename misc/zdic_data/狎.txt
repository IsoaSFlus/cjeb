						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>狎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄚˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>亲近而态度不庄重：～侮。～弄。～昵。～客（嫖客）。</li>
	<li>习惯。</li>
	<li>更替。</li>
	<li>拥挤：车骑并～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">狎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>狎</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从犬，甲声。从犬，犬善与人玩耍。本义:驯犬)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tame]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狎，犬可习也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故欲以刑罚慈民，辟其犹以鞭狎狗也，虽久弗亲矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 驯养 <span class="encs">[domesticate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫龙之为虫也，柔可狎而骑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">驯乌逐饭磬，狎兽绕禅林。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">陈</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈炯</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狎兽(驯顺之兽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 亲昵，亲近而不庄重 <span class="encs">[be improperly familiar with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">稍近，益狎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而狎风雨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自此狎媟无间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狎而不甘问焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狎游(嬉戏亲昵);狎浪(淫秽放浪);狎侮(轻慢;侮辱);狎熟(亲昵、熟悉);狎慢(亲热轻慢);狎媟(亲昵而近于放荡);狎笑(亲昵嬉笑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 亲近;接近 <span class="encs">[be close to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狎习于乱而容于治，故</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>人不能归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;南面》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>衰者，虽狎，必变。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;乡党》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狎弄(亲近戏弄);狎近(亲近);狎异(亲近异常)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 不重视或不注意 <span class="encs">[disregard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">礼，君不使无耻，不近刑人，不狎敌，不迩怨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;襄公二十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狎侮(轻视;忽视);狎敌(轻敌);狎视</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 交替 <span class="encs">[alternate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自无令王，诸侯逐进，狎主齐盟，其又可壹乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:&ldquo;强弱无常，故更主盟。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:狎主(交替主持)</span></p>

	<li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">戲弄、嬉戲。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「狎妓」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;襄公六年》：「宋華弱與樂轡少相狎。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;柳宗元〈三戒&middot;臨江之麋〉：「麋麑稍大，忘己之麋也，以為犬良我友，抵觸偃仆益狎。」</span></p></li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">狎</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集下" target="_blank">巳集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集下&amp;bs=犬" target="_blank">犬</a>】
                              狎·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/72CE.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【廣韻】胡甲切【集韻】【類篇】【韻會】轄甲切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音匣。【廣韻】習也。【說文】犬可習也。从犬，甲聲。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】狎，習也。</p>
<p>又【釋言】甲，狎也。【註】謂習狎。【禮&middot;曲禮】賢者，狎而敬之。【註】狎，近也，習也。謂附而近之，習其所行也。【周語】未狎君政。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】易也。【書&middot;泰誓】狎侮五常。【傳】輕狎五常之敎。【疏】狎，慣忽之言，慣見而忽也。【左傳&middot;昭二十年】水懦弱，民狎而玩之。【註】狎，輕也。【穀梁傳&middot;莊十七年】齊人殲焉，此謂狎敵也。【昭二十一年】不狎鄙。</p>
<p>又更也。【左傳&middot;襄二十七年】且晉楚狎主諸侯之盟也久矣。【疏】更代主諸侯之盟也。</p>
<p>又【神異經】八荒之中有毛人焉，名髥公。俗曰髥麗。一名髥狎。小兒髥狎可畏也。【玉篇】亦作狹。</p>
