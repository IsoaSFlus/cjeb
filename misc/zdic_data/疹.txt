<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>疹</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="diczx1">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 皮肤上起的红色小疙瘩。也指痘疮 <span class="encs">[rash]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:湿疹，瘙疹，瘟疹;疹粟(肌肤因受寒而起如粟一般的疹粒);疹子(麻疹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 病 <span class="encs">[disease]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂动旧疹，缠绵经月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《洛阳伽蓝记》</span></span></p>
