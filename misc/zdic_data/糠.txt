                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>糠</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄤˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>稻、麦、谷子等的子实所脱落的壳或皮：米～。糟～。～秕。～醛（有机化合物，是制造塑料、合成纤维、合成橡胶、药物等的原料）。</li>
	<li>萝卜等因失掉水分而中心呈蜂窝状。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> chaff, bran, husks; poor</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span>  Spreu, Hülse, Schalen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> balle du grain,son,chétif,mesquin,sans soin</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">糠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>糠</strong></p>
<p>穅、粇 <span class="dicpy">kāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从米，康声。本义:从稻、麦等谷皮上脱下的皮、壳)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 本作&ldquo;穅&rdquo;，&ldquo;康&rdquo;。谷的外壳 <span class="encs">[chaff;bran;husks]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邻有糠槽而欲窃之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此犹梁肉之与糠糟也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:糠菜半年粮;糠覈(糠中的粗屑。形容粗糙的饭食);糠粞(谷皮碎米。指粗劣的粮食);糠灯(一种照明用具。以苏子油渣杂粟糠抟在蓬梗上点燃。旧时吉林民间多用之)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 小麦种子、黑麦种子或其他禾谷的碎裂皮层，从籽粒上分离出来，特别用于家畜饲料 <span class="encs">[bran]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">糠和米本是相依倚，被簸扬作两处飞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《琵琶记&middot;糟糠自厌》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7CE0.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】苦岡切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】丘岡切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音康。【玉篇】穀皮也。【前漢&middot;食貨志】貧者食糟糠。</p>
<p>又【韻會小補】或省作康。【爾雅&middot;釋器】康謂之蠱。【疏】康，米皮，一名蠱。《左傳&middot;昭元年》穀之飛亦爲蠱是也。</p>
<p>又粃糠，煩碎也。【莊子&middot;逍遙遊】塵垢粃糠。【註】粃糠猶煩碎。</p>
<p>又山名。【交州記】合浦海口有糠頭山，相傳越王舂米于此，積糠而成。</p>
<p>又星名。【石氏&middot;星經】箕前亦名糠。星明，大歲豐，小微，天下饑荒無米。　【玉篇】本作穅。【集韻】或作粇。</p>
