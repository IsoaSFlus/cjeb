<p>● <strong>卓</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄛˊ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卓</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (卓的古字。形声。匕形早声。&ldquo;匕&rdquo;表示&ldquo;人&rdquo;。本义:超然独立;高明;高超)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[distinguished;outstanding]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卓，高也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十日卜为卓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《续汉书&middot;五行志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如有所立，卓尔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸿卓之义，发于颠沛之朝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">嵇延祖</span>卓卓如野鹤之在鸡群。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;容止》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不胶者卓矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《法言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卓荦不羁(卓越超群，不甘受拘束);卓尔独行(超越众人);卓夺(作出高明的决断);卓殊(卓越，特异);卓逸(卓越超绝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 高远;遥远 <span class="encs">[far-away]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卓行殊远而粮不绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;霍去病传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卓如日月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;祭遵传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必有卓诡切至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刘辅传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使卓然可观。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;成帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卓远(遥远);卓望(远望);卓卓(高远的样子);卓行(远行);卓阔(高远广大)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 正中 <span class="encs">[middle]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卓午</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 独特 <span class="encs">[special]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彼特以天为父，而身犹爱之，而况其卓乎!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 直立 <span class="encs">[erect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">王英</span>两脚蹬空，头盔倒卓，撞下马来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卓坚(直立);卓笔(直立的笔);卓锥(立锥)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>卓</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 白额的马 <span class="encs">[white-fronted horse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奉束帛匹马，卓上，九马随之。中庭西上奠币，再拜稽首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 姓</p>
