                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">聚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>聚</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆，下面是三个人，表示人多;上面的&ldquo;取&rdquo;，作声符。本义:村落)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[village]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚，会也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，邑落曰聚，今曰邨，曰镇，北方曰集皆是。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一年而所居成聚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;五帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚曰序。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所止聚落化其德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;王扶传》</span>。注:&ldquo;小于乡曰聚。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乡聚里中人哀而救之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《说苑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>无十户之聚，以王诸侯。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《上书谏吴王》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聚落(村落里邑，人群聚居的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 众;集团;一伙 <span class="encs">[group]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我是以有辅氏之聚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈</span>人恃其聚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公十七年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聚观(群聚观看);聚口(犹齐声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 地名 <span class="encs">[Ju city]</span>。在今山西省绛县东南</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>聚</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 会合;聚集 <span class="encs">[assemble;flock together;gather;get together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而发于众心之所聚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;君臣上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚室而谋日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以王命聚之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸣锣聚众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚至百有余乡。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聚散浮生(指人生聚散无定。浮生:人活着的时候是虚浮无定的);聚麀之诮(比喻和不三不四的女人鬼混在一起，遭人耻笑。麀:母鹿，泛指母兽);聚寇(聚集起来的盗寇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 积蓄，累积 <span class="encs">[accumulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聚菽粟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚贤中》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 征集 <span class="encs">[collect]</span></p>

                              聚·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/805A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】慈庾切【集韻】【韻會】在庾切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>徐上聲。【說文】會也。【易&middot;乾卦】君子學以聚之。【禮&middot;檀弓】聚國族於斯。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】五星聚于東井。【管子&middot;君臣篇】是以明君順人心，安情性而發於衆心之所聚。【註】聚謂所同歸凑也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】斂也。【禮&middot;樂記】君子聽竽笙簫管之聲，則思畜聚之臣。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;稍人】凡其余聚以待頒賜。【疏】聚是縣四百里，都五百里中畜聚之物。</p>
<p>又居也，邑落也。【史記&middot;五帝紀】一年而所居成聚。【註】聚謂村落也。</p>
<p>又【秦本紀】幷諸小鄕聚，集爲大縣。【註】萬二千五百家爲鄕聚。【前漢&middot;平帝紀】鄕曰庠，聚曰序。【註】張晏曰：聚，邑落名也。師古曰：聚，小于鄕。</p>
<p>又積也。【禮&middot;月令】孟冬之月，命有司循行積聚，無有不斂。</p>
<p>又【管子&middot;正篇】會民所聚曰道。【註】聚謂衆所宜。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;弓人】六材旣聚，巧者和之。【註】聚，具也。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;莊二十五年】城聚。【註】晉邑。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】才句切【集韻】從遇切【正韻】族遇切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2148D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡒍</span>。義同。</p>
<p>又音娵。【史記&middot;歷書】月名畢聚。日得甲子。索隱讀。</p>
<p>又與驟同。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;獸醫註】趨聚之節。【釋文】聚，本一作驟。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶組救切，音僦。【班固&middot;西都賦】毛羣内闐，飛羽上覆。接翼側足，集禁林而屯聚。</p>
