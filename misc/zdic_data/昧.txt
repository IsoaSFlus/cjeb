                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>昧</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mèi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mèi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>暗，不明：幽～。～旦（清晨还未明亮时）。</li><li>昏，糊涂，不明白：愚～。蒙～。素～平生（一向不认识）。</li><li>目不明：“目不别五色之章为～”。</li><li>隐藏，隐瞒：暧～。～心（违背良心做坏事）。</li><li>冒犯：冒～（多作谦辞）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>昧</strong> <span class="dicpy">mèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，未声。从日，与光线有关。本义:昏暗不明)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[obscure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昧，冥也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时甲子昧爽。——<span class="smcs">《书·牧誓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日昧昧其将暮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九章·怀沙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君昧爽而栉冠。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·哀公》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“昧，闇;爽，明也。谓初晓尚暗之时。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">神非其所宜而行之则昧。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽昧必亮。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·苏竟传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愚者昧于成事。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟党人之偷乐兮，路幽昧以险隘。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻身而昧大义。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:昧旦(天未全亮的时候);昧然(昏暗不明的样子);昧昧(昏暗不明的样子);昧色(暮色);昧明(天未全明的时刻;拂晓)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 眼不明的样子 <span class="encs">[blind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">耳不听五声之和为聋，目不别五色之章为昧。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 愚昧;糊涂 <span class="encs">[be ignorant of;have hazy notions about]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兼弱攻昧。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:昧陋(愚昧浅陋);味瞀(昏聩;糊涂);昧昧(糊涂无知);昧错(糊涂出错)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>昧</strong> <span class="dicpy">mèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 掩蔽;欺骗 <span class="encs">[conceal;hide;delude]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾家碑不昧。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《回棹》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:昧公义(埋没了公理和正义);昧心咒(昧了良心该受什么报应的誓言);昧心钱(坏了良心赚来的钱);昧蔽(隐瞒);昧诡(隐秘;诡秘);昧密(犹隐晦)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 违背 <span class="encs">[do evil against]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今日果成妙计，捉了唐僧，就好昧了前言?——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:昧信(背信;失信);昧良心;昧心</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 贪，贪图 <span class="encs">[be greedy for]</span>。如:昧利(贪利);昧私(贪图私利);昧冒(贪图)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 冒犯 <span class="encs">[offense]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丞相臣</span><span class="diczx2">斯</span>昧死言。——<span class="smcs">《史记·秦始皇本纪》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:昧死(冒死;冒昧);昧犯(冒犯)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
