                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">莞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>莞</strong> <span class="dicpy">guān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，完声。本义:俗名水葱、席子草。亦指用莞草织的席子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tabernaemontanus bulrush]</span>，植于水田，又名水葱( Scirpus tabernaemontani )，茎高五六尺，可织席，叶小如鳞片，花黄绿色。一名<span class="yhcs">“小蒲”</span>。如:莞蒲(蒲草);莞席(莞草编织的席子);莞蕈(草制之席及竹制之席);莞蒻(莞和蒻是两种编席的蒲草。借指用蒲草编的草席)。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">guǎn;wǎn</span></p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>莞</strong> <span class="dicpy">guǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> ——<span class="yhcs">“东莞”</span>( <span class="dicpy">Dōngguǎn</span>):市名，在广东省东江下游</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">guān;wǎn</span></p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>莞</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"
