                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>抠</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">摳</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄡˉ</span></p><ol><li>用手指或细小的东西挖。</li><li>向狭窄的方面探求、探究：～字眼儿。死～书本。</li><li>雕刻（花纹）：～镜框的花边。</li><li>吝啬：～门儿。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> raise, lift up; tight-fisted</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> geizig  (Adj)​,ausgraben  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> creuser,gratter</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>抠</strong></p><p>摳 <span class="dicpy">kōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，区声。本义:抓，提)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lift up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使数人抠耳，力掀举之。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·大力将军》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:抠衣(提起衣服前襟。表示对人恭敬);抠迎(抠衣迎接);抠裳(抠衣);抠请(抠衣延请)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挖 <span class="encs">[dig or dig out with a finger or sth.pointed;scratch;pick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抠眼睛，捻鼻子。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:在地上抠个洞;把掉在缝里的豆粒抠出来;抠心挖肚(抠心挖血。挖空心思，绞尽脑汁);抠索(掏挖，勒索)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 抓牢 <span class="encs">[seize;grasp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">卓</span>气力大，两手抠住; <span class="diczx2">吕布</span>便入，揪倒 <span class="diczx2">伍孚</span>。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>抠</strong></p><p>摳 <span class="dicpy">kōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 〈方〉∶吝啬 <span class="encs">[stingy]</span>。如:这人真抠;抠索(吝啬，寒酸);抠抠搜搜(缩缩。方言。小气，不大方)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“眍”</span>。眼睛凹陷 <span class="encs">[sunken]</span>。如;抠搂(眼珠子深陷在眼眶里)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抠哧">抠哧</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抠门儿">抠门儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抠唆，抠搜，抠挲">抠唆，抠搜，抠挲</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抠字眼">抠字眼</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
