						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>穿</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>破，透：～透。揭～。～窬（钻洞和爬墙，指盗贼）。～凿。</li><li>通过，连通：～过。～行（<span class="dicpy">xíng </span>）。</li><li>着（<span class="dicpy">zhuó</span>）衣服鞋袜：～衣。～鞋。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">穿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>穿</strong> <span class="dicpy">chuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从牙，从穴。牙在穴中，表示穿通的意思。本义:穿通，穿破)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pierce through]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穿，通也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何以穿我墉。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·行露》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何以穿我屋。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">强弩之末，势不能穿</span><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>缟。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此所谓强弩之末势不能穿鲁缟者也。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:穿墉(在墙上打洞);穿着鼻(受人摆布);穿穴(犹穿凿);穿空(犹穿孔);穿胸(胸前穿孔透背)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挖掘;开凿 <span class="encs">[dig;excavate]</span>。如:穿井(掘井);穿掘(挖掘;开凿);穿筑(挖池塘筑假山);穿壁(凿通墙壁);穿墙(墙上凿洞)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通过、透过(空隙、空间等) <span class="encs">[go through]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乱石穿空，惊涛拍岸。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">历齐河、长清，穿泰山西北谷。——</span><span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:穿针(阴历七月初七日穿针乞巧);穿心(从中间通过);穿城(穿越城镇)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 贯通;贯串 <span class="encs">[thread;pass through;bore]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赍钱三百万，皆用青线穿。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:穿鼻(用绳索或环贯穿鼻中隔);穿夜(方言。通宵);穿望(切盼。望穿眼睛的意思)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 把衣、鞋、袜等套在身体相应部位上 <span class="encs">[put on;wear]</span>。如:穿插(打扮，穿戴);穿执(穿靴执笏的省称);穿秉(谓穿礼服而执朝笏)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 败露;揭穿 <span class="encs">[reveal;expose]</span>。如:穿破(戳穿;败露);穿崩(方言。露馅)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>穿</strong> <span class="dicpy">chuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 孔;洞 <span class="encs">[hole]</span>。如:穿弊(即<span class="yhcs">“穿敝”</span>。洞穿;破烂);穿结(谓衣服洞穿和补缀);穿穴(犹钻研)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 墓穴 <span class="encs">[grave]</span>。如:穿中记(古代墓穴中的题志)</p><p>◎ <strong>穿</strong> <span class="dicpy">chuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuān.mp3"></a></span>
