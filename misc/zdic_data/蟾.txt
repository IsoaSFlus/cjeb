                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>蟾</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～蜍〕两栖动物，皮上有许多疙瘩，内有毒腺，形状像蛙。吃昆虫、蜗牛等，对农业有益。俗称&ldquo;癞蛤蟆&rdquo;、&ldquo;疥蛤蟆&rdquo;；古代称&ldquo;蟾诸&rdquo;。简称&ldquo;蟾&rdquo;，如&ldquo;～酥&rdquo;（色白，可入药）。&ldquo;～宫&rdquo;（月亮）。&ldquo;～桂&rdquo;。&ldquo;～轮&rdquo;（圆月）。&ldquo;～辉&rdquo;、&ldquo;～光&rdquo;、&ldquo;～魄&rdquo;（均指月光）。&ldquo;～宫折桂&rdquo;（旧喻科举考试登科）。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蟾</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，詹( <span class="dicpy">zhān</span>)声。本义:蟾蜍( <span class="dicpy">ch&uacute;</span>)简称&ldquo;蟾&rdquo;，即癞哈蟆)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[toad]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小蟾徐行腹如鼓。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">元好问</span><span class="smcs">《蟾池》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蟾壶(古代计时器。一种蟾蜍形的漏壶);蟾兔(蟾蜍与玉兔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 传说中月有蟾蜍。所以称月为蟾 <span class="encs">[moon]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:蟾桂(神话中的月里蟾蜍和丹桂);蟾宫(指月亮中的宫殿)</span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集中" target="_blank">申集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集中&amp;bs=虫" target="_blank">虫</a>】
                              蟾·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=19" target="_blank">19</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/87FE.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】職廉切【集韻】之廉切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音詹。【爾雅&middot;釋魚】鼃<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A4F0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪓰</span>，蟾諸。【註】似蝦蟆，居陸地，淮南謂之去蚊。【廣韻】《張衡&middot;靈憲》曰：羿請不死之藥于西王母，姮娥竊之奔月宮，蓋託身于月，是爲蟾諸。《抱朴子》曰：蟾諸壽三千歲者，頭上有角，頷下有丹書八字。【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>中記】蟾諸頭生角者，食之壽千歲。【爾雅翼】蟾蜍，今之蚵蚾，背上礧礧，好服牆隂壁下者，五月五日收之，謂之辟兵。餘詳蜍字註。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】視占切，音棎。蟾光，月彩也。　【正韻】蟾蠩之蟾與月彩之蟾同，一物而有兩音者，方言之不同也。</p>
