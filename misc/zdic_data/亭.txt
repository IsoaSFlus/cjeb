                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>亭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tíng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tíng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄧㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>有顶无墙，供休息用的建筑物，多建筑在路旁或花园里；凉～。牡丹～。</li><li>建筑得比较简单的小房子：书～。邮～。岗～。</li><li>适中，均匀：～匀。</li><li>正，当：～午（正午，中午）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>亭</strong> <span class="dicpy">tíng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tíng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。战国文字字形，其中<span class="yhcs">“T”</span>象矗立的亭柱。小篆以为从高省，丁声。本义:古代设在路旁的公房，供旅客停宿)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。后指驿亭 <span class="encs">[inn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亭，人所安定也。亭有楼。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>家因 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>十里一亭。亭，留也。——<span class="smcs">《一切经音义经》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亭，停也，亦人所停集也。——<span class="smcs">《释名·释宫释》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为</span><span class="diczx2">泗上</span>亭长。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高祖纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">安阳</span>城南有一亭，夜不可宿，宿辄杀人。—— <span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何处是归程?长亭更短亭。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《菩萨蛮》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:亭邮(驿站);亭民(驿亭附近的居民);亭寺(驿亭。寺，办公的官署);亭舍(驿亭的客舍);亭传(客栈);亭置(邮亭驿站);亭驿</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 亭子。有顶无墙，供休息用的建筑物，多建筑在路旁或花园里<span class="encs">[pavilion]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西</span><span class="diczx2">蜀</span><span class="diczx2">子云</span>亭。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">常记溪亭日暮。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《如梦令》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亭东自足下皆云漫。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亭西有岱祠。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:亭榭(亭阁台榭);亭馆(供人游憩歇宿的亭台馆舍);亭槛(亭子的栏杆;亭子);亭院(造有凉亭、楼阁等建筑物的庭园或花园);亭景(亭影);亭落(亭院;村落);亭彩(旧时办喜事扎的亭楼、铭旌)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代设在边塞观察敌情的岗亭 <span class="encs">[sentry box]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百步一亭，高垣丈四尺，厚四尺，为闺门两扇。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亭卒上楼扫除，见死妇，大惊，走白亭长。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">应劭</span><span class="smcs">《风俗通》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:亭戍(古代边境上的岗亭和营垒);亭吏(亭长);亭佐(亭长的副手);门亭(设于城门处的亭);亭卒(秦汉亭中的差役)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 像亭子的小房子 <span class="encs">[kiosk]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 如:书亭;邮亭;亭场(卖盐的地方);亭子间</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 山梨 <span class="encs">[mountain pear]</span></p><p><span
