                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鳏</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>鳏</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鰥</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">guān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guān.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>无妻或丧妻的男人：～夫。～处（<span class="dicpy">chǔ</span>）。～居。</li>
	<li>古书上说的一种大鱼。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> huge fish; widower; bachelor</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Witwer  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> célibataire,seul</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鳏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>鳏</strong></p>
<p>鰥 <span class="dicpy">guān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鱼。本义:一种大鱼)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 鱼名，即鲩鲲。又名鳡鱼 <span class="encs">[a huge fish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">卫</span>人钓于河，得鳏鱼焉，其大盈车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔丛子三抗志》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 死了妻子又未再结婚的男人 <span class="encs">[widower]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有鳏在下，曰虞舜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老而无妻曰鳏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">江子我</span><span class="diczx2">瑞友</span>知经明道，驰誉中外。后尽弃旧业，鳏居孑然。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王明清</span><span class="smcs">《挥尘录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:鳏居(老年男子无妻而独居);鳏民(鳏夫);鳏旷(鳏男和旷女);鳏居(男子无偶而独居);鳏男(无妻的成年男子);鳏孤(孤老，泛指无劳力而独居无依靠的人)。又比喻愁思不眠。如:鳏鳏(因愁思而无法闭目成眠)</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中" target="_blank">亥集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集中&amp;bs=魚" target="_blank">魚</a>】
                              鰥·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=21" target="_blank">21</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9C25.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29D8A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩶊</span>【唐韻】古頑切【集韻】【韻會】姑頑切【正韻】姑還切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音關。【說文】魚也。【詩&middot;齊風】其魚魴鰥。【傳】大魚也。【孔叢子&middot;抗志篇】衞人釣於河，得鰥魚焉，其大盈車。子思問曰：如何得之。對曰：吾垂一魴之餌，鰥過而不視，更以豚之半，則吞矣。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;堯典】有鰥在下曰虞舜。【傳】無妻曰鰥。【禮&middot;王制】老而無妻曰鰥。【釋名】鰥，昆也。昆，明也。愁悒不寐，目恆鰥鰥然也。故其字从魚，魚目恆不閉者也。【白虎通】鰥之言鰥鰥無所親，則寡者少也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】公渾切，音昆。與鯤同。【爾雅&middot;釋魚疏】鰥，魚子。鰥鯤字異，蓋古字通用也。</p>
<p>又與瘝同。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】鰥，病也。【鄭樵註】鰥，卽瘝也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>古幻切，音慣。視貌。或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25240.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥉀</span>。</p>
<p>又叶眞韻。【楚辭&middot;天問】舜閔在家，父何以鰥。堯不姚合，二女何親。</p>
