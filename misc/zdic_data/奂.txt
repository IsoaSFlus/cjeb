<p>◎ <strong>奂</strong></p>
<p>奐 <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 互易。后作&ldquo;换&rdquo; <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奂，取奂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 呼唤。后作&ldquo;唤&rdquo; <span class="encs">[call]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唤，呼也。古通作&ldquo;奂&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>奂</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 盛大;众多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奂，众多也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可以知古，可以察今，奂然而与民壹始。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;四代》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奂，盛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;韦元成传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奂然(盛大的样子);奂衍(众多散布的样子);奂烂(繁盛的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 文彩鲜明的。后作&ldquo;焕&rdquo; <span class="encs">[bright;brilliant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美哉奂焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奂奂(文彩灿烂的样子);奂若(色彩鲜明的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 悠闲的，涣散的。后作&ldquo;涣&rdquo; <span class="encs">[leisurely]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伴奂尔游矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;卷阿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君臣上下之际，奂然有离德者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传&middot;卷三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">业集累积，奂衍于其侧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;嵇康&middot;琴赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奂衍(满布的样子)</span></p>
