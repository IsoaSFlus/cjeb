						<p>● <strong>帽</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>盖头的东西：～子。草～。凉～。军～。礼～。</li>
	<li>器物的顶罩或套儿：笔～儿。螺丝～儿。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hat, cap; cap-like tops</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Hut, Mütze  (S)​,Kappe, Deckel, Verschluss  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> chapeau,bonnet,étui</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">帽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>帽</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从巾，冒声。巾是丝织品，表示材料。字本作&ldquo;月&rdquo;，象帽形，又作&ldquo;冒&rdquo;。本义:帽子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少年见罗敷，脱帽著帩头。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;陌上桑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:帽凭(盈满的样子);帽正(帽子前缘正中的装饰，一般为珠或玉);帽襻儿(帽子上布制的扣纽带);礼帽(跟礼服相配的帽子);帽套(加于帽外的衣饰);帽钉(即铆钉。因其头形如帽，故名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 形状或作用像帽子或各种保护头部的物件 <span class="encs">[helmet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">投笔掷帽丁丁然，磨墨隆隆然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;口技》</span>:注。此指笔套。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笔帽(套在笔头上保护笔的套儿);安全帽;螺钉帽</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>帽</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>戴帽 <span class="encs">[put on a cap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见人头未帽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆龟蒙</span><span class="smcs">《南泾渔夫》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">帽</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中" target="_blank">寅集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中&amp;bs=巾" target="_blank">巾</a>】
                              帽·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5E3D.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>莫報切，音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/276D5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧛕</span>。【釋名】冒也。【說文】本作冃。【徐鉉曰】今作帽。帽名猶冠。義取蒙覆其首，本纚也。古者冠無帽，冠下有纚，以繒爲之。後人因之帽於冠，或裁纚爲帽。自乗輿宴居，下至庶人無爵者，皆服之。江左時，野人已著帽，人士亦往往而見，但無頂圈矣，後乃高其屋云。【隋書&middot;禮儀志】帽，自天子下及庶人通冠之，以白紗者名高頂帽。</p>
<p>又有繒皂雜紗爲之，高屋下裙，蓋無定準。【唐書&middot;車服志】烏紗帽者，視事及燕見賔客之服也。</p>
<p>又通作冒。【史記&middot;絳侯世家】薄太后以冒絮提文帝。【註】應劭曰：陌額絮也。晉灼曰：巴蜀異物志謂，頭上巾爲冒絮。</p>

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5183_396.svg" /><span class="swbf">(冃)</span><span class="ywyy">小兒及蠻夷頭衣也。</span><span class="swdz">謂此二種人之頭衣也。小兒未冠，夷狄未能言冠，故不冠而冃。荀卿曰：古之王者，有務而拘領者矣。楊注：務讀爲冒，拘與勾同。淮南書曰：古者有鍪而綣領以王天下者。高注：古者葢三皇以前也。鍪著兜鍪帽，言未知制冠。按高注兜鍪二字葢淺人所加。務與鍪皆讀爲冃，冃卽今之帽字也。後聖有作，因冃以制冠冕，而冃遂爲小兒蠻夷頭衣。</span><span class="ywyy">从冂。二，其飾也。</span><span class="swdz">古報切。古音在三部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡冃之屬皆从冃。</span></p>                          </div>
