                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">掬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>掬</strong> <span class="dicpy">jū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 两手相合捧物 <span class="encs">[hold in both hands]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受珠玉者以掬。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“两手曰掬。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">舟中之指可掬也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十二年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“两手可掬。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:以手掬水;掬示(捧出来示众，昭示)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 置于<span class="yhcs">“可”</span>后，指可以用手捧住。多用来描写景色鲜明或情绪充溢 <span class="encs">[scoop]</span>。如:笑容可掬;娇憨可掬;恐怖之色可掬</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抓取，取 <span class="encs">[grasp]</span>。如:掬抛(谓或取或弃。犹摆布)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 卷起;卷缩 <span class="encs">[curl]</span>。如:掬缩(卷曲收缩);掬蹙(卷缩)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 撅;翘起 <span class="encs">[protrude]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">八戒</span>采着马，掬着嘴，摆着耳朵。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>掬</strong> <span class="dicpy">jū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p>犹捧。指两手相合所捧的量。如:掬壤(一捧之土);一掬之泪</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/掬诚">掬诚</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/掬饮">掬饮</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">掬</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>掬</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄩˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>jú <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3425.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用两手捧取。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>掬</b>泉」、「掬水而饮」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·段成式《酉阳杂俎·卷三·贝编》：「上令左右<b>掬</b>庭水嗅之，果有檀香气。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">情态显露于外，似可用手抓取。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「笑容可<b>掬</b>」、「真情可<b>掬</b>」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">噘、翘起。</span><span class="gc_yy">《西游记·第五四回》：「八戒采著马，<b>掬</b>著嘴，摆著耳朵。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】
                              掬·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/63AC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>居六切，音菊。與匊同。兩手曰掬。【禮·曲禮】受珠玉者以掬。【疏】謂手中也。【左傳·宣十二年】舟中之指可掬也。</p><p>又【詩詁】兩手曰臼，屈掌曰匊。</p><p>又【小爾雅】掬，一升也。今俗謂兩手所奉爲一掬，則數合也。</p><p>又【揚子·方言】離也。齊<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>曰斯。燕之外郊，朝鮮洌水之閒曰掬。　【說文】在手曰匊。俗作掬。【廣韻】與<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22C6C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢱬</span>同。【韻會】本作揈。【正字通】本作臼。</p>

                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“掬”字头，请参考“匊”字：)</span>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九">卷九</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九&amp;swbs=勹">勹</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/530A.svg">                          <p>在手曰匊。从勹米。居六切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗作掬，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/530A_564.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(匊)</span><span class="ywyy">在手曰匊。</span><span class="swdz">唐風。椒聊之實。蕃衍盈匊。小雅。終朝采綠。不盈一匊。毛皆云。兩手曰匊。此云在手。恐傳寫之誤。手部曰。持、握也。握、搤持也。搤、捉也。捉、搤也。把握也。然則在手曰捉、曰搤、曰握、曰持、曰把。不曰匊也。據篇、韵所言則許書之譌久矣。玉篇曰。古文作臼。此語尤誤。臼者、叉手也。叉者、手指相錯也。廣韵以兩手奉物訓臼、誤矣。方言曰。掬、離也。燕之外郊朝鮮洌水之閒曰掬。此方俗殊語。不係乎本字也。</span><span class="ywyy">从勹米。</span><span class="swdz">會意。米至<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22F73.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢽳</span>。兩手兜之而聚。居六切。三部。俗作掬。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
