                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>爨</strong> <span class="dicpy">cu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 烧火做饭 <span class="encs">[cook]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爨，炊也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">取其进火谓之爨，取其气上谓之炊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文系传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">许子</span>以釜甑爨，以铁耕乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:爨人(爨夫。厨师);爨室(厨房);爨妇(执炊的女人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 烧;烧煮 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常若微雷响，以草爨之，则烟腾火发。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水经注》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>爨</strong> <span class="dicpy">cu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 炉灶。一种土、陶制的厨房炉子、灶 <span class="encs">[an earthern kitchen stove]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翻怜爨下劳薪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张炎</span><span class="smcs">《风人松》</span></p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集中" target="_blank">巳集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集中&amp;bs=火" target="_blank">火</a>】
                              爨·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=29" target="_blank">29</a>　·部外筆画：25</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7228.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24456.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤑖</span>【廣韻】七亂切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】取亂切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25A25.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥨥</span>。【說文】齊謂之炊爨。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26951.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥑</span>象持甑，冂爲竈口，廾推林內火。【玉篇】竈也。【詩&middot;小雅】執爨踖踖。【傳】爨，饔爨，稟爨也。【疏】饔爨以煑肉，廩爨以炊米。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;亨人】職外內饔之爨亨煑。【註】爨，今之竈。主於其竈煑物。【儀禮&middot;士昏禮】大羹湆在爨。【註】爨，火上。</p>
<p>又姓。【華陽國志】昌寧大姓有爨習。【蜀志】建寧大姓。蜀錄有交州刺史爨深。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作熶。詳熶字註。</p>
<p>又取絹切，音縓。炊也。</p>
<p>又七丸切，音攛。義同。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;挈壷氏】及冬，則以火爨鼎水，而沸之而沃之。【註】以火炊水。◎按周禮，詩傳，釋文，爨俱音七絃切，釋經傳爨字之義，大約動音爲平聲，靜音爲去聲。然左傳宣十五年傳：析骸以爨。註：爨，炊也。及孟子，以<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2894F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨥏</span>甑爨。似皆宜讀平聲。乃孟子無釋文。左傳，釋文，亦音七亂反。則平去又未嘗拘也。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7228_3764.svg" /><span class="swbf">(爨)</span><span class="ywyy">齊謂炊爨。</span><span class="swdz">各本謂下衍之字，今正。火部曰：炊，爨也。然則二字互相訓。孟子趙注曰：爨，炊也。齊謂炊爨者，齊人謂炊曰爨。古言謂則不言曰，如毛傳婦人謂嫁歸是也。特牲、少牢禮注皆曰：爨，竈也。此因爨必於竈，故謂竈爲爨。禮器燔柴於爨同。楚茨傳曰：爨，饔爨廩爨也。此謂竈。又曰：踖踖，爨竈有容也。此謂炊。</span><span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7228_1.png" src="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7228_1.png">象持甑。</span><span class="swdz">中似甑，臼持之。今本<img class="lazy" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7228_1.png" src="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7228_1.png">譌臼。</span><span class="ywyy">冖爲竈口，推林內火。</span><span class="swdz">林，柴也。內同納。</span><span class="ywyy">凡爨之屬皆从爨。</span><span class="swdz">七亂切。十四部。</span></p>
