                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>女</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nǚ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǚ.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄩˇ</span></p><ol><li>女性，与“<span class="diczx3">男</span>”相对。古代以未婚的为“女”，已婚的为“<span class="diczx3">妇</span>”。现通称“妇女”：～人。～士。～流（含轻蔑意）。少（<span class="dicpy">shào </span>）～。</li><li>以女儿作为人的妻（旧读<span class="dicpy">nǜ</span>）。</li><li>星名，二十八宿之一。亦称“婺女”、“须女”。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>女</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">rǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">汝</span>”，你。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">女</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>女</strong> <span class="dicpy">nǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象一个敛手跪着的人形。本义:女性， 女人，与<span class="yhcs">“男”</span>相对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ woman]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">女，妇人也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王育</span>说:<span class="yhcs">“对文则处子曰女，适人曰妇。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">女也不爽，士贰其行。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一女不织，或受之寒。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">女行无偏斜，何意致不厚。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">男女衣著。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">男女无别。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·亡征》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如倩女之靧。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">男女奔窜。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:女陪堂(女帮闲);女先儿(女先生);女红(泛指妇女干的纺织、缝纫、刺绣等);女冠(女道士);女兄(姐姐);女伯(称父亲的姐姐);女嬖(受君主宠爱的女人);女隶(女官。被没入宫中为奴的女子);女中丈夫(女子中有男子气概的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申指女儿 <span class="encs">[daughter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请</span><span class="diczx2">句践</span>女女于王。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窈窕淑女，君子好逑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·关睢》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何氏之女。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·列女传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">女</span><span
