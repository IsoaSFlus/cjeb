						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>拉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄚˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>牵，扯，拽：～车。～网。～下马（喻使某人下台）。</li>    <li>用车载运：～货。</li>    <li>使延长：～延。～伸。</li>    <li>排泄粪便：～屎。</li>    <li>联络：～拢。～帮结伙。</li>    <li>摧折：摧枯～朽。</li>    <li>带领转移：把部队～出去。</li>    <li>牵引乐器的某一部分使乐器发出声音：～小提琴。</li>    <li>抚养子女：～扯。</li>    <li>帮助：他有困难，～他一把。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>拉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lá.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄚˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>割，用刀把东西切开一道缝或切断：手上～了一个口子。</li>    <li>闲谈：～话。～家常。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">拉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拉</strong> <span class="dicpy">lā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，立声。本义:摧折，折断)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[break]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拉，摧也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使力士</span><span class="diczx2">彭生</span>拉杀 <span class="diczx2">鲁桓公</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记·齐世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拉捭摧藏。——<span class="smcs">《文选·左思·吴都赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拉干而杀之。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·庄公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">范雎</span>拉胁折齿于 <span class="diczx2">魏</span>。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·邹阳传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:摧枯拉朽(摧折枯枝朽木。比喻极容易办到);拉杂(折断弄碎。后用以指杂乱而没有条理);拉齿(折齿);拉枯(摧毁枯朽之物。比喻轻而易举)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 牵;引;扯;拽 <span class="encs">[pull;drag;tug]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谁能拉花住，争换得春回。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《花下醉中联句》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:拉手(满人久别见面的一种礼节);拉拉(趿拉，即将鞋后帮踩在脚后跟下面);拉人(拉夫);拉火线(手榴弹等引爆的拉线)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 击，打 <span class="encs">[strike]</span>。如:拉三拳(打三拳);拉杀(用杖击杀)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 拉拢;联络 <span class="encs">[draw in]</span>。如:拉马(拉拢双方搞不正当的关系);打一派拉一派;拉山头(为私利而组织小集团)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 辛勤抚养;帮助 <span class="encs">[bring
