                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谬</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">謬</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">miù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧㄡˋ</span></p><ol><li>错误的，不合情理的：荒～。～论。～传（<span class="dicpy">chuán </span>）。～误。</li><li>差错：失之毫厘，～以千里。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谬</strong></p><p>謬 <span class="dicpy">miù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:极端错误，非常不合情理)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[err;false;wrong]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谬，狂者之妄言也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">考之三王而不谬。——礼记·中庸》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下悦之以谬悠之说。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">解心之谬。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·庚桑楚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故<span class="smcs">《易》</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“差以毫厘，谬以千里。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马迁传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">园喜以谬赏。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故闻之而不见，虽博必谬。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·儒效》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今言<span class="yhcs">“华”</span>如<span class="yhcs">“华实”</span>之<span class="yhcs">“华”</span>者，盖音谬也。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:谬戾(荒谬乖僻);谬谈(谬语妄说);谬举(妄举，举用不适当的人才);谬种流传(荒谬错误的文章或议论辗转流传于世)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谬</strong></p><p>謬 <span class="dicpy">miù</span> <span
