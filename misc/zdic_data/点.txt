                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">点</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>点</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">點</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">diǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>细小的痕迹或物体：～滴。斑～。～子（➊液体的小滴，如&ldquo;水～～&rdquo;；➋小的痕迹，如&ldquo;油～～&rdquo;；➌打击乐器演奏时的节拍，如&ldquo;鼓～～&rdquo;；➍主意，办法，如&ldquo;请大家出～～&rdquo;；➎最能说明问题的关键地方，如&ldquo;话没有说到～～上&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>几何学上指没有长、宽、厚而只有位置的几何图形；两条线相交处或线段的两端。</li>
	<li>数学上表示小数部分开始的符号（.），称&ldquo;小数点&rdquo;，如&ldquo;231.4&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>量词，用于小的或少的：两三～雨。几～泪水。</li>
	<li>一定的位置或限度：地～。起～。极～。居民～。</li>
	<li>项，部分，方面：优～。要～。特～。</li>
	<li>汉字笔形之一（丶）：～画。三～水。</li>
	<li>加上点子，引申为修饰：标～。评～。～缀。画龙～睛。</li>
	<li>使一点一滴地落下或发出：～种。～射（自动武器有间歇的射击）。～眼药。</li>
	<li>一落一起或一触即离的动作：～头。～穴。</li>
	<li>引火：～火（亦喻挑起是非，制造事端）。</li>
	<li>查对：～数。～名。～卯（旧时称官衙、军伍卯时开始办公、操练，官员查点人数）。</li>
	<li>指定，选派：～菜。～将（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng </span>）。听众～播。</li>
	<li>指示，启发：指～。～拨。</li>
	<li>计时的单位：更～（分为五更，一更又分五点）。三更三～。钟～。</li>
	<li>污：～污。～辱（使受污辱）。</li>
	<li>指正餐以外的暂时充饥，亦指糕饼一类的食物：～心（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">心</span>&rdquo;读轻声）。～补（吃少量的食品解饿。&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">补</span>&rdquo;读轻声）。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/踮">踮</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">点</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>点</strong></p>
<p>點 <span class="dicpy">diǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从黑，占声。本义:斑点)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 细小的黑色斑痕 <span class="encs">[spot;dot;speck]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">点，黑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，小黑曰点。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:墨点儿;斑点;污点;点缺(瑕疵，缺点);点漆(形容极黑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 小滴 <span class="encs">[drop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七八个星天外，两三点雨山前。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《西江月》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:雨点;掉点儿(落下稀疏的雨点);点叶(叶上的斑点);点璧(白璧的斑点)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 汉字笔画名 <span class="encs">[point]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:横、竖、撇、点、折。又指旧时读书标明句逗及品评文章所用&ldquo;、&rdquo;号及其动作。如:点撇(文字的点和撇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 数学名词。数学上表示小数的符号叫小数点，省称为&ldquo;点&rdquo;。 <span class="encs">[decimal point]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 几何系统，尤指欧几里得的几何系统中未下明确定义的成分之一 <span class="encs">[point]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:两点之间直线最短</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古时夜间的计时单位。一夜分五更，一更又分五点 <span class="encs">[dian， one fifth of one of the five two-hour periods into which the night was formerly divided]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五更三点索金车，尽放宫人出看花。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《宫词一百首》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 点钟，时间单位，等于时钟每昼夜的二十四分之一 <span class="encs">[o&#39;clock]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:上午九点</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 糕饼类小食 <span class="encs">[pastry]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:早点;名点;茶点</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 方面 <span class="encs">[aspect]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:特点;重点;从这点上去看</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 节奏;节拍 <span class="encs">[beat]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:点拍(音乐的节拍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 一种特制响器，两端作云状，名为云板，也称点。旧时官署、邸宅以打点为报事集众的信号 <span class="encs">[board]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">雨村</span>尚未看完，忽闻传点。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 运销各式各样产品、供应和设备的零售店，最初是为人口稀少地区服务的 <span class="encs">[country store]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:据说龚文保在白湘寺办点</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引申为玷污 <span class="encs">[stain]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:点污(玷污，特指奸污);点染(玷污)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用笔加点。多用以描绘物象，审识章句，或表示删除的意思 <span class="encs">[put a dot;delete]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灭谓之点。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释器》</span>。注:&ldquo;以笔灭字为点。&rdquo;</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 中，着 <span class="encs">[hit]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:点鼓(击鼓)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 装饰 <span class="encs">[adorn]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:点景(装饰盆景);点缀;点衬(装点)</span></p>
