                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">董</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>董</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨㄥˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>监督管理：～统。～督。～正（督察纠正）。～理。～事（某些企业、学校的资产所有者推举出来代表自己监督和主持业务的人）。～其成。</li>
	<li>正：&ldquo;余将～道而不豫兮&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>深藏：&ldquo;年六十已上，气当大～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> direct, supervise; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dong  (Eig)​,Chef; Direktor</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> administrer,diriger,surveiller,superviser,administrateur,directeur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">董</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>董</strong></p>
<p>蕫 <span class="dicpy">dǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，重声。①本义:草名，即鼎蕫。②督察)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 监督;督察 <span class="encs">[supervise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">董之用威。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大禹谟》</span>。传:&ldquo;董，督也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">董逋逃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">董之以武师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出则监察而董是非。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;陈忠传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽董之以严刑，振之以威怒，终苟免而不怀仁，貌恭而不心服。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:董正(监督纠正);董统鹰扬(为督察纲纪而大展雄才);董治(监督管理);董摄(监督治理)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 统率 <span class="encs">[command]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:董一(统一主持;一统);董率(董帅。统率;领导);董督(统率)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 主持;主管 <span class="encs">[take charge of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:董成(主持和谈)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 正，守正 <span class="encs">[rectify]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">董，正也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">随</span>人使少师董成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;桓公六年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而辱使董振择之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余将董道而不豫兮，固将重昏而终身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:董道(守正道);董役(正其事理);董齐(征伐之使归一统)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>董</strong> <span class="dicpy">dǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 今简称董事为董 <span class="encs">[director]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:校董;董事会(某些公私合营企业、中外合资企业或私人所办企业、学校、团体等的领导机构)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古地名。春秋时晋地，在今山西省万荣县境 <span class="encs">[Dong]</span></p>

<ol>

</ol></div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">董</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              董·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8463.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26C66.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦱦</span>【唐韻】多動切【集韻】覩動切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音懂。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】董，督，正也。【書&middot;大禹謨】董之用威。</p>
<p>又【博雅】固也。</p>
<p>又深藏也。【史記&middot;倉公傳】氣當大董。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;春官】辨九<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22DCE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢷎</span>，四日振動。【鄭註】動讀爲董。書亦或爲董。振董，以兩手相擊也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】藕根也。</p>
<p>又董蕖。【續博物志】董蕖者，婆羅門云阿苗根，似白芷。</p>
<p>又亭名。【左傳&middot;文六年】改蒐于董。【註】河東汾隂縣有董亭。</p>
<p>又澤名。【後漢&middot;郡國志】文喜邑有董池陂，古董澤。</p>
<p>又姓。【左傳&middot;昭二十九年】昔有飂叔安，有裔子曰董父，實甚好龍，龍多歸之，服事帝舜，賜之姓曰董。</p>
<p>又【宣二年】董狐，古之良史也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】主勇切，音腫。【羣經音辨】短也。《左傳》余髮董董，今本作種種。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】董正之董，讀若督，東谷切。　【集韻】通作蕫。</p>
