                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">流</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>流</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>液体移动：～水。～汗。～血。～泪。～程。～泻。～质。～水不腐。汗～浃背。随波逐～（随着波浪起伏，跟着流水漂荡，喻没有主见，随着潮流走）。</li>
	<li>像水那样流动不定：～转（<span class="dicpy">zhuǎn </span>）。～通。～寇。～浪。～离。～散。～失。～沙。～露。～萤。</li>
	<li>传播：～言。～传。～芳。～弊。～毒。～行（<span class="dicpy">x&iacute;ng </span>）。</li>
	<li>指江河的流水：河～。江～。溪～。激～。奔～。</li>
	<li>像水流的东西：气～。暖～。电～。</li>
	<li>向坏的方面转变：～于形式。</li>
	<li>旧时的刑罚，把犯人送到荒远的地方去：～放。～配。</li>
	<li>品类，等级：～辈。～派。</li>
	<li>指不正派：二～子。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">流</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>流</strong> <span class="dicpy">li&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。本义:水流动)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[flow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">观其流泉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;公刘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顄淡滂流。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;马融&middot;长笛赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">血流殷地。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">黄河</span>流水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;林兰诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">碧水东流自此回。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《望天门山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桃花流水鳜鱼肥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张志和</span><span class="smcs">《渔歌子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:流迸(涌流而出);流水行云(流动的水和飘浮的云彩);流冰(冰块在河面上漂动和流动);流泉(流动的泉水);引申为流动。如:流丹(流动的红色;指流血);流脓搭水(流脓水)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 顺水漂流 <span class="encs">[drift about;be driven by the current]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">譬彼舟流，不知所届。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小弁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:流觞(在环曲水道;旁宴聚时，将杯放入水中，任其漂流，如停在某人面前，某人当即取饮);流柿(江中漂流的木片);流泊(在水面漂流);流花(水面漂流的落花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 漂没;移动不定;流浪 <span class="encs">[lead a wandering life]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子和而流。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:流穴(辖转流落，居无定所);流沔(流连沉溺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 传布;扩散 <span class="encs">[propagate;diffuse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其故家贵俗，流风善政，犹有存者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑上》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6D41.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㳅【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>力求切，音留。【說文】水行也。【爾雅&middot;釋言】流，覃也。【疏】謂水之流，必相延及。【詩&middot;大雅】如川之流。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】流，擇也，求也。【詩&middot;周南】左右流之。</p>
<p>又下也。【詩&middot;豳風】七月流火。</p>
<p>又流漫無節制也。【禮&middot;樂記】樂勝則流。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;禹貢】二百里流。【禮&middot;王制】千里之外曰采，曰流。</p>
<p>又放也。【書&middot;舜典】流宥五<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>。</p>
<p>又視不端諦曰流。【左傳&middot;成六年】鄭伯視流而行速。</p>
<p>又走也。【戰國策】楚襄王流淹于城陽。【註】謂走而自匿。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;冬官&middot;玉人註】鼻勺曰流，凡流皆爲龍口。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;食貨志】朱提銀重八兩爲一流。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;藝文志】儒家流，道家流，隂陽家流，法家流，墨家流，縱橫家流，雜家流，農家流，小說家流，各有所從出，分九家。</p>
<p>又流布也。【易&middot;謙卦】地道變盈而流謙。</p>
<p>又流轉也。【禮&middot;仲尼燕居】周流無不徧。</p>
<p>又【張協&middot;七命】傾罍一朝，流湎千日。【註】齊顏色，均衆寡謂之流。</p>
<p>又長流，官名。【顏之推&middot;家訓】名治獄參軍爲長流。</p>
<p>又黃流，酒名。【詩&middot;大雅】黃流在中。</p>
<p>又雙流，縣名。【廣輿記】屬成都府。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23F0A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣼊</span>如切，音閭。【陸雲詩】樂奏聲哀，言發涕流。唯願君子，德與福俱。</p>
<p>又叶力救切，音溜。【博<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤣥</span>詩】悠悠建平，皇澤未流。朝選于衆，乃子之授。</p>
