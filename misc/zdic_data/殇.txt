<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> die young; national mourning</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> jung sterben  (V)​,sterben  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mourir jeune,mort de guerre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">殇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>殇</strong></p><p>殤 <span class="dicpy">shāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从歺( <span class="dicpy">è</span>)，伤省声。<span class="yhcs">“歺”</span>是剔肉剩下的骨头，与<span class="yhcs">“死”</span>有关。本义:未成年而死)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。亦称<span class="yhcs">“殇折”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“殇夭”</span> <span class="encs">[die young]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">殇，不成人也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">年十九至十六为长殇，十五至十二为中殇，十一至八岁为下殇，不满八岁以下为无服之殇。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·丧服传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未家短折曰殇。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·谥法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">固知一死生为虚诞，齐彭殇为亡作。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王羲之</span><span class="smcs">《兰亭集序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:殇折，殇夭(夭折)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 横死，非正常死亡 <span class="encs">[die a violent death]</span>。如:殇亡(横死)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>殇</strong></p><p>殤 <span class="dicpy">shāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 未成年而死的人。亦称<span class="yhcs">“殇子”</span> <span class="encs">[die-young person]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">病变而药不变，向之寿民，今为殇子矣。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:殇服(古代为殇亡者居丧的服制);殇宫(殇者的灵魂)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 死在外面的人;战死者 <span class="encs">[the dead in a battle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无主之鬼谓之殇。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禁迁葬者与嫁殇者。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·媒氏》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓嫁死人也。今时娶会是也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">投躯报明主，身死为国殇。——</span><span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《代出自蓟北门行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:国殇(为国牺牲死于战场的人)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
