<p>● <strong>创</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">創</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chu&agrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨㄤˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>开始，开始做：～造。～制。首～。开～。～立。～演。～议。</li>
	<li>独特的：～见。～意。～举。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">创</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>创</strong></p>
<p>創 <span class="dicpy">chuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。从刀，仓声。金文，象一个躺着的人，手上脚上都有小竖，表示受了创伤，是个指事字。本义:创伤，伤口)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[damage;wound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刅，伤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">创钜者其日久。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;三年问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">项王</span>身亦被十八余创。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>复击 <span class="diczx2">轲</span>，被八创。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不呼则杖其背，尽创。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:身受重创;创痕(伤痕);创病(伤病);创残(伤残);创瘢(伤疤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 疮疖。皮肤或粘膜上的溃烂处。通&ldquo;疮&rdquo; <span class="encs">[scar]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:创疤(伤疤);创痏(疮伤;受伤)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>创</strong></p>
<p>創 <span class="dicpy">chuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 损伤;伤害 <span class="encs">[injure]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5275.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕戧<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20740.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠝀</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206F2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛲</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】初良切【正韻】初莊切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音瘡。【說文】傷也。本作刅，或作創。【徐曰】此正刀創字。言刃所傷也。【前漢&middot;曹參傳】身被七十創。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;班固敘傳】禮義是創。【註】師古曰：創，始造之，音初良反。</p>

<p>又傷也。【前漢&middot;匈奴傳】人民創艾戰<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28DD6.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨷖</span>。</p>
<p>又懲也。【書&middot;益稷】子創若時。【註】禹自言懲丹朱之惡，不敢慢遊也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】千羊切，音鏘。瑲，或作創。瑲瑲，玉聲。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】古文瘡字。【禮&middot;曲禮】頭有創，則沐。餘詳疒部十畫。</p>
