						<p>● <strong>瘢</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>疮痕，疤瘌，斑点：刀～。疮～。汗～。雀～。</li>
	<li>皮肤上的一点：&ldquo;美玉可以减～。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>缺点或过失。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瘢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瘢</strong> <span class="dicpy">bān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 创伤或疮疖等愈后的疤痕 <span class="encs">[scab;scar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瘢，痍也。从疒，般声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瘢，痕也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视其面，果有瘢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;朱博传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瘢疣(瘢痕和赘疣);瘢夷(创伤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 皮肤上的斑点 <span class="encs">[mark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两边腮上微微的几点雀瘢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瘢迹(瘢痕;痕迹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 比喻缺点或过失 <span class="encs">[error]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:瘢疵;瘢痍(比喻过失，缺点)</span></p>
