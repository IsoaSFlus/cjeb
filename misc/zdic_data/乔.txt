                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>乔</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">喬</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>高：～木。～松。～岳。～迁（自低处升高处。后喻人搬到好地方居住或升官）。</li>
	<li>木名：～梓。</li>
	<li>做假，装：～装。</li>
	<li>无赖，狡诈：～才。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> tall, lofty; proud, stately</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Hochwald  (S, Bio)​,sich verkleiden  (V)​,groß (gewachsen)​  (Adj)​,hoch  (Adj)​,Qiao  (Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> grand (de taille)​,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乔</strong></p>
<p>喬 <span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从夭，从高省，高亦声。夭，象人(大)行走的样子。本义:高)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 高耸。多用以形容树木 <span class="encs">[tall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乔，高而曲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厥木惟乔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山有乔松。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;山有扶苏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南有乔木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;汉广》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乔木世家(指贵族世家。乔木代指贵族、高官);乔松(高大的松树);乔柯(高枝);乔峰(高峰);乔乔(高大的样子);乔竦(高耸);乔干(高高的树干);乔树(高大的树)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 詈词。宋元以后，曲中用乔者甚多，常随文为释，含有坏、窝囊、糊涂、狡猾、怯懦无用等贬义。形容某种恶劣的表现 <span class="encs">[bad]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乔才心邪，不来一月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金瓶梅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乔人(无赖，坏蛋);乔人物(无赖;怪人);乔材(亦作&ldquo;乔才&rdquo;。无赖，恶棍);乔男女(恶徒);乔相识(不诚实的怪伙伴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;骄&rdquo;。骄傲 <span class="encs">[arrogant;assuming]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小人贫斯约，富斯乔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;坊记》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>乔</strong></p>
<p>喬 <span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> 做作;装假 <span class="encs">[disguise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戏子乔装打扮混了进来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乔模乔样(装模做样);乔张乔致(乔张致);乔做作(装腔作势);乔神道(装神弄鬼);乔乔(乔模乔样);乔断案(指装腔作势耍威风);乔乔画画(装扮);乔公道(装好意;假作公平)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>乔</strong></p>
<p>喬 <span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;峤&rdquo;。尖峭的高山 <span class="encs">[high mountain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二矛重乔，河上乎逍遥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;清人》</span></span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">喬</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“乔”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/喬">喬</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上" target="_blank">丑集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集上&amp;bs=口" target="_blank">口</a>】
                              喬·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/55AC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】巨嬌切【集韻】【韻會】渠嬌切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音僑。【說文】高而曲也，从夭从高省。【爾雅&middot;釋木】句如羽喬。【註】樹枝曲卷，似鳥毛羽。【又】下句曰朻，上句曰喬，如木楸曰喬。【又】小枝上繚曰喬。【書&middot;禹貢】厥木惟喬。【傳】喬，高也。【詩&middot;周南】南有喬木。【傳】喬，上竦也。</p>
<p>又矛之上句曰喬。【詩&middot;鄭風】二矛重喬。【傳】重喬，累荷也。【箋】喬，矛矜，近上及室題所以縣毛羽。</p>
<p>又姓。【後漢&middot;光武紀】雲中太守喬扈。</p>
<p>又人名。【戰國策】世世稱孤，而有喬松之壽。【註】喬，王子喬。松，赤松。</p>
<p>又通橋。【詩&middot;周南&middot;喬木釋文】亦作橋喬。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】舉橋切【集韻】居妖切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音驕。【書&middot;禹貢惟喬釋文】喬，徐音驕。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;鄭風&middot;重喬釋文】喬，居橋反。</p>
<p>又與驕通。【禮&middot;樂記】齊音敖辟喬志。【釋文】喬，音驕。本或作驕。</p>
<p>又【表記】喬而野。</p>
<p>又【集韻】丘祅切，音蹺。喬詰，意不平。【莊子&middot;在宥篇】天下始喬詰卓鷙。【釋文】喬，欽消反，或云去夭反，郭音矯，李音驕。</p>
<p>又【集韻】舉夭切，音矯。</p>
<p>又祛矯切，音槁。義<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。</p>
<p>又渠廟切，音轎。木枝上曲。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">喬</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“乔”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/喬">喬</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=夭">夭</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/55AC.svg">                          <p>高而曲也。从夭，从高省。《詩》曰：&ldquo;南有喬木。&rdquo;巨嬌切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/55AC_863.svg" /><span class="swbf">(喬)</span><span class="ywyy">高而曲也。</span><span class="swdz">爾雅釋詁、詩伐木時邁傳皆曰：喬，高也。釋木曰：上句曰喬，句如羽喬。漢廣傳曰：喬，上竦也。按喬不專謂木，淺人以說木則作橋，如鄭風山有橋松是也。以說山則作嶠，釋山銳而高嶠是也。皆俗字耳。許云高而曲，卽爾雅之上句如羽。木有如是者，他物亦有如是者。</span><span class="ywyy">从夭，从高省。</span><span class="swdz">會意。以其曲，故从夭。巨嬌切。二部。依小徐本無詩曰南有喬木六字。</span></p>                          </div>
