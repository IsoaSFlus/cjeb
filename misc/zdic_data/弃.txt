						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>弃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>舍去，扔掉：抛～。遗～。～权。～市（古代在闹市执行死刑，并将尸体暴露街头）。～世（超出世俗或指去世）。～养（父母死亡的婉辞）。～置不顾。～瑕录用。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> reject, abandon, discard</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ablegen, ausschalten ,aufgeben ,verscherzen, wegwerfen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> abandonner,rejeter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弃</strong></p>
<p>棄 <span class="dicpy">q&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，上面是个头向上的婴孩，三点表示羊水，头向上表示逆产。中间是只簸箕 ，下面是两只手。合起来表示将不吉利的逆产儿倒掉之意。小篆写法大同小异。本义:扔掉;抛弃)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[throw away;discard;abandon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃，捐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;弃者，不孝子人所弃也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天之弃商也久矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抛子弃草间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王粲</span><span class="smcs">《七哀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举以予人，如弃草芥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敌弃炮仓皇遁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子孙弃者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃其妇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃甲投戈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃厌(嫌弃;厌恶);弃亲(抛弃亲族);弃礼(抛弃礼制);弃秽(抛弃秽浊);弃失(丢失);弃地(丢弃田地);弃灰(把灰烬弃在路上);弃物(被丢失之物);弃国(丢失封国或都城);弃才(被遗弃的人才)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 废，废除 <span class="encs">[abolish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水官弃矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十九年》</span>。注:&ldquo;废也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃言(已经废弃之言);弃法(废弃法度);弃井(废井)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 忘记 <span class="encs">[forget]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃，忘也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其庸可弃乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十三年》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹忘。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃忽(遗忘，忽略)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 违背;背叛 <span class="encs">[violate;betray]</span>。如:弃命(违命;抛弃命令);弃天(违背天意);弃言(背弃诺言);弃信(违背信义);弃约(违约，不守前约)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 离开 <span class="encs">[leave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子弃寡人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span>。注:&ldquo;去也。&rdquo;</span></p>
