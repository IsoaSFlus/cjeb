                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>沓</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">t&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄚˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>多，重复：纷至～来。杂～。</li>	<li>松懈，松弛：疲～。拖～。</li>	<li>水翻腾沸涌：&ldquo;漏流昔吞翕，～浪竞奔注&rdquo;。</li>	<li>合：天与地～。</li>	<li>贪，黩：～贪。～吏。</li></ol><p>● <strong>沓</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">d&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&aacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄚˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>量词，用于叠起来的纸张或其他薄的东西：一～儿纸。一～子。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> connected, joined; repeated</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> viele ,Ta  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> accumuler,crue</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">沓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>沓</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 堆，叠。堆在一起的或逐个叠放的薄的东西 <span class="encs">[pile]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:一沓银洋</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用于叠起来的纸张(如书写纸等)，可一张张扯 <span class="encs">[pad]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:一沓信纸</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>沓</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 借指叠起来的纸张或薄的东西 <span class="encs">[pile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此诗今在案沓卷中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">俞文豹</span><span class="smcs">《吹剑录》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>沓</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从水，从曰。说话多象水流。曰，说。本义:话多)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[talkative;verbose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沓，语多沓沓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">噂沓背憎&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:沓舌(多舌，多话);沓沓(语多的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 众多;重叠 <span class="encs">[numerous;crowded and repeated]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">香炉瀑布遥相望，回崖沓嶂凌苍苍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《庐山谣寄卢侍御虚舟》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:重沓(重复繁冗);沓飒(群舞的样子)，沓合(重叠);沓障(沓嶂。重重叠叠的山峰);沓拖(相重的样子)</span></p>
