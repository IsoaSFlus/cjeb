						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>箱</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>收藏衣物的方形器具，通常是上面有盖扣住：～子。衣～。书～。药～。</li><li>像箱子的器具：冰～。风～。信～。集装～。</li><li>同“<span class="diczx3">厢</span>”。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>箱</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，相声。本义:车箱)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[compartment]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">箱，大车牝服也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不以服箱。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·大东》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“箱，大车之箱也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃求万斯箱。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·甫田》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">絷騕裹以服箱。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“箱，大车也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尔乃乘露箱，御良马。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">夏侯湛</span><span class="smcs">《猎兔赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 厢房，古代居室前堂两旁的房屋，也称<span class="yhcs">“个”</span>或<span class="yhcs">“序”</span>。后作<span class="yhcs">“厢”</span> <span class="encs">[wing-room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">篬，序也。——<span class="smcs">《埤苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公揖，退于箱。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·公食礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“东夹之前俟事之处。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俟几俟于东箱。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·觐礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“东夹之前相翔待事之处。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西箱踟蹰。——</span><span class="diczx2">王逸</span><span class="smcs">《鲁灵光殿赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“西序也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旁。后作<span class="yhcs">“厢”</span> <span class="encs">[side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两箱悬崖数万仞，窥不见底。——<span class="smcs">《水经注》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 靠近城的地区。后作<span class="yhcs">“厢”</span> <span class="encs">[the vicinity outside of the city gate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而城箱内外，义民不下四万，实不忍委之于贼，惟有竭力固守待援。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏源</span><span class="smcs">《圣武记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 箱”字 <span class="diczx2">汉</span>以前无箧笥之称。盛物的箱笼，有盖有底的方形盛物器 <span class="encs">[chest;case;box]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">箱帘六七十。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">开箱睹黑裘。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《村雨》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十指不动衣盈箱。——</span><span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《当窗织》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:箱钱(嫖妓的花费);箱匣(盛物的器具);箱箧(方形与矩形的盛物竹器)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>箱</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p>一个箱所装的量 <span class="encs">[case;box]</span>。如:一箱宝石;一箱珠宝</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div                     <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>箱</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄒㄧㄤ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>xiāng <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4060.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">车内载人或存放东西的地方。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「车<b>箱</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·竹部》：「<b>箱</b>，大车牝服也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">段玉裁注引郑司农云：「牝服谓车<b>箱</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《隋书·卷六八·何稠传》：「旧制，五辂于辕上起<b>箱</b>，天子与参乘同在<b>箱</b>内。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">收纳东西的器具。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「封<b>箱</b>」、「纸<b>箱</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《乐府诗集·卷七三·杂曲歌辞十三·古辞·焦仲卿妻》：「箱帘六七十，绿碧青丝绳。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《醒世姻缘传·第五回》：「这班人挑了<b>箱</b>，唤到衙内扮戏上寿。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">正屋两旁的房间。</span><span class="gc_yy">《仪礼·公食大夫礼》：「公揖，退于<b>箱</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·傅玄·杂诗》：「蝉鸣高树间，野鸟号东<b>箱</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「厢」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">仓库。</span><span class="gc_yy">《诗经·小雅·甫田》：「乃求千斯仓，及求万斯<b>箱</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·皮日休〈橡媪叹〉：「持之纳于官，私室无仓<b>箱</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量词。计算<b><b>箱</b></b>装物的单位。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「两<b>箱</b>衣服」、「三<b>箱</b>水果」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7BB1.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【正韻】息良切【集韻】【韻會】思將切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音廂。【說文】大車牝服也。【篇海】車內容物處爲箱。【詩·小雅】睆彼牽牛，不以服箱。【傳】箱，大車之箱也。</p><p>又廩也。【詩·小雅】乃求千斯倉，乃求萬斯箱。</p><p>又竹器，箱篋也。</p><p>又與廂同。【儀禮·公食大夫禮】賔升，公揖退于箱。</p><p>又【覲禮】几俟于東箱。【註】東箱，東夾之前相翔待事之處。【前漢·周昌傳】上欲以戚姬子如意爲太子，昌曰：臣期期知其不可。上欣然笑，卽罷。呂后側耳於東箱聽。【註】師古曰：正寢之東、西室皆曰箱，言似箱篋之形。</p><p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7BB1_4701.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(箱)</span><span class="ywyy">大車牝服也。</span><span class="swdz">考工記。大車牝服二柯。又參分柯之二。注云。大車平地載任之車。牝服長八尺。謂較也。鄭司農云。牝服謂車箱。服讀爲負。小雅傳曰。服、牝服也。箱、大車之箱也。按許與大鄭同。箱卽謂大車之輿也。毛二之。大鄭一之。要無異義。後鄭云較者。以左右有㒳較。故名之曰箱。其實一也。假借爲匧笥之稱。又假借爲東西室之稱。禮經箱字俗改爲廂字。非也。</span><span class="ywyy">从竹。相聲。</span><span class="swdz">息良切。十部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
