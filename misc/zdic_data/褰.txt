                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">褰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>褰</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>(形声。从衣，寒省声。本义:套裤) 同本义 <span class="encs">[leggings]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">褰，绔也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">征褰与襦。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">振养矜寡，衣之褰襦。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《潭州新学并序》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>褰</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 撩起<span class="encs">[衣服等]</span> <span class="encs">[lift up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">褰，褰衣。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暑无褰裳。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与其有事，则褰裳濡足，冠挂不顾。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·崔骃传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子惠思我，褰裳涉</span><span class="diczx2">溱</span>。——<span class="smcs">《诗·郑风·褰裳》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">揽辔褰帷。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:褰裳避之(提衣躲避，比喻不愿接近某些人或事，远避之);褰衣(提起衣服，揭起衣裳);褰裳(提起衣裳)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 散开 <span class="encs">[spread out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自非烟褰雨霁，不辨此远山矣。——<span class="smcs">《水经注》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
