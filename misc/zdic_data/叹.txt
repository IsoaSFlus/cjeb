                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>叹</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">嘆</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>因忧闷悲痛而呼出长气：～气。～息。悲～。～惋。长吁短～。</li><li>因高兴、兴奋、激动而发出长声：～赏。～服。赞～。～观止矣（指赞美看到的事物好到极点）。</li><li>吟咏：咏～。一唱三～。</li></ol>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从欠(另一繁体从<span class="yhcs">“口”</span>)，<span class="yhcs">“難”</span>省声。欠，打呵欠，与出气有关。本义:叹息)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sigh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叹，吞叹也。一曰太息也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">禽子</span>再拜而叹。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·备梯》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戏而不叹。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·坊记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">望洋向若而叹。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·秋水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆叹惋。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">默叹曰。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子·千里马篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">婉贞</span>微叹曰。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叹借者之用。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:叹气嗐声(唉声叹气);叹恨(叹息抱恨);叹憾(叹息遗憾);叹笑(叹息讥笑);叹悔(叹息后悔);叹喟(叹息);叹嗟(叹息);叹想(叹息怀念);叹诧(叹息诧异);长叹;叹一口气</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 赞叹，赞美 <span class="encs">[highly praise]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">默叹。——<span class="smcs">《虞初新志·秋声诗自序》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亟叹其技。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span
