<p>● <strong>宁</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">寧</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>平安，安定：～静。～谧。息事～人。</li>
	<li>旧时已嫁的女子或在外子女回家省视父母：～亲。归～。</li>
	<li>中国江苏省南京市的别称。沪～铁路</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>宁</strong></p>
<p>寍、寧 <span class="dicpy">n&iacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本作&ldquo;寍&rdquo;。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mi&aacute;n</span>)，从心，从皿。表示住在屋里有饭吃就安心了。后世假&ldquo;寧&rdquo;为&ldquo;寍&rdquo;，&ldquo;寧&rdquo;行而&ldquo;寍&rdquo;废。今用&ldquo;宁&rdquo;字作&ldquo;寧&rdquo;简化字。&ldquo;宁&rdquo;本读 <span class="dicpy">zh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ，是&ldquo;贮&rdquo;的本字。本义:安宁，平安)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[peaceful]</span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5B81_1425.svg" /><span class="swbf">(宁)</span><span class="ywyy">辨積物也。</span><span class="swdz">辨今俗字作辦。音蒲莧切。古無二字二音也。周禮：以辨民器。辨，具也。分别而具之，故其字从刀。積者，聚也。宁與貯葢古今字。周禮注作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A84.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡪄</span>。史記作積著。釋宫：門屛之閒曰宁。郭云：人君視朝所宁立處。毛詩傳云：宁立，久立也。然則凡云宁立者，正積物之義也引申。俗字作佇，作竚，皆非是。以其可宁立也，故謂之宁。齊風作著。</span><span class="ywyy">象形。</span><span class="swdz">其旁有禦，其下有阯，其上有顚，辦積之形也。直侣切。五部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡宁之屬皆从宁。</span></p>                          </div>
