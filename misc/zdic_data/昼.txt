						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>昼</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">晝</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhòu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhòu.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄡˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 白天：～夜。白～（白天）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> daytime, daylight</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> am Tage vorkommend, Tageszeit  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> jour</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>昼</strong></p><p>晝 <span class="dicpy">zhòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从日，从畫(画)省。从日，表示太阳。从畫，表示一种界限。本义:白天。与<span class="yhcs">“夜”</span>相对)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[day;daylight;daytime]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昼，明也。日之出入，与夜为介。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昼者，阳。——<span class="smcs">《周髀算经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">昼夜之象也。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“乾为昼。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">横柯上蔽，在昼犹昏。——</span><span class="diczx2">吴均</span><span class="smcs">《与朱元思书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:昼日昼夜(日日夜夜);昼夕(日夜);昼眠(白天睡眠;午睡);昼锦(衣锦昼行的省称，称富贵还乡);昼暮(日夜);昼宵(白昼与黑夜);昼景(白昼的日光);昼伏(白天隐蔽不动)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 中午、正午 <span class="encs">[noon]</span>。如:昼饭(昼食。午饭);昼暑(中午燠热);昼时(中午时分)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Zhou town]</span>。在今山东省淄博市临淄西北</p><p><span class="diczx2">孟子</span>去 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>，宿于 <span class="diczx2">昼</span>。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/昼光">昼光</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/昼盲">昼盲</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/昼日">昼日</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/昼夜">昼夜</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
