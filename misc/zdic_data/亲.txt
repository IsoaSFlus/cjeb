                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>亲</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">親</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīn.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>有血统或夫妻关系的：～属。～人。～缘。双～（父母）。～眷。</li>
	<li>婚姻：～事。</li>
	<li>因婚姻联成的关系：～戚。～故。～邻。～朋。</li>
	<li>称呼同一地方的人：乡～。</li>
	<li>本身，自己的：～睹。～聆。～笔。</li>
	<li>感情好，关系密切：～密。相～。～睦。～疏。</li>
	<li>用嘴唇接触表示喜爱：～吻。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>亲</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">親</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&igrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄥˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 〔～家〕夫妻双方的父母彼此的关系或称呼（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">家</span>&rdquo;读轻声）。</p>
<ol>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">亲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>亲</strong></p>
<p>親 <span class="dicpy">qīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从见，亲( <span class="dicpy">shēn</span>)声。&ldquo;见&rdquo;与&ldquo;至&rdquo;义相通。本义:亲爱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dear;beloved;intimate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亲，至也。从見，亲声，字亦作儭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亲，近也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">同人亲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;杂卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亲者属也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人之亲其兄之子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:&ldquo;爱也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爱臣太亲，必危其身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;爱臣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我有亲父兄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我有亲父母。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:亲父(生父);亲爹(亲父);姑表亲;亲如一家;亲心(亲爱之心;父母爱护子女之心);亲厚(亲爱并加以厚待)</span></p>

<p>又六親，父母、兄弟、妻子也。【管子&middot;牧民篇】上服度則六親固。</p>

<p>又姓。【史記&middot;孟嘗君傳】齊王逐周最，而聽親弗。【註】親弗，人姓名。【戰國策】作祝弗。</p>
