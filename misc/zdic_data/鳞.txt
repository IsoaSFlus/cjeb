                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>鳞</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鱗</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lín  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lín.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄣˊ</span></p><ol><li>鱼类、爬行动物和少数哺乳动物身体表面长的角质或骨质小薄片：鱼～。～片。</li><li>鳞状的：～爪（<span class="dicpy">zhǎo </span>）。～波。～茎。～屑。～集（群集）。遍体～伤。～次栉比。</li><li>泛指有鳞甲的动物：～鸿（指“鱼雁”，即书信）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鳞</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>鳞</strong></p><p>鱗 <span class="dicpy">lín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从鱼，粦( <span class="dicpy">lìn</span>)声。本义:鱼甲)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[scale of fish，etc.]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鳞，鱼甲也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鳞罗布烈。——</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《羽猎赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鳞浪层层。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鳞爪飞扬。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朱鳞大鬣。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:鳞爪(喻残存、零碎或无足轻重之物);鳞沦(水纹相次连接的样子);鳞接(密接如鱼鳞);鳞萃(荟萃如鱼鳞)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鳞片状物 <span class="encs">[sth.shaped like the scales of a fish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鳞，甲也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其动物宜鳞物。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大司徒》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 动物学名词。为被覆在动物体表的硬质小形薄片，是一种多样性的构造。如:鳞鼠(野鼠的一种。身有鳞甲，故名)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 植物学名词。如:鳞叶</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 鱼的代称<span class="encs">[fish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鳞集仰流。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《难蜀父老》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“鳞集，相次也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">呷浪之鳞。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">锦鳞游泳。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:鳞族(鱼类);鳞羽(泛指鱼类和鸟类);鳞物(指鱼类);鳞鸿杳绝(杳无音信);鳞集仰流(鱼群迎向上游。比喻四方之民慕德来归)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 有鳞动物的总称 <span class="encs">[scaly animal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鳞虫三百六十而龙为之长。——<span class="smcs">《孔子家语·执辔》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">介鳞夏食冬蛰。——<span class="smcs">《易·主命》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:鳞介(泛指有鳞片介甲的水生动物);鳞虫(有鳞的动物)</p><p><span
