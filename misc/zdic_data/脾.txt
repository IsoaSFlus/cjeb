						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>脾</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>人和动物内脏之一，在胃的左下侧，是重要的贮藏血液的器官和最大的淋巴器官：～脏。</li>    <li>中医学指五脏之一：～胃。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>脾</strong> <span class="dicpy">pí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从月(肉);卑声。本义:脾脏。人和脊椎动物的内脏之一，是贮血和产淋巴与抗体的器官，有调节新陈代谢的作用。中医称<span class="yhcs">“五脏”</span>之一)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[spleen]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">脾，土藏也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">脾者，谓之主。——<span class="smcs">《春秋元命苞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祭先脾。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:脾土(中医用语，指脾);脾析(牛胃);脾家(脾脏所在之处);脾和(脾性相合，意气相投);脾味(兴趣爱好;脾性，脾气)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“髀”</span>。大腿 <span class="encs">[hip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸿蒙方将拊脾雀跃而游。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·在宥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">达于右脾。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·桓公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“膍”</span>。牛胃 <span class="encs">[tripe of ox]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">嘉殽脾臄，或歌或咢。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·行苇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蠃醢脾析。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官·醢人》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脾大">脾大</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脾气">脾气</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脾胃">脾胃</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/脾性">脾性</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
