                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">问</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>问</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">問</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>有不知道或不明白的事请人解答：询～。～答。～题。～鼎（指图谋夺取政权）。～津。质～。过～。</li>
	<li>为表关切而询问：慰～。～候。～长～短。</li>
	<li>审讯，追究：审～。～案。唯你是～。</li>
	<li>管，干预：概不过～。</li>
	<li>向某人或某方面要东西：我～他借几本书。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ask about, inquire after</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> fragen, befragen  (V)​,jd. verhören, jd vernehmen  (V)​,sich nach etwas erkundigen  (V)​,jd. zur Rechenschaft ziehen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> demander,se renseigner,questionner,interroger</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">问</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>问</strong></p>
<p>問 <span class="dicpy">w&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，门声。本义:问，询问)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ask about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问，讯也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皇帝请问下民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;吕刑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">时聘曰问。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宗伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小聘曰问。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问人于他邦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善问者如攻坚木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;学记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问女何所思，问女何所忆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问其故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">跪问其故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">景公</span>问政 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;孔子世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">豹</span>往到 <span class="diczx2">邺</span>，会 <span class="diczx2">长老</span>，问之民所疾苦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;滑稽列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既克，公问其故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笑问客从何处来。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贺知章</span><span class="smcs">《回乡偶书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">康肃</span>问曰。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公问不至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:到食堂问一下开饭的时间;问途(打听路径);问信(打听消息);问官答花(答非所问);不耻下问;查问;发问;讯问;追问;自问;不懂就问;问字(称从人受学或请教为问字);问岁(询问年岁的丰歉);问禁(初入他国，问其政教上的禁令);问遂(问路。遂，水中可涉的径路);问对(问，发问);问知(向有学问的人请教);问俗(查访风俗);问卦(以卜得的卦象来探求吉凶);问经(学习或请教有关经书的问题);问龟(占卜);问课(请教课业)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 访问 <span class="encs">[visit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">问士以璧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓访其国事。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天王使</span><span class="diczx2">南季</span>来聘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋&middot;隐公九年》</span>。<span class="smcs">《谷梁传》</span>解释说:&ldquo;聘，问也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 责问 <span class="encs">[ask reprovingly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">昭王</span>南征而不复，寡人是问。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公四年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:质问;诘问</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 问候;慰问 <span class="encs">[ask after]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">伯牛</span>有病， <span class="diczx2">子</span>问之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐王</span>使使者问 <span class="diczx2">赵威后</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">周游城中，家家致问。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吕蒙传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:问牛(形容官吏关怀民间疾苦);问疾(探视病情);问慰(慰问);问省(问侯尊长的起居);问宁(问安);问劳(问候慰劳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 过问 <span class="encs">[take an interest in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不问之秋。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《杜少府之任蜀州》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今不问农蚕，而但以饱暖相矜耀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">章学诚</span><span class="smcs">《文史通义》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 审案，审讯 <span class="encs">[罪犯]</span> <span class="encs">[try]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">每得降卒，必亲引问委曲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:审问;拷问;问头(对罪犯的起诉文书);问拟(审问罪犯);问结(审问结案);问断(经过审问判了罪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 引申为&ldquo;判决&rdquo; <span class="encs">[sentence]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:问绞(判绞刑);问发(判发配)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 追究 <span class="encs">[look into]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">引问委曲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">按问其家。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是不必问。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不问罪之有无。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:问革(问罪罢官);问遣(问罪发配)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 寻访 <span class="encs">[look for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后遂无问津者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源诗并记》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 聘问;旧时订婚，男方向女方下聘礼。源于古&ldquo;纳采&rdquo;、&ldquo;问名&rdquo;的礼节 <span class="encs">[bride-price]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:问亲(求亲);问聘(行聘;订亲);问肯(求亲)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/554F.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20CC5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠳅</span>【唐韻】亡運切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】文運切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>聞去聲。【說文】訊也。【書&middot;仲虺之誥】好問則裕。【詩&middot;邶風】問我諸姑，遂及伯姊。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋言】聘問也。【儀禮&middot;聘禮】小聘曰問。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大宗伯】時聘曰問。</p>
<p>又【秋官&middot;大行人】閒問以諭諸侯之志。【又】凡諸侯之邦交，歲相問也。</p>
<p>又【正字通】古謂遺曰問。【詩&middot;鄭風】雜佩以問之。【傳】問，遺也。【禮&middot;曲禮】凡以苞苴簞笥問人者。【左傳&middot;哀二十六年】衛侯使以弓問子貢。</p>
<p>又訊罪曰問。【詩&middot;魯頌】淑問如臯陶。【註】淑，善。問，訊囚也。</p>
<p>又命也。【左傳&middot;莊八年】期戍，公問不至。【註】問，命也。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】今襄州有之。【正字通】明問智，成化貢士。</p>
<p>又【正韻】與聞同，聲問也。【詩&middot;大雅】宣昭義問。【又】亦不隕厥問。</p>
