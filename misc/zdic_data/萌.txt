						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>萌</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">méng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/méng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>植物的芽：～蘖。</li><li>发芽，开始发生：～生。～发。～芽。～动。故态复～。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">氓</span>1”。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>萌</strong> <span class="dicpy">méng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/méng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，明声。本义:草木的芽)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bud]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">萌，草芽也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">句者毕出，萌者尽达。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“句，屈生者;芒而直曰萌。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃舍萌于四方。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官·占梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是其日夜之所息，雨露之所润，非无萌蘖之生焉。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">目注勾萌，以望其拆。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·葛巾》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:萌甲(嫩芽，花木的芽苞)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻事情刚刚显露的发展趋势或情况;开端 <span class="encs">[beginning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">圣人见微而知萌。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说林上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:萌兆(预兆);萌象(事物的萌芽和征象);萌孽(祸端;邪恶)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“民”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“氓”</span>。人民 <span class="encs">[the people]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奸巧边萌。——<span class="smcs">《史记·三王世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轨谓高田之萌曰。——<span class="smcs">《管子·山国轨》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:萌人(乡民);萌俗(民俗);萌庶(萌隶。百姓);萌黎(人民，平民);萌隶(平民，人民)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“甿”</span>。农民 <span class="encs">[peasant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四鄙之萌人闻之。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·尚贤上》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>萌</strong> <span class="dicpy">méng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/méng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 草木发芽 <span class="encs">[sprout]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">草木萌动。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:萌茁(草木发芽);萌动(开始发芽);萌发(发芽)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 开始;产生 <span class="encs">[begin;start]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知者见于未萌。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·更法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">亲法，则奸无所萌。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·心度》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今日反情已萌，可速诛之。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:萌阳(新生的阳气。我国古代谓宇宙间有阴阳二气，阴主衰落，而阳主新生);萌达(发生滋长);萌通(萌发生长);萌起(开始;发生)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div
