						<p>● <strong>健</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>强壮，身体好：～康。～全。康～。稳～。～美。～身。～旺。～在。～壮。～朗。保～。</li>
	<li>善于，精力旺盛：～步如飞。～谈。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">健</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>健</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，建声。本义:强有力)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[strong]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">健，伉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">震其究为健。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四健骄也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天行健，君子以自强不息。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;乾》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下健者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与汝幸双健。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:健举(刚劲有力);健舞(刚健风格的舞蹈)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 健康;健壮 <span class="encs">[healthy;robust]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纵有健妇把锄犁，禾生垄亩无东西。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">健卒十人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子弟素健。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:健节(健壮，有力);健夫(强壮的男子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 敏捷;高明;有才能 <span class="encs">[agile;nimble;bright;clever]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚客</span>来使者多健，与寡人争辞，寡人数穷焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;秦策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:健妇(能干的妇女)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 勇猛 <span class="encs">[of valour]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:健翼(猛禽);健侠(刚勇侠义);健鹘(勇猛矫健的鹘)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>健</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 使精力充沛 <span class="encs">[invigorate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:健身;健胃;健脾</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 善于;擅长 <span class="encs">[be good at]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有健啖客。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:健讼(好打官司，以诉讼为能事);健笔(文笔练达明畅。指精于写作);健羡(贪婪无厌)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>健</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
