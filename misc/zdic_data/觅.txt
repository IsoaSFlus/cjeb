                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>觅</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3"><img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">覓</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>找，寻求：～食。～取。寻～。搜～。</li>
	<li>数量单位，中国唐代南诏以十六枚贝为一觅。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> seek; search</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> positionieren, suchen</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">觅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>觅</strong></p>
<p>覓、覔 <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从爪，从见。表示用手和眼去寻找。本义:寻找，到处寻找)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[seek;look for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是犹欲登山者，涉舟航而觅路。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;武帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自行搜觅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">英雄无觅</span><span class="diczx2">孙仲谋</span>处。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐&middot;京口北固亭怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:觅汉(雇工);觅举(士人到处请托人，求人荐举);觅句(指诗人构思、寻觅诗句);觅食(寻找食物);觅死(求死，寻死);觅取(求取);觅活(求活命);觅婚(求婚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 偷窃 <span class="encs">[steal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我觅得禁魂</span><span class="diczx2">张员外</span>的一包儿细软，我将归客店里去，安在头边，枕着头。你觅着我的时，你便去上 <span class="diczx2">东京</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《喻世明言》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 雇 <span class="encs">[hire;employ]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:觅车子(雇车子);觅轿(雇轿子)</span></p>
