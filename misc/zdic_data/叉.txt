                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>叉</strong></p><p>扠 <span class="dicpy">chā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。小篆为<span class="yhcs">“又”</span>(即手)上加一点，指出叉手的动向。本义:交错，交叉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 手指相交错。也泛指一般的交错、交叉 <span class="encs">[cross]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叉，手指相错也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓手指与物相错也。凡布指错物间而取之曰叉，因之凡岐头皆曰叉。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡八叉手而八韵成。——<span class="smcs">《全唐诗话》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:叉手</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 刺;扎取 <span class="encs">[stick;work with a fork;fork]</span>。如:叉了一槽干草;叉鱼</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>叉</strong> <span class="dicpy">chā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代的一种兵器，头有分杈 <span class="encs">[cha，a weapon in anciant China，which the end is forked]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">红漆了叉。——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">睢景臣</span><span class="smcs">《<span class="encs">[般涉调]</span>哨遍·高祖还乡》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 叉子 <span class="encs">[fork]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挺叉来往。——<span class="smcs">《文选 ·潘岳·西征赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“叉，取鱼叉也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:鱼叉;粪叉;叉竿(带叉头的竿)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 两短直线相交所成的图形 <span
