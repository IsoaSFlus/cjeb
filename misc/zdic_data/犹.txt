                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>犹</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">猶</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>相似，如同：～如。过～不及。</li>
	<li>尚且：～且。～自。困兽～斗（喻濒于失败的人，虽走投无路还要顽强抵抗）。</li>
	<li>〔～豫〕迟疑不决。</li>
	<li>〔～疑〕迟疑。</li>
	<li>仍然，还（<span class="dicpy">h&aacute;i </span>）：～然。记忆～新。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">犹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>犹</strong></p>
<p>猶 <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从犬，酋(尤)声。本义:一种猿类动物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 兽名。猴属，也叫&ldquo;犹猢&rdquo;，形如麂 <span class="encs">[a kind of monkey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猶，愑属。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作猷。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹如麂，善登木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹，五尺大犬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释文》</span>引<span class="smcs">《尸子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹，兽名也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训&middot;书证》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹与未决。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;吕后纪》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;犹，猿类也。夘鼻长尾 ，性多疑。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山多犹猢，似猴而短足好游崖树，一腾百步，或三百丈，顺往倒返，乘空若飞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水经注》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 犬子 <span class="encs">[son of dog]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹，<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;陇西谓犬子为犹。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>犹</strong></p>
<p>猶 <span class="dicpy">y&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 如同。好比 <span class="encs">[like]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此犹文轩之与敝舆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">梁肉之与糠糟。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此犹锦绣之与短褐。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今之乐犹古之乐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹鱼之有水也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:犹子(如同儿子);犹父(如同父亲);犹龙(道之高深奇妙，如龙之变化不可测);犹言(好比说;等于说);过犹不及</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 踌躇疑惧<span class="encs">[shilly-shally]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心犹嶑而狐疑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豫兮若冬涉川，犹兮若畏四邻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:犹预(犹与。犹夷。犹豫);犹犹(迟疑不决)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">猶</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“犹”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/猶">猶</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十">卷十</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十&amp;swbs=犬">犬</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7336.svg">                          <p>玃屬。从犬酋聲。一曰隴西謂犬子爲猷。以周切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7336_3860.svg" /><span class="swbf">(猶)</span><span class="ywyy">玃屬。</span><span class="swdz">釋獸曰：猶如麂，善登木。許所說謂此也。曲禮曰：使民決嫌疑，定猶豫。正義云：《說文》：猶，玃屬。豫，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C3C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧰼</span>屬。此二獸皆進退多疑，人多疑惑者似之，故謂之猶豫。按古有以聲不以義者，如猶豫雙聲，亦作猶與，亦作冘豫，皆遲疑之皃。老子：豫兮如冬涉川，猶兮若畏四鄰。離騷：心猶豫而狐疑。以猶豫二字皃其狐疑耳。李善注洛神賦乃以猶獸多豫、狐獸多疑對說，王逸注離騷絕不如此，禮記正義則又以猶與豫二獸對說，皆郢書燕說也。如九歌君不行兮夷猶，王逸卽以猶豫解之。要亦是雙聲字。春秋經猶三望、猶朝於廟，猶繹今。謂可已而不已者曰猶，卽猶豫、夷猶之意也。釋詁曰：猷，謀也。釋言曰：猷，圖也。召南傳曰：猶，若也。《說文》：圖者，畫也，計難也。謀者，慮難也。圖謀必酷肖其事而後有濟。故圖也、謀也、若也為一義。周禮：以猶鬼神示之居。猶者，圖畫也。是則皆從遲疑鄭重之意引伸之。魏風毛傳：猷，可也。可之義與庶幾相近，庶幾與今語猶者相近也。釋詁又曰：猷，道也。以與由音同。秩秩大猷，漢書作大繇可證。釋詁又云：猷，已也。謂已然之䛐，亦卽猶三望之類也。</span><span class="ywyy">从犬酋聲。</span><span class="swdz">以周切。三部。今字分猷謀字犬在右，語助字犬在左。經典絕無此例。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰隴西謂犬子爲猶。</span><span class="swdz">此別一義。益證从犬之意。</span></p>                          </div>
