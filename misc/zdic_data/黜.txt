						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>黜</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 降职或罢免：罢～。废～。贬～。～退。～斥。～免。～逐。～陟。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dismiss; demote, downgrade</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> abaisser,destituer</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>黜</strong> <span class="dicpy">chù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从黑，出声。本义:黑暗)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 降职或罢免 <span class="encs">[degrade or dismiss]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黜，贬下也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黜，贬也，下也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黜陟幽明。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“退其幽者。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何以黜朱于朝。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咸黜不端。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">台臣惭，追受其牒，为复太守官而黜臧使者。——</span><span class="diczx2">高适</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">理乱不知，黜涉不闻。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《送李愿归盘谷序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:罢黜百家;黜落(罢免，除名);黜升(官吏的罢免与升迁);黜放(革职放逐)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 废除;取消 <span class="encs">[abolish;abandon;annul]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汤</span>既黜 <span class="diczx2">夏</span>命。——<span class="smcs">《书·汤诰序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爱</span><span class="diczx2">季佗</span>而黜 <span class="diczx2">仆</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公十八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王黜</span><span class="diczx2">翟</span>后。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君将黜嗜欲。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·徐无鬼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:黜华(摈除文饰，不事虚华)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/黜退">黜退</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/黜免">黜免</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/黜逐">黜逐</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
