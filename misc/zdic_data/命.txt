                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>命</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mìng.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>动植物的生活能力：生～。救～。逃～。拼～。～脉。性～。相依为～。</li><li>迷信认为生来就注定的贫富、寿数等：天～。～相（<span class="dicpy">xiàng </span>）。～运（a.迷信指生死、贫富和一切遭遇；b.喻发展变化的趋向，如“人民一定能掌握自己的～～”）。</li><li>上级对下级的指示：奉～。遵～。～令。使～。</li><li>给予（名称等）：～名。～题。～意。</li><li>指派，使用：～官。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">命</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>命</strong> <span class="dicpy">mìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从口从令。表示用口发布命令。本义:指派;发号)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命，使也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>按:<span class="yhcs">“在事为令，在言为命，散文则通，对文则别。令当训使也，命当训发号也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出君下臣名曰命。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">语下命者，制令也。——<span class="smcs">《贾子·礼容》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡尊者之言曰命。——<span class="smcs">《文选·闲居赋序》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维君子命。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·卷阿》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">某固辞不得命。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·燕礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命田舍东郊。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·孟春纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“命，令也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命</span><span class="diczx2">夸娥氏</span>二子负二山。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令将军</span><span class="diczx2">诚</span>能命猛将统兵数万。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·赤壁之战》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命妪煮芋。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂命酒，使快弹数曲。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:命酌(使充酌酒者);命席(下令安排座位);命酒(命人置酒);命乐(命人奏乐);命官(任命官吏);命材(任用有才干的人);命事(安排职事);命使(任命使者);命将(任命将领);命相(命令三公辅政、行事);命讨(下令讨伐);命教(命令)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 取名;命名 <span class="encs">[nominat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今之命师者。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命曰琵琶行。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:命名(给予名称，定名);命姓(赐姓;建姓);命爵(赐给爵位、官职)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 动，用 <span class="encs">[use]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欣然命笔。——</span><span class="diczx2">毛泽东</span><span
