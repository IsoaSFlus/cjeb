                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">七</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>七</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qī.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>数名，六加一（在钞票和单据上常用大写&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">柒</span>&rdquo;代）。</li>	<li>文体名，或称&ldquo;七体&rdquo;，为赋体的另一种形式。</li>	<li>旧时人死后每隔七天一祭，共七次，称&ldquo;做七&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> seven</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> sieben (7)​ (Num)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sept,7</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">七</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>七</strong> <span class="dicpy">qī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">数</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。从一ㄣ。画以纪数。本义:六加一的和)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[seven]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七，阳之正也。从一，微阴从中斜出也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其数七。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七者，天地四时人之始也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;律历志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七主星。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;易本命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">七日得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;既济》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;震为七。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡攻木之工七。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有子七人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;凯风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:七宝(佛经上指金、银、琉璃、砗磲、玛璃、珍珠、玫瑰);七出(旧指休妻的七种理由:无子，淫泆报会，不事舅姑，口舌，盗窃，妒忌，恶疾);七年;七秩(七十大寿);七雄(指战国时秦、韩、魏、楚、燕、齐、赵七国)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 七次 <span class="encs">[seven times]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:诸葛亮七擒孟获</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>七</strong> <span class="dicpy">qī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 文体名。亦称七体，骚体的一种 <span class="encs">[a kind of literary form]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:七体(沿袭汉&middot;枚乘<span class="smcs">《七发》</span>而成的一种文体;汉&middot;傅毅有<span class="smcs">《七激》</span>，刘广有<span class="smcs">《七兴》</span>，曹植有<span class="smcs">《七启》</span>，张协有<span class="smcs">《七命》</span>)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 旧时人死后每七天为一祭，直到四十九天为止 <span class="encs">[seventh]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:头七，二七，&hellip;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓(明代有七希贤)</p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>七</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄑㄧ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>qī <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3522.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">介於六與八之間的自然數。大寫作「柒」，阿拉伯數字作「7」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">文體名。參見「<a href='/hans/七體' target=_blank>七體</a> 」條。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">民俗稱喪事每七日設奠一次為「作七」，由頭七到尾七共需進行七次，稱為「<b>七</b>七」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《北史&middot;卷八一&middot;儒林傳上&middot;孫惠蔚傳》：「從綽死後，每至<b>七</b>日至百日，靈暉恆為綽請僧設齋行道。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《喻世明言&middot;卷一○&middot;滕大尹鬼斷家私》：「善繼只是點名應客，全無哀痛之意，<b>七</b>中便擇日安葬。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item">
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">（「七」字通常讀為陰平，惟口語中連用在去聲字及輕聲字之前時讀為陽平，如：「七月」、「七個」。）​</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/4E03.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】親吉切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】戚悉切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2160B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡘋</span>音桼。少陽數也。【說文】陽之正也，从一，微隂从中衺出也。【書&middot;舜典】在璿璣玉衡，以齊七政。【註】七政，日月五星也。【詩&middot;唐風】豈曰無衣七兮。【註】侯伯七命，車服皆以七爲節。</p><p>又詞家以七名篇，雖八首，問對凡七。七者，問對之別名，始枚乗七發，後傅毅七激，崔駰七依，曹植七啓，張協七命，繼之凡十餘家。</p><p>又三七，藥名。【本草綱目】言葉左三右四，故名。一說本名山桼。</p><p>又姓。明七希賢。</p><p>又人名。【續仙傳】殷七七，名文祥。【蘇軾詩】安得道人殷七七，不論時節遣花開。　【正字通】或通作㭍桼漆。柒。</p>
