                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>香</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>气味好闻，与“<span class="diczx3">臭</span>”相对：～味。～醇。芳～。清～。</li><li>舒服：睡得～。</li><li>味道好：这鱼做得真～。</li><li>受欢迎：这种货物在农村～得很。</li><li>称一些天然或人造的有香味的东西：麝～。灵猫～。龙涎～。檀～。沉～。</li><li>旧时用以形容女子事物或作女子的代称：～闺。～艳。</li><li>祭祖、敬神所烧的用木屑搀上香料做成的细条：～火。烧～拜佛。～炉。～烛。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fragrant, sweet smelling, incense</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Weihrauch  (S, Philos)​,appetitlich, gut schmecken  (Adj, Ess)​,duftend, riechend  (Adj)​,Xiang  (Eig, Fam)​,Radikal Nr. 186 = Duft, duftend, fest, tief, Weihrauch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> épice,aromate,encens,parfumé,odorant,aromatique,délicieux,appétissant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">香</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>香</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意字。据小篆，从黍，从甘。<span class="yhcs">“黍”</span>表谷物;<span class="yhcs">“甘”</span>表香甜美好。本义:五谷的香)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sweet-smelling;fragrant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">香，芳也。春秋传曰:<span class="yhcs">“香稷馨香。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“按，谷与酒臭曰香。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黍稷靡馨。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卬盛于豆，于豆于盛，其香始升。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·生民》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 有飶其香，邦家之光。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·载芟》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">稻花香里说丰年。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《西江月》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">淅玉炊香粳。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="diczx2">孟郊</span><span class="smcs">《城南联句》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指好闻的气味</p><p><span class="diczx1">二十里中香不断，</span><span class="diczx2">青羊宫</span>到浣花溪。—— <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《梅花绝句》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:香鼠(一种皮毛名贵的貂);香醪(芳香的酒);香楠(芳香的楠木)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 气味美好 <span class="encs">[savory;appetizing;delicious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">树橘柚者，食 之则甘，嗅之则香。——<span class="smcs">《雅南子·外储说左下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">香远益清。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">香而甘也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《春酒堂遗书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">香且甘者。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span>
