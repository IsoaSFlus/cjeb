						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>夙</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>早：～夜。～兴（<span class="dicpy">xīng </span>）夜寐。</li><li>素有的，旧有的：～日。～怨。～愿。～志。～敌。～诺。～嫌。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">夙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>夙</strong> <span class="dicpy">sù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。夕是夜间，丮( <span class="dicpy">jí</span>)表示以手持物。天不亮就起来做事情，表示早。所以除了早晨的意义外，还有早起劳作，以示恭敬的意思。本义:早晨)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[morning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夙，早敬也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夙夜在公。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·小星》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岂不夙夜，谓行多露。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·行露》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夙夜惟寅。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夙兴。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">策蹇驴，囊图书，夙行失道，望尘惊悸。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:夙兴(早起);夙宵(朝夕，日夜)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旧;平素 <span class="encs">[one's early years;in normal times;usually]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">虞</span>虽为上公，天性节约，…远近豪俊，夙僭奢者，莫不改，而归心焉。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·刘虞传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">良觌违夙愿，含凄向寥廓。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《昔游》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:夙话(老话;旧话);夙好(老交情);夙契(老朋友);夙憾(旧恨;旧怨);夙觏(旧友。觏:老朋友);夙分(旧缘，往世的缘分);夙素(平日的心愿);夙心(平素的心愿);夙志(平素的志愿);夙尚(平素的志愿或心愿)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 前，早年 <span class="encs">[former]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乩仙批了，死者</span><span class="diczx2">冯渊</span>与 <span class="diczx2">薛蟠</span>原系夙孽，今狭路相遇，原因了结。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仗策相从似夙因，草堂促膝意相亲。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄景仁</span><span class="smcs">《夜宿西山下偕冯九健一》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:夙世(前生，前世);夙根(佛家称前世的修行根底);夙世之因(前世的缘分)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>夙</strong> <span class="dicpy">sù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 长期存在的，存在已久的，经久的 <span
