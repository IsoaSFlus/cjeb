						<p>● <strong>俦</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">儔</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同辈，伴侣：～俪（同辈，同一类的人物）。～侣。～列。～类。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> companion, mate, colleague</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Intimus, Gefährte, Gefährtin</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> associés,gens de même sorte,compagnon,camarade</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>俦</strong></p>
<p>儔 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，寿声。本义:伴侣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[companion]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俦，侣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无介俦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《太玄》</span>卷六剧</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 注:&ldquo;俦，匹也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命俦啸侣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:俦侣(朋友，伴侣);俦匹(伴侣);俦伴(伴侣，同伴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 同类，辈 <span class="encs">[gal&egrave;re]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">俦善博惠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《鬼谷子&middot;中经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我闻今相国昆山顾秉谦者，严相国俦也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">侯方域</span><span class="smcs">《马伶传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:俦类(同辈);俦俪(同一品类。同辈);俦伍(同类之人;同等之人)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>俦</strong></p>
<p>儔 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>相比 <span class="encs">[compare]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:俦比(可与相比者);俦拟(比拟，匹拟)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>俦</strong></p>
<p>儔 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p>表示疑问，相当于&ldquo;谁&rdquo; <span class="encs">[who]</span></p>

<p>又通作疇。【前漢&middot;韓信傳】其疇十三人。</p>
<p>又誰也。【揚子&middot;法言】儔克爾。與疇同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】大到切。與翿同。【說文】翳也。【玉篇】隱蔽也。</p>
<p>又叶<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>如切，音除。【韓愈&middot;盧夫人墓誌銘】伊昔淑哲，或圖或書。嗟咨夫人，孰與爲儔。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">儔</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“俦”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/儔">儔</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八">卷八</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八&amp;swbs=人">人</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5114.svg">                          <p>翳也。从人壽聲。直由切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5114_339.svg" /><span class="swbf">(儔)</span><span class="ywyy">翳也。</span><span class="swdz">翳者，華葢也。引伸爲凡覆蔽之偁。按玉篇儔直流切，侣也。又大到切。翳，隱蔽也。廣韵尤韵：儔，侣也。直由切。号韵：儔，隱也，徒到切。是儔有隱蔽之訓，而其音與疇侣絕不同，與翿、纛音同，由其義相近也。翳義廢而侣義獨行矣。然自唐以前用儔侣皆作疇，絕無作儔者，葢由古者一井爲疇，並畔爲疇，是以釋詁曰：疇，誰也。注易注國策漢書者曰：疇，類也。注國語者曰：疇，匹也。下逮六朝辭賦皆不作儔。玄應之書曰：王逸云二人爲匹，四人爲疇。疇亦類也。今或作儔矣。然則用儔者起唐初，以至於今。</span><span class="ywyy">从人壽聲。</span><span class="swdz">直由切。三部。按大徐此音失之不攷。</span></p>                          </div>
