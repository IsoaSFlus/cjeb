<p>◎ <strong>巍</strong> <span class="dicpy">wēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wēi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，魏声。本义:高大) 同本义 <span class="encs">[towering]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巍，高也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巍巍乎</span><span class="diczx2">尧</span><span class="diczx2">舜</span>之有天下也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;泰伯》</span>。集解:&ldquo;巍巍者，高大也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">崛巍巍而特秀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;嵇康&middot;琴赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:巍科(高级的科举高试);巍廓(高大开阔);巍耸(高耸);巍蟠(高大而盘曲);巍崇(高大);巍张(高大宽阔);巍峻(高峻);巍冠(高冠);巍奕(高大);巍怪(高大奇特)</span></p>
