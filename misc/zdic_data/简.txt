                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>简</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">簡</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>古代用来写字的竹板：竹～。～牍。～策。～册。</li>
	<li>书信：～帖。～札。信～。书～。</li>
	<li>不复杂：～单。～易。～略。～要。～便。～洁。～练。册繁就～。言～意赅。</li>
	<li>选择：～拔。～选。～任。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> simple, terse, succinct, a letter</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> einfach, kurz, unkompliziert (Adj)​,vereinfachen (V)​,Brief (S)​,auswählen, aussuchen (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> simple,simplifié,lettre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">简</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>简</strong></p>
<p>簡 <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，间声。本义:竹简。古代书写了文字的狭长竹片)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。战国至魏晋时代的书写材料，是削制成的狭长竹片或木片，竹片称&ldquo;简&rdquo;，木片称&ldquo;札&rdquo;或&ldquo;牍&rdquo;，统称为&ldquo;简&rdquo;。若干简编缀在一起的叫&ldquo;策&rdquo;(册) <span class="encs">[bamboo slips]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畏此简书。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;出车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">执简记。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span>。注:&ldquo;策书也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小简而长。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工记&middot;弓人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">昭王</span>读法十余简而睡卧矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">南史氏</span>闻太史尽死，执简以往。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十五年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:简素(竹简与缣素。古代用来记载文字的竹帛);简书(古代的公文书于竹简，称为简书);简札(古代用以书写的竹简木札);简帛(简牍与帛。古代书写用品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 书籍;信札 <span class="encs">[book;letter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">呻吟槁简，诵死人之语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;大论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辱致来简，受赐无量。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《答贡士元公瑾论仕进书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:简末(文牍书简末幅);简策(指书籍);简囊(装书籍的袋子);简书(泛指文书，信札)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古兵器。鞭类 <span class="encs">[whips]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其人年少，虎体人形，腕悬铁简，身挂绿袍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《薛仁贵征辽事略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:简格(盛放弩矢的用具及放刀剑的架子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 两根长约六十五厘米的竹片组成的打击乐器，名云扬板，以左手夹击发声来显示节拍 <span class="encs">[bamboo&#39;s clips]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:简子(简板。说唱曲艺时用以伴奏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 水名 <span class="encs">[Jian River]</span>。古黄河下游的支流之一，故道约在今山东省阳信县、栗陵县境</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>简</strong></p>
<p>簡 <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 简省;简易;简单 <span class="encs">[simple;simplified;brief]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">繁文简节之音作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span>。注:&ldquo;少易也。&rdquo;</span></p>
