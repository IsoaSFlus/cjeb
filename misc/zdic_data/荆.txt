                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荆</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>荆</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>落叶灌木，叶有长柄，掌状分裂，开蓝紫色小花，枝条可编筐篮等（亦称“<span class="diczx3">楚</span>”）：紫～。～条。～棘。披～斩棘。</li><li>古代用荆条做的刑仗：负～请罪。</li><li>中国古代“九州”之一，春秋时楚国别称：～州。～璞（喻美质、未经任用的卓越人才）。</li><li>旧时对人谦称自己的妻子：拙～。山～。～妻。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> thorns; brambles; my wife; cane</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Brombeersträucher  (S)​,Dornen  (S)​,Jing  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> gattilier ou baies</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>荆</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，刑声。本义:一种灌木)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 灌木名。又名楚 <span class="encs">[brambles]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荆，楚木也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以牡荆书幡。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·郊祀志》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“作幡柄也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">白刑堪为履。——<span class="smcs">《广州记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 马鞭草科牡荆属落叶灌木。种类很多，有牡荆，黄荆，紫荆等。枝条柔韧，可编筐篮;果实可入药。如:荆榛(有棘刺的灌木丛);荆钗裙布(荆枝作钗，粗布为裙。指妇女朴素或贫寒的服饰);荆天棘地(遍地荆棘。比喻艰险困厄的境地);荆花(即紫荆花，比喻兄弟昆仲同枝并茂)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 荆条。古代用为刑杖 <span class="encs">[twigs of the chaste tree]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肉袒负荆。——<span class="smcs">《史记·廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:荆子(荆条作的挞具);荆朴(鞭笞，杖击);荆门(柴门);荆革(荆条皮鞭。鞭笞用的刑具);荆室(用荆条搭建的屋舍;借指穷苦人家);荆冠(用荆条编的帽子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 荆山的简称 <span class="encs">[Jing mountain]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:荆玉(荆山之玉;喻美质贤才);荆山玉(荆山产的美玉。相传楚卞和得璞玉于荆山);荆襄(泛指古荆州及襄阳郡地区);荆岑(荆山。泛指古楚国境内的高山)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Jing state]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">荆</span>之地。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">荆</span>有云梦。</p><p><span class="diczx2">荆</span>有长松。</p><p><span class="diczx2">荆</span>人欲袭 <span class="diczx2">宋</span>。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:荆凡(西周时代的荆国(楚国)和凡国);荆尸(春秋时楚军阵法名);荆王(楚王);荆巫(荆楚的巫祝);荆吴(春秋时的楚国与吴国。后泛指长江中下游地区)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古九州之一荆州的简称 <span class="encs">[Jing prefecture]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正南曰</span><span class="diczx2">荆州</span>。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·职方氏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">荆州</span>之民附 <span class="diczx2">操</span>者。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:荆州瘿(古代流行于荆州一带的颈生囊状赘生物的疾病);荆南(荆州一带。亦泛指南方);荆扬(荆州和扬州。亦泛指长江中下游地区)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎
