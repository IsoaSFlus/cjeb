						<p>● <strong>厄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄜˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>困苦、灾难：～运。～境。</li>
	<li>阻塞：阻～。</li>

	<li>木节。</li>
	<li>没有肉的骨头。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> adversity, difficulty, distress</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schwierigkeiten, Probleme</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">厄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>厄</strong></p>
<p>戹 <span class="dicpy">&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p> (厂卪会意。&ldquo;厂&rdquo;象山崖，&ldquo;卪&rdquo;象人在崖洞下卷曲身子不得伸展。本义:困厄，遭遇困境) 同本义 <span class="encs">[poverty-stricken]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">戹，隘也。从户，乙声，字亦作厄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厄，困也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厄难，勤苦之事也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;谷风》</span>笺</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能济其厄涣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;解注》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">仲尼</span>厄而作<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厄气(倒霉气);厄难(困苦;遭遇不幸);厄困(艰难窘迫);厄苦(困苦);厄急(艰难急迫)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>厄</strong> <span class="dicpy">&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> 为难;迫害 <span class="encs">[make things difficult for;injure;persecute]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两贤岂相厄哉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;季布栾布列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厄日(遭灾受难之日)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>厄</strong> <span class="dicpy">&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 灾难 <span class="encs">[disaster]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">能济其厄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">西晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厄劫(危难，灾难);厄会(众灾会合);厄害(危难祸害)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 险要的地方 <span class="encs">[strategic point]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:险厄</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>厄</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄜˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>&egrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5731.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">樹木的節。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;?部》：「<b>厄</b>，科<b>厄</b>，木節也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">災難、困難。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;左思&middot;魏都賦》：「英辯榮枯，能濟其<b>厄</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈三槐堂銘〉：「盜蹠之壽，孔顏之<b>厄</b>，此皆天之未定者也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">車轅前端套在牛、馬等頸上的橫木。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;大雅&middot;韓奕》：「鞹鞃淺幭，䩦革金<b>厄</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「軛」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">困窘的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「厄運」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈贈徐州族姪〉詩：「歲時易遷次，身命多<b>厄</b>窮。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5384.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>五果切，音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/216D6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡛖</span>。【說文】科厄，木節也。从卩从厂。會意。【廣韻】木節，亦作〈厂下巾〉。</p>
