                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">农</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>农</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">農</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">n&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄨㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>种庄稼，属于种庄稼的：务～。～业。～田。～产。～垦。～家。～忙。～民。～妇。～奴。</li>	<li>种庄稼的人：贫～。菜～。谷贱伤～。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> agriculture, farming; farmer</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Landwirtschaft, Bauer  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">农</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>农</strong></p>
<p>農、辳 <span class="dicpy">n&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，从林，从辰。古代森林遍野，如要进行农耕，必先伐木开荒，故从&ldquo;林&rdquo;;古代以蜃蛤的壳为农具进行耕耨，故从&ldquo;辰&rdquo;。小篆认为从晨，囟( <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;n</span>)声。从&ldquo;晨&rdquo;，取日出而作、日入而息之意。本义:耕，耕种) 同本义 <span class="encs">[till]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">农，耕也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，耕必作于晨，故从晨。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辟土植谷曰农。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">农，天下之大本也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;文帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贫生于不足，不足生于不农。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殴民而归之农。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:农家子(村野耕作之人);农舆(耕作用的车);农战(从事农耕，以为攻战之本);农月(立夏以后农事的忙月)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;努&rdquo;。努力，勉力 <span class="encs">[effort;exert]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小人农力以事其尚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>农</strong></p>
<p>農 <span class="dicpy">n&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 农业 <span class="encs">[agriculture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">方今之务，莫若使民务农而已矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晁错</span><span class="smcs">《论贵粟疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:务农(从事农业生产);农田水利;农林牧;农末(农业和商业);农政(有关农业的政令、制度);农书(有关农业的书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 农民 <span class="encs">[peasant]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8FB2.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕䢉<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/269A5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦦥</span>辳<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28447.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨑇</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/232A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣊤</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2844B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨑋</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28446.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨑆</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/269A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦦤</span>莀【唐韻】【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>奴冬切，音儂。【說文】耕也，種也。【書&middot;洪範】農用八政。【註】農者，所以厚生也。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;大宰】以九職任萬民。一曰三農生九穀。【註】三農：山農、澤農、平地農也。【左傳&middot;襄九年】其庶人力于農穡。【註】種曰農，斂曰穡。</p><p>又神農，古炎帝號。【前漢&middot;食貨志】闢土植穀曰農。炎帝敎民植穀，故號神農氏，謂神其農業也。</p><p>又萬山氏有子曰農，能植百穀，後世因名耕甿爲農。</p><p>又司農，官名。【前漢&middot;百官志】秦曰治粟內史，漢景帝更名大司農。</p><p>又弘農，郡名。【前漢&middot;地理志註】武帝元鼎四年置。</p><p>又姓也。【風俗通】神農氏後。</p><p>又叶奴當切，音囊。【潘岳&middot;籍田頌】思樂甸畿，薄采其芳。大君戾止，言籍其農。</p><p>又叶奴刀切，音猱。【束皙&middot;勸農賦】惟百里之置吏，各區別而異曹。考治民之踐職，美莫富乎歡農。</p>
