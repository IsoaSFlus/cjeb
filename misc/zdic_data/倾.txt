						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>倾</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">傾</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>斜，歪：～斜。～侧。～塌。～圮。～跌。</li><li>趋向：～向。～心。～慕。～注。左～。右～。</li><li>倒塌：～颓。～覆。～轧（<span class="dicpy">yà</span>）（在同一组织中互相排挤）。</li><li>使器物反转或歪斜以倒出里面的东西；引申为尽数拿出，毫无保留：～箱倒箧（亦称“倾筐倒庋”）。～盆大雨。～城。～洒。～销。</li><li>用尽（力量）：～听。～诉。～吐。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">倾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>倾</strong></p><p>傾 <span class="dicpy">qīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从人，从顷，顷亦声。<span class="yhcs">“顷”</span>有偏侧的意思。从人，多表示人的动作行为。本义:偏侧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bend;incline;lean]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倾，仄也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倾则奸。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼下》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“倾或为侧。”</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“倾，欹侧也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">安无倾。——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">重钧则衡不倾。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日既西倾。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倾耳而听。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高下相倾。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。释文:<span class="yhcs">“不正貌。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俯身倾耳。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:倾葵(向日葵。借指尽忠);倾倚(偏斜不正);倾低(倾斜，歪斜);倾昃(偏斜;不正);倾返(形容峰峦险峻，向下倾斜);倾柯(使枝条倾斜下垂);倾倚(倾斜，歪斜)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 倾塌，倒下 <span class="encs">[collapse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">安无倾。——<span class="smcs">《论语·季氏》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">高台未倾。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">对此欲倒东南倾。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span>诗</p><p><span class="diczx1">樯倾楫摧。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:倾崩(崩溃);大厦将倾;倾国(倾覆邦国;形容容貌极美);倾朝(倾覆朝廷);倾弛(倒塌残破)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 向往，钦佩 <span class="encs">[admire whole-heartedly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一坐尽倾。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马相如传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:倾奉(倾心敬奉);倾瞩(倾心向往)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 死;丧 <span class="encs">[die]</span>。如:倾害(杀害);倾送(断送;葬送);倾送(害死;累死);倾世(亡故);倾生(丧生);倾命(丧命)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 倒出来 <span class="encs">[empty;overturn and pour out]</span></p><p><span
