                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">级</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>级</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">級</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>层次：石～。拾～而上。</li><li>等次：～别。～差（<span class="dicpy">chā</span>）。</li><li>学校里学生所在学年的分段：年～。～任。</li><li>古代指战时或用刑斩下的人头：首～。</li><li>量词，用于台阶、楼梯：从一楼到三楼有四十多～台阶。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> level, rank, class; grade</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Klasse, Stufe, Ebene, Niveau  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> marche,étage,niveau,échelon,classe,promotion</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>级</strong></p><p>級 <span class="dicpy">jí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，表示与线丝有关，及声。本义:丝的次第)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[degree]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">级，丝次弟也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“本谓丝之次弟，故其字从系。引申为凡次弟之称。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为等级，特指官阶爵位的品级 <span class="encs">[level;rank;grade]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">级，等也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拾级聚足。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“等也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">级，阶次也。——<span class="smcs">《声类》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以别贵贱等级之度。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百姓内粟千石，拜爵一级。——<span class="smcs">《史记·秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:超级(超过一般等级的);震级(地震等级);晋级(升到较高的等级);级差(等级之间的差距)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 石阶 <span class="encs">[step]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃领宾者延之而上，分级而立。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">澄源寻视其侧，得级。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其级七千有余。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:石级</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 形容词或副词用于比较中的一种形式或一组形式，以表示数量、质量或关系的特定强度或标准 <span class="encs">[degree]</span>。如:比较级;最高级</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 年级<span class="encs">[school course;grade;class]</span>。如:同级生;留级;升级</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>级</strong></p><p>級 <span class="dicpy">jí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 上或下石阶或楼梯时放脚的踏脚处 <span class="encs">[step]</span>。如:十几级台阶</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一系列层中的一层 <span class="encs">[stage]</span>。如:成斜坡的花园，一级一级陡峭地向水边倾斜;多级火箭</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 秦制，战争中斩敌首一，赐爵一级，称为首级。后以<span class="yhcs">“级”</span>为所斩之首的量词 <span class="encs">[head]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡斩首七千六百级。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·赵充国传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:斩级万余</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr"
