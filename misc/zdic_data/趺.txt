                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">趺</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>趺</strong> <span class="dicpy">fū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;跗&rdquo;。脚背 <span class="encs">[instep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">摩趺，攘臂以游其间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《送方希则序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:趺踝(脚背和踝骨。指趺坐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同&ldquo;跗&rdquo; <span class="encs">[foot]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 脚</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昧爽窗前，双趺独立。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《刻兵略纂闻述》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:趺迹(脚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 山脚</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桥在山趺之次，故流颇急。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">平云</span><span class="smcs">《孤儿记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 花萼 <span class="encs">[calyx]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:趺萼(花的萼片)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 足迹 <span class="encs">[footmark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在</span><span class="diczx2">南安</span>十四年，每执书就明，倚立庭砖，岁久双趺隐然。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 碑下的石座 <span class="encs">[base]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">趺上不得过四尺。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">封演</span><span class="smcs">《封氏闻见记&middot;碑碣》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:趺坐(石碑底座。也作趺座);趺莲(莲座);螭首龟趺。又指器物的底座。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:趺架(放置物件的座架)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>趺</strong> <span class="dicpy">fū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>两足交叠而坐 <span class="encs">[sit cross-legged on]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:跌坐(盘腿而坐);趺足(脚心朝上、脚背朝下盘起腿来);趺跏(双足交叠而坐)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
