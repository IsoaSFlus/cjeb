						<p>● <strong>勒</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄜˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>套在牲畜上带帽子的笼头：马～。</li>
	<li>收住缰绳不使前进：悬崖～马。</li>
	<li>强制：～令。～索。</li>
	<li>统率：～兵。</li>
	<li>雕刻：～石。～碑。～铭。</li>
</ol>

<p><span class="dicpy">lēi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lēi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄟˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用绳子等捆住和套住，然后用力拉紧：～紧。</li>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">勒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>勒</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从革，力声。从&ldquo;革&rdquo;，表示与皮革有关。本义:套在马头上带嚼子的笼头)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bridle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勒，马头络衔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勒面绩总。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;巾车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">案勒一具。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;匈奴传》</span>。注:&ldquo;马辔也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弓矢鞍勒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;乌桓鲜卑传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缨辔贝勒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;既夕礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:马勒;勒面(古代马面饰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 对数能量损失，中子能量损失的一种量度，等于中子初始能量与在慢化过程中任一点上能量之比的自然对数值 <span class="encs">[lethargy]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 勒克司的简称(照度单位) <span class="encs">[Lux]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓。</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>勒</strong> <span class="dicpy">l&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 约束;收紧缰绳以止住马骡等或使马转身 <span class="encs">[restrain;rein in]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勒骐骥而更驾兮，</span><span class="diczx2">造父</span>为我操之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;思美人》</span></p>
