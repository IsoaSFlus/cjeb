						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>伙</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">➊-➎夥</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">huǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huǒ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄛˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>同伴，伙计：同～。～伴。</li>
	<li>旧指店员：店～。～计。</li>
	<li>合伙，结伴，联合起来：～办。～同。</li>
	<li>由同伴组成的集体：合～。入～。</li>
	<li>〈量〉用于人群：一～人。三个一群，五个一～。</li>
	<li>伙食：起～。伙补。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>伙</strong></p>
<p>夥 <span class="dicpy">huǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;火&rdquo;。古兵制十人为火，同火的人互称火伴。俗作伙伴，同伴 <span class="encs">[partner;mate;companion]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:同伙;伙家(伙伴，相与共事的人;对同辈人或同伴的称呼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 合伙;由若干人结成的一群 <span class="encs">[partnership]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:入伙;成群结伙;合伙(合股营业);伙中(大家的银钱中)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>伙</strong></p>
<p>夥 <span class="dicpy">huǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>群聚;结伴;联合一起干 <span class="encs">[combine;join]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:从不伙他们去干;伙会(结伙会合);伙着(伙同，合伙);伙着用</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>伙</strong></p>
<p>夥 <span class="dicpy">huǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>一群的人 <span class="encs">[group;band]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:一伙武装歹徒;这伙学生</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>伙</strong> <span class="dicpy">huǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 伙食。饭食 <span class="encs">[mess;food;meals]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:包伙;搭伙;伙伕(伙夫。旧指军队、单位的炊事员);伙勇(清代湘军、淮军中的伙头军);伙头军(旧指军队中烧饭的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">huo</span></p>
