                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">秉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>秉</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从又，从禾，表示以手持禾。<span class="yhcs">“又”</span>篆体象一只手。本义:禾把，禾束)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a handful of crops]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">秉，禾束也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼有遗秉。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·大田》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或取一秉秆焉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:秉穗(收稻时遗留在田中的禾把与禾实);秉握(一握稻把。言数量少)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 量词。十六斛。如:秉刍(十庾数量的草把)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“柄”</span>。权力，权柄 <span class="encs">[power and authority]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">治国不失秉。——<span class="smcs">《管子·小匡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国子实执</span><span class="diczx2">齐</span>秉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十七年》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>秉</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 手拿着，手持(是一只手从旁边拿着小物) <span class="encs">[grasp;hold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">秉，执也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">武王</span>载旆，有虔秉钺。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·长发》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">右手秉遗穗，左臂悬敝筐。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《观刈麦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:秉圭(持着玉圭);秉烛云长(即云长秉烛，指三国关云长执灯夜读的事);秉笔(执笔;握笔);秉烛达旦(指关云长在护送两位皇嫂途中，秉烛达旦事);秉节(持节。节，古代使臣所持的符节;保持节操，守节);秉牍(手执简牍)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为主持，掌握 <span class="encs">[preside over]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">光</span>秉政前后二十年。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·霍光传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共秉朝政。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·吕布传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:秉公灭私(主持公道，灭除私念);秉正无私(主持正义，没有私念);秉教伽持(佛教语。指执行佛法、施加佛力于众生，以保护扶持之)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 保持;坚持 <span class="encs">[keep;persist in;persevere in]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民之秉彝。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·丞民》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子秉心。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小弁》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span>
