                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蚁</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蚁</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">蟻</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˇ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔蚂～〕昆虫的一科，种类很多，一般体小，呈黑、褐红等色，多在地下做窝，群居生活。简称“蚁”：～封（蚁穴外隆起的小土堆）。～聚。～命（喻微贱的生命）。～民（旧时人民对官府的自称，多用于诉状呈文）。</p></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蚁</strong></p><p>蟻、螘 <span class="dicpy">yǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，义声。本作<span class="yhcs">“螘”</span>。本义:蚂蚁)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 膜翅类昆虫名。俗称蚂蚁 <span class="encs">[ant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">羊肉不慕蚁，蚁慕羊肉，羊肉螘也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蚁附蝇趋(如蚁附膻，如蝇逐臭。意指攀附、奉承);蚁聚蜂屯(像蚂蚁和蜜蜂一样杂乱地聚集在一起)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 酒的泡沫 <span class="encs">[foam]</span>。如:蚁绿(有浮沫的酒);蚁尊(酒杯。借指酒);蚁瓮(酒坛)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 玄色 <span class="encs">[black]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">麻冕蚁裳。——<span class="smcs">《书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“蚁谓色玄也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:蚁裳(玄色下衣)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>蚁</strong></p><p>蟻 <span class="dicpy">yǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>比喻卑微;微末;小 <span class="encs">[tiny;humble]</span>。如:蚁悃(谦称自己的诚挚心意);蚁鼻(比喻微细);蚁窍(小洞);蚁斗(比喻微末的争斗);蚁斗蜗争(比喻微末的争斗)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁蚕">蚁蚕</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁巢">蚁巢</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁垤">蚁垤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁甲">蚁甲</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁民">蚁民</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁丘">蚁丘</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁酸">蚁酸</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁王">蚁王</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/蚁冢">蚁冢</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/87FB.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】魚倚切【集韻】魚綺切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音艤。【說文】作螘，蚍蜉也。【爾雅·釋蟲】蚍蜉，大螘。【疏】螘，通名也，其大者別名蚍蜉，俗呼馬蚍蜉。小者卽名螘，齊人呼螘蛘，《方言》曰蚍蜉，齊魯之閒謂之蚼蟓，西南梁益之閒謂之元蚼，燕謂之蛾蛘。其場謂之坻，或謂之垤是也。其大而赤色斑駁者名蠪，一名朾螘，有翅而飛名<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27548.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧕈</span>，卽飛螘也。【古今注】河內人<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>河而見人馬數千萬，皆如黍米，遊動往來，以火燒之，人皆是蚊蚋，馬皆是大蟻，故名蟻曰元駒。【續博物志】白蟻聞竹雞之聲化爲水。</p><p>又蟻裳。【書·顧命】麻冕蟻裳。【傳】蟻，裳名，色元。</p><p>又蟻丘，山名。【莊子·則陽篇】孔子之楚，舍于蟻丘之漿。</p><p>又白蟻，馬名。【博物志】周穆王八駿，三曰白蟻。</p><p>又浮蟻，醪汁滓酒也。【張衡·南都賦】浮蟻若萍。</p><p>又【唐韻古音】魚我切。義同。　【爾雅】本作螘。【禮記】通作蛾。詳蛾字註。</p>
