                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>辀</strong></p><p>輈 <span class="dicpy">zhōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 车辕 <span class="encs">[shaft(of a cart)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辀，辕也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，大车左右两木直而平者谓之辕，小车居中一木曲而上升谓之辀，故亦曰轩辕，谓其穹隆而高也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荐车直东荣，北辀。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·既夕礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用于大车上的称辕，用于兵车、田车、乘车上的称辀。辀为曲木，一端为方形，置于轴中央，从车底伸出，渐渐隆起，又渐成圆木。木前端置横木，称为<span class="yhcs">“衡”</span>，衡两端作缺月形，夹贴马颈，称为<span class="yhcs">“额”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指车 <span class="encs">[carriage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挟辀以走。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驾龙辀兮乘雷，载云旗兮委蛇。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:造辀的工匠</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
