                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>卤</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鹵</span>、<span class="diczx3">滷</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>制盐时剩下的黑色汁液，味苦有毒。亦称&ldquo;盐卤&rdquo;、&ldquo;苦汁&rdquo;。</li>	<li>浓汁：～汁。～面。</li>	<li>用五香咸水或酱油等浓汁制作食品：～虾。～豆腐。</li>	<li>通&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">鲁</span>&rdquo;，鲁莽。</li>	<li>通&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">橹</span>&rdquo;，大盾。</li>	<li>通&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">掳</span>&rdquo;，掠夺。</li></ol><p>● <strong>卤</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒ〡</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/西">西</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> saline soil; natural salt, rock</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> einpökeln, salzen</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卤</strong></p>
<p>滷、鹵 <span class="dicpy">lǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形，象盐罐(或盐池)中有盐形。本义:盐碱地)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[alkaline soil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span><span class="diczx2">荀吴</span>帅师败 <span class="diczx2">狄</span>于 <span class="diczx2">大鹵</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋&middot;昭公元年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">表淳卤，数疆潦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卤中草木白。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《盐井》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卤田(盐田);卤莽(荒地上的野草);卤泻(含盐碱的土壤);卤地(盐碱地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 天然生成的盐称为&ldquo;卤&rdquo;。泛指食盐 <span class="encs">[salt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">山</span>东食海盐， <span class="diczx2">山</span>西食盐卤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">夷</span><span class="diczx2">狄</span>曰大鹵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卤池(咸水湖);卤桶(盐场盛水的木桶);卤脉(地下卤水的脉系);卤汁(盐汁);卤味(卤水之味);卤烟(熬煮卤水时冒出的烟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 卤素，氟、氯、溴、碘和砹五个元素的任一个，为周期表的ⅦA族的元素，自由状态时它们通常以双原子存在 <span class="encs">[halogen]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卤族;卤化</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用肉类、鸡蛋等做汤加淀粉而成的浓汁，用来浇在面条食物上 <span class="encs">[thick gravy used as a sauce for noodles，etc.]</span>。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/897F.svg">                          <p>鳥在巢上。象形。日在西方而鳥棲，故因以爲東西之西。凡西之屬皆从西。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/897F_1.svg" />棲，西或从木妻。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/897F_2.svg" />卥，古文西。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/897F_3.svg" />卤，籒文西。先稽切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/897F_6112.svg" /><span class="swbf">(西)</span><span class="ywyy">鳥在巢上也。象形。</span><span class="swdz">下象巢，上象鳥，會意。上、下皆非字也，故不曰會意而曰象形。鳥在巢上者，此篆之本義。今音先稽切。古音讀如詵，讀如<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/203A3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠎣</span>。如西施亦作先施。漢書曰：西，遷也。古音在十二、十三部。</span><span class="ywyy">日在㢴方而鳥㢴。</span><span class="swdz">上西，卽下文東西之西也。下西，西之本義也。</span><span class="ywyy">故因㠯爲東㢴之㢴。</span><span class="swdz">此說六書叚借之例。叚借者，本無其字，依聲託事。古本無東西之西，寄託於鳥在巢上之西字爲之。凡許言以爲者類此。韋本訓相背，而以爲皮韋。烏本訓孝烏，而以爲烏呼。來本訓瑞麥，而以爲行來。朋本古文鳳，而以爲朋攩。子本訓十一月昜气動萬物滋，而以爲人偁。後人習焉不察，用其借義而廢其本義，乃不知西之本訓鳥在巢，韋之本訓相背，朋之本訓爲鳳，逐末忘本，大都類是。微許君言之，烏能知之。</span><span class="ywyy">凡㢴之屬皆从㢴。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/897F_6113.svg" /><span class="swbf">(棲)</span><span class="ywyy">㢴或从木妻。</span><span class="swdz">葢从木，妻聲也。从妻爲聲。葢製此篆之時已分别㢴爲東㢴，棲爲鳥在巢，而其音則皆近妻矣。詩可以棲遟，漢嚴發碑作衡門西遟。然則雞棲于塒，雞棲于桀，古本必作雞西。論語爲是棲棲，古本亦必作西西。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/897F_6114.svg" /><span class="swbf">(卥)</span><span class="ywyy">古文㢴。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/897F_6115.svg" /><span class="swbf">(卤)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文㢴。</span><span class="swdz">按鹵下曰从西省。若籒文西如此，則鹵正从籒文卤矣。</span></p>                          </div>
