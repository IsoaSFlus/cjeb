                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>砚</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">硯</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>写毛笔字磨墨用的文具，多数用石做成：～台。笔～。</li><li>旧指同学关系（因同学共笔砚）：同～。～友。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>砚</strong></p><p>硯 <span class="dicpy">yàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石，见声。本义:砚台，磨墨器)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 光滑的石头 <span class="encs">[glossy stone]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">砚，石滑也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:谓石性滑利也。</p><p><span class="diczx1">砚，研也。研墨使和濡也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 砚台 <span class="encs">[inkstone]</span> 一种石刻的写字文具，供研墨、调墨之用</p><p><span class="diczx1">一砚。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">砚冰坚。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:砚瓦(砚台);笔砚</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>砚</strong></p><p>硯 <span class="dicpy">yàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>制作砚 <span class="encs">[make the inkstone]</span>。如:砚璞(可作砚台的美石);砚材(可作砚台的石料)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砚池">砚池</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砚石">砚石</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砚台">砚台</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砚田">砚田</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">硯</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“砚”字头，请参考“硯”字。)</span>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下" target="_blank">午集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下&amp;bs=石" target="_blank">石</a>】
                              硯·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/786F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】吾甸切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】倪甸切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2804D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨁍</span>。【釋名】硯，硏也，硏墨使和濡也。【說文】石滑也。【長箋】滑訓利，利猶厲也，與硏摩同義。【文房四譜】黃帝得玉一紐，治爲墨篆，曰帝鴻氏之硯。【周武王·硯銘】石墨相著而黑，邪心讒言無得汙白。</p><p>又姓。【元文類】國子監司業硯彌堅。</p><p>又【字彙補】古典切，音蹇。濡石也。　【正字通】說文硏硯音義俱分，今俗合爲一。</p>
