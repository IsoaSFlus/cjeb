                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">景</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>景</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǐng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，京声。本义:日光)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sunlight]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">景，日光也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浊明外景，清明内景。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·解蔽》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四时和谓之景风。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南方景风。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span>。按，犹日光风也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日出天而耀景。——</span><span class="diczx2">江淹</span><span class="smcs">《别赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朱光驰北陆，浮景忽西沈。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张载·七哀诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流景曜之韡晔。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">景翳翳以将入。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至若春和景明。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:景曜(光彩);景光(亮光);景焕(光彩照射);景辉(光辉)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 太阳 <span class="encs">[sun]</span>。如:景夕(黄昏;天黑);景纬(日与星);景西(太阳西斜)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 风景，景致 <span class="encs">[landscape]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四时之景不同。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">正则无景。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">景状益近于自然。——</span><span
