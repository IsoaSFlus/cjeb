                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>某</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">mǒu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǒu.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄡˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>代替不明确指出的人、地、事、物等：～人。～处。～国。</li>
	<li>自称（代替&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">我</span>&rdquo;或名字）：～姓李。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> certain thing or person</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> einige  (Zähl)​,gewiss, bestimmt</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> certain,quelque,un tel,tel</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">某</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>某</strong> <span class="dicpy">mǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形。像木上结一个果实，本是&ldquo;梅&rdquo;的象形。( <span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i</span>)本义:梅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 指一定的不明说的人或事物 <span class="encs">[certain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如古人某。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狂生某者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太监某守</span><span class="diczx2">辽东</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">某亦守法。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王某。(王安石。古人作文起稿，写到自己的名字，往往只作&ldquo;某&rdquo;，或在&ldquo;某&rdquo;上冠姓，以后誊写时才把姓名写出。根据书稿编的文集，也常保留&ldquo;某&rdquo;的字样)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:某人;某天;某官某(某某官(叫)某某的(人)。某，文言虚指代词。此处前一个代此人官职，后一个代他的姓名);某等(我等，我们);某们(我们，某是代词);某舍(犹某公子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指不定的不说明的人或事物 <span class="encs">[some]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">某所，而母立于兹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">某年月日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畿辅某官。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">决斗某所。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:某家(指不确定的一家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指代失传的或忘记的人名或时、地等 <span class="encs">[some]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:某者(表示姓名失传的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 自称之词。指代&ldquo;我&rdquo;或本名。旧时谦虚的用法 <span class="encs">[I]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">某启。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:某则以为;某不量敌;某知罪矣;我张某;某以(某矣。自称自己的代词，我);某家(戏曲中自称之词);某乙(自称代称);某甲(自称之代词)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>某</strong> <span class="dicpy">mǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>〈方〉∶妻子 <span class="encs">[wife]</span>。</p>
