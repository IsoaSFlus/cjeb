                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>拔</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bá.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄚˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>抽，拉出，连根拽出：～腿。～草。～牙。～苗助长。</li>    <li>夺取军事上的据点：连～数城。</li>    <li>吸出：～毒。～火罐儿。</li>    <li>选取，提升：提～。～擢。</li>    <li>超出，高出：海～。挺～。～地（山、树、建筑物等高耸在地面上）。～尖儿。出类～萃。</li>    <li>把东西放在凉水里使变凉：把西瓜放在冰水里～一～。</li>    <li>改变：坚韧不～。心志不可～。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拔</strong> <span class="dicpy">bá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，犮( <span class="dicpy">bó</span>)声。本义:拔起，拔出)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pull out;pull up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拔，擢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拔，引也。——<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">确乎其不可拔。——<span class="smcs">《易·乾》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“移也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拔河。——<span class="smcs">《封氏见闻录》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">杨子</span>取为我，拔一毛而利天下，不为也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·尽心上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">力拔山兮气盖世。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">拔剑切而啖之。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">拔剑撞而破之。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拔刃奋起。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:拔毛;拔草;拔秧</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 选拔;提拔 <span class="encs">[promote]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是以先帝简拔以遗陛下。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山涛作</span><span class="diczx2">冀州</span>，甄拔三十余人。—— <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《与韩荆州书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:拔用(提拔任用);拔补(提拔补官);选拔(挑选);提拔(挑选人员任更高职)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 突出;超出 <span class="encs">[stand out]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">势拔</span><span class="diczx2">五岳</span>掩赤城。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:拔绝(卓越);出类拔萃(才能出众)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 突起 <span class="encs">[rise high]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆峭拔险怪。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然峭拔秀丽皆不可与小孤比。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span
