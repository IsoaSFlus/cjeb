						<p>● <strong>镰</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鐮</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>收割谷物和割草的农具：～刀。开～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sickle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Sichel, Sense  (S, Agrar)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faucille</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">镰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>镰</strong></p>
<p>鎌、鐮、鎌 <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，廉声。本义:农具名。即&ldquo;镰刀&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sickle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">镰，锲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刈钩自</span><span class="diczx2">关</span>而西或谓之镰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言五》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:佩镰;两刃镰;开镰收割的季节，村里人都很忙</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 锋利的棱角 <span class="encs">[sharp edge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡箭镞胡合嬴者四镰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言九》</span>。注:&ldquo;棱也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:镰利(比喻议论或笔锋锐利如镰刀)</span></p>
