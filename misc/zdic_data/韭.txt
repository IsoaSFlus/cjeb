						<p>● <strong>韭</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄡˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～菜〕多年生草本植物，叶细长而扁，夏秋间开小白花；叶和花嫩时可食，种子可入药。简称&ldquo;韭&rdquo;，如&ldquo;～黄&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> scallion, leek; radical 179</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Porree  (S)​,Radikal Nr. 179 = Lauch, Knoblauch  (S)</p></div>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>韭</strong></p>
<p>韮 <span class="dicpy">jiǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆，象韭菜长在地上的形状。本义:韭菜)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Chinese chives]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">韭，菜名，一种而久者，故谓之韭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">囿有见韭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《夏小正&middot;正月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">丹熏之山</span>，其草多韭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;北山经》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">韭曰丰本。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四之日其蚤，献羔祭韭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 某些葱属植物的通称 <span class="encs">[onion]</span>。如:大花韭;多叶韭;卵叶韭;天山韭;矮韭</p>

                              韭·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：0</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/97ED.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】舉有切【正韻】已有切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音久。【說文】菜名。一種而久者，故謂之韭。象形。在一之上。一，地也。【韻會】通志云：韭性溫，謂之草鍾乳。【禮&middot;曲禮】韭曰豐本。</p>
<p>又【內則】豚，春用韭，秋用蓼。【儀禮&middot;聘禮】韭葅。【列子&middot;天瑞篇】老韭之爲莧也。【埤雅】韭之美在黃。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋草】蒮，山韭。【疏】韭生山中者名蒮。【本草】李時珍曰：諸葛韭，亦山韭也，但因人命名耳。</p>
<p>又【本草】孝文韭，生塞北，狀如韭，云是魏孝文帝所種。</p>
<p>又【羣芳譜】水韭，五六月堪食，不葷而脃。【北戸錄】水韭，生於池塘中，葉似韭，得非龍爪韭乎。字林云：簽，水中野韭也。</p>
<p>又【本草】麥門冬，一名禹韭，齊名愛韭，秦名烏韭，楚名馬韭，越名羊韭。</p>
