                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>畜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>养禽兽：～产。～牧。～养。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>畜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>禽兽，有时专指家养的禽兽：～肥。～力。～疫。幼～。牲～。家～。～生。六～兴旺。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> livestock, domestic animals</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bestiaux,animaux domestiques,élever,nourrir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>畜</strong> <span class="dicpy">ch&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> (表示牵引，下象出气的牛鼻形。牛鼻被牵着，说明是已被人类驯服豢养的家畜。本义:家畜) 同本义 <span class="encs">[farm animal;livestock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畜，田畜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大畜时也，小畜寡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;杂卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">家养谓之畜，野生谓之兽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十三年》</span>疏</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古者六畜不相为用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拥众数万，马畜弥山，富贵如此!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:家畜、牲畜、幼畜、畜牧(饲养的禽兽);畜牲(指牛、马、羊、鸡、狗、猪等六畜)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/畜肥">畜肥</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/畜圈">畜圈</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/畜栏">畜栏</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/畜力">畜力</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/畜群">畜群</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/畜生">畜生</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/畜疫">畜疫</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>畜</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 饲养 <span class="encs">[禽兽]</span> <span class="encs">[raise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畜鸟者则勿佛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畜鸾皇只。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;大招》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则架竹为巢，下畜牛羊豕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畜置(饲养)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 养育 <span class="encs">[bring up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下谁畜之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公二十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仰不足以事父母，俯不足以畜妻子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡豚狗彘之畜</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔不畜我，复我邦家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;我行其野》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畜爱(养育爱护)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 培养;培植 <span class="encs">[cultivate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子以多识前言往行，以畜其德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;大畜》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:人逸马畜</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 顺从，驯服 <span class="encs">[comply]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孝者畜也。顺于道，不逆于伦，是之为畜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭统》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝共作我畜民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;慉&rdquo;。喜欢，喜爱 <span class="encs">[like]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拊我畜我，长我育我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;蓼莪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 积;积聚。后作&ldquo;蓄&rdquo; <span class="encs">[accumulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">比必有所畜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;序卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畜种菽粟，不足以食之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;七患》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畜积收藏于秋冬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;天论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既畜王资而承故国之衅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">小娥</span>父畜巨产，隐名商贾间。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李公佐</span><span class="smcs">《谢小娥传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畜恨(积恨);畜聚(积储;积累;积储的财物);畜德(修积德行);畜锐(积蓄锐气)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 收容 <span class="encs">[house]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身边畜得一婢，小名</span><span class="diczx2">金杏</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">获罪于两君，天下谁畜之?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畜妾(娶小老婆);畜之(容纳之意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 保存;收藏 <span class="encs">[collect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">李生</span>]</span> 又畜一短剑，甚利。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">蒋防</span><span class="smcs">《霍小玉传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畜藏(积蓄储藏);畜秽(藏秽，有丑恶的行为)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 〈方〉∶熏;呛 <span class="encs">[smoke]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那里面秽气畜人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:畜人(熏人;呛人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">ch&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

</ol></div></div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畜</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上" target="_blank">午集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上&amp;bs=田" target="_blank">田</a>】
                              畜·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/755C.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕嘼【唐韻】丑六切【集韻】敕六切【正韻】昌六切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音觸。【說文】田畜也。引《淮南子》註言：田之汙下黑土者，可畜牧也。○按《淮南》無此語。</p>
<p>又積也。【禮&middot;月令】仲秋之月，乃命有司，趣民收斂，務畜菜。【註】始爲禦冬之備。【釋文】畜，丑六反。</p>
<p>又【內則】子婦無私貨，無私畜。</p>
<p>又止也。【孟子】畜君何尤。【註】畜，止也。敕六反。</p>
<p>又大畜，小畜，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>易卦名。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>許六切，音旭。養也。【易&middot;師卦】地中有水師，君子以容民畜衆。【論語】君賜生，必畜之。【史記&middot;樂毅傳】臣恐侍御者不察先王之所以畜幸臣之理。</p>
<p>又容也。【左傳&middot;襄二十六年】天下誰畜之。【註】畜，猶容也。許六反。</p>
<p>又六畜。【左傳&middot;桓六年】奉牲以告曰：博碩肥腯，謂民力之普存也。謂其畜之碩大蕃滋也。【釋文】畜，許六反。【註】六畜旣大而滋也。【禮&middot;曲禮】問庶人之富，數畜以對。【疏】謂雞豚之屬。【釋文】許六反，鄭註：周禮云始養曰畜。○按六畜之畜，古俱許六反，今人<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>讀作昌六反。</p>
<p>又起也。【詩&middot;小雅】拊我畜我。【箋】畜，起也。【釋文】喜郁反。</p>
<p>又順也。【禮&middot;祭統】孝者，畜也。順于道，不逆于倫，是之謂畜。【註】畜謂順於德敎。【釋文】許六反。</p>
<p>又孝也。【禮&middot;孔子閒居】無服之喪，以畜萬邦。【註】畜，孝也。使萬邦之民競爲孝也。</p>
<p>又【莊子&middot;徐無鬼】許由曰：堯畜畜然仁，吾恐其爲天下笑。</p>
<p>又留也。【禮&middot;儒行】易祿而難畜也。【註】難畜，難以非義久留也。</p>
<p>又姓。漢畜客，畜意，天水有畜氏。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>許救切，音齅。【左傳&middot;僖十九年】古者六畜不相爲用。【疏】養之曰畜，用之曰牲。【釋文】畜，許又反。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;庖人】掌共六畜。【註】六畜，六牲也。【釋文】畜，許又反。</p>
<p>又【集韻】丑救切，惆去聲。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;獸醫疏】在野曰獸，在家曰畜。【前漢&middot;尹翁歸傳】掌畜官，音義。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>與獸同。</p>

												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=田">田</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/755C.svg">                          <p>田畜也。《淮南子》曰：玄田爲畜。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24CB8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤲸</span>，《魯郊禮》畜从田从兹。兹，益也。丑六切〖注〗嘼，古文。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/755C_4081.svg" /><span class="swbf">(畜)</span><span class="ywyy">田畜也。</span><span class="swdz">田畜謂力田之蓄積也。貨殖列傳曰：富人爭奢侈，而任氏獨折節爲儉，力田畜。田畜人爭取賤賈，任氏獨取貴善，非田畜所出弗衣食。艸部曰：蓄，積也。畜與蓄義略同。畜从田，其源也。蓄从艸，其委也。俗用畜爲六<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21F2F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡼯</span>字。古叚爲好字。如說苑尹逸對成王曰：民善之則畜也，不善則讎也。晏子對景公曰：畜君何尤。畜君者，好君也。謂畜卽好之同音叚借也。</span><span class="ywyy">淮南王曰：玄田爲畜。</span><span class="swdz">王，大徐作子。此如引楚莊王止戈爲武，說會意也。玄田猶畇畇原隰也。丑六切。亦許六切。三部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/755C_4082.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24CB8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤲸</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">魯郊禮畜从田从茲。茲，益也。</span><span class="swdz">魯郊禮已見鳥部。此許據魯郊禮文證古文从茲乃合於田畜之解也。艸部曰：茲，艸木多益也。从艸，絲省聲。古文本从茲，小篆乃省其半，而淮南王乃認爲玄字矣，此小篆省改之失也。今本上从茲，則與茲益之云不貫矣。故正之如此。</span></p>                          </div>
