                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>哪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǎ.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄚˇ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 疑问词，后面跟名词或数量词，表示要求在所问范围中有所确定：～样。～里（a.什么地方。b.用于反问句，表示否定，如“我～～知道？”“他～～笨啊？”c.谦辞，推辞对自己的褒奖）。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>哪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">něi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/něi.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄟˇ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ “哪”和“<span class="diczx3">一</span>”的合音，但指数量时不限于一：～个。～年。～会儿。～些。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>哪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">na  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/na.mp3"></a></span> &#x2022;ㄋㄚ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 助词，“<span class="diczx3">啊</span>”字受到前一字韵母ｎ收音的影响而发生的变音：加油干～！</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>哪</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">né  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/né.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄜˊ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔～吒〕中国古代神话里的神名（“<span class="diczx3">吒</span>”读轻声）。</p></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>哪</strong> <span class="dicpy">nǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，那声。本义:表疑问的词)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示疑问，要求在同类事物中加以确指 <span
