<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">蠔</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄠˊ</span></p>

<p><span class="dicpy">c&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘˋ</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“蚝”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/蛓">蛓</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=虫">虫</a>】</div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/86D3.svg">                          <p>毛蟲也。从虫<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22994.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢦔</span>聲。千志切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/86D3_5850.svg" /><span class="swbf">(蛓)</span><span class="ywyy">毛蟲也。</span><span class="swdz">不曰毛蠹者，不居木中，但食葉也。釋蟲云：蟔，蛅蟴。郭云：蛓屬也。今靑州人呼蛓爲蛅蟴。孫叔然云：八角螫蟲。失之。按今俗云刺毛者是也。食木葉，體有棱角，有毛，有采色，毛能螫人。叔然說不誤也。其老而成蛹，則外有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23A8A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣪊</span>如雀<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21589.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡖉</span>然。本艸經謂之雀甕。或出成蛾放子如蠶子。或卽<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21589.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡖉</span>育於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23A8A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣪊</span>中。故本艸云：雀甕，蛅蟖房也。蛅蟴音髯斯。</span><span class="ywyy">从虫<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22992.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢦒</span>聲。讀若笥。</span><span class="swdz">三字依爾雅釋文補。千志切。一部。本艸作蚝。音同。</span></p>                          </div>
