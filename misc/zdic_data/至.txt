						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>至</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>到：～此。自始～终。从古～今。～于。以～。甚～。</li>    <li>极、最：～少。～亲。～交（最相好的朋友）。～诚。～高无上。～理名言。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">至</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>至</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。从一，一犹地，指事，不去而下来。古文从土，上亦象飞下之形。尾上首下。本义:到来，到达)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(上古时期多用<span class="yhcs">“至”</span>，中古时期多用<span class="yhcs">“到”</span>) <span class="encs">[arrive;reach]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至，鸟飞从高下至地也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凤鸟不至。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至，到也。——<span class="smcs">《字林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至，到也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王朝至于</span><span class="diczx2">商</span>郊 <span class="diczx2">牧野</span>。——<span class="smcs">《书·牧誓》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我征聿至。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·东山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">远道不至。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·辞过》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故不积跬步，无以至千里。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至则行矣。——<span class="smcs">《论语·微子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至则无可用。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风雨骤至。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王粲</span><span class="smcs">《登楼赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民至老死不相往来。——<span class="smcs">《老子·小国寡民》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孝武帝</span>时，至代相。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李将军列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至</span><span class="diczx2">通州</span>，几以不纳死。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《<指南录>后序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">富者不能至。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">彭端淑</span><span class="smcs">《为学一首示子侄》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前狼又至。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至子之世。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:至旦(到天明);水流至足;至竟(到底;毕竟);至止(到达，到来。止，语气词);无微不至;自春至冬;由东至西;至于(到;达到);至乎(至于。到;达到);至至(达到，道的最高境地);至到(指程度达到极点;到)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 来;去 <span class="encs">[come;go]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至，来也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子是以知</span><span class="diczx2">桓王</span>之失 <span class="diczx2">郑</span>也…己弗能有，而以与人;人之不至，不亦宜乎!——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公五年》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>至</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉?</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 形容事物的尽善尽美。犹言最好、最高、最大 <span <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾爱汝至。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span
