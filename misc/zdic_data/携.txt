<p>● <strong>携</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>带：～手。～带。扶老～幼。</li>
	<li>离，叛离：～离。～贰。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">携</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>携</strong></p>
<p>攜、擕 <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，巂( <span class="dicpy">xī</span>)声，俗作攜。本义:提着)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(把地上的物品举提起来) <span class="encs">[carry in hand]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">携，提也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如璋如圭，如取如携。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;板》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:携壶;携带(提挈，照顾);携酒牵羊;携杖</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拿着，持 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闲携杖，漫出门，官槐满路叶纷纷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孔尚任</span><span class="smcs">《桃花扇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:携盘;携帚</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 牵挽;挽扶 <span class="encs">[lead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相携于道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;览冥》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾与汝并肩携手。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:携抱(牵挽扶抱);携率(率领);携将(扶持);相携于道;携幼扶老;携扶;携负(牵背)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 带，随身一道 <span class="encs">[bring along]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">携其妻子。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;襄公二十七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是夫负妇戴，携子入于海。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;让王》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民扶老携幼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">携幼入室。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">携童几数人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:携子同行;携女归宁;携行;携家眷同行;携款潜逃;携械投诚</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;愶&rdquo;。离;背离;离散 <span class="encs">[discord]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">招携以礼，怀远以德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不如私许复曹，卫以以携之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其刑矫诬，百姓携贰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">须要宣布皇灵，携其党羽，则王直势孤，自不能容。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西湖二集》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:携爽(背离);携弛(有二心而松懈);携泮(离散);携贰(有二心，叛离);携背(背离，背叛);携叛(背叛)</span></p>
