						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>渔</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">漁</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˊ</span></p><ol><li>捕鱼：～业。～翁。～村。～船。～网。～民。～汛。～歌。～火。竭泽而～。</li><li>谋取，夺取不应得的东西：～夺。～利。～色（猎取美色）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">渔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>渔</strong></p><p>漁 <span class="dicpy">yú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从水，从鱼。小篆从二鱼。本义:捕鱼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渔，捕鱼也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命渔师伐蛟，命渔师始渔。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">獭祭鱼然后渔。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渔者走渊。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以渔采为业。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">临溪而渔，溪深而鱼肥。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:渔樵(打鱼砍柴);渔采(捕捞采集);渔事(捕渔业);渔弋(捕鱼猎禽);渔泽(可供垂钓的水泽);渔矶(可供垂钓的水边岩石);</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 掠夺 <span class="encs">[plunder;rob]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民方苦于侵渔，果所在响应。——</span><span class="diczx2">方勺</span><span class="smcs">《方腊起义》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:渔色渔财(猎取美女和财物);渔猎女色(似捕鱼打猎那样谋取、玩弄女性);渔人得利(比喻双方相争，却让第三者坐收其利)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 猎取，寻觅 <span class="encs">[seek]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小鸡在笼子里悠然地渔着食。——</span><span class="diczx2">郭沫若</span><span class="smcs">《归去来·鸡之归去来》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>渔</strong></p><p>漁 <span class="dicpy">yú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>名</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 捕鱼的人 <span class="encs">[fishman]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">神心重丘壑，散步怀渔樵。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘孝威</span><span class="smcs">《奉和六月壬午应令诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:渔父之图(比喻行动之中别有所图);渔叟(渔翁);渔蓑(渔人的蓑衣);渔樵(渔人和樵夫);渔乡(渔民聚居的地区);渔阳三弄(渔阳三叠，渔阳曲，渔阳鼓，渔阳掺，渔阳操。均为渔阳参挝，鼓曲名);渔阳鼙鼓(指公元755年安禄山于渔阳举兵叛唐事。鼙鼓，骑兵用的小鼓)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr                         </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上" target="_blank">巳集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=巳集上&amp;bs=水" target="_blank">水</a>】
                              漁·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6F01.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】語居切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】牛居切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音魚。【說文】捕魚也。【易&middot;繫辭】以佃以漁。</p>
<p>又侵取無擇曰漁。【禮&middot;坊記】諸侯不下漁色。【註】漁色，取象捕魚，然中網取之，是無所擇。</p>
<p>又或作。【周禮&middot;天官】人掌以時。</p>
<p>又水名，在漁陽。【水經注】漁水，出縣東南。</p>
<p>又姓，宋漁仲脩。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>牛據切，音御。義同。　【韻會】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29F2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩼪</span>。【徐曰】从二魚，魚多也。篆文从省。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/29F2A.svg">                          <p>捕魚也。从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29EB0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩺰</span>从水。漁，篆文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29F2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩼪</span>从魚。語居切〖注〗金文漁从魚从廾。文二　重一&nbsp;</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/29F2A_10358.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29F2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩼪</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">搏魚也。</span><span class="swdz">搏，舊作捕，今正。搏，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A61.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡩡</span>持也。漢人用搏字多如此。捕魚字古多作魚，如周禮䱷人，本作魚。此與取鼈者曰鼈人，取獸者曰獸人同也。左傳：公將如棠觀魚者。魚者，謂捕魚者也。呂氏春秋、淮南鴻烈高注每云：漁讀如論語之語，讀如相語之語。尋其文義皆由本文作魚，故爲讀若以别諸水蟲。周禮音義：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248CF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣏</span>本作魚。又音御。御音卽高氏之語音也。然則古文本作魚，作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29EB0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩺰</span>。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29F2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩼪</span>其籒文乎。至小篆則<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2179E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡞞</span>爲漁矣。周禮當从古作魚人。作䰻者，次之。作䱷者，非也。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29EB0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩺰</span>水。</span><span class="swdz">必从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29EB0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩺰</span>者，捕魚則非一魚也。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29EB0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩺰</span>水者，魚之驚透於水也。語居切。五部。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/29F2A_10359.svg" /><span class="swbf">(漁)</span><span class="ywyy">篆文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29F2A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩼪</span>。从魚。</span><span class="swdz">後篆文者，亦先二後上之例也。</span></p>                          </div>
