                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>划</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字">➊<span class="diczx3">劃</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄚ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>用刀或其它东西把物件分开或从物件上面擦过：～一道口子。</li>	<li>用桨拨水使船行动：～水。～船。～艇。</li>	<li>合算，按利益情况计较是否相宜：～算。</li></ol><p>● <strong>划</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">劃</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hu&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄚˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>分开：～界。～分。～时代意义。</li>	<li>设计：计～。筹～。策～。出谋～策。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">划</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>划</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 以桨拨水使船前进 <span class="encs">[paddle;row]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:划行(拨桨行船);划船运动(水上运动之一)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 合算 <span class="encs">[be to one&rsquo;s profit;pay]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:划得来(合算，值得);划不来(不合算，不值得)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指二人伸指猜拳行酒。也作&ldquo;豁拳&rdquo; <span class="encs">[finger-guessing game;a drinking game]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:划拳</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>划</strong> <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>划子，小船 <span class="encs">[small rowboat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:划揪(船桨);划子船(小船);划木(小木船)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>划</strong></p>
<p>劃 <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用尖利物把东西割开 <span class="encs">[cut the surface of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:划上消息(闩上扣键);划玻璃;手上划了一个口子</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 摩擦;抹拭 <span class="encs">[scratch;strike]</span>。</p>
