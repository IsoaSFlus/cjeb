                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">择</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>择</strong></p><p>擇 <span class="dicpy">zé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，睪( <span class="dicpy">yì</span>)声。本义:选取，挑选)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[select;choose;pick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">择，柬选也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与恶剑无择。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·简选》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“别也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而错择名利。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·俶真》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“取也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">申此择辞。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·韦元成传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓创艾也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">择师而教之。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">择其一二。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不择食矣。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不择人而问焉。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:择席之癖(有选择睡铺的习惯);择吉结缡(选择吉日，迎娶完婚);择邻(选择邻居);择木(选择主人，为其效劳);择术(选择职业);择尤(挑选突出的);择决(挑选);择车(经过挑选的战车);择官(挑选官职);择婿(挑选女婿;挑选夫婿)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 捉 <span class="encs">[catch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">神武</span>后见之，哀其憔悴，以膝承其首，亲为择虱。——<span class="smcs">《北史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“殬”</span>( <span class="dicpy">dù</span>)。败坏;不合法度 <span class="encs">[ruin;undermine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罔有择言在身。——<span class="smcs">《书·吕刑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">口无择言，身无择行。——<span class="smcs">《孝经》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:择言(败言或不合法度的议论);择行(败坏的德行或不合法度的行为)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 挑剔 <span class="encs">[nitpick]</span></p><p><span
