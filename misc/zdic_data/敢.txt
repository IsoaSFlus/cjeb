                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>敢</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄢˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>有勇气，有胆量：勇～。果～。～闯。～死队。～作～为。～怒而不～言。</li>	<li>谦辞，&ldquo;不敢&rdquo;的简称，冒昧的意思：～问。～请。～烦。</li>	<li>方言，莫非：～许。～怕。～是哥哥回来了？</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">敢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>敢</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p>(会意。本义:勇敢，有胆量)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bold;courageous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敢，进取也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敢，勇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">信理遂惔谓之敢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">洁廉而果敢者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记&middot;文王官人》</span>。注:&ldquo;不忧不惧也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下有中敢直其身。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;性恶》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">刚毅勇敢不以伤人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;非十二子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">九国之师，逡巡而不敢进。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">袭</span>与 <span class="diczx2">凌统</span>俱为前部，各将敢死百人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;董袭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">平原君</span>从之，得敢死之士三千人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敢毅(勇敢刚毅);敢士(勇士);敢直(果敢正直);敢勇(果敢英勇);敢断(果敢决断)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>敢</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 有胆量做某种事情 <span class="encs">[dare to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乡之行劫缚者，侧目莫敢过其门。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡人帅不腆</span><span class="diczx2">吴国</span>之役，遵汶之上，不敢左右，唯好之故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人不敢与忤视。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
