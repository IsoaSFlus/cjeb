<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cord; woven belt; hem; hemming</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kordel, Kord  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cordon,ceinture brodée,coudre</p></div>

data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 织成的带子 <span class="encs">[band;tape]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绲，织带也。从糸，昆声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">竹闭绲縢。——<span class="smcs">《诗·秦风·小戎》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“绳也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绲带。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·南匈奴传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“织成布也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“衮”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 帝王及公侯的礼服 <span class="encs">[robes worn by the emperor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当穷台绲，松侨协轨。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《领校巴郡太守樊敏碑》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 衮职，三公之职 <span class="encs">[high-ranking government officials]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">维时假阶，将受绲职。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《卫尉卿方碑》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“昆”</span>( <span class="dicpy">kūn</span>)。后代子孙 <span class="encs">[descendants]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是刊碑，以示后绲。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span><span class="smcs">《绥民校尉熊君碑》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>绲</strong></p><p>緄 <span class="dicpy">gǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用带子保护、加强或装饰 <span class="encs">[band]</span>。如:在裤口上绲一条边</p><p><span
