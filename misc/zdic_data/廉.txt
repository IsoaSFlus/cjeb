						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>廉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lián  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lián.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>堂屋的侧边：～隅（棱角，喻品行端方，有气节）。</li><li>不贪污：～洁。～正。～明。</li><li>便（<span class="dicpy">pián </span>）宜，价钱低：物美价～。</li><li>察考，访查：“且～问，有不如吾诏者，以重论之”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">廉</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>廉</strong></p><p>亷 <span class="dicpy">lián</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lián.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，兼声。从<span class="yhcs">“广”</span>，表示与房屋有关。本义:厅堂的侧边)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[side of hall]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">廉，仄也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，堂之侧边曰廉，故从广。天子之堂九尺，诸侯七尺，大户五尺，士三尺。堂边皆如其高。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">设席于堂廉东上。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡饮礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“侧边曰廉。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故陛九级上，廉远地，则堂高。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·贾谊传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 边 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">边谓之廉，角谓之隅。——<span class="smcs">《九章算术》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">入鲜水北句廉上。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·赵充国传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓水岸曲而有廉棱也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 棱角。亦指物体露出棱角;有棱角 <span class="encs">[edge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">进而眂之，欲其帱之廉也。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哀以立廉。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“廉，廉隅也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:廉正(有棱角而方正);廉利(锋利);廉角(廉隅，廉棱。棱角);廉锷(棱角锋利)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 旧称有节操、不苟取的人 <span class="encs">[honest and clean man]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">廉干如古人某。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若翁廉。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓。如:廉颇(赵国的名将，多次打败秦军)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>廉</strong> <span class="dicpy">lián</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lián.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 正直;刚直;品行方正 <span class="encs">[honest and upright]</span></p><p><span
