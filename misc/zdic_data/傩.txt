                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">傩</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>傩</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">儺</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">nu&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄨㄛˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>行走姿态柔美：&ldquo;佩玉之～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古代腊月驱逐疫鬼的仪式：～舞。～戏（中国地方戏曲剧种之一，演员戴木面具，多用反复的、大幅度的程式动作表现请神驱邪、祈福及简单的战斗故事）。～神（传说中驱除瘟疫的神灵）。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>傩</strong></p>
<p>儺 <span class="dicpy">nu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，难声。本义:步行有节度)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[temperance;abstinence]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巧笑之傩，佩玉之傩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;竹竿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指古迎神赛会 <span class="encs">[an ancient festival to exorcise the devil causing any plague]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乡人傩，朝服而立于阼阶。(阼:古指东面的台阶。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;乡党》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:傩祓(驱除疫鬼，祓除灾邪);傩逐(驱除疫鬼仪式中所唱的歌);傩鼓(驱除疫鬼仪式中敲击的鼓声)</span></p>

<p>又儺神凡十二，皆使之追惡凶。詳見【後漢&middot;禮儀志】。</p>
<p>又猗儺，柔順貌。【詩&middot;檜風】隰有萇楚，猗儺其枝。</p>
<p>又【小雅】隰桑有阿，其葉有難。【註】盛貌。</p>
<p>又【集韻】乃可切，音娜。【詩&middot;衞風】佩玉之儺。【註】儺，行有度也。【毛詩&middot;古音考】猗儺，音阿那。</p>
<p>又與旎那娜<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>通。【楚辭&middot;九辯】紛旖<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29C8C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩲌</span>乎都房。【宋玉&middot;高唐賦】猗旎豐沛。【張衡&middot;南都賦】阿那蓊茸。【<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>思王&middot;洛神賦】華容婀娜。音義<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>同。</p>
