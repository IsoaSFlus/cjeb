                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>铮</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">錚</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>〔～～〕ａ．象声词，金属撞击声；ｂ．喻才能突出，如“铁中～～”；ｃ．喻刚正不阿，如“～～铁骨”。</li><li>（<span class="diczx3">錚</span>）</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>铮</strong></p><p>錚 <span class="dicpy">zhēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><象></p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，争声。本义:金属相击声) 同本义 <span class="encs">[clang;clank]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铮，金声也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冲牙铮鎗。——</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《藉田赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“玉声。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铮鏦然有京都声。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">繁华远容绮，铮鏦美金错。——</span><span class="diczx2">王禹偁</span><span class="smcs">《酬种放征君一百韵》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">却好那一鞭打将下来，正在刀口上，铮地一声响，火光迸散。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:铮枪(玉相撞击声);铮鏦(争枞。形容金属触击声)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhèng</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/铮铮">铮铮</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>铮</strong></p><p>錚 <span class="dicpy">zhèng</span></p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 〈方〉∶<span class="encs">[器物表面]</span>光亮耀眼 <span class="encs">[shining]</span>。如:玻璃擦得铮亮</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
