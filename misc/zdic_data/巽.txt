                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">巽</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>巽</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>八卦之一，卦形为☴。又为六十四卦之一，卦形为䷸，巽下巽上。《玉篇&bull;丌部》：&ldquo;巽，卦名。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>具备。《説文&bull;丌部》：&ldquo;巽，具也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>卑顺；怯懦。《廣雅&bull;釋詁一》：&ldquo;巽，順也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/遜">遜</a>&rdquo;。谦恭；谦让。《字彙&bull;已部》：&ldquo;巽，與遜同。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>消散。《釋名&bull;釋天》：&ldquo;巽，散也，物皆生布散也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。《萬姓統譜&bull;願韻》：&ldquo;巽，見《姓苑》。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> 5th of the 8 trigrams; south-east; mild, modest, obedient</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ausführen, befolgen  (V)​,sanft, milde ,verzichten, abdanken</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>巽</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆。从丌)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[have]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巽，具也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 消散 <span class="encs">[disperse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巽，散也，物皆生布散也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释天》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>巽</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 八卦之一。代表风 <span class="encs">[wind;one of the eight diagrams of bagua]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巽为木，为风。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:巽地(吉利的地方);巽二郎(传说中的风神);巽二(古时传说中的风神名。<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span>有&ldquo;巽为木，为风&rdquo;，故名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 东南方 <span class="encs">[southeast]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:巽方(东南方);巽地(东南方位);巽风(东南风。又称&ldquo;清明风&rdquo;、&ldquo;景风&rdquo;。古有八卦主八风之说);巽隅(指东南角)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>巽</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>卑顺;怯懦 <span class="encs">[subservient]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">童蒙之吉，顺以巽也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;蒙》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏引 <span class="diczx2">褚</span>氏曰:&ldquo;顺者心不违也，巽者外迹相卑下也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:巽顺(卑顺、顺从);巽词(巽辞。委婉的言词);巽与(顺从，附和)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">巽</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中" target="_blank">寅集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集中&amp;bs=己" target="_blank">己</a>】
                              巽·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：9</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5DFD.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22045.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢁅</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>蘇困切，音遜。【說文】本作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22334.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢌴</span>，具也。篆文作巽。【徐鉉曰】庶物皆具，丌以薦之。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】卦名。【韻會】巽，入也。柔也，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>也。【易&middot;巽卦疏】巽者，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>順之名。說卦云：巽，入也。蓋以巽是象風之卦，風行無所不入，故以入爲訓。若施之於人事，能自<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>巽者，亦無所不容。然巽之爲義，以<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>順爲體，以容入爲用，故受巽名矣。</p>
<p>又與遜通。【書&middot;堯典】汝能庸命巽朕位。【釋文】巽，讓也。【集傳】巽遜古通用。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】雛睆切，音撰。持也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶須閏切，音濬。【易&middot;蒙卦】童蒙之吉，順以巽也。叶下順。</p>
<p>又叶須絹切，選去聲。【易&middot;家人】六二之吉，順以巽也。叶上變。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5DFA.svg">                          <p>具也。从丌<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A0E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠨎</span>聲。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22045.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢁅</span>，古文巽。巽，篆文巽。蘇困切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：庶物皆具丌以薦之。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DFA_1647.svg" /><span class="swbf">(巺)</span><span class="ywyy">具也。</span><span class="swdz">孔子說易曰：巺，入也。巺乃愻之假借字。愻，順也。順故善入。許云具也者，巺之本義也。巺今作巽。</span><span class="ywyy">从丌<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A0E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠨎</span>聲。</span><span class="swdz">形聲包會意。卪部曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A16.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠨖</span>，二卪也。巺从此。按二卪者，具意也。蘇困切。古音在十四部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DFA_1648.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22045.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢁅</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文巺。</span><span class="swdz">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A0E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠨎</span>从幵。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DFA_1649.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2204C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢁌</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">篆文巺。</span><span class="swdz">汗<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>、古文四聲韵載此體各乖異，未詳宜何從也。竊疑此篆字當作籒，字之誤也。古文下从幵，幵亦具意也。籒文緐重，則从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A0E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠨎</span>从幵而又从丌。古文四聲韵作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2204C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢁌</span>，葢不誤。小篆則省幵作巺。後人<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/256EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥛬</span>字則从籒變之作巽。說文仿<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/256EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥛬</span>爲之，非也。</span></p>                          </div>
