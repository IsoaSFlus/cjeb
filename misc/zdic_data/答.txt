                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>答</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&aacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄚˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>回话，回复：回～。对～。～辩。～复。～案。对～如流。</li>
	<li>受了别人的好处，还（<span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;n </span>）报别人：报～。～报。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>答</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">dā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄚˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>义同（d&aacute;），用于&ldquo;答应&rdquo;&ldquo;答理&rdquo;等词。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> answer, reply; return; assent to</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Antwort  (S)​,antworten  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> adresser la parole,répondre à un salut,faire attention à,s'occuper de,répondre,rendre,payer de retour</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">答</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>答</strong> <span class="dicpy">dā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> &mdash;&mdash;义同&ldquo;答&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">d&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> )，专用于&ldquo;答应&rdquo;、&ldquo;答理&rdquo;等词</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">d&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答白">答白</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答茬儿">答茬儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答腔">答腔</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答讪">答讪</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答声">答声</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答言">答言</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答应">答应</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答允">答允</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/答嘴">答嘴</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>答</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，合声。①本义:以竹补篱。②答谢，报答)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 回话;应对 <span class="encs">[reply;answer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听言则答。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;雨无止》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既发则答君而俟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡射礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见渔人，乃大惊，问所以来。具答之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">兰芝</span>仰头答:&ldquo;理实如兄言。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">渔歌互答。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆缄口不答。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:应答如流;答教(预先写好的准备答问的书面文字);答言(用言语回答);答语(回答的话;应对的话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 报礼;报答。引申为酬答 <span class="encs">[reciprocate(another&#39;s kindness，favor，etc.)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">适不答兹谓不次。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;五行志》</span>。注:&ldquo;报也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因厚赂</span><span class="diczx2">单于</span>，答(报答)其善意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:答施(报答恩惠);答效(报效;为报答恩德而为对方尽力)</span></p>
