                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">薄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>薄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>厚度小的：～片。～饼。</li>
	<li>冷淡，不热情：～待。</li>
	<li>味道淡：酒味很～。</li>
	<li>土地不肥沃：～田。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>薄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄛˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>义同&ldquo;薄&rdquo;（<span class="dicpy">b&aacute;o</span>），用于合成词或成语，如&ldquo;厚薄&rdquo;，&ldquo;浅薄&rdquo;，&ldquo;尖嘴薄舌&rdquo;等。</li>
	<li>轻微，少：～礼。～产。～命。～寒。～酬。菲～。</li>
	<li>不庄重，不厚道：～夫。～幸（负心）。～情。轻～。刻～。</li>
	<li>轻视：鄙～。厚今～古。</li>
	<li>不充实，不坚强：～弱。</li>
	<li>迫近：～近。～暮（傍晚）。日～西山。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/箔">箔</a>&rdquo;，帘子。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>薄</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄛˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>[薄荷]，多年生草本植物。茎和叶有清凉香味，可入药。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">薄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>薄</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="encs">[口]</span>∶不厚。引申为稀薄，淡薄 <span class="encs">[thin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如临深渊，如履薄冰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:薄衣(单薄的衣服);薄眉(淡扫蛾眉，用浅黛画的眉毛);薄唇轻言(嘴唇薄，说话声音轻细。引申指说话刻薄);薄纸;薄肉片</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 土地含养分少，不肥沃的 <span class="encs">[infertile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地薄，寡于积聚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:土地薄;薄田(不肥沃的田地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 感情不深;冷淡 <span class="encs">[indifferent;be cold toward sb.;lack in warmth]</span>。</p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 味淡 <span class="encs">[tasteless; weak]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:薄咸(味淡和味咸);薄薄(形容滋味淡薄)</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;博&rdquo;。搏击;拍;击 <span class="encs">[beat]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 减少，减损，减轻 <span class="encs">[reduce]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 轻视，看不起 <span class="encs">[look down upon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而夫婿乐逸，为婢仆所惑，日以厌薄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:厚此薄彼;薄慢(怠慢);薄情(不念情义。多用于男女情爱);薄悻(薄情;负心);薄待(轻视;亏待)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 侵入;混杂 <span class="encs">[trespass upon;mix]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矢溺皆闭其中，与饮食之气相薄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>

<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 物体扁平，与&ldquo;厚&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[thin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">战战兢兢，如临深渊，如履薄冰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">太古薄葬，棺厚三尺，衣衾三领。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 微薄 <span class="encs">[meagre;scant;simple and crude]</span>。</p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 急迫;迅速 <span class="encs">[urgent;fast]</span>。</p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:薄德(菲德，道德不厚;缺德);薄相(轻薄之相。有捉弄、玩弄的意思)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:薄夫(刻薄的人);薄心肠(言待人寡恩无义)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> <span class="encs">[土地]</span>贫瘠 <span class="encs">[infertile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薄田十五顷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陈寿</span><span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">薄</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              薄·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=19" target="_blank">19</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8584.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】傍各切，音泊。林薄也。【楚辭註】林草不交錯曰薄。【揚雄&middot;甘泉賦】列新雉于林薄。【註】草叢生曰薄。</p>
<p>又簾也。【禮&middot;曲禮】帷薄之外不趨。【史記&middot;周勃世家】勃以織薄曲爲生。【索隱曰】織蠶薄也。</p>
<p>又厚薄。</p>
<p>又少也。【詩&middot;周南】薄澣我衣。</p>
<p>又聊也。【詩&middot;周南】薄言采之。</p>
<p>又輕也。【前漢&middot;董仲舒傳】愍世俗之靡薄。</p>
<p>又嫌也。【前漢&middot;張安世傳】薄朕忘故。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】勉也。秦晉曰釗，或曰薄。故其鄙語曰薄努，猶勉努也。</p>
<p>又【博雅】薉也。</p>
<p>又集也。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】奄薄水渚。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;蘇秦傳】心搖搖如懸旌，而無所終薄。</p>
<p>又被也。【書&middot;益稷】外薄四海。</p>
<p>又水名。【山海經】蟲尾之山，薄水出焉。</p>
<p>又姓。【史記&middot;外戚世家】薄太后父，吳人，姓薄氏。</p>
<p>又亭名。【後漢&middot;郡國志】滎陽有薄亭。</p>
<p>又草名。薄荷，見荷字註。</p>
<p>又通亳。【禮&middot;郊特牲】薄社北牖。【註】殷社。【荀子&middot;議兵篇】湯以薄，武以鎬。</p>
<p>又迫各切，音博。廹也。【易&middot;說卦傳】雷風相薄。【左傳&middot;僖二十四年】薄而觀之。</p>
<p>又迫晚曰薄暮。</p>
<p>又侵也。【荀子&middot;天論篇】寒暑未薄而疾。</p>
<p>又匹各切，音粕。【詩&middot;齊風】載驅薄薄。【傳】疾驅聲也。</p>
<p>又薄革切，同欂。壁柱也。【爾雅&middot;釋宮】屋上薄謂之筄。</p>
<p>又【唐韻古音】讀蒲。《書》序：成王旣踐奄，將遷其君於蒲姑。《左傳》《史記》作薄姑。</p>
