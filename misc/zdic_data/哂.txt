<p>◎ <strong>哂</strong> <span class="dicpy">shěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 微笑 <span class="encs">[smile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫子何哂</span><span class="diczx2">由</span>也?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;先进》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聊博一哂;哂然(笑貌);哂笑(微笑);哂存(笑纳);哂收(笑纳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 讥笑 <span class="encs">[sneer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">童奴哂笑妻子骂，一字不给饥寒驱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">戴表元</span><span class="smcs">《少年行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:哂笑(讥笑);哂谑(调笑;戏谑)</span></p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“哂”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/弞">弞</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八">卷八</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷八&amp;swbs=欠">欠</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5F1E.svg">                          <p>笑不壞顔曰弞。从欠，引省聲。式忍切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/3C1D_1.svg" /> <span class="swbf">(㰝)</span> <span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>不壞顏曰<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23887.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣢇</span>。</span> <span class="swdz">各本篆作弞。今正。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/264B1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦒱</span>廣韵：弞式忍切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>不壞顏也。集韵&bull;類篇同。今按曲禮：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>不至矧。注云：齒本曰矧，大<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>則見此。然則笑見齒本曰矧，大笑也。不壞顏曰<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23887.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣢇</span>，小笑也。二義不當同音，淺人因己與弓略相似，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/216B6.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡚶</span>合之耳。玉篇於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23891.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣢑</span>欣二文下曰：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23887.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣢇</span>，呼來切。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>不壞顏也。此希馮時所據說文也。於歛歈二文之閒曰：弞，式忍切。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>不壞顏也。此孫強、陳彭年所據誤本說文也。學者可以悟矣。廣雅：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23887.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣢇</span>，笑也。楚辭、吳都賦作咍。○齒本曰矧，謂矧卽齗之叚借也。大戴：高柴執親之喪，未嘗見齒。盧注曰：哂則齒見，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>則矧見。按論語：夫子哂之。馬曰：哂，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25B07.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥬇</span>也。葢哂卽矧。盧語未覈。說文無哂，後人因哂矧造弞耳。</span> <span class="ywyy">从欠己聲。</span> <span class="swdz">各本作引省聲。式忍切。今正。呼來切。一部。</span></p>                          </div>
