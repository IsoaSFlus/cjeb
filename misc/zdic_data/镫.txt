<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鐙</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&egrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>镫</strong></p>
<p>鐙 <span class="dicpy">d&egrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&egrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代盛熟食器皿 <span class="encs">[cooker]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">镫，锭也。从金，登声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，即登也。古用瓦，后世范金为之，又制此字。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">执醴授之，执镫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭统》</span>。注:&ldquo;镫，豆下跗也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 马镫 <span class="encs">[stirrup]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">镫，鞍镫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古同&ldquo;灯&rdquo; <span class="encs">[lamp;light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兰膏明烛，华镫错些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9419.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】都滕切【集韻】【韻會】都騰切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音登。【說文】錠也。【徐鉉曰】錠中置燭，故謂之鐙。今俗別作燈，非是。【楚辭&middot;招魂】華鐙錯些。【劉公幹&middot;贈五官中郞將詩】明鐙熺炎光。</p>
<p>又山名。【山海經】鼓鐙之山。</p>
<p>又與登同。【儀禮&middot;公食大夫禮】實于鐙。【註】瓦豆謂之鐙。【疏】詩：于豆于登。毛傳：木曰豆，瓦曰登。【後漢&middot;禮儀志】瓦鐙一，彤矢四。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】都鄧切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】丁鄧切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音嶝。【廣韻】鞍鐙。【集韻】馬鞁具。【正字通】馬鞍兩旁，足所踏也。【韓致堯詩】和裙穿玉鐙。</p>
<p>又【韻會】豆下跗。【禮&middot;祭統】執鐙。【註】鐙，豆下跗也。【釋文】鐙，音登。</p>
<p>又丁鄧反。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9419.svg">                          <p>錠也。从金登聲。都滕切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：錠中置燭，故謂之鐙。今俗别作燈，非是。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9419_7235.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鐙)</span><span class="ywyy">錠也。</span><span class="swdz">祭統曰：夫人薦豆執校，執醴授之執鐙。注曰：校，豆中央直者也。鐙，豆下跗也。執醴者以豆授夫人，執其下跗，夫人受之，執其中央直者。按跗，說文作柎，闌足也。鐙有柎，則無足曰鐙之說未可信。豆之遺制爲今俗用燈盞。徐氏兄弟遂以膏鐙解說文，誤矣。生民傳曰：木曰豆，瓦曰登。豆薦菹醢，登薦大羹。箋云：祀天用瓦豆，陶器質也。然則瓦登用於祭天。廟中之鐙，笵金爲之，故其字从金。</span><span class="ywyy">从金登聲</span><span class="swdz">都滕切。六部</span></p>                          </div>
