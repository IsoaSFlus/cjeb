                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>迟</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">遲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>慢，缓：～缓。～笨。～钝。～疑。～重（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）。～滞。</li>
	<li>晚：～到。～暮，～早。推～。延～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> late, tardy; slow; delay</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> spät ,spät</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tard,en retard,lent</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">迟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>迟</strong></p>
<p>遲 <span class="dicpy">ch&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:慢慢走)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。泛指慢 <span class="encs">[walk slowly;slow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迟，徐行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迟，缓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">废彻不迟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行道迟迟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;谷风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迟归有待。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;归妹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陵迟故也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;宥坐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三日断五匹，大人故嫌迟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迟徐(缓慢);迟滞(缓慢阻滞)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 迟钝;反应慢，不灵敏 <span class="encs">[slow;obtuse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">料事同而迟速不同。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">魏源</span><span class="smcs">《默觚下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周</span>少言重迟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;杜周传》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓性非敏速也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 迟疑;犹豫 <span class="encs">[hesitate to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寻声暗问弹者谁，琵琶声停欲语迟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 晚 <span class="encs">[late]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迟，晚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汤降不迟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;长发》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:他来迟了;迟疾(太晚，耽搁太久)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 早 <span class="encs">[early]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:迟旦(黎明，清晨);迟明(黎明，清晨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 长久 <span class="encs">[long]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昭假迟迟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;长发》</span></span></p>
