                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">肤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>肤</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">膚</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>肉体表面的皮：皮～。肌～。切～之痛。体无完～。</li>
	<li>表面的，浅薄的：～浅。～泛。</li>
	<li>大：&ldquo;薄伐猃狁，以奏～公&rdquo;（&ldquo;肤公&rdquo;即&ldquo;大功&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> skin; superficial, shallow</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> peau</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">肤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>肤</strong></p>
<p>膚 <span class="dicpy">fū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，盧省声。本义:人的皮肤)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[skin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肤受之愬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;颜渊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣不暖肤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;算地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">手如柔荑，肤如凝脂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;硕人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:肌肤(肌肉皮肤);肤革(皮肤的表皮和真皮;外面，表面);肤不生毛(皮肤上长满了厚茧而长不出毛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 树木、果实的表皮 <span class="encs">[bark;skin]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:肤部(表皮);肤果(可连皮吃的果实)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 某些像皮层那样的东西 <span class="encs">[sth.resembling skin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纸光洁如玉，肤如卵膜。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陈师道</span><span class="smcs">《后山谈丛》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:草皮;纸张</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古代用于祭祀或供食用的肉类 <span class="encs">[sacrificial meat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">麋肤，鱼醢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;肤，切肉也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代长度单位，一指为寸，四指为肤 <span class="encs">[measure unit of length]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:肤寸(古长度单位。一指宽为寸，四指宽为肤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 较短的距离 <span class="encs">[short distance]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:肤寸之地无得</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 外表 <span class="encs">[appearance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而短小精悍者，游侠之徒也。人何足以知之?此皆其肤也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《王定国真赞》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通&ldquo;簠&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">fǔ</span>)。古祭祀燕享，以盛稻粱的器皿 <span class="encs">[a square grain receptacle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剥床以肤，凶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;剥卦》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>肤</strong></p>
<p>膚 <span class="dicpy">fū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 浅薄 <span class="encs">[superficial]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所谓末学肤受，贵耳而贱目者也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:肤见(浅陋的见解);肤学(浅学，所学浅陋);肤俗(肤浅庸俗);肤庸(浅薄平庸);肤语(肤辞，肤浅空泛的言语)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[big]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薄伐猃狁，以奏肤公。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;六月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:肤功(大功)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/81DA.svg">                          <p>皮也。从肉盧聲。膚，籒文臚。力居切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/81DA_5340.svg" /><span class="swbf">(臚)</span><span class="ywyy">皮也。</span><span class="swdz">今字皮膚從籒文作膚，膚行而臚廢矣。晉語：聽臚言於市。史、漢臚句傳。蘇林曰：上傳語吿下爲臚。此皆讀爲敷奏以言之敷也。史記:臚於郊祀。漢書：大夫臚岱。韋昭辨釋名：鴻，大也。臚，陳序也。謂大以禮陳序賔客。此皆讀爲廷實旅百之旅也。劉熙釋名：鴻臚，腹前肥者曰臚。以京師爲心體。王矦外國爲腹腴。以養之也，此讀爲夏右腴之腴。皆假借也，其本義則皮膚也。</span><span class="ywyy">从肉。盧聲。</span><span class="swdz">甫無切。五部。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/81DA_5341.svg" /><span class="swbf">(膚)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文臚。</span><span class="swdz">經籍通用此字。禮運曰：膚革充盈。引伸爲狼跋文王之膚美，爲六月之膚大，爲論語之膚受。</span></p>                          </div>
