						<p>● <strong>捶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄨㄟˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>敲打：～衣。～鼓。～打。～拓。～挞。～楚（古代的杖刑）。～胸顿足。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">捶</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>捶</strong></p>
<p>搥 <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，垂声。本义:棒打)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[beat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">捶，以杖击也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">捶笞膑脚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span>。注:&ldquo;捶笞皆杖击也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:捶鼓;捶衣裳;捶楚(用杖或板拷打)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 拳打 <span class="encs">[hit with fist]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:捶背;捶殴(殴打);捶胸跌脚(表示极为悲伤或悲愤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 舂;捣 <span class="encs">[pestle]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:捶骨沥髓(同&ldquo;敲骨吸髓&rdquo;。喻残酷搜刮民财)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 同&ldquo;锤&rdquo;。锻，锻炼 <span class="encs">[forge]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:捶钩(锻打带钩);捶句(锤炼文句)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>捶</strong> <span class="dicpy">chu&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chu&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 马杖。通&ldquo;箠&rdquo; <span class="encs">[whip]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">檄以马捶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;至乐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一尺之捶，日取其半，万世不竭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">捶马衔走。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;定公八年》</span></span><span class="diczx2">何</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">执捶拊以鞭笞天下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;秦始皇本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:捶策(扬鞭击马)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 棍棒 <span class="encs">[stick]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:捶表(即邮表。指立于边界邮舍旁的木制标志)</span></p>
