month]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">适冬(阴历十一月)之望日(十五日)前后，窗外疏梅筛月影。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span
