                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">震</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>震</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>疾雷（霹雳）使物体振动：&ldquo;～夷伯之庙&rdquo;。～霆。</li>
	<li>雷：&ldquo;烨烨～电。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>巨大的力等使物体剧烈颤动：～撼。～荡。～颤。～响。～动。声～遐迩。</li>
	<li>迅速或剧烈地颤动：地～。身子不由得一～。</li>
	<li>特指&ldquo;地震&rdquo;：～灾。～源。～中。～级。～情。防～。抗～。余～。</li>
	<li>〔～旦〕佛教经籍中的译名，指中国。</li>
	<li>惊恐或情绪过分激动：～惊。～怒。～骇。～慑。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> shake, quake, tremor; excite</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Betroffenheit  (S)​,Schock  (S)​,schütteln, bewegen  (V)​,Sechstes der Acht Trigramme im Bagua des I Ging, "Erregung&quot</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ébranler,secouer,trembler,vibrer,être bouleversé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">震</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>震</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="diczx1">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从雨，辰声。雷、雨常常并作，故从雨。本义:雷，疾雷)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[thunders]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">震，劈历振物者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三月癸酉，大雨震电。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏:&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">何休</span>云:震，雷也。电，霆也。&rdquo;。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震震(雷、鼓、车、马等所发出的巨响);震厉(雷声);震霆(轰雷，响雷);震雷(响雷);震响(震雷似的响声)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 八卦之一。雷之象 <span class="encs">[one of the Eight Diagrams]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万物出乎震。震，东方也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震宫(东方);震方(震位，震维。东方);震区(东方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 与天子有关 <span class="encs">[imperial]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:震位(指东宫，即&ldquo;太子之宫&rdquo;);震宫(太子所住的宫殿);震业(帝王的事业);震储(皇储，太子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 威势;威严 <span class="encs">[power and influence;prestige;dignity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫兵戢而时动，动则威，观则玩，玩则无震。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畏君之震。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公二年》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震灼(威势极盛);震肃(因慑于威势而肃然)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>震</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="diczx1">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 震动，物体自身动荡或使物体动荡 <span class="encs">[shake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后数日驿至，果地震</span><span class="diczx2">陇西</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雷奔电泄，震天动地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水经注&middot;河水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以类天之震曜杀戮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">震惊朕师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余威震于殊俗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">内地为之震。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震掉(颤动);震域(地震波及的范围);震裂(物体因受震动而裂开);震主之威(指人威势极为盛大，使君王畏忌不安)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 震惊;惊惧;使惊惧 <span class="encs">[fear;dread;shock]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿承闲而自察兮，心震悼而不敢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;抽思》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">簿者数内大使以震其君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;八奸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">权</span>以示群下，莫不响震失色。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震汗(因惊惧而颤抖出汗);震怖(惊恐);震悸(惊怖恐惧);震慑(震惧。形容极害怕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 心动;激动 <span class="encs">[(of heart) beat;excite]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若听乐而震，观美而眩，患莫甚焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语下》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震怜(动情怜恤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 振兴;振奋 <span class="encs">[develop vigorously;rouse oneself]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫不震叠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;时迈》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">滞久而不震，生乃不殖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震奋(振作奋发);震发(奋发，奋起);震激(振作激发)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 怒 <span class="encs">[anger]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">震于廷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;释》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">范望</span>注:&ldquo;震，怒也。&rdquo;。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震恚(大怒，盛怒);震扑(怒而攻打);震愤(大怒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 地震 <span class="encs">[have an earthquake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">八月丁酉，</span><span class="diczx2">南宫极</span>震。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">震之所在。(地震所在的方位。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:震中;震波;震级</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中" target="_blank">戌集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集中&amp;bs=雨" target="_blank">雨</a>】
                              震·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9707.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29189.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩆉</span>【唐韻】章刃切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】之刃切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音振。【說文】劈歷振物者。从雨辰聲。【註】徐鉉曰：今俗別作霹靂，非是。【易&middot;說卦】震爲雷。【詩&middot;小雅】㷸㷸震電。【傳】震，雷也。【春秋&middot;僖十五年】震夷伯之廟。【疏】雷之甚者爲震。【釋名】震，戰也。所擊輒破，若攻戰也。</p>
<p>又卦名。【易&middot;說卦】萬物出乎震。震，東方也。</p>
<p>又【易&middot;說卦】震，動也。【書&middot;舜典】震驚朕師。【盤庚】爾謂朕，曷震動萬民以遷。【詩&middot;周頌】薄言震之。莫不震<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24D01.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤴁</span>。【春秋&middot;文九年】地震。【疏】公羊傳曰：震者何，動地也。【周語】伯陽父曰陽伏而不能出，隂迫而不能<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/241CF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤇏</span>，於是有地震。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】震，懼也。【易&middot;震卦】洊雷震，君子以恐懼修省。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】威也。【易&middot;未濟】震用伐鬼方。【詩&middot;大雅】王奮厥武，如震如怒。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】起也。【易&middot;雜卦傳】震，起也。</p>
<p>又【公羊傳&middot;僖九年】葵丘之會，桓公震而驚之，叛者九國。震之者何，猶曰振振然。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】娠，震動也。【註】娠，猶震也。【疏】大雅生民云：載震載夙。昭元年左傳曰：邑姜方震大叔。哀元年左傳曰：后緡方震。皆謂有身爲震，故云娠猶震也。</p>
<p>又【書&middot;禹貢】震澤底定。【傳】震澤，吳南太湖名。</p>
<p>又【集韻】升人切，音申。與娠同。女姙身動也。【左傳&middot;震動釋文】震又音申，懷姙也。【集韻】通作㑗。</p>
<p>又【集韻】一曰官婢女隷謂之娠。</p>
<p>又【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>之人切，音眞。怒也。【班固&middot;東都賦】赫然發憤，應者雲興。霆擊昆陽，憑怒雷震。【前漢&middot;敘傳】票騎冠軍，猋勇紛紜。長驅六舉，電擊雷震。【註】師古曰：震音之人反。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】震旦，中國也。【梁書】盤盤國稱梁主爲震旦天子。从之人切。</p>
