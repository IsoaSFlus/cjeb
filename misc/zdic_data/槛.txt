                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>槛</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">檻</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>栏杆：&ldquo;阁中帝子今何在？～外长江空自流&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>圈（<span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n </span>）兽类的栅栏：～车（①运兽用的有栏杆的车；②古代押运囚犯的车）。～阱。～圈（<span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n </span>）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>槛</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">檻</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔门～〕门下的横木。亦作&ldquo;门坎&rdquo;；亦称&ldquo;门限&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">槛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>槛</strong></p>
<p>檻 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 槛车;囚车 <span class="encs">[cart with a cage for prisoners]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">槛，栊也。从木，监声。所以囚罪人之牢也。故囚车曰槛车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">槛，牢也。今监狱字以监为之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">槛，车上施栏，以格猛兽，亦囚禁罪人之车也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释车》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中尚书宦官槛塞大异。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;谷永传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃槛车与王诣</span><span class="diczx2">长安</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;陈馀传》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:「槛车者，车而为槛形，谓以板四周之，无所通也。」</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:槛车(四周设有栅栏的车。用以囚禁犯人或装载猛兽);槛送(用囚车押送);槛槛(车子行进时发出的声音)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 关牲畜野兽的栅栏 <span class="encs">[wooden cage;pen for animal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一曰圈也。谓所以閑兽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:槛羊(栅栏中的羊);槛緤(拴兽的绳索);槛兽(笼中之兽。喻失去自由者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 栏杆的纵木 <span class="encs">[baluster;banister]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">云</span>攀殿槛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;朱云传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 栏杆的横木 <span class="encs">[rail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐堂伏槛，临曲池些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指栏杆 <span class="encs">[balustrade]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6ABB.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】胡黯切【集韻】【韻會】戸黤切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音艦。【說文】櫳也，房室之疏也。【徐曰】軒牕，下爲櫺曰闌，以板曰軒、曰檻。【前漢&middot;朱雲傳】攀殿檻，檻折。【班固&middot;西都賦】舍櫺檻而却倚。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】檻，楯也。【楚辭&middot;招䰟】高堂邃宇，檻層軒些。【註】縱曰檻，橫曰楯。</p>
<p>又檻，車也。【前漢&middot;陳餘傳】貫高乃檻車詣長安。</p>
<p>又圈也，以檻禽獸，故曰圈檻。【淮南子&middot;主術訓】養虎豹犀象者，爲之圈檻。</p>
<p>又檻檻，車行聲。【詩&middot;王風】大車檻檻。</p>
<p>又泉正出者曰檻泉。【詩&middot;大雅】觱沸檻泉。</p>
<p>又與濫通。浴器也。【莊子&middot;則陽篇】同檻而浴。【註】一作濫。　【正韻】分上、去二音。欄義去聲，胡監切。車行聲及檻泉義作上聲，讀胡覽切。</p>
<p>又叶形甸切，音現。【歐陽脩&middot;新霜詩】芭蕉芰荷不足數，狼籍徒能汙池檻。時行收斂歲將窮，冰雪嚴凝從此漸。</p>
