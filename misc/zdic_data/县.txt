                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>县</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">縣</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>行政区划单位，旧时属于州、府、道，现由直辖市、地级市、自治州等领导。</li>
	<li>姓</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>县</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">縣</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/悬">悬</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">县</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>县</strong></p>
<p>縣 <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从系持倒首。象悬挂的样子。( <span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )本义:悬挂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 县假借为&ldquo;寰&rdquo;，为州县的县 <span class="encs">[county]</span>。行政区划单位。周代县大于郡，秦以后县属于郡。今为一级行政区划，隶属于地区、自治州、直辖市之下</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂缚诣县。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邯郸淳</span><span class="smcs">《笑林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南番二县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">龙泉</span>县。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开道置县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:县尹(一县的长官);县学(旧制设在县里的学校，以培养人才，教育百姓，称县学);县委(中国共产党县一级委员会的简称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 官府 <span class="encs">[local authorities]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:县君(宋代官员妻子的一种封号);县治(县衙;县政府)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代天子所治之地，在京都周围千里之内，即王畿 <span class="encs">[feud]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:县内(儒家之说，以四海之内为九州，其一为畿内，天子所治);县主(皇族女子的封号);县公主(汉代皇女的封号)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

<p>◎ <strong>县</strong></p>
<p>縣 <span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 系挂 <span class="encs">[hang]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">縣，系也。从系，持县会意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">骊姬</span>请使申，处 <span class="diczx2">曲沃</span>以速縣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;缢也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不狩不猎，胡瞻尔庭有县鹑兮?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔庭有县特兮。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔庭有县鹑兮。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:县令(高悬的命令。即&ldquo;高名&rdquo;、&ldquo;虚名&rdquo;);县车(悬挂缰绳，停放车子不用。比喻退隐不再出仕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 维系;拴系;系联 <span class="encs">[tie]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">县联房植。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">县棺而封。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:县绝(悬系之绳断绝);县联(椽子和屋檐木相接之处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 揭示;颁立 <span class="encs">[reveal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">县法以诱民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:县法(古代公布法令，皆悬在阙下，使众周知，故以称颁布法令)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 称量;衡量 <span class="encs">[weigh]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:县石(称量石头);县轻重(权衡利弊)</span></p>
