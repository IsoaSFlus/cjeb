						<p>● <strong>甾</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄞˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>有机化合物的一类，广泛存在于动植物体内，胆固醇和多种激素都属于甾类化合物。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/灾">灾</a>&rdquo;，祸难。</li>
</ol>

<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/淄">淄</a>&rdquo;，水名。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>甾</strong> <span class="dicpy">zāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同&ldquo;灾&rdquo;。灾害，灾难 <span class="encs">[calamity;disaster]</span>。如:甾民(灾民，难民);甾害(灾害)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 有机化合物的一类 <span class="encs">[steroid]</span>，广泛存在于动植物体内。胆固醇和很多种激素(如性激素)，都属于甾类化合物</p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>甾</strong> <span class="dicpy">zī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代一种盛酒浆的陶器 <span class="encs">[an ancient pottery]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">甾，东</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>名缶曰甾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 水名。后作&ldquo;淄&rdquo;。在今山东省境内 <span class="encs">[Zi River]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">嵎夷</span>既略， <span class="diczx2">淮</span>、 <span class="diczx2">甾</span>其道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
