                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瘪</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>瘪</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">癟</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">biē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biē.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄝˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～三〕方言，指城市中无正当职业而以乞讨或偷窃为生的流氓游民。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>瘪</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">癟</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">biě  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biě.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄝˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～子〕①指处境窘迫，如&ldquo;作（<span class="dicpy">zuō</span>）～～&rdquo;；②方言，秕子。</li>
	<li>不饱满，凹下：～瘦。～干。～谷。～花生。～桃干（中药名）。～螺痧（中医指霍乱一类的病）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> shrivelled up, dried up; vexed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ratatiné,contracté,dégonflé</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瘪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瘪</strong></p>
<p>癟 <span class="dicpy">biē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biē.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用同&ldquo;憋&rdquo;。闷<span class="encs">[在心里]</span> <span class="encs">[hold back]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既然要钱，为什么不说明，叫我瘪了两三个月呢?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《官场现形记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">biě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biě.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/瘪三">瘪三</a></span></div>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瘪</strong></p>
<p>癟 <span class="dicpy">biě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biě.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 皱缩的，不饱满的 <span class="encs">[shrivelled]</span>。如:干瘪;瘪壳;瘪瘦;瘪窳</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 患枯萎病的，受到枯萎病侵染的 <span class="encs">[blighted]</span>。如:瘪花生</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泄了气的&mdash;&mdash;主要用于充气轮胎 <span class="encs">[flat]</span>。如:车胎瘪了</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瘪</strong></p>
<p>癟 <span class="dicpy">biě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biě.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
