                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铫</strong></p>
<p>銚 <span class="dicpy">di&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 〈方〉∶铫子 <span class="encs">[a pot for decocting herbal medicine or heating up water]</span>。煎药或烧水用的器具，形状像比较高的壶，口大有盖，旁边有柄，用沙土或金属制成。如:沙铫儿;药铫儿</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铫</strong></p>
<p>銚 <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一种带柄有嘴的小锅 <span class="encs">[small cooking pan with handle and sprout]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:铫盏(即铫子。又叫铫铛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 大锄 <span class="encs">[farm implement]</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“铫”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/銚">銚</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=金" target="_blank">金</a>】
                              銚·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/929A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】以招切【集韻】【韻會】餘招切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音遙。【說文】溫器也。【廣韻】燒器。【正字通】今釜之小而有柄有流者亦曰銚。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25044.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥁄</span>，宋楚魏之閒，或謂之銚銳。</p>
<p>又【說文】一曰田器。</p>
<p>又姓。【後漢&middot;銚期傳】銚期，字須况，潁川郟人。</p>
<p>又國名。【前漢&middot;禮樂志】銚四會員十二人，齊四會員十九人。【註】銚，國名。音姚。</p>
<p>又【馬融&middot;長笛賦】剺櫟銚㦎，晢龍之慧也。【註】皆分別節制之貌。</p>
<p>又【集韻】千遙切，音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23080.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣂀</span>。臿也。【詩&middot;周頌&middot;庤乃錢鎛疏】世本云：垂作銚。宋仲子註云：銚，刈也。然則銚刈物之器也。【釋文】銚，七遙反。【莊子&middot;外物篇】銚鎒于是乎始修。【註】銚，七遙反。削也。能有所穿削也。</p>
<p>又他堯反。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】吐彫切【集韻】他彫切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音祧。義同。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>徒弔切，音調。燒器。【集韻】或作鑃鑵。</p>
<p>又【集韻】他弔切，音糶。義同。</p>
<p>又弋笑切，音燿。與<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26FBA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦾺</span>同。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26FBA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦾺</span>芅，草名。【爾雅&middot;釋草】長楚銚芅。【註】今羊桃也。或曰鬼桃。葉似桃，華白，子如小麥，亦似桃。○按釋文銚，音姚，與集韻音異。</p>
<p>又【集韻】田聊切，音迢。長矛也。【呂氏春秋】長銚利兵。</p>
