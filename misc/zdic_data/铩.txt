                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铩</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>铩</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鎩</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">shā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄚˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代一种长矛。</li>
	<li>摧残，伤残：～羽（羽毛掉落，喻失意受挫折）。～羽之鸟。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> moult</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufspießen, durchbohren</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> lance longue</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">铩</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铩</strong></p>
<p>鎩 <span class="dicpy">shā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 长刃矛，古代的一种兵器 <span class="encs">[long spear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不敌于鉤戟长铩也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;陈胜项藉传》</span>。注:&ldquo;铍也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鉏耰棘矜，非铦于钩戟长铩也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 铍有镡 <span class="encs">[long lance with handle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铩，铍有镡也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">五代</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐锴</span><span class="smcs">《说文系传》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>铩</strong></p>
<p>鎩 <span class="dicpy">shā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 摧残;伤害<span class="encs">[羽翅]</span> <span class="encs">[hurt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铩羽暴鳞，复见翻跃。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《拜侍郎上疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:铩羽(毛羽伤残，不能高飞。比喻人受摧残而失志);铩翼(羽毛摧败，不能高飞);铩翅(折翅);铩翮(即铩羽);铩羽暴鳞(铩羽涸鳞。毛羽伤残，鱼池水干。比喻不得志，处境极为困难)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="encs">[飞鸟]</span> 张翼 <span class="encs">[wing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铩，<span class="smcs">《字林》</span>:&ldquo;张翼也&rdquo;。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">玄应</span><span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/93A9.svg">                          <p>鈹有鐸也。从金殺聲。所拜切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/93A9_7196.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鎩)</span><span class="ywyy">鈹有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28C33.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨰳</span>也。</span><span class="swdz">鐔，劒鼻也。云鈹有鐔者，則知鈹有不爲鼻者矣，如刀裝之鈹不爲鼻者也。賈誼曰：鉏櫌棘矝，非銛於句戟長鎩。張衡曰：植鎩縣瞂，用戒不虞。曰長曰植，則鎩有柄，有柄故不爲鼻。薜綜解一曰鋌，似㒳刃刀。鋌謂其上出之鋒也。淮南書：飛鳥鎩羽。許注曰：鎩，殘也。左思賦亦曰鳥鎩翮。此等鎩字乃引申之義。鎩可殘羽，故凡見殘者曰鎩。公羊作摋：宋萬臂摋仇牧，碎其首。何云：側手擊曰摋。</span><span class="ywyy">从金殺聲。</span><span class="swdz">所拜反。十五部。廣韵又所八切。</span></p>                          </div>
