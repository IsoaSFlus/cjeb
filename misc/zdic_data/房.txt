                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>房</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fáng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fáng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄤˊ</span></p><ol><li>住人或放东西的建筑物：～屋。～产。～舍。～租。库～。楼～。书～。</li><li>结构和作用类似房子的东西：蜂～。莲～。心～。</li><li>量词：两～儿媳。</li><li>家族的一支：大～。长（<span class="dicpy">zhǎng </span>）～。</li><li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">房</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>房</strong> <span class="dicpy">fáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从户，方声。上古边室皆用单扇门(即<span class="yhcs">“户”</span>)，庙门大门才用双扇门，故<span class="yhcs">“房”</span>从<span class="yhcs">“户”</span>。本义:正室左右的住室)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代堂中间叫正室，两旁的叫房 <span class="encs">[side room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">房，室在傍者也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">房，室旁夹室也。——<span class="smcs">《六书故》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“凡堂之内，中为正室，左右为房，所谓东房、西房也”</span>。 <span class="diczx2">桂馥</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“古者宫室之制，前堂后室。前堂之两头有夹室，后室之两旁有东西房。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">记宰夫宴出自东房。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡食礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在东房。——<span class="smcs">《书·顾命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">郤子登，妇人笑于房。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十七年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:厢房(正房前面两旁的房屋);房植(房室四角的柱);房奥(房室之深处)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指房屋 <span class="encs">[building;house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无门无房，四达之皇皇也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·知北游》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:库房(储存财物的房屋);住房;私房;房宇(屋宇，房子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 房间 <span class="encs">[room]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">店主</span><span class="diczx2">王公</span>迎接了，慌忙指派房头，堆放行旅。——<span class="smcs">《古今说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:房卧(卧房;嫁妆);房头(房间);房户(房间门窗)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 官署及办公处所 <span class="encs">[government organ]</span>。如:房科(旧时官衙里的下级办事人员);房省(宫室，官署)。又指明清乡、会试分房阅卷处所。亦指分房阅卷的同考官。如:房书(八股文的选集，所选的是进士的文章);房考(亦称<span class="yhcs">“房官”</span>。明清时乡、会试时分房阅卷的考官);房差(房考官的差使)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 祠堂;庙宇 <span class="encs">[temple]</span>。如:房祀(祠堂，庙宇);房庙(祠堂、庙宇)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 结构和作用像房子的东西 <span class="encs">[house-like structure]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蜂房不容鹄卵。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·泛论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">露冷莲房坠粉红。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《秋兴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:心房;蛎房</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 房族及其宗支的单位 <span class="encs">[branch of a
