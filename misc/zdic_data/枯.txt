                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枯</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>枯</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>失去水分，水全没有了：干～。～萎。～槁。～荣。～鱼衔索（串在绳索上的干鱼，形容存日不多）。</li>
	<li>没趣味，无生趣：～燥。～肠（喻枯涩贫乏的思路）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> dried out, withered, decayed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ausgedorrt</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> flétri,fané,épuisè,à sec</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枯</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>枯</strong> <span class="dicpy">kū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，古声。本义:枯槁，草木干枯)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wither]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枯，槁木也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草木蚤枯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">离离原上草，一岁一枯荣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《赋得古草原送别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草枯鹰眼疾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《观猎》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载燥荻枯柴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">菀枯顷刻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="encs">[英]</span></span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">八月禾未枯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枯荣(草木的繁荣与凋零。后指盛衰;也指干涸，干竭);枯凋(枯槁凋零);枯索(草木枯萎全无生机);枯梧(枯萎的梧桐;泛指枯木。喻卑小的职位);枯鱼(干鱼;鱼干;比喻处于困境的人);枯腊(枯干的肉，指尸体)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 弃市暴死 <span class="encs">[in ancient time criminals were executed in public squares and then abandoned there]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">捶笞膑脚，斩断枯磔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;正论》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杨倞</span>注:&ldquo;枯，弃市暴尸也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枯磔(古代的两种重刑。枯，指弃市暴死;磔，指车裂)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>枯</strong> <span class="dicpy">kū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 干涸 <span class="encs">[dried up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江河之水，驰涌滑漏，席地长远，无枯竭之流，本源盛矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;效力》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">渊生珠而崖不枯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山林川泽之数。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;司书》</span>。注:&ldquo;山林川泽童枯则不税。&rdquo;。疏:&ldquo;山林不茂为童、川泽无水为枯。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枯池(干涸的池塘);枯渴(干渴);枯鱼之肆(比喻身在穷途)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 空 <span class="encs">[empty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">过其枯城，或蘖青青。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄经》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">范望</span>注:&ldquo;枯，虚也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枯守(空守);枯城(荒废的旧城);枯煎(空受熬煎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 干 <span class="encs">[dry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曾不如早索我于枯鱼之肆。<span class="smcs">《外子&middot;外物》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枯窘(枯竭贫乏);枯泪(泪已哭尽);枯灭(消灭尽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 干瘦;憔悴 <span class="encs">[haggard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开若枯骸，心若死灰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;知北游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颜色憔悴，形容枯槁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枯悴(憔悴);枯黧(憔悴黧黑);枯黯(憔悴而灰暗);枯羸(憔悴羸弱)枯毁(瘦弱，毁损)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 乏味，无趣 <span class="encs">[interestless]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:枯住(枯燥乏味地居住);枯禅(佛教徒指静坐参禅。又指闲坐，闲聊)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>枯</strong> <span class="dicpy">kū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 枯朽的树木 <span class="encs">[withered tree]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人皆集于苑，己独集于枯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 肉已烂尽的骸骨 <span class="encs">[skeleton]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">泽及骴骨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高诱</span>注:&ldquo;骨有肉曰骴，无曰枯。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 油科作物果籽榨油后剩余的渣滓 <span class="encs">[dregs]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茶枯;菜枯</span></p>
