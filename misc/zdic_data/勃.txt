                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">勃</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>勃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&oacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&oacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄛˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>突然，忽然：～然（①突然，如&ldquo;～～大怒&rdquo;；②兴起的样子，如&ldquo;～～作色&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>变色的样子：～腾腾（怒气上冲的样子）。</li>
	<li>旺盛，兴起：～起。～发。～蓬。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>勃</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/悖">悖</a>&rdquo;，违背事理，惑乱糊涂。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> suddenly, sudden, quick</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> subitement,brusquement,soudainement,abondant,exubérant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">勃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>勃</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>(形声。从力，孛( <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:排，推动) 同本义 <span class="encs">[push]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勃，排也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勃，展也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span>。今苏俗语以力旋转物曰勃。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:勃郁(风回旋的样子;郁结);勃屑(即勃窣)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>勃</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 兴起;旺盛 <span class="encs">[vigorous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气喷勃以布覆兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马融</span><span class="smcs">《长笛赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勃勃，盛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勃然平世之俗起焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;非十二子》</span>。注:&ldquo;兴起貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蓬勃(繁荣;旺盛);勃腾腾(烟气上升的样子;怒气上升的样子);勃勃(烟气上升的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;悖&rdquo;。乖戾 <span class="encs">[stubborn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彻志之勃，解心之谬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;庚桑楚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利在故新相反，前后相勃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;定法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:勃乱(行止违背常理);勃逆(叛逆);勃谿(相争吵。今婆媳不和叫妇姑勃谿)</span></p>
