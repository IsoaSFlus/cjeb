                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>拙</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄛˉ</span></p><ol><li>笨，不灵巧：笨～。弄巧成～。～劣。</li><li>谦辞，称自己的：～作。～见。～笔。～著。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> stupid, clumsy, crude; convention</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> maladroit,gauche</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">拙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拙</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhuō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuō.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，出声。本义:笨拙，不灵活)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[clumsy;awkward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拙，不巧也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拙，钝也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不利于人谓之拙。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·贵义》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予亦拙谋。——<span class="smcs">《书·盘庚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">理弱而媒拙兮。——<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大直若屈，大巧若拙，大辩若讷。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>四十五章</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每动作而受嗤，言发口而违理者，拙人也。——<span class="smcs">《抱朴子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拙者不足。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">理弱而媒兮，恐导言之不固。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>家青史上，计拙是和亲。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">戎昱</span><span class="smcs">《咏史》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">姐妹们虽拙，大家一处作伴，也可解些烦闷。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:性拙(生性笨拙);拙行(外行;不在行);拙诚(笨拙而真诚);拙鸠(相传鸠鸟性笨拙，不善营巢，常占据他鸟的巢而栖息);拙薄(拙劣浅薄);拙守(安于愚拙，不取巧)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 我的 <span class="encs">[my]</span>。自谦之辞</p><p><span class="diczx1">拙妻好乘鸾，娇女爱飞鹤。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《题嵩山逸人元丹邱山居》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">这妇人便插口道:<span class="yhcs">“这个叔叔，便是拙夫新认识的兄弟。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:拙子(对人称自己的儿子);拙分(谦称自己的名分、职分);拙作(称自己作品的谦词);拙室(拙妇。称自己妻子的谦词);拙恶(谦称自己的作品)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 粗劣 <span class="encs">[coarse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黄金即为侈，白石又太拙。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆龟蒙</span><span class="smcs">《酒樽》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:拙易(粗陋肤浅);拙野(粗糙);拙率(粗率);拙浅(粗浅，浅陋);拙恶(粗劣)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 粗俗 <span class="encs">[vulgar]</span>。如:拙夫(粗俗的男人，或指粗俗的丈夫);拙俗(粗俗)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 短浅 <span class="encs">[narrow and shallow]</span>。如:拙智(短见);拙目(眼光短浅的人);拙眼(眼光短浅的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 倒霉 <span class="encs">[evil]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缅思桃源内，益叹身世拙。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《北征》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中了一个甚么举人，就得了这个拙病。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:运拙(命运不济;命该倒霉)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 质朴无华 <span class="encs">[simple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巧诈不如拙诚。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:拙木(质朴);拙句(自然质朴的诗句)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>拙</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhuō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuō.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 不善于 <span class="encs">[not good at]</span></p><p><span
