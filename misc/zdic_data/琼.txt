                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">琼</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>琼</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">瓊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄩㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>美玉：～玉。～莹。</li>
	<li>喻美好的：～瑶。～室。～姿。～筵。～章（美好的诗文）。～葩。～林宴（泛指皇帝宴请新科进士的宴会）。～枝玉叶。玉液～浆（美酒）。</li>
	<li>中国海南省的别称：～崖。～州。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> jade; rare, precious; elegant; (Cant.) to coagulate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schön</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> beau jade,exquis,abrév. pour la province du Hainan</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">琼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>琼</strong></p>
<p>瓊 <span class="dicpy">qi&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。左形，右声。本义:赤色玉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。泛指美玉 <span class="encs">[red jade;fine jade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">投我以木瓜，报之以琼琚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;木瓜》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">精琼靡与秋菊兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚子</span>玉自为琼弁玉缨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十八年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:琼斝(一种玉质酒器);琼窗(玉砌的窗户。比喻窗户异常华美);琼琚(美好的佩玉);琼珠(美玉所制的珍珠);琼音(玉声。比喻清脆美好的声音)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 博具，相当于后来的骰子 <span class="encs">[dice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行五道而投琼曰搏，不投琼曰塞。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">成玄英</span><span class="smcs">《庄子注疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻雪 <span class="encs">[snow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雪地里踏着碎琼乱玉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:琼玉(比喻霜雪);琼田(形容晶莹如玉的江湖田野);琼妃(喻雪花);琼玖(喻冰雪);琼花(喻雪花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 府名。琼州 <span class="encs">[Qiong prefecture]</span>。汉置珠崖、儋耳郡;三国吴复置珠崖郡;晋废;唐贞观五年，始置琼州;宋迁治所于今海南省琼山县南;明改置琼州府;清仍之;民国废;今设海南省</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>琼</strong></p>
<p>瓊 <span class="dicpy">qi&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p> 喻事物的美好 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我欲乘风归去，又恐琼楼玉宇，高处不胜寒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《水调头歌》</span>词</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">华酌既陈，有琼浆些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:琼酥金脍(指极名贵的食品)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“琼”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/瓊">瓊</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上" target="_blank">午集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上&amp;bs=玉" target="_blank">玉</a>】
                              瓊·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：15</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/74CA.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【正韻】渠營切【集韻】葵營切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音煢。【說文】赤玉也。【詩&middot;衞風】投我以木瓜，報之以瓊琚。【傳】瓊，玉之美者。【韻會】錢氏曰：詩言玉以瓊者多矣。瓊華，瓊英，瓊瑩，瓊瑤，瓊琚，瓊玖，皆謂玉色之美爲瓊，非玉之名也。許叔重云：瓊，赤玉也。然木瓜所謂瓊玖，玖乃黑玉。亦非赤也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】積石爲樹，名曰瓊枝，其高一百二十仞，大三十圍，以琅玕爲之寶。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】咀噍芝英兮嘰瓊華。【張揖註】瓊樹生崑崙西流沙濵，大三百圍，高萬仞，華蘂也，食之長生。</p>
<p>又人名。【古今注】魏文帝宮人絕愛者，有莫瓊樹，薛夜來，田尚衣，段巧笑，四人日夕在側。</p>
<p>又州名。漢朱厓郡。唐析置瓊州。</p>
<p>又【集韻】旬宣切，音旋。同琁。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶渠陽切，音强。【楚辭&middot;招魂】砥室翠翹，挂曲瓊些。翡翠珠被，爛齊光些。</p>

						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/74CA.svg">                          <p>赤玉也。从玉敻聲。 <img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/74CA_1.svg" />璚，瓊或从矞。 <img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/74CA_2.svg" />瓗，瓊或从巂。 <img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/74CA_3.svg" />琁，瓊或从旋省。渠營切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今與璿同。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74CA_4011.svg" /><span class="swbf">(瓊)</span><span class="ywyy">亦玉也。</span><span class="swdz">亦各本作赤，非。説文時有言亦者，如李賢所引診亦視也，鳥部鸞亦神靈之精也之類。此上下文皆云玉也，則瓊亦當爲玉名。倘是赤玉，當廁璊、瑕二篆閒矣。離騷曰：折瓊枝以爲羞。廣雅玉類首瓊支。此瓊爲玉名之證也。唐人陸德明、張守節皆引作赤玉，則其誤已久。詩瓊琚、瓊瑶、瓊華、瓊瑩、瓊英、瓊瑰，毛傳云：瓊，玉之美者也。葢瓊支爲玉之最美者，故廣雅言：玉，首瓊支。因而引伸凡玉石之美皆謂之瓊。應劭曰：瓊，玉之華也。是其理也。</span><span class="ywyy">从王夐聲。</span><span class="swdz">渠營切。古音在十四部。招䰟與姦安軒山連寒湲蘭筵韻。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74CA_4012.svg" /><span class="swbf">(璚)</span><span class="ywyy">瓊或从矞。</span><span class="swdz">巂聲也。矞爲夐之入聲，角部觼或作鐍，此十四部與十五部合音之理。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74CA_4013.svg" /><span class="swbf">(瓗)</span><span class="ywyy">瓊或从瓗。</span><span class="swdz">巂聲也。此十四部與十六部合音之理。虫部蠵亦作蠵。</span></p>                          </div>
