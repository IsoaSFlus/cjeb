                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>烂</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">爛</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>因过熟而变得松软：～糊。煮～。</li>
	<li>程度极深：台词背得～熟。</li>
	<li>东西腐坏：腐～。</li>
	<li>灼伤：焦头～额。</li>
	<li>崩溃，败坏：敌人一天天～下去。</li>
	<li>破碎：破～。～纸。</li>
	<li>头绪杂乱：～摊子。</li>
	<li>明，有光彩：～银枪。～漫。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> rotten, spoiled, decayed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> scheiße,  beschissen, mies, ätzend (umg.)​  (Adj)​,sanft, sacht  (Adj)​,verfault  (Adj, Ess)​,zerkocht  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> attendri,amolli,pourri,usé,en morceau,bien cuit</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">烂</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>烂</strong></p>
<p>爛、孏 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，阑声。本义:煮烂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[thoroughly cooked;tender;soft]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孏，孰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作爛。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熟而不烂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;本味》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三日三夕不烂。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此儿头不烂。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是必烂也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三首俱烂。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:烂饭(加水较多而煮成的又软又烂的饭);烂煮(煮至熟烂);烂蒸(蒸至烂熟);烂椹(熟透的桑椹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 火烧伤 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焦头烂额为上客。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;霍光传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 头绪乱 <span class="encs">[messy;be all in a mess]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:烂子(乱子);烂事(方言。犹言麻烦事或办糟了的事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 零碎，断片 <span class="encs">[scraps]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:烂纸片;衣服穿烂了</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>烂</strong></p>
<p>爛 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 腐烂，腐败 <span class="encs">[become putrid;fester;rot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烂灰生蝇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山》</span>。注:&ldquo;腐也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿凭三寸不烂之舌，往江东说此人来降。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:一场大雨使麦子烂了;烂黄齑(烂腌菜，比喻爱情不专的人);烂败(腐烂);烂损(腐烂败坏);烂碎(腐烂破碎);烂溃(溃烂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 明亮、光明;色彩绚丽<span class="encs">[bright;radiant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明星有烂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;女曰鸡鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烂昭昭兮未央。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;云中君》</span>。注:&ldquo;光貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
