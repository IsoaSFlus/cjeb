                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骑</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>骑</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">騎</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>跨坐在牲畜或其他东西上：～马。～射。～兵。～者善堕（经常骑马的常会掉下马来；喻擅长某事物的人，反而容易大意，招致失误）。</li>
	<li>兼跨两边：～缝盖章。</li>
	<li>骑的马或乘坐的其他动物（旧读<span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span>）：坐～。</li>
	<li>骑兵，亦泛指骑马的人（旧读<span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span>）：轻～。铁～。车～。</li>
	<li>一人一马的合称（旧读<span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span>）：千～。千乘万～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ride horseback; mount; cavalry</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> (Fahrrad, Motorrad)​ fahren  (V)​,reiten  (V)​,Qi  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> chevaucher,monter à califourchon,monter à (vélo,...)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>骑</strong></p>
<p>騎 <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，奇声。本义:跨马)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ride]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骑，跨马也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">步骑罗些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脱身独骑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其子好骑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;人间训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骑大马。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骑马挟矢。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骑射(骑马射箭);骑寇(骑马入侵的贼寇，也是对北方游牧民族的鄙称);骑猎(骑马打猎);骑战(骑马打仗)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 跨于物上或两边叫骑，跨坐 <span class="encs">[stride]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不骑衡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;袁盎传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:骑两头马(比喻两头观望，看风向);骑鲸(乘鲸;借指死亡或隐遁);骑驴觅驴(是说忘记自己已有又向外追求);骑鹤上扬州(比喻妄想、奢望)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 跨越 <span class="encs">[surmount]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爽气收回骑月雨，快风散尽满天云。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《村社祷晴有应》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>骑</strong></p>
<p>騎 <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 马 <span class="encs">[horse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">前有车骑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不闻爷娘唤女声，但闻</span><span class="diczx2">燕山</span>胡骑鸣啾啾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上" target="_blank">亥集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=亥集上&amp;bs=馬" target="_blank">馬</a>】
                              騎·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=18" target="_blank">18</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9A0E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】渠羈切【正韻】渠宜切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音奇。【說文】跨馬也。【䆁名】騎，支也，兩脚支別也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>奇寄切，音芰。義同。</p>
<p>又【增韻】馬軍曰騎。【禮&middot;曲禮】前有車騎。【疏】古人不騎馬，故經典無言騎。今言騎，是周末時禮。【能攺齋漫錄】左傳昭二十五年，左師展將以公乗馬而歸。劉炫謂欲與公單騎而歸，此騎馬之漸也。【正字通】古者服牛乗馬，馬以駕車，不言單騎。至六國時，始有單騎，蘇秦所謂車千乗，騎萬匹是也。</p>
<p>又票騎，官名。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】以霍去病爲票騎將軍。【註】位三司，品秩同大將軍。</p>
<p>又旄頭騎。【漢官儀】舊選羽林爲旄頭，被髮先驅。或云：旄頭騎自秦始。</p>
<p>又飛騎。【正字通】唐貞觀中擇膂力驍捷善射者，謂之飛騎。衣五色袍，乗六閑馬，每上出遊幸，從駕行。</p>
<p>又姓。【戰國策】燕將騎劫。</p>
