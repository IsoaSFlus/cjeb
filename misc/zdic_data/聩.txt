                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>聩</strong></p>
<p>聵 <span class="dicpy">ku&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 先天性耳聋。后泛指耳聋 <span class="encs">[deaf]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聩，生而聋也。从耳，贵声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">蕲州</span><span class="diczx2">庞</span>君 <span class="diczx2">安常</span>善医则聩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《东坡志林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">聋聩不可使听。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聩眊(耳聋眼花。引申为昏聩)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>聩</strong></p>
<p>聵 <span class="dicpy">ku&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 昏聩，不明事理 <span class="encs">[unknown]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">近愚则聩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《耳箴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开聋启聩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孔尚任</span><span class="smcs">《桃花扇》</span></p>
