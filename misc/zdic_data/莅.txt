						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>莅</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 到：～止。～任。～临。～场。～会。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> attend, be present; arrive at</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> bedienen, erledigen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> gouverner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">莅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>莅</strong></p><p>涖、蒞 <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，位声。从立，隶声。本义:走到近处察看)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[approach and watch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涖，视也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宿眂涤濯，莅玉鬯。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 治理;统治;管理 <span class="encs">[administer; rule; manage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">刁</span>莅事三年。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">楚庄王</span>莅政三年。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莅中国而抚四夷也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:莅官(官员到任);莅会(到会);莅政(莅正。掌管政事);莅民(管理百姓)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 来;到 <span class="encs">[come to;arrive; at present]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">方叔</span>莅止，其车三千。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采芑》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今年蒞</span><span class="diczx2">淮海</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">王禹偁</span><span class="smcs">《扬州寒食赠屯田张员外》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故君子不得已而临莅天下。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·在宥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">矜庄以莅之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·非相》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以道莅天下，其鬼不神。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾子将莅之。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士冠礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:莅止(来到);莅临(亲自到达。多指为政者或上级官吏的到来)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>莅</strong> <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈象〉</p><p>形容水声、风声的象声词。如:莅莅(形容流水声);莅飒(树木摆动的声音。形容行动迅速)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/莅任">莅任</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/莅事者">莅事者</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
