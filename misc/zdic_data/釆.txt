<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>釆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ &ldquo;<span class="diczx3">辨</span>&rdquo;的古字。像兽爪分别之形，义为辨别。</p>
