                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>宸</strong> <span class="dicpy">chén</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chén.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从宀( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，辰声。从<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>，表示与房屋有关。本义:屋檐)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[eave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宸，屋宇也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而为敝邑宸宇。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“屋霤。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">消雰埃于中宸。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“天地之交宇也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:宸宇(屋檐。比喻庇荫)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又深又大的房屋 <span class="encs">[great mansion]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宸，</span><span class="diczx2">贾逵</span>曰:室之奥者。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 帝王的住处 <span class="encs">[imperial palace]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宸扉既辟。——</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《九成宫颂》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:宸扉(宫门)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 帝王、王位的代称 <span class="encs">[emperor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">后有一行小字:<span class="yhcs">“某年某月日，书赐荣国公贾源”</span>，又有<span class="yhcs">“万机宸翰之宝”</span>。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span>。注:翰，墨迹，书法。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:宸游(旧称皇帝出游);宸极(北极星，喻帝王)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5BB8.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】植鄰切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】丞眞切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音辰。【說文】屋宇也。【賈逵曰】室之奧者。後人稱帝居曰宸。【增韻】帝居北宸宮，故从宀从辰。亦曰楓宸。帝居高廣，惟楓修大可構也。【越語】君若不忘周室，而爲敝邑宸宇，亦寡人之願也。　【正字通】本作宸，六書故又作桭。引揚雄賦柍桭註，服虔曰：柍，中央也。桭，屋梠也。◎按宸取北辰義，今人言宸極，與汎言屋梠別，宸桭合爲一，非。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5BB8_1472.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(宸)</span><span class="ywyy">屋宇也。</span><span class="swdz">屋者、以宫室上覆言之。宸謂屋邊。故古書言柍桭者、卽棟宇也。甘泉賦。日月纔經於柍桭。伏虔曰。柍、中央也。桭、屋梠也。魏都賦。旅楹閑列。暉鑒柍桭。張載曰。柍、中央也。桭、屋宇檼也。是知柍桭卽上棟下宇之謂。柍卽央字。桭卽宸字。西京賦。消氛埃於中宸。集重陽之淸澂。中宸卽柍桭。韋昭注國語云。宸、屋<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29178.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩅸</span>也。宇、邊也。若玉篇引賈逵云宸室之奥者、當亦是國語注。而其說異矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从宀。辰聲。</span><span class="swdz">植鄰切。十三部。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
