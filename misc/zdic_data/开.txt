                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>开</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">開</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kāi.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄞˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>启，张，把关闭的东西打开：～启。～化。～诚布公。</li>	<li>分割：对～。三十二～本。</li>	<li>通，使通：～导。～窍。</li>	<li>使显露出来：～采（挖掘矿物）。～发。</li>	<li>扩大、发展：～扩。～拓。</li>	<li>发动或操纵：～动。～车。</li>	<li>起始：～始。～宗明义。</li>	<li>设置、建立：～创。～国。～设。</li>	<li>列举，写出：～单子。～发票。</li>	<li>支付：～销。～支。</li>	<li>沸腾，滚：～水。</li>	<li>举行：～运动会。</li>	<li>放在动词后面，表示效果：躲～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">开</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>开</strong></p>
<p>開 <span class="dicpy">kāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，两边是两扇门，中间一横是门闩，下面是一双手，表示两手打开门闩之意。本义:开门)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[open the door]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">開，张也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天门开阖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>十章</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善闭，无关楗而不可开。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开物成务。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开我东阁门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂开门纳众。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开轩面场圃。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孟浩然</span><span class="smcs">《过故人庄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">午后又开西门归德门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:开城(打开城门);开门钱(男家迎亲的轿子到女家，女家开门前索取的钱财)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 打开，开启 <span class="encs">[open]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">微子</span>开封于 <span class="diczx2">宋</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;议兵》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公辨其声，而目不可开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晶晶然如镜之新开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">明星荧荧，开妆镜也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:开卷有益;旗开得胜;顿开茅塞;开封(拆开书函);开窗放入大江来(只要一打开窗子，那汹涌奔腾的江水就会滚滚扑来);开箱(女子出嫁后第一次打开其配嫁的箱柜);开着眼(睁着眼)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为开放;舒放 <span class="encs">[come into bloom; unfold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千树万树梨花开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山寺桃花始盛开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">只在花开之数日。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:春暖花开;盛开(花开得茂盛);开眉(舒展双眉。解愁、欣喜的意思);开眉展眼(形容高兴的样子。应作开眼展眉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 创立，建立，开创，设立 <span class="encs">[set up;found]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲开置县。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《海瑞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁开小窗&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:开工厂;开店;开茶馆;开屏(张设屏风)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 摆开 <span class="encs">[put;set up; place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">添酒回灯重开宴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:开宴(摆开酒席)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 开掘;开通 <span class="encs">[dredge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天门中断</span><span class="diczx2">楚江</span>开。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《望天门山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:开天辟地;开源节流;继往开来;开达(开通明达);开敏(开明通达)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 教导，启发 <span class="encs">[instruct;inspire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">移风易俗之本，乃在开其心而正其精。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王符</span><span class="smcs">《潜夫论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">便开此议。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:开诱(启发诱导);开晓(开导启发，让人明白);开譬(开导警惕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 开拓，扩展 <span class="encs">[open up;initiate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开地数千里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;初见秦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开荒南野际。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武皇</span>开边意未已。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诚宜开张圣听，以光先帝遗德。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:开封(开拓疆土);开境(开拓疆域);开济(开创大业，匡济时艰);开边(开拓疆域);开张圣听(扩大圣明的听闻。意思是要后主广泛的听取别人的意见)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 开始，开端 <span class="encs">[begin;start]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:开演;开业;开沽(卖酒);开笔(学童开始学做文章);开江(船由内河启程驶入长江);开八(十年一秩，七十一岁为八十纪数的开始，称为开八。同此，五十一岁称开六，六十一岁称开七)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 戏剧开场角色的说白 <span class="encs">[soliloquy speak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外扮老夫人上开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王实甫</span><span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 释放;赦免 <span class="encs">[release]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:开释(赦免，释放);开放(开脱;释放)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 解除，免去 <span class="encs">[remove;get rid of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:开除(开脱，免除;杀戮，消灭);开复(官员被降职、免官，后又免除处分恢复原职)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 分离，分开 <span class="encs">[separate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">连月不开。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:开交(分开;解决)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 起刺激作用，尤指诱发特征性的生命活动 <span class="encs">[stimulate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:开胃;开口(初生婴儿喂奶之前，请有奶水的妇女用咸菜抹上香油在婴儿口内抹上数次，称为:&ldquo;开口&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 书写，开列，填写 <span class="encs">[make out]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:开票;开账;开报(开列呈报)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 放晴 <span class="encs">[(of sky)clear up (after rain)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若夫霪雨霏霏，连月不开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若夫日出而林霏开。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>

<p>◎ <strong>开</strong></p>
<p>開 <span class="dicpy">kāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 黄金成色单位，等于纯金占合金的1/24 <span class="encs">[karat，carat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:16开金含16/24纯金</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 印刷上指相当于整张纸的若干分之一，附在数词之后 <span class="encs">[mo]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:十六开</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 〈方〉∶冲茶一次，叫做一开 <span class="encs">[times]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吃过五六开茶，</span><span class="diczx2">朴斋</span>将一角小洋钱会了茶钱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《海上花列传》</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="tipright">↳</span><span class="orth">開</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“开”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/開">開</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              開·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/958B.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D02.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨴂</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28D51.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨵑</span>【廣韻】苦哀切【集韻】【韻會】丘哀切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音侅。【說文】張也。【廣韻】解也。【韻會】啟也。【爾雅&middot;釋言】闢也。</p><p>又【五音集韻】通也。【易&middot;乾卦疏】亨通也。會合萬物，令使開通而爲亨也。</p><p>又發也。【禮&middot;學記】故君子之敎喻也。開而勿達。【註】開謂發頭角。【疏】但爲學者，開發大義頭角而已。</p><p>又釋也。【易&middot;乾坤卦文言疏】諸卦及爻，皆從乾坤而出，故特作文言，以開釋之。【書&middot;多方】殄戮多罪，亦克用勸，開釋無辜，亦克用勸。</p><p>又【韻會】條<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>也。</p><p>又始也。【後漢&middot;馮衍傳】開歲發春兮，百卉含英。【註】開、發，皆始也。【禮&middot;檀弓】曩者爾心或開予。【註】開，謂諫爭有所發起。</p><p>又星名。【史記&middot;天官書北斗七星註】春秋運斗極云：斗第六開陽。</p><p>又【集韻】州名。本漢朐䏰縣地，後魏置開州鎭。</p><p>又縣名。【史記&middot;高祖紀】與偕攻開封。</p><p>又【功臣年表】開封侯陶舍。【註】縣名。屬河南。</p><p>又【集韻】人名。【左傳&middot;襄二十三年】曹開御戎。【註】齊臣。</p><p>又【韻會】姓也。</p><p>又【集韻】通作闓。詳闓字註。</p><p>又【集韻】輕煙切，音牽。山名。在雍州。通作汧。</p><p>又【韻補】叶音虧。【謝惠連&middot;擣衣篇】盈篋自余手，幽緘<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠋫</span>君開。腰帶準疇昔，不知今是非。</p><p>又叶音欺。【左思&middot;蜀都賦】宣化之闥，崇禮之闈。華闕雙邈，重門洞開。</p><p>【說文】開本字。</p><p>&nbsp;</p>
