                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>看</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">k&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>使视线接触人或物：～见。～书。～齐。</li>
	<li>观察，判断：～病。观～。～好（根据市场情况，估计某种商品好销）。～透（透彻深刻地了解或认识。亦称&ldquo;看破&rdquo;、&ldquo;看穿&rdquo;）。～风使舵。</li>
	<li>访问，拜望：～望。～朋友。</li>
	<li>照应，对待：～重（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）。～轻。～待。</li>
	<li>想，以为：～法。</li>
	<li>先试试以观察它的结果：做做～。</li>
	<li>提防，小心：别跑，～摔着。</li>
	<li>安排：～茶。～酒。～座。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>看</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kān.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>守护，监视：～护。～押。～门。～青（看守正在结实还未成熟的庄稼）。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>看</strong> <span class="dicpy">kān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 守护 <span class="encs">[look after;take care of;tend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">原是一个老军看管。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒全传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:看坡(守护庄稼);看陵(看守皇帝陵墓);看养(守护，照料);看钱奴(守财奴);看鸡(孵鸡);看肚兜铜钱(仅有的几个钱);看坟的打抽丰(歇后语。吃鬼;向鬼索取财物。比喻极为刻薄，吝啬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 看押，监视以防逃跑 <span class="encs">[keep under surveillance]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:看住他，别让这小子跑了!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">k&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>看</strong> <span class="dicpy">k&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/k&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形，上面是&ldquo;手&rdquo;字的变形，下面是&ldquo;目&rdquo;，意思是用手遮住眼睛远望。本义:用手加额遮目而远望)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[look into the distance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">看，睎也。(睎，望也。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遥看瀑布挂前川。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《望庐山瀑布》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:这棵雅致的常青树很好看</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 探望;问候 <span class="encs">[call on;visit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">荀巨伯</span>远看友人疾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;德行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:看理(探听);看家(方言。探亲;看望家庭);看视(探望;问候)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 瞧;观看 <span class="encs">[look at;watch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">出门看火伴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">晓看红湿处。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春夜喜雨》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老妇出门看。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《石壕吏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却看妻子愁何在。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《闻官军收河南河北》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:看着(眼看着);看镜(照镜子);看电影;看戏;看拉不上(瞧不顺眼);看乔(误解，小看);看街(一种临街开的窗户，装有槅子，可以观看街景)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 料理，准备 <span class="encs">[manage;prepare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">急炊香饭来看客。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范成大</span><span class="smcs">《田家留客行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:看下(准备，收拾);看坐(安排坐位;关照坐下);看活(服侍;照顾);看斋(准备和尚的饮食);看坐儿的(戏院里帮观众安排座位的人);看板娘(方言。指商店的女服务员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 估量 <span class="encs">[estimate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">看君</span><span class="diczx2">颖</span>上去，新月到应圆。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《送别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如: 看小(歧视， 看不起);看长(看得远，有远见);看候(照看，察看情况)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 看见<span class="encs">[see]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">王戎</span>七岁，尝与诸小儿游。看道边李树多子折枝，诸儿竞走取之，唯 <span class="diczx2">戎</span>不动。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 阅读<span class="encs">[read]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">殷中军</span>被废 <span class="diczx2">东阳</span>，始看佛经。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;文学》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:看书;看报</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 观察;察看<span class="encs">[observe;watch;look carefully at]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帝微服往看。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;术解》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">看伺空隙，欲复为乱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;周鲂传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 认为<span class="encs">[consider;deem;think]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:你看这个计划行不行?</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 看顾;照料，料理 <span class="encs">[take care of]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:看视(照顾，看待);看觑(看;照料，照顾);看取(照看;照顾;观察;估量);看当(看待，照料)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 观赏 <span class="encs">[view and admire]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:看耍(观看玩耍);看棚(临时搭建的看台);看街(可以观赏街景的窗户)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 听，闻 <span class="encs">[listen;hear]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 物色 <span class="encs">[look for;select]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我家做官的到任所，还望大人替他看所宅舍儿，好搬取家小。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金瓶梅词话》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 当心 <span class="encs">[look out;be carefully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">你倒是去罢，这里有老虎，看吃了你。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
