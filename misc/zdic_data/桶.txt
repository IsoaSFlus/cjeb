                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>桶</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄨㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>盛水或其他东西的器具，深度较大，用金属、木材或塑料等制成：水～。饭～。马～。塑料～。</li><li>形状像桶的：皮～（做皮衣用的成件的毛皮）。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>桶</strong> <span class="dicpy">tǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，甬( <span class="dicpy">yǒng</span>)声。本义:量器名)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古量器名。方形斛 <span class="encs">[bucket]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">桶，木方，受六升。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“疑当作方斛，受六升。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">方斛谓之桶。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">角斗桶。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·仲春》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平斗桶。——<span class="smcs">《史记·商君书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 盛水或其他东西的圆柱形容器，有提梁 <span class="encs">[tub;pail;keg;barrel]</span>。如:木桶;铁桶;桶裙(圆桶状的裙子);桶盘(一种盛肴馔的桶形盘子);桶底脱(禅宗喻悟脱之境)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 供做皮衣用的兽毛皮的成件。因多缀成圆桶状，故称 <span class="encs">[cylindrical fur]</span>。如:皮衣桶子(供做皮衣用的毛皮成件。亦指毛皮衣)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>桶</strong> <span class="dicpy">tǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p>一桶的量 <span class="encs">[barrel]</span>。如:百十来桶纸，五十桶汽油;两桶水</p><p>◎ <strong>桶</strong> <span class="dicpy">tǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>同<span class="yhcs">“捅”</span> <span class="encs">[disclose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晁大官儿</span>，你消停，别把话桶得紧了，收不进去。——<span class="smcs">《醒世姻缘传》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
