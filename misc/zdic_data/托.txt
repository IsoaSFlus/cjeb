						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>托</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tuō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuō.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨㄛˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>用手掌承着东西：两手～着下巴。</li>    <li>陪衬，铺垫：衬～。烘～。</li>    <li>承着器物的东西：～盘。</li>    <li>寄，暂放：～身，～售。～儿所。</li>    <li>请求、帮助：请～。恳～。</li>    <li>借故推诿躲避：推～。假～。</li>    <li>依赖：～福。～庇。</li>    <li>委任：～付。委～。</li>    <li>压强单位。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">托</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>托</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuō.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 推。同<span class="yhcs">“拓”</span> <span class="encs">[push]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">托，推也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拓，手推物也。或作托。——<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用手掌附着或承着 <span class="encs">[hold in the palm]</span>。如:托钵(手托钵盂。指斋人赴斋堂吃饭或向施主乞食。钵，意为应器。比丘的食器);托着盘子;托着下巴;两手托腮</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指承托。支持使稳定在某一高度的位置<span class="encs">[support]</span>。如:托架(支撑重物的架子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用作…的衬里 <span class="encs">[line]</span>。如:托一层纸</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>托</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuō.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 承托某些器物的座儿 <span class="encs">[pad]</span>。如:茶托;花托儿;盏托;托子(承物的器具。即承盘。也叫托儿)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 安装步枪、猎枪的枪筒、接受器和其他装置的木头制作的供端起来瞄准射击的部件 <span class="encs">[stock]</span>。如:枪托</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 压力单位 <span class="encs">[torr]</span>，等于1333.2巴，为0°C和标准重力下1毫米汞柱的压力</p><p>◎ <strong>托</strong></p><p>託 <span class="dicpy">tuō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuō.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。左形，右声。本字是<span class="yhcs">“乇”</span>( <span class="dicpy">zhé</span>)，小篆字形，象形。象初生的嫩芽，<span class="yhcs">“一”</span>表地面，枝叶有根，有所寄托。后加<span class="yhcs">“手”</span>作<span class="yhcs">“托”</span>。汉字简化后，<span class="yhcs">“托”</span>又兼代<span class="yhcs">“託”</span>字。本义:寄托)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[entrust to the care of sb.;commit sth. to sb.'s care]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">託，寄也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东方不可以託些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">託山阪以孤魂。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张衡·思玄赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">久矣，予之不托于音也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自托于</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岂足托乎。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span>
