                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>吝</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lìn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lìn.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄣˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>当用的财物舍不得用，过分爱惜：～惜。悭～。～啬（小气）。</li>    <li>耻辱：“得之不休，不获不～”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">吝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
