                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>虹</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hóng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄥˊ</span></p><ol><li>雨后天空中出现的彩色圆弧，有红、橙、黄、绿、蓝、靛、紫七种颜色。是大气中的小水珠经日光照射发生折射和反射作用而形成的，出现在和太阳相对着的方向。</li><li>指桥（因形似虹）：～桥（中国古代的一种木拱桥。外形如长虹贯空。亦称“飞桥”）。断～。长～卧波。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>虹</strong> <span class="dicpy">hóng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hóng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从虫，工声。甲骨文<span class="yhcs">“虹”</span>与杠梁、古玉璜之形相似。本义:雨后天空出现的弧形彩晕。主虹称虹，副虹称霓)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rainbow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虹，螮蝀也，状似虫。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虹霓也，云雾也，风雨也，四时也，此积气之成乎天者也。——<span class="smcs">《列子·天瑞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虹双出，色鲜盛者为雄，雄曰虹。暗者为雌，雌曰霓。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释天》</span>疏</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不霁何虹。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虹亘中天。——<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:虹女(古代把虹比作美人，故称虹霓为虹女);虹梁(形状似虹的曲梁);虹霓(即彩虹);虹霓吐颖(吐气成虹霓。形容有丰富的诗文才华)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旌旗 <span class="encs">[flag]</span>。如:虹采(旗子)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 彩色 <span class="encs">[color]</span>。如:虹旆(绘有颜色的旗帜);虹裳(彩色的衣服)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 桥的代称 <span class="encs">[bridge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跨虹连绝岸，浮鼋续断航。——</span><span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《忝在司水看治谓桥》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“讧”</span>。惑乱 <span class="encs">[strife]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼童而角，实虹小子。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅》</span></span></p><p><span
