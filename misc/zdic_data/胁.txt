                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>胁</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">脅</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>从腋下到肋骨尽处的部分：～下。</li>
	<li>逼迫恐吓：～迫。威～。裹～。～持。～从。</li>
	<li>收敛：～肩谄笑（收缩肩膀，强为媚悦之颜，形容谄媚人的丑态）。～肩低眉（低三下四的样子）。～肩累（<span class="dicpy">lěi </span>）足（形容恐惧。&ldquo;累足&rdquo;，小步快走）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ribs, armpits; flank; threaten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> androhen, bedrohen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> flanc,menacer,forcer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">胁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胁</strong></p>
<p>脅、脇 <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉(月)，劦( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:腋下肋骨所在的部分)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[the upper part of the side of the human body]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胁，两膀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，腋下之名也。其骨谓之肋，牲体则谓之拍。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两胁里急。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;至真要大论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵尚胁盾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;版法》</span>。注:&ldquo;盾或著之于胁，固曰胁盾。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脊胁肺肩在上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">右胁夹大铁椎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">越天都之胁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胁胑(由腋下到腰以上的部位);胁窝里(比喻两侧);胁不沾席(形容佛教徒勤苦修行);胁骨(肋骨);胁衣(女人的内衣。或称&ldquo;袜&rdquo;);胁肢(胁和臂之间的部位);胁盾(盾的或称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 肋骨 <span class="encs">[rib]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">曹共公</span>闻其骈胁，欲观其裸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十三年》</span>。注:&ldquo;骈胁，合干也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旁边，边侧 <span class="encs">[side]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁著岸胁，去地二百余丈，民俗名曰石鼓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胁士(佛教语。侍立在佛两旁的菩萨)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胁</strong></p>
<p>脅 <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 挟持 <span class="encs">[coerce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是谓胁君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃胁</span><span class="diczx2">栾中</span>行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;劫也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裹胁(用胁迫手段使人跟从);胁君(胁持国君;被胁持的国君);胁权(挟权。假用君上权名胁迫别人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 威逼，用威力促使 <span class="encs">[force]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公欲从之。众从者胁公，不得归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使使胁求君主。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;常惠传》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓以威迫之也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淫而相胁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">武</span>终不可胁。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胁服夺其官。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胁污(被胁迫而致玷污);胁劫(威胁而劫持之);胁和(迫使讲和);胁服(迫使服从);胁降(迫使降服;被迫投降)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;翕&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">xī</span>)。收缩 <span class="encs">[contract;shrink]</span>?</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">易系坤动，动静辟胁，万物生焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;王莽传中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:胁肩(耸起肩膀，故示敬畏);胁肩低眉(胁肩低首。缩肩低头。形容故作恭敬畏惧的样子);胁肩累足(缩紧肩膀，不敢立正。形容极为恐惧)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8105.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】虛業切【集韻】【韻會】迄業切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音熁。【說文】兩膀也。【玉篇】身左右兩膀。【廣韻】胷脅。【增韻】腋下也。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;醢豚拍魚醢註】拍爲膊，謂脅也。【晉語】重耳過曹，聞其駢脅，欲觀其狀。</p>
<p>又【正字通】牲體，前爲代脅，中爲長脅，後爲短脅。</p>
<p>又【正韻】迫脅，以威力恐人也。【書&middot;胤征】殲厥渠魁，脅從罔治。【疏】其被迫脅而從，距王師者，皆無治責其罪。</p>
<p>又【泰誓】脅權相滅。【疏】假用在上之權命脅之。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;秦風】遊環脅驅。【傳】脅驅，愼駕具，所以止入也。</p>
<p>又斂也。【前漢&middot;王莽傳】動靜辟脅，萬物生焉。【註】師古曰：脅，收斂也。</p>
<p>又責也。【公羊傳&middot;莊二十五年】以朱絲營社，或曰脅之。【註】脅之，與責求同義。</p>
<p>又脅盾，盾名。【管子&middot;幼官篇】兵尚脅盾。【註】象時物之閉盾，或署之於脅。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】許欠切【集韻】虛欠切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音搚。妨也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】迄及切，音吸。脅肩，竦體也。或書作脇。</p>
