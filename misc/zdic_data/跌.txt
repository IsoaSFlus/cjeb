                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>跌</strong> <span class="dicpy">diē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/diē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，失声。本义:失足摔倒)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fall;tumble]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">失蹑曰跌。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跌而不振。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·晁错传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“足失据也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若跌而据。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·缪称》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:他跌伤了;跌磕(挫折，栽筋斗);跌翻(跌倒，摔倒);跌跌滚滚(跌交打滚的样子);跌跌爬爬(连跌带爬);跌坐(身体迅速坐下)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 失足。喻指犯过失 <span class="encs">[fault;slip]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一跌将赤吾之族。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:跌磕(受到挫折);跌磕蹭蹬(倒霉;不顺利);跌失(闪失);跌蹼(喻指挫折和灾难)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 落，降低 <span class="encs">[drop]</span>。如:跌坡(陷落的坡岸);跌课(算卦，占卜)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 快走 <span class="encs">[fast walk]</span>。如:跌蹄(快步行走，赶路);跌跌(疾行)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 跺脚，用脚踩踏 <span class="cino">(2)</span> 放荡不羁 <span class="encs">[unrestraint]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肆者何?跌也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·庄公二十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 行步不稳 <span
