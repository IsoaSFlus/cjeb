                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贰</strong></p><p>貳 <span class="dicpy">èr</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/èr.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从贝，弍，( <span class="dicpy">èr</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/èr.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:副)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[deputy;assistant;auxiliary;subsidiary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贰，副益也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:当云副也，益也。”</p><p><span class="diczx1">簋贰用缶。——<span class="smcs">《易·坎卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大祭三贰。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·乃酒正》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">取贰绥。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受其贰令。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·职内》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其卜贰圉也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以贰六官。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官·大史》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘副车则式，佐车则否。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·少仪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:贰佐(州县官的副);贰相(副相);贰官(副职)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 二的大写 <span class="encs">[two]</span>——用于会计账中以防伪造</p><p><span class="diczx1">因贰以济民行。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贰偷之不暇。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十三年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“不壹也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">命西鄙北鄙贰于己。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无是贰言也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">从</span><span class="diczx2">许子</span>道，则市贾不贰。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:贰端(二端);贰统(分一统的帝系为二)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Er state]</span>。故地在今湖北省应山县境</p><p><span class="diczx2">楚</span> <span class="diczx2">屈瑕</span>将盟 <span class="diczx2">贰</span>、 <span class="diczx2">轸</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>贰</strong></p><p>貳 <span class="dicpy">èr</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/èr.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一分为二 <span class="encs">[a whole divides into two;everthing tends to divide into two]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">既而大叔命西鄙北鄙贰于己。公子</span><span class="diczx2">吕</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“国不堪二。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贰，益也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂一》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:贰觞(再三献酒);贰纪(古礼中添饭加菜的规章)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 背离;怀有二心 <span class="encs">[be double-minded]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">百姓携贰。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语上》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“二心也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君立臣从，何贰之有?——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸侯贰，则</span><span class="diczx2">晋国</span>坏。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十四年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:贰志(异志;二心);贰虑(二心;三心二意)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 辅佐 <span
