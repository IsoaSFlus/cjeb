						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>愆</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>罪过，过失：～忒。～尤。罪～。</li><li>耽误：～期。～滞。</li><li>过：～伏（天气冷暖失调，多指大旱或酷暑，有变化无常的意思）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a fault, mistake, error, transgression</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Fehler, Mangel  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">愆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>愆</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，衍声。本义:过错;罪过)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fault;sin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愆，过也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">失所为愆。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不愆不忘。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·假乐》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">街亭</span>之役，咎由 <span class="diczx2">马谡</span>，而君引愆。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:愆锁(罪锁);愆戾(过失;罪咎);愆尤(过失，罪咎);愆忒(差错);愆罪(罪咎，罪过);愆殃(过失，罪恶);愆负(过失);愆过(罪恶，罪过)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[引起的]</span> 原因 <span class="encs">[cause]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泪出羞涩疼痛甚，大人肝肾虚风愆。——<span class="smcs">《医宗金鉴》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>愆</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 违背、违反 <span class="encs">[violate]</span>。又如:愆忘(违反);愆面(久违，久未见面);愆位(未能坚守工作岗位，克尽职责);愆义(违反道义)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 超过;延误 <span class="encs">[pass the appointed time;delay]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愆期。——<span class="smcs">《易·归妹》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">冬无愆阳。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不愆于素。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪我愆期。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:愆亢(久旱);愆阳(阳气过盛);愆晴(久雨未晴);愆程(误了预定的旅程);愆悬(失期不至);愆候(失时);愆素(越过原来计划)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 失掉，丧失 <span class="encs">[lose;miss]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王昏不若，用愆厥位。——<span
