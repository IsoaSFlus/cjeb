class="diczx1">不能禁失吾之悲。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:禁久(经久，耐久);禁奈(禁得起，受得起);禁不过(禁不的，禁不起。承受不住，受不了);禁当(担当;承受);禁招(招架;忍受)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 忍住 <span class="encs">[restrain oneself; contain oneself (used in negative construction)]</span>。如:不禁痛哭流涕;不禁失笑 </p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 折磨，使受苦 <span class="encs">[torment]</span>。如:禁虐(折磨受苦);禁害(损害;折磨)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>禁</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 腰带 <span class="encs">[girdle]</span>。如:禁节儿(筋节，关键)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">jìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禁不起">禁不起</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禁不住">禁不住</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禁得起">禁得起</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禁得住">禁得住</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禁受">禁受</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>禁</strong> <span class="dicpy">jìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从示，林声。本义:禁忌)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[taboo]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禁，吉凶之忌也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使帅其属而掌邦禁。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·秋官》</span>。注:<span
