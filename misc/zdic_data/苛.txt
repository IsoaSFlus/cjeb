                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>苛</strong> <span class="dicpy">kē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:小草)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[little grass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苛，小草也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 疥疮 <span class="encs">[scabies]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疾痛苛痒。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:苛痒(疥疮痒)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 病。通<span class="yhcs">“疴”</span> <span class="encs">[illness]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其形安而不移，能守一而弃万苛。——<span class="smcs">《管子·内业》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身无苛殃。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·审时》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“病也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:苛殃(疾病和灾患);苛疾(疾病)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>苛</strong> <span class="dicpy">kē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 繁杂，烦琐 <span class="encs">[miscellaneous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">好苛礼。——<span class="smcs">《史记·郦生陆贾传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">内无苛慝。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“烦也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">去烦蠲苛。——</span><span class="diczx2">王褒</span><span class="smcs">《四子讲德论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:苛礼(苛刻微细的礼节);苛难(以琐事诘难);苛敛(烦重地征收赋税);苛小(烦琐细小。亦指繁杂细微之事)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 狠虐、刻薄(侧重于残暴狠毒) <span class="encs">[onerous;harsh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弭其百苛。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“虐也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">关市苛难之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·内储说上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父老苦秦苛法久矣。——<span class="smcs">《史记·高祖本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苛政猛于虎也。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:苛虐(苛刻虐待);苛法(烦琐严厉的法令);苛吏(酷吏);苛求(过严过分地要求);苛征(横征暴敛)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 沉重，剧烈 <span class="encs">[heavy;violent]</span>。如:苛役(繁重的劳役);苛罚(重罚)</p><p>◎
