                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>轹</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">轢</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>车轮碾过：“火燔野草，车～所致。”</li><li>敲打，欺压：～辐（敲击车辐，促马快跑）。凌～（欺压）。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>轹</strong></p><p>轢 <span class="dicpy">lì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 车轮碾压 <span class="encs">[roll over by cart]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轹，车所践也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徒车之所轥轹。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">值轮被轹。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:轹人(车轮辗轧行人。指行车撞人)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 滚压;碾压 <span class="encs">[roll]</span>。如:轹谷(碾压稻谷等粮食)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 欺凌，威胁、恐吓 <span class="encs">[bully by force]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">刻轹宗室。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·酷吏传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以众暴寡，以智欺愚，以富轹贫。——</span><span class="diczx2">康有为</span><span class="smcs">《大同书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:轹刍(踩踏草料。比喻欺凌百姓);轹蹙(欺凌);轹蹸(欺凌践踏)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 超过 <span class="encs">[surpass]</span>。如:轹古(超越古人)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 经历 <span class="encs">[undergo]</span>。如:轹历(经历);轹四代(经历四代)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 刮;撞击 <span class="encs">[scrape;strike]</span>。如:轹釜待炊(刮锅有声，等待烧火做饭。形容生活艰难)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
