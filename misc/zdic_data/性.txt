						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>性</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xìng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>人或事物的本身所具有的能力、作用等：～质。～格。～命（生命）。～能。个～。属～。</li><li>性质，思想、感情等方面的表现：典型～。开放～。指令～。</li><li>男女或雌雄的特质：～别。男～。女～。</li><li>有关生物生殖的：～交。～欲。～爱。～感。～解放。</li><li>表示名词（以及代词、形容词）的类别的语法范畴。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>性</strong> <span class="dicpy">xìng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xìng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，生声。本义:人的本性)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[natural instincts]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">性，人之阳气性善者也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">性，质也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天命之谓性。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·中庸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自诚明谓之性。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可学，不可事，而在人者谓之性。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·性恶篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五性者何?仁义礼智信也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">性相近也，习相远也。——<span class="smcs">《论语·阳货》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生之所以然者谓之性。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正名篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:性根(根性;本性);性行(人的禀性和行为);性气(人的个性和脾气);个性(人的本性;人所具有的正常的感情或理性);禀性(本性);性尚(本性的爱好和崇尚)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 事物的性质或性能 <span class="encs">[nature;character]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则天之明，因地之性。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物性之苦。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《诚意伯刘文成公文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物性之不同。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:性力(指药性和药力);性状;共性(共同具有的性质)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“生”</span>( <span class="dicpy">shēng)。</span>生命;生机 <span class="encs">[life]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">莫保其性。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民乐其性，而无寇雠。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为炮烙以伤民性。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·难势》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">性命寿长，终其天年而不夭伤。——<span class="smcs">《史记·范雎蔡泽列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以治气养性，则身后彭祖。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传·卷一》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:性理(谓生命之原理);性类(犹生类，指有生命的物类)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 性情;脾气 <span class="encs">[disposition]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">动心忍性。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">少无适俗韵，性本爱丘山。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾性不喜华靡。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span
