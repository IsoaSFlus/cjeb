                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">树</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>树</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">樹</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>木本植物的通称：～木。～林。～大根深（喻势力大，根基牢固）。</li>
	<li>种植，培育：～艺（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">艺</span>&rdquo;，种植）。～荆棘得刺，～桃李得荫。</li>
	<li>立，建立：～立。～敌。</li>
	<li>量词，相当于&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">株</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">棵</span>&rdquo;：一～梅花。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> tree; plant; set up, establish</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Baum</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> arbre,ériger,cultiver</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">树</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>树</strong></p>
<p>樹 <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，尌( <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:栽树)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[plant trees]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树，生植之总名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;植，立也。假借为尌竖字。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宿敢不封殖此树。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫坚树在始。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;树木也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地道敏树。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">周礼</span><span class="smcs">《中庸》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓殖草木也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二曰树艺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树之榛栗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鄘风&middot;定之方中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五亩之宅，树之以桑，五十者可以衣帛矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:树事(种植草木之事);树杨(种植杨树);十年之计，在于树木;树其树以荫行人</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 种植 <span class="encs">[plant;grow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树，种也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所食之粟，伯夷之所树与?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">后稷</span>教民稼穑，树艺五谷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:树萱(种植萱草);树稼(种植农作物);树植(种植);树养(种植培养)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 竖起或建起;树立 <span class="encs">[erect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">崇牙树羽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;有瞽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹有未树也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如是，则人有树事之患，而有争功之祸矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;富国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆稽颡树颔，扶服蛾伏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">树德而济同欲焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公二年》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6A39.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B3E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡬾</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23552.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣕒</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/236A4.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣚤</span>【唐韻】常句切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】殊遇切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>殊去聲。【說文】生植之總名。【左傳&middot;昭二年】季氏有嘉樹，宣子譽之。【禮&middot;祭義】樹木以時伐焉。【淮南子&middot;原道訓】萍樹根于水，木樹根于土。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋宮】屛謂之樹。【論語】邦君樹塞門。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】牀謂之杠。北燕、朝鮮閒謂之樹。</p>
<p>又獸名。【儀禮&middot;鄉射禮】君國中射則皮樹中。【註】皮樹，獸名。謂皮作樹形以射之。</p>
<p>又姓也。【後魏&middot;官氏志】樹洛于氏。後改爲樹氏。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>臣庾切，音豎。扶樹也。【徐鍇曰】樹之言豎也。種樹曰樹。【易&middot;繫辭】古之葬者，不封不樹。【詩&middot;小雅】荏染柔木，君子樹之。往來行人，心焉數之。</p>
<p>又立也。【書&middot;說命】樹后王君公，承以大夫師長。【泰誓】樹德務滋，除惡務本。【畢命】彰善癉惡，樹之風聲。</p>
<p>又諸侯之適子，天子命爲之嗣者，曰樹子。【穀梁傳&middot;僖九年】無易樹子。　俗作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/235F3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣗳</span>，非。</p>
