                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朔</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>朔</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shuò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuò.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>农历每月初一：～日。～望（“朔日”和“望日”）。</li><li>始：“皆从其～”。</li><li>北方：～方。～漠（北方沙漠地带）。～气。～土。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> first day of lunar month; the north</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Neumond  (S)​,Norden  (S)​,Anfang  (S)​,Anfangsstadium  (S)​,anfangen  (V)​,Shuo  (Eig, Fam)​,erster Tag des jungen Mondes und des chinesischen Monats ,= <a href="./朔">朔</a>shuo4</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> nouvelle Lune,nord</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">朔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>朔</strong> <span class="dicpy">shuò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shuò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从月，从屰，屰( <span class="dicpy">nì</span>)亦声。本义:农历每月初一)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[the first day of the lunar month]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔，月一日始苏也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，凡月与日同经度不同纬度则为合朔，同经度又同纬度即为日蚀。计173日有奇。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔，月初之名也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">月言朔。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通·四时篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔月奠。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“朔月，月朔也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔月辛卯。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·十月之交》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">初，薙发令下，诸生许用德者以闰六月朔，县</span><span class="diczx2">明太祖</span>御容明伦堂，率众拜且苦。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《阎典史传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:朔参官(能在朔日朝参的官员);朔望之礼(指初一、十五到祀堂祭祀的礼节);朔望之辰(农历每月初一、十五的时候);朔晦(朔日和晦日);朔法(确定朔日的方法);朔数(从第一年正月初一到第二年正月初一);朔食(古礼之一。帝王及贵族每月初一所备较平日丰盛的膳食);朔旦(旧历每月初一)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 朔政，历政，指中国古代帝王每年冬季发布来年十二个月的政事于诸侯，诸侯亦于月初告祖庙，受而行之 <span class="encs">[calendar policy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颁告朔于邦国。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 凌晨，清晨 <span class="encs">[early morning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朝菌不知晦朔，蟪蛄不知春秋。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 初，始 <span class="encs">[beginning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆从其朔。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 北方 <span class="encs">[north]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">城彼朔方。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·出车》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">平在朔易。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔，北方也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:朔风凛凛(北风寒冷);朔北(长城以北);朔土(北方地区);朔垂(西北边远地区);朔门(北方边境之地);朔庭(北方异族政权);朔客(北方边地的将领);朔气(北方之寒气);朔野(北方荒野之地);朔雪(北方的雪)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 车辕。一说，车上的旗 <span class="encs">[bar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">月之旦为朔，车之輈亦谓之朔，名齐实异，所宜辨也。——<span class="smcs">《西京杂记》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎
