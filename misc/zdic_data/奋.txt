                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奋</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>奋</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">奮</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>鸟张开并振动翅膀：～飞。～翅。</li>	<li>振作，鼓劲，振动：～起。～力。～勇。～不顾身。振～。勤～。兴～。</li>	<li>提起，举起：～臂。～袂（举袖，形容奋发的样子）。～笔疾书。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> strive, exert effort; arouse</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> être soulevé d'enthousiasme,s'appliquer à</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奋</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>奋</strong></p>
<p>奮 <span class="dicpy">f&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，中间是&ldquo;隹&rdquo;(鸟);外面象鸟振翅欲飞之势;下面是&ldquo;田&rdquo;，表示空旷的田野。本义:鸟类振羽展翅)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(said of a bird)to take wing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奮，翚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奋隼归凫。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羽翼奋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雉绝有力奋，鸡绝有力奋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释鸟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不能奋飞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;柏舟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是宾客无不变色离席，奋袖出臂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虫暴怒，直奔，遂相腾击，振奋作声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奋翅(振翼起飞);奋翼(振翼高飞);奋翔(振翼高飞);奋鳞(龙腾飞);奋翮(展翅，振羽)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 鼓起劲来，振作 <span class="encs">[raise;exert oneself;act vigorously]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奋，振也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">拔刀奋起，率众袭之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及至</span><span class="diczx2">始皇</span>，奋六世之余烈。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奋烈(振奋威武);奋信(振奋伸张);奋武扬威(振奋勇气，施展威风);奋气(奋发振作)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 震动 <span class="encs">[shake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雷出地奋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;豫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奋，动也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奋首(摇头，表示疲困);奋髯(抖动胡须);奋蹄(振蹄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 奋力;施展;发挥 <span class="encs">[dash ahead;put to good use]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奋至德之光。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;乐书》</span>。集解:&ldquo;发也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王奋厥武。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;常武》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂其士卒众多哉，诚能据其势而奋其威。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奋张(有力地伸展、张开);奋末(四肢用力)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 挥动;举起;舞动 <span class="encs">[wield]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">陈王</span>奋臂，为天下倡始。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;张耳陈余列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众贼环而进，客奋椎左右击，人马仆地。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂鼓吻奋爪以向先生。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奋臂(有力地举臂，表示振奋);奋袂(举袖，挥袖，形容振奋);奋笔(提笔疾书);奋戈(使劲挥舞干戈)</span></p>
