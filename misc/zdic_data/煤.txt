                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">煤</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>煤</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄟˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代的植物压埋在地底下，在不透空气或空气不足的条件下，受到地下的高温和高压年久变质而形成的黑色或黑褐色矿物：～矿。～田。～层。～气。～焦油。～精。</li>
	<li>〈方〉烟气凝结的黑灰，为制墨的主要原料：～炱。松～（松烟）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> coal, coke, charcoal, carbon</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kohle  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> charbon,houille</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">煤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>煤</strong> <span class="dicpy">m&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，某声。本义:烟尘)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[soot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:煤尾(屋中的烟尘)。又指制墨的烟灰</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">试扫其煤以为墨，黑光如漆，松墨也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指墨 <span class="encs">[ink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中官欲于苑中作墨灶，取</span><span class="diczx2">西湖</span>九里松作煤。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《老学庵笔记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:煤精(煤的一种。色黑，质硬，可用以雕刻工艺品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 灯芯的余烬，即灯花 <span class="encs">[snuff]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:煤火(煤燃烧时的火焰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 煤炭，一种黑色固体矿物 <span class="encs">[coal]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:煤毒(即煤气);煤炸(小煤块);煤掌(煤矿井下的工作面);煤气灶(以煤气为燃料的灶具)</span></p>
