<p>● <strong>脔</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">臠</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>切成小块的肉：&ldquo;尝一～肉知一镬之味。&rdquo;～割（分割）。禁～（喻独自占有而不容别人分享的东西，如&ldquo;视为～～&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> small lump of meat; sliced meat</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> mager ,geschnittene Fleischstücke</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mince,viande coupée</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">脔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>脔</strong></p>
<p>臠 <span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉。羉( <span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:小块肉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a small slice of meat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一曰切肉，脔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嘬谓一举尽脔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲记》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尝一脔肉，而知一镬之味。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说林训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脔脔(切成小块的肉);脔脍(切成块的鱼肉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 量词。用于块状的鱼肉 <span class="encs">[slice]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:脔肉(犹言一块肉。谓其量少)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>脔</strong></p>
<p>臠 <span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>切肉成块 <span class="encs">[slice meat up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">脔，臞也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">棘人脔脔兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;桧风&middot;素冠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">取新鲤鱼，去鳞讫则脔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《齐民要术&middot;作鲊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未受牺牛大羹之和，久在煎熬脔割之间。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;边让传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:脔制(分割而制伏之);脔割(分割;切碎);脔股(谓割股疗疾以示虔敬);脔身(分割身体)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“脔”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/臠">臠</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四">卷四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷四&amp;swbs=肉">肉</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/81E0.svg">                          <p>臞也。从肉䜌聲。一曰切肉，臠也。《詩》曰：&ldquo;棘人臠臠兮。&rdquo;力沇切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/268CF_1.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/268CF.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦣏</span>)</span> <span class="ywyy">臞也。</span><span class="swdz">毛詩傳曰：欒欒，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24E03.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤸃</span>瘠皃。葢或三家詩有作臠，從正字。毛作欒，從假借字。抑許所據毛作臠，皆不能肊定也。</span> <span class="ywyy">从肉䜌聲。</span><span class="swdz">力沇切。十四部。古皆讀平聲。</span> <span class="ywyy">一曰切肉也。</span><span class="swdz">依廣韵訂。切肉曰臠，臠之大者曰胾，此許義也。<span class="ywyy">詩曰：棘人臠臠。此證前一義也。</span></span></p>                          </div>
