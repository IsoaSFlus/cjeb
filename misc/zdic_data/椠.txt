                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">椠</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>椠</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">槧</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>古代以木削成用作书写的版片。</li><li>简札，书信。</li><li>书的刻本：～本。古～。宋～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wooden tablet; edition</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Auflage  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tablette en bois,édition</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>椠</strong></p><p>槧 <span class="dicpy">qiàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，斩声。本义:书板，古代削木为牍，没有书写过的素牍叫椠)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wooden tablet for writing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">椠，牍朴也。从木，斩声。谓未书之版长三尺，其小者曰札曰牒。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怀铅握椠。——<span class="smcs">《西京杂记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:椠工(刻字工);椠刻(刊刻);椠匠(刻字匠人)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 书的刻本 <span class="encs">[an edition of a book]</span>。如:椠铅(指读书、作诗文);宋椠;元椠;椠人(读书而有见识之人)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/椠本">椠本</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
