                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>揽</strong></p><p>攬、擥 <span class="dicpy">lǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，览声。本义:执，持)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俱可手揽。——<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记·游黄山记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:揽跋(揽辔跋涉);揽镜(持镜;对镜);揽持(把持;掌握);揽摄(把持)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 总揽(全面掌握);一把抓 <span class="encs">[monopolize;dominate;take on everything]</span>。如:揽延(延揽);揽取(承揽);揽活(揽取活计);揽纳(包揽代纳赋税)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 搂，拥抱，围抱 <span class="encs">[take sb.into one’s arms;embrace]</span>。如:揽抱(拥抱);揽取(抱取，搂取);揽持(犹拥抱)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 采摘 <span class="encs">[pick up]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:揽月(摘月。喻壮举);揽取(摘取，收取);揽结(采摘系结)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 挥 <span class="encs">[wield]</span>。如:揽泣(挥泪);揽涕(挥泪)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 招惹 <span class="encs">[provoke]</span>。如:揽野火(惹麻烦);揽事(惹事，管闲事)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 招引;兜揽 <span class="encs">[canvass;take on;take upon oneself]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">总揽英雄，思贤若渴。——<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:揽脚(脚夫招揽顾客);揽事(管闲事);揽买卖</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 提，撩起 <span class="encs">[raise; hold up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">揽裙脱丝履，举身赴清池。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“览”</span>。观看 <span class="encs">[look at;see]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而欲为人之国者，此揽乎三王之利，而不见其患者也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·在宥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流揽无穷，归神日母。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">枚乘</span><span class="smcs">《七发》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/揽工">揽工</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/揽活，揽活儿">揽活，揽活儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/揽客">揽客</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/揽权">揽权</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/揽胜">揽胜</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/揽总，揽总儿">揽总，揽总儿</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
