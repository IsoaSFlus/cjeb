                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尽</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>尽</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">盡</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>完毕：用～。说不～。取之不～。</li>
	<li>达到极端：～头。山穷水～。～情。自～（自杀）。</li>
	<li>全部用出，竭力做到：～心。～力。～瘁。～职。～忠。～责。人～其才。物～其用。</li>
	<li>都，全：～然。～是白的。～收眼底。～释前嫌。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>尽</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">儘</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>极，最：～底下。</li>
	<li>力求达到最大限度：～量（<span class="dicpy">li&agrave;ng </span>）。～管。</li>
	<li>都，全。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> exhaust, use up; deplete</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> le plus possible,dans certaines limites,donner la priorité</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>尽</strong></p>
<p>儘 <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (尽为&ldquo;儘&rdquo;和&ldquo;盡&rdquo;的简化字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 尽量，尽可能 <span class="encs">[to the greatest extent]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尽早;尽分(尽量);尽语(尽量告诉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 意思同&ldquo;最&rdquo;，用在方位词的前面 <span class="encs">[always]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尽南边;尽前边</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 老是;总 <span class="encs">[always]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尽子(总是;老是)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 〈方〉∶保持某种事情的连续性 <span class="encs">[keep on doing sth.]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他衣服都被汗湿透了，还尽着干呢?这些日子尽下雨</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>尽</strong></p>
<p>儘 <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示以某个范围为界限，不得超过 <span class="encs">[within]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尽着一天把事情处理完</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 让先 <span class="encs">[first;give priority to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:尽让(谦让);尽着好位子坐;尽好的吃</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 任凭，纵使 <span class="encs">[no matter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟有落红不禁，尽教飞舞出宫墙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">武衍</span><span class="smcs">《宫词》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尽下(听凭臣下;对臣下放心。帝王宽以待下)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
