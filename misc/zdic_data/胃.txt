                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>胃</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄟˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>人和脊椎动物身体里主管消化食物的器官：～脏。～液。～酸。～口（食欲，引申为兴趣、欲望。&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">口</span>&rdquo;读轻声）。</li>	<li>星名，二十八宿之一。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胃</strong> <span class="dicpy">w&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。据小篆字形。上象口袋形的消化器官，下为肉。本义:人和动物贮藏和消化食物的器官)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stomach]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疾在肠胃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;喻老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在肠胃。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:重瓣胃;胃疸(中医指胃部虚热);胃气痛(方言。胃痛);胃气(中医指胃的生理功能及其精气;泛指胃病)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 星名 <span class="encs">[name of a star]</span>。二十八宿之一，白虎七宿的第三宿。有星3颗，即白羊座35、39、41三星。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:胃维(胃星之旁)</span></p>
