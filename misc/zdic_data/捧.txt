						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>捧</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pěng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pěng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄥˇ</span></p><ol><li>两手托着：～心（用手捂住胸，喻拙劣的模仿）。～日。～读（敬辞，读别人的文章等）。～腹大笑。</li><li>量词，用手能捧的东西：一～红枣。</li><li>奉承或代人吹嘘：～场。吹～。～哏。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>捧</strong> <span class="dicpy">pěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pěng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，奉声。本义:两手承托。古多作<span class="yhcs">“奉”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carry or hold in both hands]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">捧，两手承也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">捧馈而哭。——<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“捧，两手持也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">捧土以塞孟津，多见其不知量也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·朱浮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君与</span><span class="diczx2">康先生</span>捧诏恸哭。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">两手捧头及剑奉之。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:捧戴(托举;扶拥);捧脚(捧承他人的脚。形容随从众多);捧檄(接受诏书为官就任);捧头鼠窜(形容抱头而逃、狼狈至极);捧茗(端茶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拱手 <span class="encs">[cup one's hands]</span>。如:捧手(拱手);捧袂(犹拱手)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 掬 <span class="encs">[scoop]</span>。如:捧起水来喝了一大口</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 代人吹嘘或奉承、拥戴别人 <span class="encs">[praise;support]</span>。如:捧日(拥戴帝王。旧时以日喻帝王)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 簇拥 <span class="encs">[environ]</span>。如:捧拥(簇拥);捧心西子(比喻白海棠的娇弱柔美)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>捧</strong> <span class="dicpy">pěng</span> <span class="ptr"><a
