                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>逵</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ku&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄟˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>四通八达的道路。后也泛指大道。《爾雅&bull;釋宫》：&ldquo;九達謂之逵。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>水中连通的穴道。《山海經&bull;中山經》：&ldquo;（合水）多鰧魚，狀如鱖，居逵。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>隐。《廣韻&bull;脂韻》：&ldquo;逵，隱也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。《萬姓統譜&bull;支韻》：&ldquo;逵，見《姓苑》。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> thoroughfare, crossroads</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kreuzung oder Einmündung mit Vorfahrt, Querstraßen  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> voie,chemin</p></div>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>逵</strong> <span class="dicpy">ku&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ku&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵 <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，坴( <span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;</span>)声。本义:四通八达的道路</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。后也泛指大道 <span class="encs">[thoroughfare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肃肃兔罝，施于中逵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;免罝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:大逵(大道);逵衢(大道);逵路(四通八达的大道);逵径(岔路)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 逵穴 <span class="encs">[hole]</span>，水中之穴道交通者</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">馗</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                    <span class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“逵”字头，请参考“馗”字：)</span>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四">卷十四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四&amp;swbs=九">九</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9997.svg">                          <p>九達道也。似龜背，故謂之馗。馗，高也。从九从首。逵，馗或从辵从坴。渠追切〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29812.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩠒</span>，本字。文二　重一</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9997_7710.svg" /><span class="swbf">(馗)</span><span class="ywyy">九<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28536.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨔶</span>道也。</span><span class="swdz">釋宫曰：九<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28536.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨔶</span>謂之馗。韓詩：施于中馗。</span><span class="ywyy">佀龜背，故謂之馗。</span><span class="swdz">龜背中高而四下，馗之四面無不可通，似之。龜古音如姬，如鳩。馗古音如求，以曡韵爲訓也。大徐本此下有馗高也三字，非是。</span><span class="ywyy">从九首。</span><span class="swdz">會意。首，猶向也。故道字亦从首。九亦聲。古音在三部。今渠追切。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9997_7711.svg" /><span class="swbf">(逵)</span><span class="ywyy">馗或从辵坴。</span><span class="swdz">今毛詩作此字。</span><span class="ywyy">馗，高也，故从坴。</span><span class="swdz">故字今增。徐楚金云：坴，高土也。會意。玉裁按坴亦聲。</span></p>                          </div>
