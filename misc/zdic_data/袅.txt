                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">袅</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>袅</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">裊</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">niǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄧㄠˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 柔弱，缭绕：“不声如动吹，无风自～枝”。～娜（<span class="dicpy">nuó </span>）。～～（a.形容烟气缭绕上腾的样子，如“炊烟～～”；b.形容细长柔美；c.形容随风摆动的样子，如“垂柳～～”；d.形容微风吹拂；e.形容声音绵延不绝，如“余音～～”）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> curling upwards; wavering gently</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> zierlich</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">袅</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>袅</strong></p><p>裊、嫋 、嬝 <span class="dicpy">niǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 以系带系马。代称马名 <span class="encs">[tie horse with silk ribbon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">裊，以组带马也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注<span class="yhcs">“<span class="smcs">《百官志》</span>注曰:‘ <span class="diczx2">秦</span>爵二十等…三曰簪裊，御驷马者。’”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:袅蹏(袅蹄，铸金成马蹄形。因借指金银);袅驃(古骏马名);袅骖(小马的别名。一说古骏马名。即袅驃)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 缭绕;缠绕 <span class="encs">[curl upward;twine;wind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">台上炉香袅翠烟。——</span><span class="diczx2">王明清</span><span class="smcs">《挥尘后录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:袅脚(绕腿布);袅丝(香灰的别称);袅篆(指香的烟缕)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 摇曳 <span class="encs">[wave in the wind]</span>。如:袅窕(影子动摇的样子);袅軃(摇曳下垂的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 吹拂 <span class="encs">[sway]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荷花落兮江色秋，秋风袅兮夜悠悠。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《悲清秋赋》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>袅</strong></p><p>裊 <span class="dicpy">niǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/niǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“嫋”</span>。柔弱细长的样子 <span class="encs">[slender and graceful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">披迳怜深沉，攀条惜杳袅。——</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span>· <span class="diczx2">陈</span>· <span class="diczx2">江总</span><span class="smcs">《游西霞寺》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 如:袅娉(袅娜娉婷。女子姿态美好的样子);袅嫋(纤长柔美的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 形容声音婉转悠扬 <span class="encs">[sweet and a greeable]</span>。如:袅袅悠悠(婉转悠长)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
