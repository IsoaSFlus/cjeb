                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">黑</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>黑</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hēi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hēi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄟˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>像墨和煤那样的颜色，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">白</span>&rdquo;相对：～白。</li>
	<li>暗，光线不足：～暗。～夜。</li>
	<li>隐蔽的，非法的：～枪。～市。～社会。</li>
	<li>恶毒：～心。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> black; dark; evil, sinister</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schwarz</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> noir,sombre,secret</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">黑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>黑</strong> <span class="dicpy">hēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hēi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。甲骨文从&ldquo;大&rdquo;圈出脸部，像黥刑，在脸上用墨刺字的刑罚。西周金文在脸上加二点，强调墨点。春秋金文&ldquo;大&rdquo;加点，秦文字整理为&ldquo;火&rdquo;放在下面。隶变&ldquo;大&rdquo;变成&ldquo;土&rdquo;、&ldquo;火&rdquo;在下变成&ldquo;灬&rdquo;。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>篆&ldquo;黑&rdquo;下部讹成&ldquo;炎&rdquo;。本义:黑色)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[black]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黑，火所熏之色也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纯黑而反哺者，谓之乌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坤为黑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黑气乃辱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;气交变大论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郑公孙</span>黑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十九年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚公子</span>黑肱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元报》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大夫黑弓，礼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;大略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:黑气(黑色的水气);黑章(黑色的花纹);黑麻(黑色的胡麻);黑眸(黑色的眸子);黑斑(黑色的斑点);黑参参(黑乎乎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 昏暗无光 <span class="encs">[dark]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天阴黑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">月黑见渔灯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">查慎行</span><span class="smcs">《舟夜书所见》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:黑处(暗处，光线不明的地方);黑狱(暗无日光的牢房);黑天黑地(天地一片漆黑);黑塔窟(昏暗的样子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 狠毒;象征反动，坏 <span class="encs">[evil;sinister;wicked;reactionary]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黑店;黑色恐怖(指无政府主义或恐怖主义者所进行的暗杀活动，或恐怖性的激烈行为);黑款(黑钱);黑爪(罪恶之手)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 秘密，非法 <span class="encs">[clandestine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黑档子(以非法项目贪污米粮);黑楼子(暗娼户);黑经(暗中使歪点子);黑腥事(暗中干的凶残事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 突然而猛烈的 <span class="encs">[sudden and violent]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黑风(暴风);黑浪(恶浪;巨浪;暴风浪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 比喻倒霉，不走运 <span class="encs">[run into bad luck;be unlucky]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黑路上(运气不好;倒霉)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>黑</strong> <span class="dicpy">hēi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hēi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 黑色物质、物体 <span class="encs">[black]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 含有碳元素而且通常是主要成分的各种物质。如:炭黑;灯黑;黑星星(灰尘)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 黑染料。如:酸性黑;直接黑;媒染黑</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 黑子，两人棋类游戏(如围棋)中黑色的棋子。如:执黑;黑白双丸(围棋子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 夜晚 <span class="encs">[evening]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:黑家白日(每日每夜;日日夜夜);黑赶(半夜起事;闹腾);黑个(方言。夜晚);黑车(夜行不点灯的车)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 黑色黍米 <span class="encs">[black corn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冬，王使</span><span class="diczx2">周公</span>阅来聘，飨有昌襡、白、黑、形盐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 黑色的猪羊 <span class="encs">[black pig or sheep]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">来方禋祀，以其骍黑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;大田》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:&ldquo;骍，牛也。黑，羊豕也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 黑龙江省的简称 <span class="encs">[Heilongjiang Province]</span></p>

<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 把 <span class="encs">[某物]</span> 放在隐蔽处，把&hellip;藏起来 <span class="encs">[hide]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他把钱都黑起来了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 说&hellip;的坏话，诽谤 <span class="encs">[blacken]</span>。 如: 黑了良心</p>
