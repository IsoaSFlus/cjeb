                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>奠</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">di&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄧㄢˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>向死者供献祭品致敬：祭～。～酒。</li>	<li>稳固地安置：～都（<span class="dicpy">dū ㄉㄨ</span>）。～基。～定（使稳固安定，如&ldquo;～～基础&rdquo;）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pay respect; settle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> établir,fonder,offrir en libation</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>奠</strong> <span class="dicpy">di&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形，上面是&ldquo;酋&rdquo;(即&ldquo;酒&rdquo;)，下面象放东西的基物。本义:设酒食以祭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。特指初死时的备供品敬礼 <span class="encs">[libate;make offerings to the dead]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奠，置祭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于以奠之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;采蘋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">共其奠牛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;牛人》</span>。注:&ldquo;丧所荐馈曰奠。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舍奠于其庙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭统》</span>。注:&ldquo;非时不而祭曰奠。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故既奠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特牲》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓荐熟时也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有司以几宴舍奠于其墓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奠汝又不见汝食。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁牧</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:祭奠(为死者举行仪式，表示追念);奠馔(置食物以祭);奠飨(置酒食以祭祀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 荐献;敬献 <span class="encs">[consecrate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主人坐，奠爵于阶前。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;乡饮酒》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奠献(献祭品以祀死者);奠仪(送给丧家用于祭奠的钱物);奠雁(古代婚俗。新郎用雁作见面礼到女家迎亲，表示不再娶他人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 定;确定，规定 <span class="encs">[determine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无地奠位。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《太玄》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奠高山大川。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span>。传:&ldquo;奠，定也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆辨其物而奠其禄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官&middot;职币》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奠位(定位);奠居(定居;安居)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 放置 <span class="encs">[put;place]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:奠枕(安枕以卧。形容局势安定)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>奠</strong> <span class="dicpy">di&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/di&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 祭品 <span class="encs">[libation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使</span><span class="diczx2">建中</span>远具时羞之奠。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《祭十二郎文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奠馈(祭品); 奠分(旧俗治丧，各家分摊送给丧家的奠礼);奠敬(奠仪。代作祭品而送给丧家的钱)</span></p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
