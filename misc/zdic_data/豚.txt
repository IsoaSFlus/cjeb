                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>豚</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">t&uacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～鼠〕哺乳动物，亦称&ldquo;荷兰猪&rdquo;、&ldquo;天竺鼠&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>小猪，亦泛指猪：～肩。～蹄。～儿（谦称自己的儿子）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> small pig, suckling pig; suffle</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ferkel  (S, Bio)​,Schwein  (S, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cochon de lait,porcelet</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">豚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>豚</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆作字形从彖省。彖( <span class="dicpy">shǐ</span>)，猪。从手(又)持肉，用于祭祀。本义:小猪，猪)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[suckling pig]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豚，小豕也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猪其子谓之豚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言八》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豚鱼吉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;中孚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">豚曰腯肥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸡豚狗彘之畜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郑县</span>人卖豚，人问其价。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">阳货</span>欲见 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>， <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>不见，归 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>豚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;阳货》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:豚子(谦称自己的儿子);豚犬(猪与狗);豚鱼(豚和鱼。泛指无知的动物);豚犊(愚蠢如猪的小孩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指猪 <span class="encs">[pig]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:豚肩;豚蹄</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">豚</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中" target="_blank">酉集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集中&amp;bs=豕" target="_blank">豕</a>】
                              豚·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=11" target="_blank">11</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8C5A.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】徒䰟切【集韻】【韻會】徒渾切【正韻】徒孫切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音屯。【說文】小豕也。【小爾雅】豬子曰豚。【易&middot;中孚】豚魚吉。【孔疏】豚，獸之微賤者。【禮&middot;曲禮】凡祭宗廟之禮，豚曰腯肥。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;庖人】春行羔豚，膳膏香。</p>
<p>又地名。【左傳&middot;定六年】公侵鄭，往不假道于衞。及還，陽虎使季、孟舍于豚澤，衞侯使彌子瑕追之。</p>
<p>又水名。【字彙補】豚水，在牂柯郡。</p>
<p>又姓。【印藪】有豚少公，漢人。</p>
<p>又河豚，魚名。與魨同。【博雅】鯸䱌，魨也。背靑，腹白，觸物卽怒，其肝殺人。【註】正今人名爲河豚者也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】或作㹠。【莊子&middot;德充符】適見㹠子食于其死母者。【晉書&middot;謝混傳】每得一㹠，以爲珍膳。【音義】㹠，卽豚字。</p>
<p>又【集韻】通作肫。【晉書&middot;王濟傳】蒸肫甚美。</p>
<p>又書作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C78.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧱸</span>。【石鼓文】射其<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C78.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧱸</span>蜀。【釋文】作豚。</p>
<p>又書作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2668C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦚌</span>。【石鼓文】又體如<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2668C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦚌</span>。【釋文】作豚。</p>
<p>又【集韻】杜本切【正韻】徒本切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>屯上聲。行曳踵也。【禮&middot;玉藻】圈豚行不舉足。【註】豚性散，圈之則聚而回旋于其中。圈、豚<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>上聲。</p>
<p>又【集韻】或作腯。【禮&middot;曲禮】豚曰腯肥。【釋文】腯亦作豚。</p>
<p>又【韻會小補】徒困切，屯去聲。義同。</p>
<p>又【字彙補】都昆切，音墩。土豚，土墩也。【魏志&middot;蔣濟傳】豫作土豚，遏絕湖水。</p>
<p>又叶徒丁切，音庭。【李尤&middot;席銘】施席接賔，士無過賢。値時所有，何必羊豚。賢音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>。　【說文】从彖省，象形。从又持肉，以給祠祀。篆文从肉豕作豚。【玉篇】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C54.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧱔</span>。【類篇】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2673C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦜼</span>。【集韻】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20B63.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠭣</span>。亦作腞。或作豘、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28535.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨔵</span>。</p>

												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九">卷九</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷九&amp;swbs=豚">豚</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8C5A.svg">                          <p>小豕也。从彖省，象形。从又持肉，以給祠祀。凡豚之屬皆从豚。豚，篆文从肉豕。徒魂切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8C5A_6444.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22109.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢄉</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">小豕也。</span><span class="swdz">方言：豬，其子或謂之豚，或謂之豯。</span><span class="ywyy">从古文豕。</span><span class="swdz">各本作从㣇省，象形五字。非也，今正。</span><span class="ywyy">从又持肉，以給祠祀也。</span><span class="swdz">凡祭宗廟之禮，豕曰剛鬛。豚曰腯肥。又，手也。徒䰟切。十三部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22109.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢄉</span>之屬皆从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22109.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢄉</span>。</span><span class="swdz">各本<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22109.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢄉</span>作豚。誤，今正。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8C5A_6445.svg" /><span class="swbf">(豚)</span><span class="ywyy">篆文从肉豕。</span><span class="swdz">上古文，此小篆也。亦以上附二之例。不入豚於豕部附以古文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C6F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧱯</span>者，以有从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27C6F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧱯</span>之<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/225A8.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢖨</span>則不得不立此部首也。爾雅音義曰：籒文作豚。玉篇亦曰：豚者，籒文皆誤。恐學者惑焉，故箸於此。</span></p>                          </div>
