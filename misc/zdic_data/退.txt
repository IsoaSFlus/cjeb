                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">退</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>退</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tuì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>向后移动，与“<span class="diczx3">进</span>”相对：～步。～路。～却。～让。倒～。</li>    <li>离开，辞去：～席。～伍。～职。～休。引～。</li>    <li>送还，不接受，撤销：～还。</li>    <li>脱落：～色。～毛。减～。</li>    <li>和柔的样子：～然。</li>    <li>迟缓，畏缩：“求也～”。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> step back, retreat, withdraw</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> zurückgeben, zurücktreten  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> reculer,faire reculer,baisser,descendre,rembourser,rendre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">退</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>退</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形。从彳，从日，从攵。彳( <span class="dicpy">chì</span>)，小步。攵( <span class="dicpy">suī</span>)，足的反写。本义:向后走，后退)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[retreat;move back]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">退，卻也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宾三退负序。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主人少退。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·乡射礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">待坐则必先退席。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可以退而谓之退。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·谋政》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暑为之退。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄·种植部》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">攻一时敌退。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">敌乃纷退。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:退到院子里;他往后退了几步;退缩(后退，回缩);退生(后退以求生);退厅(退堂。旧指官吏问案完毕，退出公堂);退藏于密(后退隐藏于秘密之处);退殿(君王视朝完毕而退出殿堂);退出战斗;退罢(退兵罢 战);退散(后退溃散;撤退疏散);退军(撤退军队)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 退却;打退 <span class="encs">[flinch;hang back;retreat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有能助寡人谋而退</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>者，吾与之共知 <span class="diczx2">越国</span>之政。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:退敌(打退敌军);退败(退却失败);退远(斥退疏远);退阵(临阵脱逃);退送(把作祟的鬼神驱退送走);退负(败退);退鬼(驱鬼);退舍(退却;退避)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 引申为减退;下降 <span class="encs">[decline;fade;go down]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以图国用，而进退之。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·小司寇》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹损益也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">退食自公。——<span                     <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>退</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄊㄨㄟˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>tuì <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1542.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">向后倒行、移动。与「进」相对。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「后<b>退</b>」、「撤<b>退</b>」、「进退两难」、「以退为进」、「学如逆水行舟，不进则<b>退</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">畏缩。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「退缩不前」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《论语·先进》：「求也<b>退</b>，故进之。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">谦让。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「谦<b>退</b>」、「退让」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《晋书·卷三七·宗室传·安平献王孚传》：「宣帝执政，常自<b>退</b>损。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">离去、离开。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「退席」、「迟到早<b>退</b>」、「功成身<b>退</b>」。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">辞去职务。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「退休」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷三一·吴太伯世家》：「子胥<b>退</b>而耕于野。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·王安石〈上仁宗皇帝言事书〉：「不敢以其不胜任而辄<b>退</b>之。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">取消、解除。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>退</b>约」、「退租」、「退婚」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《红楼梦·第一六回》：「闻得父母<b>退</b>了前夫，它便一根麻绳，悄悄的自缢了。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">脱去、除去。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>退</b>毛」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《西游记·第二三回》：「把他且浸在后边净水池中，浸<b>退</b>了毛衣，使盐腌著，使阴天下酒。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">减损、消失、衰减。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「退烧」、「退色」、「衰<b>退</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左传·昭公三年》：「火中，寒暑乃<b>退</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋·苏轼〈雪后书北台壁〉诗二首之二：「老病自嗟诗力<b>退</b>，空吟冰柱忆刘叉。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">归还。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「退货」、「退票」、「包<b>退</b>包换」、「货既出门，概不<b>退</b>换。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
