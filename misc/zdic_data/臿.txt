                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>臿</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄚˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>舂去麦皮。《説文&bull;臼部》：&ldquo;臿，舂去麥皮也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>锹，掘土的农具。《方言》卷五：&ldquo;臿，燕之東北，朝鮮、洌水之間謂之𣂁，宋、魏之間謂之鏵，或謂之鍏，江、淮、南楚之間謂之臿，沅、湘之間謂之畚，趙、魏之間謂之喿，東齊謂之梩。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>夹杂；穿插。后作&ldquo;插&rdquo;。《漢書&bull;司馬相如傳》：&ldquo;赤瑕駮犖，雜臿其間。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to separate the grain from the husk</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> séparer le grain de la cosse</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>臿</strong> <span class="dicpy">chā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从臼，从干，干所以臼之。与舂同意。本义:舂去麦皮。引申为舂捣)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pound;pestle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臿，舂出麦皮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">红莲米新臿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">吴景旭</span><span class="smcs">《历代诗话&middot;唐诗&middot;红莲》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 夹杂;穿插。后作&ldquo;插&rdquo; <span class="encs">[be mixed up with;insert]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赤瑕驳犖，杂臿其间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>臿</strong> <span class="dicpy">chā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>铁锹 <span class="encs">[spade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>之王天下也，身执耒臿以为民先。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:臿筑(锹与捣土的杵)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下" target="_blank">未集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集下&amp;bs=臼" target="_blank">臼</a>】
                              臿·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：3</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/81FF.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】楚洽切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】測洽切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音插。【說文】舂去麥皮也。【博雅】臿，舂也。</p>
<p>又與插同。【史記&middot;司馬相如傳】赤瑕駁犖，雜臿其閒。</p>
<p>又與鍤同。【史記&middot;始皇本紀】禹身自持築臿。【註】臿，築牆杵也。【前漢&middot;溝洫志】舉臿爲雲，決渠爲雨。【註】臿，鍫也，所以開渠者也。【淮南子&middot;精神訓】今夫繇者，揭钁臿，負龍土。【註】臿，鏵也。靑州謂之鏵，有刃也。三輔謂之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28B1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨬞</span>。【揚子&middot;方言】江淮南楚之閒謂之臿，沅湘之閒謂之畚。</p>
<p>又【韻會】或作接。【周禮&middot;地官&middot;廩人】大祭祀，共其接盛。【註】接讀爲扱。扱以授舂人舂之也。【正字通】扱以授舂人舂之，非與臿同也。韻會誤。</p>
