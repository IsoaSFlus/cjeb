						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>这</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">這</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄜˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>代词，此，指较近的时间、地点或事物，与“<span class="diczx3">那</span>”相对：～里。～些。～个。～样。</li>    <li>这时候，指说话的同时：他～就来。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>这</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">這</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhèi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhèi.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄟˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ “这（<span class="dicpy">zhè</span>）一”二字的合音，但指数量时不限于一：～个。～点儿。～些年。</p></ol>
