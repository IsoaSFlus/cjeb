						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>挑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiāo.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄧㄠˉ</span></p><ol><li>扁担等两头挂着东西，用肩担着：～土。～夫（旧时以给人挑货物行李为业的人）。</li><li>挑的东西：～担。</li><li>挖取：～荠菜。</li><li>量词，用于成挑儿的东西：一～儿白菜。</li><li>选，拣：～选。～拣。～剔。～肥拣瘦。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">佻</span>”，佻达。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>挑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tiǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄧㄠˇ</span></p><ol><li>用竿子棍棒等的一头举起或支起：～起帘子。</li><li>用条状物或有尖的东西拨开或弄出来：～打。</li><li>拨弄，引动：～拨。～动。～头。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>挑</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本义的挑读 <span class="dicpy">tiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiǎo.mp3"></a></span> ，挖取)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用肩膀担 <span class="encs">[shoulder]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">担挑双草履，壁依一乌藤。——</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《自题传神》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:挑力(挑担。以肩荷物);挑包(肩挑包裹);挑码头(在码头上挑运东西);从对门坡上挑柴火;挑米;挑油;挑谷</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为承担;担当 <span class="encs">[bear;hold]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:挑兵(选募兵);挑补(挑选补官);挑踢(挑选);挑撮(撷章摘句);挑嘴(挑拣食物)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 挑剔 <span class="encs">[pick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">他再不放人一点儿，专会挑人。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:挑抉(挑剔);挑刺</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“佻”</span>。窃取 <span class="encs">[steal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">却至挑天。——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 挖取 <span class="encs">[dig]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时挑野菜和根煮。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜荀鹤</span><span class="smcs">《山中寡妇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 抬举 <span class="encs">[praise or promote sb.to show favour;favour
