                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>渲</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>把水、墨淋在纸上再擦匀的画法：～染（ ➊ 中国画技法之一，用水墨或淡的色彩烘涂画面，以加强艺术效果； ➋ 喻夸大地形容； ➌ 文艺作品中通过多方面的描写、形容或烘托来突出形象）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> add repeated washes of color</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span>  waschen, wässern</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> délavage,délaver</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>渲</strong> <span class="dicpy">xu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，宣声。本义:一种绘画方法，先把颜料涂在纸上，再用笔蘸水涂抹使色彩浓淡适宜) 同本义 <span class="encs">[wash with water colors in the Chinese painting]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以水墨再三而淋之，谓之渲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">郭熙</span><span class="smcs">《林泉高致&middot;画诀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:渲染</span></p>
