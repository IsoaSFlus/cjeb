                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>帕</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄚˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 包头或擦手、脸用的布或绸，多为方形：首～。手～。</p>
<ol>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> turban, kerchief, veil; wrap</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schnupftuch, Taschentuch  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mouchoir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">帕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>帕</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 束额巾 <span class="encs">[scarf;kerchief]</span>。如:帕头(古代男子束发用的头巾);帕额(裹头巾);帕首(同&ldquo;帕头&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 佩巾。通&ldquo;帊&rdquo; <span class="encs">[handkerchief]</span>。如:帕腹(兜肚)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 帷帐 <span class="encs">[curtain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绿树垂垂护宝栏，床头翠帕幂双环。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陈旅</span><span class="smcs">《题春宫倦绣图诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 俗称小方巾为帕 <span class="encs">[towel;handkerchief]</span>。如:手帕</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 压强单位。帕斯卡的简称 <span class="encs">[Pascal]</span>，即单位面积上所受的压力</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>帕</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>缠绕，包裹 <span class="encs">[twine;wrap up; bind up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以锦缠股，以红帕首。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《元和圣德诗》</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">旧时的头巾。</span><span class="gc_yy">《广韵&middot;入声&middot;辖韵》：「帕，帕额，首饰。」</span><span class="gc_yy">元&middot;王逢〈叹病驼〉诗：「老奚首帕短裤靴，手持鞭策涕泗沱。」</span></p></li>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用来擦拭的方形小巾，常随身携带。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「手袙」、「香帕」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;释文莹《湘山野录&middot;卷中》：「『汝哭何因无泪？』渐曰：『以帕拭干。』」</span></p></li>
	<li>
