class="dicpy">chù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 作，建造 <span class="encs">[build]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有俶其城。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·菘高》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:俶建(建造，建立)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 整理 <span class="encs">[put in order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">占既吉而无悔兮，简元辰而俶装。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·张衡传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夕饮饯以俶装，旦出宿而言辞。——<span
