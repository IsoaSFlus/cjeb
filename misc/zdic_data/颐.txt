                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>颐</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">頤</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>面颊，腮：支～。解（<span class="dicpy">jiě</span>）～。～指气使。</li>
	<li>休养，保养：～神。～养。</li>
	<li>文言助词，无义：&ldquo;夥～！涉之为王沈沈者&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cheeks; jaw; chin; rear; to nourish</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ernähren, hegen ,Pobacken, Backen  (S)​,Yi  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> partie inférieure de la joue,nourrir,entretenir,se soigner</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">颐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>颐</strong></p>
<p>頤 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>)，臣( <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> )声。臣，面颊，繁化作&ldquo;颐&rdquo;。本义:下巴) 同本义 <span class="encs">[the lower jaw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颐，颌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言十》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颐，或曰辅车，或曰牙车，或曰颊车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后稷岐颐自求。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《春秋元命苞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">端行颐霤如矢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">手指颐指。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天地》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">擢项颐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;东方朔传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彖曰:&ldquo;颐中有物曰噬嗑。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;噬嗑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大耳横颐方面相，肩查腹满身躯胖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">方额广颐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如: 颐腮(头颅);颐脱(下颚骨脱臼); 颐溜(俯身伸首平视， 下巴如屋溜下垂。形容礼敬的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>颐</strong></p>
<p>頤 <span class="dicpy">y&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>保养 <span class="encs">[keep fit;take care of oneself]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">颐者，养也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;序卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百年曰期颐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恶饮食乎陋巷兮，亦足以颐神而保年。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《闵己赋》</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=頁" target="_blank">頁</a>】
                              頤·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9824.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】與之切【集韻】【韻會】盈之切【正韻】延知切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音移。【說文】顄也。【禮&middot;玉藻】頤霤垂拱。【疏】霤，屋簷，身俯故頭臨前，垂頤如屋霤。【又】端行，頤霤如矢。【釋文】頤音夷。【前漢&middot;賈誼傳】頤指如意。【註】但動頤指麾，則所欲皆如意。</p>
<p>又卦名。【易&middot;頤卦】頤，貞吉，觀頤，自求口實。【疏】頤，養也。【又】初九，觀我朶頤。【註】朶頤者，嚼也。</p>
<p>又期頤，老也。【禮&middot;曲禮】百年曰期頤。【註】飮食居處皆待于養也。</p>
<p>又【小爾雅】頤，深也。</p>
<p>又語助聲。【史記&middot;<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>涉世家】夥頤，涉之爲王沈沈者。【註】楚人謂多爲夥。</p>
<p>又言頤者，助聲之辭也。</p>
<p>又地名。【史記&middot;灌嬰傳】與漢王會頤鄕。【註】苦縣有頤鄕。　【說文】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/268DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦣞</span>。【集韻】亦作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29821.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩠡</span>。</p>
