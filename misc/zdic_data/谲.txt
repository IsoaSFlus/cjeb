						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>谲</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">譎</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jué  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩㄝˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 欺诈，玩弄手段：～诈（奸诈）。诡～（ａ．奇异多变；ｂ．离奇古怪；ｃ．诡诈，狡诈）。狡～。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cunning, crafty, sly, wily</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> betrügerisch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tromper</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">谲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>谲</strong></p><p>譎 <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 欺骗;诈骗 <span class="encs">[cheat;swindle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">譎，权诈也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">譎，欺也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而奸臣犹有所谲其辞矣。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·定法》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此人臣之所以谲主便私也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·孤愤》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:谲主(欺诳主上);谲诳(欺诈诳骗);谲数(诈术)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“决”</span>。判断，判别 <span class="encs">[judge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若夫谲德而定次，量能而授官。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·儒效》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>谲</strong></p><p>譎 <span class="dicpy">jué</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jué.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 诡诈 <span class="encs">[crafty;cunning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋文公</span>谲而不正。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:谲计(奸计;诡计);谲怪(诡谲狡诈);谲数(诡谲的权术);谲诡(怪异;变化多端)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 怪异 <span class="encs">[strange]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瑰异谲诡。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瑰姿谲起。——</span><span class="diczx2">傅毅</span><span class="smcs">《舞赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虚无谲诡，此乱道之根也。——</span><span class="diczx2">王符</span><span class="smcs">《潜夫论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:谲怪(奇特怪异);谲觚(奇诡不正);谲诡(怪诞，变化莫测);谲诡不经(荒唐离奇而不合常理。也称怪诞不经)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 婉曲。尤指谏劝时，不直言过失而隐约其词的 <span class="encs">[indirect]</span>。如:谲谏(托辞委婉，不直指过失的劝谏)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/谲诈">谲诈</a></span></div>
