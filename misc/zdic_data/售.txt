                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>售</strong> <span class="dicpy">shòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，雔省声。雔( <span class="dicpy">chóu</span>)。本义:卖出去)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sell]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">售，卖出手也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">卖之，不可偻售也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·儒效》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾售之，人取之，未尝有言，而独不足子所乎?——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:售卖(卖出;出售)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 买 <span class="encs">[buy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲居之以为利，而高其值，亦无售者。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">问其价，曰:<span class="yhcs">“止四百。”</span>余怜而售之。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《钴鉧潭西小丘记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲居之以为利，而高其直，亦无售者。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:售世(行世)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 引申为酬谢 <span class="encs">[present sb. with a gift as a reward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一日，以花来献，予售以斗酒。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈作喆</span><span class="smcs">《寓简》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 推行;施展 <span class="encs">[carry out one's intrigues]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挟邪作蛊，于是不售。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:售奸(施展阴谋诡计);售迹(发迹。谓扬名立业);售赇(行贿);售谤(散播毁谤他人的言语)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 指女子得嫁 <span class="encs">[remarry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">更遭丧乱嫁不售，一生抱恨长咨嗟。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《负薪行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:售子(妇女改嫁所带之子)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 考试得中 <span class="encs">[achieve]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邑有</span><span class="diczx2">成名</span>者，操童子业，久不售。(谓长久没有考取秀才。)——<span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/552E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】承臭切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】承呪切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音授。【說文】賣去手也，从口雔省聲。【廣韻】賣物出手。【詩·邶風】賈用不售。【戰國策】賣僕妾，售乎閭巷者，良僕妾也。</p><p>又【集韻】與讎同。【詩·大雅】無言不讎。【箋】敎令之出如賣物，物善則其售賈貴，物惡則其售賈賤。【前漢·宣帝紀】每買餅，所從買家，輒大讎。【註】師古曰：讎讀曰售。</p><p>又【集韻】神六切，音孰。儥也。</p><p>又【韻會】時流切，音酬。【古樂府·隴頭水歌】將頓樓蘭膝，就解郅支裘。勿令如李牧，功多信不售。</p><p>
