                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>爪</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>指甲或趾甲：趾端有～。</li>
	<li>鸟兽的脚指：鹰～。～牙（喻党羽，狗腿子）。一鳞半～。</li>
	<li>抓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>爪</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhuǎ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuǎ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄚˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>禽兽的脚（多指有尖甲的）：鸡～子。狗～子。</li>
	<li>像爪的东西，这个锅有三个～儿。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> claw, nail, talon; animal feet</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kralle  (Bio)​,Fang, Klaue  (S)​,Kralle  (S)​,Radikal Nr. 87 = Klaue, Kralle (Variante: 爫)​</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> griffes,serres,égratigner,87e classif.</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">爪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>爪</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。按甲骨文和小篆字形，&ldquo;又&rdquo;是手，两点表手爪甲形。本义:人的指甲、趾甲)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[nail]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爪，人之指叉或亦通作爪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《六书故》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:爪甲(指或趾前的角质硬壳);爪翦(修剪手指甲或脚趾甲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 鸟兽的脚趾 <span class="encs">[claw;talon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蚓无爪牙之利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虫已在爪下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鳞爪飞扬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:虎爪;猫爪;前爪;张牙舞爪;鹰爪;爪嘴(鸟类的爪和嘴);爪吻(鸷禽猛兽的爪和嘴。喻帮凶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 像爪的东西 <span class="encs">[claw-like thing]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:铁锚四爪皆折;棘爪、掣爪或制动爪</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>爪</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 抓，搔 <span class="encs">[clutch;grap; scratch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">爪其肤以验其生枯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《种树郭橐驼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 剪指甲或趾甲 <span class="encs">[trim one&#39;s nails or toes]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小臣爪足。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;丧大记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:爪足(剪脚趾甲);爪手翦须(修剪手指甲和胡须);爪翦(修剪手指甲和脚趾甲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zhuǎ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuǎ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
