                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>木</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">mù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>树类植物的通称：树～。乔～。灌～。～石（树木和石头，喻没有知觉和情感的东西）。缘～求鱼。</li><li>木料、木制品：～材。～器。～刻。～匠。～已成舟。</li><li>棺材：棺～。</li><li>质朴：～讷（朴实迟钝）。</li><li>呆笨：～鸡。</li><li>感觉不灵敏，失去知觉：麻～。</li><li>中国古代乐器八音之一。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">木</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>木</strong> <span class="dicpy">mù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形。象树木形。上为枝叶，下为树根。<span class="yhcs">“木”</span>是汉字的一个部首。从<span class="yhcs">“木”</span>的字表示树木或木器的名称。本义:树木)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tree]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">木，冒也。冒地而生。东方之行，从草，下象其根。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">五行，木之为言触也。阳气动跃，触地而出也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">木者，春生之性。农之本也。——<span class="smcs">《春秋繁露》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一树十获者木也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·权修》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">庄子</span>行于山中，见大木，枝叶盛茂。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·山木》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">舜</span>之居深山之中，与木石居，与鹿豕游。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·尽心上》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">草木之花。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鸟兽木石。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>无长木。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或在木杪。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:花木(供观赏的花和树木);木石(树木与岩石);木化石(树木的化石);木芽(草木的嫩芽);木表(树木的外层;树梢);木披(因果实太多而树枝折断);木斧(伐木斧头)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 木料，木材 <span class="encs">[timber;wood]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朽木不可雕也。——<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我善治木，曲者中钩，直者应绳。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·马蹄》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">径寸之木。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">倚一横木。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">木格贮之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">木为之者。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:木猫(木制捕鼠器);木老鸦(战船上使用的一种轻便兵器);木驴(一种装有轮轴的木制刑具，可载犯人游街示众，用于剐刑);木弓(木制的弓);木瓦(覆屋面的木板);木尺(木制尺);木功(木材建筑，制作工艺)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 五行之一 <span class="encs">[wood]</span>。如:木王(春季以木为主宰);木行(五行中的木德);木气(五行气之一;肝气)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 树叶 <span class="encs">[leaf]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无边落木萧萧下，不尽</span><span class="diczx2">长江</span>滚滚来。—— <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《登高》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:木落(叶落);木脉(叶脉);木落归本</p><p><span
