                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>宕</strong> <span class="dicpy">dàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 洞穴;洞屋 <span class="encs">[cave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宕，过也。一曰洞屋。从宀，碭省声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，字从宀，洞屋当为本训。洞屋者，四围无障蔽之谓。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为空旷或昏暗。如:宕冥(辽阔无垠的天空;迟钝昏昧)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 坑洼 <span class="encs">[pit]</span>。如:宕户(采石矿的工人);宕匠(采石工)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>宕</strong> <span class="dicpy">dàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 穿过;通过 <span class="encs">[pass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">邗子</span>寻犬，宕入仙穴。——<span class="smcs">《列仙传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 引申为流荡;游荡 <span class="encs">[loaf about]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">回过曰宕。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">长狄兄弟三人佚宕中国。——<span class="smcs">《谷梁传·文公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">借问叹者谁，言是宕子妻。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《怨歌行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:宕子(荡子。到处流浪的人);宕宕(无定止的样子);宕落(流动而富于变化)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 放纵，不受约束 <span class="encs">[indulge]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">性豪宕，不拘细行。——<span class="smcs">《金史·姬汝作传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:宕往(豪爽不羁);宕迈(放逸豪爽)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 耷拉着;悬挂 <span class="encs">[droop]</span>。如:宽皮宕肉</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 拖延 <span class="encs">[procrastinate]</span>。如:不许宕过年;宕挨，宕延(拖延);宕账(悬欠未还的账)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a
