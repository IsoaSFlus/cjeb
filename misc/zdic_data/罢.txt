                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">罢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>罢</strong></p>
<p>罷 <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从网，从能。表示用网捕住有贤能的人。&ldquo;网&rdquo;在字的上部楷书多写作&ldquo;罒&rdquo;。本义:罢官;免去;解除)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[dismiss from office]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可以罢官之无事者，去器之无用者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;仲冬》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">窦太后</span>大怒，乃罢逐 <span class="diczx2">赵绾</span>、 <span class="diczx2">王臧</span>等。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏其武安侯列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻君罢官意，我抱</span><span class="diczx2">汉川</span>湄。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《赠汉阳辅录事二首》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">徐阶</span>罢相里居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;海瑞传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">复请之，吾辈无生理，而主谳者亦各罢去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7F77.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】薄蟹切【集韻】【韻會】部買切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2278E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢞎</span>。【說文】遣有罪也。从罔，能。言有賢能而入罔，卽貰遣之。周禮曰：議能之辟。</p>

<p>又【集韻】羆，或省作罷。詳羆字註。</p>
