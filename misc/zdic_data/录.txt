                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>录</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">録</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>记载，抄写：记～。载～。抄～。～供。</li>
	<li>记载言行或事物的书册：语～。目～。回忆～。</li>
	<li>采取，任用：～取。收～。～用。甄～（经审查鉴别而任用）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> copy, write down, record</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> abschreiben, Protokoll führen, registrieren, einschreiben, eintragen ,beschäftigen, einstellen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> registre,enregistrer,inscrire</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">录</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>录</strong></p>
<p>録 <span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从金，录声。本义:绿色金属)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[green metal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">録，金色也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 记载言行事物的册籍 <span class="encs">[record]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">录，记之具也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆辨其物而奠其录。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;职币》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:言行录;名人录</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 次第，次序 <span class="encs">[order]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今大国越录。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>录</strong></p>
<p>録 <span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 总领 <span class="encs">[lead;head]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">融</span>为太尉，并录尚书事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;章帝纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:录公(对总领台阁尚书事者的敬称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 抄写(含有有选择地照样誊写的意味) <span class="encs">[copy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">手自笔录。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余之手录。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抄录(抄写);录黄(宋代中书省枢密院承旨抄录颁送门下省的文件)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 记载 <span class="encs">[record]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>录内而略外。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;隐公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">录功而与官，则莫敢索官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:采录(采集并记录);笔录(用笔记录);录囚(审讯登录囚犯的罪状)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 采取 <span class="encs">[take;adopt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君既若见录。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:录用(录取任用);录遗(选录遗才)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 记;惦记 <span class="encs">[keep thinking about; remember with concern]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君既若见录，不久望君来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 逮捕 <span class="encs">[arrest; seize]</span></p>
