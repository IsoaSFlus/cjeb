                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>针</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">針</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhēn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>缝织衣物引线用的一种细长的工具：～线。</li><li>细长像针的东西：时～。</li><li>用针扎治病：～灸。</li><li>注射用的器具和药物：～头。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">针</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>针</strong></p><p>針、鍼 <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从金，从十。最初用的竹针，写作<span class="yhcs">“箴”</span>，后来有了金属的针，写作<span class="yhcs">“鍼”</span>(针)。本义:缝衣的用具)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[needle]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 缝缀衣物的用具。也叫<span class="yhcs">“引线”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鍼，所以缝也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作针。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挫鍼治繲，足以糊口。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·人间世》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若当针，不过一二处。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·华陀传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">月小看针暗，云开见缕明。——</span><span class="diczx2">陈叔宝</span><span class="smcs">《七夕宴玄圃诗》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:针头线脑(做针线活的零碎物件);针巧(针线的工夫精巧);针缕(即<span class="yhcs">“针线”</span>。也作<span class="yhcs">“鍼缕”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“鍼镂”</span>);针关(针孔);针颖(针尖);针头(针尖。借指绣针);针锋(针尖);针帖(插针用的线板);针虱(针马虱。比喻细微之物);针尖不倒(指手不停针);针衣(藏针用的带)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 医疗用具。用石或金属做成</p><p><span class="diczx1">鍼石运乎手爪。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·文苑赵壹传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:针石(用以治病的石针);针艾(即针灸);针膏(针饵。针灸和药物);针剂(针灸和药剂)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 针状的东西。如:针芒(针尖。比喻细微。也作<span class="yhcs">“鍼芒”</span>);针水(稻种发芽后其芽露出水面);针列(密集如细针排列)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 刻度盘上的指针 <span class="encs">[hand]</span>。如:时针，分针，秒针;针路(罗盘针指的航路)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 缝衣服、编织时，针穿布的一下 <span class="encs">[stitch]</span>。如:在袜子上缝两针;织漏一针;伤口缝了四针</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>针</strong></p><p>針 <span class="dicpy">zhēn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhēn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 缝 <span class="encs">[sew]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因命染人与针女，先制两裘赠二君。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《醉后狂言酬赠萧殷二协律》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:针线娘(替富人做针线活儿的妇女);针黹(缝纫、刺绣等针线活儿);针指(缝纫、刺线之类的针线活计);针姑(制衣之神);针女(缝纫女工);针匠(裁缝);针纫(缝纫);针妇(俗名纺织娘。即络纬);针绝(绝妙的缝纫、刺绣手艺);针缀(缝纫)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 刺 <span class="encs">[stab]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鍼，刺也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">针之立死。——<span class="smcs">《物类相感志》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:针笔匠(在人身上刺画花纹的技师);针史(唐末无赖之徒于身上刺字，录入文字或记经历，称为<span class="yhcs">“针史”</span>);针绣(刺绣)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 以针刺治病 <span
