                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>尹</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形。左边一竖表示笔，右边是<span class="yhcs">“又”</span>(手)，象手拿笔，以表示治事。本义:治理) 同本义 <span class="encs">[administer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尹，治也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以尹天下。——<span class="smcs">《左传·定公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:尹京(治理京畿);尹祭(古代用于祭祀的切割方正的干肉)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>尹</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 旧时官名 <span class="encs">[magistrate;a title in old times]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">令尹</span><span class="diczx2">子文</span>，三仕为令尹，无喜色。——<span class="smcs">《论语·公冶长》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不以非</span><span class="diczx2">郑尹</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拥至尹前。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">胡仔</span><span class="smcs">《苕溪渔隐丛话》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有卿尹。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:府尹;京兆尹;尹氏(官名。掌管册命臣工之事);尹寺(宦官);尹长(官长)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓。如:尹午(复姓)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
