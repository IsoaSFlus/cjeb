                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">都</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>都</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>大都市：～市。～会。通～大邑。</li>	<li>一国的最高行政机关所在的地方，京城：首～。国～。京～。建～。</li>	<li>美好：&ldquo;雍容闲雅，甚～&rdquo;。～丽。～雅。</li>	<li>总：～为一集。</li>	<li>居：&ldquo;～卿相之位&rdquo;。</li>	<li>古代称头目、首领。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>都</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dōu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄡˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>全，完全：～要。功课学得～不错。</li>	<li>表示语气的加重：一动～不动。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">都</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>都</strong> <span class="dicpy">dōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 全部 <span class="encs">[all]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示全部包括在内</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">渐黄昏，清角吹寒，都在空城。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">姜夔</span><span class="smcs">《扬州慢》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常愿天下有情人都成眷属。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:你的话并不都对;都子(乞丐);都把势(总头目)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 跟&ldquo;是&rdquo;字合用，说明理由。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:都是你老磨蹭，害得我们来晚了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用来加强语气，表示某事物极端的、异常的或不大可能有的情况或事例 <span class="encs">[even]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:今天天气真怪，中午比早晨都冷</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表示已经 <span class="encs">[already]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茶都凉了，赶快喝吧!</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">dū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>都</strong> <span class="dicpy">dū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从邑，者声。从&ldquo;邑&rdquo;，表示与城市有关。本义:建有宗庙的城邑)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[capital]</span>，周时各国把国都叫国，把有宗庙或先君神主的城叫都，没有的叫邑</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都，有先君之旧宗庙曰都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">距闰王百里为都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡邑有宗庙先君之主曰都，无曰邑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公二十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先王之制，大都不过参国之一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田畴秽，都邑露。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;富国》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:都鄙(旧时天子宗亲及公卿大夫的采邑);都家(周王朝分封给子弟及公卿大夫的采邑);都亭(都邑中的传舍);都甸(都邑郊外之地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 邦国的都城，国都 <span class="encs">[capital]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国都曰都，都者，国君所居，人所都会也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兴复</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>室，还于旧都。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秋九月，权迁都</span><span class="diczx2">建业</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;吴主传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都门帐饮无绪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奠都;定都;故都;国都;京都(旧时称国都);旧都(故都);迁都;行都(旧时指临时的首都);都下(京都之下。即京城);都内(京城内的府库);都邑(首都);都辇(京师);都畿(京都及其附近的地区)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为城市 <span class="encs">[big city]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">傅之其人，通邑大都。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">召有司案图，指从此以往十五都予</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:都道(都城的街道);都阃(都市郭门。引申为守卫城市的统帅。阃:门坎);都庄(都城的大道)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 周代王室子弟及三公的封地、采地 <span class="encs">[fief;feud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡造都鄙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司徒》</span>。注:&ldquo;都鄙，王子弟公卿大夫采地，其界曰都。鄙所居也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邦都之赋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宰》</span>。注:&ldquo;四郊去国百里，邦甸二百里，家削三百里，邦县四百里;邦都五百里。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都城过百雉，国之害也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:都鄙，都家(周公卿、大夫、王子弟的采邑，封地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 国家或帝国的行政区域或行政分区。明、清时基层行政区划是图，图下分十庄，图有地保;图上设都，相当于区或乡 <span class="encs">[region;countryside]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">族长</span><span class="diczx2">严振先</span>，乃城中十二都的乡约，平日最怕 <span class="diczx2">严</span>大老官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 头目，首领 <span class="encs">[head]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:都老(岭南少数民族的首领之称);都长(唐末帝王侍卫军的首长);都纂(总编纂);都鬼(迷信说法，阴间鬼的头领);都判官(迷信说法，阴间主管生死簿的官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 唐、五代宋初军队编制单位，以百人或千人为都 <span class="encs">[hundred persons]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:都伯(统领百人的军官);都排(百人长。都是军队编制名称，唐和五代，都为千人，宋时缩为百人);都司(绿旗兵中的营级武官，正四品);都尉(官名。系汉代辅助郡守掌管军事的官员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 吏的俗称 <span class="encs">[official]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:都护(官名);都监(官名);都府(中央所属各部、府的长官);都校(五代时武官名);都将(后魏铸钱官);都统制(官名。南宋建炎元年始置);都察院(明清时最高监察机构);都老爷(清朝对都察院的给事中，御史的口头称呼);都头(州县的捕盗头目;总头目);都知(客店或酒店的管事人员)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>都</strong> <span class="dicpy">dū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 建都 <span class="encs">[found a capital]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">据天下之雄图，都六合之上游。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《封建论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 聚集 <span class="encs">[accumulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都，凡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都部者，都统其众也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;齐武王縯传》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水泽所聚谓之都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水经注&middot;水文注》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顷择其遗文，都为一集。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;与吴质书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:都居(水积聚的地方);都场(聚会的地方);都泽(水流汇聚的草泽地带)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 率领 <span class="encs">[command]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:都督(统率，率领);都部(统率兵众)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>都</strong> <span class="dicpy">dū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 美好的样子 <span class="encs">[elegant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身长八尺，仪貌都雅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;孙韶传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不见于子都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;山有扶苏》</span>。传:&ldquo;世之美好者也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妻子衣服丽都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:都冶(美艳的样子);都雅(优美文雅的样子);都长(指人美貌而性情温厚);都鄙(文雅与卑陋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 大 <span class="encs">[great]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">军惊而怀都舍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;察今》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中有都柱，傍行八道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:都匠(大匠);都酿(大的酿造作坊);都坑(公共厕所);都灶(蒸炊用的大灶)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>都</strong> <span class="dicpy">dū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">副</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 统统，完全 <span class="encs">[all]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">都，凡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:都齐(完全，统统);都数(总数);都领(总领，总管);都邮(古指邮驿总站);都来(统统;总共;总计);都凡(总共)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">dōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">大城市。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「花<b>都</b>」、「港<b>都</b>」、「通都大邑」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">中央政府及地方政府的所在地。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「首<b>都</b>」、「國<b>都</b>」、「京<b>都</b>」、「行<b>都</b>」。</span></p></li>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如漢代有都稽，明代有都穆。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">定都。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷七&middot;項羽本紀》：「項王自立為西楚霸王，王九郡，<b>都</b>彭城。」</span></p></li>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">總計。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文選&middot;曹丕&middot;與吳質書》：「頃撰其遺文，<b>都</b>為一集。」</span></p></li>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">優雅、優美。</span><span class="gc_yy">《詩經&middot;鄭風&middot;有女同車》：「彼美孟姜，洵美且<b>都</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《聊齋志異&middot;卷一&middot;嬌娜》：「一少年出，丰采甚<b>都</b>。」</span></p></li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">都</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷六">卷六</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷六&amp;swbs=邑">邑</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/90FD.svg">                          <p>有先君之舊宗廟曰都。从邑者聲。周禮：距國五百里爲都。當孤切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/90FD_6954.svg" /><span class="swbf">(都)</span><span class="ywyy">有先君之舊宗廟曰都。</span><span class="swdz">左傳曰：凡邑有宗廟先君之主曰都，無曰邑。周禮大司徒注曰：都鄙者，王子弟公卿大夫采地，其畍曰都。鄙，所居也。載師注曰：家邑，大夫之采地。小都，卿之采地。大都，公之采地，王子弟所食邑也。大宰八則注曰：都鄙，公卿大夫之采邑，王子弟所食邑。周召毛耼畢原之屬在畿內者，祭祀者其先君社稷五祀。按據杜氏釋例：大曰都，小曰邑。雖小而有宗廟先君之主曰都，尊其所居而大之也。又按左氏言有宗廟先君之主曰都。許改云有先君之舊宗廟，則必如晉之曲沃故絳而後可偁都，恐非左氏意也。左氏與周官合。</span><span class="ywyy">从邑者聲。</span><span class="swdz">當孤切。五部。</span><span class="ywyy">周禮：歫國五百里爲都。</span><span class="swdz">此周禮說也。周禮載師注引司馬法曰：王國百里爲郊，二百里爲州，三百里爲野，四百里爲縣，五百里爲都。大宰注曰：邦中，在城郭者。四郊，去國百里。邦甸，二百里。家削，三百里。邦縣，四百里。邦都，五百里。</span></p>                          </div>
