						<p>● <strong>抽</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄡˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>把中间的取出：把信～出。</li>	<li>从事物中提出一部分：～签。～调（<span class="dicpy">di&agrave;o</span>）。～查。～头。～薪止沸。</li>	<li>吸：～烟。～水。～泣。～咽。</li>	<li>概括：～象。～演。</li>	<li>减缩：～缩。</li>	<li>引出，长出：～青。～芽。～穗。～纱。</li>	<li>用细长的、软的东西打：用鞭子～。</li>	<li>痉挛：～搐。～风。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>抽</strong> <span class="dicpy">chōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，由声。本义:拔出，抽出)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 拔出，把夹在中间的东西取出 <span class="encs">[take out (from/in/between)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抽，拔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">每射，抽矢菆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">挈水若抽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天地》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注:&ldquo;引也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左旋右抽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;清人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抽刀断水水更流。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《宣州谢眺楼饯别校书叔云》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抽简禄马(算命);抽匣(抽剑出匣);抽毫(抽笔出套);抽宝剑;抽刀</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 从全部里取出一部分;拔除 <span class="encs">[take a part from the lot]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言抽其棘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抽其半。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;丧服传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抽分(从中取得一分);抽功夫(抽空);抽暇(抽空)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引，引出<span class="encs">[draw forth]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:抽引(抽取引出);抽导(引导);抽茧(抽引茧丝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 长出 <span class="encs">[put forth;sprout;bud]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:抽条</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用长条的东西打 <span class="encs">[lash;whip]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:抽陀螺;鞭子一抽，牲口就走快了</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 抒发 <span class="encs">[express]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:抽思(抒发情思);抽秘骋妍(拨好深意，施展美才)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 选择;挑选 <span class="encs">[select]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:抽拨(选择调拨);抽拣(挑选)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 吸 <span class="encs">[suck]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:抽抽搭搭(一吸一顿哽咽);我有好烟，你抽吗?</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">抽</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>抽</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄔㄡ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>chōu <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/4693.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">引出。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「抽絲」、「抽紗」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《廣韻&middot;平聲&middot;尤韻》：「<b>抽</b>，引也。或作紬，紬引其端緒也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">將放在中間的東西取出。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;襄公二十六年》：「戌怒<b>抽</b>戈逐王子圍，弗及。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈宣州謝朓樓餞別校書叔雲〉詩：「<b>抽</b>刀斷水水更流，舉杯銷愁愁更愁。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">從全部中取出一部分。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「抽稅」、「抽空」、「抽籤」、「從一疊信中<b>抽</b>出一封來。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">除去。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儀禮&middot;喪服》：「緦者十五升，<b>抽</b>其半。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">萌發、長出。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「稻子抽穗」、「<b>抽</b>出嫩芽」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈〈苦寒〉詩：「草木不復<b>抽</b>，百味失苦甜。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">吸。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「抽菸」、「<b>抽</b>水」、「倒<b>抽</b>了一口氣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">收、縮。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「抽筋」、「抽搐」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈李頎秀才善畫山以兩軸見寄仍有詩次韻答之〉：「詩句對君難出手，雲泉勸我早抽身。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">鞭、打。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「拿鞭子<b>抽</b>你一頓。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
