<p>◎ <strong>佗</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuō</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;拕&rdquo;。拖 <span class="encs">[dishevel]</span></p>

<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>佗</strong> <span class="dicpy">tuō</span></p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">代</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示远指，别的，其他的 <span class="encs">[other]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">制，岩邑也，</span><span class="diczx2">虢叔</span>死焉，佗邑唯命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公元年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此无佗故也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大戴礼&middot;礼察》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">佗日指动，必食异物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;郑世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:佗人(别人);佗日(他日;往日;将来的某一天);佗方(他方;他乡);佗生(他生，来生);佗年(他年，将来);佗志(异心);佗故(他故，别的原因)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示第三人称 <span class="encs">[he]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蒙佗恩(承蒙他人恩力)</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>佗</strong> <span class="dicpy">tu&oacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tu&oacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，它声。本义:负荷) 同&ldquo;驮&rdquo;。负有重担或负载 <span class="encs">[carry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">佗，负何也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
