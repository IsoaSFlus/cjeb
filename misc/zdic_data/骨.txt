                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>骨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>人和脊椎动物体内支持身体、保护内脏的坚硬组织：～头。～骼（全身骨头的总称）。～节。～肉（①指最亲近的有血统关系的人，亦称&ldquo;骨血&rdquo;；②喻紧密相连，不可分割的关系）。～干（<span class="dicpy">g&agrave;n</span>）。</li>	<li>像骨的东西（指支撑物体的骨架）：伞～。扇～。</li>	<li>指文学作品的理论和笔力：～力（①雄健的笔力；②刚强不屈的气概）。风～（古典文艺理论术语，指文章的艺术风格，亦指作品的风神骨髓）。</li>	<li>指人的品质、气概：侠～。～气。</li></ol><p>● <strong>骨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gū.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～朵儿（<span class="dicpy">duor </span>）〕尚未开放的花朵。</li>	<li>〔～碌〕滚动（&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">碌</span>&rdquo;读轻声）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">骨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>骨</strong> <span class="dicpy">gǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从冎( <span class="dicpy">guǎ)，</span>小篆象去掉肉的骨形，意思是&ldquo;剐&rdquo;。从肉，表示骨与肉相连。&ldquo;骨&rdquo;是汉字部首之一，从&ldquo;骨&rdquo;的字多与骨头和人体有关。本义:骨头)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骨，肉之覈也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骨者，髓之府。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;脉要精微论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">风生木与骨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;四时》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">马已死，买其骨五百金。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以刀刺骨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;安危》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而额焦烂不可辨，左膝以下筋骨尽脱矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 骨头，脊椎动物的骨骼的各个坚硬的部分。解剖学名词。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:骨空(即骨孔);骨腔;尺骨;肋骨;龙骨(鸟类的胸骨);听骨;颌骨</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 比喻在物体内部支撑的架子。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:钢骨水泥;船的龙骨;主心骨;骨扇(折扇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 人的躯干 <span class="encs">[torso]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:骨相(依人的骨胳与相貌，可推测其穷通祸福之五星);骨骸(形体)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 尸骨，尸首。指死去的人 <span class="encs">[dead]</span></p>
