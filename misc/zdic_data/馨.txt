                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">馨</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>馨</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>散布很远的香气：～香。如兰之～。</li>
	<li>喻长存的英名：垂～千祀。</li>
	<li>助词，作用同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/样">样</a>&rdquo;：宁～（这样，如此）。宁～儿（原意是&ldquo;这样的儿子&rdquo;，后用以赞美孩子或子弟）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fragrant, aromatic; distant fragrance</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> duftend</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> parfum,senteur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">馨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>馨</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从香，殸声。殸，籀文&ldquo;磬&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">q&igrave;ng</span>)。本义:芳香，散布很远的香气)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wide-spread fragrance]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">馨，香之远闻者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其德足以昭其馨香。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丹木五岁五味乃馨成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;西山经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔酒既清，尔淆既馨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;凫鷖》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:&ldquo;馨，香之远闻也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟吾德馨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:馨烈(芳香浓烈);馨逸(香气洋溢;芳香远播)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 比喻声誉流芳后世 <span class="encs">[good reputation]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">化盛隆周，垂馨千祀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:馨烈(比喻流芳的事业);馨德(芳馨久传的美德和教化)引申为美<span class="encs">[beauty]</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黍稷非馨，明德惟馨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;君陈》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>馨</strong> <span class="dicpy">xīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈助〉</p>
<p>有赞美的意思。后来多&ldquo;宁馨&rdquo;(如此，这样) 连用</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为问中华学道者，几人雄猛得宁馨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《赠日本僧智藏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:宁馨儿</span></p>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">散播到遠方的香氣。</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辭&middot;屈原&middot;九歌&middot;山鬼》：「被石蘭兮帶杜衡，析芳<b>馨</b>兮遺所思。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;劉兼〈蓮塘霽望〉詩：「新秋菡萏發紅英，向晚風飄滿郡<b>馨</b>。」</span></p></li>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下" target="_blank">戌集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集下&amp;bs=香" target="_blank">香</a>】
                              馨·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/99A8.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】呼<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】醯經切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20DD3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠷓</span>。【玉篇】香遠聞也。【書&middot;酒誥】黍稷非馨，明德維馨。【詩&middot;大雅】爾殽旣馨。</p>
<p>又【周頌】有椒其馨。【楚辭&middot;九歌】折芳馨兮遺所思。【韓愈&middot;答張徹詩】寒狖酸骨怨，怪花醉䰟馨。</p>
<p>又【集韻】虛映切，音釁。晉人以爲語助。【晉書&middot;王衍傳】何物老嫗，生此寧馨兒。【世說新語】王朗之雪中詣王螭，持其臂。螭曰：冷如鬼手馨，强來捉人臂。【通雅】寧<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20DD3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠷓</span>寧馨，此呼語辭，今讀能亨，亦云那，向有平去二音，可平可仄，古人多假借字也。</p>
