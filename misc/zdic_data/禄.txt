                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>禄</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>古代官吏的俸给：～米。～位（借指官职）。～蠹（指追求官禄的人）。～食。俸～。无功受～。</li><li>福：“儿已薄～相，幸复得此妇”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">禄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>禄</strong> <span class="dicpy">lù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从示，录声。本义:福气、福运)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[happiness]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禄，福也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">福禄如茨。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·瞻彼洛矣》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使女受禄如天。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·少牢馈食礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又重之以寡君之不禄。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">短折不禄。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其胤维何?天被尔禄。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·既醉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而后可以殿邦国，同福禄，来远人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十一年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">儿已薄禄相。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:禄祚(福分和寿命);禄命(古代宿命论者认为人生的盛衰，祸福、寿天、贵贱等均由天定);禄相(有禄的相的样子。旧时相术认为人的形体、气色等与人的贵贱贫富、天寿等有关);禄气(食禄之气运)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 官吏的俸给 <span class="encs">[salary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">恽</span>幸有余禄。—— <span class="diczx2">杨恽</span><span class="smcs">《报孙会宗书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">你就起个名字，叫作禄蠹。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:高官厚禄;爵禄(爵位和俸禄);薄禄(薪水);禄蠹(领取官俸的蛀虫);禄仕(为食俸禄而居官);禄糈(古代官吏的俸给)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 禄位 <span class="encs">[salary and position]</span>。如:禄利(爵禄之利);禄饵(以禄位为诱饵);禄荫(凭借先人余荫而取得的禄位)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 赏赐物 <span class="encs">[reward]</span>。如:禄料(料钱。唐宋间官吏除岁禄、月俸外的一种食料津贴)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“录”</span>。册籍 <span class="encs">[record]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆辨其物而奠其禄。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官下·职币》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>禄</strong> <span class="dicpy">lù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 给予俸禄 <span class="encs">[give salary]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故，上贤，禄天下;次贤，禄一国;下贤，禄田邑。——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:禄绝(死的讳辞);禄亲(禄养。以俸禄养亲);禄使(给俸禄供使用);禄勋(给予有功者以俸禄)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“录”</span>。总领 <span class="encs">[lead;head]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">社稷不定，臣禄</span><span class="diczx2">齐国</span>之政。——<span class="smcs">《管子·大匡》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禄米">禄米</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禄位">禄位</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/禄隐">禄隐</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">祿</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                          <span class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“禄”字头，请参考“祿”字。)</span>                        
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下" target="_blank">午集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下&amp;bs=示" target="_blank">示</a>】
                              祿·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/797F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>盧谷切，音鹿。【說文】福也。【廣韻】善也。【詩·商頌】百祿是何。【箋】當擔負天之多福。</p><p>又俸也。居官所給廩。【禮·王制】位定然後祿之。【周禮·天官·大宰】以八柄詔王馭羣臣，二曰祿。【疏】以功詔祿。祿，所以富臣下，故云。【禮·曲禮】士曰不祿。【疏】士祿以代耕。不祿，不終其祿也。</p><p>又司祿，星名。【史記·天官書】文昌宮六曰司祿。</p><p>又回祿，火神。【左傳·昭十八年】鄭禳火於回祿。</p><p>又姓。紂子祿父後。</p><p>又州名。廣南化外有福祿州，唐總章二年置。</p><p>又天祿，獸名。【前漢·西域傳註】似鹿，長尾，一角者爲天祿，兩角者爲辟邪。漢有天祿閣，因獸立名，藏祕書。</p><p>又【廣韻】龍玉切，音錄。祿祿，形貌爲禮也。陸德明說。</p><p>又叶錄直切，音力。【漢昭帝冠詞】摛顯先帝之光耀，以承皇天之嘉祿。欽奉仲春之吉辰，普遵大道之郊域。</p><p>又叶歷各切，音落。【前漢·敘傳】位過厥任，鮮終其祿。博之翰音，鼓妖先作。</p>
