                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">显</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>显</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">顯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>露在外面容易看出来：明～。～著。～学（著名的学说或学派）。</li>
	<li>表现，露出：～露。～示。～山露水（喻显示自己，引人注目）。</li>
	<li>旧时称有权势的或有名声地位的：～贵。～赫。～要。</li>
	<li>敬辞，称先人：～考（已去世的父亲）。～妣（已去世的母亲）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> manifest, display; evident, clear</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> sichtbar, zeigen, aussehen  (V)​,deutlich, offensichtlich  (Adj)​,Xian  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> manifester,apparaître,évident,visible</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">显</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>显</strong></p>
<p>顯 <span class="dicpy">xiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从页( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>)从显，显亦声。今字从页，与人头有联系。本义:头上的装饰品)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ornaments on the head]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">显，头明饰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:「头明饰者，冕弁充耳之类。」</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表面，外面 <span class="encs">[surface]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所说阴为厚利而显为名高者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>显</strong></p>
<p>顯 <span class="dicpy">xiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 明显;显示得很清楚 <span class="encs">[obvious;apparent;noticable;self-evident]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">显，见也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。注:「显，明见也。」</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天维显思。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;敬之》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不敢显然布币行礼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故法莫如显，而术不欲见。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:显轿(没有顶篷、扶手的大椅式轿子。也叫亮轿，明舆);显亮(敞亮)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 显赫，显达 <span class="encs">[be illustrious and influential]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">高爵显位。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张溥</span><span class="smcs">《五人墓碑记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赠谥美显</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:显融(显达荣耀);显人(闻名天下的人);显位(崇高的职位);显官(居高位的官员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 明，光明 <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我久不画了。如今住的房屋不比园里的显亮，所以没兴头画。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:显晦(明与暗。喻出仕与隐居);显证(明证)</span></p>
