                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>协</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">協</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄝˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>共同合作，和洽：～商。～定。～和。～调（<span class="dicpy">ti&aacute;o </span>）。～议。</li>
	<li>帮助，辅助：～助。～同。～理。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> be united; cooperate</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> fördern, assistieren, helfen  (V)​,kooperieren, zusammen arbeiten  (V)​,gemeinsam, zusammen, mit vereinten Kräften  (Adj)​,helfen ,verbinden, anfügen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> assister,aider</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">协</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>协</strong></p>
<p>協 <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从办，从十。办亦声。办( <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> )表示同力。十，表示众多。合起来表示众人同力。本义:和睦;融洽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[harmonious;in amity with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">協，众之同和也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">协和万邦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span>。传:&ldquo;合也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">协用五纪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span>。传:&ldquo;和也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今</span><span class="diczx2">刘表</span>新亡，二子不协。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与彼协心。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">协规同力。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">声律相协而八音生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;数》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:协允(合理公平);协气(和气);协恭(友好而同心协力);协睦;协畅;协调;协泰(协和安泰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 合;共同 <span class="encs">[joint;common]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纪农协功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span>。注:&ldquo;同也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔无共怒，协比谗言予一人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚下》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:&ldquo;汝无共怒我，合比凶人而妄言。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:协谋(共同谋略);协爱(共同热爱);协庆(共同庆贺);协比(勾结在一起做坏事。即朋比)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>协</strong></p>
<p>協 <span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 调和;调整 <span class="encs">[mediate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">协此四国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;孔子闲居》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:协正(调整而使之正确);协序(调和使有规律);协律(调和音乐律吕，使之和谐);协顺(调和使如意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 帮助 <span class="encs">[assist]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:协饷(由税收富裕的省调济拨款协助贫困省);协济(接济);协飞熊(相传周文王梦见飞熊，占卜得要贤臣。后果遇姜尚。比喻帝王得贤之征兆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 顺服 <span class="encs">[obey]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上帝时歆，下民祗协。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;微子之命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:协从;协附(依附);协服</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用同&ldquo;胁&rdquo;。逼迫 <span class="encs">[force]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余皆协从。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金声玉振集》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 怀抱 <span class="encs">[cherish]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:协孕(怀孕);协私网上;协情</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 勾结 <span class="encs">[collude with]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:协党(结党);协比(勾结)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>协</strong></p>

<p>〈名〉</p>
<p> 清末新军的编制单位，在镇之下。三营为标，两标一协。相当于后代的旅 <span class="encs">[brigade]</span></p>
