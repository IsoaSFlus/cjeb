                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>织</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">織</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>用丝、麻、棉纱、毛线等编成布或衣物等：～布。编～。～女（❶织布、织绸的女子；❷指织女星）。棉～物。</li>    <li>引申为构成：罗～罪名。</li>    <li>用染丝织成的锦或彩绸：～锦。～文（即“织锦”）。～贝（织成贝文的锦）。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>织</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">織</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhì.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">帜</span>”，旗帜。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">织</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>织</strong></p><p>織 <span class="dicpy">zhī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，埴( <span class="dicpy">zhí</span>)声。从<span class="yhcs">“糸”</span>，与丝织品有关。本义:织布，制作布帛的总称)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[weave]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">织，作布帛之总名也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">治丝曰织。织，绘也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥篚织贝。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“细紵。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士不衣织。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“染丝织之。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">文织良货贿之物。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·玉府》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“文织画及绣锦。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妇人不织。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">木兰</span>当户织。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一女不织。——汉·</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">织纺井臼。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span>
