                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>藿</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huò  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huò.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄛˋ</span></p><ol><li>〔～香〕多年生草本植物，叶子心脏形，花蓝紫色，瘦果倒卵性。茎叶香气很浓，可入药。</li><li>豆类植物的叶子：～食（指粗劣的食物）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>藿</strong> <span class="dicpy">huò</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huò.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 豆叶。嫩时可食 <span class="encs">[leaves of pulse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豆角谓之荚，其叶谓之藿。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释草》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">食我场藿。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·白驹》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">牛藿。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·公食大夫礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">奔蝉不能化藿蠋。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·庚桑楚》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“豆叶中大青虫也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:藿食(以豆叶为食物。指吃的东西很粗劣。古代常以藿食者指平民，而以肉食者指贵族);藿羹(豆叶煮成的菜羹。指古代平民吃的粗菜);藿蠋(生长在豆叶中的大青虫)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 藿香的简称 <span class="encs">[wrinkled giant hyssop]</span>。如:藿藜(藿香和蒺藜。泛指野草)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/藿香">藿香</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
