                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">瀹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>瀹</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 浸渍 <span class="encs">[soak]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瀹，渍也。从水，龠声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝齐戒疏蘥而心。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;知北游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">菅筲三，其实皆瀹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">贾公彦</span>疏:&ldquo;筲用菅草，黍稷皆淹而渍之。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 煮 <span class="encs">[boil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不如西邻之瀹祭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;郊祀志》</span>。注;&ldquo;谓瀹煮新菜以祭。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以汤煮物曰瀹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通欲文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兰台架列排曲瓢卷浆，乃羹乃瀹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">鲍照</span><span class="smcs">《园葵赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瀹茶(煮茶);瀹茗(煮茶);瀹祭(煮新菜以祭祀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 疏通水道，使水流通畅 <span class="encs">[dredge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>疏九河，瀹 <span class="diczx2">济</span><span class="diczx2">漯</span>而注诸海。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公上》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 亦泛指引导使畅通</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疏瀹五藏，澡雪精神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:瀹疏(疏浚)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
