                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">紫</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>紫</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄗˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>在可见光中波长最短，红与蓝合成的颜色：～红。～铜。～外线。～药水。</li>	<li>道教和某些朝代的统治者所崇尚的色彩，因而常在其宫室、服饰、用物前冠之以&ldquo;紫&rdquo;：～衣。～书（①道经；②皇帝诏书）。～诰（帝王诏令）。～台（神仙、帝王所居）。～气（祥瑞之气，多附会为帝王、圣贤或宝物出现的先兆）。～绶。～垣（皇宫）。～阙。～光阁。～禁城。～袍金带。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">紫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>紫</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，此声。本义:紫色，蓝和红组成的颜色)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[purple;violet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">紫，帛黑赤色也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">恶紫之夺朱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>注:&ldquo;间色之好者。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">玄冠紫緌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">紫衣狐裘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">红紫不以为亵服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">紫绮为上襦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;陌上桑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">佩紫怀黄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">披紫衣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绾黄纾紫。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:紫棠色(黑里带红的颜色);紫玉(紫色的宝玉;箫笛。古人多截紫竹为箫笛，因称箫笛为紫玉);紫衣(紫色的衣服;紫色的袈裟);紫衫(紫色的衣衫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古人因相传&ldquo;老子&rdquo;有紫气，故以紫为祥瑞的颜色 <span class="encs">[lucky color]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:紫房(道家称仙人所居的地方。也称&ldquo;紫府&rdquo;);紫皇(道教传说的天帝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 与帝王、皇宫有关的事物 <span class="encs">[be related with emperor and palace]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">正阳门径通黄道，长朝殿端拱紫垣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:紫微垣(星座名。星相家认为由它的明暗可以判断皇家的兴衰)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>紫</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 紫色的衣服和绶带 <span class="encs">[purple clothing and ribbon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐桓公</span>好服紫，一国尽服紫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀金垂紫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p>
