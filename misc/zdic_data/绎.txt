                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绎</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>绎</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">繹</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p><ol><li>抽出，理出头绪：寻～。演～（由一般原理推出关于个别事物、现象的结论的推理方法）。</li><li>连续不绝：～如（连续不断的样子）。络～不绝。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to unravel or unreel silk; to interpret</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> stetig, Dauer...</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> démêler,tirer au clair</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>绎</strong></p><p>繹 <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，睪( <span class="dicpy">yì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yì.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:抽丝)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[draw silk]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绎，抽丝也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绎，理也。丝曰绎之。——<span class="smcs">《方言一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绎，抽也。——<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引出头绪，寻求事理 <span class="encs">[deduce;sort out]</span>。如:绎味(细细寻味，仔细探究其中的道理);绎思(寻思);绎(寻绎义理，理其端绪)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 陈述 <span class="encs">[state]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">庶言同则绎。——<span class="smcs">《书》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:众言同则陈而布之</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:绎志(抒陈自己的志向;书名)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 陈列 <span class="encs">[display]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">射之为言者绎也，或曰舍也。绎者，各绎己之志也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“绎，陈也，言陈己之志。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 继续，连续不断 <span class="encs">[continue]</span>。如:绎络(络绎。往来不绝;接连不断);绎如(相续不绝的样子);绎绎(相连不断的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 扰动 <span class="encs">[disturb]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪绍匪游，徐方绎骚，震惊徐方。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:绎骚(骚动;扰动)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7E79.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】羊益切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】夷益切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音亦。【說文】抽絲也。【揚子·方言】繹，理也。絲曰繹之。【註】言解繹也。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋詁】繹，陳也。【書·君陳】庶言同則繹。【傳】衆言同，則陳而布之。【詩·小雅】會同有繹。【傳】陳也。【禮·射義】射之爲言繹也。或曰舍也，繹者，各繹已之志也。【疏】繹，陳也。</p><p>又【詩·魯頌】以車繹繹。【傳】善走也。</p><p>又山名。【詩·魯頌】保有鳧繹。【傳】繹，山也。</p><p>又龜名。【周禮·春官】龜人掌六龜之屬，地龜曰繹屬。【疏】仰者繹。</p><p>又祭名。【左傳·宣八年】壬午猶繹。【註】繹，又祭，陳昨日之禮，所以賔尸。【公羊傳】繹者何，祭之明日也。</p><p>又邑名。【左傳·文十三年】邾文公卜遷于繹。【註】邾邑。</p><p>又紬繹。【前漢·谷永傳】燕見紬繹。【註】紬繹者，引其端緒也。</p><p>又【揚子·方言】繹，長也。</p><p>又【博雅】繹，窮也，終也，充也。</p><p>又【玉篇】繹，大也。</p><p>又與驛同。【詩·大雅】徐方繹騷。【箋】繹，當作驛。【爾雅·釋訓】繹繹，生也。【疏】載芟云：驛驛其達。繹與驛音義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。</p><p>又【集韻】施只切，音釋。釋，或作繹。解也。</p><p>又【韻補】叶弋灼切。【楚辭·九辯】悲愁窮慼兮獨處廓，有美一人兮心不繹。</p><p>
