                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>鞠</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩˉ</span></p><ol><li>养育，抚养：～育。～养。</li><li>弯曲：～躬。～躬尽瘁。</li><li>大，穷极：～凶。</li><li>幼小：～子（稚子）。</li><li>穷困：“尔惟～自苦”。</li><li>告诫：“陈师～旅”。</li><li>古代的一种皮球：蹴～。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bow, bend; rear, raise, nourish</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufziehen, erziehen ,großziehen ,Ju  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> élever,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">鞠</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>鞠</strong> <span class="dicpy">jū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从革，匊( <span class="dicpy">jú</span>)声。从<span class="yhcs">“革”</span>，表示与皮革有关。本义:古时一种用来踢打玩耍的皮球。最早是结毛而成。后来用毛充填皮囊而成，宋代以后才有充气的皮球)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ball used in ancient China]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鞠，蹋鞠也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹋鞠</span><span class="diczx2">黄帝</span>所作或曰起战国时。—— <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《新书》</span>注</p><p><span class="diczx1">穿域蹋鞠。——<span class="smcs">《史记·卫将军传》</span>。索隐:<span class="yhcs">“鞠戏以皮为之，中实以毛，蹴蹋为戏也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:鞠室(蹴鞠之所);鞠场(毬场);鞠戏(古代踢球的游戏);鞠院(古球场，有围墙)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>鞠</strong> <span class="dicpy">jū</span> <span class="ptr"><a
