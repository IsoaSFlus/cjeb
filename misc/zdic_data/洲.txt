                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>洲</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，州声。<span class="yhcs">“洲”</span>本作<span class="yhcs">“州”</span>，后人加水以别州县之字。本义:水中的陆地)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[islet in a river]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">关关雎鸠，在河之洲。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·关雎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又有薮曰云连徒洲。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">淮</span>有三洲。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·鼓钟》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“淮上地。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">避渚洲。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《<指南录>后序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:洲沚(洲渚，洲淤。水中小块陆地);洲岛(水中陆地);洲场(指有所出产的水中陆地);洲屿(江中沙洲);洲浦(洲溆。洲边)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 大陆，地球上陆地的大的划区，特指其大小和结构上与岛屿和半岛不同的大片陆地及以山脉为界的大盆地 <span class="encs">[continent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天下有五大洲。——<span class="smcs">《明史·外国传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:北美洲，南美洲，欧洲，亚洲，非洲，大洋洲，南极洲;七大洲</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/洲际">洲际</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/洲际弹道导弹">洲际弹道导弹</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5DDE.svg">                          <p>水中可居曰州，周遶其㫄，从重川。昔堯遭洪水，民居水中高土，或曰九州。《詩》曰：“在河之州。”一曰州，疇也。各疇其土而生之。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20113.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠄓</span>，古文州。職流切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今別作洲，非是。〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200D5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃕</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206CF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛏</span>、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239D3.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣧓</span>，古文州。文十　重三</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DDE_1621.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(州)</span><span class="ywyy">水中可凥者曰州。</span><span class="swdz">凥各本作居。今正。者字今補。周南。在河之州。釋水、毛傳皆曰。水中可居者曰州。</span><span class="ywyy">水<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/208D8.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠣘</span>繞其旁。从重川。</span><span class="swdz">水字今補。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/208D8.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠣘</span>繞各本作周遶。誤。今正。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/208D8.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠣘</span>者、帀也。會意。職流切。三部。俗作洲。</span><span class="ywyy">昔堯遭洪水。民凥水中高土。故曰九州。</span><span class="swdz">州本州渚字。引申之乃爲九州。俗乃別製洲字。而小大分係矣。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰。在河之州。</span><span class="swdz">關雎文。證州之本義也。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰州、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24CAE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤲮</span>也。</span><span class="swdz">以㬪韵爲訓。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24CAE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤲮</span>、耕治之田也。</span><span class="ywyy">各<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24CAE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤲮</span>其土而生也。</span><span class="swdz">人各耕治以爲生。此說州之別一義。其實前義內可包。</span><span class="xh"></span></p><hr><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5DDE_1622.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200D5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃕</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文州。</span><span class="swdz">此像前後左右皆水。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
