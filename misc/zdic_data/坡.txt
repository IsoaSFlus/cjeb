                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>坡</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pō  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pō.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄛˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>倾斜的地方：山～。下～。</li>	<li>倾斜：～道。～降（<span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;ng </span>）（①坡；②坡度）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> slope, bank, hillside</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Abhang, Böschung, Neigung, Steigung  (S, Lit)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pente,versant,inclinaison,en pente,incliné</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坡</strong> <span class="dicpy">pō</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pō.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，皮声。本义:山地倾斜的地方)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[slope]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坡，陂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隰则有泮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;氓》</span>。传:&ldquo;泮，坡也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:山坡;上下坡;坡土(山坡地);坡陀(山势起伏的样子;不平坦;山;山坡);坡坂(倾斜的山坡);坡梁(方言。土冈，山冈);坡垅(丘陵);坡撇(鸨儿。开妓院的女老板)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 平原;原野 <span class="encs">[plain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言阜者土山也，小堆曰阜，平原曰坡，坡高曰陇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩拙</span><span class="smcs">《山水纯》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 〈方〉∶低洼的大片田地 <span class="encs">[large low-lying field]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:俺爹下坡了;坡野(田野;野外)</span></p>
