                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">弥</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>弥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">彌</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">m&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>满，遍：～满。～月（①整一个月；②婴儿满月）。～望（满眼）。～天（满天，形容极大的）。</li>
	<li>补，合：～补。～缝。～封。</li>
	<li>更加：～坚。欲盖～彰。</li>
	<li>水满的样子：～漫。</li>
	<li>久，远：～留（病久留不去，后称病重将死）。～亘（连绵不断）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>弥</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">彌</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">mǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/mǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄇㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/弭">弭</a>&rdquo;，平息，消灭。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>弥</strong></p>
<p>彌、瀰 <span class="dicpy">m&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/m&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从弓，尔声。本义:放松弓弦)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[relax bow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">彌，弛弓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弥，<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:弛弓也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 充满;填满 <span class="encs">[cover;fill]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弥缝其阙，而匡救其灾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:弥封;弥补;弥缝</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;弭&rdquo;。停止 <span class="encs">[stop;put down]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不弥人争。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;四称》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以弥乱发姦，登大司马，职在内辅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;王莽传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
