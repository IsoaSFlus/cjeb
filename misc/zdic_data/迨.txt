						<p>● <strong>迨</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>等到，达到：&ldquo;卒～于祸&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>趁：～吉（男女嫁娶及时）。&ldquo;请～其未毕陈而击之&rdquo;（趁着他们没有完全摆好阵势时攻击他们）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> until, when; seize, arrest</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> atteindre,jusqu'</p></div>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>迨</strong> <span class="dicpy">d&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/d&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>&lt;连&gt;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，台声。本义:及，到)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[until]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迨至菡萏成花。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迨及(达到)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 等到 <span class="encs">[by the time]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">迨诸父异爨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迨至(及至，等到);迨闻(等到听说);迨后(等到)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 趁;乘 <span class="encs">[while]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请迨其未毕隐(阵)而击之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;僖公二十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:迨吉(男婚女嫁皆合时宜)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
