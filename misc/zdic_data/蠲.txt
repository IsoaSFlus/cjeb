data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/juān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>虫名 <span class="encs">[julid]</span>。又名<span class="yhcs">“马蠲”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“马陆”</span>。俗称<span class="diczx1">宜弘大务，蠲略细微。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·虑植传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十家租税九家毕，虚受吾君蠲免恩。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《杜陵叟》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蠲资(出资);蠲恤(免去劳役赋税等项，并给与安慰或救济);蠲疴(除去疾病);蠲丁(免除丁口钱);蠲欠(免除积欠租税);蠲平(消除平息)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 清除，疏通 <span class="encs">[clear away]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">应时而蠲。——</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《劇秦美新》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蠲浊而流清。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《永州韦使君新堂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:蠲疏(清除疏通);蠲涤(清除，除去);蠲荡(清除;废除)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 使清洁 <span class="encs">[cleanse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">剖腹以蠲肠。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·皇甫谧传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:蠲洁(清洁;明洁);</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 明示，显示 <span class="encs">[make clear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">惠公</span>蠲其大德。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“涓”</span>。择取 <span                     <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>蠲</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄩㄢ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>juān</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">动物名。节肢动物门倍足纲。全身由许多环节构成，多脚，头生有一对触须。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·虫部》：「<b>蠲</b>，马<b>蠲</b>也。」</span><span class="gc_yx">也称为「马陆」。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">免除。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「蠲免」、「蠲除」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《红楼梦·第一四回》：「如这些无头绪，荒乱推托偷闲窃取等弊，次日一概都<b>蠲</b>了。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">光耀、显明。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左传·襄公十四年》：「惠公<b>蠲</b>其大德。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">使清洁。</span><span class="gc_yy">《文选·张衡·思玄赋》：「汤蠲体以祷祈兮，蒙庬褫以拯民。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《晋书·卷五一·皇甫谧传》：「岐伯剖腹以<b>蠲</b>肠。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
