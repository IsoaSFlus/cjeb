						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>灶</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">竈</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">z&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄠˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用砖石砌成的生火做饭的设备：锅～。炉～。～突（灶上的烟筒）。</li>
	<li>指&ldquo;灶君&rdquo;（中国民间在锅灶附近供的神）：祭～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> kitchen stove, cooking stove</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Küchenherd (S)​,Ofen (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> foyer,fourneau</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>灶</strong></p>
<p>竈 <span class="dicpy">z&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/z&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从火从土。繁体从穴，鼀( <span class="dicpy">c&ugrave;</span>)声。本义:用砖石等砌成，供烹煮食物、烧水的设备)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[kitchen range]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灶，炊穴也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灶者，火之主。人所以自养也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通&middot;五祀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灶烟阳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五行志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">灶者，老妇之祭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特牲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">井灶有遗处，桑竹残朽株。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归园田居》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灶床脚(紧靠炉灶旁边的地面);锅灶;行军灶;灶妄(掌炉煮饭的婢女);灶下养(厨役。古代对厨师的贱称);灶燎(上灶头烧火)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 烧炼或锻造的设备 <span class="encs">[forge]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:灶地(设灶制盐的地方);灶税(古代制盐人家所交纳的税)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 灶神 <span class="encs">[kitchen god]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏为太阳，其气长养，祀之于灶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">接着一声钝响，是送灶的爆竹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《彷徨&middot;祝福》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灶王(也称&ldquo;灶王爷&rdquo;。灶神的俗称);灶公(灶神的俗称。相传灶神上天奏事直言不讳，因喻正直的人);灶马(木刻印刷在纸上的灶神像;灶神的坐骑)</span></p>
