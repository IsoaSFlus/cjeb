                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>汆</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">cuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄨㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>烹调方法，把食物放在开水里稍微一煮：～丸子。～萝卜。</li><li>方言，烧水用的金属器具，能很快地把水煮开：～子。水～儿。</li></ol>
