                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>浮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>漂在水面上，与“<span class="diczx3">沉</span>”相对：～桥。～力。～标。～萍。～泛。～沉。漂～。～光掠影。</li><li>表面的：～皮儿。～土。～雕。</li><li>空虚，不切实：～夸。～华。</li><li>不沉静，不沉着：轻～。～躁。</li><li>暂时的：～记。～支。</li><li>可移动的：～财。～荡。～吊。～动。</li><li>超过，多余：人～于事。</li><li>呈现，涌现：～现。～想。</li><li>中医指脉搏浮在肌肤表层：～脉。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>浮</strong> <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，孚( <span class="dicpy">fú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fú.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:漂流，漂浮)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[float]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浮，氾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">载沉载浮。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·菁菁者莪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浮，漂也。浮游也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乘桴浮于海。——<span class="smcs">《论语·公冶长》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浮于</span><span class="diczx2">济</span><span class="diczx2">漯</span>。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">过夏首而西浮兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·哀郢》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浮天无岸。——<span class="smcs">《文选·桦·海赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皓月千里，浮光跃金。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">交语速装束，络绎如浮云。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:油浮于水面;浮梗(随水漂浮的残梗);浮蛆(酿酒未熟时，漂浮在酒浆上的泡沫);浮没(漂流淹没);浮月(浮在水面的月影)。也指飘在空中。如:浮香(漂溢的香气);浮气(浮游的云雾);浮踊(飘浮升腾的)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 游水(如挥动手脚或摆动翅鳍或尾巴)推进身体 <span class="encs">[swim]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒙冲斗舰乃以千数，</span><span class="diczx2">操</span>悉浮以沿江。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:浮涉(乘舟渡水);浮淫(划船游乐);浮舟(行船)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 超过 <span class="encs">[exceed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">罪俘于</span><span class="diczx2">桀</span>。——<span class="smcs">《书伪泰誓》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">康乃甘心鹰犬，搏噬善类，其罪又浮于高拱。——<span class="smcs">《明史·海瑞传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:人浮于事;浮征(超额征收);浮冒(虚报;假冒)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 游荡，游手好闲 <span class="encs">[idle about]</span>。如:浮沉草野(浪迹山野乡间;四处漂泊);浮客(四处漂泊的人);浮户(流动而无定籍的户口);浮人(到处流浪的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 用满杯酒罚人 <span class="encs">[punish sb. with a full cup of wine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使</span><span class="diczx2">苏子美</span>读之，必浮白曰:<span class="yhcs">“惜乎击之不中!”</span>——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></p><p><span
