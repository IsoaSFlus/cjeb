                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缕</strong></p><p>縷 <span class="dicpy">lǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，表示与线丝有关。娄声。本义:麻线)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[thread]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缕，线也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“凡蚕者为丝，麻者为缕。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">麻缕丝絮轻重同，则贾相若。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有布缕之征。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“紩铠甲之缕也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">余音袅袅，不绝如缕。——</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《赤壁赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">或断绝众缕，绕带手腕。——<span class="smcs">《潜夫论·俘侈》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:缕綦(用线织綦);缕綵(用各色不同的线缕成其文采)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指细而长的东西，线状物 <span class="encs">[filament]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">汉</span>氏诸陵无不发掘，乃至烧取玉匣金缕。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·文帝纪》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杨柳风轻，展尽黄金缕。——</span><span class="diczx2">冯延己</span><span class="smcs">《鹊踏枝》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:缕子脍(食品名。以细切的鱼肉制成);缕肉羹(肉丝羹);缕金(金丝;以金丝为饰);缕金百蝶穿花大红洋缎窄裉袄(指在大红洋缎的衣面上用金线绣成百蝶穿花图案的紧身袄。裉，上衣前后两幅在腋下合缝的部分);缕细(细如丝缕)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 帛 <span class="encs">[silks]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故为祷朝缕绵。——<span class="smcs">《管子·侈靡》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“帛也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:缕绵(绸帛丝绵)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>缕</strong></p><p>縷 <span class="dicpy">lǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 连续不断 <span class="encs">[continuously]</span>。如:缕悉(一条一条很清楚);缕堤(临河处所筑小堤。因连绵不断，状如丝缕，故名);缕缕行行(方言。成群结队。形容很多);缕续(接连不断)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“褛”</span>。衣破 <span class="encs">[worn-out]</span>。如:蓝缕(破衣)</p><p>◎ <strong>缕</strong></p><p>縷 <span class="dicpy">lǚ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǚ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 一条一条地，详尽地 <span class="encs">[in detail]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其功业相次千万者，不可复缕举也。——</span><span class="diczx2">葛洪</span><span class="smcs">《抱朴子》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:缕细(底细，详情);缕责(细责，苛责)</p><p>◎ <strong>缕</strong></p><p>縷
