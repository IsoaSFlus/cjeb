                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">曹</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>曹</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">c&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>等，辈：尔～（你们）。吾～。</li>
	<li>古代分科办事的官署：部～（中国明、清两代各部司曹的通称，源于汉代曹史的简称，相当于郡守的总务长）。</li>
	<li>诉讼的原告、被告两方。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ministry officials; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Cao</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">曹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>曹</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代指诉讼的原告和被告。即两曹 <span class="encs">[plaintiff and defendant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">两曹，今俗所谓原告被告也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代分科办事的官署或部门 <span class="encs">[divison;department of the central government in ancient times]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">品其名位，犹不失下曹从事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曹干(官署中的干事人员);曹掾(泛指一般官员);曹务(古代官署中分类所管理的事务);法曹(唐、宋地方司法机关)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 管某事的官职 <span class="encs">[official]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曹主(机关负责人);曹郎(部曹。部属各司的官吏);曹官(属官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 双方;班;组 <span class="encs">[group;team]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">分曹并进。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span>。注:&ldquo;偶也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">率其曹偶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;黥布传》</span>。索隐:&ldquo;辈也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃造其曹。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;公刘》</span>。传:&ldquo;群也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 辈，等于现代汉语中的&ldquo;们&rdquo; <span class="encs">[people of the same]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔曹身与名俱灭，不废江河万古流。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《戏为六绝句(之二)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:尔曹(你们这些人);儿曹(孩子们)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Cao state]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 西周的诸侯国</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古西域国名</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">曹</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>曹</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄘㄠˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>c&aacute;o <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5461.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代訴訟時，稱原告和被告兩方為「兩曹」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古代分科辦事的官署部門或官職。</span><span class="gc_yy">《續漢書志&middot;第二六&middot;百官志三》：「成帝初置尚書四人，分為四<b>曹</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《文明小史&middot;第七回》：「現在的人，都把知府看得是個閑<b>曹</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">群眾、同伴。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;昭公十二年》：「周原伯絞虐其輿臣，使<b>曹</b>逃。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈曲江三章章五句〉三首之一：「白石素沙亦相蕩，哀鴻獨叫求其<b>曹</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">國名。周代諸侯國之一。故址約在今大陸地區山東西部。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如三國時魏有曹植。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">曹</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上" target="_blank">辰集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上&amp;bs=曰" target="_blank">曰</a>】
                              曹·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=10" target="_blank">10</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/66F9.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】昨牢切【集韻】【韻會】財勞切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音漕。【說文】作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23358.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣍘</span>。獄之兩<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23358.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣍘</span>也。在廷東，从㯥。治事者，从曰。【註】徐鍇曰：以言詞治獄也，故从曰。【前漢&middot;成帝紀註】尙書四人爲四曹，成帝置五人，有三公曹，主斷獄事。【後漢&middot;百官志】世祖分六曹。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;大雅】乃造其曹。【傳】曹，羣也。【朱傳】羣牧之處也。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;平準書】分曹循行郡國。【註】曹，輩也。</p>
<p>又【楚辭&middot;招魂】分曹並進。【註】曹，偶也。</p>
<p>又國名。【詩&middot;曹風譜】周武王封弟叔振鐸於曹，今濟隂定陶是也。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓氏急就篇】周武王封曹叔振鐸，後以國爲氏。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶徂侯切。【楚辭&middot;招隱士】禽獸駭兮亡其曹，王孫兮歸來，山中不可以久留。</p>
<p>又叶木何切。【蘇轍&middot;嚴顏詩】斫頭除死子無怒，我豈畏死如兒曹。匹夫受戮或不避，所重壯氣吞黃河。【五經文字】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23358.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣍘</span>，經典相承，隷省作曹。《石經》作曺。</p>
