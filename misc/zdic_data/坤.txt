<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>坤</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">kūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄣˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>八卦之一：乾（<span class="dicpy">qi&aacute;n </span>）～。～舆。</li>
	<li>称女性的：～造。～宅（旧时婚礼称女家）。～表。～车。～鞋。～角儿。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> earth; feminine, female</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Empfangend, Hingabe ,weibliches Prinzip</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>坤</strong></p>
<p>堃 <span class="dicpy">kūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，申声。本义:八卦之一，象征地)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 地，大地 <span class="encs">[earth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坤，地也，易之卦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坤，土也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公二十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坤也者，地也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山岳河渎，皆坤之灵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋书&middot;乐志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坤元(坤的元始之德，指大地资生万物之德);坤母(地;火);坤后(地);坤珍(象征大地的符瑞);坤轴(想像中的地轴);坤维(地维。指大地的四方);坤仪(大地。同坤舆)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="smcs">《易》</span>卦名 <span class="encs">[one of the Eight Diagrams representingthe earth]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 八卦之一</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坤为地、为母、为布、为釜、为吝啬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:坤卦(八卦之一，代表地);坤乾(古书名。阴阳之书)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 六十四卦之一</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地势坤，君子以厚德载物。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;坤&middot;象》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>坤</strong> <span class="dicpy">kūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kūn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 后用为女性或女方的代称 <span class="encs">[female]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:坤表(女表);坤鞋(女鞋);坤旦(评剧中女伶串演旦角，叫坤旦;男子串演旦角，叫乾旦);坤宅(旧时联姻，称女家为坤宅，男家为乾宅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古以八卦定方位，西南方为坤 <span class="encs">[southwest]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:坤垠(西南边陲);坤隅(西南方);坤维(指西南方)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中" target="_blank">丑集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中&amp;bs=土" target="_blank">土</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5764.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕巛<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2051C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔜</span>【廣韻】苦昆切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】枯昆切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音髡。地也。【釋名】順也，上順乾也。【易】卦名。【象傳】地勢坤。【說卦】坤爲地。</p>
<p>又叶巨員切，音拳。【桓君山仙賦】氾氾濫濫，隨天轉旋。容容無爲，壽極乾坤。【蘇軾&middot;服胡麻賦】至陽赫赫，發自坤兮。至隂肅肅，躋于乾兮。　【說文】从土从申。土位在申，古作巛，象坤畫六斷也。　別作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20527.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔧</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26964.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥤</span>䰠䝿。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26964.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥤</span>字原作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26951.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥑</span>下介。䝿字从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26951.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥑</span>。䝿，古貴字。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">坤</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=土">土</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5764.svg">                          <p>地也。《易》之卦也。从土从申。土位在申。苦昆切〖注〗巛、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21629.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𡘩</span>，古文。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5764_998.svg" /><span class="swbf">(坤)</span><span class="ywyy">地也。易之卦也。</span><span class="swdz">象傳曰：地勢坤，君子以厚德載物。說卦傳曰：坤，順也。按伏羲取天地之德爲卦，名曰乾坤。</span><span class="ywyy">从土<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26954.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦥔</span>。</span><span class="swdz">會意。苦昆切。十三部。</span><span class="ywyy">土位在申也。</span><span class="swdz">此說从𦥔之意也。說卦傳曰：坤也者，地也。萬物皆致養焉，故曰致役乎坤，坤正在申位。自倉頡造字已然，後儒乃臆造乾南坤北爲伏羲先天之學，說卦傳所定之位爲文王後天之學，甚矣人之好怪也。或問伏羲畫八卦，卽有乾坤震巽等名與不。曰有之。伏羲三奇謂之乾，三耦謂之坤。而未有乾字坤字，傳至於倉頡乃後有其字。坤<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29567.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩕧</span>特造之，乾震坎離艮兑以音義相同之字爲之，故文字之始作也。有義而後有音，有音而後有形，音必先乎形。名之曰乾坤者，伏羲也。字之者，倉頡也。畫卦者，造字之先聲也。是以不得云三卽坤字。</span></p>                          </div>
