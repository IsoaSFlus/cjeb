                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">号</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>号</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">號</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>名称：国～。年～。字～。</li>
	<li>旧指名和字以外的别号：如&ldquo;李白字太白，～青莲居士&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>记号，标志：信～。暗号。</li>
	<li>排定的次序或等级：编～。～码。</li>
	<li>扬言，宣称：～称。项羽兵四十万，～百万。</li>
	<li>指某种人员：病～。伤～。</li>
	<li>标上记号：～房子。把这件东西～上。</li>
	<li>号令，命令：发号施～令。～召。</li>
	<li>军队或乐队里所用的西式喇叭：吹～。～兵。</li>
	<li>量词，用于人数：昨天去了几十～人。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>号</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">號</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>拖长声音大声呼叫：呼～。～叫。</li>
	<li>大声哭：哀～。～丧。～啕大哭。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">号</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>号</strong></p>
<p>號 <span class="dicpy">h&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从虎，从号。&ldquo;号&rdquo;亦兼表读音。&ldquo;号&rdquo;是&ldquo;號&rdquo;的古字，上面是&ldquo;口&rdquo;，下面代表声音，即号呼的意思。后繁化，变成&ldquo;號&rdquo;，现在简化为&ldquo;号&rdquo;。本义:大声喊叫)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[howl;yell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">号，呼也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谁之永号?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;硕鼠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公号庆</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">号呼而转徙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因大号，一墟皆惊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阴风怒号。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">号泣载道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:号噪(呼叫，喧嚷);号踯(号叫跳顿);号佛(高声念佛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 哭而有言 <span class="encs">[cry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">号，痛声也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
