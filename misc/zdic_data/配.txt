<p><span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>两性结合：～偶。～种（<span class="dicpy">zhǒng</span>）。</li>
	<li>相互分工合作：～合。～器。</li>
	<li>用适当的标准加以调和：～料。～制。～伍。～药。～色。～餐。</li>
	<li>有计划地分派、安排：～备。～置。～给（<span class="dicpy">jǐ</span>）。分～。搭～。</li>
	<li>把缺少的补足：～套。～乐（<span class="dicpy">yu&egrave; </span>）。装～。</li>
	<li>衬托，陪衬：～搭。～角儿（<span class="dicpy">ju&eacute;</span>）。～殿。红花～绿叶。</li>
	<li>够得上：～得上。</li>
	<li>古代把罪人遣放到边远地区充军：～军。发～。刺～。</li>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">配</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>配</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉，己声。按:一说不从&ldquo;己&rdquo;声，应是&ldquo;妃&rdquo;省声。本义:用不同的酒配制而成的颜色)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wine color]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">配，酒色也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">吴善述</span>广义校订:&ldquo;配即<span class="smcs">《内则》</span>注所谓以清与糟相配也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为&ldquo;妃&rdquo;。配偶，夫妻的称呼。后多指妻 <span class="encs">[spouse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遇其配主。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;丰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天立厥配。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;皇矣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">配稷契兮恢唐功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;王逸&middot;九思守志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:元配;继配;配匹(配偶);配耦(配偶);配头(方言。配偶);配丽(配偶)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>配</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 结婚，婚配 <span class="encs">[join in marriage;marry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">安得配君子，共乘双飞鸾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《感兴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:配人(出嫁，嫁人);配嫁(婚配出嫁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 衬托，陪衬 <span class="encs">[contrast;serve a contrast or foil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">独有太白配残月。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《东方半明》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:红花配绿叶;配衬(陪衬，衬托)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 分给;配给 <span class="encs">[apportion]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妒令潜配上阳宫，一生遂生空房宿。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《上阳白发人》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:调配(调动分配);搭配(按一定目的安排分配);配率(分配的比率);配当(分配，配合);配钱(折合为现钱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 充军， 流放 <span class="encs">[exile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">配向江南卑湿地。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《缚戎人》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如: 配格(依罪情的大小、流刑的轻重定配所远近的规定);配隶(流放充军);配防(发配罪人守边);配没(把罪人的家属发配为奴隶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 配合;结合 <span class="encs">[go together;meet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:配搭(相配;配合);配殿(正殿左右两旁的偏殿);配本(指合版本不同的书而配成全书);配手(配合工作的帮手);配主(对事物主体的配合);配命(配合天命)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 配制 <span class="encs">[compound;prepare;concoct]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:配药方;配颜色;配眼镜</span></p>
