                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">烟</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>烟</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">⑤菸、煙</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>物质燃烧时所生的气体：冒～。硝～。～囱。～雾。～波。～火。～尘。荒无人～。</li>
	<li>像烟的：～霞。～霭。～岚。～鬟。</li>
	<li>烟气刺激使眼睛流泪或睁不开：～了眼睛。</li>
	<li>一年生草本植物：～草。～叶。</li>
	<li>烟草制成品：香～。卷～。纸～。～蒂。</li>
	<li>指&ldquo;鸦片&rdquo;：大～。～灯。～枪。～馆。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> smoke, soot; opium; tobacco, cigarettes</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Qualm  (S)​,Rauch, Nebel  (S)​,Zigarette  (S)​,Zigarre  (S)​,Rauch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fumée,brouillard,vapeur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">烟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>烟</strong></p>
<p>煙 <span class="dicpy">yān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，因声。本义:物质因燃烧而产生的气体)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[smoke]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">火壮则煙微。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《连珠》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">煙炎之毁熸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;蔡邕传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日照香炉生紫烟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《望庐山瀑布》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟焰迷漫。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟扑口鼻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟炎张天。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟焰雾雨。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烟斜雾横(互文，表烟雾斜横。斜 横:升腾缭绕)。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:烟火邻居(指紧邻);烟扛扛(形容烟雾腾腾的样子);烟爨(炊烟);烟蓬(小火轮客舱顶上的散座);烟祀(香烟与祭祀。引申为后代);烟尘(比喻战乱;借指参与战乱的部队);烟井(住户;人家);烟火(火警，火灾);烟焰(烟和火焰);夕烟(黄昏时的烟雾);风烟(随风吹散的烟);油烟;炊烟</span></p>
