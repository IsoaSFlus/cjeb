                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>茵</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄣˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 铺垫的东西，垫子、褥子、毯子的通称：～褥。～席。～饪（寝褥和烹饪，引申为睡眠和饮食）。绿草如～。</p></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>茵</strong> <span class="dicpy">yīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，因声。本义:车垫子。字本作<span class="yhcs">“因”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mattress of a car]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茵，车重席也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">交茵畅毂。——<span class="smcs">《秦风·小戎》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“文茵虎皮也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茵席。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·少仪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“著褥也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">御者在茵上。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·五行志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">同车未尝敢均茵冯。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·周阳由传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绿草如茵。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:茵伏(茵轼。车蓐和车轼);茵冯(茵凭。车蓐与车轼)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 衬垫;褥子 <span class="encs">[mat]</span>。如:茵席(褥垫;草席)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>茵</strong> <span class="dicpy">yīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“氤”</span> <span class="encs">[cloudy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茵蕴祗冥。——<span class="smcs">《江文通集·莲花赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:茵蘊(氤氲。气弥漫的样子)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/茵褥">茵褥</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/茵茵">茵茵</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8335.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】於眞切，音因。【說文】車重席。【詩·秦風】文茵暢轂。【註】文茵，虎皮也。【前漢·丙吉傳】此不過汙丞相車茵耳。【註】茵，蓐也。</p><p>又【班固·西都賦】乗茵步輦。【漢儀註】皇后、婕妤乗輦，餘者以茵，四人對舉四角，輿而行也。</p><p>又【本草】茵蔯，蒿。【註】經冬不死，更因舊苗而生，故名因陳。【杜甫詩】茵蔯春藕香。</p><p>又茵芋，藥名。　茵蓐。或作鞇。【集韻】亦作箇，通作絪、裀。</p>
