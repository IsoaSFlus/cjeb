                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饮</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>饮</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">飲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄣˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>喝，又特指喝酒：～水思源。～酒。～泣（泪流满面，流到口里，形容悲哀到了极点）。～鸩止渴。</li>
	<li>指可喝的东西：冷～。～料。～食。</li>
	<li>含忍：～恨。～誉（享有盛名，受到称赞）。</li>
	<li>隐没（<span class="dicpy">m&ograve;</span>）：～羽。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>饮</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">飲</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  〡ㄣˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>给牲畜水喝：～马。～牛</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>饮</strong></p>
<p>飲、飮、 <span class="dicpy">yǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形。右边是人形，左上边是人伸着舌头，左下边是酒坛(酉)。象人伸舌头向酒坛饮酒。小篆演变为&ldquo;飲&rdquo;，隶书作&ldquo;饮&rdquo;。本义:喝)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。有时特指喝酒 <span class="encs">[drink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">飮，飮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。今隶作饮。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子以饮食宴乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;需》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:&ldquo;水流入口为饮。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饮酒浆饮，俟于东房。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;公食礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冬日则饮汤，夏日则饮水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子反受而饮之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;十过》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">项王</span>即日因留 <span class="diczx2">沛公</span>与饮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:豪饮(放量饮酒);饮水茹藻(喝泠水吃淡味食品。喻处境艰难或内心抑郁之情);饮茶;一饮而尽;饮水(喝水)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 含;忍 <span class="encs">[keep in the heart]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耕男饐妇猛一省，髑髅饮冤死犹警。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贝青乔</span><span class="smcs">《哀甬乐》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:饮恨而终(含恨而死);饮冤(含冤);饮默(忍着不吭声);饮气(忍气)</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/98EE.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23C8E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣲎</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2969C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩚜</span>淾<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23D97.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣶗</span>㱃<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29695.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𩚕</span><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28873.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨡳</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>於錦切，音上聲。【玉篇】咽水也。亦歠也。【釋名】飮，奄也。以口奄而引咽之也。【周禮·天官·膳夫】飮用六淸。</p><p>又【酒正】辨四飮之物：一曰淸、二曰醫、三曰漿、四曰酏。【註】淸渭醴之泲者，醫卽內則以酏爲醴者，漿今之酨漿，酏今之粥也。</p><p>又漱也。【儀禮·公食大夫禮】賔坐祭，遂飮奠於豐上。【註】飮，潄也。</p><p>又隱也。【後漢·蔡邕傳】邕上疏曰：臣一入牢獄，當爲楚毒所迫趣，以飮章辭情，何緣復聞。【註】趣音促，飮猶隱也，今匿名文書也。</p><p>又【正字通】樂律有聲飮，以聲相轉而合也。梁武帝自制四器，名曰通，每通施三絃，因以通聲，隨聲酌其淸濁高下也。</p><p>又飮器，溺器也。【戰國策】趙襄子殺智伯，漆其頭以爲飮器。【註】溺器。或曰酒器。</p><p>又受箭曰飮箭。【郭璞·蛟贊】漢武飮羽。</p><p>又【廣韻】於禁切，音蔭。以飮飮之也。【禮·檀弓】酌而飮寡人。【左傳·昭二十六年】成人伐齊師之飮馬于淄者。</p><p>又【莊子·則陽篇】或不言而飮人以和。　【說文】或作㱃。通作飮。互詳酉部酓字註。</p><p>
