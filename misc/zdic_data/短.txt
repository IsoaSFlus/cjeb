                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>短</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">duǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄢˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>长度小，与“长（<span class="dicpy">cháng </span>）”相对：～期。～暂。～促。～途。～命。～讯。～浅。～兵相接。～小精悍。</li>    <li>缺少，欠：～少。～缺。</li>    <li>缺点：～处。护～。取长补～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">短</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>短</strong> <span class="dicpy">duǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从矢，豆声。从<span class="yhcs">“矢”</span>，表示短不短可以用矢来量。古代弓长箭短，量长的用弓作标准，量短的用箭作标准。本义:不长)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[short]</span>。指两端距离小。与<span class="yhcs">“长”</span>相对。指空间或指时间</p><p><span class="diczx1">短，有所长短，以矢为正。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，短，不长也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以其长见与短见也。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·长见》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“近也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">帝</span><span class="diczx2">尧</span>长，帝 <span class="diczx2">舜</span>短， <span class="diczx2">文王</span>长， <span class="diczx2">周公</span>短， <span class="diczx2">仲尼</span>长， <span class="diczx2">子弓</span>短。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·非相》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">老臣以媪为</span><span class="diczx2">长安君</span>计短也。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·赵策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:短趁(打短工);短盘驴子(短途来往的驴子);短盘(分段作短程行走);短票(借贷的凭证，借约);短绠(短绳。比喻才学浅薄);短梦(短暂的梦)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 寿命短促 <span class="encs">[short-lived]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凶短折。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“未冠曰短。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:短局促(短卒律，短古取。短促);短岁(短命，早死)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 浅薄，简陋 <span class="encs">[shallow;superficial]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">问之人以穷其短。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:短才(智慧低下或才智笨拙的人);短笔(自谦文笔拙劣。同拙笔);短供(指粗简的菜肴)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>短</strong> <span class="dicpy">duǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/duǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 引申为不足，缺乏 <span
