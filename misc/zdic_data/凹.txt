                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>凹</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">āo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/āo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>周围高，中间低，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">凸</span>&rdquo;相对：～凸不平。～透镜。～版印刷（印刷术之一，与&ldquo;凸版印刷&rdquo;相反）。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>凹</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">wā  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wā.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄚˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/洼">洼</a>&rdquo;（用于地名）：核桃～（在中国山西省）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> concave, hollow, depressed; a pass, valley</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dépression,indentation,creux,concave</p></div>
