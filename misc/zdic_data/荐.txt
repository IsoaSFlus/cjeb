                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荐</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>荐</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">薦</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>推举，介绍：推～。举～。～擢。</li>
	<li>频仍，屡次：～仍（一再）。～饥（连年饥荒）。～食（一再吞食，喻不断侵略）。～臻（接连来到）。</li>
	<li>进献，祭献：～羞（进献肴馔）。～新（以初熟谷物或时鲜果物祭献）。～胙（供献鬼神的肉）。</li>
	<li>草：&ldquo;麋鹿食～&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>草席，垫子：草～。棕～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荐</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>荐</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从草，存声。本义:草垫子) 同本义 <span class="encs">[straw mat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荐，薦席也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，凡亲地者谓之筵，加于筵者谓之席。席即因也，重于筵也。</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>荐</strong> <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p>再;又;接连 <span class="encs">[again;also]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荐，再也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荐，重也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>荐饥。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公十三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以荐食上国。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;定公四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饥馑荐降。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荐仍(接连而来);荐更(反复经历);荐臻(荐蓁。接连地来到;一再遇到);荐饥(连年灾荒;连续灾荒)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>荐</strong></p>
<p>薦 <span class="dicpy">ji&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ji&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薦，黍蓬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。按，蒿类也，或以为野茭。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">麋鹿食荐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;齐物论》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">崔</span>注:&ldquo;甘草也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荐草(茂盛的牧草)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 细草 <span class="encs">[small grass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薦草多衍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;八观》</span>。注:&ldquo;茂草也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其民不衣而褐薦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;异法方宜论》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓细草也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北胡随畜薦居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;终军传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荐居(逐水草而居，没有固定的住所)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 假借为荐。草垫子 <span class="encs">[straw mat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薦，席也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:草荐;荐席(垫席);荐蒋(垫席);荐藉(草席)</span></p>

<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 介绍进献人才 <span class="encs">[recommend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者，尧荐舜于天，而天受之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;万章上》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx2">徐</span>公 <span class="diczx2">致靖</span>荐。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荐本(官吏向皇帝推荐人才的奏本);荐辟(举荐);荐书(推荐信);荐士(推荐有才德的人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 进;进献 <span class="encs">[offer]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 衬，垫 <span class="encs">[line]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">章甫荐履，渐不可久兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《吊屈原赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荐居(垫草而居)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 包裹 <span class="encs">[wrap]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">韦恕荐轮，第中游戏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">荀悦</span><span class="smcs">《汉纪》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指请和尚道士念经拜忏以超度亡灵 <span class="encs">[say prayers or perform Buddhist service to release souls from purgatory]</span>。</p>
