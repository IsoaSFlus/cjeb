                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">虏</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>虏</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">虜</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>俘获：～获。俘～。（➊打仗时捉住敌人；➋打仗时捉住的敌人）。</li>
	<li>俘获的人。</li>
	<li>中国古代对北方外族的贬称。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to capture, imprison, seize; a prison</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> gefangen nehmen, gefangennehmen [alt]</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> capturer,piller,prisonnier,esclave</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">虏</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>虏</strong></p>
<p>虜 <span class="dicpy">lǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，从毌( <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span>)，虍( <span class="dicpy">hū</span>)声。&ldquo;毌&rdquo;即&ldquo;贯&rdquo;字，是穿钱用的绳索。这里表示用绳索拘捕的意思。本义:俘获)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[take prisoner;capture]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虏，获也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">献民虏者操右袂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;军所获也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且为所虏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虏</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>将 <span class="diczx2">屈匄</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虏</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>王。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 亦指虏掠、抄掠 <span class="encs">[rob]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">珠柙离丘体，珍宝见剽虏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张载</span><span class="smcs">《七哀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">匈奴</span>虏略千余人及畜产而去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;韩长孺列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:虏掠(虏获掠夺);虏夺(虏掠抢夺);虏暴(残害暴虐他人，一如奴隶);虏略(虏获掠夺)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>虏</strong></p>
<p>虜 <span class="dicpy">lǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 俘虏 <span class="encs">[captive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乘奔逐北，斩首捕虏十余万。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;诛秦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾属今为之虏矣!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为降虏手蛮夷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为奔亡之虏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乞为囚虏。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:虏囚(俘虏);虏役(奴役);虏伏(如俘虏一样降服)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 奴仆 <span class="encs">[slave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽臣虏之劳不苦于此矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:虏役(奴仆);虏使(役使之如奴隶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指敌人;叛逆 <span class="encs">[enemy;betrayer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">则直前诟虏帅失信。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:虏尘(指敌寇或叛乱者的侵扰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古时对北方外族或南方人对北方人的蔑称 <span class="encs">[non-Han nationalities living in the North and West in ancient times]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诟虏帅失信。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span class="smcs">《指南录后序》</span></p>
