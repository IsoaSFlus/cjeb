                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">曲</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>曲</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">➎麯</span>、<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">➎麴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄩˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>弯转，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">直</span>&rdquo;相对：弯～。～折（<span class="dicpy">zh&eacute;</span>）。～笔（①古代史官不按事实、有意掩盖真相的记载；②写文章时故意离题而不直书其事的笔法）。～肱而枕。～尽其妙。</li>	<li>不公正，不合理：～说。～解（<span class="dicpy">jiě</span>）。委～求全。</li>	<li>弯曲的地方：河～。</li>	<li>偏僻的地方：乡～。</li>	<li>酿酒或制酱时引起发酵的东西：酒～。～霉。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>曲</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄩˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>能唱的文词，一种艺术形式：～艺。～话。</li>	<li>歌的乐调：～调（<span class="dicpy">di&agrave;o</span>）。～谱。异～同工。～高和（<span class="dicpy">h&egrave;</span>）寡。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">曲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>曲</strong> <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 弯曲，不直 <span class="encs">[bent;croo-ked]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲，象器曲受物之形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲，折也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木曰曲直。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲，不直也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">木直中绳，輮以为轮，其曲中规。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲挺纵横。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以曲为美。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">之疏之曲。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曲录(弯弯曲曲);曲律(曲吕。弯曲的样子);曲弯弯(像弓一样弯);曲蟮(蚯蚓);曲盖(古时仪杖用的曲柄华盖);曲兵(形体弯曲的兵器)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 迂曲;婉转 <span class="encs">[tortuous;mild and indirect;tactful]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曲谕(婉转动人的教诲);曲止(婉转阻止);曲言(婉言);曲譬(婉转譬喻);曲子(指肠子);曲邃(曲折深邃);曲狭(迂曲狭窄);曲复(迂回曲折)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 周遍;多方面;详尽 <span class="encs">[all;detailed]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曲止(委曲详尽);曲至(周到);曲防(遍设堤坊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 表敬之词 <span class="encs">[your]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曲延臣算(请求通融，延长我的寿命);曲延(谦称邀请你而使你受到委曲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 邪僻，不正派 <span class="encs">[evil;dishonest]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曲心矫肚(心地阴险，一肚子虚情假意);曲意(委曲己见以奉承他人);曲就(委曲成全);曲全(曲意保全)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 细，细小 <span class="encs">[trifle;petty]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曲惠(小惠);曲智(小智);曲胜(小胜);曲艺(医卜之类的小技艺)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 假借为&ldquo;局&rdquo;。狭隘，拘泥 <span class="encs">[narrow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毋使人曲之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;侈靡》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>曲</strong> <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 理屈;理亏 <span class="encs">[wrong;unjustifiable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其言曲而中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲在</span><span class="diczx2">赵</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲在</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以负</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>曲。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曲挠(无罪而被枉屈);曲论(歪曲事实的议论;狡辩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 弯曲的地方，亦指幽深之处 <span class="encs">[bend of a river，etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在</span><span class="diczx2">汾</span>一曲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;汾沮洳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">汾</span>之一曲。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《复庵记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:河曲(河流弯曲的地方);曲阿(屋的曲角);曲房(内室，密室);曲屋(周旋曲屈的楼阁);曲隈(曲折隐蔽之处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 偏僻的处所;乡里 <span class="encs">[countryside]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曲士(乡曲之士);曲落(村庄);曲道(乡曲，乡里);曲辫子(乡愚)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 小巷 <span class="encs">[lane]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曲头(巷头;街头)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 蚕箔，用苇或竹编制的养蚕的器具 <span class="encs">[a bamboo tray for raising silkworms]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">勃</span>以织曲薄为生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;周勃世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曲簿(养蚕的器具)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>曲</strong> <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 使弯曲 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饭疏食饮水，曲肱而枕之，乐亦在其中矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;述而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弘曲躬而自卑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;郑弘传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:曲匝(身体屈曲辗转);曲肱(弯着胳膊作枕头。比喻清贫而闲适的生活)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>曲</strong></p>
<p>麯、麴 <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 酒母 <span class="encs">[leaven;yeast]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:红曲;神曲;曲糊(粘浆状态的酒曲);大曲(酿造白酒用的一种曲);酒曲(酿酒用的曲)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 泛指酒 <span class="encs">[wine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:曲蘖(酒);曲王(指酒神);曲生(酒的别名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">qǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>

<p>◎ <strong>曲</strong> <span class="dicpy">qǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 乐曲，歌曲 <span class="encs">[music of song]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫辞更坐弹一曲，为君翻作琵琶行。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span>诗</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">快弹数曲。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲罢悯然。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曲终收拨。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五更转曲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:聂耳作曲，田汉作词;编曲</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一种韵文形式，盛行于元代，又称元曲 <span class="encs">[ qu ，a type of verse in singing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不复能终曲。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">侯方域</span><span class="smcs">《壮悔堂文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 是受民间歌曲的影响而形成的，句法较词更为灵活，多用口语，用韵也更接近口语。一支曲可以单唱，几支曲可以合成一套，也可以用几套曲子写成戏曲。广义的曲泛指秦、汉以来各种可以入乐的乐曲。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:汉大曲、唐宋大曲、民间小曲等</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">拐彎的地方。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「山<b>曲</b>」、「河<b>曲</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;李白〈惜餘春賦〉：「漢之<b>曲</b>兮江之潭。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">事理不直。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「是非曲直」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《左傳&middot;僖公二十八年》：「若其不還，君退臣犯，<b>曲</b>在彼矣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">心中的隱情。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「衷<b>曲</b>」、「心<b>曲</b>」、「互通款<b>曲</b>」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">偏僻的小地方。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「鄉<b>曲</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;蔣防《霍小玉傳》：「住在勝業坊古寺<b>曲</b>，甫上車門宅是也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">局部、一部分。</span><span class="gc_yy">《荀子&middot;解蔽》：「凡人之患蔽於一<b>曲</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">養蠶的器具。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷五七&middot;絳侯周勃世家》：「勃以織薄<b>曲</b>為生。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如漢代有曲證。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>

<ol>
	<li>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">周全、盡心盡力。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>曲</b>盡孝道」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《易經&middot;繫辭上》：「<b>曲</b>成萬物而不遺。」</span></p></li>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/66F2.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】丘玉切【集韻】【韻會】區玉切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音䱡。【說文】象器受物之形。【易&middot;繫辭】曲成萬物而不遺。【疏】屈曲委細。【書&middot;洪範】木曰曲直。【傳】木可以揉曲直。</p><p>又【詩&middot;秦風】亂我心曲。【傳】心曲，委曲也。【禮&middot;曲禮&middot;釋文】曲禮，委曲說禮之事。</p><p>又【禮&middot;中庸】其次致曲。【註】曲，猶小小之事。【朱註】一偏也。</p><p>又【說文】或說蠶簿。【禮&middot;月令】具曲植籧筐。【註】所以養蠶器也。曲，簿也。【前漢&middot;周勃傳】以織簿曲爲生。【註】葦簿爲曲也。</p><p>又樂曲。【宋玉&middot;對楚王問】是其曲彌高，其和彌寡。</p><p>又姓。【史記&middot;蒙恬傳】御史曲宮。</p><p>又【集韻】顆羽切，音踽。地名。【史記&middot;曹相國世家】軍於曲遇。</p><p>又【陳丞相世家】更以陳平爲曲逆侯。</p><p>又【韻補】叶區聿切。【劉植&middot;魯都賦】巖險迴隔，峻巘隱曲。猛獸深潛，介禽<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25A25.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥨥</span>匿。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/66F2.svg">                          <p>象器曲受物之形。或說曲，蠶薄也。凡曲之屬皆从曲。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/66F2_1.svg" /><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2092C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤬</span>，古文曲。丘玉切〖注〗《集韻》匚古作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2092C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤬</span>。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/66F2_2603.svg" /><span class="swbf">(曲)</span><span class="ywyy">象器曲受物之形也。</span><span class="swdz">匚象方器受物之形，側視之。曲象圜其中受物之形，正視之。引申之爲凡委曲之稱。不直曰曲。詩曰：子髮曲局。又曰：亂我心曲。箋云：心曲，心之委曲也。又樂章爲曲。謂音宛曲而成章也。周語曰：士獻詩。瞽獻曲。韋云：曲，樂曲也。毛詩傳曰：曲合樂曰歌。徒歌曰謠。韓詩曰：有章曲曰歌。無章曲曰謠。按曲合樂者，合於樂器也。行葦傳曰：歌者，比於琴瑟也。卽曲合樂曰歌也。區玉切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡曲之屬皆从曲。或說曲，蠶薄也。</span><span class="swdz">曲見月令，方言，漢書周勃傳。詳艸部薄下。其物以萑葦爲之。七月傳曰：豫畜萑葦。可以爲曲也。其字俗作䒼。又作筁。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/66F2_2604.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2092C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠤬</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文曲。</span><span class="swdz">小徐無。</span></p>                          </div>
