                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>其</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">q&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>第三人物代词，相当于&ldquo;他（她）&rdquo;、&ldquo;他们（她们）&rdquo;、&ldquo;它（它们）&rdquo;；&ldquo;他（她）的&rdquo;、&ldquo;他们（她们）的&rdquo;、&ldquo;它（们）的&rdquo;：各得～所。莫名～妙。三缄～口。独行～是。自食～果。</li>
	<li>指示代词，相当于&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">那</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;那个&rdquo;、&ldquo;那些&rdquo;：～他。～余。～次。文如～人。名副～实。言过～实。</li>
	<li>那里面的：～中。只知～一，不知～二。</li>
	<li>连词，相当于&ldquo;如果&rdquo;、&ldquo;假使&rdquo;：&ldquo;～如是，熟能御之？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>助词，表示揣测、反诘、命令、劝勉：&ldquo;～如土石何？&rdquo;</li>
	<li>词尾，在副词后：极～快乐。大概～。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>

<p>● <strong>其</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧˉ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 〔郦食（y&igrave;）～〕中国汉代人</p>

</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">其</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>其</strong> <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;諅&rdquo;。周年 <span class="encs">[anniversary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丧:父母三年，妻、后子三年，父、叔父、弟兄、庶子其，戚、族人五月。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">左右伯受沐涂树之枝阔，其年，民被白布。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;轻重戊》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亟其乘屋，其始播百谷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;豳风&middot;七月》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>其</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;期&rdquo;。期限 <span class="encs">[predetermined time;scheduled time]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既辱且危，死其将至。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞下》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>其</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。甲骨文字形象簸箕形，即&ldquo;箕&rdquo;本字。金文又加声符&ldquo;丌&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> )，变成&ldquo;其&rdquo;。( <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> )本义:簸箕。今字作&ldquo;箕&rdquo;。&ldquo;其&rdquo;假借为代词:他、他们、那等)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 彼、他 <span class="encs">[he]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">郯子</span>之徒，其贤不及 <span class="diczx2">孔子</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:独善其身</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 她 <span class="encs">[she]</span>。如:萨奇打他的妻子，将其打倒在地</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 它 <span class="encs">[it]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或者不如说希望:希望其有，又希望其无。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">鲁迅</span><span class="smcs">《祝福》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 他们;她们;它们 <span class="encs">[they]</span>。如:避其锐气，击其惰归</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 他的 <span class="encs">[his]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">他日归，则有馈其兄生鹅者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:其妻</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 她的 <span class="encs">[her]</span>。如:其夫</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 它的 <span class="encs">[its]</span>。如:鸟之将死，其鸣也哀</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 他们的;她们的;它们的 <span class="encs">[their]</span>。如:他们思想保守，固守其老一套做法</p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 根据情况所指的、提到的或认为的那个<span class="encs">[人、物、意思或时间]</span> 的 <span class="encs">[that]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其日牛马嘶，新妇入青庐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔雀东南飞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:正当其时</p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 这样，如此 <span class="encs">[such]</span>。如:不乏其人;其然(如此)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>其</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 也许;大概 <span class="encs">[perhaps;probably;most likely]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善不可失，恶不可长，其</span><span class="diczx2">陈桓公</span>之谓乎!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公六年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其自</span><span class="diczx2">桓叔</span>以下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐国</span>其庶几乎。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>其庶几乎。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其一旦将以不敬之民而驱之战。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示祈使。当，可 <span class="encs">[can;may]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾其还也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君其详之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝其善抚之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弟其宽心勿畏可也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">太平天国</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪仁玕</span><span class="smcs">《英杰归真》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 将，将要 <span class="encs">[should]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今</span><span class="diczx2">殷</span>其沦丧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;微子》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 表示诘问。通&ldquo;岂&rdquo;，难道 <span class="encs">[Does it mean&hellip;?Shouldn&#39;t it be?]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其为死君乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其敢自谓几于成乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《朱文公校昌黎先生集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其可怪也欤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 极，甚 <span class="encs">[very]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">开地数千里，此其大功也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;初见秦》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>其</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈连〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 如果，假设 <span class="encs">[if]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 或许;大概 <span class="encs">[perhaps]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:其诸(或者);其者(或者)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 还是。表选择 <span class="encs">[still;yet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其真无马耶。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其真不知马耶。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其亦足乐乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>其</strong> <span class="dicpy">q&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/q&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">代</span>
<ol>
	<li>

<p>又助語辭。【書&middot;西伯戡黎】今王其如台。【詩&middot;周南】灼灼其華。【玉篇】辭也。</p>
<p>又姓。【韻會】漢陽阿侯其石。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>居之切，音姬。【韻會】語辭。【書&middot;微子】若之何其。【詩&middot;小雅】夜如何其。</p>
<p>又人名。【史記&middot;酈生傳】酈生食其者，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>留高陽人也。【註】正義曰：酈食其，三字三音，讀曆異幾。【前漢&middot;楚元王傳】高祖使審食其留侍太上皇。【註】師古曰：食音異，其音基。</p>
<p>又山名。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】四月，幸不其。【註】其音基，山名。【廣韻】在琅邪。</p>
<p>又地名。【韻會】祝其，卽夾谷也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】居吏切。【正韻】吉器切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音寄。【韻會】語已辭。【詩&middot;檜風】彼其之子。通作記。【禮&middot;表記】引《詩》彼記之子。</p>
<p>又通已。【左傳&middot;襄二十七年】引《詩》彼己之子。</p>
<p>又【韻會】或作忌。【詩&middot;鄭風】叔善射忌。</p>
