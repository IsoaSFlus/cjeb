                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蹙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蹙</strong> <span class="dicpy">cù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，戚声。本义:紧迫，急促)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[urgent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">政事愈蹙。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小明》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蹙变(急速变化)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 困窘 <span class="encs">[embarrassed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自吾氏三世居是乡，积于今六十岁矣，而乡邻之生日蹙。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:蹙迫(困窘，窘迫);蹙境(边境防务窘迫)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 愁苦的样子 <span class="encs">[worried;be in straitened circumstances]</span>。如:蹙怖(忧愁恐惧的样子);蹙然(忧愁不悦的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 局促不安的样子 <span class="encs">[respectful and careful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹙然衣粗食恶。——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:蹙促(局促不安的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 狭窄，狭小 <span class="encs">[narrow]</span>。如:蹙弱(迫窄弱小);蹙澳(水流狭窄弯曲)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>蹙</strong> <span class="dicpy">cù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 聚拢;皱缩 <span class="encs">[knit one's brows;frown]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举疾首蹙额而相告。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:蹙恨(皱起眉头表示怨恨);蹙沓(形容多而密集的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 逼迫;追逼 <span class="encs">[force;compel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹙也百里。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·召旻》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:蹙迫(逼迫);蹙促(逼迫);蹙击(迫击;追击)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 接近;迫近 <span class="encs">[be close to;approach]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今也日蹙国百里。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·召旻》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:蹙迫(逼近)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 缩小;减削 <span class="encs">[contract;lose]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:蹙頞(缩鼻哭泣);蹙土(蹙地。损失国土);蹙动(皱缩)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“蹴”</span>。踢;踏 <span class="encs">[kick;step on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以足蹙路马刍，有诛。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼上》</span></span></p><p><span
