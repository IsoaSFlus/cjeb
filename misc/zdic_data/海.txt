                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>海</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎi.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄞˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>靠近大陆，比洋小的水域：～洋。～域。～拔。～疆。～内。～岸。～誓山盟。五湖四～。</li>    <li>用于湖泊名称：青～。中南～。</li>    <li>容量大的器皿，巨大的：～碗。～涵（敬辞，称对方大度包容）。夸下～口。</li>    <li>喻数量多的人、事物像海的：人山人～。火～。</li>    <li>漫无目标地：～骂。～找。</li>    <li>古代指从外国来的：～棠。</li>    <li>特指中国上海：～派。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">海</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>海</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，每声。本义:大海，海洋)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 百川会聚之处。后指邻接大陆而区域范围小于洋的咸水水体 <span class="encs">[sea;ocean]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">海，天池也。以纳百川者。从水，每声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，海势圆，就地心也。海味咸，湿热之气蒸也。海气绿，穹苍之映，云雾不能隔也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祖天地之左海也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乡饮酒义》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“海水之委也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孙叔敖</span>举于海。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前途似海。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">半壁见海日。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:近海(靠近陆地的海域);沿海(靠海的一带);海岳(大海和山岳);海藏(大海。藏:海的总汇)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 海水 <span class="encs">[seawater]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">煮海为盐。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·晁错传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:海老(海水枯竭);海望(海水逢望日涨潮的现象)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 大湖或大池 <span class="encs">[lake]</span>。如:洱海;里海;咸海;北海</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古人认为陆地四周皆为海，故用以指僻远地区 <span class="encs">[border]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">九夷、八狄、七戎、六蛮谓之四海。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅·释地》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">作九夷八蛮六戎五狄、校人四海山川注:四海犹四方也。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·调人》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:海北(泛指僻远的北方);海夷(边境少数民族或来自海外的外国人);海土(海内的土地);海方(犹四海。极言广远之地)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 在月球表面或火星表面上一种相当大的暗区 <span class="encs">[mare]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 较大的器皿 <span class="encs">[large vessel]</span>。如:银海;墨海</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 大口，大嘴巴 <span class="encs">[big mouth]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">面皮微白，海下无须。此人就是</span><span class="diczx2">赛叔和</span> <span class="diczx2">李怀春</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">郭小亭</span><span class="smcs">《济公全传》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>海</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 很多的 <span class="encs">[many;much]</span></p><p><span
