data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，帝声。本义:放声痛心地哭)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[weep aloud]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">嗁，号也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“号，痛声也。”</span></p><p><span 又如:啼呼(哭叫，叫唤);啼饥(因饥饿而号哭);啼天哭地(非常悲惨);啼咽(悲哽)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 叫鸣 <span class="encs">[cry]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自在娇莺恰恰啼。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江畔独步寻花》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">千里莺啼绿映红。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《江南春绝句》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">渌水荡漾清猿啼。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p><p><span
