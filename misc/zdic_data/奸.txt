						<p>● <strong>奸</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>阴险，虚伪，狡诈：～人。～狡。～刁。～佞。～雄。～智（为非作歹的心计）。藏（<span class="dicpy">c&aacute;ng </span>）～。</li>
	<li>不忠于国家或自己一方的人：～细。内～。</li>
	<li>男女发生不正当的性行为：～淫。强～（亦指统治者把自己的意志强加于人民，如&ldquo;～～民意&rdquo;）。通～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">奸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>奸</strong> <span class="dicpy">gān</span></p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 干犯，抵触 <span class="encs">[offend;conflict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奸时以动，而疲民以逞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;成公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使而失命，召而不来，是再奸也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十年》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:奸，犯也。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以奸者七十二君，论先王之道而明周召之迹，一君无所钩用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;天运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">各守其职，不得相奸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;主术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以渔钓奸</span><span class="diczx2">周西伯</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;齐太公世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">察其驰骋步骤，芒炎或长或短，所历奸犯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五行志下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>奸</strong></p>
<p>姦 <span class="dicpy">jiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从三女。本义:奸邪，虚伪狡诈)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[evil;wicked;treacherous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">姦，私也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">姦，盗也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在内曰姦，在外曰宄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">用轨之财者为姦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;鲁语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寇贼姦宄。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以其民饥寒并至，故为奸衺(</span><span class="dicpy">xi&eacute;</span>同邪)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;辞过》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故以刑治则民威，民威则无姦，无姦则民安其所乐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;开塞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荡渨湋之姦咎兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《梦辞&middot;惜贤》</span>。注:&ldquo;乱在内曰姦。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盗器为姦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">多贼姦些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span>。注:&ldquo;恶也。&rdquo;</span></p>
