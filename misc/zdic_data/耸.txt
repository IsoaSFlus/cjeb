                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">耸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>耸</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">聳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǒng.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄨㄥˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>高起，直立：高～入云。～立。～拔。～峙。</li>
	<li>惊动：～动。～人听闻。</li>
	<li>聋：～昧（又聋又瞎，引申为昏聩）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> urge on; rise up; stir, excite</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> élever,exciter,ambitieux,sourd de naissance</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">耸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>耸</strong></p>
<p>聳 <span class="dicpy">sǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>(形声。从耳，从声。本义:耳聋) 同本义 <span class="encs">[deaf]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">子野</span>听耸， <span class="diczx2">离朱</span>目眩。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">马融</span><span class="smcs">《广成颂》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耸，聋也&hellip;生而聋，</span><span class="diczx2">陈</span>、 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>、 <span class="diczx2">江</span>、 <span class="diczx2">淮</span>之间谓之耸。 <span class="diczx2">荆</span><span class="diczx2">阳</span>之间及 <span class="diczx2">山</span>之东西双聋者谓之耸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">郭璞</span>注:&ldquo;言无所闻常耸耳也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耸昧(既聋又瞎。即昏瞶)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>耸</strong></p>
<p>聳 <span class="dicpy">sǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 高起;矗立 <span class="encs">[shoot high up;lofty;towering]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予观</span><span class="diczx2">雁荡</span>诸峰，皆峭拔险，上耸千尺。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耸人瞻仰(众人耸立瞻仰);耸秀(高耸秀丽);耸出(高耸突出);耸耳(高起的耳朵;竖起耳朵);耸然(高耸貌);耸峭(高耸陡峭);耸突(高耸突起);耸干(高挺的树干);耸擢(高耸突出);耸耸(竖起貌;挺立貌);耸翠(形容山峦、树木等高耸苍翠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 比喻出众，不凡 <span class="encs">[outstanding]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">又有一人，披紫裳，执青玉，貌耸神溢，立于君左。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:耸壑昂霄(矗立山谷间，高耸入云霄。比喻超拔出众，出人头地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 向上跳;向上动 <span class="encs">[jump up;draw up suddenly]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:耸擢(跳跃);耸体(犹耸身)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 劝勉 <span class="encs">[advise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">教之春秋而为之，耸善而抑恶焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语上》</span>。又如:耸恿(劝说);耸劝(劝导;警惕自勉)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;怂&rdquo;。怂恿 <span class="encs">[instigate]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:耸恿(怂恿);耸臾(怂恿);耸踊(怂恿);耸惑(怂恿诱惑);耸踊(怂恿)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 惊动;惊惧 <span class="encs">[alarm;shock;alert]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">耸之以行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">驷氏</span>耸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十九年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身耸除洁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">体轻似无骨，观者皆耸神。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《观柘枝舞》</span></p>

<p>（聳）【廣韻】息拱切【集韻】筍勇切【韻會】荀勇切【正韻】息勇切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音竦。【揚子&middot;方言】聳，聾也。生而聾，陳楚江淮之閒謂之聳。【註】言無所聞，常聳耳也。</p>
<p>又高也。【韓愈&middot;會合聯句】劒心知未死，詩思猶孤聳。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】聳，欲也。荆吳之閒曰聳。自關而西，秦晉之閒相勸曰聳。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】聳，悚也。【註】謂警聳也。【左傳&middot;成十四年】大夫聞之，無不聳懼。【楚語】昔殷武丁，能聳其德。【註】聳，敬也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】雙講切，音。義同。</p>
