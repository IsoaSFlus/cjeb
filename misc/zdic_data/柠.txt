                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>柠</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">檸</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&iacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&iacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄋㄧㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～檬〕①常绿小乔木，果实椭圆形，两端尖，淡黄色，味极酸，可制饮料，亦可提取柠檬油；②这种植物的果实。</li>
	<li>〔～檬素〕维生素的一种。亦称&ldquo;维生素Ｐ&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>〔～檬酸〕有机化合物。无色结晶，亦称&ldquo;枸橼酸&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>（<span class="diczx3">檸</span>）</li>
</ol>
