                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">置</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>置</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">➊寘</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>放，摆，搁：安～。布～。～放。～身。位～。～信。～评。～疑。～辩。推心～腹。～若罔闻。～之度外。</li>	<li>设立，设备：装～。设～。</li>	<li>购买：添～。～办。～备。～买。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> place, lay out; set aside</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> einbauen, installieren ,legen, ausgeben ,setzen, platzieren ,anrichten  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> placer,acheter,préparer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">置</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>置</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从网直，有亦声。网直宜赦。本义:赦罪，释放)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[release]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置，赦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">见十置一。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;尹赏传》</span>。注:&ldquo;放也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">高帝</span>曰:&ldquo;置之。&rdquo;乃释通之罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;淮阴侯列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斩首捕虏，比三百石以上者皆杀之，无有所置。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;吴王濞列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 安放;搁;摆 <span class="encs">[place;put]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">覆杯水于坳堂之上，则芥为之舟，置杯焉则胶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">项王</span>则受璧，置之坐上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置酒</span><span class="diczx2">长安</span>道，同心与我违。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《送綦母潜落第还乡》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:置锥之地(安身立足之地);置室(安置妻室;取妻);置酒(陈设酒筵);置社(古时大夫、士庶共同设置的供奉社神之所);置立(设立);安置;置措(措置);置散(安置在闲散的职位);置棋不定(弈棋时不知如何下子);置锥之地(安放锥子的地方。比喻极狭小的、赖以安身立命的地方);置手(插手)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 设置，建立，设立 <span class="encs">[establish;install;set up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>始征 <span class="diczx2">晋</span><span class="diczx2">河</span>东;置官司马。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公十五年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置园邑三百家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;霍光传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置以为像兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;九章》</span></span></p>
