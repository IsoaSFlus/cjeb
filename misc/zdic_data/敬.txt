                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>敬</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄥˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>尊重，有礼貌地对待：尊～。致～。～重（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）。～爱。～仰。恭～。～辞。～慕。～献。</li>	<li>表示敬意的礼物：喜～。寿～。</li>	<li>有礼貌地送上去：～酒。～香。</li>	<li>谨慎，不怠慢：慎始～终（自始自终都谨慎不懈）。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> respect, honor; respectfully</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> beachten, achten, respektieren  (V)​,bieten, anbieten  (V)​,ehren, verehren, die Ehre erweisen  (V)​,Salut schießen, salutieren  (V)​,Jing  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> offrir,saluer,respect</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">敬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>敬</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从攴( <span class="dicpy">pū</span>)，以手执杖或执鞭，表示敲打，从苟( <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span>)，有紧急、急迫之义。本义:恭敬;端肃。恭在外表，敬存内心)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[respectfully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬，肃也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毋不敬何允。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;在貌为恭，在心为敬。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宾客主恭，祭祀主敬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;少仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入门主敬，升堂主慎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬德之聚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬诺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬受命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬进</span><span class="diczx2">如姬</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬闻命关。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敬让(恭敬谦让);敬诚缉熙(恭敬诚实而光明);敬身(施礼);敬慎(恭敬谨慎)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 慎重地对待，不怠慢不苟且;敬谨 <span class="encs">[treat carefully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬，警也，恒自肃警也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释言语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既敬既戒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;常武》</span>。笺:&ldquo;敬之言警也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬事而信。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;学而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居敬而行简。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;雍也》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慎始而敬之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:敬事(谨慎做事;恭谨伺侯);敬信(慎重而守信);敬独(在独处时能谨慎不苟);敬逊(敬慎谦逊);敬虔(庄敬虔诚);敬明(谨慎严明)</span></p>
