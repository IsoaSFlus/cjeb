                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">五</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>五</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">wǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄨˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>数名，四加一（在钞票和单据上常用大写&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">伍</span>&rdquo;代）：～彩。～官。～谷。～金。～代（中国朝代名，后梁、后唐、后晋、后汉、后周先后在中原建立政权的时期）。～帝（中国传说中的五个帝王，通常指黄帝、颛顼、帝喾、唐尧、虞舜）。～毒（指蝎、蛇、蜈蚣、壁虎、蟾蜍）。～行（指金、木、水、火、土）。～岭（指越城岭，都庞岭，萌渚岭、骑田岭、大庾岭）。～岳（指东岳泰山、西岳华山、南岳衡山、北岳恒山和中岳嵩山，是中国历史上五大名山）。～脏（指心、肝、脾、肺、肾）。</li>
	<li>中国古代乐谱的记音符号，相当于简谱&ldquo;6&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">五</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>五</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>(五，会意。从二，从乂。&ldquo;二&rdquo;代表天地，&ldquo;乂&rdquo;表示互相交错。本义:交午，纵横交错) 同本义 <span class="encs">[cross]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五，阴阳在天地之间交午也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">葛屦五两。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;南山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">羔羊之皮，素丝五紽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;羔羊》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>五</strong> <span class="dicpy">wu</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">数</span></p>
<p>四加一的和 <span class="encs">[five]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五，数也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五载一巡守。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;舜典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">塞其五山。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五战于</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">起则五六揖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">监五室。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:五路总口(通往东、西、南、北、中五路的交叉口);五车腹笥(腹中装有五车书，比喻知识丰富，学问渊博);五方旗帜(按东、西、南、北、中五个方位，插上青、黄、赤、白、黑五色旗);五伯(春秋时先后称霸的齐桓公、晋文公、楚庄王、吴王阖闾、越王勾践。另一说指齐桓公、宋襄公、晋文公、秦穆公、楚庄王);五虫(鳞虫、羽虫、倮虫、毛虫、介虫的合称);五木(泛指古时的形具，如枷、镣、铐等);五浮(上等土壤);五细(指出身贱，年辈小，关系远，资历浅，地位低的五类人);五云(青、白、赤、黑、黄五种云色);五剽(白色粉状的中等土壤);五义(父义、母慈、兄友、弟恭、子孝)。又通&ldquo;伍&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五，伍与五，音同义别，官府文书五作伍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>五</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代军队编制单位，五人为伍 <span class="encs">[five]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">入行间之治连以五。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 偶;相类 <span class="encs">[pair;couple]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妻与后子死者，五皆丧之三年。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 工尺谱中的音名之一，表示音阶上的一级 <span class="encs">[a note of the scale in gongchepu]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 五行 <span class="encs">[the five elements]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五，五行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;水火木金土，相克相生，阴阳交午也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;伍&rdquo;。古军队编制，五人为伍;行列 <span class="encs">[troops]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行间之治，连以五，辨之以章，束之以令。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;画策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">孟贲</span>过于 <span class="diczx2">河</span>，先其五。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;必己》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 指五帝 <span class="encs">[five emperors]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:五三(五帝三王)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 星名。二十八宿中的昴五 <span class="encs">[star&#39;s name]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:五佐(谓佐天行德的五星);五括(星名);五纬(金、木、水、火、土五星)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 姓</p>
