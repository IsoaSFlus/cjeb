                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>似</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙˋ</span></p><ol><li>相类，像：相（<span class="dicpy">xiāng </span>）～。类～。～是而非。</li><li>好像，表示不确定：～乎。～应如此。</li><li>表示比较，有超过的意思：一个高～一个。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>似</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 〔～的〕跟某种情况或事物相似，如“他乐得什么～～”。亦作“是的”（“<span class="diczx3">的</span>”均读轻声）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> resemble, similar to; as if, seem</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> ähneln, erscheinen, scheinen, scheinen wie  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> comme,comme,semblable,pareil</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">似</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>似</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈助〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用在名词、代词或动词后面，表示比况，相当于<span class="yhcs">“似的”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“如…一般”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">那后生就空地当中，把一条棒使得风车儿似转。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">sì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/似的">似的</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>似</strong></p><p>佀 <span class="dicpy">sì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，以声。本义:像;相像)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[look like;similar]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">似，象也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">似，有以相攖，有不相櫻也。——<span class="smcs">《墨子经上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见似目瞿。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·杂记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓容貌似其父母。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寡人虽无似也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·哀公问》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“无似犹言不肖。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">似，类也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凄然似秋，暖然似春。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·大宗师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫马似鹿者而题之千金。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说右上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玉人之所患，患石之似玉者。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·疑似》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今世之主法先王之法也，有似于此。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·察今》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">布衾多年冷似铁，娇儿恶卧踏里裂。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凄凄不似向前声，满座重闻皆掩泣。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span
