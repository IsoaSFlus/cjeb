						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>缀</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">綴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>缝：补～。～上几针。</li><li>连接：连～。～文（即作文章）。～辞。～集。～辑。笔不停～。</li><li>装饰：点～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">缀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>缀</strong></p><p>綴 <span class="dicpy">zhuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sew;stitch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缀，合箸也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缀衣。——<span class="smcs">《书·立政》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“掌衣服。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">出缀衣于庭。——<span class="smcs">《书·顾命》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为下国缀旒。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·长发》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缀甲厉兵。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策一》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:缀甲(缝制甲衣);缀拾(采集连缀);缀纯(连缀杂彩以为缘边);缀葺(修补;缝补);缀缀(相连缀的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 著作。组织文字以成篇章 <span class="encs">[write;compose;compile]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伺其疲倦，即谕之缀诗赋。——</span><span class="diczx2">白行简</span><span class="smcs">《李娃传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">李藩</span>侍郎尝缀 <span class="diczx2">李贺</span>歌诗，为之集序未成。——<span class="smcs">《李长吉歌诗》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夜书细字缀语言，雨目眵昏头雪白。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《短灯檠歌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:缀集(连缀聚集。常指著述，编辑);缀法(旧时小学里教导学生作文、造句的方法。连缀已读过的文字，改白话为文言，或改文言为白话，均属缀法之列)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 装饰 <span class="encs">[decorate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饰以文犀，雕以翠绿，缀以骊龙之珠，错以</span><span class="diczx2">荆山</span>之玉。—— <span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《七启》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赤缀户也，白缀牖也。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:缀映(点缀映衬);缀饰(点缀装饰)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 连结 <span class="encs">[connect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缀，连也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缀鬼谷于北辰。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·远逝》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青树翠曼，蒙络摇缀，参差披拂。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">缀行甚远。(紧随着走了很远。缀，连结，这里是紧跟的意思。)——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:缀恩(联系亲情);缀行(连接成行);缀连(组合;连属);缀接(联系);缀组(系结印绶)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 点缀散布 <span class="encs">[stud]</span>。如:天上缀满了星星</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>缀</strong></p><p>綴 <span class="dicpy">zhuì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 词缀 <span class="encs">[affix]</span>。如:前缀;后缀;中缀</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 物件边缘的装饰 <span class="encs">[ornament on the edge of sth.]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纲户朱缀，刻方连些。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/缀合">缀合</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
