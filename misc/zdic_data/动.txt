                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>动</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">動</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dòng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dòng.mp3"></a></span> ㄉㄨㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>改变原来位置或脱离静止状态，与“<span class="diczx3">静</span>”相对：变～。波～。浮～。振～（物体通过一个中心位置，不断作往复运动。亦称“振荡”）。震～（a.颤动或使颤动，如“门窗～～了一下”；b.重大事情或消息使人心不平静，如“～～全国”）。</li><li>使开始发生：发～。</li><li>使用：～用。～武。～问（客套话，请问）。</li><li>使起作用或变化，使感情起变化：感～。～人心弦。娓娓～听。～容。</li><li>吃（多用于否定式）：这几天不～荤腥。</li><li>非静止的：～画。</li><li>可变的：～产。</li><li>行为：举～。～作。</li><li>常常：～辄得咎。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">动</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>动</strong></p><p>動 <span class="dicpy">dòng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dòng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，重声。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>古文从<span class="yhcs">“辵”</span>。本义:行动;发作)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[act;break out;show effect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">动，作也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">动而健。——<span class="smcs">《易·彖传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“震也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">效天下之动者也。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“发也，变动不居。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将终岁勤动。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“作也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">度义而后动。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寨中人蜷伏不少动。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:按兵不动;动着(开始下棋);动止(行动和静止;训练调度);动烦(爆发战乱);动转(行动，活动)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 摇动，震动，移动。与<span class="yhcs">“静”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[move;stir]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风移影动。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">秋风之动容兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·抽思》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">物莫之能动。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·论威》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夜间，大鱼动。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·内储说上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:动耽(动弹);动履(走动);动展(伸展;活动);动息(动静;消息);动惮(动静);动程(动身;起程);风吹草动;别动人家的东西;振动;活动;动起(方言。动不动);动移(移动);动步(迈步前行);动不得;不能动</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 动摇;震撼 <span class="encs">[wave;shake;shock]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忽魂悸以魄动，恍惊起而长嗟。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥呤留别》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">所以动心忍性，曾益其所不能。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">风雨不动安如山。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span
