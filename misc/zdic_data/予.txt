						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>予</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 同“<span class="diczx3">余</span>”，我：～智自雄（自夸聪明，妄自夸大）。～取～求（原指从我这里取，从我这里求；后指任意索取）。</p></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>予</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˇ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 给与：～以。赐～。赋～。给～。生杀～夺（亦作“生杀与夺”）。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">予</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>予</strong> <span class="dicpy">yú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈代〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="yhcs">“予”</span>假借为<span class="yhcs">“余”</span>，我 <span class="encs">[I， me]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予独爱莲之出淤泥而不染。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周敦颐</span><span class="smcs">《爱莲说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然而累汝至此者，未尝非予之过也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:予知(自认为聪明);予美(自己所爱的人);予圣(自以为圣人);予一人(古代帝王的自称);予小子(古代帝王对先王或长辈的自称;常人对先辈、长者的自称);予末小子(予冲人，予冲子。古代帝王对先王或长辈的自称;常人对先辈、长者的自称);予取予携(予取予夺。从我处掠取);予违汝弼(古代天子勖勉大臣进谏之词。言我有过失，你应匡正);予智予雄(自以为聪明和英雄;形容妄自尊大)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">yǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/予取予求">予取予求</a></span></div>　<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>予</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。象两手相与之形。本义:授予;给予)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bestow;give grant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予，推予也。象相予之形，谓手拔。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予，与也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予之与夺也。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·本经》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子来朝，何锡予之?——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·干旄》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">怒不过夺，喜不过予。——<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:予宁(给丧假);予齿去角(天生动物赋予齿就不赋予角。比喻事物无十全十美);予赐(予告和赐告);予归(准予辞官回家);予夺生杀(泛指帝王掌握的生死大权);予决(给予决定或裁决)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 赞许，称誉 <span class="encs">[praise]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主盛处贤，而自予雄也。——<span class="smcs">《管子·宙合》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众必予之。——<span class="smcs">《管子·小匡》</span></span></p><p><span
