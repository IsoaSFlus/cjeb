                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>者</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhě  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhě.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄜˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用在名词、动词、形容词、数词、词组后，并与其相结合，指人、指事、指物、指时等：读～。作～。二～必居其一。来～。</li>
	<li>助词，表示语气停顿并构成判断句的句式：陈胜～，阳城人也。</li>
	<li>这，此（多用在古诗词曲中）：～个。～回。～番。～边走。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">者</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>者</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhě.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:&ldquo;别事词也。相当于&ldquo;这&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[this]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">那边走，者边走，莫厌金杯酒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">五代</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王衍</span><span class="smcs">《醉妆词》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:者回(这回);者般(这般);者流(犹言这个流派);者番(这番;这次);者里(这里);者个(这个);者边(这边)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用在形容词、动词、动词词组或主谓词组之后，组成&ldquo;者&rdquo;字结构，用以指代人、事、物</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指代人</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">知人者智，自知者明。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">射者中。(射的射中了目标。这里指宴饮时的一种游戏，射中的照规定的杯数喝酒。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">读书者有几。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后死者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略序》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指代事或物</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">逝者如斯夫!不舍昼夜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾君在前，叱者何也?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 用在数词之后，指代上文所说的几种人或几件事 <span class="encs">[that]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老而无妻曰鳏，老而无夫曰寡，老而无子曰独，幼而无父曰孤。此四者，天下之穷民而无告者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二者不可得兼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>者</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhě</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhě.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈助〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用在表时间的名词后面，表示停顿</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔者，吾舅死于虎，吾夫又死焉，今吾子又死焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 作为定语后置的标志</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">马之千里者，一食或尽粟一石。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《杂说》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用于名词之后，标明语音上的停顿，并引出下文，常表示判断</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仁者，天下之表也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北山</span><span class="diczx2">愚公</span>者，年且九十。(者，语气助词，表提顿。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中有</span><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>人 <span class="diczx2">冯三保</span>者，精技击。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不知忠义者圣贤家法，其气浩然，常留天地间。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用在句末表示语气完毕</p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">代</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">人或事物的代稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「記<b>者</b>」、「作<b>者</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;雍也》：「知者樂水，仁者樂山。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《楚辭&middot;屈原&middot;漁父》：「新沐者必彈冠，新浴者必振衣。」</span></p></li>

</ol></div>
