                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>昌</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chāng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄤˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>兴盛：～盛（<span class="dicpy">sh&egrave;ng</span>）。～乐（<span class="dicpy">l&egrave;</span>）（兴盛康乐）。～明（兴盛发达）。～隆。</li>	<li>善，正当：～言（美言，正当的话）。～言无忌（直言无隐）。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/菖">菖</a>&rdquo;，菖蒲。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/猖">猖</a>&rdquo;，凶猛。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> light of sun; good, proper</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> prospère,florissant,abondant,lumineux,splendide</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昌</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>昌</strong> <span class="dicpy">chāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从日，从曰。本义:善，正当)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[good]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昌，美言也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>拜昌言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大禹谟》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昌言(正直的善言、好话);昌教(善美的教化)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 兴旺发达，与&ldquo;亡&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[flourishing]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">邦乃其昌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;仲虺之诰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">顺之者昌，逆之者不死则亡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;太史公自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今中国未闻有因变法而流血者，此国之所以不昌也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昌瑞(昌盛祥瑞);昌乐(兴盛安乐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 光;光明 <span class="encs">[light]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一曰日光。诗曰:东方昌昌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昌晖(光明);昌华(光华)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 壮大;美好 <span class="encs">[great;fine]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猗嗟昌兮，颀而长兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;齐风&middot;猗嗟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昌大(盛大);昌化(大的恩泽);昌世(太平盛世)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>昌</strong> <span class="dicpy">chāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 生物 <span class="encs">[living things;living beings]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今夫百昌皆生于土而反于土。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;在宥》</span>。释文:&ldquo;百昌，犹百物也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;菖&rdquo;。 <span class="encs">[sweet flag]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:昌本(菖蒲根);昌羊(即菖蒲)</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昌</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上" target="_blank">辰集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集上&amp;bs=日" target="_blank">日</a>】
                              昌·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=8" target="_blank">8</a>　·部外筆画：4</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/660C.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】尺良切【集韻】【韻會】蚩良切【正韻】齒良切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音倡。【說文】美言也。【爾雅&middot;釋詁】昌，當也。【書&middot;大禹謨】禹拜昌言曰。【傳】昌，當也。</p><p>又【博雅】盛也。【書&middot;仲虺之誥】邦乃其昌。【傳】國乃昌盛。</p><p>又【詩&middot;鄭風】子之昌兮。【傳】昌，盛壯貌。</p><p>又【詩&middot;齊風】猗嗟昌兮。【箋】昌，狡好貌。</p><p>又【說文】一曰日光也。詩曰：東方昌矣。○按《詩&middot;齊風&middot;雞鳴》作東方明矣，朝旣昌矣。與《說文》所引不同。</p><p>又物也。【莊子&middot;在宥篇】今夫百昌，皆生於土而反於土。【註】司馬云：百昌猶百物也。</p><p>又【儀禮&middot;公食大夫禮】昌本。【註】昌，蒲。本，菹也。</p><p>又【史記&middot;天官書】斗魁戴匡六星，曰文昌宮。【註】文者，精所聚。昌者，揚天紀，輔拂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>居，以成天象。</p><p>又【集韻】尺亮切，音唱。倡，或作昌。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">昌</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=日">日</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/660C.svg">                          <p>美言也。从日从曰。一曰日光也。《詩》曰：&ldquo;東方昌矣。&rdquo;<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/660C_1.svg" /> <img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2314A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣅊</span>，籒文昌。尺良切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：曰，亦言也。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/660C_2533.svg" /><span class="swbf">(昌)</span><span class="ywyy">美言也。</span><span class="swdz">咎繇謨曰：禹拜昌言。今文尙書作黨。趙注孟子引尙書：禹拜黨言。逸周書祭公解：拜手稽首黨言。張平子碑：黨言允諧。劉寛碑：對策嘉黨。皆昌言字之叚借也。至於讜言，亦見漢人文字。字林：讜言，美言也。此又因黨言而爲之言傍，謂之正俗字可。</span><span class="ywyy">从日从曰。</span><span class="swdz">會意，取縣諸日月不刊之意也。不入曰部者，日至尊也。尺良切。十部。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰日光也。</span><span class="swdz">裴松之引易運期讖曰：㒳日並光日居午。㒳日，昌字。圖讖說字多不合本義。裴引孝經中黃讖：<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23358.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣍘</span>爲日載東。曹字亦本从曰，非从日。葢昌之本義訓美言，引伸之爲凡光盛之偁，則亦有訓爲日光者。日光袛爲餘義，例所不載。一曰日光也五字恐魏時因許昌之說而妄增之。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：東方昌矣。</span><span class="swdz">齊風：東方明矣，朝旣昌矣。傳曰：東方明則夫人纚筓而朝。朝巳昌盛，則君聽朝。云朝巳昌盛與美言之義相應。許幷二句爲一句，當由轉寫筆誤。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/660C_2534.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2314A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣅊</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文。</span></p>                          </div>
