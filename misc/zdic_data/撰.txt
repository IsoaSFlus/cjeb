data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，巽( <span class="dicpy">xùn</span>)声。本义:写作，纂集成整体)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[write]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">隐士休歌紫芝曲，词人解撰河清颂。——</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《洗兵马》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">撰长书以为贽。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:撰文(写文章);撰次(写作;记述);撰刻(撰写而勒刻);撰修(书写。编撰);撰碑(撰写碑文)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 纂集 <span class="encs">[compile]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顷撰其遗文，都为一集。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹丕</span><span class="smcs">《与吴质书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:撰具(编成);撰定(编定;写定);撰造(创作;编制)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 制造 <span class="encs">[make]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃采凫氏，撰鸣钟，火天地之炉，扇阴阳之炭。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《化城寺大钟铭》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 建造 <span class="encs">[build]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">撰得绝似，但欠鸡鸣犬吠耳。——</span><span class="diczx2">罗大经</span><span class="smcs">《鹤林玉露》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 拿住，持 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">撰杖屦。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹持也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">撰余辔兮高驼</span><span class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 数 <span class="encs">[natural law]</span> 指天地阴阳等自然现象的变化规律</p><p><span class="diczx1">阴阳合德，而刚柔有体，以体天地之撰。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 才具，才干 <span class="encs">[talent;ability]</span></p><p><span 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中" target="_blank">卯集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=卯集中&amp;bs=手" target="_blank">手</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/64B0.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】雛莞切【集韻】【韻會】雛綰切【正韻】雛產切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>饌上聲。具也。【增韻】造也。</p><p>又則也。【易·繫辭】以體天地之撰。【朱子·本義】撰猶事也。【論語】異乎三子者之撰。</p><p>又撰，述也。屬辭記事曰撰。【唐書·百官志】史館修撰掌修國史。或作篹。</p><p>又【唐韻】仕免切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】雛免切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音譔。讀若孱上聲。義同。通作譔。</p><p>又作僎。</p><p>又持也。【禮·曲禮】君子欠伸撰杖屨。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】雛戀切【正韻】除戀切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音饌。義同。</p><p>又集也。亦述也。【歐陽修詩】任君居太原，白首勤著撰。閉戸不求聞，忽來誰所薦。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>須兗切。與選同。遣也。一曰擇也。【周禮·夏官·大司馬】羣吏撰車徒。【釋文】息轉反。【禮·內則】栗曰撰之。【疏】數數布揀撰省視之。</p><p>又白撰，貨貝名。【前漢·食貨志】白金三品，其一曰重八兩，圜之，名白撰，直三千。</p><p>又【集韻】須絹切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/240BF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤂿</span>。亦與選同。</p><p>又損管切。與算同。數也，計也。【周禮·撰車徒註】撰讀曰算，謂數擇之也。</p>
