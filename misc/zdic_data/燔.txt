                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>燔</strong> <span class="dicpy">fán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，番声。本义:焚烧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[burn]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燔，爇也。从火，番声。与焚略同。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">藉芿燔林。——<span class="smcs">《列子·黄帝》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燔之于四通之衢。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·东方朔传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“焚烧也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">体若燔炭。——<span class="smcs">《素问·生气通天论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抱木而燔死。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·盗跖》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燔其茏城。——<span class="smcs">《史记·平津侯生父传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人民饥饿，相燔烧以求食。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·宣帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">燔诗书——<span class="smcs">《汉书·儒林传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不若燔土。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:燔燎(火把);燔石(焙烧矿石);燔刑(烧人至死的残酷刑罚);燔燃(燃烧);燔销(烧毁) 火烧整只的牲畜<span class="encs">[祭祀]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 火烧整只的牲畜 <span class="encs">[祭祀]</span> <span class="encs">[roast]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有免斯首，炮之燔之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·瓠叶》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为俎孔硕，或燔或炙。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·楚茨》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:燔柴(将玉帛、牺牲等置于积柴上而焚之以祭天);燔祀(焚柴祭祀)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/燔祭">燔祭</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
