                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">藻</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>藻</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄠˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>隐花植物的一大类，无根、茎、叶等部分的区别，有叶绿素可以自己制造养料，种类很多，海水和淡水里都有，极少数可生活在陆地的阴湿地方：水～。海～。小球～。～类植物。</li>
	<li>泛指生长在水中的植物，亦包括某些水生的高等植物：狸～。金鱼～。</li>
	<li>指华丽的文彩、文辞：～思（多采的文思）。～丽。～绘（文采）。～井（中国传统建筑物天花板上一方一方的彩画）。～镜（指品评鉴别。亦称&ldquo;藻鉴&rdquo;）。辞～。～饰。</li>
	<li>古代帝王冕上系玉的五彩丝绳：玉～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> splendid, magnificent; algae</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Alge  (S, Bio)​,Algen  (S, Bio)​,elegante Verzierung ( sprachlich, literarisch )​  (S, Lit)​,Wasserpflanze  (S, Bio)​,blumige Sprache  (S, Lit)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> algue,plantes aquatiques</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">藻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>藻</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，澡声。本义:藻类植物，古专指水藻)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[algae]</span>。隐花植物的一大类，没有根、茎、叶等部分的区别，有叶绿素可以自己制造养料，种类很多，海水和淡水里都有</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">薄采其藻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂&middot;泮水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蘋蘩蕰藻之菜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公三年》</span>。注:&ldquo;聚藻也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于以采藻，于彼行潦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;采蘋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:藻火(水藻和火焰。古时官吏绣在官服上的图案)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指水草 <span class="encs">[aquatic plants]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水中藻行交横。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《志林》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:藻川(长有绿色水藻的河川);藻花(水边泽草所开的花);金鱼藻;狸藻等</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 词藻，华丽的文辞 <span class="encs">[literary embellishment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">攦藻如春华。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《答宾戏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:藻拔(文采出众);藻朗(文辞清明);藻咏(以华美文辞咏诵。指诗文而言);藻雅(文辞典雅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 文采 <span class="encs">[rich and bright colours]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">华藻繁缛。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《七启》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:藻采(文采，辞采);藻思(做文章的才思)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;璪&rdquo;<span class="encs">[pieces of jade stringed with silk thread hanging from a coronet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子玉藻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">婴用一藻玉瘗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;中山经》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>藻</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p> 华美 <span class="encs">[magnificent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敷藻翰之陪鳃。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">潘岳</span><span class="smcs">《射雉赋》</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              藻·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=22" target="_blank">22</a>　·部外筆画：16</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/85FB.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>子皓切，音早。【說文】水草也。【詩&middot;召南】于以采藻。【箋】藻之言澡也。【陸璣云】生水底。</p>
<p>又【班固&middot;東都賦】鋪鴻藻。【陸機&middot;文賦序】故作《文賦》，以述先士之盛藻。【註】《孔安國&middot;尚書傳》曰：藻，水草之有文者，以喻文焉。</p>
<p>又【後漢&middot;劉陶傳】武旅有鳧藻之士。【註】鳧得水藻，言喜悅也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】今屋上覆橑，謂之藻井。【風俗通】宮室象東井，刻荷蔆水草，所以厭火也。【張衡&middot;西京賦】蒂倒茄于藻井。</p>
<p>又藻藉，所以薦玉者。詳藉字註。</p>
<p>又姓。【正字通】南北朝有藻重。</p>
<p>又【集韻】側絞切，音爪。義同，或作薻、<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27142.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧅂</span>。【干祿字書】俗作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26E3C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦸼</span>。</p>
