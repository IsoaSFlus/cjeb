                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>仟</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qiān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄢˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>“<span class="diczx3">千</span>”的大写。</li>    <li>古代军队中千人之长。</li>    <li>古同“<span class="diczx3">阡</span>”，阡陌。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> one thousand; leader of one thousand men</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> 1000, tausend (alternative Form für Finanzwesen, fälschungssicherer)​</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mille</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>仟</strong> <span class="dicpy">qiān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从千。本义:古代军制，千人之长)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[leader of one thousand men]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蹑足行伍之间，絻仰仟佰之中。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马贞</span>索隐:<span class="yhcs">“仟佰，谓千人百人之长也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="yhcs">“千”</span>的大写。多用于记帐、汇款等书写金额的场合 <span class="encs">[thousand]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有仟佰之得。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志上》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“仟谓千钱也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:仟佰(千钱与百钱。借指利息，盈余)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“阡”</span>。田间小道 <span class="encs">[foot path between fields]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">众庶街巷有马，仟佰之间成群。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:仟佰(田间小道)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 草木茂盛的样子 <span class="encs">[lush]</span>。如:仟眠(草木茂盛的样子)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>仟</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄑㄧㄢ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>qiān <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3649.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">千的大写。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">职官名。古代军队中一千人的指挥者。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷四八·陈涉世家》：「蹑足行伍之闲，俛仰<b>仟</b>佰之中。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·司马贞·索隐：「<b>仟</b>佰，谓千人百人之长也。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">田间的小路。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷二四·食货志上》：「坏井田，开仟伯。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐·颜师古·注：「仟伯，田间之道也。南北曰<b>仟</b>，东西曰伯。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「阡」。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
