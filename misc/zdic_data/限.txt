						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>限</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>指定的范围：期～。界～。权～。局～。～额。</li>
	<li>指定范围：～制。～于。～期。～价（官方指定最高或最低价格，不得超越）。无～。</li>
	<li>门槛：门～。</li>
	<li>险阻：关～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">限</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>限</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜，艮( <span class="dicpy">g&egrave;n</span>)声。阜，土山，与山势有关。本义:阻隔)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[separate;cut off;sunder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">限，阻也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">限之以大故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;怀沙》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">牵牛织女遥相望，尔独何辜限河梁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">曹丕</span><span class="smcs">《燕歌行》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:限绝(隔绝);限隔(阻隔;隔绝);限带(阻隔围绕);限难(阻难)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 限制;限定 <span class="encs">[limit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是身如浮云，安可限南北。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《别赞上人》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:限状(限期完成公务的文书)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>限</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 险阻 <span class="encs">[danger and barrier]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南有</span><span class="diczx2">巫山</span>， <span class="diczx2">黔</span>中之限。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 限期 <span class="encs">[deadline]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">限六日内交。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不可限以时月。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">留待限期。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">严限追比。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:限次(期限);限尽(寿命到了期限);限外(逾期)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 界限、边界、分界线(如领土、运动场所、田地等) <span class="encs">[boundary]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">限，界也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">长城之限。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《惜抱轩诗文集&middot;登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:东限琅琊台</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 限度，止境 <span class="encs">[limits]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心中无限事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地有限。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:权限(职权范围);宽限(放宽限期);极限;上限;下限;限级(限度等级)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 门槛 <span class="encs">[threshold]</span></p>
