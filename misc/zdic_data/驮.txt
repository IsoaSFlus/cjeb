                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>驮</strong></p><p>馱 <span class="dicpy">duò</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 驮着货物的牲口或牲口所负载的货物 <span class="encs">[goods on draught animals]</span>。如:驮垛(又称驮担、驮装、驮子。捆扎成垛供驮运的货物或行李)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">tuó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><strong>基本词义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>驮</strong></p><p>馱 <span class="dicpy">tuó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tuó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，大声。本作<span class="yhcs">“佗”</span>。<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>:<span class="yhcs">“负何也。”</span>本义:骡马负载东西)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carry on the back]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">驮，负物也。——<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其奇畜则橐佗。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·匈奴传上》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“橐佗，言能负橐囊而驮物也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:驮绳(捆缚负载之物所用的绳子);驮银;驮背(（ <span class="dicpy">tuóbēi</span>）。牲口负载东西);驮骡(专用来驮东西的骡子);驮家(驮户。专事驮运业务的人家);驮畜(专门用于驮东西的牲畜)。引申为用背负载。如:驮不动</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 挨;受 <span class="encs">[suffer]</span></p><p><span
