						<p>● <strong>堇</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jǐn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄣˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～菜〕草本植物，花白色，带紫色条纹，全草可入药，亦称&ldquo;堇堇菜&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>〔紫～〕草本植物，花紫色。全草味苦，可入药。</li>
</ol>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>堇</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>假借为&ldquo;仅&rdquo;。少的 <span class="encs">[few;little]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堇堇物之所有。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">豫章</span>出黄金，然堇堇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;地理志下》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>堇</strong> <span class="dicpy">jǐn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jǐn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 菜名。堇菜属植物泛称的略称 <span class="encs">[violet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堇，堇草也。根如荠，叶如细柳，蒸食之甘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">堇荁枌榆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:三色堇</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 中药乌头，有毒 <span class="encs">[aconite]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:堇色;堇荼(植物名。堇和荼);堇菜</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5807.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】巨斤切【集韻】【韻會】渠斤切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音勤。【說文】黏土也。【徐曰】黃土乃黏。【五代史】劉守光圍滄州，城中雜食堇塊。</p>
<p>又時也。【管子&middot;五行篇】修槩水土，以待乎天堇。亦作墐。</p>
<p>又【集韻】渠吝切，音覲。塗也。</p>
<p>又與僅㢙通。【博雅】堇少也。【前漢&middot;地理志】豫章出黃金，然堇堇物之所有。</p>
<p>又【集韻】居焮切，音靳。國名。堇子國，在寧波奉化縣東，境內有赤堇山。【越絕書】薛燭與越王說劒，赤堇之山破而出錫，卽今鄞地。</p>
<p>又堇隂，地名，在晉。○按烏頭堇茶之堇从艸，《字彙》附此，非。　【毛氏韻增】从廿不从卄，監本下从二畫，亦誤。</p>
