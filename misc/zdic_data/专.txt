                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">专</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>专</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">專</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuān.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>单纯、独一、集中在一件事上：～长（<span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng </span>）。～使。～心致志。</li>
	<li>独自掌握和占有：～利。～车。～有。～断。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> monopolize, take sole possession</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> besondere, gesammelt, konzentriert</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">专</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>专</strong></p>
<p>專、耑 <span class="dicpy">zhuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。甲骨文字形，右边象纺塼( <span class="dicpy">zhuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuān.mp3"></a></span> )形，即&ldquo;叀( <span class="dicpy">zhuan</span>);左边是手(寸)，合起来为用手纺织。&ldquo;叀&rdquo;兼作声符。本义:纺锤)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 纺专，收丝器 <span class="encs">[spindle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">专，纺专。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作塼，作甎，俗作磚。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">载弄之瓦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;斯干》</span>。传:&ldquo;瓦，纺塼也。&rdquo;按，原始的陶制纺锤。</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 专长 <span class="encs">[speciality]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:一专多能</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 中等专科学校(即只教授某一项或一些专门技术的学校)的简称 <span class="encs">[polytechnic school]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:新城工专;美镇医专;兴隆商专</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>专</strong></p>
<p>專 <span class="dicpy">zhuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 独占;独用 <span class="encs">[sew up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">专禄以周旋，戮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">吴</span>王擅鄣海泽， <span class="diczx2">邓通</span>专西山。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《盐铁论&middot;错币》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">罔俾</span><span class="diczx2">阿衡</span>(即伊尹)专美有商。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;说命下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">专其利三世矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衣食所安，弗敢专也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敢专承之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与其卖而分，孰若吾得专焉?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:专功(独占其功);专道(独占道路);专贵(独占尊位);专美(独享美名);专宠(独占宠爱);专据(独占)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 使专一;使集中 <span class="encs">[concentrate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不专心致志则不得也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其静也专，其动也直。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞上》</span></span></p>
