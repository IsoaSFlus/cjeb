                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>吻</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">wěn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄣˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>嘴唇：～合（意见、看法完全相同）。</li>	<li>用嘴唇接触表喜爱、亲热：接～。～别。</li>	<li>动物的嘴。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> kiss; the lips; coinciding</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kuss  (S)​,küssen  (V)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">吻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>吻</strong></p>
<p>肳 <span class="dicpy">wěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，勿声。本义:嘴唇)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[lips]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吻，唇两角头边也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《华严经音义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">黄吻澜漫赤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《娇女诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂鼓吻奋爪以向先生。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:接吻;亲吻</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 嘴;口 <span class="encs">[mouth]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使人之吻助己言谈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚同中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:吻舌(吻唇。口和舌);吻吮(用口吹奏);吻流(利口之辈);吻创(口疮);吻翰(口与笔);吻噤(闭口);吻儒(善谑利口的儒生)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 语气;口气 <span class="encs">[mood]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:口吻</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 指鸱吻。古建筑屋脊两端的装饰物 <span class="encs">[ornament on roof ridge，in the shape of a legendary animal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其制设吻者为殿，无吻者不为殿矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">叶梦得</span><span class="smcs">《石林燕语》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>吻</strong> <span class="dicpy">wěn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wěn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 合;闭 <span class="encs">[close]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道士摇着铃注卵子，念几句生意经，吻了材盖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张南庄</span><span class="smcs">《何典》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:吻着唇点点头</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 亲嘴 <span class="encs">[kiss]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他渴望着吻她一下</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 紧接;相符合 <span class="encs">[coincide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">闽城</span>吻海而派江，辅山以居。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈亚之</span><span class="smcs">《闽城开新池记》</span></p>
