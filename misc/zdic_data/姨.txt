						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>姨</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˊ</span></p><ol>    <li>称母亲的姊妹：～母。～妈。～父。</li>    <li>妻的姊妹：大～子。小～子。</li>    <li>妾：～太太。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>姨</strong> <span class="dicpy">yí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，夷声。本义:妻子的姐妹)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wife’s sister]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">姨，妻之女弟同出为姨。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妻之姊妹同出为姨。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">蔡哀侯</span>曰吾姨也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公十年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">东宫之妹，</span><span class="diczx2">邢侯</span>之姨。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·硕人》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">毛</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“妻之姊妹曰姨。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其妻又吾姨也。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">潘安</span><span class="smcs">《寡妇赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:姨夫(妻的姊妹夫;又两男共狎一妓，彼此互称姨夫)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 母之姊妹 <span class="encs">[mother’s sister;aunt]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">继室以其侄，</span><span class="diczx2">穆姜</span>之姨子也。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“据父言之，谓之姨，据子言之，当谓之从母，但子效父语，亦呼为姨。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:姨丈人(妻之姨夫);姨弟(姨表弟)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 父亲的小老婆;庶母 <span class="encs">[father’s concubine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">母之姊妹曰姨。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其父之侧庶，亦称姨者，姨本姊妹同事一夫之称，后世无媵之礼，而侧庶实与媵比，故虽非母姊妹，而得借比称之。——</span><span class="diczx2">翟灏</span><span class="smcs">《通俗篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:姨姨(称父之妾;称所亲狎的妓女);姨夫钱(可供循环挪用的他人的钱财)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
