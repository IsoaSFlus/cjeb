                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">版</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>版</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄢˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>上面有文字或图形的用木板或金属等制成供印刷用的东西：木～书。活字～。底～。修～。</li>
	<li>印刷物排印一次（可多次印刷）及有关的事物：排～。再～。～本。出～。～权。</li>
	<li>报纸的一面：头～头条重大新闻。</li>
	<li>打土墙用的夹板：～筑（筑土墙）。</li>
	<li>户籍：～图（原指户籍和地图，现泛指国家疆域，如&ldquo;我国～～辽阔&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> printing blocks; edition</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Auflage  (S)​,Ausgabe  (S, Lit)​,Druckplatte  (S)​,Seite</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> registre,plaque d'impression,cliché,édition,page,version</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">版</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>版</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从片，反声。&ldquo;片&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">pi&agrave;n</span>)，意思是破开的木片或草片。从&ldquo;片&rdquo;的字大都与木板有关。本义:筑墙的夹板)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[slab]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">版，判也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，判木也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">傅说</span>举于版筑之间。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五板(版)而堵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;定公十二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">缩版以载。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;绵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又指版筑的土墙</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝济而夕设版焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:版筑</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 供建筑或其他使用的木板。后作&ldquo;板&rdquo; <span class="encs">[board]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:版障(木板制的屏风);版屋(用木板建造的房屋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 牍。古时书写用的木简，后也指书籍 <span class="encs">[wood chip]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修业不息版。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;宙合》</span>。注:&ldquo;牍也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">送版使</span><span class="diczx2">王<span class="encs">[献之]</span></span><span class="diczx2">书之</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:版牍(古代书写用的薄木片)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 名册;户籍 <span class="encs">[register]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">听闾里以版图。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;小宰》</span>。注:户籍也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌群臣之版。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼司士》</span>。注:&ldquo;名籍也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗟哉生计一如此，廖入王民版籍论。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">梅尧臣</span><span class="smcs">《小村》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:版曹(户部的别称。以掌管版籍得名)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 国家的图籍 <span class="encs">[domain chart]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">式负版者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:&ldquo;谓邦国籍也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡五部，咸入版籍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《辽史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 朝笏，即手板。古代官吏上朝用的笏 <span class="encs">[tablet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">投版弃官而去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;范滂传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:版谒(持名帖进见)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 上面有文字或图形，用木板、石板或金属等制成供印刷用的东西 <span class="encs">[printing plate]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:制版;版子(用以印刷书刊的底板)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 印刷物印行的次数 <span class="encs">[edition]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:初版;重版书</span></p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>版</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄅㄢˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>bǎn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0120.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">木片或片狀的器物。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;片部》：「版，片也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;段玉裁&middot;注：「凡施於宮室器用者，皆曰版。今字作板。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">古時築牆所用的木夾板。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「版築」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《史記&middot;卷八二&middot;田單傳》：「田單知士卒之可用，乃身操版插，與士卒分功。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">簡牘的通稱。</span><span class="gc_yy">《管子&middot;宙合》：「故退身不舍端，修業不息版。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;尹知章&middot;注：「版，牘也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">戶籍、圖籍。</span><span class="gc_yy">《周禮&middot;天官&middot;司會》：「凡在書契版圖者之貳。」</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;鄭玄&middot;注：「版，戶籍也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;鄉黨》：「式負版者。」</span><span class="gc_yy">三國魏&middot;何晏&middot;集解：「負版者，持邦國之圖籍。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">印刷所用的版式，上面拼組或刻鑄著既定的文字或圖形。如木版、鉛版、鋅版、銅版等印刷。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">版本。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「宋版書」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">量詞：➊ ​ 計算報紙頁面的單位。如：「今天的報紙共有十六版。」➋ ​ 計算同一作品不同時期出版的單位。如：「這本書已出至十二版。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">書籍版本的簡稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「原版影印」、「宋版老子」、「鼎文版史記」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">報紙或雜誌的版別。如:「國際版」、「體育版」、「經濟版」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">電腦網路的討論區。經常劃分不同的主題，讓使用者表達意見，進行討論。每一個主題討論區即稱為「版」。也稱作「板」。</span></p></li>
