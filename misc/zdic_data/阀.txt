                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阀</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>阀</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">閥</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fá  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fá.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄚˊ</span></p><ol><li>古代指有权势的家庭：门～。～阅（封建时代指有功勋、有权势的世家。亦作“伐阅”。</li><li>凭借权势造成特殊地位的个人或集团（含贬义）：军～。财～。党～。</li><li>机器中调节流体流量、压力和流动方向的装置，如：截止阀、单向阀、减压阀、安全阀等。亦称“阀门”、“活门”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>阀</strong></p><p>閥 <span class="dicpy">fá</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fá.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从门，伐声。本义:阀阅。即仕官人家自序功状而树立在门外左边的柱子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[merit post]</span>釩 在左曰阀，在右曰阅。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 功劳 <span class="encs">[merit]</span>。也作<span class="yhcs">“伐”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">献诚</span>子 <span class="diczx2">煦</span>，积阀亦至 <span class="diczx2">夏州</span>节度使。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·张献诚传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 名门巨室，仕宦人家 <span class="encs">[first family]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">东都</span><span class="diczx2">仁和里</span><span class="diczx2">裴尚书</span><span class="diczx2">宽</span>子孙众盛，实为名阀。—— <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新唐书·柳玭传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:阀阅(有权势的仕宦人家)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 依靠权势在某一方面有特殊支配地位的人物或集团 <span class="encs">[magnate;powerfull person or family]</span>。如:财阀;党阀;学阀;军阀</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 阀门 <span class="encs">[valve]</span>。如:安全阀;止回阀</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/阀门">阀门</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上" target="_blank">戌集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=戌集上&amp;bs=門" target="_blank">門</a>】
                              閥·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/95A5.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】房越切【正韻】房滑切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音伐。【說文】閥閱，自序也。【韻會】閥閱功狀。【史記·功臣年表】人臣功有五品，明其等曰閥，積日曰閱。</p><p>又【正韻】門在左曰閥，在右曰閱。</p><p>又【正字通】元朝品制有爵者，其門爲烏頭閥閱。【冊府元龜】閥閱二柱，相去一丈，柱端置瓦筒，號爲烏頭。</p><p>又【說文】通用伐。【左傳·成十六年】卻至驟稱其伐。【杜預註】伐，功也。</p><p>又【前漢·車千秋傳】無伐閱功勞。【註】師古曰：伐，積功也。</p><p>
