						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>跳</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tiào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiào.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄧㄠˋ</span></p><ol><li>两脚离地全身向上或向前的动作：～跃。～水。～荡。～跶。～高。～远。～舞。弹（<span class="dicpy">tán </span>）～。暴～如雷。～梁小丑（指上蹿下跳，兴风作浪的微不足道的坏人）。</li><li>越过：～班。～棋。～槽。</li><li>一起一伏地动：心～。眼～。心惊肉～。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>跳</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">táo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/táo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄠˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">逃</span>”。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">跳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>跳</strong> <span class="dicpy">tiào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tiào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从足，兆声。本义:跃)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[jump;leap;spring]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跳，…一曰跃也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">特跳此者。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">跳往助之。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狂生跳掷叫号</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> —— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:跳蹋(由生气或着急而顿足);跳索(杂技中的一种绳技);跳鳞(跳出水面的鱼);跳踯(上下跳跃)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 越过障碍 <span class="encs">[skip over]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 如:跳墙;跳过篱笆;跳读(跳越章节读书);跳墙蓦圈(指偷盗的行为);跳门限(另换门庭);跳河(越过河;投河)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 内动;一起一伏地跳 <span class="encs">[move up and down;beat;twitch]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 如:心跳;眼皮跳;跳跃(跳动);跳踉(跳跃;跳动);跳峦(喻指起伏的山峦);跳萧(扑腾，跳动)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 上冒;冒出 <span
