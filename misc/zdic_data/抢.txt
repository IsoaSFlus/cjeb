data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qiāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 撞;碰;触 <span class="encs">[knock;hit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飞抢榆枋支遁。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“突也”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抢，刺也。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·扬雄传》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以头抢地。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·魏策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抢呼欲绝。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:抢地(碰触地面。指叩头求饶);抢地呼天(以头撞地，悲呼苍天。形容极其伤痛);抢跪(屈一膝的半跪礼)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 推，搡 <span class="encs">[push]</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 逆;顶;挡 <span class="encs">[go against;disobey;keep off]</span>。如:抢风</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 创伤，伤 <span class="encs">[wound]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">足伤金距缩，头抢花冠翻。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span
