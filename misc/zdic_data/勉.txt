						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>勉</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">miǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄇㄧㄢˇ</span></p><ol><li>力量不够而尽力做：～力。～强（<span class="dicpy">qiǎng </span>）。～为其难。</li><li>劝人努力，鼓励：～励。～勖。～慰。自～。互～。</li><li>努力：勤～。奋～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>勉</strong> <span class="dicpy">miǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/miǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从力，免声。本义:力所不及而强作)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[force sb. to do sth.]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勉，彊也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丧事不敢不勉。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子罕》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曰勉升降以上下兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勉力不时。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·天论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:勉意(勉强;勉为其难)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 尽力，用尽所有力量 <span class="encs">[make great efforts;do all one can]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尔其勉之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓努力。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">愿子勉为寡人治之。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说左下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公子勉之矣，老臣不能从。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知力耕可以得谷，勉贸可以得货。——<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:勉勉(努力的样子);勉旃(努力);勉农(努力从事农务);勉厉(努力)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 勉励，鼓励 <span class="encs">[encourage;exhort]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国安不殆，勉农而不偷。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·垦令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父勉其子，兄勉其弟，妇勉其夫。——<span class="smcs">《国语·越语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不加勉，而民自尽竭。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:勉学;勉勖(勉励);勉劳(勉励慰劳);勉厉(劝勉鼓励);勉慰(勉励安慰);勉喻，勉谕(晓谕，劝说)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“免”</span>。除去，脱掉 <span class="encs">[get rid of]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乃厚其外交而勉之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语八》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">七十而无德，虽有微过，亦可以勉矣。——<span
