                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">胖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胖</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(会意兼形声。从月(肉)，从半，半亦声。半，分牛。本义:古代祭祀时用的半边牲肉) 同本义 <span class="encs">[half cattle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胖，半体肉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">膴胖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;腊人》</span>。注:&ldquo;胖之言片也，析肉意也。大夫注:膴胖，皆谓夹脊肉。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">司马升羊右胖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;少牢礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鹄鸮胖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓胁侧薄肉也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>胖</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
