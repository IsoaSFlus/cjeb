						<p>● <strong>怀</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">懷</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨㄞˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>思念，想念：～念。～旧。～乡。～古。缅～。</li>
	<li>包藏：～胎。心～鬼胎。胸～壮志。～瑾握瑜。～才不遇。</li>
	<li>胸前：～抱。抱在～里。</li>
	<li>心意：心～。胸～。正中（<span class="dicpy">zh&ograve;ng </span>）下～。耿耿于～。</li>
	<li>安抚：～柔。</li>
	<li>归向，使降顺：&ldquo;～敌附远，何招而不至？&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">怀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>怀</strong></p>
<p>懷 <span class="dicpy">hu&aacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hu&aacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，褱声。本义:想念，怀念)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[think of;miss;yearn for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀，思念也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嗟我怀人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;卷耳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维以不永怀。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有女怀人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;野有死麕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惟佳人之独怀兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;悲回风》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀良辰以孤往。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">久行怀思。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而不怀仁。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏征</span><span class="smcs">《谏太宗十思疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">去国怀乡。(国，国都)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怀土(怀恋故乡;安于所处);怀香(怀念女色;风流);怀慕(怀念仰慕);怀乡(思念故乡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 心里存有;怀藏 <span class="encs">[keep in mind;cherish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心怆悢以伤怀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;班彪&middot;北征赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀瑾握瑜兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;怀沙》</span>。注:&ldquo;在衣为怀，在手为握。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀怒未发。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀信佗傺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚词&middot;屈原&middot;涉江》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀犹豫之计。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尚怀狐疑。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀其璧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怀瑾握瑜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">佩紫怀黄。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">南朝</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">丘迟</span><span class="smcs">《与陈伯之书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汝姊在吾怀。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怀玉(怀抱美玉。比喻人具有才德);怀慝(心中怀有不良的意念);怀贰(怀有二心);怀袖(拥抱);怀刺(心怀讽刺)</span></p>
