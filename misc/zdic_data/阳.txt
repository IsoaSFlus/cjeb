                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>阳</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">陽</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄤˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>明亮。</li>
	<li>中国古代哲学认为宇宙中通贯所有物质的两大对立面之一，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">阴</span>&rdquo;相对：一阴一～谓之道。阴～二气。图形：⚊(U+268A)。</li>
	<li>指&ldquo;太阳&rdquo;：～光。～面。～历。向～。夕～。</li>
	<li>山的南面或水的北面（多用于地名）：衡～（在中国湖南省衡山之南）。洛～（在中国河南省洛河之北）。</li>
	<li>温暖：～春。</li>
	<li>外露的，明显的：～沟。～奉阴违。</li>
	<li>凸出的：～文图章。</li>
	<li>关于活人的：～间（人世间）。～宅。～寿。</li>
	<li>带正电的：～极。～电。～离子。</li>
	<li>男性生殖器：～痿。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">佯</span>&rdquo;，假装。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> 'male' principle; light; sun</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Sonne  (S)​,irdisch, von dieser Welt  (Adj)​,positiv  (Adj)​,Yang (das männliche oder positive Prinzip in der Natur nach der altchinesischen Philosophie, Medizin usw.)​  (Eig, Med)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Yang (principe mâle opposé au Yin)​,Soleil,éclairé,adret (versant d'une montagne exposé au soleil)​,masculin,positif (électricité)​,(nom de famille)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">阳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>阳</strong></p>
<p>陽 <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜，昜( <span class="dicpy">y&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> )声。从阜，与山有关。本义:山南水北)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[south of a hill or north of a river]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阳，高明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山南为阳，水北为阳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;僖公二十八年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山东曰朝阳，山西曰夕阳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">利刊阳木而火之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;柞氏》</span>。注:&ldquo;生于山南为阳木。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">河阳</span>之北。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">华山</span>之阳。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阳谷皆入</span><span class="diczx2">汶</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">泰山</span>之阳， <span class="diczx2">汶水</span>西流。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:衡阳(在衡山之南)，洛阳(在洛河之北);阳陆(山之南);阳濒(水之北岸);阳林(生在山南的林木);阳木(山南之木);阳柯(向阳光或南向的树枝);阳崖(向南的山崖);阳坡(向阳的山坡)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 太阳<span class="encs">[sun]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">湛湛露斯，匪阳不晞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;湛露》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阳春布德泽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;长歌行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斜阳草树。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐&middot;京口北固亭怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少年如朝阳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳精(太阳);斜阳;阳宗(指太阳);阳明(太阳、阳光。也指光明。人体经络名);阳彩(日光，太阳的色彩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 物体的正面，前面 <span class="encs">[in front;at the head]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自昔以钱之有字处为阴，无字处为阳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾炎武</span><span class="smcs">《日知录&middot;钱面》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 男性生殖器 <span class="encs">[male genitals]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其国男子年五十余，阳多痿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《海国闻见录&middot;东洋记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳物(男性生殖器)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 农历十月的别称 <span class="encs">[the tenth month of the lunar year]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:阳月(农历十月的别名，也称小阳春);阳朔(农历十月初一)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 精液 <span class="encs">[semen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">女子七七四十九阴绝，男子八八六十四阳绝，过此为婚为野合。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉堂嘉话》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 外面 <span class="encs">[outside;out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必谋功不察志，论阳效不存阴计。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡定贤》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳浮(表面顺从);阳效(表面的功效);阳冻(地面上的冰冻);阳事(外治，宫廷以外的政事);阳冰(结在水面的冰)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 晴天 <span class="encs">[fine day;sunny day]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仰福帝居，阳曜阴藏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;张衡&middot;西京赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 天 <span class="encs">[sky;heavens]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阳灵停曜于其表，阴祗雾于其里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;左思&middot;魏都赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳灵(天神);阳祀(祭天及宗庙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 人世 <span class="encs">[the world]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">唐王</span>游地府，回生阳世，开设水陆大会，超度阴魂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳类(人世间的物类);阳寿(人在世间的寿命);阳报(在人世间得到的报应);阳功(人间功德)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 额 <span class="encs">[front]</span>。又指前面正面</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡卜，辨龟之上下，左右，阴阳，以授命龟者，而诏相之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 眸子。借指美目 <span class="encs">[pupil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阳，<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>:</span><span class="diczx2">燕</span>、 <span class="diczx2">代</span>、 <span class="diczx2">朝鲜</span>、 <span class="diczx2">洌水</span>之间或好目为阳。&mdash;&mdash;卷子本<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 中国古代哲学家认为阳是贯彻于一切事物的两个对立面之一，跟&ldquo;阴&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[(in Chinese philosophy，medicine，etc.) Yang，the masculine or positive principle in nature]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:阴阳二气;阳九</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 古代阴阳家、方士以四千六百一十七岁为一元，初入一元为一百零六岁，有旱灾九年，称为阳九。其余尚有阴九、阴七、阳七、阴五、阳五、阴三、阳三等。阳为旱灾，阴为水灾。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 太乙数以四百五十六年为一阳九，二百八十八年为一百六。阳九为奇数，为阳数之穷;百六，偶数，为阴数之穷。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 道家称三千三百年为小阳九，小百六;九千九百年为大阳九，大百六。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">(天厄称为阳九，地亏称为百六);阳干(指十干中的甲、丙、戊、庚、壬。即列于单数位天干。与阴相对);阳木(春夏所生的树木。一说为山南之木);阳辰(相术家以十二地支中的单数位，子、寅、辰、午、申、戊六日为阳辰，余六日为阴辰);阳鱼(鱼。因其生于阴生属于阳，故名);阳童(未成年而死的庶子。一说男孩夭亡为阳童，女孩夭亡为阴童);阳施(阳气散布);阳道(外事，政事);阳会(古俗妇女于每月十九日夜举行的一种聚会);阳德(阳气。也指日);阳错(阴阳家的迷信之说);阳声(古乐声分阴阳，六律为阳声。也指清声);阳灵(祭天之所。也指太阳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(18)</span> 中国中医学上指人体内部某些器官 <span class="encs">[internal organ]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太阳藏独至，厥喘虚气逆，是阴不足，阳有余也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳病(中医指阳虚有寒的病症);阳疾(中医指热邪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(19)</span> 春秋国名 <span class="encs">[Yang state]</span>。在今山东省沂水县西南</p>
<p><span class="cino">(20)</span> 春秋燕邑名 <span class="encs">[Yang city]</span>。在今河南省唐县东北</p>
<p><span class="cino">(21)</span> 指春夏 <span class="encs">[spring and summer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫人在阳则舒，在阴则惨，此牵乎天者也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳和(春天的暖气);阳夏(夏季);阳时(春夏之时);阳官(指<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>中的春官)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(22)</span> 中午 <span class="encs">[noon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏后氏祭其暗，殷人祭其阳，同人祭日以朝及暗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(23)</span> 通&ldquo;旸&rdquo;。日出 <span class="encs">[coming out of the sun]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殷人祭其阳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭义》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:&ldquo;阳，读为曰雨曰旸之旸。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">艾，时阳若。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;五行志中之上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(24)</span> 姓。如:阳成(复姓)</p>

<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 凸出 <span class="encs">[in relief]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:阳沟里失风(喻在安全场合出事);阳文(镂刻在器物上凸起的文字。也称阳识);阳刻(浮雕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 带正电的 <span class="encs">[positive]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:阳电;阳极</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">神光离合，乍阴乍阳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳回(阳气回转);阳晁(晴朗的早晨);阳嘉(春日的暖气);阳春有脚(比喻为人带来春天般的温暖)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 干旱 <span class="encs">[dry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太白司艾，西岳国师典致时阳，白炜象平，考量以铨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 诈伪 <span class="encs">[fake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆阳应曰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3"> 如:阳言(诈言。同佯言)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 奇数 <span class="encs">[odd]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阳数奇，阴数偶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《白虎通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:阳爵(奇数的爵位等级);阳数(奇数)</span></p>

<p>〈动〉</p>

<p><span class="diczx1">故胜己者，则不得闻，闻亦阳不知也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">葛洪</span><span class="smcs">《抱朴子》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 复苏;生长 <span class="encs">[resuscitate;grow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日月还复周，我去不再阳。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《杂诗》</span></p>
