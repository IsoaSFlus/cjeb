						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>旁</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄤˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>左右两侧：～边。～侧。～门。～出。～听。～若无人。</li>
	<li>其他，另外：～人。～的话。触类～通。责无～贷。～证。～白（戏剧角色背着台上其他剧中人对观众说的话）。～落。</li>
	<li>广，广泛：～征博引。</li>
	<li>邪、偏：～门左道。</li>
	<li>汉字形体中常常出现的某些组成部分：偏～。竖心～儿。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">旁</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旁</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (古通&ldquo;傍&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 依傍;依附 <span class="encs">[depend on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">匈奴</span>大发十余万骑，南旁塞至符奚庐山。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;赵充国传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:旁沿(依附沿袭);旁缘(依仗)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 靠近 <span class="encs">[be near to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾灵尚依依旁汝也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">林觉民</span><span class="smcs">《与妻书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">引</span><span class="diczx2">渭</span>穿渠起 <span class="diczx2">长安</span>，旁南山下，至 <span class="diczx2">河</span>三百余里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四时之内，飘风怒吹，或西发西洋，或东起北海，旁午交扇，无时而息。&mdash;&mdash;〔英〕</span><span class="diczx2">赫胥黎</span>著、 <span class="diczx2">严复</span>译<span class="smcs">《天演论》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:旁午(将近中午);旁车(靠近车子)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 依照;仿效 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span>又旁<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span>作重一篇，名曰<span class="smcs">《广骚》</span>;又旁<span class="smcs">《惜诵》</span>以下至<span class="smcs">《怀沙》</span>一卷，名曰<span class="smcs">《畔牢愁》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;谤&rdquo;。诽谤 <span class="encs">[defame;slander]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不窥密，不旁狎，不道旧故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;少仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">p&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>旁</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从二阙，方声。本义:大;广)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[great;broad;extensive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁，溥也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁，广也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁求俊彦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;太甲上》</span>。传:&ldquo;旁，非一方。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">群后旁戾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁震八鄙。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陶冶大炉，旁薄群生。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将旁礴万物以为一世蕲乎乱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">杂能旁魄而无用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;性恶》</span>。注:&ldquo;旁魄，广博也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:旁畅(广通博晓);旁行(广泛流传);旁收(广泛收罗);旁求(四处征求;广泛搜求);旁施(广施);旁开(广开);旁薄(旁礴、旁魄。广博;无际;广被充塞);旁罗(遍布);旁瞩(遍视);旁靡(普及);旁罗(遍及);旁雪(普遍洗雪);旁通(遍通)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 横 <span class="encs">[transverse]</span>。如:旁午(纵横交错);旁行(横写);旁行书(横写的文字);旁穿(横穿);旁师(横向学习);旁贯(横贯)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 别的，其他的 <span class="encs">[else;other]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">此吾所以旁推交通，而以为之文也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《答韦中立论师 道书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:旁入(其他收入);旁言(他人的话);旁省(别的宫廷);旁推(由此及彼地推论);旁讼(诉讼涉及他人);旁寄(别有寄托);旁贷(谓推卸他人)</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>旁</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 旁边。古同&ldquo;傍&rdquo; <span class="encs">[side]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁，犹边也，侧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其旁可以圉风寒，上可以圉雪霜露。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;节用中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旁天子而立于堂上。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《逸周书&middot;王会》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士旁三揖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;丧大记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂旁设一虚坐，置琵琶于其上。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王傥</span><span class="smcs">《唐语林&middot;雅量》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斫其正，养其旁条。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:两旁(左右两边);旁首(旁边);旁训(正文旁边附注的释义或音读);旁生(旁边生长);旁立(在旁边侍立);旁仄(近侧;左右)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 附近 <span class="encs">[nearby]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天之中央亦高四旁六万里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周髀算经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:四旁(前后左右很近的地方);近旁(附近;旁边);旁民(附近之民);旁谷(附近的山谷);旁近;旁郡(附近的州郡);旁座(指邻座的人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 四面 <span class="encs">[round]</span>。如:旁戾(从四方而至);旁射(犹四射);旁溢(向四面八方流去);旁午(四面八方;到处)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 汉字的偏旁 <span class="encs">[lateral radical of a Chinese character]</span>。如:形旁，声旁</p>
<p>◎ <strong>旁</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 辅助，辅佐 <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日有志极而无旁，终老独以离异兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;九章》</span>。注:&ldquo;旁，辅也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_2491.svg" /><span class="swbf">(旁)</span><span class="ywyy">溥也。</span><span class="swdz">司馬相如封禪文曰：㫄魄四塞。張揖曰：㫄，衍也。廣雅曰：㫄，大也。按㫄讀如<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23DA2.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣶢</span>，與溥雙聲。後人訓側，其義偏矣。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2011E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠄞</span>。闕。</span><span class="swdz">闕謂从<img class="lazy" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_001.png" src="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_001.png">之說未聞也。李陽冰曰：<img class="lazy" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_001.png" src="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_001.png">象㫄達之形也。按自序云：其於所不知，葢闕如也。凡言闕者，或謂形，或謂音，或謂義，分別讀之。</span><span class="ywyy">方聲。</span><span class="swdz">凡徐氏鉉鍇<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2011E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠄞</span>本不同，各从其長者，如此處鍇作方聲闕。闕字在方聲下，於未聞从<img class="lazy" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_001.png" src="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_001.png">之說不瞭，故不从之是也。後不悉注。步光切。十部。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_2492.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230DF.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣃟</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文㫄。</span><span class="swdz">以許說推之，亦小篆从二，古文从一也。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_2493.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/230D9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣃙</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">亦古文㫄。</span><span class="swdz">李斯改一爲二，則爲小篆。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65C1_2494.svg" /><span class="swbf">(雱)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文。</span><span class="swdz">詩：雨雪其雱。故訓傳曰：雱，盛皃。卽此字也。籒文从雨，衆多如雨意也。毛云盛：與許云溥正合。今人不知㫄、雱同字，音讀各殊。古形、古音、古義、皆廢矣。</span></p>                          </div>
