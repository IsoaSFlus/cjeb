                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>窃</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">竊</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>偷盗：偷～。～取。</li>
	<li>用不合法不合理的手段取得：～位。～夺。</li>
	<li>私自，暗中：～笑。～听。</li>
	<li>谦辞，指自己：～谓。～以为可行。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> secretly, stealthily; steal; thief</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> etw stehlen  (V)​,verstohlen, heimlich  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> secrètement,voler</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">窃</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>窃</strong></p>
<p>竊 <span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。 从米，以米为穴，意为虫在穴中偷米吃，是会意字。本义:偷)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[steal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窃，盗自穴中出曰窃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敝舆而欲窃之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡窃木者有刑罚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;山虞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">小臣窃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相窃妻妾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;桑中序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃攘窃神祗之牺牲牲用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;微子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">奸臣窃命。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆机</span><span class="smcs">《辩亡论上》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窃窥(偷看);失窃(财物被人偷走);偷窃(盗窃。用不合法的手段秘密取得);窃宝;窃铁(偷取斧钺);窃药(偷药);窃食(偷吃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 篡夺。指非其有而取之;不当受而受之 <span class="encs">[usurp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虽赏之不窃，其窃位者与。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;卫灵公》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窃比于我老彭。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窃国者为诸侯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;胠箧》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窃誉(窃取声名);窃政(窃取政权);窃食(窃取俸禄);窃柄(窃夺权柄);剽窃(抄袭窃取)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 侵害;危害 <span class="encs">[encroach on;endanger]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:窃肆(侵犯骚扰，横行无忌);窃犯(侵犯);窃攘(侵犯)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>窃</strong></p>
<p>竊 <span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p> 用作表示自己的谦词 <span class="encs">[I]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窃爱怜之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《触詟说赵太后》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:窃以为</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>窃</strong></p>
<p>竊 <span class="dicpy">qi&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 私下;私自。多用作谦词 <span class="encs">[in private;secretly]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">竊</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“窃”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/竊">竊</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下" target="_blank">午集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下&amp;bs=穴" target="_blank">穴</a>】
                              竊·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=22" target="_blank">22</a>　·部外筆画：17</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7ACA.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22FDD.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢿝</span>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>千結切，音切。盜也。【集韻】盜自中出曰竊。【書&middot;微子】殷民攘竊神祇之犧牷牲。【傳】往盜曰竊。【春秋&middot;定八年】盜竊寶玉大弓。【史記&middot;叔孫通傳】䑕竊狗盜。【博雅】竊，取也。</p>
<p>又私也。【論語】竊比於我老彭。【孟子】竊負而逃。【前漢&middot;公孫弘傳】尚竊遲之。</p>
<p>又非所據而據亦曰竊。【論語】臧文仲其竊位者與。【疏】知賢不舉，偸安於位，故曰竊位。</p>
<p>又竊竊，猶察察。【莊子&middot;齊物論】竊竊然知之。【註】猶察察也。</p>
<p>又鳥名。【爾雅&middot;釋鳥】桑鳸竊脂。【疏】桑鳸，一名竊脂，好盜脂膏，因名。</p>
<p>又淺也。【轉注古音略】竊，卽古淺字。九扈中，竊<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>，淺黑也。竊藍，淺靑也。竊黃，淺黃也。竊丹，淺赤也。四色皆具，則竊脂亦淺白也。【爾雅&middot;釋獸】虎竊毛謂之虦貓。【疏】虎之淺毛者，別名虦貓。</p>
<p>又叶七計切，音砌。【王筠詩】寶地恣憑陵，神樂忽侵竊。猛將窮奮擊，勇夫貪搏噬。</p>
