                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">产</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>产</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">産</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">chǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人或动物生子：～子。～卵。～妇。助～士。</li>
	<li>制造，养种植或自然生长：工业生～。～值。</li>
	<li>制造、养、种植或自然生长的东西：土～。特～。</li>
	<li>生出，出现：～生。出～。～地。</li>
	<li>财物：财～。遗～。～权。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> give birth, bring forth, produce</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> produzieren  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> donner naissance,accoucher,mettre au monde,engendrer,produire,produit,propriété,biens</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">产</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>产</strong></p>
<p>産 <span class="dicpy">chǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从生，彦省声。本义:出生，生育)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[give birth to]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">産，生也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百物之产。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宗伯》</span>。注:&ldquo;生其种曰产。以天产作阴德，以地产作阳德。&rdquo;注:&ldquo;天产者动物，地产者植物，谓九谷之属。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">同产。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;明帝纪》</span>。注:&ldquo;同母兄弟也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其妻重身当产。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">干宝</span><span class="smcs">《搜神记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:产子(生孩子);产育(养育;生育);产母(产妇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 出产 <span class="encs">[produce;yield]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">永州</span>之野产异蛇。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">江宁</span>之 <span class="diczx2">龙蟠</span>， <span class="diczx2">苏州</span>之 <span class="diczx2">邓尉</span>， <span class="diczx2">杭州</span>之 <span class="diczx2">西溪</span>，皆产梅。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:产马;产粮;产煤;产油;产机器</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 发生 <span class="encs">[happen]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:产怨(产生怨毒恨怒)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>产</strong></p>
<p>産 <span class="dicpy">chǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 产业;财产 <span class="encs">[property]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">里胥猾黠，假此科敛丁口，每责一头，辄倾数家之产。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:私产;房地产;房产;家产</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 出产之物、物产 <span class="encs">[product]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:水产;畜产;渔产;土特产</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 产生或制造的行动或过程 <span class="encs">[production]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:增产，欠产</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 生产农产品或其他产品的产量 <span class="encs">[yield]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:水稻亩产超千斤</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7522.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>所<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥳑</span>切，音剗。【說文】生也。【正字通】婦生子曰產，物生亦曰產。【禮&middot;鄕飮酒禮】產萬物者，聖也。【註】聖之言生也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大宗伯】以天產作隂德，以中禮防之。以地產作陽德，以和樂防之。【註】天產者動物，謂六牲之屬。地產者植物，謂九穀之屬。【前漢&middot;昭帝紀】金芝九莖，產于函德殿銅池中。</p>
<p>又本其所生長之地曰產。【孟子】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>良，楚產也。【左傳&middot;僖二年】屈產之乗。【註】屈地生良馬。趙岐以屈產爲地名，誤。</p>
<p>又民業曰產。【孟子】有恆產者有恆心。【前漢&middot;高祖紀】常有大度，不事家人生產作業。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】大籥似笛，三孔而短。【爾雅&middot;釋樂】大籥謂之產。</p>
<p>又水名。與滻同。【前漢&middot;郊祀志】霸產豐勞。</p>
<p>又與嵼同。【楚辭&middot;九章】思蹇產而不釋。</p>
<p>又姓。【何氏姓苑】彭城人也。【正字通】明產麟，產瓘。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】所晏切，音訕。生育也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24564.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤕤</span>舉切，數上聲。【漢樂章】安其所，樂終產，樂終產，世繼緒。</p>
<p>又所蹇切，先上聲。【張協登北芒山賦】靈嶽鬱以造天，連岡巖以蹇產。伊洛渾而東流，帝君赫以崇顯。　或作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21FB0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡾰</span>嵼。</p>
