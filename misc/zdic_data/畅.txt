                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>畅</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">暢</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chàng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chàng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄤˋ</span></p><ol><li>没有阻碍地：～通。～达。～销。流～。通～。</li><li>痛快，尽情地：～快。～谈。～游（ａ．畅快地游览：ｂ．畅快地游泳）。欢～。舒～。～所欲言。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>畅</strong></p><p>暢 <span class="dicpy">chàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从申，昜( <span class="dicpy">yáng</span>)声。本义:畅通，无阻碍)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[smooth;unimpeded]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">登台四望，三面皆畅。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说林上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">美在其中，而畅于四支。——<span class="smcs">《易·坤》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:通畅(运行无阻);顺畅(顺利通畅，没有阻碍)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 舒畅 <span class="encs">[comfortable]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旧国旧都，望之畅然。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·则阳》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">感条畅之气。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">美声畅于</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>氏。——<span class="smcs">《文选·张衡·西京赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:宽畅(心里舒畅);酣畅(畅快)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 茂盛 <span class="encs">[flourishing]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">草木畅茂。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 流畅，言辞敏捷、自在而流利 <span class="encs">[fluent]</span>。如:畅利(流利);明畅(明白流畅)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>畅</strong></p><p>暢 <span class="dicpy">chàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 正;极;甚 <span class="encs">[very]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青衫忒离俗，栽得畅可体。——<span class="smcs">《董西厢》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:畅好(畅好是，畅好道。真是，真正;正好，甚好)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 尽情;痛快 <span
