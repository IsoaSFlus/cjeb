                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>笈</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，及声。本义:竹书箱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。多用竹、藤编织，用以放置书籍、衣巾、药物等 <span class="encs">[bamboo box for storing books，cloth，etc.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">极，驴上负也。从木，及声，读若急，字亦作笈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">负笈从师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;苏秦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笈，谓学士所以负书箱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周处风土记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:笈囊(笈与囊)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 书籍;典籍 <span class="encs">[book]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山坛</span><span class="diczx2">毛公</span>蜕，古笈 <span class="diczx2">灵威</span>守。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王识禛</span>诗</p>
