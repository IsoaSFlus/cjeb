                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>批</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>用手掌打：～颊（打嘴巴）。</li>
	<li>刮：～凿。</li>
	<li>口头或用文字判定是非、优劣、可否：～准。～示。～复。～语。～阅。～判。眉～（写在书页天头上的批语）。</li>
	<li>大量：～购。～销。</li>
	<li>量词，用于大宗的货物或数量众多的事物：一～货物。</li>
	<li>棉麻等未捻成线、绳时的细缕：线～儿。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">批</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>批</strong> <span class="dicpy">pī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，比声。本义:反手打)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[slap]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:批他几个嘴巴;批颊(打耳光)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 引申为打、攻击 <span class="encs">[beat;strike;attack]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:批挞(敲打);批拉(搏击;扑打);批头竹片(衙役手中的打人竹片。一头扎紧，一头劈分细条);批难(抗击仇敌);批鳞(触犯君王和显贵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为刺入 <span class="encs">[stab;prick into]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">依乎天理，批大郤，导大窾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 批示。用钢笔或铅笔在纸上或其他材料上写指示 <span class="encs">[write instruction]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:批文件;批作业;审批(审查批示);批帖(批文);批回(中央对地方官署奏请事宜的批复文书);批详(已批的公文);批子(支取银钱的批条);批反(批示答复)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 分析;评论 <span class="encs">[analyse;comment]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后人有<span class="smcs">《西江月》</span>二词，批宝玉极恰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:揭批(揭发批判);批削(即批改);批拓(讥讽;轻视);批点(批评指画);批搨(即批评)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 排除;排挤 <span class="encs">[get rid of;remove;push aside]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:批黜(开除贬斥);批抵(排挤打击);批根(排斥，摈弃);批倾(犹排斥);批患(排除祸患)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 披露 <span class="encs">[reveal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">妙玉</span>扶乩批出来，众人不解。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>批</strong> <span class="dicpy">pī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用为众多人或大批货物。如:一批游客;大批货物</p>
<p>◎ <strong>批</strong> <span class="dicpy">pī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 评语 <span class="encs">[remark on a piece of writing]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:眉批(在书眉或文稿上方写的批注);朱批(用朱笔写的评语);批本(加有批语的著作本子);批尾(在别人著作后面加评论批注);批抹(犹言批注校改);批笔(对作品加的评语)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="encs">[口]</span>棉麻等未捻成线、绳时的细缕 <span class="encs">[fibres of cotton，flax etc. ready to be drawn and twisted]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:线批儿;麻批儿</span></p>
