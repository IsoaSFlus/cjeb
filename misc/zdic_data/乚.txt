                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">乚</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十二">卷十二</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十二&amp;swbs=乚">乚</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4E5A.svg">                          <p>玄鳥也。齊魯謂之乚。取其鳴自呼。象形。凡乚之屬皆从乚。鳦，乙或从鳥。烏轄切〖注〗徐鍇曰：&ldquo;此與甲乙之乙相類，其形舉，首下曲，與甲乙字少異。&rdquo;〖注〗字或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/200C9_9162.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">燕燕，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>鳥也。</span><span class="swdz">燕燕見前篇。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>鳥二字淺人所增。</span><span class="ywyy">齊魯謂之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>。取其鳴自謼。象形也。</span><span class="swdz">舊作呼，今依韵會正。也字今依韵會補。謼者，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2799D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧦝</span>也，號也。山海經說鳥獸多云其名自號。燕之鳴如云<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>。燕<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>雙聲。莊子謂之鷾鴯，鷾亦雙聲也。旣得其聲而像其形，則爲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>。燕篆像其籋口、布翄、枝尾、全體之形。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>篆像其于飛之形。故二篆皆曰像形也。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>象翅開首竦，横看之乃得。本與甲乙字異。俗人恐與甲乙亂，加鳥旁爲鳦，則贅矣。本音烏拔反。十五部。入於筆切者，非是。</span><span class="ywyy">凡<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>之屬皆从<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/200C9_9163.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鳦)</span><span class="ywyy"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200C9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠃉</span>或从鳥。</span><span class="swdz">按此葢非古字。今爾雅、毛傳皆如此作。</span></p>                          </div>
