						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蓄</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩˋ</span></p><ol><li>积聚，储藏，保存：～藏。～洪。～发（<span class="dicpy">fà </span>）。～养。积～。储～。兼收并～。</li><li>存于心中：～志。～怨。～谋。～意。含～。</li><li>等待：“孰谓时之可～？”</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蓄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蓄</strong> <span class="dicpy">xù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，畜声。本义:积聚，储藏)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[store up;accumulate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蓄，积也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蓄，聚也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我有旨蓄，亦以御冬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·谷风》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">洪恩素蓄。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蓄积者，天下之太命也。——<span class="smcs">《贾子·无蓄》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无三年之蓄。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蓄积索，田畴荒。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·初见秦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">积货滋多，蓄怨滋厚。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将军蓄米，将疗饥乎;将破敌乎?——<span class="smcs">《新五代史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蓄火(蓄留火种);蓄菜(把蔬菜制成菜干、咸菜等加以储藏。也指菜干，咸菜等);蓄余(储蓄节余的财物);蓄毓(储积培育);蓄租(积聚);蓄艾(本指蓄藏多年之艾以治久病，后比喻应长期积蓄以备急用)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 畜养 <span class="encs">[raise (domestic animals)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使得植桑麻、蓄鸡豚，以为岁时伏腊婚嫁之资。——<span class="smcs">《美芹十论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">视成所蓄。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蓄劣物。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:蓄内(蓄养内人);蓄私(蓄养妾媵私人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 蕴藏;怀有 <span class="encs">[hold in store;cherish;keep in mind]</span>。如:蓄疑自误(蓄藏疑虑，自我耽误);蓄怨(累积的怨恨;聚积怨恨);蓄思(积存已久的思念)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 留着不剃掉 <span class="encs">[grow]</span>。如:蓄发(留发使长。多指和尚尼姑还俗而言)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 等待 <span class="encs">[await]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盍远迹以飞声兮，孰谓时之可蓄?——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>蓄</strong> <span class="dicpy">xù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 积蓄 <span class="encs">[storage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国无九年之蓄曰不足，无六年之蓄曰急。——<span
