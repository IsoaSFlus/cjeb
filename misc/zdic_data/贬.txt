                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贬</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>贬</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">貶</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">biǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄧㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>给予低的评价，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">褒</span>&rdquo;相对：～低。～义。褒～（①评论好坏；②指出缺点）。</li>
	<li>减低，降低：～值。～职。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> decrease, lower; censure, criticize</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dégrader,dévaluer,déprécier,diminuer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>贬</strong></p>
<p>貶 <span class="dicpy">biǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/biǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，乏声。本义:减少;减损)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[abase;detract;lower;reduce]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贬，损也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贬，减也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何以不氏，贬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;隐公二年》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹损也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贬食省用。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贬颜(容颜瘦损);贬乐(减少声色之娱);贬价(降低价格);贬悴(瘦损憔悴)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 降级 <span class="encs">[demote;reduce to a lower rank]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请自贬三等，以督厥咎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指降职并外放;贬谪 <span class="encs">[demote;oust;dismiss from office]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遇用事者得罪，例出为刺史;未至，又例贬</span><span class="diczx2">永州</span>司马。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《柳子厚墓志铭》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其后</span><span class="diczx2">修</span>贬 <span class="diczx2">夷陵</span>，太夫人言笑自若。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《泷冈阡表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贬</span><span class="diczx2">连州</span>刺史。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧唐书&middot;刘禹锡传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:贬斥(官吏被贬黜);贬书(黜退的文书);贬谪(把有过错的官员降职并派往远离京城的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 给予低的评价。与&ldquo;褒&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[belittle;degrade;underrate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="smcs">《春秋》</span>以一字为褒贬，然皆数句以成言。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span><span class="smcs">《春秋左氏传序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:褒贬(评论好坏);贬身(谦冲有抑，不与人争名利);贬谤(贬低，毁谤);贬弹(贬低和指斥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 抑退;谦退 <span class="encs">[resign;depreciate]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:贬晦(谦退，韬晦);贬匿(谦退，韬晦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 掖;塞进 <span class="encs">[tuck into a pocket;thrust into a crack]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:把钱贬在腰里</span></p>
