                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>印</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yìn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yìn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄣˋ</span></p><ol><li>图章，戳记：～章。～玺。～记。～把子（亦喻政权）。</li><li>痕迹：手～。指～。～子（ａ.痕迹；ｂ．高利贷的一种，全称“～～钱”）。</li><li>用油墨、染料之类把文字或图画留在纸、布、器皿等材料上：～刷。排～。～制。～发（<span class="dicpy">fā</span>）。</li><li>彼此符合：～证。心心相～。</li><li>外界事物反映在脑中所留下的形象：～象。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">印</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>印</strong> <span class="dicpy">yìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形。左是手爪，右象跪着的人，合起来表示用手按人使之跪拜。本义:官印)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[official seal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">印，执政所持信也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玺者，印也。印者，信也。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">印，验也。——<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玺谓之印。——<span class="smcs">《小尔雅》</span>。按，古上下通曰玺。秦以来，天子诸侯王称玺，独以玉;列侯至二千石曰章，千石至四百石曰印。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每字为一印。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">密布字印。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每一字皆有数印。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其印自落。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:印务(有关印章管理和收发文书的工作;也指官印和职务);印券(盖有官印的凭证);印佩(官印);印牌(将印或令牌);印囊(古代装印信的口袋);印曹(掌刻印的官)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指图章 <span class="encs">[seal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡写诗文，名印当在上，字印当在下，道号又次之…试看</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>诸儒真迹中，用印皆然。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐官</span><span class="smcs">《古今印史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:打印(盖图章);印匣(印章盒);印篆(印章);印合(印盒，模子)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 痕迹，标记 <span class="encs">[print]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">原来</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>时，但是犯人，徒流迁徙的，都脸上刺字，怕人恨怪，只唤做<span class="yhcs">“打金印”</span>。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:手印;印痕;印识(犹印记)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 用尖头工具划的线 <span class="encs">[score]</span>。如:活塞在汽缸壁上划的一道印</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>印</strong> <span class="dicpy">yìn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yìn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 盖章 <span class="encs">[seal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">县啬夫若丞及仓、乡相杂以印之。——<span class="smcs">《睡虎地秦墓竹简》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:印子债(一种高利贷，即印子钱);印结(清制外省人在京考试或捐官，皆须在京的同乡京官为具保结，上盖六部印，这文书叫<span class="yhcs">“结”</span>。盖印的<span class="yhcs">“结”</span>叫<span class="yhcs">“印结”</span>);印马(验收马匹时诸监按马之优劣，在不同部位加不同印记);印子铺(房)(放印子钱的处所);印封(盖印的封泥);印署(盖印签押)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 印刷 <span class="encs">[print]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">板印书籍。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若止印二三本。</span></p><p><span
