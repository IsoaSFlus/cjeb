						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>句</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jù.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩˋ</span></p><ol><li>由词组成的能表示出一个完整意思的话：～子。～法。</li><li>〔～读（<span class="dicpy">dòu</span>）〕古代称文词停顿的地方为“句”或“<span class="diczx3">读</span>”。</li><li>量词，用于语言：三～话不离本行（<span class="dicpy">háng</span>）。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>句</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">gōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄡˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 〔高～骊〕古国名，即“高丽”。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">句</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>句</strong> <span class="dicpy">gōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口。正当读今言钩，俗作勾。本义:弯曲)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">句，曲也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“凡曲折之物，侈为倨，敛为句。考工记多言倨句。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">越子</span>为左右句卒。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十七年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">句兵欲无弹。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·庐人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">覆之而角至谓之句弓。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·弓人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">履句履者知地。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·田子方》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“方也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">句者毕出。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">古之人衣上有冒而句领者。——<span class="smcs">《尚书大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">句者毕出，萌者尽达。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> (草木出土时，弯的叫句，直的叫萌。)。又如:句爪(钩形的尖爪);句枉(曲枝);句曲(弯曲);句倨(弯曲);句圜(屈曲连环)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 勾销 <span class="encs">[strike]</span>。如:句抹(去掉文辞中的某些字句);句除(删除;删改)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 查考 <span class="encs">[examine]</span>。如:句校(查考校核);句考(查考);句稽(查考;核算)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 搜取;捕捉 <span class="encs">[catch]</span>。如:句押(拘捕扣押);句追(拘捕查究);句剥(搜刮)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 逗引;纠结 <span class="encs">[seduce]</span>。如:句引(引诱);句结(暗中串通结合)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 勾画 <span class="encs">[delineate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">说书虽小技，然必句性情，习方俗。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span
