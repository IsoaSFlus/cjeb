                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茜</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>茜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄑㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>茜草。多年生草本。方茎有刺毛，秋天开花，根黄红色，含茜素等，可作红色染料和入药。《説文&bull;艸部》：&ldquo;茜，茅蒐也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>绛色(深红色)。晋佚名《休洗紅》：&ldquo;休洗紅，洗多紅色淡。不惜故縫衣，記得初按茜。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>用同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/倩">倩</a>&rdquo;。秀美，生动。元周德清《中原音韻&bull;作詞十法》：&ldquo;前輩云：街市小令，唱尖新茜意。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>茜</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒ〡</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>人名用字。多用于外国妇女名字的译音。按：人名中也有读qi&agrave;n的。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>茜</strong></p>
<p>蒨 <span class="dicpy">qi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，西声。本义:草名) 即&ldquo;茜草&rdquo;( Rubia cordifolia ) <span class="encs">[Indian madder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茜，茅蒐也。字亦作蒨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若千亩巵茜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐广</span>曰:&ldquo;一名红蓝其花，染缯亦黄也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 按，一种茜草属多年生草本，茎方形，有逆刺，叶4枚轮生，长卵形或长心脏形，有叶柄，花小，淡黄白色，果实球形，熟果黑色，可食，根可制染料和入药</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>茜</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 大红色 <span class="encs">[deep red]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茜袖捧琼姿，皎日丹霞起。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《和郑愚赠汝阳王孙家筝妓》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茜纱(红纱);茜衫(红色的衣衫);茜红(绛红色);茜罗(绛红色的薄丝织品);茜裙(红色的裙子);茜绶(红色的印绶。譬喻官爵尊贵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 鲜明;鲜艳 <span class="encs">[bright]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茜练(鲜明的样子);茜绚(鲜明绚丽);茜璨(鲜明貌);茜茜(鲜明;鲜艳)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 草茂盛的样子 <span class="encs">[luxuriant]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茜茜(草茂盛的样子);茜蔚(草木茂盛的样子)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>茜</strong> <span class="dicpy">qi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 染红 <span class="encs">[dye red]</span></p>
