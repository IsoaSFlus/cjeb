                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疟</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>疟</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">瘧</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">n&uuml;&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&uuml;&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄩㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～疾〕急性传染病，病原体是疟原虫，由疟蚊传播，症状是周期性发冷发热，热后全身无力。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>疟</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">瘧</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～子〕&ldquo;疟（<span class="dicpy">n&uuml;&egrave;</span>）疾&rdquo;的通称，如&ldquo;发～～&rdquo;（亦作&ldquo;打摆子&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疟</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>疟</strong></p>
<p>瘧 <span class="dicpy">n&uuml;&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&uuml;&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;ng</span>)，虐声。本义:病名，即疟疾)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[malaria]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疟者，风寒之气不常也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;疟论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">许悼公</span>疟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十九年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疟，寒热休作。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疟，酷虐也。凡疾或寒或热耳，而此疾先寒后热两疾，似酷虐者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名、释疾病》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狂诵新诗驱疟鬼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《寓叹》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:疟病(疟疾);疟患(疟病的祸患);疟寒(疟病);疟疠(疟疾;疟疫)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;虐&rdquo;。残暴;灾害<span class="encs">[abuse]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">静作相养，德疟相成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《马王堆汉墓帛书》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>疟</strong></p>
<p>瘧 <span class="dicpy">n&uuml;&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/n&uuml;&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 受虐待 <span class="encs">[be abused]</span>。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7627.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】魚約切【集韻】【韻會】逆約切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音虐。【說文】熱寒休作。【玉篇】或寒或熱病。【釋名】瘧，酷虐也。凡疾或寒或熱耳，而此疾先寒後熱，兩疾似酷虐者也。【禮&middot;月令】孟秋之月，寒熱不節，民多瘧疾。【周禮&middot;天官&middot;疾醫】秋時有瘧寒疾。【疏】秋時陽氣漸消，隂氣方盛，惟火沴金，兼寒兼熱，故有瘧寒之疾。【正字通】瘧有風，寒，暑，熱，濕，食，瘴，邪八種。久瘧腹有痞塊，名瘧母。獨寒不熱為牝瘧，獨熱不冷爲牡瘧。發無期度爲鬼瘧，先寒後熱爲寒瘧，先熱後寒曰溫瘧，熱而不寒曰癉瘧，卽脾瘧。皆痰中中脘，脾胃不和所致。詳見靈樞經瘧論。</p>
