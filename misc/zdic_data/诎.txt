                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>诎</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">詘</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">qū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩˉ</span></p><ol><li>弯曲：诘～（同“诘屈”）。“～五指而顿之”。</li><li>屈服，折服：“公输盘～”。</li><li>嘴笨：“辩于心而～于口”。</li><li>戛然而止的样子：“扣之，其声清越以长，其终～然”。</li><li>尽，穷：“其兵不～”。</li><li>短缩。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">诎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>诎</strong></p><p>詘 <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，出声。本义:言语钝拙)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stutter]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诎，辞塞。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诎，字从言，当与吃同意。——<span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">公输盘</span>诎。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·公输》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">轻财重士，辩于心而诎于口。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李斯列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 尽，穷尽 <span class="encs">[exhausted]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">利出于一孔者，其国无敌;出二孔者，其兵不诎;出三孔者，不可以举兵;出四孔者，其国必亡。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">理诎势穷。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">林则徐</span>文</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 声音戛然而止貌 <span class="encs">[suddenly-mute]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叩之，其声清越以长，其终诎然。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">孔颖达</span>疏:<span class="yhcs">“诎谓止绝也。”</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>诎</strong></p><p>詘 <span class="dicpy">qū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“屈”</span>。弯曲 <span class="encs">[bend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诎，屈也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅·释诂一》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诎五指而顿之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·劝学》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诎要桡腘。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诎右臂支船。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:诎伸(诎申。弯曲与伸直);诎曲(诎折。弯曲);诎体(弯曲肢体);诎膝(下跪);诎柔(屈曲柔弱)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 屈服 <span class="encs">[yield]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然而敌国不待试而诎。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·议兵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诎敌国。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策一》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:诎意(屈就);诎志(曲意迁就);诎服(屈服)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 屈辱;冤屈 <span class="encs">[humiliate;wrong]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宋王</span>因怒而诎杀之。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·壅塞》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:诎身(降低身分，屈己下人);诎辱(委屈和耻辱);诎容(屈辱容忍);诎节(降低身分，降心相从)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“黜”</span>（ <span class="dicpy">chu</span>）。贬退 <span class="encs">[demote;oust;relegate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼公仲者，</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>势能诎之。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·韩策三》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可谓能诎免变化以致之。——<span class="smcs">《说苑·敬慎》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“讫”</span>。止 <span class="encs">[complete;be over]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叩之其声清越以长，其终诎然，乐也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·聘义》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              詘·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8A58.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】區勿切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】曲勿切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音屈。【說文】詰詘也。一曰屈襞。【禮·喪大記】凡<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>衣不詘。【註】謂舒而不卷也。【楚辭·九思】思哽饐兮詰詘。</p><p>又【博雅】曲也，折也。【玉篇】枉曲也。【禮·樂記】習其俯仰詘伸。【史記·管晏列傳】君子詘於不知己，而信於知己者。【前漢·孫寶傳】道不可詘，身詘何傷。</p><p>又【廣韻】辭塞也。【戰國策】於是魏王聽此言也，甚詘。</p><p>又【類篇】充詘，喜失節貌。【禮·儒行】不充詘于富貴。</p><p>又姓。漢有詘強。見【印藪】。</p><p>又【韻會】通作屈。【荀子·勸學篇】若挈裘領，詘五指而頓之。【註】詘與屈同。</p><p>又【集韻】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>渠勿切，音掘。與充詘義同。</p><p>又絕止貌。【禮·聘義】叩之，其聲淸<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27ED7.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧻗</span>以長，其終詘然樂也。</p><p>又【正韻】盡也。【前漢·司馬相如傳】徼<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27BAD.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧮭</span>受詘。【師古註】言獸有力盡者，受而有之。</p><p>又【集韻】敕律切，音怵。與黜同。【戰國策】彼公仲者，秦勢能詘之。【註】詘，貶下也。</p><p>又【集韻】通作絀。【禮·射義】進爵絀地。【註】《內則》以前作絀，《明堂位》以後作詘。</p><p>又【集韻】或作出。【左傳·襄三十年】譆譆出出。【周禮·秋官·庭氏鄭註】引作詘詘。劉昌宗音出。本亦作出。</p><p>又【集韻】奴骨切，嫩入聲。與訥同。詳訥字註。</p><p>又【說文】或作誳。【長箋】作誳。</p>
