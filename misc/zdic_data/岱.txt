                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>岱</strong> <span class="dicpy">Dài</span></p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从山，代声。本义:泰山的别称。也叫<span class="yhcs">“岱宗”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“岱岳”</span>。)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Taishan mountain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岱，太山也。从山，代声。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱</span>按，在今 <span class="diczx2">山东</span><span class="diczx2">泰安府</span><span class="diczx2">泰安县</span>北。</p><p><span class="diczx2">河</span>东 <span class="diczx2">岱</span>。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。按，此言 <span class="diczx2">岱</span>与 <span class="diczx2">华岳</span><span class="diczx2">恒</span><span class="diczx2">衡</span>为五镇之一也。</p><p><span class="diczx1">则有九镇。又</span><span class="diczx2">泰山</span>为东岳。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·职方式》</span>。按，此言 <span class="diczx2">岱</span>与 <span class="diczx2">华</span><span class="diczx2">霍</span><span class="diczx2">恒</span><span class="diczx2">嵩</span>为五岳之长也。 <span class="diczx2">唐</span><span class="diczx2">虞</span>则言四岳， <span class="diczx2">霍</span>即 <span class="diczx2">衡山</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx1">东方为</span><span class="diczx2">岱宗</span>者，言万物更相代于东方也。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通·巡狩》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">太山</span>王者告代之处，为五岳之宗，故曰岱宗。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·安帝纪》</span>注</p><p><span class="diczx1">古树森齐，聒聒幽禽鸣远</span><span class="diczx2">岱</span>。——<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:岱斗(泰山、北斗);岱舆(传说中的海上仙山)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古国名 <span class="encs">[Tai state]</span>。在今河北省蔚县东北</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/岱祠">岱祠</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
