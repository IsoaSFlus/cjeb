                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>余</strong></p>
<p><strong>（➋➌➍<span class="diczx3">餘</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄩˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>文言代词，我：&ldquo;～将老&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>剩下来的，多出来的：剩～。～粮。～兴。～悸。～孽。节～。～生。～荫（指前人的遗泽，遗留的庇荫）。～勇可贾（gǔ）（还有剩余的力量可以使出来）。</li>
	<li>十、百、千等整数或名数后的零数：十～人。</li>
	<li>后：&ldquo;劳动之～，欢歌笑语。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>农历四月的别称。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">余</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>余</strong></p>
<p>餘 <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，余声。本义:饱足)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 饱足。足食得饱 <span class="encs">[have eaten one&#39;s fill;be full]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">余，饶也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 剩下;剩余 <span class="encs">[remain;leave orer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">昔人已乘黄鹤去，此地空余黄鹤楼。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">崔颢</span><span class="smcs">《黄鹤楼》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>余</strong></p>
<p>餘 <span class="dicpy">y&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 剩下的，多余的 <span class="encs">[surplus;spare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行有余力，则以学文。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦无使有余。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;辩士》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹多也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">父有余(余)财。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吴仲山碑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今也每食无余(余)。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;秦风&middot;权舆》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:余夫(指一家五口或八口为率以外多余的人口);余润(利润);余资(剩余的钱);余师(很多余地;<span class="encs">[空余的地方]</span>的老师)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 残留的;遗留的 <span class="encs">[remaining]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孤嶂</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>碑在，荒城 <span class="diczx2">鲁</span>殿余。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《登兖州城楼》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肯与邻翁相对饮，隔篱呼取尽余(余)杯。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《客至》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:余蔌(剩余的菜肴);余庆(祖先留下的福泽。即因前辈的善行而使子孙得到某些好处);余水(剩余的水);余芳(残花;死后遗留的美名);余杯(杯中残余的酒);余珍(前代遗留的遗物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 次要的 <span class="encs">[second]</span></p>
