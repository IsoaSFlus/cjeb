                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">啬</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>啬</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">嗇</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">s&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄜˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>小气，该用的财物舍不得用：吝～。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/穑">穑</a>&rdquo;，收割庄稼。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> miserly, thrifty; stingy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> geizig ,Se  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> avare</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">啬</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>啬</strong></p>
<p>嗇 <span class="dicpy">s&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象粮食收入谷仓形。小篆从來，从㐭，&ldquo;來&rdquo;指麦子，&ldquo;㐭&rdquo;(<span class="dicpy">lǐng</span>)指粮仓。本义:收获谷物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[harvest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">(嗇)此字本训当为收谷，即穑之古文也。转注为爱濇之义，或借为濇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腊之祭也，主先啬而祭司啬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特牲》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">啬夫承命。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;觐礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王狩，啬人不从。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;夏小正》</span>。按，重农事也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主人出，写啬于房。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;特牲礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;啬者，农力之成功。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">主先啬而祭司啬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;郊特牲礼》</span>。疏:&ldquo;种曰稼，敛曰啬。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">舍我啬事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;殷本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">啬黍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;少牢礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;收敛曰啬。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">服田力啬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;成帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:啬事(农事);啬夫(农夫，同穑夫，穑人);啬黍(收获的黍稷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 爱惜 <span class="encs">[cherish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">啬，爱濇也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">治人事天，莫若啬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡事之本，必先治身，啬其大宝。&mdash;&mdash;《吕氏春秋&middot;先己</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:吝啬(过分爱惜自己的财物);啬神(爱惜精神);啬气(爱惜保养元气);啬养(犹保养)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 节省;节俭 <span class="encs">[economize;save]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少费谓之啬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;解老》</span></span></p>
