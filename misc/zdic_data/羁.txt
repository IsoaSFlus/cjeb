                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>羁</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">羈</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>马笼头：无～之马。～绁（a.马笼头和缰绳；b.喻束缚）。</li>    <li>束缚，拘束：～押。～绊。～缚。～囚。放荡不～。</li>    <li>停留，使停留：～旅（长久寄居他乡）。～留。～泊。～滞。</li>    <li>古代女孩留在头顶像马笼头的发型。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>羁</strong></p><p>羈、羈 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从网(罒)，从革(皮革)，从马。合起来表示用皮革制成的网络来把马络住。本义:马笼头)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bridle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">红牛缨绂黄金羁。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《汴泗交流赠张仆射》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:羁縻(羁，马络头;縻，牛紖。喻联络、维系);羁勒(马络头)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代女孩留在头顶像马络头一般的头发 <span class="encs">[(hairdressing) a bun]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">男角女羁。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:男角女羁;羁角(孩童发髻。又称羁卯，羁贯);羁贯(古时儿童发式)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>羁</strong></p><p>羈 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 套上笼头 <span class="encs">[fasten]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使麒麟可系而羈兮，岂云异夫犬羊。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《吊屈原赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此犹禽鹿，少见驯育，则服从教制，长而见羈，则狂顾頓缨。——</span><span class="diczx2">三囯</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>· <span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《与山巨源绝交书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 系，捆绑;束缚，拘束 <span class="encs">[bind;restrain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仆少负不羈之才。——</span><span class="diczx2">司馬遷</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p><p><span
