                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>铰</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">鉸</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jiǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄠˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>用剪刀的两刃相夹切，用剪刀剪：把绳子～开。</li>    <li>一种金属切削工具，称“铰刀”（方言，亦指剪刀）。</li>    <li>用绞刀切削：～孔。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hinge; shears, scissors</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schneiden, kürzen ,Marihuana, Messer  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pivot,pointe,couper,dorer</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>铰</strong></p><p>鉸 <span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 剪刀，亦称铰刀 <span class="encs">[scissors]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">欲将鬃鬣重剪裁，乞借新成利铰刀。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹唐</span><span class="smcs">《病马》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铰，即今妇功缝人所用者，俗呼剪手。——<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 钉铰，即补钉。又为铜匠之称 <span class="encs">[patch]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">铰，今凡刀柄、鞍首皆有钉铰。——<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 金属装饰<span class="encs">[metal ornament]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宝铰星缠，镂章霞布。——<span class="smcs">《文选·颜延之·赭白马赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 机械工业中的一种用铰刀切削修光孔的加工方法 <span class="encs">[ream]</span>。如:铰孔</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 铰链 <span class="encs">[hinge]</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>铰</strong></p><p>鉸 <span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用剪刀剪东西 <span class="encs">[cut with scissors]</span>。如:铰一件男衬衣;铰成两半;铰头发</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 用铰刀扩大或修整 <span class="encs">[ream]</span>。如:铰孔</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/铰刀">铰刀</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/铰接">铰接</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/铰链">铰链</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9278.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>古巧切，音絞。【玉篇】釘鉸。【釋名】鏑，敵也。齊謂之鏃，關西謂之釘鉸，今凡刀柄鞍首，皆有釘鉸。</p><p>又【廣韻】鉸刀。【六書故】交刃刀也。利以翦。【李賀·五粒小松歌】綠波浸葉滿濃光，細束龍髥鉸刀翦。【註】束龍髥，形容松葉之齊，如刀翦截也。鉸，卽今婦功縫人所用者。俗呼翦刀。</p><p>又【廣韻】古肴切，音交。義同。</p><p>又【廣韻】古孝切【韻會】【正韻】居效切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音敎。義同。</p><p>又【類篇】以金飾器也。【顏延之·赭白馬賦】寶鉸星纏。【註】鉸，裝飾也。</p>
