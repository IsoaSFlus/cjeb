                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>班</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>一群人按次序排成的行列：排～。按部就～。</li><li>工作或学习的组织：～组。～级。～长。～主任。领～。</li><li>军队编制中的基层单位，在“<span class="diczx3">排</span>”以下。</li><li>工作按时间分成的段落，亦指工作场所：早～。下～。值～。～房。</li><li>定时开行（<span class="dicpy">xíng</span>）的：～车。～机。～期。</li><li>量词（a.用于人群，如“这～人真能干”；b.用于定时开行的交通运输工具，如“他搭下一～飞机走”）。</li><li>调回或调动（军队）：～师。～兵。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">斑</span>”，杂色。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">班</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>班</strong> <span class="dicpy">bān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从玨，从刀。金文，中间是刀，左右是玉。象用刀割玉。本义:分割玉)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 分瑞玉。瑞玉是古代玉质的信物，中分为二，各执其一以为信 <span class="encs">[divide equally]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">班，分瑞玉也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">班瑞于群后。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:班瑞(颁还瑞玉;颁赐祥瑞)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 分开;离群 <span class="encs">[separate;part]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挥手自兹去，萧萧班马鸣。——</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《送友人》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:班马</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 赏赐;分给 <span class="encs">[reward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">晋</span>侯执 <span class="diczx2">曹伯</span>，班其所取侵地于诸侯也。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·僖公三十一年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:班扬符赏(颁布相应的赏赐);班授(颁发授予);班散(分给);班赐(颁赐;分赏)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 分布;铺开 <span class="encs">[distribute;spread]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遇之于</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>郊，班荆相与食。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十六年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:班卖(摊开出卖);班荆</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 颁布。后作<span class="yhcs">“颁”</span> <span class="encs">[promulgate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">周公</span>…制礼乐，班度量，而下大服。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·翟方进传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:班政(颁布政令);班论(颁布);班布(犹公布)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 返回 <span class="encs">[call back]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">请班师。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“还也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 又如:班军(回师，撤军);班旋(犹班师)</p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 排列 <span class="encs">[arrange;put in order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">班位于天下。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·存韩》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>班</strong> <span class="dicpy">bān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 职位等次，位次;等级 <span class="encs">[order]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">班在九人。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公六年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“位也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">徐勉</span>为吏部尚书，定为十八班。——<span class="smcs">《隋书·百官志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
