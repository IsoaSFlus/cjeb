						<p>● <strong>呕</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">嘔</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ǒu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ǒu.mp3"></a></span> ㄡˇ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>吐：～吐。～血。～心沥血（形容费尽心血）。作～（恶心，厌恶）。</li></ol><p>● <strong>呕</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">嘔</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">ōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄡˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>〔～哑〕形容管弦乐曲、婴儿说话、摇橹、鸟鸣等声音，如&ldquo;～～学语&rdquo;、&ldquo;～～管弦&rdquo;。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/讴">讴</a>&rdquo;，歌颂。</li></ol><p>● <strong>呕</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">嘔</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄡˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/怄">怄</a>&rdquo;，怄气。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>呕</strong></p>
<p>嘔 <span class="dicpy">ǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，区( <span class="dicpy">ōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ōu.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:东西在胃喉中上涌，从口中出来)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[vomit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欧，吐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作呕。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾伏嘔呕血。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其音如呕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《山海经&middot;东山经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有女子方跪，据树而呕丝北海外也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《博物志&middot;异人》</span></span></p>
