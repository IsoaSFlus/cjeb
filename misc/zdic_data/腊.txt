                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">腊</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>腊</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">臘</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄚˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古代在农历十二月合祭众神叫做腊，因此农历十二月叫腊月。</li>	<li>冬天（多在腊月）腌制后风干或熏干的肉：～肉。～鱼。～味。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol><p>● <strong>腊</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>干肉：&ldquo;噬～肉，遇毒。&rdquo;</li>	<li>晾干：&ldquo;燃得而～之以为饵。&rdquo;</li>	<li>皮肤皴皱。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">腊</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>腊</strong></p>
<p>臘、臈 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>(形声。从肉，巤( <span class="dicpy">li&egrave;</span>)声。本义:年终祭祀) 祭名。古代阴历十二月的一种祭祀。冬至后第三个戌日祭祀众神 <span class="encs">[sacrifice at the end of the lunar year]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">冬至后三戌，腊祭百神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作臈。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">夏</span>曰嘉平， <span class="diczx2">殷</span>曰清祀， <span class="diczx2">周</span>曰大蜡， <span class="diczx2">汉</span>曰腊。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">蔡邕</span><span class="smcs">《独断》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腊先祖五祀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">虞</span>不腊矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公五年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:腊日(古时腊祭之日。农历十二月初八);腊祭(古时岁终祭祀);腊会(古代腊祭时的集会);腊鼻(本为劣等鹞鹰，喻指无能之人)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>腊</strong></p>
<p>臘 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/814A.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>思積切，音昔。【說文】乾肉也。从殘肉，日以晞之。【周禮&bull;天官】腊人掌乾肉，凡田獸之脯腊。【註】大物解肆乾之，謂之乾肉。薄析曰脯，捶之而施薑桂曰鍛脩。腊，小物全乾者。【易&bull;噬嗑】六三噬腊肉。【疏】腊，是堅剛之肉也。</p><p>又久也。【禮&bull;郊特牲&bull;猶明淸與醆酒於舊澤之酒也註】爲其味厚腊毒也。【釋文】腊，音昔。隱義云：腊，久也。久酒有毒。【前漢&bull;五行志】味厚者腊毒。【註】腊，久也。味厚者爲毒久也。</p><p>又極也。【鄭語】毒之酋腊者，其殺也滋速。【註】腊，極也。</p><p>又亟也。【周語】厚味實腊毒。【註】腊，亟也。</p><p>又措也。【釋名】齊人云搏腊，搏腊猶把作，麤貌也。荆州人曰麤麻，韋草，皆同名也。麤，措也，言所以安措之也。</p><p>又官名。【周禮&bull;天官&bull;獸人】凡獸入于腊人。</p><p>又體皴也。【山海經】錢來之山，有獸焉，名曰羬羊，其脂可以已腊。【註】治體皴。腊音昔。</p>
