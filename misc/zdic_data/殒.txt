                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>殒</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">殞</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yǔn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔn.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>死：～命。～殁。～身。～阵。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">陨</span>”，坠落。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> die, perish; vanish; fall</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> krepieren, verenden ,Gewindeschneidbacke  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mourir,périr</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>殒</strong></p><p>殞 <span class="dicpy">yǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从歺(<span class="yhcs">“歹”</span>是隶楷写法)，员声。歺( <span class="dicpy">è</span>)，剔肉残余的骨头，常与<span class="yhcs">“死亡”</span>有关。本义:死亡)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[die;perish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">殒，没也。——<span class="smcs">《一切经音义》</span>引<span class="smcs">《声类》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">殒，字书作陨，同于敏反。——<span class="smcs">《一切经音义三》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">妻子颠殒。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·隗嚣传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未尝殒于城中。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:殒亡(灭亡);殒骨(亡躯);殒没(死亡);殒逝(丧亡;逝世);殒谢(谢世，死亡)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“陨”</span>。坠落 <span class="encs">[fall from the sky or outer space]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">列星殒坠。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">殒越为期。——</span><span class="diczx2">任昉</span><span class="smcs">《为齐明帝让宣城郡公第一表》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>为天子，而 <span class="diczx2">启</span>为嗣，持 <span class="diczx2">禹</span>之功而不殒。—— <span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《列女传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:殒越(坠落;毁败);殒涕(殒泣。落泪)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/殒灭">殒灭</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/殒命">殒命</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/殒殁">殒殁</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/殒身">殒身</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/殒阵">殒阵</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
