						<p>● <strong>术</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">術</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>技艺：技～。艺～。武～。学～。不学无～。</li>
	<li>方法：战～。权～。心～。</li>
	<li>古代城市中的道路。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>术</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/秫">秫</a>&rdquo;。黏谷子。《説文&bull;禾部》：&ldquo;秫，稷之黏者。或省禾。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。《章丘志》：&ldquo;（章丘）縣有术姓，自云金丞相术虎高琪之後。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>术</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>草名。菊科术属植物的泛称。多年生草本。有白术、苍术等数种。也作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/𦬸"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26B38.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦬸</span></a>&rdquo;。《爾雅&bull;釋草》：&ldquo;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26B38.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦬸</span>，山薊，楊枹薊。&rdquo;《集韻&bull;術韻》：&ldquo;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26B38.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦬸</span>，草名。《説文》：&lsquo;山薊也。&rsquo;或作术。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>术</strong></p>
<p>術 <span class="dicpy">sh&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从行，术声。行，甲骨文中指道路。本义:城邑中的道路)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[road]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">術，邑中道也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">术，道也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">审端径术。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">园圃术路。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span>。注:&ldquo;大道也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">横术何广广兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;燕刺王旦传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">归空城兮，狗不吠，鸡不鸣，横术何广兮，固知国中之无人!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指街道、道路 <span class="encs">[street;road]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐城</span>、 <span class="diczx2">高唐</span>当术而大败。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙膑兵法&middot;擒庞涓》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:术阡(道路);术径(大道与小路);术路(大道);术衢(道路)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 方法;策略 <span class="encs">[method;tactics]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣有百胜之术。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;魏策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">叩其术，曰:&ldquo;是无难，别具本章，狱词无易，但取案末独身无亲戚者二人易汝名。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子有何术可导我耶?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李朝威</span><span class="smcs">《柳毅传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:术法(方法);术鹄(方法与目的)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 特指君主控制和使用臣下的策略、手段 <span class="encs">[tactics]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孤不度德量力，欲信大义于天下，而智术浅短，遂用猖獗，至于今日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 权术;计谋 <span class="encs">[political trickery]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:术数(方法和谋略);术计(权术计谋);术略(韬略;谋略);术谋(讲求权术谋略)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 技艺;业术 <span class="encs">[art;skill;techinque]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古之学术道者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乡饮酒义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">役民三司盗者，授以击刺之术。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《教战守》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 法，法律 <span class="encs">[law]</span></p>
