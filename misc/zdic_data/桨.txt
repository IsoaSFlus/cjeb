<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">槳</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎng.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄤˇ</span></p>
