                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>运</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">運</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>循序移动：～行。～动。～转（<span class="dicpy">zhu&agrave;n </span>）。</li>	<li>搬送：～输。～载。～营（交通工具的运行和营业）。～力。～销。空～。海～。</li>	<li>使用：～用。～算。～笔。～筹（制定策略）。</li>	<li>人的遭遇，亦特指迷信的人所说的遭遇：～气。命～。幸～。国～。</li>	<li>南北距离：广～百里。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">运</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>运</strong></p>
<p>運 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chu&ograve;</span>)，军声。本义:运行;运转;转动)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[revolve]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">運，移徙也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日、月运行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;系辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日运为躔，月运为逡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹行也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是鸟也，海运。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;逍遥游》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">简文</span>注:&ldquo;徙也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天下可运于掌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:运衡(指运转的玉衡星);运玑(谓运转测天之器。玑，璇玑);运斡(运转);运掌(放在手掌上运转。比喻极为轻易的事情)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 搬运;运输 <span class="encs">[transport]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">运量万物而不匮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;知北游》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">运，转也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三卜之运也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;定公十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将运舟而下浮兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;哀郢》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浑元运物。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《幽通赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">运于渤海之尾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子&middot;汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">(</span><span class="diczx2">建兴</span>)九年， <span class="diczx2">亮</span>复出 <span class="diczx2">祁山</span>，以木牛运，粮尽退军。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">三国</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《嵇中散集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">运一大石块。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:运行李;运兵;运粮;运漕(由水路运粮);运船(载运物资的船只)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 运用 <span class="encs">[use;utilize;wield]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或运智御世，不婴祸故。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">嵇康</span><span class="smcs">《嵇中散集》</span></p>
