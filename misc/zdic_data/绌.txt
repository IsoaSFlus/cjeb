                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>绌</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">絀</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chù  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨˋ</span></p><ol><li>不足，不够：支～（款项不够分配）。左支右～。相形见～（相比之下显得不足）。</li><li>古同“<span class="diczx3">黜</span>”，罢免，革除。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sew, stitch; insufficient</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> inadéquat,insuffisant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">绌</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>绌</strong></p><p>絀 <span class="dicpy">chù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，出声。本义:深红色)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[deep red]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绌，绛也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">却冠林绌。——<span class="smcs">《史记·赵世家》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 不足 <span class="encs">[inadequate;insufficient]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然，其短长优绌，不尽出于一轨。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">冯俊</span><span class="smcs">《谋臣论》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我们山西没有外销的款子，所以有些事情绌于经费，都不能办。——<span class="smcs">《官场现形记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:相形见绌</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>绌</strong></p><p>絀 <span class="dicpy">chù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“诎”</span>。屈服;制服 <span class="encs">[surrender;check;subdue]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">范雎</span>绌 <span class="diczx2">白起</span>， <span class="diczx2">公孙弘</span>抑 <span class="diczx2">董仲舒</span>，此同朝共君宠禄争故耶?——<span class="smcs">《潜夫论·贤难》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“黜”</span>。废除;贬退 <span class="encs">[dismiss]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不孝者，君绌以爵。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">世子学老子者则绌儒学，儒学亦绌老子。——<span class="smcs">《史记·老子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">屈平</span>既绌，其后 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>欲伐 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>， <span class="diczx2">齐</span>与 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>从亲。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:绌臣(被放逐的臣子);绌约(黜退而穷困);绌陟(升降，同黜陟)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
