                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>晚</strong> <span class="dicpy">wǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从日，免声。本义:傍晚;黄昏)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[evening]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晚，莫也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晚，暮也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四时失序，则辰星作异。…政急则出蚤，政缓则出晚。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李寻传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晚成单罗衫。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晚泊沙夹。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一屠晚归。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·狼三则》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">望晚日照城郭。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:晚曦(晚霞);晚夕(傍晚，晚上);晚半天儿(晚夕，晚晌，晚间，晚上);晚钟(傍晚的钟声);晚寒(傍晚的寒气);晚晴;晚饷(晚餐);晚阴(傍晚时的阴霾);晚来(傍晚)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 夜晚 <span class="encs">[night]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晚星没。——</span><span class="diczx2">庾信</span><span class="smcs">《对烛赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:晚吹(夜晚的风);晚头(晚上);晚月(夜月);晚炬(夜间的火炬);晚漏(晚更);晚魄(月亮);晚逻(夜间巡逻)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 晚年 <span class="encs">[old age;one’s later years]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">晚出</span><span class="diczx2">榆关</span>逐北征，惊沙飞迸冲貂袍。—— <span class="diczx2">温庭筠</span><span class="smcs">《塞寒行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:晚运(晚年。运指时期);晚涂(晚年);晚境(晚年的境遇;晚年);晚悟(晚年觉悟)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 秋季 <span class="encs">[antumn]</span>。如:晚菘(秋末冬初的大白菜);晚造(秋季作物);晚果(秋天成熟的果实);晚热(秋天的炎热)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 旧时官场后辈对前辈、下级对上级称晚生，简言曰晚。多用于书信 <span class="encs">[pupil (oft.self-reference in writing or speaking to teacher)]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">恕治晚不能躬送了。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 时间靠后。属于或关于时间上或发展上的后期阶段的 <span class="encs">[late;far on in time]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兴尽晚归舟。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《如梦令》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事之晚矣。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴·汉纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">土气有早晚。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span
