                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>霖</strong> <span class="dicpy">lín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从雨，林声。本义:久下不停的雨)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[long-continued rain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">霖，雨三日以往也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天之无烈风淫雨。——<span class="smcs">《书·大传四》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“淫雨谓之霖。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡雨三日以往为霖。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公九年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">用汝作霖雨。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">七月大霖雨者，人怨之所致。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·高帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">动商则秋霖春降。(动商:吹奏商调之曲。)——</span><span class="diczx2">成公子安</span><span class="smcs">《啸赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:霖雨;霖霖(形容久雨不停)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 雨 <span class="encs">[rain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不知短发能多少，一滴秋霖白一茎。——</span><span class="diczx2">韩偓</span><span class="smcs">《秋霖夜忆家》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:甘霖</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>霖</strong> <span class="dicpy">lín</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lín.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>久雨不止 <span class="encs">[It rained endlessly]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">景公</span>之时，霖雨十有七日。——<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋·谏》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">日夜霖雨。——<span class="smcs">《六韬》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/霖雨">霖雨</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
