                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">祥</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>祥</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从示，羊声。本义:凶吉的预兆，预先显露出来的迹象)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[omen]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无道曰祥。——<span class="smcs">《周书·武顺》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吉事有祥。——<span class="smcs">《易·系辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辰马，农祥也。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">将有大祥。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公十八年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“变异之气。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">卓</span>问 <span class="diczx2">肃</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“此何祥也?”</span>——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是何祥也，吉凶焉在?——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 特指吉兆</p><p><span class="diczx1">以观妖祥，辨吉凶。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑玄</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“妖祥，善恶之征。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">袭于休祥。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“福之先见者也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">台不过望氛祥。——<span class="smcs">《国语·楚语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“吉气为祥。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不祥之气。——<span class="smcs">《老子·佳兵》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“喜也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">期而小祥。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士虞礼记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“吉也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指凶兆</p><p><span class="diczx2">亳</span>有祥，桑谷共生于朝。——<span class="smcs">《书·咸有一德》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“祥，妖怪。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孽狐为之祥。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·庚桑楚》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“怪也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:祥桑(妖桑，不吉祥之桑)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古丧祭名，有小祥、大祥之分。周年祭为小祥，两周年祭为大祥<span class="encs">[sacrifice]</span>。如:祥日(亲丧的祭日);祥祭(亲丧满十三个月或二十五个月的祭祀)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 幸福 <span class="encs">[happiness]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">神光并见，咸受祯祥。——<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>祥</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiáng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiáng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 吉利 <span class="encs">[auspicious;lucky;propitious]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祥，福也。从示，羊声。一云善。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祥，善也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“福之善也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">文定厥祥。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·大明》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">忠言信慈祥。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士相见礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是谓大祥。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">弃德不祥。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">知渊中之鱼者不祥。——<span
