                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蛎</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">蠣</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">lì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄧˋ</span></p><ol>    <p>　◎ 〔牡～〕软体动物，有两个贝壳，生活在浅海泥沙中。肉可食，味鲜美，亦能提制蚝油。壳烧成灰可入药。亦称“<span class="diczx3">蚝</span>”、“海蛎子”。</p></ol>
