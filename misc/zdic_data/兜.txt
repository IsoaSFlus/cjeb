                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>兜</strong></p><p>兠 <span class="dicpy">dōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 兜鍪，即胄，古代头盔名 <span class="encs">[helmet]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:兜鞬(头盔和皮革制的弓箭袋，也泛指武器装备);兜鍪铠(战士戴的头盔和铠甲);兜巾(有后兜的帽子)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 缝在衣服里面的或旁边开口的小袋 <span class="encs">[pocket]</span>。如:裤兜</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 装盛东西之物，通常除留一边作开口外，其余各边都是封闭的或网状的 <span class="encs">[bag]</span>。如:网兜儿;兜络(网袋)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>兜</strong> <span class="dicpy">dōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 迷惑;受蒙蔽 <span class="encs">[confuse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在列者献诗，使勿兜。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“感也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:兜罗(笼络;收拾);兜结(奉承，巴结);兜答(兜搭。周折;麻烦;难对付;心眼多)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用兜兜着;用手或衣襟等承装事物 <span class="encs">[wrap in]</span>。如:用毛巾兜着苹果</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 包裹;围绕;环绕 <span class="encs">[wrap up;bind up;move around]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">腰间解下麻索来，兜住</span><span class="diczx2">卢俊义</span>肚皮去那松树上只一勒，反拽过脚来绑在树上。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:在城里兜了一圈;兜翻(捆绑拖翻)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 走遍各地招揽或向个人征求订货或订购 <span class="encs">[canvass，canvas]</span>。如:兜生意</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 承担某事物的责任 <span class="encs">[take responsibility for sth.]</span>。如:出了问题我兜着;兜柁(兜驮。背，驮)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 包围 <span
