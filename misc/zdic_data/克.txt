                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">克</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>克</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">剋</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kè.mp3"></a></span> ㄎㄜˋ</span></p><ol><li>能够：～勤～俭。</li><li>战胜，攻下：攻～。～复（战胜敌人并收回失地）。</li><li>制伏：～服。～制。～己奉公。以柔～刚。</li><li>严格限定：～日。～期。～扣。</li><li>消化：～食。</li><li>公制重量单位或质量单位：一克等于一公斤的千分之一。</li><li>中国西藏地方的容量单位，一克青稞约二十五市斤；亦是其地积单位，播种一克种子的土地称为一克地，约合一市亩。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">克</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>克</strong></p><p>剋、尅 <span class="dicpy">kè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，下面象肩形。整个字形，象人肩物之形。本义:胜任)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be competent]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">克，肩也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，以肩任物曰克。物高于肩，故从高省，下象肩形。古文亦象肩形。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佛时仔肩。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·敬之》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“仔肩，克也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子克家。——<span class="smcs">《易·蒙》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">邾子克。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">周</span>王子克。——<span class="smcs">《左传·桓公十八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">伐柯如何?匪斧不克。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·伐柯》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">克勤于邦，克俭于家。——<span class="smcs">《书·大禹谟》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:克当(担当，承受);克堪(胜利);克家(能承担家事);克祚(能继承祖辈的福禄);克家子(克家儿。能继承祖业的子弟);克明(能察是非)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 能够 <span class="encs">[can;be able to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">克，能也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪斧不克。——<span class="smcs">《诗·齐风·南山》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二曰刚克，三曰柔克。——<span class="smcs">《书·洪范》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“能也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如其克谐，天下可定也。——<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:克化(能够消化);克尽(能够尽力);克协(能够符合;等同);克长(能教诲不倦);克果(能成功，能实现);克承(能够继承);克荷(能够承担);克臬(能奉法行事);克能(能够)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 攻下;战胜;打败 <span class="encs">[defeat;overcome;capture]</span></p><p><span
