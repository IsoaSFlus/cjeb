						<p>● <strong>貰</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>出租，出借：～器店（出租婚喪喜慶應用的某些器物的店鋪）。</li>	<li>賒欠：賒～。</li>	<li>寬縱，赦免：～赦。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> borrow; pardon; loan</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> entleihen, leihen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> emprunter,acheter à crédit,louer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">貰</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>貰</strong></p>
<p>貰 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">動</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形聲。从貝，世聲。本義:借，借貸)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本義 <span class="encs">[borrow or lend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">貰，貸也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《說文》</span>。受者曰賒，予者曰貰。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">則賒貰而予之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周禮&middot;司市》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">民無貸(錢)，則賒貰而予之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周禮&middot;司市》</span></span><span class="diczx2">鄭</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:貰代(借貸);貰貸(借貸;賒欠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 賒欠 <span class="encs">[buy or sell on credit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">縣官無錢，從民貰馬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;汲鄭列傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">常從</span><span class="diczx2">王</span>媼、 <span class="diczx2">武</span>負貰酒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;高祖本紀》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:貰酒(賒酒);貰賬(賒賬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 租佃，租賃 <span class="encs">[hire out;rent]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃貰貸陂田千餘頃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《漢書&middot;寧成傳》</span>。注:「假取之也。」</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:貰錢(租金);貰廛(租地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 抵押 <span class="encs">[mortgage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">蜀</span>江 <span class="diczx2">吳</span>水不通潮，曾共黃壚貰紫貂。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王士禎</span><span class="smcs">《遙送魏推官赴成都》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 買 <span class="encs">[buy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">餘乃就火炙衣，貰燒酒飲五杯乃行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客遊記》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 赦免，寬縱 <span class="encs">[pardon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因貰其罪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《漢書&middot;張敞傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不貰不忍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《國語&middot;吳語》</span>。注:「赦也。」</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂貰寬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《漢書&middot;劉向傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:貰赦(赦免其罪);貰過(寬恕過錯);貰患(免除禍患);貰忍(寬恕容忍);貰死(免死)</span></p>
