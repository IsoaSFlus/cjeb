						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>清</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄥˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>水或其他液体、气体纯净透明，没有混杂的东西，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">浊</span>&rdquo;相对：～水。～泉。～流（ａ．澄澈的水流，如&ldquo;一股～～&rdquo;；ｂ．旧时指负有名望，不肯与权贵同流合污的士大夫）。～澈。～碧。～朗。～新。～醇。月白风～。</li>
	<li>安静，不烦：冷～。凄～。～闲。～静。～淡。～幽。～谧（宁静）。</li>
	<li>单纯不杂：～唱。～茶。</li>
	<li>明白，明晰：～楚。～晰。～醒。～通（文章层次清楚）。～亮。</li>
	<li>一点不留，净尽：～除。肃～。～剿。～洗。～君侧（清除国君身边的亲信）。</li>
	<li>整理，查验：～理。～查。～点。～仓。</li>
	<li>详细登记：～册。～单。</li>
	<li>公正，廉洁：～廉。～正。～官。～绩。</li>
	<li>洁净，纯洁：～洁。～爽。冰～玉洁。</li>
	<li>高洁，高尚的，高明的：～高。～绮。～雅。～操。～介（清高耿直）。～望（清白高尚的声望）。～识（高明的见识）。</li>
	<li>太平，不乱：～平。～泰。～和。</li>
	<li>中国朝代名：～代。～宫秘史。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>清</strong> <span class="dicpy">qīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，青声。&ldquo;青&rdquo;，碧绿透彻，也有表意作用。本义:水清)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。与&ldquo;浊&rdquo;相对 <span class="encs">[limpid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沧浪之水清兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">河水清且涟猗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;魏风&middot;伐檀》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举身赴清池。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">临清流而赋诗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">发之欲其清。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水尤清冽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:清直(河水清澈顺流貌);清波(清澈的水流);清泚(清清的河水);清涟(清澈的细水波);清活活(水清澈而流动貌);清照(清澈明亮);清悠悠(清澈明亮貌);清深(水色清澈);清酤(清圣。清酒);清雨(清净的雨);清英(清酒);清茗(清茶)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 清洁;洁净;纯洁 <span class="encs">[clean;pure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">京室密清。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">圣人守清道而抱雌节。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身中清。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;微子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太清之始也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伏清白以死直兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:清人(纯洁的人);清交(纯洁的交情);清休(纯洁美善);清行(纯洁的品行)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 清明 <span class="encs">[clear and bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视若清明。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中心不定，则外物不清。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;解蔽》</span>。注:&ldquo;明审也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:清化(清明的教化);清治(清明的治绩)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 清凉 <span class="encs">[cool]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">其候清切。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;五常正大论》</span>。注:&ldquo;大凉也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">清风半夜鸣蝉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《西江月》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">清风徐来。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学</span>洢<span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:清快(清凉爽快);清风明月(清凉的风，明亮的月);清宫(清凉的宫室);清室(清凉的屋室);清软(清凉柔和)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 清冷，凄清 <span class="encs">[chilly]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">清角吹寒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姜夔</span><span class="smcs">《扬州慢》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">更那堪冷落清秋节。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 清平;太平 <span class="encs">[peaceful and orderly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">当</span><span class="diczx2">纣</span>之时，居 <span class="diczx2">北海</span>之滨，以待天下之清也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;万章下》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:清世(清平盛世);清夷(清平安定)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 寂静 <span class="encs">[quiet]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 清雅高尚 <span class="encs">[morally lofty or upright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举世皆浊我独清，众人皆醉我独醒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;渔父》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 又如:清修如鹤(指人品高洁);清立(清高特立);清抗(清高超拔)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 清白 <span class="encs">[fair]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 又如:清廉(清白廉洁);清心涂胆(心地清白，肝胆可照);清洁(贞洁);清操(清白的操守);清臣(志行清白的人)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(18)</span> 清静;恬静 <span class="encs">[quiet]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(20)</span> 清正 <span class="encs">[clear and upright]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(21)</span> 又如:清耿耿(清正耿直);清抗(清高正直);清良(清正贤良);清忠(清正忠诚);清徽(清正的风操。高雅的谈吐);清劲(清正刚直)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(22)</span> 清廉 <span class="encs">[honest and upright]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(23)</span> 又如:清鲠(清廉鲠直):清俭(清廉俭朴);清头(清廉);清己(以清廉之德要求自己);清公(清廉公正)</p>
