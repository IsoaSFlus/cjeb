                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>仓</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">倉</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">cāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄘㄤˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>收藏谷物的建筑物：米～。粮～。～储。～房。</li>
	<li>匆忙。也作&ldquo;～猝&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> granary; berth; sea</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Laden, Kaufhaus  (Wirtsch)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> dépôt,magasin,grenier,grange,soute</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>仓</strong></p>
<p>倉 <span class="dicpy">cāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，上象盖儿，中间象一扇门，下面是进出的口儿，合起来表示仓库这个概念。本义:粮仓)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[granary;barn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仓，谷藏也，仓黄取而藏之，故谓之仓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">仓廪实而知礼节。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">藏帝藉之收于神仓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span>。注:&ldquo;藏祭祀之谷为神仓。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:谷仓(粮仓);仓廒(粮库);仓敖(储藏米谷的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指储藏物资的建筑物 <span class="encs">[warehouse;storehouse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:盐仓;货仓;添仓;填仓;义仓;仓府(贮存钱财和粮食的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 匆忙急迫 <span class="encs">[all of a sudden;hurriedly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:仓卒;仓皇;仓遽;仓迫</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5009.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕仺【唐韻】七岡切【集韻】千岡切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音蒼。【說文】穀藏也。【國策註】圓曰囷，方曰倉。【詩&middot;小雅】乃求千斯倉。【禮&middot;月令】季秋，命冢宰舉五穀之要藏，帝籍之，收于神倉。</p>
<p>又官名。【周禮&middot;地官】倉人掌粟入之藏。</p>
<p>又倉卒，悤遽貌。【杜甫&middot;送鄭虔詩】倉皇已就長途往。</p>
<p>又姓。周倉葛。</p>
<p>又與蒼通。【禮&middot;月令】駕倉龍。【前漢&middot;蕭望之傳】倉頭廬兒。</p>
<p>又與臓通。五倉，五臟也。【前漢&middot;谷永傳】成帝曰：化色五倉之術，皆左道以欺妄。</p>
<p>又與滄通。【揚雄&middot;甘泉賦】東燭倉海。</p>
<p>又與桑通。亢倉子，亦作庚桑子。</p>
<p>又【集韻】楚亮切，借作愴。【詩&middot;大雅】倉兄塡兮。</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5009.svg">                          <p>榖藏也。倉黃取而藏之，故謂之倉。从食省，口象倉形。凡倉之屬皆从倉。仺，奇字倉。七岡切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5009_239.svg" /><span class="swbf">(倉)</span><span class="ywyy">穀藏也。</span><span class="swdz">藏當作臧。臧，善也。引伸之義，善而存之亦曰臧。臧之之府亦曰臧。俗皆作藏，分平去二音。榖臧者，謂榖所臧之處也。广部曰：府，文書藏。庫，兵車藏。廥，芻稾藏。今音皆徂浪切。</span><span class="ywyy">蒼黃取而臧之，</span><span class="swdz">蒼，舊作倉，今正。蒼黃者，匆遽之意。刈穫貴速也。</span><span class="ywyy">故謂之倉。</span><span class="swdz">蒼倉㬪韵。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2028A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠊊</span>省，口象倉形。</span><span class="swdz">七岡切。十部。</span><span class="ywyy">凡倉之屬皆从倉。</span></p>
<hr class="swhr" />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5009_240.svg" /><span class="swbf">(仺)</span><span class="ywyy">奇字倉。</span><span class="swdz">葢从古文巨。</span></p>                          </div>
