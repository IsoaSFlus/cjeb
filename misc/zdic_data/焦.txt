                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>焦</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiāo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄠˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>物体经火烧变成黑黄色并发硬、发脆：烧～。～土。～头烂额。</li>
	<li>烦躁，着急：～急。～虑。～灼。～躁不安。</li>
	<li>酥，脆：～脆。～枣。</li>
	<li>喻干燥到极点：唇～口燥。～枯。～裂。～渴。</li>
	<li>一种质硬、多孔、发热量高的固体燃料：～炭。～煤。～炼。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">焦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>焦</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，上面是&ldquo;隹&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)，短尾鸟，下面是&ldquo;火&rdquo;。把鸟放在火上烤。本义:物经火烧而变黄或成炭)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[charred;burnt;scorched]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焦，火所伤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">焦，火烧黑也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">濡灸之举焦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡气因火变则为焦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:焦盆(焦杯。焚纸的火池);焦鎚(一种烤熟的饼食);焦尾(焦桐，焦尾瑟。东汉蔡邕用一端有焦痕的桐木所制的琴。后多指名贵的琴);焦霹雳(能对人畜、植物或建筑物造成很大危害的霹雷)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 干枯;枯焦 <span class="encs">[withered;desiccated]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五谷焦死。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;非攻下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:焦芽(枯焦的幼芽);焦死(枯死);焦瘦(枯瘦)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 叫喊，吵闹 <span class="encs">[cry; noise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">梅香</span>嗏省闹，小姐哎你休焦</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>焦</strong> <span class="dicpy">jiāo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiāo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 着急 <span class="encs">[worry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">假如你焦他没有房屋，何不替他娶上一个孙媳妇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:焦劳(焦躁不安);焦躁(着急而烦躁);焦闷(焦躁烦闷);</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 干燥 <span class="encs">[dry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">少洎之则焦而不熟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;应言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唇焦口燥呼不得。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 烧焦的 <span class="encs">[burnt;charred]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人一炬，可怜焦土。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜牧</span><span class="smcs">《阿房宫赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 酥，脆 <span class="encs">[fragil]</span>。如:麻花炸得真焦</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7126.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】卽消切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】兹消切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音蕉。【說文】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/244EA.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤓪</span>。火所傷也。【玉篇】火燒黑也。</p>
<p>又炙也。【禮&middot;月令】其味苦，其臭焦。【註】火之臭味也。凡苦焦者皆屬焉。【左傳&middot;哀二年】卜戰龜焦。【禮&middot;問喪】傷腎乾肝焦肺。【史記&middot;越世家】苦身焦思。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;扁鵲傳】別下于三焦膀胱。【註】正義曰：八十一難云：三焦者，水穀之道路也，氣之所終始也。上焦在心下，下鬲在胃上也。中焦在胃中，脘不上不下也。下焦在臍下，當膀胱上口也。【廣韻】別作膲。</p>
<p>又國名。【左傳&middot;僖二十九年】虞虢焦滑霍揚韓魏皆姬姓也。【註】焦在陝縣。【史記&middot;周本紀】褒封神農之後于焦。【註】地理志，弘農陝縣有焦城，故焦國也。</p>
<p>又【秦本紀】圍焦降之。【註】括地志云：焦城在陝州城內東北百步，因焦水爲名。周同姓所封。◎按左傳之焦姬姓，神農之後乃姜姓，其所封當別是一焦。周本紀註亦云：在陝縣。誤。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】神農後，以國爲氏，出南安。</p>
<p>又晉邑名。【左傳&middot;僖三十一年】許君焦瑕。【註】晉河外五城之二邑。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】整居焦穫。【爾雅&middot;釋地】十藪，周有焦穫。【註】今扶風瓠中是也。</p>
<p>又【晏子&middot;外篇】東海有蟲，巢于蟁睫，命曰焦冥。</p>
<p>又焦明，鳥名。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】掩焦明。【註】焦明似鳳。通作鷦。</p>
<p>又【韻會】通作燋。詳燋字註。【集韻】或作㸈。</p>
<p>又【集韻】慈焦切【正韻】慈消切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音樵。【集韻】焦夷，楚地名。【左傳&middot;僖二十三年】楚伐陳取焦夷。【註】焦，譙縣也。夷一名城父。皆陳邑。</p>
<p>又與憔通。【班固&middot;答賔戲】朝爲榮華，夕而焦<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2223C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𢈼</span>。</p>
<p>又【集韻】將由切，音啾。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2894F.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨥏</span>屬。</p>
