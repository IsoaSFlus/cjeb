data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，丈声。本义:执，拿着)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hold a weapon]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">韩信</span>仗剑从之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·淮阴侯列传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">独行仗剑至韩。——<span class="smcs">《史记·刺客列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:仗斧(手持斧铖钺。表示权威);仗节(手持符节;坚守节操);仗策(手持马鞭。骑马)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 依靠，依赖 <span class="encs">[rely on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仗，凭仗。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">仗兵革之彊。——<span class="smcs">《史记·春申君传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朕自得疾，数日不能得睡，今夜仗二将军威势甚安。——<span <strong>仗</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhàng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhàng.mp3"></a></span> class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:兵仗(兵器);器仗;仪仗;明火执仗</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 仪仗队 <span class="encs">[guard of honor or guard]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡朝会之仗，三卫番上，分为五仗，号衙内五卫。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:仗士(卫士);仗下(皇帝视朝毕，所列仪仗兵卫退下);仗马(皇帝仪仗队所用的马);仗队(仪仗队)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 战争或战斗 <span
