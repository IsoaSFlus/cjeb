                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>偿</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">償</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>归还，补还：～还。～命。～债。赔～。得不～失。</li>
	<li>满足：如愿以～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">偿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>偿</strong></p>
<p>償 <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，赏声。本义:归还，赔偿)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[return;give back;refund]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偿，还也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">偿，复也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西邻责言不可偿也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使吏召诸民当偿者悉来合券。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:偿债(偿还欠债);偿付(归还所欠的债)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 报答;酬报 <span class="encs">[requite]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:偿怨(报怨)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 补偿，抵偿 <span class="encs">[pay]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相如视秦王无意偿赵城。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:偿罪;偿死(抵命);得不偿失</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 满足，实现 <span class="encs">[fulfill;meet]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:偿愿(实现心愿);如愿以偿</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>偿</strong></p>
<p>償 <span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>酬报 <span class="encs">[repayment]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:无偿</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集中" target="_blank">子集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集中&amp;bs=人" target="_blank">人</a>】
