                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">核</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>核</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄜˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>果实中坚硬并包含果仁的部分：桃～。杏～。</li>
	<li>像核的东西：～细胞。～酸。～心（中心）。结～。</li>
	<li>指原子核、核能、核武器等：～潜艇。～试验。～战争。～武器。</li>
	<li>仔细地对照、考察：～定。～计。～实。～算。～查。</li>
	<li>翔实正确：其文直，其事～。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>核</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">h&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄏㄨˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>义同&ldquo;核（h&eacute;）&rdquo;，用于某些口语词，如&ldquo;杏核儿&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">核</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>核</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，亥声。本义:果核)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pit;stone]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">核，蛮夷以木皮为箧，状如籢尊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桃李丑核。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释木》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殽核维旅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;宾之初筵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其实濡核。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《素问&middot;五常正大论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贻余核舟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">桃核修狭者。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:枣核;樱桃核;葡萄核;橘核</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 原子核的简称 <span class="encs">[atomic nucleus]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:核弹(原子武器的总称);核反应堆;核反应</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 有核的果实 <span class="encs">[fruit contained pit，stone or seed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">门启，华堂复阁甚秀，馆中有樽酒盘核。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太平广记》</span>引</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 核心;中心 <span class="encs">[core]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">文吏不学，世之教无核也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;量知》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 出现在积分方程中积分号下的已知函数 <span class="encs">[kernel]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:积分方程的核;积分变换的核</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>核</strong> <span class="dicpy">h&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/h&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 查对;审查 <span class="encs">[check]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡学问之法，不为无才，难于距师，核道实义，证定是非也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;问孔》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">综核名实。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;宣帝纪赞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其审核之，务准古法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刑法志》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">颜师古</span>云:核，究其实也。</p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>核</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄏㄜˊ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>h&eacute; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/2686.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">果實內保護果仁，具硬質外殼部分。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「果核」、「杏核」、「桃核」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">物體中像核的部分。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「細胞核」、「原子核」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">比喻事物的中心部分。</span><span class="gc_yy">漢&middot;王充《論衡&middot;量知》：「文吏不學，世之教無核也。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">原子核的簡稱。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「核能」、「核爆」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>

</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
