                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>抱</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄅㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用手臂围住：拥～。～小孩儿。合～。～残守缺（形容保守，不知进取）。</li>
	<li>围绕，环绕：山环水～。～厦（房屋前面加出来的门廊，亦指后面毗连的小房子）。</li>
	<li>胸怀愿望，志向：～负。</li>
	<li>心里存着，怀有：～怨。～恨。～歉。～憾。～病。</li>
	<li>密合：衣服～身儿。</li>
	<li>把他人的子女收养为自己的子女：～养。</li>
	<li>量词：表示两臂合围的量：一～草。</li>
	<li>孵：～窝。～小鸡。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">抱</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>抱</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，包声。本义:用手臂围住)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hold or carry in the arms]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公然抱茅入竹去。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂相与一抱而别。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">犹抱薪救火。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抱石(怀石。指投水而死);抱嫁(苏州风俗。女子出嫁时由阿舅抱上花轿);抱冰(比喻刻苦自励)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 捧着 <span class="encs">[hold in both hands]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">和</span>乃抱其璞而哭于 <span class="diczx2">楚山</span>之下。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;和氏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抱璞(春秋楚人卞和发现一块玉璞，献给楚灰王、武王，两次都被认为是顽石，并刖去双足。后楚文王即位，卞和又抱璞哭于荆山之下，文王命人剖璞，果系美玉，称和氏璧)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 环绕 <span class="encs">[encircle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">清江一曲抱村流。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江村》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陵峦抱江城。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《见会公谈陵阳山水》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:清溪抱村</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 怀藏，心里存有 <span class="encs">[cherish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大师，抱天时，与大师同车。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然则抱此无涯之憾。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至于长者之抱才而困。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宗臣</span><span class="smcs">《报刘一丈先生书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:抱学(心怀学问);抱敌对态度;抱才(怀才);抱志(胸怀大志);抱思(怀念);抱恩(怀念恩惠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;保&rdquo;。保护，爱护 <span class="encs">[protect;defend;cherish;care for]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">全汝形，抱汝生，无使汝思虑营营。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;庚桑楚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 持守;奉 <span class="encs">[maintain]</span>。</p>

<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 〈方〉∶<span class="encs">[衣、鞋]</span> 大小合适 <span class="encs">[fit]</span>。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/62B1.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】薄浩切【集韻】【韻會】簿晧切【正韻】蒲晧切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>袍上聲。懷也。【說文】袌，俗作抱。【司馬相如&middot;上林賦】長千仞，大連抱。夸條直暢，實葉葰楙。【註】楙音茆。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】持也。【增韻】挾也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】薄報切【正韻】蒲報切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音暴。與袌菢同。◎按《說文》捊，步侯切，或从包作抱，徐鉉曰：今作薄報切，以爲懷袌字，非是。廣韻，集韻去聲止有袌菢，而無抱。《正字通》云：懷袌之袌亦作抱，轉爲去聲，卽懷抱之義。今考經史抱字多無音切，讀从去聲亦可，義與上聲同。【書&middot;召誥】保抱攜持厥婦子。【詩&middot;大雅】亦旣抱子。【禮&middot;儒行】抱義而處。【儀禮士】相見禮】凡與大人言，始視面，中視抱。</p>
<p>又氣向日也。【前漢&middot;天文志】暈適背穴，抱珥<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2722B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧈫</span>蜺。【註】凡氣向日爲抱，向外爲背。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】北燕朝鮮洌水之閒謂伏雞曰抱。</p>
<p>又【唐韻】薄交切【集韻】蒲交切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音庖。【集韻】房尤切，音浮。蒲侯切，音裒。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>與掊捊同。引取也。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>披交切，與抛通。棄也，擲也。【史記&middot;三代世表】姜嫄生后稷，抱之山中。【皮日休詩】季春人病抱芳杜。</p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/634A_2245.svg" /><span class="swbf">(捊)</span><span class="ywyy">引埾也。</span><span class="swdz">埾，各本作取，今正。詩釋文作埾，今本譌爲取土二字，非也。埾義同聚。引埾者，引使聚也。玉篇正作引聚也。大雅：捄之陑陑。傳曰：捄，虆也。陑陑，眔也。箋云：捄，捊也。度，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22AA9.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢪩</span>也。築牆者捊聚壤土，盛之以虆，而投諸版中，此引聚之正義，箋與傳互相足。賓筵之仇，鄭讀爲<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2308F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣂏</span>。此捄，鄭釋爲捊。皆於其音之相近得其義也。常棣：原隰裒矣。傳云：裒，聚也。此重聚不重引，故不言引但言聚也。裒者，捊之俗。易：君子以裒多益寡。鄭、荀、董、蜀才作捊，云取也。此重引，故但言取也。</span><span class="ywyy">从手孚聲。</span><span class="swdz">步侯切。三部。</span><span class="ywyy">詩曰：原隰捊矣。</span><span class="swdz">六字小徐本有，玉篇引亦有。</span></p>
