                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>兽</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">獸</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&ograve;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄕㄡˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>哺乳动物的总称，一般指有四条腿、全身生毛的哺乳动物：～类。野～。鸟～。禽～。～医。</li>
	<li>喻野蛮，下流：～欲。～行。人面～心。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> beast, animal; bestial</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> brutaler Mensch, roher Mensch, Barbar  (S)​,Tier, Biest  (S)​,bestialisch, tierisch, barbarisch  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> bête,animal</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兽</strong></p>
<p>獸 <span class="dicpy">sh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形。上象耳，中象头，下象足踩地。兽本是禽兽的总称。古另有&ldquo;獸&rdquo;字，指捕捉禽兽的活动。二字通用。本义:禽兽的总称)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 四足的哺乳动物，多指野兽 <span class="encs">[beast;animal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兽，守备者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四足而毛谓之兽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释鸟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兽毛虫总号。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释兽释文》</span>。按，兽中可畜养者则曰兽。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兽，狐狼之属。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;庭氏》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大兽公之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;兽人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">搏兽于敖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;车攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猛兽步高岗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《却东西门行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兽同足者而俱行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山有猛兽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;说山训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山深而兽往之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兽目(野兽的眼睛);兽工(殷制天子六工之一。即治皮革的工匠);兽人(居心险恶、有如禽兽之人);兽骇(兽类受惊时狂奔乱窜);兽吻(门环上的装饰物;虎口);兽臣(古官名。掌管山泽、田猎之事);兽头大门(豪门显贵、宫殿寺观之正门上的门环，一般用铜制或鎏金的兽头铺首，故称);兽铤(兽疾走)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 干肉 <span class="encs">[dry meat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">实兽于其上，东首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 唐人因避唐高祖李渊祖李虎讳，往往把虎字改作&ldquo;兽&rdquo; <span class="encs">[tiger]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兽口(虎口);兽吻(虎口);兽君(虎的别称)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兽</strong></p>
<p>獸 <span class="dicpy">sh&ograve;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&ograve;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 比喻野蛮、残忍 <span class="encs">[beastly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外内乱，鸟兽行，则灭之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;夏官&middot;大司马》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:兽心;兽欲;兽行(比喻极端野蛮残忍、丧失人性的行为)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 兽形的 <span class="encs">[beast-like]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:兽材(雕绘兽形的柱子);兽面(兽形的面具);兽侯(画有兽形的射靶);兽铠(饰有兽首之形的铠甲);兽舰(船体雕饰兽形的战舰);兽炉(兽形的香炉)</span></p>
