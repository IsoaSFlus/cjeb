						<p>● <strong>浒</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">滸</span>）</strong></p>

<p>● <strong>浒</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">滸</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩˇ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>浒</strong></p>
<p>滸 <span class="dicpy">hǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，许声。本义:水边，指离水稍远的岸上平地)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[waterside]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岸上平地，去水稍远者名浒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释丘》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">绵绵葛藟，在河之浒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;王风&middot;葛藟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万人凿盘石，无由达江浒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《丁都护歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:河浒(黄河边)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 淮水溢出的小水 <span class="encs">[small quantity of water]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">淮</span>为 <span class="diczx2">浒</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">郭璞</span>注:&ldquo;皆大水溢出，别为小水之名。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 浒浒，伐木声 <span class="encs">[lumbering sound]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伐木浒浒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">xǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>浒</strong></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 滸 <span class="dicpy">xǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> &mdash;&mdash;地名用字 。如:浒墅关;浒浦(在江苏省常熟市东北长江岸边)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">hǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
