                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>雁</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 鸟类的一属，形状略像鹅，群居水边，飞时排列成行：～行（<span class="dicpy">háng </span>）。～序。～阵（雁行整齐，如同军队布阵）。～过拔毛（喻为牟利不放过任何机会）。</p></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>雁</strong> <span class="dicpy">yàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)，从人，厂( <span class="dicpy">hǎn</span>)声，隹，短尾鸟。厂，石山之涯岩，也有表意作用。本义:鸟名)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 鸟类的一属，形状略像鹅，颈和翼较长，足和尾较短，羽毛淡紫褐色，善于游泳和飞行 <span class="encs">[wild goose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">雁，雁鸟也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纳采纳吉，请期皆用雁。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">下大夫相见以雁。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士相见礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朔风飘胡雁，惨澹带沙砾。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《遣兴五首》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:雁塔题名(借指考中进士);雁杳鱼沉(雁字鱼书。杳无音信);雁天(雁至秋而南翔，故称秋天为雁天);雁户(指漂泊异乡居处无定的民户。雁因时迁徙，故以雁为喻);雁奴(在雁群中专司警戒的雁);雁字(雁飞行时所排成的行列。雁群飞行时，常排成<span class="yhcs">“一”</span>字或<span class="yhcs">“人”</span>字形，故云。也指官吏的排班);雁臣(指外帮的臣子朝觐有时，如雁的往返有定候)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 代书信 <span class="encs">[letter]</span>。如:雁帛(指书信);雁逝鱼沈(音讯断绝)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>雁</strong> <span class="dicpy">yàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“赝”</span>。伪造的，假的 <span class="encs">[false]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鲁以其雁往。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·说林下》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">曾廷枚</span>云:<span class="yhcs">“真赝，本作真雁，后人加贝。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浸渍数日，漆絮败溃，雁迹尽露。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">王明清</span><span class="smcs">《挥尘后录》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/雁荡山">雁荡山</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
