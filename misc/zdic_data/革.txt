                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>革</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">g&eacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄜˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>去了毛经过加工的兽皮：皮～。～履（皮鞋）。～囊。</li>
	<li>改变：～新。～命（➊原意是改变命运；➋现指改变社会制度、建立新社会的群众运动；➌亦指改造旧技术、旧思想的运动）。改～。变～。</li>
	<li>取消，除掉：～除。～职。～故鼎新（去除旧的，建立新的）。</li>
	<li>中国古代乐器八音之一，如鼓等。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">革</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>革</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形，象被剖剥下来的兽皮。中间的圆形物，是被剥下的兽身皮，余下的部分是兽的头、身和尾。&ldquo;革&rdquo;是汉字部首之一，从&ldquo;革&rdquo;的字多与皮革有关。本义:去毛的兽皮)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。皮的总称 <span class="encs">[leather;hide]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">革，兽皮治去其毛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">齿革羽毛。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">执之用黄牛之革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;遯卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌秋敛皮，冬敛革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;天官》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">江南出梓、姜、桂、金、锡、连、丹沙、犀、瑇瑁、珠玑、齿、革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:人造革;革带(皮带);革笥(用皮作的甲胄);革船(用皮革缝制的船)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用革制成的甲胄 <span class="encs">[leather armor and weaponry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">固国不以山溪之险，威天下不以兵革之利。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;公孙丑下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 车前的饰物 <span class="encs">[ornament on the front of cart]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">革车千乘。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;明堂位》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:革车(战车)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通&ldquo;勒&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">l&egrave;</span>)。马络头。有嚼口的叫勒，没有的叫羁 <span class="encs">[headstall;halter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故</span><span class="diczx2">王良</span><span class="diczx2">造父</span>，天下之善御者也。然而使 <span class="diczx2">王良</span>操左革而叱咤之，使 <span class="diczx2">造父</span>操右革而鞭笞之，马不能行十里，故共之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说右下》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>革</strong> <span class="dicpy">g&eacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/g&eacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 变革，更改 <span class="encs">[change]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">革，改也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鸟兽希革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故因</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>时，本十月为岁首，不革。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;任敖传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">愿革心易行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;严助传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天地阴阳不革而成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;执一》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">厉治革典。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语下》</span></span></p>

<p>又【正字通】人與獸皆曰革。【禮&middot;禮運】膚革充盈。【註】革，卽膚內厚皮也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;明堂位】革車千乗。【註】革車，兵車也。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;司弓矢】王弓弧弓，以授射甲革椹質者。【註】革，革甲也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】攺也。【易&middot;革卦】天地革而四時成。【書&middot;堯典】鳥獸希革。【傳】革，攺也。【疏】毛羽希少攺易。【洪範】金曰從革。【傳】金可以攺更。【管子&middot;山權數】丁氏歸革築室。【註】革，更也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋器】轡首謂之革。【詩&middot;小雅】鞗革冲冲。【傳】鞗，轡也。革，轡首也。【疏】馬轡所靶之外，有餘而垂者，謂之革。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】如鳥斯革。【傳】革，翼也。【箋】如鳥夏暑希革張其翼時。【釋文】革，如字。韓詩作勒，云翅也。</p>
<p>又軍禮曰兵革。【禮&middot;中庸】袵金革。【註】革，甲冑之屬。</p>
<p>又【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大師】皆播之以八音：金、石、土、革、絲、木、匏、竹。【註】革，鼓鼗也。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】革，老也。南楚江湘之閒代語也。</p>
<p>又姓。【廣韻】漢功臣表有煑棗侯革朱。</p>
<p>又【集韻】訖力切，音殛。本作䩯。急也。【禮&middot;檀弓】夫子之病革矣。【註】革，急也。【釋文】紀力切。</p>
