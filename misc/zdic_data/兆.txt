                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>兆</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄠˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>古代占验吉凶时灼龟甲所成的裂纹：卜～。</li>
	<li>事物发生前的征候或迹象，预示：预～。佳～。瑞雪～丰年。</li>
	<li>数名，等于百万（古代指万亿）：～周（电磁波频率单位，每秒一百万周的频率为一兆周）。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> omen; million; mega; also trillion. China = million; Japan and Taiwan = trillion</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Anzeichen, Vorbedeutung  (S)​,Billion (1.000.000.000.000, 10^12)​  (S)​,MB, Megabyte  (S)​,Zhao  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> million,présage</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">兆</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>兆</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。大篆字形像龟甲受灼所生的裂痕。本义:卜兆，龟甲烧后的裂纹)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a sign (in fortune telling)]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兆卜，灼龟拆也。&mdash;&mdash;兆，古文兆卜省。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">掌三兆卜之法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大卜》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不占卦兆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卜之龟，卦兆得大横。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:兆象(占卜时龟板上显示的征象);兆文(据以卜占吉凶的龟甲裂纹);兆卦(显示于卦象上的征兆);兆梦(占梦，圆梦)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 征兆，预兆 <span class="encs">[omen]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">相阴阳，占祲兆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其兆既成，已在宫中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:兆相(预先显示的迹象);兆见(征召显现)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 人民;百姓 <span class="encs">[common people]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">行不群以颠越兮，又众兆之所仇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九章&middot;惜诵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:兆民(古称太子之民，后泛指众民，百姓。又称&ldquo;兆人&rdquo;、&ldquo;兆姓&rdquo;、&ldquo;兆萌&rdquo;、&ldquo;兆蒙&rdquo;、&ldquo;兆庶&rdquo;、&ldquo;兆众&rdquo;、&ldquo;兆黎&rdquo;、&ldquo;兆灵&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 数词。一百万;极言众多 <span class="encs">[million]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">岂天地之大，于兆人万姓之中，独私其一人一姓乎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:兆物(兆类。万物);兆亿(数词。极言众多。又指民众);兆古(太古，远古)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古代设于四郊的祭坛 <span class="encs">[altar]</span>。如:兆日(设置祀日神坛);兆位(四郊祭坛的处所);兆祀(设坛祭祀);兆守(监守祭坛的职司)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 墓地，埋葬死人之处 <span class="encs">[grave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无入子兆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;哀公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兆五帝于四郊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;春官&middot;小宗伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾力能改葬，当葬汝于先人之兆。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《祭十二郎文》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 又如:兆域(墓地四周的疆界。亦以称&ldquo;墓地&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 姓</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5146.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】治小切【集韻】【韻會】直紹切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音肇。【說文】灼龜坼也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大卜】掌三兆之法，一曰玉兆，二曰瓦兆，三曰原兆。【註】兆者，灼龜發于火，其形可占者。【前漢&middot;文帝紀】兆得大橫。【註】應劭曰：龜曰兆。</p>
<p>又壇域塋界皆曰兆。【前漢&middot;郊祀志】謹按周官兆五帝于四郊。【註】兆謂爲壇之塋域也。【孝經&middot;喪親章】卜其宅兆，而安厝之。【註】塋墓界域也。</p>
<p>又朕兆。【老子&middot;道德經】我則泊兮其未兆。【註】意未作之時也。</p>
<p>又數名。【韻會】十萬爲億，十億爲兆。</p>
<p>又京兆。【韻會】兆者，衆數。言大衆所在也。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;曆書】游兆執徐三年。【註】游兆，景也。執徐，辰也。丙辰歲也。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶直遙切，音朝。【晉書&middot;樂志歌】神之來，光景昭。聽無聲，視無兆。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/209DE.svg">                          <p>灼龜坼也。从卜；兆，象形。兆，古文兆省。治小切〖注〗<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>省作兆。文八　重二</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/209DE_9218.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">灼龜坼也。</span><span class="swdz">周禮注曰：兆者灼龜發於火，其形可占者，其象似玉瓦原之璺罅，是用名之焉。按凡曰朕兆者，朕者如舟之縫，兆者如龜之坼，皆引伸假借也。</span><span class="ywyy">从卜兆，象形。</span><span class="swdz">治少切。二部。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/209DE_9219.svg" /><span class="swbf">(兆)</span><span class="ywyy">古文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>省。</span><span class="swdz">按古文衹爲象形之字，小篆加卜。非古文滅卜也。廣韵曰：<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>灼龜坼。出文字指歸。兆，治小切，引說文分也。分也之訓见八部<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>下。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>出說文，則不得云出文字指歸。葢古本説文卜部無<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>兆字，八部<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>字卽龜兆字。今<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>音兵列切，卜部兆中多一筆以殊於<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>，皆非古也。玉篇卜部之外，别爲兆部，云兆，事先見也，形也。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>同上。假令顧氏所據説文早同今本，何爲作此紛更乎。是必説文無兆而增此一部，曉然。據篇、韵以正説文，可無疑矣。尋此字之原委，葢由虞翻讀尚書分北三苗爲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>，云<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>，古别字。由是信之者讀説文八部之<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>爲兵列切，又增竄八亦聲於説解中，而説文乃無龜兆字矣。説文無龜兆字，梁顧氏作玉篇乃增兆部於卜部之後。隨曹憲作文字指歸，乃又收<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>爲龜兆字。而改竄説文者乃於卜部增<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>爲篆文，兆爲古文。又恐其形之溷於八部也，乃加增一筆以殊之。紕繆之由，歷歷可見。前注八部，未能了然，後之學者依此説而<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206F9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛹</span>定可也。○又按集韵、類篇皆引説文<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>古省或作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>。臣光曰：按<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>，兵列切，重八也。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/209DE.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠧞</span>，古當作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>。是則免強區分，葢由司馬公始。徐鍇、徐鉉、丁度等皆作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20501.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠔁</span>。司馬公所襲者，夏竦輩之書也。</span></p>
