                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>休</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xiū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄡˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>歇息：～整。～假。～闲。离～。</li>
	<li>停止：～业。</li>
	<li>完结（多指失败或死亡）。</li>
	<li>旧指丈夫把妻子赶回母家，断绝夫妻关系：～妻。</li>
	<li>不要：～想。～提。</li>
	<li>吉庆，美善，福禄：～咎（吉凶）。</li>
	<li>助词，用于语末，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">罢</span>&rdquo;、&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">了</span>&rdquo;等用法相当：归～。</li>
	<li>辞去官职：～官。</li>
</ol>

<p><span class="dicpy">xǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄩˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>通&ldquo;<span class="diczx3"><a href="/hans/煦">煦</a></span>&rdquo;，温和，温暖。</li>
</ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>休</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从人，从木。人依傍大树休息。本义:休息)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[rest]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">休，息止也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">休，息也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南有乔木，不可休思。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;汉广》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">休，象人息木阴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《五经文字》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诚上休陛下余光。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;王莽传》</span>。注:&ldquo;庇荫也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汔可小休。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;民劳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毋休于都。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">止宫休舍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;高帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">未休关西卒。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">景公</span>猎，休，坐地而食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晏子春秋&middot;内篇谏下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故且养兵休卒，蓄锐待敌。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">岳飞</span><span class="smcs">《五岳祠盟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">休于树&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">命士少休。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将士更休。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">劝以少休。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既暮，休于旅舍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异》</span></span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>

	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">停歇、終止。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「休會」、「休學」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;李清照〈鳳凰臺上憶吹蕭&middot;香冷金猊〉詞：「生怕閒愁暗恨，多少事、欲說還<b>休</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《水滸傳&middot;第五一回》：「若孩兒有些好歹，老身性命也便<b>休</b>了。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">辭退官職、工作。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「退<b>休</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;杜甫〈旅夜書懷〉詩：「名豈文章著，官因老病<b>休</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;白居易〈自問〉詩：「宦途氣味已諳盡，五十不<b>休</b>何日<b>休</b>？」</span></p></li>
	<li>

<p>又【周官】作德心逸日休。【詩&middot;商頌】何天之休。</p>
<p>又宥也。【書&middot;呂<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>】雖休勿休。【註】宥之也。我雖以爲宥，爾惟勿宥。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋訓】休休，儉也。【疏】良士顧禮節之儉也。【詩&middot;唐風】良士休休。</p>
<p>又休沐，言休假也。一曰下沐，一曰旬休。唐法，旬休者一月三旬，遇旬則休沐，卽十日一洗沐也。</p>
<p>又致仕曰休。</p>
<p>又休息。【禮&middot;月令】季秋之月，霜始降，則百工休。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;方言】稱傀儡戲曰休，亦曰提休。</p>
<p>又地名。漢封楚元王子爲休侯。見【史記&middot;年表】。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋木】休，無實李。【註】一名趙李。</p>
<p>又蚤休，藥名。</p>
<p>又姓。</p>
<p>又休休，人名。見【五代史】。</p>
<p>又【集韻】吁句切，音煦。氣以溫之也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】弓人角之，本蹙於<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20736.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠜶</span>，而休於氣。</p>
<p>又同咻。【左傳&middot;昭三年】民人痛疾，而或燠休之。【註】燠休，痛念聲。</p>
<p>又叶虚嬌切，音囂。【陸雲&middot;贈鄭曼季詩】拊翼墜夕，和鳴興朝。我之思之，言懷其休。</p>
<p>又叶匈于切，音虛。【吳志&middot;胡綜黃龍大牙賦】含契河洛，動與道俱。天贊人和，僉曰惟休。　【說文】休在木部，人依木則休。《爾雅》庇䕃曰休，會止木庇息意。◎按今《爾雅&middot;釋言》本作庇庥䕃也。</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
