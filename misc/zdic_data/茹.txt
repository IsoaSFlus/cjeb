                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茹</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>茹</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">rú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rú.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>吃，引申为忍受：～素。～荤。～痛（忍痛）。含辛～苦（原意吃苦辣的东西，引申为忍受辛苦）。～古涵今（接受、包含古今的所有知识）。</li><li>臭，败：“以～鱼去蝇，蝇愈至，不可禁”。</li><li>柔软：“柔～而寡断。</li><li>菜：“菜～有畦”。</li><li>根互相牵连的样子：“拔茅～”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茹</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>茹</strong> <span class="dicpy">rú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，如声。本义:吃)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[eat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茹，食也。</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span><span class="diczx2">越</span>之间，凡贪饮食者谓之茹。——<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“今俗呼能粗食者为茹。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">舜</span>之饭糗茹草也，若将终身焉。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·尽心下》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饮其血，茹其毛。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔则茹之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·庶民》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔亦不茹。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">回之家贫，唯不饮酒不茹荤者数月矣。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·人间世》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茹啖其草木荑实。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《苦斋记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:茹荤饮酒(吃肉喝酒);茹毛(太古时人们连毛带血捕食禽兽);茹草(吃草);茹恨(饮恨，含恨);茹素(吃素食，不吃鱼肉等荤腥);茹菜(吃蔬菜);茹荤(本指吃葱韭等辛辣的蔬菜。后指吃鱼肉等);茹薇(采食野菜);茹斋(吃素食)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 喂<span class="encs">[牛马]</span> <span class="encs">[feed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茹，饭牛也。——<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 贪;恣 <span class="encs">[be greedy for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茹，贪也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茹，恣也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 包含 <span class="encs">[contain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">茹古涵今，无有端涯。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">皇甫湜</span><span class="smcs">《韩文公墓铭》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:茹内(容纳);茹古涵今(犹言博古通今)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 引申为忍受 <span class="encs">[endure]</span>。如:含辛茹苦(受尽辛苦);茹恨(饮恨，含恨);茹荼(比喻受尽苦难。荼，苦菜)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 猜想 <span class="encs">[suppose]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我心匪鉴，不可以茹。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·柏舟》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 包，围裹 <span class="encs">[wrap]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十月初冻尚暖，未须茹瓮;…——<span class="smcs">《齐民要术》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 杂揉 <span class="encs">[tan]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">辰放氏</span>作，时多阴风，乃教民搴木茹皮以御风霜。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">罗泌</span><span class="smcs">《路史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 估计;猜度 <span class="encs">[estimate]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 塞 <span class="encs">[block up]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">诸船人行船、茹船、写漏、安标宿止不如法，…笞五十。——<span class="smcs">《唐律疏议》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 腐烂 <span
