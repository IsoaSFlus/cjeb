						<p>● <strong>罐</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>盛东西或汲水用的瓦器，亦泛指各种圆筒形的盛物器皿：瓦～。铁～。～头。拔火～儿。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>罐</strong></p>
<p>鏆、鑵 <span class="dicpy">gu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从缶( <span class="dicpy">fǒu</span>)，雚声。从&ldquo;缶&rdquo;，表示与瓦器有关。本义:用陶或金属制成的汲水器、容器)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[jar;pot;tin]</span>。如:火罐儿;储气罐;汤罐;盐罐;油罐;冷霜罐;罐装(货物或食品装入罐中以出售，称为罐装。大多用于食品工业)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 煤矿装煤用的斗车 <span class="encs">[coal tub]</span>。如:罐笼</p>
