                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">饷</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>饷</strong></p><p>餉、餉、饟 <span class="dicpy">xiǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。左形右声。本义:给在田间里劳动的人送饭)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[carry meal to the field]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">餉，饟也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">餉，餉馈。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饟，周人谓餉曰饟。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饟，馈也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">种饟粮食。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">輓车奉饟者。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·严助传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为雇耕佣，凭种餉。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·章帝纪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“餉，粮也，古餉字。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">数声牛上笛，何处饷田归?——</span><span class="diczx2">前蜀</span>· <span class="diczx2">韦庄</span><span class="smcs">《纪村事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:饷田(送饭到田头);饷人(送饭食的人);饷馌(往田头送饭)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 招待，供给或提供 <span class="encs">[吃喝的东西]</span> <span class="encs">[entertain (with food and drink)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有童子以黍肉饷。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·滕文公下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">幼弟不饷。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:饷客(以饮食招待客人);饷宾(以食物招待宾客)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 吃饭，进餐<span class="encs">[eat]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是故求其诚者，非归饷也不可。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·外储说左上》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 赠送 <span class="encs">[present]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以诗赋饷</span><span class="diczx2">孙权</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">胡冲</span><span class="smcs">《吴历》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:饷亿(赠送，补给);饷献(馈赠奉献)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>饷</strong></p><p>餉 <span class="dicpy">xiǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 军粮及军队的俸給 <span class="encs">[army provisions]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">老弱转饷。——<span class="smcs">《汉叔· 严助传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丁壮苦军旅，老弱罢转饷。——<span class="smcs">《史記·高祖本紀》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饷无所出。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵長蘅</span><span
