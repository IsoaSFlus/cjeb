						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>北</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">běi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/běi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄟˇ</span></p><ol><li>方向，早晨面对太阳，左手的一边，与“<span class="diczx3">南</span>”相对：～方。～辰（古书指北极星）。～上（古代以北为上，后指去本地以北的某地，与“南下”相对）。～极星（出现在天空北部的一颗亮星，人常靠它辨别方向）。～国（指中国北部）。</li><li>打了败仗往回逃：败～。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>北</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">bèi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bèi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄟˋ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 古同“<span class="diczx3">背</span>”，违背，违反。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">北</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>北</strong> <span class="dicpy">běi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/běi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 方位名。与<span class="yhcs">“南”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[north]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人坐立皆面明背暗，故以背为南北之北。——</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span><span class="smcs">《说文通训定声》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">万物负阴而抱阳。——<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王力</span>按，山北为阴，山南为阳，老子的话等于说万物负背而抱南。</p><p><span class="diczx1">然则北通</span><span class="diczx2">巫峡</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">南声函胡，北音清越。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">沛公北向坐。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又北向。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:正北;西北;东北;北邙(即氓山。在河南洛阳市北。东汉及魏晋的王侯公卿多葬于此。泛指墓地);北闱(清代在顺天(今北京)的乡试称<span class="yhcs">“北闱”</span>，在江宁(今南京)的乡试称<span class="yhcs">“南闱”</span>);北里(妓院);北阙(皇宫。借指帝王);北鄙(北方的边邑);北雍(北京的国子监);北邙乡女(代指死去的女性);北津(北方的渡口)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 败逃的军队 <span class="encs">[defeated troops]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">燕</span>兵独追北，入至 <span class="diczx2">临淄</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策一》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>北</strong> <span class="dicpy">běi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/běi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 败北 <span class="encs">[be defeated]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">连战皆北。——<span class="smcs">《史记·项羽本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">鲁</span>人从君战，三战三北。——<span
