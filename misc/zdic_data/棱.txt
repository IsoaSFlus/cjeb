                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>棱</strong> <span class="dicpy">lēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lēng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> ——<span class="yhcs">“不棱登”</span>( <span class="dicpy">bulēngdēng</span>):助词。常用为某些形容词的后缀。如:红不棱登;花不棱登</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">léng;líng</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>棱</strong></p><p>稜 <span class="dicpy">léng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/léng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (俗作<span class="yhcs">“楞”</span>和<span class="yhcs">“稜”</span>。形声。从木，夌( <span class="dicpy">líng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/líng.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:有四角的木)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[square timber]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上觚稜而栖金爵。——</span><span class="diczx2">班固</span><span class="smcs">《西都赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">木四方为棱。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">服虔</span><span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 同一物体的面与面的交接处，即棱角 <span class="encs">[arris;edge;ridge]</span>。如:冰棱，瓦棱;桌子棱儿;搓边棱儿</p><p>◎ <strong>棱</strong> <span class="dicpy">léng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/léng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span
