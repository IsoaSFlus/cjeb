                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>俭</strong></p><p>儉 <span class="dicpy">jiǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从人，佥( <span class="dicpy">qiān</span>)声。本义:自我约束，不放纵)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be cautious about;keep watch over]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俭，约也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君子以俭德避难。——<span class="smcs">《易·否象传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俭德之共也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">严公子</span>广而俭，文而有礼。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十三年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恭俭下人。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:俭貌(态度谦逊);俭退(俭约谦让);俭然(自谦的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 节俭，节省 <span class="encs">[thrifty;frugal;economical]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俭于财用，节于衣食。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·难二》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俭，德之共也;侈，恶之大也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今先君俭而君侈，令德替矣。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俭素为美。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以俭为美。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以俭相诟病。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">由俭入奢易。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:俭克(勤俭而能克制);俭礼(俭约的礼仪);俭德(俭约的品德);俭戚(俭约而悲伤)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 贫乏;贫苦 <span class="encs">[poor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时岁荒民俭。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如: 俭贫(贫乏);俭乏(贫乏，生活困难);俭腹(腹中空虚。喻指知识贫乏)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 歉收 <span class="encs">[bad-harvest]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">丰则籴，俭则粜。——</span><span class="diczx2">房玄龄</span><span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荆扬稻收俭薄。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·陈忠传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:俭年(歉收的年景);俭时(歉收的时日);俭岁(荒年;歉收的年岁)</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 薄;少 <span class="encs">[thin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恭俭而好礼者，宜歌<span class="smcs">《小雅》</span>。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 又如:俭率(俭薄);俭葬(薄葬);俭薄(犹言微薄)</p><p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“险”</span>。险要 <span class="encs">[strategicauy located and difficult of access]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俗险而百姓不一。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·富国》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惠而不俭，直而不径。——<span
