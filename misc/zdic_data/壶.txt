                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>壶</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">壺</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hú.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨˊ</span></p><ol><li>陶瓷或金属制成的一种有把有嘴的器具，通常用来盛茶、酒等液体：茶～。酒～。喷～。油～。</li><li>像壶的形状或出水状态的东西：～铃（举重辅助器械之一，形状像水壶）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>壶</strong></p><p>壺 <span class="dicpy">hú</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hú.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，象壶形。本义:古代盛器)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pot;kettle;bottle]</span>。深腹，敛口，多为圆形，也有方形、椭圆等形制</p><p><span class="diczx1">壶，昆吾圆器也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国子执壶浆。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·昭公二十五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“礼器，腹方口圆曰壶，反之曰方壶，有爵饰。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">八壶设于西序。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">引壶觞。——</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">箪食壶浆。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·诸葛亮传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一片冰心在玉壶。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王昌龄</span><span class="smcs">《芙蓉楼送辛渐二首》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">炉上有壶。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:壶瓶(盛酒器);壶滥(又名壶鉴。器皿的名称);壶尊(古代盛酒器);壶芦(指茶壶或酒壶)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代滴水计时的器具 <span class="encs">[water clock;hourglass]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狄人出壶。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·丧大记》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“漏水之器也。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:壶漏(古代计时器的一种);壶郎(掌管刻漏计时的官员);壶人(管理刻漏掌报时的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代宴饮时投壶的用具 <span class="encs">[vase]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">投壶。壶脰脩七寸，口径二寸半，壶高尺二寸，受豆斗五升，壶腹脩五寸。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:壶矢(壶与矢为投壶用具，因以称投壶);壶飧(用壶盛水泡饭)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通“瓠”</span>。瓠瓜，也叫葫芦 <span class="encs">[bottle gourd]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">七月食瓜，八月断壶。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尝抱壶而度水者，抱而蒙火。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·说林》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">中流失船，一壶千金。——<span class="smcs">《鹖冠子·学问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如:壶庐(瓠、匏、蒲芦等瓜类的总称)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
