                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>砖</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">磚</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuān.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄢˉ</span></p><ol><li>用土坯烧制而成的建筑材料：～坯。～瓦。～砚（制成的砚）。</li><li>形状像砖的东西：茶～。冰～。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>砖</strong></p><p>磚、甎、塼 <span class="dicpy">zhuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuān.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从石，专声。本义:用粘土烧成的长方形块状建材)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[brick]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是犹以塼涂塞江海也。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·正论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“塼涂，以涂垒塼也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒙诏赐银百两，已于</span><span class="diczx2">扬州</span>小郊北地烧砖。——<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:青砖;红砖;砖匠(从事铺方砖或雕砖的工人);砖橛子(碎砖;断砖);砖位(古代朝官班位的标识);砖塔(以砖为主要建筑材料的佛塔);砖块(块砖)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 像砖的东西<span class="encs">[anything shaped like a brick]</span>。如:金砖;茶砖;冰砖</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖茶">砖茶</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖厂">砖厂</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖雕">砖雕</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖工">砖工</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖模">砖模</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖坯">砖坯</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖头">砖头</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖瓦厂">砖瓦厂</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖砚">砖砚</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/砖窑">砖窑</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
