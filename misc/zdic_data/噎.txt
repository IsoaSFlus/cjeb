                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">噎</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>噎</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yē  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yē.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄝˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>食物塞住了嗓子：因～废食。～着了。</li>    <li>因为迎风而呼吸因难：这风真～人。</li>    <li>说话顶撞人，使人无话可答：他说话能把人～死。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> choke; hiccup</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> an einem Bissen würgen ,Drossel  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> s'étouffer,s'étrangler</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">噎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>噎</strong> <span class="dicpy">yē</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yē.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，壹( <span class="dicpy">yī</span>)声。本义:食物堵住喉咙)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[choke]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">噎，饭窒也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">塞喉曰噎。——<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行迈靡靡，中心如噎。——<span class="smcs">《诗·王风·黍离》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:噎塞(消化系统通道受阻);噎呕(喉塞作呕)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 阻塞，蔽塞 <span class="encs">[block up]</span>。如:噎气(因气逆而呼吸困难)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 使某人放弃做某事 <span class="encs">[choke sb. off]</span>。如:噎人(出言顶撞，使人受窘或生气而无法继续说话)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 哽咽 <span class="encs">[choke with sobs]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">张氏</span>]</span>梦神人以宿生事责之曰:<span class="yhcs">“明当死雷斧下”</span>。觉而大恐，流泪悲噎。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">洪迈</span><span class="smcs">《夷坚甲志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:噎噎咽咽(断断续续的哽咽声)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 郁闷;压抑 <span class="encs">[be depressed]</span>。如:噎猺(气结难言);噎喑(沉默，忍气吞声)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/噎嗝">噎嗝</a></span></div><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>噎</strong> <span class="dicpy">shà</span></p><p>〈形〉</p><p>亦作<span class="yhcs">“嗄”</span>。气逆。 <span class="encs">[hoarse of voice]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="yhcs">“嗄，气逆也。或作噎。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《集韵&#x2022;怪韵》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>噎</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄧㄝ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>yē <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5967.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">食物塞住咽喉，气透不过来。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「因噎废食」、「慢点吃，别<b>噎</b>著了。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《诗经·王风·黍离》：「行迈靡靡，中心如<b>噎</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">遮蔽、蔽塞。</span><span class="gc_yy">《管子·度地》：「夏有大露，原烟<b>噎</b>下百草。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《三国志·卷五八·吴书·陆逊传》：「城门<b>噎</b>不得关，敌乃自斫杀己民，然后得阖。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/564E.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】烏結切【集韻】【韻會】一結切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音咽。【說文】飯窒也。【廣韻】食塞。【詩·王風】中心如噎。【傳】噎，憂不能息。【疏】噎者，咽喉蔽塞之名。【後漢·禮儀志】鳩者，不噎之鳥也。【廣韻】或作咽。【集韻】或作䭇饐。</p><p>又【集韻】益悉切，音一。義同。或作䬥。</p><p>又壹計切，音翳。咽痛。【揚子·方言】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24E8A.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤺊</span>嗌，噎也。【註】皆謂咽痛也，音翳。</p><p>又【類篇】乙界切，音噫。與嗄同，詳嗄字註。</p>
