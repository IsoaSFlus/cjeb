                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>橘</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jú  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jú.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄩˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 常绿乔木，果实称“橘子”，多汁，味酸甜可食。种子、树叶、果皮均可入药：～红。～络。～黄色。～化为枳（喻人必然受环境的影响而发生变化）。</p></ol>
