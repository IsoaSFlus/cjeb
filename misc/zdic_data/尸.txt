                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>尸</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>死人的身体：～体。～骸。僵～。借～还魂。</li>    <li>〔～位〕空占着职位而不做事，如“～～素餐，“～～误国”。</li>    <li>古代祭祀时，代表死者受祭的人。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">尸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>尸</strong> <span class="dicpy">shī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆字形，屍像卧着的人形。本义:祭祀时代表死者受祭的人)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[person on behalf of the dead]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尸，神像也。象卧之形。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，凭几曰卧。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">神具醉止，皇尸载起。鼓钟送尸，神保聿归。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·楚茨》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祝延尸。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士虞礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“尸，主也。孝子之祭不见亲之形，象心无所系，立尸而主意焉。又，男，男尸;女，女尸，必使异姓，不使贱者。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孙可以为王父尸。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尸，所祭者之孙也。祖之尸则主人乃宗子。祢之尸则主人乃父道。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·特牲礼》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 神主牌，以木为之 <span class="encs">[a spirit tablet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">载尸集战何所急。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·天问》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:载尸以行(载着神主牌而行)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>尸</strong> <span class="dicpy">shī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 陈尸示众，陈列 <span class="encs">[put a corpse in display]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尸，陈也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有母之尸饔。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·祈父》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尸之为言失也，陈也，失气亡陈，形体独陈。——<span class="smcs">《白虎通·崩薨》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">楚武王</span>刑尸。——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公四年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫却昭子…其身尸于朝，其宗灭于绛。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:尸谏(陈尸以谏)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 立神像或神主 <span class="encs">[erect a spirit tablet]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子胡不相与尸而祝之，社而稷之乎?——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 担任;承担 <span class="encs">[bear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">康王</span>既尸天子，遂诰诸侯，作<span class="smcs">《康王之诰》</span>。——<span class="smcs">《书·康王之诰序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 执掌，主持 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">谁其尸之?有齐季女。——<span class="smcs">《诗·召南·采蘋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:尸盟(主持盟会);尸祭(主祭)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 在其位而无所作为 <span class="encs">[be in a state of inertia]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫子立而天下治，而我犹尸之。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span>
