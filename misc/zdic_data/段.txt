						<p>● <strong>段</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>事物、时间的一节：阶～。地～。片～。～落。</li>
	<li>工矿企业中的行政单位：工～。机务～。</li>
	<li>围棋棋手等级的名称：～位。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/缎">缎</a>&rdquo;，绸缎。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/锻">锻</a>&rdquo;，锻炼。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">段</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>段</strong> <span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从殳( <span class="dicpy">shū</span>)，耑( <span class="dicpy">duān</span>)声。&ldquo;殳&rdquo;，是古代的一种兵器，这里用作意符，表示与手持器械的动作有关。金文字形，会意，字形象手持物在山崖中敲棰石之形。本义:锤击)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[forge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">段，椎物也&hellip;其藉以椎物之石曰碫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:段氏(锻工。古代金工六种之一。主要从事农具制作)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 分段;截断 <span class="encs">[cut off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">段，分段也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后人&hellip;以段为分段字，读徒乱切。分段字自应作断，盖古今字之不同如此。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古时在石上用棒打干肉(并施加姜、桂皮等) <span class="encs">[beat with a stick]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:段脩(经捶捣并加姜桂的干肉)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>段</strong> <span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 某些部门的基层机构 <span class="encs">[section]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:工段;机务段，排字工段;公路段，总段</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 围棋棋手等级的名称 <span class="encs">[grade]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:他是九段棋手</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 部分 <span class="encs">[part]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:地段;阶段</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;缎&rdquo; <span class="encs">[satin]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:段子(即&ldquo;缎子&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 碫。砺石 <span class="encs">[anvil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美人赠我锦绣段。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《四愁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我有一匹好素绢，重之不减锦绣段。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《戏为韦偃双松图歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:段工(周代冶炼金属的工匠有六，其一为段氏，从事铸造田器钱镈等物)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 身段;体段。指妇女的身段或体态 <span class="encs">[figure]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然大段未改，所以认得。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p>
<p>◎ <strong>段</strong> <span class="dicpy">du&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/du&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>布帛或条形物的一截 <span class="encs">[section]</span>。</p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下" target="_blank">辰集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集下&amp;bs=殳" target="_blank">殳</a>】
                              段·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6BB5.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20A7B.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠩻</span>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】徒玩切【正韻】杜玩切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音緞。【說文】椎物也。一曰分段也。帛二曰緉，分而未丽曰匹，旣丽曰段。【張衡詩】美人贈我錦繡段。</p>
<p>又款段，馬名。【後漢&middot;馬援傳】御款段。【註】款，猶緩也，言形段緩也。</p>
<p>又段谷，水名。【通典】秦州上邽縣有段谷水，姜維爲鄧艾破於此。【水經注】藉水又東合段溪水，出西南馬門溪，東北流。</p>
<p>又阿段，蠻獠之稱。【北史&middot;蠻獠傳】獠無名字，以長幼次第呼之，丈夫稱阿謩、阿段。</p>
<p>又姓，出武威。【風俗通】段干木之後，有出遼西者，本鮮<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>檀石槐之後。</p>
<p>又段干，複姓。</p>
<p>又卵不成也。與毈通。【管子&middot;五行篇】羽卵者不段。</p>
<p>又【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>都玩切。同鍛。鍊也。一曰小冶也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】段氏爲鏄器。</p>
<p>又通腶。【禮&middot;昏義】婦執笲、棗、栗、段、脩以見。段，丁亂反。　从殳，耑省聲。與叚別。叚乃假借之義，古馬切。俗通用，非是。</p>
