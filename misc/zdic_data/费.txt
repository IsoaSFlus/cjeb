                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>费</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">費</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&egrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>用钱财：花～。消～。</li>
	<li>用，消耗：～事。～时。～解。煞～苦心。</li>
	<li>需用的钱财：～用。学～。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">费</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>费</strong></p>
<p>費 <span class="dicpy">f&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，弗声。从&ldquo;贝&rdquo;表示与钱财有关。本义:花费 <span class="encs">[钱财]</span>)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cost;spend;expend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">费，散财用也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">费，耗也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">非爱其费也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;安死》</span>。注:&ldquo;财也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子惠而不费。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;尧曰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上不费粟，民不慢农。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;垦令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:费耗(费用);费钞(花费钱钞);费钞费贯(耗贯钱财);费出(花费，支出)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 耗损 <span class="encs">[consume;waste;lose]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">费神伤魂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;禁塞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">伤形费神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;所染》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">用咫尺之木，不费一朝之事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说左上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:费眼(耗损眼力);费讲(费唇舌;难以说解);费想(费心，劳神);费损(耗费，耗损)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 浪费 <span class="encs">[lavish;spend;squander]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国侈则用费，用费则民贫。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;八观》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3"> 如:费资(挥霍钱财);费设(在供设方面花费多);费捐(耗费)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;拂&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">f&uacute;</span>)。逆，违背 <span class="encs">[transgress;violate;infringe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君子之道费而隐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:费民(违背民意，违逆民心)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>费</strong></p>
<p>費 <span class="dicpy">f&egrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&egrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 钱财;费用 <span class="encs">[fee;dues;charge]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>无亡矢遗镞之费。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">求脱械居监外板屋费亦数十金。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">老爷断一千也可，五百也可，与</span><span class="diczx2">冯</span>家作烧埋之费。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:道路通行费;报名费;学费;手续费;诊费</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Fei town]</span>。在今山东省鱼台县西南</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;悖&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;i</span>)。谬误 <span class="encs">[mistake;error]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">战而死，而子愠，而犹欲粜，籴仇则愠也，岂不费哉?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;鲁问》</span></span></p>
