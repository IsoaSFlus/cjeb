class="dicpy">rén</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rén.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> class="dicpy">rèn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/rèn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《江赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是任是负。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·生民》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“任，犹抱也。”</span></span></p><p><span
