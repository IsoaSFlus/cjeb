						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>腕</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<p>　◎ 胳膊下端与手掌相连的部分：手～。～骨。～力。扼～（表示惋惜等情绪）。</p>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>腕</strong> <span class="dicpy">w&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，宛声。本义:臂下端与手掌相连可以活动的部分)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wrist]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:腕表(手表);腕脱(手腕脱位。形容书写忙迫紧张);腕钏(手镯);腕法(写字用腕的方法);腕头(犹言手上);腕阑(手镯类的饰物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 和臂相仿的任何某些突起物 <span class="encs">[brachium]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 海百合的放射线</p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 头足类的触手</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 似触手的突起物</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 某些水母脑内的白色带状物</p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 构成鳃足类触手冠的任意卷曲的肌肉附肢</p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> <span class="encs">[量词]</span>∶串。用于连贯起来可以绕在手腕上的东西 <span class="encs">[string]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妾身有随身的翠珠囊一枚，更有二十五轮香串一腕，与秀才权为信物。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">武汉臣</span><span class="smcs">《玉壶春》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 手段;伎俩 <span class="encs">[means]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何功使愿果，尽力输老腕。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">张雨</span>诗</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>腕</strong> <span class="dicpy">w&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>握 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腕，握也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p>
