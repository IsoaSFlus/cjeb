<p><span class="dicpy">xuān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄢˉ</span></p>

<ol>
	<li>大声说话，声音杂乱：～哗。～闹。～嚷。～腾。～嚣。～宾夺主。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> lively, noisy; clamor, talk loudly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schreien, toben</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>喧</strong> <span class="dicpy">xuān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xuān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，宣声。&ldquo;宣&rdquo;有&ldquo;宣扬&rdquo;义，也有表义作用。本义:声音大而嘈杂)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[noisy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">喧，大语也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">结庐在人境，而无车马喧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《饮酒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上林催猎响，河桥争渡喧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">北周</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">庚信</span><span class="smcs">《同州还》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夜风一何喧，大舶双橹。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《金山寺》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蛙蛤徒自喧，蛟龙卧如蛰。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《喜雨》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:喧议(喧闹议论);喧埃(尘世的喧嚷);喧吓(喧哗恐吓);喧卑(喧闹低下);喧哄(喧闹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 显赫 <span class="encs">[illustrious]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赫兮喧兮者，威仪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p>
