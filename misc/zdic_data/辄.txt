                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辄</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>辄</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">輒</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhé  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄜˊ</span></p><ol><li>总是，就：动～得咎。浅尝～止。</li><li>古代车箱两旁的板上向外翻出的部分，像耳下垂那样。</li><li>仗恃胡作非为：“甘受专～之罪”。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">辄</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>辄</strong></p><p>輒、輙 <span class="dicpy">zhé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:车箱左右板上端向外翻出的部分，亦称<span class="yhcs">“车耳”</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[sides of a chariot，etc.，that point downward at the front]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>辄</strong></p><p>輒 <span class="dicpy">zhé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 专擅，独断专行，专权 <span class="encs">[act personally in all affairs; decide and act alone]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辄，专辄也。——<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">辄，遇事既然也。皆倚恃妄作之意。——<span class="smcs">《韵会》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">臣辄敕主者及黄门令罢</span><span class="diczx2">爽羲</span>训吏兵，以就第。——<span class="smcs">《三国志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">甘受专辄之罪。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·刘弘传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:辄代(擅自替代);辄行(辄自行使);辄尔(任意);辄悔(擅自反悔;随便反悔)</p><p>◎ <strong>辄</strong></p><p>輒 <span class="dicpy">zhé</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhé.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 立即;就 <span class="encs">[immediately]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饮少辄醉。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故有所览辄省记。(因为迫切的要读书，又得不到书，所以有看过的就记在心里。所览，所看过的书。辄，副词，立即，就。省记，用心记住。省，深思。)——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《黄生借书说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:辄议蠲除(马上商议取消或废除);辄造(就来造访;冒然造访);辄斩(就杀;立即斩杀)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 总是，每次 <span class="encs">[always]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每冒风驰行，未百步辄返。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:动辄得咎</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 则 <span class="encs">[then]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">地方百里之增减，辄为粟百八十万石。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/8F12.svg">                          <p>車兩輢也。从車<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2652E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦔮</span>聲。陟葉切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <span class="jieshi">車㒳輢也。</span>㒳各本作兩。今正。車㒳輢謂之輒。按車必有㒳輢。如人必有㒳耳。故从耴。耴、耳垂也。此篆在輢篆之先。故輢篆下但云車旁、而不言㒳。凡許全書之例。皆以難曉之篆先於易知之篆。如輯下云車輿也。而後出輿篆。輒下云車㒳輢也。而後出輢篆。是也。廣韵作車相倚也。乃字之誤。不可從。凡專輒用此字者、此引申之義。凡人有所倚恃而妄爲之。如人在輿之倚於輢也。<span class="jieshi">从車。耴聲。</span>陟葉切。八部。                          </div>
