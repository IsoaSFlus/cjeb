                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>黎</strong> <span class="dicpy">lí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从黍，利省声。本义:黍胶。以黍米制成。古代用以粘履)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“齐”</span>。众多，数目很多 <span class="encs">[numerous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民靡有黎，具祸以烬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·桑柔》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黎，众也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黎民于变时雍。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“众也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以能保我子孙黎民。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·大学》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“众也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">觉悟黎烝。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马相如传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“黎烝，众庶也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黎民不饥不寒。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span>。又如:黎庶(民众;百姓);黎氓(黎民，人民);黎甿(黎民，人民);黎首(黎民·黔首)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 颜色黑中带黄 <span class="encs">[with a dark complexion;weather beaten]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">土青曰黎。似黎草色也。则谓借为藜。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今王播弃黎老。——<span class="smcs">《国语·吴语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“东棃寿征也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥土青黎。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">面目黎黑。——</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 老，年纪大。通<span class="yhcs">“梨”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“耆”</span> <span class="encs">[old]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">黎，老也。——<span
