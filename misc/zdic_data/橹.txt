                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>橹</strong></p><p>櫓、艪、艣 <span class="dicpy">lǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，鲁声。本义:大盾牌)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[big shield]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">橹，大盾也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蒙之以甲以为橹。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">泰山</span>为橹。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马相如传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">礼义以为干橹。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·儒行》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">流血漂橹。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:橹楯(大盾);橹子(方言。手枪)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 顶部没有覆盖的远望楼 <span class="encs">[watch tower]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">修橹。——<span class="smcs">《孙子·谋攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:楼橹(古时军中用以侦察、防御或攻城的高台);橹巢(上古人类的居处。有柴谓橹，在树曰巢);橹棹(橹和棹都是划船的工具)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 比桨长大的划船工具，安在船尾或船旁 <span class="encs">[yuloh]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在旁曰橹。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:橹人(船工);橹声(摇橹声)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">櫓</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中" target="_blank">辰集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=辰集中&amp;bs=木" target="_blank">木</a>】
                              櫓·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=19" target="_blank">19</a>　·部外筆画：15</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/6AD3.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【正韻】郞古切【集韻】【韻會】籠五切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音魯。【說文】大盾也。【禮·儒行】禮義以爲干櫓。</p><p>又【玉篇】櫓，城上守禦望樓。【釋名】櫓，露也，露上無覆屋也。【後漢·公孫瓚傳】樓櫓千里。一作㯭。</p><p>又【韻會】戰陳高巢車亦爲櫓。【太公·六韜篇】陷堅陣，敗强敵，武翼大櫓，提翼小櫓。</p><p>又進船具。【釋名】船尾曰柂，在旁曰櫓。櫓，膂也，用膂力然後舟行也。【通鑑】呂蒙取荆州，使白衣搖櫓。</p><p>又櫓<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26296.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦊖</span>子，果名。【桂海虞衡志】大如椀，數十房欑聚成毬，食之微甘。</p><p>
