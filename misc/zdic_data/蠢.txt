						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>蠢</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chǔn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǔn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨㄣˇ</span></p><ol><li>愚笨，笨拙：～人。～材。～话。～事。～然。～拙。～笨。</li><li>虫子蠕动：～动。～～。～～欲动（指坏人伺机欲动或敌人准备进犯）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wriggle; stupid; silly; fat</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> dumm</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> stupide,imbécile</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蠢</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>蠢</strong></p><p>惷 <span class="dicpy">chǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。本义:昆虫慢慢地爬行)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wriggle;squirm]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蠢，虫动也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蠢尔蛮荆。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采芑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今王室实蠢蠢焉。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">万物蠢生。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:蠢动;蠢蠢(蠕动的样子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为骚动，动乱 <span class="encs">[move]</span>。如:蠢尔(无知蠢动貌);蠢生(谓万物萌动而生);蠢迪(动扰;骚动)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>蠢</strong> <span class="dicpy">chǔn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chǔn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 愚蠢;愚昧 <span class="encs">[stupid]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">时人愚蠢，不知相绳责也。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span
