                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">斡</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>斡</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄛˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>杓柄；瓢把。《説文&bull;斗部》：&ldquo;斡，蠡柄也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>旋转；运转。《廣雅&bull;釋詁四》：&ldquo;斡，轉也。&rdquo;</li>
	<li>姓。《奇姓通》：&ldquo;斡道仲，靈武人，其先從夏主遷興州，世掌夏國史。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>斡</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">guǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/guǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄨㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>车毂孔外围金属包裹的圆管状部分。《説文&bull;斗部》：&ldquo;斡，揚雄杜林説，皆以為軺車輪斡。&rdquo;《集韻&bull;緩韻》：&ldquo;輨，《説文》：&lsquo;轂端沓也。&rsquo;或作斡。&rdquo;按：一说为小车轮。</li>
	<li>主管。《漢書&bull;食貨志下》：&ldquo;（桑弘羊）盡代（孔）僅斡天下鹽鐵。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">斡</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>斡</strong> <span class="dicpy">w&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从斗，倝（ <span class="dicpy">g&agrave;n</span>）声。本义:勺把)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[handle of a ladle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斡，蠡柄也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;判瓠为瓢以为勺，必执其柄而后可以挹物。执其柄则运旋在我，故谓之斡。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古小车轮 <span class="encs">[wheelet]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斡&hellip;</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="diczx2">杜林</span>说皆以为轺车轮斡也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。 <span class="diczx2">段玉裁</span>注:&ldquo;轺车者，小车也。小车之轮曰斡，亦取善转运之意。&rdquo;</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>斡</strong> <span class="dicpy">w&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 旋转 <span class="encs">[turn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斡，转也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释诂四》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斡弃周鼎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:斡旋天地(转变世界);斡流(流转);斡运(旋转运行);斡维(运转的枢纽);斡萦(旋转;运转);斡转(运转)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 挑，负 <span class="encs">[carry]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">也强如提关列窖，也强如斡担挑箩。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杨显之</span><span class="smcs">《酷寒亭》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 挖;掏取 <span class="encs">[dig]</span>。</p>

												<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四">卷十四</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十四&amp;swbs=斗">斗</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/65A1.svg">                          <p>蠡柄也。从斗倝聲。楊雄、杜林說：皆以爲軺車輪斡。烏括切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/65A1_2473.svg" /><span class="swbf">(斡)</span><span class="ywyy">蠡柄也。</span><span class="swdz">此蠡非蟲齧木中，乃本無其字依聲叚借之字。見瓢字下，又見<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27BF7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧯷</span>字下。方言則从瓜作瓥矣。楊雄曰：瓢也。郭云：瓠勺也。判瓠爲瓢以爲勺，必執其柄而後可以挹物。執其柄則運旋在我，故謂之斡。引申之，凡執柄樞轉運皆謂之斡。賈誼鵩鳥賦云斡流而遷，張華勵志詩云大儀斡運皆是也。或叚借筦字。楚詞云：筦維焉繫，天極焉加。或作幹字。程氏瑤田云：考工記旋蟲謂之幹，葢斡之譌也。</span><span class="ywyy">从斗，</span><span class="swdz">瓢亦枓之類，故从斗。</span><span class="ywyy">倝聲。</span><span class="swdz">烏括切。古音在十四部。匡謬正俗云：斡音筦，不音烏活反。引陸士衡愍思賦爲證。按其字倝聲，則顏說是也。然俗音轉爲烏括切，又作捾，作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23073.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣁳</span>，亦於六書音義無甚害也。</span><span class="ywyy">楊雄、杜林說皆㠯爲軺車輪斡也。</span><span class="swdz">漢志，楊雄倉頡訓纂一篇，杜林倉頡訓纂、倉頡故各一篇。軺車者，小車也。小車之輪曰斡，亦取善轉運之意，亦本義之引申也。</span></p>                          </div>
