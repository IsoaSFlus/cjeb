						<p>● <strong>裨</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>增添，补助：大有～益。～补。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>裨</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">p&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古代的次等礼服。</li>
	<li>副，偏，小：～将。偏～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">裨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>裨</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 弥补;补助 <span class="encs">[make up; remedy]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裨，接益也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若以同裨同。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;郑语》</span>。注:&ldquo;益也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裨辅先君、裨诸侯之阙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;补也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">必能裨补阙漏，有所广益。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裨正(裨补匡正);裨益(补益;益处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/裨补">裨补</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/裨益">裨益</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>裨</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从衣，卑声。本义:古代祭祀大夫所穿的一种次等礼服) 同本义 <span class="encs">[second-class formal attire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裨，衣别也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侯氏裨冕。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;觐礼》</span>。注:&ldquo;天子六服，大裘为上，其余为裨，以事尊卑服之，而诸侯亦服焉。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裨冕鴵搢笏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;乐记》</span>。注:&ldquo;衣衮之属也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">裨冕以朝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;玉藻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裨衣(古代帝王所穿的次等礼服;又诸侯的礼服也叫裨衣);裨冕(着裨衣，戴冕。古代诸侯卿大夫朝觐或祭祀时所穿冕服的通称。与衮冕或上一等冕服相对而言)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>裨</strong> <span class="dicpy">p&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 副佐 <span class="encs">[vice;small]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">豫州</span>授以裨师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;为袁绍檄》</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/88E8.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】府移切【集韻】【韻會】賔彌切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤰞</span>。【說文】接益也。【徐曰】若衣之接益也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】與也，附也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】補也。【晉語】所以紀綱齊國，裨輔先君而成霸者也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】符支切【集韻】頻彌切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26709.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦜉</span>。【正韻】裨冕。【禮&middot;玉藻】諸侯<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>端以祭，裨冕以朝。【荀子&middot;富國篇】大夫裨冕。【楊倞註】天子六服，大裘爲上，其餘爲裨。裨之爲言<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤰞</span>也。</p>
<p>又裨襦。【博雅】作襦，謂之裨襦。</p>
<p>又輔也。【前漢&middot;項籍傳】梁爲會稽將，籍爲裨將。【註】裨，相輔助也。</p>
<p>又小也。【史記&middot;衞靑傳】得右賢裨王十餘人。【註】裨王，小王也。</p>
<p>又【孟子&middot;荀卿列傳】於是有裨海環之。【註】裨海，小海也。</p>
<p>又邑名。【左傳&middot;文十六年】裨鯈魚人實逐之。【註】裨，庸邑。</p>
<p>又姓。【通志&middot;氏族略】鄭大夫裨諶、裨竈。</p>
<p>又【姓苑】今宣州有裨氏。</p>
