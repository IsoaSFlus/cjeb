<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">瀨</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄞˋ</span></p>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>濑</strong></p>
<p>瀨 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 从沙石上流过的水 <span class="encs">[water flowing over shallows]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">濑，水流沙上也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">石濑兮浅浅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;九歌&middot;湘君》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北揭石濑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">溪谷之深，流者安洋;浅多沙石，激扬为濑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 流得很急的水;急流 <span class="encs">[rapids]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抑淢怒濑，以扬激波。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;本经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">混涛并濑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;左思&middot;吴都赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">石濑汤汤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《魏都赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">涖涖下濑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何必湘渊与涛濑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;扬雄传上》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7028.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】洛帶切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】落蓋切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音賴。【說文】水流沙上也。【楚辭&middot;九歌】石瀨兮淺淺。</p>
<p>又湍也。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】遣甲爲下瀨將軍。【註】瀨，湍也。吳越謂之瀨，中國謂之磧。</p>
<p>又水名。【水經注】瀨水，出蒼梧荔浦縣西北魯山之東。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶力制切，音利。【劉楨&middot;魯都賦】龜螭潛滑于黃泥，文魚游踊于淸瀨。浚迅波以遠騰，正泌瀄于湄㵝。</p>
