						<p>● <strong>妨</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">f&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄤˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>阻碍，伤害：～碍。～害。无～。不～。何～。</li>
	<li>迷信的人指某人或某物对人不利：～主。～家。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> interfere with, impede, obstruct</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schaden  (V)​,hindern, behindern, hemmen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> empêcher</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>妨</strong> <span class="dicpy">f&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从女，方声。本义:损害;有害于)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[damage;injure;harm]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">妨，害也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将妨于国家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;越语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">敬而无妨些。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">令人行妨。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span>。注:&ldquo;伤也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不以自妨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;解蔽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">贼父之子，妨兄之弟，与此同召。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论衡&middot;偶会》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妨要(妨害要务);妨紊(伤害扰乱)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 阻碍 <span class="encs">[hamper]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无发令而干时，以妨神农之事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;季夏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:妨贤(阻碍贤者登进)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 妨碍 <span class="encs">[hinder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不以小功妨大务。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;难二》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:不妨;何妨;妨蔽(妨碍)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 中止 <span class="encs">[stop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丁亥，敕翰林直学士</span><span class="diczx2">陈大任</span>妨本职专修<span class="smcs">《辽史》</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《金史》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 迷信者以为因命相、时辰、方位等凶像而人造成的灾厄，即所谓犯尅<span class="encs">[restrict]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自从你昨日下了书呈，将俺员外急心疼一夜妨杀了。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">马致远</span><span class="smcs">《荐福碑》</span></p>
