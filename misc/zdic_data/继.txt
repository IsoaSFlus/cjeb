                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">继</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>继</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">繼</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>连续，接着：～续。～任。～承。～武（足迹前后相接，喻后人接续前人的事业）。～往开来。前仆后～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> continue, maintain, carry on</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anhalten, andauern ,folglich, mithin ,fortsetzen ,gelingen, Erfolg haben ,beerben, erben  (V)​,Ji  (Eig, Fam)​,dann, danach</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> continuer,poursuivre,après,puis</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">继</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>继</strong></p>
<p>繼 <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义(把断了的丝接续上) <span class="encs">[continue;follow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">继，续也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大人以继明照于四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;象传》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓不绝也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">折琼枝以继佩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">齐</span>人未尝赂 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>，终继五国迁灭。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先后相继。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄&middot;种植部》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">学即继以问。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘开</span><span class="smcs">《问说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:继继(前后相继，接续不断);继踪(继续前人所做的事);继踵(步履前后相接。指相传不绝);继志(继续前辈的志意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 继承 <span class="encs">[succeed;inherit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今夫封建者，继世而理。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《封建论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">继吾志事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">九族无可继。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:继世(儿继承父的地位);继轨(承继祖业);继体(继承王位);继绪(继承祖业;传祚无穷)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 接济 <span class="encs">[give material or financial help to]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:继褰(接济缩减)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通过正式合法手续，将别人孩子自愿地代替或作为自己的孩子 <span class="encs">[adopt]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:继子</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 随后;跟着 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宾继主君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司仪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">瑜</span>等率轻锐继其后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:继而</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;系&rdquo;。挂，拴缚;系念 <span class="encs">[tie up;miss]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言&ldquo;以&rdquo;者，见行遂意也。见继重在遂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;宣公元年》</span></span><span class="diczx2">何休</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:继念(系念);继绊(系绊，束缚牵制);继缠(系缠，纠缠)</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>继</strong></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7E7C.svg">                          <p>續也。从糸㡭。一曰反<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇍</span>爲繼。古詣切</p>                          </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7E7C_1.svg" />
<span class="swbf">(繼)</span>
<span class="ywyy">續也。</span>
<span class="swdz">虞翻注易曰：繼，統也。</span>
<span class="ywyy">从糸<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇍</span>。</span>
<span class="swdz">各本篆文作繼，解作从糸㡭，則不可通。今正。此會意字。从糸<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇍</span>者，謂以糸聯其絕也。自傳寫譌亂，倂篆體改之，因又刪㡭篆矣。古詣切。十五部。</span></p>
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7E7C_2.svg" />
<span class="swbf">(㡭)</span>
<span class="ywyy"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/261D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦇓</span>或作㡭，反<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇍</span>爲㡭。</span>
<span class="swdz">大徐無篆文，但有一曰反<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇍</span>爲㡭六字。不可了。小徐本云：或作㡭，反<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇍</span>爲㡭。今依以補一篆文，乃使文從字順矣。反之而成字者，如反巳爲㠯，反人爲匕，反正爲乏是也。小徐本見韵會。莊、列皆云得水爲㡭。此篆見古書者惟此，而莊譌作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/221CD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢇍</span>。</span></p>                          </div>
