                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>纠</strong></p><p>糾、糺 <span class="dicpy">jiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">mì</span>)，表示与线丝有关，丩( <span class="dicpy">jiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:三股的绳子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hinged cord]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纠，绳三合也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，俗字作糺。单股曰纫，两股曰纠，三股曰纠，亦曰徽。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">徽以纠墨。——</span><span class="diczx2">扬雄</span><span class="smcs">《解嘲》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:纠纆(绳索。纠，二合绳;纆，三合绳)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>纠</strong></p><p>糾 <span class="dicpy">jiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 缠绕 <span class="encs">[wind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其笠伊纠。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·良耜》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何异纠纆。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·贾谊传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“绞也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">青纶竞纠。——</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《江赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纠思心以为纕兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·悲风歌》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“戾也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">横厉纠纷。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·刘琨传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“乱貌。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">福与祸如纠缠。——<span class="smcs">《鶡冠子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:纠蓼(互相牵引);纠结(连结);纠纠(缠绕的样子);纠曲(纠结盘曲)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 纠集;集结 <span class="encs">[gather;collect]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纠合宗族于</span><span class="diczx2">成周</span>。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公二十四年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">收离糺散。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·荀彧传》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“合也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纠合俊杰披奸猖。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《开元行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:纠办(收集，备办);纠合(联络，联合);纠勒(聚集统率);纠会(会合);纠约(纠合约集)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 矫正 <span class="encs">[rectify]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以纠邦国。——<span
