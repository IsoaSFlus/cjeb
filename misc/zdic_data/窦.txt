                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>窦</strong></p><p>竇 <span class="dicpy">dòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从穴，卖声。本义:孔穴)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[hole]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窦，空也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">穿窦窖。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·月令》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“入地隋曰窦。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顺人情之大窦也。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·礼运》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“孔穴也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在刑部狱，见死而由窦出者日三四人。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 如:窦径(洞穴和小路)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 门旁小户 <span class="encs">[side door]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">筚门闺窦之人而皆陵其上。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">杜预</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“闺窦，小户;穿壁为户，上锐下方，状如圭也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指简陋门户 <span class="encs">[door]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荣族子敝，小随母养于宗中，及十二，自窦而走，至大街。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">冯梦龙</span><span class="smcs">《智囊补》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 古代椭圆形地窖 <span class="encs">[cellar]</span>。如:窦窖(积藏谷物的地窖)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 水沟，阴沟 <span class="encs">[sewer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宫中之窦，其崇三尺。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王闻变，易敝衣，匿水窦中，久而得之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">周密</span><span class="smcs">《齐东野语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 水道口 <span class="encs">[mouth of a sewer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不见四窦者，四窦，五岳之匹。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·春官·大宗伯》</span></span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注</p><p><span class="diczx1">窦，水沟口也。——</span><span class="diczx2">五代</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐锴</span><span class="smcs">《说文解字系传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">泽居苦水者，买庸而决窦。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 人体或某些器官或组织的内部凹入部分 <span class="encs">[sinus]</span>。如:鼻窦;胃窦;额窦</p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 古州名 <span class="encs">[Dou prefecture]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">窦州</span>， <span 						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下" target="_blank">午集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集下&amp;bs=穴" target="_blank">穴</a>】

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7AC7.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】田<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】大透切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音豆。【說文】空也。【禮·月令】穿竇窖。【註】入地隋曰竇。</p><p>又鑿垣爲孔曰竇。【左傳·襄十年】王叔之宰曰：蓽門圭竇之人。【註】竇，穿壁爲小戸。</p><p>又穴也。【禮·禮運】禮義者，順人情之大竇。【註】竇，孔穴。</p><p>又水道也。【左傳·襄二十六年】齊烏餘襲我，高魚有大雨，自其竇入。【註】雨故水竇開。</p><p>又決也。【周語】不竇澤。【註】竇，決也。</p><p>又地名。【左傳·莊九年】殺子糾于生竇。【註】生竇，魯地。【北史·魏本紀】泰常七年，自雲中西幸屋竇城。【水經注】漢武微行柏谷，遇辱竇門。</p><p>又感其妻深識之，饋賜以河津令。其鬻渡，今竇津是也。</p><p>又姓。【風俗通】夏后相遭有窮氏之難，后緡方姙，逃出自竇而生少康，其後氏焉。漢有魏其侯竇嬰。</p><p>又通作窬。【禮·儒行】蓽門圭窬。【釋文】音竇。</p><p>又【集韻】徒谷切【正韻】杜谷切。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>與瀆通。【周禮註】西竇五嶽。</p>
