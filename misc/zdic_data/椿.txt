                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">椿</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>椿</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">chūn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chūn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄔㄨㄣˉ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 〔香～〕落叶乔木，嫩枝叶有香味，可食。简称“椿”，如“～芽”，“～龄”（祝人长寿之辞），“～庭”（古称父亲），“～萱”（父母的代称）。</p></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> father; a plant with white flowers</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Cedrela [Meliaceae]</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> cédrèle ou acajou de Chine,cédrèle ou acajou de Chine,arbre du ciel</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">椿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>椿</strong> <span class="dicpy">chūn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chūn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，春声。本义:木名。指大椿。古代传说大椿长寿，后因以喻父。如:椿年)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a long-living tree]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">上古有大椿者。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“椿木一名橓。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 因其长寿，后用来形容高龄。亦用以指父亲。如:椿年(椿树的年龄。比喻长寿);椿龄(祝人寿考之词);椿寿(大椿的寿命。比喻长寿，高龄)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 即香椿 <span class="encs">[Chinese toon]</span>，一种楝科落叶乔木。叶有特殊气味，花芳香，嫩芽可作菜食，木材通直，是造船、建筑材料，种子椭圆形，一端有膜质长翅，可榨油，根、皮及果可入药。如:椿芽(香椿的嫩芽，甘美可食);椿皮(香椿的树皮)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/椿庭">椿庭</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/椿萱">椿萱</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
