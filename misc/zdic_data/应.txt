                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>应</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">應</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yīng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˉ</span></p><ol><li>该，当，又引申料想理该如此：～当。～该。～分（<span class="dicpy">fèn </span>）。～有尽有。</li><li>回答：答～。喊他不～。～承。</li><li>随，即：“桓督诸将周旋赴讨，～皆平定”。</li><li>姓。</li></ol><p><strong>其它字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>应</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">應</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yìng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yìng.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>回答或随声相和：～答。呼～。～对（答对）。～和（<span class="dicpy">hè</span>）。反～（ａ．化学上指物质发生化学变化，产生性质和成分与原来不同的新物质；ｂ．人和动物受到刺激而发生的活动和变化；ｃ．回响，反响）。</li><li>接受，允许，答应要求：～邀。～聘。～考。</li><li>顺合，适合：顺～。适～。～机。～景。～时。～用文。</li><li>对待：～付。～变。～酬。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">应</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>应</strong></p><p>應 <span class="dicpy">yīng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yīng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 应当，应该 <span class="encs">[should]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">应，当也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">应，当也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">文王</span>既勤止，我应受之。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周颂·赉》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故国神游，多情应笑我。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">苏轼</span><span class="smcs">《念奴娇·赤壁怀古》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">应是良晨好景虚设。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳永</span><span class="smcs">《雨霖铃》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡所应有。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">李渔</span><span class="smcs">《闲情偶寄·种植部》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">应得之资。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不应重罚。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">法应立决。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:应然(应该如此);应伯爵(白吃白喝。<span class="yhcs">“应白嚼”</span>之谐音);应合(应当;该当);应是(应当是)</p><p><span
