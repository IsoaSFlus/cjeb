                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>熬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">āo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/āo.mp3"></a></span>  ㄠˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>烹调方法，把蔬菜等放在水里煮：～白菜。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>熬</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span>  ㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>久煮：～粥。～药。</li>
	<li>忍受，耐苦支持：煎～。～夜。～炼。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/嗷">嗷</a>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cook down, to boil; endure</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aushalten (schweres,hartes Leben oder Leid)​; kochen, schmoren, dünsten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> faire bouillir,mijoter,souffrir,faire bouillir,endurer</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>熬</strong> <span class="dicpy">āo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/āo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (词源见 <span class="dicpy">&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> 声) 把蔬菜等加水并放在文火上煮 <span class="encs">[stew]</span>。如:熬白菜;熬豆腐</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>熬</strong> <span class="dicpy">&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，敖声。本义:煎干;炒干)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[extract sth.by heating]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熬，干煎也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熬，火干也。以火而干五谷之类。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言七》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">淳熬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;内则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">设熬。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;小祝》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何物中长食?胡麻慢火熬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王建</span><span class="smcs">《隐者居》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:熬谷(干炒过的谷物);熬稃(爆米花)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用小火慢煮 <span class="encs">[stew;boil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太宗遣使取熬糖法。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《新唐书&middot;摩揭陀传》</span></span></p>
