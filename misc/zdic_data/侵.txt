                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>侵</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄧㄣˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>（敌人）进入境内，（外来的或有害的事物）进入内部：～害。～吞。～略。～染。～蚀。</li>
	<li>渐近：～晨。～晓。～早（破晓，天刚亮）。</li>
	<li>古代称荒年：五谷不生，谓之大～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">侵</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>侵</strong> <span class="dicpy">qīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qīn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形从人，手(又)拿扫帚，一步一步地扫地。本义:渐进)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[advance gradually;progress step by step]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侵，渐进也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侵淫谿谷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋玉</span><span class="smcs">《风赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侵淫促节。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《上林赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;渐进之貌。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:侵润(逐渐发展);侵寻(渐进);侵淫(渐进的样子);侵寻(范围逐渐扩大)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 侵犯:进攻 <span class="encs">[encroach on;infringe upon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡师有钟鼓曰伐，无曰侵，轻曰袭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公二十九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">袭侵之事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span>。注:&ldquo;无钟鼓曰侵。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">觕者曰侵，精者曰伐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苞人民、殴牛马曰侵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;隐公五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">项子牛</span>三侵 <span class="diczx2">鲁</span>地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;鲁问》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">韩</span><span class="diczx2">魏</span>相与争侵地。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;让王》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侵之愈急。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《权书&middot;六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自海入侵。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:入侵(敌军侵入国境);侵官暴国(侵犯他人职守，欺侮当今国君);侵傍(侵谤。侵犯);侵陵(侵犯欺凌);侵克(侵占克扣);侵匿(侵占吞没);侵田(侵占的田地);侵斥(侵占);侵用(非法占用公物或他人之物);侵并(侵占兼并);侵耗(侵吞克扣);侵匿(侵吞，侵没);侵抄(侵害掠夺);侵苦(侵害受苦);侵冒(侵害，侵犯)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 侵蚀 <span class="encs">[corrode;erode]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">加以风雨稍侵，渐致亏坠。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">李百药</span><span class="smcs">《北齐书》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 侵凌 <span class="encs">[invade and bully]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">侵，凌也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">六伐既侵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大传三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无相侵渎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;楚语下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">义不侵为然诺者也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;除馀传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 如:侵轧(侵凌排挤，倾轧);侵虐(侵凌残害);侵冤(侵凌，使之受冤枉);侵慢(欺凌轻慢)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 临近，到 <span class="encs">[approach]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隔夜相期侵早发。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">方千</span><span class="smcs">《采莲》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:侵晚(入夜);侵明(天刚亮);侵晓(拂晓);侵早(凌晨)</span></p>
