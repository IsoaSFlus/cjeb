                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">怜</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>怜</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">憐</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>可怜；同情：～悯。～恤。可～。同病相～。</li>	<li>爱：～才（爱惜人才）。～念。～爱。爱～。～香惜玉（因香、玉可供玩赏，使人起怜爱之心，特指对女子的爱惜）。顾影自～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pity, sympathize</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> bemitleiden</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> avoir pitié de,compatir à,chérir,aimer tendrement</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">怜</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>怜</strong></p>
<p>憐 <span class="dicpy">li&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/li&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，粦声。本义:哀怜，怜悯)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">憐，哀也。从心。粦声。字亦作怜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">矜憐抚掩之也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">独不怜公子姊邪?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若俯首贴耳，摇尾而乞怜者，非我之志也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《应科目时与人书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">且喜且怜之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;淮阴侯列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子怜百姓新劳苦，故且休之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;郦生陆贾列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所怜者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:可怜(值得怜悯);怜见(哀矜，怜悯);怜拯(怜悯拯救);怜鉴(哀怜审察);怜闵(哀怜，同情)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 爱，惜 <span class="encs">[love]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">憐，爱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>人怜之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丈夫亦爱怜其少子乎?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:爱怜(十分疼爱);怜才(爱惜人才);怜忪(爱怜其惺忪之态);怜香(喜爱花香);怜遇(宠爱恩遇)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;吝&rdquo;。吝惜 <span class="encs">[stint]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不慕往，不闵来，无邑怜之心。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怜下(怜惜下人);怜嗟(怜惜嗟叹);怜察(察知其情而怜惜之)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 遗憾 <span class="encs">[deplore; regret;sorrow over]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所怜者，吾自戊寅年读汝哭侄诗后，&hellip;至今无男。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p>
