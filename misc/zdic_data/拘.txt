                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">拘</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>拘</strong> <span class="dicpy">jū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从手，从句，句亦声。本义:拘留，拘禁)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[arrest;detain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拘，止也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">艮为拘随物系之。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span>传</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拘系之乃从。——<span class="smcs">《易·随》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">盖</span><span class="diczx2">文王</span>拘而演。——<span class="smcs">《周易》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">狎</span><span class="diczx2">徐</span>君，拘 <span class="diczx2">齐</span><span class="diczx2">庆封</span>。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:拘证(缉拿审问);拘刷(通缉;拘捕);拘究(拘留审察);拘制(拘禁，系缚); 拘责(拘留责罚);拘闭(拘禁，扣押)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 束缚;限制 <span class="encs">[restrain;constrain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使人拘而多畏。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马迁传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其流也，埤下裾拘，必循其理。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·宥坐》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">井蛙不可以语于海者，拘于虚也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·秋水》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拘，犹限也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·王霸传》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而不肖者拘焉。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·泛论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹检也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:拘紧(约束、束缚得紧);拘管(管教约束);拘逼(束缚，强迫);拘定(限定);拘卷(拘倦。约束，不自在);拘虚(受限于居所)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 拘守;拘泥于 <span class="encs">[stick to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不拘于时。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《师说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拘以定月。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span>
