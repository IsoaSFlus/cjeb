                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>痛</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tòng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tòng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄨㄥˋ</span></p><ol><li>疾病、创伤等引起的难受的感觉：头～。肚子～。～风。～痒（ａ．喻疾苦，如“～～相关”；ｂ．喻紧要的事，如“不关～～”）。</li><li>悲伤：悲～。哀～。～楚。～惜。～不欲生。</li><li>尽情地，深切地，彻底地：～击。～悼。～责。～快。～改前非。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pain, ache; sorry, sad; bitter</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schmerz, Leiden, Weh  (S)​,schmerzen, wehtun  (V)​,äußest, sehr, höchst  (Adj)​,Tong  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fort,excessivement,avoir mal,douleur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">痛</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>痛</strong> <span class="dicpy">tòng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tòng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒，甬声。疒( <span class="dicpy">chuáng</span>)，与疾病有关。本义:疼痛)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ache;pain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">痛，病也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坎为耳痛。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">桓侯</span>体痛。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身体痛。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">王充</span><span class="smcs">《论衡·订鬼篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">病痛恐惧。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">身自疾痛。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:痛不欲生;痛定思痛;创巨痛深;累得酸痛;痛毒(痛苦，残酷);痛疾(病痛);痛脚(疼痛的脚);痛煞(疼痛之甚);痛痹(病名。中医指以疼痛剧烈为主症的痹症);痛痒(疼痛，发痒)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 痛恨 <span class="encs">[hate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自痛负</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·李广苏建传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">叹息痛恨。——</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:痛入骨髓(形容痛恨到极点);深恶痛绝(极端痛恨、厌恶);痛绝(痛恨到极点)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 痛惜;怜惜;叹惜<span class="encs">[deeply regret;take pity on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">苦痛之。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·博志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哀痛未尽，思慕未忘。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·礼论》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如∶痛惋(谓极其怅恨而惋惜)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 损削;损伤<span class="encs">[damage]</span>。如:痛创(难受的创伤);痛毒(使痛苦;毒害);痛处(指隐痛所在)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 痛爱，怜爱<span class="encs">[love dearly;have tender affection for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">痛玉不痛身，抱璞求所归。——</span><span class="diczx2">孟郊</span><span class="smcs">《古兴》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:痛爱(怜爱;疼爱;关切喜爱);痛热(疼爱亲热)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 尽力;竭力<span class="encs">[do one’s utmost;try one’s best]</span>。如:痛贬(极力贬低);痛诋(竭力诋毁、辱骂);痛毁(竭力诋毁)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>痛</strong> <span class="dicpy">tòng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tòng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 痛苦，身体或精神感到非常难受<span class="encs">[painful;sad]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疾痛惨怛。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">痛定思痛。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">文天祥</span><span
