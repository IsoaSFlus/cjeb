<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˋ</span></p>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>怿</strong></p>
<p>懌 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>(形声。从心，睪( <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:喜悦) 同本义 <span class="encs">[rejoice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怿，悦也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怿，悦也，乐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">说怿女美。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;静女》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辞之怿矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;板》</span>。传:&ldquo;怿，悦也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是故其成也怿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;文王世子》</span>。注:&ldquo;怿，悦怿。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上不怿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;萧何传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;怿，悦也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是</span><span class="diczx2">秦王</span>不怿，为一击缶。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怿怿(欢乐的样子)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>怿</strong></p>
<p>懌 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 悦服 <span class="encs">[obey happily]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怿，服也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞注</span>:皆谓喜而服从</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:怿悦(使心情怡悦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 更改 <span class="encs">[alter]</span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/61CC.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】羊益切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】夷益切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音睪。【說文】悅也。从心睪聲。【書&middot;康誥】則予一人以懌。</p>
