                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枳</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>枳</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǐ.mp3"></a></span> ㄓˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>落叶灌木或小乔木，小枝多刺，果实黄绿色，味酸不可食，可入药（亦称&ldquo;枸橘&rdquo;）：～实（中药称&ldquo;枳&rdquo;、&ldquo;构橼&rdquo;等的近成熟的果实）。～壳（中药称&ldquo;枳&rdquo;、&ldquo;枸橼&rdquo;等的成熟的果实）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> trifoliate orange; hedge thorn; (Cant.) a plug</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Dreiblättrige Orange (Poncirus trifoliata)​  (S, Bio)​,dreiblättrige Zitrone (poncirus trifoliata)​  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> citronnier épineux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">枳</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>枳</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 枸橘，又称&ldquo;臭橘&rdquo; <span class="encs">[trifoliate orange]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">枳木似橘。从木，只声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">殷以椇。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;明堂位》</span>。注:&ldquo;椇之言枳椇也。谓曲桡之也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">楗六枳而为篱兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;冯衍传》</span>。注:&ldquo;枳，芬木也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">橘逾</span><span class="diczx2">淮</span>而北为枳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记&middot;序宫》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 一种耐寒的中国落叶橘树，有三小叶的叶片，果实小有芳香，很酸。广泛栽种作为观赏叶或作为绿篱，尤其作为砧木以芽接各种食用的柑桔</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古县名 <span class="encs">[Zhi county]</span>。战国时楚有枳邑，汉置枳县，属巴郡。故地在今四川省涪陵县</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/67B3.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【廣韻】【類篇】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>諸氏切，音紙。木名。枳也。【說文】木似橘。【徐曰】卽藥家枳殼也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】橘踰淮而北爲枳。</p>
<p>又木高多刺，可爲籬落。【張衡&middot;西京賦】楷枳落突棘藩。</p>
<p>又【博雅】枳，股也。</p>
<p>又【小爾雅】枳，害也。【孔叢子&middot;<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>論】率過以小罪謂之枳。</p>
<p>又與軹通。地名。一在巴郡，一在魏地。</p>
<p>又【集韻】頸爾切【韻會】居紙切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/23CB5.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣲵</span>。義同。</p>
<p>又【集韻】舉綺切【韻會】居矣切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音已。枳椇。</p>
<p>又一名白石李。</p>
<p>又【集韻】章移切，音支。【爾雅&middot;釋蟲】枳首蛇，歧蛇也。</p>
<p>又翹移切，音岐。義同。</p>
