<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，束声。本义:恐惧)</p>

<p><span class="diczx1">悚，惧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人君闻此，可以悚惧。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王符</span><span class="smcs">《潜夫论&middot;慎微》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 欢悦 <span class="encs">[please]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:悚抃(欢愉);悚戴(欢欣奉戴);悚跃(欢悦跳跃)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 耸立;高耸 <span class="encs">[tower]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:悚立(耸立，竖立);悚峙(高耸峙立)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 恭敬 <span class="encs">[respect]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:悚敬(恭敬)</span></p>
