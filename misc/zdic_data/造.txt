						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>造</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zào  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zào.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄠˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>制作，做：制～。创～。～物。～反。～孽。建～。～表。～册。～价。～型。粗制滥～。</li>    <li>瞎编：胡编乱～。捏～。</li>    <li>成就：～诣。</li>    <li>培养：～就。</li>    <li>相对两方面的人，法院里指诉讼的两方。即原告和被告：两～。甲～。乙～。</li>    <li>到，去：～访。</li>    <li>稻子等作物从播种到收割的次数：一年两～。晚～。</li>    <li>时代，年代：末～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">造</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>造</strong> <span class="dicpy">zào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从辵( <span class="dicpy">chuò</span>)，告声。本义:到;往某地去)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[arrive;go to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">造，就也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咸造勿亵在王庭。——<span class="smcs">《书·盘庚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡四方之宾 客造焉。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·司门》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">造于西阶下。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士丧礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而造大国之城下。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·宋策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">造饮辄尽。——</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《五柳先生传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">明日造朝。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河东集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">造</span><span class="diczx2">玉清宫</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">造</span><span class="diczx2">左公</span>第。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《左忠毅公逸事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">径造</span><span class="diczx2">袁</span>所寓之 <span class="diczx2">法华寺</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:造劫历世(佛教语。来到人世间经历一番苦难生活);造历幻缘(佛教语。去到人世间经历一番像梦幻一样的情缘);造适(寻访);造胜(访求胜迹);造门(上门;到别人家去)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 拜访 <span class="encs">[尊贵者]</span> <span class="encs">[call to pay respect;pay a visit]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">庾公</span>造 <span class="diczx2">周伯仁</span>。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·言语》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:夜造(深夜前往造访);造府拜瞻(敬辞。到府上去拜访);造请(前往问候、拜见);造谒(造请);造谢(登门致谢)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“作”</span>，制造;制作 <span class="encs">[make;build;create]</span></p><p><span
