又如:捎(抽打割勒。捎:拂掠);捎儿(手巾);捎星(拂到天上的星，形容极高);捎捩(拂掠转折)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 破除;砍杀;芟除 <span class="encs">[get rid of;kill]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拔剑捎罗网。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《野田黄雀行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 捎带;顺带 <span class="encs">[take along sth. to or for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孟姜女</span>时常到村口去看，希望过路的人给捎封信来。——<span class="smcs">《孟姜女》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:捎搭(装载在牲口背上);请把这张报捎给她;捎个口信</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 放置，安放 <span <strong>捎</strong> <span class="dicpy">shào</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shào.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span
