						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>片</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">pi&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>平而薄的物体：卡～。名～。</li>
	<li>切削成薄的形状：～肉片。</li>
	<li>少，零星：～段（整体当中的一段）。～刻。～面。～甲不存。</li>
	<li>指较大地区内划分的较小地区：分～儿开会。</li>
	<li>〔～假名〕日本文所用的楷书字母。</li>
	<li>量词，指面积、范围、景象、心意等或成片的东西：两～药。一～新气象。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>

<p>● <strong>片</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">piān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piān.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧㄢˉ</span></p>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>片</strong> <span class="dicpy">piān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/piān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 指有图像、景物或录有声音的片子 <span class="encs">[film]</span>(用于口语一部分词)。如:唱片儿;画片儿;相片儿;影片儿</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">pi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/片儿">片儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/片头">片头</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/片子">片子</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>片</strong> <span class="dicpy">pi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (指事。甲骨文字形，象劈开的木片。本义:劈开树木之类)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义<span class="encs">[cut open]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">片，判木也，从半木。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;谓一分为二之木片。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:片批(切肉的一种刀法。刀略倾斜，切之使肉成片状)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 用刀将物斜削成扁薄形状 <span class="encs">[pare]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:两只山鸡已经都片出来了，又片了些羊肉片子</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>片</strong> <span class="dicpy">pi&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pi&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 扁而薄的东西 <span class="encs">[a flat，thin piece;slice;flake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">雪片一冬深。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《寄杨五桂州谭因州参军段子之任》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:唱片;画片;相片;影片;片石(片状石料);木片;玉兰片;虾片;名片;瓦片;片楮(片纸)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 半;整体中的一小部分或较大地区内划出来的较小地区 <span class="encs">[half;part of a place]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 花瓣 <span class="encs">[petal]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:花片;牡丹片</span></p>
