<p><span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄌㄨˋ</span></p>

	<li>地名用字。江苏省苏州市吴中区有甪直镇。又浙江省有甪里堰。</li>
	<li>姓。又作&ldquo;<a href="/hans/甪里">甪里</a>&rdquo;，复姓。</li>
</ol>
