                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>隙</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">x&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>裂缝：～罅（裂缝）。缝～。～大而墙坏。</li>	<li>感情上的裂痕：～嫌。有～。仇～。</li>	<li>闲，空：～地（空地）。农～。</li>	<li>机会，空子：乘～。</li>	<li>际，接近：&ldquo;北～乌丸、夫馀&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> crack, split, fissure; grudge</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fente,fissure,occasion</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">隙</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>隙</strong> <span class="dicpy">x&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/x&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从阜( <span class="dicpy">f&ugrave;</span>)亦声。阜，土山，与土有关。本义:墙上开裂的裂缝)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。也泛指孔穴、空隙 <span class="encs">[crack;chink]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隙，壁际孔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隙，裂也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">墙之隙坏，谁之咎也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若驷之过隙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;三年问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">二间夹钟出四隙之细也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">柳</span>每以暇日隙壁窥 <span class="diczx2">韩</span>( <span class="diczx2">韩翃</span>)所居，即萧然葭艾，闻客至，必名人。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">孟棨</span><span class="smcs">《本事诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">谚曰:&ldquo;蠹众而木折，隙大而墙坏。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;修权》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:裂隙;罅隙;隙穴(空隙与洞穴);隙地(空地);隙积(带有空隙垛体的体积)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 空子 <span class="encs">[loophole;opportunity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辅隙则国必弱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;谋攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">狡冠窥窬，伺国瑕隙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;刘琨劝进表》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">连抵其隙。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">纪昀</span><span class="smcs">《阅微草堂笔记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:无隙可乘;乘隙突围;乘隙;无隙可寻</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 要道;孔道 <span class="encs">[thoroughfare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">文</span>、 <span class="diczx2">德</span>、 <span class="diczx2">缪</span>居 <span class="diczx2">雍</span>，隙 <span class="diczx2">陇</span>、 <span class="diczx2">蜀</span>之货物而多贾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
