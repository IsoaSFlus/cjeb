                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畴</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>畴</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">疇</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> ㄔㄡˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>田地：田～。平～。</li>
	<li>类，同类的：范～。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/俦">俦</a>&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>使相等：～其爵禄。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> farmland, arable land; category</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gebiet, Flur  (S, Agrar)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> terres arables,champs cultivés,sorte,catégorie</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">畴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>畴</strong></p>
<p>疇 <span class="dicpy">ch&oacute;u</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/ch&oacute;u.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从田，寿声。本义:已耕作的田地)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[farmland]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畴，耕地也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《苍颉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">畴，耕治之田也。象耕屈之形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">取我田畴而伍之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">田畴荒芜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语 》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修农圃之畴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;萧望之传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">农人告余以春及，将有事于西畴。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《归去来兮辞》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:田畴(田地);畴陇(畦田;田亩)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 特指种麻的田 <span class="encs">[hemp field]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:畴土(田地)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 同类，类别。后作&ldquo;俦&rdquo; <span class="encs">[kind;category]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十日不食，无畴类尽死矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;枢言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人与人相畴，家与家相畴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;齐语》</span>。注:&ldquo;畴，匹也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫物各有畴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">翼佐之功，超世无畴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;董昭传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不畀洪范九畴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草木畴生，禽兽群焉，物各从其类也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;劝学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:范畴;畴类(同类;同辈);畴偶(成对;两两相伴)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>畴</strong></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">疇</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“畴”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/疇">疇</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上" target="_blank">午集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=午集上&amp;bs=田" target="_blank">田</a>】
                              疇·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=19" target="_blank">19</a>　·部外筆画：14</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7587.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22454.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢑔</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20F89.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠾉</span>【唐韻】直由切【集韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>留切【正韻】徐留切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音酬。耕治之也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;月令】季夏之月，可以糞田疇。【疏】穀田曰田，麻田曰疇。【齊語】陸阜陵墐，井田疇均，則民不憾。【註】麻地曰疇。</p>
<p>又【左傳&middot;襄三十年】取我田疇而伍之。【註】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>畔爲疇。【史記&middot;秦始皇紀】男樂其疇，女修其業。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】疇，誰也。【書&middot;堯典】帝曰：疇咨若時登庸。【傳】疇，誰也。</p>
<p>又【韻會】曩也。【左傳&middot;宣二年】羊斟曰：疇昔之羊子爲政。【註】疇昔，猶前日也。【左思&middot;蜀都賦】嗟見偉於疇昔。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;歷書】故疇人子弟分散。【如淳註】家業世世相傳爲疇。</p>
<p>又類也。【書&middot;洪範】帝乃震怒，不畀洪範九疇。【傳】疇，類也。【戰國策】今髠賢者之疇也。【註】疇，類也。</p>
<p>又匹也。【易&middot;否卦】九四，有命无咎，疇離祉。【疏】疇謂疇匹。【嵆康&middot;贈秀才入軍詩】咬咬黃鳥，顧疇弄音。【註】疇，匹也。</p>
<p>又【五音集韻】雍也。【淮南子&middot;俶眞訓】今夫樹木者，灌以瀿水，疇以肥壤。【註】疇，雍也。</p>
<p>又等也。【前漢&middot;宣帝紀】疇其爵邑。【註】漢律，非始封十減二。疇者，等也，言不復減也。【後漢&middot;祭遵傳】疇，等也。言功臣子孫，襲封與先人等。</p>
<p>又澤名。【淮南子&middot;本經訓】堯乃使羿誅鑿齒於疇華之野。【註】疇華，南方澤名也。</p>
<p>又國名。【周語】摯疇之國也，由太任。【註】摯，疇，二國名。</p>
<p>又姓。摯疇之後。【左傳&middot;哀十三年】疇無餘，謳陽，自南方。【註】二子，越大夫。</p>
<p>又【韻補】叶<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>如切，音除。【韓愈&middot;王弘中銘】方乎所部，禁絕浮屠。風雨順易，秔稻盈疇。</p>
