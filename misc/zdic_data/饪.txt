<p>◎ <strong>饪</strong></p>
<p>飪、餁 <span class="dicpy">r&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从食，壬( <span class="dicpy">r&eacute;n</span>)声。本义:做饭做菜) 同本义 <span class="encs">[completely boil]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">饪，大熟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">餁，熟也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">烹饪也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;鼎》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">失饪不食。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:失饪(烹调失当);饪鼎(烹饪的鼎);饪熟(烹调)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/98EA.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26719.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦜙</span>【唐韻】如甚切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】忍甚切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音荏。【玉篇】大熟也。【揚子&middot;方言】徐揚之閒曰飪。【論語】失飪，不食。【釋常談】過熟謂之失飪。</p><p>又【儀禮&middot;聘禮】賜饔唯羹飪。【註】羹飪謂飪一牢也。肉謂之羹。</p><p>又【特牲饋食禮】請期曰羹飪。【註】飪，熟也。</p><p>又【集韻】如鴆切，音妊。義同。</p>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/98EA.svg">                          <p>大孰也。从食壬聲。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/98EA_1.svg" />䏕，古文飪。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/98EA_2.svg" />恁，亦古文飪。如甚切〖注〗<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/26719.svg"><span class="k_txt">𦜙</span>，亦古文飪。</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/98EA_7655.svg" /><span class="swbf">(飪)</span><span class="ywyy">大孰也。</span><span class="swdz">特牲禮請期曰羹飪注：飪，孰也。飪亦假稔爲之。釋言：饙餾稔也。字又作餁，餁同飪。</span><span class="ywyy">从<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2028A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠊊</span>壬聲。</span><span class="swdz">如甚切。七部。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/98EA_7656.svg" /><span class="swbf">(䏕)</span><span class="ywyy">古文飪。</span><span class="swdz">从肉。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/98EA_7657.svg" /><span class="swbf">(恁)</span><span class="ywyy">亦古文飪。</span><span class="swdz">心部恁下云：齎也。此古文系後人增羼。小徐說，李舟切韵不云亦古文飪。</span></p>                          </div>
