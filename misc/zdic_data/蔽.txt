						<p>● <strong>蔽</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>遮，挡：～野。遮～。掩～。</li>
	<li>隐藏：～匿。隐～。</li>
	<li>欺骗，隐瞒：蒙～。～美扬恶。</li>
	<li>概括：一言以～之。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蔽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蔽</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，敝声。本义:小草)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 遮住，遮掩 <span class="encs">[cover;shelter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔽，蔽蔽小草也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>曰:&ldquo;此字本训盖覆也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蔽，障也，隐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蒲蔽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;巾车》</span>。注:&ldquo;车旁御风尘者。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乘马其蔽五。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span>。注:&ldquo;所以捍车马。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">景以蔽日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;修务》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故能蔽不新成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旌蔽日兮敌若云。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">楚</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">屈原</span><span class="smcs">《九歌&middot;国殇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">项伯</span>亦拔剑起舞，常以身翼蔽 <span class="diczx2">沛公</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阴翳蔽日。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:隐蔽(借旁的事物来遮掩);屏蔽;蔽天(遮蔽天空，布满了整个天空)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 隐藏 <span class="encs">[hide]</span>蔽林间窥之。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">先生&hellip;引蔽驴后。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">马中锡</span><span class="smcs">《中山狼传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蔽贤(隐蔽贤者，不使上知);蔽形(遮掩身体)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 庇护 <span class="encs">[shelter;shield]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盖借塞墙为蔽也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞&middot;战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蔽护(掩护;庇护);蔽橹(古代防御用的大盾);蔽辜(抵罪)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 掩饰 <span class="encs">[cover up; conceal]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">廉不蔽恶，耻不从枉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;牧民》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蔽美(掩盖他人的美德、长处);蔽恶(掩饰过错和坏行为)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 蒙蔽 <span class="encs">[deceive]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">由此观之，王之蔽甚矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">屈平</span>疾王听之不聪也，谗谄之蔽明也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></p>
