                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>所</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">suǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span> ㄙㄨㄛˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>处，地方：住～。哨～。场～。处～。</li>    <li>机关或其他办事的地方的名称：研究～。派出～。</li>    <li>量词，指房屋：一～四合院。</li>    <li>用在动词前，代表接受动作的事物：～部（所率领的部队）。～谓（a.所说的；b.某些人所说的，含不承认意）。无～谓（不关紧要，不关心）。～有。各尽～能。～向无敌。有～不为而后可以有为。</li>    <li>用在动词前，与前面的“<span class="diczx3">为</span>”或“<span class="diczx3">被</span>”字相应，表示被动的意思：为人～敬。</li>    <li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">所</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>所</strong> <span class="dicpy">suǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从斤，户声。斤，斧子。( <span class="dicpy">hǔ</span>)本义:伐木声)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> <span class="yhcs">“所”</span>假借为<span class="yhcs">“处”</span>。处所;地方 <span class="encs">[place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有截其所。——<span class="smcs">《诗·商颂·殷武》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不可不为择所而后错之。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王霸》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">必于无人之所。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·达郁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">僻远悠闲之所。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·谨听》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不如早为之所。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">持童抵主人所，愈束缚牢甚。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">成反复自念，得无教我猎虫所耶?——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝生于浙而葬于斯，离吾乡七百余里矣，当时虽觭梦幻想，宁知此为归骨所耶?——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:哨所;寓所;诊所;交易所;看守所;托儿所;招待所; 收容所;诊疗所;观察所;指挥所;各得其所</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 道理;方法 <span class="encs">[reason]</span>。如:所以然(所以如此。指原因或道理);所诠(佛教语。所解的义理)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 地方 <span class="encs">[place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">虽众，无所用之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 进行科学研究的机构 <span class="encs">[institute]</span>。如:物理所;农民问题研究所。又指机关或其他机构的名称。如:派出所;招待所;指挥所</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 着落;结果 <span class="encs">[result]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">勤而无所，必有悖心。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今也天下之人怨恶其君，视之如寇仇，名之为独夫，固其所也。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《原君》</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>所</strong> <span class="dicpy">suǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/suǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈助〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 表示结构 <span class="encs">[used before a verb or a V-C construction together with 被 or 为 to indicate the passive voice]</span> </p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 与后面的动词结合，构成名词性结构</p><p><span class="diczx1">生，亦我所欲也，义，亦我所欲也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·告子上》</span></span></p><p><span
