                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>陬</strong> <span class="dicpy">zōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zōu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">fù</span>)，取声。<span class="yhcs">“阜”</span>是土山，与区域有关。本义:山的角落)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[corn of a mountain]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陬，阪隅也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陬，角也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在陵之诹。——<span class="smcs">《补亡诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">五岳</span>之土…若在陬，陵之阳。——<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">归路意昏，落日在岭陬。——</span><span class="diczx2">杨万里</span><span class="smcs">《次日醉归》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 隅，<span class="encs">[城市，村镇，国家的]</span>各个角落 <span class="encs">[corner]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:陬隅(室内西南角);陬维(边隅，角隅)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 春秋鲁地。在今山东曲阜东南 <span class="encs">[Zou place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孔子</span>生 <span class="diczx2">鲁</span><span class="diczx2">昌平乡</span><span class="diczx2">陬邑</span>。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 边远偏僻的地方 <span class="encs">[an outlying place]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其荒陬谲诡。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">监土木于僻陬，绝交通于首善。——<span class="smcs">《新罗马》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:陬隧(边邑的通道);陬澨(僻远处);陬邑(边远乡邑);陬芒(边远偏僻之地)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
