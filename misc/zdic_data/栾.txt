                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">栾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>栾</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">欒</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄢˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>落叶乔木。叶含鞣质，可制栲胶。花可入药，又可作黄色染料。木材可制器具，种子可榨油。</li>
	<li>柱上的曲木，两端以承斗拱。</li>
	<li>钟口的两角。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/孪">孪</a>&rdquo;，双生子。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> name of tree; a part of cornice</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Luan  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Koelreuteria paniculata,Savonnier</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">栾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>栾</strong></p>
<p>欒 <span class="dicpy">lu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 木名。也叫&ldquo;栾华&rdquo;、&ldquo;灯笼树&rdquo; <span class="encs">[goldenrain tree]</span>。无患子科。落叶乔木，羽状复叶，小叶卵形，花淡黄色，圆锥花序，结蒴果，长椭圆形。种子圆形黑色，叶子含鞣质，可制栲胶。花可提黄色染料，又可入药</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 拱，建筑物立柱和横梁之间成弓形的承重结构 <span class="encs">[arch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">结重栾以相承。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span>。注:&ldquo;柱上曲木，两头受栌者。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:栾拱(屋架中承受梁木的曲木);栾栌(屋中柱顶承梁之木，曲者为栾，直者为栌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;孪&rdquo;。双生子 <span class="encs">[twin]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">薛公</span>知之，故与二栾博。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;外储说右上》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/6B12_3049.svg" /><span class="swbf">(欒)</span><span class="ywyy">欒木，似欄。</span><span class="swdz">欄者今之楝字。本艸經有欒華，未知是不。借爲圜曲之偁，如鐘角曰欒，屋曲枅曰欒是。</span><span class="ywyy">從木䜌聲。</span><span class="swdz">洛官切。十四部。</span><span class="ywyy">禮：天子樹松。諸侯柏，大夫欒，士楊。</span><span class="swdz">士楊二字當作士槐庶人楊五字，轉寫奪去也。禮，謂禮緯含文嘉也。周禮冢人：以爵等爲丘封之度，與其樹數。賈疏引春秋緯：天子墳高三仞，樹以松。諸侯半之，樹以柏。大夫八尺，樹以藥草。士四尺，樹以槐。庶人無墳，樹以楊桺。藥草二字，欒之誤也。白虎通引春秋，含文嘉語全同，正作大夫以欒。又廣韵引五經通義士之冢樹槐，然則此士下有奪可知矣。含文嘉是禮緯。白虎通云春秋，含文嘉。葢引春秋，禮二緯。而春秋下有奪字。唐封氏閒見記引禮經及說文皆譌舛。</span></p>                          </div>
