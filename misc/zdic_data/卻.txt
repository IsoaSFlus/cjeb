						<p>● <strong>卻</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄑㄩㄝˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>退：～步（因畏懼或厭惡而後退，如「望而～～」）。退～。</li>
	<li>退還，不受：盛情難～。</li>
	<li>表示轉折：我來了，他～走了。</li>
	<li>去掉：失～。了（<span class="dicpy">liǎo</span>）～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> still, but; decline; retreat</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aber, jedoch, dennoch ,zurückweisen, ablehnen, ausschlagen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mais,cependant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">卻</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>詳細字義</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卻</strong></p>
<p>卻 <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈動〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形聲。本作「卻」，俗字作「却」。從卩，谷( <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> )聲。卩( <span class="dicpy">ji&eacute;</span>)，象人下跪的樣子，即腿骨節屈曲的樣子。從「卩」與腳的活動有關。本義:退)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本義 <span class="encs">[step back;retreat;withdraw]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卻，節慾也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《說文》</span>。按:「退也」。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卻行仄行。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《考工記&middot;梓人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾行卻曲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《莊子&middot;人間世》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怒戰慄而卻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《戰國策&middot;秦策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卻下而載之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儀禮&middot;既夕禮》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卻騏驥以轉運兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辭&middot;愍命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北救</span><span class="diczx2">趙</span>而卻秦。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;魏公子列傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">已卻</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>存 <span class="diczx2">趙</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">持璧卻立。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;廉頗藺相列傳》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">曳笏卻立。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《柳河東集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:退卻(向後撤退;畏難後退);卻走(退避;退走);卻還(退還);卻避(退避);卻歸(退歸);卻略(退身);卻去(後退;離去);卻生(後退貪生);卻立(後退站立)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 除;去 <span class="encs">[get rid of]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卻扇(古代婚禮行禮時，新娘以扇遮面，交拜後去扇);卻惑(去邪;消除疑難);了卻(了結)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 避;避免 <span class="encs">[avoid]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是時</span><span class="diczx2">李少君</span>亦以祠竈穀道卻老方見上，上尊之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史記&middot;封禪書》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卻老(避免衰老);卻死(避死)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 拒絕 <span class="encs">[refuse]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卻情勿過(礙着情面推辭不掉);卻絕(拒絕);卻妝(拒受嫁妝)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 迴轉;返回 <span class="encs">[turn back]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卻回(迴轉);卻望(回頭遠望);卻顧(回顧，迴轉頭看)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 反覆 <span class="encs">[repeat]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卻論(反覆論難);卻顧(反覆考慮)</span></p>
<p><strong>詞性變化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卻</strong> <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈連〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用在偏正複句的正句中，提出跟偏句相反或不一致的動作、行爲或狀況，表示轉折，相當於「但」、「但是」、「可是」 <span class="encs">[but]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一連數日，</span><span class="diczx2">施恩</span>來了大牢裏三次，卻不提防被 <span class="diczx2">張團練</span>家心腹人見了。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水滸傳》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 「反而」「然而」 <span class="encs">[at the same time]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:這個嚇得膽戰心驚、惶惶不安並滿腹牢騷的人&hellip;卻具有掌權和發號施令的能力</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>卻</strong> <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈助〉</p>
<p>用在動詞後，相當於「掉」，「去」 <span class="encs">[off;out;away]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:冷卻;忘卻;失卻信心;拋卻舊包袱</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>卻</strong> <span class="dicpy">qu&egrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qu&egrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 固然 <span class="encs">[admittedly]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">好卻十分好了，只是一件，這孩兒生下來，晝夜啼哭，乳也不肯吃。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《喻世明言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 再 <span class="encs">[then]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卻與小姑別。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉臺新詠&middot;古詩爲焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卻看妻子愁何在。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《聞官軍收河南河北》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卻坐促弦弦轉急。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(並序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:卻又(再);卻再(再)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 還 <span class="encs">[still]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卻是(還是)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 才 <span class="encs">[just]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卻纔(方纔)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 恰;正 <span class="encs">[just]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卻好(恰好;正好);卻如(恰如;好像);卻待(恰待;正要)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 剛剛 <span class="encs">[a moment ago]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:卻纔(剛纔)</span></p>
