                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>越</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄝˋ</span></p><ol><li>度过，超出：～过。～冬。～级。～轨。～权。～境。～位。～狱。～俎代庖。</li><li>声音、情感扬起，昂扬：激～。声音清～。</li><li>表示程度加深：～发（更加）。～加。～快～好。</li><li>消散：“精神劳则～”。</li><li>失坠，坠落：陨～。“射其左。～于车下”。</li><li>中国古民族名：百～（亦作“百粤”）。</li><li>中国周代诸侯国名。后用作浙江省东部的别称：～剧。～凫楚乙（“<span class="diczx3">乙</span>”，燕子。喻对于同一事物，由于自身条件的局限而作出不同的判断）。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">越</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>越</strong> <span class="dicpy">yuè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从走，戉( <span class="dicpy">yuè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuè.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:经过，越过)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pass over;cross]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越，度也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越，渡也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越十七扼。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·长攻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越陌度阡。——</span><span class="diczx2">曹操</span><span class="smcs">《短歌行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">崖可越焉?——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·天问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越天都之胁。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予越其右。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越长城之限。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越中岭。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:越位;越度(跨越);越绝(越过，度过);越陌度阡(穿过田间小路);越冬(过冬)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 超越，跃过 <span class="encs">[get over;jump over]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戒勿越。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如今敕令吾，不越一家，搜捉太子并</span><span class="diczx2">胡嵩</span>。——<span class="smcs">《武王伐纣平话》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:越墙而逃;越级升迁;越先(超过，超越)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 超过;超出 <span class="encs">[exceed;overstep]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">越职逾法，以取名誉。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·宣帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">必使为善者不越月逾时而得其赏。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《断刑论下》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:越礼(不守礼规;超越礼仪范围);越次(越出位次;越出序列);越职;越分(超过本分);越俎(比喻逾越自己的职分而代人做事);越规(超越规范)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 传播，宣扬 <span class="encs">[propagate]</span></p><p><span
