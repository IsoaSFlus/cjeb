                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">在</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>在</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zài  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zài.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄞˋ</span></p><ol><li>存，居：存～。健～。青春长～。</li><li>存留于某地点：～家。～职。～位。</li><li>关于某方面，指出着重点：～于（ａ．指出事物的本质所在，或指出事物以什么为内容；ｂ．决定于）。～乎。事～人为。</li><li>表示动作的进行：他～种地。</li><li>介词，表示事情的时间、地点、情形、范围等：～逃。～望。～握。～理。～朝（<span class="dicpy">cháo </span>）。～野。～世。</li><li>和“<span class="diczx3">所</span>”连用，表示强调：～所不辞。无所不～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> be at, in, on; consist in, rest</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> sich befinden in, an, auf, bei (V)​,Partikel <progressiver Aspekt> (gerade ablaufende Handlung)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> être en train de,à,être situé (à)​,en</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">在</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>在</strong> <span class="dicpy">zài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形。从土，才声。表示草木初生在土上。本义:存活着，生存，存在)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be living;exist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在，存也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，字从土，与坐同意。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见龙在田。——<span class="smcs">《易·乾》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">父母在，不远游。——<span class="smcs">《论语·里仁》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">疾在腠理。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·喻老》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">病在肌肤。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则无所不在。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在往者，在来者。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼·曾子立事》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:在生日(在世时);祖父已经不在了;在日(在世之日);在堂(母亲健在);父母健在</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 居于，处于 <span class="encs">[be at;be on]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朕在位七十载。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在河之洲。——<span class="smcs">《诗·周南·关雎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公在乾侯。——<span class="smcs">《春秋·昭公三十年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在</span><span class="diczx2">冀州</span>之南。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">平原</span>不在。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·自新》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人在室中。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>·<span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其在一室。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:文件在桌上;在侍(儿女在父母身旁);在室(女子未婚);在丧(居丧)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 在于;决定于 <span class="encs">[depend on;rest with;be in]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">山不在高。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>·<span class="diczx2">刘禹锡</span><span class="smcs">《陋室铭》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">水不在深。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不在他人。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>·<span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集·文集》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">全在我少年。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:贵在坚持;这事在你自己;要学习好，主要在自己努力;在道(在于正道)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 留在<span class="encs">[某团体]</span>，属于<span class="encs">[某团体]</span><span class="encs">[be a member of an organization;join or
