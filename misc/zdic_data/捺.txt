						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>捺</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">nà  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nà.mp3"></a></span>  ㄋㄚˋ</span></p><ol><li>用手按，抑制：按～。～手印。</li><li>笔形之一，由上向右斜下。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to press down heavily with the fingers</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> nach rechts gezeichneter Kalligraphiestrich (English: downwards-right concave character stroke)​</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> presser avec la main,piquer,rembourrer,(trait d'un sinogramme, concave du haut vers le bas, de gauche à droite)​</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>捺</strong> <span class="dicpy">nà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，奈声。本义:用手重按)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[press with force;press down]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先须捺后脚，然后勒前腰。——<span class="smcs">《游仙窟》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:捺上花冠;捺头向水下;捺印(盖指模或图章);捺抉(用力挤压);捺弮(向下弯曲如弓弩。形容禾穗丰硕)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 抑制 <span class="encs">[restrain]</span>。如:按捺(抑制兴头，扫兴);捺硬(勉力抑制);勉强捺住心头的怒火</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 搁置;扣压 <span class="encs">[suspend]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">这事断断破不得，既承头翁好心，千万将呈子捺下。——<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>捺</strong> <span class="dicpy">nà</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nà.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>汉字笔画的一种，向右斜下，近末端微有波折。古又名<span class="yhcs">“磔”</span> <span class="encs">[right-falling stroke in Chinese characters]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俗云捺，捺之祖磔法也，今人作捺多是。——<span class="smcs">《书法离鉤·八磔》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>捺</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄋㄚˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>nà <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1592.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">用手重按。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>捺</b>手印」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史·第三九回》：「将只手<b>捺</b>著左眼，飞跑出来。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">压抑。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「按捺不住」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儒林外史·第二回》：「周进只得捺定性子，坐著教导。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">截留、搁置。</span><span class="gc_yy">《二十年目睹之怪现状·第四八回》：「于是又在臬司和抚台那里，设法耽搁，这里面已经不知<b>捺</b>了多少日子了。」</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">一种书法笔法。笔画向右斜下，近末端处微有波折。永字八法称为「磔」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《老残游记·第一一回》：「一撇一<b>捺</b>。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
