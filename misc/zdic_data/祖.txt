                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>祖</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǔ.mp3"></a></span> ㄗㄨˇ</span></p><ol><li>父亲的上一辈：～父。～考。～母。～妣。</li><li>称与祖父同辈的人：外～父。外～母。</li><li>先代：～宗。～业。～制。～祭。～国。</li><li>初，开始：鼻～。</li><li>某种事业或派别的创始人：～师。</li><li>出行时祭路神，引申为送行：“五月金陵西，～余白下亭”。～饯。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> ancestor, forefather; grandfather</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Ahne, Vorfahr</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ancêtres,aïeux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">祖</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>祖</strong> <span class="dicpy">zǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从示，且( <span class="dicpy">jǔ</span>)。从<span class="yhcs">“示”</span>与祭祀、宗庙有关。本义:祖庙)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ancestral temple of a ruling house]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祖，始庙也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“始兼二义，新庙为始，远庙亦为始。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">左祖右社。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·考工记·匠人》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">受终于文祖。——<span class="smcs">《书·舜典》</span></span><span class="diczx2">孔</span>传:<span class="yhcs">“文祖者， <span class="diczx2">尧</span>文德之祖庙。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">启</span>乃下， <span class="diczx2">武王</span>善之，封之于 <span class="diczx2">宋</span>，立其祖。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·成相》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:祖祢(祖祢庙。祖庙与父庙)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 祖父 <span class="encs">[grandfather]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾祖死于是。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与吾祖居者。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾祖</span><span class="diczx2">太常公</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 祖宗 ;祖先;自祖父以上各辈尊长 <span class="encs">[ancestor]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">祖考来格。——<span class="smcs">《书·益稷》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故先祖基之，子孙成之。——<span class="smcs">《盐铁论·结和》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无念尔祖。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·文王》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:祖积(祖宗遗传下来);祖贯(祖辈居住之处;祖籍);祖陇(祖传的土地);祖烈(祖宗的功业);祖家(同一祖先的家族);祖训(祖先的遗训;祖父的训戒)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 祖师。指言行、功业为后世所宗仰者 <span class="encs">[the founder;the founder of a school of learning ，a craft，etc.]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx1">第一祖</span><span class="diczx2">摩诃迦叶</span>。——<span class="smcs">《景德传灯录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:祖心，祖意(祖师的心意);祖印(祖师法印);祖位(祖师);祖系(祖师世系)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 帝王的庙号 <span class="encs">[posthumous title of an emperor]</span> 。从汉代开始，凡创业的国君死，其庙号称为<span class="yhcs">“祖”</span>。如汉称刘邦为汉高祖，唐称李渊为唐高祖</p><p><span class="diczx1">盖闻古者祖有功而宗有德。——<span class="smcs">《史记·孝文帝本纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 初，开始。亦指事物的本源 <span class="encs">[beginning]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浮游乎万物之祖。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·山木》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span>
