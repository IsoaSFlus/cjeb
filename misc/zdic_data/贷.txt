                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贷</strong></p><p>貸 <span class="dicpy">dài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，代声。从<span class="yhcs">“贝”</span>，表示与钱财有关。本义:施予;给予)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[give]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贷，施也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贷，予也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以财投长曰贷。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记·千乘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡民之货者。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·泉府》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“谓从官借本贾也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">又称贷而益之。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不贷无出也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天运》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贳贷卖买。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·食货志下》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:贷施(施舍);贷恤(赈施抚恤)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 借出钱财 <span class="encs">[lend;loan]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">宁积粟腐仓而不忍贷人一斗。——<span class="smcs">《潜夫论·忠贵》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:贷借(金钱物品的借出和借入);贷项(记入账户贷方的账项);贷卖(放贷或出卖)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 借入 <span class="encs">[borrow]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贷，借也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">庄周</span>家贫，故往贷粟于 <span class="diczx2">监河侯</span>。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·外物》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:向银行贷款;贷钱(借钱);贷赊(借贷，赊欠)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 推卸<span class="encs">[责任]</span> <span class="encs">[shirk]</span>。如:责无旁贷</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 饶恕，宽恕 <span class="encs">[forgive]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">每具狱上闻，辄贷其死。——<span class="smcs">《宋史·刑法志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有系狱者，皆挠法贷减。——<span class="smcs">《旧唐书·王世充传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">今姑贷汝。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《凫藻集》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:严惩不贷;贷罪(免罪);贷减(宽宥减罪)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>贷</strong></p><p>貸 <span class="dicpy">dài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>要付利息的借款 <span class="encs">[loan]</span>。如:农贷;贷券(债券)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/贷方">贷方</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
