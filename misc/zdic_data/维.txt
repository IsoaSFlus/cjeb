                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">维</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>维</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">維</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄟˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>系，连结：～系。～絷。</li>
	<li>保持：～持。～护。～修。～生素。</li>
	<li>纲：纲～（总纲，亦指法度）。</li>
	<li>数学名词，几何学及空间理论的基本概念，通常的空间有&ldquo;三维&rdquo;，平面是&ldquo;二维&rdquo;，直线只有&ldquo;一维&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>思考：思～（亦作&ldquo;思惟&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>以，因为：&ldquo;～子之故，使我不能餐兮&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>文言助词，用于句首或句中：～新。～妙～肖。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">维</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>维</strong></p>
<p>維 <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，隹( <span class="dicpy">zhuī</span>)声。本义:系物的大绳)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cord]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维，紘也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维，车盖系也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维，系也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">斡维焉系。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;天问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维王之大常。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;节服氏》</span>。注:&ldquo;维之以缕。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">居其维首。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十年》</span>。疏:&ldquo;纲也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天柱折，地维绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文训》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:维纲(用以系物和提网的绳。亦指维系、保持)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 隅，角落 <span class="encs">[corner]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维，隅也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">东北为报德之维也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;天文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:四维(四角);维那(寺院里地位次于首座的僧职);维岳降神(高山降其神灵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 关键 <span class="encs">[key]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">人君者，摄天地之政，秉四海之维。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;陈蕃传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">法令为维纲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;禁藏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:维纲(纲纪;法度)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 几何学上指空间独立而互相正交的方位数，通常的空间有三维，平面或曲面有二维，直线或曲线只有一维 <span class="encs">[dimension]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 网 <span class="encs">[net]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">维，网也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《集韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 古地名 <span class="encs">[Wei，an ancient place name]</span>。故地在今四川省理县</p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 生物体中的丝状组织 <span class="encs">[fiber]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:纤维(动、植物体中的丝状组织)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>维</strong></p>
<p>維 <span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&eacute;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 拴;系 <span class="encs">[tie up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">絷之维之，以永今朝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;白驹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">诸侯维舟。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">李</span>注:&ldquo;中央左右相维持者曰维舟。&rdquo;</p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“维”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/維">維</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中" target="_blank">未集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中&amp;bs=糸" target="_blank">糸</a>】
                              維·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7DAD.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】以追切【集韻】【韻會】夷隹切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音惟。【說文】車蓋維也。</p>
<p>又【博雅】係也。【詩&middot;小雅】縶之維之。【傳】維，繫也。【公羊傳&middot;昭二十四年】且夫牛馬維婁。【註】繫馬曰維，繫牛曰婁。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】四方是維。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;大司馬】以維邦國。【註】維，猶連結也。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;太射禮】中離維綱。【註】侯有上下綱，其邪制射舌之角爲維。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋天】太歲在巳曰屠維。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;賈誼傳】是猶度江河亡維楫。【註】維，所以繫船。</p>
<p>又【管子&middot;牧民篇】國有四維，一曰禮，二曰義，三曰廉，四曰恥。</p>
<p>又【淮南子&middot;天文訓】帝張四維，運之以斗。</p>
<p>又【楚辭&middot;天問】幹維焉繫。【註】維，綱也。</p>
<p>又【爾雅&middot;釋詁】伊維侯也。【註】發語辭。【韻會】案六經惟維唯三字皆通作語辭，又訓獨，尚書助辭皆用惟字，詩助辭多用維字，左傳助辭用唯字，論語助辭用惟字。新安朱氏曰：惟从心，思也。維糸，繫也。唯从口，專辭也，應辭也。然皆語辭，古書皆通用之。</p>
<p>又地名。【史記&middot;管晏列傳】晏平仲嬰者，萊之夷維人也。【註】應劭曰：故萊夷維邑。</p>
<p>又姓。【姓氏急就篇】漢維汜，妖巫，卷縣人。</p>
<p>又於恭切，音雍。【周禮&middot;夏官&middot;職方氏】其浸盧維。【釋文】盧音雷。維，於恭反。</p>
