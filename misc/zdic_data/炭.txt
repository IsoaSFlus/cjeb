                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>炭</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">tàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tàn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄊㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>把木材和空气隔绝，加高热烧成的一种黑色燃料：木～。～素。～笔。～画。</li><li>像炭的东西：山楂～。</li><li>煤：石～。焦～。泥～。</li></ol>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>炭</strong> <span class="dicpy">tàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，岸省声。本义:木炭)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[charcoal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">炭，烧木余也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">火所烧余木曰炭。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">坐于涂炭。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“墨也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">可怜身上衣正单，心忧炭贱愿天寒。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:木炭;草炭;骨炭;活性炭;炭炉(烧木炭的炉子);炭薪(柴炭);炭精纸(一种涂有炭精供复写的纸);炭笔(用细木枝烧焦或用炭粉制成的笔);炭冰(炭热而冰冷。喻不能相容);炭火(燃烧的炭)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 像炭的东西 <span class="encs">[charcoal-like thing]</span>。如:山楂炭</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 火。比喻灾难;困苦 <span class="encs">[fire]</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 〈方〉∶煤炭，也称石炭 <span class="encs">[coal]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为其主人入山作炭。——<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭棒">炭棒</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭笔">炭笔</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭黑">炭黑</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭画">炭画</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭精">炭精</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭精棒">炭精棒</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭疽">炭疽</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭窑">炭窑</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炭渣">炭渣</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">炭</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>炭</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄊㄢˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>tàn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1399.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">含碳物质在不完全燃烧或加热时所得到的残留物。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「焦<b>炭</b>」、「木<b>炭</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·火部》：「<b>炭</b>，烧木余也。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">方言。指煤。</span><span class="gc_yy">明·张自烈《正字通·火部》：「<b>炭</b>，石<b>炭</b>。今西北所烧之煤，即石<b>炭</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《史记·卷四九·外戚世家》：「（少君）​至宜阳，为其主入山作<b>炭</b>，（寒）​暮卧岸下百余人，岸崩，尽压杀卧者，少君独得脱，不死。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">姓。如汉代有<b><b>炭</b></b>虬。</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/70AD.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】他案切【正韻】他晏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音歎。【說文】燒木餘也。从火，岸省聲。【玉篇】灺也，火也。【禮·月令】草木黃落，乃伐薪爲炭。</p><p>又塗炭，塗泥也。炭，火也。【書·仲虺之誥】民墜塗炭。</p><p>又蜃炭。【周禮·秋官·赤犮氏】掌除牆屋，以蜃炭攻之。</p><p>又【正字通】石炭，卽今西北所燒之煤。【前漢·地理志】豫章郡出石，可燃爲薪。【徐陵春情詩】石炭擣輕紈。</p><p>又烰炭，俗作麩炭。【白居易詩】日暮半爐麩炭火。【本草】煎藥焙火宜用麩炭。</p><p>又懸炭，古<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/202EB.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠋫</span>氣法也。【後漢·律曆志】權土炭放隂陽。日冬至陽氣應則景長極，黃鐘通土炭，輕而衡仰。日夏至隂氣應，則景短極，蕤賔通土炭，重而衡低。【淮南子·天文訓】水勝，故夏至濕。火勝，故冬至燥。燥，故炭輕，濕，故炭重。【梁<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25CD1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥳑</span>文帝詩】月暈蘆灰缺，秋還縣炭枯。</p><p>又姓。【西京雜記】炭虯，長安人。</p>
