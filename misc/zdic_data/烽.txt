						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>烽</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">fēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄥˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>古代边防报警的烟火：～火（ ➊ 古代边防报警的烟火； ➋ 喻战争或战乱）。～烟。～燧。～火台。～鼓（烽火和战鼓，指战争）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">烽</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>烽</strong> <span class="dicpy">fēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，夆( <span class="dicpy">fēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fēng.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:烽火，古时边防报警的烟火)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[signal fire;beacon fire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公子与</span><span class="diczx2">魏王</span>博，而北境传举烽，言&ldquo; <span class="diczx2">赵</span>寇至，且入界。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;魏公子列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:烽警(报警的烽火)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 指战火 <span class="encs">[flames of war]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:烽火连天(形容战火到处燃烧);烽燹(战火)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指烽火台 <span class="encs">[beacon tower]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:烽子(守卫烽火台的士兵);烽侦(烽火台);烽候(指烽火台);烽台(即烽火台);烽墩(烽火台)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 泛指举火 <span class="encs">[fire]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后<span class="encs">[</span><span class="diczx2">许</span>]</span> <span class="diczx2">章</span>坐走马 <span class="diczx2">上林</span>下烽驰逐，免官。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
