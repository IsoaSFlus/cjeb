						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>佐</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zuǒ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǒ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄨㄛˇ</span></p><ol><li>辅助，帮助：～证（证据）。～餐。</li><li>处于辅助地位的人：僚～。</li><li>劝：～食。～酒（ａ．陪伴喝酒；ｂ．就着菜肴把酒喝下去）。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>佐</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (本作左。形声。从人，左声。本义:辅助，帮助)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(古人以<span class="yhcs">“右”</span>为尊，<span class="yhcs">“左”</span>为卑。<span class="yhcs">“佐”</span>常用于下对上、弱对强的帮助。上对下，强对弱时用<span class="yhcs">“佑”</span>) <span class="encs">[assist]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佐，助也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">故</span><span class="diczx2">周公旦</span>佐相天子。——<span class="smcs">《墨子·贵义》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">陈胜</span>佐之。——<span class="smcs">《史记·陈涉世家》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">太宰之职，常建邦之六典，以佐王治邦国。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·天官》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四岳佐之。——<span class="smcs">《国语·周语下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予佐尔贾。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佐以科学。——</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">佐读勤苦。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:佐理(助理，帮助处理);佐事(辅助事奉);佐戎(参与军事);佐相(辅助);佐佑(辅助;支持);佐检(辅助);佐州(辅佐州政);佐谋(辅助筹划);佐治(辅佐治理)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 陪同 <span class="encs">[company]</span>。又如:佐觞(陪酒助兴);佐享(陪同受祭祀)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 劝 <span class="encs">[advise]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">召之，使佐食，比已食，三叹。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">韦昭</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“佐，犹劝也。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:佐觞(劝酒)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>佐</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 处于辅助地位的官员，僚属 <span class="encs">[assitant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有</span><span class="diczx2">赵孟</span>以为大夫，有 <span class="diczx2">伯瑕</span>以为佐。——<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:佐杂(地方官署的辅佐官员);佐吏(汉时辅佐地方政事的小官);佐将(太平天国前期对军中带兵官和地方行政名的通称)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 姓</p><p>◎ <strong>佐</strong> <span class="dicpy">zuǒ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zuǒ.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p>副，第二位 <span class="encs">[second]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">掌佐车之政。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·田仆》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">郑</span><span class="diczx2">周父</span>御佐车。——<span class="smcs">《左传·成公二年》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/佐餐">佐餐</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
