                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>烦</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">煩</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">fán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fán.mp3"></a></span> ㄈㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>苦闷，急躁：～躁。～乱。～恼。～闷。～郁。心～意乱。</li><li>又多又乱：不厌其～。要言不～。～絮。～琐。～冗。～文。麻～。</li><li>搅扰：～扰。～嚣。</li><li>敬辞，表示请、托：～劳。～请。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">烦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>烦</strong></p><p>煩 <span class="dicpy">fán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从页( <span class="dicpy">xié</span>)，从火。从<span class="yhcs">“页”</span>表示与头部有关;从<span class="yhcs">“火”</span>，表示发烧。本义:头痛发烧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 烦躁，烦闷 <span class="encs">[be annoyed;be vexed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烦，热头痛也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烦则喘喝。——<span class="smcs">《素问·生气通天论》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“谓烦躁。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">烦气为虫。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·精神》</span>。按，旱热也。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">阿兄得闻之，怅然心中烦。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">新鬼烦冤旧鬼哭。(烦，愁苦。)——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《兵车行》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至于烦乃止也已，无以生疾。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:心烦(心里烦躁);烦积(烦闷积郁);烦困(烦躁疲劳);烦怨(烦恼怨恨)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“繁”</span>。烦琐;繁多 <span class="encs">[be superfluous and confusing;overelaborate;numerous]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小人不避其禁，故刑烦。——<span class="smcs">《商君书·算地》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">法省则不烦。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·主术》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“多也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">世浊则礼烦。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·音初》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">列五王之德，烦烦如繁诸乎。——<span class="smcs">《大戴礼记·少间》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“众也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以奉使称臣，频烦至</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·费伟传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">话休絮烦。——<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:烦务(繁杂的事务);烦细(繁杂琐碎);烦富(繁多;庞杂);烦几(繁杂的政事);烦鄙(繁杂卑俗);要言不烦</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 厌烦，腻烦 <span class="encs">[be sick of]</span>。如:耐烦(不急躁，不厌其烦);烦倦(厌烦困倦);烦厌(厌烦)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 乱;纠缠 <span class="encs">[confuse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">拨烦理乱，兼肃文教。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·刘颂传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:烦委(杂乱堆积);烦悖(牵缠;纷乱);烦悖(纷乱谬误);烦舌(纷繁杂乱;心情烦闷，思绪混乱)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>烦</strong></p><p>煩 <span class="dicpy">fán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span>
