                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">极</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>极</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">極</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>顶端，最高点，尽头：登～（帝王即位）。登峰造～。</li>
	<li>指地球的南北两端或电路、磁体的正负两端：～地（极圈以内的地区）。～圈。北～。阴～。</li>
	<li>尽，达到顶点：～力。～目四望。物～必反。</li>
	<li>最高的，最终的：～点。～限。～端。～致。</li>
	<li>副词：表示最高程度：～其。～为（<span class="dicpy">w&eacute;i </span>）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> extreme, utmost, furthest, final</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Gipfel, Extrem  (S)​,Pol  (S)​,extrem; äußerst  (Adv)​,auf die Spitze treiben  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> sommet,extrémité,pôle,extrêmement,excessivement,atteindre,parvenir à</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">极</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>极</strong></p>
<p>極 <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，亟声。本义:房屋的正梁)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[ridgepole]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">極，栋也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，在屋之正中至高处。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万载宫极。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;天文志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有夫妻臣妾登极。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;则阳》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马</span>注:&ldquo;屋栋也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">茂</span>初在 <span class="diczx2">广汉</span>，梦坐大殿，极上有三穗禾， <span class="diczx2">茂</span>跳取之，得其中穗，辄复失之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;蔡茂传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 最高的地位 <span class="encs">[highest position]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今以三寸之舌，为帝者师，封万户，位列侯，此布衣之极，于良足矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;留侯世家》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:极峰(最高层的官员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指君位 <span class="encs">[throne]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">建用皇极。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;洪范》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:登极(登君位)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 顶点，最高处;最高最远的处所 <span class="encs">[top]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不知便可登峰造极不?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;文学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">屋极有窗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:登峰造极;极巅(山顶端);极至(极度，极顶。顶点);极峰(最高层的官员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 尽头;极限 <span class="encs">[extremity]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何使我至于此极也!(极，指最痛苦的地步。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">至治之极。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:极地(绝处);极界(界限);极际(边际，尽头);极尽(终点，尽头);极则必反(事物发展到极端，就会向相反的方面转化)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 地球的南北两端 <span class="encs">[pole]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:极流(从地球南北极移向赤道的潮流);极光;极冠。又指磁体的两端;电源或电器上电流的输入输出端。如:南极;北极;正极;负极</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 北极星 <span class="encs">[Polaris]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">譬众星之环极。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《西京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:极枢(指北极星和天枢星)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 最高准则、标准 <span class="encs">[highest norm，highest standard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">莫匪尔极。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周颂&middot;思文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">立名者，行之极也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>极</strong></p>
<p>極 <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈副〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 最，非常，狠，满 <span class="encs">[extremely;exceedingly;oumost;very]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">初极狭。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">陶渊明</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">极为神速。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈&middot;活板》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">备极欢洽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广东军务记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">极盛于十六世纪。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">蔡元培</span><span class="smcs">《图画》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:极荷(深深承蒙);极通(很对);极打(狠打，痛打)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>极</strong></p>
<p>極 <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 至，到达 <span class="encs">[reach]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">意有所极，梦亦同趣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《永州八记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 极天际地(直达天边，遍及大地。形容广大无边);极天爬地(用尽一切办法);极明(直至天明);极格(及格。达到标准);极海(至海);极晨(直到次日清晨)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 穷尽，竭尽 <span class="encs">[exhaust]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">平原远而极目兮。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王粲</span><span class="smcs">《登楼赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不得极夫游之乐。(极，使尽，使动用法。夫，指示代词，那。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《游褒禅山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">南极</span><span class="diczx2">潇</span><span class="diczx2">湘</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">极力为之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 极情尽致(感情充沛，神态逼真);极望(尽目所见，放眼远望);极言(尽言无余);极目(穷尽目力，眺望远方)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 深探，穷究 <span class="encs">[study deeply]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:极本穷源(彻底地推究本源);极问(追根问底);极深研几(探讨研究事物的深奥隐微之处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;殛&rdquo;。惩罚，诛杀 <span class="encs">[punish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子之罪大极重。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;盗跖》</span>。<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释言》</span>:殛，诛也。言罪大而诛重也。极、殛古字通。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故至治之国，有赏罚，而无喜怒，故圣人极。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;用人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:极法(极刑。死刑;重刑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 到达极点 <span class="encs">[reach the limit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">感极而悲者矣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">所得</span><span class="diczx2">表</span>众，亦极七八万耳。(极，至多，最多，动词状语。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>

<p>極 <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 最高的、最远的，达到极点的 <span class="encs">[extreme]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地势极而南溟深。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">王勃</span><span class="smcs">《滕王阁赋》</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">极天云一线。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:极塞(遥远荒僻的边塞地区);极处(极高、极远之处);极路(非常遥远的路);极源(远源)。如:极口(满口);极仄(很险的诗韵);极色(黑白两色。白色明度最高，黑色最低，均为无彩色，正好处于两个极端，故称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;亟&rdquo;。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 急速 <span class="encs">[fast]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予不敢不极卒宁王图事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;大诰》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 儆戒 <span class="encs">[respectfully]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:极戒(儆戒)</span></p>
