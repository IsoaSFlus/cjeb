                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>纲</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">綱</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄍㄤˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>提网的总绳。</li>
	<li>事物的关键部分：大～。～领。～目。～要（➊提纲；➋概要）。</li>
	<li>中国从唐代起转运大批货物所行的办法：一～（把货物分批运行，每批车辆船只的计数编号）。花石～。生辰～。</li>
	<li>生物学分类的一种类别（生物学把同一门的生物按照彼此相似的特征和亲缘关系再分成若干群，每一群为&ldquo;一纲&rdquo;，&ldquo;纲&rdquo;以下再分为&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">目</span>&rdquo;）。</li>
	<li>统治者认为维持正常秩序的必不可少的行为规范：～纪。～常（&ldquo;三纲&rdquo;、&ldquo;五常&rdquo;的简称，是一种封建道德）。朝（<span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;o </span>）～（统治集团内部应遵守的法纪）。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">纲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>纲</strong></p>
<p>綱 <span class="dicpy">gāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gāng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从糸( <span class="dicpy">m&igrave;</span>)，表示与线丝有关，冈声。本义:提网的总绳)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[headrope of a fishing net]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纲，维紘绳也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若网在纲，有条而不紊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;盘庚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纲纪四方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;棫朴》</span>。笺:&ldquo;张之为纲，理之为纪。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四方之纲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;卷阿》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举其宏纲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">纪纲之仆。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公二十四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">善张网者引其纲，不一一摄万目而后得。则是劳而难，引其纲则鱼已囊矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非记&middot;外储说右下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纲挈目张(犹纲举目张);纲提领挈(提起网纲，挈住裘领。比喻抓住要领)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 事物的关键部分，事理的要领 <span class="encs">[outline]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为政贵当举纲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史&middot;源贺传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然洽闻之士，宜撮纲要，揽华而食实，弃邪而采正。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙&middot;诸子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熔则纲领昭畅。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文心雕龙&middot;熔裁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">法令为维纲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;禁藏》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:纲辖(朝廷中枢总要之职。唐、宋多指尚书省执政官);大纲(内容要点);一部两卷本的世界史纲</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 纲维;法度 <span class="encs">[key link;guiding principe]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:纲条(法纪);纲理(纲纪;法度);纲网(纲维;法度);纲宪(法纪;法度)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 生物分类中的一个大类，位于目之上，在现代分类中位于门或部之下，而在林奈分类法中为最高种类 <span class="encs">[class]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:鱼纲;鸟纲;哺乳纲</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 唐、宋时成批运输货物的组织 <span class="encs">[transportation of goods under convoy]</span>。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7DB1.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕㭃<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/234E1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣓡</span>【廣韻】古郞切【集韻】【韻會】居郞切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音岡。【說文】維紘繩也。【書&middot;盤庚】若網在綱，有條而不紊。【詩&middot;大雅】綱紀四方。【傳】張之爲綱。【疏】綱者，網之大繩。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;鄕射禮】乃張侯下綱。【註】綱，持舌繩也。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記&middot;梓人】梓人爲侯上綱與下綱，出舌尋，絹寸焉。【註】綱，所以繫侯於植者也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;緯含文嘉】君爲臣綱，父爲子綱，夫爲妻綱。</p>
