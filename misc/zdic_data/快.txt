                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>快</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">kuài  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuài.mp3"></a></span>  ㄎㄨㄞˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>速度大，与“<span class="diczx3">慢</span>”相对：～车。～件。～步。～速。～捷。～马加鞭。</li>    <li>赶紧，从速：赶～。</li>    <li>将，就要；接近：天～亮了。</li>    <li>灵敏：他脑子真～。眼明手～。</li>    <li>锐利，锋利，与“<span class="diczx3">钝</span>”相对：王麻子刀剪真～。</li>    <li>爽利，直截了当：爽～。心直口～。～人～语。</li>    <li>高兴舒服：～乐。～意。痛～。愉～。～感。～事。～慰。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">快</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>快</strong> <span class="dicpy">kuài</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kuài.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，( <span class="dicpy">guài</span>)声。本义:高兴，痛快)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[pleasant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">快，喜也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">文信侯</span>去而不快。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·秦策》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心不快。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">快意留之，非忠臣也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·班超传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">则不快吾意。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若所为诚快。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:快观(让人看了高兴);快吾意(使吾意快，符合我的意思)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 舒适;舒畅 <span class="encs">[comfortable;pleased;gratified]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有一士大夫不快。——<span class="smcs">《三国志·方伎传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">体中不快。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">使快弹数曲。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">快哉此风。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋玉</span><span class="smcs">《风赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:快畅(痛快，舒畅);快当(痛快，爽快);快饮(畅饮;痛饮);快意(痛快)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 动作迅速，与<span class="yhcs">“慢”</span>相对 <span class="encs">[fast;quick;rapid;speedy]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俗谓急速曰快。——<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">此马虽快，然力薄不堪苦行。——<span class="smcs">《晋书·王湛传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">彭城王</span>有快牛，至爱惜之。——<span class="smcs">《世说新语·汰侈》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">健儿须快马，快马须健儿。——古乐府<span class="smcs">《折杨柳歌辞》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:快拍(戏曲中拍子急速的调子);快当(快;迅速);快马(善于奔驰的健马);快迅(速度快)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 锋利 <span class="encs">[sharp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">快刀断其头。——</span><span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《行次西郊作》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 又如:快谈(痛快锋利的言谈)</p><p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 直爽，爽快 <span class="encs">[straight forward]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人见邻里有佳快者，使子弟慕而学之。——<span class="smcs">《颜氏家训·勉学》</span></span></p><p><span
