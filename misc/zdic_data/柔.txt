                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>柔</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">róu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/róu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄖㄡˊ</span></p><ol><li>植物初生而嫩：～荑（初生嫩芽，喻女子白嫩的手）。</li><li>软，不硬：～软。～韧。～嫩。～滑。</li><li>软弱，与“<span class="diczx3">刚</span>”相对：～懦。～弱。刚～相济。</li><li>温和：～顺。～和。～曼（柔和妩媚）。～情。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">柔</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>柔</strong> <span class="dicpy">róu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/róu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，矛声。本义:树木可曲可直)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[supple;soft]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔，木曲直也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。段注:<span class="yhcs">“凡木曲者可直，直者可曲，曰柔。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">荏染柔木。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小弁》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:柔木(柔软而又坚韧的木)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 柔弱;细嫩 <span class="encs">[tender]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔，弱也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爰求柔桑。——<span class="smcs">《诗·豳风·七月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">薇亦柔止。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·采微》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔弱者，生之徒也。——<span class="smcs">《说苑·敬慎》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔弱者，道之要也 。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·原道》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柳条将舒未舒，柔梢披风。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁宏道</span><span class="smcs">《满井游记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:柔枝嫩叶;柔条(嫩枝);柔红(柔嫩的花);柔莩(幼芽附着的种子薄膜);柔荏(草木枝叶柔韧);柔桑(嫩桑叶);柔稚(幼嫩)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 柔软 <span class="encs">[lithe;soft;flexible;supple]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寂寞深闺，柔肠一寸愁千缕。——</span><span class="diczx2">李清照</span><span class="smcs">《点绛唇》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 又如:柔滑如荑(柔荑);柔穰(柔软的穰草);柔暖(柔软温暖);柔腴(柔软丰满);柔钝(柔软而不锋利);柔毳(柔软的皮毛);柔革(柔软的皮革)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 柔和;温和 <span class="encs">[mild;gentle]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔而立。——<span class="smcs">《书·皋陶谟》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">郑</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“性行和柔。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔而正。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·乐记》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其声和以柔。——<span class="smcs">《史记·乐书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">然则柔风甘雨乃至。——<span class="smcs">《管子·四时》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士不偏不党，柔而坚。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·士容》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">柔色以温之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·内则》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 又如: 柔谨(温和恭谨);柔缓(温和宽厚);柔调(温和地调治);柔愿(温和朴实);柔慈(温和仁慈);柔雅(温和文雅);柔黄(嫩黄);柔良(柔顺善良);柔明(柔顺而聪明);柔合(柔顺弥合);柔讷(柔顺而不直言); 柔谦(柔和谦逊); 柔嘉(柔和美善);柔雌(柔和);柔仁(柔和而仁慈);柔心(性情柔和);柔奸(表面柔和而内心奸诈);柔气(气质柔和的人)</p><p><span
