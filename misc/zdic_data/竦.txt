						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>竦</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sǒng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǒng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄥˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>伸长脖子，提起脚跟站着：～立。</li>
	<li>恭敬，肃敬：&ldquo;～然异之&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">悚</span>&rdquo;。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">竦</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>竦</strong> <span class="dicpy">sǒng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sǒng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从立，从束。立，站着。束，约束。本义:肃敬;恭敬)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[respect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竦，敬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:&ldquo;敬者，肃也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巫从旁望空代祝，唇吻翕辟，不知何词。各各竦立以听。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:竦然(肃然起敬的样子);竦听(肃然敬听);竦立(恭敬的站着);竦敬(肃然起敬);竦惧(肃立惶恐)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> (企立，伸长脖子、提起脚跟站着) <span class="encs">[crane]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡人将竦意而览焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;东方朔传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竦余身而顺止兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;张衡传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士卒皆山东人，竦而望归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;韩王信传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫临搏而企竦，闻乐而窃者，或有赏音而识道也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;曹植&middot;求自试表》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:竦首(抬头);竦企(引领举踵以待);竦耳(竖起耳朵)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 耸立 <span class="encs">[shoot high up;tower]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">水何澹澹，山岛竦峙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;曹操&middot;步出夏门行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:竦杰(高高地耸立);竦立(竖立);竦峙(耸立，高耸峙立);竦拔(高耸挺拔);竦峭(高峻;陡峭);竦竦(高耸貌);竦垒(耸立叠起);竦石(耸立的石头);竦秀(挺拔秀丽;庄重文雅)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;耸&rdquo;。往上跳 <span class="encs">[jump up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若士举臂而竦身，遂入云中。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;道应》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">举戈林竦，挥锋电灭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《文选&middot;木华&middot;海赋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我很想借此算是竦身一摇，将悲哀摆脱。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《为了忘却的纪念》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:竦身(耸身。纵身向上跳);竦辔(纵辔。谓骑马)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 振动;振作 <span class="encs">[vibrate;bestir oneself]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫</span><span class="diczx2">周</span> <span class="diczx2">秦</span>之末，三五之隆，已尝专意散财，厚爵禄，竦精神，举天下以求之矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:竦神(振作精神);竦健(振奋强健);竦动(抖动;震动)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 执;握 <span class="encs">[hold]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竦长剑兮拥幼艾，荪独宜兮为民正。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;屈原&middot;九歌&middot;少司命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:竦剑(仗剑;持剑)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 通&ldquo;悚&rdquo;。恐惧 <span class="encs">[fear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竦，惧也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释诂》</span></span></p>
