                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">匪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>匪</strong> <span class="dicpy">fěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fěi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从匚( <span class="dicpy">fāng</span>)，盛物之器。非声。本义:<span class="yhcs">“篚”</span>的古字。竹器，形似竹箧)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[round bamboo basket]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪，器似竹筐。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，古者盛币帛以匪，其器椭方。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共设匪瓮之礼。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·肆师》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“斐”</span>。五色相错 <span class="encs">[rich and bright colors]</span>斐</p><p><span class="diczx1">有匪君子，如切如瑳。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·淇奥》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 盗匪 <span class="encs">[bandit]</span> 以打家劫舍为生、有组织、有武装的团伙。如:剿匪;匪荒(盗匪引起的灾祸);匪党(盗匪党徒);匪营(匪军的营地)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>匪</strong> <span class="dicpy">fěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/fěi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 假借为<span class="yhcs">“非”</span>，表示否定 <span class="encs">[not;no]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪寇，昏媾。——<span class="smcs">《易·屯卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">比之匪人。——<span class="smcs">《易·比卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪夷所思。——<span class="smcs">《易·涣卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先祖匪人。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·四月》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我心匪石，不可转也。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·柏舟》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪女之为美，美人之贻。——<span class="smcs">《诗·邶风·北风》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:匪懈(不懈怠，不怠情);匪独(不单是);匪惟(不是);匪夷(不寻常)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示否定，相当于<span class="yhcs">“无”</span><span class="encs">[not; no]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">深言匪由衷，白首为无误。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">何景明</span><span class="smcs">《送崔氏》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:匪什(无篇什，谓没有写作才能);匪止(不止);匪啻(不止，不只);匪惶(没有闲暇;来不及)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 表示关联，用于<span class="yhcs">“非…不…”</span>之类的句式 <span class="encs">[must…not]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪安匪舒。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·江汉》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪富匪势，穷年无冀。——<span class="smcs">《抱朴子·外篇·吴失》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 不仅，不但 <span class="encs">[not only]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">匪手携之，言示之事;匪面命之，言提其耳。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“彼”</span>( <span class="dicpy">bǐ</span>)。那，那个 <span class="encs">[that;that one]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">如匪行迈谋，是用不得于道。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小旻》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">彼交匪纾，天子所予。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传·卷四》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>匪</strong> <span class="dicpy">fěi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr"                                                                               <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/532A_584.svg"  /><span class="swbf">(匪)</span><span class="ywyy">器佀竹匧。</span><span class="swdz">小徐衹云如篋。小雅。承筐是將。傳曰。筐、篚屬。所以行幣帛也。按此筐與飯器之筐。異名同實。故毛訓之曰。匪屬也。小雅言匡。禹貢、禮記言匪。應劭漢書注曰。漢書作棐。應劭曰。棐、竹器也。方曰箱。隋曰棐。隋者、方而長也。他果反。古盛幣帛必以匪。匪篚古今字。有借匪爲斐者。如詩有匪君子是也。有借爲分者。周禮匪朌、鄭司農云匪分也是也。有借爲非者。如詩我心匪鑒、我心匪石是也。有借爲彼者。如左傳引詩如匪行邁謀杜曰匪彼也、荀子引匪交匪舒卽詩彼交匪紓是也。</span><span class="ywyy">从匚。非聲。</span><span class="swdz">非尾切。十五部。按竹部曰。篚、車笭也。非匪之異體。故不錄於此。</span><span class="ywyy">逸周書曰。實玄黃于匪。</span><span class="swdz">按此句今惟見孟子滕文公篇引書。其上文云。綏厥士女。篚厥玄黃。昭我周王見休。惟臣附于大邑周。似必爲周書。趙氏亦云。從有攸以下道武王伐紂時。皆尙書逸篇之文也。</span><span class="xh"></span></p>                          </div>
