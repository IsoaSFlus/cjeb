                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>巴</strong> <span class="dicpy">bā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。小篆象蛇形。本义:大蛇)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古代传说中的一种大蛇 <span class="encs">[big snake]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巴，巴虫也，或曰食象蛇。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巴蛇食象，三岁而出其骨。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·海内南经》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“说者云，长千寻。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朱卷之国，有黑蛇青象，食象。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·海内经》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“即巴蛇也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 因加热、干燥或粘结而成的东西 <span class="encs">[crust]</span>。如:盐巴;泥巴;锅巴</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古族名;古代国名，辖境在今四川省东部 <span class="encs">[Ba]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">西南有巴国。——<span class="smcs">《山海经·海内经》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“今三巴是。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">巴人以比翼鸟。——<span class="smcs">《周书·王会》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>西有巴戎。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·彊国》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“巴在西南，戎在西，皆隶属。”</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 古时这里出产巴蛇，因此周朝分封在该地区的诸侯国叫<span class="yhcs">“巴子国”</span>。秦惠文王灭巴后，改置巴、蜀、汉中三郡</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 压强的单位，等于10 5 帕，或10 5 牛顿/米 2 ，或10 6 达因/厘米 2 (0.986923标准大气压) <span class="encs">[bar]</span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>巴</strong> <span class="dicpy">bā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bā.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 盼，期待着愿望的实现 <span class="encs">[wait anxiously]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">暗潮巴到无人会，只有篙师识水痕。——</span><span class="diczx2">杨万里</span><span class="smcs">《过沙头》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:巴得(盼望)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 紧紧贴在 <span class="encs">[cling to]</span>。如:爬山虎巴在墙上</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 粘结在…上 <span class="encs">[stick to]</span>。如:粥巴锅了</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 靠近;贴近 <span class="encs">[be close to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前不巴村，后不着店，怎生是好?——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">王晔</span><span class="smcs">《桃花女》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">我只道是谁，巴着窗户眼儿一瞧，原来</span><span class="diczx2">宝妹妹</span>坐在炕沿上。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 爬，攀登 <span
