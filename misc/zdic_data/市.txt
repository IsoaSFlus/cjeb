                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">市</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>市</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>做买卖或做买卖的地方：开～。菜～。～井（街，市场）。～曹。～侩（旧指买卖的中间人、唯利是图的奸商；现泛指贪图私利的人）。</li>
	<li>买：～义。～恩（买好，讨好）。</li>
	<li>人口密集的行政中心或工商业、文化发达的地方：城～。都（<span class="dicpy">dū</span>）～。～镇。</li>
	<li>一种行政区划，有中央直辖和省（自治区）辖等：北京～。南京～。</li>
	<li>中国人民习惯使用的度量衡单位：～制。～尺。～斤。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">市</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>市</strong> <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形，上面是&ldquo;之&rdquo;(往)，下面是&ldquo;兮&rdquo;，表市场嘈杂声。本义:市场)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[market]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">市，买卖之所也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，古者</span><span class="diczx2">神农</span>作市，或曰 <span class="diczx2">祝融</span>也。</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">市者，货之准也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;乘马》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大市日昃而市，百族为主;朝市朝时而市，商贾为主;夕市夕时而市，贩夫贩妇为主。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司市》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">因井田以为市，故俗语曰市井。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;宣公十五年》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肆之市朝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">商贾皆欲藏于王之市。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">市南门外泥歇。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">置于市，贾十倍。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《卖柑者言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:市征(市场的税征);市曹(市中商店聚集的地方。即市场。引申为买卖官爵);市声(街市中的喧闹声);市人(集市或城中街道上的人);市吏(市官。管理市场的官吏);市刑(古代市场中的刑罚);市司(古代管理市场的官员)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 集镇;城镇;城市 <span class="encs">[town;city]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃与其徒日张&ldquo;屈&rdquo;字游</span><span class="diczx2">金陵</span>中。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">之</span><span class="diczx2">盱眙</span>市中为人说书，已能倾动其市人。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:市稍头(城镇接近农村的地方);市井之夫(市俗的人);市俗(普通人，一般人);市肆(市内店铺);市心(城镇中心地区);市语(行话。常指下层社会中用的隐语);市门(商店的门;城门);市口(市镇);市邑(市镇;城镇);市列(市场中的店铺);市中心;北京市;天津市</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 街市 <span class="encs">[street]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徇诸市。(徇:示众。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:市肆(市廛。街市上的店铺)</span></p>
