                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>曼</strong> <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。小篆字形，从又，冒声。又，手。从<span class="yhcs">“又”</span>与手的动作有关。本义:长)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[long-draw-out distant;prolonged]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曼，引也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孔曼且硕，万民是若。——<span class="smcs">《诗·鲁颂·閟宫》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">韩娥因曼声哀哭。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">侯同曼声之歌。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·泛论》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">娥眉曼睩。长发曼鬋。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·招魂》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曼寿。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·礼乐志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曼辞以自解。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·司马迁传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">路曼曼其修远兮，吾将上下而求索。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·离骚》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曼声歌之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:曼胡(无刃的长戟。一作镘胡);曼寿(长寿);曼啸(长啸);曼志(远大的志向);曼靡(形容乐声悠长而柔和);曼曼(形容距离远或时间长)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 柔美;细润 <span class="encs">[graceful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">秀曼都雅，一军惊视。——<span class="smcs">《新唐书·李光颜传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:曼睇(媚视，美盼);曼颊(细嫩的面颊);曼丽(柔媚美丽);曼妙(美艳;柔美);曼帛(细腻精美的丝帛);曼理(细腻的肌肤)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>曼</strong> <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 延长;拉长 <span class="encs">[lengthen]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">曼余目以流观兮。——<span class="smcs">《楚辞·九章·哀郢》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“无”</span>。没有 <span class="encs">[not have;there is not]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行有之也，病曼之也。——<span class="smcs">《法言·五百》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“蔓”</span>。蔓延 <span class="encs">[creep]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂至延曼</span><span class="diczx2">连州</span>。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·王莽传下》</span></p><p>◎ <strong>曼</strong> <span class="dicpy">màn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/màn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p>通<span class="yhcs">“蛮”</span>。古时对南方少数民族的泛称 <span class="encs">[Man nationalities]</span></p><p><span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/66FC.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>無販切，音萬。【說文】作曼。引也。从又冒聲。【玉篇】長也。【詩·魯頌】孔曼且碩。【傳】曼，長也。【箋】脩也，廣也。</p><p>又【前漢·司馬相如傳】鄭女曼姬。【註】曼者，言其色理曼澤也。【楚辭·天問】平脅曼膚。【註】曼，輕細也。</p><p>又【前漢·司馬遷傳】曼辭以自解。【註】如淳曰：曼，美也。</p><p>又【莊子·馬蹄篇】馬知介倪闉扼鷙曼詭銜竊轡。【註】曼，突也，言曲頸於扼以抵突也。</p><p>又【揚子·法言】周之人多行，秦之人多病。行有之也，病曼之也。【註】曼，無也。</p><p>又姓。【前漢·五行志】曼滿。【註】鄭大夫。</p><p>又【廣韻】母官切【集韻】【韻會】謨官切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音瞞。【博雅】曼曼，長也。【屈原·離騷】路曼曼其修遠兮。【註】曼，或作漫。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>莫半切，音幔。曼衍，無極也。【莊子·寓言篇】因以曼衍，所以窮年。</p><p>又【集韻】母伴切，音滿。曼漶，不分別貌。</p><p>又與蠻同。【公羊傳·昭十六年】楚子誘戎曼子殺之。【釋文】曼音蠻。二傳作戎蠻。</p><p>又【正韻】微夫切，音無。《揚子》聖人曼云。註：曼音無。○按《法言·重黎篇》曼訓無，不音無。</p><p>又【韻補】叶無沿切。【楚辭·大招】嫮目宜笑，蛾眉曼只。容則秀雅，稚朱顏只。魂兮歸來，靜以安只。顏，倪堅切。安，於虔切。</p>
