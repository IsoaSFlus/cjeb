						<p>● <strong>送</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">s&ograve;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄥˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>把东西从甲地运到乙地：～信。～审。输～。护～。呈～。</li>
	<li>赠给：～礼。赠～。雪中～炭。</li>
	<li>陪伴人到某一地点：欢～。～别。～亲。</li>
	<li>丢掉：断～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">送</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>送</strong> <span class="dicpy">s&ograve;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/s&ograve;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。小篆字形)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[accompany bride to bridegroom&#39;s family on wedding day]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">送，遣也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">送迎无礼。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;富国》</span>。注:&ldquo;致女也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:送嫁(送亲);送娘(方言。送新娘子);送房(结婚之夕，亲友陪送新郎入洞房);送亲(娘家人送出嫁的姑娘上轿或送新娘到男家)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 送行;送别 <span class="encs">[see sb.off wish sb.bon voyage]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寒雨连江夜入</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>，平明送客 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>山孤。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王昌龄</span><span class="smcs">《芙蓉楼送辛渐》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">太子及宾客知其事者，皆白衣冠以送之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;燕策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:送车(到车站送行;又指送行的车);送迎钱(迎送官员的开销);送任(送亲属去当人质);送迎(送往迎来);送路(送行;饯别);送故迎新(送旧官，迎新官;送旧岁，迎新年)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 将东西运去或传交 <span class="encs">[deliver;carry]</span>。又</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">录毕，走送之，不敢稍逾约。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">宋濂</span><span class="smcs">《送东阳马生序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赵王乃斋戒五日，使臣奉璧，拜送书于庭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">干戈日滋，行者赍，居者送，中外骚扰相奉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:送茶(送礼);送还(送给);送款(表示诚心诚意的投降);送风(传递消息，通风报信);送盏(递送酒器以劝饮);送语(传话);送意(传送情意);送怀(传达思想感情)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 遣送 <span class="encs">[send back;repatriate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">愬</span>以槛车送 <span class="diczx2">元济</span>诣京师。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;唐纪》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 送葬;送丧 <span class="encs">[take part in a funeral procession;attend a funeral]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丧出江上，白衣冠送者夹岸，酹而器者百里不绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《明史&middot;海瑞传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:送老(安葬老人或长辈);送横(为横死者送丧);送哭(哀哭而送葬)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 馈赠 <span class="encs">[give as a present]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 了结;断送 <span class="encs">[bring upon oneself]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 度过，排遣 <span class="encs">[spend]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:送老(排遣年老的时光);送春(送社。送别春天);送夏(送别夏天);送腊(辞送腊月)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 送神 <span class="encs">[send off the gods after the offering of sacrifices]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:送社(送春);送祟(巫师祛送鬼祟的仪式);送佛送到西天(帮人帮到底)</span></p>
