                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">湾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>湾</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">灣</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">wān  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> ㄨㄢˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>水流弯曲的地方：河～。</li>
	<li>海岸凹入陆地、便于停船的地方：海～。港～。</li>
	<li>使船停住：把船～住。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bay, cove, inlet, bend of stream</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bucht  (S, Geo)​,Flussbiegung  (S, Geo)​,Golf  (S, Geo)​,Meerbusen  (S, Geo)​,vertäuen, festmachen (eines Schiffes)​  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> golfe,baie,tournant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">湾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>湾</strong></p>
<p>灣 <span class="dicpy">wān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，弯声。本义:河水弯曲处)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[bend in a stream]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">湾，水曲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">潮来汹涌，水浸湾环。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:河湾;湾矶(弯曲水流中的石头);湾湾(水边湾曲处);湾头(水湾);湾埼(弯曲的水岸);湾澳(弯曲的水边);湾回(河水弯曲处);湾浦(水流弯曲的水滨);湾曲(水湾曲折处);湾山角络(偏僻的地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 海湾 <span class="encs">[bay;gulf]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:渤海湾;墨西哥湾</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 村庄 <span class="encs">[village]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:毛家湾</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>湾</strong></p>
<p>灣 <span class="dicpy">wān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>系泊，在水湾处停泊 <span class="encs">[moor]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">就桥下湾住船，上岸独步。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">冯梦龙</span><span class="smcs">《喻世明言》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:把船湾在那边</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>湾</strong></p>
<p>灣 <span class="dicpy">wān</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/wān.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈量〉</p>
<p>用于水或水面，相当于&ldquo;处&rdquo; <span class="encs">[place]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一湾斜照水，三版顺风船。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">钱起</span><span class="smcs">《江行无题一百首》</span></p>
