                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>祭</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>对死者表示追悼、敬意的仪式：～奠。～礼。～灵。～典。～扫。</li>
	<li>供奉鬼神或祖先：～祖。～天。～祀。～灶。</li>
	<li>使用（法宝）：～起一件法宝。</li>
</ol>

<p><span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄞˋ</span></p>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">祭</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>祭</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，左边是牲肉;左边是&ldquo;又&rdquo;(手);中间象祭桌。表示以手持肉祭祀神灵。古人杀牲，一是为自己吃，再就是常把牲肉放在祭台上，&ldquo;祭&rdquo;字就是有酒肉的祭祀，即牲祭。本义:祭祀)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[obit;offer a sacrifice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祭，祀也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祭之为言索也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说苑&middot;权谋》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祭者，荐其时也，荐其敬也，荐其美也，非享味也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;成公十七年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无牲而祭曰荐，荐而加牲曰祭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;桓公八年》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祭者，所以追养继孝也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭统》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祭于公，不宿肉。祭肉不出三日。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;乡党》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以灵鼓鼓社祭。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:公祭死难烈士;主祭;陪祭;祭扫(祭祀并扫墓)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 使用，旧小说中谓用咒语施放神秘武器 <span class="encs">[wield]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:祭起一件法宝来</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">zh&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>祭</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄐㄧˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>j&igrave; <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/3091.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">動</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">拜鬼神。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「祭祀」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《論語&middot;八佾》：「<b>祭</b>如在，祭神如神在。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">對死者致敬追思。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「祭弔」。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;張籍〈沒蕃故人〉詩：「欲<b>祭</b>疑君在，天涯哭此時。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">唸咒以施用法寶，常用於古小說中。</span><span class="gc_yy">《封神演義&middot;第五四回》：「不防土行孫<b>祭</b>起綑仙繩，一聲響，把哪吒平空拿了去。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">祭奠的儀式。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「家<b>祭</b>」、「公<b>祭</b>」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>
