						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>皮</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">pí  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pí.mp3"></a></span>  ㄆㄧˊ</span></p><ol><li>动植物体表的一层组织：～毛。</li><li>兽皮或皮毛的制成品：裘～。</li><li>包在外面的一层东西：封～。书～。</li><li>表面：地～。</li><li>薄片状的东西：豆腐～。</li><li>韧性大，不松脆：花生放～了。</li><li>不老实，淘气：顽～。</li><li>指橡胶：胶～。～球。</li><li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">皮</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>皮</strong> <span class="dicpy">pí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文字形上面是个口，表示兽的头;一竖表示身体;右边半圆表示已被揭起的皮;右下表手。<span class="yhcs">“皮”</span>是汉字部首之一。本义:用手剥兽皮)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[peel]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皮，剥取兽革者谓之皮。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皮，剥也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皮面抉眼。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·韩策》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皮瓠以为蓄。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:皮面</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 酥脆的东西变韧 <span class="encs">[become soft and soggy]</span>。如:花生放皮了;饼皮了</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>皮</strong> <span class="dicpy">pí</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/pí.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 兽皮 <span class="encs">[hide]</span>。带毛叫皮，去毛叫革。引申指人的皮肤或动植物表面的一层组织 <span class="encs">[skin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">孤执皮帛。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“虎豹皮。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">俪皮。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·士昏礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“鹿皮。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">事之以皮币。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">禽兽之皮足衣也。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:皮条(用皮子做成的绳、带);皮鼓(军用小鼓);皮松肉紧(喻指淡漠;宽泛);皮甲(用兽皮制的软甲);皮船(古时用牛皮蒙罩船身以御矢石的战船);皮胶(用动物皮熬成的粘性物质);皮舰(古代用牛皮蒙罩船身以防御矢石的战舰)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 皮毛;皮革 <span class="encs">[fur coat;leather]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岛夷皮服。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span>
