                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>誓</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕˋ</span></p><ol>    <li>当众或共同表示决心，依照说的话实行：～师。～词。～愿。～死。</li>    <li>表示决心的话：发～。宣～。立～。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">誓</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>誓</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，折声。本义:发誓;立誓)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[swear]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">誓，以言约束也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“凡自表不食言之辞皆曰誓，亦约束之意也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">约信曰誓。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">予誓，告汝誓命。——<span class="smcs">《书·甘誓》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“要信也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">信誓旦旦，不思其反。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">司射西面誓之。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射仪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹告也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">而誓之曰:<span class="yhcs">“不及黄泉，无相见也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·隐公元年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">终待说山盟海誓。——</span><span class="diczx2">赵长卿</span><span class="smcs">《贺新郎》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不久当还归，誓天不相负。——<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏·古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为誓与城为殉。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">全祖望</span><span class="smcs">《梅花岭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">至于誓天断发，泣下沾襟，何其衰也。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《新五代史·伶官传·序》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:誓志(发誓立志);誓死不贰(发誓至死不变心);誓辞(立誓的言辞);誓戒(誓约警戒);誓剑(对着剑发誓;皇帝赠与出巡大臣的宝剑，即后世所称的“上方宝剑”</span>);誓书铁券(即<span class="yhcs">“丹书铁券”</span>。古代帝王颁赐功臣的享有免罪等特权的证件)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 接受爵位 <span class="encs">[accept a rank of nobility]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凡诸侯之适子，誓于天下，摄其君，则下其君之礼一等。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 告诫;告知 <span class="encs">[warn;tell]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">司射西面誓之曰:<span class="yhcs">“公射大候，大夫射参，士射干。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·大射仪》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹告也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">惟君有黼裘以誓省。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·玉藻》</span>。疏:<span class="yhcs">“告勑也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[五戒]</span>一日誓，用之于军旅。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·秋官·士师》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:誓戒(约束警戒);誓民(告戒吏民);誓众(告诫众人)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 铭刻;牢记 <span class="encs">[engrave on one's mind;keep firmly in mind]</span>。如:誓肌(刻骨铭心)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>誓</strong> <span class="dicpy">shì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span
