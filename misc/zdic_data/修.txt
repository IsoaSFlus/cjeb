                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">修</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>修</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xiū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄡ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>装饰，使完美：～饰。～辞。装～。</li>	<li>整治，恢复完美：～复。～治。～缮（修理）。～浚（修理疏通）。～好。～明（古代指政治清明）。</li>	<li>剪或削：～剪。</li>	<li>兴建，建造：～建。～筑。</li>	<li>编纂，撰写：～书。～史。～纂。</li>	<li>（学问，品行方面）钻研、学习、锻炼：～学。～业。～养。</li>	<li>长（<span class="dicpy">ch&aacute;ng </span>），高：～长。</li>	<li>信奉宗教的人虔诚地学习教义，并付诸行动：～行。～女。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">修</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>修</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从彡( <span class="dicpy">shān</span>)，攸( <span class="dicpy">yōu</span>)声。&ldquo;彡&rdquo;有装饰义。本义:修饰，装饰)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[embellish;decorate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修，饰也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">义之修而礼之藏也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span>。注:&ldquo;犹饰也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">美要眇兮宜修。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以修敬也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;廉颇蔺相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修容(整饰容貌);修词(修饰词句。亦指作文;文辞);修饰边幅(形容注意仪容、衣着祳恼);修娖(整理修补)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 整修;修理 <span class="encs">[repair]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">古不修墓。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;檀弓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修橹轒辒。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;谋攻》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修守战之具。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">吾妻死，室坏不修。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修娖(整理修补);修完补辑(修葺整治;补缀损缺);修月(古代传说月由七宝合成，人间常有八万二千户给它修治)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 兴建;建造 <span class="encs">[build]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">钟鼓不修。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋&middot;先已》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃重修</span><span class="diczx2">岳阳楼</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修堤梁，通沟浍。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修立(修建);修治(制作);修构(修建);修废(兴复废业);修营(修建);修辟(修整开垦)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> <span class="encs">[学问、品行方面]</span>学习、锻炼和培养 <span class="encs">[study]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修武之德。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;晋语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">束发修学。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;叙传下》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">一善易修。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《原毁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忌者畏人修。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">怠者不能修</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修省(提高道德修养，不断反省自己);修省退悔(修身反省，退而自悔);修己(自我修养)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 修行 <span class="encs">[practise Buddhism or Taoism]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:修积(行善积德);修服(指道教的修炼服气。服气，即吐纳);修持(持戒修行);修真(道教指学道修行为修真)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 整治 <span class="encs">[put in order;administer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">管子修之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;货殖列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">外结好</span><span class="diczx2">孙权</span>，内修政理。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修之于朝廷。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《答司马谏议书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修文偃武(修明文教，停止武备);修文(采取措施加强文治，主要指修治典章制度，提倡礼乐教化等);修近(整顿内务);修言(统一号令);修事(治理政事)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 实行;从事某种活动 <span class="encs">[practise;carry out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">地之不休。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;吴语》</span>。注:&ldquo;垦也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修其宗庙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;中庸》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓埽粪也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修全(补救;变通);修削(进攻;砍杀);修禊(古代习俗，在三月三日到水边游玩，以祓除不祥)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 编纂;撰写;写 <span class="encs">[compile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">始末修撰，凡十六载。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史&middot;序传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修撰(编辑又指掌修国史的官);修文郎(称阴曹掌著作之官);修札(写信)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 设。置备 <span class="encs">[be ready]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">修其簠簋。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span>。注:&ldquo;备也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修名(修刺。置备名帖，以作通报姓名之用);修馔(准备饭食)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 循;遵循 <span class="encs">[follow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以圣人不期修古，不法常可。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修今(拘守现状);修古(遵行古道);修本(犹溯源);修名(循名，依照名称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 通&ldquo;休&rdquo;。休整;休养 <span class="encs">[rest]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:修摄(保养;调护);修鳞养爪(喻保养、积蓄战斗力)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 修订，修改，改正 <span class="encs">[revise;amend]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲自修改。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;自新》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx2">邹忌</span>修八尺有余。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云髻峨峨，修眉联娟。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">曹植</span><span class="smcs">《洛神赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盖简桃核修狭者为之。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">巨身修尾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修龄(长龄，长寿);修短固天(生命的长短本由天定);修亘(连绵不断);修篁(修筠。长长的竹子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 高;大 <span class="encs">[tall;big]</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修茂(树木高而繁茂);修波(大波);修峻(高峻);修雅(高雅，不粗俗);修皙(身材高而皮肤白)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 远 <span class="encs">[distant]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:修阻(路途遥远而阻隔)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:修名(美好的名声);修直(高尚正直);修眸(漂亮的眼睛;美目);修禀(良好的禀赋)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>修</strong> <span class="dicpy">xiū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xiū.mp3"></a></span> </p>

<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 修正主义 <span class="encs">[revisionism]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:防修反修;帝、修、反</span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4FEE_229.svg" /><span class="swbf">(修)</span><span class="ywyy">飾也。</span><span class="swdz">巾部曰：飾者，㕞也。又部曰：㕞者，飾也。二篆爲轉注。飾卽今之拭字，拂拭之則發其光采，故引伸爲文飾。女部曰：妝者，飾也。用飾引伸之義。此云修飾也者，合本義引伸義而兼舉之。不去其塵垢，不可謂之修。不加以縟采，不可謂之修。修之从彡者，洒㕞之也。藻繪之也。修者，治也。引伸爲凡治之偁。匡衡曰：治性之道，必審己之所有餘，而強其所不足。</span><span class="ywyy">从彡攸聲。</span><span class="swdz">息流切，三部。經典多假肉部之脩。</span></p>                          </div>
