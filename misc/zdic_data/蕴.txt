                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>蕴</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">藴</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄣˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>积聚，蓄藏，包含：～蓄。～藏。～结（郁结）。～～（多指暑气郁积）。～含。～涵（①包含，蕴含；②判断中前后两个命题间存在的条件关系，表现形式是&ldquo;如果&hellip;&hellip;则&hellip;&hellip;&rdquo;）。～藉（<span class="dicpy">ji&egrave;</span>）（①宽和有涵容，如&ldquo;然少～～，不修威仪，亦以此见轻&rdquo;，亦作&ldquo;温藉&rdquo;、&ldquo;酝藉&rdquo;；②含蓄有余，含而不露，如&ldquo;风流～～&rdquo;）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">蕴</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蕴</strong></p>
<p>蘊 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，蕴( <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ) 声。本义:积聚，蓄藏)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[accumulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蕰，积也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span> 按：字亦作&ldquo;蕴&rdquo;。</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蘊，聚也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">芟夷蘊崇之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蓄而弗治将蘊。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而以是相蕴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;齐物论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我心蕴结兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;桧风&middot;素冠》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蕴利生孽，姑使无蕴乎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蕴积(蕴藏;积聚);蕴哲(蓄藏智慧);蕴艺(藏蓄的才华)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 包藏;包含 <span class="encs">[contain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万物尽然，而以是相蕴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蕴</strong></p>
<p>蘊 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 乱麻 <span class="encs">[coarse hemp]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1"><span class="encs">[里媪]</span> 即束蕴请火去妇之家，曰:&ldquo;吾犬争肉相杀，请火治之。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 佛教语意为荫覆 <span class="encs">[shadow]</span>。也译作&ldquo;阴&rdquo;，如佛经以色、受、想、行、藏为五阴，也作五蕴</p>
<p>◎ <strong>蕴</strong></p>
<p>蘊 <span class="dicpy">y&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 闷热 <span class="encs">[hot]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蕴隆(热气很盛);蕴暑(酷热)</span></p>
