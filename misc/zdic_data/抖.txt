                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>抖</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">dǒu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǒu.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄡˇ</span></p><ol>    <li>振动，甩动：～去身上的土。</li>    <li>哆嗦，战栗：发～。颤～。</li>    <li>〔～擞〕振作，振奋，如“～～精神”。</li>    <li>称人突然发迹而有钱有势（多含讥讽意）：这两年～起来了。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>抖</strong> <span class="dicpy">dǒu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dǒu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，斗声。本义:抖擞)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 振动;甩动 <span class="encs">[quiver;shake]</span>。如:抖了抖翅膀;抖搜(同<span class="yhcs">“抖擞”</span>。振作<span class="encs">[精神]</span>)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 颤动，哆嗦 <span class="encs">[shiver]</span>。如:吓得浑身乱抖;抖索索(哆嗦);抖抖瑟瑟(抖抖簌簌，抖抖擞擞，抖索。颤抖);抖搐(颤抖抽搐);发抖;浑身直抖</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 把隐讳的事情揭露出来 <span class="encs">[reveal;expose;bring to light]</span>。如:抖漏(揭露);抖落(揭露);抖风(走露风声)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 振作 <span class="encs">[enliven;rouse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">抖起精神</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 讽刺人突然得势或生活水平突然提高 <span class="encs">[get on in the world]</span>。如:抖落(挥霍而败落)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 讽刺或骂人耍威风 <span class="encs">[mock or scold to win over]</span>。如:抖威风;抖毛儿(野兽发威时往往抖动身上的毛，故骂人或开玩笑称人耍威风是抖毛儿);抖积伶儿(卖弄聪明)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖颤">抖颤</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖动">抖动</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖劲">抖劲</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖搂">抖搂</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖露">抖露</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖落">抖落</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖然">抖然</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖神">抖神</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖索">抖索</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖擞">抖擞</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖擞">抖擞</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖擞">抖擞</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/抖战">抖战</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">抖</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>抖</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄉㄡˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>dǒu <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/1063.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">颤动。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「发<b>抖</b>」、「颤<b>抖</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《儿女英雄传·第一三回》：「把个管狱的典史，登时吓得浑身乱<b>抖</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">振动。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>抖</b>落一身的灰尘。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《儿女英雄传·第四回》：「谁想那女子放下石头，把手上、身上的土，拍了拍<b>抖</b>了<b>抖</b>。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">振奋、鼓起。参见「<a href='/hans/抖擞' target=_blank>抖擞</a> 」条。</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">俗称得志、得意为「<b><b>抖</b></b>」。多含讽刺的意味。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「靠著逢迎拍马的技俩，没几年他便抖起来了。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《红楼梦·第六○回》：「你老把威风<b>抖</b>一<b>抖</b>，以后也好争别的礼。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">揭穿。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「事情抖开了，对大家都没有好处。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
