                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>嵩</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sōng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sōng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄙㄨㄥˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>〔～山〕山名，在中国河南省。</li>
	<li>高：～峦（高耸的峰峦）。～呼（亦称&ldquo;山呼&rdquo;）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> high, lofty; one of the 5 peaks, situated in Hunan</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> Mont Song (Henan)​,élevé</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>嵩</strong></p>
<p>崧 <span class="dicpy">sōng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sōng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>山高 <span class="encs">[high;lofty]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嵩，中岳嵩，高山也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文新附》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">嵩，高也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅&middot;释诂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山大而高曰嵩。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名&middot;释山》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">崧高维岳。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;崧高》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:嵩呼(祝颂皇帝，高呼万岁);嵩崇(高大);嵩峦(高峻的峰峦)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>◎ <strong>嵩</strong> <span class="dicpy">Sōng</span></p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>中国山名 <span class="encs">[Song mountain]</span>。古称&ldquo;中岳&rdquo;。在河南省登封县北。高峰有三:东为太室山(1，440米)，中为峻极山，西为少室山。如:嵩岳之妃(指嵩山神的夫人灵妃)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5D69.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕崇【玉篇】思融切。同崧。【說文】中嶽嵩高山也，从山高，指事。【白虎通】中嶽獨加高字，中央在四方之中可高，故曰嵩高。【後漢&middot;郡國志】穎川陽城有嵩高山。【註】禹貢有外方山，卽嵩也。【前漢&middot;武帝紀】翌日親登嵩高，御史乘屬在廟旁。吏卒咸聞呼萬歲者三。</p>
<p>又姓。　【正譌】嵩古作崇。【韋昭國語註】通用崈。【正字通】按崇崈音義同，不當合崇嵩爲一。互見前崧字註。</p>
