                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">视</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>视</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">視</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄕˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>看：～觉。～力。～野。鄙～。注～。近～。～而不见。熟～无睹。</li>
	<li>亲临某事：～事。～察。</li>
	<li>看待：藐～。重～。等闲～之。</li>
	<li>看望：探～。省（<span class="dicpy">xǐng </span>）～。</li>
	<li>比照：&ldquo;天子之卿受地～侯&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/示">示</a>&rdquo;，表明。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> look at, inspect, observe, see</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anschauen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> vue,vision,regarder,voir,regarder attentivement,inspecter,considérer</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">视</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>视</strong></p>
<p>視、眡、眎 <span class="dicpy">sh&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/sh&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意兼形声。从见示，示亦声。见，看见。示，表现。本义:看)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[look at]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视，瞻也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天视自我民视，天听自我民听。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;泰誓中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">目不能徧视，手不能徧操。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;辞过》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子兴视夜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;子曰鸡鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视而不见，听而不闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;大学》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">熟视之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然往来视之，觉无异能者。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">撤屏视之，一人、一桌、一椅、一扇、一抚尺而已。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《虞初新志&middot;秋声诗自序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">穆公</span>使视客馆，则束载、厉兵、秣马矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;僖公三十三年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:视探(看望，探望);视瞻(顾盼的目光);视日(观日影而察早晚时刻;注视太阳);视远步高(高视阔步);视习(见习)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 考察，察看，审察 <span class="encs">[inspect]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视其所以。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;为政》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">下视其辙，登轼而望之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窥镜而自视，又弗如远甚。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:视师(视察军队);视草(检视天子所写文词诏令的草稿);视药(查看汤药);视荫(观察日影)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 治理，处理 <span class="encs">[administer;treat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">崔子</span>称疾不视事。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十五年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:视朝(天子朝临治理政事);视篆(古代官印皆用篆文，故官吏上任治事称&ldquo;视篆&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 看待 <span class="encs">[regard;look upon]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">弃其妇，致郁郁死，是芋视乃妇也。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子孙视之不甚惜，举以予人，如弃草芥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">苏洵</span><span class="smcs">《六国论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">然言其户，则视三十年以前增五倍焉。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">洪亮吉</span><span class="smcs">《治平篇》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:视遇(看待，照顾);视同膜外(视若无睹;漠不关心);视民如伤(将百姓的疾苦看作自己的伤痛);视为同路人;视伟(看重)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 通&ldquo;示&rdquo;。向&hellip;表示 <span class="encs">[show]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">指视我。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;周勃传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视民不奢。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;晁错传》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">师古</span>曰:&ldquo;视读曰示。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">视民不恌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;鹿鸣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">亦视</span><span class="diczx2">项羽</span>无东意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:视化(显示教化。视，同&ldquo;示&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 比照 <span class="encs">[compare]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">环顾国内，贼氛方炽，杌陧之象，视清季有加。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">孙文</span><span class="smcs">《黄花冈七十二烈士事略序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:视秩(比照品级次第)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 效法 <span class="encs">[imit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王懋乃德，视乃厥祖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;太甲中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:视效(仿效;效法)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 接纳 <span class="encs">[admit]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故君子于有馈者，弗能见，则不视其馈。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;坊记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 照顾，照看 <span class="encs">[care for]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:视护(照看护理);视濯(古代祭祀时照料洗濯祭器);视寝(侍寝);视养(照料养育);视候(看望，问安)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 发令 <span class="encs">[give order]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:视师(督率军旅);视撝(指挥。视与指通)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“视”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/視">視</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=見" target="_blank">見</a>】
                              視·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：5</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8996.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25119.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥄙</span>眡<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2511A.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥄚</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2516D.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥅭</span><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25259.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥉙</span>眎<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/242C7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤋇</span>【集韻】時利切【韻會】是義切【正韻】時吏切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音嗜。【說文】瞻也。【博雅】明也。【易&middot;履卦】視履考祥。【書&middot;大甲】視遠惟明。</p>
<p>又【字彙】看待也。【左傳&middot;成三年】鄭賈人如晉，荀罃善視之。</p>
<p>又【博雅】效也。【書&middot;大甲】視乃厥祖。【疏】言當法視其祖而行之。</p>
<p>又【小爾雅】比也。【左傳&middot;襄二十七年】季武子使謂叔孫以公命，曰：視邾滕。【註】欲比小國。【禮&middot;檀弓】公室視豐<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/254D3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥓓</span>。【疏】言視者不正，相當比擬之辭也。</p>
<p>又猶納也。【禮&middot;坊記】君子於有饋者弗能見，則不視其饋。【註】不視，猶不納也。</p>
<p>又猶敎也。【儀禮&middot;鄕射禮】命釋獲者設中，遂視之。【註】視之，當敎之。</p>
<p>又【釋名】視，是也，察是非也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;曲禮】兔曰明視。【疏】兔肥，則目開而視明也。</p>
<p>又【儀禮&middot;士虞禮】明齊溲酒。【鄭註】明齊，當爲明視，兔腊也。</p>
<p>又【山海經】狄山有視肉。【註】聚肉，形如牛肝，有兩目也。食之無盡，尋復更生如故。</p>
<p>又山水名。【山海經】帝囷山東南五十里曰視山。【又】葴山，視水出焉。【註】或曰視宜爲瀙。瀙水今在南陽。</p>
<p>又人名。【陶潛&middot;羣輔錄】伏羲六佐，其一曰視默，主災惡。</p>
<p>又姓，見【姓苑】。</p>
<p>又通作示。【詩&middot;小雅】視民不恌。【箋】視，古示字。【前漢&middot;高帝紀】視項羽無東意。【史記】作示。【師古註】漢書多以視爲示，古字通用。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】承矢切【集韻】【韻會】善旨切【正韻】善指切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>嗜上聲。義同。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】看也。【書&middot;洪範】五事，二曰視。【疏】視，常止反。</p>
<p>又【詩&middot;小雅】小人所視。叶上矢履。</p>
