						<p>● <strong>赉</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">賚</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄞˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>赐予，给予：～赏。～赐。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> give, present, confer; surname</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> verleihen ,Lai  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> octroyer,accorder</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>赉</strong></p>
<p>賚 <span class="dicpy">l&agrave;i</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&agrave;i.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，来声。本义:赏赐)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[award]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赉，赐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">予其大赉汝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君&middot;汤誓》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赉，大封于庙也。赉，予也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;赉序》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">徂赉孝孙。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">赉我思成。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;商颂&middot;烈祖》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">周有大赉，善人是富。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">抚军亦厚赉</span><span class="diczx2">成名</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赉假(古代帝王下诏赐给假期);赉赏(赏赐);赉奖(奖赏，赏赐);赉赐(赏赐);赉功(赏赐有功者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 赠送 <span class="encs">[give as a present;present as a gift]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:赉赠(赠送);赉赙(以财物助人办丧事)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
