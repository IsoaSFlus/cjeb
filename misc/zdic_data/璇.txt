						<p>● <strong>璇</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄩㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>美玉：～宫（旧指帝王后妃住处）。～玑（①古代天文仪器；②古代称北斗星的第一星至第四星）。～闺（旧指华丽的闺房）。～室。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">璇</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>璇</strong></p>
<p>璿 <span class="dicpy">xu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从玉，旋声。本义:美玉)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[beautiful jade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">璿，美玉也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天子之瑶:玉果、璇、珠、烛银、黄金之膏。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">桀</span>作璇室、象廊， <span class="diczx2">纣</span>为倾官、鹿台。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;杨阜传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:璇室(璇室。玉饰的宫室。一说能旋转的宫室;亦指传说中仙人的居所);璇花(如玉的白花。比喻雪花)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 次于玉的石头 <span class="encs">[stone inferior to jade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">璇，美石次玉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若不能用玉，可用白璇珠。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:;璇玉(美玉。一说次于玉的美石)璇源(琁源。产珠的水流;亦指皇族)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 北斗七星的第二星 <span class="encs">[the second star in the bowl of the Big Dipper]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:璇柄(北斗七星的斗柄);璇星(星名。北斗第二星);璇枢(琁枢。星名。北斗第一星为枢，第二星为璇。泛指北斗星;又比喻枢纽，关键)</span></p>

<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">美玉。</span><span class="gc_yy">《山海經&middot;大荒西經》：「爰有甘華、甘柤、白柳、視肉、三騅、<b>璇</b>瑰。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">星名。北斗七星的第二顆星。</span><span class="gc_yy">《宋史&middot;卷四九&middot;天文志二》：「魁第一星曰天樞，&hellip;&hellip;二曰<b>璇</b>，法星，主地。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>

                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“璇”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/璿">璿</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷一">卷一</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷一&amp;swbs=玉">玉</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/74BF.svg">                          <p>美玉也。从玉睿聲。《春秋傳》曰：璿弁玉纓。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/74BF_1.svg" /><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24A45.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤩅</span>，古文璿。<img class="swtimg" src="//img.zdic.net/swxz/74BF_2.svg" />叡，籒文璿。似沿切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74BF_4005.svg" /><span class="swbf">(璿)</span><span class="ywyy">美玉也。</span><span class="swdz">山海經：西王母之山，有璿瑰瑤碧。郭傳：璿瑰，玉名。竹書穆天子傳：重<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/22004.svg"><span class="k_txt">𢀄</span>氏之所守曰枝斯璿瑰。郭注：璿瑰，玉名。引左傳：贈我以璿瑰。按左傳成公十八年。今本作瓊瑰。僖公廿八年璿弁，今本作瓊弁。張守節史記璿璣作瓊璣。璿與瓊，古書多相亂。璿瑰，郭音旋回，合二字爲美玉名。山海經單言琁者，亦美玉也。</span><span class="ywyy">从王睿聲。</span><span class="swdz">似沿切。十四部。</span><span class="ywyy">春秋傳曰：璿弁玉纓。</span><span class="swdz">見上。張衡西京賦：璿弁玉纓。薛敬文解：弁，馬冠也，又髦以璿玉作之。纓，馬鞅也，以玉飾之。鍇本弁作冠，諱李昪嫌名也。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74BF_4006.svg" /><span class="swbf">(琁)</span><span class="ywyy">璿或从旋省。</span><span class="swdz">各本廁瓊璚瓗三字之下，解云：瓊或从旋省。考文<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28556.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨕖</span>陶徵士誄：璿玉致美。李善注曰：山海經云：升山，黃酸之水出焉，其中多琁玉。說文云：琁亦璿字。李氏以琁注璿，引說文爲證，然則李所據說文，不同今本，今據以訂正。楊倞注孫卿引說文：琁，赤玉，音瓊。則同今本矣。中山經、海內西經言琁者皆美玉也。郭云：琁，石次玉者也。又云：琁，玉類。又云：璿瑰亦玉名。是未知璿、琁同字矣。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74BF_4007.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24AC0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤫀</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文璿。</span><span class="swdz"><img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239BC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𣦼</span>部曰：壡，籒文叡。疑此籒當作<img class="lazy" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74BF_1.png" src="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74BF_1.png">，壡聲。小篆叡字省大篆爲之也。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/74BF_4008.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24A45.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤩅</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">古文璿。</span><span class="swdz">疑當古文作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24A45.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤩅</span>，小篆作璿。</span></p>                          </div>
