                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>陪</strong> <span class="dicpy">péi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/péi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从阜( <span class="dicpy">fù</span>)，咅( <span class="dicpy">pǒu</span>)声。左耳旁为<span class="yhcs">“阜”</span>的变形。阜，土山。本义:重叠的土堆)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mound]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陪，重土也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">朱骏声</span>通训:<span class="yhcs">“按，重阜也，所谓再成邱也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">分之土田陪敦，陪犹山也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·定公四年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“增也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 辅佐之臣，公卿之臣 <span class="encs">[subject;assistant]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陪臣也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:陪贰(副手，助手);陪推(以推官出任主考官的副官)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>陪</strong> <span class="dicpy">péi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/péi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 伴随，陪伴 <span class="encs">[accompany]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以无陪无卿。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·荡》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“无陪贰也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自称曰陪臣某。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·曲礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“重也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">陪臣敢辞。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公十二年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“重也。诸侯之臣于天子故曰陪臣。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">士有陪乘。——<span class="smcs">《国语·鲁语》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“犹重也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">行则陪乘。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·齐右》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:奉陪(敬辞，陪伴);陪位(在席位上奉陪);陪京(陪侍京都，陪都);陪乘(伴随主车的车子);陪床的(陪房，随嫁仆婢);陪花(陪酒女郎);陪奉(陪同侍奉)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 增加 <span class="encs">[increase]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">飱有陪鼎。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公五年》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“陪，加也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:陪敦(增厚);陪鼎(增加的食鼎。鼎，古代用来盛食物的器具)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 辅佐;帮助 <span class="encs">[assist;help]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">秉德以陪朕。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·文帝纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:陪堂(帮闲，帮人做事的食客)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 同<span class="yhcs">“赔”</span>。赔偿，偿还 <span class="encs">[compensate;indemnify]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在放牧处相觝，请陪半价。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《判题》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 又如:陪告(赔话，解释说明)</p><p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“倍”</span>。违背 <span class="encs">[violate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尔德不明，以无陪无侧。——<span class="smcs">《韩诗外传》</span></span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span
