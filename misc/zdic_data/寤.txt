                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>寤</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄨˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>睡醒：&ldquo;七日而～&rdquo;。～寐以求。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/悟">悟</a>&rdquo;，理解，明白。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/牾">牾</a>&rdquo;，逆。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> few, scarce; empty, deserted</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> s'éveiller,réaliser,prendre conscience</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>寤</strong> <span class="dicpy">w&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/w&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wake up]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寤，寐觉而有言曰寤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">窈窕淑女，寤寐求之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;周南&middot;关雎》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:寤言(醒后说话);寤寐(指醒时和睡时);寤宿(醒而卧;躺卧);寤怀(睡不着而怀念);寤辟(醒来以手拍胸);寤叹(睡不着而叹息);寤迁(刚刚醒来就迁移);寤觉(睡醒)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 通&ldquo;悟&rdquo;。觉悟，认识到 <span class="encs">[realize]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">静言思之，寤擗有摽。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;柏舟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">而心尚未寤也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;李斯列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">身死东城，尚不觉寤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;陈胜项籍传赞》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">欲一言而寤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;要略》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">哲王又不寤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">别为<span class="smcs">《音图》</span>，用祛未寤。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《尔雅序》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:寤然(醒悟貌);寤移(使觉悟而转变)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;晤&rdquo;。见面 <span class="encs">[meet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:寤言(相会而对话)</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=宀" target="_blank">宀</a>】
                              寤·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=14" target="_blank">14</a>　·部外筆画：11</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5BE4.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>五故切，音誤。【說文】寐覺而有言曰寤。从㝱省，吾聲。一曰晝見而夜㝱也。【周禮&middot;秋官】有司寤氏。【註】寤，覺也，主夜覺者。</p><p>又【春官&middot;大卜疏】精神寤見，覺而占之。</p><p>又【左傳&middot;隱元年】莊公寤生，驚姜氏。【註】遌生也。遌，逆也。杜預曰：寤寐而莊公生。【風俗通】凡兒墮地，能開目視者，謂之寤生。</p><p>又與悟通。【爾雅序】別爲音圖，用祛未寤。【註】用此音圖，以祛除未曉寤者也。孔伋曰：吾終日思而未之得，於學則寤焉。</p><p>又【說文】一作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21A02.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡨂</span>。【正字通】俗作窹，非。窹，竈名。</p><p>又<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/259DD.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥧝</span>，同窹。</p>

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5BE4_1499.svg" /><span class="swbf">(寤)</span><span class="ywyy">寐覺而有言曰寤。</span><span class="swdz">有言，今鍇本作省信，鉉本作有信，皆誤，今依韵會所據鍇本。釋玄應引倉頡篇：覺而有言曰寤。左傳季寤字子言是其證。按周南毛傳曰：窹，覺也。衛風鄭箋同，言其大略而巳。鄭釋周禮寤夢云：覺時道之而夢。亦與倉頡篇同也。古書多叚寤爲悟。</span><span class="ywyy">从㝱省，吾聲。</span><span class="swdz">五故切。五部。</span><span class="ywyy">一曰晝見而夜㝱也。</span><span class="swdz">葢亦周禮寤夢之說。陳風傳云：晤，遇也。箋云：晤，對也。寤與晤義相通。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5BE4_1500.svg" /><span class="swbf">(<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21B11.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡬑</span>)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文寤。</span><span class="swdz">周禮占夢釋文云：寤本又作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2712F.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧄯</span>。此用籒文而小變也。</span></p>                          </div>
