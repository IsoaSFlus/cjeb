                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>笨</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&egrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>不聪明：～人。～拙。～伯（➊傻瓜，愚蠢的人；➋肥大的人）。愚～。</li>
	<li>不灵巧：嘴～。～口拙舌。～鸟先飞。</li>
	<li>粗重，费力气的：～重。～活。粗～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> foolish, stupid, dull; awkward</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> dumm</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> idiot,bête,maladroit,lourd,pesant</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">笨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>笨</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从竹，本声。本义:竹里。指竹的内表面) 竹子的内层。通称&ldquo;竹黄&rdquo; <span class="encs">[inner layer of bamboo]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笨，竹里也。从竹，本声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">徐锴</span>系传:&ldquo;笨，竹白也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">竹其表曰笢，其里曰笨，谓中之白质者也。其白如纸，可手揭者，谓之竹孚俞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅&middot;释草》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>笨</strong> <span class="dicpy">b&egrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&egrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 笨重，拙劣，粗大沉重 <span class="encs">[cumbersome;unwieldy]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:笨车(粗陋而不加装饰之车);笨窳(笨重粗劣);笨胶(质量较差的粗胶)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 智力差，愚笨 <span class="encs">[stupid;foolish;dull]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:笨头笨脑;笨人(愚蠢的人);笨汉(干粗活的人);笨牛(愚笨如牛)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 不灵巧;不灵活 <span class="encs">[clumsy;awkward]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:笨作(作事刻板，不变通);笨贼(笨蛋)</span></p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>笨</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄅㄣˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>b&egrave;n <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/0136.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">理解能力或記憶能力不佳。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「笨蛋」、「愚<b>笨</b>」、「短短的一首詩都背不熟，真<b>笨</b>！」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">不靈巧、不靈活。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「笨手笨腳」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《兒女英雄傳&middot;第一三回》：「我的嘴<b>笨</b>，也說不上個甚麼來。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">龐大沉重。</span><span class="gc_yy">《清史稿&middot;卷一四九&middot;交通志一》：「有鐵路，則機器可入，<b>笨</b>貨可出。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《兒女英雄傳&middot;第一三回》：「有粗<b>笨</b>活，只管交給我。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
