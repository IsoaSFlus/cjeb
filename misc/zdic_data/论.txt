                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>论</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">論</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄣˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>分析判断事物的道理：～断。～点。～辩。～据。～者。议～。讨～。辩～。</li>
	<li>分析阐明事物道理的文章、理论和言论：理～。舆～。专～。社～。</li>
	<li>学说，有系统的主张：系统～。</li>
	<li>看待：一概而～。</li>
	<li>衡量，评定：～罪。～功行赏。</li>
	<li>按照：～件。～资排辈。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>论</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">論</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄨㄣˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/伦">伦</a>&rdquo;，条理。</li>
	<li>〔～语〕中国古书名，内容主要是记载孔子及其门人的言行。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/抡">抡</a>&rdquo;，挑选。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> debate; discuss; discourse</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Abhandlung, wiss. Arbeit, Beitrag  (S)​,Meinung, Auffassung  (S)​,Theorie, (wiss., phil., weltanschl.)​ Position  (S)​,analysieren, bewerten  (V)​,Lun  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> (abrév. de LunYu, signifiant les Entretiens ou Analectes -de Confucius-)​,essai,théorie,doctrine,traiter,disserter de,discourir</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">论</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>论</strong></p>
<p>論 <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> <span class="smcs">《论语》</span>的简称 <span class="encs">[Analects of Confucius]</span>。内容主要是记录孔子及其门徒的言行</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><strong>常用词组</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/论语">论语</a></span></div>　
<p><strong>基本词义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>论</strong></p>
<p>論 <span class="dicpy">l&ugrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&ugrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，仑( <span class="dicpy">l&uacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&uacute;n.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:评论，研究。引申为&ldquo;议论&rdquo;)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 议论， 分析和说明事理 <span class="encs">[discuss;talk about]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">论，议也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">论理也，次也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语序集解》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坐而论道。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;考工记》</span>。注:&ldquo;谓谋虑治国之政令也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">臣请论其故。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;张仪传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">留连论诗。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">胡仔</span><span class="smcs">《苕溪渔隐丛话》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与</span><span class="diczx2">蒙</span>论议。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴&middot;汉纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">每与臣论此事。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">诸葛亮</span><span class="smcs">《出师表》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十几人论议。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;平原君虞卿列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:论道(讲论治国道术);论黄数黑(议论是非曲直);论冒(相当于八股文中的&ldquo;破题&rdquo;，有&ldquo;开宗明义&rdquo;之意)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 思;思考 <span class="encs">[think carefully; ponder over]</span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“论”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/論">論</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              論·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=15" target="_blank">15</a>　·部外筆画：8</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8AD6.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>盧昆切，音崘。【說文】議也。【廣韻】說也。【周禮&middot;春官&middot;大司樂&middot;賈疏】直言曰論，答難曰語。論者，語中之別，與言不同。</p>
<p>又【論語&middot;序解正義】論者，綸也，輪也，理也，次也，撰也。以此書可經綸世務，故曰綸，圓轉無窮故曰輪，蘊含萬理故曰理，篇章有序故曰次，羣賢集定故曰撰。【文心雕龍】昔仲尼微言，門人追述，故仰其經目，稱爲論語，蓋羣論立名，始于兹矣。</p>
<p>又【釋名】倫也，有倫理也。【玉篇】思理也。【詩&middot;大雅】於論鼓鐘。【傳】思也。【鄭箋】論之言倫也。【朱傳】言得其倫理也。</p>
<p>又【增韻】紬繹討論也。【孔安國&middot;尚書序】討論典墳。</p>
<p>又【正韻】決罪曰論。</p>
<p>又官名。【金史&middot;百官志】其官長皆稱曰勃極烈，次曰國論、忽魯、勃極烈。國論，言貴。忽魯，猶總帥也。</p>
<p>又諸羌州名。【唐書&middot;地理志】劒南道有論川州，開元後置。</p>
<p>又姓。【唐書】論弓仁，本吐蕃族也。</p>
<p>又三字姓。【金史&middot;國語解&middot;姓氏】烏古論曰商。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】力迍切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】龍春切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音倫。言有理也。</p>
<p>又【禮&middot;王制】凡制五<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>，必卽天論。【註】天論，天理也。與倫同。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】力困切【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】盧困切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>崘去聲。義同。</p>
<p>又【正韻】辨論也。【書&middot;周官】論道經邦。【傳】論者，講明之謂。【禮&middot;王制】凡官民材，必先論之。【註】謂考評其行藝之詳也。【史記&middot;蕭相國世家】論功行封。</p>
<p>又叶盧健切，音練。【前漢&middot;敘傳】兵家之策，惟在不戰。營平皤皤，立功立論。</p>
<p>又叶聖閏切，音舜。【馮衍&middot;顯志賦】澄德化之陵遲兮，烈<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛬</span>罰之峭峻。燔商鞅之法術兮，燒韓非之說論。</p>
<p>又叶閭員切，音攣。【曹植&middot;文帝誄】考諸先紀，尋之哲言。生若浮寄，惟德可論。　【字彙補】譌作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2041C.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠐜</span>，非。</p>

                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/8AD6_6309.svg" /><span class="swbf">(論)</span><span class="ywyy">議也。</span><span class="swdz">論以侖會意。亼部曰：侖，思也。龠部曰：侖，理也。此非㒳義。思如玉部䚡理，自外可以知中之䚡。靈臺：於論<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/21537.svg"><span class="k_txt">𡔷</span>鍾。毛曰：論，思也。此正許所本。詩於論正侖之假借。凡言語循其理，得其宜謂之論。故孔門師弟子之言謂之論語。皇侃依俗分去聲、平聲異其解，不知古無異義，亦無平、去之别也。王制：凡制五㓝，必卽天論。周易：君子以經論。中庸：經論天下之大經。皆謂言之有倫有脊者。許云：論者，議也。議者，語也。似未<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/275CA.svg"><span class="k_txt">𧗊</span>。</span><span class="ywyy">从言侖聲。</span><span class="swdz">當云从言侖，侖亦聲。盧昆切。十三部。</span></p>                          </div>
