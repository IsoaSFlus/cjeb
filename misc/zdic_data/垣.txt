						<p>● <strong>垣</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄩㄢˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>矮墙，墙：短～。城～。墙～。</li>	<li>城：省～（省城）。</li>	<li>官署：谏～（谏官官署）。</li>	<li>星的区域，古代把众星分为上、中、下三垣。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">垣</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>垣</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从土，亘( <span class="dicpy">xuān</span>)声。本义:矮墙，也泛指墙)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wall]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">垣，墙也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既勤垣墉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;梓材》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">马</span>注:&ldquo;卑曰垣，高曰墉。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">周垣之高八尺。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;备城门》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尽毁其馆之垣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公三十一年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为家不治垣产。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;萧何传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:垣衣(生在墙垣上的苔);垣堵(墙);垣屋(有围墙的房室;围墙和房屋)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 城墉 <span class="encs">[wall]</span>。后叫省城为省垣</p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 官署的代称 <span class="encs">[government]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">花隐掖垣暮，啾啾栖鸟过。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《春宿左省诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>垣</strong> <span class="dicpy">yu&aacute;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yu&aacute;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 筑墙围绕 <span class="encs">[build a wall around sth.]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天云垣其既立兮，家愿得乎双逞。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中" target="_blank">丑集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=丑集中&amp;bs=土" target="_blank">土</a>】
                              垣·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=9" target="_blank">9</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/57A3.svg">						  <p>〔古文〕<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/29AE7.svg"><span class="k_txt">𩫧</span>【唐韻】雨元切【集韻】【韻會】于元切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音袁。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>曰垣，高曰墉牆也。【釋名】援也。人所依阻，以爲援衞也。【詩&middot;大雅】大師維垣。【左傳&middot;襄三十一年】子產盡壞其館之垣，而納車馬焉。</p><p>又星有上中下三垣。【史記&middot;天官書】上垣太微宮垣十星，東垣北上相，名左掖門，西垣北上將，名右掖門。【唐書&middot;權德輿傳】左右掖垣，承天子誥命，中垣紫微宮垣十五星，左右掖與太微垣同。【湘山野錄】藝祖居潛日，爲趙普飮，居席左。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>搏怒曰：紫微垣一小星，居上次，可乎。斥之，使居帝右。下垣天市宮垣二十二星。【癸辛雜誌】揚州分野，正値天市垣，所以人多好市。</p><p>又古邑名。【一統志】垣卽周召分陝處，宋攺曰垣曲。</p><p>又東垣，秦縣。【史記&middot;高帝紀】代相<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg"><span class="k_txt">𨻰</span>豨反，帝親征，豨將以東垣降，因攺曰眞定。</p><p>又玉名。【騈雅】嬰垣，美玉。【山海經】羭次之山，其陽多嬰垣之玉。</p><p>又姓。漢西河太守垣恭。</p><p>又【集韻】胡官切，音桓。義同。亦作<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25963.svg"><span class="k_txt">𥥣</span>。</p><p>又【易林】噂噂所言，莫如我垣。歡嘉堅固，可以長安。◎按言垣本元韻，安字宜叶音鴛。《正字通》垣攺音延，安音煙，則言字錯入先韻矣。</p><p>又叶夷然切，音延。【劉楨&middot;贈徐幹詩】誰謂相去遠，隔此西掖垣。拘限淸切禁，中情無由宣。</p>
