                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">戚</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>戚</strong> <span class="dicpy">qī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从戉，尗声。戉:( <span class="dicpy">yuè</span>)，斧子。本义:古兵器名，斧的一种)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。亦用为舞具 <span class="encs">[a kind of axe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">戚，戉也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">干戈戚扬。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·公刘》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">朱干玉戚。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·明堂位》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">执干戚舞。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:戚扬(斧钺)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 亲属，亲戚 <span class="encs">[relative]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何谓六戚?父母兄弟妻子。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:;戚好(亲戚友好);戚里(戚畹。帝王外戚所居住的地方);戚属(母及妻的亲属);戚爱(亲属和宠爱的人);戚家(贵戚之家;亲戚家);外戚(指帝王的母族、妻族);戚宠(外戚亲贵)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>戚</strong> <span class="dicpy">qī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/qī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 忧愁;悲伤。通<span class="yhcs">“慽”</span>、<span class="yhcs">“慼”</span> <span class="encs">[sorrowful;sad]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心之忧矣，自始伊戚。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·小明》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">貌若甚戚者。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">蒋氏大戚。</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">婉贞</span>独戚然。—— <span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">汝又虑戚吾心。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《祭妹文》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:戚容(愁容);戚切(悲切);戚休(休戚。忧愁和欢乐);戚欣(忧愁与欢乐);戚言(忧戚的言词);戚嗟(忧伤嗟叹);戚意(忧伤的心情);戚惨(悲伤);戚忧(忧伤);戚颜(忧伤的容颜)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 亲近，亲密 <span class="encs">[intimate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">未可以戚我先王。——<span class="smcs">《书·金滕》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span>
