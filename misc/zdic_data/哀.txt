                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>哀</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">āi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/āi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄞˉ</span></p><ol>    <li>悲痛：悲～。～求。～叹。～鸣。～思。～鸿遍野（“哀鸿”，哀鸣的大雁；喻到处都是呻吟呼号，流离失所的灾民）。～艳（诗文凄测动人而华丽）。</li>    <li>悼念：～悼。默～。</li>    <li>旧时称死去母亲：～子（a.母丧而父存；b.古称居父母丧的人）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">哀</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>哀</strong> <span class="dicpy">āi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/āi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从口，衣声。本义:悲痛;悲伤)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[grieved;sorrowful]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哀，闵也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">哀，痛也。——<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以凶礼哀邦国之忧。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·大宗伯》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“救患分灾。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">舍正路而不由哀哉。——<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“伤也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">呜呼哀哉。——<span class="smcs">《柳敏碑》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有妇人哭于墓间而哀。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·檀弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">僵卧孤村不自哀。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《十一月四日风雨大作》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">子言未发而哀乐具乎其前(悲哀、欢乐的神情都先流露了出来)。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">黄宗羲</span><span class="smcs">《柳敬亭传》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:哀咜(悲哀叹息);哀诏(皇上驾崩后，新君通告全国的文书);哀哉(表示悲伤或痛惜的感叹词);哀毁骨立(形容对父母的故去非常悲哀，以致瘦得皮包骨了)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 形容声音凄清尖锐 <span class="encs">[desolate]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">杜鹃啼血猿哀鸣。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:哀弦(悲凉的弦乐声);哀笳(悲凉的胡笳声);哀鸿(哀鸣的大雁。比喻流离失所的灾民)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>哀</strong> <span class="dicpy">āi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/āi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 同情，怜悯 <span class="encs">[pity;sympathize with]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">君将哀而生之乎。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天子作诗三章以哀民。——<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span> 。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:哀恕(同情宽恕)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 慰问;哀悼 <span class="encs">[express sympathy and solicitude for;mourn or grieve for the deceased]</span> </p><p><span class="diczx2">秦</span>不哀吾之丧而伐吾同姓。——<span class="smcs">《左传·僖公三十三年》</span>。</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:哀文(哀辞)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 哀求 <span class="encs">[supplicate;beg]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爱公者皆为公惧，劝罄橐以哀之。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·妖术》</span>。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:哀请(哀告，哀求;苦苦请求);哀祈(哀求)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“爱”</span>。爱护 <span class="encs">[care for;cherish]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">国虽弱，令必敬以哀。——<span class="smcs">《管子·侈靡》</span></span></p><p><span
