<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">剴</span>）</strong></p>

</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>剀</strong></p>
<p>剴 <span class="dicpy">kǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(形声。从刀，豈声。本义:大镰) 同本义 <span class="encs">[sickle]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剀，大镰也。切地以芟刈者，亦名鉊。字亦作鐖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">鐖凿棘矜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;淮南衡山传》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>剀</strong></p>
<p>剴 <span class="dicpy">kǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 磨，磨刀 <span class="encs">[grind]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剀&hellip;一曰摩也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">剀，磨也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 规劝，讽喻 <span class="encs">[admonish]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">言古以剀今，讽刺也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span>注</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:言古以剀今;剀拂(劝戒匡正);剀讽(讽喻)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 杀;砍 <span class="encs">[kill;cutt]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">郡王这里是帝辇之下&hellip;如何胡乱剀得人?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《警世通言》</span></span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>剀</strong></p>
<p>剴 <span class="dicpy">kǎi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/kǎi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>中肯，切实 <span class="encs">[pertinent;true]</span></p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">剴</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>

                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mini_h2">(康熙字典未收录“剀”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/剴">剴</a>”字。)</div>  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下" target="_blank">子集下</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=子集下&amp;bs=刀" target="_blank">刀</a>】
                              剴·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5274.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】古哀切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】柯開切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音該。【說文】鎌也。从刀，豈聲。一曰摩也。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】切也，動也。【唐書&middot;魏徵傳】二百餘奏，無不剴切當帝心者。</p>
