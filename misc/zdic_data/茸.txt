                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茸</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>茸</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄖㄨㄥˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>草初生纤细柔软的样子：绿～～的草地。</li>
	<li>细柔的毛、发：&ldquo;半脱紫～裘&rdquo;。～毛。</li>
	<li>指&ldquo;鹿茸&rdquo;（带细毛的才生出来的鹿角，可入药）：～客（指鹿）。参（<span class="dicpy">shēn </span>）～（指人参和鹿茸）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> soft, downy; buds, sprouts</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> flaumig (Haar)​; saftig und zart (Gras)​; Bast (Hirschgeweih)​  (Adj)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> fin,duveteux</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茸</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>茸</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p>(形声。本义:草初生时又细又柔软) 同本义 <span class="encs">[fine and soft]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">茸，草茸茸貌。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">在阘茸之中。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马迁</span><span class="smcs">《报任安书》</span>。注:&ldquo;细毛也。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:茸壑(长着青草、苔藓的山洞、土穴)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>茸</strong> <span class="dicpy">r&oacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/r&oacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 鹿茸的简称 <span class="encs">[young pilose antler]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">河天月晕鱼分子，槲叶风微鹿养茸。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">黄庭坚</span><span class="smcs">《诗》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 柔细的毛 <span class="encs">[down]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茸毛;茸茸</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 刺绣用的丝缕。同&ldquo;绒&rdquo; <span class="encs">[velvet]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:茸线(刺绣用的丝线。即&ldquo;绒线&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其桑其松，其杞其茸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;地员》</span></span></p>

	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">草木初生時的嫩苗。</span><span class="gc_yy">唐&middot;韓愈、孟郊〈有所思聯句〉：「臺鏡晦舊暉，庭草滋深<b>茸</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">鹿茸的簡稱。因初生的鹿被有細短的毛，所以稱為「鹿茸」。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;黃庭堅〈夏日夢伯兄寄江南〉詩：「河天月暈魚生子，槲夜風微鹿養<b>茸</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">鳥獸細柔的毛。</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈正月一日雪中過淮謁客回作〉詩二首之一：「水崖落屐齒，風葉亂裘<b>茸</b>。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">繡線。</span><span class="gc_yy">明&middot;高啟〈效香奩〉詩二首之一：「青瑣初空別恨長，繡<b>茸</b>留得唾痕香。」</span><span class="gc_yx">通「絨」。</span></p></li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">茸</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						  
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上" target="_blank">申集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=申集上&amp;bs=艸" target="_blank">艸</a>】
                              茸·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=12" target="_blank">12</a>　·部外筆画：6</div>                               
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8338.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】而容切【集韻】而融切【韻會】如容切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音戎。【說文】草茸茸貌。【張衡&middot;南都賦】阿那蓊茸。【謝靈運詩】新蒲含紫茸。</p>
<p>又【史記&middot;晉世家】狐裘蒙茸。【註】蒙茸，以言亂貌。【左傳】作尨茸。</p>
<p>又木名。【管子&middot;地員篇】其杞其茸。</p>
<p>又鹿茸，藥名。【本草】四月、五月解角時取，隂乾，使時燥。</p>
<p>又【飛燕外傳】賜紫茸雲氣帳。【杜牧詩】醉脫紫茸裘。</p>
<p>又五茸，地名。【陸龜蒙詩】五茸春草雉媒驕。【自註】五茸，吳王獵所。</p>
<p>又【集韻】乳勇切，音宂。草生貌。【前漢&middot;司馬相如傳】叢以蘢茸。【師古註】聚貌。</p>
<p>又【前漢&middot;司馬遷傳】僕又茸以蠶室，重爲天下觀笑。【師古註】茸，推也。</p>
<p>又闒茸，不肖也。【鹽鐵論】賢知之士，闒茸之所惡也。</p>
