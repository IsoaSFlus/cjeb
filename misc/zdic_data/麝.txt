                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">麝</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>麝</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">shè  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shè.mp3"></a></span>  ㄕㄜˋ</span></p><ol><li>〔～鼠〕哺乳动物，毛棕黑色。生活在沼泽地带，毛皮可做衣服。</li><li>〔～牛〕哺乳动物，体形像牛而稍小，皮下有腺体，分泌物有特殊气味。生活在北美洲的极北地区。</li><li>哺乳动物，形状像鹿而小，无角。雄的脐部有香腺，能分泌麝香。通称“香獐子”。</li></ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>麝</strong> <span class="dicpy">shè</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/shè.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 动物名 <span class="encs">[musk deer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">麞，麞如小麋。脐有香。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。字亦作麝。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">翠山，其阴多旌牛麞麞。——<span class="smcs">《西山经》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“似獐而小，有香。”</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 产于中亚山地的一种小型粗腿的鹿，雄兽有值钱的麝囊，在鹿类中是唯一具有胆囊者。如:麝父(雄麝);麝脐(雄麝的脐，麝香腺在此)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="yhcs">“麝香”</span>的简称。亦泛指香气 <span class="encs">[musk]</span>。如:麝鹿(极细的麝香粉);麝枕(香枕);麝脑(麝香精);麝酒(麝香浸制的酒)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/麝香">麝香</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
