                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">转</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>转</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">轉</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhuǎn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuǎn.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄢˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>迁徙；流亡：老弱～乎沟壑。</li>
	<li>不直接的，中间再经过别人或别的地方：～送。～达。～发。～运。周～。</li>
	<li>改换方向：～弯。向左～。</li>
	<li>改变位置：～移。</li>
	<li>改变形势、情况：～败为胜。天气～睛。病情好～。</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>转</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">轉</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhu&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄢˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>旋转：车轮飞～。</li>
	<li>使旋转：～动地球仪。</li>
	<li>围绕着一个中心运动：越陷越深绕着太阳～。</li>
	<li>量词，旋转一圈为一转：绕了几十～。</li>
	<li>量词，古代勋位每升一级称&ldquo;一转&rdquo;：&ldquo;策勋十二～，赏赐百千强。&rdquo;</li>
</ol>
<p>● <strong>转</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">轉</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhuǎi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuǎi.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄞˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>[转文]指说话或写文章时有意不用通俗易懂的词句，而使用文言文词语以显高雅。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>转</strong></p>
<p>轉 <span class="dicpy">zhuǎn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuǎn.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从车，专声。转运要用车，故从车。本义:转运)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义(通过陆路运输) <span class="encs">[carry in a cart]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转，运也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">胡转予于恤?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;祈父》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">丁壮苦军旅，老弱罢转饷。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">却骐骥以转运兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;刘向&middot;九叹愍命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转漕甚辽远。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司马贞</span>索隐:&ldquo;车运曰转，水运曰漕。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">辇车相属，转粟流输，千里不绝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;邹阳传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转饷(转运粮饷);转输(转运输送物资);转海(海运);转粟(运送谷物);转漕(转运粮饷);转毂(转运货物的车子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 回还，转动 <span class="encs">[turn;turn around and around]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我心匪石，不可转也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;柏舟》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故善战人之势，如转圆石于千仞之山者，势也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孙子&middot;势》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转轴拨弦三两声，未成曲调先有情。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行(并序)》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">回头转眄似雕鹗，有志飞鸣人岂知!&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李颀</span><span class="smcs">《别梁锽》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转盼(转动眼珠);转眄(转动眼珠);转规(像圆规旋转一样，比喻一往无阻);转侧(在床上转动身体)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 指情况的变化、改变 <span class="encs">[change;shift]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">兵生于治而异，俗生于法而万转。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《商君书&middot;立本》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">高亨</span>注译:&ldquo;万转，万变。指风俗多变化，有好有坏。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">道直身还在，恩深命转轻。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">裴度</span><span class="smcs">《中书即事》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">其为政也，善因祸而为福，转败而为功。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;管晏传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转靶子(改变主意);转角头(转弯处);转日回天(形容力量大，能扭转很难挽回的局面)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 转移，辗转 <span class="encs">[divert;transfer]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转送其家。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;高帝纪上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">浊之则郁而无转。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;泛论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转而攻</span><span class="diczx2">秦</span>。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《过秦论》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转而缘南晦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">路转溪头忽见。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《西江月》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 丢弃 <span class="encs">[abandon;discard]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生无乏用，死无转尸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转尸(人死而不能葬，尸体弃于沟中);转死(弃尸);转尸沟壑(弃尸于山沟水渠)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 迁官转任 <span class="encs">[be transferred to another post]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">王述</span>转尚书令，事行便拜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;方正》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">密</span>有才能，常望内转，而朝廷无援，乃迁 <span class="diczx2">汉中</span>太守。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转除(升官);转任(转换所任职务);转补(迁调官职，以补缺额)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 掉转;转向 <span class="encs">[turn;change direction]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">东亭</span>转卧向壁。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;品藻》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转头向户里。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">童自转。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《童区寄传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">云何裁转背，便卖恶于人?&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《宋书&middot;蔡廓传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">若夫转身易位，全众传国，最其病也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子&middot;说疑》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转视积薪后。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;狼三则》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转步(改变走向);转舵(转动船舵，以改变航向;比喻转变方向)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 移动;迁徙 <span class="encs">[move]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">盤石无转移。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">转入石门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《徐霞客游记&middot;游黄山记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转手(移动手臂);转居(移居)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 离去，离开 <span class="encs">[leave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">衙门无事，相公且转厅。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">马致远</span><span class="smcs">《大妇小妻还牢末杂剧》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(11)</span> 摇动;飘荡 <span class="encs">[wave]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">光风转蕙，泛崇兰兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;招魂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四角龙子帆，婀娜随风转。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉台新咏&middot;古诗为焦仲卿妻作》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转蓬身(比喻到处飘泊的人);转磷(飘忽不定的磷火);转烛(风摇烛火。用以比喻世事变幻莫测);转萍(比喻飘泊不定)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(12)</span> 避 <span class="encs">[evade]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">引而使之，民不敢转其力。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(13)</span> 缠绕 <span class="encs">[wind]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以红绡转其头。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《席上腐谈》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转踅(回旋缠绕)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(14)</span> 卖 <span class="encs">[sell]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">现今这房子转的出四十两银子&hellip;竟托书店里人把房子转了，拿了银子回来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儒林外史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转物(买卖货物);转肩(一种不正当的买卖。高价赊入，低价卖出);转货(做买卖);转卖(把买进的东西再卖出去)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(15)</span> 诵<span class="encs">[经]</span> ;念<span class="encs">[咒]</span> <span class="encs">[chant]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">素公不出院，转<span class="smcs">《法华经》</span>三万七千部。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">段成式</span><span class="smcs">《酉阳杂俎》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">三藏转咒，震舌呼祝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太平广记》</span>引<span class="smcs">《宣室志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转经(诵经);转读(诵读佛经)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(16)</span> 曲折，盘曲 <span class="encs">[winding;circuitous]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">千岩万转。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李白</span><span class="smcs">《梦游天姥吟留别》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">山回路转。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">岑参</span><span class="smcs">《白雪歌送武判官归京》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(17)</span> 量词。等于说&ldquo;次&rdquo;。古时军功每加一等，则官爵随升一级，叫做一转</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">策勋十二转。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;木兰诗》</span></span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>转</strong></p>
<p>轉 <span class="dicpy">zhu&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhu&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 旋转;打转 <span class="encs">[turn;revolve]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">峰回路转。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《醉翁亭记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">下者飘转。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《茅屋为秋风所破歌》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:转丸(转动的弹丸。比喻升降沉浮不定);转灯(走马灯);机器转子团团转;砂轮转得快;地球绕着太阳转</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 绕过 <span class="encs">[wind around]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">由</span><span class="diczx2">千佛山</span>脚下往东，转过山坡，竟向南去。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老残游记》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 迷路或迷失方向 <span class="encs">[lose]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:晕头转向</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[口]</span>∶转悠 <span class="encs">[stroll]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">公园关了门，只好去转</span><span class="diczx2">文殊院</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《大波》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 通&ldquo;啭&rdquo;。鸟鸣，蝉鸣 <span class="encs">[warble;chirp;chirrup]</span></p>
