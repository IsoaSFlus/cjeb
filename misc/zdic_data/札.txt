                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>札</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zh&aacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄚˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>古时写字的小木筒：～记。</li>	<li>信件：手～。信～。</li>	<li>旧时的一种公文：～子。</li>	<li>夭死。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> letter, note; correspondence</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> mince morceau de bois utilisé comme tablette d'écriture (dans la Chine ancienne)​,sorte de document officiel (autrefois)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">札</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>札</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从木，札声。本义:古代用来写字的小木片)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[wooden strip]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">札，牒也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">皆一札十行，细书成文。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;循吏传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卿有札书。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;郊祀志上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上令尚书给笔札。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;司马相如传上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上许，令尚书给笔札。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;司马相如列传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">郡国诸户口黄籍，籍皆用一尺二寸札，已在官役者载名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太平御览》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 古代公文的泛称 <span class="encs">[official document]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:札子(古代官方公文的一种);札付(官府上行下的文书，多指手谕);札吏(旧时官府撰拟公文的使员);札委(旧时官府委派差使的公文);札书(牒，公文);札函(奏折);札稿(文稿);札牍(借指簿册)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 引申为书信 <span class="encs">[letter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">客从远方来，遗我一书札。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《古诗十九首》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:札帙(犹书函、书套);札诲(赐函教诲。旧时书信套语);札翰(书信)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 疫病。也指遭瘟疫死亡 <span class="encs">[plague]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大札则不举。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">土气和，亡札厉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:札荒(瘟疫与灾荒);札丧(因疫病死亡);札厉(因疫病死亡);札瘟(因疫疠、疾病而死);札手风(手张开而不能自由伸缩的一种病。亦比喻醒态)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>札</strong> <span class="dicpy">zh&aacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zh&aacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 拔出;拔除 <span class="encs">[pull out]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">毫末不札，将寻斧柯。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孔子家语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 夭死 <span class="encs">[die young]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疠疾不降，民不夭札。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 书写 <span class="encs">[write]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:札记</span></p>
