                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>胥</strong> <span class="dicpy">xū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，疋( <span class="dicpy">shū</span>)声。本义:蟹酱)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[scrab paste]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胥，蟹醢也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx2">青州</span>之蟹胥。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·庖人》</span>注</p><p><span class="diczx1">蟹胥，取蟹藏之，使骨头解胥胥然也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">共祭祀之好胥。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 片刻，一会儿 <span class="encs">[moment]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胡蝶胥也。——<span class="smcs">《列子·天瑞》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胡蝶胥也化而为虫。——<span class="smcs">《庄子》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>胥</strong> <span class="dicpy">xū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 古代官府中的小吏;特指古代掌管捕捉盗贼的小官吏。后作<span class="yhcs">“偦”</span> <span class="encs">[petty official]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">里胥猾黠。——<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异·促织》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">就老胥而问。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">部中老胥。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胥某谓曰。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胥某笑曰。</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:胥里(乡里小吏);胥役(胥吏与差役);胥靡(刑徒);胥祝(司祭的小吏);胥耆(老吏);胥师(管市场的小吏)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 有才智的人 <span class="encs">[man of mind]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">胥，十有二人;徒，百有二十人。——<span class="smcs">《周礼》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代乐官 <span class="encs">[musical officer]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">小胥大胥。——<span class="smcs">《礼记·王制》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“皆乐官属也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大胥是敛，众胥佐之。——<span class="smcs">《礼记》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:胥人(古代乐官)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 姓</p><p>◎ <strong>胥</strong> <span class="dicpy">xū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈副〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 相互 <span class="encs">[each other]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以比追胥之事。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·士师》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">兄弟昏姻，无胥远矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·角弓》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:胥命(诸侯相见，约言不誓);胥溺(相继沉没);胥译(互译，翻译);胥怨(互怨)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 都;皆 <span class="encs">[all;whole]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">尔之教矣，民胥效矣。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·角弓》</span></span></p><p>◎ <strong>胥</strong> <span class="dicpy">xū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xū.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“须”</span>。等待 <span class="encs">[await]</span></p><p><span
