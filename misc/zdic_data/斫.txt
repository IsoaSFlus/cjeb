                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>斫</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhuó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从斤，石声。斤，斧属。本义:斧刃)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[edge (of an axe)]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斧以金为斫。——<span class="smcs">《墨子》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 大锄 <span class="encs">[big hoe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">二月冰解地干，烧而耕之，仍即下水，十日块既散液，持木斫平之，纳种如前法。——<span class="smcs">《齐民要术》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>斫</strong> <span class="dicpy">zhuó</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuó.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 用刀、斧等砍劈 <span class="encs">[hack with a knife or an axe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">因拔刀斫前奏案。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斫而为琴，弦而鼓之。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">刘基</span><span class="smcs">《郁离子·千里马篇》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">斫直、删密、锄正。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">龚自珍</span><span class="smcs">《病梅馆记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">挥刀奋斫所当无不披靡。(奋斫:尽力砍杀)——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞·战事类》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:斫旗(砍倒彩旗);斫柴(砍柴);斫柴囡(砍柴的小孩);斫木(被砍削的树);斫刈(砍杀);斫地(砍地。表示气愤);斫伐(砍伐)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span>
