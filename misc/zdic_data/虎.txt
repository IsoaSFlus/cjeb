						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>虎</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">hǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>哺乳动物，毛黄褐色，有黑色条纹，性凶猛，力大。骨和血及内脏均可入药（通称&ldquo;老虎&rdquo;）：～口（➊喻危险境地；➋手上拇指和食指相交的地方）。～穴（喻危险境地）。～符（古代调兵的凭证，用铜铸成虎形，分两半）。～狼（喻凶残的人）。～头蛇尾。～踞龙盘。龙腾～跃。</li>
	<li>勇猛、威武：～将。～势。～劲。～威。～～。～气。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">唬</span>&rdquo;，威吓。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">琥</span>&rdquo;，琥珀。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">虎</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>虎</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。金文字形象以虎牙、虎纹为特征的虎形。本义:老虎)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[tiger]</span>，亚洲产的一种大型食肉类哺乳动物( Panthera tigris )，在黄褐色的毛皮上有黑色横纹，尾长而无簇毛，有黑圈，下体大部白色，无鬣，典型的体形比狮子略大</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虎，山兽之君。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">阚如虓虎。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;常武》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">虎狼之心。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;项羽本纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">苛政猛于虎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《捕蛇者说》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">曹</span>公豺虎也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">气吞万里如虎。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">辛弃疾</span><span class="smcs">《永遇乐&middot;京口北固亭怀古》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:虎螭(虎与龙);虎啸(虎吼叫);虎跃(猛虎腾跃);虎残(虎口余生)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 凡伤害物类之虫，也以虎名之 <span class="encs">[insect]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蝇虎;蝎虎</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">守宫善捕蝎蝇，故得虎名。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《本草纲目》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 姓</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>虎</strong> <span class="dicpy">hǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 比喻威武勇猛 <span class="encs">[brave]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:虎虎势势(形容勇猛的样子);虎豹(比喻勇猛的战士);虎旅(指勇猛的军队)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 比喻残酷凶暴 <span class="encs">[cruel]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">或问酷吏。曰:&ldquo;虎哉!虎哉!角而翼者也&rdquo;。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《法言》</span></span></p>

                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/864E_5807.svg" /><span class="swbf">(虎)</span><span class="ywyy">山獸之君。从虍从儿。</span><span class="swdz">會意。</span><span class="ywyy">虎足象人足也。</span><span class="swdz">已上八字，鉉本妄改，張次立復以鉉本改鍇本，惟韵會如是，此古本之眞也。从儿，韵會作从人，此其誤已久耳。儿部曰：孔子曰在人下，故詰屈，謂人之股腳也。虎之股腳似人，故其字上虍下儿。虍謂其文，儿謂其足也。說文龜頭、<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A4D1.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𪓑</span>頭似它頭，燕㞑似魚㞑，兔頭似㲋頭，萈足似兔足，能足、彘足似鹿足。㲋頭似兔，足似鹿。文義相同。儿有其字，故先言从儿而後言虎足象人足。篆體改作虎，則象人足之云不可通。顧氏藹吉乃疑虎下當从爪矣，今正之。凡篆虎字依<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/256EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥛬</span>體从儿為是。呼古切。五部。五部與十七部通，故左氏陽虎，論語作陽貨，非一名一字也。邢昺、孫<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20057.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠁗</span>乃有虎名貨字之說。</span><span class="ywyy">凡虎之屬皆从虎。</span></p>
