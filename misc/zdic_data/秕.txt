						<p>● <strong>秕</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bǐ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧˇ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>子实不饱满：～子（不饱满的子实）。～糠（秕子和糠，喻没有价值的东西）。</li>
	<li>坏，不良：～政（不良的政治措施）。</li>
	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/纰">纰</a>&rdquo;，纰谬。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> empty grain or rice husk, chaff</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> siehe <a href="./粃">粃</a>pi3, auch bi3</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">秕</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>秕</strong></p>
<p>粃 <span class="dicpy">bǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p>中空或不饱满的谷粒 <span class="encs">[blighted grain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">秕，不成粟也。从禾，比声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">用秕稗也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;定公十年》</span>。注:&ldquo;谷不成者。今</span><span class="diczx2">苏</span>俗呼谷不充者曰瘪谷，盖即此字。字亦以粃为之。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:秕稗(秕与稗。喻败坏无用之物);秕蠹(瘪谷和蠹虫。比喻不良、有害之物);秕粮(稗草)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>秕</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">形</span></p>
<p>坏，恶 <span class="encs">[evil]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:秕僻(比喻政事和教化的不善);秕政(弊政，指不良的有害的政治措施)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>秕</strong> <span class="dicpy">bǐ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǐ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>败坏 <span class="encs">[ruin;undermine]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:秕敝(败坏，破旧);秕僻(邪僻败坏)</span></p>

                  <div class="nr-box-header">
                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">秕</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>秕</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄅㄧˇ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>bǐ</rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">穀類植物所結的果實，虛有外殼，裡面卻是中空的，稱為「秕」或「秕穀」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《說文解字&middot;禾部》：「<b>秕</b>，不成粟也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《書經&middot;仲虺之誥》：「若苗之有莠，若粟之有<b>秕</b>。」</span><span class="gc_yy">宋&middot;蘇軾〈稼說〉：「故富人之稼常美，少<b>秕</b>而多實，久藏而不腐。」</span><span class="gc_yx">俗稱為「癟子」。</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">穀物中空不飽滿的。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「<b>秕</b>子」。</span></p></li>
	<li>
