                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>装</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">裝</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zhuāng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuāng.mp3"></a></span> ㄓㄨㄤˉ</span></p><ol><li>穿着的衣物：服～。便～。军～。中山～。～裹。</li><li>特指演员演出时的穿戴打扮：上～。卸～。</li><li>特指出行时带的东西：治～。整～待发。</li><li>特指工业生产或军队作战所需的用具和器械：～备。～置。～甲。</li><li>用服饰改变人的原来原貌：化～。乔～打扮。</li><li>假作，故意做作：佯～。～相。～模作样。</li><li>安置，安放：～载。～卸。</li><li>布置，点缀：～修。～饰。</li><li>特指对书籍、字画加以修整或修整成的式样：～订。～帧。～裱。线～。精～。～潢（原指用黄檗汁染的纸装裱书画，现泛指装饰物品使之美观）。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">装</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>装</strong></p><p>裝 <span class="dicpy">zhuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从衣，壮声。本义:行装)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[baggage]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">装，裹也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">橐中装。——<span class="smcs">《史记·郦生陆贾传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">简元辰而俶装。——</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《思玄赋》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“束也。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">顾形影，自整装。——<span class="smcs">《文选·傅毅·舞赋》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是约车治装，载卷契而行。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·齐策四》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">为装遣</span><span class="diczx2">荆轲</span>。——<span class="smcs">《战国策·燕策》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:装囊(行囊，远行者携带的袋子);装束(束装，整理行装)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 服装 <span class="encs">[outfit;clothing;clothes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">富者赂数十百金，贫亦罄衣装。——</span><span class="diczx2">方苞</span><span class="smcs">《狱中杂记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:童装;春装;夏装;冬装;男装;女装;猎装;学生装;军装;古装;装束(衣着穿戴)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 亦泛指物品 <span class="encs">[clothing and other articles of daily use;dowry]</span>。如:装送(嫁妆);装遣(嫁妆);装资(置办嫁妆的费用);装新(穿戴结婚时的礼服和饰物)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 服装的式样 <span class="encs">[style of dress]</span>。如:西装(洋装)</p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 书籍装订的式样 <span class="encs">[the style of the bookbinding]</span>。如:平装;精装;线装</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>装</strong></p><p>裝 <span class="dicpy">zhuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 装饰;打扮(指一般的装点)<span class="encs">[dress up;attire;deck]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">体美容冶，不待饰装。——<span
