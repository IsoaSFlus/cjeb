                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">惧</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>惧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">懼</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄩˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>害怕：恐～。～怕。～惮。～怯。～色。临危不～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fear, be afraid of, dread</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Angst haben, befürchten</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> craindre,avoir peur</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">惧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>惧</strong></p>
<p>懼 <span class="dicpy">j&ugrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&ugrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从心，瞿声。本义:害怕，恐惧)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[be afraid of;dread;fear]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">惧，恐也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以举天下之人，皆恐惧振动惕栗，不敢为淫暴。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;尚同中》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">楚</span>兵惧，自 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫大国，难测也，惧有伏焉。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;庄公十年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故有知非以虑是，则谓之惧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;解蔽》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">喜怒哀惧爱恶欲。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;礼运》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勇者不惧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;子罕》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:惧思(因恐惧而慎重考虑);惧选(害怕获罪);惧高症(心理学名词。身处高处所产生的过分恐惧反应，不敢往下看、双腿发软、颤抖或晕眩等)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> <span class="encs">[形]</span>∶惊慌失措的样子 <span class="encs">[frightened out of one&#39;s wits]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">闻</span><span class="diczx2">叔孙通</span>之谏则惧然，纳 <span class="diczx2">曹</span>相国之对而心说。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;惠帝纪赞》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 恐吓 <span class="encs">[threaten]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">危以动，则民不与也;惧以语，则民不应也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">围</span><span class="diczx2">徐</span>以惧 <span class="diczx2">吴</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公十二年》</span></p>
