						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>粲</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">c&agrave;n  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span>  ㄘㄢˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>鲜明：～烂（同&ldquo;灿烂&rdquo;）。～～。～然。</li>
	<li>美：～者。～花（形容言谈之美）。</li>
	<li>笑：以博一～。</li>
	<li>古称上等的米。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> polish; bright, radiant; smiling</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> strahlen  (V)​,strahlendhell</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> riz blanc,net</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粲</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>粲</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从米，( <span class="dicpy">c&aacute;n</span>)声。本义:上等白米)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义;又指美食 <span class="encs">[polished white rice]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">还予授子之粲兮。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;郑风&middot;缁衣》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 露齿笑 <span class="encs">[smile]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">军人粲然皆笑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;昭公四年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:以博一粲</p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>粲</strong> <span class="dicpy">c&agrave;n</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/c&agrave;n.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 鲜明的样子 <span class="encs">[bright]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粲，文也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粲，鲜也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释言》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">如此粲者何。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;唐风&middot;绸缪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">粲粲衣服。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;大东》</span>。传:&ldquo;鲜盛貌。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">角枕粲文茵，锦衾烂长筵。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;排调》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:粲花(形容言谈生动优美，如百花灿烂);粲粲(鲜明的样子)</p>

                    <div class="gycd">
<div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>粲</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄘㄢˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>c&agrave;n <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5475.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span>
<ol>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">精米。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字&middot;米部》「粲」字&middot;清&middot;段玉裁&middot;注：「稻米九斗而舂为八斗，&hellip;&hellip;则曰粲。&hellip;&hellip;稻米至于粲，皆精之至矣。」</span></p></li>
	<li>
	<p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">笑。</span><span class="gc_yy">清&middot;徐珂《清稗类钞&middot;讥讽类&middot;官金二十字之别解》：「今之弄笔，意在一粲。」</span></p></li>
</ol></div>
<div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">形</span>
<ol>
	<li>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粲</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上" target="_blank">未集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集上&amp;bs=米" target="_blank">米</a>】
                              粲·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=13" target="_blank">13</a>　·部外筆画：7</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7CB2.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】【集韻】【韻會】蒼案切【正韻】蒼晏切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音燦。【說文】稻重二䄷，爲粟二十斗，爲米十斗，曰毇。爲米六斗大半斗曰粲。【篇海】精鑿食也。</p>
<p>又餐也。【詩&middot;鄭風】還予授子之粲兮。【傳】粲，餐也。今河北人呼食爲粲，謂餐食也。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】優也，察也，明也。【詩&middot;唐風】角枕粲兮。【小雅】粲粲衣服。【爾雅&middot;釋訓】宴宴粲粲，尼居息也。【註】盛飾晏安，近處優閒。【前漢&middot;宣帝紀】骨肉之親，粲而不殊。【註】粲，明也。</p>
<p>又衆意。【詩&middot;鄭風】三英粲兮。【箋】衆意。【註】采諫反。【朱註】又光明也。</p>
<p>又三女爲粲。【詩&middot;唐風】今夕何夕，見此粲者。【周語】密康公遊于涇，有三女奔之。其母曰：必致之王，女三爲粲。粲，美物也。</p>
<p>又笑貌。【穀粱傳&middot;昭四年】軍人皆粲然而笑。【註】粲然，盛笑貌。</p>
<p>又漢<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>有白粲。【前漢&middot;孝惠紀】當爲城旦舂者，皆耐爲鬼薪白粲。【註】白粲，坐擇米使正白爲白粲，三歲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>也。</p>
<p>又精潔貌。【荀子&middot;榮辱篇】粲然有秉芻豢稻粱而至者。【註】粲，精潔貌。</p>
<p>又姓。隴西族，出【姓苑】。</p>
<p>又叶倉甸切，音茜。【陸雲&middot;贈張府君詩】被繡晝行，昔人攸羨。階雲飛藻，孰與同粲。</p>
<p>又【廣韻】與㛑同。　【海篇】別作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/25EA4.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𥺤</span>。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">粲</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七">卷七</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷七&amp;swbs=米">米</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/7CB2.svg">                          <p>稻重一䄷，爲粟二十斗，爲米十斗，曰毇；爲米六斗太半斗，曰粲。从米<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239BC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣦼</span>聲。倉案切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/7CB2_4781.svg" /><span class="swbf">(粲)</span><span class="ywyy">稻重一䄷，爲粟二十斗，</span><span class="swdz">見禾部。</span><span class="ywyy">爲米十斗。曰毇。</span><span class="swdz">此當有奪文。當以爲米十斗句絕。下云爲米九斗曰毇，稻粟二十斗爲米十斗者，九章筭術所謂稻率六十糲米率三十也。稻粟二十斗爲米十斗，今目驗猶然，其米甚粗，不得曰毇明矣。爲米九斗曰毇者，下文云米一斛舂爲九斗曰毇是也。毇卽粺。禾黍言粺，稻言毇。稻米九斗而舂爲八斗則亦曰糳，八斗而舂爲六斗大半斗則曰粲，猶之禾黍糳米爲七斗則曰侍御也。禾黍米至於侍御，稻米至於粲，皆精之至矣。不言亦曰糲不言爲米八斗亦曰糳者，名各有所系，欲讀者參伍而得之。</span><span class="ywyy">爲米六斗大半斗曰粲。</span><span class="swdz">謂以八斗舂爲六斗大半斗也。以今目驗言之，稻米十斗舂之爲六斗大半斗，精無過此者矣。漢㓝法有鬼薪、白粲。白粲謂舂也，粲米冣白，故爲鮮好之稱。穀粱：粲然皆笑。謂見齒也。鄭風傳曰：粲，餐也。此謂粲爲餐之叚借也。</span><span class="ywyy">从米。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/239BC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𣦼</span>聲。</span><span class="swdz">舂之義亦與𣦼相近。倉案切。十四部。</span></p>                          </div>
