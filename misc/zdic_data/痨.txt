                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">痨</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>痨</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">癆</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">l&aacute;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄠˊ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>中医指积劳损削之病：五～（五脏劳损，&ldquo;心劳&rdquo;、&ldquo;肝劳&rdquo;、&ldquo;肺劳&rdquo;、&ldquo;脾劳&rdquo;、&ldquo;肾劳&rdquo;的总称）。</li>
	<li>结核病的俗称：肺～。骨～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> consumption; tuberculosis</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Schwindsucht, erschöpft  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> tuberculose</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>痨</strong></p>
<p>癆 <span class="dicpy">l&aacute;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/l&aacute;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从疒( <span class="dicpy">chu&aacute;ng</span>)，表示与疾病有关，劳声。本义:积劳瘦削)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 劳损病 <span class="encs">[internal injury caused by overstrain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">疲，今人以积劳瘦削为痨病。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《正字通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:痨怯(虚劳);痨伤(犹劳伤)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 结核病的俗称。多指肺结核 <span class="encs">[tuberculosis]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:痨瘵(肺结核病。俗称&ldquo;肺痨&rdquo;);痨嗽(患痨病而咳嗽);痨虫(旧时称专染肺病的微生物为痨虫)</span></p>
