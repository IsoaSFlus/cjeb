                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">它</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>它</strong> <span class="dicpy">tā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p>(象形。小篆字形。象虫形。本义:虫) 同本义。后作&ldquo;蛇&rdquo; <span class="encs">[snake]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">它，虫也。从虫而长，象冤曲垂尾形，上古草居患它，故相问&ldquo;无它乎?&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>它</strong> <span class="dicpy">tā</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/tā.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 称代人以外的事物 <span class="encs">[it]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:煤炭，很多工业离不开它;香山观，从老远的地方就能看见它&mdash;&mdash;口语中与&ldquo;他、她&rdquo;无分别。如:它比(引他类比附)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 表示第三人称 <span class="encs">[he;she]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:生它个儿子(生他一个儿子)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 表示指称。相当于&ldquo;别的&rdquo;、&ldquo;其他的&rdquo; <span class="encs">[other]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无它异。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;列女传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">贝勒</span>及它将。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">帛布及它物。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">与它石迥异。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子&middot;公输》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:它罪(别的罪名);它肠(它心，二心);它人(别人);它年(以后的某年或某一时期);它名(另外的名);它所(其他地方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 虚指。如:干它一场;吃它一顿</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>

                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上" target="_blank">寅集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=寅集上&amp;bs=宀" target="_blank">宀</a>】
                              它·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=5" target="_blank">5</a>　·部外筆画：2</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/5B83.svg">						  <p>【玉篇】古文佗字。佗，蛇也。【說文】虫也。本作它，从虫而長。上古艸居，慮它，故相問無它乎。</p>
<p>又【玉篇】非也，異也。【正字通】與佗他同。【易&middot;比卦】終來有它吉。【禮&middot;檀弓】或敢有它志，以辱君義。</p>
<p>又【揚子&middot;法言】適堯舜文王爲正道，非堯舜文王爲它道，君子正而不它。</p>
<p>又【正譌】它，虫之大者。象寃曲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂹</span>㞑形。今文加虫作蛇，食遮切與托何切，二音通用。</p>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">它</span><span class="span dictname"> 《说文解字》</span></h2>
                                                  </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions snr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三">卷十三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷十三&amp;swbs=它">它</a>】</div>
                                                    <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字』<hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/5B83.svg">                          <p>虫也。从虫而長，象冤曲垂尾形。上古艸居患它，故相問無它乎。凡它之屬皆从它。蛇，它或从虫。託何切〖注〗臣鉉等曰：今俗作食遮切。文一　重一</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5B83_1427.svg" /><span class="swbf">(它)</span><span class="ywyy">虫也。从虫而長，象冤曲<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂹</span>尾形。</span><span class="swdz"><img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂹</span>各本作垂。今正。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂹</span>者，艸木華葉<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂹</span>也。引申爲凡物下<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂹</span>之偁。垂者遠邊，非其義。冤曲者，其體。垂尾者，其末。它象其臥形，故詘尾而短。它象其上冤曲而下垂尾，故長。詘尾謂之虫，垂尾謂之它，它與<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/200B9.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠂹</span>古音同也。詩：維虺維蛇，女子之祥。吳語：爲虺弗摧，爲蛇將若何。虺皆虫之叚借。皆謂或臥或垂尾耳。臥者較易制，曳尾而行者難制。故曰爲虺弗摧，爲蛇將若何也。託何切。十七部。今人蛇與它異義異音。蛇食遮切。</span><span class="ywyy">上古艸凥患它，故相問無它乎。</span><span class="swdz">上古者，謂神農以前也。相問無它，猶後人之不恙無恙也。語言轉移，則以無别故當之。而其字或叚佗爲之。又俗作他。經典多作它。猶言彼也。許言此以說叚借之例。羔羊傳曰：委蛇，行可從迹也。亦引申之義也。</span><span class="ywyy">凡它之屬皆从它。</span></p>
<hr />
<p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/5B83_1428.svg" /><span class="swbf">(蛇)</span><span class="ywyy">它或从虫。</span><span class="swdz">它篆本以虫篆引長之而巳。乃又加虫左旁，是俗字也。</span></p>
