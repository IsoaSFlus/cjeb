                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">灾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>灾</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_yt.svg" title="异体字"><span class="diczx3">災、烖、菑</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zāi  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāi.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗㄞˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>水、火、荒旱等所造成的祸害：水～。火～。～难（<span class="dicpy">n&agrave;n </span>）。～害。～患。</li>	<li>个人的不幸遭遇：招～惹祸。幸～乐祸。破财消～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> calamity, disaster, catastrophe</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Unglück  (S)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">灾</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>灾</strong></p>
<p>災、烖、菑 <span class="dicpy">zāi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zāi.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，象火焚屋的形状。小篆从川，表水;从火。水火都是灾祸之源。本义:火灾)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[fire as a disaster]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天火曰烖，从火，哉声。古文从才，籀文从巛声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。或体灾。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大者曰灾，小者火。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《公羊传&middot;襄公九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">国曰灾，邑曰火。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;昭公九年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">火所烧灭之余曰烖，言其余物如是也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡火，人火曰火，天火曰灾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;宣公十六年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏四月，</span><span class="diczx2">陈</span>灾。(陈地发生火灾。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;昭公九年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:灾火(火灾);灾燀(火灾)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">大烖。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;司服》</span>。注:&ldquo;水火为害。&rdquo;</span></p>
