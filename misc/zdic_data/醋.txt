                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>醋</strong> <span class="dicpy">cù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从酉( <span class="dicpy">yǒu</span>)，声。从<span class="yhcs">“酉”</span>，表示与酒有关。本义:用酒或酒糟发酵制成的一种酸味调料)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[vinegar]</span>。如:醋钵儿(盛醋用的陶钵);醋户(明代专门负责酿醋以供官府之用的民户)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 酸味 <span class="encs">[acidity]</span>。如:醋味(指酸味的食物);醋浸曹公(酸梅的谑称)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>醋</strong> <span class="dicpy">cù</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cù.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 比喻嫉妒 <span class="encs">[jealous]</span>。如:醋八姐(妒妇);醋妒(吃醋，妒忌);醋性(忌妒心。多用于男女之间);醋劲(嫉妒的情绪。多用于男女之间)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 旧时对文人的蔑称 <span class="encs">[(of a poor scholar)miserable and shabby]</span>。如:醋滴滴(讥讽文人出言吐语喜欢引经据典，语带之乎者也)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醋大">醋大</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醋劲儿">醋劲儿</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醋酸">醋酸</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醋坛子">醋坛子</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醋心">醋心</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/醋意">醋意</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">醋</span><span class="span dictname"> 國語辭典</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>醋</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄘㄨˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>cù <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5501.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">名</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">以米、麦、高粱等酿成，用来调味的酸味液体。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「米<b>醋</b>」、「黑<b>醋</b>」。</span><span class="gc_yy">《宋史·卷一八五·食货志下七》：「卖<b>醋</b>毋得越郡城五里外，凡县、镇、村并禁，其息悉归转运司。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">嫉妒的心理。</span><span class="gc_lz">如：「吃<b>醋</b>」、「醋坛子」。</span></p></li></ol></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">害怕、畏惧。</span><span class="gc_yy">《西游记·第二六回》：「他见拿我不住，尽有几分<b>醋</b>我。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">嫉妒。</span><span class="gc_yy">《红楼梦·第九回》：「因此贾瑞、金荣等一干人，也正醋妒他两个。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div><div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>醋</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄗㄨㄛˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>zuò</rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">客人以酒回敬主人。「酢」的本字。</span><span class="gc_yy">《说文解字·酉部》：「<b>醋</b>，客酌主人也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">清·段玉裁·注：「诸经多以酢为<b>醋</b>；唯礼经尚仍其旧，后人<b>醋</b>、酢互易。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《仪礼·特牲馈食礼》：「祝酌授尸，尸以<b>醋</b>主人。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>
