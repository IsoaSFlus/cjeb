                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>玑</strong></p><p>璣 <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从玉， 幾( <span class="dicpy">jī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jī.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:不圆的珠子)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[a pearl that is not quite round]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">璣，珠不圆者也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">厥篚元纁玑组。——<span class="smcs">《书·禹贡》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">傅玑之珥。——<span class="smcs">《史记·李斯传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">爱已之一苍璧小玑。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·重已》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">赬丹明玑。——</span><span class="diczx2">左思</span><span class="smcs">《吴都赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">瑇瑁珠玑。——<span class="smcs">《史记·货殖列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:玑镜(以有光的大珠制成的镜子;也指明镜。用以赞美他人的鉴识能力);玑贝(珍珠宝贝;比喻佳作)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 玑衡，古代的一种天文仪器 <span class="encs">[an ancient astronomical instrument]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">在璿玑玉衡。——<span class="smcs">《虞书》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玑为转运。——<span class="smcs">《北堂书钞引书大传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">玑衡(<span class="yhcs">“璇玑玉衡”</span>的省称。古代观测天体的仪器)</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 北斗的第三星 <span class="encs">[thirth star of the Big Dipper]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">文仰陟天玑。——<span class="smcs">《宋文皇帝元皇后哀策》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:玑璇(北斗七星中的星名。璇是其中第二星，玑是其中第三星，今称<span class="yhcs">“天玑”</span>，合称<span class="yhcs">“璇玑”</span>);玑衡(星名。指北斗七星中的天玑与玉衡。也用以称观测天文的仪器)</p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/74A3.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】居衣切【集韻】【韻會】居希切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音機。【說文】珠不圓者。【書·禹貢】厥篚<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/248E5.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𤣥</span>纁璣組。【傳】璣，珠類，生于水。【釋文】璣，珠不圓也。字書云：小珠也。【前漢·景十三王傳】遣建荃葛珠璣。【註】師古曰：璣，珠之不圓者。</p><p>又鏡名。【說文徐註】按符瑞圖有璣鏡。註：大珠而琕，有光曜，可爲鏡。</p><p>又器名。【書·舜典】在璿璣玉衡，以齊七政。【註】在，察也。璣，正天文之器，璣爲轉運，衡爲橫簫，運璣使動。璣徑八尺，圓周二尺五寸而强。衡長八尺，孔徑一寸，下端望之，以占星辰吉凶之象。七政者，日月五星也。得失由政，故稱政焉。</p><p>又星名。【史記·天官書北斗七星註】春秋運斗極云：斗第三璣。</p><p>又【集韻】渠希切，音祈。</p><p>又【集韻】【韻會】其旣切【正韻】吉器切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音禨。</p><p>又【集韻】巨至切，音<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2690C.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦤌</span>。義<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>同。</p>
