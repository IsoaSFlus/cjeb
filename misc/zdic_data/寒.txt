                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寒</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>寒</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">hán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hán.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄢˊ</span></p><ol><li>冷，与“<span class="diczx3">暑</span>”相对：～冬。～色。～衣。～冽。～带。～战。～噤。～食（节名，在清明前一天。古人从这一天起不生火做饭，也有的地区把清明当作“寒食”）。～喧。～来暑往。唇亡齿～。</li><li>害怕：～心。</li><li>穷困，有时用作谦辞：～门。～伧。～舍。～窗（喻艰苦的学习环境）。～酸。～士（旧指贫穷的读书人）。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> cold, wintry, chilly</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> kalt  (Adv)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> froid,pauvre</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">寒</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>寒</strong> <span class="dicpy">hán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。金文外面是<span class="yhcs">“宀”</span>( <span class="dicpy">mián</span>)，即房屋;中间是<span class="yhcs">“人”</span>;人的左右两边是四个<span class="yhcs">“草”</span>，表示很多;下面两横表示<span class="yhcs">“冰”</span>。寒冷是一种感觉，人们虽能感觉到，但是却看不见。于是古人就采用上述四个形体来创造这个字，人踡曲在室内，以草避寒，表示天气很冷。本义:冷，寒冷)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[cold]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寒，冻也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凉是冷之始，寒是冷之极。——<span class="smcs">《列子·汤问》</span>注</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">乾为寒。——<span class="smcs">《易·说卦》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">若不可寻也，亦可寒也。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公十二年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以享司寒。——<span class="smcs">《左传·昭公四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">北方曰寒风。——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋·有始》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">岁寒，然后知松柏之后凋也。——<span class="smcs">《论语·子罕》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">饥渴寒暑，是事之变。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·德充符》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寒光照铁衣。——<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集·木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">寒雨连江夜入</span><span class="diczx2">吴</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">王昌龄</span><span class="smcs">《芙蓉楼送辛渐》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凄神寒骨。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">心忧炭贱愿天寒。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:寒肃(寒冷肃杀);寒悄(冷气侵入);寒衾(冰冷的床铺);寒噤(由于天冷而咬紧牙关或牙齿打战)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 贫困 <span class="encs">[poor;poverty-stricken]</span>。如:寒门薄宦(穷家小吏);寒畯(贫寒的读书人);寒劣(贫弱无助的人);寒女(贫穷家庭的女孩)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 卑微;低微 <span class="encs">[petty and low]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾本寒家。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 又如:寒官(下级官吏);寒品(出身寒微的人);寒族(寒微的家族);寒穷(寒微贫穷)</p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 冷清 <span class="encs">[desolate]</span>。如:寒山(冷落寂静的山;寒天的山);寒芒(使人感冷清的光芒);寒汀(清寒冷落的小洲);寒月(清冷的月光)</p><p><span
