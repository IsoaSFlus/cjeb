<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> hoe, rake; weed</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Hure, Nutte  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> houe,bêcher</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">耨</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>耨</strong></p><p>槈、鎒 <span class="dicpy">nòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从耒，从辱。耒，翻土的工具。字本作<span class="yhcs">“辱”</span>，从辰，从寸。<span class="yhcs">“辰”</span>表示蜃的贝壳，古代的耕具，<span class="yhcs">“寸”</span>表示用手操作。<span class="yhcs">“辱”</span>借为<span class="yhcs">“耻辱”</span>字，遂产生<span class="yhcs">“耨”</span>字。本义:锄草，耕作)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[weed]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">人耨必以旱，使地肥而土缓。(以:由于。)——<span class="smcs">《吕氏春秋》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:耨耕(泛指农活)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 骂 <span class="encs">[abuse]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">早忘了咒，不记得低低耨。——<span class="smcs">《翠裙腰·闺怨》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 呻吟 <span class="encs">[groan]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">一个姿情的不休，一个哑声儿厮耨。——<span class="smcs">《西厢记》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong><hr class="dichr" /></p><p>◎ <strong>耨</strong> <span class="dicpy">nòu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/nòu.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 钩儿锄，一种锄草的农具 <span class="encs">[weeding hoe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">释其锄耨而下泪。——</span><span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《旱云赋》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 〈方〉∶元明戏剧中常见，状男女狎昵之态 <span class="encs">[indulge in sensual pleasures]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">有耨处散诞松宽着耨，有偷处宽行大步偷，何须把一家苦苦死淹留?——</span><span class="diczx2">元</span>· <span class="diczx2">关汉卿</span><span class="smcs">《金线池》</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">耨</span><span class="span dictname"> 《康熙字典》</span></h2>
                                                  
                          
                        </div>
                        
                        <div class="content definitions knr">
						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中" target="_blank">未集中</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=未集中&amp;bs=耒" target="_blank">耒</a>】
                              耨·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=16" target="_blank">16</a>　·部外筆画：10</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8028.svg">						  <p>【廣韻】奴豆切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】乃豆切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音槈。【說文】薅器也。本作槈，今文作耨。【廣韻】同鎒。纂文曰：耨如鏟，柄長三尺，刃廣二寸，以刺地除草。【呂氏春秋】耨柄尺，其長六寸，所以閒稼。【字詁】頭長六寸，柄長六尺，以芸田也。</p><p>又【玉篇】耘也。【釋名】耨，以鋤耨禾也。【易·繫辭】耒耨之利，以敎天下，蓋取諸益。【左傳·僖三十三年】臼季使過冀，見冀缺耨。【註】耨，鋤也。【釋文】耨，乃豆切，鉏田也。【晉語註】耨，茠也。或作薅，拔田草也。【周禮·天官·甸師】掌帥其屬，而耕耨王藉，以時入之。【註】耨，芸芓也。</p><p>又【類篇】奴沃切，音褥，治草也。</p><p>又【字彙補】內典，阿耨多羅。阿，此云無也。耨多羅，此云上也。</p>
