                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">西</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>西</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">xī  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span>  ㄒㄧˉ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>方向，太阳落下的一边，与&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">东</span>&rdquo;相对：～面。～晒。～域。～方。～席（旧时对幕友或家塾教师的敬称。古代主位在东，宾位在西。亦称&ldquo;西宾&rdquo;）。～宫（借指妃嫔）。</li>	<li>事物的样式或方法属于西方的（多指欧美各国）：～学。～画。～餐。～医。</li>	<li>姓。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> west(ern); westward, occident</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Westen  (Adj)​,Radikal Nr. 146 = bedecken, Westen, westlich, abendländisch</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> ouest,occidental</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">西</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>西</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。据小篆字形，上面是鸟的省写，下象鸟巢形。&ldquo;西&rdquo;是&ldquo;栖&rdquo;的本字。本义:鸟入巢息止)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[(of birds) perch]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西，鸟在巢上，象形。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">棹歌惊起乱西禽，女伴各归</span><span class="diczx2">南浦</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《敦煌曲子词集》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:西迟(游息，流连。同栖迟)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 往西走;向西去 <span class="encs">[go to west]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西通</span><span class="diczx2">巴蜀</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《三国志&middot;诸葛亮传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何时复西归。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《乐府诗集&middot;长歌行》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">从小丘西行&hellip;&hellip;&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《至小丘西小石潭记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">西出阳关无故人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">王维</span><span class="smcs">《送元二使安西》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:西上(向西去);西驾(皇帝的车驾西行);西夕(太阳西下而天黑)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>西</strong> <span class="dicpy">xī</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xī.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 方位词。日落的方向，西方 <span class="encs">[west]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">日在西方而鸟栖，故因以为东西之西。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王用享于西山。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;随》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">宅西。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以白琥礼西方。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宗伯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">赵王</span>因起兵南戍 <span class="diczx2">韩</span><span class="diczx2">梁</span>之西边。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;赵策一》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">故有东宫、有西宫、有南宫、有北宫，异居而同财，有余则归之宗，不足则资之宗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;丧服》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:西班(武官。唐凡朝会，武官班于西);各奔西东(各奔前程);西帮(经营钱庄业的山西人);西都(古指洛阳);西裔(西部边远地区);西牛贺洲(佛经中四大部洲之一，在西方)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 古称西边的邻国 <span class="encs">[western neighbouring state]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">越</span>山千万云门绝，西僧貌古还名月。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">皎然</span><span class="smcs">《寄题云门寺梵月无侧房》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:西僧(西蕃僧人);西人(宋时指西夏人)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 现代我国称欧美为&ldquo;西洋&rdquo;或&ldquo;泰西&rdquo;，简称&ldquo;西&rdquo; <span class="encs">[Western;Occidental]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">初</span><span class="diczx2">中</span>西不睦，地方官出示，禁止华人受雇外洋，供其服役。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">夏燮</span><span class="smcs">《粤民义师记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:西珠(西洋商人贩运到中国来的珍珠);西番柿子(番茄)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 西天的简称。佛经中指佛祖所在之处为极乐世界，也叫&ldquo;西天&rdquo; <span class="encs">[Western Paradise]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:西土(指佛教发源地印度。印度古称天竺，因在中国之西，故称);西方净土(佛教语。西方之极乐世界，即佛国)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 复姓。如:西乞;西王;西宫;西郭</p>

                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/897F_6112.svg" /><span class="swbf">(西)</span><span class="ywyy">鳥在巢上也。象形。</span><span class="swdz">下象巢，上象鳥，會意。上、下皆非字也，故不曰會意而曰象形。鳥在巢上者，此篆之本義。今音先稽切。古音讀如詵，讀如<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/203A3.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠎣</span>。如西施亦作先施。漢書曰：西，遷也。古音在十二、十三部。</span><span class="ywyy">日在㢴方而鳥㢴。</span><span class="swdz">上西，卽下文東西之西也。下西，西之本義也。</span><span class="ywyy">故因㠯爲東㢴之㢴。</span><span class="swdz">此說六書叚借之例。叚借者，本無其字，依聲託事。古本無東西之西，寄託於鳥在巢上之西字爲之。凡許言以爲者類此。韋本訓相背，而以爲皮韋。烏本訓孝烏，而以爲烏呼。來本訓瑞麥，而以爲行來。朋本古文鳳，而以爲朋攩。子本訓十一月昜气動萬物滋，而以爲人偁。後人習焉不察，用其借義而廢其本義，乃不知西之本訓鳥在巢，韋之本訓相背，朋之本訓爲鳳，逐末忘本，大都類是。微許君言之，烏能知之。</span><span class="ywyy">凡㢴之屬皆从㢴。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/897F_6113.svg" /><span class="swbf">(棲)</span><span class="ywyy">㢴或从木妻。</span><span class="swdz">葢从木，妻聲也。从妻爲聲。葢製此篆之時已分别㢴爲東㢴，棲爲鳥在巢，而其音則皆近妻矣。詩可以棲遟，漢嚴發碑作衡門西遟。然則雞棲于塒，雞棲于桀，古本必作雞西。論語爲是棲棲，古本亦必作西西。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/897F_6114.svg" /><span class="swbf">(卥)</span><span class="ywyy">古文㢴。</span></p><p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/897F_6115.svg" /><span class="swbf">(卤)</span><span class="ywyy">籒文㢴。</span><span class="swdz">按鹵下曰从西省。若籒文西如此，則鹵正从籒文卤矣。</span></p>                          </div>
