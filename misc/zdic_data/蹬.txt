<p><span class="dicpy">dēng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dēng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄉㄥ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>腿和脚向脚底的方向用力：～水车。～三轮车。</li>
	<li>踩；踏。～在窗台上。</li>
	<li>穿（鞋、裤子等）：脚～长筒靴。～上裤子。</li>
</ol>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>蹬</strong> <span class="dicpy">dēng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/dēng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 踩;踏 <span class="encs">[step on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">蹬，履也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">功蹬王府。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《槀长蔡湛颂》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">唿喇一声蹬倒八卦炉，往外就走。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《西游记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:蹬空(踏空);蹬脱(踢开;甩脱，抛开);蹬足(以足顿地);蹬踏(登;蹬上高处)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 穿 <span class="encs">[wear]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:蹬上裤子;蹬了一双绒靴</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">d&egrave;ng</span></p>
