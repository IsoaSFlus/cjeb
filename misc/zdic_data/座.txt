                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>座</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄗ　ㄨㄛˋ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>坐位：入～。茶～。～谈。宝～。在～。～右铭。</li>	<li>托着器物的东西。底～儿。</li>	<li>星空的区域，一群星：星～。天琴～。</li>	<li>量词，多用于较大或固定的物体：一～山。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">座</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>座</strong> <span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从广( <span class="dicpy">yǎn</span>)，坐声。&ldquo;坐&rdquo;的后起字。本义:座位)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。供人坐的地方，也指位置，座次 <span class="encs">[seat]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">隔座送钩春酒暖。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">李商隐</span><span class="smcs">《无题》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">座上有健啖客。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:宝座(指帝王或神佛的座位);上座(坐位分尊卑时，最尊的坐位叫上座);落座(坐到位置上);满座;空座;首座;末座;楼座;茶座;专座;雅座</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 坐具 <span class="encs">[bed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">清辇道于上林，肃华台之金座。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《梁书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:石座;软座;硬座;玉座;蒲座</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 旧时对官长或对他人的敬称 <span class="encs">[your]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:座主(科举时代称呼主考官或总裁官);军座;总座;座下(对尊者的敬称);座前(旧时书信中对尊长的敬称)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 底座，器物的基础部分或托底的部件 <span class="encs">[stand;base;pedestal]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:花瓶座儿;塑像座儿;梯座</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 引申为在座的人 <span class="encs">[those present]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凄凄不似向前声，满座重闻皆掩泣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《琵琶行》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">座中泣下谁最多?</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 星座 <span class="encs">[constellation]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:猎户座;小熊座;大熊座;天琴座;仙后座;大犬座;天鹰座</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>座</strong> <span class="dicpy">zu&ograve;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zu&ograve;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">量</span></p>
<p>多用于较大或固定的物体。</p>
