						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>猪</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">zhū  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhū.mp3"></a></span>  ㄓㄨˉ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>哺乳动物，肉可食，鬃可制刷，皮可制革，粪是很好的肥料：～倌。～场。～圈（<span class="dicpy">ju&agrave;n </span>）。～肉。生～。野～。种（<span class="dicpy">zh&oacute;ng </span>）～。</li>
	<li>古同&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">潴</span>&rdquo;，水积存之处。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> pig, hog, wild boar</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Hausschwein, Schwein (S, Bio)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> porc,cochon</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">猪</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>猪</strong></p>
<p>猪 <span class="dicpy">zhū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 猪只 <span class="encs">[swine;hog;pig]</span>，组成猪科(Suidae)的各种哺乳动物的任一种，四肢短小，尾短，体毛稀疏，鼻面短凹或平直，耳大下垂或前挺，被毛较粗，有黑、白或黑白花等色，汁腺不发达，热时喜浸水散热;特指家养种的成员</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">猪，豕而三毛丛居者。从豕，者声。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，豕子也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3"> 如:猪魈(猪怪;猪精);猪膀胱(又称&ldquo;猪尿泡&rdquo;、&ldquo;猪胞&rdquo;、&ldquo;猪尿脬&rdquo;、&ldquo;猪脬&rdquo;);小猪(又叫&ldquo;猪牙子&rdquo;、&ldquo;猪仔&rdquo;、&ldquo;猪娃&rdquo;、&ldquo;猪娃子&rdquo;);母猪(又叫&ldquo;猪婆&rdquo;、&ldquo;猪婆子&rdquo;、&ldquo;猪娘&rdquo;)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 十二生肖之一，配地支的&ldquo;亥&rdquo; <span class="encs">[swine]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:属猪的;猪年(俗称十二生肖之一猪所代表的年份，即亥年)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>猪</strong></p>
<p>猪 <span class="dicpy">zhū</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/zhū.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>通&ldquo;潴&rdquo;。水积聚<span class="encs">[(of water) accumulate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">大野既猪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;禹贡》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">规偃猪。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十五年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以猪畜水。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;地官&middot;稻人》</span></span></p>
