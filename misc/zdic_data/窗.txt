                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>窗</strong></p><p>窓、窻、牎 <span class="dicpy">chuāng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/chuāng.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。从穴，囱( <span class="dicpy">cōng</span>)声。<span class="yhcs">“窗”</span>本作<span class="yhcs">“囱”</span>( <span class="dicpy">cōng</span>)，小篆字，象天窗形，即在屋上留个洞，可以透光，也可以出烟(后来灶突也叫<span class="yhcs">“囱”</span>)。后加<span class="yhcs">“穴”</span>字头构成形声字。本义:天窗)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。也作<span class="yhcs">“囱”</span> <span class="encs">[window]</span>。泛指房屋、车船上通气透光的洞口</p><p><span class="diczx1">囱，在墙曰牅，在屋曰囱。窗，或从穴。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四旁两夹窗。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·匠人》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“窗助户为明。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">天窗绮疎。——</span><span class="diczx2">王逸</span><span class="smcs">《鲁灵光殿赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">凿窗启牖，以助户明也。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·别通》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">前辟四窗，垣墙周庭。——</span><span class="diczx2">归有光</span><span class="smcs">《项脊轩志》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">当窗理云鬓，对镜帖花黄。——北朝乐府<span class="smcs">《木兰诗》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">旁开小窗，左右各四，共八扇。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏学洢</span><span class="smcs">《核舟记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:窗下(窗户下;上学读书);窗友(旧时指塾中同学);窗格(有格子的窗户;又称窗槅);窗子眼(窗格子);窗下功夫(指学问)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> <span class="encs">[解]</span>∶一种小的开口，尤指在中耳与内耳之间的骨上有膜覆盖的两个小口之一 <span class="encs">[fenestra]</span>。如:前庭窗;蜗窗</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
