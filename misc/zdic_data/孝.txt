                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>孝</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> ㄒㄧㄠˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>对父母尽心奉养并顺从：～敬。～廉（①中国汉代选拔官吏的科目之一，&ldquo;孝&rdquo;指孝子；&ldquo;<span class="diczx3">廉</span>&rdquo;指廉洁的人；②中国明、清两代对举人的称呼）。～道。～子贤孙。</li>
	<li>居丧的事：守～。吊～。</li>
	<li>丧服：戴～。～衣。</li>
	<li>姓。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> filial piety, obedience; mourning</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Kindespflicht, Pietät  (S)​,Xiao  (Eig, Fam)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> piété filiale,deuil</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">孝</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>孝</strong> <span class="dicpy">xi&agrave;o</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xi&agrave;o.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从老省，从子，像一个孩子扶持老人之状。本义:尽心奉养和服从父母)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[filial;dutiful]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孝，善事父母者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孝利亲也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《墨子经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子爱利亲谓之孝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《贾子道术》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">慈惠爱亲为孝。协时肇享为孝、五宗安之曰孝，秉德不回曰孝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周书&middot;谥法》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">众之本教曰孝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;祭义》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫孝，德之本也。又，天之经也，民之行也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孝经》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孝，文之本也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《国语&middot;周语》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孝，礼之始也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;文公二年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">中和祗庸孝友。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大司乐》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">靡有不孝，自求伊祜。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂&middot;泮水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">孝悌之义。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;梁惠王上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">性婉顺有容德，事父母以孝闻。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《旧唐书》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孝弟(孝顺父母，敬爱兄长);孝慈(孝顺父母，慈爱幼弱);孝养(孝顺父母，奉养父母)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 祭，祭祀 <span class="encs">[sacrifice]</span>。向神或祖先供财物以示感激</p>
<p><span class="diczx2">禹</span>，吾无间然矣。菲饮食而致孝乎鬼神&hellip;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 能继先人之志 <span class="encs">[succeed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">追孝于前文人。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;文侯之命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 居丧 <span class="encs">[mourn]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">后丧母，居丧，哀毁骨立。人云:&ldquo;</span><span class="diczx2">崔九</span>作孝，风吹即倒。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史&middot;崔逞传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孝门(孝悌之家);孝家(居丧守孝的人家);孝堂(灵堂)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 孝敬 <span class="encs">[present gifts to one&rsquo;s elders or superiors]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:孝爱(孝敬爱重)</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx1">原来这婆娘自从药死了</span><span class="diczx2">武大</span>，那里肯戴孝。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:孝白(举丧时穿的白色孝服);穿孝;带孝;挂孝;重孝;热孝;脱孝</span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:吊孝;谢孝</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 居丧的人 <span class="encs">[mourner]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">今日与</span><span class="diczx2">谢</span>孝剧谈一出来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语》</span>。按:时 <span class="diczx2">谢玄</span>居父丧，故称 <span class="diczx2">谢</span>孝</p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 姓</p>
