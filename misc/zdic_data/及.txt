						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>及</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">j&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧˊ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>从后头跟上：来得～。赶不～。</li>
	<li>达到：～格。～第（古代科举考试中选，特指考取进士）。普～。过犹不～。</li>
	<li>趁着，乘：～时。～早。～锋而试。</li>
	<li>连词，和，跟：阳光、空气～水是生物生存的基本条件。以～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">及</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>及</strong> <span class="dicpy">j&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/j&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。甲骨文字形，从人，从手。表示后面的人赶上來用手抓住前面的人。本义:追赶上，抓住)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[overtake;catch up with]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及，逮也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">使人追</span><span class="diczx2">宋义子</span>，及之 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>，杀之。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记`项羽本纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">无庸，将自及。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:赶不及(来不及);企及(希望赶上);來得及(能够赶上);及面(见面);及时应令(与季节令令相适应)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 至，达到 <span class="encs">[attain;reach]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及，至也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及期。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼聘礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不及黄泉，无相見也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传隐公元年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及郡下，诣太守，说如此。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陶潜</span><span class="smcs">《桃花源记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及诸河，则在舟中矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传僖公三十三年》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">汤熨之所及也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子喻老》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">针石之所及也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">将有及。(还來得及。)</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">骇而图之，岂将有及乎?&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">贾谊</span><span class="smcs">《论积贮疏》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">祸且及汝。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:及瓜(到了出嫁的年龄);及瓜而代(指为官任职期满，由人接代);及事(女子到结婚年龄);及肩(高仅与肩齐，比喻相差甚远)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 待，等到 <span class="encs">[wait]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及日中则如盘盂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《列子汤问》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及日中如探汤。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">鲁肃</span>过 <span class="diczx2">寻阳</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《资治通鉴汉纪》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及敌枪再击。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">徐珂</span><span class="smcs">《清稗类钞战事类》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">及既上。(等到已经登上。及:等到。既:已经。)&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span><span class="diczx2">姚鼐</span><span class="smcs">《登泰山記》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3"> 如:及夫(等到)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 遭受 <span class="encs">[suffer from]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">铁铦短者及乎敌。(及乎敌，被敌所伤，极言战争之激烈。)&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《韩非子五蠹》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:及难(遭到灾难);及祸(遭灾难);及溺呼船(喻祸到临头，求救无及);及身(亲身受到)</span></p>
