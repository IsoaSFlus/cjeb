						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>搅</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">攪</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">jiǎo  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> ㄐㄧㄠˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>扰乱：～乱。～哄。～闹。～扰。</li>
	<li>拌：～拌。～匀。～动。～浑。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> disturb, agitate, stir up</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> anregen, bewegen ,ärgern, belästige ,durcheinander bringen, beunruhigen ,mischen, mixen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> remuer,mélanger,déranger</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>搅</strong></p>
<p>撹 <span class="dicpy">jiǎo</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jiǎo.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从手，觉声。本义:扰乱)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[disturb]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">搅，乱也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">袛搅我心。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;何人斯》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:搅攘(扰乱);搅恼(搅扰);搅家精(拨弄是非、寻衅闹事，搅得家庭不和的人);搅尸灵(方言。顽皮、捣乱，使不安宁)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 混合;拌合;搅拌 <span class="encs">[stir;mix]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:搅合(搅拌);搅海翻江(形容水势浩大。多比喻力量或声势极其壮大)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 胡闹;嬉戏 <span class="encs">[act wilfully and make a scene]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">这不是搅吗!&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《儿女英雄传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:搅七念三(胡闹);搅诨(戏谑)</span></p>
