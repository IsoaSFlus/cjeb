						<p>● <strong>刿</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">劌</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">gu&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄍㄨㄟˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>刺伤。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> to cut, injure, stab, stick on</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schneiden, kürzen</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> couper,blesser</p></div>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>刿</strong></p>
<p>劌 <span class="dicpy">gu&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/gu&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从刀，岁声。本义:刺伤，划伤)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[stab]</span>。如:刿目(刺眼，触目)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 割开，切口 <span class="encs">[cut off]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">是以圣人方而不割，廉而不刿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《老子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;会&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">hu&igrave;</span>)。聚合，汇合 <span class="encs">[meet;get together]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天地相对，日月相刿。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《太玄&middot;玄告》</span></span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>
