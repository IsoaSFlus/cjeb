<p><span class="dicpy">yǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧㄤˇ</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>卬</strong> <span class="dicpy">&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈代〉</p>
<p>我 <span class="encs">[I]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">招招舟子，人涉卬否。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;匏有苦叶》</span></span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卬</strong> <span class="dicpy">&aacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 通&ldquo;昂&rdquo;。抬起;抬高 <span class="encs">[hold (one&#39;s head) high]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卬车以其辕表门。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;昭公八年》</span></span><span class="diczx2">范守</span>注</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">万物卬贵，过平一钱，则以平贾卖与民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;食货志》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 激励 <span class="encs">[excite]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">意慷慨而自卬。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">司马相如</span><span class="smcs">《长门赋》</span></p>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>卬</strong> <span class="dicpy">yǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (&ldquo;仰&rdquo;的古字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 向上;抬头向上 <span class="encs">[face upward]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卬，望欲有所庶及也。从匕，从卩，会意。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">瞻卬昊天。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;云汉》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">卬引弓射。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">邵长蘅</span><span class="smcs">《青门剩稿》</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 仰仗;依赖 <span class="encs">[rely on]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">上足卬则下可用也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《荀子&middot;议兵》</span></span></p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/536C.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】五剛切【集韻】魚剛切【韻會】疑剛切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>諤平聲。【說文】我也。【詩&middot;邶風】卬須我友。【爾雅註】卬，猶姎也。【疏】女人稱我曰姎，由其語轉，故曰卬。</p>

<p>又激厲也。【司馬相如&middot;長門賦】貫歷覽其中操兮，意慷慨而自卬。</p>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/536C.svg">                          <p>望，欲有所庶及也。从匕从卪。《詩》曰：&ldquo;高山卬止。&rdquo;伍岡切</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          『說文解字注』<hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/536C_1.svg" /> <span class="swbf">(卬)</span> <span class="ywyy">望也。</span> <span class="swdz">鉉無也，非。卬與仰義别，仰訓舉，卬訓望。今則仰行而卬廢，且多改卬爲仰矣。小雅車舝曰：高山卬止。箋云：卬慕。過秦論：常以十倍之地、百萬之衆，卬關而攻秦。俗本作叩、作仰，皆字誤、聲誤耳。晉語：如川然，有原，以御浦而後大。孔晁本作卬浦。牛亮反。言川仰浦而大，人仰教而成。廣雅：仰，恃也。仰亦卬之誤。大雅傳曰：顒顒卬卬，盛皃。引伸之義也。釋詁、毛傳皆曰：卬，我也。語言之叚借也。</span> <span class="ywyy">欲有所庶及也。从匕卪。</span> <span class="swdz">匕同比，庶及意。庶及猶庶幾也。卪者，其欲庶及之所也。伍岡切。十部。亦讀去聲。</span> <span class="ywyy">詩曰：高山卬止。</span></p>                          </div>
