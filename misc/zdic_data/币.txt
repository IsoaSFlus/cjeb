                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>币</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">幣</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">b&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>钱币，交换各种商品的媒介：货～。外～。人民～。～值（货币的价值）。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> currency, coins, legal tender</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span>  Münze, Währung  (S)​,Geld</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> monnaie,pièce,devise</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">币</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>币</strong></p>
<p>幣 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从巾，敝声。从巾表示与布帛有关。本义:古人用作礼物的丝织品)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[silks]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幣，帛也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">四曰幣贡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《周礼&middot;大宰》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">司农</span>注:&ldquo;绣帛。&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">幣曰量幣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;曲礼》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">用圭璧更皮幣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">事之以皮幣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">寡君是故使</span><span class="diczx2">吉</span>( <span class="diczx2">游吉</span>)奉其皮幣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;襄公二十八年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">请具车马皮幣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《战国策&middot;齐策三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:皮币(兽皮和缯布);币玉(帛和玉，祭祀用品);币号(祭祀用的物品名称);币献(进献的礼品)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 泛指车马皮帛玉器等礼物 <span class="encs">[present]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凡执幣者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《仪礼&middot;十相见礼》</span>。疏:&ldquo;玉马皮圭璧帛，皆称幣。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">宋公</span>以幣请于 <span class="diczx2">卫</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《左传&middot;隐公八年》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">惠王</span>患之，乃令 <span class="diczx2">张仪</span>佯去 <span class="diczx2">秦</span>，厚幣委质事 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;屈原贾生列传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:币马(用作礼物的马匹)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 泛指财物 <span class="encs">[property;belongings]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">以珠玉为上幣，以黄金为中幣，以刀布为下幣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子&middot;国蓄》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 货币，钱 <span class="encs">[coin;money;currency]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有司以幣轻多奸。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;武帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:金币，银币;外币;纸币;硬币</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>币</strong></p>
<p>幣 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p>赠送 <span class="encs">[present]</span>。</p><p><span class="diczx3">如:币仪(敬献礼品);币器(赠给丧家奠仪与随葬品)</span></p>
<p>◎ <strong>币</strong></p>
<p>幣 <span class="dicpy">b&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/b&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈形〉</p>
