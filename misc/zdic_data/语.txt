						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>语</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">語</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yǔ  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>话：～言。汉～。英～。～录。～汇。～重心长。</li>
	<li>指&ldquo;谚语&rdquo;或&ldquo;古语&rdquo;：～云：&ldquo;皮之不存，毛将焉附&rdquo;。</li>
	<li>代替语言的动作：手～。旗～。</li>
	<li>说：细～。低～。</li>
</ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">语</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>语</strong></p>
<p>語 <span class="dicpy">yǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，吾声。本义:谈论;议论;辩论)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[discuss;talk about;comment;argue;debate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">语，论也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于时语语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;大雅&middot;公刘》</span>。传:&ldquo;论难曰语。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦日，卒中往往语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">子不语:怪、力、乱、神。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;述而》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夏虫不可以语于冰者，笃于时也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:语怪(谈论怪物);语冰(谈论冰);语议(言谈议论)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 交谈，说话 <span class="encs">[speak;say;talk]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">语，言也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">语使能也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;文王世子》</span>。注:&ldquo;语，言也。&rdquo;</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">既歌而语。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">可与晤语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;陈风&middot;东门之池》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">笑语卒获。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;楚茨》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">尔将可与语大理矣。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《庄子&middot;秋水》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">旦日，卒中往往语，皆指目</span><span class="diczx2">陈胜</span>。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《史记&middot;陈涉世家》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">食不语，寝不言。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《论语&middot;乡党》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乃向西北奋头，喃喃细语。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:语涩(说话艰难，不流利);语薄言轻(语言轻薄;说话轻佻，不稳重);语吐珠玑(说话、语言像珠玑般优美);语不间歇;语冰(比喻人的识量受到时、地所拘束，见识不广);语语(语其所当语。即说所该说的话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 鸟兽虫类鸣叫 <span class="encs">[cry;chirp;roar]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">君裙香暖金泥凤，画梁语燕惊残梦。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">前蜀</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">牛峤</span><span class="smcs">《菩萨蛮》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:语风(在风中鸣叫);语燕(会说话的燕子);语鸟(会说话的鸟);语关关(形容鸟儿鸣叫之声)</span></p>

<p>◎ <strong>语</strong></p>
<p>語 <span class="dicpy">yǔ</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yǔ.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 说的话 <span class="encs">[spoken language;word]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">张胜</span>闻之，恐前语发。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;李广苏建传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">语曰:&ldquo;唇亡则齿寒。&rdquo;&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《谷梁传&middot;僖公二年》</span></span></p>

<p><span class="diczx3">又如:语体诗(用口语写的诗);语话(话语;言语);土语(土话);评语(评论的话)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 特指谚语、古语或成语 <span class="encs">[idiom;set phrase;proverb;saying]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:语例(用语的惯例);口头语;熟语;韵语;妙语;俚语;豪语</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 词语 <span class="encs">[word]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为人性僻躭佳句，语不惊人死不休。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">杜甫</span><span class="smcs">《江上值水如海势聊短述》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:语惊四座;语泄(密语外泄);语义(也称词义。指词语的意义);语妙天下(言语精妙，无人可及)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 语言 <span class="encs">[language]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有</span><span class="diczx2">楚大夫</span>于此，欲其子之 <span class="diczx2">齐</span>语也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;滕文公下》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:语支(语言学。依谱系分类法分出的比语族小的语言系统);语言文字(语言学与文字学的合称);语简意赅(语言简练，意思完备);语学(研究言语、文字的性质及用法的学问)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 用以示意的动作或信号 <span class="encs">[signal]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:目语;手语;灯语</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 语法学科用来表示句子成人的术语 <span class="encs">[term used in analysis of sentences]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:主语;谓语;宾语、表语、状语</span></p>
