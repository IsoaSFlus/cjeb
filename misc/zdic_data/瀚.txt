                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>瀚</strong> <span class="dicpy">hàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，翰声。本义:瀚海:古代指北方的大海。明代以来指戈壁沙漠)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[Baikal lake]</span>。其含义随时代而变。原指北方的大湖。或指贝加尔湖。唐代是指蒙古高原大沙漠以北及西至今准噶尔盆地一带广大地区的泛称。亦多用为征战、武功等典故</p><p><span class="diczx1">校尉羽书飞瀚海，单于猎火照</span><span class="diczx2">狼山</span>。—— <span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">高适</span><span class="smcs">《燕歌行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 沙漠 <span class="encs">[desert]</span>。如:瀚漠(北方大沙漠)</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>瀚</strong> <span class="dicpy">hàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈形〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 水浩大的样子;广大 <span class="encs">[flooding;vast]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">浩浩瀚瀚。——<span class="smcs">《淮南子·俶真》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:瀚瀚(广大的样子);浩瀚(广大辽阔的样子);瀚漫(广大的样子);瀚浩(浩瀚。广大的样子)</p><p>◎ <strong>瀚</strong> <span class="dicpy">hàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p>澣。洗涤 <span class="encs">[wash]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">空负黄花羞短发，寒衣三瀚客心惊。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">宋琬</span>诗</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/瀚海">瀚海</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
