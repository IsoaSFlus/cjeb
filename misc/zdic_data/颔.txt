                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>颔</strong></p><p>頷 <span class="dicpy">hàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从页( <span class="dicpy">xié</span>)，含声。从<span class="yhcs">“页”</span>，表示与头有关。本义:下巴颏)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[chin]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颔，含也，口含物之车也。——<span class="smcs">《释名》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">颐，或曰颔车。——<span class="smcs">《释名·释形体》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">绝其颔。——<span class="smcs">《公羊传·宣公六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">夫千金之珠，必在九重之渊，而骊龙颔下。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·列御寇》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">生燕颔虎颈，飞而食肉，此万里侯相也。——<span class="smcs">《后汉书·班超传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">相逢应不识，满颔白髭须。——</span><span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《东南行》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:颔车(齿下骨的别名);颔雪(颔下的白须);颔颏(下巴)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 位于颈的前上方，相当于颏部的下方，结喉的上方 <span class="encs">[submental region]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">热争则腰痛，不可用俯仰，腹满泄，两颔痛。——<span class="smcs">《素问·刺热篇》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>颔</strong></p><p>頷 <span class="dicpy">hàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/hàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 点头 <span class="encs">[nod]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">逆于门者，颔之而己。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十六年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">但微颔之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">欧阳修</span><span class="smcs">《归田录》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:颔首;颔头(点头);颔命(接受旨意)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 摇动 <span class="encs">[shake]</span>。如;颔颐(动动腮巴)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit
