                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>译</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">譯</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄧˋ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>把一种语言文字依照原义改变成另一种语言文字：～本。～文。～注。～著。～制。～音。口～。笔～。意～。直～。翻～。</li>
	<li>解释；阐述。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> translate; decode; encode</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> übersetzen, dolmetschen  (V)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> traduire,interpréter</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">译</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>译</strong></p>
<p>譯 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从言，睪( <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> )声。本义:翻译，把一种语言文字翻译成另一种语言文字)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[translate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">译，传译四夷之言者。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">译，传也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《方言十三》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">北方曰译。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;王制》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">重舌之人九译。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">张衡</span><span class="smcs">《东京赋》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">译者称西人。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">薛福成</span><span class="smcs">《观巴黎油画记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">译欧西人之言。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《谭嗣同传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:汉语被译成日语;译品(翻译的作品);译义(意译);译写(翻译写作);译经(翻译经典)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 解释;阐述 <span class="encs">[explain]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">夫圣人为天口，贤者为圣译。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《潜夫论》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 通&ldquo;择&rdquo;。选择 <span class="encs">[choose;select]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">周</span>道衰微，失爵亡邦，后嗣乖散，各相土译居。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《隶释&middot;汉孟郁修尧庙碑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:译居(择居)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>译</strong></p>
<p>譯 <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 翻译人员 <span class="encs">[interpreter]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">于是乃召</span><span class="diczx2">越</span>译，乃 <span class="diczx2">楚</span>说之。&mdash;&mdash; <span class="diczx2">刘向</span><span class="smcs">《说苑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:译费(支付给翻译人员的钱);译界(翻译界);译员(翻译工作者)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 异域 <span class="encs">[foreign lands]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">沧波伏忠信，译语辨讴谣。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">顾况</span><span class="smcs">《送从兄使新罗》</span></p>

						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上" target="_blank">酉集上</a>】【<a href="/zd/kx/?zm=酉集上&amp;bs=言" target="_blank">言</a>】
                              譯·康熙筆画：<a href="/zd/kx/bh/?bh=20" target="_blank">20</a>　·部外筆画：13</div>
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/8B6F.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】羊昔切【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】夷益切，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>音繹。【說文】傳譯四夷之言者。【揚子&middot;方言】傳也。</p>
<p>又見也。【註】傳宣語卽相見。【禮&middot;王制】北方曰譯。【疏】通傳北方語官謂之譯。譯，<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>也。謂<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/28EF0.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𨻰</span>說外內之言。劉氏曰：譯，釋也。猶言謄也。謂以彼此言語相謄釋而通之也。越裳氏重九譯而朝是也。【周禮&middot;秋官&middot;象胥&middot;賈疏】北方曰譯者，譯卽易，謂換易言語，使相解也。</p>
<p>又【正字通】凡詁釋經義亦曰譯。</p>
<p>又官名。【前漢&middot;百官公卿表】大鴻臚屬官有行人譯官。</p>
<p>又典屬國屬官有九譯令。</p>
<p>又叶弋灼切，音藥。【張華&middot;七命】語不傳於輶軒，地不被乎正朔，莫不駿奔稽顙，委質重譯。　【說文】本作<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/27B33.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𧬳</span>。</p>
