                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>涎</strong> <span class="dicpy">xián</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xián.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从水，延声。本义:唾沫，口水)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[saliva]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">涎，小儿唾也。——<span class="smcs">《三苍》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">喷浪飞唌(涎)。——</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《江赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">群犬垂涎。——</span><span class="diczx2">柳宗元</span><span class="smcs">《三戒》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:口角流涎;垂涎三尺</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 粘液 <span class="encs">[mucus]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">咳则腰背相引而痛，甚则咳涎。——<span class="smcs">《素问》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>涎</strong> <span class="dicpy">xián</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/xián.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 流口水 <span class="encs">[drool]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">见</span><span class="diczx2">元妃</span>痰塞口涎，不能言语。——<span class="smcs">《红楼梦》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 贪羡，贪图 <span class="encs">[be greedy for]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">主人涎我色，奸我，不从，则鞭挞之。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">袁枚</span><span class="smcs">《新齐谐》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:涎利(贪图利益)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 吐;流 <span class="encs">[vomit]</span>。如:涎津溢(唾沫液);涎缕(吐丝)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 厚着脸皮;嬉皮笑脸 <span class="encs">[brazen it out]</span>。如:涎邓邓(含攫取挑逗的样子);涎皮(嘻皮赖脸，厚脸皮);涎脸(厚脸皮;嬉皮笑脸)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 拖;伸 <span
