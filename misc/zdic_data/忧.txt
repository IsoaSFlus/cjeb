                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">忧</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>忧</strong></p>
<p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">憂</span>）</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">yōu  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> ㄧㄡˉ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>发愁：～愤。～惧。～烦。～惶。～急。～煎。～思（➊忧念；➋忧愁的思绪）。～戚。杞人～天。</li>
	<li>可忧虑的事：～患。内～外患。乐以忘～。高枕无～。</li>
	<li>指父母之丧：丁～。</li>
</ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> sad, grieved; grief, melancholy</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> besorgt, abgequält</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> chagrin,souci,tristesse,mélancolie,se soucier de,s'affliger</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">忧</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>忧</strong></p>
<p>憂 <span class="dicpy">yōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p> (本字古作上&ldquo;页&rdquo;（xie）下&ldquo;心&rdquo;。会意。心中有了忧愁，必然在脸上(用&ldquo;页&rdquo;即人头代表)反映出来。后加&ldquo;攵&rdquo;( <span class="dicpy">suī</span> 表示行走)，形成&ldquo;憂&rdquo;字。&ldquo;忧&rdquo;是形声字。本义:担忧;发愁)同本义 <span class="encs">[worry;be worried]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忧，愁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忧，愁也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《玉篇》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忧悲者德之失也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《淮南子&middot;原道》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">勿忧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;丰》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">坎为加忧。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《易&middot;说卦》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">我心忧伤。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;小弁》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忧心忡忡。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;召南&middot;草虫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">何忧令名不彰。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《世说新语&middot;自新》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">心忧炭贱。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忧谗畏讥。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">范仲淹</span><span class="smcs">《岳阳楼记》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忧其民。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:忧忿滞中(中医指忧愁忿懑滞郁在胸中);忧蹙(因忧愁而紧锁眉头);忧愠(忧愁懊恼);忧忆(忧愁担心);忧心如捣(忧愁得像有什么东西捣心一样);忧邑(愁闷抑郁);忧畏(忧虑畏怯);忧世(为世时或时事而忧虑);忧怀(忧虑怀念);忧怯(担忧畏怯);忧天(担心天塌下来);忧公忘私(关心公益，不顾私利);忧鱼(担心水患)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<p>◎ <strong>忧</strong></p>
<p>憂 <span class="dicpy">yōu</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yōu.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈名〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 居丧 <span class="encs">[mourning]</span>。多指居父母丧</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">王宅忧，亮阴三祀。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;说命上》</span></span></p>

			<div class="gscdl">——《为政》子曰：“贤哉，回也！一箪食，一瓢饮，在陋巷，人不堪其<span>忧</span>，回也不改其乐。贤哉，回也！”</div><div class="gscdr">先秦·孔子弟子及其再传弟子 《《论语》十二章》</div><div class="gscdl">杞人有<span>忧</span>天地崩坠，身亡所寄，废寝食者。</div><div class="gscdr">《列子》《杞人忧天》</div><div class="gscdl">又有<span>忧</span>彼之所<span>忧</span>者，因往晓之，曰：天，积气耳，亡处亡气。</div><div class="gscdr">《列子》《杞人忧天》</div><div class="gscdl">若屈伸呼吸，终日在天中行止，奈何<span>忧</span>崩坠乎？其人曰：天果积气，日月星宿，不当坠邪？晓之者曰：日月星宿，亦积气中之有光耀者，只使坠，亦不能有所中伤。</div><div class="gscdr">《列子》《杞人忧天》</div><div class="gscdl">若躇步跳蹈，终日在地上行止，奈何<span>忧</span>其坏？其人舍然大喜。晓之者亦舍然大喜。</div><div class="gscdr">《列子》《杞人忧天》</div><div class="gscdl">然后知生于<span>忧</span>患，而死于安乐也。[1]</div><div class="gscdr">《孟子·告子下》《生于忧患 死于安乐》</div>      </div>
