						<p>● <strong>乂</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">y&igrave;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˋ</span></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<ol>
	<li>治理，安定：～安（太平无事）。</li>
	<li>贤才：俊～在官。</li>
	<li>割：～草。～麦。</li>
</ol>

<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>乂</strong> <span class="dicpy">y&igrave;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&igrave;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>〈动〉</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (会意。从丿从乂相交。又作&ldquo;刈&rdquo;。本义:割草或收割谷类植物)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[mow]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乂，芟草也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 治理;安定 <span class="encs">[govern;settle;stable]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">乂，治也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">有能俾乂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《书&middot;尧典》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">保国乂民。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《汉书&middot;武五子传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">朝野安乂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《北史》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:乂安(太平无事)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/4E42.svg">                          <p>芟艸也。从丿从乀，相交。刈，乂或从刀。魚廢切</p>                          </div>

                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/4E42_43.svg" /><span class="swbf">(乂)</span><span class="ywyy">芟艸也。</span><span class="swdz">艸部曰：芟，㐅艸也。二篆爲轉注。周南曰：是刈是濩。周頌曰：奄觀銍艾。艾者，㐅之叚借字。銍者，所以㐅也。禾部曰：穫，㐅榖也。是則芟艸穫榖總謂之㐅。鄭箋詩云。芟末曰艾。刀部有<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206CE.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠛎</span>。金部有鎌，有鍥，所以芟艸也。銍則穫禾短鎌也。引申之，㐅訓治也。見諸經傳。許辟部云。䢃，治也。引唐書有能俾䢃。則䢃爲正字。</span><span class="ywyy">从丿乀相交。</span><span class="swdz">象左右去之會意也。魚廢切。十五部。</span></p>
