						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>● <strong>膀</strong></p>
<p><span class="dicpy">bǎng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎng.mp3"></a></span>  ㄅㄤˇ</span></p>
<ol>
	<li>胳膊的上部靠肩的部分：～臂。肩～。～阔腰圆。</li>
	<li>鸟类和昆虫的飞行器官：翅～。～儿。</li>
</ol>
<p><strong>其它字义</strong></p>

<p><span class="dicpy">p&aacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/p&aacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄆㄤˊ</span></p>
<p>　◎ [膀胱]俗称尿脬，暂存尿液的囊状体，在骨盆腔内。</p>

                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>膀</strong></p>
<p>髈 <span class="dicpy">bǎng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/bǎng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p>名</p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从肉，旁声。本义:肩膀)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[shoulder]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">十分腰围膀阔。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《水浒传》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:膀阔腰圆;腰细膀宽</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 臂膀。指肩和肘之间的手臂 <span class="encs">[upper arm]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:膀扇子(方言。臂膀)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 兽类或家畜腿的上部或前肢和躯干相连的部分 <span class="encs">[upper leg]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">肉则羊膀豕胁，脂肤相半。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《初学记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 鸟类等的翅膀 <span class="encs">[wing]</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 另见 <span class="dicpy">b&agrave;ng;pāng;p&aacute;ng</span></p>
