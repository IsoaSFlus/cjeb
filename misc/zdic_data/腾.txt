                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">腾</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>腾</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">騰</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">t&eacute;ng  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> ㄊㄥˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>奔跑，跳跃：～骧。奔～。欢～。～跃。～越。</li>	<li>上升：～空。～云驾雾。升～。飞～。飞黄～达。</li>	<li>空出来，挪移：～退。～个地方。</li>	<li>词尾，用在动词后面表示动作的反复连续（读轻声）：翻～。折～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> fly; gallop; run; prance; rise</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> aufsteigen, sich erheben ,auseinander ziehen, ausziehen ,entspringen, anschwellen ,galoppieren ,herumhüpfen, herumtanzen ,schweben</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> galoper,bondir,déplacer</p></div>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>腾</strong></p>
<p>騰 <span class="dicpy">t&eacute;ng</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&eacute;ng.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从马，朕( <span class="dicpy">zh&egrave;n</span>)声。本义:马奔腾)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[gallop]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腾，传也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。谓传车马驰。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腾，奔也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广雅》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腾众车使径待。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《离骚》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腾驴驘以驰逐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;愍命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腾驾步游。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;大招》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">士饱而歌，马腾于槽。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">韩愈</span><span class="smcs">《平淮西碑》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:奔腾(许多马跳跃着奔跑);腾夷(在平地上奔驰。喻宦途顺利);腾逐(奔驰追赶);腾藉(奔腾践踏);腾噬(奔腾咬啮);腾驾(使车马奔驰);腾嬉(奔腾嬉戏)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 奔驰 <span class="encs">[run quickly;speed]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">潜龙腾渊。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>&middot; <span class="diczx2">梁启超</span><span class="smcs">《饮冰室合集&middot;文集》</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 跳跃 <span class="encs">[jump]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">遂相腾击。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《聊斋志异&middot;促织》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:腾那(窜跳躲闪。指武功);腾身(跃身，纵身);腾超(跳跃腾空);腾距(跳跃);腾身而过;腾希(腾跃于空中);腾奔(腾跃奔驰);腾轩(腾跃高举);腾倚(或腾跃或倚立);腾闪(腾跃闪避);腾驰(腾跃奔驰);腾跣(赤足腾跃);腾奋(腾跃奋飞);万马奔腾;腾踯(腾空跳跃，向上跳);腾霄(腾空，冲天);腾凌(腾跃);腾逸(腾空跨越);腾击(腾空攻击)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 使房屋空出 <span class="encs">[vacate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不如就把书院腾了出来。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《官场现形记》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:腾房子给客人住</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 升入空中;上升 <span class="encs">[rise]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">天气下降，地气上腾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《礼记&middot;月令》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:升腾(向上升起);腾上(向上升);腾降(升降);腾掉(升空漂动);腾矫(飞升，升高);腾薄(上下起伏);腾海(升于海上);腾起(升起);腾蛟起凤;腾化(飞升成仙);腾达(升腾;上浮);腾掷(向上飞起);腾冲(飞上天空);腾举(飞升);腾骞(飞升，升空)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 传递 <span class="encs">[文书]</span> <span class="encs">[transfer]</span></p>

<p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 翻腾 <span class="encs">[seethe]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">不震不腾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;鲁颂&middot;閟宫》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">百川沸腾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;小雅&middot;十月之交》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">忽风云腾涌，急系缆。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">陆游</span><span class="smcs">《过小孤山大孤山》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:腾波(翻腾的波浪);腾踊(水翻腾上涌);腾倾(腾涌倾注);腾溢 (翻腾漫溢);腾扬(翻腾上涌)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(9)</span> 驾;乘 <span class="encs">[ride]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">腾驴赢以驰逐。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《楚辞&middot;刘向&middot;九叹&middot;愍命》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:腾龙(驾龙，乘龙)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(10)</span> 凌驾 <span class="encs">[override]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">为人臣者，变故易常，而巧官以谄上，谓之腾。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《管子》</span></span></p>
