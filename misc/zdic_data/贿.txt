                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>贿</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">賄</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">huì  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span>  ㄏㄨㄟˋ</span></p><ol><li>财物：“以尔车来，以我～迁”。</li><li>赠送财物：厚～之。</li><li>以财物买通公职人员：行～。受～。～赂。～通。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> bribe; bribes; riches, wealth</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Bestechung  (S)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> pot-de-vin</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">贿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>贿</strong></p><p>賄 <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从贝，有声。本义:财物)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[property;belongings]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贿，财也。——<span class="smcs">《尔雅》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">窃贿为盗。——<span class="smcs">《左传·文公十八年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以尔车来，以我贿迁。——<span class="smcs">《诗·卫风·氓》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">商贾阜通货贿。——<span class="smcs">《周礼·太宰》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“金玉曰货，布帛曰贿。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贿用束纺。——<span class="smcs">《仪礼·聘礼》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“贿予人财之言也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">先诸侯之贿，聚于公室。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十四年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">假货居贿。——<span class="smcs">《国语·晋语》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:贿交(以财物相交);贿货(财物);贿赂(财货)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 贿赂的财物 <span class="encs">[bribe]</span>。如:行贿;受贿</p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>贿</strong></p><p>賄 <span class="dicpy">huì</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/huì.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 赠送财物 <span class="encs">[give as a present]</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">孟献子</span>聘于 <span class="diczx2">周</span>，王以为有礼，厚贿之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·宣公九年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">贿用</span><span class="diczx2">周</span>室之璧。——<span class="smcs">《穆天子传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx2">宋</span>人重贿之。——<span class="smcs">《左传·襄公二十年》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:贿赠(以财物奉赠);贿谢(赠礼酬谢。亦指赠送或酬谢的礼物);贿币(赠送的礼物)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 贪财 <span class="encs">[be covetous]</span>。如:贿求(以行贿手段营求好处);贿交(因贪图其财富而与之结交)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 用财物买通别人 <span class="encs">[bribe;offer bribes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">政刑驰紊，贿货公行。——<span class="smcs">《陈书·后主沈皇后传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:贿免(用财物买通);贿嘱(贿赂嘱托);贿遗(行贿的财物);贿卖(收受贿赂，卖官爵，通关节)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 受贿 <span class="encs">[accept bribes]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">居官必贿。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">非贿得之。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">崔铣</span><span class="smcs">《记王忠肃公翱三事》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">污贿不饬。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">周容</span><span class="smcs">《芋老人传》</span></p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr
