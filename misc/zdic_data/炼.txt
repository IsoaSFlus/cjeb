                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>炼</strong></p><p><strong>（<span class="diczx3">煉</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">liàn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liàn.mp3"></a></span> ㄌㄧㄢˋ</span></p><ol><li>用火烧制或用加热等方法使物质纯净、坚韧、浓缩：～钢。～焦。～油。～乳。～狱。锤～。</li><li>用心琢磨使精练：～字。～句。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> <p>smelt, refine; distill, condense</p></p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> schmelzen, härten, stählen  (V)</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">炼</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>炼</strong></p><p>煉、鍊 <span class="dicpy">liàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/liàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从火，柬声。本义:提炼)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义。用加热等方法使物质溶化并趋于纯净或坚韧 <span class="encs">[smelt;refine]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">炼，铄冶金也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">王筠</span>曰:<span class="yhcs">“煉鍊同字。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">鍊药瞩虚幌。——</span><span class="diczx2">江淹</span><span class="smcs">《杂体诗·王征君》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">公宫令舍之堂，皆以炼铜为柱质。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·十过》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">何意百炼钢，化为绕指柔。——</span><span class="diczx2">刘琨</span><span class="smcs">《重赠卢谌》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:提炼(用理化方法从化合物或混合物中提取);精炼(提炼精华，除去杂质);炼石(烧炼丹石);炼金(炼丹);炼汞(道家烧炼金石药物，以制成丹药)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 修炼;锻炼;造就 <span class="encs">[cultivate and temper oneself]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">服此二物，炼人身体。——<span class="smcs">《抱朴子·金丹》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:炼丹(以气功修炼人体精、气、神，谓之内丹);炼性(道教谓之修炼心性);炼养(修炼养生)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 比喻下苦功以求其精 <span class="encs">[polish]</span>。如:炼贫(精于耍贫嘴)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/炼丹">炼丹</a></span><span class="crefe"><a
