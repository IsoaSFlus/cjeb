                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>类</strong></p><p>類 <span class="dicpy">lèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从犬，頪( <span class="dicpy">lèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lèi.mp3"></a></span> )声。从<span class="yhcs">“犬”</span>，因犬种类最相似。本义:种类)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[kind;class;type;category]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">类，种类相似，惟犬最甚。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆守株之类。——<span class="smcs">《韩非子·五蠹》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">无法者以类举。——<span class="smcs">《荀子·王制》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">壹统类。—<span class="smcs">《荀子·非十二子》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">其负类反伦，不可胜言也。——<span class="smcs">《列子·仲尼》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">王之不王，是折枝之类也。——<span class="smcs">《孟子·梁惠王上》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">同类相从，同声相应，固天之理也。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·渔父》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">纸灰之类冒之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">沈括</span><span class="smcs">《梦溪笔谈·活板》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又</p><p><span class="diczx1">亦类此耳。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">梨栗枣柿之类。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2">司马光</span><span class="smcs">《训俭示康》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 又如:类考(学院的科考);类族(事物因习性相近而形成的类别);类神(迷信占卜名词。六壬课中十二个用来判别吉凶的<span class="yhcs">“十二支神”</span>的总称)</p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 族类 <span class="encs">[race]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">吾三番擒之而不杀，诚欲服其心，不欲灭其类也。——<span class="smcs">《三国演义》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 事例;条例 <span class="encs">[example;regulations;rules]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">举类迩而见义远。——<span class="smcs">《史记·屈原贾生列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(7)</span> 通<span class="yhcs">“颣”</span>。缺点，毛病 <span class="encs">[fault]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">民之生也，辟则愚，闭则类。——<span class="smcs">《管子·乘马》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">大者大类，小者则治。——<span class="smcs">《管子·地员》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(8)</span> 古祭名，祭天 <span class="encs">[sacrificial rites;offer a sacrifice to heaven]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">肆类于上帝，禋于六宗。——<span class="smcs">《书·尧典下》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">是类是妈祃，是致是附。——<span class="smcs">《诗·大雅·皇矣》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>类</strong></p><p>類 <span class="dicpy">lèi</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/lèi.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 相似;像 <span class="encs">[resemble;be similar to]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">遂入，杀</span><span class="diczx2">孟阳</span>于床。曰:<span class="yhcs">“非君也，不类。”</span>——<span class="smcs">《左传·庄公八年》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不类前人。——<span class="smcs">《论衡·自纪》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">与</span><span class="diczx2">张</span>氏之吝书若不相类。—— <span                           <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7C7B.svg">						  <p>【五音篇海】與類同。詳頁部類字註。</p><p>（類）【唐韻】【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>力遂切，音戾。【爾雅·釋詁】善也。【詩·大雅】克明克類。【箋】類，善也。勤施無私曰類。【又】孝子不匱，永錫爾類。【傳】類，善也。</p><p>又【玉篇】種類也。【易·乾卦】則各從其類也。</p><p>又【繫辭】方以類聚。</p><p>又比也。【禮·學記】知類通達。【註】知事義之比也。</p><p>又【緇衣】子曰：下之事上也，身不正，言不信，則義不壹，行無類也。【註】類謂比式。【疏】言行之無恆，不可比類也。</p><p>又【左傳·襄九年】晉君類能而使之。【註】隨所能。</p><p>又肖似也。【禮·曲禮】諸侯旣塟見天子曰類見。【註】代父受國。類，猶象也。執皮帛，象諸侯之禮見也。</p><p>又祭名。【書·舜典】肆類于上帝。【詩·大雅】是類是禡。【周禮·春官·肆師】類造上帝。【鄭註】類禮依郊祀而爲之者。</p><p>又【爾雅·釋魚】龜左倪不類。【疏】倪，庳也，不發聲也。謂行時頭左邊庳下者名類。</p><p>又【山海經】亶爰之山有獸焉，其狀如貍而有髦，其名曰類。自爲牝牡，食者不妒。</p><p>又【爾雅翼】載兩類曰<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2676D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦝭</span>類、奇類，卽兩體自爲牝牡者。<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2676D.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𦝭</span>與膟同。</p><p>又國名。【後漢·西域傳】有蒲類國。</p><p>又姓。【史記·梁孝王世家】類犴反。【正字通】宋類演，福州寧德尉。</p><p>又【集韻】盧對切，音壘。偏也。【左傳·昭十六年】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/206EC.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠛬</span>之頗類。</p><p>又【集韻】劣戌切，音律。似也。【曹植·七啟】麗草交植，殊品詭類。綠葉朱榮，熙天曜日。　【說文】種類相似，唯犬爲甚。从犬頪聲。</p><p>（類）
