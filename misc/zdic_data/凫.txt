                          <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凫</span><span class="span dictname"> 基本解释</span></h2>
                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">

						<p>● <strong>凫</strong></p><p><strong>（<img class="jbjs_ico" src="/images/yico_ft.svg" title="繁体字"><span class="diczx3">鳬</span>）</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">f&uacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&uacute;.mp3"></a></span>  ㄈㄨˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>水鸟，俗称&ldquo;野鸭&rdquo;，似鸭，雄的头部绿色，背部黑褐色，雌的全身黑褐色，常群游湖泊中，能飞。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/浮">浮</a>&rdquo;。</li>	<li>〔～茈〕古书上指&ldquo;荸荠&rdquo;。</li>	<li>同&ldquo;<a href="/hans/洑">洑</a>&rdquo;。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> wild duck, teal; swim</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> canard sauvage</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">凫</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凫</strong></p>
<p>鳬 <span class="dicpy">f&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 动物名。俗名野鸭 <span class="encs">[teal]</span>。鸟纲，雁鸭目，雁鸭科。体长60余厘米，喙宽而扁平且短，除繁殖期外，都过群集生活</p>
<p><span class="diczx1">凫，水鸭也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《广韵》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 另一说凫是&ldquo;家鸭&rdquo;</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:凫雏(幼凫);凫雁(野鸭与大雁);凫胫(野鸭的小腿)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>凫</strong></p>
<p>鳬 <span class="dicpy">f&uacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/f&uacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p>浮游;泅水 <span class="encs">[swim]</span>。</p>
<p><span class="diczx3">如:凫水(泅水);凫泛(浮游)</span></p>                    <div class="div copyright"> 【漢典】 </div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/9CE7.svg">						  <p>【唐韻】防無切【集韻】【韻會】馮無切【正韻】逢夫切，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠀤</span>音扶。【爾雅&middot;釋鳥】舒鳧，騖。【郭註】鴨也。【疏】野曰鳧，家曰鴨。【又】鳧，雁醜，其足蹼，其踵企。【郭註】鳧雁腳閒有幕蹼屬相著，飛卽伸其腳跟企直。【疏】醜類也。【又】鸍，沈鳧。【註】狀似鴨而小，背文靑色，<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/24C1E.svg"><span class="k_txt">𤰞</span>腳紅掌，短喙長尾。【詩&middot;鄭風】弋鳧與雁。</p><p>又【大雅】鳧鷖在涇。【註】鳧，水鳥。鷖，鳧屬。</p><p>又【南越志】有私鳧棲息松閒不水處，宿必以樹。</p><p>又【揚子方言郭註】江東有小鳧，其多無數，俗謂之寇鳧。</p><p>又【山海經】鹿臺山有鳥，狀如雄雞，人面，曰鳧徯。</p><p>又官名。【周禮&middot;冬官考工記】鳧氏爲鍾。【正字通】鳧入水不溺，以名鍾工，取虛浮之義。</p><p>又山名。【詩&middot;魯頌】保有鳧繹。【註】鳧繹，二山名。</p><p>又鳧麗山。見【山海經】。</p><p>又魚鳧，人名。蜀山氏之君也。見【成都記】。</p><p>又鳧茨，草名。【後漢&middot;劉玄傳】人掘鳧茨而食。</p><p>又【廣韻】茆鳧，葵也。俗省作鳧。</p>

						<div class="mini_h2">(说文解字未收录“凫”字头，请参考“<a href="/hans/鳧">鳧</a>”字：)</div>						<div class="mob">【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三">卷三</a>】【<a href="/zd/sw/?swju=卷三&amp;swbs=𠘧">𠘧</a>】</div>                          
						                            <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/swxz/9CE7.svg">                          <p>舒鳧，鶩也。从鳥<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20627.svg"><span class="k_txt">𠘧</span>聲。房無切文三</p>                          </div>
                                                                              <div class="swnr">
                          <span class="swsm">《說文解字注》</span><hr class="dichr">
                          <p><img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/xz/swdz/9CE7_7965.svg" /><span class="swbf">(鳧)</span><span class="ywyy">舒鳧，鶩也。</span><span class="swdz">釋鳥曰：舒鴈，鵝。舒鳧，鶩。内則注同舍人，李巡云：野曰鴈，家曰鵝。野曰鳧，家曰鶩。按野曰鴈、鳧，而畜於家者曰舒鴈、舒鳧，是爲鵝、鶩。舒者，謂其行舒遟不畏人也。詩弋鳧與鴈，以及他言鴻鴈鳧鷖，皆謂野鳥，非舒鳧、舒鴈也。大雅傳曰：鳧，水鳥也。鷖，鳧屬也。然則說文於鳧下舉舒鳧。葢謂統言可不別，但云舒鳧，則固析言之矣。尋許意不以鳧入鳥部而入几部，此句丩二部之例。<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A057.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪁗</span>之羽短不能飛，故其字從几，豈知野<img class="lazy k_img" data-original="//img.zdic.net/song/cn/2A057.svg"><span class="k_txt">𪁗</span>亦短羽而能飛乎。</span><span class="ywyy">从几鳥，几亦聲。</span><span class="swdz">各本作從鳥几聲，今補正。房無切。古音葢在四部。</span></p>                          </div>
