                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>篡</strong></p><p>簒 <span class="dicpy">cuàn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/cuàn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从竹，算声。本义:非法地夺取)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[usurp;seize]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">自</span><span class="diczx2">关</span>而西， <span class="diczx2">秦</span><span class="diczx2">晋</span>之间，凡取物而逆谓之篡。——<span class="smcs">《方言》</span>一</p><p><span class="diczx1">且如人有财，见篡于盗。——</span><span class="diczx2">王安石</span><span class="smcs">《原过》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">豪民子闻难，鸠宗族僮奴百许人，欲要篡以归。——</span><span class="diczx2">高启</span><span class="smcs">《书博鸡者事》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:篡逆(用强力夺取君位的叛逆);篡取(夺取);篡事(篡夺之事)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 特指臣子夺取君位 <span class="encs">[usurp]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">及</span><span class="diczx2">王莽</span>篡位， <span class="diczx2">歆</span>为国师。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·刘歆传》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(5)</span> 又如:篡臣(篡夺君权之臣);篡杀(弑君而夺其位);篡弑(犹篡杀);篡绝(篡夺君位而绝其后)</p><p><span class="cino">(6)</span> 以私意歪曲 <span class="encs">[twist]</span>。如:篡易</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/篡党">篡党</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/篡夺">篡夺</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/篡改">篡改</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/篡权">篡权</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/篡位">篡位</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>

                  <div class="content definitions gnr">
                    <div class="gycd"><div class="pz"><ruby><rbc><rb>篡</rb></rbc><rtc><rt>ㄘㄨㄢˋ</rt></rtc><rtc><rt>cuàn <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/gycd/zd/5534.ogg"></a></span> </rt></rtc></ruby></div><div class="gycd-item"><span class="gc_cx">动</span><ol><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">夺取。</span><span class="gc_yy">《汉书·卷五五·卫青传》：「大长公主执囚青，欲杀之。其友骑郎公孙敖与壮士往<b>篡</b>之。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">以不正当的手段夺取君位或权力地位。</span><span class="gc_yy">《孟子·万章上》：「而居尧之宫，逼尧之子， 是<b>篡</b>也，非天与也。」</span><span class="gc_yy">《后汉书·卷一四·宗室四王三侯传·齐武王縯传》：「自王莽<b>篡</b>汉，常愤愤，怀复社稷之虑。」</span></p></li><li><p class="def"><span class="gc_sy">猎取。</span><span class="gc_yy">《后汉书·卷八三·逸民传·序》：「鸿飞冥冥，弋者何<b>篡</b>焉。」</span></p></li></ol></div></div>

                          <img class="lazy kxtimg" data-original="//img.zdic.net/kxzd/7BE1.svg">						  <p>【集韻】【韻會】【正韻】<img class='lazy k_img' data-original='//img.zdic.net/song/cn/20024.svg'><span class='k_txt'>𠀤</span>初患切。逆而奪取曰篡。【爾雅·釋詁】篡，取也。【註】篡者，奪取也。【前漢·衞靑傳】靑給事建章，未知名。大長公主執囚靑，欲殺之，其友公孫敖與壯士往篡之。【註】師古曰：逆取曰篡。</p><p>又弋取也。【字彙補】《揚子》鴻飛冥冥，弋人何篡焉。別本作篡，非。篡原字从艹，不从。</p>
