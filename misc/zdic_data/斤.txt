                        </div>
                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>斤</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">jīn  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span>  ㄐㄧㄣˉ</span></p><ol><li>中国市制重量单位：二～。～～（过分计较琐细的或无关紧要的事物，如“～～计较”）。</li><li>古代砍伐树木的工具：斧～。</li></ol>                        
<div class="enbox"><p><span class="z_ts2">英语</span> a catty (approximately 500 g); an axe; keen, shrewd; KangXi radical number 69</p><p><span class="z_ts2">德语</span> Jin (chinesische Gewichtseinheit = 500g)​, Pfund (Zähl)​,Radikal Nr. 69 = Axt, Pfund (Eig)</p><p><span class="z_ts2">法语</span> unité de poids chinoise égale à 500g,0,5kg,livre (n.f.)​</p></div>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">斤</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>斤</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (象形。甲骨文字形，上面是横刃，下为曲柄，象斧斤形。本义:斧子一类的工具)</p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义 <span class="encs">[axe]</span>。一般用以砍木，与斧相似，比斧小而刃横</p><p><span class="diczx1">今，斫木斧也。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。</span><span class="diczx2">段</span>注:<span class="yhcs">“凡斫物者皆曰斧，斫木之斧，则谓之斤。”</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">于是乎丌釿锯制焉。以釿为之。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·在宥》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">皆执利兵，无者执斤。——<span class="smcs">《左传·哀公二十五年》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不夭斤斧。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·逍遥游》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">以斧斤考击而求之。——</span><span class="diczx2">宋</span>· <span class="diczx2"> 苏</span>轼<span class="smcs">《石钟山记》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 又如:斤斧(斧头);斤墨(斧头与墨斗);斤凿(斧头与凿子)</p><p><span class="cino">(4)</span> 古代一种似锄但比锄小的农具 <span class="encs">[small hoe]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">恶金以铸锄、夷、斤…——<span class="smcs">《国语》</span></span></p><p><strong>词性变化</strong></p><p>◎ <strong>斤</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈动〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 砍削，砍杀 <span class="encs">[cut]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">不斤不斧。——</span><span class="diczx2">皮日休</span><span class="smcs">《河桥赋》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">横斤山木。——<span class="smcs">《南史·宋测传》</span></span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:斤削(砍削);斤迹(斧头削砍的痕迹);斤械(用斧砍削)。又指对文字的删削修饰。如:斤正(斤斧，斤削。请人修改诗文的敬辞)</p><p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 过分计较 <span class="encs">[be too calculating]</span>。如:斤斤较量(在琐碎的小事上过分计较)</p><p>◎ <strong>斤</strong> <span class="dicpy">jīn</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/jīn.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈量〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 也作<span class="yhcs">“觔”</span>。中国和东南亚各国所用的各种重量单位中，均在600克左右;亦指中国在1929年规定的标准单位，等于1.1023磅或500克 <span class="encs">[catty]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">十六两为一斤。——<span class="smcs">《汉书·律历志》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四十斤金椎。——<span class="smcs">《史记·魏公子列传》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">千余斤。——</span><span class="diczx2">唐</span>· <span class="diczx2">白居易</span><span class="smcs">《卖炭翁》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">四五十斤。——</span><span class="diczx2">明</span>· <span class="diczx2">魏禧</span><span class="smcs">《大铁椎传》</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">市肉二斤。——</span><span class="diczx2">清</span>· <span class="diczx2">张廷玉</span><span class="smcs">《明史》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span>
