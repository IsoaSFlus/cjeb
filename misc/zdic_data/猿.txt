                        <div class="content definitions jnr">
						<p><strong>基本字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>● <strong>猿</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">yuán  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span>  ㄩㄢˊ</span></p><ol><p>　◎ 哺乳动物，与猴相似，比猴大，颊下没有囊，没有尾巴，猩猩、大猩猩、长臂猿等都是：～猴。～人。</p></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">猿</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>

                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><p>◎ <strong>猿</strong></p><p>猨、蝯 <span class="dicpy">yuán</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/yuán.mp3"></a></span> </p><p>〈名〉</p><p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从犬，袁声。猿似犬，故从犬。本义:灵长类动物，形态与猴相似) 同本义 <span class="encs">[ape]</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">蝯善援，禺属。——<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。通常写作<span class="yhcs">“猨、猿”</span>。</span></p><p><span class="diczx1">貉逾汶则死。——<span class="smcs">《考工记·总目》</span>。注:<span class="yhcs">“貉或为猨。谓善缘木之猨也。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">毋教猱升木。——<span class="smcs">《诗·小雅·角弓》</span>。传:<span class="yhcs">“猱，猨属。”</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">猿狙之便自山林来。——<span class="smcs">《庄子·天地》</span></span></p><p><span class="diczx1">猿之所以寿者，好引其末，是故气四越。——</span><span class="diczx2">汉</span>· <span class="diczx2">董仲舒</span><span class="smcs">《春秋繁露》</span></p><p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 又如:猿猴献果(指将人的四肢在胸前捆在一起的姿势);猿狖(猿猴);猿眩(猿临悬崖而目眩。极言险峻);猿臂(臂长如猿，运用自如，亦比喻攻守自如的作战形势)</p><p><strong>常用词组</strong></p><hr class="dichr" /><div class="cit type-xxjs"><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/猿猴">猿猴</a></span><span class="crefe"><a href="/hans/猿人">猿人</a></span></div>                    <div class="div copyright"> © 汉典 </div>
