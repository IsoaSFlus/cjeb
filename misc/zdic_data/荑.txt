						<p>● <strong>荑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">y&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/y&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>割除田里的野草，引申为削平：芟～。</li></ol><p>● <strong>荑</strong></p><p><span class="dicpy">t&iacute;  <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> ㄧˊ</span></p><hr class="dichr" /><ol>	<li>茅草的嫩芽：手如柔～。</li>	<li>草木初生的嫩芽：丹～。</li>	<li>一种似稗的杂草，通&ldquo;稊&rdquo;：不如～稗。</li></ol>

                    <h2 class="h2_entry"><span class="orth">荑</span><span class="span dictname"> 详细解释</span></h2>
                  </div>
                  <div class="content definitions xnr">
                    <p><strong>详细字义</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>荑</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">名</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> (形声。从艸，夷声。本义:茅草的嫩芽)</p>
<p><span class="cino">(2)</span> 同本义，引申之为草木嫩芽 <span class="encs">[tender bud]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荑，荑草也。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《说文》</span>。按，茅之初生也。</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">自牧归荑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;邶风&middot;静女》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">手如柔荑，肤如凝脂。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《诗&middot;卫风&middot;硕人》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">五谷者，种之美者也;苟为不熟，不如荑稗。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《孟子&middot;告子上》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">荑者，茅始熟中穰也，既白且滑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《风俗通》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荑英(新生的草);荑杨(新生的杨树)</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(3)</span> 通&ldquo;稊&rdquo;。草名。一种像稗子的草 <span class="encs">[a kind of grass which is like barnyard grass]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx2">炳</span>复次禁枯树，树即生荑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《后汉书&middot;方术传》</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">生繁华于枯荑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《晋书&middot;元帝纪》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx3">又如:荑枯(即&ldquo;枯杨生稊&rdquo;之意。喻绝处逢生;起死回生);荑稗(荑、稗为二草名，似禾，实比谷小，亦可食)</span></p>
<p><strong>词性变化</strong></p>
<hr class="dichr" />
<p>◎ <strong>荑</strong> <span class="dicpy">t&iacute;</span> <span class="ptr"><a class="audio_play_button i_volume-up ptr" data-src-mp3="//img.zdic.net/audio/zd/py/t&iacute;.mp3"></a></span> </p>
<p><span class="xx_cx">动</span></p>
<p><span class="cino">(1)</span> 发芽 <span class="encs">[germinate]</span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">草陆生曰荑。&mdash;&mdash;<span class="smcs">《通俗文》</span></span></p>
<p><span class="diczx1">陵罔掇丹荑。&mdash;&mdash;</span><span class="diczx2">郭璞</span><span class="smcs">《游仙诗》</span></p>
